<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>地底传出小狗哭声 男孩向大人求助展开救援</h2>
<a href="https://d78f5frq5siq8.cloudfront.net/S5mBnwV7h"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月28日讯】地底下传来微弱的声音，听起来像是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md#1">小狗</a>在哭，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%94%B7%E5%AD%A9.md#1">小男孩</a>赶紧向大人求助，他的叔叔马上带着钻孔工具来救援！这实在太令人震惊了，为什么小狗狗会被困在地底呢？希望它能平安获救！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10636580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10636580" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/83e9eabd8233b1f87352aa8e1d441923.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10636580 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/83e9eabd8233b1f87352aa8e1d441923-450x456.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10636580" class="wp-caption-text">工地的地底下居然传出<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md#1">小狗</a>的哭泣。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里看起来像是一段铁轨或一处工地，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%94%B7%E5%AD%A9.md#1">小男孩</a>无意间发现了小小的呜咽声，声源在地底下，狗狗的哭声就顺着一根小小的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E7%AE%A1.md#1">水管</a>传出来。男孩吓了一跳，赶快通知大人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10636581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10636581" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/a499afab4d4a2f0e694a494c63c2e37d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10636581 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/a499afab4d4a2f0e694a494c63c2e37d-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10636581" class="wp-caption-text">男孩发现了微弱的声音，向大人求救。尽管艳阳高照，他们依旧赶工，希望尽快就出小狗。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实有一只狗妈妈不久前才生了一窝小狗，后来其中一只走失，已经3天了，会不会地底下的这只小狗就是它的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">狗宝宝</a>？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10636582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10636582" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9cba16748f11aeb68f4b7ed9f504b4d3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10636582 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9cba16748f11aeb68f4b7ed9f504b4d3-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10636582" class="wp-caption-text">叔叔用钻孔的工具小心翼翼将地面凿开。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男孩的叔叔带着钻孔工具，开始在地上挖洞，他必须很小心，避免机器或落石伤害到小狗狗。他们在大太阳底下施工，汗流浃背，但没有停下手边的动作到一旁休息，他们希望赶紧将小狗救出来。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">看完整影片?</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-450be3c4-467d-40ce-7d84-7017fb794b85" data-id="player-450be3c4-467d-40ce-7d84-7017fb794b85"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/450be3c4-467d-40ce-7d84-7017fb794b85?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F14%2Fn10636490.md#1"></a></p>
<p>地面显然是水泥和钢筋的结构，非常坚硬，外层则有黄沙滚滚。男孩的叔叔钻出了一个小洞口，大约能让小孩的手进出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10636588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10636588" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c588b6ac25ff2bd7e5eed494d5f086af.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10636588 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c588b6ac25ff2bd7e5eed494d5f086af-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10636588" class="wp-caption-text">果然看到一只很小的幼犬！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男孩伸手进去摸索，“我摸到它的脚了！”小狗狗原本惊惶失措躲在狭小的角落，经过小男孩的抚摸，它知道有人要来救它，出口就在上方，于是自己也探出头来，但它被卡住无法自己脱困。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10636589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10636589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d8ecf544c9f257f8de8a6cb0bf31d12a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10636589 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d8ecf544c9f257f8de8a6cb0bf31d12a-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10636589" class="wp-caption-text">轻轻地将小狗拉出地底洞穴。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男孩的叔叔赶快抓住小狗狗的狗耳朵，轻柔且小心翼翼地把它拉出来……小狗狗终于离开地底了！真是太好了，拯救了一个脆弱的小生命，所有人都很高兴！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10636597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10636597" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/38c3279598d3b92dd67f5923f3b9a539.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10636597 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/38c3279598d3b92dd67f5923f3b9a539-450x454.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="454" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10636597" class="wp-caption-text">看起来才出生没有多久，真是可怜的小家伙！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虚弱的小狗狗在地上瑟瑟发抖，它现在已经脱离险境，应该不是害怕，可能是饿坏了，毕竟它已经3天没有好好吃东西。男孩赶紧把小狗抱还给狗妈妈，母子团圆，可喜可贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10636592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10636592" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9bfe726ad2c7e75462859c7ec0302a3e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10636592 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9bfe726ad2c7e75462859c7ec0302a3e-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10636592" class="wp-caption-text">赶快让它回到妈妈身边吧！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>让人感到纳闷的是，小狗到底如何从小<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E7%AE%A1.md#1">水管</a>“钻”进洞里去？这题真是太难了，不过眼下最重要也最珍贵的就是小狗得救，狗妈妈找回宝宝，男孩、叔叔和其他人也都感到欣慰！故事结局很圆满！</p>
