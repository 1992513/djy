<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>岁月不饶人？找借口之前 看看80岁的她</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月15日讯】随着时代进步，越来越多的发明，渐渐取代了人们的手脚，以前动手动脚才能做到的，现在往那一躺，按几个按键、甚至张张嘴，自动化的电器就帮你轻轻松松的做到了，科技越是发达，人要做的事也就越来越少了，看似何尝不是一件好事呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10605795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10605795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10605795 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-2-600x478.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="478" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10605795" class="wp-caption-text">这位阿嬷一样，身手矫健，不说你可能不知道她已经高龄80岁了！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10605797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10605797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10605797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2-1-600x482.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="482" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10605797" class="wp-caption-text">仰卧起坐、倒立也都难不倒她，灵活的就像个年轻人一样。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过也因为这样，人越来越懒，越来越多事情做起来也变得吃力了，搬东西曾经是再普通不过的事了，现在才搬没几下就在喊腰酸背痛，而许多3C产品也逐渐取带儿童的玩具、和想外出游玩的心了。</p>
<p>不过也有人不愿意这种风潮低头，继续用自己的那一套哲学生活，不过你能想像吗？原本应该待在家含饴弄孙、享受后半辈子的阿嬷，运动起来却没在喊累，反而胜过一堆年轻人呢！</p>
<p>“流水不腐，户枢不蠹”，只要你肯天天鞭策自己，就能像这位阿嬷一样，身手矫健，不说的话你会知道她已经高龄80岁了吗？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10605798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10605798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10605798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3-1-600x485.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="485" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10605798" class="wp-caption-text">原本应该待在家含饴弄孙的阿嬷，运动起来没在喊累，反而胜过一堆年轻人呢！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10605794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10605794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10605794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4-1-600x483.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="483" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10605794" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">有句话说“流水不腐，户枢不蠹”，人越是安逸，身体就会愈来越差。</span>（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当同龄的老人，坐轮椅的坐轮椅，躺病床的躺病床，她却在公园里抓起吊梯开始一根根往前吊，相当灵活，就连仰卧起坐、倒立也都难不倒她，阿嬷踏起漫步机更是跟其他人不一样，灵活的就像个年轻人一样。</p>
<p>看了这位不老阿嬷之后，东西搬不动、跑步没跑多久就气喘吁吁、刚出门就想回家的你，还在为你的懒惰找借口吗？相信你的身体绝对比这位阿嬷还要健壮，现在就放下电动、手机，走出户外拥抱阳光大自然吧！</p>
