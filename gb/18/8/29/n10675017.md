<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>教你做出完美义式炖饭 菌菇姜黄炖饭佐时蔬</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月27日讯】“意大利炖饭”是一道用高汤把米粒，煮成奶油般浓郁质地的意大利经典料理。意大利炖饭的味道主要来源于高汤，所以高汤品质要好，通常会以鱼、肉或蔬菜为基底。</p>
<p>意大利米也是很特别的品种，除了米粒较大以外，也容易释放淀粉，帮助炖饭中的高汤变浓稠，米粒的形状也不容易被破坏。</p>
<p>这道“菌菇姜黄炖饭佐时蔬”，高汤使用芹菜、洋葱、胡萝卜来熬煮，综合菇用奶油来拌炒提升香味跟色泽。最后等到意大利米快熟时加入姜黄粉，让米饭染上金黄美丽的外观。</p>
<p>如果是使用台湾米的话，烹煮前只需要浸泡几分钟就可以，但意大利米比较硬，烹煮前需要浸泡一、两个小时，才能有比较软的口感喔！赶快来动手做做看，感受这异国滋味的风采吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675129" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675129" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-202.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10675129 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-202-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675129" class="wp-caption-text">菌菇姜黄炖饭佐时蔬。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【准备材料】</h2>
<p>洋葱 6克<br />
蒜头 6克<br />
姜黄 3克<br />
综合菇 20克<br />
金针菇 6克<br />
意大利米 200克<br />
奶油 4克<br />
黑胡椒 少许<br />
生菜 适量<br />
枸杞 少许<br />
柠檬 少许<br />
高汤 250cc</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 洋葱及蒜头切末，锅中加橄榄油，放入洋葱及蒜头末下锅炒香。<br />
2. 加入意大利米拌炒，加入高汤并陆续加二、三次水煮熟（约七、八分钟）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675130" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2-116.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10675130 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2-116-450x379.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675130" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱及蒜头末下锅炒香，加入意大利米拌炒。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10675131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675131" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3-79.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10675131 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3-79-450x379.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675131" class="wp-caption-text">加入高汤并陆续加二、三次水煮熟（约七、八分钟）。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.?倒入姜黄粉于炖饭中。<br />
4. 综合菇下锅与黑胡椒炒香后，加入奶油及柠檬汁后备用。<br />
5. 油炸金针菇。<br />
6. 姜黄炖饭放入盘中，上面倒入综合菇、金针菇、枸杞及少许生菜装饰后完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675133" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4-54.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10675133 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4-54-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675133" class="wp-caption-text">倒入姜黄粉于炖饭中。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10675134" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675134" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5-33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10675134 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5-33-450x376.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675134" class="wp-caption-text">综合菇下锅与黑胡椒炒香后，加入奶油及柠檬汁后备用。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
