<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【组图】加拿大加斯佩半岛美景——海中巨石</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>游客抵达加斯佩半岛，必游佳景之一就是派赫塞巨石。乘船靠近派赫塞巨石，可以清楚看到巨石上的孔洞。（青云提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">文、图 | 青云</span></p>
<p>加拿大东部有着绵延千里的海岸线，顺着圣劳伦河（St-Laurent）沿岸直达加拿大东端魁北克省境内的加斯佩半岛（Gaspé Peninsula）是备受推崇的自驾车之旅。</p>
<p>游客抵达加斯佩半岛，必游佳景之一就是派赫塞巨石（法语：Rocher Percé，英语：Percé Rock），中文意思就是“穿孔的巨石”。这块巨石矗立海中，底部有一个大孔洞，高约15米，可容一条小船穿过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671469" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10671469 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671469" class="wp-caption-text">巨石底部的孔洞，高约15米，可容一条小船穿过。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10671441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10671441 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671441" class="wp-caption-text">巨石在水下的部分与陆地相连，据说落潮时可以走到巨石那边去。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10671442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671442" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock5.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10671442 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671442" class="wp-caption-text">巨石的另一侧呈现不同的景象。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>派赫塞巨石位于海中，距离附近的派赫塞镇海岸约800米。派赫塞巨石长约433米，宽约90米，高约88米，是世界少有的几块海中巨石之一，有人形容它“从某些角度看起来像一个漂白的超级油轮”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671447" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10671447 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671447" class="wp-caption-text">派赫塞巨石长约433米，宽约 90米，高约88 米，图中箭头处可乘坐几十人的船看起来太小了。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1844年，加拿大地质之父、威廉?爱德蒙?洛根爵士首次对这块巨石进行了地质考察，发现派赫塞巨石上的石灰岩地层可以追溯到3.75亿年至4亿多年前的泥盆纪时期，岩石包含150种不同的化石，如泥盆纪的三叶虫、达尔曼石、珊瑚和泥盆纪的海洋蠕虫等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10673472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10673472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10673472 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10673472" class="wp-caption-text">从不同的方位看巨石，观感不同。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10673445" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10673445" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10673445 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock13-600x292.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10673445" class="wp-caption-text">从不同的方位看巨石，观感不同。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10671451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180804_PerceRock8-600x292.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10671451 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180804_PerceRock8-600x292.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671451" class="wp-caption-text">太阳从巨石间升起，耀眼夺目。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10671459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10671459 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock10-600x292.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671459" class="wp-caption-text">巨石地处派赫塞小海湾，海湾的另一侧也很美。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>巨石被探险家发现并做记载最早是在1534年，当时第一位抵达加东海岸的殖民者雅克?卡蒂埃（Jacques Cartier）看到它，称巨大的岩石中有三个“拱门”。随着时间的流逝，其中两个“拱门”消失了，最后一个“拱门”也于1845年6月17日倒塌，留下了一根柱子。而人们现在看到的巨石，只有一个孔洞，即被称为“哥特式岩石拱”的拱门洞。</p>
<p>海中巨石独特的美丽令游客们着迷。来到加斯佩半岛，游客们不只在岸边欣赏派赫塞巨石，也一定会乘船近观其详。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10671464 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180803_PerceRock11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671464" class="wp-caption-text">从附近的圣安妮山上俯瞰巨石。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当然，乘船参观了派赫塞巨石之后，游客一般也会继续参观附近著名的鸟岛——博纳旺图岛（法语：?le Bonaventure，英语：Bonaventure Island）。</p>
