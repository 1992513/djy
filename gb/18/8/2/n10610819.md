<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《北风》红毯活动 朱志勋等人热情与粉丝自拍</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115306100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国电影《北风》8月2日于首尔松坡区乐天大楼举办红毯活动，左起赵震雄、黄晸玟、李圣旻、朱智勋。（全景林／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月03日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）电影《北风》昨（2）日晚间于首尔松坡区乐天大楼举行红毯活动，导演尹钟彬、主演的黄晸玟、赵震雄、李圣旻及朱智勋都参加了这天的活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10610858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10610858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115338100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10610858" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115338100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10610858" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《北风》红毯活动，朱智勋与粉丝自拍。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10610870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10610870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115333100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10610870" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115333100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10610870" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《北风》红毯活动，赵震雄与粉丝合影。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>红毯活动吸引众多民众围观，而演员群也回应粉丝们的热情，替粉丝签名并与大家自拍合影留念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10610864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10610864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115321100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10610864" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115321100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10610864" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《北风》红毯活动，李圣旻为粉丝签名。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10610861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10610861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115248100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10610861" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115248100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10610861" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《北风》红毯活动，黄晸玟与粉丝合影。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《北风》（韩文：??／汉字：工作）是以韩国国家安全企划部的情报员“黑金星”的真实故事改编的剧情片，由尹钟彬执导，入围第71届坎城影展午夜单元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10610862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10610862" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115255100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10610862" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115255100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10610862" class="wp-caption-text">黄晸玟出席《北风》8月2日的红毯活动。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该剧以1990年代中期的朝鲜半岛为故事背景，主角伪装成商人以渗透至各军事单位打探情报，却陷入两国权力高层之间的恐怖斗争。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10610868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10610868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115311100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10610868" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115311100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10610868" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《北风》红毯活动，导演尹钟彬与粉丝合影。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10610866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10610866" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115329100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10610866" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180802115329100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10610866" class="wp-caption-text">《北风》红毯活动，赵震雄与粉丝合影。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《北风》将于8月8日于韩国上映。</p>
