<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>收获皇后镇的美 动、静两套攻略</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-photo.jpg" />
<h6>大纪元合成图。
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月07日讯】（大纪元记者徐亦扬报导）在新西兰南岛的南湖地区，有一个被南阿尔卑斯山脉环绕的依山傍水的美丽小镇，她宛如风姿卓越的少女，在湖光山色里亭亭玉立。这里的四季气候分明：春季百花盛开，夏季蓝天艳阳，秋季红黄辉映、落英缤纷，冬季清爽晴朗。当时的殖民者说，这里美得应属女王所有；也有人说，以前的淘金者拜服于此地皇室般的高贵华美，小镇便由此得名——皇后镇。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-1-photo5.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10620308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-1-photo5-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620308" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p>每年，皇后镇都要接待大约200万名游客。有人来寻求这里天堂般的美景和宁静，在湖边悠闲地喝着咖啡、喂着鸭子、看看夕阳，一副岁月静好的模样；而“冒险之都”的美誉则让热爱户外运动的人们纷至沓来。这里是新西兰高空跳伞的发源地，全国最高的蹦极跳也在这里。如果你是《指环王》的影迷，那就前往拍摄地，身临其境感受中土世界的美丽与神秘。动静皆相宜，每个人都能在这里度过属于自己的完美假期。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620328" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-2-photo2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10620328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-2-photo2-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620328" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>如何到达</strong></p>
<p>从奥克兰出发，乘坐新西兰航空或捷星航空的直飞航班，行程约1小时50分钟；若是自驾，从奥克兰开到首都惠灵顿，乘坐渡轮前往南岛之端的皮克顿，再继续南下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620340" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-3-photo4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10620340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-3-photo4-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620340" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p>皇后镇位于新西兰南岛奥塔哥大区的西南部，是南岛著名的旅游度假地。小镇建在瓦卡蒂普湖（Lake Wakatipu）名为皇后湾（Queenstown Bay）的入口处。这是一个由冰川形成的细长Z型湖泊，可欣赏到卓越山脉（The Remarkables）、塞西尔峰（Cecil Peak）、瓦尔特峰（Walter Peak）以及小镇上方的本洛蒙德山（Ben Lomond）和皇后镇山（Queenstown Hill）的景色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-1-photo3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10620329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-1-photo3-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620329" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<p>皇后镇约8,705平方公里，人口却相当稀少。截止到去年六月，皇后镇的城市人口仅15,300人，常住人口为37,100人。</p>
<p><strong>皇后镇的邻镇包括</strong><br />
箭镇（Arrowtown）、格林诺奇（Glenorchy）、金斯顿 Kingston）、瓦纳卡（Wanaka）、亚历山大（Alexandra）和克伦威尔 Cromwell）。</p>
<p>离皇后镇最近的城市是但尼丁（Dunedin）和Invercargill。</p>
<p>皇后镇的气候偏寒冷。如夏天来访，白天可穿些轻便的衣服，但晚上需要添加衣物。在冬天，御寒衣服、围巾、手套和帽子是必备品。</p>
<p>户外冒险运动是到皇后镇旅游的重头戏。和新西兰其他城市的都会风光不同，皇后镇的特有景观是林立的一家家高空弹跳、激流泛舟、滑雪、喷射快艇、骑马、健行等旅游咨询中心。皇后镇店家所贩售的户外活动用品相当齐全，帐篷、睡袋、靴子、防寒衣、单车等一应俱全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620330" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-1-photo4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10620330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-1-photo4-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620330" class="wp-caption-text">高空跳伞 Skydive Nzone Skydive Queenstown（Dreamstime）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美食是度假体验不可或缺的一部分，皇后镇有一些新西兰最好的餐厅和酒吧，从精致的餐饮、名厨的餐厅、友好的小餐馆到人气甜品店，这里应有尽有。皇后镇因其美味的食物、美酒和传奇的夜生活而闻名于全球。</p>
<p><strong>瓦卡蒂普湖 Lake Wakatipu?</strong></p>
<p><strong>Queenstown 因你而美</strong></p>
<p>瓦卡蒂普湖是万年的冰川运动形成的冰湖，是新西兰第三大湖，全长84公里，湖水深335米。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620333" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-2-photo.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10620333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-2-photo-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620333" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10620335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-3-photo3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10620335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-3-photo3-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620335" class="wp-caption-text">SU-CHUL. LEE</figcaption></figure>
<p>瓦卡蒂普湖的形状似一道闪电，湖水在阳光的照耀下深邃幽蓝。湖的四周高山林立，最高的山厄恩斯洛山（Mount Earnslaw）海拔2819米。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620338" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-3-photo.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10620338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/queenstown-3-photo-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620338" class="wp-caption-text">Dreamstime</figcaption></figure>
