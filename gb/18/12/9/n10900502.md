<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大空间 节能 中小型 SUV首选</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/Skoda-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大纪元合成图。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年12月10日讯】（大纪元工商记者李维新西兰奥克兰报导）大众车系斯柯达继推出7座位 SUV 科迪亚克 Kodiaq、并因此车成为新西兰得奖最多的品牌后，今年再次在新西兰推出了一款中型、紧凑 SUV 卡洛克 Karoq（取代原来的野帝 Yeti），希望为家庭提供经济适用、具备各种智慧功能的运动休旅车；既适合日常交通，又足够全家人探险、旅行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900506" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m70-safety.cd8181d0091dcd11807bc195d3ae9ec0.fill-960x548-450x257.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10900506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m70-safety.cd8181d0091dcd11807bc195d3ae9ec0.fill-960x548-450x257.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900506" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新斯柯达卡洛克 SKODA Karoq 是一款宽敞的中型SUV，采用斯柯达领先技术。所有设计都针对人生活中的重要活动，无论您是要去健身房、还是周末的户外探索活动。</p>
<p>强有力的设计、经济实惠的涡轮增压发动机、最先进的辅助系统、以及创新的连接解决方案，使Karoq成为您的完美搭档。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900507" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1-450x262.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10900507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1-450x262.png" alt="" width="450" b="262" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900507" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>具有众多水晶元素的情感和动感设计，则是SKODA新SUV设计语言的特色。</p>
<p>SKODA Karoq提供卓越的内部空间、全新的驾驶辅助系统和全LED大灯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900508" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900508" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m62-exterior-01.c3d6093786e84603734e9b0734960f51.fill-999x499-450x225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10900508" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m62-exterior-01.c3d6093786e84603734e9b0734960f51.fill-999x499-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900508" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>省油 远超同类型SUV</h2>
<p>Karoq延续了斯柯达科迪亚克Kodiaq的设计。如果遵循北美阿拉斯加人的概念，从科迪亚克Kodiaq到卡洛克Karoq是“大熊”变成了“小熊”——小熊Kaloq在同类型紧凑SUV中，不但宽敞、而且节能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900510" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m70-safety.cd8181d0091dcd11807bc195d3ae9ec0.fill-960x548-1-450x257.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10900510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m70-safety.cd8181d0091dcd11807bc195d3ae9ec0.fill-960x548-1-450x257.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900510" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Karoq使用1.5升汽油涡轮四缸增压发动机，功率输出可以达到110千瓦和250牛顿米，与科迪亚克相同，但这款发动机具有气缸停用等多种节能特性，在油耗方面可以栖身于小型省油房车之列。</p>
<p>新西兰机动车杂志（Autocar）曾把斯柯达Karoq与同类的尼桑Qashqai做了对比，Karoq的油耗平均为每100公里5.2升，而Qashqai的油耗则为6.9升，这个数字对于SUV车来说并不算高，只是Karoq相比来讲确实省油而已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900511" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m70-roominess.5b1b39737ba7c12701f26df06526d6be.fill-960x548-450x257.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10900511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m70-roominess.5b1b39737ba7c12701f26df06526d6be.fill-960x548-450x257.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900511" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Karoq还提供3.0升柴油发动机，因为新西兰柴油价格比汽油便宜将近三分之一，柴油车在这里往往是更受欢迎的选择；况且Karoq二氧化碳排放量相对也较低，使得这款SUV更加经济实惠。</p>
<h2>超宽敞行李空间</h2>
<p>在同类型SUV中，Karoq目前是行李空间最宽敞的一个。通过选择可添加、可拆卸的3个独立后排座椅，来增加行李箱空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900512" style="width: 280px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m76-clever-boot-01.aa3a2444bb132c2ddf7397db46e398f6.fill-280x280.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10900512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m76-clever-boot-01.aa3a2444bb132c2ddf7397db46e398f6.fill-280x280.jpg" alt="" width="280" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900512" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着“VarioFlex”座椅向前滑动、折叠或完全拆除，行李空间则由最初的479升，逐渐上升，直至最后达到1810升。这时，Karoq可以转变为送货车。相比之下，7座SUV科迪亚克，最大空间也只可达到2005升。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900514" style="width: 280px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m76-clever-boot-02.203742aeffacbd8c7b4cb85010d56ee3.fill-280x280.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10900514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m76-clever-boot-02.203742aeffacbd8c7b4cb85010d56ee3.fill-280x280.jpg" alt="" width="280" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900514" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Karoq的宽敞行李空间，比一般的中型SUV都要大出许多，足够装载高尔夫套装和全套钓鱼设备，也是和全家人野外旅行。</p>
<p>对于许多买家来说，Varioflex后排座椅可能是必备功能，但它不是标配。一般以1765元的Ambition +套件、或者作为2500元的Plus Style on Style套件的一部分，还搭配皮革、运动型方向盘、带拨片和运动踏板。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900513" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m62-interior-04.967232fe1b0691da20ec7878566a8da1.fill-659x420-450x287.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10900513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m62-interior-04.967232fe1b0691da20ec7878566a8da1.fill-659x420-450x287.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900513" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>外形设计独特</h2>
<p>Karoq的外部设计，一改Yeti的“丑陋”，一定程度上回归热了“大熊”科迪亚克的强壮、锐利风格，并强化了斯柯达SUV的设计特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900517" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-M62-gallery-2.6d6537670c37159d9f19727ea789c0f4.fill-999x499-450x225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10900517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-M62-gallery-2.6d6537670c37159d9f19727ea789c0f4.fill-999x499-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900517" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大灯的设计灵感是来自波西米亚传统的水晶工艺，分体式LED大灯的设计有些复古，塑造车头立体感，“睫毛”状灯带则是车灯上比较亮眼的设计。</p>
<p>车身线条则参考了钻石切割工艺，简洁紧凑，立体感十足。“直瀑”式进气格栅，感觉动力内饰的造型一甩传统“大众”式的设计风格，用简单的线条凸显出简洁富有张力的视觉效果。</p>
<h2>实用方便</h2>
<p>斯柯达Karoq在行驶质感上可以说不输给“大熊”柯迪亚克，并且这种高档感充斥在整个驾驶环境内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900518" style="width: 280px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m76-clever-details-02.5d3839551882baf75ed273d6d22e693b.fill-280x280.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10900518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m76-clever-details-02.5d3839551882baf75ed273d6d22e693b.fill-280x280.jpg" alt="" width="280" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900518" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>整车的悬挂系统偏向厚重，给人以高档豪华的感觉，平稳、大气，甚至连路面上的细微振动，都能够很完整的过滤掉，非常适用于城市道路上较多的颠簸。</p>
<p>值得一提的是，Karoq在噪声和振动（NVH）方面做的也可以说相当完美，尤其是静音性能方面。在车速高达120公里/小时的情况下，车内仍可达到“绝对”安静，整个车对于路面噪音和发动机噪音的控制相当严格，这一点也能说明斯柯达在造车工艺上的领先地位。</p>
<p>机械式按键搭配旋钮开关的空调系统，在Karoq上得到了保留，因为大众设计师确信，这样的设计更符合驾驶员的使用与盲操作时的安全性。</p>
<p>电子手刹设置在了排挡杆后方，这也是大众集团车型常见的、确保安全又方便驾驶员的设计。</p>
<h2>智慧安全辅助</h2>
<p>与其他的新型车辆一样，斯柯达Karoq也延续使用了智慧安全辅助操作系统，并引进最新技术进行改进。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900519" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m70-connectivity.7f4d29fd0b1b78b16a6e3c1e3311a760.fill-960x548-450x257.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10900519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/skoda-karoq-m70-connectivity.7f4d29fd0b1b78b16a6e3c1e3311a760.fill-960x548-450x257.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900519" class="wp-caption-text">Skoda Karoq。（图片来源：Skoda官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Ambition +系统配备了自动制动功能，包括行人识别、巡航控制、车道辅助、盲点检测、后方交通警报、带摄像头的前/后停车传感器、无钥匙进入/启动、LED头灯和带电话投影的触摸荧屏。</p>
<p>Karoq还选择了斯柯达手机应用程式SkodaApp，它可以通过您的手机运行，并可以监控一系列驾驶数据。</p>
<p>TSI和TDI两种风格都采用了自适应巡航、驾驶模式选择、还带有虚拟踏板的动力后门，三键个性化，双区空调，以及像较大的SUV科迪亚克Kodiaq一样，更全面的货舱配件，包括分区网屏和Veltro货物元件。</p>
<h2>看看别人怎么说</h2>
<p>下面摘抄一些专业媒体的评论，从中可看出斯柯达Karoq的特别。</p>
