<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>铜锣湾怀旧风味茶餐厅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130110021366-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（米芝Gi提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年12月13日讯】70年代初适逢香港开始经济起飞，香港人的经济及生活条件越来越好，进入了另一个阶段。据说“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E9%BA%A6%E8%AE%B0.md#1">罗麦记</a>”这间餐厅是由两位姓罗及姓麦的老板一同开设，他们以六、七十年代的香港作为蓝本，把餐厅设计成一间怀旧冰室，更把那时的昔日美食放在其中，让大家能够重温这种地道滋味。</p>
<p><strong>完整影片：</strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-fd0255f9-81d6-491e-79a3-39c390f5c8dd" data-id="player-fd0255f9-81d6-491e-79a3-39c390f5c8dd"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/fd0255f9-81d6-491e-79a3-39c390f5c8dd?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&amp;d=54&&cat=ncid1251282&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F12%2F13%2Fn10907907.md#1"></a>
<p>餐厅内外的环境和装潢都是浓浓的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%80%E6%97%A7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">怀旧风</a>，先说说餐厅外吧，右边用了枣红色的门闸，底下放置了拨号电话、大大部连天线的收音机、要自己扭转台的黑白电视、还有那个放置玻璃樽的绿色架和行李箱，这都是六、七十年代港人熟悉的家俬和电器。踏入餐厅后，坐在卡位还可以看到怀旧窗花装饰，墙身除了漆上怀旧品牌广告标贴，更有模仿“九龙皇帝”的字迹，踏进餐厅就像进了了怀旧小香港。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10907988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10907988" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109471366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10907988" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109471366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10907988" class="wp-caption-text">餐厅内外的环境和装潢都是浓浓的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%80%E6%97%A7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">怀旧风</a>。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10908102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10908102" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109331366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10908102" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109331366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10908102" class="wp-caption-text">餐厅内外的环境和装潢都是浓浓的怀旧风。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我选择坐卡位怀旧味更浓，打开餐牌款款食物都吸引，包括一些经<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">茶餐厅</a>食物，例如虾多士、炒牛河、捞一丁，还有近年兴起的面包砖头饭等等。可惜当晚只得我和朋友两人吃不了得多，最后点了炸鸡髀拼薯条、避风塘鲜鱿、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%BF%E9%A3%8E%E5%A1%98%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">避风塘海鲜炒饭</a>和两杯特饮包括红豆冰粒粒奶茶和菠萝粒粒冰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10907948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10907948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130110021366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10907948" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130110021366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10907948" class="wp-caption-text">这次点了炸鸡髀拼薯条、避风塘鲜鱿、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%BF%E9%A3%8E%E5%A1%98%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">避风塘海鲜炒饭</a>和两杯特饮：红豆冰粒粒奶茶和菠萝粒粒冰。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>先上来两杯特饮，两杯特饮的冰都是用菠萝和红豆做成冰粒，令人惊喜，很有小时候把红豆沙，或者原片菠萝，雪在冰柜造成雪条的感觉。好吃之余也能确保冰溶掉，也不会使整杯饮品变淡，真的很赞。而且两杯饮品甜度刚刚好，菠萝粒粒冰甜中带微果酸，很醒胃，杯内有超过半杯的菠萝片。有点美中不足是，吃得出用了地扪的糖水菠萝，而不是新鲜菠萝肉。粒粒红豆奶茶的红豆软腍，奶茶香滑，好喝！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10907925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10907925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109361366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10907925" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109361366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10907925" class="wp-caption-text">菠萝粒粒冰（左）和红豆冰粒粒奶茶。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10907931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10907931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109441366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10907931" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109441366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10907931" class="wp-caption-text">菠萝粒粒冰甜中带微果酸，很醒胃，杯内有超过半杯的菠萝片。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10907929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10907929" style="width: 433px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109401366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10907929" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109401366.jpg" alt="" width="433" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10907929" class="wp-caption-text">粒粒红豆奶茶的红豆软腍，奶茶香滑。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>炸鸡髀薯条炸得香脆，薯条咬落充满薯蓉，很好吃。避风塘鲜鱿韧但味道一般。最后上来的是避风塘海鲜炒饭，炒饭炒得够干身粒粒分明也不会过油，入口烟韧香口。炒饭除了有蛋、还有爽口大虾、炸鱼块和鲜鱿在内，炒饭真的做得很不错，很好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10907935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10907935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109511366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10907935" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109511366-600x391.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10907935" class="wp-caption-text">炸鸡髀薯条炸得香脆，薯条咬落充满薯蓉。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10907944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10907944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109581366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10907944" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109581366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10907944" class="wp-caption-text">避风塘鲜鱿韧但味道一般。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10907939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10907939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109551366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10907939" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812130109551366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10907939" class="wp-caption-text">避风塘海鲜炒饭的饭炒得够干身粒粒分明也不会过油，入口烟韧香口。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
