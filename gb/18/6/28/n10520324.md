<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>浓郁起司情牵牛肉时蔬贝果 满足你挑剔味蕾</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10603615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月02日讯】有时不想吃正餐，那来点创意轻食吧！诱人牛肉炒新鲜时蔬，光是馅料魅力就无法挡！再配上浓郁诱人的万恶牵丝起司，跟饱足感十足的贝果，完美组合满足你挑剔的味蕾。好吃的让人忍不住还想再来一个！</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">看完整影片?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-10bf185d-1a53-4514-5402-5e47661742e9" data-id="player-10bf185d-1a53-4514-5402-5e47661742e9"></div></div><a data-cfasync="false" src="/cdn-cgi/as/5c5dd728/cloudflare-static/email-decode.min.js"></a><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/10bf185d-1a53-4514-5402-5e47661742e9?r=16x9&amp;s=720x620&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fcn.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F28%2Fn10520324.md#1"></a></p>
<h2>【食材准备】</h2>
<p>贝果 3个<br />
牛肉 325克<br />
洋葱 切块<br />
青椒 切块<br />
红椒 切块<br />
蘑菇 切片<br />
帕芙隆起司<br />
焗烤起司</p>
<h2>【调味料】</h2>
<p>盐 1茶匙<br />
黑胡椒粒 1/4茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_10520536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10520536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/30308516fbe8356fff8c4937f541f4bc.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10520536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/30308516fbe8356fff8c4937f541f4bc-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10520536" class="wp-caption-text">有时不想吃正餐，那来点创意轻食吧！诱人牛肉炒新鲜时蔬，光是馅料魅力就无法挡！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.热锅加入食用油，将牛肉放入锅内拌炒，约7分熟可先捞起锅备用。（可以个人喜好调整）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10520463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10520463" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/ab2ab619f2587023ecba18fc1eb7a400.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10520463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/ab2ab619f2587023ecba18fc1eb7a400-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10520463" class="wp-caption-text">香气扑鼻的炒牛肉。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.接着，放入切成块状的洋葱，下锅藉由刚才留在锅内的肉汁，继续拌炒；再放入块状青椒、红椒，均匀拌炒，让食材充分吸收肉汁。加入切片蘑菇增添香气和口感，均匀翻炒后，即可倒入牛肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10520464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10520464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c11bedec93abd2cf2ec922d07ae010d9.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10520464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c11bedec93abd2cf2ec922d07ae010d9-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10520464" class="wp-caption-text">再配上缤纷时蔬均衡一下。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.再来添加点调味料提味：盐 1茶匙、黑胡椒粒 1/4茶匙，均匀拌炒。馅料部分完成就完成啰！</p>
<p>4.紧接着，将贝果切对半，铺上帕芙隆起司，再将适量的牛肉馅料放上，最后，撒上焗烤起司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10520465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10520465" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/86fd27890a046414e8e80fe48ab71ad0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10520465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/86fd27890a046414e8e80fe48ab71ad0-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10520465" class="wp-caption-text">当牛肉时蔬贝果，遇上牵丝起司，疗愈滋味让你一吃就爱上。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.最后关卡，放进烤箱，烘烤10分钟，温度约控制在摄氏200°C／华氏400°F，等待烤箱跳起来，就可以大口享用热呼呼的牛肉起司贝果啰！</p>
<p>口味咸淡及食材，皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
