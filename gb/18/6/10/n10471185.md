<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>正体汉字文化节融入科技展演 惊艳全场!</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图：观摩会盛况。 （联合中文学校提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月10日讯】联合中文学校一年一度的华语文教与学观摩会，于六月九日下午1:30假Moscrop Secondary School大体育馆举行。此次观摩会主题是“学华语向前走”虚拟实境及游戏式学习，场面热闹有趣。会中除了中文学校师生，还有本地社区家庭、主流人士，约450人参与盛会。中华民国驻温哥华台北经文处副处长刘汉青、侨务秘书黄俪萱、教育组长唐天华也应邀莅临指导。</p>
<p>还有两位来自台湾，现任B.C.省省议员康安礼和陈苇蓁女士也现身说法在海外学习中文的重要性，并盛赞联合中文学校将虚拟实境及iPad游戏融入华语文学习的教学模式，比起当时她们在海外学习中文的方式，更活泼且更能够激发学习中文的兴趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10471246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471246" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-5-e1528615353257.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10471246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-5-e1528615353257.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471246" class="wp-caption-text">图：驻温哥华台北经文处刘汉青副处长致词。 （联合中文学校提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>联合中文学校赖飞钟校长表示，本年度将举办四场正体汉字文化节活动。其中第一场“看图说中文竞赛”和第二场“唐诗朗诵竞赛”于今天同时颁奖。</p>
<p>“看图说中文竞赛”总计参加人数300余人，参赛者以侨委会出版品“学华语向前走”的情境图娓娓道来图中的意涵，创意十足。表现优异者颁发卓越和优等奖。今年“唐诗朗诵竞赛”是第二年举办，参赛者各个铆足了劲，可谓有备而来。竞争激烈，道具、服装融入唐诗时代背景，辅以科技录影技巧，将唐诗朗诵、人物、背景融入视频。现场播出竞赛成果，惊艳四座！海外推动汉字文化，责无旁贷!</p>
<figure id="attachment_10471242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471242" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-1-e1528615282301.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10471242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-1-e1528615282301.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471242" class="wp-caption-text">（联合中文学校提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10471245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471245" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-4-e1528615302447.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10471245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-4-e1528615302447.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471245" class="wp-caption-text">（联合中文学校提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“学华语向前走”虚拟实境及游戏式学习观摩会，赖校长说明“学华语向前走”VR虚拟实境及教学运用。现场并提供由赖校长亲自制作的“学华语向前走”虚拟实境课程，以六部VR让来宾体验，沉浸在“学华语向前走”虚拟世界情境当中。更有丰富有趣的“学华语向前走”iPad游戏导引说明。现场分12个iPad体验区，由12位老师担任解说员，说明融入华语文学习要领及示范，并让所有来宾体验iPad游戏学习华语文的乐趣。游戏中还应用人工智慧语音辨识来学习中文，是学习中文的一大创举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10471243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471243" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-2-e1528615318339.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10471243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-2-e1528615318339.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471243" class="wp-caption-text">（联合中文学校提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>观摩会中还用心提供“台湾民俗文化服务”香包制作，让近450位来宾及师生们亲自动手做端午香包。只见人手一个DIY香包，专注的缝制黏贴，落实文化在地传承、在地扎根、在地发扬，也展现了台湾端午文化的丰富意涵，更将文化融入中文教学，令人感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10471240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10471240" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-6-e1528615332514.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10471240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/United-Chinese-School-180610-culture-festival-6-e1528615332514.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10471240" class="wp-caption-text">（联合中文学校提供）</figcaption></figure>
