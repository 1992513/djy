<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10553562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-6-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月10日讯】一般的煎<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9.md#1">培根</a>片、荷包蛋、烤吐司吃腻了吗？来点不一样的变化吧！利用培根、吐司做成像杯子状，放入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>和鸡蛋，做成蛋卷。早餐换一种吃法，心情也会变不一样哟！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494098" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-102.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-102-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494098" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E8%9B%8B%E5%8D%B7.md#1">培根起司蛋卷</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9.md#1">培根</a> 6片<br />
吐司 3片<br />
莫札瑞拉<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>（丝）<br />
鸡蛋 6颗<br />
橘甜椒 1/4颗<br />
黄甜椒 1/4颗<br />
小黄瓜 1/4条<br />
融化的奶油 少许<br />
有凹槽的烤盘</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.橘甜椒、黄甜椒、小黄瓜切成粗丝。<br />
2.烤箱预热至摄氏200度／华氏400度。<br />
3.取一个有凹槽的烤盘，并涂上少许奶油。<br />
4.将每一片培根卷成圆圈，立起来放进烤盘凹槽中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494093" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-154.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-154-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494093" class="wp-caption-text">培根卷成圆圈，放进烤盘凹槽中，圆形吐司片放底层。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.吐司用小杯子或圆形模型切成圆片。<br />
6.在最底层放进刚切好的圆形吐司片。<br />
7.再铺上莫札瑞拉起司、蛋、起司、蔬菜丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494095" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-125.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-125-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494095" class="wp-caption-text">铺上莫札瑞拉起司、蛋、起司、蔬菜丝。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.烤盘放入烤箱，烘烤15分钟（摄氏200度／华氏400度）。<br />
9.取出烤箱。<br />
10.装盘时可撒上些许香芹末。<br />
11.完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494097" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-111.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-111-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494097" class="wp-caption-text">烤盘放入烤箱，烘烤15分钟。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
