<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10574187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-10-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月29日讯】电视中美食画面，总是好吸引人，都忍不住要擦一擦嘴角的口水。尤其是烤盘上热情舞动的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">韩国烤肉</a>，是不是常让你也心动不已，好想尝一口呢？</p>
<p>简单几步骤你也可以在家独享，多汁好吃的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">韩国烤肉</a>，美味口感在家大口吃肉超过瘾。</p>
<h2>【主要食材】</h2>
<p>牛肉 适量</p>
<h2>【腌料伙伴】</h2>
<p>梨子切丁<br />
洋葱切丁<br />
苹果切丁<br />
大蒜<br />
白芝麻<br />
水<br />
酱油<br />
糖<br />
芝麻油</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p><strong>备料准备：</strong>将梨子、洋葱、苹果切丁，放置一旁备用。</p>
<p><strong>腌料制作：</strong>容器中放入水、酱油、糖、大蒜，再将切丁的梨子、洋葱、苹果倒入，最后，倒入芝麻油和白芝麻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10510211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10510211" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3f598504407161c21c9551c7d0009fc8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10510211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3f598504407161c21c9551c7d0009fc8-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10510211" class="wp-caption-text">清爽口感的秘密全在腌料里。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>腌制牛肉：</strong>腌制前，先将肉片两面划开，接着再把肉卷起来，并依序放入腌料容器中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10510294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10510294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/31b3f178b08bfe83b3927775504e92ed.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10510294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/31b3f178b08bfe83b3927775504e92ed-450x356.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10510294" class="wp-caption-text">腌制牛肉前，先将肉片两面划开。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10510295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10510295" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/67bff0de860bb45fcd12f3953c34eed5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10510295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/67bff0de860bb45fcd12f3953c34eed5-450x376.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10510295" class="wp-caption-text">接着再把肉卷起来，并依序放入腌料容器中。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>发酵24小时。即可取出，放置烤盘或锅中，煎熟即可享用美味的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10510214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10510214" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/dfd0e1a9f18b7ae8298df23c93f53bdd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10510214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/dfd0e1a9f18b7ae8298df23c93f53bdd-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10510214" class="wp-caption-text">腌制过后，放置烤盘或锅中，煎熟即可享用美味的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E8%82%89.md#1">烤肉</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【备注】口味咸淡及食材，皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
