<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一群乳牛欢乐奔跑 整座牧场都是我的游乐园</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10554314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月22日讯】牛牛们出来放风啰！哇！好久不见的青草蓝天，整座牧场都是我的游乐园。牛牛们开心地蹦来蹦去，简直就像放大版的山羊宝宝，好可爱喔！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10484421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10484421" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-123.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10484421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-123-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10484421" class="wp-caption-text">这些牛群刚刚经过了“5个月”的“闭关休养”，终于放风了！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>通常小山羊喜欢这种走走路就弹跳一下的特殊“羊步”，没想到这群乳牛也会这样大步跳跃，而且还绕着圈圈跑个不停！真是非常欢乐的活动呢！好像在举办特殊的庆典，投入得很。</p>
<p>白底黑色斑点、白底浅棕色斑点、黑底白色斑点……整座牧场各色各样的乳牛，看见草坪都开心得不行！跑啊跳的，真的是跟平时对它们“缓慢”又“淡定”的印象差很大！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10484422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10484422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-97.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10484422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-97-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10484422" class="wp-caption-text">这群乳牛大步跳跃，还绕着圈圈跑个不停！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10484423" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10484423" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-91.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10484423" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-91-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10484423" class="wp-caption-text">白底黑斑、白底棕斑、黑底白斑……各色各样的乳牛看见草坪都开心得不行！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原来这些牛群刚刚经过了“5个月”的“闭关休养”，在比较寒冷的地区，农民为了避免牛只在寒冬中不慎被冻坏了，强制将牛群关在较温暖的马厩中。</p>
<p>好不容易春天来临，没有冻坏但快闷坏的牛牛们，一听见栅门打开，简直乐坏了！群起向前冲啊！“好开心！好开心！好开心！”牛牛看到绿色的青草地，就是开心！真是单纯又容易满足！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10484424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10484424" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-85.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10484424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-85-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10484424" class="wp-caption-text">牛牛们，一听见栅门打开，简直乐坏了！群起向前冲啊！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10484425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10484425" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-79.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10484425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-79-600x392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10484425" class="wp-caption-text">牛牛说：“你在看我吗？没关系，可以近一点。”（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友笑说，每一种动物内心果然都住着快乐的灵魂！也有网友表示，他曾亲眼目睹这样的牛群在草地奔跑，当下牛群散发的喜悦氛围，让他也觉得“噢！真是幸福！”</p>
<p>牛牛们被关久了所以想念大自然，一放风就像美梦成真，欢天喜地、手舞足蹈！想想平常许多人也都被“关在”水泥建筑物，是否也该放放风去大自然快乐奔跑一回呢？</p>
