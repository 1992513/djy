<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>他自愿照料动物 没想到小羊驼不领情专找麻烦</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-10435201 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-2-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月30日讯】（大纪元记者莫琳报导）动物电视节目Animal Bytes TV制作人布莱恩?巴洁克（Brian Barczyk）本人热爱动物，善于应付各种各样的小动物，举凡各种爬行动物如蛇、壁虎、蓝舌龙，甚至遇到顽固或不可预期的动物也大都难不倒他。直到这一次他遇到了米斯蒂……</p>
<p>这一天，巴洁克来到了美国马里兰州的Tri State动物园，打算亲自照料动物。于是，他进入了一个专门保育幼小动物的宠物栏。这里住着各种不同的可爱小动物，有小羊、小马、小狗、鸡等等，还有一只4岁羊驼米斯蒂（Misty）。米斯蒂其实是羊驼和美洲驼（骆马）杂交的骆驼，模样俏皮可爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-shouhuihsu-zoo-04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10524345 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-shouhuihsu-zoo-04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524345" class="wp-caption-text">骆驼米斯蒂不让巴洁克工作，专找他麻烦。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但这只外表可爱的骆驼见到不速之客可有它自己的想法。正当巴洁克用铲耙清里栏内的垃圾时，米斯蒂从右后方顶撞巴洁克，让巴洁克吓了一跳，“这是怎么回事啊？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-shouhuihsu-zoo-01.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10524224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-shouhuihsu-zoo-01-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524224" class="wp-caption-text">小骆驼米斯蒂从巴洁克身后顶撞上来。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>事情还没结束，只要巴洁克一工作，米斯蒂就来捣乱，一会儿将它长长的脖子顶住巴洁克的上身，再用一只前脚钩住巴洁克的腿，让巴洁克动弹不得.</p>
<p>过一会儿，它又从巴洁克的身后撞上来，整个身子扒在巴洁克的背上，两只前脚抱住巴洁克的腰，让巴洁克根本无法工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-shouhuihsu-zoo-02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10524227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-shouhuihsu-zoo-02-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524227" class="wp-caption-text">米斯蒂就是缠着巴洁克不让他工作。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>巴洁克没遇过这么难缠的动物。尽管他对米斯蒂说：“别打扰我。”米斯蒂仍不为所动。只要巴洁克拿起铲耙，米斯蒂就不放过他，甚至追起巴洁克来了。巴洁克被米斯蒂追着在栏内拼命奔跑的模样实在太有趣了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-shouhuihsu-zoo-03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10524235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-shouhuihsu-zoo-03-600x339.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524235" class="wp-caption-text">巴洁克拿米斯蒂一点办法都没有，只能被米斯蒂追着跑了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后巴洁克不得不投降，丢下铲耙，翻过栏杆，逃到栏外去了。</p>
