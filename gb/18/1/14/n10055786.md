<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>张惠妹重返《巨星红白》 近40组歌手团体接力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132032541487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《2018红白》红白艺人出场。（ 台视提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年01月14日讯】台湾除夕特别节目《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/2018%E8%B6%85%E7%BA%A7%E5%B7%A8%E6%98%9F%E7%BA%A2%E7%99%BD%E8%89%BA%E8%83%BD%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%8F.md#1">2018超级巨星红白艺能大赏</a>》于昨天（13日）在小巨蛋热闹登场，集结40组海内外艺人、团体献艺陪观众迎接新年。其中，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E6%83%A0%E5%A6%B9.md#1">张惠妹</a>（阿妹）是睽违5年再重返《红白》舞台，一连演唱3首歌，让气氛更为热烈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055800" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033011487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10055800" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033011487.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="661" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055800" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》嘉宾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E6%83%A0%E5%A6%B9.md#1">张惠妹</a> 。（ 台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿妹以大红装扮登场，一连演唱了《连名带姓》、《戒断》、《你说了算》，她打趣说：“真的很久没回来小巨蛋唱，但我不能再叫大家‘跳起来’。”不过在她演唱完后，有部分观众也跟着离场，让最后表演的韩团NCT127有些尴尬，但他们仍然卖力演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033331487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10055830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033331487-600x529.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="529" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055830" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》表演嘉宾韩团NCT127。（ 台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年《红白》由Ella担任“红白大使”，她与年仅10岁、被誉为“乌克丽丽神童”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AF%E7%BE%BF.md#1">冯羿</a>带来开场秀。她身穿红色连身裤装与大衣搭配千万珠宝，展现女王风范。看到两位表演完后，黄子佼也一身喜气西装打扮惊喜登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055804" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132137441487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10055804" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132137441487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055804" class="wp-caption-text">Ella担任“红白大使”，她与年仅10岁、被誉为“乌克丽丽神童”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AF%E7%BE%BF.md#1">冯羿</a>带来开场秀。（台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他打趣说：“好感动刚一上场就看到你把孩子劲宝带上来，原来他已经长这么大了！”Ella则接着得意回应：“而且他非常有才华、还是网路红人喔！”两人妙语如珠炒热现场气氛。而活动依照惯例两队用滚红白大球的方式决定出场顺序。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132032491487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10055805" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132032491487-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055805" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》主持群（左起）纳豆、玛丽、黄子佼、王少伟、花花 。（ 台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由红队的恺乐先攻，一身劲装载歌载舞带来歌曲《久等了》、《Chocolate》；白队则派出Spexial应战，一连带来《Buddy Buddy》等组曲，当唱到《Super Style》时，原本因脚伤无法登台的队长宏正，最后神秘现身同台，让现场粉丝又惊又喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033231487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10055994" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033231487-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055994" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》表演嘉宾，图为男团SpeXial。（ 台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年台语歌手则邀请了许富凯与曹雅雯两位唱将，去年刚拿下金曲奖台语歌后的曹雅雯带来《思念的歌》，丰厚饱满的唱腔回荡全场，观众掌声如雷，两人随后更合唱《梦中的情话》、《甘有速配》，将气氛炒到最高点，宛如一场大型的歌舞秀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055811" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132032581487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10055811" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132032581487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="589" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055811" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》表演嘉宾曹雅雯（左）、许富凯（右）。（ 台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>成军20年的动力火车一连带来四首经典好歌，伴随动力火车的歌声，配上舞台的超大型LED屏幕，带大家一起穿越更多的明天，并创造万人齐唱的盛大场面，连红队主持人纳豆也说：“在后台跟着合唱都唱到沙哑、太感动了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033121487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10055812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033121487-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055812" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》嘉宾动力火车。（ 台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>即将要在四月举办演唱会的“宇宙人”，今年也首登红白，第一首就带来节奏十足的《你以为》，第二首则是致敬张惠妹的经典《我可以抱你吗》，并表示希望新的一年可以跟着大家一起探险，最后送给大家《我们的探险计划》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055813" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033071487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10055813" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033071487-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055813" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》嘉宾宇宙人。（ 台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>紧接在后的乐团“八三夭”的《我不想改变世界我只是不想被世界改变》，乐团的鼓声、让观众跟着节奏情绪更加沸腾，接着八三夭再演唱《眉飞色舞》，更让现场气氛飙到最高点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055814" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055814" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033181487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10055814" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132033181487-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055814" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》表演乐团八三么 。（ 台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/2018%E8%B6%85%E7%BA%A7%E5%B7%A8%E6%98%9F%E7%BA%A2%E7%99%BD%E8%89%BA%E8%83%BD%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%8F.md#1">2018超级巨星红白艺能大赏</a>》节目将于2月15日除夕夜在台视播出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10055824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10055824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132036261487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10055824" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1801132036261487-600x492.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="492" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10055824" class="wp-caption-text">《2018红白》表演嘉宾杨丽花（左）、陈亚兰（右）。（台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
