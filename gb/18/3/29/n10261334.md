<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>闲时做一坛梅子果酱 配这种茶味道最美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/guojiang2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黄梅衬上高山乌龙茶，茶香甘甜沉稳，解了黄梅甜腻。（幸福文化出版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>黄梅闻来有股令人惊喜的蜜桃香，富含大量果胶，做成果酱要失败也难，衬上高山乌龙茶，茶香甘甜沉稳，解了黄梅甜腻。清明前后，正是梅树结果累累，青梅渍蜜饯，黄梅熬果酱，有得忙了。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">茶渍五月梅果酱做法</span></h2>
<p><strong>食材：</strong>黄梅 600g、水 50g、乌龙茶叶 20g、细砂糖 400g、梅子醋 50g、海盐少许。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10261336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10261336" style="width: 471px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/2-45.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10261336 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/2-45-e1524347164648.jpg" alt="" width="471" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10261336" class="wp-caption-text">茶渍五月梅果酱食材：黄梅、乌龙茶叶、细砂糖、梅子醋、海盐。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 用流动的水漂洗黄梅，黄梅已熟透，不要过度浸在水中，也要避免用力拿捏，免得伤到果实。</p>
<p>2. 用牙签挑掉黄梅的果蒂，这是需要耐心的程序，但无法省略，挑掉果蒂能让口感更干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10261339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10261339" style="width: 325px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/3-29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10261339 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/3-29.jpg" alt="" width="325" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10261339" class="wp-caption-text">用牙签挑掉黄梅的果蒂。</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将黄梅用盐抓搓至果肉略软的程度。</p>
<p>4. 将带盐的黄梅入水后洗净，捞起彻底沥干。</p>
<p>5. 将黄梅放入蒸笼中蒸熟。</p>
<p>6. 将乌龙茶叶放入热水中，马上关火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10261341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10261341" style="width: 321px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/4-21.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10261341 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/4-21-e1524347476596.jpg" alt="" width="321" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10261341" class="wp-caption-text">乌龙茶叶放入热水。</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 用盘子盖住，焖出一杯浓茶。</p>
<p>8. 蒸熟后将黄梅放凉，挤出黄梅肉汁，除去果核与果皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10261343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10261343" style="width: 325px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/5-13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10261343 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/5-13.jpg" alt="" width="325" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10261343" class="wp-caption-text">挤黄梅肉汁。</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 将软烂的果肉放入锅中，倒入细砂糖，稍微搅拌一下。</p>
<p>10. 倒入梅子醋，再稍微搅拌。</p>
<p>11. 最后倒入乌龙浓茶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10261345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10261345" style="width: 325px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/6-15.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10261345 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/6-15.jpg" alt="" width="325" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10261345" class="wp-caption-text">倒入乌龙浓茶。</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 均匀搅拌后静置4小时糖渍。</p>
<p>13. 完成糖渍后，放到炉上，开中小火加热，煮到沸腾。沸腾后， 转中火续煮。</p>
<p>14. 仔细捞去浮沫与涩汁。</p>
<p>15. 不时的搅拌，以防止果酱在锅底烧焦。</p>
<p>16. 煮至浓稠状，用木匙挖取果酱，果酱滑落至锅中有滞重感即可趁热起锅。</p>
<p>17. 将装好的果酱倒放降温，放凉后就完成真空了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10261346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10261346" style="width: 325px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/7-14.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10261346 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/7-14.jpg" alt="" width="325" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10261346" class="wp-caption-text">果酱倒放降温。</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>果酱小帮手</strong></span></h2>
<p>黄梅独有的水蜜桃味，请好好保留下来，除了与茶搭配，让茶香带出黄梅的细致余韵，其实我都不太建议再搭配风味过强的食材，免得抢了风采。但黄梅倒是可与同个时期出道的李、桃一起登台，效果大好。@</p>
