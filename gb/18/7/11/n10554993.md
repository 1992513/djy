<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月13日讯】（大纪元记者陈安报导）许多重视养身的族群，总喜欢买一些按摩器具，其中有一样叫“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9%E6%9E%95.md#1">按摩枕</a>”，特点是有“按摩球”会在枕头里滚来滚去。这玩意儿，如果给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>用，会是什么样子呢？</p>
<p>南韩一名女网友在网路分享一段影片，片中<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>肯定是主人掌心上的一块宝，受到无微不至的照顾，躺在暖床要“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9%E6%9E%95.md#1">按摩枕</a>”伺候，无聊时还有主人“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%A5%E7%BA%B8.md#1">报纸</a>供应”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10556969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10556969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/a122dd3df0ae7b219b0bdaf61385c689.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10556969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/a122dd3df0ae7b219b0bdaf61385c689-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10556969" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗正在享受“按摩枕”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>身上盖着<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%A5%E7%BA%B8.md#1">报纸</a>正享受“按摩”的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%8C.md#1">萌</a>狗，水汪汪大眼睛一直看着镜头。随着按摩枕内部的“按摩滚球”不断地转呀转，狗狗的萌眼也跟着眨呀眨，头缓缓地晃来晃去。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…</strong></span></p>
<p>内心已默默为它的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%8C.md#1">萌</a>萌分数”打了一百分，真是萌到无以覆加啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10556972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10556972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/72971d9425119e5ac71146c4607ddb3b.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10556972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/72971d9425119e5ac71146c4607ddb3b-600x389.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10556972" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的眼睛眨呀眨。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>

<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“哇，好厉害，我家小狗会‘吃’这神奇的枕头。”“太舒服，狗狗几乎读不了报纸了。”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“哇，这太<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E7%88%B1.md#1">可爱</a>了！”“我需要这个枕头，我家的猫喜欢我给它按摩脖子、肩膀。”</p>
