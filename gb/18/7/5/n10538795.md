<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>惊险片！超近距离拍摄巨大“沙尘卷”</h2>
<a href="https://dylb6p9h9pzya.cloudfront.net/N7nTUDgji"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10560626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-8-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月14日讯】在美国亚利桑那州的高速公路上，出现了一阵相当怪异的旋风，卷起了漫天沙尘，还快速的旋转移动，看起来就像是个“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">龙卷风</a>”。</p>
<p>这名男子驾着车在高速公路上行驶时，发现了一个巨大的旋风，不过这个旋风并不是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">龙卷风</a>，而是“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%98%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">尘卷风</a>”，他好奇的停下车来用镜头捕捉了这惊人的一幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538851" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/11-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10538851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/11-3-600x501.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538851" class="wp-caption-text">当这名驾驶发现一个巨大的旋风时，他正在高速公路上行驶。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10538852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/22-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10538852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/22-2-600x502.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="502" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538852" class="wp-caption-text">这个旋风是“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%98%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">尘卷风</a>”，驾驶停下车来用镜头捕捉了这惊人的一幕。。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一开始尘卷风离他还有段距离，但它移动的速度之快，一下子就接近了车的后方，驾驶赶紧逃命，把车往前开了一段距离，才又再次拍摄这个巨大的尘卷风，而尘卷风也很快的移动到男子方才停车的地方，幸好他赶紧开车离开，否则后果可能不堪设想。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/33-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10538853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/33-1-600x506.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="506" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538853" class="wp-caption-text">这个旋风卷起了漫天沙尘，还快速的旋转移动，看起来就像是个“龙卷风”。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“尘卷风”和“龙卷风”是两种不同的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E6%B0%94%E7%8E%B0%E8%B1%A1.md#1">天气现象</a>。龙卷风（tornado）是出现在积雨云的底部，且破坏力大；尘卷风（dust devil）通常在空旷又炎热的地形和气候比较容易发生，杀伤力也比较小。</p>
<p>屏东气象站主任陈建明说：“地面受热之后，产生气流上升的现象，在空旷的地方，天气热的情况下，天气好的时候才会产生。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/55.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10538850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/55-600x498.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538850" class="wp-caption-text">一开始尘卷风离他还有段距离，但它移动的速度之快，一下子就接近了车的后方。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10538849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/44-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10538849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/44-1-600x504.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="504" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538849" class="wp-caption-text">尘卷风很快的移动到男子方才停车的地方，幸好他赶紧开车离开。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾也曾拍到类似的沙尘卷，如果下次遇到的话也别紧张，这类的沙尘卷很快就会消失了！</p>
