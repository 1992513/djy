<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>半颗酪梨、1颗蛋 教你做出高营养早餐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/shutterstock_246331183-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>只要半颗酪梨和一颗鸡蛋，就可以获得一份好吃的高营养早餐！(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>的营养满点，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%9B%8B.md#1">鸡蛋</a>也被营养学家称为“完全蛋白质模式”。只要半颗酪梨和一颗鸡蛋，就可以获得一份好吃又可爱的高营养<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A9%E9%A4%90.md#1">早餐</a>！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>抗氧化 对身体非常有益</span></h2>
<p>陈小薇营养师的贴心小叮咛：</p>
<p>酪梨富含植物性单元不饱和脂肪酸、蛋白质与维生素B群、C、E，因为营养丰富又具有强抗氧化力、免疫调节的效果，因此被归属在“超级食物”里。剖析酪梨的营养成分，内含的单元不饱和脂肪酸除了能为身体提供好的油脂来源之外，还能帮助细胞修复、抵御破坏营。尤其酪梨所含的脂溶性维生素E，是主要抗氧化剂之一，能增进皮肤与血球的健康。</p>
<p>但也要叮咛大家，每100公克绿皮酪梨含热量约72大卡、脂肪4.8公克左右，相当于是1茶匙（5公克）的油，而国人饮食指南指出，每人每日应摄取3～7茶匙的油脂，而酪梨是属于含脂肪量高的食材，因此，食用酪梨需要注意每日的饮食代换喔！每食用100公克酪梨，应减少1茶匙的油脂摄取！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">5步做<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8%E7%83%A4%E8%9B%8B.md#1">酪梨烤蛋</a></span></h2>
<p><strong>食材（两人份）：</strong>酪梨 1颗、蛋 2颗、培根适量、小蕃茄 1小把。</p>
<p><strong>调味料：</strong>岩盐适量、黑胡椒适量、意大利香料 1小匙。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src=""></a><a href="https://d14a0yccee2yym.cloudfront.net/L3akVBJuk"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/S0QV4aCAf18?wmode=transparent&#038;wmode=opaque" allowfullscreen></a>
	</div></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #008000;"><em>（完整视频）</em></span></p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 酪梨切对半，取出核，用汤匙把中心稍微挖深，挖到大约可以将蛋置入的空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10563318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10563318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.51.49-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10563318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.51.49-PM-600x336.png" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10563318" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A9%E9%A4%90.md#1">早餐</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8%E7%83%A4%E8%9B%8B.md#1">酪梨烤蛋</a>步骤之：酪梨去核。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 在酪梨凹陷里，一边一颗打入两颗蛋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10563319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10563319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.52.35-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10563319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.52.35-PM-600x336.png" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10563319" class="wp-caption-text">早餐酪梨烤蛋步骤之：在酪梨凹陷里打一颗蛋。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将酪梨与蛋放进烤箱，用160℃烤10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10563320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10563320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.53.03-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10563320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.53.03-PM-600x334.png" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10563320" class="wp-caption-text">早餐酪梨烤蛋步骤之：烤箱烘烤10分钟。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 在烤酪梨和蛋的时候，将培根切丁，煎至酥脆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10563321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10563321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.53.39-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10563321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.53.39-PM-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10563321" class="wp-caption-text">早餐酪梨烤蛋步骤之：煎培根丁。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 取出烤好的酪梨蛋，撒上小番茄和煎好的培根丁，再撒上意大利香料、胡椒与岩盐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10563322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10563322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.54.18-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10563322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screen-Shot-2018-07-14-at-6.54.18-PM-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10563322" class="wp-caption-text">早餐酪梨烤蛋步骤之：烤好的酪梨蛋洒上调味食材和调味料。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
