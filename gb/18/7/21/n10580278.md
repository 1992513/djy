<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>幸福怎能轻易放手？宝宝示范“坚守拥有”</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月22日讯】几个月大的小宝宝，被众星捧月般抱到了妈妈的肩头，她双腿夹住妈妈的脖颈，双手扶在妈妈的手上，舒服极了，她可以真切地感受到妈妈的柔情与温度。<br />
<span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-eac126ad-c146-4fb7-602b-ba60014f0455" data-id="player-eac126ad-c146-4fb7-602b-ba60014f0455"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/eac126ad-c146-4fb7-602b-ba60014f0455?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F21%2Fn10580278.md#1"></a></p>
<p>哇！宝宝从来没用这个视角看过这个世界，以前躺在床上只能仰视一切的她，瞬间登到了“世界之巅”。这种感觉真是美妙至极。宝宝开怀大笑，妈妈也因为能给予宝宝如此的快乐而非常开心。</p>
<p>给宝宝短暂的体验之后，这些“天真的”的大人们想把她抱下来，他们以为会像抱上去一样轻松。然而，他们都错了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10580287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10580287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/a-19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10580287 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/a-19-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10580287" class="wp-caption-text">妈妈的头发被抓得揪心地疼痛。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>即便是几个月大的宝宝，也不肯轻松放弃已经拥有的幸福。</p>
<p>家人抱住宝宝的胳膊，宝宝就用小腿紧紧钩住妈妈的脖子，试图松开她用力的双腿，宝宝就用手猛抓妈妈的头发。</p>
<p>几番努力，几番挣扎，妈妈开始还觉得很有趣，笑了几声之后，开始变调，她几乎要窒息，头发也被抓得揪心疼痛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10580291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10580291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/b-15-600x391.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10580291 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/b-15-600x391.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10580291" class="wp-caption-text">上去容易下来难。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宝宝对于到手的幸福是如此的坚守，我们还有什么理由在困难面前退缩……</p>
