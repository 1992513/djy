<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>她自幼未曾剪发 当带着3个女儿出门 更惊艳！</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月10日讯】（大纪元记者许彦综合报导）很多人都知道德国童话《长发公主》（Rapunzel）的故事，那么现实社会中有长发“公主”吗？<br />
<strong><span style="color: #008000;">看影片完整版&gt;&gt; </span></strong><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-be30f40f-cf8b-4929-7e22-ca112a9dd384" data-id="player-be30f40f-cf8b-4929-7e22-ca112a9dd384"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/be30f40f-cf8b-4929-7e22-ca112a9dd384?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F9%2Fn10549597.md#1"></a></p>
<p>童话中，公主的头发长到了令人难以置信的长度，且头发的长度和强度让她的监护人和王子可以毫无问题地在长发上攀爬。人们都知道这仅仅是一个美丽的童话故事，而一名美国妈妈和她的三个女儿却堪称现实中的长发公主；令人惊艳的是，一家就有四位！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10549611" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10549611" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Johnny_Gruelle_illustration_-_Rapunzel_-_Project_Gutenberg_etext_11027.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10549611 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Johnny_Gruelle_illustration_-_Rapunzel_-_Project_Gutenberg_etext_11027-450x611.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="611" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10549611" class="wp-caption-text">童话中，公主的头发长到了令人难以置信的长度。（公有领域）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗素（Tere Lynn Svetlecich Russell）今年43岁，是五个孩子的妈妈。她的家在美国伊利诺斯州很出名，因为她和三个女儿堪称真人版“长发公主”，甚至因为长发赢得了绶带。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10549612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10549612" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/mp37617223_1445828394286_4-copy.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10549612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/mp37617223_1445828394286_4-copy-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10549612" class="wp-caption-text">罗素女士和她的女儿是真人版的长发公主。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗素本人自婴幼儿时期就没有剪过头发，如今头发长达188厘米。大女琳恩（Lynn）和二女儿森达琳（Cendalyn）的头发都有90多厘米，最小的女儿切斯妮（Chesney）的头发则有66厘米长。</p>
<p>每次母女四人出门时，都赢得很高的回头率。当她们四人一起回头，长发飘逸之际，更是令路人感到惊艳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10549613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10549613" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/mp37617223_1445828394286_5-copy.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10549613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/mp37617223_1445828394286_5-copy-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10549613" class="wp-caption-text">长发母女们去理发店，对理发师来说也是挑战。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，打理长长的秀发并不是一件容易的事。妈妈和每个女儿洗一次头发，每人要用掉四分之一瓶护发素。罗素表示，每次女儿洗头发，自己都要站在旁边帮她们挤护发素；而当自己洗头发时都需要保持跪下来的姿势，不然的话，浸水的长发重量会让她抬不起头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10549608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10549608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/ussuperlonghair1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10549608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/ussuperlonghair1-600x428.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10549608" class="wp-caption-text">打理长长的秀发并不是一件容易的事。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>拥有飘逸的长发固然美丽，但在日常生活中也会带来不少麻烦，如关门不小心就会夹住长发，吸尘器有时也会夹住头发，孩子们在玩的时候也会踩上头发等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10549614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10549614" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/mp37617223_1445828394286_6-copy.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10549614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/mp37617223_1445828394286_6-copy-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10549614" class="wp-caption-text">罗素女士自己留长发的起因是丈夫切普。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗素自己留长发的起因是丈夫切普（Chip），当时还是男友的切普因她的长发而邀她去约会。但她表示，自己不会强迫女儿们一辈子都要留着长发，她们长大后就自己决定是否要把长发剪短。</p>
<p>看了现实版长发公主视频的女孩们，是不是也想尝试留长发呢？</p>
