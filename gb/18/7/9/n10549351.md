<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美职棒大联盟明星赛最受欢迎球星──阿土伯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-951216436-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>明星赛最受欢迎球员阿土伯（Jose Altuve）(Photo by Bob Levey/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月10日讯】（大纪元记者宋昇桦报导）美国职棒大联盟（MLB）明星赛即将在7月17日开打，球迷票选最受欢迎的球员是太空人队的阿土伯<ahref="http://m.mlb.com/player/514888/jose-altuve">Jose Altuve</a>），得票数高达4,849,630，第二名则是红袜队的贝兹（ <ahref="http://m.mlb.com/player/605141/mookie-betts">Mookie Betts</a>）4,286,994票，国联最受欢迎的球员则是勇士队的弗里曼<ahref="http://m.mlb.com/player/518692/freddie-freeman">Freddie Freeman</a>），得票数4,039,219。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10479279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10479279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-957830004.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-10479279 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-957830004-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10479279" class="wp-caption-text">勇士队一垒手Freddie Freeman。(Photo by Mark Brown/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>美联今年明星赛先发选手除了捕手（光芒队<ahref="http://m.mlb.com/player/467092/wilson-ramos">Wilson Ramos</a>）、一垒手（白袜队<ahref="http://m.mlb.com/player/547989/jose-abreu">Jose Abreu</a>）和游击手（金莺队<ahref="http://m.mlb.com/player/592518/manny-machado">Manny Machado</a>）不同，其他大致都跟去年一样。值得关注的是洋基超级新秀二垒手<ahref="http://m.mlb.com/player/650402/gleyber-torres">Gleyber Torres</a>，今年刚加入职棒第一年就首度入围美联明星队。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10479278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10479278" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-966130550.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-10479278 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-966130550-450x572.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="572" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10479278" class="wp-caption-text">洋基二垒手Gleyber Torres。(Photo by Al Bello/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>国联方面今年明星赛先发选手跟去年都不太一样，只有三垒手（洛矶队Nolan Arenadoc）和外野手哈波（国民队Bryce Harper）一样外，其余都换人。而且很多都是今年首度才进明星赛：小熊队二垒手Javier Báez，小熊队捕手Willson Contreras和勇士队的Nick Markakis。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10549389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10549389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-681678662.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10549389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-681678662-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10549389" class="wp-caption-text">国民队外野手哈波。(Photo by Matt Hazlett/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
