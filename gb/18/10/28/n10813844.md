<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>高惠芬师生画展  27日起斗六文化处展出</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3a1143aae0dfa627e3faff2e4e9bd18e-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“怡然自得－高惠芬师生画展”高惠芬老师（中）与6位参展学员合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）“怡然自得－高惠芬师生画展”10月27日至11月7日在云林县政府文化处一楼展览馆， 展出画家高惠芬与开朗艺术中心6位学员，近期创作的水墨、水彩、油画、彩绘瓷器等近60幅作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10813849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10813849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/048486133002ae9cd144bda94bdcc18d.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10813849 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/048486133002ae9cd144bda94bdcc18d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10813849" class="wp-caption-text">高惠芬老师精美高雅的彩绘陶瓷。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10813848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10813848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/d3df9d24573c32d2841b8372136caa59.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10813848 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/d3df9d24573c32d2841b8372136caa59-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10813848" class="wp-caption-text">高惠芬老师的彩绘陶瓷作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/62f495ed2331e9df7190518a13ea8c99.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-10813850 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/62f495ed2331e9df7190518a13ea8c99-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a></p>
<p>高惠芬老师的水墨作品。（高惠芬提供）</p>
<p>28日上午举行开幕茶会，现场贵宾云集，高朋满座，县府秘书长黄玉霜、文化处长林孟仪、斗六市长谢淑亚及来自台中、嘉义、台南及县内艺文界、教育界人士二百多人莅临观赏！看了让人心旷神怡、美不胜收的作品！令现场来宾赞赏不已！</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/a5a8b7b420444164ff18c1647b2e4a39.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-10813854 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/a5a8b7b420444164ff18c1647b2e4a39-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a></p>
<p>高惠芬老师的作品。（高惠芬提供）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/675626740f02fb31f0140bc7acbe4d91.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-10813852 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/675626740f02fb31f0140bc7acbe4d91-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a></p>
<p>高惠芬老师的作品（高惠芬提供）</p>
<p>黄玉霜表示，高惠芬老师为云林知名女性画家，台湾师范大学美术系毕业后，从事艺术创作四十多年，在国内举办无数次的个展与联展，与县内艺术家们创立上上画会，并积极参与县内艺文团体活动，曾担任云林县青溪新文艺学会及云林县浊水溪书画学会理事长等职，对于本县艺文推动及教学不遗余力；在个人艺术成就上，作品曾多次荣获海、内外各类美术奖项等殊荣，是台湾女性艺术家中有杰出表现与贡献者，是“云林之光”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10813847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10813847" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/0871366586e08068c4c9a37c2b2981e0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10813847 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/0871366586e08068c4c9a37c2b2981e0-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10813847" class="wp-caption-text">开幕茶会高惠芬老师（右五）和与会来宾。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10813853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10813853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8d2c8b0036001a94e6b3a7a4967cc606.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10813853 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8d2c8b0036001a94e6b3a7a4967cc606-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10813853" class="wp-caption-text">高惠芬老师（右二）和与会来宾。 （廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>文化处长林孟仪表示，高惠芬老师常年在台湾、美国及加拿大等地开设画室指导艺术后进，让东、西方艺术彼此交融；这次画展高老师带领着斗六“开朗艺术中心”的6位学员，包含纪雯馨、林谨、郭慧贞、郑春玫、巫素华、颜秀英等，分水墨组与西画组展出近60件作品，作品丰富多元、细腻或粗旷，有水墨的优雅与西画的写实，欢迎喜爱艺术创作的民众及县内美术班学子们，踊跃前往观赏！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10813857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10813857" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/c0c2d3b20e1925adea3ac7235148dd12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10813857 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/c0c2d3b20e1925adea3ac7235148dd12-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10813857" class="wp-caption-text">开幕茶会，琳琅满目的贺词形成另一种风景。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10813855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10813855" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1170cfd5a6b74efae2c84ae2786ccdef.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10813855 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1170cfd5a6b74efae2c84ae2786ccdef-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10813855" class="wp-caption-text">参展的学生作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高惠芬表示，“艺术工作是她的兴趣，传承是她的责任”，从事美术创作40多年的她，领悟到创作应该是件很愉悦的事，因此她现阶段指导学生作画时，题材不拘、素材不拘、学生可以选择自己喜欢的题材尽情的发挥，她认为“艺术不是只描写外在的型态、必须有内在的深层情感”因此在指导学生作画时，会告诉学生技巧固然重要，但怎样把情感融入画中更重要！“要入画”画出来的画，才能吸引人！才会感动人！因此这次画展特别以“怡然自得”为主题，希望每幅画都能带给观众愉悦的心情！</p>
<p>跟随高惠芬老师习画多年的学员郑春玫、巫素华、严秀英表示，老师教学很认真，可以一语道破学员的问题在哪里？因此她们学起来进步神速，老师也会针对学生的不足个别指导，也会告诉学员适合哪种画风！而且有问必答，你想学什么她就可以教你什么！</p>
