<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>针对公园市场农工商业 建设处施政展活力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/f517ba20da43301a993cc466f577bb69-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>建设处施政成果展示。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月11日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>报导）嘉义市政府<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%BA%E8%AE%BE%E5%A4%84.md#1">建设处</a>管理民生有关的事务，连流浪犬猫也要费心，在市长涂醒哲的领导下一切都能上轨道，在稳定中求进步，由各式的统计图不难看出进3年来的施政成果，11日的记者会中再展辉煌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776597" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/9aa6794811a74684c26be1c349e6c355.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/9aa6794811a74684c26be1c349e6c355-450x542.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="542" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776597" class="wp-caption-text">新政府拼经济，成果看得见。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公园展活力、市场造新象、农业显特色、工商创商机在在都是时刻不放松的工作。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%BA%E8%AE%BE%E5%A4%84.md#1">建设处</a>长李国荣也在辛苦中得到很多宝贵的管理经验，在涂醒哲的率领下建设处积极将100公顷公园绿地朝特色化发展，举办动保宣导活动，提高宠物晶片施打率，首创“食品安全中心”，同时招商引资并致力于店家及产业的创生及辅导，鼓励青年创业并提供青年创业贷款补助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776598" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/07a23127ad5d051e37e1ae970e415b76.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/07a23127ad5d051e37e1ae970e415b76-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776598" class="wp-caption-text">商业及公司登记资本额近4年逐年增加。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/c718df77e210f3f47d986924366dd252.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/c718df77e210f3f47d986924366dd252-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776600" class="wp-caption-text">公园电费106年PK 103年省电37%。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>商业及公司登记资本额近4年逐年增加。（记者李撷璎／摄影）公园电费106年PK 103年省电37%。（记者李撷璎／摄影）</p>
<p>充满人情味，有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>市“灶咖”美称的东公有市场启动改造，第一步办理路面、水沟及通风设备整修工程，彻底改善市场环境，加强通风与汰换老旧管线提升水质。继之启动摊位暂停转让公告，以缓和逐步清理空摊，以提高未来空间规划的幅度。更进一步清理公共区域杂物、全面性处理旧建物漏水问题。最后型塑市场图腾找寻品牌故事，朝观光市场迈进。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776601" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/fc1309764e1db74f91343748b3236d9e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/fc1309764e1db74f91343748b3236d9e-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776601" class="wp-caption-text">市场用地租金收入统计逐年成长。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776602" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b29b9011935f22921df2186339466251.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b29b9011935f22921df2186339466251-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776602" class="wp-caption-text">启动东市场改造。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>规划新建动物收容所，预计108年3月完工，目前进度超前，收容所设计具人道关怀、动物需求等理念，并兼具环保、永续经营特色，获国家卓越建设奖规划设计类优质奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/7d53b110d9f079259cd4888e7319053c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/7d53b110d9f079259cd4888e7319053c-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776603" class="wp-caption-text">学校午餐食材农药残留抽验合格件数上升。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为活化农地，市府亦致力于推动“农村体验活动”，以农村社区为发展主轴，迎合民众偏好绿色旅游活动的趋势，更满足都市社会对自然田园生活的向往，同时也为农村创造商机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776604" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8ed2d1918bd444692c8ad5178e9b4dd4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8ed2d1918bd444692c8ad5178e9b4dd4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776604" class="wp-caption-text">通盘检讨嘉义市山坡地。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776605" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8b1dabb2701a0eba0ce8395c89387238.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8b1dabb2701a0eba0ce8395c89387238-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776605" class="wp-caption-text">动物收容处所新建工程。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为保障学童午餐安全，嘉义市105年首创“食品安全中心”，由建设处、教育处、卫生局等组成联合稽查小组，针对本市国中小学生的校园午餐源头供应商抽验食材。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776607" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/57ffb7a80f7ddc70fcc7da64275b9c89.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/57ffb7a80f7ddc70fcc7da64275b9c89-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776607" class="wp-caption-text">本市为观光型消费城市，营利事业销售额易受景气循环影响。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776610" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4c6fcebee9d0589f22b93b70b2bf985b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4c6fcebee9d0589f22b93b70b2bf985b-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776610" class="wp-caption-text">东京国际食品展强棒出击。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近年，全球最大的运动用品零售商，法商迪卡侬，及全球第9大的服饰连锁业， 日商思梦乐流行服饰馆，分别看上嘉义市的商机，于2017年分别前后进驻，台湾的集团业者包括福泰桔子商旅、宽悦花园酒店、嘉楠风华酒店也纷纷进驻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776611" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776611" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2700775a308785fd3ae567df17063f33.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2700775a308785fd3ae567df17063f33-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776611" class="wp-caption-text">营利事业家数103－106年成长1,052家，表示商机无限。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10776613" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/184f25ff8c8cf7052e8827e29b7365b2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/184f25ff8c8cf7052e8827e29b7365b2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10776613" class="wp-caption-text">设计翻转，地方创生。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后店家及产业的创生辅导方面，翻转在地的老行业，让第二代也能传承，产业升级SBIR，藉由经济部经费补助，协助在地产业，除了鼓励青年创业，并提供青年创业贷款补助。106－107年涂醒哲率在地小吃及厂商挺进东京参展，2年来争取40万美元的现场订单，预估将带来3百万美元的后续效益。◇</p>
