<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>刘谦魔术再现？华人小伙吹口气“见证奇迹”</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年10月05日讯】（大纪元记者林海柔报导）“见证奇迹的时刻到了！”这是世界知名魔术师刘谦的招牌话术。他独特的魔术表演方式，鲜少有人能够成功模仿，而来自中国沈阳、现居美国加州的网友于洋（Yang Yu），却能完美演出刘谦做过的神奇表演，一模一样的桥段，太不可思议了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10760706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10760706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10760706 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/8-1-600x367.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10760706" class="wp-caption-text">魔术师吹口气，手中绳子瞬间改变了。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/100006477860143/videos/vb.100006477860143/2614898825402687/?type=2&amp;video_source=user_video_tab" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">于洋／Facebook 视频截图</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>于洋在脸书分享了他在湾区表演绳子魔术的片段。影片中可见站在身旁的活动主持人看得惊奇，现场观众惊呼声连连。这个奇特魔术，刘谦曾经表演过。魔术师吹口气，就能直接将绳子变变变，是怎么做到的呢？</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?</span></strong><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-a71a9d43-e27f-4655-7fbb-9798faee9718" data-id="player-a71a9d43-e27f-4655-7fbb-9798faee9718"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/a71a9d43-e27f-4655-7fbb-9798faee9718?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F10%2F4%2Fn10760620.md#1"></a></p>
<p>于洋在户外活动中由节目主持人介绍出场。他拿了2条绳子，一长一短，先是请主持人握住绳子用力拉一下，确认这2条只是一般普通的绳子。接着魔术表演开始了。</p>
<p>于洋首先要将2条不同长度的绳子变成相同的长度。只见他大略将绳子对折再对折后，握在手中吹口气：“呼！”魔术师举高双手，原本2条一长一短的绳子变成了2条一样长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10760725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10760725" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2-1-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10760725 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2-1-1-600x362.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10760725" class="wp-caption-text">魔术师对着2条不同长度的绳子吹口气，绳子变成一样长了。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/100006477860143/videos/vb.100006477860143/2614898825402687/?type=2&amp;video_source=user_video_tab" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">于洋／Facebook 视频截图</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第2步，于洋要将手中2条绳子变成一条长绳子。他拿着手中的绳子分别吹2口气，并末端打结，看起来绳子似乎连成一条，不过绳子中间还有个结呢，魔术尚未结束。</p>
<p>这时魔术师在绳结位置弹了一下手指，再吹口气，神奇的一幕发生了，绳结居然能够移动，于洋顺势将绳结下滑在地上，原本绑在一起的2条绳子，变成一条直溜溜的长绳子了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10760735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10760735" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3-1-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10760735 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3-1-1-600x368.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10760735" class="wp-caption-text">绳结居然瞬间变成活动套环。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/100006477860143/videos/vb.100006477860143/2614898825402687/?type=2&amp;video_source=user_video_tab" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">于洋／Facebook 视频截图</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第3步，于洋要将手中长绳子，变出一个圆圈绳。他将长长的绳子打了一个松结，看似一个圆圈在绳子中间，这时还不算是真的圆圈。</p>
<p>魔术师对着绳子吹口气“呼！”然后慢慢拿着绳子中间的松结往绳子末端移动，当松结滑出绳子后竟然变成了一条直绳和另一个圆圈绳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10760784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10760784" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/5-1-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10760784 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/5-1-1-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10760784" class="wp-caption-text">直绳打结后吹口气，就变出一个圆圈绳。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/100006477860143/videos/vb.100006477860143/2614898825402687/?type=2&amp;video_source=user_video_tab" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">于洋／Facebook 视频截图</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第4步，魔术师最后要将直绳和圆圈绳合而为一，复原成一条长长的直绳。只见他将圆圈绳挂在直绳上，然后吹口气，再慢慢将绳子往二边拉开，这时，圆圈绳看似成了直绳的一个松结，最后全拉开后变成一条长长直绳了。</p>
<p>现场主持人表示，站得很靠近也没看清是如何变的，太神奇了！于洋变魔术擅长和观众面对面近距离表演，期待他更多的魔术分享。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10760794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10760794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/7-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10760794 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/7-1-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10760794" class="wp-caption-text">魔术师将直绳和圆圈绳合为一条长绳。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/100006477860143/videos/vb.100006477860143/2614898825402687/?type=2&amp;video_source=user_video_tab" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">于洋／Facebook 视频截图</a>）</figcaption></figure>
