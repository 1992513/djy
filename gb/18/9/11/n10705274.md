<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自己动手做 空玻璃罐的七个小妙用</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-10.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-10603171 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-10-450x58.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="58"></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月27日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400">家中是否有用完的空玻璃罐呢？先别急着回收。玻璃罐不仅是最健康又稳定的储物容器，只要再加上一点小巧思，就可以为你的居家布置大大加值喔！以下是七个空玻璃罐的小妙用，环保又省钱，让你轻松营造个人化又富有创意的收纳空间。</span>
</p>
<h2>1.超省空间的悬吊收纳罐</h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">材料和工具：玻璃罐、马口铁盖、螺丝、电钻</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤一：寻找合适的悬吊橱柜、书柜或架子，将玻璃罐的马口铁盖摆放在架子层板的下缘。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤二：用螺丝和电钻将马口铁盖拴紧在层板的下面，使得盖子的正面紧贴于层板下缘。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤三：将物品装进玻璃罐内，对着层板下的马口铁盖轻松旋上，即完成了超简单又省空间的悬吊收纳罐。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10705389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10705389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/19f947dee84d99b94f51c275dea5be7e.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10705389 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/19f947dee84d99b94f51c275dea5be7e-600x458.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="458"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10705389" class="wp-caption-text">超省空间的悬吊收纳罐，轻松收纳小东西。 （影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff">观看完整影片（video credit: Make It Easy）?</span></strong>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">2.</span><span style="font-weight: 400">精巧手作小盆栽</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">材料和工具：玻璃罐、土壤、鹅卵石、小植栽</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤一：拿取一个玻璃罐，以土壤铺满底层约</span><span style="font-weight: 400">1/3</span><span style="font-weight: 400">的部分。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤二：以鹅卵石将剩下的罐内空间填满。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤三：将小植栽放置在鹅卵石顶端，浇上一点水，即完成精巧的手作小盆栽。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10705392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10705392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6b979d2d5509c77e8cf4cac68532bbda.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10705392 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6b979d2d5509c77e8cf4cac68532bbda-600x591.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="591"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10705392" class="wp-caption-text">精巧手作小盆栽。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">?3.</span><span style="font-weight: 400">二合一的针插针线盒</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">材料和工具：玻璃罐、马口铁盖、一小块棉布、聚酯纤维布、棉花团、剪刀、热熔胶、笔</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤一：将马口铁盖放在棉布上，用笔沿着盖子边缘作记号，将棉布剪成盖子的大小。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤二：拿取比盖子稍大的棉花团，以剪完的棉布压着，再放到聚酯纤维布上。将聚酯纤维布剪成比棉花团半径稍大约</span><span style="font-weight: 400">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400">至</span><span style="font-weight: 400">1.5</span><span style="font-weight: 400">公分的圆形。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤三：使用热熔胶将聚酯纤维布黏到棉布上，将棉花团包在里面。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤四：将棉布和聚酯纤维布内侧涂上热熔胶，黏在马口铁盖的正面上，完成针插的部分。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤五：剪一块长条的棉布，用热熔胶将其黏在马口铁盖侧边，盖住聚酯纤维布和盖子之间的接缝。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤六：将玻璃罐内装进针线用品，盖上含有针插的马口铁盖，即完成了二合一的针插针线盒。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10705395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10705395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/64d6b4d04fc608c76bcd95dc0ab41928.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10705395 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/64d6b4d04fc608c76bcd95dc0ab41928-600x468.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="468"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10705395" class="wp-caption-text">二合一的针插针线盒。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">4.安全方便火柴盒</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">材料和工具：玻璃罐、马口铁盖、砂纸、黏胶</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤一：剪一小块砂纸，使用黏胶将其黏在马口铁盖的内侧。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤二：将火柴棒放入玻璃罐内，盖上马口铁盖，即完成了安全方便的火柴盒。使用时只要打开罐盖，取出火柴棒，摩擦盖子内侧的的砂纸便能轻松点燃火柴。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10705396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10705396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/18c56cc2c66787504d17194e4cda8b50.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10705396 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/18c56cc2c66787504d17194e4cda8b50-600x553.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="553"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10705396" class="wp-caption-text">安全方便火柴盒。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>5.手作浪漫烛台</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">材料和工具：玻璃罐、胶带、喷雾胶、亮粉、小蜡烛</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤一：将胶带贴满玻璃罐外侧，只留出一条空白的带状区域。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤二：使用喷雾胶喷满没有贴胶带的区域，洒上亮粉。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤三：等胶干了之后，抖掉多余的亮粉并拆掉胶带。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤四：放入蜡烛后，便完成了手作又简易的浪漫烛台。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10705397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10705397" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/23440d2f77bc75c5302421c55c1fb37b.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10705397 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/23440d2f77bc75c5302421c55c1fb37b-600x556.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="556"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10705397" class="wp-caption-text">手作浪漫烛台。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">6.个人化多功能笔筒</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">材料和工具：玻璃罐、马口铁盖、油漆或油性涂料、奇异笔</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤一：将油漆或油性涂料滴入玻璃罐内，盖上盖子后用力摇晃。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤二：将盖子打开，放干至少两个小时。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤三：使用奇异笔在罐身外侧画上可爱插画，即完成了个人化的多功能笔筒。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10705404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10705404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/575534fcc0a5c9c7e6faf0e45a7ac8c6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10705404 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/575534fcc0a5c9c7e6faf0e45a7ac8c6-600x548.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="548"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10705404" class="wp-caption-text">个人化多功能笔筒。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">7.缤纷糖果收纳罐</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">材料和工具：玻璃罐、马口铁盖、油漆或油性涂料、彩色细绳</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤一：使用油漆或油性涂料将马口铁盖变换为不同颜色。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤二：将糖果放入玻璃罐内，盖上马口铁盖。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">步骤三：在瓶口系上彩色细绳，即能轻松完成缤纷糖果收纳罐，以彩色可口的糖果装点家中的小角落。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10705405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10705405" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f2650765d68c89e9f54341bbba892c33.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10705405 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f2650765d68c89e9f54341bbba892c33-600x544.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="544"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10705405" class="wp-caption-text">缤纷糖果收纳罐。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
