<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>乃木坂46及吉冈里帆等明星齐聚GirlsAward</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062004100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>乃木坂46成员白石麻衣于GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月18日讯】日本大型时尚周活动“Rakuten GirlsAward 2018 AUTUMN/WINTER”9月16日于千叶幕张展开，邀请到生驹里奈、多位乃木坂46成员及吉冈里帆、山下智久、MOMOLAND和NCT127等各领域艺人共襄盛举。延续今年5月活动的高人气，本次活动有超过三万名粉丝参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062019100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723289" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062019100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723289" class="wp-caption-text">人气女星吉冈里帆于GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10723284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062040100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723284" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062040100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723284" class="wp-caption-text">生驹里奈（右）与新川优爱（左）参与GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场从2010年开始每年固定都会举办两届的时尚活动，今年迎来了第18届。活动结合了时尚以及音乐，期望带给到场的观众朋友们最新的流行服饰以及时下最红的流行音乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062034100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723315" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062034100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723315" class="wp-caption-text">乃木坂46成员西野七濑参与GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10723314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723314" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062009100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723314" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062009100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723314" class="wp-caption-text">AKB48成员峯岸南于GirlsAward 2018 A/W以新娘礼服登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该活动分为春夏（SPRING/SUMMER）以及秋冬（AUTUMN/WINTER），就在上半年5月19日的春夏结束后，今天秋冬场也是GirlsAward第三次办在幕张展览馆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723317" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062043100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723317" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062043100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723317" class="wp-caption-text">乃木坂46队长樱井玲香于GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动也邀来了各领域的知名嘉宾。主持人由夏菜、东方收音机成员藤森慎吾、ゆりやんレトリィバァ以及日本电视台主播笹崎里菜担任，并邀来一百多名日本一线的模特儿和偶像歌手同台演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062006100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723316" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062006100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723316" class="wp-caption-text">模特儿饭丰万理江于GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的特别来宾，包括了超人气演员吉冈里帆及杰尼斯艺人山下智久。表演嘉宾除了日本高人气女团乃木坂46及榉坂46以外，还有以“出道15年的最强新人”铃木爱理、韩国偶像团体MOMOLAND、NCT127等艺人同台演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723292" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062023100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723292" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062023100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723292" class="wp-caption-text">前“℃-ute”成员铃木爱理参与GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在模特儿方面，则由玉城Tina、新川优爱、八木亚里沙等超过百名模特儿登台，为现场观众展示时下最流行的时尚趋势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062037100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723282" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062037100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723282" class="wp-caption-text">新川优爱于GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次的GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动还与日本电影合作，其中包括主演《响-HIBIKI-》的榉坂46成员平手友梨奈，以及主演日本版《那些年，我们一起追的女孩》（原文：あの顷、君を追いかけた）的山田裕贵及乃木坂46成员斋藤飞鸟也都受邀登台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723272" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062031100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723272" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062031100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723272" class="wp-caption-text">山田裕贵（左二）及乃木坂46斋藤飞鸟（左三）于GirlsAward 2018 A/W登场。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这天活动的最后，也预告了下一次的“Rakuten GirlsAward 2019 SPRING/SUMMER”活动，将会在2019年5月18日于相同场地“幕张展览馆”举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062016100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723329" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062016100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723329" class="wp-caption-text">吉本坂46于GirlsAward 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10723323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062013100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10723323" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180918062013100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723323" class="wp-caption-text">“GirlsAward 2018 A/W”9月16日于千叶幕张成功落幕。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
