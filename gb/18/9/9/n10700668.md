<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>清风水塘中天鹅挂鱼钩苦不堪言 暖男巧施救援</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月10日讯】（大纪元记者伊遐报导）风和日丽的一天，水面上有只天鹅，微风拂面……等等，这可不是想像中的优美画面。这只傻鹅被鱼钩钩住了，停驻在岸边，一副行动不便的样子。长脖子蜷缩着，有点奇怪，一定很难受！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10704658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10704658" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/swan-3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10704658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/swan-3.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10704658" class="wp-caption-text">这只白天鹅的长脖子蜷缩着，好像很难受。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>好心的路人向动物管制中心呼救后，留着长马尾的这位先生前来观看究竟。他试着想要对天鹅来个手到擒来……对不起，天鹅可是水鸟啊！虽然受了伤，可是敏捷度还在。它马上就流利地溜掉了，在不远的水面悠转……把这位先生弄得又好气又无奈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10704655" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10704655" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Swan-2-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10704655" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Swan-2-1.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10704655" class="wp-caption-text">人鹅相对，来个远距离望、闻、切、问，到底问题出在哪儿？（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>观察、思忖片刻之后，他比了个手势，意思是不只翅膀，天鹅的喙也被钩住了。他决定回车上拿他的法宝——一个超大号的网子，外加长长的手柄。他慢慢下坡，到最靠近天鹅的立脚点，准备撒网。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10704656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10704656" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Swan-3-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10704656" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Swan-3-1.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="482" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10704656" class="wp-caption-text">祭出我的法宝?——超大扑鹅网！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>天鹅好像也知道他要干嘛，正要滑开呢，说时迟，那时快，眼睛都来不急眨，那网已经凌空抛出，果真把天鹅网在里面啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10704657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10704657" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Swan-4-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10704657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Swan-4-1.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10704657" class="wp-caption-text">天鹅：“我最讨厌看牙医！”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>岸上拍摄视频的人激动地大叫：“太棒了！你真是太棒了！”</p>
<p>马尾先生连鹅带网抱到岸上，掏出折叠刀进行小小的解套手术。 先把钩住羽毛的网子部分剪断，再轻轻地把喙扭开，清除鱼钩残片，下一步得清理翅膀上还钩着的鱼漂。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-727cea8f-834d-41ac-7396-fa23a8e14b15" data-id="player-727cea8f-834d-41ac-7396-fa23a8e14b15"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/727cea8f-834d-41ac-7396-fa23a8e14b15?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F9%2F9%2Fn10700668.md#1"></a></p>
<p>这时天鹅好像意会了这人是来帮它的，所以垂着脖子没有咬人。把鱼漂剪开后，可能还是痛了点，天鹅扑腾了一番。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10704834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10704834" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/swan-5-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10704834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/swan-5-1.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="489" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10704834" class="wp-caption-text">别担心，一定会有办法的！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>检视之后，确定没有任何“挂勾”了，就准备放生。救援先生再度抱起细长脖子的天鹅，把它放到岸上的草丛中。它踉跄了几步，并没有兴冲冲奔回水里，还在树影中磨蹭。</p>
<p>救援人笑说它还惊魂甫定，就走过去在天鹅尾翼上鼓励性地拍拍。这下天鹅回过神来了，咕咚下水去也，重拾它优雅的风范，渐行渐远……</p>
<p>马尾先生心满意足地拍着手掌，一方面是掸去尘土，一方面在含蓄地延长着心中的关怀与快意。目送天鹅远去之后，他一面笑，一面爬上岸来，又是一个助人为快乐之本的一天！</p>
