<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>情侣海上划独木舟 好奇宝宝跃上船 萌态全录</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月04日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）划独木舟是一项既悠闲、又能体验大自然的活动，从较低的视角看水面，令人不禁感到水域浩大。此时，若有一庞然大物跑到船上，更是令人乐翻，感叹大自然充满惊奇。</p>
<h2>情侣在纽西兰划小船遇惊奇</h2>
<p>来自英国的22岁女子布里特．乔治亚（Brit Georgia）与来自比利时的25岁男友马丁（Martin）在纽西兰亚伯塔斯曼国家公园阿黛尔岛（Adele Island）附近的海洋中划独木舟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10688569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10688569" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ea2f158ea5a29ee628a1f5168e00712f.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10688569 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ea2f158ea5a29ee628a1f5168e00712f-450x527.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10688569" class="wp-caption-text">布里特与男友马丁合照。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们划着划着，突有一只小海豹跃上船。小家伙似乎很好奇这海上漂浮物到底是什么东西，不断睁着水汪汪大眼东张西望。</p>
<p>海豹似乎不怎么害怕，好像到了一处秘境，心想：“咦，这是什么地方？前面这两家伙到底是……？”萌萌景象就这样被记录了下来。</p>

<figure id="attachment_10688571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10688571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ab3dc1a97f059d1fb81992fe57bf8cd9.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10688571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ab3dc1a97f059d1fb81992fe57bf8cd9-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10688571" class="wp-caption-text">海豹心中充满好奇的模样。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>海豹可能会咬人？</h2>
<p>画面虽然可爱，船上的人一开始可不一定这么觉得，布里特起初怕怕的，因当地人警告：“在那地方的海豹可能会咬人。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10688577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10688577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d60ddf3c32a8527a199fce7d241710db.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10688577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d60ddf3c32a8527a199fce7d241710db-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10688577" class="wp-caption-text">小海豹东张西望。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所以，大家可在影片中听到布里特说：“不要咬我。”不过，可能是这海豹模样实在太可爱，似乎没什么攻击性，单纯只是一只“好奇宝宝”，因而布里特的语气并没有很恐惧的样子，还带着笑声呢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10688580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10688580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0d88c4bb00df386c8e21eb35e31eeef5.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10688580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0d88c4bb00df386c8e21eb35e31eeef5-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10688580" class="wp-caption-text">小海豹游回大海。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小海豹满足完好奇心后，又跳回那浩瀚的家里去了。想必这是布里特与马丁相当难忘的经历，划船划到一半，竟会有这样的庞然大物跑到小船……</p>
