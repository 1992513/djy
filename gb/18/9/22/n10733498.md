<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在纽瓦克社区日介绍法轮功</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P1-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>德拉华州纽瓦克社区日活动中，法轮功学员演示静功功法。（鹭青/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月22日讯】(大纪元记者杨茜纽瓦克报导)上周日（9月16日），德拉华州纽瓦克市公园和娱乐部、Christina学区及德拉华大学联合，在德拉华大学校园内举办了2018年度“纽瓦克社区日”活动，吸引周边近万民众参加。</p>
<p>今年有约250家商家参展。社区日现场热闹非常，有美食广场、儿童游乐区、多个表演区、以及美术和手工艺品展售区等。德拉华州部分法轮功学员也参加了当天的社区日活动，并设立展位，向民众介绍法轮功这个来自东方的以“真、善、忍”为准则的古老修炼功法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10733539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10733539" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P04-e1537625472770.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10733539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P04-e1537625472770.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10733539" class="wp-caption-text">德拉华州纽瓦克社区日活动中，法轮功学员向民众介绍法轮功真相。（杨茜/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10733532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10733532" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P05-e1537625502517.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10733532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P05-e1537625502517.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10733532" class="wp-caption-text">德拉华州纽瓦克社区日活动中，民众签名支持法轮功反迫害。（杨茜/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>数位身着黄色炼功服的法轮功学员应邀为民众演示了动、静五套功法，吸引过往民众纷纷驻足观看。从事健保工作的Elton Kimani对打坐冥想和气功很感兴趣，他和女友、德拉华大学金融专业学生Catalina一直在旁观看功法演示。Elton说：“这看起来让人感到很放松，很舒服。”他接受了介绍法轮功的传单，并表示自己以后也想试试学炼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10733522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10733522" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P3-2-1-e1537625171759.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10733522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P3-2-1-e1537625171759.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10733522" class="wp-caption-text">印裔建筑设计师Subhas Mazumber(左一) 学炼法轮功动功。（杨茜/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>印裔建筑设计师Subhas Mazumber路过法轮功的展位，被学员炼功的场面所吸引，并表示想要学炼。他跟着法轮功学员学炼了动功第一套功法后表示感觉很好。他说：“我一直在寻找一种能让我身心更健康而又平和的功法，因为我年纪越来越大了。我试过瑜伽，好像没有什么特别的感觉。这个功法我学炼了不到10分钟，我也说不出那种感觉，好像更多的是精神上的，让人远离紧张焦虑，感觉非常好，我真的很喜欢。” Subhas说这对自己是个很好的开端，他今后会坚持学炼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10733531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10733531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P4-2-e1537625544379.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10733531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P4-2-e1537625544379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10733531" class="wp-caption-text">德拉华州纽瓦克社区日活动展位。（杨茜/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10733538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10733538" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P7-1-e1537625586371.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10733538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P7-1-e1537625586371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10733538" class="wp-caption-text">德拉华州纽瓦克社区日活动展位。（杨茜/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10733535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10733535" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P6-2-e1537625620452.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10733535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P6-2-e1537625620452.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10733535" class="wp-caption-text">德拉华州纽瓦克社区日活动展位。（杨茜/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10733534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10733534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P5-3-e1537625650489.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10733534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/P5-3-e1537625650489.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10733534" class="wp-caption-text">德拉华州纽瓦克社区日活动展位。（杨茜/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>已有四十多年历史的纽瓦克社区日活动是当地最受欢迎的秋季活动，旨在为德拉华及其周边的宾州、马里兰及南泽西的企业和组织提供与社区民众互动交流的平台，并为社区提供资讯服务。</p>
