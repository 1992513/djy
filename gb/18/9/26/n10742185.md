<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>潜入缤纷的海底世界 海洋生物令人目不暇给</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10610147 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月15日讯】印尼的海域是全球拥有最丰富海洋生物的水域之一，缤纷的海底世界宛若梦境般不真实，令潜水爱好者流连忘返。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742338" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2f5c21262798198d3f443590ad2990c6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742338 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2f5c21262798198d3f443590ad2990c6-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742338" class="wp-caption-text">有蓝色斑点的??鱼藏在泥沙里窥伺猎物。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里是印尼的苏拉威西岛（Sulawesi），位于印尼东部，菲律宾南方，岛屿形状特殊，像一个大写的字母“K”。4个半岛中央连接处是险峻山区，海陆比陆路方便，也让此处潜水观光的盛行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742342" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/07f1f1476f162ec51ef19a60e39135ff.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742342 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/07f1f1476f162ec51ef19a60e39135ff-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742342" class="wp-caption-text">和小丑鱼一样不怕海葵的透明虾。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>干净透明的海底，若不是隔着潜水眼镜，而且不断听见氧气筒冒泡泡的声音，真像是在地上的生物园区，可以走上前去触碰各种美丽的生物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/25fcac49fdc6200d5a41743ce030827d.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742343 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/25fcac49fdc6200d5a41743ce030827d-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742343" class="wp-caption-text">是不是很像虾米点缀的麻荖呢？（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>珊瑚礁与海床中间的狭窄空间，有几只小鲨鱼正在休息，看起来躺在松软的白细沙滩上，很舒服呢！鲨鱼平时不会主动攻击人类，但如果好奇心太重，也可能让鲨鱼紧张，建议保持一定距离喔！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f7a9d4a1064997746c20fe5eeb0f86e8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742345 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f7a9d4a1064997746c20fe5eeb0f86e8-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742345" class="wp-caption-text">长得和关东煮有几分像的海洋生物。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样在沙滩小憩的还有这只特殊的珍珠??（蓝点??），这只淡淡土色的生物，全身还有水蓝色的圆点斑纹，模样非常特别。蓝点点生物的眼珠子上有一层薄膜，轻轻铺上，它的眼睛却没有闲着，频频“立体扫描”，不知道是真的在休息，还是藏身沙子中准备捕食猎物呢？</p>
<p>养鱼人士喜爱的精致鱼群出现啰！纤细可爱的迷你小鱼，黑色的鱼身搭配俐落的银色线条，非常抢眼，当鱼群出没在玉米色的珊瑚附近，鲜明的对比色让人惊喜。小鱼抖动鱼尾但维持在同一个地方，像在等待排队进场，真守秩序。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742346" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1b1cd46cc90963262e20280df21c44cb.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742346 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1b1cd46cc90963262e20280df21c44cb-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742346" class="wp-caption-text">打开“美食联想”就停不下来……这不是龙虾牵丝，是龙虾须啦！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宫崎骏动画《魔法公主》里出现的萤光巨人，现在也出现在海底世界啦！不过，这只“萤光小矮人”实在非常非常微小，是一只半透明又圆滚滚的小型虾类，点缀着鲜奶油般的白色圆块，关节处则是美丽的渐层色，由靛蓝色至黑色，非常梦幻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/bafd038cab83389a2a4b519b97141cab.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742348 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/bafd038cab83389a2a4b519b97141cab-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742348" class="wp-caption-text">缓缓移动的海葵。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不知是另一品种，还是“萤光小矮人”的baby，这只透明的小虾跟鹅黄色的Q软海葵待在一起，感觉安全又安心，令它愉快地摇晃着小小的身躯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742349" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/34c904846d0db99c9a1b6193e6dd3d27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742349 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/34c904846d0db99c9a1b6193e6dd3d27-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742349" class="wp-caption-text">海中不乏许多低调的小生物。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样微小的雪花螃蟹，八只爪居然能像蕨类植物一样卷成圆形，它攀附在外形象糖霜麻荖的海洋生物上，似乎在帮忙清洁顺便打打牙祭，真是双赢的共生关系。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742351" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d65ea34ca1acb5849b642a40cc418f4a.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742351 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d65ea34ca1acb5849b642a40cc418f4a-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742351" class="wp-caption-text">当然也会有比较高调的小生物。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水平面以下，仿佛进入另一个世界，蕴藏各式各样缤纷又稀奇古怪的生物，有海水作为介质，浅水员几乎能以360度的全视角观察海中生物的千姿百态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742352" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/041733a1fa8265a418a19cf35bd035d3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10742352 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/041733a1fa8265a418a19cf35bd035d3-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742352" class="wp-caption-text">还有更缤纷的！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>人类能认识到的生命种类，与未知相比，说不定比例很小。如果哪天潜水时发现了新奇的物种，别忘了摄影下来与大家分享喔！</p>
