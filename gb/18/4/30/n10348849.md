<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>农场办亲子焢窑  推广有机农业</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/59d76bc497ae5ff7ff7e1225b3896a65-600-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>为了让小朋友实际到田间来认识有机，将来也能从事有机，华兴有机农场特别举办拔空心菜体验！ （廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年04月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）华兴有机农场（西螺有机蔬菜74班）为了推广云林有机农业，结合在地12位从事有机的农民，29日在该农场举行亲子焢窑体验活动，吸引大批民众们携家带眷体验使用有机蔬菜焢窑活动！县长李进勇及多位民意代表也到场与民众一起同乐！</p>
<figure id="10348873" aria-describedby="caption-10348873" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/633be70b9a29947be5265ad755a2062f-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/633be70b9a29947be5265ad755a2062f-450x338.jpg" alt="亲子开心一起焢窑。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10348873" class="wp-caption-text">亲子开心一起焢窑。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="10348851" aria-describedby="caption-10348851" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/757c436bd62d908cada6d67e19b8e075-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/757c436bd62d908cada6d67e19b8e075-450x300.jpg" alt="县长李进勇与民众一同体验焢土窑的乐趣" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10348851" class="wp-caption-text">县长李进勇与民众一同体验焢土窑的乐趣。（云林县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李进勇表示，兴华有机农场（西螺镇蔬菜产销班第74班）自88年成立以来一直是县内推广安全农业的金字招牌，91年获得产销绩优的肯定，班长廖瑞生更在94年获神农奖殊荣并开始推广有机农业，该产销班所生产的蔬菜更成为新北市学童营养午餐的食材来源，期盼藉由农民们持续投入有机农业的耕作，让云林的食材更获得消费者信赖</p>
<figure id="attachment_10348939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10348939" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d16685bcdfbef86b1fd18104c236727f-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10348939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d16685bcdfbef86b1fd18104c236727f-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10348939" class="wp-caption-text">华兴有机农场负责人廖瑞生（右一）颁奖给蔬菜比赛前三名者。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>华兴有机农场负责人廖瑞生表示，这次活动的目的是鼓励大家来种有机，吃有机！毕竟有机农业在乡下不管是消费者还是农民接受度都不高！因此希望透过这场活动，让民众多了解有机农业！鼓励大家来种有机，希望从点线拓展到面！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10349012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10349012" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/7e8cd04de64498b8a14ac2384ae50d33-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10349012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/7e8cd04de64498b8a14ac2384ae50d33-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10349012" class="wp-caption-text">亲子开心一起焢窑。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天提供的食材全部都是有机的，包括那头山猪也是自己养的！因此特别好吃！另外为了让小朋友实际到田间来认识有机，将来也能从事有机，我们特别举办亲子控窑及拔空心菜体验！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10348949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10348949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d1d338f279ad7f6670f92788077a5231-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10348949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d1d338f279ad7f6670f92788077a5231-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10348949" class="wp-caption-text">老农正在处理烤好的山猪。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10348956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10348956" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/47adcaa3be94944bbef3335dbca92382-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10348956" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/47adcaa3be94944bbef3335dbca92382-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10348956" class="wp-caption-text">热腾腾的烤鸡和烤地瓜出炉了！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>农民最担忧的通路问题，廖瑞生说，他们的通路目前还是以大都会为主，一个星期约有3万公斤的销售量，价格稳定，是一般蔬菜的二倍，比种一般蔬菜较有利润，只是必须要有设施才能种植，他鼓励农民来种有机，他们有很好的辅导方式 欢迎农民加入有机行列！</p>
<p>国际狮子会西螺狮子会会长廖明科表示，现在的人很重视养生，但很多食品都是化学的，今天很难得吃到的全部都是有机蔬菜，希望消费者能以实际行动来支持有机农业！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10348981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10348981" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/21c1a1314f5bc881d82cb0aad5ed1180-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10348981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/21c1a1314f5bc881d82cb0aad5ed1180-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10348981" class="wp-caption-text">华兴有机农场办亲子控窑 吸引大批民众携家带眷参加。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>汉光果菜生产合作社理事廖学典表示，18年前他就开始从事有机农业，那时台湾还没有有机这个概念，因此推得很辛苦，也亏了很多，直到最近几年因为食安问题，有机意识抬头，他说，有机农业最重要的是消费者的认同愿意购买，再来是政府要起带头作用，目前新北市政府及台中市政府都有采购供应学生营养午餐，希望其他县市也能跟进，这样农民种的菜就不怕卖不出去，另外，希望政府能设立有机专区！解决林田污染问题！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10348961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10348961" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/13980ba4a9ad4a41009f182ce3eef001-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10348961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/13980ba4a9ad4a41009f182ce3eef001-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10348961" class="wp-caption-text">丰盛的午餐全都是有机食材料理！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10348965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10348965" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/efc64b755da9577d3299ab47a182329e-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10348965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/efc64b755da9577d3299ab47a182329e-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10348965" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友表演肚皮舞。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10348970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10348970" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/1638973bd3c726b34e2997abb90d770f-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10348970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/1638973bd3c726b34e2997abb90d770f-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10348970" class="wp-caption-text">热腾腾的烤鸡和烤地瓜上桌了。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自南非的小学外聘英语教师玛莎，也参与了这次活动，她说，台湾人很友善，她很爱台湾！这次活动很精彩！食物都很好吃！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_10348967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10348967" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/995099ecf0245e9980862f2b62f4aa56-450x338-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10348967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/995099ecf0245e9980862f2b62f4aa56-450x338-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10348967" class="wp-caption-text">来自南非的玛莎（左一），也参与了这次活动！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
