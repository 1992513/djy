<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>乐烧茶碗古朴沉静 原来花纹是“闷”出来的</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月01日讯】你知道日本茶道里那些充满古朴奇趣的茶碗图案是怎么烧出来的吗？其实，那些千变万化、很像山水画或大理石的流畅纹路，是用一把熊熊烈火“闷”出来的，看完影片就知道了。</p>
<p>短短4分钟的影片，展示了乐烧法的制作过程，而真正的制作过程约2、3个小时左右。示范者是来自苏格兰艾伦陶艺工坊（Arran Ceramics）的陶艺家赛门·松伯恩（Simon Thorborn）。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Simon-Thorborn-at-Arran-Ceramics-516393178453367/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">赛门</a>自从1985年以来，就一直钻研乐烧法的各种创新技巧。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>▼ 看完整影片</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-cc1f6f9c-176b-403f-75ef-06b2edaae261" data-id="player-cc1f6f9c-176b-403f-75ef-06b2edaae261"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/cc1f6f9c-176b-403f-75ef-06b2edaae261?r=16x9&amp;s=720x640&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F4%2F28%2Fn10345007.md#1"></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_10346041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10346041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.49.32-PM.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10346041 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.49.32-PM-600x437.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10346041" class="wp-caption-text">整理陶土，准备坯体制作。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>乐烧法（Raku）最早出现的时候是16世纪的日本幕府时代，它记录着日本人追求极致的茶道文化，用创意陶烧来制作独一无二的茶具。到了本世纪，美国人特别喜爱它的丰富变化，便开始广为流行，还发展出“美式乐烧”，让人人得以发挥创意、享受创作的乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10346042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10346042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.50.06-PM-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10346042 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.50.06-PM-1-600x468.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="468" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10346042" class="wp-caption-text">坯体制作。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>令人期待的薰烧装饰法</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10346055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10346055" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.49.54-PM.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10346055 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.49.54-PM-600x479.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="479" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10346055" class="wp-caption-text">成型。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10346043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10346043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.50.36-PM.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10346043 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.50.36-PM-600x470.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="470" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10346043" class="wp-caption-text">上釉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>乐烧法最引人入胜的地方，就属薰烧装饰的过程了，它让创作者很容易获得成就感。每一个随机的薰烧技法，都能制作出独一无二的特殊花纹和令人惊艳的美丽图案，充满天然的古风和诗意。</p>
<p>薰烧的方法，就是让陶碗烙上不同燃烧物的烙印。 在一个铁桶里放置碎报纸、木屑、枯叶或龙眼壳等等有机物质，将刚出窑的高温坯体用火钳迅速放入铁桶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10346044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10346044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.51.21-PM.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10346044 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/Screen-Shot-2018-04-17-at-12.51.21-PM-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10346044" class="wp-caption-text">薰烧。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这时，坯体的高温会点燃铁桶里的有机物，并喷出一把大火，燃烧的痕迹便成为坯体上随机而成的特殊机理纹路了。</p>
<p>影片中的成品，用干草熏烧出来的纹路，就像历史悠久的古董文物一般，拿它来当茶具，更增添生活乐趣呢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10345125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10345125" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/770f2be19358b3785cb86e5f622462ad.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10345125" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/770f2be19358b3785cb86e5f622462ad-600x556.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="556" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10345125" class="wp-caption-text">看起来充满古风的陶杯，原来是用一把火烧出来的。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>乐烧法制作过程</h2>
<p>1. 坯体制作。<br />
2. 素烧（摄氏750到900度）。<br />
3. 上釉（淋釉、浸釉、喷釉或涂彩）。<br />
4. 窑烧（摄氏1000到1150度）。<br />
5. 出窑。<br />
6. 薰烧装饰。<br />
7. 冷却。</p>
