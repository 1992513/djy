<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>肉桂调节血糖 常用八大香辛料吃对了吗？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/herbs-923496_1920-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香辛料能提升菜肴风味，事实上，香辛料用途甚广，且对健康助益良多。（pixabay）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>香辛料对人类的生活一直有很大的影响，不仅在饮食烹调的使用上，甚至是医疗用途上均有着举足轻重的地位。多数人知道要使用香辛料来提升菜肴风味，事实上，香辛料用途甚广，且对健康助益良多，能让人在品尝美食之际也吃入健康，实为不可忽视的一大食材。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">香辛料的主要作用</span></h2>
<p>香辛料有如下四个主要作用：</p>
<p>1. 增添食物美味。</p>
<p>2. 有助于身体保健和营养补给：许多香辛料的成分都具有杀菌、消炎或降低血压等功效，对健康帮助很大。更被广泛使用在饮食、熏香、营养补给品、药品甚至化妆品中。</p>
<p>3. 减少盐分摄取：例如高血压患者适合低钠饮食，味道太淡时便可加少量香辛料刺激食欲。</p>
<p>4. 增加饱足感：荷兰马斯垂克大学（Maastricht University）发现香辛料可以促进新陈代谢，而且易让人产生吃饱的感觉，就比较不会再吃不健康的零食。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">常用的八大香辛料，有哪些好处？</span></h2>
<p>据健康网站Rodale Wellness，以下八种常见的香辛料，有着不容忽视的健康益处：</p>
<p><strong>1. 大蒜</strong></p>
<p>大蒜最为人熟知的效用是强力杀菌。美国国立卫生研究院（National Institutes of Health）发现大蒜可以预防高血压和心血管硬化。</p>
<p>需注意的是：肠胃功能不好或是严重肝病患者不可食用过量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9831874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9831874" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/garlic-2546681_640-e1510482869898.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9831874 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/garlic-2546681_640-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9831874" class="wp-caption-text">研究发现大蒜可以预防高血压和心血管硬化。(explorerbob/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 肉桂</strong></p>
<p>肉桂可抗炎，降低胆固醇，增强胰岛素敏感性。不论空腹或餐后，肉桂都有助于调节血糖值。</p>
<p>需注意的是：肉桂所含的香豆素会增加肝脏代谢负担，所以一天食用量须低于2.5克。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6546695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6546695" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/01/1504171604272483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-6546695 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/01/1504171604272483-450x332.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6546695" class="wp-caption-text">肉桂（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3. 姜</strong></p>
<p>老一辈的人会喝一碗姜汤来缓解感冒症状。姜还可以抑制恶心、反胃等肠胃不适，改善消化系统问题。</p>
<p>需要注意的是：姜更适合虚寒体质的人，患有痔疮或毒疮的人不宜食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9147116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9147116" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/shutterstock_314808656.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9147116 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/shutterstock_314808656-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9147116" class="wp-caption-text">姜的天然抗炎特性早已广为人知。(Bon Appetit/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4. 迷迭香</strong></p>
<p>迷迭香（rosemary）精油非常受欢迎，淡淡芬芳气息可以提神醒脑、减轻头痛。沐浴乳加入迷迭香可以收敛毛乳，洗发精加入迷迭香能促进头皮的血液循环。英国诺桑比亚大学（Northumbria University）研究还发现，迷迭香香气有提升记忆力功效。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6630150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6630150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/10/1210040847501983.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-6630150 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/10/1210040847501983-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6630150" class="wp-caption-text">迷迭香精油可提神醒脑、减轻头痛。（ALE／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>5. 姜黄</strong></p>
<p>姜黄（turmeric）主要生长在南亚、印度和印尼，内含丰富姜黄素，能抗炎抗氧化，也能改善肠胃消化系统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8210617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8210617" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Fotolia_89843457_Subaion_L.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8210617 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Fotolia_89843457_Subaion_L-450x231.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8210617" class="wp-caption-text">姜黄在喜马拉雅地区有“厨房王后”和“生命香料”之称。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6. 甘草</strong></p>
<p>甘草可以减少发炎，提升免疫力，有助于改善血糖值。</p>
<p>需要注意的是：食用过量会使血压上升，因此高血压患者和心脏病患者比较不适合食用甘草。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5666060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5666060" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311190720131778.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-5666060 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311190720131778-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5666060" class="wp-caption-text">甘草可以减少发炎，并提升免疫力。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>7. 牛至</strong></p>
<p>牛至又称奥勒冈（oregano），原生于希腊、土耳其、意大利。牛至的抗氧化剂含量非常高，是蓝莓的4倍，橘子的12倍，苹果的42倍。牛至还有抗炎、杀菌、促进消化等功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8514170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8514170" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611210903502669.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8514170 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611210903502669-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8514170" class="wp-caption-text">牛至具有抗炎、杀菌、促进消化等功能。(Liljam/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>?8. 百里香</strong></p>
<p>百里香（thyme）含有维生素A、维生素C，可以增强免疫力，抗氧化。百里香被制成精油或茶叶，用来止咳，缓解喉咙痛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8825974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8825974" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/fresh-herbs-on-wood.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8825974 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/fresh-herbs-on-wood-450x277.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8825974" class="wp-caption-text">百里香含维生素A、C，可以增强免疫力，抗氧化。（公有领域）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些香辛料不仅美味，且具有各种各样的效用，绝对是每日饮食生活中少不了的角色。下一次烹饪别忘了加一点香辛料，亲身体验它们神奇的地方。</p>
