<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>SM娱乐群星飞杜拜 准备SMTOWN演唱会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/180405045456100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>SM娱乐群星飞杜拜，左起始源、允浩、艺声、润娥（允儿）、瑟琪。（<a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/BhLMgLmA334/?taken-by=smtown" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="color: #0000ff;">SM娱乐官方IG
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年04月05日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）韩国最大演艺公司SM娱乐的《SMTOWN LIVE WORLD TOUR VI IN DUBAI》群星演唱会，即将于明（6）日晚间于杜拜举行。为此，今（5）日SM娱乐的众星也都齐聚仁川机场，搭乘同一班机前往杜拜。而SM娱乐官方IG也与粉丝们分享众星难得的合照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10279387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10279387" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/180405045503100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10279387" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/180405045503100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10279387" class="wp-caption-text">左起SUHO、允浩、珉豪、润娥（允儿）及昌珉前往杜拜。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/BhLbsAEAOSP/?taken-by=smtown" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="color: #0000ff;">SM娱乐官方IG</span></a>／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>SM娱乐的旗下艺人演唱会“SMTOWN”，因可以同时看到许多明星，因此自2008年首度举办以来，艺人们前往各国巡演皆有众多韩流粉丝参与。除了曾于韩国首尔、纽约、巴黎、北京、上海及新加坡等地举办，去年7月造访大阪时也于两天内动员8万粉丝，东京巨蛋场更于两天内动员10万粉丝参与。</p>
<p>本次是“SMTOWN”演唱会首度进军中东，于杜拜Autism Rocks Arena举办《SMTOWN LIVE WORLD TOUR VI IN DUBAI》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10279388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10279388" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/180405045506100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10279388" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/180405045506100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10279388" class="wp-caption-text">左起艺声、允浩、始源共同前往杜拜准备SMTOWN演唱会。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/BhLL-GRAL1G/?taken-by=smtown" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="color: #0000ff;">SM娱乐官方IG</span></a>／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次于杜拜举行的演出，动员SM娱乐旗下艺人Kangta安七炫、BoA宝儿、Super Junior、TVXQ东方神起、Girls&#8217; Generation少女时代（润娥、太妍、孝渊）、SHINee、f(x)、EXO、Red Velvet、NCT、TRAX及Henny刘宪华共同参与。</p>
<p>为此，今日SM娱乐官方IG也公开了艺人们于飞机上的合照。其中不仅有SHINee及东方神起的大合照，以及Super Junior神童及东海与Kangta安七炫的合照等，少女时代的润娥（允儿）和孝渊、Red Velvet的瑟琪、EXO的队长SUHO、TraxX的GINJO、JUNGMO和JAY，以及f(x)的AMBER与LUNA都入镜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10279382" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10279382" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/180405045500100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10279382" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/180405045500100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10279382" class="wp-caption-text">SHINee、东方神起、Super Junior及安七炫皆前往杜拜。<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/BhLaatggeZ3/?taken-by=smtown" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="color: #0000ff;">SM娱乐官方IG</span></a>／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>SM娱乐的官方IG将艺人自拍照一一公开后，立刻吸引粉丝们关注与按赞，每张照片于几小时内皆突破十万人次按赞。其中少女时代的润娥（允儿）及Red Velvet的瑟琪的合照，更于5小时内吸引超过26万粉丝按赞。</p>
