<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>最棒人生经历 落基山游全球第32</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年04月17日讯】<span lang="ZH-TW">因<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E5%9F%BA%E5%B1%B1.md#1">落基山</a>脉美景获赞，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97.md#1">卑诗</a>省近日上榜旅行网站</span><span lang="EN-US">Flight Network</span><span lang="ZH-TW">最新推出的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%80%E6%A3%92%E4%BA%BA%E7%94%9F%E7%BB%8F%E5%8E%86.md#1">最棒人生经历</a>”地点，与亚省并列全球第</span><span lang="EN-US">32</span><span lang="ZH-TW">名。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">被名为“世界终极人生清单”（</span><span lang="EN-US">The World</span><span lang="ZH-TW">’</span><span lang="EN-US">s Ultimate Bucket List</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）的该排名基于超过</span><span lang="EN-US">800</span><span lang="ZH-TW">名旅行记者、博主和旅游<span style="color: #00b050;">仲</span>介的点评反馈，评选出在人生中最值得体验的世界各地活动。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97.md#1">卑诗</a>省与亚省因横跨二省的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E5%9F%BA%E5%B1%B1.md#1">落基山</a>脉“山景壮观”、“峡谷深邃”和“湖泊青绿”而榜上有名。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">排在该列表前五名的人生体验分别是：在非洲探寻野踪、南极洲探险、欣赏北极光、跋涉进入秘鲁的马丘比丘（</span><span lang="EN-US">Machu Picchu</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）古城，以及在加拉帕戈斯群岛（</span><span lang="EN-US">Galapagos Islands</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）泛舟。完整列表可登陆网站</span><span lang="EN-US">https://www.flightnetwork.com.au/blog/the-worlds-ultimate-bucket-list/</span><span lang="ZH-TW">查看。?</span><span lang="EN-US">◇</span></p>
