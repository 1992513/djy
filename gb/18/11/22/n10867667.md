<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>著名餐厅 令人意外的台湾菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220138111366-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（米芝Gi提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月22日讯】早些年每到台北都喜欢去<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%A6%E5%B0%8F%E6%9C%88.md#1">度小月</a>吃东西，觉得精致、好吃又便宜。度小月有超过120年历史，始于1895年由做渔民的洪氏创办，最初不设店铺，只以“担仔”挑住面沿街叫卖，精致小碗、特制的肉燥、虾汤底属品牌的3大特色。他们靠真材实料和品质打出名堂，也相继在大陆和澳门开设分店，去年也进驻香港，在尖沙咀海运大厦开设分店。</p>
<p><strong>完整影片：</strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-ecdf39bf-a67d-4208-741c-75d1cfa6aa3b" data-id="player-ecdf39bf-a67d-4208-741c-75d1cfa6aa3b"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/ecdf39bf-a67d-4208-741c-75d1cfa6aa3b?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&amp;d=81&&cat=ncid1251282&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F11%2F22%2Fn10867667.md#1"></a>
<p>他们称香港所用到的食材，包括肉燥、虾汤粉、贡丸及金萱绿茶均由台湾空运到港。调味方面也选用台湾“五印醋”，色泽深，醋味浓，有助带出虾汤及肉燥的鲜味。还有现场煮面的师父也是由台湾派驻，为客人烹煮担仔面，也会培训香港的面师，确保质素。</p>
<p>上星期一本相约了朋友找间环境舒适的海景餐厅叹个下午茶，但海运大厦内的海景餐厅不是没有下午茶供应，就是很普通不吸引。逛着逛着就经过<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%A6%E5%B0%8F%E6%9C%88.md#1">度小月</a>，被雅致的门面和半开放的厨房所吸引，而且记得它的食物份量都是小小的，不会太多，因此就和朋友进去，试试香港店的味道如何！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10867923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220236131366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867923" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220236131366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867923" class="wp-caption-text">经过度小月，被雅致的门面和半开放的厨房所吸引。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>店铺装潢雅致，大门位置放有传统炉灶面摊，透明玻璃可看到师傅即场煮面、肉燥和羹汤。餐馆内依照台湾传统装潢，天花有纸鸟装饰，近窗位置看到邮轮停泊，因为对正上船位置所以没有海景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10867925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220137261366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867925" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220137261366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867925" class="wp-caption-text">餐馆内依照台湾传统装潢。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10867921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220239211366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867921" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220239211366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867921" class="wp-caption-text">餐厅天花有纸鸟装饰。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>坐下点了两个茶餐，一个是小食拼盘配桂花乌龙茶，另一个是卤水拼盘配瑰丽堂煌，另点了一碗<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%85%E4%BB%94%E5%8F%8C%E6%8B%BC.md#1">担仔双拼</a>和一碟台南芋糕。先上来两杯餐饮，是两个茶包冲泡出来的，桂花乌龙茶桂花味很清香凸出，瑰丽堂煌是玫瑰普洱茶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10867864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220138111366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867864" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220138111366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867864" class="wp-caption-text">小食拼盘配桂花乌龙茶、卤水拼盘配瑰丽堂煌、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%85%E4%BB%94%E5%8F%8C%E6%8B%BC.md#1">担仔双拼</a>和台南芋糕。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小食拼盘有炸花枝丸2粒、炸盐酥鸡和台湾肠，伴有梅子味淮盐。小食味道一般，也不脆。卤水拼盘有卤蛋一只、猪大肠、鸡翼2只和贡丸2粒，伴有花生，卤水拼盘比小食拼盘好吃，食物入味但不热，也没卤水汁伴着，入口比较干，尤其卤蛋口感很干，猪大肠很多肥膏。贡丸很弹牙，应该是最好吃吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10867876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220137561366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867876" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220137561366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867876" class="wp-caption-text">小食拼盘味道一般，也不脆。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10867881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867881" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220137391366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867881" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220137391366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867881" class="wp-caption-text">卤水拼盘食物入味但不热，也没卤水汁伴着，入口比较干。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台南芋糕卖相精致，不过入口不行，芋头不够粉也不软腍，没有芋头香，不好吃！担仔双拼的水准就能保持，和当年我在台湾总店吃的味道一样，只是略嫌肉燥太少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10867901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867901" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220138211366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867901" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220138211366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867901" class="wp-caption-text">台南芋糕卖相精致，不过芋头不够粉也不软腍，没有芋头香。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10867908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867908" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220137471366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867908" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220137471366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867908" class="wp-caption-text">担仔双拼。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10867910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10867910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220138591366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867910" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811220138591366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10867910" class="wp-caption-text">香港店的担仔双拼和当年我在台湾总店吃的味道一样，只是略嫌肉燥太少。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
