<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Galveston岛冬季旅游  十大最佳假日活动</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年11月05日讯】（大纪元美国休斯顿讯）我们喜欢将Galveston称为德克萨斯州的“冬日梦幻岛”，因为从11月到1月，这里有50多天的节庆活动，岛上充满了节日乐趣。在此列出10个最受欢迎的活动供您出游参考。（所有图片来自Galveston CVB）</p>
<p>1. Moody Gardens冰雕展：极地之旅<br />
<figure id="attachment_10831752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/528272c05d31059607c5ae38f0480770-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10831752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/528272c05d31059607c5ae38f0480770-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831752" class="wp-caption-text">Moody Gardens冰雕展：极地之旅（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2018年全新主题！ 一个技艺精湛的冰雕团队用200万磅冰块为您开创从北极到南极的假日之旅。 看北极熊、企鹅、座头鲸、雪猫头鹰、海象，当然还有驯鹿！ 滑下巨型冰滑梯，体验风驰电掣的感觉。在Shivers Ice Bar的冰屋感受爱斯基摩人般的生活。</p>
<p>2. Moody Gardens 节日灯展<br />
11月17日 &#8211; 1月6日</p>
<figure id="attachment_10831751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831751" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/e5d206738cc1b7cf2b7da57b02e64326-450x298.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10831751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/e5d206738cc1b7cf2b7da57b02e64326-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831751" class="wp-caption-text">Moody Gardens 节日灯展（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>带给家庭和团体一个传统假日！1英里的步行街，超过100万盏彩灯，您可以在Moody Gardens尽情欣赏加尔维斯顿湾(Galveston Bay)的壮丽美景。 还可去该地区唯一的户外滑冰场滑冰，逛一逛热巧克力和节日美食特卖区，享用假日自助餐，观看现场娱乐表演等。</p>
<p>3. 1894大歌剧院节日演出<br />
11月24日 &#8211; 1月12日</p>
<figure id="attachment_10831749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831749" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/75c2358d33899ebb7890c18016e964ea-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10831749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/75c2358d33899ebb7890c18016e964ea-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831749" class="wp-caption-text">1894大歌剧院节日演出（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>神父合唱团The Priests，Charles Dickens的圣诞颂歌， Wynton Marsalis带领的爵士队Jazz at Lincoln Center Orchestra，乡村音乐歌手Jerry Jeff Walker，音乐剧All Is Calm: The Christmas Truce of 1914，经典故事A Charlie Brown Christmas Live on Stage 和The Other Mozart ，都将 在Galveston的这一宏伟历史剧院登场。</p>
<p>4. 市中心历史街区赏灯购物<br />
11月24日 &#8211; 1月1日</p>
<figure id="attachment_10831755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831755" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a55aa0e03f506a7b079cfc40164e0c75-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10831755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a55aa0e03f506a7b079cfc40164e0c75-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831755" class="wp-caption-text">市中心历史街区赏灯购物（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市中心灯展将在Saengerfest公园及其外面展示神奇之光和其它节日主题景观，包括互动相框和巨型雪球，供游客在指定的周末观赏。灯光将会照亮Galveston市中心的历史街区，游客还可以在该区的许多精品店、艺术画廊和古董店中找到独特的节日礼物。</p>
<p>5．SCHLITTERBAHN水上乐园节日项目<br />
11月17日 &#8211; 12月30日</p>
<figure id="attachment_10831753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/7b85e3bb04dc4f39af6ed22e3b3dd244-450x331.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10831753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/7b85e3bb04dc4f39af6ed22e3b3dd244-450x331.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="331" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831753" class="wp-caption-text">SCHLITTERBAHN水上乐园节日项目（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在休斯顿唯一的室内水上乐园尽享欢乐时光。观看圣诞老人冲浪、Bahn Borealis灯光秀，与家人、朋友一起享用假日美食套餐等。</p>
<p>6. 第45届狄更斯节<br />
11月30日 &#8211; 12月2日</p>
<figure id="attachment_10831756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831756" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/2793bbdda5602cd59ba64af9ad86a5f6-450x263.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10831756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/2793bbdda5602cd59ba64af9ad86a5f6-450x263.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831756" class="wp-caption-text">第45届狄更斯节（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一年一度的狄更斯游行与庆典是不容错过的节日盛宴！Galveston市中心的Strand 街将穿越时空，回到维多利亚时代的伦敦。独具维多利亚风情的特色游行、传统的风笛演奏、手工制作的精美礼服和首饰，还有精彩的舞台表演。</p>
<p>7. 与沙丘鹤一起共度佳节<br />
12月8日-12月9日</p>
<figure id="attachment_10831758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831758" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/0055474b6329dfb9e16267ae836f68e7.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-10831758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/0055474b6329dfb9e16267ae836f68e7.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831758" class="wp-caption-text">与沙丘鹤一起共度佳节（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4英尺高的沙丘鹤陆续飞来Galveston岛上过冬。从日出到日落，都有特别活动安排，您可欣赏到鹤群翩翩起舞的优雅身姿。</p>
<p>8. Galveston铁路博物馆的圣诞老人火车<br />
12月15日</p>
<p>Galveston岛上的圣诞老人别出心裁，他将驾驶着Harbourside特快列车与您相会在Galveston铁路博物馆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10831754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831754" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/wonderislandthumb-450x225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-10831754 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/wonderislandthumb-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831754" class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. MOODY GARDENS欢乐圣诞马戏团晚餐和表演<br />
12月21日-1月4日</p>
<p>享受令人惊叹又充满欢快的节日庆祝活动，美食、马戏和音乐尽在其中。</p>
<p>10. 圣诞老人半程马拉松和5公里跑步<br />
12月15日-12月16日</p>
<figure id="attachment_10831757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10831757" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/9378acad6acb219fa5193a41e0464127-450x298.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10831757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/9378acad6acb219fa5193a41e0464127-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10831757" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞老人半程马拉松和5公里跑步（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>伴随着欢乐与热情，与成千上万的圣诞老人一起穿过Galveston的街道，沿着海湾尽情奔跑。</p>
