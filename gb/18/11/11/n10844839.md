<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾棒球魂Naluwan@KANO经典赛 棒坛三代同堂</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/7f1b388d012d8bfb9711e167b10d87f4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大合照，现场出赛球员着白色球衣嘉义大学及蓝色球衣台东大学棒球选手也入列。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月11日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市身为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E7%90%83.md#1">棒球</a>原乡，无论球星或球迷莫不关心原乡的棒运发展，市长涂醒哲打造棒球园区，积极推展棒球交流，所以百年棒球历史古战场嘉义市立棒球场的使用率越来越高，11日更促成棒坛三代同堂，成就百年传承<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8%E8%B5%9B.md#1">经典赛</a>。</p>
<p>今天由台湾棒坛两大好手发源地区，嘉义“KANO”及花东“Naluwan”老中青三代球星齐聚嘉义市立<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E7%90%83.md#1">棒球</a>场，参加由华南金控主要赞助，台湾棒球名人堂协会与嘉义市政府共同主办的“台湾棒球魂Naluwan @ KANO 100年传承<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8%E8%B5%9B.md#1">经典赛</a>”，赛事吸引不少球迷进场支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10844842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10844842" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/11/11/n10844839.md#1/%e6%a3%92%e7%90%83%e5%90%8d%e4%ba%ba%e5%a0%82%e7%90%86%e4%ba%8b%e9%95%b7%e9%bb%83%e7%91%9b%e5%9d%a1%e3%80%81%e5%b8%82%e9%95%b7%e6%b6%82%e9%86%92%e5%93%b2%e5%8f%8a%e8%8f%af%e5%8d%97%e9%87%91%e6%8e%a7" rel="attachment wp-att-10844842"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10844842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/081c0aed6e64653aa17306059ca17792-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10844842" class="wp-caption-text">棒球名人堂理事长黄瑛坡、市长涂醒哲及华南金控副总经理徐千婷等人合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市长涂醒哲颁发感谢状给出席赛事的VIP球员及贵宾，在此同时主持人在每位VIP球星接受感谢状时，一一介绍该球星在棒球界缔造的伟大事迹，球场内掌声如潮水般滚动，嘉义市立棒球场也因为这些VIP球星而沾光，之后涂醒哲为百年传承经典赛投出历史性一球，宣告赛事正式开打。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10844843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10844843" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/11/11/n10844839.md#1/%e6%8e%a5%e5%8f%97%e7%8d%8e%e5%ba%a7%e7%9a%84%e6%9c%89%e5%8a%9f%e4%ba%ba%e5%93%a1%e8%88%87%e5%b8%82%e9%95%b7%e6%b6%82%e9%86%92%e5%93%b2%e5%b7%a64%e5%8f%8a%e6%a3%92%e7%90%83%e5%90%8d%e4%ba%ba" rel="attachment wp-att-10844843"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10844843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/bdd552a262cb9551c6ac5983a9de7010-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10844843" class="wp-caption-text">接受奖座的有功人员与市长涂醒哲(左4)及棒球名人堂理事长黄瑛坡(右4)合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>涂醒哲致词表示，1931年KANO(嘉农)打进甲子园冠军战，嘉义市成为台湾棒球发展重镇；1968年台东红叶少棒队“红叶传奇”，也掀起棒球热潮。嘉义与花东是台湾棒坛两大选手发源地，有幸邀请老中青三代球星莅临棒球原乡，进行跨世纪交会，更感谢球迷们用行动支持台湾棒球传承。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10844845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10844845" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/11/11/n10844839.md#1/%e7%8f%be%e5%a0%b4%e5%98%89%e7%be%a9%e5%a4%a7%e5%ad%b8%e8%88%87%e5%8f%b0%e6%9d%b1%e5%a4%a7%e5%ad%b8pk%e8%b3%bd%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10844845"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10844845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/62a6d0e13c529cac2f191911fb4d973e-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10844845" class="wp-caption-text">现场嘉义大学与台东大学PK赛。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今日赛事，除了场上嘉义大学棒球队(代表KANO)与台东大学棒球队(代表Naluwan)精彩的交锋之外，每局换场时间更有嘉义地区与花东地区出身的VIP球星，在镜头前与球迷们分享从小到大一路走来的棒球历程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10844846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10844846" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/11/11/n10844839.md#1/kano%e6%a3%92%e7%b3%bb%e8%ad%9c%e7%b8%bd%e8%a1%a8%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10844846"><img class="size-small wp-image-10844846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/99d0aa61f170666afe65f04d7ec1bfa6-e1541940523995-300x534.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="534" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10844846" class="wp-caption-text">KANO棒系谱总表。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>VIP球星邀请中华职棒现役球员周思齐、张志豪、王胜伟等名将，更特别请来目前高龄80岁且曾在嘉义地区执教棒球超过40余载的名教练苏栋川老前辈，苏栋川教练和“棒球太子”李宪宗同为第四届亚洲杯棒球锦标赛中华成棒代表队的成员，许多嘉义知名球星都经过他一手调教，诸如李来发、陈德华、陈昭安、陈瑞昌等人皆出自他的训练，可以说这次的赛事也是棒坛的三代同堂盛会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10844847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10844847" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/11/11/n10844839.md#1/%e8%8a%b1%e6%9d%b1%e5%8e%9f%e4%bd%8f%e6%b0%91%e6%a3%92%e7%b3%bb%e8%ad%9c%e7%b8%bd%e8%a1%a8%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10844847"><img class="size-small wp-image-10844847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/d26bf0bc0b040d79b332442970538cc6-e1541940549428-300x534.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="534" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10844847" class="wp-caption-text">花东原住民棒系谱总表。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一局上半，出身华南银行棒球队，在1950-1960年代曾叱咤台湾棒坛，素有“棒球太子”美称的李宪宗，代表KANO方投出全场比赛的第一球。李宪宗在球员时期，曾受出身嘉农，后来前往日本发展，并且双入选日本野球殿堂及台湾棒球名人堂名人的“人间机关车”吴昌征(吴波)指点，这对于当时已相当杰出的他有着十分助益的效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10844848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10844848" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/11/11/n10844839.md#1/%e5%b8%82%e9%95%b7%e6%b6%82%e9%86%92%e5%93%b2%e9%96%8b%e7%90%83%ef%bc%8c%e8%8f%af%e5%8d%97%e9%87%91%e6%8e%a7%e5%89%af%e7%b8%bd%e7%b6%93%e7%90%86%e5%be%90%e5%8d%83%e5%a9%b7%e6%89%93%e6%93%8a%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10844848"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10844848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6c4a9331a924337d9e37db4b794afb71-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10844848" class="wp-caption-text">市长涂醒哲开球，华南金控副总经理徐千婷打击。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“台湾棒球魂Naluwan @ KANO 100年传承经典赛”与球迷一同见证100年棒球历史的古老战场，昔日棒球英雄的往日荣光和丰采，现场也有棒球文物特展，让球迷更能深深体会棒球运动带来的奋斗精神与魅力，让球员及球迷朋友做一趟棒球知性之旅，别具意义。</p>
