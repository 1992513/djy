<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>1秒剥掉大蒜皮？微波炉的七大妙用</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/shutterstock_328804208-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff

<h6>大蒜用微波炉热过之后，很轻松就能把蒜皮剥下来。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>微波炉是常见的家用电器，多数人用它来加热食物。其实微波炉还有许多妙用，像剥大蒜皮、挤柠檬汁这样沾满手粘腻也做不好的事，靠微波炉可以轻松解决。来看看微波炉的特殊用法吧。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">微波炉七大妙用</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">1. 快速剥大蒜</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10864065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10864065" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/collage1-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10864065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/collage1-1-600x250.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10864065" class="wp-caption-text">微波炉加热，可以让大蒜皮更好剥。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大蒜皮很难剥，一点一点地撕，不仅费时，还弄得手上都是浓浓的蒜味。用刀将大蒜根部切除，放入微波炉，微波10～20秒时间，再拿出来（注意别烫到手）。待蒜稍凉，用手轻轻一捏，眨眼的功夫，一颗颗蒜肉就从蒜皮里滑出来了。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">2. 轻松切南瓜</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10864082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10864082" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/58b145bcea7ebdc57d6aac741f798cda.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10864082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/58b145bcea7ebdc57d6aac741f798cda-600x241.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="241" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10864082" class="wp-caption-text">难切的南瓜，微波之后，一下子就能切开，皮也好削了。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>南瓜硬梆梆，很难切？这时微波炉可以帮上大忙。首先，想办法让南瓜内部空间透气（譬如将南瓜戳几个洞），以防微波加热时爆炸；然后将南瓜放进微波炉加热一段时间取出，南瓜就可以轻易地切开，皮也更容易削了。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">3. 快速挤出柠檬汁</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10864093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10864093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/926bdf1a81933cb4cc42e7d0a979e5e5.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10864093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/926bdf1a81933cb4cc42e7d0a979e5e5-600x246.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="246" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10864093" class="wp-caption-text">柠檬不好挤，微波后就能轻易挤出一股股柠檬汁。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用新鲜柠檬挤汁很费力，不妨将柠檬剖半，放入微波炉，只要微波10秒钟取出，此时再用手一捏，柠檬汁就成股流入碗里。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">4. 奶酪变饼干</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10864108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10864108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/03be3994005e1ef2b7d06201df87ddf2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10864108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/03be3994005e1ef2b7d06201df87ddf2-600x246.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="246" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10864108" class="wp-caption-text">起士微波之后，就变成了脆脆的起士酥。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>喜欢吃奶酪（起司）的人，也可以自己制作奶酪饼干。在盘子上铺一层烘焙纸，铺上奶酪片，放入微波炉，加热90秒，味道香浓的奶酪饼干就做好了。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">5. 让饼干回春</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10864119" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10864119" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a94ef73c7fb0bf2602534da5d7bc6830.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10864119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a94ef73c7fb0bf2602534da5d7bc6830-600x247.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="247" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10864119" class="wp-caption-text">软化的饼干用微波炉加热之后，会变得松脆。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饼干放久了会受潮、软化。将饼干放入微波炉，以10秒为一单位加热数次，可使饼干恢复酥脆口感。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">6. 软化豆类</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10864131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10864131" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1e8da1227243246ebd9c9f65752da045.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10864131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1e8da1227243246ebd9c9f65752da045-600x247.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="247" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10864131" class="wp-caption-text">微波炉加热可以帮助豆子变软。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>豆类食材总是要泡几个小时才能变软，而微波炉是能快速软化豆类的工具。将豆子泡水，用微波炉加热10分钟取出，待冷却后，豆子就能拿来烹饪了。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">7. 软化冷藏的剩饭</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10864139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10864139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ed6962b41d45544e15c52579927fcfc6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10864139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ed6962b41d45544e15c52579927fcfc6-600x247.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="247" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10864139" class="wp-caption-text">变硬的饭可以在湿纸巾的帮助下微波加热变软。（视频截图/大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冰箱里的剩饭变得又干又硬？在饭碗上覆盖一张浸湿的纸巾，放入微波炉加热1～3分钟，剩饭就会恢复香软。</p>
