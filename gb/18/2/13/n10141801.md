<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>给双胞胎宝宝换睡衣 爸爸开始“追逐大战”</h2>
<a href="https://dhw62qyyxrv4n.cloudfront.net/Z0yBDN71hiob"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65"></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年02月14日讯】让宝宝好好睡觉不是一件容易的事，但试图让两个宝宝一起好好睡觉几乎是不可能做到的。</p>
<p>当新手爸爸海曼（Ben Hayman）试图帮他的双胞胎女儿换上睡衣去睡觉时，这个可怕的过程让他觉得是在与鳄鱼搏斗。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000">看完整影片?</span></strong></p>
<p>两个宝宝在爸爸给她们换睡衣期间，不仅不停地在地上爬，还对周围环境倍感兴趣，不知疲倦地摆脱爸爸的“魔掌”逃跑。这名可怜的爸爸则需要无休止地追逐两个宝贝女儿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10511862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10511862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d4da4d2995630d839791bcd2e90899a7.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10511862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d4da4d2995630d839791bcd2e90899a7-600x474.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="474"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10511862" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一旦新手爸爸抓住其中一个宝宝，把宝宝的一条胳膊或一条腿塞进睡衣内，另一个宝宝就会翻身爬走，他不得不中止手上的活，去抓爬走的那个宝宝，这一过程不停地重复。</p>
<p>不过，尽管海曼确实很难控制住这个局面，但他显然也很乐意跟女儿们一起玩追逐宝宝游戏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10511864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10511864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c3be24967032e63dd184fdaa79fedc70.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10511864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c3be24967032e63dd184fdaa79fedc70-600x511.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="511"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10511864" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过几分钟的疯狂追逐后，他设法让双胞胎女儿穿着搭配的粉红色睡衣，也证明这个艰巨的任务仅适合有强烈意志的人去完成。</p>
<p>自从他的双胞胎女儿出生以来，海曼把自己的Instagram账户改成双胞胎爸爸账户，并在上面发布了这场热闹的追逐大战。</p>
<p>“这变得越来越难了”，这名澳大利亚的父亲在视频标题中写道。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/bonus-video-link-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10500739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/bonus-video-link-1-450x43.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="43"></a>
