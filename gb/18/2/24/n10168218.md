<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>林玉山绘画艺术《未啸已风生》静观花鸟 墨泼起鸟声</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/0205A-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">编按：</span></strong><br />
<span style="color: #800000;"> 《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AA%E5%95%B8%E5%B7%B2%E9%A3%8E%E7%94%9F.md#1">未啸已风生</a>─<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E7%8E%89%E5%B1%B1.md#1">林玉山</a>的绘画艺术》画展以林玉山写生观点为主轴，“以其笔下之走兽、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E9%B8%9F.md#1">花鸟</a>、风景、人物规划出四个主题：“彩墨挥洒藏百兽”、“静观花鸟写神情”、“寄情乡土留画迹”、“静思人物画民俗”。作者梅溪子追循《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AA%E5%95%B8%E5%B7%B2%E9%A3%8E%E7%94%9F.md#1">未啸已风生</a>》四个主题，分篇逐一顾赏林玉山画作的艺术功夫与境界，并赋予即兴诗语，融画境、诗境于一炉，这是《未啸已风生》的第二个主题之2：静观<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E9%B8%9F.md#1">花鸟</a> 墨泼起鸟声。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #ff0000;">墨泼起鸟声</span></h2>
<p>从花鸟画作品、最容易看出一位画家的功力。玉山伯最爱摹写入画的禽鸟是麻雀，除此以外，玉山伯的花鸟画包括了老鹰、太平鸟、帝雉、海鸥、海鸟等等，还有斑鸠、喜鹊、鸡、鸭、鸳鸯水鸭，姿态传神、各具精神和趣韵。</p>
<p>笔者在赏画之际，即画赋诗，组合画与诗，与读者一起来顾赏：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #800000;">＊雀小小确幸，五脏全如鹰；鸿鹄苍松立，大志高空凌：</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10189297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10189297" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" style="color: #800000;" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/0206.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10189297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/0206-600x429.png" alt="" width="600" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10189297" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="color: #000000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E7%8E%89%E5%B1%B1.md#1">林玉山</a>画作。（梅溪子翻摄展品及翻拍家传旧图片／大纪元）</span></figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>＊爪利捉获快，啄勾眼目明；太平鸟吉兆，帝雉鸡贵名：</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10189301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10189301" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/0207A.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10189301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/0207A-600x430.png" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10189301" class="wp-caption-text">林玉山画作。（梅溪子翻摄展品及翻拍家传旧图片／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #800000;">＊家禽双在圃，斑鸠独自行；静观花鸟绘，笔下多神情。</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10189308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10189308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/0208B.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10189308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/0208B-600x430.png" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10189308" class="wp-caption-text">林玉山画作。（梅溪子翻摄展品及翻拍家传旧图片／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果赞赏玉山伯是“多产画家”，毋宁说他是“见多识广”。玉山伯尽一生之力静观花鸟，更及写生珍禽异兽。说他画虎“未啸已风生”，见他画雀更是“一绝”，鸟语花香的画作动人心弦、发人幽情。有人赞誉玉山伯“墨泼起鸟声”，诚哉斯言也。<br />
