<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>年初二开年饭 香港老字号五十年不变</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/G21A1299@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>裕隆海味秉承传统，在大年初二早上食开年饭。（陈仲明／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月18日讯】（大纪元记者梁珍香港报导）广东人过年传统习俗是年初一吃斋，以敬神佛；年初二吃开年饭，即一年的“头牙”， 菜式丰富兼好兆头，希望来年生意兴隆。香港一家老字号海味铺，坚持传统在年初二和员工一起吃开年饭，至今已半世纪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10152590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10152590" style="width: 711px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/18/n10152580.md#1/g21a15991200x1200" rel="attachment wp-att-10152590"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-10152590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/G21A1599@1200x1200-711x400.jpg" alt="" width="711" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10152590" class="wp-caption-text">裕隆海味秉承传统，在大年初二早上食开年饭。（陈仲明／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10152591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/18/n10152580.md#1/g21a16531200x1200" rel="attachment wp-att-10152591"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-10152591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/G21A1653@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10152591" class="wp-caption-text">裕隆海味秉承传统，在大年初二早上食开年饭。（陈仲明／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在香港上环著名的海味街，大年初二几乎店铺都关门，唯有位于德辅道西的老字号海味铺“裕隆号”，一大早忙个不停。老板按照传统和员工们一起吃开年饭，至今已半世纪，而且要吃的早，开个好年。菜式年年不变，即八??一汤，寓意长长久久。</p>
<p>今年开年饭准时于早上11点开始，但身为伙头的三姐早于清晨5时半已在厨房为饭??张罗，部分菜式更要提前几日准备。</p>
<h2>八??一汤菜式不变 寓意长长久久</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10152592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10152592" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/18/n10152580.md#1/photo60713309550769377281200x1200" rel="attachment wp-att-10152592"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-10152592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/photo6071330955076937728@1200x1200-601x400.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10152592" class="wp-caption-text">裕隆海味秉承传统，在大年初二早上食开年饭。（郭威利／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“裕隆号”第二代传人吴伟鸿10点起拜神上香，又和员工、好友们闲话家常，发利是，亲密如家人。菜式不变如一，个个都有好兆头，他如数家珍地介绍：“乳猪（寓意）红皮赤壮、身体健康；鸡是阉鸡，会啼会走；腊肠（寓意）家肥屋润，笋虾碌寓意捞到笋嘢、找到便货。”此外，还有代表“发财好市”的发菜炆蚝豉、生财的冬菇生菜、“年年有余”的煎鲮鱼和“年年都有”的莲藕生鱼汤等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10152596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10152596" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/18/n10152580.md#1/g21a16271200x1200" rel="attachment wp-att-10152596"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-10152596 size-medium_vertical" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/G21A1627@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10152596" class="wp-caption-text">裕隆海味秉承传统，在大年初二早上食开年饭。（陈仲明／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为何要坚持50年不变？吴伟鸿指，传统习俗自父亲时代已开始，“这么多年顺风顺水，无谓去改变任何事。”不过，全盛时期要办4围，“所有员工都回来吃饭，最亲切。”</p>
<p>近十多年来，老员工退休，新请的员工要出外度假或者回大陆过年，“来吃饭的只是第二代，或者朋友。”虽然来的人少了，但老板依然做足全套，而且强调买菜钱不会节省，由买菜员工“自由发挥”，因为每年第一餐饭一定要吃，“菜不会多，不会少。”</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/18/n10152580.md#1/g21a16591200x1200" rel="attachment wp-att-10152595"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium_vertical wp-image-10152595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/G21A1659@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<h2>去年生意涨一成 展望今年再旺</h2>
<p>谈及海味市道，吴透露，去年市况转好，生意额较前年增加10%。他们客路主要是本地客，不受大陆客人数增减影响，反而受整体经济影响较大，“市况好，市民就吃好一点，鲍参翅肚销情自然旺”。他展望新一年有好生意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10152589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10152589" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/18/n10152580.md#1/g21a14411200x1200" rel="attachment wp-att-10152589"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-10152589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/G21A1441@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10152589" class="wp-caption-text">裕隆海味秉承传统，在大年初二早上食开年饭。（陈仲明／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10152588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/18/n10152580.md#1/g21a14381200x1200" rel="attachment wp-att-10152588"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-10152588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/G21A1438@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10152588" class="wp-caption-text">裕隆海味秉承传统，在大年初二早上食开年饭。（陈仲明／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>相传年三十至年初一众神皆休养生息，至年初二才归位，中国人亦习惯选在这天以意头菜式开年。</p>
<p>另外，年初二，不少家庭也准备开年饭，活鸡必不可少。在九龙城街市，由于来货价较贵，活鸡零售价也较平日高约两成，卖180至220元一斤。</p>
