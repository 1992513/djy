<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>莫斯科法轮功学员庆祝法轮功洪传26周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_05-ss.jpg" />
<h6>5月26日，法轮功学员在莫斯科市中心的鲍曼花园举行游行等活动，庆祝法轮大法日。（明慧网）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月28日讯】2018年5月26日，在位于莫斯科市中心的鲍曼花园（Sad Im. N.e. Baumana）内，法轮功学员与民众共同庆祝“世界法轮大法日”及法轮功洪传26周年。活动结束时公园管理工作人员对法轮功学员表示非常感谢。</p>
<p>自1992年5月13日李洪志先生将法轮功在中国大陆传出至今，法轮功修炼者已遍布世界上百个国家。2000年，世界各国的法轮功学员共同约定，将5月13日作为“世界法轮大法日”。自此，在每年的这个日子的前后，全世界的法轮功学员都会以各种方式庆祝法轮功的洪传。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10434506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10434506" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_01-ss.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10434506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_01-ss-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10434506" class="wp-caption-text">民众阅读法轮功真相展板。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10434507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10434507" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_02-ss.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10434507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_02-ss-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10434507" class="wp-caption-text">民众阅读法轮功真相展板。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>

<p>法轮功学员首先在公园内举行游行，拉开了庆祝活动的序幕。游行队伍由仙女队、功法展示队、腰鼓队和舞龙舞狮队组成。园内游客纷纷拿出手机拍照，有的人在道路两旁喊：“你们太棒了，谢谢你们！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10434513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10434513" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_04-ss.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10434513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_04-ss-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10434513" class="wp-caption-text">法轮功学员在鲍曼花园内的游行活动。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在公园的主要舞台上，主持人向大家介绍了法轮功及其洪传世界的盛况。法轮功学员们展示了法轮功的五套功法动作，同时向大家介绍了法轮功学员在中国遭到的残酷迫害。</p>
<p>在舞台上法轮功学员载歌载舞，表演了“法轮大法好”、“纯洁的莲”、“仙笛”和扇子舞等舞蹈，赢得了阵阵掌声。腰鼓表演、舞龙舞狮更是受到观众的青睐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10434539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10434539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_06-ss.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10434539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_06-ss-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10434539" class="wp-caption-text">在公园的主要舞台上法轮功学员展示功法。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10434540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10434540" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_07-ss.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10434540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_07-ss-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10434540" class="wp-caption-text">在公园的主要舞台上法轮功学员展示功法。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>

<p>舞台旁边设有法轮功信息台，法轮功学员举办了“和平的花瓣”和征签活动。民众纷纷签名支持法轮功学员反迫害，有的人当场就向法轮功学员学炼功法动作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10434552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10434552" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_09-ss.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10434552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-5-27-moscow-hongfa_09-ss-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10434552" class="wp-caption-text">法轮功学员教炼法轮功功法。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
