<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宜兰五结观光大使“小河鸭”亮相</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/9e749a311296a8130c02a4641383f167-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>宜兰五结观光大使“小河鸭”。 （曾汉东／大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月03日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9C%E5%85%B0.md#1">宜兰</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E7%BB%93.md#1">五结</a>乡鸭赏远近驰名，而鸭赏起源于利泽简地区，为连结在地文化特色，五结乡公所特地打造<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%89%E7%A5%A5%E7%89%A9.md#1">吉祥物</a>“小河鸭”，并授职宜兰五结<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">观光</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E4%BD%BF.md#1">大使</a>。5月3日在利泽国小师生及来宾面前正式亮相，由简松树乡长为小河鸭配戴宜兰五结观光大使彩带上任仪式与颁发证书。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10358052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10358052" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/cee39468edf37f1ae0d9fab0198c1d50.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10358052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/cee39468edf37f1ae0d9fab0198c1d50-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10358052" class="wp-caption-text">简松树乡长为小河鸭戴上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9C%E5%85%B0.md#1">宜兰</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E7%BB%93.md#1">五结</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">观光</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E4%BD%BF.md#1">大使</a>彩带上任仪式与颁发证书。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10358053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10358053" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/558268e40dd4e003132a66120cbdd142.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10358053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/558268e40dd4e003132a66120cbdd142-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10358053" class="wp-caption-text">宜兰五结观光大使“小河鸭”与来宾合影。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10358054" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10358054" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/a3afe4fe3d95735a0ab9d1ad5ab540cf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10358054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/a3afe4fe3d95735a0ab9d1ad5ab540cf-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10358054" class="wp-caption-text">利泽国小师生与“小河鸭”。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宜兰县五结乡具有鸭乡美称，从早期在小河道行走的交通工具“鸭母船”到现今的名产“鸭赏”，都是过往生活遗留的无形文化，有感于在地特有文化逐渐飘零，为唤醒早期居民生活回忆并与现代生活并行，五结乡公所以鸭子作为原形设计制作一款鸭乡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%89%E7%A5%A5%E7%89%A9.md#1">吉祥物</a>小河鸭，由乡长授职宜兰五结观光大使。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10358055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10358055" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4d47e0b3613678b4b1003306f910ad02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10358055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4d47e0b3613678b4b1003306f910ad02-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10358055" class="wp-caption-text">利泽国小小朋友高兴的与“小河鸭”互动。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10358057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10358057" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/963a51f87b5f1f4a61741c7cdccf102d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10358057" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/963a51f87b5f1f4a61741c7cdccf102d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10358057" class="wp-caption-text">利泽国小小朋友“画我鸭乡”彩绘鸭子。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10358060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10358060" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/6da490cf0019475a050ab7f6232727c9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10358060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/6da490cf0019475a050ab7f6232727c9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10358060" class="wp-caption-text">利泽国小小朋友“画我鸭乡”彩绘鸭子。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>五结乡长简松树表示，宜兰五结观光大使吉祥物“小河鸭”，将于台北国际观光博览会、宜兰国际童玩节乡镇日活动中，与游客、外国团队交流互动，行销五结观光产业，提升五结观光能见度，让在地文化与观光具有特色话题，一同推广五结乡提升品牌意象。</p>
<p>同时为庆祝“小河鸭”上任，五结乡公所特别举办“画我鸭乡”彩绘鸭子系列活动做为开幕，邀请五结乡内国小共同参与，透过竞赛的方式让学童对于小河鸭有所认识，奖品除了奖金之外，乡内伴手礼业者也一同共襄盛举，提供特色伴手礼作为奖项。◇</p>
