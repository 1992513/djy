<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>躲进机车喵喵叫 台湾人自掏腰包救流浪小猫</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: center;"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10327568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="38" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月28日讯】（大纪元记者陈紫岚综合报导）有缘千里来相会，善心男骑士上班途中发现流浪小猫咪躲在自己的机车里，便自掏腰包救出小猫，路边民众也好心提供猫罐头给阿咪吃，一群人共同拯救一只小猫咪超暖心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10425382" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10425382" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-144.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10425382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-144-600x602.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10425382" class="wp-caption-text">民众骑机车前往公司的路途上，听到有猫叫声，“奇怪？怎么叫声越来越大？”（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10425385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10425385" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-115.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10425385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-115-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10425385" class="wp-caption-text">“啊！该不会是阿咪躲在车子里吧！？”他将机车牵去车行请老板帮忙找猫。（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2018年5月10日下午2时许，一位民众骑着机车准备去上班，发动车子前往公司的路途上，听到周围有猫叫声，“嗯？怎么一直有叫声？”好像是身旁的机车骑士有载猫，就继续骑啊骑，“奇怪？怎么叫声越来越大？”</p>
<p>骑士放慢速度，停靠路边找寻猫，但停下来之后叫声就停了，也不见猫影，所以又开始骑，但骑没多久又听到猫的叫声，“啊！该不会是阿咪躲在车子里吧！？”他就将车子熄火，牵去车行请老板帮忙找猫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10425390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10425390" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-83.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10425390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-83-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10425390" class="wp-caption-text">老板也不知从何开始找起，只好从龙头开始开壳，拆开了之后，不见猫影。（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到了车行后，猫咪不叫了，老板也不知从何开始找起，只好从龙头开始开壳，拆开了之后，不见猫影，只好再拆座椅下的车壳，才刚拔起坐垫，就看见一只可爱小猫咪躲在那里。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">观看完整影片?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-23b9fcaa-5ca6-4ed8-5152-cdb43fbff88e" data-id="player-23b9fcaa-5ca6-4ed8-5152-cdb43fbff88e"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/23b9fcaa-5ca6-4ed8-5152-cdb43fbff88e?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F5%2F25%2Fn10425214.md#1"></a></p>
<p>它睁着大眼睛、楚楚可怜的样子，看起来是惊吓过度。原来是因为小猫咪躲在机车引擎旁，车子一发动就会发热，过几分钟后温度渐高，小猫叫声越来越频繁，幸好男骑士没骑太远，赶紧停车熄火牵去车行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10425391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10425391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4-68.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10425391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4-68-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10425391" class="wp-caption-text">老板拆座椅下的车壳，才刚拔起坐垫，就看见一只可爱小猫咪躲在那里。（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10425394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10425394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/6-43.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10425394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/6-43-600x602.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10425394" class="wp-caption-text">小猫说什么也不肯出来，害怕、担心的模样令人心疼。（Tseng Chian Kai提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>老板伸手试着靠近小猫，将它抱出来，但因它反抗作势要咬人不肯出来，老板只好继续拆车壳，终于能看到小猫的全貌，是一只没几个月大的猫宝贝。但是小猫说什么也不肯出来，害怕、担心的模样令人心疼。老板和骑士讨论说先牵到旁边放一放，让小猫情绪平复下来，说不定就会自己跑出来了。</p>
<p>然而，等了一阵，或许是小猫咪实在太害怕，而且躲在那边比较有安全感，一直不愿离开。</p>
<p>不过，台湾真是处处有爱心，路过的民众前来关心，并表示自己家里有养宠物，愿意试试看协助安抚小猫咪，并顺利抱出来；另一位民众则去认识的商家索取一罐猫咪罐头饲料，让这只流浪小猫出来后能吃点东西，饱餐一顿收收惊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10425393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10425393" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-59.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10425393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-59-600x601.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10425393" class="wp-caption-text">路过民众表示自己家里有养宠物，愿意试试看协助安抚小猫咪，并顺利抱出来。（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10425397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10425397" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/7-32.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10425397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/7-32-600x622.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="622" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10425397" class="wp-caption-text">另一位民众拿了一罐猫咪罐头饲料，让这只流浪小猫饱餐一顿收收惊。（Tseng Chian Kai提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大家同心协力救出这只可爱小猫，希望这只小猫咪别再跑进机车里面了～一不小心很可能受伤的！这次幸好遇到一群好心人，果然台湾处处人情味呀！</p>
