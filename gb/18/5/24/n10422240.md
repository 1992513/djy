<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鸟也懂得存钱 还不喜欢小钱？路人惊叹</h2>
<a href="https://d1ff3ajoaf61nd.cloudfront.net/W1oD1KHbl"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月25日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F.md#1">鸟</a>类的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E6%8A%80.md#1">特技</a>，各位看过哪些呢？“学说话”、“跳舞”、“飞鸽传书”……和狗狗相比，好像比较少特技。</p>
<p>在这就给大家展示一个“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F.md#1">鸟</a>鸟绝技”！这段拍摄于北京街头的影片中有一只鸟，还有一只<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E7%8C%AA%E5%85%AC.md#1">大猪公</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%98%E9%92%B1.md#1">存钱</a>罐，上面有“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%9B%E8%B4%A2%E8%BF%9B%E5%AE%9D.md#1">招财进宝</a>”组成的大大的字。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10424379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10424379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fullsizeoutput_73.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10424379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fullsizeoutput_73-600x367.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10424379" class="wp-caption-text">小鸟叼着一枚硬币。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个是要<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%98%E9%92%B1.md#1">存钱</a>？让路人“随喜”投钱？嗯，这样可能一整天都存不到钱钱，那到底是怎么存呢？答案就是靠影片中那只神奇的“存钱鸟”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10424595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10424595" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Bird.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10424595 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Bird-600x319.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10424595" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看似平凡的鸟，在地上跳呀跳。唔，突然有民众伸出一只手，上面有硬币。鸟鸟见状，马上跳到手前。不到一秒，头往下低，立刻就叼在嘴上了，竟然不会“滑掉”，迅即转身跳跳跳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10424385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10424385" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fullsizeoutput_72.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10424385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fullsizeoutput_72-600x353.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="353" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10424385" class="wp-caption-text">小鸟要将钱币投入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E7%8C%AA%E5%85%AC.md#1">大猪公</a>里啰。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大家期待的一刻到了，鸟鸟跳到大猪公面，展示叼着“盾牌”的英姿，转身一圈，嘿！将钱币投入大猪公上的“小裂缝”，实在是太厉害啦！竟然是只会存钱的鸟！</p>
<p>观众试了好多次，都是同一结果，可见这只鸟鸟真是训练有素。有路人直呼：“太聪明啦！”“怎么训的？”</p>
<p>据《每日邮报》报导，主人介绍说，鸟鸟不喜欢1毛以下的硬币。有的路人试探地提供纸币，它却不感兴趣，路人于是笑着收起。</p>
