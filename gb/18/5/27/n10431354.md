<h6>研究人员发现多数袋鼠是左撇子。(AFP)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月31日讯】澳洲是个特别的地方，哪里特别呢？也就是野生动物常常会出现在你的日常生活。</p>
<p>视野一片漆黑，借着微弱的灯光，看到一只一蹦一跳的袋鼠，怕打扰到它只能放慢车速的缓慢前进，以为这就是故事的结局，但事情不是观众想的这么简单。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10431501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10431501" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/a838b3d7aa06d96cd1bd309be16532ec.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10431501 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/a838b3d7aa06d96cd1bd309be16532ec-600x465.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="465" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10431501" class="wp-caption-text">视野一片漆黑，借着微弱的灯光，看到一只一蹦一跳的袋鼠，怕打扰到它只能放慢车速的缓慢前进。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当驾驶还在惊叹在夜晚遇到可爱袋鼠的时候，袋鼠也从原本的在车子右侧，逐步的变换到车子的左侧，并逐渐的放慢脚步，最后甚至转向车驾驶，不断在原地弹跳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10431500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10431500" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/11c47dcfca5ea039828c2dbdcad1d8f7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10431500 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/11c47dcfca5ea039828c2dbdcad1d8f7-600x527.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10431500" class="wp-caption-text">袋鼠突然的转身，让驾驶也将车子停下。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>袋鼠突然间停下转身与驾驶面对面，先是站稳脚步，接着抬头挺胸的架高自己的胸膛，双手紧靠在身前，驾驶试着前进想要驱赶袋鼠，但袋鼠仍然一动也不动，而且一脸怒气的看着车驾驶，虽然驾驶一脸黑人问号觉得莫名其妙。</p>
<p>出手就在下一秒！袋鼠紧握车边的杆子，身体一蹦一跳再对车盖打个几下，可能用铁做的车盖让它打的有点痛，也让它意识到这个大家伙好像不简单。</p>
<p>逐步退后，紧握它疼痛的双手，一脸镇定得看着车驾驶，即便相当害怕，但仍然泰山崩于前而色不变，紧盯着车驾驶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10431497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10431497" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-158.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10431497 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-158-600x485.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="485" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10431497" class="wp-caption-text">袋鼠转身与驾驶面对面，先是站稳脚步，接着抬头挺胸的架高自己的胸膛，双手紧靠在身前。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10431499" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10431499" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/605ce087ce4d2eef8e59cf8cb4c3c847.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10431499 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/605ce087ce4d2eef8e59cf8cb4c3c847-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10431499" class="wp-caption-text">袋鼠紧握车边的杆子，身体一蹦一跳再对车盖打个几下。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但表情相当霸气，身体已经不断的向后退，随着驾驶持续前进，袋鼠也转头跳入草丛了。</p>
<p>只能说虽然小袋鼠萌萌的很可爱，但成年袋鼠其实相当具有攻击性，也常常发生袋鼠攻击人的事件，而不乱喂食也是避免袋鼠攻击人事件的最好办法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10431498" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10431498" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/36190152b7f5c1188aff3ea519acffbe.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-10431498 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/36190152b7f5c1188aff3ea519acffbe-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10431498" class="wp-caption-text">驾驶试着前进想要驱赶袋鼠，但袋鼠仍然一动也不动，而且一脸怒气的看着车驾驶，虽然驾驶一脸黑人问号觉得莫名其妙。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
