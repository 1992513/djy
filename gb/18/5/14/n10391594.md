<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>认识传统及在地文化 别开生面的母亲节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/04c61fd50d78540b244c88c182929ca2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>与会长官合影。（卢桂莲／大纪元） </h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞云林报导）斗南镇人文协会5月13日于斗南他里雾文化园区举办一场别开生面的母亲节活动，除表达对母亲的感恩祝福外更结合影片欣赏、闯关游戏及中国服走秀等活动，丰富的内容，带领民众认识传统文化并了解他里雾文化园区的整体风貌。</p>
<p>斗南镇人文协会理事长卢桂莲表示，这场活动特别选在他里雾文化园区举办实则有特别意义，一方面让斗南民众透过活动走进园区68电影馆、环境教育馆外，也让大家认识中华传统文化。因此我们结合“新唐人亚太电视台云林办事处”将中华传统文化、品德教育，藉由“中国服走秀”、“弟子规3D动画”的播放，寓教于乐！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10391601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10391601" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/edb759e8491e14d7a5fe316bf099d70f.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10391601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/edb759e8491e14d7a5fe316bf099d70f-450x282.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="282"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10391601" class="wp-caption-text">与会来宾大合照。（卢桂莲／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次由“诺贝儿手染中国服”提供独特的中国服走秀，充分展现棉、麻、丝、绸材质的舒适感，与典雅的中国服装风格；改良式的手染中国服的创新设计，更是轻松又舒适，呈现的是细致大方的穿着艺术之美！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10391603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10391603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5cd97a4edf55396e90e47fa119acd38e.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10391603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5cd97a4edf55396e90e47fa119acd38e-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10391603" class="wp-caption-text">改良式的手染中国服的创新设计，穿起来更舒适大方！ （卢桂莲／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10391596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10391596" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/84a0eb0bc26a9d00b2ce379a099377bc.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10391596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/84a0eb0bc26a9d00b2ce379a099377bc-450x341.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="341"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10391596" class="wp-caption-text">走秀的素人模特儿大合照。（卢桂莲／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>特别的是这些素人模特儿轻松自在的台步，显得端庄稳重，脱俗的气质秀出女性自信之美。是一场服装艺术的视觉飨宴，让现场来宾度过一场温馨而感人的母亲节。而环境教育馆的闯关活动，只见大家在四个关卡中铆足了劲、亲子合作无间，笑声此起彼落。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10391598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10391598" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/91d062f00d13dfbdbdfe6eb75cac2a6e.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10391598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/91d062f00d13dfbdbdfe6eb75cac2a6e-450x283.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="283"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10391598" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友进行闯关游戏。（卢桂莲／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10391600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10391600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5740de36bc332667a4dd50a3d79f8bd5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10391600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5740de36bc332667a4dd50a3d79f8bd5-450x333.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="333"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10391600" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友进行拼图闯关活动。 （卢桂莲／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云林县政府教育处梁恩嘉处长致词说，每位妈妈都是最美丽的，每位妈妈都是家庭的核心，因为她们无私的爱是让人感动的；藉由活动可以把爱把和道德注入家庭，是很有意义的活动！</p>
<p>他里雾文化园区是早期斗南火车站的仓库，因时代的变迁早已功成身退，原本冰冷的仓库，经过活化改造后成了斗南的特色景点，透过这次活动，让来宾及在地人对园区的功能和内容留下深刻印象，丰富的文化资源及完善的软硬体，让不少在地民众惊讶的说：我是斗南人竟然不知道有这么一个美丽又有文化内涵的地方！◇</p>
