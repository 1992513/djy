<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿清洁日闪亮华埠街区</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-1Shine-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>妇女新运会资深会员们热心服务社区，前排左一为主席李卢莲馨。(黄剑宇/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月11日讯】（大纪元记者黄剑宇<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）?5月5日上午9点，天晴暖和，数十名华人、西人的社区成员和志愿者们聚集在中国城牌坊，参加<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>主街组织的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>地区“闪亮<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%85%E6%B4%81%E6%97%A5.md#1">清洁日</a>”(Helping Boston Shine One Block at a Time)活动。</p>
<p>他们手拿扫把、垃圾斗、铁铲等清洁工具，身穿统一写有英文“Love Your Block”(爱你的街区)蓝色心形标志的白衣，或者自己社团的衣服，分布在各个街区，辛勤卖力的打扫清洁，力争把唐人街变得更闪亮、更洁净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10384738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10384738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-2Shine-e1526048694970.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10384738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-2Shine-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10384738" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员费连携带妻子及女儿、儿子，一起动手扫地。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10384741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10384741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-3Shine-e1526048741127.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10384741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-3Shine-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10384741" class="wp-caption-text">黄光野和GE通用电气志愿者(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10384743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10384743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-4Shine-e1526048785703.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10384743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-4Shine-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10384743" class="wp-caption-text">义工报到后领取，身穿印有“Love Your Block”(爱你的街区)蓝色心形标志的白衣。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10384745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10384745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-5Shine-e1526048834248.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10384745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-3-5Shine-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10384745" class="wp-caption-text">大家努力共同清洁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>华埠主街董事何远光介绍说，当天参加<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%85%E6%B4%81%E6%97%A5.md#1">清洁日</a>活动的社团有华埠居民会、纽英崙妇女新运会、黄氏宗亲会、GE(通用电气公司)志愿者、塔夫茨医学院的志愿者，还有耶鲁大学的志愿者等。其中GE的志愿者们是黄氏宗亲会黄光野退休前所在的通用电气公司的职员和朋友。</p>
<p>妇女新运会主席李卢莲馨表示，好些年轻人周六还要上班不能来，所以她带了新运会的一些阿姨们来参加支持这次的社区活动。后来，年轻一辈的会员Irene Ruan(东波士顿储蓄银行)也赶来参加。</p>
<p>波士顿市议员费连不但自己参加，还携带妻子及女儿、儿子，一起动手扫地，为这次有意义的美化波士顿华埠尽一份心。</p>
<p>本次活动，APM Coffee赞助了咖啡，长兴豆腐赞助了豆浆饮料。◇</p>
