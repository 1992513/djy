<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台电公司中秋前夕与乡亲携手净滩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/b690547c528a85eec9563ba9b9dd729e-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>通霄发电厂厂长李明道致词感谢大家热情参与。（通霄发电厂提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年09月26日讯】（大纪元记者陈丽燕台湾苗栗报导）台电公司26日中秋节前夕办理全台同步净滩活动，通霄发电厂此次与苗栗区营业处共同举办，净滩范围包括该厂厂外附近道路、西南角海滩。台电公司副总经理王耀庭专程南下参与；通霄发电厂动员员工、通东、通西、平安里里民、环保义工及通霄镇镇长陈汉志、地方政要人士等425人共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12432284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12432284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/e8915029611ad13597f28bd3b725f48d.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-12432284 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/e8915029611ad13597f28bd3b725f48d-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12432284" class="wp-caption-text">净滩前来段劲歌热舞再出发。（通霄发电厂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12432286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12432286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/fab3ee9bf8a45ca0bdee991e6f183c3f.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-12432286 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/fab3ee9bf8a45ca0bdee991e6f183c3f-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12432286" class="wp-caption-text">大家一起动手清洁海岸。（通霄发电厂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12432287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12432287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/46f067fd3f9eb141f909da571df5f61e.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-12432287 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/46f067fd3f9eb141f909da571df5f61e-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12432287" class="wp-caption-text">透过实际参与净滩，亲身感受海洋废弃物的严重性。（通霄发电厂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>通霄发电厂厂长李明道致词表示：台电从1994年起，迄今已26年，为维护滨海地区海滩的清洁，每年中秋节前夕都会发起净滩活动，是非常有意义的，期盼每个人能珍惜自然资源，还给海岸原有的清洁风貌。</p>
<p>今年净滩主题为“不只‘捡’更要‘减’”，台电公司说明，台湾四面环海，海洋环境除了让我们拥有引以为傲的美景，更与我们的生活息息相关，希望藉由净滩活动，带动更多人投入永续海洋的实质行动。</p>
<p>台电公司过去在办理净滩活动上不遗余力，由实际的参与，提升社会大众对环境保护的重视，更获得环保署认证成为“净滩场次最多”的单位，希望能与民众为守护海洋环境一同努力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12432380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12432380" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/461707.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12432380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/461707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12432380" class="wp-caption-text">净滩还给海岸原有的清洁风貌。（通霄发电厂提供）</figcaption></figure>
