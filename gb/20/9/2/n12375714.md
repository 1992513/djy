<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：旅途中</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1505162043352039.jpg" />
<h6>(Fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>真紧，去得真紧<br />
真紧，彼些倒退而去的<br />
窗外的风景</p>
<p>包括彼些山水树木花草……<br />
包括彼些幼年、童年、青年……<br />
彼些风景，真紧<br />
倒退入历史，成为回忆</p>
<p>亲像一支射出的<br />
箭，真紧<br />
去得真紧</p>
<p>举头向前看<br />
头前还有<br />
多少空间？还有<br />
多少风雨和金阳？</p>
<p><strong>En Route</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>It’s truly quick, it’s truly quick pass away<br />
It’s truly quick, it’s truly quick go backwards<br />
The landscape which outside the window</p>
<p>Includes those mountains water trees and flowers and grasses…<br />
Includes those childhood boyhood and youth…<br />
Those landscapes, truly quick<br />
Go backwards into history, form into reminiscences</p>
<p>Likes a shooting<br />
Arrow, truly quick<br />
Truly quick pass away</p>
<p>Lift my head and look forwards<br />
In front of me<br />
Is it still how much space left? Still<br />
How many wind and rain and golden sunshine left? @</p>
