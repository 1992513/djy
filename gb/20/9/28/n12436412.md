<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>煮这一锅省下煮好几道菜！2款简易西式炖肉作法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7f59dd5fb8120e2919d9e9e8c2fc435c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香喷喷、入口即化的马铃薯炖鸡肉，这在西方是常见的家常菜。（野人文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：不用高超的厨艺，只要学会炖肉，煮一锅可以省下煮好几道菜，而且超级下饭，料理简易快速又兼具美味、营养等多重好处。</p>
<p>传统炖肉，会使用姜、酱油、蒜等食材，西式炖肉大多会增加香料、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>等材料。如果家里有小孩，想变化一点花样，可以多方尝试欧式炖肉的作法，也可以根据家人的口味适度调配调味料。</p>
<p>下面的食谱来自厨艺经验丰富的水瓶妈妈，为了给家人制作暖心的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%BF%E5%BD%93.md#1">便当</a>所推荐的食谱，可以试着做看看！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>炖鸡</span></h2>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">材料</span></h2>
<p>去骨鸡腿?400g<br />
黑木耳?100g<br />
马铃薯?300g<br />
红萝卜?100g<br />
洋葱?150g<br />
葱末?少许</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">调味</span></h2>
<p>金兰松露酱油?40ml<br />
玉泰白酱油?30ml<br />
本味醂?30ml<br />
清酒?60ml<br />
白胡椒粉?1/4 小匙<br />
大红袍花椒粉?1/4 小匙<br />
清水?150ml</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<p>1. 鸡腿切成适口大小，黑木耳切片、洋葱切大丁，马铃薯及红萝卜皆去皮切块。<br />
2. 起油锅，冷油开始炒洋葱，闻到香气后加入红萝卜及黑木耳大略翻炒盛起备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12436472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12436472" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/36-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12436472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/36-1-450x310.jpg" alt="洋葱" width="450" b="310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12436472" class="wp-caption-text">起油锅，冷油开始炒洋葱。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12436473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12436473" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/36-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12436473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/36-2-450x311.jpg" alt="红萝卜,黑木耳" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12436473" class="wp-caption-text">加入红萝卜及黑木耳大略翻炒盛起备用。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 原锅将鸡肉半煎半炒至断生，加入作法2 及马铃薯，依序将调味料一一入锅，最后加入清水煮滚加盖转小火炖煮30 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12436474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12436474" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/36-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12436474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/36-3-450x312.jpg" alt="鸡肉" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12436474" class="wp-caption-text">将鸡肉半煎半炒至断生。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12436475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12436475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/36-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12436475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/36-4-450x313.jpg" alt="炖煮" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12436475" class="wp-caption-text">最后加入清水煮滚加盖转小火炖煮30 分钟。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;">香料<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>炖肉</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12436421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12436421" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/c51e3a966d2b70ce548a1ca038ecffb3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12436421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/c51e3a966d2b70ce548a1ca038ecffb3-450x527.jpg" alt="香料蕃茄" width="450" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12436421" class="wp-caption-text">暖呼呼的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%BF%E5%BD%93.md#1">便当</a>～香料<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%95%83%E8%8C%84%E7%82%96%E8%82%89.md#1">蕃茄炖肉</a>。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">材料</span></h2>
<p>猪梅花肉前段?1000g（切块）<br />
洋葱?2 颗<br />
葱白?5 支<br />
牛番茄?3 颗（去皮切块）<br />
有机香料番茄块罐头?1 罐</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">调味料</span></h2>
<p>冰糖粉?1.5 大匙<br />
玉泰白酱油?4 大匙<br />
米酒?6 大匙<br />
泰国柠檬叶?3 片<br />
黑胡椒粉?适量</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<p>1. 起油锅加入2大匙热炒油（份量外），从冷油开始炒香洋葱，闻到香气后投入葱白继续拌炒至香气四溢，炒好的洋葱及葱白先盛出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12436417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12436417" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/74-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12436417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/74-1-450x312.jpg" alt="洋葱" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12436417" class="wp-caption-text">起油锅加入2大匙热炒油（份量外），从冷油开始炒香洋葱。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 同一锅子补上1大匙热炒油（份量外），将肉块入锅以中大火煎至上色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12436418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12436418" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/74-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12436418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/74-2-450x313.jpg" alt="肉块" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12436418" class="wp-caption-text">肉块入锅以中大火煎至上色。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 作法1投入作法2，加糖拌炒，糖融化后续加入白酱油、米酒、柠檬叶翻炒均匀。<br />
4. 投入牛番茄、罐头番茄块，加盖煮至冒出白烟，转成小火炖煮90分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12436420" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12436420" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/74-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12436420" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/74-4-450x312.jpg" alt="番茄" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12436420" class="wp-caption-text">投入牛番茄、罐头番茄块，加盖煮至冒出白烟，转成小火炖煮90分钟。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. ?起锅前添加适量黑胡椒粉即完成。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">料理笔记</span></h2>
<p>如果买到无调味番茄块罐头，请再额外添加些许海盐及普罗旺斯综合香料或意大利香料。</p>
