<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉市府于9处大型路口增设高灯 让夜路更明亮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/72ed7d662d93d0de8ad3987c7b557d73-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义市世贤路与博爱路口_施工后。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年09月16日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）当夜幕低垂时，谁都希望路途是明亮的，嘉义市政府工务处办理“高灯计划”，将9处大型路口加设比一般路灯更高的高灯，使照明范围与亮度皆为提升，让夜晚的道路更为明亮，使夜归的用路人都能平安回家。</p>
<figure id="12407110" aria-describedby="caption-12407110" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/12ea80b389f3972cc82c102c38d16c62-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/12ea80b389f3972cc82c102c38d16c62-450x337.jpg" alt="嘉义市世贤路与博爱路口_施工前。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12407110" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市世贤路与博爱路口_施工前。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了解架设高灯的需求，市府工务处向警察局调阅108年度18时至06时、前50处易肇事路口交通事故统计数据，于夜间派员至现场勘查、评估；并陆续于9处可加装高灯大型路口增设高灯。</p>
<figure id="12407112" aria-describedby="caption-12407112" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/e9fe2fb61e51e9a956cf1d738bba1380-450x327.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/e9fe2fb61e51e9a956cf1d738bba1380-450x327.jpg" alt="兴达路与八德路口_施工前。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12407112" class="wp-caption-text">兴达路与八德路口_施工前。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12407113" aria-describedby="caption-12407113" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/71bda7c7ee20371b1db0adf610976c7c-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/71bda7c7ee20371b1db0adf610976c7c-450x338.jpg" alt="兴达路与八德路口_施工后。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12407113" class="wp-caption-text">兴达路与八德路口_施工后。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>已完成高灯增设路口分别有：健康九路与新民路口、兴达路与八德路口、弥陀路与兴业东路口、世贤路与博爱路口、民族路共和路与光华路口、垂杨路与新民路口、新生路与博东路口，而目前正在进行施作的路口则有玉山路与博爱路口、博爱路与兴业西路口，总计共9处路口、加设或更换高灯共15杆，总计60个灯具。</p>
<figure id="12407114" aria-describedby="caption-12407114" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/62c63026d6032bffbcb89659743931d1-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/62c63026d6032bffbcb89659743931d1-450x337.jpg" alt="弥陀路与兴业东路口_施工前。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12407114" class="wp-caption-text">弥陀路与兴业东路口_施工前。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12407115" aria-describedby="caption-12407115" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/c95c012b4fd69d139be5474aa6c069c0-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/c95c012b4fd69d139be5474aa6c069c0-450x338.jpg" alt="弥陀路与兴业东路口_施工后。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12407115" class="wp-caption-text">弥陀路与兴业东路口_施工后。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>工务处表示，相较于一般路灯高度约7.5米左右、一根灯杆平均装设1至2个灯具，增设的高灯高度约为10米，一根灯杆可装设4至8个灯具。高灯计划不但大幅增加了照明范围与亮度，明亮的夜晚也兼具美化市容的效果，让夜归的市民朋友能够平安返家。</p>
