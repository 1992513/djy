<h2><strong>槭叶牵牛Ipomoea cairica (L.) Sweet旋花科</strong></h2>
<p>又称碗公花、番仔藤、台湾牵牛花、五爪金龙、五爪龙、上竹龙、牵牛藤、黑牵牛、假土瓜藤。为藤本植物，具有平滑、纤细的缠绕性匍匐茎，长可达十几公尺。单叶互生，掌状全裂；裂片5~7枚，叶形像槭叶所以称为槭叶牵牛花。表面绿色，背面淡绿色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12408508" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12408508" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/399452_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12408508" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/399452_medium-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12408508" class="wp-caption-text">花为淡紫红色，直径可达8公分，形状很像一支小喇叭。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12408510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12408510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/399454_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12408510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/399454_medium-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12408510" class="wp-caption-text">朝颜迎风笑脸开，微凉秋风轻拂来，万种风情娇欲滴，蜂儿勤快把花采。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>槭叶牵牛属于很常见的野花，生长在平地到低海拔山区的林缘，在乡下，甚至在城市较空旷处或围篱、路旁等地方都可见到它的芳踪。花为淡紫红色，直径可达8公分，形状很像一支小喇叭。可做绿篱、绿廊、地被，美化用，盛夏时期，它的花朵多得犹如一片紫色花海，蔓延在堤边、野地，甚至在许多人家的墙垣、篱笆上，既是艳丽的观赏植物，也是夏季极佳的凉棚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12408509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12408509" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/399453_medium1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12408509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/399453_medium1-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12408509" class="wp-caption-text">可做绿篱、绿廊、地被，美化用，盛夏时期，它的花朵多得犹如一片紫色花海。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
