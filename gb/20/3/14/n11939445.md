<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>坐拥湖景的新屋 成熟社区的方便</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-walk-along-the-lake-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>卡城西北Arbour Lake社区。（建筑商Hopewell提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月14日讯】想坐拥湖景而不又需远行吗？在华人圈中炙手可热的卡城西北Arbour Lake社区，正在兴建的Arbour Lake West by Hopewell为你圆梦。</p>
<p>本是成熟社区的Arbour Lake已有年不兴土木，如今新屋再建，机不可失，时不再来！</p>
<p>650套公寓和镇屋，起价分别为20余万和45万；139套独立屋，起价60余万，其中9套为平层出入地库。</p>
<p>房主可自由享受广达10英亩，相当于10个足球场大小的湖景，以及湖周一应公园设施。夏季游泳、划船、钓鱼、沙滩漫步、湖畔烧烤；冬天溜冰、堆雪、赏景、耍玩冰球、沐浴暖阳……一年四季开放，社区独有福利。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-lake.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11939477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-lake-450x333.jpg" alt="卡城西北Arbour Lake社区" width="450" b="333" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-fishing.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-11939478 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-fishing-450x450.jpg" alt="卡城西北Arbour Lake社区" width="450" b="450" /></a></p>
<p>步行可至Crowfoot Crossing购物中心，200余家店面，涵盖美食、休闲、文化、医疗、娱乐。紧邻Crowfoot轻轨站，走路即到，坐上轻轨，三站至卡尔加里大学，六站到SAIT，八站进入卡城市中心。</p>
<p>社区内已建有三所中小学校： St. Ambrose School(K-9)、Arbour Lake School (5-9) 、 Robert Thirsk High School (10-12) 。</p>
<p>触手可及的奢华，近在咫尺的方便。</p>
<p>我们并不只是在建造房屋，我们在构筑你的家园。──Arbour Lake West by Hopewell</p>
<p>公寓：两种单元，725平方英尺（1卧室，2卫生间）和1247平方英尺（2卧室，2卫生间）。阳台有顶，夏挡烈日，冬隔落雪。转角单元透过大片落地窗，落基山景扑面而来，连绵不绝，犹如天然画墙。楼高五层，楼顶设休闲花园，可聊天，可读书，可品茗，可烧烤。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-bedroom.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11939481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-bedroom-450x329.jpg" alt="卡城西北Arbour Lake社区" width="450" b="329" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-bathroom.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11939482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-bathroom-450x329.jpg" alt="卡城西北Arbour Lake社区" width="450" b="329" /></a></p>
<p>镇屋和独立屋：9英尺天花板，8磅耐用涤纶地毯，22立方英尺冰箱，不锈钢厨具，E-star洗碗机，石英台面，高效供暖炉，低辐射双层隔音玻璃窗，数字温度控制器，高效耐用节能灯泡，节水厕所， 土壤气体控制系统(将通过土壤自然产生的污染气体排出房屋)……</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-living-room.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11939483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-living-room-450x329.jpg" alt="卡城西北Arbour Lake社区" width="450" b="329" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-dinner-area.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11939484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Arbour-Lake-West-by-Hopewell-dinner-area-450x329.jpg" alt="卡城西北Arbour Lake社区" width="450" b="329" /></a></p>

公寓展示屋现已在Arbour Lake社区开放，镇屋和独立屋的展示屋可到其它Hopewell开发的社区参观，详情请向销售代表查询。</p>
