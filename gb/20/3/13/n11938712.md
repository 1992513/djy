<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】荷香蟹肉糯米饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/e949e69ea65cf72c778891755a179f87-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的荷香蟹肉糯米饭，糯米清香软滑，加入鲜美无比的蟹肉浓汤后，蟹的鲜美和荷叶的清香融合，口感更加诱人。（视频截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>荷香蟹肉糯米饭是传统名菜，以蟹、糯米为主要材料，加入适量调味，放置荷叶上清蒸而成。蟹肉含有丰富的蛋白质、钙及锌，对身体有滋补作用，与糯米同蒸，原汁原味品尝，是受欢迎的美食。</p>
<p>自古以来，蟹被视为“水产三珍”，历代美食家对其称颂备至。宋代的大文豪苏东坡嗜蟹成癖，就是在仕途失意时仍不忘此物，发出“不到庐山辜负目，不食螃蟹辜负腹”的喟叹。</p>
<p>蟹肉不仅含有丰富的蛋白质，而且氨基酸种类齐全，含有丰富的脂肪和不饱和脂肪酸，特别是人体内不能合成的ω-3脂肪酸、EPA、DHA含量高。其中EPA可明显降低血清中甘油三脂和胆固醇值，抗血小板聚集，延缓血栓形成，防治心脑血管疾病及前列腺增生，而DHA可促进脑发育，增强脑的学习记忆能力，防止脑老化。 另外，蟹肉还是低钠食品，适于高血压患者食用。</p>
<p>糯米味甘性温，归脾胃肺经。中医认为，食用糯米有三种功效：补中益气、祛寒补虚、防尿频盗汗；糯米还可以补气，帮助身体发热御寒，也能滋补脾胃、促进食欲；糯米也具收涩（收敛固涩）作用，所以对于尿频或盗汗有一定的食疗效果。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道蟹肉糯米饭，蟹的鲜美和荷叶的清香融合，糯米清香软滑，加入鲜美无比的蟹肉浓汤后，糯米饭口感更加诱人。</p>
<h2>蟹肉糯米饭</h2>
<p>主料：螃蟹4只、浸过一夜的糯米1.25杯约20汤匙。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/889bc2f88ab35a184f65daaf085d561e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/889bc2f88ab35a184f65daaf085d561e-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ecb9efc1701ee380969749d2ec2eba47.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ecb9efc1701ee380969749d2ec2eba47-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：姜片、葱段（2根葱切段）、熟南瓜3汤匙。<br />
调料：糖0.5茶匙、盐1茶匙、胡椒粉1/3茶匙、香油0.5茶匙、生粉水4汤匙。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 湿糯米加入调料搅拌后放入荷叶筒中，大火蒸25分钟至熟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1-30.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1-30-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 蒸熟的螃蟹4只，拆肉待用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 用油爆香姜片、葱段，再把蟹壳炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3-17.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 放入约6碗水，慢火煮成蟹汁浓汤。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 用漏斗过滤，变成纯蟹浓汤。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 放入熟南瓜粒。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 加入盐、胡椒粉、糖，再用生粉水勾芡成浓芡汤。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 将蒸熟的荷叶糯米饭取出，拆除荷叶。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 将蟹肉和蟹黄放在糯米顶部。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/9-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/9-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 然后用青菜摆边。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/10-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/10-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 最后，将蟹肉浓汤淋在糯米饭上即可。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/11-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11959584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/11-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
