<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>辛龙停工照顾刘真 阿Ken打气祝福早日康复</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030535072384-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>阿Ken（林暐恒）（左）、纪培慧（右）3月3日在台北出席电影《练爱iNG》记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年03月03日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）“国标舞女王”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E7%9C%9F.md#1">刘真</a>因心脏开刀，正在排队等待心脏移植，丈夫<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%9B%E9%BE%99.md#1">辛龙</a>全面停工专心陪伴；好友<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BFken.md#1">阿Ken</a>（林暐恒）今天（3日）出席自编自导自演的新电影《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%83%E7%88%B1ing.md#1">练爱iNG</a>》记者会，也特别为刘真加油打气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11912305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11912305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030535502384-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11912305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030535502384-ss1.jpg" alt="阿Ken（林暐恒）" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11912305" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BFken.md#1">阿Ken</a>（林暐恒）为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E7%9C%9F.md#1">刘真</a>加油打气。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%9B%E9%BE%99.md#1">辛龙</a>在片中饰演剧组制片，他原定要出席记者会，但因忙于照顾妻子与女儿而缺席，由陈怡?（Yuri）上阵。</p>
<p>导演阿Ken透露，早在年初就已经安排好电影宣传跟档期，“那时候还不知道状况，可能连辛龙哥都没想到，但后来事情发生之后，就想说不要打扰他。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11912336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11912336" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2002070453331487-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11912336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2002070453331487-ss1-600x442.jpg" alt="练爱iNG" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11912336" class="wp-caption-text">阿KEN（左）首部自编自导自演的电影《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%83%E7%88%B1ing.md#1">练爱iNG</a>》邀来辛龙参与演出。（艺起娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11912337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11912337" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2002070453211487-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11912337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2002070453211487-ss1-600x399.jpg" alt="辛龙" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11912337" class="wp-caption-text">Ella（左）与辛龙（右）在《练爱iNG》中演出一对欢喜冤家。（艺起娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿Ken分享分享两人最常聊的话题是宇宙力量、吸引力法则等话题，他觉得辛龙的精神能量很强。而阿Ken看到刘真的新闻之后，也日夜帮刘真祈祷，希望她早日康复。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11912309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11912309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030535192384-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11912309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030535192384-ss1.jpg" alt="阿Ken（林暐恒）、纪培慧" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11912309" class="wp-caption-text">阿Ken（林暐恒）（左）、纪培慧（右）出席电影《练爱iNG》记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11912310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11912310" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030535282384-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11912310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030535282384-ss1.jpg" alt="陈怡?（Yuri）（左）、彭小刀" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11912310" class="wp-caption-text">陈怡?（Yuri）（左）、彭小刀（右）出席电影《练爱iNG》记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>电影《练爱iNG》筹备5年，阿Ken率领纪培慧亮相宣传，纪培慧在片中参加歌唱大赛，在摄影机以及上百观众面前对嘴唱周杰伦的经典RAP曲《双截棍》，她直呼：“天啊！要达到那样的HIGH度对我来说真的很困难！”在该片也有客串演出的周杰伦，看完初剪影片夸她说一句：“哎呦，不错喔！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11912308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11912308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030534432384-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11912308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2003030534432384-ss1-600x399.jpg" alt="彭小刀、阿Ken、纪培慧、陈怡?" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11912308" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）彭小刀、阿Ken（林暐恒）、纪培慧、陈怡?（Yuri）出席电影记者会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
