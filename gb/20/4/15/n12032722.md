<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344182378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年04月15日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台北报导）台湾是兰花王国，栽培石斛兰有数十年历史。摄影师宋永泰推荐赏花秘径嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开宛如“粉红色花的瀑布”，花期预计至4月底。</p>
<p>石斛兰具鞭状茎及垂落的粉红花瓣，又名瀑布兰、倒吊兰，按照花季节可分为春石斛及秋石斛两大类。从台湾、中国大陆、日本、太平洋岛屿到澳洲北部都有石斛兰的踪迹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12046488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12046488" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344212378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12046488" title="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344212378.jpg" alt="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" width="337" b="598"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12046488" class="wp-caption-text">台湾嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰表示，兰花是很珍贵娇嫩的花种，“佳乡庭园餐馆”的石斛兰已经种植20多年，沿着餐厅的外缘有8颗很高大的茄苳树上都有寄生的石斛兰，赏花不额外收费，“餐厅老板乐于把植栽很壮观的花海景色，让大家来欣赏。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12046489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12046489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344122378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12046489" title="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344122378-600x338.jpg" alt="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12046489" class="wp-caption-text">台湾嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12046490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12046490" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344072378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12046490" title="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344072378.jpg" alt="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" width="337" b="598"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12046490" class="wp-caption-text">台湾嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>石斛兰茎类似竹子有节，花朵形似蝴蝶兰，花瓣类型分为圆瓣、尖瓣与卷瓣系，石斛属兰科中最大型的种类，原生种达1,600种以上。他说，茄苳树寄生的花以石斛兰为主，还有不同兰花依附在树上，可以吸收大树的水分、养分。兰花喜欢潮湿凉爽的环境，台湾很适合兰花的生长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12046494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12046494" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344092378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12046494" title="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344092378-600x338.jpg" alt="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12046494" class="wp-caption-text">台湾嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾出口兰花以蝴蝶兰占8成为最大宗，农委会种苗改良繁殖场曾表示，台湾栽培石斛兰冬季、春季的海内外商机最好，但因不能控制花期无货可出，于是花3年的时间开发出超低价人工催花术，盼搭配政策性海外行销，跟进蝴蝶兰成外销明星。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12056931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12056931" style="width: 341px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004232223332378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12056931" title="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004232223332378.jpg" alt="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" width="341" b="598"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12056931" class="wp-caption-text">台湾嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12046508" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12046508" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344032378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12046508" title="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004200344032378-600x338.jpg" alt="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12046508" class="wp-caption-text">台湾嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>“数大便是美。”宋永泰说，石斛兰沿着枝条两边对开，整个树上花开的密度很高，一朵叠着一朵绽放，有成千上万朵花，数量真的很壮观，看起来特别的壮丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12056934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12056934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004232222082378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12056934" title="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004232222082378-600x337.jpg" alt="“花的瀑布” 台湾石斛兰盛开花海壮观" width="600" b="337"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12056934" class="wp-caption-text">台湾嘉义县番路乡“佳乡庭园餐馆”庭园8棵巨大茄苳树寄生的石斛兰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>石斛兰生性强健，繁殖力强，栽培容易，不用特别照顾，有阳光就会开花，适合在高温湿热的台湾栽种。他表示，这次去时正好是在花期最漂亮的时间点，可以看到石斛兰花从树上垂挂下来，宛如“粉红色花的瀑布”，花像瀑布般倾泻而下很漂亮。</p>
