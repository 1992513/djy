<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】美味茶叶蛋～入口回甘 香气四溢</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/12-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在家自己卤茶叶蛋，卫生又放心，家人吃了营养又低热量，一举多得。（图：梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>【小编语】</h3>
<p>蛋几乎是每户人家的必备食材，肚子饿或用餐时间，如果还在苦恼要煮什么菜肴，来一份煎蛋或蛋炒饭、煮面下蛋，再加上一盘青菜就可以简单解决，更何况蛋的营养素不容小觑。每年到了四月天，气候渐渐转热，这时候烹煮一些有特殊香气的小菜佳肴，可以提振食欲，茶叶蛋就是一道很好的料理，做常备菜也很适合。</p>
<p>很多人都在思考，要怎么料理才能让茶叶的香气和鸡蛋融合呢？其实只要在鸡蛋下卤汁前，先用汤匙将蛋壳的底部及侧边打裂，这样就可以帮助它入味了；也有人会疑问卤包要怎么搭配比较适合，现在各大超市、商场都有贩售现成的卤包，当然如果想尝试一下不同的风味，不妨试试这一份食谱，相信您会喜欢！</p>
<h3>【材料】</h3>
<p>鸡蛋12个（Egg）<br />
红茶包4个（Red Tea bag）</p>
<h3>【卤包材料】</h3>
<p>花椒1汤匙（Sichuan pepper）<br />
八角4粒（Star anise）<br />
白胡椒粒1茶匙（Whole white pepper）<br />
草果一粒（Caoguo）<br />
月桂叶4片（Bay leaf）<br />
肉桂2条（Cinemon stick ）<br />
干葱2个、姜2片拍扁（Shallot &amp; ginger）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019501" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-29.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12019501 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-29-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019501" class="wp-caption-text">卤包材料有：月桂叶、白胡椒粒、八角、花椒粒、肉桂、草果、姜片、干葱（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【调味】</h3>
<p>老抽6汤匙（Dark soy sauce）<br />
红麹酱油3 汤匙（Anka soy sauce）<br />
盐1茶匙（Salt）<br />
绍兴酒2 汤匙（Shaoxing wine）<br />
水8 杯（Water）<br />
黑蔗糖2oz（Brown slab candy）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019507" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-23.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-23-450x505.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="505" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019507" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【做法】</h3>
<p>1. 鸡蛋用清水及菜瓜布洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019520" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-20.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-20-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019520" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋用清水及菜瓜布洗干净。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 鸡蛋放入冷水中煮，加入半茶匙盐（加盐是为了防止蛋壳爆裂之后蛋白流出），煮滚之后再多煮5分钟，捞出冲冷水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019521" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-14-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019521" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋放入冷水中煮，加入半茶匙盐（加盐是为了防止蛋壳爆裂之后蛋白流出）。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 在锅内放入8 杯水，卤包的材料用布袋装好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019522" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019522" class="wp-caption-text">卤包的材料用布袋装好。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 袋口绑紧后放入水中，加入老抽、红麹酱油、黑蔗糖和盐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019523" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019523" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019523" class="wp-caption-text">袋口绑紧后放入水中，加入老抽、红麹酱油、黑蔗糖和盐。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 水滚后多煮15分钟，然后取出卤包，放入茶叶包浸泡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019524" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-7-450x284.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019524" class="wp-caption-text">水滚后多煮15分钟，然后取出卤包，放入红茶包浸泡。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 鸡蛋用汤匙从蛋的底部及侧边打裂蛋壳，这么做可令它更容易入味，然后轻轻把蛋放入卤汁中再煮滚，水滚之后盖上锅盖，再用小火浸煮10分钟关火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019525" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-5-450x302.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019525" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋用汤匙从蛋的底部及侧边打裂蛋壳，这么做可令它更容易入味。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 取出茶叶包，加入绍兴酒搅匀，让鸡蛋浸泡至水冷却为止，大约需浸泡8小时的时间，要吃的时候可以再煮热卤汁。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019527" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019527" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019527" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,茶叶蛋" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019527" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
