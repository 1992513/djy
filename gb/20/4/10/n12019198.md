<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】 在家烟熏、烘烤茶香鸡～齿颊留香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/12-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在家用烤箱烘烤乌龙茶香鸡一点都不困难，大人小孩都喜欢，这是一道会齿颊留香的美食。（图：梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>【小编语】</h3>
<p>防疫期间在家时间变多，此时是展现厨艺的最佳时机，如果你想吃烤鸡、茶熏鸡只要跟着食谱的详细步骤制作就能实现。这一次梁厨为我们揭秘这一道“大菜”的制作过程，就算是厨房“新手”也能轻松驾驭，只要将鸡肉进行腌渍、蒸、烤（或熏）三个主要步骤就万事妥当！而且不用担心没有烤箱，有一个大炒锅就可以进行熏鸡了！不论烘烤或茶熏，最后鸡肉都会鲜嫩多汁，料理方式不同只是鸡皮酥脆口感会有所差异，但是都会是<b>齿颊留香</b>的好滋味。</p>
<p><span style="color: #000080; font-size: 16px;">烘烤茶香鸡</span></p>
<h3>【材料】</h3>
<p>新鲜鸡一只（Chicken）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019271" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-28.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12019271 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-28-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡," width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019271" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【配料】</h3>
<p>姜3片（Ginger）<br />
青葱2条（Green onion）<br />
八角3粒（Star anise）<br />
月桂叶3片（Bay leaf）</p>
<h3>【调味料】</h3>
<p>五香粉1/2茶匙（Five spices）<br />
乌龙茶叶1包（Oolong tea）撕开倒出<br />
生抽3汤匙（Soy sauce）<br />
老抽1茶匙（Dark soy sauce）<br />
盐1茶匙（Salt）<br />
糖1茶匙（Sugar）<br />
绍兴酒1汤匙（Shaoxing wine）<br />
四川红花椒 1茶匙（Dried red prickly ash）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019272" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-22.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12019272 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-22-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡," width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019272" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【做法】</h3>
<p>1. 鸡从背部切开并斩去中间大骨，鸡胸骨轻微斩开但不要斩断，反转在砧板上，用手压下，将整只鸡压平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-19.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12019284 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-19-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019284" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 茶叶先用开水浸泡10分钟，滤去茶叶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019285" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-13-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019285" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 姜葱切碎，八角、月桂叶、五香粉全部加入茶叶水中，并加入生抽和老抽，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019286" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-8-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019286" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将整只鸡浸在腌料中，浸约1小时让它入味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-4-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019287" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 浸泡完成的鸡取出放在蒸碟上，大火蒸约20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019288" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-6-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019288" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 烤箱预热400℉（200℃），另外开始准备涂料酱汁。在小碗中加入生抽1汤匙、蚝油1汤匙、老抽1/2茶匙、蜜糖1汤匙，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019289" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-4-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019289" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 接着用刷子均匀地将酱汁刷在鸡皮上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019290" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-4-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019290" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 把整只鸡放在烤盘上，鸡皮向上，烤至鸡皮有金黄色便可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019291" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-6-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019291" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 取出稍为放凉之后，切块装盘便可上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12019293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12019293" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12019293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-11-450x338.jpg" alt="乌龙茶,梁厨美食,香鸡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12019293" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #000080;">烟熏茶香鸡</span></h3>
<h3>【准备一些烟熏材料】</h3>
<p>面粉5汤匙、白米2汤匙、茶叶1包倒出、黄糖2汤匙。</p>
<h3>【作法】</h3>
<p>预备一个大炒锅，上头铺上锡箔纸，烟熏料放在上面，再放上一个蒸架，鸡只放在蒸架上后盖上锅盖，用中大火烧热，起初会见到有白色烟从边缘释出，跟着会变成黄色的，这时候便可关火，再多放置5分钟便可取出。鸡只的鸡皮会有金黄色，闻起上更有烟熏茶叶的香味，切块装盘后便可食用。◇</p>
