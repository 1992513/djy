<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在家工作别穿睡衣 12基本款轻松穿又不失礼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1682776399-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在家开视讯会议，上半身正式服装，下半身轻松裤装；同事也不会发现你的“轻松兼具正式”。（图：shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年04月19日讯】（大纪元记者江桦、绮芳综合报导）中共病毒（武汉肺炎）席卷全球，在传染途径尚未厘清的情形下，多国政府要求民众保持安全的社交距离，许多企业也纷纷安排员工在家工作。原先朝九晚五的生活日常，如今突如其来的转变可能很多上班族还没有做好心理准呢！</p>
<p>在家工作不同于在办公室工作，是因为人们对于家的感受是舒适、自在，心态容易停留于休息模式。如果想要让自己切换到工作模式又享受这天外飞来的“宅居”生活，建议可以从休闲又不失专业仪态感的“穿搭”开始。
</p>
<div class="primary-cli cli cli-text ">根据《实验社会心理学杂志》（Journal of Experimental Social Psychology, JESP）的一项研究发现，专业人士穿着象征其专业的服装，工作的表现更佳。例如医生的专业形象是白色制服，当我们看到穿白色医师袍的医师时很自然的对医师产生敬意，同理，医师也会因为大家对他的尊敬而更重视自己的专业。</div>
<p>
服装不仅影响他人对我们的看法，也影响我们看待自己的方式。比如参加婚宴时，我们会穿着正式服装或晚礼服，我们的仪态自然而然变得优雅；而从事瑜珈穿着瑜伽服时，身体因为处于不受约束的状态会显得放松自在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10765290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10765290" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810060824352384.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-10765290 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810060824352384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10765290" class="wp-caption-text">侯佩岑穿着甜美粉色爱心丝质乌干纱礼服。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因此，一整天穿着家居服、睡衣或运动服在书桌或电脑旁工作，可能会让你的工作心态变得松散，影响工作效率。
<div class="primary-cli cli cli-text ">
<p>那么在家工作应该如何穿着才能既保持工作时的专业心态，又能有居家的舒适感呢？</p>
</div>
<p>我们建议在家工作的穿着方式可以调整成“上半身正式服装，下半身轻松裤装”或是“随时可以出门的完装状态”，倒是不用穿着贴身的西装，或是配戴过多的配件如同上班那么装备齐全。</p>
<div class="primary-cli cli cli-text ">以下归纳三个面向（上衣、外套、裤裙）12款服饰，打造在家工作的“仪式感”，让你的穿着不会太邋遢又有品味。这也是确保如果临时有线上视讯会议时，你不会因为让同事看到自己蓬头垢面的形象而懊恼。</div>
<h3><span style="color: #008000">上衣类</span></h3>
<h3><strong>1. 针织衫上衣</strong></h3>
上衣建议挑选布料硬挺，会让人有“挺起来”的精神抖擞感，版型宽松、柔软的布料容易显得没有生气，刚好落在肩线位置的短袖、七分袖上衣绝对是你的首选！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12042594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12042594" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_648909343-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12042594 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_648909343-600x400.jpg" alt="在家工作,着装" width="600" b="400"/></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12042594" class="wp-caption-text">针织类上衣布料偏硬挺、稍微贴身的设计，给人朝气感。图为细针织上衣相对粗针织类布料柔软，给人优雅、高贵感，也比较适合天气渐热的春夏之际。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12044791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12044791" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_794105284.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12044791 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_794105284-600x400.jpg" alt="毛衣,针织衣,在家工作,休闲,冬季" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12044791" class="wp-caption-text">天气偏冷的地区，可以穿着粗针织上衣。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>2. 素色T-shirt</h3>
T-shirt建议挑选素雅、明亮、单色系，比方白色、天空蓝或鹅黄色，避免选择让人看起来“黯淡”的灰、黑、卡其色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12044792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12044792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1019264083.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12044792 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1019264083-600x400.jpg" alt="Ｔ-shirt,夫妻,青春" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12044792" class="wp-caption-text">白色T-shirt 给人有朝气的感觉，也是百搭不厌的单品，下半身穿着西裤或牛仔裤、裙都很适合。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>3. 衬衫类上衣</h3>
除非你在时尚产业、创新型的公司上班，否则衬衫是公认上班最保险的制服。如果觉得单色系太单调可以挑选有印花、几何图案、条纹的上衣，尽量避免碎花款式，以免开视讯会议让同事觉得眼花撩乱。此外，不喜欢穿正式外套的人可以选“有领子”的上衣，而衬衫类上衣就是不错的选择！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12044787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12044787" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_417173125-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12044787 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_417173125-600x400.jpg" alt="在家工作,衬衫,背心" width="600" b="400"/></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12044787" class="wp-caption-text">在家工作也适合衬衫加上背心。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12042633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12042633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1682776399.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12042633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1682776399-600x402.jpg" alt="在家工作" width="600" b="402"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12042633" class="wp-caption-text">在家开视讯会议，上半身正式服装，下半身轻松裤装；同事也不会发现你的“轻松兼具正式”。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>4. POLO衫</h3>
有领子的上衣除了衬衫之外，也可以选择休闲运动感的POLO衫；如果不喜欢有领子，可以找假两件的上衣，在领口边有图案设计的上衣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12044810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12044810" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_681507220.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12044810 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_681507220-450x345.jpg" alt="polo衫,马球" width="450" b="345"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12044810" class="wp-caption-text">有领口的polo衫原本是网球、高尔夫及马球选手们的标志性服装，现在则是代表时尚、运动休闲，也是轻松气氛的正式场合很适合的穿搭。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>5. 亚麻混纱类上衣</h3>
亚麻混纱类材质比较轻薄，在气温渐升的季节适合穿着，稍硬挺的质料会让人有生气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12044860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12044860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1117396364.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12044860 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1117396364-600x401.jpg" alt="亚麻混纱上衣" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12044860" class="wp-caption-text">亚麻材质的上衣具有光泽感，稍微硬挺的布料，表现出休闲正式感。因为薄透清凉，很适合天气炎热时穿着。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12045223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12045223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_703649008-1-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12045223 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_703649008-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"/></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12045223" class="wp-caption-text">简单造型的上衣看起来很有精神。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000">二、外套</span></h3>
<h3>1. 西装外套</h3>
能够突显专业感的外搭非“西装外套”莫属了！只要一件简单的衬衫、Ｔ-shirt再配上西装外套整个人都会明亮起来。想要兼具休闲风可以选择有条纹、格纹的款式，不建议奇异造型、混色，因为上视讯会议可能会影响同事开会的专注力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12044836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12044836" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1023860488-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12044836 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1023860488-1-450x300.jpg" alt="在家工作" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12044836" class="wp-caption-text">办公室基本穿搭就是白衬衫与西装外套。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12042615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12042615" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1400052623-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12042615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1400052623-450x300.jpg" alt="在家工作" width="450" b="300"/></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12042615" class="wp-caption-text">在家办公可以随意内搭素色T-shirt、衬衫再穿上西装外套就能提升工作的专业度。西装外套不一定只有基本款颜色（黑、灰），也可以尝试活泼色调。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>2.?翻领针织外套</h3>
对于合身的西装外套不感兴趣的人也可以挑选“硬挺厚织数的翻领针织外套”，材质保暖舒适，只要内搭素雅上衣就可以了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12044790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12044790" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_569143744.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12044790 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_569143744-450x300.jpg" alt="冬季,翻领毛衣外套,外套,在家工作" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12044790" class="wp-caption-text">翻领针织外套保暖舒适，不失休闲正式感。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000">三、裤裙</span></h3>
在家久坐更需要注意下半身穿着的舒适度和活动性，除了睡裤、运动裤之外，我们建议还是穿着有伸展性的正式裤裙为佳，免得心态不小心又回复到休闲模式。而且穿得太宽松可能不自觉在家多吃了零食，等回到职场上班又要重新治装，得不偿失。我们建议您可以挑选硬挺布料宽裤、合身有弹性的西裤，或是连身的洋装、裤装，长裙等。
<h3>1. 硬挺布料宽裤</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12042619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12042619" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1298927113.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12042619 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1298927113-450x675.jpg" alt="在家工作,衣服" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12042619" class="wp-caption-text">布料硬挺的宽裤有助于你在家保持活动性，又不失悠闲与正式感。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>2. 连身洋装</h3>
女性在家可以穿着连身洋装，不仅休闲、优雅，也是一套可以随时出门购物，或是到朋友家里拜访的服装。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12042618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12042618" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1142306090.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12042618 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1142306090-450x300.jpg" alt="在家工作,洋装" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12042618" class="wp-caption-text">如果懒得搭配衣服，直接选择连身的裤装、裙装，女性还可以选择洋装等，都不失很好的选择。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>

<h3><span style="color: #333300">4. 合身有弹性的西装裤</span></h3>
在家办公不用穿着合身的套装，但是不想失去“工作的仪式感”，可以挑选有弹性的西裤，舒适不失正式，由于视讯会议大多是坐着开会，选择颜色鲜艳有潮流感也无妨。</p>

<figure id="attachment_12044786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12044786" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_410100337.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12044786 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_410100337-450x675.jpg" alt="长裙,套装,宴会,在家工作" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12044786" class="wp-caption-text">长裙给人稳重、成熟、优雅柔美的印象，也是随时可以出门的穿搭。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在家工作这一段期间，相信你对服装会有意想不到的新发现。穿着悠闲、稍微正式、又保持活力的服装可能就是你的最佳选择。◇</p>
