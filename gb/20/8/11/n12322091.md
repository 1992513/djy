<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：世界上七种最独特美丽的鸭子</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世界上大约有120种类的鸭子，每一种鸭子都有其独特的特征。图为王绒鸭。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年08月11日讯】（大纪元记者张雨霏编译报导）世界上大约有120种类的鸭子，每一种鸭子都有其独特的特征。有的因拥有惊人的美丽<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E6%AF%9B.md#1">羽毛</a>脱颖而出，有的则因其罕见的喙而独领风骚。</p>
<p>这里给大家介绍的是来自世界各地七种最美丽、最不寻常的鸭子。</p>
<p><strong>1.<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E7%A7%8B%E6%B2%99%E9%B8%AD.md#1">白秋沙鸭</a>（Smew Duck）</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E7%A7%8B%E6%B2%99%E9%B8%AD.md#1">白秋沙鸭</a>是世界上最美丽的鸭子之一，原产于欧洲和亚洲的北部森林，偶尔在英国和北美发现。这种细小的鸭属於潜水鸭，仅在湖泊、水库和河流中个体或成群出现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322248" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322248 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322248" class="wp-caption-text">白秋沙鸭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于雄性白秋沙鸭的雪白外表，飞行时黑白分明，故易于辨别；雌性及雏鸭胸部、前额及顶冠则呈灰色。其共同特征是翼上都有白圆点以及灰色的脚。鸟喙呈钩状及有锯齿，可以帮助捕捉鱼类。</p>
<p><strong>2.连帽秋沙鸭（Hooded Merganser）</strong></p>
<p>连帽秋沙鸭原产于北美地区，以其具有可升降功能的硕大羽冠而著称。这些美丽的鸭子属于秋沙鸭中第二小的物种。这些小型潜水鸭多见于淡水池塘、湖泊和湿地中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322251 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World7-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322251" class="wp-caption-text">连帽秋沙鸭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>雄性繁殖鸭外形非常漂亮，醒目的黑色身体和白色的条纹，以及鲜明的黄色眼睛。巨大的扇形羽冠隆成脊状时，有大片的白色斑羽和界限分明的黑边；雌性颜色比较单调，都呈灰棕色，拥有銹色的羽冠。雌雄外形差异很大。</p>
<p><strong>3.<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%B3%E9%B8%AF.md#1">鸳鸯</a>（Mandarin Duck）</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%B3%E9%B8%AF.md#1">鸳鸯</a>是世界上最漂亮的鸭子之一，原产于东亚，包括中国和日本等国家，后来被引向世界，现已在俄罗斯、英国和爱尔兰的部分地区出现。这种独特的物种在野外很少见。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322252 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World8-600x395.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322252" class="wp-caption-text">鸳鸯。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>雄性鸳鸯色彩极为艳丽，喙为少见的鲜红色，端部具亮黄色嘴甲。额部和头顶中央为带有金属光泽的翠绿色，下体浅色；雌性鸳鸯远不如雄性鸳鸯漂亮，通体颜色为暗哑的灰色，喙为灰色。</p>
<p>在某些亚洲文化中，这些鸭子被视为爱情的象征，与其它种类的鸭子不同，它们被认为是彼此相伴一生的伴侣。其中鸳指雄鸟，鸯指雌鸟。</p>
<p><strong>4.长尾鸭（Clangula hyemalis）</strong></p>
<p>长尾鸭最引人注目的特征是其细长、尖尖的尾羽。长尾鸭属中等体型，其喙相对较小，冬天喜欢海水，夏天更喜欢北极苔原池。繁殖期的雄性长尾鸭全身主要呈黑色、白色和灰色块状，其<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E6%AF%9B.md#1">羽毛</a>在冬季和夏季会发生巨大变化；而雌性总体上呈棕色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322253 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322253" class="wp-caption-text">长尾鸭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据北美鸟类指南奥杜邦（Audubon ）称，这种鸭子潜水很深，可以在水面以下200英尺处觅食。</p>
<p><strong>5.<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%98%A4%E9%B8%AD.md#1">瘤鸭</a>（Knob-billed Duck）</strong></p>
<p>这种大型的两色水禽也被称为梳鸭（comb ducks），它们生活在撒哈拉以南非洲、马达加斯加、印度和东南亚的热带湿地中。这类鸭种确实不容错过，翼展范围从116到145厘米不等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322254 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322254" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%98%A4%E9%B8%AD.md#1">瘤鸭</a>。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>雄性和雌性在其背部都拥有惊艳的虹彩紫绿色羽毛，胸部为白色或棕褐色。雄鸭的喙上有一个黑色的大旋钮，与雌性相比更具独特性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322255 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World5-600x435.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322255" class="wp-caption-text">瘤鸭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6.班头海番鸭（Surf Scoter）</strong></p>
<p>这是一种北国寒带的海鸟，以其雄性所具有的独特多彩鸟喙吸引着无数观鸟者。雄鸭有着天鹅绒般的黑色羽毛，额头和脖子后部有醒目的白色斑点；雌鸭身体呈浅黑色，喙呈绿黑色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322256 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322256" class="wp-caption-text">班头海番鸭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种鸭子通常出现在港口、海湾和沿海栖息地中。</p>
<p><strong>7.王绒鸭（King Eider）</strong></p>
<p>王绒鸭是最大的海鸭，生活在北半球，已经适应了寒冷的气候。该物种主要分布在东北欧、北美和亚洲的沿海地区，头部形状颇为奇特，脖颈呈圆形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322257 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World1-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322257" class="wp-caption-text">王绒鸭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>雄性的繁殖羽比雌性的更加鲜艳。雄鸭拥有粉蓝色的项部、绿色的脸颊、红色的喙和淡黄色的额头；雌性总体上是温暖的棕色，黑色的喙使它们看起来像在微笑。夏季，王绒鸭迁移到北极苔原上繁殖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12322258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12322258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12322258 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ET-7-Most-Uniquely-Beautiful-and-Special-Ducks-From-Around-the-World2-600x433.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12322258" class="wp-caption-text">王绒鸭。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
