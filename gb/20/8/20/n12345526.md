<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>青春专案系列活动成果发表暨预防犯罪宣导</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8b0ad6fa47fff2aa665d1a82779d8e14-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加强反毒宣导，多数人持手拿牌合照的宣传力道更大。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年08月20日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）坏人最不喜欢嘉义市政府警察局，因为智慧巡逻车功能太强大，让坏人不敢作恶，为预防青少年暑假期间涉足不当场所、或流连网咖衍生偏差行为，嘉义市政府警察局于109年7月20日起至8月21日止，举办暑期保护少年“充电一夏”相关活动。</p>
<p>并于20日举办109年“暑期保护青少年&#8211;青春专案”系列活动、“全龄共享、世代宜居成果发表会”暨实施网路直播扩大预防犯罪宣导，现场许多大小朋友使出浑身解数，展现所学成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12345530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12345530" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4b555df36196330191677c64c4cd3e8c-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12345530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4b555df36196330191677c64c4cd3e8c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12345530" class="wp-caption-text">在嘉义市警察局中庭，市长黄敏惠及警察局长张树德与大家见面，市长黄敏惠表示，看到这么精彩又丰硕的青春专案成果展非常感动，感谢各局处的配合，以及老师的指导、家长的协助，更感谢小朋友用心学习。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市长黄敏惠表示，看到这么精彩又丰硕的青春专案成果展令人非常感动，感谢各局处的配合，以及老师的指导、家长的协助，更感谢小朋友用心学习，我们这边的活动报名都是秒杀的，今年也增加网路报名的方式，希望大家能够到警察局，发现警察并不是很凶的警察伯伯，而是最能亲近的警察哥哥、警察姐姐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12345531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12345531" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/de21497b3d6da6df8039cd4430059137-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12345531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/de21497b3d6da6df8039cd4430059137-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12345531" class="wp-caption-text">经过有系统的严格训练，这批员警表现可圈可点。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小朋友们也穿上了制服成为萌警。今天的成果展有文有武、有动有静相当多元，让我们骄傲又感动，市府的努力为的就是要让孩子们有一个充实快乐的暑假。我们也希望寓教于乐，培养孩子们反毒、拒毒等正确的观念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12345534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12345534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/d0f05fc68c107765896d1dabc9349769-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12345534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/d0f05fc68c107765896d1dabc9349769-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12345534" class="wp-caption-text">跆拳道表演者飞跃数人又来个空中击破木板动作，在场给予热烈掌声。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警察局局长张树德表示，新兴毒品流入校园及青少年药物滥用为社会各界最关心的重要治安议题之一，也是政府反毒工作重点。警察局秉持“除恶务尽”原则，共同确保青少年暑假期间活动空间的安全，防制青少年被害情事发生，同时运用内政部警政署配置警察局专任警力，共同携手反毒，藉由今日“全龄共享、世代宜居成果发表会”，将“拒毒、反毒”最大诉求传达给每位家长，多关心孩子的生活作息，共创全龄共享、世代宜居健康嘉园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12345535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12345535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/099335496d4ab5d0c4331a384bd3e625-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12345535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/099335496d4ab5d0c4331a384bd3e625-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12345535" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市长黄敏惠以手刀劈木板，手刀一击木板当场凹陷，击退所有不利城市发展的因素。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12345537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12345537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/cdba7fdd44b1b6272b8c3461e10bf459-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12345537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/cdba7fdd44b1b6272b8c3461e10bf459-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12345537" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友气质优雅，他们的作品很美，品学兼优是本次青春专案最大的成果。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警察局青春专案系列活动成果发表会在公益彩劵盈余分配基金支持下，分别以动态、静态方式展现成果，动态活动计有“舞动青春研习营”、“武动青春研习营”、“桌球研习营”；静态活动计有“素描绘画班”、“美劳DIY”、“手脚并用趣味多—黏土手作营”、“桌游研习营”等，并透过有趣的游戏，对小朋友进行反毒与预防犯罪宣导。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12345538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12345538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/cbee96784bfec0a23f31dab66ca41222-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12345538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/cbee96784bfec0a23f31dab66ca41222-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12345538" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友作品集体展示，单个作品3~4小时即可完成，令人惊讶。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12345539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12345539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/d47dad1c375c92216ff593ae965c47ec-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12345539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/d47dad1c375c92216ff593ae965c47ec-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12345539" class="wp-caption-text">跆拳向下扎根，许多家长认为孩子学跆拳好处多。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
