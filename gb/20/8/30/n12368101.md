<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>虎女狮子会暖心送鞋 让家扶孩子开心上学</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7c68c9fcd46d4399fb3a4a2d095be899-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>虎女狮子会30日在虎尾全家鞋店举办“虎女暖家扶．狮子顾亲子”活动。（廖素贞／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）开学在即，虎女狮子会为了让家扶的孩子有双舒适的鞋子上学，特别集结了狮姊们的三倍劵，捐赠云林家扶40户80位学子，每户2000元置鞋费，及1000元的助学金，30日于虎尾全家鞋店，在狮姊们热心的陪伴下，开心选购自己喜欢的鞋子！家扶国一的冠霖穿上新鞋开心的说：平时喜欢运动，鞋子常穿到开口笑，很谢谢虎女狮子会的阿姨们给我一双这么赞的鞋子！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12368810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12368810" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/638a5097c0b37589e6e8545ae2620a4b.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12368810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/638a5097c0b37589e6e8545ae2620a4b-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="282"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12368810" class="wp-caption-text">狮友及狮姊们与家扶亲子开心合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12368808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12368808" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/94b157083f411ae2e066fb400a541ea5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12368808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/94b157083f411ae2e066fb400a541ea5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12368808" class="wp-caption-text">前虎女狮子会会长钟丽玉（左一）狮子会国际基金会LCIF远东暨东南亚宪章区主席林齐国（右二）云林家扶副主委李万吉（右一）开心与家扶亲子合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虎女狮子会会长许淑惠表示，受新冠肺炎影响，全球经济受到冲击，今天能举办这场“虎女暖家扶．狮子顾亲子”活动，要感谢20位狮姊奉献出振兴三倍劵，提供家扶40户80位亲子每户2000元来采买新鞋，更颁发每户1000元奖励金。更要感谢国际狮子会LCIF国际基金及国际狮子会300-D1区的鼎力支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12368806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12368806" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/e52f66a4cb333ed520b0e6d01611bbdc.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12368806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/e52f66a4cb333ed520b0e6d01611bbdc-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12368806" class="wp-caption-text">虎女狮子会会长许淑惠（后排中）开心的和小朋友及与会狮友合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前虎女狮子会会长钟丽玉表示，每年的妇幼节，我们都会举办送新鞋活动，今年因新冠肺炎而延期，一双新鞋对一般人来说不算什么，可是对弱势孩子就是一种渴望，每次看到孩子们穿上新鞋的那份喜悦，心里就特别感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12368807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12368807" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1c1d9996dae9b74020af412bc83e7e6d.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12368807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1c1d9996dae9b74020af412bc83e7e6d-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12368807" class="wp-caption-text">虎女狮子会狮姊们陪伴家扶亲子采购新鞋。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12368809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12368809" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/a13ed3b56b3158937fc2027290715ee1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12368809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/a13ed3b56b3158937fc2027290715ee1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12368809" class="wp-caption-text">狮友及狮姊们一起帮小朋友试穿新鞋。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国际狮子会300-D1区总监林江和狮友表示：虎女狮子会爱心捐款不落人后，除每年捐赠LCIF国际基金外，也将振兴消费三倍券捐出，并向国际总会申请基金，关怀受扶助家庭，落实在地服务。</p>
<p>狮子会国际基金会LCIF远东暨东南亚宪章区主席林齐国说：“狮子会希望用生命感动生命！用生命影响生命！”全世界因为有狮子会而变得更美好、更温馨，感谢虎女狮子会，把爱送给云林家扶。</p>
<p>云林家扶主委陈灿勋感谢虎女狮子会多年来对家扶的帮助，在开学前举办这么暖心活动，让云林家扶的孩子及家长不再为了开学购鞋烦恼而倍感温馨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12368805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12368805" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4783b6a8fe59f526e8da38946942f531.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12368805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4783b6a8fe59f526e8da38946942f531-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12368805" class="wp-caption-text">云林家扶主委陈灿勋（左）颁赠感谢状给虎女狮子会会长许淑惠（右）。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
