<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>水果芝麻菜沙拉 香气浓郁口感清脆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/Fotolia_145787806_Subscription_L-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>芝麻叶沙拉在美国相当受欢迎，微微的辛辣味搭配水果，或是用于烹饪披萨，都有独特风味、不突兀。（Fotolia）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>芝麻菜又称作火箭菜和箭生菜，它适合用来制作沙拉，香气浓、口感清脆，因为品尝起来有一点辣，可以搭配着不同的水果一起吃。选择芝麻菜的时候，要挑选新鲜且带有嫩叶的芝麻菜，避免选到叶子过于柔软，或者已经枯黄长了斑点的叶菜。</p>
<p>芝麻菜买回家后，先去除比较硬的茎部，在流动的水下冲洗，洗干净后先滤掉多余的水分，再用厨房纸巾吸干。先取出马上要食用的部分后，用餐巾纸将剩余的芝麻叶包起来，放进保鲜盒或塑胶袋中，再放入冰箱冷藏，约可放一星期左右。
</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>
100克 芝麻菜<br />
1颗 葡萄柚<br />
半个 酪梨<br />
1颗 柠檬<br />
1匙 橄榄油<br />
鹰嘴豆罐头 适量<br />
盐和胡椒粉 适量<br />
香蒜酱 适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_12367941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12367941" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_223410400.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12367941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_223410400-450x300.jpg" alt="芝麻叶" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12367941" class="wp-caption-text">芝麻叶，又称火箭菜，是十字花科，热量低又有大量膳食纤维。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000">【作法】</span></h2>
1. 葡萄柚去皮去籽，将果肉切成小块。酪梨去籽切成片状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12367947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12367947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1645118419.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12367947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1645118419-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12367947" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄柚去皮去籽，将果肉切成小块。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 从鹰嘴豆罐头中捞出适量的鹰嘴豆，将水分沥干后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12367945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12367945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1264130005.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12367945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1264130005-450x297.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="297"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12367945" class="wp-caption-text">（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 取一沙拉盘，放入洗干净的芝麻菜、葡萄柚、酪梨和鹰嘴豆。</p>
<p>4. 淋上橄榄油，撒上盐和胡椒粉，略微搅拌。最后依照自己的口味，加入香蒜酱。
