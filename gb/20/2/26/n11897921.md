<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】韭菜花炒鲈鱼片 鲜甜爽脆 唇齿留香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-12-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韭菜花炒鲈鱼片，鲜甜暖胃又营养。〈图／梁志生提供〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【小编语】“老板，来一份热炒韭菜花！”韭菜花是中国南北乡城常见菜肴，下锅前先热油爆姜、蒜最后加点盐巴就可以上桌，味道鲜甜有脆感，是暖胃的好佳肴。梁厨这次提供我们另一个方法，先把韭菜花用油盐水烫半分钟捞起，最后热火拌炒两下即可食用，省时又保留鲜香美味。</p>
<p>“韭菜花”是从韭菜的菜心生长出来的花茎，含苞待放时采收料理最美味；韭白上长出的白色花簇，摘下后磨碎腌渍是东北著名“韭菜花酱”，冷冷的天搭配酸菜白肉锅实在幸褔。此外，本道料理烹煮过程要注意时间拿捏，免得韭菜花梗过老口感不佳，也可以和其他食材进行搭配，例如鲜鱼片、豆皮或皮蛋，满足味蕾之外，营养不缺席。</p>
<p>【材料】</p>
<p>韭菜花12安士<br />
鲈鱼一条1.5磅，去骨起肉留用<br />
姜、蒜、干葱片各少许<br />
蛋白半个</p>
<p>【调味】<br />
鸡汤1/4杯<br />
盐1/4茶匙<br />
糖1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-50.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-50-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905329" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>【做法】<br />
1. 韭菜花切去1/4末段硬梗，其余切成1.5吋长度备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/94668b948ee72d84837ab813c255285e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/94668b948ee72d84837ab813c255285e-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,韭菜" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905305" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 鲈鱼肉连皮切成双飞薄片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-31.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-31-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905303" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 用少许盬、胡椒粉、粟粉和蛋白轻轻拌匀，腌5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-26.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-26-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905308" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 完成做法2后再下少许生油拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905310" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-18.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-18-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905310" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 韭菜花用油盐水烫半分钟后捞出来备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-14-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,韭菜" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905311" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 热镬下油一杯，中火把鱼片泡油至七成熟后倒出，沥掉鱼片上的油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905312" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905312" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 爆香姜、蒜、干葱片，放回韭菜花轻炒两下盛出放在盘子里。再放回鱼片，搅匀调味汁液倒下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905322" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 快手翻炒后再将锅中的鱼放在韭菜花上，便可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11905324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11905324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11905324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11905324" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>【梁师傅小贴士】<br />
1. 炒鱼片时动作要快，力气不要过大，否则会炒碎鱼肉。<br />
2. 泡油可以让鱼肉快速熟，只要先七成熟就可以，接下来再下调味汁液翻炒便全熟了。◇</p>
