<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】金菇鲈鱼卷?滋补养身好滋味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-4-e1581859141790-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>金菇鲈鱼卷（图/梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p style="font-weight: 400;"><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【前言】</span></strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%88%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲈鱼</a>是大病初愈的患者恢复体力的优质食材。在寒流来袭、气候多变化的春季，鲈鱼更是滋补养身的最佳选择。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%88%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲈鱼</a>肉质细致绵密，切除头尾去除鱼骨后，鱼肉片铺开卷上金针菇丝、红萝卜丝、芦笋段蒸煮后，口感更加鲜甜，而切下的鲈鱼头尾煮熟后可以摆排，骨腩与蔬菜熬汤后鲜甜的汤汁用栗粉勾芡后，淋上蒸好的鱼卷上，不但可增添鱼肉的甜味，更让这道清淡的鱼料理，有着湿润滑顺的口感。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">?读者可以搭配食谱详细的制作说明，尝试在家下厨，也能呈现餐厅飨宴的顶级水准，秀色可餐的金菇鲈鱼卷，一端上桌，必定会让来客食指大动。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></strong><br />
大鲈鱼1条约1.5磅（Sea Bass 1.5 lbs）<br />
金针菇1扎（Enoki mushroom 4 oz）<br />
芦笋尖12棵（Aspragus 12 strips）<br />
红萝卜丝2安士（Shreded Carrot 2 oz）<br />
小棠菜10棵（Boy choy 10 strips）</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></strong><br />
少许盐、糖、胡椒粉和粟粉水各1茶匙</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【煮鱼汤材料】</span></strong><br />
鲈鱼骨腩1条（Sea bass bone 1 piece）<br />
西芹、红萝卜、洋葱、蕃茄、芫茜梗共12安士（Celery, carrot, onion, tomato, cilantro 12 oz）<br />
姜片4块（Ginger 4 slices）<br />
水3杯（Water 4C）</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span><br />
1.将鲈鱼的鱼肉切下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11873231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11873231" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-23.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11873231 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-23-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11873231" class="wp-caption-text">将鲈鱼的鱼肉切下。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.切下的鱼肉连皮切成双飞鱼片备用。鱼头鱼尾留下蒸熟装盘用。骨腩用来煮汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11873232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11873232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11873232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-16-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11873232" class="wp-caption-text">切下的鱼肉连皮切成双飞鱼片备用。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.金针菇切去末段，洗干净备用。红萝卜切丝，芦笋尖切成1.5吋长度，烫热备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11873233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11873233" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11873233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-16-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11873233" class="wp-caption-text">金针菇切去末段，洗干净备用。红萝卜切丝，芦笋尖切成1.5吋长度，烫热备用。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.鱼片铺开，放上芦笋、金针菇、红萝卜丝卷起，放在蒸碟上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11873234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11873234" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11873234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11873234" class="wp-caption-text">鱼片铺开，放上芦笋、金针菇、红萝卜丝卷起，放在蒸碟上。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.鲈鱼头用蔬菜煮成鱼汤后隔渣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11873236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11873236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11873236 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11873236" class="wp-caption-text">鲈鱼头用蔬菜煮成鱼汤后隔渣。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.接着用粟粉水加少许盐、糖、胡椒粉勾芡后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11873246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11873246" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11873246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11873246" class="wp-caption-text">接着用粟粉水加少许盐、糖、胡椒粉勾芡后备用。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.小棠菜洗干净，插上红萝卜装饰，用油盐水烫熟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11873247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11873247" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11873247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11873247" class="wp-caption-text">小棠菜洗干净，插上红萝卜装饰，用油盐水烫熟备用。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.鱼卷隔水蒸6分钟，取出后排好在碟上，淋上鱼汤，小棠菜摆在旁边，鱼头鱼尾放上装饰便成。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11873248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11873248" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11873248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲈鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11873248" class="wp-caption-text">鱼卷隔水蒸6分钟，取出后排好在碟上，淋上鱼汤，小棠菜摆在旁边，鱼头鱼尾放上装饰便成。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
