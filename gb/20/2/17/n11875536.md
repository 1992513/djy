<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】海味豪门盘菜?宾客尽欢家宴菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>海味豪门盘菜〈图／梁志生提供〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></strong><br />
即食罐头鲍鱼4只（Canned abalone 4 pieces）<br />
煮熟冬菇4个（Cooked Mushroom 4 pieces）<br />
湿花胶4个（Soaked Fish Maw 4 pieces）<br />
湿海参4块（Soaked Sea Cucumber 4 pieces）<br />
熟瑶柱4 颗（Dried Scallop 4 pieces）<br />
熟蚝豉4颗（Cooked Dried oyster 4 pieces）<br />
熟豉油煎虾4条（Cooked Shirmp 4 pieces）<br />
髪菜1/2 oz （Dried Blackmoss ? oz）<br />
豆腐袋4个（Bean curd bags 4 pieces）<br />
大白菜8oz（Napa Cabbage 8 oz）<br />
豆皮（湿枝竹）3 oz（Soaked Dried bean curd 3 oz）</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></strong><br />
蚝油1汤匙（Otyster sauce 1 T）<br />
糖1/4茶匙（Sugar ? t）<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少量（Sesame oil &amp; Pepper a little）<br />
粟粉1茶匙+水勾芡用（Constarch solution 1 t）</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span><br />
1.即食鲍鱼开罐后，连汁一起倒进瓦煲内，慢火煮热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11880498" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11880498" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-30.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11880498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-30-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11880498" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.花胶和海参用姜汁酒烫过，捞起放进鲍鱼汁内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11880495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11880495" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-22.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11880495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-22-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11880495" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.加入煮熟冬菇、熟蚝豉，再加入调味，慢慢煮滚约5分钟。因为食材的胶质多，中间需不断用汤勺搅拌，以免底部黏锅。接着取出菜肴准备装盘，最后把汁液用粟粉水勾芡备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11880496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11880496" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-24.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11880496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-24-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11880496" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.大白菜和豆皮用油盐水烫熟。发菜用水浸软，用少许生油擦洗，冲干净后用姜汁酒滚一会后捞起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11880499" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11880499" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-21.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11880499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-21-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11880499" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.虾子四条用豉油煎熟后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11880497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11880497" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11880497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-14-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11880497" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.豆腐袋蒸熟后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11880500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11880500" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-10-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11880500 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11880500" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.烧热另一瓦煲，放入菜和豆皮在煲底，再放上冬菇、花胶、海参、熟瑶柱、熟蚝豉、髪菜、豆腐袋、煎虾和鲍鱼等，将汁液淋回煲内，煮滚便可上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11880543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11880543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11880543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11880543" class="wp-caption-text">〈图／梁志生提供〉</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></strong><br />
?浸泡的花胶和海参如果没有用完，可以用保鲜纸包卷放进冰箱急冻，可保存一个月以上，食用时提早从冰箱取出，放在冰箱的冷藏区解冻，再用冷水浸泡，就会还原有弹性，用姜汁酒滚过就可以食用。<br />
?这个菜式十分名贵，用即食鲍鱼方便又快速，很快就可做出一款请客菜肴。<br />
?熟瑶柱可直接摆盘，或者是镶在瓜中。◇</p>
