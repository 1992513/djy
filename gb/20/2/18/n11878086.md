<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>近体诗：情义两乡心（外十首）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在武汉的大学同学口罩及香快用完了，我给他寄，可是快递公司只收寄口罩或衣服，不让寄香，无奈。（图片由朝晖提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><strong>情义两乡心</strong></p>
<p>晓色犹存千里梦，春寒未冷两乡心。<br />
已同玉露邀良会，先借祥烟寄好音。</p>
<p>《平水韵》下平十二侵</p>
<p>注释：1、玉露：指秋天。古人云，“金风玉露一相逢，便胜却人间无数。”我们相约在秋天聚会。2、良会：美好的聚会。唐·杜甫《送高司直寻封阆州》诗：“良会苦短促，溪行水奔注。”3、好音：好消息。晋·潘岳《为贾谧作赠陆机》诗：“子其超矣，实慰我心。发言为诗，俟望好音。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878094" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11878094 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878094" class="wp-caption-text">今年正月十六福州三坊七巷的景色，往常摩肩接踵；今年因为防范<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%BA%E7%82%8E.md#1">肺炎</a>病毒，空巷无人。（图片由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%9D%E6%99%96.md#1">朝晖</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>自在安身</strong>（配画诗）</p>
<p>彩树春灯空巷月，幽兰夜气独居香。<br />
安身自在观浮世，乐土前行是故乡。</p>
<p>《平水韵》下平七阳</p>
<p>注释：1、春灯：春夜的灯。唐·杜甫《船上夔州郭宿雨湿不得上岸别王十二判官》诗：“风起春灯乱，江鸣夜雨悬。”2、安身：存身，容身。《国语·鲁语下》：“叔仲 曰：‘子之来也，非欲安身也，为国家之利也，故不惮勤远而听于楚 。’”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878095" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11878095 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878095" class="wp-caption-text">（图片由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%9D%E6%99%96.md#1">朝晖</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>元宵节快乐</strong></p>
<p>琼树为邻多乐事，芳兰作伴过元宵。<br />
不愁春意清风散，自有真香浊气消。</p>
<p>《平水韵》下平二萧</p>
<p>注释：1、琼树：喻品格高洁的人。语本《晋书·王戎传》：“王衍神姿高彻，如瑶林琼树。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878096" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11878096 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878096" class="wp-caption-text">爱好香道同学的书法作品（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我有一位制香的学生，热爱传统文化，不止是制香，她在刺绣、书法、古琴方面都学得很好。有诗为证：</p>
<p><strong>秀外慧中</strong></p>
<p>书香润屋身安定，忠孝传家德自丰。<br />
点翰援琴人忘倦，风神秀慧复谁同。</p>
<p>《平水韵》上平一东</p>
<p>注释：1、点翰：染翰，挥笔书写。《晋书·王羲之传》：“末代去蒲归华，舒笺点翰，争相夸尚，竞其工拙。”2、援琴：持琴；弹琴。《韩非子·十过》：“ 平公 曰：‘善。’乃召师涓，令坐师旷之旁，援琴鼓之。” 3、风神：风采；神态。《晋书·裴楷传》：“楷风神高迈，容仪俊爽，博涉群书，特精理义，时人谓之‘玉人’。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878097" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11878097 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878097" class="wp-caption-text">（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>立春</strong></p>
<p>草色同沾经岁绿，兰花独得几枝春。<br />
宜多向道清怀抱，且自闻香去病身。</p>
<p>《平水韵》上平十一真</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878098" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11878098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_6-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878098" class="wp-caption-text">春晖不见红尘客，恰是樱花绽放时。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>樱花开</strong></p>
<p>何必蓬庐宽眼界，会同山水论襟期。<br />
春晖不见红尘客，恰是樱花绽放时。</p>
<p>《平水韵》上平四支</p>
<p>注释：1、蓬庐：茅舍。本诗借指室内、家里。三国·魏·曹植《愍志赋》：“去君子之清宇，归小人之蓬庐。”2、襟期：怀抱、志趣。北齐·高澄《与侯景书》：“缱绻襟期，绸缪素分。”3、春晖：春日的阳光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878099" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878099" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11878099" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_7-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878099" class="wp-caption-text">福州市鼓楼区五四路，往常车水马龙；近期因为防范<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%BA%E7%82%8E.md#1">肺炎</a>病毒而人车稀少。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>闻好香</strong></p>
<p>萦萦几缕度时光，读罢经书已夕阳。<br />
城内萧疏多谢客，空间洁净且闻香。</p>
<p>《平水韵》下平七阳</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11878100 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878100" class="wp-caption-text">兰花在正月初一开放，祝大家今年都有一个好的开端。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>元日</strong></p>
<p>书生意气谁相似？炉有沉香室有兰。<br />
聚散年年过佳节，平安岁岁足清欢。</p>
<p>《平水韵》上平十四寒</p>
<p>注释：1、元日：正月初一。2、清欢：清雅恬适之乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878101" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11878101 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878101" class="wp-caption-text">性静偏从香作伴，耐寒常借雪为媒。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>陌上寻梅</strong></p>
<p>隆冬陌上寻芳客，永泰城南几树梅。<br />
性静偏从香作伴，耐寒常借雪为媒。</p>
<p>《平水韵》上平十灰</p>
<p>注释：1、永泰：指隶属福州六区六县之一的永泰县。2、偏从：偏偏跟从；却从。这个词义古诗词中常见，但被《汉语词典》遗漏。宋·苏辙《中秋夜八绝（之一）》诗：“谁遣常时月，偏从此夜明。”宋·欧阳修《渔家傲》词：“看柳意，偏从东面春风至。”明·欧必元《紫骝马》诗：“不傍将军泣，偏从伯乐鸣。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11878102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11878102" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11878102 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0218_10-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11878102" class="wp-caption-text">昨日江湖听雨夜，今晨里巷看花时。（图片由朝晖提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>里巷看花</strong></p>
<p>昨日江湖听雨夜，今晨里巷看花时。<br />
不求往事浑无迹，且喜幽怀半是诗。</p>
<p>《平水韵》上平四支</p>
