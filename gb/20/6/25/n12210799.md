<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】豆酥酱炒甜豆茄子～酥软润香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-25-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>豆酥酱炒甜豆茄子（图：梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E9%85%A5.md#1">豆酥</a>是黄豆渣、酱油、蒜末、姜炒制的干料，高纤维又营养；如果不知道怎么炒制在华人超市一般可以购买到豆酥酱（fried crispy soy bean）。豆酥酱料理上也相当多变，除了蒸鱼后洒上一点香气十足的豆酥相当对味有口感；也适合炒菜或是清烫蔬菜后拌酱，除了不油腻还有“卡兹”的酥脆口感，是很下饭的佳肴。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%B1%86.md#1">甜豆</a>12安士（Sugar peas 12 oz）<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%84%E5%AD%90.md#1">茄子</a>8安士（Eggplant 8 oz）<br />
猪绞肉5安士（Ground pork 5 oz）<br />
蒜蓉、姜蓉各少许（Ginger &amp; garlic a little）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210885" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-35.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-35-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210885" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%B1%86.md#1">甜豆</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%84%E5%AD%90.md#1">茄子</a>、猪绞肉（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E9%85%A5.md#1">豆酥</a>酱两汤匙（Fried crispy soy bean 2 T）<br />
盐、胡椒粉各少许（Salt &amp; pepper a little）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210887" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-26.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-26-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210887" class="wp-caption-text">蒜蓉、姜蓉、胡椒粉、盐、豆酥酱（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. ?甜豆撕掉头尾及旁边的粗丝，洗干净后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210888" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-19-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210888" class="wp-caption-text">甜豆撕掉头尾及旁边的粗丝（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. ?茄子连皮切成条状，切碎姜和蒜。热锅下油，先把茄子略炒后盛出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210890" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-18-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210890" class="wp-caption-text">茄子连皮切成条状，热锅下油略炒（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. ?猪绞肉放进锅内炒熟，并加入姜蓉、蒜蓉和豆酥酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210891" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210891" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. ?接着再把甜豆放入，兜匀后加水1杯，煮约3分钟。再把茄子放回锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210893" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210893" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. ?加入少许盐、胡椒粉翻炒均匀后，用少量粟粉水勾芡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210894" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210894" class="wp-caption-text">用少量粟粉水（太白粉水）勾芡（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. ?完成上述步骤后，将菜肴装盘，最后再放少量豆酥酱在上面装饰即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210895" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210895" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【梁厨小贴士】</span></h2>
<ol>
<li>?豆酥酱是台湾的酱料，是由黄豆、大豆油、盐、糖和姜等制成。</li>
<li>?豆酥酱可以直接加热，淋在豆腐、蔬菜、鱼和肉上面，可变化多种菜肴，尝起来美味可口。</li>
</ol>
