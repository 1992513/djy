<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>椰奶可做甜点也可入菜 全方位食材你会几种？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_513228217_coconut_milk_04-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>椰奶加到南瓜汤另有一番风味。（图/Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年06月04日讯】（大纪元记者林达、莫琳综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E5%A5%B6.md#1">椰奶</a>（coconut milk）在世界上许多地方流行已久，近年来由于盛行原始人饮食法（paleo diets）和无乳饮食，使得可以代替牛奶、带着浓郁椰香的椰奶在美国也广受欢迎。你可能经常听到椰奶这个词，但你可能还未真正知道如何用它来烹调食物。在中共肺炎疫情流行、出门购物不便期间，家里多准备些椰奶罐头，不仅方便烹煮食物，也兼顾健康美味，可谓一举多得。</p>
<p>首先，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E5%A5%B6.md#1">椰奶</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E5%AD%90%E6%B0%B4.md#1">椰子水</a>是不一样的；剖开一颗椰子，里面香甜半透明的液体是椰子水，而椰奶则是来自于白色的椰肉提取出来的白色液体。从超市冰柜买回的盒装椰奶，是椰浆（coconut cream）加过滤水制成，通常含有蔗糖、甜味剂、防腐剂等，比椰奶更稀、更甜，主要用作饮料或添加于早餐的谷物粥，不适合用于烹饪。如果你要用椰奶烹调，记得买超市货架上摆列的罐装椰奶。当然如果你所在的地方容易买到新鲜的椰子，也可以尝试用新鲜椰子自制椰奶。</p>
<p>无论是为了瘦身，或想添加食材美味，你都可尝试用椰奶做出各种餐点，可以说一天三餐外加甜点都可以用椰奶料理。</p>
<h3>早餐</h3>
<p>椰奶可以代替牛奶做成无奶早餐或甜点。如果你喜欢做松饼，可以用椰奶和椰子粉制作松饼粉，用椰奶代替牛奶制作法式吐司。如果早上要上班，没有太多时间准备早餐，可以直接加椰奶在燕麦粥里一起吃，或者制作椰奶奶昔（Coconut Milk Smoothie）。椰奶奶昔的做法：可以在平时将椰奶做成冰块冷冻，早上只要将椰奶冰块和喜欢的水果放入食物调理机，再加<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E5%AD%90%E6%B0%B4.md#1">椰子水</a>或果汁打成奶昔状即可。</p>

<h3>汤品和炖菜</h3>
<p>椰奶最热门的做法是做汤和炖菜，烹调出带有椰香风味的美食。椰奶的天然脂肪搭配香料和酸味特别美妙，这也是传统泰式料理的特色，例如，辛辣的泰式椰子鸡汤、泰式红咖喱炖鱼。椰奶也可加到南瓜汤和越南蛤浓汤，增添美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12159416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12159416" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_1285664737_coconut-milk_02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12159416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_1285664737_coconut-milk_02-600x401.jpg" alt="coconut milk, 椰奶" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12159416" class="wp-caption-text">椰奶炖鱼印度料理。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12159424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12159424" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_513228217_coconut_milk_04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12159424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_513228217_coconut_milk_04-600x400.jpg" alt="coconut milk, 椰奶" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12159424" class="wp-caption-text">椰奶加到南瓜汤另有一番风味。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>海鲜</h3>
<p>椰奶也非常适合跟海鲜一起烹调，可以中和蛤蜊的咸味又可添加菜肴的滑润感。汆虾仁扇贝浇椰奶是一道简单清爽的菜式，将虾仁、扇贝各自川烫好后加冰块置于碗中，椰奶混合柠檬汁、甜椒、辣椒、香菜、生姜和盐拌入虾和扇贝，加盖放冰箱静置6小时到一天即可拿出食用。这道料理不仅提升海鲜纯净的风味，同时也把椰奶的天然风味彰显出来。如果要吃热食，可以用椰奶炖咖喱入菜，如椰奶炖明虾料理、椰香海鲜意大利面等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12159429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12159429" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_1727941417_coconut_milk_06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12159429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_1727941417_coconut_milk_06-600x400.jpg" alt="coconut milk, 椰奶" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12159429" class="wp-caption-text">椰奶炖煮明虾料理。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>肉类</h3>
<p>与椰奶搭配的最佳蛋白质之一是鸡肉。把它做成口感丰富又曼妙的椰味传统菜肴，如慢炖<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/20/4/11/n12021798.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">咖喱椰奶鸡</a>或用椰奶制成的酱汁烧一道甜辣的红咖喱烤鸡。如果你喜欢红肉，也可以用椰奶慢火炖煮做一道香菜椰奶排骨，或泰式椰奶辣红咖喱牛肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12023035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12023035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1017155896.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12023035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_1017155896-600x400.jpg" alt="coconut milk, 椰奶" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12023035" class="wp-caption-text">在咖喱中加入椰奶是南洋风咖喱的典型作法，这种料理方式可以让咖喱的风味更圆润浓郁！喜欢香料的人，可以加入辣椒、姜或香菜。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12159436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12159436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_768840226_coconut-milk_03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12159436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_768840226_coconut-milk_03-600x400.jpg" alt="coconut milk, 椰奶" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12159436" class="wp-caption-text">泰国传统红咖喱牛肉。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>素菜</h3>
<p>对素食者来说，用椰奶来提味的素菜有很多。蒸米饭时，用一半水一半椰奶，可把米饭的香味提升至另一个层次，煮出来的米饭搭配各种素菜都很合宜。还有姜黄椰奶烤白花椰，或者配以富含蛋白质的豆类菜肴：蔬菜鹰嘴豆咖喱椰奶等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12159448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12159448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_1521352493_coconut_milk_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12159448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_1521352493_coconut_milk_09-600x400.jpg" alt="coconut milk, 椰奶" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12159448" class="wp-caption-text">姜黄椰奶烤白花椰。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>甜品</h3>
<p>椰奶可以做成各式各样的点心，也可以代替牛奶做成各种甜点，如凤梨芒果椰奶果昔、芋头炖椰奶、椰汁西米露等，素食者的最爱。椰奶亦可用来烘焙蛋糕，如<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/20/4/11/n12021798.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">椰奶特蕾丝蛋糕（Tres Leche）</a>，素食者也可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12159460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12159460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_1704698812_coconut_milk_07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12159460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200604_shouhui_shutterstock_1704698812_coconut_milk_07-600x400.jpg" alt="coconut milk, 椰奶" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12159460" class="wp-caption-text">由腰果，核桃，椰奶和干果制成的蛋糕（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>

<h3>饮料</h3>
<p>试试用姜黄、生姜和蜂蜜制成的润喉金奶茶（Golden Milk Tea），或用椰奶代替牛奶做成热椰奶巧克力、椰香咖啡（冷热皆宜）、椰香红茶拿铁、香草抹茶冰沙等。</p>
