<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：讲不轮转</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>妈妈的话，这阵<br />
我讲不轮转<br />
亲像我不是妈妈生的<br />
实在真见笑</p>
<p>我敢不是妈妈生的？<br />
每一次讲妈妈的话<br />
讲不轮转<br />
我就会按呢问自己<br />
感觉没面见人</p>
<p>这是按怎造成的？<br />
检讨起来，拢嘛是<br />
彼阵讲国语运动<br />
推行的太过磅了<br />
什么讲方言就要<br />
挂狗牌，罚钱啰<br />
哪有按呢的代志哩？<br />
世界的文化渐渐<br />
走上统一的路<br />
是没错；但是<br />
要顺其自然，尤其<br />
要尊重各地的特性！</p>
<p>我要讲妈妈的话<br />
因为我是妈生的<br />
我不使得没妈妈<br />
我不使得没根<br />
<strong><br />
I can’t speak appropriately </strong>&emsp; Hsu Chi Cheng </p>
<p>Mother’s language, now<br />
I can’t speak appropriately<br />
Just as I’m not born by my mother<br />
I feel shy truly</p>
<p>Am I not born by my mother?<br />
I will ask myself like this<br />
And feel shy<br />
Every time as I can’t speak<br />
My mother’s language appropriately</p>
<p>How does it cause like this?<br />
I exterminated it, all is<br />
Due to the surpass of<br />
The movement of pursue national language of the Republic of China<br />
What about speaking dialect must be<br />
Held a dog brand, and be fined<br />
What the thing likes this?<br />
It’s no mistake that<br />
The culture of the world marches toward<br />
The road of unity slowly; but<br />
It must go with the flow<br />
Must respect the specific property of the local</p>
<p>I want to speak mother’s language<br />
Due to I’m born by my mother<br />
I can’t free from my mother<br />
I can’t get rid of my root@</p>
