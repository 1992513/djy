<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>张立昂演霸道总裁 跃上货车打斗英雄救美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354112122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>三立台视周日偶像剧《浪漫输给你》首映会6月5日在台北举行。图左起为连晨翔、宋芸桦、张立昂、许孟哲。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年06月05日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）张立昂在新戏《浪漫输给你》饰演霸道总裁，勤健身2个月展现好身材、晋升为英勇打手，从跑车跃上冷冻货车英雄救美，他5日出席首映会时大呼过瘾：“很喜欢武打戏，希望多来一点，感觉像拍玩命关头。”甚至有一场要倒挂在4、5层楼高的地方，他也克服内心恐惧，完成拍摄。<br />
<figure id="attachment_12164392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12164392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354082122-ss1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354082122-ss1-600x463.jpg" alt="浪漫输给你" width="600" b="463" class="size-large wp-image-12164392"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12164392" class="wp-caption-text">《浪漫输给你》首映会6月5日在台北举行。图左起为连晨翔、宋芸桦、张立昂、蔡瑞雪、许孟哲、廖奕琁。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>《浪漫输给你》5日举行首映会，主要演员张立昂、宋芸桦、连晨翔、廖奕琁、许孟哲、汤志伟、萧瑶、林敬伦、蔡瑞雪、陈歆妍、刘主平、李迪恩等人出席造势。</p>
<p>女主角宋芸桦在剧中穿梭现实世界和言情小说，为爱上霸道总裁无所不用其极，浪漫谈情说爱，激发了她的少女心，每天拍完戏回家都high到出不了戏，笑说：“家人都问我是不是喝醉了？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12164397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12164397" style="width: 368px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354472122-ss1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354472122-ss1.jpg" alt="宋芸桦" width="450" b="599" class="size-full wp-image-12164397"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12164397" class="wp-caption-text">宋芸桦出席首映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12164401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12164401" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050355242122-ss1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050355242122-ss1.jpg" alt="蔡瑞雪" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12164401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12164401" class="wp-caption-text">蔡瑞雪出席首映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12164402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12164402" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354322122-ss1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354322122-ss1.jpg" alt="张立昂" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12164402"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12164402" class="wp-caption-text">张立昂出席首映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12164403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12164403" style="width: 414px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354442122-ss1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354442122-ss1.jpg" alt="连晨翔" width="450" b="599" class="size-full wp-image-12164403"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12164403" class="wp-caption-text"><br />连晨翔出席首映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>连晨翔则把饭店房间布置成家，把除尘蹒机、除湿机、烘衣机都带去台中片场，更大方出借给其他演员。宋芸桦封晨翔为“楼长”，只要缺锅子、湿纸巾都找他。</p>
<p>连晨翔也称赞张立昂很够义气：“有次我点了外卖，联络不上公司同仁，只好敲立昂的房门，他裹着浴袍出来借我钱，我大概是第一个敲他房门的男演员。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12164462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12164462" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050355052122-ss1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050355052122-ss1.jpg" alt="廖奕琁" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12164462"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12164462" class="wp-caption-text"><br />廖奕琁出席首映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12164465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12164465" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050355082122-ss1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050355082122-ss1.jpg" alt="许孟哲" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12164465"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12164465" class="wp-caption-text">许孟哲出席首映会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖奕琁剧中饰演美丽却跋扈的集团千金，但她自嘲私下像“树獭”，个性有极大反差；许孟哲为拍戏大都待在台中，但他仍心系怀孕中的太太，“只要有空档就会陪老婆去做产检，老婆现在最关心的就是看我的班表。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12164411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12164411" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354052122-ss1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006050354052122-ss1-600x418.jpg" alt="浪漫输给你" width="600" b="418" class="size-large wp-image-12164411"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12164411" class="wp-caption-text">《浪漫输给你》首映会6月5日在台北举行，众演员出席现场。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
