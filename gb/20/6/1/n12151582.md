<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宝马个性化定制服务BMW Individual——你的选择你做主</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Main-Image-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（BMW Australia提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月01日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>墨尔本工商讯）<span style="color: #2b2bcc;"><em>“当今<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A2%E4%BE%88%E5%93%81.md#1">奢侈品</a>购买者一般都有明确诉求，他们要寻求非常个性化的体验。——这一切尽在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E9%A9%AC.md#1">宝马</a>个性定制版。”</em></span></p>
<p>曾几何时，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A2%E4%BE%88%E5%93%81.md#1">奢侈品</a>仅仅是一类有形的、触手可及的稀有物品；时至今日，人们通过奢侈品所显示的已不再是“我拥有什么”，更多的是在表达“我是谁”——比如表达个人对环保的态度，或者彰显个人的创造力。 因此，当今奢侈品购买者一般都有明确诉求，他们要寻求非常个性化的体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12176738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12176738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/P90333171_highRes_the-all-new-bmw-indi.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12176738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/P90333171_highRes_the-all-new-bmw-indi-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12176738" class="wp-caption-text">（BMW Australia提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12151662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12151662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Meteorite.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12151662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Meteorite-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12151662" class="wp-caption-text">（BMW Australia提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种变化在豪华车市场上表现尤为显着。过去，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E9%A9%AC.md#1">宝马</a>车客户所关心的无非是漂亮的外形、舒适度和良好的驾驶感受；如今，在这些考量的基础上，必须加入匠人精神和个性化诉求，才能满足诸多宝马车客户对奢华的全新定义。</p>
<p>面对这种趋势，<strong>宝马旗下的个性化定制服务BMW Individual</strong>在1990年代应运而生。开始为时尚大师卡尔·拉格斐（Karl Lagerfeld）、世界三大男高音之一的何塞·卡雷拉斯（José Carreras）乃至众多王室成员量身定制他们的专属座驾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12151665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12151665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Main-Image-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12151665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Main-Image-2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12151665" class="wp-caption-text">（BMW Australia提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12151666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12151666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Special-Interior-with-Wooden-Decking.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12151666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Special-Interior-with-Wooden-Decking-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12151666" class="wp-caption-text">（BMW Australia提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宝马个性定制版不但能将理念变为现实，更能保证这些理念完全源于客户的自身需求。无论是精美的车漆，非常规的材料还是超常规的需求，宝马定制版都可以借助其谙熟的制造经验和精湛的手工工艺，将客户专有的个性理念变为现实。与此同时，宝马还会兼顾严格的安全标准、固有的品牌特性以及各种法律要求。除了面向个人客户外，宝马个性定制服务还开发并完成了各种特别版、展示车和针对特定市场的特别版车型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12151667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12151667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Stitching.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12151667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/Stitching-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12151667" class="wp-caption-text">（BMW Australia提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宝马个性定制服务第一时间捕捉到了豪华车市场高度个性化的变化趋势。目前，其产量已达到每年约两万辆的水平。每一辆定制车都彰显着该系列轿车特殊的质量标准、精湛的手工工艺和独一无二的设计能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12176744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12176744" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/thumbnail_P90333172_highRes.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12176744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/thumbnail_P90333172_highRes-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12176744" class="wp-caption-text">（BMW Australia提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12176745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12176745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/P90333165_highRes_the-all-new-bmw-indi.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12176745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/P90333165_highRes_the-all-new-bmw-indi-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12176745" class="wp-caption-text">（BMW Australia提供）</figcaption></figure>
