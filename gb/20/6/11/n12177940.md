<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>翁章梁赞誉   嘉义县的警察很认真</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/0bc6be3315032d56b75410a801ca3c2f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义县警察局在11日下午在3楼大礼堂举行庆祝大会，邀请各级民意代表、地方士绅共襄盛举。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月11日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）许多员警表现暖警形象，感人的事迹层出不穷，6月15日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">警察节</a>为警察重要节日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%8E%BF.md#1">嘉义县</a>警察局在11日下午在3楼大礼堂举行庆祝大会，邀请各级民意代表、地方士绅共襄盛举，县长翁章梁也亲临主持庆祝大会，嘉勉警察同仁平日的辛劳，藉以表扬绩优员警、警友、协勤民力及志工。</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%8E%BF.md#1">嘉义县</a>的警察很认真！”翁章梁说，藉6月15日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">警察节</a>，一方面向警察同仁鼓舞士气，另一方面也透过此机会颁奖，包括模范警察、协勤民力等，感谢所有人的辛劳，近期辖内刑案皆已最快的速度破案，在警方努力下百分百侦破，包括义竹的安毒工厂、大林邮局抢案，加上11万包咖啡包毒品案，警方以科学办案，增加破案效率。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12177948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12177948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/d1cc7e8dc1e1ce1c43f0599d94ac3ba1-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12177948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/d1cc7e8dc1e1ce1c43f0599d94ac3ba1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12177948" class="wp-caption-text">县长翁章梁也亲临主持庆祝大会，嘉勉警察同仁平日的辛劳，藉以表扬绩优员警、警友、协勤民力及志工。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁强调，过去曾声明任何在嘉义县的犯罪行为，都是对警察的挑衅行为，嘉义县的警察不容挑衅，警察同仁将以最快时间侦破，展现破案决心，翁章梁除大力赞美警方的努力外，更在警察节前夕献上最诚挚的祝福，祝福警察同仁平安、快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12177951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12177951" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/999229cceaa3c2947ef7c850079fbb01-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12177951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/999229cceaa3c2947ef7c850079fbb01-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12177951" class="wp-caption-text">县长翁章梁表扬绩优员警。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义县警察局指出，平时即要求同仁时刻不忘维护警察尊严，充实<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F%E4%B8%93%E4%B8%9A.md#1">警察专业</a>，做好警察工作，达成维护人民安全、社会安定任务，并积极走入社区，参与社区营造，以降低犯罪率、维持良好的治安。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12177952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12177952" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/c065c740007515dfe3f77538edc77453-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12177952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/c065c740007515dfe3f77538edc77453-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12177952" class="wp-caption-text">县长翁章梁表扬义勇警察大队的队员。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动现场表扬警察局模范警察中埔分局番路分驻所所长吕俊生、侦防电信网路诈欺犯罪及精进缉毒绩优单位、绩优警友、义警、守望相助队、山地义警、民防、义交及辅导志工，共计39人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12177954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12177954" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1125121f82f09bfd2b7875b9a786af4a-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12177954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1125121f82f09bfd2b7875b9a786af4a-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12177954" class="wp-caption-text">员警受奖后合影留念。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除庆祝大会外，嘉义县警察局也举办嘉警最爱“嘉”，前往辖内弱势社福团体关怀慰问，警察节前夕展现警察关怀弱势族群爱心，抛砖引玉，营造温馨、祥和社会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12177955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12177955" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/99f0967add1b1c174c863109d5696a4c-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12177955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/99f0967add1b1c174c863109d5696a4c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12177955" class="wp-caption-text">守望相助队员等人受奖后合影。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12177957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12177957" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9eee479374c966af9148df4871003d82-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12177957" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9eee479374c966af9148df4871003d82-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12177957" class="wp-caption-text">义勇警察大队队员等人受奖后合影。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
