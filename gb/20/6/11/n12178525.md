<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】炒面和煮粥好吃的秘诀～2道简便主食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-20-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日式蔬菜炒面～在家快速料理美食，不用让家人等到饿肚子。（图／梁厨美食提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这次梁厨示范了两道简便的餐点，搭配在一起食用，也很美味。熬粥时可以一次多煮一些，再慢慢享用！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12178536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12178536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178536" class="wp-caption-text">日式炒面和<span style="color: #000000;">陈皮瑶柱</span>白粥，两种主食煮少份量，提供家人更多选择也吃得完。2种搭配起来吃更是另一种风味，可以尝试看看！（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">1. 日式蔬菜炒面</span></h2>
<h2><span style="color: #000000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>高丽菜12oz<br />
红萝卜2oz<br />
洋葱3oz<br />
冬菇2个<br />
日本生炒面2包（Nama Yaki-Soba）每包有5.6oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉适量<br />
盐、胡椒粉适量</p>
<h2><span style="color: #000000;">【调味】</span></h2>
<p>炒面所附的调味包</p>
<h2>【做法】</h2>
<p>1. 高丽菜、红萝卜、洋葱和冬菇全部切丝备用。<br />
2. 热锅下油，先把蔬菜用少许姜蒜蓉炒香，加入少许盐及胡椒粉，炒至六成熟，盛出备用。<br />
3. 锅中再加少许油及水半杯，煮滚后放入生炒面，用筷子轻轻拨散，煮约1分半钟，水分差不多收干后，再加入调味包里面的调味粉，洒在面上，继续用筷子炒匀，再把之前的蔬菜放进兜匀后，便可上碟食用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">2. 陈皮瑶柱白粥</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12178581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178581" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/dba247ca2f71fb03598841535f56f7b1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12178581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/dba247ca2f71fb03598841535f56f7b1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178581" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="color: #000000;">陈皮瑶柱白粥～只要准备3种材料就可以开火了。</span>（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p>旧陈皮2块<br />
干瑶柱8粒<br />
白米1杯半</p>
<figure id="attachment_12178577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178577" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-15.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12178577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-15-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178577" class="wp-caption-text">白米、瑶柱、陈皮。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 陈皮和干瑶柱先洗干净，再用清水浸泡半小时备用。准备一个大汤锅，放入清水15杯，将陈皮和干瑶柱先煮30分钟，让它的味道出来。中间把白米洗干净后，拌入少许盐及生油备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12178578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178578" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-10.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12178578 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178578" class="wp-caption-text">陈皮和干瑶柱先洗干净，再用清水浸泡半小时备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 30分钟过后再加入白米，继续用中大火煮滚，注意不要盖上锅盖，以免液体溢出。白米需煮至绵滑，中间要用汤勺略为搅拌一下，以免黏锅，煮好后加盐调味便可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12178579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12178579" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-8.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12178579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食提供" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12178579" class="wp-caption-text">白米需煮至绵滑，中间要用汤勺略为搅拌一下，以免黏锅，煮好后加盐调味便可食用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【梁师傅小贴士】</h2>
<p>陈皮有化痰止咳的功效，尤其越旧的陈皮效果越好。</p>
