<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】银杏腐竹糖水～软绵清甜 养颜美容</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-23-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>港式经典煲汤甜品，白果（银杏）腐竹鸡蛋糖水，口感绵密软嫩，夏季清凉上选之品，也是家常点心。（图：梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编按】</strong>广东煲汤富有盛名，银杏（白果）腐竹糖水是一道家常甜品，因为腐竹煲至如“豆浆”般的清甜，或片状般的软绵带滑润都很可口，再搭配白果的松软口感，夏季来一碗清胃祛湿、清凉解热，建议也可以加入鸡蛋一起煮，更是对味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>新鲜银杏（白果）带壳4安士（Ginkgo 4 oz）<br />
冰糖2.5安士（Rock Sugar 2.5 oz）<br />
湿腐竹 10安士（Soaked bean curd skin 10 oz）<br />
姜3片（Ginger slices 3）<br />
鸡蛋4个（Egg 4 pieces）<br />
水4杯（Water 4 C）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209507" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-32.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12209507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-32-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209507" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 银杏用槌子敲裂开壳，取出果肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209509" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-23.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12209509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-23-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209509" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 用滚水将果肉煮一会， 用网勺不断磨擦，让果肉上的外皮剥落。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209513" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12209513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-16-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209513" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将去皮后的银杏果肉清洗干净，然后去芯避免苦味，如果购买真空包装的银杏大多已经去芯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209514" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-15.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12209514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-15-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209514" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 腐竹用清水浸泡至软，剪成小块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209515" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209515" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/95c5ab51f049d59965b8006393e689c4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12209515 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/95c5ab51f049d59965b8006393e689c4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209515" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 锅内放入水4杯，先加入冰糖、姜片和白果煮10分钟，之后放入腐竹后再多煮5分钟，关火备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209516" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12209516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-10-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209516" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 水波蛋做法：将鸡蛋分别打在小碗中，水烧开后放入一汤匙白醋，用筷子搅拌至中间起漩涡，立即放入鸡蛋在漩涡中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209517" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12209517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209517" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 用慢火浸煮3分钟，捞出水波蛋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209518" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12209518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209518" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 最后将蛋放入糖水中便可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209519" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12209519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209519" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h2>
<p>1. 水烧开后放入1汤匙白醋，鸡蛋便会很快凝结，用筷子搅拌至中间起漩涡，立即放入鸡蛋再浸煮三分钟。用这个方法做出来的水波蛋，内里的蛋黄是流动的，非常嫩滑。</p>
<p>2.?银杏是人从银杏树采摘下来，再交到店铺售卖的，必须在某些季节才买得到。 如果找不到可以买一些干货，或已经剥了壳的真空包。</p>
<p>3. 银杏的营养价值高，富含蛋白质、维生素C、胡萝卜素，以及钙、磷、铁、钾、镁等微量元素。</p>
