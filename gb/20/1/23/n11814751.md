<h6>炸春卷（C2食光）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E6%98%A5%E5%8D%B7.md#1">炸春卷</a>是一道非常传统的菜色。带有迎新春的意义，包入韭菜象征长长久久～</p>
<p>外表象征金条的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E6%98%A5%E5%8D%B7.md#1">炸春卷</a>，一直都是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">年菜</a>餐桌最受喜爱的菜色之一！</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【食材】</span></strong><br />
洋葱1/2颗<br />
韭菜1小把<br />
虾仁200g<br />
绞肉200g</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【调味料食材】</strong></span><br />
酱油1小匙<br />
糖1小匙<br />
盐适量<br />
蒜泥适量<br />
香油1小匙<br />
绍兴酒1大匙<br />
白胡椒粉适量</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【做法】</span></strong><br />
1. 将香菜、韭黄切段，洋葱切丝，虾仁切丁备用。<br />
2. 取一碗，将绞肉、虾仁丁、蒜末、白胡椒、绍兴、糖、香油、盐、酱油抓匀。<br />
3. 再拌入步骤1拌匀备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11814820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11814820" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/56244d63f8654d1060e1d34d3702a526.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11814820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/56244d63f8654d1060e1d34d3702a526-450x254.png" alt="炸春卷,C2食光,年菜" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11814820" class="wp-caption-text">春卷的内馅有香菜、韭黄、洋葱、虾仁，调味料有白胡椒、绍兴、糖、香油、盐、酱油，将内馅和调味料两者倒入碗中抓匀。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 取润饼皮，将料填入，再用面粉糊涂在封口上，包紧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11814811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11814811" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9a958bfda62aa0a30144ec8693309343.png"><img class="wp-image-11814811 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9a958bfda62aa0a30144ec8693309343-450x252.png" alt="炸春卷,年菜,C2食光" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11814811" class="wp-caption-text">取润饼皮后，将馅料填入，最后用面粉糊涂在封口上，才容易包紧春卷。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 热油锅，约160度慢慢炸，炸至上色即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11814812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11814812" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/003980172acaf8b2c51e7480e55f2b1f.png"><img class="wp-image-11814812 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/003980172acaf8b2c51e7480e55f2b1f-450x254.png" alt="炸春卷,C2食光,年菜" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11814812" class="wp-caption-text">热油锅后慢慢炸春卷，才不至于高温过焦。（C2食光）</figcaption></figure>
