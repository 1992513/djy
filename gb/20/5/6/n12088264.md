<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】木瓜煲唐排</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/0483f04ec0be0004af1cd187788fa375-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道木瓜煲唐排的最大特色就是纯天然的口味，不仅色彩漂亮、营养丰富，而且是美颜圣品，老少皆宜。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>唐排为猪的颈和胸部之间、连骨带肉的肋骨。肌肉纤维细软，夹杂的筋膜较少，肉质较滑，鲜香味浓。性平，味甘咸；适宜红烧、炖汤、蒸煮、油炸、烧烤、酱卤等。</p>
<p>唐排最好等待至要料理时才洗净，尤其不要先泡水，否则唐排的肉质会变得失去弹性或令养分流失。唐排含较少脂肪，肉较嫩滑，在烹调上，常会用来煲汤。木瓜煲唐排就是一道经典菜肴。</p>
<p>果皮美观又不失光滑的木瓜，其果肉软嫩细致，味道香甜浓郁，汁水又多。木瓜富含蛋白质及氨基酸等17种以上的营养，具有“水果之皇”、“百益之果”、“万寿瓜”的称号。木瓜中的维生素C特别丰富，差不多是苹果的40多倍，成人一天的维生素C仅需半个木瓜就够了。</p>
<p>木瓜具有健脾消食的功效。对身体的吸收消化有促进的作用，木瓜中的蛋白酶是很特殊的，可将我们人体的脂肪进行有效分解，然后形成脂肪酸，并能消化蛋白质，有利于对我们常吃的食物进行吸收和消化，故有健脾消食的良效。</p>
<p>木瓜还具有解毒消肿的作用。木瓜里含有的霉素对我们的身体有很好的解毒功效，据可靠实验显示，我们每天只需大约200克的木瓜，就能排除人体内的各种有害毒素。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道木瓜煲唐排，选用的是全熟的甜木瓜，入汤后香气四溢。罗厨特别强调，这道菜的最大特色就是纯天然的口味，不仅色彩漂亮、营养丰富，而且是美颜圣品，老少皆宜。</p>
<h2>木瓜煲唐排</h2>
<p>主料：木瓜1个（1.5磅），唐排 （2磅），鸡脚4只。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/ccc8eaa235b230ca012fe46cfbcefd71.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/ccc8eaa235b230ca012fe46cfbcefd71-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：姜片、葱段、红枣、葱粒、枸杞。<br />
调料：菜品做好后添加少许的盐。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将甜木瓜去皮、去核。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 将木瓜去筋后切成小方块。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 唐排切块，洗干净去除血水。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/3-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/3-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 鸡脚洗干净，去除异味。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/4-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/4-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 唐排和鸡脚一起放入瓦煲中，注入清水浸过唐排一半为好。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加入姜片、葱段，加盖将其煮浓。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 去掉葱段，耐心将汤上面浮油和浮沫去除。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 加入红枣，慢火煲1个小时左右。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 排骨煮至软嫩，加入木瓜，加盖慢火再煲40分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 最后放入枸杞子、葱花即可。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/10-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/10-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴色彩亮丽，营养丰富。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/11-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12088808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/11-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
