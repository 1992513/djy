<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】四季豆炒牛肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/190f164a2640ec4fbaf921ff3741fb8a-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道四季豆炒牛肉，软嫩不干柴，鲜甜又味美，满满的家乡味，令人入口难忘。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>四季豆营养价值高、口感好，是广泛栽培的大众化蔬菜之一，其普及程度位居各类蔬菜中的第一位。四季豆无论单独清炒还是和肉类同炖同炒，亦或是焯熟凉拌，都很符合人们的口味。</p>
<p>牛肉含有丰富的蛋白质，氨基酸组成比猪肉更接近人体需求，能提高机体抗病能力，对生长发育及手术后、病后调养的人，在补充失血和修复组织等方面特别适宜。</p>
<p>牛肉中的肌氨酸含量比任何其它食品都高，它对增长肌肉、增强力量特别有效。另外，牛肉含有足够的维生素B6，可增强免疫力，促进蛋白质的新陈代谢和合成，有助于紧张训练后身体的恢复。</p>
<p>和鸡肉、鱼肉相比，牛肉含有较高的肉毒碱，肉毒碱可支持脂肪的新陈代谢，产生支链氨基酸，这是对增长肌肉起重要作用的一种氨基酸。</p>
<p>四季豆炒牛肉是一道常见的经典中式小炒菜，四季豆可调和牛肉浓郁的味道，加入蒜头爆炒，香气四溢，不仅清爽可口，而且开胃增力。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道四季豆炒牛肉，选用的是自家栽种的新鲜四季豆，切薄片的牛肉经过30分钟的腌制，猛火快速爆炒后，软嫩不干柴，鲜甜又味美，满满的家乡味，令人入口难忘。</p>
<h2>四季豆炒牛肉</h2>
<p>主料：四季豆1磅、牛肉0.5磅。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5b8a3cd0af113a4611ade9417095083c.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5b8a3cd0af113a4611ade9417095083c-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/ec542f80740ea8d85d6616712eebae1a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/ec542f80740ea8d85d6616712eebae1a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：葱一根切粒、姜1小块切小片、蒜头4粒拍烂。<br />
调料：老抽、生抽、胡椒粉、盐、色拉油、生粉。</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 新鲜四季豆洗干净沥干水分。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1-40.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1-40-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 将牛肉切片。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2-26.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2-26-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 用生抽、老抽腌制牛肉。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/3-20.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/3-20-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 添加盐、胡椒粉、生粉，腌制牛肉30分钟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/4-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/4-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 猛火阴油将葱、姜、蒜爆香，加入牛肉。<br />
（注：酱油的制作原料一般有黑豆与黄豆之分，由黄豆制作通称为“酱油”；而由黑豆传统荫酿而成称为“荫油”。）<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 牛肉爆炒至七成熟捞起。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 用锅底余油爆香四季豆至八成熟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 加入牛肉炒至香浓。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 加入生粉水勾芡。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136057" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 起锅装盘。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/10-32.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/10-32-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴清爽鲜香，满满的家乡味儿。<br />
<strong> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/11-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12136060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/11-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
