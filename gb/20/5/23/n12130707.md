<h6>在家也能轻松制作关西平民小吃～大阪烧。（图：Ｃ2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%98%AA%E7%83%A7.md#1">大阪烧</a>是来自于日本关西的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E6%B0%91%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">平民美食</a>，简单的食材，简单的美味！</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%98%AA%E7%83%A7.md#1">大阪烧</a>在日文称作お好み焼き，意思是“随意<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A7%E7%83%A4.md#1">烧烤</a>自己喜欢的东西”，所以大家可以随意调整食谱加入自己喜欢的食材！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【食材】</span></h2>
<p>高丽菜 1/4颗<br />
火腿 2片<br />
蛋 3颗<br />
面粉100g<br />
盐巴 适量<br />
牛奶适量</p>
<p>大阪烧酱适量<br />
美乃滋适量<br />
香松 适量<br />
海苔粉适量<br />
柴鱼片</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 将高丽菜、火腿切丝</p>
<figure id="attachment_12130749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12130749" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2496d14c97575b54164438a294b14a77.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12130749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2496d14c97575b54164438a294b14a77-450x292.jpg" alt="大阪烧,关西美食,平民美食,C2食光,烧烤,高丽菜" width="450" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12130749" class="wp-caption-text">（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 取碗将鸡蛋打入内，搅散后，加入火腿、高丽菜丝、面粉、牛奶、香松、盐</p>
<figure id="attachment_12130750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12130750" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/b0491db15ca330ee642241cf546ee551.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12130750 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/b0491db15ca330ee642241cf546ee551-450x369.jpg" alt="大阪烧,关西美食,平民美食,C2食光,烧烤,高丽菜" width="450" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12130750" class="wp-caption-text">取碗将鸡蛋打入内，搅散。（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12130751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12130751" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8218a9248899b8cfa2d94f8f38ff01fd.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12130751 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8218a9248899b8cfa2d94f8f38ff01fd-450x310.jpg" alt="大阪烧,关西美食,平民美食,C2食光,烧烤,高丽菜" width="450" b="310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12130751" class="wp-caption-text">搅成蛋汁（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12130752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12130752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/29a84d0b4400080c3196637f44ecb906.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12130752 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/29a84d0b4400080c3196637f44ecb906-450x353.jpg" alt="大阪烧,关西美食,平民美食,C2食光,烧烤,高丽菜" width="450" b="353" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12130752" class="wp-caption-text">在蛋之内加入火腿丝、高丽菜丝、面粉、牛奶、香松、盐（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅，倒入适量油，热油，将作法2倒入锅内<br />
4. 盖上锅盖，单面煎熟后，用磁盘助翻面动作</p>
<figure id="attachment_12130753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12130753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/d0566264079a43ccc2c49602001b961e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12130753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/d0566264079a43ccc2c49602001b961e-450x313.jpg" alt="大阪烧,关西美食,平民美食,C2食光,烧烤,高丽菜" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12130753" class="wp-caption-text">（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 第二面煎熟后，即可盛盘<br />
6. 淋上全部酱料即可享用</p>
<figure id="attachment_12130754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12130754" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/81f69cfd22ff8767b3a06ed4481c182d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12130754 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/81f69cfd22ff8767b3a06ed4481c182d-450x304.jpg" alt="大阪烧,关西美食,平民美食,C2食光,烧烤,高丽菜" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12130754" class="wp-caption-text">淋上全部酱料就完成了！（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
