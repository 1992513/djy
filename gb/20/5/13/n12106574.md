<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美华裔舞协与加台联捐口罩 助美以美医院防疫</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6b33a05b83e20ac12763ba58865b4f34-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美国华裔舞蹈协会5月12日捐4千口罩助美以美医院防疫，加台联亦捐出1千个口罩表达爱心。（袁玫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年05月14日讯】（大纪元记者袁玫<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">中共病毒</a>（又称武汉肺炎，COVID-19）疫情蔓延全球，为感谢从事救助工作的第一线医疗人员，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E5%8D%8E%E8%A3%94%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">美国华裔舞蹈协会</a>（America Chinese Dance Association ）5月12日捐出4,000个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%BB%E7%96%97%E5%8F%A3%E7%BD%A9.md#1">医疗口罩</a>予美以美医院，在防疫上尽一份心力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12106592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12106592" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/b414d849905d001b261e3119631f2205.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12106592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/b414d849905d001b261e3119631f2205-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12106592" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E5%8D%8E%E8%A3%94%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">美国华裔舞蹈协会</a>5月12日捐4千口罩助美以美医院防疫。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12106587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12106587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8e42980c00142701fc2eff0802fa3ad0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12106587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8e42980c00142701fc2eff0802fa3ad0-600x484.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="484" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12106587" class="wp-caption-text">美国华裔舞蹈协会5月12日捐4千口罩助美以美医院防疫，会长袁璐感谢第一线防疫医疗人员。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12106602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12106602" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6d26019f1ec75d81fa5d587337c38911.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12106602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6d26019f1ec75d81fa5d587337c38911-600x279.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12106602" class="wp-caption-text">美国华裔舞蹈协会5月12日捐4千口罩助美以美医院防疫，加台联亦捐出1千个口罩表达爱心。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长袁璐表示，在疫情蔓延时就思考为社会服务并有所贡献，当接到美以美医院的讯息，得知他们需要一些防疫物资，该协会便购得4千个口罩。袁璐说小小的善心能对疫情有所助益。她也提醒民众外出一定要戴口罩，保护自身安全，目前疫情尚未结束，多忍耐，多小心共度难关。</p>
<p>美以美医院基金会总裁迈克.椎比（Michael D. Driebe）表示，舞蹈协会的捐献，对医护人员和病人有极大的帮助，在此充满挑战的非常时期是有深远的意义。椎比同时强调，该院一本以往提供民众安全的全面医疗服务，民众有病痛时不要犹豫应及早就医。</p>
<p>当天，一同捐赠的单位还有加州台湾同乡联谊会，会长谢传佐表示，继上周捐给6家老人中心等弱势团体，今天亦共襄盛举再次捐出口罩1,000个。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12106598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12106598" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/eb92a8a68d13ea4c981665c444b0d1bd.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12106598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/eb92a8a68d13ea4c981665c444b0d1bd-600x499.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12106598" class="wp-caption-text">5月12日，加台联会长谢传佐现场捐赠1千个口罩助美以美医院防疫。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12106605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12106605" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/a963b0d41110ad2eb3b13dd7c1435bc3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12106605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/a963b0d41110ad2eb3b13dd7c1435bc3-600x632.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="632" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12106605" class="wp-caption-text">美国华裔舞蹈协会5月12日捐4千口罩助美以美医院防疫，加台联亦捐出1千个口罩表达爱心。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美以美医院市场部代表表示，许多前线医疗人员每天都需要大量防疫物资，如口罩、防护服等，尤其医用防护服更是急需，期望有更多民众或团体能多加支持并提供。</p>
<p>当天出席捐赠仪式的有：舞蹈协会会长袁璐、财务长陈西园、理事曹笑莲、会员曾翔渝，及加台联会长谢传佐、副会长王竹青、秘书长储锦琪等人。◇</p>
