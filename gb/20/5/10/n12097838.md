<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>惠灵顿24小时内50人违反3级警报限制</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/GettyImages-1221729500-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为2020年4月29日新西兰惠灵顿市城市和到海大桥广场。 (Mark Tantrum/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年05月11日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者安琪<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%A0%E7%81%B5%E9%A1%BF.md#1">惠灵顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E6%96%B9.md#1">警方</a>表示，在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">24</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小时内，有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">50</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人违反了三级警报限制，这些被<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%87%E6%8E%A7.md#1">指控</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9D%E8%A7%84%E8%A1%8C%E4%B8%BA.md#1">违规行为</a>发生在周五（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日）下午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">点到周六（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日）下午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">点之间。可能因为这是二级警报生效前的最后一个周末，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>人太开心了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">自</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">27</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日下午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">59</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分降至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">级警报以来，全国共发生</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1051</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">起违规事件。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E6%96%B9.md#1">警方</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">向其中</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">253</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">起案件提起诉讼，向</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">723</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人发出警告，并将</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">75</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人转交给了青年援助组织</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Youth Aid</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%A0%E7%81%B5%E9%A1%BF.md#1">惠灵顿</a>市议会发言人理查德·麦克莱恩</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Richard MacLean)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">周日</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">说，很明显，首都很多人都没有遵守社交距离规则</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">“昨天就像</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在交通</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">高峰</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">期。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">政府将于周一（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日）宣布，新西兰是否将从</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">级警报降至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">级。最早</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">会</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在周三降到2级警报，而且规则会明显放宽。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">但麦克莱恩说，考虑到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Newtown</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">一家咖啡馆外的人群很拥挤，委员会在周末期间放置了锥标来分隔人群。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Oriental Bay</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Evans Bay</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和惠灵顿南海岸，违背</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">社交</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">距离规定的现象尤其严重。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Oriental Bay</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">已安装电子告示牌，提醒市民遵守这些规则。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">市议会</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">收到了许多关于人们藐视规定的投诉。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">周六，奥克兰东部的海滩，包括</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mission Bay</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kohimarama</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，都人满为患。一位摄影师周日表示，海滩上“相当繁忙”，但人们似乎试图保持距离。同样，上周六，数百名冲浪者涌入惠灵顿的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Lyall Bay</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。不过，一名警方发言人表示没有接到有关投诉。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在警方记录的全部</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">级违法事件中，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Manukau</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">是最多的，有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">243</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">起。其次是中心</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">地</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">123</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">起，接下来是南区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">107</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">起，奥克兰市</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">100</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">起</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Canterbury 90</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">起。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Northland</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">是违规最少的地区，为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">19</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">起。</span></p>
