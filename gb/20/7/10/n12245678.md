<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【纪元播报】反中共渗透《外国代理人法》成热点</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/a13b8cc6ea220999d40ead687b6b663d.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>【大纪元2020年07月10日讯】（大纪元记者蔡溶美国纽约报导）前中国国航经理林英帮中共驻联合国代表团军官偷运包裹时，未曾想过会触发“外国代理人”注册的门槛。去年底她因“在未报告司法部长的情况下担任中共代理人”一罪被判5年缓刑。</p><p>除了林英案，亲共中文媒体（红色媒体）也被当作“外国代理人”，说明《外国代理人登记法》（FARA）的适用范围广泛。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p>美国智库“哈德逊研究所”长达52页的《中国共产党的对外干涉行动：美国与其它民主国家如何应对》（The Chinese Communist Party’s Foreign Interference Operations: How the U.S. and Other Democracies Should Respond）报告，是关于中共统战部在海外渗透活动的系统性报导，侧面印证了加强FARA执法的必要性。事实上，FARA起诉的数量近年已有了明显增加。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><center><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/SN4BEKZaHvU" width="560" b="315" frameBorder="0" allowFullScreen="" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center><p>（接上）<ahref="https://www.epochtimes.com/b5/20/6/24/n12208404.md#1" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class">反中共渗透《外国代理人登记法》应用广（上）</a></p><p>原文：<ahref="https://www.epochtimes.com/b5/20/6/25/n12210926.md#1" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class">反制中共渗透 FARA应用广（下）</a></p><p>【<ahref="https://www.epochtimes.com/b5/tag/%E7%B4%80%E5%85%83%E6%92%AD%E5%A0%B1.md#1" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class">纪元播报</a>】制作组</p><p>责任编辑：孙芸
