<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>佛罗里达各地炼功烛光夜悼　呼吁结束迫害</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/614456457855dc207e085556387ad0ca-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>南佛州的部分法轮功学员在Las Olas 海滩炼功， 向世人传递大法的美好。（张启明/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年07月22日讯】（大纪元记者吴蔚溪劳德代尔堡报导）佛罗里达州各地的法轮功学员在<span class="s3">7</span>月<span class="s3">18</span>日和<span class="s3">19</span>日集体炼功，讲真相，烛光夜悼，呼吁解体中共，结束对<span class="s5">法轮功学员们长达21年的迫害。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12273473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-43.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-43-600x340.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273473" class="wp-caption-text">南佛州的部分法轮功学员在著名的Las Olas 海滩炼功，呼吁结束长达二十一年的迫害。（古朴/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12273419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273419" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5a46ed40d9a8dec87ea3916f846e6bf6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5a46ed40d9a8dec87ea3916f846e6bf6-600x292.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273419" class="wp-caption-text">北佛州圣奥古斯丁市宪法广场（Plaza de la Constitución，St. Augustine, FL）路过的行人向法轮功学员了解真相。（Michelle Brazeau/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12273421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273421" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/f239213d3811d3d0cc15ec2de1b03225.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/f239213d3811d3d0cc15ec2de1b03225-600x292.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273421" class="wp-caption-text">北佛州圣奥古斯丁市宪法广场（Plaza de la Constitución，St. Augustine, FL）游客驻足阅读展板,听法轮功修炼者讲述在中共治下的中国内正在发生的迫害和器官活摘的罪恶。（Michelle Brazeau/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12273420" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273420" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/500b05b2b7b367fb09d2459ec31b4c79.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273420" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/500b05b2b7b367fb09d2459ec31b4c79-600x292.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273420" class="wp-caption-text">游客在北佛州圣奥古斯丁市宪法广场（Plaza de la Constitución，St. Augustine, FL）了解了真相后，在征签薄上签名表示支持法轮功，要求停止迫害。（Michelle Brazeau/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span class="s3">2020</span>年<span class="s3">7</span>月<span class="s3">20</span>日是法轮功学员一直坚持反迫害<span class="s3">21</span>周年的日子。佛罗里达因疫情原因，不能举行大型集会<span class="s5">。</span> <span class="s7">佛州</span><span class="s8">各地的法轮功学员们</span>集体炼功，传递大法的美好，反对这场史无前例的迫害，向当地民众征集拒绝中共、反对迫害的签名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12273417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273417" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8ceadd292e0835ba0e3552977e0f95d6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8ceadd292e0835ba0e3552977e0f95d6-600x368.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273417" class="wp-caption-text">佛州中部奥兰多市的部分法轮功学员聚集在市中心的Lake Eola湖边集体炼功。(Chris Jasurek /大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学员们还举办了烛光守夜活动，他们<span class="s4">盘腿打坐，</span>手捧蜡烛，场面肃穆，悼念因坚守<span class="s3">“</span>真、善、忍<span class="s3">”</span>遭受中共残酷迫害致死的法轮功学员。当地许多民众对法轮功学员因坚持信仰而遭受的迫害表示同情，谴责正在发生在中国的残酷迫害，同时希望这场迫害能早日结束。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12273418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273418" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2c653eb911647aff8a1722e1aa17c5ed.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2c653eb911647aff8a1722e1aa17c5ed-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273418" class="wp-caption-text">佛州中部奥兰多市的部分法轮功学员聚集在市中心的Lake Eola湖边进行烛光夜悼，悼念因坚守“真、善、忍”遭受中共残酷迫害致死的法轮功学员。（Chris Jasurek /大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12273470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/802c29fe518a0f20b474d629624815b4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/802c29fe518a0f20b474d629624815b4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273470" class="wp-caption-text">南佛州的部分法轮功学员在Las Olas海滩悼念因坚守“真、善、忍”被中共残酷迫害致死的法轮功学员。（古朴/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p5"><span class="s3">21</span>年前的<span class="s3">7.20</span>，以江泽民为首的共产党出于妒嫉，动用国家全套暴力专政机器，悍然发动了对信仰<span class="s3">“</span>真、善、忍<span class="s3">”</span>的法轮功学员的血腥镇压。<span class="s3">21</span>年来，法轮功学员风雨无阻地向全世界发出强有力的声音、使大陆民众和国际社会越来越多的人明白了真相、越来越多的正义之士及各级政府纷纷谴责中共迫害法轮功的罪行。世界各地，社会各界正义善良的力量正在聚集，共同制止这场惨绝人寰的迫害。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12273564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273564" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2d4a70dc856fb3e7d4dd5c70462d7bbb.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2d4a70dc856fb3e7d4dd5c70462d7bbb-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273564" class="wp-caption-text">南佛州行人了解法轮功真相和在中共统治下的中国正在发生的迫害。（张启明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12273408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12273408" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/ee6f8af13f0dff769db93f3eb56eba39.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12273408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/ee6f8af13f0dff769db93f3eb56eba39-600x343.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12273408" class="wp-caption-text">南佛州的部分法轮功学员在Las Olas 海滩炼功， 向世人传递大法的美好。（古朴/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p5">21年来，佛州各地的法轮功学员每年都会以各种方式向世人讲述真相，希望世上更多的民众认清中共的邪恶本质，了解法轮功被迫害的真相，共同结束这场长达二十一年的残酷迫害，进入一个没有共产党的新时代。</p>
