<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】西葫芦炒牛肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/3e137176e22eb867fd236826fc273feb-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这道西葫芦炒牛肉充满浓郁的家乡味，牛肉鲜嫩软滑，西葫芦瓜清脆入味，西葫芦瓜的清香和牛肉浓郁的香味完全融合在一起，入口难忘。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>西葫芦是夏日餐桌的主要蔬菜之一，以皮薄、肉厚、汁多、味美而深受人们喜爱。西葫芦可搭配肉类，也可素炒，既可以入菜，也可以做各种馅料。</p>
<p>中医认为西葫芦具有清热利尿、除烦止渴、润肺止咳、消肿散结的功能，可用于辅助治疗水肿腹胀、烦渴、疮毒以及肾炎、肝硬化腹水等症，具有除烦止渴、润肺止咳、清热利尿、消肿散结的功效。</p>
<p>西葫芦含有较多维生素C、葡萄糖等营养物质，还含有丰富的纤维素，能够促进胃肠的蠕动，加快人体的新陈代谢。对人体排毒养颜、预防治疗便秘有很好的作用。</p>
<p>另外，西葫芦含有一种干扰素的诱生剂，可刺激机体产生干扰素，提高免疫力，发挥抗病毒和肿瘤的作用。因富含水分，西葫芦也有润泽肌肤的作用，因而深受女士们喜爱。</p>
<p>牛肉蛋白质含量高，而脂肪含量低，所以味道鲜美，受人喜爱，享有“肉中骄子”的美称。因为牛肉中氨基酸的组成比猪肉更接近人体需要，能提高机体抗病能力，对生长发育及手术后、病后调养的人在补充失血、修复组织等方面物别适宜。</p>
<p>这道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%91%AB%E8%8A%A6%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">西葫芦炒牛肉</a>是一道简单易做的家常菜，罗厨强调，牛肉切片后要用调料拌匀并腌制一下，而且在翻炒食材时，一定要用猛火爆炒。如果西葫芦瓜比较老的话，爆炒后要加锅盖焖一会儿，这样会使葫芦瓜更入味。</p>
<p>罗厨介绍，这道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%91%AB%E8%8A%A6%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">西葫芦炒牛肉</a>充满浓郁的家乡味，牛肉鲜嫩软滑，西葫芦瓜清脆入味，西葫芦瓜的清香和牛肉浓郁的香味完全融合在一起，入口难忘。</p>
<h2>西葫芦炒牛肉</h2>
<p>主料：西葫芦2磅、牛肉1磅<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/7a207e1735866589f0ec288ffbba48a0.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/7a207e1735866589f0ec288ffbba48a0-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1c916f413ca07025b5bea3e75a8aeda0.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1c916f413ca07025b5bea3e75a8aeda0-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：姜片、葱段、蒜头、香菇<br />
调料：蚝油、生抽、老抽、生粉、糖、盐、胡椒粉、照烧酱、香油、白酒</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 西葫芦去胆，切厚片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 牛肉切片。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 用蚝油、生抽、老抽、生粉、糖腌制牛肉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/3-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/3-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 猛火阴油将香菇、姜片和蒜头爆炒至香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/4-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/4-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 加入牛肉猛火爆香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 添加胡椒粉、照烧汁和葱段，快炒后捞起。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/6-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/6-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 利用锅底余油爆炒西葫芦。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 加盖焗5～6秒。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 添加盐、糖、香油、白酒后爆炒。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 最后放入牛肉快速爆炒至香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/10-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/10-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴清脆入味、鲜滑浓郁。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12228883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
