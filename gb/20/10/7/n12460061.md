<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】梅菜扣肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/a7798f95eae673badd014aed473a12d4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道梅菜扣肉造型别致、颜色亮丽，汤汁黏稠鲜美，扣肉肥而不腻，食之软烂醇香。（视频截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>梅菜历史悠久，闻名中外，是岭南三大名菜之一，为岭南著名传统特产，历史上作为宫廷食品而被称为“惠州贡菜”。</p>
<p>民间用新鲜的梅菜经凉晒、精选、飘盐等多道工序制成，色泽金黄、香气扑鼻，清甜爽口，不寒、不燥、不湿、不热，有增强消化、清热解暑、消滞健胃、降脂降压的功效。悠久的历史、独特的风味和功效以及近千年的传播，铸就了“惠州梅菜”的盛名。</p>
<p>晒干后的梅干菜呈酱褐色，有独特的菜干香味。用整株芥菜腌制晒成的叫“长吊干菜”，切碎后随制晒成的叫“短吊干菜”。梅干菜常用来清蒸、油焖、烧汤，食之能解暑热、清脏腑，生津开胃。</p>
<p>梅菜扣肉是一道客家传统名菜，与盐焗鸡、酿豆腐一道被称为“客家三件宝”。梅菜扣肉的制作材料主要有五花肉、梅菜、葱、姜等。成菜后，肉烂味香，吃起来咸中略带甜味，肥而不腻。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道梅菜扣肉造型别致、颜色亮丽，汤汁黏稠鲜美，扣肉肥而不腻，食之软烂醇香。梅菜吸收了五花肉的油和汤汁，味道立即丰富起来，五花肉又带着梅菜的清香，彼此互相配合。</p>
<p>罗厨强调，制作这道菜有几个关键的步骤千万不能忽略。首先，五花肉在下锅煮之前，要将肉皮煎至金黄；其次，肉煮好后要用生抽腌制一下，然后放入油锅烧至金黄色；另外，烧好的肉切厚片后，还要腌制一下，保证肉充分入味。
</p>
<h2>梅菜扣肉</h2>
<p>
主料：五花肉、梅干菜</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/fd86d9c6a85b8ba45e1cd06c886943b0.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/fd86d9c6a85b8ba45e1cd06c886943b0-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/81222dfee0acfbcca43bce18de7d1be3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/81222dfee0acfbcca43bce18de7d1be3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
配料：葱、八角、月桂叶、蒜末、姜片</p>
<p>调料：海盐、生抽、五香粉、南乳、柱侯酱、美极酱、蚝油、生粉水、冰糖</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将五花肉的皮煎至金黄色取出。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
2. 利用锅余油爆香葱、八角、月桂叶等配料。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
3. 放热水至淹过五花肉，慢火煮约25分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
4. 猛火阴油炒香蒜末，放入切好的梅干菜炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
5. 取出煮熟的五花肉，放生抽上色至均匀。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
6. 用八成油温将五花肉烧至金黄色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
7. 将烧好的五花肉切厚片，放入生抽等调料后拌均匀。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
8. 锅底放调料配芡汁，然后放入扣肉和梅菜的碗中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
9. 放入蒸箱蒸2个小时。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9-1-450x286.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="286"></a><br />
10. 蒸好后倒出汤汁配绿色青菜。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/10-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/10-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><br />
11. 将盆中菜肴倒扣入盘中，再次放芡汁。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/11-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12460501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/11-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a>
