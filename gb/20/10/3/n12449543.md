<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在家煮“栗子巧克力奶茶” 乳糖不耐也可以喝！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/caafa91f98d83eb1209b0f5b6f479810-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>奶茶香中蕴含着淡淡的甜栗味。（图／商周出版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：秋天是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%97%E5%AD%90.md#1">栗子</a>盛产的季节，圆滚滚的栗子在小石头内翻炒，或是在炭火上烧烤得啪哒作响，这都是很多人的美味记忆。这一道手作栗子<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E8%8C%B6.md#1">奶茶</a>，让我们在奶茶香中感受淡淡的甜栗味，品尝出金秋的迷人气息。假如你不能喝牛奶，那么这次准备的杏仁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%97%E5%AD%90.md#1">栗子</a>奶茶也很适合你！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12451433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12451433" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_517264075.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12451433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_517264075-450x229.jpg" alt="栗子" width="450" b="229" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12451433" class="wp-caption-text">秋天是吃烤栗子的季节。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;">栗子巧克力<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E8%8C%B6.md#1">奶茶</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">材料</span></h3>
<p>栗子<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8C%B6.md#1">红茶</a>6g<br />
杏仁奶180ml<br />
巧克力酱20ml<br />
装饰用可可粉</p>
<figure id="attachment_12451434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12451434" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_602988734.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12451434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_602988734-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12451434" class="wp-caption-text">可可粒、巧克力块、巧克力酱、巧克力粉（由左至右）都很适合巧妙的和奶茶做搭配，苦甜的滋味特别有青春活力的气息。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 在牛奶锅中放入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E5%8F%B6.md#1">茶叶</a>和杏仁奶，均匀搅拌使茶叶充分浸润。<br />
Tip：煮茶的同时，在杯中倒入热水温杯后，再把水倒掉。煮茶的途中就算需要加入其它食材混合，也不需要额外增加浸泡的时间，茶的味道和香气还是会充分释出。浸泡太久反而会泡出<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E5%8F%B6.md#1">茶叶</a>中苦涩的味道，影响奶茶的风味，请务必小心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12451436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12451436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1072313420.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12451436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1072313420-450x300.jpg" alt="栗子" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12451436" class="wp-caption-text">栗子<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8C%B6.md#1">红茶</a>跟香醇的杏仁奶也很搭！（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将1. 以小火加热，待牛奶锅边缘开始出现小气泡，茶叶舒展开来之后，倒入巧克力酱均匀混合后关火。<br />
3. 用滤网过滤泡好的茶叶，将奶茶倒入奶泡杯中，再使用电动奶泡器打出奶泡。<br />
4. 将打出奶泡的奶茶倒入杯中，撒上装饰用可可粉，完成。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">NOTE</span></h3>
<p>? 栗子红茶是加入栗子香气的调味红茶，跟香醇的杏仁奶很搭。如果不用栗子红茶，可以使用适合搭配杏仁滋味，香气浓郁的中国红茶或者印度红茶。<br />
? 用一般牛奶代替杏仁奶，也能和栗子的香气调和得很好。</p>
