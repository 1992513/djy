<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】烤牛油磅蛋糕 浓郁奶香享健康</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/10-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>奶香浓郁的磅奶油蛋糕，只要跟著作法做，零失败又简单。（图／梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>每一口都是浓郁奶香的湿润扎实口感！磅奶油蛋糕是常见的蛋糕，但是要怎么烘培才会好吃，有个中诀窍，这一次让我们跟着梁厨的脚步做“餐厅版本”的蛋糕。刚好中共病毒防疫期间，人人都注重健康和卫生安全，在家做糕点，家人品尝也安心。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>淡牛油 8oz（放室温至软）<br />
面粉7oz<br />
发粉1茶匙<br />
白砂糖4oz<br />
鸡蛋4个<br />
盐半茶匙<br />
鲜奶4汤匙<br />
香草精一茶匙</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 预热烤箱350℉。取一烤蛋糕长型盆（8寸乘8寸），涂上牛油备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12484015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12484015" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-20.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12484015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-20-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12484015" class="wp-caption-text">预热烤箱350℉，取一烤蛋糕长型盆（8寸乘8寸），涂上牛油备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 面粉、发粉和盐拌匀之后，过筛备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12484016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12484016" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12484016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-16-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12484016" class="wp-caption-text">面粉、发粉和盐拌匀之后，过筛备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 用打蛋器将糖和牛油打至淡黄色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12484017" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12484017" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-15.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12484017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-15-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12484017" class="wp-caption-text">用打蛋器将糖和牛油打至淡黄色。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 接着再逐一加入鸡蛋，分4次加入，打至顺滑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12484018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12484018" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12484018" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12484018" class="wp-caption-text">分4次加入鸡蛋，用搅拌器打至顺滑。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 加入香草精混和，接着再加入面粉，轻轻翻入蛋浆内，最后加鲜奶拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12484019" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12484019" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12484019" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12484019" class="wp-caption-text">加入香草精混和，接着再加入面粉，轻轻翻入蛋浆内，最后加鲜奶拌匀。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将“做法5”倒入烤盘内，轻轻用手摇匀，放入预热好的烤箱内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12484020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12484020" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12484020" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12484020" class="wp-caption-text">将混合均匀的面团倒入蛋糕模内。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 蛋糕烤约40分钟，烤到蛋糕呈金黄色熟透为止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12484021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12484021" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12484021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-9-450x303.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12484021" class="wp-caption-text">蛋糕烤约40分钟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 取出蛋糕放凉5分钟后，趁热切开来享用。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_12484022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12484022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12484022" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12484022" class="wp-caption-text">取出蛋糕放凉5分钟后切开享用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
