<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狮宝环岛送爱心助学 汇集善的力量社会更好</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464536-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>全员大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年10月16日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">国际狮子会</a>的广大视野及胸怀受社会的肯定，长期关怀弱势族群的国际狮子会300-D1区发起劝募<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%BF%83%E5%8A%A9%E5%AD%A6%E5%8C%85.md#1">爱心助学包</a>，此活动由2014-2015年度台南市新化狮子会会长许德华发起，今年轮到台南市博仁狮子会主办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12480799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464537.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464537-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480799" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市议员张秀华接受记者联访。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台南市博仁狮子会由会长黄子哲带领，11位勇士以重型机车<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E5%B2%9B.md#1">环岛</a>的方式，为期6天5夜的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E7%9B%8A%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">公益活动</a>，将资源分送给全台10所偏乡小学，包括台东县、宜兰县、桃园市、南投县、云林县、嘉义市及台南市等学校，于16日上午抵达嘉义市北园国小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12480800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480800" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464538.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464538-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480800" class="wp-caption-text">总监张秀华赠送每位骑士纪念品，由今年主办的博仁狮子会会长黄子哲（右）代表接受。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>渠等秉持在服务的过程中认识自我，及对全球及当地社区作出贡献，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">国际狮子会</a>300-D1区<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E5%B2%9B.md#1">环岛</a>送爱心助学今年已迈入第七届，希望透过“WE SERVE”的精神，为下一代的教育尽份心力，将服务公益的精神发扬光大，传送到全台湾每一个角落，以实际行动将“爱”送到偏乡，让更多人一同重视偏乡教育问题，响应爱心助学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12480801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464539.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464539-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480801" class="wp-caption-text">嘉华狮子会第六分区主席郑丽兰（左）加码赠送营养品给北园国小，由校长吴长颖代表接受。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市议员张秀华称，从募款一路到筹办活动，就是希望透过社会服务唤起大众的重视，一起加入社会公益，延续这份爱心，因了解北园国小需要助学的学童较多，选择将助学包送到这里，同时也致赠学校助学金1万元，让偏乡孩子能背起书包快乐上学之外，也能感受社会温暖，受助学生会因为这个机会来认识狮子会，长大后能想到加入狮子会，跟咱们一起来做社会服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12480802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480802" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464540.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464540-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480802" class="wp-caption-text">台南市博仁狮子会第廿一届会长吴呈隆（左）加赠参万元红包给北园国小，其照顾偏乡儿童的精神值得效法。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉凤狮子会会长罗惠美感谢发起人许德华狮友与今年主办的博仁狮子会会长黄子哲，透过环岛送爱心，提供需要帮助的孩童实用的助学包，让孩子们开心享受上学求知的乐趣，将爱送到各个社会角落，哪里有需要狮子会就到哪里，只要有需要狮子会从来不喊苦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12480803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480803" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464541.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464541-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480803" class="wp-caption-text">嘉凤狮子会会长罗惠美（左）代表该会致赠社会服务金给北园国小。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>欧前总监镇平表示，环岛送爱心活动已办理第七年，而狮子骑院去年才组成，今天有11位勇士来到现场，他们年龄最长68岁、最年轻30来岁，他们骑重型机车环岛一周，由台南出发、到花莲、苏澳、桃园、嘉义等地，不计较汗水及疲累感，每天骑200多公里深入偏乡送爱心，因为他们觉得这是很有意义的活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12480804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464543.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464543-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480804" class="wp-caption-text">北园国小由校长吴长颖（左）回赠感谢状给嘉凤狮子会。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张秀华最后表示，有他们的爱心才有狮子会今天在国际上壹百多年的历史，谢谢狮友你们的付出，你们的贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12480805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464544.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464544-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480805" class="wp-caption-text">北园国小由校长吴长颖（左）回赠感谢状给今年主办的博仁狮子会会长黄子哲。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12480806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464545.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464545-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480806" class="wp-caption-text">国际狮子会300-D1区发起劝募<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%BF%83%E5%8A%A9%E5%AD%A6%E5%8C%85.md#1">爱心助学包</a>，让偏乡孩子能背起书包快乐上学之外，也能感受社会温暖，期许借着环岛送爱心之旅，让更多人愿意投入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E7%9B%8A%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">公益活动</a>，帮助更多弱势团体。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12480807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12480807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464546.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12480807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464546-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12480807" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市议员张秀华称，她小时候家住在北园国小学区，是北园国小的校友，了解此地学生的家庭背景，工农较多，是需要各界多些关心的。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
