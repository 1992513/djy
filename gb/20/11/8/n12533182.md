<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>让家人吃好油！手揉“橄榄油酪梨面包”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6f46fe3f2854ef37087c33a69944c15f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>橄榄油酪梨腰果面包，健康美味！（图／日日幸福提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>酪梨通常是拿来做沙拉，在这里酪梨取代了本来面团材料的油脂角色，将酪梨果肉打入面团中，让面包的色泽美丽，吃起来也更加柔软。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">前置作业</span></h2>
<p>腰果事先以150℃烤15分钟至有香气，略切至葡萄干大小。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">份量 </span></h2>
<p>10个 / 每个60g</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">面团材料?</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">A</span></h3>
<p>高筋面粉 255g<br />
低筋面粉 65g<br />
细砂糖 20g<br />
盐 5g<br />
低糖酵母粉 3.2g<br />
酪梨果肉 130g<br />
水 95g<br />
初榨橄榄油 10g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">B</span></h3>
<p>腰果 50g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">表面装饰</span></h3>
<p>高筋面粉˙˙˙˙ 适量</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">做法 Steps</span></h2>
<p>1、将A材料倒入搅拌机的钢盆中搅拌面团至可扩展的状态，取出面团后包入B材料切拌均匀，基本发酵大约60分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12533207" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12533207" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/b5733fe63057535cbb266a7e3243d87c.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12533207 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/b5733fe63057535cbb266a7e3243d87c-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12533207" class="wp-caption-text">将A材料倒入搅拌机的钢盆中搅拌面团至可扩展的状态，取出面团后包入B材料切拌均匀，基本发酵大约60分钟。 （图文／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2、将面团分割成10个，滚圆后进行中间发酵20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12533209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12533209" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2e85c6983b8ae8fb5ac24a3e8289a57b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12533209 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2e85c6983b8ae8fb5ac24a3e8289a57b-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12533209" class="wp-caption-text">面团分割成10个。（图文／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3、轻拍面团排掉大气泡，将一半面团再次滚圆；另一半做成椭圆形，再做最后发酵30～40分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12533205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12533205" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3f9823d533c7b4272f5905f0d13f471c.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12533205 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3f9823d533c7b4272f5905f0d13f471c-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12533205" class="wp-caption-text">轻拍面团排掉大气泡，将一半面团再次滚圆；另一半做成椭圆形。（图文／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4、烤箱预热220℃；烤焙前面团表面撒高筋粉、划线，再放进烤箱烤15分钟即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12533204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12533204" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4fd79494c864172c149b35ef2454dd98.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12533204 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4fd79494c864172c149b35ef2454dd98-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12533204" class="wp-caption-text">烤箱预热220℃；烤焙前面团表面撒高筋粉、划线。（图文／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12533198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12533198" style="width: 150px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8c81ada9c01afd1e016e8f44abca4f42-e1604798600330.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12533198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8c81ada9c01afd1e016e8f44abca4f42-e1604798794607.jpg" alt="小面包" width="150" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12533198" class="wp-caption-text">《小面包 Little Bread》书封（图／日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
