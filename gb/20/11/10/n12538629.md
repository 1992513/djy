<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《不读书俱乐部》 张再兴被林映唯发好人卡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011100237182122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《不读书俱乐部》记者会于2020年11月10日在台北举行。（黄宗茂／大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年11月10日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）网剧《不读书俱乐部》10日举办上线记者会，林映唯被主持人问及“张再兴是不是你的菜？”她尴尬笑说：“张再兴人很好。”被发卡的张再兴则回说：“你也不是我的菜。”还伤心喊：“我有我的市场”?两人斗嘴展现好交情。</p>
<figure id="12538632" aria-describedby="caption-12538632" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468485-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468485-450x675.jpg" alt="林映唯。　" width="450" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12538632" class="wp-caption-text">林映唯。　（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12538633" aria-describedby="caption-12538633" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468486-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468486-450x675.jpg" alt="张再兴。　" width="450" b="525" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12538633" class="wp-caption-text">张再兴。　（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《不读书俱乐部》演员萧子墨、JR纪言恺、张再兴、林映唯、陈加恩以及导演刘亚菱连袂出席，期许打造台版《六人行》。</p>
<p>该剧卡司有近期以《可不可以，你也刚好喜欢我》突破千万票房的女明星林映唯，以及金钟新人奖李沐镶金，JR纪言恺直指：“出品方跟制作方根本是赚到啦！”</p>
<p>JR纪言恺也戏称男主角萧子墨打扮很像“徐志摩”，萧子墨也不落人后，把不在场的李沐拖下水，说绑丸子头的她像“纸扎人”，两人因连夜拍戏十分疲惫，站在一起仿佛是“纸扎兄妹”，半夜让工作人员觉得“阴森森”。</p>
<figure id="12538637" aria-describedby="caption-12538637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468490-450x319.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468490-450x319.jpg" alt="　" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12538637" class="wp-caption-text">众演员出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12538636" aria-describedby="caption-12538636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468489-450x315.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468489-450x315.jpg" alt="图左起为萧子墨、JR纪言恺。　" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12538636" class="wp-caption-text">图左起为萧子墨、JR纪言恺。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12538635" aria-describedby="caption-12538635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468488-450x428.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468488-450x428.jpg" alt="萧子墨搞笑喂食李沐的人牌。" width="600" b="571" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12538635" class="wp-caption-text">萧子墨搞笑喂食李沐的人形立牌。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>女主角之一的李沐因另有工作不克出席，剧组贴心准备了“人形立牌”，更让演员们好心喂她现场“吃面”，画面十分逗趣；新人演员陈加恩演出韩国练习生，现场也秀一段韩文祝贺戏剧大卖。</p>
<p>音乐剧版的男主角周定纬在最后一集客串现身，他现身站台赠送小吉他礼物，夸赞：“萧子墨是音乐剧的颜值升级版。”另一客串的演员还有美女棋士黑嘉嘉。</p>
<figure id="12538640" aria-describedby="caption-12538640" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468493-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468493-450x675.jpg" alt="新人演员陈加恩。　" width="450" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12538640" class="wp-caption-text">新人演员陈加恩。　（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12538639" aria-describedby="caption-12538639" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468492-450x660.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468492-450x660.jpg" alt="林映唯。" width="450" b="513" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12538639" class="wp-caption-text">林映唯。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12538638" aria-describedby="caption-12538638" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468491-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468491-450x675.jpg" alt="周定纬。" width="450" b="525" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12538638" class="wp-caption-text">周定纬。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《不读书俱乐部》由音乐剧改编，是台湾首部以“泡面剧”为主题的网剧，“泡面剧”对许多台湾观众还是陌生的词汇，早先由日、韩引领风潮，意即一碗泡面的时间就可看完1集，约10到15分钟，十分符合现代人通勤、快生活的需求。</p>
