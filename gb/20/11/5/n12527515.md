<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>想要增肌减脂吗？隔夜燕麦简易上手</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/shutterstock_388506751-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>隔夜燕麦搭配各种水果，营养价值高。（示意图）（图／shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：隔夜燕麦是很多控制体重、维持身材的健身人士爱好的餐点，但是怎么搭配才会好吃不失败呢？添加什么材料可以确保达到油脂、蛋白质足够，下面食谱可以尝试看看！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">隔夜燕麦</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12527629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12527629" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/472ee574cf1b4f4fba91db320881ef83.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12527629 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/472ee574cf1b4f4fba91db320881ef83-450x573.jpg" alt="隔夜燕麦" width="450" b="573" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12527629" class="wp-caption-text">隔夜燕麦制作简单。（图／悦知文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>热量527kcal<br />
碳水化合物83g<br />
脂肪18g<br />
蛋白质18g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">◎ 材料</span></h3>
<p>燕麦?70g<br />
有机奇亚籽?15g<br />
无糖杏仁奶?200ml<br />
PB2花生酱?10g<br />
巴西莓粉?10g<br />
0卡甜菊液?10g<br />
蓝莓?100g<br />
香蕉?40g</p>
<figure id="attachment_12527590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12527590" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/shutterstock_1151797121.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12527590 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/shutterstock_1151797121-450x300.jpg" alt="巴西莓粉" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12527590" class="wp-caption-text">巴西莓粉，具有丰富花青素。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12527631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12527631" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/shutterstock_1639601440.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-12527631 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/shutterstock_1639601440-450x338.jpg" alt="花生" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12527631" class="wp-caption-text">PB2花生粉比一般花生酱热量低，花生粉搭配水或牛奶，混匀后即可食用，相当方便，是很多健身人士的最爱。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">◎ 作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 取一个玻璃罐，加入燕麦、有机奇亚籽、无糖杏仁奶、一匙PB2花生巧克力酱、巴西莓粉和0卡甜菊液搅拌均匀。<br />
2. 将作法1放上蓝莓与香蕉片，盖上盖子放入冰箱冷藏一晚。<br />
3. 隔天早上从冰箱取出，即可马上食用！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>◎ 雀儿料理笔记</strong></span></h3>
<p>做法相当简单，将所有材料都放入玻璃罐中即可。放一夜冷藏，可让风味更加融合。巴西莓粉有丰富的维生素、矿物质、花青素、Omega-3及莓果多酚，抗氧化效果是蓝莓的22倍，是最近风靡欧美的超级水果之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12505122" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12505122" style="width: 150px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7378e9aa163be9a05da5243791465a24-e1603785772216.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12505122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7378e9aa163be9a05da5243791465a24-e1603785772216.jpg" alt="减脂力" width="150" b="172" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12505122" class="wp-caption-text">减脂力！21天有感快瘦计划书封。（图／悦知文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
