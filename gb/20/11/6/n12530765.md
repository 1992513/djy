<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】宽粉炖鱼头</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/471e92586bbbdfb7890307cf608d717c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香嫩的鱼头搭配东北宽粉，充满浓郁的农家味道，看着不起眼的一道菜好吃到无法停口，而且超级下饭。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>鱼头，自古就是一道美味，很多人都非常爱吃鱼头。鱼头富含胶质，肉质腴美，食之有暖胃补虚的功效，常吃鱼头可以健脑并增智。</p>
<p>鱼头营养丰富，含有鱼肉中缺乏的卵磷脂，还有丰富的DHA。DHA是一种高度不饱和脂肪酸，对促进神经细胞，特别是神经传导和神经纤维细胞生长发育极为重要，乃健脑之上品。猪、牛、羊等畜肉中DHA含量不超过10%，而鱼头远远高出这个水平，尤其是鱼眼，堪称DHA的富矿。另外，鱼头内的锌和铁微量元素较高，这些微量元素对大脑确有天然的滋补作用。</p>
<p>正因为鱼头自有诱人之处，所以中国各地有不少“鱼头”名菜，如江苏的“拆烩鲢鱼头”、杭州的“砂锅鱼头豆腐”、宁波的“干烧鱼头”、苏州的“砂锅鱼头”等，还有广东的“鱼头煲”和四川的“浣花鱼头”。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%BD%E7%B2%89%E7%82%96%E9%B1%BC%E5%A4%B4.md#1">宽粉炖鱼头</a>是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%A9%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲩鱼</a>系列菜肴之一。香嫩的鱼头搭配东北宽粉，充满浓郁的农家味道，看着不起眼的一道菜好吃到无法停口，而且超级下饭。鱼肉嫩滑味浓且不俗，宽粉鱼味十足熟而不烂。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%BD%E7%B2%89%E7%82%96%E9%B1%BC%E5%A4%B4.md#1">宽粉炖鱼头</a></h2>
<p>主料：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%A9%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲩鱼</a>鱼头一个<br />
配料：葱、姜、蒜、宽粉<br />
调料：盐、生粉、八角、花椒、料酒、老抽、生抽、胡椒粉、糖</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将鱼头洗干净吸干水分，用生粉腌制后待用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 将香料葱、姜、蒜爆香后捞起。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 起锅倒油，鱼头两面都煎到金黄色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 去除余油，放入香料。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 调入生粉、八角、花椒等调料。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加盖慢火炖10分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 放入浸泡好的宽粉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/7-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/7-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 放入些许料酒后再炖15分钟到鱼头酥软。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 将宽粉捞起放于碟中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/9-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531072" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/9-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 将鱼头捞起后淋上浓汁。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/10-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/10-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 最后在上面洒些葱花。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/11-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12531075" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/11-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
