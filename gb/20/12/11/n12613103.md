<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>杨祐宁首谈为人父：还在跟女儿培养感情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434302122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>电影《腿》上映记者会于2020年12月11日在台北举行。图左起为桂纶镁、杨祐宁。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月11日讯】（大纪元记者黄钟台湾台北报导）金马影后桂纶镁（小镁）与杨祐宁11日出席电影《腿》上映记者会，杨祐宁除了分享升格父亲的心情，也表示自己曾和舒淇、陈妍希、周迅、周冬雨等大咖女演员合作，这次终于等到桂纶镁，直呼：“圈内最难合作到的人！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12613685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613685" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110435092122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110435092122-ss1.jpg" alt="桂纶镁" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12613685" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613685" class="wp-caption-text">桂纶镁出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12613682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613682" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110435022122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110435022122-ss1.jpg" alt="杨祐宁" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12613682" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613682" class="wp-caption-text">杨祐宁出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导演张耀升率领主演桂纶镁、杨祐宁与张少怀盛装出席，这也是杨祐宁成为奶爸之后首度露面。</p>
<p>杨祐宁过去曾合作过的大咖女演员包括舒淇、陈妍希、周迅、周冬雨等，他坦言：“桂纶镁是圈内最难合作到的人，我工作这么久，碰到她那么多次，终于可以合作到。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12613688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613688" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110435052122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110435052122-ss1.jpg" alt="腿" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12613688" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613688" class="wp-caption-text">桂纶镁被其他演员称为是“圈内最难合作到的人”。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张少怀也点头表示：“我也真的没想到有机会能跟她合作。”主持人黄子佼也帮腔︰“真的，我也没想过我可以再跟她同台，下次十年后见。”让桂纶镁笑到不行。</p>
<p>桂纶镁与杨祐宁在戏中因跳舞结缘，两人特地在开拍前勤练舞蹈，一天练5到7小时，杨祐宁说：“小镁比较辛苦，女生一直要延伸颈椎，所以在练习期间她的颈椎一直不太舒服。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12613669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613669" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434462122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434462122-ss1.jpg" alt="桂纶镁" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12613669" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613669" class="wp-caption-text">桂纶镁（左）与杨祐宁共谱情缘。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12613677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613677" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434502122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434502122-ss1.jpg" alt="张耀升" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12613677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613677" class="wp-caption-text">导演张耀升（左）与张少怀搞笑共舞。　（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，初为人父的杨祐宁也首度公开畅聊当爸心情，他说原本以为女儿出生时自己会哭，甚至会感动到讲不出话，结果却很冷静。他笑说：“我每天都会跟肚子说话，所以我觉得自己会跟她很熟，结果女儿出生后，我看到她的脸却觉得很陌生，所以现在还在培养感情中。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12613693" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613693" style="width: 429px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434182122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434182122-ss1.jpg" alt="杨祐宁" width="450" b="599" class="size-full wp-image-12613693" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613693" class="wp-caption-text">杨祐宁首度公开畅聊当爸的心情。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前一晚适逢全台有感地震，杨祐宁说，当下第一时间并未察觉，直到听到太太的呼叫声才赶快蹲低，他先把门打开后再去找太太和女儿，“结果小孩睡得很好，可能她常被摇。”同样是爸爸的张少怀在地震当下正要睡觉，他说：“我以为是小孩在踢，还一直凶他们说不要踢了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12613696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613696" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434582122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434582122-ss1.jpg" alt="张少怀" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12613696" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613696" class="wp-caption-text">张少怀出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>《同学麦娜丝》接棒传好运</h2>
<p>而获得金马奖的《同学麦娜丝》派来神秘嘉宾，举行“同学有一腿”祝贺仪式，由演员陈以文与施名帅代表剧组送上创意贺礼“烤鸡腿抱枕”，象征台湾电影接棒再创口碑票房佳绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12613697" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613697" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434382122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434382122-ss1-600x507.jpg" alt="同学麦娜丝" width="600" b="507" class="size-large wp-image-12613697" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613697" class="wp-caption-text">获得金马奖的《同学麦娜丝》派出演员陈以文（左1）与施名帅（右1）代表剧组为《腿》送礼祝贺。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《腿》荣获本届金马奖最佳女主角、男配角、原著剧本与造型设计等四项大奖提名，并入选东京影展、香港亚洲电影节与夏威夷国际影展。剧情藉由一段笑点、泪点齐发的寻腿之旅带领观众看见爱情的模样，定于12月24日圣诞档上映。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12613698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12613698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434032122-ss1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012110434032122-ss1-600x399.jpg" alt="腿" width="600" b="399" class="size-large wp-image-12613698" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12613698" class="wp-caption-text">电影《腿》上映记者会在台北举行，（左起）导演张耀升率领主演桂纶镁、杨祐宁与张少怀盛装出席。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
