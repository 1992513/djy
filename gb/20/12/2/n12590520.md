<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>好吃又纾压 美式燕麦软饼颠覆你的想法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_1130833784-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>年终派对要来了！手感温度的燕麦饼干也是送礼的好选择。(图／shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：喀滋、喀滋！香酥脆的饼干一口接一口，真的很难停下来耶！尤其坐在沙发上，一边看着泡沫剧一边吃着饼干，就会觉得很放松。我也从来没想过要吃健康的食物！但是自从咬了一口美式燕麦软饼，那好吃的滋味令人有种很纾压的感受，想要自制这款健康的饼干？那么看过来吧！相信你也一样可以轻松驾驭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12592028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12592028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_345372449.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12592028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_345372449-450x300.jpg" alt="燕麦饼干" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12592028" class="wp-caption-text">如果喜欢吃酥脆感的燕麦饼可以尝试加入坚果、水果干碎片，并延长烘烤时间。(图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">有咀嚼口感的美式软饼</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>燕麦片100g<br />
燕麦粉40g<br />
香蕉1 根（100g）<br />
甜菊糖20g<br />
葡萄籽油15 ～ 20g（或花生酱）<br />
肉桂粉1/2 小匙<br />
泡打粉1/8 小匙<br />
香草精1/2 ～ 1/4 小匙（可省略）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11305878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11305878" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/oat-powder_521620303.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11305878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/oat-powder_521620303-450x300.jpg" alt="被蚊子咬得全身痒，不妨泡个燕麦浴。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11305878" class="wp-caption-text">燕麦片100公克，燕麦粉40公克（图／Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">推荐这些配料！</span></h3>
<p>? 巧克力碎片<br />
? 坚果类<br />
? 果干</p>
<figure id="attachment_12450244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12450244" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-03_222235.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12450244" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-03_222235-450x231.png" alt="每天适量吃坚果，有助减少癌症发生率。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12450244" class="wp-caption-text">坚果有好的油脂和香气，作为燕麦饼干的配料也很对味！（图／Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 将香蕉放入碗中压碎。<br />
* 烤箱预热170 度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12600053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12600053" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/660aa5a77d3f202e25e9bb22b15808c3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-12600053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/660aa5a77d3f202e25e9bb22b15808c3.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12600053" class="wp-caption-text">将香蕉放入碗中压碎（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2 将配料以外的所有材料放入碗中，搅拌均匀后，加入配料稍微混合。<br />
3 将烤盘铺上料理纸，用冰淇淋挖勺（或汤匙）挖起面团，以5cm 的大小铺在烘培纸上。<br />
* 烤箱预热170 度。<br />
* 用挖勺挖起面团后，一定要用力压平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12600055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12600055" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/92298e0f84daf638076b12f511f15936.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-12600055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/92298e0f84daf638076b12f511f15936.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="300" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12600055" class="wp-caption-text">用冰淇淋挖勺（或汤匙）挖起面团，约5cm 的大小。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12600056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12600056" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/eca3afb7bcad5118c97fb153757260eb.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-12600056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/eca3afb7bcad5118c97fb153757260eb.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12600056" class="wp-caption-text">用挖勺挖起面团后，一定要用力压平（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4 放入预热170 度的烤箱中，烤约20 ～ 25 分钟，烤至变成黄褐色，表面些许裂开的状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12600052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12600052" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2f10415439b3cecf7e45f8f578aebe33.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12600052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2f10415439b3cecf7e45f8f578aebe33-450x540.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12600052" class="wp-caption-text">放入预热170 度的烤箱中，烤约20 ～ 25 分钟。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000080;">Tip 用气炸锅代替烤箱制作</span>：进行到步骤3 时，将面团放入气炸锅中，并紧紧压平面团，使其厚度为0.5cm。先以160 度烤10 分钟，翻面后再烤5 ～8 分钟，直到呈现黄褐色为止。<strong>@</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12590820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12590820" style="width: 102px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/eefe36f2e9e4dd38e14287c6fc5d660e-e1606912703904.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12590820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/eefe36f2e9e4dd38e14287c6fc5d660e-450x659.jpg" alt="燕麦" width="102" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12590820" class="wp-caption-text">《好想吃喔！燕麦的美味新吃法》书封。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
