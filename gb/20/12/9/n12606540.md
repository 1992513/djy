<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】炸鸡排丼饭 好吃关键在蛋汁淋酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/11-23-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日式炸鸡排丼饭好吃的关键是“蛋汁淋酱”。（图／梁厨美食提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>咬一口酥脆的炸鸡排，一扫灰黑疲倦感，这就是炸物的神奇之处。但是要吃到咸香有口感的满意炸鸡有时候还是不太容易，不是太油腻、不够酥、面粉粿太多，或是没有肉汁太干。这主要是因为“炸物”本身就是一项不容易的料理；食材大小及厚度、油温控制、下锅时间、面衣选择，有太多变化因素存在。所以，多尝试才会找到自己喜爱的料理，今天推荐你餐厅主厨的食谱，试试看，你可能会满意！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">日式炸鸡排饭</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>鸡胸肉2块 8oz<br />
洋葱半个<br />
鸡蛋2个<br />
葱2棵<br />
盐、胡椒粉少许（腌鸡排用）<br />
煮熟白饭2碗</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【炸鸡排用材料】</span></h3>
<p>面粉、鸡蛋液、面包糠各适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>柴鱼汤1杯（或者用烹大师1茶匙加开水1杯）<br />
酱油2汤匙<br />
味醂2汤匙<br />
糖1茶匙<br />
盐半茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606623" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606623" class="wp-caption-text">调味材料：柴鱼汤、味醂、糖、盐、酱油。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 鸡肉轻轻拍扁后，用刀子在肉上划刀纹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606624" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606624" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉轻轻拍扁后，用刀子在肉上划刀纹。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 鸡肉放在碗中，洒上盐和胡椒粉腌一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606625" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606625" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉放在碗中，洒上盐和胡椒粉腌一下。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 腌好的鸡肉按照顺序沾上面粉、鸡蛋和面包糠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606626" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606626" class="wp-caption-text">腌好的鸡肉按照顺序沾上面粉、鸡蛋和面包糠。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 用中大火油温，将鸡肉炸至金黄色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606627" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606627" class="wp-caption-text">用中大火油温，将鸡肉炸至金黄色。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 待鸡排熟透后，取出切块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606628" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606628" class="wp-caption-text">待鸡排熟透后，取出切块备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 洋葱切长条，葱切段，鸡蛋打匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606629" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606629" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱切长条，葱切段。 （图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 取一锅加入所有调味煮滚，接着加入洋葱和葱煮一段时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606636" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606636" class="wp-caption-text">取一锅加入所有调味煮滚，接着加入洋葱和葱煮一段时间。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 放入鸡排，再放入鸡蛋液，蛋液半熟时，再撒上剩下的葱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606638" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606638" class="wp-caption-text">放入鸡排，再放入鸡蛋液，蛋液半熟时，再撒上剩下的葱。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 将鸡排和酱汁从锅中取出，淋在热饭上面即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606642" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/13-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12606642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/13-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606642" class="wp-caption-text">日式炸鸡排淋上咸甜酱汁与蛋液，非常好下饭。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【小贴士】</span></h3>
<p>下鸡蛋液时要从锅的中间部分倒入，蛋液的厚度才会均匀。◇</p>
