<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】红烧牛腩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2a5e926f03aafa7d27eb013f3715eda7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨在户外点燃柴火用大生铁锅烧出的牛腩肉，软滑香浓、香气四溢，牛肉的味道保存得相对完美。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>牛腩指牛腹部下的侧肉，即牛腹部及靠近牛肋处的松软肌肉，是指带有筋、肉、油花的肉块，这只是一种统称。</p>
<p>牛腩提供高质量的蛋白质，含有全部种类的氨基酸，各种氨基酸的比例与人体蛋白质中各种氨基酸的比例基本一致，其中所含的肌氨酸比任何食物都高。</p>
<p>牛腩含有矿物质和维他命B群，包括烟酸、维生素B1和核黄素。牛肉还是每天所需要的铁质的最佳来源。牛腩还含肉毒碱。牛腩的脂肪含量很低，却是低脂的亚油酸的来源，还是潜在的抗氧化剂。</p>
<p>若依部位来分，牛身上许多地方的肉都可以叫做牛腩，其中以牛坑腩牛肉味最浓。坑腩（boneless short rib，即无骨牛小排）是取自牛胸前的牛小骨/肋排（short rib）或旁边牛肋条（Finger meat）部位的肉。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道红烧牛腩选用的是坑腩，并以白萝卜为配料。罗厨在户外点燃柴火用大生铁锅烧出的牛腩肉，软滑香浓、香气四溢，牛肉的味道保存得相对完美。和牛腩一起焖煮的白萝卜也特别好吃，萝卜吸收牛腩汁后醇香浓郁，萝卜独特的淡淡甜味令人回味。吃完牛腩和萝卜后，剩余的汤汁用于拌面、拌饭也是不错的选择。</p>
<h2>红烧牛腩</h2>
<p>主料：牛坑腩、白萝卜</p>
<p>配料：姜片、葱段、大料、花椒</p>
<p>调料：柱侯酱、海鲜酱、胡椒粉、黄酒、生抽</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将洗干净的牛坑腩用水煮过。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/d5a52f07dfe6dfaec60e5234c07c3b2a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/d5a52f07dfe6dfaec60e5234c07c3b2a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 将牛腩切成块状。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-18.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-18-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>3. 用热油锅把姜片、葱段、大料、花椒大火爆香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 放入柱侯酱、海鲜酱。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623359" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5. 放入牛腩炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>6. 放入胡椒粉、黄酒、生抽。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7. 添加肉汤，浸过牛腩2公分为好。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>8. 慢火焖40分钟至牛腩足够软滑。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>9. 加入炸过的白萝卜焖3分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623365" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10. 放3汤匙生粉水收汁出锅。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623366" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>11.?最后放入少许葱段上碟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/11-26.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12623375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/11-26-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>

<h2>粤菜名厨罗子昭</h2>
<p>入行近40年，获得过许多荣誉。曾任锦江集团北方公司厨师评委、昆仑饭店中餐出品总监、沈阳市富虹酒店行政总厨，被业内的同行推为“北京粤菜四大名厨”之一。“中国烹饪大师”、“粤港澳名厨”、“亚太厨皇勋章”等头衔都是对他本人精湛厨艺的肯定。2011年，在新唐人电视台主办的第四届全世界中国菜厨技大赛上，他以传统的中国菜一举摘得粤菜金奖。</p>
