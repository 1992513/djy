<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>维州短途旅行好去处 20个景点精彩不断（上）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Wilsons-Promontory-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在威尔逊岬国家公园海滩漫步。（Visit Victoria提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者清心澳洲墨尔本编译报导）圣诞假期不想长途跋涉去旅行？那麽就来看看旅游网站escape推荐的20个维州短途旅游好去处吧。</p>
<h2>莫宁顿半岛（Mornington Peninsula）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12639061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12639061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/146895-56-e1608684179384.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12639061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/146895-56-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12639061" class="wp-caption-text">Mornington Peninsula。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>距墨尔本东南部一个多小时车程的莫宁顿半岛，到处都是美食、美酒和自然风光。<br />
在那里，你可以与海豹、海豚一起游泳，在酒庄品酒并品尝当地美食，还可以探索各种丛林小径。<br />
住宿可以选择附近历史悠久的弗林德斯小镇。</p>
<h2>亚拉河谷（Yarra Valley）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Yarra-Valley-e1608631395925.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12637797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Yarra-Valley-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637797" class="wp-caption-text">Yarra Valley。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚拉河谷距Lake Mountain Alpine度假胜地仅一臂之遥，距墨尔本仅1个半小时车程。<br />
该地区有很多博物馆，特别是塔拉·沃拉（Tarra Warra）艺术博物馆。<br />
步行道、蒸汽火车、登山之旅和郁郁葱葱的花园，都是必去之地。</p>
<h2>本迪戈（Bendigo）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Bendigo-1-e1608631773576.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12637801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Bendigo-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637801" class="wp-caption-text">Bendigo。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>维州第四大城市、内陆小镇本迪戈是周末度假的理想之地。您可以去本迪戈美术馆、坐电车去澳州最深的金矿、在Rosalind Park公园野餐，或去陶器店逛逛。</p>
<h2>巴拉瑞特（Ballarat）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12639063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12639063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/151208-56-e1608684531229.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12639063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/151208-56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12639063" class="wp-caption-text">Ballarat。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>距墨尔本西区仅一小时路程，这座历史悠久的淘金小镇拥有油菜籽田、酒庄和餐馆。在镇上几家画廊漫步也是很好的享受。<br />
这里全年都很好玩，七月还有巴拉瑞特冬季节，人们可以品尝美食、美酒，欣赏娱乐表演。</p>
<h2>卢瑟格兰（Rutherglen）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Rutherglen-e1608631640118.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12637830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Rutherglen-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637830" class="wp-caption-text">Rutherglen。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>距墨累河（Murray River）与新州（NSW）交界处仅10公里，这是一座充满着悠久历史和葡萄酒的前淘金小镇，距墨尔本三个小时车程。</p>
<h2>大洋路（Great Ocean Road）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637835" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Great-Ocean-Road-3-e1608632009874.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12637835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Great-Ocean-Road-3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637835" class="wp-caption-text">Great Ocean Road。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在位于墨尔本西边著名的大洋路上，您可以看到12门徒、学习冲浪，并探索维州最好的国家公园之一奥特威（Otway）国家公园，还可以露营。</p>
<h2>梅通（Metung）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637838" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Metung-e1608632117378.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12637838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Metung-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637838" class="wp-caption-text">Metung。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位于墨尔本以东约四小时车程的梅通，是一个绝佳的海滨度假和品酒好去处。在这里您可以体会当地悠闲的生活方式，钓鱼、学开船，还可以开车20分钟去品尝葡萄酒。</p>
<h2>威尔逊岬（Wilsons Promontory）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Wilsons-Promontory.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12637839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Wilsons-Promontory-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637839" class="wp-caption-text">Wilsons Promontory。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>威尔逊岬国家公园（Wilsons Promontory National Park）位于墨尔本东南三小时车程处，是远离喧嚣的城市生活和深入自然的好去处。在那里，您可以享受丛林漫步、露营、钓鱼和冲浪。</p>
<h2>Queenscliff</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Queenscliff-e1608632456727.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12637845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Queenscliff-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637845" class="wp-caption-text">Queenscliff。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海边小镇Queenscliff在墨尔本以南约1.5小时车程处的贝拉林半岛上，靠近菲利普港湾的入口。该镇可通过公路和渡轮到达，维多利亚时代的建筑遍布街道，安静、优雅地贯穿整个地方。<br />
在那里，您可以欣赏当地音乐节目、品尝每日新鲜海鲜，还可以坐在百年老码头上休闲。</p>
<h2>米尔杜拉（Mildura）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Mildura-e1608632602527.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12637854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Mildura-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637854" class="wp-caption-text">Mildura。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>米尔杜拉距墨尔本不到6小时车程。该镇位于墨累河沿岸，并拥有“盛宴街”美名，从意大利、泰国、西班牙到现代澳大利亚美味餐馆，应有尽有。斯特凡诺餐厅（Stefano）尤其有名。<br />
酒庄和柠檬树林环绕着小镇，您还可以参观艺术中心和乡村音乐节。</p>
