<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】清炖番茄牛肉汤 营养满分</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_1831782052-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>番茄牛肉蔬菜汤，简单好料理，营养又丰富，一锅就能配饭吃到饱！（图／shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：“番茄<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>蔬菜汤”是在乌克兰起源的浓汤，当时加入甜菜被称为“罗宋汤”，后来亚洲人料理大多不加甜菜，加入番茄。这一道炖汤优点是简单炖煮又充满营养，而且冷热都可以食用！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>番茄<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>蔬菜汤</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12619167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619167" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619167" class="wp-caption-text">在香港茶馆，随餐很常附赠一碗“番茄牛肉蔬菜汤”。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%90%8E%E8%85%BF%E8%82%89.md#1">牛后腿肉</a> 1.5磅<br />
蕃茄 六个<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a> 12oz<br />
红萝卜 12oz<br />
西芹 12oz<br />
高丽菜 半个<br />
姜 3片<br />
水（约16杯）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619161" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-12-450x362.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619161" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%90%8E%E8%85%BF%E8%82%89.md#1">牛后腿肉</a> 1.5磅。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>蕃茄汤 1罐<br />
盐 适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>和红萝卜去皮后切大块，高丽菜、蕃茄和西芹同样切大块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6ebe93fde90a03a2fe42e06d059d349e.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6ebe93fde90a03a2fe42e06d059d349e-450x334.png" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619318" class="wp-caption-text">马铃薯、红萝卜去皮切大块，高丽菜、蕃茄和西芹同样切大块备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 牛腿肉切块后用姜汁酒烫过。锅内放水和所有材料，大火煮滚后转中火煮1小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619165" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619165" class="wp-caption-text">牛腿肉切块后用姜汁酒烫过。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 接着再放入番茄和高丽菜，煮1.5小时左右。<br />
4. 最后加入蕃茄汤以及适量盐调味，便可上桌食用。<strong>@</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12619319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f44a343944cd8c57118059c11ed5d17e.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f44a343944cd8c57118059c11ed5d17e-450x329.png" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619319" class="wp-caption-text">最后加入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84%E6%B1%A4.md#1">番茄汤</a>、些许盐巴就可以上桌。（图／梁厨美食）</figcaption></figure>
