<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>掉发显老？2处穴位按压随时保养</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5c44692ee15892c84d7b39a59a258244-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>忽然发现这阵子掉很多头发？保养头发可以尝试按压反射区穴位。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #188638;"><strong>【编者按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%89%E5%8F%91.md#1">掉发</a>已经不是中年男士的象征了，现在很多年轻人也掉发。按压手脚两处<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E5%B0%84%E5%8C%BA.md#1">反射区</a>穴位，可以保养<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B4%E5%8F%91.md#1">头发</a>。</span></p>
<p>环境污染并非唯一会造成毛发问题的原因，疲劳、荷尔蒙、压力等，都有影响。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B4%E5%8F%91.md#1">头发</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E5%B0%84%E5%8C%BA.md#1">反射区</a>恰好就在指甲上，必须用力夹掐才有效。按压时精油建议搭配“柠檬”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12594079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12594079" style="width: 394px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/fanshequ-toufa-1203-health11.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12594079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/fanshequ-toufa-1203-health11.jpg" alt="头发的反射区恰好就在指甲，图为足部和手部反射区。（采实文化提供／大纪元合成）" width="394" b="537" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12594079" class="wp-caption-text">头发的反射区恰好就在指甲，图为足部和手部反射区。（采实文化提供／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>选用合适的洗发精、护发乳或其他护发产品是必要的，也要特别注意氯（如游泳池）、安全帽（如机车、脚踏车）和品质不佳的洗发精（如含硅的洗发精）。</p>
