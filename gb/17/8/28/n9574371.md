<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：这些食物让你美白又漂亮</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者罗蓉蓉综合报导）夏季到了，就算<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%99%92.md#1">防晒</a>、墨镜、遮阳伞齐上阵，也是难逃无孔不入的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E5%A4%96%E7%BA%BF.md#1">紫外线</a>！想要<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E7%99%BD.md#1">美白</a>，多吃点抗光感的食物就可以了，以下几种食物，可以轮着吃，每天吃，对皮肤真的很有好处，不单单是美白，还能祛<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E6%96%91.md#1">黑斑</a>。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>柠檬</strong></span></h2>
<p>水果中最能<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E7%99%BD.md#1">美白</a>皮肤的莫过于柠檬，因为柠檬中含有丰富的维生素C和钙质，多吃柠檬可以增白皮肤、去除色斑、紧致肤质、消除自身的疲劳和肌肤的抗老化作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574381" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/lemon-600x375.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574381 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/lemon-600x375.jpg" alt="柠檬。(dennisyang988/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574381" class="wp-caption-text">柠檬。(dennisyang988/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>苹果</strong></span></h2>
<p>苹果营养丰富，是一种广泛使用的天然<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%AE%B9.md#1">美容</a>品，被许多爱美人士奉为美容圣品。苹果中所含的大量水分和各种保湿因子对皮肤有保湿作用，维他命C能抑制皮肤中黑色素的沉着，常食苹果可淡化面部雀斑及黄褐斑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574385" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/apple-1589874_640-e1503928606396.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574385 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/apple-1589874_640-e1503928606396.jpg" alt="苹果。(Couleur/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574385" class="wp-caption-text">苹果。(Couleur/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>樱桃</strong></span></h2>
<p>樱桃中含有丰富的维生素A，可以有效的滋润皮肤，对皮肤起到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%AE%B9.md#1">美容</a>的作用，能够有效的抵抗黑色素的形成。新鲜樱桃中含有糖、蛋白质、β胡萝卜素、铁等丰富的营养，而铁是血液中血红素的重要成分，血液充足可以使肌肤变得白里透红，而β胡萝卜素以及维生素C都是皮肤美白的重要元素，多吃樱桃可以使皮肤快速变白。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574386" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/cherry-167341_640-e1503928639811.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574386 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/cherry-167341_640-e1503928639811.jpg" alt="樱桃。(Hans Braxmeier/ CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574386" class="wp-caption-text">樱桃。(Hans Braxmeier/ CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>鱼类</strong></span></h2>
<p>1周吃3次鱼可保护皮肤免受<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E5%A4%96%E7%BA%BF.md#1">紫外线</a>侵害；长期吃鱼，可以为人们提供一种类似于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%99%92.md#1">防晒</a>霜的自然保护，使皮肤增白。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574388" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/fish-2304990_640-e1503928675923.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574388 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/fish-2304990_640-e1503928675923.jpg" alt="鱼类。(LuckyLife11/ CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574388" class="wp-caption-text">鱼类。(LuckyLife11/ CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>坚果</strong></span></h2>
<p>坚果中含有的不饱和脂肪酸对皮肤很有好处，能够从内而外软化皮肤、防止皱纹，同时保湿，让肌肤看起来更年轻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/nuts-e1503928707489.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574389 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/nuts-e1503928707489.jpg" alt="坚果。(Ale MaGu/ CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574389" class="wp-caption-text">坚果。(Ale MaGu/ CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>小黄瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>你是否有下眼袋经常浮肿的烦恼？那么就可以试试用小黄瓜来做眼膜，它含有天然的抗氧化剂和黄酮成分，可以帮助舒缓眼周的不适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5793248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5793248" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/11/1406260045532483-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-5793248 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/11/1406260045532483-600x400.jpg" alt="黄瓜（fotolia）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5793248" class="wp-caption-text">黄瓜（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>哈密瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>哈密瓜和其他深黄色水果一样，含有丰富的抗氧化物质，例如β-胡萝卜素、维生素C等。β-胡萝卜素具有治疗光过敏性疾病及皮肤癌的效果，而维生素C，能预防<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E6%96%91.md#1">黑斑</a>、减少皮肤黑色素的形成，帮助皮肤美白、防止老化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574410" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/cantaloupe-e1503928836811.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574410 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/cantaloupe-e1503928836811.jpg" alt="哈密瓜。(Hans Braxmeier/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574410" class="wp-caption-text">哈密瓜。(Hans Braxmeier/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>葡萄</strong></span></h2>
<p>葡萄富含单宁酸、脂肪酸、维生素B群，具有抗氧化功效，是优越的抗老水果，且外皮、果肉到葡萄籽皆富含美白漂亮成分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574411" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/grapes-e1503928869909.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574411 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/grapes-e1503928869909.jpg" alt="葡萄。(Ursula Paczkowski /CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574411" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄。(Ursula Paczkowski /CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>奇异果</strong></span></h2>
<p>奇异果含有维生素C及维生素E，可以中和自由基，将因日晒、压力等产生的自由基消除，保护肌肤，增强皮肤抗老化功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574412" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/kiwi-e1503928901962.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574412 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/kiwi-e1503928901962.jpg" alt="奇异果。(matczysko/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574412" class="wp-caption-text">奇异果。(matczysko/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>洋葱</strong></span></h2>
<p>洋葱富含维生素C和尼克酸，它们能够促进细胞间质的形成和损伤细胞的修复，使皮肤光洁，红润而富有弹性。此外， 吃洋葱能灭菌、治感冒鼻塞，神经衰弱者可将洋葱放在枕边，有助改善睡眠品质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574414" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/onion-e1503928935705.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574414 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/onion-e1503928935705.jpg" alt="洋葱。(Rotozld/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574414" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱。(Rotozld/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>芝麻糊</strong></span></h2>
<p>芝麻糊有加速新陈代谢功效，可使肤色更红润滑溜。黑芝麻糊效用更胜鲜奶，食用芝麻皮肤会滑溜、少皱纹，令肤色红润白净。不过一碗芝麻糊差不多有大半碗白饭热量那么高，不要贪多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574416" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/sesame-e1503928963114.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574416 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/sesame-e1503928963114.jpg" alt="芝麻糊。(Philip Lai/CC/Flickr)" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574416" class="wp-caption-text">芝麻糊。(Philip Lai/CC/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>番茄</strong></span></h2>
<p>打算从事户外活动的人，最好在晚上吃些番茄！原因是茄红素被小肠吸收后流通到全身，在6～8小时后抵达肌肤细胞。在充满紫外线的早晨前传送到肌肤，帮助发挥“吃的防晒乳”的功效喔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/tomato-1235662_640-e1503929111225.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9574422 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/tomato-1235662_640-e1503929111225.jpg" alt="番茄。(Sharon Ang/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574422" class="wp-caption-text">番茄。(Sharon Ang/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
