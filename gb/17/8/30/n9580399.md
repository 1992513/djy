<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：八种夏季可消暑解热食物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1707141656572483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西瓜饮料。（fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者罗蓉蓉综合报导）人们在夏天最常出现的症状就是中暑，而体内有火气滋生的情况也十分常见，因此，要想有效的预防中暑的发生，可参考以下一些家常必备的消暑食物。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>苦瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>有“瓜中之王”之称的苦瓜，苦瓜中含有丰富的营养成分，有消暑清热，明目解毒的作用。含有丰富维生素C、胡萝卜素、膳食纤维、磷、铁、钾和多种矿物质，且苦瓜性凉，能消炎退火，可改善火气大、口干舌燥的问题，还有预防中暑和解暑的效果。苦瓜的瓜肉和种子可以促进糖分分解，抑制糖分的吸收，是很好的降血糖食物，其中的纤维也可以帮助降低血糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9599831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9599831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/120514040846100088.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9599831 size-large" title="凉拌苦瓜" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/120514040846100088-600x402.jpg" alt="凉拌苦瓜" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9599831" class="wp-caption-text">凉拌苦瓜。（王仁骏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>绿茶</strong></span></h2>
<p>在中医养生中，夏季炎热出汗多，宜吃苦味食物。而绿茶味甘涩，夏季饮用， 能消暑清热、醒脑提神，在增强食欲、健脾利胃方面，也有明显的效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7408369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7408369" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1409282132332483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7408369 size-large" title="绿茶。(fotolia)" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1409282132332483-600x456.jpg" alt="绿茶。(fotolia)" width="600" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7408369" class="wp-caption-text">绿茶。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>西瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>西瓜是瓜类中清暑解渴的首选。夏天出现中暑、发热、心烦、口渴或者其他急性热病时，均宜用西瓜进行辅助食治疗。但是也因为西瓜太甘寒的特性，所以，脾胃比较虚弱的人，应避免猛吃西瓜，容易引起腹泻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9580409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9580409" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/watermelon-600x382.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9580409 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/watermelon-600x382.jpg" alt="西瓜。(congerdesign/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9580409" class="wp-caption-text">西瓜。(congerdesign/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>绿豆</strong></span></h2>
<p>酷热袭来，民间一直有喝绿豆汤消暑的习俗。绿豆性凉、味甘，具有清热解毒、消暑除烦、止渴健胃等功效。喝一碗甘凉的绿豆汤，能预防中暑、清热解毒，因而，绿豆汤成了颇受老百姓喜欢的消暑饮品。不过寒凉体质者、老人、儿童、体质虚弱者、正在服用药物者、经期女性，都不适合喝绿豆汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9599794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9599794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1507071340092483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9599794 size-large" title="绿豆甜点（fotlia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1507071340092483-600x400.jpg" alt="绿豆甜点（Fotolia）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9599794" class="wp-caption-text">绿豆汤。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>黄瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>黄瓜是夏季最佳的美容减肥蔬菜，以常食用黄瓜可有效地抗皮肤老化，减少皱纹的产生，并可防止唇炎、口角炎。但脾胃虚弱、腹痛腹泻、寒咳嗽者都应少吃黄瓜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9580414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9580414" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/84226990543e5f828c5f630d3891da3c-e1504070861610.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9580414 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/84226990543e5f828c5f630d3891da3c-e1504070861610.jpg" alt="凉拌黄瓜的爽脆、酸辣入味，冰镇爽口也开胃。(BernadetteWurzinger/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9580414" class="wp-caption-text">凉拌黄瓜的爽脆、酸辣入味，冰镇爽口也开胃。(BernadetteWurzinger/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>冬瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>说到消暑食疗，冬瓜是不二之选。夏季多吃些冬瓜，解渴消暑、利尿。因其利尿，且含钠极少，所以是慢性肾炎水肿、营养不良性水肿、孕妇水肿的消肿佳品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9580415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9580415" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/4711d11e912234dcdfd9b4c75dbb620a-600x424.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9580415 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/4711d11e912234dcdfd9b4c75dbb620a-600x424.jpg" alt="冬瓜汤。(Kent Wang/CC/Flickr)" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9580415" class="wp-caption-text">冬瓜汤。(Kent Wang/CC/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>茄子</strong></span></h2>
<p>茄子是夏天最常见的蔬菜之一，茄子本身没有什么味道，所以可塑性很强。常吃茄子可防治脑溢血、高血压、动脉硬化等病症，对慢性胃炎等也有一定治疗作用。但茄子不宜多食，也不宜生吃或和蟹肉一同食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9580417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9580417" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1d4cbe8bffc61f298edfa9e41f7948d3-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9580417 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1d4cbe8bffc61f298edfa9e41f7948d3-600x400.jpg" alt="鱼香茄子是一道常见川菜，是一道简单又下饭的茄子料理。(Popo le Chien/CC/Wikipedia)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9580417" class="wp-caption-text">鱼香茄子是一道常见川菜，是一道简单又下饭的茄子料理。(Popo le Chien/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>丝瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>丝瓜是常见的家常蔬菜，味道清香又柔嫩爽口；除此之外，丝瓜的药用价值高，丝瓜皮可清热解毒，丝瓜汁有美容效果，其他部位如叶、蒂、藤、根等，也都有各种不同的效用。丝瓜性凉，也不适合大量食用，食用过多损伤阳气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7728986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7728986" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/07/1106210333341839.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7728986 size-large" title="当季蔬菜最好吃 （一）蛤蜊丝瓜" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/07/1106210333341839-600x399.jpg" alt="蛤蛎蒸丝瓜。" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7728986" class="wp-caption-text">蛤蛎蒸丝瓜。（江柏逸／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
