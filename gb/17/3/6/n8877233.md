<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>节气养生／惊蛰清肝醒脑 多吃春菜和4种药草</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/shutterstock_80756956-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>多吃芦笋等新鲜时蔬，可以很好地净化血液、减去冬日增加的体重。(shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><em><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%e4%ba%8c%e5%8d%81%e5%9b%9b%e7%af%80%e6%b0%a3.md#1">二十四节气</a>是中国古人在两千多年前发明的一套补充历法，主要用于指导农事。节气与人体健康有何关联？在本系列文章中，东西方草药专家墨翎（Moreen Liao）带您走近中国古人的智慧，并提供实用养生建议，帮助读者因应节气来调适身心。</em></p>
<p><strong>节气名：惊蛰?</strong> jīngzhé</p>
<p><strong>日期：</strong>2017年3月5日至19日</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>节气特征：</strong></span></h3>
<p>惊蛰的意思是：雷声惊醒冬眠的昆虫。历史上，第一声春雷常常在这个时节打响。</p>
<p>在中国神话故事中，雷声是盘古的叱咤声，盘古是开天辟地的神祇。传说他“开目成昼，合目成夜，呼为暑，吸为寒，吹气成风云，叱声为雷霆”，也有说他“泣为江河，气为风，声为雷，目瞳为电”。</p>
<p>也有传说惊蛰的节气神“雷公”是一只大鸟，雷公冬眠于地下，当农人的春耕将其唤醒，雷公会冲破大地，用手中的铁锤在天地间打出隆隆雷声，也使蛰居冬眠的动物们惊醒。这就是“惊蛰”的来历。</p>
<p>之后，昆虫会开始在地下活动，帮助松土以便春耕——中国大部分地区的农事活动从此时开始。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8927022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8927022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/Insectsawaken-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8927022 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/Insectsawaken-450x300.jpg" alt="惊蛰。(shutterstock)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8927022" class="wp-caption-text">惊蛰时分，昆虫开始活动，中国大部分地区的农事活动从此时开始。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>对人的影响：</strong></span></h3>
<p>此时，不仅地下的昆虫苏醒，所有的虫豸，包括蜜蜂、蝴蝶、蟑螂等都开始活动。我们通常看不到的细菌，也因雨水节气带来的湿度而大量增殖。</p>
<p>那些对花粉过敏、呼吸系统较弱或有皮肤疾病的人，在这段时间经常感到不舒服。有心理疾患的人，此时也容易受到温度和湿度变化的刺激。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>惊蛰时节如何养生：</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>饮食之道</strong></p>
<p><span class="text30">⊙</span> 在中医里，春季是排毒季节，中国人传统上讲究少食红肉、少酒少糖，因为这些食物都容易使人燥热，导致炎症和疾病。根据五行学说，红色属于火。这时吃红肉，容易导致春季常见的皮肤过敏。对于那些很容易感冒咳嗽的人来说，这个季节尤其要少吃糖。</p>
<p><span class="text30">⊙ </span>多吃芹菜、番茄、香柠檬（香柑）、苦瓜、青豆、南瓜、胡萝卜、白萝卜、油菜和芦笋等蔬果。</p>
<p><strong>烹饪之道</strong></p>
<p><span class="text30">⊙</span> 对于有前述健康问题的人来说，此时最宜蒸食和煲汤，且应避免吃油炸和生冷食品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8926990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8926990" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/shutterstock_534918238-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8926990 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/shutterstock_534918238-450x300.jpg" alt="shutterstock_534918238" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8926990" class="wp-caption-text">春季饮食适合蒸食。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span class="text30">⊙ </span>用醋或柠檬汁来调制沙拉，则可以很好地净化血液、减去冬日增加的体重。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8877692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8877692" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/1608032252082483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8877692 size-medium" title="Fresh salad with cherry tomatoes and asparagus" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/1608032252082483-450x300.jpg" alt="Fresh salad with cherry tomatoes and asparagus" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8877692" class="wp-caption-text">多吃番茄、芦笋等新鲜时蔬，并用醋或柠檬汁来调制沙拉，可以很好地净化血液、减去冬日增加的体重。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>药草之选</strong></p>
<p><span class="text30">⊙</span> <strong>薄荷</strong>：入茶饮，可健胃祛风、祛痰，改善感冒发烧、咽喉肿痛以及抑郁等不良情绪，也有助于缓解烦躁失眠、提振精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8877938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8877938" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/1703060130422669.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8877938 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/1703060130422669-450x294.jpg" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8877938" class="wp-caption-text">薄荷入茶饮，可健胃祛风、祛痰，改善感冒发烧、咽喉肿痛、不良情绪，还有助缓解烦躁失眠、提振精神。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span class="text30">⊙</span><strong> 白菊（即干菊、雏菊）</strong>：含有丰富的氨基酸、类黄酮、菊色素、维生素A、B、E及微量元素，具有清肝明目、治疗头疼眩晕等功效。中医讲“白菊性甘温，久服最有益，古人春食苗、夏食英、冬食根，有以也”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8877872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8877872" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/101215124008100445.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8877872 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/101215124008100445-450x301.jpg" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8877872" class="wp-caption-text">中医讲“白菊性甘温，久服最有益”。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span class="text30">⊙</span> <strong>车前草</strong>（plantain）：车前草含有一种称为桃叶珊瑚甙的化学物质，具有清热解毒、保肝护肝等功效。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8877750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8877750" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/IMG_20150618_100757811-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8877750 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/IMG_20150618_100757811-450x253.jpg" alt="IMG_20150618_100757811" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8877750" class="wp-caption-text">车前草含有一种称为桃叶珊瑚甙的化学物质，具有清热解毒、保肝护肝等功效。(Conan Milner/Epoch Times)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span class="text30">⊙</span> <strong>欧蓍草</strong>（yarrow）：泡茶能利汗、助消化、化痰；入菜能刺激食欲、促进吸收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8877773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8877773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/3658902526_62b4ce6409_z-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8877773 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/3658902526_62b4ce6409_z-450x301.jpg" alt="Steven Depolo " width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8877773" class="wp-caption-text">欧蓍草（yarrow）泡茶能利汗、助消化、化痰；入菜能刺激食欲、促进吸收。(Steven Depolo/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
