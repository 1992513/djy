<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>抗衰老的10种超级食物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/pumpkin-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>南瓜是抗衰老食品。(U. Leone/CC/Pixabay)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年07月23日讯】（大纪元记者罗蓉蓉综合报导）现代人越来越重视抗老，爱美的女性更视“老化”为一大天敌，稍有一点皱纹出现，就让女人们烦恼不已，但“抗老”不是只用擦的就好，还要配合食物，外敷内用之下，效果更加倍。以下整理出10种专家推荐的超抗老食物，常吃有助让你有张不老童颜。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>1. 蓝莓</strong></span></h2>
<p>蓝莓一向是欧美国家的“超级食物”，抗氧化能力强，健康好处多多！蓝莓含有丰富的抗氧化物，可减缓衰老。此外，蓝莓富含有助抗癌的物质，帮助减缓肿瘤生长。另外美国有研究显示，蓝莓有助于降低得阿兹海默症的风险、改善平衡感、协调能力及提升短期记忆力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453956" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/blueberries-e1500799864260.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453956 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/blueberries-e1500799864260.jpg" alt="蓝莓。(Jill Wellington/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453956" class="wp-caption-text">蓝莓。(Jill Wellington/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>2. 番薯、南瓜和胡萝卜</strong></span></h2>
<p>南瓜和胡萝卜、番薯等蔬菜一样，拥有丰富的β-胡萝卜素和其他胡萝卜素，可以转化成身体所需的维生素A，使皮肤紧实、不易受损，并拥有较佳的自我修复能力。此外，这些营养成分也是一种抗氧化剂，可以防止细胞自由基损伤，使身体较不容易老化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453964" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/sweetpotato-e1500800149847.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453964 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/sweetpotato-e1500800149847.jpg" alt="番薯。(Steve Adcock/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453964" class="wp-caption-text">番薯。(Steve Adcock/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9453966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453966" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/carrot-e1500800209284.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453966 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/carrot-e1500800209284.jpg" alt="胡萝卜。(Couleur/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453966" class="wp-caption-text">胡萝卜。(Couleur/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>3. 番茄</strong></span></h2>
<p>番茄富含茄红素、维生素C、膳食纤维等营养，其中的茄红素是天然色素，也是让番茄呈现鲜红色的原因。茄红素的抗氧化功能很强，可以减少身体的氧化伤害，达到抗老化的保护作用，对皮肤和身体好处多多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453968" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/tomato-e1500800291886.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453968 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/tomato-e1500800291886.jpg" alt="番茄。(Bruno Gl?tsch/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453968" class="wp-caption-text">番茄。(Bruno Gl?tsch/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>4. 酪梨</strong></span></h2>
<p>有“森林中的奶油”之称的酪梨，曾被金氏世界纪录列为世界上营养价值最高的水果。酪梨富含能抗发炎、抗老的植化素，可减缓老化的过程。而其丰富膳食纤维与钾、镁、叶酸，则能降血压、预防中风。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453969" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/avocado-e1500800362362.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453969 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/avocado-e1500800362362.jpg" alt="酪梨。(silverstylus/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453969" class="wp-caption-text">酪梨。(silverstylus/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>5. 绿叶蔬菜</strong></span></h2>
<p>深色绿叶蔬菜像是菠菜、甘蓝菜、芥蓝菜等，这些蔬菜富含叶黄素及玉米黄素，可以保护眼睛以及避免视力模糊等眼疾问题。而每天保证足够的蔬菜摄入，可以集中注意力，延缓大脑功能退化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453970" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/leafy-greens-e1500800407245.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453970 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/leafy-greens-e1500800407245.jpg" alt="绿叶蔬菜。(Pexels/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453970" class="wp-caption-text">绿叶蔬菜。(Pexels/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>6. 西兰花</strong></span></h2>
<p>西兰花也是餐桌上的常客，西兰花中含有丰富的维生素A、维生素C和胡萝卜素，能增强皮肤的抗损伤能力，还有助于保持皮肤弹性，减少皱纹生长。西兰花还有促进肝脏解毒的作用。研究发现，西兰花能够活化肝脏中的酶类，从而促进肝脏对毒物和致癌物的解毒作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453979" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/broccoli-e1500800458829.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453979 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/broccoli-e1500800458829.jpg" alt="西兰花。(Kathryn Gjerseth/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453979" class="wp-caption-text">西兰花。(Kathryn Gjerseth/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>7. 鲑鱼及鲔鱼</strong></span></h2>
<p>富含Omega-3脂肪酸的鱼种以来自寒带的深海鱼居多，包括鲑鱼、鲔鱼等。Omega-3脂肪酸能减少体内发炎反应、帮助调节免疫力，有利于肌肤保养，并帮助降低皮肤过敏的机会。美国健康协会建议每一个星期最少要吃两次这种富含Omega-3的鱼肉，这能降低心脏病的发生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453981" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/salmon-e1500800501810.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453981 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/salmon-e1500800501810.jpg" alt="鲑鱼。(Wow Phochiangrak/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453981" class="wp-caption-text">鲑鱼。(Wow Phochiangrak/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9453982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453982" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/tuna-e1500800549100.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453982 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/tuna-e1500800549100.jpg" alt="鲔鱼沙拉。(Sharon Aschaiek/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453982" class="wp-caption-text">鲔鱼沙拉。(Sharon Aschaiek/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>8. 橄榄油</strong></span></h2>
<p>橄榄油（Olive Oil）是由新鲜的油橄榄果实直接冷榨而成的，不经加热和化学处理，保留了天然营养成分。橄榄油被认为是迄今所发现的油脂中最适合人体营养的油脂。在地中海沿岸国家有几千年的历史。实验证明，橄榄油含有的抗氧化剂可以恢复人体脏腑器官的健康状态，能防止脑衰老，并能延年益寿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453985" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/oliveoil-e1500800594467.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453985 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/oliveoil-e1500800594467.jpg" alt="橄榄油。(Steve Buissinne/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453985" class="wp-caption-text">橄榄油。(Steve Buissinne/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>9. 小黄瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>具有鲜艳绿色外皮的小黄瓜，是天然抗老圣品之一。小黄瓜含有维生素E，是天然的抗老防衰营养素，对于预防心血管疾病，也扮演着重要角色。小黄瓜中所含的二氧化硅（silica），对维护关节与治疗关节炎有好处，此外，二氧化硅也可以对抗皱纹，防止老化。小黄瓜还有一个最大特色就是热量低，对于控制体重，是很好的选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453986" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/cucumbers--e1500800638710.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453986 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/cucumbers--e1500800638710.jpg" alt="小黄瓜。(congerdesign/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453986" class="wp-caption-text">小黄瓜。(congerdesign/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>10. 西瓜</strong></span></h2>
<p>西瓜是最“夯”的消暑圣品之一，从西瓜的英文Watermelon，就知西瓜是水分多的甜瓜。吃西瓜除了解渴，很少人知道西瓜也是一种抗老食品。红肉西瓜含有较多的B-胡萝卜素，可以在体内转化成维生素A，提升皮肤对抗外界病菌的能力，也能加速受损肌肤的复原。另外，β-胡萝卜素本身也是一种抗氧化剂，有助预防肌肤老化。西瓜皮当中所含的维生素C和维生素E很高，是外用美肤的好材料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9453988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9453988" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/watermelon-e1500800690178.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-9453988 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/watermelon-e1500800690178.jpg" alt="西瓜。(hyun chun kim/CC/Pixabay)" width="500" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9453988" class="wp-caption-text">西瓜。(hyun chun kim/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
