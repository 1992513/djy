<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>半岛景观豪宅 尽收无敌美景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/135713293-81-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2017年新建的现代式景观豪宅。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年07月15日讯】（大纪元记者李欧综合报导）加州阳光在湾区尽显无遗，住在这里能拥有景观宅邸是多少人的梦想！自然环境持续恶化的今天，湾区仍保有天然林地和相对干净的资源，难能可贵。综观世界，即使有钱也难以买到和湾区景观豪宅相匹配的价值。本周上市的景观豪宅不可多得，更不要错过本周末的实地赏房机会。</p>
<p>一、481 West Maple Way, Woodside<br />
<figure id="attachment_9404649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404649" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/11.53.34.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/11.53.34-450x203.png" alt="湾区上市房481 West Maple Way, Woodside。" width="450" b="203" class="size-medium wp-image-9404649" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404649" class="wp-caption-text">湾区上市房481 West Maple Way, Woodside。</figcaption></figure><br />
这座2017年新建的定制现代豪宅，由著名的建筑师Studio S Squared设计，拥有独特的景观，可俯瞰广阔的圣马刁山谷，以及Crystal Springs水库闪闪发光的水域，从早晨到黄昏，完全享有湾区一流风采。而且户外营造耐旱景观，尽情享受阳光。</p>
<p>这个宽敞的三层楼住宅，拥有节能系统和奢华的极简装饰，提供了超凡的加州生活。建筑有钢骨、水泥和木头结构建成，除了主屋外，还有全套设备的独立客房。不要错过周末的赏房机会。<figure id="attachment_9404650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404650" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/05_135713293-84.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/05_135713293-84-450x300.jpg" alt="这处Woodside现代式豪宅，能远眺湾区山光湖色。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9404650" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404650" class="wp-caption-text">这处Woodside现代式豪宅，能远眺湾区山光湖色。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_9404652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404652" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/06_135713293-86.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/06_135713293-86-450x300.jpg" alt="室内窗户完全敞开，每个时辰都有美景相伴。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9404652" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404652" class="wp-caption-text">室内窗户完全敞开，每个时辰都有美景相伴。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>二、7 Coal Mine View, Portola Valley<br />
<figure id="attachment_9404658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404658" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/11.53.47.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/11.53.47-450x198.png" alt="湾区上市房7 Coal Mine View, Portola Valley。" width="450" b="198" class="size-medium wp-image-9404658" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404658" class="wp-caption-text">湾区上市房7 Coal Mine View, Portola Valley。</figcaption></figure><br />
这座经过精心改造的4卧3浴，位于Portola Valley Ranch珍贵的宝地，结合了宁静的现代风格和传统的优雅风格。尽管是1979年建造，但精美的设计使之成为永恒的精湛工艺。天花板的高度，玻璃墙和宽敞的露台，与迷人的自然环境无缝连接，并可以欣赏到西部山脉（包括Windy Hill）的壮丽景色。</p>
<p>豪华的设施，确保全年的舒适和便利，包括一个专业设备齐全的厨房、美丽的燃气壁炉、内置扬声器、电子天窗、全屋硬木地板，以及一个巨大的家庭房。<figure id="attachment_9404660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404660" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/07_135519153-0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/07_135519153-0-450x300.jpg" alt="高耸的木头天花板，结合户外风景。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9404660" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404660" class="wp-caption-text">高耸的木头天花板，结合户外风景。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_9404661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404661" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/08_135519153-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/08_135519153-1-450x300.jpg" alt="客厅能直接通往露台欣赏山林与天际线。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9404661" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404661" class="wp-caption-text">客厅能直接通往露台欣赏山林与天际线。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>三、1795 Bay Laurel Drive, Menlo Park<br />
<figure id="attachment_9404663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404663" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/11.53.57.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/11.53.57-450x198.png" alt="湾区上市房1795 Bay Laurel Drive, Menlo Park。" width="450" b="198" class="size-medium wp-image-9404663" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404663" class="wp-caption-text">湾区上市房1795 Bay Laurel Drive, Menlo Park。</figcaption></figure><br />
沿着San Francisquito Creek河畔，这套豪邸建于2009年，建筑有三层楼，室内面积超过6,000平方英尺（按图纸），占地面积约13,700平方英尺（政府数据）。迷人的魅力、精湛的工艺和奢华的材料，为初始的设计增添了精致的情调。</p>
<p>此屋有花格镶板的木质天花板，以及用桃花心木装饰的图书馆，优雅贵气，还有可折叠的玻璃门通往户外泳池、池畔小屋和户外厨房的私家花园。地下室有桑拿浴室、酒窖和家庭影院。这里可漫步到优秀的门罗帕克学校，还可以快速前往斯坦福大学（Stanford University）、沙丘路（Sand Hill Road）以及迷人的门罗公园市中心。<figure id="attachment_9404664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404664" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/01_ExtFront_02_IC-1030x686.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/01_ExtFront_02_IC-1030x686-450x300.jpg" alt="经典的加州风格豪邸，后院还有大泳池。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9404664" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404664" class="wp-caption-text">经典的加州风格豪邸，后院还有大泳池。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_9404665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404665" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/02_Kitchen_01_IC-1030x686.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/02_Kitchen_01_IC-1030x686-450x300.jpg" alt="纯白雅致的厨房。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9404665" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404665" class="wp-caption-text">纯白雅致的厨房。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>四、165 Patricia Drive, Atherton<br />
<figure id="attachment_9404668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404668" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/11.54.09.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/11.54.09-450x198.png" alt="湾区上市房165 Patricia Drive, Atherton。" width="450" b="198" class="size-medium wp-image-9404668" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404668" class="wp-caption-text">湾区上市房165 Patricia Drive, Atherton。</figcaption></figure><br />
威严的大铁门身后，矗立着这套占地约1.05英亩的大宅，这是钻石级别的豪邸。经由著名的林云风水大师指点，其巧妙的设计皆为健康、富裕的生活而设计。室外充足的自然光线洒满屋内的每一个角落，可谓“地气盛，屋则光亮”，“居者得其气，自然获福”。</p>
<p>一楼的主套房可通往后花园，还有一个超大奢华的步入式衣帽间，和一个令人兴奋的水疗中心，这包括一个雪松木桑拿室，带座位的双人淋浴间和从法国进口的水疗浴缸。二楼卧室均配有独立的大理石浴室，而且都带有步入式衣帽间。二楼露台还可欣赏门口花园的景致。<figure id="attachment_9404669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404669" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/03_2.ExtFront_02_AH-5-1030x686.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/03_2.ExtFront_02_AH-5-1030x686-450x300.jpg" alt="仿法式庄园风格的白色豪宅，深藏在树林之间。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9404669" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404669" class="wp-caption-text">仿法式庄园风格的白色豪宅，深藏在树林之间。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_9404670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9404670" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/04_26.MasterBathrm_01_AH-1030x686.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/04_26.MasterBathrm_01_AH-1030x686-450x300.jpg" alt="豪华贵气的主卧卫浴，有大片窗户享受户外景色。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9404670" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9404670" class="wp-caption-text">豪华贵气的主卧卫浴，有大片窗户享受户外景色。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
