<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宋米秦盼年底怀孕 吴怡霈明年冲绳结婚</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200751592384-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（左起）王思平、宋米秦、吴怡霈7月20日在台北出席美妆活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年07月21日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）韩国籍台湾女星<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E7%B1%B3%E7%A7%A6.md#1">宋米秦</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%80%A1%E9%9C%88.md#1">吴怡霈</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E6%80%9D%E5%B9%B3.md#1">王思平</a>三位闺蜜20日出席美妆活动，宋米秦透露目前正在喝鸡汤补身，预计年底怀孕生宝宝，从人妻升格人母，丈夫陈铠文也爱相随出席活动。<br />
<figure id="attachment_9445362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9445362" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200752122384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9445362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200752122384-600x400.jpg" alt="" title="" width="500" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9445362" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E7%B1%B3%E7%A7%A6.md#1">宋米秦</a>盼年底怀孕生宝宝。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>宋米秦虽曾是Dream Girls团长，但她表示自己私下非常小女人，任何事情都会询问丈夫的认可，就连香水味道也是，“除了工作之余，我的生活必须顾及很多事，其实常常时间会不够用，跟老公感情升温的秘招就是香水。”<br />
<figure id="attachment_9445368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9445368" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200752332384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9445368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200752332384-600x400.jpg" alt="" title="" width="500" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9445368" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%80%A1%E9%9C%88.md#1">吴怡霈</a>甜蜜透露明年结婚。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>活动主持人吴怡霈和日文家教Aki交往4年，透露男友去年就问她妹妹“吴怡霈喜欢怎样的戒指款式？”但她先前还没做好心理准备，拜托男友先别求婚。如今甜蜜透露明年结婚，她与好友爱纱、Gigi同样都选在冲绳举办婚礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9445353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9445353" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200752172384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9445353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200752172384-600x400.jpg" alt="" title="" width="500" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9445353" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E6%80%9D%E5%B9%B3.md#1">王思平</a>最近刚拍完网路剧。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于王思平的感情世界，她今年4月才遭中介骚扰，最近刚拍完网路剧，又被剧组里一个“怪人”频约喝酒，她坚持拒绝才免遭灌醉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9445355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9445355" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200751542384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9445355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707200751542384.jpg" alt="" title="" width="350" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9445355" class="wp-caption-text">王思平、宋米秦、吴怡霈一起出席美妆活动。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
