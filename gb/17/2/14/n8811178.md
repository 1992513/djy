<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>情人节不孤单 李亚鹏携女儿李嫣游旧金山</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年02月15日讯】13日，在美国旅游的李亚鹏于微博发文，说“路遇一小妞儿”，最终“情定旧金山”。在李亚鹏发布的组照中看到，他提到的“小妞儿”原来是他的11岁女儿李嫣，父女俩一起在旧金山旅游，并开心自拍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8811244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8811244" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcotplezi5j20zk0qq794-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8811244 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcotplezi5j20zk0qq794-450x338.jpg" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8811244" class="wp-caption-text">李亚鹏与女儿李嫣喝咖啡。（李亚鹏微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李亚鹏13日更新微博，写道：“路遇一小妞儿，一路跟随结伴而行请她喝了一杯咖啡，终于情定旧金山。”从他晒出的自拍照中，网友发现原来李亚鹏说的“路遇一小妞儿”及“情定旧金山”，是他带着女儿李嫣在旧金山旅游。有网友感慨，女儿是爸爸的上辈子情人，难怪父女间的感情如此深厚。</p>
<p>有网友直呼：“最好的父女大概就是这样了。”也有的说：“李嫣挺幸福的，父母虽离异但是都爱她并且关系良好。”还有网友表示：“ 李亚鹏是很有责任感的父亲，点赞。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8811251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8811251" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcotpfg1njj20qo0zkjwo-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8811251 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcotpfg1njj20qo0zkjwo-300x400.jpg" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8811251" class="wp-caption-text">在旧金山金门大桥畔，李亚鹏抱着女儿观景。（李亚鹏微博）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8811259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8811259" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcotpjmc4jj20f00m876m-300x444.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8811259 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcotpjmc4jj20f00m876m-300x444.jpg" width="300" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8811259" class="wp-caption-text">李亚鹏带李嫣游玩。（李亚鹏微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，13日，李亚鹏还在微博自曝一件尴尬事：“顾着卖萌，老司机把柴油直接加进了汽油发动机，（车）没开五分钟就开始剧烈抖动，只能趴路边了。”</p>
<p>他接着写道：“还好有APP‘对话世界’即时翻译搞定租车公司和警察，两个半小时后新车送到，一个小插曲让一场旅行变成了真正的旅行，这都是我的功劳。”不过，从李亚鹏晒出的照片中看，父女俩心情似乎没有受到影响，李亚鹏还比剪刀手卖萌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8811265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8811265" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcorjf15gxj20zk0rkamm-450x349.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8811265 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcorjf15gxj20zk0rkamm-450x349.jpg" width="450" b="349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8811265" class="wp-caption-text">司机把柴油当汽油加，致使车子抖动不能开。幸亏警察帮忙。（李亚鹏微博）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8811294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8811294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcorjdccugj20qa0mgn1k-450x384.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8811294 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/6d1142c1ly1fcorjdccugj20qa0mgn1k-450x384.jpg" width="450" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8811294" class="wp-caption-text">经历乌龙事件，李亚鹏心情似没受影响，还比剪刀手卖萌。（李亚鹏微博）</figcaption></figure>
