<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>湾区轻松怡人赏房趣 首购、换屋皆适宜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1172-DOVER-LANE_03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>湾区城市Foster City的水岸房产。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年02月12日讯】本周来看到几处轻松怡人的房产，不仅室内设计自然轻快，连价格也很宜人，不同于前几周介绍的豪宅级房产。</p>
<p>一、1060 Cambridge Avenue<br />
Menlo Park, CA 94025<br />
1图：这处房产的外墙是特殊的湖水蓝，开价近两百万，是住进Menlo Park的好价位。<br />
2图：硬木地板的卧室有多面窗户。<br />
这处房产有一个前门庭院，内有三房两浴，经过改造的时尚厨房有温暖的樱桃木橱柜和白色台面，搭配黑色家电。客厅可以直接看到前院，而且有一个传统壁炉。主人房有浪漫的法式门和步入式衣橱，其它两间卧室空间也不小。后院有一个温馨的小花园。附近能到优美的Allied Arts Guild，也能骑自行车到热闹的学校和充满活力的市中心。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8803298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803298" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1.42.38.png"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1.42.38-450x301.png" alt="本周旧金山湾区赏房一：1060 Cambridge Avenue, Menlo Park, CA 94025。" width="450" b="301" class="size-medium wp-image-8803298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803298" class="wp-caption-text">本周旧金山湾区赏房一：1060 Cambridge Avenue, Menlo Park, CA 94025。</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8803301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803301" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/01_1060-Cambridge-Avenue_01.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/01_1060-Cambridge-Avenue_01-450x300.jpg" alt="这处房产的外墙是特殊的湖水蓝，开价近两百万，是住进Menlo Park的好价位。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-8803301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803301" class="wp-caption-text">这处房产的外墙是特殊的湖水蓝，开价近两百万，是住进Menlo Park的好价位。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8803302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803302" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/02_1060-Cambridge-Avenue_03.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/02_1060-Cambridge-Avenue_03-450x300.jpg" alt="硬木地板的卧室有多面窗户。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-8803302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803302" class="wp-caption-text">硬木地板的卧室有多面窗户。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>二、498 South Frances Street<br />
Sunnyvale, CA 94086<br />
图：有童话气质的历史房，占地近1/4英亩。<br />
图：后院泳池经过修整。<br />
这处房产是建于1936年的历史房，四房两浴，室内空间虽然不大，但占地近1/4英亩。房子由著名建筑师Louis A. Scott设计，造型典雅具有童话般的气质，在Sunnyvale难得一见。房子做过新的粉刷，并修复了后院的游泳池。后院还一个菜园和一个双车库，与灵活的车间。这里靠近市中心，可以轻松步行到火车站Caltrain、购物中心，在S. Murphy大道有多家热门的餐厅和。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8803305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1.42.58.png"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1.42.58-450x285.png" alt="本周旧金山湾区赏房二：498 South Frances Street, Sunnyvale, CA 94086。" width="450" b="285" class="size-medium wp-image-8803305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803305" class="wp-caption-text">本周旧金山湾区赏房二：498 South Frances Street, Sunnyvale, CA 94086。</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8803306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803306" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/03_498-South-Frances-Street_01.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/03_498-South-Frances-Street_01-450x281.jpg" alt="有童话气质的历史房，占地近1/4英亩。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）" width="450" b="281" class="size-medium wp-image-8803306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803306" class="wp-caption-text">有童话气质的历史房，占地近1/4英亩。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8803307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803307" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/04_498-South-Frances-Street_03.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/04_498-South-Frances-Street_03-450x300.jpg" alt="后院泳池经过修整。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-8803307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803307" class="wp-caption-text">后院泳池经过修整。（湾区房地产公司DeLeon Realty提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>三、1172 Dover Lane<br />
Foster City, CA 94404<br />
5图：屋后就有优美的水岸景致。<br />
6图：水蓝色的房子就是这次的上市房。<br />
房产位在Foster City的水道边上，房子是水蓝色的外观，不同一般。屋后就是优美的水上风光，还可停泊小船。舒适的客厅和餐厅有水景，打开滑动玻璃门可通往户外甲板和海滨。宽敞的主卧室、多功能室都有水景可观赏，房间都有充足的阳光。三个卧室都有订制的颜色，活泼且不俗气。这里有HOA屋主协会费用，并提供2个游泳池和网球场让居民使用。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8803309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1.43.08.png"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1.43.08-450x365.png" alt="本周旧金山湾区赏房三：1172 Dover Lane, Foster City, CA 94404。" width="450" b="365" class="size-medium wp-image-8803309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803309" class="wp-caption-text">本周旧金山湾区赏房三：1172 Dover Lane, Foster City, CA 94404。</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8803310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803310" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/05_1172-DOVER-LANE_01.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/05_1172-DOVER-LANE_01-450x300.jpg" alt="屋后就有优美的水岸景致。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-8803310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803310" class="wp-caption-text">屋后就有优美的水岸景致。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8803311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8803311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/06_1172-DOVER-LANE_02.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/06_1172-DOVER-LANE_02-450x300.jpg" alt="水蓝色的房子就是这次的上市房。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-8803311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8803311" class="wp-caption-text">水蓝色的房子就是这次的上市房。（湾区房地产公司Alain Pinel Realtors提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>四、1 Appian Way, # 706-3<br />
South San Francisco, CA 94080<br />
7图：这个康斗刚换过新的客厅地板。<br />
8图：有两层楼的康斗，楼上是卧室。<br />
这个康斗刚刚经过大幅的装修，厨房有新的白色石英台面、磁砖地板，还有新的洗衣机、烘干机；客厅和餐厅有新的新地板；两间浴室也经过改造；主卧可以通向一个观景阳台。康斗附有一个车库，公共设备有游泳池、水疗会所，HOA费用每月$588。附近有小公园和一个超市购物中心，可以买到多样的亚洲食物和美味的咖啡、比萨。这里开向280高速公路也很快速。<br />
