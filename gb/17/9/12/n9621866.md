<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超美味常备泡菜：韭菜泡菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/a2141509398142ee6eb1b890de6cb858-434x400.jpg" />
<h6>韭菜泡菜(方言出版提供)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年09月13日讯】</p>
<p><strong>韭菜泡菜　小知识</strong></p>
<p>1 这是道不需将韭菜浸泡在盐水里，就能腌制而成的简单泡菜。</p>
<p>2 韭菜如果越搓揉，就越容易产生特有的草味，因此要轻轻搅拌。</p>
<p>3先将韭菜与辣椒粉拌在一起，能使韭菜更均匀地沾上酱料。鱼露及虾酱一起使用时，味道会更香且清爽。</p>
<p><strong>韭菜　小知识</strong></p>
<p><strong>挑选</strong></p>
<p>?产季在夏天。</p>
<p>?韭菜大致可分为三种：用于泡菜、各式料理的一般韭菜；厚长且色泽翠绿的中国韭菜；纤细且长度较短的营养韭菜，而韭菜泡菜用一般韭菜来腌制是最好的。</p>
<p>?不萎烂且香气浓郁的韭菜较新鲜。</p>
<p><strong>保存</strong></p>
<p>不须事先清洗，直接用厨房纸巾将每次要食用的量分开包起来装进夹链袋，再送入冰箱冷藏可存放5天。</p>
<p><strong>调味秘诀</strong></p>
<p>熟成后品尝一下味道，如果觉得太咸，就加入1?/?4颗洋葱丝（50g）拌匀，接着冷藏1～2天使其熟成后再食用。若觉得太淡，则放点盐巴（可依据喜好增减）拌匀，再尝尝酱料（非韭菜）的味道，如果还是太淡就继续调整。</p>
<p>25～35分钟（＋韭菜沥干水分30分钟、熟成时间最多2天）</p>
<p>15小盘份量</p>
<p>冷藏1个月</p>
<p>1 韭菜静置于筛子上约30分钟将水分完全沥干后，切成5公分长段。洋葱、红萝卜切0.5公分厚的丝状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9621878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9621878" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1-87.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9621878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1-87.jpg" alt="图1(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9621878" class="wp-caption-text">图1(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>2 将酱料食材放入碗里拌匀，放置15分钟待酱料熟成后，再倒入洋葱、红萝卜一起混匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9621881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9621881" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/2-23.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9621881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/2-23.jpg" alt="图2(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9621881" class="wp-caption-text">图2(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>▲酱料熟成之后辣椒粉会膨胀，因此较容易与食材拌匀，也能避免产生特有的生味。</strong></p>
<p>3 再拿一个碗装韭菜及辣椒粉，将两者轻轻拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9621882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9621882" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/3-22.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9621882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/3-22.jpg" alt="图3(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9621882" class="wp-caption-text">图3(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>▲韭菜须轻轻搅拌才不会产生草味。</strong></p>
<p>4 将步骤2的食材倒入步骤3的碗内，轻轻拌匀后装进泡菜保鲜盒，而这时先不要清洗残有酱料的碗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9621883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9621883" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/4-18.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9621883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/4-18.jpg" alt="图4(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9621883" class="wp-caption-text">图4(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 在残有酱料的步骤4碗中倒入1?/?4杯矿泉水及盐巴，与剩下的酱料混匀后，倒进保鲜盒内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9621884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9621884" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/5-14.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9621884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/5-14.jpg" alt="图5(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9621884" class="wp-caption-text">图5(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>6 用力压实泡菜后，先铺上一层塑胶袋，再用力压实尽量将空气挤出。夏天时，置于室温下10～12小时，春、秋、冬天时放在室温下1～2天，待其熟成后再放入冰箱冷藏保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9621885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9621885" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/6-12.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9621885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/6-12.jpg" alt="图6(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9621885" class="wp-caption-text">图6(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>▲泡菜如果接触到空气会产生异味，因此建议铺上塑胶袋，尽量减少与空气的接触。</strong></p>
<p>先将韭菜与辣椒粉混合，有助于韭菜均匀沾附酱料。◇</p>
