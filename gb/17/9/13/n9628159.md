<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超美味常备泡菜：甜椒白泡菜</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年09月14日讯】<strong><br />
</strong></p>
<p><strong>甜椒白泡菜　小知识</strong></p>
<p>1 不加砂糖，而是放入甜椒、梨子、洋葱这类甜味浓郁的蔬果，以增加甜味。</p>
<p>2这是道汤料比汤汁还要多的白泡菜料理。</p>
<p>3加入虾酱清爽口感更加倍。</p>
<p><strong>甜椒　小知识</strong></p>
<p><strong>挑选</strong></p>
<p>?产季在春天到夏天。</p>
<p>?果肉肥厚、无伤口、骨干鲜明且蒂头为草绿色的甜椒较为新鲜。</p>
<p>?不同颜色的甜椒味道各有些微差异，其中以黄甜椒的甜味最为浓厚。</p>
<p><strong>保存</strong></p>
<p>不须事先清洗，直接用保鲜膜包起来放入冰箱冷藏，可存放10天。切开过的甜椒，用保鲜膜包覆其横切面后，放进夹链袋再送入冰箱冷藏，可存放5天。</p>
<p><strong>调味秘诀</strong></p>
<p>熟成后品尝一下味道，如果觉得太咸，就加入1?/?2颗的甜椒丝（100g）拌匀，或将2片娃娃菜（60g）切小片后加入拌匀，接着冷藏1～2天待其熟成后再食用。若觉得太淡，就放点盐巴（可依据喜好增减）拌匀，再尝尝汤头（非甜椒）的味道，如果还是太淡则继续调整。</p>
<p>20～30分钟（＋腌大白菜1小时、熟成时间最多2天）</p>
<p>7小盘份量</p>
<p>冷藏15天</p>
<p>1 娃菜叶先按长度对半切，再切3公分宽的片状，切好后将菜梗及菜叶分开放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9628190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9628190" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-11.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9628190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-11.jpg" alt="图1(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9628190" class="wp-caption-text">图1(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>2 在碗中放入腌料食材，不断搅拌至盐巴完全溶解。接着放入娃娃菜的菜梗一起拌匀，腌制约45分钟，过程中可上下多翻搅几次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9628191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9628191" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-12.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9628191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-12.jpg" alt="图2(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9628191" class="wp-caption-text">图2(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>3 甜椒切0.5公分厚的细丝、青葱则切4公分长段。把酱料食材放入搅拌器中打成泥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9628192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9628192" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-13.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9628192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-13.jpg" alt="图3(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9628192" class="wp-caption-text">图3(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>4 将娃娃菜的菜叶放进步骤2的碗里拌匀，接着再继续腌制15分钟，过程中可上下多翻搅几次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9628193" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9628193" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-14.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9628193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-14.jpg" alt="图4(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9628193" class="wp-caption-text">图4(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 菜放在筛子上以水流冲过，接着用手将水分沥干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9628196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9628196" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-15.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9628196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-15.jpg" alt="图5(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9628196" class="wp-caption-text">图5(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>▲刚腌制好的大白菜通常偏咸，但如果觉得过咸，就倒入能盖过娃娃菜的水量并静置30</strong><strong>分钟。</strong></p>
<p>6 将所有食材装入泡菜保鲜盒一起拌匀，接着铺上一层塑胶袋。夏天时，置于室温下10～12小时；春、秋、冬天时，放在室温下1～2天，待其熟成后，再放入冰箱冷藏保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9628197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9628197" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-16.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9628197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170914-yichun-kuo-sweet-pepper-16.jpg" alt="图6(方言出版提供)" width="460" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9628197" class="wp-caption-text">图6(方言出版提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>用温水腌制，可以加速腌制时间。◇</p>
