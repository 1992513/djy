<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>豪华跑车首次亮相爱城车展</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年04月20日讯】（记者李彬报导）汽车爱好者将在本周末的埃德蒙顿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">车展</a>获得意外惊喜。有史以来福特所生产的最昂贵超级跑车Ford GT将首次亮相。</p>
<p>小编日前被邀参加了2017埃德蒙顿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">车展</a>新闻发布会。在简短介绍之后，展览筹备方负责人首先带领各路媒体记者来到Jeep车的展台。一辆大器稳重的铁红色大切诺基（Grand Cherokee）映入眼帘。厂家介绍这是目前市场上最大马力的SUV。小编之前就知此车是道奇（Dodge)车的标杆，所以不禁点头称是。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9055547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9055547" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Grand-Cherokee-450x342.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9055547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Grand-Cherokee-450x342.png" alt="图：大切诺基（Grand Cherokee）。（摄影／蒋维）" width="450" b="342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9055547" class="wp-caption-text">图：大切诺基（Grand Cherokee）。（摄影／蒋维）</figcaption></figure>
<div class="living-simple-text" style="text-align: center; color: #000000; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: lighter;">
<p>这是一则广告。谢谢您的支持！</p>
<p><ins data-revive-zoneid="389" data-revive-id="c9295b5f9707574a26385cec6f59175b"></ins><a async src="//das.epochtimes.com/www/delivery/asyncjs.php"></a></p>
</div>
<p>第二个推荐展位停放的是斯巴鲁（Subaru）WRX。鲜红的外表加上一个超大夸张的涡轮增压进气风口，一看便知是款张力十足的运动型车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9055553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9055553" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Subaru-WRX-450x383.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9055553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Subaru-WRX-450x383.png" alt="图：斯巴鲁WRX。（摄影／蒋维）" width="450" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9055553" class="wp-caption-text">图：斯巴鲁WRX。（摄影／蒋维）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到最后一个展位，是一款福特车。小编看到此车标价为$450,000，不禁心生疑问，有没有搞错？！什么福特车这么贵？是不是多写了一个零？</p>
<figure id="attachment_9055565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9055565" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-cover-450x318.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9055565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-cover-450x318.png" alt="图：此车标价为$450,000。（摄影／蒋维）" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9055565" class="wp-caption-text">图：此车标价为$450,000。（摄影／蒋维）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当工作人员掀开盖在车身上的蓝色绸缎，小编不禁失声“哇”了一下。好COOL的车！旁边的一名资深记者告诉小编，就是这么高的价格还不一定买得到。因为下订单购车的人实在太多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9055586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9055586" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-door-open-450x286.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9055586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-door-open-450x286.png" alt="图：好COOL的车！（摄影／姜维）" width="450" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9055586" class="wp-caption-text">图：好COOL的车！（摄影／蒋维）</figcaption></figure>
<p>福特加西总经理Gerald Wood说Ford GT在2015年首次在底特律车展亮相，并于2016年量产。647马力,碳纤维和铝合金打造，在加拿大制造。定位于兰博基尼级别，成本约为40万元。因为需求火爆，福特将原定的2年生产计划延长至4年。并宣布参加以后4年的IMSA、WEC赛事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9055578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9055578" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-manager-450x383.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9055578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-manager-450x383.png" alt="图：福特加西总经理Gerald Wood介绍Ford GT。（摄影／蒋维）" width="450" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9055578" class="wp-caption-text">图：福特加西总经理Gerald Wood介绍Ford GT。（摄影／蒋维）</figcaption></figure>
<p>听到这些，小编再次仔细打量眼前的这个尤物。整台车运用大量的边线，如同水滴的设计加上多处大型风洞和扰流板，无疑降低了行车阻力。看来Ford GT在空气动力学方面做了不少功课。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9055595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9055595" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-wind-tunnel-450x261.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9055595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-wind-tunnel-450x261.png" alt="图：如同水滴的设计。（摄影／蒋维）" width="450" b="261" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9055595" class="wp-caption-text">图：如同水滴的设计。（摄影／蒋维）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.5升EcoBoost V6发动机置放在驾驶座的后面，透过后车窗可以看到它巨大的体积。不知驾驶这样的车，感受发动机强劲的动力，是何感觉样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9055605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9055605" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-back-window-450x334.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9055605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-with-back-window-450x334.png" alt="图：透过后车窗可以看到发动机。（摄影／蒋维）" width="450" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9055605" class="wp-caption-text">图：透过后车窗可以看到发动机。（摄影／蒋维）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车尾的排气口几乎和尾灯在一个水平位置，大小也差不多，仿佛就在威吓后面的车辆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9055609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9055609" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-on-the-back-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9055609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Ford-GT-on-the-back-450x338.jpg" alt="图：Ford GT车尾的排气口几乎和尾灯在一个水平位置，大小也差不多。（网络截图）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9055609" class="wp-caption-text">图：Ford GT车尾的排气口几乎和尾灯在一个水平位置，大小也差不多。（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，埃德蒙顿车展将亮相750款车，从最新的汽车，卡车和SUV， 到各类老爷车，异类车和订制车。展览会从星期四到周日在埃德蒙顿博览会中心举行。小编希望各位不要错过这次机会，也来一饱眼福。</p>
