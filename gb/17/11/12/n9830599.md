<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>法轮功参加纽约老兵节游行 民众惊呼：太美了</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/14f62fe6a1b3d016_ttl7dayHe8_BGZ03223.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
