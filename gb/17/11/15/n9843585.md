<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图ㄧ：2017亚洲明星盛典 众多明星聚集</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1711151009251487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“2017 Asia Artist Awards”亚洲明星盛典颁奖典礼红毯，众星云集。图上排（左起）为秀智、朴敏英、润娥、金喜善，下排（左起）为朴叙俊、李准基、朴海镇与黄致列均出席盛会。（全景林／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年11月15日讯】（大纪元记者全宇韩国报导）15日下午，“2017 Asia Artist Awards”（简称：2017AAA）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2%E6%98%8E%E6%98%9F%E7%9B%9B%E5%85%B8.md#1">亚洲明星盛典</a>颁奖典礼在首尔蚕室室内体育场举行，由Super Junior队长利特与李泰林担任MC。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9845581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9845581" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1711151942351487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9845581" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1711151942351487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9845581" class="wp-caption-text">“2017 Asia Artist Awards”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2%E6%98%8E%E6%98%9F%E7%9B%9B%E5%85%B8.md#1">亚洲明星盛典</a>颁奖典礼在首尔举行，图为主持人利特和李泰林 。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
