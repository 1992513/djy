<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA威少31分 雷霆仍遭篮网逆转</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月09日讯】（大纪元记者乔奕综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%B7%E9%9C%86.md#1">雷霆</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AF%AE%E7%BD%91.md#1">篮网</a><span lang="EN-US">8</span>日于墨西哥进行海外赛，外界多预期是一场轻松赛事，没想到握有<span lang="EN-US">16</span>分领先的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%B7%E9%9C%86.md#1">雷霆</a>，在比赛后段却惨遭逆转，最终以<span lang="EN-US">95</span>：<span lang="EN-US">100</span>败给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AF%AE%E7%BD%91.md#1">篮网</a>。</p>
<p>雷霆首节多点开花，强大火力让他们半场打完以<span lang="EN-US">58</span>：<span lang="EN-US">49</span>领先篮网<span lang="EN-US">9</span>分；但下半场开始，雷霆手感逐渐失温，第三节全队命中率竟不到三成，主将威斯布鲁克（<span lang="EN-US">Russell Westbrook</span>）更是只有<span lang="EN-US">10</span>投<span lang="EN-US">2</span>中，领先优势也逐渐被耗尽。</p>
<p>第四节雷霆仍然找不到准星，让篮网趁势翻盘、一口气逆转战况，终场就以<span lang="EN-US">5</span>分差逆转雷霆，也让威斯布鲁克拿下的<span lang="EN-US">31</span>分毫无用武之地。</p>
<p>威斯布鲁克此役<span lang="EN-US">27</span>投<span lang="EN-US">10</span>中，拿到全场最高<span lang="EN-US">31</span>分，外带<span lang="EN-US">8</span>篮板<span lang="EN-US">6</span>助攻；全队共<span lang="EN-US">3</span>人得分达双位数；而篮网则是火力平均，共<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人得分破<span lang="EN-US">10</span>，勒佛特（<span lang="EN-US">Caris LeVert</span>）替补攻下<span lang="EN-US">21</span>分<span lang="EN-US">10</span>助攻<span lang="EN-US">5</span>篮板，助篮网中断雷霆<span lang="EN-US">3</span>连胜。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
