<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>想和小动物们近距离接触就来悉尼野生动物园</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小朋友与考拉深情对视（Walkabout Wildlife Park 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年12月07日讯】曾经有部美国电影《我家有个动物园》，没想到在澳洲纽省的中央海岸，有人真的在家开了一个动物园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9932728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9932728" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9932728" class="wp-caption-text">园主Tassin 女士在自家后院开办了野生动物园。（Walkabout Wildlife Park 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当初，园主Tassin 女士买下这栋房子的时候，她发现屋后院有特别多的袋鼠，和其他澳洲本土的小动物。Tassin自己本身酷爱小动物，所以就萌生了一个想法，在自家后院开设一间野生动物园。当然，仅凭原有的几种动物还不足以设置动物园，于是Tassin女士接收了许多丧失了生存能力、需要照顾的澳洲本土小动物，精心在后院饲养。</p>
<p>Australia Walkabout Wildlife Park 距离悉尼市区2小时车程，位于中央海岸Tassin女士家里的动物园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9932733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9932733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-12-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9932733" class="wp-caption-text">园主Tassin 女士与小动物亲密接触（Walkabout Wildlife Park 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走进了这里，就好像走进了动物们的世界，一切都那么自然、随意。和普通动物园不同的是，这里的动物更原生态一些，因为这里本来就是他们的家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9932734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9932734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-13-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9932734" class="wp-caption-text">园主Tassin 女士亲自照料小动物（Walkabout Wildlife Park 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里的动物都是由园主Tassin亲手照顾，游客在园内可以和每一个小动物近距离接触。</p>
<figure style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-14.gif"><img class="size-full wp-image-9932735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-14.gif" alt="" width="480" b="270" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鑫鑫喂<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%83%E6%8B%89.md#1">考拉</a>。（Walkabout Wildlife Park ）</figcaption></figure>
<p>相信大家都认识它吧，没错，就是澳洲代表性小动物<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%83%E6%8B%89.md#1">考拉</a>，别看它有些慢慢吞吞的，它可是爬树的高手。你看，它吃吃停停的模样是不是萌萌嗒？</p>
<p>澳洲特有的小动物兔耳袋狸，是夜间生活的小动物，白天的时候喜欢藏在草垛下面。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-05-600x250.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="250" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">兔耳袋狸超级可爱（Walkabout Wildlife Park ）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哇，兔耳袋狸超级可爱～它的毛发软软的。因为兔耳袋狸长着非常大、非常坚硬的鼻子，所以它特别擅长刨洞。它最喜欢吃的就是树根底下的小虫子。因为鑫鑫是白天来打扰它，所以园主特意为它准备了它最爱吃的零食。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-10-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-10-1-600x360.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">澳洲特有的动物<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%8B%E7%86%8A.md#1">袋熊</a>（Walkabout Wildlife Park 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>澳洲特有的动物<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%8B%E7%86%8A.md#1">袋熊</a>，是我非常喜欢的动物，别看它走起路来慢悠悠的，一旦它跑起来真的是很快的呦！</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-19.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9932740 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-19-600x581.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="581" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鑫鑫给袋熊喂食 （Walkabout Wildlife Park ）</figcaption></figure>
<p>袋熊的习惯也是白天睡觉，鑫鑫为了看它专门准备了它最喜爱的零食，补偿一下被打扰的睡眠。大家知道袋熊最爱吃的是什么零食吗？就是澳洲人传统的一款早餐食物Weet Bix麦片， 是不是有些意外？要知道它一天可以吃一大包呢。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-01-600x250.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="250" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">与蛇亲密接触需要一点勇气。（Walkabout Wildlife Park ）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了野生小动物，园内还有非常多澳洲农场特有的小动物，比如<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E9%A9%BC.md#1">羊驼</a>。大家在动物园入口可以从Tassin那里花一块钱买一包羊驼最喜欢吃的零食就可以喂给它们了。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-02-600x250.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="250" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">与刺猬亲密接触。（Walkabout Wildlife Park ）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些小动物们的性情都非常温和，在Tassin的精心照料下很愿意与人亲近。怎么样，你是不是也想来体验一下呢？</p>
<h2>园内特色: 露营+夜游野生动物园</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9932744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9932744" style="width: 360px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-04.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-9932744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-04.jpg" alt="" width="360" b="235" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9932744" class="wp-caption-text">夜间露营（Walkabout Wildlife Park ）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Australia Walkabout Wildlife Park 夜晚也开放呢。游客们可以选择在丛林里搭帆布帐篷露营，或是住在森林小木屋里。夜晚可以参观及喂食夜间活动的澳洲本土小动物们哟～</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_9932824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9932824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-06-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9932824" class="wp-caption-text">游客与小动物亲密接触（Walkabout Wildlife Park 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9932730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9932730" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-09.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9932730 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-09-600x250.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9932730" class="wp-caption-text">Walkabout Wildlife Park可提供住宿（Walkabout Wildlife Park 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>远离喧闹的市区，置身于丛林中，与野生动物共眠，呼吸大自然的新鲜空气。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-16.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9932737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171207-xinxin-zoo-16-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Walkabout Wildlife Park 路标（Walkabout Wildlife Park ）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Australia Walkabout Wildlife Park 的丛林中有澳洲土着人的遗迹和很多澳洲珍贵的草木药材，可以来个徒步丛林探寻之旅哟～</p>
<p>Australia Walkabout Wildlife Park距离悉尼市区车程2小时，位于中央海岸。</p>
<p>地址： 1 Darkinjung Rd (old maps say Flynns Rd), Calga NSW 2250</p>
<h2>营业时间：</h2>
<p>周一至周日：9am-5pm，5pm之后可以住宿（野营住帆布帐篷或森林小木屋，可以参观夜间生活小动物，需要预约。预约电话：（02）4375 1100）</p>
