<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>年菜话吉祥 大吉大利好彩头</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/01/1001270329281983.jpg" />
<h6>过年吃全鸡象征“起家”，家庭兴旺。（林仕杰／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">合家欢团圆饭 ?<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%A4%E5%A4%95.md#1">除夕</a>夜吉利语</span></strong></p>
<p>在华人的民俗节庆里，“过年”是一个最受重视的传统节日。爆竹声中辞旧岁，过了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%A4%E5%A4%95.md#1">除夕</a>夜，崭新的一年就来临了。迎接新年除了除旧布新，敬神祭祖外，一年尾声除夕夜的团圆饭更是华人最重视的一刻。清人顾录所着《清嘉录．卷十二．<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E5%A4%9C%E9%A5%AD.md#1">年夜饭</a>》说道：“除夜，家庭举宴，长幼咸集，多作吉利语，名曰年夜饭，俗称合家欢。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_7286376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7286376" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1601280848342699-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-7286376 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1601280848342699-450x300.jpg" alt="台湾国健署表示，年菜多为高油、高脂、勾芡等，民众应慎选食材、自己动手做年菜。图为示范套餐。（陈柏州 /大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7286376" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E5%A4%9C%E9%A5%AD.md#1">年夜饭</a>，俗称合家欢。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“阖家团圆”是这顿除夕家宴最重要的象征意义。昔日的农村社会物质较缺乏，趁着家人团聚摆上各式山珍海味，平时不常吃的鸡鸭鱼肉样样上桌，也是犒赏一家子整年来的辛劳。另外，食物的名称也取其圆满吉利，象征大吉大利，为来年先博个好彩头。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>大吉大利年夜菜　博得好彩头</strong></span></p>
<p>以前的农业社会办年货不像现在到处有现成的可购买，过年前，家家户户忙着做年糕，加上曝晒阴干即将熟成的腊肉飘香，乡里巷弄间散发着一股浓浓的年味儿。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">年糕</span></strong>，<span style="color: #800000;">年高</span>也，吃年糕意味着“年年高升”。萝卜因闽南语俗称菜头，萝卜糕因而寓有“好彩头”之意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6897271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6897271" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/1002101119101456-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-6897271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/1002101119101456-450x300.jpg" alt="年糕（摄影：林仕杰/大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6897271" class="wp-caption-text">年糕（林仕杰／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6900494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6900494" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/1002121733301939-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-6900494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/1002121733301939-450x338.jpg" alt="传统石磨与年糕制作法。（摄影:子杨）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6900494" class="wp-caption-text">传统石磨与年糕制作法。（子杨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>发糕</strong></span>，从字面上看就有“发”的含意，且裂缝越大越明显越好，象征家业大发。以前，人们也会在除夕的供桌摆上年饭祭祖，象征年年春，年年有余，五谷丰登。究其因，“春”闽南语发音同“剩余”之意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6902192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6902192" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/1002152156441983-450x340.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-6902192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/1002152156441983-450x340.jpg" alt="新年发财的发糕（刘玉婵／大纪元）" width="450" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6902192" class="wp-caption-text">新年发财的发糕（刘玉婵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过年期间鸡、鱼、蹄、菜、糕等做成的年节大菜、甜点在团圆的时刻都一道道的摆上桌。只要菜名带有喜气，或寓意吉祥的，都是过年必尝的佳肴好菜，除了名称吉利讨喜外，也表达人们对来年美好幸福生活的向往追求。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>鸡，</strong>就是吉<b>！</b></span>团圆夜的餐桌上一定要有鸡，鸡音近“吉”，闽南语的鸡又与“家”同音，过年吃鸡，有“起家”之意，食鸡大吉也。台湾人通常以全鸡的方式烹调，有全家福的意思。随着丁酉年鸡的到来，今年的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">年菜</a>更有鸡料理表现的空间。</p>
<p><strong style="color: #800000;">鱼</strong>，因以祈愿<span style="color: #800000;">“年年有余”</span>，所以不能将鱼吃个精光，有些地方的习俗甚至一筷未动，保持完好。白鲳鱼在过年期间特别抢手，因为鲳昌同音，象征“昌盛”也。近年有一种金鲳鱼也渐受消费者的青睐，除了昌盛昌隆的寓意外，金象征吉祥喜气、招财进宝，喜气更晋一级。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6265749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6265749" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/01/901190909291983-450x293.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-6265749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/01/901190909291983-450x293.jpg" alt="鱼表示“年年有余”且不能吃光(图：彩霞/大纪元)" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6265749" class="wp-caption-text">鱼表示“年年有余”，要有剩余。（彩霞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>圆蹄</strong></span>是一道淮扬菜、湖南菜、客家菜都必备的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">年菜</a>。圆蹄是猪的后腿肉，台湾最常见的就属红烧蹄膀了，常常将肉炖煮得金黄油亮，加上“<span style="color: #800000;">团圆圆润</span>”的讨喜品相，也是年节喜庆的菜肴佳品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7284576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7284576" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/02/1302080735492478-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-7284576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/02/1302080735492478-450x300.jpg" alt="红麹香蹄膀。（曾汉东／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7284576" class="wp-caption-text">红麹香蹄膀。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>三丸，及第。</strong></span>围炉是年夜饭最常见的进行方式，家人围拢着吃火锅也象征团圆之意。坊间的各种丸类多不胜数，但必须有<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>三种不同的丸子</strong></span>，通常是鱼丸、虾丸和肉丸，因丸与圆谐音，三种不同的丸子代表“<span style="color: #800000;">三元及第</span>”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6099413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6099413" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/1201252319081983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-6099413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/1201252319081983.jpg" alt="三元及第酸白锅（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）" width="500" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6099413" class="wp-caption-text">三元及第酸白锅（新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>元宝</strong></span>是大陆北方年菜少不了的，也就是<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>饺子</strong></span>。饺子外型像元宝，象征<span style="color: #800000;">招财进宝，大吉大利</span>。包饺子时会选几个放进钱币，谁吃到包钱币的饺子，代表他来年财源广进大丰收。还有，北方人爱吃的<strong><span style="color: #800000;">包子</span></strong>此时会有咸甜两种馅料，意指“<span style="color: #800000;">咸的包金、甜的包银</span>”，富贵又吉祥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6559586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6559586" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1202030024211839-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-6559586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1202030024211839-450x299.jpg" alt="水饺元宝。（江柏逸 / 大纪元） " width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6559586" class="wp-caption-text">形状肖似元宝的水饺。（江柏逸／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>长年菜</strong></span>是芥菜的一种，闽南语又称刈菜，是传统年节餐桌上不可缺的一道吉祥菜。长年菜体积庞大，翠绿油亮，叶片厚实，象征旺盛的生命力，可带起年节欢乐的气氛。年夜饭吃长年菜，祈望家中长辈身体健康，<span style="color: #800000;">长命百岁</span>。 客家菜<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>姜丝炒大肠</strong></span>意谓<span style="color: #800000;">“金玉绵长</span>”。当然，“<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>五更肠旺</strong></span>”更有运势“<span style="color: #800000;">长旺</span>”的涵义在。此外，“<strong><span style="color: #800000;">发菜</span></strong>”有“<span style="color: #800000;">发财</span>”谐音， “<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>竹笙</strong></span>”意指“<span style="color: #800000;">竹报平安</span>”，都是过年期间的吉祥菜色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6270543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6270543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/01/901242023421983-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-6270543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/01/901242023421983-450x337.jpg" alt="长年菜取意长寿要整根头尾吃完。（林秀霞／大纪元）" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6270543" class="wp-caption-text">长年菜取意长寿要整根头尾吃完。（林秀霞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于过年期间畅销的水果有：<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>苹果</strong>“表平安</span>”，<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>橘子、椪柑、金桔</strong>“好吉利</span>”，<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>凤梨</strong>“旺旺来</span>”。甜品中<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>红枣</strong>“年年好</span>”，故而红枣木耳莲子汤人人爱喝，<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>汤圆</strong></span>则以外形讨好<span style="color: #800000;">，有团圆、圆满之意</span>。</p>
<p>在人类生命的长河中，中华民族发展出饶富特色的饮食文化，并透过文字语言形音义的特性，在欢庆的年节里，赋予食物吉祥吉利的意涵，映现庶民追求丰衣足食、家道兴旺的普遍愿望。这些先民的智慧在我们围炉夜话时也添加了喜愿话题。＠* #</p>
