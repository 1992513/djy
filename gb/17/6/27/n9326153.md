<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>郭富城娇妻方媛 看展俏丽晒孕肚</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年06月27日讯】（大纪元记者黄钟台湾台北报导）香港男星郭富城的新婚娇妻方媛，26日晒出在上海参观展览的照片，她穿着黑白色的优雅洋装与遮阳帽，即使四肢纤瘦，但高腰的腰带设计显出圆润的孕肚，网友目测她怀胎约4、5个月，预祝她生产顺利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9326205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9326205" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/5f0bad64gy1fgyyijocskj20yf171kjl.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9326205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/5f0bad64gy1fgyyijocskj20yf171kjl.jpg" alt="方媛穿着优雅洋装与遮阳帽。（方媛微博）" width="350" b="550" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9326205" class="wp-caption-text">方媛穿着优雅洋装与遮阳帽观展。（方媛微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>51岁郭富城（城城）与29岁中国小模方媛今年结婚，方媛近来微博照片多以上半身为主，这次可见明显孕肚，郭富城的粉丝开心送祝福，表示：“又一个辣妈出世，怀孕还这么美。”“恭喜城城，恭喜方媛！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_9326215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9326215" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/270477.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-9326215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/270477.jpg" alt="方媛穿着黑白色的优雅洋装与遮阳帽。（方媛微博）" width="400" b="440" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9326215" class="wp-caption-text">方媛只胖孕肚，四肢依然纤瘦。（方媛微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而郭富城日前被拍到开着价值约2500万台币（约824,000美元）的麦拉伦超跑，载方媛到湾仔用餐。被媒体问及方媛是否怀女娃、将在9月生产？城城以保护家人为由，不公开谈论家庭私事，但他透露：“如果有一天在街上看到我携老扶幼，祝福我就OK啦。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_9326220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9326220" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/161128072300100311.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-9326220" title="郭富城" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/161128072300100311.jpg" alt="郭富城" width="350" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9326220" class="wp-caption-text">郭富城不公开谈论家庭私事，但盼大家祝福。图为城城资料照。（宋祥龙／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
