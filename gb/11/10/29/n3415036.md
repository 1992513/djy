<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：828名跨国电信诈骗嫌疑犯被捕</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>新闻出处：新唐人英语新闻</p>
<p>828 Transnational Telecommunications Fraud Suspects Apprehended<br />
828名跨国电信诈骗嫌疑犯被捕</p>
<p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />
1. <b>transnational</b>: adj. [tr?nsˋn???n?l]跨国的<br />
2. <b>telecommunication</b>: n. [?t?l?k??mjun?ˋke??n]电信<br />
3. <b>fraud</b>: n. [fr?d]骗局、欺骗<br />
4. <b>apprehend</b>: v. [??pr?ˋh?nd]逮捕<br />
5. <b>repatriate</b>: v. [riˋpetr??et]把…遗返回国<br />
6. <b>crack</b>: v. [kr?k]解开、破解<br />
7. <b>joint</b>: adj. [d???nt]共同的<br />
8. <b>commit</b>: v. [k?ˋm?t]犯(罪)<br />
9. <b>involve</b>: v. [?nˋvɑlv]牵涉<br />
10. <b>trafficking</b>: n. [ˋtr?f?k??]非法交易(尤指毒品买卖)<br />
11. <b>swindle</b>: v. [ˋsw?nd!]诈骗、骗取<br />
12. <b>set up</b>: ph.建造</p>
<p>On Thursday, 45 people accused of transnational telecommunications fraud were <b>repatriated</b> from Indonesia to Beijing. They’re part of 828 suspects who have been apprehended, suspected of stealing a total of around $34 million in over 1,800 cases.<br />
周四，45名被指控跨国电信诈骗的人从印尼被遣返回北京。他们是828名遭逮嫌疑犯中的一部分，涉嫌从超过 1,800个案件中，窃取共约3,400万美元。</p>
<p>According to a statement released by China’s Ministry of Public Security, police <b>cracked</b> two large transnational telecom fraud groups on Wednesday. The bust was a<b> join</b>t effort from police in China, Taiwan, Indonesia, Cambodia, the Philippines, Vietnam, Thailand, Laos, Malaysia and Singapore.<br />
根据中国公安部发表的一份声明，警方于周三，破获了两个大型的跨国电信诈骗集团。这次逮捕是由中国、台湾、印尼、柬埔寨、菲律宾、越南、泰国、寮国、马来西亚和新加坡警方共同的努力。</p>
<p>Chinese police explain how telecommunications fraud is typically <b>committed</b>.<br />
中国警方解释典型电信诈骗是如何犯罪。</p>
<p>[Huang Zuyue, Criminal Investigation Bureau]:<br />
&#8220;The suspects use this a to fake roles such as administrators, judges or police officers. In calls to victims, they try to cheat victims by making them believe they are <b>involved</b> in money laundering, drug <b>trafficking</b> or other criminal cases and then <b>swindle</b> large sum of the victims&#8217; money and property.&#8221;<br />
刑事侦查局黄祖跃说：<br />
“嫌疑用假扮如管理者、法官或警官的剧本。与受害者的通话中，试图欺骗他们，使他们相信自己涉及洗钱、贩毒或其它刑事案件，然后诈取受害者大笔金钱或财产。”</p>
<p>Networks are <b>set up </b>across the countries to cheat people of their money, and the majority of victims were Mainlanders and Taiwanese.<br />
设立跨国网络以骗取人们的钱，大多数受害者是大陆和台湾人。</p>
<p>The investigation is still underway and police continue to pursue suspects.<br />
调查仍在进行中，警方持续追补嫌犯。</p>
