<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>探索石垣岛 川平湾、公设市场、御神崎灯塔</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319334-705081-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>石垣岛上的川平湾美景。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬日本报导）石垣岛是日本冲绳的一颗明珠，而这次将带你走访岛上的三个必去景点：川平湾、石垣公设市场和御神崎灯塔。准备好跟我们一起享受这趟轻松有趣的旅程吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319398" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319398-705083_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319398-705083_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319398" class="wp-caption-text">石垣岛港边景色。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>川平湾 美得让人屏息的秘境</h2>
<p>川平湾，这个被米其林绿色指南评选为三星级景点的地方，无疑是石垣岛上最令人惊艳的景点之一。这里的海水清澈见底，白沙非常美，还有保存良好的珊瑚礁。如果运气好的话，你还有机会见到海龟呢！不管是自由行、跟团或是邮轮观光，来到石垣岛的游客几乎都会把这里列入行程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319401" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319401-705114_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319401-705114_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319401" class="wp-caption-text">石垣岛绿树成荫，搭配湾景，形成一幅画作。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从石垣港或石垣市区开车到川平湾大约只需30分钟，Google地图导航轻松抵达。现场有一大片付费停车场，也可以尝试找到隐藏在小路里的免费停车空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319399" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319399-705084_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319399-705084_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319399" class="wp-caption-text">来到川平湾可以体验玻璃船的行程。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到川平湾，别忘了登上展望台，是一个大凉亭，可以俯瞰整个海湾的美景。这里的海水带有多层渐层的蓝色，仿佛是一幅自然的画作。虽然川平湾禁止游泳，但你还是可以在浅水处踏浪，或是参加玻璃船之旅，透过船底的玻璃窗欣赏海底世界。搭船约30分钟。虽然隔着玻璃看海底风景不如潜水来得震撼，但这样轻松的方式依然让人感到满足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319400" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319400-705085_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319400-705085_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319400" class="wp-caption-text">川平湾的沙很细。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也可以站在凉亭里，望着川平湾的美景发呆一下，形容不出来的美丽，即便是暑假八月底的夏天，日本的热还可以用停车场旁的干净厕所来洗个脸，或是伴手礼商店，买个日本冰淇淋来消暑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319405" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319405-705113_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319405-705113_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319405" class="wp-caption-text">川平湾的小铺，有冰淇淋，也有冲绳特产。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>旅客最感兴趣的是来了几辆餐车，大都贩卖现烤的石垣牛，碍于要现点现烤，搭游览车的游客就不太方便了。</p>
<p>别忘了还有很有历史味道的小神社—川平观音堂，周边珊瑚礁岩搭的围墙也很有特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319402" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319402-705087_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319402-705087_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319402" class="wp-caption-text">川平观音堂。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>石垣公设市场 当地风情的最佳体验</h2>
<p>石垣市公设市场位于石垣市中心，是一个结合购物、饮食与文化体验的好地方。在这里，你可以找到当地新鲜的海产、各种特色小吃，以及手工艺品和纪念品。清晨的市场尤其热闹，新鲜捕获的海产陆续登场，满足了当地居民与游客的味蕾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319403" style="width: 512px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319403-705111_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319403-705111_medium.jpg" alt="" width="512" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319403" class="wp-caption-text">石垣公设市场商店多，伴手礼或文创品都有。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>逛完市场，你可以随意漫步于步行街上，两旁林立的小店铺售卖着具有冲绳特色的商品，买件写着Drion字样的T恤，穿上它让你一边走一边体验当地的生活氛围。从传统的冲绳拉面到各种特色小吃，每一口都充满了浓浓的冲绳风味。这里的购物和美食体验绝对是你不容错过的一部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319404" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319404-705112_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319404-705112_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319404" class="wp-caption-text">石垣公设市场不怕下雨，周边的特色店也可以逛一逛。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这边的免税商店冷气很凉，伴手礼的品项超级多，糖果、饼干、石垣牛、酱料啦应有尽有。文创店的项链、手环也很吸睛，价格的话，台湾人就会直接除以四，价位上比起西元二千年来讲，真的便宜许多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319414" style="width: 512px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319414-705110_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319414-705110_medium.jpg" alt="" width="512" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319414" class="wp-caption-text">冲绳名产：黑糖。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>御神崎灯塔 壮丽景观的最佳观赏地</h2>
<p>位于石垣岛西边的御神崎灯塔，虽然不如川平湾般人气鼎盛，但这里的壮丽景观绝对让人难忘。灯塔附近有两条路线可走，建议都走一遍，一边可看到深蓝色的海水和远方的西表岛，另一边则展现了悬崖峭壁的雄伟美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319409" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319409-705082_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319409-705082_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319409" class="wp-caption-text">位于石垣岛西边的御神崎灯塔。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灯塔旁还有一座观音像，是为了纪念多年前在此沉船事件中罹难的中国商船而设立的。这里也是当地许多潜水业者喜爱的地点，如果你也是潜水爱好者，不妨考虑来这里一探究竟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319410" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319410-705090_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319410-705090_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319410" class="wp-caption-text">灯塔旁还有一座观音像，是为了纪念多年前在此沉船事件中罹难的中国商船而设立的。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此趟行程是租计程车跑的，为的是要品尝道地的石垣牛，也可以避免走错路，因为沿途都是山和甘蔗田，不然就是无尽的大海。事先请计程车司机帮忙预订好知名店家，下午四点半一开店就进去，没想到好多台湾人也来了，跟台湾的烧烤来讲，因为石垣岛非大都市，所以老店的装潢比不上台湾百货公司新颖的烧烤店，但有很浓的日本味道，因为要自己烧烤，所以记得要算好时间，避免担误行程。</p>
<p>这就是这次石垣岛六小时之旅的精彩缩影，三个各具特色的景点，让你一次感受岛上的美丽与文化。如果你正计划前往石垣岛，千万不要错过这些美丽的地方！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14319413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14319413" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319413-705088_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14319413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319413-705088_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14319413" class="wp-caption-text">灯塔旁悬崖峭壁的雄伟美景。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
