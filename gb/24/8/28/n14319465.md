<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小鸭失去所有“亲人”后与大纽芬兰犬成朋友</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14319471-dog-and-duck-1080x720-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>失去家人后的小鸭子与纽芬兰小狗成为朋友——它们的滑稽动作融化了人们的心。（插图：《大纪元时报》，杰西?瓦利埃提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年08月28日讯】（英文大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／王净一编译）失去所有兄弟姐妹后，鸭子奥拉夫（Olaf）与一只名叫斯文（Sven）的纽芬兰小狗建立了美好的友谊。</p>
<p>它们在主人的农场里建立的不可思议的情谊和嬉戏打闹的场景被绘制在一本名为《比斯和奥利历险记》（Adventures of Biss and Olee）的儿童图画书中。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676210-210510087_832402747393713_7086705495699532409_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676210-210510087_832402747393713_7086705495699532409_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">奥拉夫（奥利）和斯文（比斯）。（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在2020年的疫情封锁期间，这只鸭子和它的兄弟姐妹们被俄克拉荷马州（Oklahoma）的一个农场主家庭收留，在最糟糕的情况发生之前，它们一起快乐地生活了一段时间。由于当时是3月份，晚上还很冷，小鸭子们先是住在家里，等它们长出羽毛。</p>
<p>35岁的杰西?瓦利埃（Jessie Vallier）告诉《大纪元时报》：“它们实际上在我丈夫的办公室里住了将近一个月，然后我们才把它们搬到外面。”</p>
<p>她说：“所有的东西都用栅栏围起来，受到很好的保护，但是我们很确定，土狼还是发现了一个薄弱点，然后除了奥拉夫，其它所有的鸭子都被吃掉了。”</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676220-312205918_1191961268399077_1830879784672298737_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676220-312205918_1191961268399077_1830879784672298737_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><figure style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676216-275834481_2775617549413574_7678731173227567431_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676216-275834481_2775617549413574_7678731173227567431_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="449" b="561" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">驯马师兼骑术教练瓦利埃太太说，她的家人随时准备帮助救助动物。[由苔丝?莫恩（Tess Maune）提供]</figcaption></figure>就在这对夫妇同意收养这些3周大的小鸭子的同时，内布拉斯加州（Nebraska）的一只5个月大的小狗也进入了他们的视线；大流行病期间，这只小狗的主人正在苦苦挣扎。于是，瓦利埃一家把斯文带到了他们位于断箭镇（Broken Arrow）的农场。</p>
<p>驯马师兼骑术教练瓦利埃太太说：“我们从不拒绝救助动物。”</p>
<p>两三周后，奥拉夫失去了兄弟姐妹，瓦利埃太太和丈夫还没来得及想办法，小鸭子就和斯文结下了不解之缘。它真的开始对这只大狗感兴趣了。慢慢地，小鸭子开始跟着斯文——这只“棕色大玩具熊”——很快，它们就成了好朋友。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676215-271334394_331853761933166_8173906756734940909_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676215-271334394_331853761933166_8173906756734940909_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="454" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676211-238831117_556892818968434_660532091703462085_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676211-238831117_556892818968434_660532091703462085_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><a src="https://vod.brightchat.com/embed/289ea2b5-dbe4-4af4-b5b9-ee3d1a1124a7?r=16x9&amp;d=79" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></p>
<p>（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</p>
<p>瓦利埃太太认为，有一天她发现这对朋友一起在儿童游泳池里玩耍时，它们的感情才开始爆发。</p>
<p>“斯文躺在泳池里，奥拉夫和它在一起。我觉得从那时起，奥拉夫就像在说：‘哦，你喜欢水；我们是一样的，我们是朋友。’老实说，那一刻之后，它们两个就成了最好的朋友。”</p>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676221-358349004_3413592502225141_589053744217539110_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676221-358349004_3413592502225141_589053744217539110_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="434" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676214-265247262_1416987408697058_4509244149311175228_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676214-265247262_1416987408697058_4509244149311175228_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="599" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，这两个朋友的性格还是很不一样，瓦利埃太太说。</p>
<p>这只卢昂鸭（Rouen duck）“很霸道，很有主见”。奥拉夫负责管理农场。</p>
<p>“如果它担心一只鸡，甚至一只狗，它就会把危险从它们身边赶走。它就像一只强大的小鸭子，觉得自己需要照顾所有人。”她说。</p>
<p>斯文则被描述为“一个大块头，非常可爱，非常温顺”。它和孩子们相处得很好，和动物们相处得也很好。</p>
<p>它的主人说：“它从来都不亢奋。它会精力充沛地跑来跑去，转两圈后，它就累了，想躺下。”</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676212-238911327_525448558527029_6251285788604794119_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676212-238911327_525448558527029_6251285788604794119_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676219-304484019_1352428625290602_5053620763583777048_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676219-304484019_1352428625290602_5053620763583777048_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>奥拉夫和斯文之间的亲密关系表现得十分可爱。奥拉夫很爱护自己的朋友，它会低下头，试图把人们从这个温顺的大块头身边赶走。它还会不停地给狗梳毛。</p>
<p>瓦利埃太太说：“它好像一直在啄它。但它也会坐下来，用它的喙沙沙地刮斯文的皮毛。斯文经常流口水，奥拉夫喜欢给斯文擦嘴。它把整个喙伸进斯文的脸颊，看起来就像在（给它）刷牙。”</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676213-240987149_2944553905818191_200489496866245480_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676213-240987149_2944553905818191_200489496866245480_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据了解，这对快乐的小家伙还玩起了接球游戏，并享受着打闹。</p>
<p>“我们会给斯文扔一个球，鸭子会和它一起追球。如果它们摔跤了，斯文就会站起来跑，鸭子就会飞快地追上去。”它们的主人说。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676217-278627387_718871742464143_3589957672856809406_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2F27%2Fid5676217-278627387_718871742464143_3589957672856809406_n.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（由杰西?瓦利埃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现在，这两个朋友都已经4岁了，却从未有过争吵。</p>
<p>“它们相处得非常融洽。如果奥拉夫咬得太用力，斯文就会转过鼻子把它推开。但从来没有过任何攻击行为。”</p>
<p>瓦利埃夫妇说，虽然他们知道鸟类会在不同的生物身上留下印记，但他们自己从未见过这样的事情。斯文和奥拉夫的友谊已经持续了四年，这给它们带来了无尽的欢乐。</p>
<p>她说：“这是一段非常特殊的友谊，我们能成为其中的一员，真的很荣幸。”</p>
