<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠灭罪安全夜警民同乐 表彰王氏青年会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306929-DSC04701-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>8月6日，波士顿华埠庆祝“全美灭罪安全夜”，王氏青年会获褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年08月08日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）波士顿王氏青年会（Wang YMCA）扎根华埠地区，已有约110年，在身心健康、文化娱乐、治安改善等方面贡献良多。因而今年的华埠“全美灭罪安全夜”（National Night Out）活动向其颁发了“杰出社区服务奖”。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">8月6日下午，波士顿华埠大同村户外人群熙熙攘攘，市内政要嘉宾、警察、EMS等公职人员穿行其间，一同庆祝这个警民同乐的日子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14306921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306921-DSC04571.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306921-DSC04571-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306921" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿警察们在现场参与庆祝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14306926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306926-DSC04582.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306926-DSC04582-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306926" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿华埠庆祝“全美灭罪安全夜”，大同村广场上人来人往。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14306930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306930-DSC04709.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306930-DSC04709-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306930" class="wp-caption-text">华埠治安巡逻队的义工们参与“全美灭罪安全夜”活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">据主持人曾雪清介绍，王氏青年会长年运营着许多优秀的项目，包括帮助青少年改善身心，与塔夫茨医院合作帮助长者减少孤独等等。近期，王氏青年会在华埠设立了食物分发站，还在热浪侵袭期间开放了冷气中心。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在大同村广场，王氏青年会代表们接受了由市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）、警察局长考克斯（Michael Cox）等多位嘉宾共同颁发的奖状。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">王氏青年会执行长帕伊（Sokthea Phay）说，很荣幸能与YMCA的老、中、青世代一同上台领奖，也很荣幸能成为这个社区的一部分，为社区的福祉和安全而服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14306918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306918-DSC04560.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306918-DSC04560-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306918" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员Ed Flynn向华埠居民们打招呼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14306927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306927-DSC04590.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306927-DSC04590-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306927" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭参与庆祝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14306928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306928-DSC04639.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306928-DSC04639-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306928" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿警察局长Michael Cox参与庆祝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天还有许多政府和社区团体参与庆祝活动，与居民交流。现场有萨福克地区检察官海登（Kevin Hayden）办公室的小礼品摊位，有塔夫茨医院的血压检测摊位，塔夫茨牙医学院的健康摊位，波士顿EMS的游戏摊位等等。服务华埠社区多年的中华公所治安巡逻队亦有多名义工在场，得到政要嘉宾们的赞誉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14306916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306916-DSC04528.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306916-DSC04528-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306916" class="wp-caption-text">现场有免费派发棉花糖的摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14306917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306917-DSC04548.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306917-DSC04548-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306917" class="wp-caption-text">现场有免费测血压的摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14306925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14306925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306925-DSC04579.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14306925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14306925-DSC04579-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14306925" class="wp-caption-text">萨福克地区检察官Kevin Hayden办公室带来小礼品摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">波士顿市长吴弭尽管已怀孕半年，仍然坚持连续两天参与市内的灭罪安全夜活动，奔波于10多个社区。在华埠的活动中，吴弭感谢所有公职人员，包括警察、消防员、急救人员等等致力于保卫社区安全。她还特别用中文感谢社区及政府各部门的支持。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">警察局长考克斯称赞，华埠是波士顿市内凝聚力最强的社区之一，正因为有了居民团体的合作，警方才能更有效地维持社区安全。◇</p>
