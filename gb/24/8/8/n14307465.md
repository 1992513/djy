<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯三个区房价过去五年翻倍</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14307466-704-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>根据房地产集团Domain日前发布的房价报告，过去五年中，珀斯三个地区的房价翻了一倍。图为珀斯房产。（林文责/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年08月08日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">根据房地产集团</span><span lang="en-AU">Domain</span><span lang="zh-TW">日前发布的房价报告，过去五年中，珀斯三个地区的房价翻了一倍。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据“</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">WA Today</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">”</span><span lang="zh-TW">网站</span><span lang="zh-TW">报导，</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Cooloongup</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">区的房产中位价上涨了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">114%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">54.2</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，是涨幅最大的郊区。涨幅前十的其它地区区包括</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Parmelia</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（上涨近</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">109%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">50.1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元）和</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Armadale</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（上涨近</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">100%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">45</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元）。</span></p>
<p>尽管大多数房价大幅上涨的郊区位于珀斯相对可负担的外围郊区，但珀斯山地地带的<span lang="en-AU">Darlington</span>区（上涨<span lang="en-AU">92%</span>，达到<span lang="en-AU">105</span>万澳元）和海滨地区——<span lang="en-AU">Kallaroo</span>（上涨<span lang="en-AU">89%</span>，达到<span lang="en-AU">117</span>万澳元）也录得了显着的涨幅。</p>
<p>所谓的“伴娘”郊区（这里是指位于知名地区旁边的非知名地区）也表现良好，例如<span lang="en-AU">Mullaloo</span>的房价上涨了<span lang="en-AU">67%</span>，达到<span lang="en-AU">112</span>万澳元，比它相邻的<span lang="en-AU">Sorrento</span>便宜了<span lang="en-AU">30</span>万澳元，而<span lang="en-AU">Sorrento</span>区的中位价为<span lang="en-AU">144</span>万。</p>
<p>根据<span lang="en-AU">Domain</span>的数据，珀斯的房价在第二季度上涨了<span lang="en-AU">52,000</span>澳元，即<span lang="en-AU">6.6%</span>，达到创纪录的<span lang="en-AU">852,240</span>澳元。这比<span lang="en-AU">1990</span>年的房价——<span lang="en-AU">96,000</span>澳元上涨了<span lang="en-AU">815%</span>。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">InSynergy</span> <span lang="en-AU">Advisory</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的房产研究主管黄先生（音译）（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Kevin</span> <span lang="en-AU">Hoang</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，珀斯的住宅物业市场发展非常迅速，几乎所有销售都没有价格指南，导致销售价格比保留价高出数万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“这可能是（买家）由于担心错失机会，试图抓住进一步价格上涨的机会。”他说。</span></p>
<p>黄先生表示，供应需要多年才能赶上不断增长的需求，这将进一步推高价格上涨。</p>
<p>他说：“在过去的<span lang="en-AU">12</span>个月中，新住宅完工量为<span lang="en-AU">15,500</span>套，而人口增加了<span lang="en-AU">94,000</span>人，导致严重的供应不足，（这意味者）每建成一处新住宅就有六名新移民到达。”</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据最新的住房协会发布的行业报告</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">HIA</span> <span lang="en-AU">Trades</span> <span lang="en-AU">Report</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">，对于建筑行业来说，另一个令人担忧的迹象是，学徒开始从事建筑行业的数量急剧下降，比前一年减少了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">17%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">HIA</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">执行董事穆雷（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Geordan</span> <span lang="en-AU">Murray</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，技能短缺最严重的是砖瓦工、陶瓷贴砖工、屋顶工、木工和抹灰工，这导致了房产交易价格上涨，进而推高了新房的价格。</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：杨新云</p>
