<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金黄花海盛开了 快来太麻里金针山赏花吧！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304047-699831-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>太麻里金针山花季来了哦。（金针山休闲农业发展协会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月03日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）你是否准备好被一片金黄的花海震撼了？台东太麻里金针山花季，每年的八月到九月隆重登场，这场视觉与心灵的盛宴绝对不容错过！站在1450公尺的金针山顶端，你会看到一片如诗如画的美景，成千上万朵金针花在阳光下热情绽放，让人心醉神迷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14304050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304050" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304050-699834.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14304050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304050-699834-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304050" class="wp-caption-text">花季期间一直到九月底。（金针山休闲农业发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>太麻里金针山不仅是东部三大金针花产地之一，还与花莲的六十石山、赤柯山齐名。这里的高山气候和怡人环境，让游客不仅能观赏金黄花海，还能在清凉的微风中尽情享受大自然的馈赠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14304052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304052" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304052-699836.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14304052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304052-699836-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304052" class="wp-caption-text">太麻里金针山是东部三大金针花产地之一。（金针山休闲农业发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在这里，每一朵金针花都是大自然的画笔，用金黄为山坡填色，组成了一幅幅绝美的自然画卷。走在花海中的游客，无不被这醉人的美景吸引，沿着蜿蜒的步道，从不同角度探索金针山的优雅与魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14304051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304051" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304051-699835.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14304051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304051-699835-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304051" class="wp-caption-text">忘忧谷七月下旬时的花况。（金针山休闲农业发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金针山休闲农业发展协会透露，千鹤亭的花况特别好，花海的金针花如金黄地毯般铺展，双乳峰、忘忧谷以及石头屋，也开始有零星的花朵绽放。若你打算上山赏花，记得留意道路施工，慢行欣赏这一年一度的美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14304048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304048" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304048-699832.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14304048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304048-699832-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304048" class="wp-caption-text">金针山休闲农业发展协会透露，千鹤亭的花况特别好。（金针山休闲农业发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动期间一直到九月底，太麻里乡公所提供了最新的花季资讯。赶紧规划你的行程，体验金针山的自然美景吧！</p>
<p>更多资讯请参阅：太麻里乡公所 FB与Google 地图位置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14304053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304053" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304053-699837.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14304053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304053-699837-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304053" class="wp-caption-text">金针山农业发展协会，邀请大家来赏花哦。（金针山休闲农业发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
