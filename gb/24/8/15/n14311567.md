<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>皮肤美白大作战 中医推荐三大关键策略</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311799-da9875ec04ffe529f6e9421cea8d87db-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美白药膳采用枸杞、薏米、红枣、白茯苓等中药材。（大纪元制图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">皮肤美白是许多人关注的话题。中医提供“食补、自制面膜、穴道按摩”三大关键策略，助你改善肤色和肌肤明亮度：</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14311644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311644" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311644-4c069bdcaa12165d146d203e0f356236.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14311644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311644-4c069bdcaa12165d146d203e0f356236-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311644" class="wp-caption-text">（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>1.食补吃出美白明亮肌肤</b></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>
<h3><b><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>食用清热、抗氧化食材</b></h3>
</li>
</ul>
<figure id="attachment_14311645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311645" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311645-d4441e10cc9ef6bbf7cdad24bf0b38ac.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14311645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311645-d4441e10cc9ef6bbf7cdad24bf0b38ac-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311645" class="wp-caption-text">（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">中医认为，肤色暗沉与体内的湿气和热气有关。因此，建议多食用具有清热和解毒作用的食物，如苦瓜、黄瓜、柠檬、绿茶等。还可以摄入富含维生素C和抗氧化剂的食物，如番茄、橙子、草莓等，以提升皮肤的亮白度和抗氧化作用。</span></p>
<h3><b><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>饮用中药饮品</b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">有一些中药材具有美白的作用，如白芷、白术、白茯苓等，可以用来泡水或煮汤后服用，以达到美白的效果。需留意的是，</span><span style="color: #800000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">使用中药之前，最好咨询中医师或药剂师适合个人情况的药材和剂量</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><b>?</b></span></p>
<h3><b><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>摄取美白药膳</b><b>：</b><b>枸杞薏米汤</b></h3>
<p><b>食材：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">排骨150克、枸杞10克、薏米20克、红枣5-8颗、白茯苓10克、生姜3片、大葱1根、温水适量</span></p>
<p><b>料理方式：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.将排骨、枸杞、薏米、红枣和茯苓洗净备用。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.小锅内加入适量温水，放入所有材料炖煮，煮沸后转小火，继续煮约1小时，直到排骨熟烂、汤汁浓郁。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.根个人口味加入适量盐调味，再煮沸一会即可。</span></p>
<p><b>[注意]</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 某些中药材可能对胎儿或婴儿产生影响，</span><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">孕妇或正在哺乳期的妇女，应咨询中医师的建议才能食用中药汤。</span></p>
<p><b>疗效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">枸杞和薏米具有美白功效，白茯苓有益气养阴的作用，红枣可增加汤的甜味。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.</span><b>自制美白面膜</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14311643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311643" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311643-ee1ab5f731a6ff246f63c351a80277f3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14311643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311643-ee1ab5f731a6ff246f63c351a80277f3-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311643" class="wp-caption-text">（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">自制“薏仁美白面膜”，既方便又省荷包，还能打造亮白的肌肤。以下是制作方法：</span></p>
<p><b>材料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">薏仁粉 2汤匙、优格 适量、蜂蜜 1茶匙、柠檬汁 1茶匙</span></p>
<p><b>步骤：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.将薏仁粉倒入小碗中。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.一边慢慢地加入优格，一边将两者搅拌，直到形成均匀的糊状物质。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.添加蜂蜜和柠檬汁，接着搅拌，确保所有成分都充分混匀。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.涂抹面膜于脸部和颈部，注意要避开眼睛和嘴巴的周围。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.保持面膜服贴15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20分钟，直到面膜变干燥。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">6.用温水轻轻洗去面膜，然后用柔软的毛巾将肌肤轻拍干净。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">每周使用这个面膜1～2次。它具有美白、保湿的作用，还能增加肌肤亮度，使肤色均匀。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">薏仁粉富含维生素B和多种矿物质，有助于使肤色均匀，减少黑斑；优格中的乳酸可以去除角质；柠檬汁含有维生素C，能提升肌肤亮度；蜂蜜具有保湿和滋润的效果。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>3.按压美白穴道</b></span></h2>
<p><b>太阳穴：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于眉骨和太阳窝之间。轻轻按压，能促进血液循环，增加皮肤亮度。</span></p>
<div class="mceTemp">
<figure id="attachment_12646993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12646993" style="width: 372px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Motion-sickness-acupuncture-point-3-1226-health11-e1723692518518.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-12646993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Motion-sickness-acupuncture-point-3-1226-health11-e1723692518518-450x508.png" alt="晕车想吐的救急穴位：太阳穴、风池穴、翳风穴和内关穴。（健康1+1／大纪元）" width="372" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12646993" class="wp-caption-text">（健康1+1／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>风池穴：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于颈部的两侧，与颈椎下方的凹陷处相对应。用指腹按压，有助于纾解压力，改善肌肤暗沉现象。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14311647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311647" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311647-eccf79cacc0f3d1430379bb62c48330f.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14311647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311647-eccf79cacc0f3d1430379bb62c48330f-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311647" class="wp-caption-text">（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>涌泉穴：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于足底部前三分之一处。它与体内能量相关，泡脚时按摩或用手指按压，可以促进血液循环，改善脚部疲劳，进而提高肌肤亮度。<strong>◎</strong></span></p>
