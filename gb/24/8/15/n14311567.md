<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>皮肤美白大作战 中医推荐三大关键策略</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311645-d4441e10cc9ef6bbf7cdad24bf0b38ac-e1724683832276.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr><span style="font-weight:400">皮肤美白是许多人关注的话题。中医提供“食补、自制面膜、穴道按摩”三大关键策略，助你改善肤色和肌肤明亮度：</span></p><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311644-4c069bdcaa12165d146d203e0f356236.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311644-4c069bdcaa12165d146d203e0f356236.jpg" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311644-4c069bdcaa12165d146d203e0f356236.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311644-4c069bdcaa12165d146d203e0f356236.jpg" alt="（马光医疗网提供）" width="450" b="450"/></picture></a><figcaption>（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure></div><h2><span style="color:#008000"><b>1.食补吃出美白明亮肌肤</b></span></h2><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><ul>
 	<li>
<h3><b><span style="font-weight:400">?</span>食用清热、抗氧化食材</b></h3>
</li>
</ul><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p><span style="font-weight:400">中医认为，肤色暗沉与体内的湿气和热气有关。因此，建议多食用具有清热和解毒作用的食物，如苦瓜、黄瓜、柠檬、绿茶等。还可以摄入富含维生素C和抗氧化剂的食物，如番茄、橙子、草莓等，以提升皮肤的亮白度和抗氧化作用。</span></p><h3><b><span style="font-weight:400">?</span>饮用中药饮品</b></h3><p><span style="font-weight:400">有一些中药材具有美白的作用，如白芷、白术、白茯苓等，可以用来泡水或煮汤后服用，以达到美白的效果。需留意的是，</span><span style="color:#800000"><span style="font-weight:400">使用中药之前，最好咨询中医师或药剂师适合个人情况的药材和剂量</span><span style="font-weight:400">。</span><b>?</b></span></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311645-d4441e10cc9ef6bbf7cdad24bf0b38ac.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311645-d4441e10cc9ef6bbf7cdad24bf0b38ac.jpg" type="image/webp"/><source b="450"/></picture></a><figcaption>（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure></div><p><b>食材：</b><span style="font-weight:400">排骨150克、枸杞10克、薏米20克、红枣5-8颗、白茯苓10克、生姜3片、大葱1根、温水适量</span></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p><span style="font-weight:400">2.小锅内加入适量温水，放入所有材料炖煮，煮沸后转小火，继续煮约1小时，直到排骨熟烂、汤汁浓郁。</span></p><p><span style="font-weight:400">3.根个人口味加入适量盐调味，再煮沸一会即可。</span></p><p><b>[注意]</b><span style="font-weight:400"> 某些中药材可能对胎儿或婴儿产生影响，</span><span style="font-weight:400;color:#800000">孕妇或正在哺乳期的妇女，应咨询中医师的建议才能食用中药汤。</span></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden 2汤匙、优格 适量、蜂蜜 1茶匙、柠檬汁 1茶匙</span></p><p><b>步骤：</b></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad"
