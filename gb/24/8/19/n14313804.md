<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉县平林暨民和国小 获国教署午餐菜单午星奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313805-703253-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义县平林国小暨民和国小，荣获国教署午餐菜单“午星奖”表扬。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月19日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）教育部国民及学前教育署为使学校了解午餐菜单的多样性，日前办理“学校午餐菜单表扬活动”，透过搜集各县市中央厨房学校午餐菜单，今年度以“午星奖”为题，表扬符合异国料理、创意设计、护眼料理、高钙料理及在地食材等面向，以美味又健康的标准，表扬菜色优良的学校，公开菜单设计内容供其他学校参考。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14313806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313806" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313806-703254-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14313806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313806-703254-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313806" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县平林国小暨民和国小，荣获国教署午餐菜单“午星奖”表扬。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义县平林国小透过多样食材与营养素结合互补，如香菇的维生素D、小鱼干的钙，满足高钙护眼且均衡，采用嘉义特产如空心菜、虱目鱼和凤梨，营养丰并达地产地销，融合异国烹调技巧，创造多元口味和色彩，吸引兴趣与培养开放审美能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14313807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313807" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313807-703255-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14313807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313807-703255-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313807" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县平林国小暨民和国小，荣获国教署午餐菜单“午星奖”表扬。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民和国小菜单以学生许愿义式料理为主题，搭配在地时蔬融合大量茄红素、叶黄素、高钙等营养成分，配色鲜艳，让人视觉丰富，菜单使用异国香料及其料理手法，特殊风味蕴含异国料理多元饮食文化，进而提升学生国际观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14313808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313808" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313808-703256-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14313808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313808-703256-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313808" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县平林国小暨民和国小，荣获国教署午餐菜单“午星奖”表扬。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育处长李美华表示，感谢学校营养师与厨妈们的用心付出，透过他们的专业，以选择在地食材入菜及异国料理手法，让学生在校能够享用到营养均衡、美味且丰富的午餐。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14313809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313809" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313809-703257-450x311.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14313809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313809-703257-450x311.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313809" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县平林国小暨民和国小，荣获国教署午餐菜单“午星奖”表扬。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
