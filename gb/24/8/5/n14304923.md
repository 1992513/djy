<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>白石镇海洋节炬光游行隆重回归</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304957-DSC_4734-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2024年8月3日，第73届海洋节炬光游行隆重回归海滨城市白石镇。（宇生/大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月05日讯】（大纪元记者杨清清加拿大温哥华报导）8月2至4日，卑诗省海滨城市白石镇迎来第73届海洋节和Semiahmoo社区日盛大的庆典活动。</p>
<p>暌违5年，最受欢迎的海洋节炬光游行于8月3日晚再度登场。正值盛夏时节和卑诗日长周末，上万名白石镇居民和游客聚集在海滨大道，享受这难得一遇的夏日盛会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14304933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304933" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304933-DSC_4719.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14304933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304933-DSC_4719-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304933" class="wp-caption-text">2024年8月3日，第73届海洋节炬光游行隆重回归海滨城市白石镇。（宇生/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14304935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304935" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304935-DSC_4743.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14304935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304935-DSC_4743-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304935" class="wp-caption-text">2024年8月3日，第73届海洋节炬光游行隆重回归海滨城市白石镇。（宇生/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚8时30分，在晚霞的映衬下游行正式开始。来自不同团体的精湛表演和沿路两旁观众的热情互动，交织成一片欢乐的海洋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14304936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304936" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304936-DSC_4723.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14304936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304936-DSC_4723-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304936" class="wp-caption-text">2024年8月3日，第73届海洋节炬光游行隆重回归海滨城市白石镇。（宇生/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>数十个团体应邀参加了游行，阵容最大的法轮大法团体备受瞩目，人们纷纷举起相机录影拍照，各具特色的花车赢得观众们的阵阵喝采与掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14304937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304937" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304937-DSC_4746.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14304937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304937-DSC_4746-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304937" class="wp-caption-text">2024年8月3日，法轮大法方阵由天国乐团、炼功队和合腰鼓队组成。（宇生/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14304938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14304938" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304938-DSC_4765.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14304938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14304938-DSC_4765-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14304938" class="wp-caption-text">2024年8月3日，法轮大法方阵由天国乐团、炼功队和合腰鼓队组成。（宇生/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
