<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：世上最美的乳品店 店内全是手绘瓷砖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14315215-443450-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534-1080x720-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世界最美丽的普芬德牛奶店（Pfunds Molkerei），文艺复兴风格光彩夺目。（Courtesy of Pfunds Molkerei）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年08月25日讯】（英文大纪元记者Michael Wing报导／孟晓闻编译）德国德累斯顿有条老街，几百年来似乎未曾改变，在一个不起眼的角落，有家世界上最美的牛奶店。1997年，它收入吉尼斯世界纪录，店名叫普芬德牛奶店（Pfunds Molkerei）。</p>
<p>这家牛奶店历经磨难，经过两次世界大战，遭苏联军队占领、关门，甚至几乎摧毁。最终挺了过来，依然光彩夺目，身后故事跌宕起伏。</p>
<p>今天，你步入店内，会为它的美叹为观止，目眩神迷。商店除了优质乳制品闻名外，还有内部装潢。店内墙壁和天花板上覆盖着手绘瓷砖，色彩斑斓，由Villeroy &amp; Boch出品，具有新文艺复兴风格。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530492-PfundsMolkereiFotoThalerTamas2.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530492-PfundsMolkereiFotoThalerTamas2.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:PfundsMolkereiFotoThalerTamas2.jpg">Thaler Tamas</a>/CC BY-SA 4.0)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530498-Pfunds-Molkerei-8.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530498-Pfunds-Molkerei-8.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="416" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图片来自公有领域）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530500-Molkerei-Gebruder-Pfund-5.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530500-Molkerei-Gebruder-Pfund-5.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="272" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Courtesy of Pfunds Molkerei）</figcaption></figure>
<p>瓷砖上画着天使、嬉戏的孩童、花环花圈、松鼠蝴蝶等森林里的动物，还有与商店相关的奶牛在画中四处可见。柜台也交相辉映，饰有黄铜和瓷砖镶板。</p>
<p>这里你可以品尝店里有名的奶酪，美味冰淇淋，或喝上一杯纯正鲜奶。旁边还有一家专卖店，卖香肠、汤和味道浓烈的德国芥末等特产。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>除了表面之美，这家奇幻店铺背后还有着引人入胜的故事。</p>
<p><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter " src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530503-Molkerei-Gebruder-Pfund-4.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="450" /></p>
<p><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter " src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530531-345675643-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="338" /></p>
<p><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter " src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530520-3455643-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="338" /></p>
<p>普芬德家族在1880年德累斯顿工业化时开了这家店, 成为他们企业精神的见证。</p>
<p>城市当时不断发展，创始人保罗?普芬德（Paul Pfund）却发现，那时的牛奶却没达到最基本的卫生标准，牛奶常遭污染、发酸，甚至掺了水。</p>
<p>为改善当地人生活，他开了一家乳制品疗养院，提供优质牛奶，生意逐渐兴隆。随后，他们在外城的鲍茨纳大街上开了牛奶店，就是今天的普芬德牛奶店。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530496-Molkerei-Gebruder-Pfund-1.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530496-Molkerei-Gebruder-Pfund-1.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Courtesy of Pfunds Molkerei）</figcaption></figure>
<p>身为企业家和农场主，保罗?普芬德和他当名演员的兄弟弗里德里希?普芬德（Friedrich Pfund）一起把家族企业发展了起来。</p>
<p>他们增加产品线，扩大业务，扩展配送网络。引入黄油压榨机和奶分离器，生产出浓缩奶，普芬德牛奶店在海外市场也成功起步。由最初每天供应500升牛奶，到1895年已增长至4万升。</p>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530506-34575643-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530506-34575643-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="340" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530524-34565643-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530524-34565643-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="277" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530521-345043-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530521-345043-Pfunds-Molkerei-sfd-345534.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="277" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们不依赖其它企业，自己养殖牲畜，设立了纸箱厂、印刷厂、鞋匠铺、油漆车间，自制员工制服，还有洗衣设施。</p>
<p>公司也开辟了员工福利，自己承担健康和产假保险及员工补贴，在公司工作25年的员工65岁退休后能享有终身福利。</p>
<p>后来厄运出现，特别是二战，几乎将德累斯顿市夷为平地。惊奇的是，普芬德牛奶店在1945年2月的轰炸和大火中奇迹般幸存了下来。</p>
<p>更难以置信的是，主店铺及其新文艺复兴风格的瓷砖竟然“奇迹般毫发无损”，如他们在网站上所写。</p>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530507-Molkerei-Gebruder-Pfund-3.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530507-Molkerei-Gebruder-Pfund-3.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="272" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Courtesy of Pfunds Molkerei）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530508-Pfunds-Molkerei-2.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530508-Pfunds-Molkerei-2.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="377" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Pfunds_Molkerei_01_%28js%29.jpg">Jerzy Strzelecki</a>/CC BY-SA 3.0）</figcaption></figure>
<p>战后，东德落入苏联之手，普芬德牛奶店面对共产专制政权，商店面临国有化。</p>
<p>店主一度顶住了压力，但于1972年遭强制征收，1978年关闭。精美的古典装潢显然与社会主义美学格格不入。</p>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530513-Pfunds-Molkerei-5.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530513-Pfunds-Molkerei-5.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="374" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（公有领域）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530511-Milchladen05-11-final-1.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F15%2Fid5530511-Milchladen05-11-final-1.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Courtesy of Pfunds Molkerei）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于缺乏维护，大部分业务设施被拆除。然而那优雅的营业厅，装饰有神话里的生物、天使和华丽装潢，奇迹般保留了下来。</p>
<p>店主及时联系了历史古迹保护研究所，为店铺争取到了保护令。</p>
<p>最终，随着苏联解体，这家店在1990年重新回到家族手中。虽然不得已将管理权移交给一位德累斯顿商人，但普芬德牛奶店营业至今，已接近150年历史。</p>
<p>令人惊叹的是，只有5%的瓷砖需要更换，其余瓷砖都是原装。仍在运营的Villeroy &amp; Boch公司提供了新瓷砖，和原始装潢完美匹配。</p>
<p>这家乳品企业曾经经营广泛，有各种连锁企业和生产设施，现在已将业务外包给了当地农场。</p>
<p>如今，人们依然可以走入店内，感叹那色彩斑斓的瓷砖装潢，欣赏优雅迷人的文艺复兴风格设计，喝上一大杯纯正鲜奶。</p>
