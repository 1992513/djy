<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《劝世三姊妹》8月25日多伦多台湾文化节登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14315445-00503530ab15a748bf699091392b47f2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月22日讯】<span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">感动无数台湾观众的音乐剧《劝世三姊妹》，即将在</span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">8</span></span></span><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">月</span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">25</span></span></span><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">日于多伦多台湾文化节（TAIWANfest Toronto）</span><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">登场。</span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">《劝世三姊妹：影像特别版》于疫情期间拍摄，8月25日将在加拿大多伦多<span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">湖滨中心（Harbourfront Centre）的</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">Studio Theatre</span></span></span><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">剧场演出。</span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">《劝世三姊妹》以虎尾乡下家庭的三姊弟的家族故事作为出发，讲述大姊宋国珍从小“姊代父职母职”，一手拉拔弟妹长大，养成呛辣不服输的个性。因为家族遗产而回乡的三姊弟，和故乡亲族间发生了一连串令人又哭又笑的故事。</span></p>
<h1 align="left"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;"><b><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/8/21/n14315425.md#1/12-392" rel="attachment wp-att-14315444"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14315444" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14315444-12-450x180.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="240" /></a>《劝世三姊妹》影像特别版</b></span></h1>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">演出时间：</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">8</span></span></span><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">月</span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">25</span></span></span><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">日（周日），下午?</span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">4 点 &#8211; 5 点 30 分</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left">地点：</p>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;"><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">湖滨中心</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC, serif;">Studio Theatre</span></span></span><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">剧场（Harbourfront Centre Studio Theatre, 235 Queens Quay West, Toronto ON M5J 2G8）</span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="font-family: Heiti TC;">获取《劝世三姊妹》与多伦多台湾文化节更多的节目信息，请前往：</span></p>
