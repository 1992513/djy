<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Jimin与桢国入伍前旅行 实境秀收录逗趣点滴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308676-240810062050100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>防弹少年团（BTS）JIMIN与Jung Kook拍摄旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》照。（Disney+提供） 
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年08月10日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）防弹少年团（BTS）最年轻的两位成员Jimin（朴智旻）和Jung Kook（桢国／柾国），在入伍前录制了旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》。想在当兵前与成员一起旅行的Jimin，藉由此机会与Jung Kook前往纽约等地旅行，节目中也收录了两人为对方着想、同时又逗趣的真实互动。</p>
<p>在旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》中，Jimin和Jung Kook在当兵前飞往美国纽约、韩国济州岛、以及日本札幌旅行，节目中没有浮夸的后制特效、也没有精致的运镜或灯光，却为观众纪录下两个大男孩像VLOG一般的超真实日常互动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14308682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14308682" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308682-240810062059100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14308682" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308682-240810062059100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14308682" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（桢国／柾国）拍摄旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》照。（Disney+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一集中可见两人甚至在开拍后，都还没定下作品名称，更没有脚本、不知道自己手持相机拍摄的画面将会如何在全球观众眼前呈现，两人也好好享受了难得的旅行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14308677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14308677" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308677-240810062052100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14308677" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308677-240810062052100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14308677" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）JIMIN与Jung Kook拍摄旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》照。（Disney+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一集中两人前往湖边划独木舟，没想到Jung Kook刚出发就戏剧性的大翻船，让Jimin笑不停，但落水后比起全身湿透的衣服发型，Jung Kook只在乎手机不要坏掉就好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14308678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14308678" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308678-240810062054100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14308678" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308678-240810062054100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14308678" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）Jung Kook拍摄旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》照。（Disney+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14308679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14308679" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308679-240810062047100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14308679" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308679-240810062047100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14308679" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）JIMIN（朴智旻）拍摄旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》照。（Disney+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚上Jung Kook还在睡梦中不小心肘击Jimin的鼻子，让当时已经肠胃炎的Jimin半夜独自坐在冰箱前用饮料冰敷，觉得又无奈又好笑。此外，两人也在逛超市时也遇见粉丝，Jimin表示“我们还没过气，真是太好了！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14308680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14308680" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308680-240810062042100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14308680" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308680-240810062042100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14308680" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）JIMIN（朴智旻）与Jung Kook（桢国／柾国）拍摄旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》照。（Disney+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《Are You Sure ?!》能成功拍摄，Jimin绝对是功臣之一，Jung Kook在节目中感谢Jimin不论两人行程多忙碌，都不断推进节目筹备，“我们要是没做这个企划，大概也不会见面吧”。Jimin坚持想在当兵前一圆与好兄弟去旅行梦想，也因此诞生出笑果百出的《Are You Sure ?!》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14308681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14308681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308681-240810062045100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14308681" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14308681-240810062045100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14308681" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）JIMIN（朴智旻）拍摄旅行实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》照。（Disney+提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Disney+ 旅游实境秀《Are You Sure ?!》本周首播前两集，并将于每周四更新至9月19日完结。</p>
