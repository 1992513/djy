<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>排湾族收获祭！三地门部落文化大联欢</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309127-701627-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>开幕现热闹到让族人感动，大家一起随着音乐舞动。（黄荣群提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月11日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）在屏东三地门11日下午举办收获祭，这活动几乎成了南台湾的嘉年华会，一场排湾族的文化大联欢会！乡公所以“三地门乡收获节暨运动大会─丰收的故事”为题，开幕现热闹到让族人感动，大家一起随着音乐舞动。原住民族委员会的曾智勇主委也特地回乡，与族人一起参加这个满怀喜悦的活动，吸引许多人来参与，认识和了解原住民的独特文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14309130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14309130" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309130-701630-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14309130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309130-701630-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14309130" class="wp-caption-text">三地门乡收获节暨运动大会─丰收的故事。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开幕仪式在地磨儿国小的操场上展开，下午三点一到，传统的排湾族大会舞，便拉开了序幕，族人们手牵手，随着音乐步伐，跳出丰收的喜悦。接着，各式各样的传统乐舞、古谣表演纷纷上演，场面壮观又充满热情。从点燃圣火到运动员宣誓，每一个环节，都蕴含着排湾族深厚的文化底蕴，让人感受到文化传承的使命感，连运动员宣誓都全程用族语，让在场观众直呼“好强大”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14309124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14309124" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309124-701624-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14309124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309124-701624-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14309124" class="wp-caption-text">三地门乡联合收获祭活动。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了这些精彩的表演，现场还设有热闹的市集摊位，让大家在欢庆丰收的同时，也能逛逛小摊，买点原民住文创小物，把这份喜悦带回家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14309128" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14309128" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309128-701628-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14309128" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309128-701628-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14309128" class="wp-caption-text">开幕仪式在地磨儿国小的操场上展开。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曾智勇主委感动地说：“原住民文化不仅是台湾珍贵的资产，也是全球少见的文化瑰宝。”他表示，没有原住民文化，台湾的文化就不完整，这次他亲自率领原民会高层出席，便是为了展现对文化推广的重视。三地门乡长曾有钦也感慨万分，他说，每一颗金黄的稻穗，都是族人辛勤耕耘的成果，这一天不论族人身在何处，大家的心都连在一起，分享这丰收的故事与无尽的喜悦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14309131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14309131" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309131-701631-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14309131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309131-701631-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14309131" class="wp-caption-text">部落族人高举小米，开心庆丰收。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，曾智勇这位三地门之光还收到了特别的奖状，让整场活动的气氛更加热烈。这次的丰收节，不仅是一场文化盛宴，更是族人心灵的连结，让人深刻体会到什么叫做“文化薪火相传”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14309126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14309126" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309126-701626-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14309126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14309126-701626-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14309126" class="wp-caption-text">每一个环节，都蕴含着排湾族深厚的文化底蕴。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
