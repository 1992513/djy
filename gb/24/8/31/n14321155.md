<h6>2024 Volkswagen Atlas Peak Edition SEL。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月31日讯】Volkswagen在北美的最高版本SUV的Atlas，是拥有三排座位，在2024年Volkswagen为它进行了中期改款，主要是前后作出了少改变，以及内部也更新了，并且加入了更粗犷的Peak Edition版本，也是本周测试的车辆。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://di88ceki78g29.cloudfront.net/Z6e&#29983/embed/MMenhVRP_7o?si=KGIgQKU6z5filJZt" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>现在的Volkswagen Atlas车长逾5米（近200吋），体型巨大，设计来自2013年展出的概念车Volkswagen CrossBlue，Atlas的方正外形，极具力量感。2024年Altas刚经过中期升级，前脸及尾闸设计更新颖，进风口栏栅加大了少许，并以黑色代替了镀铬处理，巨大Volkswagen厂徽在头灯亮起时也会有灯光。Atlas的头灯组变得更为巨大，有“C”的日间行车灯围着。尾闸两边后灯以红色反光塑胶板“连”起来，看起来更时尚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321156" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321156-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321156-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_03-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321156" class="wp-caption-text">2024 Volkswagen Atlas Peak Edition SEL。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试车是Peak Edition版，车身采用“Avocado Green Metallic”深绿色，配以深灰及浅灰双色内装，地毯也是以防水塑胶制造。前脸设计与其他Atlas车型不同，银色粗大的前防撞器，并加上与Atlas Sport相同的亮黑色“X”饰条，带背光的前厂徽在黑色格栅上份外显眼。沿着Peak Edition侧面，亮黑色徽章装饰连接着车门及前轮拱，徽章上有橙色的“Peak Edition”标志。车轮采用18吋全亮黑轮圈，并使用255/60R18越野轮胎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321157" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321157-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_07.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321157-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_07-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321157" class="wp-caption-text">厂徽在头灯亮起时也会有灯光。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在Facelift的同时，引擎也有改变，以往有两种选择，现在只剩下2升直四涡轮增压引擎，3.6升V6自然吸气引擎已经退役。EA288的2升涡轮引擎具有269匹马力及273磅呎扭矩，比以往为高，而且更为接近旧有V6引擎的276匹马力及266磅呎扭矩。搭配8速自动变速箱，附+/-手动变档。测试车配备4Motion四驱系统，预设了雪地、公路、越野及自订越野四种行车设定，此外，Atlas也有多种行车模式，分为一般，省油及运动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321158" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321158-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321158-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_19-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321158" class="wp-caption-text">2升直四涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Atlas的另一个特点，是它拥有全虚拟仪表板，即仪表板是一个彩屏，尺寸是10.25吋，再以电脑控制输出影像，由于技术已经成熟，画面中的车速及引擎转速，基本上没有什么迟缓。最特别是它可以在仪表板上显示地图，在使用卫星导航时，便不用分心转头看中控台了，当然，如果嫌仪表板地图太小的话，也可将地图“转移”回中控台萤幕，地图是可以随时“转移”的。测试车也装有Head-Up Display抬头显示器，可显着重要资讯，例如车速，驾驶时不用分心看仪表板。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321159" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321159-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321159-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_20-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321159" class="wp-caption-text">全虚拟仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Atlas装有的第三排座椅，可以真正予成人乘坐。中排座椅有两种选择，有三座位的bench座椅，又或是两张独立的captain座椅，前者可以用尽空间，载最多乘客（七人）；后者虽然只能载六人，但是中排座椅舒适，后排出入也更方便。今年测试车是是装有bench座椅，就是7座位的车款。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321161" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321161-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_21.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321161-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_21-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321161" class="wp-caption-text">12吋中控台萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>汽车是有三区恒温系统，前座并有加热座椅与降温功能，而且具有10方向电动调节，方向盘也有加热功能。虽然中排及后排是同一区恒温，但也各有送风口。中排座位也设有加热功能，座位角度可调节，也可前后作少许移动，当然全是手动式。后排座位靠背也可改变角度，而且折叠时与行李舱底板成一平面，载货搬出搬入时更为方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321162" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321162-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_23.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321162-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_23-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321162" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆改为定位底。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了更省油（耗油数据更好），现今大多数汽车设有“自动引擎熄火”功能，Atlas也不例外，在短暂停车（如红灯时）便会关掉引擎，减少耗油。当然也有解除此功能的接键，但是此功能在中控台萤幕，如萤幕显示其他东西时便用不了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321163" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321163-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321163-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_27-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321163" class="wp-caption-text">前排座位舒适。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台上半部分装有一个12吋萤幕，比旧有的8吋增大了50%，运行MIB3资讯系统，可用作导航、音响、汽车设定等，倒车时也可显示后方影像。萤幕下方只有少量按键，都是用作恒温系统的。由中控告伸延下去，就是引擎启动键及小型变速杆。由于变速杆改为定位底，前推是R档，往后拉是D档，推或拉都会弹回中间，P档键在变速杆前边，与引擎启动键太接近，有好几次也按错了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321169" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321169-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_28.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321169-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_28-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321169" class="wp-caption-text">整个前台设计简洁。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Atlas Peak Edition虽是挂着越野的外表，确实也装上了4MOTION四驱系统，但只配2升引擎，马力也只有269匹马力，在轻度越野时是绝对可应付，但要应付困难的地型就不太现实了，作为日常用车Atlas是非常好用！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321170" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321170-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14321170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14321170-2024_VW_Atlas_Peak_Edition_10-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321170" class="wp-caption-text">255/60R18越野轮胎。 （李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
