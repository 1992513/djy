<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国中小开学日 全面启用智慧校园平台暨数位学生证</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320867-705679-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>智慧校园亲师生平台及数位学生证启用仪式（苗县府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）国中小113年第1学期8月30日开学，县长钟东锦伉俪在头份市蟠桃国小化身导护志工协助小朋友安全通过路口，与学校教师共同携手维护学童安全，并带领小一新生通过“聪勤智慧门”期望他们开创美好的学习旅程，同时全面启用“智慧校园亲师生平台”及“数位学生证”，让学童在智慧安全的校园环境中快乐学习与成长。</p>
<p>在AI资讯化的浪潮下，县府为因应国际智慧城市愿景，从112(2023)年开始推动全县国中小学建置“智慧校园亲师生平台”及“数位学生证”计划，以打造智慧校园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320876-705688.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320876-705688-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320876" class="wp-caption-text">县长伉俪带领新生通过聪勤智慧门.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“数位学生证”结合了学生证、书香苗栗卡及悠游卡的功能，学童到校后，在校内差勤卡机靠卡，透过整合式APP发送“讯息通知”，以数位签到方式让家长及老师即时掌握“学生出缺席”状况，强化学童到校就学的安全性，有效达成一卡在手“交通到学安全双重保护”的整合应用功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320875-705687.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320875-705687-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320875" class="wp-caption-text">县长赠送小一新生小桃子提袋及绘本.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14320874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320874" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320874-705686.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320874-705686-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320874" class="wp-caption-text">县长夫人赠送新生牙刷、牙膏组礼物（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，学校教师及家长可透过手机或平板下载亲师生平台《苗栗e校园APP》，家长透过行动装置可以帮孩子线上请假，藉由APP线上查询成绩了解孩子学习状况，也可以利用“我的图书馆”关心孩子的阅读习惯，老师透过“电子联络簿”及“公告推播”与家长进行即时讯息远达，另外，APP具有“IPoint”数位积点功能，学校可透过奖励及活动的设定，以线上化方式分配点数，激励学生积极参与各式活动，创造校园特色，建构亲师沟通网络，营造亲师生智慧校园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320873" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320873-705685.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320873-705685-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320873" class="wp-caption-text">县长伉俪展示数位学生证（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14320868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320868-705680.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320868-705680-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320868" class="wp-caption-text">用数位学生证刷卡签到.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在今天开学的第1天，县长钟东锦与夫人陈美琦连袂至头份市蟠桃国小化身导护志工，协助小朋友安全通过路口，与老师共同维护学童的安全，他俩且穿着学生制服展示数位学生证，并使用数位学生证快速完成到校刷卡，体验即时通知学童安全到校的功能，同时与副议长张淑芬、县议员徐功凡、曾玟学、萧咏萱、头份市长罗雪珠、市代会主席温宜静、副主席张家荣、校长沈羿成等人共同启用“智慧校园亲师生平台”及“数位学生证”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320871" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320871-705683.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320871-705683-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320871" class="wp-caption-text">县长夫人协助小朋友安全通过马路（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14320870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320870-705682.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320870-705682-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320870" class="wp-caption-text">县长担任导护志工维护学童安全.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14320869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320869-705681.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320869-705681-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320869" class="wp-caption-text">县长伉俪化身导护志工守护学生安全（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦并宣布，为保障全县师生及导护志工的人身安全，教育处每年购足学校交通安全装备，并且全额补助导护志工保险费；为了感谢交通服务队值勤人员辛劳，113学年度起将导护老师每周值勤补休时数由半天调增为一天，另外针对导护志工的部分，补助每日值勤餐费50元。希望借此感谢所有导护人员的辛劳，并号召更多交通导护志工投入维护学生交通安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320872" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320872-705684.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320872-705684-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320872" class="wp-caption-text">县长伉俪欢迎小一新生到校.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另为鼓励从小培养阅读兴趣与习惯，县长钟东锦特别在蟠桃国小活动中心3楼为小一新生讲解绘本，并赠送每位新生“小桃子布布高升提袋”及绘本，作为小一新生们进入国小学习阶段满满的期待与祝福的礼物，县长人陈美琦也为新生准备了精美牙刷、牙膏组入学好礼，为孩童们的崭新校园生活打气，县长伉俪与校长并一同带领新生通过“聪勤智慧门”，期盼他们在新学期中能够聪明勤奋，从阅读中汲取知识，开创美好的学习旅程。</p>
