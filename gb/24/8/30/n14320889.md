<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宣信迎小一新生 以生命斗士“珣珣”做榜样</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320890-705693-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中年级爵士鼓全国第一的陈芷安小朋友在迎新活动上表演。（嘉义市立宣信国小提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）宣信国小8月29日早上特别办理小一迎新暨庆祝祖父节活动，包含:“喜颜展臂迎新生”、“铜乐欢庆具贤才”、“童颜美声天上来”、“文昌降福学当先”等多元丰富的活动，透过仪式来欢迎小一新生进入宣信的大家庭。</p>
<p>“喜颜展臂迎新生”活动包含赠送新生“入学礼”，更安排特别的生命学习，是由今年刚获得总统教育奖的生命小斗士“珣珣”，将部分奖金透过母亲捐赠给学校，成为小小学弟妹生命教育的最佳学习典范。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320891-705694.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320891-705694-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320891" class="wp-caption-text">生命小斗士“珣珣”由妈妈代表将总统教育奖部分奖金捐赠给母校。（嘉义市立宣信国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14320892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320892" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320892-705695.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320892-705695-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320892" class="wp-caption-text">迎新点文昌活动。（嘉义市立宣信国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>透过“珣珣”的妈妈说明，让小一新生认识“珣珣”是因车祸而颈椎受创、四肢瘫痪，但没有因此失志，虽每日背着沉重维生设备却勤学不懈，2022年在宣信国小毕业典礼上获颁校长奖，嘉义市长黄敏惠也特地到场送上祝福。这次捐赠仪式是感谢黄市长的关心及鼓励她读书之外，也特别感谢宣信国小团队和老师们将“珣珣”当成自己的孩子一样照顾，希望学弟妹也能学习不怕困难，勇敢接受所有的挑战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320893-705696.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320893-705696-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320893" class="wp-caption-text">迎新赠送小宝贝见面礼。（嘉义市立宣信国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“铜乐欢庆具贤才”活动是由荣获2024福尔摩沙音乐大赛中年级爵士鼓第一名的陈芷安小朋友精湛演出，而“童颜美声天上来”则呈现宣信那音合唱团唱出日、客、闽等不同语言的优美歌声，这些活动都是希望透过音乐的呈现，展现对小一迎新的热情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320894-705697.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320894-705697-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320894" class="wp-caption-text">宣信那音合唱团在迎新活动中演唱。（嘉义市立宣信国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“文昌降福学当先”活动是由谢圣雅校长、家长会张原瑄会长和杜孟宗副会长、陈冠名和殷大为里长等五人，以点文昌的文化仪式，期待每个新生都能开启智慧、勤学向上、认真读书。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320895-705698.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320895-705698-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320895" class="wp-caption-text">宣信家长会张原瑄会长致词。（嘉义市立宣信国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谢圣雅校长表示，宣信国小南田古道特色课程在去年荣获“台湾永续教育奖”殊荣，今年更发展成生命和永续目标的学习，感谢市府教育处对学校在永续教育的重视和对特教生的支持与协助，让“珣珣”等人在学校有完善的无障碍设施及友善的照顾，因此能在人生的道路上展现了令人感动的奋斗与坚持。透过“珣珣”的故事，成为小一新生学习有意义的教育价值。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320896-705699.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320896-705699-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320896" class="wp-caption-text">张原瑄会长代表赠送小一新生绘本。（嘉义市立宣信国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14320897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320897" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320897-705700.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14320897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320897-705700-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320897" class="wp-caption-text">欢迎小一新生进场。（嘉义市立宣信国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
