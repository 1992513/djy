<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夏日红龙果好味道 消暑解渴风味佳</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320006-705289-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>十分讨喜的圆滚滚红龙果。（农粮署东区分署提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月29日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）红龙果外表亮丽艳红，冰镇食用十分清凉消暑，营养丰富且热量低，目前正鲜采上市。农粮署东区分署辅导台东县长滨乡农会于8月29日至30日，在新北市板桥区农会活力超市前广场举办展售活动，可现场品尝新鲜美味红龙果及采购各种东部特色农产品，亦可预购宅配到府，省时又省力，欢迎消费者前往选购。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320007" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320007-705290.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14320007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320007-705290.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="804" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320007" class="wp-caption-text">红龙果外表艳丽讨喜，营养丰富热量低。（农粮署东区分署提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14320004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320004" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320004-705287.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14320004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320004-705287.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320004" class="wp-caption-text">黄龙果为近年推广新品种，非外皮染色。（农粮署东区分署提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内涵丰富热量低，鲜食打汁两相宜。东区分署表示，红龙果主要的成分包括蛋白质、维生素及矿物质等，大量的膳食纤维亦可促进消化。除了鲜食，果肉亦可加鲜奶、蜂蜜或其它蔬果一同打成果汁，冰凉后饮用为消暑圣品。红龙果品种众多，除传统白肉、红肉之红龙果外，近年亦由农会配合辅导种植双色果肉品种及黄龙果，皆有其独特之风味，消费者在挑选时可选择果皮颜色亮丽无皱缩，鳞片及果梗切口新鲜者为佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14320005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320005" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320005-705288.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14320005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320005-705288.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320005" class="wp-caption-text">白肉及红肉红龙果的剖面。（农粮署东区分署提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14320008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14320008" style="width: 589px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320008-705291.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14320008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14320008-705291.jpg" alt="" width="589" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14320008" class="wp-caption-text">双色红龙果。（农粮署东区分署提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>东区分署表示，此次辅导台东县长滨乡农会于都会区办理展售活动，现场除以多样品种之红龙果鲜果展售外，亦安排料理教学及DIY活动增进买气，消费者可就近前往新北市板桥区农会活力超市前广场购买，让夏日更增添清凉。◇</p>
