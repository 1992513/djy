<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>FNC娱乐家族演唱会 四组男团明年桃园开唱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311104-240814072609100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>FNC娱乐旗下四大乐团CNBLUE、FTISLAND、N.Flying与Hi-Fi Un!corn将共同参与FNC娱乐家族演唱会“FNC BAND KINGDOM”。（SHOW Office提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年08月14日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）集结韩国FNC娱乐旗下四大男团CNBLUE、FTISLAND、N.Flying与Hi-Fi Un!corn的家族演唱会“FNC BAND KINGDOM”，确定2025年1月4日与5日两天在桃园市立综合体育馆（桃园巨蛋）举行。</p>
<p>“FNC BAND KINGDOM”集结了CNBLUE成员郑容和、姜敏赫与李正信、FTISLAND成员李洪基、李在真与崔敏焕、N.Flying成员李承协、车勋、柳会胜、徐东成，以及刚出道便受到高度关注的阳光男孩团体Hi-Fi Un!corn成员金贤率、孙基允、严太民、福嶌崇人与许民，四组人将带来超过四小时的演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14311105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311105-240814072225100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14311105" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311105-240814072225100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311105" class="wp-caption-text">FNC娱乐旗下乐团CNBLUE资料照。（SHOW Office提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14311106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311106-240814072222100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14311106" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311106-240814072222100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311106" class="wp-caption-text">FNC娱乐旗下乐团FTISLAND资料照。（SHOW Office提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>聊到粉丝最期待的歌单与舞台部分，CNBLUE、FTISLAND、N.Flying与Hi-Fi Un!corn不约而同保证，当天将使出浑身解数唱好、唱满，把超过四小时的舞台完美呈现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14311107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311107" style="width: 462px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311107-240814072219100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14311107" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311107-240814072219100707.jpg" alt="" width="462" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311107" class="wp-caption-text">FNC娱乐旗下乐团N.Flying资料照。（SHOW Office提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>难得以“大家庭”形式召开演唱会，四组“爱讲话男团”的TALKING秀也令粉丝期待，他们也有机会带来跨团的特别合作舞台演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14311108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311108" style="width: 593px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311108-240814072217100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14311108" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311108-240814072217100707.jpg" alt="" width="593" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311108" class="wp-caption-text">FNC娱乐旗下乐团Hi-Fi Un!corn资料照。（SHOW Office提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这将是CNBLUE、FTISLAND、N.Flying与Hi-Fi Un!corn首次以FNC旗下艺人齐聚的“家族形式”共同访台，2025年1月4日与5日他们将在桃园小巨蛋举办“FNC BAND KINGDOM”家族演唱会，详情可上SHOW OFFICE ENTERTAINMENT.CO.LTD官方粉丝团查询。</p>
