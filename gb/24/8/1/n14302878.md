<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：超暖心 3岁可爱宝宝照顾小鸭子</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14302886-ksenia_belanger-1080x720-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>可爱的阿里阿德内?贝朗格（Ariadne Belanger）像鸭妈妈一样照顾小鸭子。（@ksenia_belanger提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年08月07日讯】（Deborah George报导／大纪元记者艾琛编译）三岁的阿里阿德内?贝朗格（Ariadne Belanger）对小鸭子的关爱无人能及。尽管年纪尚小，但这个蹒跚学步的孩子对小鸭子非常亲昵，甚至把自己当成了鸭妈妈。</p>
<figure style="width: 479px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F01%2Fid5678136-Ksenia-Belanger-baby1.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F01%2Fid5678136-Ksenia-Belanger-baby1.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="479" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（@ksenia_belanger提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>42岁的母亲克塞尼娅?贝朗格（Ksenia Belanger）告诉《大纪元时报》，“阿里阿德内从小就对小鸭子们很温柔，她会把它们带到屋子里来玩，甚至偷偷把它们抱到自己的床上和它们一起睡午觉！”</p>
<p>阿里阿德内最喜欢的活动是在小浴盆或水槽里给小鸭子们洗澡，然后用毛巾把它们擦干，同时用她自己的婴语哼着甜美的曲调。</p>
<figure style="width: 481px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F01%2Fid5678135-Ksenia-Belanger-baby0.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F01%2Fid5678135-Ksenia-Belanger-baby0.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="481" b="602" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（@ksenia_belanger提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>《这些可爱的时刻》作品的灵感来源</h2>
<p>贝朗格太太来自俄罗斯，大约二十年前搬到北卡罗来纳州，在那里遇到了正在服兵役的丈夫埃罗尔?贝朗格（Errol Belanger）。之后，他们育有两子一女：11岁的布鲁斯（Bruce）、9岁的安德烈亚斯（Andreas）和3岁的阿里阿德内。</p>
<figure style="width: 433px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F01%2Fid5678114-apples-blossoms-24-6.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F01%2Fid5678114-apples-blossoms-24-6.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="433" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（@ksenia_belanger提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布鲁斯出生时，贝朗格太太希望记录下他成长过程中的每一个珍贵时刻和里程碑，因此开始学习摄影。当阿里阿德内出生时，家里有了一个后院农场，农场里有小兔子和小鸡。布鲁斯和安德烈亚斯尽情享受着农场的乐趣，因为他们喜欢看着小鸡孵化和养育它们。随着阿里阿德内的长大，事实证明，她也不例外。</p>
<p>贝朗格太太说，“我女儿从小就喜欢我们后院的每一个小动物，她尤其喜欢小鸭子，这一点也不奇怪。看着她和小鸭子一起玩耍，以及在玩耍过程中感受到的无尽快乐，激发了我创作小鸭子系列作品的灵感。”</p>
<figure style="width: 414px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680195-Ksenia-and-daughter0.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680195-Ksenia-and-daughter0.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="414" b="736" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（@ksenia_belanger提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>抚养阿里阿德内启发了这位尽职尽责的母亲的怀旧感，她与女儿之间充满惊奇的经历让她想起了养育儿子的情景。</p>
<p>这位三个孩子的母亲称，“当阿里阿德内与小鸭子互动时，我试图抓拍她的原始情感和惊奇。”</p>
<p>贝朗格太太还对小鸭子们饶有兴趣和亲切地注视着小女孩的样子非常着迷。她透露说，“有一次，阿里阿德内和小鸭子们在床上玩耍，阿里阿德内在指示它们不要跳，不要靠近她。它们似乎在听她说话，它们知道她非常关心它们，都把阿里阿德内当成了妈妈。”</p>
<figure style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680196-Ksenia-and-daughter1.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680196-Ksenia-and-daughter1.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="399" b="709" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（@ksenia_belanger提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>贝朗格太太使用索尼Alpha 7R IV相机和配以一把白色反光伞的Profoto B10闪光灯。</p>
<p>她分享了自己的拍摄技巧，“我将闪光灯设置在窗户等自然光源附近，以突出太阳光线，同时在需要时光线可以互补。”</p>
<p>贝朗格太太在努力抓拍与女儿在一起的可爱瞬间时，还学会了荷兰大师对光影的运用。</p>
<figure style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680197-Ksenia-and-daughter2.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680197-Ksenia-and-daughter2.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="399" b="710" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（@ksenia_belanger提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在典型的摄影过程中，阿里阿德内帮助安排小细节，而她的母亲则负责准备器材。</p>
<p>贝朗格太太说，“我总是让阿里阿德内帮忙，让她尽情发挥创意。”</p>
<p>有时，贝朗格太太会引导女儿向窗边移动，以获得更好的光线。有时，她会顺着女儿的自然行为进行拍摄。</p>
<figure style="width: 379px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680198-Ksenia-and-daughter3.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680198-Ksenia-and-daughter3.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="379" b="670" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（@ksenia_belanger提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>贝朗格太太曾是一名法语教师，拥有语言学学位，她的粉丝超过90万，大家都很喜欢她女儿对小鸭子细微照顾的视频。</p>
<p>一位网民写道，“她长大成人后会留下多么美好的回忆，我真的很激动。”</p>
<p>另一位网友写道，“看到她的爱、她的快乐和她的温柔，真是令人感动。”</p>
<figure style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680199-Ksenia-and-daughter4.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F07%2F04%2Fid5680199-Ksenia-and-daughter4.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="400" b="706" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（@ksenia_belanger提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于自己的照片所获得的热烈反响，贝朗格太太表示，“我觉得我的观众喜欢看到这些可爱的瞬间，这让我们想起了我们小时候，想起了对动物宝宝的无条件关爱。没有什么比这更美妙的了。”</p>
<p>贝朗格太太有幸亲眼目睹了这些珍贵时刻，她对其中一些场景尤为珍视。她说，“我觉得我最喜欢的时刻是我女儿把一顶小花帽戴在小鸭子头上，然后给小鸭一个吻的那一瞬间。”</p>
