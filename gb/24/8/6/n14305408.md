<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珍惜生命｜男警疑不堪工作压力 吞枪自杀</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14305442-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一名男子今早在荃湾木棉下村吞枪，昏迷送院抢救后不治，消息指男子为一名警员。警方封锁附近一带调查。（唐健丰／大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年08月06日讯】（大纪元记者麦碧香港报导）一名男子今早在荃湾木棉下村吞枪，昏迷送院抢救，最终证实不治。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14305443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14305443" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14305443-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14305443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14305443-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14305443" class="wp-caption-text">警方封锁现场调查。（唐健丰／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>（5日）上午11时44分左右，有途人发现一名男子倒卧在荃湾木棉下村一草地位置，其身旁有一支手枪，遂报案。人员到场，随即将伤者送往仁济医院抢救，最终伤重不治。</p>
<p>据了解，死者为一名男警，有留下遗书，疑因工作压力问题轻生，警方就事件展开调查。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14305444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14305444" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14305444-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14305444" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14305444-6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14305444" class="wp-caption-text">多名便衣警员在仁济医院内调查。（梁智宁／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14305445" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14305445" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14305445-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14305445" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14305445-7-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14305445" class="wp-caption-text">鉴证科人员抵达仁济医院。（梁智宁／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
