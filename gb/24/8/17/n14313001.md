<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“原力崛起 竞技玛家” 玛家乡收获祭暨运动会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313002-702853-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>玛家乡收获祭暨运动会，热闹登场！（周永田提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）屏东县玛家乡的部落们，他们一年一度的盛大活动——收获祭暨全乡运动会！活动于8月15日至17日在屏东县立北叶国小举行。这场活动可是玛家乡一年中最热闹的时刻，不仅是族人们一起感谢上天赐予的丰收与平安，大家回家团聚、共度美好时光的机会，虽然下雨还是浇不息族人的热情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14313008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313008-702861-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14313008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313008-702861-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313008" class="wp-caption-text">虽然下雨还是浇不息族人的热情。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14313009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313009-702862-600x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14313009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313009-702862-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313009" class="wp-caption-text">族人们一起感谢上天赐予的丰收与平安。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的主题是“原力崛起 竞技玛家”，主题让人感觉到满满的能量！不论是田径赛、球类比赛还是原住民传统竞技，每一个项目都充满了挑战与乐趣。特别是原住民传统竞技赛，如射箭、路跑、负重、摔角、拔河、锯木和抓山猪，这些可都是对体能和传统智慧的双重考验呢！而且，每个部落还会带着自家的特产来到现场，山猪、槟榔、香蕉、芒果…通通都有，让大家一饱口福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14313005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313005-702856-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14313005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313005-702856-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313005" class="wp-caption-text">今年的主题是“原力崛起 竞技玛家”，主题让人感觉到满满的能量！（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14313012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313012-702866-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14313012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313012-702866-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313012" class="wp-caption-text">族人一起传唱古谣，庆丰收。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14313004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313004-702855-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14313004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313004-702855-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313004" class="wp-caption-text">每个部落还会带着自家的特产来到现场，山猪、槟榔、香蕉、芒果…通通都有。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>除了竞技，趣味竞赛也不能错过！滚铁圈、竹筒取水、槟榔拉车、滚轮胎和袋鼠跳，这些项目让参赛者和观众开心不已，欢乐无比。而且，玛潮舞大赛也非常震撼，个个舞林高手，无论是团体组还是个人创意组，大家都使出浑身解数来展示他们的才艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14313006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313006-702859-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14313006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313006-702859-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313006" class="wp-caption-text">除了竞技，趣味竞赛也不能错过！（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次活动可不仅仅是比赛，还是部落之间凝聚力的最佳展现。大家一起跳舞、比赛、品尝美食，族人相聚在一起，共度这场融合了传统与现代、力量与趣味的玛家乡年度盛会！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14313010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14313010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313010-702863-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14313010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14313010-702863-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14313010" class="wp-caption-text">部落之间凝聚力的最佳展现。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
