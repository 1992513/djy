<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>关山阿美族丰年祭 舞蹈、撒鱼网热闹爆表！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312939-702844-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>关山镇镇长彭成丰参加丰年祭活动。（Calvin Liu提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）17日上午台东县关山镇热闹翻天！阿美族的联合丰年祭在亲水公园举行，不仅有传统的锯木头、撒鱼网活动，还加入了全新的滚轮胎比赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14312942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14312942" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312942-702847-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14312942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312942-702847-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14312942" class="wp-caption-text">关山镇阿美族群的联合丰年祭，在亲水公园举行。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场盛会由关山镇的5个部落共同举办，每两年一次，今年轮到电光部落来担当主角。近千名族人身穿艳丽的传统服饰，齐聚在亲水公园的原住民文化广场上，场面壮观！张万生总头目带着8名精壮的青年进场，与其他部落头目一同进行祈福仪式，感受满满的祝福力量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14312945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14312945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312945-702850-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14312945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312945-702850-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14312945" class="wp-caption-text">张万生总头目带着8名精壮的青年进场，与其他部落头目一同进行祈福仪式。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动现场还吸引了不少贵宾，包括原民会副主委杜张梅庄、国民党立委黄仁以及台东县长饶庆铃等都特地来共襄盛举，就连屏东县东港镇公所也组团来观摩学习！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14312940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14312940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312940-702845-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14312940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312940-702845-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14312940" class="wp-caption-text">活动现场还吸引了不少贵宾，包括原民会副主委杜张梅庄、国民党立委黄仁，都特地来共襄盛举。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饶庆铃县长在致词时更是大力赞扬了阿美族的文化特色，表示县府非常重视这些独特的族群文化传承，特别成立了全台最多的文健站，让长者们可以在这里开心健康地度过晚年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14312947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14312947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312947-702852-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14312947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312947-702852-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14312947" class="wp-caption-text">饶庆铃县长在致词时更是大力赞扬了阿美族的文化特色。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当千人齐舞后，大家最期待的抓猪竞技也正式登场！虽然有动保团体抗议过，因为这是传统狩猎活动，族人们认为这是保留文化的一部分，抓猪的过程就像是重温祖先的生活方式，既刺激又具意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14312946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14312946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312946-702851-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14312946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312946-702851-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14312946" class="wp-caption-text">抓猪的过程重温祖先的生活方式，既刺激又具意义。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了抓猪大战，还有锯木头、负重、撒鱼网等多项竞赛，甚至还加入了创新的滚轮胎比赛，每个比赛项目都列入计分，最后获胜者还能拿到新台币1万6000元的奖金呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14312941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14312941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312941-702846-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14312941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312941-702846-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14312941" class="wp-caption-text">活动首度加滚轮胎比赛。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>穿着传统服饰的阿美族族人，让整个活动充满了色彩和欢乐，现场气氛嗨到爆，让人忍不住跟着他们一起跳舞、唱歌，玩得不亦乐乎！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14312944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14312944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312944-702849-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14312944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14312944-702849-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14312944" class="wp-caption-text">穿着传统服饰的阿美族族人，让整个活动充满了色彩和欢乐。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
