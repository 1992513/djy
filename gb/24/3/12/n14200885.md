<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北美最大海产展迎人潮 参展商表乐观</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200857-DJY05162-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>3月10日至12日的波士顿北美海产展有来自世界各地的1200多家厂商参与。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月13日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨、章佩儿波士顿报导）“你看这些乌贼，这些美国红鲷鱼，这是很漂亮的鱼。”在今年的波士顿北美海产展中，一名参展商热情地为人们介绍新鲜渔获。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">3月10日至12日的海产展给海鲜爱好者们带来了喜讯，尽管面临着后疫情时期的挑战，但海产商们对市场前景表达了乐观预期。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14200856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200856" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200856-DJY05163.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200856-DJY05163-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200856" class="wp-caption-text">3月10日至12日的波士顿北美海产展有来自世界各地的1200多家厂商参与。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200884-51001f0276065fd4f8f5395fd3a27498.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200884-51001f0276065fd4f8f5395fd3a27498-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200884" class="wp-caption-text">3月10日至12日的波士顿北美海产展有来自世界各地的1200多家厂商参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这个为期3天的北美最大型海产展在波士顿会展中心（Boston Convention and Exhibition Center）举办。主办方表示，今年的展览场地总共有近25万平方英尺，比去年增加了5%。今年有来自49个国家的1210家厂商参与展览，展出产品包括新鲜水产、加工水产食品、食品调料、水产处理机器等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">波士顿北美海产展今年已来到第42届。主办方系统显示，今年中国大陆有175家厂商登记参展，香港也有2家厂商前来参展。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“台湾水产工业同业公会”表示，今年聚集了11家台湾优质厂商，组成台湾馆参展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14200879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200879-7839d0521046eb2b9c76bcb08ed1e856.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200879-7839d0521046eb2b9c76bcb08ed1e856-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200879" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展中的台湾水产厂商。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200881" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200881-cd0623c5927da8b68a2112d640ba2e38.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200881-cd0623c5927da8b68a2112d640ba2e38-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200881" class="wp-caption-text">各大参展厂商吸引人们关注。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200880-9d0e1e9f34c894437656dca047469da8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200880-9d0e1e9f34c894437656dca047469da8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200880" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展的中国水产厂商。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“全球海鲜联盟”（Global Seafood Alliance）资深副主席哈特（Steve Hart）说，目前海产厂商面临的最大挑战，仍然是疫情时期的遗留问题。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“由于供应链的中断，我们看到很多生产过剩的海产，所以现在还有很多海产储存在冷冻库里。即便到了今天，疫情已经过去几年了，许多海产还是储存着。”他说。哈特还说，对于海产商家而言，困难是如何在确保盈利的同时将产品送到消费者手中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14200859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200859-DJY05156.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200859-DJY05156-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200859" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展中的新鲜鱼类。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200860-DJY05153.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200860-DJY05153-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200860" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展中的新鲜鱼类和龙虾。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200861-DJY05152.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200861-DJY05152-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200861" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展中的新鲜水产。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200869-DJY05110.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200869-DJY05110-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200869" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展中展出的大龙虾。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">参展商之一、经营食品调料的“汉堡弗兰克食品公司”（Humble and Frank Food）表示，他们希望通过展会来扩展本地联系。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“也要找到人们的新口味，可能有南美风味的潮流，或者有些融合的风味以及亚洲的风味。所以我们会尝试，会保持美味，但也要与时俱进。”该公司总裁普雷兹（Megan Du Preez）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14200870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200870-DJY05098.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200870-DJY05098-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200870" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展中的新鲜龙虾和鱼肉。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200871" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200871-DJY05094.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200871-DJY05094-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200871" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展中的加工水产。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200872" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200872-DJY05093.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200872-DJY05093-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200872" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿北美海产展也有水产烹饪展示。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">另一家参展商、出售加工鲑鱼产品的“MOWI”公司对市场前景表示乐观，并且正在推出新的口味，以吸引更多顾客。该公司资深市场主管杜梅姆（Diana Dumetm）说，鲑鱼仍然是驱动水产行业的最重要产品之一，但现在的顾客对产品有着更高的期待。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“他们正提出更多的要求，这是个好现象。他们要求更高的品质，更严格的标准，更多的产品种类。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14200882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200882-ed3902daa57ebaeb89ec58cc3db404e9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200882-ed3902daa57ebaeb89ec58cc3db404e9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200882" class="wp-caption-text">参与海产展的厂商现场示范海鲜料理。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200867" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200867-DJY05133.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200867" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200867-DJY05133-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200867" class="wp-caption-text">参与海产展的厂商现场示范海鲜料理。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200855" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200855-DJY05168.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200855-DJY05168-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200855" class="wp-caption-text">众多厂商参与波士顿北美海产展，希望扩展商机。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14200858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14200858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200858-DJY05158.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14200858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14200858-DJY05158-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14200858" class="wp-caption-text">众多厂商参与波士顿北美海产展，希望扩展商机。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">来自佛罗里达州的“Lake Trucking/Stokes Fish Co.”公司近几年一直有着不错的营业额。该公司CEO布罗赞斯基（Ted Brozanski）说，在疫情之前，该公司的年增长率达到15%或16%。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“在COVID期间，我们实际上以20%的速度增长。在那之后，营业增长有点减弱，我想那是经济条件的原因。但是我们还是非常乐观。”他说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">布罗赞斯基还说，其他一些参展商也都相信，海产行业未来会持续增长。◇</p>
