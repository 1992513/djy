<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>游韩国必尝甜食 网友热推12种</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203226-shutterstock_2286967445-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国传统甜点遵循药食同源的饮食文化，大家到访韩国不容错过。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月15日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a>旅游，坐在餐厅里享受正餐时别吃太饱，糕饼专卖店、传统市场还有种类繁多的传统甜食等着你去品尝呢！马上来看看网友热推的12种甜食吧。</p>
<h2>药果（Yakgwa）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203191-shutterstock_2281589827.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203191-shutterstock_2281589827-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203191" class="wp-caption-text">韩式点心药果。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>药果是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a>的传统点心，通常使用面粉、芝麻油、韩国烧酒、蜂蜜和姜汁揉成面团，然后擀平、折叠成多层面皮，用模具压成一个个小饼坯，油炸而成。药果多是漂亮的花朵造型，有的会在中间点缀松仁，金灿灿的色泽让人食指大动。药果不是太甜，适合搭配茶饮。</p>
<h2>油果（Yugwa）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203190-shutterstock_2271599011.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203190-shutterstock_2271599011-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203190" class="wp-caption-text">韩式点心油果。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>油果也是一种历史悠久的韩果，在一般传统市场也能品尝到。油果是用糯米粉与酒发酵的粉团，油炸之后依序裹上糖浆和米香碎等，口感酥脆，有别于药果的扎实质感。</p>
<h2>蜜煎果（Jeonggwa）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203187" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203187-shutterstock_1932001574.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14203187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203187-shutterstock_1932001574-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203187" class="wp-caption-text">韩式甜食莲藕蜜煎果（左一）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蜜煎果是可以长期保存的可口小食，由莲藕、人参、生姜、苹果等蔬果糖渍而成。糖渍后，蔬果保留一些原色和形状，美味又漂亮。</p>
<h2>茶食（Dasik）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203189-shutterstock_2286967453.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203189-shutterstock_2286967453-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203189" class="wp-caption-text">韩式点心茶食。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一口大小的茶食由谷物、坚果等香气食材研磨后与蜂蜜混合，在模具中压实而成，类似中国传统的炒米饼。茶食颜色缤纷，每种颜色代表一种风味，饼面上还有各式精致花纹，雅致悦目。</p>
<h2>饴糖（Yeot）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203188-shutterstock_1697159569.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203188-shutterstock_1697159569-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203188" class="wp-caption-text">韩式甜食白饴糖。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在古籍中，可以了解到古人用粮食酿酒制糖的历史相当悠久。古时用粮食制成的糖称为饴糖。韩国饴糖由蒸煮过的大米、玉米、红薯等粮食与麦芽一起发酵制成，遵循药食同源的传统。</p>
<p>小小一块饴糖的背后，是熬糖匠人付出的十足辛苦和耐心。韩式传统方法制饴糖，需经过人工熬煮糖浆、拉糖条等繁复工艺。从加热糖水开始，随含水量变化，糖水会变成糖浆。最后变成橡皮般柔韧的棕色糖块时，经过拉伸，则变成白色糖条。白饴糖多孔但坚硬，可以含在嘴里慢慢融化。</p>
<h2>米发糕（Jeungpyeon）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203195" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203195-shutterstock_1889530012.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203195-shutterstock_1889530012-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203195" class="wp-caption-text">韩式甜食米发糕。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酒酵点心由来已久，韩式发糕便是用米浆混合酒等食材发酵的传统糕点。发酵米浆蒸熟后，弥漫淡淡酒香和酸味，吃起来松软、易消化。</p>
<h2>糯米糕（Yaksik）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203186-shutterstock_2034204590.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203186-shutterstock_2034204590-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203186" class="wp-caption-text">韩式甜食糯米糕。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>糯米糕与其它韩式传统糕饼一样也使用天然食物制作，包括整粒糯米、板栗、去核红枣、松仁、葡萄干、红糖、肉桂粉和酱油等。食材经过蒸或煮制熟，最后切成小方块享用。</p>
<h2>柿饼核桃卷（Gotgamssam）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203192" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203192-shutterstock_1140730613.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203192-shutterstock_1140730613-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203192" class="wp-caption-text">韩式甜食柿饼核桃卷。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>成分最简单的韩式甜点大概要属柿饼核桃卷了，仅用柿饼与核桃两样食材制作。柿饼本身就是果糖浓郁、余味悠长的天赐美味，核桃嵌在棕色果肉中间相得益彰，令每一片点心都有自己的独特花纹，别具仪式感。</p>
<h2>核桃烧（Hodu-gwaja）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203197" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203197-shutterstock_2017012745.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203197-shutterstock_2017012745-600x432.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="432" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203197" class="wp-caption-text">韩式甜食核桃烧。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1934年一对韩国夫妇发明了核桃烧，如今这阵美食风也刮到了韩国以外的许多地方。核桃烧的制作过程类似港式鸡蛋仔，是在核桃形状的模具中填充面糊并烤熟，烤出来就是逼真的核桃模样了。知名馅料是红豆沙与整颗核桃，近年也有发展出奶油、起司等口味。</p>
<h2>糖饼（Hotteok）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203193" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203193" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203193-shutterstock_1228413106.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203193-shutterstock_1228413106-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203193" class="wp-caption-text">韩式甜食糖饼。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>糖饼成为超人气的街头小吃是有原因的！糖饼馅料包含涮嘴的坚果、糖和肉桂等，经过油炸色美味美。冬天在街头吃上一个热乎乎的糖饼，无疑是对身心最温暖的慰籍。购买时现场观看糖饼在油锅中膨胀变化的过程，令不少网友惊呼相当疗愈。此外，一些咖啡店会在糖饼上面点缀冰淇淋，带给食客糖饼的清凉吃法。</p>
<h2>水正果（Sujeonggwa）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203194" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203194-shutterstock_1816249382.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203194-shutterstock_1816249382-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203194" class="wp-caption-text">韩式茶饮水正果。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>姜有帮助消化的作用，由新鲜姜、肉桂、糖或蜂蜜煮成的水正果，在韩国是受欢迎的饭后茶饮。享用时，撒几颗松仁装饰，也有放入柿饼浸泡饮用，冰冻、热饮都别有风味。</p>
<h2>甜米露（Sikhye）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14203196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14203196" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203196-shutterstock_1241484598.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14203196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203196-shutterstock_1241484598-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14203196" class="wp-caption-text">韩式甜饮甜米露。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>甜米露在韩国随处可以找到，也是汗蒸幕必饮品。作为传统饮品，原味甜米露是用天然麦芽浸泡的水混合米饭发酵制成，其它风味还有南瓜、地瓜等。麦芽中含有助消化的淀粉酶，因此当地人习惯在享用丰盛美食后喝点清爽的甜米露缓解肠胃。除了室温饮用，冰冰爽爽的甜米露沙冰也深得大众喜爱。</p>
