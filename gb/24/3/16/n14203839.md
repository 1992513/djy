<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>获救的猫“忠贞不渝”迷恋主人的双胞胎</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14203841-Alfycat-t2-1080x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>被救的猫迷恋主人的双胞胎，从不离开他们身边。（Rachel Petersen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月16日讯】（英文大纪元Epochtimes inspired Staff报导／王万舍编译）阿菲猫（Alfycat）总是陪伴在它最要好的朋友、5岁的双胞胎桑尼（Sunny）和尼亚（Nyah）身边。这只7岁的获救的猫咪在双胞胎刚出生时第一次见到她们以后，就立即与她们建立了不可思议的友谊。从第一天起就开始黏着她们、模仿她们。</p>
<figure style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580714-Rachel-Alfy-13.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580714-Rachel-Alfy-13.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Rachel Petersen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三个孩子的妈妈雷切尔·马奇（Rachel March）表示，这是她见过的最甜蜜的动物行为。34岁的马奇女士来自澳洲，经营时尚及古董收藏品。她的阿菲猫对这对双胞胎的热爱并不只限于笨笨的滑稽动作。最难忘的是阿菲猫救了险些被一条东部小棕蛇咬伤的双胞胎。当时勇敢的小猫待在双胞胎身边，保护她们免受那条致命爬行动物的伤害。</p>
<p>“那蛇有剧毒”，马奇女士告诉《大纪元时报》，“我永远也不会忘记那一刻。”</p>
<p>马奇女士在新南威尔斯州纽卡斯尔长大，至今仍与家人生活在那里。她曾经帮助阿菲猫脱离了困境。</p>
<p>“它当时身上有跳蚤、蠕虫，患有皮肤炎，而且体重不足。”她说，“我们精心照顾、爱护它，最终让它恢复了健康。”</p>
<p>阿菲猫的毛发黑白相间，对生活充满热情。它最初是为马奇女士11岁的儿子菲尼克斯 （Pheonix）准备而带回家的。回家后菲尼克斯立即与它成了朋友，现在两个小家伙有着“兄弟情谊”。每次等到双胞胎入睡后，阿菲猫就跟菲尼克斯一起玩，友善的小猫总是坐在旁边看着菲尼克斯画画。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580722-Rachel-Alfy-16-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580722-Rachel-Alfy-16-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Rachel Petersen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于双胞胎女孩们，阿菲猫与她们则有着完全不同的友谊方式。</p>
<p>“我在整个双胞胎妊娠期间，阿菲猫都和我在一起，所以它将双胞胎视为自己的孩子，而不是兄妹。”马奇女士说，在马奇女士怀孕期间，“这只有趣、人模人样的猫”不喜欢离开马奇女士身边一步。她每次出门后它都会等着她回家。</p>
<p>“它就像是我的铁磁。”马奇女士说。</p>
<figure style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580728-Rachel-Alfy-22-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580728-Rachel-Alfy-22-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Rachel Petersen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>双胞胎出生并从医院回家后，这只猫似乎已经对她们很熟悉了。它趴在地板上，躺在她们中间，嗅着她们的气味，并且轻轻地抚摸着她们。</p>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580729-Rachel-Alfy-23-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580729-Rachel-Alfy-23-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Rachel Petersen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580731-Rachel-Alfy-24-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580731-Rachel-Alfy-24-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Rachel Petersen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当女孩们长大，做一些事情时，阿菲猫开始模仿她们。</p>
<p>“它会学她们爬到尿布台上躺下，就像需要换尿布一样。”马奇女士说。</p>
<p>阿菲猫不想跟她们分开，它学会了如何打开她们房间的每一扇门，这样它就可以随时关注着女孩们并跟随她们到处走动。</p>
<p>当她们玩的时候，阿菲猫会旁观她们玩游戏，甚至玩她们的玩具。阿菲猫似乎与她们有着不可分割的联系，它会推着她们的助行器帮她们走路，它不喜欢她们把它独自留在家里。</p>
<figure style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580703-Rachel-Alfy-1-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580703-Rachel-Alfy-1-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Rachel Petersen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着时间的推移，这对双胞胎逐渐长大，并形成了截然不同的个性——桑尼变得更加外向和时髦，她喜欢跳舞，是一名“社交蝴蝶”；而尼亚则性格安静，她喜欢绘画、喜欢动物和拼图。</p>
<p>但阿菲猫并没有太大改变。</p>
<p>“双胞胎在哪里，它就在哪里。”马奇女士说，“她们本周开始要上幼儿园了。我知道阿菲猫会送她们去上学，并可能会去接她们。”</p>
<p>马奇女士没有农场，但她家有一个大后院，后院里有鸭子、菜园和很多动物。</p>
<p>“能够给我的孩子们有这样的生活真是太美好了。”她说，“阿菲猫是孩子们忠实的伴侣。”</p>
<figure style="width: 513px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580705-Rachel-Alfy-3-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580705-Rachel-Alfy-3-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="513" b="513" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Rachel Petersen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿菲猫、菲尼克斯和双胞胎特别喜欢在户外和其它动物一起玩。无论桑尼和尼亚去哪里，阿菲猫也都会跟到哪里。</p>
<p>说起她心爱的猫的温驯性格，马奇女士非常感动，尤其是考虑到它以前的生活处境是多么艰难。</p>
<p>“猫经常被误解，并受到最糟糕的批评，但如果你给它们机会，它们可以成为很棒的伴侣。”她说。</p>
<figure style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580708-Rachel-Alfy-6-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F02%2F05%2Fid5580708-Rachel-Alfy-6-rotated.jpeg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Rachel Petersen提供）</figcaption></figure>
