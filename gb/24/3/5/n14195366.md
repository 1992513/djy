<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>重光国小体验童军趣 云林庆祝三五童军节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195367-668014-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>重光国小太鼓队表演。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月05日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）5日云林斗南重光国小热闹非凡，欢聚了来自县内小学和国中男女童军，共同参与了一场充满活力的“好品德 童军趣”嘉年华，暨庆祝三五童军节的盛大活动。这一活动是云林县在113年度推动的童军教育整体推动计划的一部分，重光国小校长杨松田表示透过品德教育、领导培养和交谊活动，培养学生的核心素养及良善品德，并发扬童军精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195374" style="width: 513px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195374-668021-450x288.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14195374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195374-668021-450x288.jpg" alt="" width="513" b="328" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195374" class="wp-caption-text">五日在斗南重光国小举办云林三五童军节表扬活动。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14195371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195371" style="width: 504px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195371-668018-450x268.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14195371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195371-668018-450x268.jpg" alt="" width="504" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195371" class="wp-caption-text">颁奖典礼时，与会所有童军表演双旗。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14195369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195369" style="width: 507px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195369-668016-450x326.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14195369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195369-668016-450x326.jpg" alt="" width="507" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195369" class="wp-caption-text">幼稚园小童军的表演最吸睛。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>重光国小校长杨松田开幕典礼时表示，今天的活动内容丰富多彩，包括庆祝大会、颁奖典礼、闯关活动以及追踪旅行等。学生们积极参与各项童军活动，从中体验团队合作的重要性，并通过手机导航、打卡拍照等方式，在追踪旅行中展现了他们的智慧和技能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195368" style="width: 507px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195368-668015-450x415.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14195368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195368-668015-450x415.jpg" alt="" width="507" b="468" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195368" class="wp-caption-text">关关游戏，小朋友特别开心。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5日同时也是重光国小童军实验学校102岁的生日，这一里程碑般的时刻，也成为了活动中的亮点之一。在活动的最后，学生们齐聚一堂，在古坑绿色隧道举办颁奖典礼，上午开幕式也表扬了优秀童军人员、吴兆棠博士奖学金得主，以及其他绩优团领导人和服务员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195370" style="width: 494px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195370-668017-450x375.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14195370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195370-668017-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="494" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195370" class="wp-caption-text">云林三五童军节表扬绩优学校。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14195372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195372" style="width: 504px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195372-668019-450x375.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14195372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195372-668019-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="504" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195372" class="wp-caption-text">追踪旅行终点站古坑绿色隧道。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自西螺东南国中林同学开心的表示，这场活动不仅丰富了课余生活，更促进了学生与老师之间的互动和交流，今天也交到新朋友，每个参与者都充满了快乐与收获。云林斗南重光国小童军趣，庆祝三五童军节的活力场景，展现了童军活动的魅力和价值，为学生们留下了难忘的美好回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14195373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14195373" style="width: 532px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195373-668020-450x375.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14195373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14195373-668020-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="532" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14195373" class="wp-caption-text">追踪旅行时，采用手机定位导航，再依指示打卡拍照完成关卡。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
