<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>昔蓝队遭评散沙 林襄迎《炸裂》总决赛：活出自己</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208219-2403220237071487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《炸裂吧！女孩》花费钜资搭建演唱会舞台，本周末最终回宣布总冠军得主。（好看娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月22日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）台湾实境节目《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E8%A3%82%E5%90%A7%EF%BC%81%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%A9.md#1">炸裂吧！女孩</a>》，本周迎来最终回，除了砸重金搭建演唱会舞台，让24位女孩展现舞台魅力，邀请评审团罗志祥、邱胜翊、NIKE老师，并由陈大天担任主持人，在总决赛中由召集人钱薇娟宣布最终总冠军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208220" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208220-2403220237041487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208220" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208220-2403220237041487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208220" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E8%A3%82%E5%90%A7%EF%BC%81%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%A9.md#1">炸裂吧！女孩</a>》篮队演出画面，图左三为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E8%A5%84.md#1">林襄</a>。（好看娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>首先表演的蓝队带来科幻感十足展现魅惑力的舞蹈赢得满堂彩。队长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E8%AF%97%E6%95%8F.md#1">关诗敏</a>说：“欢迎大家来我们的毕业典礼，为了这次的演出，队员们每天都练习到半夜，想说不要留下遗憾，让大家看到蓝队的团魂。”</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E8%A5%84.md#1">林襄</a>分享因为《炸裂吧！女孩》我们学会充分沟通、不走心，有事就会讲出来。要感谢蓝队粉丝，之前评论说蓝队像一团散沙，这次表演想表达我们是要挣脱这些虚拟的枷锁，开发潜能活出自己。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208221-2403220237101487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208221" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208221-2403220237101487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208221" class="wp-caption-text">《炸裂吧！女孩》本周迎来最终回，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E8%AF%97%E6%95%8F.md#1">关诗敏</a>有精彩演出。（好看娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来红队化身特务的舞蹈，召集人钱薇娟夸赞说：“每次公演，红队都是我最喜欢的，但演出的精彩度都无法转换成人气投票数，有点可惜。”更点名宋宋以前跟林襄在同一队，光芒被盖住，我一开始就觉得宋宋是24位女孩中最漂亮的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208224-2403220236561487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208224" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208224-2403220236561487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208224" class="wp-caption-text">《炸裂吧！女孩》红队Reddiebae：（前排左起）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%84%E9%94%AD%E6%AC%A3.md#1">庄锭欣</a>、各务礼美奈、卢以恩、吴心缇、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E5%A9%89%E5%8D%89.md#1">宋婉卉</a>（宋宋）、林芷芸。（好看娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着紫队表演异国风扇子舞，评审罗志祥问排球甜心廖苡任挑战唱跳，是否你有后悔来这节目？廖苡任回说：“有后悔！我本来一直很想放弃，唱跳不是我熟悉的领域，学习扇子舞，刚开始一直掉扇子，还飞出去打到队友。感谢队友一起鼓励，携手努力，完成了炸裂的自己。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14208223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14208223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208223-2403220237011487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14208223" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14208223-2403220237011487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14208223" class="wp-caption-text">《炸裂吧！女孩》紫队Purpiebomb，图（左起）为排球甜心廖苡任、Dora、莎莎。（好看娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
