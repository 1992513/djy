<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>余风采堂敬老春宴 宗亲齐聚贺龙年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205462-DSC00651-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙余风采堂3月16日举办龙年敬老春宴，余氏宗长、职员和“余南狮”舞狮团合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年03月19日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“在这个龙年，我有四个字，就是龙飞凤舞。”纽英崙余风采堂主席余立昌在春宴中说。他的寓意包括当晚的“舞”狮贺岁、妇女组“凤”舞，以及“龙”喻示的积极精神。</p>
<p>3月16日晚上，余风采堂在华埠帝苑酒楼举办龙年春宴，与宗亲及好友总计300多人共聚一堂，致敬耆英，庆贺新年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14205458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205458" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205458-DSC00609.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205458-DSC00609-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205458" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂3月16日举办龙年敬老春宴，余氏宗长、职员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205459-DSC00613.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205459-DSC00613-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205459" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂3月16日举办龙年敬老春宴，余氏妇女组合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>余立昌欢迎所有来宾，祝福他们大富大贵，龙马精神；也祝福余氏族务兴隆，团结长久。“公所的未来要靠我们宗亲的支持，我希望每一位宗亲，无论老少，希望来到公所走一走，几分钟或者几秒钟都好，给予我们公所很大的支持。”他说。</p>
<p>余立昌期待，余氏子弟未来不仅多多参加公所活动，更要借公所这个平台发光发热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14205464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205464-DSC00659.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205464-DSC00659-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205464" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂主席余立昌欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205467" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205467-DSC00679.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205467-DSC00679-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205467" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂主席余雅伦是3月16日春宴的主持之一。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205475" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205475-DSC00712.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205475-DSC00712-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205475" class="wp-caption-text">妇女组主任余艳玲是3月16日春宴的主持之一。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周六晚上的宴会由余雅伦和余艳玲主持。庆典从舞狮贺岁开始。麻州昆士（Quincy）的“余南狮”武馆带来5头彩狮，在锣鼓声中翻腾献瑞，还走进宴席中，为每一桌宾客送去祝福。据赴宴宗亲介绍，“余南狮”及其创始人余德华作为余氏本家，非常支持余风采堂，不仅没收佣金，还向公所捐赠了数百美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14205460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205460-DSC00632.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205460-DSC00632-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205460" class="wp-caption-text">麻州昆士的“余南狮”武馆舞狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205461-DSC00638.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205461-DSC00638-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205461" class="wp-caption-text">麻州昆士的“余南狮”武馆舞狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在舞狮贺岁之后，余风采堂妇女组的成员们换上鲜艳的服装，为宾客们表演了两段她们排练许久的舞蹈，赢得热烈欢呼和掌声。随后，她们还与公所其他职员及元老合唱歌曲“万水千山总是情”和“友谊之光”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14205463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205463-DSC00655.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205463-DSC00655-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205463" class="wp-caption-text">余氏职员和妇女组成员合唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205465" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205465-DSC00668.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205465-DSC00668-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205465" class="wp-caption-text">余氏妇女组成员表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>余风采堂美东总长余文博为宗亲们对春宴的支持而深表谢意。“我们余风采堂，差不多在侨社春宴里是数一数二的这么多人。很多谢大家这么赏面。今天也很多谢我们妇女组，练了很久，才来表演给大家看。”他说。</p>
<p>余风采堂元老余超群代表余风采总堂，向所有来宾送上新年祝福，祝大家身体健康，万事胜意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14205466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205466" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205466-DSC00672.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205466-DSC00672-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205466" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂美东总长余文博感谢大家对公所和春宴的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205471" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205471-DSC00696.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205471-DSC00696-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205471" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂元老余超群参加春宴，与夫人跳舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205472-DSC00701.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205472-DSC00701-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205472" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂元老们演唱歌曲。左起：余宗涛、余厚利、余文博、余积尧。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205469" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205469-DSC00692.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205469-DSC00692-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205469" class="wp-caption-text">前主席余宝爱在3月16日的春宴中演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>余氏另一位主席余雅伦亦对所有宗亲和友人表示感谢。他说，往年的春宴都会邀请其他侨团和政要嘉宾参与，而今年只宴请本堂宗亲及友人。“看到这里有30多桌宾客真是非常好，我们起初并没有预料到这么多，非常高兴看到所有朋友和支持者。”余雅伦说。</p>
<p>当晚宴席的一大重点，是向4位长者赠送百元敬老红包。他们分别是100岁的余荣灿，以及3位90岁的耆英余丽侬、余华波和余国徽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14205468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205468" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205468-DSC00688.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205468-DSC00688-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205468" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂主席们向90岁及以上耆英赠送敬老红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205473-DSC00707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205473-DSC00707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205473" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂3月16日举办龙年春宴，宗亲及友人总共300多人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，余风采堂向优秀学子们颁发了第7届余氏奖学金。获得者都是取得了GPA 3.5或以上的优秀学业成绩，或已获得所在学校奖学金的余氏子弟。</p>
<p>本科生奖学金得主有余正跃、余智琪、余汉恩、余汉荣、余嘉慧、Sydney Mackler和Dylan Mackler。</p>
<p>研究生奖学金由余建元和余国济这两位宗亲捐赠，获得者有余了了、余湘儿、余万辉和余慧秀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14205474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205474-DSC00719.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205474-DSC00719-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205474" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂向优秀学子颁发奖学金。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14205470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14205470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205470-DSC00694.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14205470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14205470-DSC00694-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14205470" class="wp-caption-text">春宴宾客们在歌声中热情跳舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除此之外，元老余超群还特别捐赠了两个50美元的抽奖红包，从所有奖学金得主中抽出两位幸运儿。</p>
<p>宴会还有面向所有来宾的抽奖环节，令大家欢呼不断，现场气氛非常热闹。◇</p>
