<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】杏仁西兰花~5分钟上菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14197888-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>除了蘸酱，西兰花搭配起司享用也很美味。（李若琳／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>想快速烹饪一顿营养可口的小菜吗？如下的微波炉西兰花5分钟就能上桌。西兰花容易吸附酱汁，搭配营养丰富的起司和烤杏仁片十分美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14197887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14197887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14197887-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14197887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14197887-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14197887" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【食材准备】</strong></p>
<p>2杯西兰花，约200克<br />
1汤匙橄榄油<br />
现磨黑胡椒<br />
1瓣蒜，压泥<br />
1/4杯生杏仁片<br />
1杯橘色切达起司碎（Cheddar cheese），可换用乳香更浓郁的现磨意大利fontina起司<br />
适量盐，可选，因为起司比较咸</p>
<p><strong>【做法】</strong></p>
<p>1. 西兰花切成小块，不用中间粗茎。</p>
<p>2. 取一个带盖子的微波炉专用碗，放入油、黑胡椒和蒜泥拌匀。加入西兰花与调料拌匀，平铺。</p>
<p>3. 若使用盘子加热，上方倒扣一个碗或盘子</p>
<figure id="attachment_14197886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14197886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14197886-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14197886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14197886-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14197886" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 微波炉（950W）加热3分半钟，静置1分钟。</p>
<p>5. 撒上少许盐，拌匀。上面铺起司，不加盖加热1分钟至起司融化。</p>
<p>6. 杏仁片在平底锅中烘烤至微黄，撒在菜肴上即可享用。◇</p>
