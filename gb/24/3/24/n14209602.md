<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“回家吧音乐祭” 医师为南横小学艺文深耕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209603-671657-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>金曲歌手王宏恩、苏瓦那等轮番上台表演，与雾鹿国小学生互动。（周颢承提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年03月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）位于南横山区的台东海端乡“雾鹿村”，在23日周休假日涌现千人次，警察出动协助指挥交通，此次人潮源于当地喜事，和“林思宏X5医师慈善基金会”联合雾鹿国小举办的“2024年回家吧！唱我们的歌音乐祭”活动。</p>
<p>这次活动和海端乡公所联合举办，设置关卡让民众了解布农文化，并享受音乐盛宴。活动由“林思宏X5医师慈善基金会”三年前提出的，希望支持雾鹿国小学生学习艺术及乐器，他不仅购置乐器，更全额支付指导老师钟点费。林思宏表示，音乐祭不仅是展现学习成果，更是布农文化的融合与发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14209604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209604-671658.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14209604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209604-671658-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209604" class="wp-caption-text">海端乡雾鹿国小，在23日周休假日涌现千人次。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14209609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209609-671666.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14209609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209609-671666-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209609" class="wp-caption-text">第二届“回家吧音乐祭”活动，林思宏医师为南横小学艺文深耕。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14209608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209608-671662.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14209608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209608-671662-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209608" class="wp-caption-text">文化体验活动，让参与者体验布农族人的童玩。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的音乐祭在阳光明媚的天气下举办，吸引了更多人参与。活动中，海端乡公所设置了文化体验活动，让参与者体验原住民的生活方式，并享受布农元素风味餐料理。林思宏表示，除了音乐课程外，基金会还赞助雾鹿国小的“食寨美力食农课程”，希望孩子们从小认识自己与土地文化的连结。今年更首次开放外界预订风味餐，由小朋友负责“说菜”，让食客品味文化底蕴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14209607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209607" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209607-671661.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14209607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209607-671661-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209607" class="wp-caption-text">活动当天，由部落工坊提供布农元素的风味餐。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14209606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209606-671660.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14209606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209606-671660-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209606" class="wp-caption-text">这次活动和海端乡公所联合举办，设置关卡让民众了解布农文化。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14209605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209605" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209605-671659.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14209605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209605-671659-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209605" class="wp-caption-text">雾鹿部落耆老盛装出席。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>音乐祭上，当地耆老和金曲歌手王宏恩、苏瓦那等轮番上台表演，与雾鹿国小学生互动。演出精彩动人，让在场观众纷纷预约下次再相会。这次音乐祭不仅带动当地文化发展，更展现社区的凝聚力，成为海端乡的一大亮点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14209610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14209610" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209610-671672.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14209610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14209610-671672-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14209610" class="wp-caption-text">“回家吧音乐祭”精彩动人，让在场观众纷纷预约下次再相会。（周颢承提供）</figcaption></figure>
