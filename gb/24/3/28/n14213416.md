<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【健康养成记】久坐腰酸背痛？5个动作可减缓</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213493-2.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>你是否常觉得腰酸背痛、肩颈酸痛，甚至出现下肢水肿？这些都常常跟久坐有关，久坐还会造成代谢不佳、肥胖、心血管疾病、便秘、颈椎疾病等问题，下半身的肌肉无力，也有可能是久坐不锻炼造成的。</p><p>减少久坐造成的危害，最好的方法就是每坐30-40分钟起身，活动一下僵化的筋骨，帮助骨盆及下肢血液循环，同时可以重新将自己的坐姿调整到正确的姿势，也就是不要让骨盆前倾或后倾，这样也可以减少腰酸背痛等不适。</p><p></p><p>什么是骨盆的正确位置？将食指跟拇指分别放在髋部的髂前上棘跟髂后上棘，食指跟拇指保持水平高度就是骨盆归正。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p><strong>Step2：</strong>右手往下，左手往上推，将骨盆往左上翘，停留三秒后换边，左手往下，右手往上推，将骨盆往右上移，停留三秒后换边，重复10次。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p></p><p><strong>Step2：</strong>将后腿侧的手臂上抬至180度，注意抬手不耸肩，停留十秒钟之后放下，重复10次后换边伸拉。</p><p></p><p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213551-1.png" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-14213551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213551-1-450x384.png" alt="" width="450" b="384"/></a>
</p><div class="img-caption">?<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213562-2.png" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-14213562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213562-2-450x416.png" alt="" width="450" b="416"/></a></div><div class="image-container"><h2><span style="color:#188638"><b>第三招</b><b>?</b><b>弓箭步</b></span></h2>弓箭步锻炼到臀肌跟股四头肌，这是下肢中最大也最重要的两块肌肉，背痛跟膝盖的不适或上下楼无力，多跟这两个肌群有关系。</div><p><strong>Step1：</strong>双脚跟肩同宽，右脚往后跨一步，让下蹲的时候双腿膝盖均可以呈现90度。</p><p><strong>Step2：</strong>重心在两脚中间，身体保持直立的情况下往下蹲，至双腿膝盖均呈现90度。这个姿势会平均练到臀肌跟股四头肌的力量。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p><strong>Step3：</strong>停留三秒后起身。重复10次。换另一只脚做弓箭步。</p><div class="image-container" style="text-align:center">?<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213565-4.png" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-14213565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213565-4-450x376.png" alt="" width="450" b="376"/></a></div><div class="dable_placeholder"><h2><span style="color:#188638"><b>第四招</b><b>?</b><b>左右弓箭步</b></span></h2>左右下蹲的弓箭步更能练到臀肌力量、强化核心肌群，同时训练下半身的稳定性与协调性。</div><p><strong>Step1：</strong>双脚打开（大于髋部）站立，脚尖朝向前面，此时的重心在两腿中间。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p><strong>Step2：</strong>身体前倾45度但保持腰挺直，将重心移往右边，髋部往右后推，右腿屈膝，左腿打直。</p><p><strong>Step3：</strong>停留三秒后将重心移回两腿中间。重复10次。换另一只脚做弓箭步。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><div class="image-container" style="text-align:center"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213569-2.png" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14213569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14213569-2.png" alt="" width="371" b="319"/></a></div><div class="image-container">
<div class="image-container"><b>第五招</b><b>?</b><b>踮脚尖</b></div>踮脚尖不但可以让小腿肌肉收缩，促进下肢血液循环，还可以促进足弓健康。</div><p>Step1：双脚站立，身体挺直但微微往前倾，不稳的人可以用手轻扶椅子。</p><p>Step2：踮起脚尖。注意脚掌不要内翻，就是单纯用脚尖的力量把脚垫起，停留20秒后放下。重复10次。注意踮脚的过程中要直上直下，身体重心稳定。</p><p></p><p><em><strong><span style="color:#800000">观看影片：久坐族少运动，久坐造成的腰酸背痛，五招伸展及肌肉训练方法！</span></strong></em></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>纷乱之世，心存健康之道，就看<span style="color:#188638"><a style="color:#188638" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/b5/nsc1002.md#1" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class">健康1+1</a></span>！◇</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p>责任编辑：李凡
