<h2><strong>黄花菱Ludwigia sedoides柳叶菜科</strong></h2>
<p>
又称浮叶水丁香、小叶菱、菱叶丁香蓼。原产于南美洲热带环境的水域，为浮叶性多年生草本。根生长于水中底泥，茎绿色或红色，菱形叶片互生聚集在茎端放射状排列，叶面光滑、叶背有细毛，藉以产生表面张力浮于水面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14206120" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14206120" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14206120-639484_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14206120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14206120-639484_medium-600x589.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="589"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14206120" class="wp-caption-text">叶片排列整齐，宛如马赛克拼贴。因叶片形似菱角而开黄花得名，但是与菱角无亲缘关系。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚上黄花菱叶片会有上举的睡眠运动。叶片排列整齐，宛如马赛克拼贴。因叶片形似菱角而开黄花得名，但是与菱角无亲缘关系。常被栽植为个人水族观赏及水池美化用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14206123" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14206123" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14206123-639489_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14206123" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14206123-639489_medium-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14206123" class="wp-caption-text">根生长于水中底泥，茎绿色或红色，菱形叶片互生聚集在茎端放射状排列，叶面光滑、叶背有细毛，藉以产生表面张力浮于水面。</figcaption></figure>
