<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】枫糖菠萝翻转蛋糕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259438-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>枫糖蛋糕湿软细腻，搭配菠萝造型，让美味多了几分仪式感。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月04日讯】枫树汁液蒸发水分后得到枫糖浆。枫糖浆质地顺滑，甜而不腻，美味吃法除了甜点浇头、酱汁，还可以烘烤出湿软质地的蛋糕。这里分享一款枫糖浆翻转蛋糕，表层使用酸酸甜甜的菠萝（凤梨）片造型，让美味再添几分仪式感。</p>
<h2>食材准备：</h2>
<p>180克无漂白中筋面粉<br />
1/2茶匙无铝双效泡打粉<br />
1/4茶匙小苏打<br />
1/2茶匙盐<br />
1/2杯无盐黄油（butter），软化<br />
200毫升枫糖浆<br />
2/3杯低脂白脱牛奶<br />
2个中号鸡蛋<br />
1个新鲜菠萝</p>
<figure id="attachment_14259439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14259439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259439-2-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14259439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259439-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14259439" class="wp-caption-text">枫糖菠萝翻转蛋糕所需食材。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>所有食材需要室温。如下是料理步骤：</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14259440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14259440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259440-3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14259440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259440-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14259440" class="wp-caption-text">预热烤箱350华氏度。菠萝切成如图的圆片，每片再切成6等份。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14259441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14259441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259441-4-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14259441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259441-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14259441" class="wp-caption-text">9英寸的金属烤盘用一汤匙黄油涂抹，摆入菠萝。烤30分钟。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14259442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14259442" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259442-5-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14259442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259442-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14259442" class="wp-caption-text">烤干的菠萝片出炉后放烤架上冷却。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>开始制作面糊：</h2>
<p>1.在温暖的室内操作。搅拌碗中混合面粉、小苏打、泡打粉和盐。</p>
<p>2.另一个容器中放入枫糖浆和涂抹烤盘剩下的黄油，用电动搅拌棒搅打融合，然后加鸡蛋搅融合，接着混合面粉。最后加入白脱牛奶搅匀，快速倒入模具。烤40分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14259443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14259443" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259443-6-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14259443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259443-6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14259443" class="wp-caption-text">出炉的蛋糕放烤架上降温，到不烫手的时候，取一个大盘子，将蛋糕倒扣，美味的翻转蛋糕即成。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>枫糖如何制作</h2>
<p>枫糖浆如此美味，是如何制作的呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_14259445" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14259445" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259445-7-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14259445" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259445-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14259445" class="wp-caption-text">制作枫糖浆，需要先收集枫树汁液。早春是枫糖浆的产季，在枫糖农场，工人会用电钻在树干上钻洞，然后插入塑胶导管。导管将每棵树流出的树汁运送到一个收集桶。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14259447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14259447" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259447-8-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14259447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14259447-8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14259447" class="wp-caption-text">透明瓶子装的是枫树原汁，原汁蒸发后得到棕色糖浆。右图为糖色样品。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
