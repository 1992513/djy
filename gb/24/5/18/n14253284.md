<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】合掌瓜炒素鸡块～粤式家常菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253285-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>合掌瓜炒素鸡块（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：炒合掌瓜清脆香甜，在粤菜里还会加入鸡柳一块热炒，就是一道咸甜可口的家常菜。这里我们变化一下食材，让鸡柳变成素鸡排，也成了一盘素食佳肴！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>合掌瓜一个14 oz<br />
素香鸡排12 oz<br />
甜红椒2 oz<br />
姜、蒜、干葱粒各少许<br />
麻油少许<br />
粟粉水2汤匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味：</span></h3>
<p>素蚝油1汤匙<br />
糖1/4茶匙<br />
蘑菇粉1茶匙<br />
美极鲜酱油少许<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
水2/3杯</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></h3>
<p>1. 合掌瓜刨去外皮去核，再切成小块备用。素鸡排、红椒排切成小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14253287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14253287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253287-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14253287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253287-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14253287" class="wp-caption-text">合掌瓜刨去外皮去核，再切成小块备用。素鸡排、红椒排切成小块备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 热锅下油，先把素鸡块两面煎香，盛出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14253290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14253290" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253290-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14253290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253290-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14253290" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，先把素鸡块两面煎香，盛出备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 再下少许油，放入料头及合掌瓜炒一会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14253291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14253291" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253291-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14253291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253291-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14253291" class="wp-caption-text">再下少许油，放入料头及合掌瓜炒一会。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 加入水及所有调味料，用中火煮3分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14253292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14253292" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253292-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14253292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253292-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14253292" class="wp-caption-text">加入水及所有调味料，用中火煮3分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放回煎香的鸡块和甜红椒，翻炒均匀后煮2分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14253293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14253293" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253293-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14253293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253293-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14253293" class="wp-caption-text">放回煎香的鸡块和甜红椒，翻炒均匀后煮2分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 用粟粉水勾芡，最后加一点麻油便可上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14253294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14253294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253294-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14253294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14253294-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14253294" class="wp-caption-text">用粟粉水勾芡，最后加一点麻油便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">食谱说明：</span></h3>
<p>? 如果是吃素的人，就不要加姜、葱等辛香料。<br />
? 素香鸡排的成分：水、非基因改造大豆蛋白、小麦淀粉、小麦纤维、介花油、乳清，麦芽糊精、黏稠剂、糖、盐、白胡椒粉、香料等等。◇</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
