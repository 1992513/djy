<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2024造桥南瓜美食季 在地优质农特产品发表</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244645-681092-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>造桥乡农会表示一定会带给所有参与的民众更多视觉、听觉、味觉全方位的享受 （许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年05月09日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）南瓜美食季5月9日在造桥乡牛奶的故乡举办记者会，邀请贵宾与农会同仁，身兼2024苗栗造桥南瓜季产业文化活动代言人的角色，现场让所有贵宾长官们了解南瓜营养丰富，不再是只有清蒸料理，也可有多变化如凉拌、焗烤等等..南瓜美食料理。</p>
<p>今天在现场，与会嘉宾亲手制作好吃又营养的南瓜创新料理/福气金瓜猪笼粄，因应母亲节到来，献给全天下的妈妈们母亲节快乐，希望做出自己手上的特制南瓜料理美食，又达到宣传南瓜营养价值。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14244652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14244652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244652-681099.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14244652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244652-681099-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14244652" class="wp-caption-text">南瓜美食 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14244651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14244651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244651-681098.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14244651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244651-681098-600x391.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14244651" class="wp-caption-text">福气金瓜猪笼粄 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>造桥乡农会总干事彭森华，致力推广西洋南瓜，品种有东昇、瑞福、李白、一品，传统南瓜品种有阿成、金Q、牛奶南瓜等果色及大小丰富多变，又耐贮放，兼具观赏价值。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14244650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14244650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244650-681097.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14244650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244650-681097-600x901.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="901" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14244650" class="wp-caption-text">农粮署北区副分署长林美华(现场DIY猪笼粄的乐趣)表示，农产品要4吃：当季、在地、原形、健康溯源。 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14244649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14244649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244649-681096.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14244649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244649-681096-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14244649" class="wp-caption-text">与会贵宾走南瓜隧道的惊喜与赞赏（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>观赏南瓜有千奇百怪的小型玩具南瓜，更有重量可达上百斤的大南瓜，增添农村栽培作物的生活乐趣，为活动宣传。“2024苗栗造桥南瓜季创新产业文化活动”蓄势待发，造桥乡农会表示一定会带给所有参与的民众更多视觉、听觉、味觉全方位的享受，让来到造桥的朋友们不虚此行，更让你在充满童趣的南瓜传奇中，带回满满的惊喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14244648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14244648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244648-681095.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14244648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244648-681095-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14244648" class="wp-caption-text">户外教学（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14244647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14244647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244647-681094.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14244647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244647-681094-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14244647" class="wp-caption-text">造桥乡农业推广志工们竭诚欢迎来走迷人的南瓜隧道，品美食，慢游造桥乡酪农村的美景 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14244646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14244646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244646-681093.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14244646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14244646-681093-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14244646" class="wp-caption-text">造桥乡农会总干事彭森华表示，因气候关系，量较少以台湾的LV袋装质优的造桥南瓜行销；同时呼吁大家多利用高铁、台铁或巴士，有紧密接驳车至会场。欣赏多样品种南瓜与美食。 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主办单位于5月18、19、25、26四天活动期间的上午8点至下午4点，于台铁丰富车站东侧出口备有会场往返对开免费接驳车服务，敬请搭乘大众运输民众多加利用。</p>
