<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2024年 雪梨Campsie Centre“台湾好食日”免费试吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239817-d6307b5d7113bab2caac7c91757e081c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>雪梨Campsie Centre体验“台湾好食日”惊喜连连。（大纪元提供） 
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年05月03日讯】雪梨Campsie Centre将于6.07（五）-6.09（日）举办“台湾好食日”免费试吃活动，欢迎与家人好友结伴同行，以免向隅！精心挑选台湾好吃又优质的风味美食、伴手礼……错过就可惜啰！喜欢尝鲜钟情台湾美食的伙伴们，快来一饱口福！体验“台湾好食日”，肯定带给你惊喜连连。</p>
<p><strong>【活动地点】雪梨Campsie Centre</strong><br />
<strong> 欢迎莅临、大享口福！</strong><br />
<strong> 28/14-28 Amy St Campsie NSW 2194 Australia</strong><br />
<strong> 【Free Tasting！免费试吃 】6.07（五）-6.09（日）</p>
<p></strong></p>
<h2>▍“淬鱼精”精工淬炼 食在健康</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14239819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14239819" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239819-502002fd3bfd1e29050d16ec5dc173af.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14239819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239819-502002fd3bfd1e29050d16ec5dc173af-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14239819" class="wp-caption-text">方便快速补充营养。（联瑞提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日芳珍馔在台南深耕50年，生产冷冻调理海鲜，更推出可常温保存的“淬鱼精”，将虱目鱼的营养，以科技工法淬取，无任何添加，完整保留18种氨基酸、牛磺酸、多糖体，且无普林。一包淬鱼精等于两条鱼的营养，超好喝，无腥味。</p>
<h2>▍太阳烤的面【上智关庙面】</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14239821" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14239821" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239821-c03e31ad540d1b1cb7e5893073460be6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14239821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239821-c03e31ad540d1b1cb7e5893073460be6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14239821" class="wp-caption-text">传承30年古法手工。（上智食品提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“上智关庙面”坚持传统日晒，深受各大连锁餐饮品牌喜爱，是消费者心中的第一选择。试吃品：刀削面，使用小麦面粉、水、食盐为主，无添加防腐剂，面条香Q有劲、五分钟便利快煮、久煮不软烂、干汤皆宜。不拆封可常温保存1年，并外销十几个国家。</p>
<h2>▍“庙口”好滋味 道地台湾味</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14239822" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14239822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239822-c60183c82b2dfcd9e77d0860439cd525.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14239822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239822-c60183c82b2dfcd9e77d0860439cd525-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14239822" class="wp-caption-text">提供客户高品质的面食。（大庙口食品提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大庙口食品专门做面类、米粉、冬粉、罐头等南北杂货批发生意，并自创“庙口”、“美味”品牌，在台湾南北杂货批发市场占有一席之地。试吃品项：手工拉面、日晒手工面线、高级冬粉、关庙面(粗/细)。</p>
<h2>▍“米屋”台湾顶级米的典范</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14239823" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14239823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239823-fe609bcd2fe109c03a769864457498cd.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14239823" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239823-fe609bcd2fe109c03a769864457498cd-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14239823" class="wp-caption-text">米质优良、晶莹饱满。（米屋智农提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>米屋，台湾最早导入农民契作栽培管理的碾米厂，引领台湾米迈向精致化。<br />
◆米屋馥米，结合印度香米的清香以及日本越光米的Q弹，咀嚼时清甜回甘，热软冷Q。◆米屋新米，产地低温鲜藏直送、日本碾制技术，米饭香甜可口。</p>
<h2>▍宏益制茶以细腻口感引领茶文化</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14239824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14239824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239824-38bbef2e3658d77c3f827798b4a4f865.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14239824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14239824-38bbef2e3658d77c3f827798b4a4f865-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14239824" class="wp-caption-text">七十余年制茶经验。（宏益制茶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宏益制茶，让世界品味台湾茶的价值。◆铁观音，冲泡七道有余香◆清香高山绿茶，清汤绿叶、味纯◆大观冻顶乌龙茶，甘醇回润◆清香茉莉绿茶， 3次薰制花香浓郁◆大观金萱乌龙茶，有淡淡的奶香◆大观翠玉乌龙茶，蕴含花香口感清新。</p>
