<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>阿里山丰山村之旅 体验自然之美品尝风味餐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256389-684119-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义阿里山丰山村之旅，可以体验自然之美，品尝风味餐。（嘉义县政府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年05月23日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）阿里山不只有小火车及森林游乐区，还有一处隐藏在嘉义、云林、南投交界处的丰山村，聚落四周群山环绕，拥有高山、瀑布及巨石三大美景，春天可以欣赏樱花及李花，5、6月可体验采摘红肉李，秋冬可以欣赏红枫，嘉义县文化观光局推荐游客前来丰山村一探石盘谷步道，走访秘境农场、品尝风味餐。</p>
<figure id="14256398" aria-describedby="caption-14256398" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256398-684129-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256398-684129-450x299.jpg" alt="图为嘉义阿里山丰山村美景。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14256398" class="wp-caption-text">图为嘉义阿里山丰山村美景。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14256397" aria-describedby="caption-14256397" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256397-684128-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256397-684128-450x338.jpg" alt="到宾拉赏农场可以体验采摘红肉李（季节限定）。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14256397" class="wp-caption-text">到宾拉赏农场可以体验采摘红肉李（季节限定）。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文观局指出，丰山村的“石盘谷步道”魅力无穷，游客可沿着步道漫步，欣赏茂密竹林、清澈瀑布，尤其在丰水期间走访，还能欣赏壮丽的瀑布美景。</p>
<p>丰山村也以传统制糖工艺闻名，每年11月至隔年3、4月，当地居民开始手工制作黑糖，游客可亲眼见证制糖过程，品尝新鲜制作的黑糖爱玉，清凉爽口的滋味令人赞不绝口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14256390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256390" style="width: 473px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256390-684121-600x398.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14256390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256390-684121-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="473" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256390" class="wp-caption-text">图为石盘谷吊桥（之一）。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14256391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256391" style="width: 481px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256391-684122-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14256391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256391-684122-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="481" b="361" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256391" class="wp-caption-text">图为石盘谷吊桥（之二）。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丰山村还有一处秘境“宾拉赏农场”，农场环境优美，布满许多巨石和丰富多样的植被，游客可以在这里品尝农村风味餐，不论是炸红苋草、冬笋汤、烤山猪或是烤香肠，都让人一吃就爱上。宾拉赏农场简姓主人表示，农场一砖一瓦都是亲手打造，花了近20多年的时间才有如今的样貌， 欢迎大家前来旅游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14256392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256392" style="width: 476px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256392-684123-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14256392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256392-684123-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="476" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256392" class="wp-caption-text">图为石盘谷步道。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
