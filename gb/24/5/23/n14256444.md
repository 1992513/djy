<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>恒丰银行26周年庆晚宴 吴文龙致词 一路有你相伴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256449-IMG_3008-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>吴文龙带领董事群和贵宾切26周年庆蛋糕。</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年05月23日讯】（大纪元讯）5月18日，恒丰银行在Crown Seafood Restaurant举办了26周年庆晚餐，恒丰银行休斯顿全体员工和董事，以及来自达拉斯、加州、内华达州的经理、部分员工，近三百人出席庆祝活动。台北驻休士顿经济文化办事处处长处长萧伊芳、侨教中心主任王盈蓉、休斯顿市审计长Chris Hollins均应邀出席庆祝活动，并上台致辞祝贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14256451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256451-IMG_2943.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14256451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256451-IMG_2943-600x454.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="454"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256451" class="wp-caption-text">张永男总经理报告公司财务情况。（孙玉玟 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14256452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256452-IMG_2948.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14256452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256452-IMG_2948-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256452" class="wp-caption-text">驻休士顿台北经济文化办事处萧伊芳处长出席并致词。（孙玉玟 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14256450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256450-IMG_2950.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14256450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256450-IMG_2950-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256450" class="wp-caption-text">休斯顿市审计长Chris Hollins出席并致词。（孙玉玟 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动开始，播放恒丰银行建行以来的回顾影片、记录及美好回忆，董事长吴文龙致词提到，整组50分钟循环播放的照片，让他内心无比激动，看到了整个银行一步步慢慢成长的历程，看到所有员工在过去26年间对银行的信任，所有董事的齐心协力，所有这一切都深深的感动着他。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14256454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256454-IMG_2984.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14256454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256454-IMG_2984-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256454" class="wp-caption-text">吴文龙演讲介绍他的精彩的事业奋斗史。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14256453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256453" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256453-IMG_3001.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14256453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256453-IMG_3001-600x391.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="391"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256453" class="wp-caption-text">吴文龙手持被授予的荣誉表彰。</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴董事长在讲话中回顾了26年前银行刚刚起步时的情景，他也特别请Original 38员工，至今还在银行工作及应邀出席的退休员工起立，让所有人认识他们，非常感人的一幕。在讲话中，吴董事长特别强调，恒丰银行取得今日亮丽的成绩，跟所有董事及员工的辛勤努力分不开，但是更重要的是，恒丰银行始终严格控制贷款的品质，严格执行内部机制管理，这才使得无论外部环境如何变化，都能取得稳步向前的成绩。</p>
<p>吴董事长强调说，在今年的26周年庆祝之际，他向全体员工提出一个请求，就是大家共同努力，将银行的总资产达成30亿的目标！当董事长提出这一目标时，得到了全体在场员工的热烈回应，充分的显示出恒丰银行的企业向心力，以及强有力的团队精神！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14256456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256456-IMG_3024.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14256456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256456-IMG_3024-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256456" class="wp-caption-text">员工卡拉OK大赛，这组员工演唱台湾原住民歌曲高山青。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14256455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14256455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256455-IMG_3044.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14256455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14256455-IMG_3044-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14256455" class="wp-caption-text">吴文龙和董事群演唱“爱拼才会赢”。</figcaption></figure>
<p>庆祝活动安排有卡拉ok比赛，各个部门各个分行共计12组员工参加比赛，场面热烈，每个团队各显神通，发挥特长，将歌曲演唱及表演合为一体，让所有到到场嘉宾赞叹不已。活动高潮连连不断，在卡拉ok评奖过程中，照例举办了现金抽奖活动，每一位员工都有抽到现金奖，奖金最高为$888.88！</p>
