<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小苏打代替洗洁精去污除臭 居家清洁3大妙用</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242347-shutterstock_1938014329-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小苏打应用广泛，单在居家清洁方面，小苏打有时可代替洗洁精，去污除菌、除臭。学会使用小苏打居家清洁事半功倍。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">如果你从北美的超市买来一盒小苏打，会发现上面写着：</span><span class="s2">100+USES</span><span class="s1">，意思是小苏打有超过</span><span class="s2">100</span><span class="s1">种用途，说明小苏打这种无机化合物应用实在太广泛。单单在居家清洁方面，小苏打有时甚至可以代替洗洁精，有效去污除菌、消除异味，让居家清洁事半功倍。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span style="color: #188638;"><span class="s1">小苏打3大功效</span>?</span></h2>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打用作天然清洁</span><span class="s3">剂</span><span class="s1">，在很多时候可以代替洗洁精，去除家中的各种</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">垢，其秘诀就在于它</span><span class="s1">的这几个特性：</span></p>
<h3 class="p4"><span class="s1"><b>① 分解油</b></span><span class="s3"><b>污</b></span></h3>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打与水混合后呈弱碱性，可以中和油</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">等酸性</span><span class="s3">污渍，</span><span class="s1">而家中的大部分</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">垢都含有皮脂等油污，因此可以利用小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打的碱性来去除这些油污。</span></p>
<h3 class="p4"><span class="s1"><b>② 具有研磨作用</b></span></h3>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打</span><span class="s3">还</span><span class="s1">具有不易溶于水的特性，其</span><span class="s3">细</span><span class="s1">小的</span><span class="s3">颗</span><span class="s1">粒会永久存在，因此它会粘附在某些</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">垢上，就像清洁</span><span class="s3">剂</span><span class="s1">一</span><span class="s3">样来</span><span class="s1">清除掉这些污垢。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14242348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242348-shutterstock_1949690758.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14242348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242348-shutterstock_1949690758-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242348" class="wp-caption-text">小苏打细小的颗粒会粘附在某些污垢上，就像清洁剂一样来清除掉这些污垢。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 class="p4"><span class="s1"><b>③ 具有杀菌、除臭除湿作用</b></span></h3>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打可以破坏细菌的生存环境，从而具有除菌作用，因此可以中和和消除这些细菌发出的异味。它也可以吸收水分，因此还具有除湿效果。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span style="color: #188638;"><span class="s1">小苏打代替清洁剂 3大清洁妙用</span></span></h2>
<h3 class="p3"><span class="s1" style="color: #800000;">妙用一、 制作喷雾清洁剂?清理常见油渍?</span></h3>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><strong>方法：</strong>在200毫升温水中加入2茶匙小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打，然后将其放入</span><span class="s3">喷雾</span><span class="s1">瓶中，充分摇匀，直至完全小苏完全融入水中。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><strong><span class="s1">用途：</span></strong></p>
<p class="p3"><strong><span class="s4">1. 清洁厨房油渍</span></strong></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s4">将小苏打喷雾剂放在厨房中，做饭后煤气灶、吸油烟机、及其周围墙壁的磁砖表面都会留下一层油渍，每次烹饪后喷上这种清洁剂，静置片刻再擦干净，这样厨房的重</span><span class="s5">污</span><span class="s4">染区就不会累积太多油渍，出现清洁困难的局面。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14175959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14175959" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175959-00-shutterstock_2324929903.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14175959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14175959-00-shutterstock_2324929903-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14175959" class="wp-caption-text">油污太重，厨房清洁起来很让人头痛。其实只要学会使用小苏打，日常清洁就会变得更容易。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p3"><strong><span class="s4">2. 清洁发粘的地板</span></strong></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">卧室或客厅的地板发粘时，很可能是赤脚接触而产生的皮脂</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">渍造成，使用小苏打喷雾清洁，可令地板恢复光泽。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><strong><span class="s1">3. 清洁地毯和沙</span><span class="s3">发</span></strong></p>
<p class="p7"><span class="s1">小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打</span><span class="s3">喷雾</span><span class="s1">也适用于</span><span class="s3">难</span><span class="s1">以清洗的</span><span class="s3">织</span><span class="s1">物，例如地毯和沙</span><span class="s3">发</span><span class="s1">。地毯和沙发也会发出</span><span class="s3">难闻</span><span class="s1">的气味，这可能是由皮脂</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">垢引起的，因此只需在其表面上</span><span class="s3">轻轻</span><span class="s1">一</span><span class="s3">喷</span><span class="s1">就能起到清除异味的效果。</span></p>
<p class="p7"><span class="s1"><b>在清洁地毯或沙发时，如果根据自己的喜好在</b></span><span class="s3"><b>喷雾</b></span><span class="s1"><b>中添加薰衣草精油，不</b></span><span class="s3"><b>仅</b></span><span class="s1"><b>可以杀菌除臭，</b></span><span class="s3"><b>还</b></span><span class="s1"><b>可以</b></span><span class="s3"><b>让</b></span><span class="s1"><b>房</b></span><span class="s3"><b>间</b></span><span class="s1"><b>充</b></span><span class="s3"><b>满</b></span><span class="s1"><b>香味。</b></span></p>
<h3 class="p3"><span style="color: #800000;"><span class="s1">妙用二. 制作小苏打糊 清理重油</span><span class="s3">污</span></span></h3>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><strong>方法：</strong>将小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打和水按照 2:1 的比例倒入容器中，用勺子</span><span class="s3">搅</span><span class="s1">拌直至</span><span class="s3">变</span><span class="s1">成糊状。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"> 这种小苏打糊质地</span><span class="s3">较</span><span class="s1">厚，容易粘附</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">垢，因此具有很强的分解力。同时利用它的研磨特性，重脏</span><span class="s3">污颗</span><span class="s1">粒就容易脱落。适合清洁厨房的排气扇、烤箱、空气炸锅或微波炉等具有较重油</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">的厨具。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13604735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13604735" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13604735-shutterstock_749593555-e1645790166604.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13604735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13604735-shutterstock_749593555-600x399.jpg" alt="5种异味" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13604735" class="wp-caption-text"><span class="s1">小苏打糊质地</span><span class="s3">较</span><span class="s1">厚，可用来清洁重油污</span>。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 class="p3"><span style="color: #800000;"><span class="s1">妙用三、直接使用小苏打粉 研磨去</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">垢</span></span></h3>
<p class="p3"><strong>方法：</strong>用小苏打粉、水和海绵直接擦拭脏污的物品表面即可。</p>
<p class="p3"><strong><span class="s1">用途：</span></strong></p>
<p class="p3"><strong><span class="s4">1. 清洗茶渍<span class="Apple-converted-space">? </span></span></strong></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s4">器皿上的茶渍有时很难清理，加少量小</span><span class="s5">苏</span><span class="s4">打粉和水，然后用海绵</span><span class="s5">轻轻</span><span class="s4">擦拭茶渍，可将器皿上附着的</span><span class="s5">污渍</span><span class="s4">去除。<br />
</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14242346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14242346" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242346-shutterstock_433705303.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14242346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14242346-shutterstock_433705303-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14242346" class="wp-caption-text">加少量小苏打粉和水，用海绵轻轻擦拭茶渍，可将污渍去除。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p3"><strong><span class="s4">2. 清洁浴缸</span><span class="s5">污</span><span class="s4">垢</span></strong></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">浴缸上的粗糙</span><span class="s3">污</span><span class="s1">垢通常是洗澡后留下的身体皮脂。将小</span><span class="s3">苏</span><span class="s1">打粉涂在湿海</span><span class="s3">绵</span><span class="s1">上并擦洗浴缸内壁，</span><span class="s1">浴缸很快就会变回光滑状态，最后</span><span class="s3">轻轻</span><span class="s1">冲洗即可。</span></p>
