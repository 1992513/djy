<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳廉价航班计划将扩展至西北旅游城镇</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年05月11日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张小凡澳洲珀斯编译报导）西澳航空业的新生力量获得了慷慨的政府机票补贴，政府将重新引入廉价航班，以方便想要访问西北地区的游客。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据“</span><span lang="en-AU">WA Today</span><span lang="zh-TW">”网站报导，西澳州的廉价航班计划已经进入第六年，使用纳税人的钱大幅补贴从珀斯到主要旅游城镇如</span><span lang="en-AU">Broome</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-AU">Kununurra</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-AU">Exmouth</span><span lang="zh-TW">的机票。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在某些情况下，补贴可以将单程机票价格降低</span><span lang="en-AU">400</span><span lang="zh-TW">至</span><span lang="en-AU">500</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">首次，这些补贴将延伸至由偏远地区航空公司</span><span lang="en-AU">Airnorth</span><span lang="zh-TW">和西澳州小型航空业的新参与者</span><span lang="en-AU">Nexus</span><span lang="zh-TW">运营的</span><span lang="en-AU">Broome</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-AU">Kununurra</span><span lang="zh-TW">之间的航班。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">去年从珀斯到西北地区的航班共售出近</span><span lang="en-AU">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-AU">9000</span><span lang="zh-TW">张机票，州政府希望今年更多，为即将到来的旱季提供</span><span lang="en-AU">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-AU">5600</span><span lang="zh-TW">张单程航班机票。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">今年的计划将耗资</span><span lang="en-AU">330</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">偏远地区发展厅长庞奇（</span><span lang="en-AU">Don Punch</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，航班将有助于推动</span><span lang="en-AU">Kimberley</span><span lang="zh-TW">地区在去年洪水后的复苏。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“通过吸引更多游客到</span><span lang="en-AU">Kimberley</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-AU">Gascoyne</span><span lang="zh-TW">地区，我们的目标是促进偏远地区经济增长，增强偏远地区社区的活力，并提升我们充满活力的偏远地区中心的知名度。”他说。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据</span><span lang="en-AU">2024</span><span lang="zh-TW">年的廉价航班计划，维珍澳洲将提供</span><span lang="en-AU">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">万零</span><span lang="en-AU">316</span><span lang="zh-TW">张折扣单程机票，价格为</span><span lang="en-AU">219</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，适用于珀斯和</span><span lang="en-AU">Broome</span><span lang="zh-TW">之间的航班，直到</span><span lang="en-AU">11</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">25</span><span lang="zh-TW">日，以及价格为</span><span lang="en-AU">259</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元的珀斯和</span><span lang="en-AU">Kununurra</span><span lang="zh-TW">之间的航班，直到</span><span lang="en-AU">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">6</span><span lang="zh-TW">日。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">维珍的网站上已经应用了补贴机票，但“</span><span lang="en-AU">WA Today</span><span lang="zh-TW">”发现有些机票从珀斯到</span><span lang="en-AU">Kununurra</span><span lang="zh-TW">的价格高达</span><span lang="en-AU">750</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">Qantas</span><span lang="zh-TW">将提供</span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-TW">万零</span><span lang="en-AU">480</span><span lang="zh-TW">张价格在</span><span lang="en-AU">199</span><span lang="zh-TW">至</span><span lang="en-AU">209</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元之间的折扣单程机票，适用于</span><span lang="en-AU">5</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-TW">日至</span><span lang="en-AU">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">31</span><span lang="zh-TW">日的航班。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">Airnorth Airlines</span>和<span lang="en-AU">Nexus Airlines</span>一共将售出<span lang="en-AU">4880</span>张折扣机票，价格为<span lang="en-AU">199</span>澳元的<span lang="en-AU">Broome</span>和<span lang="en-AU">Kununurra</span>之间的单程航班，适用于<span lang="en-AU">5</span>月<span lang="en-AU">1</span>日至<span lang="en-AU">8</span>月<span lang="en-AU">31</span>日。</p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">Nexus</span><span lang="zh-TW">去年</span><span lang="en-AU">7</span><span lang="zh-TW">月开始在北部地区运营。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">旅游厅长萨菲奥蒂（</span><span lang="en-AU">Rita Saffioti</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，该计划有可能为旅行者节省数百澳元，同时带领人们前往西澳大利亚令人难以置信的西北地区，并支持那里的旅游经营者。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">她表示，该计划基于另一个生活成本措施——偏远地区航空票价封顶计划——该计划将西澳大利亚偏远地区居民来往珀斯的机票价格限制在</span><span lang="en-AU">199</span><span lang="zh-TW">至</span><span lang="en-AU">299</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元之间。</span></p>
