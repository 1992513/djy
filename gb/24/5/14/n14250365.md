<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：第77届戛纳电影节开幕 众星闪耀</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250495-GettyImages-2152956407-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>当地时间5月14日，第77届戛纳电影节开幕电影《第二幕》（The Second Act）剧组主创人员在庆宫登上红毯。(Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年05月15日讯】（大纪元记者佟亦加报导）当地时间5月14日晚，第77届戛纳（坎城）电影节开幕。梅丽尔·斯特里普（Meryl Streep）、蕾雅·赛杜（Léa Seydoux）、格蕾塔·葛韦格（Greta Gerwig）、莉莉·格莱斯顿（Lily Gladstone），以及巩俐、熊黛林、关晓彤、赵涛、管虎、梁静、佟丽娅、彭于晏等一众华裔明星在庆宫亮相红毯。</p>
<p>现年74岁的美国影星、奥斯卡奖得主梅丽尔·斯特里普，获颁本届电影节“荣誉金棕榈奖”。斯特里普与法国著名影星朱丽叶·比诺什（Juliette Binoche）共同宣布本届电影节开幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14250453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250453" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250453-GettyImages-2152939165-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250453-GettyImages-2152939165-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250453" class="wp-caption-text">美国知名影星梅丽尔·斯特里普（Meryl Streep）获颁“荣誉金棕榈奖”。(Andreas Rentz/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14250455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250455" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250455-GettyImages-2152939489-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250455-GettyImages-2152939489-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250455" class="wp-caption-text">法国著名影星朱丽叶·比诺什（Juliette Binoche）走上红毯。(Andreas Rentz/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>本届戛纳电影节主竞赛单元评委以及开幕电影《第二幕》剧组主创人员等电影业界人士亮相电影节开幕典礼红毯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14250476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250476" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250476-GettyImages-2152931009-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250476-GettyImages-2152931009-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250476" class="wp-caption-text">担任本届戛纳电影节评委会主席的格蕾塔·葛韦格（Greta Gerwig）登上红毯。(Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14250458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250458" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250458-GettyImages-2152931490-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250458-GettyImages-2152931490-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250458" class="wp-caption-text">主演《第二幕》（The Second Act）的法国当红影星蕾雅·赛杜（Léa Seydoux）走上红毯。(Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>法国导演昆汀·杜皮约（Quentin Dupieux）执导的《第二幕》（The Second Act）被选为开幕片，该片由法国当红影星蕾雅·赛杜主演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14250465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250465" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250465-GettyImages-2152946257-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250465-GettyImages-2152946257-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250465" class="wp-caption-text">开幕片《第二幕》（The Second Act）剧组亮相红毯。(Kristy Sparow/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>本届电影节海报展现的是电影《八月狂想曲》中的镜头，该片是日本电影大师黑泽明的晚期作品，曾代表日本参加第64届奥斯卡金像奖最佳外语片奖角逐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14250468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250468" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250468-GettyImages-2152930245-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250468-GettyImages-2152930245-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250468" class="wp-caption-text">华人影星巩俐亮相红毯。(Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>和外籍丈夫常居法国的华人影星巩俐，此次是第20次登上戛纳红毯。她以一身粉色镶钻拖地礼裙闪耀全场，备受瞩目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14250470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250470" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250470-GettyImages-2152926785-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250470-GettyImages-2152926785-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250470" class="wp-caption-text">大陆女星赵涛亮相红毯。(Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>有22部影片入围本届电影节主竞赛单元，在华语圈，贾樟柯的新片《风流一代》入围，该片由赵涛主演。</p>
<p>此外，由陈可辛导演，章子怡、雷佳音主演的《酱园弄》入围本届戛纳电影节“非竞赛单元”；由管虎导演，彭于晏、佟丽娅主演的电影《狗阵》入围本届电影节“一种关注”单元。由郑保瑞执导，古天乐、林峯等主演的《九龙城寨之围城》入选电影节的“午夜展映”单元。娄烨作品《一部未完成的电影》入选电影节“特别展映”单元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14250474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250474" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250474-GettyImages-2152938147-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250474-GettyImages-2152938147-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250474" class="wp-caption-text">大陆女星关晓彤亮相红毯。(Victor Boyko/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>因执导《芭比》（Barbie）创造票房佳绩的女导演格蕾塔·葛韦格成为本届戛纳电影节评委会主席。新一波的“#MeToo运动”，给本届戛纳电影节抹上了特殊色彩，在“5男4女”共9名的其他8名评审团成员中，包括日本著名导演是枝裕和与法国女星伊娃·格林（Eva Ga?lle Green）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14250484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14250484" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250484-GettyImages-2152932724-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14250484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14250484-GettyImages-2152932724-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14250484" class="wp-caption-text">担任本次电影节司仪的法国女演员卡蜜尔·科坦（Camille Cottin）。(Andreas Rentz/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>法国女演员卡蜜尔·科坦 (Camille Cottin) 被选为本次电影节的司仪，主持开幕式和闭幕式。为期12天的第77届届戛纳电影节将于5月25日闭幕。</p>
