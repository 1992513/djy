<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>首届新英格兰香港节开办 萨莫维尔迎人潮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255111-DJY06535-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>首届新英格兰香港节5月19日在萨莫维尔市开办，迎来庞大人潮。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年05月22日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿萨默维尔市报导）5月19日傍晚，麻州萨默维尔市（Somerville）联合广场（Union Square Plaza）迎来摩肩接踵的人群。这是首届“新英格兰香港节”，从当天下午4点至8点，吸引了大批游客参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14255116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255116" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255116-DSC02215.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255116" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255116-DSC02215-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255116" class="wp-caption-text">首届新英格兰香港节5月19日在萨莫维尔市开办，迎来庞大人潮。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14255112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255112" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255112-DJY06562.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255112-DJY06562-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255112" class="wp-caption-text">表演嘉宾演唱粤语歌曲。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14255114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255114" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255114-DSC02197.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255114-DSC02197-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255114" class="wp-caption-text">表演舞台下聚集了众多观众。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">节日中的多元文化表演、互动游戏和各式各样的香港美食摊位，让参与者亲身体验到香港的传统与现代面貌。这场节庆从传统舞狮、武术开始，接下来还有各种粤语歌、粤曲表演，为观众提供了一个深入了解香港文化的机会。此外，现场还有如波士顿台湾影展协会、“Boston One”龙舟队等其它文化摊位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14255110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255110" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255110-DJY06521.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255110-DJY06521-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255110" class="wp-caption-text">首届新英格兰香港节的香港士多摊位。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14255106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255106-DJY06482.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255106-DJY06482-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255106" class="wp-caption-text">首届新英格兰香港节的香港商品摊位。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14255113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255113" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255113-DSC02195.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255113-DSC02195-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255113" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿台湾影展协会共襄盛举。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">此次活动由萨默维尔艺术委员会（Somerville Arts Council）与多方合作伙伴一同主办，在展示香港文化的同时，也促进了当地社区的文化交流。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">萨默维尔市长巴兰坦（Katjana Ballantyne）亦派代表出席了活动，在开场时强调了萨默维尔市支持多元文化活动的重要性，并对能在该市举办如此具有文化意义的活动感到自豪。市长不仅表达了对主办方的感谢，也展望了未来城市将持续支持类似活动，以增强社区的多样性和包容性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14255104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255104" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255104-DJY06439.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255104-DJY06439-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255104" class="wp-caption-text">萨莫维尔市移民办公室是主办方之一。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14255107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255107" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255107-DJY06492.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255107-DJY06492-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255107" class="wp-caption-text">首届新英格兰香港节的工艺品摊位。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14255109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255109-DJY06519.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255109-DJY06519-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255109" class="wp-caption-text">现场也有急救指导。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">首届“新英格兰香港节”负责人Natalie说，希望透过这个活动聚集社区中的香港人，共同庆祝和推广香港文化。她亦感谢萨莫维尔市政府、移民办公室及艺术委员会的支持。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Natalie表示，这次活动不仅是为了香港人，也旨在为所有讲广东话的人、当地华人、萨默维尔居民、亚裔，以及所有对亚洲文化感兴趣的人提供一个交流平台。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">她强调，5月是亚太裔传统月（AAPI Heritage Month），这个活动正好可以作为庆祝的一部分，增加亚裔群体间的联系和亲切感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14255115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255115" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255115-DSC02207.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255115-DSC02207-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255115" class="wp-caption-text">“Boston One”龙舟队带来龙舟模拟器，给大家体验。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14255105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255105-DJY06480.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255105-DJY06480-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255105" class="wp-caption-text">首届新英格兰香港节的香港跳蚤市场摊位。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14255126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14255126" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255126-DSC02232.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14255126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14255126-DSC02232-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14255126" class="wp-caption-text">现场的“飞行棋”游戏得到很多人喜爱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这次新英格兰香港节原定于5月18日举办，但因为雨天而延迟了一天。随着活动的圆满结束，组织者和参与者都期待，这个香港节成为每年都会回归的文化节庆。◇</p>
