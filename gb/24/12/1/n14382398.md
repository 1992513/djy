<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】冰糖南瓜盅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382401-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冰糖南瓜盅容易料理，好吃又有营养。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>南瓜和冰糖都具有养生功效。买到小南瓜可以当成容器，填入蔗糖味浓郁的黄冰糖蒸熟，南瓜肉搭配糖水享用，好吃又有营养。这里用嫩南瓜，甜度低，所以用50克冰糖，若用甜南瓜可减少糖量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382400" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382400-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382400-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382400" class="wp-caption-text">冰糖南瓜盅所用食材。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1个小南瓜，400克</p>
<p>50克黄冰糖</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382399" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382399-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382399-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382399" class="wp-caption-text">蒸好后，冰糖在南瓜里化成糖水。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>料理步骤：</strong>洗净的南瓜顶部切成盖子，挖空瓜瓤，放入冰糖，盖上南瓜盖子。南瓜放入深边小碗，入蒸锅中小火蒸60分钟，轻捏一下南瓜感觉很软，并且冰糖融化就好了。蒸好关火，等几分钟再出锅。</p>
