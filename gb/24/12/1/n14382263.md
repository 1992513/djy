<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>屏东点灯 2024最缤纷的圣诞童话盛事！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382267-727727-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>屏东圣诞节正式拉开序幕，整座城市被点亮成为童话中的梦幻欧洲小镇。（黄荣群提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）每年年底，屏东总能带来令人眼睛一亮的惊喜，今年也不例外！2024屏东圣诞节正式拉开序幕，整座城市被点亮成为童话中的梦幻欧洲小镇。缤纷的灯饰、浪漫的飘雪以及欢乐的市集活动，让这里成为南台湾冬季最具代表性的节庆盛事。这场活动不仅吸引无数游客到场，更让许多摄影爱好者大展身手，其中就包括知名摄影家——黄荣群，他的作品完美捕捉了这场盛会的欢乐气氛，让人仿佛亲临现场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382264" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382264-727724.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382264-727724-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382264" class="wp-caption-text">屏东点灯，2024最缤纷的圣诞童话盛事开始了！（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>童话中的圣诞屋　摄影师黄荣群的视角</h2>
<p>主灯秀“圣诞童话屋”是今年活动的焦点之一，由两层楼的姜饼屋为造型，外墙被鲜亮的黄色和白色糖霜覆盖，搭配屋顶的绿色花环与灯饰，如同梦中的圣诞村。每晚定时启动的咕咕钟音乐展演，更让游客流连忘返。</p>
<p>黄荣群捕捉到的“圣诞童话屋”照片，色彩鲜明、构图精致。他特别提到：“这座姜饼屋环绕着圣诞树，真的好梦幻！今年灯饰的设计比以往更加细致，拍照起来特别有故事感。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382266-727726.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382266-727726-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382266" class="wp-caption-text">黄荣群捕捉到的“圣诞童话屋”照片，色彩鲜明、构图精致。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14382265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382265-727725.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382265-727725-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382265" class="wp-caption-text">每晚定时启动的咕咕钟音乐展演，更让游客流连忘返。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>18组梦幻灯饰　怎么拍都好看！</h2>
<p>屏东圣诞节今年以“欧风童话”为主题设计，从屏东火车站到屏东公园，共有18组主题灯饰，让游客一拍再拍。其中，除了“圣诞童话屋”，“愿望树”和“松鼠公爵城堡”也是必看亮点。</p>
<p>愿望树：自然树木装点成圣诞树，搭配浪漫的灯光效果，让人忍不住在树下许愿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382274-727734.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382274-727734-600x899.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="899" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382274" class="wp-caption-text">愿望树：自然树木装点成圣诞树，搭配浪漫的灯光效果，让人忍不住在树下许愿。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14382275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382275" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382275-727736.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382275-727736-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382275" class="wp-caption-text">黄荣群：“今年灯饰的设计比以往更加细致，拍照起来特别有故事感。”（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>松鼠公爵城堡：可爱的松鼠公爵与公爵夫人随着音乐舞动，为现场注入更多活力。</p>
<p>夜晚时分，整座城市被幸福的灯光包围，每一个角落都散发着浓浓的节庆气息，成为拍照打卡的绝佳场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382269" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382269-727729.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382269-727729-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382269" class="wp-caption-text">夜晚时分，屏东火车站附近被幸福的灯光包围。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14382276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382276-727743.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382276-727743-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382276" class="wp-caption-text">屏东圣诞节今年以“欧风童话”为主题设计。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>亮灯秀与飘雪奇景　打造童话般的冬季夜晚</h2>
<p>除了静态的灯饰，屏东圣诞节的亮灯秀也是一大亮点！每晚6点，圣诞童话屋、奇幻梦游、松鼠公爵城堡等主题区域依次展开灯光演出。尤其是在屏东公园的耶林大道，随着灯光闪烁，雪花缓缓飘落，让人仿佛置身北国的梦幻场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382268" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382268-727728.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382268-727728-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382268" class="wp-caption-text">从屏东火车站到屏东公园，共有18组主题灯饰，让游客一拍再拍。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>不可错过的活动：拍贴机、圣诞市集、欢乐游行</h2>
<p>活动现场更设有多项互动体验：</p>
<p>圣诞拍贴机：免费拍摄的圣诞限定贴纸照，成为游客最珍贵的回忆之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382271" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382271-727731.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382271-727731-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382271" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞拍贴机：免费拍摄的圣诞限定贴纸照，成为游客最珍贵的回忆之一。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>圣诞市集村：贩售异国美食、圣诞甜点及创意小物，满足视觉与味觉的双重享受。</p>
<p>欢乐游行派对：圣诞老人带着小精灵一起热舞，现场还发放惊喜小礼物，充满欢乐氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382270" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382270-727730.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382270-727730-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382270" class="wp-caption-text">屏东点灯活动，很有圣诞节氛围。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>浪漫40天　来屏东过一场难忘的圣诞节！</h2>
<p>2024屏东圣诞节从即日起至2025年1月1日，每天傍晚5点半到晚上10点点灯。这场持续40天的盛会，将屏东打造为南国最温暖的节庆小镇。不论是与家人共享天伦之乐，还是与朋友、伴侣一同拍照打卡，这里都能满足你对圣诞的所有幻想。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382272" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382272-727732.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14382272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382272-727732-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382272" class="wp-caption-text">2024屏东圣诞节从即日起至2025年1月1日，每天傍晚5点半到晚上10点点灯。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄荣群表示：“这样的活动不仅让屏东充满活力，也为摄影提供了最好的题材。只要带上相机或手机，就能轻松捕捉满满的欢乐时光。”</p>

<h2>屏东圣诞节，等你来点亮心中的快乐！</h2>
<p>无论是梦幻的灯饰、精彩的演出，还是温馨的市集，2024屏东圣诞节绝对值得一游。带上家人朋友，一起来屏东点亮心中的快乐吧！</p>
