<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花莲的冬日诗篇 陈明忠用镜头记录太平洋之美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382358-727810-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>花莲的冬日诗篇。（陈明忠提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）冬日的花莲，东海岸线在晨曦中轻轻苏醒。这里的自然风光宛如一幅幅活生生的画布，每一处景点都展现出四季更迭的灵动之美。作为花莲在地的资深摄影师，陈明忠以手机镜头捕捉这片土地的灵魂，将石梯坪、石门与矶崎等景点转化为一幅幅震撼心灵的影像。他的作品不仅仅是影像纪录，更是对自然的深情告白。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382359" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382359" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382359-727811.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382359" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382359-727811-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382359" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠以镜头捕捉这片土地的灵魂，将石梯坪、石门与矶崎等景点转化为一幅幅震撼心灵的影像。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>石梯坪：壮观的地质与海岸生态</h2>
<p>石梯坪被誉为“世界级的户外地质教室”，其壮观的单面山与独特的海蚀壶穴，构成了台湾东海岸无可比拟的自然景观。12月1日的清晨，陈明忠选择在石梯坪拍摄。镜头下，岩石表面的纵向纹理清晰可见，仿佛记录着数百万年的地质变迁。潮间带的池水静如镜面，倒映着灰蓝的天空与绵延的山峦，静谧而神秘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382360" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382360-727812.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382360-727812-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382360" class="wp-caption-text">12月1日的清晨，陈明忠选择在石梯坪拍摄。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冬天的东北季风带来强劲的海浪，拍打在岩岸上激起飞溅的浪花，为石梯坪增添了生命的律动。海蚀壶穴的存在更显现出海浪数万年来的雕琢痕迹，陈明忠形容这里是“时间与自然的对话场域”，让人感受地球的伟大与自身的渺小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382361" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382361" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382361-727813.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382361-727813-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382361" class="wp-caption-text">冬天的东北季风带来强劲的海浪，拍打在岩岸上激起飞溅的浪花。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>石门：静谧的避世之地</h2>
<p>位于台11线的石门，以其规模虽小但独具特色海蚀洞，吸引游客前来拍照。陈明忠以低角度拍摄，捕捉海浪，他说，这样的拍摄不仅能展现洞穴的结构，也能呈现光影变化带来的艺术效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382362" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382362-727814.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382362-727814-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382362" class="wp-caption-text">位于台11线的石门，以其规模虽小但独具特色海蚀洞，吸引游客前来拍照。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冬天的石门，游人稀少，适合沉浸于自然的静谧之中。陈明忠指出，清晨是最佳拍摄时间，因为阳光的角度能凸显岩壁的层次与纹理。对于热爱拍摄自然风光的他而言，石门是一处能让人完全放松心灵的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382363" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382363" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382363-727815.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382363" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382363-727815-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382363" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠指出，清晨是最佳拍摄时间，因为阳光的角度能凸显岩壁的层次与纹理。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>矶崎：潮间带与冬季风光的结合</h2>
<p>矶崎的亮点在于大石鼻山步道，登顶后即可俯瞰太平洋的壮丽景色。陈明忠形容，这里是“海岸山脉与海洋的交会点”，冬季的风更增添了一份豪迈的气息。拍摄时，他特别着重于捕捉山势的线条与海天相连的画面，展现矶崎独有的地理风貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382364" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382364-727816.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382364-727816-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382364" class="wp-caption-text">矶崎：潮间带与冬季风光的结合。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>矶崎的潮间带在冬季展现出另一种面貌，海浪与潮池的互动营造出动静相宜的景象。陈明忠喜爱拍摄浪花拍打岩石的一刻，将大自然的力量凝固于瞬间。他说：“这是大自然最原始的样貌，也是人类永远无法完全掌握的美。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382365" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382365-727817.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382365" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382365-727817-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382365" class="wp-caption-text">他说：“大自然最原始的样貌，也是人类永远无法完全掌握的美。”（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>冬季海浪的挑战　安全与创作并重</h2>
<p>冬季海浪强劲，对摄影师而言既是挑战也是机遇。陈明忠分享，拍摄东海岸时需特别注意安全，避免过于靠近海岸边缘。他通常会选择相对稳定的地点，并使用手机镜头捕捉远处的浪花，以兼顾创作与安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382366" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382366-727818.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382366" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382366-727818-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382366" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠分享，拍摄东海岸时需特别注意安全，避免过于靠近海岸边缘。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>面对冬季的海浪，陈明忠深有感触：“自然的力量令人敬畏，我们能做的，是以谦卑的态度记录下这一切。”他的作品不仅展现了东海岸的冬日之美，也提醒人们珍惜这片土地的珍贵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382367" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382367-727819.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382367-727819-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382367" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠深有感触：“自然的力量令人敬畏，我们能做的，是以谦卑的态度记录下这一切。”（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>用影像传递自然之美</h2>
<p>从石梯坪到石门，再到矶崎，陈明忠以镜头串联起花莲东海岸的壮丽景观。他的作品不仅是一种艺术创作，更是一种环保理念的传递。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382368" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382368-727820.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382368-727820-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382368" class="wp-caption-text">从石梯坪到石门，再到矶崎，陈明忠以镜头串联起花莲东海岸的壮丽景观。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他希望透过影像，唤起更多人对自然的关注与珍视。这片土地的每一寸，都值得被用心记录、细细体会。正如他所说：“自然的美无需修饰，只有亲临其境，才能感受到那份真实与震撼。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14382369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14382369" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382369-727821.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14382369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14382369-727821-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14382369" class="wp-caption-text">花莲日出，陈明忠说：“自然的美无需修饰。”（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
