<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2024竹南蛇窑驻村艺术家 Ruby Yeh陶艺之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385946-729115-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>Ruby Yeh表示，深受马可奥理略的沉思录影响，将刚性与柔性，理性与感性融合，以蛇窑的独特高温柴烧、不施釉与柴灰的特质，赋予每件作品的独特性，获取新的种子。感谢竹南蛇窑为我办第一次个展。（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月06日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）2024年竹南蛇窑驻村陶艺家是来自南半球澳洲的Ruby Yeh，她有着与台湾紧密相连的关系，出身于台湾11岁移民澳洲，在澳洲多元文化的背景成长，使她拥有极其开放的心胸与视野，游历21国，于日本、昆士兰、韩国、美国创作用“土”交流，近年来于澳洲各项展览与奖项展露头角。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385947-729116-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14385947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385947-729116-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385947" class="wp-caption-text">获选“北昆士兰陶瓷奖”(2024 North Queensland Ceramic Awards)首奖！（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14385948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385948-729117-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14385948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385948-729117-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385948" class="wp-caption-text">Ruby Yeh 在驻村将竹南蛇窑的高温柴烧，不施釉，柴灰的特质熔入来展现她的理想作品，赋予每件作品一种有独特生命。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14385954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385954" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385954-729123-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14385954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385954-729123-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385954" class="wp-caption-text">Ruby Yeh 淘醉于陶艺创作乐趣。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2024年竹南蛇窑驻村陶艺家是来自南半球澳洲的Ruby Yeh，她有着与台湾紧密相连的关系，出身于台湾11岁移民澳洲，在澳洲多元文化的背景成长，使她拥有极其开放的心胸与视野，游历21国，于日本、昆士兰、韩国、美国创作用“土”交流，近年来于澳洲各项展览与奖项展露头角。同时令人非常开心的是在她启程当天，得知获选“北昆士兰陶瓷奖”(2024 North Queensland Ceramic Awards)首奖！这是澳洲颇负盛名且行之有年的陶瓷艺术比赛，竹南蛇窑深感荣幸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385955" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385955-729124-450x675.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14385955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385955-729124-450x675.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385955" class="wp-caption-text">Ruby Yeh 叶聿儒的陶艺生涯中的第一个个展，主要以重回台湾后深入体感人与人、文化之间的冲击，所发展出不同以往的作品风格及面向。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14385949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385949" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385949-729118-300x450.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14385949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385949-729118-300x450.jpeg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385949" class="wp-caption-text">驻村一个半月所创作的新作品，深入体感人与人、文化之间的冲击，所发展出不同以往的作品风格及面向。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
