<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>富里农会稻草艺术季活动 非洲大草原之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399978-734902-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025年富里农会稻草艺术季活动开跑了。（富里乡农会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）你有想过，稻草也能带你穿越时空，来到非洲大草原吗？在富里乡农会举办的第六届稻草艺术季中，这些看似简单的稻草被赋予了全新的生命。活动以“动物迷踪—非洲大草原”为主题，汇聚了来自社区居民、学校与艺术家的创意，让大人小孩都能在自然与艺术的结合中找到惊喜与乐趣！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399979" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399979-734903.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399979-734903-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399979" class="wp-caption-text">第六届稻草艺术季以非洲大草原为主题。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>创意满分的稻草装置艺术</h2>
<p>活动现场，一件件稻草艺术作品栩栩如生，仿佛让人置身于非洲草原。参观者不禁惊叹：这真的是稻草做的吗？让我们来看看几件不可错过的作品吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399984" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399984-734909.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399984-734909-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399984" class="wp-caption-text">所有稻草艺术作品皆使用稻秆、竹子及漂流木等自然材料制作。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狮子王的荣耀：由富南社区打造的稻草狮子，不仅威风凛凛，还以象征非洲霸主的姿态坐镇展区，成为打卡热点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399980" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399980-734904.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399980-734904-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399980" class="wp-caption-text">富南社区的狮子，不仅威风凛凛，还以象征非洲霸主的姿态坐镇展区，成为打卡热点。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饥饿的鳄鱼：明里社区的作品让人紧张又兴奋，栩栩如生的稻草鳄鱼仿佛下一秒就会扑向水面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399981" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399981-734905.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399981-734905-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399981" class="wp-caption-text">明里社区的作品让人紧张又兴奋，栩栩如生的稻草鳄鱼。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灰冠鹤之舞：富里国中以非洲特有的灰冠鹤为题，展现了稻草艺术的优雅之美。</p>
<p>非洲野水牛：永丰国小以纯朴的稻草和竹子创作出野水牛，坚毅的眼神象征着对大地丰收的期盼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399988" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399988-734913.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399988-734913-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399988" class="wp-caption-text">富里国中以非洲特有的灰冠鹤为题。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>河马家族：罗山社区创作的河马群充满童趣，适合一家大小拍照留念。</p>
<p>蛇鹫俯冲：大庒liuliu队精心打造的蛇鹫，展翅飞翔的英姿让人屏息凝视。</p>
<p>南非剑羚的骄傲：东里玉莲寺的剑羚雕塑让人感受到非洲动物的优雅与力量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399983-734908.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399983-734908-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399983" class="wp-caption-text">东里玉莲寺的剑羚雕塑让人感受到非洲动物的优雅与力量。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>环保与永续理念的实践</h2>
<p>富里乡农会此次活动不仅是视觉飨宴，更是一堂环保教育课。所有稻草艺术作品皆使用稻秆、竹子及漂流木等自然材料制作，展现资源再利用的可能性。即便是旧作品，也在修复后再次展出，诠释永续精神。展览结束后，所有材料将回收或自然分解，对环境零负担。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399991" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399991-734916.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399991-734916-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399991" class="wp-caption-text">可爱的水豚君。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>异国风情结合在地创意</h2>
<p>今年的主题“非洲大草原”令人联想到迪士尼的经典动画《狮子王》。然而，这些稻草动物并非来自非洲，而是出自“被种田耽误的艺术家”之手。他们用满腔热情和巧手，让每件作品活灵活现，仿佛能和观众对话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399985" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399985-734910.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399985-734910-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399985" class="wp-caption-text">今年的主题“非洲大草原”令人联想到迪士尼的经典动画《狮子王》。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>富里社区的大象：象征智慧与力量的大象作品，成为孩子们的最爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399986" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399986-734911.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399986-734911-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399986" class="wp-caption-text">富里社区的大象：象征智慧与力量的大象作品，成为孩子们的最爱。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吉拉米代部落的非洲鹰：这只展翅欲飞的海雕，象征自由与力量，让人深感震撼。</p>
<p>姆拉丁的斑马：充满趣味的斑马作品，带来轻松愉悦的氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399987" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399987-734912.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399987-734912-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399987" class="wp-caption-text">姆拉丁的斑马：充满趣味的斑马作品，带来轻松愉悦的氛围。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>天气冷也挡不住热情</h2>
<p>虽然活动期间天气湿冷，但游客的热情丝毫不减！无论是穿着厚外套，还是拿着相机捕捉瞬间，现场都洋溢着欢乐的氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399989" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399989-734914.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399989-734914-600x447.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399989" class="wp-caption-text">虽然活动期间天气湿冷，但游客的热情丝毫不减！（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一同参与 感受艺术的温度</h2>
<p>稻草艺术季不仅适合拍照打卡，更是一个与家人朋友共度美好时光的好地方。大家可以在艺术装置间穿梭，感受非洲大草原的野性之美，也能透过环保艺术，学习如何珍惜自然资源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14399990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14399990" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399990-734915.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14399990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14399990-734915-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14399990" class="wp-caption-text">稻草艺术季不仅适合拍照打卡，更是一个与家人朋友共度美好时光的好地方。（富里乡农会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论是带着孩子体验、和朋友打卡，还是自己来寻找创作灵感，这里都能找到属于你的惊喜！赶快来富里，感受这场稻草艺术的奇幻之旅吧！</p>
