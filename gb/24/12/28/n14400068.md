<h6>2025 Honda Odyssey Elite。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年12月28日讯】面对越来越多的7座位，甚至是8座位的SUV出现，市场上剩下的Minivan的选择不多，只有Kia Carnival、Chrysler Pacifica、Toyota Sienna及这周测试的Honda Odyssey。2025年款Honda Odyssey是车系的第五代产品，自1995年在北美推出第一代，至今已超过廿年。</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d27l6dsifacvoi.cloudfront.net/W6lJBA6Df"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/9b4MmTF_g4U?si=fpK3pd80sZiNMxmc" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>全新Odyssey的外型第一眼看上去跟旧有款式差别不大，都是有着庞大的身驱，市场上其他Minivan也是这个样子。当然，新车的前脸，尤其是头尾灯部分都是新设计，前车灯采用全LED灯，车身侧面加深了线条，车窗下方的镀铬线刻意在滑门后向下沉，也加强了视觉效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400069" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400069-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400069-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_04-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400069" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Honda Odyssey Elite。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025 Honda Odyssey只有一种引擎选择，3.5L自然吸气 i-VTEC V6引擎，压缩比为11.5：1，引擎的最大马力280 hp，扭矩262 lb/ft。变速箱统一采用10速自动变速箱，车内的变速控制是由Acura移值过来的变速按键，设在中控台下方，由上至下为P、R、N及D／S，按键大小各有不同，一目了然。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400070" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400070-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_17.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400070-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400070" class="wp-caption-text">3.5L i-VTEC V6引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在变速按键两旁是恒温控制，加热座椅开关等，音响与导航则全部浓缩在顶部9”萤幕内。中控台底部是一个巨大的置物空间，而在两前座中间，是另一个大型的储物箱，顶部是两个杯座及无线充电板，如Samsung手机及新iPhone都可使用。滑门开关仍在驾驶席的左方，与车尾电动闸门开关放在一起，安排合理。仪表板设计特别，半萤幕半仪表，左方是一个7” TFT彩屏，显示引擎转速、数字式车速、行车里数及平均耗油量等，右方则是实体的车速仪表。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400071" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400071" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400071-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_19.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400071" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400071-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_19-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400071" class="wp-caption-text">中控台顶部9”萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中排座位上方设有一个12.8” 显示屏，连接了Amazon FireTV，可在旅程中播放Netflix或YouTube串流影片，比以往使用光碟或HDMI更为方便，而且有无限的影片可观看。另有两个无线耳机，那么乘客玩游戏或看电影时，也不会影响驾驶者。新Odyssey也设有车内WiFi热点（Hot Spot），分享由AT&amp;T提供的4G上网，但要缴交月费的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400072" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400072" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400072-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_28.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400072" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400072-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_28-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400072" class="wp-caption-text">12.8” 显示屏连接了Amazon FireTV。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>电动尾闸可用遥控车匙或车内按键开关，也可在车后按尾闸上的按键开关，在开启时再按一下尾闸的按键可马上停止，防止尾闸碰撞上方东西。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400073" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400073-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_12.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400073-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_12-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400073" class="wp-caption-text">电动尾闸。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>CabinWatch是这一代Odyssey的新设计玩意，在第二排座位上方装有一个鱼眼摄像机，并在中控台萤幕上播放，那么驾驶者可清晰地看到第二及三排座位的乘客。虽然在旧有车款上的鱼眼镜仍在，但是它太过细小，看起来不清楚。此外，CabinWatch附有CabinTalk功能，就是车内广播，那么在外游时，驾驶者就可为作为导游，向车内乘客讲解名胜历史。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400075" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400075" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400075-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_29.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400075" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400075-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_29-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400075" class="wp-caption-text">鱼眼摄像机。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>试驾的Odyssey设有中排三座位及后排三座位，中排三座位均可拆除，如中排中间座位拆除后，左右两个Captain Seat都可向左右移动，所以Honda称之为“Magic Slide”，腾出空间让后排乘客上落。后排座位则以60/40分，可独立收纳至后方，成为平地台，增加载物空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400076" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400076-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_32.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400076-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_32-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400076" class="wp-caption-text">“Magic Slide”座位。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全方面，Odyssey设有Honda Sensing系统，包括一系列安全设备，例如碰撞缓解制动系统（Collision Mitigation Braking System）、车道保持辅助（Lane Keeping Assist）及自适应巡航控制（Adaptive Cruise Control ）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400077" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400077-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_27.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400077" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400077-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_27-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400077" class="wp-caption-text">Odyssey的内部设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，Honda Odyssey曾获得Ward&#8217;s杂志的十大最佳用家体验（Wards 10 Best UX）奖项，同时Odyssey车内的CabinWatch也获得了Popular Science杂志的最佳创新大奖（Best of What’s New）。 虽然感觉上Odyssey Elite乘客空间比较细小，但事实上，Odyssey的乘客空间达到160.1 cu ft，甚至比Toyota Sienna的159.7 cu ft更大。另外，CabinWatch及CabinTalk都是Odyssey独有设施，而且Magic Slide可使座位组合更多更灵活。耗油量虽不及对手Sienna的Hybrid系统，但3.5L 引擎动力表现更直接，加速更有力！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14400078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14400078" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400078-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_22.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14400078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14400078-2025_Honda_Odyssey_Elite_22-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14400078" class="wp-caption-text">手机充电板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
