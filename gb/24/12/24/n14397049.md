<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冬日鲤鱼潭的静谧与美学视角</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397050-733804-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冬日花莲鲤鱼潭的静谧。（陈明忠提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）位于花莲寿丰乡的鲤鱼潭，是花莲县内最大的内陆湖泊，湖水清澈见底，周围被群山环绕，宛如自然的静谧乐章。</p>
<p>摄影家陈明忠镜头下的鲤鱼潭，冬日景致更是展现出与其他季节截然不同的气质。无论是晨曦中的薄雾，还是夜幕降临的灯影点缀，每一帧画面都蕴含深刻的故事与情感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397059" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397059-733813.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397059-733813-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="597"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397059" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠拍到三只可爱的猴子。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>晨雾中的金山倒影 构图中的细腻思考</h2>
<p>
鲤鱼潭的冬日清晨，阳光透过薄雾洒向湖面，湖水如镜般映出周围群山的倒影。摄影家陈明忠描述道：“日照金山的美幻倒影，搭配浮云掠过山腰的壮阔，是大自然最静谧的舞台。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397052" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397052-733806.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397052-733806-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="597"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397052" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠巧妙运用光影变化，捕捉到金色阳光与蓝色湖水的对比。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他的作品中，透过低角度构图，将前景的湖面波纹与远处的山峦倒影结合，形成层次分明的画面。同时，他巧妙运用光影变化，捕捉到金色阳光与蓝色湖水的对比，赋予画面生命力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397057" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397057-733811.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397057" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397057-733811-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397057" class="wp-caption-text">低角度的取景可以更突出前景细节，增强画面的层次感。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>入夜的浪漫氛围 灯影与自然的交织</h2>
鲤鱼潭的夜晚，街灯的倒影如珠帘般投映在湖面上，勾勒出金色浪漫的线条。陈明忠分享：“隐约看见几只水鸟滑过水面，远处传来莫氏树蛙的交错缭亮竞唱，还有山羌的有力叫声，让人不忍入梦。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397053" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397053-733807.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397053-733807-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397053" class="wp-caption-text">鲤鱼潭的夜晚，街灯的倒影如珠帘般投映在湖面上。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在夜景摄影中，他利用长时间曝光技巧，将湖面的光影拉长，使灯光倒影呈现出梦幻般的柔和感。背景的远山与近处的湖岸灯火形成对比，增添了画面的深度与层次感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397054" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397054" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397054-733808.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397054-733808-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397054" class="wp-caption-text">背景保持柔和，形成动静相宜的对比。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>动态中的宁静 水鸟与湖面的谐和</h2>
陈明忠的作品中常见鲤鱼潭的水鸟与湖面互动的瞬间。拍摄时，他使用高速快门冻结水鸟飞行的瞬间，并将鸟儿置于画面的黄金比例点上，强调主题的同时，也保留了背景的广阔空间，让观者能感受到鲤鱼潭的自然与静谧共存的氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397055" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397055-733809.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397055-733809-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397055" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠说：“几只水鸟划过水面，带起细致的涟漪。”（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>四季皆美 冬日尤静</h2>
鲤鱼潭四季皆有不同的情境之美，但冬日的景致尤为迷人。陈明忠在谈及他的冬季摄影经验时提到：“虽然冷空气不够，没能如愿拍到湖面上的大雾，但一连三周的夜宿，让我更深入感受到这里的静美。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397061" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397061-733815.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397061-733815-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397061" class="wp-caption-text">天刚亮，水汽冲上天，好似仙境。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他的作品不仅展现了湖泊的自然景观，也通过构图与细节处理，让人仿佛置身其中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397056" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397056-733810.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397056-733810-600x818.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="615"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397056" class="wp-caption-text">在夜景拍摄中，运用长时间曝光拉长光影，使画面更具氛围感。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>摄影技巧分享 如何捕捉鲤鱼潭的静与动</h2>
光影的运用：利用清晨与黄昏的低光环境，捕捉湖面光影的变化，特别是倒影的细节。</p>
<p>长时间曝光：在夜景拍摄中，运用长时间曝光拉长光影，使画面更具氛围感。</p>
<p>动静结合：拍摄水鸟时，选择高速快门冻结动态，同时让背景保持柔和，形成动静相宜的对比。</p>
<p>低角度构图：低角度的取景可以更突出前景细节，增强画面的层次感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397057" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397057-733811.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397057" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397057-733811-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397057" class="wp-caption-text">低角度的取景可以更突出前景细节，增强画面的层次感。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>静谧中的感动</h2>
冬日的鲤鱼潭，透过摄影家陈明忠的镜头，将静谧与动态完美结合，展现了自然的多样性与情感深度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397060" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397060-733814.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397060-733814-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397060" class="wp-caption-text">黄色透亮的蝴蝶。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每一帧作品，既是对景色的真实描绘，也是对生命的深刻诠释。无论是晨雾中的金山倒影，还是夜幕降临的灯影交织，鲤鱼潭的冬景都让人难以忘怀，也为爱好摄影的人提供了无尽的灵感与挑战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397058" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397058-733812.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397058-733812-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397058" class="wp-caption-text">夜幕降临的灯影交织，鲤鱼潭的冬景都让人难以忘怀。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
