<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>落羽松之美 黄连兴与Nina的摄影对话</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397135-733845-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日月潭向山游客中心落羽松红了。（黄连兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）每年冬日，落羽松以其变化万千的色彩，吸引无数摄影爱好者前来捕捉这份自然的诗意。在今年的拍摄季节里，两位摄影大师——黄连兴与Nina——分别以其独特的视角与技巧，在台湾的不同落羽松秘境中留下了动人影像。他们的作品不仅呈现出落羽松的天然之美，也展现出摄影艺术的深厚功底。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397136" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397136-733846.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397136-733846-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397136" class="wp-caption-text">Nina以其独特的视角，为青埔落羽松留下动人影像。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>黄连兴：捕捉落羽松的多层次诗意</h2>
<p>
黄连兴以其细腻的构图和巧妙的光影运用闻名，这个冬季，他走访了多处落羽松秘境，包括云林林内农田水利文物陈列馆、林内九芎落羽松、虎尾青埔落羽松、南投日月潭向山游客中心，以及嘉义民雄大崎村。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397137-733847.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397137" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397137-733847-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397137" class="wp-caption-text">云林林内农田水利文物陈列馆的落羽松。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>光影交错的晨夕时刻</h2>
黄连兴特别热爱在晨光或夕阳的柔和光线下进行拍摄，因为这些时刻的光线角度低，能够突显落羽松的层次感。他的作品以树叶的金色和红色为主色调，透过光影的变化，营造出温暖而柔和的氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397140-733850.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397140-733850-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397140" class="wp-caption-text">黄连兴在水利博物馆，特别热爱在晨光下拍摄。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>水面的梦幻对称</h2>
黄连兴善于选择靠近水面的场景，利用水面倒影创造出对称的画面效果。例如，在日月潭向山游客中心，他将落羽松的倒影与蓝天白云巧妙融合，呈现出如梦似幻的视觉享受。正如他所言：“日月潭向山游客中心的落羽松今天最美，好在天气晴朗，拍起来特别有感觉。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397139-733849.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397139-733849-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397139" class="wp-caption-text">“向山游客中心的落羽松，今天最美，因为天气晴朗，拍起来特别有感觉。”黄连兴表示。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>引导视线的构图</h2>
在林内九芎落羽松道路，黄连兴巧妙地利用蜿蜒的步道与水渠作为引导线，将观者的视线自然引向画面深处，增添作品的故事性和层次感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397138-733848.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397138-733848-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397138" class="wp-caption-text">林内九芎落羽松秘境。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>俯瞰广角的壮观之美</h2>
黄连兴在嘉义民雄大崎村采用了俯拍和广角的手法，完整地捕捉整片落羽松林的壮丽景象。他利用高角度的视野，展现自然的广阔，让观者仿佛置身于大自然的怀抱中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397141" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397141" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397141-733851.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397141" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397141-733851-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397141" class="wp-caption-text">黄连兴在嘉义民雄大崎村采用了俯拍，和广角的手法拍摄。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>空拍达人Nina　兔宝与落羽松的生命之舞</h2>
与黄连兴的作品不同，Nina以她的创意性构图和对动物与自然的融合而闻名。她的落羽松摄影作品带有一种灵动的生命气息，而她的宠物兔宝更是其中的点睛之笔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397143-733853.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397143-733853-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397143" class="wp-caption-text">nina的作品带有一种灵动的生命气息，而宠物兔宝更是其中的点睛之笔。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina的作品中少不了她的宠物兔子。她将兔宝置于前景，与背景的落羽松林形成有趣的对比，营造出自然与生命的和谐之美。她笑言：“为了拍兔宝，我什么动作都可以，甚至还被侧拍笑说是在练腹肌！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397144" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397144-733854.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397144-733854-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397144" class="wp-caption-text">nina笑言：“为了拍兔宝，我什么动作都可以，甚至还被侧拍笑说是在练腹肌！”（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>低角度的视觉冲击</h2>
在云林虎尾青埔落羽松秘境，Nina善于采用低角度拍摄，接近水面，让落羽松的高度与倒影形成对称之美。这种视角使画面更具视觉冲击力，仿佛让观者能深入景中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397145-733855.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397145-733855-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397145" class="wp-caption-text">Nina善于采用低角度拍摄，接近水面，让落羽松的高度与倒影形成对称之美。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>框架式构图的画面引导</h2>
Nina喜欢利用垂挂的树叶作为自然框架，将观者的视线引导至画面深处。例如，她特别强调上午柔和的光线，利用逆光突显叶片的透明质感，使得整个画面充满诗意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397146-733856.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397146-733856-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397146" class="wp-caption-text">Nina喜欢利用垂挂的树叶作为自然框架，将观者的视线引导至画面深处。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>色彩的对比与细节的呈现</h2>
Nina善于捕捉多彩的自然色调，将水面的蓝绿与树叶的金红形成强烈对比，增强画面的吸引力。此外，她也注重细节，特写拍摄叶片的纹理与层次，让观者能感受到自然的精致之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397147-733857.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397147-733857-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397147" class="wp-caption-text">Nina善于捕捉多彩的自然色调，将水面的蓝绿与树叶的金红形成强烈对比。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管黄连兴与Nina的拍摄风格截然不同，但他们都展现了对落羽松的深厚情感与艺术表达力。黄连兴专注于场景的宏观壮丽与自然层次，而Nina则以生命力与创意取胜，两者共同为落羽松的冬日之美增添了更多可能性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397148-733858.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397148-733858-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397148" class="wp-caption-text">黄连兴专注于场景的宏观壮丽与自然层次。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论是黄连兴在云林林内九芎落羽松蜿蜒小径中的细腻构图，还是Nina在云林青埔落羽松秘境中兔宝与红叶的动人画面，这些作品都让人深刻感受到落羽松的独特魅力与摄影艺术的无限潜力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397149-733859.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14397149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397149-733859-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397149" class="wp-caption-text">摄影作品都让人深刻感受到落羽松的独特魅力，与摄影艺术的无限潜力。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
