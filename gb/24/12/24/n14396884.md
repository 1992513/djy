<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>慰劳员警辛劳 桃园分局长蔡启仁亲赠圣诞礼物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396886-733698-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园警察分局长蔡启仁24日率秘书室同仁，携带圣诞礼物，至各派出所等单位，亲自赠送给每位同仁。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月24日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）为嘉勉慰劳平日辛劳执行勤务保障社会治安的警察同仁，桃园警察分局长蔡启仁24日率秘书室同仁，携带圣诞礼物，至各派出所等单位，亲自赠送给每位同仁，祝福大家圣诞快乐，并叮咛同仁执勤注意安全第一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14396885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14396885" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396885-733697.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14396885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396885-733697.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14396885" class="wp-caption-text">桃园分局长蔡启仁警大57期行政警察学系，是位受尊敬的局长。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14396887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14396887" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396887-733699.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14396887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396887-733699.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14396887" class="wp-caption-text">蔡启仁的贴心祝福，令员警感到既温馨又高兴。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14396888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14396888" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396888-733700.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14396888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396888-733700.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14396888" class="wp-caption-text">员警纷纷与蔡启仁及圣诞老人合影留念，并互相祝福平安快乐。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同仁在圣诞节前夕收到了意外的惊喜，对于蔡启仁的贴心祝福感到既温馨又高兴，纷纷与蔡启仁及圣诞老人合影留念，并互相祝福平安快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14396889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14396889" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396889-733701.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14396889" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396889-733701.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14396889" class="wp-caption-text">员警纷纷与蔡启仁及圣诞老人合影留念，并互相祝福平安快乐。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14396890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14396890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396890-733702.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14396890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396890-733702.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14396890" class="wp-caption-text">员警纷纷与蔡启仁及圣诞老人合影留念，并互相祝福平安快乐。（桃园市桃园警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园分局长蔡启仁警大57期行政警察学系，曾历任航警局保安警察大队长、安全检查大队长、台北分局长、桃市龙潭及龟山分局长，是位受尊敬的局长。◇</p>
