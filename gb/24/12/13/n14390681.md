<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>士林官邸菊花展倒数二天 冬日赏菊的最后机会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390682-730914-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>士林官邸菊花展倒数三天。（nina提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月13日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）2024年的士林官邸菊花展正如火如荼进行中，倒数二天，空拍达人Nina分享了一系列精彩照片，将这场花卉盛会的魅力展现得淋漓尽致。今年的菊展以“菊伴动物派对”为主题，不仅有多达15万盆的菊花，更融合了动物元素及环保理念，带给参观者耳目一新的视觉享受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390683-730915.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390683-730915-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390683" class="wp-caption-text">今年的菊展以“菊伴动物派对”为主题。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina说：“出乎意料之外，花还蛮新鲜的耶！追上大立菊的尾巴，今年的大立菊最多有750朵，比起往年稍微逊色一点，但冬日赏菊，怡然自得。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390684-730916.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390684-730916-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390684" class="wp-caption-text">Nina说：“出乎意料之外，花还蛮新鲜的耶！追上大立菊的尾巴”。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>15万盆菊花与14个展区 盛大的花卉派对</h2>
<p>士林官邸每年都会推出主题花展，而今年的菊展结合了自然媒材与环境永续理念。整个士林官邸公园被妆点得宛如花卉天堂，呈现热带、温带、寒带与沙漠四大气候带的特色。展览期间免费开放户外庭园参观，吸引了大量游客流连忘返。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390685" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390685-730917.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390685-730917-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390685" class="wp-caption-text">整个士林官邸公园被妆点得宛如花卉天堂，呈现热带、温带、寒带与沙漠四大气候带的特色。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影作品中，最吸睛的莫过于入口处的“熊熊遇见鹿”，熊与鹿交错而立，宛如童话场景。另一大亮点则是“狐朋引伴~台日友好笠间菊花节”展区，3公尺高的菊花狐狸与鸟居神社，展现了台日文化交流的成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390686-730918.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390686-730918-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390686" class="wp-caption-text">另一大亮点则是“狐朋引伴~台日友好笠间菊花节”展区，3公尺高的菊花狐狸与鸟居神社。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>镜头中的环保理念与动物装置艺术</h2>
<p>Nina的镜头将14个展区的特色一一捕捉。她特别指出展览布置全数采用树木枝条、椰纤、竹子等天然媒材，展现环境永续的概念。她说道：“走在园区里，感觉像是陈姥姥进大观园，随处可见动物装置与菊花的完美融合，像是台湾黑熊、大象、麋鹿、孔雀等，都栩栩如生。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390687-730919.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390687-730919-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390687" class="wp-caption-text">nina说道：“走在园区里，感觉像是陈姥姥进大观园，随处可见动物装置与菊花的完美融合。”（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尤其是“拢来看菊”展区内的巨大伞蜥蜴装置艺术，吸引了不少游客驻足拍照，成为社群平台上的热门打卡点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390688" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390688-730920.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390688" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390688-730920-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390688" class="wp-caption-text">“拢来看菊”展区内的巨大伞蜥蜴装置艺术。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>寿菊、大立菊与悬崖菊的绝美排列</h2>
<p>Nina利用手机技术，完美捕捉了寿菊、大立菊与悬崖菊的排列之美。特别是大立菊展区，最大一株包含750朵菊花，气势非凡。她表示：“真的很壮观，尤其是悬崖菊的视觉效果，让人印象深刻。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390689-730921.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390689-730921-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390689" class="wp-caption-text">最大一株包含750朵菊花，气势非凡。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同时，她也在菊花丛中拍到了一只特别的黄尾鸟，为这次赏花之旅增添了意外的惊喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390690" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390690-730922.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390690" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390690-730922-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390690" class="wp-caption-text">拍到了一只特别的黄尾鸟，为这次赏花之旅增添了意外的惊喜。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>展区彩蛋 寻找隐藏的惊喜</h2>
<p>本次菊展的一大亮点是与日本动物原型大师朝隈俊男合作，将其创作的动物公仔放大并隐藏于花丛中。Nina提醒参观者：“别忘了仔细寻找这些俏皮的动物装置，像是小猪、大象和孔雀，藏身在各个角落，真的很有趣！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390691" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390691-730923.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390691" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390691-730923-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390691" class="wp-caption-text">Nina提醒参观者：“别忘了仔细寻找这些俏皮的动物装置，像是小猪、大象和孔雀，藏身在各个角落，真的很有趣！”（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>夜间延长开放 浪漫的赏花新体验</h2>
<p>今年首次将开放时间延长至晚上9点，让上班族与学生也能下班后享受夜间赏花的乐趣。夜晚的灯光搭配菊花，增添了许多浪漫氛围。尤其是展区内的光影效果，让菊花展更加迷人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390692-730924.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390692" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390692-730924-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390692" class="wp-caption-text">夜间延长开放，浪漫的赏花新体验。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>交通资讯与参观提醒</h2>
<p>士林官邸菊展交通便利，民众可搭乘捷运红线至“士林站”，步行约7分钟即可抵达。展览期间，户外庭园免费参观，而进入官邸内部则需购票，全票优惠价80元，学生与65岁以上长者仅需50元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390693" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390693" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390693-730925.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390693" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390693-730925-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390693" class="wp-caption-text">展览期间，户外庭园免费参观，而进入官邸内部则需购票。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina最后提醒大家，趁展览最后三天，带着家人朋友一起来士林官邸菊展，感受这场花卉与动物的派对吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390694" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390694" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390694-730927.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390694" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390694-730927-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390694" class="wp-caption-text">趁展览最后2天，带着家人朋友一起来士林官邸看菊展吧！（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
