<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>用手机拍微距—摄影家廖翠环的创作之路</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390655-730896-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>廖翠环能捕捉到夕阳的余晖。（廖翠环提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月13日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）摄影家廖翠环，虽然是一名家庭主妇，但对摄影的热情却丝毫不输专业摄影师。她的微距摄影之路刚刚开始，仅仅半个月的时间，便取得了令人惊叹的成果。谈到她为何突然投入微距摄影，廖翠环回忆说，这源于台南新建成的生态公园—哈赫拿尔森林。这座公园特别培育了青纹细蟌，第一次透过手机微距镜头观察这些蜻蜓时，她惊讶于青纹细蟌身体的构造与配色之精美，瞬间爱上了这种摄影形式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390644" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390644-730885.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14390644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390644-730885-600x660.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="494" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390644" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环惊讶于青纹细蟌身体的构造与配色之精美，瞬间爱上了这种摄影形式。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“青纹细蟌在微距镜头下，仿佛缩小版的龙虾，每个细节都清晰可见，让人叹为观止。”廖翠环说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390645" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390645-730886.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14390645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390645-730886-600x915.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="685" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390645" class="wp-caption-text">“青纹细蟌在微距镜头下，每个细节都清晰可见，让人叹为观止。”廖翠环说。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>初尝成果—昆虫的世界</h2>
<p>短短半个月的微距创作，廖翠环已经拍摄了多种昆虫：毛毛虫、蜜蜂、蝗虫等。她特别喜欢研究昆虫细微的特征。例如，毛毛虫那满身的细刺，让她理解了为何它爬过皮肤时会引起痒感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390646" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390646-730887.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14390646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390646-730887-600x859.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="646" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390646" class="wp-caption-text">短短半个月的微距创作，廖翠环已经拍摄了多种昆虫：毛毛虫、蜜蜂、蝗虫等。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我想要透过镜头研究，为什么毛毛虫这么令人害怕，却能蜕变成蝴蝶，这种生命的转变让人着迷。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390647-730888.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390647-730888-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390647" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环：“毛毛虫这么令人害怕，却能蜕变成蝴蝶，这种生命的转变让人着迷。”（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在她的作品中，有一张蜜蜂钻进空心菜花并沾满花粉的照片，获得朋友们的赞叹，甚至被用来当作小学生课堂的教学素材。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390662" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390662-730910.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14390662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390662-730910-600x728.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="547" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390662" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环的作品，蜜蜂钻进空心菜花并沾满花粉的照片，获得朋友们的赞叹。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>构图与光影的探索</h2>
<p>对于构图，廖翠环直言这全凭兴趣驱动。她笑称，拍微距的过程中，跪着、蹲着、甚至躺着，都是家常便饭。她特别分享了一张紫色花朵的照片，为了捕捉最佳角度，她跪在花圃中，利用早晨柔和的自然光线，让光影完美衬托出花朵的细节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390648" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390648-730889.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14390648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390648-730889-600x657.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="495" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390648" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环笑称，拍微距的过程中，跪着、蹲着、甚至躺着，都是家常便饭。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“手机让我随时随地捕捉瞬间，尤其在微距摄影上，方便性是无可替代的优势。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390649-730890.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390649-730890-600x509.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="509" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390649" class="wp-caption-text">“手机让我随时随地捕捉瞬间，尤其在微距摄影上，方便性是无可替代的优势。”廖翠环说。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>微距拍摄中的挑战与乐趣</h2>
<p>微距拍摄并非一帆风顺，需要克服许多挑战。例如，手机的稳定性对于捕捉细节至关重要。廖翠环强调，拍摄时几乎需要让镜头贴近拍摄物，稍有抖动便可能影响画质。她还提到，腰力与手部稳定性在微距摄影中至关重要。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390650" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390650-730891.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14390650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390650-730891-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390650" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环强调，拍摄时几乎需要让镜头贴近拍摄物，稍有抖动便可能影响画质。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有一次，她在哈赫拿尔森林拍摄时，意外发现一只伪装在叶片上的蝗虫。她形容蝗虫的外壳像战士的盔甲，充满力量感，这启发了她对昆虫世界更深层次的探索。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390651" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390651-730892.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14390651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390651-730892-600x859.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="646" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390651" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环形容蝗虫的外壳像战士的盔甲，充满力量感。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>与自然对话—作品背后的故事</h2>
<p>除了昆虫，廖翠环也将微距摄影延伸至其他主题。例如，她自己制作的巴斯克提拉米苏蛋糕，搭配自家花圃的绿意，创作出兼具美味与艺术感的作品。此外，她经常在附近的一处渔池拍摄水面倒影。这个地方成为她灵感的来源，清晨的日出与傍晚的夕阳映照在水面上，形成绝美的自然画卷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390652" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390652-730893.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14390652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390652-730893-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390652" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环也将微距摄影延伸至其他主题。例如，她自己制作的巴斯克提拉米苏蛋糕。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>那是廖翠环的秘密基地，位于台南一条不起眼的马路旁，那里有一个宁静的渔池，渔池上方坐落着一间简单的工寮，工寮旁边则长着两三棵树。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390654-730895.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390654-730895-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390654" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环的秘密基地，位于台南一条不起眼的马路旁，那里有一个宁静的渔池。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖翠环：“某天我骑着脚踏车路过这里，因为它离我家很近，便成了我经常停下来休息的地方。我喜欢坐在这里，看着平静的水面，欣赏工寮的倒影映入水中。当没有风的时候，水面像一面镜子，倒映得清晰如画。我每次骑车骑累了，就会在这里放松自己，看着眼前的美景。有时候，还能看到一群鸭子悠然地从水面划过，这一幕总让我感到无比幸福。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390653-730894.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390653-730894-600x467.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390653" class="wp-caption-text">当没有风的时候，水面像一面镜子，倒映得清晰如画。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个地方特别之处在于，它不仅拥有平静的美，还能随着时间展现不同的风采。清晨向东可以拍到日出的金光，而傍晚向西则能捕捉到夕阳的余晖。这里的每一刻，都让人感受到大自然的魅力。</p>
<p>“我希望透过镜头，将那些日常中容易被忽略的细节分享给大家，让更多人看到这个世界的美。” 廖翠环笑着说。</p>
<p>这是廖翠环-一位手机为创作工具的微距摄影家，带给我们的启发与感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14390661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14390661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390661-730902.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14390661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14390661-730902-600x630.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="630" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14390661" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环的缤纷花园。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
