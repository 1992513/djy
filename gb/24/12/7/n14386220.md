<h6>2025 Genesis G80 3.5T。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年12月07日讯】经过中期改款的Genesis G80，虽不是全新形象，但也给人新鲜感，尤其全新内部的27”宽屏萤幕，启动时及关掉引擎时的动画，都是非常震撼！</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d377tzsbzf1wvt.cloudfront.net/J7q5bnufu"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/kQEGMGwo6s8?si=GVuYAiWpPwoWZbNw" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>G80属中型四门轿车类别，然而体形总是像一辆全尺寸轿车，长度是近196.7”，车宽为78.5”。Genesis G80前脸的钻石形进风口栏栅，双线四头灯内藏多颗LED灯，翼徽一样的头灯设计伸延到车侧的方向灯，再到车尾的双线车尾灯。车轮则是标配20”轮圈及245/40R20（前）及275/35R20（后）四季轮胎。车身使用了更多的高强度钢和热冲压件，比上一代热冲压件多42%，所以G80的一体式结构抗拉强度比上一代G80增加6%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386240" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386240-20245_Genesis_G80_06-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386240-20245_Genesis_G80_06-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386240" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Genesis G80 3.5T。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14386228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386228" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386228-20245_Genesis_G80_08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386228-20245_Genesis_G80_08-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386228" class="wp-caption-text">钻石形进风口栏栅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>动力系统来自一副汽油双涡轮增压引擎，排汽量3.5L，采用V6排列，最高375 hp马力，而扭矩为391 lb-ft，标配四轮驱动，加速力很强，0-60 mph的加速只需4.9秒便可完成。搭配8速自动变速箱。汽油版Genesis G80 3.5T Sport Prestige会有四种不同的驾驶模式，分别为Comfort、Sport及My，另外Sport+是Sport Prestige版独有，足够应付各种路况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386230" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386230-20245_Genesis_G80_16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386230-20245_Genesis_G80_16-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386230" class="wp-caption-text">3.5L V6双涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>G80的内舱采用了柔软触感材料，白色皮革的座椅与内装非常清新高贵，再配上一些黑色饰板作点缀，并有不少磨沙金属车内部件，提高整体格调。G80采用变速盘，中间是“P”按键，顺时针转动是“D”档，而逆时针转动是“R”档，并配有Paddle Shift，可随时手动变换档数。方向盘改为三幅式设计，更有运动感，并且有二段式加热功能及四方向电动调节。Nappa皮革座椅，并有加热和通风功能，驾驶席更有三种不同的按摩程序，称为“Ergo Motion Driver Seat”。这次中期升级其中一亮点就是前台萤幕，尺寸达到27”的超宽萤幕，结合了仪表板及中控台萤幕的功能，而且在启动时及关掉引擎时，播出画面会用上整个萤幕，加上音响效果，非常震撼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386231" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386231-20245_Genesis_G80_29.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386231-20245_Genesis_G80_29-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386231" class="wp-caption-text">内舱采用了柔软触感材料。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>萤幕右方用作各种音响的控制，连接手机，导航系统及倒车摄像显示等。信息娱乐系统可连接Apple CarPlay或Android Auto，Sirius/XM卫星、HD收音机、导航系统、无线充电板和环视摄像头系统也是G80 3.5T Sport Prestige的标准配置，平视显示器同样也是标配装备。另外，Bang &amp; Olufsen音频系统也是标配。用作控制萤幕的“Infotainment Controls”圆盘，在之前测试的G80是一个平面的圆形，要转动外圈控制挺费劲，应该是Genesis也注意到，所以在这一辆2023年款G80 Electrified作出了改良，变成一个凸起的圆盘，使用上方使太多了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386232-20245_Genesis_G80_19.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386232-20245_Genesis_G80_19-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386232" class="wp-caption-text">27”宽屏萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14386233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386233" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386233-20245_Genesis_G80_21.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386233-20245_Genesis_G80_21-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386233" class="wp-caption-text">萤幕右方用作各种控制。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>G80配备了不少豪华设备，后挡风玻璃的电动太阳挡、后座车窗的手拉式太阳挡、两侧后视镜的投影厂徽、前座加热及降温功能、方向盘加热、电动折叠侧镜、二个驾驶席记忆及电动车尾门。与所有Genesis车辆一样，G80提供了标准主动和被动的安全设备， 其中包括刹车辅助、盲点防止碰撞辅助、车道保持辅助和驾驶员注意警告等。G80提供最高标准的安全设备，就像G70和G90轿车一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386234" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386234-20245_Genesis_G80_23.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386234-20245_Genesis_G80_23-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386234" class="wp-caption-text">G80采用变速盘。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>G80的引擎强而有力，加速力很迅速，要超车切线也极容易。要舒适驾驶也可以，G80的悬挂吸震系统很称职，可化解路上的各种崎岖路况。测试的G80 3.5T Sport Prestige内装查尽豪华时尚，豪华中又带着精致，并有不同的高科技设备，部分应用更胜欧日系豪华车，例如前车开车提示，即当前方车辆已经开走，而驾驶者仍踏着刹车的时候，系统会为分神的驾驶者作提醒，以及盲点摄像机在仪表板显示，是非常实用先进！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386235" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386235-20245_Genesis_G80_28.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386235-20245_Genesis_G80_28-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386235" class="wp-caption-text">Nappa皮革后座豪华舒适。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
