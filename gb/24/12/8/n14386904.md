<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>田园诗画 贡寮区内寮的梯田、水牛与石头屋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386905-729397-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>贡寮区内寮的梯田景观。（Kimball Wen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月08日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新北市报导）清晨薄雾中，新北市贡寮区内寮的梯田慢慢揭开面纱，仿佛在大地间轻轻舒展。摄影家Kimball Wen说：“田水是一渠一渠的流，阳光洒下来，就像大地在微笑。”层层相叠的梯田不仅是自然与人类智慧的结晶，也是农村生命的见证。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386906" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386906-729398-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386906-729398-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386906" class="wp-caption-text">摄影家Kimball Wen说：“田水是一渠一渠的流，阳光洒下来，就像大地在微笑。”（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些梯田沿着山坡地势而建，在过去曾是一家家人的粮仓。随着时间推移，梯田一度荒废，但在政府与居民的努力下，它们又重新披上翠绿的外衣，成为涵养水源与孕育生命的重要场域。每逢稻穗成熟，田野间传来的稻香与笑声，让人仿若置身童年的田园梦境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386907" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386907-729399-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386907-729399-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386907" class="wp-caption-text">梯田一度荒废，但在政府与居民的努力下，它们又重新披上翠绿的外衣。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>石头屋的守护者 阿嬷的岁月与阳光</h2>
<p>“入内坐啦！”阿公慈祥的声音在石头屋的庭院响起，让人感受到旧时光的温暖。这座石头屋，是阿公住了一辈子的家，也是内寮的灵魂所在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386908" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386908-729400-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386908-729400-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386908" class="wp-caption-text">“入内坐啦！”阿公慈祥的声音在石头屋的庭院响起。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影家Kimball Wen记录了阿公的日常：“阿公住石头厝，趁着阳光晒人生。”在庭院里，阿公熟练地将切好的瓜片一片片平铺在绿色的晒网上，瓜片在阳光下闪着淡淡的光泽，仿佛是时光为他的努力所绣上的金线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386909-729401-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386909-729401-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386909" class="wp-caption-text">阿公熟练地将切好的瓜片一片片平铺在绿色的晒网上。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影家Kimball Wen:“辛勤的人有福了 !（贡寮区．内寮）当你穿梭在寻觅的行旅里，遇见了一幅风景，山岚里飘渺着几许云烟，这山与水，田和石头屋，以阳光腌渍的记忆味道，品尝阿公人间岁月，难以忘怀的恬园佳肴。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386910" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386910-729402-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386910-729402-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386910" class="wp-caption-text">摄影家Kimball Wen:“辛勤的人有福了 !遇见了一幅风景，山岚里飘渺着几许云烟。”（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石头屋的外墙上，斑驳的石块见证着这个家庭的兴衰起伏。院子里几盆生机盎然的花草，更添一份生气。阿公说：“石头屋里有我们的故事，你们现在来，是在听我们以前的生活。”他的笑容如阳光般温暖，让人感受到这座小屋的坚韧与希望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386911" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386911-729403-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386911-729403-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386911" class="wp-caption-text">阿公说：“石头屋里有我们的故事，你们现在来，是在听我们以前的生活。”（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>田野乐章 水牛与白鹭的共舞</h2>
<p>内寮的梯田间，最动人的画面莫过于水牛与白鹭的身影。水牛在田间悠闲地啃草，白鹭轻巧地停在牛背上，像是在为这片田地演奏一曲自然乐章。摄影家Kimball Wen深情描述：“牛背上的白鹭鸶，仿佛为水牛按摩抓痒，画面好疗愈。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386912" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386912-729404-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386912-729404-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386912" class="wp-caption-text">这样的景象，不仅是内寮农村生活的缩影，也是人与自然共生的最佳诠释。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样的景象，不仅是内寮农村生活的缩影，也是人与自然共生的最佳诠释。水牛踏出的田间小径，带着泥土的芬芳，而白鹭轻巧的身影，则为这幅田园画增添了一抹灵动的诗意。阳光下，它们的一举一动，仿佛唤醒了人们对自然与生命的珍视。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386913-729405-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386913-729405-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386913" class="wp-caption-text">Kimball Wen深情描述：“水牛旁的白鹭鸶，仿佛为水牛按摩抓痒，画面好疗愈。”（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>惜福的传承 农村景象的守护与再生</h2>
<p>“辛勤的人有福了！”这句话在内寮不仅是对农夫的赞美，更是对这片土地的深深敬意。梯田、水牛与石头屋，是这片土地的生命象征，也是农村文化的核心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386914" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386914-729406-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386914-729406-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386914" class="wp-caption-text">梯田、水牛与石头屋，是这片土地的生命象征，也是农村文化的核心。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内寮的居民与志愿者正努力复育梯田与石头屋，希望将这片大地的故事传递给下一代。摄影家Kimball Wen感慨：“这里的一切，古拙而美好，提醒我们珍惜拥有的一切。”他记录下梯田里的水流、屋前晒干的瓜片，以及阿公手上刻画出的岁月痕迹，这些画面勾勒出一幅幅令人动容的田园诗画。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386915-729407-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386915-729407-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386915" class="wp-caption-text">摄影家Kimball Wen感慨：“这里的一切，古拙而美好。”（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>找回心灵的家园</h2>
<p>内寮，不仅仅是一个地方，更是一种生活的记忆与对家的深情依恋。在这里，水梯田的清新、石头屋的坚韧、水牛的安逸，与阿公的笑容交织成令人难以忘怀的景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386916-729408-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386916-729408-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386916" class="wp-caption-text">内寮，不仅仅是一个地方，更是一种生活的记忆与对家的深情依恋。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到内寮，你会发现，这片土地不仅仅是风景，更是一种珍惜与惜福的生活哲学。内寮的每一幕，都在提醒我们珍惜当下、守护土地。让我们一同记住这片田园风光，并将它的故事传递给更多人，为未来的世代留下一份珍贵的生命礼物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14386917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14386917" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386917-729409-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14386917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14386917-729409-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14386917" class="wp-caption-text">内寮的每一幕，都在提醒我们珍惜当下、守护土地。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
