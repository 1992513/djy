<p>讲座之后“聚餐联谊房东交流”<wbr />地点会有改变，名额有限。如果你想参与，欢迎与潘树仁联络。<wbr />也请随时到网站“<ahref="http://www.midtwn.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?q=http://www.midtwn.com&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1734228433738000&amp;usg=AOvVaw0pulSMsTPStfkXbeZ_dWkW">www.midtwn.com</a>”查询最新的消息。<wbr />如有任何问题，请咨询，请致电/短信 ：617-834-4071 ，或发送电子邮件至：<ahref="mailto:japan@midtownboston.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">japan@midtownboston.com。</a>期待与您见面。◇</p>
