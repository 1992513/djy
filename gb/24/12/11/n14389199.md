<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>余风采堂立昌和雅伦连任主席 盼推总堂候选人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389150-DSC09303-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月8日，纽英崙余风采堂新届主席就职。左起：元老余文博、主席余立昌、副主席余雅伦、元老余超群。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年12月11日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）作为唐人街的古老传统侨社之一，纽英崙余风采堂的宗亲们说，“向心力”是公所保持旺盛人气和欢乐氛围的原因。这也是12月8日余氏新届职员就职典礼的主题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14389156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14389156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389156-DSC09320.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14389156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389156-DSC09320-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14389156" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂新届职员就职。宗亲们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天在华埠的余风采堂驻地，余立昌和余雅伦在元老和宗亲们的见证下连任主席。大家祭拜祖先，庆祝冬至，齐享台山汤圆，还展望3年后的北美余氏恳亲大会。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“记得去年我曾经讲过我的心情，怎么样的心情呢？第一任做主席，我的心又惊，脚又震。这次我讲我的心情，我很兴奋。为什么呢？因为我就快身退了。”余立昌笑言，令宗亲们大笑。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">关于余氏宗亲们的氛围，他说，大家很有“向心力”，“有力出力，有得玩就来玩”。比如妇女组的女士们包揽了全部活动，将气氛搞得很旺；男士们也不遑多让，热心帮忙解决建筑漏水之类的问题。余立昌还说，公所里人才济济，明年到他任满卸职的时候，一定后继有人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14389152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14389152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389152-DSC09308.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14389152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389152-DSC09308-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14389152" class="wp-caption-text">余立昌连任主席，称赞大家的向心力。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14389153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14389153" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389153-DSC09311.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14389153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389153-DSC09311-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14389153" class="wp-caption-text">余雅伦连任副主席，感谢大家支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">副主席余雅伦对于“向心力”的说法深表赞同。他也期待，大家未来能多多介绍亲朋好友加入余风采堂。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天中午，宗亲们首先祭拜祖先余忠襄公。随后在元老余文博、余超群的见证下，余立昌和余雅伦捧起印信，正式连任纽英崙余风采堂主席。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14389149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14389149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389149-DSC09302.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14389149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389149-DSC09302-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14389149" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂新届职员就职。宗亲们祭拜祖先。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>余文博回顾美东总长事务</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">全美余风采堂元老余文博分享了他担任美东总长期间的见闻，并提出，希望推举波士顿的宗亲，参与3年后沙加缅度（Sacramento）恳亲大会的总堂选举。他谈到，上次推举竞选副总长的宗亲没能成功，希望这次大家能推出更有力的候选人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14389155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14389155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389155-DSC09316.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14389155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389155-DSC09316-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14389155" class="wp-caption-text">余文博分享他担任总长期间的见闻。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">余文博提到，当前的经济环境正给一些地方的公所带来困难。比如旧金山余风采堂，由于商业楼宇租不出去，公所收入减少，导致奖学金也显着减少。新环境意味着公所需要做出改变。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">余文博还展望说，在沙加缅度恳亲大会之后，再下一次的恳亲大会应该会在波士顿举办。所以希望能选出一位有经验、负责任的总长，来主持大会相关工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14389154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14389154" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389154-DSC09313.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14389154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389154-DSC09313-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14389154" class="wp-caption-text">余超群祝贺职员们就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">另一位元老余超群恭喜所有职员就任，以及卸任总长余文博晋升元老。余超群也期待大家继续做好族务，明年“更上一层楼”。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">中文书记余美玲主持就职典礼。妇女组主任余艳玲、余艳清，顾问余丽媖等职员也都向宗亲们送上祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14389151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14389151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389151-DSC09307.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14389151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389151-DSC09307-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14389151" class="wp-caption-text">余美玲主持就职典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14389157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14389157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389157-IMG_7409.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14389157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14389157-IMG_7409-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14389157" class="wp-caption-text">余氏宗亲历史悠久。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
