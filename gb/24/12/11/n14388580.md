<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚特兰大台湾商会第36届第二次理事会及餐叙</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388592-IMG_5044-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年12月11日讯】12月8日（星期日），亚特兰大台湾商会假商会理事Jacky的餐馆Sushi Ming完成了第36届第二次理事会议及餐叙活动。在一次犹如同学会聚餐的欢乐气氛中，经由多位荣誉会长的提领下，圆融讨论完成了多项的会务议题讨论。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388589-4712755783520904760-e1733881404849.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14388589 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388589-4712755783520904760-e1733881404849.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="912" /></a></p>
<p>会议上，理事会将亚特兰大台湾商会的成立宗旨定位为：开展人脉：纵向连结北美各地商会、参加世界总会活动。横向积极赞助、协办参与本地其他侨团活动；学习成长：安排所有理事会员有关人际关系、领导统御、活动规划之学习课程。让所有会员言之有物、行之有为；拓建商机：重新整理商会网站更新个人资料内容，建立起交流平台连结北美总会。请专业摄影拍摄会员形象照。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388594-IMG_5046-e1733880476220.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14388594 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388594-IMG_5046-e1733880476220.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="187" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388599-IMG_5045-e1733880530873.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14388599 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388599-IMG_5045-e1733880530873.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="187" /></a></p>
<p>理事会还通过了会务推行计划：确保会务活动的无断续与顺利推行，所有会员轮续担任活动负责人，自行组建工作小组、专案专责。人人有机会学习。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388600-IMG_5051-e1733880593584.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14388600 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388600-IMG_5051-e1733880593584.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="187" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388601-IMG_5050-e1733880651995.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14388601 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388601-IMG_5050-e1733880651995.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="187" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388603-IMG_5049-e1733880736110.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14388603 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388603-IMG_5049-e1733880736110.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="187" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388605-IMG_5052-e1733880866148.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14388605 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14388605-IMG_5052-e1733880866148.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="187" /></a></p>
<p>理事会近期内推动的三项活动方案是：警察局和消防局的岁末感恩活动，商会岁末联欢卡拉ok舞会，石头山公园的新年文化活动。</p>
