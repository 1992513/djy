<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Costco12月十款新品上架 令会员无法抗拒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137618-bfac83a6c9bfaacbda3d230c0226cf84-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>Costco是倍受消费者喜爱的一家美国仓储式零售商。图为示意图。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年12月05日讯】（大纪元记者夏雨编译报导）转眼时序进入12月，在繁忙的购物季节，商家们也纷纷上架新品，消费者喜爱的美国仓储式连锁店Costco（好市多）也不例外。那么Costco有哪些新品上架，且让会员们无法抗拒呢？</p>
<p>个人理财网站FinanceBuzz刊文，整理了一份最新最棒的消费者热议商品清单，从节日零食和舒??适的冬装，再到冷冻美食，应有尽有。</p>
<p>文章说，这些新品对筹划节日派对、购买礼物、准备晚餐还是更新季节性衣橱，都是不错的选择。</p>
<p>以下精选其中的10款新品或回归商品，供读者参考。价格会根据时间、地点有所不同。</p>
<h2>1. 防风雨男士运动鞋（Weatherproof Men’s Sneakerboots）</h2>
<p>价格：31.99美元</p>
<p>Costco购物者Buddha称赞这款寒冷天气穿的靴子说：“它们非常适合休闲散步和远足……天气总是刮风下雨，靴子很舒服，让我的双脚保持完全干燥，系好（鞋带）一次后，它们就再也没有松开过！我从未滑倒过，整个远足过程都感到很安全！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385542" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385542-s-e5d5dcd3bc6a5f2fae9f5bcf.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14385542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385542-s-e5d5dcd3bc6a5f2fae9f5bcf.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385542" class="wp-caption-text">防风雨男士运动鞋（Weatherproof Men’s Sneakerboots）。（培智／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2. A&#8217;Capella Chocolate Tri-Tin（三罐装巧克力）</h2>
<p>价格：15.79美元</p>
<p>每个包装有一罐6盎司的威士忌味牛奶巧克力和干樱桃、一罐6.5盎司的巧克力花生和一罐6.5盎司的巧克力苹果酒杏仁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385541" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385541-s-a698abe6f544d1fc21bd578d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14385541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385541-s-a698abe6f544d1fc21bd578d.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385541" class="wp-caption-text">A&#8217;Capella Chocolate Tri-Tin三罐装巧克力。（培智／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3. Copper Kettle Candy Co. 海盐松露黑巧克力（Dark Chocolate Truffles with Sea Salt）</h2>
<p>价格：11.59美元（16盎司）</p>
<p>这款商品是季节性的，倍受会员喜爱。今年12月回归货架。</p>
<p>Copper Kettle网站上24块焦糖海盐口味巧克力售价28.95美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385536-s-23ba947a6c4f92acf9d0ec5d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14385536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385536-s-23ba947a6c4f92acf9d0ec5d.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385536" class="wp-caption-text">Copper Kettle Candy Co. 海盐黑巧克力（Dark Chocolate Truffles with Sea Salt）。（培智／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>4. Amaryllis and Juniper沐浴露系列</h2>
<p>价格：12.99美元</p>
<p>这款三件装假日香味沐浴露售价12.99美元，非常划算。每瓶28盎司且价格为4.33美元。</p>
<p>Bath and Body Works的一瓶10盎司冬季Snowflakes and Cashmere沐浴露售价为15.95美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385534-s-6a6dcc81db1acfb043c97dba.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14385534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385534-s-6a6dcc81db1acfb043c97dba.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385534" class="wp-caption-text">Amaryllis and Juniper沐浴露系列。（培智／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>5. Mobile Warming加热内衣背心（Mobile Warming Heated Baselayer Vest）</h2>
<p>价格：99.99美元</p>
<p>这件背心具有四种加热设置、四个加热区，每次充电可使用长达12小时，穿上它再也不会在户外感到寒冷。通过简单的触摸控制，可选择100%、75%、50%或25%的热量水平。</p>
<p>Dick’s Sporting Goods以179.99美元价格出售ActionHeat男士5V电池加热软壳背心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385539-s-6141e15a991d97be0af0444e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14385539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385539-s-6141e15a991d97be0af0444e.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385539" class="wp-caption-text">Mobile Warming加热内衣背心。（培智／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>6. EOS Super Balm（超保湿唇膏）</h2>
<p>价格：11.99美元（原价14.99美元）</p>
<p>这个倍受喜爱的美容品牌又回到Costco。超保湿配方可提供24小时的保湿。每套包装包括西瓜冰冻、芒果甜瓜汁、蜂蜜苹果、椰奶，以及生日蛋糕等5种味道。</p>
<p>一管0.35盎司的蜂蜜苹果超级润唇膏在该品牌网站上零售价为5.99美元。按照这个价格，你要花29美元买五管。但在Costco，仅花11.99美元买到这个套装。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385535-s-9dbcf001beb7a2e2dddfce7b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14385535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385535-s-9dbcf001beb7a2e2dddfce7b.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385535" class="wp-caption-text">EOS Super Balm超保湿唇膏。（培智／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>7. 热辣牛肉紫菜包饭（Hot Maki Beef Bulgogi Kimbap）</h2>
<p>价格：18.99美元</p>
<p>韩式牛肉、蔬菜和米饭做成的紫菜包饭是粉丝最爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385538-s-1008f43ede493143ae16792f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14385538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385538-s-1008f43ede493143ae16792f.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385538" class="wp-caption-text">热辣牛肉紫菜包饭。（培智／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>8. 节日毯（Casual Living Holiday Throw）</h2>
<p>价格：17.99美元</p>
<p>这个毯子大小为50&#215;70英寸，温暖舒适，是一款100%涤纶雪尼尔针织毯，可机洗。</p>
<p>Costco购物者ShariPDX写道：“有史以来最柔软、最可爱的产品。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14385533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14385533" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385533-s-0ec1a622e1872bf1d3afbc08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14385533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14385533-s-0ec1a622e1872bf1d3afbc08.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14385533" class="wp-caption-text">Casual Living Holiday Throw。（培智／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>9. Lasko Motion X全室加热器（Whole Room Heater）</h2>
<p>价格：69.99美元</p>
<p>这款5,128 BTU 11x7x26.49英寸的振荡式空间加热器包括三种加热设置、300平方英尺覆盖范围、自动恒温器、12小时定时器，还有过热保护和远程控制访问功能。</p>
<p>Costco客户Michael S.评论这款加热器说：“非常安静，但提供强劲热风。尺寸令人印象深刻。”</p>
