<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新诗：中继（中英对照）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396456-shutterstock_2514590557-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">气喘吁吁，汗流浃背<br />
艰辛地攀爬岁月的阶梯<br />
到达这一高度<br />
已是黄昏，暮色深浓<br />
你还想做什么？</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">同行的<br />
有的已悄然退下<br />
有的已然迷失<br />
你还不顾前程遥远<br />
还想继续前进</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">问你要去向何方，目标何在<br />
你说，只要向前<br />
管它何方？管它目标在哪<br />
一意向前就是了<br />
向前就是方向，向前就是目标</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">黄昏呀黄昏，暮色呀暮色<br />
让它去茫然不清吧<br />
不去顾虑太多，不去犹疑<br />
向暮色走去，向茫茫走去<br />
到那里自然就会阳光普照</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;"><strong>Relay</strong> Hsu chicheng</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">Out of breath, soaked with sweat<br />
Hard to climb the step of the age<br />
You arrived some more high degree<br />
Now it&#8217;s the dusk, and deep dense also<br />
What do you want to do?</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">Your companies<br />
Some are retired silently<br />
Some are missed their road<br />
Only you still go ahead sucessivedly<br />
No matter of how far the prospect</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">Inquire you where are you go, where is the target<br />
You said, just go ahead<br />
In spite of where is I go, where is the target<br />
Go ahead with body and soul is the all<br />
Go ahead is the orientation, go ahead is the target</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">Oh dusk, oh evening<br />
Let it be utterly ignorant<br />
Don&#8217;t care more, don&#8217;t hesitate<br />
Go forward to the dusk, go forward to the ignorant<br />
Of cause there will be having much sunshine</p>
