<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冬日溪头 静谧森林中的舒心之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396185-733320-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>成功到达目的地，那份成就感与身心舒展的愉悦令人难忘。（nina提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月23日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾南投报导）每到冬季，南投竹山镇溪头自然教育园区，就成为空拍达人nina与好友的必游之地。这次与朋友一起前往，挑战神木步道，重温久违的森林步道之旅。沿途清新的空气和自然的芬多精，让人彻底放松。nina：“即使是冬日，溪头却丝毫不觉寒冷，凉爽中还透着一丝暖意，走一走竟还得脱外套，让人倍感舒适。”</p>
<figure id="14396172" aria-describedby="caption-14396172" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396172-733300-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396172-733300-450x338.jpg" alt="冬日溪头。" width="449" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396172" class="wp-caption-text">冬日溪头。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14396173" aria-describedby="caption-14396173" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396173-733301-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396173-733301-450x338.jpg" alt="nina：“即使是冬日，溪头却丝毫不觉寒冷，凉爽中还透着一丝暖意。”" width="448" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396173" class="wp-caption-text">nina：“即使是冬日，溪头却丝毫不觉寒冷，凉爽中还透着一丝暖意。”（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>银杏与枫叶的冬季盛宴</h2>
<p>冬季的溪头有着独特的风情。这次造访时，银杏与枫叶正值最佳观赏时节。银杏树满布金黄的叶子，像是一片片阳光洒落地面，而枫叶则呈现出浓烈的红与橙，为冬季的森林增添一抹暖色调。沿途走着，不时会拾起一片落叶，感受它背后的季节流转，这片红与黄的交织，为每位游客打造一场视觉的飨宴。</p>
<figure id="14396174" aria-describedby="caption-14396174" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396174-733302-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396174-733302-450x338.jpg" alt="冬季的溪头有着独特的风情。这次造访时，银杏与枫叶正值最佳观赏时节。" width="448" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396174" class="wp-caption-text">冬季的溪头有着独特的风情。这次造访时，银杏与枫叶正值最佳观赏时节。（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14396183" aria-describedby="caption-14396183" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396183-733318-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396183-733318-450x600.jpg" alt="nina：“这次路过银杏大道，树影斑驳的光影特别吸引人。”" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396183" class="wp-caption-text">nina：“这次路过银杏大道，树影斑驳的光影特别吸引人。”（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>神木步道的挑战与欣赏</h2>
<p>溪头最让人期待的便是神木步道。这次nina与朋友慢慢攀登，途中多次停下休息，既是为了欣赏沿途的景致，也是为了调整节奏。步道蜿蜒于茂密的森林中，每一步都能感受到自然的气息。nina：“走到神木前，那棵超过2800年的红桧以其粗壮挺拔的姿态静静矗立，让人不禁赞叹大自然的鬼斧神工。”</p>
<figure id="14396175" aria-describedby="caption-14396175" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396175-733304-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396175-733304-450x600.jpg" alt="步道蜿蜒于茂密的森林中，每一步都能感受到自然的气息。" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396175" class="wp-caption-text">步道蜿蜒于茂密的森林中，每一步都能感受到自然的气息。（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina的朋友因为膝盖积水问题，最近才刚恢复，这次挑战神木步道对她而言是一大进步。慢慢走，多休息，最终我们成功到达目的地，那份成就感与身心舒展的愉悦令人难忘。</p>
<h2>忘忧森林的片刻静谧</h2>
<p>步行至忘忧森林，雾气弥漫的景象让整个人仿佛进入了童话世界。这片林地的木栈道蜿蜒其中，特别适合放慢脚步，聆听树叶间的风声和不远处的鸟鸣。忘忧森林的名字真的名副其实，让人彻底忘却城市的喧嚣，沉浸在自然的怀抱中。</p>
<figure id="14396176" aria-describedby="caption-14396176" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396176-733306-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396176-733306-450x338.jpg" alt="步行至忘忧森林，雾气弥漫的景象让整个人仿佛进入了童话世界。" width="451" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396176" class="wp-caption-text">步行至忘忧森林，雾气弥漫的景象让整个人仿佛进入了童话世界。（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>大学池的镜中世界</h2>
<p>离开步道后，nina来到了大学池。池面如镜，将四周的森林倒映在水中，宛如一幅动人的山水画。阳光洒落池面时，闪耀的波光仿佛在向我们微笑。站在池边，微风吹拂，心中泛起阵阵暖意。</p>
<figure id="14396177" aria-describedby="caption-14396177" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396177-733307-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396177-733307-450x338.jpg" alt="大学池边景致。" width="449" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396177" class="wp-caption-text">大学池边景致。（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次在溪头还意外发现了几处充满惊喜的花卉景致。天使喇叭花盛开在路旁，黄色的花朵垂挂枝头，犹如小号般散发出优雅的气质。而沿着步道，还有一排艳红的圣诞红在冬日阳光下格外耀眼，与森林的绿意形成了强烈对比。</p>
<figure id="14396178" aria-describedby="caption-14396178" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396178-733308-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396178-733308-450x338.jpg" alt="在溪头还意外发现了几处充满惊喜的花卉景致。" width="449" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396178" class="wp-caption-text">在溪头还意外发现了几处充满惊喜的花卉景致。（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>空中走廊的独特视角</h2>
<p>溪头的空中走廊也是一个不得错过的景点。高架的步道让我们能从树冠层的高度俯瞰整片森林，感受不一样的视角之美。nina：“走在空中走廊上，抬头是蓝天白云，低头则是绿意盎然的森林，这种“被大自然包围”的感觉，让人由衷感到幸福。”</p>
<figure id="14396179" aria-describedby="caption-14396179" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396179-733309-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396179-733309-450x338.jpg" alt="nina：“走在空中走廊上，抬头是蓝天白云。”" width="449" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396179" class="wp-caption-text">nina：“走在空中走廊上，抬头是蓝天白云。”（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>与朋友的点滴回忆</h2>
<p>这趟溪头之旅，不仅是欣赏自然之美，更是一场心灵的放松。朋友与nina边走边聊，分享生活的近况与挑战。尤其在走神木步道时，我们互相鼓励，彼此打气，深感友谊的珍贵。</p>
<figure id="14396184" aria-describedby="caption-14396184" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396184-733319-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396184-733319-450x338.jpg" alt="这趟溪头之旅，不仅是欣赏自然之美，更是一场心灵的放松。" width="449" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396184" class="wp-caption-text">这趟溪头之旅，不仅是欣赏自然之美，更是一场心灵的放松。（nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>朋友笑着说：“希望这次不会铁腿。”而nina则回应：“只要能看到这么美的风景，铁腿也值得啊！”</p>
<figure id="14396180" aria-describedby="caption-14396180" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396180-733311-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396180-733311-450x338.jpg" alt="nina说：“只要能看到这么美的风景，铁腿也值得啊！”" width="448" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396180" class="wp-caption-text">nina说：“只要能看到这么美的风景，铁腿也值得啊！”（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>冬日溪头的疗愈之旅</h2>
<p>溪头的冬天是独一无二的，它没有寒冷刺骨的气温，反而给人一种暖心的感觉。丰富的景点与自然景致，让每位游客都能找到属于自己的舒心角落。如果有空档，nina就会搭游览车，跟着退休人士，一起拜访这片森林，重温每一个令人感动的瞬间。</p>
<figure id="14396181" aria-describedby="caption-14396181" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396181-733312-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396181-733312-450x338.jpg" alt="现在有许多退休人士，会搭游览车，一起拜访这片森林。" width="449" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396181" class="wp-caption-text">现在有许多退休人士，会搭游览车，一起拜访这片森林。（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论是银杏、枫叶，还是神木步道与空中走廊，溪头的每一处都充满了冬季的惊喜，是一场身心的双重飨宴。</p>
<figure id="14396182" aria-describedby="caption-14396182" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396182-733313-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14396182-733313-450x338.jpg" alt="溪头的每一处都充满了冬季的惊喜，是一场身心的双重飨宴。" width="451" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14396182" class="wp-caption-text">溪头的每一处都充满了冬季的惊喜，是一场身心的双重飨宴。（nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
