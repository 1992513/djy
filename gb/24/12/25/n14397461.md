<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>熟龄结伴轻旅行 山光水色与人情味的美好邂逅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397462-733927-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>盐水八角楼。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）初冬的清晨，手工腊烛工艺家萧绫美，与几位多年好友相约展开一次轻旅行。目标地是台南盐水，一个以其浓厚的历史气息与在地美食而闻名的小镇。大伙都步入熟龄阶段，旅行不求紧凑，只想在悠然的节奏中品味生活的温度。</p>
<p>抵达盐水后，迎面而来的是老街特有的怀旧气息。首先映入眼帘的是著名的盐水八角楼。这栋建于清朝道光年间的建筑，外观庄重而不失优雅。它的八角形设计不仅别具一格，也体现了当时富商对建筑美学的追求。二楼的木造回廊带着岁月的痕迹，让人不禁联想起昔日人们在此谈天说地的场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397602" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397602-733953_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397602-733953_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397602" class="wp-caption-text">老铁铺现况。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14397603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397603" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397603-733954_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397603-733954_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397603" class="wp-caption-text">盐水银锋冰菓室。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14397604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397604" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397604-733958_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397604-733958_medium.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397604" class="wp-caption-text">盐水八角楼采用传统建筑工法。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14397591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397591" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397591-733929_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397591-733929_medium.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397591" class="wp-caption-text">八角楼，这栋建于清朝道光年间的建筑。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>老街散步　时光倒流的美好</h2>
<p>一行人沿着桥南老街慢行，这里是盐水最繁华的旧城区。街巷中隐藏着众多百年历史的店铺，其中最让我们着迷的是泉利打铁铺。店内的打铁工匠年过八旬，但手工技艺依然娴熟。他向旅人展示了风柜与削刀板凳等古老工具，并细心讲解如何将农具打磨至最理想的形状。这不仅是一种传承，更是一种对生活的专注与热爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397601" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397601-733946_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397601-733946_medium.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397601" class="wp-caption-text">老匠人手工制作的刀具。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>站在盐水桥南老街的这栋古屋前，萧绫美不禁被它斑驳的木门，与岁月侵蚀的屋瓦深深吸引。这些历史的痕迹，仿佛在述说着曾经的繁华与时光的无情。她说：“古屋虽然显得老旧，但它散发出一种朴实无华的韵味，仿佛仍能感受到曾经人来人往的热闹景象。”心中油然升起一种敬意，觉得这些古建筑正是盐水文化的载体，默默地守护着这片土地的故事与记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397592" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397592-733931_medium-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397592-733931_medium-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397592" class="wp-caption-text">萧绫美说：“古屋虽然显得老旧，但它散发出一种朴实无华的韵味。”（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>逛着逛着，大家的肚子开始抗议，决定前往一家小店品尝盐水意面。这里的意面别有一番风味，面条弹牙有劲，搭配浓郁的肉燥酱汁，每一口都令人回味。老板还热情地与我们聊起意面的由来，原来这道美食是由早期的制面技艺延续而来，代表了盐水人的勤劳与智慧。</p>
<p>店家老板还收藏了许多五ｏ年代的生活用具，包括大大小小的算盘啦、唱片啦、还有这么可爱小喇叭，看不完、拍不尽，面香和仿古厝的氛围，让众人话题多了起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397593" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397593-733935_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397593-733935_medium.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397593" class="wp-caption-text">店家老板还收藏了许多五ｏ年代的生活用具。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>山水相依的月津港</h2>
<p>饱足后因为冬阳和煦，熟女们来到月津港，一处充满诗意的地方。清领时期，月津港曾是南台湾的重要商港，如今则成为一个亲水公园。环绕港边的小径上，有游客轻声细语，湖面上倒映着蓝天与翠绿的树影，我们站在岸边，感受微风拂面，仿佛连时间也随之缓慢了下来。大伙相约元宵灯节再造访一次，因为每年灯会时，月津港都有不同的美丽样貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397599" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397599-733943_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397599-733943_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397599" class="wp-caption-text">月津港另一视角。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14397594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397594" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397594-733936_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397594-733936_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397594" class="wp-caption-text">清领时期，月津港曾是南台湾的重要商港，如今则成为一个亲水公园。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>高空美景与咖啡香</h2>
<p>结束了盐水的行程，我们上中山高速公路驱车前往台南东边的东山区，拜访网红景点174翼骑士驿站。这家餐厅的最大特色是其12公尺长的玻璃天空步道，站在步道中央，仿佛悬浮于天地之间。远处的山峦连绵起伏，近处则是翠绿的树林，令人心旷神怡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397595" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397595-733937_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397595-733937_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397595" class="wp-caption-text">台南东边的东山区，拜访网红景点174翼骑士驿站。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14397596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397596" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397596-733938_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397596-733938_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397596" class="wp-caption-text">这家餐厅的最大特色是其12公尺长的玻璃天空步道。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>餐厅内的咖啡香气浓郁，坐在露天座位上，与好友闲聊当年的趣事，笑声不绝于耳。这样的片刻时光，既惬意又温暖。旅人站在玻璃步道上，神采飞扬，露出灿烂的笑容，右手高举作出欢呼的姿势，仿佛在说：“这一刻，真是美好又自由！”她的表情洋溢着兴奋与满足，身后是葱郁的山林美景，仿佛整个世界都为她而明亮。这种站在高空欣赏壮丽景色的体验，无疑带给她一种超脱日常、心旷神怡的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397600" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397600-733944_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397600-733944_medium.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397600" class="wp-caption-text">174驿站另一视角。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每次旅行的尾声总让人感到不舍，但盐水的每个角落、每个细节，似乎都在低声述说着它的过去与现在。回程中来到东山休息区，采买台南在地的伴手礼，一行人再度展现超级购物员的姿态，从烘焙屋一直到鞋子、包包。大伙笑说，下次再找一个晴朗的日子，继续走访这片土地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397597" style="width: 472px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397597-733940_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397597-733940_medium.jpg" alt="" width="472" b="629" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397597" class="wp-caption-text">东山休息区。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>旅人的心声　细品生活的美好</h2>
<p>这趟轻旅行，对于熟龄旅人来说，不仅是一场心灵的放松，更是一场与历史、人情味的相遇。盐水的老街、意面、月津港，以及东山的高空景观，都在提醒我们：生活的美好，不仅仅在于远方，更在于当下的每一刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397598" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397598-733942_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14397598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397598-733942_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397598" class="wp-caption-text">桥南老街的古厝。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
