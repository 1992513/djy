<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>灯光交织的节日奇景 台南与台东灯节的温暖回忆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397722-734192-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2024年台东铁花灯之祭活动开跑了！（张春梅提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）2024年台东铁花灯之祭新主题“极地童话”，自圣诞季节至明年2月16日期间，成为冬日旅游的必访之地，摄影作品由台东当地知名地瓜酥第二代张春梅拍摄。而摄影家王建中则于台南西街教会捕捉到了，仅限于平安夜展出的限定灯光秀，将历史与创意完美结合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397723-734193-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397723-734193-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397723" class="wp-caption-text">即日起至明年2月16日，成为冬日旅游的必访之地。（张春梅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>台东灯节　多元创意与节庆氛围的交融</h2>
<p>铁花灯之祭的主题洋溢着童趣与温馨。入口处巨大的圣诞树灯饰闪烁着星形灯光，让人仿佛进入童话世界。附近，发光的独角兽装置展现了梦幻与神秘的氛围，吸引旅人驻足拍照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397724" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397724-734194-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397724-734194-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397724" class="wp-caption-text">台东灯节今年的主题洋溢着童趣与温馨。（张春梅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>特色造型与互动设计 另一个让人印象深刻的作品，是形似巨大礼盒的灯饰，蓝色的蝴蝶结在灯光中闪烁，仿佛为游人准备的惊喜礼物。还有一个圣诞帽形状的拱门灯饰，设计成可以供人穿越的形式，让人瞬间融入到灯光氛围中。灯节还有几何造型的透明灯饰，内部透着多彩的灯光，变化莫测，特别适合家庭和朋友一起观赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397725" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397725-734195-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397725-734195-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397725" class="wp-caption-text">让人印象深刻的作品，是形似巨大礼盒的灯饰。（张春梅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>动物灯饰展现童趣 灯节也包含许多可爱的动物造型灯饰。例如发光的北极熊家族，以及色彩鲜艳的企鹅灯饰，为整个灯节注入了活力。这些灯饰不仅吸引了小朋友，也让成人忍不住停下脚步欣赏，唤起心中的童心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397726" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397726-734196-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397726-734196-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397726" class="wp-caption-text">灯节也包含许多可爱的动物造型灯饰。（张春梅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>台南西街教会　历史与信仰的灯光故事</h2>
<p>王建中捕捉的台南西街教会灯光秀，仅在平安夜这一周展出，但留下了永恒的画面。教堂圆顶以蓝色灯光渲染，静谧而神圣，白色十字架指引着信仰方向。罗马柱结构被暖黄色灯光包围，与冷色调的蓝光形成对比，营造出庄严且温暖的气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397727" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397727-734197-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397727-734197-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397727" class="wp-caption-text">王建中捕捉的台南西街教会灯光秀。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>水中倒影　完美对称的艺术</h2>
<p>特别值得一提的是，王建中巧妙利用雨后积水的倒影，捕捉到教堂灯光的对称之美，宛如一幅静止的圣诞夜画作。倒影中的教堂与实景交相呼应，让观者感受到平静的节日氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397728" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397728-734198-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397728-734198-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397728" class="wp-caption-text">王建中巧妙利用雨后积水的倒影，捕捉到教堂灯光的对称之美。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>节庆细节与宗教意涵</p>
<p>教堂的圣诞装饰也别具一格：门廊上的圣诞花圈、小型圣诞树、马槽造型装饰，将节日的温馨与宗教的意涵紧密结合。内部以耶稣为主题的壁画，在柔和灯光的衬托下，让信仰故事更深植人心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397729" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397729-734199-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397729-734199-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397729" class="wp-caption-text">透过王建中的镜头，这些景象被永久保存。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>灯光艺术　温暖的视觉与心灵飨宴</h2>
<p>无论是台东铁花灯之祭的童趣装置，还是台南西街教会的平安夜圣诞灯光，这些艺术创作都展现了光影的魅力。透过王建中的镜头，这些景象被永久保存，让人们在欣赏之余，也感受到对传统与创意的感恩之心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397730-734200-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397730-734200-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397730" class="wp-caption-text">灯光不仅照亮了冬日的夜晚，更温暖了人们的内心。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灯光不仅照亮了冬日的夜晚，更温暖了人们的内心，唤起彼此对生命与信仰的感恩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14397731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14397731" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397731-734202-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14397731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14397731-734202-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14397731" class="wp-caption-text">动物灯饰展现童趣。（张春梅提供）</figcaption></figure>
