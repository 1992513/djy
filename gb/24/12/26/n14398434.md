<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>磁吸青年移居来嘉 黄敏惠宣布加码三补助</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398435-734507-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>启动仪式（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月26日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市政府今(26)日在市府1楼中庭举办“嘉义够‘吸’力、青年‘嘉’团聚”十大旗舰计划-“青世代磁吸”成果记者会，全面回顾“安心生养、青年培育、人才就业”三大核心策略执行成效，市长黄敏惠也当场宣布三项政策加码，包括“青年租屋加码补助”、“求职加薪补助”及“生育津贴加码补助”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14398436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14398436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398436-734508-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14398436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398436-734508-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14398436" class="wp-caption-text">磁吸青年移居来嘉 ，市长黄敏惠宣布加码三补助。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动开场也由嘉义女中热舞社、兴仁Shock Cool乐团，以及新二代青年英家绮带来充满活力的表演揭开序幕。现场以走秀方式“吸力秀”生动呈现政策成果，同时设置主题展示区，让参与者身临其境感受嘉义市在生养支持、人才培育与就业促进等方面的亮点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14398437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14398437" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398437-734509-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14398437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398437-734509-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14398437" class="wp-caption-text">青年纷纷来嘉走秀。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄敏惠市长宣布，明(114)年将以“齐发三补助，年轻世代好Easy”为主轴，推出“青年租屋加码补助”，针对设籍并租屋于嘉义市的18-45岁青年，及符合中央扩大租金补贴者，每月额外加码补助500元，减轻居住压力；“求职加薪补助”月薪介于基本工资至4万元的18-29岁青年，每月可领3,000元补助，助力青年职涯起步，实现职业梦想。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14398438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14398438" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398438-734510-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14398438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398438-734510-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14398438" class="wp-caption-text">青年租屋加码补助走秀。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“生育津贴加码补助”每胎津贴由2万2,000元提高至3万元，完善生育支持系统，鼓励迎接新生命。感谢议会支持以及市民的共同努力，嘉义市不仅是青年成家立业的理想之地，更是创造无限可能的舞台。未来市府团队将持续以创新与实践为核心，吸引更多人才，携手创造更美好的城市未来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14398439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14398439" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398439-734511-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14398439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398439-734511-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14398439" class="wp-caption-text">求职加薪走秀。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄敏惠市长表示，青年是带动城市发展的重大引擎，嘉义市政府致力于吸引优质人才，整合生育、托育与教育资源，打造完整的城市支援系统，助力青年成家立业。今年，市府成立了嘉义市精忠公共托婴中心，提供28名婴幼儿友善托育环境；并成立“嘉义市永续发展青年推动小组”，鼓励青年参与在地议题解决，充分利用市府资源执行相关政策，实现城市永续发展；推动“二地居政策”，吸引更多人口链接嘉义市，从短期移居到最终成为永久居民；也积极扶植新型态工作型态，发展新世代经济，让宜居嘉义成为青年落脚的新选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14398440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14398440" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398440-734512-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14398440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398440-734512-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14398440" class="wp-caption-text">生育津贴补助走秀。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>社会处处长李思贤表示，嘉义市政府为吸引青年回流与扎根，进行1,010份青年需求问卷调查，从“就业创业”、“育儿健康”到“安居租屋”等六大面向，整合10个局处、70项计划资源，建置“城市备援整合平台”。该平台提供一站式服务，为嘉义市专属青年资源网站，推动智慧城市发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14398441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14398441" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398441-734513-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14398441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398441-734513-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14398441" class="wp-caption-text">青年人口渐增市长感到欣慰走秀。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14398442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14398442" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398442-734519-450x161.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14398442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14398442-734519-450x161.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="161" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14398442" class="wp-caption-text">皆大欢喜大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
