<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿安良会长就职 盼参与华埠规划讨论</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402901-DSC09561-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月30日正午，波士顿安良工商会新届会长就职。左起：会长陈伟民、经文处处长廖朝宏、安良元老陈仕维、会长黄展堂。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年01月01日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）12月30日正午在华埠的波士顿安良工商会大楼，陈伟民和黄展堂在庄严的仪式中就任新届会长。安良成员及来宾们希望在新一年继续参与讨论华埠重新分区方案，向市政府发出业主和商家的声音。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14402914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402914-DSC09618.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402914-DSC09618-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402914" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿安良工商会新届职员就职，与嘉宾们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14402905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402905" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402905-DSC09582.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402905-DSC09582-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402905" class="wp-caption-text">侨社众多代表观礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>新届职员就职</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我会同我的伙伴James发展好我们的会务，尽量帮助侨社。”黄展堂说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“接下来的一年，我们期待与社区的每一位维持关系，面向未来建立更强劲的唐人街。在这里看到这么多来自不同团体的代表，我期待与你们每一位建立联系。”陈伟民说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这两位新届会长，在全美安良工商会元老陈仕维、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏，以及上百位来宾的见证下，接过印信，正式就职。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14402904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402904" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402904-DSC09574.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402904-DSC09574-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402904" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长黄展堂期待与大家协力建设侨社。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14402911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402911" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402911-DSC09604.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402911-DSC09604-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402911" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长陈伟民希望与每位来宾建立联系。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14402903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402903" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402903-DSC09572.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402903-DSC09572-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402903" class="wp-caption-text">卸任会长伍伟业感谢大家过去一年的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天，侨社嘉宾们齐聚安良大楼，参与就职典礼。随着司仪梅宇国宣布典礼开始，安良职员们首先敬拜关羽神像及本楼土地。在会长交接仪式之后，全体新届职员在陈仕维的带领下宣誓就职。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14402900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402900" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402900-DSC09558.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402900-DSC09558-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402900" class="wp-caption-text">全体职员敬拜关公神像。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14402902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402902" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402902-DSC09564.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402902-DSC09564-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402902" class="wp-caption-text">新届职员宣誓就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">接下来，卸任会长伍伟业感谢各位会员的支持，并与观礼嘉宾们一起向新届职员送上祝福。发言者包括中华公所主席雷国辉、经文处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任高家富、洪门致公堂元老伍伯和、安良元老陈仕维等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">廖朝宏在感谢新、老职员服务和团结社区的同时，也谈到安良工商会与中华民国台湾从100多年前至今的良好关系。“我很荣幸能够维持与在座所有人的关系，我也期待未来继续提升我们的良好关系。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14402907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402907" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402907-DSC09587.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402907-DSC09587-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402907" class="wp-caption-text">司仪梅宇国主持就职典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14402906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402906" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402906-DSC09583.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402906-DSC09583-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402906" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉期待与安良继续合作。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14402908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402908" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402908-DSC09588.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402908-DSC09588-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402908" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏感谢安良服务社区。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">洪门与安良向来是兄弟团体。洪门元老伍伯和谈到，他1963年加入安良工商会，多年来也参与洪门工作。他期待，两个团体未来继续团结一致，为侨社服务。</p>
<p>就职典礼结束后，安良工商会在华埠翠苑酒家宴请宾客，现场推杯换盏，气氛热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14402909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402909-DSC09597.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402909-DSC09597-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402909" class="wp-caption-text">洪门元老伍伯和回顾两个团体的兄弟情谊。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14402910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402910-DSC09599.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402910-DSC09599-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402910" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富代表侨委会问候来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_14402913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402913" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402913-DSC09611.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402913-DSC09611-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402913" class="wp-caption-text">两位新届会长及元老向关公神像敬香。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>参与华埠分区讨论</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>支持业主和商家</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">大会现场，陈仕维还介绍了华埠重新分区的情况，并呼吁大家向市政府发出业主和商家的声音。“特别大的影响，就是对我们在唐人街做生意的，我们在唐人街有物业的公所或者私人。这个划分是影响到我们唐人街未来最少10年的发展。”他说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">市政府之前发布的重新分区规划草案，将华埠商业区划分为两个区域，其中“MU-10”区域的建筑限高为150英尺，“CC”区域的限高是80英尺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14402912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14402912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402912-DSC09607.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14402912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14402912-DSC09607-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14402912" class="wp-caption-text">陈仕维呼吁大家新一年继续向市政府发声，提出关于华埠重新分区的意见。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">陈仕维谈到，关于这个草案，社会各方纷纷提出不同意见；华埠商会也将集中业主和商家的意见，反映给市政府。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">他说，许多其它地区的唐人街，近年来出现生意凋零、游客稀少的窘境；大家不希望波士顿唐人街步其后尘。另一方面，之前也有老侨谈到，来到波士顿40年了，看见唐人街基本没有变化，这种缺乏进步的情况是否健康，值得讨论。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">陈仕维希望，未来一年继续与各团体合作，呼吁市政府尊重业主和商家的意见。◇</p>
