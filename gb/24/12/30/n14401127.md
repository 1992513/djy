<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>布里斯本明慧学校办年终音乐会 传承华夏文化</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401145-DSC06585-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>布里斯本明慧学校12月22日举办年终音乐会，表演结束后参与表演者合影。（倪尔森/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月30日讯】<span class="s1">（</span><span class="s2">大纪元特约记者倪尔森澳洲布里斯本报导</span><span class="s1">）</span><span class="s3">2024</span>年<span class="s3">12</span>月<span class="s3">22</span>日，布里斯本明慧学校于<span class="s3">Upper Mt Gravatt Progress Hall</span>举行了一场精彩的年终音乐会，透过丰富多彩的表演展现中华传统文化的魅力，为海外华人子女搭建了一座连接东西方文化的桥梁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401140-Image16-600x338.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401140-Image16-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401140" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会在舞龙进场中揭开序幕。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">音乐会由电影《梨花开》女主角凯莉（<span class="s1">Kelly</span><span class="s2">）与弗拉德（</span><span class="s1">Vlad</span><span class="s2">）共同主持，这是明慧学校第二次举办年终表演会，共呈现了</span><span class="s1">14</span>个精心准备的节目。演出内容涵盖范围广泛，既有传统又具创新：从展现中国传统节气文化的《<span class="s1">24</span>节气歌》，到古意盎然的古筝琵琶合奏；从妙趣横生的相声表演，到动感十足的蒙古筷子舞；更有小提琴、大提琴的优美旋律，以及竖琴与古筝的和谐共鸣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401139-Image8-600x338.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401139-Image8-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401139" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会中Tom（大提琴）与 Ava（竖琴）合奏名曲《夜莺》（Nightingale），富有抒情、优美流畅，仿佛夜莺在月下歌唱。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">演奏曲目从意大利民间舞蹈音乐《<span class="s1">Tarantella</span>》（塔朗泰拉舞曲）、旋律优美清新的《<span class="s1">Sonatina</span>》（小奏鸣曲）、马蹄奔腾般的《骑兵之歌》、富有中国传统韵味的《彩云追月》、富有禅意的《风动铃动心不动》到表达深挚爱情与悲剧色彩的《梁祝》（梁山伯与祝英台），节目精彩纷呈，赢得现场观众的一致好评。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401138-Image1-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401138-Image1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401138" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会中小提琴（Kaya）与大提琴（Ivy）合奏名曲《梁祝》，优美的旋律获得在场观众热烈的掌声。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">参演学生年龄跨度从<span class="s1">7</span>岁至<span class="s1">14</span>岁不等，他们为这次演出付出了大量心血。值得一提的是，部分学生甚至从黄金海岸远道而来，每周坚持参加培训和排练。学校和家长对此次演出的重视，不仅在于为孩子们提供展示才艺的舞台，更着眼于通过传统文化的传承，培养下一代正确的价值观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401137-Image19-600x338.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401137" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401137-Image19-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401137" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会参演学生年龄从7岁到14岁，纯真的舞蹈《小小莲花》诠释出莲花的清新脱俗。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">明慧学校一位教师表示，中国传统文化对海外成长的华人子女来说意义重大。文化是民族的根基，尤其是中共统治前的五千年文明，在西方社会中备受推崇。让在海外出生的孩子从小浸润于纯净的传统文化环境中，学习待人接物之道与传统礼仪，理解民族文化内涵，对他们的未来发展具有深远影响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401136" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401136-Image22-600x338.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401136-Image22-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401136" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会中小朋友表演蒙古族独特的舞蹈《蒙古筷子舞》，那份认真劲儿特别讨喜。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">明慧学校秉持回归传统文化的办学理念，致力于为海外华人子女营造纯净愉快的学习氛围，帮助他们建立正面的人生价值观。除了校内活动，学校还积极组织学生参与社区公益表演，为当地社会贡献力量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401135" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401135-DSC06588-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401135-DSC06588-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401135" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会中〈干净世界〉熊宝宝也出现在现场，为音乐会增添热闹气份。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">本次活动得到了全球友谊协会的大力支持，该协会特别准备了节日礼品包，与观众共同分享圣诞的喜悦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401134" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401134" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401134-Image18-600x338.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401134-Image18-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401134" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会得到全球友谊协会的大力支持，当天表演者与观众都获得一份礼物。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14401133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401133" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401133-DSC06560-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401133" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401133-DSC06560-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401133" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会获得学生家长与社区人士的支持，圆满成功。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">此次音乐会的成功举办，不仅展现了明慧学校在传承中华文化方面的努力，更体现了海外华人社区对下一代文化教育的重视。通过这样的文化活动，年轻一代得以在多元文化的环境中，更好地传承和发扬中华传统文化的精髓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401132-DSC06592-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401132-DSC06592-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401132" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会结束，家长与嘉宾共享晚餐，分享圣诞节前的喜悦。（倪尔森/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
