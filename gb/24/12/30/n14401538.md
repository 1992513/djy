<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>屏东公园下雪了 南台湾欧式圣诞的绚丽盛宴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401539-735339-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>屏东公园圣诞节下雪了。（黄荣群提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）每年岁末，屏东公园都会化身为一座充满欧式风情的圣诞村庄，吸引无数游客前来感受浓厚的节日氛围。2024年的圣诞节活动更是盛大，成为南台湾最大的欧式圣诞庆典之一。摄影师黄荣群以其独特的视角，捕捉了这场视觉盛宴的精华，透过他的镜头，我们得以一窥这片梦幻世界的美丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401540-735340.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401540-735340-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401540" class="wp-caption-text">摄影师黄荣群以其独特的视角，捕捉了这场视觉盛宴的精华。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>欧风童话主题的灯饰艺术</h2>
<p>
今年的屏东圣诞节以“欧风童话”为主题，从屏东火车站至屏东公园，共设置了18组大型灯饰。其中，双主灯“圣诞童话屋”和“圣诞村水晶球”成为整个活动的焦点。每当夜幕降临，灯光点亮，整个公园仿佛化身为童话中的梦幻国度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401541-735341.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401541-735341-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401541" class="wp-caption-text">今年的屏东圣诞节以“欧风童话”为主题，从屏东火车站至屏东公园，共设置了18组大型灯饰。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄荣群的作品中，最引人注目的是他对“圣诞童话屋”的捕捉。这座高耸的灯饰建筑，融合了欧式建筑的元素，灯光透过精心设计的窗户和屋顶，散发出温暖而迷人的光芒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401543" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401543-735342.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401543-735342-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401543" class="wp-caption-text">黄荣群的作品中，最引人注目的是他对“圣诞童话屋”的捕捉。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄荣群：“我想透过不同角度的拍摄，展现灯饰在夜色中的立体感和层次感，让观者仿佛置身其中，感受童话般的美好。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401547" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401547-735346.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401547-735346-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401547" class="wp-caption-text">水面上的天鹅也很可爱。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>冰雪世界与飘雪大道</h2>
屏东公园的椰林大道在圣诞期间摇身一变，成为“冰雪世界飘雪大道”。每逢周末及国定假日的晚上，随着灯光的变幻，人工雪花纷纷飘落，营造出南国少见的雪景。黄荣群捕捉了这一奇景：“游客们在雪花中嬉戏，笑容洋溢，灯光与雪花交织，构成一幅幅温馨而浪漫的画面，我想抓住那瞬间。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401544" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401544-735343.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401544-735343-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401544" class="wp-caption-text">屏东公园的椰林大道在圣诞期间摇身一变，成为“冰雪世界飘雪大道”。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在他的镜头下，椰林大道两侧的灯饰与中央飘落的雪花相互辉映，营造出如梦似幻的氛围。这些作品不仅展现了活动的盛况，更传递出人们在节日中所感受到的喜悦与幸福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401545-735344.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401545-735344-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401545" class="wp-caption-text">飘落的雪花相互辉映，营造出如梦似幻的氛围。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>摄影师的视角　光影中的圣诞魔法</h2>
作为一名资深摄影师，黄荣群对光影有着敏锐的洞察力。他善于利用夜间的灯光效果，结合现场的氛围，创作出具有强烈感染力的作品。在此次屏东圣诞节活动中，他选择了多个拍摄点，从不同的角度展现灯饰的美丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401548-735347.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401548-735347-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401548" class="wp-caption-text">黄荣群对光影有着敏锐的洞察力，他善于利用夜间的灯光效果。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14401546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401546" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401546-735345.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401546-735345-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401546" class="wp-caption-text">在摄影师的视角下，不仅展现了活动的盛况，更传递出人们在节日中所感受到的喜悦与幸福。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄荣群：“在拍摄“圣诞村水晶球”时，我选择在夜晚的蓝调时刻进行拍摄，利用灯光与夜空的对比，突出水晶球的晶莹剔透。”而在拍摄“奇幻梦游”灯区时，他则选择低角度，强调灯饰的高大与宏伟，给人以震撼的视觉效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401549" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401549-735349.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401549-735349-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401549" class="wp-caption-text">选择低角度，强调灯饰的高大与宏伟，给人以震撼的视觉效果。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄荣群表示，屏东圣诞节的灯饰活动为摄影爱好者提供了丰富的创作素材。他建议，拍摄时可多尝试不同的曝光时间和角度，捕捉灯光变化的瞬间，这样才能展现出灯饰的动感与美感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14401550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14401550" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401550-735350.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14401550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14401550-735350-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14401550" class="wp-caption-text">黄荣群建议，拍摄时可多尝试不同的曝光时间和角度，捕捉灯光变化的瞬间。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
