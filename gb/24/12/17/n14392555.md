<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>送电送爱心 台电赠公益团体慰问金尽责任</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392557-731750-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台电北北区处捐赠两万元予圣安娜之家。（北北区处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者宋顺澈台湾台北报导）送电也送爱心，台电台北北区营业处致赠公益团体慰问金8万元，岁末寒冬之际，台电台北北区营业处为善尽CSR企业社会责任，今（17）日由处长许一女偕同台湾电力工会36分会常务理事陈大业、同心园地园丁及志工团志工前往“创世基金会”“华山基金会”“圣安娜之家”及“圣道儿童之家”等公益团体，各致赠慰问金2万元合计8万元，以实际行动支持公益团体及关怀社会上弱势族群，提前祝福他们圣诞佳节快乐，也深刻感受到台电是非常有温暖的公司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392556" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392556-731749.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14392556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392556-731749.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392556" class="wp-caption-text">台电北北区处捐赠两万元予创世基金会。（北北区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392558" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392558-731751.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14392558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392558-731751.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392558" class="wp-caption-text">台电银发关怀活动捐赠两万元予华山基金会。（北北区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392559" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392559-731752.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14392559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392559-731752.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392559" class="wp-caption-text">捐赠两万元予华山基金会。（北北区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>处长许一女表示，台电公司平日肩负稳定供电之任务，提供民众生活便利的电力，此外也希望持续付出实际行动关怀公益团体及弱势族群，让“电力就像爱心”，哪里有需要就送到哪里去，一份温暖的心及棉薄之力，期盼发挥抛砖引玉而唤起社会各界共同关怀公益团体及弱势族群。岁末寒冬温馨送暖，台电献上最实质的帮助，让爱心如同电力般，无远弗届传送到社会的每个角落，为需要帮助的人们尽一份“真善美爱”的力量，一起点亮生命的希望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392560" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392560-731753.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14392560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392560-731753.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392560" class="wp-caption-text">圣安娜之家回赠感谢状。（北北区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392561" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392561-731754.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14392561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392561-731754.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392561" class="wp-caption-text">圣道儿童之家回赠台电感谢状。（北北区处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>创世基金会公关暨社资部主任吴昭芬说，除感谢台电提供稳定的电力给民众便利生活外，也给予基金会在物资上的关怀与协助，让弱势族群都能感受到岁末寒冬的温暖。另外，也期盼能激发更多真善美爱的良性循环，让爱持续深耕，为弱势族群带来更多关心和希望，成为社会稳定的重要力量。?</p>
