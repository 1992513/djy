<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台侨团6校友会联合年终聚餐 奖丰欢乐多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392965-1-1-1Alumni-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月14日，参加波士顿政大、台大、师大、辅大、北一女、建中等6校校友会和波士顿急难救助协会联合举办年终聚餐的部分参加者合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年12月18日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿摩顿市报导）近来大波士顿地区的台侨校友会流行举办联合餐会：一来人多热闹，互相支援，节省人力；二来有些会员重叠，或夫妻是不同校的，就可联袂参加，皆大欢喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392966-1-1-2Alumni.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392966-1-1-2Alumni-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392966" class="wp-caption-text">干部们与嘉宾合影。左起：政大会长林游岚、北一女会长林丽珠、侨教中心主任高家富、经文处处长廖朝宏、北一女前会长吴杏玫、辅大会长吴萍萍、建中会长郑新霖等。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12月14日中午，上百人汇聚在摩顿的“Fleming Grill”自助餐厅，将整个包厢挤得满满的。政大、台大、师大、辅大、北一女、建中等6校校友会和波士顿急难救助协会联合举办年终聚餐，并邀请嘉宾经文处处长廖朝宏及波士顿侨教中心主任高家富共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392968-1-1-4Alumni.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392968-1-1-4Alumni-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392968" class="wp-caption-text">师大校友会老中青校友们合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖朝宏在致辞中感谢各校友会积极参与和支持各项活动，例如元旦升旗、台湾日、台湾夜市、台湾影展等。并代表侨委会徐佳青委员长，颁发贺状予林丽珠、林游岚、林致中及吴宇怀等各侨团会长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392969-1-1-5Alumni.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392969-1-1-5Alumni-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392969" class="wp-caption-text">政大校友会校友们合影，左四为接任的吴宇怀会长。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高家富强调，12月23日是台湾全民健保“停复保”的重要截止日期，提醒大家注意保障自身权益，有关取消停复保措施相关的资讯，可洽询侨教中心。他同时提醒在海外的侨胞需警惕诈骗和虚假信息。他邀请大家踊跃参加明年1月1日上午11点，在侨教中心举行的元旦升旗活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392970" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392970-1-1-6Alumni.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392970-1-1-6Alumni-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392970" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿地区辅大校友会校友们合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>说起6校联合活动的起源，不得不提及一位大功臣——林丽珠。她曾经于1993-94担任勒星顿中文学校校长，近几年退休得空后，便积极投入侨团会务活动。她先后接任政大校友会会长、北一女校友会会长和急难救助协会会长，并对社区其它社团的活动也经常出席支持。其组织能力颇强，对策划活动细节均亲力亲为，而且与干部们合作无间，使得如此盛大的活动办得有条有理，顺顺利利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392971-1-1-7Alumni.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392971-1-1-7Alumni-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392971" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿地区台大校友会老中青校友们合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392972-1-1-8Alumni.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392972-1-1-8Alumni-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392972" class="wp-caption-text">历届北一女校友会会长合影。左起：赵小怡、江佩蓉、李欣、吴萍萍、吴杏玫、饶雨涵、林丽珠、林游岚。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了丰盛的午餐外，现场还有张扬明、杨哲修、吴萍萍等唱将的卡拉OK演唱助兴。</p>
<p>当天活动的奖品非常丰富，有北一女匿名校友捐赠的2000美元现金，还有各校友会提供的很多抽奖礼物。在饶雨涵的幽默主持下，活动高潮迭起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392973-1-1-9Alumni.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392973-1-1-9Alumni-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392973" class="wp-caption-text">身兼台大及建中校友的林致中获侨委会热心公益嘉奖。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392967" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392967-1-1-3Alumni.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392967-1-1-3Alumni-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392967" class="wp-caption-text">饶雨涵主持抽奖，还自摸一个大奖。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会上林丽珠宣布，现任政大校友会会长林游岚将担任北一女校友会下一任会长。政大下届会长由吴宇怀接任。其余各校会长尚无变动。◇</p>
