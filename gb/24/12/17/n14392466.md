<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【旅行趣】台湾马鞍坝生态园区 陪鱼儿爬梯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392478-8ac81d39e68454b75c87f5c969372c08-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>原来，台电不仅供电，还为旅人提供身心充电的一处绝美秘境！（图／作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>“马鞍坝生态园区”（也叫“大甲溪发电厂生态园区”）位于台湾的大甲溪流域，是一个结合水利设计和生态保护的特别园区。马鞍坝水利系统主要用于收集上游的水资源，并将水引至下游的发电厂，进行水力发电，不仅能稳定供应电力，还能保护河川的自然生态。</p>
<p>园区采预约制，游客需于三天前打电话联系并完成登记，才能进入参观。由于许多人不知道此处对外开放，使其成为绝世隐密而迷人的秘境。距离台中市区仅约一小时车程，免收门票，是平日市郊轻旅行不容错过的世外桃源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392481-2c24a0460f69a833907eeec98f11f608.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392481-2c24a0460f69a833907eeec98f11f608-600x459.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="459" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392481" class="wp-caption-text">园区入口处，有两个身穿“泰雅族”传统服饰的可爱公仔迎宾。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392473-a7f3711a4aa054b214a27cf6885bc11f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392473-a7f3711a4aa054b214a27cf6885bc11f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392473" class="wp-caption-text">壮阔的碧绿湖水，秀丽的自然风光，令人陶醉。水坝中心的小岛，“鹭鸶岛”，常有白鹭鸶聚集。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>秒入绝美秘境</h2>
<p>车子停妥后，步行约一分钟，壮阔的碧绿湖水随即映入眼帘，马鞍坝生态园区秀丽的自然风光，令人陶醉。这里不仅能让银发族悠闲散步，也能满足年轻人在美景前拍照打卡的乐趣，是适合全家同游的小天堂。站在水坝旁，远眺鹭鸶岛，辽阔的碧湖尽收眼底。马鞍坝仿佛一位与世隔绝的美人，静静等待有缘人来一亲芳泽。亲眼目睹这抹隐世的绝美，令人流连忘返！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392483-1ce31085730b8fa81504d84c4f96b661.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392483-1ce31085730b8fa81504d84c4f96b661-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392483" class="wp-caption-text">简单易懂的鱼道解说牌。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392472" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392472-e4130491ef6414991af6e44e0231197d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14392472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392472-e4130491ef6414991af6e44e0231197d-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392472" class="wp-caption-text">水池式鱼道（右侧）、丹尼尔式鱼道（左侧）。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>陪鱼儿爬梯</h2>
<p>马鞍坝生态园区最具特色的设施，莫过于“鱼道”（又称“鱼梯”）。作为国内第一座大型拦河坝鱼道，其设计考量了不同鱼类的习性，巧妙的设置两种鱼道：</p>
<p>1.水池式鱼道：为“缨口鳅”、“爬岩鳅”和“鰕虎”等鱼类设计，提供平缓的水池区块，让鱼儿能轻松上溯。</p>
<p>2.丹尼尔式鱼道：为“鲈鳗”和“台湾铲颔鱼”等鱼类打造，设置隔板供鱼类暂时休息，让鱼儿能逐步穿越坝体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392475-d5b80782bd82f436fdccfa1f635c456e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14392475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392475-d5b80782bd82f436fdccfa1f635c456e-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392475" class="wp-caption-text">苦花（台湾鲴鱼）模型，与各种常见鱼类介绍。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392477-2468e4e3c529112d92dd166959f8ddf6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392477-2468e4e3c529112d92dd166959f8ddf6-600x471.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="471" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392477" class="wp-caption-text">马口鱼（山鲢仔）模型，与鱼道介绍。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鱼道的设计让鱼类能够轻松克服水坝的阻隔，顺利游向上游产卵或觅食。游客可以边在鱼道旁的人行道漫步，边陪着鱼儿“爬楼梯”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392480-a32d6c808089d5f735531eb08171e5e0.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392480-a32d6c808089d5f735531eb08171e5e0-600x936.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="936" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392480" class="wp-caption-text">鱼道视窗上长满了绿藻，是“爬岩鳅”最爱的美食。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在马鞍坝鱼道中，还设有几个大型的鱼道视窗，游客可在窗前静心等待鱼儿的身影出现。此处最常出现的常客，莫过于小巧玲珑的“爬岩鳅”，瞧它们奋力逆流而上，还不忘沿途吸食窗上的绿藻，模样既可爱又逗趣。马鞍坝鱼道寓教于乐，是个适合全家出游的生态旅游胜地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392476" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392476-6399d041e56280d83d106d4db69953ca.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392476-6399d041e56280d83d106d4db69953ca-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392476" class="wp-caption-text">马鞍坝仿佛一位与世隔绝的美人，静静等待有缘人来一亲芳泽。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>《结语》</h2>
<p>无论是欣赏美景、了解生态保育，还是单纯放松心情，马鞍坝生态园区都能满足旅人的需求。这里远离尘嚣，让人拥抱秀丽的山水。水坝壮阔的流水声，仿佛将所有烦忧都洗去。在山林间微风轻拂下，仿佛时间也按下暂停键。原来，台湾电力公司不仅提供电力，还为旅人提供身心充电的一处绝美秘境！</p>
