<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>JCCAA德州台湾校友排球联赛 圆满落幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392848-02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第三届德州台湾校友排球联赛大合影。（陆希／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年12月18日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）由美南大专院校联合校友会（JCCAA）主办、贝勒医学院台湾学生会协办的第三届德州台湾校友排球联赛，于12月15日（周日）在圣汤姆士大学的体育馆（Jerabeck Activity and Athletic Center）举行，来自台湾留学生和旅居德州的逾80 名球友组队以球会友。经文处处长萧伊芳、科技组组长陈品铨、教育组组长杨淑雅、休台姐妹会会长Amy Coburn应邀出席为球员加油打气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392847" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392847-01.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392847-01-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392847" class="wp-caption-text">萧伊芳、Amy Coburn（右五、四）和嘉宾前往加油合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392849-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392849-03-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392849" class="wp-caption-text">颁奖之后前三名大合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>JCCAA会长辜千慈带领团队一早便前往球场准备迎接球员报到，参赛队从八个团队扩大到了今年有10个团队参赛，有来自Port Lavaca、奥斯丁、达拉斯。女性球员人数达40%，台湾裔球员为76.5%。今年的年龄层较往年更广，从高中生、留学生到在职青年，而最年轻的队伍是青年文化大使（FAFSA）。该活动特别感谢驻休士顿经文处教育组、Suhjen Tsai、Wendy Lam、Annie Yen、Eric Chang的热情赞助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392850-04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392850-04-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392850" class="wp-caption-text">萧伊芳（右）和辜千慈（左）与冠军团队合影。（图/辜千慈提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392851" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392851-05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392851-05-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392851" class="wp-caption-text">萧伊芳（右）和辜千慈（左）与亚军团队合影。（图/辜千慈提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>萧伊芳鼓励运动强健体魄，同时也感谢JCCAA和团队规划该活动，让来自德州各地台湾留学生以球会友，相互交流。休台姐妹会Amy Coburn表示，欢迎大家来到休斯顿庆祝我们的姊妹城市关系。杨淑雅提到教育组一直非常努力地支持所有的台湾留学生组织，鼓励协会的学生参与许多课外活动，例如排球、探险或跑步等，除了您的专业研究之外，还有各种适合的活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14392852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392852-06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392852-06-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392852" class="wp-caption-text">季军团队合影。（图/辜千慈提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14392853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14392853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392853-07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14392853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14392853-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14392853" class="wp-caption-text">殿军团队合影。（图/辜千慈提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过一整天激烈比赛后，傍晚大会举行颁奖仪式，成绩结果如下：Pikatzu（冠军）获$200+奖牌；Crazy VBallers（亚军）获article content begin -->50+奖牌；Choo Choo（季军）获article content begin -->00+奖牌及Teatop No.1（殿军）获$50。</p>
