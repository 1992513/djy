<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>龙潭区凌云及三坑公托家园开幕 完善各项妇幼政策</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14393998-732391-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政与来宾欢庆龙潭凌云社区公共托育家园开幕。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月19日讯】（大纪元记者陈建霖台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政19日前往龙潭区，出席“公设民营龙潭凌云及龙潭三坑社区公共托育家园开幕”。张善政表示，龙潭地区原有2处公托家园，今日再开设2处，成为龙潭区第3及第4家公共托育设施，同时亦是本市的第52及53家，市府将随着龙潭的发展，持续努力寻觅合适地点，设立更多公共托育设施，以满足在地乡亲托育需求。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14393999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14393999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14393999-732392.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14393999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14393999-732392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14393999" class="wp-caption-text">龙潭凌云社区公共托育家园内部环境。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政感谢各级民代给予市府支持，让市府有足够的资源加速拓展收托服务量能，市府将持续完善各项妇幼政策，让市民朋友放心生儿育女。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14394000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14394000" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14394000-732393.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14394000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14394000-732393.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="324"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14394000" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政与来宾共同参与开幕仪式。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14394001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14394001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14394001-732394.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14394001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14394001-732394.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="352"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14394001" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政与幼儿园孩子合影。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妇幼局表示，公设民营龙潭凌云社区公共托育家园及公设民营龙潭三坑社区公共托育家园，由市府委托“桃园市婴幼儿教保协会”经营管理，将各收托12名满2足月至未满2岁之婴幼儿，自今年12月20日中午12时起受理报名登记至明（2025）年1月3日下午4时止，民众若有送托意愿，可至桃园育儿资源网（网址：<ahref="https://babycare.tycg.gov.tw/#/home" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow noreferrer">https://babycare.tycg.gov.tw/#/home</a> ）线上登记，或至现场由服务人员协助预约报名，预订于明年1月6日上午10时办理抽签作业，抽签结果同日公布于该家园门口、桃园育儿资源网及该家园脸书粉丝专页。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14394002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14394002" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14394002-732395.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14394002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14394002-732395.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14394002" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政与幼儿一起制作圣诞花圈。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14394003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14394003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14394003-732396.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14394003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14394003-732396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14394003" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政与幼儿一起拿着制作完的圣诞花圈合影。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妇幼局指出，目前桃园市已开办36家公设民营托婴中心、17家公共托育家园及24处亲子馆，其中今年已完成设置9处公托家园，预计年底前再增加1处，总计至今年底共可提供1,778名收托量能，并持续辅导私立托婴中心及居家托育人员加入准公共托育服务，积极建置公共托育设施，打造友善育儿环境。◇</p>
