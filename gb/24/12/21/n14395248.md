<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>兴嘉国小32周年校庆 全员容光焕发 成绩耀眼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395249-732884-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大合照。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年12月21日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市兴嘉国小庆祝创校32周年校庆，典礼于体育馆内隆重举行，场面热闹非凡，洋溢着庆祝的氛围。舞台上贵宾云集座无虚席，台下预备给家长的座位早已满座，许多家长站在礼堂周围观看表演。</p>
<p>学生带来充满活力的舞蹈表演，看台上各年级高喊响亮的口号，为兴嘉送上生日祝福。运动员代表以国语、台语及英语多语种进行宣誓，展现学生多语学习的成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14395250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395250" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395250-732885.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395250-732885-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395250" class="wp-caption-text">颁奖。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14395251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395251-732886.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395251-732886-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395251" class="wp-caption-text">运动员代表以国语、台语及英语多语种进行宣誓，展现学生多语学习的成果。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>幼童军护托会旗进场，象征运动精神与团队合作。市赛10连霸的管乐团现场演奏，为会场带来美妙的音乐，增添庄重与喜庆气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14395252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395252-732887.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395252-732887-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395252" class="wp-caption-text">校方特别为棒球队授旗，预祝球队在即将举行的诸罗山杯棒球赛中旗开得胜、再创佳绩。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>校方特别为棒球队授旗，预祝球队在即将举行的诸罗山杯棒球赛中旗开得胜、再创佳绩。典礼中为近期在国际赛事中表现优异的叠杯选手颁发奖状与奖励金，表彰他们的努力与成就，成为全校的骄傲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14395253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395253-732888.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395253-732888-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395253" class="wp-caption-text">典礼中为近期在国际赛事中表现优异的叠杯选手颁发奖状与奖励金。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>运动场上的趣味竞赛充满欢乐气氛，笑声不断。滚轮胎比赛：家长会、志工团及教师团队率先登场竞技，最终由男生居多的家长会夺得冠军。各年级趣味竞赛，各班同学全力以赴，无不使出浑身解数完成任务，活动趣味十足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14395254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395254-732889.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395254-732889-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395254" class="wp-caption-text">趣味竞赛传球。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>科学与童玩体验市集，市集活动多元而有趣，适合全家参与。跨校合作：北兴国中与玉山国中的科技中心参与展出，让学生接触到更多科学知识。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14395255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395255-732890.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395255-732890-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395255" class="wp-caption-text">合照。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>魔术互动秀，现场表演让学生与家长目不转睛，惊喜连连。公益义卖与童玩、折气球体验：活动结合爱心与趣味，增添了温馨氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14395256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395256-732891.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395256-732891-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395256" class="wp-caption-text">兴嘉国小生日快乐。（右为校长，左为家长会长）。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下午的大队接力是校庆的高潮，各年级学生在跑道上你追我赶，竞争激烈，加油声此起彼落。许多家长为学生加油助威，场面热血又感人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14395257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395257-732892.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395257-732892-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395257" class="wp-caption-text">下午的大队接力是校庆的高潮，各年级学生在跑道上你追我赶，竞争激烈，加油声此起彼落。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在闭幕典礼上，校长朱丽乖表示，感谢所有亲师生的用心与努力，让32周年校庆圆满成功！本次校庆展现了兴嘉国小的团队凝聚力与校园特色，也为参与的每骄傲个人留下了难忘的回忆，以充满活力与多元特色的活动圆满落幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14395258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14395258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395258-732893.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14395258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id14395258-732893-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14395258" class="wp-caption-text">校长（中）颁给志工团感谢状。（嘉义市立兴嘉国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
