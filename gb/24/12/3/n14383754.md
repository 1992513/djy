<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳人今夏乘公共交通工具免费</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年12月03日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张小凡澳洲珀斯报导）西澳推出今夏免费乘坐公共交通工具福利。所有持有</span><span lang="en-AU">SmartRider</span><span lang="zh-TW">乘车卡的乘客都可以享受此福利。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">免费乘车活动于</span><span lang="en-US">2024</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US">14</span><span lang="zh-TW">日（周六）开始，持续至</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US">5</span><span lang="zh-TW">日（周三）。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">为享受免费乘车服务，</span><span lang="en-US">SmartRider</span><span lang="zh-TW">持卡人必须在行程开始时刷卡上车</span><span lang="en-US">/</span><span lang="zh-TW">进站（</span><span lang="en-US">Tag On</span><span lang="zh-TW">），并在行程结束时刷卡下车、出站（</span><span lang="en-US">Tag Off</span><span lang="zh-TW">）。如果未刷卡，将会收取默认票价，并可能面临罚款。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">为了确保每个人都能享受免费乘车的便利，公共交通管理局将限时免费发放</span><span lang="en-US">Smart Rider</span><span lang="zh-TW">乘车卡。</span></p>
<p>从<span lang="en-US">12</span>月<span lang="en-US">11</span>日起，乘客可以前往任意<span lang="en-US">Transperth</span>信息中心（<span lang="en-US">Info Centre</span>）领取一张免费<span lang="en-US">SmartRider</span>卡，或者致电<span lang="en-US">136213</span>咨询乘车卡邮寄事项。每人限领一张卡片，</p>
<p>以下五个地点设有<span lang="en-US">Transperth</span>信息中心：</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Perth Railway Station(Forrest</span>广场对面<span lang="en-US">)</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Perth Underground</span>（<span lang="en-US">Murray</span>步行街）</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Perth Busport</span>（<span lang="en-US">Yagan</span>广场）</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Elizabeth Quay</span>汽车站</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Perth</span>机场</p>
