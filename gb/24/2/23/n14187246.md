<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>电锅轻松做出餐酒馆料理：蒜香鲑鱼芜菁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193689-P.108-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蒜香鲑鱼芜菁。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>用电锅便能轻松做出道地的餐酒馆料理。因为不是用高温调理，不仅能保留恰到好处的口感，食材的美味也不流失。</p>
<p>使用工具：耐热密封袋</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>材料　2人份</strong></span></h2>
<p>生鲑鱼……………………2片<br />
芜菁（切扇形）………2个<br />
蒜（压碎）……………..1瓣<br />
辣椒……………………… 1根<br />
柠檬（切圆片）………1/4颗<br />
月桂叶……………………1片<br />
橄榄油……………………4大匙<br />
盐…………………………? 1/2小匙<br />
黑胡椒……………………少许</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>准备</strong></span></h2>
<p>．用滚水淋在生鲑鱼上，去除腥味。<br />
．芜菁削皮。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h2>
<p>1. 将材料全放入耐热密封袋内，在袋内拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193691" style="width: 387px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193691-P.108-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14193691" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193691-P.108-2.jpg" alt="" width="387" b="258" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193691" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 在电锅的外锅内倒入六分满的水，按下按键，将水加热至约80℃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193690" style="width: 387px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193690-P.108-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14193690" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193690-P.108-1.jpg" alt="" width="387" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193690" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 把1.放进2.内，盖上外锅盖，按下保温键焖1小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193692" style="width: 387px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193692-P.108-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14193692" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193692-P.108-3.jpg" alt="" width="387" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193692" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14193693" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193693" style="width: 387px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193693-P.108-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14193693" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193693-P.108-4.jpg" alt="" width="387" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193693" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 取出密封袋，倒入器皿内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14193689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14193689" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193689-P.108.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14193689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14193689-P.108-450x611.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="611" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14193689" class="wp-caption-text">蒜香鲑鱼芜菁。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
