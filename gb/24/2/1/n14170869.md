<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澳批发电价大幅下跌 零售电价有望下降</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/0976130731e06968b281f4ae3faeddd1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>能源市场监管机构称，去年全澳批发电价大幅下跌， 零售电价有望下降。(Quinn Rooney/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月01日讯】<span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">（大纪元记者夏楚君澳洲悉尼编译报导）能源市场监管机构称，去年全澳批发电价大幅下跌，增加了家庭和小型企业在经历两年电费飙升后最终将得到缓解的预期。不过，监管机构警告说，批发电价的下降需要一段时间才能转化为零售电价。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #111111;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #4d5156;">澳洲能源监管局（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">AER</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）的报告称，去年南澳的平均年批发价下降了</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">44%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，塔州的年平均批发价下降了</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">64%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，部分原因是政府的价格上限。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #111111;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">据澳洲金融评论网站报导，</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">AER</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">表示：“一旦零售商的批发成本适应了未来较低的价格，消费者面临的价格应该反映这些较低的成本。”</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #111111;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">AER</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">指出，批发价格与消费者面临的价格之间预计存在滞后。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #111111;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">AER</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">将于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">3</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月初发布拟议的长期基准价，该基准价将于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">7</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">日起在新州、昆州和南澳适用，最终基准价将于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">5</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月决定。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #111111;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">AER</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">表示，<span lang="en-US">2023</span>年新州的电力平均批发价格最高，为每兆瓦时<span lang="en-US">105</span>元，紧随其后的是昆州和南澳。塔州的平均价格最低，为<span lang="en-US">每</span>兆瓦时<span lang="en-US">56</span>元。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #111111;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">联邦气候变化与能源部长博文（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #000000;">Chris Bowen</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">）</span>表示，</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">AER</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的最新数据“再次证实，政府政策和创纪录的廉价可再生能源正在导致批发价格大幅下降，最终影响消费者的账单”。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #111111;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">去年第四季度屋顶太阳能发电量年增</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">17%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，全年发电量较</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2022</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年增长</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">24%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。在这三个月中，每个地区的屋顶太阳能发电量都创下了纪录，这也使得维州和南澳的电网最低电力需求量达到了历史最低水平。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #111111;">AER</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">董事会成员<span style="color: #000000;">鲍尔（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #000000;">Jarrod Ball</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">）</span><span style="color: #111111;">表示，第四季煤炭和天然气发电比例降至</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #111111;">66%</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #111111;">，创历史新低，但每个地区同期都创下了新的太阳能发电量记录。</span></span></span></span></p>
