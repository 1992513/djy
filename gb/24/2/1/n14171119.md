<h6>巴斯克焦香起士蛋糕。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d22owz1tif1d5u.cloudfront.net/G0b2wAc6o"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ELnts-e1bp0?si=x6qAl8iotLxgk2l7" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">西班牙巴斯克焦香起士蛋糕的做法与食谱（糖饺子Sweet Dumpling提供）</span></p>
<p><b>编者按：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">巴斯克焦香起士蛋糕，曾被纽约时报评选为2019年的年度甜点。有着浓浓的起士香，融合著烟熏的焦香与柔和的奶香；冷藏后吃，质地扎实、绵密，非常疗愈。值得一提的是，它的材料简单，做起来不太费工，自制成功率很高。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">西班牙著名餐酒馆La Vi?a bar的经典甜点：巴斯克焦香起士蛋糕（Basque Burnt Cheesecake），受欢迎的程度，可以用洗版来形容。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">不同于美式的起司蛋糕，巴斯克焦香乳酪蛋糕有着深棕色的焦糖外表，表面略为起皱，边缘一点都不平整，但口味非常独特，浓浓的起士香，融合著烟熏的焦香与柔和的奶香。它的材料简单，做起来不太费工，几乎不会出错。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">本食谱参考了La Vi?a Bar的配方，这是西班牙巴斯克地区最早制作这份甜点的餐酒馆，但有做一点点调整：味道不会太甜，并加入香草精和柠檬汁。值得一提的是，出炉后稍微放凉吃，口感像布丁般滑嫩；而冰过后吃，质地扎实、绵密。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>工具：</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">圆型烤模：7吋（直径17.78cm</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">高5cm）</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>材料</b><b>：（约4?5人份）</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪（Cream Cheese）450g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">细砂糖 135g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">鸡蛋 3颗</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">鲜奶油 225g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">低筋面粉 11g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">香草精 5g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">柠檬汁 10g</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b><ahref="https://www.sweet-dumpling.com/doc/d67ef994018d51ae553240902ad828fb" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">做法</a></b><b>：</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">请</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">参考：<ahref="https://www.sweet-dumpling.com/doc/d67ef994018d51ae553240902ad828fb" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.sweet-dumpling.com/doc/d67ef994018d51ae553240902ad828fb</a></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14171329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171329-a8bab9681615a543a16cee6d6940b017.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171329-a8bab9681615a543a16cee6d6940b017-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171329" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪要软化到图示的程度，然后再搅打，蛋糕吃起来才不会有颗粒感。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</span></figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.把奶油乳酪切成小块状，摊平在烘焙纸上，放到温暖的地方让它软化。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14171330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171330" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171330-57575683b797dba365eba02ece612b24.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171330-57575683b797dba365eba02ece612b24-450x247.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="247" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171330" class="wp-caption-text">将两层烘焙纸以十字交叉方式铺在烤模上，以防面糊会溢出。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.将两层烘焙纸铺在烤模上，裁剪过长的部分，确保烘焙纸高出烤模约2吋。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14171331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171331-8984c941d8269444787c250db6c558b7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171331-8984c941d8269444787c250db6c558b7-450x248.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="248" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171331" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪以中速搅打，以免打进太多空气，影响蛋糕的口感。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</span></figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171332-777e988c1f1efea8e6021d59978c3043.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171332-777e988c1f1efea8e6021d59978c3043-450x250.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="250" /></a></p>
<h5><span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪以中速搅打，以免打进太多空气，影响蛋糕的口感。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</span></h5>
<p><a style="font-size: 16px;" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171333-90e296cb90d27903b2c2e5eb669447e0.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171333-90e296cb90d27903b2c2e5eb669447e0-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a></p>
<h5>（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</h5>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.<span style="color: #800000;">奶油乳酪以中速搅打直到非常柔滑</span></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，接着加入细砂糖，继续以</span><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">中速搅打成糊状</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，要搅拌到摸不出有糖的颗粒感。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14171334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171334" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171334-d409a9c7f8c306edb8472e7a12234411.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171334-d409a9c7f8c306edb8472e7a12234411-450x250.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171334" class="wp-caption-text">（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.三颗鸡蛋分次加入，一次只加入一颗鸡蛋，</span><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">用中速搅打均匀</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，搅匀后再加入下一颗。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14171335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171335-d18ced9c751f49f9d8deea2d130398bf.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171335-d18ced9c751f49f9d8deea2d130398bf-450x249.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="249" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171335" class="wp-caption-text">（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.取另一个碗，把面粉过筛，加入少量鲜奶油，充分混合两者，确认没有面粉结块后，再倒入剩余的鲜奶油，并充分混匀。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14171336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171336" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171336-f237c8a68a904b8a4f48f2cec463a0cd.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171336-f237c8a68a904b8a4f48f2cec463a0cd-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171336" class="wp-caption-text">（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">6.把调好的鲜奶油面糊倒入奶油乳酪糊里，再加入香草精跟柠檬汁，</span><span style="color: #800000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">改用低速搅打到完全融合</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14171337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171337" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171337-edbf1940d0ec702ddd396fe6412c633f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171337-edbf1940d0ec702ddd396fe6412c633f-450x241.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="241" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171337" class="wp-caption-text">将完成的<span style="color: #800000;">面糊过筛</span>，可滤掉小结块和气泡，蛋糕口感会较绵密。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14171338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171338" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171338-f6eeaa573bff6f557c01e8814e02bcc6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171338-f6eeaa573bff6f557c01e8814e02bcc6-450x249.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="249" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171338" class="wp-caption-text">把面糊缓慢地倒入烤模里，以免产生过多气泡。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14171339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171339" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171339-3c597ea66e965a5207adbd804a4545e3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171339-3c597ea66e965a5207adbd804a4545e3-450x246.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="246" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171339" class="wp-caption-text">把面糊缓慢地倒入烤模里，以免产生过多气泡。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.将完成的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面糊过筛</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，然后</span><span style="color: #800000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">缓慢地倒入烤模里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">8.烤箱预热220°C，烘焙35</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">45分钟。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14171340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171340" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171340-949174a6b9fe59170ede6b3b3e09459b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171340-949174a6b9fe59170ede6b3b3e09459b-450x250.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171340" class="wp-caption-text">切蛋糕前，把刀子浸一下热水，再擦干，比较不会沾黏。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14171341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14171341" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171341-26ab60eb181f51797599da9b567dc49a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14171341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171341-26ab60eb181f51797599da9b567dc49a-450x248.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="248" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14171341" class="wp-caption-text">蛋糕趁温热时吃，或者冷藏后再享用，各有其独特的风味。（糖饺子授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">9.<span style="color: #800000;">出炉后稍微放凉，等蛋糕略为定型后，再移除烤模</span></span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #800000;">。</span>完全放凉后，冷藏6个小时。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">【制作技巧、注意事项】</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span></span></h2>
<p><b>★奶油乳酪：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">让奶油乳酪快速软化，也可以用微波卢或隔水加热。要彻底软化，再来搅打；若没有完全软化，蛋糕吃起来有颗粒感。</span></p>
<p><b>★</b><b>搅打</b><b>速度：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪混合其他材料，不管用手持或桌上型的搅拌机，用中速就可以了，若速度太快，或是打得太久，会打进太多空气，影响蛋糕的口感。</span></p>
<p><b>★过筛：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">将面糊过筛，可以滤掉小结块、气泡，蛋糕的口感会更绵密。</span></p>
<p><b>★食用时机：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋糕温热时吃，里面有一点流心感；冰过后吃，口感比较扎实。</span></p>
<p><b>★烤色：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">巴斯克蛋糕偏向焦糖色，并没有烤得那么焦黑。可以依据个人喜好调整烘烤时间。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>【Q&amp;A】：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">（摘编自影片的留言区）</span></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>★材料问题：</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：这是什么奶油乳酪？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：曾</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">用安佳跟Pacific Cheese做过。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：减糖，只是甜度会降低吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：任何甜点都可以减糖，但不要过度，否则会影响口感和烤色。本食谱已先减过糖；如果不想吃太甜，可以先试着减10～15%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">看看效果如何。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：希望蛋量用公克数标示？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">食谱若没有特别注记，通常以中型蛋为主，大约是50</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">55克。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：可以用whipped cream代替heavy cream吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：可以。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：买不到whipping cream，能用牛奶和奶油替代吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：以牛奶替代</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，不用加奶油。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：低筋面粉能省略吗？?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面粉能让蛋糕定型，结构更稳定；不加的话，蛋糕会少一点支撑力。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：没有香草精怎么办？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：不加没关系，放香草精只是要添香、去蛋腥味。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：柠檬汁要100%的吗</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">？</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">柠檬汁是我们自己的偏好，不加也没关系。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>★做法问题：</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">换烤模，如何调整食谱里的配方？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：方法1. 不同烤模，食谱的配方调整，请</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">参考：</span><ahref="https://tahini.funique.info/doc/9e4fb19282319ccde7525d421b14adca"><span style="font-weight: 400;">https://tahini.funique.info/doc/9e4fb19282319ccde7525d421b14adca</span></a></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">鸡蛋的部分，要打散后秤重调整。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">注意：改烤模，配方没算对，如面粉跟鸡蛋的量不够，会影响蛋糕的凝结。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">方法</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">或者份量不变，将多出来的面糊装在小烤模里，把纸托放在马芬模里一起烤，可以做</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2?3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杯迷你巴斯克。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：使用不沾的蛋糕模，需要铺上烘焙纸吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">建议要用，不然很难脱模</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">为何用两层烘焙纸？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">如果烘焙纸够大张，铺一张即可。食谱</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">用7吋烤模，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">若只铺一层烘焙纸，有两边高度不足，面糊会溢出，因此用两张以十字交叉方式铺上。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：用手持搅拌机搅打奶油乳酪和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖，与使用刮刀拌匀，有何差别？?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：用机器较省力、且有效率。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪先搅打到很软，再拌入糖，这样比较省力，否则有点吃力，花得时间也较长。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：奶油乳酪糊</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加入鸡蛋搅打后，是否会油水分离？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">油水分离是正常的。鸡蛋内的液体，结合乳酪的油脂，一开始会有油水分离的状态，由于不断地搅打，注入了空气，质地会变得一致。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪加细砂糖时就很稀，加了蛋就更稀，何故？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪软化过度了。如果奶油乳酪软化成流动状，混合面糊后会呈现液状。奶油乳酪软化最佳状态：压下去有小小阻力即可。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：鲜奶油要放在室温吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：鲜奶油放在室温会变质。若是备好料，隔段时间才做，可以冰一下；但做之</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">前，在室温稍微放置十几分钟。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">若面粉未先跟鲜奶油混合，会影响成品吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">先混合面粉和鲜奶油，可以避免结块。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加入香草精以低速搅打，我打了几分钟，面糊无法像影片里那样流动，哪个步骤出错了？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">那个步骤只要混匀即可停止。你的面糊很稠，可能是多秤了面粉份量，使面糊干湿比例不同，或</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">是没有让奶油乳酪足够软化（留意不要软化到变成液体）。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：放</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在烤箱的最下层吗？?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">放中层。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：用六</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">吋烤模，时间需减短吗？?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">时间是估不准的，最好用观察，基本就是蛋糕要膨胀、上色、飘出香气，试过几次就很好拿捏。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">建议烤温先不变，只调整时间，这样比较好试。第一次烤要顾炉，从蛋糕的口味和上色，判断是否要微调。差不多是上色、有香味</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">就ok；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">不要烤太干，表面不必很焦黑，怕会烤到没口感。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">用八或九吋烤模，烘培时间不必延长吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">若体积变大，烘焙时间要增加。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：蛋糕趁</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">温热时吃，有流心的口感；若要吃到流心口感，在室温冷却多久？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">如果喜欢流心口感，可以缩短烘烤时间。出炉时摇晃蛋糕，周围一圈不会晃，中间一圈有点微晃即可，这样蛋糕中心会保有湿润感。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>★脱模和冷藏问题：</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋糕出炉时，需要脱模吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋糕出炉时都很软，千万不要脱模，否则会崩塌；要等到完全凉透再脱模。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">如何脱模？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">建议用活动模，蛋糕完全放凉后，提起烘焙纸就可以脱模，或者活动底盘向上一推就脱模了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：蛋糕</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">冷藏时，需要封盖或用保鲜膜吗？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">冷藏或室温的糕点，都要密封保存，以防水分蒸发太快。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">要冰六</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">时吗，还是冷藏一两小时即可？?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：蛋糕只冷藏</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">一两个小时，无法很好地定型。起士蛋糕需冷冻足够时间才能凝固，且能更好地融合味道。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>★成品案例探讨：</b><b>?</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋糕不够细致，有粗糙气孔，哪个环节需要调整？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">搅打过度，造成内部气泡过多，过筛有帮助。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：蛋糕</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">吃起来有点粉粉的，哪里出问题？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">烤太干了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">烤完蛋糕，为何表面会龟裂？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：一般来说，表面有一些裂纹算正常。若爆裂得很严重，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">通常是烤太久，可以缩短一点时间；若是没烤很久却裂了，就是烤温略高，可以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">减5?10 ?C；也可能是搅拌时，拌入太多空气，尽可能搅匀，但不要太过度。若面糊里有太多气泡，可以过筛2次看看。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：我的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋糕质感很松散，也不顺滑？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">可能是鸡蛋加入后，搅打时没有完全乳化。鸡蛋与奶油乳酪</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">乳化过程会花一点时间，完全乳化后会变得绵密浓稠，成品就会扎实，最多是中间有些湿软，但那是正常口感。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：我的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋糕有点太湿，不够扎实？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：可能是</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">烘烤时间不够，也可能是烤箱温度偏低。先确认有否到达烤箱的预热温度，只要烤温够，烘烤时间足，就不会水水的。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">总之，</span><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">蛋糕出炉时，边缘不太会摇晃，但中间会微晃，这是理想的成品。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">如果中间不太会晃，就是烤过熟了；如果中间是松散地晃动，则是未烤足够时间。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：蛋糕</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">冰过后，底下有奶油流出，何故？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：奶油乳酪过度软化，有点液化了。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">较常发生在软化奶油乳酪时，隔水加热的水温太热，或用微波炉软化，温度太高，让奶油乳酪油水分离。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Q：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋糕放凉冷藏，隔天拿出来，为何底层有点回潮湿湿的？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A：在高温下，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋糕隔着烘焙纸会产生水蒸气，可以放凉后取下烘焙纸，但要小心，此时蛋糕还是比较软；或者放进冰箱冷藏半小时，再拿出来移除烘焙纸。<strong>◎</strong></span></p>
