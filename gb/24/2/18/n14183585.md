<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】咸鱼头芥菜豆腐排骨汤～鲜甜靓汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183635-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>咸鱼头芥菜豆腐排骨汤。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong>芥菜是过年期间常见的菜肴，但是普遍不受青睐，是因为它的味道偏苦、纤维粗、口感不佳。那么要如何去除芥菜的苦涩味又能熬出最佳汤品呢？只要善用一些高汤或是高蛋白食物来煲汤就能中和苦味，而且吃出甘味，不妨试试看！下面介绍这一道靓汤，钙质丰富，其中使用硬豆腐和芥菜煲汤很耐煮不易过烂，保留口感又有食物本身的美味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">咸鱼头芥菜豆腐排骨汤</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>咸鱼头骨6 oz<br />
豆腐1盒<br />
芥菜1.5磅<br />
排骨1磅<br />
姜4片</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183629" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183629-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183629-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183629" class="wp-caption-text">材料（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 芥菜切成小段清洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183630" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183630-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183630-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183630" class="wp-caption-text">芥菜切成小段清洗干净备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 豆腐切小块。排骨洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183631" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183631-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183631-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183631" class="wp-caption-text">排骨洗干净备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油，放入姜片及咸鱼头骨煎香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183632" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183632-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183632-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183632" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，放入姜片及咸鱼头骨煎香。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 加入清水8杯，放入豆腐、排骨，水滚后转为中火煲30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183633" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183633-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183633-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183633" class="wp-caption-text">加入清水8杯，放入豆腐、排骨，水滚后转为中火煲30分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再加入芥菜煮约10分钟便可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183634" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183634-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183634" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183634-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183634" class="wp-caption-text">再加入介菜煮约10分钟便可。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>食谱说明：因为咸鱼头骨已经够咸了，所以不用再加盐调味。也可以加少许胡椒粉。◇</strong></span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>
<p>经历：</p>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
