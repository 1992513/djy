<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】三色四季豆炒鸡柳～百吃不腻的私房家常菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183575-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>多彩丰富的四季豆料理。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong>四季豆是搭配任何红萝卜、花枝、鸡柳或牛肉条等都好吃的蔬菜，它可以单做主菜也可以辅以配菜，百搭的食谱只要对味就是下饭的好滋味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">三色四季豆炒鸡柳</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>三色四季豆（共1磅）<br />
鸡胸肉8 oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许<br />
山核桃1 oz</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183576" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183576-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183576-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183576" class="wp-caption-text">材料（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>鸡汤1/3杯<br />
蚝油1汤匙<br />
糖1/2茶匙<br />
美极鲜酱油少许<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183577" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183577-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183577-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183577" class="wp-caption-text">调味（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 四季豆切去头尾，切半后洗净。鸡胸肉切成鸡柳，用盐、糖、胡椒粉和粟粉水拌匀，腌15分钟。</p>
<p>2. 烧热水，加入少许盐、鸡粉和姜汁酒，放入四季豆煮约2分钟，捞出沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183578" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183578-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183578-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183578" class="wp-caption-text">烧热水，加入少许盐、鸡粉和姜汁酒，放入四季豆煮约2分钟，捞出沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 鸡柳放入热水中浸煮一分半钟后捞出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183579" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183579-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183579-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183579" class="wp-caption-text">鸡柳放入热水中浸煮一分半钟后捞出备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉，放入四季豆和鸡柳炒1分钟，搅匀调味汁液倒入。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183580" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183580-daa57e7c4c67dc5ccbdd4986d2624b62.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183580-daa57e7c4c67dc5ccbdd4986d2624b62-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183580" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉，放入四季豆和鸡柳炒1分钟，搅匀调味汁液倒入。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 翻炒均匀后便可上碟，旁边放上山核桃便可。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183581" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183581-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14183581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183581-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183581" class="wp-caption-text">翻炒均匀后便可上碟，旁边放上山核桃便可。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>
<p>经历：</p>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
