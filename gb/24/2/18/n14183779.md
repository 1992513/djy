<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】粉葛盲鰽排骨汤～袪湿解热 简单易煲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183786-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>粉葛盲鰽排骨汤。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;">【小编语】“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%89%E8%91%9B.md#1">粉葛</a>”这种植物对某些人可能很陌生，它是制作太白粉的原料，而且有很高的药用价值，可以解毒袪湿。当天气潮湿和炎热时，来一碗粉葛汤，可以消除水肿，去除身体的疲倦感，而且简单好做，一点都不困难！</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%89%E8%91%9B.md#1">粉葛</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B2%E9%B0%BD.md#1">盲鰽</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8%E6%B1%A4.md#1">排骨汤</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>粉葛1.5磅<br />
赤小豆3 oz<br />
扁豆3 oz<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B2%E9%B0%BD.md#1">盲鰽</a>1条1.5磅<br />
排骨1磅<br />
蜜枣4粒<br />
陈皮1 块<br />
姜5片</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183789" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183789-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183789-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183789" class="wp-caption-text">材料（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【工具】</span></h3>
<p>1块纱布和棉绳</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 盲鰽洗净后沥干水，撒上少许盐两面抹匀，腌30分钟后备用。排骨用沸水烫过。<br />
2. 粉葛去皮后切块备用。赤小豆、扁豆、蜜枣、陈皮洗净后再用水浸泡30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183790" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183790-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183790-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183790" class="wp-caption-text">粉葛去皮后切块备用。赤小豆、扁豆、蜜枣、陈皮洗净后再用水浸泡30分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油，放入鱼，加入姜片，两面煎香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183791" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183791-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183791-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183791" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，放入鱼，加入姜片，两面煎香。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 鱼煎好后，用纱布和绵绳捆绑好备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183792" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183792-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183792-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183792" class="wp-caption-text">鱼煎好后，用纱布和绵绳捆绑好备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 汤锅内放16碗水和所有材料，大火煮滚15分钟后再转为小火煮2小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183793" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183793-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183793-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183793" class="wp-caption-text">汤锅内放16碗水和所有材料，大火煮滚15分钟后再转为小火煮2小时。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 最后加盐调味便可，材料可捞出沾酱油吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14183794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14183794" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183794-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14183794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14183794-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14183794" class="wp-caption-text">最后加盐调味便可，材料可捞出沾酱油吃。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">食谱说明：汤用小火炖了一个小时后，水可能会减少，可以再添加约2杯热水，然后再炖一个小时。◇</span></strong></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>

2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
