<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>去年珀斯房价百万俱乐部新增八区</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年02月11日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者彭妮澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span></span>2023<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年，珀斯</span><span lang="zh-TW">房价百万俱乐部迎</span><span lang="zh-TW">来了</span></span>8<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个新区，这些新区主要为沿海与河畔地区</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p>据《西澳人报》报导，新增的8个地区为Iluka、Shelley、Alfred Cove、Coogee、Melville、Bedfordale、Ocean Reef和White Gum Valley。珀斯现在有60多个地区的房价中位数超过100万澳元。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳房地产协会主席哈特女士（</span></span>Cath Hart<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，过去十年里，这八个地区的房价都接近百万澳元。她说</span></span>:<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">“近年来，许多地区都出现了扩张和发展，推高了这些地区的价格。</span></span>Shelley<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的房价增幅最高，其房屋销售中位价格从</span></span>2022<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>12<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月的</span></span>89.7<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元上涨到</span></span>2023<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>12<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月的</span></span>117.5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，上涨了</span></span>30.9%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span><span lang="zh-TW">”</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳房地产网站</span></span>Reiwa.com<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的数据显示，</span></span>Shelley <span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">区</span></span>2023<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年卖出最昂贵的房产位于</span></span>225 Riverton Drive North<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span>3<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月以</span></span>315<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的价格售出。</span></span>Iluka<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的房价增幅位居第二，年增长率为</span></span>19.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，其次是</span></span>Coogee<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（</span></span>14.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）、</span></span>Bedfordale<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（</span></span>11.4%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）和</span></span>Ocean Reef<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（</span></span>8.9%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">房产中介</span></span>Peard Real Estate<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">销售助理唐纳利（</span></span>Bridget Donnelly<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，</span></span>Iluka<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的房源数量不足以满足北部沿海地区规模适中的街区前所未有的需求。她说</span></span>:<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">“因为没有足够的房源，所以当我们拿到房源时，来电咨询就没停过。我们甚至在房子公共开放之前就已经收到报价，有时比我们的要价高出</span></span>10<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">至</span></span>20<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span><span lang="zh-TW">”</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">唐纳利女士表示，房子公共开放吸引了</span></span>50<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">到</span></span>100<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">组买家，但当地人面临来自东海岸买家的激烈竞争。她说：“我第一天就以</span></span>160<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的价格售出了一个房源。当我把它放到网上时，新南威尔士州的买家甚至没有见过它就买了它。</span><span lang="zh-TW">”</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">在珀斯房价百万的榜单中，</span></span>Peppermint Grove<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">区去年以</span></span>400<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元中位房价和</span></span>23.5%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的房价增长率位居第一</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">哈特女士表示，该地区一直位居排行榜榜首，在过去十年中每年都位居第一，但</span></span>2022<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年例外，当年该地区仅次于</span></span>Dalkeith<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">哈特女士预计珀斯今年的房价将强劲增长</span></span>10%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">左右</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
