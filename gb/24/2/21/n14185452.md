<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>至孝笃亲公所春宴 高朋满座庆龙年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185442-DSC06880-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙至孝笃亲公所2月17日在帝苑酒楼欢庆龙年，公所宗长、嘉宾及舞狮团合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年02月21日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿波士顿市报导）纽英崙地区侨界今年第二场、也是每年人数最多的春宴2月17日晚上盛大开场。在华埠帝苑酒楼，至孝笃亲公所携近800位来宾共同欢庆龙年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185435-DSC06569.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185435-DSC06569-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185435" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙至孝笃亲公所2月17日在帝苑酒楼举办春宴，与近800位来宾共庆龙年。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185449-DSC07339.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185449-DSC07339-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185449" class="wp-caption-text">职员们上台向所有宾客敬酒。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">至孝笃亲公所主席陈文珊、副主席陈铁坚感谢各位来宾热情参与这场庆典。“龙年代表力量和运气，巨龙将我们带入2024年。在新年里希望大家身体健康、繁荣昌盛。”?陈文珊说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">全美舜裔笃亲公所总理陈建立特别为这场春宴作诗：“龙飞凤舞兆丰年，至仁本孝艳阳天。笃亲公所同欢笑，守望相助是根源。帝苑楼头摆春宴，华人福利手相连。承先启后纽英崙，北美舜裔陈胡袁。”</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这场晚宴由陈黄海兰主持。宴会厅内座无虚席，现场气氛非常热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185440-DSC06715.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185440-DSC06715-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185440" class="wp-caption-text">胡清白鹤派舞狮献瑞。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185441-DSC06761.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185441-DSC06761-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185441" class="wp-caption-text">胡清白鹤派舞狮献瑞。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">到场参加春宴的嘉宾包括麻州经济发展厅厅长郝伊平（Yvonne Hao）、老人事务局局长陈倩（Elizabeth Chen）等州政府官员；有波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu），市议会主席路易琼（Ruthzee Louijeune?）、市议员费连（Ed Flynn）、桑塔纳（Henry Santana）、墨菲（Erin J. Murphy）、杜尔坎（Sharon Durkan）等波士顿政要；有驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏和侨教中心主任潘昭荣；有中华公所主席雷国辉及其他侨界代表；还有波士顿周边地区的政要嘉宾，如波士顿地区检察官海登（Kevin Hayden）、麦德福市议员曾御宸（Justin Tseng）等等。州长希莉（Maura Healey）亦委托代表，向至孝笃亲公所赠送了褒奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185434-DSC06557.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185434-DSC06557-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185434" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙至孝笃亲公所春宴，宗长们与嘉宾合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185439-DSC06660.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185439-DSC06660-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185439" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙至孝笃亲公所春宴，宗长们与嘉宾合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185448-DSC07291.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185448-DSC07291-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185448" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙至孝笃亲公所春宴，波士顿警员亦获邀参加。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">波士顿市长吴弭带着丈夫和两个儿子参加了当晚的春宴，共同感谢社区热情参与各项活动。她说：“非常荣幸代表这个社区，与大家一如既往地庆祝新年的到来。特别感谢公共安全合作伙伴、波士顿警察以及所有确保社区安全的人。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14185436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185436-DSC06574.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185436-DSC06574-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185436" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭抵达帝苑酒楼，参与纽英崙至孝笃亲公所龙年春宴。左起：陈仕维、陈建立、吴弭、陈文珊。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185438-DSC06644.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185438-DSC06644-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185438" class="wp-caption-text">吴弭与丈夫和两个儿子一起参与纽英崙至孝笃亲公所龙年春宴。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14185443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14185443" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185443-DSC07117.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14185443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14185443-DSC07117-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14185443" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员们参加纽英崙至孝笃亲公所龙年春宴，并颁赠褒奖。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">市议会主席路易琼说：“这个城市之所以伟大，是因为你们所有人，因为我们强大的社区。”</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">晚宴充满了文化交流和互动，不仅有胡清白鹤派舞狮表演，还有激动人心的抽奖环节。最后还有热闹的流行歌曲演唱。宾客们共同迎接龙年的来临，期盼着新一年的幸福和顺遂。</p>
