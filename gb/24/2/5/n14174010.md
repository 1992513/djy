<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】三文鱼福袋~贺年美食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174015-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>用菲洛皮（filo pastry）可以快速制作美味的酥皮料理。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>年节餐桌少不了煎炸的菜肴。今天分享用菲洛皮（filo pastry）制作咸味菜肴，不用油炸也十分酥脆。三文鱼馅料用意大利香醋调味，经过高温短时间烘烤，果香味被激发吸入鱼肉，非常美味。</p>
<p><strong>料理贴士：</strong>酥皮在室温下几分钟就会变干，所以要先在酥皮周边涂抹足够的油，再涂中间部分；这样酥皮的边缘才能烤出金黄色，否则容易烤焦。菲洛皮可换用其它酥皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14174025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14174025" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174025-2-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14174025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174025-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14174025" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>5张市售菲洛酥皮（filo pastry），每张尺寸为9X14英寸，制作6个福袋<br />
1片野生三文鱼片，约150克<br />
1汤匙意大利香醋<br />
1/4茶匙盐<br />
1/4茶匙现磨黑胡椒<br />
1汤匙橄榄油，蒸鱼用</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1. 碗中放入鱼，添加盐、黑胡椒和油拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14174039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14174039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174039-3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14174039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174039-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14174039" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 烧沸蒸锅的水，放入鱼肉蒸12分钟。蒸的时候用碗盖或锡箔纸盖住碗，以免水蒸汽进入碗中。若用电蒸汽炉，以400华氏度蒸12分钟。<br />
3. 蒸好的鱼肉静置到不烫手，捣碎并混合意大利香醋。<br />
4. 预热烤箱350华氏度。<br />
5. 准备菲洛酥皮：5张酥皮，每张都涂抹橄榄油，先涂抹周边再中间，然后叠加在一起。<br />
6. 叠加的酥皮分切成6小片。每片中间放入鱼肉，捏合酥皮四边，留出中间的酥皮别捏紧，以帮助散发鱼肉的热蒸汽，同时烤干香醋激发香味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14174041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14174041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174041-4-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14174041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174041-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14174041" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 最后以350华氏度烤15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14174043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14174043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174043-5-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14174043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174043-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14174043" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
