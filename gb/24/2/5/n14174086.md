<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】杏子酱脆皮鸡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174123-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>法国传统杏子酱内含大块果肉，用作烤鸡酱汁酸甜可口。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年02月05日讯】法国传统杏子酱含有至少一半果肉，其制作的酱汁使烤鸡色泽明亮，滋味酸甜可口。这份食谱使用童子鸡，适合大多数小烤箱和气炸锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14174122" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14174122" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174122-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14174122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174122-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14174122" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14174121" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14174121" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174121-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14174121" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174121-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14174121" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食材准备</h3>
<p>1只童子鸡（cornish hen），约1.5磅<br />
1茶匙盐<br />
1茶匙现磨黑胡椒<br />
2汤匙无盐黄油，软化<br />
1/4杯法国杏子酱（Apricot jam）<br />
1小块新鲜姜，压榨出1茶匙姜汁<br />
1/2杯市售无盐鸡汤</p>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1. 童子鸡里外涂抹盐、黑胡椒，然后外皮涂抹黄油。静置半小时。<br />
2. 气炸锅开启350华氏度，煮45分钟。若用烤箱，烹煮温度和时间相同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14174120" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14174120" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174120-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14174120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14174120-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14174120" class="wp-caption-text">刚出炉的脆皮鸡。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 鸡只快烤好时，在锅中用小火将鸡汤、杏酱、姜汁和1/4茶匙盐煮至粘稠，即酱汁浓缩为1/4杯。</p>
<p>4. 涂完酱汁的鸡只再送回气炸锅以425华氏度烤5分钟。出炉静置到不烫手即可分切。◇</p>
