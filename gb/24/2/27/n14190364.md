<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>卫生局表扬卫生保健志工 朝向智慧苗栗迈进</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190365-666654-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>县府结合产官学 齐力创智慧苗栗迈进 幸福起步走（许享富／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月27日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）卫生局2月27日上午在县府大厅表扬绩优卫生保健志工，感谢他们无私的奉献与辛劳，并展示各项智慧医疗科技与创新应用，宣示苗县的智慧健康照顾继续往前迈进。</p>
<p>卫生局今天上午召开“智慧苗栗 幸福起步走”记者会，除表扬125位健生保健绩优志工，卫生局长蕊仙并说明苗县发展医疗结合科技创新应用的成果，国卫院研究员林俊吉简报说明建置偏乡远距照护医疗网络、文大运健系主任戴旭志简报创新AI Coach体感侦测健身房智慧科技辅助系统等，现场且展示智慧随身包、高智能成人尿布等尖端设备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14190366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190366" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190366-666655-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14190366" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190366-666655-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190366" class="wp-caption-text">活泼的头份市卫生局志工。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14190367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190367" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190367-666656-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14190367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190367-666656-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190367" class="wp-caption-text">卫生局长张蕊仙表示，感谢产官学与中央的协助，配合，朝卫生局的计划：智慧苗栗-智慧医院、偏乡远距医疗与5G远距诊疗加倍护心。同时感谢卫生保健志工的贡献，朝幸福苗栗。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会中，县长钟东锦、县议员廖秀红、黎尚安、廖英利、张志宇、杨明烨、杨文昌、禹耀东、许樱萍、余文忠、孙素娥等人矢志继续朝向智慧医疗迈进，以提高医疗服务的效率，加强对乡亲的关爱与照顾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14190368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190368" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190368-666657-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14190368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190368-666657-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190368" class="wp-caption-text">国卫院群体健康科学研究所林峻吉博士的以 ［5G智慧科学善偏乡医疗环境计划］成果简报。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14190369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190369" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190369-666658-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14190369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190369-666658-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190369" class="wp-caption-text">台湾远距智慧之爱公益联盟徐克宇常务理事以［智慧医疗服务以低碳永续健康福址城市为目标］作简报。 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14190370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190370" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190370-666659-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14190370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190370-666659-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190370" class="wp-caption-text">文大运动与健康学系主任戴旭志教授以［智慧健康城市2.0］为简报。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局长张蕊仙指出，该局在110年推动“远距照护医疗网络”，运用5G远距会诊平台，达成医疗零偏乡、零距离的照护，112年推动的健康福祉计划，利用大数据及结合视觉化应用，建立卫生业务的战情仪表板，掌握县市卫政数据，进行分析及综合应用、风险预警、紧急应变、施政规划等决策管理，现场展示的心情温度计APP、12导程人工智慧心电图判读系统等创新科技，为乡亲带来更多的便利和安全感，为乡亲打造更加健康、更加幸福的生活环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14190371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190371" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190371-666660-450x217.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14190371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190371-666660-450x217.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="217" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190371" class="wp-caption-text">厂商的智慧医疗 产品展现。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14190372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190372" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190372-666661-450x178.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14190372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190372-666661-450x178.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="178" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190372" class="wp-caption-text">厂商的智慧医疗产品展现。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>县长钟东锦表示，这是张蕊仙任内的最后一场记者会，感谢她在任内努力认真的付出，尤其防疫工作表现的可圈可点，苗栗医疗资源不足，卫生局积极规划将智慧科技带进苗栗，透过远距照护医疗网不必舟车劳顿，甚至1台手机，当身体出现状况自然就会接到通知，是很好的保健，希望乡亲也改变一下观念，就近就医，才能促使苗栗的医疗越来越好，期盼大家一起努力，开创苗栗更好的幸福感。</p>
