<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】味噌萝卜汤~双煎溏心蛋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190477-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>萝卜搭配鸡蛋可以快速煮出鲜美的汤料理。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年02月27日讯】过年大鱼大肉吃多了，来一碗萝卜汤舒缓肠胃吧。唐代《食疗本草》曰：“萝卜（莱菔）根：消食下气。甚利关节，除五脏中风，练五脏中恶气。服之令人白净肌细。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14190479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190479" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190479-2-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14190479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190479-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190479" class="wp-caption-text">萝卜搭配鸡蛋可以快速煮出鲜美的汤料理。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>2杯白萝卜，切细丝<br />
2枚鸡蛋<br />
1汤匙白味噌<br />
3杯沸水<br />
适量白胡椒粉、盐</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1.烧一壶沸水待用。<br />
2.加热炒锅，放油，磕入两枚鸡蛋。鸡蛋上面撒适量白胡椒粉和盐。<br />
3.待鸡蛋底部煎定型，用锅铲将其中一个煎蛋盖在另一个煎蛋上，让未凝固的蛋液将煎蛋粘在一起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14190481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190481-3-450x321.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14190481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190481-3-450x321.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190481" class="wp-caption-text">萝卜搭配鸡蛋可以快速煮出鲜美的汤料理。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.加入3杯沸水，放入萝卜丝，继续煮5分钟即可出锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14190483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190483" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190483-4-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14190483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190483-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190483" class="wp-caption-text">萝卜搭配鸡蛋可以快速煮出鲜美的汤料理。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.汤快煮好时，取锅中热汤化开小碗中的味噌，待汤煮好时拌入。汤中可以额外添加姜、白胡椒粉调味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14190486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14190486" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190486-5-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14190486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14190486-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14190486" class="wp-caption-text">萝卜搭配鸡蛋可以快速煮出鲜美的汤料理。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>料理贴士：味噌可以用适量盐替换。此外，将两个煎蛋对折煮，蛋黄不易煮熟就能得到溏心蛋。不喜欢吃溏心蛋的话，多煮几分钟蛋黄就全熟了。加入沸水后一直保持大火是煮出奶白色汤汁的关键步骤。◇</p>
