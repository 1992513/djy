<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：高雄黄色小鸭风潮 吸引逾四百万游客</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14180929-2402140432082378.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
