<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>家庭日长周末大多地区举办加拿大最大钓鱼展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171790-1-600x400.png" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年02月02日讯】加拿大家庭日2月16日至18日长周末，大多地区密西沙加的国际中心将举办为期三天的春季钓鱼和船艇展（Spring Fishing &amp; Boat Show）：加拿大最大的钓鱼展。</p>
<p class="p1">喜欢钓鱼的朋友，别错过这个家庭日长周末好去处——钓鱼爱好者的天堂！</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/2/n14171738.md#1/4-946" rel="attachment wp-att-14171794"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14171794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171794-4-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">在春季钓鱼和船艇展上，加拿大钓鱼界名人等众多钓鱼好手，将举办研讨会，上演钓鱼真人秀。</p>
<p class="p1"><strong>渔具盛宴?</strong></p>
<p class="p1">这将是一场展示所有新钓具和配件的渔具盛宴，还提供去年产品的大量特价商品！</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/2/n14171738.md#1/9-340" rel="attachment wp-att-14171799"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14171799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171799-9-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">零售参展商提供各种钓具、钓竿、钓线轮、船艇和马达，以及拖钓船马达、GPS导航装置、水下摄影机等配件，以及其他钓鱼和船艇相关产品和服务。</p>
<p class="p1">面见制造商和专业工作人员，他们将展示并会回答您有关其新产品的所有问题。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/2/n14171738.md#1/3-1291" rel="attachment wp-att-14171793"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14171793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171793-3-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">对法规或照顾鱼类有疑问？ 安省自然资源厅的专家将在春季钓鱼和船艇展上为您解答，满足有关您最爱的钓鱼运动的所有问题！</p>
<p class="p1"><strong><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/2/n14171738.md#1/7-468" rel="attachment wp-att-14171797"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14171797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171797-7-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></strong></p>
<p class="p1"><strong>船艇盛宴</strong></p>
<p class="p1">了解新渔船和配件、获取资讯，来春季钓鱼和船艇展吧！新船、引擎、拖车？在大量的船艇品牌和发动机中，货比三家，做出您最明智的新船购买决定。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/2/n14171738.md#1/5-767" rel="attachment wp-att-14171795"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14171795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171795-5-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">友好的当地经销商，会为您提供度过一个充满水上钓鱼乐趣的夏季所需的一切。</p>
<p class="p1">记得查看全新、非当前型号的产品回扣和折扣，充分利用展会的特别定价。</p>
<p class="p1"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14171792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171792-2-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></p>
<p class="p1"><strong>培养下一代</strong></p>
<p class="p1">点燃孩子们的热情，培养下一代钓鱼者！ 孩子们整个周末都可以在免费鳟鱼池塘享受钓鱼乐！</p>
<p class="p1"><strong><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/2/2/n14171738.md#1/6-539" rel="attachment wp-att-14171796"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14171796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id14171796-6-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></strong></p>
<p class="p1"><strong> 度假村、木屋和旅行用品</strong></p>
<p class="p1">多个度假村和旅行用品商，将乐于为您展示众多安省的户外景点、度假胜地和活动。</p>
<p class="p1">无论是钓鱼或狩猎小屋、荒野导览，还是户外探险之旅，旅行用品商和度假村业主提供的住宿、设施和工作人员，将为您与家人带来美妙的户外体验。</p>
