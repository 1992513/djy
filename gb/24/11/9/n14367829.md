<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：阿尔卑斯高山秋色与巴洛克艺术教堂</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367836-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一座建于18世纪初的巴洛克教堂位于法国阿尔卑斯山海拔 1816 米的“神奇”山泉旁边。（张妮/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年11月10日讯】穿过金黄的落叶松森林，眼前突然一亮，一座美丽的教堂屹立在高山脚下，米白色的墙壁和背后山峰形成鲜明的对比。</p>
<p>这座教堂名叫维尔纳特圣母院（Notre-Dame des Vernettes） ，位于法国萨瓦省的 Peisey-Nancroix 市，于18世纪初建于一个位于海拔 1816 米的“神奇”山泉旁边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367837" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367837-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367837-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-6-600x400.jpg" alt="一座建于18世纪初的巴洛克教堂位于法国阿尔卑斯山海拔 1816 米的“神奇”山泉旁边。（张妮/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367837" class="wp-caption-text">一座建于18世纪初的巴洛克教堂位于法国阿尔卑斯山海拔 1816 米的“神奇”山泉旁边。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这座教堂是巴洛克艺术和洛可可风格的骄傲代表，建于1722年至1742年间。走进教堂内，前方主祭坛多彩而精美木雕刻和教堂顶部的壁画展现了圣神的辉煌，让人印象深刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367848-6a6ba6951e8acb5c7cc2e98c510405f0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367848-6a6ba6951e8acb5c7cc2e98c510405f0-600x397.jpg" alt="教堂内主祭坛多彩而精美木雕刻。（维基百科）" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367848" class="wp-caption-text">教堂内主祭坛多彩而精美木雕刻。（维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14367839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367839-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367839-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-8-600x400.jpg" alt="教堂顶部的壁画展现了圣神的辉煌。（张妮/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367839" class="wp-caption-text">教堂顶部的壁画展现了圣神的辉煌。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14367838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367838" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367838-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367838-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-7-600x400.jpg" alt="教堂顶部的壁画展现了圣神的辉煌。（张妮/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367838" class="wp-caption-text">教堂顶部的壁画展现了圣神的辉煌。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教堂俯瞰山谷，位于 Aiguille Grive山（2,732 米）的山脚下，面对Bellec?te山脉，其最高峰为 3,417 米。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367841-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367841-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-10-600x400.jpg" alt="维尔纳特圣母院（Notre-Dame des Vernettes）的另一侧。（张妮/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367841" class="wp-caption-text">维尔纳特圣母院（Notre-Dame des Vernettes）的另一侧。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14367840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367840-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-9.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367840-Jeanne-Williams-31102024-9-600x400.jpg" alt="教堂古老木门上的门把。（张妮/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367840" class="wp-caption-text">教堂古老木门上的门把。（张妮/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
