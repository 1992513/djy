<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>红炅剪短发太清秀 让卢允瑞产生危机意识</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367695-241109043006100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>卢允瑞、红炅、金珉周与导演出席韩国电影《听说》试映会。（车库娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月09日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）改编自同名台湾青春爱情经典电影的韩国电影《听说》，由卢允瑞、红炅、金珉周主演，电影11月6日在韩国上映首日就击退《猛毒最终章：最后一舞》空降韩国票房冠军。</p>
<p>《听说》故事叙述龙俊（红炅 饰）在打工途中对夏天（卢允瑞 饰）一见钟情，夏天的妹妹秋天（金珉周 饰）也为两人加油，希望他们会有好的结果，没想到却因一次意外，让夏天决定要远离龙俊……</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367701" style="width: 419px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367701-241109043011100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367701" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367701-241109043011100707.jpg" alt="" width="419" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367701" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《听说》由卢允瑞、红炅主演。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卢允瑞和红炅（曾译作洪庆，韩方已正名）为《听说》首度合作，对于合作感想，卢允瑞表示：“我第一次看到红炅演员时，他的头发是长的，但在试装时他把头发剪短了，看起来十分清秀、清纯，这让我产生了危机意识，觉得自己在清纯这方面可能会输给他。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367702-241109043013100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367702" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367702-241109043013100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367702" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《听说》由卢允瑞、红炅主演。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卢允瑞还透露：“在内部试映会稍微看了一下电影，有个场面让我印象深刻，就是‘龙俊’骑机车要去见‘夏天’的路上，他用手语练习比自己的名字，看了那样的即兴演出，觉得那让‘龙俊’这个角色更生动了，发现红炅演员真的做了很多功课，看到那个场面心情真的很好！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367704-241109043003100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367704" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367704-241109043003100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367704" class="wp-caption-text">红炅主演韩国电影《听说》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《听说》前几天在韩国举行试映会，金泰梨、边佑锡、申承浩、崔显旭、THE BOYZ成员柱延、aespa成员Giselle等明星都到场支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367705" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367705-241109043008100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367705" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367705-241109043008100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367705" class="wp-caption-text">边佑锡出席韩国电影《听说》试映会。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14367706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367706" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367706-241109043023100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367706" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367706-241109043023100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367706" class="wp-caption-text">金泰梨出席韩国电影《听说》试映会。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14367707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367707" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367707-241109043016100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367707" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367707-241109043016100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367707" class="wp-caption-text">申承浩、崔显旭出席韩国电影《听说》试映会。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前《听说》在韩国知名电影网“NAVER”的观众评分高达8.51分，韩国知名电影院“MEGABOX”的观众评分8.7分，CGV电影院观众满意度97%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367709" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367709-241109043026100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367709" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367709-241109043026100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367709" class="wp-caption-text">THE BOYZ成员李柱延出席韩国电影《听说》试映会。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14367710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367710" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367710-241109043001100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14367710" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367710-241109043001100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367710" class="wp-caption-text">aespa成员Giselle出席韩国电影《听说》试映会。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由卢允瑞、红炅、金珉周主演的韩国版《听说》，将于12月6日在台湾上映。</p>
