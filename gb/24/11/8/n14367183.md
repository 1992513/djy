<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>县议会第20届第4次总质询 议员 徐筱菁 翁杰 邱毓兴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367195-722275-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>县议员邱毓兴质询（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年11月08日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）徐筱菁、翁杰与邱毓兴议员分别提有关议题，如 新生儿幸福卡、AI 救灾防护SOP、台61房里匝道案（台中市部分）、大硅谷计划执行等提案，请县府参考他县的办法，符合乡民的意愿，同时合法规下去谋最大利益。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367184" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367184-722265-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14367184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367184-722265-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367184" class="wp-caption-text">县议会第20届第4次定期会11月8日进入总质询议程，（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>议员徐筱菁提：居家就诊的绿色通道、AI 救灾防护SOP、尝试新设新生儿幸福卡等探讨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367185" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367185-722266-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14367185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367185-722266-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367185" class="wp-caption-text">议员徐筱菁质询（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县议员翁杰提：收支划分法修法的看法、消防局采购不同于中央与地方的习用的M牌通讯改以K牌，所面临的整合问题、白沙屯童综合医院设立、代理教师的叙薪、老人与身心障碍的生活杂支费、敬老卡如何缩小城乡差使用等议题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367186" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367186-722267-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14367186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367186-722267-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367186" class="wp-caption-text">议员徐筱菁质询 增加儿童福利卡 财政许可下（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县议员邱毓兴提：切大硅谷推动方案的范围及采取何种方式征收用地，并质询兴建天花湖水库的可行性，竹南大埔重划区的自来水接管问题等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14367187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367187" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367187-722268-450x290.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14367187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367187-722268-450x290.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367187" class="wp-caption-text">议员徐筱菁质询 新型能 AI结合的消防SOP 规范要规划（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14367199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14367199" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367199-722279-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14367199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14367199-722279-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14367199" class="wp-caption-text">县长钟东锦答询; 大砂谷推动方案，必须全面考虑如废弃物、废弃水、水资源、电力、交通与环境共荣，同时连焚化炉的负荷均须全方位的规划，他也赞同天花湖的设立。代理教师的叙薪问题、新生儿的副利卡会在合法规、财政许可去做最大改善。台61线的匝道案，他会出席与卢市长沟通尽速解决。县府全力以赴，让苗栗县脱胎换骨。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
