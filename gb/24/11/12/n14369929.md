<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>BTBA迎新 分享求职经验 展望新年活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369918-DSC08615-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿台湾人生物科技协会（BTBA）11月10日举办迎新餐会，80多人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月13日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）11月10日中午，哈佛大学Lehman Hall大厅内欢笑连连。波士顿地区最大台湾人团体之一，“波士顿台湾人生物科技协会”（BTBA）在这里举办迎新餐会，新老成员分享求职经验，展望新一年的丰富活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14369926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369926-IMG_7266-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369926-IMG_7266-3-600x341.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="341" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369926" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿台湾人生物科技协会（BTBA）11月10日举办迎新餐会，80多人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>前辈热心分享</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>新人现学现用</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天BTBA分享会的两位主讲人，林致中是2017年加入该会的资深成员，许书瑀则是去年开始从该会获益的新成员。他们都谈到，BTBA的活动及资源，帮助他们发展技能，找到了合适的工作。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在主持人Erica Sun的询问下，两位主讲人分享了他们给职场新人的建议。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14369921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369921-DSC08629.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369921-DSC08629-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369921" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）林致中、许书瑀分享找工作经验，Erica Sun主持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">谈到面试及社交活动的“自我介绍”环节，林致中笑言，他会说一些有趣的轶事，让别人记住自己，比如说自己的名字是“陈致中的致中”，“英文名叫Jerry，因为小时候很喜欢看《Tom and Jerry》”。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">许书瑀还说，她的名字用英文很难念，有时她跟老外说念“Xiu Xiu Xiu”，一下就给别人留下深刻印象。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">谈到参加大型活动前的准备，两位主讲人分享的观点包括完善自己的Linkedin简历，了解讲者的背景，准备好问题，以及坐在前排与讲者互动等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14369922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369922-DSC08631.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369922-DSC08631-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369922" class="wp-caption-text">听众与主讲人交流。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14369923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369923-DSC08633.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369923-DSC08633-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369923" class="wp-caption-text">听众与主讲人交流。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14369925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369925-DSC08642.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369925-DSC08642-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369925" class="wp-caption-text">听众与主讲人交流。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">听了他们的分享后，听众提问时纷纷现学现用，以有趣的方式报出自己的名字和爱好。多位与会者谈到自己面对生涯抉择或生活低谷时的困惑，与主讲人交流。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">此外，已在生技行业深耕数十年的许翠玲和许益祥也参与了这次迎新餐会，与新老成员们交流。许翠玲分享了她找到第一份工作的经历，并鼓励大家追寻梦想。许益祥还介绍了自己的公司和哈佛实验室，欢迎感兴趣的人申请加入。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14369924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369924-DSC08635.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369924-DSC08635-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369924" class="wp-caption-text">资深会员许翠玲分享找到第一份工作的经历。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14369928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369928-DSC08639.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369928-DSC08639-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369928" class="wp-caption-text">资深会员许益祥介绍自己的公司和哈佛实验室。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>收获褒奖</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>展望新年</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天，波士顿侨教中心主任高家富为参与活动的80多人送上祝福；并代表侨委会，向BTBA新任会长胡金儒和罗维中，以及卸任会长吴佩容和蔡明儒赠送感谢状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14369919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369919" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369919-DSC08619.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369919-DSC08619-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369919" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿台湾人生物科技协会（BTBA）会长获褒奖，左起：现任会长罗维中、胡金儒、侨教中心主任高家富、卸任会长蔡明儒、吴佩容。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处科技组秘书杨明仪也到场，代表科技部向大家致以勉励。他介绍了科技组的业务，表达了科技部对BTBA的重视。杨明仪还提到，国科会12月将与BTBA合作举办“2030计划”说明会，鼓励学者前往台湾发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14369917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369917-DSC08612.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369917-DSC08612-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369917" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富参与BTBA迎新活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14369920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369920-DSC08622.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369920-DSC08622-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369920" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿经文处科技组秘书杨明仪向会员们致以勉励。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">两位会长胡金儒和罗维中为新老成员们介绍今年底及2025年的活动计划。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在本次迎新餐会之后，BTBA备受赞誉的“Mentor Mentee”项目将于12月8日开始。然后在12月13日，BTBA还将与日本台湾人生物科技协会（JTBA）合作举办座谈，介绍日、美生技行业生态的差别。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14369916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369916-DSC08609.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369916-DSC08609-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369916" class="wp-caption-text">会长罗维中（左）和胡金儒介绍2025年的活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14369927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14369927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369927-IMG_7267.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14369927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14369927-IMG_7267-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14369927" class="wp-caption-text">BTBA迎新餐会地点在哈佛大学Lehman Hall。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">到了2025年，BTBA计划在1月举办关于AI应用的讲座，2月邀集会员滑雪郊游。2月和4月还分别有诺贝尔奖系列讲座，主题分别关于AI和mRNA。5月BTBA将有团建活动，6月将有干部研讨会，7月将有关于传染病的座谈。2025年的年会，也将在夏季举办。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">本次餐会还有社交破冰环节，现场气氛非常热络。◇</p>
