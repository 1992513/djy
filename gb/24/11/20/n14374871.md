<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】苹果酱紫甘蓝~电饭锅做法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374872-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>紫甘蓝搭配苹果酱简单料理，即是酸甜可口的开胃菜。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月20日讯】紫甘蓝搭配苹果奶油酱在电饭锅简单料理，不用顾火就能吃到酸甜可口的小菜，果酱所含的酸还能够保留紫甘蓝的紫红色。（自制苹果奶油酱<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/10/26/n14358365.md#1">链接点这里</a>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14374874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14374874" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374874-2-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14374874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374874-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14374874" class="wp-caption-text">料理苹果酱紫甘蓝所需食材。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备：</h2>
<p>3杯切碎的紫甘蓝<br />
1/3杯苹果奶油酱<br />
1/4茶匙盐<br />
1/8茶匙芹菜籽<br />
适量初榨橄榄油<br />
适量新鲜苹果作装饰</p>
<figure id="attachment_14374875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14374875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374875-3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14374875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374875-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14374875" class="wp-caption-text">苹果酱煮紫甘蓝的料理步骤。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14374879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14374879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374879-4-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14374879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374879-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14374879" class="wp-caption-text">苹果酱煮紫甘蓝。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>料理步骤：</h2>
<p>1. 电饭锅（日本电饭锅）中放入紫甘蓝、苹果酱、芹菜籽和盐拌开，再加油拌匀。<br />
2. 开启米饭快煮功能，煮30分钟。</p>
<p>料理贴士：水洗过的紫甘蓝，叶片带了少许水分，所以锅中没有添加水。如果使用的蔬菜比较干，需要适当加水。</p>
