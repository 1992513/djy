<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>瞩目电车尽在此处  2024年度西雅图国际汽车展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372564-Corvette_2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Corvette Z06。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月17日讯】（大纪元记者李奥西雅图报导）踏入11月之际，西雅图的冬天也渐渐接近，同时也是汽车界迎接新一年的开始，在北美西海岸地区比较令人熟悉的是洛杉矶国际车展，对于较北地区的我们，就不用出远门也可参观“西雅图国际汽车展”，尤其对于准备购买汽车人士，是一处可看到各种品牌各类型号汽车的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372559" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372559-01_SIAS-e1731783408473.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372559-01_SIAS-e1731783408473.jpg" alt="西雅图国际汽车展。（李奥／大纪元）" width="600" b="400" class="size-full wp-image-14372559" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372559" class="wp-caption-text">2023年度西雅图国际汽车展。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年西雅图国际汽车展继续在西雅图市的Lumen Field Event Center举行，这次汽车展展出多种不同种类汽车，包括四门轿车、多用途休旅车、卡车、跨界车及电动汽车，当然也有令人动心的超级跑车。今年展出汽车数量达到300多辆。其中不乏在西北地区首次发表的汽车，包括Porsche Macan EV及GMC Sierra EV。</p>
<p>今年Nissan车厂除了继续展出电动跨界车Ariya，也带来了全新设计的第二代Kicks。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14372571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372571-Nissan_Kicks_1-e1731782816477.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372571-Nissan_Kicks_1-e1731782816477.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-full wp-image-14372571" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372571" class="wp-caption-text">新一代Nissan Kicks。（（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>图片Nissan_Kicks_1.JPG：李奥／大纪元）</p>
<p>在去年Honda展出了Pre-Production的Prologue，是一辆电动跨界车，今年展出的是量产车。同厂的豪华品牌Acura也展出电车ZDX，另一瞩目的是Integra S。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372567-Honda_Prologue_1-e1731782860437.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372567-Honda_Prologue_1-e1731782860437.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-full wp-image-14372567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372567" class="wp-caption-text">Honda Prologue量产车。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14372560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372560" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372560-Acura_ZDX_1-e1731782894295.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372560-Acura_ZDX_1-e1731782894295.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-full wp-image-14372560" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372560" class="wp-caption-text">Acura ZDX电动车。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14372577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372577-Acura_Integra_1-e1731783593110.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372577-Acura_Integra_1-e1731783593110.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-full wp-image-14372577" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372577" class="wp-caption-text">Acura Integra S。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Toyota则继续展出Crown轿车及Prius混能车，另外也展出了Land Cruiser 1958。至于Toyota的豪华品牌Lexus，展出了TX及GX车系。Subaru品牌展出了与Toyota bZ4X同根同源的Solterra电动车，以及它的新一代WRX。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372574-Toyota_Land_Crusier_1958_1-e1731782956942.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372574-Toyota_Land_Crusier_1958_1-e1731782956942.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-full wp-image-14372574" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372574" class="wp-caption-text">Land Cruiser 1958。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>韩国车厂Kia带来了EV9，是一辆中型电动SUV，同时也展出了新车K4，还有2025年Sportage Hybrid！Kia的母厂Hyundai展厅，则展出了IONIQ 5及IONIQ 6两兄弟，以及Sonata及Elantra。</p>
