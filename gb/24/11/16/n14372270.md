<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】十段变速 2025 Honda Pilot Black Edition</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372287-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年11月16日讯】今年Honda提供的Pilot为“全黑”版（Black Edition），虽然称为全黑版，但事实上测试的Pilot Black Edition是红色，只是某些配件是使用黑色，令车身看起来更有神秘感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372271" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372271-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372271-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_03-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372271" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Honda Pilot Black Edition。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，Pilot Black Edition装有四个“全黑”色合金轮圈，直径为20&#8243;，设计为五幅三叉式，配Bridgestone的255／50R20轮胎。前方进风口栏栅也是全黑色，并附有“Black Edition”徽章，而车窗框、门把手、前灯雾灯外框都是改以黑色涂装，务求符合“黑色”主题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372272" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372272-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372272-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_08-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372272" class="wp-caption-text">“全黑”色合金轮圈。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025年款Honda Pilot是车系的第四代产品，新Pilot的换上了LED头灯，并在头灯组下方有另一LED饰条，同时雾灯也是采用LED。尾部灯组内部指挥灯、倒车灯及刹车灯位置也有改变。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372274" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372274-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372274-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_04-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372274" class="wp-caption-text">LED头灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车内也是以“黑色”为主，但在不同地方以红色作点缀，例如仪表板是黑色底配以红色指针，黑色座椅配以红色缝线，引擎启动键也是红色。前排座椅背部都刻有“Black Edition”字样。仪表板是10.2&#8243;萤幕（Elite及Black Edition），但仍采用两圆仪表设计，在每个仪表中间均可显示不同讯息，也可显示耗油量、音响或导航讯息。在萤幕左右是两个实体仪表，显示引擎水温及油缸存量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372275" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372275-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_25.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372275-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_25-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372275" class="wp-caption-text">车内也是以“黑色”为主。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Honda Pilot有多种型号，由于LX版已被取消，入门版变为Sport版，价格为40,200美元起跳，再到EX-L、TrailSport、Touring、Elite，以至Black Edition是最高级别，Sport及EX-L是固定8座位设计，而TrailSport则是固定7座位，而其他更高级的版本，中排的中间座位可收起，所以既可成为7座位，又可成为8座位的汽车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372277" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372277-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372277-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_12-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372277" class="wp-caption-text">Black Edition是最高级别。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025年款Honda Pilot保留了“Cabin Talk In-Car PA System ”，就是车内广播，那么在外游时，驾驶者就可为作为导游，向车内乘客讲解。至于“Cabin Control”，即可连接手机的CabinControl程序，则改名为“HondaLink”，便可透过手机控制车内音响、恒温系统，也可透过HondaLink连接家里的MyQ车库开关及Alexa设备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372278" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372278-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372278-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_19-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372278" class="wp-caption-text">中控台萤幕不大。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全设备，Honda Pilot具有汽车稳定辅助（VSA）、电子刹车分布（EBD）、刹车辅助（Brake Assist），以至最普通的安全气囊、安全带，Honda都一一俱备娱乐。另外，Honda也备有Honda Sensing先进系统，包括碰撞缓解制动系统（CMBS）、前向碰撞警告（FCW）、道路偏离减轻（RDM）、车道偏离警告（LDW）、车道保持辅助系统（LKAS）和自适应巡航控制（ACC）。Honda Passport获得了NCAP五星级整车评分安全评级和IIHS最高安全评级等级。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372281" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372281-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372281-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_18-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372281" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板是10.2&#8243;萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于Pilot Black Edition的引擎则与其他版本一样，都是一副3.5升的V6 i-VTEC引擎，配缸内直喷技术，DOHC，24气阀。峰值输出285匹马力，扭矩262磅尺，所有版本均配10速自动变速箱；带有方向盘后方的Paddle Shift。而VCM技术，该系统可根据车的行驶状态，使工作的汽缸数量在6个和3个缸之间自动切换，以帮助减少燃油消耗。例如在起动、强力加速或上坡路的时候，引擎均会全力以6缸运作，而在其他如滑行时，则只会以3缸运作。第二代i-VTM4使用电子控制的液压后差速器，将引擎动力分配于前后轴，并动态分配于左右后轮。优点是可提供全天候操控，使转向在不同动力情况下更准确。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372282-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_14.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372282-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_14-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372282" class="wp-caption-text">.5升的V6 i-VTEC引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这一代Pilot没有了以往Pilot的硬朗设计，圆浑了不少，尺寸上则没有很大的分别，J35Y8引擎的输出跟上一代只有少量的提升，最特别是行车模式增加了“Trail &amp; Sand”，最多有6种模式，既可行走市内又可越野的SUV。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372283" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372283-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14372283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372283-2024_Honda_Pilot_Black_22-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372283" class="wp-caption-text">变速按钮。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
