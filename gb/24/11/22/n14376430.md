<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾会馆老人中心一人一菜庆祝感恩节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14376439-IMG_2251-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽约台湾会馆老人中心举办庆祝感恩节一人一菜大聚餐。（台湾会馆老人中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月22日讯】（大纪元记者林丹纽约报导）距离感恩节（11月29日）还有一周，纽约台湾会馆老人中心在11月21日（周四）举办庆祝感恩节一人一菜大聚餐暨11月庆生会，场面温馨热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14376540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14376540" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/11/22/n14376430.md#1/img_2252-2" rel="attachment wp-att-14376540"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14376540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14376540-IMG_2252-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14376540" class="wp-caption-text">纽约台湾会馆老人中心以一人一菜感恩节大餐活动，庆祝感恩节。（台湾会馆老人中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14376441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14376441" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/11/22/n14376430.md#1/93673220-522e-445c-8e99-80c1c1d3eba8" rel="attachment wp-att-14376441"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14376441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14376441-93673220-522E-445C-8E99-80C1C1D3EBA8-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14376441" class="wp-caption-text">感恩节感谢义工老师。（纽约台湾会馆老人中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14376440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14376440" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/11/22/n14376430.md#1/8d9a897b-cdf5-49e0-ae15-77817e8a55e4" rel="attachment wp-att-14376440"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14376440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14376440-8D9A897B-CDF5-49E0-AE15-77817E8A55E4-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14376440" class="wp-caption-text">11月庆生会。（纽约台湾会馆老人中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14376444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14376444" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/11/22/n14376430.md#1/img_3943-2" rel="attachment wp-att-14376444"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14376444" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14376444-IMG_3943-533x400.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14376444" class="wp-caption-text">耆老玩套圈圈活动。（林丹/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>耆老们从家中带来拿手好菜，有油饭、鱼香茄子、红烧豆腐、芝士蛋糕、汤等，还用小标签写上是哪位提供的。老人中心还在外面的餐馆订了火鸡、烧猪、烧肉，龙虾等大菜，非常丰盛。这是台湾会馆老人中心第二次在感恩节举办一人一菜大聚餐，去年也办过。</p>
<p class="p1">会长纪庆华表示，耆老们积极参与，分享家常好菜，展示厨艺。</p>
<p class="p1">随后，11月生日的寿星切蛋糕庆祝生日，众人与义工老师大合照——在感恩节向老师表达感恩、玩套圈圈益智游戏、卡拉OK唱唱跳跳。</p>
<p class="p1">纪庆华表示，下月将进行会长交接，他两届任期届满，按规定需要轮换。</p>
<p class="p1">纪庆华自2023年3月上任，同年12月连任。他在一年多的时间内，除了安排日常的文化娱乐活动、健身操之外，还组织耆老在纽约周边游览、参观协和山庄台湾俚语石刻，举办“风华年代老照片展”、会员美术工艺展、感恩节一人一菜大餐等活动，新颖有创意，丰富了耆老的生活，让耆老发挥各自特长展现才华，老有所乐、老有所学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14376446" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14376446" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/11/22/n14376430.md#1/img_2254-2" rel="attachment wp-att-14376446"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14376446" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14376446-IMG_2254-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14376446" class="wp-caption-text">感恩节大餐。（纽约台湾会馆老人中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
