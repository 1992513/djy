<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>穿越时光的浪漫旅程 台北“榕锦时光生活园区”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373540-724682-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台北“榕锦时光生活园区”。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年11月18日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）台北市是一个充满多样文化与历史的城市，而位于大安区金华街的“榕锦时光生活园区”，则将历史、自然与创意巧妙融合，成为了一处深受文青与旅人喜爱的日式文创景点。在这里，你能感受到历史的沉淀，也能欣赏现代设计的美学。</p>
<figure id="14373541" aria-describedby="caption-14373541" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373541-724683-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373541-724683-450x338.jpg" alt="大安区金华街的“榕锦时光生活园区”，则将历史、自然与创意巧妙融合。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14373541" class="wp-caption-text">大安区金华街的“榕锦时光生活园区”，则将历史、自然与创意巧妙融合。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>从刑务所官舍到文化地标</h2>
<p>“榕锦时光生活园区”的前身可追溯至日治时期的台北刑务所官舍群。当时，日本政府为延续清领时期的监狱建筑，将其改建为现代化监狱，并设立官舍作为职员的生活区域。这些建筑见证了清领、日治以及战后台湾的历史演变。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373542" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373542-724684.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373542-724684-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373542" class="wp-caption-text">“榕锦时光生活园区”的前身可追溯至日治时期的台北刑务所官舍群。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>战后，该区域成为台北监狱的宿舍区，直至1963年监狱迁移后，这里逐渐演变为以公务员与居民为主的华光社区。直到2000年，随着都市更新议题的兴起，这片充满历史的区域面临拆迁与保存的抉择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373543-724685.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373543-724685-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373543" class="wp-caption-text">直到2000年，随着都市更新议题的兴起，这片充满历史的区域面临拆迁与保存的抉择。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在多方努力下，台北市政府文化局于2013年将官舍及周边围墙遗址登录为历史建筑与古迹，并对区内多株老榕树进行保护。2022年，园区完成修复并对外开放，以崭新的面貌呈现昔日的历史记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373544" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373544-724686.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373544-724686-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373544" class="wp-caption-text">台北市政府文化局于2013年将官舍及周边围墙遗址登录为历史建筑与古迹，并对区内多株老榕树进行保护。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>融合自然与创意的文创园区</h2>
<p>榕锦时光生活园区以其保留的多栋日式木造建筑、浴场建筑与复古氛围，吸引了无数游客与文创爱好者。在园区的设计上，强调历史脉络的完整性，并融入现代多元商业空间，如咖啡馆、餐厅与文创店家，使之成为市民休闲与文化体验的最佳场所。空拍达人nina说:“咖啡和餐点都不错，空间的配置也很好，果然是很热门的餐厅，没多久就客满了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373545" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373545-724687.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373545-724687-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373545" class="wp-caption-text">榕锦时光生活园区以其保留的多栋日式木造建筑、浴场建筑与复古氛围。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中，园区内的咖啡厅空间尤为令人惊艳。大片落地窗引入充足的自然光线，并将窗外街景融入视野，营造出开阔又舒适的氛围。空拍达人nina表示:“长条木桌的自然木纹与现代混凝土结构形成有趣对比，既温暖又充满设计感。”不论是放松心情、与朋友聊天，还是专注于工作，这里都非常适合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373546" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373546-724688.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373546-724688-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373546" class="wp-caption-text">园区内的咖啡厅空间尤为令人惊艳。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>茂密榕树下的浪漫步道</h2>
<p>走进园区，一条平整的石板步道，引领游客穿梭在历史与自然之间。沿途可见到保留下来的红砖建筑与百年老榕树，枝叶繁茂，为整个场域增添了生机与气韵。红砖墙上挂着园区地图与导览资讯，显得既实用又古朴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373547" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373547-724689.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373547-724689-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373547" class="wp-caption-text">沿途可见到保留下来的红砖建筑与百年老榕树，枝叶繁茂。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而在夜晚，隐藏在红砖墙上的串灯点亮后，整个园区变得更加浪漫，仿佛进入了另一个时空。这里不仅适合白天的静谧散步，也适合夜晚的浪漫约会，是感受时光流转的绝佳地点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373548" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373548-724690.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373548-724690-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373548" class="wp-caption-text">这里不仅适合白天的静谧散步，也适合夜晚约会用餐。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>从老屋到未来的设计典范 获奖肯定</h2>
<p>榕锦时光生活园区的修复过程，以保留历史风貌，与引入创新设计为核心理念，其成果也赢得了多项国内外奖项的肯定，例如日本的Good Design Award与台湾的金点设计奖。这些奖项不仅展现了园区的建筑美学，更体现了老屋活化与文化保存的可能性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373550" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373550-724691.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373550-724691-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373550" class="wp-caption-text">从老屋到未来的设计典范，获奖肯定。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“榕锦时光生活园区”以其融合历史记忆、自然风光与创意设计的独特魅力，成为了台北市民与旅人心中珍贵的文化地标。当你漫步在红砖小路上，仰望榕树绿荫，或者品味一杯咖啡，听着微风拂过的声音，都能感受到一种静谧与充实的幸福感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373552" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373552-724694.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373552-724694-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373552" class="wp-caption-text">“榕锦时光生活园区”以其融合历史记忆，成为了台北市民与旅人心中珍贵的文化地标。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你有机会来到台北，不妨将“榕锦时光”列入你的旅行清单，用脚步踏访这片沉浸于历史与美学的天地，让心灵与时光共舞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373551" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373551-724693.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14373551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373551-724693-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373551" class="wp-caption-text">可将“榕锦时光”列入旅行清单，踏访这片历史与美学的天地。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
