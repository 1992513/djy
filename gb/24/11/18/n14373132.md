<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373170-551f6b901cce0c147efaee0b9f116366-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff

<h6>香煎虾子汇栉瓜。（万能阿姨提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><b>编者按：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">栉瓜高纤低热量</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，且富含多种营养素，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">是很好的减肥食材</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。美食达人分享料理小窍门，栉瓜搭配低卡高蛋白的鲜虾，并运用巧思帮美味加分，烹调出鲜甜、多汁、爽口的减肥料理。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>香煎虾子汇栉瓜</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">材料：白虾</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、栉瓜、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">七味唐辛粉、玫瑰盐</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">黑胡椒粉</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">作法：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.剥掉白虾的头部和外壳</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">保留虾尾</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">去除肠泥，把虾子洗净备用</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">虾头和虾壳则放置一旁备用</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.将栉瓜的头尾切掉</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">然后切1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2公分</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">备用</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">小窍门</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">要挑选新鲜的栉瓜，不然煮好后会带有苦味。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.平底锅内倒入少许油</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">用中火把虾子两面煎熟，盛起摆盘，撒点玫瑰盐，备用</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">小窍门：虾子</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">不要煎得太久，有熟就好，吃起来才会鲜甜。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.锅子再放些油</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，将</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">虾壳及虾头炒熟以逼出虾红素</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">接着捞起来（注1）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.锅中再加一点油，用中火把栉瓜煎至两面焦香</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">捞起备用</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">小窍门：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">利用锅中的虾红素煎栉瓜</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">让它更美味</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">6.碗中放入适量的七味唐辛粉</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">玫瑰盐</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">黑胡椒，加入煎好的栉瓜，充分搅拌均匀</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.将栉瓜和虾子一起摆盘。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">注1：有空时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">锅子内放入炒好的虾壳及虾头</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加点水</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">姜和葱</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">煮成高汤</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，待汤凉后</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">放入冰箱冷冻，往后可用于煮汤</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">担仔面</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">添加鲜味</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。<strong>◎</strong></span><br />
