<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：全球10家美得令人惊叹的咖啡馆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14370453-Cafes-thumb-1080x720-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世界上最美丽的咖啡馆拥有出色的设计和丰富的历史。（作图：The Epoch Times，摄影：Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月18日讯】（英文大纪元记者Ileana Alescio报导／赵孜济编译）这些咖啡馆以其建筑之美和丰富的历史而令人着迷。它们建造于那样一个时代，在那个时代，喝咖啡意味着停下来品尝你最喜欢的味道和享受历史氛围。</p>
<p>多年来，这些独特的咖啡馆吸引了许多杰出人物，从国王到作家和哲学家，甚至还有一位开国元勋，他们聚在一起讨论，交换思想并享用热饮。</p>
<p>让我们一起前往世界上一些最令人叹为观止的咖啡馆，揭开每家咖啡馆的特别之处。</p>
<h2>1. 纽约咖啡馆（New York Café） 匈牙利布达佩斯</h2>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F23%2Fid5535011-456678-hotel-cafe-ny-465-645-copy.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F23%2Fid5535011-456678-hotel-cafe-ny-465-645-copy.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">从二楼阳台俯瞰布达佩斯的纽约咖啡馆（New York Café）。(Dignity100/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F23%2Fid5535003-4562678-hotel-cafe-ny-465-645.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F11%2F23%2Fid5535003-4562678-hotel-cafe-ny-465-645.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">匈牙利布达佩斯著名的纽约咖啡馆内的华丽阳台栏杆。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年是这家咖啡馆成立130周年，它被誉为世界上最美丽的咖啡馆。纽约咖啡馆以委托建造的保险公司命名，自1894年落成后，迅速成为布达佩斯文学和艺术生活的中心。</p>
<p>这家咖啡馆拥有高高的天花板，装饰着壁画和闪闪发光的威尼斯吊灯、扭曲的大理石柱和镀金的栏杆，吸引着游客。除了宁静的氛围，钢琴家和弦乐四重奏定期演出，咖啡馆经常举办文学活动，以向咖啡馆的丰富历史致敬。</p>
<h2>2. 奥地利维也纳艺术史博物馆的咖啡馆</h2>
<figure style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754632-shutterstock_144054832.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754632-shutterstock_144054832.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在美丽的历史悠久的艺术史博物馆内。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754633-shutterstock_1599938797.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754633-shutterstock_1599938797.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="468" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">咖啡馆位于艺术史博物馆内。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这家咖啡馆位于奥地利最大的艺术博物馆Kunsthistorisches博物馆内，其名称的翻译为“艺术历史博物馆”。游客需要博物馆门票才能享用咖啡，但令人惊叹的咖啡馆内部和博物馆著名的艺术收藏品令门票物有所值。</p>
<p>博物馆的内部装饰华丽，有大理石、灰泥和金箔装饰以及文艺复兴时期复兴风格的壁画。周四晚上，咖啡馆变身为晚餐餐厅，提供精选饮品、菜肴和最好的维也纳咖啡。</p>
<h2>3. Confeitaria Colombo咖啡馆 巴西里约热内卢</h2>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754655-shutterstock_1195373293.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754655-shutterstock_1195373293.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">巴西里约热内卢Confeitaria Colombo咖啡馆的内部。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754656-shutterstock_2464600189.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754656-shutterstock_2464600189.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Confeitaria Colombo咖啡馆的入口。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Confeiteria Colombo咖啡馆的风格灵感来自欧洲的咖啡馆，它是巴西里约热内卢的著名地标。自1894年开放以来，它很快成为巴西作家、艺术家和知识分子的聚集地。</p>
<p>在1900年代初期，它以新艺术风格进行了翻修，这种外观自豪地保持到今天。咖啡馆拥有巨大的红木框架比利时水晶镜、大理石台面桌子和独特的天窗。</p>
<h2>4. 佛瑞恩咖啡馆（Café Florian） 意大利威尼斯</h2>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754628-shutterstock_2487347695.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754628-shutterstock_2487347695.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">意大利最古老的咖啡馆佛瑞恩（Cafè Florian）的内部。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754629-shutterstock_1059008306.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754629-shutterstock_1059008306.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">佛瑞恩咖啡馆位于威尼斯圣马可广场（Piazza San Marco）。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>佛瑞恩咖啡馆（Café Florian）位于威尼斯标志性的圣马可广场（Piazza San Marco），是意大利最古老的咖啡馆。自1720年开放以来，它已经连续运营了300多年，尽管在1800年代中期进行了重大扩建和翻新。如今，它拥有6个主题独特的大厅，每个大厅都由18世纪的著名意大利艺术家装饰。</p>
<p>20世纪初，佛瑞恩咖啡馆引入了“咖啡馆音乐会”的概念，为客人提供现场管弦乐队表演，供客人一边喝咖啡一边欣赏。一个多世纪后，这一传统继续存在，从浪漫歌曲到歌剧作品、古典音乐、常青歌曲，甚至在温暖的月份还会上演一些现代曲调。</p>
<h2>5.中央咖啡馆（Café Central） 位于奥地利维也纳</h2>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754734-shutterstock_1062236519.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754734-shutterstock_1062236519.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中央咖啡馆（Café Central）美丽的内饰，这是维也纳最负盛名的咖啡馆之一。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754737-shutterstock_2436048445.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F05%2Fid5754737-shutterstock_2436048445.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="371" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">维也纳中央咖啡馆的入口。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>中央咖啡馆（Café Central）位于维也纳市中心，因此得名，它体现了维也纳咖啡文化的精髓，环境非凡，具有浓厚的意大利风格。咖啡馆所在的豪华建筑建于1860年，它展示了威尼斯和佛罗伦萨建筑元素的优雅融合，灵感来自建筑师在意大利的旅行。</p>
<p>维也纳作家兼诗人彼得?阿尔滕贝格（Peter Altenberg）的纸制雕像在咖啡馆内迎接客人——这是对咖啡馆最忠实的顾客之一的致敬。阿尔滕贝格在咖啡馆度过了相当多的时间，他甚至将邮件和衣物送到这里。</p>
<p>多年来，中央咖啡馆还吸引了其他知名人士，包括精神分析学家西格蒙德?弗洛伊德（Sigmund Freud）、作家阿尔弗雷德?波尔加（Alfred Polgar）和政治活动家西奥多?赫茨尔（Theodor Herzl）。</p>
<h2>6. Café de la Paix咖啡馆 法国巴黎</h2>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755520-GettyImages-1210660997.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755520-GettyImages-1210660997.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">巴黎 “Café de la Paix”的休息室。(Franck Fife/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755522-shutterstock_2149717029.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755522-shutterstock_2149717029.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Café de la Paix位于法国巴黎的歌剧院附近。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>巴黎到处都是迷人的咖啡馆，每一个都为其享有“光之城”的美誉做出贡献。Café de la Paix以其丰富的历史而著称——咖啡馆的顾客包括王室成员、名人和文化偶像。它地处黄金地段，正对着法国首都最具标志性的地标之一卡尼尔歌剧院（Opera Garnier）。</p>
<p>自1862年开业以来，Café de la Paix一直是人们品尝经典咖啡和羊角面包或一杯茶搭配一块著名歌剧蛋糕的最佳场所，为此它赢得了巴黎茶室卓越的称号。</p>
<h2>7. 雄伟咖啡馆（Majestic Café） 葡萄牙波尔图（Porto）</h2>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755543-9421761927_f87fb182db_o.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755543-9421761927_f87fb182db_o.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雄伟咖啡馆（Majestic Café）的内部。(José Moutinho/Creative Commons 2.0)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755549-shutterstock_1818719615.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755549-shutterstock_1818719615.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">著名的雄伟咖啡馆的外墙。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>与前面提到的咖啡馆相比，1922年开业的葡萄牙波尔图（Porto）的雄伟咖啡馆（Majestic Café）相对年轻，只有一个多世纪的历史。那个历史阶段被称为“美好时代”，这家咖啡馆的设计证明了那个美丽的时代。它位于圣卡塔琳娜街（Santa Catarina Street），这是一条人行道，社会上最优秀的成员曾经在这里购物，现在仍然在这里购物。</p>
<p>雄伟咖啡馆采用典型的新艺术风格，配有大型佛兰德镜子、皮革内饰以及大理石和金属的精致融合。</p>
<p>从一开始，这家咖啡馆就吸引了当地和国际名人。近年来，它因被JK罗琳（JK Rowling）青睐而声名鹊起，她在这里写了第一本哈利波特书的部分内容，当时她还是一个不知名的作者。</p>
<h2>8. Le Procope咖啡馆 法国巴黎</h2>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755562-shutterstock_571585723.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755562-shutterstock_571585723.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="398" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Café Procope咖啡馆被称为巴黎历史最悠久的持续经营餐厅。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755566-shutterstock_2504924189.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755566-shutterstock_2504924189.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Café Procope位于Saint Germain des Pres区。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Le Procope咖啡馆成立于1686年，是巴黎最古老的咖啡馆。传说在17世纪，这是该市第一家供应咖啡的场所。咖啡当时是从奥斯曼帝国引入的一种异国情调的饮料。1989年经过翻修后，其内部装饰现在反映了18世纪的审美观念。</p>
<p>Le Procope是知识分子的热门聚会场所，今天，它自豪地展示着伏尔泰和让-雅克?卢梭等著名赞助人的纪念品。它甚至与美国有关：据说本杰明?富兰克林（Benjamin Franklin）在美国独立战争期间在这家咖啡馆起草了法美联盟。</p>
<h2>9.托尔托尼咖啡馆（Café Tortoni） 阿根廷布宜诺斯艾利斯</h2>
<figure style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755569-shutterstock_1104904610.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755569-shutterstock_1104904610.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">阿根廷布宜诺斯艾利斯的托尔托尼咖啡馆（Café Tortoni）。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755574-shutterstock_124988819.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755574-shutterstock_124988819.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">托尔托尼咖啡馆的外观。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>托尔托尼咖啡馆（Café Tortoni）成立于1858年，是布宜诺斯艾利斯最古老的咖啡馆。它的大理石桌子曾接待过作家豪尔赫?路易斯?博尔赫斯（Jorge Luis Borges）和探戈传奇人物卡洛斯?加德尔（Carlos Gardel）等著名人物，他甚至拥有自己的私人专用餐桌，以避开粉丝的目光。</p>
<p>托尔托尼咖啡馆的装饰和氛围受到经典巴黎咖啡馆的启发，自成立以来一直是文化和艺术的天堂。它的遗产甚至在探戈作品“Viejo Tortoni”或“Old Tortoni”中永垂不朽。今天，这家咖啡馆延续了其现场表演的传统，在其大气的地下室提供探戈和爵士乐表演。</p>
<h2>10. 双雄咖啡馆（The Two Magots） 法国巴黎</h2>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755584-shutterstock_2450454385.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755584-shutterstock_2450454385.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">俯瞰双雄咖啡馆。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755587-shutterstock_2117948372.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F11%2F06%2Fid5755587-shutterstock_2117948372.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法国巴黎的双雄咖啡馆（Les Deux Magots）。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“Les Deux Magots”这个名字指的是咖啡馆里展示的两个被称为“magots”的东方人物，这让人想起了它作为丝绸和新奇生意的起源。它于1884年转变为咖啡馆，标志着它作为文学聚会场所的遗产的开始，西蒙娜?德?波伏娃（Simone de Beauvoir）、让-保罗?萨特（Jean-Paul Sartre）和欧内斯特?海明威（Ernest Hemingway）等著名常客经常光顾。</p>
<p>如今，这家咖啡馆已经扩展到国际，在东京、布拉格和伦敦设有分店。</p>
