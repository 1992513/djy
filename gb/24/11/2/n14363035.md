<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中医推荐10分钟足部按摩操 助你摆脱水肿困扰</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13719396-shutterstock_1107894257-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>饮食太咸致体内钠浓度过高，促使水分从血液渗透至组织与细胞内，水分滞留在体内以至于发生水肿。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">许多人一觉醒来，或下班后，小腿有沉重的肿胀感，这是由于身体组织内积累过多液体，中医认为是湿邪聚集在皮下组织。水肿不仅影响美观，更甚者会造成患处僵硬、行走不利、皮肤溃疡和感染。本文提供10分钟足部按摩操，和日常生活卫教，助你摆脱水肿带来的困扰。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>水肿六大元凶</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">要想消除水肿，需先掌握六大元凶：</span></p>
<p><b>1.饮食过咸：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">饮食太咸致体内钠浓度过高，促使水分从血液渗透至组织与细胞内，水分滞留在体内以至于发生水肿。</span></p>
<p><b>2.心肝肾病变：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">心、肝或肾发生病变，分别影响血液循环、血浆蛋白生成及水分代谢等功能，因而引起水肿。</span></p>
<p><b>3.长时间维持同样姿势：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">久坐、久卧和缺乏运动，下肢静脉的血液回流变慢，水分渗入周边组织中而造成水肿。</span></p>
<p><b>4.天冷保暖不足：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">天气寒冷时，个人保暖不足，使得血液循环减慢，进而导致皮下组织水肿。</span></p>
<p><b>5.矿物质缺乏：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">钙、镁、钾、钠等矿物质，可维持体内水分的平衡及体液的渗透，摄取不足易产生水肿。</span></p>
<p><b>6.荷尔蒙影响：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">女性在月经期前及怀孕期间，黄体素分泌较多，水分的代谢变差，身体容易水肿。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>远离萝卜腿 10分钟足部按摩操</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14363039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14363039" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14363039-94c4fa1dcbf8221833d9e353ba24d817.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14363039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14363039-94c4fa1dcbf8221833d9e353ba24d817-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14363039" class="wp-caption-text">消水肿1。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14363040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14363040" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14363040-a1f85024cc6e51ea13315d67f5d1a4d7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14363040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14363040-a1f85024cc6e51ea13315d67f5d1a4d7-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14363040" class="wp-caption-text">消水肿2。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">现代人长期久坐，以至于下肢血液与淋巴循环不佳，因此容易引发水肿。想要远离萝卜腿，建议刚洗完澡，趁身体还暖呼呼之际按摩足部，每天只花10分钟就能有效消除或预防水肿。搭配润肤乳液或精油按摩，效果会更佳</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。以下是中医推荐的足部按摩操：</span></p>
<p><b>Act1</b><b>：</b><b>按压脚踝区域（太谿穴、三阴交穴）</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">太谿穴在内侧足踝后方约一指头凹陷处，其上方四指距离处及到三阴交穴；用拇指与食指轻微用力按压穴位15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20次，可刺激血液循环，促进水分代谢。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14363038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14363038" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14363038-a38e764b14fde68d2025f3b347f645b2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14363038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14363038-a38e764b14fde68d2025f3b347f645b2-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14363038" class="wp-caption-text">太谿穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14363037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14363037" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14363037-1bba56c88d6a59575e93730d4ea8b5bc.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14363037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14363037-1bba56c88d6a59575e93730d4ea8b5bc-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14363037" class="wp-caption-text">三阴交穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>Act2</b><b>：</b><b>按压腘窝</b><b>、</b><b>足三里穴</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">足三里穴在膝盖外侧凹窝往下四指幅处；用食指按压足三里（按：贴着小腿骨边缘按压此穴效果最佳），拇指按压腘窝，两处各按压15?20次，能改善淋巴循环、血液循环，并促进废物代谢。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14341180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14341180" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14341180-75a807c25c6c721a73cf78cbf73306da.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14341180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14341180-75a807c25c6c721a73cf78cbf73306da-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14341180" class="wp-caption-text">足三里穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>Act3</b><b>：按摩</b><b>腿部</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">用双手圈住脚踝，从下往上拉至大腿区域，左右腿各拉15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20次，可让腿部肌肉放松，有助于消除水肿。</span></p>
<p><b>Act4</b><b>：</b><b>腿部拧毛巾</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">双手分别圈住腿部的前、后侧，如拧毛巾一样拧捏腿部肌肉，也就是一手朝顺时针方向，另一手则朝逆时针方向拧捏，力道以感到轻微疼痛为准，扭捏大腿与小腿合计10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15次，能消除水肿，加强脂肪代谢。小腿肚较粗的人，可在该处多按摩几次。</span></p>
<p><b>Act5</b><b>：按摩</b><b>小腿肌肉</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这是预防萝卜腿的秘武器。先摆出屈膝动作，双手并排圈住小腿中央区域，拇指用力下压并捏住小腿后侧肌肉，双手往上、下两侧推，分别推至膝盖及脚踝处，两侧各按摩15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20次。</span></p>
<p><b>Act6</b><b>：</b><b>按压足底（涌泉穴）</b></p>
<div class="mceTemp">
<figure id="attachment_14311646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14311646" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311646-9cb5fb334e698cd7778d2a7a14ab487c.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14311646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id14311646-9cb5fb334e698cd7778d2a7a14ab487c-450x672.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14311646" class="wp-caption-text">涌泉穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">涌泉穴位在足底前段1/3距离的凹陷处；用拇指按压3分钟，可以帮助畅通足部血液循环，消除足部疲劳。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>抬腿收操</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">躺在床或软垫上抬腿，腿部靠着墙壁，与躯干呈接近90度夹角姿势，持续5分钟，膝盖尽量打直，可适度活动脚踝。如此能伸展整个腿部肌肉，并促进下肢血液、淋巴液回流。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>日常生活消水肿要领</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">饮食方面，摄取西瓜、冬瓜、绿豆、红豆、薏仁等均可利水祛湿。另外，适量补充富含钾的蔬果，以帮助身体排湿，如香蕉、奇异果、酪梨、菠菜、马铃薯等。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">小腿容易水肿或是需要久站的人，建议白天穿压力袜；晚上睡觉时，不妨用枕头或浴巾垫高小腿，如此可促进下肢血液循环，改善水肿。<strong>◎</strong></span></p>
