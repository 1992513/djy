<h6>2024 Toyota RAV4 TRD Off-Road。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年11月02日讯】既然RAV4是Toyota最热卖车款，所以Toyota要将这金蛋推出更多版本，使不同消费者也可找到一款适合他们的RAV4，已有版本有Prime的Plug-In Hybrid，又或是Hybrid，纯汽油版也新加入了以越野为主题的Adventure及TRD Off-Road版。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d22owz1tif1d5u.cloudfront.net/J2cLTijuy"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/pQZ0rppTI8Y?si=YzmPJX9yeXrKFIha" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>这次测试的2024年Toyota RAV4属于第五代车型，这一代RAV4更为粗犷的设计，有别以往的圆润“家庭车”的形象，尤其前脸的巨大六角进风口设计，类似同厂皮卡，具有“越野”的个性。车身离地距8.6吋，比其他版本高一点，使攀爬山坡石块更为容易。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14362914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362914" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362914-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_05-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362914-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_05-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362914" class="wp-caption-text">2024 Toyota RAV4 TRD Off-Road。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Adventure和TRD Off-Road型号均配备“动态扭矩向量全轮驱动（Dynamic Torque Vectoring AWD ）”和“标准后传动系统断开（Rear Driveline Disconnect standard）”，可将高达50%的引擎扭力传递至后轮轴，该系统还可以将更多扭力传递至左或右后轮，以增强路面或路面外的操控性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14362742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362742" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362742-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362742-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_10-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362742" class="wp-caption-text">粗犷的设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>RAV4 Adventure有不同颜色，包括Ice Cap、Midnight Black Metallic、Ruby Flare Pearl等，以及新推出的军绿色或带有Ice Edge车顶的双色军绿色。在内部，可以选择棕色或黑色SofTex装饰座椅。TRD Off-Road也会有Adventure所有颜色，并添加了金属灰色和骑兵蓝色，这两种颜色都有Ice Edge车顶双色调选项；内部专门配备了黑色 SofTex 装饰座椅。测试车是军绿色车身颜色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14362900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362900" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362900-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362900-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_11-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362900" class="wp-caption-text">越野用轮胎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>汽油版RAV4是使用2.5公升容积引擎，马力输出有203匹，以及184磅呎扭矩，引擎压缩比为13：1。变速箱是8速自动变速箱，表现不错，加速反应极为快速，动力输出并没有迟缓感。驾驶模式有三种，分别为Normal、Sort及Eco。TRD Off-Road另外有越野的旋扭，左转是“Mud &amp; Sand”，右转则是“Rock &amp; Dirt”，按下中间键是回复到Normal，并且不同设定时发出不同颜色的光，“Mud &amp; Sand”是黄色，Normal是盖色，“Rock &amp; Dirt”是白色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14362901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362901" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362901-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362901-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_13-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362901" class="wp-caption-text">2.5公升容积引。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>RAV4 TRD Off-Road的车内以黑色为主题，再加一些棕色点缀。中控台萤幕颇大，尺寸是10.5吋，运行Entune 3.0系统，可支援Apple CarPlay、Android Auto及Amazon Alexa，可是还没有Wireless Carplay。另外，音响系统可升级到JBL系统及11个扬声器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14362902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362902" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362902-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362902-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362902" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台最下方是无线充电板，可为手机充电。RAV4的前座有三段冷热控制。方向盘也有加热功能，也有自动关闭高灯功能，同时也装有前后左右四个摄像机，可显示鸟瞰式影像予中控台萤幕。还可，尾闸内部上方装有另一个摄像头，用作中间虚拟倒后镜之用，可放大、上下左右、旋转及明暗等调节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14362904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362904-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362904-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_18-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362904" class="wp-caption-text">10.5吋中控台萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>变速杆个头大，而驾驶模式设在变速杆与驾驶席中间，令变速杆用起来有点远。除了有D及S两种行车档位之外，变速杆在S档时又可使用+/-来手动介入变速箱运作，TRD Off-Road版RAV4并没有Paddle Shift，但有下坡限速功能（Downhill Assist Control）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14362905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362905" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362905-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362905-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_20-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362905" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆个头大。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Toyota是以耐用而闻名，而近年也加入了不少新技术，所以RAV4集耐用及新技术于一身，而TRD Off-Road更加使RAV4有了卓越的越野能力，虽然难度高的越野路段可能仍不能应付，但轻度越野绝对没问题。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14362906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362906" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362906-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362906-2024_Toyota_RAV4_TRD_Offroad_22-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362906" class="wp-caption-text">TRD Off-Road越野旋扭。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
