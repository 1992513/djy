<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>有闲来食饭 – 客家同乡会办小吃美食聚会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365247-DSC02545-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>会长李采真做开场白。（冯洛伊/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月06日讯】（大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大报导）11月3日（周日）中午，亚特兰大客家同乡会在亚特兰大华侨文教中心举办了“有闲来食饭”的客家小吃聚会活动。上百名客家乡亲或客家美食爱好者参加，侨教中心欧宏伟主任以及侨务委员范琪君也亲自捧场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14365248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14365248" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365248-DSC02550-e1730851873932.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14365248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365248-DSC02550-e1730851873932.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14365248" class="wp-caption-text">主任欧宏伟说简单的小吃也是文化传承。（冯洛伊/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14365277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14365277" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365277-DSC_2780-e1730855333509.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14365277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365277-DSC_2780-e1730855333509.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14365277" class="wp-caption-text">左起，会长李采真，主任欧宏伟，侨务委员范琪君，客家同乡会理事黄森勇。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同乡会会长李采真向与会众人说，这次聚会的目的很简单，就是有好手艺的义工们烹调一些传统客家小吃，让大家有个机会欢聚一堂、品尝美食与聊天。欧宏伟主任也告诉大家，这些客家小吃看似简单，却代表了客家文化的传承，因为所谓的文化就是融于衣食住行中的一切。欧主任还告诉了大家他本人虽非客家人，但是太太是客家人，所以也与客家文化有不解之缘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14365250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14365250" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365250-DSC_2783-e1730851996595.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14365250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365250-DSC_2783-e1730851996595.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14365250" class="wp-caption-text">会长李采真展示她母亲拿手的客家咸汤圆。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14365253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14365253" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365253-DSC_2784-e1730852118156.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14365253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365253-DSC_2784-e1730852118156.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14365253" class="wp-caption-text">金黄的香蕉以及清凉解渴的爱玉。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14365255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14365255" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365255-DSC02558-e1730852195155.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14365255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365255-DSC02558-e1730852195155.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14365255" class="wp-caption-text">大排长龙等好菜。（冯洛伊/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次聚会的美食有原来计划中的客家炒米粉、麻油鸡以及爱玉，后来又增加了客家咸汤圆以及绿豆糕，以及南方蔬菜公司捐赠的香蕉。有客家乡亲们尝到这久违的家乡味，也有对客家传统不太熟悉的食客对这些简单的小吃感到惊艳。无论对这些菜色熟悉与否，大家都在大快朵颐中感到其乐融融。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14365261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14365261" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365261-DSC_2785-e1730852991135.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14365261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365261-DSC_2785-e1730852991135.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14365261" class="wp-caption-text">美食当前令人喜悦。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14365262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14365262" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365262-DSC_2787-e1730853070787.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14365262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14365262-DSC_2787-e1730853070787.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14365262" class="wp-caption-text">米酒做的正宗麻油鸡让人开怀。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
