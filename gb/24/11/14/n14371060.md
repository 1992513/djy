<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园黑猪创新亮点 创意料理研发中心揭牌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371066-723777-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“桃园黑猪创意餐点研发中心”成立记者会。（桃园市农业局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年11月14日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市政府农业局积极推广黑猪肉产业，桃园市政府农业局长陈冠义出席14日于万能科技大学举办之“桃园黑猪创意餐点研发中心”成立记者会及揭牌仪式，活动展示的黑猪肉创意餐点，将黑猪肉结合创意思维，推广黑猪精致高端的饮食文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14371067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14371067" style="width: 1438px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371067-723778.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14371067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371067-723778.jpg" alt="" width="1438" b="628" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14371067" class="wp-caption-text">活动展示的黑猪肉创意餐点，将黑猪肉结合创意思维，推广黑猪精致高端的饮食文化。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园饲养猪只约12万头，黑猪约占7～8成，为北台湾之冠，桃园黑猪一般至少要饲养12～15个月，其肉质Q弹、油脂丰厚而不腻，由于外型、口感具有明显的市场区隔，深受婆婆妈妈及老饕的喜爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14371068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14371068" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371068-723779.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14371068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371068-723779.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="327" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14371068" class="wp-caption-text">将桃园黑猪肉发扬光大，发展出结合低碳料理与在地食材的创意餐，落实节能减碳，形成在地的美食观光地图，打造桃园黑猪肉的美食之旅。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14371061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14371061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371061-723762.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14371061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371061-723762.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14371061" class="wp-caption-text">桃园黑猪肉质Q弹、油脂丰厚而不腻。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过去客家人不浪费食材的考量下，使用桃园黑猪肉全猪料理出客家经典美食-四炆四炒：炆爌肉、咸菜炆猪肚、菜头炆排骨、肥汤炆笋干、客家炒肉、姜丝炒大肠、凤梨木耳炒猪肺、韭菜炒红旺，更是各式喜庆宴客桌上佳肴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14371062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14371062" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371062-723763.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14371062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371062-723763.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14371062" class="wp-caption-text">桃园黑猪肉质Q弹、油脂丰厚而不腻。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14371063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14371063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371063-723764.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14371063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371063-723764.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14371063" class="wp-caption-text">烟熏脆皮黑猪肉。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈冠义表示，为推动桃园特有黑猪肉产业及饮食文化，透过“桃园黑猪创意餐点推广活动”，将桃园特有黑猪研发具有创意的餐食与精致化以颠覆传统，并以影音社群方式推广桃园在地美食“黑猪创意餐食文化”，塑造桃园“黑猪美食”的品牌形象，提升附加价值以促进永续经营，引领桃园市饮食文化产业成长，让更多民众体验黑猪肉餐食特色，传承独特珍贵价值的黑猪文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14371064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14371064" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371064-723765.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14371064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371064-723765.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14371064" class="wp-caption-text">黑猪肉约克夏小点。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14371065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14371065" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371065-723775.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14371065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14371065-723775.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14371065" class="wp-caption-text">黑猪肉约克夏小点。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年桃园市政府农业局与万能科技大学合作成立“桃园黑猪创意餐点研发中心”，并推出多道黑猪创意餐点，将桃园黑猪肉发扬光大，发展出结合低碳料理与在地食材的创意餐，落实节能减碳，形成在地的美食观光地图，打造桃园黑猪肉的美食之旅。◇</p>
