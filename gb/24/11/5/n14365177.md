<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>泰勒·斯威夫特11月多伦多演唱会主题活动一览</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14135670-GettyImages-1250380068-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>泰勒·斯威夫特（Taylor Swift，粉丝昵称“莓莓”）将于11月在多伦多举办演唱会。(Photo by SUZANNE CORDEIRO / AFP) (Photo by SUZANNE CORDEIRO/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年11月06日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元记者李平多伦多报导）美国巨星泰勒</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">斯威夫特（</span><span class="s2">Taylor Swift</span><span class="s1">，粉丝昵称</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">莓莓</span><span class="s2">”</span><span class="s1">）将于</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月在多伦多举办演唱会，除了罗渣士中心（</span><span class="s2">Rogers Centre</span><span class="s1">）的六场演出，全城还将举行多达九场主题活动，带动粉丝狂欢并促进当地珠宝产业热销。</span></p>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b>粉丝活动一览</b></span></h2>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>莓粉之夜</b></span></h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2"> 11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">12</span><span class="s1">日，</span><span class="s2">Bata</span><span class="s1">鞋子博物馆将举办粉丝视频舞会派对，参与者可以打扮成喜爱的造型，老少皆宜。活动提供点心、拍照台和友情手链制作区。门票</span><span class="s2">30</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>莓莓美食之旅</b></span></h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">13</span><span class="s1">日至</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">24</span><span class="s1">日，粉丝可以参加下午的美食之旅，参观五家由女性创办的美食企业。为时两个半小时的行程中，粉丝可以品尝</span><span class="s2">10</span><span class="s1">道美食，包括手工馅饼和意大利风味美食，地点还包括斯威夫特短片《</span><span class="s2">All Too Well</span><span class="s1">》首映地。每团</span><span class="s2">13</span><span class="s1">人，门票</span><span class="s2">89</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>多伦多</b></span><span class="s2"><b>aERAs</b></span><span class="s1"><b>城市之旅</b></span></h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2"> 11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">15</span><span class="s1">日至</span><span class="s2">17</span><span class="s1">日、</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">21</span><span class="s1">日至</span><span class="s2">25</span><span class="s1">日，游客可参与</span><span class="s2">Gray Line</span><span class="s1">举办的三小时主题城市游，欣赏多伦多美景的同时，参与寻宝游戏和享受</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">红色</span><span class="s2">”</span><span class="s1">天鹅绒蛋糕。参观多伦多音乐花园和涂鸦巷等景点，票价</span><span class="s2">70</span><span class="s1">至</span><span class="s2">79</span><span class="s1">元（税前）。</span></p>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>多伦多版</b></span><span class="s2"><b>Taylgate &#8217;24</b> </span></h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">演唱会当天粉丝可以前往罗渣士中心附近的多伦多都会会议中心（</span><span class="s2">MTCC</span><span class="s1">）北楼参加</span><span class="s2">“Taylgate”</span><span class="s1">活动。每场演出日的下午</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">点至晚上</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">点，粉丝可以体验现场</span><span class="s2">DJ</span><span class="s1">表演、手链制作、拍照台等活动，门票</span><span class="s2">55</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>时代巡回演唱会后派对（</b></span><span class="s2"><b>Eras After Parties</b></span><span class="s1"><b>）</b></span></h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">想继续狂欢的粉丝可在罗渣士中心旁的</span><span class="s2">The Loose Moose</span><span class="s1">参加演出后派对，由</span><span class="s2">Taylor Swift Dance Parties</span><span class="s1">主办，面向</span><span class="s2">19</span><span class="s1">岁以上人群，每场演出结束后均会有现场</span><span class="s2">DJ</span><span class="s1">播放斯威夫特的热门歌曲。门票</span><span class="s2">37.29</span><span class="s1">元，演出前一周开售。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span class="s2"><b>Tdot Swift 4</b></span><span class="s1"><b>猫（</b></span><span class="s2"><b>Tdot Swift 4 cats</b></span><span class="s1"><b>）</b></span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">20</span><span class="s1">日，未能入场的粉丝可前往</span><span class="s2">El Mocambo</span><span class="s1">参加活动，包括现场音乐会、手链制作和艺术品展示。该活动支持动物庇护所，门票</span><span class="s2">44.28</span><span class="s1">元（含税）。</span></p>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>斯威夫特画作（</b></span><span class="s2"><b>Paint n&#8217; Vibe</b></span><span class="s1"><b>）</b></span></h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">22</span><span class="s1">日，粉丝可在</span><span class="s2">Stackt Market</span><span class="s1">参与绘画活动，创作灵感源自斯威夫特的艺术作品，并享受趣味问答和歌词游戏。门票</span><span class="s2">65.23</span><span class="s1">元，售票截止日期为</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">18</span><span class="s1">日。</span></p>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>独一无二的泰勒版冬季秀（</b></span><span class="s2"><b>One Of A Kind Winter Show</b></span><span class="s1"><b>）</b></span></h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2"> 11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">22</span><span class="s1">日，泰勒主题冬季集市将在多伦多展览中心举行，活动包括友情手链工作坊、翻唱乐队表演和无声迪斯科，粉丝还有机会参与服装比赛赢取奖品。</span></p>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>斯威夫特节日寻宝活动（</b></span><span class="s2"><b>Swiftie Holiday Hunt</b></span><span class="s1"><b>）</b></span></h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">酿酒厂区冬季村的斯威夫特节日寻宝活动开放至</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">24</span><span class="s1">日，粉丝可以赢得定制手链、购买主题饼干等。这一活动将延续至</span><span class="s2">12</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">23</span><span class="s1">日，但仅在</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">24</span><span class="s1">日之前有斯威夫特主题内容。</span></p>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b>珠宝市场热度激增</b></span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">随着多伦多演唱会临近，珠宝和手链材料销售火爆。多伦多巡演后，温哥华将在</span><span class="s2">12</span><span class="s1">月接棒。卑诗省坎贝尔河镇的养老院护士圣路易斯（</span><span class="s2">Mikaela St Louis</span><span class="s1">）已制作了</span><span class="s2">275</span><span class="s1">条手链，打算到时带到温哥华现场交流。圣路易斯为了材料不惜花费</span><span class="s2">1,000</span><span class="s1">元，甚至开车一小时到邻镇采购。友情手链灵感源自歌曲《</span><span class="s2">You’re on Your Own, Kid</span><span class="s1">》中的一句歌词，已成为粉丝们的标志，大家会在演唱会现场互换手链。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">多伦多巡演前，</span><span class="s2">Michaels</span><span class="s1">手工品连锁店为</span><span class="s2">Taylgate</span><span class="s1">活动提供了</span><span class="s2">500</span><span class="s1">万颗珠子，珠宝产品销量增长</span><span class="s2">300%</span><span class="s1">。渥太华</span><span class="s2">Shopify Inc.</span><span class="s1">平台珠宝线材销量增长</span><span class="s2">57%</span><span class="s1">，手链制作套件在加拿大沃尔玛销量同比增长</span><span class="s2">250%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b>超越粉丝圈的手链文化</b></span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">零售顾问利</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">麦考尔（</span><span class="s2">Leigh McCall</span><span class="s1">）指出，随着手链制作热潮蔓延，其他演艺明星的粉丝也开始加入这股潮流。圣路易斯还在温哥华观赏冰球比赛时看到观众戴手链，也打算在即将举行的萨布丽娜</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">卡彭特音乐会上分发。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">对于很多年轻人来说，这种</span><span class="s2">DIY</span><span class="s1">珠宝制作活动已超越狂欢派对的传统使用场景，成了一种表达情感和自我展示的方式。圣路易斯透露，许多粉丝还组建社交平台群组分享最新创作，交换手链和装饰品，借此庆祝青春和自由的美好。</span></p>
