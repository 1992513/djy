<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯多地房产销售速度领跑全澳</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14364151-eac026a402fd294ecd896b50d78cbaa7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>最新数据显示，珀斯多个地区房屋销售速度明显领先全澳。图为珀斯一套房产待售。（周鑫/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月04日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳大利亚的房地产热潮持续升温，但珀斯的多个地区房屋销售速度明显领先全澳。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，根据抵押贷款网站</span><span lang="en-US">MortgageBroker.com.au</span><span lang="zh-TW">的数据，澳洲房产销售最快的前四个地区都位于珀斯；在澳洲前</span><span lang="en-US">14</span><span lang="zh-TW">个销售最快的地区中，珀斯就占了</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">个。</span></p>
<p>该数据还显示，销售速度最快的地区是珀斯南部的<span lang="en-US">Leda</span>，该区的中位房价为<span lang="en-US">57</span>万澳元，房屋平均售出时间为<span lang="en-US">8</span>天。<span lang="en-US">Leda</span>区的房产主要吸引拥有家庭的购房者、首次购房者、通勤者和投资者，因为该区靠近主要交通枢纽、拥有丰富的便利设施，同时价格比邻近的<span lang="en-US">Medina</span>、<span lang="en-US">Orelia</span>和<span lang="en-US">Permelia</span>等区更为低廉。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">过去一年中，</span><span lang="en-US">Leda</span><span lang="zh-TW">区售出了</span><span lang="en-US">52</span><span lang="zh-TW">套房产，最近一个月有</span><span lang="en-US">9</span><span lang="zh-TW">套房屋挂牌，共吸引了</span><span lang="en-US">241</span><span lang="zh-TW">位潜在买家。</span><span lang="en-US">Southern Gateway</span><span lang="zh-TW">房地产公司销售顾问哈利</span><span lang="zh-TW">根</span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US">Chloe Halligan</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，</span><span lang="en-US">Leda</span><span lang="zh-TW">区因距离海滩仅</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">分钟，距离市中心</span><span lang="en-US">30</span><span lang="zh-TW">分钟，且房价仍比珀斯的平均房价低，受到了家庭和投资者的欢迎。她还表示，许多房产在首次开放展示时就售出，挂牌不到一周。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">紧随其后的是</span><span lang="en-US">Brookdale</span><span lang="zh-TW">区，房产平均销售时间为</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">天。该区中位房价为</span><span lang="en-US">50</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，是大都会地区最具性价比的区域之一，租金回报率高达</span><span lang="en-US">6%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>并列第三的是<span lang="en-US">Hillman</span>和<span lang="en-US">Darling Downs</span>。尽管这两个地区的房屋平均销售时间同为<span lang="en-US">12</span>天，但其中位房价差别巨大。<span lang="en-US">Hillman</span>的中位房价为<span lang="en-US">56.5</span>万澳元，而<span lang="en-US">Darling Downs</span>则高达<span lang="en-US">120</span>万澳元。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯北部的</span><span lang="en-US">Ridgewood</span><span lang="zh-TW">以</span><span lang="en-US">14</span><span lang="zh-TW">天的销售天数排在第七位，中位房价为</span><span lang="en-US">61</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。随后是南部的</span><span lang="en-US">Seville Grove</span><span lang="zh-TW">区（中位房价：</span><span lang="en-US">59.6</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元）和北部的</span><span lang="en-US">Hocking</span><span lang="zh-TW">区（中位房价：</span><span lang="en-US">76.25</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元），两区的房产平均</span><span lang="en-US">15</span><span lang="zh-TW">天售出。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Kwinana town centre</span><span lang="zh-TW">（中位房价：</span><span lang="en-US">53</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元）和</span><span lang="en-US">Pearsall</span><span lang="zh-TW">（中位房价：</span><span lang="en-US">71.7</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元）均以</span><span lang="en-US">16</span><span lang="zh-TW">天的销售周期并列第十，紧随其后的是</span><span lang="en-US">Hilbert</span><span lang="zh-TW">（中位房价：</span><span lang="en-US">60</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元），该区与</span><span lang="en-US">Darling Downs</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US">Brookdale</span><span lang="zh-TW">相邻，房产平均</span><span lang="en-US">17</span><span lang="zh-TW">天售出。</span></p>
