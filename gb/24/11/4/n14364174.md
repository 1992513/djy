<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中医师：立冬进补 3类情况需当心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/shutterstock_320919194-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>进补前，先认识自己的体质，再依据体质选择合适的进补方式－－温补、平补或凉补，才不会适得其反。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><b>编者按：<span style="font-weight: 400;">立冬表示冬季到来，</span></b><span style="font-weight: 400;">古人认为“立冬宜进补”，因为此时进补可以犒赏自己一整年的辛劳，让身体恢复元气，所以很重要。但中医师提醒，立冬进补也有讲究，要依照体质选择最适宜的补品。另外，有3类情况需当心。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>了解自己体质 选择最佳进补方式</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14364211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14364211" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14364211-b71c2e50e619478e9e79809dd631187b.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14364211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14364211-b71c2e50e619478e9e79809dd631187b-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14364211" class="wp-caption-text">依体质进补。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">进补前，先认识自己的体质，再依据体质选择合适的进补方式－－温补、平补或凉补，才不会适得其反。</span></p>
<p><b>1.温补：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">适合虚寒体质者，例如手脚易冰冷、精神不振、大便偏稀软的人；可食用十全大补汤（药炖排骨）。</span></p>
<p><b>2.平补：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">偶尔怕冷或怕热的人适用；不宜摄取过冷或过热的食材，可选择温和的四神汤，以稳定肠胃道功能。</span></p>
<p><b>3.凉补：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于燥热体质者，也就是容易长痘痘、嘴破、口干舌燥、便秘的人；选项为九尾草鸡汤。九尾草又称为狗尾草，具有开脾健胃、补虚补气等功效。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>冬令进补调养身体 三类情况需当心</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><b>气温未降：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">“立冬一日，水冷三分”指的是立冬时节天气急剧转变，然而现代常见立冬节气一到，气温却迟迟不降，此时若贸然进补，可能会引发上火，例如冒痘、口干舌燥、便秘、血压升高等症状。</span></p>
<p><b>?染患感冒：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">如果有感冒症状，像是发烧、咳嗽、喉咙痛等，不宜进补，以免让身体更加不适。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><b>慢性病患者病况：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">慢性疾病患者，如高血压、糖尿病、高血脂等，进补与否要格外小心；若病情不稳定，则不应进补；进补前，可询问专业医师的建议。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>补过头肠胃不舒服 两穴位按摩缓解</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14364212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14364212" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14364212-7fd7d2ab9417b161447cb131403774ef.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14364212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14364212-7fd7d2ab9417b161447cb131403774ef-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14364212" class="wp-caption-text">补过头自救法。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">若因进补过头造成肠胃不舒服，比如胀气、腹痛、腹泻、便秘等症状，可透过穴位按摩来缓解。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.内关穴：位在手臂内侧，腕横纹上三指宽处；可缓解胸闷、恶心、腹胀。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14327039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327039" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327039-dab5e8f0148b1bb19089cd3cd760f9f1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327039-dab5e8f0148b1bb19089cd3cd760f9f1-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327039" class="wp-caption-text">内关穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.中脘穴：位在肚脐正上方四寸处；可舒缓消化不良、打嗝。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14327038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327038" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327038-74dfdcdd32a23b36e85bba1036142ef9.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327038-74dfdcdd32a23b36e85bba1036142ef9-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327038" class="wp-caption-text">中脘穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
