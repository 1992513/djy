<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>运动前要暖身 3动态拉筋 让身体更快暖起来</h2>

<h6>运动前要暖身 3动态拉筋可让身体更快暖起来。图为上半身与脊椎的动态拉筋。（詹珞瑶 物理治疗师授权youtube撷图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><b>澄清错误认知</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">运动前的暖身要做什么动作</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">？人们多半</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">沿用小时体育课所教的转手腕</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">转脚踝</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">转脖子和转膝盖</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">但转完后</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">身体并没有真正的热起来</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">其实不同的运动项目要求暖身的部位也不同</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，比如</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">跑马拉松的人</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">要强调脚的暖身</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">跳舞的话</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，则是</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">上半身脊椎和下半身都要热起来</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">如何帮助身体更快地暖活起来</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">？</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">本文会演示上半身脊椎和下半身的动态拉筋</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">动态拉筋最大特点是能促进血液循环</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">运动时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">肌肉可以发挥最大的效能</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。它不只</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于运动前的暖身</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">早上起床若感到全身僵硬，这个运动可放松全身肌膜，应付一整天的疲劳。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">动态拉筋有好几百种变化，以下是三种简单的动态拉筋：</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>大腿后侧肌群的动态拉筋</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14362602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362602" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362602-d20ca4a4b787205ad05d8d027295ba9e.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362602-d20ca4a4b787205ad05d8d027295ba9e-450x268.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="268" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362602" class="wp-caption-text">大腿后侧肌群的动态拉筋。（詹珞瑶 物理治疗师授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">现代人长期久站或久坐，大腿后侧筋群变得既短又紧，日子久了，易造成脊椎僵硬，所以松开大腿后筋是首要动作。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">双手插腰，站好后，把单脚往后踩一小步，直接往后坐下去，同时保持前脚伸直，你会有前脚的大腿后侧肌群被拉紧的感觉，一秒钟后往前站起来。再来换另一只脚往后踩一小步，其它动作同前。两脚来回重复拉筋，合计做20下。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">注意：动态拉筋是一边动一边把筋松开，所以不要停留太久；也就是感觉有拉到筋时，就往前站起来。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">运动的同时，要把脊椎打直，不能驼背，否则不易拉到大腿后侧的肌群。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>上半身与脊椎的动态拉筋</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14362605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362605" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362605-e378245c513168a77e01cd781e50ab8f.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362605-e378245c513168a77e01cd781e50ab8f-450x272.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="272" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362605" class="wp-caption-text">上半身与脊椎的动态拉筋1<span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span>（詹珞瑶 物理治疗师授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14362604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362604" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362604-bc986fb21c5ddd90445e38bb7b307e7f.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362604-bc986fb21c5ddd90445e38bb7b307e7f-450x271.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362604" class="wp-caption-text">上半身与脊椎的动态拉筋2<span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span>（詹珞瑶 物理治疗师授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14362603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362603-912f15c0842e465b87220c56839dcc2a.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362603-912f15c0842e465b87220c56839dcc2a-450x273.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362603" class="wp-caption-text">上半身与脊椎的动态拉筋3<span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span>（詹珞瑶 物理治疗师授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">第二种动态拉筋是拉开上半身与脊椎，共有三组动作。肩膀跟脊椎的肌肉连在一起，因此做暖身运动时会一起拉开。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">双脚与肩膀同宽，膝盖微蹲，双手搭在肩膀上，把一只手往上延伸，感到侧腰的关节和肌肉被拉开时，就换另一只手往上延伸。双手来回重复共10次。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">注意：很多人只是把手往上举，并没有把手往上延伸，这样做无法拉开侧边的关节和肌肉。建议想像一只手往上摸到天花板，并确定腋下的肌肉被拉开后，再换另一手往上延伸。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">下一组动作是换成一只手往后延伸，同时带动脊椎的旋转，双手来回重复共10下。最后一组是手往斜前方延伸，同时带动脊椎的旋转，拉到脊椎后，就回到正中心，双手来回重复共10下。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">注意：若肩膀疼痛，可以用眼睛取取代手，眼睛往上看时，一样要同时做出身体的侧弯和旋转。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>合并上、下半身的动态拉筋</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14362599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362599" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362599-e788bf39a804c9bc047081cfa185c4ee.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362599-e788bf39a804c9bc047081cfa185c4ee-450x261.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="261" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362599" class="wp-caption-text">合并上下半身的动态拉筋<span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span>（詹珞瑶 物理治疗师授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">第三种动态拉筋是合并上半身和下半身。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">双脚与肩同宽，右脚往后踩呈弓箭步，且右脚跟一定要着地，左手摸到右边的肋骨，右手往斜上方延伸，确认拉到侧边的肌肉后，往前站起来。接着换左脚往后踩呈弓箭步，且左脚跟一定要着地，右手摸到左边的肋骨，左手往斜上方延伸，确认拉到侧边的肌肉后，往前站起来。左右两边连续做20下。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">重复动作次数越多时，除了拉到侧边的肌肉，还会延伸拉到腹部或是大腿根部的肌肉。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">注意：做这个动作时，胸口和手不能往前盖住，胸口、手和脸要完全朝向正前方。<span style="color: #000000;"><strong>◎◇</strong></span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14362600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14362600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362600-80e55de01771ebe4357e29fc695893e8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14362600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362600-80e55de01771ebe4357e29fc695893e8-450x268.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="268" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14362600" class="wp-caption-text">合并上下半身-注意事项-错误动作。（詹珞瑶 物理治疗师授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
