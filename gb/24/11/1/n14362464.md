<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>去年珀斯医疗事故致85人亡</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月01日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张小凡澳洲珀斯编译报导）最新数据显示，过去一年，珀斯医院的临床决策失误或错误导致</span><span lang="en-AU">85</span><span lang="zh-TW">人死亡。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据“西澳人报”报导，儿童与青少年健康服务局、北部大都会健康服务局、东部大都会健康服务局和南部大都会健康服务局发布的年度报告揭露了医疗系统的弊病，并指出急诊部门在患者治疗目标的达成方面表现不佳。?</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2023-24</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">财年，共报告了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">318</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">起严重性评估代码</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">SAC1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）事</span><span lang="zh-TW">故</span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US">Severity Assessment Code 1</span>，简称<span lang="en-US">SAC1</span>）<span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">SAC1</span><span lang="zh-TW">事故被定义为“已经或可能（近乎失误）造成严重伤害或死亡的临床医疗事故，而且该事故的发生归咎于医疗服务的提供（或缺乏），而不是病人的基本状况或疾病而造成。</span>”</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">儿童与青少年健康服务局（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Childand</span> <span lang="en-AU">Adolescent</span> <span lang="en-AU">Health</span> <span lang="en-AU">Service</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">，简称</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">CAHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">CAHS</span>（包括珀斯儿童医院）已审查或正在审查<span lang="en-AU">12</span>起<span lang="en-AU">SAC1</span>事件，其中<span lang="en-AU">5</span>起导致患者死亡，另有<span lang="en-AU">4</span>起造成严重伤害。这一死亡数字与<span lang="en-AU">2022-23</span>年相差仅<span lang="en-AU">1</span>例，但导致严重伤害的事件数量翻倍。</p>
<p>其急诊部门表现也进一步恶化，仅有<span lang="en-AU">30.7%</span>的三级优先患者在建议的<span lang="en-AU">30</span>分钟内得到救治，低于<span lang="en-AU">2022-23</span>年的<span lang="en-AU">32.4%</span>。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">北部大都会健康服务局（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">North</span> <span lang="en-AU">Metropolitan</span> <span lang="en-AU">Health</span> <span lang="en-AU">Service</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">，简称</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">NMHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">NMHS</span>报告了<span lang="en-AU">22</span>起<span lang="en-AU">SAC1</span>死亡事件，较前一年的<span lang="en-AU">24</span>起有所减少，但<span lang="en-AU">64</span>起事件导致严重伤害，增长了<span lang="en-AU">25%</span>。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">NMHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">涵盖</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Sir</span> <span lang="en-AU">Charles</span> <span lang="en-AU">Gairdner</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">King</span> <span lang="en-AU">Edward</span> <span lang="en-AU">Memorial</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Osborne</span> <span lang="en-AU">Park</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Graylands</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院和</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Joondalup</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院，其中</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Sir</span> <span lang="en-AU">Charles</span> <span lang="en-AU">Gairdner</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">King</span> <span lang="en-AU">Edward</span> <span lang="en-AU">Memorial</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院和</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Joondalup</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院均设有急诊科。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">NMHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">急诊部门共接诊</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">18</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">7200</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">人次，但仅</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">27%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的三级患者在规定时间内得到治疗，远低于</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">75%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的目标，并且这是该指标连续第五年未达标。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">东部大都会健康服务局（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">East</span> <span lang="en-AU">Metropolitan</span> <span lang="en-AU">Health</span> <span lang="en-AU">Service</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">，简称</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">EMHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">EMHS</span>共有<span lang="en-AU">20</span>起<span lang="en-AU">SAC1</span>死亡事件，比<span lang="en-AU">2022-23</span>年多<span lang="en-AU">3</span>起，还有<span lang="en-AU">52</span>起事件造成严重伤害。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">EMHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">下属的医院包括</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Royal</span> <span lang="en-AU">Perth</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">St</span> <span lang="en-AU">John</span> <span lang="en-AU">of</span> <span lang="en-AU">God</span> <span lang="en-AU">Midland</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">公立医院、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Bentley</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Armadale</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院和</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Kalamunda</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院。其急诊科接诊了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">217,500</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">人次，但仅有</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">18.8%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的三级患者在建议时间内接受治疗，表现不如</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">NMHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">南部大都会健康服务局（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">South</span> <span lang="en-AU">Metropolitan</span> <span lang="en-AU">Health</span> <span lang="en-AU">Service</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">，简称</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">SMHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">SMHS</span>在<span lang="en-AU">119</span>项完成或正在进行的调查中，确认了<span lang="en-AU">38</span>起死亡事件，相当于每三起事件中就有一起导致死亡。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">SMHS</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的医院中超过一半（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">71</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">起）事件导致严重伤害，涉及的医院包括</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Fiona</span> <span lang="en-AU">Stanley</span> <span lang="en-AU">Hospital</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Fremantle</span> <span lang="en-AU">Hospital</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Rockingham</span> <span lang="en-AU">General</span> <span lang="en-AU">Hospital</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Peel</span> <span lang="en-AU">Health</span> <span lang="en-AU">Campus</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Murray</span> <span lang="en-AU">District</span> <span lang="en-AU">Hospital</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Fiona</span> <span lang="en-AU">Stanley</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Rockingham</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院和</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Peel</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院的急诊部门共接诊</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">22</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1100</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">人次，三级患者的响应时间在各医院中表现最差。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">其中，</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Fiona</span> <span lang="en-AU">Stanley</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">医院的表现最差，仅有</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">14.5%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的三级患者在</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">30</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">分钟内得到救治，远低于</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">75%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的目标，并比</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2022-23</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年的</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">16.9%</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">进一步恶化。</span></p>
<p>即使是“危及生命”的二级优先患者中，也有超过一半未能在建议的<span lang="en-AU">10</span>分钟内得到治疗，尽管目标为<span lang="en-AU">80%</span>以上。</p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">Peel</span>医院同样未能达到二级和三级患者的救治目标，分别有<span lang="en-AU">65.6%</span>的二级和<span lang="en-AU">17.6%</span>的三级患者按时就诊。</p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">Rockingham</span>医院的表现也未达标，仅有<span lang="en-AU">72.3%</span>的二级和<span lang="en-AU">18.4%</span>的三级患者按时接受治疗。</p>
<p>所有年度报告均已提交议会，其中提到部分<span lang="en-AU">SAC1</span>事件仍在审查中。<span lang="en-AU"><br />
</span>在<span lang="en-AU">NMHS</span>，有<span lang="en-AU">24</span>项审查仍在进行；<span lang="en-AU">SMHS</span>有<span lang="en-AU">23</span>项未完成；<span lang="en-AU">CAHS</span>则有<span lang="en-AU">7</span>项仍在进行中。</p>
