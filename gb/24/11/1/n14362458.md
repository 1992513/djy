<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Telethon筹款8300万 查尔斯国王发贺信</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月01日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者夏墨竹澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="en-AU">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">19</span><span lang="zh-TW">日，西澳举办了年度</span><span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span><span lang="zh-TW">慈善募款活动。多位政要名流出席了筹款晚会，在澳洲访问的查尔斯国王也发来贺</span><span lang="zh-TW">信</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><a name="_Hlk180702049"></a> <span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">被称年度电视马拉松，每年于</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">10</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月举办。它由慈善家克鲁瑟斯</span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">James </span>Cruthers</span><span lang="zh-TW">和榷苒（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Brian Treasure</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">于</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1968</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年创办。“</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">7</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">号台”是</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的制作单位。</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">募款活动的宗旨是改善西澳病弱儿童的健康与福祉。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《今日珀斯》报导，尽管面临生活成本危机，西澳社区仍为其全力以赴筹款，今年的</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">已经筹集超过</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">8300</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。自</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1968</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年以来，</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">筹集了超过</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">6.88</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">亿澳元善款，帮助了西澳无数弱势儿童。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">7</span>号台<span lang="en-AU">Telethon Trust</span>主席戈伊德（<span lang="en-AU">Richard Goyder</span>）表示，今年<span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span>真正展现了社区精神。</p>
<p>“来自社区个人的捐款真是令人难以置信”，戈伊德说，“即使在这种经济环境下，人们仍然热衷于团结起来，支持我们的孩子。”</p>
<p><a name="_Hlk180702323"></a> <span lang="zh-TW">周六晚，多位政商界名流参加了此次筹款晚宴，包括总理阿尔巴尼斯与未婚妻海顿（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Jodie</span> <span lang="en-AU">Haydon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="zh-TW">联邦反对党领袖达顿（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Peter</span> <span lang="en-AU">Dutton</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）与妻子吉莉</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">·</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">达顿（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Kirilly</span> <span lang="en-AU">Dutton</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）、州长库克与妻子、前州长麦高恩</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯市长</span><span lang="zh-TW">泽皮拉斯（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Basil</span> <span lang="en-AU">Zempilas</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">、能源公司</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Woodside</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">主席戈伊德（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Richard</span> <span lang="en-AU">Goyder</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）等。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">此外，查尔斯国王于周二（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">10</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">22</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">日）也发来一封贺信，表达了他对筹款成就的赞扬。查尔斯国王与妻子卡米拉王后于</span><span lang="zh-TW">上</span><span lang="zh-TW">周五（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">10</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">18</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">日）抵达悉尼，他们对澳洲进行</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">5</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">天国事访问。</span></p>
<p>他在信中写道：“我和妻子很高兴听到今年七号台的<span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span>创下了破纪录的成功。”</p>
<p>“多年来，这项年度活动已筹集到惊人的<span lang="en-AU">6.88</span>亿澳元，这证明了澳大利亚人民的慷慨、同情心和社区精神，我们在这次访问中以及有幸访问的每一次场合都见证了这一点。</p>
<p>“我们向所有参与并做出巨大贡献的人表示祝贺和感谢。”</p>
<p>西澳州长库克（<span lang="en-AU">RogerCook</span>）表示，“国王陛下注意到西澳是一个多么美好的地方，西澳是一个多么热情和乐于奉献的社区，这真是太棒了。”</p>
<p>他说：“我非常高兴他为我们所做的一切感到骄傲，我为西澳感到非常自豪。”</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">西澳州长</span><span lang="zh-TW">库克日前在议会宣布了州政府将为</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">捐款</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1300</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">周日（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">10</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">20</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">日），总理阿尔巴尼斯（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Anthony</span> <span lang="en-AU">Albanese</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）宣布联邦政府将为</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Telethon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">捐款</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">600</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
