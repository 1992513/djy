<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳首家星巴克咖啡店周四开业</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月01日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者林达澳洲珀斯编译报导）饮料巨头跨国连锁星巴克（</span><span lang="en-AU">Starbucks</span><span lang="zh-TW">）将于周四（</span><span lang="en-AU">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">24</span><span lang="zh-TW">日）在珀斯</span><span lang="en-AU">Piara Waters</span><span lang="zh-TW">区推出第一家车道型门店（</span><span lang="en-AU">drive-through store</span><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></p>
<p>据《今日珀斯》报导，星巴克拿铁（<span lang="en-AU">lattes</span>）、焦糖玛奇朵（<span lang="en-AU">caramel macchiato</span>）、星冰乐（<span lang="en-AU">frappuccino</span>）和清凉饮料等招牌饮品均在这家星巴克汽车直通店的菜单上。</p>
<p>万圣节（<span lang="en-AU">Halloween</span>）前夕，饮料单上还将包括白巧克力榛子星冰乐（<span lang="en-AU">Friendly Ghost White Chocolate Hazelnut Frappucino</span>）和深受人们喜爱的“南瓜五香拿铁”（<span lang="en-AU">Pumpkin Spiced Latte</span>）。</p>
<p>店内还提供新鲜食品、烤面包、蛋糕、甜甜圈（<span lang="en-AU">Doughnut</span>）和糕点。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲星巴克咖啡董事长史密斯</span><span lang="en-AU">(Michael Smith)</span><span lang="zh-TW">表示，在</span><span lang="en-AU">Piara Waters</span><span lang="zh-TW">区，</span><span lang="en-AU">Erade Drive</span><span lang="zh-TW">上的车道型门店的开业是该品牌的一个里程碑。</span></p>
<p>这家饮料巨头此前称其在西澳的推广将创造<span lang="en-AU">200</span>多个就业岗位，并于本周透露，星巴克西澳团队已聘用了<span lang="en-AU">65</span>名咖啡师。</p>
<p>星巴克将与当地供应商合作，例如老牌奶制品公司<span lang="en-AU">Brownes Dairy</span>和位于<span lang="en-AU">Morley</span>的德国面包厂家<span lang="en-AU">Little Home Bakery</span>。</p>
<p>星巴克本周确认，其下一家门店将于数周后开在珀斯北郊<span lang="en-AU">Clarkson</span>。也是车道型门店。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">近几个月来，一系列旨在开设星巴克咖啡馆的大都市开发项目也已获批，其中包括</span><span lang="en-AU">Bayswater</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-AU">Hammond Park</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：杨新云</p>
