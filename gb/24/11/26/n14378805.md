<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新诗：感谢（中英对照）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14379133-2410291714142483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云海。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">感谢，我感谢<br />
贫困给我的恩惠，我感谢<br />
父母给我的默化，我感谢</p>
出生在贫困的环境中<br />
常常咬着牙忍人之所不能忍<br />
使我不致浮华不实<br />
使我得以磨练出<br />
许多别人没有的技能<br />
许多别人没有的经验<br />
许多坚强的意志<br />
这些成了我日后<br />
克服困难的资产</p>
父母不允许我做某些事<br />
也不允许我说某些话<br />
他们没有别人父母那种显赫的身世<br />
也没有讲出许多大道理<br />
只是以其行为暗中影响我<br />
使我沉默寡言，行事低调<br />
使我不会去投机取巧<br />
使我没走入歧途<br />
使我到老仍能把持得住自己</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">我自觉我很幸运<br />
虽然一生没有别人那样的物质享受<br />
也没有来自父母的庇荫<br />
可是我自觉很满足<br />
我自觉对得起自己<br />
所以我很感谢<br />
我贫困的环境，我很感谢<br />
我不显赫的父母</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;"><strong><br />
Thank</strong>? ?Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">Thank, I thank for<br />
The kindness of the poor which gives me, I thank for<br />
The silent education from my parents, I thank for</p>
I born in the poor circumstance<br />
Must often be born which can&#8217;t bear by others with grind my teeth<br />
Made me not to be showy<br />
Made me go through the mill<br />
Get many skills which other person have not had<br />
Much experience which other person have not had<br />
Much firm intermination<br />
Which are the assets thereafter<br />
To conquer so much dificulty</p>
Parents banned me to do something<br />
And banned me to say some words<br />
They had not their life greatly distinguished as the others<br />
And can&#8217;t let out major principle as well<br />
Only give me with their action in a hint<br />
Made me recticent, low tone in action<br />
Let me not speculate and gain thing by tickery<br />
Let me don&#8217;t go on wrong way<br />
Let me can hold my ownway till old age</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">I feel myself that I&#8217;m very lucky<br />
Although I haven&#8217;t enjoy the matter as other&#8217;s<br />
As well as shelter by parents<br />
But feel very satisfy<br />
I feel I&#8217;m worthy of myself<br />
So I thank very much<br />
My poor circumstence, I thank<br />
I haven&#8217;t greatly distinguished parents</p>
