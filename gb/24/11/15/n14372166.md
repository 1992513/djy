<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】苹果奶油甜面包</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372167-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>面包中的苹果酱与姜饼相得益彰，带出多层次风味。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月16日讯】这款甜面包模仿美国老式“假”苹果派做法，馅料用了饼干。不过这里用的是姜饼，以及真苹果慢煮而成的苹果奶油酱（apple butter）。姜饼本身有浓郁的香料和甜味，作馅料增加香气又能帮助吸收果酱水分。</p>
<p>最后，铺在表面的果酱与粗糖形成类似果丹皮口感的甜酸脆皮，风味特别。（自制苹果奶油链接<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/10/26/n14358365.md#1">请点这里</a>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372169-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372169-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372169" class="wp-caption-text">苹果奶油甜面包所需食材。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>240克普通无漂白面包粉</p>
<p>60克普通全麦面粉</p>
<p>4克普通速发酵母</p>
<p>1个大号鸡蛋</p>
<p>4克盐</p>
<p>45克无盐黄油（butter），软化</p>
<p>45克白砂糖</p>
<p>165克冷藏的全脂牛奶，微波炉（900W）加热30秒</p>
<p>混合以下食材作为馅料：1/3杯捣碎的市售糖蜜姜饼、1/2杯苹果奶油酱（apple butter）</p>
<p>抹面：适量粗蔗糖和苹果奶油酱</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372171-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372171-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372171" class="wp-caption-text">苹果奶油面包的料理步骤。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>料理步骤</h2>
<p>1. 冬天室温的做法：搅拌机碗中放入酵母、温牛奶拌一拌；加鸡蛋、面粉搅成面团。</p>
<p>2. 取出面团，覆盖后室温静置。</p>
<p>3. 搅拌桶中放入黄油、糖和盐，用平搅拌桨打均匀、松散。换成面团钩，加入面团，揉成可稍微拉薄膜的状态即可。</p>
<p>4. 面团放入容器，覆盖，温暖地方或发酵箱松弛45分钟，至稍微膨发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372172-b7269c33b4d5f5ae7f9e4b59.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372172-b7269c33b4d5f5ae7f9e4b59-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372172" class="wp-caption-text">面团。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 制作馅料：姜饼捣碎后与苹果奶油酱拌匀，待用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372173-6b1698f3a6bd9b3213cda919.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372173-6b1698f3a6bd9b3213cda919-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372173" class="wp-caption-text">馅料食材。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 取9英寸金属烤盘，涂抹适量软化黄油。</p>
<p>7. 面团压成（不揉面）长方形，再擀成12X18英寸薄片。涂抹馅料后切成9个长条，逐个卷成松散的圆筒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372174-91374984946c2fed603faf09.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372174-91374984946c2fed603faf09-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372174" class="wp-caption-text">面团造型。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 面团摆入烤盘，放发酵箱，启动自动发酵功能。若是调温发酵箱，以90华氏度（32摄氏度、湿度75%）发酵60分钟，至面团膨发几乎1倍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372175-272f2729b459b2028c836487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372175-272f2729b459b2028c836487-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372175" class="wp-caption-text">发酵前。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14372179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372179-ebde0d91140bc0e0d88a8429.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372179-ebde0d91140bc0e0d88a8429-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372179" class="wp-caption-text">发酵后。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 预热烤箱350华氏度（177摄氏度）。面团表面铺一层苹果酱并撒粗粒蔗糖，烤30~35分钟。喜欢吃焦糖脆皮可多撒粗糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372180-245ed95c1e7d734ee534d229.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372180-245ed95c1e7d734ee534d229-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372180" class="wp-caption-text">烘烤前涂抹果酱和粗糖。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10.面包出炉在烤盘中降温一会儿再取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14372181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14372181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372181-20241110_140237.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14372181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372181-20241110_140237-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14372181" class="wp-caption-text">出炉的甜面包。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>料理贴士：美式“假”苹果派的做法，是用饼干替代苹果果肉铺在派皮中，浇上柠檬糖浆简单调味烘烤而成。饼干会吸收酸甜味道的糖浆，变成果泥一般绵软，吃上去就像苹果泥。</p>
