<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>慈济德州分会“2024感恩音乐会”汇聚善念传播爱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374085-Z01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗济峣，萧伊芳和王盈蓉合影。（陆希／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）慈济德州分会“2024感恩音乐会”于11月17日（星期日）下午2点，在慈济德州分会静思堂举行。驻休士顿经文处处长萧伊芳、侨教中心主任王盈蓉、副主任黄依莉、台商会会长徐世钢及理事团队、德州分会执行长罗济峣及民众逾200人共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14374080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14374080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374080-Z02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14374080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374080-Z02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14374080" class="wp-caption-text">（左至右）徐世钢、周政贤夫妇、方台生夫妇、张园立，及台商会代表们出席音乐会。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗济峣感恩所有的工作人员、志工和才华横溢的表演者，使感恩音乐会圆满进行，并合十感恩与会者的热情参与和支持，让这个活动更加圆满。萧伊芳应邀上台致词表示，慈济散播爱和慈善是无边界，特别是投入急难救助行动，总是给人最温暖、安定的力量，感谢慈济人的付出，让世界更美好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14374081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14374081" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374081-Z03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14374081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374081-Z03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14374081" class="wp-caption-text">北美青少年国乐团演出。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14374082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14374082" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374082-Z04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14374082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374082-Z04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14374082" class="wp-caption-text">德州分会2024年回顾影片和会员演出祈祷。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14374083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14374083" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374083-Z05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14374083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14374083-Z05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14374083" class="wp-caption-text">陈羿帆（右）的二胡和施怡璇的柳叶琴演奏。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当日有慈济休士顿青少年、休士顿铃木音乐学校弦乐团师生、大爱合唱团、台声合唱团、印尼赤道安格隆表演志工、声乐家王沛君博士、中提琴演奏家简韶芹、小提琴演奏家刘馨、二胡演奏家陈羿帆、琵琶演奏家吴长璐与北美青少年国乐团的国乐表演等精彩表演，回顾德州分会2024共同携手，为遭遇灾变的飓风灾民的灾后重建工作。而音乐会募集的点滴善款，也将用于帮助飓风灾区。</p>
