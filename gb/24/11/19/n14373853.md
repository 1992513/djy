<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>支持华人参政 新英格兰华人联盟欢度年会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373651-DSC08808-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新英格兰华人联盟（NECAA）11月15日在州府大楼举办年会，杨书旺的“变脸”表演赢得喝采。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）提升华人影响力，支持华人参政。带着这样的理念，新英格兰华人联盟（NECAA）11月15日在麻州政府大楼举办年会，邀华人社区、企业代表与政坛嘉宾聚会欢宴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373638-DSC08656.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373638-DSC08656-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373638" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰华人联盟（NECAA）11月15日在州府大楼举办年会，200多人参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">当天有200多人来到州府大楼的大会堂，参与NECAA成立4周年庆典。州长希莉（Maura Healey）亦派州府社区联络主任戈登（ Art Gordon）出席，向该会颁发褒奖状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373642-DSC08697.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373642-DSC08697-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373642" class="wp-caption-text">州长办公室代表颁赠褒奖状。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14373641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373641-DSC08676.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373641-DSC08676-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373641" class="wp-caption-text">NECAA感谢联盟理事们的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">根据该会网站介绍，非营利组织NECAA成立于2020年11月16日，目标是帮助美国华人参与社区公共活动，改善华人生活，提升华人影响力。自建立以来，该会举办或参与了诸多活动，包括反对歧视亚裔的集会，推动亚裔历史课程进入学校，庆祝亚裔传统月等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373653-DSC08816.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373653-DSC08816-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373653" class="wp-caption-text">NECAA褒奖杰出青年。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14373652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373652-DSC08811.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373652-DSC08811-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373652" class="wp-caption-text">NECAA感谢长期支持者。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">“我们有一个帮助和支持我们项目的社区，我们已经看到了这些努力的影响。”NECAA共会长、麻州亚美局（AAC）委员俞国梁说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">另一位共会长王华谈到，NECAA在Covid疫情高峰时期成立，当时在10多个华人团体的合作下，募集、捐赠了大量医疗防护物资。“自那以后，我们很高兴地说，NECAA成为了这个社区不可分割的一部分。我们对外联络，我们合作无间。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373639-DSC08661.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373639-DSC08661-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373639" class="wp-caption-text">NECAA共会长俞国梁致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14373640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373640-DSC08663.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373640-DSC08663-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373640" class="wp-caption-text">NECAA共会长王华致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">新近当选麻州众议员的马惠美（Amy Mah Sangiolo），是NECAA支持华裔美国人参政的一个例子。来到州府大会堂的年会，马惠美祝贺NECAA在联合社区方面取得的成就，并感谢该会一直服务社区。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">随后，会长王华也祝贺马惠美当选议员，并期待她未来在议会发出华裔美国人的声音。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">在今年的大选中，马惠美以超过81%的得票率，赢得麻州众议员第11选区的席位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373644-DSC08707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373644-DSC08707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373644" class="wp-caption-text">当选麻州众议员的马惠美（Amy Mah Sangiolo）感谢NECAA的社区服务。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14373648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373648-DSC08775.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373648-DSC08775-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373648" class="wp-caption-text">华埠大同药房创办人周树昂参与年会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14373649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373649-DSC08796.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373649-DSC08796-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373649" class="wp-caption-text">麻州亚裔餐饮协会会长邝炎彬参与年会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">今年的年会也有不少在商界和侨社颇有影响力的华人领袖出席，比如麻州亚裔餐饮协会会长邝炎彬，以及华埠大同药房创办人周树昂等等。NECAA也表彰了该会的义工和长期支持者，感谢他们对华人权益的贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373643-DSC08701.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373643-DSC08701-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373643" class="wp-caption-text">亦知音筝乐团古筝重奏《采薇》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14373645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373645" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373645-DSC08750.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373645-DSC08750-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373645" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团带来中国舞表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">年会现场还有中国文化文艺表演。亦知音筝乐团带来了古筝三重奏《采薇》；梦舞团带来了中国舞表演；Phoebe Tian为与会者们演唱《灯塔》；詹俊逸还献上古琴独奏《阳关三叠》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14373646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373646-DSC08753.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373646-DSC08753-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373646" class="wp-caption-text">Phoebe Tian演唱《灯塔》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14373647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14373647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373647-DSC08772.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14373647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14373647-DSC08772-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14373647" class="wp-caption-text">詹俊逸古琴独奏《阳关三叠》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
