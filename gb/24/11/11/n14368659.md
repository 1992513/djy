<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【旅行趣】克罗埃西亚一日跳岛 寻找神秘“蓝洞”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368662-ffce9457f7f10f89d8ba361d02bdec18-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在Hvar入境随俗，摘下所有的防晒装备，尽情拥抱金色阳光。（图／作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<figure id="attachment_14368667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368667-80148cec36410390132a99d2d2c5b6cd.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368667-80148cec36410390132a99d2d2c5b6cd-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368667" class="wp-caption-text">乘风破浪，前进亚得里亚海(Adriatic Sea)诸岛。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>史普利特（Split）是达尔马提亚地区（Dalmacija）最大的港都，也是前往亚得里亚海诸岛的最佳出发点。克罗埃西亚（Croatia）拥有许多美丽的小岛，就像散落在亚得里亚海上的一颗颗小珍珠。其中，位于比谢沃岛（Bi?evo）的蓝洞（Modra ?pilja, Blue Cave），更是可遇不可求的神秘美景，只有在天候状况符合特定条件时，才会向旅人展现她的奇幻之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14368668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368668" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368668-479fc9172f42f5fe844e499dde920d71.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368668-479fc9172f42f5fe844e499dde920d71-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368668" class="wp-caption-text">特吉尔岛（Trogir）有许多运河交织其中。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14368664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368664-3edf05ff49f38a9028be4aa8c9d7ea22.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368664-3edf05ff49f38a9028be4aa8c9d7ea22-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368664" class="wp-caption-text">托吉尔（Trogir）是一座世界文化遗产古都，城内有著名的运河交织其中。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14368663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368663-0ed5800bf8a54424f14d9dc786876c8d.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368663-0ed5800bf8a54424f14d9dc786876c8d-600x440.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="440" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368663" class="wp-caption-text">托吉尔（Trogir）的运河。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14368665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368665-ad8604c27bc7638a081e50a8c0be8e6d.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368665-ad8604c27bc7638a081e50a8c0be8e6d-600x630.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="630" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368665" class="wp-caption-text">托吉尔（Trogir）圣罗浮大教堂（Katedrala Sv. Lovre）的古城风采。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>若想畅游诸岛，推荐参加当地旅行社的一日跳岛行程。从斯普利特乘坐快艇出发，首先抵达托吉尔（Trogir），这是一座世界文化遗产古都，城内有著名的运河交织其中，我们在此一睹圣罗浮大教堂（Katedrala Sv. Lovre）及古城风采后，便前往下一座小岛赫瓦尔（Hvar）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14368673" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368673" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368673-c3ca537963d873754d675e3726d59014.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368673" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368673-c3ca537963d873754d675e3726d59014-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368673" class="wp-caption-text">赫瓦尔（Hvar），是亚得里亚海最火红的度假岛屿之一。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<div class="mceTemp"></div>
<p>曾被票选为世界十大美丽小岛之一的赫瓦尔（Hvar），是亚得里亚海最火红的度假岛屿之一，因温暖的地中海气候致使此地日照时间较长，吸引来自世界各地的旅客到此享受明媚的阳光。放眼望去，沙滩上挤满努力将身体晒成古铜色的游客，我们也入境随俗，摘下所有的防晒装备，尽情拥抱金色阳光。</p>
<p>但要特别小心的是，附近全是布满黑色长刺海胆的浅滩，我们还差点被海胆刺伤呢！见我们吓得花容失色，一位当地人热情的与我们分享小秘诀，他说只要在身上涂上橄榄油，海胆就会慢慢散开。有海胆密集恐惧症的朋友，下次不妨试试看！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14368661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368661-e3b66c4b60c98b271c4d970362aac8ef.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368661-e3b66c4b60c98b271c4d970362aac8ef-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368661" class="wp-caption-text">史提尼瓦（Stiniva）可以尽情跳水，与亚德里亚海玩成一片！（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着来到史提尼瓦（Stiniva），是赫瓦尔岛北海岸的一个小海湾。由于天然的地理屏障，所以这里很适合从事水上活动，尤其是跳水。只见海湾内不分男女老幼，大家都兴奋的从快艇上纵身一跳再跳，尽情徜徉在大海中，与亚得里亚海玩成一片！最让我惊奇的是，这里没有任何人使用游泳圈，似乎所有人都是天生泳将，一到海中便能自在漂浮，三五好友围成一圈，在海面上边喝饮料边谈笑风生，此情此景让我对克罗埃西亚有种相当鲜明的新奇感受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14368669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368669-cdabade50bae9b9d4bc1a37f2a80cd38.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368669-cdabade50bae9b9d4bc1a37f2a80cd38-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368669" class="wp-caption-text">换乘小船，探访秘境“蓝洞”（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14368672" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368672" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368672-ed17ada4505d7e5695e43769bcd60da9.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368672" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368672-ed17ada4505d7e5695e43769bcd60da9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368672" class="wp-caption-text">搭乘小船，准备探访隐秘的蓝洞（Modra ?pilja, Blue Cave）。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着我们改乘小船，准备探访隐秘的蓝洞（Modra ?pilja, Blue Cave）。由于洞窟入口非常狭小，每次只容一条小船通过，当进入洞口时，所有人都要趴下才不会被石灰岩洞窟撞伤脑袋。好不容易挤进蓝洞，大家除了连声惊叹之外，想不出任何形容词来赞叹这大自然的鬼斧神工。据说蓝洞的成因，是因洞窟内近海平面处，刚好有合适的小洞，当天气晴朗时，阳光顺着小洞折射进洞穴，便会呈现耀眼的蓝色光辉，将洞窟染成一片湛蓝。蓝洞像大自然的隐藏版彩蛋，使旅人瞬间置身琉璃仙境，美得令人陶醉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14368675" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368675" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368675-25d15d36435d78ef34e8066e2b6b6871.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368675-25d15d36435d78ef34e8066e2b6b6871-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368675" class="wp-caption-text">蓝洞像大自然的隐藏版彩蛋，使旅人瞬间置身琉璃仙境。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14368674" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14368674" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368674-edb024f927bc393537c2a17585c48356.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14368674" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14368674-edb024f927bc393537c2a17585c48356-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14368674" class="wp-caption-text">蓝洞是可遇不可求的神秘美景。（图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到克罗埃西亚，参加当地旅行社的一日跳岛之旅，是一亲亚得里亚海诸岛芳泽的好方法，在各小岛停留的时间也很充裕，能以悠闲的步伐，细细品味克罗埃西亚的独特风情与自然魅力。唯一要叮咛大家的就是：搭乘快艇在海面上随浪飞驰，船身忽上忽下，旅客就像一个个跳跃的音符，要紧紧抓稳把手喔！@*</p>
