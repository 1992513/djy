<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澎湖南寮咕咾石建筑 浮球秘境一起抢戏</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286860-694481-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>南寮古厝特色：建材为咕咾石。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月09日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾澎湖报导）澎湖不仅拥有美丽的海景，还有一个被称为“浮球秘境”的超人气IG打卡景点！今天，我们跟随空拍达人nina Chen，一起探访这个隐藏在南寮古厝的神秘景点，享受一段充满惊喜的旅程！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286861-694482.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286861-694482-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286861" class="wp-caption-text">澎湖县花天人菊。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>南寮古厝咕咾石建筑</h2>
<p>南寮古厝位于奎壁山摩西分海附近，这里的古厝群大多是用澎湖特有的咕咾石建成，nina Chen：“仿佛置身于古代农村，满满的历史气息。这里曾经入选德国柏林国际旅展公布的全球百大绿色旅游景点，不要错过！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286863" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286863-694484.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286863-694484-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286863" class="wp-caption-text">记得带上防晒装备，澎湖的夏天可不是开玩笑的！（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>潮汐未到　先来拍拍南寮古厝</h2>
<p>由于潮汐时间还没到，我们决定先在南寮古厝里随意拍拍。这里近年来推动社区改造，不仅修复了许多咕咾石屋舍，还设计了许多大小不一的拍照景点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286864-694485.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286864-694485-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286864" class="wp-caption-text">南寮社区地标之一。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>特别是那彩色的浮球装置艺术，让这里成为澎湖的热门IG打卡点，吸引游客前来朝圣！nina Chen：“我们骑摩托车来的，超热的，可是都来澎湖了，没来打卡太可惜了，巷弄啦、彩绘啦、还有最美的天人菊都非常好拍，让人惊艳。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286862-694483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286862-694483-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286862" class="wp-caption-text">南寮社区彩绘墙。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>时空停止 走进古色古香的南寮村</h2>
<p>走在南寮村里，仿佛时光倒流，来到一个古色古香的老农村。这里有许多咕咾石建筑，nina Chen：“像是许家古厝、湖西辛家古宅和福记鱼灶等，都充满了澎湖特色。当然，最吸引我们的就是那神秘的‘浮球秘境’！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286869-694490.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286869-694490-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286869" class="wp-caption-text">蓝天、地上的影子，还有缤纷的浮球，一起来抢戏。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>寻找浮球秘境之旅</h2>
<p>澎湖的夏天真的很热，走在外面简直是挑战极限！</p>
<p>nina Chen用Google导航带我们到了南寮古厝，经过木造广场和红色浮球，终于找到了入口！从左边的小路走到底右转，浮球秘境就在眼前啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286865-694486.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286865-694486-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286865" class="wp-caption-text">海岛上难得见到的绿意。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>缤纷的浮球在蓝天下闪耀，这个秘境真的让人惊喜不已。虽然天气很热，nina Chen还是冒险前行，而她的同伴则在阴影处避暑。nina Chen：“这个景点的独特之处一一展现，值得一来！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286868-694489.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286868-694489-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286868" class="wp-caption-text">就地取材，把浮球变美了。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的南寮古厝与浮球秘境探险真是充满惊喜，不仅拍了好多美照，还发现了这么一个隐藏的宝地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286867" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286867-694488.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286867" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286867-694488-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286867" class="wp-caption-text">很气派的古厝，还有美丽的雕梁画栋。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>贴心小提醒：</p>
<p>1、记得带上防晒装备，澎湖的夏天可不是开玩笑的！</p>
<p>２、Google导航是你最好的朋友，寻找秘境时别忘了用它。</p>
<p>３、尊重当地居民和文化，保持环境整洁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14286870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14286870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286870-694491.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14286870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14286870-694491-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14286870" class="wp-caption-text">南寮社区的冰室。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
