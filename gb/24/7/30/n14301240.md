<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>芦竹炫YA夜农游季 大古山步道闪耀到八月底</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301242-698945-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“芦竹炫YA农游季”与动物互动。（桃园市农业局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月30日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）2024“芦竹炫YA农游季”落幕，活动期间30梯次免费小旅行及10场次食农教育体验，场场爆满，好评不断，活动超过10家在地农场参与，推出夏季限定专属游程，包含：山脚鹿场与鹿互动、秀嵋园艺多肉组盆，双源鱼菜蚯共生农场水耕蔬菜、及成质朴香茅精油防蚊液、蜂中鸡园蜂蜜手摇饮DIY、竹峰茗茶识茶体验、新星茶业品茗活动、羽泰制茶厂捡茶枝、小木屋休闲农场甲虫装置DIY、坑子社区土角厝热缩片、山鼻社区德馨堂古迹巡礼导览等，让民众有机会深入认识桃园芦竹在地优质休闲农游场域。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14301241" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14301241" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301241-698944.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14301241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301241-698944.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14301241" class="wp-caption-text">食农教育体验，场场爆满，好评不断。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>更多秋季限定活动持续规划中，未来可继续关注各农场粉丝专页，及大古山休闲农业区<ahref="https://reurl.cc/5vD3Oy" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow noreferrer">https://reurl.cc/5vD3Oy</a>）、坑子溪休闲农业区粉丝专页<ahref="https://reurl.cc/MO624X" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow noreferrer">https://reurl.cc/MO624X</a>）报名参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14301243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14301243" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301243-698946.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14301243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301243-698946.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14301243" class="wp-caption-text">竹峰茗茶识茶体验。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大古山步道公园及坑子溪游客中心夜间科技灯饰将持续闪耀到8月底，还没来过的朋友请把握最后机会，此外，机场捷运A9、A10站内设置的美拍打卡装置也将持续展示到12月，美拍打卡装置以芦竹在地农产品绿竹笋、稻米、鹿、茶叶、火龙果为特色布置，装置结合农特产品陈列，让通勤族、游客等可以了解芦竹在地特色，大家走过路过千万别错过，快来与美丽的打卡装置拍照留念吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14301244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14301244" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301244-698947.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14301244" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301244-698947.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14301244" class="wp-caption-text">羽泰制茶厂捡茶枝。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14301245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14301245" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301245-698948.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14301245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14301245-698948.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14301245" class="wp-caption-text">小木屋休闲农场甲虫装置DIY。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦竹在地农村美食也不容错过，今招窑烤柴烧坊融入古早在地窑烧文化，不定期推出健康又多元的红茶系列面包、机捷下的稻田-天赐米的桃园三号香米、传统米食英智农场的草仔粿及肉粽、羽泰制茶厂的茶香雪Q饼、在地小农现采季节蔬菜在坑子溪游客中心新鲜贩售等，桃园休闲农业活动多元又精彩，更多活动资讯可关注桃园好农<ahref="https://reurl.cc/oRVv73" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow noreferrer">https://reurl.cc/oRVv73</a>）。◇</p>
