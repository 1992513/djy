<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澎湖吉贝屿的探险日记</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281791-692514-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>澎湖吉贝屿的全貌。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾澎湖报导）当提到澎湖群岛，你会想到什么？白沙、阳光、海浪？今天，让我们一起跟着空拍达人Nina Chen，踏上澎湖北海中最大的有人岛——吉贝屿，揭开这颗隐藏在台湾西南方海域中的明珠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282564" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282564-25977_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282564-25977_0-600x333.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282564" class="wp-caption-text">吉贝屿的晨昏，云彩特别有戏。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>旅游体验与感受</h2>
<p>6月29日，Nina Chen来到吉贝屿。下午四点多和早上五点多两次来到石沪观景，却因风大浪大而无缘一见。她分享道：“旅馆就位于海堤边，没有石沪和日出（多云），只有滔滔海浪…居然有点失落的感觉。趁着早餐前，我骑着欧兜迈前往拍摄沙丘，那是我在吉贝最爱的景点。”（<strong>延伸阅读：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/26/n14277580.md#1?utm_term=djy_a_rec_in" target="_blank" rel="noopener">跟着摄影家 游澎湖南方花屿、桶盘和猫屿</a></span></strong>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282568-25884_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282568-25884_0-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282568" class="wp-caption-text">骑着摩托车去拍沙丘。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282574-25973_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282574-25973_0-600x332.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282574" class="wp-caption-text">海边的景致好像一幅画作。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina Chen说：“事先查潮汐时间，就可以知道涨退潮，才能看到石沪的样貌。</p>
<p>Nina特别喜欢吉贝的沙丘，远眺目斗屿的美景，沙丘上长满了滨刺麦，仿佛置身于自然的诗画中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282567-25873_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282567-25873_0-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282567" class="wp-caption-text">沙丘上长满了滨刺麦。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282565-ed117eb73bfdea695c35e78081c8098f.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282565-ed117eb73bfdea695c35e78081c8098f-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282565" class="wp-caption-text">吉贝屿石沪。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>农渔业的变迁</h2>
<p>吉贝屿岛上地势平坦，耕地广大。早期居民以农渔业为生，但随着人口的增长，传统产业逐渐衰退。日治时期，许多居民迁往台湾本岛谋生。战后，政府积极推动农渔业发展，改善农作物品种，提供肥料补助，放领渔船，并建设渔港码头，让岛上农渔业逐渐复苏，一度享有“小美国”之称。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282570" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282570-25969_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282570-25969_0-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282570" class="wp-caption-text">在吉贝屿的空拍景色。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282579-26031.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282579-26031-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282579" class="wp-caption-text">吉贝屿上的街景。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>游客大推的美食</h2>
<p>随着时代变迁，吉贝屿成为游客们避暑度假的好去处。空拍达人Nina Chen推荐了一家热炒店，可惜由于游客减少，另一家无菜单料理餐厅，现在晚餐时间已经不营业了，想吃的话要先预约哦。不过，她还分享了一家超浮夸的贝壳冰店，炎热的夏天来上一大碗清凉的雪绵冰，让她和家人瞬间消暑。（<strong>延伸阅读：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/5/11/n14246234.md#1?utm_term=djy_a_rec_in" target="_blank" rel="noopener">在地人带路 感受太麻里独特的吸引力</a></span></strong>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282577-25889.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282577-25889-600x359.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282577" class="wp-caption-text">吉贝屿岛上热炒店推出的菜单，让nina Chen赞不绝口。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282578-25892.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282578-25892-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282578" class="wp-caption-text">因为澎湖本岛的花火节，入住其他岛上的游客就变少了。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>岛屿地貌与历史背景</h2>
<p>吉贝屿位于白沙岛北方约5.5公里，是一个典型的海积地形岛屿。全岛地势东高西低，由沙滩及沙嘴构成，西南方的沙滩延伸至海中形成一条全长约700公尺的沙咀。这里的潮间带广大，四周点缀着约八十余座大小不一的石沪，犹如大自然的艺术品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282576-25877_0.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282576-25877_0-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282576" class="wp-caption-text">吉贝屿可以眺远目斗屿。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吉贝屿的古地名叫“嘉贝屿”，这是因为岛屿形状宛如一枚漂浮在海面的螺贝。历经数代的更替，最终改名为吉贝。这里隶属澎湖县白沙乡，自清朝初期至今，经历了多次行政组织变革，终于成为今日独立设置的行政村。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282571-25970_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14282571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282571-25970_0-600x326.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282571" class="wp-caption-text">海边柱状的玄武岩。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>岛民们的期盼</h2>
<p>尽管吉贝晚上的游客越来越少，民宿主人说：应该跟澎湖本岛举办花火节的缘故。但这里依然拥有无与伦比的自然美景和丰富的历史文化。每一位踏上这片土地的游客，都能感受到那份与众不同的宁静与美好。希望未来有更多人能够发现吉贝屿的魅力，体验这片纯净天地带来的惬意。</p>
