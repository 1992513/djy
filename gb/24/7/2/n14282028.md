<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>排湾部落小确幸 Sho Pai 14 杂货店重现</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282443-26225_0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）在屏东县玛家乡排湾村14号，有一间独特的小杂货店，名叫“Sho Pai 14 拾肆号杂货”。这家店坐落在185沿山公路上，从三地门往垦丁的路上，充满着部落浓厚的生活气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282274" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282274-a961f55eb90eff068e0b9cf8b8bcbb66.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282274-a961f55eb90eff068e0b9cf8b8bcbb66-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282274" class="wp-caption-text">在大武山脚那偏远而寂静的夜晚，Sho Pai 几乎成了族人唯一的生活供给点。（Zuljezulj提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这间小店什么都有卖，从泡面、零食到各式啤酒、咖啡、冰淇淋、简餐、厚片、奶茶，甚至还有红白酒。更别提那神秘的部落私房惊喜饮品，总能带给族人及来访的客人意想不到的惊喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282280" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282280-4bb68e33d181e2a16bbdfdd8c9c4c296.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282280-4bb68e33d181e2a16bbdfdd8c9c4c296-450x511.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="511" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282280" class="wp-caption-text">路过的游客也可以停下来，感染一下原住民欢乐知足的气氛。（Zuljezulj提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杂货店主人Zuljezulj表示：“Sho Pai”这名字源自日治时期传入部落的外来语，意思就是“杂货店”。而“14号”则是国民政府将族人从山上原居地，迁移至山脚平地所给予的门牌号码。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282276" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282276-26226_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282276-26226_0-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282276" class="wp-caption-text">14号是国民政府将族人迁至平地，给予的门牌号。（Zuljezulj提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这一百多年来，两个外来政权带来的巨大冲击，让部落传统生活经历了不少辛酸与无奈。但即便如此，部落族人依然坚毅乐天，继续过着安好、乐天的生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282277" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282277-26224_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282277-26224_0-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282277" class="wp-caption-text">杂货店成为部落孩子们，开心聚会的好所在。（Zuljezulj提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在大武山脚那偏远而寂静的夜晚，Sho Pai 几乎成了族人唯一的生活供给点，部落族人：“这里是我们获得小确幸的地方。这里没有特色，但却是满足各种生活饮食需求的快乐聚会地点。”路过的游客也可以停下来，感染一下原住民欢乐知足的气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282283" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282283-45c60764a221edbc363c614323b04e94.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282283-45c60764a221edbc363c614323b04e94-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282283" class="wp-caption-text">这里没有特色，但却是满足各种生活饮食需求的快乐聚会地点。（Zuljezulj提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Zujlezulj表示小时候就是在母亲开的杂货店帮忙学习，她说：“从小就看着母亲帮助部落弱势的族人，随着渐渐长大外出求学工作，母亲的杂货店也就走入历史，2023年9月在哥哥胡健kui 及家人们的支持下，杂货店用崭新面貌重新回归部落！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282282-26220_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282282-26220_0-450x340.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282282" class="wp-caption-text">2023年9月在哥哥胡健kui和家人支持下 ，重新回归部落！（Zuljezulj提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>可爱的Zuljezulj分享杂货店最夯的饮料：“炎热的正午，什么都吃不下，这时候只需要来一杯满满维他命C的透心凉冻柠雪。做法超简单，只要在冰块杯中倒入清凉有劲的雪碧，再加上几片新鲜的柠檬片就搞定了！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282281" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282281-d9bd105ab3c73bb9faadfc97ef706253.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282281-d9bd105ab3c73bb9faadfc97ef706253-450x707.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="551" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282281" class="wp-caption-text">透心凉冻柠雪。（Zuljezulj提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论是来买瓶超冰冷饮解渴，还是想喝杯热咖啡取暖，亦或是来点特制冰淇淋解暑，Sho Pai 14 拾肆号杂货店总是能带给人温暖的感受。在这里，族人们笑语盈盈，享受着简单而美好的生活。这小小的杂货店，仿佛是部落生活的缩影，满载着部落族人的幸福与希望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282275" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282275-62fc55a883e51d0b65f6d986efadbce5.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282275-62fc55a883e51d0b65f6d986efadbce5-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282275" class="wp-caption-text">Sho Pai 14 杂货店提供的巧克力布朗尼。（Zuljezulj提供）</figcaption></figure>
