<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>政治捐款新法律生效一周后 西澳工党披露已获得捐款逾12万</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年07月21日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">根据</span><span lang="en-AU">7</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-TW">日一生效的政治捐款新法律，工党在第一周内就记录了</span><span lang="en-AU">122,300</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元的捐款。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">该法律于去年年底获得西澳议会通过，要求政党在收到累计超过</span><span lang="en-AU">2,600</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元的捐款后的一周内进行披露，并需要在新的在线捐款系统上做出声明，该系统由西澳选举委员会运作。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，在</span><span lang="en-AU">2022-23</span><span lang="zh-TW">财年，工党从捐助者那里获得了超过</span><span lang="en-AU">660</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的捐款，而自由党筹集了约</span><span lang="en-AU">450</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">自</span><span lang="en-AU">7</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-TW">日以来，工党最大的捐助者之一是</span><span lang="en-AU">CFMEU</span><span lang="zh-TW">（建筑、林业、采矿和能源工会），他们向工党捐赠了</span><span lang="en-AU">25,000</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。矿业和能源商会捐赠了</span><span lang="en-AU">16,500</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，安德鲁</span><span lang="en-AU">·</span><span lang="zh-TW">福雷斯特（</span><span lang="en-AU">Andrew Forrest</span><span lang="zh-TW">）与前妻尼科拉</span><span lang="en-AU">·</span><span lang="zh-TW">福雷斯特（</span><span lang="en-AU">Nicola Forrest</span><span lang="zh-TW">）共同拥有的私人投资集团</span><span lang="en-AU">Tattarang</span><span lang="zh-TW">捐赠了</span><span lang="en-AU">15,000</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，埃里森（</span><span lang="en-AU">Chris Ellison</span><span lang="zh-TW">）的矿产资源公司分两次捐赠了</span><span lang="en-AU">8,250</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。房地产开发商</span><span lang="en-AU">Urban Collectives</span><span lang="zh-TW">捐赠了</span><span lang="en-AU">3,500</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，</span><span lang="en-AU">Satterley Property Group</span><span lang="zh-TW">捐赠了</span><span lang="en-AU">3,860</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">工党收到的个人捐款包括卡纳加辛甘博士（</span><span lang="en-AU">Dr.Yogesan Kanagasingam</span><span lang="zh-TW">）捐赠的</span><span lang="en-AU">5,500</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，胡行阮（</span><span lang="en-AU">Huu Hanh Nguyen</span><span lang="zh-TW">）和</span><span lang="en-AU">Tony Chong</span><span lang="zh-TW">各自捐赠的</span><span lang="en-AU">5,000</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p>在此期间，还未有其它政党注册收到任何捐款。</p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">2023-24</span><span lang="zh-TW">财年各政党收到的捐款将在今年年底公布。</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：杨新云</p>
