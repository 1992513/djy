<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夏季漂流 ~ 上山，下海！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289587-694781-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在群山环抱的南澳南溪，体验泰雅式漂流。（Yama漂流-亚溤而夏提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月12日讯】（大纪元记者杨子樊台湾宜兰报导）炎炎夏季，上山享受森林大地能量，来场溪谷漂流，下海徜徉沁凉海水，感受渔村魅力！让我们细细探索，走读不一样的苏澳小镇，一同奔向自然，在翠绿山峦与湛蓝大海，留下难忘夏日回忆！</p>
<p>位于宜兰东南方的苏澳，是北台湾通往东台湾的必经之地，每年夏季，正值旅游旺季，著名观光景点有终年恒温约22℃的苏澳冷泉，以及仅次于屏东东港的第二大黑鲔鱼产地——南方澳。</p>
<p>许多游客行经苏澳，会将此地作为苏花公路前往花东地区的中继站，匆匆停留。其实，游人不妨驻足于此，来个两天一夜轻旅行，更能深入当地，体验小镇魅力。</p>
<p>相较于其他乡镇，纯朴的苏澳是富庶丰饶的鱼米之乡，因当地复杂且多元的地形，形塑出具有独特韵味的人文情调。为了抓住在地之美，深度体验当地观光文化，有深根在地的饭店业者珑山林苏澳冷热泉度假饭店，于今夏推出了以奔向自然、探索在地，感受地方魅力为主题的活动，推荐几项刺激好玩、具人文特色、阖家适合的游程。</p>
<h2>冒险阖家欢：南澳漂漂河</h2>
<p>深入山林，除了大口吸进芬多精，还可以有一个趣味新选择——体验泰雅式漂流！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289587" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289587-694781-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289587-694781-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289587" class="wp-caption-text">在群山环抱的南澳南溪，体验泰雅式漂流。（Yama漂流-亚溤而夏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到群山环抱的南澳南溪，在原乡部落青年教练热情的带领下，我们前往当地秘境，体验时而惬意，时而刺激的溪谷漂流。透过自小玩到大，各种地势再熟悉不过的教练，悉心解说漂漂河的各种注意事项与游戏规则，例如：如何搭乘漂流圈、不幸“翻船”该怎么脱身等情况，让一个半小时的漂流行程，在尖叫、放空的心境转变下，达到充分的放松，一扫夏日暑气。担心孩童不宜吗？Yama漂流-亚溤而夏的教练说：“放心，小朋友往往是最能上手的。这是一项从6岁到65岁身心健康者都能安心玩乐的游戏喔！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289588" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289588-694782-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289588-694782-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289588" class="wp-caption-text">在群山环抱的南澳南溪，体验泰雅式漂流。（Yama漂流-亚溤而夏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14289589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289589-694783-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289589-694783-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289589" class="wp-caption-text">在群山环抱的南澳南溪，体验泰雅式漂流。（Yama漂流-亚溤而夏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小提醒：若要体验此项活动，建议民众安排半日行程。因为全程活动从解说、安全装备着装、车程，以及盥洗时间，大约需要3.5~4小时。</p>
<h2>走读在地：南方澳渔港</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14289593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289593" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289593-694797-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289593-694797-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289593" class="wp-caption-text">纯朴的南方澳渔港，可以买到新鲜的鱼货。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14289600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289600-694809-450x327.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289600-694809-450x327.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="327" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289600" class="wp-caption-text">港口老街上的摊贩与名产店。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14289601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289601" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289601-694810-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289601-694810-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289601" class="wp-caption-text">港口老街上贩卖海味的名产店。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>山谷体验漂漂河后，我们往海边前进。来到南方澳渔村，可以看见不少游客特地来此地享用美味的海鲜料理，甚是购买新鲜鱼货。因为这里其实是台湾三大渔港之一，更是东部远洋渔业的重要基地。要在这里购买海鲜，须特别注意时间！第一渔市于每天下午2时开始，由渔会主持拍卖渔获，量大且不提供一般民众购买。第二渔市于每日3 时至 5 时，有许多摊商零售鱼货，除了“冷冻”，更有“现捞仔”，种类繁多，提供小量购买。至于第三渔市，位于渔港路上，沿路海鲜快炒店林立，有些店家甚至可以提供代客料理服务，十分有趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289597" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289597-694806-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289597-694806-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289597" class="wp-caption-text">南方澳南天宫供奉着金妈祖、玉妈祖。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>南方澳港口建港已超过一百年。在渔港正前方，有一间声名远播的妈祖庙南天宫。该庙是1950年，由乡绅简阿祥发起筹建，而后，利用1990年扩建时信众捐募之基金，以8千万元采购203.8公斤纯金，打造出6尺3寸高的金妈祖神像。金妈祖于1995年9月10日安座，是全台最早打造金妈祖的庙宇。该庙除了金身妈祖，还有罕见的玉身妈祖，两者含“金玉满堂尊”之美意，庇佑南方澳进出讨海的船只，让妈祖信仰在此地兴盛不衰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289599" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289599-694808-450x350.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289599-694808-450x350.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289599" class="wp-caption-text">从南天宫眺望南方澳渔港。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14289591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289591" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289591-694792-450x410.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289591-694792-450x410.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289591" class="wp-caption-text">金妈祖与玉妈祖，两者含“金玉满堂尊”之美意。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>老店新创意：阿赛喇冰</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14289598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289598" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289598-694807-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289598-694807-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289598" class="wp-caption-text">百年老店阿赛喇冰的四果冰。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14289592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289592" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289592-694793-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289592-694793-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289592" class="wp-caption-text">百年老店阿赛喇冰的四果冰。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到南天宫旁边，一家百年冰店吸引着炎热夏季“找凉”观光客的目光。创立于1923年的老店，至今传承到第四代店老板邱先生，他回忆，“阿赛是阿祖的名字。在30年代的南方澳，为了让小孩可以顺利健康长大，于是取名阿赛，希望他的生命能如野草般坚韧。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289594" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289594-694801-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289594-694801-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289594" class="wp-caption-text">南方澳老铺冰店自家研发的签诗枝仔冰。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他与太太庄小姐共同经营冰店，除了贩售着传统的四果冰、招牌手打三花冰（花生酱、花生粒、花豆），还研发新产品——签诗枝仔冰。从台北嫁到这里的庄小姐热情与我们分享着，自己如何深深的被夫家冰店百年故事所吸引。成为南方澳媳妇后，她更爱上了当地的人文特色，为了这片土地，庄小姐义无反顾的开始推动地方创生计划。过程中，尽管面临许多碰撞，但就像她脸上笑容透露出的坚毅、乐观，感染了周边的人、事、物，也支持着这对夫妻一路往前。</p>
<h2>热血冲浪女孩：做出好面包</h2>
<p>离开南方澳，车行至无尾港附近的马赛小镇，有一家特别的面包店，店内竖起几块冲浪板，卖的是欧式面包与贝果等。皮肤黝黑的老板娘告诉我们，她原是学服装设计相关科系，曾经到欧洲留学，在那里爱上了充满麦香的面包，回来台湾之后，开始学习面包制作。一开始，用摊车的形式贩售，地点选在无尾港，这是因为自己爱冲浪，享用美味面包填饱浪人肚子。没想到生意越来越好，如今，面包小屋落址在无尾港附近的马赛小镇，提供给更多面包同好享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289595" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289595-694802-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14289595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289595-694802-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289595" class="wp-caption-text">热爱冲浪的老板娘手作道地的欧式面包。（廖伟汎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
