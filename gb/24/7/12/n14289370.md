<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“童心协力，迈向永续！”18年后全国童军大露营再见台南</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289371-695275-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>海外童军团代表。（陈伟雄提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）睽违18年，期待已久的第12次全国童军大露营再次回到台南走马濑农场举办，这次的主题是“童心协力、怀抱国际、迈向永续”。来自日本、韩国、香港、马来西亚、菲律宾、斯里兰卡等18个国家的童军朋友们，还有来自台湾各地的8,000多名童军，齐聚一堂，准备展开7天6夜的超级冒险！</p>
<p>在10日的开幕式上，场面热闹非凡，台南市长黄伟哲用幽默的方式欢迎大家：“在台南，连空气都是甜的，欢迎多吸几口空气，感受一下‘台南甜’！”他还特别提到，目前是芒果季节，邀请大家品尝台南的夏日红宝石。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289373-695277.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14289373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289373-695277-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289373" class="wp-caption-text">云林童军团代表。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14289372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289372-695276.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14289372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289372-695276-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289372" class="wp-caption-text">期待已久的第12次全国童军大露营再次回到台南走马濑农场举办。（台南市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次大露营的筹备工作可是非常用心。中华民国童军总会秘书长苏德祥表示：“一切都已准备就绪！”为了因应炎热的天气和午后的雷阵雨，他们特别和专业帐篷公司合作，确保大家无论是面对热浪还是大雨，都能舒适又安全。营地还铺设了2300块木栈板，预防泥泞影响活动，真是贴心到位！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289375" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289375-695279.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14289375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289375-695279-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289375" class="wp-caption-text">面对热浪还是大雨，都能舒适又安全。营地还铺设了2300块木栈板，预防泥泞影响活动。（台南市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了冒险活动，黄伟哲市长还安排了文化采风行程，让大家能深度感受台南这座古都的文化魅力。不论是品尝在地美食，还是欣赏自然美景，都让童军们充满期待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289376" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289376-695280.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14289376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289376-695280-600x800.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289376" class="wp-caption-text">参访萧珑文化园区。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14289378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289378-695290.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14289378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289378-695290-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289378" class="wp-caption-text">童军伙伴们，参访萧珑文化园区。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自香港的童军团代表陈伟雄说：“童军对小朋友的训练真的很有帮助，我从小学四年级就参加了，现在还在持续，不仅学到了生活技能，还拓展了人际关系。”而来自云林的童军团代表蔡智惠则表示：“天气真的很热，体感温度超过40度，但这就是情义相挺，发挥童军精神：仁爱、互助！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289374-695278.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14289374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289374-695278-600x800.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289374" class="wp-caption-text">体感温度超过四十度，但童军团长们仍然全力以赴。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次大露营不仅是一次冒险之旅，更是一个国际交流的平台。来自世界各地的童军们聚在一起，共同学习，共同成长，这样的经验绝对是无价的。让我们一起期待这次大露营的精彩瞬间吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14289377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14289377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289377-695281.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14289377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289377-695281-600x618.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="618" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14289377" class="wp-caption-text">香港童军总会。（陈伟雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
