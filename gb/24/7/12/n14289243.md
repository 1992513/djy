<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>清凉一夏！来场金门撸麦虾初体验</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289244-695145-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黄历6月至8月是金门麦虾盛产的季节，这段时间可是讨海人的黄金时刻。（古岗社区发展协会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾金门报导）黄历6月至8月是金门麦虾盛产的季节，这段时间可是讨海人的黄金时刻。麦虾，是早期渔民生活艰辛时的重要粮食，如果没有捕到鱼获，麦虾便成了救命的次要之粮食。</p>
<figure id="14289250" aria-describedby="caption-14289250" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289250-695152-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289250-695152-450x338.jpg" alt="耆老董群省：“麦虾盛产于黄历6到8月。”" width="601" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289250" class="wp-caption-text">耆老董群省：“麦虾盛产于黄历6到8月。”（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>你知道吗？渔民们会在潮间带处待涨潮时，将网具架于肩上往返捕捞，撸后再带回清洗晒干煮食，沾酱油吃、煮咸粥、炒面线、沾面粉油炸炒菜，样样都好吃！</p>
<figure id="14289253" aria-describedby="caption-14289253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289253-695164-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289253-695164-450x337.jpg" alt="麦虾饼。" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289253" class="wp-caption-text">麦虾饼。（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当你漫步在古岗村，你会看到讨海人手持两根长竹竿挂着一张网，来来回回在海水中忙碌。太阳高挂，古岗讨海人推着叉手网，不辞辛劳地在水中穿梭，为的就是捕获那美味可口的麦虾。</p>
<figure id="14289251" aria-describedby="caption-14289251" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289251-695162-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289251-695162-450x338.jpg" alt="渔民们会在潮间带处待涨潮时，将网具架于肩上往返捕捞。" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289251" class="wp-caption-text">渔民们会在潮间带处待涨潮时，将网具架于肩上往返捕捞。（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>耆老董群省：“麦虾盛产于黄历6到8月，尤其是夏季西南气流过后，大量麦虾为躲避大鱼的捕食，往内海逃窜。”它们成了渔人的“虾”场。</p>
<figure id="14289246" aria-describedby="caption-14289246" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289246-695147-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289246-695147-450x338.jpg" alt="讨海人手持两根长竹竿挂着一张网，来来回回在海水中忙碌。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289246" class="wp-caption-text">讨海人手持两根长竹竿挂着一张网，来来回回在海水中忙碌。（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14289245" aria-describedby="caption-14289245" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289245-695146-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289245-695146-450x338.jpg" alt="董群省：“从事捕捞二十多年，这种海产煮稀饭最好吃。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289245" class="wp-caption-text">董群省：“从事捕捞二十多年，这种海产煮稀饭最好吃。（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>你一定想问，这麦虾怎么捕？很简单，把竹竿插入海中大约一公尺左右，待退潮四成水之后，便可以开始捕捞了。董群省：“从事捕捞二十多年，这种海产煮稀饭最好吃，以往因为捕不到大鱼，只好捕这个回家给小孩吃。”</p>
<p>麦虾体色近透明，体长约2～3公分，味美甘甜，好消化，是小朋友成长补充钙质的圣物。</p>
<figure id="14289247" aria-describedby="caption-14289247" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289247-695148-450x231.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289247-695148-450x231.jpeg" alt="麦虾体色近透明，体长约2～3公分，味美甘甜。" width="600" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289247" class="wp-caption-text">麦虾体色近透明，体长约2～3公分，味美甘甜。（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在早期，生活艰辛的渔民，若没有捕到鱼获，麦虾就是次要的粮食及收入来源。而如今，从事鱼业捕捞二十多年的董群省，捕捞麦虾成了他的休闲娱乐。他说，渔民在捕获麦虾后，会依个人喜好，现煮或清洗，再经过川烫、晒干后利于收藏保存。</p>
<figure id="14289249" aria-describedby="caption-14289249" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289249-695150-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289249-695150-450x338.jpg" alt="太阳已升起，渔人还在努力。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289249" class="wp-caption-text">太阳已升起，渔人还在努力。（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日头炎炎，渔民小雀幸，这阵子不妨到海边，或许能遇见董群省的小战车来来回回，亲眼目睹这一场独特的捕捞活动。这可是古岗才懂的人间美味，错过可惜！</p>
<figure id="14289248" aria-describedby="caption-14289248" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289248-695149-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289248-695149-450x338.jpg" alt="若没有捕到鱼获，麦虾就是次要的粮食及收入来源。" width="601" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289248" class="wp-caption-text">若没有捕到鱼获，麦虾就是次要的粮食及收入来源。（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次夏日之行，不仅可以享受清凉的海风，还能体验这独特的麦虾捕捞，快来金门一探究竟吧！</p>
<figure id="14289252" aria-describedby="caption-14289252" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289252-695163-450x601.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289252-695163-450x601.jpg" alt="渔民满载而归。" width="449" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14289252" class="wp-caption-text">渔民满载而归。（古岗社区发展协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
