<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多美食节来了 有机会赢得两人香港之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284625-1-600x400.png" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月06日讯】Summerlicious 是多伦多市一年一度的夏季美食节，2024年的活动从 7 月 5日举办至 7 月 21 日。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14284613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284613-bee2a8df15f1809646e66545c9f04124-450x148.png" alt="" width="605" b="199" /></p>
<p class="p1">来品味美味佳肴吧！超过 200 家多伦多餐厅参与，提供三道式固定价格的菜单。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a loading="lazy" width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src=""></a><a href="https://d2r9hn1obbkp2q.cloudfront.net/R2cxm5Cta"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ypHU5RO1zo8?wmode=transparent&#038;wmode=opaque" allowfullscreen></a>
</div>
<p class="p1">午餐价格从 20 到 55 元不等，晚餐则介于 25 到 75 元之间。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/5/n14284608.md#1/c7fb9c827c4077b46b3b14ed74cb1165" rel="attachment wp-att-14284615"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14284615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284615-c7fb9c827c4077b46b3b14ed74cb1165-450x141.png" alt="" width="450" b="141" /></a> <ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/5/n14284608.md#1/6c731ce19d4d85ebae59133a6d86d30b" rel="attachment wp-att-14284614"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14284614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284614-6c731ce19d4d85ebae59133a6d86d30b-450x177.png" alt="" width="450" b="177" /></a><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/5/n14284608.md#1/4b879e1b0a791180dd9e6369465f4aee" rel="attachment wp-att-14284616"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14284616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284616-4b879e1b0a791180dd9e6369465f4aee-450x181.png" alt="" width="450" b="181" /></a> <ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/5/n14284608.md#1/deee0e253348b6290476914cc6ebb65c" rel="attachment wp-att-14284617"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14284617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284617-deee0e253348b6290476914cc6ebb65c-450x185.png" alt="" width="450" b="185" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">现在就预约您的夏日午餐和晚餐吧。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14284618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284618-4f03a35989abfa14578c8378d3a6ac65-450x189.png" alt="" width="602" b="253" /></p>
<p class="p1">如果你对独特的美食体验感兴趣， 7 月 18 日还有一个由本地和香港米其林星级厨师共同呈献的<ahref="https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/culinary-legends-michelin-experience-from-hong-kong-tickets-928134353837"><strong>八道式晚餐活动</strong></a>，由香港旅游发展局赞助，值得一试！</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14284619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284619-d3b60f90863e0a9df83c96a2dac39095-450x173.png" alt="" width="603" b="232" /></p>
<p class="p1">Summerlicious 赞助商香港旅游发展局及其合作伙伴加拿大航空，将为 Summerlicious 参与者提供赢得一次两人香港之旅的机会。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14284620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284620-07dd0f871fe27a0d8d0505cf9e40a14d-450x206.png" alt="" width="603" b="276" /></p>
<p class="p1">参与者只需在 Summerlicious 期间，扫描任何参与餐厅的二维码并填写表格即可！</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14284621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284621-b9a0cf3318328205556fb1121faf5a48-450x199.png" alt="" width="602" b="266" /></p>
<p class="p1">获奖者将获得两张加拿大航空飞往香港的往返豪华经济舱机票、四晚酒店住宿以及两张 3 天全包景点通行证，尽情体验 20 多种不同的香港风情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14284622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284622" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/5/n14284608.md#1/51747bea58c6fbc2636f5c03b82df08e" rel="attachment wp-att-14284622"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14284622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284622-51747bea58c6fbc2636f5c03b82df08e-450x153.png" alt="" width="603" b="205" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284622" class="wp-caption-text">（所有图片来自市府网站）</figcaption></figure>
