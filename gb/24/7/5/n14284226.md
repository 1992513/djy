<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>布市海外青年文化大使培训?欢迎热血侨青加入</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284235-b4287f9176aba5766a03cd6f84e0d352-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>布里斯本FASCA海外青年文化大使培训招生，欢迎热血侨青加入。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月05日讯】侨务委员会<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年“海外青年文化大使<span lang="EN-US">(FASCA)</span>”<wbr />布里斯本新生培训活动即将于<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日举行，<wbr />昆士兰台湾中心于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日上午举行首次招生说明会，<wbr />出席贵宾有驻布里斯本办事处处长范厚禄伉俪、中心主任欧阳群、<wbr />侨务委员罗富龄、陈秋燕、侨务咨询委员柯文耀、黄素珍、<span lang="EN-US">FASC<wbr />A</span>咨询导师杨淑珠、林季平、卢宛玉、警民联络官黄莉善及岑永雯、<wbr />多位台侨社团会长、家长及学生约<span lang="EN-US">60</span>人到场参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14284234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284234-25532d3e2c4556345f1779753c280f5e-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14284234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284234-25532d3e2c4556345f1779753c280f5e-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284234" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本FASCA海外青年文化大使培训招生，欢迎热血侨青加入。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14284233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284233-d990515954f09ed3e798618648168b13-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14284233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284233-d990515954f09ed3e798618648168b13-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284233" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本FASCA海外青年文化大使培训招生，欢迎热血侨青加入。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>范厚禄表示年轻学子都是未来侨界优秀领导人才，<wbr />鼓励侨青踊跃参与本次培训活动，借此学习领导力、<wbr />体验文化服务并永续传承台湾文化。</p>
<p>欧阳群介绍<span lang="EN-US">FASCA</span>是由侨委会针对海外青年规划之长期培育计划<wbr />，以“领导、文化、服务、传承”为四大培训主轴，<wbr />采学习及体验并行，本年委由已执行<span lang="EN-US">FASCA</span>培训十余年的专业团<wbr />队进行授课，内容丰富扎实，<wbr />完成新生培训后亦可参与后续年度培力课程，促进学员自我成长。</p>
<p>当日特别安排布里斯本<span lang="EN-US">FASCA</span>咨询导师杨淑珠、林季平、<wbr />卢宛玉等<span lang="EN-US">3</span>人，分别上台介绍师资背景以及<span lang="EN-US">FASCA</span>年度培训未来<wbr />规划，包括各类台湾文化课程、社团活动参与、民俗技艺等内容，<wbr />活动下半场另特别邀请昆士兰警务处警民联络官黄莉善及岑永雯分享<wbr />志工服务安全与防诈骗宣导，<wbr />驻布里斯本办事处林鸿尧副组长亦介绍领务服务，<wbr />现场乡亲皆收获满满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14284232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284232-fadd2f9201a103e42d7170cc064074bc-600x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14284232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284232-fadd2f9201a103e42d7170cc064074bc-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284232" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本FASCA海外青年文化大使培训招生，欢迎热血侨青加入。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年“海外青年文化大使<span lang="EN-US">(FASCA)</span>”<wbr />布里斯本新生培训将于<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日在昆士兰台湾中心展开<span lang="EN-US">3</span><wbr />天的专业培训活动，欢迎<span lang="EN-US">12</span>岁到<span lang="EN-US">17</span>岁华裔青少年踊跃报名参加，<wbr />成为<span lang="EN-US">?FASCA</span>团队一员。</p>
