<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2024福隆沙雕季 LINE FRIENDS夏日明星派对登场！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284022-693448-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>福隆海水浴场沙雕季开跑了！（陈秀丽提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新北报导）大家期待已久的福隆国际沙雕艺术季又来啦！今年的沙雕艺术季一直持续到10月13日，主题是“LINE FRIENDS夏日明星派对”。这次活动在新北市贡寮区的福隆海水浴场举办，带来一系列让人目不暇给的黄金沙雕，大家最爱的LINE FRIENDS经典角色如熊大、兔兔和莎莉将变身成可爱又精致的沙雕登场。</p>
<figure id="14284032" aria-describedby="caption-14284032" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284032-693458-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284032-693458-450x338.jpg" alt="沙雕展全景。" width="499" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14284032" class="wp-caption-text">沙雕展全景。（陈秀丽提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14284029" aria-describedby="caption-14284029" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284029-693455-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284029-693455-450x600.jpg" alt="LINE FRIENDS经典角色如熊大、兔兔和莎莉变身。" width="350" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14284029" class="wp-caption-text">LINE FRIENDS经典角色如熊大、兔兔和莎莉变身。（陈秀丽提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的沙雕艺术季不仅有LINE FRIENDS的经典角色，还有风靡全球的韩团BTS携手打造的BT21，也将现身沙滩。这些角色不仅在萤幕上深受大家喜爱，现在更将以沙雕的形式出现在我们面前，让大家可以零距离接触，拍照打卡绝对不容错过！</p>
<figure id="14284027" aria-describedby="caption-14284027" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284027-693453-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284027-693453-450x338.jpg" alt="大家可以零距离接触，拍照打卡绝对不容错过！" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14284027" class="wp-caption-text">大家可以零距离接触，拍照打卡绝对不容错过！（陈秀丽提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，这次沙雕艺术季还引进了LINE FRIENDS的全新系列作品minini。这些小巧又乐观的角色，外型讨喜、性格活泼，绝对会让你爱不释手。而且，你还能看到诙谐幽默的恐龙JOGUMAN，以及最近在韩国爆红的短尾矮袋鼠QUOKKA，他们都将以超萌的沙雕形式现身福隆沙滩。</p>
<figure id="14284026" aria-describedby="caption-14284026" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284026-693452-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284026-693452-450x338.jpg" alt="全新系列作品minini超萌现身。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14284026" class="wp-caption-text">全新系列作品minini超萌现身。（陈秀丽提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>福隆沙滩本身就是一个夏日度假的好去处，沙雕艺术季的到来更是为这里增添了无限乐趣。无论是清晨的微风还是傍晚的夕阳，福隆沙滩都散发着无与伦比的魅力。当你在沙滩上漫步，欣赏这些精美的沙雕作品时，蓝天、白云、金色的沙滩和湛蓝的海水交织在一起，形成一幅绝美的画面。</p>
<figure id="14284024" aria-describedby="caption-14284024" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284024-693450-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284024-693450-450x338.jpg" alt="碧海蓝天景色惬意又有海风吹彿。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14284024" class="wp-caption-text">碧海蓝天景色惬意又有海风吹彿。（陈秀丽提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14284023" aria-describedby="caption-14284023" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284023-693449-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284023-693449-450x338.jpg" alt="福隆沙滩本身就是一个夏日度假的好去处。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14284023" class="wp-caption-text">福隆沙滩本身就是一个夏日度假的好去处。（陈秀丽提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影家陈秀丽也特别推荐这次的沙雕展：“福隆沙雕展真的是太棒了！这么多可爱的沙雕，再加上夏日的海边，真的让人觉得好舒心啊。”这些沙雕作品不仅技术精湛，还充满了创意和童趣，无论是大人还是小朋友，都会爱上这里。</p>
<figure id="14284028" aria-describedby="caption-14284028" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284028-693454-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284028-693454-450x338.jpg" alt="可爱指数爆表。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14284028" class="wp-caption-text">可爱指数爆表。（陈秀丽提供）</figcaption></figure>
