<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>沙美老街金门版小摩洛哥 充满北非风情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284158-693478-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>沙美老街金门版小摩洛哥。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾金门报导）走进金门沙美老街，你会仿佛穿越到中东沙漠中的摩洛哥，这里的颓屋以沙黄色为主，让人感觉置身于异国风情中。沙美摩洛哥是一处位于沙美老街的颓屋，充满了神秘的北非风情，成为许多年轻人来金门必访的热门打卡点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14284283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284283" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284283-f8ef0b9663e02f02d6881099c0c2b396.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14284283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284283-f8ef0b9663e02f02d6881099c0c2b396.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284283" class="wp-caption-text">悠闲漫步，老街与颓屋之间。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>独特景点　沙美摩洛哥</h2>
<p>沙美摩洛哥是一个废弃建筑，经过巧妙改造，成为了沙美老街的亮点之一。走进这里，你会感受到与众不同的异国风情，每一个角落都充满了摄影的魅力。咖啡色的单一色系背景，更是为拍照增添了许多张力，不少人都说只要找对角度，随手一拍就能获得梦幻的照片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14284285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284285" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284285-72410eeadcb4f1e98145821191f0067c.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14284285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284285-72410eeadcb4f1e98145821191f0067c.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284285" class="wp-caption-text">几棵绿色植物点缀其间，更有摩洛哥味道。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>悠闲漫步 老街与颓屋</h2>
<p>漫步在沙美老街，除了沙美摩洛哥之外，还有许多小店和历史悠久的建筑。这些地方不仅保留了当年的繁华，还增添了一份悠闲的步道氛围。你可以一边走，一边寻找那些隐藏在巷弄中的宝藏。当你走累了，不妨在老街的小店坐下来，品尝一下当地的美食，比如馄饨加料、烧饼和牛轭饼，这些都是不容错过的美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14284288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284288" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284288-3b98a2d51d98e6c168da5752173dc62b.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14284288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284288-3b98a2d51d98e6c168da5752173dc62b.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284288" class="wp-caption-text">沙美摩洛哥景点里的烧饼店，酥脆料多。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14284297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284297" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284297-260e6029831cf8c5c09e5647a48ec09f.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14284297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284297-260e6029831cf8c5c09e5647a48ec09f.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284297" class="wp-caption-text">沙美老街街景。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">拍照技巧　梦幻照片</span>如果你想在沙美摩洛哥拍出梦幻般的照片，只需要抓住合适的角度即可。即使你觉得照片不够美，也可以套用滤镜来增加照片的张力。这里的每一个角落都充满了拍摄的魅力，只要你细心观察，就能发现无数的美丽景致。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14284279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14284279" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284279-7437f42141c7555095b86059acc9c2d5.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14284279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284279-7437f42141c7555095b86059acc9c2d5.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14284279" class="wp-caption-text">不同角度的沙美摩洛哥景色。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>位置</h2>
<p>沙美摩洛哥位于金门县金沙镇信义路，这里的景点主要偏向于静态参观，适合那些喜欢悠闲旅游的人。如果你是一位插画爱好者，不妨来这里看看结合金门特色绘制的壁画，这些壁画为老街增添了更多的艺术气息。</p>
<figure style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284277-1d9a0631c7d8aad9d0a23fd9127f7c7d.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14284277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14284277-1d9a0631c7d8aad9d0a23fd9127f7c7d.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="420" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">狭长窄巷更添古味。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
