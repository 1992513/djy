<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨教中心主任潘昭荣调任 纽英崙侨界欢送</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296946-DSC04290-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>7月21日，纽英崙侨界欢送侨教中心主任潘昭荣调任，至孝笃亲公所赠礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年07月23日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨、章佩儿波士顿报导）波士顿侨教中心主任潘昭荣即将于8月返回台湾。7月21日，纽英崙地区侨界代表总计近150人齐聚中华公所，感谢他在过去4年的服务，并欢送他任满荣调。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“在我们这边服务了4年的潘主任，可以说非常热诚地为我们侨胞服务。”中华公所主席雷国辉说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14296939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296939-DJY07686.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296939-DJY07686-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296939" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉赠礼。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296942" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296942-DJY07746.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296942-DJY07746-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296942" class="wp-caption-text">安良工商会赠礼。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296948-DSC04255.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296948-DSC04255-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296948" class="wp-caption-text">近150人齐聚中华公所，欢送潘昭荣主任。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天中午，前来参加欢送餐会的侨胞坐满了中华公所活动厅，参与者包括安良工商会、洪门致公堂、至孝笃亲公所等各传统侨社成员；有台商会、台湾龙舟队、中华专协、校友会、荣光会、国民党、台湾影展等诸多郊区侨团代表；还有华埠退伍军人会、龙舟博物馆等友好团体等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">侨务委员郭竞儒感谢潘主任4年多以来几乎出席每一场侨团活动，给大家送去鼓励。另一位侨务委员陈仕维说，时间过得飞快，而潘主任在短暂的任期内做了非常多的工作，热心为各侨团服务，为此要向他致以感谢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14296949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296949-DSC04276.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296949-DSC04276-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296949" class="wp-caption-text">华埠退伍军人会赠礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296950-DSC04284.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296950-DSC04284-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296950" class="wp-caption-text">妇女新运会赠礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296944-DJY07760.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296944-DJY07760-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296944" class="wp-caption-text">近150人齐聚中华公所，欢送潘昭荣主任。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">餐会期间，不少侨团还准备了赠别礼物，纷纷祝福潘昭荣主任荣调之后仕途顺利。北美舜裔笃亲公所总理陈建立特别作诗一首，称赞潘昭荣的服务：“四年服务纽英崙，广结善缘大家来，战胜新冠莫怀旧，修齐治平细剪裁。潘兄幕后及前台，昭告安排心常开，荣民甘苦信望爱，侨教主任是英才。”</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">其他侨团代表也纷纷借临别赠礼的机会发表感言。黄氏公所主席黄伟健称赞，潘主任这4年给大家留下了非常亲切的形象。中华书法会会长周丽桃说，希望潘主任以后有机会回来看望乡亲，“波士顿就是你在美国第二个家”。在会前、会后，大家也纷纷与潘昭荣合影留念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14296940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296940-DJY07689.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296940-DJY07689-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296940" class="wp-caption-text">陈建立朗读诗歌，欢送潘昭荣主任。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296941-DJY07734.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296941-DJY07734-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296941" class="wp-caption-text">中华书法会赠礼。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296947-DSC04259.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296947-DSC04259-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296947" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙侨界欢送侨教中心主任潘昭荣调任，前排左起：侨务委员陈仕维、郭竞儒、侨教中心主任潘昭荣、中华公所主席雷国辉、中文秘书翁宇才、核数张青梅。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">潘昭荣感谢各位侨领、各位朋友在过去4年的支持。临告别之际，他回顾了每年参加的?诸多活动，从元旦升旗开始，到春宴、牛顿台湾日、罗德岛台湾日、夏季烤肉、健行、侨台商讲座、中秋庆祝，再到双十国庆活动等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“天下无不散的筵席，你们给我的支持和协助，我点滴都记在心头。”潘昭荣说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14296943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296943" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296943-DJY07751.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296943-DJY07751-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296943" class="wp-caption-text">潘昭荣感谢大家4年来的支持和帮助。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296945-DJY07765.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296945-DJY07765-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296945" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们一起切蛋糕。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">参加周日餐会的侨胞人数太多，以致于现场的菜肴一时不够吃，中华公所立刻向餐厅增加订单，才满足大家的需求。潘昭荣还与侨领们一起切蛋糕，共享热闹且欢乐的筵席。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">新届侨教中心主任高家富将于8月中旬抵达波士顿，并拜会当地各侨团。潘昭荣在餐会中说，希望侨胞们持续支持新主任的工作。◇</p>
