<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加拿大最大菲律宾美食节8月回归多伦多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14297914-089a5ef61cba118d5c272818274341c9-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月25日讯】备受期待的“马尼拉美食节”（<span class="s1">Taste Of Manila</span>）庆祝活动又回归多伦多了！今年的活动规模有望比以往更大、更好、更多样化。庆祝活动为期两天，<span class="s1">8</span>月<span class="s1">17</span>日和<span class="s1">18</span>日来多伦多的马尼拉社区吧。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14297908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14297908-451077090_452081391127056_609116803881502890_n-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></p>
<p class="p1">去年，该活动的出席人数超过<span class="s1"> 50 </span>万人，创下了破纪录的新纪录，已成为文化日历上的重要活动。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a loading="lazy" width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src="https://www.ganjing.com/embed/1g480a4gi071BSDdNzbyoVith1qi1c" allowfullscreen></a>
</div>
<p class="p1">今年，由菲律宾艺术家、娱乐和社区协会（<span class="s1">SPARC</span>）主办的马尼拉美食节，将邀请<span class="s1"> 140 </span>家供应商，不仅分享菲律宾美食，还分享无数其他文化风味。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/24/n14297904.md#1/c43248a23d746044dea85b7f0c9849ff" rel="attachment wp-att-14299335"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14299335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14299335-c43248a23d746044dea85b7f0c9849ff-450x803.png" alt="" width="450" b="803" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">今年庆祝活动的主题是<span class="s1">“</span>每个人的节日<span class="s1">”（A Festival for Everyone）</span>，强调包容性和多样性。欢迎并鼓励每个民族参与和宣传自己的国家，无论是通过本国的食品还是产品。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/24/n14297904.md#1/313bf9d006b947ab2759230612fad653" rel="attachment wp-att-14299340"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14299340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14299340-313bf9d006b947ab2759230612fad653-450x661.png" alt="" width="450" b="661" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">来马尼拉美食节吧，准备好度过一个充满娱乐的难忘周末！</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14299338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14299338-e8241deb61bb8927f7741241d939e825-450x803.png" alt="" width="450" b="803" /></p>
<h1><strong>2024年马尼拉美食节</strong></h1>
<p class="p1"><strong>时间：</strong></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">8</span>月<span class="s1">17</span>日（星期六），上午<span class="s1"> 9 </span>点至晚上<span class="s1"> 11 </span>点</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">8</span>月<span class="s1">18</span>日（星期日），上午<span class="s1"> 9 </span>点至下午<span class="s1"> 6 </span>点</p>
<p class="p1"><strong>地点：</strong></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s2">多伦多小马尼拉社区（</span>Wilson Ave<span class="s2">夹</span>Bathurst St, Toronto,ON<span class="s2">）</span></p>
<p class="p1">获取更多信息，请前往<ahref="https://www.sparccanada.ca/events"><strong>主办方网站</strong></a></p>
