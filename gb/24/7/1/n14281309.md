<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园“义捐秀发美丽重生”捐发 伴癌友重拾自信</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281310-692363-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“义捐秀发 美丽重生”捐发活动。（桃园市府新闻处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月01日讯】（大纪元记者陈建霖台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政出席“义捐秀发 美丽重生”捐发活动。张善政表示，活动今（113）年已迈入第17年，所有捐发将透过长庚医院制成假发，提供给有需要的癌症病友使用，陪伴他们更有自信地回归社会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281311-692364-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281311-692364-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281311" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政与捐发者开心合影。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政指出，近年癌症确诊人数持续攀升，且治疗过程多会经历化疗及掉发，难免影响其社交信心，感谢所有捐发者响应活动，捐出平时悉心照顾的秀发，给予癌友充分支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281312" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281312-692365-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281312-692365-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281312" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政感谢民众发挥爱心响应捐发。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>立法委员暨桃园市家长会长协会理事长万美玲表示，约有320位捐发者至现场响应，另有近200位捐发者已事先剪发完成义捐；捐发者不分性别、年龄，甚至已多次响应，令人十分感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281313" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281313-692366-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281313-692366-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281313" class="wp-caption-text">立委暨桃园市家长会长协会理事长万美玲致词。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>万美玲感谢长庚医院为癌症病友制作假发，以及所有协助活动的美发师、设计师及志工伙伴等，陪伴癌友们在复健路上更加顺遂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281314" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281314-692367-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281314-692367-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281314" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政与来宾合影。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林口长庚纪念医院副院长赖琼慧表示，感谢万美玲委员及桃园市儿童少年保护协会发起此项极具意义的捐发活动，长庚医院体系照护全台约四分之一的癌症病患，并成立癌症资源中心提供癌友充分资源，包含免费秀发、头巾帽及气切领巾等，协助癌友度过难关，迈向康复之路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14281315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14281315" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281315-692368-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14281315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281315-692368-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14281315" class="wp-caption-text">许多孩童响应捐发活动。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局表示，桃园市今年截至6月15日共完成22万6,601人次的五大癌症筛检，包含肺癌、子宫颈癌、乳癌、大肠癌及口腔癌，其中1,654人确诊，平均每筛检137位即找出1位癌前病变或癌症个案，呼吁市民朋友主动受检，透过早期治疗避免严重个案发生，为自身健康把关。◇</p>
