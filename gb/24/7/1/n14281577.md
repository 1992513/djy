<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金门版摩西分海 建功屿夏季正午游客多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14281578-692492-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>金门版摩西分海。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬金门报导）位于金门县金城南门的浯江溪口，有一个小小的秘境——建功屿。这座面积约500平方公尺的小岛，因其在金门本岛与烈屿之间的重要地理位置，被赋予“建功砥柱”的美名。当潮水退去时，石板道会逐渐露出，仿佛一条通往小岛的神秘之路，吸引着无数游客前来探险。7月1日正午太阳晒的让人发晕，游客反而往海里走去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282459" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282459-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282459-1-450x540.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282459" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日退潮时间，游客能走的时间，大约是正午后，太阳最晒的时候。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>潮间带生物　欣赏大自然美景</h2>
<p>当你沿着石板道走向建功屿，旧的军事基地时，两旁潮间带的丰富生物让人目不暇给。大小朋友们蹲在石板道边，兴奋地观察螃蟹、招潮蟹和弹涂鱼的动作。招潮蟹挥舞着钳子，让大家忘了退潮时，已经是正中午了，顾不得大太阳，大小朋友专注的想拍它们而已。（<strong>延伸阅读：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/5/n14284157.md#1?utm_term=djy_a_rec_in" target="_blank" rel="noopener">沙美老街金门版小摩洛哥 充满北非风情</a></span></strong>）</p>
<h2>艺术地景“牡蛎人”　不同时间拍的效果也不同</h2>
<p>建功屿的另一个亮点，是由芬兰建筑师兼艺术家Marco Casagrande为2013年金门艺术节创作的地景艺术——牡蛎人（Oystermen）。这四个巨大的钢铁雕塑，灵感来自于军用反登陆桩和铁条，身上配有太阳能板。夜晚来临时，牡蛎人的斗笠会发出迷人的蓝光，仿佛海上的守护者，静静地注视着来往的游客。如果时间对的话，还可以拍夕阳下的“牡蛎人”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282460-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282460-2-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282460" class="wp-caption-text">2013年金门艺术节创作的地景艺术——牡蛎人（Oystermen）。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>碉堡与历史　探索金门的过往</h2>
<p>从起点一直走在石板路上，一路向前，终点站就是旧的建功屿军事基地，你还可以参观面向金门大桥的郑成功雕像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282461" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282461-3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282461-3-450x540.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282461" class="wp-caption-text">面向金门大桥的郑成功雕像。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金门大学观光管理学系兼任讲师王芳堃表示：“登上碉堡，回望金门本岛，仿佛时光倒流，让人感受到这片土地上的风云变幻。这些军事建筑，不仅是金门历史的一部分，也是对当年郑成功和金门人民不屈精神的致敬，这里的云彩也特别漂亮壮观。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282462" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282462-4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282462-4-450x540.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282462" class="wp-caption-text">建功屿上军事遗迹。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282463" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282463-5.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282463-5-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282463" class="wp-caption-text">当潮水退去时，石板道会逐渐露出，仿佛一条通往小岛的神秘之路。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>站上雕堡的最高处，可以回眺金门本岛、金门大桥，还有远方的厦门，视野相当辽阔。（<strong>延伸阅读：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/3/n14282942.md#1?utm_term=djy_a_rec_in" target="_blank" rel="noopener">金门翟山坑道 探索最美洞穴水上步道</a></span></strong>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282464-6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282464-6-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282464" class="wp-caption-text">前景是蚵田，中景是金门大桥，远方是厦门。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282465" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282465-7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282465-7-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282465" class="wp-caption-text">建功屿四面都有不同的风景。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>很晒但是一趟轻松愉快的旅程</h2>
<p>记得先导航到延平郡王祠，将车停在那边的停车场，然后步行至建功屿的沙滩。起点处设有洗手间和洗脚池，非常方便。由于石板道可能泥泞且有不少石蚵和藤壶，建议穿着防滑、防晒且不怕脏的鞋子，避免穿拖鞋。帽子的话，风大时就戴不住了，雨伞也拿不了。此外，了解潮汐时间也是必须的，这样才能确保你能顺利在退潮时走到小岛，并且安全返回。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282466" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282466-8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282466-8-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282466" class="wp-caption-text">石柱上布满了牡蛎。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282467" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282467-9.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282467-9-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282467" class="wp-caption-text">中午刚退潮，石板上有缝隙，鞋子脏的话，可以来洗脚池洗哦。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在建功屿军事雕堡大门前，也有水龙头可以洗手，入园后也有厕所，登高望远欣赏金门大桥之外，还可以看到一大片牡蛎养殖区，喜欢拍照的朋友，怎会错过呢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282468" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282468-10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282468-10-450x540.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282468" class="wp-caption-text">建功屿旧军事用地。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>延平郡王祠　建功屿停车场旁的景点</h2>
<p>如果你对历史感兴趣，不妨在前往建功屿之前，先造访一下延平郡王祠。这座仿古宫殿建筑，占地约300平方公尺，位于金城西南郊。这里曾是郑成功反清复明的基地，视野开阔，象征着毋忘大陆的精神。在这里，你可以感受到浓厚的历史氛围，并了解郑成功当年在金门的丰功伟绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282472" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282472-14.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282472-14-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282472" class="wp-caption-text">延平郡王祠。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
