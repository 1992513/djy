<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金门翟山坑道 探索最美洞穴水上步道</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282943-693071-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>坑道内光线充足，非常凉爽。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月03日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬金门报导）翟山坑道是金门金城乡最具魅力的景点之一，也是每位游客来到金门必游之地。这里曾经是战争时期的小艇补给通道，现已成为一处神秘而迷人的旅游景点。37度酷夏最适合去的地方，因为坑道里就像大冰箱一样，走着走着还会觉得想穿外套，重点是连需要轮椅的游客，都能入内欣赏国军打造的巨型建筑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282996" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282996-24029_1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282996-24029_1-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282996" class="wp-caption-text">广场入口大大的“毋忘在莒”四个字。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>翟山坑道景点特色</h2>
<p>音乐会：每年秋季举办，体验独特的音乐盛宴。</p>
<p>同时它也是金门拍照打卡的热点之一：坑道内的倒影，壮观美丽，让人忍不住快门一直按。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282992" style="width: 481px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282992-23384_1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282992-23384_1-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="481" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282992" class="wp-caption-text">坑道的倒影相当漂亮。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>历史深厚：曾经是战时重要的补给通道</h2>
<p>翟山坑道原名大帽山坑道，于1963年为应对战争需求而开挖。这项庞大的工程动员了大量人力，历经五年才得以完工。坑道分为水道与坑道两部分，设计之初是为了容纳小艇运送补给品，坑道内还设有码头和兵舍。这里可谓是金门岛上一座独特的“地下金门”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282988" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282988-23392_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282988-23392_0-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282988" class="wp-caption-text">进入幽暗的翟山坑道，仿佛步入一个时光隧道。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>结构庞大：坑道内部宽阔可以容纳多艘小艇</h2>
<p>走下阶梯，进入幽暗的翟山坑道，仿佛步入了一个时光隧道。坑道内部宽阔而长，结构精密，让人不禁惊叹于当年军事建设的鬼斧神工。随着海水在坑道尽头微微晃动的倒影，游客们不仅能感受到一丝凉意，更能想像当年军队在这里忙碌的景象。这让我想起爸爸二十几岁在金门当兵退伍的那张照片，帅气十足让我记忆深刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282990" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282990-23412_1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282990-23412_1-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282990" class="wp-caption-text">可谓金门岛上一座独特的“地下金门”。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导游蔡小林说：“坑道里湿气重，国军晚上睡在坑道入口处，只有长官才能睡在现在的纪念品贩售部里面，起床后再把棉被拿出去晒干，晚上继续用。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282989" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282989-23429_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282989-23429_0-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282989" class="wp-caption-text">坑道入口处地上湿冷，它曾是士兵们睡觉的地方。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>坑道内可以容纳42艘小艇进出，当年是为了因应八二三炮战期间的需求而设计。现在，这里成为一处凉快而神秘的探险之地，每年秋季还会举办翟山坑道音乐会，吸引了大量游客前来欣赏音乐与历史的完美结合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282991" style="width: 481px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282991-23404_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282991-23404_0-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="481" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282991" class="wp-caption-text">每年秋季会举办翟山坑道音乐会。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>户外展示与必打卡景点</h2>
<p>走出坑道，广场上陈列着退役的海军船舰和各式防空武器，这些都是曾经的军事记忆，为游客提供了另一层次的历史体验。广场上的大大“毋忘在莒”四字，是金门著名的拍照打卡点，每位游客都不会错过这里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282995" style="width: 482px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282995-24039_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282995-24039_0-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="482" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282995" class="wp-caption-text">户外广场展示退役的海军舰艇。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14283001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283001" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283001-23268_1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283001-23268_1-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283001" class="wp-caption-text">翟山坑道户外景色。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>免费开放：全区免门票非常适合自由行游客</h2>
<p>翟山坑道全区免费开放，并且配备有完善的无障碍设施，包括无障碍厕所、坡道和车位，非常适合家庭出游或行动不便的朋友们。建议游玩时间为0.5至1小时，让你充分体验英勇国军的历史。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282994" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282994-23282_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282994-23282_0-600x720.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="541" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282994" class="wp-caption-text">坑道内还有无障碍设施。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每日固定有六场解说导览，分别在09:00、10:00、11:00、14:00、15:00、16:00举行。</p>
<p>参加解说可以更深入了解翟山坑道的历史背景与建造故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282993" style="width: 479px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282993-23308_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14282993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282993-23308_0-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="479" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282993" class="wp-caption-text">有专人导览解说，可以深入了解坑道的历史。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>如何到达</h2>
<p>翟山坑道位于金门岛上，从金门市区出发，乘坐公共交通工具或自驾车均可轻松抵达。建议提前查询公共交通班次或租借自驾车，以便更灵活地安排你的行程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283002" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283002-23275_0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283002-23275_0-600x720.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283002" class="wp-caption-text">建议提前查询公车班次或租借自驾车，以便灵活安排行程。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>注意事项</strong></h2>
<p>参观时间：每日09:00至17:00，请提前安排好时间。</p>
<p>安全注意：坑道内光线较暗和湿，请注意脚下安全。</p>
<p>环保出行：游览时请勿随意丢弃垃圾，保护环境。</p>
