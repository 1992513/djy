<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园市各机关学校绩优事务人员表扬 发挥影响力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282711-692907-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政期许得奖同仁发挥正面影响力激励其他同仁。（桃园市府新闻处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月03日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政3日上午主持市政会议，表扬桃园市112年各机关学校绩优事务人员。张善政表示，感谢5位绩优事务人员的努力付出，即使在机关学校服务薪资不若私人企业优渥，仍愿意在岗位上发挥所长，展现杰出工作成果，令人敬佩；期许得奖同仁继续在工作上百尺竿头、更进一步，也希望激励其他同仁，发挥正面影响力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282715-692912-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282715-692912-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282715" class="wp-caption-text">杨梅国中工友郑紫绮在岗位上表现杰出。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秘书处表示，桃园市各机关学校工友365人、技工69人及驾驶14人，为表扬敬业乐群、奉公守法及热心服务的事务同仁，由各机关学校推荐优秀同仁参加选拔，并按各类人员百分之一比例的原则进行遴选，以作为桃园市各机关学校事务人员的表率。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14282713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282713" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282713-692910-450x255.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282713-692910-450x255.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="255" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282713" class="wp-caption-text">新荣国小工友刘碧霞获得绩优人员的殊荣。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282714" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282714-692911-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282714-692911-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282714" class="wp-caption-text">中坜高商技工翁昌明为学校奉献心力获得表扬。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14282712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14282712" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282712-692908-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14282712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14282712-692908-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14282712" class="wp-caption-text">龙潭区公所工友张仕谦为机关的付出获得表扬。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市112年各机关学校绩优事务人员遴选出工友4名、技工1名，分别为龙潭区公所工友张仕谦、新荣国小工友刘碧霞、龟山区公所工友黄郁芳、杨梅国中工友郑紫绮，及中坜高商技工翁昌明等5位，各颁发奖座1座及奖金新台币5,000元，并给予公假5天，以资鼓励。◇</p>
