<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花莲美式猎人学校体验 让教育更多元</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283722-693415-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>水琏部落族人介绍传统弓箭。（芭奈法老提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月04日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）花莲县一群充满热情和专业的教育工作者齐聚一堂，共同参与了一场别开生面的教育增能与服务活动。这次的活动由花莲县原住民族教育辅导团主办，地点选在寿丰乡水琏部落猎人学校，目的是通过部落在地文化技艺的学习体验，提升教师们的多元教育能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283729" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283729-693422.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283729-693422.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283729" class="wp-caption-text">这次活动让每一位参加的教育工作者都受益匪浅。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>重新学习 为了下一代</strong></p>
<p>来自各国小的校长、主任和老师们在这次活动中放下了平时的身份，转变成为学生。他们深知，教育不仅仅是课本上的知识，更需要多元文化的熏陶和实践经验的积累。因此，他们主动归零学习，融入不同的职场文化教育知识，为的是让教育内容更丰富，模式更多元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283735" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283735-693428.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283735-693428.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283735" class="wp-caption-text">校长、主任和老师们在这次活动中放下了平时的身份，转变成为学生。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>猎人学校理事长芭奈法老：“身为猎人学校的团员，我们感到非常光荣能够分享部落生活的文化知识和技艺。”我们深知，这些传承自阿美族人对山与海共存的智慧，不仅仅是文化的宝藏，更是教育的宝贵资源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283736" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283736-693429.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283736-693429.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283736" class="wp-caption-text">让人食指大动的阿美族美食佳肴。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>阿美族的美式生活体验</strong></p>
<p>这次活动的亮点之一是阿美族的美式走读生活体验。活动内容丰富多彩，芭奈法老：“包括部落故事分享、阿里凤凤编织、浪花蟹捕捉、撒网技巧传授、传统射箭训练、植物导览、植物编织、美食飨宴、容器制作、编绳技艺、达路岸制作、传统生火技巧展示，以及植物应用童玩鸟鸣体验等。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283732" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283732-693425.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283732-693425.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="606" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283732" class="wp-caption-text">花莲美式猎人学校体验。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>部落故事与阿里凤凤</strong></p>
<p>活动开始，芭奈法老：“我们先从部落故事说起。这些故事讲述了阿美族人如何在自然中生存和繁衍，如何与自然和谐共处。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283734-693427.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283734-693427.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283734" class="wp-caption-text">体验走简易的绳索吊桥。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着，阿里凤凤编织，这是阿美族的重要传统技艺，芭奈法老：“象征着部落人民对自然的敬畏和感恩及爱的表现，老人家说：没有一定的耐心和爱心，编一编会迷路，找不到回家的路。也是妻子送给上山打猎的先生，做的爱心便当。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283739" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283739-693432.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283739-693432.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283739" class="wp-caption-text">阿里凤凤编织，这是阿美族的重要传统技艺。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>老师们在编织中体验到了阿美族人爱的连结和团结与协作精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283726" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283726-693419.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283726-693419.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283726" class="wp-caption-text">学员们互相学习互相观摩。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>浪花蟹捕捉与撒网</strong></p>
<p>接下来的浪花蟹捕捉和撒网技巧传授让老师们大开眼界。这些技巧是阿美族人在海边生活的重要技能。老师们兴致勃勃地学习，并亲自尝试捕捉浪花蟹和撒网，体验到其中的乐趣和挑战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283731" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283731-693424.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283731-693424.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283731" class="wp-caption-text">教育不仅仅是课本上的知识，更需要多元文化的熏陶。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14283727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283727" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283727-693420.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283727-693420.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283727" class="wp-caption-text">水琏部落的海滩，是花莲海岸线少有，能亲海的地点。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>传统射箭与植物导览</strong></p>
<p>传统射箭训练则让老师们感受到阿美族人的勇敢和智慧。每一支箭都蕴含着射手的专注和技巧。植物导览和植物编织环节，老师们学习了如何辨识和利用当地的植物，体验了将植物编织成各种实用物品的乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283723" style="width: 596px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283723-693416.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283723-693416.jpg" alt="" width="596" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283723" class="wp-caption-text">游客射箭体验。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>美食飨宴与容器制作</strong></p>
<p>美食飨宴则是活动的另一个高潮。老师们品尝到了阿美族的特色美食，这些美食不仅美味，更包含了阿美族人的生活智慧。植物叶子用来当容器的制作，和编绳技艺展示，老师们亲手制作了各种实用的生活用品，感受到传统技艺的魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283724" style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283724-693417.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283724-693417.jpg" alt="" width="595" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283724" class="wp-caption-text">阿美族人的原民料理，以树叶当餐盘。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>达路岸制作与传统生火</strong></p>
<p>达路岸制作和传统生火技巧展示，芭奈法老：“不论是阿美族年龄阶层的聚会所，或是猎人外出狩猎休息的地方，都称为‘达路岸’，让老师们了解了阿美族人在自然环境中的生存智慧。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283737" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283737-693430.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283737-693430.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283737" class="wp-caption-text">了解阿美族人在自然环境中的生存智慧。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>特别是传统生火技巧，让大家体会到在没有现代工具的情况下，如何利用自然资源生火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283738" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283738-693431.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283738-693431.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="458" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283738" class="wp-caption-text">传统生火技巧展示。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>植物鸟鸣的辨识</strong></p>
<p>最后，植物鸟鸣的辨识环节，老师们学习了如何根据植物的特性，和鸟鸣声音来辨识不同的植物和鸟类，这些知识在日常生活中同样实用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14283728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14283728" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283728-693421.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14283728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14283728-693421.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14283728" class="wp-caption-text">认识植物、采集植物。（芭奈法老提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次活动让每一位参加的教育工作者都受益匪浅，大家不仅学到了丰富的部落文化知识和技艺，更深刻认识到教育的多样性和包容性的重要性。老师们在活动中展现出的学习热情和团队合作精神，深受感动同时也收获满满。◇</p>
