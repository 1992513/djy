<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>甲辰正科三大迎王论坛暨摄影木船展　在潮州上场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285148-693770-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>烧王船仪式。（周永田提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月06日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）屏东县潮州镇公所于6日举办一场盛大的文化活动，名为“2024甲辰正科三大迎王论坛暨摄影木船展”。这一活动以文化传承和纪录为宗旨，涵盖屏东县内最具代表性的三大宗教盛事：东港、小琉球及南州的迎王祭典。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14285147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285147" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285147-693769.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285147-693769.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285147" class="wp-caption-text">屏东迎王祭典。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14285149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285149" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285149-693771.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285149-693771.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285149" class="wp-caption-text">东隆宫前的迎王祭典。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14285154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285154" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285154-693776.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285154-693776.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285154" class="wp-caption-text">迎王古礼祭仪。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次活动特别征集全国摄影作品，共有超过四百件作品参赛，最终遴选出150件优秀作品参展。展览于6日起8月25日在潮州镇日式历史建筑文化园区举行。这次的摄影展由郭宏德老师和林君谕老师共同策展，希望透过摄影作品，让更多人认识这些传统祭典文化及木作工艺彩绘之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14285151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285151" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285151-693773.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285151-693773.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285151" class="wp-caption-text">选在港口边举办，场面壮观严肃。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影家周永田此次入选了22张作品，他兴奋地表示：“很高兴有政府机关团体透过甄选的方式开放广大摄友参加，线上投稿免洗照片，不用排名争论高低，入选的都是优良作品，让我可以将之前二科的迎王祭典拍摄的影像，可以从电脑中跳出来与大众分享，展出质感也很值得称许，很喜欢这操作模式。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14285152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285152" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285152-693774.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285152-693774.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285152" class="wp-caption-text">摄影家周永田的作品，入选２２张。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次活动不仅是展示优秀的摄影作品，更是一个了解屏东县丰富文化和历史的机会。参观者可以透过这些精彩的影像，仿佛身历其境地感受迎王祭典的热闹与庄严。潮州镇公所表示，此次摄影展和木船展希望让更多人了解，和欣赏这些传统文化，并将这些瞬间的美好转化为永恒的纪录。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14285150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285150" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285150-693772.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285150-693772.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285150" class="wp-caption-text">每个仪式和服装，都遵循传统仪式。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动期间，潮州镇日式历史建筑文化园区，将成为文化爱好者和摄影爱好者的聚集地。无论是本地居民还是外地旅客，都可以在这里感受到浓厚的文化氛围，并透过这些摄影作品，更深入地了解屏东县的历史和文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14285153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285153" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285153-693775.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285153-693775.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285153" class="wp-caption-text">屏东仍保留传统的迎王祭仪。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14285155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285155" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285155-693777.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285155-693777.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285155" class="wp-caption-text">工作人员挂上传统灯笼。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>值得一提的是，2024年甲辰正科的迎王祭典将于9月正式登场，这次的摄影展和木船展可谓是为迎王祭典预热。潮州镇公所热情邀请大家前来参观，共同感受这一文化盛事的魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14285156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285156" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285156-693778.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285156-693778.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285156" class="wp-caption-text">潮州镇公所表示，此次摄影展和木船展，希望让更多人了解和欣赏这些传统文化。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14285157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14285157" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285157-693779.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14285157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14285157-693779.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14285157" class="wp-caption-text">这次的摄影展由郭宏德老师和林君谕老师共同策展。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
