<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自己在阳台种贝比生菜 现采鲜吃乐趣多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298256-20240725_shouhui_baby_leaf.01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>适合初学者种植的贝比生菜。（图／苹果屋提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：喜欢吃沙拉，不妨在自家阳台种贝比生菜，随时可以吃到新鲜美味又安全的生菜，想吃什么就种什么，还可以感受“现采鲜吃”的乐趣，鲜菜种在盆栽里，不用一亩田，也不须太多空间，就能享有田园乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14298229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298229" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298229-P.44-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14298229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298229-P.44-1-450x734.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="734" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298229" class="wp-caption-text">（图／苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>适合初学者的蔬菜</h3>
<p>贝比生菜除了盛夏、严冬之外，几乎一整年都可栽种，而且生长期很短，非常适合初学者栽种。若盆栽有一～二个月的空窗期，不妨尝试种看看贝比生菜。</p>
<h2>●播种</h2>
<h3>条播播种</h3>
<p>挖出深约0.5cm的圆形浅沟，圆与圆的间隔距离3～4cm。种子不重叠地散播在土壤中，补土后轻压，再浇满水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14298231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298231-P.44-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14298231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298231-P.44-3-600x812.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="812" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298231" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>（图／苹果屋提供）</strong></span></figcaption></figure>
<h3>浅播</h3>
<p>由于综合种子内含需要阳光才会发芽的好光性种子，在播种时挖出浅浅的沟，也铺上薄薄一层土即可。浇水时也要十分轻柔，否则水压会把种子冲走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14298232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298232-P.44-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14298232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298232-P.44-4-600x553.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="553" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298232" class="wp-caption-text">（图／苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>●追肥</h2>
<p>播种后过一个月左右，将肥料施于植株周围，稍微拨松土壤表面使两者混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14298233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298233-P.45-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14298233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298233-P.45-1-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298233" class="wp-caption-text">（图／苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>●采收</h3>
<h3>从植株底部剪下</h3>
<p>叶片长至10～15cm时，底部留下2～3cm的长度，再用剪刀采收下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14298234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298234-P.45-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14298234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298234-P.45-2-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298234" class="wp-caption-text">（图／苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>POINT</strong></span></h3>
<h3>留下植株底部一小部分</h3>
<p>收割重点在于要切除比叶片的分歧点（生长点）更上面的部分。如此一来，留下的茎会再继续生长，便可采收多次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14298235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298235-P.45-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14298235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298235-P.45-3-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298235" class="wp-caption-text">（图／苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>施肥</h3>
<p>采收后再将肥料施于植株周围，稍微拨松土壤表面使两者混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14298236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298236-P.45-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14298236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298236-P.45-4-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298236" class="wp-caption-text">（图／苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>再次采收</h3>
<p>待叶片再次长到10～15cm，一样留下2～3cm的长度，再用剪刀剪下来。采收后再施肥，之后便能以同样的方式重复采收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14298237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298237" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298237-P.45-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14298237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298237-P.45-5-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298237" class="wp-caption-text">（图／苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
