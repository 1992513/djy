<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美南区台湾民俗文化教师培训圆满成功</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296377-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>活动始业式大合照，王盈蓉（前排右六）、黄依莉（前排右七）、罗兆渝（前排右五）、冉茂萍（中排左二）与文化培训学员合影。（图/侨教中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年07月23日讯】（大纪元讯）7月18日至19日休士顿侨教中心举办“台湾民俗文化教师培训”活动，由来自台湾2位支援“113年海外侨民青少年夏令营”教学讲座冉茂萍及罗兆渝老师分别教授功夫扇、团扇、太极剑和彩巾课程，吸引来自休斯顿、达拉斯、奥斯汀、艾尔帕索侨校TCML及主流学校教师约24人参加，侨教中心主任王盈蓉及副主任黄依莉亦出席与会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14296379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296379-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296379-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296379" class="wp-caption-text">冉茂萍讲授功夫扇。（图/侨教中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296380" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296380-04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296380-04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296380" class="wp-caption-text">罗兆渝讲授团扇。（图/侨教中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>王盈蓉感谢参加培训教师提升文化教学能力，鼓励担任台湾文化导览志工及于台湾传统周与国庆文化访问团访演期间一同设摊展演，推广台湾传统文化。她提到文化种子教师的培训，除了可透过参加在地研习活动外，还可参加侨委会补助的文化种子教师回台培训计划。此外，这是首次为了海外侨民青少年夏令营而来的民俗舞蹈和体育老师在中心举办文化种子教师培训营，盼大家收获满溢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14296378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296378-02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296378-02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296378" class="wp-caption-text">冉茂萍（最右侧）教导学员功夫扇之招式。（图/侨教中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296381" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296381-05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296381" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296381-05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296381" class="wp-caption-text">学员们开心练习团扇舞。（图/侨教中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296382" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296382" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296382-06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296382-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296382" class="wp-caption-text">学员们专心练习太极剑。（图/侨教中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动一开始，冉茂萍带领学员热身练习功夫扇和太极剑招式，待熟悉步伐和架式后，便播放音乐让学员动作更为流畅优美，她说扇子自古用来祭天和祭神，接着转变为与人对话、遮阳挡风和纳凉的工具，学员听闻皆感到有趣。随后由罗兆渝解释团扇又称合欢扇，圆形的形状代表着圆圆满满，具对新婚男女祝福之意，接着引导学员运用彩巾轻柔挥动，展现彩巾在手上所呈现的不同美感，从舞蹈中开发想像力，同时感受传统艺术文化的趣味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14296383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296383" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296383-07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296383-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296383" class="wp-caption-text">罗兆渝讲授彩巾。（图/侨教中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14296384" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14296384" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296384-08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14296384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296384-08-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14296384" class="wp-caption-text">成果展结业大合照，王盈蓉（中排右五）、黄依莉（中排左二）、罗兆渝（前排右四）、冉茂萍（前排右五）与文化培训学员合影。（图/侨教中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动除了让侨校、主流学校教师及FASCA学员共同研习武术及民俗舞蹈外，还促进美南地区文化种子教师彼此间交流。学员表示本次文化节庆课程可增添侨校学生学习华语文的动力，学习团扇舞蹈和太极剑则对于向当地主流人士推展台湾文化颇有助益。活动最后学员热烈地讨论本次所学及分享自身在校教学经验，期望将台湾及中华文化博大精深之处广传至世界各地。</p>
