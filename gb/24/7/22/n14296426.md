<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加国Costco和沃尔玛多款维生素产品召回</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14296427-Supermarket-496x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为Costco和沃尔玛门店资料照。（Shutterstock/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月23日讯】（大纪元记者王兰多伦多报导）由于可能存在金属纤维，多种维生素（包括Costco和沃尔玛出售的一些维生素）正在被召回。</p>
<p>周五，加拿大卫生部发布了三份召回通知，重点说明哪些产品受到影响。</p>
<p>这些品牌包括在Costco有售的Kirkland Signature品牌，以及在沃尔玛和Shoppers Drug Mart等全国多家零售商有售的Webber Naturals等。</p>
<h2>此次召回的产品包括以下维生素：</h2>
<p>●Kirkland Signature – B100 Complex Timed Release<br />
●Life – Vitamin B Complex with Vitamin C<br />
●Option + &amp; Life – Prenatal Multi-vitamins 100 Tablets with Folic Acid<br />
●Option + &amp; Life – Spectrum Prenatal Postpartum<br />
●Option+ &amp; Life – Multivitamins &amp; Minerals Women<br />
●Option+ &amp; Life – Spectrum Women 22 Vitamins &amp; Minerals plus Lutein<br />
●Option+ &amp; Life – Lycopene<br />
●Webber Naturals – B50 Complex Timed Release<br />
●Webber Naturals – Most Complete Multi Womens 50+ One Per Day<br />
●Webber Naturals – Most Complete Multi Mens 50+ One Per Day<br />
●Webber Naturals – Vitamin B50 Complex<br />
●Wellness by London Drugs, Option+ &amp; Life – Multi Women/Femmes 50+<br />
with Lycopene and Lutein Multivitamin and Mineral<br />
●Wellness by London Drugs, Option+ &amp; Life – Multivitamin &amp; Minerals<br />
●Women 50+<br />
●Wellness by London Drugs, Option+ &amp; Life – 50+ SPECTRUM Women/Femmes 50+ 23 Vitamins &amp; Minerals plus Lutein<br />
●Wellness by London Drugs, Option+ &amp; Life – Lycopene<br />
●Wellquest, Equate, Option+, Wellness by London Drugs &amp; Life – Vitamin B100 Complex, Timed Release<br />
●Natural Factors – Hi Potency B Complex<br />
●Natural Factors – RevitalX<br />
●Natural Factors – Super Multi? IRON FREE<br />
●VegiDay Vegan Organic ALL IN ONE – VegiDay Vegan Organic ALL IN ONE</p>
<p>加拿大卫生部表示，如果你确认所购买的产品受到召回的影响，应该停止使用并咨询你的医疗保健提供者，检查是否有任何健康问题。</p>
<p>有疑虑的客户可以联系召回公司。任何副作用或保健产品安全投诉都应报告给加拿大卫生部。</p>
