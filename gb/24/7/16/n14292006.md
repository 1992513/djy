<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>捕捉沙滩上的保龄球：滨刺麦</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292007-696115-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>捕捉沙滩上的保龄球：线西海边的滨刺麦。（黄连兴提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾彰化报导）在台湾的沙滩上，有一种神奇的植物——滨刺麦，仿佛沙滩上的保龄球。这种植物学名为 Spinifex littoreus，属于禾本科，雌雄异株。滨刺麦耐盐，常生长在西海岸的河口砂地，澎湖也能见到它的身影。滨刺麦的颖果球形长刺状，随风滚动，寻找新的成长地，为沙滩增添了不少乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14292013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14292013" style="width: 395px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292013-696124-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14292013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292013-696124-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="395" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14292013" class="wp-caption-text">展现了滨刺麦的自然之美。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄连兴是位热爱滨刺麦的摄影师，平时忙于装潢工作，只有周末才有空拍照。他拍照已有四、五年，曾在台中摄影学院学过摄影，每年这个季节他都会来彰化线西的海边，在这里捕捉滨刺麦的美丽瞬间。他说：“这几天都有午后雷阵雨，雷阵雨过后的夕阳特别漂亮！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14292014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14292014" style="width: 578px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292014-696125-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14292014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292014-696125-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="578" b="385" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14292014" class="wp-caption-text">蚵田、风车、滨刺麦呈现线西海边之美。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>滨刺麦的拍摄秘诀之一是选择适合的时间。“七月是拍滨刺麦最好的时间点，因为它有一点绿又有一点黄，而且滨刺麦已经开始掉出来了。”黄连兴介绍说，当滨刺麦成熟时，它会掉出来，随风滚动在沙滩上，非常美丽。他接着说：“如果有大一点的风，滨刺麦就会在沙滩上滚动啦，所以有人称它‘沙滩上的保龄球’。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14292009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14292009" style="width: 534px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292009-696117-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14292009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292009-696117-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="534" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14292009" class="wp-caption-text">黄连兴：“白色衣服很百搭，因为滨刺麦的颜色比较偏黄。”（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>拍摄滨刺麦时，黄连兴也有一些小技巧。他说“模特儿穿白色衣服很百搭，因为滨刺麦的颜色比较偏黄。”他还会稍微调整色调，让滨刺麦的颜色更黄一点。黄连兴：“我用Canon的5D4相机，配上24-70mm和70-200mm两个镜头，再加上离机闪灯，把夕阳的细节拍得更好看。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14292009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14292009" style="width: 522px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292009-696117-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14292009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292009-696117-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="522" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14292009" class="wp-caption-text">黄连兴：“白色衣服很百搭，因为滨刺麦的颜色比较偏黄。”（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄连兴最喜欢傍晚时分拍滨刺麦，虽然他不太喜欢傍晚出门，因为车多，但这个时候的色温最美。“这次模特儿黄樱花真的牺牲很大，滨刺麦很刺，她坐下去还是完全配合。”他笑着说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14292008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14292008" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292008-696116-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14292008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292008-696116-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14292008" class="wp-caption-text">这次模特儿黄樱花真的牺牲很大，滨刺麦很刺，她坐下去还是完全配合。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>捕捉夕阳下的风车也是黄连兴的拿手好戏。黄连兴：“风车和夕阳是绝配，拍的时候，我尽量往顺光的方向拍。如果非要逆光，我就用闪灯补光。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14292010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14292010" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292010-696118-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14292010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292010-696118-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14292010" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳下的滨刺麦。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄连兴热爱拍摄，无论是滨刺麦、夕阳还是风车，他都能捕捉到最美的瞬间。他的故事和作品，不仅展示了滨刺麦的自然之美，更让我们看到了摄影师背后的热情与坚持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14292012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14292012" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292012-696123-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14292012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14292012-696123-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14292012" class="wp-caption-text">白色风车一起入镜也很美丽。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
