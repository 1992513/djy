<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>朗诵词：反迫害25周年 法轮功美国华府烛光夜悼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14290303-IMG_7591-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>反迫害25周年，法轮功学员2024年7月11日在美国首都华盛顿DC烛光夜悼，缅怀被中共迫害致死的学员，要求中共停止迫害。（戴兵/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年07月14日讯】<span class="s1">7</span><span class="s2">月</span><span class="s1">11</span><span class="s2">日晚，夜幕低垂，城市的喧嚣散去，天地归于宁静，在美国首都华盛顿DC的国会山前，中西族裔的法轮功学员比邻而坐。他们手捧点点烛光，默默地悼念</span><span class="s1">25</span><span class="s2">年来为坚持正信而被迫害离世的法轮功学员，一曲曲悲壮的歌曲在夜空中回荡.……</span></p>
<p><span class="s2">下面是当晚活动的中英文朗诵词：</span></p>
<p><a title="朗诵词：反迫害25周年 法轮功美国华府烛光夜悼 Falun Gong Candlelight Vigil in DC" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/zh-CN/embed/1gt5okh44e04TwSeenVA6ytQr1n91c" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>在这喧嚣伴着落日散尽，天与地融归于苍穹的时刻，我们再一次聚集在美国国会山前，手捧点点烛光，悼念二十五年来，为坚守信仰而被中共迫害至死的法轮功学员。</p>
<p>法轮功是以真、善、忍为指导的佛家修炼大法。1992年由李洪志先生传出，其祛病健身的神奇功效和使人道德升华的感召力，迅速吸引上亿人修炼。</p>
<p>然而，1999年7月20日，中共独裁者动用手中的国家机器，对上亿修炼法轮功的善良民众从肉体、精神和经济上开始了史无前例的残酷迫害。</p>
<p>一夜之间，谎言、污蔑、仇恨铺天盖地；面对酷刑、劳教、甚至活摘器官……无数大法弟子坚定的走出来维护信仰，让世人了解真相，见证法轮大法的美好。</p>
<p>由于中共的信息封锁，我们无从得知，因为坚持修炼法轮功而被中共杀害的确切人数。虽然他们离开了人间，但他们的精神光耀千古。</p>
<p>二十五年来，身在海外的法轮功学员也坚持反迫害，得到了美国和全世界的正义支持。</p>
<p>几周前，美国国会众议院通过《法轮功保护法案》，两党一致要求中共停止对法轮功的迫害，并对实施活摘器官的犯罪者进行制裁。几乎与此同时，美国国务院发布年度《国际宗教自由报告》，其中58次提及法轮功，详细记录了法轮功学员当前在中国受到迫害的情况。</p>
<p>二十五年来，面对中共的镇压，大法弟子在承受巨大苦难的同时，始终以和平的方式传播着真相，帮助人们从谎言中清醒过来。即使在面对施暴者时，法轮功学员仍然报以善心，希望人们都能做出正确的选择。</p>
<p>二十五年来，大法弟子以坚韧不屈的精神，让全世界了解了法轮功，也看清了中共的邪恶本质。</p>
<p>这场迫害终将结束。大法弟子维护真、善、忍宇宙真理的壮举，为整个世界带来了光明与希望。</p>
<p>As the sun sets on the nation’s capital, we gather here once again… this time to mark 25 years of the Chinese communist regime’s persecution of Falun Gong.</p>
<p>Falun Gong is a traditional spiritual practice that combines meditation and gentle exercises similar to tai chi. Its moral philosophy teaches people to live by the principles of Truthfulness, Compassion, and Tolerance. Falun Gong became very popular in China in the 90s, due to its health benefits. Moreover, the practice allowed Chinese to reconnect with their spiritual heritage, which was almost lost under communist rule. Within seven years of it being made public, upwards of 100-million people had taken up the practice.</p>
<p>However, in July 1999, the Chinese Communist Party began a ruthless persecution against Falun Gong followers. Overnight, the Chinese regime targeted Falun Gong as the enemy of the state, persecuting and killing young and old alike. Suddenly millions of innocent people, as well as their families, faced brutal persecution from a modern-day Gestapo.</p>
<p>The Chinese regime seeks to eradicate people’s belief in God… and replace it with unwavering loyalty to the Communist Party. From one day to the next, Falun Gong practitioners became the largest group of prisoners of conscience in China. The persecution continues to this day. Falun Gong followers are thrown in jails, labor camps, mental hospitals, and brainwashing centers. And not long after the persecution began… Chinese military hospitals expanded organ transplant programs around the country—and advertised the short wait times for organ transplants worldwide. Untold thousands and possibly millions of Falun Gong followers have been killed for their organs. It’s become a multi-billion dollar state-sanctioned industry in China.</p>
<p>In the course of 25 years of persecution, we have received so much support from the US government. Just a few weeks ago, the House of Representatives unanimously passed the Falun Gong Protection Act. Its goal is to end forced organ harvesting of Falun Gong in China, which continues to this day. We are very grateful for the tremendous support we’ve received from both sides of the aisle. And the State Department’s 2023 Report on International Religious Freedom mentions Falun Gong more than 50 times.</p>
<p>We may never know how many millions have been killed. Although they have left this world, their spirits live on.</p>
<p>For twenty-five years, our faith and perseverance have endured. We have peacefully shed light on the persecution without wavering for one moment. The wind and rain, the cold of winter and heat of summer have never stopped us from taking a stand against this unprecedented persecution. With compassion for the abusers as well as for the Falun Gong followers suffering abuse, we make phone calls to the dark dens where our people are tortured and killed… and give even the worst abusers a chance to choose a brighter future.</p>
<p>And in fact, our efforts have not been in vain. People the world over have come to know what Falun Gong is and why the Chinese Communist Party wants to eradicate the practice. And while our numbers may not be large, our impact is immeasurable. We will never give up and never give in… until our brethren in China can breathe the air of freedom. That day is sure to come. One day soon, the sun will set on this persecution for good.</p>
<p>If you’re just passing by, we invite you to come and take a candle and sit with us now. Grab a yellow mat and join us in taking a stand against the persecution.</p>
