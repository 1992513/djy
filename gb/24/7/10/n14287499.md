<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>武陵绿色隧道2626市集 惊喜寻宝之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287500-694597-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>武陵绿色隧道，暑期每周六下午都有哦！（2626市集提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）夏天热得像沙漠的台东，有一个超凉超赞的市集——2626市集！这个市集位于美丽的武陵绿色隧道，暑假每个星期六下午两点到六点，这里变身成为一个农村假日市集，满满的惊喜等您来发现！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14287501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14287501" style="width: 960px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287501-694598.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14287501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287501-694598.jpeg" alt="" width="960" b="720" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14287501" class="wp-caption-text">知名的手作馒头店。（2626市集提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>为什么要来2626市集？</strong></p>
<p>这里有最纯净的环境和最友善的居民。永安社区理事长廖中勋：“社区号召大家一起推动这个市集，让最优质的农特产品和手工艺品有个展示的平台，也让消费者能找到品质佳、价格适宜的产品。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14287505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14287505" style="width: 960px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287505-694602.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14287505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287505-694602.jpeg" alt="" width="960" b="720" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14287505" class="wp-caption-text">台东2626市集将朝向民间自主营运。（2626市集提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>多元开放式市集 琳琅满目</strong></p>
<p>台东2626市集是一个多元开放式的市集，这里有：</p>
<p>有机商品：新鲜蔬果、蜂蜜产品、红乌龙相关产品等</p>
<p>环保创意：环保商品、养生醋、手工香皂等</p>
<p>手工艺品：精美的文创手工艺品、手工饼干、手工蛋糕等</p>
<p>休闲体验：各种DIY活动，还有街头艺人表演！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14287507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14287507" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287507-694604.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14287507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287507-694604.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14287507" class="wp-caption-text">也有街头艺人即兴表演。（2626市集提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>参加市集的摊位来自四面八方</strong></p>
<p>目前已有将近七十个摊位报名参加，来自池上、关山、鹿野、海端、延平、卑南、台东市、东河、金峰等乡镇。在这里，您可以逛街、品尝美食、参加DIY活动，还能享受凉爽的绿色隧道！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14287506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14287506" style="width: 960px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287506-694603.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14287506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287506-694603.jpeg" alt="" width="960" b="720" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14287506" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友开心的体验手作。（2626市集提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>社区诚挚的邀请</strong></p>
<p>永安社区理事长廖中勋表示：“暑假7-8月每周六下午都有台东2626市集！欢迎大家来武陵绿色隧道避暑逛街，体验DIY和享受好康喔！傍晚还可以再上鹿野高台看热气球～有环保热气球DIY和SDGS推广送兰花～还可以带60个干净的手提硬纸袋到服务台，可换1个2626市集LV茄芷包～欢乐暑假，2626市集等您来消暑喔！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14287504" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14287504" style="width: 960px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287504-694601.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14287504" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287504-694601.jpeg" alt="" width="960" b="720" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14287504" class="wp-caption-text">举办市集是为了让优质的农特产品，和手工艺品有展示的平台。（2626市集提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台东2626市集将朝向民间自主营运，未来希望每周都有常态性农村假日市集发展，需要大家的支持与鼓励，让台东的社区部落有更好的未来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14287503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14287503" style="width: 960px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287503-694600.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14287503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14287503-694600.jpeg" alt="" width="960" b="720" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14287503" class="wp-caption-text">用海边漂流木制作的生活用品。（2626市集提供）</figcaption></figure>
