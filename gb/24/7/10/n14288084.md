<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加拿大平均租金创三年最大跌幅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13582822-shutterstock_422413534-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>最近几个月，租房市场总体呈现降温趋势，与过去几年的租金飙升形成鲜明对比。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月11日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元记者周行多伦多报导）新发布的一份<span class="s2">租金报告</span>显示，加拿大</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月的平均租金环比下降</span><span class="s3">0.8%</span><span class="s1">，这是过去</span><span class="s3">3</span><span class="s1">年的最大跌幅。租金年同比增幅也明显减低。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">根据<span class="s4">Urbanation</span><span class="s2">和</span><span class="s4">rentals.ca</span><span class="s2">发布的租金报告</span>，</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月所有类型房产的平均租金，比</span><span class="s3">5</span><span class="s1">月下降</span><span class="s3">0.8%</span><span class="s1">，至平均</span><span class="s3">2,185</span><span class="s1">元，这是自</span><span class="s3">2021</span><span class="s1">年初以来租金的最大月度降幅；同比上涨</span><span class="s3">7%</span><span class="s1">，则是过去</span><span class="s3">13</span><span class="s1">个月中最低的年增长率。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">该报告称，</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月通常是每年租金季节性上涨的时间，但今年出现了趋势逆转。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">最近几个月，租房市场总体呈现降温趋势，与过去几年的租金飙升形成鲜明对比。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">报告说，过去的</span><span class="s3">3</span><span class="s1">个月，租金</span><span class="s3">“</span><span class="s1">基本持平</span><span class="s3">”</span><span class="s1">，从</span><span class="s3">3</span><span class="s1">月至</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月，全国租金仅上涨了</span><span class="s3">0.2%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">今年</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月，专用出租公寓和共管公寓的租金环比下降</span><span class="s3">1.0%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">2,146</span><span class="s1">元。与去年同期相比，专用出租公寓的租金上涨</span><span class="s3">11.0%</span><span class="s1">，至平均</span><span class="s3">2,121</span><span class="s1">元；共管公寓的租金仅上涨</span><span class="s3">2.6%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">2,320</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">公寓单间的租金，同比下降</span><span class="s3">5.1%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">1,823</span><span class="s1">元。专用出租公寓的单间，租金录得同比上涨</span><span class="s3">14.6%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">1,613</span><span class="s1">元。在</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月，所有类型专用出租公寓的租金，年增长率均接近或超过</span><span class="s3">10%</span><span class="s1">；对于共管公寓单元，卧室数量越多，涨幅越高，三卧室单元的租金同比增长高达</span><span class="s3">8.0%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>

<h2 class="p1"><span class="s1">安省是唯一租金同比下降的省</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">除安省外，其它各省的租金同比均上涨。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月份出租公寓租金环比下降的地区，只有安省和魁北克省。卑诗省的租金持平。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在安省，专用出租公寓和共管公寓的平均租金，在</span><span class="s3">5</span><span class="s1">月至</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月期间下降了</span><span class="s3">1.7%</span><span class="s1">，同比则下降了</span><span class="s3">1.3%</span><span class="s1">，至</span><span class="s3">2,382</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">魁北克省的公寓平均租金环比下降了</span><span class="s3">1.0%</span><span class="s1">，但同比上涨了</span><span class="s3">5.1%</span><span class="s1">，至</span><span class="s3">1,979</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">萨斯喀彻温省的租金继续加速增长，连续第三个月领跑全国，其专用出租公寓和共管公寓的租金同比增长了</span><span class="s3">22.1%</span><span class="s1">。尽管增长速度快，萨省的租金仍比全国平均水平低</span><span class="s3">38%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">1,339</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span class="s1">多伦多租金跌至</span><span class="s3">22</span><span class="s1">个月新低</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在多伦多，专用出租公寓和共管公寓的平均租金，环比下降</span><span class="s3">2.5%</span><span class="s1">，同比也下降了</span><span class="s3">3.5%</span><span class="s1">，跌至</span><span class="s3">22</span><span class="s1">个月以来的最低水平</span><span class="s3">2,715</span><span class="s1">元。</span><span class="s1">报告称，多伦多的公寓租金已连续</span><span class="s3">5</span><span class="s1">个月同比下降。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">相比之下，温哥华的租金已连续</span><span class="s3">7</span><span class="s1">个月同比下降。温哥华的公寓租金，<span class="s3">6</span>月份环比上涨<span class="s3">1.1%</span>，但同比下降了<span class="s3">7.8%</span>，平均租金为<span class="s3">3,042</span>元。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月，全国的合租租金（室友租金）已连续</span><span class="s3">4</span><span class="s1">个月环比下降。</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月份下降</span><span class="s3">0.3%</span><span class="s1">，自</span><span class="s3">3</span><span class="s1">月以来，共下降了</span><span class="s3">2.1%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p><span class="s1">多伦多的合租租金</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月份环比下降</span><span class="s3">1.7%</span><span class="s1">，同比下降</span><span class="s3">4.0%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">1,236</span><span class="s1">元。渥太华的合租租金同比小幅下降</span><span class="s3">1.0%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">938</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<p><span class="s1">加拿大其它所有大城市的合租租金，均录得同比上涨，其中卡尔加里涨幅最大，达</span><span class="s3">8.8%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">916</span><span class="s1">元。温哥华仍是合租租金最昂贵的城市，平均为</span><span class="s3">1,471</span><span class="s1">元，同比上涨</span><span class="s3">1.2%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<h2>其它大城市租金依然上涨</h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在加拿大六大城市中，埃德蒙顿已连续</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">个月成为公寓租金涨幅最大的城市，</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">月份的年涨幅达</span><span class="s3">14.3%</span><span class="s1">，平均为</span><span class="s3">1,564</span><span class="s1">元。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">加拿大中小型城市中，平均公寓租金的排序，卑诗省是：北温哥华（</span><span class="s3">3,285</span><span class="s1">元）、本拿比（</span><span class="s3">2,918</span><span class="s1">元）、列治文（</span><span class="s3">2,836</span><span class="s1">元）、高贵林（</span><span class="s3">2,809</span><span class="s1">元）和兰里（</span><span class="s3">2,604</span><span class="s1">元）；安省的排序是：列治文山（</span><span class="s3">2,856</span><span class="s1">元）、密西沙加（</span><span class="s3">2,681</span><span class="s1">元）、怡陶碧谷（</span><span class="s3">2,614</span><span class="s1">元）、旺市（</span><span class="s3">2,590</span><span class="s1">元）和万锦（</span><span class="s3">2,562</span><span class="s1">元）。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">据</span><span class="s7">Global News</span><span class="s8">报导，加鼎银行</span><span class="s1">经济师兰德尔</span><span class="s3">·</span><span class="s1">巴特利特（</span><span class="s3">Randall Bartlett</span><span class="s1">）表示，由于租房者无法负担多伦多和温哥华等城市的租金，很多人涌向</span><span class="s3">“</span><span class="s1">更便宜的居住地</span><span class="s3">”</span><span class="s1">，比如亚伯塔省，这些地区的租金随之飙升。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">他说，随着联邦政府限制非永久居民的数量，以及各级政府努力增加出租房屋供应量，未来几个月的租金压力，应该会进一步减轻。</span></p>
