<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西屿乡旅游口袋名单 沙滩！夕阳！夜景！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288255-694832-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月11日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾澎湖报导）准备好一起探索澎湖西屿乡的美景吗？这里可是拥有梦幻夕阳和迷人沙滩的好地方！让我们一起走进这片被称为“落日缤纷的彩霞”和“梦幻星海”的天堂吧！</p>
<figure id="14288262" aria-describedby="caption-14288262" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288262-694838-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288262-694838-450x338.jpg" alt="澎湖夏日星河。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14288262" class="wp-caption-text">澎湖夏日星河。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>大菓叶码头</h2>
<p>先来介绍一下大菓叶码头吧！这个地方可是澎湖县西屿乡二崁村的宝藏呢！从清领时期起，大菓叶就成为了西屿岛的交通枢纽。虽然现在的码头状况有点低迷，但这里依然保留着过去的重要地位。</p>
<p>如果你是历史爱好者，这里绝对不容错过！漫步在大菓叶码头，仿佛可以听到过去渔船的喧嚣声和海风的轻拂，它的夜实在太美了。</p>
<figure id="14288258" aria-describedby="caption-14288258" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288258-694835-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288258-694835-450x301.jpg" alt="西屿的夜。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14288258" class="wp-caption-text">西屿的夜。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>赤马沙滩</h2>
<p>接下来，让我们前往赤马沙滩吧！这里有一片宁静的沙滩，就藏在北环203县道的赤马村庄内。空拍达人nina Chen说：“沿着赤马樊桃殿对面的小巷道走进去，你会发现一片宽广的海与沙滩，瞬间被那细致的砂质所吸引。”</p>
<figure id="14288257" aria-describedby="caption-14288257" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288257-694834-450x254.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288257-694834-450x254.jpg" alt="赤马渔港。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14288257" class="wp-caption-text">赤马渔港。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>nina Chen提供她心中的西屿亮点：</strong></p>
<p>细沙触感：这里的沙子非常细腻，适合玩水，让你感受到海滩的温柔。</p>
<p>夕阳绝美：夕阳西下，金黄色的沙滩上演一场日落盛宴，火红的天空和金黄色的沙滩相互辉映，真是美得令人窒息。</p>
<p>悠闲时光：在沙滩上躺下来，看着远方的落日和孩子们无忧无虑地游玩，所有的烦恼都会随风而去。</p>
<p>最不能错遇的就是，无敌的夜景、星河、还有让游客拍手叫好的西屿日出。</p>
<figure id="14288256" aria-describedby="caption-14288256" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288256-694833-450x251.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288256-694833-450x251.jpg" alt="西屿日照。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14288256" class="wp-caption-text">西屿日出。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>拍照小角落：港口堤防边还有可遮荫的小凉亭，这里是拍照的绝佳地点，不仅可以赏海还能避开酷热的阳光哦！</p>
<figure id="14288261" aria-describedby="caption-14288261" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288261-694837-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288261-694837-450x301.jpg" alt="摄影师抓住那美丽的片刻。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14288261" class="wp-caption-text">摄影师抓住那美丽的片刻。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不管是大菓叶码头的历史气息，还是赤马沙滩的细腻沙质和绝美夕阳，澎湖西屿乡都能让你留下难忘的回忆。赶快把这些景点加入你的口袋名单，带上相机，和我们一起发现澎湖的夏日之美吧！</p>
<figure id="14288260" aria-describedby="caption-14288260" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288260-694836-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288260-694836-450x301.jpg" alt="西屿晚霞。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14288260" class="wp-caption-text">西屿晚霞。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
