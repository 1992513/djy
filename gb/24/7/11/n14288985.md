<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多凯悦酒店举办宝石矿物珠子珠宝展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289005-27b2a069755d7067c0716b4af7315af2-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月12日讯】来自世界各地的宝石的美丽和辉煌，将于7月26日至28日在多伦多凯悦酒店大放光彩。数十家供应商，将在2024年多伦多宝石博览会（The Gem Expo 2024）上，展示蕴含各地丰富历史和文化特色的宝藏。</p>
<p>宝藏种类有用于收藏的原始宝石和矿物，治疗作用的水晶，家具装饰品，以及在珠宝店难以找到的纯手工珠宝成品。在多伦多宝石博览会上，来自本地和国际供应商提供的宝石、矿物、珠子和珠宝，应有尽有。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/11/n14288985.md#1/7-551" rel="attachment wp-att-14289004"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14289004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289004-7-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="451" /></a></p>
<p class="p1"><strong>手工制作的珠宝：</strong>大师级工匠将展示价格实惠且独特的手工制作珠宝。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14289001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289001-4-450x193.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="257" /></p>
<p class="p1"><strong>纯银首饰：</strong>您可以探索各种纯银首饰，包括戒指、项链和手镯。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14289002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289002-5-450x192.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="256" /></p>
<p class="p1">
<p class="p1"><strong>正宗的波罗的海琥珀。</strong></p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/7/11/n14288985.md#1/8-462" rel="attachment wp-att-14289008"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14289008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289008-8-450x192.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="256" /></a></p>
<p class="p1"><strong>宝石珠：</strong>遍布全球的宝石珠供应商将为您提供有关宝石的宝贵建议。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14289000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289000-3-450x190.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="253" /></p>
<p class="p1"><strong>博物馆品质的矿物标本：</strong>您可以欣赏到高质量的矿物标本。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14288998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288998-1-450x192.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="257" /></p>
<p class="p3">多伦多宝石博览会设有众多课程，激发您的创造力，提供向国际知名工匠和从业者学习新技能的机会。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenterwp-image-14288999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288999-2-450x192.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="257" /></p>
