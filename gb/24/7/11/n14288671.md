<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>肉类冷冻关键 这样做可保留食材鲜味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14293283-shutterstock_2044332302-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>只要了解冷冻保存的重点，就能活用依分量来分门别类，取用时更方便，更让食材效益最大化。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：您是否因为价钱便宜而买了大分量盒装肉，或是趁打折时一次买了很多，却又因为吃不完而放在冰箱，还是不晓得该怎么买肉呢？只要了解冷冻保存的重点以及解冻的秘诀，冷冻前多一道手续就能保存美味，活用依分量来分门别类，取用时更方便，更让食材效益最大化。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">冷冻保存的基本</span></h2>
<p>最理想的是不失鲜度与味道、快速且方便使用的状态下冷冻，本书整理了实践完美冷冻的秘诀。只要照着做就不怕失败。</p>
<h3>【冷冻保存六法】</h3>
<p>冷冻前多一道手续能保存美味是理所当然的，但冷冻后能方便使用会更好。</p>
<h3>1. 趁新鲜就冷冻</h3>
<p>肉类等生鲜食品即使冷藏保存，但冰久了鲜度还是会流失。要是冰了几天才拿去冷冻，鲜度流失就是流失了。为了维持其美味度，买回家后就要尽快分装处理再冷冻起来！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14288673" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288673" style="width: 320px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288673-P.8-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14288673" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288673-P.8-1.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="320" b="235" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288673" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>2. 去除多余水分</strong></h3>
<p>食材上多余的水分没有除掉就冷冻，不仅会结霜也是造成美味流失的原因，而且食材本身结在一起也很难进行料理。将血水连同水分等导致腐坏的原因一起去除掉，就能美味地冷冻了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14288674" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288674" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288674-P.8-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14288674" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288674-P.8-2-450x300.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288674" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>3. 尽可能切薄片，且厚薄要均一</h3>
<p><strong>冷冻保存的成功关键在于结冻的时间以及解冻的时间要短。</strong>例如有厚度的鸡肉、猪五花等肉类要切厚薄均一的薄片，冷冻得快也能均匀地解冻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14288675" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288675" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288675-P.8-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14288675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288675-P.8-3-450x299.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288675" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14288676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288676" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288676-P.8-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14288676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288676-P.8-4-450x299.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288676" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在保鲜袋上标示肉的种类、部位、状态（切法等）、日期，便可以很快地拿取所需的肉，也能帮助自己随时留心在保存期限内用完。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14288677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288677" style="width: 266px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288677-P.8-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14288677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288677-P.8-5-266x400.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="266" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288677" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>4. 分小包装以方便使用</h3>
<p>以一次用完的分量分装冷冻，要用的时候就可以很快地从冰箱拿出来，解冻时间最短，也不会因为解冻到用不到的部分而造成浪费。请100g、200g等容易使用的分量分装冷冻吧！特卖时一次买齐或是买大包装，只要冷冻保存起来，节约效果也超棒！<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288678-P.8-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14288678 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288678-P.8-6-601x400.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="601" b="400" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">（台湾广厦提供）（台湾广厦提供）</p>
<h3>5. 尽量压出空气</h3>
<p>食材一但接触到空气就容易结霜，细菌也容易繁殖。脂肪氧化就会使味道走样。将肉类放进保鲜袋后尽可能摊平，并用手将里面的空气压出来后再封口冷冻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14288680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288680" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288680-P.8-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14288680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288680-P.8-8-602x400.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="602" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288680" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>6. 放在盘子里一起冷冻</h3>
<p>食材在完全冷冻前的时间越短，越能保持其鲜美度。因为金属制浅盘的热传导率高，食材放上去再一起放进冷冻室便能快速冷冻。等完全冷冻后再把盘子拿出来将肉装袋就行了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14288681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288681-P.8-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14288681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288681-P.8-9-600x400.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288681" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">解冻的基本</span></h2>
<p>依照状态使用不同方法。说解冻方法会左右肉质的美味程度一点也不为过！这里将介绍必须知道的解冻秘诀。</p>
<h3>推荐NO.1：冷藏室自然解冻</h3>
<p>虽然会花一些时间，但低温慢慢地解冻生肉的优点就是血水少、也不易流失其美味。解冻时，保鲜袋外面会有水珠出现，因此，请先放在盘子后再一起移入冷藏室。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14288682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288682" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288682-P.9-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14288682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288682-P.9-1-600x400.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288682" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>放在室温3～4小时就能解冻。不过依肉的厚度和内容物会有时间差，如果是半解冻，原则上需要1～2小时。但若遇到梅雨季或酷暑时期，放在室温解冻反而容易腐坏，要避免！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14288683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288683-P.9-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14288683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288683-P.9-2-600x400.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288683" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>急着料理时：微波炉解冻</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14288684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288684-P.9-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14288684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288684-P.9-3-600x400.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288684" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>想立刻料理或是想稍微加热时。解冻冷冻生肉时，为避免过度加热，请将时间设定短一点，边看解冻情况边解冻吧。也可以利用微波炉的解冻功能。</p>
<h3>提早一点：活水解冻</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14288685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288685" style="width: 425px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288685-P.9-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14288685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288685-P.9-4-425x400.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="425" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288685" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>连同保鲜袋一起放在有水的碗里解冻。最好是不时地翻面、换水。优点是能比冷藏室解冻来得更加快一点。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">半解冻到什么程度？</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14288686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14288686" style="width: 553px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288686-P.9-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14288686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14288686-P.9-5.jpg" alt="（台湾广厦提供）" width="553" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14288686" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>●能用手掰开的程度<br />
●能用菜刀切开的硬度</p>
<p>外面已经解冻，但里面还是冰硬的状态。放在冷藏室、用流动的水、微波炉的解冻功能等任何一种解冻方法都OK。能用手掰开、用菜刀切开就行了。<br />
鸡肉等肉质较软的肉类，在半解冻状态下会比较好分切。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">解冻后再冷冻，NG！</span></h2>
<p>凡是解冻过，美味、水分、味道都会流失。再加上接触到空气，细菌更容易繁殖。因此，就卫生方面来说，不建议解冻后再冷冻。</p>
