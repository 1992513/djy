<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园拉拉山寒流来袭 大溪警方交通管制措施</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14164509-660815-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园拉拉山寒流来袭，大溪警方交通管制措施。（桃园市大溪警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年01月23日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）23日清晨3时许，复兴区桃116县道11K终点处气温达冰点摄氏0度，伴随持续细雨逐渐降下冰霰，造成路面形成结冰，车辆易造成打滑，大溪分局巴陵派出所派遣警力于巴陵派出所前实施交通管制，车辆无加装雪链，禁止进入桃116县道及中心路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14164510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14164510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14164510-660816.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14164510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14164510-660816-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14164510" class="wp-caption-text">大溪分局巴陵派出所派遣警力于巴陵派出所前实施交通管制。（桃园市大溪警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大溪警方呼吁，因寒流来袭气温骤降，请民众外出注意保暖，山区道路易降霜或路面湿滑，易影响行车安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14164511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14164511" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14164511-660817.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14164511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14164511-660817-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14164511" class="wp-caption-text">车辆无加装雪链，禁止进入桃116县道及中心路。（桃园市大溪警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14164512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14164512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14164512-660818.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14164512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14164512-660818-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14164512" class="wp-caption-text">民众如欲行经管制路段，请配合管制人员并放慢速度以维安全。（桃园市大溪警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为确保用路人行车安全，相关单位进行管制措，民众如欲行经管制路段，请配合管制人员并放慢速度以维安全。◇</p>
