<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】清水羊排~搭配美味薄荷酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155295-25a7e813911855fb41dfdd5acaceaf5f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冬季进补，羊肉不可或缺。清汤羊肉用料简单、易作，羊肉和汤都可以享用。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年01月10日讯】小寒节气到了，天寒地冻，大家要好好保暖，吃一些补益食物温暖身体，积蓄力量。今天带来一道搭配美味薄荷酱吃的清水羊排。</p>
<p>羊肉选用农场饲养的新鲜羔羊肉（Lamb），基本没有膻味，适合清煮。羊肉冷水下锅煮约30分钟就可以吃了。清甜的羊肉蘸酱享用，锅中的清汤加盐调味可以直接喝或另作料理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14155298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14155298" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155298-812aa9f0be126e004dfa8bf0e3c1e5c8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14155298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155298-812aa9f0be126e004dfa8bf0e3c1e5c8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14155298" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>3磅羔羊腹排</p>
<p>适量姜片</p>
<p>一汤匙白胡椒粒</p>
<p>一块拇指大小的白芷</p>
<p>如下食材搅打融合制作薄荷芝麻酱</p>
<p>1/4杯新鲜薄荷叶，切碎压实计算</p>
<p>2瓣大蒜，去皮</p>
<p>3汤匙芝麻酱（TAHINI）</p>
<p>3茶匙新鲜压榨柠檬汁</p>
<p>1茶匙孜然粉</p>
<p>1/2茶匙盐</p>
<p>3汤匙蜂蜜</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1. 羊腹排外层有时覆盖一层肥油，料理前可用刀切下来另外料理，这样水煮羊排吃起来较不油腻。</p>
<p>2. 取一口深锅，放入整块羊排（若锅小，羊排切半）、姜片，加入没过羊排的水（水位至少高出羊排一个拳头）煮沸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14155301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14155301" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155301-5dd33f2f349bbd1e7ec3ac0534d8dded.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14155301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155301-5dd33f2f349bbd1e7ec3ac0534d8dded-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14155301" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 快煮沸时，锅中会出现浮末，用漏勺捞出。煮沸后保持火力2分钟再调小火，保持羊汤微微翻腾，就像水下有鱼吐气泡的状态，直到出锅。</p>
<p>4. 撇净浮末后加入白芷和胡椒粒，不加锅盖煮20至30分钟，直到羊肉全熟可出锅。</p>
<p><strong>料理贴士</strong>：大火浓汤，小火清汤。这道料理全程不加锅盖煮。煮清汤选用新鲜羊肉，浮末会少。用小锅煮羊肉中途不加凉水，否则肉质变硬。羊油遇冷凝固，羊肉出锅后宜尽快食用。</p>
<p>此外，羊排切下来的肥油适合做手抓饭、藏红花炒饭，或者炒萝卜、煎豆腐等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14155304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14155304" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155304-dfad1c76c1750a1b7873b41108e80d11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14155304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155304-dfad1c76c1750a1b7873b41108e80d11-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14155304" class="wp-caption-text">羊排清汤。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>羊汤食谱</h2>
<p>锅中羊汤倒入滤网，滤除香料等物可得到清汤。清汤适合作火锅汤底、涮菜、加盐调味直接喝或搭配泡馍等。如下介绍2个膳食纤维丰富的羊清汤料理。</p>
<h3>一、羊汤萝卜饭</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14155307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14155307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155307-85b137c8e478de04c95f690a3e07e817.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14155307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155307-85b137c8e478de04c95f690a3e07e817-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14155307" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>羊汤中添加了白芷。白芷既是中药材也作调味料，其香气有增进食欲的作用。所以清汤即使搭配寡淡的白萝卜和豆腐，也能让人吃得津津有味。</p>
<p>白萝卜挑选成熟度好，清甜多汁的品种。料理前削皮，辛辣味会少。豆腐选用裂纹多的老豆腐，容易入味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14155309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14155309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155309-85c6f8b87051d586c0abf5e5b1a560bd.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14155309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155309-85c6f8b87051d586c0abf5e5b1a560bd-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14155309" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食材准备</h3>
<p>2杯白萝卜，切短粗幼条后撒盐拌匀</p>
<p>1杯茉莉香米，洗净</p>
<p>2杯羊肉清汤</p>
<p>250克老豆腐，切1英寸方粒</p>
<p>1茶匙盐</p>
<p>1.高压锅中放入羊汤、白萝卜、大米和盐，拌匀。最后表面铺上豆腐块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14155310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14155310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155310-52d5dee526290115967def4465d076c2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14155310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155310-52d5dee526290115967def4465d076c2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14155310" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.加盖大火煮，当高压气阀弹起就转入中小火煮10分钟。关火，待气阀自然落下饭就煮好了。</p>
<h2>二、羊汤白菜</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14155312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14155312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155312-90aea54cd7d0cf4ad487c51619b2724e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14155312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14155312-90aea54cd7d0cf4ad487c51619b2724e-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14155312" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1.锅中放入能没过蔬菜的羊汤煮沸，添加白菜嫩叶、枸杞一起煮软，加盐调味便可上桌。◇</p>
