<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】南瓜布丁~自制腰果奶代替牛奶</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152389-9a4f6bba4291496c50c34d30928ef421-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>南瓜布丁用自制腰果奶替代牛奶，坚果香浓郁。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这道蛋奶布丁风味经典。明亮的金黄色来自黄油、橡子南瓜泥（Acorn squash）与鸡蛋，枫糖浆带入温润的甜蜜，面包丁吸足腰果奶的坚果香气。布丁表面还有甜酸可口的Q弹坚果糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152388" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152388-fccc366e203971f3d2097a4dfb47dcde.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152388" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152388-fccc366e203971f3d2097a4dfb47dcde-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152388" class="wp-caption-text">橡子南瓜的味道像栗子一样软糯甜美，尤其烤着吃，焦糖香浓郁。它的个头通常在2磅以下，瓜皮比栗子薄得多。小型瓜用微波炉就可以煮熟。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152387" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152387-f7b7655af106d314820332e4a8baf40d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152387-f7b7655af106d314820332e4a8baf40d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152387" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>9英寸烤盘的食材准备</h2>
<p><strong>布丁食材：</strong></p>
<p>1杯新鲜橡子南瓜泥<br />
1/2杯生腰果<br />
欧式面包，切方丁后铺满9英寸派盘<br />
1/4条黄油（butter），微波炉加热10秒融化<br />
1个大号鸡蛋，室温<br />
1/2杯枫糖浆</p>
<p><strong>坚果糖食材：</strong></p>
<p>1/4杯枫糖浆<br />
1/4杯山核桃<br />
1小撮蔓越莓干</p>
<h3><strong>做法</strong></h3>
<p>1. 自制南瓜泥：1.5磅的橡子南瓜洗净，整个放入高压锅蒸10分钟。出锅后，瓜皮轻轻一刮就掉了，然后挖掉瓜瓤，剩下的便是南瓜泥。当次用不完的南瓜泥最好冷冻保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152386" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152386-ea4782f18e26f602d2e4bdadbe4ed684.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152386-ea4782f18e26f602d2e4bdadbe4ed684-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152386" class="wp-caption-text">用高压锅煮熟的橡子南瓜。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>料理贴士：</strong>建议1.5磅以下的橡子南瓜用微波炉加热，6到10分钟就能煮熟，十分方便。加热时，南瓜放入深容器有利于热蒸汽聚集，熟得快。加热4分钟后翻面再继续加热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152396-3e3d3bdda1e0608aecd120e5e83d4f40.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152396-3e3d3bdda1e0608aecd120e5e83d4f40-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152396" class="wp-caption-text">微波炉加热时，南瓜放入深容器有助于聚集热蒸汽，熟得快。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 取小锅烧沸两杯水，放入洗净的生腰果，关火加盖焖15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152395-d7c81260492af25a59a926d3a60670e3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152395-d7c81260492af25a59a926d3a60670e3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152395" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 破壁机加入1.5杯水和泡软的腰果搅打成浆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152394-54611b874980d1e397ad2d3e66cfe61d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152394-54611b874980d1e397ad2d3e66cfe61d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152394" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 腰果奶中添加其它食材搅拌融合，均匀浇在面包丁上，覆盖保鲜膜冷藏30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152393" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152393-fda2204683c2565cdb12f33c3f5c3791.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152393-fda2204683c2565cdb12f33c3f5c3791-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152393" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 预热烤箱350华氏度，烤40分钟。</p>
<p>6. 煮坚果糖：锅中加热枫糖浆和山核桃，待糖浆变黏稠即刻倒在烘焙纸冷上冷却。最后掰成小块散在布丁上享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152392-85f313df791bd68eb5597eb94814c1e7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152392-85f313df791bd68eb5597eb94814c1e7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152392" class="wp-caption-text">加热枫糖浆和山核桃，待糖浆变黏稠即刻倒在烘焙纸冷上冷却。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152391-7f3b04d398013d7cd72360482c79921c.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152391-7f3b04d398013d7cd72360482c79921c-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152391" class="wp-caption-text">烤箱350华氏度烤40分钟。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
