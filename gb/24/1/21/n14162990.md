<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】芝麻酱凉拌海蜇鸡丝～快速凉拌菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163103-11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>芝麻酱凉拌海蜇鸡丝。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong>想要来点养生又不燥热的凉拌菜吗？这一道有海陆双味的食谱，一定要尝试看看！简单快速好料理，准备起来一点都不麻烦。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>芝麻酱凉拌海蜇鸡丝</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>即食海蜇&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;5 oz</p>
<p>熟鸡丝&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;6 oz</p>
<p>青瓜&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.1条（刨丝）</p>
<p>红萝卜&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;1条（刨丝）</p>
<p>紫色洋葱&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..半个（切丝）</p>
<p>煲过汤的牛蒡&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;2 oz（切碎）</p>
<p>葱&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;2条（切丝）</p>
<p>花生碎&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..2汤匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味酱：</span></h3>
<p>芝麻酱&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;3汤匙</p>
<p>热水&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;5汤匙</p>
<p>酱油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..1汤匙</p>
<p>镇江醋&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.3汤匙</p>
<p>砂糖&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.2茶匙</p>
<p>盐&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.半茶匙</p>
<p>麻油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;1汤匙</p>
<p>辣椒酱&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.半茶匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：?</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14163093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163093" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163093-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163093-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163093" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">1.青瓜和红萝卜刨成丝。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14163091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163091" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163091-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163091-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163091" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">2.紫色洋葱切丝，牛蒡切碎，葱切丝。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14163095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163095" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163095-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163095-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163095" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">3.芝麻酱加入热水，分多次拌匀。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14163096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163096" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163096-d5640549fed2d9239ee5f59b4c184432.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163096-d5640549fed2d9239ee5f59b4c184432-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163096" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">4.接着再加入酱油、镇江醋、糖、盐、麻油和辣椒酱，搅拌均匀。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14163097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163097" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163097-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163097-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163097" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">5.青瓜丝、红萝卜丝、紫洋葱丝和牛蒡捞匀后放在盘子上，再放上熟鸡丝。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14163098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163098" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163098-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163098-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163098" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">6.海蜇丝放在顶层，放上葱丝、花生碎，淋上芝麻酱汁，吃的时候拌匀。◇</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14163100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163100" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163100-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163100-10-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163100" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
