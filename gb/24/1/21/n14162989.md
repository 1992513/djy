<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】豉汁蒸三文鱼骨豆腐～老粤菜经典味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163090-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>豉汁蒸三文鱼骨豆腐。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>鲑鱼（英语：<span lang="en">salmon</span>），亦音译为三文鱼（粤语发音）。三文鱼含有高蛋白质、ω-3脂肪酸及维他命D，被视为有益的食物。三文鱼是营养丰富的食材，富含鱼油、肉质细腻，做出的料理适合全家人一同享用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>豉汁蒸三文鱼骨豆腐</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>三文鱼骨&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.12 oz</p>
<p>布包豆腐&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.2块（每块切成四小块）</p>
<p>蒜蓉豆豉酱&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;2汤匙</p>
<p>姜、蒜蓉、干葱粒&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;少许</p>
<p>葱花&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.少许（装饰用）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味：</span></h3>
<p>蚝油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;1汤匙</p>
<p>美极鲜酱油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.1/2茶匙</p>
<p>糖&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.1茶匙</p>
<p>花雕酒&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..1汤匙</p>
<p>胡椒粉、麻油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..少许</p>
<p>粟粉&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.1.5汤匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">其它工具：</span></h3>
<p>竹蒸笼&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.1个</p>
<p>荷叶&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..1块</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14163082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163082" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163082-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163082-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163082" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.三文鱼骨洗干净后沥干水斩成小块备用</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163080" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163080-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163080-1-450x271.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163080" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.布包豆腐切八小块，所有调味材料、蒜蓉酱、姜、蒜蓉、干葱粒搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163084" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163084-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163084-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163084" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.蒸笼上铺荷叶，将豆腐放在荷叶上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163085" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163085" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163085-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163085" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163085-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163085" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.将“作法2”的调味酱料与三文鱼骨拌匀，再铺在豆腐上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163086" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163086-6f064be407e7b6d69409e603c99da77d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163086-6f064be407e7b6d69409e603c99da77d-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163086" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.烧热滚水，蒸锅中放入蒸笼，盖上锅盖，大火蒸10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163087" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163087-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163087-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163087" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.料理取出后撒上葱花便可。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163088" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163088-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163088-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163088" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
