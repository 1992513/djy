<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】素食迷你冬瓜盅 充满香味超好吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163004-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>素食迷你冬瓜盅。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>迷你<a class="key" href="https://www.soundofhope.org/term/6508?lang=b5">冬瓜</a>盅要选择一些四平八稳，表皮没有破损的为佳。用少许盐涂抹均匀后，先用清水蒸15分钟，主要是让瓜盅先入底味，以及瓜肉水分逼出一点，再重新注入素上汤，这样瓜肉就会渗入汤味，让整个瓜都充满香味及好吃。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>素食迷你冬瓜盅</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>迷你小冬瓜&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..1个（约3.5磅）</p>
<p>黄耳、雪耳、竹笙、珍珠冬菇&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..各适量</p>
<p>莲子、百合、薏米、龙眼肉干、姜片&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;各适量</p>
<p>素上汤&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;6杯</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味：</span></h3>
<p>盐&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.1/2茶匙</p>
<p>蘑菇粉&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;1茶匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">?作法：</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14162998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14162998" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162998-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14162998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162998-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14162998" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.各种材料浸泡后洗净。所有材料放进锅内，大火滚后转小火煲45分钟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14162999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14162999" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162999-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14162999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14162999-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14162999" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.冬瓜切去顶部，挖掉瓜瓤，用少许盐涂抹均匀，放在深碗内。在大锅中放蒸架。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163000" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163000-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163000-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163000" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.注入滚水至架面，放入瓜盅并在瓜盅内加滚水至满，盖上锅盖，大火炖15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163001" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163001-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163001-4-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163001" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.瓜内的水用汤勺取出后倒掉不要。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163002" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163002-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163002-5-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163002" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.所有材料和素上汤一起放入瓜盅，加盐和蘑菇粉，盖上锅盖，大火再炖35分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14163003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14163003" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163003-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14163003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14163003-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14163003" class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.带上隔热手套，小心地把冬瓜盅连碗一起取出后便可享用。◇</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>
<p>经历：</p>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年</p>
<p>2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨</p>
<p>3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨</p>
<p>4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》</p>
<p>5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
