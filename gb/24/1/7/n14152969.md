<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“烙.ＬＯＶＥ.云林”烙画联展  希望透过烙画温度，温暖每个角落</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152970-657713-600x400.jpg" />
<h6> 廖淑芬“烙.ＬＯＶＥ.云林”烙画联展，4日举行开幕茶会。文观处长陈璧君，社区大学校长何美惠， 91岁的台中市政府顾问邱伯达，宏恩医疗体系总院长吴子钧、台中扶轮社主委/理事刘英山，远智证券有限公司业务经理詹冠颀及云林艺文界人士共襄盛举 。（廖素贞／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年01月07日讯】（大纪元记者 廖素贞台湾云林报导）廖淑芬“烙.ＬＯＶＥ.云林”烙画联展，1 ?4 ?起?1 ?21 ??，在云林县政府?观处展览馆二楼展出，本次展览由社区大学讲师廖淑芬，也是中华民国烙画艺术协会理事长，带领着学员共同联展，作品多元且丰富，有人物、花鸟、昆虫走兽、还有云林在地之美，希望透过烙画温度，温暖社会每个角落。</p>
<figure id="14152981" aria-describedby="caption-14152981" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152981-657738-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152981-657738-450x300.jpg" alt="学员的作品　围着厚厚毛线的猫　相当可爱有趣　" width="500"/></a><figcaption id="caption-14152981" class="wp-caption-text">学员的作品　围着厚厚毛线的猫　相当可爱有趣　（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152971-657719.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152971-657719-600x302.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="302"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152971" class="wp-caption-text">廖淑珍特别邀请精障朋友参与开幕演出。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从事护理工作的廖淑芬，民国96(2007)年在偶然的机会接触烙画，就被烙画古朴典雅之美所吸引，一头栽进烙画领域，追随启蒙老师林滋瑛学起烙画来，一开始只是抱着好玩的心态，把作品当礼物来与人分享。97 (2008)年开始投?烙画艺术创作，102(2013) 年回到故乡云林在社区大学担任烙画讲师，廖淑芬教学认真待人诚恳，师生间亦师亦友的和谐关系，让学生在没有压力下，可以自由发挥、快乐学习。他的学生来自各行各业及不同的年龄层，有家庭主妇，董事长、退休的校长老师、警察；年龄有20多岁，5、60岁的，最高94岁，从这次展出的作品，可以看出学员的水准都很高，作品令人惊艳！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152972-657722.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14152972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152972-657722-600x389.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="389"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152972" class="wp-caption-text">廖淑芬和学员及来宾在展场合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152980" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152980-657736.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14152980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152980-657736-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="398" b="597"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152980" class="wp-caption-text">学员创作的五字箴言作品“顺”。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152983" style="width: 518px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152983-657741.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14152983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152983-657741-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="518" b="345"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152983" class="wp-caption-text">廖淑芬结合书法与烙画的作品 “ 花若盛开蝶自来”。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>热心公益的廖淑芬，多年来把烙画被珍藏所得，捐献给宏恩社会福利慈善基金会，帮助一群被遗弃的精神障碍者，是她最令人敬佩的地方，开幕茶会当天，她特别邀请这群精障朋友，参与非洲鼓演出，目的就是让他们有融入社会的机会，从中能得到自我的肯定！廖淑芬说，为了这次的演出，他们特别提早一天来演练！可见他们是多么珍惜这次的活动！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152982" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152982-657740.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14152982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152982-657740-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="524" b="349"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152982" class="wp-caption-text">云林出产的文心兰（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>廖淑芬表示，烙画不仅有中国画的勾、勒、点、染、擦、白 描等手法，还可以烫出丰富的层次与色调，在技法上宜中宜西，可以呈现如中国水墨画，也可以像西画般多元变化万千，烙画艺术独特的味道，会让人不自主的着迷，而沉浸在烙画的世界里！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14152975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152975" style="width: 495px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152975-657725.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14152975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152975-657725-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="495" b="330"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152975" class="wp-caption-text">学员创作的生活作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14152979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14152979" style="width: 383px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152979-657735.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14152979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14152979-657735-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="383" b="575"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14152979" class="wp-caption-text">学员展出的作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>文化观光处长陈璧君表示，这次展出作品约有50余件，包含22位会员近年精彩的烙画作品，充满云林各地特色，烙画是一门非常生活化的艺术。理事长廖淑芬老师数年来致力推广烙画艺术，举办许多烙画培训课程与师资认证，提供云林在地民众一个更接近烙画的机会。欢迎云林乡亲及喜好艺术的朋友们于展出期间(1月4日至1月21日)一同来文化观光处展览馆观展。</p>
