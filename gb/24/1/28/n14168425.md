<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】南瓜疙瘩~奶油鼠尾草酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168427-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新鲜南瓜制作的面疙瘩口感Q弹，搭配甜、咸汤汁都美味。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年01月28日讯】面粉疙瘩汤的做法甜咸都有。今天分享一个新鲜南瓜泥制作面疙瘩的食谱，咸味版本搭配的是奶油鼠尾草酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14168430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14168430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168430-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14168430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168430-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14168430" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>150克+3汤匙的日本南瓜泥</p>
<p>60克普通面粉</p>
<p>1/4茶匙盐</p>
<p>6片新鲜鼠尾草叶子</p>
<p>1汤匙黄油（butter）</p>
<p>2杯水</p>
<p><strong>料理贴士</strong>：混合面团时不需要揉面，轻轻折叠面团几次看不见干粉即可。一旦揉面就要多加干粉，疙瘩会变成硬块不软糯。</p>
<p>面团最后分切成小块下锅。这里使用叉子给面团造型。做法：先将面团分成数份，再搓成手指粗的长条并切成1英寸左右小块。最后把面块放在叉子背面压扁，再向下滚动卷起，疙瘩表面就有凹痕了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14168432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14168432" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168432-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14168432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168432-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14168432" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1. 一个2磅的日本南瓜洗净，整个放入高压锅蒸。上蒸汽后蒸16分钟。出锅晾凉取瓜肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14168433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14168433" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168433-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14168433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168433-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14168433" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 用机器将南瓜泥打成细蓉。取150克瓜泥与水、盐混合成面团。再留出3汤匙待用，其余瓜泥冷冻保存。<br />
3. 混合时，先将瓜泥平铺，撒一层面粉后折叠并轻轻按压面粉，重复3~5次看不见干粉即可。用盘子盖住面团，静置10分钟。<br />
4. 把面团分成小块。<br />
5. 平底锅加热后关火。放入黄油、鼠尾草拌匀，摆好南瓜疙瘩后开火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14168434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14168434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168434-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14168434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168434-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14168434" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 待鼠尾草散发香气，疙瘩稍微煎出金黄色时，倒入2杯水和3汤匙南瓜泥煮沸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14168435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14168435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168435-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14168435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168435-6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14168435" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 沸腾5分钟后尝一下疙瘩是否煮熟，熟了就加盐调味出锅。想要汤汁浓稠可久煮。最后撒一些帕马森起司享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14168436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14168436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168436-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14168436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168436-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14168436" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14168437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14168437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168437-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14168437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14168437-8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14168437" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>南瓜疙瘩糖水：</h2>
<p>南瓜疙瘩也可以煮甜食。做法是先煮一锅糖水，放入南瓜疙瘩煮熟便可。◇</p>
