<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世界上最大打击乐器：洞穴中的钟乳石管风琴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id14148829-69489-organ-cave-675-94568-9739-1080x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>弗吉尼亚州雪兰多山谷的大钟乳石管风琴。(Shutterstock，公共领域)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年01月01日讯】（英文大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／赵孜济编译）世界上最大的乐器是位于弗吉尼亚州的雪兰多山谷（Shenandoah Valley）的大钟乳石管风琴（the Great Stalacpipe Orga），它深藏于美国东部最大的洞穴系列，卢雷洞穴（Luray Caverns）。它是自然与人类工程学的和谐融合。</p>
<p>这个宏伟的管风琴是由多个地下钟乳石组成的，其操作就像儿童的音乐盒，按下键盘会使橡胶头的木槌敲击经过精心挑选的钟乳石，从而在地下产生高昂和谐的声音。</p>
<p>这个令人难以置信的仪器是曾在五角大楼工作的数学家和电子科学家利兰?斯普林克尔(Leland W. Sprinkle)先生的心血结晶。1954年，来自弗吉尼亚州斯普林菲尔德的斯普林克尔先生带着他5岁的儿子参观卢雷洞穴。当时他看到导游用木槌敲击钟乳石地层，发出令人印象深刻的声音，不同的尺寸的钟乳石有不同的音调，聪明的科学家立即意识到钟乳石创造音乐的潜力。</p>
<figure style="width: 593px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552735-677945789-organ-cave-6759739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552735-677945789-organ-cave-6759739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="593" b="474" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">弗吉尼亚州雪兰多山谷的大钟乳石管风琴。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552733-677945789-organ-cave-894568-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552733-677945789-organ-cave-894568-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="597" b="398" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一张1906年拍摄的老照片展示了大钟乳石管风琴的一些早期音乐表演。（公共领域）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导游演奏了“玛丽有一只小羊羔”和其它简单的歌曲。斯普林克尔先生联系了洞穴管理员，并提出了洞穴风琴的构想。大自然的和谐音乐激发了他的灵感，他开始打磨自己的想法并付诸实现。</p>
<p>周末的晚上，他在地下度过，用13个英式音叉对声音进行采样，以找到与音阶相匹配的钟乳石。斯普林克先生的任务是让这些悬挂在天花板上的矿物地层演奏音乐。</p>
<figure style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552737-67794789-organ-cave-675-894568-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552737-67794789-organ-cave-675-894568-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="595" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大钟乳石管风琴的键盘。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 590px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552742-6745789-organ-cave-675-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552742-6745789-organ-cave-675-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="590" b="394" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洞穴内部的细节显示了与钟乳石相连的木槌系统。(Shutterstock;插图：公共领域)</figcaption></figure>
<p>传统的管风琴使空气通过专门校准尺寸的柱子来控制音高，而斯普林克先生则花费了大量时间研磨经过挑选的钟乳石的尖端来微调声音。</p>
<p>他利用了橡皮槌和管风琴的概念，创造了一种全新的乐器，一种听众在演奏时站在里面的乐器。</p>
<figure style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552736-67789-organ-cave-894568-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552736-67789-organ-cave-894568-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="597" b="398" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">键盘。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552743-645789-organ-cave-675-88-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F12%2F24%2Fid5552743-645789-organ-cave-675-88-9739.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="597" b="398" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洞穴特征的细节，包括钟乳石。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>花了三年时间，他终于完成了自己的梦想。大钟乳石管风琴位于洞穴系统的大教堂内，不同长度和厚度的钟乳石分布在3.5英亩的土地上，发出美妙的声音。</p>
<p>偶尔，管风琴师会在巡回演出中展示世界上最独特的管风琴的超凡脱俗的声音。按下一个键发送一个电脉冲，使橡胶头的木槌敲击钟乳石，由此产生的振动发出令人难以置信的美妙音乐。</p>
<p>这些音乐平静和舒缓。管风琴家们说，大教堂厅的氛围适合贝多芬的《月光奏鸣曲》和巴赫的一些前奏曲等作品。</p>
