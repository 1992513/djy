<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】蓝莓马芬顶</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333631-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>爆浆蓝莓马芬顶酸酸甜甜，喜欢马芬的朋友不要错过。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年09月18日讯】酥松的马芬顶（Muffin Tops）是最好吃的部分，搭配蓝莓烤出爆浆效果更加疗愈。这里介绍一款迷你版本的蓝莓马芬顶，每吃一口都是酸酸甜甜，喜欢马芬的朋友不要错过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14333632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333632-2-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333632-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333632" class="wp-caption-text">制作蓝莓马芬顶的主要食材。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>60克无漂白中筋面粉<br />
60克有机全麦面粉（whole wheat）<br />
1/4茶匙小苏打<br />
1/4茶匙无铝双效泡打粉<br />
1/8茶匙盐<br />
1/3杯细白砂糖<br />
3汤匙黄油（butter，奶油）<br />
1/3杯普通原味酸奶<br />
适量新鲜蓝莓、粗糖</p>
<h2>料理步骤：</h2>
<p>1. 预热烤箱400华氏度（约204摄氏度）。</p>
<p>2. 准备12个迷你马芬纸杯和一个浅边烤盘。</p>
<p>3. 在搅拌碗中，将列表中前6项食材混合均匀，直到看起来均匀蓬松。</p>
<p>4. 冰箱取出黄油，用擦丝器在搅拌碗上方擦成粗丝，再用餐叉边压边搅拌，使黄油混合干粉搓成均匀小颗粒。</p>
<p>5. 冰箱取出冷酸奶拌入干粉，也是边压边拌，拌至看不见干粉即可。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14333633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333633-3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333633-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333633" class="wp-caption-text">图为黄油擦丝和搅拌好的面糊。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 使用面糊勺分配面糊。每团面糊摆上蓝莓，同时压扁面糊，最后表面撒粗糖即可烘烤。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14333635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333635-4-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333635-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333635" class="wp-caption-text">摆放蓝莓的面糊。下图：刚出炉的马芬顶。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 400华氏度烤15分钟。出炉放架子上冷却即可。</p>
