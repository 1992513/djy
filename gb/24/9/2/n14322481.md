<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】咖喱洋葱芦笋素鸡片～超级下饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322530-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>超级下饭的家常菜，一盘就把营养补足。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="caret-color: #008000;">小编语：一锅到底就完成的好料理！超级下饭之外，有丰富的蔬菜和蛋白质，不喜欢吃菜的孩子，看到缤纷的蔬菜颜色也会垂延三尺。这一道菜肴是连厨房新手都很好上手，赶快来试试看吧！</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>洋葱1/2个<br />
芦笋10 oz<br />
红椒3 oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许<br />
素鸡片10 oz<br />
咖喱油酱1又1/2汤匙<br />
粟粉水2汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14322531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322531" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322531-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14322531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322531-1-450x338.jpg" alt="材料（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322531" class="wp-caption-text">材料（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味：</span></h3>
<p>黄姜粉1茶匙<br />
糖1茶匙<br />
盐1/2茶匙<br />
水1杯</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></h3>
<p>1. 芦笋切掉1/4末端硬梗，刨掉少许外皮切段备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14322532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322532" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322532-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14322532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322532-2-450x338.jpg" alt="芦笋切掉1/4末端硬梗，刨掉少许外皮切段备用。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322532" class="wp-caption-text">芦笋切掉1/4末端硬梗，刨掉少许外皮切段备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 洋葱切小片，红椒切片备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14322533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322533" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322533-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14322533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322533-3-450x338.jpg" alt="洋葱切小片，红椒切片备用。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322533" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱切小片，红椒切片备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 素鸡片用姜汁酒烫，同时加入芦笋一起煮1分钟，捞出沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14322534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322534-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14322534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322534-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322534" class="wp-caption-text">素鸡片用姜汁酒烫，同时加入芦笋一起煮1分钟，捞出沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、咖喱油酱，放入洋葱炒香，赞酒加入水一杯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14322537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322537-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14322537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322537-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322537" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、咖喱油酱，放入洋葱炒香，赞酒加入水一杯。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放入调味料，加入芦笋、素鸡和红椒，煮半分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14322538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322538-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14322538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322538-6-450x338.jpg" alt="放入调味料，加入芦笋、素鸡和红椒，煮半分钟。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322538" class="wp-caption-text">放入调味料，加入芦笋、素鸡和红椒，煮半分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 最后再用粟粉水勾芡便可上碟。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14322539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322539-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14322539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322539-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322539" class="wp-caption-text">最后再用粟粉水勾芡便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
