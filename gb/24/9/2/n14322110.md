<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全湾区最大新疆餐厅 真正碳烤全羊</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322150-f1d12ce438b3fe6d04e40bf277f81072-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>湾区新疆菜良汤火锅有众多经典清真、新疆美食。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月02日讯】文：工商讯<br />
新疆维吾尔族的美丽、善良、勤劳、热情的老板娘，在新疆开餐馆有近20年的历史，六十多家的家族火锅连锁店，火锅底料还是家族秘制，从新疆托运过来湾区，值得尝鲜。</p>
<p>到HalalStreet Hot Pot &#038; Xinjiang Cuisine用餐，我朋友提议到宽阔明亮的露天前院吃火锅，头顶上有夏天刚刚开过杏黄色花的青藤、绿叶。真享受！</p>
<p>火锅是浓郁的自制（活鲫鱼、牛骨）汤底，鲜嫩的肥羊卷、厚实的牛肉卷、新鲜的带头海虾、羊肚、鱼块、翠绿的各种蔬菜、新鲜的玉米、南瓜、藕片??二十几种火锅新鲜食材，额外加清炖羊肉、羊肉或牛肉蒸包、奶茶、水果茶、水果、点心??坐着吃到饱，开心极了！</p>
<p>店家不只火锅吃到饱，只要食客们不浪费食物，花费满$50还送$5代金券。</p>
<p>这里提供的羊肉，是开车两个半小时，从Napa牧场现宰杀、放血。保持新疆的清真。</p>
<p>我亲眼目睹了年轻能干的老板亲自操作烤全羊，刚刚买来大陆制造的大型碳烤炉，放上腌制一夜的整只全羊，在炭火烧烤下发出滋滋的诱人声音；羊油流到木碳上烧出明亮火焰的美景下，不断地翻转着，老板不断地用刀切割着羊肉，并撒上自制的调料。<figure id="attachment_14322128" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322128-3b9f2c1f3f8fe0b6b0fe10036d7ecbb7.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322128-3b9f2c1f3f8fe0b6b0fe10036d7ecbb7-600x355.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="355" class="size-large wp-image-14322128" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322128" class="wp-caption-text">烤全羊。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>终于等到了烤羊出炉，一行5人的食客正好看见刚出炉的热腾腾、黄澄澄、香喷喷的烤全羊，抢先一步订了一个羊大腿，才article content begin -->68。接下来，热情好客的老板娘帮我们5人端来一盘连骨带肉，冒着香喷喷热气的羊肉！<figure id="attachment_14322129" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322129" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322129-467143e7eb8cfb499d0a5c7d9c8198fb.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322129-467143e7eb8cfb499d0a5c7d9c8198fb-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-14322129" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322129" class="wp-caption-text">羊大腿。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>来美国这么多年，第一次吃到好吃、没有腥味的全羊，外酥里嫩，紧贴脊椎骨的肉都是松软的，明火烟熏火烤的孜然味道，让每个人都停不下嘴来，一会儿就抢食一光！</p>
<p>老板娘还安排了正宗新疆清真大厨，做了多道经典菜肴：大盘鸡、椒麻鸡、胡辣羊蹄、新疆拌面、烤包子、羊肉串、干煸炒面、新疆米粉、丁丁炒面、馕炒肉、黄面烤肉、馕包肉、羊腿抓饭、回民丸子汤??</p>
<p><strong>1、羊肉串：</strong>一小块羊肉软弹可口，纯正的小时候农村老家爷爷做的烤羊肉的味道，真的好吃！<figure id="attachment_14322130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322130" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322130-84b89c3a6ca27e288357a038a0e75c39.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322130-84b89c3a6ca27e288357a038a0e75c39-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-14322130" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322130" class="wp-caption-text">羊肉串。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>2、黄面烤肉：</strong>老板用新鲜牛奶和面，正宗的馕坑烤出来的馕，用整方块的羊肉加一点青红椒，炒出来的馕包肉，吃一口羊肉、吃一口吸足了羊肉味的馕，再夹一块青椒，美味极了。还有木耳、洋葱，又香又爽口。<figure id="attachment_14322132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322132-128ab47a8d635fbd4d44abeb76f49629.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322132-128ab47a8d635fbd4d44abeb76f49629-600x414.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="414" class="size-large wp-image-14322132" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322132" class="wp-caption-text">黄面烤肉。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>3、馕炒肉：</strong>满满的一长盘，非常实惠，馕炒得非常香，好像小时候吃过妈妈做的炸馒头片。羊肉一片一片的，吃一块馕、吃一片羊肉好舒服，一份两个人可以吃好饱，好像回家了的感觉。<figure id="attachment_14322145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322145-c621c5b5badc01c52d4c6c28f76db6df.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322145-c621c5b5badc01c52d4c6c28f76db6df-600x389.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="389" class="size-large wp-image-14322145" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322145" class="wp-caption-text">馕炒肉。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>4、烤包子：</strong>正宗的新疆烤羊肉包子，是我的最爱！以前吃过别家的新疆烤包子，一般都是牛肉包子，吃起来肉硬硬的。这个吃起来，羊肉馅软软的、清淡的羊肉香味，带一点点淡淡的洋葱味道，烤包子皮也是外酥里嫩，有嚼劲，味道好棒！<figure id="attachment_14322146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322146-595fe8b589bb2e686df45fc8ea4ef44c.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322146-595fe8b589bb2e686df45fc8ea4ef44c-600x535.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="535" class="size-large wp-image-14322146" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322146" class="wp-caption-text">烤包子。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>5、馕：</strong>老板娘介绍，用新鲜牛奶和面做成的，用传统的馕坑明火碳烤的馕，非常好吃，让我想起了小时候，只有学校开运动会需要带饭，妈妈才肯给出二两粮票加五分钱人民币，买一个的中国国营饭店才有的火烧。就是非常像那个味。所以离开餐馆时，又跟老板娘要了一块带回家。<figure id="attachment_14322148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322148-77e5ca6b5c52b64f0e1cd8fad70a928e.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322148-77e5ca6b5c52b64f0e1cd8fad70a928e-600x351.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="351" class="size-large wp-image-14322148" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322148" class="wp-caption-text">馕。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>6、大盘鸡：</strong>真够大的盘，老板娘介绍，选材非常重要，不能用柴鸡、又不能用急速生长的肉食鸡，所以，吃起来硬软适中，伴随着宽宽的手拉面，每一口都是家乡味，而且价格非常实惠。<figure id="attachment_14322149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14322149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322149-824fb8a5968bba254647c12059da4253.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14322149-824fb8a5968bba254647c12059da4253-600x388.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="388" class="size-large wp-image-14322149" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14322149" class="wp-caption-text">大盘鸡。（新疆菜良汤火锅提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>剩下的道道新疆美食，大家自己来品尝，保你喜出望外??湾区真正的新疆清真美食。</p>
<p>老板娘亲切地说：“很快推出手工水饺三种：羊肉、牛肉、海鲜，用做月子餐的经验，不放任何调料，用新鲜食材的自然清纯的鲜香，我就让顾客、特别是趴在电脑旁忙了一天的工作人员，吃出自家妈妈的味道。全部培训全体员工包水饺，手工擀皮、手工包，我自己调馅。保证食材的新鲜最重要。”◇</p>
