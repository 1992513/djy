<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】蒜苗炒黄鳝丝～香气十足</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327159-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【小编语】鳝鱼有丰富的DHA和卵磷脂等营养成分。中国传统菜系里有“炒鳝丝”这一道菜，看似朴实，吃起来就香气十足、口感丰富，而且简单好料理，是不可多得的家常菜。</p>
<h3>主材料：</h3>
<p>蒜苗6安士（Garlic shoots）<br />
急冻黄鳝丝8安士（eel shredded）<br />
姜、蒜片、红椒各少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327146" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327146-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327146-1-450x338.jpg" alt="蒜苗、急冻黄鳝丝、姜、蒜片、红椒。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327146" class="wp-caption-text">材料（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>黄鳝丝腌料：</h3>
<p>姜、蒜蓉少许<br />
花雕酒1汤匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
生抽1茶匙</p>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>鸡汤1/3杯<br />
生抽1茶匙<br />
糖1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油各少许<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙</p>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 黄鳝解冻后，用水冲洗2分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327147" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327147-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327147-2-450x600.jpg" alt="黄鳝解冻后，用水冲洗2分钟。" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327147" class="wp-caption-text">黄鳝解冻后，用水冲洗2分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 黄鳝沥干水，用腌料拌匀腌15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327149" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327149-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327149-3-450x338.jpg" alt="黄鳝沥干水，用腌料拌匀腌15分钟。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327149" class="wp-caption-text">黄鳝沥干水，用腌料拌匀腌15分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蒜苗切成4公分长段，冲洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327150-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327150-4-450x338.jpg" alt="蒜苗切成4公分长段，冲洗干净备用。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327150" class="wp-caption-text">蒜苗切成4公分长段，冲洗干净备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，把黄鳝丝炒至八成熟，约1分半钟，盛出沥干油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327152" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327152-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327152-5-450x338.jpg" alt="热锅下油，把黄鳝丝炒至八成熟，约1分半钟，盛出沥干油。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327152" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，把黄鳝丝炒至八成熟，约1分半钟，盛出沥干油。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再次热锅下油，放入姜、蒜片和蒜苗一起翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327153" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327153-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327153-6-450x253.jpg" alt="再次热锅下油，放入姜、蒜片和蒜苗一起翻炒。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327153" class="wp-caption-text">再次热锅下油，放入姜、蒜片和蒜苗一起翻炒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 加入少许盐和1/4杯水，炒1分半钟。再放回黄鳝，加入红椒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327154" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327154-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327154-7-450x338.jpg" alt="加入少许盐和1/4杯水，炒1分半钟。再放回黄鳝，加入红椒。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327154" class="wp-caption-text">加入少许盐和1/4杯水，炒1分半钟。再放回黄鳝，加入红椒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 倒入调味汁液，翻炒均匀便可上碟。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327155" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327155-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14327155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327155-8-450x338.jpg" alt="倒入调味汁液，翻炒均匀便可上碟。◇" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327155" class="wp-caption-text">倒入调味汁液，翻炒均匀便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
