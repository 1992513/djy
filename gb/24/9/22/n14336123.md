<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】丝瓜鲜百合炒素鸡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336124-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>丝瓜鲜百合炒素鸡。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：素菜常见食材脱离不了豆类食品，这次使用素鸡片和丝瓜、百合、红萝卜进行热炒，步骤简单不伤脑筋，零厨艺都能上手的好食谱。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>丝瓜1磅<br />
鲜百合1个6 oz<br />
素鸡片10 oz<br />
红萝卜花10片<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14336127" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14336127" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336127-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14336127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336127-1-450x338.jpg" alt="材料。（梁厨美食）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14336127" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁厨美食）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">调味：</span></h3>
<p>水1/3杯<br />
素蚝油1汤匙<br />
糖1/4茶匙<br />
烹大师1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">作法：</span></h3>
<p>1. 丝瓜刨去外皮，切小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14336130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14336130" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336130-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14336130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336130-2-450x338.jpg" alt="丝瓜刨去外皮，切小块备用。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14336130" class="wp-caption-text">丝瓜刨去外皮，切小块备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 鲜百合剥出一片一片，洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14336129" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14336129" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336129-b3916b8fca7cc563f0dbedab6c76fcf2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14336129" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336129-b3916b8fca7cc563f0dbedab6c76fcf2-450x338.jpg" alt="鲜百合剥出一片一片，洗干净备用。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14336129" class="wp-caption-text">鲜百合剥出一片一片，洗干净备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 素鸡片和红萝卜花用姜汁酒烫过，捞起沥干水备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14336131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14336131" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336131-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14336131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336131-4-450x338.jpg" alt="素鸡片和红萝卜花用姜汁酒烫过，捞起沥干水备用。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14336131" class="wp-caption-text">素鸡片和红萝卜花用姜汁酒烫过，捞起沥干水备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，放入姜、蒜蓉翻炒，再加入丝瓜、鲜百合和水1/4杯一起翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14336132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14336132" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336132-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14336132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336132-5-450x253.jpg" alt="热锅下油，放入姜、蒜蓉翻炒，再加入丝瓜、鲜百合和水1/4杯一起翻炒。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14336132" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，放入姜、蒜蓉翻炒，再加入丝瓜、鲜百合和水1/4杯一起翻炒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再加入素鸡和红萝卜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14336134" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14336134" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336134-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14336134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336134-6-450x338.jpg" alt="加入素鸡和红萝卜。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14336134" class="wp-caption-text">加入素鸡和红萝卜。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 搅匀调味汁，一边倒入一边翻炒均匀后便可上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14336135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14336135" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336135-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14336135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336135-7-450x338.jpg" alt="搅匀调味汁，一边倒入一边翻炒均匀后便可上碟。（梁志生提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14336135" class="wp-caption-text">搅匀调味汁，一边倒入一边翻炒均匀后便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>小贴士：</strong></span></h3>
<p>? 丝瓜外皮很硬，要刨去大部分，只留少许绿色皮。<br />
? 素鸡片是在素食店购买的，已经切成一片片，使用上很方便。◇</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
