<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>政府修订西澳天然气政策</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14336803-eff5baa654c8ca960c43126964c953a0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>周四（9月19日），西澳政府公布了西澳天然气修订政策。图为2013年9月24日， 必和必拓在西澳 Onslow 开设的Macedon天然气工厂。(AAP Image/Supplied by BHP Billiton ）。
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年09月23日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者正玉澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">周四（</span><span lang="en-AU">9</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">19</span><span lang="zh-TW">日），西澳政府公</span><span lang="zh-TW">布</span><span lang="zh-TW">了西澳天然气修订政策。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据新规</span><span lang="zh-TW">，从现在到</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2030</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">12</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">31</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">日，西澳将实行</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">80</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的陆上天然气储备政策（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">onshore</span> <span lang="en-AU">gas</span> <span lang="en-AU">reservation</span> <span lang="en-AU">policy,</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">），允许</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">20</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的天然气用于出口，然而从</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2031</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年起，陆上天然气项目必须为西澳市场保留</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">100</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的天然气产量。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">西澳前州长麦高恩（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Mark</span> <span lang="en-AU">McGowan</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）于</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2020</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年下令禁止从西澳开采的陆上天然气出口到海外或东部，以降低</span><span lang="zh-TW">西澳本</span><span lang="zh-TW">地能源价格。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">唯一获得豁免的陆上天然气项目是媒体大亨斯托克斯（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Kerry</span> <span lang="en-AU">Stokes</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）的</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Beach</span> <span lang="en-AU">Energy</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Mitsui</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">在西澳中西部地区的</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Waitsia</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">气田项目，从</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2023</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年开始，该气田已获得通过伍德赛德（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Woodside</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）的卡拉塔天然气厂（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Karratha</span> <span lang="en-AU">Gas</span> <span lang="en-AU">Plant</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）向海外出口天然气的绿灯。</span></p>
<p>儅<span lang="en-AU">Waitsia</span>气田获得豁免后，其它陆上天然气公司也纷纷要求获得类似待遇，并警告说如果不向国际市场销售，他们的项目将无法生存。</p>
<p>州政府周四（<span lang="en-AU">9</span>月<span lang="en-AU">19</span>日）宣布了上述新政。修订后的政策适用于新的陆上天然气项目或寻求扩大生产的现有项目。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据新政策，州政府</span><span lang="zh-TW">还</span><span lang="zh-TW">将每年发布一份《西澳天然气声明》，以提高天然气开采商是否满足国内天然气需求的透明度。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“我们的天然气属于西澳人”，库克说，“我希望确保我们的天然气资源能为西澳带来最大的利益。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">第一份《西澳天然气声明》的副本显示，伍德赛德公司（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Woodside</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）的</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Pluto</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">项目将在</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2023</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年供应</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">31</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">千万焦耳（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Petajoules</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）的国内天然气，而雪佛龙公司（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Chevron</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）的戈尔工（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Gorgon</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）天然气项目将供应</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">221</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">千万焦耳（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Petajoules</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">新政策受到反对党与业界的欢迎，反对党能源厅发言人托马斯（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Steve</span> <span lang="en-AU">Thomas</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，新政策为陆上项目提供了更大的确定性。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲能源生产商协会西澳分会主管切里女士（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Caroline</span> <span lang="en-AU">Cherry</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，这</span><span lang="zh-TW">（新政）</span><span lang="zh-TW">将使更多的项目可行，同时巩固西澳天然气市场供应。</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：杨新云</p>
