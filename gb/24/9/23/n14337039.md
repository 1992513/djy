<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>休士顿客家会中秋聚餐联谊会欢乐举行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14337044-afcb878f186d20296337913d613a5e53-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>客家会中秋聚餐联谊会开心合影。（图/客家会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年09月24日讯】（大纪元讯）9月21日（星期六）上午11:30，休士顿客家会在糖城JUICE Seafood Buffet举行“2024年度的中秋聚餐联谊会”，来自大休斯地区乡亲逾60人聚一堂，欢度中秋佳节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14337042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14337042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14337042-38c68b769f08887e4b370222ea25ca40.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14337042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14337042-38c68b769f08887e4b370222ea25ca40-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14337042" class="wp-caption-text">左至右依序是赖江椿、梁慎平、汪干雄、黄营炎及乡亲合影。（图/客家会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长詹焕彩首先致词问候所有的乡亲，并祝大家中秋佳节快乐，接着报告客家会至今年底将举办的各项活动节目，并且预告好消息，由休士顿客家会连系，台湾国宝级的剧团“郑荣兴剧团”，明年将莅临休斯顿演出。现场一片欢呼，大家都非常期待这剧团的来访。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14337043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14337043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14337043-83e17708edab40cd1a4356441052eabf.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14337043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14337043-83e17708edab40cd1a4356441052eabf-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14337043" class="wp-caption-text">客家会之妇女会团队合影。（图/客家会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14337045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14337045" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14337045-2c7af242df49507542bc41736b5bbdaf.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14337045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14337045-2c7af242df49507542bc41736b5bbdaf-600x469.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="469" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14337045" class="wp-caption-text">休士顿客家会中秋聚餐联谊歌唱表演。（图/客家会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着介绍新的会员，并表示热烈的欢迎；活动中安排有抽奖活动，以及中秋月饼品尝；看到堆积如山的奖品，詹焕彩感谢乡亲热烈的提供奖品，当天是人人都有奖，大家欢乐的度过非常美好的中秋佳节。</p>
