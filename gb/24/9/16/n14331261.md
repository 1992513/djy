<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>二宫和也说“成为岚好幸运” 竹内凉真想加入</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331321-240916030219100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>二宫和也与竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 - 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年09月16日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）二宫和也与竹内凉真13日为了日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》的见面会活动造访台湾，吸引超过800粉丝到松山机场接机。在举办见面会期间，二宫说成为“ARASHI岚”是他人生中最幸运的事，这让竹内凉真羡慕不已。</p>
<p>两人对台湾粉丝的热情惊喜不已。二宫笑说，因为他所属的团体“ARASHI岚”目前停止活动，本来担心不知道会有多少粉丝会用什么形式迎接他，结果看到这么多人在机场跟见面会现场，让他顿时感到安心，“原来我还是蛮有人气的啊！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331363" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331363" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331363-240916030240100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331363" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331363-240916030240100707-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331363" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14331350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331350" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331350-240916030300100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331350" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331350-240916030300100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331350" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>竹内凉真则是甜言蜜语表示，因为是难得的台湾行，自己在飞机上列了很多想去的地方，但一下飞机看到接机的大家就全忘光光，“满脑子只记得你们而已！谢谢！”这番话引来粉丝鼓掌与欢呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331348-240916030256100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331348" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331348-240916030256100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331348" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也与竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>二宫和也在两季《黑色止血钳》中都担任主演，他在第一季演出性格孤僻、手腕高超的外科医生“渡海征司郎”，第二季时并非以渡海身份回归，而是饰演技术同样精湛、长相与渡海十分相似，但顶着一头银发的拜金医生“天城雪彦”。这让本来听到原班人马回归，以为自己也可以重拾原角色的二宫感到惊讶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331364" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331364-240916030235100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331364" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331364-240916030235100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331364" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也与竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他在接受访问时表示，第二季内容有非常多医疗戏与手术场面，困难度比起第一季提升数倍，其中一场大型公开手术场面的拍摄场次，追求完美的众人同场飙戏，竟耗时五天拍摄才让导演与制作人点头，现在回想起来是非常难得的珍贵回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331351" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331351-240916030231100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331351" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331351-240916030231100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331351" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而之前曾发生拍摄期间二宫不小心将价格昂贵的拍摄用手表带回家，在发觉后紧急归还的糗事。饰演与恶魔同行的暖男医生“世良雅志”的竹内凉真说，自己虽然没有类似的状况，但因为他会把外科手术缝合组借回家练习打结，最近被剧组叮咛“差不多要记得还喔”，让他胆战心惊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331352" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331352-240916030221100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331352" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331352-240916030221100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331352" class="wp-caption-text">竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于其他关于拍摄的小趣事，竹内凉真说，同组演员内野圣阳非常怕热，所以只要现场突然将空调温度调得很低，就代表内野快到现场，要准备拍摄内野饰演的佐伯医生戏份了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331365" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331365-240916030226100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331365" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331365-240916030226100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331365" class="wp-caption-text">竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他还透露，小泉孝太郎会把戏服的刷手服当睡衣穿，好像很喜欢的样子等等。他也对演员们下戏后会一起约去吃饭、谁挑食等小插曲如数家珍，足见剧组众演员之间的好感情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331354" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331354-240916030247100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331354" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331354-240916030247100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331354" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也与竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于剧中设定患者必须先拿出一半财产与天城对赌，幸运赢了才能接受手术，被反问目前为止遇过最幸运的事时，二宫毫不犹豫的说：“一定是成为‘岚’。”他感性表示因为自己有幸成为岚的一员，才能与许多人事物有珍贵的邂逅，真的十分感谢。这番话让竹内凉真羡慕不已，撒娇喊说他也想要加入‘岚’，逗乐粉丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331356" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331356-240916030244100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331356" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331356-240916030244100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331356" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也与竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当被问到觉得若要对任性到令人恨得牙痒痒的天城恶作剧，世良该怎么做比较恰当时，竹内凉真顽皮地说，不如每被惹生气一次，就把天城办公室内的昂贵家具与艺术品摆设偷偷卖一件掉好了，这番回答让一旁的二宫大笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331357" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331357" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331357-240916030258100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331357" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331357-240916030258100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331357" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谈及本周即将播出《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》最后一集，二宫表示虽然拍摄期间相当辛苦，但戏剧要结束了还是感到有点寂寞，“也有一种好好将戏拍完，任务终于达成的实感”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331358" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331358-240916030242100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331358" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331358-240916030242100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331358" class="wp-caption-text">二宫和也与竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
<p>竹内凉真则说，“其实有时候觉得拍摄很累，想说这场手术戏到底要拍到几点啊，还曾萌生放弃努力的想法。”</p>
<p>但对于即将迎来最高潮结局的《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》，他同样也有点失落，“不过能在结束前来到台北，能看到真心喜欢这部戏的大家，我觉得很开心”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14331359" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14331359" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331359-240916030251100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14331359" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331359-240916030251100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14331359" class="wp-caption-text">竹内凉真为日剧《黑色止血钳2 &#8211; 恶魔外科医生 -》访台举办特别见面会照。（大鸿艺术BIG ART）</figcaption></figure>
