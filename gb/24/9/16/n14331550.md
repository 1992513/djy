<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>秒杀地瓜球轻松做 关键窍门一次看</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331551-P_20240811_153802-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>地瓜球。（郎之家简单美味家常菜提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>夜市人气小吃地瓜球，做法看似简单，但要炸得好吃却是很大的学问，除了要用网勺反复翻压油锅里的地瓜面团，过筛、油温等也处处藏着窍门。</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">自制地瓜球，我经过了几次失败</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，有时候膨得起来，有时候无法膨胀；有时候口感偏硬，有时候偏软……，最终</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">我终于找到了失败的关键窍门––面团</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，经过软硬度调整后，果然一次就成功了！地瓜球一上桌，孩子秒杀吃光光。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>秒杀地瓜球</b></span></h2>
<h3><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">地瓜&#8230;200g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">糖&#8230;&#8230;.4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">木薯粉..70g</span></p>
<p><strong>注意：</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">木薯粉的比例只是参考值</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="color: #800000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">实际得看地瓜蒸熟后的出水量</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，来调整</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">木薯粉的用量</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">这次地瓜蒸出来水分比较多，所以用了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">70g</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">木薯</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">粉。</span></p>
<h3><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法：</span></strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.将地瓜削皮</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">切块</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.把切好的地瓜放入锅中蒸</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加入糖，外锅放一杯水。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.将蒸熟的地瓜搅拌成泥。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">地瓜</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">放冷后（微温也可以）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><b>逐步</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">添</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加木薯粉</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，并揉成</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面团</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><strong>小窍门：</strong><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">理想的面团是不黏手，且表面光滑。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">也就是说，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">如果面团是黏的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">需再</span><b>慢慢</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">加木薯粉继续搓揉</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，直至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面团变得不黏手</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">且</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">表面光滑</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">才行</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面团若是硬的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，则</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">需要</span><b>慢慢</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">加水</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，直到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面团变软</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，且</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">表面光滑</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">看不到白粉为止</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.把面团搓成圆球，（建议不要搓太大颗</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">炸得时间比较短）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">6.</span><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">冷油下锅</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">油量淹过地瓜球的一半）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">以中小火油炸地瓜球使其定型</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><strong>小窍门：</strong><span style="color: #800000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">油炸过程中，要一直微微搅动，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">避免地瓜球黏在一起，也让每个地瓜球都能沾到油</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.地瓜球变大后</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">用锅铲轻轻压一下</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">会膨胀起来就表示可以大力压</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>小窍门：</strong>喜欢吃有点Q的，可以大力压个5～6次；而喜欢吃空心的，就多压几次。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">8.压完后</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">开大火逼油</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">炸至金黄色后盛盘</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<h3><b>成功秘诀</b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.</span><b>木薯粉用量：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">看地瓜蒸熟后的出水量</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><b>慢慢</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">加入木薯粉</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，关键点是</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面团不黏手</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">表面光滑，有点像软的黏土</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">但是不黏手</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.</span><b>油炸火候：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">一开始要冷油下地瓜球</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，然后</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">开中小火慢慢让它定型。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.</span><b>压地瓜球时机：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">如果面团的水分太多</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">搅动地瓜球时，面团会黏在一起</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，此时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">要一直搅动</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">但不要压</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">地瓜球变大后，这时还是生面团的颜色</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">每颗都压一压</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">压过的球会膨得更大</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">多压几次也可以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.</span><b>大火逼油</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：每颗地瓜球都压好之后</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">查看颜色</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">如果没那么金黄</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">开大火逼油</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。但是若</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">看到金黄色</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">并闻到地瓜球香气</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">就不需要开大火了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>注意：</strong>因为每个厂牌锅子的导热速度不一样</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">有时一开大火</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">地瓜球就太过焦</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">吃起来会略带苦味</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">所以还得注意颜色，判断需不需要开大火逼油。</span></p>
<h3><b>失败案例分析</b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span><b>地瓜球太硬：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">油炸地瓜球时，表面很快地就变硬了。这是因为木薯粉放太多</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，使得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面团变硬</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，也</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">无法压成空心。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.</span><b>地瓜无法成型：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">忽略了要看地瓜蒸熟后的出水量，来调整木薯粉的用量，结果木薯粉用得太少</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，下锅油</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">炸无法成型。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.</span><b>地瓜球变焦黑：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">一开始炸的时候</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，火开太大，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">油太热</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">地瓜球焦掉了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里面还有生面团味儿。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.</span><b>地瓜球未能膨胀</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">下油锅时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">面团尚未定型就压它</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，地瓜球无法膨胀起来，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">结果变成地瓜饼</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">且含油量高。</span><strong>@*</strong></p>
