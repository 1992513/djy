<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】中秋节烤肉 堪萨斯风味烤排骨</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321817-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>堪萨斯风味烤排骨使用低温慢烤，使肉质外焦里嫩，好吃到吮指。（李若琳／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年09月07日讯】中秋节与亲朋一起大啖美食，少不了烤肉。堪萨斯风味烤排骨的酱汁甜香辣，加上低温慢烤使肉质外焦里嫩，好吃到吮指。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321819" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321819-2-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14321819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321819-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321819" class="wp-caption-text">堪萨斯风味烤排骨所需食材。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备：</h2>
<p>1扇圣路易斯风格小肋排（baby back ribs），重量3磅</p>
<p>干腌料：1茶匙盐、1/2汤匙孜然粉（cumin）、1/2汤匙卡宴辣椒粉（cayenne）、1/2茶匙黑胡椒粉、1汤匙淡棕糖</p>
<p>烤肉酱：1/2杯自制奶油番茄酱、1汤匙苹果醋、2汤匙淡红糖、1/4茶匙芹菜籽（捣成粉）、1/2汤匙蒜粉、1茶匙盐、1茶匙卡宴辣椒粉</p>
<h2>料理步骤：</h2>
<p>1. 撕去排骨背面的白膜，顺着骨头挤出血管里的淤血。干腌料拌匀，先用约三分之一涂抹排骨背面，其余涂抹正面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321820" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321820-3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14321820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321820-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321820" class="wp-caption-text">左图：撕去排骨背面的白膜。右图：涂抹排骨的干腌料。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 保鲜膜包裹排骨，冷藏4小时；烤之前移除保鲜膜，用锡箔纸包裹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321821" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321821" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321821-4-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14321821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321821-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321821" class="wp-caption-text">图为排骨包裹锡箔纸、刚出炉待刷烤肉酱的排骨。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 预热（roast）烤箱275华氏度（135摄氏度）。排骨放烤盘上，烤1小时45分钟。快烤好时调制烤肉酱。（方法看最后提示）</p>
<p>4. 出炉的排骨移除锡箔纸，刷烤肉酱，返回烤箱放在上层，开启灸烤（broil）功能烤5~8分钟，刷余下烤肉酱再灸烤几分钟，至喜欢的焦度。</p>
<p>5. 出炉静置，不烫手再分切，以留住肉汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14321822" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14321822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321822-5-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14321822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14321822-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14321822" class="wp-caption-text">图为刷烤肉酱待烤的排骨；出炉的烤排骨。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>料理贴士：煮烤肉酱：冰箱取出自制番茄酱煮沸。如果番茄酱稠度不够，多煮一会儿让水分挥发。番茄酱要煮到比较稠才能吸附排骨。煮沸后加糖拌匀、关火，混合其它调味料。</p>
<p>自制番茄酱可以换用市售的减盐番茄酱，使用时不需要煮沸，直接混合调味料即可；若番茄酱较甜，请减少糖量。◇</p>
