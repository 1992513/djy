<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新英格兰台学联迎新 野餐游戏联谊</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333114-DJY09019-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新英格兰台学联9月15日举办迎新野餐会，同学们欢乐交谈和玩游戏。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年09月18日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿波士顿报导）在阳光明媚的9月14日中午，新英格兰地区台湾同学会联合会（FTSANE）在剑桥（Cambridge）的Danehy Park举办2024年迎新活动，以轻松愉快的野餐形式，吸引了波士顿地区各大学的新生及友好团体热情参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14333115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333115" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333115-DJY09011.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333115-DJY09011-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333115" class="wp-caption-text">同学们欢乐交谈和玩游戏。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14333119" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333119" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333119-DJY09072.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333119-DJY09072-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333119" class="wp-caption-text">同学们欢乐交谈和玩游戏。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在初秋的暖阳下，参与者们相互认识和联谊，在野餐和游戏中放松身心。现场有台式便当美食，有多样的团体游戏，从传递乒乓球，到意面棉花糖搭建比赛，再到猜歌比赛，场面非常热闹，活动氛围轻松愉快。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14333113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333113" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333113-DJY08993.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333113-DJY08993-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333113" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰台学联9月15日举办迎新野餐会，参与者们合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14333117" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333117" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333117-DJY09034.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333117" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333117-DJY09034-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333117" class="wp-caption-text">同学们玩吹球游戏。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14333118" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333118" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333118-DJY09037.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333118-DJY09037-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333118" class="wp-caption-text">同学们玩吹球游戏。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">新英格兰台学联会长江孟辰说，希望到场的同学们在享受美食与游戏的同时，能借此机会结交新朋友，彼此建立起未来的学术和生活支援网络。他特别感谢会内各部门干部和志工们的积极组织与无私付出。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">侨教中心主任高家富、教育组组长黄薳玉及科技组组长蔡孟勋等经文处嘉宾也到场，与学生们互动交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14333108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333108-DJY08956.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333108-DJY08956-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333108" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰台学联会长江孟辰欢迎新生们到来。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14333112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333112" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333112-DJY08979.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333112-DJY08979-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333112" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富介绍侨胞服务。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14333111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333111" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333111-DJY08969.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333111-DJY08969-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333111" class="wp-caption-text">科技组组长蔡孟勋与同学们交流。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">黄薳玉在致词中对新生们的到来表示热烈欢迎。她说：“很高兴看到这么多同学选择来波士顿这个人文荟萃的城市展开留学生活，希望大家能保持身心健康，多参与各项活动，保持积极心态。此外，若在生活中遇到困难，教育组随时乐意提供协助。”</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">她也鼓励大家踊跃参加即将于10月5日举行的国庆游行，并送上真诚祝福：“愿大家能成为自己生命中的贵人，成为他人的贵人，也能遇到生命中的贵人。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14333110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333110" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333110-DJY08959.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333110-DJY08959-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333110" class="wp-caption-text">教育组组长黄薳玉鼓励大家参与当地侨团活动。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14333116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333116" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333116-DJY09028.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333116" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333116-DJY09028-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333116" class="wp-caption-text">学联干部们合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14333107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14333107" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333107-DJY08948.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14333107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14333107-DJY08948-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14333107" class="wp-caption-text">一些参与者带着宠物来参加野餐。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天，波士顿台湾影展联合会长Andrew Lin，以及活动赞助商之一的Boston Home Group顾问Ason Peng也出席，并向新生介绍了波士顿侨团近期的一些活动，鼓励大家多多参加，融入当地社群，扩大社交圈。◇</p>
