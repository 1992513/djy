<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>丘美珍《九如图》 仿青花瓷纹饰鲤鱼作品</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月21日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）《九如图》是丘美珍老师为桃园市大园区公所创作的仿青花瓷纹饰鲤鱼作品，这幅作品是以九条鱼环绕为主题。这幅作品不仅仅是一幅静态的国画作品，更是一幅充满象征意义的艺术之作。</p>
<p>丘美珍是新生医护管理专科学校兼任，通识教育中心教美术讲师，也是芦山园社区大学“青花瓷彩绘”教授，并且在中坜救国团成人教育中心教“国画”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14335739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14335739" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335739-710397.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14335739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335739-710397-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14335739" class="wp-caption-text">丘美珍讲师作画专注，能够为区民带来身心愉悦的感受。（丘美珍讲师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14335737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14335737" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335737-710395.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14335737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335737-710395-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14335737" class="wp-caption-text">丘美珍讲师师作画专注神情。（丘美珍讲师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丘美珍说，在中国文化中，鲤鱼象征着好运、富足和幸福，九是一个象征性的数字，在汉语中代表着长久、稳定和完整。因此，这幅作品中的九条鲤鱼，象征着好运、富足和幸福源源不绝，也代表着纯洁、高雅和宁静的环境将伴随着久远的时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14335738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14335738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335738-710396.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14335738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335738-710396-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14335738" class="wp-caption-text">《九如图》是丘美珍讲师为桃园市大园区公所创作的仿青花瓷纹饰鲤鱼作品。（丘美珍讲师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丘美珍作品的放置位置恰如其分，不仅为大园区民提供了一处欣赏艺术的场所，也带来了愉悦和舒心的氛围。每三个月更换一次作品，不仅让人们能够欣赏到不同风格的艺术作品，也为办公场所增添了一份生气和活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14335741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14335741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335741-710398.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14335741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335741-710398-600x1217.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1217" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14335741" class="wp-caption-text">欣赏艺术之美，获得一丝宁静与舒适。（丘美珍讲师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丘美珍希望这幅《九如图》的作品，能够为区民带来身心愉悦的感受，让他们在繁忙的办公生活中，能够稍作休息，欣赏艺术之美，获得一丝宁静与舒适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14335742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14335742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335742-710399.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14335742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335742-710399-600x1197.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1197" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14335742" class="wp-caption-text">作品通过鱼和荷的组合，呈现了一种和谐与平静。（丘美珍讲师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14335736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14335736" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335736-710394.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14335736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14335736-710394-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14335736" class="wp-caption-text">仿青花瓷纹饰鲤鱼作品。（丘美珍讲师提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这幅作品通过鱼和荷的组合，呈现了一种和谐与平静，希望观者能够从中感受到艺术的美好和力量，并为生活增添一丝色彩和乐趣。</p>
<p>丘美珍画展瓷情花语展览日期，2024.9/25～10/13，开幕茶会9/29周日10:00在桃园市文化局3楼第二展览室。◇</p>
