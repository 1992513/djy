<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>副县长宣示反毒、反黑、反霸凌、反诈骗 营造友善校园</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323901-706645-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>友善校园周宣誓活动。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月04日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）副县长邱俐俐9月4日上午在头份市兴华高中举办的113（2024）学年度第1学期友善校园周活动中，带领师生宣誓反毒、反黑、反霸凌、反诈骗、拒绝儿少性剥削，矢志营造友善校园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323902" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323902-706646.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14323902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323902-706646.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323902" class="wp-caption-text">警方宣导反毒、反诈骗、反霸凌。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14323906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323906" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323906-706650.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14323906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323906-706650.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323906" class="wp-caption-text">学生演出的宣导话剧。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本周是113学年度第1学期开学的第1周，县府与全台各县市同步办理友善校园周宣誓活动，除加强宣导反毒、反黑、反霸凌和校园安全，教育部且订定友善校园周主题为“拒绝儿少性剥削──不拍、不传、不留、要求助”，藉以加强宣导“避免成为儿少性剥削共犯”“儿童少年不得作为性的客体”及“不性化儿少之价值观”等议题，以建立正确的自我保护观念及求助管道，减少儿少性剥削憾事发生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323903" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323903-706647.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14323903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323903-706647.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323903" class="wp-caption-text">副县长带领宣誓。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14323904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323904" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323904-706648.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14323904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323904-706648.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323904" class="wp-caption-text">学生宣誓。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天上午在兴华高中活动中心，副县长邱俐俐、县议员徐功凡、陈光轩及副议长张淑芬、县议员萧咏萱、郑聚然的秘书、教育处特教科长邱婉庭、县警局少年队副队长杨玟君、教育部苗栗县联络处副督导林家和、校长胡文生等人，共同与师生宣誓“携手连心爱苗栗，友善校园总动员。身体自主要尊重，健康人生多闪耀。网路资讯日益新，尊重彼此很重要。拒绝儿少性剥削──不拍、不传、不留、要求助。反毒、反烟、反霸凌，友善校园一起来”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323905" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323905-706649.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14323905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323905-706649.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323905" class="wp-caption-text">丢掷纸飞机，宣示营造友善校园决心。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着，副县长邱俐俐等人分别丢掷手中的纸飞机，宣示反毒、反黑、反霸凌、反诈骗、杜绝网路性别暴力、拒绝儿少性剥削及营造友善校园的决心。</p>
<p>副县长邱俐俐希望透过友善校园周宣誓活动，唤起各校师生及家长共同营造安全、温馨、友善的校园，让同学们快乐上学、安心学习。◇</p>
