<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>余文博升全美余风采堂元老 龙飞狮舞庆贺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328002-DSC05883-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>9月7日晚，纽英崙侨社祝贺余文博（左）荣升全美余风采堂元老。余翠梅（右）带领华林派献上舞龙表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年09月11日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“就如文博元老每逢春节必唱的一首歌，是《胜利双手创》，试问不拼又怎么会赢呢？也许这就是我们文博元老的成功秘诀。”纽英崙余风采堂主席余立昌说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">9月7日晚在华埠帝苑酒楼，余立昌面向150多名侨社嘉宾，祝贺出身纽英崙的宗长余文博荣升全美余风采堂元老。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14327999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327999-DSC05752.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14327999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327999-DSC05752-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327999" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂祝贺余文博荣升全美元老。左起：全美元老余宏基、纽英崙元老余超群、新晋元老余文博、美东总长余应昌、纽英崙主席余立昌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14328000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328000" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328000-DSC05840.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328000-DSC05840-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328000" class="wp-caption-text">侨界祝贺余文博荣升全美余风采堂元老，多位嘉宾现场道贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14328014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328014-DSC06014.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328014-DSC06014-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328014" class="wp-caption-text">左起：侨教中心主任高家富、全美余氏元老余文博、波士顿经文处处长廖朝宏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>服务侨社</strong><strong>50</strong><strong>多年</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">余文博早于2018年当选为全美余风采堂美东总长；卸任之后，又在今年荣升元老。他出生于1946年，19岁从香港移民来美国，曾先后取得会计硕士学位和律师执照。后来，余文博还买下了华埠地区的大厦，成为这个社区的业主。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在创业的同时，余文博也加入余风采堂服务，并以其专业技能帮助多个侨团。2022年，中华广教学校也向他赠送褒奖，感谢他多年来对社区的贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14328008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328008-DSC05955.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328008-DSC05955-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328008" class="wp-caption-text">余文博感谢侨社对他的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14328007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328007-DSC05926.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328007-DSC05926-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328007" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂主席余立昌回顾余文博服务社区的经历。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“在公所服务了54年，认识了很多朋友。我今天很感激，大家能在百忙之中来参加我这个宴会。”余文博说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">他感谢华林派为宴会带来精彩表演，并提及，他与华林派创始人陈培师傅相识50多年，很高兴看到陈师傅的后辈们表演。另一位余氏“新扎师兄”带领余南狮队伍前来演出，也令余文博非常愉快。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">他还谈到，在座的许多嘉宾，都是他会计和律师事务的顾客。“很多谢你们帮衬我这么多年，让我养大了（5名）小孩。”余文博感慨说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14328005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328005-DSC05918.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328005-DSC05918-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328005" class="wp-caption-text">舞狮、舞龙团队及嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14328003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328003-DSC05880.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328003-DSC05880-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328003" class="wp-caption-text">华林派武馆带来舞龙表演，赢得满堂喝采。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14328004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328004-DSC05904.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328004-DSC05904-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328004" class="wp-caption-text">余南狮武馆带来舞狮表演，向嘉宾们送上祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>宴会表演精彩</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>汇集嘉宾</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在这场晚宴中，两家由余氏宗亲武馆带来的表演点燃了现场的热情。华林派武馆在余翠梅的带领下，表演了华埠各家宴会都少有的舞龙节目。在并不宽敞的宴会厅舞台下，彩龙伴随锣鼓声飞腾，更显精神抖擞，赢得一片欢呼叫好声。接下来，余南狮武馆的五头彩狮登台献瑞，还下到宴席中间，向每一桌来宾送上祝福。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在热闹的龙狮表演之后，余风采堂妇女组成员们也盛装上台，带来两场舞蹈表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14328001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328001-DSC05878.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328001-DSC05878-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328001" class="wp-caption-text">华林派武馆舞龙的同时，也献上武术表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14328006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14328006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328006-DSC05922.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14328006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14328006-DSC05922-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14328006" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂妇女组盛装登台，表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚，驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任高家富、中华公所主席雷国辉、侨务委员陈仕维，以及同为余氏宗亲的洪门总理余丽媖等嘉宾，也全程参与宴会，向余文博和余风采堂表达祝贺。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">新当选全美余风采堂美东总长的余应昌，以及全美元老余宏基，从纽约来到波士顿赴宴，庆祝余文博的成就。</p>

<p style="font-weight: 400;">此外，麻州州长希莉（Maura Healey）、副州长德里斯科（Kimberley Driscoll），以及麻州参议院也都向余文博发来贺状。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在场的许多嘉宾，都是和余文博结识几十年的老朋友。他们纷纷称赞，这位新晋元老在百忙之中仍然抽时间服务社区，获得的荣誉可谓“实至名归”。◇</p>
