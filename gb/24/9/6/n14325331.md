<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>开大强棒新生接棒 113学年度新生入学讲习</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325332-707054-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>活动由林玥秀校长担任先发投手、萧君华学务长及校猫小珍珠为后援投手、打击手郭晋源副校长、捕手赖沅晖副校长及竞技啦啦队等人。（开南大学 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月06日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾报导）为使新生及早熟悉环境，完成入学准备，适应校园生活，融入开南大家庭。开南大学6日规划“开大强棒～开南大学113年新入学讲习”活动，以热闹的开大棒球日来欢迎新生，由学长姐扮演球队经理，大一导师扮演教练，带领新生入场，走向人生的另一个球场。</p>
<figure id="14325333" aria-describedby="caption-14325333" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325333-707055-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325333-707055-450x253.jpg" alt="林玥秀校长开球进场。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325333" class="wp-caption-text">林玥秀校长开球进场。（开南大学 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动由林玥秀校长担任先发投手、萧君华学务长及校猫小珍珠为后援投手、打击手郭晋源副校长、捕手赖沅晖副校长及竞技啦啦队等人，一同进行开球仪式。</p>
<figure id="14325334" aria-describedby="caption-14325334" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325334-707056-450x239.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325334-707056-450x239.jpg" alt="林玥秀校长担任先发投手、打击手郭晋源副校长（右）、捕手赖沅晖副校长（左）。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325334" class="wp-caption-text">林玥秀校长担任先发投手、打击手郭晋源副校长（右）、捕手赖沅晖副校长（左）。（开南大学 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林玥秀说，我们用“开大强棒”欢迎新生。这不仅仅是我们在郭李建夫总教练率领开南大学优秀棒球队的象征，更是我们开南大学“拼到底”的精神体现。</p>
<figure id="14325340" aria-describedby="caption-14325340" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325340-707062-450x219.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325340-707062-450x219.jpg" alt="教职员合唱迎新。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325340" class="wp-caption-text">教职员合唱迎新。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14325336" aria-describedby="caption-14325336" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325336-707058-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325336-707058-450x253.jpg" alt="社团表演迎接新生。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325336" class="wp-caption-text">社团表演迎接新生。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14325335" aria-describedby="caption-14325335" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325335-707057-450x245.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325335-707057-450x245.jpg" alt="林玥秀校长鼓励新生多学习接受挑战。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325335" class="wp-caption-text">林玥秀校长鼓励新生多学习接受挑战。（开南大学 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林玥秀鼓励新生们充分利用学校提供的各种资源和机会，积极参与校内外的活动，无论是在学术研究、体育竞技，还是与国际学生交流方面，学校都是新生坚强的后盾。未来无论面对什么样的挑战，勇敢挥棒，才能击出属于自己的全垒打！</p>
<figure id="14325339" aria-describedby="caption-14325339" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325339-707061-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325339-707061-450x253.jpg" alt="社团表演迎接新生。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325339" class="wp-caption-text">社团表演迎接新生。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14325338" aria-describedby="caption-14325338" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325338-707060-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325338-707060-450x253.jpg" alt="社团表演迎接新生。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325338" class="wp-caption-text">社团表演迎接新生。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14325337" aria-describedby="caption-14325337" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325337-707059-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325337-707059-450x253.jpg" alt="社团表演迎接新生。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325337" class="wp-caption-text">社团表演迎接新生。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以棒球场及球赛为主题的情境式设计，各种社团卖力演出，林玥秀与教职员也化身合唱团表演，让活动热闹而有趣。新生每人都会获得学生会发送的球赛入场券及应援拍拍扇，凭入场券可进入观看社团表演及参与抽奖，并结合学生会规划的社团博览会，五大性质的社团摆摊招生，祝福新生入学后能有崭新的开始。</p>
<figure id="14325341" aria-describedby="caption-14325341" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325341-707063-450x184.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14325341-707063-450x184.jpg" alt="新生参与社团博览会。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14325341" class="wp-caption-text">新生参与社团博览会。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>离场后会由大一导师教练及球队经理学长姐，带领新生展开的系所旅程，认识开大校园及进行大学四年的生活辅导。◇</p>
