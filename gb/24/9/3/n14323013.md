<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节  朗读比赛热闹非凡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323020-IMG_6043-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节，朗读比赛热闹非凡。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月03日讯】2024年昆士兰正体汉字文化节朗读决赛于8月24日假昆士兰台湾中心热闹举行，共计邀请143位透过学校团体初选或征选推荐入围决赛的参赛者到场，角逐2024年昆士兰正体汉字文化节朗读比赛的桂冠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323023" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323023-IMG_4380-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323023-IMG_4380-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323023" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节，朗读比赛热闹非凡。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当日现场热闹非凡，满堂的师长亲友到场陪伴观赛加油，会场座无虚席，秩序井然良好。朗读比赛的开场由林伊莹老师说明比赛规则，带领幼童组3-4岁的参赛者朗读一篇篇比赛诗词，在朗朗读书声中轻松揭开序幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323025" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323025-IMG_4365-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323025-IMG_4365-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323025" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节，朗读比赛热闹非凡。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年年纪最小的参赛者仅有三岁，稚嫩的三岁小朋友由家长陪同上台，流利地朗诵唐诗与童诗，表现令人赞赏！赛事继续由小学低中高年级至中学各年级依序进行。参赛学生在顺利完成朗读后，都获得参赛证明以资鼓励，许多参赛者都拿着证书与家人与同学合影，为参与朗读决赛留下美好回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323026-IMG_4362-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323026-IMG_4362-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323026" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节，朗读比赛热闹非凡。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14323021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323021-IMG_4399-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323021-IMG_4399-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323021" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节，朗读比赛热闹非凡。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本年度参赛学校包括佛光山中天学校、布里斯本慈济人文学校、亚洲语文学校等侨校，以及Calamvale Community College、Robertson State School、Rochedale State School等多所主流学校，总计超过2,000位学生参与各校校内征选与初赛。透过校际朗读比赛，学生们在公开的讲台上展现华语文学习成果，透过阖家参与增进亲师生互动关系，同时让各校及各族裔人士进行多元互动交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323022" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323022-IMG_4392-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323022" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323022-IMG_4392-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323022" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节，朗读比赛热闹非凡。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14323019" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323019" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323019-IMG_6402-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323019" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323019-IMG_6402-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323019" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节，朗读比赛热闹非凡。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主办单位昆士兰华语文教师联谊会负责人林伊莹老师感谢各校校长、老师、家长与学生们对正体汉字文化节活动的热情参与和重视，更是特别感谢当天四位评审及现场志工们的辛劳，昆士兰台湾中心主任欧阳群也到场鼓励，赞扬肯定主办单位的用心。本届正体汉字文化节朗读比赛得奖名单预计于本年9月上旬出炉，颁奖典礼预计在9月14日举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323018" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323018-IMG_6410-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323018" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323018-IMG_6410-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323018" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节，朗读比赛热闹非凡。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
