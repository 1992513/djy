<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>硬笔字书写参赛热络 圆满成功</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323047-IMG_5306-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节：硬笔字书写参赛热络，圆满成功。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月03日讯】2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节硬笔字书写比赛赛事利圆满，参赛情形热络，人气高涨，充分展现当地正体华语文蓬勃朝气，主办单位昆士兰华语文教师联谊会感谢大家热情参与此项年度活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323056" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323056-IMG_5566-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323056-IMG_5566-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323056" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节：硬笔字书写参赛热络，圆满成功。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14323053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323053" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323053-IMG_5302-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323053-IMG_5302-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323053" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节：硬笔字书写参赛热络，圆满成功。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动在6月15、18、19及22日四天，利用课余时间于昆士兰台湾中心办理八场次现场比赛，共计160位报名者出席参加，现场秩序井然。参赛者透过教师推荐报名参赛，包括佛光山中天学校、布里斯本慈济人文学校、亚洲语文学校等各侨校以及家教班，皆踊跃报名参加；现场也开放给陪同的家长亲友们报名参赛，大家反应热络，踊跃参与，令本年度社会组参赛人数大幅增加；另外在中心的赛事现场并安排侨委会“揪团办卡享好康”活动让参赛者和亲友们申办i侨卡，吸引大批家长亲友与志工等了解侨委会i侨卡资讯及服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323049-IMG_5307-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323049-IMG_5307-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323049" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节：硬笔字书写参赛热络，圆满成功。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14323045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323045" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323045-IMG_5297-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323045-IMG_5297-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323045" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节：硬笔字书写参赛热络，圆满成功。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了于中心办理赛事，主办单位也再次走进社区校园，于6月10日至21日之间在Calamvale Community Collage、Rochedale State School、Robertson State School、Woodridge State High School等主流中小学举办校内比赛，体现汉字活动的多元化和包容性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323039-IMG_5678-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323039-IMG_5678-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323039" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节：硬笔字书写参赛热络，圆满成功。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14323044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323044-IMG_5569-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323044" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323044-IMG_5569-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323044" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节：硬笔字书写参赛热络，圆满成功。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14323041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323041-IMG_5585-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14323041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323041-IMG_5585-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323041" class="wp-caption-text">2024昆士兰正体汉字文化节：硬笔字书写参赛热络，圆满成功。（昆士兰台湾中心提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本年度参赛对象含括小学至社会人士，团体或个别，汉字背景与非汉字背景各族裔的参赛者，经过主办单位初选，共计130件作品入围总决选，比赛结果将于九月初公布，颁奖暨成果展示典礼谨订于9月14日举行，欢迎各界踊跃共襄盛举！</p>
<p>（昆士兰台湾中心供稿）</p>
