<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>特别的开学仪式！下崙福安宫与国小携手迎新生</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323382-706531-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>最特别开学仪式！下崙福安宫与国小携手迎接新生。（吴登兴提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月03日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）九月三日，下崙国小一年级新生的开学日，注定与众不同。在云林口湖乡下崙福安宫前，由32位穿着古装的新生在家长和老师的陪同下，经历了一场充满传统仪式感的“朱砂启智·新生开笔礼”。这已经是福安宫与下崙国小连续五年合作举办的活动，为小朋友们送上开学的祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323383" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323383-706532-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14323383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323383-706532-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323383" class="wp-caption-text">32位穿着古装的新生由家长陪同。（吴登兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>传统古礼 开启学习之门</h2>
<p>活动开始，新生们在导师和家长的带领下，穿越三门，象征着开启新的人生旅程。接着，大家整齐地进行“正衣冠、团拜献礼”，小朋友们一丝不苟，仿佛置身于古代的书院。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323388" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323388-706539-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14323388" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323388-706539-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323388" class="wp-caption-text">传统古礼开启学习之门。（吴登兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后，福安宫主委陈博恩为每位新生进行“朱砂启智”，这是仪式的核心环节——用毛笔蘸取朱砂，在孩子们的眉心点上一抹红，寓意开启智慧，让他们从此目明心亮，爱上学习。这一环节让在场的家长和老师们纷纷感到感动和欣慰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323387" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323387-706536-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14323387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323387-706536-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323387" class="wp-caption-text">福安宫与下崙国小，连续五年合作举办这个很有意义的活动。（吴登兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>撰写“仁义忠厚” 迎接新学期</h2>
<p>接着，新生们在师长和家长的陪伴下，第一次拿起毛笔，写下了“仁义忠厚”四个大字。车达校长表示，这不仅是传统礼仪的延续，更是希望孩子们能够以这四个字为人生的指引，成为善良宽厚、勤学向上的好学生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323384" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323384" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323384-706533-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14323384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323384-706533-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323384" class="wp-caption-text">新生写下“仁义忠厚”四个大字。（吴登兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>福安宫献礼 爱的祝福伴成长</h2>
<p>活动的高潮是福安宫为每位新生精心准备的启蒙见面礼。这些礼物不仅仅是物质上的鼓励，更代表着长辈们的深深祝福，希望孩子们能在未来的学习道路上快乐成长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323386-706535-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14323386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323386-706535-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323386" class="wp-caption-text">福安宫献礼 ，爱的祝福伴成长。（吴登兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>整个活动在全体大合照中圆满结束，孩子们脸上洋溢着满满的期待与兴奋。这场充满传统韵味的仪式，为他们的新学期开启了美好的篇章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14323385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14323385" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323385-706534-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14323385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14323385-706534-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14323385" class="wp-caption-text">整个活动在全体大合照中圆满结束，孩子们脸上洋溢着满满的期待与兴奋。（吴登兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
