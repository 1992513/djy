<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>会稽国中生态保育 虎头山保育赤腹鹰赏鹰课程</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330642-709050-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>会稽国中校长李文义结合“内坜狮子会”张岱文会长及领导狮兄们协助参与此次生态保育课程。（桃园市会稽国中 提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月15日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市会稽国中结合桃园市野鸟学会15日清晨六点半集合共约100位师生、家长出发到虎头山环保公园旁停机坪最佳赏鹰地点，进行学校本位课程设计“入秋最棒的一堂户外生态”。</p>
<p>会稽国中校长李文义说，此次生态保育课程也结合“内坜狮子会”张岱文会长及领导狮兄们协助参与，现场捐赠一万元给予桃园市野鸟学会保育赤腹鹰工作，以及赞助会稽国中课程一万元给家长会，感谢桃市野鸟学会与内坜狮子会教育与公益结合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330641-709049.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330641-709049-600x362.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="362"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330641" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中生态保育家保育赤腹鹰大合照。（桃园市会稽国中 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14330643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330643-709051.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330643-709051-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330643" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市野鸟学会志工为学生先进行生态保育课。（桃园市会稽国中 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>课程分组进行由陈信维资深专业生态专家，透过教学使用高倍速望远镜，教导学生如何在桃园台地观察天际线、山陵线、电塔座标与云层之间，找寻随着气旋翱翔天际由北往南过境的赤腹鹰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330648-709056.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330648-709056-600x268.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="268"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330648" class="wp-caption-text">惊艳头顶有赤腹鹰经过。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14330644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330644-709052.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330644-709052-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330644" class="wp-caption-text">介绍过境虎头山过境赤腹鹰特征。（桃园市会稽国中 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同时也邀请到生态保育知名纪录片“梁皆得导演”，现场拍摄纪录师生保育赏鹰画面，倡议分享要保育台湾玉山特有种“熊鹰”，让学生落实保育工作认知。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330645" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330645-709053.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330645-709053-600x384.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="384"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330645" class="wp-caption-text">陈信维资深专业生态专家，透过教学使用高倍速望远镜。（桃园市会稽国中 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14330646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330646-709054.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330646-709054-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330646" class="wp-caption-text">左边带帽子李文义校长讲解反思环境教育重要。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李文义说，非常幸运的亲师生，用高倍望远镜看件盘旋天际赤腹鹰，第二小组更用肉眼看见三只赤腹鹰在自己头上穿越飞过的画面，另在场师生兴奋大叫“看到了赤腹鹰！”好棒的鹰姿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330647-709055.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330647-709055-600x713.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="713"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330647" class="wp-caption-text">左边纪录片导演梁皆得右边会稽国中李文义校长。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动结束后，李文义表示：让我们孩子们反思，我们的居住环境，桃园在地多数人都不知道虎头山有珍贵过境赤腹鹰，是不是要友善耕作，不要过量使用农药或是停止使用，老鼠药的发放也会间接造成老鹰中毒伤亡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330650-709058.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330650-709058-600x298.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="298"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330650" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中学生在桃园台地观察天际线、山陵线、电塔座标与云层之间，找寻随着气旋翱翔天际由北往南过境的赤腹鹰。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14330649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330649-709057.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330649-709057-600x268.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="268"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330649" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中学生也健走净山。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>透过进入活动会稽国中家长表示：感谢校方给予关怀在地生态保育学习，也增进亲子互动健走，最重要的是在假日可以远离3C，好好让眼睛欣赏大自然美景，谢谢会稽国中带来入秋最棒的一堂生态课。◇</p>
