<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“哈客嬉游闹诏安 ”二崙运动公园热闹展开！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340340-712152-600x400.jpg" />
<h6> 2024云林诏安客家文化节系列活动，9/28日由二崙乡公所主办的“哈客嬉游闹诏安”活动，在二崙运动公园热闹展开！（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月29日讯】（大纪元记者 廖素贞台湾云林报导）2024云林诏安客家文化节系列活动，从9月14日起至10月11日陆续在崙背、二崙及西螺3乡镇接力展开。9/28日由二崙乡公所主办的“哈客嬉游闹诏安”活动，在二崙运动公园热闹展开！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340343-712155.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340343-712155-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340343" class="wp-caption-text">2024云林诏安客家文化节系列活动，9/28日在二崙运动公园热闹展开！现场热闹滚滚。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14340345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340345-712157.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340345-712157-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340345" class="wp-caption-text">社区妈妈的舞蹈表演（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14340349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340349" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340349-712164.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340349-712164-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340349" class="wp-caption-text">社区妈妈精彩的舞蹈表演。 （廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动在社区妈妈精彩的舞蹈及辖区内国小学童精彩的才艺表演揭开序幕，并安排客语闯关、诏安客诗词童谣吟唱、诏安产物特色展、诏安艺文嘉年华、诏安客家美食体验、竹编DIY制作、二崙文学家展示等活动，展现诏安客家民俗文化与技艺，吸引喜爱诏安客家艺文人士及亲子踊跃参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340344" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340344-712156.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340344-712156-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340344" class="wp-caption-text">竹编DIY吸引不少亲子参加（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14340348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340348-712162.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340348-712162-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340348" class="wp-caption-text">Q弹美味的客家传统美食“油葱糕”（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>诏安客家美食体验，是每年活动少不了的项目，排队等着品尝的人总是大排长龙；今年的竹编DIY，也吸引不少亲子参加，为了完成作品，大家忙到下午1点多才结束，热络的程度超乎长年推广竹编的杨盛辉老师的预期，“没想到有这么多人喜爱竹编！”让他对竹编的推广重新燃起希望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340342" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340342-712154.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340342-712154-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340342" class="wp-caption-text">永定国小的国乐团精彩演出获得现场来宾及长官赞赏（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14340341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340341" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340341-712153.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340341-712153-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340341" class="wp-caption-text">代理乡长林正平与大同国小校长及同学合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化观光处副处长陈良骏表示，作为诏安客家文化的聚落，二崙乡长期以来一直是诏安客家人生活与文化交流的重要据点。透过此次文化节，县府希望让更多人了解诏安客家的语言、风俗与生活习惯，并借此机会唤醒族群认同，让下一代年轻人重新认识他们的根。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340347" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340347-712160.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340347-712160-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340347" class="wp-caption-text">三合国小小朋友口说开口狮的由来。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14340346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340346" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340346-712158.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14340346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340346-712158-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340346" class="wp-caption-text">旭光国小校长黄振崇虎虎生风的武术表演。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文观处指出，?诏安客家人是台湾重要的族群之一，其文化特色在语言、民俗活动以及饮食文化中展现得淋漓尽致。透过这场集聚社区凝聚力与文化自信的活动，期望让诏安客家文化得以在当代重新焕发光彩。活动结合地方特色产业，推出许多特制的客家美食与特色农产品，让更多人了解并爱上这片土地的独特风味。</p>
<p>二崙乡公所代理乡长林正平表示，诏安客家文化是二崙乡的瑰宝，每年在县府的支持下都盛大举行，感谢各社区的热烈参与，今天适逢教师节，感谢参与活动的各校师生及校长，对客家文化的重视，希望透过学校的向下扎根，让客家文化永续传承！</p>
<p>本身是诏安客家人的县议员洪如萍表示，虽然她的客语讲的不是很好，但她很喜欢讲客语，希望自己的客语能越讲越好，语言是文化的根，一定不能消失，她呼吁大家要多讲客语，才不枉费政府每年投入那么多的经费来推广客语！</p>
