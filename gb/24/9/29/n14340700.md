<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手中的自然画布 摄影师捕捉花莲台风前的景色</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340701-712327-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>摄影师陈明忠捕捉花莲山陀儿台风来临前的景色。（陈明忠提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月29日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）在数位摄影技术飞速发展的时代，摄影早已不再局限于高级相机和复杂的设备。花莲摄影师陈明忠用手机，捕捉他眼中最纯粹的花莲风光，记录山陀儿台风来临前，大自然的瞬间。陈明忠认为，手机摄影所带来的是一种更加轻松和随意的创作方式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340702" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340702-712328.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340702-712328-600x777.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="584" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340702" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠认为，手机摄影所带来的是一种更加轻松和随意的创作方式。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈明忠说：“花莲就是这样的悠闲，说走就走，不需提前计划或预约。”透过他的手机镜头，我们能感受到他对这片土地的热爱，无论是乡间小路、成群的白鹭鸶，还是台风前夕傍晚的天空，他总能以最简单的设备捕捉到最震撼的自然美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340703" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340703-712329.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340703-712329-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340703" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠说：“花莲就是这样的悠闲，说走就走，不需提前计划或预约。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>偶遇白鹭鸶的乡间风光</h2>
<p>陈明忠在凤林乡间小路上拍摄的白鹭鸶群落引起了许多人的关注。这些鸟儿悠然自得地在田间觅食，与背后一片绿油油的稻田相映成趣，画面充满生机勃勃的田园气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340704" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340704-712330.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340704-712330-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340704" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠在凤林乡间小路上拍摄的白鹭鸶群落，引起了许多人的关注。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他捕捉到了白鹭鸶在田野间翩然起舞的瞬间，背景则是远方绵延的山脉。画面中的动与静交织出一种难以言喻的和谐之美，仿佛时间在这一刻静止。透过手机镜头，陈明忠让我们看见了一个在城市中难得一见的纯净世界。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340705" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340705-712331.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340705-712331-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340705" class="wp-caption-text">他捕捉到了白鹭鸶在田野间翩然起舞的瞬间。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>九月盛开的台湾栾树与雨中的菌菇</h2>
<p>除了动态的鸟类，陈明忠也不忘捕捉花莲的植物之美。九月正是台湾栾树盛开的季节，这种树的花朵呈现出迷人的金黄色，当微风拂过，金色的花瓣纷纷飘落，如同一场秋日的金色雨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340706" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340706-712332.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340706-712332-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340706" class="wp-caption-text">九月盛开的台湾栾树。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14340707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340707" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340707-712333.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340707-712333-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340707" class="wp-caption-text">当微风拂过，金色的花瓣纷纷飘落，如同一场秋日的金色雨。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在一场午后小雨后，陈明忠还发现了一片悄然冒出的菌菇，这些小小的生命在湿润的枯木间偷偷冒出，带来了一丝童话般的神秘感。他拍摄的菌菇照片充满了自然的静谧与诗意，雨滴挂在菌菇伞上的细节尤为动人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340708" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340708-712334.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340708-712334-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340708" class="wp-caption-text">在一场午后小雨后，陈明忠还发现了一片悄然冒出的菌菇。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他说：“当我看到雨下的菌菇，就像大自然的微小奇迹，无论多么微不足道的生命，都能在合适的环境中展现它们的美丽。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340709" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340709-712335.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340709-712335-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340709" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠说：“当我看到雨下的菌菇，就像大自然的微小奇迹。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>台风前夕的花莲天空：壮丽与不安并存</h2>
<p>中台山陀儿即将来袭前，花莲的天空变得特别引人注目。陈明忠趁着台风来临的前夕，拍摄了一系列充满戏剧性的天空画面。那些低垂的云层与晚霞交织在一起，既壮丽又带着一丝不安，仿佛随时都要降下暴雨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340710" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340710-712336.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340710-712336-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340710" class="wp-caption-text">台风前夕的花莲天空。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈明忠认为，这样的时刻是摄影师最难得的机会。台风前的天空色彩多变，云层的形态变幻莫测，为他提供了无限的创作灵感。他说：“每当看到这样的天空，我总是充满敬畏，因为这提醒了我们人类在大自然面前是多么的渺小。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340711" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340711-712337.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340711-712337-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340711" class="wp-caption-text">台风前的天空色彩多变，云层的形态变幻莫测。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>林田山的诗意风光</h2>
<p>林田山日式建筑是陈明忠最爱的拍摄地点之一。这片曾经是台湾重要林业基地的山区，现在变成了一个自然保护区，拥有广阔的森林和古朴的建筑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340714" style="width: 484px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340714-712342.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340714-712342-600x497.jpg" alt="" width="484" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340714" class="wp-caption-text">林田山日式建筑。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我喜欢在这里漫步，感受大自然的气息。每次来到林田山，我都觉得自己像是逃离了城市的喧嚣，重新找回了内心的宁静。”他如此形容这片让他无比向往的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340712" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340712-712339.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340712-712339-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340712" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠：“每次来到林田山，觉得自己像是逃离了城市的喧嚣。”（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在他的作品中，我们不仅能够感受到自然的壮丽，更能感受到陈明忠对生活的观察与热爱。他用手机摄影记录下的每一幅作品，都是他与这片土地之间的对话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340715-712343.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340715-712343-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340715" class="wp-caption-text">陈明忠说：“每当看到这样的天空，我总是充满敬畏。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从田野间的白鹭鸶，到盛开的台湾栾树，从台风前夕的天空到林田山的诗意风光，这些画面让人感受到花莲的纯粹与美丽。在这片土地上，每一个细节都值得被记录，每一个瞬间都值得被珍藏。而陈明忠，用他的手机，为我们捕捉到了这些平凡中的非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14340713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14340713" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340713-712340.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14340713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14340713-712340-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14340713" class="wp-caption-text">用手机摄影记录下的每一幅作品，都是陈明忠与这片土地之间的对话。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
