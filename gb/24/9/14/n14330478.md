<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>探索多伦多家居展 踏上梦想家居灵感之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330509-3-600x400.jpg" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月15日讯】多伦多家居展（Toronto Home Shows）是一系列专为家居爱好者设计的展览活动，涵盖不同季节和主题。秋季家居展（Fall Home Show）是其中的一个重要活动，专门针对家居装修和设计爱好者。</p>
<p class="p1">2024年多伦多秋季家居展于9月27日至29日在多伦多能源中心（Enercare Centre）举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330490" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/9/14/n14330478.md#1/small-studio-apartment-interior-with-kitchen-area-and-rope-screen" rel="attachment wp-att-14330490"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14330490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330490-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330490" class="wp-caption-text">单间公寓的绳幕设计，增加了狭小空间的美感和层次感。</figcaption></figure>
<h3 class="p1"><strong>展览亮点</strong></h3>
<p class="p1">超过250家供应商，展示最新的家居产品和设计趋势，从家具到装饰品，应有尽有。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/9/14/n14330478.md#1/5-1034" rel="attachment wp-att-14330505"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14330505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330505-5-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a></p>
<h3 class="p1"><strong>专家讲座</strong></h3>
<p class="p1">现场有许多家居设计和装修专家提供建议和灵感，帮助您解决家居问题。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/9/14/n14330478.md#1/24d2d01b126d41b29cb8038a1337654a" rel="attachment wp-att-14330508"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14330508" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330508-24d2d01b126d41b29cb8038a1337654a-450x396.png" alt="" width="450" b="396" /></a></p>
<h3 class="p1"><strong>互动展示</strong></h3>
<p class="p1">有些供应商会设置互动展示区，让您亲身体验产品的功能与效果。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14330492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330492-4-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></p>
<h3 class="p1"><strong>特别活动</strong></h3>
<p class="p1">包括DIY工作坊、手绘南瓜等，让参观者享受动手创造的乐趣。</p>
<p class="p1">家居装修爱好者，寻找灵感和新产品的人， 房屋拥有者，计划进行居家改造或升级的人， 设计师和建筑师，寻找最新趋势和产品的人，来多伦多秋季家居展吧。</p>
<p class="p1">这个展览不仅是一个购物的好机会，也是一个学习和交流的平台，能够获取许多实用的建议和灵感，帮您打造理想的居家空间。</p>
