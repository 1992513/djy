<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>兰屿音乐与文化节 传统与现代的交融</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330141-708963-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2024年海洋杯拼板舟竞赛。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年09月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）由台东县兰屿乡公所主办的《兰花水芋音乐祭》与《2024年海洋杯拼板舟竞赛》，接连两日于兰屿热闹展开，吸引了大量当地居民与游客参与，透过音乐与竞技，展现兰屿独特的文化魅力与传承精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330142" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330142-708964.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330142-708964-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330142" class="wp-caption-text">兰花水芋音乐祭展现传统之美。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>《兰花水芋音乐祭》 展现青春回忆与传统之美</h2>
<p>首先登场的《兰花水芋音乐祭》于10日下午在兰屿乡公所广场热闹登场，从下午4:30开始的音乐会，持续至晚上十点多，演出者轮番上阵，融合传统歌谣与现代音乐，为观众带来一场视觉与听觉的飨宴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330143-708965.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330143-708965-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330143" class="wp-caption-text">《兰花水芋音乐祭》于10日下午在兰屿乡公所广场热闹登场。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>音乐祭特别邀请了岛内外知名歌手与表演团队，从原住民歌手到民歌手的精彩演出，穿插着主持人的幽默诙谐，使得现场气氛高潮不断。音乐祭开场前，东清长老教会的张杰传道为活动进行祝福祷告，紧接着便是小飞鱼文化展演队的演出，展现了兰屿传统文化的深厚底蕴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330145-708967.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330145-708967-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330145" class="wp-caption-text">小飞鱼文化展演队。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，达悟女孩与兰中舞研社的现代舞表演，展现了青春的活力与创意，而穿着传统丁字裤的街头艺人周克桂以萨克斯风演奏，则为音乐会增添了独特的亮点。整场活动最后在野东西乐团的摇滚乐表演及璀璨的烟火秀中，画下完美句点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330146-708968.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330146-708968-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330146" class="wp-caption-text">演出者轮番上阵，融合传统歌谣与现代音乐，为观众带来一场视觉与听觉的飨宴。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>海洋杯拼板舟竞赛 展现团队精神与文化传承</h2>
<p>随后举行的《2024年海洋杯拼板舟竞赛暨妇女传统舞蹈展演》于13日清早在椰油湾热烈展开，这场赛事不仅是技术与体力的比拼，更象征着部落间的团队精神与文化传承。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330147-708969.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330147-708969-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330147" class="wp-caption-text">海洋杯拼板舟竞赛暨妇女传统舞蹈展演于13日清早在椰油湾热烈展开。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动分为单人舟400公尺、双人舟800公尺与十人舟越野赛等多项赛事，其中东清部落在双人舟与十人舟比赛中表现优异，囊括多项冠军，成为此次竞赛的最大赢家。选手们克服阴雨天气，依然奋力划行，展现了无比的毅力与对荣誉的追求。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330150-708972.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330150-708972-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330150" class="wp-caption-text">活动分为单人舟400公尺、双人舟800公尺与十人舟越野赛等多项赛事。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妇女传统舞蹈展演同样备受瞩目，各部落舞团的精彩演出，展示了各自的文化特色与传统服饰，舞者们一边唱歌一边跳舞，尽显传统文化的风采，吸引了众多游客拍照留念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330148-708970.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330148-708970-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330148" class="wp-caption-text">妇女传统舞蹈展演备受瞩目。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动虽在雨中进行，但现场观众的热情不减，大家透过赛事与舞蹈，深刻感受到了兰屿部落之间的凝聚力与文化的传承价值。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330151-708973.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14330151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330151-708973-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330151" class="wp-caption-text">这场赛事不仅是技术与体力的比拼，更象征着部落间的团队精神与文化传承。（兰屿兰恩传媒网提供）</figcaption></figure>
