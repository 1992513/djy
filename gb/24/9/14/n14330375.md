<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新诗：模糊（中英对照）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/d407fa81f8a23bd3b5d07d4f8bd3801a-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>晨曦（李怡欣／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>那张照片最近好像常在我的心中出现<br />
好像在某个地方拍的而且参加过某个展览<br />
却又好像不是在某个地方拍的没参加过什么展览<br />
甚至是否最近真的常在我的心中出现都有疑问<br />
连那照片的画面看起来也不清楚<br />
为什么会这样？<br />
是纸张变质还是掉色了？<br />
好像是又好像不是<br />
是时间太久了吗？<br />
是否我的记忆日久缺损，倒带不出？<br />
还是那照片的画面本来就不清楚？<br />
记忆里好像本来就是影影幢幢的<br />
好像是又好像不是<br />
到底真相如何？<br />
到底有没有那张照片呢？<br />
我拿捏不定，我拿捏不定……</p>
<p><strong>Obscuration</strong> Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Recently the photograph seems appeared in my mind very often<br />
It seems I had shot it in somewhere and had showed in a exhibition<br />
However it seems had not shot in somewhere and had not showed in any exhibition<br />
Even it&#8217;s doubt whether it&#8217;s truly appeared in my mind<br />
It looks like the tableau of the photograph isn&#8217;t clear as well<br />
Why does it like that?<br />
Is the paper go bad or the paper lose colour?<br />
Is it the time is too long?<br />
It seems yes neither the seem<br />
Whether my memory is too old and have some drawback to reverse<br />
Or the tableau of the photograph is not clear at first?<br />
It seems it entry and detail<br />
It seems yes but neither the seem<br />
What&#8217;s the true at the last ?<br />
Is there the photograph the last?<br />
I can&#8217;t affirm, Ican&#8217;t affirm……</p>
