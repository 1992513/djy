<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>增强婴幼儿免疫力 中医师防治有妙招</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099335-shutterstock_732137110.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>近年来新冠病毒逐渐趋向流感化，常见父母携带婴幼儿来求诊，但是科学中药粉口感不佳，小孩难以吞咽；针对5?6岁以下的孩童，鉴于他们的皮肤较薄且结构还在发展中，笔者改以药浴代替，效果可谓相当不错。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000">幼儿常见病毒感染症状</span></h2><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p><strong>1.普通感冒：</strong>多数由鼻病毒或副流感病毒所造成，症状有咳嗽、流鼻水、喉咙痛、轻微发烧，有时改变甚快，但相对较轻微。可能是在学校或幼儿园交叉感染所致。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p><strong>2.流行性感冒：</strong>发病的速度快，出现高烧、喉咙剧痛、头痛、全身酸痛等症状，容易引发肺炎、支气管炎或中耳炎，甚至变成重症。通常能做快筛确认。</p><p><strong>3.病毒性肠胃炎：</strong>如轮状病毒、诺罗病毒，症状是发烧、上吐下泻、严重腹泻且次数大于进食次数：此时要注意孩子是否有脱水、心跳加速、活力降低、尿量少、口唇干燥等状况。</p><p><strong>4.肠病毒：</strong>常见症状有口足手长水泡、喉咙痛、发烧、疱疹性咽呷炎，较严重时，会出现意识不清楚、全身抽搐、呕吐、心跳快、呼吸急促。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p><strong>5.过敏性鼻炎：</strong>典型症状为季节交替或者早上起床时，容易打喷嚏、流鼻水，晚上则鼻塞、打呼等，眼睛痒、鼻子痒。父母若是过敏体质，小孩过敏概率较高。（延伸阅读：<ahref="https://www.epochtimes.com/b5/17/7/15/n9403912.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow noreferrer" class="health-article-hover-class">改善过敏性鼻炎 中医：2种饮食要少碰</a>）</p><p><strong>6.气喘：</strong>属于下呼吸道过敏。出现咳嗽；严重时，会咳不停，有吸不到空气的感觉，甚至发出喘鸣声、咻咻咻的声音，躺下后，半夜咳嗽情况较严重。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><h2><span style="color:#008000">中医治疗思路与策略</span></h2><h3><span style="color:#0000ff">急性期首重消弭病菌</span></h3><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p>急性感染期中医首重除去外感（如感冒），也就是消弭病毒细菌。中医将病证分为寒、热、虚、实等，中医先辨证，接着采用相应的对证治疗方式，通常疗效明显。</p><h3><span style="color:#0000ff">缓解期侧重提升免疫力</span></h3><p>急性症状过后，接着辅以补脏腑的药物来调理身体，以增强免疫力。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><h3><span 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div
