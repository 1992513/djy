<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新英格兰中文校际演讲歌唱比赛颁奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224471-1-1-1NEACS-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>演讲比赛中年级组得奖者与评审和主持人合影。前排左起：王月娥前会长、第二名贾康丽（慈济）、第一名林卓威（勒星顿）、第三名滕凯（威斯伍）、优胜：林容安（勒星顿）；后排左起：评审吴萍萍、冯文鸾、颜毓芬、主持人林梅英。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年04月13日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿牛顿市报导）4月6日下午，新英格兰中文学校协会（NEACS）<wbr />在侨教中心举办校际正体汉字文化节活动，<wbr />此次活动包括国语演讲和华语歌唱比赛两大环节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224479" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224479-1-1-2NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224479-1-1-2NEACS-600x487.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="487" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224479" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰中文学校协会演讲及歌唱比赛的精彩镜头。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14224480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224480-1-1-3NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224480-1-1-3NEACS-600x573.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="573" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224480" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰中文学校协会演讲及歌唱比赛的精彩镜头。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14224481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224481-1-1-4NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224481-1-1-4NEACS-600x977.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="977" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224481" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰中文学校协会演讲及歌唱比赛的精彩镜头。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次有勒星顿、广教、明慧、慈济、文诚、<wbr />威斯伍等中文学校学生参加，场面热烈温馨。<wbr />除了让大家能互相观摩切磋，评审们的讲评更加珍贵，<wbr />有助提升未来的学艺才能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224476" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224476-1-1-5NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224476-1-1-5NEACS-600x614.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="614" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224476" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰中文学校协会演讲及歌唱比赛的精彩镜头。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14224478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224478-1-1-6NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224478-1-1-6NEACS-600x541.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="541" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224478" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰中文学校协会演讲及歌唱比赛的精彩镜头。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14224477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224477-1-1-7NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224477-1-1-7NEACS-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224477" class="wp-caption-text">林梅英老师为了减低参赛者的紧张，先跟每位小朋友击掌说笑。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于黄冠群会长出差到台湾，当天特请前会长王月娥代表致开场词，<wbr />她感谢参与者和工作人员的支持。颜毓芬、冯文鸾、<wbr />吴萍萍三人担任评审。<wbr />活动主持人林梅英老师为了减低参赛者的紧张，<wbr />先跟每位小朋友击掌说笑，告诉小朋友不要紧张，<wbr />不论排名结果如何。她表示最重要的是比赛过程中的收获与成长，<wbr />能来参加就是最大的收获。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224475" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224475-1-1-8NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224475-1-1-8NEACS-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224475" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰中文学校协会演讲歌唱比赛评审，左起：颜毓芬、冯文鸾、吴萍萍。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14224473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224473-1-1-9NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224473-1-1-9NEACS-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224473" class="wp-caption-text">高年级演讲得奖者与前会长王月娥 (左一)、评审老师们和波士顿文教中心主任潘昭荣 (右一) 合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演讲比赛分为低、中、高三个年龄组，<wbr />不同年龄组的选手们各自选择了不同的主题和形式进行演讲。<wbr />稚嫩童趣的低年级组参赛者大多选取小而精的童话故事；<wbr />略显成熟紧张的中年级组则是选择演说古代历史、<wbr />名人趣事或是分享自己的学习心得；<wbr />而身经百战熟练应赛的高年级组大多选择了像是心理健康、<wbr />饮食健康、温室效应等科学主题。<wbr />参赛者们表现出了丰富多样的风格，严肃、生动、幽默、<wbr />激情的表达让现场掌声不断。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14224474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224474-1-1-10NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224474-1-1-10NEACS-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224474" class="wp-caption-text">勒星顿、广教、明慧、慈济、文诚、威斯伍等中文学校学生参加，场面热烈温馨。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14224472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14224472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224472-1-1-11NEACS.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14224472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14224472-1-1-11NEACS-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14224472" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰中文学校协会全体工作人员合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着是歌唱比赛，小朋友们以歌唱表达情感，<wbr />演唱曲风也是丰富多彩，包括儿歌、说唱、流行歌曲等。<wbr />有的选手还加入了舞蹈元素，比赛气氛十分活跃，<wbr />引得在场老师与家长不时捧腹叫好。<wbr />过程中评委颜毓芬老师提醒参赛者在选择曲目时要考虑自己的音色和<wbr />音域，以展现出最佳的表现。</p>
<p>整场活动充满了童趣与活力，小朋友们在比赛中展现了才华与魅力，<wbr />赢得了评委和家长们的赞赏与喝采。◇</p>
