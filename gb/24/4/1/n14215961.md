<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】柚子猪酿茄子南瓜～酸甜可口 简单好做</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215974-13-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>柚子猪肉酿茄子南瓜，酸甜可口的精致佳肴。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong>简单好做的精致佳肴～秘诀就在有一点巧思，今天介绍的这一道酿肉料理，让平凡菜肴变成高级宴客菜，梁厨把秘诀分享出来，而且口感多层次，尝第一口酸甜美味，再尝一口味道香浓，最后就是唇齿留香了！希望大家都喜欢这一道料理。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>肉馅材料：</strong></span></p>
<p>柚子肉3 oz<br />
碎猪肉8 oz<br />
虾胶 8 oz<br />
冬菇粒2 oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉、芫荽梗各少许<br />
鸡蛋1颗</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>其它材料：</strong></span></p>
<p>美国茄子2个<br />
小南瓜3个<br />
粟粉水2汤匙（勾芡用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215988" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215988-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215988-1-450x271.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215988" class="wp-caption-text">食材。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">腌肉馅调味料</span>：</strong></p>
<p>鸡粉1茶匙<br />
盐1/2茶匙<br />
粟粉2汤匙</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>其它调味料：</strong></span></p>
<p>茄汁2汤匙<br />
辣椒酱1茶匙<br />
鸡粉1茶匙<br />
糖1茶匙<br />
盐1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
水2杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215991" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215991-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215991-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215991" class="wp-caption-text">调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>作法：</strong></span></p>
<p>1. 碗中放肉馅材料，再加腌肉调味料，搅拌后用手摔打。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215975" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215975-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215975-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215975" class="wp-caption-text">碗中放肉馅材料，再加腌肉调味料，搅拌后用手摔打。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 茄子、南瓜用小刀环切，把中心挖出来，沾上少许生粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215977" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215977-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215977-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215977" class="wp-caption-text">茄子、南瓜用小刀环切，把中心挖出来，沾上少许生粉。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 把肉馅酿进茄子和南瓜内备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215978" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215978-60dd11d6d272725428bb1c000da6dc10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215978-60dd11d6d272725428bb1c000da6dc10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215978" class="wp-caption-text">把肉馅酿进茄子和南瓜内备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，放入酿好肉的茄子及南瓜，肉的部分朝下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215979" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215979-76d71b1cc42bb1568729c3c6a43a252f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215979-76d71b1cc42bb1568729c3c6a43a252f-450x218.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="218" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215979" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，放入酿好肉的茄子及南瓜，肉的部分朝下。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 煎至金黄之后，再翻面煎，完成后取出沥干油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215980" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215980-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215980-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215980" class="wp-caption-text">煎至金黄之后，再翻面煎，完成后取出沥干油。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 转中火，锅中加“其它调味料”，再放茄子、南瓜煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215982" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215982-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215982-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215982" class="wp-caption-text">转中火，锅中加“其它调味料”，再放茄子、南瓜煮5分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 将茄子及南瓜装盘。原本的锅中加入粟粉水勾芡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215983" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215983-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215983-9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215983" class="wp-caption-text">将茄子及南瓜装盘。原本的锅中加入粟粉水勾芡。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 把酱汁淋在成品上，放上装饰即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14215984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14215984" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215984-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14215984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14215984-10-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14215984" class="wp-caption-text">把酱汁淋在成品上，放上装饰即完成。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>食谱说明：</strong></span></p>
<p>制作肉馅时，先搅拌均匀再摔打，此举能让肉馅黏得紧一些而不会散掉。摔打时约摔打20下左右。◇</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></p>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
