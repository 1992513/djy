<h2><strong>紫斑向日葵Helianthus annuus </strong><strong>菊科</strong></h2>
<p>原产墨西哥的野生种向日葵，因民国89年满载葵花子的货轮，在彰化外海搁浅，种子漂流至沿海湿地。由于其非常适台湾环境而快速繁衍，目前在大肚溪以南和浊水溪以北数量最多。茎部有紫斑特征，为紫斑向日葵名字的由来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221261-aefe31274dcabd0901c915de92f6c0ac.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14221261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221261-aefe31274dcabd0901c915de92f6c0ac-600x442.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221261" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14221265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221265" style="width: 562px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221265-ba9e9ed411266c788597aaa30fab7b78.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14221265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221265-ba9e9ed411266c788597aaa30fab7b78.png" alt="" width="562" b="759" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221265" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14221264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221264" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221264-2b8177e0ab0b1ef42e60bb76fe78ed4b.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14221264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221264-2b8177e0ab0b1ef42e60bb76fe78ed4b-600x558.png" alt="" width="600" b="558" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221264" class="wp-caption-text">非常适应台湾环境而快速繁衍，目前在大肚溪以南和浊水溪以北数量最多。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近几年在彰化县的沿海区域泛滥。竞争力超强的紫斑向日葵，虽在花期造就了西部海岸的美丽风景，但原有海岸植物的定沙水保的功能，被高大的向日葵植株取代后，在强风豪雨来临时，必定会造成严重的海岸伤害，人民的保障将间接受到威胁，安全堪虑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14221263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14221263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221263-0e1fe5ed973c43e6c60db1fd885aea88.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14221263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14221263-0e1fe5ed973c43e6c60db1fd885aea88-600x440.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="440" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14221263" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
