<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中共驻美大使哈佛演讲 民众会场外表达抗议</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230659-DSC01477-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>4月20日，抗议者们来到哈佛大学肯尼迪学院，在中共大使演讲的场地外表达抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年04月21日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）4月20日早上，在中共驻美大使谢锋来到哈佛大学演讲的同时，各族裔抗议者总共数十人在会议厅外高喊口号，谴责中共的人权暴行和扩张行为。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14230654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14230654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230654-DSC01433.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14230654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230654-DSC01433-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14230654" class="wp-caption-text">4月20日，抗议者们在哈佛肯尼迪学院外向中共驻美大使表达抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14230653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14230653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230653-DSC01428.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14230653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230653-DSC01428-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14230653" class="wp-caption-text">抗议者们在哈佛肯尼迪学院外向中共驻美大使表达抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">“解放香港，释放香港、中国（大陆）的政治犯，解放中国的人权，还中国（大陆）、香港、澳门的人们自由。”抗议者谢中之说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">当天，哈佛大学肯尼迪政府学院中国学社在该学院举办“2024中国论坛”，并邀请中共驻美大使谢锋致词。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">早上8点前后，抗议者们冒着大雨，陆续来到肯尼迪学院室外，向中共的官员及支持者抗议。抗议者撑着雨伞、披着雨衣、举着标语，呼喊“解放香港”“解放西藏”“习近平羞耻”等口号。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14230655" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14230655" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230655-DSC01434.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14230655" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230655-DSC01434-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14230655" class="wp-caption-text">抗议者们在哈佛肯尼迪学院外，冒着大雨向中共驻美大使表达对中共暴政的抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14230657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14230657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230657-DSC01440.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14230657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230657-DSC01440-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14230657" class="wp-caption-text">抗议者们在哈佛肯尼迪学院外，冒着大雨向中共驻美大使表达对中共暴政的抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14230656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14230656" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230656-DSC01437.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14230656" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230656-DSC01437-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14230656" class="wp-caption-text">中共驻美大使谢锋在哈佛大学肯尼迪学院演讲，遭到民众抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">“我们在这里抗议中国发生的情况，他们（中共）对待人民的方式，对众多群体的迫害，包括法轮功学员、维吾尔人、西藏人、地下基督教徒。我们要在你（谢锋）所在的任何地方抗议。因为他（谢锋）来波士顿，所以我们（抗议者）在这里。”抗议者曾麦克说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">在室外抗议者们呼喊的同时，两名哈佛大学学生亦在会场内举牌，向谢锋当面抗议。随后这两名抗议的女生被工作人员带出会场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14230650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14230650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230650-DSC01400.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14230650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230650-DSC01400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14230650" class="wp-caption-text">抗议者们在哈佛肯尼迪学院外，冒着大雨向中共驻美大使表达对中共暴政的抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14230652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14230652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230652-DSC01423.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14230652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230652-DSC01423-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14230652" class="wp-caption-text">抗议者们在哈佛肯尼迪学院外，冒着大雨向中共驻美大使表达对中共暴政的抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14230658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14230658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230658-DSC01453.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14230658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14230658-DSC01453-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14230658" class="wp-caption-text">抗议者们在停车场外，向乘车离开的中共驻美大使表达对中共暴政的抗议。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">上午10:30左右，抗议者们又聚集在肯尼迪学院的停车场外，向乘车离开的谢锋表达对中共暴政的抗议。直到谢锋的车消失之后，大家才离开。◇#</p>
