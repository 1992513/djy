<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】长豆角酿猪肉虾丸～爽口咸甜好滋味！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225375-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>长豆角酿猪肉虾丸（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>“酿”在料理上意思是说，把调制好的肉末塞进某种菜肴里面。“酿菜”料理在中国菜系湘菜、苏菜、粤菜等也各有特色。话说无菜不能酿，在炎热的夏日到来前，来点清新又美味的酿肉料理“长豆角酿猪肉虾丸”，开胃好下饭，而且制作起来简单不费劲，不烧脑！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">主材料：</span></h3>
<p>长豆角25条<br />
碎猪肉5 oz<br />
去谷虾肉5 oz（用刀剁烂成为虾胶）<br />
冬菇粒1 oz<br />
姜、芫荽梗各少许<br />
鸡蛋1个<br />
红葱酱2汤匙<br />
蒜蓉少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">猪肉虾馅调味料：</span></h3>
<p>鸡粉1茶匙<br />
盐1/2茶匙<br />
大地鱼粉1茶匙<br />
粟粉2汤匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味芡汁：</span></h3>
<p>鸡汤1杯<br />
蚝油1汤匙<br />
糖1/2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉水2汤匙（勾芡用）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></h3>
<p>1. 长豆角切去头尾后洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225365" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225365-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225365" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225365-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225365" class="wp-caption-text">长豆角切去头尾后洗干净。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 长豆角放入沸水中烫2分钟，捞出后冲冷水备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225366" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225366-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225366" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225366-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225366" class="wp-caption-text">长豆角放入沸水中烫2分钟，捞出后冲冷水备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 混合所有主材料（长豆角除外），加入所有猪肉虾馅调味料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225367" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225367-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225367-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225367" class="wp-caption-text">混合所有主材料（长豆角除外），加入所有猪肉虾馅调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 豆角卷成扭纹圆圈形状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225368" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225368-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225368-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225368" class="wp-caption-text">豆角卷成扭纹圆圈形状。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放上用猪肉虾馅做成的肉丸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225369" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225369-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225369-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225369" class="wp-caption-text">放上用猪肉虾馅做成的肉丸。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 蒸锅内烧滚水，放入肉丸蒸10分钟后取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225373" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225373-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225373-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225373" class="wp-caption-text">蒸锅内烧滚水，放入肉丸蒸10分钟后取出。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 煮好芡汁，淋在菜肴上，用一些蕃茄片及辣椒花装饰。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225374" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225374-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225374-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225374" class="wp-caption-text">煮好芡汁，淋在菜肴上，用一些蕃茄片及辣椒花装饰。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
