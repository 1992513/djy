<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】意大利美国海鲜汤 ～料多美味 10分钟内完成！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225410-5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>意大利美国海鲜汤。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：想来一碗可口料多的汤品，但是苦无时间熬煮怎么办！“意大利美国海鲜汤”满足您的味蕾，10分钟内就完成！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>材料：</strong></span></h3>
<p>青口、蚬、虾、带子、鱿鱼和鱼肉各适量<br />
高丽菜适量<br />
茄酱葡萄酒汤汁包（包括茄汁、白酒、海鲜清汤、意大利香料）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225406" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225406-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225406-1-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225406" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></h3>
<p>1. 汤煲内放入水1杯、盐少许，放入高丽菜煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225407" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225407" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225407-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225407-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225407" class="wp-caption-text">汤煲内放入水1杯、盐少许，放入高丽菜煮5分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 再放入青口、蚬、虾、带子、鱼肉和鱿鱼，盖上锅盖，煮3分钟即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225408-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225408-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225408" class="wp-caption-text">再放入青口、蚬、虾、带子、鱼肉和鱿鱼，盖上锅盖，煮3分钟即可。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 最后再加入青葱便可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225409-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14225409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225409-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225409" class="wp-caption-text">最后再加入青葱便可食用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">食谱说明：</span></h3>
<p>这次食谱中用的海鲜汤包，是意大利美国口味的茄酱葡萄酒汤包，在超市可以买到。◇</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
