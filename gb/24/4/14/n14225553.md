<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】豆豉炖酿豆腐~电饭锅1键搞定</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225572-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>豆豉炖酿豆腐的汤汁，既爽辣，也有XO酱的鲜美，搭配白米饭滋味妙不可言。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年04月14日讯】这是一道简单的素菜，但风味十足。XO酱如果直接加入汤中炖煮，鲜味很快散失。但酿入豆腐，在留住鲜味的同时使豆腐变薄快速入味。当豆豉、鲜姜、干辣椒炖出的辛辣汤汁与XO酱相遇，鲜香辣爽的多层次汤汁即成，搭配白米饭享用滋味妙不可言。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225574-2-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14225574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225574-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225574" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备</h2>
<p>1/4杯干晒黑豆豆豉<br />
2个干辣椒<br />
1小块带皮新鲜姜<br />
1磅的中硬度豆腐<br />
适量XO酱</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225574" style="width: 594px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225574-2-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14225574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225574-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="594" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225574" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>料理步骤：</h2>
<p>1.姜和辣椒一同切成碎末，混合用水冲洗过的豆豉，铺在锅底。<br />
2.用餐叉在豆腐上扎出网格纹路，再顺着纹路掰成小方块，铺在豆豉上。<br />
3.每块豆腐上面放一小撮XO酱，用筷子尖将酱料轧入豆腐中心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225579-3-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14225579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225579-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225579" class="wp-caption-text">豆豉炖酿豆腐的料理过程。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.加水，但不要没过豆腐。开启（日本）电饭锅煮粥功能，煮1小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14225581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14225581" style="width: 596px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225581-4-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14225581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14225581-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="596" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14225581" class="wp-caption-text">出锅的豆豉炖酿豆腐。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>料理贴士：用叉子掰豆腐是为了让四周形成多孔结构，以挂住更多汤汁和入味。◇</p>
