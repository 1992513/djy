<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2024苗栗客家桐花婚礼 36对佳偶缔结良缘</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229970-677077-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>县长锦东锦表示，鼔励年青人勇敢恋爱，勇敢结婚生小孩；国家须要您们，也会有好政策照顾。（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年04月20日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）2024苗栗客家桐花婚礼，4月20日上午在三义乡西湖渡假村举行，36对佳偶在县长钟东锦伉俪的祝福下，互许终生，迎向幸福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14229977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229977-677084-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229977-677084-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229977" class="wp-caption-text">ALRIGHT以客语演唱，爱的序曲揭开序幕。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14229967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229967" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229967-677074-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229967-677074-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229967" class="wp-caption-text">苗栗客家桐花婚礼。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14229976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229976" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229976-677083-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229976-677083-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229976" class="wp-caption-text">立法院副院长江启臣祝福新人们百年好合、永浴爱河、早生贵子。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苗栗县政府为带动观光、产业发展，每年在桐花盛开时节举办的桐花婚礼，温馨、浪漫，今年迈入第19年，迄今已达1358对佳偶在花树下缔结良缘，留下永生难忘的回忆，是全国最具特色的集团结婚，亦是桐花祭系列活动的重头戏。</p>
<p>今天在西湖渡假村举行的桐花婚礼，以“携手桐心，花开并蒂”为主题，结合传统的客家婚礼仪式，展现隆重喜庆的客家特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14229968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229968-677075-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229968-677075-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229968" class="wp-caption-text">吻定终身。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>婚礼在客家八音伴奏中，以传统古礼迎亲的方式，由3对伴郎、伴娘引领36对佳偶踏上红地毯进场，并由新人江宸睿、张欣融代表佳偶们进行“点烛对拜”等古礼结婚仪式，Alright乐团也为新人献唱，让全场观众们沉浸在幸福歌声中。</p>
<p>新人当中，吴姿颖、吴怡华姐妹档与另一伴相约在苗栗结为连理，双喜临门；竹南地政事务所服务的曾笙富与苗栗县政府原民处服务的噶玛兰族杨雅君，一年前在公务人员受训中相识，今天俩人穿着噶玛兰族传统服饰参加婚礼，充分显示族群融合，大家都是一家人；县府农业处员工蔡雨欣与在竹科工作的吕政颖一年前经由朋友介绍相识，俩人一见钟情，欢喜结为连理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14229969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229969-677076-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229969-677076-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229969" class="wp-caption-text">互戴戒指，情定终身。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参加桐花婚礼的佳偶们，在县长钟东锦及夫人陈美琦、苗栗地方法院院长王汉章福证下，许下爱的誓言；立法院副院长江启臣、县议员陈春暖、黎尚安、卓兰镇长詹锦章、南庄乡长罗春莲、狮潭乡长刘淑能、造桥乡长徐连斌、三湾乡长李运光、三义乡长吕明忠、新人的亲友等共同见证36对佳偶温馨浪漫的幸福时刻，给予永恒祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14229971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229971-677078-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229971-677078-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229971" class="wp-caption-text">亲友祝福。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14229972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229972-677079-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229972-677079-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229972" class="wp-caption-text">县长伉俪与新人合影。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>立法院长江启臣祝福新人们百年好合、永浴爱河、早生贵子；县长钟东锦祝福每对佳偶幸福美满、钟爱一生、白头偕老。</p>
<p>36对新人在浪漫乐声中，宣读结婚誓言，互戴戒指交换信物，最后深情拥吻，真情流露，并和来宾松开手中的气球，象征“幸福升空，甜蜜久久”，现场的亲朋好友及游客们皆报以热烈掌声同享喜悦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14229973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229973-677080-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229973-677080-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229973" class="wp-caption-text">县长伉俪与新人合影。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14229974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229974-677081-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229974-677081-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229974" class="wp-caption-text">县长伉俪与县府新婚同仁合影。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府亦致赠新人幸福6礼，包括西湖渡假村平日双人住宿券（加赠旧山线铁道自行车套票2张)、幸福对戒、手工蚕丝被、电锅、喜饼、嫣然时光礼盒，献上对新人们的祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14229975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14229975" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229975-677082-600x407.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14229975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14229975-677082-600x407.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14229975" class="wp-caption-text">亲友祝福。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
