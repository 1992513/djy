<h6>Toyota Grand Highlander Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年04月06日讯】Toyota的Highlander自推出以来，已经销售了超过330万辆，为了进一步扩展市场，Toyota推出更巨大的Grand Highlander。Grand Highlander有更大的车体，更有不同的动力系统，车内也是跟Highlander有所不同。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d239x61949z6p.cloudfront.net/A4iE2Cz9n"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ejlXAInF3Yk?si=z3I6pZvHNRFEY6PD" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Grand Highlander使用TNGA-K平台，比现时的Highlander拥有更宽的轨距和更长的轴距，尺寸是在Highlander与Sequoia之间。在外形设计方面，Grand Highlander由Calty设计工作室设计，并在印第安纳州普林斯顿的Toyota工厂专门组装。Grand Highlander有独特前进风口及防撞器，尤其是前脸，超巨大的栏栅充满了霸气，可与Lexus LX一比；车尾部设计也与Highlander不一样，设计也比Highlander成熟。车侧的黑色饰边及黑银双色轮圈，呈现出高级感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219252-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219252-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_03-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219252" class="wp-caption-text">Toyota Grand Highlander Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14219253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219253-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_07.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219253-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219253" class="wp-caption-text">巨大栏栅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Grand Highlander将提供三种动力系统，跨越三个不同的等级，为顾客提供各种选择。无论是均衡的2.4升涡轮汽油引擎、高效的2.5升混合动力，还是极致性能的Hybrid MAX，Grand Highlander都能在动力上及耗油量取得平衡。Grand Highlander的汽油引擎可提供最高265匹马力，0-60加速时间为7.5秒（前驱）；混合动力版 (Hybrid）则有245匹马力，0-60加速时间为7.8秒（全驱动）；拥有最大动力的Hybrid MAX，可发出362匹马力，以及400磅呎的扭矩，0-60加速时间为三者最少，只而6.3秒，并且具有5,000磅的拖曳能力。同时，不同动力系统的燃油经济性也有所区别，从24到36英里/加仑不等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219254-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_15.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219254-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_15-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219254" class="wp-caption-text">Hybrid MAX动力系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所有三种动力系统都配备了三种驾驶模式（运动，经济，正常），汽油全驱动和混合动力MAX动力系统上提供了三种多地形选择模式（泥沙，岩石，雪地），以提供卓越的越野能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219255-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_24.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219255-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219255" class="wp-caption-text">三种驾驶模式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>汽油版的AWD可以主动在前后轴之间以及两个后轮之间分配扭力。它使用在后差速器前的电磁耦合器，根据需要启动或停用后轮。混合动力版则使用Electronic On-Demoand AWD，提供了出色的燃油效率、起步加速、操控和稳定性。使用电动马达驱动后轮，在湿滑路面可提供稳定的性能。前后轮驱动力分配在100:0和20:80之间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219256-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219256-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219256" class="wp-caption-text">车尾部设计与普通Highlander不一样。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Hybrid MAX版的Full-Time Electronic AWD提供更出色的可控性，通过前置混合动力马达和后置eAxle电动马达将动力输送到前后轮。这个系统直接与变速箱搭配，前后轮动力分配在70:30和20:80之间，具体取决于行驶情况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219257-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_32.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219257-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_32-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219257" class="wp-caption-text">Grand Highlander拥有三排座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Grand Highlander的设计也注重实用性和舒适性。从宽敞的内部空间到智能的储物设计，从中控台的配置到座椅的舒适性，每个细节都考虑到了乘客的需求。虽然车厢是以黑色为主，还好在不少位置以铜色作衬托，也带有高贵感。无论是第一排、第二排还是第三排，每个座位都配备了USB端口和储物空间，让乘客可以随时随地充电和储存物品。整个车舱设有周到的储物空间，以及充足的货物容量，第三排后方可容纳七个随身行李箱。当第二排和第三排座椅折叠下来时，提供了总共97.5立方呎的空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219258-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_31.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219258-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219258" class="wp-caption-text">全部设计注重实用和舒适性。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丰富的技术配置使Grand Highlander更加智能和便捷。从12.3英寸的触摸屏到蓝牙连接、智能助手和云端导航，再到JBL音响系统和连接服务，这款车型满足了现代人对科技的各种需求。装置的语音智能助手，可透过简单语句进行搜索路线、调整音频控制、改变车内温度等。云端导航可以提供最新的地图、交通和路线信息。同时，系统也支持无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219259-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219259-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219259" class="wp-caption-text">12.3英寸的触摸屏。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于发烧友来说，Grand Highlander可升级到JBL高级音频系统，拥有11个扬声器，可以在任何音乐类型中打造移动音乐会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219260-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219260-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219260" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>总的来说，丰田Grand Highlander是一款既实用又豪华的SUV，它将为驾驶者和乘客带来无与伦比的驾乘体验，无论是在城市中穿梭还是在郊区探险，都能展现出色的性能和便利性。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14219261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14219261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219261-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14219261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id14219261-2024_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14219261" class="wp-caption-text">全景天窗可让阳光照入车厢。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
