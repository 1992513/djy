<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>石壁绣球花守护者赖淑娟 让花期绵延全年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262532-686238-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>绣球花非常喜爱水分。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）八仙花（又称绣球花）因花卉大且美丽，深受花友喜爱。八仙花品种繁多，花色各异，每个品种适宜的生长环境略有不同。以下介绍几种常见的盆栽品种：</p>
<p>雷古拉：叶小、紧密，花淡绿白色，是由德国选育的品种。紫茎绣球花：茎暗紫色，叶椭圆形，花蔷薇色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262534-686240.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262534-686240-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262534" class="wp-caption-text">最好的赏绣球花期是五至六月。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全台各地都有美丽的赏花区，特别推荐云林古坑石壁杉木林步道的绣球花海，因为这里是志工赖淑娟，用三年时间打造出来的，因为很漂亮，吸引游客慕名而来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262524" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262524-686230.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262524-686230-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262524" class="wp-caption-text">游客停下来用手机拍照打卡。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>赖淑娟因为在杉木林步道起点摆摊的关系，空档时间，她沿路种了无数的绣球花，为了照顾和种植更广一些，她还去进修学习，很幸运的是绣球花在石壁，虽然花期最好在五至六月，但因为这里的气候非常适合它，所以整年都能欣赏到它的丰彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262525" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262525-686231.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262525-686231-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262525" class="wp-caption-text">三年前赖淑娟利用摆摊空档，从不会成为种绣球花的达人。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石壁居民赖淑娟：“绝窍就是剪枝选在9月至12月进行，因为这时花期已经结束，植物需要一段时间来酝酿明年的花芽。至于施肥，绣球花在冬天会进入冬眠状态，所以最佳施肥时间是春天。施肥种类并没有特别要求，可以使用茶园常用的氮肥或有机肥，但氮肥含量应稍高一些，这样可以促进新芽的生长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262533" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262533-686239.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262533-686239-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262533" class="wp-caption-text">往云岭之丘的路旁，可见绣球花。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14262523" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262523" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262523-686229.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262523-686229-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262523" class="wp-caption-text">在石壁可见到三十几种绣球花。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>土壤的酸碱度会影响花的颜色。酸性土壤会使绣球花呈现蓝色，而碱性土壤则会使绣球花变成粉红色。因此，根据个人喜好，可以调整土壤的酸碱度来改变花色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262527" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262527" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262527-686233.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262527" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262527-686233-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262527" class="wp-caption-text">赖淑娟剪枝会选在9月至12月进行。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>若将枝条修剪得较少，花朵会长得更大。然而，对于像“大叶绣球花”这类品种，由于花朵重量较重，赖淑娟不建议让花朵长得太大，否则容易倒伏。她说:“绣球花非常喜爱水分。在平地种植时，她建议每天早晚各浇一次水。在我们这边，夏天通常不需要浇水，但冬天则需要持续浇水。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262526" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262526" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262526-686232.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262526" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262526-686232-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262526" class="wp-caption-text">山紫阳绣球花。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于新手来说，赖淑娟建议:“先种“大叶绣球花”或“无尽夏”品种。这些品种最容易种植，也最容易开花，能让你有很大的成就感。不过，由于平地太热，所以夏天要把它当作室内植物来养，或者种在大树下面或阳台里，避免直接暴露在阳光下。”若让它在大太阳下暴晒，会导致叶片烧焦，从而影响花朵的生长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262531-686237.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262531-686237-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262531" class="wp-caption-text">八仙花（又称绣球花）因花卉大且美丽。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绣球花因花卉大且美丽。（廖俪芬／摄影）</p>
<p>杉木林步道附近有一些值得一游的景点，她推荐:“例如五元两角和云岭之丘、五元两角与我这边相连接，形成了一条绣球花的环线。八月份时，我会再种植大约五千颗绣球花，这些花都是我自己阡插培育的。”此外，县政府也有规划要在五元两角景点种植绣球花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262519-686225.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262519-686225-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262519" class="wp-caption-text">云岭之丘的视野，辽阔美丽。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绣球花刚开时，像大叶绣球花这个品种，最初颜色较绿，接着变浅白，再逐渐转为水蓝色，最后颜色会变得更深。杉木林步道的绣球花品种多达三十多种，其中，“无尽夏”是长年开花的品种，开花性非常好。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262530" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262530-686236.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262530-686236-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262530" class="wp-caption-text">从杉木林步道前往五元二角景点，路旁可见绣球花。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>赏花勿折花</h2>
<p>三年来，赖淑娟在水池边、五元二角路上、杉木林步道旁，种了许多的绣球花，但游客会将小棵的花苗拔回家，她恳请大家一起爱护山林，让她的家乡除了二月赏樱花之外，还能吸引游客上山欣赏绣球花，草岭和石壁疫情这几年，观光客少了许多，撑不住的年轻人，被迫下山找工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262528" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262528" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262528-686234.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262528-686234-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262528" class="wp-caption-text">石壁因赖淑娟无私的付出而美丽。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石壁批萨店主人苏大昆也提到花莲大地震后，游客不敢上山，现在又是梅雨季，连周六、日都没有游客，他很感谢赖淑娟长年在杉林步道旁，种下美丽的绣球花，让石壁的观光出现新亮点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262529" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262529" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262529-686235.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262529" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262529-686235-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262529" class="wp-caption-text">苏大昆的窑烤批萨。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
