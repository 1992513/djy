<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>梅山太和山上 牛爸的故事</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262278-686076-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>柴烧茶仓是钟少博自己柴烧出来的。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾嘉义报导）钟少博住在嘉义梅山太和山上，虽然已经年过八旬，但他的学习能力、各种工艺能力、盖房子能力以及种茶能力依然令人惊叹。</p>
<p>他不仅精通陶艺和藤编，还曾出国比赛并获得冠军。钟少博个性开朗，为了家乡不惜付出一切，亲自拉水管、修路、建造柴烧窑。他曾经被三百多只虎头蜂围攻，但即使在八八风灾中家园被毁，他也未曾失去对生活的热爱，依然继续为家乡奉献自己的一切。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262281-686079.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262281-686079-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262281" class="wp-caption-text">牛爸精通各项工艺。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14262275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262275" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262275-686073.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262275-686073-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262275" class="wp-caption-text">钟少博在八八风灾时失去家园。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>牛坞茶屋的诞生</h2>
<p>钟少博亲手建造了一间茶屋，成为梅山太和地区的地标，并命名为“牛坞”，这是因为大家都叫他“牛爸”。这间茶屋的建造过程完全由他自己设计和施工，没有经过任何专业训练。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262282-686080.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262282-686080-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262282" class="wp-caption-text">太和地标“牛坞”，牛爸的泡茶空间。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14262277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262277-686075.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262277-686075-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262277" class="wp-caption-text">活动式的窗户可以看到塔山。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他使用细钢管现场调整结构，设计出了精巧的内部结构，并在茶屋内设有活动窗户，以确保通风良好。整个建造过程历时一个月，展现了钟少博惊人的手工技艺和坚韧不拔的精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262284-686082.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262284-686082-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262284" class="wp-caption-text">牛坞里的物件，全部是牛爸亲手做出来的。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>茶与柴烧工艺</h2>
<p>钟少博不仅种茶、喝茶、泡茶，还亲手制作茶具。他学习柴烧技艺，第一天就捏制了一个茶壶，并用自己烧制的茶仓来存茶。他认为这些手工制作的茶具，因为留有手纹而更加有意思。这些独特的茶具，展示了钟少博对工艺的热爱和追求完美的态度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262276-686074.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262276-686074-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262276" class="wp-caption-text">茶盘、茶杯、茶壶都是牛爸亲手做的。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>竹编工艺与重建家园的梦想</h2>
<p>钟少博从小学习竹编工艺，他制作的藤编椅子结构牢固、设计独特，他说:“完全不用钉子，而是采用矛楯技术。他用孟宗竹剥制竹篾，这些竹篾韧性和硬度兼具，制作出来的椅子既耐用又美观。”在八八风灾中，牛爸的家园被毁，但他并未气馁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262279" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262279-686077.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262279-686077-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262279" class="wp-caption-text">牛爸做的椅子，传统工艺底蕴尽现。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他曾翻山越岭，亲手从阿里山拉水管、修路，为的是方便自己和邻里使用。所以干旱时，太和并没有缺水问题。八八风灾后，他在邻居的帮助下，用轻钢架搭建了临时住所。钟少博坚定地表示，虽然已经八十多岁，他仍有信心重建自己的家园，让家人安心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262285-686083.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262285-686083-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262285" class="wp-caption-text">牛爸从阿里山拉水管到太和，让家乡躲过干旱。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>清心乌龙与大难不死</h2>
<p>三十年前，钟少博回到家乡种茶。他的茶园位于1250公尺的高海拔地区，种植的清心乌龙，茶叶品质不输高山茶，产量高且风味独特，有花香和果香等多种风味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262280" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262280-686078.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262280-686078-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262280" class="wp-caption-text">茶则、茶叶也是牛爸做的。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钟少博说:“四十多岁开始参加装置艺术展览，并在捷克的比赛中脱颖而出，从七十六个国家中夺得冠军。”他的竹艺装置艺术作品在国内外多地展出，赢得了广泛赞誉。后来陆续在台中、屏东、嘉义也参与多项装置艺术的展览。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262283-686081.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262283-686081-600x437.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262283" class="wp-caption-text">当年在捷克比赛，现场制作的情况。（钟少博提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>二十多年前，钟少博曾因拉水管而被三百多只虎头蜂叮咬，命悬一线。最终，经过太和仁和国小的童子尿治疗，尔后送到嘉义地区医院、成大医院后，最后因台南胜利国小的一万多cc童子尿治疗下，奇迹般地痊愈。这场生死劫难后，钟少博更加珍惜生命，继续为家乡奉献他的智慧和力量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262286-686084.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262286-686084-600x500.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262286" class="wp-caption-text">牛爸的茶园。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钟少博的故事，是一个关于坚持与奉献的传奇。他的无私精神和多才多艺，不仅让家乡人骄傲，也成为我们学习的榜样。钟少博用他的行动告诉我们，无论面对什么困难，只要有坚定的信念和不屈不挠的精神，必能战胜一切困境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262287-686085.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262287-686085-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262287" class="wp-caption-text">牛爸的装置艺术作品。（钟少博提供）</figcaption></figure>
