<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨声音乐社85周年演出 爆满落幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264032-DSC02973-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿侨声音乐剧社6月1日带来85周年庆典粤剧演出《梦断香销四十年》，演绎陆游和唐婉的悲欢离合。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月05日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“桃花落，闲池阁。山盟虽在，锦书难托。莫！莫！莫！”伴随着深情婉转的唱词，舞台大幕缓缓拉开，展现出古色古香的厅堂场景。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这是经典粤剧《梦断香销四十年》的开场，亦是波士顿侨声音乐剧社的85周年庆典演出。6月1日晚在波士顿派拉蒙剧场（Paramount Theatre），超过3个小时的演出收获满场观众的热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264028" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264028-DSC02926-HDR.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264028-DSC02926-HDR-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264028" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿侨声音乐剧社演出《梦断香销四十年》，观众爆满。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264027" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264027-DSC02922.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264027-DSC02922-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264027" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿侨声音乐剧社演出《梦断香销四十年》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">据司仪李卢莲馨介绍，为了这场粤剧演出，侨声成员们筹备了半年以上。这次还有来自纽约的乐师共襄盛举，全部工作人员有近70人。大家全力以赴，力求为观众呈现尽善尽美的演出。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">参与幕后工作的侨声社长黄国威，以及义工何远光、郑慧民等人也说，侨声赠送了很多演出票给华埠长者住房的居民，以尽服务社区的责任。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264033" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264033-DSC02981.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264033-DSC02981-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264033" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿侨声音乐剧社演出《梦断香销四十年》，观众爆满。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264037" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264037-DSC03034.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264037-DSC03034-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264037" class="wp-caption-text">侨声85周年庆典演出，（左起）何远光、黄国威、郑慧民在幕后服务。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这次演出的剧目《梦断香销四十年》，标题出自陆游的诗《沈园》：“梦断香销四十年，沈园柳老不吹绵。?此身行作稽山土，犹吊遗踪一泫然。”这部粤剧于1984年在广东首演，次年亦在香港演出1个月，均现轰动效应。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264031" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264031" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264031-DSC02965.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264031-DSC02965-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264031" class="wp-caption-text">陆游被母亲要求休弃唐婉。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264029" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264029-DSC02931.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264029-DSC02931-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264029" class="wp-caption-text">伍国荣饰演陆游。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264030-DSC02951.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264030-DSC02951-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264030" class="wp-caption-text">雷玉霞饰演唐婉。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">《梦断香销四十年》讲述了宋代诗人陆游和首任妻子唐婉的悲欢离合。在侨声音乐剧社的演出中，伍国荣饰演陆游，雷玉霞饰演唐婉，靓筱声饰演赵仕程，黄绮玲饰演王春娥；剧中其他配角，以及幕后的乐师，也多是波士顿侨界颇有名气的演艺人士。侨声另一位社长李伍碧香还用英语为现场的非华人观众介绍了剧情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264034" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264034-DSC03017.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264034-DSC03017-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264034" class="wp-caption-text">唐婉改嫁赵仕程，陆游再娶王春娥，2对新人，4种心情。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264038-DSC03039.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264038-DSC03039-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264038" class="wp-caption-text">多年后，陆游与唐婉重逢，心中别有感触。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264039-DSC03051.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264039-DSC03051-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264039" class="wp-caption-text">陆游题写名作《钗头凤》，壁上文字由电脑特效逐句显示。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这场演出还有令人意想不到的欢乐插曲。在舞台工作人员更换道具、妆容期间，司仪李卢莲馨为观众讲起了“脱口秀”，风趣幽默地与观众分享她对“中老年危机”的看法，提醒大家不要过度焦虑，令满场观众连连欢笑。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在现场观看演出的中华公所主席雷国辉对节目效果表达了赞许，并称赞侨声为大家带来了传统粤曲享受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264036-DSC03029.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264036-DSC03029-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264036" class="wp-caption-text">换场期间，司仪李卢莲馨为观众讲起“脱口秀”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264040-DSC03065.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264040-DSC03065-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264040" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾们展示波士顿市议会赠送的褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">本次侨声音乐剧社的85周年庆典演出，得到了诸多机构和个人的赞助。中华公所亦向其捐赠5000美元。波士顿市议会还向侨声颁赠褒奖。◇</p>
