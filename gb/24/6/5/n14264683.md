<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯哪里拥枪数量最多？</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月01日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">新的数据显示，珀斯都市区注册枪击的数量超过</span><span lang="en-AU">15</span><span lang="zh-TW">万只。同时</span><span lang="zh-TW">平均</span><span lang="zh-TW">每天就有一人因家暴被吊销枪支许可证。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，上周五</span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-AU">5</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">24</span><span lang="zh-TW">日）</span><span lang="zh-TW">在珀斯</span><span lang="en-AU">Floreat</span><span lang="zh-TW">区</span><span lang="zh-TW">发生了一起</span><span lang="zh-TW">骇人听闻的枪击案。</span></p>
<p>事发当日，<span lang="en-AU">63</span>岁的邦巴拉（<span lang="en-AU">Mark Bombara</span>）去自己前妻的好友佩特尔奇克（<span lang="en-AU">Jennifer Petelczyc</span>）家去找前妻。佩特尔奇克住在<span lang="en-AU">Floreat</span>区。<span lang="zh-TW">儅邦巴拉没有找到前妻</span><span lang="zh-TW">后</span><span lang="zh-TW">，就开枪打死了佩特尔奇克与其一起居住的女儿格雷特尔（</span><span lang="en-AU">Gretl Petelczyc</span><span lang="zh-TW">），随后邦巴拉开枪自尽身亡。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">该枪击案再次引发了舆论对珀斯枪支管制的关注。</span><span lang="zh-TW">《西澳人报》本周获得西澳警方的一份最新数据。数据显示，在六个月内，西澳警方利用限制令没收了</span><span lang="en-AU">200</span><span lang="zh-TW">多人的枪支。目前珀斯都市区居民拥有的注册枪支仍有</span><span lang="en-AU">151,404</span><span lang="zh-TW">支。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在</span><span lang="en-AU">Midland</span><span lang="zh-TW">警区注册的枪支数量最多，达</span><span lang="en-AU">30,556</span><span lang="zh-TW">支，该警区覆盖</span><span lang="en-AU">Ellenbrook</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-AU">Mundaring</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-AU">Forrestfield</span><span lang="zh-TW">地区；其次是</span><span lang="en-AU">Mandurah</span><span lang="zh-TW">警区，</span><span lang="en-AU">26,150</span><span lang="zh-TW">支；</span><span lang="en-AU">Joondalup</span><span lang="zh-TW">警区，</span><span lang="en-AU">24,739</span><span lang="zh-TW">支；以及</span><span lang="en-AU">Armadale</span><span lang="zh-TW">警区，</span><span lang="en-AU">21,357</span><span lang="zh-TW">支；</span><span lang="en-AU">Fremantle</span><span lang="zh-TW">警区，</span><span lang="en-AU">17,575</span><span lang="zh-TW">支</span><span lang="zh-TW">；</span><span lang="en-AU">Mirrabooka</span><span lang="zh-TW">警区，</span><span lang="en-AU">14,914</span><span lang="zh-TW">支</span><span lang="zh-TW">；</span><span lang="en-AU">Cannington</span><span lang="zh-TW">警区，</span><span lang="en-AU">10,361</span><span lang="zh-TW">支。在珀斯市区警区，覆盖</span><span lang="en-AU">Mosman</span> Park<span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-AU">CBD</span><span lang="zh-TW">和西区（</span><span lang="en-AU">Floreat</span><span lang="zh-TW">谋杀案凶手在此注册了</span><span lang="en-AU">13</span><span lang="zh-TW">支枪），注册的枪支为</span><span lang="en-AU">5752</span><span lang="zh-TW">支。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">西澳偏远地区注册枪支数量为</span><span lang="en-AU">175,409</span><span lang="zh-TW">支，西澳州总共有超过</span><span lang="en-AU">326,813</span><span lang="zh-TW">支注册枪支，这一数字还不包括最近政府出台的价值</span><span lang="en-AU">6430</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的回购计划中上缴给州政府的</span><span lang="en-AU">12,000</span><span lang="zh-TW">支枪支。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">新数据还显示，在</span><span lang="en-AU">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-TW">日至</span><span lang="en-AU">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">31</span><span lang="zh-TW">日期间，西澳警方因家庭暴力或暴力限制令吊销了</span><span lang="en-AU">215</span><span lang="zh-TW">个枪支许可证。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">4</span><span lang="zh-TW">月份有</span><span lang="en-AU">13</span><span lang="zh-TW">起持抢人因受限制令影响而被没收枪支，在向州行政法庭上诉后已归还枪支的案件。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据《枪支法》，受暴力限制令约束的持抢人不被视为继续拥有枪支的“合适和恰当的人”。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">这些数据似乎与西澳警察局长布兰奇（</span><span lang="en-AU">Col</span> Blanch<span lang="zh-TW">）的说法相悖，他称法庭以令人担忧的速度归还了许可证，阻止警官对邦巴拉采取同样的行动。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">而在上周五谋杀案发生前的几周里，邦巴拉的前妻和女儿曾要求警方对他们母女发出临时保护令。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">邦巴拉的女儿阿里尔（</span><span lang="en-AU">Ariel</span><span lang="zh-TW">）在周二（5月28日）的一份声明中声称，这一要求被拒绝了。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">布兰奇周二说：“目前，州行政法庭在决定何时不适合没收枪支方面似乎有很大的自由裁量权或指示。”“我们已经向州行政法庭提起了许多案件，但都败诉，不得不归还那些枪支。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">但最近的一次听证会显示，法庭拒绝将枪支归还给一名在短短六个月内四次违反家庭暴力限制令的西南部男子。</span></p>
