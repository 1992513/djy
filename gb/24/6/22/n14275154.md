<h6>2024 Subaru Solterra Touring。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月22日讯】早在2021年Subaru已在洛杉矶国际车展上发表了Solterra，这车也已经推出市场，去年测试过它的兄弟车Toyota bZ4X，作为Toyota首辆纯电动车，表现虽是不错，但还有很多进步空间，而Subaru Solterra表现又怎样？</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://dhw62qyyxrv4n.cloudfront.net/Q9o33fNFq"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/jGyUG6gtLUY?si=LeNWDmFbEOuN1yaU" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Toyota bZ4X的名字别具意义，“bZ”代表了“ Beyond Zero”。Subaru Solterra则是拉丁文的太阳与大地的结合。尺寸上，Subaru Solterra与Toyota bZ4X基本上一样，车长与车宽都是184.6吋与73.2吋，只是车高比bZ4X的69吋为矮，只有65吋。Solterra跟bZ4x的其中分别是离地距，bZ4x只有8.1吋，而Solterra则有8.3吋，0.2吋的差别不是很大，但在越野时的进场角及离去角会有影响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275155-2024_Subaru_Solterra_05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275155-2024_Subaru_Solterra_05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275155" class="wp-caption-text">图片2024_Subaru_Solterra_05.jpg：2024 Subaru Solterra Touring。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>既然是与bZ4X有血缘关系，所以外形上Solterra也是有90%以上相似，如四个黑色轮拱，车尾的大分叉车灯设计等，都是跟bZ4X一样。当然，为了营造两车的分别，Solterra的前脸设计就有点分别，车侧充电口也加上大大的“EV”。头灯每边是由四个LED组成，上方有一条LED灯作为“日间行车灯”，车头下方装有两个圆圆的雾灯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275156-2024_Subaru_Solterra_09.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275156-2024_Subaru_Solterra_09-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275156" class="wp-caption-text">Solterra的前脸也跟bZ4X不一样。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>得益于e-TNGA专用平台，锂离子电池组平放在地板下，使Solterra拥有低重心和高刚性的优点。同时，也让Solterra获得极大的车厢空间，前座乘客拥有舒适的42.1吋脚部空间，后座乘客有35.3吋的脚部空间。载货区域体积为23.8立方呎。</p>
<p>Solterra分为两个级别，分别为Premium、Limited和 Touring，bZ4X的每一个型号是有FWD及AWD可选择，但Solterra统一全部均装有Subaru著名的Symmetrical AWD系统，而Premium售价也只是由44,995美元起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275157-2024_Subaru_Solterra_07.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275157-2024_Subaru_Solterra_07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275157" class="wp-caption-text">头灯由四个LED组成。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>仪表板是一个细小的萤幕，只显示车速、电池容量、续航距离及累积里数等，简单清楚，四方向手动的方向盘是手动的，但是最高及最低分别不大，无论座位高度调整如何，都会使方向盘顶部遮盖了部分仪表板，最后只有调高座位，将方向盘调低，才可看清楚仪表板。在方向盘前方有一个像红外线的灯条，用以检验驾驶者的集中度，但往往被笔者握着方向盘的手遮着，经常发出警告，非常扰人。方向盘本身不是一般的圆形，而是接近方形，有点特别，便用上也顺手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275159-2024_Subaru_Solterra_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275159-2024_Subaru_Solterra_20-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275159" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板是一个细小的萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Solterra的加速板是设定与普通汽车一样，在放开时，汽车会滑行，只会慢慢减速；Subaru也为Solterra加上One Step Control 按键，按下后汽车在没有踏下加速板时，会比较快慢下来。变速转盘只有三个档位，分别是D、R及N，把转盘按下去是N，顺时针转是D、反时针转是R，P档是转盘上的按键，使用上也方便，手刹车是电子式，在转入D档时自动解开，在按下P按键时又会自动锁上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275160" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275160-2024_Subaru_Solterra_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275160-2024_Subaru_Solterra_22-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275160" class="wp-caption-text">变速转盘只有三个档位。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Solterra Premium标配8吋萤幕，而Limited及Touring则是12.3吋宽屏，配音频多媒体系统，可经无线（OTA）随时更新。车主可透过云端导航。 智能助手可帮助来搜索方向、寻找地点、调整音频控制、改变车内温度等等。云端导航是车载导航，利用云下载最新地图、即时交通和路线信息，搜索功能及POI由通过Google服务提供。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275162-2024_Subaru_Solterra_30.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275162-2024_Subaru_Solterra_30-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275162" class="wp-caption-text">Solterra Touring 标配12.3吋宽屏。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Solterra使用StarDrive动力系统，马力输出为215匹，扭矩为249磅呎。Solterra Touring型号的续航里数约222英里，只有Premium型号的续航里程可达227英里。配备X-MODE越野模式，X-MODE有两种模式，分别为Snow/Dirt和DeepSnow/Mud，以应对湿滑或不平坦的路面。对于崎岖不平的道路，系统会对车轮施加制动，以产生限滑差速器 (LSD) 效果以增加牵引力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275164-2024_Subaru_Solterra_23.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275164-2024_Subaru_Solterra_23-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275164" class="wp-caption-text">配备X-MODE越野模式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14275166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275166-2024_Subaru_Solterra_25.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275166-2024_Subaru_Solterra_25-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275166" class="wp-caption-text">X-MODE Snow/Dirt模式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为 Solterra供电有多种选择，包括120V和240V充电器以及直流快速充电器。Solterra配备J1772/CCS1插座，可用于家庭和公共充电站。车载了6.6 kW充电器让 bZ4X可使用2级充电器，在约9.5小时内从低电量充电到充满电。Solterra如以DC快充的话，则由10%到80%只需35分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275168-2024_Subaru_Solterra_31.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275168-2024_Subaru_Solterra_31-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275168" class="wp-caption-text">虚拟后视镜。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>使用了bZ4X的平台及马达，再加上Subaru的Symmetric AWD四驱系统，以及Subaru的EyeSight驾驶辅助系统，既有Toyota的品质跟电动车的好处，又有Subaru的越野技术，价钱又差不多少，您会选择bZ4X还是Solterra呢？◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14275169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14275169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275169-2024_Subaru_Solterra_13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14275169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14275169-2024_Subaru_Solterra_13-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14275169" class="wp-caption-text">StarDrive动力系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
