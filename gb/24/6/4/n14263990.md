<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蒙特娄台湾大专校友会千岛湖一日游</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264017-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_1-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>众人在千岛湖游船前，开心合影留念。(蒙特娄台湾大专校友会提供) </h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月03日讯】（蒙特娄台湾大专校友会供稿）2024年6月1日周六，蒙特娄台湾大专校友会举办千岛湖和上加拿大村的一日游，这是本年度大专校友会举办的首场旅游活动，由当地知名旅行社协办。活动海报在各大社群媒体贴出后，立即吸引许多侨民踊跃报名。有些侨民定居蒙城几十年，这还是首次去千岛湖游玩。</p>
<p>当日清晨7点多，一行人怀着兴奋期待的心情出发前往千岛湖，车窗外阳光明媚，沿路上众人欢声笑语不断。</p>
<p>由至少1,800座以上的小岛构成的千岛湖，湖水碧蓝深邃，一望无际的湖面点缀以各式风格的城堡建筑，“繁星般的精灵花园”此封号绝非浪得虚名。间有绿荫环绕岛屿，大伙时而赞叹岛屿原生态的鬼斧神工，时而陶醉于复古建筑的怡然恬静，无限的旖霓风光尽收眼底。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264018" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264018-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_2-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264018" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264018-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264018" class="wp-caption-text">千岛湖上如繁星点缀的复古城堡建筑。（蒙特娄台湾大专校友会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264021-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_3-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264021-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264021" class="wp-caption-text">大伙在上加拿大村留下温馨的合影。（蒙特娄台湾大专校友会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>闲适午后，沿着上加拿大村（Upper Canada Village）的小径散步，马蹄声滴答滴答由远而近，马车伕驾着游客造访村内的特色建筑与景观。坐在马车上顺着河道望去，正有一匹骏马以绳索拖拉河道上的船只前进，古时候的动力运作重现眼前。造船、锡壶、畜牧养殖、烘培、修鞋等传统技艺在村内各工作坊殷实展示，于是19世纪60年代上加拿大村的生活影像鲜活起来，仿佛穿越时空，体验昔时百姓的生活状态和民俗技艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14264023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264023" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264023-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_4-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264023-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264023" class="wp-caption-text">上加拿大村内随处可见身着复古装束的工作人员，他们将古时候的生活型态原汁原味呈现出来。（蒙特娄台湾大专校友会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264025" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264025-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_5-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264025-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264025" class="wp-caption-text">上加拿大村入口处的火车站。（蒙特娄台湾大专校友会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14264026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14264026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264026-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_6-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14264026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14264026-20240601_Montreal_Taiwan_thousandIslandTour_6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14264026" class="wp-caption-text">马匹拖船”的复古体验！（蒙特娄台湾大专校友会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>夕阳向晚，一行人驱车返回蒙特娄，经历全天候的旅游，大家仍游兴不减，纷纷询问“我们何时再出来玩呢？”这场以闲适步调体会丰富游兴的旅程，为侨民搭起友谊的网络，也为蒙特娄台湾大专校友会写下新的欢乐篇章。</p>
