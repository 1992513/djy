<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马祖碱粽的独特绑法与美味吃法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266675-687572-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>马祖庆端午。（马祖国家风景区管理处粉丝专页提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月08日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾马祖报导）每逢端午节，马祖碱粽总是餐桌上的常客，这道美食不仅美味，更蕴含着深厚的传统工艺与文化。马祖碱粽的主要食材是糯米、碱块、花生和红豆，看似简单，但制作过程却大有学问。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266676" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266676-687573.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14266676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266676-687573-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266676" class="wp-caption-text">马祖传统碱粽。（马祖国家风景区管理处粉丝专页提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>包碱粽的过程可谓是一门艺术，从一开始的卷粽叶就充满挑战。许多民众想尝试，卷了好几次，叶子都破了还没卷好。然而，看着长辈们俐落地卷好粽叶、放入馅料，再以筷子搓实，整个过程行云流水，不到一分钟就完成了一个漂亮的碱粽。长辈们笑说，这技术可是从小拿沙子练出来的呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266677" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266677-687574.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14266677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266677-687574-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266677" class="wp-caption-text">长辈说从小用沙子练习，才有今天的功力。（马祖国家风景区管理处粉丝专页提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>包好的碱粽需要经过3-4小时的水煮，再闷3-4小时，才能达到软糯的口感。老一辈的吃法是直接品尝，而现代人则喜欢沾蜂蜜、糖浆或砂糖。小编个人偏爱冰过后再沾糖浆，口感特别佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266679" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266679-687576.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14266679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266679-687576-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266679" class="wp-caption-text">许多民众想尝试，叶子都破了还没卷好。（马祖国家风景区管理处粉丝专页提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>马祖碱粽还有一个特别之处，就是它独特的绑法。与一般散状绑法（一串绑一颗）不同，碱粽采用鞭炮状绑法，需要一定技术，也别有一番美感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266678" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266678-687575.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14266678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266678-687575-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266678" class="wp-caption-text">碱粽采用鞭炮状绑法。（马祖国家风景区管理处粉丝专页提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>马祖碱粽，不仅是美味的佳肴，更是一段传统文化的体现。端午节，一起品味这道充满工艺之美的传统美食吧！</p>
