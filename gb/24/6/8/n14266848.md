<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多举办All For One齐聚一堂多元文化节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266849-6a005251bc3c4bbd8f482722094d9f64-600x400.png" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月09日讯】齐聚一堂多元文化节（All For One Multicultural Festival）首届活动，将于6月21日至23日在多伦多士嘉堡区（Scarborough）举行。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d1jex6syje0yji.cloudfront.net/F5zdsf6Zn"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/cPg1RauUB6c?wmode=transparent&#038;wmode=opaque" allowfullscreen></a>
</div>
<p class="p1">这是由菲律宾菲律宾遗产与文化联盟（Philippine Legacy And Cultural Alliance，简称PLACA）和Woodside Square购物中心合作，首次举办，旨在展示社区丰富的多元文化。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/8/n14266848.md#1/941539e044a70de87274c2806ef433d8" rel="attachment wp-att-14266851"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14266851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266851-941539e044a70de87274c2806ef433d8-450x269.png" alt="" width="606" b="362" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">活动最初在2020年由PLACA构想，现在已经成为一个现场节日，庆祝加拿大多元文化的丰富多元。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/8/n14266848.md#1/5f8f81f03f8aaa29cf8bd5e43ad46c24" rel="attachment wp-att-14266855"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14266855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266855-5f8f81f03f8aaa29cf8bd5e43ad46c24-450x269.png" alt="" width="606" b="362" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">出席者可以期待一场感官盛宴，包括国际美食、正宗的文化产品、嘉年华游乐设施，以及来自参与活动的各民族社区的迷人表演。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/8/n14266848.md#1/467c58016b4415e3a1ade63948e14b6f" rel="attachment wp-att-14266852"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14266852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266852-467c58016b4415e3a1ade63948e14b6f-450x269.png" alt="" width="606" b="362" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">作为跨文化交流和合作的平台，All For One多元文化节将展示每个合作伙伴社区的独特贡献。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/8/n14266848.md#1/da168b6fc8b38828a3f8d887a0809d02" rel="attachment wp-att-14266854"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14266854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266854-da168b6fc8b38828a3f8d887a0809d02-450x269.png" alt="" width="606" b="362" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">准备好让自己沉浸在前所未有的多样性庆典中吧。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/8/n14266848.md#1/70d7f6509d1e10ae8ec89002f4320d52" rel="attachment wp-att-14266853"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14266853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266853-70d7f6509d1e10ae8ec89002f4320d52-450x269.png" alt="" width="606" b="362" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">来All For One多元文化节吧！品尝精致的美味，探索独特的文化宝藏，畅玩惊心动魄的嘉年华游乐设施，观赏令人着迷的现场娱乐表演，享受惊艳的烟花。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/8/n14266848.md#1/50fc95d72dfb97f50d80d6684fe3a755" rel="attachment wp-att-14266856"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14266856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266856-50fc95d72dfb97f50d80d6684fe3a755-450x269.png" alt="" width="606" b="362" /></a></p>
<h1 class="p1"></h1>
<h1 class="p1"><strong>2024年多伦多首届齐聚一堂多元文化节</strong></h1>
<h1 class="p1"><strong>All For One Multicultural Festival</strong></h1>
<p class="p1"><strong>时间：</strong></p>
<p class="p1">6月21日（星期五）、6月22日（星期六）、6月23日（星期日）</p>
<p class="p1"><strong>地点：</strong></p>
<p class="p1">Woodside Square购物中心（1571 Sandhurst Cir, Scarborough, ON M1V 1V2）) 户外场地</p>
