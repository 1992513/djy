<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】蟹肉山药鱼肚羹～养颜佳肴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266765-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蟹肉山药鱼肚羹。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：鱼肚羹汤有丰富的胶原蛋白，也是养颜美容的佳品。本食谱让忙碌的现代人有一个便利做法，我们使用鸡汤罐头，接着加入各种美味又新鲜的食材烹煮，汤品美味再次升级，而且营养满满。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">蟹肉山药鱼肚羹</span></h2>
<h3>材料:</h3>
<p>罐头蟹肉3 oz<br />
山药8 oz<br />
湿发鱼肚4 oz<br />
蛋白1颗</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266771" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266771-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14266771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266771-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266771" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>鸡汤4杯<br />
烹大师1汤匙<br />
盐1/2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉3汤匙（加开水勾芡用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266772" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266772" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266772-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14266772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266772-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266772" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 山药去皮蒸熟后切粒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266773-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14266773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266773-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266773" class="wp-caption-text">山药去皮蒸熟后切粒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 鱼肚浸泡后切粒备用。蛋白打散。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266774" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266774-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14266774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266774-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266774" class="wp-caption-text">鱼肚浸泡后切粒备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 鱼肚先用姜汁酒烫5分钟，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266775" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266775-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14266775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266775-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266775" class="wp-caption-text">鱼肚先用姜汁酒烫5分钟，备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 烧热鸡汤，加入烹大师和盐，放入山药和鱼肚煮5分钟，再加入蟹肉煮2分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266777-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14266777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266777-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266777" class="wp-caption-text">放入山药和鱼肚煮5分钟，再加入蟹肉煮2分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 用粟粉水勾芡后熄火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266778" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266778-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14266778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266778-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266778" class="wp-caption-text">用粟粉水勾芡后熄火。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 加入蛋白拌匀，最后加胡椒粉和麻油便可享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14266779" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14266779" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266779-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14266779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14266779-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14266779" class="wp-caption-text">加入蛋白拌匀，最后加胡椒粉和麻油便可享用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>* 梁师傅小贴士</h3>
<p>? 鱼肚是鱼鳔的干制品，鱼肚加工方法主要分为沙爆和油炸两类，因此也有“爆肚”和“炸肚”的分别。无论是沙爆或者油炸，加工前要先把鱼肚浸软，去油脂和血筋后，再晒至全干。</p>
<p>? 鱼肚可配蟹肉炒，或者做成各式烩羹菜肴。◇</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
