<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>客家同乡会端午联谊满载美食乡情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265746-DSC_1848-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>与会人士与办事处处长王翼龙（左一）合影，左二是同乡会会长李采真。（吕尚明/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月07日讯】（大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大报导）今年的端午佳节即将到来，亚特兰大客家同乡会于6月2日在亚特兰大侨教中心举行端午联谊活动，大约五、六十位乡亲共襄盛举。此外，驻亚特兰大办事处的王翼龙处长、侨教中心的欧宏伟主任、办事处的熊乐伦副领事也拨冗参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14265753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14265753" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265753-DSC_1808-e1717725330423.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14265753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265753-DSC_1808-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14265753" class="wp-caption-text">美食当前，会长李采真（左）与侨教中心欧宏伟主任。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14265758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14265758" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265758-DSC_1810-e1717725468274.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14265758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265758-DSC_1810-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14265758" class="wp-caption-text">李采真向欧宏伟主任介绍佳肴。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_14265799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14265799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265799-DSC_1845-e1717727126901.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14265799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265799-DSC_1845-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14265799" class="wp-caption-text">大家与欧宏伟主任合影。左一是眷村联谊会会长叶竣图，左二是华人餐饮协会荣誉主席孙德华，左三是侨务委员范琪君，左四是侨务委员陆复泰。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次联谊会再度由高人气的同乡会理事谢景琳主持，她一再强调活动成功主办的幕后大功臣是会长李采真与她的母亲、掌厨的“伯母”。当然，也要感谢如往年一般亲自来捧场的侨务委员范琪君与陆复泰的慷慨解囊与鼎力相助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14265763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14265763" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265763-DSC_1825-e1717725868340.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14265763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265763-DSC_1825-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14265763" class="wp-caption-text">主持人谢景琳与会长李采真登台开幕。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14265766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14265766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265766-DSC_1838-e1717726042319.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14265766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14265766-DSC_1838-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14265766" class="wp-caption-text">会长李采真开讲。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>色香味俱全的美食，有端午必不可少的客家肉粽，还有引起大家思乡情怀的艾草粿，以及扣肉、白斩鸡、葱油鸡、凉拌黄瓜、炒米粉、卤海带、卤花生、爱玉、芒果布丁等等。其中艾草粿里的萝卜丝，是李采真自己晒制的。</p>
