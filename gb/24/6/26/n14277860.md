<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】豆角两吃～港式家常菜 叉烧料理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277881-17-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>港式家常菜常见的“豆角叉烧煎蛋”，让主厨传授好吃煮食秘诀，保证好吃！而且还学到“豆角两吃”的叉烧料理做法。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：你还在炒豆角吗？炎炎夏日，餐桌上来一盘炒豆角，清脆爽口，非常下饭。但是，老吃同一种菜色也挺腻味，今天我们来学“豆角两吃”的食谱，而且是港式家常菜，还增加了叉烧风味，非常好吃！</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">青豆角洋葱炒叉烧</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14277900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277900" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277900-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277900-10-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277900" class="wp-caption-text">青豆角洋葱炒叉烧～绝对好吃的家常菜。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青豆角1磅<br />
紫洋葱1/2个<br />
叉烧8oz<br />
红萝卜2oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味：</span></h3>
<p>鸡汤1/3杯<br />
生抽1又1/2汤匙<br />
糖1/2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉水2汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277902" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277902-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277902-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277902" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 豆角切除头尾后再切段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277903" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277903-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277903-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277903" class="wp-caption-text">豆角切除头尾后再切段。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 豆角洗干净用沸水烫过，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277904-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277904-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277904" class="wp-caption-text">豆角洗干净用沸水烫过，备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 洋葱、红萝卜和叉烧等全部切丝备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277894" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277894-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277894-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277894" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱、红萝卜和叉烧等全部切丝备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，先把叉烧丝炒香后盛出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277895" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277895-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277895-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277895" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，先把叉烧丝炒香后盛出。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 爆香姜、蒜蓉、洋葱，放入红萝卜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277896" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277896-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277896-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277896" class="wp-caption-text">爆香姜、蒜蓉、洋葱。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放回豆角和叉烧翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277897" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277897-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277897-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277897" class="wp-caption-text">放回豆角和叉烧翻炒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 倒入调味汁液兜匀便可上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277898" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277898-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277898-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277898" class="wp-caption-text">倒入调味汁液兜匀便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">豆角叉烧煎蛋</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料：</span></h3>
<p>豆角叉烧适量（上面食谱所做的豆角叉烧）<br />
鸡蛋6个<br />
盐少许<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277884" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277884-11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277884-11-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277884" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">工具：</span></h3>
<p>八吋小不沾锅</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法：</span></h3>
<p>1. 取出适量的豆角叉烧，切粒后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277885" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277885-12.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277885-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277885" class="wp-caption-text">取出适量的豆角叉烧，切粒后备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 鸡蛋打散后加入少许盐和胡椒粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277886" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277886-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277886-13-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277886" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋打散后加入少许盐和胡椒粉。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将豆角叉烧粒加入蛋液中，拌匀备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277891" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277891-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277891-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277891" class="wp-caption-text">将豆角叉烧粒加入蛋液中，拌匀备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下少许油，放入一小份蛋液材料，中火煎至底部凝固。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277892" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277892-15.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277892-15-450x282.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277892" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下少许油，放入一小份蛋液材料，中火煎至底部凝固。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 用锅铲把蛋翻半后成半月形，再两边煎香便可。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277893" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277893-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277893-16-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277893" class="wp-caption-text">用锅铲把蛋翻半后成半月形，再两边煎香便可。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
