<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】蒜头南瓜炆烟卤大肠～创意开胃菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277951-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蒜头南瓜炆烟卤大肠。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：卤大肠和南瓜搭配是不是很少听说？这道料理经过主厨的巧手后，肯定让您赞不绝口！</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">蒜头南瓜炆烟卤大肠</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料:</span></h3>
<p>南瓜 1.4磅<br />
烟卤大肠1磅<br />
蒜头10粒<br />
原粒豆豉1.5 oz<br />
姜片少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277942" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277942-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277942-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277942" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味:</span></h3>
<p>水2杯<br />
酱油1.5汤匙<br />
砂糖1茶匙<br />
蘑菇粉1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉水2汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277943" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277943-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277943-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277943" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法:</span></h3>
<p>1. 南瓜去核后连皮一起切大块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277944" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277944-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277944-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277944" class="wp-caption-text">南瓜去核后连皮一起切大块备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 大肠切大块备用。原粒豆豉用水浸软，再捣烂一些备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277945-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277945-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277945" class="wp-caption-text">大肠切大块备用。原粒豆豉用水浸软，再捣烂一些备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 爆香蒜头、豆豉和姜。加入南瓜翻炒1分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277946" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277946-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277946-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277946" class="wp-caption-text">爆香蒜头、豆豉和姜。加入南瓜翻炒1分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 加入水及所有调味料煮10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277947-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277947-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277947" class="wp-caption-text">加入水及所有调味料煮10分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再加入大肠多煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277949-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277949-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277949" class="wp-caption-text">再加入大肠多煮5分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 最后用粟粉水勾芡便可上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277950-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14277950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277950-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277950" class="wp-caption-text">最后用粟粉水勾芡便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">食谱说明：</span></h3>
<p>烟卤大肠是台湾出产，味道不太咸，有烟熏味道，只需要加热便可。◇</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>经历：</strong></span></h2>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
