<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“植声疗愈”带你走进情绪花园音乐厅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277645-691377-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“植声疗愈”受到民众青睐。（陈昭宏提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月26日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）22日在台东市一凡厅进行一场精彩的活动。此次活动由园艺治疗师邱榆棋和疗愈钢琴家周美君联合呈现，主题为“内在丛林的植物絮语”，希望透过音乐和花草带领大家进入情绪的花园音乐厅，共创一场视觉与听觉的双重盛宴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277647-691379.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14277647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277647-691379-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277647" class="wp-caption-text">园艺治疗师邱榆棋与民众对话。（陈昭宏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当民众踏入会场，首先迎接他们的是阵阵花香，瞬间在空间中营造出一种幸福感。随后，他们的视野被一架摆放在花草装饰和暖光烛台环绕的钢琴吸引。在品尝迎宾花茶的同时，空气中那份温暖的人情味似乎也让人沉醉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277646-691378.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14277646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277646-691378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277646" class="wp-caption-text">民众在社区音乐厅里，倾听内在世界的心情絮语。（陈昭宏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14277653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277653-691385.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14277653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277653-691385-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277653" class="wp-caption-text">邱榆棋介绍如何运用和植物花草的特性。（陈昭宏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周美君首先演奏了芬兰作曲家西贝流士的《树木组曲》，包括“山梨花开时”、“孤独的枞树”等五首曲子。她的琴音在烛光微弱的照明下，让人仿佛走进了一个充满情感的剧场，植物和声音的交融让整个体验更加深刻。此外，她还演奏了拉赫曼尼诺夫的“紫丁香”，用音乐引导听众感受到音符中的花香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277649-691381.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14277649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277649-691381-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277649" class="wp-caption-text">钢琴家周美君演出时，让人仿佛走进情绪剧场。（陈昭宏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>邱榆棋则讲述了自我表达的重要性，并分享如何利用植物的特性来进行五感疗愈，增进自我觉察。她引导参与者透过观察、品味、闻听、触摸和聆听植物，来进行深度的心灵体验。在钢琴即兴演奏的伴随下，参与者在品聆花草茶的过程中，透过正念呼吸来缓解心理压力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277650" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277650-691382.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14277650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277650-691382-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="798" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277650" class="wp-caption-text">这活动受到大家的喜爱。（陈昭宏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是由文化部指导、国立台东生活美学馆主办的【艺术疗愈文化近用】系列活动，活动最后，参与者各自用三个形容词写下当天的感受，并自行调配一包舒缓身心的暖心花茶。整场活动在温馨的氛围中完美落幕，并受到来宾的热烈反响。国立台东生活美学馆期待透过这样的活动，增进偏乡文化资源的连结，并鼓励更多民众参与，感受艺术的疗愈力量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14277648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14277648" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277648-691380.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14277648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14277648-691380-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14277648" class="wp-caption-text">调配自己喜欢的味道。（陈昭宏提供）</figcaption></figure>
