<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：高雄凤凰花季 缤纷花海如火把般艳丽</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262102-2406010630292378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾高雄爱河河畔两旁凤凰花盛开。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月01日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾采访报导）夏季是凤凰花开季节，台湾摄影师宋永泰分享，高雄爱河河畔两旁凤凰花正盛开，当艳红花朵泛紫时好像火把一样漂亮。他推荐游客可以到高雄一游，欣赏凤凰花缤纷花海美景。</p>
<p>宋永泰表示，台湾高雄爱河湾沿岸是高雄市的进步象征之一，而在凤凰花盛开的季节，爱河沿岸两旁都成为了缤纷的花海，吸引许多民众前来散步赏花打卡。他推荐游客来此一游，“满眼可见火红的凤凰花，点缀在河畔的绿树间，构成了一幅幅美丽的风景”。（<strong>推荐阅读：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/4/11/n14223289.md#1?utm_term=djy_a_rec_in" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">组图：高雄花旗木盛开 粉红花海美不胜收</a></span></strong>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262110" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262110-2406010630382378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262110" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262110-2406010630382378.jpg" alt="" width="337" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262110" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄爱河河畔两旁凤凰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14262111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262111" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262111-2406010630362378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262111" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262111-2406010630362378-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262111" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄爱河河畔两旁凤凰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>“火树”、“凤凰花”是凤凰木的别名，为豆科凤凰木属，高大落叶乔木，高约8—25公尺（米），胸径可达1公尺，树干表面光滑，呈现灰白或淡灰色，树冠伞状，枝条多呈水平状伸展，在台湾为常见的行道树、庭园绿荫树，花期为5至7月。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262117" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262117" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262117-2406010630312378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262117" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262117-2406010630312378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262117" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄爱河河畔两旁凤凰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰说，夏天凤凰木花开时节，正逢学子毕业季，加上凤凰花的花语是“离别”，《骊歌》毕业歌曲中提及“凤凰花开”，所以又有人称为“毕业树”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262112" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262112-2406010630342378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262112" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262112-2406010630342378-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262112" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄爱河河畔两旁凤凰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>他分享，凤凰花如果开到红花泛紫的时候，“那一树的凤凰花，就像一支燃烧的火把很漂亮。当人站在凤凰树下面，你穿白色的衣服自拍，或别人帮你拍照，整个人都会变成红色的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262114" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262114-2406010630262378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262114" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262114-2406010630262378-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262114" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄爱河河畔两旁凤凰花盛开。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>“‘火树’凤凰木花开好绚丽。”宋永泰表示，五、六月在爱河畔漫步，享受着微风吹拂带来的清凉，欣赏着水面泛起的涟漪，这一刻让人心情不禁愉悦而舒畅。</p>
