<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>虎尾端午节“虎哩尽情fun粽”体验不一样的端午节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262074-686033-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>虎尾镇公所为庆祝端午节，今天（ 6月1日）于公所广场举办端午节活动！（廖素贞／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）虎尾镇公所为庆祝端午节，今天（ 6月1日）于公所广场举办端午节活动，活动从上午9：30分-16：00，安排了精彩的舞蹈比赛、寓教于乐闯关活动、DIY手作艾草花束体验、萌粽扇子体验、还有小朋友最喜爱的大小丑气球秀！现场热闹滚滚！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262075" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262075" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262075-686035.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262075" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262075-686035-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262075" class="wp-caption-text">这次端午节活动首次举办午虎 WOOHOO舞蹈比赛。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次端午节活动首次举办午虎 WOOHOO舞蹈比赛，邀请民众结合端午节元素以及专属虎尾的老虎意象，让参赛者借此平台舞出自己，跳出创意，前三名可获得奖金及可爱肉粽抱枕，每位参赛者也可领取端午手提袋一份。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262078" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262078-686038.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262078-686038-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262078" class="wp-caption-text">与会来宾与大家同乐！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场还有DIY手作体验，绘出自己的端午节萌粽扇子，还有别于传统的插艾草体验，结合花朵元素，让民众体验手绑花束的感觉，打造文青风格的避邪小物，满满的好礼及市集美食，现场还有两座游乐气垫，虎哩尽情fun粽，体验今夏最不一样的端午节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14262081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262081" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262081-686041.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262081-686041-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262081" class="wp-caption-text">现场还有两座游乐气垫，虎哩尽情fun粽。（廖素 贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14262076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262076" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262076-686036.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262076-686036-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262076" class="wp-caption-text">亲子闯关游戏 射五毒。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14262077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14262077" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262077-686037.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14262077" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14262077-686037-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14262077" class="wp-caption-text">精彩的 舞蹈比赛。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虎尾镇长林嘉弘表示，端午节是民俗传统三大节日之一，传统习俗像是立蛋、包粽子、划龙舟、准备午时水、门上插艾草菖蒲等等都是大家而耳能详的端午必备仪式，虎尾镇公所为延续传统文化，每年举办大小朋友都适合的端午节庆活动，让在外打拼事业或学子，在端午佳节回乡享受家中长辈用心准备可口肉粽同时，也能与家人一同参加端午活动，也藉由公所举办的端午节活动中，了解端午节由来。</p>
<p>这次活动有小朋友们最爱的大小丑气球秀、乐团表演、小蜡笔剧团、魔幻泡泡秀，还有陆地龙舟，让民众能够更安全的体验端午节划龙舟的趣味，现场还发送端午节吉祥香包，让民众感受端午节浓浓氛围。</p>
