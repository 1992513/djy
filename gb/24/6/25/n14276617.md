<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>布里斯本文化教师在地研习 促进台湾文化传承</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276621-f079afeda1483a5e076f2163de1cd288-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>布里斯本文化教师在地研习，促进台湾文化传承。（昆士兰台湾中心）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月25日讯】为推广台湾特色艺术文化，并增进布里斯本地区文化教师教学能力，昆士兰台湾中心于2024年6月22日下午举办两场“海外民俗文化种子教师在地研习”活动，邀请2位讲座古绣莲老师、董惠芳老师以及助教余颖雁老师及吴佩珍老师分别讲解“皮影戏制作及短剧演出教学”与“客家纸伞彩绘教学”，是日计有来自当地各侨校、主流学校老师及对艺术文化有兴趣之乡亲共约25人参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14276623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14276623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276623-ef75b463072ab29299903191fe9ded72-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14276623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276623-ef75b463072ab29299903191fe9ded72-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14276623" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本文化教师在地研习，促进台湾文化传承。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中心主任欧阳群诚挚感谢当天两位讲座老师用心准备相关教材，以及助教的热心协助，让这次海外民俗文化种子教师研习活动顺利圆满举行，并期许所有参与老师都能在当地传承台湾特色民俗文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14276626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14276626" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276626-38dffcfc7386463a718d34cea09c709e-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14276626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276626-38dffcfc7386463a718d34cea09c709e-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14276626" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本文化教师在地研习，促进台湾文化传承。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14276625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14276625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276625-32f6566960144cf7c781ba6d7a9bc0d3-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14276625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276625-32f6566960144cf7c781ba6d7a9bc0d3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14276625" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本文化教师在地研习，促进台湾文化传承。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一场研习活动则由古绣莲老师进行“皮影戏制作及短剧演出教学”，古老师首先讲解皮影戏的文化历史，并介绍皮影戏偶制作，接着给予每位老师亲手绘制西游记皮影戏角色，随后带领每位老师进行皮影操偶演出，现场此起彼落的欢笑声，让各位老师实际体验台湾传统戏偶艺术的乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14276622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14276622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276622-5d35b65db8cfa73b1df45f94abb59ade-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14276622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276622-5d35b65db8cfa73b1df45f94abb59ade-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14276622" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本文化教师在地研习，促进台湾文化传承。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14276624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14276624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276624-fd55774502b1f3a19e72aeec8f64fbd9-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14276624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276624-fd55774502b1f3a19e72aeec8f64fbd9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14276624" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本文化教师在地研习，促进台湾文化传承。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>当天第二场研习活动由董惠芳老师讲解“客家纸伞彩绘教学”，董老师首先说明客家纸伞的由来跟图案意涵，接着讲解利用客家花布元素来彩绘纸伞，并让参加的教师们发挥创意，利用各种花卉等图案在纸伞上设计自己喜爱的图样，亲自体验台湾传统民俗优质文化，并创造出自我专属的客家纸伞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14276619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14276619" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276619-83677aef53645ea0924dffdceee4ad63-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14276619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14276619-83677aef53645ea0924dffdceee4ad63-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14276619" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本文化教师在地研习，促进台湾文化传承。（昆士兰台湾中心）</figcaption></figure>
