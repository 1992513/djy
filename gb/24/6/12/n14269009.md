<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“台湾好食日”新西兰成功举行 奥克兰民众喜爱台湾美味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269247-tw1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6> 6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK'nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。 图为台湾珍增好咖啡展位。（李玉/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年06月12日讯】</p>
<figure id="attachment_14269250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269250" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269250-tw2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269250-tw2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269250" class="wp-caption-text">6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。 图为现场品尝和购买台湾美食的民众。（李玉/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>（大纪元记者林方新西兰奥克兰采访报道）本月6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。历时3天的台湾美食活动吸引了大量人潮。多种口味和品种的台湾美食让来自不同族裔的当地民众大开眼界，并一再询问类似活动何时会再次举办。还有华人顾客连续三天都来摊位品尝、试吃和购买。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14269252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269252-tw3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269252-tw3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269252" class="wp-caption-text">6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。图为台湾源顺食品展位。（李玉/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14269253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269253-tw4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269253-tw4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269253" class="wp-caption-text">6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。图为台湾信全冬粉展位。<span style="font-weight: 400;">（李玉/大纪元）</span></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14269256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269256-tw5.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269256-tw5-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269256" class="wp-caption-text">6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。图为台湾和吉冬瓜砖茶展位。（李玉/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14269257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269257-tw6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269257-tw6-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269257" class="wp-caption-text">6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。图为台湾丸文食品展位。（李玉/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的活动所展示的台湾食品共来自8家优良食品厂商，每种食品的展位设计精美，产品陈列新颖，非常吸睛。6月7日一大早，展位还没有摆设好，就有民众到展位来了解和品尝。除此之外，现场还设置了亚洲厨房，现场烹饪部分展示的食品， 让其他族裔观众近距离了解亚洲的美食文化和烹饪技术。活动现场香气四溢，热闹非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14269258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269258-tw1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269258-tw1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269258" class="wp-caption-text">6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。（李玉/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14269260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269260-tw2.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269260-tw2-600x445.png" alt="" width="600" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269260" class="wp-caption-text">6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。图为台湾MIT饮品展位。（李玉/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14269261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269261-tw3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269261-tw3-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269261" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">?6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">成功举办</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。图为台湾挚心食品坚果拌酱展位。（李玉/大纪元）</span></figcaption></figure>
<p>参加本次好食日的台湾品牌包括，台湾坚果拌酱第一品牌“五仁果油系列”、长松食品、源顺食品养生谷粉系列、和吉冬瓜茶砖和黑糖系列、信全冬粉、台湾阿里山咖啡精品豆及UPON有香咖啡、MIT热门饮品、丸文鱼松和鱼酥。三天来，每家品牌的摊位前都挤满了大批民众前来试吃和购买，Paksave 超市的员工们在休息时间也来到摊位前，好奇的询问了解情况。</p>
<p>有民众表示，台湾小吃很有名气，每次有朋友去台湾都会带一些回来，今天可以在家门口就品尝这么多的台湾美食，真是开心。</p>
<p>还有的西人顾客来到厨房，细细询问宽粉的烹饪方法，然后高兴的购买相关产品，回家自己烹饪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14269262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14269262" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269262-tw4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14269262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14269262-tw4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14269262" class="wp-caption-text">6月7日到9日，“台湾好食日”活动在新西兰奥克兰主流超市PAK&#8217;nSAVE Ormiston成功举办。图为对台湾食品感兴趣的现场民众。（李玉/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场的一位来自马来西亚的女士表示，台湾食品质量好，吃了放心，应该大力宣传。这位台湾食品的超级粉丝还说，“我们超爱台湾食品，希望下次多卖一些！”</p>
