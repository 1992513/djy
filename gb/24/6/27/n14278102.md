<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多波斯美食节体验最好的波斯美食与文化</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14278969-2-600x400.png" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月03日讯】大多伦多地区第二届波斯美食节（The Second Persian Food Festival），将于8月3日至5日在列治文山的Richmond Green Park公园举行。免费入场。</p>

<p class="p1">波斯美食节是一个缤纷多彩的节日。融入波斯文化、音乐的丰富多彩世界中吧，体验最好的传统波斯美食与文化。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14280903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14280903-4e42148c739f65026cdbd6b0310e4d39-450x744.png" alt="" width="450" b="744" /></p>
<p class="p1">去年的活动吸引了超过40,000名参与者，波斯美食节被誉为多伦多最大且最成功的波斯节日。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14280901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14280901-39fa5e3ec494cd1b82fa2d300634bb5b-450x665.png" alt="" width="450" b="665" /></p>
<p class="p1">在波斯美食节上，您可以品味传统波斯美食的浓香，陶醉于迷人音乐的节奏，感受文化活动的热情。</p>
<p><ahref="https://cn.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14280923" rel="attachment wp-att-14280923"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14280923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14280923-47edc3d432db27566bc7fce913ee05a3-450x684.png" alt="" width="450" b="684" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">来列治文山的Richmond Green Park公园，与家人朋友共同享受这个祝波斯社区遗产和喜悦的美好活动吧，这将是一个令人难忘的体验！</p>
<p><ahref="https://cn.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14280918" rel="attachment wp-att-14280918"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14280918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14280918-f4e3415c26bcffbac8761ed53b811521-450x621.png" alt="" width="450" b="621" /></a></p>
