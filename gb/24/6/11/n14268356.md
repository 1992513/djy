<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>缓解运营压力 西澳另有三个驾照中心周六营业</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年06月08日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者夏墨竹澳洲珀斯报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">为了缩短客户等候时间，缓解大都市中心客户和员工的持续压力，西澳交通厅决定在周六再开放三个驾驶员和车辆服务（</span><span lang="en-AU">Driver and Vehicle Services</span><span lang="zh-TW">，简称</span><span lang="en-AU">DVS</span><span lang="zh-TW">）中心。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">近几个月来，大都市</span><span lang="en-AU">DVS</span><span lang="zh-TW">中心的客户数量激增，服务等待时间大幅增加。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">从</span><span lang="en-AU">6</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-AU">8</span><span lang="zh-TW">日起，交通厅将在周六开放</span><span lang="en-AU">CityWest</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-AU">Cannington</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-AU">Mirrabooka DVS</span><span lang="zh-TW">中心，从上午</span><span lang="en-AU">7:15</span><span lang="zh-TW">到下午</span><span lang="en-AU">2:15</span><span lang="zh-TW">处理驾驶员和车辆交易，为期八周。实际驾驶评估</span><span lang="en-AU">(PDA)</span><span lang="zh-TW">、海事、经销商和</span><span lang="en-AU">NDIS</span><span lang="zh-TW">交易将不会在周六营业时间内进行。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">Joondalup</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-AU">Kelmscott</span><span lang="zh-TW">驾驶评估中心将继续在周六运营。</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：杨新云</p>
