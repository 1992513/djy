<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多台湾文化节2024年与西班牙对话</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289715-6b72b232dea7dc6459339f74df899ea2-600x400.png" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年07月20日讯】<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz JxVs2d ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">多伦多台湾文化节——成为我（TAIWANfest：Becoming Me）将于8月23日至25日在湖滨中心（Harbourfront Centre）举行，2024年以与西班牙对话为主题，通过表演、展览、美食、电影等多种方式探索台湾文化。免费入场，缤纷节目精彩可期。</span></span></span></p>

<p class="p1">指挥谢建得与台湾文化节管弦乐团，将携手40多位在地音乐家来到湖滨中心的舞台；加拿大佛朗明哥长笛演奏家王立娜（Lara Wong）等多元族裔音乐家带来的现场音乐表演，谨献给多元的多伦多社区。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/28/n14279631.md#1/49217faa926ab136f3df548f6dc7f407" rel="attachment wp-att-14279663"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14279663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14279663-49217faa926ab136f3df548f6dc7f407-450x563.png" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">受疯狂激情的佛朗明哥音乐和怀旧的台湾闽南语音乐启发，这场一年一度的开幕音乐会邀请大家踏上跨文化冒险的旅程。</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/28/n14279631.md#1/9eba5a11984bf9dae326528b10e51206" rel="attachment wp-att-14279662"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14279662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14279662-9eba5a11984bf9dae326528b10e51206-450x581.png" alt="" width="450" b="581" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">由薛喻鲜（Hsueh Yu Hsien）和精灵幻舞舞团（Genio Dance Group）带来的福尔摩沙上的佛朗明哥 （Flamenco de Formosa）等精彩节目。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/28/n14279631.md#1/1-3324" rel="attachment wp-att-14289712"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14289712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289712-1-450x235.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="314" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">通过舞蹈和音乐的普世语言，台湾文化节探索了佛朗明哥舞、华语音乐与众多文化间的连结。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14289718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289718-2a469ef9dfbbdf02130a4da49fd97eff-450x259.png" alt="" width="599" b="345" /></p>
<p class="p1">在艺术市集上，当地艺术家和小企业将分享他们的故事和作品。</p>
<p class="p3"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/28/n14279631.md#1/220ed166452a6c5903deed8adf800977" rel="attachment wp-att-14279660"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14279660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14279660-220ed166452a6c5903deed8adf800977-450x562.png" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a></p>
<p class="p3">想到 Tortilla ，您脑海里的画面是墨西哥玉米饼吗？在西班牙 Tortilla 可是完全不同的食物，它是马铃薯烘蛋。</p>
<p class="p3"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/28/n14279631.md#1/451a2d9c36820829945dd15dfc45c0a2" rel="attachment wp-att-14279661"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14279661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14279661-451a2d9c36820829945dd15dfc45c0a2-450x306.png" alt="" width="599" b="407" /></a></p>
<p class="p3">米在许多亚洲文化圈中是重要主食，西班牙的经典菜肴“铁锅饭”却也是以米为主体！来食品摊位吧，通过美食展开探索文化的对话。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/28/n14279631.md#1/6f57ba402bcd660ec7baff946fd8484d" rel="attachment wp-att-14289719"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14289719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14289719-6f57ba402bcd660ec7baff946fd8484d-450x249.png" alt="" width="600" b="332" /></a></p>
