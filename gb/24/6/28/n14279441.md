<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾古典诗：松泷岩瀑布</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/7b54e485026e7631259fa7eb2960302e-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>松泷岩瀑布峰岩奇秀。（曾晏均／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<figure id="attachment_10877304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10877304" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-1-1-e1543331036102.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-10877304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-1-1-450x926.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="825"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10877304" class="wp-caption-text">松泷岩瀑布素有“杉林溪首景”的名号。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: center">奔崖直下泻飞泉，</p>
<p style="text-align: center">震耳雷鸣匝地烟。</p>
<p style="text-align: center">避暑松泷谐万籁，</p>
<p style="text-align: center">迷濛漫野水连天。</p>
<p>
注：松泷岩位于杉林溪森林生态渡假园区内，是一个宽68公尺、深25公尺、高30公尺的岩洞，岩洞内有钟乳石和燕巢，气势磅礡的瀑布于岩洞上倾泻而下，穿过岩洞下方的天眼步道而行，如水濂洞， 雨季巨瀑倾泄、水花飞溅。@</p>
