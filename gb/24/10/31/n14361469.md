<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【旅行趣】“杜布罗夫尼克”旅游 欣赏中世纪古城之美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14362924-b481e13da1b25db8897ea6d3b2157287.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
