<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“波光倒影”波士顿台湾影展迎六周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342083-DSC07015-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第六届波士顿台湾影展9月28日开幕，现场几乎座无虚席。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月01日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“每次观看这部影片，我都在流眼泪。”爱默森学院副教授赛琳娜斯（Indira Salinas）说。她指的是在“波士顿台湾影展”中播放的纪录片《兰陵40：演员实验教室》。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">第六届波士顿台湾影展以“波光倒影”为主题，于9月28日和29日，在波士顿市中心的AMC电影院放映了八部风格各异的台湾影片，得到火热反响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14342082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342082" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342082-DSC07009.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342082-DSC07009-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342082" class="wp-caption-text">第六届波士顿台湾影展9月28日开幕，现场几乎座无虚席。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14342084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342084" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342084-DSC07020.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342084-DSC07020-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342084" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾和志愿者们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">主办方今年还播放了《老狐狸》、《野生之路》、《依诺物语》、《鹭鸶河》、《兰陵40》、《青春18&#215;2?通往有你的旅程》、《春形》和《下半场》这些电影和短片。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">除了影片放映之外，主办方还邀请了“金马奖”得主陈慕义、知名导演王耿瑜，以及日本演员藤井道人来到现场，与观众座谈。今年是波士顿台湾影展协会首次与日本协会合作，不仅播放一部日本电影，还邀请到日本演员来现场座谈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14342079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342079" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342079-DSC06997.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342079-DSC06997-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342079" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿台湾影展协会会长Andrew Lin（左）和林致中欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14342078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342078" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342078-1000008238.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342078-1000008238-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342078" class="wp-caption-text">本届台湾影展首次与日本协会合作。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“通过‘波光倒影’，我们希望提醒每个人停下脚步，回想生活中的挑战压向他们的时候，要寻找平衡，要继续逆风航行的时刻。”本届影展的节目册介绍说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">观看了《兰陵40》影片的许益祥感慨说，兰陵剧团的演员们在舞台上讲述自己的人生故事，这对他有所启发，让他想回顾自己的人生，或许也记录下自己的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14342086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342086" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342086-DSC07277.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342086-DSC07277-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342086" class="wp-caption-text">《兰陵40》导演王耿瑜（中）在晚上的招待会中与观众交谈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14342087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342087" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342087-DSC07279.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342087-DSC07279-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342087" class="wp-caption-text">台湾影展的成员们与“Arlington International Film Festival”创办人交流。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在28日上午的开幕式中，波士顿台湾影展协会会长林致中和Andrew Lin欢迎满座的来宾。“我们这个周末聚集在这里，我希望大家借这个机会放松舒缓，从每天的乱事中解脱出来，沉浸到电影节的气氛中，让这些影片的力量与你共鸣。”林致中说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14342081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342081" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342081-DSC07008.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342081-DSC07008-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342081" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏感谢台湾影展协会宣扬文化。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14342080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342080-DSC07002.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342080-DSC07002-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342080" class="wp-caption-text">前数位发展部部长唐凤参加了影展。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天，恰好在哈佛大学参访的中华民国前数位发展部部长唐凤参加了影展，与主办方、嘉宾及观众交流。驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏夫妇，以及侨教中心主任高家富到场共襄盛举。主要赞助商Mediatek、长荣航空等机构也都派代表来到现场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14342088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342088" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342088-DSC07282.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342088-DSC07282-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342088" class="wp-caption-text">影展招待会的龙虾卷。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14342089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14342089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342089-DSC07284.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14342089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14342089-DSC07284-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14342089" class="wp-caption-text">第六届波士顿台湾影展9月28日开幕，当晚主办方在W酒店举办招待会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">28日晚上，主办方还在波士顿市中心的W酒店举办招待晚宴，让座谈嘉宾们和观众在美食和音乐的氛围中轻松交流。◇</p>
