<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>j-hope退伍 JIN捧花束迎接还帮忙拿麦克风</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352291-241017000340100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日上午自江原道原州的陆军第36步兵师团退伍，JIN（金硕珍）前往迎接他。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月17日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）今日（17日）上午自江原道原州的陆军第36步兵师团退伍。今年6月退伍的JIN（金硕珍）前往迎接他，两人带着笑容热情拥抱。j-hope还向等待他退伍的粉丝们致谢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14352292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352292-241017000232100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352292" title="2178802749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352292-241017000232100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2178802749" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352292" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日上午自江原道原州的陆军第36步兵师团退伍。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>j-hope去年4月18日以陆军现役身份入伍，并在陆军第36步兵师团担任白虎新兵教育大队的助教。这天上午退伍的他，面对现场的记者时，他认真摆出敬礼姿势，喊出“忠诚！”，随后又开朗的以双手做出送飞吻的姿势，让前往现场的外国粉丝掀起一阵欢呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14352265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352265-241017015251100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352265" title="2178800405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352265-241017015251100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2178800405" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352265" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日退伍，他向大家行举手礼。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14352268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352268" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352268-241017000248100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352268" title="2178100328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352268-241017000248100707-600x418.jpg" alt="2178100328" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352268" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日退伍，为大家送上飞吻。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于前往现场的记者群，j-hope表示“感谢远道而来的记者们”，他还说，托粉丝们的福，他才能健康地完成兵役。</p>
<p>j-hope表示：“一年六个月的兵役期间，我在助教任务中行军很多次。每次都会遇到许多原州市民向军队士兵们打招呼或为我们加油。为此我想要向市民们表达感谢。真的很感动，不知道该怎么表达那温热的感情。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14352271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352271" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352271-241017015257100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352271" title="2178097387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352271-241017015257100707-600x403.jpg" alt="2178097387" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352271" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日退伍。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>他还说：“在这一年六个月中，我感受到很多将士们为了保卫国家而献身付出的辛劳，为国家做了很多事情。希望国民们能给予他们热烈的关爱。感谢大家等待我，并给予我声援与关爱。我健康地退伍了，非常感谢。忠诚！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14352288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352288-241017000332100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352288" title="2178100202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352288-241017000332100707-600x385.jpg" alt="2178100202" width="600" b="385" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352288" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日上午自江原道原州的陆军第36步兵师团退伍。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而今年6月退伍的JIN，上月21日就曾在直播期间透露他打算在j-hope退伍这天做的事情，是“j-hope一退伍就把他绑架走，把他拉去拍摄”。而这天他也如期前往迎接j-hope退伍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14352286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352286-241017000217100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352286" title="2178802497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352286-241017000217100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2178802497" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352286" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日上午自江原道原州的陆军第36步兵师团退伍，JIN（金硕珍）前往迎接他。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14352282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352282-241017000226100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352282" title="2178802471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352282-241017000226100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2178802471" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352282" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日上午自江原道原州的陆军第36步兵师团退伍，JIN（金硕珍）前往迎接他。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>身穿红色西装搭配太阳眼镜登场的他，除了给予j-hope拥抱，还为j-hope拿花束并代替记者为j-hope举麦克风。为了不遮挡到j-hope，同时也为了体贴记者，JIN蹲低姿势捧着多个麦克风，让j-hope能好好问候大家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14352281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352281-241017015254100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352281" title="2178800971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352281-241017015254100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2178800971" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352281" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）10月17日上午退伍，JIN（金硕珍）特地蹲低为他拿麦克风。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在j-hope问候完大家，搭上车离开时，他还打开车窗向大家送挥手，并比出“手指爱心”，感谢到场迎接他退伍的众人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14352284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14352284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352284-241017000356100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14352284" title="2178100348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14352284-241017000356100707-600x431.jpg" alt="2178100348" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14352284" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员j-hope（郑号锡）退伍离开时，特地开车窗问候大家。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
