<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>安省财政赤字减少33亿 未来两年有望盈余</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13866623-CP162296738-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>10月30日，省议会保守党议员聆听财政部长 Peter Bethlenfalvy（左前）提供经济最新情况。（加通社）（Andrew Lahodynskyj/加通社）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年10月31日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元记者周行多伦多报导）</span><span class="s1">根据安省政府</span><span class="s4">10</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s4">30</span><span class="s1">日（周三）发布的秋季经济报告，由于税收增加及借贷成本下降，政府赤字减少了</span><span class="s4">33</span><span class="s1">亿元，并决定将节余用于发放补助金，帮助省民减轻生活负担。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">省府新闻公告显示，随着贷款利率下降和税收入增加，安省经济开始显示增长迹象。不过，失业率上升、住房开工停滞以及公共支出压力，显示出多数省民尚未明显受益。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">根据秋季经济报告，</span><span class="s4">2024-25</span><span class="s1">财年赤字从原预算的</span><span class="s4">98</span><span class="s1">亿元降至</span><span class="s4">66</span><span class="s1">亿元。省府预测</span><span class="s4">2025-26</span><span class="s1">财年赤字为</span><span class="s4">15</span><span class="s1">亿元，</span><span class="s4">2026-27</span><span class="s1">财年有望转为</span><span class="s4">9</span><span class="s1">亿元盈余。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">个人所得税收入（包括资本利得税）比</span><span class="s4">2024</span><span class="s1">年预算高出</span><span class="s4">28</span><span class="s1">亿元。政府预测，随着资本利得税的改革，未来三年将带来额外</span><span class="s4">33</span><span class="s1">亿元的净税收入。省府称，额外收入源于销售税因通胀增加以及联邦资本利得税规则的变动。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">省长福特谈及联邦资本利得税上调时表示反对：</span><span class="s4">“</span><span class="s1">靠征税不能实现繁荣，这在我们国家行不通，世界上也行不通。</span><span class="s4">”</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">政府表示，继续采取</span><span class="s4">“</span><span class="s1">财政负责</span><span class="s4">”</span><span class="s1">的方法，着力建设关键基础设施以支持社区发展，减少交通拥堵，并通过保持低税率让更多钱留在省民手中，以减轻生活压力。</span></p>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b>利民支出项目</b></span></h2>
<p class="p3"><span class="s4"><b> ? 200</b></span><span class="s1"><b>元退税</b>：省府将向符合条件的安省成年人发放</span><span class="s4">200</span><span class="s1">元的退税，家庭中每一名</span><span class="s4">18</span><span class="s1">岁以下的孩子将额外获得</span><span class="s4">200</span><span class="s1">元退税。退税预计耗资</span><span class="s4">30</span><span class="s1">亿元，支票将于明年初寄出。</span><span class="s4"><br />
<p class="p3"><span class="s4"><b> ? </b></span><span class="s1"><b>燃油税削减</b>：省府决定将临时汽油和燃料税削减措施延长，直到</span><span class="s4">2025</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s4">6</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s4">30</span><span class="s1">日。这样，汽油和燃料（柴油）税每升继续减免</span><span class="s4">5.7</span><span class="s1">分，将为普通家庭节省380 美元。</span><span class="s4"><br />
</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s4"><b> ? </b></span><span class="s1"><b>助学金扩大</b>：从</span><span class="s4">2026</span><span class="s1">年起，省府将在三年内拨款</span><span class="s4">8800</span><span class="s1">万元，扩大</span><span class="s4">“</span><span class="s1">学习和留下</span><span class="s4">”</span><span class="s1">（</span><span class="s4">Learn and Stay</span><span class="s1">）助学金计划，资助</span><span class="s4">1,360</span><span class="s1">名符合条件的医科本科生，这些学生需承诺毕业后在安省任何社区担任一段时间的家庭医生。</span><span class="s4">2026-27</span><span class="s1">学年的拨款将达</span><span class="s4">1,770</span><span class="s1">万元，助学金将覆盖所有学费和其他直接教育费用，包括书籍、用品和设备。省府预计，这项资助将为</span><span class="s4">136</span><span class="s1">万名省民提供更多的家庭医生服务。</span><span class="s4"><br />
<p class="p3"><span class="s4"><b> ? </b></span><span class="s1"><b>扩大生育计划</b>：从</span><span class="s4">2025-26</span><span class="s1">年开始的两年内，省府将额外拨款</span><span class="s4">1.5</span><span class="s1">亿元，扩大安省的生育支持计划。此项资金将使体外受精（</span><span class="s4">IVF</span><span class="s1">）治疗人数增加近两倍，并提高生育诊所的容量，减少医院和社区设施的等待时间。省府将为接受生育治疗的家庭提供新的税收抵免，抵免比例为治疗费用的</span><span class="s4">25%</span><span class="s1">，每年最高达</span><span class="s4">5,000</span><span class="s1">元，包括</span><span class="s4">IVF</span><span class="s1">、生育药物、旅费及诊断测试。政府将在三年内为此项目预留</span><span class="s4">1.15</span><span class="s1">亿元。</span><span class="s4"><br />
</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s4"><b> ? </b></span><span class="s1"><b>技能发展基金</b>：省府将为技能发展基金追加高达</span><span class="s4">2.6</span><span class="s1">亿元的资金，支持求职者与制造业、建筑业和医疗保健等热门行业的雇主对接。至此，安省对技能发展基金（</span><span class="s4">SDF</span><span class="s1">）的总投资已达</span><span class="s4">14</span><span class="s1">亿元。</span><span class="s4"><br />
<p class="p3"><span class="s4"><b> ? ALS</b></span><span class="s1"><b>患者支持</b>：从</span><span class="s4">2024-25</span><span class="s1">年起三年内，政府将投入逾</span><span class="s4">1,300</span><span class="s1">万元支持安省五个区域多学科</span><span class="s4">ALS</span><span class="s1">诊所的肌萎缩侧索硬化症（</span><span class="s4">ALS</span><span class="s1">）患者护理。</span><span class="s4"><br />
</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">安省财长贝思伦法维（</span><span class="s4">Peter Bethlenfalvy</span><span class="s1">）表示：</span><span class="s4">“</span><span class="s1">今天我们有能力建设更多的基础设施，同时把更多的钱返还给省民。</span><span class="s4">” </span><span class="s1">他补充说：</span><span class="s4">“</span><span class="s1">安省目前的财政状况，比过去几十年都要好。</span><span class="s4">”</span></p>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b>住房开工量下降</b></span></h2>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">省府在秋季经济报告中预计，</span><span class="s4">2024</span><span class="s1">年至</span><span class="s4">2027</span><span class="s1">年住房开工量将继续下降。安省政府</span><span class="s4">2024</span><span class="s1">年预算估计今年住房开工量将达</span><span class="s4">87,900</span><span class="s1">套，但最新报告将预测调整为</span><span class="s4">81,300</span><span class="s1">套。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">省府计划在</span><span class="s4">2031</span><span class="s1">年前建成</span><span class="s4">150</span><span class="s1">万套住房，这意味着每年至少需建造</span><span class="s4">10</span><span class="s1">万套房屋。然而，更新后的报告显示，</span><span class="s4">2027</span><span class="s1">年预计的开工数量最高仅能达到</span><span class="s4">95,300</span><span class="s1">套。省府将住房开工量下降归咎于房屋建筑商所面临的经济压力和挑战。</span></p>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b>未来经济前景</b></span></h2>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">尽管财政状况有所好转，秋季经济报告中的其他指标表明，省民并未广泛感受到好处。安省经济在</span><span class="s4">COVID-19</span><span class="s1">疫情后复苏缓慢，</span><span class="s4">9</span><span class="s1">月份失业率已攀升至</span><span class="s4">6.9%</span><span class="s1">，省府运营的企业（包括博彩和酒品行业）收入也有所下降。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">省府指出，加拿大央行虽然最近下调了政策利率，但利率仍处于高位，短期内将继续对经济增长形成拖累。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">预计安省的实际</span><span class="s4">GDP</span><span class="s1">增长率将从</span><span class="s4">2023</span><span class="s1">年的</span><span class="s4">1.4%</span><span class="s1">降至</span><span class="s4">2024</span><span class="s1">年的</span><span class="s4">0.9%</span><span class="s1">。随着利率逐步下降，预计</span><span class="s4">2025</span><span class="s1">年实际</span><span class="s4">GDP</span><span class="s1">增长率将回升至</span><span class="s4">1.7%</span><span class="s1">，</span><span class="s4">2026</span><span class="s1">年和</span><span class="s4">2027</span><span class="s1">年将达到</span><span class="s4">2.3%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">在劳动力市场方面，省府预测，继</span><span class="s4">2023</span><span class="s1">年就业增长</span><span class="s4">2.4%</span><span class="s1">后，经济放缓将使</span><span class="s4">2024</span><span class="s1">年的就业增长降至</span><span class="s4">1.4%</span><span class="s1">，</span><span class="s4">2025</span><span class="s1">年为</span><span class="s4">1.5%</span><span class="s1">。预计</span><span class="s4">2026</span><span class="s1">年和</span><span class="s4">2027</span><span class="s1">年的就业增长率将平均为</span><span class="s4">1.3%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
