<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>强烈台风康芮逼近 桃园农业局启动防台整备计划</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360663-719929-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市政府农业局长陈冠义10月30日清晨赴桃园果菜市场了解果菜价格、交易量。（桃园市农业局 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年10月30日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）强烈台风康芮逼近台湾，桃园市政府农业局启动防台应变措施，30日兵分多路至各相关场所整备勘查，以期减少灾损。原观音莲花园休闲农业区主办的“2024观音万圣南瓜节”活动会场展示物、帐棚、舞台等活动布置也预先撤除，加强防台，届时依人事行政局公告调整活动营运时间。</p>
<figure id="14360664" aria-describedby="caption-14360664" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360664-719930-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360664-719930-450x338.jpg" alt="派员赴桃园果菜公司冷藏仓储，查验滚动式仓储之蔬菜种类及储藏量。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14360664" class="wp-caption-text">派员赴桃园果菜公司冷藏仓储，查验滚动式仓储之蔬菜种类及储藏量。（桃园市农业局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市政府农业局长陈冠义30日清晨赴桃园果菜市场了解果菜价格、交易量；并派员赴桃园果菜公司冷藏仓储，查验滚动式仓储之蔬菜种类及储藏量。30日桃农批发市场平均菜价为37.3元，相较9月平均价格为35.7元，微幅上涨，主要受台风来临，采购需求增加，桃园市政府也将持续监控农产品批发市场的供应状况。</p>
<figure id="14360665" aria-describedby="caption-14360665" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360665-719931-450x265.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360665-719931-450x265.jpg" alt="桃园市政府农业局长陈冠义关心果菜交易。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14360665" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市政府农业局长陈冠义关心果菜交易。（桃园市农业局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市农业局为因应康芮台风来袭，启动防台应变措施，于10月29、30日派员对竹围渔港、永安渔港进行巡查，针对港区域内的公共设施做好防汛整备，要求渔船进港避风，并加强缆绳固定。</p>
<figure id="14360671" aria-describedby="caption-14360671" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360671-719942-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360671-719942-450x338.jpg" alt="豆类更要预防风害。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14360671" class="wp-caption-text">豆类更要预防风害。（桃园市农业局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14360666" aria-describedby="caption-14360666" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360666-719932-450x257.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360666-719932-450x257.jpg" alt="30日派员对竹围渔港、永安渔港进行巡查。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14360666" class="wp-caption-text">30日派员对竹围渔港、永安渔港进行巡查。（桃园市农业局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另像高梁、大豆、荞麦、甘薯、仙草等杂粮作物，在台风来袭特别需要加强田间排水，农业局也派员到八德、大溪、芦竹、龙潭、平镇等地区，整备勘查蔬菜、番茄、草莓、草花、茭白笋、大豆等作物之防台整备计划。</p>
<figure id="14360670" aria-describedby="caption-14360670" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360670-719941-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360670-719941-450x338.jpg" alt="网室也该防范。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14360670" class="wp-caption-text">网室也该防范。（桃园市农业局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14360667" aria-describedby="caption-14360667" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360667-719933-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360667-719933-450x338.jpg" alt="要求渔船进港避风，并加强缆绳固定。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14360667" class="wp-caption-text">要求渔船进港避风，并加强缆绳固定。（桃园市农业局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈冠义提醒农友，水稻正值乳熟期，茭白笋正值采收期，为减轻强风造成危害，可增加完善防风设施，以减少叶片破损、黄化；果园应加强防风设施、固定枝条、减少植株摇晃而伤害根部或倒伏，已转色的甜柿可以提早采收，但也要注意是否已达药剂安全采收期。</p>
<figure id="14360669" aria-describedby="caption-14360669" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360669-719936-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360669-719936-450x338.jpg" alt="水稻正值乳熟期，要注意防风" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14360669" class="wp-caption-text">水稻正值乳熟期，要注意防风（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14360668" aria-describedby="caption-14360668" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360668-719935-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360668-719935-450x338.jpg" alt="可增加完善防风设施，以减少叶片破损、黄化。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14360668" class="wp-caption-text">可增加完善防风设施，以减少叶片破损、黄化。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈冠义强调，若发生农业灾损，受灾农民应主动通报所在地区公所，并拍摄灾损照片，清楚标示地号，以便快速申请天然灾害救助。◇</p>
