<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>想吃饭后甜点怎么办？试试营养的高蛋白点心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360897-shutterstock_498217546-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>自制甜点能够吃到市售无法复制的无限风味，用到高蛋白食材还能提升饱足感并可稳定血糖。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月30日讯】我们身处健康饮食潮流中，成分健康的点心有超多选择，因此饭后吃点心不需要感到有罪恶感。只是每个人的营养需求不同，哪种点心才适合？</p>
<p>对于想要减糖又没办法克制吃甜食欲望的人，自制高蛋白点心不失为健康之选。自制高蛋白点心可利用食材的天然甜味，不必添加精致糖，让高蛋白成分提升饱足感并稳定血糖，同时收获吃点心的幸福感。</p>
<p>日常食物中，有许多美味、蛋白质含量高的食材可供我们选择，像是品种多样的豆类、豆浆、豆腐、低脂优格、乳酪、起司、杏仁粉、椰子粉、坚果以及蛋白粉。如下介绍几种简单食谱，只要创意变换成分，就能组合成应对不同情境需求的点心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360898-shutterstock_2377158381-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14360898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360898-shutterstock_2377158381-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360898" class="wp-caption-text">希腊优格经过浓缩，蛋白质含量较高，适合制作酸奶冻疗愈又营养。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>冷冻希腊优格</h2>
<p>因为经过浓缩步骤，希腊优格蛋白质含量较高，是健身、增肌减脂人士喜欢的零食。希腊优格绵密的质地搭配坚果、水果、谷麦（Granola）等食材可以摄取多种营养，吃起来相当有饱足感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360900" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360900-shutterstock_2253616777-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14360900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360900-shutterstock_2253616777-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360900" class="wp-caption-text">意式酸奶冻。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花点时间把酸奶分装到小容器，冷冻约4小时至结冻就是酸奶冻。意式酸奶冻则用奶油调稀，加明胶后冷藏结冻。简单上手的酸奶冻有如画布，发挥自己的美感添加黑巧克力、浆果或坚果等食材装饰一番，既能增加风味和膳食纤维，又让它成为疗愈点心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360902" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360902-shutterstock_2257429037-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14360902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360902-shutterstock_2257429037-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360902" class="wp-caption-text">奇亚籽吸水会膨胀变成Q弹小果冻，因此浸泡牛奶便可得到优秀的蛋白质饮品。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>奇亚籽布丁</h2>
<p>奇亚籽给人的普遍印象是膳食纤维丰富，轻松搭配食材就有饱足感。其实它也是优秀的蛋白质食物。不擅长烘焙的话，奇亚籽浸泡牛奶或酸奶最是简单吃法。</p>
<p>奇亚籽吸水会膨胀变成Q弹小果冻，所以不单牛奶，拌入果泥作为酸奶浇头（配料）也很有趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360903" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360903-shutterstock_689562442-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14360903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360903-shutterstock_689562442-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360903" class="wp-caption-text">香蕉有天然糖味和丰富果胶，冷冻之后就能制作“冰淇淋”。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>香蕉“冰淇淋”</h2>
<p>冷冻水果带来不同体验，像是冰过的榴梿，不少人点赞说像雪糕。冷冻猕猴桃和香蕉也有冰淇淋的感觉。尤其香蕉果胶含量高，冷冻至少2小时后在料理机加喜欢的奶液搅打丝滑，就能吃到“天然软冰淇淋”。</p>
<p>如果喜欢柔滑的冰淇淋，这时可以马上搭配黑巧克力酱或花生酱享用。但要期望吃到传统冰淇淋质地，请把香蕉泥装在密封容器冷冻2小时直到冻结。这份食谱额外加糖不是必须步骤，因为香蕉有天然糖味道甜滋滋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360904" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360904-shutterstock_303092498-600x397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14360904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360904-shutterstock_303092498-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360904" class="wp-caption-text">软糯香浓的黑豆巧克力布朗尼有一种令人惊讶疗愈感。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>黑豆布朗尼</h2>
<p>1杯煮熟的黑豆含有15.2克蛋白质，用来制作无麸质布朗尼，质地会同面粉版本一样湿软，还提升蛋白质含量和摄入叶酸、铜、镁和铁等矿物质。</p>
<p>如果用布朗尼预拌粉制作，则需要准备1罐黑豆和1杯水混合，来替换食谱要求的油和鸡蛋份量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360906" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360906-shutterstock_1514278916-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14360906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360906-shutterstock_1514278916-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360906" class="wp-caption-text">巧克力鹰嘴豆泥可作任何小食的蘸酱。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>鹰嘴豆零食</h2>
<p>好吃、营养优质的芝麻酱鹰嘴豆泥收服不少人的心，不过巧克力鹰嘴豆泥也越来越受欢迎。每杯鹰嘴豆含有蛋白质14.5克，搭配可可粉可以做成任何小食的蘸酱。</p>
<p>从头开始制作巧克力鹰嘴豆蘸酱不难，需要将2杯煮熟的鹰嘴豆，四分之一杯可可粉、三分之一杯花生酱和1小撮盐，加2汤匙水在料理机搅打丝滑即可。是否添加香草精可为选项。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360908" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360908-shutterstock_303630566-600x397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14360908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360908-shutterstock_303630566-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360908" class="wp-caption-text">肉桂风味的蜜烤鹰嘴豆。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一种美味甜食是将鹰嘴豆混合蜂蜜、肉桂粉烘烤。做法是把1个罐头鹰嘴豆沥干水，在375华氏度（190摄氏度）烘烤约40分钟至变脆，然后用1汤匙油和蜂蜜，以及适量盐、肉桂粉拌匀，再次回到烤箱烤烤出焦糖效果即成。此外，其它坚果也可以比照鹰嘴豆蜜烤，作为高蛋白零食享用。</p>
<p>喜欢鹰嘴豆，同时也是巧克力迷，那你一定要试试鹰嘴豆巧克力。做法是用上面的方法把鹰嘴豆烤脆，再来将黑巧克力豆加1茶匙椰子油融化并注入铺平的豆子，顶部撒上海盐，冷却变硬即可享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360920" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360920-shutterstock_2103862817.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14360920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360920-shutterstock_2103862817.jpg" alt="自制燕麦饼干减糖少油" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360920" class="wp-caption-text"><br />自制燕麦饼干减糖少油，不失为健康小食。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>燕麦饼干</h2>
<p>燕麦饼干提供膳食纤维，不失为健康小食，但外购要留意含糖量。其实制作燕麦饼乾步骤简单，不妨在家试做看，这样就能用到多种天然的高蛋白食材，像是燕麦粉、奇亚籽、南瓜籽和花生酱，而粘合剂与甜味部分交给香蕉就好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14360909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14360909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360909-shutterstock_2109759839-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14360909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14360909-shutterstock_2109759839-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14360909" class="wp-caption-text">即使减糖，只要点缀艳丽，迷你起司蛋糕看起来就会好好吃。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>迷你起司蛋糕</h2>
<p>如果你已经决定要烤一批燕麦饼干，而下一次的甜点是纽约风格的起司蛋糕，那就太棒了。喜欢简单版本的话，可试着将饼干压碎混合椰枣作为蛋糕底，希腊酸奶配以奶油乳酪（Cream Cheese）铺在上面，撒些浆果即成。</p>
<p>水果换成棉花糖并稍微烤焦，点缀焦糖糖浆或者大麦糖浆，便是应景的冬季甜点。</p>
<p>即使不搭配新鲜水果也有美味吃法，例如用水果冻干粉混合乳酪，让色泽变艳丽，蛋糕看起来就会好好吃。</p>
