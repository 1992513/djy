<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿华埠象棋赛颁奖 筹备明年办“北美杯”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350000-IMG_7121-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>10月13日，波士顿中国象棋会在中华公所举办年度象棋赛，选手们专心下棋。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月14日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）波士顿华埠中国象棋会10月13日顺利举办年度比赛，并为优胜者颁奖。接下来，他们将筹备明年举办“北美杯”中国象棋比赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14350007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350007-IMG_7146.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350007-IMG_7146-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350007" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿中国象棋会在中华公所举办年度象棋赛，选手及颁奖嘉宾们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天，来自纽约和波士顿的10多名棋手在中华公所参与比赛，经过6轮对弈和积分，决出前6名优胜选手。据象棋会会长陈景贤介绍，比赛规则为胜者积2分，和棋双方各积1分，败者0分。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">最终，来自纽约的彭铂以12分的佳绩赢得冠军。他也曾在2023年的波士顿象棋赛中赢取亚军。如今再获佳绩，他向棋友们分享说，下棋时专注很重要。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">波士顿中国象棋会成员、本次比赛第4名的刘文哲向现场老一辈的“波士顿第一代棋手”表达敬意，还说：“我们第二代棋手也要努力，有个好名次。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14350006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350006-IMG_7139.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350006-IMG_7139-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350006" class="wp-caption-text">冠军颁奖，左起：安良工商会主席陈伟民、中华公所主席雷国辉、冠军彭铂、中华公所英文书记阮鸿灿、象棋会会长陈景贤。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14350005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350005-IMG_7135.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350005-IMG_7135-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350005" class="wp-caption-text">陈伟民为亚军赵建华（右）颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14350004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350004-IMG_7133.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350004-IMG_7133-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350004" class="wp-caption-text">阮鸿灿为季军李新荣（右）颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">波士顿华埠今年的比赛由纽英崙中华公所、华埠商会和安良工商会共同主办。作为颁奖嘉宾的中华公所主席雷国辉说，这是这3个团体第3次联合举办中国象棋比赛，感谢各位的参与，尤其感谢远道而来的朋友们的支持。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">安良工商会主席陈伟民、中华公所英文书记阮鸿灿也都来到现场颁奖。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">象棋会会长陈景贤展望说，他们正在筹划，希望明年8月或9月可以举办“北美杯”中国象棋赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14350003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350003-IMG_7131.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350003-IMG_7131-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350003" class="wp-caption-text">雷国辉为第四名刘文哲（左）颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14350002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350002" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350002-IMG_7128.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350002-IMG_7128-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350002" class="wp-caption-text">陈伟民为第五名李新达（右）颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14350001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350001-IMG_7126.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350001-IMG_7126-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350001" class="wp-caption-text">阮鸿灿为第六名黄锐辉（右）颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">今年波士顿比赛的前6名依次为：彭铂、赵建华、李新荣、刘文哲、李新达、黄锐辉。◇</p>
