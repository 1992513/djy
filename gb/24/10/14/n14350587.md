<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>社区团体参观华埠新昆士高中</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350585-DJY00271-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>10月10日，波士顿华埠社区联盟（TCC）月会参观新华埠昆士高中，游览楼顶活动平台。（章佩儿/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月15日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿波士顿报导）10月10日，波士顿华埠社区联盟（TCC）月会参观了新建成的华埠昆士高中（Josiah Quincy Upper School），在校长张可仁（Richard Chang）的带领下，了解这所于本学年启用的现代化校舍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14350579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350579-DJY00183.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350579-DJY00183-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350579" class="wp-caption-text">校长张可仁（中）向来宾们介绍这栋新教学楼。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14350578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350578-DJY00178.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350578-DJY00178-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350578" class="wp-caption-text">校长张可仁向来宾们介绍这栋新教学楼。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这座耗资约2亿美元、由波士顿市和麻州政府共同资助的新校舍，拥有六层楼，能够容纳650名学生。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">今年9月12日，波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）、麻州财政厅长戈德堡（Deborah Goldberg）等诸多嘉宾来到现场，为新教学楼剪彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14350583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350583-DJY00218.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350583-DJY00218-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350583" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们参观新教学楼的健身房。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14350586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350586" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350586-DJY00259.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350586-DJY00259-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350586" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们参观新教学楼的教室。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">华埠昆士中学服务于6至12年级的学生。新楼采用钢筋混凝土结构，内有29间教室，35个教学空间，1万平方英尺的体育馆，435座位的礼堂，125座位的剧场，以及250座位的饭堂。新楼从2021年6月破土，到今年8月完工，正好在2024-2025新学年开放。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">新楼内还有媒体中心、会议厅、健身房、舞蹈室、办公室、英语第二语言教室、隔音音乐练习室、教师休息室、洗衣房等设施。此外，在建设大楼的同时，工程人员还美化了楼外空间，包括种植植物和重铺道路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14350582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350582-DJY00208.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350582-DJY00208-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350582" class="wp-caption-text">新教学楼的餐厅。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14350581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350581-DJY00197.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350581-DJY00197-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350581" class="wp-caption-text">新教学楼的餐厅。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">张可仁校长向社区代表们强调，新校舍的设计，不仅注重学术需求，还充分考虑了学生的社会情感发展。该楼还采用了先进的空气过滤和循环系统，为在校学生提供更加健康的学习环境。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">此外，该楼还具备高度的气候适应性，采用了多项绿色技术，旨在减少碳足迹和环境污染。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14350584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350584" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350584-DJY00245.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350584-DJY00245-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350584" class="wp-caption-text">新教学楼的剧场。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14350580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14350580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350580-DJY00194.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14350580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14350580-DJY00194-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14350580" class="wp-caption-text">新教学楼内的装饰品。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">访客们纷纷表示，对这座符合LEED金级标准的建筑印象深刻。校方则希望，这座新设施能够成为社区中心，继续支持华埠及其他移民家庭的教育需求。◇</p>
