<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚特兰大甘斯维尔台湾商会青商会首次聚会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357406-dd78dd62140a675baabca353fb356d20-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>主任与会员大合照。（侨教中心）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月25日讯】甘斯维尔台湾商会青商会(Taiwanese JUNIOR Chamber of Commerce of Gainesville-TJCCGA)于10月19日在亚特兰大文教中心举办2024年第一次会员大会。这是自从本年7月28日该会举行复会仪式后的第一个会员活动。计23位年轻企业家和商业专业人士，一起参与活动，不但促进了跨文化交流，也为青商会会员提供事业和商机拓展的宝贵机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357413" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357413-03949ae5095d4eb89d9e29266df67a84-e1729813418223.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14357413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357413-03949ae5095d4eb89d9e29266df67a84-e1729813418223.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357413" class="wp-caption-text">会员大合照（侨教中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动一开始，由会长邓暐俐致词，表示感谢所有青商会的会员与其新来的朋友们共襄盛举，期望透过本次活动让大家可以彼此了解，进而让大家在自己的职场上可以互相帮助。同时在致词中提到鼓励会员申请i侨卡，期望每位会员都能够拥有一张专属自己的卡。</p>
<div class="mceTemp">
<ul id="attachment_14357408" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 400px;"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357408-0b0371905f7e7eb2ac5a010fe5f20ccd-e1729813068794.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14357408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357408-0b0371905f7e7eb2ac5a010fe5f20ccd-e1729813068794.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a>会长邓暐俐致词（侨教中心）</ul>
</div>
<p>而这次活动也特别邀请到亚特兰大华侨文教服务中心欧宏伟主任到场，欧主任致词表示：期望青商会的会员们可以多多参与侨社活动，透过侨界新生代参与侨社服务，让侨社永续发展。期许侨青藉由自身的语言优势融入主流社会，并可以协助推动外交工作，提升台湾在主流社会的能见度与知名度。最后期望连结台湾，促进国际经贸发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357409" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357409-a54b5c80ad376c239dc71c0c7db1d571-e1729813165947.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14357409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357409-a54b5c80ad376c239dc71c0c7db1d571-e1729813165947.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357409" class="wp-caption-text">中心欧主任致词（侨教中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天活动中，介绍该会现任的干部有会长邓暐俐、副会长秦文皓、副会长杨博樽、秘书长潘颖达、秘书长文致懿、财务长郭效凡、公关长邵麒睿、资讯长郭效童、机动长叶立维、活动长梁庭瑜、活动长王姿尹、活动长游孟慈及法律顾问陈韦霖；活动现场提供美味可口的PIZZA、墨西哥卷饼、薯片、起司鸡肉卷及具有台湾味的豆花，又有精彩的抽奖活动，让与会者以食会友，气氛热闹愉快。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357411" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357411-bd7da113288b1a52c737317600b15fb1-e1729813266490.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14357411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357411-bd7da113288b1a52c737317600b15fb1-e1729813266490.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357411" class="wp-caption-text">活动现场照（侨教中心）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14357412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357412" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357412-9a014c9675fc6ad68326fb166f2b76a4-e1729813341162.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14357412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357412-9a014c9675fc6ad68326fb166f2b76a4-e1729813341162.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357412" class="wp-caption-text">现场美食（侨教中心）</figcaption></figure>
