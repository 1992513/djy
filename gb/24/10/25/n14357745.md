<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>曾说“你们会成功” Ryan见BAE173再参赛落泪</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357865-241025084012100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>BAE173成员参与韩国选秀节目《PROJECT 7》挑战再次出道。（LINE TV提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月25日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）由演员李洙赫担任主持人的韩国选秀节目《PROJECT 7》，让来自世界各地的200位少年竞争仅有7位的出道名额，年纪最小的参赛者仅11岁。其中，韩国男团BAE173在历经选秀《PEAK TIME》后又参赛，让与他们合作过的知名制作人Ryan落泪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357875-241025084010100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14357875" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357875-241025084010100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357875" class="wp-caption-text">韩国演员李洙赫担任选秀节目《PROJECT 7》主持人。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《PROJECT 7》第一集就要淘汰半数选手，选手们进入小房间内由不同导师评选的过程，也会同步在外场大萤幕上直播。其中最受瞩的，除了公认颜值第一的练习生金成旼外，还有再次挑战出道的韩国男团BAE173。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357876-241025084000100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14357876" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357876-241025084000100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357876" class="wp-caption-text">金成旼参与韩国选秀节目《PROJECT 7》节目照。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>选手金成旼从海选时期，就被其他练习生公认拥有帅气面貌，站上评选舞台第一时间，他还先给了一个俏皮的“狐獴式打招呼”，随即在备受期待的状态下，开始唱跳NewJeans的《Hype boy》，没想到结束后歌声与舞蹈皆未达预期，让导师们相当意外。参与过《街头女战士2》的编舞家Bada直言：“舞不能这样跳，没有节奏，连基本功都没有。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357868-241025084007100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14357868" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357868-241025084007100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357868" class="wp-caption-text">金成旼在选秀节目《PROJECT 7》中“狐獴式打招呼”。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金成旼一度面临淘汰边缘，但MAMAMOO玟星最终评估，以将他视为潜力股的概念，和另外两位老师达成共识，给予合格票并留下期许：“我想看他（金成旼）学习过后的样子，希望能有衬托颜值的实力！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357874" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357874-241025084823100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14357874" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357874-241025084823100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357874" class="wp-caption-text">MAMAMOO成员玟星担任《PROJECT 7》评审。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>出道近四年的男团BAE173，去年参加男团选秀节目《PEAK TIME》，这次由部分团员全珉稶、金贤右、朴俊庶、柳盈署再次出战《PROJECT 7》，让导师之一的音乐制作人Ryan（Ryan Sewon Jhun）一看到他们就心疼到哭，哽咽地说：“我问不出‘为什么你们在这里’。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357869-241025084828100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14357869" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357869-241025084828100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357869" class="wp-caption-text">Ryan Sewon Jhun在选秀节目《PROJECT 7》中见到BAE173成员时泣不成声。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Ryan和BAE173过去曾经合作过迷你专辑主打曲，当时他就不断鼓励BAE173，全珉稶说“他（Ryan）是我们的哥哥也是导师，他都会对我们说，你们做得到，你们会成功，但其实我们没有成功”，令人听了鼻酸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357872" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357872-241025084005100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14357872" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357872-241025084005100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357872" class="wp-caption-text">BAE173全珉稶因想再次于舞台表演而参与选秀节目《PROJECT 7》。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BAE173为了继续追梦再度站上舞台，表演过程中意外失误不断，先是金贤右名牌脱落，后来全珉稶唱到一半时，身上的麦克风也掉了下来，让人捏一把冷汗。他们靠着丰富的经验化险为夷，反而替表演加了许多分，导师HUI红着眼眶说：“这些失误的处理，都让我觉得这是个完成度很高的表演，期待未来在舞台上相遇！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357873" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357873-241025084826100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14357873" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357873-241025084826100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357873" class="wp-caption-text">PENTAGON成员Hui在《PROJECT 7》中因BAE173眼眶泛泪。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了从200名选手中选出100名进入第二轮主题曲组装赛，也让导师们绞尽脑汁，在挑选发展新鲜感跟可塑性中争执不断，李海印看评选影片时，突然提问：“他（选手）的身高是不是有点矮？”让个子较为娇小的河成云立刻跳出来反驳：“矮哪里不行了？”还开玩笑一度作势要离场，Ryan连忙缓颊：“禁止讨论身高！”让现场气氛和缓不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14357870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14357870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357870-241025084002100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14357870" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14357870-241025084002100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14357870" class="wp-caption-text">李海印担任韩国选秀节目《PROJECT 7》评审。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终通过初选的百位选手进入了“PROJECT山庄”进行集宿，选手们将分成四个等级，拿到了四种不同颜色的练习服（黄色、粉红色、蓝色和灰色）分别代表了S、A、B、C 的级别并公开了第二轮主题曲《Up to You》组装赛，选手们的舞台位置将根据完成度进行评分分配，有注目度最高的第一阶梯，也有只能成为大家的影子的阴影区域。</p>
<p>为了站上最好的舞台，选手们都铆足了劲，但在评审的过程，选手们的表现让导师频频摇头，李海印甚至说出了：“你们也太糟糕了吧？”</p>
<p>选秀节目《PROJECT 7》每周六于LINE TV播出。</p>
