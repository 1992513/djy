<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中华民国113年国庆 新英格兰各州嘉宾齐贺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344863-DSC07439-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>庆祝中华民国113年双十国庆，新英格兰地区政要嘉宾在10月4日晚上的酒会中一起切蛋糕。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨、章佩儿波士顿报导）值中华民国113年“双十”国庆即将到来之际，驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处10月4日举办国庆酒会，与新英格兰各州政要、嘉宾共叙情谊，得到广泛支持。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚在波士顿洲际酒店（InterContinental Boston），各州政要、嘉宾、侨团及其它友好团体等总计近400人出席晚会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14344859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344859-DSC07397.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344859-DSC07397-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344859" class="wp-caption-text">诸多嘉宾参与波士顿庆祝中华民国113年国庆酒会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344860-DSC07398.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344860-DSC07398-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344860" class="wp-caption-text">诸多嘉宾参与波士顿庆祝中华民国113年国庆酒会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在演唱美国国歌和中华民国国歌之后，经文处处长廖朝宏首先回顾了台湾在民主制度、经济发展等方面的成就。随后，来自三个州的众议院议长分别发言，强调他们与台湾之间的友谊。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">来宾们还一起切开“双十”蛋糕，在生日歌的氛围下，祝中华民国生日快乐。更有一些来宾高呼“中华民国万岁”以示支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14344855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344855" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344855-DJY09352.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344855-DJY09352-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344855" class="wp-caption-text">歌手JC Alula领唱美国和中华民国国歌。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344862-DSC07437.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344862-DSC07437-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344862" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰地区政要嘉宾在10月4日晚上的酒会中一起切蛋糕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>友台盛会?</strong><strong>嘉宾云集</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">参与庆祝活动的政要，包括麻州众议院议长马里亚诺（Ronald Mariano）、缅因州众议院议长罗丝（Rachel Talbot Ross）、罗德岛州众议院代议长肯尼迪（Brian Patrick Kennedy）和总财务长迪奥萨（James Diossa），以及新罕布尔州的几位议员，还有其他一些官员、议员办公室代表、总领事团等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14344850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344850-DJY09572.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344850-DJY09572-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344850" class="wp-caption-text">罗德岛州众议院代议长肯尼迪（Brian Patrick Kennedy）参加波士顿双十酒会，与经文处处长廖朝宏夫妇合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344851" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344851-DJY09571.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344851-DJY09571-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344851" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿双十酒会展示了美国各级政府赠送的褒奖。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344852-DJY09555.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344852-DJY09555-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344852" class="wp-caption-text">在佛蒙特州军校就读的台湾学生参加波士顿双十国庆酒会。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">现场有侨务委员陈仕维、中华公所主席雷国辉、中华总会会长余丽媖等各传统侨社代表，有新英格兰台学联、台商会等台湾侨团成员，还有一些来自香港和中国大陆的支持民主自由人士。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14344857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344857" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344857-DJY09310.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344857-DJY09310-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344857" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员陈仕维夫妇（右）到场参加波士顿庆双十酒会。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344865-DJY09315.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344865-DJY09315-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344865" class="wp-caption-text">侨界代表们参加波士顿庆双十酒会，与经文处廖朝宏处长夫妇合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">酒会邀请在波士顿就读音乐学院的台湾学生组成弦乐四重奏乐团，演奏台湾民谣组曲。现场设置国庆文宣广告看板，以及播放外交部双十国庆影片“民主和平繁荣的新台湾”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14344853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344853-DJY09498.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344853-DJY09498-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344853" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿地区的台湾学生们为双十酒会演奏乐曲。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344854-DJY09414.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344854-DJY09414-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344854" class="wp-caption-text">缅因州众议院议长罗丝（Rachel Talbot Ross）向廖朝宏处长赠送褒奖。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>支持民主?</strong><strong>政界共识</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">廖朝宏首先谈到，今年1月台湾再度顺利完成总统大选，不畏中国的威吓胁迫，再次选择了自由民主，同时也向国际社会清楚传达了一项讯息：“一个没有自由、民主及人权的未来，不会是台湾人民的选项”。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">廖朝宏感谢新英格兰地区五个州的议会今年分别通过友台决议案，包括恭贺赖清德总统就职、纪念《台湾关系法》45周年，以及支持深化台美经贸及教育合作交流等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">廖朝宏说，台湾跟新英格兰地区在经贸、投资、科技及教育等领域的关系持续深化，双方不仅共享民主价值，也是值得信赖的合作伙伴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14344869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344869-DJY09365.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344869-DJY09365-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344869" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏欢迎来宾，回顾台湾的成就。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344858-DSC07378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344858-DSC07378-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344858" class="wp-caption-text">政要嘉宾参加波士顿庆双十酒会。左起：麻州众议员黄子安（Donald Wong）、议长马里亚诺（Ronald Mariano）、中华公所英文书记阮鸿灿。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344870-DJY09471.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344870-DJY09471-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344870" class="wp-caption-text">诸多嘉宾参与波士顿庆祝中华民国113年国庆酒会。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">上台发言的三位议长，也分别代表各自所在州的政府，表达了对中华民国台湾及民主自由理念的支持。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">罗德岛州众议院代议长肯尼迪谈到，该州议会每年举办“台湾日”，并通过友台决议案。缅因州众议院议长罗丝则称赞了台湾经文处在新英格兰地区所做的工作，并代表议会向廖朝宏赠送了中华民国国庆贺状。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">曾两次访问台湾的麻州众议院议长马里亚诺，将台湾称为“自由的灯塔，言论自由的灯塔”。“新英格兰各州不会在与中华民国台湾的关系方面退缩。”他说。◇</p>
