<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>庆祝民国113年国庆? 亚特兰大荣光会举办升旗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347733-DSC_2683-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>升旗典礼预备，荣光会会长张世杰就主席位。（吕尚明/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月10日讯】（大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大报导）为庆祝中华民国113年双十国庆，亚特兰大荣光联谊会于10月6日(星期日)上午十一时，假亚特兰大侨教中心举行双十国庆升旗典礼。许多身居美国但是心怀故乡的侨学界人士踊跃参加，爱国情怀洋溢于会场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14347734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347734" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347734-DSC_2685-e1728532408177.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347734-DSC_2685-e1728532408177.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347734" class="wp-caption-text">主席与参与典礼各界人士预备升旗。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14347750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347750" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347750-DSC_2693-e1728533469835.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347750-DSC_2693-e1728533469835.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347750" class="wp-caption-text">青天白日满地红国旗冉冉上升。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14347751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347751" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347751-DSC_2696-e1728533582121.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347751-DSC_2696-e1728533582121.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347751" class="wp-caption-text">民众三呼“中华民国万岁”！（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管有不少侨胞回台湾参加国庆晚会以及世界台湾商会年会，还是有大约180人到场，包括侨胞、侨友、中文学校学生、台湾留学生等。此外，驻亚特兰大台北经济文化办事处王翼龙处长及亚特兰大华侨文教服务中心欧宏伟主任、侨务咨询委员何智达、黄美美、王祥瑞、吕志全、侨务委员陆复泰等侨领均出席。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14347759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347759" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347759-DSC_2759-2-e1728534493605.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347759-DSC_2759-2-e1728534493605.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347759" class="wp-caption-text">左起，王祥瑞、吕志全、黄美美、王翼龙、张世杰、欧宏伟、陆复泰。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>升旗典礼由亚特兰大荣光联谊会会长张世杰担任主席，叶竣图担任司仪，FASCA（海外青少年文化大使协会）领唱美国国歌、中华民国国歌、及国旗歌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14347735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347735" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347735-DSC_2686-e1728532514754.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347735-DSC_2686-e1728532514754.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347735" class="wp-caption-text">FASCA年轻人（着黄衣者）与中文学校小朋友们领唱美国国歌、中华民国国歌、及国旗歌。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后，与会民众移至中心大礼堂进行庆祝大会。荣光会会长张世杰首先致词，陈述了中华民国自武昌起义开始的艰辛创立过程，又经历了与日本抗战、国共内战而最后退守台湾。在台湾经过了数十年的惨淡经营，中华民国现在是一个自由民主国家，并且在全球工业中有着举足轻重的地位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14347748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347748" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347748-DSC_2703-e1728533333456.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347748-DSC_2703-e1728533333456.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347748" class="wp-caption-text">荣光会会长张世杰致词。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻亚特兰大台北经济文化办事处王翼龙处长表示，他在亚特兰大已经参加了八次升旗典礼，每次都是风和日丽，对中华民国来说是个好兆头。王处长并告诉大家，在几天前已经在乔治亚水族馆举办了国庆酒会，选择这地点是因为其与台湾的渊源，因为水族馆的明星、几条鲸鲨都是来自台湾。此外，今年也是美国政府实行《台湾关系法》的45周年，其中提倡美国与台湾需有政治、军事、教育、文化方面的合作与交流，而其具体成果之一就是驻亚特兰大办事处管辖的美东南六个州成立了美国唯一的区域性的台湾连线，称之为“美东南州议会台湾连线”(Taiwan Caucus)，足见美国东南地区对中华民国的重视程度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14347757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347757" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347757-Screenshot-2024-10-09-232259-e1728534277212.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347757-Screenshot-2024-10-09-232259-e1728534277212.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347757" class="wp-caption-text">办事处王翼龙处长致词。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14347747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347747" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347747-DSC_2689-e1728533151672.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347747-DSC_2689-e1728533151672.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347747" class="wp-caption-text">办事处王翼龙处长（灰色西装者）、侨教中心欧宏伟主任（王处长左侧黑色西装者）与各侨务委员等人士唱中华民国国歌。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14347763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347763" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347763-DSC_2764-e1728534650672.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347763-DSC_2764-e1728534650672.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347763" class="wp-caption-text">王翼龙处长等与FASCA年轻学子及中文学校校长戴念华（右执国旗者）合影。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚特兰大华侨文教服务中心欧宏伟主任感谢所有的侨领对此升旗活动的支持及参与，并且说明华侨从民国建立、抗日救国到近年来华侨回到台湾的投资兴国，例如AI巨擘辉达的创办人黄仁勋计划在台湾设厂，都显示海外侨民对中华民国的重要性，让台湾成为经济的日不落国。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14347755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347755" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347755-DSC_2726-1-e1728533942426.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347755-DSC_2726-1-e1728533942426.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347755" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心欧宏伟主任致词。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨务委员陆复泰也代表侨领致词，祝贺中华民国国运昌隆。最后“龙凤鼓队”给大家带来表演，以隆隆鼓声伴着中华民国的陆、海、空三军军歌向三军致敬，最后以“男儿当自强”、“今山古道”两首作为压轴演出、结束庆典。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14347756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14347756" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347756-DSC_2732-e1728534054380.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14347756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14347756-DSC_2732-e1728534054380.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14347756" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员陆复泰致词。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
