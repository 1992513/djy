<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>避免心毒上身 中医师教你如何护心清毒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354640-d0aff5be09f555257bd6f3f2f1428ce4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>避免心毒上身，中医师教你居家生活如何护心清毒。（马光医疗网提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">生活步调紧凑、工作繁忙、考试不断，每个阶段都有不同的心理压力；你是否常日思夜想、忧虑烦心？是否总有挥之不去的负面情绪？中医师强调排忧解难，必须从“心”认识，自我调理，方可避免心毒上身。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>心火过旺 常见心毒症状</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">中医强调“心藏神”、“心主神明”，也就是说“心”统管精神、意识、认知、感受等思维活动。若是心情时常忧虑、烦躁，长期处于高度压力，五志郁极化火，可能导致心火过亢，常见的心毒症状有睡眠障碍、烦躁焦虑、心悸心慌、皮肤痒疹、口苦、小便赤涩等。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>护心清毒 居家保健法</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14354640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354640" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354640-d0aff5be09f555257bd6f3f2f1428ce4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354640-d0aff5be09f555257bd6f3f2f1428ce4-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354640" class="wp-caption-text">护心清毒这样按。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">改善心火旺引起的不易入睡</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">皮肤和泌尿等问题，中医师教你居家生活如何护心清毒：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?不易入睡：评估睡眠障碍与否的项目，包括</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">入睡所需时间、夜醒次数、夜尿次数、做梦、早醒等；心火亢引起的失眠，大多表现为“不易入睡”。临床上常以栀子豉汤、黄连阿胶汤做为治疗主方。</span></p>
<h3><strong>居家保健法：</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-改善失眠、焦虑、心悸等症状，避免摄取咖啡因，睡前做些伸展运动；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-多按压“神门穴”、“劳宫穴”，有助于宁心安神。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14354639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354639" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354639-3f7ea6a5a7e3916a260b318baa57f567.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354639-3f7ea6a5a7e3916a260b318baa57f567-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354639" class="wp-caption-text">神门穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14354638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354638" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354638-726d25cfe9de404c19982d5f2d1bfa74.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354638-726d25cfe9de404c19982d5f2d1bfa74-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354638" class="wp-caption-text">劳宫穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-皮肤问题：天气炽热时，容易汗流浃背，加上生活压力大、作息不规律，导致免疫系统紊乱，许多患者因汗疱疹、湿疹、荨痳疹等皮肤问题求诊。《素问．至真要大论》提到：“诸痛痒疮，皆属于心”，所以改善此类问题，临床上常从治疗“心火”着手，处方以黄连解毒汤、连翘、牡丹皮等为主。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-减少直接曝晒于阳光下；出汗时，尽快以湿纸巾擦拭，以防汗液不断刺激患部；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-多按压“少府穴”、“少冲穴”，有助于泻热解毒。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14354641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354641" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354641-026fd5cd3aecef02ab155ad1a17fb1d8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354641-026fd5cd3aecef02ab155ad1a17fb1d8-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354641" class="wp-caption-text">少府穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14354866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354866" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354866-shutterstock_1146436469.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354866-shutterstock_1146436469-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354866" class="wp-caption-text">少冲穴。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-泌尿问题：中医认为“心”和“小便”息息相关；心经与小肠经互为表里经，有经络相连，若心火亢盛移热于小肠（小肠主液），泌别清浊（注1）功能失司，则会发生小便色深、灼热、疼痛等泌尿道症状。因此，若患者出现泌尿道感染、小便断续不畅、排尿迟滞，抑或有余尿感，临床上常开立“清心火”处方，如清心莲子饮、导赤散等。</span></p>
<h3><strong>居家保健法：</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-多饮水，使火热从小便顺利排出；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-多按压“液门穴”、“中渚穴”，有助于袪湿清热。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14354654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354654" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354654-49d223126b3ba58b613ead99f7ee416a.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354654-49d223126b3ba58b613ead99f7ee416a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354654" class="wp-caption-text">多按压“液门穴”、“中渚穴”，有助于袪湿清热。（健康1+1／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>医师小叮咛：</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">心为一身之主，心病了会衍生出许多病症，生活忙碌之余，千万别忘了休息片刻，倾听自己的“心”声；若有医疗相关问题则需咨询医师，不可自行诊断。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">注：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.泌别清浊：指小肠将从胃而来的饮食水谷消化后，分清别浊的功能。“分清”是小肠吸收饮食精微（营养成分）后，由脾转输到身体各部位；“别浊”则是小肠消化后的糟粕，下注大肠，或渗入膀胱，成为大小便排出体外。<strong>◎</strong></span></p>
