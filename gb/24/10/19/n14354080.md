<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义家扶  “不给糖，就捣蛋”亲子黏土DIY</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354081-717194-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大合照。（嘉义家扶提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年10月19日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义家扶中心于今(19)日办理“茉莉阅读苗圃”推广活动-“不给糖，就捣蛋”亲子黏土DIY，共计9户27人报名参与，本次活动透过手指谣、故事绘本、创意黏土DIY等多元课程，创造出与有趣的亲子互动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14354083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354083" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354083-717196-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354083-717196-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354083" class="wp-caption-text">课程以创意黏土DIY，随意捏塑黏土的方式刺激孩子的触觉，加深孩子的创造力、想像力。（嘉义家扶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“茉莉阅读苗圃”推广阅读与亲子多元课程，本次活动一开始，老师藉由手指谣开场暖身，让孩子训练小肌肉发展及口语上的练习，接着生动有趣地进行《不给糖，就捣蛋》故事导读，藉由绘本提及的段落“给你糖！不捣蛋！”、“彩色的糖果有彩虹的颜色”，介绍万圣节的意义及由来，让孩子们学习到数量、颜色和形状，最后课程以创意黏土DIY，随意捏塑黏土的方式刺激孩子的触觉，加深孩子的创造力、想像力，更藉由亲子一起创作的过程留下共创的作品与难忘的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14354082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354082" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354082-717195-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354082-717195-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354082" class="wp-caption-text">期望透过茉莉阅读推广计划，提升家庭读书风气学习环境，增加家庭亲子互动。（嘉义家扶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>恩恩跟璇璇常常参加茉莉阅读苗圃活动，每当恩恩姊妹得知有活动时，都会立马请母亲帮忙报名，恩恩与璇璇喜欢万圣节快乐的气氛，能在万圣节前夕一起跟母亲用黏土制作“会飞的巫婆”，真是好不快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14354084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354084" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354084-717197-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354084-717197-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354084" class="wp-caption-text">藉由亲子一起创作的过程留下共创的作品与难忘的回忆。（嘉义家扶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14354086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354086" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354086-717198-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354086-717198-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354086" class="wp-caption-text">藉由亲子一起创作的过程留下共创的作品与难忘的回忆。（嘉义家扶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14354087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354087" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354087-717199-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354087-717199-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354087" class="wp-caption-text">让孩子们学习到数量、颜色和形状，最后课程以创意黏土DIY，随意捏塑黏土的方式刺激孩子的触觉，加深各种学习能力及创造力。（嘉义家扶提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“不给糖，就捣蛋”亲子黏土DIY课程为中心推出的“茉莉阅读苗圃推广计划”系列活动之一，中心主任沈明彦表示，中心结合茉莉二手书店阅读苗圃推广计划，共同为亲子互动尽一份心力。一系列的亲子活动，都非常欢迎嘉义地区的社区民众们一同来参与，亦可办理借书证借书，期望透过茉莉阅读推广计划，提升家庭读书风气学习环境，增加家庭亲子互动。</p>
