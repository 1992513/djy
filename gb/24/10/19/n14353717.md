<h6>2025 Subaru Forester Touring。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年10月19日讯】新一代Forester早在2023年洛杉矶车展上亮相，但笔者只在今年年初的Oregon车展上看到真身，虽不能第一时间目睹它，但在看到Forester第6代仍是相当兴奋。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d3ohms0ydcz20j.cloudfront.net/I7zM5JF8w"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/reRwOh2E0vw?si=TXRkkiwvI5SoXDF7" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>2025年全新Subaru Forester结合了前所未有的设计、技术和舒适性。全新Forester的内外设计现代而流畅，前脸搭载崭新格栅与LED头灯相连，营造出一体化的盾牌效果。新车的肩线突出其高度和力量，而凸出的轮拱则强调其越野能力。全新Forester的越野和操控能力同样出色，拥有8.7吋离地间隙，适合不同地形，延续Forester越野之王的称号。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14353718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14353718" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353718-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14353718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353718-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_06-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14353718" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Subaru Forester Touring。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>结构和安全方面，2025年款Forester使用了增强的Subaru全球平台（Subaru Global Platform），抗扭刚性提高10%，同时，车体刚性提升使动态表现更好，乘坐体验更为舒适，车内噪音也应有所降低，但事实上在高速公路行驶时，仍然有相当风噪传入车厢。然而，Forester在高速公路上的过弯表现非常好，在加速越线超车也得心应手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14353719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14353719" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353719-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14353719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353719-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_08-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14353719" class="wp-caption-text">新Forester头灯组。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在越野路段测试，加强的底盘应对崎岖路面也很容易，攀石上下坡也如履平地，而且刚下了点雨，也没有明显打滑情况出现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14353720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14353720" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353720-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14353720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353720-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_10-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14353720" class="wp-caption-text">雾灯组。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>标配的EyeSight驾驶辅助技术不仅进一步提升了安全性能，包括自动紧急刹车、盲点侦测、车道变换辅助、后方车流警示等。首次搭载的紧急停止辅助系统，能在驾驶者失去反应时，自动停车并通知紧急服务。此外，结合了“自适应巡航控制（Advanced Adapter Cruise Control）”及EyeSight的“路线置中Lane Centering）”，可以成为高速公路上的半自动驾驶辅助工具，但驾驶者仍需定时轻轻握着方向盘，让系统知道驾驶者仍专注路况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14353721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14353721" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353721-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_24.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14353721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353721-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_24-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14353721" class="wp-caption-text">11.6吋Subaru Starlink多媒体系统（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内部设计同样出色，配置了11.6吋Subaru Starlink多媒体系统，支援无线Apple CarPlay和无线Android Auto，垂直的萤幕有点像iPad，底部更有Home键。仪表板和内饰设计更注重实用性。全景玻璃视野、暗色车顶内衬以及后排隐私玻璃则进一步提升了乘坐体验。在舒适性和便利性方面，2025年款Forester进一步提升，车内空间更为宽敞，座椅设计更为支撑且减少疲劳感。中控台顶部装有红外线感应器（Driver Focus Distraction Mitigation System），可侦察驾驶者面部方向，当汽车行驶时而驾驶者面部不是面向前方，又或感应到驾驶者疲累，系统马上会发出警告。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14353722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14353722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353722-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14353722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353722-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_20-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14353722" class="wp-caption-text">2.5升Subaru Boxer直四水平对向引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>搭载的2.5升Subaru Boxer直四水平对向引擎，最高提供180匹马力，并搭配Lineartronic CVT自动变速器，可在不同路况下提供更敏捷的操控体验。所有车型标配 Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive系统，并配有X-MODE控制系统和陡坡缓降控制。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14353723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14353723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353723-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14353723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353723-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_16-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14353723" class="wp-caption-text">踢脚感应电动尾门。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>踢脚感应电动尾门首次在Subaru车型上亮相，方便车主在双手拿东西时进行操作。所有车型均标配无匙进入、胎压监测、转向响应式LED头灯和自动远光灯等功能。在车载技术方面，手机无线充电板、SiriusXM 和 TomTom 导航。该系统还整合了 what3words 地点技术，能精确定位。安全和安防系统则包含SOS紧急援助、车辆定位、远程锁车和维修提醒等功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14353877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14353877" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353877-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_23.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14353877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353877-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_23-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14353877" class="wp-caption-text">Forest仪表板。 （李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新2025 Subaru Forester提供多种车型选择，包括 Base、Premium、Sport、Limited 和 Touring，价格由29,695美元（Base）到39,995美元（Touring）。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14353878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14353878" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353878-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14353878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14353878-2024_Subaru_Forester_Touring_26-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14353878" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
