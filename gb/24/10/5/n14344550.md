<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗栗市首办歌唱大赛 青少年的“畅歌”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344551-713789-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>以歌唱大赛“畅歌”，让年轻人有大展才艺与互相观摩的舞台。（苗市公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年10月05日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗市公所为倡导青少年从事正当休闲娱乐并激发更多热情活力，于10月5日在苗栗市玉华里社区活动中心首次举办以青少年为主的歌唱大赛“畅歌”，藉由歌唱比赛让年轻人有大展才艺与互相观摩的舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14344552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344552-713790.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344552-713790-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344552" class="wp-caption-text">得奖前10名皆可获得奖金1万元（苗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次比赛招募40位参赛者，经开放报名后民众相当踊跃。参赛者来自四面八方，除了苗栗市民外，更多来自于苗栗县十八乡镇市，甚至有远自桃园市及台中市的跨县市参赛者；参赛的曲目更是琳琅满目，除了传统及现代的客家歌曲之外，还有为数不少的国语流行歌曲以及自弹自唱的创作歌曲，百家争鸣、竞争激烈，相当精彩。</p>
<p>经黎俊平、谢其国、赖仁政、何易峰、庄红如等五位在歌唱界专业评审综合评分后，得奖者如下:黄羽彤、陈育煊、刘尚康、赖苡辰、刘子豪、吴沛昀、陈岳均、萧正佑、罗士筌以及赖建霆等10位参赛者；除了可以获得1万元奖金，更将于10月26日于猫里喵亲子公园旧经国路旁广场举办的四炆四炒美食飨宴时授奖及献唱，让大家可以欣赏素人唱将的歌艺与风采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14344553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344553-713791.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344553-713791-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344553" class="wp-caption-text">第一名黄羽彤。（苗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344555-713793.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344555-713793-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344555" class="wp-caption-text">参赛者大展才艺。（苗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344554-713792.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344554-713792-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344554" class="wp-caption-text">参赛者大展才艺。（苗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14344556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14344556" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344556-713794.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14344556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14344556-713794-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14344556" class="wp-caption-text">参赛者大展才艺（苗市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苗栗市长余文忠表示，唱歌是一种可以纾发心情的管道，许多银发族更都表示过，透过歌曲演绎更能让心情保持愉悦，也是有助长寿的秘诀之一，所以歌唱大赛的举办就是想让乡亲都能在歌曲弦律中获得人生中的勇气与力量。</p>
