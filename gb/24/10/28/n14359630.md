<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“菊月咏筝”传承古筝之美 “水底鱼”鼓乐助兴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359625-DSC08295-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在10月26日晚的“菊月咏筝”音乐会中，杨信宜老师演奏古筝。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2024年10月29日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿勒星顿镇报导）10月26日晚，波士顿古筝乐团举办了主题为“菊月咏筝”的音乐会，由乐团创办人杨信宜领衔，带领学生胡巧颖、胡凯楹和李泽恩同台演出，通过筝乐向观众传递中国音乐的深情与细腻。“水底鱼打击乐团”亦作为演出嘉宾，热情献上鼓乐演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14359619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359619" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359619-DJY00354.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359619-DJY00354-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359619" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）胡凯楹、胡巧颖、李泽恩、杨信宜演奏古筝乐曲。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14359620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359620" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359620-DJY00375.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359620-DJY00375-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359620" class="wp-caption-text">水底鱼打击乐团作为演出嘉宾献上鼓乐表演。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在勒星顿镇（Lexington）的苏格兰仪式共济会博物馆（Scottish Rite Masonic Museum &amp; Library），当晚的演出曲目丰富，既包含传统经典，也有当代创作，得到观众们的热烈掌声。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">音乐会上，杨信宜与她的学生们以潮州筝曲《锦上添花》开场，轻快的旋律为观众带来了如花似锦的意象。随后由杨信宜编曲的《太湖船》，透过多段表演呈现不同角色的乐句变化，李泽恩、胡凯楹及胡巧颖轮番上场，演绎出湖面上渔舟泛影的场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14359626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359626" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359626-DSC08296.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359626-DSC08296-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359626" class="wp-caption-text">胡巧颖演奏古筝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14359627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359627" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359627-DSC08298.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359627-DSC08298-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359627" class="wp-caption-text">胡凯楹演奏古筝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14359628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359628" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359628-DSC08300.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359628-DSC08300-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359628" class="wp-caption-text">李泽恩演奏古筝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">《道情》和《上楼》等作品在不同搭配的演奏下，展现出筝乐多样的曲风层次。《声声慢》带有浓厚的情感表达，将观众带入悠然自得的意境。而充满现代气息的《权力游戏》以古筝重新诠释西方影视配乐，让观众耳目一新，展现出筝乐跨文化的可能性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14359621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359621" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359621-DJY00388.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359621-DJY00388-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359621" class="wp-caption-text">下半场换装后继续演奏古筝。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14359622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359622-DJY00418.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359622-DJY00418-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359622" class="wp-caption-text">“菊月咏筝”音乐会结束，表演者们谢幕。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14359629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359629-DSC08313.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359629-DSC08313-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359629" class="wp-caption-text">在10月26日晚的“菊月咏筝”音乐会中，听众们享受古筝音乐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">特邀演出嘉宾——“波士顿水底鱼打击乐团”在老师陈志新的带领下，演奏了《龙行天下》以及安徽花鼓灯《喜庆锣鼓》。颇具气势的表演，令观众备受感染，爆发雷鸣掌声。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">杨信宜在演出之后感慨，推广古筝音乐的过程不易，每次年度演出之后，都觉得下次可能演不下去了。“每次都觉得难以继续时，总有志同道合的学生和朋友陪伴我，让我感受到支持。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14359624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359624-DJY00433.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359624-DJY00433-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359624" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会后，杨信宜老师与丈夫陈志新老师合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14359623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14359623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359623-DJY00429.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14359623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14359623-DJY00429-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14359623" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿水底鱼打击乐团的成员们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">杨信宜还提到，学生胡巧颖自大学起便跟随她学习，如今已成家立业，连她的孩子李泽恩也在学习筝乐，这样的代际传承让她深感欣慰。◇</p>
