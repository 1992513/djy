<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>113年苗栗县士子开中门活动 鼓励学子奋发向上</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358253-718979-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>士子开中门.（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年10月26日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）县府为发扬客家地区耕读传家精神，10月26日上午在苗栗市文昌祠举办2024士子开中门活动，10位博士及20位高考及格的士子披红挂彩，循古礼“开中门”祭圣，感受光耀门楣的至高荣誉，并藉以鼓励苗栗子弟见贤思齐，奋发向上，出人头地。</p>
<p>苗栗市文昌祠至今保持“开中门”的传统习俗，县府为奖掖文风，从民国96年起办理士子开中门活动，获得热烈回响，至今已迈入第18年，是全台唯一由官方举办的开中门祭圣活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358261-718987.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358261-718987-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358261" class="wp-caption-text">颁发奖牌、奖金（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14358260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358260-718986.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358260-718986-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358260" class="wp-caption-text">颁发士子奖状和奖金.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年开中门的10位博士：彭松能、周竹一、刘昌邑、张月珍、黄恺文、陈佑杰、杨融华、彭慧婷、张元昆、魏廷秝。</p>
<p>20位高考及格士子：钟孟琦、梁丹宁、刘劼政、杨玉晨、伍书萱、钟佳妤、郭圣平、徐荣均、谢佩欣、赖晓君、李燕珊、张碧蓉、冯曼玲、陈怡帆、何筱薇、何欣怡、曾立君、陈功业、刘俊义、王伟伦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358257-718983.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358257-718983-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358257" class="wp-caption-text">64岁取得博士学位的彭松能通过中门.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中，64岁彭松能4年前进入国立台北科大博士班，经过4年半时间取得博士学位，由于县府举办的开中门活动名额有限且须现场报名，他在开始报名前1天带着睡袋睡在县府第2办公大楼大厅抢得“头香”，是第1位完成报名的士子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358258-718984.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358258-718984-600x520.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="520" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358258" class="wp-caption-text">士子通过中门（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14358259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358259-718985.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358259-718985-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358259" class="wp-caption-text">士子通过中门（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>客家戏曲剧团乐师张元昆曾担任荣兴客家采茶剧团、复兴京剧团、国光剧团乐师，取得中央大学中文系戏曲组博士，西湖乡侨文国小校长及3位国小老师亦皆取得博士。年轻士子曾立君、刘俊义年仅22岁即高考及格，也有9位在县府服务的高考及格士子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358256-718982.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358256-718982-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358256" class="wp-caption-text">士子献酒祭圣（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14358255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358255-718981.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358255-718981-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358255" class="wp-caption-text">士子们披红挂彩（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天的开中门活动，县长钟东锦、行政院中部联合服务中心副执行长李贵富、县议员杨明烨、徐筱菁、许樱萍、曾美露、禹耀东、市长余文忠、县府民政处长巫恒召、副处长林国竹、宗教礼俗科长赖木山、联大校长、仁德医专校长、育达科大院长等人皆到场致意，并为士子们披挂彩带，一同合影留念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358254-718980.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358254-718980-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358254" class="wp-caption-text">县长上香祈福.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦期许士子们继续充实自己，贡献社会、国家，同时欢迎士子们返乡服务，回馈乡里、建设家乡。</p>
<p>在开中门仪式中，30位士子披红挂彩，在钟鼓声中，依序从中门进入文昌祠，循古礼进行三献礼祭圣，仪式庄严肃穆，并颁发士子奖牌、奖金，士子的家人、朋友都到场观礼，分享荣耀。</p>
<p>由于古时只有状元才享有穿越状元门的殊荣，今年亦特地规划过状元门、拿状元饼的体验活动，让民众亦感受“开中门”的意义，藉以奖掖文风，提升读书风气。</p>
