<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“乐活新屋海客伴你行”享受运动乐趣欣赏海岸风情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358209-718947-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园副市长王明钜参加“113年新屋区全民运动─乐活新屋 海客伴你行”健行活动。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2024年10月26日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义桃园报导）桃园市新屋区公所26日于新屋区永安渔港盛大举办“113（2024）年新屋区全民运动─乐活新屋 海客伴你行”健行活动，吸引了近两近名市民热情参与。活动主题配合新屋区“海客文化”，办理乐活健走、趣味运动及摸彩，让在地居民们不仅能够享受运动的乐趣，更能欣赏新屋区美丽的海岸风情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358210" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358210" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358210-718948.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358210-718948-600x369.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358210" class="wp-caption-text">桃园副市长王明钜抽奖。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园副市长王明钜指出，非常高兴到美丽的新屋区，参加这场盛大的113年新屋区全民运动，“乐活新屋 海客伴你行”健行活动每年都能够吸引到这么多热情的朋友，证明大家对于健康生活的重视。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358211-718949.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358211-718949-600x255.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="255" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358211" class="wp-caption-text">桃园副市长王明钜颁奖给得奖者。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>王明钜说，桃园市政府一直致力于推广全民运动，让乐活不再只是口号，我们希望透过这样的活动，让更多人走出来，享受运动的乐趣。我们也非常重视地方文化的融合，健行活动结合新屋区的海客文化，让大家在运动的同时，也能够更加了解这片土地的历史和特色，未来我们将持续推动更多的活动，让桃园成为一个健康、快乐、活力四射的城市。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358212-718950.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358212-718950-600x318.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358212" class="wp-caption-text">113年新屋区全民运动开跑。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14358213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358213-718951.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358213-718951-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358213" class="wp-caption-text">113年新屋区全民运动热身运动。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>健走活动路线涵盖了新屋区的经典景点，从永安渔港出发，经过笨鲨桥、永安海滨公园、牵罟文化馆等，民众不仅在健走中锻炼身体，还能欣赏壮丽的海景与安海滨公园、牵罟文化馆等。健走中锻炼身体，还能欣赏壮丽的海景与田园风光两相宜，感受新屋区独特的自然魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358214" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358214-718952.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358214-718952-600x346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358214" class="wp-caption-text">113年新屋区全民运动健走活动路线涵盖了新屋区的经典景点。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新屋区长郑诗钿指出，新屋区的全民运动活动每年都在进步，补给物资规划更加精致，活动内容也规划更加精致，活动内容也更具趣味性和挑战性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358215-718953.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358215-718953-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358215" class="wp-caption-text">“113年新屋区全民运动─乐活新屋 海客伴你行”健行活动，吸引了近两近名市民热情参与。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14358217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358217-718955.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358217-718955-600x306.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358217" class="wp-caption-text">活动现场设置的趣味运动摊位也吸引了大量家庭参与。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年主打新屋的海客文化融入民众互动游戏，希望每位参与的朋友都能走进新屋，深入了解新屋区的文化底蕴。感谢市府及各界的支持，期许全民运动能成为促进地方发展的动力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14358216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14358216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358216-718954.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14358216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14358216-718954-600x249.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="249" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14358216" class="wp-caption-text">健走活动路线涵盖了新屋区的经典景点，从永安渔港出发，经过笨鲨桥、永安海滨公园、牵罟文化馆等。（桃园市新屋区公所 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动现场设置的趣味运动摊位也吸引了大量家庭参与，融合了海洋元素的“海景套圈圈”游戏，成为活动的一大亮点。压轴的摸彩更将气氛推向高潮，现场参加者在紧张的抽奖环节中，期待能够抱回精美奖品，活动最后也摸出最受瞩目的头奖—55吋智慧联网液晶电视，画下完美的句点。◇</p>
