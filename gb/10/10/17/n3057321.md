<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：晨(中英对照)</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>系着一个铃铛<br />一步一声响<br />晨，从朝露中来了<br />来了，自东方</p>
<p>自东方，叮当<br />响一声，闪一次光亮<br />叮当，自东方<br />响一声，发一次热量</p>
<p>闪一次光亮<br />群树便一次昂头凝望<br />发一次热量<br />生命便一次跳动震荡</p>
<p>叮当，晨来了<br />叮当，白日的轮子转了<br />铃铛同时伴着响<br />叮当，叮当，叮当……</p>
<p><b>Morning　 Hsu ChiCheng</b></p>
<p>Tied with a ring<br />Which is ring each step<br />Morning comes through the dew<br />From the east</p>
<p>Ding dang, from the east<br />It glitters each time it rings<br />Ding dang, from the east<br />It heats each time it rings</p>
<p>With each glittering <br />Trees look up and gaze<br />With each heating <br />Life leaps and bounces</p>
<p>Ding dang, morning has come<br />Ding dang, the bright day turns<br />The ring resounds at the same time<br />Ding dang, ding dang, ding dang……</p>
