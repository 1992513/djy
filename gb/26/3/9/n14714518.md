<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨声音乐剧社迎马年 演粤曲唱乡音</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714359-DSC09006-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>3月7日，波士顿侨声音乐剧社举办马年春宴。黄宝娥和黄伟健演唱粤曲，茉莉花舞蹈团的孩子们伴舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月09日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）3月7日晚在华埠帝苑酒楼，二胡、扬琴等乐师奏响古典乐曲，演员以沉稳而抑扬顿挫的腔调唱出粤剧戏词。这是波士顿侨声音乐剧社87周年春宴，获赞传承文化、传播乡音。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14714362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714362" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714362-DSC09023.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714362-DSC09023-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714362" class="wp-caption-text">3月7日，波士顿侨声音乐剧社举办马年春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714364" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714364-DSC09041.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714364-DSC09041-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714364" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社资深会员演唱粤曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714369" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714369-DSC09077.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714369-DSC09077-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714369" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社职员们上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚近300位来宾欢聚一堂，共同庆祝黄历新年马年的到来。侨声音乐剧社的资深会员们依次登台，唱响经典粤剧篇目，幕后的乐师们也全程配合演奏。</p>
<p>波士顿茉莉花舞蹈团表演了多个节目，还为粤曲演唱伴舞。席间也有抽奖活动，送出餐厅礼券，气氛非常热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14714351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714351" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714351-DSC08966.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714351-DSC08966-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714351" class="wp-caption-text">茉莉花舞蹈团开场表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714352" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714352-DSC08967.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714352-DSC08967-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714352" class="wp-caption-text">李卢莲馨担任司仪。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714360" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714360-DSC09010.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714360-DSC09010-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714360" class="wp-caption-text">乐师们认真演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>热爱曲艺 传承经典文化</strong></h2>
<p>声情并茂地唱完《醉打金枝》，侨声资深会员雷玉霞感慨：“我们真切希望尽力传承这种艺术形式。我们沉醉、我们沉迷、我们奉献，我们希望继续表演粤剧。”</p>
<p>她感谢昆士高中（Quincy Upper School）和爱默森学院（Emerson College）支持侨声的演出，为他们提供场地。她特别提到，今年2月7日的演出恰逢大雪，但仍得到近300名观众的捧场，这意味着确实有很多人热爱粤剧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14714366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714366" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714366-DSC09063.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714366" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714366-DSC09063-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714366" class="wp-caption-text">演员一曲唱完，观众送上花束。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714367" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714367-DSC09064.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714367-DSC09064-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714367" class="wp-caption-text">雷玉霞演唱之后，她的先生梁永基浪漫地送上玫瑰花。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714353" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714353-DSC08972.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714353-DSC08972-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714353" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社社长黄国威感谢来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714358" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714358-DSC09004.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714358-DSC09004-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714358" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社社长李伍碧香感谢来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周六的春宴由李卢莲馨担任司仪，以侨声乐师的“鼓乐迎新春”开场。侨声音乐剧社社长黄国威、李伍碧香向来宾表达欢迎与感谢。</p>
<p>中华公所主席雷国辉说，侨声音乐社走过87年，每年带来精彩演出，实在是不简单。“侨声一路为我们侨社做了很多贡献，令我们海外侨民听到故乡的声音。侨声过去80多年来也栽培了很多音乐爱好者，让大家彼此有个温暖的地方。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14714354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714354" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714354-DSC08978.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714354-DSC08978-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714354" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉称赞侨声服务侨社87年。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714370-DSC09085.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714370-DSC09085-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714370" class="wp-caption-text">侨声会员们合唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
