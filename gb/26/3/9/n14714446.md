<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世界台湾商会联合总会感谢林佳龙部长亲赴雪梨致贺会务圆满成功</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714469-33632c4ad613f6a646feb71aed2eae08-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世界台湾商会联合总会第32届第二次理监事联席会议，于2026年3月9日在澳洲雪梨国际会议中心（ICC）隆重开幕。图为出席贵宾合影。（夏楚君／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月09日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">世界台湾商会联合总会第</span><span lang="en-US">32</span><span lang="zh-TW">届第二次理监事联席会议，于当地时间</span><span lang="en-US">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US">9</span><span lang="zh-TW">日在澳洲雪梨国际会议中心（</span><span lang="en-US">ICC</span><span lang="zh-TW">）隆重开幕。外交部长林佳龙此次担任总统特使出访南太平洋友邦吐瓦鲁，于过境雪梨期间特别以录制影片方式向大会致贺，多位澳洲政要出席开幕式，肯定台商在促进台澳经贸与地方发展上的卓越贡献。林佳龙部长提前抵达会议场地，并与国策顾问林见松名誉总会长、世总秘书长游政霖及财务长郑婷文等主要干部，以及雪梨当地侨领合影致意。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14714462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714462-IMG_7565.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714462-IMG_7565-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714462" class="wp-caption-text">世界台湾商会联合总会第32届第二次理监事联席会议，于2026年3月9日在澳洲雪梨国际会议中心（ICC）隆重开幕。图为出席贵宾合影。（夏楚君／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714473-WTCC-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714473-WTCC-2-600x277.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714473" class="wp-caption-text">图左起分别为世界台湾商会联合总会总会长熊强生侨务委员、 新南威尔斯州友台小组主席The Hon. Anthony Roberts, MP、 驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处吴正伟处长、小企业政务次长Hon. Emily Suvaal, MLC、驻澳大利亚台北经济文化代表处代表徐佑典大使合影。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714474-WTCC-3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714474-WTCC-3-600x277.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714474" class="wp-caption-text">贵宾合影。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">林佳龙部长此次奉派担任赖清德总统特使，在前往吐瓦鲁途中过境雪梨。在紧凑的行程中，林部长仍特别在雪梨歌剧院前录制影片祝福世界台湾商会联合总会第</span><span lang="en-US">32</span><span lang="zh-TW">届第二次理监事会议圆满成功，这不仅是台湾外交部长首度以录影方式参与海外台商活动，更展现政府对全球海外台商的高度重视，为经济外交增添实质互动。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14714484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714484" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714484-ac4fa7ed3c5ed3c813fb5425c2b289d7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714484-ac4fa7ed3c5ed3c813fb5425c2b289d7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714484" class="wp-caption-text">中华民国外交部长林佳龙在雪梨歌剧院前录制影片，祝福世界台湾商会联合总会第32届第二次理监事会议圆满成功。（夏楚君／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">当天开幕式冠盖云集，展现台澳紧密关系。澳方出席政要包括新南威尔斯州（</span><span lang="en-US">NSW</span><span lang="zh-TW">）贸易及中小企业副厅长苏瓦尔（</span><span lang="en-US">Emily Suvaal</span><span lang="zh-TW">，代表州长柯民思）、新州议会友台小组主席暨影子警政及反恐厅长罗伯兹（</span><span lang="en-US">Anthony Roberts</span><span lang="zh-TW">）下议员等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14714485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714485-Emily.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714485-Emily-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714485" class="wp-caption-text">新南威尔斯州（NSW）贸易及中小企业副厅长苏瓦尔（Emily Suvaal，代表州长柯民思）致词。（夏楚君／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714487-Antony.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714487-Antony-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714487" class="wp-caption-text">新州议会友台小组主席暨影子警政及反恐厅长罗伯兹（Anthony Roberts）下议员致词。（夏楚君／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">台湾方面则有侨务委员会委员长徐佳青、驻澳洲代表处大使徐佑典、立法院副院长江启臣及驻雪梨办事处处长吴正伟等出席，共同见证全球台商领袖齐聚雪梨。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14714491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714491" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714491-55aade0e395cdf98c4b741d1e5dee8cb.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714491-55aade0e395cdf98c4b741d1e5dee8cb-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714491" class="wp-caption-text">中华民国立法院副院长江启臣致词。（夏楚君／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714489-b64ab95c59055f4938c290b16a3a7108.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714489-b64ab95c59055f4938c290b16a3a7108-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714489" class="wp-caption-text">中华民国侨务委员会委员长徐佳青致词。（夏楚君／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14714490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714490-9565de293d58c11b5ab95625fdea2ad7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714490-9565de293d58c11b5ab95625fdea2ad7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714490" class="wp-caption-text">驻澳洲代表处大使徐佑典致词。（夏楚君／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">总会长熊强生在致词中指出：</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年台湾经济表现令人振奋。受惠于强劲的出口表现，全年</span><span lang="en-US">GDP</span><span lang="zh-TW">成长率已超过</span><span lang="en-US">8.5%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，位居全球领先地位；人均</span><span lang="en-US">GDP</span><span lang="zh-TW">更突破日、韩，成为亚洲高收入经济体的新标竿。他进一步提到，台湾股市总值已超越</span><span lang="en-US">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">兆美元，名列全球第八，充分展现台湾企业在创新实力与市场信心的厚实基础。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14714488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14714488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714488-1125cab65f515caf1ee567fcb467b529.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14714488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14714488-1125cab65f515caf1ee567fcb467b529-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14714488" class="wp-caption-text">世界台湾商会联合总会总会长熊强生侨务委员致词。（夏楚君／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">熊强生表示，在全球供应链重组的趋势下，台湾凭借稳健的科技实力与民主价值，已成为全球不可或缺的重要节点。特别是在人工智慧（</span><span lang="en-US">AI</span><span lang="zh-TW">）发展的核心领域，台湾半导体与服务器产业的领导地位，使其成为全球</span><span lang="en-US">AI</span><span lang="zh-TW">基础建设的中心。他吁请全球台商掌握此绝佳时机，结合各国产业特色，开创台商的新世代优势。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">面对全球局势变动，熊强生强调，台商应团结一致坚守信念，与民主伙伴国家并肩维护和平与繁荣。他同时欣见欧洲、非洲及大洋洲区的新任总会长展现年轻世代的活力，期盼更多年轻台商加入，肩负传承与创新的任务。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">世界台湾商会联合总会成立于</span><span lang="en-US">1994</span><span lang="zh-TW">年，由分布于北美、亚洲、欧洲、非洲、中南美及大洋洲等六大洲的台商组织组成。该组织长期扮演海外台商与台湾之间的桥梁，并深耕当地主流社会，是全球最具影响力的台商民间团体之一。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="zh-TW">（世界台湾商会联合总会供稿）</span></span></p>
