<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>钻石舞台之夜 余天、黄西田首日登北流献艺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724327-2603212212311487.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
