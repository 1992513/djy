<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿华埠独一无二 余氏春宴龙飞狮舞</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725571-DSC09997-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙余风采堂3月21日举办马年春宴。华林派舞龙贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月24日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）金龙飞舞摇曳，彩狮欢贺满堂，这是纽英崙余风采堂的春宴开场，在波士顿华埠今年的各大春宴中，可谓是独特的胜景。</p>

<p>3月21日，余风采堂在华埠帝苑酒楼举办马年春宴，邀公所会员及亲朋好友总计300多人欢聚一堂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14725555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725555-DSC00048.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725555-DSC00048-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725555" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂3月21日举办马年春宴。嘉宾及舞龙、舞狮团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14725560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725560" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725560-DSC00114.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725560-DSC00114-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725560" class="wp-caption-text">300多人参加春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的余氏春宴，仍延续新冠疫情之后的习惯，聚集了纽英崙当地宗亲，以及旧金山和纽约余风采堂的代表，但并未邀请政要嘉宾或其它侨社团体。</p>
<p>宴会开始，余翠梅带领的华林派武术学员首先登场舞龙。金龙在锣鼓和呐喊声中飘逸飞舞。当它盘旋歇息时，武术学员依次登场，表演短刀、拳脚、对抗等武术技巧，赢得热烈喝采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14725552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725552-DSC00009.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725552-DSC00009-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725552" class="wp-caption-text">华林派舞龙贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14725553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725553-DSC00011.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725553-DSC00011-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725553" class="wp-caption-text">华林派学员表演武术。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14725569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725569-DSC09990.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725569-DSC09990-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725569" class="wp-caption-text">华林派学员擂鼓敲锣。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金龙离场后，余南狮醒狮团的6头彩狮跳跃进厅，翻腾献瑞，深入宴席中向每一桌来宾送上祝福，还抛洒生菜和橘子，祝愿大家生财、吉祥。</p>
<p>接下来，余氏妇女组成员献唱“欢乐年年”和“甜蜜蜜”，还表演舞蹈节目，迎来掌声与喝采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14725554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725554-DSC00037.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725554-DSC00037-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725554" class="wp-caption-text">余南狮醒狮团舞狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14725556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725556" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725556-DSC00057.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725556-DSC00057-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725556" class="wp-caption-text">余氏妇女组成员演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><strong>宗亲欢乐贺年 父子主席自豪</strong></h2>
<p>这场晚会由余绍强和余艳玲主持。纽英崙余风采堂主席余雅伦首先上台，用中、英文欢迎来宾，祝福大家“马年行大运”、“年年有余”，也期盼余风采堂“蒸蒸日上”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14725570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725570" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725570-DSC09994.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725570-DSC09994-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725570" class="wp-caption-text">余绍强（左）和余艳玲主持晚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14725557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725557-DSC00082.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725557-DSC00082-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725557" class="wp-caption-text">主席余雅伦欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14725564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725564" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725564-DSC00137.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725564-DSC00137-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725564" class="wp-caption-text">余雅伦与资深余氏职员余宝爱合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>余雅伦特别感谢他的父亲余景新，后者也曾任余风采堂主席，目前担任顾问。余雅伦说，父亲当天未能出席春宴，“但如果他今天在这里，他一定会很开心，看到这么多宗亲在这里，这么多嘉宾在这里参加”。他说，他是家族里第二代主席，他父亲为此也非常高兴。</p>
<p>余雅伦还说，每年参加余风采堂春宴，都感觉很荣幸。特别当看到每年都有30多桌来宾，感到宗亲们都非常支持公所，这令他倍觉感激。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14725566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725566" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725566-DSC00180.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725566-DSC00180-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725566" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙及纽约、旧金山余风采堂嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14725565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725565-DSC00142.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725565-DSC00142-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725565" class="wp-caption-text">职员们上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>余风采堂副主席余立昌也祝福大家“马年大吉、龙马精神”。他希望，大家以后继续像今天这样支持公所；公所主席和职员也保持“马不停蹄、勇往直前”的气势，促使公所族务“马到功成”。</p>
<p>余风采堂元老余超群、余文博也分别向来宾送上祝福，鼓励新、老职员继续服务。宴席间，职员们上台敬酒，气氛热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14725563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725563" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725563-DSC00131.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725563-DSC00131-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725563" class="wp-caption-text">副主席余立昌致送新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><strong>外地宗亲道贺 嘉奖优秀子弟</strong></h2>
<p>来自纽约的全美余风采堂总长余应昌，代表总堂职员向纽英崙地区的宗亲送上祝福。他感慨，听到波士顿新一代主席讲粤语的水平这么好，真是很感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14725562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725562-DSC00122.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725562-DSC00122-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725562" class="wp-caption-text">全美余风采堂总长余应昌致送新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14725567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14725567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725567-DSC00214.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14725567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14725567-DSC00214-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14725567" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙余风采堂向优秀余氏子弟颁发奖学金。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚宴期间，纽英崙余风采堂依循传统，向余氏家族中攻读高级学位，以及在大学中成绩优异的，总计5名子弟颁发奖学金。这是余风采堂连续第9年颁发余氏奖学金。</p>
<p>除此之外，余超群元老还特别捐出两份50美元红包，以抽奖形式赠予这5名子弟中的幸运儿。</p>
