<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯入门级房价五年内翻倍</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月12日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者</span><span lang="zh-TW">杨达延</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯入门级房产的价格比五年前贵了一倍多。新数据显示，首次购房者预计需要准备约</span><span lang="en-AU">78</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元买房。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《悉尼晨锋报》报导，房地产集团</span><span lang="en-US">Domain</span><span lang="zh-TW">最新发布的《</span><span lang="en-US">2026</span><span lang="zh-TW">年首次购房者报告》显示，去年第四季度，珀斯平均入门级房产价格从</span><span lang="en-US">2024</span><span lang="zh-TW">年第四季度的约</span><span lang="en-US">63.9</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，涨幅超过</span><span lang="en-US">22%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。而过去五年间，价格涨幅达</span><span lang="en-US">105.8%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，从</span><span lang="en-US">37.9</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元上涨至目前接近</span><span lang="en-US">80</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">达尔文录得次高的同期涨幅，为</span><span lang="en-US">21.5%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，其次是布里斯班（</span><span lang="en-US">20.6%</span><span lang="zh-TW">）和阿德莱德（</span><span lang="en-US">20%</span><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">西澳首府在入门级单元房（</span><span lang="en-US">Unit</span><span lang="zh-TW">）购买价格涨幅上也领跑全国，涨幅达</span><span lang="en-US">25%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>珀斯首次购房者预计需为单元房支付<span lang="en-US">55</span>万澳元，而<span lang="en-US">2024</span>年第四季度的价格约为<span lang="en-US">44</span>万澳元。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">单元房涨幅次高的是布里斯班（</span><span lang="en-US">24%</span><span lang="zh-TW">），其次是达尔文（</span><span lang="en-US">21.4%</span><span lang="zh-TW">）。然而，五年涨幅最高的是阿德莱德，那里的入门级单元房在</span><span lang="en-US">2020</span><span lang="zh-TW">年第四季度约为</span><span lang="en-US">27.5</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，而去年年底为</span><span lang="en-US">50.8</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，涨幅</span><span lang="en-US">84.7%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲房地产数据机构</span><span lang="en-US">Cotality</span><span lang="zh-TW">发布的最新住房可负担性报告</span><span lang="zh-TW">显示</span><span lang="zh-TW">，全国住宅价值与收入比率在</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">9</span><span lang="zh-TW">月达到</span><span lang="en-US">8.2</span><span lang="zh-TW">，而</span><span lang="en-US">20</span><span lang="zh-TW">年平均值为</span><span lang="en-US">6.8</span><span lang="zh-TW">。现在需要</span><span lang="en-US">11</span><span lang="zh-TW">年才能存够</span><span lang="en-US">20%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的首付，且需要用家庭毛收入的</span><span lang="en-US">45%</span><span lang="zh-TW">来偿还新房贷。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Ray White</span><span lang="zh-TW">集团首席经济学家科尼斯比女士（</span><span lang="en-US">Nerida Conisbee</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，房屋拥有率正在下降，尤其是年轻澳大利亚人，可负担性压力是这一趋势的明显驱动因素。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“高企的价格收入比表明进入市场的结构性障碍日益升高。但市场仍在成交</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span><span lang="zh-TW">”她说</span><span lang="zh-TW">，</span><span lang="zh-TW">“价格仍然保持韧性，而首次购房者仍就活跃。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">最新</span><span lang="en-US">Domain</span><span lang="zh-TW">数据显示，西澳</span><span lang="en-US">25</span><span lang="zh-TW">至</span><span lang="en-US">34</span><span lang="zh-TW">岁的平均首次购房夫妇，需要五年零四个月才能为珀斯入门级住宅存够</span><span lang="en-US">20%</span><span lang="zh-TW">首付。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">同一对夫妇为该住宅的房贷还款将占收入的</span><span lang="en-US">42%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>科尼斯比表示，首次购房者通常不会购买中位价住宅。</p>
<p>她说：“这在销售数据中可见。过去十年，全国售价低于<span lang="en-US">75</span>万澳元的房屋数量急剧下降，从<span lang="en-US">2015</span>年的约<span lang="en-US">24.8</span>万套降至<span lang="en-US">2025</span>年的约<span lang="en-US">15.3</span>万套。”</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“入门级独立屋供应大幅收缩。相比之下，售价低于</span><span lang="en-US">75</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的单元房销售表现更稳，从</span><span lang="en-US">2015</span><span lang="zh-TW">年的约</span><span lang="en-US">7.7</span><span lang="zh-TW">万套上升至</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年的接近</span><span lang="en-US">9.6</span><span lang="zh-TW">万套。</span><span lang="zh-TW">”</span></p>
