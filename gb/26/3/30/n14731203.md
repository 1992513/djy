<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>哈佛和MIT台湾学者分享“学术白话文”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731191-DSC00486-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>哈佛大学台湾同学会3月29日在该校生物科技大楼举办“7乘7学术白话文”分享会，以线下和线上方式同时进行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月31日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）哈佛大学台湾同学会3月29日在该校生物科技大楼举办“7乘7学术白话文”活动，邀请哈佛和麻省理工学院（MIT）的7位研究生，以“大家听得懂”的方式分享他们的科研内容。</p>
<p>这场分享会以现场演讲和线上直播方式同时进行。3位就读于哈佛，以及4位就读于MIT的台湾研究生，分别介绍了自己的研究方向和论文内容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731182-DSC00456.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731182-DSC00456-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731182" class="wp-caption-text">“7乘7学术白话文”分享会参与者合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14731192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731192" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731192-DSC00488.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731192-DSC00488-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731192" class="wp-caption-text">主办方向7位主讲人表达感谢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MIT的林宜娴介绍了“用叠层成像重构原子级微结构”，谈到了电子显微镜技术的发展，以及用其观测原子的方法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731183-DSC00458.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731183-DSC00458-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731183" class="wp-caption-text">林宜娴介绍“用叠层成像重构原子级微结构”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来MIT的王子瑞介绍了他的论文，即以台北为例，研究“永续都市更新路径之多目标优化”，谈到了都市更新的方案规划、公共效益、资金来源等方面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731184-DSC00463.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731184-DSC00463-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731184" class="wp-caption-text">王子瑞以台北为例，研究“永续都市更新路径之多目标优化”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈佛的姚俊辰介绍了“人工智慧时代的蛋白质设计革命”，谈到人为设计蛋白质的方法，以及这种技术在治疗疾病、改善生活等方面的潜力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731185-DSC00464.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731185-DSC00464-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731185" class="wp-caption-text">姚俊辰介绍“人工智慧时代的蛋白质设计革命”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后MIT的夏容涵谈到AI技术面临的电力瓶颈，以及潜在的解决方案“GaN功率元件与电力革命”，包括以新材料来降低漏电量，以及用光控设备来提升传输电压。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731186-DSC00471.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731186-DSC00471-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731186" class="wp-caption-text">夏容涵谈到“GaN功率元件与电力革命”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈佛的黄郁婷主要介绍了细胞核的“收纳学”，解释了“三维染色体架构如何影响基因表现”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731187-DSC00476.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731187-DSC00476-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731187" class="wp-caption-text">黄郁婷介绍细胞核的“收纳学”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样来自哈佛的陈曦解释了物理理论“真空的形状：从超对称到不对称”，谈到了世界的对称性、电荷守恒、超弦理论等理论物理课题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731188-DSC00479.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731188-DSC00479-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731188" class="wp-caption-text">陈曦解释“真空的形状：从超对称到不对称”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>压轴上台的MIT博士张浩扬谈到了终极能源“人造太阳”的难题“高熵合金应用于核融合炉壁材料的挑战”，表示人们至今未能做出可长期抵抗辐射损伤的核融合炉壁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731189-DSC00482.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731189-DSC00482-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731189" class="wp-caption-text">张浩扬谈到终极能源“人造太阳”的难题。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这7位主讲人，都尝试以直观或幽默的方式解释深奥的原理和技术，不时引发欢笑。例如张浩扬以充满哲学气息的口吻，讲到几十年来的前仆后继的科研人员，将其比喻为大海中的一朵朵浪花。听讲的同学们纷纷笑言他是“人生导师”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731190-DSC00484.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731190-DSC00484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731190" class="wp-caption-text">听众踊跃提问。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14731181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731181-DSC00453.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731181-DSC00453-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731181" class="wp-caption-text">经文处科技组组长蔡孟勋表示很喜欢“学术白话文”系列活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处科技组组长蔡孟勋也参加了分享会。他说，他到任波士顿以来，只要有时间就都会参加“学术白话文”分享会，非常喜欢这一系列活动，“可以在短短几分钟就了解大家过去几年花了很多时间精力的研究”。蔡孟勋期盼继续促进台美科技交流，也希望同学们在学有所成之后，乐于回台湾就职。◇</p>
