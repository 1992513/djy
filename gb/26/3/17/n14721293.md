<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英崙三益公所 何江黎三姓宗亲欢宴迎春</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721286-DSC09871-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙三益公所3月15日在波士顿华埠举办春宴。公所职员上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月18日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）职员欢乐合唱贺年歌曲，侨社友人欢歌共叙情谊，热闹的气氛充盈在纽英崙三益公所春宴之中。</p>
<p>3月15日，代表何、江、黎三姓宗亲的三益公所在波士顿华埠的帝苑酒楼举办晚宴，与侨社嘉宾总计300余人共贺马年新春。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14721282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721282-DSC09864.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721282-DSC09864-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721282" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙三益公所3月15日在波士顿华埠举办春宴，300多人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14721289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721289-IMG_0337.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721289-IMG_0337-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721289" class="wp-caption-text">职员、嘉宾及舞狮团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14721271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721271" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721271-DSC09787.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721271-DSC09787-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721271" class="wp-caption-text">两位司仪宣布晚宴开始。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“三益作为一个年轻的社团，我们的状态就是富朝气、有活力。”三益公所主席江辉辉说。</p>
<p>他说，过去一年来，三益公所成员积极参加波士顿地区的活动，公所内部也组织多场联谊活动。公所已经成为宗亲联谊的纽带，一年一度的春宴更是宗亲、朋友相聚交流的盛会。</p>
<p>“我们希望通过春宴这个平台，使我们的宗亲和朋友能够欢聚一堂，聊家常、贺新春、聚乡情，结忠义。”江辉辉说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14721274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721274-DSC09814.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721274-DSC09814-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721274" class="wp-caption-text">三益公所主席江辉辉欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14721272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721272" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721272-DSC09789.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721272-DSC09789-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721272" class="wp-caption-text">“财神”进场送祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14721273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721273-DSC09800.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721273-DSC09800-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721273" class="wp-caption-text">华林派醒狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场春宴在喧闹的锣鼓声中开始。华林派武馆的三头彩狮昂首阔步进场，翻腾献瑞，祝福来宾们“万事如意、马到成功、大吉大利”。</p>
<p>在江辉辉用粤语致欢迎词之后，第二位主席黎亦置也用英语向三益宗亲、侨社友人和政要嘉宾表示欢迎。黎亦置说，他在马年出生，如今又到马年，希望这个生肖象征的“活力、强健和坚韧”精神为大家带来成功。</p>
<p>最后他还笑言，要用他唯一说得好的中文词句来表达祝福，“恭喜发财，身体健康，龙马精神，家庭幸福”。来宾们纷纷报以欢呼和掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14721275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721275" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721275-DSC09827.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721275-DSC09827-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721275" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙三益公所主席黎亦置欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14721276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721276-DSC09834.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721276-DSC09834-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721276" class="wp-caption-text">美东三益总公所元老江家安向大家拜年。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚亦有来自纽约的三益宗亲共襄盛举。美东三益总公所元老江家安向波士顿地区的宗亲拜年，并称赞纽英崙三益公所的会务蒸蒸日上，可喜可贺。</p>
<p>纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉也称赞道，三益公所在波士顿成立17年来，一直支持中华公所的活动，团结造福侨社。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14721277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721277-DSC09836.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721277-DSC09836-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721277" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉称赞三益公所宗亲服务侨社。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14721278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721278-DSC09840.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721278-DSC09840-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721278" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员Ed Flynn（左2）向三位主席颁赠褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14721279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14721279" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721279-DSC09845.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14721279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721279-DSC09845-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14721279" class="wp-caption-text">麻州参议员Nick Collins办公室代表颁赠褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）到场，向华埠侨社表达马年新年祝福，也向三益公所颁赠市议会褒奖。麻州参议员柯林斯（Nick Collins）也派代表到场，颁赠州议会褒奖。</p>
<p>在三益公所主席何焯光致答谢词后，公所职员携手上台，合唱贺年歌曲“恭喜恭喜”、“祝福你”。</p>
