<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿北美海产展开幕 商家注重便捷与健康</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720790-DSC09661-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>3月15日，第44届北美海产展（Seafood Expo North America）在波士顿举办，迎来1200多家厂商及2万多名访客。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月17日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）3月15日至17日，北美地区最大型的海产展在波士顿举办，为世界各地的海鲜水产厂商创造扩展商机的平台。参展商对本报谈到，海鲜水产市场的趋势，是消费者日益注重便捷与健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14720808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720808" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720808-DSC09783.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720808-DSC09783-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720808" class="wp-caption-text">第44届北美海产展在波士顿举办，迎来1200多家厂商及2万多名访客。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720796" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720796-DSC09700.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720796-DSC09700-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720796" class="wp-caption-text">厂商代表为买家介绍海鲜水产。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第44届北美海产展（Seafood Expo North America）在波士顿万宁路会展中心（Thomas Menino Convention &amp; Exhibition Center）举办。据主办方公告，今年的展会场地有近25万平方英尺，容纳来自50多个国家的总计1215家公司。在展会官网的参展商名录中，来自中国的公司有178家，香港有3家，台湾有16家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14720791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720791" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720791-DSC09671.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720791-DSC09671-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720791" class="wp-caption-text">厂商代表为买家介绍海鲜水产。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720792-DSC09672.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720792-DSC09672-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720792" class="wp-caption-text">水产加工机器厂商参展。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据主办方“Diversified”公司的副总裁汉斯利（Brenna Hensley）介绍，去年的海产展迎来2万多名海鲜水产从业者，估计今年的展会将有差不多的访客规模。会展中心内还有40多场专题讲座，由业内专家讲解海鲜水产行业的动态。</p>
<p>“我们这里有完整的产业链，有海鲜生产商、加工机器、加工公司、物流公司，在3天时间里，你可以在这一个场地完成生意。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14720605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720605" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720605-DSC09687.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720605-DSC09687-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720605" class="wp-caption-text">海产展中的台湾区。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720806-DSC09758.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720806-DSC09758-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720806" class="wp-caption-text">海产展中的韩国区。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720795-DSC09698.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720795-DSC09698-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720795" class="wp-caption-text">海产展中的日本区。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>便捷易煮是消费趋势</strong></h2>
<p>佛罗里达州大型海鲜批发商BSF 的共同创办人兼副总裁弗利士（Mark Frisch）说，海鲜水产市场的一大趋势，就是消费者越来越追求便捷易煮的产品。</p>
<p>“我觉得买家到了一天很晚的时候才会决定晚餐吃什么，”他说，“如果我们可以提供一种便捷易食、美味、健康又优惠的选项，那么买家就会被它吸引。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14720789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720789" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720789-DSC09657.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720789-DSC09657-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720789" class="wp-caption-text">BSF公司共同创办人兼副总裁Mark Frisch认为水产市场的一大趋势是便捷性。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720793-DSC09678.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720793-DSC09678-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720793" class="wp-caption-text">海产展厂商展出的螃蟹。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一方面，弗利士也说，美国的海鲜市场比较平静，增长缓慢。要鼓励消费者食用更多海鲜，厂商必须有所创新；也要“让海鲜变得更简单”，让顾客在拿起产品包装后，立刻就了解关于产地、口味、价值点等各方面信息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14720797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720797-DSC09703.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720797-DSC09703-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720797" class="wp-caption-text">海产展厂商展出龙虾产品。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720801-DSC09719.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720801-DSC09719-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720801" class="wp-caption-text">海产展厂商展出龙虾和鱼类产品。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>消费者日益重视健康</strong></h2>
<p>华盛顿州海鲜批发商SOGDA公司的副总裁格甚伯格（Robert Gershberg）说，现在的消费者对产品怀有很谨慎的态度。“他们想要野生的，想要自然生长的产品，他们想要比其它类型蛋白质，比如鸡肉、牛肉、猪肉，更健康的替代品。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14720799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720799-DSC09708.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720799-DSC09708-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720799" class="wp-caption-text">SOGDA公司的副总裁Robert Gershberg认为顾客日益重视健康。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720798-DSC09706.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720798-DSC09706-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720798" class="wp-caption-text">海产展厂商展出丰富的海鲜水产。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>格甚伯格说，健康的海鲜，意味着没有添加抗生素，吃自然食物，生长在江河湖海自然环境的水产。“健康意味着有机，意味着野生。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14720800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720800" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720800-DSC09712.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720800-DSC09712-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720800" class="wp-caption-text">海产展厂商展出丰富的海鲜水产。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720805-DSC09749.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720805-DSC09749-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720805" class="wp-caption-text">海产展厂商展出丰富的海鲜水产。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>面临挑战 灵活应变</strong></h2>
<p>弗利士谈到，BSF公司最畅销的产品包括鲑鱼和虾类。这些虾类通常由印度、越南、印尼等东南亚国家进口，因而公司面临关税、航运等方面的挑战。</p>
<p>他说，由于美国贸易政策的改变，过去一年经历了非常不稳定的时期；而应对挑战的关键在于紧跟时事，灵活应变，根据价格、地缘政治等因素迅速调整供应链。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14720807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720807-DSC09768.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720807-DSC09768-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720807" class="wp-caption-text">海产展厂商展出活龙虾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720794-DSC09681.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720794-DSC09681-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720794" class="wp-caption-text">海产展中的中国厂商。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14720803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14720803" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720803-DSC09741.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14720803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14720803-DSC09741-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14720803" class="wp-caption-text">海产展中的印度厂商。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>格甚伯格则谈到，他的公司面临着逐年增长的需求，但野生水产的产量“依赖自然母亲”，“我们从来不会知道我们能生产多少”。为了照顾到每一位客户，公司在分配产品的时候必须谨慎。</p>
