<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】时尚升级 科技满载 2026 Honda HR-V AWD EX-L</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718710-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_28-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 Honda HR-V AWD EX-L。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月14日讯】2026年Honda HR-V，属紧凑形SUV，在2022年北美版推出了第二代设计。Honda HR-V 作为美国首购族与Z世代最受欢迎的SUV，现在将透过时尚的设计升级与强化的标配科技，将其领导地位推向新高度。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d2jsgnf2snq1t5.cloudfront.net/H8m2JUuui0Oy"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/t_OcKxgHDG0?si=BgdYTn5D5NBqRRu-" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>HR-V无论在长阔高都比上一代加大了，长度增加了9.4英寸，轴距则增加了1.7英寸，阔度增加了2.6英寸，最后高度是增加最少的了，只有0.2英寸。新HR-V的引擎舱相对更长，车尾设计也更流线，整体来说外型设计更为简约，就像轮拱及车顶都更为平顺。HR-V平台是Honda的ACE底盘，有接近44%部件是与CR-V共用，而6%与Civic共用。在底盘材料上，使用了大量的铝合金及高钢材物料，可增加车体抗扭性及减轻重量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718621" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718621-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718621-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718621" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Honda HR-V AWD EX-L。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前方宽大的LED大灯和尾灯令 HR-V看起来更宽阔，前方进风口栏栅有点小，而下方栏栅两侧的空气导风口，可将空气引导到前轮周围，从而提高空气流动效率，又可为前刹车降温。HR-V的LX版标配17英寸铅锡灰色铝合金轮圈，而Sport型号会配备18英寸柏林黑色铝合金轮圈。最后，测试的EX-L型号是18英寸机械加工铝合金轮圈。后窗隐私玻璃现已列为全车系标配。Sport 版有亮黑尾门徽章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718623-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_35.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718623-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_35-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718623" class="wp-caption-text">宽大的LED大灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14718683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718683-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_36.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718683-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718683" class="wp-caption-text">进风口栏栅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新HR-V的车厢设计有点类似新Civic，拥有良好视野，有赖于较幼的A柱，另外移到车门的两侧后视镜也减少了盲点。仪表板的设计具有最小的切割线，以减少挡风玻璃反射和视觉干扰，半虚拟的仪表板是一项进步，引擎转速表在左边的7英寸数位萤幕上，可同时显示其他功能，如耗油量，又或圆驱系统的前后轮动力输出等。中间的显示都是在同一萤幕内，而右边的汽车车速则是独立的一个实体仪表，在美国测试车只有mph，不同改变。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718687-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_65.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718687-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_65-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718687" class="wp-caption-text">车厢设计类似Civic。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车厢前方的送风口设计与Civic一样，采用金属蜂巢设计，从乘客车门那边一直延伸到仪表板，同时也成为音响、信息显示和恒温控制区中间建立了一条视觉分界线。内部使用优质物料，开关与旋钮的触感佳，驾驶席门边上的车窗按键都是附有银色边。新9英寸HD触控萤幕，比旧有7英寸增大2英寸，是标准配置，具有无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto兼容性。带有USB充电端口的手枕桥梁设计是手机等小物件的存放地边。后座虽然车门比前门细小很多，但乘坐时尚且舒适，不大感到有压迫感。EX-L版本采用银色方向盘辐条、动态恒温控制旋钮照明、前座脚部照明。可随温度变色的动态恒温控制旋钮，会根据情况发出红、蓝、白灯光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718688" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718688-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_53.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718688" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718688-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_53-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718688" class="wp-caption-text">半虚拟仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>HR-V的标准载货区（后座后面）为增大到24.4立方英尺，地台的离地距仅超过27英寸，使装载大型或重型物品变得更容易。60/40分体式后排座椅靠背可轻松折叠，折叠后跟载货区成为一个平地台，将载物空间扩大到55.1立方英尺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718689-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_54.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718689-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_54-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718689" class="wp-caption-text">9英寸HD触控萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新HR-V使用全新的Honda架构，如第11代Civic一样，在操控和整体各方面都有改进，前部独立悬架采用麦弗逊式支柱，后部刚采用多连杆装置，组件摩擦力更低，在提高乘坐感的同时，可提供更好的驾驶反应和运动感。更长的轴距可加强行驶的稳定性，又可加大乘座空间。USB-C连接埠也配置于车室各处，方便乘客使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718691" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718691-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_62.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718691" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718691-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_62-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718691" class="wp-caption-text">前座空间宽阔。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026 Honda HR-V使用2升直四引擎，可发出158匹马力和138磅英尺扭矩，搭配无段自动变速器（CVT），经过重新设计改善了G-Design和Step-Shift编程，使新变速箱具有更顺畅的反应。排档杆除了有D档行车，也另有S档及L档。装有驾驶模式选择，三种驾驶为标准（Normal）、节能（Eco）与雪地（Snow）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718694" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718694" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718694-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_47.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718694" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718694-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_47-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718694" class="wp-caption-text">2升直四引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>智能控制AWD四驱系统是距加设备，无论入门LX到最高阶的EX-L均可加装，在路面打滑时向后轮输出动力，以便脱困。Hill Descent Control功能，可助HR-V在陡峭的滑坡行驶时更得心应手，而且是标准装备。当开启HDC功能时，系统会在2到12mph时锁定车速，驾驶者可更专注路况，不用分心控制车速。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718695-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_66.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718695-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_66-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718695" class="wp-caption-text">60/40分体式后排座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>总体而言，HR-V不仅在每一区域都有显着进步，引擎动力、车内空间、操控及安全都可看到HR-V的实力。而且，HR-V配备了四驱系统，令它的实用性更高。难怪HR-V能成为了美国首购族与Z世代心目中排名第一的SUV。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14718696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14718696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718696-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_57.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14718696" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14718696-2026_Honda_HRV_AWD_57-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14718696" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
