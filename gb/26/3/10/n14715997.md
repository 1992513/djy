<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>艺联慈善社演武欢歌贺年 盼持续支持公义</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715967-DSC09175-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿艺联慈善社3月9日举办马年春宴。华林派成员表演武术。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月11日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）在波士顿艺联慈善社的马年春宴中，主席发言支持公义，武馆成员演示武技，侨社友人欢乐唱歌，来宾们还为餐桌上的烤乳鸽和其它美食而欣喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14715977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14715977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715977-DSC09235.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14715977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715977-DSC09235-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14715977" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿艺联慈善社3月9日举办马年春宴。约300人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14715963" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14715963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715963-DSC09133.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14715963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715963-DSC09133-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14715963" class="wp-caption-text">华林派醒狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14715962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14715962" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715962-DSC09110.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14715962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715962-DSC09110-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14715962" class="wp-caption-text">政要嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3月9日晚上，艺联慈善社在波士顿华埠帝苑酒楼举办73周年春宴，与侨团成员、友社代表及政要嘉宾总计约300人共同庆祝黄历马年的到来。</p>
<p>这场晚会由黄爱梅主持，以华林派两头彩狮的醒狮贺岁热闹开场。随后，华林武馆的成员们还表演武术技法，拳术、腿法、关刀、短匕、对攻等节目依次登场，赢得来宾们热烈欢呼及喝采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14715965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14715965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715965-DSC09157.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14715965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715965-DSC09157-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14715965" class="wp-caption-text">华林派成员表演武术。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14715966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14715966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715966-DSC09166.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14715966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715966-DSC09166-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14715966" class="wp-caption-text">华林派成员表演武术。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><strong>传承慈善及公义精神</strong></h2>
<p>艺联慈善社主席汤伟雄首先上台欢迎来宾，祝福大家新年一切顺利，最重要的是身体健康。</p>
<p>另一位主席郑慧民除了向嘉宾和奖品赞助商一一表达感谢之外，还自豪地表示，“艺联固有的精神，是不偏不倚，不趋炎附势，不随风摆柳”。</p>
<p>他还回顾道，过去28年来，自己每年都像这样给大家拜年，也希望未来能看到更多年轻的新成员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14715969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14715969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715969-DSC09178.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14715969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715969-DSC09178-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14715969" class="wp-caption-text">黄爱梅主持晚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉感谢艺联长期支持慈善项目，参与公益活动，助力侨社事务，并向大家送上马年新年祝福。</p>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任高家富也到场向大家拜年。廖朝宏称赞，艺联慈善社不仅为侨胞提供联谊的场所，还热心于公益慈善活动，为此特别向其表达感谢。</p>

<h2><strong>欢歌飨宴 抽奖助兴</strong></h2>
<p>这场春宴的一大亮点，是其与众不同的餐点。一些来宾在看到烤乳鸽上桌之后，连声称赞。娱乐节目主唱之一的“唐人街歌王”谭达儿也称赞道：“艺联的餐总是这么好。”</p>

<p>在饮宴的同时，多位嘉宾都上台热情献唱。两位主席汤伟雄、郑慧民与好友们合唱“朋友”、“友谊之光”等经典歌曲。陈仕维、黄伟健等侨团领袖也各自一展歌喉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14715981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14715981" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715981-DSC09294.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14715981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715981-DSC09294-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14715981" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾们合唱歌曲“友谊之光”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14715982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14715982" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715982-DSC09302.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14715982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14715982-DSC09302-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14715982" class="wp-caption-text">艺联慈善社职员上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
