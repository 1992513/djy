<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Sushi Masa重新定义美国“高级感”自助日料</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723000-07-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>限量供应的生鱼片，最好吃的“金枪鱼肚腩”。（图/Sushi Masa提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月20日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）在美国，日本料理正经历一场前所未有的转型。过去，提到“日式自助餐（All Your Can Eat）”，许多人的脑海中总会浮现出加热盘上疲软的炸卷或干巴巴的寿司。然而进入2026年，以Sushi Masa为首的连锁品牌正打破这项刻板印象，将“高效率”与“极致品质”完美结合，引领一场从“吃到饱”跨越到“吃得好”的餐饮运动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14722998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14722998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722998-05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14722998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722998-05-600x750.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="750" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14722998" class="wp-caption-text">Sushi Masa从“吃到饱”跨越到“吃得好”的AYCE。（图/Sushi Masa提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>匠心经营：余森的“踏实”哲学</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14722994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14722994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722994-01.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14722994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722994-01-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14722994" class="wp-caption-text">Sushi Masa企业负责人余森。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Sushi Masa的成功，离不开其灵魂人物——企业负责人余森。从澳洲、英国、爱尔兰到美国，这段横跨大半个地球的历练，造就了他沈稳且积极的经营特质。即便在疫情重创餐饮业的艰难时刻，余森仍以幽默感与伙伴们并肩作战，坚持“一步一脚印”的经营理念。</p>
<p>“我们追求的是极致的性价比，”余森表示。在Sushi Masa，午餐约$20.95、晚餐约$32.95的定价背后，隐藏着对品质的近乎固执的坚持：吃到饱的食材，必须与单点菜单拥有相同的规格。</p>
<h2>供应链的胜利：新鲜度是唯一的标签</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14722996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14722996" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722996-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14722996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722996-03-600x394.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14722996" class="wp-caption-text">Sushi Masa的菜单是一场视觉与味觉的盛宴。（图/Sushi Masa提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14722997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14722997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722997-04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14722997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722997-04-600x751.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="751" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14722997" class="wp-caption-text">即使手握寿司生鱼片讲究色泽与油脂，新鲜。（图/Sushi Masa提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>能让Wismettac、Sysco、Kazy Food等顶级供应商主动寻求合作，靠的是Sushi Masa建立起的信誉。<br />
· <strong>拒绝冷冻</strong>：这里坚持使用新鲜金枪鱼（Fresh Tuna），绝不使用成本低廉六成的冷冻替代品。<br />
· <strong>两天原则</strong>：生鱼片讲究色泽与油脂，一旦超过两天绝对不再上桌。<br />
· <strong>期间限定</strong>：只有在周一至周三供应的“金枪鱼肚腩（Tuna Belly）”，是熟客间心照不宣的新鲜保证。<br />
· <strong>菜单艺术</strong>：当“相机先吃”遇上“Omakase”，Sushi Masa的菜单是一场视觉与味觉的盛宴，完美契合Instagram与TikTok的分享文化。<br />
· <strong>明星单品</strong>：畅销的“金枪鱼库拉卡（Tuna Kuraka）”以炸海苔为底，层叠辣金枪鱼与牛油果，口感丰富；而“??鱼（Yellowtail）”则选用最鲜嫩的脖子肉，满足老饕的挑剔味蕾。<br />
· <strong>板前等级体验</strong>：提供现点现做的手卷，确保紫菜脆度，并在生鱼片中加入黑松露油或鱼子酱，提升层次感。<br />
· <strong>标准化美味</strong>：全美分店菜单统一率达95%，连酱汁都经秤重计量，确保您不论是在德州、路易斯安那州还是佛罗里达州，都能品尝到同样的惊艳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14722999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14722999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722999-06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14722999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722999-06-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14722999" class="wp-caption-text">拒绝冷冻，坚持使用新鲜金枪鱼（Fresh Tuna）。（图/Sushi Masa提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14723001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723001-08.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723001-08-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723001" class="wp-caption-text">现点现做的菜单。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>欢乐氛围：不只是餐厅，更是社交场域</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14722995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14722995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722995-02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14722995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14722995-02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14722995" class="wp-caption-text">走进Sushi Masa，现代时尚的酒吧与热闹的氛围交织。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走进Sushi Masa，现代时尚的酒吧与热闹的氛围交织。这里实行“打私场”小费制，每位服务员仅负责五张桌子，确保服务细致。</p>
<p>最令人印象深刻的，莫过于庆生时刻。工作人员会高举闪烁的LED祝贺牌，伴随欢快的歌声出场，将用餐气氛推向最高潮。这种充满活力的互动，让每一位宾客都能在享受手工握寿司的同时，感受到家一般的温暖与欢乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14723002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723002" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723002-09.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723002-09-600x804.png" alt="" width="600" b="804" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723002" class="wp-caption-text">开放的工作台，品质看得见。（图/Sushi Masa提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>未来展望：引领日料新浪潮</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>目前在德州、路易斯安那州和佛罗里达州拥有12家据点的Sushi Masa，正以严谨的选址策略（锁定人口密度高、多元化社区）持续扩张。在通膨时代，这种能以固定价格享受高品质Sushi的“心理安全感”，使其在各大社群平台均获得极高评价。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14723003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723003-10.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723003-10-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723003" class="wp-caption-text">店内随时可以看到员工开心的工作和互动，整个餐厅给人欢乐轻松愉悦。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在德州驻扎，Sushi Masa证实了只要坚持品质与创意，自助餐也能拥有“Omakase”的灵魂。这不仅是一家餐厅的成功，更是美国日料水平集体提升的缩影。</p>
<p>想要亲自体验这场舌尖革命吗？下次来到德州或佛州，别忘了造访Sushi Masa，体验那份“新鲜、趣味、高品质”的日料精粹。</p>
