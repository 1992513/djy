<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】油电旗舰 尊荣驾乘 2026 Hyundai Palisade Hybrid Calligraphy AWD</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713521-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_28-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 Hyundai Palisade Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月07日讯】现时市场上的汽车，占了大部分是Hybrid汽车，因为它既有汽油引擎的可靠性，又可像电动车般回收能源再用。Hyundai作为韩国车的代表，当然不输给日系汽车，而其最大型SUV的Palisade，也推出了Hybrid版本，Palisade虽只属中型SUV车型，但其三排座却很宽敞。今年测试的是2026年款Palisade Hybrid Calligraphy型号，也是Hybrid版的最高型号。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d124e4v75n9sum.cloudfront.net/W0zMKUXO2GJa"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/8KcuUGqCZjc?si=6c704bArO36_16ez" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>外形上，前脸采用垂直LED日行灯与水平定位灯的独特组合，搭配主动进气格栅，营造出强烈视觉冲击力。A柱更为挺直，勾勒出流畅却坚韧的侧身线条，让整车散发出顶级SUV的权威姿态。搭配高位刹车灯与垂直尾灯，以及标配屋顶行李架，呈现出稳重而实用的冒险风范。同时Hyundai也兼顾了空气力学表现，风阻系数低至0.31 Cd。搭配高位刹车灯与垂直尾灯，以及标配屋顶行李架，呈现出稳重而实用的冒险风范。此外，Palisade Calligraphy也标配21英寸合金轮圈，与Palisade的巨大身驱非常搭配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14713128" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14713128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713128-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14713128" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713128-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14713128" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Hyundai Palisade Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14713129" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14713129" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713129-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_38.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14713129" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713129-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_38-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14713129" class="wp-caption-text">LED日行灯与水平定位灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>引擎方面，所有Palisade汽油版均改为使用3.5公升V6自然吸气引擎，最大马力约287匹，扭矩达260磅英尺，搭配8速自排变速箱。而Palisade Hybrid Calligraphy配以2.5升涡轮直列四缸引擎输出约258匹马力，结合双电动马达，总输出跃升至329匹马力与339磅英尺扭力，前驱版在高速公路的油耗为32英里/加仑，四驱版则是30英里/加仑。HTRAC AWD四驱系统，是Limited版及Calligraphy版的标配，而SEL及SEL Premium均可由前驱版升级到四驱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14713130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14713130" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713130-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_51.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14713130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713130-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_51-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14713130" class="wp-caption-text">2.5升涡轮直列四缸引擎配电马达。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Palisade的车厢内，横向仪表板以柔触材质包裹，营造出宁静奢华的氛围，让长途驾驶变得如居家般惬意。中控台设计灵感源自高端家饰，采用双层结构并融入实体按键，提升操作直觉性。无线充电座不仅支援15瓦快速充电，还配备冷却风扇，避免过热。作为最高版本，Palisade Calligraphy配以高级Nappa皮革座椅（加热/通风）为主，加上Bose 14喇叭环绕音响、气氛灯、电动天窗、115伏电源插座与加热方向盘，奢华不失实用。标配双12.3英寸曲面萤幕整合仪表与导航，画面细腻、反应迅捷。前排与第二排座椅升级为Relaxation放松模式，可大幅倾斜并配备腿靠，长途旅行时宛如头等舱般舒适。第三排座椅则是全新亮点：支援电动折叠、滑动、加热与电动调整功能，空间灵活性大幅提升，让七人或八人家庭出游不再拥挤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14713131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14713131" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713131-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_69.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14713131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713131-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_69-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14713131" class="wp-caption-text">横向仪表板以柔触材质包裹。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14713132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14713132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713132-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_54.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14713132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713132-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_54-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14713132" class="wp-caption-text">虚拟仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，每排座椅皆配备100瓦USB-C充电孔与杯架，确保电子设备随时满电。第二代数位钥匙支援手机或手表取代传统钥匙，甚至可共享给家人，提升使用弹性。车载Wi-Fi热点与Alexa语音助理，让导航与娱乐随声唤醒；OTA无线更新更能远端升级系统，新增功能无需回厂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14713134" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14713134" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713134-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_55.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14713134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713134-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_55-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14713134" class="wp-caption-text">中控台萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>安全科技同样领先同级，双镜头行车记录器可同时录制行驶与停车画面，支援感测器触发自动储存，避免遗漏关键时刻。十个安全气囊全面守护，第三排预缩式安全带与限力装置则专为儿童设计，提供额外保护。UV-C消毒舱藏于中控储物空间，能快速杀菌个人物品，特别适合疫情后的卫生需求，而且在打开储物空间时，会自动关闭UV-C清毒，十分安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14713136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14713136" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713136-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_61.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14713136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713136-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_61-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14713136" class="wp-caption-text">UV-C清毒。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>天窗分为前后两部分，前天窗只是让前座乘客享用，是可以开启的；后座天窗尺寸更大，有电动太阳挡，但是窗户本身并不能开启。各有好处，最重要是可让车厢更光亮。当夜幕低垂时，Palisade Calligraphy装有64色气氛灯，在车门前台各部分，微微渗透出不同色彩的灯光，可营造不同的气氛，而且不会太亮而影响驾驶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14713137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14713137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713137-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_67.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14713137" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14713137-2026_Hyundai_Palisade_Hybrid_67-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14713137" class="wp-caption-text">中间后视镜萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Palisade Hybrid Calligraphy作为中型SUV，空间有如大型SUV一样，乘客会很舒适。而且，内在的科技也不少，如仪表板上的两侧后视影像、UV-C清毒箱、中间虚拟后视镜等，再加上三种按摩座椅，是令Hyundai Palisade Hybrid Calligraphy比对手走得更前。Hybrid版的2.5升引擎配合电动马达，动力扎实，要加速时力量充沛，轻柔加速又可只用马达，减少油耗。此车最令人印象深刻是车内有极多的USB端口，坐满了也可每人一USB端口为手机或平板电脑充电。最重要的，最高版的Hyundai Palisade Hybrid Calligraphy售价只由$56,780起，非常实惠。◇</p>
