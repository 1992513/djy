<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>朱柏康被指撞脸霍元甲 网友：真的有轮回转世</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710595-2501111045221487.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>【大纪元2026年03月04日讯】（大纪元记者钟又淳报导）现年43岁的香港艺人朱柏康（又名：朱康），曾凭电影《破．地狱》夺得金像奖最佳男配角奖。再度备受关注的他，日前获一名网友在社交平台发文，并分享他与霍元甲的对比照。两人的相似度令人惊讶，网友直呼“原来真的有轮回转世”。</p><p>日前有网友在threads上以“我以为霍元甲张相系朱康嚟（我以为照片上的霍元甲是朱康）”为题发文，分享朱柏康与已故武术宗师霍元甲的照片作对比。两人除了五官相似，连表情都一模一样，引发关注和热议。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710569-zhu-baikang.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14710569-zhu-baikang.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710569-zhu-baikang.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14710569-zhu-baikang.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="香港艺人朱柏康被指撞脸已故武术宗师霍元甲。（threads截图）" width="450" b="355"/></picture></a><figcaption>香港艺人朱柏康被指撞脸已故武术宗师霍元甲。（threads截图）</figcaption></figure></div><p>发帖的网友称，朱柏康与霍元甲的神态和五官相似度至少有百分之八十，但其他网友在留言区带点反驳的意思称“根本同一人”、认为朱柏康简直是“霍元甲转世”。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p>还有影迷搞笑建议，朱柏康可以考虑接拍关于霍元甲的传记片，并预期会达到“睇到返唔到转头（看了朱柏康拍的这部片都不会想看之前其他人演的霍元甲的传记片了）”</p><p>责任编辑：杨明
