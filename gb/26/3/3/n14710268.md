<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台元宵节灯会 赖清德邀请民众赏灯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710394-2603030934342378.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />

World馆、千架无人机展演、2公顷玛利欧亲子专区、日本青森睡魔、Team Taiwan大游行、嘉义梦灯区、国际艺文团队演出及高空烟火等，展现文化创意与科技能量。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden"
