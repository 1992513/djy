<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英伦客家乡亲会 美食好礼庆新年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709703-IMG_0052-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2月28日，纽英伦客家乡亲会在侨教中心举办年会兼庆祝新年，近80人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月03日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨牛顿市报导）纽英伦客家乡亲会2月28日在侨教中心举办新年庆典，制作诸多美食款待来宾，更有数十份抽奖礼品，皆大欢喜。</p>
<p>当天中午在牛顿市（Newton）的侨教中心，客家乡亲会会员及嘉宾总计近80人欢聚一堂，回顾去年活动，展望未来发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14709694" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709694" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709694-DSC08106.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709694" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709694-DSC08106-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709694" class="wp-caption-text">纽英伦客家乡亲会在侨教中心举办年会兼庆祝新年，近80人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14709693" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709693" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709693-DSC08098.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709693" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709693-DSC08098-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709693" class="wp-caption-text">会长宋玉琴送上新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14709695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709695-DSC08113.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709695-DSC08113-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709695" class="wp-caption-text">乡亲们享用美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长宋玉琴在送上新年祝福之后，特别提到，去年招募了6位颇有能力的青年理事，期待他们逐渐熟悉会务，帮助客家会恢复以往活动的荣景。</p>
<p>当天的主持人是从罗德岛赶来的邱明慧。她笑言，之前在家乡祭祖，周三才刚从台湾回到美国，主要就是为了赶上客家乡亲会的聚会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14709691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709691" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709691-DSC08095.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709691" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709691-DSC08095-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709691" class="wp-caption-text">邱明慧主持午宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14709692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709692-DSC08097.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709692" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709692-DSC08097-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709692" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员郭竞儒表达新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14709696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709696-DSC08115.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709696" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709696-DSC08115-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709696" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富表达新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨教中心主任高家富、侨务委员郭竞儒也参加了这场餐会，并向来宾们拜年。高家富祝福道，客家乡亲们共聚一堂，像家人一样吃团圆饭，希望未来会务昌隆，大家身体健康。</p>
<p>远在台湾的客家委员会青年委员们，也都录制视频，向纽英崙地区的乡亲致送新年祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14709697" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709697" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709697-DSC08117.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709697" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709697-DSC08117-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709697" class="wp-caption-text">台湾客家委员会青年委员通过视频表达新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14709698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709698-DSC08119.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709698-DSC08119-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709698" class="wp-caption-text">抽奖环节，中奖者欢喜领红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14709699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709699-DSC08120.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709699-DSC08120-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709699" class="wp-caption-text">会员周一男抽中3个奖项，欢乐领奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>乡亲们亲手烹制的美食，向来是客家乡亲会系列活动的一大亮点，这场新年聚餐也不例外。大家享用了14道佳肴，例如前副会长林上田烹饪的万峦猪脚，前会长曾秀梅制作的客家卤笋干，前副会长邱西蔷带来的梅菜丸子等等。会长宋玉琴每介绍一道美食及其厨师，都得到全体来宾的热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14709701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709701-DSC08134.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709701-DSC08134-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709701" class="wp-caption-text">徐满娇抽到当天最大的红包奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14709700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709700" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709700-DSC08127.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709700-DSC08127-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709700" class="wp-caption-text">两位资深会员登记来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14709702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14709702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709702-DSC08138.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14709702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709702-DSC08138-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14709702" class="wp-caption-text">会员们欢乐参加年会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>抽奖环节也非常热闹，乡亲们带来的礼品，加上客家乡亲会准备的红包，总共送出45份礼物。负责主持抽奖的有邱明慧、夏淑曼以及黄文彦这三位年轻新理事。</p>
<p>最大的奖项是80美元红包，由徐满娇抽到。她笑言，自己只要抽中，就是大奖，前几年还曾抽中大奖100美元红包。另一位会员周一男有3张奖券，结果连中3个奖项。乡亲们纷纷向中奖者道贺，气氛喜庆。◇</p>
