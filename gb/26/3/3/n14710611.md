<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>至孝笃亲公所办纽英崙最大春宴 吴弭到贺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710594-DSC08416-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙至孝笃亲公所2月28日举办马年春宴，职员及元老向来宾们敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年03月04日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）2月28日晚上，陈、胡、袁三姓宗亲及政要嘉宾总计800余人齐聚波士顿华埠的帝苑酒楼，参与至孝笃亲公所举办的这场纽英崙地区最大型春宴，热闹喜庆，更得到波士顿市长全家的亲临道贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14710587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14710587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710587-DSC08364.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14710587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710587-DSC08364-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14710587" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙至孝笃亲公所2月28日举办马年春宴，800余人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14710586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14710586" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710586-DSC08359.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14710586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710586-DSC08359-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14710586" class="wp-caption-text">800余人参加春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14710590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14710590" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710590-DSC08398.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14710590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710590-DSC08398-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14710590" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭及市政府、警察局人员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>高朋满座 迎春敬老</strong></h2>
<p>当晚纽英崙至孝笃亲公所开筵80余桌，与宗亲及各界友人庆祝黄历马年的到来。晚会以胡清白鹤派醒狮贺岁开始。波士顿消防局副局长黄瑞瑜亲自上台擂鼓，5头彩狮为所有嘉宾及宗亲送福献瑞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14710576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14710576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710576-DSC08238.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14710576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710576-DSC08238-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14710576" class="wp-caption-text">胡清白鹤派醒狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14710574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14710574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710574-DSC08221.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14710574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710574-DSC08221-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14710574" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿消防局副局长黄瑞瑜（中）擂鼓。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14710577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14710577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710577-DSC08240.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14710577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710577-DSC08240-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14710577" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾及舞狮团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会由陈黄海兰主持。至孝笃亲公所主席陈秀珍首先欢迎大家同享美好时刻，并祝福所有宗亲及各方好友马年顺利。</p>
<p>“今晚的场面非常温馨热闹，有超过830人在座，大堂和偏厅全满，很感谢你们的踊跃支持。”她说。</p>
<p>陈秀珍还说，这场晚宴不仅是新春庆典，也是敬谢长者的晚会。至孝笃亲公所向每一位70岁以上的长者会员派送红包，祝福他们身体健康，笑口常开，万事顺利。</p>

<figure id="attachment_14710578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14710578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710578-DSC08262.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14710578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710578-DSC08262-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14710578" class="wp-caption-text">至孝笃亲公所主席陈秀珍欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>晚宴来宾，除了纽英崙地区的陈、胡、袁宗亲之外，还有当地侨社代表，北美舜裔笃亲总公所嘉宾，以及波士顿市政府、警察局、麻州政府官员等等。不少来宾特别问候年过百岁的前辈阮陈金凤，向她表达敬意。</p>
<p>至孝笃亲公所也向5个公益机构捐赠红包，包括中华总会、中华颐养院、中华耆英会、中华广教学校，以及波士顿青年服务组织（Boston YES）。</p>

<h2><strong>嘉宾如云 市长褒奖</strong></h2>
<p>诸多政要嘉宾莅临宴会，向华人社区表达马年祝福。波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）也携先生Conor及3个孩子出席，热情地与大家交谈、合影。</p>
<p>吴弭感谢至孝笃亲公所在提升城市活力及促进社区团结等方面的贡献，感谢在场的所有公职人员的服务，包括至孝笃亲公所另一位主席、同时担任波士顿警察局社区服务总监的陈孔恩。吴弭也祝福大家，在新一个“火马年”中迸发更强的活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14710588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14710588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710588-DSC08362.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14710588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14710588-DSC08362-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14710588" class="wp-caption-text">市长吴弭感谢至孝笃亲公所会员服务社区。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏特别用粤语祝福大家“马到成功，一马当先”。他感谢至孝笃亲公所长期支持中华民国及经文处，以及在社区中起到改善环境和促进团结的作用。</p>
