<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园彩色海芋季开幕结合棒球文化 花季与职棒赛场双喜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728435-838343-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园彩色海芋季开幕结合棒球文化，花季与职棒赛场双喜临门。（桃园市府农业局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月27日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政27日上午前往大园区，出席“2026桃园彩色海芋季开幕式”。张市长表示，“2026桃园彩色海芋季”自27日起至4月12日于大园溪海休闲农业区盛大举行。今年活动规模显着提升，种植逾6万5,000株彩色海芋，全区花卉总数更突破20万株，整体花海景致缤纷壮丽，呼吁全台乡亲把握未来两周花期前来赏花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728436" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728436-838344.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14728436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728436-838344.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728436" class="wp-caption-text">“2026桃园彩色海芋季”自27日起至4月12日于大园溪海休闲农业区盛大举行。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政指出，今年活动特别结合“乐天桃猿（Rakuten Monkeys）”棒球文化，将应援元素融入装置艺术，展现城市活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728437-838345.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14728437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728437-838345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728437" class="wp-caption-text">设置“大圣花园”。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>适逢中华职棒28日开打，且为乐天桃猿赛季首战，海芋季的展开正好与球赛热潮相呼应，期盼透过跨域盛事为桃园营造“双喜临门”氛围，并预祝乐天桃猿再次卫冕总冠军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728438-838346.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14728438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728438-838346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728438" class="wp-caption-text">乐天女孩。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>农业局长陈冠义表示，今年活动将球场应援氛围延伸至花海，园区不仅有乐天桃猿吉祥物“Rocky”与“猿气小子”惊喜亮相，更设置“大圣花园”“胜利飞船”等主题装置艺术。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728439-838347.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14728439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728439-838347.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728439" class="wp-caption-text">“胜利飞船”。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开幕现场特别邀请职棒球星、乐天女孩、乐天桃猿宣传大使嘎琳共同参与，成功为即将到来的职棒赛季暖身造势，充分展现桃园农业、观光与运动文化跨域结合的城市亮点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728440" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728440-838348.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14728440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728440-838348.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728440" class="wp-caption-text">职棒球星、乐天女孩、乐天桃猿宣传大使嘎琳共同参与。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈冠义指出，活动期间规划“乐天女孩园梦专车”，首发场次27日由桃园好农代言人嘎琳领衔登场，其余活动包含吉祥物见面会及电子集章抽奖等互动体验。民众利用桃园市民卡APP完成电子集章任务，就有机会抽中AirPods Pro 3、乐天桃猿主题摆饰及纪念棒球等精美好礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728441" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728441-838349.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14728441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728441-838349.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728441" class="wp-caption-text">植逾6万5,000株彩色海芋。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>更多关于“2026桃园彩色海芋季”的活动详情，请至官方脸书粉丝专页<ahref="https://reurl.cc/Gnm9yv" target="_blank" rel="noopener">https://reurl.cc/Gnm9yv</a>）查询。◇</p>
