<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>内湖红楼郭家古宅山腰上的一段家族记忆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728572-838445-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>正面山头带有仿巴洛克曲线，搭配圆窗与阳台设计，是当时流行的建筑语汇。（Kimball Wen提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月27日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）在台北内湖市街西侧，有一处被树林包围的小丘。沿着坡地往上走，一栋红砖建筑静静伫立，就是内湖人口中的“红楼”，也是郭氏古宅。位置不显眼，但一站上去，视野开阔，环境清静，和周围的城市节奏形成对比。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728565" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728565-838438.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728565-838438-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728565" class="wp-caption-text">庭院洗石子水池与彩瓷装饰（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14728566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728566" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728566-838439.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728566-838439-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728566" class="wp-caption-text">几何与花纹交织的花砖，来自日本进口，色彩至今仍保留当年的风格。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14728567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728567" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728567-838440.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728567-838440-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728567" class="wp-caption-text">屋内保留传统神龛配置，木作结构与灯光相互搭配，呈现当时家族生活与信仰的样貌。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>日治时期的兴建背景</h2>
<p>这栋宅邸约建于公元1919年，大正年间。当时台湾社会逐渐稳定，经济条件改善，地方仕绅开始兴建规模较大的住宅。郭氏家族为内湖首任庄长，选择在此建宅，也象征当时的身份与地位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728568" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728568-838441.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728568-838441-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728568" class="wp-caption-text">整体以红砖砌成，立面简洁，搭配局部装饰与对称设计，呈现日治大正时期的建筑风格。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14728569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728569" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728569-838442.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728569-838442-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728569" class="wp-caption-text">墙面保留通风孔与厚实砖墙结构，展现当时对气候与居住需求的回应。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>建筑样式的时代痕迹</h2>
<p>古宅为砖木构造，外墙使用红砖，搭配洗石子与彩色瓷砖装饰。这些材料在当时相当讲究，也受到官方建筑风格影响。立面造型带有巴洛克曲线，窗型多变，并设有弧形阳台，呈现出大正时期常见的建筑特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728570" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728570-838443.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728570-838443-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728570" class="wp-caption-text">窗户以砖造格栅设计，兼具通风与安全功能，也让立面多了一层节奏感。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14728571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728571" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728571-838444.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728571-838444-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728571" class="wp-caption-text">院内摆放盆栽，空间不大，但能看出当时日常生活的使用痕迹。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>室内结构与生活样貌</h2>
<p>走进屋内，可以看到大量木造结构。楼板由木梁支撑，横梁上还设有灯梁，过去可悬挂灯笼或天公炉。这些细节，不只是建筑工法，也反映出当时的生活方式。空间规划简单，但实用而稳定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14728573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728573" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728573-838446.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728573-838446-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728573" class="wp-caption-text">屋顶山头以洗石子塑出卷草与花饰，带有明显的巴洛克风格，是当时流行的装饰语汇。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14728574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728574" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728574-838447.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728574-838447-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728574" class="wp-caption-text">阳台下方的浮雕，以竹子与鹿为主题，象征吉祥与长寿，也展现当时工艺的细致。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14728575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14728575" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728575-838448.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14728575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14728575-838448-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14728575" class="wp-caption-text">山头中央题有“汾阳”，为郭氏家族的堂号，旁侧刻有年代字样，记录建造时期。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>山头题字的变化</h2>
<p>屋顶山头原本延续巴洛克风格，近年则题上“碧奉宫”字样。曾有人计划将古宅改为寺庙，但最终未实现。这段变化，也让建筑多了一层不同时期的痕迹。</p>
<p>如今的内湖已经不同以往，高楼与车流取代了过去的农村景象。但这栋红楼仍在山腰上静静存在。没有太多修饰，也没有刻意整理，反而更能看出时间留下的样子。</p>
<p>摄影家Kimball Wen的照片，没有刻意强调壮观，而是记录这栋老宅的日常样貌。红砖的色泽、墙面的纹理，以及周围的光影，都让人慢慢看见这栋房子的本来面貌。</p>
<p>对经过的人来说，这里或许只是旧建筑；但对熟悉它的人而言，这是一段还留在原地的记忆。</p>
