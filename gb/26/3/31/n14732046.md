<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>溯源公所三姓宗亲贺年 获总堂主席盛赞</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732037-DSC00723-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙溯源公所3月30日举办马年春宴。旧金山溯源总堂嘉宾与波士顿溯源公所主席及顾问合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年04月01日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）纽英崙溯源公所3月30日举办马年春宴，汇聚雷、方、邝三姓宗亲、友社代表及政要嘉宾总计200多人，更得到旧金山总堂嘉宾的盛情赞誉。</p>
<p>当晚在波士顿华埠帝苑酒楼，来自旧金山的溯源公所总堂主席方国宗，对纽英崙地区宗亲的热情和成就大加赞赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14732035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732035-DSC00706.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732035-DSC00706-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732035" class="wp-caption-text">200多位来宾参加宴席。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14732030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732030-DSC00675.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732030-DSC00675-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732030" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾和舞狮团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他特别向年近九旬的资深元老邝元杰致敬，称赞后者长年为公所尽心尽力。方国宗也赞赏全美溯源堂最年轻的顾问雷展灏从小在公所服务，担任过两届主席，能力出众。方国宗及另一位总堂主席雷奇安为纽英崙地区的这两位宗亲配戴总堂徽章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14732036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732036-DSC00720.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732036-DSC00720-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732036" class="wp-caption-text">溯源总堂主席方国宗（右）为纽英崙溯源公所顾问邝元杰佩戴徽章。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14732034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732034" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732034-DSC00698.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732034-DSC00698-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732034" class="wp-caption-text">溯源公所总堂主席方国宗对纽英崙的宗亲大加赞赏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们纽英崙公所成立虽然时间不算很长（和其它分堂比较），但是一直以来人才济济，大家团结一致，齐心协力，为宗亲谋福祉，为侨社发展、为华人利益做出巨大的贡献。”方国宗说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14732042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732042-DSC00754.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732042-DSC00754-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732042" class="wp-caption-text">职员上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14732043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732043-DSC00759.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732043-DSC00759-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732043" class="wp-caption-text">溯源公所为优秀子弟颁赠奖学金。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他谈到，他与夫人，以及雷奇安主席夫妇，都是第一次参加纽英崙溯源公所的春宴，但当地宗亲的细心照顾、热情款待，令他们感到特别亲切，像回家一样。例如现任中华公所主席的雷国辉和夫人陈秀珍，前一天亲临酒店迎接总堂来宾，带他们参观中华公所，介绍其历史和贡献，与年轻的武术学员交谈，令来宾们印象深刻。</p>
<p>“由此我更加深刻感受到，公所在辉哥的带领下，为侨社所做的贡献有多大，对下一辈的正面影响有多深。”方国宗说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14732033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732033" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732033-DSC00688.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732033-DSC00688-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732033" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉送上新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14732031" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732031" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732031-DSC00678.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732031-DSC00678-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732031" class="wp-caption-text">溯源公所三位主席（左起）邝满雄、雷洪任、雷昌凡致送新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14732038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732038-DSC00729.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732038-DSC00729-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732038" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富致送新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场晚宴由方柳君担任司仪，以美东国术会胡清白鹤派的醒狮贺岁拉开序幕。纽英崙溯源公所主席雷洪任、邝满雄、雷昌凡欢迎来宾，为大家送上马年祝福。</p>
<p>中华公所主席雷国辉是纽英崙溯源公所的创会元老之一。他说，溯源公所1984年复会以来，得到了各位宗亲的大力支持，同时热心助力侨社事务，敬老扶幼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14732029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732029" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732029-DSC00653.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732029-DSC00653-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732029" class="wp-caption-text">胡清白鹤派醒狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14732028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14732028" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732028-DSC00649.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14732028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14732028-DSC00649-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14732028" class="wp-caption-text">方柳君主持晚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
