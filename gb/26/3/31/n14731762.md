<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金山田园古厝“芑丰居”  一段仍在延续的生活</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731774-839268-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>正厅居中，左右护龙延展，院埕开阔。（Kimball Wen提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月31日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬新北市报导）在新北金山一处田园间，红砖古厝静静存在。没有明显标示，也不见观光人潮，只有一弯半月池贴着屋前，水面不大，却映出整座三合院的样子。这里，是李芑丰古厝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731770" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731770" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731770-839264.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731770-839264-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731770" class="wp-caption-text">门楣“芑丰居”匾额，字迹端正厚实，见证李氏家族由清寒起家、逐步兴旺的历史轨迹。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14731786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731786-839279.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731786-839279-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731786" class="wp-caption-text">古厝屋脊上剪黏装饰依旧可见，掩映于树林之间，远方山城与聚落交织出地方的生活纹理。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14731781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731781" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731781-839275.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731781-839275-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731781" class="wp-caption-text">屋檐下的红砖墙与石刻匾额，色彩沉稳内敛，透露出古厝低调而长久的生活痕迹。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>半月池与三合院 仍在运作的格局从远处看，古厝格局完整。正厅居中，左右各有双护龙，形成典型的闽南三合院配置。屋脊燕尾上翘，线条清楚，剪黏装饰仍可辨识，显示当年李家的地位与家势。屋前外埕宽阔，半月形水池紧贴宅院。水不深，却稳定存在，将红砖墙、灰瓦屋顶收进水面。后方则是一片竹林，绿意包围整个空间。依传统风水观念，“前有水、后有靠”，这样的配置被视为安稳与兴旺的象征。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731769" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731769" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731769-839263.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731769" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731769-839263-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731769" class="wp-caption-text">半月池畔，一朵红花静静绽放，与老厝相映成景，为古老时光添上一抹柔软的生命气息。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14731773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731773-839267.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731773-839267-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731773" class="wp-caption-text">半月池前，芑丰居三合院静静展开，红砖、石墙与远山相映，依旧维持着昔日的格局与气势。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14731768" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731768" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731768-839262.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731768" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731768-839262-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731768" class="wp-caption-text">燕尾脊上细腻的剪黏工艺，历经百年风雨仍屹立，述说家族繁盛与匠师技艺的结晶。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>红砖与石条 写在墙上的时间走近建筑，可以看到明显的材料分层。下半部以石条堆砌为基础，上半部则是红砖砌成的“斗子墙”，红与灰交错，是台湾早期红砖大厝常见的形式。墙面已有风化，局部出现裂纹与补痕，但整体结构仍稳固。窗框为水泥雕饰，外侧加上铁窗，铁锈斑驳却未被更换。门楣上题着“兴丰居”，字体端正，略显岁月痕迹；另一侧可见“居之安”的题字，简单却直接。正厅门前红联仍在，“书香名第”、“福泽善人”，字迹清楚。门是关着的，但整体氛围不显荒废，反而像是屋内仍有人出入，只是此刻不在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731778" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731778-839272.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731778-839272-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731778" class="wp-caption-text">红门紧扣，门联“书香名宅、人居福泽”字迹鲜明，与斑驳石墙并存，呈现古厝在时间中仍保留的气度。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14731780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731780" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731780-839274.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731780-839274-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731780" class="wp-caption-text">正面门面对称端正，石窗、红门与匾额构成完整格局，仍可见当年家族讲究的建筑秩序。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14731771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731771" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731771-839265.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731771-839265-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731771" class="wp-caption-text">正厅内悬挂香炉，香烟袅袅，承载祖先记忆与世代传承。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>院子里的日常 没有被拿走古厝的生活感，藏在细节里。院子里的果树照常结果。一颗柑橘挂在枝头，叶影摇动，旁边还有未熟的果实，静静垂着。这些不是刻意种来观赏，而是长年存在的日常。角落里，一颗石臼躺在地面，表面长满青苔。这样的器具，曾经是家户必备，如今少见，但在这里仍被保留下来。屋侧的小石屋，以不规则石块堆砌，门口堆放木板、渔网与工具，显示空间仍在使用，而不是单纯保存。屋顶瓦片层层排列，有些已松动，边角出现裂痕。燕尾脊的装饰颜色褪去，但形仍在。远方山坡与零散聚落隐约可见，电线横过天空，这里不是停在过去，而是持续与现在共存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731772" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731772" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731772-839266.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731772-839266-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731772" class="wp-caption-text">枝叶间一颗橘子静静成熟，带着微微斑点，像时间留下的印记。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>从渡海来台 到一方富户的兴起根据地方记录，李家先祖李布公于清嘉庆七年（1802年）自福建漳州渡海来台，落脚于金包里堡清水庄。当年仅二十七岁，从苦工起家，长年勤俭，逐步累积财富。1810年兴建祖厝，1817年获皇恩赐封“汝颜进士”，随后返回漳州迎妻徐氏与子来台定居。</p>
<p>父子共同经营，开垦土地、置产拓业，逐渐成为地方上具影响力的家族之一。李红砖之后育有四子，各掌家业，家族关系和睦，产业持续扩展。当时在金山一带，李家被视为富甲一方，“芑丰居”之名，也象征家族兴盛。随着子孙增加，族人另择邻地兴建新宅，称为“新厝”，逐渐形成今日的聚落样貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14731764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731764-839259.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731764-839259-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731764" class="wp-caption-text">红砖与石条交织的斗子墙，映着半月池与远山。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_14731765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14731765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731765-839260.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14731765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14731765-839260-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14731765" class="wp-caption-text">上方开设石窗，简朴中见工艺，展现早期闽南建筑的实用与美感。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>没有被整理的地方 反而留下真实这座古厝没有刻意修复成展示空间，也没有观光化的设计。墙面有斑驳，瓦片有裂痕，器物堆放在角落，一切都维持在“还在使用”与“慢慢老去”之间。半月池仍在，水还在流。果树还在结果，屋子也还在撑着。这里不需要过多说明。它只是安静地存在，把一段尚未离开的生活，留在眼前。</p>
