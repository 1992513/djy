<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：社区嘉年华热闹登场 墨尔本民众欢聚</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724545-983FEFAE-420E-440C-9593-6ABBF74C28C7_1_201_a-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>3月22日下午，墨尔本东南区克雷顿嘉年华（Clayton Festival）热闹登场，法轮大法展位前人流如织。（Bill Liu／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月22日讯】（大纪元墨尔本记者站采访报导）3月22日下午，位于墨尔本东南区的克雷顿（Clayton）社区中心热闹非凡，一年一度的克雷顿嘉年华（Clayton Festival）如期登场，吸引大量民众趁着好天气前来游玩。活动自中午展开后，人潮逐渐涌现，处处充满了欢声笑语。</p>
<p>主舞台区持续带来音乐与舞蹈表演，节奏明快的演出吸引民众驻足观看，不少人干脆坐在草地上，一边休息、一边欣赏。孩子们则在游戏区玩得不亦乐乎，游乐设施与脸部彩绘摊位前排起小长队，童声童趣笑声不断。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14724426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14724426" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724426-cb44db73509ed849848692501f78df77.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14724426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724426-cb44db73509ed849848692501f78df77-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14724426" class="wp-caption-text">3月22日下午，墨尔本东南区克雷顿嘉年华（Clayton Festival）热闹登场。（Claire Liu／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14724427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14724427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724427-853109cf62419b139434acbd27086aa0.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14724427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724427-853109cf62419b139434acbd27086aa0-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14724427" class="wp-caption-text">3月22日下午，墨尔本东南区克雷顿嘉年华（Clayton Festival）热闹登场。（Claire Liu／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14724428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14724428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724428-adfdc5cf78e3ee410bbaea778a0f4624.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14724428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724428-adfdc5cf78e3ee410bbaea778a0f4624-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14724428" class="wp-caption-text">3月22日下午，墨尔本东南区克雷顿嘉年华（Clayton Festival）热闹登场。图为脸部彩绘摊位前排起长队。（Claire Liu／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>沿着动线排列的餐车与市集摊位同样人气十足，各式异国料理香气四溢，人们边走边吃，成为一种享受。不少家庭与朋友聚在一起聊天用餐，整个场地弥漫着周末特有的悠闲感。</p>
<p>除了娱乐与美食，现场还设有社区展位与互动区，吸引民众纷纷参与，乐在其中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14724438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14724438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724438-ce7f8a634d50fc147905a0a30da40ac3.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14724438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724438-ce7f8a634d50fc147905a0a30da40ac3-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14724438" class="wp-caption-text">3月22日下午，墨尔本东南区克雷顿嘉年华（Clayton Festival）热闹登场。（Claire Liu／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天，墨尔本部分法轮大法学员也在嘉年华中设立展位，向当地民众介绍大法的美好。展位鲜明亮丽的色彩、优雅舒缓的功法展示以及纯正祥和的能量场，吸引了许多游客驻足。现场还有义工派发介绍法轮大法的资料，并为孩子们送上精致的小莲花挂饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14724431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14724431" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724431-670594861472935d8099e76b360fa4fa.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14724431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14724431-670594861472935d8099e76b360fa4fa-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14724431" class="wp-caption-text">3月22日下午，墨尔本东南区克雷顿嘉年华（Clayton Festival）热闹登场，法轮大法展位前人流如织。（Claire Liu／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
