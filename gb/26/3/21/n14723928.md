<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】都会休旅 稳重实用 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross XLE AWD</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723960-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_28-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月21日讯】Toyota的紧凑型SUV的C-HR，由汽油车转为纯电动车，而汽油车的紧凑型SUV位置换上Corolla Cross，上一周才刚测试了2026 Honda HR-V，这周测试Corolla Cross刚好做一个比较。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d2tkl2xkgbxyfv.cloudfront.net/E1cBMVgWYPVw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/F1Tfe_fy478?si=Jo2h2nf37ttMPDVV" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Toyota Corolla Cross在尺寸上，长度为176.1英寸、71.9英寸宽，车高则希64.9英寸，比HR-V的179.8英寸长、72.4英寸宽、63.4英寸高，相差不大，各有千秋。然而，Corolla Cross由于车身高，看起来个头更大，风格也较稳重；HR-V则像轿车，也更为运动化。Corolla Cross更接近RAV4的方型设计，载客容量为93.8立方英尺，而载货区则有21.5立方英尺。Honda HR-V由于更长及更宽，载客及载货容量分别为98.7及24.4立方英尺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14723931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723931-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723931-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723931" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Toyota Corolla Cross XLE AWD。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Corolla Cross的外形是很Toyota的设计，前脸采用宽阔且粗犷的格栅设计，汽油版与今代RAV4比较接近，而Hybrid版配备现代感十足的同色水箱护罩。XLE版配备深灰色涂装18英寸铝合金轮圈，XSE版则配备黑色涂装18英寸铝合金轮圈。部分等级可选新增的“骑兵蓝（Cavalry Blue）”；SE 与 XSE更提供“骑兵蓝”搭配“喷射黑（Jet Black）”车顶的双色设计。测试车则采用“灵魂红（Soul Red）”，极为鲜艳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14723932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723932-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_35.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723932-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_35-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723932" class="wp-caption-text">前脸采用宽阔格栅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14723933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723933-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_36.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723933-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723933" class="wp-caption-text">雾灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在底盘方面，Corolla Cross使用TNGA-C，与 Corolla的一样，三款汽油版均提供FWD或AWD选择，原厂预估综合油耗为32 MPG。Hybrid版则采用再第五代油电系统，标配电子四驱系统（Electronic AWD system），令Hybrid版的四驱系统更简单。Hybrid的动力系统由一副2升直四引擎，再加上一个53kW的永磁同步马达，组合而成，总马力输出达到196匹，由0到60英里／时的加速厂方数据为8秒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14723935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723935-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_37.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723935-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_37-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723935" class="wp-caption-text">18英寸铝合金轮圈。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试的纯汽油版采用2升直四Dynamic Force引擎，马力输出169匹及151磅英尺扭矩，搭载智慧型动态扭力控制系统，可视需求将最高50%的动力传送到后轮，不需四驱时则完全断开以节省燃油。Honda HR-V同样是2升引擎，动力输出较低，马力只有158匹及138磅英尺扭矩。Corolla Cross采用CVTi-S 变速箱，而HR-V也是CVT变速箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14723936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723936-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_49.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723936-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_49-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723936" class="wp-caption-text">2升直四Dynamic Force引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内装部分带来了全新的中控台设计，极大化了置物空间，并在XLE等级新增了时尚的“波托贝洛”(Portobello) 内装色。科技配置更上一层楼，提供全新的10.5英寸多媒体触控萤幕、7英寸多功能显示幕（MID）或12.3英寸全数位仪表板。此外，“寒冷气候套件”（含加热方向盘与加热前座）现已列为 XLE AWD 与油电 XSE 的标配。Corolla Cross提供三种等级：L、LE和XLE，L及LE版标配8英寸触控萤幕、6个扬声器、LED头灯和尾灯以及智慧钥匙。测试的XLE AWD版则标配10.5英寸多媒体触控萤幕，以蓝牙技术接驳手机，让驾驶者可透过车内音响通话或播放手机内的音乐，测试车音响已升级为JBL高级音响及9个扬声器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14723945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723945-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_50.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723945-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_50-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723945" class="wp-caption-text">内装部分带来了全新的中控台设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14723950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723950-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_51.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723950-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_51-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723950" class="wp-caption-text">12.3英寸全数位仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全是Corolla Cross的核心，全车系标配TSS 3.0主动安全科技套件与STAR安全系统，并配置 9 具安全气囊。TSS包括“预警式防护系统附行人侦测（PCS w/PD）”、“全速域动态雷达巡航控制系统（DRCC）”、“车道偏离警示系统附转向辅助（LDA w/SA）”、“智慧型远光灯自动切换（AHB）”、“车道循迹辅助系统（LTA）”、“道路标志辨识辅助（RSA）”及“主动驾驶辅助系统（PDA）”。Toyota STAR 安全系统包含“增强型车辆稳定控制系统（VSC）”、“循迹防滑控制系统（TRAC）”、“防锁死刹车系统（ABS）”、“电子刹车力道分配系统（EBD）”、“刹车辅助系统（BA）”和“智慧停煞科技（SST）”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14723951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14723951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723951-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_52.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14723951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14723951-2026_Toyota_Corolla_Cross_52-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14723951" class="wp-caption-text">10.5英寸多媒体触控萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年式Toyota Corolla Cross既延续了Corolla Cross的精髓，又再加上新涂装及新设备，让Corolla Cross的吸引力不减，虽然试驾时偶有加速力不够强的时候，尤其在上坡时，但大部分时间仍是足够有余的。另外，此车也欠缺了驾驶模式选择，只有手动模式的“M”档，却又没有Paddle Shift。整体来说，应付日常用途是很好的选择。◇</p>
