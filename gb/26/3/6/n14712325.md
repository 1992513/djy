<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美国宗教自由报告：中共严重侵犯宗教自由</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14712335-DSC00248.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
