<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>相隔12年台北开唱 孙燕姿5月中旬唱进大巨蛋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709403-2603020445171487.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
