<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>带着蔬菜香的蛋料理︰义式烘蛋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709448-a7e0d45ed27b534816628308b5f4de4b-e1772451653277.png" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>曾经询问同业的主厨是否有“令人印象深刻的义式料理？”时，得知这道加入了栉瓜、薄荷的煎蛋料理。虽是鸡蛋料理却带着蔬菜、薄荷新鲜气味的的崭新组合。半熟蛋的质地，一切开会流出浓稠的内馅。</p><p><strong>材料（2人份）：</strong></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p>鸡蛋 3颗
起司粉 20g（3又1/3大匙）
洋葱 125g（1/2颗）
栉瓜 1条
薄荷 (叶片) 5g
橄榄油 适量
盐 适量
大蒜蕃茄酱 适量
（可使用蕃茄酱替代）</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p><strong>作法：</strong></p><p>1.将洋葱切成薄片。栉瓜则切成7～8mm宽度的半月形。</p><p>2.平底锅内倒入1/2大匙的橄榄油，并加入洋葱以中火炒香。当洋葱变成焦褐色时，撒入少许盐，就可以先取出备用。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p>接着再加入栉瓜以中火稍微拌炒，与锅内的油份混合均匀后，先取出备用。</p><p>3.在碗内打散蛋液，拌入起司粉。将步骤2.的蔬菜、薄荷叶加入拌匀。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p>4.取一口径稍小的平底锅（直径约14cm），倒入1/2大匙的橄榄油于锅内加热。</p><p>接着倒入步骤3.持续以中火加热，待周围凝固，中心呈现半熟的状态时将蛋皮折半。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p>5.整体呈现焦褐色时，就可以取出盛盘。</p><p>可以沾大蒜蕃茄酱一同享用。</p><p><strong>大蒜蕃茄酱的作法：</strong></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><div><figure style="max-width:300px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709449-9902a8360e3c8c9b99c6d1e77c4c1856.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14709449-9902a8360e3c8c9b99c6d1e77c4c1856.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709449-9902a8360e3c8c9b99c6d1e77c4c1856.png" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14709449-9902a8360e3c8c9b99c6d1e77c4c1856.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="简易版的红酱风味手打意大利面。（晴好出版提供）" width="300" b="212"/></picture></a><figcaption>简易版的红酱风味手打意大利面。（晴好出版提供）</figcaption></figure></div><p>在锅内放入4瓣打碎的大蒜、30g（2又1/2大匙）的橄榄油、300g的整颗蕃茄，以小火煮约30分钟，再以手持式搅拌机或食物调理机打成糊状。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p>煮过的冷冻乌龙面条和大蒜蕃茄酱拌匀，吃起来就像是简易版本的红酱风味手打意大利面。</p><p>（网站专文）</p><p>（本文摘编自《<ahref="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010974099" target="_blank" rel="noopener" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class">世界冠军主厨的宇宙级美味意大利面</a>：独家秘技不失手！义式经典面食×下酒小菜×百搭酱料×佐餐甜点》，晴好出版提供）</p><p>责任编辑：曾佳
