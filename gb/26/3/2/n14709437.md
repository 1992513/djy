<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>浓浓的起司味 美食达人教你做义式炸鸡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709444-ae12bf9936b4dabc007450d392f20680-e1772451705418.png" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>比起一般的炸鸡，光是在面衣内加入起司就吃得到浓浓的义式风味。酥脆的鸡皮不仅好吃，即使冷掉了也好吃的一道料理。</p><p>在欧洲，通常鸡肉料理都是使用鸡胸肉，加热后往往容易变得干柴，因此这边以盐麹腌渍，用酵素的作用让鸡肉更软嫩，再用泰式鱼露增添鲜味。由于没有加入酱油，外观不会变黑也是使用鱼露的好处之一。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p><strong>材料（4人份）：</strong></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p>鸡腿肉 400g
盐麹 40g（满满的2大匙）
泰式鱼露 4g（4/5小匙）
蒜泥 2g（1/2小匙）</p><p><strong>面衣材料：</strong></p><p>太白粉 60g（略少于 1/2杯）
起司粉 20g（3又1/3大匙）
炸油 适量</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p><strong>作法：</strong></p><p>1.将鸡肉切成一口大小，加入盐麹、泰式鱼露、大蒜充分揉捏入味。以保鲜膜密封静置于冰箱至少2个小时以上（可以的话一个晚上）。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p>2.将太白粉、起司粉混合均匀。</p><p>3.将步骤2.加入步骤1.内，使鸡肉整体都均匀裹上面衣。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p>4.在锅中倒入炸油，油温加热至160℃后，再放入步骤3.油炸。</p><p>5.炸至表面呈现褐色时取出，放置约5分钟以余温持续加热鸡肉。</p><p><strong>Point：</strong></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p><strong>1.用盐麹调味鸡肉</strong></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><div><figure style="max-width:300px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709447-87a146e53354059b10b8274fb7d345d2.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14709447-87a146e53354059b10b8274fb7d345d2.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709447-87a146e53354059b10b8274fb7d345d2.png" type="image/jpeg"/><img b="202"/></picture></a><figcaption>用盐麹调味鸡肉。（晴好出版提供）</figcaption></figure></div><p>盐麹的酵素可以分解鸡肉的蛋白质，让鸡肉呈现出乎意外的软嫩口感。为了让酵素彻底发挥作用，至少要放置2个小时以上。</p><p><strong>2.面衣加入太白粉与起司</strong></p><div><figure style="max-width:300px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709446-bbfd57a905fbce95105ce752ce63769f.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14709446-bbfd57a905fbce95105ce752ce63769f.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14709446-bbfd57a905fbce95105ce752ce63769f.png" type="image/jpeg"/><img b="202"/></picture></a><figcaption>面衣加入太白粉与起司。（晴好出版提供）</figcaption></figure></div><p>面衣以太白粉与起司粉3：1的比例混合，再放入调味过的鸡肉。光是起司这一味就可以感受到浓厚的义式风味。</p><p><strong>3.揉捏鸡肉与粉类拌匀</strong></p><div class="hidden srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14709445-6718536a26e7786ce3d17f2e0a552bff.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source
