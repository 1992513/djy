<h2 class="img-caption"><span style="color:#3e7100"><strong>夜尿可能来自哪些西医因素？</strong></span></h2></div><p>西医对“夜尿症”（Nocturia）的原因分类相当明确，主要从肾脏浓缩功能、膀胱功能、心血管代谢、内分泌、药物及年龄因素等角度来解释。以下为常见原因整理：</p><p><strong>．肾脏因素</strong></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p>肾小管浓缩功能下降（老年人常见）、慢性肾脏病、肾性尿崩症等都会使夜间尿量增加。</p><p><strong>．心血管因素</strong></p><p>心衰竭者白天下肢水肿，平躺后水分回流入血液，导致夜间尿量显著增加。高血压与冠心病也会改变肾脏血流。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p><strong>．膀胱与前列腺因素</strong></p><p>膀胱过度活动症、慢性膀胱炎、膀胱顺应性下降，或男性常见的前列腺肥大，都可能导致夜间排尿次数明显偏多。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p><strong>．内分泌与代谢因素</strong></p><p>糖尿病因高血糖产生渗透性利尿，使夜尿明显增加；若夜间抗利尿激素分泌不足，夜尿也更容易发生。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p><strong>．药物与刺激物</strong></p><p>常用于高血压、心衰治疗的利尿剂，以及咖啡因、酒精、某些镇静安眠药等，都会提升排尿频率或增加觉醒次数。</p><p><strong>．年龄因素</strong></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>随年龄增加，肾脏浓缩能力下降、膀胱容量减少、逼尿肌活动增加，使夜尿更常见。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p><strong>．睡眠与情绪</strong></p><p></p><p><b><b>⊙小便清长，量多</b></b></p><p></p><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721475-4@1200x1200.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721475-4%401200x1200.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source <div class="img-caption"><strong>
肾阳虚体质：</strong>若尿液颜色偏淡、每次排尿量明显偏多，同时又容易怕冷、手脚冰凉、腰膝酸软，这类情况多半属于“肾阳虚”。这表示身体的温煦与推动能力下降，水分较难被稳定地留在体内。</div></div><p>中医认为肾的功能由“肾阴”与“肾阳”彼此配合，就像水与火需要平衡。肾阳偏弱时，身体暖气不足，容易出现畏寒、四肢冰冷、后腰无力；若肾阴不足，滋润功能下降，则常见皮肤干燥、皱纹增加、掉发，女性也可能出现更年期不适。</p><p><strong>肾气虚体质：</strong>若除了尿量偏多之外，还常觉得精神差、说话没力气、容易疲倦，排尿时也感觉使不上力，则较偏向“肾气虚”或膀胱气化功能减弱，使膀胱无法顺利收摄尿液。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_5"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_5"></div></div></div><p>中医认为，肾气与肾精是支撑人体生长、发育、生殖与老化历程的重要能量，当肾气逐渐不足时，不只排尿控制力会下降，整体体力、耐力与恢复力也会明显变弱。</p><h4><strong><b>⊙</b>小便频数，量不一定多</strong></h4><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721477-5@1200x1200.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721477-5%401200x1200.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721477-5@1200x1200.png" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721477-5%401200x1200.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="小便频数，量不一定多，可能是肾阴虚或心肾不交体质。（医道心传制作组制图）" width="450" b="300"/></picture></a><figcaption>小便频数，量不一定多，可能是肾阴虚或心肾不交体质。（医道心传制作组制图）</figcaption></figure></div><div class="image-container">
肾阴虚体质：</strong>如果夜间排尿次数明显变多，但每次尿量却不一定多，同时还常觉得口干舌燥、手脚心发热、睡不好、容易作梦，这类情况多半属于“肾阴虚”。</div></div><p>“肾阴虚”在中医来看，这代表身体滋养与降火的功能不足，虚火容易上扰，除影响睡眠，也会让膀胱变得比较敏感，夜尿次数自然增加。</p><p><strong>心肾不交体质：</strong>若夜尿量不一定多，却频频想上厕所，同时还出现心悸、记忆力变差、耳鸣、腰酸等情况，则较可能是“心肾不交”。</p><p>中医所说的“心肾相交”，指的是心与肾在循环、体液与神志调控上彼此互相配合；一旦形成“心肾不交”，心的功能无法顺利下行支持肾脏，肾的调节也无法上济于心，便容易出现心悸、失眠、夜尿频繁等问题。</p><h4><strong><b>⊙</b>夜尿偏多并伴消化不佳</strong></h4><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721478-5@1200x1200-1.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721478-5%401200x1200-1.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721478-5@1200x1200-1.png" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721478-5%401200x1200-1.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="夜尿偏多并伴消化不佳，可能是脾肾两虚体质。（医道心传制作组制图）" width="450" b="300"/></picture></a><figcaption>夜尿偏多并伴消化不佳，可能是脾肾两虚体质。（医道心传制作组制图）</figcaption></figure></div><div class="image-container">
脾肾两虚体质：</strong>若夜尿次数偏多，同时常觉得食欲不好、大便不成形、手脚容易冰冷、整个人特别容易疲倦，这类情况多半属于“脾肾两虚”。</div></div><p>“脾肾两虚”指脾气与肾气同时不足。脾虚时，经常腹痛、身体乏力、大便不成形；肾虚则常见频尿、精力衰减、耳鸣、身体虚弱、手足冰冷。当后天运化的脾与先天蓄能的肾都变弱时，身体制能与储能功能同步下降，容易出现疲惫、消化差与夜间排尿增加等问题。</p><p><strong><b>⊙</b>尿黄赤、有灼热刺痛</strong></p><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721479-5@1200x1200-2.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721479-5%401200x1200-2.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721479-5@1200x1200-2.png" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721479-5%401200x1200-2.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="尿黄赤、有灼热刺痛，可能是下焦湿热体质。（医道心传制作组制图）" width="450" b="300"/></picture></a><figcaption>尿黄赤、有灼热刺痛，可能是下焦湿热体质。（医道心传制作组制图）</figcaption></figure></div><div class="image-container">
下焦湿热体质：</strong>若出现夜尿急且频，尿液颜色偏黄，排尿时伴随灼热刺痛，小便量少且不易排尽，同时下腹感到沉胀不适，这多半属于“下焦湿热”。</div></div><p>“下焦湿热”在中医来看，是指湿邪与热邪一起郁积在膀胱，影响膀胱的气化功能，导致尿液排出变得急、涩、痛，类似西医所说的泌尿道发炎。这种体质常与长期饮食偏辛辣油腻、天气闷热潮湿、情绪压力大、憋尿习惯，或脾肾功能偏弱有关。当湿热卡在下焦，就像排水管被堵住一样，尿液排出不顺，也更容易在半夜被强烈尿意唤醒。若症状反复出现，甚至合并腰痛、尿中混浊或血色，建议及早评估处理，避免湿热久留转伤肾阴。</p><h4><strong><b>⊙</b>夜尿随情绪波动</strong></h4><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721480-6@1200x1200.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721480-6%401200x1200.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721480-6@1200x1200.png" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721480-6%401200x1200.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="夜尿随情绪波动，可能是肝气郁滞体质。（医道心传制作组制图）" width="450" b="300"/></picture></a><figcaption>夜尿随情绪波动，可能是肝气郁滞体质。（医道心传制作组制图）</figcaption></figure></div><div class="image-container">
肝气郁滞体质：</strong>有些人的夜尿会跟情绪变化有关。当压力大、心情容易烦躁时，常会觉得胸胁闷闷的、比较容易生气，夜间尿意也特别明显，这类情况在中医称为“肝气郁滞”。</div></div><p>中医认为“气”是推动身体各项运作的能量，而肝脏正是负责气运行的关键脏腑，同时对情绪变化特别敏感。当人长期处在紧张、压抑或情绪不顺的状态下，肝气容易受阻，气在体内运行不畅，进一步影响膀胱的收放与排尿调节，夜尿自然变得更频繁。这类体质若未适当调整，久而久之还可能影响血液循环，出现“气滞血瘀”的倾向，除了夜尿，也常合并情绪不稳、胃口变差，女性甚至可能出现月经失调。</p><h2><span style="color:#3e7100"><strong>五种实际可行的中医改善方式</strong></span></h2><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721482-7@1200x1200.png" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721482-7%401200x1200.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721482-7@1200x1200.png" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F03%2Fid14721482-7%401200x1200.png&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="五种实际可行的中医改善夜尿方式。（医道心传制作组制图）" width="450" b="300"/></picture></a><figcaption>五种实际可行的中医改善夜尿方式。（医道心传制作组制图）</figcaption></figure></div><div class="image-container">
1. 穴位按摩</strong></div></div><p>肾俞穴、膀胱俞穴、三阴交是改善夜尿的常用穴位。每天一至两次，每次按压一至两分钟，有助温养肾气并稳定膀胱功能。</p><p>肾俞穴：位于第2腰椎棘突下，旁开两指（食指中指）宽。</p><p>膀胱俞穴：下背部、骶骨中上段、第二骶后孔水平位置，脊柱旁开两指宽的位置。</p><p>三阴交穴：内踝尖（脚踝最凸起处）上方约四横指宽，在胫骨后面的凹陷处。
</p><p style="text-align:center"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721483-8@1200x1200.jpg" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-14721483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721483-8@1200x1200-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"/></a></p><div class="image-container">

</p><p style="text-align:center"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721486-10@1200x1200.jpg" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-14721486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14721486-10@1200x1200-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"/></a></p><div class="image-container">
<div class="img-caption"><strong>4. 睡前控水</strong></div></div><p>睡前三小时开始降低饮水量，并避免咖啡、茶、酒精与寒凉水果（例如瓜类、梨等），是减少夜间尿量最直接有效的方法。</p><h4><strong>5. 调息养肾</strong></h4><p>睡前端正坐着，双手搓热覆于腰部，然后缓慢深呼吸十次，有助安定神经并强化肾气。</p><h2><span style="color:#3e7100"><strong>临床医案</strong></span></h2><p>1.
