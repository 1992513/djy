<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东引慢行一圈 风景在转角等你</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726790-837903-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“国之北疆”观景平台面向辽阔海域，站在此处可直视东引最北端的边界景象。（赖小芳提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬马祖报导）马祖东引，不是那种一眼就让人惊呼的地方。它安静、风大、路弯，很多景都藏在你还没准备好的时候。摄影家赖小芳说：“在东引，最美的风景往往出现在你意想不到的转角。”这句话，不是形容，是走过之后才会懂的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726788" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726788-837901.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726788-837901-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726788" class="wp-caption-text">东引海岸公路旁巨石刻着“其介如石”。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726789" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726789-837902.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726789-837902-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726789" class="wp-caption-text">东涌灯塔矗立海崖边，白色塔身在蓝海背景下格外清楚，是东引最具代表性的地标之一。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海风很直　中柱一带的清醒感从码头一路往上，先看到的是中柱一带的海岸。堤防延伸进海，远远一座亭子立在岩石上，没有太多装饰，但线条干净。海水颜色偏冷，风一直吹，站久了会有点冷，但也让人清醒。这里不是热闹景点，却有人愿意停下来看海，静静待一会。再往前走，是聚落与军事地景交错的地方。“忠诚门”几个大字很醒目，旁边是简单的广场与雕塑。背景是贴着山坡的建筑，没有刻意整理的整齐感，反而更像生活本来的样子。东引很多地方都是这样，军事与居民日常混在一起，看久了也就自然。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726791" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726791-837904.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726791-837904-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726791" class="wp-caption-text">傍晚山区雾气缓缓升起，弯曲道路穿过山丘，呈现东引特有的层叠地形。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726792" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726792-837905.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726792-837905-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726792" class="wp-caption-text">中柱一带的堤岸与凉亭构成简单景观，海水与岩岸交会，是旅人常停留的视角。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走到最北端　风把界线吹得很清楚沿着海边走到“国之北疆”，是一段很多人会停下来拍照的地方。平台不大，但面海视野开阔。海风从正面吹过来，没有遮挡。这里不像本岛景点那样设计完整，却保留了边界的感觉。站在这里，你会很清楚知道，这里已经是台湾很北的一角。傍晚时分，天色慢慢转暖。山路弯曲，远方的海上开始有雾气，光线不强，但层次变多。有人说东引的夕阳不一定最亮，但收得很慢，让人愿意多待一点时间。这种时候，不需要特别找景点，随便一个弯，都可能刚好遇到风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726793" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726793-837906.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726793-837906-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726793" class="wp-caption-text">忠诚门前广场与雕塑并存，后方聚落依山而建，呈现军事与生活交织的样貌。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726794" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726794-837907.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726794-837907-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726794" class="wp-caption-text">“铁坑的秘密”主题展入口，透过展示与图像，记录东引过去的历史与海域故事。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726795" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726795-837908.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726795-837908-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726795" class="wp-caption-text">铁坑主题展示区以模型船与老照片呈现历史场景，带出东引与海域之间的故事与记忆。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灯塔很白　风很大　画面很简单东涌灯塔，是很多人来东引一定会走到的地方。白色塔身干净，面海而立，周围没有太多遮蔽物。走上去的阶梯不长，但风会变强。站在灯塔旁边，看海、看天，会有一种很单纯的感觉。没有太多声音，只有风声和海声。这个季节，岛上的南国蓟开始长出花苞。还没完全开，但已经可以看到一点点颜色。路边、山坡都有，有时候不注意就会错过。赖小芳说，现在的天气刚好，不会太热，很适合用走的，把东引绕一圈。这句话很实在，因为这里的风景，本来就不是给匆匆看的人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726796" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726796-837909.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726796-837909-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726796" class="wp-caption-text">窗边熟睡的白猫晒着海风与日光，安静地陪着这座岛屿的慢节奏。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726797" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726797-837910.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726797-837910-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726797" class="wp-caption-text">简单的一角，成为旅人记录东引时光的起点。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726798" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726798-837911.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726798-837911-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726798" class="wp-caption-text">咸味岛合作社木质空间与暖色灯光交织，呈现东引少见的温暖室内风景。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>没有最美　只有一路慢慢累积东引不大，但也不算好走。坡多、风强，没有太多遮荫。但也因为这样，留下来的画面都很干净。没有过度开发，也没有太多商业气息。有人来这里，是为了拍照；有人来，是为了放空；也有人只是想离开城市几天。走一圈之后会发现，东引没有一个“最美”的地方。它的美，是一段一段累积起来的。有时候是灯塔、有时候是海、有时候只是路边的一株植物。真正留下来的，不一定是照片，而是那种走在风里的感觉。这里不需要安排太多行程。慢慢走，就好。因为下一个转角，可能就刚好遇见你想记住的画面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726799" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726799-837912.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726799-837912-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726799" class="wp-caption-text">鲜虾馄饨汤清爽不油，汤头带着海味，是岛上常见的简单美食。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
