<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>跟着镜头慢慢走  林桔春阿里山、奋起湖游记</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726908-837956-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>乐龄团队在樱花树下展开双手合影，笑容灿烂，活力十足。（林桔春提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年03月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾嘉义报导）睽违超过十年，再次走进阿里山。摄影家林桔春说，这趟旅程没有特别安排什么壮阔场面，就是跟着团队，慢慢走、慢慢看，把眼前的景色收进镜头。这样的节奏，反而让人更靠近山。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726899" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726899" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726899-837947.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726899" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726899-837947-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726899" class="wp-caption-text">云雾笼罩下的受镇宫，红瓦飞檐在山林间若隐若现，带着静谧的宗教氛围。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726900" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726900-837948.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726900-837948-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726900" class="wp-caption-text">正面殿宇金碧辉煌，香炉居中，游客入内参拜，晨雾让整体更显庄严。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726901" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726901-837949.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726901-837949-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726901" class="wp-caption-text">木栈桥横跨溪谷，游客漫步其上，感受阿里山林间步道的悠闲节奏。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>山里的桥与旧石构 时间留下的痕迹一座木桥横跨溪谷，桥下是石砌的护岸与干涸溪床。再往里走，是一座带着年代感的石拱桥，青苔沿着石缝生长，像把时间一层层叠上去。这些不是特别被介绍的景点，却很容易让人停下来。林桔春没有多说什么，只是用镜头记下来。在阿里山，很多风景不是“名胜”，而是“刚好被你遇见”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726902" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726902-837950.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726902-837950-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726902" class="wp-caption-text">石拱桥与层层砌石护岸，青苔覆盖其上，呈现岁月留下的痕迹。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726903" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726903-837951.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726903-837951-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726903" class="wp-caption-text">樱花枝条交错延展，淡粉花朵在枝头绽放，点缀春日山林。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>樱花还在 沼平一带的春意这次行程，让人印象最深的还是樱花。林桔春分享，在沼平车站与沼平公园一带，还能看到整片樱花。不是满开到爆的那种，而是刚好，带点稀疏，也带点春天快结束的味道。枝干横向延展，花开在每个分岔，站在树下往上看，整个天空都被淡粉色填满。风吹过时，有些花瓣会掉下来，落在木栈道上，也落在人群之间。这样的画面，很安静。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726904-837952.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726904-837952-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726904" class="wp-caption-text">游客在樱花树下停留欣赏，粉色花海与绿地交错，形成温和的春日景象。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726905" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726905-837953.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726905-837953-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726905" class="wp-caption-text">一株老樱树枝干盘旋，花朵点缀其上，展现自然生长的力道。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>等不到的火车 却还是有收获林桔春说，他在奋起湖车站等火车。“可惜火车要11点30分才到站，所以这次就拍不到阿里山小火车了。”语气里没有太多遗憾，反而像在记录一件日常。后来在沼平车站，还是拍到了森林火车。有时候旅行就是这样，错过一个画面，却在另一个地方补回来。而奋起湖的时间，也没有白等。他和太太在车站前留下合影，“到此一游”，简单但真实。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726906" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726906-837954.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726906-837954-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726906" class="wp-caption-text">草地前一排公仔整齐排列，与后方樱花形成可爱又有趣的对比画面。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726907" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726907-837955.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726907-837955-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726907" class="wp-caption-text">山林薄雾中点缀着稀疏樱花，画面带着淡淡的静谧与诗意。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一群人的旅行 比风景更热闹这趟旅程，是苗栗头份舞蹈协会的自强活动。穿着亮眼的协会成员们，在樱花树下排开，手伸向镜头，笑得很开。有人戴帽子，有人背包，有人站在最前面带头。这样的画面，比风景更有温度。旅游不只是看景点，很多时候，是跟谁一起走。对他们来说，阿里山不只是山，而是一段共同的记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726909-837957.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726909-837957-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726909" class="wp-caption-text">林桔春夫妻在景点前留影，笑容温暖，记录旅途中的幸福时刻。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726910" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726910-837958.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726910-837958-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726910" class="wp-caption-text">游客于樱花步道与游客中心前拍照打卡，人潮与花景交织成热络画面。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>奋起湖的中午 一个便当的味道中午，大家在奋起湖梅谷餐厅吃便当。林桔春特别提到这一段，没有形容菜色，也没有多做描写。但熟悉的人都知道，奋起湖便当就是那样，简单、份量足，吃得饱，也吃得安心。在山里，吃饭本来就不是主角，但却会让整趟旅程更完整。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14726911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726911" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726911-837959.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726911-837959-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726911" class="wp-caption-text">一行人于沼平车站前开心合照，为赏樱行程留下热闹纪念。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726912" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726912-837960.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726912-837960-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726912" class="wp-caption-text">小火车缓缓驶入站区，成为游客镜头中最吸睛的画面。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14726913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14726913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726913-837961.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14726913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/03/id14726913-837961-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14726913" class="wp-caption-text">车厢沿着山林铁道前行，与周边自然景观相互映衬。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再访的意义 不是新，是回来十年后再来阿里山，景色有变，也没变。桥还在，森林还在，铁道还在。人变了，时间也走了。林桔春用镜头，把这次的“再访”记下来。没有刻意追求什么壮观画面，而是把走过的路，一段一段留下。有些地方，不需要每年来。但隔了十年再回来，会知道自己走了多远。</p>
