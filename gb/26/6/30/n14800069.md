<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>万人齐聚西南区 自由节欢庆美国250周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800074-P01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西南管理委员会理事团队一同上台。（陆希／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年07月01日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）由西南管理委员会（Southwest Management District）主办的年度盛事“Liberty Fest 2026自由节”，于6月28日（星期日）下午4点至晚上9点在PlazAmericas Mall盛大举行！由于今年正值美国建国250周年的历史性时刻，活动吸引了来自不同族裔的民众携家带眷、全家出动。虽然德州夏日天气炎热，但现场民众的热情丝毫不减，人潮不断涌入，共同交织出一场融合多元文化、Live巨星演唱、百家摊位与震撼烟火的夏日狂欢庆典。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14800075" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800075" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800075-P02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800075" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800075-P02-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800075" class="wp-caption-text">台商会设摊促进社区交流互助。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>面对德州的酷暑，主办单位展现极大贴心，特别设置了“Cool Zone”户外降温区，并在舞台两侧及儿童区旁停放免费的冷气巴士与风扇。</p>
<p>舞台上的节目则展现了休斯顿引以为傲的多元文化包容性；现场有充满活力的印度舞“Sri Pandam表演艺术团”、墨西哥传统舞蹈等，带来的异国风情舞蹈以及十多只色彩鲜艳喜庆的舞狮队。在锣鼓喧天的热闹气氛中，小舞狮走到台下与民众热烈互动，绚丽的色彩与丰富的文化融合，让现场惊呼连连。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14800076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800076" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800076-P03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800076-P03-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800076" class="wp-caption-text">休斯顿翔凤龙狮会的精彩表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14800078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800078" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800078-P05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800078-P05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800078" class="wp-caption-text">墨西哥传统舞蹈表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14800079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800079" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800079-P06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800079-P06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800079" class="wp-caption-text">Sri Pandam表演艺术团精彩演出。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14800082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800082" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800082-P09.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800082-P09-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800082" class="wp-caption-text">退役军人欢庆美国250周年表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动主席、同时也是商业与经济委员会主席的David Peters在受访时表示：“自由节过去五年来定期举办，初衷就是为了让无法大老远跑到市中心的西南区居民，在本地就能享受同等规格的节庆氛围。”</p>
<p>David Peters指出，今年规划的122个摊位完全爆满，还有许多非营利组织（NGO）进驻以促进社区互助。他强调，这场盛会的核心目的之一正是活络地方经济，透过引入四面八方的人潮，有效协助在地商家提升知名度，带动长远的商业回流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14800081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800081" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800081-P08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800081-P08-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800081" class="wp-caption-text">动感魅力歌星Diana Torres火辣的肚皮舞。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14800083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800083" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800083-P10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800083-P10-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800083" class="wp-caption-text">民众排队和饶舌歌手Paul Wall签名及合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的主舞台星光熠熠。被誉为“冰人”的美国南方饶舌传奇Paul Wall亲临现场，重现白金专辑《The People’s Champ》的经典震撼，民众为了与他合影在拍照区排起长龙；紧接着，由休斯顿在地双语实力派歌手Diana Torres领衔的夏奇拉致敬秀《She Wolf》登场，火辣的肚皮舞配合现场乐团，完美还原拉丁天后的动感魅力，更安排了三个科技机器人与台下大人小孩共舞。在“儿童专区（KidZone）”里，充气城堡、欢乐小火车、脸部彩绘、小丑及魔术表演，同样让孩子们尽情放电、欢乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14800077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800077" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800077-P04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800077" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800077-P04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800077" class="wp-caption-text">活动中民众挥舞国旗欢庆美国250周年。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14800080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14800080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800080-P07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14800080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14800080-P07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14800080" class="wp-caption-text">从舞台往外看人潮不停涌进。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>临近晚间9点，哈理斯县第四区区长Lesley Briones与市议员Edward Pollard登台致词，除了感谢西南管理区、夏普斯敦公民协会（Sharpstown Civic Association）及幕后推手外，也强调了在7月4日独立纪念日前夕，社区团结与自由的珍贵价值。随后，理事Harry Shani用印度语、主席李雄及理事David Peters，林富桂等也一同上台，带领全场用多国语言祝美国生日快乐，并为现场插播了休斯顿警消单位的招募新血宣传。</p>
<p>随着全场“10、9、8……”的倒数声，长达16分钟的压轴天际线烟火秀在9点整准时震撼施放。璀璨的光芒点亮了休斯顿的夜空，甚至让59号公路上的车流都减速观看。现场响起充满情感的爱国歌曲，内容是追忆为国奉献的军人，在感人且绚烂的氛围中，为这场欢庆250周年的盛典画下最完美的句点。</p>
