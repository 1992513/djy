<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【厨娘香Q秀】巧克力优格蛋糕卷</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799950-2017-10-17-59e568d0d43d1-780x438-169.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>现为“巧思厨艺教室”创办人与专任教师的曾美子，极力推广选择天然与无化学添加物的材料，曾经也受教于曾老师的主持人，更是亲自卷蛋糕卷及洒防潮糖粉，共同为全球观众制作自然、健康、美味的巧克力优格蛋糕卷。</p><h2>巧克力优格蛋糕卷24 x28 cm</h2><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><h3>【材料】</h3><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p>蛋黄(3个)60g、苦甜巧克力适量、液体油15g、牛奶30g、香草荚酱适量、蛋白(3个)90g、细砂糖60g、低筋面粉50g</p><h3>【内馅材料】</h3><p>原味优格100g、鲜奶油100g、蜂蜜15g、明胶3g、防潮糖粉适量</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><h3>【作法】</h3><p>1. 蛋黄（天然乳化剂）加入少许香草荚拌匀，再加糖再拌至微白，加入液体油、牛奶、融化的巧克力拌匀。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p>2. 蛋白稍微打发后加糖，打发后再下全部的糖，最后用低速打至硬性发泡。（糖可以安定蛋白）</p><p>3. 将蛋白霜挖一部分放入作法1.搅拌均匀后，加入过筛的低筋面粉拌匀，再将剩下的蛋白霜全部加入拌匀。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p>4. 将面糊倒入烤盘，用刮板将面糊抺平，放入已经预热的烤箱（200度），烤12－15分钟即可。</p><p>5. 烤好的蛋糕轻敲一下后取出烤盘，倒扣在桌上。（盖湿布可防太干燥）</p><p>6. 内馅部分，将鲜奶油加入蜂蜜、优格，将明胶隔水融解后，先取部分鲜奶油内馅加入明胶中，混合均匀后，再倒回内馅中拌匀（避免内馅结块）即可。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>7. 将蛋糕体于结束端以45度斜切，并在蛋糕体上轻画线。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p>8. 放上鲜奶油内馅抺平。</p><p>9. 卷起蛋糕体，放冷藏三十分钟。</p><p>10. 在蛋糕卷上放一白纸，洒上防潮糖粉，空白处即可挤花做变化。</p><p><strong>来宾介绍：</strong></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_5"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_5"></div></div></div><p>曾美子现为“巧思厨艺教室”创办人与专任教师，从小学时期就爱上西点烘焙与料理制作，因而赴日留学。取得Home made果子协会教师资格、法国蓝带糕点等培训证书。1998年返台成立“巧思厨艺教室”。著作包括：《曾美子的黄金比例蛋糕》、《曾美子教你第一次做面包》、《卷起袖子学做蛋糕卷Roll
