<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园青年职涯探索一日体验营 引领学子走进AI与生技现场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798371-857909-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市政府青年事务局推动“桃园青年职涯发展计划”。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年06月28日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）为协助青年探索未来职涯方向并提前认识产业发展趋势，桃园市政府青年事务局推动“桃园青年职涯发展计划”，今(115)年暑假推出“YOUNG桃职涯趣－暑期一日探险体验营”。桃园市青年局长侯佳龄表示，青年职涯探索不只是了解职业名称，更重要的是透过亲身体验认识工作内容与产业发展趋势，因此青年局持续串联桃园在地优质企业资源，打造从校园到职场的探索平台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14798374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14798374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798374-857912.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14798374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798374-857912-600x387.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14798374" class="wp-caption-text">来思企业职场体验。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侯佳龄指出，青年局今年上半年已携手大专校院及在地企业，完成包含龙华科技大学与欣铨科技股份有限公司的半导体产业体验、机械工程系参访钜祥企业股份有限公司智慧制造产线、国立台北商业大学前往乐天桃园棒球场，以及开南大学形象管理系至昇恒昌、康是美、来思企业及发漾发型美妆沙龙等不同领域的职场体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14798372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14798372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798372-857910.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14798372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798372-857910-600x294.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14798372" class="wp-caption-text">主办单位说明不同领域的职场体验。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>延续此热潮，7月份首波暑期体验营内容将涵盖科技、生技、智慧农业、食品制造及物流等热门产业。7月9日将前往研华AIoT智能共创园区及南亚塑胶工业股份有限公司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14798373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14798373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798373-857911.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14798373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798373-857911-600x283.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14798373" class="wp-caption-text">今(115)年暑假推出“YOUNG桃职涯趣－暑期一日探险体验营”。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7月15日前往源鲜智慧农场及宏亚食品公司；7月22日则安排参访葡萄王生技及昶捷物流。各场次均安排企业主管职涯分享及交流时间，让青年从真实职场中发掘未来发展方向。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14798379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14798379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798379-857917.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14798379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798379-857917-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14798379" class="wp-caption-text">智慧农业体验。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14798380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14798380" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798380-857918.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14798380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798380-857918-600x243.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14798380" class="wp-caption-text">龙华科大体验。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青年局表示，除7月份公开场次外，8月份预计将邀请欣兴电子、景硕科技、华城电机、时硕工业及IKEA宜家家居等知名企业参与，让青年有机会接触半导体、电子科技、智慧制造、机电工程及服务零售等多元职涯选项。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14798375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14798375" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798375-857913.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14798375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798375-857913-600x394.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14798375" class="wp-caption-text">青年从真实职场中发掘未来发展方向。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为鼓励青年勇敢跨出探索职涯的第一步，凡参加任一场次职涯体验营，每场皆将抽出500元超商礼券及电影票等好礼，并可获得年度大奖抽奖资格，有机会抽中iPad Air 11吋（128GB）、Nintendo Switch 2、AirPods Pro 3及WILLEN II蓝牙音响等热门奖品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14798376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14798376" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798376-857914.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14798376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798376-857914-600x327.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="327" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14798376" class="wp-caption-text">暑期体验营内容将涵盖科技、生技、智慧农业、食品制造及物流等热门产业。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14798378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14798378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798378-857916.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14798378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14798378-857916-600x293.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14798378" class="wp-caption-text">欣铨科技体验。（桃园市政府青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青年局进一步说明，计划将持续透过职涯课程、企业参访、职场体验及职涯咨询等多元服务，陪伴应届毕业生或转职青年迎接职场。欢迎15至35岁青年朋友至线上平台“求职应援 相约桃园”网站( https://events.104.com.tw/tycg/20240425103544/ )报名职涯咨询、浏览职缺，更多暑期职涯探索活动、抽奖资讯及最新消息，亦可至桃园市政府青年事务局官方网站及Facebook粉丝专页(https://reurl.cc/2aXMR6 )浏览。◇</p>
