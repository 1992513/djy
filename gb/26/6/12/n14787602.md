<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】霸气征服路途之选 2026 RAM 1500 Rebel</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787618-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 RAM 1500 Rebel。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年06月13日讯】Ram的1500是北美的最受欢迎皮卡之一，以往RAM是在Dodge品牌旗下，至2010年为独立品牌，1500皮卡每年销量都多达数十万部，在北美曾经创下一年销售达70万部的纪录，但近年受电动车影响下有所下降，然而不减1500的吸引力，而这次测试的2026 RAM 1500是Rebel型号，Rebel也只有Crew Cab配5英尺7英寸货卡。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://dp5vkf7bbqo9d.cloudfront.net/O5lBGb6aHAni"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Rlplp_ReVtc?si=Toe8nEdapDga87yB" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>外型上Rebel的前脸，装有黑色进风口栏栅，隆起的引擎盖设有导风口，然而Rebel这个导风口其实只是装饰之用，在盖下并没有开通。防撞器左右各有一个小雾灯，并且配有黑色拖勾。测试车装有LT275/70R18E OWL All–Terrain Tires越野轮胎，车胎壁特厚，所以要跨越石块路面也没难度，配上18英寸哑黑色轮圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14787604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14787604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787604-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14787604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787604-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14787604" class="wp-caption-text">2026 RAM 1500 Rebel。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车尾最显眼的是那双粗圆的排气喉，货卡尾闸就相对简单，并不采用“Multifunction Tailgate”，不用复杂的机械设计增加车重。然而，为了方便上下货卡，RAM 1500在后左角装有伸缩的脚踏。两侧后视镜巨大，每边在外上角也装有一片小凸镜，用作覆盖盲点，但是笔者不太习惯，仍是要Shoulder Check一下才放心切线。而且，Ram 1500也装备了盲点监察系统（Blind Spot Monitoring），当有汽车进入盲点范围内便会作出提醒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14787605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14787605" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787605-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14787605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787605-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14787605" class="wp-caption-text">18英寸哑黑色轮圈。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14787606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14787606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787606-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14787606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787606-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14787606" class="wp-caption-text">伸缩的脚踏。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年款Ram 1500有四种引擎动力，从5.7升V8 HEMI eTorque hybrid引擎，又有3.6升Pentastar V-6 eTorque hybrid引擎，3升 Hurricane SO双涡轮增压引擎则有两种输出，分别为420匹马力和469磅英尺扭矩；另一种输出是540匹马力和521磅英尺扭矩。全都是配备不同型号的8速自动变速箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14787607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14787607" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787607-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_24.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14787607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787607-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14787607" class="wp-caption-text">3升 Hurricane SO双涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Ram 1500 Rebel有五种行车模式，包括“自动（Auto）”、“运动（ Sport）”、“拖拉（Tow）”、“雪地（Snow）”及“自定（Custom）”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14787608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14787608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787608-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14787608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787608-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14787608" class="wp-caption-text">五种行车模式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车内空间宽阔，既高级又时尚，座椅装有冷冻功能（及加热功能），同时方向盘也有加热功能。方向盘本身采用四幅式设计，方向盘上方有巡航及各安全设备开关，在背后又有音响的控剩，右下方有变速限制按钮，可手动限制升档。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14787609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14787609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787609-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_35.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14787609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787609-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_35-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14787609" class="wp-caption-text">车内空间宽阔。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台上方是一个12英寸的直立式触控萤幕，内里运行Uconnect 5系统，也可支援Apple Carplay及Android Auto。萤幕下方有AUX输入、两个USB Type C及两个Type A四个端口，再往下装有一片直立的塑胶板，下方是U形夹，可以将手机竖立放在上面，左侧并装有无线充电板。前排两座位中间的变速盘。标准装置了9个扬声器的音响系统，另外，测试的RAM 1500 Rebel也配置了电动脚踏，可整组脚踏移近驾驶者或远离，适合不同脚长的驾驶者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14787610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14787610" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787610-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14787610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787610-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14787610" class="wp-caption-text">12英寸的直立式触控萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驾驶1500皮卡最享受是那种霸气，在切线时很多旁车也会自动让路。货卡在搬东西时，也极为方便！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14787612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14787612" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787612-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14787612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14787612-2026_RAM_1500_Rebel_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14787612" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
