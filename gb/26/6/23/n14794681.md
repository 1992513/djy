<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>去干洗店和自助洗衣店 你要学会这些英文</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14692079-NYC-The-Orchid-Show-2026-07.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>衣服送洗这件事，在美国和中国国内的习惯不太一样。很多美国家庭自己在家洗衣服，干洗店主要处理那些不能机洗的衣物——西装、羊绒大衣、丝质衬衫、礼服这一类。今天把去干洗店会遇到的英文从头说清楚。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000"><strong>干洗店叫什么</strong></span></h2><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p>干洗店英文叫dry cleaner或者dry cleaning shop。路上看到招牌写着Cleaners或Dry Cleaners就是了。有些干洗店同时提供普通洗涤和熨烫服务，叫laundry and dry cleaning。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p>干洗叫dry cleaning，普通水洗叫laundry或wet cleaning，熨烫叫pressing或ironing。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000"><strong>衣服上的洗涤标签</strong></span></h2><p>送洗之前先看衣服内侧的洗涤标签，英文叫care label或laundry label。标签上除了图示，通常也有文字说明，几个最常见的：</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p>Dry clean only，只能干洗，不能水洗。这类衣服送干洗店就对了。</p><p>Do not wash，不能洗，只能干洗或者局部处理。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p>Hand wash only，只能手洗，不能机洗。</p><p>Machine washable，可以机洗。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p>Do not tumble dry，不能用烘干机，要自然晾干。</p><p>Do not iron，不能熨烫。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000"><strong>进了干洗店怎么说</strong></span></h2><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>走进干洗店，把衣服交给柜台，工作人员会问你：What would you like done with these?（这些衣服要做什么处理？）</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p>你可以说：</p><p>I&#x27;d like these dry cleaned, please. 这些衣服我想干洗。</p><p>I need this suit dry cleaned and pressed. 这套西装我想干洗加熨烫。</p><p>Can you just press this shirt? It doesn&#x27;t need cleaning, just ironing. 这件衬衫只需要熨烫就好，不需要洗。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_5"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_5"></div></div></div><h2><span style="color:#008000"><strong>说清楚污渍在哪里</strong></span></h2><p>如果衣服上有污渍，一定要主动告诉工作人员，指着污渍的位置说，不要假设他们自己会发现。工作人员通常会在衣服上别一个小标签，记录污渍位置和处理方式。</p><p>There&#x27;s a stain here. Can you try to remove it? 这里有一个污渍，可以洗掉吗？</p><p>I&#x27;m not sure what caused the stain. It might be wine. 我不确定是什么污渍，可能是红酒。</p><p>This is an oil stain on the front. 这是正面的一个油渍。</p><p>工作人员可能会说：</p><p>We&#x27;ll do our best, but we can&#x27;t guarantee the stain will come out completely. 我们会尽力，但不能保证污渍能完全去除。</p><p>这是很标准的回答，不是推托，是因为有些污渍确实无法百分之百去除。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000"><strong>取衣服的时候</strong></span></h2><p>送衣服的时候店员会给你一张取件单，叫claim ticket或者receipt，上面有取件号码。取衣服的时候把这张单子带去，给店员看：</p><p>Hi, I&#x27;m here to pick up my dry cleaning. Here&#x27;s my ticket. 你好，我来取干洗的衣服，这是我的取件单。</p><p>如果找不到取件单，可以说：</p><p>I lost my ticket. My name is Wang Wei. I dropped off a suit on Monday. 我弄丢取件单了，我叫王伟，周一送来一套西装。</p><p>店员通常会用你的姓名或电话号码查询记录。取到衣服之后，当场检查一下，确认污渍是否去除、衣服有没有损坏。如果发现问题，当场说比事后再来投诉容易处理得多。</p><p>The stain is still there. Is there anything else you can do? 污渍还在，还有其它办法吗？</p><p>There&#x27;s a button missing. It wasn&#x27;t like that when I brought it in. 少了一颗扣子，送来的时候不是这样的。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000"><strong>自助洗衣店</strong></span></h2><p>说完干洗店，顺便说说自助洗衣店，英文叫laundromat或者coin laundry。这个词很多新移民不知道，路上看到Laundromat的招牌，就是投币自助洗衣的地方，不是干洗店。</p><p>进去之后找一台空着的洗衣机，叫washer或washing machine。把衣服放进去，投币或者刷卡，选水温——cold冷水、warm温水、hot热水——然后按Start开始。</p><p>洗完之后把衣服移到烘干机，叫dryer，同样投币启动，选择温度，一般选medium heat就好，delicate是低温适合容易缩水的衣物，high heat是高温适合棉质厚重衣物。</p><p>自助洗衣店里如果有问题，可以问其他顾客，也可以找店员，通常会有一个工作人员（attendant）在场负责维护机器和找零。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000"><strong>关键词汇速查</strong></span></h2><p><strong>? 干洗店基本词汇</strong></p><p>干洗店：dry cleaner　/dra? ?kli?n?r/</p><p>干洗：dry cleaning　/dra? ?kli?n??/</p><p>水洗：laundry　/?l??ndri/</p><p>熨烫：pressing / ironing　/?pres??/ /?a??rn??/</p><p>洗涤标签：care label　/ke?r ?le?b?l/</p><p>取件单：claim ticket　/kle?m ?t?k?t/</p><p>污渍：stain　/ste?n/</p><p>去渍：stain removal　/ste?n r??mu?v?l/</p><p><strong>? 常见衣物</strong></p><p>西装：suit　/su?t/</p><p>西装上衣：suit jacket / blazer　/su?t ?d??k?t/ /?ble?z?r/</p><p>西装裤：dress pants / trousers　/dres p?nts/ /?tra?z?rz/</p><p>领带：tie　/ta?/</p><p>大衣：overcoat　/?o?v?rko?t/</p><p>羽绒服：down jacket　/da?n ?d??k?t/</p><p>羊绒大衣：cashmere coat　/?k??m??r ko?t/</p><p>礼服：formal dress / gown　/?f??rm?l dres/ /ɡa?n/</p><p><strong>? 自助洗衣店</strong><strong>?</strong></p><p>自助洗衣店：laundromat　/?l??ndr?m?t/</p><p>洗衣机：washer / washing machine　/?w???r/ /?w???? m???i?n/</p><p>烘干机：dryer　/?dra??r/</p><p>冷水：cold water　/ko?ld ?w??t?r/</p><p>温水：warm water　/w??rm ?w??t?r/</p><p>热水：hot water　/h?t ?w??t?r/</p><p>中温烘干：medium heat　/?mi?di?m hi?t/</p><p>低温烘干：delicate　/?del?k?t/</p><p>店员：attendant　/??tend?nt/</p><h2><span style="color:#008000"><strong>常用口语</strong></span></h2><p>这些衣服我想干洗。I&#x27;d like these dry cleaned, please.</p><p>这套西装我想干洗加熨烫。I need this suit dry cleaned and pressed.</p><p>只需要熨烫，不需要洗。Just pressing, no cleaning needed.</p><p>这里有污渍，可以洗掉吗？There&#x27;s a stain here.
