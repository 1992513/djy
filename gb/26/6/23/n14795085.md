<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠狮子会余艳玲接会长 新会员获麻州区嘉奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795062-DSC04183-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿华埠狮子会6月20日举办年会晚宴。新届职员宣誓就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年06月24日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）在6月20日的波士顿华埠狮子会年会中，资深会员余艳玲接任会长，期待为社区带来更多公益服务。刚入会一年的林柔妤，获得麻州33k分区颁发的褒奖，亦谈到她参与公益活动的喜悦和感动。</p>
<p>当晚在波士顿华埠的新月宫酒家，总计近百人参加年度晚宴，包括华埠狮子会成员，麻州各地狮子会友人，以及华埠多个公益团体的代表等等。来宾们飨宴欢歌，见证新一届职员宣誓就职，还为“关键服务奖”（Keys to Service Award）和“茂文钟士奖”（Melvin Jones Fellowship Award）的得主们鼓掌喝采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14795065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795065" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795065-DSC04209.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795065-DSC04209-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795065" class="wp-caption-text">参与晚宴的华埠及麻州各地狮子会代表合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14795057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795057" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795057-DSC04165.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795057" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795057-DSC04165-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795057" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们共同祈祷。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>新届职员就职 余艳玲会长承诺助力公益</strong></h2>
<p>“作为今年的会长，我对你们的承诺非常非常简单，我会努力工作，我会倾听，我会确保我们继续对唐人街有所助益。我希望每一位会员都感觉到自己的价值。”余艳玲说。</p>
<p>除了华埠狮子会之外，余艳玲也是纽英崙余风采堂的活跃成员，经常参加各项公益活动。在晚宴中，她从卸任会长汤伟雄手中接过明灯，并敲响铜铃，就任新届会长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14795063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795063-DSC04202.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795063-DSC04202-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795063" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长余艳玲敲响铜铃，正式就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14795060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795060" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795060-DSC04175.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795060-DSC04175-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795060" class="wp-caption-text">卸任会长汤伟雄（右）献花感谢余丽媖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>汤伟雄感谢各位会员在他任期内的支持，并特别向财政职员余丽媖、秘书伍国光，以及刚入会一年但非常活跃的林柔妤献花，感谢他们大力协助会务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14795061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795061-DSC04176.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795061-DSC04176-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795061" class="wp-caption-text">卸任会长汤伟雄（右）献花感谢伍国光。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场晚会以狮子会传统的“四部分”开场，蔡倩婷领唱“美国第一章歌”（First Verse of America），李源沛领读效忠誓词，伍国光带领大家祈祷，阮鸿灿带领狮子祝酒。</p>
<p>在国际狮子会麻州33k分区总监巴利奥拉（Anthony Baliola）的见证下，波士顿华埠狮子会新届职员宣誓就职，发愿服务社区，团结会员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14795056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795056" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795056-DSC04159.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795056-DSC04159-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795056" class="wp-caption-text">蔡倩婷领唱“美国第一章歌”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14795059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795059" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795059-DSC04168.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795059-DSC04168-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795059" class="wp-caption-text">阮鸿灿（左）带领狮子祝酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14795093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795093-DSC04179.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795093-DSC04179-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795093" class="wp-caption-text">李源沛介绍各地狮子会来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>林柔妤获关键服务奖 中华公所获茂文钟士奖</strong></h2>
<p>巴利奥拉还代表麻州33k分区，向林柔妤颁发了关键服务奖，赞赏后者对于社区服务的热心、勤劳和影响力。</p>
<p>林柔妤受访时笑言，参加华埠狮子会的一年以来，最令她高兴的，就是真正能帮助到别人。她特别谈到自己参与眼镜募捐服务的经历。她和会员们一同整理、清洗由热心人士捐赠的数百副眼镜，乐此不疲。</p>
<p>“我在洗（眼镜）的时候，我就觉得，如果谁戴上了这些眼镜，那我会觉得很开心，因为可以帮到他。有些地方，第三世界国家，因为他们没资金或没能力去买眼镜，不过有我们的捐赠，去帮到他，让他看清楚这个世界是这么美好、这么温暖的。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14795066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14795066" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795066-DSC04212.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14795066" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14795066-DSC04212-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14795066" class="wp-caption-text">狮子会麻州33k分区总监Anthony Baliola（左）向林柔妤颁发关键服务奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来宾们还向国际狮子会最高奖项——茂文钟士奖的得主们表达祝贺。这是嘉奖为公益募款和社区服务做出卓越贡献的个人和团体。本届获奖者，包括华埠狮子会成员赵娟和李典儒，以及植根于波士顿华埠100余年的纽英崙中华公所。</p>
<p>中华公所主席雷国辉、中文书记张青梅、英文书记阮鸿灿、核数伍振中共同领取了这一奖项。</p>
<p>此外，当天晚宴餐桌的布置亦是别出心裁。总管职员关美玲为每一桌摆放了彩灯花瓶，增添欢快气氛。</p>
