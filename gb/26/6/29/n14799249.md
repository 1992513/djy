<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英崙中华总会 欢庆美国建国250周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799228-DSC04314-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月27日晚上，纽英崙中华总会在波士顿华埠龙凤酒楼设宴，欢庆美国建国250周年。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年06月29日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）纽英崙中华总会6月27日设宴欢庆美国建国250周年，并向杰出的华裔美国人致敬，包括波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu），以及华埠昆士中学创始校长黄伯勋（Dr. Bak Fun Wong）。</p>

<p>当晚在波士顿华埠的龙凤酒楼，政要名流及侨社成员总计250多位来宾欢聚一堂，庆祝即将到来的美国第250届“独立日”（Independence Day），以及彰显华裔美国人的贡献和成就。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14799229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799229-DSC04316.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799229-DSC04316-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799229" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华总会在波士顿华埠龙凤酒楼设宴，欢庆美国建国250周年。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14799231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799231-DSC04340.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799231-DSC04340-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799231" class="wp-caption-text">政要嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>吴弭与华埠渊源深厚</strong></h2>
<p>宴席间，纽英崙中华总会主席余丽媖携中华公所主席雷国辉，向波士顿市长吴弭颁赠“勤政泽波城，典范耀华裔”奖杯。</p>
<p>雷国辉介绍吴弭时谈到，他大约15年前刚认识她时，这位年轻的政坛新星“就像我的女儿”。当时中华总会创始人、已故侨领陈毓礼介绍她加入广教学校等华埠社区团体。如今，作为城市首位华裔市长，吴弭亦对华埠社区的福祉多有助益。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14799226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799226-DSC04296.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799226-DSC04296-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799226" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉介绍吴弭的履历。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴弭领奖后笑言，唐人街可谓“我在波士顿的第一个家”，“没有在座的各位，就不会有我的今天”。她回忆道，华埠侨领们“鼓励我，信任我，在我陷入艰难的角色、政治或时刻时拉我一把”。</p>
<p>她亦感慨，她的父母刚移民美国时，虽然还不太会讲英语，并且从事着辛苦的工作，但已经对后代子孙有所期待。“他们仍然希望，他们的孩子，他们的孙辈，曾孙辈，有一天能有机会回馈这个伟大的国家。”吴弭说。她还说，类似的事情，在美国历史的每个阶段都持续回响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14799227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799227-DSC04304.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799227-DSC04304-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799227" class="wp-caption-text">中华总会主席余丽媖（左）向波士顿市长吴弭颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>黄伯勋深耕教育 广受尊敬</strong></h2>
<p>余丽媖也携华埠昆士小学（Josiah Quincy Elementary School）校长司徒丽英，向昆士中学创始校长黄伯勋博士颁发“树人殊可敬，杏坛传德誉”奖杯。</p>
<p>黄伯勋在波士顿公立学校系统服务了40多年，曾任昆士中学创始校长，以及昆士小学校长，目前是哈佛大学教育学研究生院导师。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14799230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799230" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799230-DSC04334.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799230-DSC04334-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799230" class="wp-caption-text">中华总会向两位杰出的华裔美国人颁奖。左起：波士顿市长吴弭、纽英崙中华总会主席余丽媖、黄伯勋夫人、昆士中学创始校长黄伯勋、昆士小学校长司徒丽英。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>司徒丽英笑言，她在昆士小学读4年级的时候，就认识黄伯勋了，当时后者已担任校长。在他的带领下，昆士中学从一所无名学校，成为了远近闻名的优秀教育机构。司徒丽英从昆士学校毕业，如今又回来当校长，过程中得到了黄伯勋的诸多指点，更加敬重他的远见卓识。</p>
<p>黄伯勋表示珍视他与司徒丽英家庭的长期友谊，也特别感谢吴弭市长支持教育事业。他很高兴看到，昆士小学和昆士中学已是硕果累累，在司徒丽英校长和张可仁校长的带领下，不仅闻名于当地社区，也开展了卓有成效的国际项目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14799220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799220" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799220-DSC04255.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799220-DSC04255-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799220" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿华裔退伍军人会参加晚宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14799221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799221-DSC04259.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799221-DSC04259-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799221" class="wp-caption-text">主办方分发“America 250”纪念帽。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>侨社珍惜美国民主自由</strong></h2>
<p>这场庆祝晚会，由波士顿洪门致公堂主席朱耀钟担任司仪，由中华公所财政余宝爱领唱美国国歌。中华总会还特别定制了“America 250”主题鸭舌帽，发给每一位来宾，颇受好评。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14799222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799222-DSC04266.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799222-DSC04266-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799222" class="wp-caption-text">朱耀钟担任司仪。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14799223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799223-DSC04269.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799223-DSC04269-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799223" class="wp-caption-text">余宝爱领唱美国国歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14799224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799224-DSC04271.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799224-DSC04271-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799224" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们起立唱美国国歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中华总会主席余丽媖介绍道，该团体成立于2000年，持有“推广民主和自由理念”的宗旨，并且长年支持中华民国，每年主办“双十国庆”游行庆典。而在每年的美国独立日前夕，中华总会也会举办晚宴，嘉奖卓越的华裔美国人，包括官员、警察、消防员等等。各项活动，亦得到侨社各团体的大力支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14799225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799225-DSC04275.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799225-DSC04275-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799225" class="wp-caption-text">中华总会主席余丽媖介绍该团体历史。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14799235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799235-DSC04290.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799235-DSC04290-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799235" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏向获奖人致敬。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏，侨教中心主任高家富，也都参加了晚宴。廖朝宏特别谈及吴弭家庭的台湾渊源，以及昆士学校系统与台湾学校的合作，并向两位获奖人表达敬意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14799232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799232-DSC04374.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799232-DSC04374-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799232" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们热情唱歌，左起：侨教中心主任高家富、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨务委员陈仕维。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14799233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14799233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799233-DSC04389.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14799233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14799233-DSC04389-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14799233" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们热情唱歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>宴席间，余宝爱、朱耀钟等领唱“America The Beautiful”歌曲。其他诸多来宾也踊跃献唱，例如廖朝宏、高家富及侨务委员陈仕维热情演唱国语歌“朋友”，波士顿市政府华埠联络员林天琪加入合唱“我只在乎你”，谭达儿、谢如健、黄展唐等多人合唱粤语版“朋友”等等。现场掌声和欢呼声不绝于耳，非常热闹。◇</p>
