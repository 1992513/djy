<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>经典酱油腌肉 10分钟完成照烧鸡肉蛋盖饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14785085-970367dd85c5e3bbb6fba38b945e5b33-e1781007858483.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr><strong>编者按：</strong>繁忙的日子里，善用事先制作的冷冻常备肉做为主菜，副菜则使用很快煮熟的食材，节省时间，能更快地备妥餐点。本料理运用冷冻常备鸡肉，10分钟就完成照烧鸡肉蛋盖饭。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000">冷冻常备鸡肉：照烧腌鸡肉</span></h2><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p>大人小孩都爱吃的经典酱油腌料。鸡肉切成一口大小再放入袋中，节省烹调的时间。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p><strong>材料（1袋份）：</strong></p><p>用于唐扬鸡的鸡腿肉…300g<br/>A：<br/>酱油…2大匙<br/>酒、味醂…各1大匙</p><p><strong>作法：</strong></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p>1.鸡肉切成一口大小。</p><p>2.将A倒入冷冻保鲜袋中<strong>混合均匀</strong>，再加入1<strong>搓揉入味</strong>。<strong>压平后挤出空气，将袋口紧闭再冷冻</strong>。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><h2><span style="color:#008000">照烧鸡肉蛋盖饭</span></h2><p><strong>烹调时间：</strong>10分钟</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p><strong>材料（2人份）：</strong></p><p>照烧腌肉…1袋<br/>蛋…2个<br/>美乃滋…1大匙<br/>盐、胡椒…各少许<br/>沙拉油…3小匙<br/>热饭…2碗份<br/>水菜…1/3把</p><p><strong>作法：</strong></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>1.将鸡蛋、美乃滋、盐、胡椒倒入调理盆中搅拌均匀。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p>2.在平底锅中涂上1小匙沙拉油后以中火加热，倒入1再用力搅拌，半熟时暂时取出盛盘。</p><p>3.将2的平底锅擦拭一下，再涂上2小匙沙拉油，倒入“照烧腌肉”、1大匙水（材料份量外）后盖上锅盖，以中小火蒸煮。</p><p>4.等到肉变色之后打开锅盖将鸡肉翻面，煮至熟透为止。</p><p>5.分别将1人份的饭盛入碗中，放上4、2，并用料理剪刀将水菜剪成3～4cm长后摆上去。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px"
