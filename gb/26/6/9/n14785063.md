<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>简单3步骤 煮出台湾经典卤肉饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14785067-3e3e8806ea853bc53107a8b56ae7de6a.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr><strong>编者按：</strong>本食谱教你透过调味、腌渍、冷冻三步骤，预先制作冷冻常备的五香腌猪肉，就能大大地缩短料理时间。在忙碌的日子里，快速变化出浓郁飘香的台湾经典卤肉饭。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000">冷冻常备猪肉：五香腌肉</span></h2><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p>基本的酱油、酒、砂糖加上五香粉的异国调味。看似困难的卤肉，只要用这种调味方式也能简单煮出卤肉饭！</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p><strong>材料（1袋份）：</strong></p><p>猪五花肉块…400g<br/>盐、胡椒…各少许<br/>A：<br/>酱油、酒…各2大匙<br/>砂糖…1大匙<br/>五香粉…少许</p><p><strong>作法：</strong></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p>1.猪肉切成1.5cm宽，用盐、胡椒腌渍入味。</p><p>2.将A倒入冷冻保鲜袋中混合均匀，再加入1搓揉入味。<strong>压平后挤出空气，将袋口紧闭再冷冻</strong>。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><h2><span style="color:#008000">免炖煮卤肉饭</span></h2><p>短时间煮出台湾经典菜色！</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p><strong>烹调时间：</strong>20分钟</p><p><strong>材料（2人份）：</strong></p><p>五香腌肉…1袋<br/>青江菜…1/2把<br/>香菇…2朵<br/>竹笋（水煮）…100g<br/>沙拉油…1大匙<br/>热饭…2碗份<br/>白葱丝…5cm份<br/>水煮蛋…2个（去壳）<br/>熟芝麻（白）…适量</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p><strong>作法：</strong></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p>1.青江菜切成3～4cm长，<strong>在热水中加入少许盐</strong>（材料份量外）后烫熟，再泡在流水中，然后将水分挤干。香菇去柄，与竹笋一起切成丁。（延伸阅读：<ahref="https://www.epochtimes.com/b5/22/3/7/n13628365.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="health-article-hover-class">汆烫蔬菜该不该加盐？用这招味道不易流失</a>）</p><p>2.在平底锅中涂上一层沙拉油，倒入“五香腌肉”、1大匙水（材料份量外）后盖上锅盖，以中小火蒸煮。</p><p>3.等到肉变色之后打开锅盖，用料理剪刀剪开，再加入1的竹笋、香菇后炒熟。</p><p>4.将饭、3分成1人份盛盘。再放上1的青江菜、白葱丝，并搭配上切成一半的水煮蛋后撒上芝麻。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_5"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_5"></div></div></div><p><strong>Point：</strong>肉用料理剪刀剪成适口大小。</p><p><strong>（网站专文，禁止转载）</strong></p><p>（本文摘编自《<ahref="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0011006665"
