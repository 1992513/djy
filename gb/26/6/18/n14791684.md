<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯房产平均售出时间增至14天</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年06月18日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者林达澳洲珀斯编译报导）西澳房地产协会（</span><span lang="en-US">Real Estate Institute of WA</span><span lang="zh-TW">，简称</span><span lang="en-US">REIWA</span><span lang="zh-TW">）日前公布的数据显示，过去三个月内，珀斯独立房的平均售出时间几近翻倍，延长至</span><span lang="en-US">14</span><span lang="zh-TW">天。</span></p>
<p>据《西澳人报》报导，作为珀斯房市放缓的核心指标，<span lang="en-US">5</span>月份售出一栋独立房平均需要两周，而今年<span lang="en-US">2</span>月份仅需<span lang="en-US">9</span>天。</p>
<p>上个月单元房（<span lang="en-US">Units</span>）的平均售出时间为<span lang="en-US">13</span>天，而<span lang="en-US">2</span>月份为<span lang="en-US">8</span>天。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">REIWA</span><span lang="zh-TW">主席布朗女士（</span><span lang="en-US">Suzanne Brown</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，珀斯房市在此期间有所降温。</span></p>
<p>这在一定程度上归因于待售房源数量的大幅增加。截至<span lang="en-US">5</span>月底，通过西澳房地产协会平台挂牌销售的房产达<span lang="en-US">5137</span>套，较<span lang="en-US">4</span>月份增长了<span lang="en-US">16%</span>，比去年同期增长了<span lang="en-US">20.7%</span>。</p>
<p>她说：“新挂牌房源的增加为买家提供了更多选择，也让他们有更充裕的时间做出决定，同时他们也在应对连续三次加息带来的冲击。”</p>
<p>“这影响了买家的贷款能力，以及购房后的还贷负担。因此，买家在出手时变得更加挑剔。”</p>
<p>不过布朗表示，目前的销售速度仍快于疫情前五年<span lang="en-US">50</span>天的平均水平。</p>
<p>根据西澳房地产协会的数据，<span lang="en-US">5</span>月独立房销售最快的区是<span lang="en-US">Nollamara</span>和<span lang="en-US">Coolbellup</span>，仅需<span lang="en-US">6</span>天；紧随其后的是<span lang="en-US">Subiaco</span>、<span lang="en-US">Hillarys</span>、<span lang="en-US">Forrestfield</span>、<span lang="en-US">Dianella</span>和<span lang="en-US">Iluka</span>，需<span lang="en-US">7</span>天；<span lang="en-US">Yokine</span>、<span lang="en-US">Woodvale</span>和<span lang="en-US">Lesmurdie</span>则需要<span lang="en-US">8</span>天。</p>
<p>单元房销售最快的地区是<span lang="en-US">Tuart Hill</span>，仅需<span lang="en-US">5</span>天；<span lang="en-US">Cockburn Central</span>为<span lang="en-US">6</span>天；<span lang="en-US">Wembley</span>为<span lang="en-US">7</span>天；<span lang="en-US">Yokine</span>为<span lang="en-US">8</span>天；<span lang="en-US">Maylands</span>为<span lang="en-US">9</span>天；<span lang="en-US">Subiaco</span>和<span lang="en-US">Scarborough</span>为<span lang="en-US">10</span>天；<span lang="en-US">Rockingham</span>、<span lang="en-US">Perth</span>市区和<span lang="en-US">Midland</span>为<span lang="en-US">12</span>天。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">5</span>月珀斯独立房中位数价格大幅飙升<span lang="en-US">2.2%</span>，达到<span lang="en-US">92</span>万澳元，而单元房销售中位数价格也上涨了<span lang="en-US">2.3%</span>，至<span lang="en-US">66</span>万澳元。</p>
<p>布朗预计，在此之后价格增长速度将有所放缓。她同时强调，市场依然保持健康。</p>
