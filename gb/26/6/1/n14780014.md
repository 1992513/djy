<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨教中心办文化培训 助民俗手艺进中文课堂</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780004-DSC02563-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月30日，侨教中心举办波士顿地区文化种子教师培训课，邀张君芳老师分享在中文课堂上做汤圆、元宵和12生肖海报的心得。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年06月02日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨牛顿市报导）在中文课堂上，教师带学生搓汤圆、画生肖海报，既增进课堂趣味，又帮助学生体验中华文化。侨教中心5月30日下午举办的培训课，目标是帮助教师经营这样的特色课堂。</p>
<p>当天，牛顿市（Newton）的侨教中心在其活动厅举办“波士顿地区文化种子教师培训课”，邀蔚蓝中学（Wayland Middle School）资深中文老师张君芳，分享她带领学生滚元宵、搓汤圆、做“纸上生肖”手工艺的心得。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780005-DSC02567.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780005-DSC02567-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780005" class="wp-caption-text">中文老师们愉快地做元宵和汤圆。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>培训课参与者，既有主流公立学校的中文老师，也有文诚、沙龙、慈济等中文学校的老师。大家在动手制作的同时，还讨论降低课堂成本，控制课堂纪律等话题，气氛非常欢快。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780007-DSC02570.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780007-DSC02570-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780007" class="wp-caption-text">中文老师们愉快地做元宵和汤圆。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张君芳从课前征求家长同意的流程讲起，讲到向学校申请资金，谈到网购原料，也讲到提升课堂效率的具体流程。比如，她通常在第一节课介绍文化和做法，第二节课带学生制作元宵和汤圆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780006-DSC02569.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780006-DSC02569-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780006" class="wp-caption-text">张君芳老师介绍原料。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780010-DSC02586.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780010-DSC02586-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780010" class="wp-caption-text">参与者展示自己刚刚搓的汤圆。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，参与培训的老师们在现场滚元宵、搓汤圆，并且品尝自己的作品。有的老师说，张君芳准备的芝麻糊、抹茶、红豆冰等原料非常好，她也要购买给自己的课堂使用；还有的老师在研究如何使元宵大小适中且不易开裂，准备下次带自己的学生制作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780008-DSC02581.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780008-DSC02581-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780008" class="wp-caption-text">老师们煮熟自己刚刚制作的元宵。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780011-DSC02598.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780011-DSC02598-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780011" class="wp-caption-text">参与者做12生肖海报字卡。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在关于12生肖海报的分享中，张君芳特别强调，“手工艺可以让中文课变得更有趣，但不能让手工艺取代中文课，只能起辅助作用”，“重点是，在这过程中，学生学到了什么”。</p>
<p>她还谈到，她有时让学习快的学生先做海报，在此期间给程度较低的学生补课，并让这些学生回家才做海报，以期让孩子们共同进步。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780009-DSC02585.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780009-DSC02585-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780009" class="wp-caption-text">煮熟汤圆和元宵后，培训课参与者合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨教中心主任高家富也参与了活动，品尝老师们制作的元宵和汤圆，连连称赞。◇</p>
