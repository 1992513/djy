<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>客家乡亲会换届 新会长黄文彦盼吸纳年轻人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780794-DSC02637-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月31日，纽英伦客家乡亲会在侨教中心举办换届餐会。左起：侨教中心主任高家富、客家会副会长邱明慧、卸任会长宋玉琴、新届会长黄文彦、经文处处长廖朝宏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年06月03日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨牛顿市报导）素以美食和热情闻名的纽英伦客家乡亲会，5月31日在侨教中心举办换届餐会，庆祝新会长黄文彦及团队就职，也期盼吸引更多年轻人加入。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780796" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780796-DSC02650.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780796-DSC02650-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780796" class="wp-caption-text">5月31日，纽英伦客家乡亲会在侨教中心举办换届餐会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780788" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780788-DSC02609.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780788-DSC02609-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780788" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780786-DSC02604.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780786-DSC02604-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780786" class="wp-caption-text">客家乡亲会理事们开箱验票，宣布新届会长人选。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天中午在牛顿市（Newton）的侨教中心，新届会长黄文彦，副会长邱明慧接过信物，正式就任。另一位副会长李沅臻因事忙而未能到场。乡亲们还热情欢迎新理事赖绍强和曾雅怡加入这个大家庭。</p>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏，侨教中心主任高家富，侨务委员郭竞儒等嘉宾，皆到场同乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780789" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780789-DSC02616.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780789-DSC02616-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780789" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏向新届会长、副会长道贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780791" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780791-DSC02626.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780791-DSC02626-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780791" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富称赞客家人热情。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新会长黄文彦在生物科技公司工作，并长期活跃于波士顿台湾影展协会、台湾人生物科技协会等团体。副会长邱明慧和李沅臻皆居住于罗德岛，前者为贷款经纪人，后者是布朗大学博士生及台湾研究生学生会会长。</p>
<p>黄文彦感谢各位前辈和长官帮助客家乡亲会奠定了坚实基础，随后也表达了新届团队的愿景。“我们希望让更多的年轻人加入。即使你是客家人或不是客家人，你闻到、看到客家的美食，你愿意来，我觉得都是一个很好的开始。”他笑言。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780795-DSC02648.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780795-DSC02648-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780795" class="wp-caption-text">新会长黄文彦希望吸引更多年轻人入会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780792-DSC02628.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780792-DSC02628-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780792" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员郭竞儒向新届会长和副会长道贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们都是异乡人，在这边打拼，其实心里多多少少都承受一些辛苦跟挑战。我自己是觉得，如果在这边，大家可以聚在一起吃饭，其实这是一件很温暖的事情。”黄文彦说。</p>
<p>他还希望，客家乡亲会也将与波士顿地区其它团体增进合作，“碰撞出新的火花”，发扬客家人“包容跟团结”的精神。</p>
<p>卸任会长宋玉琴感谢各位会员大力支持会务，让她在担任纽英崙客家乡亲会及北美总会职务的8年来，过得平稳且快乐。她也特别感谢各位赞助者，尤其是捐赠大笔款项的Wellesley Toyota老板柯乃南，以及侨务顾问游胜雄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780793-DSC02634.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780793-DSC02634-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780793" class="wp-caption-text">卸任会长宋玉琴感谢各位会员大力支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780799-DSC02662.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780799-DSC02662-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780799" class="wp-caption-text">新理事赖绍强加入客家会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780798-DSC02659.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780798-DSC02659-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780798" class="wp-caption-text">新理事曾雅怡加入客家会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>嘉宾道贺 齐享客家美食</strong></h2>
<p>当天，客家乡亲及家人总计约50人共享餐会。乡亲们制作了客家麻糬、客家小炒、粿条汤、卤牛肉等诸多美食。向来支持客家会的蔡坤喜医师，还特地买来烧鸭，热情地向大家打招呼。资深会员陈裕逢主持餐会，以其一贯的幽默风格，令大家欢笑连连。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14780790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780790" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780790-DSC02620.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780790-DSC02620-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780790" class="wp-caption-text">陈裕逢主持餐会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780800" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780800-DSC02664.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780800-DSC02664-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780800" class="wp-caption-text">蔡坤喜医师送来烧鸭，向乡亲们打招呼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14780801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14780801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780801-DSC02668.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14780801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14780801-DSC02668-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14780801" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们乐享客家美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经文处处长廖朝宏称赞，来到客家会，就感到特别亲切，乡亲们不仅彼此支持，也在当地传播着台湾的文化。他感谢宋玉琴多年来的辛勤服务，也表示很高兴看到年轻人接棒会务。◇</p>
