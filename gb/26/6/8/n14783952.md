<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨声音乐剧社庆87周年 重演“帝女花”全剧</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783933-DSC03066-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月6日，波士顿侨声音乐剧社庆87周年，在派拉蒙中心重演“帝女花”全剧。演员们盛装登场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年06月08日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“心好似月挂银河静，身好似夜鬼谁能认，劫后弄玉怕箫声，说什么连理能同命。”粤剧名篇《帝女花》讲述明末长平公主夫妇的生离死别，6月6日再次登上侨声音乐剧社的年度演出舞台。</p>
<p>当天下午，波士顿侨声音乐剧社在爱默森学院（Emerson College）派拉蒙中心（Paramount Center）举办年度演出，以传统唱功演绎300多年前的悲欢离合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14783932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783932-DSC03052.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783932-DSC03052-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783932" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿侨声音乐剧社庆87周年，在派拉蒙中心重演“帝女花”全剧。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14783939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783939-DSC03136.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783939-DSC03136-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783939" class="wp-caption-text">诸多观众欣赏演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着大幕拉开，古典音乐奏响，演员们穿着华丽戏服登场。无论是雷玉霞饰演长平公主，穿着的绣花裙服，还是伍国荣饰演驸马周世显，穿着的书生白袍，各位主角、配角皆有特色鲜明的妆扮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14783937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783937" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783937-DSC03119.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783937-DSC03119-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783937" class="wp-caption-text">剧组成员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《帝女花》全剧共有8场，从长平公主亲选驸马讲起，铺叙至明室灭亡、公主流落尼庵，再到两人久别重逢，终以两位主角共同殉国落幕。在长达4个多小时的演出期间，演员们抑扬顿挫、情感丰沛的唱功，乐师们平稳的演奏，赢得满场观众的热烈掌声。</p>
<p>为帮助观众理解粤语剧情，现场设有大屏幕，显示中、英文字幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14783935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783935-DSC03098.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783935-DSC03098-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783935" class="wp-caption-text">第二幕，崇祯皇帝欲杀长平公主。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14783934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783934-DSC03071.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783934-DSC03071-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783934" class="wp-caption-text">乐师们在幕后演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>剧组盛情奉献 嘉宾赞赏文化传承</strong></h2>
<p>每一幕的剧情结束之后，主持人李卢莲馨都会盛装登台，介绍下一幕的剧情，同时分享剧组筹备演出的辛劳。</p>
<p>她谈到，侨声音乐剧社13年前曾演过《帝女花》全剧，如今再次演出，可谓“驾轻就熟，精益求精”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14783936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783936-DSC03105.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783936-DSC03105-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783936" class="wp-caption-text">主持人李卢莲馨介绍剧情。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14783942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783942" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783942-DSC03173.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783942-DSC03173-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783942" class="wp-caption-text">第四幕，周世显（伍国荣饰）和长平公主（雷玉霞饰）重逢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>剧组成员皆为义工，包括演员、乐师、搬运组等等，总计有80多人，包括从纽约赶来的20多位粤曲艺术家和爱好者。大家辛苦筹备几个月，才得以奉献这场大型演出。</p>
<p>李卢莲馨还感谢观众，像“水和阳光”一样，滋润着侨声不断成长。</p>
<p>侨声音乐剧社两位社长黄国威和李伍碧香也登台感谢观众、赞助商和华埠社团的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14783938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783938-DSC03122.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783938-DSC03122-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783938" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社两位社长黄国威（左）和李伍碧香感谢观众支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14783941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783941-DSC03150.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783941-DSC03150-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783941" class="wp-caption-text">义工在剧场外检票。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14783940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14783940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783940-DSC03143.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14783940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14783940-DSC03143-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14783940" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影，左起：社长黄国威、爱默森学院副校长Margaret Ann Ings、社长李伍碧香、主持人李卢莲馨、中华公所主席雷国辉、侨声成员梁永基。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉称赞，侨声音乐剧社成立87年以来，不仅为侨社提供了娱乐，也栽培了很多传统文化、粤曲艺术方面的人才。</p>
<p>爱默森学院主管社区关系的副校长英斯（Margaret Ann Ings）亦在现场观赏演出。她说，看到这么多观众出来欣赏演出，理解到剧组义工们奉献的意义，希望这样的演出传统能够每年持续下去。◇</p>
