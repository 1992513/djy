<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“慢游嘉义”官方LINE推好康 发送夏日专属福利</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784428-854009-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“咩咩上树萌宠乐园”之1。（嘉义县政府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年06月08日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）嘉义县文化观光局为推广数位智慧旅游及在地特色景点，透过“慢游嘉义”官方LINE发送夏日专属福利。嘉义最新的玩水打卡圣地“芯浪滑板冲浪公园”日前推出“买大送小”方案，文观局携手园区推出加码好礼，购入“芯浪”买大送小联票并加入“慢游嘉义”官方LINE，入园即可获赠价值240元的沁凉冰沙兑换券，限量2000份送完为止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14784432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14784432" style="width: 459px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784432-854013.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14784432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784432-854013-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="459" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14784432" class="wp-caption-text">“芯浪滑板冲浪公园”之1。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芯浪滑板冲浪公园主打“精致水上度假村”路线，引进国际级造浪设施，游客可在专业教练带领下无限次畅玩，园区更打造刺激的8米高综合滑水道（含皮艇等6种体验），以及专为孩童设计的“2米高亲子滑道”。玩累了能在充满海岛风情的沉水式包厢与漂浮躺床休憩，轻松拍出绝美渡假照片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14784434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14784434" style="width: 476px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784434-854015.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14784434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784434-854015-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="476" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14784434" class="wp-caption-text">“芯浪滑板冲浪公园”之3。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14784435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14784435" style="width: 463px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784435-854016.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14784435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784435-854016-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="463" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14784435" class="wp-caption-text">“芯浪滑板冲浪公园”之4。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>园区另推出“三岁以下且100公分以内”孩童免费入园的友善优惠，绝对是亲子家庭的夏日首选。</p>
<p>文观局指出，芯浪的门票皆为“水陆联票”，游客可以享受水上设施，接着转身到隔壁的“咩咩上树萌宠乐园”与小动物互动，两处景点都坐落于嘉义羊角艺术村内，紧邻故宫南院，距离高铁嘉义站仅需5分钟车程，交通便利，一站满足大人小孩对夏日旅游的期待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14784429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14784429" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784429-854010.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14784429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784429-854010-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="524" b="349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14784429" class="wp-caption-text">“咩咩上树萌宠乐园”之2。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14784430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14784430" style="width: 525px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784430-854011.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14784430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784430-854011-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="525" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14784430" class="wp-caption-text">“咩咩上树萌宠乐园”之3。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14784431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14784431" style="width: 513px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784431-854012.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14784431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14784431-854012-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="513" b="342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14784431" class="wp-caption-text">“咩咩上树萌宠乐园”之4。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>只要完成简单三步骤，就能获得“慢游嘉义LINE独家限定优惠”。</p>
<p>① 购买“芯浪滑板冲浪公园”买大送小联票（网址：https://reurl.cc/L2xGy4）</p>
<p>② 加入“慢游嘉义”官方LINE并领取专属优惠券（网址：https://lin.ee/p85nTGg）</p>
<p>③ 持以上2券入园即可获得价值240元冰沙兑换券1张（可兑换2杯）。每个LINE账号限领一份，数量有限领完为止。</p>
<p>更多“芯浪滑板冲浪公园”的票价与设施介绍可至官方网站查询（https://www.xinflowsurfingpark.com/Tickets），或致电服务专线 05- 3621652洽询。</p>
<p>文观局提醒，活动相关规范依官方公告为准，并保留活动最终解释、调整与变更之权利。欢迎民众拿起手机搜寻并加入“慢游嘉义”官方LINE账号，一键领取独家优惠，开启最精彩的嘉义夏日消暑之旅！若想发掘更多嘉义暑假亲子景点与游程可至“嘉义县亲子旅游官网”探索。◇</p>
