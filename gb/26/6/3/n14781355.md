<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>千年神木遗迹 漫游阿里山森林铁道秘境</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14781362-8bfa0ac40edbd8a88b55ef399892d6a5.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>提到阿里山，许多人首先想到的是日出、云海、神木与森林铁路。没错！但是除了耳熟能详的景点之外，阿里山其实还藏着许多值得细细品味的森林秘境与充满故事的铁道车站。</p><p>这次来到阿里山森林游乐区，我们特别安排了一趟结合森林步道、樱花美景与森林铁路的轻旅行，在山林间感受阿里山独有的宁静与魅力。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div style="max-width:554px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14781364-468a4a4103cf0cfe44fae3e025226bf8.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener b="415"/></picture></a><figcaption>旧月台遗址。（Lana提供）</figcaption></figure></div><p>穿过旧月台遗址、走过木栈桥后，再沿着阶梯往上走约三分钟，便可抵达步道终点——水山巨木。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p>离开水山巨木后，我们来到沼平公园。三月正值阿里山樱花季，园区内樱花陆续绽放，虽然部分花况尚未达到满开，但沿途依然能欣赏到粉嫩花朵点缀山林的美景。</p><div><figure style="max-width:554px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14781365-3e40308ae6a71148679ecd9b67602028.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" 探访神木遗址</h3><p>午后，我们搭乘阿里山森林铁路神木线前往神木站。短短约七分钟的车程，窗外尽是高山森林景色，让人充分感受百年森林铁路的魅力。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>抵达神木站后，车站旁就是著名的神木遗址。昔日被誉为拥有三千年树龄的阿里山神木，曾经是无数游客慕名前来朝圣的地标。虽然神木已于多年前倒伏，但庞大的树身依然静静地躺卧在森林之中，化作珍贵的历史遗迹，向人们诉说着阿里山悠久的岁月与自然生命的轮回。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_5"><div
