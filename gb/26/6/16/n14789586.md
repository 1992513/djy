<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿梦舞团公演 主题“昇”融汇东西方舞艺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789574-DSC03482-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月13日晚上，波士顿地区颇有名气的“梦舞团”（CJT Dream Dance）在麻州勒星顿基督学院（Lexington Christian Academy）礼堂举办年度公演。芭蕾舞“月光下的舞会”拉开演出序幕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年06月16日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨勒星顿镇报导）6月13日晚上，波士顿地区颇有名气的“梦舞团”（CJT Dream Dance）举办年度公演，在麻州勒星顿基督学院（Lexington Christian Academy）礼堂，与不同族裔观众分享他们对东、西方舞蹈艺术的诠释。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14789575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789575" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789575-DSC03501.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789575-DSC03501-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789575" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团举办年度公演，展现风格独特的舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14789573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789573" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789573-DSC03478.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789573-DSC03478-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789573" class="wp-caption-text">主持人介绍节目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场由梦舞团创办人蔡君柔老师带领的演出，以“昇”为主题，上演了34个节目，包括芭蕾舞、中国舞、现代舞、流行舞等不同风格。登场的舞蹈演员，亦有老、中、青、幼不同年龄的舞团学员。现场观众也是非常热情，不时爆发欢呼及掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14789576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789576-DSC03508.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789576-DSC03508-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789576" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团举办年度公演，展现风格独特的舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14789577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789577-DSC03555.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789577-DSC03555-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789577" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团学员表演获奖舞蹈“蝶”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>梦舞团由蔡君柔在2018年创办，一直教授古今中外不同风格的舞蹈，8年以来持续发展壮大。“我们要秀出所有舞蹈的特色。以中国舞的内敛和呼吸来展现内在跟情绪，现代舞的情绪张力跟延展来表现他们的深情，流行舞的节奏来展现他们当下真实的自己。”蔡君柔说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14789578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789578-DSC03568.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789578-DSC03568-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789578" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团举办年度公演，展现风格独特的舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14789579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789579-DSC03574.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789579-DSC03574-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789579" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团举办年度公演，展现风格独特的舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于参加演出的学员，蔡君柔笑言，大家这些年来真的进步很多。例如，今年一名中国舞学生获得了全美舞蹈比赛波士顿区的冠军，其他多名学生也获得了评审特别奖项。评审老师们还特别就这些学生的精彩表现而发表感言。</p>
<p>“我希望可以继续延续，向东方介绍西方艺术的美，向西方介绍我们东方的这个底蕴，这一直是我们的目标。”蔡君柔说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14789580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789580-DSC03605.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789580-DSC03605-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789580" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团举办年度公演，展现风格独特的舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14789581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789581-DSC03632.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789581-DSC03632-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789581" class="wp-caption-text">流行舞学员们全员登台，表演“梦之舞”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周六晚的演出包含了东西方多种风格的节目，以芭蕾舞“月光下的舞会”拉开序幕，接下来有流行舞“向光而上”，现代舞“悼念”，中国舞“蝶”等34场不同的舞蹈。在最后的3个节目中，梦舞团的中国舞学生、现代舞学生和流行舞学生分别全体登场，同台共舞，感染得观众也一同拍打节奏，将气氛推入高潮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14789582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789582-DSC03644.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789582-DSC03644-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789582" class="wp-caption-text">梦舞团创办人蔡君柔（中）感谢大家的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14789583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789583-DSC03649.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789583-DSC03649-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789583" class="wp-caption-text">舞蹈学员献花感谢老师。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大波士顿中华文化协会（GBCCA）前会长任继敏也是参与演出的舞蹈学员之一。她称赞，蔡君柔老师对各种舞蹈都非常了解，教学非常细致，帮助学员体会舞蹈的内涵。“她非常有耐心，而且又温柔。”任继敏说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14789584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14789584" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789584-DSC03657.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14789584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14789584-DSC03657-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14789584" class="wp-caption-text">演出参与者欢乐合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她还说，自己从小就喜欢舞蹈，接受艺术的熏陶。“不想停下来，因为我觉得可以通过舞蹈放松自己的心身，更主要就是可以通过舞蹈表达自己的情感，也给所有的人带来更多的快乐。”任继敏说。◇</p>
