<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世足派对多元融合 林富桂领军展现魅力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790095-01-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>西南区世足委员会主席暨西南管委会理事林富桂（Dawn Lin）亲自召集规划西南区世足观球派对，感谢有9个社团共同赞助举办。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年06月17日讯】（大纪元记者孙玉玟休斯顿报导）随着2026年FIFA世界杯足球赛在休斯顿火热开踢，西南管会于6月14日在Hilton Houston Westchase盛大举办“世足观球派对”（Watch Party）。本次活动由西南区世足委员会主席暨西南管理委员会理事林富桂（Dawn Lin）亲自召集规划，携手Sharpstown扶轮社、中华文化服务中心、台商会、韩国商会及菲律宾商会等九个多元文化组织联合打造，成功吸引了大批社区民众与政要代表共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14790096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790096" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790096-02.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790096-02-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790096" class="wp-caption-text">西南区世足委员会主席林富桂（左2）接受Sharpstown扶轮社致赠的锦旗以感谢其为社区的贡献。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动当天是世界杯德国对战库拉索（Cura?ao）的精彩开幕赛，活动地点Westchase区距离现场正在进行的比赛场地Reliance Center仅17.5英里，现场气氛热烈、座无虚席。主席林富桂深受参与者的热情所感动，并对社区展现的凝聚力感到鼓舞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14790097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790097" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790097-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790097-03-600x543.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="543" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790097" class="wp-caption-text">林富桂主席和世华美南分会部分姐妹合影。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14790098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790098" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790098-04.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790098-04-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790098" class="wp-caption-text">林富桂和东方之星表演团体合影。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林富桂介绍，这是一场强调多元文化融合、堪称“空前绝后”的盛会。活动于上午11点由舞狮表演拉开序幕，随后登场的SREEPADAM印度舞团、厄瓜多尔民俗舞，以及东方之星带来的东方风格时装秀，还有Maila Hula Hut夏威夷舞蹈，精彩不停歇。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14790099" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790099" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790099-05.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790099" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790099-05-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790099" class="wp-caption-text">西南区世足委员会主席林富桂和厄瓜多尔表演者以及社团代表合影。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14790105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790105-11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790105-11-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790105" class="wp-caption-text">宴会厅现场在二边准备大银幕让大家方便观球赛。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场的多国美食小吃摊位更是受到欢迎。民众得以一边观看现场直播，一边品尝各国美食，包含台湾的刈包、菲律宾肉菜春卷、韩国年糕香肠串、越南酥脆夏卷、印度咖喱角，以及拉丁风情的起司薄饼、德国椒盐圈饼与美式热狗披萨，还有喝不完的无酒精饮料，呈现休斯顿西南区多元文化魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14790100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790100-06.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790100-06-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790100" class="wp-caption-text">林富桂（右后）和台商会代表共同庆祝此盛会。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14790101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790101" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790101-07.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790101-07-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790101" class="wp-caption-text">现场的多国美食小吃摊位，民众得以一边观看现场直播的震撼赛事，一边品尝世界级美味。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林富桂指出，本次观球派对只是西南管理委员会支持世足赛事的重要一环。在派对举行前一周（6月7日），委员会便已成功举办“首踢”（First Kick）活动，并特别邀请到休斯顿市长亲临现场踢出第一球，为赛事注入官方强力支持。</p>
<p>为了营造浓厚的世足氛围并活络地方经济，委员会在西南区主要街道悬挂了48个参赛国的国旗，更设立专门网站（www.hostcityhouston.com）提供西南区的餐饮、文化与必访资讯。此外，委员会也提供“世足专案补助金”（business grants）给社群内厂商与组织，鼓励办理相关活动，其中台湾商会亦是受惠社团之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14790102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790102" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790102-08.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790102-08-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790102" class="wp-caption-text">西南区世足委员会主席林富桂（右3）和表演舞者和代表社区合影。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14790103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790103-09.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790103-09-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790103" class="wp-caption-text">民众为正在为球赛欢呼。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14790104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14790104" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790104-10.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14790104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14790104-10-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14790104" class="wp-caption-text">Maila Hula Hut夏威夷风情舞。（孙玉玟/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林富桂强调，此次活动的成功不仅仅是一场派对，更是一个促进各社群相互联谊与认识，实现了跨文化、跨国界与跨种族的合作。</p>
<p>林富桂期待以此次经验为基础，未来能与休斯顿各商会及非营利组织建立更长期的长期合作关系，借此茁壮区与区之间的友谊，并深耕经济与商机。最后，她也热情预告了管委会的下一个大型活动——将于6月28日盛大登场的“Liberty Fest”，届时将有精彩的烟火秀与知名表演者Paul Wall的演出，期盼社区民众持续关注与参与。</p>
