<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台南美术馆二馆顶楼 光影与茶的空间</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736163-840431_medium-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月07日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）台南美术馆二馆顶楼一走上来，不是先看展，而是先感觉到光。白色结构撑起整片玻璃屋顶，阳光透进来，落在地面与桌面上，一格一格移动。人走在里面，不用找角度，随手一拍就是画面。空间很开，声音却不多。有人站着拍照，有人坐着发呆，也有人只是看光影慢慢变。这里不像展场，更像一个让人停下来的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14736167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14736167" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736167-840435_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14736167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736167-840435_medium.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14736167" class="wp-caption-text">光影洒下来，每个角落都能拍，难怪成为国内外旅客口袋名单。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>透明庙仔与茶室 拍照也变成一种节奏</h2>
<p>中央的“透明庙仔”是视觉焦点，用玻璃与结构组成，没有传统庙宇的厚重感，反而轻盈。桌面上摆着各种玻璃小物，像水果、像糖果，在光线下发亮，让人忍不住靠近拍。走到茶室，气氛又变得更安静。每一桌的摆设不同，搭配花草茶或气泡饮，简单却有设计感。窗外是台南的城市线条，低矮屋顶一片延伸，很多人一坐下，就待很久。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14736168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14736168" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736168-840436_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14736168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736168-840436_medium.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14736168" class="wp-caption-text">窗边图像结合台南意象，老街与工艺被重新画进现代空间。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14736165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14736165" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736165-840433_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14736165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736165-840433_medium.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14736165" class="wp-caption-text">每一桌都不一样，摆盘都有不同的作品。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14736170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14736170" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736170-840438_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14736170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736170-840438_medium.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14736170" class="wp-caption-text">细节更迷人，小小玻璃器物，像糖果一样可爱。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14736177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14736177" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736177-840447_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14736177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14736177-840447_medium.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="550" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14736177" class="wp-caption-text">花草茶搭配玻璃壶，简单却很有仪式感。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>两种走法</h2>
<p>看展或直接上来，多数游客会买全票，从一楼一路逛到顶楼，慢慢看展、再走进茶室休息。整栋建筑的动线完整，也能把展览与空间一起看完。</p>
<p>但也有人只为顶楼而来。花50元玩扭蛋，手上绑一条绿色手环，就能直接上楼，不用走完整个展馆。</p>
<p>对想拍照、喝茶的人来说，是另一种更快的方式，而且打卡还赠送可爱爆表的红龟粿。</p>
