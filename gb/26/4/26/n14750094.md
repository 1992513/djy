<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西雅图海湾人胜达拉斯 创主场20场不败纪录</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750116-1bba0881ab3660d03e80b0e7998ce2bc-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月26日讯】（大纪元记者Richard Xu美国西雅图报导）2026年4月25日晚，西雅图海湾人队在流明球场主场迎战达拉斯FC。海湾人队以2:1获胜，创下了对达拉斯队主场20场（15 胜 5 平）不败纪录。这在竞争激烈的 MLS 联盟赛中亦属罕见。</p>
<p>这场比赛也是海湾人中场克里斯蒂安·罗尔丹（Cristian Roldan）的重要里程碑之夜。赛前，球会为他举行俱乐部第400场出赛纪念仪式，罗尔丹携家人接受纪念球衣，全场球迷鼓掌致意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750115" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750115-ed2014e90e1b127b3cb909e25443ec4f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750115-ed2014e90e1b127b3cb909e25443ec4f-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750115" class="wp-caption-text">罗尔丹（Cristian Roldan ）赛前迎来职业生涯第400场俱乐部出赛里程碑，携家人接受纪念球衣致敬，全场球迷鼓掌致意（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>比赛开局，达拉斯反客为主，连续在海湾人禁区内制造威胁，场面一度倾向客队。然而率先破门的仍是主队。</p>
<p>第15分钟，后卫杰克逊·拉根（Jackson Ragen）从后场送出长传，找到右路的莫里斯。莫里斯（Jordan Morris）快速处理后将球送到中路，费雷拉（Jesús Ferreira）接球起脚，皮球经折射入网，帮助海湾人取得1:0领先。面对老东家，费雷拉（Jesús Ferreira）没有庆祝，而是高举双手致意。</p>
<p>第30分钟，海湾人扩大比分。斯奈德·布鲁内尔（Snyder Brunell）送出直塞，撕开达拉斯防线。莫里斯（Jordan Morris）前插形成单刀，面对门将迈克尔·科洛迪（Michael Collodi）冷静推射远角破门，将比分改写为2:0。莫里斯（Jordan Morris）也完成一传一射的关键表现。</p>
<p>达拉斯在第40分钟追回一球。诺兰·诺里斯（Nolan Norris）在角球进攻中摆脱防守，头球破门，将比分追成1:2。上半场补时阶段，海湾人门将安德鲁·托马斯（Andrew Thomas）作出关键扑救，力保球队带着领先优势进入中场休息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750117" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750117" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750117-ec96202639c64a297f0242021250a03f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750117" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750117-ec96202639c64a297f0242021250a03f-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750117" class="wp-caption-text">费雷拉（Jesús Ferreira）前场持球推进，在达拉斯后卫夹防下加速突破。（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750118" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750118" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750118-456ea5c1d4eef3dca60f24ef99011782.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750118-456ea5c1d4eef3dca60f24ef99011782-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750118" class="wp-caption-text">费雷拉（Jesús Ferreira）禁区内把握机会果断起脚，皮球经折射入网，攻入面对旧主的关键进球。（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750119" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750119" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750119-f8a629feecce384baa3d4d01f47e4801.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750119-f8a629feecce384baa3d4d01f47e4801-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750119" class="wp-caption-text">莫里斯（Jordan Morris）高高跃起争顶，在空中对抗中与对手激烈拼抢。（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750122" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750122" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750122-9d49cdb1cbd687e569a44cd8ef552431.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750122-9d49cdb1cbd687e569a44cd8ef552431-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750122" class="wp-caption-text">托马斯（Andrew Thomas）上半场补时阶段飞身扑救，单手将变线射门挡出，力保海湾人领先优势。（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下半场，海湾人原本有机会锁定胜局。第53分钟，保罗·罗斯罗克（Paul Rothrock）左路突破制造犯规，为球队赢得点球。但费雷拉（Jesús Ferreira）主罚的射门角度和力度不足，被科洛迪（Michael Collodi）判断对方向后扑出。</p>
<p>此后比赛进入拉锯。海湾人逐渐放慢节奏，通过控球与防守组织限制达拉斯的进攻空间，没有再给对手太多明确机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750124" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750124-93ee40d654cc6a132c71ca276150c413.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750124-93ee40d654cc6a132c71ca276150c413-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750124" class="wp-caption-text">穆索夫斯基（Danny Musovski）禁区前沿横向带球寻找空当起脚，但未能把握机会。（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750125" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750125-6513f5879c72ae8752125fe06a0efa99.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750125" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750125-6513f5879c72ae8752125fe06a0efa99-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750125" class="wp-caption-text">罗斯罗克（Paul Rothrock）下半场门前高高跃起头球攻门，但角度过正，被门将没收。（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750127" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750127" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750127-81e18e0224eb1bef064a008cc63b5b36.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750127-81e18e0224eb1bef064a008cc63b5b36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750127" class="wp-caption-text">托马斯（Andrew Thomas）高高跃起抢先出击，在人群中稳稳摘下皮球，化解对手空中威胁。（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终，西雅图海湾人以2:1守住胜局，赛季战绩提升至6胜1平1负，并延续主场对达拉斯的强势纪录。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750133" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750133-a95fe966bb72088ad4ae9dcf4a86f3dd.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14750133" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750133-a95fe966bb72088ad4ae9dcf4a86f3dd-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750133" class="wp-caption-text">终场哨响后，主场看板亮起“SOUNDERS WIN”，海湾人球员相互致意庆祝胜利，达拉斯球员神情落寞。（Richard／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自2011年以来，达拉斯从未在流明球场取胜，这项延续多年的纪录，也让两队每一次在西雅图的交锋更添看点。</p>
