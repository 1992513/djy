<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>老牛安养中心 土库青农林家良的三头水牛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750867-844678_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>经常会有网友慕名而来，为了探望老牛。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月26日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）走进这个园区，不会有什么“来到景点”的感觉。地上是黑泥，旁边是绿色的玉米田，围栏里三头牛低头吃着东西，还有四只黑色的流浪狗。</p>
<p>很普通，但待久一点，就会觉得不一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750868" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750868-844651_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750868-844651_medium.webp" alt="" width="448" b="797" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750868" class="wp-caption-text">三头老牛在泥地里对望，一头直接泡进水里降温，夏天的日子就这样慢慢过。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750869" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750869-844655_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750869-844655_medium.webp" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750869" class="wp-caption-text">泥地、阳光、围栏，三头老牛各自找位置休息，没有工作，只剩生活。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一天180公斤</h2>
<p>不是养牛，是在陪它们生活，云林土库青农林家良从2019年开始做这个老牛安养中心。</p>
<p>现在园区已离世二头老牛，还有三头牛，一头一天要吃60公斤牧草，加起来就是180公斤。</p>
<p>他自己会去捡甘蔗叶、割牧草，除非不得已，吃从彰化福兴买回来的草之外，都是林家良一个人去张罗的，全年无休。</p>
<p>他讲这件事的时候语气很平常，好像只是日常的一部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750870" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750870-844652_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750870-844652_medium.webp" alt="" width="449" b="598" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750870" class="wp-caption-text">牧草一把一把长，却永远不够吃，一头牛一天60公斤，是这里最真实的日常。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750871" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750871-844653_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750871-844653_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750871" class="wp-caption-text">“伴它终老”四个字写在看板上，这里不是牧场，是老牛最后的家。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>老牛也会跑</h2>
<p>而且跑起来很快，很多人以为老牛动作慢，其实不是。</p>
<p>林家良说，有一次牛跑出去，里长还帮忙广播请他去带回来。</p>
<p>“跑很快，追不到。”久了之后，整个地方的人都知道，如果看到有老水牛在路上，大概就是他这里的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750872" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750872-844658_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750872-844658_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750872" class="wp-caption-text">林家良站在一旁喂草，一口一口慢慢吃，这里没有赶时间这件事。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>偷吃玉米</h2>
<p>被抓到还是让它吃，采访那天，刚好看到一头牛在偷吃旁边种的玉米。</p>
<p>大家还在看他会怎么处理，他只看一眼说：“让它吃啦。”吃剩的，他再拿去卖。</p>
<p>但下一秒，牛开始去顶旁边的帆布看板，他马上跑过去制止。“那个不行。”很简单的界线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750873" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750873-844668_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750873-844668_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750873" class="wp-caption-text">被抓到还是让它吃，采访那天，刚好看到一头牛在偷吃旁边种的玉米。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750874" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750874-844670_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750874-844670_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750874" class="wp-caption-text">老牛安养中心是林家良把稻子卖掉，用心盖起来的。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>三头牛、四只狗</h2>
<p>晚上会一起睡，园区里除了三头老牛，还有四只浪浪狗。</p>
<p>林家良有装监视器，晚上也会看它们的状况。</p>
<p>有一次，他看到两只狗直接睡在牛身上。“就这样躺着睡，也没掉下来。”他自己讲到这里也笑了。</p>
<p>那画面不用看到，也能想像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750875-844660_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750875-844660_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="590" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750875" class="wp-caption-text">夜晚的监视器画面里，狗趴在牛背上，它们一起过夜，也一起守着这个地方。(（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750876" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750876-844656_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750876-844656_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750876" class="wp-caption-text">两只流浪狗守在一旁，和牛一起过日子，彼此早已习惯对方的存在。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>有人只记得这里</h2>
<p>看完牛，就安心了，有一位家住二崙乡失智的阿伯，一直跟儿子说要来看牛。儿子上网查，才找到这里。</p>
<p>来了之后，阿伯在牛旁边待了半小时，没有特别做什么，就看着。最后很满足地回家。</p>
<p>这段话他讲得很淡，但听的人都安静了一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750877" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750877-844654_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750877-844654_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750877" class="wp-caption-text">围栏内两头牛静静看着镜头，像在打量来访的人，也像在等下一餐。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750878" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750878-844657_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750878-844657_medium.webp" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750878" class="wp-caption-text">老牛低头啃草，长角与鬃毛在阳光下闪着光，岁月都写在它们身上。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>对牛弹琴</h2>
<p>真的有人来，每年6月21日，这里会办一个活动，叫“对牛弹琴”。</p>
<p>不是说说而已，是真的有人来演奏。以前有小提琴、二胡。</p>
<p>今年是谁，他也不知道。</p>
<p>“反正会有人来。”牛听不听得懂，好像也没那么重要。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750879" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750879-844659_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750879-844659_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="717" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750879" class="wp-caption-text">有人拉着大提琴，在牛舍旁静静演奏，牛抬头看了一眼，又低头吃草。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14750880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750880" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750880-844663_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750880-844663_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750880" class="wp-caption-text">两头水牛在阴影下觅食，烈日很大，但它们已不再需要赶路。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>老了 就不用再工作</h2>
<p>林家良说，他不会让这些牛再去耕田。它们来这里，就是终老。</p>
<p>其中有一头，是在水里玩，后来离开的。</p>
<p>他讲这件事的时候，只说了一句：“就是终老。”没有多说。</p>
<p>这个地方没有什么华丽的东西。但有三头牛，每天吃草、发呆、偶尔偷吃玉米。</p>
<p>有四只狗，晚上会跑去牛身上睡觉。</p>
<p>还有一个人，每天在这里，看着它们。日子过得很慢，但很安静。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14750881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14750881" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750881-844661_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14750881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750881-844661_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14750881" class="wp-caption-text">低头吃着地上的番薯，这些不再下田的水牛，慢慢学着过另一种日子。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
