<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台外长出访非洲友邦 赖清德致词影片获热烈掌声</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14750949-2604270341452378.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
