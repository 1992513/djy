<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全球不到三千只却在这里筑巢 记录东方白鹳</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751252-844882-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>电塔顶端筑起的巢，成为东方白鹳一家育雏的家园，也让这片农田成为生态焦点。（高铭杉提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月27日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬云林报导）在云林二崙的一条防汛道路旁，最近多了一排静静守候的人。没有喧哗，只有一支支架好的长镜头，对着远方的电塔。大家的目标很一致——那里，有一窝东方白鹳。东方白鹳是国际保育类鸟种，全球数量不到三千只。能在台湾看到已不容易，更别说筑巢、产卵、育雏。这次在云林二崙电塔上的这一窝，从年初传出消息后，就引起不少关注。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14751250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751250" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751250-844880.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751250-844880-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751250" class="wp-caption-text">防汛道路上人龙绵延，专业长镜头对准高空电塔，见证全球仅存约三千只的东方白鹳在台繁殖。（高铭杉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14751249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751249" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751249-844879.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751249" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751249-844879-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751249" class="wp-caption-text">摄影人沿着堤防排队架设器材，耐心守候电塔上的白鹳一家，纪录珍贵的生命时刻。（高铭杉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14751264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751264" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751264-844894.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14751264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751264-844894-600x801.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751264" class="wp-caption-text">原本荒凉的堤坊，最近热闹许多。（李健竹提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李健竹的等待　从好奇变成日常笔芯雕刻工艺家李健竹是常客之一，他说，一开始只是听朋友提起，没想到来到现场后，一待就是好几个小时。业余摄影家高铭杉几乎天天报到，从清晨、甚至到傍晚。“它们变化不大，但每天都有一点不一样。”他说。电塔顶端的巢，是用枯枝慢慢堆叠起来的。从地面看只是一团影子，但透过镜头，可以看到亲鸟不断叼回树枝，调整巢的形状。有时还会展翅站立，维持平衡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14751261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751261-844891.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751261-844891-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751261" class="wp-caption-text">巢中三只幼鸟逐渐长大，依偎在高空巢位，画面温馨而珍贵。（李健竹提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14751263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751263" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751263-844893.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14751263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751263-844893.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751263" class="wp-caption-text">幼鸟排遗，这也是鸟观家喜欢拍摄的画面。（李健竹提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三只幼鸟　慢慢长大年初产下的四颗蛋，有三颗顺利孵化。幼鸟一开始只有白色绒毛，如今已能站立，还会张嘴讨食。喂食的画面，是许多摄影人最期待的时刻。亲鸟低头，幼鸟抬头，一来一往，很安静，但很有感觉。现场的人，多半带着脚架和长镜头。有人坐着，有人站着，有人撑伞遮阳。从不同县市开车过来，就为了这一窝白鹳。器材排成一整排，画面也成了另一种风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14751252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751252-844882.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751252-844882-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751252" class="wp-caption-text">电塔顶端筑起的巢，成为东方白鹳一家育雏的家园，也让这片农田成为生态焦点。（高铭杉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14751253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751253-844883.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751253-844883-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751253" class="wp-caption-text">守护巢中幼鸟，雏鸟逐渐长大，展现旺盛生命力，画面温馨动人。（高铭杉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14751257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751257-844887.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751257-844887-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751257" class="wp-caption-text">摄影师肩扛长镜头徒步前行，为了拍摄白鹳，不辞辛劳深入田野。（高铭杉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>人造电塔　成了它们的家有网路新闻报导后，知道的人变多了。现场人数一度增加，但大家大致保持距离，也会互相提醒不要靠太近、不喧哗。让白鹳能够安心待在原地。白鹳没有选择树，而是选择电塔筑巢。这里视野开阔，也相对安全。对人来说是设施，对它们来说却是一个可以生活的地方。人与自然，就这样交错在一起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14751260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751260-844890.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751260-844890-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751260" class="wp-caption-text">亲鸟守护巢中幼鸟，时而鸣叫、时而整理羽毛，展现自然界的亲情互动。（李健竹提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14751259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751259-844889.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751259-844889-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751259" class="wp-caption-text">东方白鹳站上电塔筑巢，以枯枝细心加固巢体，在高空中打造育雏的家。（李健竹提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14751251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751251-844881.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751251-844881-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751251" class="wp-caption-text">摄影师们顶着烈日守候，只为捕捉亲鸟喂食幼鸟的瞬间，每一张画面都来之不易。（高铭杉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每天一点变化　慢慢看见生命高铭杉说，他最喜欢的是幼鸟长大的过程。“一开始很小，现在已经会站，再过一段时间，可能就会飞了。”这些变化不快，但很清楚。这些画面，不是一次拍到的，而是一天天累积出来的。有人拍喂食，有人拍展翅，有人只拍巢。每个人看到的都不一样，但都是同一件事。在这条防汛道路上，时间过得很慢。风声、鸟声，还有偶尔的快门声。大家只是等，等亲鸟回来，等幼鸟抬头，等一个刚好出现的瞬间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14751255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14751255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751255-844885.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14751255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14751255-844885-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14751255" class="wp-caption-text">巢中幼鸟探头张望，在高空中学习成长，象征新生命的延续。（高铭杉提供）</figcaption></figure>
