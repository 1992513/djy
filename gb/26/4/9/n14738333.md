<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA：雷霆、马刺和凯尔特人最具“冠军相”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738460-GettyImages-2270486807.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
