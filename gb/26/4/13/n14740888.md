<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>梁朝伟抵台参与大师课、跑宣传 刘嘉玲连袂同行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740893-2604131038211487.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
