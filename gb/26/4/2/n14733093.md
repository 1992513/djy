<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“儿童节”童心送暖 一日小站长发餐关怀寒士</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733099-839722-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>园长带领孩童将物资送到寒士手中。（人安基金会桃园平安站 提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月02日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）在儿童节前夕，2日人安基金会桃园平安站邀请童安园幼儿园的学童担任“一日站长”。在老师的带领下，小朋友们合唱给予祝福、发放餐食，以实际行动为寒士送上温暖与关怀，现场洋溢着温馨的气氛。</p>
<figure id="14733094" aria-describedby="caption-14733094" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733094-839717-450x255.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733094-839717-450x255.jpg" alt="人安基金会与民族幼儿园大合照。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14733094" class="wp-caption-text">人安基金会与民族幼儿园大合照。（人安基金会桃园平安站 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一名85岁、独自生活的李阿嬷前来领餐。由于年事已高、无力工作，她平日仅靠捡拾回收物维持微薄生计。</p>
<figure id="14733095" aria-describedby="caption-14733095" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733095-839718-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733095-839718-450x253.jpg" alt="孩童将物资递给寒士。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14733095" class="wp-caption-text">孩童将物资递给寒士。（人安基金会桃园平安站 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李阿嬷感动地表示：“很感谢人安基金会，让我可以来这里领三餐，不用担心饿肚子。今天还看到这么多可爱的小朋友来帮忙，心里真的很开心。”</p>
<figure id="14733096" aria-describedby="caption-14733096" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733096-839719-450x181.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733096-839719-450x181.jpg" alt="孩童用声音温暖寒士。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14733096" class="wp-caption-text">孩童用声音温暖寒士。（人安基金会桃园平安站 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>童安园幼儿园陈园长表示，很高兴能在儿童节前夕带着孩子们参与这样富有教育意义的活动。孩子走出校园参与公益，关怀弱势、同理他人，也理解资源得来不易，学会“惜福”。</p>
<figure id="14733097" aria-describedby="caption-14733097" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733097-839720-450x231.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733097-839720-450x231.jpg" alt="民族幼儿园孩童合唱。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14733097" class="wp-caption-text">民族幼儿园孩童合唱。（人安基金会桃园平安站 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>人安基金会桃园平安站常年提供防饥、防寒避暑、防病、工作转介及三节关怀等服务。人生沉浮，在所难免，现正进行“寒士庆端午”，邀您伸手济贫，陪伴寒士，助寒士失去的人生重新启航。</p>
<figure id="14733098" aria-describedby="caption-14733098" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733098-839721-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733098-839721-450x253.jpg" alt="孩童递上苹果并给予祝福。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14733098" class="wp-caption-text">孩童递上苹果并给予祝福。（人安基金会桃园平安站 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每月支持平安站600元，平安站常年服务能细水长流，爱心专线：（03）-3348325。划拨账号：19859690（户名：人安基金会）。◇</p>
