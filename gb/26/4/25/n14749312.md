<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】流光溢彩 2026 Nissan Murano Platinum AWD</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749340-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月25日讯】去年，Nissan推出了全新设计的第四代Murano，重新定义豪华与科技的完美结合。这款车不仅以高端设计和先进科技吸引目光，更为日常通勤与长途旅行注入新意。全新Murano以优雅的外观、精致的内饰、先进的驾驶辅助系统，从竞争者中脱颖而出。今年，Nissan再次让我们测试2026年款Murano Platinum。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749315-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749315-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749315" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Nissan Murano Platinum。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新Murano的外观设计以“前卫优雅”为核心，呈现出无缝、稳固且干净的视觉效果。车身宽度增加超过2.6英寸，搭配优雅外扩的后轮拱，展现出强烈的力量感。前脸设计极为简洁，超薄“水晶立方”LED头灯位于光泽黑色饰条两侧，与格栅分离，呈现出Nissan标志性的V-motion图形。尾部设计同样令人印象深刻，尾闸更为干净俐落，后雨刷巧妙置于后车窗上方，排气管隐藏于防撞器下方。横跨车尾的LED尾灯有时尚感，夜晚点亮起时更形独特，一看便知这车是Murano。近密封式21英寸轮圈（Platinum等级专属）进一步强化了车辆的气势，而且也减少了风阻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749318-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749318-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749318" class="wp-caption-text">“水晶立方”LED头灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>进入Murano的内舱，以极简主义为设计主调，水平设计主题让空间更显开阔。标准配备的双12.3英寸显示萤幕无缝融入缝线材质的环境中，触感柔软的材质遍布座椅与中控台，展现出高级质感。名为“Murano Glass”的仪表板饰板灵感来自威尼斯玻璃工艺，融入色彩变换图形与纹理，与光线互动，彰显精湛工艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749319-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_14.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749319-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_14-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749319" class="wp-caption-text">Murano的内舱。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车内装有64色氛围照明、全景天窗、加热/冷却/按摩前座椅以及加热方向盘，并且与Lexus有类似的“自动”座椅温度调节，让乘客提升了奢华体验。后排乘客同样备受礼遇，后门窗遮阳帘、加热后座椅以及更宽敞的膝部空间，得益于前座椅更薄设计，让长途旅行更舒适。Murano首次采用的Zero Gravity后座设计提供更佳支撑力，后车门开启角度更大，开启角度近乎直角，方便进出或安装儿童安全座椅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749322-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749322-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_13-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749322" class="wp-caption-text">“自动”座椅温度调节。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>载物表现同样出色，60/40分割平折第二排后方提供32.9立方英尺储物空间，座椅放平后扩展至63.5立方英尺。电动尾门为标准配置，Platinum等级更配备手势感应功能，方便日常使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749323-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_09.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749323-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_09-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749323" class="wp-caption-text">Murano Glass仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年Murano搭载一系列先进科技，让驾驶更轻松且充满信心。Nissan Safety Shield 360为标准配备，提供全方位的安全保障。此外，Murano首次搭载的3D智能环景监控系统，允许驾驶者从8个虚拟视角观察车辆周围环境，较鸟瞰视野更为全面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749325-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749325-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_10-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749325" class="wp-caption-text">3D智能环景监。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>ProPILOT Assist系统在Murano SV等级提供智能巡航控制与转向辅助，进阶的ProPILOT Assist 2.1（SV及Platinum等级）利用导航数据，根据路线提前调整弯道速度，提升驾驶流畅度。与1.1版本比较，最大分别是可放开方向盘（Hands-off），以及支援自动变更车道与超车，令自动驾驶时更为方便安全。其他安全升级包括乘客膝部气囊、侧视镜从A柱移至车门以改善前方视野，以及后座的座椅下方强化结构与安全带负载限制器，全面提升后排乘客的碰撞保护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749327" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749327-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749327-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_12-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749327" class="wp-caption-text">ProPILOT Assist系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Murano的双12.3英寸显示萤幕为标准配置，中间触控萤幕提供娱乐与资讯功能，支援无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto。左侧显示萤幕作为仪表组，可切换多种显示模式，满足不同驾驶需求。SL与Platinum等级搭载Google内建功能，将Google Maps、Google Assistant和Play Store直接整合至车辆系统，带来熟悉且高效的操作体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749328-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749328-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_11-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749328" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新Murano搭载2升VC-Turbo可变压缩比涡轮增压引擎，输出241匹马力与260磅英尺扭矩，较前代增加20磅英尺扭矩，搭配9速自动变速箱与换档拨片，比以往CVT更为灵敏。怠速启停功能为标准配置，提升燃油效率。Murano SV提供前轮驱动或智能全轮驱动选项，SL与Platinum等级标配全轮驱动，确保多种路况下的稳定性。Murano的拖曳能力达1,500磅，满足轻度拖曳需求。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749329-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749329-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_08-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749329" class="wp-caption-text">2升VC-Turbo可变压缩比涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新2026年Nissan Murano拥有优雅外观、奢华内饰、先进科技与安全各方面优势。无论是日常通勤还是长途旅行，Murano都能以舒适、便利与信心陪伴驾驶者与乘客。从细腻的设计细节到强大的功能阵容，这款车不仅满足了对豪华跨界车的期待，更在竞争激烈的市场中脱颖而出。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14749330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14749330" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749330-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_07.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14749330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749330-2026_Nissan_Murano_Platnium_07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14749330" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Nissan Murano Platinum。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
