<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超：曼城短暂登顶 阿森纳多赛一场后反超</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14749820-GettyImages-2272484759.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
