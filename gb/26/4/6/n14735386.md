<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在台南孔庙商圈  遇见一个“会呼吸的甜点”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735675-840304_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>在台南孔庙商圈 ， 遇见一个“会呼吸的甜点”。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月06日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）在台南孔庙对面的街角，摊位不大，却常有人停下来。不是因为招牌特别，而是那一口小炉，还在冒着微微的热气。</p>
<p>一个老师傅蹲在炉前，手里拿着铁勺，不急不徐地搅着糖。旁边没有太多设备，几个铁锅、几支木棒、一罐罐糖粉，这样就开始了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14735387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735387" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735387-840294-e1775459840555.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735387-840294-e1775459840555.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735387" class="wp-caption-text">在台南孔庙对面商圈，师傅蹲在小炉前，慢慢熬煮糖液，火候与时间全靠经验拿捏。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>火候与颜色</h2>
<p>糖是二砂糖混黑糖。先加热，慢慢融化。颜色从透明转成淡黄，再变成琥珀色。这段时间不能急，火太大会焦，太慢又不成形。</p>
<p>他看着糖的变化，不太说话。时间到了，手一转，加入一点小苏打粉。下一秒，糖开始起泡。那个画面很快，几乎来不及反应。</p>
<p>原本黏稠的一小勺，突然膨起来，像面团一样鼓起。有人在旁边忍不住惊呼，也有人拿起手机录影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14735668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735668" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735668-840302_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735668-840302_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735668" class="wp-caption-text">摊位设于台南孔庙对面街角，空间不大，但吸引游客驻足观看。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14735669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735669" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735669-840294_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735669-840294_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735669" class="wp-caption-text">在台南孔庙对面商圈，师傅蹲在小炉前，慢慢熬煮糖液，火候与时间全靠经验拿捏。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14735670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735670" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735670-840298_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735670-840298_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735670" class="wp-caption-text">二砂糖与黑糖混合，是椪糖的基底，比例与火候会影响最后风味。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14735671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735671" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735671-840303_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735671-840303_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735671" class="wp-caption-text">游客实际参与制作，在一旁操作小炉与糖锅，从观看到动手，成为完整的体验过程。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一颗一颗做 手感决定成败</h2>
<p>老师傅没有停，快速翻动、稍微放凉，再回火一下。时间抓得很准，多一秒、少一秒都不行。等到表面稳住，他才把整团糖倒出来。这就是椪糖。</p>
<p>很多人以为这只是甜点，但站在现场看一轮，才知道它更像一种手艺。没有量杯、没有温度计，全靠经验。</p>
<p>一锅一锅做，每一颗都重新开始。没有复制，也没有修正。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14735672" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735672" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735672-840296_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735672" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735672-840296_medium.webp" alt="" width="449" b="798" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735672" class="wp-caption-text">糖水搅拌会椪的关键，是加入一点点小苏打粉。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14735673" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735673" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735673-840297_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735673" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735673-840297_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735673" class="wp-caption-text">糖液膨起的瞬间，体积快速变化，像面团一样鼓起，是最精彩的一刻。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14735674" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735674" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735674-840299_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735674" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735674-840299_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735674" class="wp-caption-text">现场告示写着制作流程：加热、搅拌、膨胀、定型，看似简单，其实每一步都需要经验累积。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>看的人 比买的多</h2>
<p>旁边的架子上，已经排满做好的椪糖，一包一包装好，贴上标签。有人买来吃，也有人带回去当伴手礼。但更多人，是坐在旁边等。</p>
<p>这里不只是卖，也开放体验。付了钱，可以自己试一次。</p>
<p>年轻人坐在矮凳上，学着搅糖、加粉、等时间。看起来简单，实际上常常失败。糖要嘛不膨，要嘛塌掉。老师傅会在旁边提醒，但不会帮忙太多。“这个要自己抓。”他说。</p>
<p>成功的那一刻，大家会笑。那种笑，不只是完成一颗糖，而是抓到那个节奏。</p>
<p>在这条街上，很多东西都变了。咖啡店、文创店、观光人潮，一直在换。但这一摊，还是用一样的方法在做。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14735676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735676" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735676-840305_medium-e1775461277747.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735676-840305_medium-e1775461277747.webp" alt="" width="452" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735676" class="wp-caption-text">椪糖冷却后定型，表面带有自然气孔与纹路，每一颗外观都略有不同。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>慢慢做的手作</h2>
<p>一锅糖的时间，大概几分钟。不长，但也不快。你如果愿意站着看完，就会发现，这种手作，其实很安静。没有夸张的表演，也没有刻意的包装。只是有人，还在这里，一颗一颗地做。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14735677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735677" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735677-840300_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735677-840300_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735677" class="wp-caption-text">架上整齐陈列已完成的椪糖，一包包封装，成为可带走的古早味。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14735678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14735678" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735678-840301_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14735678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14735678-840301_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14735678" class="wp-caption-text">透明圆桶装的椪糖礼盒，外贴手作标签，转为伴手礼形式贩售。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
