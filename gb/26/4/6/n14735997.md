<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>袁红冰：“万古第一祭”书——中国共产主义罹难者祭文</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14566805-785432.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
