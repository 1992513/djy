<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>跟着妈祖走进去 才看懂北港</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738694-841207_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>后院大型装置艺术，以花饰与容器拼组出马的轮廓，色彩鲜明，吸引游客驻足拍照。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）很多人来云林北港，拜完北港朝天宫就走。但只要多走几步，会看到一间不太一样的地方——北港工艺坊。这里不是单纯展示。是把北港的生活、信仰、手艺，全部放在一起。</p>
<p>而带你看懂的人，是北港文史工作者、馆长蔡享润。他讲的，不是课本。是北港每天在发生的事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14738696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738696" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738696-841202_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738696" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738696-841202_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738696" class="wp-caption-text">北港工艺坊外观保留红砖与洗石子结构，呈现老屋修复后的质朴样貌，低调却耐看。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14738695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738695" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738695-841209_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738695-841209_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738695" class="wp-caption-text">导览过程中，蔡享润老师指着墙面诗句，带领游客理解北港街区的文化脉络与文字意涵。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一面墙 撑起一段历史</h2>
<p>全台唯一这个样式的“瓮墙”，工艺坊最先让人停下来的，是外面的墙。那一排一排嵌在墙上的陶瓮，整齐、安静，但很有存在感。</p>
<p>这面“瓮墙”，是全台唯一。九二一地震时曾经受损，不是换掉，而是由在地工艺家，一个一个修回来。所以你看到的，不只是修复。是“留下来”这件事。每一个瓮，都像一段被保住的记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14738698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738698" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738698-841227_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738698-841227_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738698" class="wp-caption-text">921时瓮墙毁损，由当地工艺家协力修护起来的。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14738697" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738697" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738697-841213_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738697" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738697-841213_medium.webp" alt="" width="448" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738697" class="wp-caption-text">全台唯一墙内高于外墙的瓮墙。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>墙上不只画 是北港的缩影</h2>
<p>导览壁画与赖和的竹枝词，走到外墙另一侧，画面完全不同。有颜色、有故事、有节奏。那不是装饰，是导览。北港的特产、信仰、生活，全部画在上面。旁边还刻着赖和的“竹枝词”。</p>
<p>一百多年前，他用台语写下北港的热闹。</p>
<p>现在的人站在这面墙前，还是看得到那个场景。这里，不只是拍照点。是把时间放在墙上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14738702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738702-841226_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738702-841226_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738702" class="wp-caption-text">彩绘内容包括北港特产：大饼、蚕豆、花生、麻油和千里眼、顺风耳等。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14738701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738701" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738701-841212_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738701-841212_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738701" class="wp-caption-text">这个是北港朝天宫才有的香火袋符咒，求平安、求功名之用。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14738700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738700" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738700-841211_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738700-841211_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738700" class="wp-caption-text">赖和用台语诗，形容一百年前北港热闹情景。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14738699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738699" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738699-841210_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738699-841210_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738699" class="wp-caption-text">一整排赖和的竹枝词诗句，排列于墙面，形成街区中的文化长廊，让人边走边读。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>后院最有趣 幽默，也是一种文化</h2>
<p>很多人走到这里会笑出来。因为馆长的设计，很“北港”。假花、白色小鸡的饲料盒、再加上四个跟马年有关的甲骨文。</p>
<p>看起来有点玩笑，但其实很用心。这种组合，没有教科书。就是生活。好拍，也好记。很多人会在这里停很久。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14738704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738704" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738704-841214_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738704-841214_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738704" class="wp-caption-text">“飞黄腾达”马年生肖联展主视觉，展期自2026年3月4日至5月3日，于北港工艺坊展出，集结多位工艺师创作。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14738703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738703" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738703-841201_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738703-841201_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738703" class="wp-caption-text">后院装置艺术以大量花饰与容器拼组成图案，搭配马年意象，成为拍照热点。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>马年的作品 每个工艺家都有自己的语言</h2>
<p>走进馆内，会慢下来，这里展的是“马年主题”，但不是同一种马。有昂首前进的，刻着“马到成功”，有圆润带点趣味的，有保留木纹、让时间自己说话的，每一件都不一样。</p>
<p>你会发现，同样一个“马”，每个工艺家都有自己的想法。有的细致，有的粗犷，有的像祝福，有的像陪伴。不是看一眼就走的东西，是真的会让人停下来看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14738707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738707" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738707-841204_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738707-841204_medium.webp" alt="" width="452" b="803" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738707" class="wp-caption-text">铅笔芯创作李健竹老师的马年作品。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>扭币机 自己选祝福</h2>
<p>馆里还有一个很多人会玩很久的东西。不是扭蛋，是“扭币机”。你可以自己选——求财、求平安或不同寓意的纪念币。投币、转动，掉下来的，是你刚刚选的那一种。简单，但很有参与感，很多人会多转几次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14738709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738709" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738709-841195_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738709-841195_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738709" class="wp-caption-text">馆内设置的扭币机，游客可自行选择“平安”“招财”等主题，转动后压制出专属纪念币，结合趣味与信仰。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14738708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14738708" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738708-841197_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14738708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14738708-841197_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14738708" class="wp-caption-text">北港工艺坊馆长蔡享润现场示范扭币机操作，介绍其设计理念与文化寓意。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>小东西 也有北港味</h2>
<p>马克杯、报马仔，馆方有一些设计品，像是仿北港花灯的马克杯（还分公母）、超级吸睛的报马仔公仔，都是蔡享润设计的。</p>
<p>不是纪念品而已，是把北港的文化，变成可以带走的东西。看得出来，是在地人做的。不是复制的。</p>
