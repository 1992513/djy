<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>太阳一低 羊就回家 七美羊伯朱得胜的日常</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743549-842633_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>群石头羊聚集在沙丘上休息、觅食，形成岛上常见的自然放牧画面。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬澎湖报导）在澎湖七美乡，傍晚有一段固定的时间。不是看钟，是看太阳。当光线压低到一个角度，原本散在各处的羊，会慢慢动起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14743550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743550" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743550-842628_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743550-842628_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="739" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743550" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳下羊群穿越水泥路面，准备回到熟悉的栖息地，画面充满生活感。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14743551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743551" style="width: 458px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743551-842626_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743551-842626_medium.webp" alt="" width="458" b="622" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743551" class="wp-caption-text">棕色石头羊站在石堆上张望，角形弯曲、神情警觉，是典型在地放牧样貌。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14743552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743552-842619_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743552-842619_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="548" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743552" class="wp-caption-text">羊群沿着熟悉路径移动，节奏一致，逐步往固定方向前进。（杨铭洲提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>三十年，只做一件事</h2>
<p>朱得胜，大家叫他羊伯。</p>
<p>三十年来，他每天差不多就是这样过：放羊、走路、巡视，再走一圈。</p>
<p>一天四万步，是日常。哪里草长得好、哪里要注意，他一看就知道。</p>
<p>他骑着机车跟着羊跑，两天就要加满一次油。不是赶时间，是在顾一群会自己生活的动物。他说不多，但做得很稳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14743553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743553" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743553-842637_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743553-842637_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="827" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743553" class="wp-caption-text">傍晚时分，牧人停下机车守望四周，远方夕阳低垂，岛屿生活节奏缓慢而踏实。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14743554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743554" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743554-842632_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743554-842632_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743554" class="wp-caption-text">更多羊群集中于沙丘与草地之间活动，形成层次分明的群体景象。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>石头地上的“石头羊”</h2>
<p>七美风大、土薄、石头多，农作不易，却养出一种很特别的羊。它们毛色多半偏深，有黑、有棕，也有混色，身形结实，腿细但有力，角往后弯。走在石头地上很稳，不太滑，动作俐落，这里的人叫它们“石头羊”。</p>
<p>它们吃野草，不吃饲料。风吹、日晒、活动量大，长得慢，但肉扎实。</p>
<p>羊伯只给井水，其它让它们自己找。养两年才卖，不赶。他也说得直接，这种羊不适合喝奶，味道重。</p>
<p>七美的羊，多半是做羊肉炉。外地人记得那一锅，但在地人看的是这些羊怎么长、怎么走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14743555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743555-842618_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743555-842618_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="502" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743555" class="wp-caption-text">棕黑交错、角往后弯，体型结实，长期在石地活动，脚步稳健。（杨铭洲提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14743556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743556" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743556-842627_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743556-842627_medium.webp" alt="" width="602" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743556" class="wp-caption-text">两只石头羊顶角较劲，展现群体中的互动与地盘意识。（杨铭洲提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>羊的样子 很有个性</h2>
<p>靠近看，石头羊其实不怕人，但会保持距离。</p>
<p>有的站得笔直盯着人看，有的低头吃草，有的耳朵左右摆动，随时在听动静。</p>
<p>几只会靠在一起，像在等什么讯号。</p>
<p>小羊会跟着母羊走，动作比较急，常常快半步。</p>
<p>成羊反而稳，一步一步踩得很准。它们不是被驯得很乖，而是习惯了这个地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14743557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743557-842622_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743557-842622_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="439" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743557" class="wp-caption-text">外地人来看，会觉得这是一个景。但对羊伯来说，就是生活。早上放，下午收。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14743558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743558" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743558-842635_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743558-842635_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743558" class="wp-caption-text">近距离拍摄，羊群表情各异，眼神灵动，带着几分好奇与防备。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>不用赶 时间到了就动</h2>
<p>傍晚才是重点。太阳往海面落，羊开始聚。</p>
<p>不是有人喊，也不是被驱赶，而是自己慢慢往同一方向靠。</p>
<p>有的从坡上走下来，有的从草丛里钻出来，路线不一样，但最后会汇在一起。</p>
<p>羊伯坐在机车上，看着。最多按一下喇叭，提醒一下位置。其它时间，他几乎不动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14743559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743559" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743559-842638_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743559-842638_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="547" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743559" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳下羊群成群穿越水泥路面，准备回到熟悉的栖息地，画面充满生活感。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14743560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743560" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743560-842636_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743560-842636_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="781" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743560" class="wp-caption-text">七美在地牧羊人骑着机车巡牧，脸上带着笑容，日常就是与风、与羊群相伴。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>最关键的几分钟</h2>
<p>羊过马路，是整件事最整齐的时刻。</p>
<p>它们会自己抓时间，一批一批快速通过。没有停留，也不会乱冲。脚步快，但不慌。</p>
<p>车子会停，人会让，但羊不等人。几分钟内，全数过完。接着往羊圈走，节奏一样稳。</p>
<p>羊伯说，三十年来没有发生过车祸。唯一出过状况，是羊自己走偏掉到海里。其它时间，都照规矩走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14743561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743561" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743561-842629_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743561-842629_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="743" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743561" class="wp-caption-text">羊排成一列小跑前行。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14743562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743562" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743562-842625_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743562-842625_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743562" class="wp-caption-text">海边的石头羊，羊伯说他们就近会在海边休息、过夜。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>人和羊之间的默契</h2>
<p>羊不会攻击人，也不会靠太近。它们怕狗，一旦被追就会乱，但平常很稳。</p>
<p>因为它们知道路，也知道要回去。</p>
<p>这不是训练出来的，是时间累积出来的。羊伯不用多讲，羊也不用多想。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14743563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743563" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743563-842624_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743563-842624_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743563" class="wp-caption-text">它们怕狗，一旦被追就会乱，但平常很稳。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14743564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14743564" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743564-842621_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14743564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14743564-842621_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="463" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14743564" class="wp-caption-text">羊群聚集时层次分明，小羊依附成羊，形成稳定的群体结构。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>曾经的风光</h2>
<p>他提过，曾经有一段时间，一只羊可以卖到两万元。</p>
<p>那几年行情好，一年收入几百万。讲出来，旁边的人都会愣一下。</p>
<p>但现在，他还是照原来的方式养。不多，也不急。</p>
<p>外地人来看，会觉得这是一个景。但对羊伯来说，就是生活。</p>
<p>早上放，下午收。看风、看草、看羊。没有安排，也没有表演。三十年，一样的步调。</p>
<p>最后一批羊进了羊圈，天色也差不多暗了。</p>
<p>风还在吹，海声在旁边，人慢慢散去。羊安静下来。</p>
<p>羊伯把门关上，今天就结束。隔天，还是一样走四万步。</p>
