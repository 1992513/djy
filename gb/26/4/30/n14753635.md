<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯热门购房区揭晓</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月30日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者</span><span lang="zh-TW">杨达延</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">最新数据显示，珀斯南部郊区——</span><span lang="en-AU">White Gum Valley</span><span lang="zh-TW">成为西澳购房者最追捧的区。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《今日珀斯》报导，根据房地产数据分析机构</span><span lang="en-US">PropTrack</span><span lang="zh-TW">的最新数据，今年</span><span lang="en-US">White Gum Valley</span><span lang="zh-TW">区每套房产收到的查询量超过西澳其它任何地区，平均每套挂牌房产获得</span><span lang="en-US">123.5</span><span lang="zh-TW">次查询。该区距离</span><span lang="en-US">Fremantle</span><span lang="zh-TW">仅</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">公里，房屋销售中位价为</span><span lang="en-US">147</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，去年已加入“房价百万澳元俱乐部”。</span></p>
<p>珀斯大都会区一些最富裕的区包括<span lang="en-US">Floreat</span>、<span lang="en-US">Trigg</span>、<span lang="en-US">Applecross</span>和<span lang="en-US">East Fremantle</span>等区也位居购房者最心仪的地区之列。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Wembley</span><span lang="zh-TW">区房屋中位价高达</span><span lang="en-US">193</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，每套房产平均吸引</span><span lang="en-US">122.6</span><span lang="zh-TW">次查询。</span><span lang="en-US">Bateman</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Floreat</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US">Trigg</span><span lang="zh-TW">区每套房屋也平均吸引超过</span><span lang="en-US">100</span><span lang="zh-TW">次购房者查询，而</span><span lang="en-US">Beaconsfield</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Applecross</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">East Fremantle</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Manning</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US">Joondanna</span><span lang="zh-TW">区同样是买家愿望清单上的热门选择。</span></p>
<p>房地产中介<span lang="en-US">Empire Residential Property</span>经纪人艾伦（<span lang="en-US">Linton Allen</span>）表示，买家一直在持续询问<span lang="en-US">White Gum Valley</span>区和<span lang="en-US">Beaconsfield</span>区的房产，这两个区受益于<span lang="en-US">Fremantle</span>的极高人气。</p>
<p>“过去<span lang="en-US">12</span>至<span lang="en-US">18</span>个月，<span lang="en-US">White Gum Valley</span>和<span lang="en-US">Beaconsfield</span>的查询量明显增加，”他说。<span lang="zh-TW">“人们想在</span><span lang="en-US">Fremantle</span><span lang="zh-TW">买房，但又无法如愿，于是只好把搜索范围进一步扩大。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">尽</span><span lang="zh-TW">管生活成本不断上升，但买家最受欢迎的前</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">个郊区中，每一个的格都远高于珀斯整体房屋中位价</span><span lang="en-US">89</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">REA</span><span lang="zh-TW">集团高级经济学家摩尔（</span><span lang="en-US">Angus Moore</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，尤其是在珀斯较昂贵区域，房产受到的高度关注反映了住房供应紧张的状况。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">西澳房地产协会的数据显示，今年年初待售房产数量较一年前减少了一半。</span></p>
<p>“那些绿化更好的区往往房屋持有率很高，”摩尔说，“查询量确实非常大。珀斯房屋通常的查询量在<span lang="en-US">55</span>次左右，而<span lang="en-US">120</span>次属于非常高的水平，这反映出珀斯市场一直非常强劲，且房源供应不足。”</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在单元房（</span><span lang="en-US">Units</span><span lang="zh-TW">）方面，珀斯北部的</span><span lang="en-US">Glendalough</span><span lang="zh-TW">是最受欢迎的选择，平均每套挂牌获得</span><span lang="en-US">71.3</span><span lang="zh-TW">次查询。</span><span lang="en-US">Wembley</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US">Joondanna</span><span lang="zh-TW">也是单元房热门区域；南部则以</span><span lang="en-US">Bicton</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Cottesloe</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Palmyra</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Hamilton Hill</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US">East Fremantle</span><span lang="zh-TW">需求最旺；而东部地区王后河滨区（</span><span lang="en-US">Queens Riverside precinct</span><span lang="zh-TW">）的单元房最受欢迎。</span></p>
<p>在西澳偏远地区，靠近<span lang="en-US">Busselton</span>的<span lang="en-US">Broadwater</span>是唯一能与珀斯需求相媲美的区域，单元房挂牌平均吸引<span lang="en-US">63.3</span>次查询。</p>
