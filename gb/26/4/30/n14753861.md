<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>营养专家评选5种最健康坚果 各有哪些好处</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818173-shutterstock_112318868.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>【大纪元2026年05月01日讯】（大纪元记者李言综合报导）坚果被广泛视为健康食品，因富含健康脂肪、纤维和蛋白质而闻名，且非常适合随身携带。然而，并非所有坚果的营养价值都相同，且由于热量较高，专家建议仅少量食用。</p><p>福克斯新闻咨询了多位营养专家，列出以下5种最健康的坚果及其推荐理由：</p><h2>1. 核桃：脑部与心血管首选</h2><div><figure style="max-width:640px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669109-shutterstock_328436765-1.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F01%2Fid14669109-shutterstock_328436765-1.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669109-shutterstock_328436765-1.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F01%2Fid14669109-shutterstock_328436765-1.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="每天只需吃一小把核桃就能降低胆固醇，改善饮食品质。(Shutterstock)" width="640" b="auto"/></picture></a><figcaption>每天只需吃一小把核桃就能降低胆固醇，改善饮食品质。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure></div><p>德州营养师兼糖尿病教育专家惠特尼?斯图尔特（Whitney Stuart）表示，核桃（Walnuts）“在心血管和脑部健康方面领先群雄”。</p><p>核桃是唯一含有大量ALA（α-亚麻酸）Omega-3脂肪酸的坚果，有助于抗炎并支持认知功能。</p><p>注册营养师麦肯齐?伯吉斯（Mackenzie Burgess）指出，近期研究显示核桃对提升睡眠质量有积极影响，建议可加入燕麦、酸奶或作为餐后小点。</p><h2>2. 开心果：营养最均衡</h2><div><figure style="max-width:640px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/10/1310220538142599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2013%2F10%2F1310220538142599.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/10/1310220538142599.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2013%2F10%2F1310220538142599.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="开心果富含蛋白质、纤维和优质脂肪。（苏玉芬／大纪元）" width="640" b="auto"/></picture></a><figcaption>开心果富含蛋白质、纤维和优质脂肪。（苏玉芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></div><p>达拉斯注册营养师艾米?古德森（Amy Goodson）指出，开心果（Pistachios）拥有“最均衡的营养配置之一”。</p><p>这种坚果富含钾以及叶黄素和玉米黄质（抗氧化剂），对保护视力至关重要。</p><p>由于开心果通常带壳，剥壳的过程能自然地减缓进食速度，帮助控制摄入量。</p><p>斯图尔特补充，开心果的蛋白质含量在坚果中名列前茅，且研究证实其能改善餐后血糖反应。</p><h2>3. 杏仁：纤维与维生素E宝库</h2><div><figure style="max-width:640px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408010432402599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F08%2F1408010432402599.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408010432402599.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F08%2F1408010432402599.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="粒粒饱满的杏仁果。（赖友容／大纪元）" width="640" b="auto"/></picture></a><figcaption>粒粒饱满的杏仁果。（赖友容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></div><p>杏仁（Almonds）是管理血糖与心脏健康的理想选择。</p><p>古德森称之为“纤维宝库”，特别是可溶性纤维，有助于降低“坏胆固醇”（LDL）。同时，它富含维生素E，能支持皮肤健康与免疫功能。</p><p>斯图尔特指出，杏仁中的镁与健康脂肪组合能减缓葡萄糖吸收，提升胰岛素敏感性。</p><h2>4. 巴西栗：高效补硒</h2><div><figure style="max-width:640px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13961057-shutterstock_1291555399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F03%2Fid13961057-shutterstock_1291555399.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13961057-shutterstock_1291555399.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F03%2Fid13961057-shutterstock_1291555399.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="巴西栗（Brazil Nuts）富含硒，在特定营养领域具有无可比拟的优势。(Shutterstock)" width="640" b="auto"/></picture></a><figcaption>巴西栗（Brazil Nuts）富含硒，在特定营养领域具有无可比拟的优势。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure></div><p>虽然知名度稍低，但巴西栗（Brazil Nuts）在特定营养领域具有无可比拟的优势。</p><p>这种坚果富含硒（Selenium）。古德森表示，“每天只需一颗巴西栗，就能满足人体全天的硒需求。”</p><p>硒在甲状腺功能、免疫健康及抗氧化防御中发挥关键作用。</p><p>温馨提示：由于巴西栗营养极其浓缩，务必适量食用，避免摄入过量。</p><h2>5. 腰果：强健骨骼与代谢</h2><div style="margin-left:0;margin-right:0;max-width:1200px"><figure style="max-width:640px;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14263839-shutterstock_1621724497.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F06%2Fid14263839-shutterstock_1621724497.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14263839-shutterstock_1621724497.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img b="auto"/></picture></a><figcaption>腰果（奶）是镁、锌、铁和维生素K的良好来源。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure></div><p>腰果（Cashews）提供的单不饱和脂肪与橄榄油中的成分类似，对心脏极为友好。</p><p>这种为很多人所喜爱的坚果富含镁与锌，这两者在免疫功能、骨骼健康和能量代谢中扮演核心角色。</p><p>伯吉斯引用研究指出，食用腰果有助于维持健康的血压水平与整体心血管健康，建议可直接食用或作为腰果酱。</p><p>责任编辑：林妍#
