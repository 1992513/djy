<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>街屋里的金华府 还在撑着五条港</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734513-840071_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>内殿视角，红灯笼与神龛交织，空间狭长却层次分明，是典型街屋型庙宇格局。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）走进神农街，人很多，声音也多。但一转进金华府，空气会慢下来。这间庙不大，甚至像被挤在街屋里。没有庙埕，没有退缩空间，门一开就是街。</p>
<p>这不是设计，是留下来的格局。清代这里是五条港边，货物、人力、船只都在这条街流动。讨生活的人多，信仰也跟着进来。金华府最早只是民宅供奉，之后才慢慢修成庙。它的起点，不是气派，而是需要。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14734514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734514" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734515-840058_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734515-840058_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734514" class="wp-caption-text">从清朝就有的庙。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14734514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734514" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734514-840078_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734514-840078_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="614" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734514" class="wp-caption-text">神农街午后人潮，灯笼高挂、老屋相连，走在这条路上，时间像慢了下来。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14734513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734513" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734513-840071_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734513-840071_medium.webp" alt="" width="500" b="666" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734513" class="wp-caption-text">内殿视角，红灯笼与神龛交织，空间狭长却层次分明，是典型街屋型庙宇格局。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一条街屋 就是一间庙</h2>
<p>金华府最特别的，是它的“长”。从门口到正殿，一路往内延伸，空间狭窄却完整。三川门、拜亭、正殿都在，只是被压在一条街屋里。你拍到的画面，就是这种结构：前窄后深，光线一段一段进来。这种庙，不是为了好看，是因为土地有限。街多宽，庙就只能长成那样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14734516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734516" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734516-840068_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734516-840068_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="718" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734516" class="wp-caption-text">墙面彩绘人物故事，线条细腻，带有传统教化意味，过去也是传递忠孝节义的重要媒介。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14734517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734517" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734517-840067_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734517-840067_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734517" class="wp-caption-text">金华府入口木构门面，梁柱厚实、雕刻细致，保留老街庙宇最原始的气息。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>墙上的画 是以前的课本</h2>
<p>墙面那些人物彩绘，不只是装饰。以前识字的人不多，故事要用画讲。忠义、孝道、做人分寸，都画在墙上。看久了，就记住了。墙上的衣纹淡淡、线条不重，颜色也退了，但神情还在。那不是精致取胜，是让人看得懂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14734518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734518" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734518-840075_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734518-840075_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="645" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734518" class="wp-caption-text">墙面“人安”字样与老旧浮雕人物并存，岁月斑驳中更显祈福意味。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14734519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734519" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734519-840057_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734519-840057_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734519" class="wp-caption-text">讨生活的人多，信仰也跟着进来了。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14734520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734520" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734520-840070_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734520-840070_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="548" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734520" class="wp-caption-text">人物彩绘结合书法题字，画面温润古朴，呈现早期庙宇艺术与文人美学融合。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14734521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734521" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734521-840080_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734521-840080_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734521" class="wp-caption-text">梁柱转角的托梁人物雕像，神情用力、姿态夸张，是传统建筑中少见的趣味细节。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>撑住屋檐的人</h2>
<p>转角那尊托梁人物，很容易被忽略。他不是多余的雕刻。在传统建筑里，他真的在“分担重量”。人被放在梁下，是一种象征：房子靠人撑，日子也靠人撑。他微蹲、出力，姿势不轻松。那种用力感，过了百年还在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14734522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734522" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734522-840082_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734522-840082_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="590" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734522" class="wp-caption-text">人被放在梁下，是一种象征：房子靠人撑，日子也靠人撑。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>门钉 比门神更安静</h2>
<p>金华府的门没有画门神，取而代之的，是整排门钉。一颗一颗，规则排列。这种门，本来和等级、制度有关。到了这里，变得很安静。没有脸、没有神像，只有光影打上去的起伏。你靠近看，会觉得那不是装饰，是时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14734523" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734523" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734523-840076_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734523-840076_medium.webp" alt="" width="453" b="742" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734523" class="wp-caption-text">门板满布门钉，排列有序，为金华府最具辨识度的特色之一。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14734524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734524" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734524-840056_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734524-840056_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734524" class="wp-caption-text">墙面浮雕猛虎下山图，气势沉稳，寓意镇煞避邪，也象征守护地方平安。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>新与旧 在同一条街</h2>
<p>走出庙门，是咖啡店、文创店、游客。走回庙里，是木头、香火、旧墙。两种时间没有分开。神农街变了，但没有全变。金华府还在原来的位置，用原来的方式存在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14734525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734525" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734525-840077_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734525-840077_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="590" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734525" class="wp-caption-text">神农街外景，彩绘灯笼高挂，老屋与店面交织，呈现台南特有的生活与信仰融合。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14734526" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734526" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734526-840084_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734526" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734526-840084_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734526" class="wp-caption-text">走出庙门，是咖啡店、文创店、游客。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>留下来的东西</h2>
<p>这间庙没有要被看见的野心。它只是一直在。街变窄，它就变长。人变多，它就更安静。墙会剥落，木头会暗下来，但那些工法、那些比例、那些用力的姿态，都还在。金华府不是一间大庙。它只是把一段五条港的日子，留在街屋里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14734527" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14734527" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734527-840069_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14734527" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14734527-840069_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="595" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14734527" class="wp-caption-text">石雕花鸟图样，枝叶与鸟形生动，象征吉祥与生命延续，展现匠师细腻手工。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
