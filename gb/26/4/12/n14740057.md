<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>岁月留在九份的角落  看见不一样的人情与风景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740069-841619-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>站在高处看九份，山、屋、海连在一起，路弯弯绕绕，像一条走不完的记忆。（Kimball Wen提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新北市报导）摄影家Kimball Wen这一趟上九份，没有急着往人多的地方去。他说，自己只是慢慢走，看到什么，就停下来拍。“岁月腌渍的九份，转角心情大不同。”他这样形容。九份不只是山城风景，更多时候，是被时间留下来的细节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14740058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740058" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740058-841608.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740058-841608-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740058" class="wp-caption-text">两张老藤椅靠着斑驳墙面，像是等人，也像在等一段还没说完的故事。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14740059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740059" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740059-841609.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740059-841609-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740059" class="wp-caption-text">巷弄转进绿意深处，花低垂、叶层层，九份的时间不急，连呼吸都慢了下来。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14740060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740060" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740060-841610.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740060-841610-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740060" class="wp-caption-text">木牌写着“有树”，字迹鲜明却带旧意，像是在提醒——这里的一切，都还活着。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>老屋门前 是一段一段被留下的日子一扇写着“九份文史工作室”的老木门，站在巷子里，不特别显眼。门上的红对联已经有些褪色，木头纹理也被风吹雨打过。Kimball Wen停了很久，他说这样的门，不只是门，“它是在讲一个地方怎么走过来的。”门边的小窗，摆着几个布偶，像是有人还在这里生活。上方一盏灯泡，简单到几乎会被忽略，但在阴天里，却显得刚刚好。他说，九份很多地方，不用刻意整理，“就这样放着，反而比较真。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14740061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740061" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740061-841611.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740061-841611-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740061" class="wp-caption-text">红砖老屋贴着春联，窗帘微掩，生活就在这样的光影里，一天一天过去。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14740062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740062" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740062-841612.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740062-841612-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740062" class="wp-caption-text">白猫坐在石阶边，安静看人来人往，它比谁都熟悉这条路，也比谁都不急着离开。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14740063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740063" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740063-841613.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740063-841613-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740063" class="wp-caption-text">弯曲的山城道路，一位老人慢慢走着，背影被阳光拉长，日子也跟着延伸。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>转进屋里山和海就坐在眼前顺着指标“观山看海楼上请”，走进一间老茶馆。室内光线偏暗，视线却被窗外的山海拉出去。远方海面带着雾气，山一层一层往外推，屋内的人影变成剪影。Kimball Wen站在门口没有进去，他说这一幕刚好，“人在里面，风景在外面，但其实都在一起。”桌椅不多，没有太多声音，偶尔有人轻声说话。这样的地方，他说不是来打卡的，是来坐一下的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14740064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740064" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740064-841614.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740064-841614-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740064" class="wp-caption-text">花垂落屋前，淡紫色在风中轻晃，像一段不说话的心情，静静挂着。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14740065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740065" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740065-841615.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740065-841615-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740065" class="wp-caption-text">观山看海的老店门前，灯笼微亮，“吃饭、喝茶”几个字，留住过客，也留住一点人情味。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灯笼亮起来 ?才知道九份的夜会慢下来走到阿妹茶楼，红灯笼一颗一颗挂着，从楼上垂到楼下。白天看是热闹，到了傍晚，反而多了一点安静。Kimball Wen说，这里的颜色很特别，“红色很亮，但不会刺眼，因为旁边都是旧木头。”窗户一扇扇打开，里面的人吃饭、喝茶，有人看风景，也有人只是坐着。他拍了一张门口的画面，一个人站在阶梯上，看着茶楼，没有走进去。他说，那一刻，“像在想要不要进去，也像在想要不要留下来。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14740066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740066" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740066-841616.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740066" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740066-841616-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740066" class="wp-caption-text">山坡间一块风化石碑，字迹已淡，像把过去埋进泥土里，只有细看的人才会停下来。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>矿工还在 只是变成另一种方式存在在一段石阶旁，一座矿工雕像低着头，双手推着矿车。动作停在用力的那一刻。Kimball Wen拍下来后说，这不是装饰，“这是以前真的有人这样过日子。”九份的风景很多，但他特别注意这些和矿业有关的东西。墙上的浮雕、石头上的刻字、甚至被忽略的工具，都还在。他说，“如果只看风景，会觉得漂亮，但如果知道以前的事情，会多一点重量。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14740067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740067" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740067-841617.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740067-841617-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740067" class="wp-caption-text">从外头看进去，小庙藏在转角下方，红布、香火与生活杂物交错，是信仰也是日子。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14740068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740068" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740068-841618.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740068-841618-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740068" class="wp-caption-text">石制香炉刻着字，边角长了青苔，烟灰一层层堆着，时间就在这里慢慢累积。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一壶茶一朵花 都是慢下来的理由巷子里一块布帘写着“卖茶”，字体简单，挂在红灯笼下。旁边的老茶壶，壶身已经有岁月的痕迹。他拍这张的时候说，“茶不只是喝的，是一种停下来的方式。”另一个转角，他看到一棵开花的树，花不多，但开得刚好。背景是矿业浮雕墙，前面是新种的植栽。新和旧在同一个画面里，他说这就是九份，“不是分开的，是混在一起的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14740070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740070" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740070-841620.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740070-841620-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740070" class="wp-caption-text">木门老屋前挂着“九份文史工作室”，字迹斑驳，像在替这条山城，把故事一段段留下来。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14740071" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740071" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740071-841621.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740071" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740071-841621-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740071" class="wp-caption-text">抬头看匾额，岁月在玻璃上留下痕迹，一盏灯泡守着门口，也守着过往的记忆。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14740072" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740072" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740072-841622.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740072" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740072-841622-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740072" class="wp-caption-text">门上红联写着“摄影纪录九份景”，像一种提醒，这里的一切，都值得被记下。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走在九份 不用找景点这一趟拍下来，Kimball Wen没有列什么景点。他说自己只是一路走，看到喜欢的就停。“人生如戏梦人生，”他笑着说，“有时候不用安排太多。”九份的路本来就弯，走着走着会迷路，但也会看到一些原本没打算看到的东西。一只猫、一张椅子、一扇门，甚至一块石头，都可能让人停下来。他最后说了一句话：“观山看海楼上请，吃饭泡茶里面坐。”对他来说，九份不是要赶的地方，是可以慢慢待的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14740073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14740073" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740073-841623.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14740073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14740073-841623-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14740073" class="wp-caption-text">视线被山海拉远，窗外的雾气与光影，让人一时忘了时间。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
