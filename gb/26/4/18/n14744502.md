<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纪念4.25上访 台各界声援法轮功反中共迫害</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14745113-260419070208100311.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
