<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第130届波士顿马拉松万人空巷 肯亚人再获双冠</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746119-DSC01487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第130届波士顿马拉松4月20日举办。选手们在终点前最后一段直道上冲刺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年04月21日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）4月20日，第130届波士顿马拉松在晴朗而清凉的天气下举办，吸引数十万观众沿途喝采。来自肯亚（Kenya）的选手再次包揽男、女冠军，并且男子冠军还打破了赛事纪录。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14746117" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746117" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746117-DSC01454.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746117" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746117-DSC01454-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746117" class="wp-caption-text">第130届波士顿马拉松4月20日举办。选手们在终点前最后一段直道上冲刺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746120" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746120" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746120-DSC01508.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746120-DSC01508-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746120" class="wp-caption-text">数十万观众沿途为选手们加油。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746121" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746121" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746121-DSC01528.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746121" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746121-DSC01528-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746121" class="wp-caption-text">数十万观众沿途为选手们加油。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>肯亚男女选手皆卫冕 男子冠军破纪录</strong></h2>
<p>当天上午11:39左右，来自非洲肯亚的科里尔（John Korir），在万众欢呼中冲过波士顿公共图书馆旁边的终点线，连续第二年斩获波士顿马拉松冠军，更以2小时1分52秒的成绩，打破该赛事历史纪录。</p>
<p>波士顿马拉松男子精英组的上一个纪录，是2小时3分2秒，由肯亚人穆泰（Geoffrey Mutai）在2011年创造。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14746108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746108-DSC01215.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746108-DSC01215-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746108" class="wp-caption-text">来自非洲肯亚的科里尔（John Korir）在直道上冲刺，最终摘得男子冠军。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746106-DSC01206.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746106-DSC01206-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746106" class="wp-caption-text">计时车辆在男子精英组领跑者前方行驶。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在本届赛事的前中期，科里尔一直在选手群中跟跑。到了牛顿市（Newton）著名的“心碎山丘”（Heartbreak Hill），他才开始发力，逐渐与领跑群的其他人拉开40秒的距离。最终，夺冠的科里尔比第二名、来自坦桑尼亚的辛布（Alphonce Felix Simbu）领先了55秒。</p>
<p>科里尔将领走15万美元冠军奖金，以及5万美元的历史纪录奖金。他的本次成绩，是世界马拉松历史第5快。</p>
<p>他对终点处的媒体说，他跑到40公里路标的时候，就感觉自己的配速前所未有的好；当最后得知自己打破赛事纪录时，他高兴得跳了起来。“我知道我会卫冕，但我不知道我竟然能跑那么快。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14746109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746109-DSC01227.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746109-DSC01227-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746109" class="wp-caption-text">男子精英组选手在最后的直道上冲刺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746111" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746111-DSC01303.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746111-DSC01303-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746111" class="wp-caption-text">赛道旁的观众为选手们喝采加油。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样来自肯亚的女子选手洛克迪（Sharon Lokedi）以2小时18分51秒的佳绩，也连续第二年赢得波士顿马拉松冠军。去年她曾以2小时17分22秒的成绩，打破波马历史纪录。</p>
<p>今年她从赛事中段就开始领跑，在越过“心碎山丘”之后与其他选手拉开了距离。最终夺冠后，她比第二名领先了40多秒。“我就是一心想跑得越快越好。”洛克迪说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14746113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746113" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746113-DSC01327.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746113-DSC01327-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746113" class="wp-caption-text">来自肯亚的洛克迪（Sharon Lokedi）在直道上冲刺，最终摘得女子冠军。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746115" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746115-DSC01333.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746115-DSC01333-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746115" class="wp-caption-text">赛道旁的观众为女子精英组领跑者加油。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男女精英组赛事中，跑得最快的美国人，分别是男子第五名的塔尔比（Zouhair Talbi），以及女子第五名的麦克莱恩（Jess McClain）。麦克莱恩已连续第二年成为跑得最快的美国选手。</p>
<p>来自台湾的朱孟宗（Meng-Tsung Chu）以第58名的成绩完成比赛，成为本届波士顿马拉松最快的华人选手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14746110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746110" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746110-DSC01242.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746110-DSC01242-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746110" class="wp-caption-text">男子精英组第五名的塔尔比（Zouhair Talbi），是今年跑得最快的美国人。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746116" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746116-DSC01362.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746116" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746116-DSC01362-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746116" class="wp-caption-text">女子精英组第五名的麦克莱恩（Jess McClain），是今年跑得最快的美国人。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746112" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746112-DSC01314.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746112-DSC01314-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746112" class="wp-caption-text">来自台湾的朱孟宗（Meng-Tsung Chu）以第58名的成绩完成比赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>轮椅组“银色闪电”第9次夺冠</strong></h2>
<p>来自瑞士的“银色闪电”哈格（Marcel Hug），今年再次以巨大优势完成波士顿马拉松比赛，第九次赢得冠军。</p>
<p>当天上午10:22左右，哈格以1小时16分6秒的成绩冲线，比他本人创下的赛事纪录慢了33秒。他比本届比赛的轮椅组第二名快了6分多钟。</p>
<p>哈格已成为波士顿马拉松历史上，赢得男子轮椅组比赛次数第二多的选手，仅落后于南非选手戴克（Ernst van Dyk）的10次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14746104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746104" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746104-DSC01147.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746104-DSC01147-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746104" class="wp-caption-text">来自瑞士的“银色闪电”哈格（Marcel Hug）第九次赢得波马冠军。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746107" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746107-DSC01187.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746107-DSC01187-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746107" class="wp-caption-text">轮椅组的选手们在直道上冲刺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>女子轮椅组的冠军，由来自英国的彩虹库柏（Eden Rainbow-Cooper）摘得。她以1小时30分51秒的成绩冲线，比第二名快了约2分钟。</p>
<p>两名轮椅组冠军共同领奖时，彩虹库柏特别称赞哈格说：“看到有人能参赛这么长时间，并且始终保持领先，真是令人惊叹，这给每个人都带来了希望。”</p>
<p>哈格亦对彩虹库柏称赞道：“她向我们展示了一条成为冠军，一位真正冠军的绝佳途径。为了来波士顿赢得马拉松冠军，她做对了许多事情，甚至可以说每一步都走对了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14746105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746105-DSC01182.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746105-DSC01182-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746105" class="wp-caption-text">来自英国的彩虹库柏（Eden Rainbow-Cooper）摘得女子轮椅组冠军。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14746118" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14746118" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746118-DSC01466.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14746118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14746118-DSC01466-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14746118" class="wp-caption-text">选手们在终点前最后一段直道上冲刺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男子轮椅组最快的美国人，是第二名的罗曼丘克（Daniel Romanchuk）。女子轮椅组最快的美国人，是第三名的麦克法登（Tatyana McFadden）。</p>
<p>男子轮椅组最快的华人选手，是来自中国，排在第四名的罗兴传（Luo Xingchuan）。据网络信息，他从2018年开始参加轮椅竞速比赛，后来曾在多次比赛中获得冠军。◇</p>
