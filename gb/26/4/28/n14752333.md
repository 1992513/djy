<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿台商会举办就业博览会 促台美交流</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752326-IMG_2983-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会4月26日举办“2026台商就业博览会与职涯创新论坛”。嘉宾合影。（台商会提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年04月29日讯】（大纪元讯）新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会（TCCNE）于4月26日在麻省理工学院（MIT）Stata Center举办“2026台商就业博览会与职涯创新论坛”，吸引学生、专业人士及产业代总计近300人参与。活动结合产业趋势分享、企业征才、专题座谈与交流平台，有效促进台美人才与企业之间的交流与连结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14752324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752324-IMG_0243.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752324-IMG_0243-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752324" class="wp-caption-text">参与者听主讲嘉宾分享见解。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14752320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752320-4-26-Group-photo.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752320-4-26-Group-photo-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752320" class="wp-caption-text">参与者及主讲嘉宾合影。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动由麻州助理检察长张富杰、新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会会长赖怡音、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏，以及波士顿华人经济发展协会会长蒋宗壬共同致词揭开序幕，为论坛注入专业视野与国际格局。</p>
<p>在北美洲台湾商会联合总会会长白越珠的大力支持，以及北美学人与台商资源媒合工作委员会主任委员蒋序龙的积极推动下，这场就业博览会邀请到鸿海（Foxconn）、广达（Quanta）、英业达（Inventec）与纬创（Wistron）等全球知名电子大厂代表。企业代表亲自说明海外布局及美国扩厂计划，展现未来发展动能，并提供多元职涯机会，吸引众多工程与科技人才踊跃交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14752321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752321-IMG_0214.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752321-IMG_0214-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752321" class="wp-caption-text">企业代表与来宾交流。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14752322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752322-IMG_0233.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752322-IMG_0233-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752322" class="wp-caption-text">学生们与企业代表交流。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就业博览会现场交流气氛热络，与会者除了能与企业代表直接对话外，还可以在“专属面谈室”即时面谈，进一步促进职务媒合。完善的就业媒合机制，有效提升企业与人才之间的对接效率，成为本次活动的重要成果之一。</p>
<p>此外，北美名誉会长史孟珠律师与芝加哥荣誉会长李嘉展亦拨冗出席，分享职涯发展经验与产业观察，为现场求职者提供宝贵建议与启发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14752323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752323-IMG_0239.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752323-IMG_0239-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752323" class="wp-caption-text">活动义工合影。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14752325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752325-IMG_0246.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752325-IMG_0246-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752325" class="wp-caption-text">众多学生及专业人士参加本次就业博览会。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动亦成功串联MIT台湾学生会、新英格兰地区台湾同学会联合会，以及台美菁英协会波士顿分会等团体，扩大参与规模，展现跨领域资源整合的动能。</p>
<p>在北美总会及多位关键推动者的共同努力下，本次活动于企业邀请、活动规划及产业连结等面向，充分展现团队合作精神，成功促成高品质交流与多方合作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14752328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752328-IMG_2988.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752328-IMG_2988-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752328" class="wp-caption-text">众多学生及专业人士参加本次博览会。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14752327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752327" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752327-IMG_2987.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752327-IMG_2987-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752327" class="wp-caption-text">众多学生及专业人士参加本次博览会。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14752329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14752329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752329-IMG_2990.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14752329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14752329-IMG_2990-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14752329" class="wp-caption-text">本次博览会提供面谈室，供求职者与企业代表交谈。（台商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会表示，未来将持续推动具影响力之跨界交流平台，协助台湾企业拓展国际布局，同时支持优秀人才走向全球舞台，进一步深化台湾与新英格兰地区之经贸合作与人才交流。◇</p>
