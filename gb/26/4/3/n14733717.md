<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一颗轮子撑起四个孩子的成长</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733719-839878-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>带着一群孩子在校园中练习，从姿势到节奏，一步一步教，陪伴，是他最重要的教学。（苏家庆提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年04月03日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）在云北港朝天宫的傍晚，灯笼一盏盏亮起，石板地微湿反光。几个孩子骑着独轮车，穿过人群，没有音乐，也没有掌声，就是日常。这样的画面，已经很多年。</p>
<p>四个孩子，从还站不稳的年纪开始，一路骑到现在。</p>
<p>他们的爸爸苏家庆，是北港钟表店老板，白天修表，晚上陪孩子。不是职业教练，只是业余教练，但这件事，他做得很久。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14733735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733735" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733735-839894.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733735-839894.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733735" class="wp-caption-text">第一次拥有自己的单轮，还有点害羞的笑。从这一刻起，世界多了一个可以努力的方向。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733726" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733726-839885.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733726-839885.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733726" class="wp-caption-text">还不会骑的年纪，就先学会抓平衡。小小的手扶着桌边，一步一步，开始和单轮熟悉。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733723-839882.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733723-839882.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="818" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733723" class="wp-caption-text">不会骑的年纪，就先认识单轮。从摸、从看、从跌倒开始，这条路，走得很早。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733737" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733737-839896.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733737-839896.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733737" class="wp-caption-text">跟着北港艺阁游街，在街头练习的日子。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733738" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733738-839897.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733738-839897.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733738" class="wp-caption-text">在北港朝天宫前的夜晚，一群孩子、一排单轮，青春就在灯光下发光。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>从扶墙到放手</h2>
<p>慢慢敢，而不是突然会。一开始，孩子靠着墙，脚踩不上去，身体晃。有人扶，才敢站；有人牵，才敢动。每天多一点，多撑一秒，多踩一下。</p>
<p>没有捷径，就是反复。摔倒很平常，破皮、擦伤，回家处理，隔天再来。</p>
<p>苏家庆很少教口诀，他多半站在旁边看。孩子自己找到平衡的那一刻，比被教会更重要。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14733731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733731" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733731-839890.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733731-839890.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733731" class="wp-caption-text">家里的角落，也能是练习场。只要有一台单轮，哪里都可以开始。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733734-839893.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733734-839893.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733734" class="wp-caption-text">还不会骑，只会扶着。两个孩子围着单轮转，从好奇开始，这就是一切的起点。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一颗轮子的运动</h2>
<p>身体稳，心才会稳。独轮车很单纯，一颗轮子，没有支撑。也因此，什么都藏不住。身体不稳会倒，心不稳也会乱。</p>
<p>骑的时候，眼睛看前，核心撑住，脚稳定踩。太快不行，太慢也不行，脑袋要一直修正。这是全身的运动，也是专注的练习。久了，专注成习惯，稳也变自然。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14733724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733724" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733724-839883.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733724-839883.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733724" class="wp-caption-text">一台单轮，从拆解、调整到组装，孩子亲手学会的不只是骑，是理解与专注。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733720" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733720-839879.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733720-839879.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733720" class="wp-caption-text">靠着墙、踩着单轮，孩子的眼神很稳。从不会到会，这段路，只有自己知道有。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>921之后留下的东西</h2>
<p>不只是技术，是一种方式。这项运动，在921大地震之后，由日本引进台湾。带来的不只是技巧，而是一种想法——在不稳的世界里，让孩子学会站稳。</p>
<p>苏家庆没有想太多，只是觉得可以陪孩子。不是为比赛，也不是为表现，就是一起做一件事。做久了，孩子会变。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14733722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733722" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733722-839881.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733722-839881.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733722" class="wp-caption-text">夏令营的合照，一群孩子、一排单轮，这不只是活动，是一段一起成长的记忆。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733736" style="width: 444px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733736-839895.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733736-839895.jpg" alt="" width="444" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733736" class="wp-caption-text">家人的合照，单轮就在中间。这不只是运动，是一个家庭共同走过的路。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>四个孩子 一起长大</h2>
<p>不是比快，是互相等。兄弟姊妹一起练，没有谁一定比较快。有人先会了，会回头等；有人卡住了，旁边会扶一下。不多说，但彼此都懂。</p>
<p>那种默契，不是教出来的，是一起跌过来的。现在的他们，可以在庙前骑、在街上骑，甚至牵着手前进。不是表演，是习惯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14733725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733725" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733725-839884.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733725-839884.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733725" class="wp-caption-text">长大后的样子，稳稳站在单轮旁，眼神不再慌张。时间会留下痕迹，也会留下底气。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733733" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733733-839892.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733733-839892.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733733" class="wp-caption-text">庙会阵头中穿梭的单轮，不只是技术，是文化与年轻生命的连结，也是一种自信的展现。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>父亲的角色</h2>
<p>不是教会，是一直在。苏家庆没有课表，也没有硬性时间。只是每天差不多的时段，带孩子去骑，有时操场，有时街上。像修表一样，慢慢调整。</p>
<p>他对时间很准，但陪孩子这件事，从不计时。只是一直做。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14733728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733728" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733728-839887.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733728-839887.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733728" class="wp-caption-text">一整排单轮，像一段段岁月的累积。苏家庆 不只是教骑车，是陪一群孩子长大。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733721" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733721-839880.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733721-839880.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733721" class="wp-caption-text">最早的样子，在庙前的夜晚，小小的身影与单轮一起长大，笑容里全是单纯的快乐。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14733730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14733730" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733730-839889.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14733730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/id14733730-839889.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14733730" class="wp-caption-text">夜晚的北港朝天宫前，灯光下的单轮，是青春，也是默默练出来的稳。（苏家庆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>长大的 ?不只是技术</h2>
<p>是没有支撑时，仍能站稳。现在的他们，站上去是稳的，看人是自然的。有人看也不紧张，骑就骑，做自己在做的事。</p>
<p>那种稳，不只是技术，是一种状态。</p>
<p>未来会不会继续骑，不那么重要。重要的是，他们已经学会：没有支撑的时候，怎么不倒。</p>
<p>日常，就是答案。傍晚的北港，灯笼亮了，石板路泛着光。两个孩子牵着手骑过去，后面有人慢慢跟。</p>
<p>没有安排，也没有刻意，但看着会安静下来。那不是表演，是一段很长的陪伴走到这里。</p>
<p>一颗轮子，撑起四个孩子的成长。也撑住一个父亲，安静而长久的心意。</p>
