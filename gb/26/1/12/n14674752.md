<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世华美南新春首场活动 引领女性健康与美学风潮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674761-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>世华美南分会2026年“美的盛宴”讲座合影。（陆希／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年01月13日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯报导）世华工商妇女企业管理协会美南分会为迎接2026年，于1月11日（周日）在侨教中心展览室举办开春首场活动“美的盛宴”，邀请吴尚真医师与美妆造型大师Nancy 黄老师主讲，吸引近60位会员踊跃参与，现场气氛热络。活动以科学健康与专业美学为主轴，期盼协助女性在新的一年由内而外焕发自信与光彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14674762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14674762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674762-02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14674762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674762-02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14674762" class="wp-caption-text">理事团队分工合作负责接待并合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14674763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14674763" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674763-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14674763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674763-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14674763" class="wp-caption-text">理事团队分工合作负责点心。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天贵宾云集，包括经文处处长萧伊芳、侨教中心主任王盈蓉及副主任房亭妤、名誉总会长黎淑瑛及多位前会长均应邀出席。新会长孙玉玟致词时表示，期盼透过多元且高品质的活动，持续为会员与社区带来正向能量。萧伊芳处长鼓励大家“马上开始”关注自身健康；王盈蓉主任则强调女性内外兼修的重要性，并肯定世华长期推动女力与性别平权的努力。活动同时介绍三位新会员李懋卿、Winnie及蔡米惠，为世华注入新活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14674764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14674764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674764-04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14674764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674764-04-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14674764" class="wp-caption-text">吴尚真医师讲座主题体重管理。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14674767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14674767" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674767-07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14674767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674767-07-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14674767" class="wp-caption-text">孙玉玟赠送礼卡给吴尚真医师（左）。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一场讲座由吴尚真医师从代谢医学角度解析现代女性减重困难的根本原因。她指出，肥胖是一种需以科学方式管理的慢性疾病，而非单靠意志力即可解决。吴医师说明体重设定点、荷尔蒙调控、线粒体功能及压力与睡眠对代谢的影响，并提出系统性体重管理策略，包括蛋白质优先饮食、选择原型食物、规律阻力训练与HIIT运动、稳定作息及必要时在医师指导下使用药物辅助。她鼓励大家以长期习惯取代短期节食，才能达成健康且可持续的目标。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14674765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14674765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674765-05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14674765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674765-05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14674765" class="wp-caption-text">美妆造型大师Nancy 黄老师示范专业化妆技巧。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14674766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14674766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674766-06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14674766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674766-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14674766" class="wp-caption-text">Nancy 黄老师（左）、蔡米惠担任模特儿。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二场由美妆造型大师Nancy黄老师示范专业化妆技巧，模特儿由蔡米惠担任。Nancy老师以“三庭五眼”为基础，说明发型、眉型与妆容如何修饰脸型比例，并实际示范底妆、眉妆、眼妆、腮红与唇妆的完整步骤。她分享2026年流行的“眉流感”画法，以及假睫毛、双眼皮贴与眼线的实用技巧，帮助学员掌握自然、持久又具专业感的彩妆方法，让现场学员收获满满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14674768" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14674768" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674768-08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14674768" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14674768-08-600x442.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14674768" class="wp-caption-text">（前排右至左）萧伊芳、王盈蓉、刘秀美及房亭妤应邀出席嘉宾。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动尾声，主办单位致赠讲师纪念礼物表达感谢，并安排全体合影留念，为本次“美的盛宴”画下圆满句点。会长孙玉玟表示，未来将持续规划结合健康、专业与生活美学的主题活动，陪伴会员在新的一年持续成长，展现女性自信与力量。</p>
