<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园市三和木球小将 勇夺五金风靡全场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670724-822000-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>三和国高年级男子双人金牌颁奖。（桃园市府教育局 提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月07日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）“114年全台小学生暨中小学教师木球锦标赛”于12月6日至7日在台中市北屯木球场举行，来自全台28校、两百多位小选手及师生齐聚挥杆竞技；今年赛事首度以独立全台赛形式办理，小学生与教师共同参与，开辟一个属于国小木球选手的专属舞台。桃园市龙潭区三和国小木球小荣获五面金牌、三面银牌、二面铜牌佳绩，风靡全场。</p>
<figure id="14670725" aria-describedby="caption-14670725" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670725-822001-450x277.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670725-822001-450x277.jpg" alt="三和国小一杆过门颁奖。" width="601" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14670725" class="wp-caption-text">三和国小一杆过门颁奖。（桃园市府教育局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市龙潭区三和国民小学教导主任钟荣祥指出，本次赛事依年龄划分为低年级、中年级、高年级三级，设置个人、双人与团体组。选手们在烈阳下展现绝佳的专注力与稳定度，每一杆都反映平日训练成果。</p>
<figure id="14670731" aria-describedby="caption-14670731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670731-822006-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670731-822006-450x253.jpg" alt="三和国总教练赛前叮咛。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14670731" class="wp-caption-text">三和国总教练赛前叮咛。（桃园市府教育局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14670726" aria-describedby="caption-14670726" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670726-822002-450x259.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670726-822002-450x259.jpg" alt="三和国小中年级个人赛穿金戴银。" width="601" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14670726" class="wp-caption-text">三和国小中年级个人赛穿金戴银。（桃园市府教育局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过两天激烈角逐，以5面金牌成为本次赛事最大金牌亮点赢家。一年级余映里同学球龄很短，凭借着专注认真学习，享受挥杆乐趣，勇夺低年级女生组个人赛金牌。</p>
<figure id="14670728" aria-describedby="caption-14670728" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670728-822003-450x243.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670728-822003-450x243.jpg" alt="男子双人比赛实况。" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14670728" class="wp-caption-text">男子双人比赛实况。（桃园市府教育局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>毛恺同学凭着优异的球技勇夺中年级组个人及团体金牌、双人赛银牌；曾国铠获得中年级组团体金牌、个人及双人赛银牌。</p>
<figure id="14670729" aria-describedby="caption-14670729" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670729-822004-450x271.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670729-822004-450x271.jpg" alt="三和国小低年级女子个人金牌。" width="601" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14670729" class="wp-caption-text">三和国小低年级女子个人金牌。（桃园市府教育局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>朱辰右、曾国玙及陈致诚、曾紫萦凭借着绝佳默契分别夺得高年级组男子双人及混合双人金牌。</p>
<figure id="14670730" aria-describedby="caption-14670730" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670730-822005-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670730-822005-450x338.jpg" alt="三和国小选手与裁判合影。" width="602" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14670730" class="wp-caption-text">三和国小选手与裁判合影。（桃园市府教育局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钟荣祥说，本次赛事感谢教练、老师们与家长后援会在两天烈阳下的陪伴与支持，更佩服参赛的选手，展现出最佳的运动精神与学习态度。期许下一届能有更好的成绩展现。◇</p>
