<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】日制水平对向混能 2026 Subaru Crosstrek Limited Hybrid</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682913-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_29-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 Subaru Crosstrek Limited Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月24日讯】既然Subaru Crosstrek拥有Boxer水平对向引擎，又有引以自豪的Symmetric四驱系统，那么还有什么可以令Crosstrek更为吸引人？答案是融合了水平对向引擎的Hybrid系统，使第三代的Crosstrek续航更长、走得更远，也节省更多燃油。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d1tuw6k1rjq3u6.cloudfront.net/L9yUJQv9ehia"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/uGVj80OTMtQ?si=IfxuOuG6pymG8FBC" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Crosstrek Limited Hybrid在外形设计，与一般Crosstrek没两样，只是在车侧与尾闸有“Hybrid”的徽章，Crosstrek有独特的方六边形进风口栏栅、更大的轮拱包围、圆形雾灯和旋风形四幅式轮圈等。Crosstrek Hybrid提供多种外观颜色，测试车选用了其中一种较特别的Sand Dune Pearl车身颜色。可惜是Crosstrek并没有电动尾闸，需手动开关。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14682877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682877" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682877-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_32.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682877-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_32-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682877" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Subaru Crosstrek Limited Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14682878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682878-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_38.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682878-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_38-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682878" class="wp-caption-text">车侧“Hybrid”徽章。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Crosstrek Limited Hybrid搭载了一台2.5升Boxer水平对向引擎，结合电动马达及高效能的锂离子电池，综合马力为194匹，单是引擎可发出162匹马力和154磅呎扭矩。动力通过Lineartronic CVT变速箱传送。此外，Crosstrek Limited Hybrid的最大拖车能力达到1,500磅。Crosstrek Limited Hybrid拥有与汽油版Crosstrek一样的8.7英寸的离地间隙。进场角与纯汽油版Crosstrek车型一样是18.0度；离去角则有30.1度；斜坡过渡角有19.7度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14682879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682879-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_43.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682879-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_43-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682879" class="wp-caption-text">2.5升Boxer水平对向引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Crosstrek Hybrid配备了Subaru的可选择性双功能X-MODE系统。该系统提供雪地/泥土（Snow／Dirt）模式和深雪/泥泞（Deep Snow／Mud）模式，再加上陡坡缓降控制。为了在不同的路况下最大限度地提高牵引力，每种模式都专门对引擎输出和CVT传动比位置作调整，并增强了牵引控制系统的控制逻辑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14682881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682881" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682881-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_52.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682881-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_52-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682881" class="wp-caption-text">X-MODE系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与2026 Crosstrek的所有车型一样，Limited Hybrid也配备了EyeSight驾驶辅助技术，其中包括自适应巡航控制、碰撞预警制动、碰撞时油门管理、车道偏离和摆动警示以及前车启动警示。此外，盲点侦测和车道变换辅助以及后方交叉警示都是标配。Crosstrek Wilderness还标配了自动紧急转向。该功能与EyeSight和盲点侦测协同工作，以帮助控制转向，以避免在时速不超过50英里的情况下发生碰撞。标配的反向自动制动功能在狭窄的越野道路和停车时提供了额外的安全保护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14682882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682882-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_54.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682882-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_54-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682882" class="wp-caption-text">三幅式方向盘。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车内配备11.6英寸触控萤幕资讯系统，名为Starlink，提供Apple CarPlay、Android Auto、蓝牙通讯、AM/FM、倒车影像、SiriusXM与HD Radio，在萤幕下方是空调与车辆设定控制。而驾驶座前方是一个12.3英寸数位仪表板。另外，其他新标配包括：电动车窗（前座自动升降）、Auto Stop-Start、电动门锁与后视镜、可调式方向机柱、Keyless Entry、引擎防盗系统、前方USB-A及USB-C与3.5mm AUX端口、后座 60/40 分离倒覆、地毯脚踏垫、车顶横杆固定点与迎宾灯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14682883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682883-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_45.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682883-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_45-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682883" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14682884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682884-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_49.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682884-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_49-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682884" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Crosstrek Limited Hybrid内装采用StarTex灰色耐用材质，并使用橙色线缝，增加视觉效果。音响方面，Limited是最高版本，所以选用Harman Kardon 10 喇叭音响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14682886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682886-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_56.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682886-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682886" class="wp-caption-text">行李舱容量不少。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14682887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14682887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682887-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_41.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14682887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14682887-2026_Subaru_Crosstrek_Hybrid_41-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14682887" class="wp-caption-text">尾闸也有“Hybrid”徽章。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Crosstrek Hybrid于日本群马县工厂生产，其余车型则于美国印第安纳州Lafayette工厂制造，所以要求日本制造的消费者，就不用想也是要选择Hybrid版Crosstrek了。◇</p>
