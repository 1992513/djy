<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中华公所新届职员上任 获赞唐人街最有影响力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669177-DSC06329-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙中华公所1月3日举办新届职员就职典礼。左起：英文书记阮鸿灿、中文书记张青梅、主席雷国辉、财政陈余宝爱、核数伍振中。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年01月05日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）1月3日中午，纽英崙中华公所活动厅内高朋满座，5位新届职员在各界嘉宾的见证下正式就职，被波士顿市长称赞为“唐人街最有影响力”的选举结果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14669164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669164-DSC06243.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669164-DSC06243-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669164" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所1月3日举办新届职员就职典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14669172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669172-DSC06296.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669172-DSC06296-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669172" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所1月3日举办新届职员就职典礼。侨社及政界诸多嘉宾莅临。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中华公所新届主席雷国辉、中文书记张青梅、英文书记阮鸿灿、财政陈余宝爱、核数伍振中当天正式就职，开启两年任期。其中，雷国辉、阮鸿灿和陈余宝爱都连任上届职位，张青梅从核数转任中文书记，伍振中是第一次加入职员团队。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14669173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669173-DSC06300.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669173-DSC06300-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669173" class="wp-caption-text">政要嘉宾祝贺中华公所新届职员。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14669175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669175-DSC06317.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669175-DSC06317-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669175" class="wp-caption-text">新届5位职员切蛋糕，庆祝就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14669159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669159-DSC06192.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669159-DSC06192-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669159" class="wp-caption-text">就职典礼的蛋糕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>发展平价住房 支持侨社活动</strong></h2>
<p>纽英崙中华公所不仅是波士顿华埠的龙头团体，还拥有价值上亿美元的平价住房产业，包括大同村、华福楼，还有即将开工的Herald街50号大楼等等。雷国辉在就职演说中谈到了中华公所在发展平价住房和支持侨社活动等方面的计划。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14669169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669169-DSC06284.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669169-DSC06284-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669169" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉介绍未来两年的发展计划。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14669160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669160" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669160-DSC06203.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669160-DSC06203-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669160" class="wp-caption-text">麻州参议员柯林斯（Nick Collins，左4）向中华公所职员颁赠州议会褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他谈到，任期内最重要的工作，是装修有52年历史的大同村。服务社区半个世纪，这些建筑已积累许多问题，需要妥善维修。</p>
<p>Herald街50号平价住房大楼工程正在推进，今年春季可能邀请大家参与破土仪式。新楼建成后，将提供117个单元的平价住房，以及25,000平方英尺的超市空间。</p>
<p>中华公所本部的电梯已经完工，正等待市政府检查；接下来，楼内将设立华人移民历史传统中心，这项工作应在本届任期内完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14669162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669162-DSC06234.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669162-DSC06234-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669162" class="wp-caption-text">参与就职典礼的余风采堂妇女组合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14669163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669163" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669163-DSC06239.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669163-DSC06239-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669163" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn，右）向中华公所职员颁赠褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了发展产业之外，雷国辉也展望“与我们侨社和谐合作，我们董事会大力支持，以及政府方面给予足够支持”。</p>
<p>雷国辉感谢来宾们支持中华公所，欢迎其他4位职员投入服务，也感谢卸任中文书记翁宇才过去几年的工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14669178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669178-DSC06241.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669178-DSC06241-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669178" class="wp-caption-text">政要嘉宾祝贺中华公所新届职员。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><strong>政要嘉宾道贺</strong></h2>
<p>当天，华埠各侨团代表，以及麻州多位政要嘉宾都到场向新届职员道贺。这场就职典礼由陈黄海兰主持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14669165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669165-DSC06246.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669165-DSC06246-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669165" class="wp-caption-text">陈黄海兰主持就职典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）笑言：“这是我们城市非常重要的传统之一，就是庆祝唐人街真正的选举结果，也是最有影响力的，代表着久远的服务社区历史的选举。”</p>
<p>她谈到，近几年中华公所取得了很多成果，她很自豪能参加其平价住房剪彩典礼，也期待看到更多住房竣工，看到华人传统文化得到传承，居民获得公益服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14669167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669167-DSC06262.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669167-DSC06262-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669167" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）向中华公所职员致贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14669161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669161" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669161-DSC06218.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669161-DSC06218-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669161" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员墨菲（Erin Murphy）致贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿市议员墨菲（Erin Murphy）、费连（Ed Flynn），麦德福市（Medford）市议员曾御宸（Justin Tseng），麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）、参议员柯林斯（Nick Collins），还有来自萨福克县（Suffolk County）地区检察官办公室、麻州高等法院等机构的代表，也都到场致词。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14669174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14669174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669174-DSC06304.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14669174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14669174-DSC06304-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14669174" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾左起：经文处处长廖朝宏、麦德福市市议员曾御宸、侨教中心主任高家富、经文处组长黄耀良。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>廖朝宏称赞，雷国辉主席连续当选，说明其工作得到社区的高度认可。他也期待未来与中华公所维持合作，为侨社带来更多精彩活动。</p>
