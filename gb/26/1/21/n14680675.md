<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桶柑熟了 年也近了</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680688-825202-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>近看桶柑的自然样貌。（林桔春提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月21日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾苗栗报导）一月的苗栗头份，天气转冷，却不显萧瑟。沿着宝山柑仔崎往水流东方向的山路前行，转过弯，一片柑橘园映入眼帘。橘红色的桶柑挂满枝头，在冬阳照射下显得特别亮眼。这不是刻意安排的观光行程，而是日常生活中最真实的采收现场，却因为季节对了，多了一份年味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14680676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680676" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680676-825189.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680676-825189-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="499" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680676" class="wp-caption-text">戴着手套的采果工人，小心剪下成熟的桶柑，枝头橘红饱满，是一月最当季的风景。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14680679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680679" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680679-825192.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680679-825192-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="498" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680679" class="wp-caption-text">桶柑成串挂在树上，果实紧实饱满，是冬季日夜温差孕育出的自然甜味。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14680681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680681" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680681-825194.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680681-825194-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="499" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680681" class="wp-caption-text">红色农用车停在园中，黄色塑胶篮层层堆叠，准备将新鲜桶柑送往下一站。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>果园里的节奏 不急却不停</h3>
<p>果园里，采果的工人各自忙碌。有人剪果，有人整理，有人将装满的黄色塑胶篮搬到一旁集中。剪刀“喀嚓”一声，桶柑落入掌心，再被轻轻放进篮中。动作熟练而安静，没有多余的言语，却彼此配合得刚刚好。树下排好的空篮，很快就被一篮篮橘红色填满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14680684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680684" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680684-825198.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680684-825198-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680684" class="wp-caption-text">果园里的运输节奏。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14680682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680682" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680682-825195.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680682-825195-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680682" class="wp-caption-text">采好的桶柑被倒入大篮中，果香随着动作在空气中散开。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14680677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680677" style="width: 502px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680677-825190.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680677-825190-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="502" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680677" class="wp-caption-text">果树间，一篮篮黄色塑胶篮整齐排放，采果、整理、搬运各司其职，节奏不急却不停。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>不求完美外表 却是最好滋味</h3>
<p>桶柑的外皮不算细致，表面带着自然斑点，却是懂吃的人最期待的冬季水果。这个时节的桶柑，果肉紧实，甜中带点微酸，水分充足。果农说，最近日夜温差够、天气稳定，正是桶柑最对味的时候。再过一阵子，风味就不一样了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14680678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680678" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680678-825191.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680678-825191-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680678" class="wp-caption-text">采果工人低身伸手，在茂密树叶间挑选成熟桶柑，动作熟练而专注。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14680687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680687" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680687-825201.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680687-825201-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680687" class="wp-caption-text">桶柑成串挂满枝条，在阳光下微微下垂，显示今年收成的丰厚。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>一篮一篮 装的是整个冬天</h3>
<p>果园一角，红色的小农用车停着，黄色塑胶篮一层层堆叠上去，形成很有画面的对比。有人把满篮桶柑倒进大篮，有人再将空篮送回树下。这些看似重复的动作，其实承载着一整年的期待，也是农家迎接新年的准备。摄影家林桔春是在拍完木炭窑后，返程途中偶然遇见这个采柑场景。没有事先约定，纯粹是刚好路过、刚好遇上。热心的年轻采柑工人还当场递上一颗现采桶柑，剥开后立刻品尝，果香在嘴里散开，那一口甜，让人忍不住想起过年桌上的水果盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14680683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680683" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680683-825197.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680683-825197-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="499" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680683" class="wp-caption-text">剪刀轻合，桶柑落入掌心，这样的动作在果园里重复了无数次。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14680680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680680" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680680-825193.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680680-825193-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680680" class="wp-caption-text">两位采果工人在树下分工采摘，满篮的桶柑很快堆满果园一角。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>桶柑下树 年味上门</h3>
<p>在苗栗头份的山坡上，桶柑一颗颗被摘下，时间也慢慢往年节靠近。没有喧闹的叫卖声，只有冬日阳光、果树与人默默合作的画面。对许多人来说，过年的感觉，或许就是这样悄悄来的，看见收成、闻到果香，心里自然就暖了起来。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14680689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14680689" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680689-825203.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14680689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14680689-825203-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14680689" class="wp-caption-text">采果工人分散在树列间，各自专注手上的工作，黄色篮子沿着行间一路排开。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
