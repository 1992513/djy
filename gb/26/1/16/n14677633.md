<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>清闲俱乐部新春联欢 歌舞抽奖笑声满堂</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677637-1-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>清闲俱乐部新春联欢合影。（图/游云真提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年01月17日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）清闲俱乐部于1月15日（周四）中午在台湾人活动中心举行2026年新春年会，在副会长林翠梅、活动组黄灿琴、顾问游云真及干部团队通力合作规划下，活动内容丰富多元，现场准备多道台湾家乡味佳肴、大蛋糕与水果，吸引近百位新旧会员共襄盛举，会场洋溢着浓浓的新年喜气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14677638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14677638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677638-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14677638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677638-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14677638" class="wp-caption-text">义工团队为新春联欢午餐准备中。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14677639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14677639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677639-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14677639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677639-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14677639" class="wp-caption-text">副会长林翠梅（左）和顾问游云真主持摸彩环节。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>年会由副会长林翠梅代表会长吴本立致词。她表示，吴会长因早已安排好的行程正在外地旅游，特别交代向会员致上问候与祝福。林翠梅首先感谢会员在新的一年拨冗参与年会，也肯定干部团队长时间用心筹备，期望带给大家一场丰富又欢乐的联欢活动。她同时感谢会长四处奔波募款，让现场抽奖活动更加精彩。</p>
<p>林翠梅并向所有热心捐助现金、提供抽奖机会的朋友表达诚挚谢意，感谢大家的慷慨与爱心。她强调，年会中每位与会者都是主角，“独乐乐不如众乐乐”，大家聚在一起，笑声越多，福气越旺。特别提醒，最大奖200元现金将留到最后抽出，鼓励大家全程参与、共享喜悦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14677640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14677640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677640-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14677640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677640-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14677640" class="wp-caption-text">黄荣富演唱《成功的条件》。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在热烈掌声中，年会进入余兴节目时段。首先由陈琼晖与Mike Kidder带来华尔滋国标舞表演，舞姿优雅、掌声不断。接着会员黄荣富、陈元华、王庆隆、李海伦、李宽成、江朝雄、吴真惠及黄灿琴等人轮番登台卡拉OK演唱，歌声动人，气氛温馨热络。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14677641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14677641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677641-5.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14677641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677641-5-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14677641" class="wp-caption-text">郭正泉（中）率领气功团队“雄而有力”表演。（图/游云真提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14677643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14677643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677643-7.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14677643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677643-7-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14677643" class="wp-caption-text">全体一起进行健康手指操。（图/游云真提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>顾问游云真穿插分享幽默的台湾俗语歇后语，以猜谜方式与全场互动，笑声此起彼落，为会场增添欢乐气氛。曾美玉则带领大家进行健康手指操，让会员在欢乐中兼顾养生；游云真并分享水上有氧运动经验，鼓励大家保持规律运动。随后，郭正泉率领气功团队“雄而有力”带来气功表演，动作整齐有力，获得满堂喝采。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14677642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14677642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677642-6.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14677642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677642-6-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14677642" class="wp-caption-text">陈琼晖（左）与Mike Kidder夫妇出席并表演国标舞。（图/游云真提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动中穿插礼物与现金红包抽奖，将现场气氛推向高潮。与会者皆获赠内含金元宝的福袋，象征财运亨通、迎新纳福。活动尾声，赠红包者、中奖人及元月份寿星一同于蛋糕前合影留念，在温馨欢笑中为联欢年会画下圆满句点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14677644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14677644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677644-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14677644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14677644-8-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14677644" class="wp-caption-text">与会者排队取餐。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次新春年会不仅展现会员间深厚情谊，也再次体现俱乐部凝聚力与向心力，为2026年揭开温馨欢乐的序幕。</p>
