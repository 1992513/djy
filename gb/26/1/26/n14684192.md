<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠狮子会新年筹款 感恩社区助力公益</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684175-DSC06885-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>1月24日，波士顿华埠狮子会在新月宫酒家举办年度筹款晚宴。华埠狮子会及麻州友会代表合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年01月26日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）免费健康检查、服务老人院、社区保健讲座……波士顿华埠狮子会持续提供这些公益服务，离不开热心会员及社区伙伴的支持。</p>
<p>1月24日晚上，华埠狮子会在新月宫酒家举办年度筹款餐会，与上百名嘉宾一起庆祝过去一年的公益成就，感恩大家的积极助力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14684174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684174-DSC06880.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684174-DSC06880-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684174" class="wp-caption-text">参与晚宴的华埠狮子会成员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14684170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684170-DSC06864.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684170-DSC06864-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684170" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿华埠狮子会在新月宫酒家举办年度筹款晚宴，上百人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚恰逢本次冬季最冷的寒潮，但新月宫酒家仍济济一堂，气氛热烈。一些参与者笑言，来到人多、热闹的酒家，很快就感觉暖和了。</p>
<p>参与晚宴的嘉宾，除了波士顿华埠狮子会成员之外，还有麻州多地分会的代表，以及中华公所、黄氏公所、妇女新运会等华埠团体成员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14684171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684171-DSC06868.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684171-DSC06868-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684171" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿华埠狮子会在新月宫酒家举办年度筹款晚宴，上百人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14684165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684165-DSC06849.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684165-DSC06849-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684165" class="wp-caption-text">林柔妤（左）和伍国光担任司仪。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚宴由林柔妤和伍国光担任司仪，一如既往地以“四部分”开场。蔡倩婷领唱《美国第一章歌》（First Verse of America）；阮鸿灿领读《效忠誓词》（Pledge of Allegiance）；伍国光带领祈祷；余丽媖带领“狮子祝酒”：“我不在你之上，我不在你之下，但我们一齐努力。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14684166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684166-DSC06854.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684166-DSC06854-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684166" class="wp-caption-text">蔡倩婷领唱《美国第一章歌》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14684167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684167-DSC06858.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684167-DSC06858-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684167" class="wp-caption-text">阮鸿灿领读《效忠誓词》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，国际狮子会大使李源沛介绍各地分会来宾，华埠狮子会会长汤伟雄向友会及侨社伙伴表达感恩。</p>
<p>“尽管外面是那么寒冷，但我们在餐厅内是这么温暖！”汤伟雄说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14684168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684168-DSC06859.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684168-DSC06859-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684168" class="wp-caption-text">余丽媖带领“狮子祝酒”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14684169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684169-DSC06862.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684169-DSC06862-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684169" class="wp-caption-text">国际狮子会大使李源沛介绍各地分会来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他特别向前麻州狮子会33k区域总监理查森（Matthew L. Richardson）表达感恩，称赞后者“总是指导我去做正确的事”。汤伟雄还感谢黄国威在中华公所支持狮子会，也感谢陈国航和Ming Wong的“国王娱乐”每次为狮子会的活动提供音像技术支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14684172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684172-DSC06871.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684172-DSC06871-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684172" class="wp-caption-text">华埠狮子会会长汤伟雄欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14684173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14684173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684173-DSC06875.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14684173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14684173-DSC06875-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14684173" class="wp-caption-text">汤伟雄（右）感谢前麻州狮子会33k区域总监Matthew L. Richardson。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中国新年将至，汤伟雄还向大家拜早年，祝福大家马年“马到功成、龙马精神、财源滚滚”。◇</p>
