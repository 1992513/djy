<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超：曼联“换帅如换刀”在主场完胜曼城</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678858-GettyImages-2256725797.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
