<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>网红中介赴陆医美 抽佣高易逃跑难究责</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678719-824166-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>医美示意图。（123RF）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月18日讯】（大纪元记者赖玟茹台湾台北报导）跨境医美乱象频传，有网红积极中介中国医美疗程，引发多起医疗纷争后推托责任，还举办说明会招募更多业务。专家分析两个原因，一是高达75%的中介抽成暴利，二是事故发生后中介能直接消失，令受害者无从究责。</p>
<p>近期有受害者向媒体投诉，指某网红积极在社群中介中国医美疗程，却在引发多起医疗纠纷后推托责任，该网红也以高抽成为诱因招募业务。受害者还提及，网红与中国一位无任何相关执照却号称医学咨询师的程姓男子合作，集结粉丝在网路攻击台湾的医美疗程，间接推广网红在中国的医美中介生意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14678722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14678722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678722-824169.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14678722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678722-824169-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14678722" class="wp-caption-text">医美示意图。（ANTONIO SCORZA/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14678721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14678721" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678721-824168.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14678721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678721-824168-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14678721" class="wp-caption-text">医美示意图。（NEGAR/Middle East Images/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14678720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14678720" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678720-824167.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14678720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678720-824167-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14678720" class="wp-caption-text">医美示意图。（JOSE JORDAN/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>熟知中国医美产业的医美专家陈家骏（化名）向《大纪元时报》表示，医美中介与咨询系统这种模式最早源自于中国，在中国的医美产业，医美咨询师（早期称美容设计师）的地位凌驾于医师，由咨询师负责设计整套疗程，医师仅是执行者，且通常照单全收。由于手术量即是收入，医师往往忽视术后效果是否更佳，优先配合咨询师的操作。</p>
<p>以利益为核心的转介模式也衍生出惊人的抽佣文化。陈家骏表示，以双眼皮手术收费两万元为例，背后的抽佣比率高达50%~75%，等同于给中介的佣金最高可达1.5万元，这也间接解释私人诊所就算经营艰难仍不惜争取病源，也促使台湾网红积极中介国人赴中国大陆做医美手术。相较之下，台湾因为税务透明且医疗分润（PF）制度健全，抽佣鲜少超过一成，难以提供如中国市场的暴利诱因。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14678718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14678718" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678718-824158.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14678718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678718-824158-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14678718" class="wp-caption-text">医美示意图。（STR/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14678717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14678717" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678717-824157.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14678717" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678717-824157-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14678717" class="wp-caption-text">医美示意图。（STR/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<p>除高额利润外，另一项诱因在于逃避法律规范。在法治不透明下，万一发生手术事故，中介往往能直接跑掉或消失，在缺乏保障下受害者根本无从追究责任。</p>
<p>医疗操作端同样混乱不堪。陈家骏说，医美微整形最简单的项目如施打玻尿酸、肉毒杆菌，早期在中国情况很混乱。以肉毒杆菌为例，当时医生竟然会在脸部施打3、4支的剂量，然而一般情境下不会在脸部施打完1支的剂量。若真的在脸上一次施打多支剂量，恐怕导致失明或歪嘴等严重后果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14678715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14678715" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678715-824155.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14678715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678715-824155-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14678715" class="wp-caption-text">医美示意图。（STR/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14678714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14678714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678714-824154.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14678714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14678714-824154-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14678714" class="wp-caption-text">医美示意图。（LIU JIN / AFP) (Photo by LIU JIN/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<p>为了应对超量开药，中国医师也发展出一套技巧，例如病患若开了10支玻尿酸，医师施打时会手持纱布，一边将少量药剂打在病人身上，一边将剩余药剂排在纱布上，虽然浪费了药剂，但诊所仍会按使用数量收费。</p>
<p>陈家骏说，在中国进行微整型或医美手术，医师常会采取不必要的操作，“不应该进行的手术也给你做，这真的很糟糕。”不过，病患对手术真实的处理情况往往无从得知。</p>
<p>在中国医美产业，消费者不仅处于资讯不对称的弱势，更在中共威权体制框架下失去了争取医疗保障与法律救济的空间。◇</p>
