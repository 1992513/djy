<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>迎中华民国115年 波士顿200侨胞元旦升旗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667973-DSC06116-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿地区侨界1月1日举办升旗典礼及茶话会，欢庆中华民国115年到来。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年01月03日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨牛顿市报导）在1月1日的寒冷雪天，波士顿地区200多位侨胞欢聚在牛顿市（Newton）的侨教中心，升青天白日满地红旗，迎接中华民国开国115年。</p>

<p>当天早上风急雪大，而到了上午11点，风雪已经停止。在侨教中心室外广场，波士顿台湾龙舟队的孙育民和苏家民，以及波士顿台湾影展协会的林致中和夏承贤展开两面大国旗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667963" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667963-DSC06075.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667963-DSC06075-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667963" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿台湾龙舟队的孙育民和苏家民展示国旗。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667962" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667962-DSC06073.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667962-DSC06073-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667962" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿台湾影展协会的林致中和夏承贤展示国旗。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>剑桥合唱团副团长林泓安领唱美国和中华民国国歌，随后唱中华民国国旗歌。侨胞们在歌声中注视青天白日满地红旗缓缓升起。待歌声结束，港澳之友会的谢中之和其他一些侨胞高喊“中华民国万岁”，气氛颇为热烈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667964-DSC06081.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667964-DSC06081-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667964" class="wp-caption-text">剑桥合唱团副团长林泓安（中）领唱国歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667965-DSC06090.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667965-DSC06090-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667965" class="wp-caption-text">200多位来宾在室外参加升旗典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>侨胞支持民主自由</strong></h2>
<p>这场庆典由纽英崙中华专业人员协会主办。在升旗典礼过后，专协董事长游子扬、会长卢彦君首先向来宾们表达欢迎，感谢大家在海外支持中华民国台湾。</p>
<p>游子扬提到中共最近进行的围台军事演习，认为元旦升旗活动的盛况向“北京的独裁者”传递了清晰的信息，即“民主并不孤单、人权并不孤单、独立并不孤单、台湾并不孤单”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667968-DSC06102.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667968-DSC06102-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667968" class="wp-caption-text">黄顗蓉主持大会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667970" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667970-DSC06107.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667970-DSC06107-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667970" class="wp-caption-text">专协董事长游子扬（左）、会长卢彦君欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大会期间，驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任高家富特别颁赠褒奖状，感谢在2025年“双十国庆”系列庆典中做出卓越贡献的侨领。获奖者包括纽英崙中华总会主席余丽媖、纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉、国民党波士顿分部常委张韵兰、波士顿中华书法会会长黄周丽桃，以及纽英崙艺术学会会长梅宇国。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667977-DSC06129.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667977-DSC06129-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667977" class="wp-caption-text">大会颁发褒奖状。左起：侨教中心主任高家富、纽英崙艺术学会会长梅宇国、波士顿中华书法会会长黄周丽桃、中华公所主席雷国辉、经文处处长廖朝宏、中华总会主席余丽媖、国民党波士顿分部常委张韵兰。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667966-DSC06093.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667966-DSC06093-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667966" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿荣光会参加升旗典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667967" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667967-DSC06097.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667967-DSC06097-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667967" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿明慧学校及台湾华语文学习中心的老师参加升旗典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>雷国辉谈到，过去一年来，华埠侨团坚持服务侨胞，也参与支持中华民国的活动。每年在侨教中心和波士顿市政府广场升旗，代表着侨胞对中华民国的热爱。</p>
<p>侨务委员陈仕维说，早上还在下大雪，而在升国旗的时候完全没有风雪，“好像就是，我们中华民国面对什么困难，最后还是风平浪静”。</p>
<p>“我们中华民国还是维护自由、民主、法治，我们中华民国还是傲然地在国际上发挥我们应有的力量。”他说。</p>
<p>前中华民国立法委员、现任华侨救国联合总会理事长童惠珍也从纽约来到波士顿，参加了元旦升旗典礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667974-DSC06120.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667974-DSC06120-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667974" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员陈仕维祝愿中华民国国泰民安。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><strong>回顾台湾成就 歌舞欢庆新年</strong></h2>
<p>经文处处长廖朝宏回顾了2025年台湾在经济、外交等方面的成就。他谈到，去年台湾的经济成长率超过7.32%，达到15年来最高，就业率也达到25年来最佳，台北股市在年底创下历史新高。</p>
<p>廖朝宏还谈到，新英格兰地区各州都通过了决议案或声明，支持与台湾深化合作关系。前两天中国举行军事演习时，诸多国家皆表示支持台湾、支持自由民主。新年到来，廖朝宏也期盼侨胞们继续支持台湾向国际社会前行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667972-DSC06114.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667972-DSC06114-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667972" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏回顾台湾在2025年的成就。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667985" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667985-DSC06169.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667985-DSC06169-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667985" class="wp-caption-text">参与升旗典礼的部分嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>牛顿高地社区发展协会董事局代表西克伦（Garrett Van Siclen）也参加了这场庆典，并感谢大家通过“牛顿台湾日”活动，为牛顿市带来丰富的文化。</p>
<p>他还说，作为35年的牛顿市居民，他很自豪地看到台湾人社区在此扎根，牛顿市与台湾人民和政府建立了坚实的关系。他期待今年春季再次参加牛顿台湾日的精彩活动。</p>
