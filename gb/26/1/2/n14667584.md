<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>文观七年成果获肯定 陈璧君正式就任云林副县长</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667585-821080-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>文官七年成果获肯定  副县长陈璧君今天正式履新。（廖素贞／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月02日讯】（大纪元记者 廖素贞台湾云林报导）云林县政府人事再添新战力。原文化观光处处长陈璧君，2日正式履新，出任云林县副县长。县长张丽善表示，陈璧君深耕文化与观光七年有成，不仅成功带动云林观光量能成长，更翻转外界对云林的城市印象，此次拔擢实至名归，未来将在县政推动上发挥更全面的影响力。</p>
<p>张丽善指出，陈璧君任内展现高度专业与执行力，推动多项指标性文化与观光政策，让云林逐步站上全国与国际舞台。近年全县年观光人次突破2,000万，各类大型活动与政策成果屡获国内外评比肯定，展现县府团队长期耕耘的实力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667594" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667594-821101.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667594-821101-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667594" class="wp-caption-text">副县长陈璧君与艺文界人士合影（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667586" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667586-821081.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667586-821081-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667586" class="wp-caption-text">副县长办公室设于县府第二办公大楼5楼，履新当日，县长张丽善率县府各局处首长 议员到场祝贺（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667587-821083.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667587-821083-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667587" class="wp-caption-text">副县长陈璧君与艺文界及各界人士大合照（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张县长也提到，陈璧君与副县长谢淑亚，长期与她并肩作战，三人以女性特有的坚韧与务实精神，共同推动县政发展，宛如县府稳定运作的“金铁三角”。她期许在未来一年，云林能持续精进、异军突起，不仅在国内受到瞩目，也能与国际接轨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667588-821085.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667588-821085-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667588" class="wp-caption-text">艺文界人士赠送副县长陈璧君画作（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667590" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667590-821088.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667590-821088-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667590" class="wp-caption-text">浊水溪书画协会以英才得展，祝贺陈璧君荣任副县长（廖素真／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667592" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667592-821096.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667592-821096-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667592" class="wp-caption-text">副县长陈璧君与各社团理事长合影（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈璧君表示，返乡服务云林并担任文化观光处处长七年，是一段充实且珍贵的历程。在张丽善县长带领下，县府团队目标明确、分工清楚，让她从民间进入公部门，累积了宝贵的治理经验，也为云林未来发展奠定良好基础。</p>
<p>她指出，升任副县长后，初衷不变，将与张丽善县长及谢淑亚副县长携手合作，统筹县府23个局处，延续过去七年累积的成果，进一步深化政策、落实施政，确保各项建设与规划稳健推进。</p>
<p>陈璧君强调，这是一项全新的任务与挑战，未来将结合县府优秀团队力量，从更宏观的视野进行县政规划，持续为云林乡亲服务。能在任期最后一年以不同角色投入县政，她深感荣幸，也将以谦逊态度全力以赴。</p>
<p>因应立法院于114年11月三读通过《地方制度法》修正案，自115年元旦起，县市政府得设副县市长2人、副秘书长1人。张丽善县长经通盘考量后，任命陈璧君出任副县长，并于今日正式履新。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667589" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667589-821086.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667589-821086-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667589" class="wp-caption-text">副县长办公室设于县府第二办公大楼5楼（廖素贞／大纪元）<span style="font-size: 16px;">）</span></figcaption></figure>

<p>陈璧君副县长办公室设于县府第二办公大楼5楼，履新当日，县长张丽善、秘书长曾元煌、县府局处首长、议员及艺文、社团界人士逾百人到场祝贺，现场花篮林立，展现高度人气与支持。</p>
