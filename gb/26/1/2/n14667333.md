<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【看电影学英文】 ‘Mina’ (1) Wizard vs. Sorcerer</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667335-12801-600x400.webp" />
<h6>《美娜传奇》动画片海报（干净世界提供，甄宇合成）</h6>
<hr>
<p>这次【看电影学英文】系列分享的是奇幻历险动画片《美娜传奇》。《美娜传奇》讲述了公主美娜被专门绑架未婚公主的卡匝伯尔所掳，国王许诺救回公主者即为驸马，并继承王国。于是，几位各有所图的追求者踏上奇幻冒险之旅。</p>
<p>《美娜传奇》中的奇幻元素有很多，比如卡匝伯尔的胡子有强大魔力。娜希娜会变形，类似于孙悟空七十二变，不过除了人形，她只会变两种动物。芬恩能用神奇液体让池水变为遥视镜，而他家里茶壶会准备热饮，花儿会扫地、烤点心，椅子能让客人坐下……家伙什能像仆从一样自己做家务，是不是很让人羡慕啊？这次我们就先看看《美娜传奇》里面跟奇幻元素相关的英文吧。</p>
<div>
<h2 id="ggWrapper_z_a_c_1" class="ggAdZone_gg-banner-ad_container__nctW7 ggAdZone_gg-banner-ad_in_article_container__OT2Hh" data-ggwrapper-uniqid="z_a_c_1|1767320999587" data-ggwrapper-zoneid="z_a_c_1" data-ggwrapper-extzoneid="z_a_c_1"><strong>Wizard vs. Sorcerer</strong></h2>
</div>
<p>《美娜传奇》中，卡匝伯尔被称为 sorcerer，芬恩被称为 wizard，而娜希娜则是 witch。wizard 与 witch 是很多人熟悉的单词，意为男巫师和女巫师，那麽 sorcerer 是什么意思呢？跟 wizard 有什么不同呢？</p>
<p>Sorcerer 常常被翻译成巫师，术士，魔法师，但这个术士的翻译容易让人困惑，因为术士或方术士这样的词语在中文中是有明确定义的，如方术士是修习方术、道术、医术、命术、相术、卜术之人，包括以占卜（术数）、预言、法术、气功、炼丹、打坐等方式，跟奇幻世界的魔法师虽有部分意思重叠，但用术士来翻译 sorcerer 并不贴切。Sorcery 在英文中的意思是<span dir="ltr" lang="zh-Hant">通过灵体来做事，尤其是邪灵</span>，可翻译成<span dir="ltr" lang="zh-Hant">魔法、妖术</span>。所以 sorcerer 作为施法者，往往是施魔法，所以翻成魔法师比较贴近原意。</p>
<p>我们先看一下动画片中出现这几个单词的例句。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667832" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667832" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667832-128011.webp"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667832-128011-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667832" class="wp-caption-text">罗南告诉熊爸爸卡匝伯尔绑架了美娜（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>I’m looking for my fiancée Mina. An evil sorcerer named Kezabor kidnapped her.<br />
我在找我未婚妻美娜。一个叫卡匝伯尔的邪恶魔法师绑架了她。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667833" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667833-1280122.webp"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667833-1280122-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667833" class="wp-caption-text">熊爸爸提出带罗南找善良巫师芬恩（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当熊爸爸介绍芬恩时，这样说——<br />
But I can take you to a kind wizard named Finn. He might know how to find that villainous sorcerer.<br />
但我可以带你去找一个叫芬恩的善良巫师。他或许知道怎样找到那个作恶多端的魔法师。</p>
<p>罗南见到芬恩后说——<br />
Losing the girl you loved to a maniacal sorcerer is a fate far worse than death.<br />
失去你所深爱的女孩，看她落入一个疯狂的魔法师之手，这样的命运比死亡更残酷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667834" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667834-128013.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667834-128013-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667834" class="wp-caption-text">芬恩介绍绑架美娜的女巫（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>The dragon that serves Kazabor is a witch named Nahina, my fatal passion.<br />
听命于卡匝伯尔的龙是名叫娜希娜的女巫，她是我的致命诱惑。</p>
<p>Sorcerer 与 wizard 前面常有表达邪恶与善良的形容词，说明这两个单词本身的褒贬含义并非完全固化。如《魔戒》中的甘道夫一开始是灰袍巫师，而比他更高级别的白袍巫师萨鲁曼后来却魔变了。《美娜传奇》中，卡匝伯尔专门绑架未婚公主，是个臭名昭&#33879;的邪恶魔法师；他的巨人兄弟有吐风喷火的异能，虽然被卡匝伯尔胁迫看守关隘，但他赠给罗南的秘密武器是破解卡匝伯尔魔法的关键，应该算是个不坏的魔法师吧。</p>
<p>此外，sorcerer 与 wizard 法术的来源也不同。在动画片《美娜传奇》中，卡匝伯尔的魔法来自于他的胡子；而芬恩要教罗南法术时，摆出一摞书，要罗南要花时间研习。这恰好说明了 sorcerer 与 wizard 的区别，即 sorcerer 的超能力来自血统或天赋，而 wizard 则通过学习获得。《魔戒》中的哈利?波特一开始天赋异禀，有点像 sorcerer，但后来进入魔法学校学习，正式走上 wizard 专业道路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667835" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667835-128014.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667835-128014-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667835" class="wp-caption-text">芬恩想教罗南法术（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Wizardry isn’t an easy thing to learn!<br />
法术可不是容易学的！</p>
<h2><strong>Spell</strong></h2>
<p>在奇幻作品里，spell是指咒语或魔法等，如《格林童话》里的诅咒把王子变成了青蛙，而在《美娜传奇》中，戒指的魔法可以让公主变成雕像，或昏睡不醒。</p>
<p>作为名词，spell 前面可以用 cast, put 或 place 等动词来表示施咒或作法的意思。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667836" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667836-128015.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667836-128015-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667836" class="wp-caption-text">年轻的芬恩害怕娜希娜拒绝自己</figcaption></figure>
<p>So I cast a spell that would guarantee she would fall in love.<br />
于是我施咒来确保她会爱上我。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667837" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667837-128016.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667837-128016-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667837" class="wp-caption-text">娜希娜身上的咒语解除了（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>As I fade away, so will the spell I placed on you.<br />
当我逐渐消逝时，我施加在你身上的咒语也将随之解除。</p>
<p>解除魔法的动词可以用 break, lift 或 take off 等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667838" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667838-128017.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667838-128017-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667838" class="wp-caption-text">芬恩请求娜希娜解除魔法（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>I&#8217;m the one who wronged you, not her. Take the spell off!<br />
我是那个伤害你的人，不是她。解除魔法吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667839-128018.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667839-128018-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667839" class="wp-caption-text">芬恩想到了解除魔法的办法（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>I know a way to break the spell and save Mina.<br />
我知道一个解除魔法，解救美娜的办法。</p>
<p>饶阁岱把昏睡不醒的美娜带回王宫，并要求国王为他和美娜举办婚礼时说——<br />
I&#8217;m certain the joy of our wedding ceremony will break any spell.<br />
我相信我们婚礼的喜庆会让任何魔法失效。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667840-128019.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667840-128019-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667840" class="wp-caption-text">芬恩终于看到咒语解除了（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>The spell has lifted!<br />
咒语解除了！</p>
<h2><strong>Enchant</strong></h2>
<p>Enchant 有使人陶醉，令人着迷的意思；在魔法世界里，则是施以魔法的意思。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667841-128020.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667841-128020-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667841" class="wp-caption-text">美娜要求卡匝伯尔放了自己和其他公主（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Let me go and free all the princesses you’ve enchanted.<br />
放我走，并放了所有被你施魔法的公主们。</p>
<p>Are you enchanted?<br />
你着了魔吗？</p>
<p>She’s enchanted to sleep.<br />
她中了魔咒，陷入沉睡。</p>
<h2><strong>Mirage?</strong></h2>
<p>重音在第二个音节，意为海市蜃楼，幻景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667842" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667842-128021.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667842-128021-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667842" class="wp-caption-text">不同族裔都在讲述同一个传说（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Deep inside an endless desert, past the lands where no one ever enters, lies a mirage that hides a phantom castle.<br />
在无尽沙漠的深处，穿越那片从未有人踏足之地，海市蜃楼中隐藏着一座幽灵城堡。</p>
<p>Eastern treats and banquets-No mirage!<br />
东方的珍馐与盛宴——绝非幻象！</p>
<h2><strong>Phantom</strong></h2>
<p>Phantom 作为名词，指幽灵、鬼魂；作为形容词，则指像幽灵或鬼魂的，或者幻觉的，虚幻的，虚假的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667848-128081.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667848-128081-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667848" class="wp-caption-text">传说中卡匝伯尔的幽灵城堡无人曾及（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>And not a single warrior can find the phantom castle of the evil sorcerer.<br />
却无任何一位勇士能寻得那邪恶巫师的幽灵城堡。</p>
<p>顺便说，剧名中带有 phantom 一词的音乐剧 The Phantom of the Opera（《歌剧魅影》）是史上最成功的音乐剧之一，并于2004年被翻拍为同名电影。</p>
<h2><strong>Hear things</strong></h2>
<p>Hear things 指听到不存在的声音，幻听。</p>

<h2><strong>Invisibility hat</strong></h2>
<p>Invisibility hat 是指具有隐形功能的帽子，戴上可以隐身；而 invisible hat 则指帽子本身具有看不见的特点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667846" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667846-128023.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14667846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667846-128023-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667846" class="wp-caption-text">美娜发现包头巾的隐身功能（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>No way! An invisibility hat!<br />
不会吧！一顶隐形帽！</p>
<p>顺便说一下，《美娜传奇》中那顶隐身帽的英文名字是 turban, 意为包头巾或缠头巾，是指用布料缠绕在头上的饰物或头饰，在南亚、中亚、西亚和北非等地尤为常见，与锡克教徒、印度教徒、穆斯林等宗教信仰有关。</p>
