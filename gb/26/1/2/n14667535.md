<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>照顾生活 守护心灵 陈清波文教基金会陪伴孩子好好长大</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667536-821037-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>照顾生活，守护心灵，陈清波文教基金会陪伴孩子好好长大。（许享富／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月02日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）成立二十八年的陈清波文教基金会，初心是回馈乡里，照顾苗栗孩子的生活，守护他们的心灵，陪伴他们好好长大。从“生命教育巡回演讲”“奖助学金”“进步奖”“年节礼金”的发放，到举办“清波夏令营”“清波小客厅演讲”“亲子手作课程”“大孩子的团聚”“进步奖欢聚”等等，基金会始终不忘初心，谨记创办人陈荣秋的叮咛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667540" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667540-821041.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667540-821041.jpeg" alt="" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667540" class="wp-caption-text">基金会董事长王励新表示，感谢董事会全力支持、同事与志工的辛劳，同时感受到小朋友在困难中逐渐站稳脚步，更感谢大哥大姐愿意将自己的经验带回来，照顾其他人。　（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667538" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667538-821039.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667538-821039.jpeg" alt="" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667538" class="wp-caption-text">陈清波文教基金会的2025年度成果报吿。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667539" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667539-821040.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667539-821040.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667539" class="wp-caption-text">受惠学子的心得报告：每一句感谢都是最动人的情话，每一个笑容都是最珍贵的宝物。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一年一度的“清波奖助学金颁奖典礼”，将在1月2日于照南国中视听中心举行，除了最重要的颁发助学金、奖学金外，更邀请即将大学毕业的孩子，与展开新生活的家长分享心路历程，同时回顾2025年温暖动人的时刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667537-821038.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667537-821038.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667537" class="wp-caption-text">陈清波文教基金会的精神——我能行，做自己的贵人。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667541" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667541-821042.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667541-821042.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667541" class="wp-caption-text">钟县长强调，陈清波文教基金会创办人陈荣秋先生除了将善心化为举动，更用智慧创办基金会，让基金会生生不息，造福更多孩子，期望大家都伸出援手帮助，相信力量会更大，齐心让孩子们看到希望，也让苗栗越来越幸福。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667542" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667542-821043.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667542-821043.jpeg" alt="" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667542" class="wp-caption-text">竹南镇长方进兴代表受奖乡镇（造桥乡、后龙镇）学生发表感言，以生活的小故事，来叙述基金会创办人陈荣秋的克己精神，宁节约自己，来帮受困的学生与家庭，完成陈清波父亲的大爱精神。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基金会很荣幸地邀请到苗栗县县长钟东锦、议长李文斌、教育处叶芯慧处长、竹南镇长方进兴、后龙镇长谢清辉、造桥乡长徐连斌等地方首长，亲临指导，为孩子们送上祝福。</p>
<p>基金会深耕在地，与各校情谊深厚。各校的校长、在地社团，与受助家庭，都出席典礼。是学校的鼎力相助、在地社团的共同参与，与家长们的信任，让基金会可以实践对孩子的承诺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667543" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667543-821044.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667543-821044.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667543" class="wp-caption-text">陈清波基金会颁发奖助学金。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667544" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667544-821045.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667544-821045.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667544" class="wp-caption-text">县长等人颁发奖助学金。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基金会的承诺，包括经济的支持，与心灵的守护。在经济支持方面，每年资助一百个家庭，每月五千元助学金。这笔助学金，不只让父母安心，更能补足孩子们学习所需。进步奖与年节礼金的颁发，更能激励孩子向上，同时让每个家庭都能好好过年。</p>
<p>心灵的守护，则让孩子们重拾笑容。连续三年举行的“清波夏令营”，是孩子们的年度大事；“亲子手作课”，让家长们暂时抛下烦恼，享受亲子的甜蜜时刻；“清波小客厅演讲”，开拓孩子视野；“大孩子的相聚”，让离乡求学的孩子们，知道基金会永远是他们的后盾；“进步奖”除了颁发奖金，更让得奖孩子邀请家长兄弟姐妺同来，一起看电影，欣赏舞台剧，得奖的骄傲里，有更多欢喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14667545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667545" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667545-821046.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667545-821046.jpeg" alt="" width="602" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667545" class="wp-caption-text">县长等人颁发奖助学金。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14667546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14667546" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667546-821047.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14667546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14667546-821047.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14667546" class="wp-caption-text">县长等人颁发奖助学金。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>二十八年来，陈清波文教基金会已经帮助超过五千名学生，耗资上亿元，用最实际的行动，照顾苗栗的孩子。有许多基金会照顾的孩子，已经长大成人，成为家庭的支柱；每年也都有新的小小孩，加入基金会。未来，基金会将持续努力，陪伴孩子好好长大。◇</p>
