<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】粗犷升级 征服野径 2026 Subaru Forester Wilderness</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668187-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 Subaru Forester Wilderness。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月03日讯】2026年Subaru Forester结合了前所未有的设计、技术和舒适性。新一代Forester的内外设计现代而流畅，前脸搭载崭新格栅与LED头灯相连，营造出一体化的盾牌效果。新车的肩线突出其高度和力量，而凸出的轮拱则强调其越野能力。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://ddpul9mprf1bn.cloudfront.net/E3kJNPHQvg0e"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/DcYt11Af5ec?si=8OQp5pwRbGiuNhfb" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Forester的越野和操控能力同样出色，拥有8.7英寸离地间隙，适合不同地形，而测试的Forester Wilderness更进一步，离地间隙增至9.3英寸，可跨越更严峻地形，成为Forester中的越野王者。Forester Wilderness也装上了特别为越野用的235／60 R17 102T All-Terrain轮胎，并配上17英寸哑黑色专用轮圈。另外，装配了两个雾灯，每个雾灯由6个LED组成，还有防撞器上的铜色装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668171-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668171-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668171" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Subaru Forester Wilderness。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14668172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668172-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_15.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668172-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_15-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668172" class="wp-caption-text">235／60 R17 102T All-Terrain轮。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>结构和安全方面，2026年款Forester使用了增强的Subaru全球平台（Subaru Global Platform），抗扭刚性提高10%，同时，车体刚性提升使动态表现更好，乘坐体验更为舒适，车内噪音也应有所降低，但事实上在高速公路行驶时，仍然有相当风噪传入车厢。然而，Forester在高速公路上的过弯表现非常好，在加速越线超车也得心应手。在越野路段测试，坚实底盘应对崎岖路面也没有扭曲，攀石上下坡也如履平地，悬挂系统应记一功。在下雨时，地上满是泥泞也难不倒Forester Wilderness。X-Mode越野系统有两种选择，分别为Snow／Mud及Snow／Dirt，再加上陡坡缓降控制，完全可用作挑战不同场合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668173-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668173-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668173" class="wp-caption-text">前大灯组。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14668174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668174-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668174-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668174" class="wp-caption-text">防撞器上的铜色装饰。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>标配的EyeSight驾驶辅助技术不仅进一步提升了安全性能，包括自动紧急刹车、盲点侦测、车道变换辅助、后方车流警示等。首次搭载的紧急停止辅助系统，能在驾驶者失去反应时，自动停车并通知紧急服务。此外，结合了“自适应巡航控制（Advanced Adapter Cruise Control）”及EyeSight的“路线置中Lane Centering）”，可以成为高速公路上的半自动驾驶辅助工具，但驾驶者仍需定时轻轻握着方向盘，让系统知道驾驶者仍专注路况。拥有双镜头的Eyesight，在半自动驾驶时表现出色，但转向角度有时不够准确，需要手动调节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668175-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668175-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668175" class="wp-caption-text">D柱上的“Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive”徽章。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内部设计同样出色，配置了11.6英寸Subaru Starlink多媒体系统，支援无线Apple CarPlay和无线Android Auto，垂直的萤幕有点像iPad，底部更有Home键，然而在刚启动时反应有点慢。仪表板和内饰设计更注重实用性，且为防止日常使用中的磨损而进行了改良。全景玻璃视野、暗色车顶内衬以及后排隐私玻璃则进一步提升了乘坐体验。在舒适性和便利性方面，新一代Forester进一步提升，车内空间更为宽敞，座椅设计更为支撑且减少疲劳感。中控台顶部装有红外线感应器（DriverFocus Distraction Mitigation System），可侦察驾驶者面部方向，当汽车行驶时而驾驶者面部不是面向前方，又或感应到驾驶者疲累，系统马上会发出警告。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668177-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_28.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668177-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668177" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14668178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668178-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668178-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668178" class="wp-caption-text">11.6英寸Subaru Starlink萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>搭载的2.5升Subaru Boxer直四水平对向引擎，最高提供180匹马力，以及178磅英尺扭矩，再加上标配 Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive系统，可在不同路况下提供更敏捷的操控体验，这些Forester Wilderness跟其他版本完全一样。搭配Lineartronic CVT自动变速器，入门版Base及Premium是Lineartronic CVT with low shift mode，而其他较高级则有手动介入的Lineartronic CVT with 8-speed manual mode。另外，Forester Wilderness的拖力有3,500磅，其他版本只有1,500磅，所以要拖拉东西的人，选择Forester Wilderness会较佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668179-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668179-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668179" class="wp-caption-text">2.5升Subaru Boxer直四水平对向引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新的踢感应式电动尾门首次在Subaru车型上亮相，方便车主在双手被占用时进行操作。所有车型均标配无钥匙进入、胎压监测、转向响应式LED头灯和自动远光灯等功能。在车载技术方面，手机无线充电板、SiriusXM 和 TomTom 导航。该系统还整合了 what3words 地点技术，能精确定位。安全和安防系统则包含SOS紧急援助、车辆定位、远程锁车和维修提醒等功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668180-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_41.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14668180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668180-2026_Subaru_Forester_Wilderness_41-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668180" class="wp-caption-text">Forester Wilderness内舱。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新2026 Subaru Forester提供多种车型选择，包括 Base、Premium、Sport、Limited 和 Touring，价格由29,995美元（Base）到41,595美元（Touring），而测试的Wilderness的价格为$38,385美元。◇</p>
