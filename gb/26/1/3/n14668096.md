<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自制5款保湿霜 助皮肤顺利过冬</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668103-shutterstock_1672540288-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冬季严寒，皮肤易食水，更需要精心的呵护。（Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年01月03日讯】（大纪元记者夏瑜编译报导）雪花飘飘，北风萧萧。冬季天寒地冻，带来皮肤干燥、脱皮、不适等问题。无论身处屋内还是室外，这个季节的朔风都会导致皮肤失去天然油脂，造成水分流失。市面上有很多保湿产品可以舒缓肌肤，但没有什么比自制保湿霜更好，它能提供不含化学物质的天然滋养。</p>
<p>这些保湿霜成分简单易得，不仅锁水润燥，而且非常适合敏感肌肤。wionews网站介绍以下五款，助你顺利度过冬天。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">芦荟凝胶+维生素E</span></p>
<p>这款凝胶状保湿霜能够深层补水，不会堵塞毛孔，令肌肤柔软饱满。它质地轻盈，非常适合混合性或油性肌肤。</p>
<p>使用方法很简单，只需将2汤匙芦荟凝胶与1粒维生素E胶囊充分混合即可。将混合物装入干净的罐子中保存，以备后用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668105" style="width: 801px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668105-a8124d7656643b21b77d236af8043b4b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14668105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668105-a8124d7656643b21b77d236af8043b4b.jpg" alt="" width="801" b="265" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668105" class="wp-caption-text">芦荟凝胶+维生素E，质地轻盈，深层补水。（Shutterstock/大纪元制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">椰子油+玫瑰水</span></p>
<p>椰子油是深层滋养肌肤的理想之选，与补水保湿的玫瑰水混合后，更添清爽，虽名为油，却不会油腻。</p>
<p>将2汤匙椰子油与1茶匙玫瑰水混合，即可制成适合干性肌肤的理想保湿霜。使用前请摇匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668106" style="width: 802px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668106-5d4fa426b933a62df07f02a2039c9bdb.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14668106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668106-5d4fa426b933a62df07f02a2039c9bdb.jpg" alt="" width="802" b="264" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668106" class="wp-caption-text">椰子油+玫瑰水，清爽不腻，有效养肤。（Shutterstock/大纪元制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">乳木果油+杏仁油</span></p>
<p>这款产品是极度干燥和起皮肌肤的福音。其浓稠的质地，能够锁住水分长达数小时，并有效滋养、温润肌肤。</p>
<p>在家自制方法：融化2汤匙乳木果油，加入1汤匙杏仁油，冷却后装入干净的罐子中保存，随时涂抹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668107" style="width: 801px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668107-197a094ad082c40a111f6cfb4e842904.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14668107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668107-197a094ad082c40a111f6cfb4e842904.jpg" alt="" width="801" b="265" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668107" class="wp-caption-text">乳木果油+杏仁油，质地浓稠，温润肌肤。（Shutterstock/大纪元制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">蜂蜜+甘油</span></p>
<p>蜂蜜具有保湿功效，还能除皱、美白；甘油则能锁住水分，保持皮肤丰满。因此，蜂蜜和甘油的组合非常适合干裂或粗糙的皮肤。只需几次使用即可见效。</p>
<p>将等量的蜂蜜和甘油混合，然后大量涂抹于干燥部位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668108" style="width: 800px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668108-b70c74bb95f76d849d80a68ecd3cb333.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14668108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668108-b70c74bb95f76d849d80a68ecd3cb333.jpg" alt="" width="800" b="264" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668108" class="wp-caption-text">蜂蜜+甘油，除皱美白，锁水养肤。（Shutterstock/大纪元制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">奶露+橄榄油</span></p>
<p>奶露（milk cream），印度语为Malai，是将全脂牛奶加热后冷却形成的凝脂奶油，广泛用于印度的烹饪，尤其是甜点制作中。因此，这是印度久经考验的冬季护肤秘方。</p>
<p>奶露和橄榄油混合后，能深层滋养肌肤。在家制作方法：取1汤匙新鲜奶露，与1茶匙橄榄油混合。充分按摩至全部被肌肤吸收，效果更佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14668111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14668111" style="width: 802px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668111-0c6dab7f67ced7933a2d68f4903e3515.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14668111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14668111-0c6dab7f67ced7933a2d68f4903e3515.jpg" alt="" width="802" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14668111" class="wp-caption-text">奶露+橄榄油，深层滋养肌肤。（Shutterstock/大纪元制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">额外提示</span></p>
<p>使用上述5款保湿霜前，务必进行皮肤测试。产品不含防腐剂，因此不耐久置，请只制作少量，存放在阴凉干燥处，并在4～6周内用完。</p>
<p>此外，请务必多喝水，保持身体水分充足。◇</p>
