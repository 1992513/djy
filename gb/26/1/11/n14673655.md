<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园中山国小岁末赴日交流 跨越国界迎来温暖的童心祝福</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673656-822845-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市中山国小每位学生亲自制作台湾地形拼图，作为送给日本同学的礼物。（桃园市中山国小提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月11日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市桃园区中山国民小学于2025年12月16日至20日，在蔡淑华校长和叶中强家长会长带领下，前往日本埼玉市小室小学及东京中华学校，展开为期五天的国际教育交流行程。孩子们在岁末时节走进日本校园，透过入班学习、文化体验与校际互动，包含书法、折纸、剑玉及户外体育课，亲身感受不同文化的教育现场，而东京中华学校采用世界咖啡馆换站上课活动，也为2025年画下充满学习与感动的句点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673672" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673672" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673672-822862.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673672" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673672-822862-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673672" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小日本交流结束回台湾后，收到来自日本东京中华学校同学的贺年卡，中山国小的同学们都惊喜又感动，台日友谊将绵延留长。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14673671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673671-822861.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673671-822861-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673671" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小日本交流结束回台湾后，收到来自日本东京中华学校同学的贺年卡，中山国小的同学们都惊喜又感动，台日友谊将绵延留长。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14673670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673670" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673670-822860.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673670-822860-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673670" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小日本交流结束回台湾后，收到来自日本东京中华学校同学的贺年卡，中山国小的同学们都惊喜又感动，台日友谊将绵延留长。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蔡淑华表示，感谢日本埼玉县小室小学宝田校长及东京中华学校吴育珊校长的欢迎与亲自接待，伊奈町的町长大岛渚也偕同议员亲临小室小学迎接中山师生并献上祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673657" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673657-822846.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14673657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673657-822846.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="759" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673657" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小蔡淑华校长和埼玉市小室小学宝田校长互赠纪念品。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蔡淑华进一步表示，这次国际交流圆满成功，特别感谢日本台湾Okami会会长纪秋美协助联系与到场陪伴，而副会长武川莉玲及会员洪美智，及小室小学家长会长清水宏利夫妇也全程陪伴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673659-822848.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673659-822848-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673659" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小学生和日本小室小学同学一起上书法课。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14673658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673658" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673658-822847.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14673658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673658-822847-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673658" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小学生和日本小室小学同学一起上书法课。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>东京中华学校吴育珊校长表示，她本身是台湾人，看到中日二校学生相处融洽、上课气氛热络，非常感动。</p>
<p>交流返台后不久，在2026年新年伊始，中山国小收到一份令人惊喜又感动的礼物——来自东京中华学校学生亲手绘制、亲笔书写的新年贺卡祝福。一张张色彩缤纷的卡片上，有富士山、鸟居、达摩、不倒翁与生肖图像，搭配中日文“谨贺新年”“新年快乐”“2026 令和八年”等祝福语，童趣十足、诚意满满，为校园增添温暖的新年气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673660" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673660-822849.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673660-822849-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673660" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小学生在小室小学操场上体育课。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些贺卡不只是节庆问候，更是孩子们在交流过程中建立情谊的延续。有的画下当时交流中最难忘的画面，有的以略显稚嫩却真诚的文字，祝福中山国小的老师与同学新年平安、学习进步。即使相隔千里，情感却透过画笔与文字紧紧相连，让中山国小师生在新年伊始深受感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673661-822850.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673661-822850-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673661" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小同学和小室小学同学一起吃午餐，感情融洽。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14673662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673662-822851.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673662-822851-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673662" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小同学和小室小学同学一起吃午餐，感情融洽。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>回顾交流当时，中山国小的孩子们也曾以满满心意回应这段跨国友谊。出发前，学生亲手制作台湾地形拼图，并绘制介绍台湾特色文化的明信片，致赠东京中华学校及埼玉市小室小学的同学。</p>
<p>这份结合课堂学习、文化认识与创意表达的礼物，让日方学生感到十分惊喜；而如今在新年收到对方回寄的贺年卡，成为最真实、也最动人的回馈，让中山的孩子感动万分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673666-822855.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673666-822855-600x568.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="568" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673666" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小同学赠送亲手制作的台湾地形拼图给东京中华学校同学，并收到他们回赠的联名卡片。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>温暖而真诚的学生相互回馈，也正呼应蔡淑华于交流期间，致赠埼玉市小室小学及东京中华学校“友谊长存”与“盟情永固”纪念杯的深层意义。纪念杯不仅是一份象征性的赠礼，更代表三校以教育为本、以孩子为中心所建立的长久伙伴关系；而学生从主动准备台湾文化礼物，到在新年收到跨海而来的祝福回信，正是“友谊长存、盟情永固”最具体、也最感人的实践。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673664-822853.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673664-822853-600x603.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="603" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673664" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小蔡淑华校长致赠感谢状给东京中华学校吴育珊校长。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蔡淑华校长，国际教育的价值，不只在于走出国门拓展视野，更在于让孩子在真实互动中学会尊重、表达与珍惜。当孩子愿意为朋友用心准备礼物，也能感受到被世界温柔回应，这份经验将成为他们一生难忘的学习记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673663" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673663-822852.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14673663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673663-822852-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673663" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小同学赠送小室小学同学台湾特色的明信片，小室小学同学送中山的孩子自己做的月历。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学生谢守训分享，东京都中华学校的同学“中、日文都很好，也非常热情”，让他在交流过程中感到自在与开心，并期待未来还能再次参与交流活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14673665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673665-822854.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673665-822854-600x511.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="511" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673665" class="wp-caption-text">东京中华学校吴育珊校长致赠感谢状给中山蔡淑华校长。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14673669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14673669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673669-822858.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14673669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14673669-822858-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14673669" class="wp-caption-text">中山国小学生和东京中华学校学生以世界咖啡馆的方式换站上课，气氛融洽。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>学生林郑文庭则表示，这次交流让他感到“很开心，也很特别”，因为中华学校的学生能使用中、日、英等多种语言沟通，当大家一同演唱〈泪光闪闪〉时，让他深受感动，也更加体会音乐与语言跨越文化的力量。</p>
<p>叶娜安同学说，收到日本同学寄来的贺年卡特别高兴，很惊喜，也想回寄卡片给她，跟他做好朋友。</p>
