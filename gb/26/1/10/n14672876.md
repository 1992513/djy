<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】老EV的转型 2026 Nissan Leaf Platinum+</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672916-2026_Nissan_Leaf_28-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 Nissan Leaf Platinum+。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月10日讯】在EV的大战当中，Nissan曾经是领先者，早在2010年已推出初代Leaf，初代Tesla Roadster也只是在2008年推出，可见Nissan在那时候洞悉先机。回到今天，2026年Nissan Leaf是车系的第三代，这次测试车是Pre-Production预产车。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://dme0bxbvomkno.cloudfront.net/I3dG0Pvzkkfi"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/1959xfFnWD8?si=lMiwHk1BWtiUU3Bs" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>2026 Nissan Leaf是一辆集时尚、先进设备，可以让每天开车都更有乐趣的纯电车。全新Leaf拥有动感的车身线条，与以往两代不同是Leaf不再是一辆五门揭背轿车，而是变化成跨界车型。为了使外型更消爽，新一代Leaf采用了电动伸缩式隐藏前门把，也是Nissan量产车首次采用，而后门把则隐藏于C柱。因此，风阻系数也由上一代的0.29Cd，大幅降低至仅0.26Cd。由于测试车是预产车，充电口的活门与车身的接口不够平顺，相信真正量产车会解决问题。此外，测试车采用Seabreeze Blue Pearl双色车身与黑色车顶，非常亮丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672882-2026_Nissan_Leaf_33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672882-2026_Nissan_Leaf_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672882" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Nissan Leaf Platinum+。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14672883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672883-2026_Nissan_Leaf_30.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672883-2026_Nissan_Leaf_30-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672883" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Nissan Leaf Platinum+。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前脸采用黑色栏栅，可选配发光Nissan厂徽、矩形堆叠灯组。车顶采用流线弧形设计，并以类似日本刀刃的饰条点缀。车顶玻璃采用固定式，虽不能开启，却可让阳光柔和地进入车厢，提升车内亮度。后轮拱大幅上扬，与倾斜的后挡风玻璃相接。尾门上采用45度角设计的扰流尾翼虽低调，却对整体空气动力表现至关重要。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672884-2026_Nissan_Leaf_37.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672884-2026_Nissan_Leaf_37-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672884" class="wp-caption-text">发光Nissan厂徽。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14672885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672885" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672885-2026_Nissan_Leaf_36.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672885-2026_Nissan_Leaf_36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672885" class="wp-caption-text">Leaf头灯组。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nissan Leaf有两个充电端口，在车侧左侧是J1772，支援Level 1及Level 2充电；而车身右侧是NACS（北美充电标准），可使用Tesla的充电站，但只会支援Level 3的DC快充，而一般的Tesla充电则不会工作，去Tesla的充电站“蹭饭”也要留意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672886-2026_Nissan_Leaf_39.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672886-2026_Nissan_Leaf_39-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672886" class="wp-caption-text">NACS充电端口。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14672888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672888" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672888-2026_Nissan_Leaf_42.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672888-2026_Nissan_Leaf_42-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672888" class="wp-caption-text">J1772充电端口。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>标配全新75 kWh液冷锂电池组，支援150 kW快充，可于35分钟内从10%充至80%，充满后的续航力最高可达303英哩。电池组底部加装树脂保护盖，隔绝冷空气、雪与泥浆；同时回收马达与车载充电器的废热，用来预热电池至理想温度范围，让冬季也能拥有更佳能效、更长续航与更稳定充电速度。Nissan Leaf装置了交流同步电机，最大马力输出为214匹，峰值扭矩为261磅呎，前轮驱动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672890-2026_Nissan_Leaf_48.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672890-2026_Nissan_Leaf_48-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672890" class="wp-caption-text">交流同步电机。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新Leaf内装空间开阔明亮，设计简单且实用，仪表台下方采开放式平整地板，变速按钮移到中控台，所以空间感更大。座椅也经重新设计，大幅提升第二排膝部空间。首创可调式全景天幕（选配），一键即可从全透光切换至几乎完全不透光，采用多层玻璃通电变色技术，不仅视觉震撼，还能阻隔热能与光线，比传统天窗更增加头部空间并提升空气动力。搭配双14.3英寸大萤幕，一个是作为仪表板，另一个是中控台萤幕，虽然是采用Google built-in车载系统（含 Google 地图、Google 助理、Play Store），但仍可支援无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto。Platinum+型号标配64色氛围灯，以及Bose Personal Plus 10个喇叭音响，包括前座头枕喇叭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672891-2026_Nissan_Leaf_65.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672891-2026_Nissan_Leaf_65-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672891" class="wp-caption-text">内装空间开阔明亮。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14672892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672892" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672892-2026_Nissan_Leaf_62.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672892-2026_Nissan_Leaf_62-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672892" class="wp-caption-text">车顶玻璃采用固定式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>后行李厢最大容积达55.5立方英尺，测试车使用了Divide-N-Hide隐藏式分层收纳系统，让行李厢地板下方有隐藏地方可收纳物品。前座标配2个USB Type-C充电口，而SV+与Platinum+装有无线充电板及后座2个Type-C充电口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672893-2026_Nissan_Leaf_61.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672893-2026_Nissan_Leaf_61-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672893" class="wp-caption-text">座椅经重新设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>标配 Nissan Safety Shield 360? 主动安全系统，包含行人侦测自动紧急刹车、后方车侧警示、车道偏离防治、盲点主动介入等。智慧环景影像，加上全新前广角视野、隐形引擎盖视角。全车系标配 ProPILOT Assist进阶驾驶辅助系统，结合智慧巡航与车道维持辅助。另搭载Nissan首创“二次碰撞缓解自动刹车”：当气囊模组侦测到撞击时，系统会自动刹车，降低二次碰撞风险。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672896-2026_Nissan_Leaf_52.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672896-2026_Nissan_Leaf_52-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672896" class="wp-caption-text">14.3英寸中控台萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14672897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672897" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672897-2026_Nissan_Leaf_53.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672897-2026_Nissan_Leaf_53-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672897" class="wp-caption-text">变速按钮设在中控台。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在中控台的e-Pedal，可让驾驶者只用一个加速踏板，便可加速及减速，当驾驶者踏着加速踏板时，汽车便会行驶，需当放开它时，汽车便会顺畅地迅速慢下来，以约0.2g的力度减速，最后会完全停下来，如要紧急停下，仍是需要使用刹车踏板。但是在e-Pedal启动后，汽车减速时，刹车踏板会自动踏下一点，要使用刹车踏板时不太习惯。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672898-2026_Nissan_Leaf_55.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672898-2026_Nissan_Leaf_55-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672898" class="wp-caption-text">四种驾驶模式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
