<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>曼谷国际发明展项奖项 桃园会稽国中接轨国际创新</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672907-822710-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市会稽国中心发明社1特别奖2金4银2铜。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月10日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）曼谷国际发明展是配合泰国“发明家节”举办的国际级盛会，象征科技创新与全球交流的重要平台。展览内容涵盖典礼、国际展览与研讨会，并设有国际展馆，展示来自世界各地八大类具市场潜力的创新作品，吸引发明家、研究人员与投资人齐聚一堂。桃园市会稽国中心发明社在本届展览中勇夺2金、4银、2铜，并荣获1项特别奖，成果丰硕，为校争光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672906" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672906-822709.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672906-822709-600x441.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672906" class="wp-caption-text">2026曼谷国际发明展台湾代表团。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市会稽国中校长李文义指出，2026曼谷国际发明展在曼谷国际贸易展览中心举行，现代化的场馆设施与完善的服务，为参展团队提供专业且友善的展示空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672908" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672908-822711.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672908-822711-600x225.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672908" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中李珮玄同学（左3）荣获二金一铜。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学生李珮玄同学表示，这次国际展览让她学会从不同文化与市场角度思考发明价值，也更加确立持续投入创新学习的方向。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672913" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672913-822715.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672913-822715-600x340.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672913" class="wp-caption-text">2026泰国国际发明展铜牌得奖者。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林信如同学分享，从实作到简报训练的过程，让他体会到创意不仅是想法，更需要清楚传达。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672909-822712.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14672909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672909-822712-600x681.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="511" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672909" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中李珮玄同学（右1）荣获简报赛佳作奖。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林品钧同学则认为，与各国选手交流的经验，让他更加勇于尝试与挑战自我。廖家进同学分享，从反复修正作品到接受国际评审提问的过程，让他学会面对挑战并提升自信。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672910-822713.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672910-822713-600x276.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="276" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672910" class="wp-caption-text">2026泰国国际发明展金牌得奖者。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>指导老师官贤相表示，在师长持续的引导与陪伴下，学生逐步学会为自己设定人生目标，妥善规划读书、运动与良心行动，并循序完成创意发想与实作历程，同时接受国际评审的专业检视，进而培养面对问题时的解决能力与坚定自信。期盼让孩子在心中装上持续前进的马达，在脑中植入良心的指南针，走出稳健踏实、行稳致远的全人教育之路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14672912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14672912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672912-822714.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14672912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14672912-822714-600x267.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14672912" class="wp-caption-text">2026泰国国际发明展银牌得奖者。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李文义指出，会稽国中以国际发明展作为实践创新教育的重要场域，让学生的学习不仅止于课堂，而是真正走向世界、与国际接轨。◇</p>
