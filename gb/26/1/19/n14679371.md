<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>飞到金门 欣赏闽南建筑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679385-824778-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>建筑不语，却完整保存了一段地方记忆。（杨建业提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月19日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬金门报导）1月18日清晨，风带着海味，从机场外吹过来。澎湖摄影家杨建业把相机背在肩上，从澎湖转机到金门，目的很单纯，来拍他心中一直惦记的那一种建筑风景。不是名胜清单上的必拍角度，而是红砖、石墙、屋脊线条，还有午后光影慢慢移动的样子。这趟行程，他几乎每天都在找“光落下来的地方”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14679377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679377-824770.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679377-824770-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679377" class="wp-caption-text">透过老屋墙面与石框取景，红砖屋脊在蓝天下显得简洁有力，展现闽南建筑的线条之美。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14679379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679379-824772.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679379-824772-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679379" class="wp-caption-text">午后阳光洒落在老宅天井，红砖、灰墙与树影交织，静静保存着岁月的温度。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>红砖、石墙与时间留下的痕迹</h2>
<p>金门的闽南建筑，第一眼就很实在。红砖砌墙、花岗岩门框，墙面没有刻意修补，斑驳反而成了风景。杨建业特别喜欢站在老屋前，看阳光从屋脊滑下来，打在墙面上，留下树影与窗櫺的线条。有些墙面已经泛黄，砖缝里藏着年代感。他说，这不是“老”，而是“用过”。屋子曾经有人住过、守过，也经历过战地时期的日子，现在静静站着，让光把故事慢慢说完。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14679380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679380" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679380-824773.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679380-824773-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679380" class="wp-caption-text">屋脊山墙上的泥塑装饰，在蓝天映衬下更显立体，细节中可见早年工匠的手艺。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14679372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679372-824765.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679372-824765-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679372" class="wp-caption-text">金门聚落沿着水岸展开，红砖古厝与宗祠错落其间，屋脊线条顺着地势起伏，呈现典型金门闽南建筑与自然共存的样貌。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>祠堂前的安静午后</h2>
<p>走进聚落里的祠堂，红瓦屋顶低低地延伸，屋脊上的剪黏不张扬，却很耐看。杨建业常常等在这里，不急着按快门。他想拍的，是祠堂前那种没有声音的时刻，没有人潮，只有风、光，还有石阶的阴影。金门的宗祠建筑，多半坐北朝南，讲究方位，也重视家族秩序。墙上留下的痕迹，不只是岁月，也是一代一代人共同生活的证明。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14679373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679373-824766.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679373-824766-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679373" class="wp-caption-text">聚落中的宗祠建筑，红瓦屋顶与花岗岩基座相互映衬，正中央的门神彩绘在阳光下显得沉稳而庄重。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14679391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679391-824784.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679391-824784-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679391" class="wp-caption-text">老宅正厅门面，木作门扇、石阶与地砖保存完整，中轴对称的格局仍可见家族生活的秩序。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14679381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679381" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679381-824774.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679381" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679381-824774-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679381" class="wp-caption-text">传统闽式合院正立面，红砖、燕尾脊与灯笼排列整齐，呈现家族聚落的生活尺度。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>拱门、巷道与转弯的风景</h2>
<p>穿过老聚落的拱门，外头是另一个世界。门内是石板地与老墙，门外则是柏油路与现代生活。杨建业很喜欢站在拱门里拍外面的景色，像是在用建筑替风景加了一个框。他说，这样的画面很金门。不是刻意保存，而是自然并存。走在巷道里，转个弯，可能就遇见一面被树影覆盖的墙，或是一扇只剩半边的窗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14679374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679374-824767.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679374-824767-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679374" class="wp-caption-text">穿过石砌拱门，视线从老聚落延伸至现代道路，门内门外形成时间与生活节奏的对比。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14679378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679378-824771.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679378-824771-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679378" class="wp-caption-text">午后阳光洒落在老屋墙面，斑驳砖墙与树影交织，留下时间走过的痕迹。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14679382" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679382" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679382-824775.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679382-824775-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679382" class="wp-caption-text">山墙彩绘与题字并存，线条简洁却不失庄重，是老屋最醒目的门面之一。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>战地记忆与民居并肩而立</h2>
<p>在金门，战地设施与民居没有距离感。碉堡、堡垒，常常就在老房子旁边。杨建业拍下这样的画面时，没有刻意强调历史，只是让建筑自己说话。他觉得，金门的特殊之处就在这里——生活曾被战争打断，又慢慢接回来。民居继续存在，成了最日常的风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14679386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14679386" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679386-824779.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14679386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14679386-824779-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14679386" class="wp-caption-text">在金门，战地设施与民居没有距离感。碉堡、堡垒，常常就在老房子旁边。（杨建业提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>不是拍完 而是看懂</h2>
<p>这趟拍摄，杨建业拍得不快。很多时候，他只是站着看，看光怎么移动，看墙面怎么变化。他说，金门的建筑不适合匆忙，越慢，画面越清楚。离开前，他回头看了一眼老屋与祠堂。相机里存的是影像，心里留下的，则是对金门闽南建筑更深的理解，那是一种不喧哗、不急着被看见，却始终存在的风景。</p>
