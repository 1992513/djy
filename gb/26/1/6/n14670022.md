<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>萨默维尔市长威尔逊就职 首要目标为增加住房</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670018-DSC06378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>1月3日晚上，麻州萨默维尔市（Somerville）举办市政官员就职典礼。市长Jake Wilson（中）及市议员宣誓就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年01月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨萨默维尔市报导）麻州萨默维尔市（Somerville）1月3日举办市长及市议员就职典礼。新市长威尔逊（Jake Wilson）强调其首要任务是增加平价住房，也提出改善道路、提升学校水平、支持移民社区等多项目标。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14670010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670010-DSC06332.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670010-DSC06332-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670010" class="wp-caption-text">萨默维尔市举办市政官员就职典礼。数百人在萨默维尔市高中礼堂观礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14670031" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670031" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670031-Somerville-Mayor-Inauguration.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670031-Somerville-Mayor-Inauguration-600x442.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670031" class="wp-caption-text">就职典礼提供中文节目册。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>根据人口普查数据，萨默维尔市有8万2千多名居民，是麻州人口最密集的市镇之一。市内有约11%的居民是亚裔。</p>
<p>威尔逊从2022年1月开始担任萨默维尔市不分区市议员。他在2025年9月的市政选举初选中获得了最多选票，随后又于11月决选中击败对手，当选为市长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14670011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670011-DSC06336.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670011-DSC06336-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670011" class="wp-caption-text">就职典礼开始，国旗卫队进场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年1月3日晚上在萨默维尔市高中礼堂，威尔逊和新届市议员及学校委员共同宣誓就职。麻州州长希莉（Maura Healey）、MBTA总经理兼代理交通部长伍华伟（Phillip Eng），以及多位麻州议员都到场祝贺。现场观礼者众多，几乎坐满了礼堂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14670015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670015-DSC06368.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670015-DSC06368-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670015" class="wp-caption-text">市长Jake Wilson及市议员宣誓就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14670017" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670017" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670017-DSC06371.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670017-DSC06371-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670017" class="wp-caption-text">市长Jake Wilson、市议员和学校委员宣誓就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>建设“人们住得起的城市”</strong></h2>
<p>在就职演讲中，威尔逊强调“必须让萨默维尔市成为人们住得起的城市”。他说，他将立刻与住房建设伙伴开展合作，在公共交通路线和经济成长区域建设住房。他还希望提供多样化的房屋种类，扩宽非营利机构参与建设的渠道，以及加强对租客的保护。</p>
<p>“22年前，我夫人和我在萨默维尔市这里安家。我希望为每个人创造同样的机会。所以住房是我们政府至关重要的任务。”威尔逊说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14670020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670020" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670020-DSC06401.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670020" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670020-DSC06401-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670020" class="wp-caption-text">市长Jake Wilson发表就职演说，强调增加住房。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他举例说，在州政府的支持下，萨默维尔市去年12月拆除了Winter Hill地区空置18年的前Star Market建筑，未来该处将建设两栋混合用途大楼，总共提供319个单元的住房，其中40%是平价住房。</p>
<p>同样在Winter Hill社区，威尔逊承诺尽快建好一座新学校。对于公立学校师生，他也保证为教师提供优越的薪资，让所有学生获得优质教育。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14670014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670014-DSC06366.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670014-DSC06366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670014" class="wp-caption-text">就职典礼期间，当地中学生朗诵诗歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>威尔逊将执政愿景形容为“提供高质量的核心服务，让公共空间清洁，道路铺平，路灯点亮，图书馆开放，建筑得到修缮”。他还表示将保护移民社区，让他们免受被驱逐的恐惧。</p>
<p>威尔逊承诺建立开放且受监督的政府，频繁地举办开放日，与居民沟通。“我们将努力工作，清晰沟通，取得真正的进步。从当政第一天，也就是昨天开始，市长办公室的大门将一直打开，不会上锁。”威尔逊说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14670019" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670019" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670019-DSC06384.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670019" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670019-DSC06384-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670019" class="wp-caption-text">萨默维尔市市议会议长Lance Davis发表演说。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14670021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670021-DSC06410.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670021-DSC06410-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670021" class="wp-caption-text">就职典礼最后，乐队进场欢庆。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>麻州和萨默维尔市紧密合作</strong></h2>
<p>MBTA总经理兼代理交通部长伍华伟谈到萨默维尔市近几年新建或修缮的公共交通设施，包括绿线地铁延长线、East Somerville地铁站新行人通道等等。他也谈到，萨默维尔市是首批与MBTA合作提供低收入者优惠票的市镇。未来，麻州交通部门将继续与萨默维尔市政府合作，为居民提供安全可靠的公共交通。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14670012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670012-DSC06345.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670012-DSC06345-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670012" class="wp-caption-text">伍华伟（Phillip Eng）回顾当地近几年的公共交通项目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14670013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14670013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670013-DSC06360.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14670013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14670013-DSC06360-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14670013" class="wp-caption-text">州长希莉（Maura Healey）期待与萨默维尔市维持紧密合作。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>值美国建国250周年之际，麻州州长希莉说，美国的民主制度从麻州的市镇层级公共服务开始，由许多敢于走出来维护社区的普通人建立。她称赞，威尔逊市长拥有资深的社区服务和公共事务经验，期待双方继续合作，为当地居民谋福祉。◇</p>
