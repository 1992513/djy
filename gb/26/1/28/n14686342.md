<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：英超球队强势 六队参赛五队直接晋级</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14686373-GettyImages-2258121327.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
