<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园表扬好人好事运动协会公益团体跨世代志工关怀弱势</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683710-826119-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市表扬好人好事运动协会理事长林綋诘，号召桃园市爱心义剪协会，等一起携手做公益，下一次预计在暑假的时候再寿山高中办理龟山区的爱心物资发放。（桃园表扬好人好事运动协会提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年01月25日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）为让弱势家庭与独居长者在寒冬中感受到社会的温暖与实质关怀，桃园市表扬好人好事运动协会25日，在南崁高中活动中心举办“寒冬送暖?爱在芦竹”爱心物资发放活动，结合芦竹区多个公益团体与在地力量，携手为社区注入温暖能量。</p>
<p>桃园市芦竹区教育文化推展协会、桃园市家长会长协会芦竹分会、桃园市高中小学家长会长协会芦竹分会、桃园市福兴福德会、桃庚联合医疗诊所、桃园市爱心义剪协会等单位共同协办，展现社区公私协力、携手行善的公益精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14683719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683719-826135.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683719-826135-600x213.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="213" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683719" class="wp-caption-text">.桃园市表扬好人好事运动协会活动前志工大合照。（桃园市爱心义剪协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14683711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683711" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683711-826121.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683711-826121-600x358.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683711" class="wp-caption-text">.芦竹区李岳坛区长感谢民间社团办理此项活动，跟政府部门ㄧ起做社会安全防护网，帮忙接助这些弱势家庭。（桃园表扬好人好事运动协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>爱心物资全数来自公益团体、爱心人士及社会大众集资捐赠，透过民众自发性参与，汇聚点滴善意，实践“1份不嫌少、50份不嫌多”的爱心精神，让更多弱势家庭在寒冬中获得实质支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14683712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683712" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683712-826122.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683712-826122-600x307.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683712" class="wp-caption-text">许清顺议员肯定民间社团携手做公益，他也愿意与大家携手做公益，让弱势家庭能够得到更多的帮助。（桃园表扬好人好事运动协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除物资发放外，活动现场亦结合多元公益服务，由桃园市爱心义剪协会提供免费剪发服务；并由桃庚联合医疗诊所提供免费血糖与血压检测，提醒民众关注自身健康，将关怀从生活层面延伸至健康照护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14683713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683713-826123.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683713-826123-600x438.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683713" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市福兴福德会理事长林宏哲请福德正神土地公一起来做公益。（桃园表扬好人好事运动协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动动员超过50位志工参与现场服务，志工年龄层横跨国中、高中学生、大学生、硕士班学生，更有高龄90岁的长者志工亲自到场协助，展现跨世代携手行善的感人画面。志工们以实际行动服务弱势，充分体现“老中青携手、爱心不分年龄”的公益精神，令人深受感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14683714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683714-826126.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683714-826126-600x504.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="504" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683714" class="wp-caption-text">.桃庚联合诊所总院长 林铭政表示控制血糖跟血压，是避免许多严重疾病的重要方向。（桃园市爱心义剪协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14683716" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683716" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683716-826128.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683716" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683716-826128-600x312.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683716" class="wp-caption-text">桃庚诊所免费帮民众量测血压血糖，提醒民众注意自己的健康。（桃园市爱心义剪协会 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>物资发放对象包含芦竹区低收入户、中低收入户及独居长者，受惠户数共计1,071户。主办单位期盼，透过物资支援与贴心服务并行，让爱不只是短暂的帮助，而是陪伴民众度过寒冬的重要力量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14683715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683715" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683715-826127.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683715-826127-600x511.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="511" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683715" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市高中小学家长会长协会芦竹分会总会长林月娥一同来参与物资发放。（桃园市爱心义剪协会 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14683717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683717" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683717-826133.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683717" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683717-826133-600x219.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="219" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683717" class="wp-caption-text">爱心物资发放现场。（桃园市爱心义剪协会 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市表扬好人好事运动协会理事长林綋诘表示：“寒冬送暖不仅是物资的传递，更是世代之间爱与关怀的串联。感谢所有参与集资捐赠的爱心人士，以及每一位默默付出的志工朋友，让‘爱在芦竹’化为实际行动，温暖每一位需要帮助的民众。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14683718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14683718" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683718-826134.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14683718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/01/id14683718-826134-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14683718" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市爱心义剪协会协助现场民众免费剪头发。（桃园市爱心义剪协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林綋诘号召桃园市爱心义剪协会，桃庚联合诊所，桃园市福兴福德会等一起携手做公益，这样的公益活动将持续在桃园市各个行政区域，下一次预计在暑假的时候再寿山高中办理龟山区的爱心物资发放。◇</p>
