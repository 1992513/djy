<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>声讨共产主义罪恶 纽约上州韩战纪念碑揭幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801282-192571-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>主办方和各界人士合影（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年07月02日讯】为纪念美国独立250周年暨韩战76周年，纽约、新泽西等地多个韩裔社团领袖，周四（6月25日），在纽约上州举行韩战纪念碑揭幕献礼仪式，感恩美国当年协助韩国，抵抗苏共、中共军队和朝鲜武装力量的入侵，捍卫世界和平。</p>
<figure id="14801288" aria-describedby="caption-14801288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801288-192577-450x261.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801288-192577-450x261.jpg" alt="全体嘉宾合影。" width="600" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14801288" class="wp-caption-text">全体嘉宾合影。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14801285" aria-describedby="caption-14801285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801285-192574-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801285-192574-450x338.jpg" alt="徐孝锡院长和海兵队合影。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14801285" class="wp-caption-text">徐孝锡院长和海兵队合影。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14801284" aria-describedby="caption-14801284" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801284-192573-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801284-192573-450x338.jpg" alt="嘉宾合影。" width="599" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14801284" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>韩裔韩战将军，韩战、越战老兵及子女，美国退伍军人协会，纽约橙县官员和民众，来到中城市退伍军人纪念公园，为韩战纪念碑剪彩。</p>
<p>纪念碑由韩战幸存者设计，主碑刻有介绍韩战和向参战老兵致敬的文字，同时记载着麦克阿瑟、范弗里特和沃克将军的韩战历史功勋。</p>
<p>记者：“现场人士表示，纪念碑承载着1950到1953年，那段韩战的历史。承载着美韩两国的友谊，更将永远提醒社区，自由和和平的来之不易。”</p>
<p>与会人士表示，将继续通过多种方式缅怀先烈，让子孙后代永远铭记韩战，呼吁解体中共，让韩朝统一，清算共产主义对人类犯下的罪恶。</p>
<p>扁康丸创始人徐孝锡院长：“今天这个场合也是为了创立韩战英雄论坛，即麦克阿瑟、范弗里特和沃克将军，我是这个论坛的韩国代表。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14801289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14801289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801289-192578.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14801289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801289-192578-600x326.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14801289" class="wp-caption-text">徐孝锡院长致词。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14801283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14801283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801283-192572.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14801283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801283-192572-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14801283" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14801287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14801287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801287-192576.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14801287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801287-192576-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14801287" class="wp-caption-text">徐院长太太指着纪念碑里刻着的徐孝锡院长的名字。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14801286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14801286" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801286-192575.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14801286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/07/id14801286-192575-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14801286" class="wp-caption-text">徐孝锡代表院长和李浩齐合影。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与会人士：“在中国（中共）抓（法轮功）团体的人，摘取他们的器官，这太不人道了。这件事必须公诸于世，必须受到世人的谴责，必须被社会彻底排斥，必须停止活摘。明白我的意思吗？这太可怕了。为了摘取器官而杀人，残忍夺走年轻人的生命。”</p>
<p>纽约橙县治安官Paul：“我一生都在目睹共产主义的破坏，不仅对我们国家，对其他国家也一样。我觉得有必要推翻那里的政权，建立新的政权。”</p>
<p>1953年，交战国各方签署《韩战停战协议》后，韩朝之间从未签订正式的和平条约，实质上两国一直处于停战状态。而中共和俄罗斯当局持续支持朝鲜金正恩政权，使朝鲜半岛和平成为国际关注的一个焦点。</p>
