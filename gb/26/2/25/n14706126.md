<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>围棋：棋仙战决赛揭幕 首局朴廷桓先拔头筹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706137-532349work-20260225-016-vcv.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
