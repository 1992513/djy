<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>哈佛MIT台湾学生会庆新年 联合办春酒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705380-DSC07834-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2月22日，哈佛大学和麻省理工学院（MIT）的台湾同学会联合举办春酒，近150人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年02月25日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）时值大年初六，哈佛大学和麻省理工学院（MIT）的台湾同学会2月22日联合举办春酒，与同学们及侨界嘉宾享美食、玩游戏、听歌曲、抽奖品，热闹庆祝新年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705386" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705386-DSC07845.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705386-DSC07845-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705386" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛大学和MIT台湾同学会联合举办春酒，近150人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705377-DSC07796.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705377-DSC07796-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705377" class="wp-caption-text">主办方准备诸多美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚在哈佛大学的Lehman Hall活动厅，台湾学生、学者及嘉宾总计近150人欢聚一堂。两大同学会准备了炒米粉、四季豆炒腊肉、干炒牛河、珍珠奶茶等美食，以及“乖乖”、雪饼、小熊饼干等零食，待大家吃饱喝足后，开始玩游戏和表演节目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705390" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705390-DSC07862.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705390-DSC07862-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705390" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛的陈曦（右）和MIT的陈孟葳主持晚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705382" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705382" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705382-DSC07838.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705382-DSC07838-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705382" class="wp-caption-text">同学们玩抢答游戏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场庆典活动，由哈佛的陈曦和MIT的陈孟葳主持。开场的第一个活动，是挑战脑力和反应力的数学游戏。主持人抽出扑克牌数字，各桌参与者运算得出特定结果，然后举手抢答。另一个游戏，是竞猜屏幕上的照片由哪两个人物合成。这里既有马斯克、赖清德等特征出众的名人，也有两个同学会的干部。待谜底揭晓时，大家纷纷为答对者欢呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705383" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705383-DSC07842.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705383-DSC07842-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705383" class="wp-caption-text">高名震（左）、蔡灿扬演奏音乐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705384" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705384" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705384-DSC07844.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705384-DSC07844-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705384" class="wp-caption-text">刘昕甯演奏音乐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>春酒的音乐表演，也收获热烈掌声。第一个节目是高名震、蔡灿扬、刘昕甯带来的三重奏演出。另一个节目是伯克利音乐学院的郑新治献上的歌曲演唱。他回顾过去的一年，感慨道，担任伯克利台湾同学会会长，遇到了很多新的人、新的挑战，也怀念放不下的人、事、物。带着充沛的情感唱歌，他赢得热烈的“Encore”呼声，为回应大家的热情而多唱一曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705389-DSC07851.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705389-DSC07851-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705389" class="wp-caption-text">郑新治演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705378-DSC07802.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705378-DSC07802-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705378" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会会长赖怡音（左）和台湾同学会联合会会长李典璋参加晚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场春酒，也得到驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处及当地多个侨团的支持。经文处处长廖朝宏、教育组组长黄薳玉、科技组组长蔡孟勋、新英格兰台湾同学会联合会会长李典璋，以及新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会、玉山科技协会、台湾影展协会、剑桥合唱团、波士顿台湾人生物科技协会等多个团体的代表也都参加了春酒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705379-DSC07808.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705379-DSC07808-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705379" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏感谢两个同学会举办精彩的晚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705388" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705388-DSC07849.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705388" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705388-DSC07849-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705388" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705391-DSC07871.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705391-DSC07871-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705391" class="wp-caption-text">教育组组长黄薳玉（右）向同学们赠送红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖朝宏特别感谢两个学生会的干部们辛苦筹备，举办这场有年味的精彩活动，让海外的同学们欢聚一堂。他也鼓励同学们，在完成课业之余，多多接触各界人士、同学好友；同时也不忘台湾的美好。</p>
<p>李典璋除了送上祝福之外，还欢迎大家参与学联的活动，包括联合春酒、金曲之夜、医疗讲座、《看不见的国家》电影放映会等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705392-DSC07891.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705392-DSC07891-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705392" class="wp-caption-text">两个同学会的干部们大秀舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705393" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705393-DSC07908.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705393-DSC07908-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705393" class="wp-caption-text">幸运儿抽中大奖Apple Watch。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705394-DSC07909.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705394-DSC07909-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705394" class="wp-caption-text">同学会向春酒参与者们赠送幸运红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>席间还有充满欢乐的抽奖环节。参与春酒的嘉宾和友社皆带来了礼品，例如经文处送出3份红包、台湾影展协会送出电影票等等。最大奖项则是主办方提供的Apple Watch智能手表。</p>
<p>到了压轴节目，哈佛和MIT台湾同学会的干部们上台PK，大秀舞姿。随着礼炮爆响，礼花散落，同学们也热情欢呼，非常热闹。◇</p>
