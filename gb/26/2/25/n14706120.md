<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>郑宜农海外巡回来到亚特兰大</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706122-IMG_5668-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年02月26日讯】Hello Atlanta? ！我们正式宣布 True Talent 的首场演出计划！</p>
<p>我们非常荣幸能邀请到曾获金曲奖双料肯定的实力派才女 —— 郑宜农 (Enno Cheng) 来到亚特兰大！Enno 不只是歌手，更是演员与作家。2023 年，她凭借专辑《水逆》一举夺下第 34 届金曲奖“最佳台语专辑”与“最佳台语女歌手”两项大奖，展现了极致的创作实力。</p>
<p>Enno 的音乐总是能温柔地接住每颗疲惫的心，从迷幻民谣到大胆的电子实验都能完美驾驭。这次她将来到亚特兰大，在 Vinyl 这个能近距离互动的空间，与大家分享她对当代与传统交织的音乐诠释。</p>
<p>//演出资讯//</p>
<p>2026 郑宜农《圆缺 2.0》海外巡回</p>
<p>我们只能一直走、一直走、一直走</p>
<p>日期｜2026 年 5 月 13 日</p>
<p>时间｜19:00入场、20:00 开演</p>
<p>地点｜Vinyl @ Center Stage</p>
<p>售票｜购票连结 ?<ahref="https://www.ticketmaster.com/event/0E006444A7D36198">https://www.ticketmaster.com/event/0E006444A7D36198</a></p>
<p>Hello Atlanta</p>
<p>True Talent is officially kicking off our very first concert project!</p>
<p>We are incredibly honored to announce that the multi-talented Enno Cheng is coming to ATL! Enno is a versatile singer-songwriter, actress, and writer. In 2023, she made a historic sweep at the 34th Golden Melody Awards, winning both &#8220;&#8221;Best Taiwanese Female Singer&#8221;&#8221; and &#8220;&#8221;Best Taiwanese Album&#8221;&#8221;.</p>
<p>Enno’s music is a captivating journey—blending heartfelt storytelling with bold electronic experimentation. Join us at Vinyl for an intimate experience with one of Taiwan&#8217;s most original and soulful voices.</p>
<p>&#8220;We move forward, again and again.&#8221;</p>
<p>Don&#8217;t miss out on this milestone event!</p>
