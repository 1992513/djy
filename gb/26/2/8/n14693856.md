<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯房价1月飙涨近2万</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月08日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">最新报告表明：珀斯房地产市场延续了猛烈的上涨势头，独立房中位价在</span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-TW">月份飙涨近</span><span lang="en-AU">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，</span><span lang="zh-TW">房地产数据机构</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Cotality</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">公布</span><span lang="zh-TW">的最新数据显示，珀斯住宅价值在</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月上涨</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，中位价升至</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">961,898</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，单月增加</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">19,238</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，相当于每周上涨超过</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">4,800</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。这一涨幅是全职成年雇员平均周薪</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2,155</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元的两倍多。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">然而，房地产集团</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Domain</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">近日发布的去年第四季度独立房价格报告显示，珀斯独立房中位价在该季度上涨近</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">10</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，已经升至</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">108</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">7762</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Cotality</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">经济学家劳利斯（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Tim</span> <span lang="en-AU">Lawless</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，尽管市场仍在以不可持续的速度增长，但</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月的涨幅相比去年年底已有所放缓。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">他说，去年</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">10</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月市场上涨</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2.7</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">11</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月则跃升</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2.9</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>他表示：“我们可能已经度过了与通胀几乎同步的那一轮增长高峰。”</p>
<p>“但在市场回落到可持续状态之前，仍然还有很长一段路要走。”</p>
<p>劳利斯指出，市场最活跃的仍然是低价区间，因为相对更具可负担性。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">不过他也表示，整个市场都在承受选择不足的压力，</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月珀斯的房源挂牌量较去年同期下降了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">39</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>由于住房可负担性持续恶化，房地产市场中价格较低的房源正面临更大的需求压力。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Cotality</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">数据显示，珀斯单元房在</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月份上涨</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2.3</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，略高于独立房</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1.9</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的涨幅。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">他说：“房价上涨速度如此之快，市场中弥漫着明显的紧迫感，而且几乎没有可选择的房源。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">数据显示，珀斯</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月房价涨幅高于全国所有首府城市</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">0.7</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的平均水平。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在过去三个月中，珀斯的表现同样更为强劲，房价累计上涨</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">7</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，而全国首府城市平均仅为</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2.1</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>劳利斯表示，进入<span lang="en-AU">2026</span>年，珀斯与全国其它地区一样，将面临多重利好与压力并存的局面。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">他说，供应端受限以及具有韧性的劳动力市场将继续支撑房价，而联邦政府推行的首次置业者</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">5</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">首付担保计划也将为需求提供支撑。</span></p>
<p>他还表示，极度紧张的租赁市场同样会推动更多人转向购房。</p>
<p>劳利斯指出市场面临的不利因素：预计利率上升将削弱购买力和信心；可负担性问题依然严峻，在生活成本危机背景下，消费者情绪也较为脆弱。</p>
<p>不过他也称，除非经济状况恶化程度超出预期，或信贷监管明显收紧，否则澳大利亚任何地区都不太可能出现实质性的市场下行。</p>
