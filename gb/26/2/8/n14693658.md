<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>曾玲兰“花语鸟鸣?纸雕生命之美”  温润苗医友善艺廊的心灵飨宴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693659-829392-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>苗医秘书黄堂彦(左)颁发感谢状，谢谢纸雕艺术家曾玲兰老师(右)策展，带给乡亲视觉享受和心灵温暖.（许享富／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月08日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）纸雕艺术家曾玲兰以“花语鸟鸣”，展示纸雕生命之美，在部苗栗医院营造出唯美的空间，不同于常见的水彩或油画，纸雕利用简单的工具、技法与纸张堆叠，结合室内灯光产生的光影效果，创造出强烈的立体感,，让作品呈现出仿佛“飞出来”或“站出来”的感觉，让观赏者惊叹：原来一张纸，也能有如此多样的变化，让人感觉心情愉悦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693660" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693660-829393.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693660-829393.jpeg" alt="" width="455" b="683" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693660" class="wp-caption-text">吉祥如意（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14693661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693661" style="width: 467px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693661-829394.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693661-829394.jpeg" alt="" width="467" b="700" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693661" class="wp-caption-text">祥鹤贺松（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14693663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693663" style="width: 470px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693663-829395.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693663-829395.jpeg" alt="" width="470" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693663" class="wp-caption-text">无事为乐　（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纸雕是一门“减法”与“空间”的艺术，利用纸、刀具（如剪刀、压线笔 、切割垫等）以工法与呈现方式的不同，展现平面剪纸、立体纸雕、影绘与光影的纸雕，创作极具立体感与生命力的作品。</p>
<p>纸雕艺术的迷人，展现作者的艺术涵养，工艺精湛的升华，“极致匠心”的层次，“精湛”不仅是技巧的堆叠，更是艺术家耐心的总和。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693664" style="width: 525px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693664-829396.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693664-829396.jpeg" alt="" width="525" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693664" class="wp-caption-text">留白的书法亦展现流畅舒展、潇洒飘逸 、奔放连绵、气韵生动（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14693665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693665" style="width: 478px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693665-829397.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693665-829397.jpeg" alt="" width="478" b="717" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693665" class="wp-caption-text">励志禅机（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14693666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693666" style="width: 459px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693666-829398.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693666-829398.jpeg" alt="" width="459" b="689" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693666" class="wp-caption-text">樱枝清吟（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纸雕艺术家通常在以下三个维度展现其深厚的功力<br />
1. 毫厘之间的控刀：如镂空工艺、零失误挑战。<br />
2. 空间结构的建构：如单张纸极限、层次透视。<br />
3. 生物态势的模拟：如羽毛与毛发、拟真动态。<br />
同时作品也是光影艺术的呈现，犹如一幅立体画。</p>
<p>曾玲兰委婉倾诉分享她的创作过程中的心路历程：观察美好事物，只要会拿剪刀、愿意张开眼睛观察，就能发现世界的美好，溶入作品中；投入剪纸的乐趣中需要极大的耐心，这能让创作者暂时抽离现实，忘掉许多烦恼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693667" style="width: 478px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693667-829399.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693667-829399.jpeg" alt="" width="478" b="717" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693667" class="wp-caption-text">蓝羽飞翔（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14693668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693668" style="width: 461px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693668-829400.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693668-829400.jpeg" alt="" width="461" b="536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693668" class="wp-caption-text">静观（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14693669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693669" style="width: 409px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693669-829401.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693669-829401.jpeg" alt="" width="409" b="613" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693669" class="wp-caption-text">荷　（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她更分享，将平面的纸张转化为具有立体感的艺术品纸雕艺术，透过物理上的堆叠与环境光影的交互作用。如1.层层堆叠与垫高技法<br />
2.光线与影子的交互作<br />
3. 简单工具与色彩辅助。</p>
<p>总结来说，纸雕的立体感是藉由纸张空间的位移（垫高堆叠），诱发光线产生阴影，从而让观赏者在视觉上感受到超越平面的生命力。</p>
<p>她建议心情不好的人（无论是医护人员或民众）在作品前驻足，直接盯著作品慢慢欣赏，将负面情绪放下，透过短暂的“与美对话”的艺术洗礼，让医护人员能带着美感的心情回到工作岗位上。她深信人若多接触“善”与“美”的事物，思想与行为都会变得更好，这便是她想传递的正能量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693670" style="width: 503px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693670-829402.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14693670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693670-829402.jpeg" alt="" width="503" b="664" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693670" class="wp-caption-text">晨曦报春　（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>部苗医院院长徐国芳表示，友善艺廊的设置，为推动人本医疗、艺术疗愈的友善职场重要实践，以纸雕艺术透过反复剪裁与细腻观察，能培养耐心、沉淀心灵，进而在生活中感受细微而真实的美好。细腻而富生命力的纸雕作品，为医疗空间注入静谧而深刻的艺术气息。感谢曽玲兰老师协助苗医的心灵上的疗愈。</p>
<p>曾玲兰老师希望透过这些精致的纸雕作品，让人在繁忙且高压的医院环境中，能有一段“与美对话”的时间，借此重拾平静与吉祥如意的祝福。</p>
