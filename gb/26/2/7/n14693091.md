<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】夜色霸气现身 2025 Toyota Grand Highlander Nightshade Hybrid</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693112-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_28-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Toyota Grand Highlander Nightshade Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月07日讯】Toyota的Highlander自推出以来，已经销售了超过330万辆，为了进一步扩展市场，Toyota推出更巨大的Grand Highlander。Grand Highlander有更大的车体，更有不同的动力系统，车内也是跟Highlander有所不同。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d2fq83y0z2h6n4.cloudfront.net/X2vNVSpJs5we"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/PvNUQe1hMlA?si=6mVKzrVI8JU2wy4o" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Grand Highlander使用TNGA-K平台，比现时的Highlander拥有更宽的轨距和更长的轴距，尺寸是在Highlander与Sequoia之间。在外形设计方面，Grand Highlander由Calty设计工作室设计，并在印第安纳州普林斯顿的Toyota工厂专门组装。Grand Highlander有独特前进风口及防撞器，尤其是前脸，超巨大的栏栅充满了霸气，可与Lexus LX一比；车尾部设计也与Highlander不一样，设计也比Highlander成熟。车侧的黑色饰边及黑银双色轮圈，呈现出高级感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693093-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_32.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693093-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_32-600x400.jpg" alt="Toyota Grand Highlander Nightshade Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693093" class="wp-caption-text">Toyota Grand Highlander Nightshade Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Grand Highlander将提供三种动力系统，跨越三个不同的等级，为顾客提供各种选择。无论是均衡的2.4升涡轮汽油引擎、高效的2.5升混合动力，还是极致性能的Hybrid MAX，Grand Highlander都能在动力上及耗油量取得平衡。Grand Highlander的汽油引擎可提供最高265匹马力，0-60加速时间为7.5秒（前驱）；混合动力版 （Hybrid）则有245匹马力，0-60加速时间为7.8秒（全驱动）；拥有最大动力的Hybrid MAX，可发出362匹马力，以及400磅呎的扭矩，0-60加速时间为三者最少，只而6.3秒，并且具有5,000磅的拖曳能力。同时，不同动力系统的燃油经济性也有所区别，从24到36英里/加仑不等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693095" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693095-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_47.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693095-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_47-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693095" class="wp-caption-text">2.5升混合动力系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所有三种动力系统都配备了四种驾驶模式（运动，经济，正常及拖车），汽油全驱动和混合动力MAX动力系统上提供了三种多地形选择模式（泥沙，岩石，雪地），以提供卓越的越野能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693096" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693096-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_54.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693096-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_54-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693096" class="wp-caption-text">四种驾驶模式按钮。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>汽油版的AWD可以主动在前后轴之间以及两个后轮之间分配扭力。它使用在后差速器前的电磁耦合器，根据需要启动或停用后轮。混合动力版则使用Electronic On-Demand AWD，提供了出色的燃油效率、起步加速、操控和稳定性。使用电动马达驱动后轮，在湿滑路面可提供稳定的性能。前后轮驱动力分配在100:0和20:80之间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693097" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693097-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_51.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693097-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_51-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693097" class="wp-caption-text">Electronic On-Demand AWD。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Hybrid MAX版的Full-Time Electronic AWD提供更出色的可控性，通过前置混合动力马达和后置eAxle电动马达将动力输送到前后轮。这个系统直接与变速箱搭配，前后轮动力分配在70:30和20:80之间，具体取决于行驶情况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693098" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693098-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_59.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693098-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_59-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693098" class="wp-caption-text">宽敞的内部空间。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Grand Highlander的设计也注重实用性和舒适性。从宽敞的内部空间到智能的储物设计，从中控台的配置到座椅的舒适性，每个细节都考虑到了乘客的需求。虽然车厢是以黑色为主，还好在不少位置以铜色作衬托，也带有高贵感。无论是第一排、第二排还是第三排，每个座位都配备了USB端口和储物空间，让乘客可以随时随地充电和储存物品。整个车舱设有周到的储物空间，以及充足的货物容量，第三排后方可容纳七个随身行李箱。当第二排和第三排座椅折叠下来时，提供了总共97.5立方呎的空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693099" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693099" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693099-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_62.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693099" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693099-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_62-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693099" class="wp-caption-text">中排Captain座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14693100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693100-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_61.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693100-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_61-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693100" class="wp-caption-text">后排三座位。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丰富的技术配置使Grand Highlander更加智能和便捷。从12.3英寸的触摸屏到蓝牙连接、智能助手和云端导航，再到JBL音响系统和连接服务，这款车型满足了现代人对科技的各种需求。装置的语音智能助手，可透过简单语句进行搜索路线、调整音频控制、改变车内温度等。云端导航可以提供最新的地图、交通和路线信息。同时，系统也支持无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693101" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693101-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_50.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693101-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_50-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693101" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14693102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693102" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693102-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_53.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693102-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_53-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693102" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于音乐发烧友来说，Grand Highlander可升级到JBL高级音频系统，拥有11个扬声器，可以在任何音乐类型中打造移动音乐会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14693103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14693103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693103-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_55.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14693103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14693103-2025_Toyota_Grand_Highlander_Hybrid_55-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14693103" class="wp-caption-text">JBL高级音频系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Toyota Grand Highlander Nightshade是一款既实用又豪华的SUV，在Grand Highlander的基础上加上黑色配备，使外型更加独特、高贵，而Hybridh系统无论是在城市中穿梭还是在郊区探险，都能展现出色的性能和节能性。◇</p>
