<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【看电影学英文】&#8217;Mina&#8217; (2) Ask me for your hand</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688602-1280-600x400.webp" />
<h6>《美娜传奇》动画片海报（干净世界提供，甄宇合成）</h6>
<hr>
<p>动画片<ahref="https://www.ganjingworld.com/s/GYA6RxQVQw" rel="nofollow">《美娜传奇》</a>以关于邪恶巫师卡匝伯尔专门绑架未婚公主的传说开篇，美娜被掳走后，几位各有所图的追求者踏上拯救美娜的奇幻冒险之旅。上次跟大家分享了<ahref="https://www.ganjingworld.com/s/GYA6RxQVQw" rel="nofollow">《美娜传奇》</a>里面跟奇幻元素相关的英文，这次来看看跟美娜，还有她的追求者们相关的英文。</p>
<h2>小试牛刀</h2>
<p>下面是影片<ahref="https://www.ganjingworld.com/s/GYA6RxQVQw" rel="nofollow">《美娜传奇》</a>中几句对白，若是你，会怎么翻译？</p>
<p>1）Soldier: Our princess sure is something else!<br />
士兵们是在说，咱们的公主真是另外的东西吗？</p>
<p>2）Ask for your hand<br />
要你的手？要手干嘛？</p>
<p>3）Momma&#8217;s Boy<br />
妈妈的男孩应该是儿子吧，可为什么不直接说儿子？</p>
<p>如果你对上面几句的翻译不太有把握，那下面的内容可能会对你有所帮助。</p>
<h2><strong>Someone is something else</strong></h2>
<p>Something else 是习惯用语，意思是不寻常的，尤其是说特别好，当然也有指特别差的时候。</p>
<p>Someone is something else 口语中常用来夸奖人，形容某人能力出众、与众不同，或出人意料地棒。通常用于对方做出了超乎想像的事情时，表示对对方的极大认可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688574-1280_1.webp"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688574-1280_1-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688574" class="wp-caption-text">美娜疾驰而去，让士兵惊艳。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Soldier: Our princess sure is something else!<br />
士兵：咱们的公主真是非同凡响啊！</p>
<h2><strong>Ask for one&#8217;s hand</strong></h2>
<p>Hand 在这里不仅仅是字面上的手，而是具有象征意义，代表着婚姻。这是一种请求与某人结婚的传统表达方式，体现出对婚姻的承诺和尊重。通常是男性向女性的父亲或家人请求，以获得他们同意将女儿嫁给自己。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688575" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688575-1280_2.webp"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688575-1280_2-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688575" class="wp-caption-text">国王跟美娜说起求婚者。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>King: Rogdai, Kabir and Pha Chan’s mother?have been?asking me for your hand.<br />
国王：饶阁岱、凯博还有陈发的妈妈一直都在跟我提亲。</p>
<p>Have her hand in marriage 就表达得更直接，意思是娶她过门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688576-1280_3.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688576-1280_3-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688576" class="wp-caption-text">国王许诺救回美娜者可成为驸马，并统治王国。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>King: Whoever rescue Mina, will have her hand in marriage as well as the Kingdom.<br />
国王：谁救回美娜，谁就可以娶她，并统治这个王国。</p>
<h2><strong>Marry someone off</strong></h2>
<p>Merry someone off 为某人安排婚姻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688577-1280_4.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688577-1280_4-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688577" class="wp-caption-text">美娜跟罗南谈起国王选驸马的传统。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Mina: He doesn’t believe a woman can rule a kingdom and is ready to marry me off to the first guy who fits his idea of a warrior.<br />
美娜：他不相信女子能统治王国，并已经准备把我嫁给第一个符合他心中“勇士”标准的人。</p>
<h2><strong>Have my blessing</strong></h2>
<p>Have my blessing 有我的祝福或准许，意即我同意、我支持，或我祝福，通常用于授权某人做某事，尤其是在需要父母或长辈批准的情形，如求婚、创业、搬家等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688578-1280_5.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688578-1280_5-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688578" class="wp-caption-text">国王同意美娜跟罗南成婚。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>King: I hear you&#8217;ve secretly stashed some things in your boat to make a getaway. You won&#8217;t need it. You and Ronan have my blessing.<br />
国王：我听说你为逃亡而悄悄在船上藏了一些东西。你不需要这样。我同意你和罗南结婚。</p>
<h2><strong>Dream on</strong></h2>
<p>Dream on 表示某事绝不可能发生，带有不屑或嘲讽的语气，相当于“痴心妄想！”“做你的白日梦吧！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688579-1280_6.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688579-1280_6-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688579" class="wp-caption-text">美娜对三位追求者不屑一顾。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Mina: Dream on, I&#8217;m not marrying any of you.<br />
美娜：想得美，我才不会嫁给你们任何一个呢。</p>
<p>在史密斯飞船（Aerosmith）的同名歌曲中，dream on 也带有“坚持梦想”的励志含义。</p>
<h2><strong>Born ready</strong></h2>
<p>Bore ready 强调某人准备就绪，处于随时可以行动的状态。带有幽默或夸张的语气，就像“我打从娘胎就准备好了”那样，帅气回应“你准备好了吗？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688580-1280_7.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688580-1280_7-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688580" class="wp-caption-text">美娜与罗南期待国王宣布他们的婚讯。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Ronan: Your father is about to announce us. Are you ready?<br />
罗南：你父亲即将宣布我们的婚讯。你准备好了吗？<br />
Mina: I was born ready!<br />
美娜：我早就准备好啦！</p>
<h2><strong>Pamper vs. Spoil</strong></h2>
<p>表达宠爱时，spoil 与 pamper 不同：Pamper 指悉心照料、呵护、宠爱、娇惯，指提供奢侈或舒适的待遇，使人感到放松和享受，通常带有正面意味；而 spoil 则比较负面。可以用下面一句话来体现 pamper 与 spoil 之间的差异：</p>
<p>You can be pampered without being spoiled, but excessive pampering often leads to spoiling someone, as seen with children.<br />
你可以被宠爱而不被宠坏，但过度的宠爱往往会导致一个人被宠坏，正如我们在孩子身上常见的那样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688581-1280_8.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688581-1280_8-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688581" class="wp-caption-text">卡匝伯尔说罗南无法提供对美娜的极致宠爱。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Kezabor: You can&#8217;t compete with the lavish life of luxury and pampering that only I can provide.<br />
卡匝伯尔：你比不过我能提供的奢华生活和极致宠爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688584" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688584-1280_9.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688584-1280_9-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688584" class="wp-caption-text">你会被卡匝伯尔宠上天。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Song: You will be spoiled and pampered: Spa massage.<br />
歌中唱到：你将被宠爱、被宠上天：水疗按摩。</p>
<h2><strong>Charming</strong></h2>
<p>Charming 用来形容某人或某物具有吸引力、能使人产生好感和愉悦的特质，通常指内在的气质、举止或性格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688585" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688585-1280_10.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688585-1280_10-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688585" class="wp-caption-text">凯博有迷之自信。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Kabir:?Mina, if you are lucky enough tomorrow and the King chooses me as your husband, I will be far more charming than these two.<br />
凯博：美娜，如果明天你够幸运，国王选了我做你的丈夫，我会远比这两个家伙更有魅力。</p>
<h2><strong>Dashing</strong></h2>
<p>Dashing 作为形容词，意思是潇洒的、气派的、时髦的、英勇的、有冲劲的等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688586" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688586-1280_11.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688586-1280_11-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688586" class="wp-caption-text">美眉觉得陈发太帅了（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Lady: He&#8217;s so manly! And so dashing!<br />
美眉：他好有男人味哦！还超级帅！</p>
<h2><strong>Mama&#8217;s boy</strong></h2>
<p>Mama&#8217;s boy，亦称 mommy&#8217;s boy 或 mother&#8217;s boy，并不是真的在说妈妈的男孩，而是依赖母亲的男性——妈宝男，即永远长不大的男人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688587-1280_12.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688587-1280_12-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688587" class="wp-caption-text">饶格岱气急败坏威胁陈发闭嘴。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Rogdai:?And if you don&#8217;t shut up, you&#8217;ll be the next, Momma&#8217;s Boy!<br />
饶格岱：再不闭嘴，下一个就是你，妈宝男！</p>
<h2><strong>Barbarian?</strong></h2>
<p>Barbarian 意思是野蛮人，无教养的人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14688588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14688588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688588-1280_13.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14688588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14688588-1280_13-600x338.webp" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14688588" class="wp-caption-text">卡匝伯尔质疑美娜的出身与教养。（干净世界视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Kezabor:?I need to ask you, the new girl, Mina, you sure she comes from a good family? &#8216;Cuz she behaves like a barbarian.<br />
卡匝伯尔：我得问问你，这个新来的女孩美娜，你确定她真的是出自好人家吗？她那行为举止简直像个野蛮人。</p>
<h2><strong>Mortal</strong></h2>
<p>Mortal 作为形容词，主要意思是会死的、终有一死的，还可表示致命的、极度的或不共戴天的；作为名词，指凡人、普通人、人类，与 immortal 意思相对。</p>
