<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波克莱台商会获赞年轻化 陈冠中就任会长</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689129-DSC07126-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波克莱台湾商会1月24日举办换届晚宴，新会长就职。左起：元太科技财务长林朝仲、北美洲台湾商会联合总会名誉总会长江俊霖、波克莱台商会卸任会长陈奕如、新任会长陈冠中、经文处处长廖朝宏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年02月02日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）波克莱台湾商会1月24日晚上在珍宝轩酒家举办新届会长就职典礼，得到波士顿地区及北美总会嘉宾的热情祝贺，还获赞年轻成员越来越多，商会越来越有活力。</p>
<p>当晚在60多位嘉宾的见证下，陈奕如卸下波克莱台商会会长职务，交棒给陈冠中。这位新会长是资深中医师，在麻州North Reading、Medford等地都开有诊所。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14689135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689135" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689135-IMG_9895.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689135-IMG_9895-600x266.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="266" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689135" class="wp-caption-text">波克莱台湾商会1月24日举办换届晚宴，60位嘉宾参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14689126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689126" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689126-DSC07096.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689126-DSC07096-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689126" class="wp-caption-text">波克莱台湾商会1月24日举办换届晚宴，60位嘉宾参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>“在商会认识人生另一半”</strong></h2>
<p>卸任会长陈奕如笑言：“我自己就是通过商会赞助的活动，认识我人生未来的另一半。”她说，她与未婚夫在台湾龙舟队认识，然后在商会加深了解，今年就要结婚了。来宾们以热烈掌声和欢呼表达祝贺。</p>
<p>陈奕如回顾道，过去一年商会举办了14场活动，总共有500多人参与。此外，会员和干部们还参加了诸多外部活动，如世界台湾商会联合总会年会、侨委会领导班、波士顿台湾龙舟队等等。她感慨，任内时间飞快，但商会确实达成了“带进更多年轻人”的目标。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14689125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689125" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689125-DSC07094.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689125" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689125-DSC07094-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689125" class="wp-caption-text">卸任会长陈奕如希望商会带进更多年轻人。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14689128" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689128-DSC07123.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689128" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689128-DSC07123-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689128" class="wp-caption-text">卸任会长陈奕如向干部们颁赠感谢状。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于商会提供给年轻人的资源，陈奕如还说：“我自己在参加北美商会之前，我从来没有想过，台湾人在海外有个这么大的社团，大家都非常用心，非常真诚地在教导、提携这些后辈。”</p>
<p>晚宴期间，陈奕如还特别向干部团队的每一位成员颁赠感谢状，感恩他们在过去两年的辛苦和支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14689127" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689127" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689127-DSC07105.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689127-DSC07105-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689127" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏（左）、侨教中心主任高家富（右）向新任会长陈冠中颁赠侨委会贺状。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14689131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689131" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689131-DSC07137.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689131-DSC07137-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689131" class="wp-caption-text">贵宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>嘉宾及会员支持商会 “很有台湾味”</strong></h2>
<p>陈冠中医师在就任会长之后感言，他了解了波克莱台商会之后，感觉这个团体“很有台湾味，很有人情味”，会员们也都想为台湾尽一份心力。这样的氛围也激励他在海外为台湾贡献力量。</p>
<p>对于新一届干部团队，陈冠中感谢愿意留任的资深干部，也欢迎新人加入服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14689130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689130" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689130-DSC07131.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689130-DSC07131-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689130" class="wp-caption-text">陈冠中医师称赞波克莱台商会很有人情味。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14689136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689136" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689136-IMG_9898.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689136-IMG_9898-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689136" class="wp-caption-text">陈冠中医师夫妇向来宾们致谢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长交接典礼的监交嘉宾，包括驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、E Ink元太科技公司财务长林朝仲，以及北美洲台湾商会联合总会名誉总会长江俊霖。</p>
<p>赴宴嘉宾还包括侨教中心主任高家富，以及中华专协、新英格兰大波士顿台商会、台美菁英协会、新英格兰台湾同学会联合会等多个团体的会长。</p>
<p>廖朝宏感谢陈奕如带领商会走向年轻化，举办更丰富的活动；也祝福商会新年在新届会长的带领下更加成功。</p>
<p>江俊霖特别从纽约搭火车赶来参加晚宴。他感慨，既看到了北美商会前辈游胜雄、苏鸿昌等许多老朋友，也看到年轻成员越来越多，“商会越来越蓬勃”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14689134" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689134" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689134-DSC07147.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689134-DSC07147-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689134" class="wp-caption-text">台学联会长李典璋（左）抽中罗静春捐赠的红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14689132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14689132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689132-DSC07142.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14689132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14689132-DSC07142-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14689132" class="wp-caption-text">新会员邱亮德（右）抽中江俊霖捐赠的红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为支持这场餐会，创会会长游胜雄特别捐赠1100美元，荣誉会长苏鸿昌亦捐款1000美元，芝加哥台美商会荣誉会长李嘉展捐赠500美元。资深会员杨罗东、许寿文、郭竞儒也各自捐赠100或200美元。</p>
<p>此外，多位嘉宾也带来了礼券、现金红包、精美礼品用作抽奖。当晚的最大奖项，是元太科技公司与亚马逊合作推出的最新款彩色Kindle阅读器。抽中者喜笑颜开，赢得热烈掌声。</p>
