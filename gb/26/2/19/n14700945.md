<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>盐埕千灯点亮南区 台湾第一盐的夜间风景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700954-831120-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>两座竹架灯塔并立，一侧为书法灯笼，一侧为彩绘作品，展现传统与创意交融。（陈锡兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月19日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）2月16日除夕夜，台南南区盐埕出张所旁，多了一片蓝色的光。灯一亮，人群就慢慢聚过来。有人带着孩子，有人牵着长辈，更多的是在地居民，熟门熟路地走进草地中央。</p>
<p>蓝色灯串从高处垂落，像网子一样铺开。底下是一座座三角形的灯塔，竹架搭起，层层挂满灯笼。远看像盐山，近看才知道每一盏灯都不同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14700946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14700946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700946-831111.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14700946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700946-831111-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14700946" class="wp-caption-text">盐埕出张所入口夜景，蓝色灯串与灯塔交织成光网。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14700947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14700947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700947-831113.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14700947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700947-831113-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14700947" class="wp-caption-text">白色书法灯笼整齐排列，写满祝福与单位名称。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14700948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14700948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700948-831114.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14700948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700948-831114-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14700948" class="wp-caption-text">三角灯塔层层挂满灯笼，象征盐山意象。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>金灯塔与盐业记忆</h2>
<p>今年的“盐埕千灯季”以“台湾第一盐”为主题。</p>
<p>南区曾是重要盐田地带，盐业带来生计，也留下历史。这次灯会把盐山意象做成“金灯塔”，塔底铺上白沙，象征过去堆盐成山的画面。</p>
<p>灯塔不是金属钢架，而是竹子搭成。这样的材质，反而更贴近土地。灯笼一层层往上排，像盐堆慢慢堆高。灯光透出来，不强烈，却稳定。</p>
<p>走近看，每盏灯都画得不一样。有的写着“平安”“温暖”“成功”，有的画着马、动物、房子、星星。笔触有大人的工整，也有孩子的涂鸦。灯笼下还挂着小卡片，标示单位与年份。</p>
<p>这些灯不是装饰而已，是大家一起完成的成果。从里办公室到学校、社区团体，都能在灯上找到名字。夜色里，祝福变得具体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14700949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14700949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700949-831115.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14700949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700949-831115-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14700949" class="wp-caption-text">孩子笔触与生肖图案清晰可见，每一盏都是在地心愿。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14700950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14700950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700950-831116.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14700950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700950-831116-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14700950" class="wp-caption-text">多层灯笼排列成墙，写着“平安”“成功”“千安”等字样，年节氛围浓厚。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>老建筑的新角色</h2>
<p>盐埕出张所本身就是一段历史。日治时期的建筑，见证盐业与港口发展。如今成为文化空间，再加上灯会活动，老屋多了新用途。</p>
<p>出张所前草地上，一只大型彩绘公仔坐着，旁边是美丽的灯饰。老屋、灯塔、公仔，摆在一起不突兀，反而有生活感。有人在灯下聊天，有人拍照，有人只是站着看。蓝色灯串从灯塔顶端向四周延伸，像星空落在地面。</p>
<p>站在灯网下抬头，光点一闪一闪，不刺眼，也不喧闹。这里没有舞台音响的吵杂，多的是慢慢走、慢慢看的节奏。夜深时，人群渐少，灯还亮着。三角灯塔在草地中央静静站着，像守着这片土地的记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14700951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14700951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700951-831117.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14700951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700951-831117-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14700951" class="wp-caption-text">盐埕出张所老建筑前的大型公仔与灯饰相映，历史空间在夜色中重现新生命。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14700952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14700952" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700952-831118.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14700952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700952-831118-600x648.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="487" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14700952" class="wp-caption-text">灯饰立于草地前方，后方三角灯塔与蓝色灯网交织，成为热门打卡景点。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>属于南区的新年风景</h2>
<p>选在除夕前后举办，灯会多了一层团圆意味。新的一年，有人期待平安，有人希望顺利。这些愿望写在灯上，也挂在心里。</p>
<p>摄影家陈锡兴记录下这一晚。镜头里没有夸张构图，多半是自然的角度。远景是一整片灯海，近景是彩绘的细节。画面简单，却真实。</p>
<p>2026年的南区，多了一场属于自己的灯会。不是烟火的瞬间，而是一种可以停下来看的亮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14700955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14700955" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700955-831121.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14700955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14700955-831121-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14700955" class="wp-caption-text">仰角拍摄灯塔，蓝色灯串向四方延伸，如星河倾泻而下，灯笼层层发亮。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
