<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英国电影学院奖揭晓 《一战再战》获六大奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14703579-20260223-mark-WB01.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
