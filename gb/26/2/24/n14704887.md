<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>元宵节走春秘境——台湾屏东南州糖厂花海</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704943-2602230232252378-600x381.jpg" />
<h6>台湾屏东南州糖厂有美丽的百日草（百日菊）。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾采访报导）元宵节将至，欢庆新春佳节，除了可在3月3日晚间观赏今年的重要天象“月全食”之外，摄影师宋永泰也分享元宵节前后走春秘境──台湾屏东南州糖厂花海，这是全家出游可避开人潮的美丽景点。</p>
<p>台湾中央气象署表示，3月3日元宵节傍晚起，台湾各地皆有机会看见“月出带食”的天文现象，晚间7时4分至8时3分达到月全食阶段。预估下次台湾见到全程月食要等到2028年12月31日。</p>

<figure id="attachment_14704944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704944" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704944-2602230232272378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704944" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704944-2602230232272378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="928" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704944" class="wp-caption-text">台湾屏东南州糖厂有美丽的百日草（百日菊）。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704950-2602230225062378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704950" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704950-2602230225062378-600x271.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704950" class="wp-caption-text">台湾屏东南州糖厂有美丽的橘色波斯菊。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰表示，黄历新年前后，他们两度走访台湾屏东南州糖厂看花海。屏东南州糖厂原为甘蔗产业用地，现已多元化经营。每年黄历新年前后会在广大腹地种植观赏性植物，形成花海景观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704945-2602230234192378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704945" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704945-2602230234192378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="847" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704945" class="wp-caption-text">台湾屏东南州糖厂将花装饰在牛的头上。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰说，屏东南州糖厂常见花种包含百日草（百日菊）、波斯菊，且不同花期错开，让游客可以一路欣赏至元宵节前后。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704946" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704946-2602230232272378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704946" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704946-2602230232272378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="928" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704946" class="wp-caption-text">台湾屏东南州糖厂有美丽的百日草（百日菊）。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>百日草（百日菊）的花语是“天长地久”“想念远方的朋友”“步步高升”“坚韧不拔”，橘色波斯菊的花语是“热情”“活力”，粉色波斯菊的花语为“纯真”“永远快乐”“温柔浪漫”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704947-2602230232232378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704947" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704947-2602230232232378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704947" class="wp-caption-text">台湾屏东南州糖厂有美丽的百日草（百日菊）。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰表示，屏东南州糖厂是过元宵赏花的秘境，不需到热门景点人挤人，以免影响全家走春游兴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704948" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704948-2602230227522378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704948" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704948-2602230227522378.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704948" class="wp-caption-text">台湾屏东南州糖厂有美丽的粉色波斯菊。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他说，南州糖厂园区内景观多元，包含日治时期神社遗址、烤土窑（童年记忆的焢窑）、烤肉区、运动空间，并与南州运动公园相连，这里腹地广大，停车相当方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704949-2602230225082378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704949" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704949-2602230225082378-600x296.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704949" class="wp-caption-text">台湾屏东南州糖厂有美丽的橘色波斯菊。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他也提醒，元宵节当晚，记得要去看难得天象“月全食”。台湾气象署指出，在天候条件许可的情况下，自3月3日下午5时50分左右月亮东升后，各地皆可见到 “月出带食”的月食过程，其中最精彩的是晚间7时4分至8时3分月面全食阶段，将可见到暗红色的大满月月面。</p>
