<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>彩竹映恶地 王建中镜头下的清晨与黄昏</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705116-831798-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日落时分，整个月世界被染成橘红色，彩竹与恶地交织成一幅自然画卷。（王建中提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾高雄报导）“现在月世界是彩竹正美的季节，清晨或黄昏时金色阳光洒下，整个月世界彩竹瞬间变成金黄色，搭配恶地的地形非常美丽。”摄影家王建中这样说。他拍的画面，不夸张，也不刻意追求戏剧效果。就是在对的时间，站在对的位置，等光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705115" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705115-831797.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705115-831797-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705115" class="wp-caption-text">山壁水蚀纹理在侧光下更加立体，显现地质岁月痕迹。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705114" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705114-831796.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705114-831796-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705114" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳低垂，橘红天空映照泥岩山壁与水面。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>泥岩恶地的地质身世灰白色的泥岩山脊一层层堆叠，像刀削过的断面，线条分明。这是田寮月世界最典型的景观。这一带属于泥岩恶地地形，地质年代约在上新世到更新世之间，泥岩质地松软，遇水容易崩解，再加上长年雨水冲刷，形成锯齿状的山棱与深刻沟谷。</p>
<p>远远望去，宛如月球表面，因此得名。泥岩含盐量高、排水不良，植物难以生长，才会呈现裸露、干裂的样貌。但也因为如此，山壁纹理格外清楚，每一道水蚀痕迹都像时间刻下的笔划。王建中的镜头，没有把恶地拍得荒凉，反而让人看见它的层次。</p>
<p>清晨时分，光线从山侧斜斜照下，原本冷灰色的泥岩慢慢转成暖黄。山谷间的彩竹在阳光下发亮，像被点燃一样。竹林并不整齐，有的金黄，有的仍带绿意，与裸露的山壁交错，形成鲜明对比。那不是滤镜效果，而是阳光真正落在土地上的颜色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705111" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705111-831793.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705111-831793-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705111" class="wp-caption-text">远望村落与竹林，金色光影覆盖整片山谷。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705110" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705110-831792.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705110-831792-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705110" class="wp-caption-text">锯齿状的泥岩棱线清晰可见，呈现典型恶地地形纹理。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705109-831791.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705109-831791-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705109" class="wp-caption-text">清晨斜光洒落，泥岩山脊转为暖黄，彩竹在山谷间闪着金色光芒。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水塘与夕阳的柔软看到恶地间的小水塘，水面平静，倒映天空与山形。四周是干燥的泥岩，却因为这一汪水，多了柔软。黄昏时，夕阳落在地平线上，天空转成橘红色，水面跟着染色。整个山谷像被包在一层温热的光里。原本坚硬的地形，在那一刻显得温和许多。</p>
<p>他把镜头拉远，高速公路从恶地间穿过。车流不断，却没有破坏画面，反而让人意识到，这片地形就在我们生活旁边。荒野与城市，不是对立，而是并存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705112" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705112-831794.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705112-831794-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705112" class="wp-caption-text">高速公路穿越恶地地形，城市与荒野并存于同一画面。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14705113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705113" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705113-831795.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14705113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705113-831795-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705113" class="wp-caption-text">小水塘静静躺在山谷中央，为干燥地貌添上一抹柔软。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>光，只停留几分钟他说，彩竹最漂亮的时候，就是日出前后与日落前那段时间。太阳高挂时，颜色反而平淡。拍月世界，需要早起，也需要耐心。因为光只停留几分钟，错过就没了。看着这些照片，会发现月世界并不只有灰与荒。在对的季节、对的时间，它也会金黄，也会温柔。</p>
<p>恶地是长年侵蚀留下的痕迹；彩竹是季节更替的表情。两者放在一起，本来冲突，却在光线中找到平衡。王建中的作品，没有多余的语言，只是把土地在某一刻的样子，好好留下。月世界不是教科书里冷冰冰的名词，它会变色，清晨一种样子，黄昏又是另一种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14705117" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14705117" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705117-831799.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14705117" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705117-831799-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14705117" class="wp-caption-text">月世界不是教科书里冷冰冰的名词，它会变色，清晨一种样子，黄昏又是另一种。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
