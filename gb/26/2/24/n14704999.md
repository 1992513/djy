<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《阳光》票房破6.5亿 安心亚：很开心觉得幸运</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14705006-260224053823100311.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
