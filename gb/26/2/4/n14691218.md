<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯独立房中位价突破100万</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月04日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯的独立房中位价在去年最后一个季度创下新高，正式跻身“百万澳元俱乐部”。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，房地产集团</span><span lang="en-US">Domain</span><span lang="zh-TW">的去年第四季度独立房价格报告显示，珀斯独立房中位价在该季度几乎上涨</span><span lang="en-US">10%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，升至</span><span lang="en-US">108</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">7762</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">西澳房地产协会主席布朗女士（</span><span lang="en-US">Suzanne Brown</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，房源挂牌量偏低、土地供应不足、新房完工量有限，以及联邦政府的首次置业者担保计划，共同推动房价不断刷新高点。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">2019</span>年<span lang="en-US">12</span>月，珀斯独立房中位价仅略高于<span lang="en-US">52.8</span>万澳元，但到<span lang="en-US">2024</span>年同月，这一数字几乎翻倍，达到<span lang="en-US">91.9</span>万澳元；再过<span lang="en-US">12</span>个月，中位价又上涨了超过<span lang="en-US">16.8</span>万澳元，这是近年来最大的涨幅。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">与此同时，珀斯单元房价格在过去一年也上涨了近</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，从</span><span lang="en-US">2024</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">月的</span><span lang="en-US">51</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">6425</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元升至</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">月的</span><span lang="en-US">60</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">8520</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Domain</span><span lang="zh-TW">首席研究与经济学主管鲍威尔（</span><span lang="en-US">Nicola Powell</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，可负担性压力正在重塑买家行为。</span></p>
<p>她说：“房价的快速上涨让许多买家更难实现置业目标，迫使他们作出更艰难的取舍，并延长储蓄时间。”</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“虽然房价涨幅预计会放缓，但如果住房供应得不到改善，可负担性挑战仍将持续存在。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">自</span><span lang="en-US">2019</span><span lang="zh-TW">年以来，入门级独立房价格已上涨近</span><span lang="en-US">75%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，购房成本增加约</span><span lang="en-US">27.5</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，涨幅远远超过工资增长。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">目前，珀斯家庭用于偿还中位按揭的支出已接近收入的</span><span lang="en-US">40%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，而</span><span lang="en-US">2019</span><span lang="zh-TW">年这一比例仅为</span><span lang="en-US">22%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>过去五年中，珀斯已有超过<span lang="en-US">50</span>个城区的独立房价格实现翻倍，其中包括<span lang="en-US">Orelia</span>、<span lang="en-US">Parmelia</span>、<span lang="en-US">Cooloongup</span>、<span lang="en-US">Armadale</span>和<span lang="en-US">Merriwa</span>。</p>
<p>鲍威尔表示，尽管独立房中位价已突破<span lang="en-US">100</span>万澳元，但在较高平均工资水平以及首次置业者储蓄周期相对较短的支撑下，珀斯相较其它首府城市仍具备一定性价比。</p>
<p>继去年<span lang="en-US">4</span>月阿德莱德之后，珀斯成为第六个独立房中位价突破<span lang="en-US">100</span>万澳元的首府城市。</p>
<p>最新加入“百万澳元俱乐部”的西澳城区包括<span lang="en-US">Bayswater</span>、<span lang="en-US">Bedford</span>、<span lang="en-US">Canning Vale</span>、<span lang="en-US">Darch</span>、<span lang="en-US">Hilton</span>、<span lang="en-US">Madeley</span>、<span lang="en-US">Padbury</span>和<span lang="en-US">Roleystone</span>。</p>
<p>布朗提醒仍在找房的买家，市场上依然存在价格低于中位价的独立房。</p>
<p>目前仍有多个城区的独立房中位价低于<span lang="en-US">100</span>万澳元，其中<span lang="en-US">Midland</span>、<span lang="en-US">Northbridge</span>、<span lang="en-US">Medina</span>、<span lang="en-US">Armadale</span>和<span lang="en-US">Osborne Park</span>的平均价格介于<span lang="en-US">57</span>万至<span lang="en-US">59.6</span>万澳元之间。</p>
<p>而珀斯市区、<span lang="en-US">Mandurah</span>、<span lang="en-US">Greenfields</span>、<span lang="en-US">Parmelia</span>以及<span lang="en-US">East Perth</span>的独立房中位价则在<span lang="en-US">60.5</span>万至<span lang="en-US">62.05</span>万澳元之间。</p>
