<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】豪华七人座 驾乘更舒适 2026 Infiniti QX60 Autography AWD</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707753-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_28-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 Infiniti QX60 Autography AWD。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月28日讯】不知不觉中，Infiniti品牌已全部去掉了轿车选择，现时只剩下SUV／CUV的汽车，当中QX80这巨兽在去年更新之外，Infiniti车系中也有QX50、QX55和QX60，由于QX60可坐入最多七人，非常实用，所以大受欢迎，并在去年进行了Facelift小更新。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d10efcw5kfoez9.cloudfront.net/O0yBST1pAbHf"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/D5olPGhu75w?si=rbDJmLrvNmveJvPm" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>既然进行了更新，2026年款QX60的外形与以往也有分别，首先是车前的厂徽，采用立体设计，比以往更为耐看，再配上新的进气口栏栅，由旧有的菱形改为与厂徽下方的左右斜线平行的“八”字形条栏，更有特式。头灯组顶部的LED饰灯也由三个变七个。立体厂徽附有背光灯，在晚上灯光把厂徽淡淡包围，比其他车厂的强光厂徽更为好看。车身颜色有多种选择，并且更有双色Two-Tone选择，测试车上半身（车窗及以上）是黑色，下半色是“Radiant Whitel”白色，两色相差很大，才发现两侧后视镜外壳也分为上下两种颜色。C柱与D柱中间的玻璃寞，2026年QX60 Autograph加上了“Autograph”字体。另外，QX60在车顶上装上了双鱼鳍天线，有点奇怪！车尾的改动小，也只集中在下部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707730" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707730-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14707730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707730-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707730" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Infiniti QX60 Autography AWD。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最大改动的，要算是动力心脏了，将旧有的VQ35DD引擎去掉，换上KR20DDET，排气量由3.5升变为2升，虽然最大马力由295减少到268匹，扭矩却由270增至286磅呎，拥有6,000磅拖力，搭配9速自动变速箱，并有引擎自动熄火功能之助，耗油量也比旧有为佳，城市由21mpg增加到22mpg、公路由26mpg增加到27/28mpg。新引擎的声音仍是沉稳悦耳，加速反应在起步、中段加速都非常理想。车厢也能保持宁静，归功于引擎室的隔音物。QX60有最新的智能AWD，可最多将动力平均分配至前后轴，即50／50动力分布，可应付雪地上坡起步等，相当不错。行车模式有多种，包括Auto、Sport、Personal、Snow，更有专为拖车的Towing模式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707731-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_51.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14707731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707731-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_51-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707731" class="wp-caption-text">KR20DDET引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车厢内装潢豪华，黑色真皮皮革不仅用在座椅上，前台及车门内侧也有用上，用繨线交织出菱形格调，既不单调又显优雅。前座是“零重力（Zero-gravity）”结构座椅，可减少长途驾驶的疲累，并且装有三种按摩位置设定，分别是“Refreshing”、“Relaxing”及“Lumbar”。测试车QX60 Autograph，中排采用两个Captain座位，各有三段加热。座位只需按一键便会升起前移，让出更大地方予乘客出入。至于第三排座椅，也是很特别，采用半手动半自动方式，折叠时需要手动拉动放下，但要使用座位时，货舱内侧左右各有一个按钮，可电动升起座位靠背。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707733-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_72.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14707733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707733-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_72-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707733" class="wp-caption-text">内装潢豪华。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707737" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707737-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_73.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14707737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707737-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_73-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707737" class="wp-caption-text">中排座位空间大。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>QX60的恒温系统分为三区，前二后一，后方并有独立操作介面，而且中排及后排左右座位上方都有独立送风口。测试的QX60并装有虚拟后视镜，称为“Smart Rear View Mirror”，尺寸为9.6英寸萤幕，可视角度达到50度，大大减少视角盲点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707739" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707739-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_68.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14707739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707739-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_68-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707739" class="wp-caption-text">“Smart Rear View Mirror”。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车系也装有热点连接（Hot Spot）、7个USB充电端口、中排座位有120伏电压插口。中控台底部装有无线充电板，配合无线Apple CarPlay最方便好用，QX60也支援Android Auto连接。Head up display的尺寸为10.8英寸，中控台萤幕为12.3英寸。萤幕下方是恒温系统及音响控制，除了一些旋扭凸起来，其他按键也在此面版上，使用时要把面版按下去，解决了“轻触式”面版的问题，不会轻轻一碰就“按”了。音响换上了Klipsch品牌，Autograph型号更是有20个扬声器的Klipsch高级音响，前座头枕两侧也装有高音喇叭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707741-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_70.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14707741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707741-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_70-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707741" class="wp-caption-text">前座头枕装有高音喇叭。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707742-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_54.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14707742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707742-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_54-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707742" class="wp-caption-text">中控台萤幕有12.3英寸。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试车的2026 QX60装有“Technology Package”套装，其中之一的配件是ProPILOT Assist 2.1，与1.1版本比较，最大分别是可放开方向盘（Hands-off），以及支援自动变更车道与超车，令自动驾驶时更为方便安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707743-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_52.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14707743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707743-2026_Infiniti_QX60_Autograph_52-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707743" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年Infiniti QX60，虽然是经过了Facelift，外形更为好看，然而并没有给人耳目一新之感。另外，以2升直四引擎换掉了3.5升V6引擎，是现时流行的做法，既减耗油量也能保持相对大的动力。使用双活塞阻尼器和增加了车体侧倾刚度，行车时车体倾侧明显改善，不论在高速公路或市区驾驶都很舒适。以豪华中型SUV来说，顶级的Infiniti QX60的售价由66,690美元起，算是物有所值了！◇</p>
