<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狮潭新凤吊桥旁 薰衣草全面盛开中</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704336-831580-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黄色公仔与鲸尾装置艺术矗立花海中，增添童趣氛围。（林桔春提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月23日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾苗栗报导）大年初七，新的一周开始，山城已悄悄换上春装。苗栗狮潭乡义民庙右侧、新凤吊桥旁，一大片薰衣草正盛开。紫色花穗在阳光下闪着细微光泽，远远望去像铺在山脚的一块柔软地毯。摄影家林桔春特地前往拍摄。他说，第一次看到这么壮观的一片薰衣草花田。站在新凤吊桥上往下望，红色桥身与紫色花海形成强烈对比，画面自然干净，不需要太多修饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704330" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704330-831574.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704330-831574-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704330" class="wp-caption-text">红色新凤吊桥入口映入眼帘，桥身笔直延伸，连接山城与春日花景。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704332" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704332-831576.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704332-831576-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704332" class="wp-caption-text">整片薰衣草盛开，紫色铺满视野，与老树与红屋顶形成对比。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704333" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704333-831577.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704333-831577-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704333" class="wp-caption-text">近拍薰衣草花穗，细长花梗随风摇曳，紫色小花绽放其间。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吊桥与古道 交织山城风景新凤吊桥横跨溪谷，是狮潭乡著名地标之一，也是鸣凤古道的起点之一。鸣凤古道属于入门型健行步道，沿途竹林成荫，坡度不陡，假日常见一家大小前来踏青。如今加上花田景观，让原本单纯的健行路线，多了赏花的理由。义民庙则是客庄重要信仰中心，平日香火不断。庙宇旁出现整片薰衣草，让庄严的庙埕多了一点春日柔软。山林、吊桥、古道与花海交织成一幅平实却耐看的风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704331" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704331-831575.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704331-831575-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704331" class="wp-caption-text">吊桥侧拍画面，弧形桥身与远方山峦交织，展现狮潭山城的静谧风光。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704341" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704341-831584.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704341-831584-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704341" class="wp-caption-text">黄色公仔静立紫色花丛中，笑脸为春日增添温暖色彩。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704334" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704334-831578.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704334-831578-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704334" class="wp-caption-text">花田特写画面，密集花穗层层排列，展现春日最柔美的色彩。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花况正好 把握这几天目前花况全面盛开，是最佳观赏时段。清晨光线柔和，午后阳光明亮，各有不同味道。微风吹过时，整片花穗轻轻晃动，空气中隐约飘着淡淡香气。狮潭位于苗栗内山，四周环山，少了都市喧嚣。来这里，不必安排复杂行程，走过吊桥、沿着花田散步，再顺道探访古道，就是一趟刚刚好的春日小旅行。春天的消息，有时就藏在山脚。紫色铺满眼前的那一刻，便知道，花开正好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704335" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704335-831579.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704335-831579-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704335" class="wp-caption-text">薰衣草前景与老屋相映，山城景色多了一抹浪漫色彩。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704337" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704337-831581.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704337-831581-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704337" class="wp-caption-text">细拍花朵盛开瞬间，柔焦背景衬托薰衣草的优雅姿态。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704339" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704339-831582.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14704339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704339-831582-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704339" class="wp-caption-text">木头拱门伫立花田中央，自然与艺术静静交会。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>交通资讯地点：苗栗县狮潭乡义民庙右侧，新凤吊桥旁建议自行开车前往，假日车流较多，请提早抵达。赏花时请勿踩踏花田，共同维护这片难得的山城春景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704340" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704340-831583.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704340-831583-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704340" class="wp-caption-text">辽阔花海延伸至远方，公仔与装置点缀其间，画面童趣十足。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
