<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>勒星顿中文学校贺马年 师生展演才艺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704478-DSC07714-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>勒星顿中文学校2月22日在贝尔蒙特镇（Belmont）中学礼堂举办马年庆典。东北大学舞龙舞狮队带来献瑞表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨贝尔蒙特镇报导）2月22日大年初六，麻州最大的中文学校之一，勒星顿中文学校（Lexington Chinese School）举办新年庆典，在展示孩子们才艺的同时，还联合中华民俗艺术工作坊，为师生和家长带来精彩演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704481-DSC07754.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704481-DSC07754-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704481" class="wp-caption-text">勒星顿中文学校2月22日在贝尔蒙特镇中学礼堂举办马年庆典。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704471" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704471-DSC07638.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704471-DSC07638-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704471" class="wp-caption-text">师生和家长总计数百人参加庆典。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>师生齐上台 热闹庆新年</strong></h2>
<p>当天下午在贝尔蒙特镇（Belmont）中学礼堂，勒星顿中文学校的学生、老师和家长总计数百人欢聚一堂，在热闹的气氛下迎接黄历马年的到来。</p>
<p>节目一开始便充满欢乐。副校长吴正江以幽默和略显夸张的风格主持趣味问答，让孩子们展示他们学到的过年文化习俗。随后，吴正江还戴上鸭舌帽，表演起“中文欢乐说唱”，一边调动现场气氛，一边向孩子们提出学业问题。礼堂内笑声连连，欢呼不断。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704470-DSC07634.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704470-DSC07634-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704470" class="wp-caption-text">副校长吴正江首先主持暖场抢答活动，还表演欢乐说唱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704472-DSC07640.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704472-DSC07640-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704472" class="wp-caption-text">庆典由中文学校学生简伟哲、赖纪玮主持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场庆典由中文学校学生简伟哲、赖纪玮主持。在副校长的暖场活动之后，中华民俗艺术工作坊的师生首先带来武术表演“以武会友”和舞蹈节目“红焰凝香”、“张灯结彩”。随后，东北大学舞龙舞狮团的两头彩狮跳进礼堂，伴随喧闹的锣鼓声，沿着过道向师生和家长们献瑞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704473-DSC07652.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704473-DSC07652-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704473" class="wp-caption-text">中华民俗艺术工作坊表演武术。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704475" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704475-DSC07669.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704475-DSC07669-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704475" class="wp-caption-text">中华民俗艺术工作坊表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704476" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704476-DSC07674.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704476-DSC07674-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704476" class="wp-caption-text">中华民俗艺术工作坊表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在一个多小时的庆典中，中文学校幼年班和双语班的孩子们轮番登台，用中文演唱歌曲，以及表演舞蹈、武术、二胡音乐、扯铃等文艺节目，展示他们学习中文和文化的成果。家长们也纷纷涌到台前，用手机记录下孩子们的精彩表现。</p>
<p>庆典最后，教务主任陈淑君和副主任陈馨宜为在学艺竞赛中获奖的孩子们颁发奖状，鼓励他们在新一年里继续学好中文。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704479" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704479-DSC07726.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704479-DSC07726-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704479" class="wp-caption-text">勒星顿中文学校师生表演二胡音乐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704480-DSC07741.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704480-DSC07741-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704480" class="wp-caption-text">孩子们表演贺年歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704482" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704482" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704482-DSC07778.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704482-DSC07778-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704482" class="wp-caption-text">学生们表演扯铃。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>传承文化 感恩付出</strong></h2>
<p>回顾过去一年，勒星顿中文学校校长薛铭仁的总结是“感恩”、“承诺”和“传承”。</p>
<p>他感谢老师们认真教好学生，感谢家长在百忙之中抽时间陪孩子上中文课，感谢孩子们每周末都来学习中文，也感谢经文处及侨教中心提供支持。薛铭仁特别提到，学校本学期募款超过1万美元，为此必须感谢各位家长、赞助商和热心人士。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704474-DSC07660.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704474-DSC07660-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704474" class="wp-caption-text">勒星顿中文学校校长薛铭仁感谢老师们的付出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704468" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704468-DSC07623.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704468-DSC07623-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704468" class="wp-caption-text">礼堂外有点心义卖摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704469" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704469-DSC07625.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704469-DSC07625-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704469" class="wp-caption-text">黄明嘉老师在礼堂外展示书法。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于学生，薛铭仁勉励说：“马向前跑，不是一瞬间的速度，而是靠持久的耐力。学习中文也是这样，你们要持之以恒。”他说，学校对学生和家长做出了承诺，希望孩子们也对自己做出承诺，面对困难不要退缩。</p>
<p>薛铭仁也鼓励孩子们报名参加新英格兰及美东地区中文学艺竞赛，展示学习成果。他说，语言是文化的根，看到孩子们用中文写下感恩和祝福，就看到了文化的延续；希望孩子们未来在中文学习方面走得更远。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14704477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704477-DSC07688.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704477-DSC07688-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704477" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏勉励学生们继续学好中文。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14704483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14704483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704483-DSC07794.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14704483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14704483-DSC07794-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14704483" class="wp-caption-text">勒星顿中文学校向学艺竞赛获奖者颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、新英格兰中文学校协会会长冯文鸾也都到场共襄盛举。</p>
<p>廖朝宏称赞说，勒星顿中文学校不仅是该地区最大的中文学校之一，也提供着最高水准的教学服务。他感谢薛铭仁校长及团队为教学工作付出，也勉励孩子们继续学好中文，“开启另一扇门，探索很多很多不同的世界跟新奇的文化”。◇</p>
