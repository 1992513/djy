<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿安良工商会 携三城会员同贺新春</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706964-DSC07994-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿安良工商会2月25日举办马年春宴。政要嘉宾和安良醒狮团合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年02月27日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）黄历新年马年到来，波士顿安良工商会2月25日依循传统举办春宴，送好礼、助公益，携当地侨社伙伴及纽约、罗德岛、芝加哥的安良会员共同庆贺佳节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14706963" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706963-DSC07986.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706963-DSC07986-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706963" class="wp-caption-text">安良醒狮团舞狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706965-DSC08007.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706965-DSC08007-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706965" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706966-DSC08008.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706966-DSC08008-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706966" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>侨社砥柱 喜迎新年</strong></h2>
<p>当晚在波士顿华埠帝苑酒楼，约350位来宾欢聚一堂，迎贺新春。其中，有波士顿安良工商会会员，也有来自罗德岛、芝加哥和纽约的安良代表，以及波士顿华埠各侨团、当地政要及警察等各界嘉宾。</p>
<p>晚会由阮鸿灿和汤伟雄主持，以安良醒狮团的舞狮贺岁拉开序幕。三头彩狮在锣鼓声中进场，时而翻腾，时而跳跃，时而高高站立，还抛出寓意吉祥的生菜和橘子，向来宾们送上祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14706961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706961" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706961-DSC07929.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706961-DSC07929-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706961" class="wp-caption-text">来自罗德岛的安良会员参加波士顿春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706967" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706967-DSC08010.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706967-DSC08010-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706967" class="wp-caption-text">会长黄展唐欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706972-DSC08035.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706972-DSC08035-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706972" class="wp-caption-text">会长伍伟业感谢大家支持安良春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿安良工商会会长黄展唐感慨，“一年之计在于春”，这是新一年发展最重要的时刻。他欣喜于安良工商会的会务蒸蒸日上，与社区的关系日益良好；也期待与另一位主席伍伟业及团队一起，为社区带来更好的服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14706976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706976" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706976-DSC08082.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706976-DSC08082-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706976" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿安良工商会职员及外埠代表向来宾敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706977-DSC08087.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706977-DSC08087-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706977" class="wp-caption-text">侨社嘉宾们合唱“朋友”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706974-DSC08066.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706974-DSC08066-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706974" class="wp-caption-text">全美安良工商会元老陈仕维献唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏夫妇、组长黄耀良、侨教中心主任高家富都参加了晚宴。廖朝宏首先向两位侨务委员郭竞儒、及同时担任全美安良工商会元老的陈仕维致意。他先用粤语祝福大家“马到成功，一马当先”，随即感谢安良对中华民国台湾及办事处的支持，期待双方继续维持良好关系。</p>
<p>纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉称赞，安良工商会走过了133年历史，一路以来帮扶华侨。他说，过去一年，安良与侨社各团体合作举办多场活动，也就城市分区议题积极参与市政府的讨论，为此特向安良会员们表达感谢与敬意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14706968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706968-DSC08013.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706968-DSC08013-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706968" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏感谢安良支持中华民国台湾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706969-DSC08014.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706969-DSC08014-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706969" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉称赞安良工商会的悠久历史。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><strong>融入主流 热心公益</strong></h2>
<p>参与晚宴的嘉宾，还包括两位波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）和墨菲（Erin Murphy），麻州参议员柯林斯（Nick Collins），众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）等等。安良工商会也特别宴请波士顿警察局A-1分区的警员，感谢他们保护华埠社区的安全。</p>
<p>波士顿市和麻州议员们分别向安良工商会颁赠褒奖，感谢会员们传承华人文化，促进社区团结，支持商业发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14706970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706970" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706970-DSC08031.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14706970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706970-DSC08031-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706970" class="wp-caption-text">麻州参议员Nick Collins颁赠褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>晚宴期间，安良工商会还向5个公益组织捐赠红包，包括中华耆英会、中华广教学校、侨立中文学校、波士顿青年服务组织（Boston YES），以及中华颐养院。</p>
<p>当晚还有抽奖环节，送出小南阁、帝苑酒楼、唐朝酒家的礼券，还有安良捐赠的10份现金红包。唱歌表演由谭达儿主持，陈仕维、余宝爱等资深侨领纷纷献唱，现场掌声和欢呼不断，非常热闹。◇</p>
