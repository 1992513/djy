<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Thingz Gifts将关闭多家门店</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月26日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者正玉澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">西澳家居用品连锁店企业</span><span lang="en-AU">Thingz Gifts</span><span lang="zh-TW">日前发布公告，他们将在</span><span lang="en-AU">2027</span><span lang="zh-TW">年关闭大部分门店。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《今日珀斯》报导，</span><span lang="en-US">Thingz Gifts</span><span lang="zh-TW">于</span><span lang="en-US">1975</span><span lang="zh-TW">年开设首家门店，他们主打销售“时尚潮流且价格亲民”的家居用品。</span></p>
<p>在经历了半个世纪的成功经营后，该品牌即将落幕。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Thingz Gifts</span><span lang="zh-TW">创始人兼董事雪力（</span><span lang="en-US">Rob Shirley</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，上述决定是基于生活成本危机影响了消费者的购物方式。</span></p>
<p>他说：“许多人的可支配收入正面临压力。”</p>
<p>“虽然顾客仍会购买日常必需品，但随着时间推移，装饰类礼品的销售变得越来越困难。”</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据了解，此次关店将涉及其在西澳</span><span lang="en-US">15</span><span lang="zh-TW">家门店中的</span><span lang="en-US">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">家。雪力说，到</span><span lang="en-US">2027</span><span lang="zh-TW">年，西澳境内将仅剩</span><span lang="en-US">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">家门店继续营业。</span></p>
<p>据称<span lang="en-US">Thingz Gift</span>的姐妹品牌<span lang="en-US">Living Emporium</span>也将面临关店风险，位于<span lang="en-US">Albany</span>、<span lang="en-US">Geraldton</span>、<span lang="en-US">Joondalup</span>、<span lang="en-US">Kalgoorlie</span>、<span lang="en-US">Mandurah</span>和<span lang="en-US">High Wycombe</span>的门店均受到影响。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在宣布关店的信函中，公司通知顾客，</span><span lang="en-US">Thingz</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US">Living Emporium</span><span lang="zh-TW">门店将不再销售礼品卡。</span></p>
<p>公司鼓励顾客尽快使用现有礼品卡。</p>
<p>这两个品牌均由<span lang="en-US">StarRetailGroup</span>管理，该集团同时拥有西澳零售店<span lang="en-US">RedDot</span>。</p>
<p>雪力确认，<span lang="en-US">RedDot</span>不会关闭任何门店，相反，该连锁品牌计划在<span lang="en-US">2026</span>年前再新开<span lang="en-US">4</span>家门店。</p>
