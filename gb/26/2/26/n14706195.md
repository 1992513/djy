<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>七十岁张茹真老师 从体育场到竹编桌的第二人生</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706482-832064_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>完成后，她提起竹篮展示成果，脸上露出满足笑容。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月26日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）七十岁张茹真老师 从体育场到竹编桌的第二人生七十岁的云林县东南国中退休教师张茹真，走路有风，说话爽朗。认识她的人，多半记得她在操场上带队跑步的样子。</p>
<p>她当了四十多年的体育老师，退休后却坐在小板凳上，一刀一刀劈竹，一针一线缝布，过着另一种节奏的生活。她说，自己其实闲不下来。退休后报名社区大学的课程“学裁缝”，以前也接触过拼布、竹编。</p>
<p>起初只是好奇，没想到越做越有兴趣。布料从市场挑回来，慢慢车成衣服、包包；竹子从竹管开始处理，削成细篾，编成篮子。她笑说，运动教人流汗，手作让人沉住气。</p>
<div class="mceTemp">完成后，她提起竹篮展示成果，脸上露出满足笑容。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14706483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706483" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706483-832069_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706483-832069_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706483" class="wp-caption-text">双手细细调整竹篾交错的位置，篮口的每一寸，都要对齐、压紧。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706484" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706484-832070_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706484-832070_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="770" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706484" class="wp-caption-text">坐在书柜前，她低头专注收边，竹篮在膝上慢慢成形。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706485" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706485-832065_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706485-832065_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="756" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706485" class="wp-caption-text">近看篮内人字纹编法，纹理紧密，线条整齐，是多年功夫累积。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>车祸之后 把竹编当复健</h2>
<p>几年前的一场车祸，改变了她的生活节奏。左手粉碎性骨折，医师一度担心复原情况。对一个习惯活动的人来说，手不能动，比什么都难受。她没有放弃，反而把竹编当成复健。“劈竹最难，手要稳。”她说。</p>
<p>从整枝竹子开始，先劈开，再修整厚薄，接着倒角，让竹篾边缘平滑不刮手。这些步骤，都得用力，也得耐心。左手不灵活，她就慢慢来，一次做一点。医师后来看到她的恢复情况，都觉得不可思议。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14706486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706486" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706486-832072_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706486-832072_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="617" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706486" class="wp-caption-text">戴上手套劈竹取篾，从原竹开始处理，一条龙完成整只竹篮。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706487" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706487-832058_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706487-832058_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706487" class="wp-caption-text">近拍分篾过程，竹条在刀口下慢慢展开，准备成为篮身材料。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706488" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706488-832067_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706488-832067_medium.webp" alt="" width="453" b="762" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706488" class="wp-caption-text">她拿起喷水瓶润湿竹篾，让材料更柔软，方便定型。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一条龙完成的竹篮功夫</h2>
<p>现在的她，从劈竹到收编，一条龙自己来。竹篾在她手中交错排列，底部先定型，四周慢慢立起，最后收边。熟练的时候，一个晚上就能完成一只竹篮。篮子不花俏，但结实耐用。有时她还会变化花样，让图纹在篮身自然浮现。</p>
<p>她做的竹篮，有人拿去装水果，有人当茶具收纳。她不急着卖，反而常送给朋友。“自己做得开心，比卖多少重要。”她说这话时，很笃定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14706489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706489" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706489-832066_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706489-832066_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="552" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706489" class="wp-caption-text">编纹整齐细密，竹色温润，呈现自然质感。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706490" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706490-832061_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706490-832061_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706490" class="wp-caption-text">完成的竹篮外观端正，提把圆润，从劈竹到成品，全由她一手完成。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706491" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706491-832060_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706491-832060_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706491" class="wp-caption-text">篮底以交叉竹片加强支撑，结构扎实耐重。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>换个场地 继续努力</h2>
<p>体育老师的底子，让她做事有纪律。每天安排时间骑脚踏车到市场买菜，运动运动，也安排时间做手作。她说，手作不是打发时间，而是让日子有重心。</p>
<p>车祸那段时间，她更深刻体会到，身体会老，但心不能停。张老师不觉得自己有多厉害。她只是把日子过好，把手上的事情做好。从操场到竹编桌，看似华丽转身，其实只是换个场地继续努力。</p>
<p>七十岁的她，左手已能灵活地拿刀、拿针、握竹篾。她说，复健没有捷径，就是天天做。竹编一条一条交错，也像她的人生，一步一步走过来。没有夸张的故事，只有踏实的手劲。</p>
<p>中午时分，阳光落在工作桌上，她低头编著篮子。那双手，曾经带学生跑过无数圈操场，如今在竹篾之间来回穿梭。不同的舞台，一样的认真。</p>
