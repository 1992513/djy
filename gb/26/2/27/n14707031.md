<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>眷村联谊会庆祝马年聚会暨新会长选举</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707026-IMG_3308-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年02月27日讯】（大纪元记者毕倩亚特兰大报导）马年伊始，人气爆棚的眷村联谊会庆祝马年聚会暨新会长选举活动，于2月25日（星期三）中午十二时，于Stone Age Korean Steak house圆满举行。眷村联谊会会长叶竣图率同各位热心相助的朋友们约100余人到场庆祝。热热闹闹地度过了马年开年的第一次聚会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707020" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707020-IMG_3299-e1772155084144.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14707020" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707020-IMG_3299-e1772155084144.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707020" class="wp-caption-text">老友欢聚一堂（毕倩/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当日的活动联谊会包下餐厅，为大家提供各式韩式烤肉、小菜。前台在几位义工大姐一致的默契配合之下, 登记、收餐费、拿抽奖号码样样井井有条。</p>
<p>餐聚开始，会长叶俊图主持餐聚，他感谢大家一直以来的支持、鼓励。他表示，会员们有钱出钱、有力出力。在联谊会没有经费的情况下，会员们全力配合捐钱、捐礼物，让整场活动充满活力及欢乐。大家现场每桌好友亲朋围绕着升起的炭火、品尝美味的各式烧烤，笑声、掌声充满整个餐厅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707025" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707025-IMG_3293-e1772154133870.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14707025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707025-IMG_3293-e1772154133870.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707025" class="wp-caption-text">前台义工服务的大姐姐们 （毕倩/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次活动嘉宾有亚特兰大华侨文教中心阎树荣主任、现任侨务委员陆复泰委员、眷村联谊会总顾问潘兰生、前会长袁丹青、荣光会长张世杰等，他们皆热情地融入整个活动。除了会长叶浚图及以上提及嘉宾大方捐赠抽奖礼品和现金外，吕志泉委员、王祥瑞委员虽未到场但也均为当天活动捐款。现场压轴幸运抽奖环节让大家的热情期盼指数进入另一波高潮。许多奖品、现金奖一一被幸运者拿到，大家其乐融融，共庆新年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707023" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707023-IMG_3310-e1772153969139.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14707023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707023-IMG_3310-e1772153969139.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707023" class="wp-caption-text">阎树荣主任当天抽得第一个奖品 （毕倩/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707029" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707029-IMG_3313-e1772154347478.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14707029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707029-IMG_3313-e1772154347478.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707029" class="wp-caption-text">月秋得到当天最高奖400元现金奖 （毕倩/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>值得一提的是，联谊会新会长在全体人员一致通过下，叶竣图会长再次当选，叶会长热心公益可谓众望所归。会长更于活动的尾声中再次感激大家热烈的投入，活动在期待下回再次齐聚一堂的祝愿中圆满结束。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707028" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707028-IMG_3311-e1772154282928.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14707028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707028-IMG_3311-e1772154282928.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707028" class="wp-caption-text">得奖者很开心 （毕倩/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
