<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一块布的快乐 平原社大校长何美慧的裁缝日常</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707222-832322_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>对何美慧来说，做衣服不是炫耀技术，而是一种生活方式。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月27日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）在云林西螺，提起何美慧，大家多半想到她的身份：西螺生态博物馆馆长，也是西螺平原社区大学校长。但若走进她的生活，你会发现，让她真正放松的，是一块布。</p>
<p>她国中时就开始玩布，她回家试着裁剪，照着简单的样式做了一条裙子。裙子不完美，却是她第一次穿上“自己做的衣服”。那份成就感，让她记了很多年。</p>
<p>现在西螺市场每周都有人卖布，因为台湾制衣产业没落，布料便宜又多样，让她开心不已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707207" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707207" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707207-832310_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707207-832310_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="787" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707207" class="wp-caption-text">替学员量腰围、调整尺寸。她不只自己做，也耐心陪着别人找到合身的比例。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707208" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707208-832309_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707208-832309_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="773" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707208" class="wp-caption-text">摊开纸样解说版型结构，指出腰身与剪接线的位置。好的衣服，从打版就决定了七成。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>在平原社区大学 重新找回手作的自己</h2>
<p>工作多年后，她在西螺平原社区大学开课，也重新当回学生。她学缠绕画、蓝染，也报名裁缝课。原本只是想多学一点，没想到越做越投入。</p>
<p>她说，裁缝最迷人的地方，是一个版型可以变出很多样子。改改领口、换块布料，就像是全新的设计。上衣、裙子、裤子、贝雷帽、围巾，她一样一样学，也一件一件完成。</p>
<p>一件上衣做好才一百多元，成本不高，却是完全属于自己的尺寸与样式。对她来说，比买名牌还开心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707209" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707209-832307_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707209-832307_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="713" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707209" class="wp-caption-text">在教室里摊开布料、整理版型，双手按压布面准备裁剪。从设计、剪裁到缝制，每一步都亲自完成。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707211" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707211-832316_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707211-832316_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="776" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707211" class="wp-caption-text">紫灰色上衣搭配格纹长裤，都是她亲手打版、缝制的作品。合身的剪裁，让她自在站在镜前。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<div class="mceTemp">
<figure id="attachment_14707212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707212" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707212-832314_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707212-832314_medium.webp" alt="" width="599" b="476" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707212" class="wp-caption-text">戴上自制贝雷帽合影，简单的线条与布料，成为她日常最自然的装扮。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707213" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707213-832317_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707213-832317_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="700" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707213" class="wp-caption-text">整体穿搭一气呵成，衣服线条简洁、比例俐落。她笑说，自己做衣服，最清楚身形需要什么。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>因为不好买衣服 所以自己做</h2>
<p>她坦白说，自己的身形不好买衣服。常常试穿很多件，都不够合身。与其勉强穿，不如自己打版修改。现在她量尺寸、画版、试穿、再调整。</p>
<p>衣服不一定华丽，但穿起来自在。她说，穿自己做的衣服出门，心情都不一样。做久了，她也不只做衣服，包包开始出现了。布料有时来自市场，有时是活动剩下的素材。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707214" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707214-832323_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707214-832323_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="863" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707214" class="wp-caption-text">平原社大活动用过的关东旗留下来，拆开、重组，变成提袋。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707215" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707215-832308_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707215-832308_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="762" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707215" class="wp-caption-text">坐在缝纫机前专心车缝，布料在针脚间前行。一针一线，都得自己掌握。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707216" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707216-832312_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707216-832312_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="646" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707216" class="wp-caption-text">在工作室持续缝制，桌上摆着半成品。从裁剪到完成，往往是一整天的投入。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>关东旗变身提袋</h2>
<p>最有意思的是，她把西螺生态博物馆，和平原社大活动用过的关东旗留下来，拆开、重组，变成提袋。<br />
原本只是一面宣传旗帜，现在成了可以天天背着走的作品。她觉得这样很好，既环保，也有纪念意义。布料不浪费，故事还能延续。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707217" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707217-832306_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707217-832306_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="544" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707217" class="wp-caption-text">特写粉色提袋上的“西螺老街 Folk Art”字样。活动布条回收再利用，让地方文化延续在生活中。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707218" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707218-832305_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707218-832305_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707218" class="wp-caption-text">手提牛仔布袋，袋面缤纷的缠绕画图样，是她亲手绘制再缝制而成。裁缝与创作，在布面上交会。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14706780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14706780" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706780-832304.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14706780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14706780-832304.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="634" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14706780" class="wp-caption-text">何美慧站在西螺老屋前，肩背以活动布旗改制的提袋。原本是宣传关东旗，如今成了实用又有故事的作品。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>从西螺到法国的分享</h2>
<p>有一次，她把自己做的衣服与包包带到国外交流，甚至到了法国，还教志工们做围裙和头巾。她没有刻意强调，只说那是一种分享。</p>
<p>在她心里，做衣服不是为了成名，而是因为喜欢。喜欢布料在手中的触感，喜欢缝纫机规律的声音，也喜欢完成后那一刻的安心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707219-832318_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707219-832318_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707219" class="wp-caption-text">走进国外料理教室，围裙下的红色上衣也是她的手作。边教边笑，把台湾味带进厨房。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707220" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707220-832319_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707220-832319_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707220" class="wp-caption-text">在厨房与学生一起备料、切菜。围裙底下的衣服，仍是自己打版缝制。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14707221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707221-832320_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707221-832320_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="475" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707221" class="wp-caption-text">与当地朋友合影留念。简单俐落的剪裁，穿在她身上格外自然。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>身份很多 快乐很单纯</h2>
<p>身为基金会董事长、博物馆馆长、社区大学校长，她的工作其实很忙。但只要有空，她还是会坐在缝纫机前。她也愿意把经验分享给学员。</p>
<p>她常说，不用一下子做到多专业，只要开始，就会慢慢找到乐趣。学会一个版型，就能做出很多变化；学会一门手艺，就多了一份底气。</p>
<p>对何美慧来说，做衣服不是炫耀技术，而是一种生活方式。从国中市场买布的小女孩，到今天带著作品走出西螺，她把一块块布，缝成衣服，也缝成自己的自在与自信。</p>
<p>她笑着说：“做衣服真的很开心。”那种开心，很真，也很踏实。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14707223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14707223" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707223-832315_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14707223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14707223-832315_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="713" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14707223" class="wp-caption-text">倚在门边望向远方。从一块布开始，她把兴趣做成生活，也做成分享给他人的力量。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
