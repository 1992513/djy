<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“奔马开春年货大街”贺年 青商会活力四射</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14701152-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>驻多伦多办事处处长梁毅鹏(后右二)、副组长王庆祯、文教中心蓝柏青主任(左二)、多伦多台湾青商专业协会会长陈彦宇(前右三)及其团队于“奔马开春年货大街”现场合影。(主办方提供)</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年02月19日讯】多伦多台湾青商专业协会（<span class="s2">TYPEA</span>）于<span class="s2"> 2 </span>月<span class="s2"> 14 </span>日、<span class="s2">15 </span>日在市中心<span class="s2"> College </span>地铁站旁的<span class="s2"> <b>Aura Concourse Shopping Mall</b> </span>举办首届“奔马开春年货大街”，为市中心带来浓浓的黄历新年气氛。活动两天吸引逾万名民众到场，展现台裔青年在文创与社区活动上的活力与创意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14701156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14701156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14701156" rel="attachment wp-att-14701156"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14701156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14701156-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14701156" class="wp-caption-text">多伦多台湾青商专业协会会长陈彦宇会长，首届亲善大使、侨务促进委员青年插画家梁一念及成员与蓝柏青主任合照 。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p2">驻多伦多台北经济文化办事处处长梁毅鹏与文教中心主任蓝柏青亲自到场支持。梁毅鹏表示，青商会是充满活力、理想、创意与动能的青年社团，不仅在加拿大努力发展事业，也积极推广台湾文化，令人感佩。他并向民众送上马年祝福，祝大家“马年马力充沛、马上成功、马上发财”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14701154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14701154" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14701154" rel="attachment wp-att-14701154"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14701154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14701154-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14701154" class="wp-caption-text">财神爷、台湾黑熊与电音三太子在年货大街出巡带来喜气。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p2">活动以热闹舞狮揭幕，财神爷、台湾黑熊与电音三太子接连亮相，吸引许多家庭与游客拍照留念。青商会也准备限量福袋，与不同族裔的市民分享台湾新年的喜气。文教中心则在现场推广“<span class="s2">i</span>侨卡”、<span class="s2">TCML </span>华语课程与侨委会社群平台，获得热烈回响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14701153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14701153" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14701153" rel="attachment wp-att-14701153"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14701153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14701153-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14701153" class="wp-caption-text">舞狮游街献瑞。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">两天的年货大街，共 <span class="s1">60 </span>余个展摊，汇聚了各式台湾小吃，从东山鸭头、豆花、车轮饼、花生冰淇淋到台式饭团、章鱼烧、鸡蛋仔等美食一应俱全。文创摊位同样亮眼，包括手工饰品、皮革作品、软陶公仔、永生花与新年限定商品，展现台裔青年的设计能量。多伦多首届亲善大使、插画家梁一念也带来《公恰孙的台语诗画》展览与春联体验，深受民众喜爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14701157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14701157" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14701157" rel="attachment wp-att-14701157"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14701157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14701157-6-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14701157" class="wp-caption-text">文教中心现场设置推广“i侨卡”摊位。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p2">青商会会长陈彦宇表示，希望把台北迪化街的年货氛围带到多伦多，让市民在采买年货的同时，也能感受台湾新年的热闹与温度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14701155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14701155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14701155" rel="attachment wp-att-14701155"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14701155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/02/id14701155-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14701155" class="wp-caption-text">驻多伦多办事处梁毅鹏处长 (右)与文教中心蓝柏青主任（左）舞狮采青。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
