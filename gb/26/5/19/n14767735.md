<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>松年2026春季结业典礼 艺文展演与中医智慧交织</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767739-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>哈里斯县第4区幕僚长李成纯如（Alice Lee）颁赠证书予陈美芬（右二）、许胜弘（右一）并邀房亭妤（左一）合影。（陆希／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年05月19日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）休士顿松年学院（HTISC）于5月16日上午在学院大厅隆重举行2026春季结业典礼。本次活动结集了“松年台湾华语文学习中心（TCML-HTISC）”的课程成果展示与松年各班级的精彩艺文表演，展现长者们充满活力的第二、第三人生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14767740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14767740" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767740-P02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14767740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767740-P02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14767740" class="wp-caption-text">副主任房亭妤在松年学院暨TCML-HTISC 2026春季结业典礼致词。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼在温馨的气氛中拉开序幕。松年学院董事长陈美芬首先向全体教授、老师、学员及董事致谢，肯定大家清晨出席的付出。致词中，她特别感念近日过世的台湾侨社开拓者吴文龙董事长及叶德云侨领，并对吴董事长过去全力支持学院购置现址的恩情表达永远的怀念。</p>
<p>随后，侨教中心副主任房亭妤致词，强调侨社会持续支持退休族群，并推广TCML在美弘扬华语与台湾文化的宗旨；哈里斯县第4区幕僚长李成纯如（Alice Lee）亦代表颁赠证书，高度肯定松年学院的社区贡献。美南旅馆公会会长严杰以及115年国庆主委洪良冰应邀出席并致词。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14767741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14767741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767741-P03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14767741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767741-P03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14767741" class="wp-caption-text">TCML学生与吴怡萱老师（左三）、黄灿琴主任（左二）合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14767742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14767742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767742-P04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14767742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767742-P04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14767742" class="wp-caption-text">松年合唱团在陈淑芬教授指挥下表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14767743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14767743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767743-P05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14767743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767743-P05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14767743" class="wp-caption-text">Kevin Griffe老师（右二）带领乌克丽丽班演出之后合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在成果表演环节，松年丰富的音乐资源惊艳全场。许胜弘院长笑称，学院拥有竖笛、钢琴、合唱团等多元课程，常被打趣建议转型为音乐学院。在吴怡萱老师与黄灿琴主任指导下，TCML学生现场进行中文名字书写考核与两分钟自我介绍，并温馨合唱《甜蜜的家庭》。接着，松年合唱团在陈淑芬教授指挥下，深情献唱希伯来文作品《Al Shlosha D&#8217;Varim》、流行乐《陪我看日出》以及融入布农族元素的《回家吧》，以朗诵与歌声抚慰人心；Kevin Griffe老师则带领乌克丽丽班弹唱《Jambalaya》等多首曲目，现场互动热烈。此外，手机运用班、Jayme Long教授的英文班、王丽纯老师的舞蹈班，以及刘根明大师率领的太极拳班也轮番展现学习成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14767744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14767744" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767744-P06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14767744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767744-P06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14767744" class="wp-caption-text">李元平中医师主题演讲“漫谈中医急救”。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14767745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14767745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767745-P07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14767745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767745-P07-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14767745" class="wp-caption-text">王丽纯老师舞蹈班表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天的压轴高潮为李元平中医师的主题演讲“漫谈中医急救”。李医师深入浅出地分享了传统中医急救方法，包括利用缝衣针进行双重消毒，或以糖尿病刺针在耳尖放血（3–4滴）来缓解高血压与昏倒症状。他强调，操作时必须同步拨打911求援，并当场示范了人中、合谷、内关及涌泉等常用穴位。李医师也特别提醒，在美国因法规与文化差异，下针风险较高，操作务必审慎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14767746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14767746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767746-P08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14767746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14767746-P08-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14767746" class="wp-caption-text">刘根明大师率领的太极拳班表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动最后，许胜弘院长以“享受晚年的自由生活（Enjoying the Freedom of Later Life）”为题为典礼画下完美句点，祝福所有学员在松年学院持续发光发热，活出精彩的银发岁月。</p>
