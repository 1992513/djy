<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】全地形王者  2026 Subaru Outback Touring XT</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754805-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 Subaru Outback Touring XT。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年05月02日讯】Subaru在2025年纽约国际车展上正式揭晓全新2026年式Subaru Outback，适逢Outback问世30周年，这款第七代车型持续创新，这次的强杆外型更胜从前！</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://duxu97yt14q37.cloudfront.net/U5vBNYYhu85f"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/hjpCp6I9byg?si=XzhEJZvHQt_fUOph" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>以往Outback是旅行车车型，来到2026年车款，Outback的车身更高，蜕变成为SUV，然而仍保持有实用的日常代步核心。外观设计更加强悍，车头造型更显宽阔，垂直排列的LED大灯强调了其全地形的机能性。前防撞器比前代更加直挺，进风口栏栅与车身同色饰条营造出粗犷视觉感，并且可连同引擎盖可向上揭起。车侧部分，Outback 保持了标志性的比例，但轮廓更趋方正。平坦的车顶线条等同于有更大的行李箱与后座乘客空间。C柱上的饰板跟车身同色、D柱设计也加宽了，强调了刚性与支撑力，并加上扰流装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754786-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754786-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754786" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Subaru Outback Touring XT。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14754788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754788" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754788-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754788-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_08-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754788" class="wp-caption-text">外观设计更加强悍。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新车车顶高度增加2英寸，侧倾幅度较前代降低了42%，此外，新Outback也加强了车顶与顶篷吸音效能，以及改良了车身与车顶架风阻，行驶风切音降低了10%。标准配备的车顶行李架具备800磅载重量与220磅动态酬载量，方便车主悬挂吊床或露营帐篷。来自WRX的双齿轮电子动力辅助转向齿条（Dual-pinion EPS），提供更直接、自然的转向手感。X-Mode越野模式的开关移至方向盘上，让驾驶者能在不移开视线的情况下切换地形模式。另外，车身离地高度维持为8.7 英寸， Wilderness版本则搭配17英寸轮圈与全地形胎，测试的Touring XT则配19英寸轮圈及轮胎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754789" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754789-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754789-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754789" class="wp-caption-text">尾灯更时尚。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14754792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754792-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_09.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754792-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754792" class="wp-caption-text">19英寸轮圈及轮胎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Subaru的“对称全时四驱系统”(Symmetrical AWD)，对汽车加速、减速、转向及车辆抓地力各方面监察，从而提供最佳动力予每一车轮，投入使用至今已有40年以上历史。EyeSight系统，在车内挡风玻璃中间后方，装有一对摄像头，可提供“预碰撞刹车”、“预碰撞动力管理”、“自适应巡航”及“偏离行车线警告”等功效，而且在美国则是标配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754793-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754793-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754793" class="wp-caption-text">2.4 升Boxer涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年款Outback有两款动力配置，一为2.5升Boxer自然吸气引擎，最大马力180匹，扭力178磅英尺；另一款是这周测试车使用的2.4 升Boxer涡轮增压引擎，最大马力260匹，扭力277磅英尺。两款引擎均搭配更新后的 Lineartronic CVT 无段变速系统，备有8速手动模式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754794-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_38.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754794-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_38-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754794" class="wp-caption-text">简洁、平静的设计线条。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内装方面，采用简洁、平静的设计线条，并大量使用环保材质。前台装有全新的12.1英寸资讯娱乐萤幕与12.3英寸虚拟仪表板。新萤幕搭载先进处理器，反应更灵敏且支援高度自定义。数位仪表板有“平静模式（Calm Mode）”以减少干扰，亦可切换至全萤幕地图或EyeSight专属模式。混合导航系统结合了云端与车载数据，确保在离线环境下也能快速存取相关地图。恒温系统移回萤幕下方，并保留了独立的实体按钮与旋钮，方便在寒冷天气穿戴手套时操作。使用了最新一代的EyeSight驾驶辅助系统，除了原有的防撞自动刹车、车道偏移警示外，还新增了多项功能，如紧急停止辅助（Emergency Stop Assist），在辅助系统时失去反应，系统会自动切换至路肩安全停车、启动警示灯、通报紧急救援并自动解锁车门。高速公路辅助驾驶（Highway Hands Free Assist），在指定高速公路上，支援最高时速85英里的自动驾驶功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754795-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754795-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754795" class="wp-caption-text">12.3英寸虚拟仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14754796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754796" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754796-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_37.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754796-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_37-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754796" class="wp-caption-text">座椅舒适。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车内车外都有不少隐藏与Subaru有关的图案，例如引擎室内的“Since 1966”，又或者尾闸扰流底的四车图案等，非常有趣。另外，其他SUV行李箱大多采用回弹式软帘，但Outback却使用软布，需要用挂勾及磁石固定。而以往垂直的萤幕，新一代却换回普通的横置萤幕，当然更为好用，视觉上也更大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754797-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754797-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754797" class="wp-caption-text">12.1英寸资讯娱乐萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而，Subaru却想强调新Outback的高速公路辅助驾驶（Highway Hands Free Assist）功能，是一款可让驾驶者不用握着方向盘的自动驾驶，也可自动切线，但是当EyeSight“看不清”时，仍是需要驾驶者接替，驾驶者仍然不能完全放松下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754798-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754798-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754798" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>整体来说，Subaru Outback保持了Outback的水准之外，有更好看的外型，也有更为先进的技术。对于消费者来说，绝对是好事。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754799-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_39.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14754799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754799-2026_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_39-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754799" class="wp-caption-text">行李舱装上了胶垫。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
