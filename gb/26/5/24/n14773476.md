<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华夏文协庆亚裔传统月 邀移民分享逐梦历程</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773465-DSC02301-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月23日，摩顿市（Malden）华夏文化协会庆祝亚太裔传统月。主讲嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年05月25日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨摩顿市报导）5月23日，摩顿市（Malden）华夏文化协会（Chinese Culture Connection）举办亚太裔传统月庆祝活动，邀请4位亚洲移民，分享人生经历，表演各自的文化节目，吸引不少居民到场交流。</p>
<p>当天在麻州摩顿市老人中心，主讲嘉宾们各自分享了他们的家庭移民美国，克服困境，追寻梦想的故事。市长柯廷森（Gary Christenson）及多位市议员都到场，与主讲人和居民交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14773461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773461-DSC02272.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773461-DSC02272-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773461" class="wp-caption-text">摩顿市华夏文化协会庆祝亚太裔传统月。不少居民到场参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14773470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773470-DSC02370.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773470-DSC02370-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773470" class="wp-caption-text">活动在摩顿市老人中心举办。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>华裔及印度裔移民回顾人生</strong></h2>
<p>当天的分享嘉宾，包括来自香港的Natalie Ip，祖籍中国台山而出生地在古巴的Rick Wong，柬埔寨华侨后代Brian Lim，以及波士顿印度协会前会长Tanu Phoenix。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14773463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773463-DSC02293.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773463-DSC02293-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773463" class="wp-caption-text">主讲嘉宾们与观众交流。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14773464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773464-DSC02297.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773464-DSC02297-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773464" class="wp-caption-text">观众向主讲人提问。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Rick Wong简要介绍了先辈从中国到古巴，又到西班牙，最后移民美国的经历。“为什么古巴会有中国移民？”围绕这个问题，他从1860年代的中国移民潮，一直讲到1949年的逃难潮。在相当长一段时间，古巴有过全世界第二大的唐人街，仅次于美国旧金山。</p>
<p>他怀念道，自己的祖父母和父母都是非常特别的人，他们曾在一个国家失去一切，又在另一个国家重新建立生活，经历过许多磨难。在分享家庭故事之后，作为理疗师和武术老师的Rick Wong，还为观众表演了太极拳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14773468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773468" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773468-DSC02359.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773468-DSC02359-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773468" class="wp-caption-text">Rick Wong表演太极拳。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自印度的Tanu Phoenix将其人生历程比喻为“凤凰涅盘”。她怀揣建立美好生活的梦想来到美国，却遭遇婚姻变故，人生陷入黑暗。而在父母、女儿及亲友的支持下，她又走出困境，直至成为科技公司高管和公益组织负责人。</p>
<p>她将这些年的经历，概括为“从一个刚来美国的年轻新娘，到一个找到自己力量的女人。”在分享之后，Tanu Phoenix带来印度协会的女士们，表演轻快而有带有传统气息的舞蹈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14773462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773462-DSC02275.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773462-DSC02275-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773462" class="wp-caption-text">来自印度的Tanu Phoenix分享走出困境的历程。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14773466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773466" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773466-DSC02308.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773466-DSC02308-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773466" class="wp-caption-text">印度移民表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外两位主讲人也都有丰富多彩的经历。Natalie Ip是上届“美国小姐”（National American Miss）麻州赛区冠军。她盛装登台，谈及她突破按部就班工作的舒适区，参加选美比赛，开创更广大舞台的心路历程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14773459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773459-DSC02241.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773459-DSC02241-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773459" class="wp-caption-text">Natalie Ip分享参加选美比赛的经历。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Brian Lim的祖籍是中国汕头，父母先移民至柬埔寨，后又作为难民前往美国。他在美国出生，热爱街舞和功夫，曾获得多个舞蹈奖项，还两次担任凯尔特人篮球队（Celtics）的主场表演嘉宾。在分享故事之后，他和两名伙伴表演了充满热情的街舞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14773460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773460-DSC02271.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773460-DSC02271-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773460" class="wp-caption-text">Brian Lim分享他对街舞和功夫的热爱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14773467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773467" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773467-DSC02318.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773467-DSC02318-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773467" class="wp-caption-text">Brian Lim的伙伴们表演街舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>分享文化 宣扬贡献</strong></h2>
<p>华夏文化协会行政主任洪梅说，这场庆祝活动的目标，不仅是宣扬亚裔文化，也是彰显移民对美国社会的贡献。</p>
<p>“然而他们的贡献并不是总会被看见、被认可。通过我们这些尚未讲述的故事，我们希望彰显这些经常不被分享的旅程和经历。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14773458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773458" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773458-DSC02227.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773458-DSC02227-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773458" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾参与庆祝活动。左起：摩顿市议员Carey McDonald、华夏文化协会行政主任洪梅、摩顿市长Gary Christenson。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14773455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773455-DSC02220.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773455-DSC02220-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773455" class="wp-caption-text">活动现场展出香港特色。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14773456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773456-DSC02221.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773456-DSC02221-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773456" class="wp-caption-text">活动现场展出中国织物。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摩顿市长柯廷森称赞，嘉宾的分享确实彰显了亚太裔移民的成就。他还说，华夏文化协会为摩顿市带来积极的影响；他本人也从协会的活动中了解了不少语言、文化和故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14773457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773457-DSC02222.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773457-DSC02222-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773457" class="wp-caption-text">活动现场展出老电影和老照片。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14773469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14773469" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773469-DSC02366.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14773469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14773469-DSC02366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14773469" class="wp-caption-text">主办方提供诸多美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了嘉宾分享和文艺表演之外，主办方还准备了诸多亚洲美食，如尼泊尔饺子、越南春卷、港式西多士等等。现场还有抽奖环节，欢笑连连。◇</p>
