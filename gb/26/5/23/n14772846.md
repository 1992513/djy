<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】经典回归 油电新旅程 2026 Jeep Cherokee Overland</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772872-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2026 Jeep Cherokee Overland。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年05月23日讯】Cherokee是Jeep的老牌越野车，虽然没有Wrangler般的越野能力，但其历史也非常悠久，来到2026年，Jeep为Cherokee推山新一代的Cherokee，代号KM，取代上一代KL车款。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d3siwajeg0khti.cloudfront.net/U8uBSaJExJIp"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/aJEqubDfF7A?si=SonnFCeRkbOed-Jq" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>第6代Cherokee采用全新外观设计，从过去50年的经典款式中汲取灵感。新车比前代更长、更高、更宽，线条锐利且更加挺拔。前脸设计采用经典的七孔格栅，配上方正的LED头灯搭配U型日间行车灯，防撞器下方装有小小的雾灯。侧面轮廓方正，尾灯设计灵感来自“油桶”（Jerrican）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772850-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_07.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772850-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772850" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Jeep Cherokee Overland。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然新Cherokee外型没有给人惊喜，但测试车采用了珍珠银蓝色（Hydro Blue Pearl），亮眼程度很高，很好看。此外，新Cherokee装有免钥匙进入系统（Passive Entry），在门把手内侧按一下就可解锁，而门把手采用嵌入式，与身平顺嵌合，从最新的Cherokee，以及上周的BMW iX都是采用嵌入式门把手，相信车厂都务求减少风阻而减少使用外露设计。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772852-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772852-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772852" class="wp-caption-text">经典的七孔格栅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内装则融合了“活跃、玩味、实用、先进”四大主题。采用永续性材料，包括非皮革材料与回收材料制成的地毯。同时注意高科技，标配10.25英寸全彩数位仪表板，以及12.3英寸中控触控萤幕，运行Uconnect 5资讯娱乐系统，并支援无线Apple CarPlay与Android Auto，系统反应也很快，主页面可自订各Widget，创造属于自己的主页面，也可放入不同功能的捷径，所以也很实用。Energy Flow可看到即时使用的是引擎，还是马达，还可显示即时能量输出，唯一缺少是电池电力容量。仪表板也有多种不同设计可选购，方向盘呈八角形，转动有点怪，但握感不错，并装有三段加热。前座位也有三段加热及冷却功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772854-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_36.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772854-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_36-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772854" class="wp-caption-text">融合了“活跃、玩味、实用、先进”四大主题的内装。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14772855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772855" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772855-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772855-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772855" class="wp-caption-text">12.3英寸中控触控萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年Cherokee采用跟Grand Cherokee一样的变速盘，节省了不少位置，Grand Cherokee的变速盘档位有“P”、“R”、“N”及“D”，前方则是引擎启动键。后方是Selec-Terrain牵引力管理系统选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772856" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772856-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772856-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772856" class="wp-caption-text">变速盘。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026 Jeep Cherokee首次搭载全新的1.6升涡轮四缸油电混合动力系统。该系统预估综合油耗为37MPG，单缸汽油可行驶超过500英里，表现优于许多同级对手。这是Jeep品牌首款混合动力系统汽车，也是Stellantis在北美推出的首款，彰显了Stellantis的多元能源战略。Jeep Cherokee 提供了传奇的4&#215;4 能力与多项同级领先功能，其中一为Jeep Active Drive I 4&#215;4 系统，具备后轴分离功能以节省油耗。Selec-Terrain牵引力管理系统提供自动 (Auto)、运动 (Sport)、雪地 (Snow)、沙地/泥地 (Sand/Mud) 四种模式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772857" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772857-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772857-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772857" class="wp-caption-text">1.6升涡轮四缸油电混合动力系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新Jeep Cherokee也不省略安全设备，具有具备行人与单车辨识功能的自动紧急刹车、路口碰撞辅助（Intersection Collision Assist）、盲点侦测与后方横向车流警示及驾驶疲劳侦测。另外，在遇到有紧急车辆经过时，系统会在中控台萤幕作出提示，使驾驶者可预早让路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772858-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_25.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772858-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772858" class="wp-caption-text">10.25英寸全彩数位仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>整体而言，Cherokee在动力上转为Hybrid系统，对城市人来说非常适合，但越野能力就相对降低了，所以新一代Cherokee定位更为接近Crossover车型类别，也应更为受欢迎！测试车Cherokee Overland的售价为$44,995起跳。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772859-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_23.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772859-2026_Jeep_Cherokee_Overland_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772859" class="wp-caption-text">后行李舱容量非常大。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
