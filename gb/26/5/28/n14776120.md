<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳矿业重镇房价涨幅领跑全澳</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年05月28日讯】（大纪元记者林达澳洲珀斯编译报导）最新数据显示，西澳矿业重镇卡拉萨（<span lang="en-US">Karratha</span>）创下全澳房价最高年度涨幅。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在过去</span><span lang="en-US">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">个月中，卡拉萨房价激增</span><span lang="en-US">26.4%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，珀斯上涨</span><span lang="en-US">26%</span><span lang="zh-TW">、西澳金矿小镇卡尔古利</span><span lang="en-US">&#8211;</span><span lang="zh-TW">博尔德（</span><span lang="en-US">Kalgoorlie-Boulder</span><span lang="zh-TW">）上涨</span><span lang="en-US">23.1%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，班伯里（</span><span lang="en-US">Bunbury</span><span lang="zh-TW">）上涨</span><span lang="en-US">22.3%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，巴瑟尔顿（</span><span lang="en-US">Busselton</span><span lang="zh-TW">）上涨</span><span lang="en-US">22%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据房地产集团“</span><span lang="en-US">Domain</span><span lang="zh-TW">”报导，业内人士认为，卡拉萨价格上涨源于住房供应短缺以及投资者的重新入场。房地产中介</span><span lang="en-US">Realmark</span><span lang="zh-TW">负责人兼销售总监詹姆斯（</span><span lang="en-US">Jordan James</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，市场转折始于去年</span><span lang="en-US">11</span><span lang="zh-TW">月，并在今年</span><span lang="en-US">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">月达到顶峰。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“此前，约</span><span lang="en-US">90%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的房产由自住业主购买。但现在，自住业主与投资者的购房比例已接近各占一半。”詹姆斯表示，“在澳纽军团日后的三周内，卡拉萨就有超过</span><span lang="en-US">35</span><span lang="zh-TW">套房产挂牌，这是我从业八年来在如此短的时间内见过的最高供应量。”</span></p>
<p>目前，卡拉萨的房产中位数价格为<span lang="en-US">765,275</span>澳元。而珀斯中位价为<span lang="en-US">1,038,344</span>澳元，奥尔巴尼小镇（<span lang="en-US">Albany</span>）为<span lang="en-US">802,805</span>澳元。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">除了卡拉萨，西澳多个偏远小镇房价涨势同样亮眼。</span><span lang="en-US">CoreLogic</span><span lang="zh-TW">最新数据显示，在全澳</span><span lang="en-US">50</span><span lang="zh-TW">个最大的偏乡城市中，巴瑟尔顿（</span><span lang="en-US">Busselton</span><span lang="zh-TW">）于年初录得最强季度增幅，上涨</span><span lang="en-US">7.5%</span><span lang="zh-TW">；紧随其后的是奥尔巴尼（</span><span lang="en-US">Albany</span><span lang="zh-TW">），上涨</span><span lang="en-US">7.2%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，杰拉尔顿（</span><span lang="en-US">Geraldton</span><span lang="zh-TW">）上涨</span><span lang="en-US">6.8%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，班伯里（</span><span lang="en-US">Bunbury</span><span lang="zh-TW">）则上涨</span><span lang="en-US">5.8%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：杨新云</p>
