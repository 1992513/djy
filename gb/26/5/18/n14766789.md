<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>剑桥合唱团突破传统 以应景戏服秀歌剧风采</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766665-DSC02079-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>剑桥合唱团5月16日举办春季公演。在一个歌剧选段节目中，领唱者林泓安穿国王戏服登场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年05月18日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）波士顿地区成立最久的中文合唱团——剑桥合唱团5月16日举办春季公演，不仅像往年一样演唱经典歌曲，还为其中的歌剧选段节目搭配应景戏服，得到观众盛赞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14766661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766661-DSC02020.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766661-DSC02020-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766661" class="wp-caption-text">剑桥合唱团5月16日在MIT举办春季公演，献唱经典歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766658-DSC02005.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766658-DSC02005-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766658" class="wp-caption-text">团员们深情唱歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766659-DSC02008.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766659-DSC02008-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766659" class="wp-caption-text">团员们深情唱歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天下午在麻州剑桥市（Cambridge），麻省理工学院（MIT）克雷斯格剧场（Kresge Theatre），剑桥合唱团的30多位成员，由颜毓芬博士指挥，演绎他们排练几个月的歌曲。毕业于伯克利音乐学院的刘昕甯弹钢琴伴奏。</p>
<p>此外，合唱团还邀请了特别演出嘉宾，包括“Boba Swing”摇摆舞团，以及波士顿舞蹈老师Mona Reddad，为部分曲目伴舞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14766660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766660" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766660-DSC02016.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766660-DSC02016-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766660" class="wp-caption-text">刘昕甯弹钢琴伴奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766666-DSC02090.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766666-DSC02090-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766666" class="wp-caption-text">“Boba Swing”摇摆舞团伴舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766667-DSC02100.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766667-DSC02100-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766667" class="wp-caption-text">舞蹈老师Mona Reddad伴舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前3个节目都是华语乐团经典之作，包括“榕树”、“无言花”和“在银色月光下”；接下来，他们还演唱了6首歌剧选段，包括“A Million Dreams”、“For Good”、“You’ll Be Back”、“Razzle Dazzle”、“Hakuna Matata”，以及“One Day More”。</p>
<p>在观众的热烈掌声和“Encore”（再来一首）的呼声中，刚刚离台的团员们随即返回，载歌载舞，表演热情洋溢的安可曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14766662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766662-DSC02049.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766662-DSC02049-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766662" class="wp-caption-text">罗轩领唱“A Million Dreams”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766663-DSC02053.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766663-DSC02053-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766663" class="wp-caption-text">黄郁婷领唱“A Million Dreams”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766664-DSC02072.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766664-DSC02072-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766664" class="wp-caption-text">领唱者穿上特色戏服。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演出结束后，观众纷纷称赞今年节目中的独特戏服，以及穿戏服领唱者的演技。这些特色，为观众带来不少欢笑。</p>
<p>比如有观众称赞，在歌剧“Hamilton”选段“You’ll Be Back”中，林泓安穿着国王戏服出场，表现出国王“很跩”的感觉。</p>
<p>来自昆士市（Quincy）的池元山说，戏服和表演很形象，让他一下子就看明白了内涵。此外他还说，演出整体让他感受到“淡淡的哀愁”气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14766668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766668" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766668-DSC02126.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766668-DSC02126-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766668" class="wp-caption-text">团员们热情表演安可曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766669-DSC02130.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766669-DSC02130-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766669" class="wp-caption-text">观众为精彩演出鼓掌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766671-DSC02137.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766671-DSC02137-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766671" class="wp-caption-text">演出结束后，观众向表演者献花。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨教中心主任高家富也到场欣赏演出，随后向团员们表达赞赏。</p>
<p>剑桥合唱团团长陈韦同介绍说，每年团员们都会提出希望表演的曲目，每年都有人提议表演音乐剧，于是他们今年挑选了一些百老汇经典剧目的选段，为观众呈现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14766657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766657-DSC02002.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766657-DSC02002-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766657" class="wp-caption-text">剑桥合唱团团长陈韦同介绍节目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766672" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766672" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766672-DSC02142.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766672" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766672-DSC02142-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766672" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾莅临观赏演出。左起：钢琴演奏者刘昕甯、指挥颜毓芬、侨教中心主任高家富、团长陈韦同。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14766673" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766673" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766673-DSC02147.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766673" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766673-DSC02147-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766673" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿台湾影展协会成员向合唱团道贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈韦同说，很多人都看过这些音乐剧，各自对不同的曲目深有共鸣。分享这些经典作品，是团员们“一直以来的梦想”。他还说，今年的节目首次结合戏服，加入一点表演元素，希望给观众不一样的体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14766670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14766670" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766670-DSC02136.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14766670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14766670-DSC02136-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14766670" class="wp-caption-text">参与演出者合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>剑桥合唱团成立于1992年，一直是非营利，由志愿者运营的音乐团体。其宗旨是聚集不同背景的音乐爱好者，分享各个时代的华语歌曲。合唱团今年的招新将于9月初进行。陈韦同希望迎接更多喜爱音乐的人加入。◇</p>
