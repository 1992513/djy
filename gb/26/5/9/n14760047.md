<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>邓丽君逝世31周年 93岁日本经纪人赴台悼念</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14760056-20260508-Teresa-Teng-06.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
