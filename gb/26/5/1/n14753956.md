<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在风里开的花 二崙沐心荷园的母亲节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754137-845766_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>含苞待放的荷花挺立水面，孕育着下一刻的绽放。（廖显能提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年05月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬云林报导）母亲节前后，云林二崙的风还带着一点凉意。田边一处不显眼的角落，荷叶已经铺开，几朵花慢慢冒出头。这里叫“沐心荷园”，没有门票，也没有招牌，很多人却记得时间，一到五月，就会打电话来问：“开了没？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754138" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754138-845784_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754138-845784_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="598" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754138" class="wp-caption-text">粉红荷花在绿意中绽放，一抹柔色，静静点亮整片田野。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14754139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754139" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754139-845769_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754139-845769_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754139" class="wp-caption-text">盛开的粉红荷花在大片绿叶间绽放，细致花心如晨光般明亮。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14754140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754140" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754140-845763_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754140-845763_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754140" class="wp-caption-text">好友齐聚沐心荷园，在大树下留下难得的合影，笑容里都是岁月的温度。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一句建议 种出二十年</h2>
<p>二十年前，这块地还很普通。廖显能说，是一位住在台南白河的赖老师跟他提起：“这里可以种荷花，让人来看。”他听进去了，开始试着种。</p>
<p>一种，就种了二十年。他说荷花很“吃水”，水不够就长不好，所以每年都得顾水、耕锄。</p>
<p>土松了，根扎得深，梗就粗，花自然也开得大。叶子要是嫩绿的，表示状况好。</p>
<p>也不是没有遇过难关。有一年雨少，毛毛虫很多，整片园子差点撑不住，那一次，他才破例喷药。</p>
<p>之后又回到原来的方式，慢慢照顾。这些年，他没有把这里当景点经营，比较像一个习惯，一种生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754141" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754141" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754141-845767_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754141" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754141-845767_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754141" class="wp-caption-text">廖显能表示每年都会翻土，让花朵更娇嫩，叶更大。（廖显能提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14754142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754142" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754142-845765_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754142-845765_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754142" class="wp-caption-text">耕锄过后的田地，泥土翻新，为下一季的生长做准备。（廖显能提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14754143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754143" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754143-845777_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754143-845777_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754143" class="wp-caption-text">耕锄后的景象。（廖显能提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一个母亲 看了近二十年</h2>
<p>园子里的花，最早的观众之一，是他的母亲。廖显能的妈妈，活到一百零一岁，去年才离开。</p>
<p>她看着这片荷花，一年一年长大、开花，也陪着儿子走过这二十年。</p>
<p>有人说，种花的人是在等花开；但他心里清楚，其实也在陪人。</p>
<p>母亲节来临时，园子照样开花，只是少了一个熟悉的身影。</p>
<p>来看花的人变多了，心里却有一块安静下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754144" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754144-845774_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754144-845774_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754144" class="wp-caption-text">廖显能老师的全家福。（廖显能提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14754145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754145-845778_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754145-845778_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754145" class="wp-caption-text">今年花季刚开始而已。（廖显能提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>没有舞台的音乐会</h2>
<p>四月三十日，午后刚下过雨，地还湿着，风也不小。没有舞台、没有音响，几张椅子、一把吉他，人就聚在棚子下。</p>
<p>廖显能说，为了今年的荷花季，他自己写了一首歌，先唱原曲英文版、再唱自己改编的中文版，边弹边唱。</p>
<p>很多人其实不知道，他年轻时就是民歌高手，只是平常很少在大家面前表演。</p>
<p>这一次，为了这片园子，他开口唱了。歌声不大，但很稳。风把声音吹散一点，大家却听得很专心。</p>
<p>大同国小退休校长吴俊谕也到了。他没有多说话，拿起萨克斯风，吹起〈亲密爱人、女人花〉。</p>
<p>声音在风里转了一圈，落在荷叶上，也落在人群里。</p>
<p>没有掌声的起点，也没有结束的指令，但那一刻，大家都知道，这就是一场音乐会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754147" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754147-845779_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754147-845779_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754147" class="wp-caption-text">抱着吉他，笑着弹唱，为荷花园添上一段旋律。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>花开了 人就会来</h2>
<p>这些年，因为这片荷园，廖显能认识了很多朋友。有人从北部来，有人从南部来，每到五月，他的电话就响个不停。</p>
<p>有人问花况，有人只是想确认：“今年还在吗？”他笑说，花开了，人自然会来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14754150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14754150" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754150-845786_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14754150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14754150-845786_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14754150" class="wp-caption-text">含苞与初开交错，生命的节奏，在叶间悄悄推进。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>母亲节的意义</h2>
<p>母亲节对很多人来说，是一顿饭、一束花；对廖显能来说，是一片年年如期绽放的荷花。</p>
<p>花不是为了节日开的，但人会在这个时候特别想起一些人。那位看了近二十年荷花的母亲，今年不在了。但花还在，水还在，风也一样。</p>
<p>有人弹吉他，有人吹萨克斯风，有人只是静静坐着。</p>
<p>在二崙的田边，这片荷花园，没有太多安排，却刚刚好，像一种记得。</p>
<p>5月17日上午将有来自彰化的国乐团，前来赏花并做快闪演出，廖显能期待爱花者一起来共享美好时光。</p>
