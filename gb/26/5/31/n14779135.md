<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>休士顿台湾日登场 强势席卷Daikin赛场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779164-04-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾国旗在“2026休士顿台湾日”球场上向世界展现侨胞凝聚力及爱国心。（陆希／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年06月01日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）强劲的台湾旋风，再度刮向美国职棒大联盟（MLB）赛场！由大休斯顿地区四大侨团携手打造的第二届“2026休士顿台湾日（Houston Taiwan Day）”，于5月29日晚间在休斯顿太空人队主场Daikin Park隆重登场。适逢亚洲传统周，现场吸引逾千名台湾侨胞与主流球迷共襄盛举，成功将台湾的棒球热情与传统文化推向国际体坛舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14779163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779163" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779163-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14779163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779163-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779163" class="wp-caption-text">主办单位及Uni-Girls在赛前之夜活动合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年休士顿台湾日由休士顿台青商会（TJCC）、休士顿台湾同乡会（TAA）、休士顿台湾商会（TCC）以及大专院校联合校友会（JCCAA）首度联合主办。延续去年的热烈回响，今年全美各地的“台湾日”热潮席卷而至。活动当天，老中青三代侨胞纷纷穿上印有“TAIWAN”字样的专属纪念T恤，自信地在镜头前向世界展现台湾的凝聚力与骄傲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14779160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779160" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779160-02.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14779160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779160-02-450x600.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779160" class="wp-caption-text">萧伊芳（右）和嘉宾，开球之后与太空人队吉祥物欧比特（Orbit）合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚比赛由休斯顿太空人队（Houston Astros）主场对阵密尔瓦基酿酒人（Milwaukee Brewers）。开赛前，主办单位特别邀请驻休经文处处长萧伊芳担任开球嘉宾。萧处长在数万名观众的欢呼声中登板，投出象征台美坚实友谊与运动精神的关键一球，为这场体育文化盛事揭开序幕，也显着提升了台湾在国际上的能见度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14779165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779165-05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14779165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779165-05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779165" class="wp-caption-text">台湾侨胞热情参与。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14779166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779166-06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14779166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779166-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779166" class="wp-caption-text">侨务咨询委员杨朝谆。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14779168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779168-08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14779168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779168-08-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779168" class="wp-caption-text">世华名誉总会长黎淑瑛三代同堂看球赛。 （陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了精彩球赛，最令球迷沸腾的莫过于两大“台味十足”的表演。台湾中华职棒统一7-ELEVEN狮的超人气啦啦队“Uni-Girls”派出8位队员亲赴现场，不仅在赛前举办拍照及签名见面会与球迷热烈互动，更在球场上带来正宗的台式应援。配合着《登峰造极》与《统一尚勇》等经典应援曲，强烈节奏带动全场气氛。此外，由FASCA学员组成的巨型舞龙团也震撼登场，传统文化与热血节奏完美结合，让现场数千名球迷留下深刻印象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14779159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779159-01.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14779159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779159-01-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779159" class="wp-caption-text">当晚比赛台将邓恺威登板。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>值得骄傲的是，当天同时也是旅美台湾投手邓恺威先发登板的重要日子。休斯顿太空人队先前正式宣布邓恺威进入球队开季26人名单，这不仅是继2023年张育成之后，再次有台湾选手于大联盟开季出发，更是自2019年陈伟殷以来，相隔七年首度有台湾投手获此殊荣，为台湾棒球史写下崭新的一页，也为球队增添了话题性与即战力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14779167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779167-07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14779167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779167-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779167" class="wp-caption-text">休斯顿太空人队主场Daikin Park球场一景。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14779169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779169-09.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14779169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779169-09-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779169" class="wp-caption-text">球场上“TAIWAN”字样纪念T恤十分亮眼。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台式应援的呐喊声贯穿全场，虽然当晚太空人队最终以4比5惜败对手，但全场台湾侨胞的热情丝毫未减，球赛结束后，夜空中绽放出璀璨夺目的烟火秀，为主办社团与全体侨胞共同打造的“台湾之夜”画下了最完美的句点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14779170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14779170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779170-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14779170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/06/id14779170-10-600x490.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="490" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14779170" class="wp-caption-text">支持太空人球队30年的新世纪保险公司董事长陈舜哲和台商会张园立、张硕仁赛前之夜合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场盛会不仅凝聚了美南侨界的情感，更让台湾的声音在国际舞台上嘹亮回荡。</p>
