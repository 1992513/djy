<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>文协展望70周年庆 国乐团演绎“丝竹流年”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778446-DSC02401-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月29日晚上，大波士顿中华文化协会（GBCCA）国乐团在勒星顿镇卡里纪念堂举办年度演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年05月31日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨勒星顿镇报导）大波士顿中华文化协会（GBCCA）国乐团5月29日在麻州勒星顿镇（Lexington）举办年度演出，以现代编曲，展现传统丝竹的细腻与辉煌。来宾们也展望GBCCA今年迎来的70周年庆典。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14778451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778451-DSC02428.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778451-DSC02428-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778451" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日晚上，文协国乐团在勒星顿镇卡里纪念堂举办年度演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14778447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778447" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778447-DSC02408.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778447-DSC02408-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778447" class="wp-caption-text">二胡小组热情演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>新老团员同台演出 精彩乐曲感染观众</strong></h2>
<p>当晚在勒星顿镇卡里纪念堂（Isaac Harris Cary Memorial Building），文协国乐团的约30位成员盛装登台，演奏12首乐曲。乐队中有中国传统的二胡、琵琶、古筝，也有西方的大提琴、长笛等乐器。</p>
<p>上半场演出，以充满热情的“水乡欢歌”开场，以轻快响亮的乐曲，展现江南庙会的热闹场面。接下来，他们还演奏了“小河淌水”、“东北摇篮曲”、“采茶歌”和“丝竹新韵”这些风格各异的音乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14778449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778449-DSC02417.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778449-DSC02417-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778449" class="wp-caption-text">团员们认真演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14778450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778450-DSC02422.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778450-DSC02422-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778450" class="wp-caption-text">团员们认真演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下半场节目，以威猛刚强的大鼓乐“牛斗虎”开始，同样十分热闹。接下来的节目，有情感各异的“采红菱”、“春郊试马”、“小镇”、“雪莲花”，以及压轴节目，描绘台湾社会数十年发展的“台湾追想曲”。最后，在观众经久不息的掌声中，艺术总监及指挥家陈志新刚离场即返回，带领乐团再演奏一首安可曲。</p>
<p>“本季音乐会以‘丝竹流年’为题，融合传统丝竹的细腻与现代编制的大气，展现音乐在岁月流转中的传承与新生。”团长杨信宜在节目册中说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14778448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778448-DSC02413.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778448-DSC02413-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778448" class="wp-caption-text">团长杨信宜（中）演奏古筝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14778452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778452-DSC02440.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778452-DSC02440-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778452" class="wp-caption-text">下半场演出以大鼓乐开场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>关于这场演出的选曲，陈志新说，大家都希望演奏一些近年来还没试过的新曲目，同时也会考虑哪些曲目比较适合中小型乐团演奏。一些乐团成员也会提出希望演奏的曲目，例如他们讨论“台湾追想曲”很多年了，直到今年才得以演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14778454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778454-DSC02474.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778454-DSC02474-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778454" class="wp-caption-text">艺术总监陈志新指挥乐队。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14778453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778453" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778453-DSC02447.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778453-DSC02447-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778453" class="wp-caption-text">演出结束，乐队谢幕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在舞台上，陈志新还特别介绍了团员刘健。后者1991年加入文协国乐团，2022年前后搬到了加州，但今年特地回来参与演出。</p>
<p>刘健受访时笑言，这次行程可谓是“一时兴起”。他近两周都在加州家中，一边听乐团演奏的录音，一边跟随演奏排练。他5月28日来到麻州，到演出当天才参与乐团彩排，最终与大家协调配合，奉献了优美乐曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14778455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778455-DSC02483.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778455-DSC02483-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778455" class="wp-caption-text">资深团员刘健特地从加州赶来参与演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14778456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778456-DSC02493.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778456-DSC02493-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778456" class="wp-caption-text">观众向乐队成员献花。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>文协筹备庆祝70周年</strong></h2>
<p>在开场致词中，大波士顿中华文化协会会长吴德惠提到，文协今年迎来70周年，将于9月举办庆典，欢迎大家共襄盛举。</p>
<p>她说，在这个庞大的协会中，成立42年的国乐团无疑是最受欢迎的团体。不论是成年人还是青少年团员，都非常热爱经典音乐，在新英格兰地区各项活动中分享这些传统文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14778907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778907" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778907-DSC02392.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778907-DSC02392-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778907" class="wp-caption-text">演出之前，文协会长吴德惠（右）、前青少年国乐团团长欧阳东美（左）为刚毕业的高中生团员颁奖 。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14778909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778909-DSC02502.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778909-DSC02502-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778909" class="wp-caption-text">演出成员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14778457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14778457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778457-DSC02509.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14778457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14778457-DSC02509-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14778457" class="wp-caption-text">艺术总监陈志新（右）和夫人及团长杨信宜合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴德惠也谈到，团员们几乎每个周六都在排练，这样的坚持，令她感动。“非常美好的是，可以看到这个乐团不仅仅演奏优美的音乐和分享我们的文化，还一起欢笑、娱乐和成长，成为彼此的朋友、老师和引导者。”她说。◇</p>
