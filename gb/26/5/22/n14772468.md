<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>传承忠义仁勇精神 休龙冈亲义公所新旧会长交接</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772472-P01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>休士顿龙冈亲义公所2026年新旧主席交接由张海主席（中）将印信交给新任主席赵婉儿（左），由法律顾问关振鹏（右）主持监交。（图/Christi Mei提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2026年05月23日讯】（大纪元讯）休士顿龙冈亲义公所于5月17日（周日）下午1时，在Fu Fu Restaurant餐厅隆重举行2026年度新旧任会长交接典礼。当天冠盖云集，包括经文处副组长李俊毅、侨教中心主任王盈蓉，以及该公所元老、顾问、（刘、关、张、赵四姓）宗亲兄弟姐妹、伍氏公所代表与“龙冈之友”等80余位贵宾齐聚一堂。午餐会在2025年会长张海致欢迎词，并由关振业进行餐前祈祷后正式拉开序幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772473-P02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772473-P02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772473" class="wp-caption-text">休士顿龙冈亲义公所2026提名官员登台。（图/Christi Mei提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼中，副组长李俊毅与主任王盈蓉分别上台致词。他们对龙冈亲义公所多年来在侨社的卓越贡献与服务给予高度肯定，并一致祝愿公所未来会务蒸蒸日上，全体会员身体健康、万事如意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772474-P03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772474-P03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772474" class="wp-caption-text">休士顿龙冈亲义公所2026年官员在关振鹏法律顾问主持下宣誓就职。（图/Christi Mei提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后，交接仪式在该会法律顾问关振鹏的主持与监交下正式展开。关律师首先带领2026年获得核准的提名官员集体宣誓就职。紧接着，在庄严的气氛中，2025年主席张海将象征权力与责任的印信，正式移交给新任主席赵婉儿，顺利完成世代传承与交接。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772475-P04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14772475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772475-P04-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772475" class="wp-caption-text">新任龙冈亲义公所主席赵婉儿致词。（图/Christi Mei提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新任会长赵婉儿在发表就职演说时表示，能够接任2026年会长并为宗亲服务，她深感荣幸。她强调：“今天的交接不只是职务的转换，更是责任、传承，以及四姓‘忠义仁勇’精神的延续。”赵婉儿承诺，未来一年将全力以赴，与理监事团队同心协力推动会务，深化宗亲情谊，并期盼大家持续给予支持。她期许龙冈亲义公所能成为宗亲的精神家园，并在休斯顿社区中继续熠熠生辉、再创里程碑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14772476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772476" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772476-P05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772476-P05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772476" class="wp-caption-text">龙冈亲义公所及出席佳宾为龙冈会友关毓骏（中）90大寿庆祝，旁为寿星家人。（图/Christi Mei提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14772477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772477-P06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772477-P06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772477" class="wp-caption-text">龙冈亲义公所主席及贵宾合唱“龙冈之歌”。（图/Christi Mei提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14772478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14772478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772478-P07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14772478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14772478-P07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14772478" class="wp-caption-text">出席大会龙冈亲义公所成员及来宾。（图/Christi Mei提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天除了严谨的交接仪式，主办单位也特别为会友关毓骏举办90大寿庆祝活动，全场齐唱“生日快乐歌”，场面温馨感人。随后的卡拉OK演唱与幸运大抽奖更是将现场气氛推向最高潮。赵婉儿会长也特别代表公所，衷心感谢Fort Bend县长黄安祥（Daniel Wong）、经文处、侨教中心、伍氏公所及Peggy等人慷慨捐赠精美抽奖礼品。餐会在欢笑与热烈的掌声中圆满落幕。</p>
