<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>风很大 水很少 却还有人守着家</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759288-847231_medium-600x400.webp" />
<h6>东屿坪海域海水清澈见底，黑色玄武岩与蓝色海面形成强烈对比。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年05月08日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬澎湖报导）澎湖望安乡东屿坪，一座慢慢安静下来的小岛。退休返乡的吕先生说：“回来的人，不一定是为了生活。”</p>
<p>在东屿坪，没有便利商店，也没有热闹街道。</p>
<p>双吉福气客货轮靠岸后，游客只有一个多小时，可以慢慢走上山，看海、看石头屋、看海水底下的珊瑚。但真正留在岛上的人，比想像中少。</p>
<p>六十多岁的吕先生，退休后从外地回到家乡。他笑着说，回来也没什么特别的事，“钓鱼也好啦。”只是岛上的生活，不像游客看到的那么浪漫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14759289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759289" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759289-847237_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759289-847237_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="657" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759289" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪沙滩洁白安静，海面平稳，充满离岛气息。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14759290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759290" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759290-847243_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759290-847243_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="636" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759290" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪港边巨大黑色岩石矗立海面旁，形成特殊海港景观。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14759291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759291" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759291-847253_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759291-847253_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="645" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759291" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪坪屿宫色彩鲜艳、屋脊剪黏华丽，是岛上重要信仰中心。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14759292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759292" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759292-847259_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759292-847259_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="701" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759292" class="wp-caption-text">退休返乡的吕先生说：“每天海水淡化厂会给半吨的水，生活上非常不方便。”（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一天半吨水 是日常不是抱怨</h2>
<p>“淡化厂一天只给半吨水。”他说，有时连冲马桶都不够，只能重新使用早期先民挖的井水。</p>
<p>现在整个东屿坪，居民大约十来位，加上公务员、海巡、卫生室人员，大约三十人左右。</p>
<p>另一位年长奶奶则说，她从来没离开过这里，和先生靠捕鱼生活，一直到现在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14759293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759293" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759293-847236_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759293-847236_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="733" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759293" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪石墙与水井保留传统聚落生活痕迹，展现在地工法特色。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14759294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759294" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759294-847240_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759294-847240_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="465" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759294" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪游客中心外观简洁朴实，提供旅人短暂休憩与资讯服务。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14759295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759295" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759295-847242_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759295-847242_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="464" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759295" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪社区活动中心蓝白色建筑，在灰色天空下格外显眼。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>码头很安静 风声比较大</h2>
<p>码头很安静，灰色天空下，小船停靠在港边，海风吹过空荡的道路，让人感觉时间变得很慢。</p>
<p>船来的时候短暂热闹，船一走，又回到原本的安静。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14759298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759298" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759298-847247_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759298-847247_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759298" class="wp-caption-text">从东屿坪海岸远望西屿坪，海水呈现深浅不同的蓝色层次，美丽而宁静。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14759297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759297" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759297-847241_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759297-847241_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="654" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759297" class="wp-caption-text">双吉福气轮停靠东屿坪码头，载送旅客往返澎湖南方四岛。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>海底比陆地还热闹</h2>
<p>东屿坪属于澎湖南方四岛国家公园范围，附近海域因为玄武岩地形与黑潮支流经过，海底生态丰富，珊瑚覆盖率很高。</p>
<p>即使不下海，在岸边就能看到大片珊瑚群贴着港边生长，海水清到像透明玻璃。</p>
<p>现在也有不少浮潜业者带游客来体验海底世界，有人漂浮在海面上，慢慢看着脚下的珊瑚与鱼群。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14759300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759300" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759300-847257_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759300-847257_medium.webp" alt="" width="449" b="620" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759300" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪港边蓝色小船静静停泊，倒映在平静海面上，充满渔村气息。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14759299" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759299" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759299-847255_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759299" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759299-847255_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759299" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪海水清澈见底，岸边可见珊瑚与海底纹理，展现南方四岛海域生态之美。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14759116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759116" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759116-847230.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759116" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759116-847230.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="426" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759116" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪浮潜游客漂浮在清澈海面上，远方潮间带与海水层次分明，画面悠闲。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>石头屋还在 人却少了</h2>
<p>东屿坪另一个让人印象深刻的，是那些留下来的石头屋。</p>
<p>岛上的建筑，大多用玄武岩与珊瑚礁石堆砌而成。以前为了挡风，把房子盖得低矮厚实。</p>
<p>但后来居民外移，加上台风曾经掀走屋顶，很多房子只剩墙身。</p>
<p>现在会看到很特别的画面——路是新的，但房子是旧的，而且没有屋顶。</p>
<p>内院长满植物，像是时间慢慢接管这里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14759302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759302-847246_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759302-847246_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759302" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪传统石屋以礁石堆砌而成，历经海风与岁月，依然保留离岛聚落风貌。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>字还在 生活不在了</h2>
<p>有些门框上的字还清楚，红砖与石墙中间，还看得到过去人们生活留下的痕迹。那不是观光设计，而是真正留下来的时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14759305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14759305" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759305-847232_medium.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14759305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14759305-847232_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="796" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14759305" class="wp-caption-text">东屿坪老屋墙面仍可见“旭光”与老字样，红砖与石材充满岁月感。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>走上去 看整座岛的样子</h2>
<p>从港口一路往上走，可以走到东屿坪较高的位置。山不高，但风很强，视野很好。</p>
<p>可以看到整片海，还有岛的轮廓。有人看山边的叠石头，一步一步往山顶步道上走去，好像在帮自己留下什么愿望。</p>

<h2>留下来的人 比风还稳</h2>
<p>岛上还留着旧时的瞭望设施，静静面向大海。旁边没有太多人，只有风声与浪声。</p>
<p>双吉福气轮与其它客货船，现在仍会把游客送进来。大家下船、走一圈、拍照，再回去。</p>
<p>短短一个半小时，很多人只是经过。但对留下来的人来说，这里不是景点，而是他们一直住着的地方。</p>
