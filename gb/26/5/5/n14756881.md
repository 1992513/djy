<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>赖清德：台湾有权利走向世界 不因打压而退缩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14756296-2605040350142378.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
