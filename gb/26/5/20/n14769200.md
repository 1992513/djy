<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>赖清德执政两周年：维持现状是台湾战略目标</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14769390-260520001201100311.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
