<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国际华文教师协会携手侨校举办汉字嘉年华</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770488-2-e1779310440408-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蓝柏青主任、贵宾、加拿大国际华文教师协会成员、才艺演出学生大合照。（主办方提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2026年05月21日讯】加拿大国际华文教师协会于 5 月 16 日在多伦多侨教中心举办“正体汉字嘉年华暨侨校博览会”，结合 AI 科技与民俗艺文特色，汇聚加东 3 所慈济人文学校、维德中文学校、易学堂等侨校及侨教组织资源，共同推广正体汉字文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14770497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770497" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770497" rel="attachment wp-att-14770497"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770497-11-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770497" class="wp-caption-text">维德中文学校展摊以同学成品展现丰富多元的学习样貌。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="152" data-end="258">活动吸引多位侨界及教育界人士出席，包括驻多伦多办事处处长梁毅鹏、侨教中心主任蓝柏青、约克区教育局教育委员 Michael Chen，以及侨务委员简许邦、黄煌裕、林美玲，侨务咨询委员汤远芬、范玮与侨务顾问施善美等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14770489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770489" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770489" rel="attachment wp-att-14770489"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770489-3-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770489" class="wp-caption-text">梁毅鹏处长在象棋小学堂与小朋友对弈。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="260" data-end="375">梁毅鹏表示，很高兴国际华文教师协会再次举办正体汉字嘉年华活动。他指出，汉字不仅是中华文化的重要基础，更是人类珍贵的文明资产，现今古文明文字中，唯有汉字依然“可读、可看、可用、可理解”，期盼活动透过科技创新，进一步提升华文教育品质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14770490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770490" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770490" rel="attachment wp-att-14770490"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770490-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770490" class="wp-caption-text">蓝柏青主任(右四)、许彩凤副主任(左一)与北多伦多慈济人文学校TCML黄淑珍校长(左三)、TCML程安琪老师(后排左二)及储备老师何星烨(右二)等合照。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="377" data-end="523">蓝柏青则表示，首届活动已率先以 AI 形式展现创新，今年延续相关理念，感谢所有参与者的投入与付出。他指出，虽然安省教育界正面临许多变动与挑战，但仍期许各界携手推动华语文教育发展。此外，“台湾华语文学习中心”（TCML）第一期推行顺利，未来也将与主流大学合作，推出更多兼具实用与趣味的课程内容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14770487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770487" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770487" rel="attachment wp-att-14770487"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770487-1-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770487" class="wp-caption-text">梁毅鹏处长、蓝柏青主任代表侨委会致颁贺状予杨远龄理事长、感谢状予赵美然前理事长。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="525" data-end="577">活动中，梁毅鹏与蓝柏青并代表侨委会颁发贺状与感谢状予新任理事长杨远龄及前理事长赵美然，完成理事交接仪式。</p>
<p data-start="525" data-end="577">现场共设有 25 个摊位，包括机器人与 AI 跨界体验、手作教学、AI 互动游戏、书法体验、象棋小学堂、i 侨卡专区、画扇技艺、绘本阅读，以及台湾慈济素食与胖贡贡美食等，吸引众多家长与孩童驻足参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14770492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770492" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770492" rel="attachment wp-att-14770492"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770492-6-450x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770492" class="wp-caption-text">民俗文化摊位剪纸教做。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14770493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770493" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770493" rel="attachment wp-att-14770493"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770493-7_-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770493" class="wp-caption-text">民俗文化摊位捏面人。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="579" data-end="702">加东 3 所慈济人文学校也依据各校人文教育理念设摊，内容涵盖“静思语”、夏令营、TCML 课程及各式汉字游戏，让大小朋友在动脑挑战中感受文化学习乐趣。维德中文学校家长会长谢宏谟则介绍学校办学特色，希望透过侨校博览会促进交流、共享资源，并扩大招生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14770491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770491" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770491" rel="attachment wp-att-14770491"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770491-5-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770491" class="wp-caption-text">AI互动游戏。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14770494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770494" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770494" rel="attachment wp-att-14770494"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770494-8_-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770494" class="wp-caption-text">AI机器人体验区。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="805" data-end="857">舞台节目同样精彩，包括《茉莉花》扇舞、小提琴演奏、演讲、说故事等各校才艺表演轮番登场，展现学生学习成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14770495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770495" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770495" rel="attachment wp-att-14770495"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770495-9-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770495" class="wp-caption-text">才艺表演“茉莉花扇舞”。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="859" data-end="917">此外，TCML 成果展示区特别针对主流社会人士设计“台湾模式”商业与生活华语课程，也吸引不少民众询问并索取相关资料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14770496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14770496" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14770496" rel="attachment wp-att-14770496"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14770496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2026/05/id14770496-10-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14770496" class="wp-caption-text">拼字游戏。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="919" data-end="1011" data-is-last-node="" data-is-only-node="">理事长杨远龄表示，本次活动以“传承、创新、共融”为核心理念，强调语言与文化需要透过亲身体验学习，并结合 AI 科技作为辅助工具，希望学生能在寻宝活动与 AI 体验中激发创意、收获满满。</p>
