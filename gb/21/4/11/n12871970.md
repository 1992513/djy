<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>13种过期食物不能吃 引发健康问题</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871995-f82687266945b5e731f4c3dabc5816bc-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>有些食物过期后会滋生大量致病菌，引发健康问题。（ShutterStock／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月11日讯】（大纪元记者李怡雯编译报导）食物过期不仅影响其口感或外观，有些还会滋生大量致病菌，引发健康问题。以下列举的13种。</p>
<h2>1、豆芽</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871985" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871985-shutterstock_632023145.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871985-shutterstock_632023145-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871985" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>豆子发芽需要潮湿和温暖的环境，这种环境非常适合细菌生长。此外，大规模种植的豆芽在收获、运输或储存过程中也有被污染的可能。</p>
<p>豆芽中的细菌可能包括沙门氏菌和大肠杆菌等，还包括危险的EHEC（肠出血性大肠杆菌）变体。特别在种子萌发期，EHEC可以迅速生长到致病水平。</p>
<p>由于豆芽的这种特点，它在冰箱中最多只能保存两至三天。</p>
<p>烹饪豆芽时务必洗净并煮熟，高温可以杀灭细菌。</p>
<h2>2、绿叶蔬菜</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871987" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871987-shutterstock_796836577.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871987-shutterstock_796836577-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871987" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>超市卖的包装绿叶蔬菜，通常被标注为已经预清洗过。</p>
<p>但是，它们仍可能携带大肠杆菌等细菌。你在食用之前须将其洗净，最重要的是在有效期之前食用。</p>
<h2>3、调味酱</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871993-shutterstock_1905855622.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871993-shutterstock_1905855622-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871993" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>调味酱除了要留意保质期，还需注意开封调味酱的细菌滋生问题。</p>
<p>当勺子或刀反复浸入调味酱时，就有可能把细菌带入，使调味酱在到期之前就变质，即使调味酱开封后放入冰箱储存，也不能保证其不会滋生细菌。所以，开封后你需要时刻留意调味酱的气味、外观和口感，以判断其是否仍能食用。</p>
<h2>4、冷榨果汁</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871983-shutterstock_551531482.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871983-shutterstock_551531482-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871983" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新榨果汁与超市中加工果汁相比，有一个很大的不同：加工过的果汁经过巴氏杀菌，而原汁没有。经过巴氏杀菌的果汁，其保质期可以达到一个月左右，而新榨果汁只能保留几天。</p>
<h2>5、生乳制品</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871974-shutterstock_65467756.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871974-shutterstock_65467756-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871974" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>生乳制品，如奶酪，黄油，奶油和酪乳之类可能含有沙门氏菌，VTEC，李斯特菌，大肠杆菌和弯曲杆菌等有害细菌，且其未经巴氏消毒或任何其它热处理的方式消毒，当它们过期后，会滋生大量细菌而使人致病。</p>
<p>某些特定人群，如孕妇、婴幼儿、老年人和免疫力弱者，在食用这些生乳制品时应格外小心。</p>
<h2>6、新鲜肉</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871978" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871978-shutterstock_297774047.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871978-shutterstock_297774047-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871978" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新鲜肉、碎肉和熟肉的保质期也相对较短。</p>
<p>新鲜肉可能被沙门氏菌或大肠杆菌等污染。搅碎的肉被细菌污染的范围更大，因为在将肉切碎的过程中，会将肉表面细菌带入全部肉中。而熟食肉在新鲜切片过程中也存在细菌滋生问题，应尽快食用。熟食肉食用前再加热也有助于灭菌。</p>
<h2>7、鱼</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871986" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871986-shutterstock_777768646.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871986-shutterstock_777768646-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871986" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在超市购买鲜鱼后，应始终让鱼保持低温，包括从超市到家这段时间，将鱼放在保温袋中是个不错的主意。</p>
<p>由于鱼极易感染细菌，因此其存放的时间不应超过2天，如果需要更长的保质期，将鱼放入零下18度的冰冻室内，保质期可以有三个月到半年。</p>
<h2>8、牛肉糜</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871975" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871975-shutterstock_80936095.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871975-shutterstock_80936095-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871975" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过期的牛肉糜可能会滋生金黄色葡萄球菌或沙门氏菌，沙门氏菌可让人出现腹泻、发热、腹部绞痛症状，而感染金黄色葡萄球菌者，轻症会得皮肤病，重者可能会得肺炎或出现心脏瓣膜感染和骨感染。</p>
<h2>9、鸡肉</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871981" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871981-shutterstock_374862373.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871981-shutterstock_374862373-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871981" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过期鸡肉可能会被大肠杆菌污染，能引起多种疾病，如肠胃炎、脑膜炎和泌尿系统感染。加工前，你需留意包装上的过期日，看起来不错的鸡肉不一定在保质期内。</p>
<h2>10、预制沙拉</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871991" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871991-shutterstock_1783991306.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871991-shutterstock_1783991306-600x479.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="479" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871991" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>预制沙拉要在保质期前食用，在食用时才浇上酱汁，过期的沙拉会滋生细菌，同时蔬菜叶会迅速萎缩，变得无法食用。</p>
<h2>11、草莓</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871976" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871976-shutterstock_195118382.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871976-shutterstock_195118382-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871976" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>草莓保质期只有3天。新鲜草莓应存放在凉爽的地方。高温下，草莓保质期会变短。过期草莓有的会变干瘪，有的变软烂，容易成为细菌滋生的温床。</p>
<h2>12、蛤蜊</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871977-shutterstock_196013192.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871977-shutterstock_196013192-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871977" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>带壳的蛤蜊在冰箱中可保存3天左右，去壳的蛤蜊只能保存24小时。过了这段时间，蛤蜊将散发出强烈的腥臭味且不可食用。</p>
<h2>13、牡蛎（生蚝）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12871989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871989" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871989-shutterstock_1408680860.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12871989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871989-shutterstock_1408680860-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871989" class="wp-caption-text">（ShutterStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>牡蛎应煮熟再吃，因其可能携带创伤弧菌，该细菌会致人伤口感染，或让人出现原发性败血病。</p>
<p>常温下，牡蛎可以保存1-2天，如果放入冰箱，条件好的可保存最多1个星期。过期牡蛎会散发出难闻的气味，其携带的细菌更是大量繁殖，不可再食。</p>
