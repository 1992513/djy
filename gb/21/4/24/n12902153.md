<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黑洋装搭低跟鞋 轻松穿出法式优雅品味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902810-GettyImages-1287347069-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2020墨尔本时装周，轮廓优美、细节精致的黑洋装带来迷人、知性的氛围。（Quinn Rooney/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月26日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）法国女人那看似漫不经心，却总能散发优雅气质，那充满韵味的知性穿搭品味，是否让你心动？</p>
<p>比起遮盖身型的宽松衣物，法国女人更喜欢合身的剪裁。这里的“合身”并不是指贴身洋装那一类款式，而是布料与皮肤间保持着舒适距离的同时，又能顺着每个人的身材曲线，展现慵懒、随性却各具特色的风格。</p>
<p>趁着换季的时刻，挥别你常穿的紧身裤、亮片洋装与高跟鞋，换成锥形裤、黑洋装与低跟鞋款，再搭配西装外套、连身套装与包包，一展法式时尚的秘诀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12774357" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12774357" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1159677440.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12774357" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1159677440-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 穿搭, 巴黎" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12774357" class="wp-caption-text">卡萝琳?黛?麦格雷特（Caroline de Maigret）出席巴黎2020秋冬时装周。（Julien M. Hekimian/Getty Images for Chanel）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>锥形、直筒或老爷裤，取代紧身裤</h3>
<p>随着近年健身风气兴起，贴身、机能性极佳的运动紧身裤（Leggings）热卖，穿着范围也从健身房、爬山活动延伸至日常生活中，这自然也是现今潮流中的一环，但法国女人绝对不会如此穿出门。</p>
<p>锥形裤，是由臀部至脚踝逐渐收紧的裤种，既贴合腿型却又留有空间，活动方便，符合法国女人追求的悠闲与自在。这种剪裁能使双腿视觉上显得笔直，对下半身圆胖的女孩来说，也是显瘦救星。</p>
<p>除了全长、七分裤或九分裤的设计也相当常见，同时让你露出双腿中最细的部位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12807646" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-188009235.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12807646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-188009235-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12807646" class="wp-caption-text">2013 梅赛德斯奔驰时装周。（Andreas Rentz/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12902346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12902346" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902346-GettyImages-455173698.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12902346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902346-GettyImages-455173698-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 穿搭, 美国" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12902346" class="wp-caption-text">2015春夏梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周，美国品牌 Michael Kors。（Peter Michael Dills/Getty Images for Michael Kors）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>黑色或素色洋装取代浮夸、缀满亮片的迷你裙和紧身洋装</h3>
<p>比起追求表面上的光鲜亮丽，法国女人更注重衣物的剪裁与质感。</p>
<p>在一百件裙子中，艳丽缤纷的裙子或许能在瞬间抓住你的目光，但有着设计得当的裙摆轮廓与独特剪裁方式的裙子，才是真正耐看又令人惊艳的款式，若细水长流，再加上富有质感的布料及精致的细节，更能凸显穿搭者的品味与眼光。</p>
<p>法国女人因而钟情于黑色洋装，搭配简单的包包、鞋子就非常好看；亦或许戴上些许华丽元素，如别致优雅的珠宝、羽毛与流苏缀饰等，再化上素雅的妆容，便能散发成熟知性女人味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12902331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12902331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902331-GettyImages-1275826152.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12902331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902331-GettyImages-1275826152-450x676.jpg" alt="时装周, 穿搭, 时尚, 意大利" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12902331" class="wp-caption-text">米兰2021春夏时装周，意大利品牌 Maryling。（Pietro S. D&#8217;Aprano/Getty Images for Maryling）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12808333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12808333" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-188009336.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12808333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-188009336-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12808333" class="wp-caption-text">2013 梅赛德斯奔驰时装周。（Andreas Rentz/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>平底、低跟取代高跟鞋款</h3>
<p>法国有着相当多的石板步行街道，而法国人不分男女老少，普遍也习惯以步代车，因此鞋子是否好走耐穿相对更加重要。</p>
<p>相比西方国家、法国女人偏爱舒适、美丽的平底鞋款，像是乐福鞋、芭蕾舞鞋，甚至运动鞋都是她们衣柜中的必备款式，能够走上半小时、一小时都不会脚痛。</p>
<p>另外，如小猫垫起脚尖的猫跟鞋（Kitten heel）既可爱又时尚，在石板路上也较高跟鞋不易跌倒，同样广受法国女性欢迎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12902351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12902351" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902351-GettyImages-1309392538.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12902351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902351-GettyImages-1309392538-450x300.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 穿搭, 韩国" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12902351" class="wp-caption-text">首尔2021秋冬时装周，品牌PARTsPARTs。（Justin Shin/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12902356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12902356" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902356-GettyImages-1205859169.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12902356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902356-GettyImages-1205859169-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 穿搭, 美国" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12902356" class="wp-caption-text">纽约2020秋冬时装周，美国品牌Marc Jacobs。（Slaven Vlasic/Getty Images for Marc Jacobs）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>西装外套</h3>
<p>法国女人可说是将西装外套发挥得淋漓尽致，无论里面穿着衬衫、洋装甚至印花Ｔ恤，只要外搭西装外套，都能使整体造型上升一个层次，融合休闲与正式，在随性中自带昂首阔步的自信与优雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12807683" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-982655808.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12807683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-982655808-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12807683" class="wp-caption-text">巴黎2019春夏时装周，Officine Generale秀场。（Thierry Chesnot/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>连身套装搭凉鞋</h3>
<p>连身套装穿起来相当有气势，简单又高雅，搭配时省去上下身拆开的烦恼，只要注意包包与鞋子的选择，而在春夏季则推荐平底凉鞋或低跟凉鞋，减弱套装的拘束感同时多了一分清凉与活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12902321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12902321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902321-GettyImages-1275862909.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12902321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12902321-GettyImages-1275862909-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 穿搭, 意大利" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12902321" class="wp-caption-text">米兰2021春夏时装周，意大利品牌 Alberta Ferretti 秀场。（Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<h3>优雅、容量足够的包包</h3>
<p>包款一季一季的推陈出新，但法国女人不会盲目的追逐流行。</p>
<p>找寻一个万用好搭的色系是重点，务必要让包包能搭配衣柜中大部分的衣物。一个小巧精致的手拿包固然引人注目，但选择容量适中的包包才是真正实用；另外，若能放进水壶，在炎炎夏日也方便补充水分。</p>
<p>背带和提把也是值得考量的细节，能手拿又能肩背，甚至可以调整成单或双肩的款式，更能适用于多种场合和状况。</p>
