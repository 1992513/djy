<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>基底油常见5种大公开 淡疤、改善脱皮都好用</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875731-shutterstock_1522814642-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>基底油本身的功效也不逊色于精油。如荷荷芭油能稳定湿疹肌肤、保养头皮。(Shutterstock)</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #188638;"><strong>【编者按】</strong>精油大多需要与基底油混合用，而基底油本身的功效也不逊色。如荷荷芭油能稳定湿疹肌肤、保养头皮；金盏花油有淡化疤痕的功效。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">用精油前必知 5种最常见的基底油</span></h2>
<p>多数精油不适合直接涂在肌肤上，所以通常会跟一种叫作基底油的东西稀释使用。通常是98% 基底油加上2% 精油。基底油容量、价格和品质各有不同。下列是五种最常见的基底油。根据价格和方便取得与否来决定你的基底油。</p>
<p><strong>1. 酪梨油 适合干燥敏感肌肤</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12875733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12875733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875733-shutterstock_1892070883-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12875733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875733-shutterstock_1892070883-600x400.jpg" alt="常见基底油之一：酪梨油。适合干燥敏感肌肤。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12875733" class="wp-caption-text">常见基底油之一：酪梨油。适合干燥敏感肌肤。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>浓稠、深绿色的油脂从酪梨果肉萃取而来，富含必需脂肪酸和脂溶性维生素，特别是维他命A、D、E，钾、卵磷脂。</p>
<p>酪梨油非常滋润效果很好，也常建议有敏感肌的人使用。不过如果你对乳胶过敏，可能也会对酪梨油过敏。建议在肌肤小面积测试过再涂在身上。酪梨油有绝佳的肌肤穿透分子，非常适合干燥和脱皮肌肤。</p>
<p><strong>2. 金盏花油 润肤、淡化疤痕</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12875735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12875735" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875735-shutterstock_155929091-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12875735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875735-shutterstock_155929091-600x400.jpg" alt="常见基底油之二：金盏花油。润肤、淡化疤痕。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12875735" class="wp-caption-text">常见基底油之二：金盏花油。润肤、淡化疤痕。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>金盏花油是从金盏花用浸渍法得到的药草油。这种精油很适合疗愈肌肤，也用来淡化疤痕。干燥肌肤的所有问题都能用它来改善。</p>
<p><strong>3.?荷荷芭油 适合湿疹养护、头皮保养</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12875732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12875732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875732-shutterstock_1534707548-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12875732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875732-shutterstock_1534707548-600x400.jpg" alt="常见基底油之三：荷荷芭油。适合湿疹养护、头皮保养。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12875732" class="wp-caption-text">常见基底油之三：荷荷芭油。适合湿疹养护、头皮保养。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种常见的基底油很接近自然的肌肤油脂，能轻易吸收，不会感到油腻厚重。</p>
<p>荷荷芭油其实不算是油脂；比较像是液态蜡，因此让荷荷芭油跟其他基底油比起来还要稳定，也适合用在婴儿和敏感性肌肤上。像是干癣和湿疹这类的肌肤问题也很适用。对于头发和头皮也是很好的保养。（推荐阅读：<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #188638;"><a style="color: #188638; text-decoration: underline;" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/20/8/17/n12338598.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">湿疹就是皮肤发炎？湿疹症状、治疗和保养全图解</a></span></span>）</p>
<p>荷荷芭可以单独使用，也可以跟其他基底油混合当作基底。</p>
<p><strong>4. 橄榄油 安全可食用</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12204830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12204830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3cf7bb2ee58bd10e7e1002e710e29f9a-600x404.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12204830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3cf7bb2ee58bd10e7e1002e710e29f9a-600x404.jpg" alt="标有“特级初榨”的橄榄油可能不是真的。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12204830" class="wp-caption-text">常见基底油之四：橄榄油。安全可食用<strong>。</strong>(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种厨房常备食材是非常好的基底油。若要跟精油混合，建议使用清淡的橄榄油，因为初榨橄榄油有强烈的香味，会掩盖宝贵精油的香气。（推荐阅读：<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #188638; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #188638; text-decoration: underline;" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/20/6/22/n12204592.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">橄榄油抗癌防晒、抵御发炎 冷吃最营养</a></span></span>）</p>
<p><strong>5.?水蜜桃籽油 修复皮肤</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12875734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12875734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875734-shutterstock_1133945129-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12875734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12875734-shutterstock_1133945129-600x400.jpg" alt="常见基底油之五：水蜜桃籽油。可修复皮肤。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12875734" class="wp-caption-text">常见基底油之五：水蜜桃籽油。可修复皮肤。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>水蜜桃籽油有高度的必要脂肪酸，含有维生素E，适合各种修复肌肤的功能，也适合涂抹在全身。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">可以直接抹在皮肤上的精油</span></h2>
<p>稀释精油无害，甚至还有益处。所以不妨在一开始接触精油时先稀释再使用。</p>
<p>多数精油都应该稀释，不过下列几种可以未经稀释就直接涂抹在皮肤上，也就是“无添加”。不过，先在小面积肌肤测试，确定不会红肿发炎还是较好——只需在手腕涂抹少许，看肌肤的反应即可：</p>
<p>尤加利、茶树、檀香、胡椒薄荷、乳香、依兰、绿薄荷、罗马洋甘菊、薰衣草。</p>
