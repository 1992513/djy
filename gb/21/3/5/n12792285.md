<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】蟹肉浓汤冬瓜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/76d1692a4c135c3218008cd94aa78123-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蟹肉浓汤冬瓜用冬瓜和螃蟹搭配，嫩滑细腻，清香鲜甜，滋补养身，老少皆宜。（普照荟苑提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>冬瓜营养丰富，含油酸、丙醇二酸、氨基酸和丰富的膳食纤维。丙醇二酸能有效控制体内糖类转化为脂肪，防止体内脂肪堆积，还能把多余的脂肪消耗掉，对防治高血压、动脉粥样硬化有良好的效果。</p>
<p>冬瓜中的膳食纤维含量很高，膳食纤维能降低体内胆固醇、降血脂。冬瓜中的粗纤维能刺激肠道蠕动，使肠道里积存的致癌物质尽快排泄出去。</p>
<p>冬瓜中富含鸟氨酸和Y-氨基丁酸，天冬氨酸、谷氨酸、精氨酸的含量也较高。此外，冬瓜籽所含的蛋白质和瓜氨酸更可润泽皮肤，还能抑制黑色素的形成。</p>
<p>蟹肉乃食中珍味，素有“一盘蟹，顶桌菜”的民谚。它不但味美，且营养丰富，是一种高蛋白的补品。蟹肉浓汤冬瓜是很有特色的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">粤菜</a>之一，用冬瓜和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9E%83%E8%9F%B9.md#1">螃蟹</a>搭配，嫩滑细腻，清香鲜甜，滋补养身，老少皆宜。</p>
<p>制作这道菜时，罗厨选用了家种的冬瓜，口感香滑细致，冬瓜吸收了鸡汤的香浓和红萝卜的清香后，口感更加完美。这是一道功夫菜，醇香嫩滑而且味道、质地都异常自然细腻，堪称一道完美而且原汁原味的佳肴！</p>
<p>罗厨认为，清蒸是最能保留食材营养与味道的一种烹饪方法，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">粤菜</a>中经常采用。制作这道蟹肉浓汤冬瓜时，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9E%83%E8%9F%B9.md#1">螃蟹</a>、红萝卜和冬瓜都要事先蒸好。蒸螃蟹的时候，蟹肚要朝上，这样蟹汁不会过多流失。蒸冬瓜的时候，清汤要浸过冬瓜为好。</p>
<h2>蟹肉浓汤冬瓜</h2>
<p>主料：蟹肉、冬瓜</p>
<p>配料：姜片、葱、金钩虾米、红萝卜</p>
<p>调料：盐、生粉水、清汤、浓鸡汤</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将姜片和葱段摆放在蟹上面，放蒸箱蒸15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792289" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792289" class="wp-caption-text">将姜片和葱段摆放在蟹上面。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 把冬瓜改成圆柱形，放于容器中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792291" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792291" class="wp-caption-text">把冬瓜改成圆柱形，放于容器中。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 冬瓜上摆放金钩虾米，加入清汤，蒸25分钟至熟透后取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792292" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792292" class="wp-caption-text">冬瓜上摆放金钩虾米。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 螃蟹蒸15分钟后取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792295" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792295" class="wp-caption-text">螃蟹蒸15分钟后取出。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 红萝卜切粒蒸15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792298" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792298" class="wp-caption-text">红萝卜切粒。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将蒸好的红萝卜搅拌成汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792301" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/6-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/6-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792301" class="wp-caption-text">将蒸好的红萝卜搅拌成汁。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 蟹拆开取肉，放冬瓜上面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792307" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792307" class="wp-caption-text">蟹拆开取肉，放冬瓜上面。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 浓鸡汤汁加入红萝卜汁制作芡汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/8-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/8-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792309" class="wp-caption-text">浓鸡汤汁加入红萝卜汁。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 锅内添加1茶匙盐和2茶匙生粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792312" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/9-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/9-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792312" class="wp-caption-text">锅内添加1茶匙盐和2茶匙生粉。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 做好的芡汁淋在已摆好的蟹肉冬瓜盅上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792313" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/10-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/10-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792313" class="wp-caption-text">将芡汁淋在已摆好的蟹肉冬瓜盅上。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 成品菜肴香滑鲜甜，原汁原味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12792317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/11-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12792317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/11-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12792317" class="wp-caption-text">成品菜肴香滑鲜甜，原汁原味。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
