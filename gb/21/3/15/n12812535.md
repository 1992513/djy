<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>锅具这样收纳 不仅实用还能美化厨房</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1848093856-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>锅具是厨房最有韵味的装饰，放在不同的位置有不同的空间风格。（图/Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年03月15日讯】（大纪元记者林海柔编译报导）厨房就像是一个家的缩影，保留了主人内心私密的空间，炉灶区、锅具和收纳设计，都映照出主人的个性和品味。许多家庭大多有各式美丽的锅具，像陶锅、铜锅、不銹钢锅或铸铁锅等，除了提升料理美味之外也为厨房的质感加分不少，而厨房空间风格往往也和锅具的收纳方式相连结。</p>
<p>走进厨房不单只有柴米油盐酱醋茶，还有乐享生活，特别是在防疫期间，都市外食族在家用餐的机会增多了，家人们使用厨房更显得重要。锅具收纳有很多种方式，将空间和配件整合运用，能提高收纳效率，突破空间限制，美化厨房不零乱。</p>
<p>锅具收纳的秘诀大致有三点：摆在随手好拿的位置，使用较方便；擅用收纳配件，打造实用又有设计感的厨房；收纳不落地，尽量往上堆放可以腾出更多空间；以这3个重点发挥创意，刷新老房子的厨房空间，锅碗瓢盆更加井然有序。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>善用整片墙</strong></span></h2>
<p>如果您有许多只锅具，选择壁挂的收纳方式很实用。在一堵墙上钉3排横杆，自行调整合适的高度以方便吊挂各种尺吋的炊具。或是依照自己的需求，钉上固定钉钩也可以。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812727" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1071038213.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12812727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1071038213-450x675.jpg" alt="Pots,And,Pans,Hanging,On,A,Kitchen,Wall,To,Save,Shutterstock,锅" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812727" class="wp-caption-text">在墙面上钉挂杆吊挂锅具，方便又不占空间。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>锅具也可用作厨房的装饰元素，一系列老式厨房用品或是有着亮丽外观的铜锅、彩色锅具，可挂在有背景色料的墙上，成为厨房中醒目的焦点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1848093856.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12812732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1848093856-600x401.jpg" alt="Details,Of,A,Cozy,Kitchen,Interior,With,A,Brick,Wall" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812732" class="wp-caption-text">将锅具挂在墙上，厨房增添了些许生活的韵味。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有许多孔洞的钉板（pegboard）固定在一片墙面上很特别，不论是铝质、木质或不銹钢材质的钉板，都能随意在任何位置挂上挂钩，锅具、剪刀、打蛋器、铲子等必需用具都可以挂在同一区，美观又方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_274445612.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12812734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_274445612-600x450.jpg" alt="Stainless,Steel,Cookware,,,Kitchenware,Set,Shutterstock,锅" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812734" class="wp-caption-text">洞洞钉板可以随心所欲爱怎么挂就怎么挂。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>直立式展示</strong></span></h2>
<p>为节省宝贵的橱柜空间，可利用垂直收纳方式，例如由上至下交错排列，可请木工施做实木座也很有质感。这种收纳方式还为厨房的角落增添了视觉吸引力，并且可将特定的锅具或餐盘轻松分类。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1339846463.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12812753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1339846463-450x656.jpg" alt="Kitchen,Pan,Holder,Stand,On,Concrete,Wall,Background,With,Copy,Shutterstock,锅" width="450" b="656" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812753" class="wp-caption-text">交错式的垂直陈列锅具，节省空间又很有设计感。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也可在厨房角落放置四方体的6层柜，不銹钢或木制的窄板柜子紧贴冰箱或是其它柜体，直立展示锅具也很好看。</p>
<p>好锅可以长久使用，如果您有“收集”各种喜爱的锅具，订制一个展示架靠墙放，或当做开放式客厅与厨房的隔间，摆上所有喜爱的锅子～炒锅、炖锅、汤锅、珐琅锅……既实用又让人赏心悦目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812764" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1191644734.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12812764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1191644734-450x300.jpg" alt="Modern,Restaurant,Outdoor,Kitchen,Cookware,Decoration,Background,Shutterstock,锅" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812764" class="wp-caption-text">锅具展示架示意图。客厅和厨房之间以锅具展示架做隔间，令人赏心悦目。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>吊挂炉灶附近</strong></span></h2>
<p>锅架可以设置在炉灶上方和周围的墙面空间，多加一些S形挂钩使用，吊挂各种大小形状的炊具，以及较小的工具和器皿，例如带有弯曲手柄的大汤匙、漏杓、铲子等，这样做料理时便能轻松取用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1775092613.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12812773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1775092613-600x400.jpg" alt="Wooden,Kitchen,Facade,In,Apartment,With,Modern,Interior,,New,Furniture,Shutterstock,锅" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812773" class="wp-caption-text">在炉灶附近收纳锅具和厨房用品，料理工作更得心应手。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果您厨房的抽油烟机高度够高的话，也可以参考餐馆的专业厨房，直接安装钩架在抽油烟机上，吊挂较小的锅具和用具，确保吊架够高不致遮挡视线，看起来好像跟大厨一样专业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1726922641.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12812806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1726922641-600x390.jpg" alt="Professional,Kitchen,In,A,Restaurant,Shutterstock,图" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812806" class="wp-caption-text">高高的抽油烟机吊挂着小锅具，做菜好像都有餐馆大厨的架势了。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>橱柜收纳</strong></span></h2>
<p>橱柜收纳锅具也是很常见的方式，为节省空间，可多加利用角落柜做滑轨拉出；也可使用不銹钢层架和直立架放入柜内可堆叠更多锅具；或在柜门内钉上钩子，这样可容纳更多用具。厨房系统柜的上下柜也可收纳一般锅具和锅盖，较重的锅子和烤盘尽量放在下层抽屉比较好拿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812836" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_591534062.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12812836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_591534062-600x414.jpg" alt="Interior,Design,Decor,Of,Kitchen,In,Luxury,Apartment,Showing,Closeup,Shutterstock,锅" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812836" class="wp-caption-text">橱柜角落加上滑轨后，拿取锅具很轻松。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1842964336.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12812844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1842964336-600x399.jpg" alt="White,Cozy,And,Comfy,Contemporary,Classic,Kitchen,Interior,With,Wooden,Shutterstock,锅" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812844" class="wp-caption-text">厨房上柜和下柜收纳锅具看起来干净整齐。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>固定在天花板</strong></span></h2>
<p>重型锅具需要较坚固的支撑，为了提高安全性，可安装金属锅架到天花板梁柱上。坚固的锅架非常适合吊挂铸铁煎锅之类的沉重锅具。也可安装靠近窗户的位置，整排锅具好似窗户帷幕，增强了厨房鲜明的特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812853" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_18416926.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12812853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_18416926-450x675.jpg" alt="Home,Interior-,Kitchen,Sink,Shutterstock,锅" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812853" class="wp-caption-text">“锅具帷幕”映衬着窗外景色，成了厨房的美丽风光。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还可将吊锅架固定在中岛桌上方的天花板，可以代替吊灯成为厨房的焦点。挑选喜爱的吊架或双层架，例如工业风、休闲复古风或时尚精致的款式，搭配整体空间与厨房相得益彰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812857" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_176810891.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-12812857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_176810891-600x460.jpg" alt="Living,And,Dining,Room.,Open,Wall,Design,Idea,Shutterstock,锅" width="600" b="460" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812857" class="wp-caption-text">中岛桌上方豪气闪光的锅架特别吸睛。（图/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在中岛桌上方悬挂锅架，烹饪和清洗都非常方便。如果您的中岛桌特别大，请确保将锅架放置在中间适当的位置，以便从多个角度都好使用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>更多运用</strong></span></h2>
<p>此外还有许多特别配件也可以美化厨房或节省空间，诸如附有置物层板的挂钩可固定在墙上装饰，而单一挂杆显得较简单；中岛桌侧边的开放式层架，或是餐车下方也可放置锅具；现有的橱柜侧边可钉上挂杆等等，利用现有的配备发挥更多功用。</p>
<p>整理锅碗瓢盆的第一步通常是将常用的放在方便拿取的位置，不常使用的烤盘或用具可收放起来，这样使用厨房锅具等用品会更有条理，整理更加容易。</p>
