<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>和风家常菜 咸甜好吃的“鲔鱼玉子茶巾”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-49-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>贴心又充满惊喜感的玉子茶巾，料理不费力却可口。（图／悦知文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：黄澄澄柔软的蛋皮包裹上咸甜滋味的鲔鱼内馅，这是一道充满惊喜感的宴客菜“鲔鱼玉子茶巾”。在除夕夜或是节庆时分，端上桌来很有喜气，口感上不腻味，料理的厨师也不用大费周章的烹饪就可以完成，是贴心好吃的精致家常菜。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">鲔鱼玉子茶巾</span></h2>
<p>利用蛋皮做成的玉子茶巾不仅简单还非常喜气，和家人一起动手做做看这道菜吧！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>鲔鱼罐头 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..2罐（300g）<br />
盐 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.1小匙<br />
黑胡椒粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..2小匙<br />
桂冠沙拉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..1/2条<br />
小蛋皮 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.6张（约3颗鸡蛋份）<br />
熟水莲 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.数根</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 鲔鱼罐头沥去油汁后，倒入调理盆中加入盐、黑胡椒粉和沙拉酱，以汤匙搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12838973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12838973" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-30.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12838973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-30-450x272.jpg" alt="鲔鱼" width="450" b="272" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12838973" class="wp-caption-text">鲔鱼罐头沥去油汁后，倒入调理盆中加入盐、黑胡椒粉和沙拉酱，以汤匙搅拌均匀。（图／悦知文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 蛋皮铺上适量的鲔鱼内馅。（蛋皮作法请参考下方说明）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12838975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12838975" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-29.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12838975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-29-450x273.jpg" alt="蛋皮" width="450" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12838975" class="wp-caption-text">蛋皮铺上适量的鲔鱼内馅。（图／悦知文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将蛋皮往中间收成茶巾状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12838976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12838976" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-24.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12838976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-24-450x273.jpg" alt="蛋皮" width="450" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12838976" class="wp-caption-text">蛋皮往中间收成茶巾状。（图／悦知文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 取一条事先烫好的水莲在蛋皮上绑蝴蝶结，即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12838977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12838977" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12838977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5-13-450x273.jpg" alt="蛋皮" width="450" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12838977" class="wp-caption-text">取一条事先烫好的水莲在蛋皮上绑蝴蝶结。（图／悦知文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">蛋皮</span></h2>
<p>蛋皮和蛋丝是许多日式料理中，会应用到的基础蛋作法。蛋皮除了应用在蛋包饭外，还可以做出计多创意料理，而蛋丝更是十分好用的内馅材料，举凡寿司、饭团以及春卷都可以看到它。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（使用20公分的平底锅）</span></h3>
<p>蛋液 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..1/2颗</p>
<p>*当煎锅越大时，使用的蛋液愈多*</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">蛋皮作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 鸡蛋放入碗中，以筷子打匀。</p>
<p>2. 锅内喷上少量食用油（或以纸巾涂抹）。</p>
<p>3. 冷锅下蛋液，转动锅子让蛋液均匀分布。</p>
<p>4. 转小火，让蛋液慢慢熟成。</p>
<p>5. 当表面都凝固后，翻面稍微煎一下，蛋皮完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12838970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12838970" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/d4c638223a8d33a339f7a9b4cc1c1b51.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12838970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/d4c638223a8d33a339f7a9b4cc1c1b51-450x260.jpg" alt="蛋皮" width="450" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12838970" class="wp-caption-text">当表面都凝固后，翻面稍微煎一下，蛋皮完成。（图／悦知文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">TIPS</span></h3>
<p>如果要做出没有蛋白块的蛋皮，在打匀鸡蛋后可以再将蛋液过滤，煎出来蛋皮色泽就会更加均匀。</p>
