<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>军辉桥畔黄花风铃木  越夜越美丽</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486345-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夜拍值得一试。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年03月04日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）像诗一样美的八掌溪流过幸福城市嘉义市，流域广绿草如茵是踏青的好地方，话说嘉义市辅仁中学对面的八掌溪堤防<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E8%8A%B1%E9%A3%8E%E9%93%83%E6%9C%A8.md#1">黄花风铃木</a>盛开，白天吸引满满的赏花人潮，入夜后在夜色灯光下，金黄璀璨的黄花，更是美丽。市府表示，夜拍黄花风铃木是另一种不同的美感，市民朋友不妨在晚餐之后，前来堤防散步赏花，相信定能创造新的视觉感受、感受嘉义市夜的美丽。</p>
<p>市府表示，黄历新年假期嘉义市迎来满满走春人潮，接着繁花盛开的二二八连假，再次吸引旅客游人、市民朋友前来追花赏花。军辉桥两侧的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E8%8A%B1%E9%A3%8E%E9%93%83%E6%9C%A8.md#1">黄花风铃木</a>今年再度盛开，黄花结满枝头，金灿灿的景色，让人赞叹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12789439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12789439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486346.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12789439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486346-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12789439" class="wp-caption-text">夜晚的黄花风铃木更美。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12789440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12789440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486347.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12789440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486347-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12789440" class="wp-caption-text">游人来去金碧辉煌的八掌溪堤坊。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>假日白天是赏花好时机，平日夜晚散步更是悠闲赏花的美妙时刻。军辉桥堤防步道，还有设置公共艺术与休憩座椅，晚餐之后，前来散步、赏花，令人心旷神怡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12789441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12789441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486348.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12789441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486348-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12789441" class="wp-caption-text">湛蓝的夜空映出另一种美。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12789442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12789442" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486349.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12789442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486349-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12789442" class="wp-caption-text">夜拍不妨试试。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市府表示，从军辉桥头往绿映水漾公园散步前行，堤防两侧都有黄花风铃木可以欣赏。欢迎市民朋友把握黄花风铃木盛开期，感受白天黑夜都美丽的嘉义市风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12789443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12789443" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486350.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12789443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486350-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12789443" class="wp-caption-text">很悠闲的夜晚。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12789444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12789444" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486351.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12789444" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/486351-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12789444" class="wp-caption-text">骑公共自行车夜访黄花风铃木。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
