<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】松茸炒皇帝蟹爪肉</h2>
<a href="https://d2agd1dsojkgmu.cloudfront.net/B6sRwyrnb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>松茸炒皇帝蟹爪肉。（图／梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这次设计的菜单，采用了美味的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E8%8C%B8.md#1">松茸</a>，让菜肴的味道层次更丰富。松茸是属于口蘑科口蘑属下的一种食用蕈，具有独特的浓郁香味。在日本，松茸被视为食用蕈中的极品，松茸之于日本人就犹如松露之于法国人。准备松茸时不要用水洗，以免味道和香气流失。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E8%8C%B8.md#1">松茸</a>炒皇帝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9F%B9%E7%88%AA%E8%82%89.md#1">蟹爪肉</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【材料】</strong></span></h3>
<p>松茸 4 oz<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%B1%86.md#1">甜豆</a> 2 oz<br />
皇帝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9F%B9%E7%88%AA%E8%82%89.md#1">蟹爪肉</a> 6 oz<br />
姜、蒜、红萝卜花 各少许<br />
牛油 2汤匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【调味】</strong></span></h3>
<p>水 1/4杯<br />
烹大师 1茶匙<br />
糖 1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、酱油 少许<br />
粟粉 1/4茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782393" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-4-450x338.jpg" alt="调味料" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782393" class="wp-caption-text">调味料：水、烹大师、糖、胡椒粉、酱油、粟粉。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【作法】</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%B1%86.md#1">甜豆</a>撕掉旁边老筋和头尾，洗干净备用。松茸不要水洗，用小刀削去根部少许皮。<br />
2.用毛刷扫掉松茸表面灰尘，再用湿布抹干净，切成长条薄片备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782394" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-3-450x338.jpg" alt="松茸" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782394" class="wp-caption-text">用毛刷扫掉松茸表面灰尘，再用湿布抹干净，切成长条薄片备用。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蟹爪肉先用热水浸暖约3分钟，再捞出沥干水分备用，甜豆用油盐水烫过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782397" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782397" class="wp-caption-text">蟹爪肉先用热水浸暖约3分钟，再捞出沥干水分备用。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅放入牛油，火勿太大，先把松茸煎香至金黄盛出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782398" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/daa5624bab8317b3633a5da7d45cde9c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/daa5624bab8317b3633a5da7d45cde9c-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782398" class="wp-caption-text">热锅放入牛油，火勿太大，先把松茸煎香至金黄盛出备用。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放入姜蒜，把蟹肉和甜豆放进炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782399" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/ffaf4aa5fe281865a490d750879ea62f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/ffaf4aa5fe281865a490d750879ea62f-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782399" class="wp-caption-text">放入姜蒜，把蟹肉和甜豆放进炒香。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.再把松茸放进，搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入兜匀，即可上碟。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782400" class="wp-caption-text">把松茸放进，搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入兜匀，即可上碟。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
