<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】西兰花杏鲍菇配豆酥酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西兰花杏鲍菇配豆酥酱。（图／梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E9%85%A5%E9%85%B1.md#1">豆酥酱</a>（fried crispy soy bean）的料理相当多变，而且香气十足，和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0%E8%8A%B1.md#1">西兰花</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8F%E9%B2%8D%E8%8F%87.md#1">杏鲍菇</a>一起拌炒，非常的下饭，而且高纤又营养。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【材料】</strong></span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0%E8%8A%B1.md#1">西兰花</a> 1磅<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8F%E9%B2%8D%E8%8F%87.md#1">杏鲍菇</a> 8 oz<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E9%85%A5%E9%85%B1.md#1">豆酥酱</a> 2汤匙<br />
牛油 2汤匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【调味】</strong></span></h3>
<p>蘑菇粉 1茶匙<br />
糖 1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油 少许<br />
粟粉 半茶匙<br />
水 1杯</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【作法】</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 杏鲍菇切成环形状，在一边顶上切十字花纹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782447" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782447" class="wp-caption-text">杏鲍菇切成环形状，在一边顶上切十字花纹。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 西兰花洗干净后用油盐水烫熟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782448" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782448" class="wp-caption-text">西兰花洗干净后用油盐水烫熟备用。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅放入牛油，将有花纹一边的杏鲍菇向下放入，煎至金黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782449" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782449" class="wp-caption-text">热锅放入牛油，将有花纹一边的杏鲍菇向下放入，煎至金黄。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 再加入水及调味料，慢火煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782451" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782451" class="wp-caption-text">再加入水及调味料，慢火煮5分钟。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 最后用粟粉水勾芡，排放在碟中央，旁边伴以西兰花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12782452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12782452" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12782452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12782452" class="wp-caption-text">最后用粟粉水勾芡。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 最后将豆酥酱放在杏鲍菇上面，便可享用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【梁师傅小贴士】</strong></span></h2>
<p>杏鲍菇切成环形状，并在一边顶上切十字花纹，其作用是比较容易入味又美观。◇</p>
