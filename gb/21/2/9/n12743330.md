<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味之年菜系列】翡翠比目鱼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/0abf50945aa7b5752161c6de32be68c8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道翡翠比目鱼，晶莹剔透，色香味俱全。玉米的清甜被冬瓜卷吸收，比目鱼肉质爽滑、口味独特，鲜美的蟹肉可谓锦上添花。（普照荟苑提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>最能够将美食的魅力和内涵展现出来的莫过于中国新年，人们似乎将所有的美味都汇聚在这个华人最重视的日子里。</p>
<p>准备<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">年菜</a>是每个华人家庭过年的重头戏，而年菜的餐桌一定少不了一道饱含美好寓意的美味——鱼。“年年有余”蕴含着我们对未来美好生活的向往。今年就让罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BF%A1%E7%BF%A0%E6%AF%94%E7%9B%AE%E9%B1%BC.md#1">翡翠比目鱼</a>伴我们走进牛年，迎接新的开始。</p>
<p>比目鱼富含蛋白质、维生素A、维生素D、钙、磷、钾等营养成分，尤其维生素B6的含量颇丰。科学家曾对超过1000种食物进行调查，以精确的分析数据解析热量和营养成分，比目鱼被评为十个最有营养的食物之一（第四名）。</p>
<p>比目鱼具有祛风湿、活血通络等功效。其所含的不饱和脂肪酸易被人体吸收，有助于降低血中胆固醇，而且比目鱼还富含大脑的主要组成成分DHA，经常食用可增强智力。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BF%A1%E7%BF%A0%E6%AF%94%E7%9B%AE%E9%B1%BC.md#1">翡翠比目鱼</a>，晶莹剔透，色香味俱全。新鲜玉米的清甜被冬瓜卷吸收，深海比目鱼肉质爽滑、口味独特，鲜美的蟹肉可谓锦上添花。</p>
<p>这道菜配料丰富、调料精致，罗厨精湛的厨艺在这道菜中充分展现出来，不仅每个食材都保留了真正的原汁原味，整体风味更是独特鲜美，令人回味。</p>
<p>在色彩的配置上，这道菜以辅助的色彩来衬托、突出主料，形成菜肴色彩的均匀柔和，外观看起来谐和悦目、相映成趣，堪称<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">年菜</a>餐桌上一道亮眼的美食风景。</p>
<h2>翡翠比目鱼</h2>
<p>主料：比目鱼、蟹肉</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/13681622c60bf96074fd77c81b4ae988.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/13681622c60bf96074fd77c81b4ae988-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：冬瓜、香菜、姜丝、玉米</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/330d3d382767dccb0f30d875fce66277.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/330d3d382767dccb0f30d875fce66277-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>调料：盐、糖、胡椒粉、生粉、蛋清、清鸡汤</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 比目鱼洗净沥干水分，取出鱼柳切薄鱼片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-27.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-27-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 用蛋清、胡椒粉、生粉腌制鱼片待用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>3. 冬瓜洗干净，沥干水分，并将其片成薄片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 新鲜玉米取玉米粒放入碟中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5. 在冬瓜片上加香菜、鱼片和蟹肉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>6. 再添加少量生粉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7. 加入姜丝后卷成条状。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/7-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/7-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>8. 将冬瓜卷摆放于玉米粒上面。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>9. 将盘子放入蒸箱大火蒸6~7分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10. 用清鸡汤调入盐、糖和生粉水成芡汁。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>11. 琉璃芡淋于冬瓜卷上即完成。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12743410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
