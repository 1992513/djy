<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【地中海炖汤】番茄鲜蕈炖鸡肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/77a62821dff99e609b5d8de998c4d722-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>番茄鲜蕈炖鸡块。(图／日日幸福提供)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%AD%E6%B5%B7%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">地中海料理</a>回归天然饮食，以初榨橄榄油为基底，搭配大量的蔬果，从生菜沙拉到主食都可以简单烹饪而完成。</p>
<p>如果想来一点汤品，使用慢火<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E7%85%AE.md#1">炖煮</a>的方式，让鸡骨头于锅中缓缓释放出精华，放入大量蔬食绽放出天然的香气，佐以适合的香料更充分展现食物的原味与香甜。</p>
<p>地中海风的经典食材-<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>，是你绝对不容错过的料理，简单易做又有异国风，赶快试做看看吧。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>鲜蕈炖鸡肉</span></h2>
<p>鸡肉块要使用带骨的，这要在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E7%85%AE.md#1">炖煮</a>过程中，能让酱汁更浓郁，鸡肉的香气也会更足。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>
<p>4人份</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>仿土<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E5%9D%97.md#1">鸡块</a>1/2只<br />
洋葱150g<br />
小番茄20粒（切半）<br />
鸿喜菇150g<br />
大蒜40g<br />
红葱头30g<br />
九层塔叶15片<br />
新烟百里香5支<br />
面粉　适量<br />
特级初榨橄榄油100cc<br />
基础鸡高汤200cc<br />
白酒150cc</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味料</span></h3>
<p>海盐　适量<br />
现磨黑胡椒　适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">准备工作</span></h3>
<p>洋葱切丁；小番茄对切；鸿喜菇切除根部，收剥成小朵；大蒜切碎；红葱头切小丁备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724635" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/de713c81ce5fb1eee6699140e411cc74.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/de713c81ce5fb1eee6699140e411cc74-450x300.jpg" alt="番茄" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724635" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱切丁；小番茄对切；鸿喜菇切除根部，收剥成小朵；大蒜切碎；红葱头切小丁(图／日日幸福提供)</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将鸡腿切块撒上调味料，再裹一层面粉。<br />
2. 锅中倒入橄榄油，放入鸡腿以中火煎上色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724636" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/e8fe5b43fb12c72c2f884b53b1a9df23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/e8fe5b43fb12c72c2f884b53b1a9df23-450x300.jpg" alt="橄榄油" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724636" class="wp-caption-text">锅中倒入橄榄油，放入鸡腿以中火煎上色。(图／日日幸福提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 放入洋葱与红葱头炒软，再放大蒜碎炒香。<br />
4. 接着加入鸿喜菇炒软<br />
5. 再放小番茄、所有香草与白酒煮一下。<br />
6. 倒入鸡高汤，煮滚后转小火炖煮。</p>
<p>7. 至酱汁浓稠，再以海盐与黑胡椒调味即可。</p>
