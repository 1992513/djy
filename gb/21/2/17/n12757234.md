<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】炸蟹柳卷&#038;炸皇帝蟹钳 香酥Q弹</h2>
<a href="https://d3t1om6knkjnq4.cloudfront.net/B5znTzkXq"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>炸蟹柳卷和炸皇帝蟹钳，海味炸物赞不绝口。（图／梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong>佳节时分餐桌上少不了山珍<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%91%B3.md#1">海味</a>，烹饪方式煮、炸、炒、炖多种料理法，其中<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E7%89%A9.md#1">炸物</a>更是老少喜爱的佳肴菜；口感上要做到外酥内香软或有弹性，不只是湿润的炸料要掌握比例和份量，也有相当多的小细节。下面让梁厨亲自示范<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%91%B3.md#1">海味</a>炸物的作法，让在家料理师不慌乱，轻松上菜就让宾客与家人吃得回味无穷。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">炸蟹柳卷</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12757303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757303" class="wp-caption-text">炸蟹柳卷除了山珍还有蔬菜，酥脆口感又有饱满营养。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【材料】</strong></span></h3>
<p>皇帝蟹柳六条<br />
秋葵六条<br />
杏鲍菇切成六幼条<br />
越南米纸六块<br />
脆浆1.5杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_12757298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757298" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-39.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-39-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757298" class="wp-caption-text">脆浆、越南米纸、秋葵、杏鲍菇、皇帝蟹柳。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【脆浆材料】</strong>（应预早半小时调好）</span></h3>
<p>面粉1杯<br />
粟粉2汤匙<br />
发粉 1茶匙<br />
水1.1/4杯<br />
盐1/4茶匙<br />
生油2汤匙（最晚放）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 米纸稍微沾温水15秒，立即拿出放在菜板上，放入蟹柳、秋葵、杏鲍菇条。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12757300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757300" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757300" class="wp-caption-text">米纸稍微沾温水15秒，立即拿出放在菜板上，放入蟹柳、秋葵、杏鲍菇条。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将米纸左右包向中央，再从下往上卷成长条型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12757301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757301" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-25.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-25-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757301" class="wp-caption-text">将米纸左右包向中央，再从下往上卷成长条型。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蟹柳卷沾上脆浆，放入油中炸至金黄色便可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12757302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757302" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757302" class="wp-caption-text">蟹柳卷沾上脆浆，放入油中炸至金黄色便可。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 炸好的蟹柳卷捞出沥干油，放在装饰的菜叶上便可上桌。</p>
<p>5. 炸蟹柳卷也可以沾甜辣酱吃。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>炸皇帝蟹钳</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12757311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757311" class="wp-caption-text">炸皇帝蟹钳是佳节十分很有气场的海味菜。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【材料】</strong></span></h3>
<p>皇帝蟹钳2只<br />
鱼浆4oz<br />
虾浆4oz<br />
鸡蛋1个<br />
面粉2汤匙<br />
面包糠1杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_12757308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757308" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋、面粉、面包糠、皇帝蟹钳、鱼浆、 虾浆。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 鱼浆分成两份（每份3oz），包住蟹钳，弄好形状后先沾上面粉，再沾蛋液和面包糠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12757309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757309" class="wp-caption-text">鱼浆分成两份，包住蟹钳。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 放入中温油中炸至金黄色，捞起沥干油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12757310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12757310" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12757310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12757310" class="wp-caption-text">放入中温油中炸至金黄色，捞起沥干油。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 这到炸蟹钳可以沾点Tartar sauce 吃。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【梁师傅小贴士】</strong></span></h2>
