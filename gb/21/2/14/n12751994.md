<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>后院的小客人 东蓝鸲奇遇记</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>后院的小客人：东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>去年夏天大约6月份时，有那么一星期左右的时间，天天一大早就被楼下传来的敲玻璃声音吵醒。等下楼查看，敲窗者毫无踪影。直到有一天，窗边阳台围栏上站着一只漂亮的小蓝鸟，很绅士般的向我点头示意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12752009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752009" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_02-450x312.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752009" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我赶紧取出专门拍鸟的长镜头给它来个特写，再蹑手蹑脚的打开阳台门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12752008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752008" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_03-450x336.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752008" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12752007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752007" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_04-450x308.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752007" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小家伙也不怎么害怕，飞到阳台外的树枝上再转身从树叶下打量我。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12752006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752006" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_05-450x588.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="588" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752006" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再飞到树下挂着的装鸟食的小木屋嘬几颗葵花籽，期间一直转头看看我。</p>
<p>就这么陪着我玩了十几分钟，然后就飞走了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12752005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752005" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_06-450x305.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752005" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二天一早，敲窗声如期而至。这次下楼小家伙和我玩起了躲猫猫。它不停的在外面用嘴敲窗玻璃，但我一开门，它马上飞走。一关门，又飞过来一阵猛敲。我索性不出去了，拿着相机在里面守窗待鸟。大概知道里面有观众，还有相机大炮，小家伙兴奋了，给我晒了场敲窗十八般武艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12752004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752004" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_07-450x614.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="614" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752004" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12752003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752003" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_08-450x566.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="566" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752003" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12752002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752002" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_09.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_09-450x315.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752002" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12752001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752001" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_10-450x300.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752001" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不看照片我还真不知道小家伙的眼睫毛有这么长，弯弯翘翘的，像爱美的姑娘们贴的假睫毛一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12752000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12752000" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12752000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_11-450x359.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12752000" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>敲了一阵大概敲累了，它飞到旁边的纱窗上想歇歇脚。我有点担心它那锋利的爪子会不会把纱窗抓破，就开门出去跟它商量是不是换个地方休息。它好像真听得懂似的，飞到了阳台围栏上，还低头跟我说：“对不起！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12751999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12751999" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_12.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12751999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_12-450x582.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="582" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12751999" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此后它就再也没有停在纱窗上过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12751998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12751998" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12751998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_13-450x318.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12751998" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>又有一天，小家伙敲完窗看我下楼就往后院的柳树上飞，怕我跟不上它又飞回到我跟前再往柳树上飞。我一看，哇，原来是带着另一半来见家长？有了对比就很容易的分辨出原来小家伙是个“他”而右边的是“她”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12751997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12751997" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12751997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_14-450x597.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12751997" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小家伙一会儿飞走了，再一会儿又飞回来了。仔细一看嘴里还叼着虫子呢。顺着它的目光往下瞧，哦，还有个嗷嗷待哺的小小家伙呢！原来人家也不小了，已经是爸爸了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12751996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12751996" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_15.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12751996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210213_bird_helen_chou_15-450x618.jpg" alt="鸟, 东蓝鸲, Eastern Bluebird" width="450" b="618" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12751996" class="wp-caption-text">东蓝鸲（Eastern Bluebird）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不知今年春暖花开时这一家东蓝鸲会不会回来看看我呢？＠*</p>
