<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在家做餐厅菜 橙汁排骨炸烤煎都好吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971947-a2f7b9a942d66e1e8c35db18caaeaa62-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>餐厅菜橙汁排骨一点都不困难，只要掌握技巧也可以轻松上桌。（野人文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%99%E6%B1%81%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8.md#1">橙汁排骨</a>口味酸甜适口、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8.md#1">排骨</a>酥香软嫩，是一道非常受欢迎的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85%E8%8F%9C.md#1">餐厅菜</a>，虽然看似困难，但只要掌握排骨的基本调味，接着或炸或烤或煎都可以，最后外层再裹上橙汁酱，就大功告成啰！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%99%E6%B1%81%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8.md#1">橙汁排骨</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（2 ～ 3 人份）</span></h3>
<p>猪里肌小排?400 克<br />
糖?50 克（炒糖色用）<br />
沙拉油?1 大匙（炒糖色用）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8.md#1">排骨</a>腌料</span></h3>
<p>酱油?1 大匙<br />
米酒?1 大匙<br />
蛋?1/2 颗<br />
盐、糖、白胡椒粉?1/4 小匙<br />
玉米粉?2 大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味料</span></h3>
<p>柳橙汁?100cc<br />
（可用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E5%90%89%E5%A3%AB.md#1">香吉士</a>代替）<br />
柠檬汁?25cc<br />
糖?1/4 小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>① 蛋打散、排骨洗净沥干、柠檬与柳橙挤成汁备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971948-fce78e64627cb05fcacbe0d17ad50cc6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971948-fce78e64627cb05fcacbe0d17ad50cc6-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971948" class="wp-caption-text">蛋打散、排骨洗净沥干、柠檬与柳橙挤成汁备用。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>② 排骨以排骨腌料腌2小时备用（小知识 ?）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971949-d68db0fe1daefcad538461ff2ba0f3e8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971949-d68db0fe1daefcad538461ff2ba0f3e8-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971949" class="wp-caption-text">排骨以排骨腌料腌2小时备用。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>③ 起摄氏1 6 0度油锅，放入排骨以中大火炸至上色且熟透（约3 ～ 4 分钟）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971950-30acd80ea4dabd129817bb373d710547.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971950-30acd80ea4dabd129817bb373d710547-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971950" class="wp-caption-text">起摄氏1 6 0度油锅，放入排骨以中大火炸至上色且熟透。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>④ 取出后沥油备用。<br />
⑤ 锅内下1大匙油，放入糖（炒糖色用）以中小火加热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971951" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971951-3fd5893939149a1ad2d47866f53905b0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971951-3fd5893939149a1ad2d47866f53905b0-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971951" class="wp-caption-text">锅内下1大匙油，放入糖（炒糖色用）以中小火加热。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑥ 加热至糖融化且呈枣红色（小知识 ?）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971952" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971952-54033cf778415f8d8b05a9bfbfba21f4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971952-54033cf778415f8d8b05a9bfbfba21f4-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971952" class="wp-caption-text">加热至糖融化且呈枣红色。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑦ 关火并放入炸好的排骨，翻炒均匀裹上糖色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971953" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971953-b64ed03bd9c6ab965ab3d36928bed302.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971953-b64ed03bd9c6ab965ab3d36928bed302-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971953" class="wp-caption-text">关火并放入炸好的排骨，翻炒均匀裹上糖色。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑧ 下调味料并开中大火滚煮收汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971954" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971954-377b76509ea5b5bce0c1197755615e1e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971954-377b76509ea5b5bce0c1197755615e1e-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971954" class="wp-caption-text">下调味料并开中大火滚煮收汁。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑨ 酱汁收浓再次翻拌均匀，让排骨均匀裹上糖浆即完成！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">小知识</span></h3>
<p>?? 腌料调出来应为流动状，而非浓稠状，故玉米粉不要下太重。<br />
?? 此时的糖浆很烫，请小心不要滴水进去会喷；另炒出的糖色不会甜，并带有焦糖香气。</p>
