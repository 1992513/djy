<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>双重风味香气四溢 蒜香金沙虾仁炒饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971339-9d5abec4618ab1d5b721699b433f0392-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>金光闪闪的黄金炒饭，一吃就爱上。（野人文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这道金光闪闪的黄金<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炒饭</a>，充满咸蛋黄与鸡蛋黄的双重风味，再搭配超级大的虾仁，除了视觉效果一级棒之外，尾韵那蒜香与虾油香的风味真的有够赞！强烈推荐对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%B8%E8%9B%8B%E9%BB%84%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">咸蛋黄料理</a>情有独钟的朋友试作，保证一吃就爱上。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">蒜香金沙虾仁<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炒饭</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（2 人份）</span></h3>
<p>白饭?2 碗（400 克）<br />
虾仁?12 只（依家境增减）<br />
咸蛋黄?3 颗<br />
鸡蛋黄?2 颗<br />
葱?2 支<br />
蒜头?6 瓣</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">虾仁腌料</span></h3>
<p>米酒?1/2 大匙<br />
盐?1/4 小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味料</span></h3>
<p>盐?1/2 小匙<br />
糖?1/4 小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>① 葱切葱花、蒜头切末、鸡蛋黄打散、咸蛋黄压扁切碎、虾仁去肠泥以虾仁腌料抓腌10分钟备用（ 小知识?、??）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971340" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971340-c2847ab850983743a7ac5e5ab4983726.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971340-c2847ab850983743a7ac5e5ab4983726-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971340" class="wp-caption-text">葱切葱花、蒜头切末、鸡蛋黄打散、咸蛋黄压扁切碎、虾仁去肠泥以虾仁腌料抓腌10分钟备用。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>② 打散的鸡蛋黄加入白饭中抓匀备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971341" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971341-1e2b350e8161118f7706e9b75bad74e2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971341-1e2b350e8161118f7706e9b75bad74e2-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971341" class="wp-caption-text">打散的鸡蛋黄加入白饭中抓匀备用。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>③ 原锅内下4大匙油，放入虾头与虾壳中火煸5分钟，煸出虾油（油呈红色）后丢弃虾头与虾壳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971342-c6b72481038b2b33f6d6f96a14a35c00.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971342-c6b72481038b2b33f6d6f96a14a35c00-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971342" class="wp-caption-text">原锅内下4大匙油，放入虾头与虾壳中火煸5分钟，煸出虾油（油呈红色）后丢弃虾头与虾壳。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>④ 接着放入虾仁大火煎熟取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971343-16c46cb5c6e9a4de1d5ae370998792ba.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971343-16c46cb5c6e9a4de1d5ae370998792ba-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971343" class="wp-caption-text">接着放入虾仁大火煎熟取出备用。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑤ 放入咸蛋黄并以中火拌炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971355" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971355-7a9a61fb242355c786dd31ad5d4029b6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971355-7a9a61fb242355c786dd31ad5d4029b6-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971355" class="wp-caption-text">放入咸蛋黄并以中火拌炒。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑥ 炒至起泡后，下蒜末爆香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971356" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971356-2f24360aeaa23e12cffd52fa8b476ac1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971356-2f24360aeaa23e12cffd52fa8b476ac1-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971356" class="wp-caption-text">炒至起泡后，下蒜末爆香。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑦ 接着加入饭以大火炒松及上色（小知识?）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12971357" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12971357" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971357-7c82f5ca184a9562ae3deaadcff276b0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12971357" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12971357-7c82f5ca184a9562ae3deaadcff276b0-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12971357" class="wp-caption-text">接着加入饭以大火炒松及上色。（野人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑧ 最后放虾仁、葱花及调味料，再次拌炒均匀即完成。</p>

? ?炒的过程若觉得太干可补1大匙米酒或 水增加湿润度。</p>
