<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不会失败 有坚果香的牛肝菌意大利面作法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927644-P.43-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>型男主厨的手艺“牛肝菌奶油坚果意大利面”。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%9D%E8%8F%8C.md#1">牛肝菌</a>香气特殊浓郁、营养价值高，适用于各类料理，其中最经典的就是牛肝菌<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E9%9D%A2.md#1">意大利面</a>或炖饭了！在餐厅点这一道主菜通常价格不菲，但是自己料理会担心酱汁比例调配不精准，吃不到食物的美味。现在我们将解决你的烦恼，推荐的这一款牛肝菌意大利面食谱是来自《料理123》、《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9E%8B%E7%94%B7%E5%A4%A7%E4%B8%BB%E5%8E%A8.md#1">型男大主厨</a>》新锐主厨Jerry的手艺，保证不会失败！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%9D%E8%8F%8C.md#1">牛肝菌</a>奶油坚果<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E9%9D%A2.md#1">意大利面</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【食材】</span></h3>
<p>意大利面 100g<br />
牛肝菌菇 6朵<br />
洋菇（切片） 5朵<br />
芦笋（切斜片） 2支<br />
核桃（压碎） 50g<br />
花生（压碎） 50g<br />
帕玛森<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>粉 适量<br />
干燥<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%A5%BF%E9%87%8C.md#1">巴西里</a> 少许<br />
新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%A5%BF%E9%87%8C.md#1">巴西里</a>碎 少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味料】</span></h3>
<p>鲜奶油 200c.c.<br />
奶油 60g<br />
牛肝菌菇水 250c.c.<br />
盐 适量<br />
糖 适量<br />
黑胡椒 适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_12927643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927643" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927643-P.42.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927643-P.42-450x303.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927643" class="wp-caption-text">食材和调味料先准备起来！（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 将牛肝菌菇略洗净后泡热水泡开，再剥小块备用（泡牛肝菌菇的水不要倒掉）。</p>
<p>2. 准备一锅滚水，下意大利面煮约4分钟，捞起备用。</p>
<p>3. 热锅，先放入奶油稍微融化后，加入牛肝菌菇和洋菇片炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12927645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927645" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927645-P.44-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927645-P.44-1-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927645" class="wp-caption-text">热锅，先放入奶油稍微融化后，加入牛肝菌菇炒香。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12927646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927646" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927646-P.44-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927646-P.44-2-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927646" class="wp-caption-text">加入洋菇片炒香。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12927647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927647" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927647-P.44-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927647-P.44-3-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927647" class="wp-caption-text">牛肝菌和洋菇炒香。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 接着倒入鲜奶油、牛肝菌菇水、约2/3分量的核桃碎与花生碎、干燥百里香一同烧热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12927649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927649" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927649-P.44-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927649-P.44-4-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927649" class="wp-caption-text">倒入鲜奶油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12927652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927652" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927652-P.44-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927652-P.44-5-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927652" class="wp-caption-text">倒入牛肝菌菇水。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12927656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927656" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927656-P.44-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927656" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927656-P.44-6-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927656" class="wp-caption-text">倒入约2/3分量的核桃碎与花生碎。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再放入煮过的意大利面与芦笋片，略煮1-2分钟至面条吸附酱汁，</p>
<figure id="attachment_12927730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927730-P.45-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927730-P.45-1-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927730" class="wp-caption-text">放入煮过的意大利面。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12927731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927731" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927731-P.45-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927731-P.45-2-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927731" class="wp-caption-text">放入芦笋片。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12927732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927732" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927732-P.45-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927732-P.45-3-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927732" class="wp-caption-text">起锅前用盐、糖、胡椒调味。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将意大利面盛盘后，撒上<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>粉、剩余的核桃碎与花生碎，再加上新鲜巴西里碎装饰即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12927733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12927733" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927733-P.45-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12927733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12927733-P.45-4-450x311.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12927733" class="wp-caption-text">加上新鲜巴西里碎装饰即完成。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
