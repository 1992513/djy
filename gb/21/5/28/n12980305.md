<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>居家防疫不无聊 “手作材料包”热销7倍</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12981236-498832.jpg" />
<h6>多肉盆栽材料包。（Pinkoi提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月28日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）近期减少外出、宅在家防疫，边玩边学的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E4%BD%9C.md#1">手作</a>材料包，近期销售量在网购平台上暴增7倍，例如刺绣、编织、植栽等，成为寓教于乐的首选之一。</p>
<figure id="12980308" aria-describedby="caption-12980308" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980308-498827-450x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980308-498827-450x338.jpeg" alt="奶油曲奇饼编织抱枕。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12980308" class="wp-caption-text">奶油曲奇饼编织抱枕。（Pinkoi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中央疫情流行指挥中心宣布，全台疫情提升至三级警戒，5月19日起全国各级学校停课，呼吁民众减少非必要性外出。如何让防疫生活不沉闷？家长又要如何聪明快乐带小孩？</p>
<figure id="12980312" aria-describedby="caption-12980312" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980312-498831-450x450.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980312-498831-450x450.jpeg" alt="手作羊毛毡草泥马。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12980312" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E4%BD%9C.md#1">手作</a>羊毛毡草泥马。（Pinkoi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“亚洲领先设计购物网站Pinkoi”发现到过去几天内，站上“材料包”、“DIY”等关键字搜寻量大幅飙升，与去年同期相比成长7倍。近一步观察消费者前3大喜爱的手作材料包分别为“刺绣”、“羊毛毡”与“编织”材料包。</p>
<figure id="12980310" aria-describedby="caption-12980310" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980310-498829-450x450.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980310-498829-450x450.jpeg" alt="编织信件收纳挂饰。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12980310" class="wp-caption-text">编织信件收纳挂饰。（Pinkoi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>统计显示，从桌面摆饰、墙壁挂饰到居家办公必备抱枕、靠垫等丰富种类中，消费者多偏好带有清新植物图腾、萌系动物造型的图样，色系上则以带有温馨暖橘、清凉象牙白色为主，推测不少人开始藉由动手作来增添<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%85%E5%AE%B6%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">居家防疫</a>时光乐趣，也为心灵带来简单美好的平静。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12980307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12980307" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980307-498826.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12980307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980307-498826-450x450.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12980307" class="wp-caption-text">柴犬纸模型。（Pinkoi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Pinkoi精选各式适合亲子同乐的材料包，鼓励爸妈与孩童在家一起边玩边学，包含猫咪、狗狗动物立体纸模型，为家中增添疗愈感；或是奶油曲奇饼造型的编织抱枕，圆弧造型的美食抚慰人心。</p>
<figure id="12980311" aria-describedby="caption-12980311" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980311-498830-450x450.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980311-498830-450x450.jpeg" alt="手作体验仙人掌刺绣。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12980311" class="wp-caption-text">手作体验仙人掌刺绣。（Pinkoi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>喜爱出门踏青的家庭，则适合水泥多肉盆栽材料包、干燥花植栽材料包，不仅能保有与大自然接触，更能以趣味方式带孩童认识种植的美妙；还有具精湛纸雕工艺的森林露营风景材料包等，在家就能打造专属的自制美景。◇</p>
<figure id="12980309" aria-describedby="caption-12980309" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980309-498828-450x675.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12980309-498828-450x675.jpeg" alt="森林露营纸风景。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12980309" class="wp-caption-text">森林露营纸风景。（Pinkoi提供）</figcaption></figure>
