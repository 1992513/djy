<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不失败的树苗移植法 种出好吃的果树盆栽</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969571-20210523_tree_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>移植果树盆栽的方法。（台湾广厦提供／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>盆栽栽培中最重要的作业就是树苗移植。移植前要先整理树苗的旧根，才能促进新根的成长。此外，为了帮助新芽生长，也必须将地上的部分做切除。不需要担心“切除”会造成损害，务必确实执行。</p>
<h2>一手掌握移植树苗的好坏关键</h2>
<p>要如何在盆栽栽培中种出好吃的水果？这其中最大的关键在于强壮的根。结出果实后，首要目标就是储蓄养分和水分，让果实能够长大。而提升甜度，则取决于叶子量和根量（丰厚量）。换句话说，就是要尽可能好好培育果树的根。所以移植是最重要的第一步。</p>
<h2>移植的方法</h2>
<p>和种在宽敞土地时不同，盆器种植的根部发展空间会受到局限，所以根部的照顾格外重要。移植前，先将根部土块的位置上下切除（根与根间凝固的土壤部分），再从侧面纵切几刀，可以帮助新根发育。一开始先种到不会让根卷曲成团状的CS盆器中，之后再移植到素烧盆。如果是使用容器苗，可以先种到7号盆（直径15～18cm），3年后再换盆到10号盆（直径30cm）中栽培。（编者按，<ahref="https://trinityalaska.com/5-1-2-round-green-azalea-pot-351-cs/">CS盆器</a>是一种底部有缝隙和内部突出的片状物的盆器，可以帮助根部茁壮生长。）</p>
<p>移植容器苗的的方式和换盆相同，使用一样的培养土即可。</p>
<h2>移植至新盆器</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12969456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969456" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969456-P.24-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969456-P.24-1-450x580.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="580" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969456" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>先剥落包裹住根部的泥炭土或苔藓，再拨掉根部土壤，整理一下根部。</p>
<p>1. 将树根和盆器摆在一起，调整种植深度和要切除的根部位置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969459" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969459-P.24-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969459-P.24-2-450x534.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="534" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969459" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 切除粗根的下方部分，让最上层的根可以贴近土壤表面。拆除嫁接的胶带。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969460-P.24-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969460-P.24-3-450x649.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="649" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969460" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 横长出来的根，就如同照片般全部切除。以根部可以轻易放入盆器中为准。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969461" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969461-P.24-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969461-P.24-4-450x585.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="585" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969461" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 准备CS盆器，将用土倒入约1/3左右的位置。在盆器中央放入树苗后，盖上土壤至掩盖住整个根部。<br />
Point 根部向四周均匀拓展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969462" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969462-P.25-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969462-P.25-1-450x656.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="656" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969462" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 两手轻轻插入土壤，用指尖拨动土壤，让土壤和根部更密合。90度转动盆器后再做一次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969464-P.25-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969464-P.25-2-450x597.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969464" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 地上部分保留50cm左右长度后，其余切除。切口涂上木工用接着剂，避免感染。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969466" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969466-P.25-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969466-P.25-3-450x1202.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="1202" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969466" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 立支柱避免根部晃动，用束带确实捆绑住。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969467" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969467-P.25-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969467-P.25-4-450x773.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="773" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969467" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 完成后要充分浇水，浇至水从盆底流出来为止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12969469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969469" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969469-P.25-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969469-P.25-5-450x478.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="478" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969469" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>柑橘类的移植要点</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12969470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12969470" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969470-P.25-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12969470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12969470-P.25-6-450x590.jpg" alt="移植" width="450" b="590" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12969470" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>柑橘类的树苗，很常出现上方根少、下方根多的情况。但因为一般常绿树都是由上方的根在主导树的成长，所以如果切掉上面的根而留下下面的根，反而不易栽种。必须保留上方的根，将下方的根切除后，直接埋入土里。</p>
