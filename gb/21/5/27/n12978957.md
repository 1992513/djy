<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蔬食烘焙DIY 充满南国风情的佛罗伦丁脆饼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12978970-179cc116117a0d071886d71d241e0e72-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>佛罗伦丁脆饼。（台湾东贩提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%9B%E7%BD%97%E4%BC%A6%E4%B8%81%E8%84%86%E9%A5%BC.md#1">佛罗伦丁脆饼</a>”是一款经典的欧洲饼干，与厚底的派塔皮相比它的塔皮相对薄而且有变化，有些厨师会添加橘皮或是巧克力来创造不同风味，下面介绍的这2款脆饼，取用蔬食的材料，无奶蛋、有过敏体质的人不用担心，使用植物油茹素者也可以享用，坚果香加上天然蔬食食材，健康美味，口感多层次！</strong></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">芝麻<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%9B%E7%BD%97%E4%BC%A6%E4%B8%81%E8%84%86%E9%A5%BC.md#1">佛罗伦丁脆饼</a> ＆ 椰子佛罗伦丁脆饼</span></h3>
<p>这2种佛罗伦丁脆饼的面团都相同。芝麻佛罗伦丁脆饼是加入了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB.md#1">白芝麻</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB.md#1">黑芝麻</a>，制作重点在于使用较多的白芝麻，让外观不会显得繁杂。椰子佛罗伦丁脆饼吃起来则是充满了南国风情。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>18㎝的方形模具 1个</p>
<p>A ?全麦低筋面粉? 90g<br />
A ?低筋面粉? 90g<br />
A ?甜菜糖? 30g<br />
B ?植物油? 65?<br />
B ?枫糖浆? 15?<br />
B ?无添加豆浆? 35?<br />
C ?<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E9%A5%B4.md#1">米饴</a>? 2 ? 大匙<br />
C ?枫糖浆? 2 ? 大匙<br />
C ?无添加豆浆? 1 ? 大匙<br />
C ?植物油? 1 ? 大匙</p>
<p><strong>＊芝麻佛罗伦丁脆饼</strong></p>
<p>杏仁片? 60g<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB.md#1">白芝麻</a>? 1 ? 大匙<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB.md#1">黑芝麻</a>? 1 大匙</p>
<p><strong>＊椰子佛罗伦丁脆饼</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E5%AD%90%E4%B8%9D.md#1">椰子丝</a>? 70g<br />
杏仁片? 60g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 烤箱预热至180℃。将A和B分别混合后，把B加入A中，用硅胶刮刀大略搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13012778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012778" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012778-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012778-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012778" class="wp-caption-text">将A（全麦低筋面粉、低筋面粉、甜菜糖）和B（植物油、枫糖浆、无添加豆浆）分别混合后，把B加入A中，用硅胶刮刀大略搅拌。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 接着用手揉成团状。一边想像把沾附在搅拌盆侧面的粉类擦下来，一边聚集成团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13012779" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012779" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012779-2-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012779-2-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012779" class="wp-caption-text">把沾附在搅拌盆侧面的粉类擦下来（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13012781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012781" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012781-2-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012781-2-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012781" class="wp-caption-text">面团聚集成团。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13012782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012782" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012782-2-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012782-2-3-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012782" class="wp-caption-text">面团已经用手揉成团状。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将2.的面团放在台面上，塑形成方形后，用擀面棒由上而下地按压，将整体延展开来。然后擀成18×18 ㎝的大小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13012783" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012783" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012783-3-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012783-3-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012783" class="wp-caption-text">将面团放在台面上，塑形成方形后，用擀面棒由上而下地按压。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13012784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012784" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012784-3-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012784-3-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012784" class="wp-caption-text">面团擀成18×18 ㎝的大小。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 在模具内铺烘焙纸。将模具放在面团上确认大小，然后把面团卷在擀面棒上，铺入模具，用手指按压底部和侧面的接缝，让面团贴合模具。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13012792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012792" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012792-4-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012792-4-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012792" class="wp-caption-text">在模具内铺烘焙纸。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13012793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012793" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012793-4-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012793-4-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012793" class="wp-caption-text">把面团卷在擀面棒上，铺入模具。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13012794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012794" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012794-4-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012794-4-3-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012794" class="wp-caption-text">用手指按压底部和侧面的接缝，让面团贴合模具。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 用叉子在整个底部戳洞。以180℃的烤箱烤约20 分钟，直到表面稍微产生焦痕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13012795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012795" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012795-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012795-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012795" class="wp-caption-text">用叉子在整个底部戳洞。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 在锅中放入C，开中火煮到起泡后，再一边搅拌、一边继续加热约1分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13012796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012796" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012796-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012796-6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012796" class="wp-caption-text">在锅中放入C<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E9%A5%B4.md#1">米饴</a>、枫糖浆、无添加豆浆、植物油），开中火煮到起泡后。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 在6.中加入芝麻佛罗伦丁脆饼或椰子佛罗伦丁脆饼的材料，混合均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13012800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012800" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012800-7-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012800-7-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012800" class="wp-caption-text">加入芝麻佛罗伦丁脆饼的材料，混合均匀。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13012801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012801" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012801-7-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012801-7-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012801" class="wp-caption-text">加入椰子佛罗伦丁脆饼材料，混合均匀。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 将7.放入5. 中抹平。以180℃的烤箱烤15～20 分钟，直到整体呈现金黄色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13012802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012802" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012802-8-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012802-8-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012802" class="wp-caption-text">内馅放入模具内。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13012803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13012803" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012803-8-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13012803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id13012803-8-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13012803" class="wp-caption-text">馅料必须抹平。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
