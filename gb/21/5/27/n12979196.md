<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>家门前的天鹅湖与萌鹅宝～一起来看天鹅有多美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979298-20210527_swan_helen-chou_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>恩爱的天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>离家5分钟的一个湖湾近几年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E9%B9%85.md#1">天鹅</a>数目逐年增长，成了名副其实的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E9%B9%85%E6%B9%96.md#1">天鹅湖</a>。春暖花开，冰雪融化后，一对对的天鹅就在湖面上嬉戏，约会，再一起养儿育女。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979234-20210527_swan_helen-chou_02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979234-20210527_swan_helen-chou_02-600x431.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979234" class="wp-caption-text">疣鼻<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E9%B9%85.md#1">天鹅</a>(Mute swan)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们这里常见的天鹅叫疣鼻天鹅（Mute swan），因其前额有一块瘤疣的突起而得名。其实按着英文名字叫哑天鹅更确切，因为传说中的疣鼻天鹅不会发声。但显然这传说有误。我常常听到它们发出怪叫的。</p>
<p>还有两种常见的天鹅，一种叫黑嘴天鹅（Trumpeter swan），另一种叫小天鹅（Tundra swan）。</p>
<p>疣鼻天鹅是天鹅中体型最大的一种。一般体长125-170厘米，翅膀张开可达200-240厘米，体重可达15公斤。它们颈部细长，羽毛洁白，体态优雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979238" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979238-20210527_swan_helen-chou_03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979238-20210527_swan_helen-chou_03-600x401.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979238" class="wp-caption-text">长头颈可绕成S型的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12979249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979249" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979249-20210527_swan_helen-chou_04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979249" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979249-20210527_swan_helen-chou_04-600x396.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979249" class="wp-caption-text">展翅击水的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12979253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979253-20210527_swan_helen-chou_05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979253-20210527_swan_helen-chou_05-600x451.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979253" class="wp-caption-text">秀羽毛的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大概它们也知道自己长的美，所以常常会在我面前各种晒羽毛，各种摆拍。有时还要冲我抛媚眼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979255" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979255-20210527_swan_helen-chou_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12979255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979255-20210527_swan_helen-chou_06-450x607.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="450" b="607" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979255" class="wp-caption-text">拗造型摆拍的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12979258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979258-20210527_swan_helen-chou_07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979258-20210527_swan_helen-chou_07-600x369.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979258" class="wp-caption-text">湖面上嬉戏的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12979260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979260-20210527_swan_helen-chou_08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979260-20210527_swan_helen-chou_08-600x378.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979260" class="wp-caption-text">湖面上嬉戏的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一般青少年精力旺盛，喜欢打打闹闹。与人类相似，在湖面上嬉戏的多是尚未成年的青少年天鹅，这从它们的羽毛及缘的颜色可以识别。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979269" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979269-20210527_swan_helen-chou_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979269-20210527_swan_helen-chou_09-600x413.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979269" class="wp-caption-text">恋爱中的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12979272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979272" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979272-20210527_swan_helen-chou_10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979272-20210527_swan_helen-chou_10-600x363.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979272" class="wp-caption-text">恋爱中的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而3-5岁的疣鼻天鹅就可以谈恋爱成家了。多数天鹅一旦求偶成功，终生为伴，所以它们也成为了美好的爱情与忠贞不二的象征。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979275" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979275-20210527_swan_helen-chou_11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12979275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979275-20210527_swan_helen-chou_11-450x579.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="450" b="579" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979275" class="wp-caption-text">恋爱中的疣鼻天鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>热恋中的天鹅几乎形影不离，交颈依偎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979278" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979278-20210527_swan_helen-chou_12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12979278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979278-20210527_swan_helen-chou_12-450x600.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979278" class="wp-caption-text">比比谁的头颈长。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这一对像不像电影“史密斯夫妇”（Mr. and Mrs. Smith）?</p>
<p>成家后夫妻俩一起动手，在水边筑巢。随后就有了爱情的结晶。一窝4-8个鹅蛋鹅爸鹅妈一起孵，5个星期后鹅宝宝就出世了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979279-20210527_swan_helen-chou_13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12979279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979279-20210527_swan_helen-chou_13-450x623.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="450" b="623" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979279" class="wp-caption-text">驮在鹅妈背上的鹅宝宝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>通常到5月中下旬就可以在湖面上看到新生的萌鹅宝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979283" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979283-20210527_swan_helen-chou_14.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12979283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979283-20210527_swan_helen-chou_14.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="602" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979283" class="wp-caption-text">萌萌的鹅宝宝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安徒生的“丑小鸭”大家都读过吧。我怎么觉得鹅宝宝一点也不丑呢？当然跟鹅爸鹅妈的颜值还不能比。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979287-20210527_swan_helen-chou_15.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12979287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979287-20210527_swan_helen-chou_15-600x830.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="450" b="623" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979287" class="wp-caption-text">在鹅妈背上捉迷藏的鹅宝宝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12979290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979290-20210527_swan_helen-chou_16.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979290-20210527_swan_helen-chou_16-600x425.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979290" class="wp-caption-text">躲在鹅妈背上看世界。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>渐渐长大的鹅宝宝们可以自己入水觅食了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12979296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12979296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979296-20210527_swan_helen-chou_18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12979296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12979296-20210527_swan_helen-chou_18-600x410.jpg" alt="天鹅" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12979296" class="wp-caption-text">跟着爸妈去春游。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
