<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>张震为戏减12公斤 林晖闵挑战超难角色</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050346252122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（左起）林晖闵、导演程伟豪、张震、监制唐在扬。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年01月05日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）电影《缉魂》5日举办正式预告发布会，导演程伟豪率领男主角张震、新生代演员林晖闵首度亮相。张震为戏激瘦12公斤，苦笑表示一度好像食物中毒，呕吐又拉肚子；林晖闵则表示：“这是我演过最难演的角色！”</p>
<p>张震为了诠释癌末的检察官，在开拍前3个月狂吃沙拉，并且运动降肌降脂，从74公斤减到61.5公斤，“有一整个月都在吃沙拉，那时候比较虚弱怕冷，容易感冒！”他笑说：“本来减了10公斤，但导演的表情好像不太满意，所以又继续减下去。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12668631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12668631" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050346102122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-12668631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050346102122-ss1.jpg" alt="张震" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12668631" class="wp-caption-text">张震为戏激瘦12公斤。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12668635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12668635" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050345572122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-12668635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050345572122-ss1.jpg" alt="张震" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12668635" class="wp-caption-text">张震出席记者会现场。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有一场戏是张震在车内突然癫痫发作，全身抽蓄且大翻白眼。谈及“如何揣摩发作症状？”他指着导演程伟豪笑称：“跟他拍戏就会一直翻白眼！”</p>
<p>这次张震在片中与张钧甯饰演夫妻，两人多年前曾合作过，再续前缘仍有默契。两人在办案时不仅是追拿歹徒的搭档，回家也是伴侣。</p>
<p>《缉魂》电影情节烧脑、探讨人性情感，开创了台湾犯罪惊悚电影的新格局。林晖闵在片中饰演信奉魔道的富二代，第一次定装就花了11个小时，加上拍摄时正值冬天，他的角色几乎需要天天裸上身上阵，且每次拍摄前都要花费5至8小时在身上画满符咒的刺青，让他直叹：“这是我演过最难演的角色！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12668638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12668638" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050346002122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-12668638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050346002122-ss1.jpg" alt="林晖闵" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12668638" class="wp-caption-text">林晖闵第一次定装就花了11个小时。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12668639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12668639" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050346032122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-12668639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101050346032122-ss1.jpg" alt="林晖闵" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12668639" class="wp-caption-text">林晖闵出席记者会现场。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
