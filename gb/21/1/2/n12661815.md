<h6>中华民国国旗飘扬在麻州牛顿市的侨教中心外面。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年01月02日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨牛顿市报导）2021年1月1日，天气寒冷而晴朗。驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处在牛顿市(Newton)的侨教中心举办新年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%87%E6%97%97.md#1">升旗</a>仪式，与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8B%B1%E5%B4%99.md#1">纽英崙</a>侨领、嘉宾共同庆贺<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%B0%91%E5%9B%BD.md#1">中华民国</a>开国110年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12661826" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661826" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-10NewYear.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-10NewYear-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661826" class="wp-caption-text">飘扬在晴空中的青天白日满地红旗。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12661836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661836" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-2NewYear2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-2NewYear2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661836" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们向<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%B0%91%E5%9B%BD.md#1">中华民国</a>国旗致敬。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上午9点，来宾们聚集在海德社区中心(Hyde Community Center)的侨教中心室外唱中华民国国歌。随后，青天白日满地红旗在国旗歌声中冉冉上升。音乐声结束时，有侨胞高呼“中华民国万岁”，祝愿中华民国国运昌隆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12661819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661819" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-3NewYear2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-3NewYear2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661819" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长孙俭元(右)向波克莱台商会代表郭兢儒赠送侨委会奖牌。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12661847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661847" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-4NewYear2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-4NewYear2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661847" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长孙俭元致词。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>经文处处长孙俭元致词说，由于麻州的防疫政策，今年只邀请了20余位嘉宾(遵照麻州规定集会人数内)到现场参与庆祝。其他人则观看直播视频。“无论参与的方式为何，大家支持及爱护中华民国台湾的心是一致的。谨代表中华民国政府，向各位致上最诚挚的敬意和谢意。”孙俭元说。</p>
<p>孙俭元夫妇在2020年7月抵达波士顿，表示感受到侨界的热情和温暖。孙俭元说，去年虽然由于疫情而无法正常举办10月庆典，但仍看到中华总会和其他侨胞们在波士顿市政府广场<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%87%E6%97%97.md#1">升旗</a>，在中国城沿街悬挂青天白日满地红旗。“再次展现了波士顿和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8B%B1%E5%B4%99.md#1">纽英崙</a>侨界对国家的坚定支持，我们非常感动和感谢。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12661821" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661821" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-5NewYear.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-5NewYear-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661821" class="wp-caption-text">海德社区中心主任、前牛顿市议员莱斯(John Rice)。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>海德社区中心主任、前牛顿市议员莱斯(John Rice)表示，他担任市议员10年，见证了牛顿市和台湾、侨教中心和海德社区中心之间的良好关系。“每一年5月，这里的‘台湾日’活动都越来越盛大。希望今年的‘台湾日’能举办，尽管可能会推迟，不一定在5月。我们美好的台湾花园每年都变得越来越漂亮。”莱斯说。</p>
<p>当天到场的嘉宾还包括：侨务委员梅锡锐、侨务委员蒋宗壬、纽英崙中华公所主席郑慧民夫妇、全美安良工商会总理陈仕维、波士顿洪门致公堂主席余丽媖、国民党波士顿分部常委张韵兰、纽英崙广东同乡总会主席雷国辉、纽英崙阮氏公所主席阮鸿灿、纽英崙李氏公所主席李奇舜、波士顿港澳之友社主席谢中之、侨务咨询委员陈家骅、侨务顾问张昆等等。</p>
<p>孙俭元还向波克莱台商会代表郭兢儒赠送侨委会银质奖牌，感谢该商会参与“一千万个口罩，一千万个爱心”活动，热心为本地区捐赠口罩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12661822" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-6NewYear.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-6NewYear-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661822" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所主席郑慧民。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑慧民说，很高兴参与这个隆重的活动，与各位乡亲见面。他期盼，新一年能与经文处继续合作，让中华公所参与到更多社区公益活动之中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12661825" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661825" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-9NewYear.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661825" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-9NewYear-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661825" class="wp-caption-text">全美安良工商会总理陈仕维。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈仕维向来宾们致以新年祝福，期盼疫情能够缓解，让大家顺利举办更多活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12661824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-8NewYear.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-8NewYear-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661824" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员蒋宗壬。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>蒋宗壬说，他作为侨务委员的任期即将结束，因而期盼能在今年卸任之前最后一次筹划牛顿“台湾日”活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12661823" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12661823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-7NewYear.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12661823" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-1-7NewYear-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12661823" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员梅锡锐。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>龙凤酒楼老板梅锡锐为来宾们带来了餐点。他笑言，被疫情困了这么久之后，很想去旅行。“旅行去什么地方呢？最想去中华民国台湾。”他说。◇</p>
