<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【简单做蛋糕卷】砂糖鸡蛋糕 松软甜香卡滋口感</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/5e4a584311f08754a906874abad0790d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>古早味的砂糖鸡蛋糕卷。（图／日日幸福提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：台式古早味的鸡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>，是很多人小时候的回忆。扎实松软的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>体，有浓浓的蛋奶香，外面包裹着酥脆口感的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A0%82%E7%B3%96.md#1">砂糖</a>，吃起来卡滋卡滋的作响，融合著柔软的蛋糕，纯粹朴实美味好吃。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">古早味<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A0%82%E7%B3%96.md#1">砂糖</a>卷卷</span></h2>
<p>蓬松柔软，最纯粹的蛋香与奶香，古早味最能呈现这样简单的幸福。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>
<p>39.5cm，长2卷</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>体</span></h3>
<p><strong>A 蛋 黄 面 糊</strong></p>
<p>沙拉油－35g<br />
水－35g<br />
细砂糖－7g<br />
低筋面粉－57g<br />
玉米粉－5g<br />
泡打粉－1g<br />
蛋 黄 －77g</p>
<p><strong>B 蛋 白 霜</strong></p>
<p>蛋白－150g<br />
细砂糖－60g</p>
<h3>内馅</h3>
<p><strong>奶油霜</strong></p>
<p>无盐奶油－150g<br />
（室温软化） 细砂糖－30g</p>
<p><strong>装饰</strong></p>
<p>细砂糖－适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将沙拉油、水与细砂糖放入钢盆中拌匀。<br />
2. 加入过筛的低筋面粉、玉米粉与泡打粉搅拌均匀。<br />
3. 再加入蛋黄拌匀即为蛋黄面糊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724533" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/63be225be99cec1ef70f1b3a5f585008.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/63be225be99cec1ef70f1b3a5f585008-450x298.jpg" alt="鸡蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724533" class="wp-caption-text">蛋黄面糊。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 另取一锅，将蛋白放入钢盆中，打至粗泡沫。<br />
5. 分次加入细砂糖。<br />
6. 将蛋白打至勾起有鸟嘴状，即为蛋白霜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/c22d100d76f605e84a1a00936885e5cf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/c22d100d76f605e84a1a00936885e5cf-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724534" class="wp-caption-text">蛋白打至勾起有鸟嘴状，即为蛋白霜。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 取1/3份量的蛋白霜加入蛋黄糊中拌匀。<br />
8. 再倒回剩余蛋白霜中，用刮刀划“J”字形搅拌均匀。<br />
9. 将面糊倒入铺有白报纸烤盘中。<br />
10. 用软刮板将面糊表面轻轻抹平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1bb3856fc1d6ac49cd6349786bbc6fb3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1bb3856fc1d6ac49cd6349786bbc6fb3-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724535" class="wp-caption-text">用软刮板将面糊表面轻轻抹平。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 烤箱预热后，烤时，盘轻敲一下，再将烤盘放入上火 190℃，下火170℃ 的烤箱中烘烤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7727b5c83000175146cb44a5d5dda4e0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7727b5c83000175146cb44a5d5dda4e0-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724536" class="wp-caption-text">烤箱预热后，烤时，盘轻敲一下，再将烤盘放入烤箱中烘烤。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 烘烤至12分钟时，取出烤盘调头，再放回烤箱。<br />
13. 烘烤约6分钟后取出，重摔一下烤盘释出热气。<br />
14. 迅速脱模撕除四边白报纸，放置网架上冷却备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/0628e746052923db1163874b8da15de1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/0628e746052923db1163874b8da15de1-450x276.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="276" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724537" class="wp-caption-text">迅速脱模撕除四边白报纸，放置网架上冷却备用。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>15. 将奶油霜所有材料放入钢盆中打发泛白后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/539f562000acdcca7758058751b0ccef.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/539f562000acdcca7758058751b0ccef-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724538" class="wp-caption-text">将奶油霜所有材料放入钢盆中打发泛白后备用。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>16. 先覆盖一张新白报纸，翻面将蛋糕纸膜撕除。<br />
17. 再将蛋糕对切。<br />
18. 先取其中一片，抹上薄薄的奶油霜馅。<br />
19. 然后在长边先轻划三刀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7ab6f06ebacc1f2cf02a3671dfa1296a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7ab6f06ebacc1f2cf02a3671dfa1296a-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724539" class="wp-caption-text">在长边先轻划三刀。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>20. 在前端的白报纸底下，用湿抹布擦一下防滑。<br />
21. 接着将擀面棍置于底部白报纸下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724540" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/22304fc353bc5006a9da338728543344.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/22304fc353bc5006a9da338728543344-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724540" class="wp-caption-text">将擀面棍置于底部白报纸下（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>22. 双手轻轻卷起白报纸。<br />
23. 直到将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>卷好。<br />
24. 稍微用力推回擀面棍，让蛋糕卷更紧实。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724541" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/561f6a7427439d09e0010ccd5b696293.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/561f6a7427439d09e0010ccd5b696293-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724541" class="wp-caption-text">稍微用力推回擀面棍，让蛋糕卷更紧实。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>25. 用双手整理一下蛋糕卷。<br />
26. 两端扭紧，放入冰箱冷藏定型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724542" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/da74899fa449807919d92fae85b87f9d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/da74899fa449807919d92fae85b87f9d-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724542" class="wp-caption-text">两端扭紧，放入冰箱冷藏定型。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>27. 将蛋糕卷在砂糖上滚几圈即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12724543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1c570f7ef658fd4dae54f9daf6881d90.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1c570f7ef658fd4dae54f9daf6881d90-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12724543" class="wp-caption-text">蛋糕卷在砂糖上滚几圈即可。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
