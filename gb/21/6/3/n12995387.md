<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>用再制布料打环保诉求 “BTS”代言休闲品牌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995432-2106030213181487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“BTS”防弹少年团最近与意大利某运动休闲品牌合作，推出联名系列。（FILA／公关提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年06月03日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）“BTS”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%BC%B9%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%9B%A2.md#1">防弹少年团</a>最近与某知名意大利运动休闲品牌合作，为响应6月5日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83%E6%97%A5.md#1">世界环境日</a>，推出“BACK TO NATURE”联名系列，呼吁ARMY（粉丝）与消费者采取积极的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E4%BF%9D.md#1">环保</a>行动，以独木成林的合作力量共同保护地球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12995435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12995435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995435-2106030226331487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12995435" title="BTS,防弹少年团,Jung Kook" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995435-2106030226331487-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS,防弹少年团,Jung Kook" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12995435" class="wp-caption-text">“BTS”防弹少年Jung Kook。（FILA／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该品牌诉求爱护地球环境，在原料上使用100%废料回收的再制布料，具有凉感效果。今（3）日公开了BTS<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%BC%B9%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%9B%A2.md#1">防弹少年团</a>为品牌最新拍摄的一组运动时尚形象照，让七个大男孩各自展现不同风格的穿搭。</p>
<p>其中一款，由废弃宝特瓶PET制成，除了凉感、快干效果，更获得OEKO-TEX?国际<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E4%BF%9D.md#1">环保</a>纺织协会的人类与生态环境无害认证。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12995440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12995440" style="width: 458px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995440-2106030201111487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12995440" title="BTS,防弹少年团,v" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995440-2106030201111487.jpg" alt="BTS,防弹少年团,v" width="458" b="641" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12995440" class="wp-caption-text">“BTS”防弹少年团V。（FILA／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该系列的颜色与款式设计，连结Newtro（新复古风）与回归自然的概念，带入土壤、树木、蓝天、白云等元素，并与BTS防弹少年团创造专属意义的紫色融合。</p>
<p>其中代表性的服装是一款T-Shirt，背面有BTS防弹少年团七个大男孩保护地球的插画家手绘图。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12995441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12995441" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995441-2106030201061487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12995441" title="BTS,防弹少年团 RM" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995441-2106030201061487-600x600.jpg" alt="BTS,防弹少年团 RM" width="452" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12995441" class="wp-caption-text">“BTS”防弹少年RM。（FILA／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12995471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12995471" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995471-2106030236271487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12995471" title="BTS,防弹少年团,Jin" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995471-2106030236271487.jpg" alt="BTS,防弹少年团,Jin" width="453" b="634" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12995471" class="wp-caption-text">“BTS”防弹少年Jin。（FILA／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不只有服饰，鞋履使用柔软的编织鞋面，配件则主推信差包和托特包。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12995444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12995444" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995444-2106030201031487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12995444" title="BTS,防弹少年团 Jimin" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995444-2106030201031487-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS,防弹少年团 Jimin" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12995444" class="wp-caption-text">“BTS”防弹少年Jimin。（FILA／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12995446" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12995446" style="width: 468px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995446-2106030201011487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12995446" title="BTS,防弹少年团 j-hope" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995446-2106030201011487.jpg" alt="BTS,防弹少年团 j-hope" width="468" b="655" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12995446" class="wp-caption-text">“BTS”防弹少年团j-hope。（FILA／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12995448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12995448" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995448-2106030200481487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12995448" title="BTS,防弹少年团,SUGA" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12995448-2106030200481487-600x600.jpg" alt="BTS,防弹少年团,SUGA" width="453" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12995448" class="wp-caption-text">“BTS”防弹少年团SUGA。（FILA／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该品牌系列期望透过100%废料回收、再生制造的理念，达成人与自然的和平连结，为时尚与环保之间找到平衡。</p>
