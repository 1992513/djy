<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩国首尔最大端午庆典 祭礼传统表演祈平安</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014922-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>永登浦文化院院长韩天熙（音）担任祭礼的初献官在祭拜神灵，祈求护佑国泰民安。（李裕贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年06月11日讯】（大纪元记者安景韩国报导）6月11日上午9点，在韩国民族四大传统节日之一的端午即将来临之际，第18届<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%96%E5%B0%94%E6%B0%B8%E7%99%BB%E6%B5%A6.md#1">首尔永登浦</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%AF%E5%8D%88%E5%BA%86%E5%85%B8.md#1">端午庆典</a>在永登浦公园圆形广场中央舞台正式开启。</p>
<p>永登浦区厅长蔡铉一、永登浦文化院院长韩天熙（音）及十余名国会议员、市议员地区议会议长及区议员出席了当日的庆典。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13014924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13014924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014924-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13014924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014924-2-600x345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13014924" class="wp-caption-text">出席“第18届永登浦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%AF%E5%8D%88%E5%BA%86%E5%85%B8.md#1">端午庆典</a>”的各级议员在祭拜神灵，祈求护佑<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%B3%B0%E6%B0%91%E5%AE%89.md#1">国泰民安</a>。（李裕贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在此次的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%96%E5%B0%94%E6%B0%B8%E7%99%BB%E6%B5%A6.md#1">首尔永登浦</a>区的端午庆典上，当地首先举行了向神灵祈求护佑<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%B3%B0%E6%B0%91%E5%AE%89.md#1">国泰民安</a>的祭礼。之后，准备了多项极具民俗韵味的传统表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13014925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13014925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014925-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13014925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014925-3-600x360.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13014925" class="wp-caption-text">庆典上演欢快的农乐队演出。（李裕贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13014927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13014927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014927-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13014927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014927-5-600x355.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13014927" class="wp-caption-text">韩国非物质文化遗产——北青狮子舞表演。（李裕贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了再现岁时风俗，继承<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E6%B0%91%E6%97%8F.md#1">韩民族</a>悠久的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">传统文化</a>，永登浦区自2003年起每年都举办端午庆典，并逐渐发展成首尔地区最大的端午庆典，仅在去年疫情爆发时考虑到政府的防疫措施临时取消。</p>
<p>主办本次活动的永登浦文化院院长韩天熙（音）在致辞中表示，“如今虽然疫情仍未撤退，但考虑到延续民族固有的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">传统文化</a>的重要性，今年的端午庆典虽然规模与往届相比有所缩小，未能上演菖蒲水洗发、荡秋千、摔跤、手工制扇等民俗活动和游戏，但祭礼与舞台表演仍如期举行。”</p>
<p>韩天熙院长和蔡铉一区厅长担任本次祭礼的初献官和亚献官，向天地神明祈愿永登浦区民安康和当地的蓬勃发展。祭礼结束后，舞台依次上演了欢快的农乐队演出、伽倻琴演奏和被选为韩国非物质文化遗产的北青狮子舞，传达对当地居民的祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13014926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13014926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014926-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13014926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014926-4-600x348.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13014926" class="wp-caption-text">韩国传统乐器伽倻琴演奏。（李裕贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13014928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13014928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014928-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13014928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13014928-6-600x365.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13014928" class="wp-caption-text">北青狮子舞演员与观众互动。（李裕贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
