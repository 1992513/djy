<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>打造芳香小花园 9种香氛植物别错过</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13049451-shutterstock_1032591361-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香氛植物让庭院充满花香，无论休憩或阅读都能增添浪漫氛围。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>花是上天的宠儿，有娇艳的外貌使人驻足，芬芳的花香让人流连，承载传递一份份的情谊，活跃于古今中外的优美诗篇中；而与生俱来的芳香更为她们增添浪漫的风情！</p>
<p>想像在慵懒的假日午后，砌一壶茶、带一本书，坐在庭院小花园的摇椅上，感受暖阳照拂。吐纳间尽是微风所带来花朵的清香，是不是放松又惬意呢？又或者偶而选择在露天小花园，享受与三五好友相聚的午茶时光，置身花香四溢的美景中，肯定洋溢满满的幸福！</p>
<p>花的芬芳不但为户外空间打造优雅梦幻的氛围，无形中也舒缓紧绷的情绪，拉近人心之间的距离，如果你也着迷于满溢的花香，想打造专属自己的小花园，就一起来看看有哪些香气绿植能列入栽种清单吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047196" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047196-shutterstock_224534080.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047196-shutterstock_224534080-600x400.jpg" alt="大花曼陀罗" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047196" class="wp-caption-text">大花曼陀罗（Brugmansia）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>1.大花曼陀罗（Brugmansia）</h2>
<p>喇吧状垂吊的花型让她们拥有“天使的号角(angel’s trumpet)”美称，不仅花期长，盛放时节更可见百花齐放的美景，就像优雅的交响乐团吹奏着赞颂大自然的圣洁曲调，而在晚间微风轻拂下，还能散发清香的气味，但值得注意大花曼陀罗虽美却有毒性，因此建议种植于通风良好的户外庭院，作为景观装饰绿植。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047198" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047198-shutterstock_1226288293.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047198-shutterstock_1226288293-600x391.jpg" alt="茉莉花" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047198" class="wp-caption-text">茉莉花（Jasmine）。（Shutterstock）（Jasmine）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2.茉莉花（Jasmine）</h2>
<p>“虽无艳态压群目，幸有浓香压九秋。”茉莉花的清香淡雅自古以来就被文人墨客所传颂赞叹，没有玫瑰的张扬娇艳，也不似牡丹华丽雍容，但她们莹白的花瓣清丽素雅，像是一群冰清玉洁的花仙子降落人间，低调又脱俗，浓郁的花香更为她们增添迷人的风采，从古传唱至今依旧魅力不减！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047201-shutterstock_1410774533.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047201-shutterstock_1410774533-600x400.jpg" alt="鸡蛋花" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047201" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋花（Plumeria）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3.鸡蛋花（Plumeria）</h2>
<p>鸡蛋花喜欢温暖且日照充足的环境。洁白的花瓣中央有鲜黄颜色点缀，沐浴于阳光中显得朝气蓬勃。她们姿态优雅、色调活泼，仿佛蕴含无穷的希望而盛放，且散发极香的气味，带来舒缓放松的感受。倘若想打造生机盎然的小花园又不失高雅芬芳氛围的话，鸡蛋花想来是一个不错的选择哦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047208" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047208-shutterstock_1970213845.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047208-shutterstock_1970213845-600x401.jpg" alt="金银花" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047208" class="wp-caption-text">金银花（Honeysuckle）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>4.金银花（Honeysuckle）</h2>
<p>金银花即为“忍冬”，是一种适应力很强的花卉，能抵抗严寒，在多种土质都能生长。她们不畏艰钜环境依然绝美绽放的风骨，更被文人们推崇赞扬为“花之高士者”；且花型极美，几条细长花蕊探出花瓣，特殊的型态别有风韵。此外，也不能忽略她们盛放时散发的阵阵幽香，在外貌、香气、姿态都如此完美的金银花，怎么可能不令人着迷呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047209" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047209-shutterstock_1988420531.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047209-shutterstock_1988420531-600x450.jpg" alt="栀子花" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047209" class="wp-caption-text">栀子花（Gardenia）。(Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>5.栀子花（Gardenia）</h2>
<p>谈到香氛植物，那绝不能错过浪漫的栀子花！她们象征永恒的爱情，纯白花瓣层层叠叠，点缀在油绿的叶片中，散发沁人心脾的香气，不仅是园艺景观花卉的绝佳选择，也常见于婚礼花圈，见证过无数动人的爱情宣誓。想为小花园营造唯美诗意的气氛，就将栀子花纳入选择清单吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047212-shutterstock_607255886.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047212-shutterstock_607255886-600x397.jpg" alt="飘香藤" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047212" class="wp-caption-text">飘香藤（Mandevilla）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>6.飘香藤（Mandevilla）</h2>
<p>很显然的，飘香藤因她们的香气而得名，桃红鲜艳的花办盛放时显得艳丽又自信，如同身着舞衣的舞者，挺立身姿尽情展现自己的美，更拥有着“热带的藤本皇后”美誉，与生俱来的攀缘性让她们成为园艺景观植物的热门选择，不论是围篱、窗台，亦或栽植于盆栽作为庭园装饰都非常适合！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047216-shutterstock_1662322435.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047216-shutterstock_1662322435-600x400.jpg" alt="丁香花" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047216" class="wp-caption-text">丁香花（Syringa）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>7.丁香花（Syringa）</h2>
<p>有着“天国之花”美名的丁香花香气高贵浓郁，纯净的白丁香显得纯真美好、淡雅的紫丁香则感觉温柔可人，她们花瓣小而密集，喜欢暖阳和肥沃排水性佳的土质，娇弱的外貌之下包裹着耐寒耐旱的韧性，秀美的花型和浓烈的香气让她们成为不少人的心头好，你是不是也为她们的纯真娇柔所倾心呢？</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047220" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047220-shutterstock_1946840977.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047220-shutterstock_1946840977-600x350.jpg" alt="天蓝绣球" width="600" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047220" class="wp-caption-text">天蓝绣球（Phlox）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>8. 天蓝绣球（Phlox）</h2>
<p>天蓝绣球又名福禄考。绽放时花朵多而且茂密生长呈球状，颜色缤纷又香气四溢，像是欢快的小精灵以花香热烈欢迎蝴蝶来访，充分展现欣欣向荣、花团锦簇的生机。若栽植于小花园中，想必能带来满满的活力与蓬勃的生命力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13047225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13047225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047225-shutterstock_764614135.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13047225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13047225-shutterstock_764614135-600x400.jpg" alt="薄荷" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13047225" class="wp-caption-text">薄荷（Mentha）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>9. 薄荷（Mentha）</h2>
<p>提到薄荷，想必能立刻想到他们清新的气味。在花园里栽种薄荷不仅能增添绿意与美感，清爽的气息让人感受心旷神怡，淡淡清香与小巧翠绿的外观非常适合居家种植和庭园美化！</p>
