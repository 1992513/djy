<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>卡城Symon by Partners样板房6月26日开张</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13048002-20200624-Partners-Development-Group-Ltd-Symon-by-Partners-townhouse-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Symon by Partners 连排镇屋。（Partners Development Group Ltd提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月26日讯】亚省著名房地产公司Partners Development Group最新项目──卡尔加里Symon by Partners连排镇屋项目正在Evanston社区兴建中，样板房将于6月26日周六开张。周末两日签购Waterton镇屋者将获得$2,500优惠。</p>
<p>如今加拿大房市火爆，卡城也不例外，可谓“一房难求”。Symon by Partners位于卡城西北部Evanston社区，计划建造99栋连排镇屋，包括Hudson和Waterton两种户型，面积分别为1435平方英尺和989平方英尺，起价$27万。</p>
<p>Evanston属成熟社区，各种生活和娱乐设施齐全，包括公园绿地、散步小径、杂货店、咖啡馆、餐馆、学校等，靠近Stoney Trail，交通方便。距丰山大统华超市仅11分钟车程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13048009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13048009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13048009-20200624-Partners-Development-Group-Ltd-Symon-by-Partners-school-02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13048009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13048009-20200624-Partners-Development-Group-Ltd-Symon-by-Partners-school-02-600x352.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13048009" class="wp-caption-text">Evanston社区学校。（Partners Development Group Ltd提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>样板房于6月26日周六中午开张。在周末两日签约购买Waterton户型镇屋者将获得$2,500优惠。</p>
<p><strong>Symon by Partners 样板房</strong></p>
<p>地址：Evanscrest Court NW, Calgary<br />
时间：周一 ~ 周三，2:00pm-8:00pm<br />
周末 ~ 节假日，12:00pm-5:00pm<br />
电话：587.755.1166<br />
