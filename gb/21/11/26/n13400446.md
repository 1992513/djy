<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蛤虾高汤食谱 简单食材熬煮出浓郁海味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400764-P79-2-594x400.png" />
<h6>简单食材就能熬煮出鲜甜的高汤。（山岳文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>小编语</strong>：气温骤降，喝一碗热汤，暖了身子也暖了胃，真得好幸福！但是要如何熬制美味的汤品，萃取出天然食材的精华呢？精进厨艺的秘诀就在“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E6%B1%A4.md#1">高汤</a>”的制作。经由日本高汤研究家、料理研究家和医师共同研究，在家就能轻松制作出简单高汤，这一款海味（蛤虾）食谱相当适合喜爱鲜香甜的汤头又想温补暖身的人。</span></p>
<h3><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E6%B1%A4.md#1">高汤</a>专家亲自指导！<br />
在家就能做的超简单<span style="color: #000080;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%A4%E8%99%BE%E9%AB%98%E6%B1%A4.md#1">蛤虾高汤</a>食谱</span></h3>
<p>以蛤蜊与带壳虾为主食材，精心熬制的奢华万能高汤。再加入奶油跟白酒拌炒，能增加高汤的浓郁口感与风味。多了这道简单程序，就是一道极具深度的美味高汤。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">食材（完成后的分量约1.3 公升）</span></h3>
<p>蛤蜊（已吐沙※） ??400公克<br />
虾子（去头／带壳） ?100公克（约10只）<br />
无盐奶油 ???? 20公克<br />
白酒 ???? 4大匙<br />
水 ???? 1.5公升</p>
<p><strong>※ 将蛤蜊放在拖盘上，倒入与海水相同浓度的盐水后，再铺上报纸，放入冰箱冷藏半天以上。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13400744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400744" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400744-f0d42b1c995eca55f8784fc8fc444d44.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13400744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400744-f0d42b1c995eca55f8784fc8fc444d44-450x321.png" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400744" class="wp-caption-text">准备蛤蜊、虾子、无盐奶油和白酒、水。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">制作方式</span></h3>
<p>1.蛤蜊连壳清洗干净，虾子则用清水稍微冲洗后擦干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13400741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400741" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400741-d714c57e369e4c995fe52b396f54a3c9.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13400741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400741-d714c57e369e4c995fe52b396f54a3c9-450x428.png" alt="" width="450" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400741" class="wp-caption-text">蛤蜊连壳清洗干净。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>Point</strong></span>：没有带壳虾，也可用30公克的虾米取代。在步骤③时倒入即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13400743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400743" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400743-0b2da75b6194b32e80da3a886a4e77fd.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13400743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400743-0b2da75b6194b32e80da3a886a4e77fd-450x338.png" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400743" class="wp-caption-text">没有带壳虾，也可用30公克的虾米取代。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将奶油放入锅中开火，待溶化后再倒入蛤蜊跟虾子拌炒。虾子变色后，即可倒入白酒并加盖蒸煮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13400762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400762" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400762-P79-1.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13400762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400762-P79-1-450x657.png" alt="" width="450" b="657" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400762" class="wp-caption-text">虾子变色后，即可倒入白酒并加盖蒸煮。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蛤蜊打开后，倒入食材里的水。熬煮时别忘了将浮渣捞干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13400764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400764" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400764-P79-2.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13400764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400764-P79-2-450x661.png" alt="" width="450" b="661" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400764" class="wp-caption-text">熬煮时别忘了将浮渣捞干净。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将步骤③的食材以筛网过滤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13400765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400765" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400765-P79-3.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13400765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400765-P79-3-450x643.png" alt="" width="450" b="643" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400765" class="wp-caption-text">食材以筛网过滤。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #000080;"> Point</span></strong>：以奶油拌炒，可增加油脂，让高汤味道更甘甜。使用无盐奶油，则是因蛤蜊会释放咸味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13400767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400767-P79-4.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13400767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400767-P79-4-450x653.png" alt="" width="450" b="653" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400767" class="wp-caption-text">以奶油拌炒，可增加油脂，让高汤味道更甘甜。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #000080;">Point</span></strong>：熬煮时间太长，会释出独特的臭味。因此，煮滚后要立刻起锅。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">保存</span></h3>
<p><strong>冷藏3天、冷冻约1个月。</strong>使用保鲜盒、保鲜夹链袋保存。熬完高汤的蛤蜊、虾子，则可作为汤品配料或其他菜色。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13400779" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400779" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400779-P79-5.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13400779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400779-P79-5-450x458.png" alt="" width="450" b="458" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400779" class="wp-caption-text">使用保鲜盒、保鲜夹链袋保存高汤；冷藏3天、冷冻约1个月。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
