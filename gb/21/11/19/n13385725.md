<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：2021年洛杉矶国际车展——电动车时代</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385784-IMG90-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2021年度洛杉矶国际车展于11月19日开幕，其中Subaru展厅最花心思，布置的像丛林，背景是一个180度的弧形萤幕，地面也是由LED灯组成的萤幕，令人犹如置身场景之中。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者李奥洛杉矶报导）经历一年的寂静，各式活动又开始展开。2021年度洛杉矶国际车展是首个重新开放的北美大型车展，于11月19日到11月28日，在洛杉矶市中心会议展览中心开放予公众参观，媒体则可在11月17和18日一睹为快。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">相比于2019年，本届车展的新车发表数量明显有所不及，但这次厂商集中于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%A8%E8%BD%A6.md#1">电动车</a>市场，同时也可以看到不少初创电动车厂，各显神通。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">车展首先由NACTOY总裁Gary?Witzenburg宣布2022年度最佳汽车入选名单，入选汽车分为三个类别：<br />
最佳轿车?– Honda Civic、Lucid Air及?Volkswagen Golf R / GTI<br />
最佳卡车?– Ford Maverick、Hyundai Santa Cruz及Rivian?RIT<br />
最佳多功能车?– Ford Bronco、Genesis GV70及Hyundai IONIQ 5</p>
<figure id="attachment_13385742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385742-NACTOY_Finalist-e1637301868851.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385742-NACTOY_Finalist-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385742" class="wp-caption-text">NACTOY宣布2022年度最佳汽车入选名单。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13385744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385744" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385744-NACTOY-e1637301905249.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385744-NACTOY-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385744" class="wp-caption-text">NACTOY入选车之一的?Volkswagen Golf R / GTI。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">之后由<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%A8%E8%BD%A6.md#1">电动车</a>厂Fisker拉开序幕，发表新车Ocean，Ocean分为Sport、Ultra及Exteme三版本，入门Sport版售价由37,499美元起跳，但马力只有275hp，续航力也只有250英里；最高版续航力350英里以上，拥有双马达及550hp马力，而0-60mph加速只需3.6秒，售价却高达68,999美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13385735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385735" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385735-Fisker_Ocean-e1637301672432.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385735-Fisker_Ocean-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385735" class="wp-caption-text">Fisker Ocean。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Hyundai也展出了SEVEN电动概念车，SEVEN车前后装上了数十个LED灯，最特别是对向式车门，内部车顶部分除了是玻璃，也是一个大显示屏，后排座椅是一张“L”形沙发，前方两座椅均可转动，形成一个流动会议室，也是一个客厅。SEVEN驾驶席并不设方向盘，只有一支可收纳的操控杆。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385736-Hyundai_SEVEN-e1637301682979.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385736-Hyundai_SEVEN-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>Hyundai SEVEN概念车。（李奥／大纪元）</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Subaru展厅最花心思，布置的像丛林，背景是一个180度的弧形萤幕，地面也是由LED灯组成的萤幕，令人犹如置身场景之中。Subaru发表了2023 Subaru?Solterra电动车，由背景萤幕中驶出，Solterra拥有Subaru独家Symmetrical AWD系统，离地距8.3吋，并配有X-Mode，Solterra就如其它Subaru汽车一般可应付不同的越野环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13385746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385746-Subaru_Solterra-e1637301964543.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385746-Subaru_Solterra-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385746" class="wp-caption-text">Subaru Solterra。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Kia一次过介绍了两款新车，分别为2023年款Sportage Hybrid，以及电动概念车EV9。Sportage Hybrid装有226hp的涡轮Hybrid引擎，耗油量达到39mpg，最长行车距离500英里。EV9概念车外形方正时尚，使用E-GMP平台，低重心而又有足够空间装入三排座椅，续航力为300英里，使用350kW充电机可在20分钟由10%充至80%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13385739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385739" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385739-Kia_Sportage-e1637301778116.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385739-Kia_Sportage-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385739" class="wp-caption-text">Kia Sportage Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13385738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385738-Kia_EV9-e1637301750134.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385738-Kia_EV9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385738" class="wp-caption-text">Kia EV9概念车。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">另一电动车初创公司Mullen发表了电动SUV，名为FIVE，FIVE将会有两型号，售价由55,000至75,000美元，为了令消费者对他们的产品有信心，厂方将提供60个月保修，电池另有10年保用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13385741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385741-Mullen_FIVE-e1637301832193.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385741-Mullen_FIVE-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385741" class="wp-caption-text">Mullen FIVE。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Canoo的Lifestyle电动货车，车型细小，同样具有对开门及U形沙发，内部空间大，既可载货又可载人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13385732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385732-Canoo_Lifestyle-e1637301638163.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385732-Canoo_Lifestyle-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385732" class="wp-caption-text">（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">?越南品牌VinFast则发表了VF e35和VF e36两款全新电动豪华SUV车款，将于2022年上半年开始接受预订。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13385775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13385775" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385775-Vinfast-e1637302343735.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13385775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13385775-Vinfast-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13385775" class="wp-caption-text">Vinfast。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
