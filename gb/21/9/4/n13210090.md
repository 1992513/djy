<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：一亿像素特制相机拍出的精美雪花照</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210259-Snowflake-Triptych-we-5copy-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美国摄影师米尔沃尔德（Nathan Myhrvold）通过自制的一台1亿像素微距相机，拍摄出分辨率极高的雪花照，清晰展示了它的惊奇结构。（Nathan Myhrvold/Modernist Cuisine Gallery, LLC提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年09月04日讯】（英文大纪元记者Michael Wing报导／张雨霏编译）作为自然界的奇观之一，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%B1.md#1">雪花</a>有着极其精密复杂的结晶结构，可是仅通过肉眼无法窥其根本，而美国一位<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>则通过自制的一台一亿像素微距<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B8%E6%9C%BA.md#1">相机</a>，拍摄出分辨率极高的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%B1%E7%85%A7.md#1">雪花照</a>，清晰展示了它的惊奇结构。</p>
<p>居住在华盛顿州西雅图的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>内森?米尔沃尔德（Nathan Myhrvold）爱好非常广泛，包括科学、烹饪、艺术，当然还有摄影。他对很多平凡的事物都有想尽力展现其不平凡一面的好奇心。</p>
<p>“在我11岁的时候，我购买了属于自己的第一台<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B8%E6%9C%BA.md#1">相机</a>，当时我在旧货店找到蔡司公司的一台Contax II相机，从那时起就一直在拍照”，米尔沃尔德告诉《大纪元时报》，“在我的脑海里，我一直酝酿着能拍到微观<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%B1.md#1">雪花</a>照片的想法。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13210260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13210260" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210260-No-Two-Alike-1-1200x900.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13210260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210260-No-Two-Alike-1-1200x900.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13210260" class="wp-caption-text">（Nathan Myhrvold/Modernist Cuisine Gallery, LLC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13210261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13210261" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210261-Snowflake-Triptych-1-1200x1200.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13210261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210261-Snowflake-Triptych-1-1200x1200.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13210261" class="wp-caption-text">（Nathan Myhrvold/Modernist Cuisine Gallery, LLC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“大约两年前，我认为时机成熟了，于是决定组装一台最先进的雪花摄影器材……但这比我想像的要困难得多。”</p>
<p>“我花了18个月的时间设计和打造了这台特制雪花相机，这样我就能以相当高的清晰度拍摄到雪花晶体的精细结构”，米尔沃尔德说。</p>
<p>为了更好地记录雪花的完整形状，他在由碳纤维制成的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%BE%E5%BE%AE%E9%95%9C.md#1">显微镜</a>的物镜上加入了Phase One的背照式传感器（一亿像素），并研发了特殊光路，能捕获比大多数显微照片更大、更清晰的图像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13210262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13210262" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210262-Snowflake-Triptych-2-1200x1200.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13210262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210262-Snowflake-Triptych-2-1200x1200.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13210262" class="wp-caption-text">（Nathan Myhrvold/Modernist Cuisine Gallery, LLC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13210263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13210263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210263-Ice-Queen-1200x900.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13210263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210263-Ice-Queen-1200x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13210263" class="wp-caption-text">（Nathan Myhrvold/Modernist Cuisine Gallery, LLC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为解决雪花融化的问题，他还在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%BE%E5%BE%AE%E9%95%9C.md#1">显微镜</a>上加入热电冷却系统，防止雪花过快蒸发，还配备了工业级短脉冲高速LED灯，能有效减低拍摄时散发的热力。</p>
<p>只有数毫米大小的雪花易碎易融化，其尖端结构在短时间内便会消失，拍摄时必须仔细考虑时长、温度、天气条件、设备等因素。这位摄影师发现拍摄雪花的最佳温度介于零下15至零下20摄氏度之间。</p>
<p>因此，为了拍摄出唯美的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%B1%E7%85%A7.md#1">雪花照</a>，米尔沃尔德需要前往阿拉斯加或加拿大等温度适宜的地区进行拍摄。</p>
<p>“所以，我把相机带到了阿拉斯加的费尔班克斯（Fairbanks），以及加拿大安大略省的蒂明斯（Timmons, Ontario）和该国西北地区的黄刀镇（Yellowknife）”，他解释道，“我将捕捉到的雪花放在泡沫夹芯板上，然后将它们放到安置在我们所住酒店阳台外面的摄像机上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13210266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13210266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210266-NPM_catching_snowflakes-1200x675.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13210266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210266-NPM_catching_snowflakes-1200x675.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13210266" class="wp-caption-text">（3ric Johanson/Modernist Cuisine Gallery, LLC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>照片显示，他拍摄到的雪花花样繁多，每一片雪花都像一幅精美的艺术品，而且所有照片显示确实没有两片雪花是完全相同的。</p>
<p>米尔沃尔德在他位于西雅图、拉霍亚、拉斯维加斯和新奥尔良的摄影画廊中都张贴了这些令人惊叹的清晰雪晶照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13210264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13210264" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210264-Snowflake-Triptych-3-1200x1200.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13210264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13210264-Snowflake-Triptych-3-1200x1200.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13210264" class="wp-caption-text">（Nathan Myhrvold/Modernist Cuisine Gallery, LLC提供）</figcaption></figure>
