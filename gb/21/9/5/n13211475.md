<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超人气烘培老师教绝活 蒜香面包老少皆宜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211546-44094f692038c4d61edff8ad325241a8-393x400.png" />
<h6>柔软香浓的蒜香面包。（采实文化提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【编按】咸甜的蒜香面包向来受到人们欢迎，市面上大多是法式作法，偏向“香酥硬”口感的法国面包。但是，小孩和长辈们因为牙齿的关系，如果有柔软绵密的蒜香面包就更好了！</p>
<p>在烘培界有20年丰富经验的面包师吴介甫听到了大家的心声，所以研发了香浓柔软的蒜香奶油面包。这一款食谱在家手作就能轻松上手，保证让你闻到想到香喷喷的蒜香面包即将出炉时，就已经胃口大开了。
</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000">高钙蒜香面包</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #000080">材料（4个）</span></h3>
<h3>【主面团】</h3>
<p>
高筋面粉 200g<br />
细糖 24g<br />
盐 4g<br />
干酵母 2g<br />
水 90g<br />
鲜奶 40g<br />
汤种 20g<br />
无盐发酵奶油 20g
<h3>【配料】</h3>
起士片 半片<br />
帕马森起士粉 适量<br />
番茄酱 适量
<h3>【蒜味奶油酱】</h3>
无盐发酵奶油 80g<br />
蒜泥 18g<br />
新鲜巴西里叶 14g<br />
盐 1g<br />
糖 4g
<h3>【A 制作面团】</h3>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">1. 制作主面团</span></p>
<p>请参考“<span style="color: #000000"><strong><a style="color: #000000" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/21/8/30/n13197774.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">双倍起司面包</a></strong></span>”步骤1～5的作法。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">2. 分割面团</span></p>
<p>将面团分成80g一份，滚圆后捏起面团底部收合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13211551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211551" style="width: 555px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211551-53f5b9beb59699920c894e736f4d9b8d.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211551-53f5b9beb59699920c894e736f4d9b8d-450x137.png" alt="" width="555" b="169"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211551" class="wp-caption-text">将面团分成80g一份，滚圆后捏起面团底部收合。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">3. 中间发酵</span></p>
<p>将整圆好的小面团放上烤盘并保持距离，室温静置或放入发酵箱（温度30度，湿度75度）发酵40分钟。
<h3>【B整形】</h3>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">4. 杆平面团</span></p>
<p>将面团稍微拉长、擀开，将靠近自己一侧的面团用手指压平固定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13211554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211554" style="width: 536px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211554-6149300d5b803e7adcd8d4497841fc17.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211554-6149300d5b803e7adcd8d4497841fc17-450x142.png" alt="" width="536" b="169"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211554" class="wp-caption-text">面团拉长、擀平 。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13211555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211555" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211555-768e4d6d408b618724bb47e51a526714.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211555-768e4d6d408b618724bb47e51a526714.png" alt="" width="338" b="220"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211555" class="wp-caption-text">面团翻面固定。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">5. 包入馅料</span></p>
<p>放上起司片，从前向后卷成长条，再将两端搓尖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13211559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211559" style="width: 631px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211559-4a7ae15d01a4bad63d957973d40d6634.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211559-4a7ae15d01a4bad63d957973d40d6634-450x95.png" alt="" width="631" b="133"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211559" class="wp-caption-text">放上起司片，从前向后卷成长条，再将两端搓尖。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">6. 整形装饰</span></p>
<p>在面团表面喷上一点水，沾上帕马森起司粉后，用小刀在表面浅浅画出刻痕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13211561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211561" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211561-c7514f730dcea4056231bb2c2181f1e3.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211561-c7514f730dcea4056231bb2c2181f1e3.png" alt="" width="337" b="220"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211561" class="wp-caption-text">在面团表面喷上一点水，沾上帕马森起司粉后。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13211562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211562" style="width: 344px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211562-e972bca27a0ad9346a4e349c4053503c.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211562-e972bca27a0ad9346a4e349c4053503c.png" alt="" width="344" b="217"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211562" class="wp-caption-text">面团表面用小刀在表面浅浅画出刻痕。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">7. 最后发酵</span></p>
<p>将面团放上烤盘，室温静置或放入发酵箱（温度34~36度，湿度75度）最后发酵60～70分钟。
<h3>【C 制作蒜味奶油酱】</h3>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">8. 混合材料</span></p>
<p>巴西里叶打成泥，再将蒜味奶油酱的所有材料均匀混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13211565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211565" style="width: 321px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211565-2659db914989da3253a0ba83ad47865d.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211565-2659db914989da3253a0ba83ad47865d.png" alt="" width="321" b="219"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211565" class="wp-caption-text">蒜味奶油酱制作的所有材料。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13211567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211567" style="width: 332px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211567-1ed7856581a8887e7df639e6d49f2020.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211567-1ed7856581a8887e7df639e6d49f2020.png" alt="" width="332" b="217"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211567" class="wp-caption-text">巴西里叶打成泥，再将蒜味奶油酱的所有材料均匀混合。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080">Tips 没使用完的蒜酱可冷涷保存约90天，使用前退冰至软化即可。</span></p>
<p>【D 烘烤】</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">9. 烤前装饰</span></p>
<p>将蒜味奶油与番茄酱装进塑胶袋里，剪一个小洞，在最后发酵完成的面团上分别挤上蒜味奶油与番茄酱装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13211594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211594" style="width: 531px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211594-cca193c18cd845c96b1bc458c2f78876.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13211594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211594-cca193c18cd845c96b1bc458c2f78876-450x144.png" alt="" width="531" b="170"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211594" class="wp-caption-text">将蒜味奶油与番茄酱装进塑胶袋里，剪一个小洞，在最后发酵完成的面团上分别挤上蒜味奶油与番茄酱装饰。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">10. 烘烤</span></p>
<p>烤箱预热至上火200度，下火200度，烘烤12～14分钟即完成。
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><strong>制作汤种面团</strong></span></h2>
“汤种”对台湾人来说想必是一点都不陌生，街坊的面包店里应该都有贩卖“汤种面包”，吃起来特别柔软美味，可说是风靡一时的热门品项。其实汤种就是用沸水煮过的面种，用汤种法做出来的面包，具有湿润、柔软的口感，并有着良好的保水性，即使放了一段时间，面包依然能保有水分，吃起来有弹性。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800080">Tips：制作好的汤种面团可冷冻保存约一年，使用前再退冰软化。</span>
<h3><span style="color: #000080">材料</span></h3>
高筋面粉50g<br />
细糖 5g<br />
煮沸水 50g
<h3><span style="color: #000080">作法</span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">1. 混合材料</span></p>
<p>将高筋面粉与糖分别测量好并混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13198026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13198026" style="width: 332px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198026-ff523afcc96ff613877988ad0164a1a2.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13198026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198026-ff523afcc96ff613877988ad0164a1a2.png" alt="" width="332" b="226"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13198026" class="wp-caption-text">将高筋面粉与糖分别测量好并混合。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">2. 沸水煮拌</span></p>
<p>将水煮沸后，关火，放入面粉与糖，轻轻快速搅拌至成团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13198029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13198029" style="width: 333px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198029-c1a78526c21def456d54c6c2c78ab798.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13198029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198029-c1a78526c21def456d54c6c2c78ab798.png" alt="" width="333" b="220"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13198029" class="wp-caption-text">将水煮沸后，关火，放入面粉与糖。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13198027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13198027" style="width: 335px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198027-8fa24cee68b44e74e215836da1676be3.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13198027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198027-8fa24cee68b44e74e215836da1676be3.png" alt="" width="335" b="223"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13198027" class="wp-caption-text">轻轻快速搅拌至成团的样子。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff">3. 完成</span></p>
<p>持续搅拌至面团呈光滑状即可。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13198028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13198028" style="width: 363px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198028-4ea967719b79d0689be50771f0f3aed5.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13198028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198028-4ea967719b79d0689be50771f0f3aed5.png" alt="" width="363" b="237"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13198028" class="wp-caption-text">持续搅拌至面团呈光滑状即可。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
