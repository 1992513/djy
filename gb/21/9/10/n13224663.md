<p>“熟”即是熟成：运用配方大量比例先制作发酵种，发酵种熟成的过程是一种酸比过程，藉由酵种所散发出来的发酵香味，促使与新面团发酵熟成，以缩短发酵所耗的时间，最后让产品带着迷人的发酵熟成风味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">1、冷藏中种制作</span></h3>
<p>材料A搅拌至面团表面微光滑，(建议发酵种温度24°C)，放入密封容易，于28°C发酵60分钟，再5°C冷藏12～16小时。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">2、搅拌制程</span></h3>
<p>材料B与发酵种放入搅拌缸，以低速搅拌成团，转中速搅拌至面团表面微光滑，再加入材料C搅拌至完全扩展，面团终温25°C。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13224837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13224837" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13224837-99e9850dd18a17fbf3e969e94c2b168e.png"><img class="size-small wp-image-13224837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13224837-99e9850dd18a17fbf3e969e94c2b168e-300x195.png" alt="" width="300" b="195" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13224837" class="wp-caption-text">搅拌制程。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">3、基本发酵、排气翻面</span></h3>
<p>面团于28°C发酵30分钟，面团压平后翻面。采3折1次法，于28°C继续发酵20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13224839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13224839" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13224839-df0c9d4a08e012b9e2654f254932ab40.png"><img class="size-small wp-image-13224839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13224839-df0c9d4a08e012b9e2654f254932ab40-300x199.png" alt="" width="300" b="199" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13224839" class="wp-caption-text">基本发酵、排气翻面。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">4、分割、中间发酵</span></h3>
<p>发酵好的面团分割成每个250g，共12个，分别折叠后收圆。将分割好的面团于28°C发酵25分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13224842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13224842" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13224842-2a9111488e91cac84b3513fc780625f3.png"><img class="size-small wp-image-13224842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13224842-2a9111488e91cac84b3513fc780625f3-300x215.png" alt="" width="300" b="215" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13224842" class="wp-caption-text">发酵好的面团分割成每个250g，共12个。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">5、整型、最后发酵</span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #993366;">第1 次 擀卷</span></h4>
<p>轻拍面团排出气体后，从中间朝上下擀成厚薄一致的椭圆形，拉正成方形，卷起并收合成长圆柱，松弛5~10分钟。</p>

<p>整型好的面团2个一组，收口朝下放入土司模，共完成6模，于32°C发酵60分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13229308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13229308" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13229308-4a9db1ca5aa32bc38542323774f1cfa4.png"><img class="size-small wp-image-13229308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13229308-4a9db1ca5aa32bc38542323774f1cfa4-300x212.png" alt="" width="300" b="212" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13229308" class="wp-caption-text">整型好的面团2个一组。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h3><span style="color: #993366;">Tips 擀卷面团力道需一致</span></h3>
<p>擀卷面团的力道需要平均控制，较容易擀出厚薄一致的面皮；勿太用力，也不宜时而用力、时而放松，如此会造成每层卷度厚度不同、影响<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E5%9F%B9.md#1">烘培</a>完成的口感。</p>
