<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自制天然手工皂 愈洗愈健康的“泡泡浴芭”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213819-P.222-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>洗去一整天疲劳的入浴剂“星月泡泡浴芭”。如果每天都能按照自己肌肤的状态，搭配手工皂使用的话，就能在不知不觉间发现自己的肌肤明亮又透净。（苹果屋提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【编者按】本文介绍泡泡浴芭（bubble bar）的制作配方。洗去一整天<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%B2%E5%8A%B3.md#1">疲劳</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A5%E6%B5%B4%E5%89%82.md#1">入浴剂</a>，能为浴室转换为不同的气氛。如艺术作品般的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E5%B7%A5%E7%9A%82.md#1">手工皂</a>提案，点亮平凡生活的视觉享受。将美感带入手工皂，让日子充满身心疗愈的小角落。</p>
<p>自然生活设计师试图用“草本、香氛与美感”唤醒现代人对生活的疲乏——因为“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E5%B7%A5%E7%9A%82.md#1">手工皂</a>”的意义不仅仅是洁净身体的商品，更应该是满足自己所需，且能提升生活质感的疗愈体验。如果每天都能按照自己<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%8C%E8%82%A4.md#1">肌肤</a>的状态，搭配手工皂使用的话，就能在不知不觉间发现自己的肌肤明亮又透净。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%9F%E6%9C%88%E6%B3%A1%E6%B3%A1%E6%B5%B4%E8%8A%AD.md#1">星月泡泡浴芭</a></h2>
<p>在家就能轻松做出缤纷色彩的泡泡浴芭！只要混合好喜欢的颜色跟香氛，再揉捏成团，最后用喜爱的模具压出来就完成了。搭配具有滋润<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%8C%E8%82%A4.md#1">肌肤</a>效果的天然油，享受一边泡澡一边洗出柔嫩肌肤的迷人感受。</p>
<h2>材料（70g＊7个）</h2>
<h3>基底</h3>
<p>碳酸氢钠 250g<br />
酸类混合物 100g<br />
玉米粉 30g<br />
SLSA起泡粉 50g<br />
甜杏仁油 20mL<br />
椰子油甜菜碱 50mL</p>
<h3>精油</h3>
<p>柑橘精油 5mL</p>
<h3>色泽添加物</h3>
<p>粉红色食用色素 1滴<br />
蓝色食用色素 1滴</p>
<h2>设计草图</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13213796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213796" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213796-P.223.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13213796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213796-P.223-450x472.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="450" b="472" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213796" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%9F%E6%9C%88%E6%B3%A1%E6%B3%A1%E6%B5%B4%E8%8A%AD.md#1">星月泡泡浴芭</a>的设计草图。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>●粉红色泡泡浴芭团 250g<br />
? 泡泡浴芭团250g、粉红色食用色素1滴<br />
●薄荷色泡泡浴芭团 250g<br />
? 泡泡浴芭团250g、绿色食用色素1滴</p>
<h2>制作基底</h2>
<p>1. 把碳酸氢钠、酸类混合物、玉米粉跟SLSA起泡粉装入碗内并均匀搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213801" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213801-P.224-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213801-P.224-1-300x230.jpg" alt=" 手工皂" width="300" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213801" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭制作过程。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将甜杏仁油、椰子油甜菜碱装到烧杯内量测。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213803" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213803-P.224-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213803-P.224-2-300x280.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213803" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭制作过程。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将柑橘精油放入步骤2中并充分混匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213805" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213805-P.224-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213805-P.224-3-300x279.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213805" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭制作过程。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将步骤3倒入粉状材料的不锈钢盆中均匀搅拌成泡泡浴芭团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213806" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213806-P.224-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213806-P.224-4-300x230.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213806" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭制作过程。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 依据设计草图将泡泡浴芭团分两份，并各自加入食用色素调色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213808" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213808-P.224-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213808-P.224-5-300x230.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213808" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭制作过程。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将两块泡泡浴芭团一起放入盆中，稍微揉成一团（不要混匀）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213810" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213810-P.224-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213810-P.224-6-300x230.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213810" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭制作过程。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>入模</h2>
<p>7. 将泡泡浴芭团捏成高约1.5公分厚的片状，用饼干模具压出造型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213811" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213811-P.225-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213811-P.225-1-300x230.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213811" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭制作过程。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 修整一下泡泡浴芭的边缘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213812" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213812-P.225-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213812-P.225-2-300x232.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="232" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213812" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭制作过程。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 重复步骤7，将剩余的泡泡浴芭团压出形状，如果最后分量不够用模具压制，可以直接手捏成三角形或四角形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213817" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213817" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213817-P.225-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213817-P.225-3-300x229.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="229" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213817" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭模具压制完成。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>保存</h2>
<p>10. 放置干燥约三小时后即可使用，建议密封保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213818" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213818" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213818-P.225-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213818" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213818-P.225-4-300x232.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="232" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213818" class="wp-caption-text">星月泡泡浴芭保存方式。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">tip </span></h3>
<p>如果泡泡浴芭团偏黏，可以在饼干模具上沾植物油或玉米粉再压制。另外，若想让泡泡浴芭的香味更浓郁，可以使用香精。⊙</p>
