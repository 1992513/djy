<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>干燥花草本皂：初学者好上手的透明感手工皂</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213865-P.160-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>初学者简单上手的手工皂：图为金盏花制成的干燥花草本皂。（苹果屋提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【编者按】简单好上手的热制法再制皂，其特点是可以做出透明感的药草<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E5%B7%A5%E7%9A%82.md#1">手工皂</a>，相当适合喜爱植萃气氛的你。“热制法”指的是将皂基加热融化，再加入所需的色泽、香气添加物，接着放置凝固的手工皂制作技巧，是不是非常简单呢！希望在热制手工皂里增添保湿成分或植物油也没问题，完成后的润泽保水度可不会输给冷制皂喔。一起用皂基来轻松做出天然手工皂吧！</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E7%87%A5%E8%8A%B1%E8%8D%89%E6%9C%AC%E7%9A%82.md#1">干燥花草本皂</a></h2>
<p>这是最具代表性的热制法再制皂配方，只要融化透明皂基后再放入干燥花或其他干燥植物，就能为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E5%B7%A5%E7%9A%82.md#1">手工皂</a>带来清新的草本气息。本图使用的是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%9B%8F%E8%8A%B1.md#1">金盏花</a>，也可以放入不同形状的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%93%A3.md#1">花瓣</a>，打造出独特的色系和样貌。</p>
<h2>材料（100g＊4个）</h2>
<h3>皂液</h3>
<p>透明皂基 420g<br />
甘油 4g<br />
山茶花油 4g</p>
<h3><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%BE%E6%B2%B9.md#1">精油</a></h3>
<p>玫瑰天竺葵<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%BE%E6%B2%B9.md#1">精油</a> 4mL</p>
<h3>其他添加物</h3>
<p>干燥花 适量<br />
酒精 适量</p>
<h2>设计草图</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13213835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213835" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213835-P.161.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213835-P.161-300x280.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213835" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E7%87%A5%E8%8A%B1%E8%8D%89%E6%9C%AC%E7%9A%82.md#1">干燥花草本皂</a>的设计草图。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>●皂液 400mL<br />
? 透明皂基400g<br />
●干燥花 适量</p>
<h2>制作皂液</h2>
<p>1. 将透明皂基切丁后隔水加热，约融化70％后关火，用余温融化剩下的皂基完成皂液。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213838" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213838-P.162-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213838-P.162-1-300x300.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213838" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 取量杯与喜欢的干燥花备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213841" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213841-P.162-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213841-P.162-2-300x300.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213841" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 在皂液中加入甘油、山茶花油与精油拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213842" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213842-P.162-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213842-P.162-3-300x415.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213842" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将完成的皂液分成四等份，并分别加入准备好的干燥药草。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213844" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213844-P.162-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213844-P.162-4-300x300.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213844" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">tip </span></h3>
<p>若皂液的温度比较高，放入干燥花后，皂液或干燥花有可能会变色，但并不会影响成皂后的品质，可以安心使用。</p>
<h2>入模</h2>
<p>5. 用酒精消毒模具。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213848" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213848-P.163-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213848-P.163-1-300x231.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213848" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将混合干燥药草的皂液倒入模具。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213851" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213851-P.163-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213851-P.163-2-300x232.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="232" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213851" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 在皂液表层喷一点酒精消除气泡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213853" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213853-P.163-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213853-P.163-3-300x229.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="229" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213853" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 皂液凝固前，可以用木棒调整干燥花至理想的位置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213856" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213856-P.163-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213856-P.163-4-300x230.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213856" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>熟成</h2>
<p>9. 放置干燥两小时即完成，手工皂脱模后建议密封保存。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13213858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13213858" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213858-P.163-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13213858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213858-P.163-5-300x231.jpg" alt="手工皂" width="300" b="231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13213858" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
