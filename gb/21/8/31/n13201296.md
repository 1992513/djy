<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>文协开放日 展示中华民俗艺术</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201280-DSC04025-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>文协开放日，杨信宜指导的古筝演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年09月01日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E9%A1%BF%E5%B8%82.md#1">牛顿市</a>报导）8月29日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">大波士顿中华文化协会</a>（GBCCA）在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E9%A1%BF%E5%B8%82.md#1">牛顿市</a>（Newton）的会所举办开放日活动。老师们和各年龄段的学生在室外表演各种<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">民俗艺术</a>，吸引许多华人和当地居民到场观看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13201281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201281-DSC04028.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201281-DSC04028-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201281" class="wp-caption-text">陈志新指导的击鼓表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201279" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201279-DSC04023.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201279-DSC04023-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201279" class="wp-caption-text">扬琴演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>上午的室外展演包括多种古典和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">民俗艺术</a>节目，都由华人社区知名的老师带领，例如郭雅志指导的葫芦丝演奏，陈志新的击鼓和国乐，杨信宜的古筝，王丽文的独舞等等。文协前会长谈继欣担任主持。牛顿市市长福勒（Ruthanne Fuller）到场致词。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13201275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201275" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201275-DSC04000.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201275-DSC04000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201275" class="wp-caption-text">古典舞蹈表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201273-DSC03980.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201273-DSC03980-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201273" class="wp-caption-text">观众在文协驻地室外观看表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现任会长赖超强说，经过一年半的疫情时期，他一方面很高兴看到文协和其它华人团体保持活跃，另一方面也对Zoom网络会议感到厌烦了。“今天是文协开始快速回归正常活动的日子。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13201271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201271" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201271-DSC03974.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201271-DSC03974-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201271" class="wp-caption-text">郭雅志指导的葫芦丝演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201276-DSC04017.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201276-DSC04017-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201276" class="wp-caption-text">功夫对抗表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为此，文协特此带来一系列的室外演出，以及室内的课程内容展示。值得一提的是，文协会所楼房近日刚刚完成装修，师生们已开始在宽敞明亮的新多功能课室教学。赖超强感谢市长、市政府、各位来宾及各位文协成员的支持。他还说，文协的课程项目不仅招收华人学员，也欢迎各族裔、各种语言背景的人士参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13201282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201282-DSC04046.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201282-DSC04046-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201282" class="wp-caption-text">在文协驻地室内，工作人员带来宾欣赏书法作品。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201270" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201270-DSC03954.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201270-DSC03954-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201270" class="wp-caption-text">活动协调人欧阳东美、主持人谈继欣、牛顿市市长Ruthanne Fuller、文协会长赖超强合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市长福勒祝贺文协完成装修工程，并感谢文协自90年代入驻牛顿市以来，为当地带来独特的文化艺术与活力。</p>
<p>福勒说，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md#1">亚裔</a>在过去一年受到了一些仇恨攻击，但她看到文协成员们依然在善意回馈社会，例如为消防员送餐，支持食品分发等等。她为此感谢大家的奉献。</p>
<p>此外，福勒还谈到今年6月和7月发生的5起<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md#1">亚裔</a>家庭被盗案。她说，市政府快速地举办了网络会议，向居民通报案情；牛顿市警方也正把此案作为首要任务之一来调查。◇</p>
