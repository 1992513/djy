<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>铸铁锅法式舒芙蕾松饼 任谁都难抗拒的美味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466362-P.114-17-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>铸铁锅法式舒芙蕾松饼。顶级法式吐司般湿润绵密的口感，让人忍不住上瘾。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>煎得蓬松柔软的舒芙蕾松饼吸饱了浓厚蛋液，再用铸铁锅烘烤的奢侈甜点。顶级法式吐司般湿润绵密的口感，让人忍不住上瘾。在刚出炉的热腾腾松饼上放一球透心凉的冰淇淋，任谁都无法抗拒！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>制作顺序</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.制作基底面糊<br />
2.制作蛋白霜<br />
3.混合基底面糊和蛋白霜<br />
4.煎松饼<br />
5.制作蛋液&amp;打发鲜奶油<br />
6.烘烤?盛盘</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>材料（直径</strong><strong>20cm</strong><strong>／</strong><strong>1</strong><strong>片／</strong><strong>1</strong><strong>盘）</strong></span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>◎基底面糊</strong></span></h4>
<p>蛋黄 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?1颗<br />
细砂糖① <u>????????????????????????????????????? </u>?13g<br />
鲜奶① <u>????????????????????????????????????? </u>?1大匙<br />
低筋面粉 <u>????????????????????????????????????? </u>?26g<br />
无铝泡打粉 <u>??????????????????????????????????? </u>?1g</p>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>◎蛋白霜</strong></span></h4>
<p>蛋白 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?2颗<br />
细砂糖② <u>????????????????????????????????????? </u>?13g<br />
柠檬汁 <u>????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?2滴<br />
无盐奶油 <u>????????????????????????????????????? </u>?15g</p>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>◎蛋液</strong></span></h4>
<p>鸡蛋 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?1颗<br />
鲜奶② <u>????????????????????????????????????? </u>?100?<br />
鲜奶油① <u>??????????????????????????????????? </u>?50?</p>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>◎打发鲜奶油</strong></span></h4>
<p>鲜奶油② <u>??????????????????????????????????? </u>?50?<br />
细砂糖③ <u>??????????????????????????????????????? </u>?5g<br />
香草精 <u>??????????????????????????????????????? </u>?少许</p>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>◎配料</strong></span></h4>
<p>香草冰淇淋 <u>????????????????????????????????? </u>?50g<br />
蓝莓 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?6颗<br />
覆盆子 <u>????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?4颗<br />
薄荷叶 <u>??????????????????????????????????????? </u>?少许<br />
焦糖酱（市售）<u>??????????????????????? </u> 1大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>前置准备</strong></span></h3>
<p>?蛋黄和蛋白分开，蛋白在使用前都先放在冰箱冷藏备用。<br />
?准备一盆冰水。<br />
?预热好平底锅或电烤盘。<br />
?烤箱预热至200℃。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>?</strong><strong>制作基底面糊</strong></span></h4>
<p>1.在大调理盆中放入蛋黄、细砂糖①、鲜奶①、低筋面粉、无铝泡打粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448839" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448839-P.114-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448839-P.114-1-450x445.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448839" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.用打蛋器充分混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448840" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448840-P.114-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448840-P.114-2-450x480.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448840" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>?</strong><strong>制作蛋白霜</strong></span></h4>
<p>1.在另一个调理盆中放入蛋白、细砂糖②、柠檬汁，并且在底部垫一盆冰水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448841" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448841-P.114-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448841-P.114-3-450x414.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448841" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.用电动搅拌器打发成蛋白霜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448842" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448842-P.114-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448842-P.114-4-450x424.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448842" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>?</strong><strong>混合基底面糊和蛋白霜</strong></span></h4>
<p>1.将打好的蛋白霜一口气倒入基底面糊的盆中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448843" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448843-P.114-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448843-P.114-5-450x475.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="475" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448843" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.用一点力道快速搅拌，避免蛋白霜消泡，只要水分没有分离就OK。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448844" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448844-P.114-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448844-P.114-6-450x445.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448844" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>?</strong><strong>煎松饼</strong></span></h4>
<p>1.确认火力在小火与极小火之间，或测量温度为160～170℃后，放入无盐奶油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448845" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448845-P.114-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448845-P.114-7-450x442.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448845" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.用尖嘴勺或汤勺挖入面糊，上面再加一些面糊堆高。不盖锅盖煎约5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448846" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448846-P.114-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448846-P.114-8-450x471.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="471" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448846" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.用锅铲翻面后，盖锅盖续煎5分钟左右。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448847" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448847-P.114-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448847-P.114-9-450x413.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448847" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>?</strong><strong>制作蛋液</strong><strong>&amp;</strong><strong>打发鲜奶油</strong></span></h4>
<p>1.制作蛋液。在调理盆中放入鸡蛋、鲜奶②、鲜奶油①，充分拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448848" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448848-P.114-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448848-P.114-10-450x270.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448848" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.制作打发鲜奶油。在调理盆中放入鲜奶油②、细砂糖③、香草精，用电动搅拌器打发到举起搅拌棒时，鲜奶油前端有小尖角的程度后，装入装有花嘴的挤花袋中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448849" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448849-P.114-11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448849-P.114-11-450x321.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448849" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>?</strong><strong>烘烤?盛盘</strong></span></h4>
<p>1.将煎好的舒芙蕾松饼放入小铸铁锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448850" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448850-P.114-12.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448850-P.114-12-450x445.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448850" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.倒入蛋液后，稍微静置。待松饼充分吸收蛋液，是让口感更为湿润的关键。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448852" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448852-P.114-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448852-P.114-13-450x475.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="475" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448852" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.放入预热到200℃的烤箱中，以180℃烘烤15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448853" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448853-P.114-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448853-P.114-14-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448853" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.烤到上色后，从烤箱取出静置5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448854" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448854-P.114-15.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13448854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448854-P.114-15-450x415.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448854" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.在松饼上面放一球香草冰淇淋，旁边再挤上打发鲜奶油。</p>
