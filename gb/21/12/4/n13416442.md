<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>给圣诞老人写信</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416568-IMG_3279-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月3日，橙县塔斯汀市第一街邮局举办给圣诞老人写信活动。（李梅／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年12月04日讯】（大纪元记者李梅橙县报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E9%82%AE%E5%B1%80.md#1">美国邮局</a>（USPS）在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%99%E5%8E%BF%E5%A1%94%E6%96%AF%E6%B1%80%E5%B8%82.md#1">橙县塔斯汀市</a>第1街的分局，周五（12月3日）第一次举办<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%99%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%80%81%E4%BA%BA%E5%86%99%E4%BF%A1.md#1">给圣诞老人写信</a>活动，许多家长带孩子们来参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416572" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416572" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416572-IMG_3220.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416572" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416572-IMG_3220-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416572" class="wp-caption-text">孩子们在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%99%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%80%81%E4%BA%BA%E5%86%99%E4%BF%A1.md#1">给圣诞老人写信</a>。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416574-IMG_3216.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416574-IMG_3216-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416574" class="wp-caption-text">孩子们在给圣诞老人写信。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416576-IMG_3187.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416576-IMG_3187-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416576" class="wp-caption-text">在看拿到的画图本的女孩。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们装饰了屋子，孩子们可以在桌上给圣诞老人写信。我们还准备好邮票，然后把信放进寄去<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%9E%81.md#1">北极</a>的邮箱里。”邮局工作人员加文斯（Natashi Garvins）介绍说，“我们非常兴奋，能见证这一激动人心的美妙时刻。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416577-IMG_3201.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416577-IMG_3201-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416577" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞快乐！（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416579-IMG_32062.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416579-IMG_32062-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416579" class="wp-caption-text">孩子们在给圣诞老人写信。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>邮局的工作人员还为孩子们准备了圣诞小糖果和饼干，当孩子们写完信后可以和圣诞“老爷爷、老奶奶”合影。“圣诞老人”很耐心地和每个小朋友交谈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416581-IMG_3209.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416581-IMG_3209-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416581" class="wp-caption-text">家长还带上很小的孩子来参加此活动。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416582-IMG_3245.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416582-IMG_3245-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416582" class="wp-caption-text">和圣诞老人合影。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>居民帕森斯（Amy Parsons）从朋友那里知道的这个消息，她说：“非常好！邮局应该贴个大张的活动通知，很多人都不知道。”</p>
<p>帕森斯有5个孩子，从4岁到10岁，她付出了很多辛苦，但帕森斯说：“虽然累，做妈妈还是非常自豪的。孩子都很懂事，我给他们都布置了任务，大的帮助洗碗、洗衣服，最小的帮助摆放鞋子。”</p>
<p>圣诞节是孩子们开心的日子，拿到白色的印着圣诞老人的信纸，女孩们认真地讨论著怎么写信。小的孩子则是由母亲在旁边指导。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416583-IMG_3254.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416583-IMG_3254-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416583" class="wp-caption-text">把信放在小精灵守着的信箱就可以寄给圣诞老人了。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416585" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416585-IMG_3239.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416585-IMG_3239-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416585" class="wp-caption-text">“这是我写给圣诞老人的信。”（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416586" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416586-IMG_3260.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416586-IMG_3260-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416586" class="wp-caption-text">和圣诞老人合影。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小孩子需要参加一些这样的活动，比如练习写信。有的孩子封好信封后被提醒要按格式写好寄信和收信地址，还有不要忘记贴邮票；出来参加活动也可以多和人交流。家长丽贝卡·洛芙莱斯（Rebecca Lovelace）说：“我们很喜欢邮局，他们乐于助人，孩子们今天也很开心。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416591-IMG_3191-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416591-IMG_3191-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416591" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞老奶奶送的彩色手杖糖。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>邮局会有专人帮助圣诞老人处理这些信件，等待着有人认领，帮助把礼物送到孩子们的家里。◇</p>
