<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>卡城成熟社区Evanston最后的机会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079729-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Hudson-kitchen-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Symon by partners镇屋Hudson户型宽敞明亮的厨房。（建筑商Partners Development Group提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: left;">【大纪元2021年07月10日讯】二十余年来，我们精心构筑的社区让我们生活得更加美好。我们知道，只有当我们有足够的空间休憩、玩耍、欢笑、与所爱之人共度时光时，我们才会生活得更幸福、更健康、更有意义。</p>
<p style="text-align: right;">──Partners Development Group</p>
<p>唐诗有云：“花开堪折直须折，莫待无花空折枝。”春花在枝头怒放的时候，千万要把握时机，否则错过了，徒留遗憾。虽然说的是鲜花与青春，但搁在如今火爆的房市上，又何尝不是如此呢？</p>
<p>位于卡城西北的Evanston社区，近年来已成为炙手可热的地段。不仅面积广、绿地多，且交通便利，Beddington Trail、Stony Trail、14 Street沿区而行，距301公车总站不足10分钟，离飞机场只15分钟车程。购物更是方便，社区内即有三家购物中心：Creekside Shopping Centre、Symon&#8217;s Valley Ranch (Farmer&#8217;s Market) 和Evanston Towne Square；丰山大统华也只数分钟车程之遥。</p>
<p>难怪Symon by partners连排镇屋正在兴建中，已售出近30套。建筑商Partners Development Group地区销售经理罗伯森(Darcey Robertson)介绍，Symon by partners镇屋是Evanston社区的最后一期新屋，之后，该社区将不再开发新地。她鼓励潜在买家，不要错过这最后的机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13079759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13079759" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079759-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Hudson-living-room-03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13079759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079759-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Hudson-living-room-03-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13079759" class="wp-caption-text">Symon by partners镇屋Hudson户型的起居室。（建筑商Partners Development Group提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前镇屋有两种户型可选：Waterton，989平方英尺，$27万余，单加热车库；Hudson，1435平方英尺，$34万余，双加热车库。Waterton户型某些单元并有限时优惠。很快将再有两种户型上市：Sylvan，1175平方英尺；Tahoe，1486平方英尺。多数户型有围篱庭院和大量绿地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13079784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13079784" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079784-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Waterton-living-room-04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13079784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079784-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Waterton-living-room-04-600x348.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13079784" class="wp-caption-text">Symon by partners镇屋Waterton户型的起居室。（建筑商Partners Development Group提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13079788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13079788" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079788-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Waterton-bedroom-05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13079788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079788-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Waterton-bedroom-05-600x364.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="364" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13079788" class="wp-caption-text">Symon by partners镇屋Waterton户型的卧室。（建筑商Partners Development Group提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>户外农场 室内敞亮</h3>
<p>进入样板房，顿觉阳光撒满室内，客厅与卧室落地窗占据整面墙壁，甚至一楼与二楼之间的转角处也开设了长长的窗子。客厅和厨房采取开放设计，更增添敞阔无阻的感觉。</p>
<p>站在阳台前，举目远望，不期然大片绿地绵延伸展开来，不见尽头，近处十余牛只悠闲啃草，间或甩一甩尾巴，颇具“岁月静好”的氛围。镇屋竟与私人农场相邻，也算是意外的惊喜了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13079742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13079742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079742-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Hudson-dinner-room-02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13079742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079742-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Hudson-dinner-room-02-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13079742" class="wp-caption-text">开放设计的餐厅，室内宽敞明亮，室外悠然见牛羊。（建筑商Partners Development Group提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>更健康 更清洁 更节能</h3>
<p>作为采取最新绿色科技的公司项目，Symon by partners获得加拿大Built Green认证，每年可为房主节约数百元的能源花费。</p>
<p>每栋镇屋标准配备HRV（热回收通风系统），每日在门窗紧闭的情况下将全屋通风20次，把可能的室内污染如化学物质、宠物毛发等排出，净化空气，减少毒素和过敏原，降低住户罹患哮喘等过敏性疾病的可能性。同时显着减少室内的漏气、冷点和不同房间存在温度差的问题。</p>
<p>壁挂式热水器不再需要巨大的锅炉提供热水，也不再需要时时保温，维护更加简单高效，想要热水，打开即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13079791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13079791" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079791-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Waterton-bathroom-06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13079791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13079791-20210709-Partners-Development-Group-Waterton-bathroom-06-600x347.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13079791" class="wp-caption-text">Symon by partners镇屋Waterton户型的卫生间。（建筑商Partners Development Group提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所有光源均采LED节能灯，一楼与二楼转角处的厕所和二楼洗衣间可感应人体，自动亮灯。</p>
<p>先进的QuietWall墙面设计将相邻住户隔绝开来，使室内更安静，减少空气与气味的“互相往来”。建筑业界的声音传输标准为50分贝，而QuietWall可达到65分贝，比传统房屋减少了23%的声音传输。</p>
<p>镇屋的外墙摒弃最普通最轻便的乙烯塑料，而采高质纤维水泥板，坚固又耐用，防火等级高，并可将冰雹损害程度降至最小。</p>
<p>怎么样？这枝花，你要不要摘？</p>
