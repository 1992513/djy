<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中共百年 卡城民众中领馆前打幡上香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063934-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会，控诉中共百年罪恶，并为中共送终。（林采枫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年07月02日讯】（大纪元记者林采枫报导）7月1日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%99%BE%E5%B9%B4.md#1">中共百年</a>党庆之际，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会，以送花圈、打招魂幡、坟头上香等形式为中共送终。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13063948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063948-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063948-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-04-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063948" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13063972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063972-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063972-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-07-600x414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063972" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当日下午，约50名来自民主党派、香港之友、新香港文化协会、藏族和维吾尔社区的中西民众在卡尔加里中领馆前集会，现场呼喊口号，高举横幅和标语牌，上书“打倒中共”“中共纳粹”“新闻自由”“光复香港”“停止新疆种族灭绝”等。过往车辆鸣笛声响成一片，以示支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13063945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063945-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063945-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-02-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063945" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13063965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063965-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063965-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-05-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063965" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13063968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063968-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063968-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-06-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063968" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民众并在门前置放嵌有中共党魁照片的白色花圈，白花上洒有点点鲜血；地上铺设红布，摆好水果、面包和冥币，插香点燃；扬起白纸招魂幡，幡上写有“暴政必亡”“共匪完蛋”字样；把Happy Birthday（生日快乐）改成Happy Deathday（忌日快乐）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13063954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063954-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063954-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063954" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13063958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063958" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063958-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063958" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063958-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-08-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063958" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动发言人汤般若表示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%99%BE%E5%B9%B4.md#1">中共百年</a>来坏事做尽，丧尽民心，“渴求自由的人士无不希望它早日灭亡”。</p>
<p>他说，今年未到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%83%E4%B8%80.md#1">七一</a>，中共已完全控制及赤化香港，《苹果日报》被迫关闭，学生课本须以中共思维编写，公务员全部大换血，“港英时代之自由消失，港人之自由生活彻底完结。香港人今日所受之压迫与屈辱，尤甚于日本侵华时期。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13063961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063961" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063961-a4c75226dd40bd60e11548b0db2044cf.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063961-a4c75226dd40bd60e11548b0db2044cf-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063961" class="wp-caption-text">活动发言人汤般若。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但他请港人不要气馁，因为世界各国已看清中共的真面目，其战狼外交、学术剽窃、校园侵蚀、政治渗透、阴谋收买、病毒输出等手段让全球惊醒，“自由世界正对中共联合围攻，与中共正面对垒。”</p>
<p>他坚信，虽然中共撑过了百年，但“必亡无疑，倒台不远”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13063974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13063974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063974-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13063974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13063974-20210701-LINCAIFENG-Communist-Party-100-years-10-600x284.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13063974" class="wp-caption-text">7月1日，卡尔加里部分民众在中领馆前集会。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
