<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗栗县照南非营利幼儿园落成启用典礼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311982-522645-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>县长徐耀昌与嘉宾剪彩，祝贺照南国小非营利幼儿园启用。（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年10月18日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗县府为配合中央推动“扩大幼儿教保公共化计划”政策，积极争取教育部“校园社区化改造计划-营造友善幼儿空间”补助经费，提供优质平价的托育服务。照南非营利幼儿园新建工程落成<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%AF%E7%94%A8%E5%85%B8%E7%A4%BC.md#1">启用典礼</a>，10月18日上午9时隆重举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13311983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311983" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311983-522646.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13311983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311983-522646-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311983" class="wp-caption-text">县长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E8%80%80%E6%98%8C.md#1">徐耀昌</a>感谢明星科大的协助与规划，落实政府的托婴计划。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E8%80%80%E6%98%8C.md#1">徐耀昌</a>表示，竹南镇是苗栗县面积最小行政区域，人口密度却是苗栗县最高，除了有竹南科学园区、苗北艺文中心、竹南头份运动公园等公共建设，还有去年启用的竹南狮山户外大型亲子公园，就为将苗栗县打造成为育儿友善的城市；而照南非营利幼儿园的落成启用，将再度为竹南地区的年轻父母带来满满的育儿教育幸福感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13311995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311995" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311995-522654.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13311995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311995-522654-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311995" class="wp-caption-text">优静的校园。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13311984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311984" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311984-522647.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13311984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311984-522647-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311984" class="wp-caption-text">照南国小太鼓队精彩表演，庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%AF%E7%94%A8%E5%85%B8%E7%A4%BC.md#1">启用典礼</a>。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13311991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311991" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311991-522651.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13311991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311991-522651-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311991" class="wp-caption-text">小儿班上课，环境优雅整洁。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13311992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311992" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311992-522652.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13311992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311992-522652-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311992" class="wp-caption-text">中小班上课。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13311994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311994" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311994-522653.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13311994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311994-522653-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311994" class="wp-caption-text">整洁的厨房。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本案是由周衍均建筑师事务所设计监造，腾骏营造股份有限公司承揽，总经费约3,731万元，设置4班招收120人的非营利幼儿园。建筑物以大地色系为主色调，巧妙融入照南国小周边地景环境中，并与校园内丰富的翠绿植栽辉映成趣，营造温馨典雅的园舍风貌。此外，照南非营利幼儿园最大特色是与国小、国中共用广阔的运动场，资源共享，扩大教育设施使用功效，提供幼儿健康成长的教保环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13312012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13312012" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13312012-522670.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13312012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13312012-522670-450x675.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13312012" class="wp-caption-text">明星科大校长刘国伟感谢县府的信任，让教学并进优质管理。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>照南非营利幼儿园委由“明新科技大学”专业团队经营，以关怀、专业及协力的核心理念，用心经营充满“爱、健康、生活、学习”的教学氛围，希冀在这优质完善的学习环境里，共同创造苗栗幼儿教育崭新愿景。◇</p>
