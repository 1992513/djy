<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>香港9月失业率降至4.5% 中位数家庭月入2.7万元</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13336328-GettyImages-1234206747@1200x1200-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年10月28日讯】</p>
<p class="p1">香港政府统计处今天（<span class="s1">21</span>日）公布最新劳动人口统计临时数字，经季节性调整的<span class="s1">7</span>至<span class="s1">9</span>月统计<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B1%E4%B8%9A%E7%8E%87.md#1">失业率</a>为<span class="s1">4.5%</span>，比上<span class="s1">3</span>个月（即<span class="s1">6</span>至<span class="s1">8</span>月）的数值<span class="s1">4.7%</span>下降了<span class="s1">0.2</span>个百分点，相等于过往<span class="s1">3</span>年的均值<span class="s1">4.5%</span>。就业不足率由<span class="s1">2.2%</span>下降到<span class="s1">2.1%</span>，过往<span class="s1">3</span>年平均亦为<span class="s1">2.2%</span>。</p>
<p class="p1">受疫情冲击严重的板块当中，餐饮服务活动业的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B1%E4%B8%9A%E7%8E%87.md#1">失业率</a>由上月所报的<span class="s1">8.6%</span>回落至<span class="s1">8.5%</span>，零售业由<span class="s1">6.4%</span>回落至<span class="s1">6.3%</span>，至于住宿服务业则由<span class="s1">6.9%</span>回落至<span class="s1">5.9%</span>。此外，金融界失业率最新报<span class="s1">2.8%</span>（上月录<span class="s1">3.0%</span>），而保险及地产分别报<span class="s1">2.4%</span>（<span class="s1">2.4%</span>）与<span class="s1">2.6%</span>（<span class="s1">2.9%</span>）。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13336326-56bf0cb3a0fa9f08d3bdc4d0d7ebf161.png"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-13336326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13336326-56bf0cb3a0fa9f08d3bdc4d0d7ebf161-600x639.png" alt="" width="600" b="639" /></a></p>
<h5 class="p1">香港<span class="s1"><i>2021</i></span>年<span class="s1"><i>9</i></span>月各行业的失业率与上月比较。（来源：香港政府统计处／大纪元制图）</h5>
<p class="p1">本港最新总就业人数录<span class="s1">365.8</span>万人，按月增加<span class="s1">6,300</span>人，而总劳动人口报<span class="s1">383.9</span>万人，环比减少<span class="s1">900</span>人。</p>
<p class="p1">整体失业人数（不经季节性调整）由<span class="s1">18.8</span>万人减少至<span class="s1">18.1</span>万人。中位数家庭每<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E6%94%B6%E5%85%A5.md#1">月收入</a><span class="s1">8</span>月录得<span class="s1">27,000</span>港元，按年上升<span class="s1">400</span>港元。</p>
<p class="p1">展望将来，政府发言人表示：“劳工市场情况将取决于经济复苏的步伐。消费券计划应在短期内继续支持与消费相关的活动。然而，环球疫情的发展仍是经济及劳工市场前景的主要不确定因素。”</p>
<p class="p1">大型招聘会方面，劳工处将于<span class="s1">2021</span>年<span class="s1">11</span>月<span class="s1">25</span>日及<span class="s1">26</span>日在麦花臣场馆举办“中高龄就业新里程招聘会”，约有<span class="s1">50</span>个雇主参与，提供大量不同行业的职位空缺。劳工处在<span class="s1">2021</span>年<span class="s1">7</span>月至<span class="s1">9</span>月平均每月录得<span class="s1">102,742</span>个来自私营机构的空缺，较<span class="s1">6</span>月至<span class="s1">8</span>月的平均数字上升<span class="s1">6.5%</span>，按年上升<span class="s1">68.2%</span>。本次失业率乃在<span class="s1">2021</span>年<span class="s1">7</span>月至<span class="s1">9</span>月期间进行统计调查得出的结果，抽样约<span class="s1">25,000</span>个住户，共约<span class="s1">69,000</span>人。<span class="s1">@</span></p>
<p class="p1">（转自香港大纪元）</p>
