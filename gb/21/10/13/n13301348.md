<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台南牛肉汤时尚升级 “鲜烫玫瑰牛汤”超吸睛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301357-521808-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台南牛肉汤远近驰名，现在更从市场美食升级为东区时髦料理。（小觅秘Little Secret提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年10月13日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）台南牛肉汤远近驰名，现在更从市场美食升级为东区时髦料理。创意面食餐酒馆“小觅秘Little Secret”把人气餐点“鲜烫玫瑰牛汤”打造成华美的玫瑰花造型，上菜还有专人淋汤桌边服务，让视觉与味觉“面面”俱到。</p>
<figure id="13301351" aria-describedby="caption-13301351" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301351-521803-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301351-521803-450x675.jpg" alt="“鲜烫玫瑰牛汤”拥有华美的玫瑰花造型，上菜还有专人淋汤桌边服务。" width="500"/></a><figcaption id="caption-13301351" class="wp-caption-text">“鲜烫玫瑰牛汤”拥有华美的玫瑰花造型，上菜还有专人淋汤桌边服务。（小觅秘Little Secret提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小觅秘Little Secret由女星臧芮轩及台南小觅秘创办人共同成立，从传统面食找寻灵感，鲜烫玫瑰牛汤不仅拥有丰盛的份量、华美的外观，搭配上古早味蒜香干拌面，将台式传统美食提升到新层次。</p>
<figure id="13301358" aria-describedby="caption-13301358" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301358-521809-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301358-521809-450x338.jpg" alt="在网美风格的时髦餐厅享用新颖的台式面食。" width="500"/></a><figcaption id="caption-13301358" class="wp-caption-text">在网美风格的时髦餐厅享用新颖的台式面食。（小觅秘Little Secret提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>隐藏版新品“泰式海鲜干拌面”，集结大白虾、帆立贝、九孔鲍、蛤蛎等海鲜，佐以特制酸辣酱与Q弹波菜面，酸辣开胃。</p>
<p>霸气十足的“雷神战锤慢炖牛膝面”，严选带骨牛小腿肉，添加多种中药与蔬果，耗时8小时熬煮，肉质软嫩，汤头鲜美入味，在秋冬之际来上一碗相当疗愈。</p>
<figure id="13301354" aria-describedby="caption-13301354" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301354-521806-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301354-521806-450x675.jpg" alt="霸气十足的“雷神战锤慢炖牛膝面”，带骨牛小腿肉添加多种中药与蔬果，耗时8小时熬煮而成。" width="500"/></a><figcaption id="caption-13301354" class="wp-caption-text">霸气十足的“雷神战锤慢炖牛膝面”，带骨牛小腿肉添加多种中药与蔬果，耗时8小时熬煮而成。（小觅秘Little Secret提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其他创意面食还有“蒸笼海鲜西瓜绵锅面”，以古早味西瓜绵搭配豪华海鲜；“淘金流沙双拼干拌面”，炸猪排搭配炸虾，淋上金莎酱，口感香气丰富。</p>
<figure id="13301352" aria-describedby="caption-13301352" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301352-521804-450x680.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301352-521804-450x680.jpg" alt="“蒸笼海鲜西瓜绵锅面”，以古早味西瓜绵搭配豪华海鲜。" width="500"/></a><figcaption id="caption-13301352" class="wp-caption-text">“蒸笼海鲜西瓜绵锅面”，以古早味西瓜绵搭配豪华海鲜。（小觅秘Little Secret提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13301353" aria-describedby="caption-13301353" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301353-521805-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301353-521805-450x675.jpg" alt="“淘金流沙双拼干拌面”，将炸猪排搭配炸虾淋上香浓金莎酱。" width="500"/></a><figcaption id="caption-13301353" class="wp-caption-text">“淘金流沙双拼干拌面”，将炸猪排搭配炸虾淋上香浓金莎酱。（小觅秘Little Secret提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，特制调酒以小米酒作基底搭配多元丰富的食材制成，如祖灵之眼、彩虹桥的神话、树屋上的童年等，值得细细品味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13301350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13301350" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301350-521802.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13301350" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301350-521802-600x899.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="598"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13301350" class="wp-caption-text">“彩虹桥的神话”使用独家蜂年祭啤酒及小米酒，以拉茶方式调制，点缀手工水果干。（小觅秘Little Secret提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13301356" aria-describedby="caption-13301356" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301356-521807-450x674.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13301356-521807-450x674.jpg" alt="“祖灵之眼”以小米酒取代兰姆酒，打造台版Mojito。" width="400" b="599"/></a><figcaption id="caption-13301356" class="wp-caption-text">“祖灵之眼”以小米酒取代兰姆酒，打造台版Mojito。（小觅秘Little Secret提供）</figcaption></figure>
