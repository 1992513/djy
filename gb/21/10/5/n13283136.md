<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：蓝绿色“亮片”羽毛的紫冠木蜂鸟</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283180-Woodnymph-Hummingbird-700x420-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蓝绿色“亮片”羽毛的紫冠木蜂鸟。(Ondrej Prosicky/Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月05日讯】（英文大纪元记者Louise Bevan报导／文韵编译）有一种小鸟从全球的三百多种蜂鸟中脱颖而出，因其具有令人眼花缭乱的特征：它那五彩缤纷明亮的蓝绿色<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E7%BF%BC.md#1">羽翼</a>，看起来像一件由亮片制成的斗篷。这颗小鸟翡翠似乎是直接从童话故事中飞出来的。</p>
<p>雄性<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E5%86%A0%E6%9C%A8%E8%9C%82%E9%B8%9F.md#1">紫冠木蜂鸟</a>（Thalurania colombica）的头部、喉咙和胸部呈亮绿色。身体的其余部分呈闪闪发光的深蓝色，并带有深蓝色的叉状<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%BE%E5%B7%B4.md#1">尾巴</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13283197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13283197" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283197-Crowned-Woodnymph-Hummingbird-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13283197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283197-Crowned-Woodnymph-Hummingbird-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13283197" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>雌性鸟的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E7%BF%BC.md#1">羽翼</a>同样美丽但更加柔和，喉咙和胸部呈灰色，腹部有绿色斑点，尾部为烟熏蓝色，尾端为白色<strong>。</strong>成熟的鸟儿体长约10厘米（4英寸），重量仅为4.5克（0.15盎司）。</p>
<p>原产于伯利兹、危地马拉，穿过中美洲，一直到哥伦比亚和秘鲁，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E5%86%A0%E6%9C%A8%E8%9C%82%E9%B8%9F.md#1">紫冠木蜂鸟</a>喜欢潮湿低地森林、亚热带和热带潮湿的山地森林。它们往往在繁殖季节之外迁移到更高的海拔地区，但繁殖期间是这些不“成对结合”的小鸟脱离独处生活的唯一时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13283199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13283199" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283199-shutterstock_1772130809-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13283199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283199-shutterstock_1772130809-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13283199" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>雄性以U形飞行表演引起雌性注意后，与其交配，有时是多只雌鸟，然后由雌鸟以苔藓和植物纤维筑巢并独自抚养雏鸟。</p>
<p>然后，雄鸟会忙于保卫自己的领地，展现恐吓性的飞行抵御“入侵者”，例如大黄蜂和鹰蛾。然而，根据“<ahref="https://www.beautyofbirds.com/greencrownedwoodnymphs.md#1">鸟之美</a>”（Beauty of Birds）网站的说法，勤劳的雌鸟独自管理得很好，甚至用蜘蛛网来弹性化鸟巢的结构，使其随着雏鸟的成长而伸展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13283205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13283205" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283205-shutterstock_432099460-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13283205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283205-shutterstock_432099460-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13283205" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>雌性品种会照顾她的雏鸟约两周，通常每窝有两只，在它们成长过程中以反刍的昆虫喂食以使其获得高蛋白来源。</p>
<p>雌性品种甚至用它的长喙将雏鸟喉咙里的食物直接推到它们的胃里。据了解，一只筑巢的雌鸟每天可以捕获2000只昆虫。</p>
<p>雏鸟在长大到20～26天时离开巢穴。它们会用自己长长的、像稻草一样的舌头，从树木、灌木和草本植物明亮、芳香的花朵中寻找含糖的花蜜，作为它们的主要食物来源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13283209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13283209" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283209-shutterstock_1508040938-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13283209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283209-shutterstock_1508040938-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13283209" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>像其它蜂鸟品种一样，紫冠木蜂鸟是所有鸟类中最小的，它们有能力在空中盘旋觅食。根据“<ahref="https://www.birdsandblooms.com/birding/attracting-hummingbirds/how-fast-do-hummingbirds-fly/">鸟类和花朵</a>”（Birds and Blooms）网站的说法，它们的翅膀在飞翔时发出嗡嗡声——大约每秒50次。</p>
<p>它们对甜花蜜的喜爱也使这些小鸟对植物授粉产生了惊人的好处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13283214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13283214" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283214-shutterstock_1489188656-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13283214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283214-shutterstock_1489188656-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13283214" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>紫冠木蜂鸟作为一个品种，正在茁壮成长。它们在《世界自然保护联盟红色名录》（IUCN Red List）中被列为“无危”（least concern），使它们的蓝绿色“亮片”羽翼有机会在现在和将来都非常耀眼。◇</p>
