<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>刘香慈甜蜜出嫁 赞夫婿EQ高人风趣</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年11月27日讯】曾演出电视剧《<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%85%B5%E6%97%A5%E8%AE%B0.md#1">新兵日记</a>》走红，被封为台湾“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%80%E7%BE%8E%E5%A3%AB%E5%AE%98%E9%95%BF.md#1">最美士官长</a>”的女艺人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E9%A6%99%E6%85%88.md#1">刘香慈</a>，今年初与任职药厂高层的夫婿Vick登记结婚，27日在台北补办婚宴，会前两人着婚纱礼服跟媒体见面并拍照，分享喜悦。不少演艺圈艺人也到场祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8533431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8533431" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270942291487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8533431" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270942291487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8533431" class="wp-caption-text">艺人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E9%A6%99%E6%85%88.md#1">刘香慈</a>（右）与先生Vick（左）27日在台北举办婚宴，会前两人身着婚纱礼服拍照，向媒体分享喜悦。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在刘香慈的脸书上也直播婚宴前两人与媒体见面的画面，可以看到刘香慈身穿低胸白纱礼服挽着夫婿Vick甜蜜亮相受访，两人应媒体要求在镜头前深吻晒恩爱，还分享两人结识的情形，直到两人第三次见面，加上同是台中人的缘分开启话题，男方也顺利要到女方电话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8533434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8533434" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270942241487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8533434" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270942241487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8533434" class="wp-caption-text">艺人刘香慈（右）与先生Vick（左）27日在台北举办婚宴，会前两人身着婚纱礼服拍照，向媒体分享喜悦。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>被问到喜欢夫婿的哪一点，刘香慈表示他的EQ很高，也很幽默；“之前在活动上，看到一个女生一直想灌他酒，就觉得他真的脾气很好。”开始对他有好印象。而Vick也表示，刘香慈不仅人漂亮、对父母孝顺，个性又好，“真的是100分女神”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8533424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8533424" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270943441487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8533424" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270943441487-600x439.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8533424" class="wp-caption-text">艺人刘香慈（右）与先生Vick（左）27日在台北举办婚宴，会前两人身着婚纱礼服向媒体分享喜悦，刘香慈贴心为先生擦汗。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据台媒报导，当晚婚宴上，演艺圈好友除了Gino与刘香慈的主播妹妹刘方慈一起担任主持人，并兼跳开场舞为这场婚礼暖场外，还有方馨、唐美云、雷洪、石英、小娴夫妻、阿翔夫妻和王乐妍等艺人均盛装出席送祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8533438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8533438" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270942341487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8533438" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270942341487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="465" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8533438" class="wp-caption-text">刘香慈的妹妹刘芳慈与艺人Gino一起担任婚宴主持。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8533440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8533440" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270942391487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8533440" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611270942391487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8533440" class="wp-caption-text">由刘香慈（左）的妹妹刘芳慈（右）担任婚礼主持人，两人会前出席接受媒体访问。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
