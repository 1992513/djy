<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《700岁旅程》特映 众资深艺人投保曾遭拒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952211487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《700岁旅程》主要演员（左起）丁强、上官鸣、王满娇、应采灵、唐川、朱陆豪、喜翔。（台视提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年11月09日讯】台视八点档新戏《700岁旅程》，昨（8）日在台北举办文创特映会，主要演员丁强、上官鸣、喜翔、王满娇、朱陆豪、唐川、应采灵等皆盛装出席。现场共聚集百位各界人士一同欣赏，其中还包括许多专业的部落客及当地长者。</p>
<p>荣获台湾文化部103年“高画质电视节目旗舰型连续剧类”补助的台视《700岁旅程》，叙述10位长辈的圆梦故事，全剧网罗了80个台湾美景，拍摄期间接连经历好几个风灾曾撤班快90天，气候状况、场景安排等拍摄过程相当艰辛。</p>
<p>由于要带着一群资深艺人上山下海，制作人陈慧玲相当重视安全问题，她透露因为资深艺人年龄偏高，在事前投保还一度被保险公司拒保，“希望透过这部戏让更多银发族的心声和议题被大家重视，也希望趁现在将台湾最美好的风光留住，带给台湾戏剧更不一样的题材。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477547" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952281487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-8477547" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952281487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="475" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477547" class="wp-caption-text">《700岁旅程》主要演员喜翔。（台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演员喜翔在剧中卖力演出，一场跳溪的戏因为安全考量，前后总共拍了5次才大功告成。在拍摄过程中，资深艺人王满娇还因不慎跌倒撞断牙、咬破嘴，问起当时状况她一派轻松的开玩笑说：“当时缝了好几针，为此幸运得到了一颗‘唇珠’。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477552" style="width: 293px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952051487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-8477552" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952051487.jpg" alt="" width="293" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477552" class="wp-caption-text">《700岁旅程》主要演员王满娇。（台视</figcaption></figure>
<p>全剧以银发族圆梦故事为主轴，也让朱陆豪过足了赛车瘾，他表示：“因为这部戏我第一次到大鹏湾开赛车，虽然辛苦但非常值得、也算圆了自己的梦。”</p>
<p>资深艺人唐川去年脚痛就诊，却意外检查出罹患肝癌一期，所幸及早发现手术治疗，他有感而发地表示：“很多事想做要马上去做、及时行乐，这是我拍了《700岁旅程》后给最大的启发和获得，现在我很珍惜每一刻。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477560" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952111487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-8477560" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952111487.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="490" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477560" class="wp-caption-text">《700岁旅程》主要演员朱陆豪。（台视</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8477563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477563" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952161487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-8477563" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952161487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="464" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477563" class="wp-caption-text">《700岁旅程》主要演员唐川。（台视</figcaption></figure>
<p>文创特映会同时邀请许多专业的部落客一起欣赏，大家也都表示：“看到一部戏将这么多台湾美景真的很令人感动，连自己都有想要去旅游的冲动。”会后大家和制作人及资深艺人们交换心得，对《700岁旅程》这部剧都给予极高的评价。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477566" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090951551487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-8477566" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090951551487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477566" class="wp-caption-text">《700岁旅程》主要演员丁强。（台视</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8477570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477570" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952001487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-8477570" title="" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1611090952001487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477570" class="wp-caption-text">《700岁旅程》主要演员上官鸣。（台视</figcaption></figure>
