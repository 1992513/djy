<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>盘点十二大有助抗癌的超级食物</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年07月11日讯】（大纪元记者王知涵编译报导）癌症是全球最可怕杀手，一年有800万人死于癌症，且人数还在持续上升中，2030年预估癌症死亡人数会攀升到1300万人。虽然大多数人都闻癌色变，但曾有研究发现：三分之一的癌症死亡率是因为人为饮食和行为所造成。</p>
<p>Prevention.com网站自《Eat for Extraordinary Health &amp; Healing》一书摘录了其中12种常见食物。营养价值高，并含维持身体机能的抗氧化物，对于抗癌有非常好的效果。</p>
<p><strong>一．豆类：</strong>一杯豆子提供13克纤维素，是身体每天需要量的一半。豆子也富含蛋白质（一杯约15克蛋白质），还有钙、钾和镁等重要营养素。红豆、斑豆和菜豆都含有很多抗氧化物质。黑豆和绿豆也是很好豆类选择，其抗氧化物质虽没有前三种豆类多，但也很好的蛋白质来源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8012639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8012639" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1508261017282483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8012639 size-medium" title="Green beans, black beans, red beans in wooden spoon, white backg" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1508261017282483-450x288.jpg" alt="Green beans, black beans, red beans in wooden spoon, white backg" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8012639" class="wp-caption-text">豆子也富含蛋白质及钙、钾和镁等重要营养素。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>二．牛奶：</strong>牛奶是钙及维生素D很好的来源。据科学期刊《PLOS ONE》报导，选择有机的或用草喂养牛的牛乳有较好的Omega-3，对心脏健康有帮助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7873485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7873485" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1411261429572483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-7873485 size-medium" title="有机白杏仁奶（fotolia）" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1411261429572483-450x300.jpg" alt="有机白杏仁奶（fotolia）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7873485" class="wp-caption-text">牛奶是钙及维生素D很好的来源。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>植物性乳制品也很有益，例如豆浆是很好的蛋白质来源。虽然大豆的异黄酮（isoflavones, 植物的雌激素）会让人联想到乳癌，但美国营养与饮食协会（Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics）认为乳癌患者也可以安心食用大豆。</p>
<p><strong>三．红萝卜：</strong>一杯切好的生红萝卜只有52卡与12克碳水化合物，其中只有小部分来自天然糖，其余则大多是维生素、矿物质和纤维素等多重营养素。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_5781064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5781064" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/141004173726985.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-5781064 size-medium" title="fresh carrot juice" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/141004173726985-450x300.jpg" alt="fresh carrot juice" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5781064" class="wp-caption-text">胡萝卜富含维生素、矿物质和纤维素等多重营养素。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>四．蜂蜜：</strong>天然蜂蜜好比是甜性治疗，味道可口。一个研究报告发现每天一匙马来西亚蜂蜜（tualang honey）可以改善更年期女性的记忆力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7374625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7374625" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1405231629592551.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-7374625 size-medium" title="fresh honey with lemon and orange fruits" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1405231629592551-450x300.jpg" alt="fresh honey with lemon and orange fruits" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7374625" class="wp-caption-text">蜂蜜可以改善更年期女性的记忆力。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>五．甘蓝菜</strong>：紫甘蓝菜含有多酚（polyphenols）以及非常多抗氧化物，能减少发炎。身体组织、细胞会因为氧化损伤而导致慢性病出现，例如心血管疾病、癌症及衰老，而多酚有非常好的抗氧化功能，能减缓这类情况的发生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6495066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6495066" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1403011245032483-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-6495066 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1403011245032483-450x300.jpg" alt="甘蓝菜的嫩芽可以降低血糖和胆固醇，保护心脏，而且嫩芽正在生长期，精气旺盛，它们的效用比成长后的甘蓝菜高出10倍到30倍。(fotolia)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6495066" class="wp-caption-text">甘蓝菜可以降低血糖和胆固醇，并保护心脏。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>六．苹果：</strong>一个意大利研究发现，每天吃一个或多个中等尺寸苹果，比起每天吃不到一个苹果的人来说，癌症风险相对较低。更详细来说，以下癌症风险均降低：包括口腔和咽头 降低18%、食道降低22%，其他如结肠直肠(30%)、喉头(41%)、胸部(24%)、卵巢(24%)、以及前列腺(7%)等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8086696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8086696" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1511251042162483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8086696 size-medium" title="篮子装满的红苹果（fotolia）" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1511251042162483-450x315.jpg" alt="篮子装满的红苹果（fotolia）" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8086696" class="wp-caption-text">苹果对健康有非常大的助益。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>七．燕麦：</strong>五谷杂粮包括燕麦，会联想到降低低密度胆固醇（LDL cholesterol），减少动脉硬化和心血管疾病危险。燕麦含有纤维素和抗氧化物，也可减少发炎。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_5732688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5732688" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405220924381778.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-5732688 size-small" title="Oatmeal porridge with grapes,banana and honey" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405220924381778-300x450.jpg" alt="Oatmeal porridge with grapes,banana and honey" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5732688" class="wp-caption-text">燕麦含有纤维素和抗氧化物，也可减少发炎。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>八．蓝莓：</strong>蓝莓常常被列于营养食物之首，因为蓝莓有丰富抗氧化物、维生素和纤维。最近的两个研究发现蓝莓能降低血压，这也是归功于其丰富的抗氧化物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5826981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5826981" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/1412171458432483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-5826981 size-medium" title="新鲜浆果（fotolia）" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/1412171458432483-450x319.jpg" alt="新鲜浆果（fotolia）" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5826981" class="wp-caption-text">蓝莓有丰富抗氧化物、维生素和纤维。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>九．大蒜</strong>：2015年一个整合分析，有8621位胃癌患者和14889位正常个体的对照组，发现每天吃大蒜可以降低胃癌，吃比较多大蒜而降低胃癌风险就更明显。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5838600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5838600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/03/150312195331985.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-5838600 size-medium" title="garlic on a wooden board" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/03/150312195331985-450x313.jpg" alt="garlic on a wooden board" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5838600" class="wp-caption-text">大蒜有助降低胃癌风险。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>十．咖啡：</strong>这是咖啡爱好者的福音：咖啡可以抵抗肝癌。2015年期刊《Diet, Nutrition, Physical Activity and Liver Cancer》的一项研究表明，咖啡的保护功效和肝酶（liver enzymes）有关，可减少致癌物质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5803492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803492" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/141213220713985.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-5803492 size-medium" title="Coffee Espresso. Cup Of Coffee" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/141213220713985-450x394.jpg" alt="Coffee Espresso. Cup Of Coffee" width="450" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803492" class="wp-caption-text">咖啡的保护功效和肝酶有关，可减少致癌物质。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>十一．香菇：</strong>塔夫茨大学（Tufts University）研究人员发现白色洋菇在老鼠身上可以抵御病毒和肿瘤、提升免疫系统并杀死不好的细胞。这都是归功于香菇所富含的抗氧化物，能有效预防癌症。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8012658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8012658" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1505121434192483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-8012658 size-small" title="香菇（fotolia）" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1505121434192483-300x451.jpg" alt="香菇（fotolia）" width="300" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8012658" class="wp-caption-text">香菇所富含的抗氧化物，能有效预防癌症。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>十二．樱桃</strong>：美国癌症研究协会（American Institute for Cancer Research）研究显示樱桃果汁可以减少发炎，樱桃所含的纤维素高，能降低直肠癌危险。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5806669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5806669" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/11/1408242137372483.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-5806669 size-medium" title="樱桃在碗里（fotolia）" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/11/1408242137372483-450x303.jpg" alt="樱桃在碗里（fotolia）" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5806669" class="wp-caption-text">樱桃所含的纤维素高，能降低直肠癌危险。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
