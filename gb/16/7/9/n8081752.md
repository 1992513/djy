<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新片《爱宠大机密》 一探宠物秘密</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/88128deb121b686d2664bef1e84202f7.jpg" />
<h6>《爱宠大机密》（The Secret Life of Pets）剧照。（环球影业提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年07月09日讯】<span lang="ZH-TW">（</span><span lang="ZH-TW">大纪元记者苏湘岚好莱坞报导</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">想过</span><span lang="ZH-TW">没？您</span><span lang="ZH-TW">心爱的宠物</span><span lang="ZH-TW">在您</span><span lang="ZH-TW">工作或上学后</span><span lang="ZH-TW">都</span><span lang="ZH-TW">在家</span><span lang="ZH-TW">里</span><span lang="ZH-TW">做什么？新片《爱宠大机密》（</span>The Secret Life of Pets<span lang="ZH-TW">）会告诉你这个秘密。由《神偷奶爸》系列导演克里斯</span>·<span lang="ZH-TW">雷纳德执导</span><span lang="ZH-TW">的影片</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，叙述纽约曼哈顿一栋公寓大楼</span><span lang="ZH-TW">里</span><span lang="ZH-TW">形形色色宠物</span><span lang="ZH-TW">的</span><span lang="ZH-TW">精彩生活。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8081812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8081812" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/d0be2536a4fd536f12537698d152c576-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-8081812" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/d0be2536a4fd536f12537698d152c576-450x300.jpg" alt="《神偷奶爸》剧照(图UIP)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8081812" class="wp-caption-text">《神偷奶爸》剧照(图UIP)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">好莱坞男星凯文</span>·<span lang="ZH-TW">哈特</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（</span>Kevin Hart<span lang="ZH-TW">）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">为电影配音，他说看了电影，觉得该随时带着自己</span><span lang="ZH-TW">的</span><span lang="ZH-TW">宠物：“如果你在乎的话，就不要把宠物独自留在家里。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">制片人表示</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">电影灵感来自小时</span><span lang="ZH-TW">候</span><span lang="ZH-TW">和家人养过的宠物，希望透过电影带给观众温馨、欢笑与感动。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">另一部新片《网聘女伴》（</span>Mike and Dave Need Wedding Dates<span lang="ZH-TW">）故事围绕花花公子兄弟戴夫与麦克</span><span lang="ZH-CN">（</span><span lang="ZH-TW">札克</span>·<span lang="ZH-TW">埃夫隆与亚当</span>·<span lang="ZH-TW">德维尼所饰演</span><span lang="ZH-CN">）将</span><span lang="ZH-TW">参加妹妹在夏威夷的婚礼，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">为</span><span lang="ZH-TW">改</span><span lang="ZH-CN">变</span><span lang="ZH-TW">父母对</span><span lang="ZH-CN">他</span><span lang="ZH-TW">俩搞砸所有家庭聚会的印象，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">两人努力寻找</span><span lang="ZH-TW">完美女伴</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。两兄弟情急之下，一同上网刊登了征伴广告，本以为这个计划万无一失，没想到征来的竟是一对更疯狂的姐妹</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，上演一连串爆笑故事。</span></p>
<p><span class="attrs"><span lang="ZH-TW">好莱坞新一代女星安娜</span>·<span lang="ZH-TW">肯德里克</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（</span></span><span class="im">Anna Kendrick<span lang="ZH-TW">）</span></span><span class="attrs"><span lang="ZH-TW">挑战喜剧，她说相当有趣，尤其是马匹向他们冲来的一幕</span><span lang="ZH-TW">：</span></span><span class="im"><span lang="ZH-TW">“</span></span><span lang="ZH-TW">我们前面设置了木</span><span lang="ZH-TW">制</span><span lang="ZH-TW">的</span>V<span lang="ZH-TW">型障碍，马儿不会直接冲向我们，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">不过</span><span lang="ZH-TW">那种视觉冲击很强烈，当你看到</span>10<span lang="ZH-TW">匹马向你</span><span lang="ZH-TW">迳</span><span lang="ZH-TW">直跑来，然后在最后一秒钟分开到两侧。很好玩，但也很恐怖。”</span><span lang="ZH-TW">◇</span></p>
