<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Gucci纽约新品展示 图案精美来自东方</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年02月05日讯】（大纪元记者臣倩纽约报导）2月4日，Gucci在Bergdorf Goodman店内召开了新品展示会，展示了Gucci2016春夏的设计，令人惊喜的是，展出的设计色彩鲜艳亮丽，图案精美，带有东方神韵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7286570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7286570" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602041730032803-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7286570" class="wp-caption-text">刺绣的图样带有东方的神韵，非常细腻精致。（Craig Barritt/Getty Images for Bergdorf Goodman）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7286571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7286571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602041729552803-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7286571" class="wp-caption-text">撞色搭配的包包尽显优雅。（Craig Barritt/Getty Images for Bergdorf Goodman）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自从去年Gucci有了新的设计师，设计风格开始偏向大胆使用明亮的颜色，并且加进一些全新的花纹图案元素，使得整体的感觉更加华贵，但并不会有任何俗气之感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7286572" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7286572" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602041730002803-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7286572" class="wp-caption-text">Gucci2016年春夏的新设计。（Craig Barritt/Getty Images for Bergdorf Goodman）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7286573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7286573" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602041729572803-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7286573" class="wp-caption-text">使用蝴蝶花朵等图案，使得Gucci的经典背景更添生机。（Craig Barritt/Getty Images for Bergdorf Goodman）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次展出的设计，除了有花鸟的图案设计，还有不同材质的拼接，蕾丝的运用更是独具一格。颜色的搭配给人以温暖之感，各有特色而又相得益彰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7286574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7286574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602041730092803-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7286574" class="wp-caption-text">整体配色温暖华贵。（Craig Barritt/Getty Images for Bergdorf Goodman）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7286575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7286575" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602041729522803-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7286575" class="wp-caption-text">新一季的设计饱含着春日气息。（Craig Barritt/Getty Images for Bergdorf Goodman）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7286576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7286576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1602041730122803-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7286576" class="wp-caption-text">Gucci的设计灵感来自东方。（Craig Barritt/Getty Images for Bergdorf Goodman）</figcaption></figure>
<p>正值中国新年，这次花鸟图案的灵感来源也正是18世纪中国的纺织品，带有繁复绣工及华丽色调的图腾，可见群鸟嬉戏、飞舞的蝴蝶与盛开的牡丹，呈现春暖花开、欣欣向荣的景象，设计师也特别将象征富贵与好运的蓝鹊、锦鸡、蜂鸟、青鸟作为设计元素，期待新春为大家带来好彩头。</p>
