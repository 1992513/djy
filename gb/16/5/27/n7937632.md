<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>GUCCI腕表珠宝中国巡展 李宇春帅气站台</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年05月29日讯】（大纪元记者曾晏均采访报导）世界知名奢侈品牌GUCCI腕表和珠宝巡回展在中国西安隆重登场，知名艺人李宇春获邀担任嘉宾，为 GUCCI站场，当然少不了GUCCI品味，一身蓝色丝质羊毛混纺白色绲边外套，内搭白色领饰衬衫，帅气的黑色长裤及ACE 刺绣装饰球鞋，加上手上配戴的腕表，清丽中隐显出帅气。</p>
<p>这次的展览以“藏宝阁”为主题，近期推出的新品腕表与珠宝系列新作也都在展览中亮相，在瑞士制造完成的腕表臻品，融汇摩登创意设计彰显出色品质与精湛工艺。珠宝首饰每件精品透过陈列展示，恣意演绎着属于自己的故事，也展现GUCCI创意总监Alessandro Michele独特的美学观点。</p>
<p>这些新品包括在时装秀上首次曝光的表款和珠宝首饰，灵感来源竟是汲取生活中形形色色的符号，如蜜蜂、爱心、星星、饰钉和各种动物图案，这些添加了童趣的设计元素，营造出品牌独特风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7937652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7937652" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/e6dac18e93e7696ec2f3a715992f70b4-300x449.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="wp-image-7937652 size-small" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/e6dac18e93e7696ec2f3a715992f70b4-300x449.jpg" alt="李宇春佩戴GUCCI全新Diamantissima系列腕表，清丽中隐显出帅气。（GUCCI）" width="300" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7937652" class="wp-caption-text">李宇春佩戴GUCCI全新Diamantissima系列腕表，清丽中隐显出帅气。（GUCCI）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_7937653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7937653" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/ef1c4d2adb3728bc606d2b0cd508dd0f-450x266.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-7937653 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/ef1c4d2adb3728bc606d2b0cd508dd0f-450x266.jpg" alt="2016新款腕表。（GUCCI）" width="450" b="266" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7937653" class="wp-caption-text">2016新款腕表。（GUCCI）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>GUCCI</strong><strong>腕表 </strong><strong>五种表款展现女性时尚</strong></p>
<p>继享有盛誉的Diamantissima首饰系列后，GUCCI又倾情推出全新女表系列，五款不同表款以低调典雅的精湛工艺展现时尚女性柔美风采。DIAMANTISSIMA系列腕表以不同色彩呈现，白色精钢搭配黑色表盘及黑色小牛皮表带，玫瑰金色表壳色彩百变，蓝宝石水晶玻璃表镜下，四颗内镶的钻石闪闪发光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7937654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7937654" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1-5GG2570-300x322.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-7937654" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1-5GG2570-300x322.jpg" alt="“GG2570”系列腕表。（GUCCI）" width="300" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7937654" class="wp-caption-text">“GG2570”系列腕表。（GUCCI）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>全新的</strong><strong>“GG2570</strong><strong>”</strong><strong>系列腕表</strong></p>
<p>Gucci推出GG2570系列，包括一系列全新的男士和女士腕表，该系列名称中的“25”是GUCCI创意总监Alessandro Michele的幸运数字，“70”则是为了纪念GUCCI的黄金十年——1970年代。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7937655" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7937655" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/758ce977ee406dc7579dd02bf5db6783-450x281.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-7937655 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/758ce977ee406dc7579dd02bf5db6783-450x281.jpg" alt="G-TIMELESS自动腕表。（GUCCI）" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7937655" class="wp-caption-text">G-TIMELESS自动腕表。（GUCCI）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>G-TIMELESS</strong><strong>自动腕表</strong></p>
<p>GUCCI腕表首饰G-Timeless系列优雅又摩登，是GUCCI的旗舰系列之一。蜜蜂和五角星造型融入设计，还加上爱心，整体充满律动童趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7937656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7937656" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/ad857c9902adb5976e5d1089ef6fc4a8-450x447.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-7937656 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/ad857c9902adb5976e5d1089ef6fc4a8-450x447.jpg" alt="LE MARCHé DES MERVEILLES腕表。（GUCCI）" width="450" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7937656" class="wp-caption-text">LE MARCHé DES MERVEILLES腕表。（GUCCI）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>LE MARCHé DES MERVEILLES</strong><strong>腕表</strong></p>
<p>色彩丰富的表盘，黄金色镀层表壳搭配典雅的小牛皮或鳄鱼皮表带，18K金及镶嵌16颗钻石的表款让Le Marché des Merveilles系列简洁而又不墨守成规。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7937658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7937658" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/be8b987f0928d6c53196438ed483e1ca-450x442.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-7937658 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/be8b987f0928d6c53196438ed483e1ca-450x442.jpg" alt="HORSEBIT腕表。（GUCCI）" width="450" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7937658" class="wp-caption-text">HORSEBIT腕表。（GUCCI）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>HORSEBIT</strong><strong>腕表</strong></p>
<p>Gucci经典的马衔扣装饰图案已转变成时尚的腕表设计，运用红色、棕色到砖红色等季节色彩对比鲜明，珍珠贝母表盘镶上钻石，丰富了时尚精品的艺术风貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7937659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7937659" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/848ec53d39f40813ee0e802062612e7c-450x468.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-7937659 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/848ec53d39f40813ee0e802062612e7c-450x468.jpg" alt="INTERLOCKING腕表。（GUCCI）" width="450" b="468" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7937659" class="wp-caption-text">INTERLOCKING腕表。（GUCCI）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>INTERLOCKING</strong><strong>腕表</strong></p>
<p>新款的Interlocking腕表互扣双G，以牡丹红和靛青色衬托双“G”字母的互扣设计，俏皮风格似乎成为今年夏天的点缀。<strong>@*</strong></p>
