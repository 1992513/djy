<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>抢先看！2016纽约圣派翠克节游行盛大登场</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年03月17日讯】（大纪元记者臣倩纽约报导）2016年3月17日上午11时，纽约圣派翠克游行在纽约的第五大道拉开序幕，标志性的绿色带来春天的气息，感染了整个的纽约市。</p>
	<figure id="attachment_7429921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7429921" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603171249182803-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7429921" class="wp-caption-text">2016年纽约圣派翠克大游行。（王姿懿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_7429898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7429898" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603171223522273-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7429898" class="wp-caption-text">人们穿戴绿色，庆祝圣派翠克节。（王姿懿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_7429899" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7429899" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603171223072273-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7429899" class="wp-caption-text">人们穿戴绿色，庆祝圣派翠克节。（王姿懿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_7429892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7429892" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603171143582803-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7429892" class="wp-caption-text">2016年纽约圣派翠克大游行。（王姿懿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>纽约圣派翠克大游行已有255年的历史，这一天，纽约的爱尔兰人都会穿戴绿色，庆祝这个属于爱尔兰的节日。游行每年都会吸引数百万的观众，是纽约历史最悠久的游行。 </p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_7429922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7429922" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603171249252803-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7429922" class="wp-caption-text">2016年纽约圣派翠克大游行。（王姿懿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_7429893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7429893" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603171144012803-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7429893" class="wp-caption-text">2016年纽约圣派翠克大游行。（王姿懿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_7429900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7429900" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603171224102273-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7429900" class="wp-caption-text">人们穿戴绿色，庆祝圣派翠克节。（王姿懿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>3月17号的圣派翠克节是为了纪念爱尔兰的守护者圣派翠克，他用三叶草（shamrock）阐明了三位一体的概念，引导当地人皈依基督教。 绿色也是爱尔兰的标识，逐渐成为圣派翠克游行的特征颜色。美国的爱尔兰人喜欢佩带三叶苜蓿，用爱尔兰的国旗颜色——绿黄两色装饰房间，身穿绿色衣服，并向宾客赠送三叶苜蓿饰物等。</p>
