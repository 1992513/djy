<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2017新年前夕美食攻略</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/01-BOULEVARD_Shrimp-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（图片由BOULEVARD KITCHEN &#038; OYSTER BAR提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年12月24日讯】新年前夕是一年之中最令人期待与兴奋的时刻之一。无论是坐在家中壁炉旁享受2016年最后的几分钟，还是在市中心人们的倒计时声中迎接新年的到来，都寄托着人们对新一年的期盼与希望。为了庆祝这个美好时刻的到来，温哥华的各大餐厅也纷纷推出了新年前夕晚餐，一起来看看吧。</p>
<p><strong>BOULEVARD KITCHEN &amp; OYSTER BAR</strong><br />
<strong>新年午夜倒计时活动</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8627008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8627008" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/01-BOULEVARD_Shrimp-450x261.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8627008 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/01-BOULEVARD_Shrimp-450x261.jpg" alt="（图片由BOULEVARD KITCHEN &amp; OYSTER BAR提供）" width="450" b="261" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8627008" class="wp-caption-text">（图片由BOULEVARD KITCHEN &amp; OYSTER BAR提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8627007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8627007" style="width: 267px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/01-BOULEVARD_Black-Cod-267x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8627007 size-medium_vertical" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/01-BOULEVARD_Black-Cod-267x400.jpg" alt="（图片由BOULEVARD KITCHEN &amp; OYSTER BAR提供）" width="267" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8627007" class="wp-caption-text">（图片由BOULEVARD KITCHEN &amp; OYSTER BAR提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菜单包括当季美味：“松茸法式清汤”（local pine mushroom consommé），“水煮条纹虾与胡桃南瓜”（soft poached side stripe shrimp with butternut squash），“自制黄油龙虾意面”（housemadebucatini with butter poached lobster）和“咸味芝麻棒与焦糖冰淇淋”（salted sesame bar with caramel ice cream）等。晚餐有两个时段可以选择，分别是下午5时至7时的三道菜晚餐与下午8时到10时的四道菜晚餐。前一时段有现场爵士乐伴奏，而后一时段不仅有派对DJ现场打歌，还有午夜倒计时活动。</p>
<p>可网上预订座位<ahref="http://www.boulevardvancouver.ca/">www.boulevardvancouver.ca</a>?或致电604-642-2900</p>
<p><strong>AU COMPTOIR</strong><br />
<strong>西海岸风格的法式大餐</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8626996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8626996" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/02-AuComptoir_BlanquetteDeVeau_CreditAmyHo-e1482541397443.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-8626996" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/02-AuComptoir_BlanquetteDeVeau_CreditAmyHo-e1482541397443.jpg" alt="（图片由AU COMPTOIR提供。摄影：AmyHo）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8626996" class="wp-caption-text">（图片由AU COMPTOIR提供。摄影：AmyHo）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8627009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8627009" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/02-AuComptoir_AssietteDeCharcuterie_CreditAmyHo-e1482541269539.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-8627009" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/02-AuComptoir_AssietteDeCharcuterie_CreditAmyHo-e1482541269539.jpg" alt="（图片由AU COMPTOIR提供。摄影：AmyHo）" width="450" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8627009" class="wp-caption-text">（图片由AU COMPTOIR提供。摄影：AmyHo）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位于Kitsilano社区里的Au Comptoir餐厅将在新年前夜供应西海岸风格的法式大餐，菜单由主厨Dan McGee主持设计。该餐厅提供两个时段，分别从当日下午5时30分至6时15分与下午8时至8时45分。特色美食包括Bavarois De Panes 法式蛋糕，加入欧洲防风草慕斯、苹果、焦糖蜂蜜以及基尔酒调味；秘制烟熏三文鱼（ballotine de saumonfumé），搭配甜菜、辣根和欧洲防风草；香煎黑鳕鱼（le black cod），搭配胡萝卜与莳萝；以及榛子巧克力蛋糕（declinaisonautor du chocolate）等。</p>
<p>请致电604-569-2278订位。</p>
<p><strong>FARMER’S APPRENTICE</strong><br />
<strong>为素食者设计一份菜单</strong></p>

<figure id="attachment_8626997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8626997" style="width: 286px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/03-FarmersApprentice_1103_CreditAndrewQuerner-e1482541379976.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-8626997" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/03-FarmersApprentice_1103_CreditAndrewQuerner-e1482541379976.jpeg" alt="（图片由FARMER’S APPRENTICE提供。摄影：AndrewQuerner）" width="286" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8626997" class="wp-caption-text">（图片由FARMER’S APPRENTICE提供。摄影：AndrewQuerner）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主厨David Gunawan将呈现一场独特的新年前夜美食盛宴。该餐厅提供两个时段，开始时间分别是下午5时30分与下午8时，相应菜单分别是六道菜和七道菜。值得一提的是，该餐厅还特意为素食者提供了相应的菜单，每位价格比非素食者少10元。主厨Gunawan秉持“从农场到餐桌”的哲学，无论是哪种菜单都以取材于本地农场和牧场为先，搭配食物的酒品则以自然和有机为特色。</p>
<p>可致电604-620-2070 或电邮<ahref="mailto:info@farmersapprentice.ca">info@farmersapprentice.ca</a>预订座位</p>

<p>该餐厅的特色菜单包括：蛋黄意大利饺子，使用里考塔、帕玛森干酪和黑松露调味；黑鳕鱼，搭配青口、蛤蜊和其他贝类海鲜；法芙娜巧克力，搭配椰子马卡龙和巧克力甘那许等。该餐厅提供的两套晚餐分别是下午5时30分的四道菜晚餐和下午8时的五道菜晚餐。</p>
<p>请致电604-390-1910订位</p>
