<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>意大利中世纪教堂里流泪的圣母塑像</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09201-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>意大利中世纪教堂中流泪的圣母塑像。（刘佳丽/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年08月14日讯】<span style="line-height: 1.5;">以前</span><span style="line-height: 1.5;">虽然也读过圣母和菩萨塑像流泪的报导，但近日亲眼看到圣母的眼泪时，我</span><span style="line-height: 1.5;">心里</span><span style="line-height: 1.5;">还是不免一震。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8200222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200222" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09201-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200222 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09201-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC09201" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200222" class="wp-caption-text">圣.朱斯蒂娜（CHIESA DI SANTA GIUSTINA）教堂中流泪的圣母塑像。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>意大利北部帕多瓦省的一些小村庄看起来就像是凝固的历史。中世纪时期的城墙依然完美地座落在小山上。在奇塔代拉、蒙塞利切、埃斯特和蒙大拿尼这些保留着中世纪古镇气氛的小城，时间似乎停止了流动。13世纪的场景清晰浮现在眼前。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09241-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200294 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09241-450x338.jpg" alt="意大利北部帕多瓦省的一些小村庄看起来就像是凝固的历史。中世纪时期的城墙依然完美地座落在小山上。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200294" class="wp-caption-text">意大利北部帕多瓦省的一些小村庄看起来就像是凝固的历史。中世纪时期的城墙依然保存完好。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蒙赛利切小城最具有代表性的古建筑是圣.朱斯蒂娜（CHIESA DI SANTA GIUSTINA）教堂。这座教堂以罗马式的风格建造而成，以哥特式的风格进行了装修。就在这座教堂里，我看到了流泪的圣母塑像。圣母塑像旁边有一些铁架子，上面是儿童绘制的圣母像。不过没有一幅儿童画绘制的圣母在掉眼泪。由于没有神职人员在场，也无法询问这眼泪究竟是神迹，还是艺术家的创作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200223" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09202-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200223 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09202-450x600.jpg" alt="DSC09202" width="338" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200223" class="wp-caption-text">圣.朱斯蒂娜（CHIESA DI SANTA GIUSTINA）教堂中流泪的圣母塑像。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>圣母像位于圣.朱斯蒂娜教堂内。圣.朱斯蒂娜教堂是蒙塞利切小城历史上最有价值的地方。它也被称为老教堂，以和新教堂区分开来，半世纪多来新教堂（Duomo nuovo San Giuseppe di Monselice，蒙塞利切新圣约瑟夫大教堂）已经取代了它的教区功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200215" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09186-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200215 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09186-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC09186" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200215" class="wp-caption-text">半世纪多来新教堂（Duomo nuovo San Giuseppe di Monselice，蒙塞利切新圣约瑟夫大教堂，照片右侧）已经取代了圣.朱斯蒂娜教堂的教区功能。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>圣.朱斯蒂娜教堂坐落在科尔德拉罗卡的山坡上，在教堂前后的山路上，游客可以欣赏到整个城市的景色。自古以来，蒙塞利切被称为沃欧加内丘陵（Euganean Hills）入口。这里是山丘与平原交界的地带。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200217" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09191-450x334.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200217 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09191-450x334.jpg" alt="DSC09191" width="454" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200217" class="wp-caption-text">圣.朱斯蒂娜教堂建于1256年。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>圣.朱斯蒂娜教堂建于1256年。这座教堂以罗马式风格建造，配有高大的锯齿形钟楼，呈现哥特式的装饰元素。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200228" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09206-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200228 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09206-450x600.jpg" alt="DSC09206" width="338" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200228" class="wp-caption-text">教堂后殿的墙壁有一些十四世纪壁画的片段。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教堂后殿的墙壁有一些十四世纪壁画的片段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200225" style="width: 438px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09204-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200225 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09204-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC09204" width="438" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200225" class="wp-caption-text">十五世纪所绘的谦卑圣母（Madonna dell&#8217;umiltà）（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教堂保存的其他艺术作品还有：十五世纪所绘的谦卑圣母（Madonna dell&#8217;umiltà），十六世纪的圣朱斯蒂娜雕像，公元一世纪的罗马陪葬石碑，四个十八世纪的大理石浮雕，和十七和十八世纪威尼斯学校的画作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200227" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09205-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200227 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09205-450x600.jpg" alt="DSC09205" width="337" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200227" class="wp-caption-text">十八世纪的大理石浮雕。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8200224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200224" style="width: 339px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09203-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200224 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09203-450x600.jpg" alt="DSC09203" width="339" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200224" class="wp-caption-text">十八世纪的大理石浮雕。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8200219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200219" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09193-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200219 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09193-450x600.jpg" alt="DSC09193" width="338" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200219" class="wp-caption-text">十八世纪的大理石浮雕。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8200221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200221" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09198-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200221 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09198-450x600.jpg" alt="DSC09198" width="338" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200221" class="wp-caption-text">十八世纪的大理石浮雕。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8200220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200220" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09194-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200220 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09194-450x600.jpg" alt="DSC09194" width="338" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200220" class="wp-caption-text">教堂墙壁上的画作。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8200218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200218" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09192-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200218 " src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09192-450x600.jpg" alt="DSC09192" width="338" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200218" class="wp-caption-text">教堂墙壁上的画作。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>归功于完善的音响效果，如今，圣.朱斯蒂娜教堂被用于举行宗教仪式和古典音乐会。</p>
<p>教堂后院是一座优雅的花园，有一条设有七个神祠（Santuario Giubilare delle Sette Chiese）的山路，第七神祠藏有第一批基督教殉难者的遗骸。沿着鹅卵石铺就的山路向上走，就来到Duodo别墅。Duodo别墅大约在1592年为威尼斯贵族Francesco Duodo所建造。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200230" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09215-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="wp-image-8200230 size-medium" src="https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DSC09215-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC09215" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200230" class="wp-caption-text">Duodo别墅旁边的建筑。（刘佳丽/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
