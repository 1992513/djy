<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>烧烤达人尝遍全美 评百佳烤肉</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年08月20日讯】（大纪元记者陈洁云编译报导）美国海军大兵约翰尼?福吉特（Johnny Fugitt）对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A7%E7%83%A4.md#1">烧烤</a>的热情远远超出了一般的喜爱。他花了一年时间吃遍了美国48个州的365家烧烤餐厅。</p>
<p>在密苏里州圣路易斯服役的他，利用周末演习的空当展开了他的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A7%E7%83%A4.md#1">烧烤</a>之旅。他表示，整个项目进行期间，他一点也没有增重。</p>
<p>在新书《全美百佳烧烤》<ahref="https://www.amazon.com/The-Best-Barbecue-Restaurants-America/dp/099623330X?tag=bisafetynet-20" target="_blank">The 100 Best Barbecue Restaurants in America</a>）中，福吉特详细记述了自己的美食体验，书中先给出了前25名的排行榜，之后按地区逐一探访其余的75家。</p>
<p><strong>下面就是排名前25的烧烤餐厅：</strong></p>
<p><strong>1. Kerlin BBQ（德州奥斯汀）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8219426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8219426" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1-kerlin-bbq-austin-texas-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8219426 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1-kerlin-bbq-austin-texas-450x338.jpg" alt="1-kerlin-bbq-austin-texas" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8219426" class="wp-caption-text">(Daniela R./Yelp)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这家夫妻店是在一辆永久停放的拖车中经营的。大多数牛腩会以225至250华氏度的低火烤6到20小时，然后转至400度的高温下再熏12小时。其结果，外面是厚厚的黑痂，里面则肉质鲜嫩。餐厅还提供应免费啤酒。</p>
<p><strong>2. Hometown Bar-B-Que（纽约布鲁克林）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8219427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8219427" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2-hometown-bar-b-que-brooklyn-new-york-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8219427 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2-hometown-bar-b-que-brooklyn-new-york-450x338.jpg" alt="2-hometown-bar-b-que-brooklyn-new-york" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8219427" class="wp-caption-text">(Hometown BBQ/Facebook)</figcaption></figure>
<p>店主比利?德尼（Billy Durney）在开烧烤餐厅前做过20年名人私人保镖，走遍了全美各地锤炼熏肉手艺，2013年在家乡开了这家餐厅，除了本地产的酸黄瓜外，全部食品都是自家精制。福吉特推荐牛腩、牛排、猪排和豆类。</p>
<p><strong>3. Joe&#8217;s Kansas City Bar-B-Que（堪萨斯州堪萨斯城）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8219429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8219429" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/3-joes-kansas-city-bar-b-que-kansas-city-kansas-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8219429 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/3-joes-kansas-city-bar-b-que-kansas-city-kansas-450x338.jpg" alt="3-joes-kansas-city-bar-b-que-kansas-city-kansas" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8219429" class="wp-caption-text">(Bob B./Yelp)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这家建在老加油站的餐厅先前叫Oklahoma Joe&#8217;s，获得过很多饮食界的殊荣。美国电视主持人、美食家安东尼?波登（Anthony Bourdain）将其列入其“此生必吃的13个地方”（13 Places to Eat Before You Die）名单。福吉特推荐牛肋骨和三明治，特别是塞满慢熏胸肉、烟熏波萝伏洛起司、和香脆洋葱圈的Z-Man三明治。</p>
<p><strong>4. The Granary&#8217;Cue &amp; Brew（德州圣安东尼奥）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8219430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8219430" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/4-the-granary-cue-and-brew-san-antonio-texas-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8219430 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/4-the-granary-cue-and-brew-san-antonio-texas-450x338.jpg" alt="4-the-granary-cue-and-brew-san-antonio-texas" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8219430" class="wp-caption-text">(candidcommuter/Tripadvisor)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这家餐厅中午供应传统烧烤，晚餐则多有创新，比如牛肩肉是和咖啡风味的藜麦脆片、酸芹菜及焦糖番茄酱一起上桌的。该餐厅的玉米饼也大获作者肯定。</p>
<p><strong>5. 4 Rivers Smokehouse（佛罗里达州奥兰多）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8219431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8219431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/5-4-rivers-smokehouse-orlando-florida-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8219431 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/5-4-rivers-smokehouse-orlando-florida-450x338.jpg" alt="5-4-rivers-smokehouse-orlando-florida" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8219431" class="wp-caption-text">(managemant/tripadvisor)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这间家庭式熏肉餐厅相当有故事。店主约翰?里弗斯爱上了德州牛腩，走遍全美来创制独门胸肉制法。所以在这里点什么真的没有悬念。<br />
