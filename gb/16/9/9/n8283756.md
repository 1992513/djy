<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台湾中秋赏月观星秘境</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月09日讯】（大纪元记者张雅云综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B.md#1">中秋</a>佳节月圆人团圆，一家人齐聚自家庭院，一边烤肉、吃着月饼与柚子，一边欣赏天空的一轮明月。今年中秋的假期，建议民众除了可以考虑出门欣赏皎洁月光，若能够登高远眺繁华的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E6%99%AF.md#1">夜景</a>与满天璀璨的繁星那就太浪漫了。以下推荐您全台5大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E6%9C%88.md#1">赏月</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E6%98%9F.md#1">观星</a>热门景点。</p>
<p><strong>台北象山</strong></p>
<p>最广为人知的就是坐落在信义区的象山，只要半小时就能攀登至观景台，象山步道不仅是观赏台北101跨年烟火的好去处，也是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B.md#1">中秋</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E6%9C%88.md#1">赏月</a>绝佳景点。六巨石为象山奇特景观之一，午后，在巨石能够欣赏夕阳余晖，夜晚，攀上巨石，不仅能够一览台北信义区繁华<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E6%99%AF.md#1">夜景</a>，与月亮的距离也似乎更近了些。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8283929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8283929" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609081230011758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8283929 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609081230011758-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8283929" class="wp-caption-text">由象山看到的2008跨年台北101烟火（Ukyo Hsieh／维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8283919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8283919" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609081225571758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8283919 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609081225571758-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8283919" class="wp-caption-text">自象山眺望台北信义区繁华夜景。(中岑 范姜／Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>台北猫空</strong></p>
<p>从木栅猫空山上可尽揽台北的美丽夜色，因为山区光害较少，比起市区更能清楚的看到天上美景，也可在露天茶座或庭园茶坊里悠闲的品茗、赏月、数星星，还有大自然虫鸣搭配夜景。猫空昔日曾是台北市最大产茶区，目前，以观光休闲为主，将传统茶艺与餐饮结合，成为悠闲假日的好去处，还可乘猫空缆车观赏沿途风景，全线长4.03公里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8283943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8283943" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609081238131758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8283943 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609081238131758-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8283943" class="wp-caption-text">从猫空山上可尽揽台北的美丽夜色。（senryuto／维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6682945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6682945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/1303131827142378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6682945 size-medium" title="中日游客爱故宫 港澳旅客偏好木栅动物园" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/1303131827142378-450x300.jpg" alt="中日游客爱故宫 港澳旅客偏好木栅动物园" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6682945" class="wp-caption-text">台北市&#8211;猫空缆车。（交通部观光局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>台东星星部落</strong></p>
<p>星星部落从咖啡厅外的草地看出去就是台东市夜景，一片灯海真的是美极了！这里虽然海拔不高，但周围光害少，抬头可看到满天星空，10点前还可看到丰年机场飞机起降。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8283951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8283951" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609090245351758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8283951 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609090245351758-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8283951" class="wp-caption-text">从星星部落看出去就是台东市夜景，抬头可看到满天星空。（绿鱼／Flickr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8283956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8283956" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609090249431758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8283956 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609090249431758-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8283956" class="wp-caption-text">在星星部落看台东市夜景和满天星空。(中岑 范姜／Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8283945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8283945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1509261233411758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8283945 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1509261233411758-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8283945" class="wp-caption-text">台东村星星部落是赏夜景、约会的好去处。中后是丰年机场，可看飞机起降。（张雅云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>台中望高寮</strong></p>
<p>望高寮又称“王田坎”，是大肚台地制高点，向西可以远看，彰化大肚溪以南平地和台中港区，如在夜景公园眺望，整个台中市的繁华尽收眼底。夜晚，站在望高寮居高临下，放眼是万家灯火和辽阔的夜景，还有流动车河浪漫加分，可惜光害严重不适合<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E6%98%9F.md#1">观星</a>，但这里还是台中观看都市夜景及赏月谈心的最好去处。</p>

<p><strong> 高雄忠烈祠</strong></p>
<p>位在高雄寿山上方的忠烈祠原本是人烟鲜少，但在几年前，规划了一个“LOVE景观平台”后就成了爆红的赏景名地，成了恋人们口耳相传的约会景点，并一次将市、海景尽收眼底，尤其当夜幕低垂之际，更能欣赏到最浪漫梦幻的大高雄夜景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8283974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8283974" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609090309351758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8283974 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609090309351758-300x451.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8283974" class="wp-caption-text">高雄忠烈祠LOVE景观平台(yudeLin/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8283994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8283994" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609090309211758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8283994 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609090309211758-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8283994" class="wp-caption-text">从忠烈祠LOVE景观平台看高雄夜景。（吴金成／Flickr）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>屏东垦丁</strong></p>
<p>垦丁国家公园景色休闲浪漫，是非常好的中秋赏月景点，不妨约三五好友或与亲人一起到垦丁共赏明月与观星，这里除了有皎洁的明月、浪漫的沙滩及海浪之外，还可以趁黄昏时到关山欣赏有名的夕照。鹅銮鼻灯塔也是夜游最好的地方，月圆的时候连手电筒都不用，景色漂亮到不行，又因为辽阔无光害，所以，吸引很多人来此观赏星星。</p>
