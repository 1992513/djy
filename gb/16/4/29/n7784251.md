<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>11年前枪杀圣盖博高中生的凶徒落网受审</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月29日讯】<span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者夏君编译报导）当局说﹐目前﹐一名被控于</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">11</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">年前谋杀圣盖博加布列里诺高中（</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Gabrielino High School</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）一名</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">17</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">岁学生的柔斯密帮派成员正在帕萨迪纳高等法院接受审讯。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">法院官员说﹐在周三（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">27</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">日）的庭审中﹐检察官陈述了案情﹐并继续征集证人。受审者是</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">29</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的丹尼尔</span></span></span></span></span><span lang="zh-TW">·</span>?<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">格雷罗（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Daniel Guerrero</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">），他被控于</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">2005</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">27</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">日杀死了达萨拉（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Ryan Dasalla</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">检察官说﹐当时</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">18</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的丹尼尔</span></span></span></span></span><span lang="zh-TW">·</span>?<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">格雷罗与他</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">23</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的哥哥加布列尔</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">·</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">格雷罗（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Gabriel Guerrero</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）和另一名街头帮派成员</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">托莱多（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Sarah Toledo</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）在街头寻仇﹐误认</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">17</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的达萨拉为仇家﹐</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">并</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">开枪杀死</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">了他</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">根据检察官和洛县警署<span lang="zh-CN">凶</span>杀案侦探的描述﹐他们当时将达萨拉误认作了于头天晚上殴打格雷罗弟弟的另一个帮派组织的成员之一。托莱多是帮助他们兄弟俩找到达萨拉的人。达萨拉是背部中枪致死。枪击事件发生在离加布列里诺高中一个街区远的地方。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">这起致命枪击案发生不久后﹐加布列尔</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">·</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">格雷罗和托莱多被警方逮捕了。但据信真正开枪的凶手丹尼尔</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">·</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">格雷罗却逃走了﹐而且多年逍遥法外。一直到</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">2013</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">年底﹐警方才在墨西哥的蒂华纳（</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Tijuana</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Mexico</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）发现并逮捕了他。他在那里居住时一直用着一个不同的名字。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">地区检察官办公室发言人瑞斯林（</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Greg Risling</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）说﹐他现在被指控犯有谋杀和共谋谋杀罪。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">瑞斯林说﹐<span lang="zh-CN">纪</span>录显示﹐陪审团选择的案件开始日为</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">日，开案报告日为</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">20</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">日。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">之前﹐另外两名被告已被定罪﹐并开始在监狱长期服刑。加布列尔</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">·</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">格雷罗被判处了</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">84</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">年至终身监禁的徒刑。陪审团于</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">2010</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">年认定其犯有一级谋杀罪，及帮派犯罪和枪支犯罪。托莱多被判处了</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">50</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">年至终身监禁的徒刑。陪审团于</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">2011</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">年底认定其犯有谋杀和共谋谋杀罪，及帮派犯罪和枪支犯罪。◇</span></span></span></p>
