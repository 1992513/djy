<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯房价年涨前十区揭晓</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13834579-2904e9dc02a901a1cfd4330d8970c79b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在截至7月的12个月期间，北部沿海地区Marmion的房产中位价涨幅最高—41.7%，达到136.9万元。图为珀斯一房产待售。（林文责/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年09月24日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者杨新云澳洲珀斯编译报导</span><span lang="zh-TW">）西澳房地产协会（</span></span>REIWA<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）公布的最新数据显示，在截至</span></span>7<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月的</span></span>12<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个月期间，在房价增长最快的前</span></span>10<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">名榜单中，北部地区占前五席。</span></span></p>
<p>北部沿海地区Marmion的房产中位价涨幅最高—41.7%，达到136.9万元；其次是North Beach，增长35.9%，达到140万澳元；Mt Hawthorn，上涨34.4%；Gwelup，上涨33.7%；和North Perth上涨27.8%。<br />
REIWA<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">主席</span></span>Damian Collins<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">说，这些数据反映了买家是在</span></span>COVID<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（中共病毒）期间，利用低利率来升级到更多“理想舒适区”。</span></span><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">他说：“在这些房价增长最快区中，大部分的地区的中位价格介于</span></span>100<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万至</span></span>200<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元之间。”</span></span><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">“这些买家可能从</span></span>Duncraig<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">区或某个内陆地区搬来，并在可能的情况下抓住机会升级到靠近海滩的地方，如</span></span>Marmion<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">、</span></span>North Beach<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">和</span></span>Gwelup<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。”</span></span><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯北部房地产中介</span></span>Realmark North Coastal<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的经纪人</span></span>Ian Masterson<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">说，不可否认的是，北海岸地带的房价增长总是表现得很稳定。</span></span><br />
Masterson<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">说：“这些地区靠近海滩，可以为人们提供惬意的生活方式，这些地区周围也有好学校，疫情爆发后，人们节省了在一般情况下不会节省的花费，（从而转向升级住房），从这些区的房产销售情况，我们就可以看得出来。”</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">排名前十的地区：</span></p>
<p>1.Marmion<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">房价上涨</span></span>41.7%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
2.North Beach<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>35.9%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
3.Mount Hawthorn<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>34.4<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
4.Gwelup<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>33.7%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
5.North Perth<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>27.8%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
6.Hillman<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>26.4<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
7.West Leederville<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>26.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
8.Fremantle<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>25.7%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
9.Mount Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>25%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">；</span></span><br />
10.Parkerville<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">：</span><span lang="zh-TW">上涨</span></span>25%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
