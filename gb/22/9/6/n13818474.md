<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“真、善、忍”国际美展布里斯本开幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818482-DSC08007-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“真、善、忍”国际美展9月3日在布里斯本Inala Art Gallery揭幕。（黄连星/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年09月06日讯】（大纪元记者徐凯文昆士兰布里斯本报导）9月3日，由澳洲法轮大法佛学会昆士兰分会举办的“真、善、忍”国际美展在布里斯本的Inala Art Gallery揭幕，这是自疫情以来澳洲首次举办“真、善、忍”国际美展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13818491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818491" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818491-DSC07997-600x413.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818491-DSC07997-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818491" class="wp-caption-text">“真、善、忍”国际美展9月3日在布里斯本Inala Art Gallery揭幕。图为画展工作人员为参观者讲解。（黄连星/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13818500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818500" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818500-DSC02915-1-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818500-DSC02915-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818500" class="wp-caption-text">“真、善、忍”国际美展9月3日在布里斯本Inala Art Gallery揭幕。图为画展工作人员为参观者讲解。（凌霄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13818503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818503" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818503-DSC02911-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818503-DSC02911-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818503" class="wp-caption-text">“真、善、忍”国际美展9月3日在布里斯本Inala Art Gallery揭幕。（凌霄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昆州多元文化厅长Leanne Linard为美展开幕发来贺信，她表示此次美展彰显了“真、善、忍”的力量。这次美展是昆州多元文化的盛举，而且是一个以独特视角，即通过艺术形式了解到法轮大法精神价值的绝佳机会。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818506 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818506-MINISTERS-MESSAGE-for-Art-of-Zhen-Shan-Ren-International-Exhibition--600x662.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="662" /></p>
<p>昆州绿党州议员Michael Berkman在贺信中说：“真、善、忍国际美展已经在50多个国家，900多座城市举办，并受到到访的观众和艺术家们的一致好评。我们很幸运美展能够在当地举办。”</p>
<p>Berkman表示，美展的艺术家们通过细腻的笔法，描绘了法轮功的美好，以及面对中共残酷迫害的勇气。画展也揭露了中共对法轮功信仰团体长达23年的非人性迫害。法轮功团体多年来面对迫害展现出了非凡的坚忍不拔的意志，他愿和法轮功学员一起呼吁停止迫害。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13818505 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818505-2022-08-05-Letter-of-support-for-Falun-Gong_-The-Art-of-Zhen-Shan-Ren-International-Exhibition-450x634.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="634" /></p>
<p>此外，布里斯本Forest Lake区市议员Charles Strunk和昆士兰越南社区主席Cuong Trong Bui博士出席开幕式并发表演讲，对美展的开幕表示祝贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13818512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818512-DSC08034-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818512-DSC08034-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818512" class="wp-caption-text">“真、善、忍”国际美展9月3日在布里斯本Inala Art Gallery揭幕。布里斯本Forest Lake区市议员Charles Strunk（左）在发言。（黄连星/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13818511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818511" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818511-DSC02904-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818511-DSC02904-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818511" class="wp-caption-text">“真、善、忍”国际美展9月3日在布里斯本Inala Art Gallery揭幕。图为昆士兰越南社区主席Cuong Trong Bui博士在发言。（凌霄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原清华大学博士研究生俞平在发言中讲述自己在中国遭到迫害的经历。他表示实际的迫害比画展所呈现的内容还要残酷。他希望更多善良的人们能够前来观看画展，因为世界需要真善忍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13818509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818509" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818509-DSC08030-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818509-DSC08030-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818509" class="wp-caption-text">“真、善、忍”国际美展9月3日在布里斯本Inala Art Gallery揭幕。图为原清华大学博士研究生俞平在发言。（黄连星/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布里斯本“真、善、忍”国际美展（对公众免费开放），时间：9月6日-17日；开放时间：周二至周六，上午10点至下午2点。地址：Inala Art Gallery, Inala Plaza,37 Corsair Avenue, Inala QLD</p>
