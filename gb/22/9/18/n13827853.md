<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾美食国际巡回讲座光临夏洛特</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827855-028a255653ec88f2d9d8eafa7969c85b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>北卡夏洛特台湾同乡会和台湾商会9月15日在Huntersville红碗餐厅（Red Bowl）举行中秋聚餐。来自台湾的两位名厨献艺。（Peter Seah／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年09月19日讯】（大纪元记者李雅珍美国北卡夏洛特报导）9月15日（星期四）晚，美国北卡夏洛特台湾同乡会和台湾商会举行中秋节庆聚餐。此次正值“台湾美食国际巡回讲座”来到夏洛特，两位来自台湾的名厨潘盟仁和蔡万利制作了三道台湾美食：五味花枝、炒米粉和猪蹄，让参加活动的当地台侨品尝到地道故乡风味的菜肴，为这次中秋餐聚留下了特别的记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13827864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827864-88bacf6190fa285e37c1a47abfb3decc.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13827864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827864-88bacf6190fa285e37c1a47abfb3decc-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827864" class="wp-caption-text">台湾名厨潘盟仁（左二）、蔡万利（右二）和台湾商会前任会长、侨务顾问陈陞辉先生（左一）、侨务委员陈可征女士（右一）合影。（Peter Seah／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>五味花枝是台湾常见的一道菜。花枝就是墨鱼。潘盟仁大厨介绍，五味是番茄、香油、酱油膏、还有姜、蒜、柠檬等多种味道调和在一起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13827857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827857" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827857-051199e4aa9808315e2c3f00d837344b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13827857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827857-051199e4aa9808315e2c3f00d837344b-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827857" class="wp-caption-text">潘盟仁和蔡万利大厨制作的台湾美食：五味花枝。（Peter Seah／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13827858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827858-40d01fd7892f75b859f83d477384a8f9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13827858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827858-40d01fd7892f75b859f83d477384a8f9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827858" class="wp-caption-text">潘盟仁和蔡万利大厨制作的台湾美食：炒米粉。（Peter Seah／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13827861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827861-094c02dcafbf018e454651031302b092.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13827861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827861-094c02dcafbf018e454651031302b092-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827861" class="wp-caption-text">潘盟仁和蔡万利大厨制作的台湾美食：烧猪蹄。（Peter Seah／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主持餐会的侨务顾问陈陞辉先生介绍，这两位大厨已经造访了华盛顿DC、波士顿、亚特兰大，夏洛特是他们美东之行的第四站，最后一站是北卡的洛丽。在美国西海岸的“台湾美食国际巡回秀”，由另外两名台湾名厨担角。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13827873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827873" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827873-NC2022357EBA29A.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13827873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827873-NC2022357EBA29A-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827873" class="wp-caption-text">两位台湾名厨与夏洛特当地保定中餐馆交流厨艺和经营后合影。右一为台湾商会周依枚女士、右二为台湾商会吴俊岳会长。（当地台湾商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>被问到巡回讲座期间，是否去过美国的中餐馆，味道与台湾风味有何不同？潘大厨笑说，去过，风味当然不同，这里是做给美国人吃的。他说，美国各地中餐馆的风味也不一样，像波士顿，食材比较丰富，有些地方受制于食材，制作的味道就会不一样。他还谈到，台湾厨师现在比较喜欢用果醋，柠檬，味道香甜清爽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13827866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827866" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827866-ab1acc880ac005034b62f414c138d280.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13827866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827866-ab1acc880ac005034b62f414c138d280-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827866" class="wp-caption-text">台湾名厨潘盟仁（右一）和蔡万利（左二）参观观夏洛特当地CPCC学院（Center Piedmont Community College）厨艺系，右二为厨艺系主任Robert Morreto。左一为本地厨师王成木师傅。（当地台湾商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在北卡台湾商会安排下，潘盟仁和蔡万利还参观了夏洛特当地CPCC学院（Center Piedmont CommunityCollege）厨艺系。厨艺系主任Robert Morreto向他们解说了该系的设备。本地王成木师傅向厨艺系师生展示用小黄瓜雕刻小鸟的绝活，学生们纷纷用手机拍照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13827877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13827877" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827877-NC20220914IMG_2005.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13827877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13827877-NC20220914IMG_2005-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13827877" class="wp-caption-text">本地厨师王成木师傅给CPCC学院厨艺系师生展示用小黄瓜雕刻小鸟的绝活。（当地台湾商会提供）</figcaption></figure>
