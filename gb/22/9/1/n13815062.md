<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新泽西Hansang韩餐首尔口味 人气料理不可错过</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815086-pork-BGZ23483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新泽西Hansang韩式餐厅回头客不断，秘诀在于擅长制作独家人气料理，图为 韩式蒸五花肉。（张学慧 / 大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年09月01日工商讯】新泽西的人气韩式餐厅Hansang自2014年开业以来，不断吸引顾客慕名光顾，近100%的回头率使原先规模已难以满足需要。2021年疫后Hansang经过约20天装修，让顾客等得迫不及待。 现在装饰一新的Hansang餐厅位于韩国烤肉店林立的Broad Avenue，独树一帜，宽敞的单间、舒适明亮的大厅，规模变为原来的两倍，可容纳120人，期待您的加入。</p>
<p>Hansang使用正宗和最好的食材，呈现新泽西州最好的首尔风味韩国美食。菜单包括正宗的韩国炖菜、汤、烧烤主菜、素食和各种冷热开胃菜。</p>
<h2>人气料理 吸引回头客</h2>
<p>Hansang 拥有独家食谱、拿手好菜，令食客流连忘返，很多人吃过一次，下次必回头。以下是部分人气食谱：</p>
<p><strong>● 香辣红烧金鲳鱼</strong></p>
<p>喜爱海鲜者的必吃料理，独家秘方呈现鲳鱼之独特鲜美，搭配爽滑土豆、嫩绿葱丝，鲜香下饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13815083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13815083" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815083-1bade5d896b43592792b660f71a765e6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13815083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815083-1bade5d896b43592792b660f71a765e6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13815083" class="wp-caption-text">独家秘制香辣红烧金鲳鱼是必吃料理。（张学慧 / 大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>● 烤偏口鱼</strong></p>
<p>爱吃鱼者的一道必尝料理，精心把控的火候带出最佳新鲜风味，吃过一次终生难忘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13815080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13815080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815080-2022-08-31-11.20.39.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13815080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815080-2022-08-31-11.20.39-600x448.png" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13815080" class="wp-caption-text">精心把空的火候，带出烤偏口鱼独有的风味。（Hansang提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>● 烤鲭鱼</strong></p>
<p>特制食谱使鲭鱼外皮酥脆，内里嫩滑多汁。</p>
<p><strong>● 大排骨汤套餐</strong></p>
<p>一直以来的人气菜单，炖好的大骨头带着香嫩的肉，一口咬下去唇齿留香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13815084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13815084" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815084-20d23d5e1856e91172370d114f727631.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13815084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815084-20d23d5e1856e91172370d114f727631-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13815084" class="wp-caption-text">大排骨汤套餐一直是一道人气料理。（张学慧 / 大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>● 韩式蒸五花肉</strong></p>
<p>韩式经典美味，诱人的肉片搭配泡菜、豆腐，令人食欲大开。</p>
<p><strong>● 烤牛小排</strong></p>
<p>用甜咸酱腌制的烤牛小排，肉嫩多汁，回味无穷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13815081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13815081" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815081-2022-08-31-11.19.41.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13815081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13815081-2022-08-31-11.19.41-600x473.png" alt="" width="600" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13815081" class="wp-caption-text">烤牛小排肉嫩多汁。（Hansang提供）</figcaption></figure>
