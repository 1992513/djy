<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>利用厨余做堆肥 2种肥料详解 省钱兼环保</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819339-20220907_plant_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>吃剩的果皮都是堆肥最好的原料。（苹果屋提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>在家堆肥除了可以做环保之外，对于家庭种菜还是一种免费的肥料，并且能改善土壤使土壤团粒化、增加排水性。若能学会堆肥，不但安全有趣，同时也能节省开支。</p>
<h3>利用厨余就能自制肥料</h3>
<p>只要是生物性（动物、植物）的材料一般都是可以用于堆肥的原料。但是居家堆肥较建议使用植物性材料来堆肥，平时较易取得的材料可利用落叶、果皮、菜叶、杂草等来做成堆肥。</p>
<p>无论是通气式或密闭式堆肥，平时都要保持湿润（可将土握在手心用力捏住，感觉有水快要滴出来的样子）促使微生物繁殖；大约1个月后土表会有温热感，此乃热发酵产生，此时可重复堆肥步骤，持续将厨余往上堆叠。夏天大约3～4个月，冷天大约5～6个月就可以完全腐熟使用了；一般居家堆肥建议使用通气式堆肥方式较佳，因密闭式堆肥方式较容易产生恶臭异味。</p>
<h3>自己堆肥 STEP BY STEP</h3>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">1.通气式堆肥（又称“好氧堆肥”）</span></strong></p>
<p>使用》通气度高的容器，如：麻布袋、市售通气式堆肥桶。<br />
菌种》好氧菌。<br />
原理》使厨余等材料与空气充分接触。<br />
成果》粉状有机肥。<br />
堆肥时间》 比较快，约3～4个月。</p>
<p>STEP 1. 准备一个通气式堆肥箱，运用“三明治”的原理，先倒入一层薄土约3公分后铺平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819321" style="width: 432px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819321-P.31-01.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13819321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819321-P.31-01.png" alt="" width="432" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819321" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 2. 再放入收集好的厨余，铺上约10～15公分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819327" style="width: 432px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819327-P.31-02.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13819327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819327-P.31-02.png" alt="" width="432" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819327" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 3. 在厨余上均匀洒上“好氧菌”，再用土将厨余完全覆盖住约3公分厚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819328" style="width: 432px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819328-P.31-03.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13819328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819328-P.31-03.png" alt="" width="432" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819328" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 4. 土稍微压实，然后浇水；大约3～4个月后即可使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819329" style="width: 432px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819329-P.31-04.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13819329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819329-P.31-04.png" alt="" width="432" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819329" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>2. 密闭式堆肥（又称“厌氧堆肥”）</strong></span></p>
<p>使用》 市售密闭式堆肥桶。<br />
菌种》厌氧菌。<br />
原理》隔绝外界空气。<br />
堆肥时间》较慢，约4～5个月。<br />
成果》以液肥为主，最后产生湿润的有机肥。</p>
<p>STEP 1. 于市售厨余桶底层先覆盖上一层土，然后铺平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819330" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819330-P.31-05.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13819330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819330-P.31-05-450x279.png" alt="" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819330" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 2. 放进厨余约10公分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819331-P.31-06.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13819331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819331-P.31-06-450x300.png" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819331" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 3. 在厨余表面上均匀的洒上“厌氧菌”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819332" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819332-P.31-07.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13819332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819332-P.31-07-450x300.png" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819332" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 4. 以土覆盖住厨余约3公分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819333" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819333-P.31-08.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13819333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819333-P.31-08-450x279.png" alt="" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819333" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 5. 用手稍微压实后再浇水，只要保持湿润即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819335-P.31-09.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13819335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819335-P.31-09-450x300.png" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819335" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>STEP 6.盖上盖子前最好可以先覆上一层保鲜膜或棉布，使其紧密度更佳。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819336" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819336-P.31-10.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13819336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819336-P.31-10-450x300.png" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819336" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
