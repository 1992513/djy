<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>余风采堂庆中秋 宗亲与嘉宾齐贺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818867-DSC00823-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>余风采堂9月4日庆祝中秋节，嘉宾们切蛋糕。左起：余风采堂主席余宗涛、波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元、波士顿市议员墨菲（Erin Murphy）、侨教中心主任潘昭荣、余风采堂主席余宝爱。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年09月07日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）壬寅年中秋节周六将至。9月4日中午，纽英崙余风采堂的宗亲们在华埠驻地欢聚，与政要嘉宾一起吃月饼、切蛋糕，庆祝佳节。</p>
<p>当天中午，20多位余氏宗亲齐聚一堂，庆祝传统中秋节。主席余宝爱及几位宗亲亲手烹饪了蘑菇炖肉和蚝油菜心，并买来了贵妃鸡、炒粉等菜肴，以丰盛的午宴款待来宾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13818868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818868-DSC00827.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818868-DSC00827-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818868" class="wp-caption-text">宗亲们齐享午宴，庆祝中秋节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在正餐之后，余风采堂为大家准备了中秋月饼，而且驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处也带来了美味的水果慕斯蛋糕。公所大厅里欢声笑语，大家其乐融融。</p>
<p>当日中秋宴会的主持人是余美玲。余风采堂主席余宝爱和余宗涛，以及元老余超群分别发言，为宗亲及嘉宾们送上祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13818866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818866" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818866-DSC00819.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818866-DSC00819-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818866" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处带来水果慕斯蛋糕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13819432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819432" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819432-DSC00795.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13819432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819432-DSC00795-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819432" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂主席余宝爱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13819433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819433" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819433-DSC00808.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13819433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819433-DSC00808-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819433" class="wp-caption-text">余风采堂主席余宗涛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到场参与宴会的政要有波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）、议员墨菲（Erin Murphy）、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元和侨教中心主任潘昭荣。他们也都纷纷祝福大家健康、团圆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819434-DSC00799.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13819434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819434-DSC00799-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819434" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾们喜笑颜开。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13818865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13818865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818865-DSC00817.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13818865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818865-DSC00817-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13818865" class="wp-caption-text">20多位宗亲齐聚余风采堂，庆祝中秋节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>费连首先感谢了波士顿地区余氏宗亲和台湾办事处为华埠社区，乃至整个波士顿的居民服务。在疫情期间，余风采堂参与为长者居民提供食品和服务，做出许多贡献。费连还说，吴弭市长及各位市议员都大力支持华人，反对歧视，并将继续努力从事这方面的工作。◇</p>
