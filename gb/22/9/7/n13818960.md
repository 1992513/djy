<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>涉及多起窃案  桃园偷机车男仍难逃法网围捕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818961-573961-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园偷机车男仍难逃法网围捕。（桃园警察分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年09月07日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园警察分局青溪所追查本(9)月初辖内某工地电缆遭剪除窃取案时，发现林姓男子(46岁)先行窃取机车代步，再利用深夜时分窃取工地电缆线变卖，8月份迄今涉及5起以上窃案。</p>
<figure id="13818961" aria-describedby="caption-13818961" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818961-573961-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818961-573961-450x337.jpg" alt="桃园偷机车男仍难逃法网围捕。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13818961" class="wp-caption-text">桃园偷机车男仍难逃法网围捕。（桃园警察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13818962" aria-describedby="caption-13818962" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818962-573962-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818962-573962-450x600.jpg" alt="桃园偷机车男仍难逃法网围捕。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13818962" class="wp-caption-text">桃园偷机车男仍难逃法网围捕。（桃园警察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>员警经监控掌握林男作案时程及路线，于周边进行埋守，7日凌晨5时许，发现他骑称机车载着电缆线沿万寿路行驶，立即进行尾随拦查，林男立即加速逃逸，见无法摆脱员警，竟在行进间扔下机车狂奔，意图用机车拦阻员警追捕，但跑了几十公尺仍被员警压制逮捕。</p>
<figure id="13818963" aria-describedby="caption-13818963" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818963-573963-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13818963-573963-450x337.jpg" alt="利用深夜时分窃取工地电缆线变卖。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13818963" class="wp-caption-text">利用深夜时分窃取工地电缆线变卖。（桃园警察分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全案依法移请侦办，后续警方将持续扩大追查林男是否涉及其他是类案件。◇</p>
