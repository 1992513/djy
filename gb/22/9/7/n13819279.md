<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中秋连假亲山去 嘉义低难度步道推荐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819280-574060-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>太兴岩步道。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年09月07日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）享受辽阔山景、瀑布及茶园景致，嘉义县文化观光局推荐新手入门<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">步道</a>7条路线，简单易走，不论是大人、小孩还是长辈都能轻松征服！还加码推荐中秋节来欣赏年度生态奇景“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%87%E9%B9%AD%E6%9C%9D%E5%87%A4.md#1">万鹭朝凤</a>”，空中小白龙现身，难得一见，邀请游客把握一年一次的迁徙大秀。</p>
<p>县长翁章梁表示，近年为鼓励游客亲近大自然，政府推动“向山亲近、向海致敬”政策，邀请游客亲身感受台湾每一寸土地的美，嘉义县的山与海各有特色，想要亲山并不难，为鼓励更多新手游客走进嘉义的山林，特别推荐难度低的几条<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">步道</a>，用最没有压力的心情感受嘉义的山之美！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819289-574069.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13819289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819289-574069-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819289" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%87%E9%B9%AD%E6%9C%9D%E5%87%A4.md#1">万鹭朝凤</a>。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13819281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819281-574061.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13819281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819281-574061-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819281" class="wp-caption-text">二尖山步道。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁介绍，“太兴飞瀑步道”、“太兴岩步道”、“石棹步道群”、“二尖山步道”、“迷糊步道”、“二延平步道”及“顶湖步道”，这几条都轻松好走，适合全家一起团圆健行去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819282-574062.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13819282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819282-574062-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819282" class="wp-caption-text">太兴飞瀑步道。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中秋节即将来到，除了烤肉、吃月饼外，同时段嘉义县也即将上演“生态奇景”，就在太兴飞瀑步道附近的太兴村，数不清南迁的黄头鹭将飞进太兴村山谷间乘风盘旋，交织成一幅幅优美动态画作，社区发展协会也推出茶席体验，能自己泡茶或预约泡茶师，游客可以拨打05-2571366、0933-356466，就能在山谷中品茶，还有机会一睹黄头鹭迁徙秀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819283-574063.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13819283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819283-574063-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819283" class="wp-caption-text">石棹步道群—茶之道。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文观局长许有仁提到，他个人很喜欢走步道，近期内挑战两次“青年岭步道”，走完步道虽然身体疲惫但是通体舒畅，将平日累积的疲惫随着汗水一起挥洒，他也鼓励游客们即使上班已经很累，但利用假日到步道走走，绝对是消除疲劳的另一种解方，也提醒游客外出旅游仍须注意防疫安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13819284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13819284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819284-574064.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13819284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13819284-574064-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13819284" class="wp-caption-text">石棹步道群—樱之道。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
