<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【树木图鉴】如何分辨李子跟桃子？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824939-c4e2f73d55b9f1a9087c198815e87191-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>左边是桃子的果实，右边是李子的果实。（美艺学苑社提供／大纪元合成）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>“桃李满天下”这句成语中，桃子跟李子有什么区别呢？有人认为李子也是桃子的一种，两者看似相似，其实有细微的差距。本书作者打破“认识树木很难”的迷思，从一片叶子、一朵花、一颗果实入门，教我们认识生活中常见的树木，享受探索大自然的乐趣！</p>
<h2><strong>李子也是桃子吗？分辨重点在底部的绒毛</strong></h2>
<p>桃子原产于中国的深山，据说在日本的栽培，是从桃太郎的故乡冈山开始，由此可见，像《桃太郎》的故事中，有桃子漂流在河里的情况确实可能发生。桃树的特征是叶片细长，果实的形状像臀部一样带有下凹的接缝，还覆满了绒毛。</p>
<p>与桃子非常相似的李子，果实也是屁股的形状，差异在于个头比桃子小了一点，叶片短了一点，表面也没有绒毛。有人认为“李子也是桃子的一种”，不过只要从表面有无绒毛，就能够清楚辨识两者的不同。</p>
<h2>桃子</h2>
<p>1）结出幼果的桃树。特征是叶片细长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13824918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13824918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824918-P.38-04.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13824918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824918-P.38-04-600x448.png" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13824918" class="wp-caption-text">结出幼果的桃树。特征是叶片细长。（美艺学苑社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2）在3月时开出桃色的花。花萼也有很多绒毛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13824921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13824921" style="width: 352px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824921-P.38-05.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13824921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824921-P.38-05.png" alt="" width="352" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13824921" class="wp-caption-text">在3月时开出桃色的花。花萼也有很多绒毛。（美艺学苑社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3）树皮和樱花树很相似，都有横纹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13824922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13824922" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824922-P.38-06.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13824922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824922-P.38-06.png" alt="" width="448" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13824922" class="wp-caption-text">树皮和樱花树很相似，都有横纹。（美艺学苑社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4）桃子→叶片的长度是7 ～ 16cm。</p>
<p>a.叶缘的锯齿又细又钝。</p>
<p>b.有些叶片从叶基开始变宽，也有些在叶尖变宽。</p>
<p>c.通常叶柄上有两颗突起的蜜腺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13824935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13824935" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824935-P.38-01_new.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-13824935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824935-P.38-01_new-300x1088.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="1088" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13824935" class="wp-caption-text">（美艺学苑社提供／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>李子</h2>
<p>李子又称酸桃。原产于中国的落叶小乔木。开白色花，果实是红色～紫色。叶片的长度是5 ～ 12cm。<br />
a.叶子往前端逐渐加宽。<br />
b.有的叶子有疣状蜜腺，有的没有。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13824938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13824938" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824938-P.38-03_new.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-13824938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/09/id13824938-P.38-03_new-300x503.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="503" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13824938" class="wp-caption-text">（美艺学苑社提供／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
