<h2><strong>琉璃繁缕Anagalis arvensis L. 樱草科</strong></h2>
<p>
琉璃繁缕又称海绿、蓝繁缕、九龙吐珠，火金姑，四念癀。一年生或越年生草本，高10~30公分，全株光滑无毛；茎丛生，四方棱形。叶对生，无柄，叶片卵形至卵状披针形，全缘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13852652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13852652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852652-433595_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13852652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852652-433595_medium-600x443.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="443"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13852652" class="wp-caption-text">春风小雨绿草地，宝蓝琉璃色迷离。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花单生叶腋，花梗纤细，花冠碧蓝色或带红晕，5裂片卵圆形，微齿缘及微腺状毛；雄蕊5枚，与花瓣对生，花丝密生紫色毛。蒴果球形，种子细小，黑褐色，密生瘤状突起。花期3~5月。药效驱风散寒，活血解毒。治毒蛇及狂犬咬伤，疮伤，鹤膝风。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13852654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13852654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852654-433597_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13852654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13852654-433597_medium-600x796.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="796"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13852654" class="wp-caption-text">花单生叶腋，花梗纤细，花冠碧蓝色或带红晕，5裂片卵圆形，微齿缘及微腺状毛；雄蕊5枚，与花瓣对生，花丝密生紫色毛。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
