<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>甘斯维尔台商会端午烤肉欢乐一堂</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747067-DSC_9935-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>端午烤肉大合照。（吕尚明/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月28日讯】(大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大报导) 为了迎接即将来到的端午佳节，甘斯维尔台湾商会在5月21日这个风和日丽的日子，选择了风景宜人的蓝尼尔（Lake Lanier）湖畔的西岸公园(West Bank Park)举办烤肉聚餐。商会会长谷企平表示，依照以往的经验，他们本来预期有六、七十人会参加，结果一直到前两天还有人报名，最后报名人数高达144人！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13747069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13747069" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747069-DSC_9820-e1653694538751.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13747069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747069-DSC_9820-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13747069" class="wp-caption-text">侨界大佬王德(左)，陈洋一(中)，郝慰国(右)。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13747099" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13747099" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747099-DSC_9862-e1653696891309.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13747099" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747099-DSC_9862-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13747099" class="wp-caption-text">经文处经济组组长陈新发与儿子一起参与。(吕尚明/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13747486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13747486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747486-DSC_9832-1-e1653747203452.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13747486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747486-DSC_9832-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13747486" class="wp-caption-text">哥俩好。(吕尚明/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>谷企平在开场白中风趣地说道，现在股票猛跌，但是物价飞涨，烤肉所需的鸡翅、味精、酱油都涨价，但是唯一不涨的就是这次烤肉聚餐商会依然每人只收十美元。对此，众人报以热烈的掌声。接着大家就开始享用美味而丰盛的午餐，包括烤猪排，烤鸡腿与鸡翅，台湾香肠、烤蔬菜。当然必不可少的是端午节的主角&#8212;台湾肉粽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13747072" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13747072" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747072-DSC_9817-e1653694796924.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13747072" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747072-DSC_9817-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13747072" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长王翼龙(左)与世界台商会联合总会长王德(右)。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13747097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13747097" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747097-DSC_9793-e1653696755666.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13747097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747097-DSC_9793-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13747097" class="wp-caption-text">商会财务长秦文皓(左)，理事Jocelyn(右)。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚特兰大经文处处长王翼龙、经济组组长陈新发、领事张弘远、副领事钟贤玉，还有侨教中心的欧宏伟主任，也到场与侨胞们欢聚一堂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13747073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13747073" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747073-DSC_9836-e1653694970387.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13747073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747073-DSC_9836-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13747073" class="wp-caption-text">左起侨教中心欧宏伟主任，甘斯维尔台商会会长谷企平，经文处处长王翼龙，经文处领事张弘远。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谷企平向大家说明，这次烤肉的成本控制要感谢本地味全公司的经理王俊舜（Vincent），以及拥有七家餐馆的青商会员萧柏恩(Joseph)的协助。欧宏伟主任告诉众人，萧柏恩是被甄选上的“侨委会2022青商潜力之星”，在全美国仅有11个名额，竞争相当激烈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13747076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13747076" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747076-DSC_9838-1-e1653695259137.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13747076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747076-DSC_9838-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13747076" class="wp-caption-text">谷企平(持扩音器者)向萧柏恩(中)致谢。(吕尚明/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13747100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13747100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747100-DSC_9812-e1653697010462.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13747100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13747100-DSC_9812-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13747100" class="wp-caption-text">经文处副领事钟贤玉。(吕尚明/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>王翼龙处长称赞味全的香肠是让他怀念已久的家乡味，并表示很高兴美国的疫情阴影逐渐淡去，所以他幽默的说终于可以不须戴口罩而以“真面目”与大家见面。</p>

<p>商会财务长秦文皓（James）主持了精彩的趣味问答活动，猜对者都有现金犒赏，大家热烈参与，欢乐满场。最后进行摸彩，奖品除了处长、主任与组长带来的特别礼物，还有商会会长与其他侨界大佬鼎力赞助的奖品。商会前会长张崧轩原来已经慷慨解囊捐出一张价值1,300美元的沙发作为头奖，当场他大方地再捐出一份特奖，一张价值1,000美元的户外沙发。</p>
