<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>密西沙加面包蜂蜜节 享受美食音乐烟花表演</h2>

<h6>面包蜂蜜节(Streetsville, Town Square, June 2/19)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月26日】有50年历史的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%86%E5%B8%82%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85%E5%8F%8A%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C%E8%8A%82.md#1">密市面包及蜂蜜节</a>（Streetsville Founder’s Bread and Honey Festival）是安省的重要节日。每年有5万多人参加，被评为安省百佳节日。2022年的活动即将到来！<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/22/5/27/n13746137.md#1/d0e0831066893ca11a57e07a1488fa73" rel="attachment wp-att-13746146"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-13746146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746146-d0e0831066893ca11a57e07a1488fa73-450x200.png" alt="" width="781" b="347" /></a></p>
