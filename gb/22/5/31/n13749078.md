<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台AT-3教练机高雄坠毁 飞官徐大钧殉职</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749086-2205302324552378.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
