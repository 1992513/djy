<h2><strong>野牡丹Melastoma candidum D. Don野牡丹科</strong></h2>
<p>野牡丹又称大金香炉、豹牙兰、王不留行、九螺仔花、埔笔仔、狗力仔头、猪母草、地茄、豹牙郎木、活血丹、高脚山落苏、野石榴、金石榴、金鸡腿、牛牳稔、倒罐草、高脚稔、大杜桥、水多年、高脚埔梨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13732404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732404-425699_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13732404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732404-425699_medium-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732404" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732402" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732402-425697_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13732402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732402-425697_medium-600x570.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="570" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732402" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732401" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732401-425695_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13732401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732401-425695_medium-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732401" class="wp-caption-text">五五对分长短蕊，全身入药保平安。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>野牡丹为常绿小灌木，高可达1.5公尺，略作四方茎，嫩枝、叶片与萼筒密生倒伏状粗毛，叶对生。桃红五花瓣，硕大而明显，最特别在雄蕊，10?根分两型，长短各一半，花丝有明显关节，上段成弯勾，非常特别。蒴果，用种子繁殖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13732398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732398-425700_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13732398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732398-425700_medium-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732398" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732403-425698_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13732403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732403-425698_medium-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732403" class="wp-caption-text">常作为园艺观赏。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732399" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732399-425693_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13732399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732399-425693_medium-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732399" class="wp-caption-text">10根分两型，长短各一半，花丝有明显关节，上段成弯勾，非常特别。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>野牡丹常作为园艺观赏。是一种多用途的药用植物，能治疗肿毒、痢疾、胃病、关节炎、风湿、疮疥、肺痈、小儿发育不良、活血解毒。</p>
