<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《Drawn by stones》陶艺展在澳洲巡回展出</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738834-1652330561469-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>驻雪梨办事处范惠君处长与4A亚洲当代艺术中心Amrit Gill执行长、Lane Cove艺廊Rachael Kiang馆长与参展台裔艺术家李如诗在展品前合影。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月17日讯】《Drawn by stones》陶艺展由澳洲4A亚洲当代艺术中心策划，现正于Lane Cove Gallery展出，驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处范惠君处长及台侨应主办单位邀请，于2022年5月11日上午前往观展，4A中心执行长Amrit Gill特别到场致意，并亲自为大家导览展品。</p>
<p>范处长表示参展艺术家有两名来自台湾，包括旅英的陈文曦、旅澳的李如诗，都是在国际艺文界崭露头角的年轻女性。这次展览主题是探讨历史、土地与身份认同，文曦是用陶土绘制故乡台湾的地图，而如诗的作品透过模拟台南家乡大榕树的气根呈现思乡情怀，在在传达旅外国人的绵绵乡愁与寻根的心路历程。现场侨胞早年离乡背景移民来澳追求理想，相信对作品传达的意念，有深刻的体会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13738831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13738831" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738831-1652330544718.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13738831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738831-1652330544718.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13738831" class="wp-caption-text">范惠君处长致词说明台澳关系密切、共享多元文化价值并肯定台裔艺术家成就。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13738832" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13738832" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738832-1652330558115.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13738832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738832-1652330558115.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="241" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13738832" class="wp-caption-text">台裔艺术家李如诗解说其作品及意涵。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>范处长另指出台、澳共享自由民主价值与多元文化，透过艺术作品的呈现，更能反映出此种深厚的连结，盼未来迸发出更多合作火花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13738830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13738830" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738830-1652330003878.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13738830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738830-1652330003878.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="576" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13738830" class="wp-caption-text">李如诗作品。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13738836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13738836" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738836-Chen-wenxi-01.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13738836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13738836-Chen-wenxi-01.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="361" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13738836" class="wp-caption-text">陈文曦作品。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次国际艺展共邀获5位知名陶艺家参展，分别来自澳洲、香港与台湾，在雪梨展期自4月6日至5月14日止，后续将转赴艾丽丝泉、卧龙岗及其他澳洲城市巡展，展览相关讯息可至4A中心官网洽询。</p>
