<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>享受慢时光 手作花草刺绣：梅花</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863366-20221110_flower_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>自然系花草刺绣─梅花。（苹果屋提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong></p>
<p>：刺绣是传统的手工艺，历史悠久。《<ahref="https://reurl.cc/28oVGO" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">初学者的自然系花草刺绣【全图解】》</a>作者用不同针法呈现各种花朵样貌，像是红色的山茶花、白色的三叶草、黄色的油菜花，甚至还有花盆与整个花篮。只需绣线、绣针，以及一个小小的绣框，就能贴近自然，享受专属的慢时光。让我们跟着本文作者一起学梅花刺绣。
</p>
<h2>梅花</h2>
<p>
不畏寒冬、率先开花的春天使者。</p>
<p>将花瓣的正面和侧面的模样都呈现出来，就不会显得过于单调。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863370-P.143-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13863370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863370-P.143-1-600x101.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="101"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863370" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13863371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863371" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863371-P.143-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13863371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863371-P.143-2-600x495.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="495"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863371" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>梅花的绣法</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13863373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863373-P.144-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13863373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863373-P.144-1-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863373" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 花瓣用<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XQF9MZTJdNY" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">裂线绣</a>由外往内，一圈圈绣满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863374-P.144-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13863374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863374-P.144-2-600x465.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="465"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863374" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 树枝用<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_uNJOenXsAs" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">锁链绣</a>来绣。树枝与花瓣的接缝处要紧贴着绣在一起，中间不留空隙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863376" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863376-P.144-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13863376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863376-P.144-3-600x461.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="461"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863376" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 以<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4WPPe7QyPmA" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">缎面绣</a>将花瓣与花瓣之间、花萼、花蕾绣满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863377-P.145-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13863377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863377-P.145-1-600x464.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="464"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863377" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 用<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4WPPe7QyPmA" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">缎面绣</a>绣出稍微开花的花苞。在绣的时候要多加留意花苞与花萼间的分界线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13863379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13863379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863379-P.145-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13863379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13863379-P.145-2-600x463.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="463"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13863379" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 用卷绕二圈的<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Wo3UTn5IAlg" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">法国结粒绣</a>来绣雄蕊；再用<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U20q3SI3RcI" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">直针绣</a>呈现花丝。
