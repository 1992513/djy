<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这11种人气甜甜圈 你最爱哪一种？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870263-20221117_34donuts-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为美国纽约曼哈顿34街宾州车站内的Krispy Kreme甜甜圈店。甜甜圈摆满柜台，相当诱人食欲。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年11月21日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）喜欢<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%94%9C%E5%9C%88.md#1">甜甜圈</a>的朋友来对地方了！甜甜圈种类多样，有你爱不释手的吗？这次介绍11种最受<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD.md#1">美国</a>人欢迎的甜甜圈给你，看看有没有你喜欢的，赶快写入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>清单吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870265-20221117_34donuts2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870265-20221117_34donuts2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870265" class="wp-caption-text">图为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD.md#1">美国</a>纽约曼哈顿34街宾州车站内的Krispy Kreme<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%94%9C%E5%9C%88.md#1">甜甜圈</a>店柜台。（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美国大概是全球消费甜甜圈最多的国家了，24小时的甜甜圈店随处可见。从城市的连锁店到乡村的农场小铺，美式甜甜圈到底有多少种口味，估计没人能说清。因为甜甜圈的口味随着搭配的糖霜种类而变化，面包师不断发挥创意推出新口味的糖霜、甚至形状，甜甜圈就有新款推出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870303-shutterstock_2023193621.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13870303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870303-shutterstock_2023193621-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870303" class="wp-caption-text">土豆甜甜圈使用土豆淀粉或土豆泥制作。图为土豆甜甜圈。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然甜甜圈的口味可以多变，基础面团却只有三种。其中，酵母面团和蛋糕面糊最为流行，第三种的主料不是面粉，而是土豆泥或土豆淀粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870291-shutterstock_1611670780.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870291-shutterstock_1611670780-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870291" class="wp-caption-text">糖釉甜甜圈是环形甜甜圈中的超<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E6%B0%94.md#1">人气</a>款，它的表面裹着像蜡一样的透明糖釉。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>糖釉甜甜圈（Glazed Doughnut）</h2>
<p>糖釉甜甜圈最常见。经典的圆环形状，表面裹着像蜡一样的透明糖釉，也有的偏白色，不过都呈现出玻璃般的光泽。基础面团通常为酵母面团或蛋糕面糊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870290-shutterstock_1674590707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870290-shutterstock_1674590707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870290" class="wp-caption-text">糖霜甜甜圈的外表缤纷多彩，让人忍不住想多买几个。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>糖霜甜甜圈（Frosted Doughnut）</h2>
<p>这些甜甜圈使用发酵面团或蛋糕面糊制作，外表裹上各种颜色的糖霜或巧克力霜，最后撒上彩色糖针装饰，样子十分卡通。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870292-shutterstock_1567808380.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870292-shutterstock_1567808380-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870292" class="wp-caption-text">老派甜甜圈的口感外脆内软，其历史至少可以追溯到1830年。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>老派甜甜圈（Old-Fashioned Doughnut）</h2>
<p>恰如其名，这款甜甜圈的历史至少可以追溯到1830年。它通常使用蛋糕面糊，经过低温油炸形成不规则边缘，使其比常规版环形甜甜圈能挂住更多的糖釉，从而得到外脆内软的口感。原味糖釉和巧克力釉最为常见。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870281-shutterstock_4624900.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870281-shutterstock_4624900-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870281" class="wp-caption-text">用苹果汁作成的甜甜圈，苹果味浓郁，外层再搭配肉桂糖，每吃一口都让人倍感温暖和甜蜜。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>苹果汁甜甜圈（Cider Doughnut）</h2>
<p>它是环形甜甜圈中的经典口味，使用混合了苹果汁（Apple Cider）的蛋糕面糊来制作，外面裹上肉桂粉和细砂糖，拥有同肉桂软饼干一样的棕色外表。喜欢肉桂糖的朋友一定会喜欢它。对了，想吃特色风味吗？离开美国的大城市，找到坐落在乡村之中的苹果农场，你会在那里品尝到极具特色的苹果汁甜甜圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870311" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870311-shutterstock_1748497076.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870311-shutterstock_1748497076-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870311" class="wp-caption-text">环形甜甜圈中间的剩余面团，作成了这种圆球形的点心。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>甜甜圈洞（Doughnut Hole）</h2>
<p>物如其名，它使用环形甜甜圈中间那块剩余面团制作。与常规甜甜圈制作方法一样，面团经过油炸后，在表面裹上糖粉或糖霜即成，有的口味添加了注心奶油（夹心奶油）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870315-shutterstock_1014898309.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870315-shutterstock_1014898309-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870315" class="wp-caption-text">拿坡里甜甜圈是一种意大利的油炸点心，通常直径约为4英寸。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>拿坡里甜甜圈（Zeppole）</h2>
<p>这种油炸甜甜圈来自意大利，使用酵母面团或蛋糕面糊制作，油炸后撒上糖粉或装点可口的甜点酱即成，有些风味会添加奶油或巧克力内馅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870287-shutterstock_1035977260.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870287-shutterstock_1035977260-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870287" class="wp-caption-text">果冻甜甜圈是无孔款式的经典代表，内馅通常塞满果冻，尤其受孩子喜欢。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>果冻甜甜圈（Jelly Doughnut）</h2>
<p>甜甜圈除了环形的，还有无孔的，果冻甜甜圈是其中最见的种类。它的制作方法简单，将发酵面团炸好后，往中间填充果冻、果酱或巧克力酱等内馅即成；有些风味，表面搭配糖粉、巧克力釉、糖釉或糖针等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870284-shutterstock_592164785.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870284-shutterstock_592164785-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870284" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿奶油甜甜圈于2003年被指定为马萨诸塞州的官方甜甜圈。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>波士顿奶油甜甜圈（Boston Cream Doughnut）</h2>
<p>这款甜甜圈，像不像迷你版的波士顿奶油派？它与波士顿奶油派齐名，都是马萨诸塞州的官方甜点。这款甜甜圈用酵母面团制作，里面灌满奶油注心，表面裹了巧克力，在美国拥有超高<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E6%B0%94.md#1">人气</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870286-shutterstock_1822147061.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870286-shutterstock_1822147061-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870286" class="wp-caption-text">1986年，贝涅饼被指定为路易斯安那州的官方甜甜圈。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>贝涅饼（Beignet）</h2>
<p>贝涅饼是一种源于法国的无孔甜甜圈，金黄的外表还撒了一层糖粉，也有的加入水果、巧克力或奶油内馅，类似泡芙的风格。它在美国新奥尔良特别受欢迎，是当地狂欢节的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870295" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870295-shutterstock_1895173312.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870295-shutterstock_1895173312-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870295" class="wp-caption-text">这种甜甜圈裹上枫糖釉时，被称为枫糖棒。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>长形约翰甜甜圈（Long John Doughnut）</h2>
<p>甜甜圈并不一定都是圆形的。长形约翰甜甜圈是一款长方形的酵母甜甜圈，美国有些地方称之为泡芙。有的品种会填充蛋奶冻或果冻，有的则没有；外层的口味有糖霜、巧克力，或者枫糖釉等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13870298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13870298" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870298-shutterstock_520211284.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13870298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/11/id13870298-shutterstock_520211284-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13870298" class="wp-caption-text">图为裹糖扭花甜甜圈。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>扭花甜甜圈（Twist Doughnut）</h2>
<p>传统的扭花甜甜圈由酵母面团制成，质地蓬松、柔软。表面裹有肉桂糖或肉桂糖釉的口味比较流行，其它的口味还有裹糖、巧克力釉、糖釉或枫糖釉。◇</p>
