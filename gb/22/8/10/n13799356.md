<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：狗狗“一脸陶醉”嗅花香 模样超可爱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799460-Sarah-Geers-dog-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美国有一只2岁大的狗狗，它最大的爱好就是去闻各种各样的花花草草。（Sarah Geers提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年08月10日讯】（英文大纪元记者Louise Chambers报导／张雨霏编译）狗狗的嗅觉非常灵敏，总是喜欢闻来闻去。美国有一只2岁大的狗狗，它最大的爱好就是去闻各种各样的花花草草。</p>
<p>2岁的芬恩（Finn）是布鲁塞尔格林芬犬和狮子狗的混血儿，从九周大起就与25岁的主人莎拉?吉尔斯（Sarah Geers）生活在一起。在三个月大时，它第一次对鲜花表现出浓厚的兴趣。</p>
<p>“我看到它在我父亲的百日草花园里不停地嗅花香，看起来‘一脸陶醉’！”注册护士莎拉告诉《大纪元时报》，“我不失时机马上拍下几张照片，但没想到这仅仅是个开始。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13799455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13799455" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799455-Finley-the-flower-dog-16.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13799455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799455-Finley-the-flower-dog-16.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="449"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13799455" class="wp-caption-text">（Sarah Geers提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主人渐渐发现，芬恩几乎对所有花朵都很好奇，而且它不会啃咬，只是轻轻地凑上去闻那淡淡的花香。</p>
<p>到了春天百花盛开的季节，芬恩每天最期待的就是出门散步，因为路上可以闻到各种花香。它尤其喜欢在大片的蒲公英地里跑来跑去，时不时地停下来闻闻花朵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13799452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13799452" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799452-Finley-the-flower-dog-8-1200x904.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13799452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799452-Finley-the-flower-dog-8-1200x904.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="451"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13799452" class="wp-caption-text">（Sarah Geers提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13799451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13799451" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799451-Finley-the-flower-dog-5-1200x1500.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13799451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799451-Finley-the-flower-dog-5-1200x1500.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="564"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13799451" class="wp-caption-text">（Sarah Geers提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有一次，它从木栅栏的空隙探过去，仰头去闻盛开的三色堇，兴奋地摇着尾巴，而且闻着闻着还闭上了眼睛，就像人闻到香味时沉醉其中的样子，这一场景实在可爱极了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13799457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13799457" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799457-Finley-the-flower-dog-18.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13799457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799457-Finley-the-flower-dog-18.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="602"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13799457" class="wp-caption-text">（Sarah Geers提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>事实上，芬恩不仅喜欢新鲜的花朵，而且也喜欢跟主人一起去逛超市的装饰假花区。每次从那里经过的时候，它都会乖乖地站在购物车的架子上，一脸期待地探出小鼻子戳过去。</p>
<p>据莎拉介绍，在所有的花朵中，芬恩最喜欢鲜艳的三色堇以及五颜六色的百日草。但它也异常聪明，对一些“具危险性的花朵”一直保持谨慎态度，也从来不去闻她的天竺葵或大丽花，因为它们不适合宠物，可能会引起过敏反应。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13799454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13799454" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799454-Finley-the-flower-dog-13.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13799454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799454-Finley-the-flower-dog-13.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13799454" class="wp-caption-text">（Sarah Geers提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13799453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13799453" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799453-Finley-the-flower-dog-10-1200x1664.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13799453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799453-Finley-the-flower-dog-10-1200x1664.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="625"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13799453" class="wp-caption-text">（Sarah Geers提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>莎拉将她可爱的小狗闻花香的许多滑稽照片分享到<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/finn.the.griffy.mix/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">Instagram</a>上。</p>
<p>虽然芬恩很爱闻花香，但它最喜欢的还是人类朋友。“它是我见过的最快乐的狗狗，性格也很温顺，它喜欢被人关注，也喜欢跟人玩耍，如果有人愿意摸摸它，它就更开心了。”莎拉告诉本报，“它的爱好还包括乘车出游、像猫一样懒洋洋地躺在沙发扶手上，以及带着泳圈在游泳池中嬉戏，不一而足。”</p>
<p>除了芬恩，莎拉和她的新婚丈夫马修（Matthew）还饲养着另一名4岁大的狗狗哈利（Harley）。两只狗狗感情非常好，整天在一起玩耍、打闹，给两位年轻的主人带去无限欢乐！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13799458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13799458" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799458-Finn-ET-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13799458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13799458-Finn-ET-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13799458" class="wp-caption-text">（Sarah Geers提供）</figcaption></figure>
