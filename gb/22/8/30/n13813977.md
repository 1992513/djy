<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>兰道夫第3届中秋节 上万人联欢</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813959-DSC00567-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>8月28日晚上，众多游客前来庆祝第3届兰道夫中秋节。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年08月31日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨兰道夫镇报导）在8月27日傍晚的清凉微风中，麻州兰道夫镇（Randolph）南部公园（South Park）点亮了挂在街道半空的一串串彩色灯笼。这是当地第3届中秋联欢，迎来了上万的游客。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13813957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813957" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813957-DSC00603.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813957" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813957-DSC00603-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813957" class="wp-caption-text">第3届兰道夫中秋节8月27至28日举办，入口颇有节日气息。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13813958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813958" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813958-DSC00584.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813958" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813958-DSC00584-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813958" class="wp-caption-text">兰道夫中秋节游客众多。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>按照主办方的计划，联欢活动本应从26日下午5点开始，然而当天的暴风雨迫使开幕活动延期一天。</p>
<p>周六的庆典开始后不久，天色渐渐变暗，场地内的彩色灯笼，以及巨大的“月亮”和“玉兔”纷纷点亮，渲染浓厚的节日氛围。公园主道上的游客也越来越多，道路两边的很多餐饮和活动摊位前方都排起了长长的队伍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13813953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813953-DSC00626.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813953-DSC00626-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813953" class="wp-caption-text">巨大的月球和玉兔装饰颇有气氛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13813964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813964-DSC00558.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813964-DSC00558-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813964" class="wp-caption-text">美食摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13813968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813968-DSC00497.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813968-DSC00497-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813968" class="wp-caption-text">越南裔的诗人参与第3届兰道夫中秋节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周六和周日两天的节庆中，主舞台上都有连续不断的歌舞演出。开幕式上有多位政要嘉宾，包括前麻州国际贸易副州务卿、越南裔的范文南，麻州参议员布雷迪（Michael Brady），以及兰道夫镇的多位官员等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13813954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813954-DSC00616.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813954-DSC00616-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813954" class="wp-caption-text">越南裔孩子们表演传统节目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13813966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813966-DSC00526.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813966-DSC00526-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813966" class="wp-caption-text">灯笼制作教学的摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周六晚上，华林武馆的舞狮队及主办方志愿者们为游客带来了锣鼓喧闹的“灯笼游行”。周日晚上，主舞台上还有颁奖典礼，嘉奖当地作文比赛中获奖的中小学生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13813963" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813963-DSC00578.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813963-DSC00578-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813963" class="wp-caption-text">主舞台上有连续不断的歌舞表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13813955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813955" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813955-DSC00600.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813955-DSC00600-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813955" class="wp-caption-text">甘蔗汁摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次兰道夫中秋联欢的游客每人需要购买3美元的门票，10岁以下小孩和65岁以上长者免费进场。</p>
<p>活动筹备主任阮琳（Lynn Nguyen）说，周六这一晚就卖出了4000多张门票；周日有很多原计划周五过来的游客前来游玩，卖票数量也与周六差不多。她说，这两天的活动总共应有上万的游客。</p>
<p>兰道夫中秋联欢的创办人阮安妃（Anhphi Nguyen）也说：“非常忙，很高兴能这么忙。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13813969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813969-DSC00503.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813969-DSC00503-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813969" class="wp-caption-text">8月28日傍晚，众多游客在门口排队，等候进入第3届兰道夫中秋节庆典场地。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13813967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813967" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813967-DSC00514.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813967-DSC00514-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813967" class="wp-caption-text">兰道夫中秋节由麻州越南裔团体主办。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13813965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13813965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813965-DSC00533.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13813965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13813965-DSC00533-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13813965" class="wp-caption-text">众多游客排队等候美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>兰道夫镇的中秋节庆典是由越南裔美国人主办的公益活动。活动缘起是越南裔牙医阮安妃博士的20岁侄女死于癌症。失去亲人的痛苦令她萌生资助癌症研究的愿望。她与越南裔朋友们为筹款资助许愿基金会和吉米基金会研究，预防和抵抗癌症，于是决定举办这个为期3天的传统中秋节庆祝活动。这是仅次于新年的盛大节日，今年已是第3届了。◇</p>
