<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【视频】波士顿华埠第52届中秋节热闹举办</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804136-DSC09916-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff
- 8月14日上午，洪青体育会舞狮，为波士顿第52届华埠中秋联欢会揭开序幕。（刘景烨/大纪元）
```
<h6>8月14日上午，洪青体育会舞狮，为波士顿第52届华埠中秋联欢会揭开序幕。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）8月14日从上午一直到傍晚，波士顿华埠地区人山人海，游客络绎不绝。由纽英崙中华公所主办的“第52届华埠中秋联欢会”吸引了数十个摊位及成千上万的游客。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-d4faa87e-ba11-4fd0-9c6d-b5363ce70b81" data-id="player-d4faa87e-ba11-4fd0-9c6d-b5363ce70b81"></div></div><a src="//youmaker.com/assets/player/d4faa87e-ba11-4fd0-9c6d-b5363ce70b81?r=16x9&amp;d=185&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F22%2F8%2F17%2Fn13804159.md#1"></a>
<p>上午10点，华埠Harrison街上锣鼓齐鸣。洪青体育会的彩狮在舞台上翻腾，为节日庆典揭开序幕。在舞台下，数百观众纷纷热情鼓掌与喝采。</p>
<p>在一整天的活动中，主舞台上的表演没有间断。多个团体为游客们带来了舞狮、舞蹈、武术、唱歌等各种文艺表演。与此同时，在Beach街华埠牌楼外的公园里还有粤曲演出，也吸引众多游人围观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13804134" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804134" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804134-DSC00097.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804134-DSC00097-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804134" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿第52届华埠中秋联欢会吸引大量游客前来参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13804121" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804121" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804121-DSC00043.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804121" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804121-DSC00043-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804121" class="wp-caption-text">游客们驻足浏览摊位上的商品。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自昆士市（Quincy）的游客、生物技术从业者Scott说，他来到昆士12年了，经常参加当地以及波士顿华埠的中秋节庆典。他说，他非常喜欢这些活动，享受这人声鼎沸的环境。谈到他对中秋节庆典最热衷的部分，Scott说：“很多漂亮的姑娘，还有观看舞狮，以及功夫表演。这些风景、声音、人群，都非常有意思！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13804120" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804120" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804120-2022-08-16-at-1.42.42-PM.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804120-2022-08-16-at-1.42.42-PM-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804120" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华书法会的艺术家为游客书写名字。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13804123" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804123" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804123-DSC00083.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804123" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804123-DSC00083-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804123" class="wp-caption-text">璞石中华文化工作室带来手工艺摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13804176" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804176" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804176-Screen-Shot-2022-08-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804176" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804176-Screen-Shot-2022-08-16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804176" class="wp-caption-text">璞石中华文化工作室带来书法摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13804132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804132-2022-08-16-at-1.42.10-PM.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804132-2022-08-16-at-1.42.10-PM-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804132" class="wp-caption-text">8月14日华埠中秋联欢会中的烧烤摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了舞台表演之外，华埠的主要街道上还有数十个摊位陈列，数量比2021年大大增加。前来摆摊的有美食、杂货、首饰等商家，有纽英崙中华书法会、璞石中华文化工作室等文化艺术机构，也有法轮大法等信仰团体。主办方纽英崙中华公所亦在Harrison街设有摊位，为人们排忧解难。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13804156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804156-DSC09979.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804156-DSC09979-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804156" class="wp-caption-text">华埠中秋联欢会人声鼎沸。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13804133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804133" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804133-DSC00047.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804133" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804133-DSC00047-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804133" class="wp-caption-text">法轮大法学员为中秋联欢会的游客们带来莲花折纸。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中华公所主席雷国辉在上午的开幕式中首先致词。他特别感谢了各位赞助商的支持，并祝观众们愉快地度过佳节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13804124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804124" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804124-DSC09922.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804124-DSC09922-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804124" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉感谢各位赞助商支持华埠中秋节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）带着2个孩子前来参与庆祝。她高兴地说，这是她近期参与的人群最庞大的活动。</p>
<p>“希望大家在这里的时候，不只是欣赏精彩表演，同时也在本地商家购物，吃午餐，喝奶茶。”吴弭说。她还希望，人们不是只在庆典的时候才来唐人街，而是在全年各个时候都来支持这里的社区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13804137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804137-DSC09927.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804137" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804137-DSC09927-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804137" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）参与第52届华埠中秋联欢会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天还有多位政要嘉宾来到华埠庆祝佳节，其中包括正在波士顿参访的中华民国侨务委员会副委员长徐佳青。麻州本地嘉宾有州务卿高文（William Galvin）、麻州众议员黄子安（Donald Wong）、波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）、萨福克县治安官汤普金斯（Steven W. Tompkins）、萨福克县检察长海登（Kevin Hayden）、波士顿警察局社区服务总监陈孔恩（Jimmy Chin）、侨教中心主任潘昭荣、侨务委员郭竞儒、陈仕维等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13804135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804135" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804135-DSC09903.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804135-DSC09903-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804135" class="wp-caption-text">政要嘉宾们参与华埠中秋联欢会，在舞台上合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>副委员长徐佳青祝福大家欢度佳节，健康幸福。她亦欢迎大家去台湾旅游，欣赏美景，品尝美食，度过愉快的时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13804138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804138-DSC09938.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804138-DSC09938-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804138" class="wp-caption-text">中华民国侨务委员会副委员长徐佳青8月14日来到波士顿华埠，为中秋联欢会的游客们送上祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13804125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13804125" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804125-DSC09958.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13804125" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13804125-DSC09958-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13804125" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议会议长Ed Flynn参与华埠中秋联欢会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>代表华埠地区的市议长费连说，亚裔群体在疫情期间受到歧视，备受打击。因而他的重要工作之一，就是确保每个人都受到尊重，享有尊严。他感谢中华公所为华埠带来如此精彩的活动，也感谢在场的警察们辛勤工作，维护秩序。◇</p>
