<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>减肥无麸质瘦身餐 一次轻松备齐一周餐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_614415680-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>力行无麸质瘦身餐，3个月减10公斤！示意图。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>不吃面粉，无麸质瘦身餐，让75公斤的胖妹变身IG减重网红。韩国知名IG网红<strong>崔善女</strong>现身说法“成功瘦身”的技巧！本文示范如何一次备齐一周份，根据食材和分量，教你事先快速备料，避免懒得备餐而减重失败。</p>
<p><strong>跟着我这样做：一周一次轻松备餐</strong></p>
<p>减肥的成败取决于备餐时间的长短与难易度。昨天还很高昂的减肥意志却会被今天处理食材的繁杂手续立刻浇熄。一周只要一次就好，事先准备好一周要吃的食物吧！一周当中只要持续从冰箱里轻松拿出已经准备好的食材来吃，减肥就成功了！</p>
<p><strong>1. 蔬菜、水果</strong></p>
<p>一般蔬果大部分都要先泡在加入一滴醋或苏打粉的水中三至五分钟，再以流动的水清洗干净，沥干水分后冷藏保存。保存时建议放进铺有厨房纸巾的密封容器中，再放入冰箱里的蔬菜室。</p>
<p><strong>保鲜期较短蔬菜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893642" style="width: 335px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893642-P.20-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893642-P.20-1.jpg" alt="" width="335" b="268" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893642" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>高丽菜、紫甘蓝</strong></p>
<p>先撕成方便用手取食的大小，浸泡在溶有苏打粉的水五分钟后，以流动的水清洗多次，直到清洗干净。待除去水分后，放进铺有厨房纸巾的密封容器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13893643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893643" style="width: 239px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893643-P.20-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893643-P.20-2.jpg" alt="" width="239" b="269" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893643" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>莴苣、紫苏叶</strong></p>
<p>像这类保鲜期较短的蔬菜建议只要买非常少量即可。买回来之后不要清洗，直接放进铺有厨房纸巾的密封容器里，避免跟空气接触。要吃之前再用流动的水充分清洗干净即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13893644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893644" style="width: 413px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893644-P.20-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893644-P.20-3.jpg" alt="" width="413" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893644" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>小黄瓜</strong></p>
<p>先把盐巴抹在小黄瓜的表面后，再用水冲洗，将苦涩味洗掉。待除去水分后，每条小黄瓜都用一张厨房纸巾包好放在冰箱保存。水分太多就容易腐坏，所以一定要除去水分。<strong>※</strong>请尽快食用。</p>
<p><strong>2. 金针菇</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893645" style="width: 324px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893645-P.20-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893645-P.20-4.jpg" alt="" width="324" b="218" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893645" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>根部用刀子切除后用手撕成小条，摊平放在铺有厨房纸巾的盘子上，之后铺上一层厨房纸巾再放上金针菇，分层堆叠。然后再装在密封容器内保管，以免产生水汽。每次要吃的时候再用流动的水清洗。</p>
<p>※请尽快食用。</p>
<p><strong>3.地瓜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893646" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893646-P.20-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893646-P.20-5.jpg" alt="" width="299" b="221" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893646" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用流动的水洗净后放入锅中，再把水装入锅中，水位要盖过地瓜，煮十五至二十分钟。待地瓜冷却后分装，冷冻保存，避免与空气接触。只要在五分钟前拿出来退冰，就能尝到如雪酪般清凉又顺口的口感。</p>
<p><strong>4.鹰嘴豆</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893647" style="width: 326px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893647-P.21-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893647-P.21-1.jpg" alt="" width="326" b="223" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893647" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>食用前一天请充分浸泡，再以流动的水洗净后备用。鹰嘴豆放入锅中后，再把水装入锅中，水位要盖过鹰嘴豆，煮十至十五分钟。待鹰嘴豆冷却后，放进铺有厨房纸巾的密封容器保存。</p>
<p>※鹰嘴豆容易腐坏，请分成少量多次调理。</p>
<p><strong>5. 南瓜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893648" style="width: 324px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893648-P.21-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893648-P.21-2.jpg" alt="" width="324" b="220" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893648" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将南瓜放进微波炉加热五分钟左右，再将松软的南瓜切成入口大小后放进夹链袋保存。需要时，取出食用分量放进微波炉加热一分钟左右即可。</p>
<p><strong>6. 绿花椰菜、白花椰菜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893649" style="width: 287px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893649-P.21-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893649-P.21-3.jpg" alt="" width="287" b="224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893649" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将绿花椰菜或白花椰菜切成适口大小后，泡在溶有苏打粉的水五分钟，再以流动的水清洗干净。之后放入滚水中汆烫一至两分钟，放凉后再放进铺有厨房纸巾的密封容器中保存。</p>
<p><strong>※</strong>建议一次分量不要太多。绿花椰菜若汆烫超过十分钟，营养素会被破坏，所以只要放入少量、稍微汆烫即可。尽量要将叶缝清洗干净，也可以在水中加点盐巴后再汆烫。</p>
<p><strong>7.杂谷饭</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893650" style="width: 325px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893650-P.21-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893650-P.21-4.jpg" alt="" width="325" b="217" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893650" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将杂谷米清洗干净后煮熟放凉，再分装至小的密封容器中保存。要吃之前再微波解冻。</p>
<p><strong>8. 菠菜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893651" style="width: 224px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893651-P.21-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893651-P.21-5.jpg" alt="" width="224" b="228" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893651" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菠菜的叶片和根部容易沾上许多泥土或异物，所以要以流动的水清洗多次。先在锅中装入水位超过菠菜的水量，再放入二分之一匙的盐巴，待煮滚后再放进菠菜，汆烫十五至二十秒即可。</p>
<p><strong>9. 玉米</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13893652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13893652" style="width: 373px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893652-P.21-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13893652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13893652-P.21-6.jpg" alt="" width="373" b="217" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13893652" class="wp-caption-text">（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>玉米可以用微波炉调理或是放入锅中炖煮，料理方式相当多元。我最喜欢的方法是先处理玉米须，充分洗净后放进锅中，再把水装入锅中，水位要盖过玉米，之后盖上锅盖，以中火煮三十至四十分钟，也可以按照个人喜好加糖或盐巴调味。</p>
