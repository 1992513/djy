<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>六大动词 10天速成英语表达（4）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13882056-Snap1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（图／大是文化提供）
</h6>

<p><center><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/22/12/23/n13890691.md#1">接上篇</a></center></p>
<h2>句型3：S ＋ V ＋ C</h2>
<p>句型3 则是以be 动词及连缀动词为主，例如：taste（尝起来）、feel（觉得）、smell（闻起来）、find（发觉）、look、seem（似乎），通常用来表达当时的状态及感觉。</p>
<p>在套用句型3 的时候，只要记得：<strong>这里的补语不是动词的直接受词，而是当作主词补语，补充说明主词。</strong></p>
<p><strong>① </strong><strong>用be </strong><strong>动词表达身份或角色，把名词当作补语</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895608-wenfaP46-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895608-wenfaP46-2-600x136.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="136" /></a><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895609-wenfaP47-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895609-wenfaP47-1-600x367.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="367" /></a><strong>② </strong><strong>用be </strong><strong>动词来表达状态，把形容词当作补语</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895596-wenfaP47-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895596-wenfaP47-2-600x477.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="477" /></a><strong>③ </strong><strong>用连缀动词来表达状态及情绪</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895597-wenfaP48-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895597-wenfaP48-1-600x263.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="263" /></a>在工作场合，我们也能运用句型3，充分表达各种情境及情感。例如：<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895598-wenfaP48-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895598-wenfaP48-2-600x265.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="265" /></a>句型3 虽然很好用，但要避免中式英文。因为中文很习惯用“是”当句子的动词，然后再接动词，此时直译成英文，就很容易造成文法错误 。例如：</p>
<h2><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895599-wenfaP49-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895599-wenfaP49-1-600x175.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="175" /></a><strong>句型4</strong><strong>：S </strong><strong>＋ V </strong><strong>＋ O1 </strong><strong>＋O2</strong></h2>
<p>第4 种和第3 种很像，但在句型4，一个动词后面有两个受词，我们一般会将这类型的及物动词，称为授与动词，像是bring（带来）、buy（买）、get、give、teach（教）、tell（说）、show（表现）、offer（提供）等，都是很常用的授与动词。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895600-wenfaP49-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895600-wenfaP49-2-600x182.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="182" /></a><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895601-wenfaP50-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895601-wenfaP50-1-600x188.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="188" /></a>这类句型中，有两个受词，一个为直接受词（通常是物品），一个为间接受词（通常是人），以下为3 种常见的用法。</p>
<p><strong>① S </strong><strong>＋ V </strong><strong>＋O1</strong><strong>＋O2</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895602-wenfaP50-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895602-wenfaP50-2-600x144.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="144" /></a><strong>② S </strong><strong>＋ V </strong><strong>＋O2</strong><strong>＋ to </strong><strong>＋O1</strong></p>
<p>搭配介系词to 的授与动词，主要有：bring、give、lend（借）、sell（卖）、tell、read（阅读）、show、pass（传递）、write（写）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895603-wenfaP50-3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895603-wenfaP50-3-600x136.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="136" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895604-wenfaP51-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895604-wenfaP51-1-600x297.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="297" /></a><strong>③ S </strong><strong>＋ V </strong><strong>＋O2</strong><strong>＋ for </strong><strong>＋O1</strong></p>
<p>搭配介系词for 的授与动词，主要有以下：buy、make、find、get、order（命令）、choose（选择、决定）。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895605-wenfaP51-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895605-wenfaP51-2-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895606-wenfaP52-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-13895606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13895606-wenfaP52-1-600x150.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="150" /></a><strong>句型</strong><strong>5</strong><strong>：</strong><strong>S </strong><strong>＋</strong><strong> V </strong><strong>＋</strong><strong>O</strong><strong>＋</strong><strong> C</strong></h2>
<p>句型5 和句型3 有点类似，在动词后方加上受词，再透过受词补语（形容词或名词）做补充说明。不过，句型5 不能使用be 动词，而是像call（给～取名）、find 这类不完全及物动词（按：除了受词外，需要补语补充说明的动词）。</p>
