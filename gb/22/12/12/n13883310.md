<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：2022年野生动物摄影师获奖照片</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883315-ET-Junji-Takasago-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year--600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本艺术摄影师Junji Takasago克服了安第斯山脉的高原反应，拍摄了一组智利火烈鸟的精美照片。(Junji Takasago/Wildlife Photographer of the Year)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年12月12日讯】（英文大纪元Epoch Inspired Staff报导／繁星编译）刚刚发布的2022“年度野生动物摄影师”的获奖照片非常精彩，这使人很容易忽视摄影师拍摄这些照片所付出的努力。这些天才摄影师让千载难逢的邂逅显得如此容易捕捉。</p>
<p>“年度野生动物摄影师”由伦敦自然历史博物馆开发和制作。从来自93个国家／地区的38,575幅作品挑选出顶级照片，需要大量的工作。所有获奖照片将于今年10月14日至2023年7月2日在伦敦自然历史博物馆展出。</p>
<p>该系列突出了精选的最佳自然摄影作品，展示了令人叹为观止的风景、奇异的动物和罕见的动物行为。</p>
<p>两位大奖得主——“年度野生动物摄影师”和“年度年轻野生动物摄影师”——是从19个类别的获奖者中选出的。</p>
<p><strong>美国Karine Aigner的“The Big Buzz”|获胜者：无脊椎动物</strong></p>
<p>美国摄影师卡琳?艾格纳（Karine Aigner）凭借她拍摄的雄性仙人掌蜜蜂嗡嗡作响的球体照片赢得了大奖。在欣赏这张获奖照片的同时，人们可能永远不会意识到拍摄它需要躺在南德克萨斯牧场灼热的沙滩上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13883317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13883317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883317-ET-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year-Karine-Aigner-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13883317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883317-ET-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year-Karine-Aigner-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13883317" class="wp-caption-text">(Karine Aigner/Wildlife Photographer of the Year)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>日本Junji Takasago的“天堂火烈鸟”|优胜者：自然艺术</strong></p>
<p>在安第斯山脉的高处，日本艺术摄影师Junji Takasago克服了高原反应，拍摄了一组智利火烈鸟的精美照片。</p>
<p>乌尤尼盐沼位于安第斯山脉的高处，是世界上最大的盐田。根据自然历史博物馆的新闻稿，它也是玻利维亚最大的锂矿之一，威胁着这些火烈鸟的未来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13883318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13883318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883318-ET-Junji-Takasago-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year--600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13883318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883318-ET-Junji-Takasago-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year--600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13883318" class="wp-caption-text">(Junji Takasago/Wildlife Photographer of the Year)</figcaption></figure>
<p>水下摄影师兼生物学家洛朗?巴列斯塔（Laurent Ballesta）勇敢地潜入冰点以下，在南极海床上100多英尺深的地方展示奇特海洋生物的超凡脱俗的奇观。</p>
<p>这张令人难以置信的照片显示了一个树状海绵，上面覆盖着巨大的带状蠕虫和海星。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13883320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13883320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883320-ET-Laurent-Ballesta-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year-600x401.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13883320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883320-ET-Laurent-Ballesta-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year-600x401.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13883320" class="wp-caption-text">(Laurent Ballesta/Wildlife Photographer of the Year)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>美国／日本Tony Wu的“流星”|优胜者：水下</strong></p>
<p>摄影师托尼?吴（Tony Wu）发现自己被困在一个封闭的小海湾里，周围的水里充满了巨型海星的精子和卵子，他努力捕捉这种生物的奇妙的“舞蹈”。这是一个紧张的竞争，但值得，吴获得了水下类别的最高奖项。</p>
<p>银河般的场景展现了海星在水中帮助释放卵子和精子的“舞姿”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13883321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13883321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883321-ET-Tony-Wu-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year--600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13883321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883321-ET-Tony-Wu-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year--600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13883321" class="wp-caption-text">(Tony Wu/Wildlife Photographer of the Year)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>波兰Mateusz Piesiak的“鸟类剧场”|获奖者：新星组合奖</strong></p>
<p>新星组合奖获得者Mateusz Piesiak年轻时曾在大流行封锁期间外出探索当地的城市景观，他拍摄了当地鸟类的精彩照片。</p>
<p>他将遥控相机放在芦苇床的泥地上，捕捉到一只游隼飞过，一些黑腹鹡鸲也飞起来的瞬间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13883333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13883333" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883333-ET-Mateusz-Piesiak-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year-600x356.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13883333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883333-ET-Mateusz-Piesiak-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13883333" class="wp-caption-text">(Mateusz Piesiak/Wildlife Photographer of the Year)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>印度Anand Nambiar的“大悬崖追逐”|获胜者：行为——哺乳动物</strong></p>
<p>印度摄影师阿南德?南比亚（Anand Nambiar）捕捉到了雪豹冲向陡峭边缘的喜马拉雅山羊群的精彩瞬间。这张照片是在印度喜马偕尔邦的基伯野生动物保护区拍摄的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13883335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13883335" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883335-ET-Anand-Nambiar-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year--600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13883335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13883335-ET-Anand-Nambiar-Wildlife-Photographer-of-the-Year--600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13883335" class="wp-caption-text">(Anand Nambiar/Wildlife Photographer of the Year)</figcaption></figure>
