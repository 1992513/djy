<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>醋饭搭配不同颜色食材 手作缤纷散寿司</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537931-P.056-057-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>散寿司、寿司卷、球寿司。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>日文“散らし”有“散放”的意思，而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%A3%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">散寿司</a>就是将醋饭铺满整个碗，再散放各种食材。搭配各种不同颜色，可以让整道料理看起来更诱人。有些人喜欢放上生鱼片，不过要是等不及买回食用级生鲑鱼，就用鲜虾享受散寿司的美味吧！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%A3%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">散寿司</a>作法</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13538022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538022-fba16628519d96b867b92149294601e2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538022" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538022-fba16628519d96b867b92149294601e2-450x236.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="236" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538022" class="wp-caption-text">散寿司。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong><u>40</u></strong><strong><u>～45分钟（＋泡<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8F%87.md#1">香菇</a>20分钟）／2～3人份／酱烧香菇<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E7%93%A2.md#1">干瓢</a>冷藏7天</u></strong></span></p>
<p>糯米 2杯（泡水前，320g）<br />
冷冻虾仁 7只（约100g，或用鲜虾去壳）<br />
水 2杯（400mL）<br />
清酒（或米酒） 1大匙<br />
海带 5×5cm1片<br />
食用油 1/2大匙<br />
熟四季豆长段 2根</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>蛋丝</strong></span></p>
<p>鸡蛋 2颗<br />
糖 1小匙<br />
鲣鱼露 1小匙</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>调味醋</strong></span></p>
<p>糖 1大匙<br />
盐 1小匙</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>酱烧<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8F%87.md#1">香菇</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E7%93%A2.md#1">干瓢</a></strong></span></p>
<p>干香菇 5朵（15g）<br />
干瓢 20cm10条<br />
糖 1大匙<br />
酱油 1大匙<br />
味醂 1大匙<br />
泡香菇水 1杯（200mL）</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>醋腌<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E8%97%95.md#1">莲藕</a></strong></span></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E8%97%95.md#1">莲藕</a> 直径5cm、长3cm（50g）<br />
糖 1又1/2大匙<br />
醋 3大匙<br />
水 1/4杯（50mL）<br />
盐 少许</p>
<p><strong>酱烧香菇干瓢</strong></p>
<p>1.用热水泡干香菇20分钟，等膨胀再切蒂头，记得留下泡香菇的水。干瓢放热水中泡5分钟后把水倒掉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537933" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537933-P.59-01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537933-P.59-01-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537933" class="wp-caption-text">1 用热水泡干香菇20分钟，等膨胀再切蒂头，记得留下泡香菇的水。干瓢放热水中泡5分钟后把水倒掉。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.在汤锅中放入泡好的香菇和干瓢，以及酱烧的所有材料，盖上锅盖，用中火煮10分钟后转小火，再煮8～10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537934" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537934-P.59-02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537934-P.59-02-450x303.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537934" class="wp-caption-text">2 在汤锅中放入泡好的香菇和干瓢，以及酱烧的所有材料，盖上锅盖，用中火煮10分钟后转小火，再煮8～10分钟。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>醋腌莲藕</strong></p>
<p>3.将莲藕去皮、尽可能切成薄片后泡在冷水里，洗净后捞出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537935" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537935-P.59-03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537935-P.59-03-450x259.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="259" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537935" class="wp-caption-text">3 将莲藕去皮、尽可能切成薄片后泡在冷水里，洗净后捞出。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.汤锅中倒入2杯水和少许醋，等水滚放入莲藕煮1分30秒，捞起后冲冷水沥干。把腌渍莲藕的配料跟莲藕拌匀，静置20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537936" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537936-P.59-04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537936-P.59-04-450x284.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537936" class="wp-caption-text">4.汤锅中倒入2杯水和少许醋，等水滚放入莲藕煮1分30秒，捞起后冲冷水沥干。把腌渍莲藕的配料跟莲藕拌匀，静置20分钟。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>开始料理</strong></span></h2>
<p>5.糯米放筛网中洗净后，泡水静置30分钟。同时将冷冻虾仁泡冷水解冻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537938" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537938-P.59-05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537938-P.59-05-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537938" class="wp-caption-text">5 糯米放筛网中洗净后，泡水静置30分钟。同时将冷冻虾仁泡冷水解冻。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.在汤锅里放入糯米、水、清酒和海带。大火煮滚后转小火、盖上盖子，再煮10分钟关火。关火后静置焖10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537940" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537940-P.59-06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537940-P.59-06-450x296.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537940" class="wp-caption-text">6 在汤锅里放入糯米、水、清酒和海带。大火煮滚后转小火、盖上盖子，再煮10分钟关火。关火后静置焖10分钟。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.把蛋丝的材料拌匀后过筛，在热锅里均匀倒入食用油再放蛋液，正反两面以中火各煎1～2分钟。起锅后，将蛋皮切丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537941" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537941-P.59-07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537941-P.59-07-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537941" class="wp-caption-text">7 把蛋丝的材料拌匀后过筛，在热锅里均匀倒入食用油再放蛋液，正反两面以中火各煎1～2分钟。起锅后，将蛋皮切丝。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.2杯水煮滚，放入虾仁汆烫，约1～2分钟后过筛沥干，并将每只虾子剖半。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537942" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537942-P.59-08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537942-P.59-08-450x294.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537942" class="wp-caption-text">2杯水煮滚，放入虾仁汆烫，约1～2分钟后过筛沥干，并将每只虾子剖半。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.将步骤②的2朵香菇、4条干瓢切碎。再拿一个碗，放入调味醋材料后拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537943" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537943-P.59-09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537943-P.59-09-450x295.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537943" class="wp-caption-text">将步骤②的2朵香菇、4条干瓢切碎。再拿一个碗，放入调味醋材料后拌匀。★ 多出来的酱烧香菇干瓢，也可以当小菜吃。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>★多出来的酱烧香菇干瓢，也可以当小菜吃。</strong></p>
<p>10.煮好的饭趁热装在碗里，加入步骤⑨的调味醋，用汤匙把饭拌匀、别让饭粒结块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537944" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537944-P.59-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537944-P.59-10-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537944" class="wp-caption-text">10 煮好的饭趁热装在碗里，加入步骤⑨的调味醋，用汤匙把饭拌匀、别让饭粒结块。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11.接着加入步骤⑨切好的酱烧香菇干瓢后充分混合，然后铺入醋腌莲藕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537945-P.59-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537945-P.59-11-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537945" class="wp-caption-text">11 接着加入步骤⑨切好的酱烧香菇干瓢后充分混合，然后铺入醋腌莲藕。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12.最后再撒上蛋丝、虾仁及熟四季豆即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13537986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13537986" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537986-P.59-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13537986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537986-P.59-12-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13537986" class="wp-caption-text">12 最后再撒上蛋丝、虾仁及熟四季豆即完成。★ 也可放上汆烫后的萝卜<br />婴或飞鱼子当装饰。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>★ 也可放上汆烫后的萝卜婴或飞鱼子当装饰。</h3>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;">TIP</span></h2>
<p><strong>延伸作法－<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8%E5%8D%B7.md#1">寿司卷</a></strong></p>
<p>一种含有丰富内馅的日式饭卷。可在寿司海苔上放步骤⑩的饭、汆烫好的菠菜和步骤②的酱烧香菇干瓢，再放入厚厚的蛋卷，卷起来即完成。</p>
<p><strong>延伸作法－球寿司</strong></p>
<p>把散寿司的食材简化后，做成小巧可爱的球寿司来吃也很不错。将步骤⑩的饭捏成一口大小的圆形，在保鲜膜上放入步骤⑧的虾仁和饭球后塑形。也可以用小黄瓜、鲑鱼或章鱼等食材代替虾子。</p>
