<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一锅一餐 美味丰盛的鲑鱼锅饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13558711-p104-1-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鲑鱼锅饭。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<div><strong>编者按：</strong>来吃“锅饭”吧！在被时间追着跑、身心俱疲很想好好吃一顿饭时，只要将蔬菜、海鲜、肉类放入锅中炊煮，倒入快速高汤、加入白米，不需要花费太多心思与时间，一锅集结营养和美味的丰盛料理，轻松上桌。</div>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>鲑鱼锅饭</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>主材料</strong></span></h3>
<p>白米 ·························2杯<br />
鲑鱼排 ·······················200g<br />
青椒 ·························1颗<br />
芝麻 ·························少许<br />
冷泡昆布高汤 ···········300ml</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>调味料</strong></span></h3>
<p><u>鲑鱼腌料</u><br />
冷泡昆布高汤 ·············100ml<br />
浓酱油 ························2大匙<br />
料理酒 ························1大匙</p>
<h3><u>其他</u></h3>
<p>葡萄籽油 ····················少许</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>冷泡昆布高汤做法</strong></span></h2>
<p>需要清淡的高汤时，很适合使用此汤头，风味香醇又清爽。制作酱料时用高汤取代原本的水，味道会更有层次。</p>
<p>水500ml＋昆布1片(5cm×5cm)</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543938" style="width: 433px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543938-P22-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13543938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543938-P22-2.jpg" alt="" width="433" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543938" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13543939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543939" style="width: 434px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543939-P22-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13543939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543939-P22-3.jpg" alt="" width="434" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543939" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将昆布浸泡在饮用水中2个小时以上。建议先放在冰箱冷藏一晚，第二天即可把浸泡好的高汤拿出来使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543937" style="width: 433px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543937-P22-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13543937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543937-P22-1.jpg" alt="" width="433" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543937" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>做法</strong></span></h2>
<p>1 .白米洗净，泡水30分钟后沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543908" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543908-p105-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13543908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543908-p105-1-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543908" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2 .鲑鱼排放入腌料中，置于冰箱冷藏2小时以上腌渍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543909-p105-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13543909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543909-p105-2-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543909" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3 .青椒洗净后剖半、去除中间的膜和籽后，切成丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543910" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543910-p105-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13543910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543910-p105-3-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543910" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4 .平底锅热锅后，淋葡萄籽油，将鲑鱼的正、反两面稍微煎到上色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543911" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543911-p105-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13543911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543911-p105-4-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543911" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 .在饭锅中放入浸泡过的米，淋上2大匙鲑鱼的腌料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543912" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543912-p105-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13543912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543912-p105-5-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543912" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6 .倒入300ml冷泡昆布高汤、放上鲑鱼，用大火煮5分钟至沸腾，再转小火续煮10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543913-p105-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13543913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543913-p105-6-450x322.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543913" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7 .煮好后掀锅，放入青椒丝，再盖上锅盖，关火焖5分钟，最后撒上芝麻即可。⊙</p>
