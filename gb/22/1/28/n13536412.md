<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>明知虎山难，偏向虎山行！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536415-537349-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>水云吊桥。（张永祥提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月28日讯】（大纪元记者杨子樊台湾苗栗报导）准备好体验一趟虎虎生风、卧虎藏龙的惊奇山行了吗？</p>
<p>位于苗栗县泰安乡的虎山，又名虎子山或虎头山。因当地虎山、鸟嘴山（上岛山）、横龙山，形成虎、凤、龙三兽磐据的态势。虎山标高1,492公尺，又被称之为“水云三星”之首。同时，也因为山形壮丽峥嵘，从远处看虎山山顶，就像大霸尖山一样，因而得名“中级山的大霸尖山”。</p>
<p>虎山步道长度约4.5公里，虽名为步道，但从登山口至山顶的高度落差约900公尺，并不是一个轻松的行程。攻顶来回时间约5~6小时左右，前往挑战的民众，必须要有心理准备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13536414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13536414" style="width: 376px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536414-537348.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13536414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536414-537348-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="376" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13536414" class="wp-caption-text">登山口一开始为平坦的产业道路。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>登山口位于泰安温泉停车场处，从这里开始绵延一公里的碎石路，相对平坦好走。抵达“水云吊桥”后，有通往水云瀑布的岔路，也可在此小憩一会儿。</p>
<figure id="13536415" aria-describedby="caption-13536415" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536415-537349-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536415-537349-450x337.jpg" alt="水云吊桥。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536415" class="wp-caption-text">水云吊桥。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>巧遇虎山网红“蓝腹鹇”</h2>
<p>接着第二段路程，由吊桥到溪谷叉路之间，慢慢缓上坡。由于虎山并无里程指示牌，沿途只能沿着编号的救援桩，推算自己的步行进度。</p>
<figure id="13536418" aria-describedby="caption-13536418" style="width: 374px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536418-537352-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536418-537352-450x600.jpg" alt="8号救援桩处往上，一路需攀附绳索。" width="374" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536418" class="wp-caption-text">8号救援桩处往上，一路需攀附绳索。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在此段林荫小径中，看到“山大王”猕猴出没其间，似乎不太稀奇，然而，见到羽色斑斓的大型雉类“蓝腹鹇”（台湾蓝鹇）悠闲的身影，则是令人振奋！</p>
<figure id="13536417" aria-describedby="caption-13536417" style="width: 375px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536417-537351-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536417-537351-450x600.jpg" alt="虎山中的猴大王。" width="375" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536417" class="wp-caption-text">虎山中的猴大王。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536416" aria-describedby="caption-13536416" style="width: 375px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536416-537350-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536416-537350-450x600.jpg" alt="虎山的网红“蓝腹鹇”。" width="375" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536416" class="wp-caption-text">虎山的网红“蓝腹鹇”。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536419" aria-describedby="caption-13536419" style="width: 374px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536419-537353-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536419-537353-450x600.jpg" alt="沿途陡峭的碎石坡。" width="374" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536419" class="wp-caption-text">沿途陡峭的碎石坡。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>带着愉悦的心情，走过4~6号救援桩后，从7号、8号桩开始就进入了挑战体力的一公里连续岩沟碎石陡坡。上了棱肩，能看到绝美壮阔的虎山绝壁和群山远景。然而，最后一段路上，却得手脚并用、攀岩走壁，爬升400公尺后，才终于登上虎山。山上有一个三等三角点。</p>
<figure id="13536420" aria-describedby="caption-13536420" style="width: 375px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536420-537354-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536420-537354-450x600.jpg" alt="虎山攻顶三角点。" width="375" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536420" class="wp-caption-text">虎山攻顶三角点。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536421" aria-describedby="caption-13536421" style="width: 375px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536421-537355-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536421-537355-450x600.jpg" alt="身后方尖峰处即虎山。" width="375" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536421" class="wp-caption-text">身后方尖峰处即虎山。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下山后，可安排到就近的泰安温泉泡泡汤，或是前往清安豆腐街，大啖美食，补充体力，都是很好的选择。◇</p>
<figure id="13536422" aria-describedby="caption-13536422" style="width: 356px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536422-537356-450x974.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536422-537356-450x974.jpg" alt="尖峰处即虎山。" width="356" b="770" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536422" class="wp-caption-text">尖峰处即虎山。（张永祥提供）</figcaption></figure>
