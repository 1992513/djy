<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃环局与三三化兵群联手忠贞市场扩大消毒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480670-535417-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>桃环局与三三化兵群联手忠贞市场扩大消毒。（桃园环保局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月04日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%AE%E8%AF%8A%E8%80%85.md#1">确诊者</a>于日前前往<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BF%A0%E8%B4%9E%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">忠贞市场</a>摆摊，桃园市防疫<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E6%AF%92.md#1">消毒</a>大队与三三<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%96%E5%85%B5%E7%BE%A4.md#1">化兵群</a>也于4日下午出动54人及7部消毒车前往市场进行大规模消毒，针对市场周边10个里进行扩大消毒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13480671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13480671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480671-535418.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13480671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480671-535418-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13480671" class="wp-caption-text">桃环局与三三<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%96%E5%85%B5%E7%BE%A4.md#1">化兵群</a>联手<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BF%A0%E8%B4%9E%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">忠贞市场</a>扩大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E6%AF%92.md#1">消毒</a>。（桃园环保局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园环保局表示，一接获卫生单位通报后，随即与三三化兵群联系并拟定消毒计划，并于4日下午2点，由防疫消毒大队7部消毒车及30人，结合国军三三化兵群24人及8具背负式消毒机，针对市场内部店家、外部摊商区进行清消。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13480669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13480669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480669-535416.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13480669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480669-535416-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13480669" class="wp-caption-text">桃环局与三三化兵群联手忠贞市场扩大消毒。（桃园环保局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13480668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13480668" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480668-535415.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13480668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480668-535415-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13480668" class="wp-caption-text">桃环局与三三化兵群联手忠贞市场扩大消毒。（桃园环保局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也针对市场周边10个里进行扩大消毒，包含中坜区龙平里、龙冈里、龙东里、龙安里等4里，平镇区龙兴里、忠贞里、贸易里、中正里等4里，及八德区霄里里与龙友里等2里。环保局也说，明后二天也会持续针对该市场与周边邻里进行消毒，请市民安心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13480667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13480667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480667-535414.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13480667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13480667-535414-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13480667" class="wp-caption-text">桃环局与三三化兵群联手忠贞市场扩大消毒。（桃园环保局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有关<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%AE%E8%AF%8A%E8%80%85.md#1">确诊者</a>亲人就读的二间学校，防疫消毒大队也于一早完成校内教室、走廊及公共空间的消毒，并以水雾消毒车针对学校外围进行大规模清消。◇</p>
