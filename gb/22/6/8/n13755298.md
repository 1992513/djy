<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第二届“恒丰银行杯”摄影大赛 圆满结束</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755302-ede0fdf7beee933add198666829546a0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>网络人气奖—平步青云-林志远
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年06月09日讯】（休斯顿讯）自3月7日起开始征稿的2022年“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%81%92%E4%B8%B0%E9%93%B6%E8%A1%8C.md#1">恒丰银行</a>杯”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%9B.md#1">摄影大赛</a>，经过三个月的收件，甄选，评判，现在圆满结束。6月4日在恒丰银行12楼会议室举行了获奖作品的颁奖仪式。</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%81%92%E4%B8%B0%E9%93%B6%E8%A1%8C.md#1">恒丰银行</a>杯”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%9B.md#1">摄影大赛</a>，主旨在于征集优秀的作品收录于恒丰银行的月历的同时，加强企业同社区的紧密联系。去年的第一届恒丰银行杯摄影大赛的成功举行，受到广大民众的喜爱与好评，所有得奖作品都登录使用在了今年的台历上，让所有获奖作品的拍摄者兴奋不已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13755305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13755305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755305-23917a1d0ce2076a18652ae3d0404086-e1654731438100.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13755305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755305-23917a1d0ce2076a18652ae3d0404086-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13755305" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%98%E7%A7%80%E4%BD%9C%E5%93%81.md#1">优秀作品</a>—晨之光-Danny Li</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13755306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13755306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755306-fd198fe4ccc4d4580470d1bb1e0998f1-e1654731475467.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13755306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755306-fd198fe4ccc4d4580470d1bb1e0998f1-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13755306" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%98%E7%A7%80%E4%BD%9C%E5%93%81.md#1">优秀作品</a>—霞光普照-Danny Li</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13755307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13755307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755307-51276fcdce5d04b6c16284ba61b4f9ce-e1654731515611.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13755307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755307-51276fcdce5d04b6c16284ba61b4f9ce-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13755307" class="wp-caption-text">优秀作品—中国城地标 &#8211; Peter Yang</figcaption></figure>
<p>2023年为恒丰银行建行25周年，为配合即将到来的25周年庆，此次大赛主题为恒丰银行大楼外景彩色摄影作品。对于建筑物的拍摄实际上是有难度的，如何能明确的表现出主题，并且能在作品中体现出企业精神，是主办方以及评委，对于所有参赛者的期望。出人意料的是，今年收到了很多优秀的作品，令主办方恒丰银行以及专业评委都欣喜不已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13755308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13755308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755308-7201a808fd85c981256fea62443b4c73-e1654731553793.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13755308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755308-7201a808fd85c981256fea62443b4c73-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13755308" class="wp-caption-text">优秀作品—夜色阑珊-Danny Li</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13755309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13755309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755309-41d44feac3075e28d3565f3c4ebe5d66-e1654731581587.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13755309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755309-41d44feac3075e28d3565f3c4ebe5d66-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13755309" class="wp-caption-text">优秀作品—恒丰之魅 &#8211; Peter Yang</figcaption></figure>

<p>今年的摄影大赛继续延用了继续开设网路投票和现场评选两种方式。现场评选恒丰银行特别请旅居休斯顿华裔风光及民俗的著名摄影师贾老师，协同中国摄影家协会会员，广东省摄影家协会会员著名摄影师王桂雄老师，美国摄影学会MPSA（卓越会员），国际影艺梁梦EFIAP（影艺卓越者）会员，美国休斯顿华人摄影学会董事Tract Feng组成专业评委，专业评委对于入选作品无论的主题表现，还是技术层面都给予了高度评价。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13755311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13755311" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755311-f403e6e062a2e86e505f992878eb8f9d-e1654731644100.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13755311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755311-f403e6e062a2e86e505f992878eb8f9d-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13755311" class="wp-caption-text">优秀作品—蒸蒸日上 &#8211; 林志远</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13755312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13755312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755312-201186bc640267005d952238e94599d8-e1654731674517.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13755312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755312-201186bc640267005d952238e94599d8-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13755312" class="wp-caption-text">优秀作品—诚信首选 &#8211; 林志远</figcaption></figure>

<p>由华夏时报市场部主任林远志拍摄的“平步青云” 获得本次大赛网路人气奖。 现场评选一共评出9幅优秀作品，分别由林远志、Danny li、Peter yang、谢志慧拍摄，所有优秀作品都获得了奖状和奖金，同时，部分作品将收录于恒丰银行2023年阅历使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13755304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13755304" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755304-d6243a1e68456bd889c900d42cbe615d-e1654731736565.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13755304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13755304-d6243a1e68456bd889c900d42cbe615d-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13755304" class="wp-caption-text">谢志慧—放眼世界</figcaption></figure>
<p>在颁奖仪式结束时，恒丰银行Jennifer Zhou代表银行感谢所有的参赛选手，感谢所有的评委，正是因为广大摄影爱好者的踊跃参与，评委的认真负责，才能让本次大赛得以圆满，期望在未来由更多的人参与到摄影大赛中，使大赛越办越好。</p>
