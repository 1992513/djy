<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义市111年度社会创新及社会企业课程</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649428-545252-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>嘉有社企女力崛起。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年03月16日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市政府特别照顾辖内女性朋友，打造“嘉有社企?城市新活力”的女力计划，推动女力政策已迈向第三年度，累积经验值，今年为协助经济弱势或中高龄妇女创业，打造一系列<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A%E5%88%9B%E6%96%B0.md#1">社会创新</a>及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A%E4%BC%81%E4%B8%9A.md#1">社会企业</a>课程，拼经济最佳入门，别错过。</p>
<figure id="13649430" aria-describedby="caption-13649430" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649430-545254-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649430-545254-450x300.jpeg" alt="嘉有社企女力崛起。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13649430" class="wp-caption-text">嘉有社企女力崛起。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>课程中隆重邀请业界知名讲师：嘉义市政府社会处处长-林家纬、四季春红有限公司创办人-黄采燕、众<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A%E4%BC%81%E4%B8%9A.md#1">社会企业</a>创办人-林崇伟、世界宗教博物馆馆长-马幼娟、南仁湖协理-黄秋玉、源天然创办人-范逸娴、印好INHEART创办人-高慧桦、旅游部落客-郑兆翔（肉鲁）等等，传授全方位创业技巧与市场销售策略。</p>
<figure id="13649429" aria-describedby="caption-13649429" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649429-545253-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649429-545253-450x300.jpeg" alt="嘉有社企女力崛起。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13649429" class="wp-caption-text">嘉有社企女力崛起。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>课程设计采主题式授课，由浅入深介绍社会企业基础概念、社会影响力评估、商业育成（如财务规划、行销培训、经营管理）等内容，协助学员经济自足，建构<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%86%E4%B8%9A%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E.md#1">商业经济</a>模式。</p>
<figure id="13649433" aria-describedby="caption-13649433" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649433-545262-450x638.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649433-545262-450x638.jpeg" alt="社会创新及社会企业课程。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13649433" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A%E5%88%9B%E6%96%B0.md#1">社会创新</a>及社会企业课程。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13649432" aria-describedby="caption-13649432" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649432-545256-450x636.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649432-545256-450x636.jpeg" alt="社会创新及社会企业课程。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13649432" class="wp-caption-text">社会创新及社会企业课程。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13649431" aria-describedby="caption-13649431" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649431-545255-450x360.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13649431-545255-450x360.jpeg" alt="嘉有社企女力崛起。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13649431" class="wp-caption-text">嘉有社企女力崛起。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主要训练对象为设籍、居住或工作地在嘉义市的女性及弱势族群，搭配实作课程印证课程理论与实务运用，规划适性训练课程，让参训人员达实务需求。即日起开始网路报名<ahref="https://pse.is/3vuekd" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://pse.is/3vuekd</a>）至3月底截止，欢迎心动的你赶快来报名唷！◇</p>
