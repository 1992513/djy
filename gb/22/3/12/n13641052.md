<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：西伯利亚原住民涅涅茨人的游牧之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641105-2-1200x800-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>出生于俄罗斯的一位摄影师近距离见证了以驯养驯鹿为生的涅涅茨人的日常生活。（Daniel Kordan提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年03月12日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（英文大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／张雨霏编译</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">出生于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF.md#1">俄罗斯</a>的一位摄影师在他的冒险之旅中捕捉到了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%85%E6%B6%85%E8%8C%A8%E4%BA%BA.md#1">涅涅茨人</a>（俄罗斯北方极地区域的原住民）惊人的淳朴和活力，在此期间，他有机会近距离见证了他们的日常生活。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">现居住在意大利托斯卡纳的丹尼尔</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">科尔丹（Daniel Kordan）于2021年4月开始拜访这些以驯养<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AF%E9%B9%BF.md#1">驯鹿</a>为生的传统牧民。他最初的梦想是看到并拍摄他们向北迁徙的过程，也被称为“Argish”，但他有幸经历了更多。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">他受邀在涅涅茨一户家庭的帐篷里待了一个星期，这里是偏远的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E9%A9%AC%E5%B0%94.md#1">亚马尔</a>（Yamal）地区，是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF.md#1">俄罗斯</a>北部一个非常传统的土着区域。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13641106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641106-1-1200x800.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13641106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641106-1-1200x800.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641106" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13641107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641107" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641107-Yamal-people14-1200x800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13641107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641107-Yamal-people14-1200x800.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641107" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E9%A9%AC%E5%B0%94.md#1">亚马尔</a>让我感到非常惊讶”，科尔丹在<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/dk.scape/posts/461890031972143">脸书</a>帖子中写道，“这次旅行就像穿越到几个世纪以前，就好像你发现自己突然不合时宜地出现在苔原地带，周围是群山、雪地、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%85%E6%B6%85%E8%8C%A8%E4%BA%BA.md#1">涅涅茨人</a>和他们的鹿群。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在土着涅涅茨人的语言中，亚马尔的意思是“世界的尽头”（end of the world）。这里地处<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E4%BC%AF%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A.md#1">西伯利亚</a>永久冻土带，到处是蜿蜒的河流和矮灌木丛，地处偏远，寒风凛冽，一千多年来一直是他们的家园。对于仍然游牧的涅涅茨人来说，他们非常依赖于这片土地和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AF%E9%B9%BF.md#1">驯鹿</a>群。驯鹿几乎给他们提供了一切文化的基础，包括住房、服装、食物和交通工具等，科尔丹在2021年11月发布的系列照片中解释说。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13641109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641109" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641109-Yamal-people14-1200x800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13641109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641109-Yamal-people14-1200x800.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641109" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13641110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641110" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641110-Yamal-people7-1200x800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13641110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641110-Yamal-people7-1200x800.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641110" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">通过在帐篷里与当地人近距离接触，科尔丹有机会见证了涅涅茨人的日常生活。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“彼得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">伊万诺维奇（Pyotr Ivanovich）的妻子莉娜（Lena）担任女招待”，科尔丹说，“莉娜从早到晚都在不停地忙碌：把雪拿进来融化，稳固帐篷免受强风的袭击，或者给我们做饭。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">他说，食物很简单，但都是自制的，而且都很美味。他们通常会吃鱼或鹿肉配意大利面。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13641111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641111" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641111-3-1200x800.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13641111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641111-3-1200x800.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641111" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13641112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641112" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641112-Yamal-people12-1200x800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13641112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641112-Yamal-people12-1200x800.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641112" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在此期间，科尔丹不得不面对天气的挑战。“天气开始变冷了。第一天只有-5摄氏度左右，到处都是湿漉漉的雪。然后突然降至-25度”，他写道，“涅涅茨人晚上会将炉子加热，然后上床睡觉。到了早上，帐篷里的温度几乎和外面一样低。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">通过短暂逗留，科尔丹很幸运地捕捉到涅涅茨人日常生活的精彩照片，并更多地了解他们的传统和多样化的生活方式。他令人难以置信的系列照片以穿着民族服饰的涅涅茨人、萨摩耶犬和可爱的小孩为特色。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“涅涅茨人的孩子非常恬静”，科尔丹说，“我一个星期几乎都听不到他们的哭声或尖叫声。”</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13641113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641113" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641113-4-1200x799.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13641113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641113-4-1200x799.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641113" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13641114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641114" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641114-Yamal-people2-1200x797.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13641114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641114-Yamal-people2-1200x797.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641114" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">终于，在科尔丹准备离开的前一天，他的梦想成真，他有幸乘坐雪橇，和涅涅茨人一起北迁。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“这次事件规模宏大，也非常漂亮，十多辆由雪橇搭成的‘火车’沿着丘陵和山脉穿过山谷”，科尔丹谈及“Argish”时说，从黎明到黄昏，这段旅程共历时12个小时。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">此外，在亚马尔的最后一晚，科尔丹在新营地上方还拍到了美丽的北极光。◇</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13641115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13641115" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641115-Yamal-people10-1200x800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13641115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13641115-Yamal-people10-1200x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13641115" class="wp-caption-text">（Daniel Kordan提供）</figcaption></figure>
