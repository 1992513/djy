<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>当季水果鲜甜营养 吃对了就不容易生病</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625490-3ec1531b4cd75633a97bc9da58130460-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蜜枣是春季盛产的水果。（曾晏均／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年03月06日讯】（大纪元记者曾晏均台湾报导）台湾向来有“水果王国”的美誉，水果产量及种类丰富，一年四季皆有鲜甜多汁的水果可以品尝。由于台湾水果具有新鲜、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A5%E5%85%BB.md#1">营养</a>、低碳、安全等特色，“吃在地，食当季”，大家可以趁着盛产的季节多多食用。</p>
<p>如今时序进入了春天，惊蛰时节，台湾春季的水果有哪些呢？以农业大县的屏东产地而言，芭乐、蜜枣、莲雾、嘉宝果、木瓜、枇杷等，都是当令盛产的水果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13625491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13625491" style="width: 920px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625491-b1b8a7856df1dd003c910da4b2d4c36f-e1646553957343.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13625491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625491-b1b8a7856df1dd003c910da4b2d4c36f-e1646553957343.jpg" alt="" width="920" b="518" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13625491" class="wp-caption-text">芭乐几乎一年四季皆产。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13625495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13625495" style="width: 920px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625495-d834f702dae050158ab7df120d9ae579-e1646554166528.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13625495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625495-d834f702dae050158ab7df120d9ae579-e1646554166528.jpg" alt="" width="920" b="690" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13625495" class="wp-caption-text">枇杷是春季盛产的水果。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13625496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13625496" style="width: 980px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625496-8a24598229e36ce7a14a652668e61bc5-e1646555302143.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13625496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625496-8a24598229e36ce7a14a652668e61bc5-e1646555302143.jpg" alt="" width="980" b="735" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13625496" class="wp-caption-text">香蕉几乎一年四季皆产。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>吃对了就不容易生病，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%87%AA%E7%84%B6%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">大自然食物</a>是良药</strong></h3>
<p>依中医理论而言，大地孕育万物，每个季节所生产的食物都是神赐给人们的礼物，对应着各个节气人体<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A5%E5%85%BB.md#1">营养</a>所需，供应身体的每一个细胞、五脏六腑运转的养分，提供免疫系统的自我疗愈能力。</p>
<p>神赐的食物就是良药，能裹腹也能治病防病。中医认为，节气会影响人体脉络运行，春天情绪容易抑郁，故应多吃绿色和黄色食物补肝顾脾，像是现在当季盛产的芭乐、枣子、枇杷、木瓜，就是最适宜的水果。</p>
<p>夏天炙热季节，身心备感焦躁，应摄取黄色和红色食物护心，如红龙果、西瓜、李子、百香果，都是夏天当令的水果。</p>
<p>秋天燥邪季，多吃白色食物润肺，像荔枝、水梨、酪梨、龙眼都适合秋季食用。</p>
<p>冬天寒冷气血循环迟滞，需要黑色食物顾肾护本，紫黑色的葡萄、嘉宝果、柑橘、柿子、莲雾，都是当季的农产。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13625493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13625493" style="width: 980px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625493-ee16e2a0850858b5d919007c14456a90-e1646554404405.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13625493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625493-ee16e2a0850858b5d919007c14456a90-e1646554404405.jpg" alt="" width="980" b="735" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13625493" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宝果（亦称树葡萄）是春、冬两季盛产的水果。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13625497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13625497" style="width: 980px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625497-160105603471eee59d81606aed12bca0-e1646554284731.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13625497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625497-160105603471eee59d81606aed12bca0-e1646554284731.jpg" alt="" width="980" b="735" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13625497" class="wp-caption-text">经品种改良后，木瓜几乎一年四季皆产。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13625516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13625516" style="width: 1159px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625516-d1bfe3a4f431495d55e3eeb361ffda21.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13625516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13625516-d1bfe3a4f431495d55e3eeb361ffda21.jpg" alt="" width="1159" b="778" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13625516" class="wp-caption-text">莲雾经品种改良后，几乎一年四季皆产。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>选用<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%93%E5%AD%A3%E8%94%AC%E6%9E%9C.md#1">当季蔬果</a> 节能减碳爱地球</strong></h3>
<p>顺应季节所种植、生产的蔬果，因配合节气与土壤环境，不容易有过于严重的病虫害，大幅降低农药及肥料的使用量。加上减少冷藏、加工保存的机会，也能降低废弃物的产生。</p>
<p>“在地蔬果当季吃”，根据社团法人中华民国低碳环境学会公布的低碳饮食建议，选用在地且<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%93%E5%AD%A3%E8%94%AC%E6%9E%9C.md#1">当季蔬果</a>，可以减少食材在“生产”、“运送”、“烹调”过程中所产生的二氧化<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A2%B3%E6%8E%92%E6%94%BE.md#1">碳排放</a>。因距离愈远碳排放愈高，况且长途运送过程中为了保鲜，食材有可能会被浸泡防腐剂，间接影响身体健康。也就是说，吃在地食物，能缩短食物从产地运送到餐桌的过程中旅行的距离，节能<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E7%A2%B3%E6%95%91%E5%9C%B0%E7%90%83.md#1">减碳救地球</a>。<strong>@*</strong></p>
