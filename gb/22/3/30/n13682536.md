<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园新竹地区1期稻作顺利完成插秧</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13682538-547513-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园新竹地区1期稻作顺利完成插秧。（北水局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年03月30日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）2022年春雨丰沛，北部地区各<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">水库</a>均接近满库，1期稻作也顺利完成整田<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8F%92%E7%A7%A7.md#1">插秧</a>，北水局评估水情良好，至6月底各类用水可稳定供应。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13682541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13682541" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13682541-547515.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13682541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13682541-547515-600x1200.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="898" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13682541" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%AB%B9.md#1">新竹</a>地区1期稻作顺利完成<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8F%92%E7%A7%A7.md#1">插秧</a>。（北水局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>北水局统计30日北部地区各<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">水库</a>蓄水率均达九成五以上，甚至日前石门及翡翠水库还进行调节性放水，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%AB%B9.md#1">新竹</a>宝山及宝二水库更是自2月19日起蓄水率维持100%，区域整体水情审愼乐观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13682539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13682539" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13682539-547514.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13682539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13682539-547514-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13682539" class="wp-caption-text">桃园新竹地区1期稻作顺利完成插秧。（北水局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本年度春耕自2月开始供灌，统计桃园及新竹地区总供灌面积分别约34,762公顷及6,151公顷，已于3月中旬陆续完成整田插秧，预期6月底可顺利抽穗收割。◇</p>
