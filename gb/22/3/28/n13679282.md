<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：是要走下去</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1605091634212483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>乡村小路（fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>岁月匆匆，仿佛才一瞬间，已八十三岁了，我仍爱诗，写诗，出诗集。</p>
<p>这本诗集是我的第27本书。循着以往的路，还是多写乡土田园，大多是我早年在乡间田园的生活。人的一生，很难忘却他早年的日子，我正是如此。由于出生乡间田园，我早年在那边生活，也就爱上了。尽管台湾随时间而递变，型态已改变很多，那些乡间田园的影像，仍深植我心中。乡间田园的日子我永远紧紧抱住，即使在梦中，他们也常来找我，引我同欢。那是一段多么美好的日子呀！在那一片广袤的田野里，呼吸着清新的空气，或跑或跳，自由自在，无拘无束啊……人间天堂！</p>
<p>现在我老了，我还是要写他们，永远不弃。这条路，我走定了，要永远地走下去。他们还会引领我回到年轻呢。那是朝气蓬勃的日子呀！</p>
<p>一定！<br />
一定！<br />
即使黄昏当前<br />
晚霞一样<br />
可以多彩灿烂</p>
<p>I will walk still Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>In a hurry, the year seems a moment, I&#8217;m 83 years old, I still love poem, write poem , and publish poetry collection.</p>
<p>This poetry collection is my 27th book.Fellow the road before, I almost write native land and countryside, most of them are the life of native land and countryside of early year. It&#8217;s very difficulty to forget the early year of a person, I&#8217;m just so. Due to I was born in the native land and countryside, and I life there the early years, I love it also.Though the stye of Taiwan is change fellows the change of time, the stye is quite change, the image is deep planted in my mind still. I enbrace them closedly,even in the dream, they often call on me, made me happy all together with them. How fine are the days!I breathe the fresh air in the wide field, may run, may leap,freely, unrestained,&#8230;oh, it&#8217;s the paradise!</p>
<p>And now, I&#8217;m in old age, but I want to write them still,don&#8217;t discard them forever, I walk surely, will walk forever. May they lead me to be young.It&#8217;s the day of vitality!</p>
<p>Be sure!<br />
Be sure!<br />
Even if it&#8217;s the dusk<br />
Evening glow<br />
Can be colorful and brilliant</p>
