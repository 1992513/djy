<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新泽西法轮功参加圣派翠克节游行 获市长盛赞</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13677161-P1.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>

这是（东西方）传统的融合，文化的包容，”霍夫市长继续说到。他还邀请法轮功队伍明年再来参加游行。</p><div><figure style="max-width:640px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13677508-P-3-e1648438315758.png"
