<h2><strong>大凤蝶Papilio memnon凤蝶科</strong></h2>
<p>展翅100~120mm，雌蝶有3型，有尾型翅膀表面具白、橙、黑色斑纹，有长斑和短斑2种，无尾型下翅表面有黑、白2色的条状斑纹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13665489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665489-425669_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13665489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665489-425669_medium-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665489" class="wp-caption-text">凤蝶慢飞舞翩翩，无尾雌蝶黑白线。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13665488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665488-425668_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13665488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665488-425668_medium-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665488" class="wp-caption-text">普遍分布于平地至低海拔山区，幼虫以芸香科植物寄主。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大凤蝶普遍分布于平地至低海拔山区，幼虫以芸香科植物寄主，如食茱萸、双面刺、贼仔树及柑橘、柚、柠檬等，成虫飞行缓慢，喜爱阳光，常见于公园、学校、人工生态园及路边的野花上空飞行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13665492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665492" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665492-425666_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13665492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665492-425666_medium-600x421.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665492" class="wp-caption-text">如食茱萸、双面刺、贼仔树及柑橘、柚、柠檬等。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13665491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665491" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665491-425671_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13665491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665491-425671_medium-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665491" class="wp-caption-text">艳红肩头长披风，掬尾下蛋叶背间。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
