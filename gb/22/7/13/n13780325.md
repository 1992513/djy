<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>牛仔节手记：巧遇特鲁多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780343-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>特鲁多总理（Justin Trudeau）现身卡城牛仔节。（林采枫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月14日讯】（记者林采枫报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%8A%82.md#1">牛仔节</a>，是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94.md#1">牛仔</a>的盛会，是美食的盛会，其实，也是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BF%E5%AE%A2.md#1">政客</a>的盛会。</p>
<p>尽管知道在这10天里，平民百姓街头偶遇封疆大吏的可能性无限增大，但我未查黄历出行一次，就碰到了3千万人的总理，还真是超乎意料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13780342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780342" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780342-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13780342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780342-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-02-600x395.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="600" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780342" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E9%B2%81%E5%A4%9A.md#1">特鲁多</a>总理（Justin Trudeau）现身卡城<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%8A%82.md#1">牛仔节</a>现场。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94.md#1">牛仔</a>公园的马鞍体育馆前面，数千人或坐或站，围观越野摩托车特技表演，不时发出轰然喝采。而在这大批人群的旁边，另有几百人挤挤擦擦，里三层外三层，明显拥着一个中心，手机相机闪个不停，媒体的长枪大炮喀嚓不断，戴着酷帅墨镜嵌着蓝牙耳机的保镖左瞧右看，而身套黑色制服腰佩手枪电棍的警察则站在外围观望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13780344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780344" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780344-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-guards-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13780344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780344-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-guards-04-600x422.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780344" class="wp-caption-text">戴着酷帅墨镜嵌着蓝牙耳机的保镖。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13780346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780346" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780346-20220709-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-police-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13780346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780346-20220709-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-police-05-600x450.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780346" class="wp-caption-text">手枪电棍佩戴齐全的警察。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>见状，我立刻询问路人，“发生什么事了？”回答是：“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E9%B2%81%E5%A4%9A.md#1">特鲁多</a>来了。”我一听，当即不能免俗地挤上前去，果然看到了头戴白色牛仔帽身穿红黑牛仔衫的特鲁多。他时刻维持着笑容，握手、留影、闲话，再握手、再留影、再闲话……</p>
<figure id="attachment_13780347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13780347" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780347-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13780347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780347-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Trudeau-03-600x427.jpg" alt="卡尔加里牛仔节" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13780347" class="wp-caption-text">特鲁多应邀与游人留影。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>人群如同冬天的雪球，缓缓移动，越滚越大。短短十几米的路程，足足用了一个多小时。</p>
<p>有人激动地表达自己的崇敬，也有人呼喊着负面的评论。无论如何，比起终其一生也无法得见天颜的14亿人，这里的民众幸福多了，嬉笑怒骂皆可随意。</p>
<p>其实，何止是总理，今年的牛仔节，从市议员、市长，到省议员、省督、省长、联合保守党党魁竞争者，再到国会议员、联邦保守党热门候选人……各路人马纷纷出山，飞入寻常百姓间，热气腾腾地烤煎饼，与民同乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13785822" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13785822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785822-20110709-Anni-Hallan-pancake-breakfast-06a.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13785822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13785822-20110709-Anni-Hallan-pancake-breakfast-06a-600x333.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13785822" class="wp-caption-text">国会议员Hallan煎饼早餐。（安霓／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
