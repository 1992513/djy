<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国中小卸新任校长交接  嘉市教育薪火相传</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791758-567545-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月29日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）7月29日在文雅国小举办嘉义市111学年度国民中小学卸、新任校长交接典礼，今年新、卸任校长以“捍卫战士”飞行员穿着进场，象征着校长作为嘉义市教育领航员，用无限的创意、认真的态度，不畏困难，带领学校开创教育新局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791759" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791759-567546.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791759-567546-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791759" class="wp-caption-text">新任校长宣誓就职。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼由副市长陈淑慧代表市长黄敏惠主持并颁发聘书，并完成宣誓与印信交接仪式。本学年度计有文雅国小、志航国小、宣信国小、嘉北国小等4所学校办理新任校长交接，港坪国小郭明聪校长则争取连任成功。陈淑慧副市长也代表黄敏惠市长致赠烙烧桧木纪念品给志航国小退休校长吴秋锋、文雅国小退休校长沈焕东及嘉北国小退休代理校长蔡英仁，感谢退休校长的无私奉献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791760" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791760-567547.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791760-567547-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791760" class="wp-caption-text">新任校长与副市长陈淑慧合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈淑慧副市长表示，感谢志航国小退休校长吴秋锋、文雅国小退休校长沈焕东及嘉北国小退休代理校长蔡英仁为这3所学校奠定良好的基础，也恭喜港坪国小郭明聪校长争取连任成功，以及文雅国小黄金木校长、宣信国小谢圣雅校长、嘉北国小侯怡如校长及志航国小郑宛钰校长荣膺新职。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791761" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791761-567548.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791761-567548-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791761" class="wp-caption-text">林立生处长谈话。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>校长是学校的领航者，也是孩子们的梦想推手，期许各位校长勿忘初衷，在历任校长建立的基础下培育兼具人文与科技以及国际视野的下一代，让嘉义市的教育继续茁壮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791762-567549.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791762-567549-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791762" class="wp-caption-text">宣信校长谢圣雅意气风发。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市模拟联合国发展协会理事长李锡津表示，各位校长都是教育界的泰山，赢得教育就等于赢得国家的未来，期望各位校长在带领学校起飞后，用深度思考和半导思维来经营学校，为老师和学生植入一生受用的晶片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791763" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791763-567550.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791763-567550-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791763" class="wp-caption-text">志航国小校长郑宛钰即将大展身手。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本学年度新任校长分别由原宣信国小校长黄金木出任文雅国小校长，候用校长谢圣雅出任宣信国小校长，候用校长侯怡如出任嘉北国小校长，以及候用校长郑宛钰出任志航国小校长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791764-567551.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791764-567551-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791764" class="wp-caption-text">文雅国小校长黄金木勇往直前的精神溢于言表。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>志航国小退休校长吴秋锋代表致词时表示，感谢黄敏惠市长对教育的重视和支持，让自己可以在这座幸福城市实现教育理想和蓝图，也感谢一路走来共同打拼的教育伙伴，自己能在嘉义市担任校长是一件非常光荣的事，相信在各位新任、连任校长的带领之下，一定能为嘉义市培育出更多优秀的人才。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791765-567552.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791765-567552-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791765" class="wp-caption-text">副市长陈淑慧颁发聘书给港坪国小校长郭明聪。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>获遴选为嘉北国小新任校长侯怡如代表致词时表示，在嘉义市担任校长是一件不容易的事，但也是一件幸福的事，因为我们有非常重视教育的勇妈市长黄敏惠，让嘉义市在全台湾教育面的各项评比上都列在前段班。身为校长我们将会义无反顾地带领学校前进，打造出自己的特色，让孩子们能多元发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791766-567553.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791766-567553-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791766" class="wp-caption-text">退休代理校长蔡英仁（左1）将印信移交给嘉北国小新任校长侯怡如。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年典礼市府考量疫情尚未完全解封，仍依防疫指引规划典礼内容，与会的人员都全程戴口罩，各校老师与家长也送上祝福，来迎接新任校长及欢送卸任校长，再加上别开生面的捍卫战士概念，让会场充满创意与活力。典礼最后在来宾及与会人员的同声祝福与见证下圆满落幕，为嘉义市的教育揭开新的序幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13791767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791767" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791767-567554.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791767-567554-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791767" class="wp-caption-text">嘉北国小校长侯怡如代表新任校长致词。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13791768" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13791768" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791768-567555.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13791768" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13791768-567555-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13791768" class="wp-caption-text">志航国小退休校长吴秋锋代表退休校长致词。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
