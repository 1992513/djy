<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加拿大国庆日 班芙镇盛景再现</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772651-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-09-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2022年班芙国庆日。（林采枫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月03日讯】被誉为“落基山脉的灵魂”，著名旅游景点班芙镇于7月1日迎来了不同的国庆日。因病毒大流行门庭冷落两年后，在加拿大155周年生日之际，班芙镇恢复了节庆活动，行人车辆川流不息，再现疫情前盛况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13772658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772658-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772658-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-03-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772658" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>班芙镇坐落于亚省落基山脉，每年有超过400万来自世界各地的游客。今年是疫情后首个国庆日活动，在镇中心最繁华的班芙大道上，不同国家、不同族裔、不同年龄的游客络绎不绝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13772650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772650-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-01.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772650-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-01-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772650" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不少人或穿着象征枫叶旗的红色服装，或身披加拿大国旗，或在面部头顶装饰枫叶标志，加拿大小国旗更是举目皆见。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13772659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772659-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772659-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-02-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772659" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13772660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772660" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772660-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772660-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-04-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772660" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13772661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772661-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772661-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-05-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772661" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13772663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772663-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772663-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-06-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772663" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在镇中心的不同地段都有娱乐表演，如苏格兰军乐队、印第安舞蹈、魔术等，吸引大批游客驻足。</p>
<p>卡尔加里法轮大法腰鼓队受主办方邀请，定点表演法轮功五套功法和打腰鼓。</p>
<p>在腰鼓队入场时，主持人向西方观众解说，“腰鼓在中国已经有几千年的历史，在远古时期，鼓常用于战争，用来传递信号、鼓舞士气、指挥进攻、庆祝胜利等。后来，它逐渐演变成在丰收时节和节庆宴会上表达欢欣喜悦的乐器。法轮大法腰鼓队成员全部是法轮大法修炼者。鼓手身着金黄色丝绸服装，风格取自唐朝──中国历史上最辉煌的王朝。鼓声雄浑磅礴，表演美丽精彩。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13772666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772666-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-07.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772666-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-07-600x392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772666" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日，法轮功功法演示。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13772669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772669-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772669-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-10-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772669" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日，腰鼓表演。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13772670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772670" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772670-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772670-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-11-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772670" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日，腰鼓表演。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在班芙大道上，二十余人的腰鼓队整齐排列，金黄服装，嫩粉绸带，映衬着背后苍山负雪，青天浮云。在《法轮大法好》《喜庆》《打鼓歌》的歌声中，前进后退，左穿右插，不断变换队形，赢得观众掌声雷动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13772671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13772671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772671-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13772671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13772671-20220702-LINCAIFENG-Banff-Canada-Day-12-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13772671" class="wp-caption-text">2022年班芙国庆日，腰鼓表演。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><center><a loading="lazy" data2-src="https://vod.brightchat.com/embed/90950525-bf16-4e1d-a019-96e80c6e7b6c?r=16x9&amp;d=54" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center>腰鼓队成员张萍表示，今天是加拿大国庆节，非常高兴。“在加拿大这快自由的土地上，我可以打鼓，赞颂法轮大法给人带来的祥和幸福与给这个国家带来的多元文化。国庆日，庆祝的是自由，我在这里找到了自由。”</p>
<p>她说，7月1日，在中国是所谓的“建党日”，而在这里是加拿大民众庆祝他们幸福生活的一天，“日子相同，但内涵、意义是完完全全不同的。我非常喜欢加拿大，祝加拿大生日快乐！”</p>
<p>来自多伦多的Riam Sharal看得兴高采烈，“非常喜欢，喜欢他们的服装，喜欢他们的编舞，非常酷！感受到强大的能量，令人振动的能量。看得出来，观众都特别高兴，每个人都很陶醉。这是庆祝加拿大国庆日的好方式。”</p>
