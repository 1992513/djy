<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>燃料费开征了 多元缴纳真方便</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）2022年自用汽车及机车汽燃费开征了，本(111)年度自用汽车及机车汽燃费缴纳通知书已于6月29日寄出，缴费期间至7月31日止，如不依规定缴纳将处以新台币300元以上3,000元以下罚锾，并移送行政执行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774079" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774079-563376.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13774079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774079-563376-600x833.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="625" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774079" class="wp-caption-text">汽燃费多元缴纳海报图档。（嘉义市监理站提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13774078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774078" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774078-563375.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13774078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774078-563375-600x848.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="636" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774078" class="wp-caption-text">汽燃费电子缴费单海报图档。（嘉义市监理站提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因新冠肺炎疫情仍持续严峻，为减少感染概率降低群聚风险及达到省纸减碳绿地球目的，公路总局配合数位经济环境，已营造“临柜至网路”多元缴纳汽车燃料使用费服务，免出门也能线上缴纳，方便又迅速。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774079" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774079-563376.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13774079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774079-563376-600x833.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="623" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774079" class="wp-caption-text">汽燃费多元缴纳海报图档。（嘉义市监理站提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>请车主朋友于收到缴纳通知书后：</h2>
<h3>一、临柜缴纳：</h3>
<p>持纸本缴纳通知书至金融机构、邮局、超商(统一、全家、莱尔富、来来)或驻各区监理所、站代收公库缴纳。</p>
<h3>二、网路缴纳：</h3>
<p>(一)监理服务网(https://www.mvdis.gov.tw/)或手机下载“监理服务App”、一卡通MONEY、哔哔缴、橘子支付、虾皮购物、街口支付、永丰银行及元大行动等APP，以信用卡或金融活期账户线上缴纳。</p>
<p>(二)银行公会“e-Bill全国缴费网”，以金融活期账户或晶片金融卡输入销账编号线上转账缴纳。</p>
<h3>三、电话语音转账：</h3>
<p>服务专线412-1366，41-1366。</p>
<p>若使用信用卡缴纳，手续费请洽各发卡银行，其余缴纳方式均免手续费。</p>
<p>嘉义市监理站站长洪广诚鼓励车主朋友，可以透过申办“电子缴款单”或“约定转账代缴汽燃费”，以减少遗失或收不到缴款通知书的困扰喔!既简单又方便，而且环保、e化、爱地球，相关申请请至监理服务网。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774080" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774080-563377.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13774080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774080-563377.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774080" class="wp-caption-text">公路总局监理服务网QR Code。（嘉义市监理站提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而且即日起于嘉义市监理站临柜新增申办完成“设定约定账户扣款”或“电子缴款单”后，可获得精美小礼物喔！</p>
<p>尚未办理的车主朋友们，赶快向嘉义市监理站洽询办理事宜，数量有限，送完为止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774082" style="width: 305px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774082-563378.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13774082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774082-563378.png" alt="" width="305" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774082" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市监理站约扣QR Code。（嘉义市监理站提供）</figcaption></figure>
