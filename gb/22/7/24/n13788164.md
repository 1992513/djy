<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美国会委员会：停止迫害法轮功</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12993164-6.jpeg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
