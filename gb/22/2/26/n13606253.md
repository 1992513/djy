<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>荟萃各国风味 卡城Avenida美食世界</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606320-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>卡尔加里农贸市场——Avenida食堂和生鲜市场（Avenida Food Hall and Fresh Market）。（王颖／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年02月26日讯】卡尔加里Canyon Meadows社区有一个独特的、充满生活气息的农贸市场——Avenida食堂和生鲜市场（Avenida Food Hall and Fresh Market）。每周四至周日，超过50个商家出售本地生产的蔬菜、食品及日用品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13606325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13606325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606325-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13606325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606325-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-04-600x373.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13606325" class="wp-caption-text">Avenida食堂和生鲜市场。（王颖／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13606326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13606326" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606326-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13606326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606326-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-03-600x382.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13606326" class="wp-caption-text">Avenida食堂和生鲜市场。（王颖／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13606327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13606327" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606327-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13606327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606327-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-06-600x502.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="502" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13606327" class="wp-caption-text">Avenida食堂和生鲜市场。（王颖／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市场内最具特色的是美食广场，新鲜制作世界各地的街头美食：意大利、匈牙利、日本、孟加拉、泰国、夏威夷、秘鲁、非洲、拉丁、印第安等等。价廉物美，绝对让你食指大动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13606328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13606328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606328-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13606328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606328-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-02-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13606328" class="wp-caption-text">美食广场。（王颖／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13606330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13606330" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606330-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13606330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606330-20220219-YingWang-Avenida-05-600x558.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="558" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13606330" class="wp-caption-text">美食广场。（王颖／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>食堂和生鲜市场是由家族经营的企业，老板Darrell表示，他们会不定期推出不同主题的食品，比如以夏威夷为主题的美食日，每个商家会制作一个夏威夷特色食品以飧新老顾客。</p>
<p>拿起购物篮，花点时间在这里闲逛，你一定能满载而归。</p>
