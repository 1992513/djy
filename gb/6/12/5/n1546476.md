<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第204课</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元12月5日讯】<br /><center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa204.mp3> MP3下载</a></center><br /><center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa204.rm> Real下载</a></center><br />　<br />Larry最近去约塞美蒂国家公园度假。他正在告诉李华旅游的情况。李华会学到两个常用语：blown away和bone head。</p>
<p>LL: Yosemite was fantastic. You really should go sometime, Li Hua. I know you would be completely blown away by the scenery.</p>
<p>LH: 你是说那儿的风很大，会把我吹走呀？我本来倒是很想去，可是要是会被吹走的话，那不是太危险了嘛？</p>
<p>LL: No, no. By &#8220;blown away&#8221; I mean you would be astounded. When something blows you away, it amazes you.</p>
<p>LH: 噢，blown away 在这里指的是感到惊异，吃惊。对了，我看到过约塞美蒂国家公园的照片。照片上的风景就让我惊叹不已了。</p>
<p>LL: But being there in person is so much more amazing. You won&#8217;t believe how high some of the waterfalls are; when you go, it will blow you away!</p>
<p>LH: 身临其境那当然更好了。我是听说了那儿的瀑布从很高的山上流下来，非常惊人。其实你知道吗，Larry，中国也有许多有名的山脉。下回你去中国一定要去黄山玩。那里的景色也会让你惊叹不已！</p>
<p>LL: I am sure I will be blown away. I&#8217;ve heard friends who have been to Huang Shan and talk about it, and it does sound amazing.</p>
<p>LH: 对，很多美国人都去黄山玩过，他们都觉得很美。对了，Larry，你去约塞美蒂有没有照相哪？</p>
<p>LL: I sure do. Before I left, my uncle came by and gave me a new camera to take with me to Yosemite. I was blown away by his generosity.</p>
<p>LH: 你叔叔那么好呀！知道你要出去玩还特地送你一个新的照相机呀！他这么慷慨，人人都会感到惊异，而且还会让人羡慕你呐。</p>
<p>LL: Yes, sometimes when someone does something unexpectedly nice, it really blows you away.</p>
<p>LH: 对，像我刚到美国的时候没有汽车，有的人开车带我去买菜，然后告诉我到什么地方该怎么走。我没有想到人们会对我这么好，真是让我感到又感动，又惊讶呢。</p>
<p>LL: You know, the scenery was beautiful, but I was also blown away by the number of tourists at Yosemite.</p>
<p>LH: 那有什么奇怪的？放暑假嘛，旅游的人当然多了！</p>
<p>******</p>
<p>LL: Li Hua，I was also blown away by some of the stupid things people would do in the park!</p>
<p>LH: 那不奇怪，在哪儿都有人做一些不得体的事。你看见有些人做什么啦？</p>
<p>LL: Well, there was one guy who was feeding the deer. What a bone head!</p>
<p>LH: 给鹿吃东西？公园里一般都不让大家给动物吃东西。你说bone head，你是指那头鹿头上有骨头？谁头上没有骨头呀？</p>
<p>LL: No, I mean the guy who fed the deer was a bone head. A bone head is someone who has no brains and does something really stupid.</p>
<p>LH: 嗯，bone head就是没有脑筋的人，所以你是指那些给鹿吃东西的人没有脑子。对了，我听说鹿应该在他们所在的栖息地自己找东西吃。</p>
<p>LL: Yes, there are thousands of tourists who come to Yosemite every day. If everyone fed them, the deer would probably get fat and sick!</p>
<p>LH: 还有那些随地扔东西的人也很讨厌，他们也都是bone-headed。</p>
<p>LL: Exactly. Not picking up your trash just spoils the view for others and is bad for the environment.</p>
<p>LH: 就是嘛，到处扔垃圾不但会影响公园的景色，而且也有害与保护环境。也许他们是因为没有扔垃圾的口袋？</p>
<p>LL: That still seems like a bone-headed excuse to me. You can carry your trash until you find a park trash can or bring it with you to your car.</p>
<p>LH: 你说我没有脑筋才会问这样的问题？我看你才没脑筋呐！把垃圾拿在手里，等到看见垃圾筒再扔？或者把垃圾带回到你汽车里？什么馊主意呀！</p>
<p>LL：I wonder why don&#8217;t parks give out trash bags to everyone who comes into the park, Li Hua, Do you think that would prevent littering?</p>
<p>LH: Larry，这才是个好主意呐！ 公园应该给每个来公园玩的人发一个垃圾袋，这样人们就不会随地扔东西了。</p>
<p>LL：In fact, there are some parks in the U.S. that do that.</p>
<p>LH: 有的公园已经这样做了，那别的公园也应该这样做。</p>
<p>LL：So, I had a good idea! I guess that means you don&#8217;t think I&#8217;m a bone head!</p>
<p>LH：对，这个主意很好，所以你不是一个bone head.</p>
<p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是blown away，意思是惊叹，惊奇。另一个常用语是bone head，意思是没有脑筋的人。<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
