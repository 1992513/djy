<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第207课</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元12月22日讯】<center><ahref=https://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa207.mp3>(MP3下载)</a></center><center><ahref=https://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa207.rm>(Real下载)</a></center><br />Larry 刚在银行开了一个账户。他一走出银行的大门就遇到了李华。今天李华会学到两个常用语：freebie和megabucks。</p>
<p>LL: Hi there, Li Hua! You should open a savings account today. Look at the freebie I got at the bank.</p>
<p>LH: Freebie? 你手里拿的好像是个计算机。</p>
<p>LL: You are right, Li Hua. It is a calculator and, you know, it is free. And that is what makes it a freebie!</p>
<p>LH: Free？你说，只要开个账户就能得到一个免费的计算机。所以免费送的东西就是freebie。真的是送给你的吗？</p>
<p>LL: Yes, it was a special promotion. I love getting freebies. And a calculator is a good freebie.</p>
<p>LH: 原来是银行在开展特别的推销活动，给开账户的人送一个礼物。 计算机是有用的东西。不过，凡是免费的我也都喜欢。</p>
<p>LL: Banks often give away candy as freebie or a pen with the bank logo. But a calculator is something I will really use. That makes it a good freebie.</p>
<p>LH: 是啊，送给我一支笔，那也有用。送我糖我才不要呢！在所有的免费礼品中我最喜欢香水了。</p>
<p>LL: It is a good way to try expensive perfumes before spending the money. I guess.</p>
<p>LH: 对呀！买一瓶香水很贵。送我一瓶小小的香水，要是喜欢我下回可以去买，不喜欢就算。这样就不会浪费钱了。Larry, 还有哪里可以得到免费商品啊 ?</p>
<p>LL: Well, book stores often give away freebie bookmarks. But, I think my favorite freebie is food!</p>
<p>LH: 书店给的书签没有什么吸引力。我也喜欢超市里为了推销而提供的那些免费食品，什么面包呀，蛋糕呀，肚子饿的时候可好啦！</p>
<p>LL: Speaking of freebie food, here is a grocery store across the street. Let&#8217;s go in and see if they have any freebies.</p>
<p>LH：对马路的超市有免费食品呀？那还等什么呀？快走呀！</p>
<p>(Sound of door opening, grocery store sounds)</p>
<p>LL: Let&#8217;s go to the freezer compartments. There is usually a lady giving out freebie samples of pizza.</p>
<p>LH: 我看见了， 后面是有一个女服务员在送比萨饼呢。Larry， 你可真行呀！哪里有freebie 你都知道。</p>
<p>******</p>
<p>LL: Say， Li Hua， would you like to win megabucks?</p>
<p>LH: Megabucks? Mega意思是大， buck是指美元。 如果我没有猜错的话， megabucks的意思是很多钱。</p>
<p>LL: Wow, Li Hua. Your English is getting really good. That&#8217;s right, megabucks is a lot of money.</p>
<p>LH: Larry, 我当然想赢megabucks。谁不想发财呢？问题是怎么才能得到那么多钱呢？</p>
<p>LL: Well, we can buy a lottery ticket at the front of the grocery store. Who knows, we may win megabucks.</p>
<p>LH: 你要到前面去买一张彩票呀？我才不信我们会赢megabucks呢！不过， Larry，要是我们赢了，那会有多少钱呀?</p>
<p>LL: If all six of the numbers on our ticket match the winning numbers, we will win 5 million dollars.</p>
<p>LH: 要真能赢五百万， 那该多好呀！我们就不用再打工了。可是，要六个数字全和彩票一样才能赢，那中彩的概率不会很高吧？</p>
<p>LL: It really isn&#8217;t. We would have to spend megabucks buying lottery tickets to even have a small chance of winning.</p>
<p>LH: 我就知道嘛，这种机会是很小的。即便花好多钱去买好多彩票，我们也不见得会赢。与其这么毫无希望地等着中彩票还不如我们自己去赚大钱呢。</p>
<p>LL: You mean go to work? I don&#8217;t know about you, Li Hua, but I&#8217;m not being paid megabucks.</p>
<p>LH: 我知道你工资不高，我的工资也很低啊。 不过如果我们努力工作的话， 说不定哪一天我们会发财的。</p>
<p>LL: You mean like Bill Gates of Microsoft? He&#8217;s certainly made megabucks.</p>
<p>LH: 是啊，所以微软的Bill Gates把很多钱都捐给了慈善事业。</p>
<p>LL: I&#8217;m not sure I&#8217;ll be making megabucks &#8212; so for now I&#8217;ll just keep living off of freebies in the grocery store. Want some more pizza?</p>
<p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是freebie，意思是免费送的东西。还有一个是megabucks，意思是很多钱。<font color=#ffffff>(https://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
