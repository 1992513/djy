<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第200课</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元11月3日讯】<br /><center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa200.mp3>(MP3下载)</a></center><center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa200.rm>(Real下载)</a></center></p>
<p>Larry最近一直在忙着帮老板准备讲话稿。李华到Larry的办公室来看望他。李华今天会学到两个常用语：holed up和come up for air。</p>
<p>LL: It is so nice to see you Li Hua. I&#8217;ve been holed up in the office for days trying to get this presentation done for my boss.</p>
<p>LH: Larry，我知道你忙着给你老板准备报告，可你说在办公室好几天干什么来啦？Hold up，这不是抢劫吗？</p>
<p>LL: No, not a hold up. I said &#8220;holed up&#8221;. Well, when I said I had been holed up here in the office, I meant I hadn&#8217;t left my office in days,</p>
<p>LH: 原来如此。你是说你在办公室好几天没出去。Holed up就是由于某种原因呆在一个地方很久没法出去。</p>
<p>LL: Right. I&#8217;ve been holed up here for three whole days now. I feel kind of like a rabbit sleeping in his rabbit hole for the winter.</p>
<p>LH: 你觉得自己好像是只兔子钻在洞里过冬！说得真形象。对了，我是有好几天没见到你了，好像不止三天哪！</p>
<p>LL: When you were holed up working on your thesis, Li Hua. I didn&#8217;t see you for a long time.</p>
<p>LH: 那到也是。我在赶论文的那段时间常常好几天都不出门， 真有钻进了洞里的感觉。</p>
<p>LL: Well, if you have to be holed up somewhere, your apartment is a pretty good place. At least it is comfortable.</p>
<p>LH: 这倒是真的。 我呆在家里好几天，是觉得很舒服，不像你这办公室，累了连个躺的地方都没有。</p>
<p>LL: There is a sofa in the break room. I&#8217;ve been taking naps there.</p>
<p>LH: 你在休息室里的沙发上睡个午睡就行啦？ Larry, 你还要多久才能准备好你老板的报告呀？你这么老呆在这里不回家，对你的身体不好。</p>
<p>LL: Never mind, I&#8217;m strong. I&#8217;ll finish my boss&#8217;s presentation in a few days.</p>
<p>LH: 还要几天才能完成这报告呀？这样做，时间长了你会生病的。生了病，你可就真的不能出门了。You&#8217;ll be holed up at home。</p>
<p>LL: At this point, being holed up at home sounds a lot better than being holed up here at work.</p>
<p>LH: Larry, 还是跟我出去呼吸一下新鲜空气，你再回去继续干。</p>
<p>******</p>
<p>LL: Thanks for getting me out of the office, Li Hua. I just had to come up for air.</p>
<p>LH: 你不用谢我，我写论文的时候，你也把我拽出来休息休息。Larry，你说&#8221;come up for air&#8221; 大概是指换换环境，呼吸点新鲜空气，休息一下，是吗？</p>
<p>LL: Right, I felt like I was drowning under all that work. Coming up for air was just what I needed.</p>
<p>LH: 我跟你有同样的感觉。学期结束前我赶着写一篇报告时候，好几天没出门；我觉得自己真的好像被那些书和笔记本给淹没了。</p>
<p>LL：I know. You looked so pale and evenly sickly. That&#8217;s why I practically dragged you out to come up for air.</p>
<p>LH: 我当时确实感到好像要生病的样子。幸亏你拉我出去换换空气，休息了一下。好吧，你现在想做些什么呢？</p>
<p>LL: Well, I remember when you were working on your thesis, Li Hua, I&#8217;d get you to come up for air by asking you to go for a walk in the park.</p>
<p>LH: 我记得你带我去的那个公园。那里有各式各样的花。好吧，我们上那公园去散步。</p>
<p>LL: OK, let&#8217;s go for a walk in the park for a little while. I know I&#8217;ll work better after coming up for some air.</p>
<p>LH: 我敢肯定你散步，休息后工作起来会更有效的。对了，明天我要说服我的同Lisa 也出来休息一下。</p>
<p>LL: Why? What is she working on?</p>
<p>LH: 她正在找工作，所以忙着在网上，报上查招聘广告，寄几十份履历给公司，填写申请工作的表格&#8230;&#8230; 她已经有一个星期没有出门了。</p>
<p>LL: Sounds like she does need to come up for some air. If she wants to come out for air tomorrow, maybe I can join you too.</p>
<p>LH：那太好了。你们俩都应该注意休息。You two should come up for air sometimes!</p>
<p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是&#8221; holed up&#8221;, 意思是一个人由于某种原因在一个地方不能出门。李华学到的另一个常用语是&#8221;come up for air&#8221;, 意思是换换环境，休息一会儿。<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
