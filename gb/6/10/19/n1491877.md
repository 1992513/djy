<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第198课</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元10月19日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa198.mp3>(MP3下载)</a></center><center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa198.rm>(Real下载)</a></center></p>
<p>Larry 在跟李华讲他办公室里新来的实习生。今天李华会学到两个常用语：eager beaver和grunt work。</p>
<p>LL: A new intern started working in my office today. He sure is an eager beaver, Li Hua!</p>
<p>LH: Beaver不是海狸吗？动物怎么能到办公室实习呢？</p>
<p>LL: No, the new intern isn&#8217;t an actual beaver! But he works hard and is always busy like a beaver building a dam. And he is always eager to do work, so we say he is an &#8220;eager beaver.”</p>
<p>LH: 对了，我想起来了。Beaver不仅指海狸，有时也指工作勤劳的人。Eager就是很热情的意思，所以&#8221;eager beaver”指的是做事特别努力，特别积极的人。 Larry， 我看你也是个&#8221;eager beaver”，早出晚归的。</p>
<p>LL: I am not quite the eager beaver that the new intern is &#8211; I&#8217;ve been in the office a while. People are generally eager beavers when they are new to a job and want to impress their bosses.</p>
<p>LH: 没错，一般新到一个地方工作的人会特别热情积极，因为他们要让老板有个好印象。我刚开始教中文的时候， 也特别卖力，每天都花很多时间备课。</p>
<p>LL: And would you say you still are an eager beaver when it comes to teaching Chinese?</p>
<p>LH: 现在呢，不像刚开始那么卖劲了。像你一样， 我也不是以前的eager beaver了。</p>
<p>LL: That&#8217;s OK, Li Hua. While being an eager beaver can be a good thing, someone can also take it too far.</p>
<p>LH: 勤奋当然是好事呀！居然还会有的人勤奋过头？这是什么意思？</p>
<p>LL: Well, the intern in my office is such an eager beaver he wants to do everything! He ends up making my work more complicated.</p>
<p>LH: 这个实习生什么都要做，这不是很好吗， 怎么会给你添麻烦呢？</p>
<p>LL: Yeah, that&#8217;s right. He always wants to help, but sometimes I work better on my own.</p>
<p>LH: 你要是有的时候喜欢自己做，不要别人插手的话，你不能对他说你不需要帮忙嘛！对了，Larry，也许你可以 想办法让他作一些你不喜欢作的事呀。</p>
<p>LL: What a good idea!</p>
<p>******</p>
<p>LL: Thanks to your advice, Li Hua, I&#8217;ve now got the new intern doing some of my grunt work.</p>
<p>LH: 什么是grunt work? Grunt不是指猪发出呼噜呼噜的声音吗？</p>
<p>LL: Yes, pigs grunt. And people make grunting sounds, too, when they are working really hard at something like digging ditches.</p>
<p>LH: 这我知道。人在做挖沟之类的工作时也会发出表示疲劳的声音。Larry, 那grunt work 是不是就是指繁重的体力劳动？</p>
<p>LL: Not necessarily. Grunt work is basically routine, menial work. There are always some repetitive, boring tasks people working in offices do.</p>
<p>LH: 我明白了，grunt work就是简单的，枯燥乏味的工作。</p>
<p>LL: Yeah, and grunt work generally doesn&#8217;t require a lot of knowledge or experience. That is why I am having the intern do my grunt work.</p>
<p>LH: 做简单的工作当然不需要丰富的知识，也不需要很多经验。Larry，说了半天，你究竟让这位实习生做什么呢?</p>
<p>LL: I am having him reorganize the office files, make new labels for the file folders, filling out forms and typing up letters.</p>
<p>LH: 你还真让他干不少活耶！整理办公室的文件，并把它们存档， 还要为文件夹贴新的标签、填表格、打信件。这些事的确是既费事又枯燥的。这位实习生该不会在意吧？</p>
<p>LL: No, I am sure he won&#8217;t mind doing the grunt work.</p>
<p>LH: 你怎么肯定他不在意呀？</p>
<p>LL: He is such an eager beaver, Li Hua, that he will be delighted to do whatever grunt work I give him!</p>
<p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是&#8221;eager beaver&#8221;, 意思是做事特别积极，努力的人。李华学到的另一个常用语是，grunt work意思是简单枯燥的工作。<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
