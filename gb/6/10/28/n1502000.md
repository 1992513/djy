<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第199课</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元10月28日讯】<center><ahref=https://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa199.mp3>(MP3下载)</a></center><center><ahref=https://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa199.rm>(Real下载)</a></center></p>
<p>Larry和李华的朋友David这个周末要举行聚会。Larry和李华在谈论为什么一个朋友不能参加。李华今天会学到两个常用语：baloney和party animal。</p>
<p>LH: Larry，Laura说她下个周末不能去参加David的聚会，因为她要去看望她的阿姨。</p>
<p>LL: That&#8217;s baloney. She&#8217;s just making up an excuse not to go to the party.</p>
<p>LH: Laura是在找借口？ 我想不会吧！对了，Larry，你说&#8221;That&#8217;s baloney&#8221;，这是什么意思呀？Baloney不是做三明治用的一种大香肠吗？</p>
<p>LL: Yes, literally, baloney is a lunchmeat; however, in this case, baloney means &#8220;nonsense.&#8221;</p>
<p>LH: 在这种情况下，baloney是指胡说，废话。你的意思是Laura在胡说。嗨，Laura不去就不去吧， 为什么要骗人呢？</p>
<p>LL: She doesn&#8217;t want to go to the party either because her ex-boyfriend Jack will be there and doesn&#8217;t want to be rude.</p>
<p>LH: Larry，婉言谢绝邀请也没有什么不礼貌。另外，Laura上次对我说她和Jack 虽然分手了，可还是好朋友。我不相信Laura是在找借口。</p>
<p>LL: Now, that&#8217;s really baloney. The two of them don&#8217;t even speak to each other anymore.</p>
<p>LH: 他们俩现在见了面都不理睬，也不说话？这跟Laura说的完全相反，是谁告诉你的呀？That&#8217;s baloney!</p>
<p>LL: That&#8217;s what Jack told me the last time I spoke with him. He seemed very disappointed that they weren&#8217;t getting along. He sounded really sincere.</p>
<p>LH: 他对和Laura分手感到很失望！That&#8217;s baloney! 要是他感到惋惜，Laura又说他们还是朋友，那为什么他告诉你两人见了面象仇人那样不理睬， 不说话的？我才不相信Jack!</p>
<p>LL: Come on, Jack is a good guy. He wouldn&#8217;t tell me something that is baloney.</p>
<p>LH: 可是Laura也不是坏人，她也不是说谎的人哪！。</p>
<p>LL: Okay, okay. Forget what I said! It was all just a bunch of baloney, anyway!</p>
<p>******</p>
<p>LL: Even if Laura isn&#8217;t coming, I am glad Tom is coming to David&#8217;s party. Tom is a real party animal!</p>
<p>LH: Larry， 你说什么？Tom是party animal？Tom是人，不是什么动物！</p>
<p>LL: Yes, he is a human being, but he is a human being that loves to go to parties! People that love to party are party animals.</p>
<p>LH: 原来party animal指的是特别喜欢参加聚会的人，也就是聚会狂。Tom哪一次聚会都不错过，要这么说，他的确是个party animal。 Larry，我看你也是一个喜欢参加聚会的人，也是个party animal！</p>
<p>LL: Yeah, but I am not as much of a party animal as Tom.</p>
<p>LH: 你倒挺谦虚的，觉得自己还比不上Tom。你说说，你哪一点比Tom差呢？</p>
<p>LL: Well, Tom goes out to parties nearly every weekend. I&#8217;ve got too much to do, and I really can&#8217;t go out all the time.</p>
<p>LH: 那倒是，Tom确实每个周末都去参加聚会，你还没到这个程度。Larry，David平时很严肃，还很害羞，没想到他会举办这个聚会。他绝不是像你和Tom那样的party animal。</p>
<p>LL: Well, you might be surprised! Sometimes at a party David relaxes and acts just like Tom &#8211; like a real party animal!</p>
<p>LH: 我才不信呢！David 参加聚会时比平时更放松一点是可能的，但绝不会像Tom那样活宝。我看见过Tom在聚会上的样子， David不可能是个party animal。</p>
<p>LL: Sure, he laughs, tells jokes and has a good time &#8211; and some times he gets crazy and does the funniest things.</p>
<p>LH: 这可出乎我的意料。David居然还会如此谈笑风生，讲笑话，做逗人笑的事。说明我对他还不了解。</p>
<p>LL: What about you, Li Hua? Are you a party animal?</p>
<p>LH: Larry, 你别胡扯了！ 你看我像聚会狂吗？</p>
<p>LL: Let&#8217;s just say if someone said to me &#8220;That Li Hua is a real party animal&#8221; &#8211; I&#8217;d say that is baloney!</p>
<p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是baloney, 意思是骗人的鬼话， 胡扯。李华学到的另一个常用语是party animal， 指的是特别喜欢参加社交聚会的人。<br />	<font color=#ffffff>(https://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
