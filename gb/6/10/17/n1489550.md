<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第197课</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元10月17日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa197.mp3>(MP3下载)</a></center><center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa197.rm>(Real下载)</a></center><br />李华今天请Larry到家里来一起看电视转播的世界杯比赛。今天是美国队对捷克队。李华会学到两个常用语：make a big stink和to be creamed。</p>
<p>(Sound of knocking door)</p>
<p>LH：请进来！ Come on in, Larry!</p>
<p>LL: Oh, hey there, Li Hua, I see the game has started. What&#8217;s the big stink?</p>
<p>LH: 球赛刚开始。你说这儿有臭味？ 我没有闻到什么臭味呀！</p>
<p>LL: No, I don&#8217;t mean your apartment is stinking. I was asking why the soccer players are making a big stink. Big stink means making a big fuss or complaining.</p>
<p>LH: 噢，你是问电视上这些球员在吵什么？嗨，美国队踢进了一个球，可是没有得分，因为裁判判定失误，所以就吵了起来。</p>
<p>LL: So, the players are mad, and the U.S. soccer coach is making a big stink by arguing with the referee.</p>
<p>LH: 是啊， 美国队个个气得火冒三丈。美国教练通常比较低调， 这次也忍不住跟裁判争执，表示强烈不满。</p>
<p>LL: Yeah, but I don&#8217;t think making a big stink about the call is going to change the referee&#8217;s mind.</p>
<p>LH: 我也这么想呢， 大吵大闹是不会改变裁判的决定的，还不如就算了， 接着比赛就好了嘛。</p>
<p>LL: This reminds me of when I played baseball as a kid. Every once in a while a parent would get upset with the umpire and make a big stink.</p>
<p>LH: 这不仅是你小时候打棒球的情况，现在也是那样。美国家长特别重视孩子的体育活动，有比赛必到现场，为孩子打气。觉得不公平还会对裁判大喊大叫。 我父母只有在我学习成绩不好的时候才会严厉批评。</p>
<p>LL: Li Hua, weren&#8217;t you the perfect student? I can&#8217;t imagine your parents having anything to make a stink about.</p>
<p>LH: 谁说他们不生气？我上中学的时候， 有个学期我的数学成绩很差。 我父母就特别生气。这对他们来说才是件大事。</p>
<p>LL: Well, they had your best interests at heart. Say, you know what I want to make a big stink about now?</p>
<p>LH: 我当然知道他们是为了我好。You want to make a big stink？有什么事让你不满意呀？</p>
<p>LL: Where is the snack food? You can&#8217;t watch soccer without having some snacks.</p>
<p>LH: 嗨，你们美国人呀，看球就非得吃零食。不过，这不是什么大事。You don&#8217;t have to make a big stink。 厨房里有好多吃的，你自己去拿 吧。</p>
<p>*******</p>
<p>LL: Have some chips, Li Hua. Hmm&#8230;it looks like the U.S. is getting creamed.</p>
<p>LH: Creamed? Cream不是奶油吗？</p>
<p>LL: No, creamed mean getting beaten. And badly.</p>
<p>LH：噢，creamed在这里的意思是被打败，而且是惨败。</p>
<p>LL：That&#8217;s right. Say, Li Hua, why are you rooting for the U.S. team, anyway?</p>
<p>LH: 我为什么帮美国队呀？你忘啦，中国队在亚洲地区赛中输给了科威特队， 根本没有资格参加世界杯赛。</p>
<p>LL: Oh, yeah, I remember that. They were creamed pretty badly &#8211; Six to one, as I recall.</p>
<p>LH: 没错，输给科威特六比一！别说了， 一提起中国队的惨败， 我就伤心。 所以现在我支持美国队， 因为我在美国留学， 可没想到美国队也要输了。The US team is going to be creamed!</p>
<p>LL: The Czech Republic team is really good. I am not surprised the U.S. is getting creamed.</p>
<p>LH: 美国队看来是输定了，我们别看球了。出去玩吧。</p>
<p>LL: It&#8217;s a nice day, Li Hua. Let&#8217;s go out and play some badminton.</p>
<p>LH: Larry，打羽毛球 你可不是我的对手， 说不定会被打得一败涂地呦。I&#8217;m sure you&#8217;ll get creamed.</p>
<p>LL: I tell you what, Li Hua, if you cream me at badminton, I promise&#8230;</p>
<p>LH: 你保证什么？</p>
<p>LL: I promise not to make a big stink about it!</p>
<p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是make a big stink, 意思是大吵大闹、表示强烈不满。李华学到的另一个常用语是to be creamed意思是被击败。 <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
