<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第194课</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元9月25日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa194.mp3>(MP3下载)</a></center><center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa194.rm>(Real下载)</a></center>　　 <br />　　<br />Larry 刚刚给自己的公寓贴了新的墙纸，可是他觉得效果很不理想。 李华今天会学到两个常用语：diddlysquat和rub it in。 <br />　　<br />LL: Today, Li Hua, I discovered that I don&#8217;t know diddlysquat about putting up wallpaper. <br />　　<br />LH: 什么? diddlysquat？听你的意思，你好像是说你对贴墙纸什么也不懂，是吗？ <br />　　<br />LL: That&#8217;s right. I don&#8217;t know diddlysquat means I don&#8217;t know anything. <br />　　<br />LH：噢，Don&#8217;t know diddlysquat就是什么也不懂，一窍不通。 <br />　　<br />LL：Right. When I was wallpapering my apartment, I didn&#8217;t get the pieces perfectly straight before I put them on, and now the pattern doesn&#8217;t line up. <br />　　<br />LH: 我也对贴墙纸一窍不通。不过，贴之前好像总得把墙纸弄直了。否则，墙纸的花样怎么对得起来呢！ <br />　　<br />LL: Yeah, but it is harder to do than you might think. I really needed to read instructions in a how-to book before I started, but I didn&#8217;t do diddlysquat to prepare. <br />　　<br />LH: 原来你什么准备都没有做就开始贴墙纸呀！美国有很多关于如何装修房屋的书，你什么没看就动手啦？你胆子也够大的。得了，你来帮我点忙， 分散一下注意力，心情就会好一些。好吗？ <br />　　<br />LL: Sure, how can I help? <br />　　<br />LH: 我昨天买了一个新的电脑硬盘，可我在这方面什么也不懂，I don&#8217;t know diddlysquat about computer。 你来帮我装一下，好吗？ <br />　　<br />LL: You are just like my mom, Li Hua. She doesn&#8217;t know diddlysquat about computers either─but I am happy to help out. When should I come by? <br />　　<br />LH: Larry，你能帮我装上，那太好了。午饭以后可以过来吗？ <br />　　<br />LL: OK, I&#8217;ll do that. But maybe you can come help me remove the wallpaper I put up. <br />　　<br />LH: 帮你撕墙纸，I don&#8217;t know diddlysquat about that! 让我看看再说吧。 </p>
<p>***** <br />　　<br />LH: Oh, Larry, 这墙纸还真是没对齐。 <br />　　<br />LL: Yeah, I know. Please don&#8217;t rub it in. <br />　　<br />LH: 不要抹进去？我们不是要把墙纸撕下来吗？ <br />　　<br />LL: No, no. By “rub it in”, I didn&#8217;t mean rub the wallpaper into the wall. I meant that I already know the wallpaper doesn&#8217;t line up, so you don&#8217;t need to tell me again and make me feel worse. <br />　　<br />LH: 我明白了， rub it in的意思是在伤口上抹盐，令人更难堪。 墙纸没贴好，你已经很难过了，我又提起这件事，就是rub it in &#8211; 让你更难受，对吗？ <br />　　<br />LL: Yeah, especially when it is something you are not happy about. “Rubbing it in” just makes a person feel worse. <br />　　<br />LH: Rub it in，只会让人更难过， 所以我们的朋友Rick撞车之后，你并没有对他说， “你怎么这么不小心”，因为你不想让他更难堪。 <br />　　<br />LL: Right, I didn&#8217;t want Rick to feel even worse about his car. <br />　　<br />LH: 上个星期考试，我考得好， 你考得不好，我说，“你本来就该好好复习嘛”。这又是 rubbing it in，往伤口上抹盐，对 不对？ <br />　　<br />LL: Exactly. <br />　　<br />LH: 可是我只是想对朋友提出忠告，并没有 抹盐的意思。 <br />　　<br />LL: I know you didn&#8217;t mean to rub it in, Li Hua. Well, let&#8217;s go see what we can do about your hard drive. <br />　　<br />LH: Great. 好吧，去看看我的电脑吧。 <br />　　<br />LL: You are really lucky that we are friends! I mean, you don&#8217;t know diddlysquat about computers. You&#8217;d never be able to install the hard drive without me. <br />　　<br />LH: 我知道我对电脑一窍不通， 你就别往伤口上抹盐了。You don&#8217;t have to rub it in！ <br />　　<br />今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是don&#8217;t know diddlysquat about something，意思是对某件事一无所知，一窍不通。李华学到的另一个常用语是 rub it in， 意思是在某个人的伤口上抹盐，令人更加难过。<br /><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
