<p>春夜喜雨　杜甫</p>
<p>好雨知时节，<br />当春乃发生。<br />随风潜入夜，<br />润物细无声。<br />野径云俱黑，<br />江船火独明。<br />晓看红湿处，<br />花重锦官城。</p>
<p>Spring Night, Happy For the Rain<br />　　　　　　　　　　By Du Fu</p>
<p>Good rain knows the season and the time,<br />When spring comes it begins to descend.<br />With the breeze it sneaks into the night,<br />Moistening all things so quietly and soft.<br />The wild paths and the clouds are all dark,<br />Just on the river the boat&#8217;s lantern is bright.<br />In the morning let&#8217;s see the places red and wet&#8211;<br />The flowers becoming heavy in Chengdu city.@</p>
