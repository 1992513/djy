<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：大地行吟 颜子后代颜圣哲彩墨画展</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2014年10月08日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾台北报导）“大地行吟─颜圣哲彩墨画展”即日起至11月20日于台湾新北市客家文化园区举行，展出颜子第82代裔孙颜圣哲30余件精彩的彩墨及油彩创作作品。</p>
<p>孔子弟子颜子名回，字子渊，春秋时鲁人。颜子第82代裔孙颜圣哲，1943年生于台南麻豆镇，就读中国文化大学美术系，师事灵沤馆江派文人画大师江兆申，毕业获水墨画、篆刻首奖，2000年荣获全球中华文化艺术薪传奖等极多奖项的肯定。</p>
	<figure id="attachment_5784201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5784201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://cn.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410080938502378-600x462.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5784201" class="wp-caption-text">颜圣哲油彩画作《黄金田园》。（新北市客家文化园区）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5784216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5784216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://cn.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410080937162378-600x561.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5784216" class="wp-caption-text">颜圣哲彩墨画作《曲涧幽鸣》。（新北市客家文化园区）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5784226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5784226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://cn.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410080939502378-600x573.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5784226" class="wp-caption-text">颜圣哲彩墨画作《鹰嘴山风云》。（新北市客家文化园区）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>国立台湾戏曲学院教授颜圣哲，以墨色油彩造境，扎实的传统技法，承袭了文人画风的坚实脉络，他将西方绘画的透视与光影概念，巧妙地融入画作当中，让每一件作品，无论在东方彩墨或西方油画的展现，皆随着向外延伸的空间性，突破画作的格局，带出深远辽阔之感；佐以线条纵横勾勒出周密的结构，绘画前后堆叠层次分明，呈现气势磅礡之景，建构传统创作的新风貌。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5784244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5784244" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://cn.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410080933112378-600x565.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5784244" class="wp-caption-text">颜圣哲彩墨画作《古厝乡情》。（新北市客家文化园区）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5784261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5784261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://cn.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410080937342378-600x409.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5784261" class="wp-caption-text">颜圣哲油彩画作《初鹿野影》。（新北市客家文化园区）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5784278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5784278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://cn.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410080939182378-600x465.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5784278" class="wp-caption-text">颜圣哲油彩画作《绿野大地》。（新北市客家文化园区）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>颜圣哲为了近距离观察自然，感受山川雄伟壮丽，体现造物之美与大地生生不息，他常常由南到北，全台湾到处行走写生，踏遍群山峻岭，遨游高峰之间，亦或畅游离岛仙境。故乡宝岛带给他无比丰富的视觉印象与内心感受，进而以笔代心，透过彩笔挥洒，将壮丽山川所带来的感动转述于纸或画布之上。</p>
<p>除了对台湾山水所表示的敬意，颜圣哲对植物花鸟也观察入微，常常将之独自绘画成小品，凸显其形象美感与特性，栩栩如生，活灵活现，展现动态之美。**<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5784289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5784289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://cn.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410080938172378-600x593.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5784289" class="wp-caption-text">颜圣哲画作《茶花系列》。（新北市客家文化园区）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5784305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5784305" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://cn.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410080937472378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5784305" class="wp-caption-text">颜圣哲画作《茶花系列》。（新北市客家文化园区）</figcaption></figure><br />
