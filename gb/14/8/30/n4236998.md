<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：泰国奇趣“颠倒屋”</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>一栋三层楼的大房屋整个翻转倒地，被风吹倒的吗？外表崭新倒不像是饱受风雨肆虐的样子。原来这是泰国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8.md#1">旅游</a>的新景点“颠倒屋”（泰文Baan Teelanka，是“上下颠倒屋”之意，也称“反转屋”）。颠倒屋让人上下倒转过来，从不同的角度观看外界。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5764862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5764862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408221609361848-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5764862" class="wp-caption-text">泰国<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E5%90%89%E5%B2%9B.md#1">普吉岛</a>三层楼“颠倒屋”的外观。 （孙明国/大纪元）</figcaption></figure> <br /> <br />
	<figure id="attachment_5764878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5764878" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408221610091848.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5764878" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E5%90%89%E5%B2%9B.md#1">普吉岛</a>“颠倒屋”的邮筒。 （孙明国/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>占地约350平方米的颠倒屋，坐落在泰国南方普吉岛热门<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8.md#1">旅游</a>购物点Siam Niramit和Premium Outlet之间。这里也是泰国唯一的颠倒屋。这一栋约十三米高的三层楼房，屋顶朝地，地板朝天倒着立。“笃笃车”、狗屋、邮筒等等也都是倒着立地。颠倒屋内部家具齐全，厨房、浴室、睡房及楼梯等等，所有设备家具全颠倒过来倒挂于天花板上。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5764892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5764892" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408221608391848-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5764892" class="wp-caption-text">普吉岛“颠倒屋”的 “狗屋”。 （孙明国/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5764909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5764909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408221610281848-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5764909" class="wp-caption-text">普吉岛“颠倒屋”的 “笃笃车”。（孙明国/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5764920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5764920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408221609141848-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5764920" class="wp-caption-text">普吉岛“颠倒屋”挑空天花板。（孙明国/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>游客们一入颠倒屋，感受到熟悉的日常事物竟然大不相同了；人们感觉到打破习惯角度看世界真是不习惯呢！到底是被谁颠倒了呢？</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5764933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5764933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408221607381848-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5764933" class="wp-caption-text">普吉岛“颠倒屋” “厨房”。有趣的“倒挂照”。（孙明国/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />无论如何，拍张照吧！看起来像飞天照、吸顶功一般的逗趣照片，也是一种引人缪思的奇趣！＠*</p>
