<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【厨娘香Q秀】百吃不腻的余氏蛋饼</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>记得有一次回花莲，在三姊家附近的早餐店看到千变万化的蛋饼菜单，真是傻眼，等了好久才轮到我，肚子早就咕噜咕噜叫了！</p>
<p>仔细看老板娘作蛋饼的步骤，真简单！</p>
<p>回到台北，把原本早餐店的 cheese片改成cheese丝，没想到变成我家的超人气早餐！</p>
<p>更印证了～行万里路胜读万卷书！（又找到出去玩的理由了！嘻！嘻！）</p>
<p><B>材料： </B></p>
<figure id="attachment_6604835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6604835" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207160923101983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6604835" class="wp-caption-text">材料（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蛋饼皮（超市有卖现成的）一张<br />起士丝适量<br />蛋一个<br />葱末一大匙<br />肉松两大匙</p>
<p><B>作法： </B></p>
<p>1. 先将蛋打散，加入葱末拌匀</p>
<p>2. 锅中放少许油</p>
<p>3. 将蛋液倒入锅中成一大圆片，趁未熟之前放上蛋饼皮<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6604840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6604840" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207160924191983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6604840" class="wp-caption-text">将蛋液倒入锅中成一大圆片（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<figure id="attachment_6604842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6604842" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207160924051983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6604842" class="wp-caption-text">趁未熟之前放上蛋饼皮（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 饼皮与蛋结合后，立即翻面（火要小）</p>
<p>5. 将起士丝平均铺在饼上</p>
<figure id="attachment_6604844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6604844" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207160923221983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6604844" class="wp-caption-text">将起士丝平均铺在饼上（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 此时可放肉松（铺成一长条）</p>
<figure id="attachment_6604847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6604847" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207160923401983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6604847" class="wp-caption-text">此时可放肉松(铺成一长条)（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 再用铲子将整个饼卷起</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_6604849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6604849" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207160924351983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6604849" class="wp-caption-text">再用铲子将整个饼卷起（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6604854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6604854" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207160923521983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6604854" class="wp-caption-text">再用铲子将整个饼卷起（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>8. 要吃的时候切成段就OK了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_6604857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6604857" style="width: 266px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207160922511983.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6604857" class="wp-caption-text">余氏蛋饼成品（摄影: 新唐人电视台 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><B>＊馅料的部分可自己作变化： </B></p>
<p>例如：玉米粒、培根…… 葱可改成九层塔……@*</p>
