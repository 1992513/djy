<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>玄彬访台会粉丝心情好 朴勋盼能在台湾拍戏</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415752-250117053538100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>朴勋、玄彬为韩国电影《哈尔滨》访台宣传。（网银国际影视提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年01月17日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）韩国战争片《哈尔滨》将于1月29日在台湾大银幕上映，15日导演禹民镐率领玄彬、朴勋、制片金元国访台宣传，并一连举办两场特映影人见面场，开放1,200席，在开卖后迅速完售。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415753" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415753-250117053555100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415753" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415753-250117053555100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415753" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）制片金元国、朴勋、玄彬、导演禹民镐、主持人吴姗儒畅聊韩国电影《哈尔滨》。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14415755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415755-250117053559100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415755" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415755-250117053559100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415755" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《哈尔滨》特映影人见面场照。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>见面会活动上，制片金元国、朴勋、玄彬、导演禹民镐一出场就引发全场观众尖叫，现场大展亲民魅力，不但手比爱心、更可爱摆出Fighting动作为现场所有粉丝们应援，将韩国宣传等级的宠粉互动带来台湾，让粉丝们直呼暖心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415756" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415756-250117053550100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415756" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415756-250117053550100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415756" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）制片金元国、朴勋、玄彬、导演禹民镐为韩国电影《哈尔滨》访台宣传。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14415754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415754-250117053602100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415754" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415754-250117053602100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415754" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）制片金元国、朴勋、玄彬、导演禹民镐为韩国电影《哈尔滨》访台宣传。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看到满场热情观众，玄彬感动表示：“很久没到访台湾，今天真的很开心，能和导演禹民镐、朴勋、制片金元国一起带着新作品来和大家见面。”他也希望透过电影让观众理解这位英雄人物与韩国独立军的奋斗历程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415758" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415758-250117053533100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415758" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415758-250117053533100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415758" class="wp-caption-text">玄彬为韩国电影《哈尔滨》访台宣传。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>玄彬真诚地说：“能带着这么有意义的作品来和大家见面，对我来说是非常充满纪念性的一刻。”朴勋则说：“非常荣幸和大家一起来台湾宣传，既然这次来到台湾，也希望有机会在台湾拍戏。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415759" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415759-250117053543100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415759" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415759-250117053543100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415759" class="wp-caption-text">朴勋为韩国电影《哈尔滨》访台宣传。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导演禹民镐、玄彬、朴勋、制片金元国更亲自抽出幸运儿，赠送韩国空运来台的限量礼品，再度引起全场轰动。看到热情的台湾粉丝，导演禹民镐深受感动，特别感谢大家给予满满欢呼声，玄彬则说：“时隔许久，再次来台和大家见面，心情很好！就像导演说的，大家给予我们热情和应援，希望未来有更多日子和大家见面。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415757-250117053540100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415757" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415757-250117053540100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415757" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）制片金元国、朴勋、玄彬、导演禹民镐为韩国电影《哈尔滨》访台宣传。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他更特别用中文传递感谢，朴勋也跟着用中文说“谢谢台湾”，制片金元国则分享在韩国自去年12月24日上映后至今都蝉联票房冠军，希望在台湾上映后也能称霸第一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415760" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415760-250117053545100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415760" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415760-250117053545100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415760" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）制片金元国、朴勋、玄彬、导演禹民镐为韩国电影《哈尔滨》访台宣传。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>电影《哈尔滨》将于2025年1月29日在台湾上映。</p>
