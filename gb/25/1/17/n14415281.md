<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>情真意切的冰雪祝福</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415287-60C87763AA16FF6967CA51ED19D504F66014097D-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>(甘斯维尔台湾商会)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年01月17日讯】第38届北美洲台湾商会联合总会总会长候选人白越珠博士原订于1/11/2025上午搭机来到乔治亚州，没想到迎来一场大雪，顺延于1/12/2025上午抵达。甘斯维尔台湾商会林会长恩如、刘副会长绍鹏一同前至机场，欢迎远从华盛顿DC白博士伉俪不畏冰雪莅临乔治亚州。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415290" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415290-3F56FC136726A9344A6002385520C1A42E971EE8-e1737072387659.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14415290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415290-3F56FC136726A9344A6002385520C1A42E971EE8-e1737072387659.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415290" class="wp-caption-text">(甘斯维尔台湾商会)</figcaption></figure>
<p>为白博士伉俪与甘斯维尔台湾商会理事眷属拥有美好交流时光，该会林会长恩如特别安排当天上午11点30分假金冠酒楼豪华北京厅，一起享用精致高档午宴餐点，彼此分享联络情谊。计有侨务咨询委员吕志全、侨务促进委员萧柏恩、朱佳欣；名誉会长王立纲、郝慰国、汪瀛、谷企平、孔繁华；副会长刘绍鹏、秘书长陈雯琳等理事眷属近30人参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415293" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415293-2D3FBB06504FC02D7D800D279C556A35121CE7D0-e1737072473294.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14415293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415293-2D3FBB06504FC02D7D800D279C556A35121CE7D0-e1737072473294.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415293" class="wp-caption-text">(甘斯维尔台湾商会)</figcaption></figure>
<p>席中，白博士特别赠送林会长恩如一份台湾造型精美瓷盘，盘子上方绘制一朵兰花，典雅大方，典藏限量版，是心坎儿最珍贵的真情意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415296" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415296-0AFCA4AE964F056EC139EDFD14638519145DC2AB-e1737072564284.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14415296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415296-0AFCA4AE964F056EC139EDFD14638519145DC2AB-e1737072564284.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415296" class="wp-caption-text">(甘斯维尔台湾商会)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14415297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415297" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415297-7150D2D4D672C7695258B06DDC0657D7F47ED3C4-e1737072640461.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14415297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415297-7150D2D4D672C7695258B06DDC0657D7F47ED3C4-e1737072640461.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415297" class="wp-caption-text">(甘斯维尔台湾商会)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14415299" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415299" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415299-A8528892DA80D42FA8576442616ED29267CFD88C-e1737072721166.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14415299" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415299-A8528892DA80D42FA8576442616ED29267CFD88C-e1737072721166.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415299" class="wp-caption-text">(甘斯维尔台湾商会)</figcaption></figure>
<p>同时理事眷属热情呼喊“冻蒜”，祝福白博士高票当选第38届北美洲台湾商会联合总会总会长，大家依依不舍，相约6月德州会议再相见。（世华会供稿）◇</p>
