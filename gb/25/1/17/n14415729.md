<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉县第四届青创奖励补助开跑 最高补助100万</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415730-739611-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>翁章梁县长现场体验手工皮件制作。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月17日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义县政府持续协助青年创业，今(17)日举办“第四届青年创新创业奖励补助说明暨记者会”，鼓励青年运用本身专长结合地方特色，打造具市场价值的创新产品或服务。县长翁章梁、县议员刘雅雯、江志明、姜梅红、陈文忠等人也莅临勉励创业青年持续精进提升自我价值，让自己的专长与热爱，成就一番事业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415731-739612.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415731-739612-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415731" class="wp-caption-text">嘉县第四届青创奖励补助开跑。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>计划延续去年，提供每组团队最高100万元的创业补助，更于今年度起加码补助青创贷款前三年全额利息补贴的优惠措施，大幅降低青年创业的资金门槛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415732-739613.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415732-739613-600x322.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415732" class="wp-caption-text">贵宾手举牌大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场邀请上一届获创业补助的“学长”Coblank（古果有限公司）负责人商峻恺，带领县长体验手作皮件。商峻恺分享自己从意大利学成归国后，因为家人而决定回家乡中埔创业，所有的产品都是自己一个人生产，目前以接受订制为主，由商峻恺提供皮件给客户参考，再依客户的需求做修改，目前在家边有一间个人工作室。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415733-739614.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415733-739614-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415733" class="wp-caption-text">媒体联访。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁分享，嘉义县过去已经成功辅导33个团队，劳青处提供奖励补助计划，协助解决创业初期最困扰的资金问题，希望创业青年，在过程中能摸索出市场的逻辑规律，透过专案辅导，迈向成功，由县府为各位缓解资金的问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415734-739615.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415734-739615-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415734" class="wp-caption-text">刘雅文议员体验皮件制作。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>劳青处长陈奕翰进一步说明，第四届相关征件资讯已公告，补助计划申请受理至114年2月21日止，欢迎创业青年把握机会、踊跃参加！另外，获本县青创贷款补助者，青创贷款利息补贴采每半年申请一次，应于每年4月5日及10月5日前，检具申请书、存折封面影本及缴交贷款利息收据正本等资料，主动向专案办公室申请。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415735" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415735-739616.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415735-739616-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415735" class="wp-caption-text">参与征件说明的学员。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>相关资讯可至“嘉义县政府劳工暨青年发展处官网-最新消息-公告资讯”(https://ly.cyhg.gov.tw/News_Content.aspx?n=2204&amp;s=243824)查询，或电洽专案办公室“社团法人中华创业育成协会”专线电话05-2780553伍小姐（青创奖励补助）或05-2780677姚小姐（青创贷款利息补贴）咨询。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14415736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14415736" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415736-739617.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14415736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14415736-739617-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14415736" class="wp-caption-text">现场邀请上一届获创业补助的“学长”Coblank（古果有限公司）负责人商峻恺，带领县长体验手作皮件。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
