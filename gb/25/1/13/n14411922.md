<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>画笔下的土耳其 蔡庆彰 异国速写之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411923-735117-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>旅程的第一站是番红花城，这座被列为世界文化遗产的古城。（蔡庆彰提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月13日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台中报导）教育界退休的蔡庆彰，是台中知名的水彩画家，以独特的速写风格，捕捉异国风情的瞬间美感。他的作品总是融合精细技术与深厚情感，使观者仿若身临其境。</p>
<figure id="14411937" aria-describedby="caption-14411937" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411937-735130-450x307.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411937-735130-450x307.jpg" alt="特洛伊遗址。" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411937" class="wp-caption-text">特洛伊遗址。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次，他的画笔停留在横跨欧亚大陆的土耳其，展开一场文化与自然的探索旅程。</p>
<h2>番红花城 木构之美</h2>
<p>旅程的第一站是番红花城（Safranbolu），这座被列为世界文化遗产的古城，以其鄂图曼风格的木构建筑而闻名。蔡庆彰的速写，忠实再现了木造楼房的细致结构与质感，甚至透过上下楼梯的震动感，让观者体验当地人居住的真实生活。特别是希德尔立克山丘的视野，被他用宽阔的笔触展现成充满古典美的画面，将整座山城的古香古色一览无遗。</p>
<h2>历史与现代交织 安卡拉</h2>
<figure id="14411925" aria-describedby="caption-14411925" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411925-735119-450x303.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411925-735119-450x303.jpg" alt="安纳托利亚文明博物馆。" width="599" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411925" class="wp-caption-text">安纳托利亚文明博物馆。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安卡拉城堡和文明博物馆展现了土耳其的历史深度。蔡庆彰：“以柔和色调捕捉了博物馆的外观，特别是其典型的拱门结构与石墙，体现了中世纪建筑的独特风韵。”同时，他的速写还巧妙融入了安卡拉的现代城市全景，将古老与现代的交融描绘得淋漓尽致。</p>
<figure id="14411938" aria-describedby="caption-14411938" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411938-735131-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411938-735131-450x300.jpg" alt="博斯普鲁斯海峡。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411938" class="wp-caption-text">博斯普鲁斯海峡。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>卡帕多其亚 奇幻地景</h2>
<figure id="14411928" aria-describedby="caption-14411928" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411928-735122-450x304.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411928-735122-450x304.jpg" alt="卡帕多奇亚怪石。" width="601" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411928" class="wp-caption-text">卡帕多奇亚怪石。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到卡帕多其亚，蔡庆彰用画笔展现了这片经过风化，与水蚀而成的奇岩怪石。他的速写忠实再现了凯马克利地下城那种令人震撼的结构，他说：“层层叠叠的洞窟设计显得既神秘又实用，完美展示了古代文明的创造力。”</p>
<figure id="14411932" aria-describedby="caption-14411932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411932-735125-450x306.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411932-735125-450x306.jpg" alt="蔡庆彰第一次搭乘热气球。" width="600" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411932" class="wp-caption-text">蔡庆彰第一次搭乘热气球。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，他还描绘了清晨搭乘热气球的壮丽景色，画面中的奇岩高低起伏，如同自然的艺术品，令人屏息。</p>
<h2>棉堡 温泉诗意</h2>
<figure id="14411933" aria-describedby="caption-14411933" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411933-735126-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411933-735126-450x299.jpg" alt="巴慕卡丽棉堡。" width="601" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411933" class="wp-caption-text">巴慕卡丽棉堡。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14411936" aria-describedby="caption-14411936" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411936-735129-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411936-735129-450x300.jpg" alt="棉花堡奇景。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411936" class="wp-caption-text">棉花堡奇景。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>棉堡（Pamukkale）那如白色瀑布般的梯田式景观，在蔡庆彰的画中，化为柔美的线条与淡雅的色彩。画中的温泉水缓缓流淌，营造出宁静的氛围，仿佛能让人感受到泉水的温暖，与柔和的石灰岩触感。</p>
<h2>以弗所 古希腊辉煌</h2>
<figure id="14411926" aria-describedby="caption-14411926" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411926-735120-450x308.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411926-735120-450x308.jpg" alt="希拉波里斯古城。" width="601" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411926" class="wp-caption-text">希拉波里斯古城。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14411927" aria-describedby="caption-14411927" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411927-735121-450x312.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411927-735121-450x312.jpg" alt="果里美露天博物馆" width="601" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411927" class="wp-caption-text">果里美露天博物馆（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在以弗所，蔡庆彰选择了图书馆，与露天剧场作为主要描绘对象。作品中，斑驳的石墙与拱门见证了古希腊文明的辉煌，他以细腻的笔触呈现出这座城市的庄严与宏伟，让人仿佛回到了千年前的历史现场。</p>
<h2>伊斯坦堡 蓝色清真寺</h2>
<figure id="14411924" aria-describedby="caption-14411924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411924-735118-450x302.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411924-735118-450x302.jpg" alt="伊斯坦堡的蓝色清真寺。" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411924" class="wp-caption-text">伊斯坦堡的蓝色清真寺。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为压轴，伊斯坦堡的蓝色清真寺，以其磅礴的气势成为蔡庆彰画中最耀眼的一景。蔡庆彰：“我使用鲜明的蓝色与金黄色调对比，突出清真寺的壮丽与神圣。”同时，他也捕捉了周围街头的生活气息，从街边咖啡馆到市场的小贩，将伊斯坦堡多元的文化魅力融入画中。</p>
<figure id="14411929" aria-describedby="caption-14411929" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411929-735123-450x303.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411929-735123-450x303.jpg" alt="七百年木构民宿。" width="602" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411929" class="wp-caption-text">七百年木构民宿。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14411934" aria-describedby="caption-14411934" style="width: 604px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411934-735127-450x304.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411934-735127-450x304.jpg" alt="洞穴餐厅。" width="604" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411934" class="wp-caption-text">洞穴餐厅。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14411935" aria-describedby="caption-14411935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411935-735128-450x307.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411935-735128-450x307.jpg" alt="土耳其冰淇淋。" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411935" class="wp-caption-text">土耳其冰淇淋。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14411931" aria-describedby="caption-14411931" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411931-735124-450x320.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411931-735124-450x320.jpg" alt="土耳其沙威玛。" width="601" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411931" class="wp-caption-text">土耳其沙威玛。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蔡庆彰透过这次土耳其之行，将这片横跨欧亚的土地以水彩与速写的形式，展现在世人面前。他的画笔记录的不仅是建筑与景观，更是一部充满知性与情感的文化纪录，激发人们探索这片土地的渴望。</p>
<figure id="14411939" aria-describedby="caption-14411939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411939-735132-450x311.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411939-735132-450x311.jpg" alt="伊斯坦堡：托普卡比皇宫。" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14411939" class="wp-caption-text">伊斯坦堡：托普卡比皇宫。（蔡庆彰提供）</figcaption></figure>
