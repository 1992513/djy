<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>颂乐为台湾粉丝学中文 对蝉联访台冠军有野心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412275-250112231815100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar's TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年01月13日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐（金容仙）12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》，与2,500 名粉丝共度欢乐时光。除了为了粉丝努力学中文，身为去年最频繁造访台湾的K-POP明星之一的颂乐还说，她有想要蝉联最频繁访台的韩国歌手的野心。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14412294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412294" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412294-250112231839100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412294" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412294-250112231839100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412294" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure>出道逾十年的颂乐这次挑战截然不同的TALK CONCERT的形式演出，手持厚厚一本中文稿，与粉丝对话时大量运用中文，也特地将一部分韩文歌翻成中文献给粉丝。她表示为此真的每天学习中文，甚至在昨天的飞机上都没有休息，只为带给台湾“容顺（粉丝名）”的更亲切、更专属的演出。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14412299" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412299" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412299-250112231819100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412299" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412299-250112231819100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412299" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_14412289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412289-250112231855100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412289" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412289-250112231855100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412289" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure>颂乐在舞台上以自己姓名“金容仙”延伸成语谐音，亲笔挥洒中文“画容点金”、“仙驰得点”，2025年的开始就把满满的祝福送给粉丝，更大手笔准备了30项礼物，有颂乐私下练习服装、亲笔签名杯、小时候照片及巨大瑜珈球等，甚至还有她吃完美食后的空气，逗乐粉丝。最大礼送出韩国来回机票、Apple Watch，让获奖的粉丝又惊又喜，但也有人替抽到弹跳床与巨大瑜珈球这类大型礼物的粉丝担忧“该怎么带回家”。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14412283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412283-250112231858100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412283" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412283-250112231858100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412283" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure>活动期间，颂乐也努力以中文和粉丝互动。她说她连坐飞机的时间都在学习中文，并说下次再访台湾时会以更好的面貌来与大家见面，还问大家她的发音好不好。虽然台下粉丝回应她“好”，但她用中文回复“但是你为什么听不懂？”，引来台下笑声。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14412291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412291-250112231845100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412291" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412291-250112231845100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412291" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure>看到粉丝持续举着手的颂乐，便用中文对举手的粉丝说“你有问题吗？”，这让台下粉丝大笑出声，而颂乐对此感到困惑。在经过翻译解释这句话的意思后，颂乐也笑了出来，并按照粉丝教她的，跟着说“你有想问的问题吗？”，但颂乐又说“很长！”并再次问那位粉丝“你有问题吗？”，还为了照顾粉丝心情而补充“啊…然后…你没问题”，再度让大家笑成一片。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14412293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412293-250112231806100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412293" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412293-250112231806100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412293" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure>去年才刚赢得“*台湾灶咖（台语：厨房）大赛”（*注：指频繁造访台湾的次数名列K-POP明星冠军）的MAMAMOO，新的一年才开始队长颂乐就立刻来到台湾，现场粉丝也大喊是否又要准备称霸2025年的赛事，颂乐回答：“我觉得…好，我觉得可以，谢谢。”</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14412296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412296-250112231848100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412296" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412296-250112231848100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412296" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure>不仅如此，颂乐还说：“其他成员们也是一样，每次来台北都觉得心情很轻松，而且我现在有在学中文，变得更有野心（成为常来台湾的K-POP明星冠军）了，敬请期待。”这番话也让台下粉丝开心地欢呼。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14412306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412306-250112231852100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412306" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412306-250112231852100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412306" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure>颂乐表示自己出道超过十年，意味着有许多粉丝喜欢她了十年，真的非常感谢与感动。活动最后谈到许愿券时，颂乐感性地表示自己有个愿望，便用韩文说：“希望大家能够把今天共度的这段回忆收藏在心里”，还中文说“请答应我”，接着用韩文补充“我也会记得与珍藏今天的这瞬间”。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14412302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412302-250112231842100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412302" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412302-250112231842100707-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412302" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气女团MAMAMOO队长颂乐12日在Legacy TERA举办《Solar\&#8217;s TALK CONCERT [Topic Check] in TAIPEI》演出照。（希林国际Chillin International提供）</figcaption></figure>最后，颂乐用中文说“新年快乐，我叫颂乐，真心感谢大家来见我！”为活动画下句点。</p>
