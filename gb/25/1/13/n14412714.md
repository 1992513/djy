<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中医师推荐年货中药材 为年菜增添滋补功效</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412718-725669eaa44857bebf6de08cc02ecb9c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>传统年节中，华人购买中药材用于年菜、煲汤、泡茶等，为传统团圆餐增添滋补功效。图为补中益气牛肉汤。（马光医疗网提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><b>编者按：</b>很快就要过年了。做年菜时，如果能<span style="font-weight: 400;">加入一些中药材来增加滋补功效，那就更完美了。本文将介绍一些常见中药材的养生功效、烹调方式和挑选诀窍，助您做出美味又健康的佳肴。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>补气养血类中药材</b></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.黄耆</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：黄耆素有“补气之王”之称，适合气虚体质者；能补气固表、利水消肿，并提高免疫力。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">常用于煲汤，如黄耆鸡汤，或搭配红枣和枸杞煮茶。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">挑选表皮光滑、切片均匀、香气浓郁的黄耆。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.党参</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：党参是补中益气的佳品，有助于健脾益肺、生津养血。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">用于炖汤，如党参炖猪骨汤，也可搭配黄耆使用。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">选购时，需注意根条均匀，质地坚硬，无虫蛀或断裂。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.红枣</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：红枣具有养血安神的作用。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">红枣与枸杞一起煮茶，能改善用眼过度所带来的不适，预防眼睛老化；或加入煲汤以增添甜味。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">选择表皮光滑、颜色鲜红、无虫蛀的品种。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.枸杞</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：枸杞因养肝明目的功效而广受欢迎。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">用途广泛，可泡水饮用，也能加入肉类或汤品中，以增添甘甜味。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">挑选时，以颗粒饱满、颜色红亮、不沾手为佳。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>5.山药</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：山药有健脾益胃、补肾固精的作用。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">煲汤、煮粥的常见材料。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">选购色白、片薄且无霉斑者。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14412717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412717" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412717-84e3b49dd9ed34336929a7b09da59e1d.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14412717" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412717-84e3b49dd9ed34336929a7b09da59e1d-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412717" class="wp-caption-text">年货必备中药材2。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>滋阴润燥类中药材</b></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.当归</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：当归以补血活血、调经止痛著称，是女性进补的良品。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">常用于炖鸡汤或煲汤（如当归鸭汤）。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">采买时，应选择味道浓郁、质地坚实的当归。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.北沙参</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：北沙参具有滋阴润肺、益胃生津的功效，适合在干燥季节饮用。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">搭配百合、枸杞煲汤饮用。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">购买条形粗细均匀、色泽白或微黄的品种。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.百合</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：百合可润肺止咳、清心安神。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">煮甜汤的理想食材，如百合莲子汤。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">挑选时，以片状完整、色泽洁白、无杂质的百合为佳。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>安神助眠类中药材</b></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.莲子</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：莲子具有健脾养心、宁心安神的效果。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于糖水或汤品，如莲子百合汤。</span></p>
<p><b>挑选诀窍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">选购颗粒饱满、颜色白或微黄、无裂痕的莲子。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.桂圆（龙眼干）</b></span></h3>
<p><b>功效</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：桂圆有补气血、安神益脑的作用。</span></p>
<p><b>烹饪方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">适合与红枣一起泡茶或煮甜汤。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">挑选诀窍：采买时，应挑选外皮干燥无裂缝、果肉色泽金黄的桂圆。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14412719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412719" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412719-a2aec79dd66d2aaa9a3efed1c23ffc12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14412719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412719-a2aec79dd66d2aaa9a3efed1c23ffc12-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412719" class="wp-caption-text">年货必备中药材1。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>中医师推荐两道养生食谱</b></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.补中益气牛肉汤</b></span></h3>
<p><b>材料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">牛肉300克、当归5克、黄耆10克、生姜3片、山药100克、清水1000毫升、盐适量、米酒1汤匙</span></p>
<p><b>步骤：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.将牛肉切块，汆烫去除血水，捞出沥干，备用。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.把山药去皮切块，放入清水中浸泡，防止氧化变黑。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.稍微冲洗一下当归和黄耆，沥干备用。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.锅中添加清水，放入牛肉、生姜、当归和黄耆。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.开大火煮沸后，转小火慢慢熬煮约1小时，让药材的精华充分释放。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">6.将切好的山药块加入汤中，继续用小火煮20分钟，直到山药软烂。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.加点盐和米酒调味，再煮5分钟即可关火。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14412718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412718" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412718-725669eaa44857bebf6de08cc02ecb9c.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14412718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412718-725669eaa44857bebf6de08cc02ecb9c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412718" class="wp-caption-text">补中益气牛肉汤。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.养肝护眼茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>材料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">决明子18克、杭菊3.6克、枸杞24克、水3000毫升</span></p>
<p><b>步骤：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">将材料放入锅中，倒入3000毫升水，以小火煮10分钟即可。</span></p>
<p><b>饮用方式：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">冷热不拘，随时可以喝。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14412720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412720" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412720-160966c2da10a664f2dbb1424e5d40a6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14412720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412720-160966c2da10a664f2dbb1424e5d40a6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412720" class="wp-caption-text">养肝护眼茶。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><b>?</b>选择干燥、无霉变、气味正常的优质品。<strong>◎<em>◇</em></strong></span></p>
