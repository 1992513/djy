<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠狮子会年会 伍国光和余丽媖获最高奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412756-DSC00095-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>1月11日，波士顿华埠狮子会举办年度筹款晚宴，到场的会员们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年01月14日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）1月11日晚上，波士顿华埠狮子会成员及家属总计100余人欢歌饮宴，为新一年的公益目标筹款。大家还庆祝伍国光和余丽媖获得“茂文钟士奖”（Melvin Jones Fellowship Award），其中余丽媖已是第三次得到这个国际狮子会最高贡献奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14412757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412757-DSC00101.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412757-DSC00101-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412757" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿华埠狮子会举办年度筹款晚宴，参与的各地狮子会职员及家属合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚在华埠新月宫酒家，国际狮子会麻州33K地区会长福斯特（Diana Faust）称赞大家做了卓越的工作。在热烈的掌声和欢呼声中，她为华埠狮子会秘书伍国光（Victor Ng），以及财务余丽媖（Rosemary Yee）颁发国际狮子会最高荣誉“茂文钟士奖”，并帮他们戴上徽章。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">伍国光曾于2014年担任华埠狮子会会长，长年参加狮子会的各项公益活动，担任节目主持、义工等。余丽媖已是第3次获得“茂文钟士奖”，她长期担任华埠狮子会的财务官，为公益事业投入大量时间。这两位资深会员，都因为他们投入服务的时长，而得以名列国际狮子会的荣誉墙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14412752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412752" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412752-DSC00074.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412752-DSC00074-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412752" class="wp-caption-text">伍国光（右）获得“茂文钟士奖”，地区会长福斯特（Diana Faust）颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14412753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412753" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412753-DSC00080.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412753-DSC00080-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412753" class="wp-caption-text">余丽媖（右）获得“茂文钟士奖”，地区会长福斯特（Diana Faust）颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">福斯特还颁发了3份嘉奖资深会员的“雪弗龙奖”（Chevron Award），感谢服务了15年的陈国航（Ricky Chan）和丁慧明（Bonnie Kwok），服务20年的林淑明（Ada Lam），以及服务25年的李源沛（Eddie Lee）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14412748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412748-DSC00050.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412748-DSC00050-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412748" class="wp-caption-text">会长陈国航欢迎来宾。他参与服务15年，获得了“雪弗龙奖”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14412749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412749" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412749-DSC00060.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412749-DSC00060-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412749" class="wp-caption-text">林淑明（左）因服务20年而获奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14412750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412750" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412750-DSC00066.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412750-DSC00066-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412750" class="wp-caption-text">李源沛（右）因服务25年而获奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>公益晚宴</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>气氛欢乐</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚的宴会由伍国光和周丽玲主持，依照传统由“四部分”开场：蔡倩婷领唱《美国第一章歌》（First Verse of America）；阮鸿灿带领宣读“效忠誓词”；伍国光带领大家祈祷；最后约110位来宾手拉手，共同诵念“狮子祝词”：“我不在你之上，我不在你之下，但我们一齐努力。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14412744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412744" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412744-DSC00038.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412744-DSC00038-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412744" class="wp-caption-text">周丽玲（左）和伍国光主持晚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14412746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412746-DSC00043.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412746-DSC00043-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412746" class="wp-caption-text">蔡倩婷领唱《美国第一章歌》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14412747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412747-DSC00046.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412747-DSC00046-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412747" class="wp-caption-text">会员们诵念“狮子祝词”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">国际狮子会（LCIF）大使李源沛介绍了晚宴嘉宾，除了华埠狮子会之外，还有麻州多个地区的狮子会职员、会员及家属，比如摩顿（Malden）、弗莱明翰（Framingham）等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">接下来，会长陈国航代表主办方欢迎来宾，感谢大家冒着寒冷天气前来支持，带来温暖的气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14412745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412745-DSC00040.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412745-DSC00040-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412745" class="wp-caption-text">上百位来宾参加华埠狮子会晚宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14412751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412751" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412751-DSC00069.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412751-DSC00069-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412751" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿华埠狮子会会旗。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">晚宴期间，狮子会还为33K分区“狮子国际和平海报”创作活动的优胜者，6年级学生Sueyi Wang和她的老师Lynn Ma颁奖。这名11岁的女生领奖时落落大方，介绍自己的年龄和学校。33K地区会长福斯特说，很高兴为她颁奖，还邀请她参与未来的狮子会大会。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">颁奖环节过后，在场的各地狮子会成员上台合影。会长陈国航在娱乐节目中热情献唱多首歌曲，华埠狮子会职员们还合唱《朋友》，现场气氛非常欢乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14412754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412754-DSC00086.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412754-DSC00086-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412754" class="wp-caption-text">Sueyi Wang和她的老师Lynn Ma获得“狮子国际和平海报”创作优胜奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14412758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14412758" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412758-DSC00108.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14412758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14412758-DSC00108-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14412758" class="wp-caption-text">狮子会会员们合唱《朋友》，气氛欢乐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">波士顿华埠狮子会成立于1987年，创会会长是司徒彦铿（Henry Seto）。华埠狮子会的其中一项特色服务，是他们拥有一辆视力健康移动站，每年都会在一些社区活动中为人们免费检查眼睛。◇</p>
