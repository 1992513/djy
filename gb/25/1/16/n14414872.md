<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>寒梅点点情深意远 拜访蔡家梅园与陈家梅园</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414873-739330-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>摄影家吴淑珠用她敏锐的视角，捕捉到每一朵寒梅的坚韧与美丽。（吴淑珠提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾南投报导）1月15日雨后的南投信义乡的蔡家梅园，仿佛披上一层柔光，摄影家吴淑珠用她敏锐的视角，捕捉到每一朵寒梅的坚韧与美丽。无论是古厝前静静绽放的梅花，还是水滴垂落枝头的瞬间，她的镜头仿佛在低声诉说，这些寒冬的诗意与乡村的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414883-739342.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414883-739342-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414883" class="wp-caption-text">在蔡家梅园，古厝门楣上的红联与白色梅花相映成趣。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>蔡家梅园：古厝与梅树的对话</h2>
<p>在蔡家梅园，古厝门楣上的红联与白色梅花相映成趣，形成浓厚的乡土气息。一名女子穿着白色长裙，坐在古厝门口，身后梅花如云般铺展。吴淑珠在此捕捉到一幅宛如画作的画面，柔和的光线让照片散发着安静与温暖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414874" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414874-739331.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414874-739331-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414874" class="wp-caption-text">在蔡家梅园，古厝门楣上的红联与白色梅花相映成趣。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14414882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414882-739340.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414882-739340-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414882" class="wp-caption-text">一幅宛如画作的画面，柔和的光线让照片散发着安静与温暖。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴淑珠的作品擅长运用自然光。她解释：“在雨后拍梅花，光线虽然不足，但这样的湿润感和花瓣上的水珠能让作品更具细腻情感。”透过低角度拍摄，她刻意让梅枝占满画幅，突出花与人的对话，让观者仿佛置身于其中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414881" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414881-739339.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414881-739339-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414881" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑珠的作品擅长运用自然光。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>蔡家梅园：倒影中的梦幻美景</h2>
<p>蔡家梅园水池中的倒影成为拍摄的重点。吴淑珠分享：“梅花与水相互映衬，形成独特的画面。”她以慢速快门拍摄，捕捉水面微波轻漾的瞬间，营造出如梦似幻的意境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414875-739333.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414875-739333-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414875" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑珠分享：“梅花与水相互映衬，形成独特的画面。”（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石头步道蜿蜒穿过梅林，吴淑珠在步道上摆放红灯笼作为前景，增添画面的深度与趣味性。画面中，满树白花与红色灯笼形成鲜明对比，构图简洁而耐人寻味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414876-739334.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414876-739334-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414876" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑珠在步道上摆放红灯笼作为前景，增添画面的深度与趣味性。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>摄影背后的思考：老农与寒梅的共鸣</h2>
<p>来到牛稠坑陈家梅园，吴淑珠不仅用相机记录梅花的美，也在作品中融入对土地与农村的情感。她感叹：“寒梅虽然坚韧，但每年盛开的背后，是老农们一年辛勤的付出，就像这些梅花在寒冬里孤单绽放，却蕴藏着丰收的希望。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414877" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414877-739335.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414877-739335-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414877" class="wp-caption-text">牛稠坑陈家梅园。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在她拍下的一张作品中，梅花轻轻垂下，枝头挂着几滴晶莹的水珠，背后是简陋的木门，让人想起农村生活的质朴与辛劳。这张照片被她称为《老农的辛酸》，她以特写的方式呈现细节，强化了视觉的张力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414878-739336.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414878-739336-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414878" class="wp-caption-text">梅花轻轻垂下，枝头挂着几滴晶莹的水珠。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>摄影技巧分享：在雨后捕捉细节</h2>
<p>吴淑珠特别强调雨后拍摄的技巧：“雨滴能为梅花增添立体感，而湿润的地面则让光线更加柔和。”她建议使用广角镜头结合小光圈，让前后景深更清晰，并适当调高ISO以克服低光环境。此外，她也喜欢使用三脚架，拍出稳定的慢速快门作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414879-739337.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414879-739337-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414879" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑珠特别强调雨后拍摄的技巧：“雨滴能为梅花增添立体感，而湿润的地面则让光线更加柔和。”（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>让每一张照片都诉说故事</h2>
<p>透过吴淑珠的镜头，蔡家梅园和牛稠坑陈家古宅的寒梅不仅是视觉上的享受，更是她情感与思想的延伸。正如她所说：“摄影不仅是按下快门，而是透过画面让人感受到背后的故事。”她的作品激励着更多人，去发现身边的美，珍惜每一个当下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14414880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414880-739338.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14414880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414880-739338-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414880" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑珠说：“摄影不仅是按下快门，而是透过画面让人感受到背后的故事。”（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
