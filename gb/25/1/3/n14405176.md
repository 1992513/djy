<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>山中威尼斯——双溪庄贡生古厝的守望与传承</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405177-736244-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>双溪庄贡生古厝，拥有二百年历史了。（Kimball Wen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月03日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新北市报导）记忆的竹径与遗世的静谧，双溪的山间，一条蜿蜒小径通向一片竹林。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14405183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14405183" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405183-736250.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14405183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405183-736250.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14405183" class="wp-caption-text">非常美的竹林。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>浓密翠绿的竹叶掩映着岁月的痕迹，也守护着一座拥有两百年历史的古厝——“庄贡生古厝”。这座古厝宛若静静矗立的时光机器，带着故事的重量，等待着造访者与它重逢，品味其中深藏的历史与人情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14405178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14405178" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405178-736245.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14405178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405178-736245.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14405178" class="wp-caption-text">Kimball Wen：“庄廷灿，这座古厝最著名的主人，于1901年中贡生后返乡。”（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>雕工简朴 传递吉祥的石雕艺术</strong></p>
<p>摄影家Kimball Wen：“已有200年历史的古厝，它的门面以‘凹寿门’造型呈现，左右护龙的结构，将岁月凝聚在其间。墙壁上那些素雅的石雕，无不透露出一份朴实无华的美感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14405185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14405185" style="width: 604px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405185-736252.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14405185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405185-736252.jpg" alt="" width="604" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14405185" class="wp-caption-text">在物换星移的世代里，这片古老的砖瓦仍在诉说着不朽的故事。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以‘喜上眉梢’、‘花开富贵’为题的雕刻，表达了早期农村对吉祥如意的渴望。”透过雕工线条流畅的纹路，仿佛能感受到匠人用心雕琢时的虔敬与专注。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14405179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14405179" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405179-736246.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14405179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405179-736246.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14405179" class="wp-caption-text">以“喜上眉梢”“花开富贵”为题的雕刻，表达了早期农村对吉祥如意的渴望。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>文化的传承——贡生的教育梦</strong></p>
<p>摄影家Kimball Wen：“庄廷灿，这座古厝最著名的主人，于1901年中贡生后返乡。他与当地举人连旭桩共同创立了汉学私塾‘梅竹园’，教育当地的年轻一辈，为家乡培育了许多文才济世之士。”这段历史不仅是古厝的荣耀，也是双溪地区文化的缩影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14405187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14405187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405187-736267.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14405187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405187-736267.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14405187" class="wp-caption-text">“梅竹园”教育当地的年轻一辈，为家乡培育了许多文才济世之士。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>现代与过去的交汇</strong></p>
<p>如今，庄贡生古厝依然有第五代子孙居住，让这片时光的土地延续着家庭的温度。走进这里，便能见到一块白底蓝字的介绍牌，邀请游客一同回溯古厝的历史与意义。细读这段文字，不禁令人感慨——在物换星移的世代里，这片古老的砖瓦仍在诉说着不朽的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14405180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14405180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405180-736247.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14405180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405180-736247.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14405180" class="wp-caption-text">庄贡生古厝依然有第五代子孙居住，让这片时光的土地延续着家庭的温度。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>惜福与感恩——与历史的对话</strong></p>
<p>站在古厝的庭院中，微风轻拂，竹影婆娑，仿佛耳边响起百年前的朗朗书声。这份遗世的静谧教人懂得珍惜当下，感恩过往。摄影家Kimball Wen的镜头捕捉了这座古厝的每个角落，用影像带领我们走入历史的深处，也让我们省思：如何在快速流动的现代生活中，守住这份珍贵的文化资产？</p>
<figure id="attachment_14405186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14405186" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405186-736266.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14405186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405186-736266.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14405186" class="wp-caption-text">摄影家Kimball Wen的镜头捕捉了这座古厝的每个角落。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>保存的意义与未来的期望</strong></p>
<p>庄贡生古厝的存在，如同一颗深埋土中的种子，孕育着时间的智慧与生命的韧性。它见证了过往，也承载着未来。我们不仅是它的观者，更是它的守护者。让我们珍惜这份历史的馈赠，让它的故事，随着时间继续延续下去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14405181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14405181" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405181-736248.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14405181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14405181-736248.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14405181" class="wp-caption-text">珍惜这份历史的馈赠，让它的故事，随着时间继续延续下去。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
