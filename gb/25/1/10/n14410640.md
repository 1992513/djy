<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进童话般的哈尔施塔特小镇  探索欧洲最美小镇</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410641-737994-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Nina提到，“漫步小镇，久违的阳光洒下，眼前一幕幕的风景画，人少悠闲。”（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬奥地利报导）哈尔施塔特（Hallstatt）被誉为“欧洲最美小镇”，这个位于奥地利上奥地利州的湖畔小镇，拥有得天独厚的自然风光与浓厚的文化底蕴。远眺达赫斯坦山脉（Dachstein），静谧的哈尔施塔特湖（Hallst?tter See）倒映出群山的雄伟与天际的蔚蓝，而岸边色彩斑斓的中世纪建筑则点缀其间，令人仿佛置身于童话故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410642" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410642-737995.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410642-737995-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410642" class="wp-caption-text">岸边色彩斑斓的中世纪建筑则点缀其间，令人仿佛置身于童话故事。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次，空拍达人Nina的冬日探访，从旅途安排到小镇漫步，为我们带来独特的视角与感受。让我们从她的视野出发，细细品味这座小镇的历史、人文与自然之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410643" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410643-737996.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410643-737996-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410643" class="wp-caption-text">空拍达人Nina的冬日探访，从旅途安排到小镇漫步，为我们带来独特的视角与感受。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>历史底蕴 – 来自远古的盐矿文化</h2>
<p>哈尔施塔特以其丰富的盐矿资源闻名，自公元前8世纪起便有人类居住。盐，被誉为“白色黄金”，为这座小镇带来了千年的繁荣。如今，小镇的盐矿（Salzwelten Hallstatt）成为游客探索的热门景点，参观者可深入矿坑，感受历史的脉动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410644" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410644-737997.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410644-737997-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410644" class="wp-caption-text">哈尔施塔特以其丰富的盐矿资源闻名，自公元前8世纪起便有人类居住。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina在旅程中提到，当地的交通虽然需耗时转车，但也正因如此，让她有机会慢慢感受哈尔施塔特的纯粹与静谧。这片土地的历史，宛如盐晶般闪耀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410645" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410645-737998.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410645-737998-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410645" class="wp-caption-text">当地的交通虽然需耗时转车，但也正因如此，让Nina有机会慢慢感受哈尔施塔特的纯粹与静谧。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>人文特色 – 中世纪建筑与宗教文化</h2>
<p>哈尔施塔特的街道蜿蜒狭窄，两旁是风格迥异的木质建筑，红色、黄色与蓝色的房屋彼此相依。Nina分享了她漫步小镇的照片，街巷间的一抹抹亮色，与背后巍峨的山脉形成鲜明对比。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410646" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410646-737999.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410646-737999-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410646" class="wp-caption-text">哈尔施塔特的街道蜿蜒狭窄，两旁是风格迥异的木质建筑，红色、黄色与蓝色的房屋彼此相依。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小镇中还有一处独特的宗教遗址—人骨教堂（Hallstatt Charnel House）。这里珍藏了超过1200个头骨，部分还绘有装饰性图案与姓名缩写，这是当地居民对逝者表达敬意的一种方式，也展现了这片土地独特的丧葬文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410647" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410647-738000.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410647-738000-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410647" class="wp-caption-text">小镇中还有一处独特的宗教遗址—人骨教堂（Hallstatt Charnel House）。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>自然奇观 – 冬日倒影与雪景</h2>
<p>Nina在文章中描述了她抵达哈尔施塔特时，湖面的完美倒影：“一下车就有完美倒影，太幸运了。”冬日的哈尔施塔特，阳光稀少，但偶尔露出的蔚蓝天空与白雪皑皑的群山，宛如一幅动人的山水画。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410648" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410648-738002.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410648-738002-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410648" class="wp-caption-text">哈尔施塔特的雪景。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>住在湖对岸的Nina，特别提到她的住宿点能俯瞰整个小镇风光，且附近人烟稀少，适合飞行无人机进行空拍。蓝天白雪下的小镇，展现出平时无法见到的静谧之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410649" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410649-738003.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410649-738003-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410649" class="wp-caption-text">在一间当地餐厅，Nina品尝了湖中特产鳟鱼搭配油香米饭与蔬菜的二人套餐。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>在地美食 – 湖鲜与当地特产</h2>
<p>小镇不仅风景如画，还拥有令人垂涎的美食。在一间当地餐厅，Nina品尝了湖中特产鳟鱼搭配油香米饭与蔬菜的二人套餐。她形容，这顿简单却美味的餐点，不仅饱腹，还为冬日的小镇增添了一丝温暖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410650" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410650-738004.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410650-738004-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410650" class="wp-caption-text">在冰雪覆盖的小镇拖行李箱是一件艰难的事，但这一切都在Nina看到美景后变得值得。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>交通攻略 – 穿越群山湖泊的旅程</h2>
<p>Nina的旅程从维也纳出发，经过三小时二十分钟的转车，终于抵达哈尔施塔特附近的Obertraun上特劳恩。她提到，在冰雪覆盖的小镇拖行李箱是一件艰难的事，但这一切都在看到美景后变得值得。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410651" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410651-738005.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410651-738005-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410651" class="wp-caption-text">童话般的哈尔施塔特小镇。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>小镇漫步 – 悠闲与惊喜</h2>
<p>Nina提到，“漫步小镇，久违的阳光洒下，眼前一幕幕的风景画，人少悠闲。”无论是沿着湖畔漫步，还是走入蜿蜒的街巷，每一处景致都让人流连忘返。对于热爱拍照的游客来说，这里的每个角落都值得按下快门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410652" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410652-738006.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410652-738006-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410652" class="wp-caption-text">Nina建议选择非高峰时段前往，或住在湖对岸以避开拥挤的人潮。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>旅行建议 – 避开人潮的秘诀</h2>
<p>由于哈尔施塔特的声名远播，每年吸引大量游客。Nina建议选择非高峰时段前往，或住在湖对岸以避开拥挤的人潮，这样能更加深入地体验小镇的宁静与美丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410653" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410653-738007.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410653-738007-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410653" class="wp-caption-text">哈尔施塔特的美，不仅仅在于其如诗如画的自然风光，还在于历史与人文的交融。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一次心灵的疗愈之旅</h2>
<p>哈尔施塔特的美，不仅仅在于其如诗如画的自然风光，还在于历史与人文的交融。在Nina的旅程中，我们看到了这座小镇的多样魅力：它是探索远古盐矿的窗口，是感受中世纪文化的场所，也是享受自然宁静的避风港。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410654" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410654-738008.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410654-738008-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410654" class="wp-caption-text">Nina所说，“蓝天群山，风景如画，不枉费我们跋山涉水来了！”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你想要一场能抚慰心灵的旅程，那么哈尔施塔特绝对值得被列入清单。正如Nina所说，“蓝天群山，风景如画，不枉费我们跋山涉水来了！”</p>
