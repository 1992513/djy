<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《哆啦A梦》电影 妻夫木聪为成年大雄配音</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426543-250131033252100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》（英文片名：STAND BY ME Doraemon）由《哥吉拉-1.0》导演山崎贵、《勇者斗恶龙 你的故事》导演八木龙一联合执导，是哆啦A梦第一部3D CG电影，日本卖座破83亿日元。</p>
<p>故事描述为了改变大雄悲惨的未来，他的玄孙．世修将22世纪的猫型机器人．哆啦A梦送到大雄身边。对于没什么干劲的哆啦A梦，世修设下了若大雄过得不幸福就无法回到22世纪的程序。情势所逼而开始照顾起大雄的哆啦A梦，发现大雄对同班同学．静香抱有好感，为了实现大雄与静香结婚的美好未来，哆啦A梦拿出了各种来自未来的道具，尽全力帮助大雄……</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426546" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426546-250131033301100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14426546" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426546-250131033301100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426546" class="wp-caption-text">《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》是哆啦A梦第一部3D CG电影，谈到把哆啦A梦拍成3D CG电影的原因，导演山崎贵表示，是为了提升日本制作3D CG电影的技术，“我认为这是一场豪赌，毕竟电视动画每周都会播出，电影版也每年都会推出一部。尽管如此，我还是认为没有比‘哆啦A梦’更适合拍成3D的内容了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426545-250131033257100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14426545" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426545-250131033257100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426545" class="wp-caption-text">《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一名导演八木龙一则表示，他很高兴能将大人小孩都知道的素材拍成3D CG电影，“以3D形式看‘任意门’时，那种穿越到别的空间的感觉更加真实，而使用‘竹蜻蜓’在空中翱翔的场面更是本片的看点。我认为大家会喜欢在空中飞翔的感觉。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426552-250131033254100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14426552" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426552-250131033254100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426552" class="wp-caption-text">《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》邀请妻夫木聪为成年后的大雄配音，他坦言收到邀请时非常犹豫，“因为《哆啦A梦》是一部国民漫画，对于自己能不能担纲配音，我内心有过许多纠结。那可是大雄的声音啊。感觉就像一道无法跨越的墙。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426547" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426547-250131033247100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14426547" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426547-250131033247100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426547" class="wp-caption-text">《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14426551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426551" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426551-250131033245100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14426551" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426551-250131033245100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426551" class="wp-caption-text">《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但因为制作团队不断恳求，妻夫木聪决定相信他们，接下这次的配音工作。妻夫木聪在配音的过程中，反复跟导演山崎贵讨论语气跟细节。配音完成之后，山崎贵对他的表现给予高度的正面评价，“真的很像大雄变声后的声音！”听到山崎贵的话，妻夫木聪才终于放松下来，“真的很紧张啊！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426550" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426550-250131033259100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14426550" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426550-250131033259100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426550" class="wp-caption-text">《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14426548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426548-250131033250100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14426548" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426548-250131033250100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426548" class="wp-caption-text">《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》剧照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《STAND BY ME 哆啦A梦》的国语、日语版本已于台湾同步上映。</p>
