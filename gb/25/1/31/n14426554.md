<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台湾故宫新年推“岁时吉庆”文物特展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426562-2025013105-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾国立故宫博物院推出黄历新年应景的“岁时吉庆—院藏节令文物精粹”特展，图为《清朝 徐扬 日月合璧五星联珠图（局部二）》。（台湾故宫提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾报导）台湾国立故宫博物院推出黄历新年应景的“岁时吉庆—院藏节令文物精粹”特展，“透过精选的院藏岁时文物，一览与我们生活息息相关的时序变化、节庆习俗，陪您过个知性蛇年。”</p>
<p>台湾故宫表示，“岁时吉庆—院藏节令文物精粹”特展通过“年岁时序”、“三节同乐”、“四时接应”三个单元，呈现古人“用一岁、四时、八节、十二月令、二十四节气、七十二候纪时的时间观”，以及配合历法、季节展开的各种相对应行事和节日文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426570" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426570-2501310443472378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426570" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426570-2501310443472378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="797" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426570" class="wp-caption-text">清朝院本画十二月令图。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14426565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426565-2501310348492378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14426565" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426565-2501310348492378-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426565" class="wp-caption-text">明朝嘉靖 斗彩婴戏图杯。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展出的《清徐扬日月合璧五星联珠图》为乾隆二十六年（1761）元旦，观象台钦天监观测到罕见的“日月同升，五星联珠”天文吉兆，因此皇帝下令绘图记录。画中除了绘制观测到天文吉兆的观象台，也详细记录了正月初一文武官员进京朝见皇帝拜年及百姓的拜年活动，呈现城市节庆的风俗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426571-2501310446002378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426571" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426571-2501310446002378-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426571" class="wp-caption-text">清朝院本亲蚕图 采桑（局部一）。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14426573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426573" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426573-2025013110.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426573-2025013110.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426573" class="wp-caption-text">清朝院本亲蚕图 采桑（局部二）。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《清院本亲蚕图 采桑》为国宝文物，此画为乾隆皇帝于孝贤皇后富察氏过世后下令绘制，清宫设立“皇帝亲耕”、“皇后亲蚕”典礼，后者由皇后率领群臣妻妾命妇祭拜“先蚕神”后喂养蚕，通过官方典礼仪式鼓励民间耕织活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426566" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426566-2501310418232378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426566" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426566-2501310418232378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426566" class="wp-caption-text">掐丝珐琅大香炉。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14426559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426559" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426559-2025013103.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426559-2025013103.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426559" class="wp-caption-text">清朝水仙盆景。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水仙绽放于新春佳节之际，是常见的年花，展出的“清 水仙盆景”以青玉为盆，水仙花瓣以白玉辗成，搭配鎏金花蕊与叶片，旁侧有一竹枝，并以青金石、绿松石等镶嵌为地，整体高洁清雅。展品“铜镀金镶松石斋戒牌”则呼应甫过不久的冬至，冬至圜丘祭天为清代最隆重的祭典之一，由皇帝亲祀。祭祀前需行斋戒，斋戒期间帝王与陪祀大臣皆于胸前佩戴斋戒牌，以警惕身心，竭诚致敬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426561-2025013104.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426561-2025013104.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426561" class="wp-caption-text">清朝 徐扬 日月合璧五星联珠图。（局部一）（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14426574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426574" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426574-2025013111.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426574-2025013111.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="785" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426574" class="wp-caption-text">清朝乾隆缂丝岁朝图。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>策展人、书画文献处赖玉玲副研究员表示，台湾故宫特别在迎向百岁的开春之际推出“岁时吉庆—院藏节令文物精粹”特展。此展从传统时间观出发，一探源自北方草原民族的清朝，在季节活动上展现的满、蒙风俗习尚，又遵照汉俗办理汉地传统节庆的多元民族文化共存特色。同时藉由清朝官方制定的冬至、元旦、万寿三大节庆以及传统春、夏、秋、冬四时活动，展示非常与日常时间当中，环境变换与人地互动的文化发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426567-2025013106.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426567-2025013106.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426567" class="wp-caption-text">清朝 徐扬 日月合璧五星联珠图（局部三）。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14426576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426576" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426576-2501310457052378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426576" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426576-2501310457052378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="648" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426576" class="wp-caption-text">清朝董邦达绘御笔中秋帖子诗。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾故宫表示，通过精选121组的精彩文物，从典籍、文献中应对时间的活动描述，随着时间流动呈现在书画、器物上的模样，期盼观众跟着故宫，一同展望下一个“百年”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14426577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426577" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426577-2501310459002378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426577" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426577-2501310459002378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426577" class="wp-caption-text">铜镀金镶松石斋戒牌。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14426568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14426568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426568-2025013107.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14426568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14426568-2025013107.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14426568" class="wp-caption-text">清朝 徐扬 日月合璧五星联珠图（局部四）。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾故宫为与民众同庆百岁，大年初一至初三每日将于正馆发送1,000份故宫百年院庆一元硬币给入馆参观之观众。另即日起至2月28日儿艺中心推出“印出蛇年好运气——小故宫新年春联/红包袋盖印活动”，故宫特别与插画师合作，从典藏具蛇纹图像之文物中撷取创作素材，设计三款吉祥印章，让您亲手盖印制作独特的故宫风春联及红包袋。活动并搭配学习单，可至展厅找寻印章图案中出现的文物。</p>
