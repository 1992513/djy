<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>老街巷弄等3大灯区启灯 揭开北港灯会序幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421630-741508-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>县长张丽善、文观处长陈璧君、北港镇长萧美文及来宾，为大复戏院重新开幕揭牌，揭开北港花灯序幕。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月25日讯】（大纪元记者 廖素贞台湾云林报导）2025年北港灯会即日起至2月14日止将在十大灯区盛大展开，县长张丽善24日晚间主持“老街巷弄灯区”、“义民庙”、“北港镇公所灯区”启灯暨大复戏院戏台揭牌仪式，为2025北港灯会首部曲揭开序幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421632-741510.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421632-741510-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421632" class="wp-caption-text">2025北港灯会，今天由老街巷弄、义民庙及北港镇公所抢先启灯揭开序幕。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421635-741513.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421635-741513-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421635" class="wp-caption-text">县长张丽善、北港镇长萧美文及文化观光处长陈璧君今晚穿着北港真人艺阁“扮仙”服装，至“老街巷弄灯区”启灯巡礼。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长张丽善、北港镇长萧美文及文化观光处长陈璧君今晚穿着北港真人艺阁“扮仙”服装，先为已有97年历史大复戏院戏台重新开幕揭牌，再至“老街巷弄灯区”、“义民庙”、“北港镇公所灯区”启灯巡礼，为2025北港灯会首部曲揭开序幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421639-741519.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421639-741519-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421639" class="wp-caption-text">北港工艺坊用蒸笼呈现蛇的意象，象征蒸蒸日上！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张丽善表示，今年北港灯会即日起至2月14日止，紧扣全球永续议题，融合北港的深厚历史文化与传统工艺，欢迎全国民众来北港，感受灯会原乡的魅力，今年老街巷弄灯区由王耀瑞、林聪贤、颜三泰等13位云林工艺师与艺术家联手策画，以灯笼、竹编、蒸笼墙等作品，展现北港的工艺传统与文化底蕴。义民庙灯区设置“义犬将军”花灯及招财祈福灯，充分展现愈在地、愈国际。让游客不仅能赏灯，更能深度探索北港传统文化。灯会期间还有环镇专车接驳民众到十大灯区，纾解交通拥挤！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421631-741509.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421631-741509-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421631" class="wp-caption-text">古色古香的传统花灯和雨伞花灯相辉映，是巷弄灯区的亮点！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421633-741511.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421633-741511-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421633" class="wp-caption-text">北港工艺坊的竹编灯笼，成了今年的一大特色。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421636-741514.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421636-741514-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421636" class="wp-caption-text">古色古香的传统花灯和雨伞花灯相互辉映，是巷弄灯区的亮点！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421637-741515.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421637-741515-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421637" class="wp-caption-text">巷弄灯区首日启灯，就吸引不少人潮。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421638-741516.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421638-741516-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421638" class="wp-caption-text">以金文书写的灯笼墙。（廖素贞提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文观处长陈璧君指出，2025北港灯会以“粼光绕境”为主题，共有十大灯区，今年首度分两阶段启灯，今天由老街巷弄、义民庙及北港镇公所抢先启灯，第二阶段启灯将于2月3日于颜思齐纪念碑圆环举办，届时将点亮全镇十大灯区，并有主灯声光秀，每晚6点至10点定时展演，为灯会增添更多视觉震撼。</p>
<p>文观处表示，大复戏院前身为日治时期的北港座，2009年登录为历史建筑，经修复再利用已成为地方文化地标。今年请无形文化资产保存人陈明山老师手绘戏台，彰显传统价值。灯会期间将于每日下午2点至6点演出布袋戏“水果三剑客”、金光戏及互动手偶体验，让大人小孩都能感受戏曲魅力。</p>
<p>另活动期间在水道头文化园区等十大灯区各规划打卡拍照领取“妈祖粼光祈福”小提灯、彩绘灯笼、街头艺人表演、艺阁扮仙体验（灯会期间体验价1000元）、灯会市集，千人DIY提灯踩街庆元宵、朝天宫元宵晚会等活动。民众请上云林县政府文化观光处官网、慢游云林官网及APP关注活动详情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421634" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421634-741512.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14421634" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421634-741512-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421634" class="wp-caption-text">颜三泰艺师制作的艺阁花灯。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>北港镇长萧美文表示，今年北港镇公所灯区以传统民间故事《白蛇传》来迎接象征灵动与智慧的蛇年，并在1/30日下午5点30分，邀请由黄钰珍老师率领的妙圆艺术团现场演出，欢迎民众来北港拜妈祖赏花灯。</p>
