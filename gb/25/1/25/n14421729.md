<h6>2025 Honda Prologue Elite。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月25日讯】Honda终于在北美推出第一辆全电动汽车，但其实Honda也不是完全没有经验，它以往也推出了Hybrid混能车，也曾推出以氢为能源的燃料电池车，并且也在日本及欧洲推出了小型电车Honda E，所以推出纯电车Prologue应该很容易吧！</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://dycmgeziwp90b.cloudfront.net/T9bQfRfhz"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/NR9I_IUCU68?si=qnIjW4rFHNhGs1hV" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>说回今次测试的Prologue，在多次车展上已看到过，不过今次是亲自驾驶，当然完全不一样。Prologue的外表在一众汽车中其实不是很特别，方块外型对于载人载物来说，非常实用，测试载的蓝色也很鲜艳，在阳光下份外耀眼。Prologue是由洛杉矶设计团队所主导，以简约新潮“Neo-Rugged”风格设计。Prologue的大小尺寸正好是中型SUV级别，轮轴达到121.8&#8243;，令到内舱空间可以放更大，可达到Passport级别，而Prologue的车身长度只比CR-V多出8&#8243;，宽度多出5&#8243;。车后的“Honda”纤细字体与方块车身有点不协调，奇怪是Honda不使用“H”厂徽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421732" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421732-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14421732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421732-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_03-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421732" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Honda Prologue Elite。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Prologue 提供七种引人注目的外观颜色，包括“北岸珍珠蓝（North Shore Pearl）”，其灵感来自加州塔霍湖附近自然景观和色彩的美丽，测试车也正好用上这颜色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421734-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_07.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14421734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421734-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_07-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421734" class="wp-caption-text">车身是“北岸珍珠蓝”。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Prologue有前轮驱动及四轮驱动两种版本，前驱车型搭载前置单电机。测试的Prologue Elite则标配全轮驱动（EX 和 Touring 车型亦可选配），额外添加后置电机。双电机配置可输出约288 hp和333 lb-ft扭力，提供更灵敏更快速的驾驶体验。续航里程最高约为300 miles，四驱版则只有约273 miles。车载有85 kWh锂离子电池，电池由LG Engergy Solution供应，可支持150 kW直流（DC）充电，可于10分钟充入相等于行走65 miles的电力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421735" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421735-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14421735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421735-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_18-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421735" class="wp-caption-text">双电机配置可输出约288 hp和333 lb-ft扭力。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Prologue的主动声控（Active Sound Control）进一步增添驾驶乐趣，透过与加速和减速同步的低至高频声音，营造顺畅的加速感。可选的运动模式（Sport Mode）让油门更灵敏和转向反应更紧实，以及更强的制动力等，令驾驶者有更佳的驾驶体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421737" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421737-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14421737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421737-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421737" class="wp-caption-text">尾箱容量也大。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Prologue标配了11&#8243;虚拟仪表板，另外也标配了11.3&#8243;高清触控萤幕，内建Google地图，兼容无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto系统。装有前后左右四摄像头，可呈现鸟瞰式影像，帮助泊车。Honda为Prologue设计的变速杆有别于以往，置于方向盘后方，不用占用中控台位置，使用也更方便。中控台便可有更多置物空间，并有手机充电设备，另外中控台底部的储物空间分两层。Prologue Elite版会装有抬头显示器（Head-Up Display），简称HUD，尺寸为7&#8243;x3&#8243;，就算将光亮调到很低，在晚上或室内停车场也会显得太亮，而影响前方视野。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421739" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421739-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14421739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421739-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_20-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421739" class="wp-caption-text">11&#8243;虚拟仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Prologue的行李置物空间，拥有最多25.2 cu.ft的容量，可平放三个高尔夫球袋。标配的60/40分后座椅背放平后，行李空间可扩展至57.7 cu.ft（EX 车型）。此外，行李厢地板下还隐藏了0.5 cu.ft的额外储物空间。Touring 和 Elite 车型标配免手动操作的电动尾门，进一步提升便利性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421740" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421740-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_21.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14421740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421740-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_21-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421740" class="wp-caption-text">11.3&#8243;高清触控萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，Prologue 目标获得 IIHS 的顶级安全选择+（Top Safety Pick+）和 NHTSA 的五星评价，并标配 Honda Sensing安全与驾驶辅助技术套件，其中首次引入后方横向交通刹车、盲区转向辅助及后方行人警示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421741" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421741-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14421741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421741-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_22-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421741" class="wp-caption-text">恒温系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市面上越来越多不同品牌推出电动汽车，所以出外充电也越来越困难，在附近只有一个高速DC充电站，中午的时候也有两三辆汽车等待，所以只有在另一天早上7时多去充电，但不一会三个充电桩也全满了。看来要买电动车也必须在家也装上充电器才行！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421745" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421745-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14421745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421745-2025_Honda_Prologue_Elite_27-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421745" class="wp-caption-text">Honda Prologue车内。（李奥／大纪元）<span style="font-size: 16px;">）</span></figcaption></figure>
