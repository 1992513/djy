<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>协助翻转人生 桃园社会局让街友从头到脚焕然一新</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421309-741329-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政与来宾手持工具刮除泡沫，象征洗刷掉对无家者的偏见。（桃园市社会局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月24日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）印象中无家可归露宿街头的街友总是给人脏脏的、臭臭的印象，也总让人避之唯恐不及，桃园市社会局陈宝民局长上任后，为改善此一现象，积极结合民间团体力量，协助街友从沐浴、更衣、服仪整理改造自己，不仅改变民众对游民的刻板印象，也让街友重新发现翻转人生的信心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421310" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421310-741330.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14421310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421310-741330.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421310" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政表扬长期投入街友服务的公私部门及企业。（桃园市社会局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>社会局表示，桃园列册在案的街友，约有200位左右，主要露宿地为桃园区文昌公园、朝阳公园及中坜区中正公园，为顾及市容及街友之居住自由，自2021年起已于露宿地设置置物柜，白天将棉被等随身行李收起，等到晚上再去取出，大大避免破坏公园的整洁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421311" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421311-741331.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14421311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421311-741331.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421311" class="wp-caption-text">陈宝民局长亲自发放御寒物资。（桃园市社会局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另为协助街友沐浴，除了行动沐浴车定点巡回于露宿热点外，社会局结合释宗皇师父将北区游民外展中心老旧的盥洗设备全面更新，让“永乐沐寓”成为街友们焕然一新的基地；服务对象陈大哥表示：“（外展）中心洗澡变方便之后，我都常常来报到，洗完心情都很好馁。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421312" style="width: 604px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421312-741332.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14421312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421312-741332.jpg" alt="" width="604" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421312" class="wp-caption-text">御寒物资发放。（桃园市社会局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421315" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421315-741335.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14421315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421315-741335.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421315" class="wp-caption-text">好桃洗关怀专车为街友提供沐浴服务及换洗衣物。（桃园市社会局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“永乐沐寓”除了盥洗功能外，也结合桃园市女子美容商业同业公会等单位合作“安心剪”方案，于每月最后一周星期三下午3点到5点提供义剪服务，并有民间单位捐赠之新衣、新鞋可以更换，再辅以社会局提供之福利评估、就业服务处的就业咨询，相信有助于协助街友增加社会适应力，并达成社会局“共好脱游、友善共融”的目标。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14421313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421313" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421313-741333.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14421313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421313-741333.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421313" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市女子美容商业同业公会提供义剪服务（义剪前）。（桃园市社会局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421314" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421314-741334.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14421314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421314-741334.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421314" class="wp-caption-text">义剪后（右三位）。（桃园市社会局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421316" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421316-741336.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14421316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421316-741336.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421316" class="wp-caption-text">修缮前（卫浴空间A）。（桃园市社会局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14421317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14421317" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421317-741337.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14421317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14421317-741337.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14421317" class="wp-caption-text">修缮后（卫浴空间A）。（桃园市社会局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈宝民表示，每个人都是社会的一分子，期待藉由交流、互动，强化彼此支持及情感连结，能有效减少因不了解而产生的偏见。社会局于桃园区及中坜区共设有2处游民外展服务中心，市民朋友如发现露宿街友需要协助，可拨打1999、街友外展服务中心（北区03-3395900、南区03-4353735）或社会局社会救助科电话03-3322101转6406通报，将尽速派员访视。◇</p>
