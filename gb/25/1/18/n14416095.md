<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】拥有STI灵魂的跑车 2025 Subaru WRX tS</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416120-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025 Subaru WRX tS。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月18日讯】这次试驾的Subaru WRX，虽然跟去年的都是WRX，但去年的是TR型号，而今年是经STI调校的tS，tS代表“tuned by STI”的意思，完全有了全新的感觉。</p>
<p>新一代WRX是车系的第五代产品，以最新一代Impreza为基本。前脸就跟新Impreza一样，进风口栏栅改为六角形，配上两边LED饰灯包围的头灯组；建立在Subaru Global Platform底盘之上，全内骨架结构和额外的结构黏合剂有助于提高车身刚性，同时减轻了重量。WRX与Impreza的最显眼分别是引擎盖上的冷却进风口，以及雾灯位置的设计。WRX的前轮撰后方也有数个排气口，配上车身扰流，特别有动感；车后的四出排气喉的银色，与后车底的黑色扰流板，搭配起来既实用也很好看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14416124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14416124" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416124-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_03-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14416124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416124-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_03-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14416124" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Subaru WRX tS。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>WRX tS版采用19”五幅式轮圈，设计很特别，并且配上Bridgestone Potenza S007轮胎。刹车方面采用Brembo高性能刹车系统，前6活塞刹车卡钳、后2活塞刹车卡钳，使用全金色特卡钳，比TR版的红色更出众，刹车碟也比其他版本为大，前13.4”／后12.8”透气碟。依旧标配了Subaru的“对称全轮驱动系统（Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive）”，提升了主动扭矩矢量控制，转向和操控更为灵敏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14416101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14416101" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416101-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14416101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416101-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_11-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14416101" class="wp-caption-text">19”五幅式轮圈。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>增强的Subaru Global Platform平台采用完整的内框架结构并增加了结构黏合剂，扭曲刚性提高了28%，悬挂安装点刚度增加了75%。更长的悬挂行程可提高车辆在不平坦路面上的稳定性和横向抓地力。后稳定杆直接安装在车身上，效率更高，亦有助于降低转弯时的侧倾。tS版独有电子控制的阻尼，经STI调校，可在不同驾驶模式时有不同效果；此外，驾驶模式有五种，分别为Comfort、Normal、Sport、Sport+及Individual，前四种是预设了转向、动力输出、悬挂及EyeSight的不同设定，Individual就可自定各种设定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14416102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14416102" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416102-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14416102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416102-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_10-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14416102" class="wp-caption-text">引擎盖上的进风口。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>WRX的精彩之处在于动力表现，动力心脏使用了比旧有WRX排气量更大的2.4升引擎，引擎本身是Subaru一贯的水平对向引擎，也即是Boxer引擎，马力输出271 hp，扭矩为258 lb-ft，比旧有高一点，但比起STI仍相距甚远。变速箱难得在现时流行自动变速箱的日子，Subaru也有提供6速手动变速箱的选择，只记得Honda Civic Si、Type R及Toyota GR Corolla仍有手动变速选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14416105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14416105" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416105-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_24.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14416105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416105-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_24-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14416105" class="wp-caption-text">2.4升水平对向引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2024 WRX内装设计与Impreza同样简约，最引人注目的是全新的11.6&#8243; Subaru Starlink多媒体系统，这是Impreza首次搭载这一系统，并且是直立式，即是高度大于宽度。高解析度触摸屏支援无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto，让驾驶者可以轻松地连接智能手机，使用全屏幕来控制汽车的各项功能，如音响、恒温系统和引擎自动开关等功能。系统还具备Bluetooth免提通话、AM/FM立体声、后视摄像头、SiriusXM收音机等。所有车型均标配电动门锁、电动车窗和电动侧镜、迎宾照明、智能遥控钥匙、独立轮胎压力监测系统，以及60/40可折叠后排座椅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14416106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14416106" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416106-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_34.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14416106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416106-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_34-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14416106" class="wp-caption-text">WRX内装。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>WRX tS的仪表板也跟上其他汽车，使用全萤幕作为仪表板，尺寸为12.3&#8243;，可同时显示更多资讯，像增压器的即时压力，也可显示地图。内部座椅及门内都采用黑色及蓝色的配搭，并装有Recaro跑车桶椅，驾驶席有8向电动调节，黑色绒面布料座椅带有蓝色缝线，贴身又暖心。WRX有皮革包裹方向盘和手动变速杆、铝合金踏板、加热前座椅和前后USB充电端口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14416107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14416107" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416107-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_25.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14416107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416107-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_25-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14416107" class="wp-caption-text">WRX tS的仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，新WRX还搭载了最新一代的EyeSight驾驶辅助技术，EyeSight可以识别十字路口的自行车和行人，并在必要时提醒驾驶者并启动刹车以避免碰撞。WRX还提供了自动紧急转向功能、盲点检测、车道保持辅助和后方交通警告的车型，以在速度低于50英里/小时时协助控制转向，以帮助避免碰撞。与上周测试的Outback Touring一样，在中控台顶谓有红外线设备，用于监察驾驶者状态，例如汽车前行时驾驶者没有向前看，都会发出声响提示警告。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14416109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14416109" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416109-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14416109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416109-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_27-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14416109" class="wp-caption-text">11.6&#8243; Subaru Starlink多媒体系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试的WRX tS是蓝色涂装，就是World Rally Blue Pearl，所以非常好看，也是STI专用的蓝色，所以虽然WRX tS并不是STI，但设备都与STI极为接近，况且WRX的动态表现绝佳，有涡轮增压之助，加速力很强，而且又有“对称全轮驱动系统”，令行车表现稳如泰山。由于测试期间遇上感恩节，所以测试延长一点，也可享受久一点，绝对要感恩！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14416111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14416111" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416111-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_28.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14416111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14416111-2025_Subaru_WRX_tS_28-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14416111" class="wp-caption-text">手动变速。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
