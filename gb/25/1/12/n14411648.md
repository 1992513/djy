<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>过年零食吃什么好？中医师推荐3类健康零食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13578971-shutterstock_393497851-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>各类坚果和果干。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><b>编者按：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">过年期间家家户户免不了备些零嘴应景，然而，很多零食其实暗藏高糖、高盐、高油、高热量陷阱，一不小心就容易摄取过量，不利于健康。本文推荐三大类天然、营养丰富的零食，让你既能享受美味又能维护健康。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>高糖零食不利养生</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">摄取过多糖分不仅容易导致肥胖，还会增加糖尿病、心血管疾病的风险，并加速皮肤老化。从中医角度来看，体内的糖分过高会损伤脾胃，助热生火，甚至耗损新年冬季养生肾阴；食用过多零食会破坏身体阴阳平衡，影响来年的健康，不利于冬季养生。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>三大类天然、健康零食</b></span></h2>
<p><b>1.毛豆</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">富含植物性蛋白质与膳食纤维，有助于稳定血糖，促进肠道健康；性平味甘，可健脾除湿、润燥解毒。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14274837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14274837" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274837-shutterstock_2192475793.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14274837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/06/id14274837-shutterstock_2192475793-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14274837" class="wp-caption-text"><b>毛豆</b>。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>2.果干</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?蔓越莓干能保护泌尿道。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">黑枣干具有补血养气和润肠通便的功效。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12716549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12716549" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/dried-fruits_119731735-copy.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12716549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/dried-fruits_119731735-copy-450x300.jpg" alt="水果干用来养生、作为中药材使用，能够比水果发挥更大的威力。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12716549" class="wp-caption-text">果干。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>3.坚果</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?核桃可补肾益智。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">葵瓜子有助于润肠明目。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">开心果富含抗氧化剂，能促进心血管健康；还可以温肾暖脾、益智健脑。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">松子富含健康脂肪、抗氧化物质和叶黄素，有助于保护心血管、护眼和抗衰老；并具有滋阴润燥、温胃肠的功效。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13606250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13606250" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606250-nuts_213903202.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13606250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13606250-nuts_213903202-450x300.jpg" alt="用坚果代替碳水化合物可预防高血糖、改善糖尿病。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13606250" class="wp-caption-text">坚果。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>贴心小叮咛：</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">★</span><b>选择天然、无添加物的零食：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">避免选食调味过多的零嘴，以免造成体内钠、钾含量过高，并留意补充足够水分。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">★</span><b>每日建议摄取量：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">果干为20?30克，坚果则是30克以内。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">★</span><b>肾功能不佳者宜适量食用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">以上推荐的零食多数钾含量较高，可能加重肾脏的负担，需在医师指导下适量食用。最好选择低磷、低钾、低钠的食品，并留意蛋白质总摄取量，饮用充足水分。</span><strong>◎</strong></p>
