<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大湖乡公所岁末送暖 邀弱势家庭餐叙</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417946-740232-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>发放新年慰问金。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月20日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）大湖乡公所1月20日上午在万圣宫举行岁末送暖-幸福迎新年活动，发放弱势家庭新年慰问金及民间爱心团体捐赠的民生物质，并围炉同乐，让他们感受社会的温暖，开心过好年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417954-740241.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417954-740241-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417954" class="wp-caption-text">大湖乡长致词。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14417949" aria-describedby="caption-14417949" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417949-740235-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417949-740235-450x300.jpg" alt="颁赠感谢状3" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14417949" class="wp-caption-text">颁赠感谢状。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>这项活动，大湖乡公所结合了爱心人士、企业、慈善团体共襄盛举，一同出钱出力，让社会温暖散发到偏乡角落。</p>
<p>大湖乡公所今天在万圣宫邀请了乡内约160户低收入户及近贫家庭齐聚一堂用餐，并发放新年慰问金及民间爱心人士、团体捐赠的白米、泡面、蛋糕、暖暖被、牙膏、沐浴乳、肉松、卫生纸等民生物质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417951-740238.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417951-740238-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417951" class="wp-caption-text">发放的物质。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同时现场还有爱心义剪、大湖卫生所施打流感、新冠疫苗及X光检查、戒烟卫教宣导等，亦安排歌舞表演，弱势家庭围炉同乐，感受到社会的温馨和关怀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417950-740237.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417950-740237-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417950" class="wp-caption-text">义剪。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14417953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417953-740240.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417953-740240-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417953" class="wp-caption-text">舞蹈表演。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14417952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417952-740239.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417952-740239-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417952" class="wp-caption-text">大湖岁末送暖活动弱势族群餐叙。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>副县长邱俐俐、大湖乡长黄惠琴、乡代会主席谢见德、副主席陈永豪、代表陈增堭、县府社会处副处长邱苹榛等人皆到场致意，并颁发捐赠物质的爱心人士、慈善团体、企业等感谢状，感谢他们发挥爱心的善行义举。</p>
<p>大湖乡长黄惠琴感谢爱心人士及团体赞助物质，公所筹办这项活动，希望让偏乡弱势家庭感受到社会的温暖，高兴过好年。</p>
<p>副县长邱俐俐希望藉由这项活动，让偏乡的公益活动更加完善，将爱心散发到各个角落。</p>
<p>颁赠感谢状的爱心人士和团体：树林德和福德宫、菲飞公司、鸿运寝具公司、秀峰空调企业社、腾信展业公司、大苹果小太阳活力平台、嘻me露爱心大使、苗栗火旁龙文化发展协会、濬鼎精业公司、苗邑照天宫文衡圣帝慈善会、新北市心善会、心传爱关怀协会、东东食品烘焙坊、郭尹泽、紫绮公司、张永津、善心家族、庄启明。</p>
<p>今天下午，大湖乡公所并发放约80位独居老人棉被、背心、枕头等物质。</p>
