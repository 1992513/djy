<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>林铁及文资处处长 由王昭堡接任专业领航</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417960-740258-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>代理及新任处长交接暨宣誓典礼，由林业及自然保育署副署长林澔贞监交。（林铁及文资处提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月20日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）农业部林业及自然保育署阿里山林业铁路及文化资产管理处原任处长黄妙修于114年1月16日退休，新任处长由林业保育署集水区治理组组长王昭堡升任。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417961" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417961-740259.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417961-740259-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417961" class="wp-caption-text">代理及新任处长交接暨宣誓典礼合影。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新任处长王昭堡为国立中兴大学水土保持研究所硕士，历任经济部水利署科长、正工程司、林业保育署集水区治理组组长等职位，具工程专业，并于2018年荣获公共工程金质奖个人贡献奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417962" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417962-740260.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417962-740260-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417962" class="wp-caption-text">林业及自然保育署副署长林澔贞致词。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林业保育署表示，王昭堡在担任集水区治理组组长期间，除致力推动森林水土资源整合防灾工程，更率全国各机关之先制定治理工程友善生态机制，于既有水保设施增设动物脱困通行装置等重要政策，治山兼顾生态保育；还督导各分署强化林道巡查与维护，充分展现其运用工程治理专业，践行环境友善与水土资源永续。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417963" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417963-740261.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417963-740261-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417963" class="wp-caption-text">新任处长王昭堡。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近年王昭堡也参与擘划多项阿里山林业铁路重大工程，如荣获2024公共工程金质奖的“阿里山森林铁路42号隧道”与2023公共工程金质奖之“祝山车站改建工程”，屡有建树。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417964-740262.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417964-740262-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417964" class="wp-caption-text">新任处长王昭堡宣誓就职。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于后续阿里山林业铁路及文化资产管理处将推动涉及多项阿里山林业铁路安全提升的“大阿里山轴带百年跃升计划”，也将执行数量庞大的新车采购，林业保育署因此借重王昭堡的工程与采购专业，期能如期如质顺利推动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417965-740263.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417965-740263-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417965" class="wp-caption-text">新任处长王昭堡致词。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林业保育署强调，阿里山林业铁路历经百年风华，不仅见证台湾林业的变迁，更成为乘载台湾历史与人文记忆的重要象征。新任处长交接，代表林铁及文资处将以科学治理专业领航开创新局。未来林铁及文资处将持续深化与地方的合作，并以创新思维推动阿里山林业铁路观光及文化资产融合发展，致力打造串联自然与文化的珍贵资产。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14417966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417966-740264.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14417966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417966-740264-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417966" class="wp-caption-text">新任处长王昭堡致词。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>
