<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>快速套棉被 一个人也能简单搞定</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d11dfuybe1ur8g.cloudfront.net/A3qoHKPDa"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Q055rrqmPQU?si=6WNuLjdia4YvRDSt" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a><br />
（铃木太太提供）</p>
<p><b>编者按：</b>被单洗好<span style="font-weight: 400;">了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，准备套棉被，没有助手帮忙，自己弄得手忙脚乱？本文</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">教大家一个省力、省时、又简单的方法，只要五个小动作，便能快速地套好棉被套。这个方法同样适用于独自套被单。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">动作1.把棉被套（或被单）由内向外翻出来，并铺平棉被套（或被单）。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14417835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417835" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417835-adb26f3fec9e812aa5575b98f088d2c7.jpg"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14417835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417835-adb26f3fec9e812aa5575b98f088d2c7-450x242.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="242" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417835" class="wp-caption-text">步骤1。（铃木太太授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">动作2.将棉被摊开放在棉被套（或被单）上面。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14417836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417836" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417836-7ac327a09a23575d6a38e2cf6e8ab20c.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14417836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417836-7ac327a09a23575d6a38e2cf6e8ab20c-450x243.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417836" class="wp-caption-text">步骤2。（铃木太太授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">注意：棉被要和棉被套（或被单）对齐。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">动作3.把棉被连同棉被套（或被单）一起卷起来。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14417837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417837" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417837-4aec01585dad5180e2a82d18038f3649.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14417837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417837-4aec01585dad5180e2a82d18038f3649-450x246.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="246" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417837" class="wp-caption-text">步骤3。（铃木太太授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">注意：要从棉被套（或被单）开口的正对面开始卷。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">动作4.一只手（从开口处）伸进棉被套（或被单）里，去抓着棉被的角落，并将棉被套（或被单）由内往外翻，以包覆棉被。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14417838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417838" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417838-0f1c03854b88f2a32982a56990578e73.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14417838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417838-0f1c03854b88f2a32982a56990578e73-450x242.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="242" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417838" class="wp-caption-text">步骤4-1。（铃木太太授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14417839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417839" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417839-679693be684dafb1c79fbbb85450d3d8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14417839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417839-679693be684dafb1c79fbbb85450d3d8-450x243.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417839" class="wp-caption-text">步骤4-2。（铃木太太授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #800000;">注意：此时，原来朝内的棉被套（或被单）正面，已经被翻到外面。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">动作5.走到对面，将套好棉被套（或被单）的棉被铺平。大功告成了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">！</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14417840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417840" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417840-afb287da0673be9307773f080b866a34.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14417840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417840-afb287da0673be9307773f080b866a34-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417840" class="wp-caption-text">步骤5-1。（铃木太太授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14417841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14417841" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417841-01239b7c6ed208fbc001186284dc0e1a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14417841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14417841-01239b7c6ed208fbc001186284dc0e1a-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14417841" class="wp-caption-text">步骤5-2。（铃木太太授权youtube撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #008000;">【读者反馈】</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">若是棉被套（或被单）的开口在侧边，把棉被套（或被单）转90°，横着放，这样就变成开口在正对面。</span><strong>◎</strong></p>
