<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一杯咖啡唤醒故乡灵魂 导演黄朝亮与太麻里的故事</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423025-741678-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>导演黄朝亮，曾参与的电影包括“大囍临门”“大钓哥”等。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月27日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）黄朝亮导演，出生于台东太麻里华源村，是一位深具乡土情怀的创作者。他的故事，跨越电影与咖啡馆两个截然不同的世界。他曾说：“每天追收视率真的很累，后来觉得可以尝试别的，刚好我也比较有创作的欲望。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423294" style="width: 446px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423294-741698_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423294-741698_medium.webp" alt="" width="446" b="595"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423294" class="wp-caption-text">黄朝亮导演参与过电影“夏天协奏曲”。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曾参与的电影包括“大囍临门”“大钓哥”“寒单”“夏天协奏曲”等电影。他从电视台到电影圈，再从电影圈回到家乡，开启了台东市日式警察宿舍咖啡馆的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423285" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423285-741679_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423285-741679_medium.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="597"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423285" class="wp-caption-text">日式警察宿舍的咖啡馆，位于台东市区。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>太麻里的咖啡梦</h2>
<p>
黄导演并不是天生的咖啡迷，而是因为拍摄电影《白天的星星》，开始接触黑咖啡。“当时压力大，天天头痛，喝咖啡提神，后来就习惯了。”多年后，他回到太麻里，偶然喝到当地咖啡农亲手种植的咖啡，惊艳不已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423284" style="width: 445px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423284-741681_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423284-741681_medium.webp" alt="" width="445" b="594"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423284" class="wp-caption-text">黄朝亮回到太麻里，偶然喝到当地咖啡农亲手种植的咖啡，惊艳不已。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“太麻里的咖啡很特别，因为下午的云雾和海风吹拂，让咖啡豆带有天然的咸甜口感，这是其他地方找不到的。”他立刻决定，要把这样的咖啡推广出去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423283" style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423283-741682_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423283-741682_medium.webp" alt="" width="595" b="446"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423283" class="wp-caption-text">“太麻里的咖啡很特别，因为下午的云雾和海风吹拂，让咖啡豆带有天然的咸甜口感，这是其他地方找不到的。”黄朝亮说。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>改造日式警察宿舍</h2>
太麻里咖啡馆坐落在台东市的一栋日式警察宿舍内，这座建筑因年久失修一度荒废。“我接手之前，这里是一家拉面馆，但因地点和消费习惯的关系，没能经营下去。”黄导演忆起当时的情景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423293-741683_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423293-741683_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423293" class="wp-caption-text">“我接手之前，这里是一家拉面馆，但因地点和消费习惯的关系，没能经营下去。”黄导演忆起当时的情景。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当时，我正推广太麻里咖啡，许多人问我：“有没有实体店？”甚至在杭州的朋友也说：“回台湾后找不到太麻里咖啡。”这些声音让我下定决心在家乡开一家咖啡馆。我开始寻找场地，尤其是我们村子那迷人的景色，但多数土地是国有地，无法商用，或需要大量修缮资金。一年多的努力，都没有结果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423292" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423292-741684_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423292-741684_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="560"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423292" class="wp-caption-text">“回台湾后找不到太麻里咖啡。”这些声音让黄朝亮下定决心在家乡开一家咖啡馆。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>后来，县长饶庆铃和文化局的朋友提议，让我看看这个日式宿舍。这里虽然不在太麻里，但地点不错，他们鼓励我参与竞标。当时没有人竞标，我看后决定先尝试经营。</p>
<p>起初经营并不容易，这里不在商业街，距铁花村也有距离。我们没有广告预算，只能靠口耳相传，但我坚持了下来，希望每位客人都能感受到这里的故事与用心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423303-741701_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423303-741701_medium.webp" alt="" width="450" b="601"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423303" class="wp-caption-text">黄朝亮：“希望每位客人都能感受到这里的故事与用心。”（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14423291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423291" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423291-741685_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423291-741685_medium.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423291" class="wp-caption-text">起初经营并不容易，这里不在商业街，距铁花村也有距离。黄朝亮说他没有广告预算，只能靠口耳相传。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他亲自设计了咖啡馆的格局。室内的榻榻米房间，摆放着从家里搬来的红桧木桌。“这张桌子是日据时代留下来的，我希望这样的历史感，能融入咖啡馆的氛围中。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423295" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423295-741697_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423295-741697_medium.webp" alt="" width="451" b="602"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423295" class="wp-caption-text">“这张桌子是日据时代留下来的，我希望这样的历史感，能融入咖啡馆的氛围中。”黄朝亮说。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>户外有一棵百年的茄冬树，黄导演把入口设计在树下。他说：“如果直接从马路的正门进来，就错过了这里的美景。这棵树和这块空地是这里的灵魂。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423298" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423298-741694_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423298-741694_medium.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423298" class="wp-caption-text">黄朝亮说：“如果直接从马路的正门进来，就错过了这里的美景。这棵树和这块空地是这里的灵魂。”（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>在地味道的销魂饭</h2>
咖啡馆里的招牌餐点“甘愿销魂饭”、“酸辣鸡饭”等，来源于黄导演太太的家常料理。“我太太是台东岩湾人，这些菜都是她的秘方。”黄导演笑着说。</p>
<p>“销魂饭的肉是用手切的，不是用绞肉机。我们的员工都依据她的配方，把酱料比例、火候记录下来。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423288-741688_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423288-741688_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="380"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423288" class="wp-caption-text">咖啡馆里的招牌餐点“销魂饭”，来源于黄导演太太的家常料理。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14423288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423288-741688_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423288-741688_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="380"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423288" class="wp-caption-text">色香味十足的酸辣鸡饭。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>起初，他坚持所有餐点都使用在地食材。“我甚至尝试过把释迦做成三明治，或者把洛神花融入餐点。”不过，由于成本过高，他不得不做出一些妥协。“现在我们的猪肉、鸡肉和蔬菜仍然是台东产的，但饮品方面，我们推出更多带有特色的品项，例如释迦多多和红乌龙茶。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423287" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423287-741689_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14423287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423287-741689_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="480"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423287" class="wp-caption-text">黄朝亮爸爸种的释迦和养乐多混搭后，成为店里的招牌“释迦多多”。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>打造太麻里品牌</h2>
为了推广故乡的咖啡，黄导演申请了“TaiMaLi”的商标。他解释：“这个 logo 上有两条线，一条是中央山脉，另一条是太平洋，象征太麻里的地理特色。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423286-741690_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423286-741690_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="451"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423286" class="wp-caption-text">黄朝亮凝聚在地咖啡农，他说：“如果10个咖啡农各自打造品牌，成本会很高，但如果统一品牌，就能共享资源。”（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他还希望凝聚在地咖啡农的力量。“如果10个咖啡农各自打造品牌，成本会很高，但如果统一品牌，就能共享资源，让大家的咖啡都能被看见。”他甚至无偿提供这个商标和设计，只要求每位咖啡农提供品质优良的咖啡豆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423301" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423301-741691_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423301-741691_medium.webp" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423301" class="wp-caption-text">黄朝亮设置的咖啡厅以舒适、自然为主。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>经营中的困难</h2>
虽然太麻里的咖啡品质优异，但黄导演坦言，线上的销售并不顺利。“我们的成本高，国外豆子量大又便宜，这让台湾的豆子很难与之竞争。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423296-741696_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423296-741696_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423296" class="wp-caption-text">虽然太麻里的咖啡品质优异，但黄导演坦言，线上的销售并不顺利。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>疫情期间，台湾旅游盛行，咖啡馆得以稳定经营。但疫情结束后，更多人选择出国旅游，来客数骤减。2024年初，他的咖啡馆甚至创下单日营业额仅980元的最低记录。</p>
<p>他说：“我们靠口耳相传，没钱打广告，只能尽力做好品质和服务。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423300" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423300-741692_medium.webp" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423300-741692_medium.webp" alt="" width="601" b="451"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423300" class="wp-caption-text">2024 年初，黄朝亮的咖啡馆甚至创下单日营业额仅980元的最低纪录。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>传统与创新的平衡</h2>
黄导演认为，创新与传统并不矛盾。“就像我最近写的贺岁喜剧，里面的乩童不想被叫乩童，他说自己是‘神人心理咨询师’，这也是一种文创。”</p>
<p>在咖啡馆里，他逐渐移除了与电影相关的装饰，让空间的主角回归到“太麻里”的故事。“这是我开店的初心，希望能把太麻里的咖啡和文化推向全世界。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423299" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423299" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423299-741693_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423299" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423299-741693_medium.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423299" class="wp-caption-text">在咖啡馆里，黄朝亮逐渐移除了与电影相关的装饰，让空间的主角回归到“太麻里”的故事。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄朝亮导演的太麻里咖啡馆，不仅是一家咖啡店，更是一个充满故事和梦想的地方。无论是杯中的红乌龙茶，还是碗里的销魂饭，每一口都承载着他对故乡的热爱与坚持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423290-741686_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423290-741686_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423290" class="wp-caption-text">黄朝亮导演的太麻里咖啡馆，不仅是一家咖啡店，更是一个充满故事和梦想的地方。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他说：“希望有一天，太麻里的咖啡能让全世界的人都看到，也希望这里的故事能被更多人记住。”</p>
