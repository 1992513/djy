<h6>台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年01月27日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾采访报导）今年是蛇年。黄历新年台湾放9天年假。从今天（27日）起寒流开始南下，若民众过年想躲避寒流来袭，春游何处去？摄影师宋永泰推荐，高雄美浓此刻是暖冬，很适合喜欢赏花的人到此漫游，感受湖畔花海的美好新春气象。</p>
<p>宋永泰说，“美浓秋冬乐活嘉年华暨花海彩绘大地”19日在美浓湖畔开幕，他们选择连假的第一天25日就去美浓赏花，看起来花朵都是刚盛开的状况，而且当地天气好热，气温竟然高达26、27摄氏度，一点都不像冬天，是很舒服的暖冬，而且这里除了有花景，还有山景、湖景可赏，来此过年春游是不错的选择。</p>
<p><center><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://dm41kyod3ht9r.cloudfront.net/D9b&#29983/embed/NhedVvKqTOI?si=RBKP9N0pPyZTLXDb" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center></p>
<figure id="attachment_14423323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423323" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423323-2501270057542378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423323" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423323-2501270057542378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423323" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>高雄市政府发布新闻稿指出，高雄美浓花海今年以“蛇来蛇趣，来去美浓尞”为主题，分别在湖畔、牛角湾、幸福平安及奇美4大花区融入多样装置艺术，其中幸福平安花区结合高雄冬日游乐园设置巨大化食物拍照背板，为美浓过年赏花增添趣味，打造具地方特色的多样赏花景点。</p>
<p><center><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://dm41kyod3ht9r.cloudfront.net/D9b&#29983/embed/EyP2JaO64bE?si=08ZDh5WCIxZLW9Yq" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center></p>
<figure id="attachment_14423322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423322" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423322-2501270057572378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423322" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423322-2501270057572378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423322" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14423324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423324-2501270057522378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14423324" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423324-2501270057522378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423324" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰说，他们开车去高雄美浓，到现场看到满满人潮，可能是天气好的关系，4大花区的花况正是“最佳赏花期”，种植了大片的向日葵、百日草、大波斯菊等缤纷花海，其中又以湖畔的波斯菊花况最优，有浅粉色、深粉色、黄色等的波斯菊花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423325-2501270057472378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14423325" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423325-2501270057472378-600x354.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="354" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423325" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14423326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423326" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423326-2501270057452378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14423326" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423326-2501270057452378-600x329.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423326" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">（</a><ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰表示，高雄美浓湖畔花海，是民众过年期间赏花踏青的热门景点。游客可以在花海中踏春骑行，享受美好的大自然田园风光。这里还有多处装置艺术供游客打卡拍照。他提到，民众可以站在美丽花海中，搭配美浓特色连绵的灵山、双峰山、月光山拍照，留下美好的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423328-2501270057402378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14423328" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423328-2501270057402378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423328" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14423327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423327" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423327-2501270057422378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14423327" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423327-2501270057422378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423327" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>蛇年迎新春，他说，开春之际，很高兴在高雄美浓看到很漂亮的花况，也期许国泰民安，过新年也祝福“大家都健康”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423329-2501270057372378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423329" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423329-2501270057372378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423329" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市美浓湖畔花海。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾南部客家小镇—美浓，在《天下杂志》和中华民国交通部观光署等单位举办的“微笑之乡”网络调查中，曾获十万名网民票选分数最高。宋永泰表示，美浓有超过百年历史的油纸伞、客家花布以及千年客家文化的擂茶。至于客家小吃，他认为萝卜粄、萝卜丝饼都值得游客品尝。</p>
