<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>陶艺家廖光亮的坚定伴侣 廖夏子的故事</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423316-741706-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>排湾族陶艺家廖光亮的太太廖夏子。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月27日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）排湾族陶艺家廖光亮的太太廖夏子，原本是台东乡村生活的陌生人。年轻时，她是一名教职员，心怀到日本游学的梦想，努力存钱准备踏上国外之旅。然而，她在台东布农部落的一次驻村中，意外邂逅了廖光亮。这段相遇改变了她的人生，也让她踏上一条充满挑战与感动的旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423341" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423341-741707_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423341-741707_medium.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423341" class="wp-caption-text">在台东布农部落的一次驻村中，意外邂逅了廖光亮。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖光亮对“回家”的执念深深感动了廖夏子。作为排湾族人，廖光亮自小离开家乡，而他始终渴望回到屏东牡丹乡的部落生活，重拾文化根源。这份情感不仅影响了他的创作，也吸引了廖夏子，让她决定陪伴他走上这条回归之路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423342" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423342-741708_medium-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423342-741708_medium-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423342" class="wp-caption-text">廖光亮自小离开家乡，而他始终渴望回到屏东牡丹乡的部落生活，重拾文化根源。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>决定留在部落　语言与文化的挑战</h2>
<p>父执辈从云林西螺移居台东，廖夏子原本计划到日本留学，却最终选择跟随廖光亮回到屏东牡丹乡，这对她而言无异于进入“国外”。部落中几乎只使用排湾族语，只有国小学生能够用少量的中文与她交流。她花了半年时间适应这种语言隔阂，从最初的困惑逐渐转变为能够理解一半的对话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423343-741709_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423343-741709_medium.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423343" class="wp-caption-text">廖夏子原本计划到日本留学，却最终选择跟随廖光亮回到屏东牡丹乡，这对她而言无异于进入“国外”。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这段期间，廖夏子也放弃了原本的生意与生活，将游学的积蓄投入到部落的生活中。她以实际行动支持廖光亮的艺术梦想，无论是面对经济压力，还是文化差异，她都坚持了下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423355" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423355-741724_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423355-741724_medium.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423355" class="wp-caption-text">廖光亮的作品质朴自然，技艺纯熟。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>艰难创业路　九鸟陶烧工作坊的诞生</h2>
<p>2002年，廖光亮成立了九鸟陶烧工作坊，专注于柴烧陶艺。然而，创业初期并不顺利。建造窑炉、烧制作品的高失败率，以及市场对高价陶艺品的低接受度，让这条路充满挑战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423344" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423344-741711_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423344-741711_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423344" class="wp-caption-text">2002年，廖光亮在台东成立了陶烧工作坊，专注于柴烧陶艺。然而，创业初期并不顺利。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖夏子成为廖光亮最坚强的后盾。即使家庭经济困难，她仍然支持丈夫购置瓦斯窑，甚至在晚餐、中餐时到父母家“报到”，以解燃眉之急。她坦言自己不是一名策展人，无法以高端策略推广廖光亮的作品，但她用自己的实际行动，努力将陶艺品推向市场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423345" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423345-741712_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423345-741712_medium.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423345" class="wp-caption-text">坦言自己不是一名策展人，无法以高端策略推广廖光亮的作品，但廖夏子用自己的实际行动，努力将陶艺品推向市场。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>创新与坚持 从失败到成功</h2>
<p>面对市场需求，廖夏子开始转型，将“卖作品”变为“卖体验”。她发现，让游客亲自参与陶艺创作，比单纯销售成品更具吸引力。这一策略不仅增加了收入，也让更多人能亲身感受到柴烧陶艺的魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423346" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423346-741713_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14423346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423346-741713_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423346" class="wp-caption-text">廖夏子发现，让游客亲自参与陶艺创作，比单纯销售成品更具吸引力。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>九鸟陶烧工作坊逐渐赢得了支持，甚至有朋友愿意出资购买十万元的陶壶，只为支持他们建造新窑。尽管如此，廖夏子仍然感叹创业路上的艰辛，尤其是政府补助虽然能提供部分资金，但自掏腰包的部分往往更高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423353" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423353-741722_medium-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423353-741722_medium-1.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="461" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423353" class="wp-caption-text">廖夏子仍然感叹创业路上的艰辛，尤其是政府补助虽然能提供部分资金，但自掏腰包的部分往往更高。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>母亲的角色 陪伴儿子的成长</h2>
<p>廖夏子与廖光亮的儿子廖键茗从小就耳濡目染，对陶艺产生了浓厚的兴趣。从华德福教育到台东女中美术班，再到考上国立台南艺术大学，廖键茗一路走来备受瞩目。他17岁时的作品《缝合》获得大奖，更证明了家庭陶艺氛围的深远影响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423347" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423347-741715_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14423347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423347-741715_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423347" class="wp-caption-text">廖夏子与廖光亮的儿子廖键茗从小就耳濡目染，对陶艺产生了浓厚的兴趣。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14423354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423354" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423354-741723_medium-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423354-741723_medium-1.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="543" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423354" class="wp-caption-text">廖键茗的作品“缝合”。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于儿子的选择，廖夏子既有期待，也尊重他的自主性。她坦言，虽然希望儿子能延续陶艺的传统，但更重要的是，他能找到自己的道路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423348" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423348-741717_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14423348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423348-741717_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423348" class="wp-caption-text">廖夏子坦言，虽然希望儿子能延续陶艺的传统，但更重要的是，他能找到自己的道路。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>支持与陪伴 艺术家的梦想无止境</h2>
<p>廖光亮对柴烧技术的执著，让他不断尝试新技法与设计。廖夏子形容，廖光亮的梦想就像一座永远需要燃料的窑炉，总是寻求突破。而她则是那个默默加柴添火的人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423349" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423349-741718_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423349-741718_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423349" class="wp-caption-text">廖夏子形容，廖光亮的梦想就像一座永远需要燃料的窑炉，总是寻求突破。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这对夫妻的合作不仅在家庭中展现，还延伸至艺术创作。廖光亮设计的陶壶“茑茑壶”，不仅具有实用性，还融入了排湾族文化的图纹与故事，传递着分享与土地的概念。这些作品背后的故事，也因廖夏子的讲述而更显温暖与动人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423350" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423350-741719_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14423350" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423350-741719_medium.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423350" class="wp-caption-text">廖光亮设计的陶壶“茑茑壶”，融入了排湾族文化的图纹与故事，传递着分享与土地的概念。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>生活与梦想的平衡</h2>
<p>从屏东牡丹乡到台东初鹿，廖夏子用她的毅力与智慧，陪伴廖光亮在艺术之路上披荆斩棘。他们的故事不仅是夫妻携手创业的典范，更是文化传承与家庭力量的最佳诠释。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14423351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14423351" style="width: 640px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423351-741720_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14423351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14423351-741720_medium.jpg" alt="" width="640" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14423351" class="wp-caption-text">廖夏子他们的故事，不仅是夫妻携手创业的典范，更是文化传承与家庭力量的最佳诠释。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖夏子曾说：“艺术家的特质，就是有一个梦想，就一定要圆梦。”正是这份对梦想的坚持与对家庭的热爱，让他们的生活充满意义，也让九鸟陶烧工作坊成为艺术与文化的灯塔。</p>
