<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新诗：溪亭清谈</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13798307-b9ff0504c4d4fc5c8cd7be66f50cbe06-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（画作作者：张松元 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<figure id="attachment_14404712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14404712" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14404712-915551292bf9e45da6cc21d32b38567b.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14404712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14404712-915551292bf9e45da6cc21d32b38567b.png" alt="" width="400" b="952" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14404712" class="wp-caption-text">（画作作者 张松元 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">枫红缱绻群峰<br />
这一瓣瓣绯红容颜<br />
是季节生命的底蕴<br />
姿态幻化万千</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">阳光从高大树影间筛落<br />
石径深处更有矮屋几间<br />
云岚无须赶路<br />
缓缓飘拂来此驻足</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">一份悠闲几分岑寂<br />
谁低吟这潜藏心灵奥室的绿意盎然<br />
潺潺溪水是至情至性的乐章<br />
两位隐士溪亭清谈<br />
看水流如梦幻泡影如何漂流到远洋</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">此刻何妨闭目冥想<br />
倏忽枝桠间传来清脆悦耳鸟啼<br />
那是大自然赠与的无价之宝<br />
深秋火红就这么融入天地茫茫</p>
