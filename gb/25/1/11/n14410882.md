<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>醉人的冬景 国王湖的翡翠奇境</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410883-738078-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>德国国王湖的翡翠奇境。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月11日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北◇报导）位于德国巴伐利亚州的国王湖（K?nigssee）是阿尔卑斯山脚下的一颗翡翠明珠，以其清澈碧绿的湖水、雄伟的山脉和悠久的历史文化闻名于世。这里不仅是一个自然奇观，更是一片充满灵性与人文故事的土地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410884-738079.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410884-738079-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410884" class="wp-caption-text">国王湖（K?nigssee）是阿尔卑斯山脚下的一颗翡翠明珠。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>空拍达人Nina于2025年1月10日展开了一场国王湖的冬季探访，她从奥地利的萨尔兹堡搭乘巴士840，穿越国境来到这片纯净之地。一场白雪铺陈的旅程，将国王湖最迷人的冬季风貌呈现在我们眼前。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410885" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410885-738080.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410885-738080-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410885" class="wp-caption-text">Nina从奥地利的萨尔兹堡搭乘巴士840，穿越国境来到这片纯净之地。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina说：“巴士840离开萨尔兹堡市区后，开始出现雪山，也开始飘着小雪花，迷人的冬天雪景。一下车踩在绵绵的雪上，一点都不冷喔。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410886-738081.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410886-738081-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410886" class="wp-caption-text">Nina：“一下车踩在绵绵的雪上，一点都不冷喔。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>圣巴多罗买教堂　静谧中的文化瑰宝</h2>
<p>国王湖的历史可以追溯至中世纪，湖岸著名的圣巴多罗买教堂（St. Bartholom?）建于12世纪，是湖区的重要文化地标。教堂独特的红色洋葱头穹顶与雪山相映成趣，让人仿佛置身于童话世界。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410887-738082.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410887-738082-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410887" class="wp-caption-text">国王湖的历史可以追溯至中世纪，湖岸著名的圣巴多罗买教堂（St. Bartholom?）建于12世纪。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina选择了冬季搭乘游船造访教堂。她特别提到，冬季的游船航程仅行驶到圣巴多罗买教堂，这段航行因湖区严格的环境保护政策，仅使用电动船，湖面清澈见底。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410888" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410888-738083.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14410888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410888-738083-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410888" class="wp-caption-text">冬季的游船航程仅行驶到圣巴多罗买教堂，这段航行因湖区严格的环境保护政策，仅使用电动船。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina描述道：“寒冬中的国王湖，遗世独立之感。行船中，寒冷的空气，船夫还是拿出了小喇叭，演绎一曲与山谷的对唱，好爱黑与白的倒影～”</p>
<p>国王湖著名的回声墙也成为旅途中难忘的亮点。船夫的演奏声与山谷间的回声交织，宛如一场自然与人类的合奏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410889" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410889-738084.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410889" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410889-738084-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410889" class="wp-caption-text">Nina描述道：“寒冬中的国王湖，遗世独立之感。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>地质奇观与壮丽景色</h2>
<p>国王湖由冰河时期的冰川活动所形成，是典型的冰蚀湖地形。湖泊狭长，深达192米，是德国最深的湖泊之一。湖岸的瓦茨曼山（Watzmann）主峰高耸入云，其三座山峰并列，被称为“瓦茨曼家族”，成为国王湖最具代表性的地质景观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410890-738085.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410890-738085-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410890" class="wp-caption-text">国王湖由冰河时期的冰川活动所形成，是典型的冰蚀湖地形。湖泊狭长，深达192米。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在冬季，雪覆山峦，湖面如镜，倒映着雪山和教堂的景色，呈现出一幅黑白分明的自然画作。Nina在这里捕捉到多张照片，将这一壮丽景色完美地保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410891-738086.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410891-738086-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410891" class="wp-caption-text">在冬季，雪覆山峦，湖面如镜，倒映着雪山和教堂的景色，呈现出一幅黑白分明的自然画作。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>冬日光影与午餐时光</h2>
<p>接近中午时分，阳光穿透云层，蓝天映照在湖面上，湖区的风貌随之改变。Nina选择享用了一道经典的“国王湖鳟鱼”（K?nigssee Forelle）午餐，这道菜是湖区的特色之一，鲜嫩的鱼肉搭配当地种植的马铃薯，让她倍感满足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410892" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410892-738087.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410892-738087-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410892" class="wp-caption-text">Nina选择享用了一道经典的“国王湖鳟鱼”（K?nigssee Forelle）午餐。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina分享道：“喜欢一早来时，飘着雪花，绵绵的雪地，寂静中的国王湖。太阳出来后又展现出另一个风貌，我爱国王湖。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410893-738088.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410893-738088-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410893" class="wp-caption-text">Nina分享道：“喜欢一早来时，飘着雪花，绵绵的雪地，寂静中的国王湖。太阳出来后又展现出另一个风貌。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>寂静与独享的瞬间</h2>
<p>Nina在旅程中还提到，冬季的国王湖相较于夏日更显静谧。夏日的湖岸熙熙攘攘，而冬天的湖边则显得天地苍茫，仿佛整片湖泊都属于自己。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410894-738089.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410894-738089-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410894" class="wp-caption-text">nina：“的湖边则显得天地苍茫，仿佛整片湖泊都属于自己。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nina:“好喜欢小雪花迎面飘来，夏天时熙熙攘攘的湖岸，此时只有我一人独享，天地苍茫。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410896-738091.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410896-738091-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410896" class="wp-caption-text">Nina:“好喜欢小雪花迎面飘来，夏天时熙熙攘攘的湖岸，此时只有我一人独享，天地苍茫。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冬季的国王湖是一场视觉与心灵的飨宴。在冰雪覆盖的国王湖畔，历史与自然交融，静谧与壮丽共存。Nina的旅程不仅记录了她眼中的湖光山色，也将国王湖这片纯净之地的文化与故事传递给我们。</p>
<p>如果你也向往远离喧嚣、沉浸在冬季大自然的怀抱，国王湖会是一个完美的选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410895-738090.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14410895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410895-738090-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410895" class="wp-caption-text">冬季的国王湖是一场视觉与心灵的飨宴。在冰雪覆盖的国王湖畔，历史与自然交融，静谧与壮丽共存。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
