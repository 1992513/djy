<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】旅行休旅混合体 2025 Subaru Outback Touring XT</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410989-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025 Subaru Outback Touring XT。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月11日讯】Subaru的汽车多样化，但它们都有共同特点，就是配备Boxer引擎，以及Symmetric AWD四驱系统，其中集Wagon与Crossover两者与一身的Outback，在操控及越野也同样出色。</p>
<p>Subaru在2019推出代号为BT的Outback，也是车系的第6代产品，上一次测试是2022年的Outback Wilderness，尺寸上Outback Wilderness比其他版Outback缩短了一点，而今年测到的是最高级版本Outback Touring XT，测试车采用墨绿色，汽车配上黑色的配件，相比起Wilderness版低调很多。虽然是低调不少，却不减Outback的越野硬朗形象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410974" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410974-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410974-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_04-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410974" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Subaru Outback Touring XT。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然Outback入门版是配置2.5L Boxer引擎，但Outback Touring XT是车系的最高级版本，所以使用的是较高级的2.4L 四缸水平对向Boxer引擎，并有涡轮增压系统，最大马力提升到260 hp，而峰值扭矩为277 lb-ft，于2,000转到4,800转可达到。名为Lineartronic的CVT变速箱是Outback的标配，由最入门到最高级版本的Outback都使用它，在转档时有更流畅的感觉，但加速力仍是有一点柔和，可使用方向盘后方的Paddle Shift，又或变速杆的M模式，作手动介入。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410975" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410975-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_15.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410975-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_15-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410975" class="wp-caption-text">2.5L Boxer引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Subaru的“对称全时四驱系统”(Symmetrical AWD)，对汽车加速、减速、转向及车辆抓地力各方面监察，从而提供最佳动力予每一车轮，投入使用至今已有40年以上历史。除此之外，Outback的“X-Mode”模式，启用时会对引擎、变速箱、前后轮轴、刹车及VDC等进行优化，使汽车在湿滑路面上有更好的抓地力，行车更稳定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410976" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410976-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410976-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_06-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410976" class="wp-caption-text">头灯组。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>EyeSight系统，在车内挡风玻璃中间后方，装有一对摄像头，可提供“预碰撞刹车”、“预碰撞动力管理”、“自适应巡航”及“偏离行车线警告”等功效，而且在美国则是标配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410977" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410977-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410977-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_16-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410977" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新的自适应巡航系统（Advanced Adaptive Cruise Control）可根据前车速度及预设距离，而自动减速或加速，以保持与前车距离，配合新加入的“车道居中（Lane Centering）”功能，便可成为“半自动驾驶”了，巡航系统的车速控制是电子式，每按一下是加或减5英里/时，既清楚又快捷。启动“车道居中”功能后，虽然系统会自动转动方向盘，以维持汽车在行车道的中央，但是需要驾车者不时给予反应，不然系统便会发出警告声响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410978" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410978-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410978-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_18-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410978" class="wp-caption-text">自动变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因为Touring XT是最高级版本，车内也使用Nappa皮革座椅，极为舒适。前排两座位均设有三段冷暖调节，方向盘也同样有加热功能，后排两侧座位同样有加热功能，在冬天就可齐齐享有温暖的旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410982" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410982-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410982-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_22-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410982" class="wp-caption-text">Nappa皮革座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台的萤幕作垂直摆放，尤如一个大尺寸iPad放在中间，运行Subaru Starlink系统，功能按键排列及图案都有如App一般，在底部也有“Home”键，Wilderness版加入了显示车前摄像头影像，在攀爬山坡时也可在萤幕看到前方地形，非常好用。此外，也有部分常用功能实体键设在萤幕两旁，如气温调节、音量及紧急车灯等。在中控台顶谓有红外线设备，用于监察驾驶者状态，例如汽车前行时驾驶者没有向前看，都会发出声响提示警告。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410983" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410983-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410983-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410983" class="wp-caption-text">直立萤幕运行Subaru Starlink系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Outback的Symmetrical AWD系统在市内表现极佳，但耗油量又可保持与同级汽车相约，这点是令人惊讶。然而，传言Outback的第7代将于明年下半年，以2026年款车型推出，但在官方网站上暂未看到消息！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14410984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14410984" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410984-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14410984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14410984-2025_Subaru_Outback_Touring_XT_12-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14410984" class="wp-caption-text">车尾灯组。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
