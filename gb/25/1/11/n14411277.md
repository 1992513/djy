<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狮潭乡桶柑草莓评鉴 农特产品展售添增年节欢乐气氛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411278-738130-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>县长推荐狮潭乡生产的草莓、桶柑品质优异。（苗县府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月11日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）狮潭乡“橘莓恋柑橘一桔go”苗栗县桶柑莓评鉴暨农特产品创新行销展售活动，1月11、12日在义民庙前广场热闹登场，安排的艺文表演、桶柑促销、酸柑茶DIY制作等，吸引不少游客参与，充满年节欢乐气氛，县长钟东锦推荐狮潭草莓、桶柑品质优异，欢迎到狮潭乡采果、品尝美食，体验客庄风情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14411279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14411279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411279-738131.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14411279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411279-738131-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14411279" class="wp-caption-text">表扬桶柑评鉴特等奖。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苗县是全台草莓最主要的专业栽培区，全县种植面积超过450公顷，占全台75％，其中狮潭乡种植约40公顷，年产量320公吨，狮潭乡同时也是苗县桶柑的主要产区之1，种植面积约120公顷，年产量1700公吨，县府为协助行销该乡的草莓、桶柑，每年补助乡农会办理橘莓恋活动，皆吸引大批游客参与，已形成当地年度盛会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14411280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14411280" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411280-738132.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14411280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411280-738132-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14411280" class="wp-caption-text">表扬桶柑评鉴头等奖。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14411281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14411281" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411281-738133.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14411281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411281-738133-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14411281" class="wp-caption-text">表扬桶柑评鉴头等奖。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的“橘莓恋?柑橘一桔go”活动，结合全县优质农特产品共同展售，同时推出发放农产品米食兑换券、“桔来Ya”─桔时到桶柑促销、手作主题DIY酸柑茶制作、写书法赠春联等活动，在舞台区亦安排艺文表演等，呈现年货大街的热闹情景，吸引不少游客参与。</p>
<p>在今天的开幕仪式中，县长钟东锦与县议员杨恭林、陈春暖、狮潭乡长刘淑能、乡代会主席林昌元、乡农会理事长古明育、常监谢壬滨、总干事黄焕祥、县农会总干事钟金光、南庄乡农会总干事陈乾安、头份市农会总干事罗清香、县府农业处副处长蔡政新等人共同启动这项活动，并颁奖苗县桶柑、狮潭草莓品鉴获奖农民。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14411283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14411283" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411283-738135.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14411283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411283-738135-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14411283" class="wp-caption-text">表扬草莓评鉴头等奖。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14411284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14411284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411284-738136.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14411284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14411284-738136-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14411284" class="wp-caption-text">表扬草莓评鉴头等。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>苗县桶柑评鉴：特等：三湾乡彭燕芳。头等：狮潭温兆泓、杨雯淇。贰等：狮潭叶兆棋、谢李库、温森霖。参等：狮潭黎氏丽水、吴辉虎、卓兰黄添林、头份陈柏勋。</p>
<p>狮潭草莓评鉴：特等：丁氏征。头等：沈木山、钟维祥。贰等：张炎宗、谢玉娇、曾道松。参等：谢壬滨、江怡蓁、曾明松、刘玟烜。</p>
