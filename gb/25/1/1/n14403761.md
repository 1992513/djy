<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>稻田化身梦幻乐园 田中稻草人艺术节粉美！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403762-735942-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>彰化田中在这个冬季举办一场视觉与情感的飨宴——大波斯菊与油菜花交织的12公顷绝美花海。（吴淑珠提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾彰化报导）彰化田中在这个冬季举办一场视觉与情感的飨宴——【2024田中稻草人艺术节】。从金黄色稻田的丰收，到稻草人的创意艺术，再到大波斯菊交织的12公顷绝美花海，田中这片土地将农村的朴实，转化为自然与艺术的完美结合。以“龙稻田中”为主题的今年艺术节，象征着丰收与祝福。</p>
<figure id="14403763" aria-describedby="caption-14403763" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403763-735943-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403763-735943-450x338.jpg" alt="以“龙稻田中”为主题的今年艺术节，象征着丰收与祝福。" width="601" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403763" class="wp-caption-text">以“龙稻田中”为主题的今年艺术节，象征着丰收与祝福。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>壮观视觉　摄影家吴淑珠镜头下的诗意田园</h2>
<p>田中稻草人艺术节不仅吸引了大批游客，也吸引了许多热爱自然的摄影师。以细腻、浪漫风格著称的摄影家吴淑珠，用镜头捕捉了这场艺术飨宴的精髓。她以柔和的光线运用，让花海中的稻草装置彷如跃然画布的艺术品。无论是稻草人背后蔚蓝的晴空，还是近景花朵间的光影变化，她都精准掌握了自然之美与人文情感的平衡。</p>
<figure id="14403764" aria-describedby="caption-14403764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403764-735944-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403764-735944-450x338.jpg" alt="以细腻、浪漫风格著称的摄影家吴淑珠，用镜头捕捉了这场艺术飨宴的精髓。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403764" class="wp-caption-text">以细腻、浪漫风格著称的摄影家吴淑珠，用镜头捕捉了这场艺术飨宴的精髓。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>吴淑珠的细腻创作法</h2>
<p><strong>1. 光线掌控：</strong></p>
<p>吴淑珠善于利用清晨与黄昏的斜射光，让花朵与稻草装置呈现更柔和的光影效果。她提到：“早晨的阳光可以勾勒出稻草人轮廓的细节，让整体画面充满层次感。”</p>
<figure id="14403765" aria-describedby="caption-14403765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403765-735945-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403765-735945-450x338.jpg" alt="吴淑珠善于利用清晨与黄昏的斜射光，让花朵与稻草装置呈现更柔和的光影效果。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403765" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑珠善于利用清晨与黄昏的斜射光，让花朵与稻草装置呈现更柔和的光影效果。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 构图秘诀：</strong></p>
<p>她偏好使用三分法构图，将主体如稻草人或花海放置在画面的三分之一处，借此营造视觉上的平衡。特别是红色鸟居的构图，她利用其强烈色彩对比，成功吸引视线。</p>
<figure id="14403766" aria-describedby="caption-14403766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403766-735946-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403766-735946-450x338.jpg" alt="特别是红色鸟居的构图，吴淑珠利用其强烈色彩对比，成功吸引视线。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403766" class="wp-caption-text">特别是红色鸟居的构图，吴淑珠利用其强烈色彩对比，成功吸引视线。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3. 对焦与散景：</strong></p>
<p>吴淑珠以大光圈拍摄，将前景花朵对焦清晰，背景则柔化成梦幻的散景，营造出层层叠叠的浪漫意境。</p>
<figure id="14403767" aria-describedby="caption-14403767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403767-735947-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403767-735947-450x675.jpg" alt="吴淑珠将波斯菊突显出来。" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403767" class="wp-caption-text">吴淑珠将波斯菊突显出来。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4.?善用颜色：</strong></p>
<p>田中的大波斯菊，色彩鲜艳明亮，吴淑珠巧妙调整白平衡，让花海的粉红与金黄在照片中更为动人。</p>
<figure id="14403768" aria-describedby="caption-14403768" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403768-735948-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403768-735948-450x675.jpg" alt="层次分明，把波斯菊的娇嫩聚焦出来。" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403768" class="wp-caption-text">层次分明，把波斯菊的娇嫩聚焦出来。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>艺术装置与花海的融合之美</h2>
<p>稻草人艺术节的装置艺术，从巨大的稻草雕塑到趣味十足的卡通形象，都展现了农村的创意与幽默。特别是花海中央的红色鸟居，成为整场活动的视觉亮点，象征了传统与自然的融合。</p>
<figure id="14403769" aria-describedby="caption-14403769" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403769-735949-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403769-735949-450x338.jpg" alt="将稻草放置中间，被粉红包围的感觉，好美丽。" width="601" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403769" class="wp-caption-text">将稻草放置中间，被粉红包围的感觉，好美丽。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花海本身则如诗如画，12公顷的花田中，大波斯菊在微风中摇曳生姿。当游客行走其间，仿佛置身于流动的田园画卷之中。</p>
<figure id="14403771" aria-describedby="caption-14403771" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403771-735951-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403771-735951-450x675.jpg" alt="在这里可以专心拍好美丽的波斯菊。" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403771" class="wp-caption-text">在这里可以专心拍好美丽的波斯菊。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>游客体验 沉浸式田野之旅</h2>
<p>参观者可以与稻草艺术近距离互动，拍摄独一无二的田园照。无论是全家福，还是文青风自拍，每一张照片都见证了这片土地的丰饶与创意。</p>
<figure id="14403772" aria-describedby="caption-14403772" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403772-735952-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403772-735952-450x338.jpg" alt="数大便是美，花海壮阔又漂亮。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403772" class="wp-caption-text">数大便是美，花海壮阔又漂亮。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>不只是美景 更是一份情感连结</h2>
<p>稻草人艺术节并非仅仅一场视觉的盛宴，它更是一份情感的连结。一位来自北部的游客分享：“这里的景色让我想起了小时候在乡下的回忆，那种纯朴的感觉，真的很疗愈。”</p>
<figure id="14403770" aria-describedby="caption-14403770" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403770-735950-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403770-735950-450x338.jpg" alt="红色鸟居，搭配云彩和波斯菊，这三者不冲突、不抢戏。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403770" class="wp-caption-text">红色鸟居，搭配云彩和波斯菊，这三者不冲突、不抢戏。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影家吴淑珠也在作品中传递了相同的情感。她说：“我希望能用照片唤起大家对自然的珍惜，对农村的尊重。”透过她的镜头，我们看见的不仅是花田与稻草人，还有那份静静流淌的乡土情怀。</p>
<p>无论是捕捉壮观的自然美景，还是细细品味农村的朴实魅力，这场艺术节都值得一访再访。</p>
<figure id="14403773" aria-describedby="caption-14403773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403773-735953-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14403773-735953-450x338.jpg" alt="细细品味农村的朴实魅力。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14403773" class="wp-caption-text">细细品味农村的朴实魅力。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
