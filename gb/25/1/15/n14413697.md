<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>克拉约瓦艺术博物馆：华丽的罗马尼亚宫殿</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14413720-b6331f6a0d3518b02d73a8026a3a7c52-600x400.png" />
<h6>康斯坦丁?米哈伊尔宫殿体现了完美的对称。从宫殿外面，可以看到古典科林斯柱、华丽的铁制阳台，附有镀金装饰的拱形窗户、镀金托臂和覆盖石板的斜屋顶塔楼。(Ana del Castillo/ Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>克拉约瓦艺术博物馆（Craiova Art Museum）是罗马尼亚的&#33879;名景点，收藏着该国一些最重要、最精美的绘画和雕塑，时间跨度从中世纪到现代。</p>
<p>这座博物馆位于康斯坦丁?米哈伊尔（Constantin Mihail）宫殿内，其建筑本身便是一件令人惊叹的杰作。它是克拉约瓦名流米哈伊尔?康斯坦丁（Michael Constantine）聘请法国建筑师保罗?戈特罗（Paul Gottereau）设计、于1898年到1907年建造的。在1950年代，康斯坦丁?米哈伊尔宫殿成了一座艺术博物馆。目前，馆内藏有荷兰、佛兰德斯、意大利和法国家具以及八千多件欧洲和罗马尼亚本国的艺术品。</p>
<p>这座巨大的宫殿由29个房间和附属建筑组成，设计不拘一格，融合了法国新古典主义及以奢华和宏伟为特征的巴洛克晚期元素。</p>
<p>据说建筑师戈特罗当时参考了卢瓦尔河谷的舍维尼宫（Cheverny Palace）等法国宫殿的设计理念，他的目标是：实现完美的对称，同时展现其主人的富裕奢华。</p>
<p>整个宫殿多处使用了从意大利开采的卡拉拉大理石，包括阶梯、宽阔的柱状栏杆、雕刻的涡旋装饰以及深嵌墙壁的舷窗。大理石带来的恢宏感为宫殿增色许多。</p>
<figure style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740059-2-shutterstock_2160638701.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740059-2-shutterstock_2160638701.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="452" b="678" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马车门廊（porte cochere）是一条有顶棚的通道，宾客到达时会由此经过。上面的山墙雕塑装饰着希腊式图案，包括植物和动物。(Paul Horia Malaianu/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740060-3-shutterstock_2160239701.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740060-3-shutterstock_2160239701.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="451" b="676" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">整个宫殿，包括阶梯在内，都使用从意大利开采的卡拉拉大理石。宽阔的柱状栏杆、雕刻的涡旋装饰，以及深嵌墙壁、带窗框与窗格的舷窗，美化了观者上行步入宫殿时的景观。(Paul Horia Malaianu/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>宫殿前门由锻铁制造，拱形设计有圆形涡旋装饰，古朴大气。这种大门需要经过复杂艰难的加热、锤击、成型和安装一系列过程才能完工。大门两侧的柱子上嵌有铁顶造型，上有雕刻的狼头和其它装饰物。</p>
<figure style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740061-4-shutterstock_2160239691.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740061-4-shutterstock_2160239691.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="602" b="402" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宫殿前门由锻铁制造，古朴大气，兼具艺术性和实用性。(Paul Horia Malaianu/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>参观者步入大厅，便被繁复的精美设计所包围和震撼。中庭天花板的一扇窗户两侧镶嵌着巨大的镀金徽章。大厅的楼梯与众不同，由铺有红地毯的大理石台阶和锻铁栏杆组成。从各个角度都可以看到支撑拱形开口的古典科林斯柱。</p>
<figure style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740062-5-shutterstock_1521744338.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740062-5-shutterstock_1521744338.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="453" b="679" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宫殿大厅的楼梯由铺有红地毯的大理石台阶和锻铁栏杆组成。中庭天花板的一扇窗户两侧镶嵌着巨大的镀金徽章。从各个角度都可以看到支撑拱形开口的古典科林斯柱。(Ana del Castillo/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14414538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14414538" style="width: 605px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414538-shutterstock_1328738006.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14414538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14414538-shutterstock_1328738006-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="605" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14414538" class="wp-caption-text">两扇门位于雕像壁龛的两侧。门的中间和旁边都有带装饰镶边的面板。每块面板的底部排列着较小、同样有镶边的垂直面板。配套的门上方是厚重的花边装饰。(Ana del Castillo/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>大厅两侧的水晶和黄铜吊灯突显庄重的氛围。二楼阳台的锻铁栏杆采用涡旋装饰，周围环绕着精致的镀金花环和装饰带，每个细节都显出巧思与华贵。</p>
<figure style="width: 605px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740064-7-shutterstock_614501528.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://www.theepochtimes.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimg.theepochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F10%2F11%2Fid5740064-7-shutterstock_614501528.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" width="605" b="404" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大厅两侧的水晶和黄铜吊灯突显庄重的氛围。上方是二楼的阳台，华丽的锻铁栏杆采用涡旋装饰，周围环绕着精致的镀金花环和装饰带。二楼一扇窗户的中央悬挂着一个装饰精美的镀金窗框，框内有一个人脸，两侧点缀着顽皮的小天使。(Ana del Castillo/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>宫殿的舞厅现在陈列着艺术品。一盏穆拉诺水晶灯，从模压镀金的天花板奖章（ceiling medallion）垂挂下来。同样模压镀金的墙框则凸显了墙壁装饰的细致之处。</p>
