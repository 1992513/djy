<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗栗公私立中小学校长会议 携手合作为孩子点燃希望的火炬</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419653-740877-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>苗栗公私立中小学校长会议  携手合作为孩子点燃希望的火炬（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月22日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗县113学年度第2学期公私立中小学校长会议，1月22日上午在育达科技大学国际会议厅召开，县长钟东锦颁奖表扬绩优人员，期勉校长们扮演好教育领航者的角色，带领教师推动创新教学，培养学生创造力，为孩子点燃希望的火炬。</p>
<p>校长会议由县长钟东锦主持，育达科大校长吴菊、县府教育处长叶芯慧、人事处长张翠芬、财政处长郭春美、主计处副处长王志成等人列席，全县157位公私立中小学校长出席。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419652-740876.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419652-740876-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419652" class="wp-caption-text">颁奖终身学习奖励绩效卓越学校2（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14419651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419651-740875.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419651-740875-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419651" class="wp-caption-text">颁奖终身学习奖励绩效卓越学校1（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14419650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419650-740874.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419650-740874-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419650" class="wp-caption-text">颁奖儿童课后照顾特优学校.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会中，县长钟东锦颁奖表扬荣获教育部跨领域美感教育绩优学校、苗县终身学习绩效卓越学校、中辍生预防及复学辅导工作绩优学校、阅读推动计划督导与访视特优学校、交通安全教育线上访视特优学校等，并与校长座谈交流意见。</p>
<p>有关校长反映希望分校新增护理人员问题，县长钟东锦回应将会研议处理，做适当安排，学校有任何需求，县府一定会做好后勤工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419649-740873.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419649-740873-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419649" class="wp-caption-text">家庭教育辅导特优学校（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>此外，教育处提出重要施政报告，并由学管科长林采香专题说明国中会考计分改制5级分的措施、县府配套方针等；并邀请政风处科长林淑静说明公职人员利益冲突回避法及财产申报等重要事宜，以避免逾期被裁罚。</p>
<p>县长钟东锦指出，为因应数位化与跨域学习的趋势，县府积极投入智慧学习平台、大屏设备及STEAM跨域教育中心等资源，全面提升教学环境与学习品质，为孩子的未来奠定坚实基础。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419646-740870.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419646-740870-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419646" class="wp-caption-text">表扬中辍生预防及复学辅导绩优人员.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14419645" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419645" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419645-740869.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419645-740869-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419645" class="wp-caption-text">颁奖推动阅读特优学校.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14419644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419644-740868.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419644-740868-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419644" class="wp-caption-text">县长表扬荣获教育部美感绩优学校蕉埔国小.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钟东锦引用教育家杜威的名言：“环境改变了，人也跟着变了。”强调教育不仅是知识的传递，更需创造有助于学生成长的学习环境，他期待校长们以教育领航者的角色，带领教师团队充分运用县府提供的资源，推动创新教学，培养学生批判思维与创造力，让每一间教室都成为充满活力的学习场域。</p>
<p>他并感谢校长与教师的辛劳付出，期待大家继续携手合作，为苗栗孩子点燃希望的火炬，共同书写教育的新篇章。</p>
