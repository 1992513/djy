<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“台湾灯会在桃园”12项周边商品亮相 等你来收藏</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418601-740418-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-棒球帽。（桃园市文化局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月21日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）2025台湾灯会即将于桃园盛大展开，除了丰富的主题灯区展演，当然也少不了别具创意的周边商品！桃园文化局推出包含与知名在地品牌合作的“千塘纪念酒”、创意颈枕、手机支架、夜光徽章、双面帆布袋、纪念T-shirt等共12项特色商品，让民众留下美好的灯会纪念！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14418595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418595" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418595-740412.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418595-740412.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418595" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-千塘纪念酒。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了让每位参与者都能成为灯会展出的一部分，并呼应“光聚千塘串桃园”的主题，本次灯会商品以“Light up Together与光共舞”为设计核心概念，发展出“亮点推广互动”“实用商品”及“设计师单品”三个系列，将灯会专属IP融入日常生活，成为连结人与人的媒介。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14418597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418597" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418597-740414.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418597-740414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418597" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-反光双面手提袋。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如“千塘纪念酒”选用桃园知名酒厂“邓爸麦酒”，搭配蛇年意象专属木盒包装，镂空设计有如元宵节灯笼；“千塘创意颈枕”则可翻转为台湾灯会公仔，为实用的颈枕增添文化意义；“雷射胸章组”更联名萌军“宇宙版ㄚ桃园哥”，极具收集、纪念价值！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14418598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418598" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418598-740415.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418598-740415.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418598" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-夜光胸章。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14418599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418599" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418599-740416.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418599-740416.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="683" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418599" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-保温瓶。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>设计团队期望以多元的面向开展，透过光感材质形成流动式的互动展演，创造台湾灯会共同价值，更让光影记忆能够延续到生活的每个角落。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14418600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418600" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418600-740417.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418600-740417.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418600" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-纹身贴纸。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14418596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418596" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418596-740413.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418596-740413.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418596" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-T-shirt。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾灯会睽违9年重返桃园，于2月12日至23日盛大登场，展区位于桃园高铁站前广场及桃园机场捷运A19桃园体育园区站周边场域，灯会以“光聚千塘串桃园”为主题，透过光影及灯艺的展演，呈现桃园“台湾国门”形象、多元文化、丰富观光资源及产业特色，展现出桃园多元文化之都、科技之城的独特魅力，为民众带来不一样的光影艺术体验，邀请各地民众前来共襄盛举！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14418602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418602" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418602-740419.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418602-740419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418602" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-雷射胸章。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14418604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418604" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418604-740420.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418604-740420.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="672" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418604" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-压克力吊饰。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灯会周边商品即将在1月22日于合作设计团队电商通路“趣活cheer for网路商城”开放预购。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14418605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14418605" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418605-740421.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14418605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14418605-740421.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14418605" class="wp-caption-text">2025台湾灯会在桃园文创商品-磁吸手机支架。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，台湾灯会开展期间（2/12-2/23），灯会展区、中原文创园区、马祖新村及趣活全台实体商店也都有贩售多项周边商品，欢迎民众届时前往选购。</p>
