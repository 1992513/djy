<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>格林德瓦的冬日童话 与韩剧《爱的迫降》的浪漫记忆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419016-740666-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>无论是滑雪、探索雪雕、还是感受韩剧浪漫，格林德瓦的冬天绝对值得每一位旅行者亲身体验。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年01月21日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）在瑞士阿尔卑斯山脉深处，格林德瓦（Grindelwald）以其如梦如幻的雪景，成为摄影爱好者的天堂。Nina，一位热爱捕捉自然美景来自台湾的摄影家，分享了她在格林德瓦的旅程。在这个被白雪覆盖的小镇，冰雕、滑雪者、木屋与雪山，仿佛构成了一幅纯白的冬日画作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419001" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419001-740653.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14419001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419001-740653-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419001" class="wp-caption-text">Nina，一位热爱捕捉自然美景来自台湾的摄影家，分享了她在格林德瓦的旅程。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>格林德瓦的清晨，Nina描述道：“山坡上错落一幢幢的小木屋，就像进入童话世界。”她特别提到2020年启用的艾格快线（Eiger Express），能迅速将旅客从格林德瓦转运站送到艾格冰川站，减少了上山的时间，让她能更快捕捉到壮丽的雪山景致。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419002" style="width: 532px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419002-740654.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14419002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419002-740654-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="532" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419002" class="wp-caption-text">Nina描述道：“山坡上错落一幢幢的小木屋，就像进入童话世界。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>韩国人气剧集《爱的迫降》取景地</h2>
<p>韩剧《爱的迫降》将格林德瓦和其周边地区，推向了全球粉丝的视野。剧中男女主角在小夏戴克车站（Kleine Scheidegg）的初次相遇场景，令人印象深刻。Nina特地造访了这里，并分享了她的感受：“虽然场景平静，但四周的雪山与铁路完美融为一体，让人不禁想起剧中那些感人至深的片段。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419003-740655.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419003-740655-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419003" class="wp-caption-text">Nina：“虽然场景平静，但四周的雪山与铁路完美融为一体，让人不禁想起剧中那些感人至深的片段。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>冰雕艺术与滑雪的魅力</h2>
<p>在冬季的格林德瓦，雪雕展览是一项不可错过的活动。Nina提到：“成群游客聚集在格林德瓦车站前，欣赏着各种造型的雪雕。这些冰雕向艾格峰（Eiger）北面的壮丽景致致敬。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419006-740658.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419006-740658-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419006" class="wp-caption-text">在冬季的格林德瓦，雪雕展览是一项不可错过的活动。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而作为著名的滑雪胜地，格林德瓦吸引了无数滑雪爱好者。Nina也在旅程中拍摄了滑雪缆车与滑雪者的画面，特别是前往First地区的悬崖步道。她回忆道：“挑战胆量的悬崖步道，能俯瞰群山与村落，全景令人屏息。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419007-740659.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419007-740659-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419007" class="wp-caption-text">Nina也在旅程中拍摄了滑雪缆车与滑雪者的画面。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>格林德瓦的日与夜</h2>
<p>在格林德瓦的夜晚，灯光与雪景交织出梦幻的氛围。Nina捕捉到了一张迷人的照片，一座彩灯点缀的咖啡壶雪雕在夜色中格外耀眼。她描述道：“这是冬天特有的魔力，无论是滑雪后的放松，还是单纯漫步于冰雪小镇，这里的每一刻都值得纪录。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419005" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419005-740657.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14419005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419005-740657-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419005" class="wp-caption-text">Nina提到：“成群游客聚集在格林德瓦车站前，欣赏着各种造型的雪雕。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>少女峰：冬日之巅</h2>
<p>从格林德瓦前往少女峰（Jungfraujoch）是旅程的高潮，然而这次经历却因天气而变得特别。Nina说：“刚到山上时，大雪纷飞，一片白茫茫。这是我人生中第一次那么不喜欢下雪。”虽然天气不佳，她仍努力捕捉到壮观的缆车景色，并在下山后遇见晴朗的天气，俯瞰山脉的景象壮丽无比。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419010" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419010-740662.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14419010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419010-740662-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419010" class="wp-caption-text">Nina说：“刚到山上时，大雪纷飞，一片白茫茫。这是我人生中第一次那么不喜欢下雪。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>格林德瓦的独特体验</h2>
<p>Nina还分享了她造访First地区的冒险经历。这里不仅以悬崖步道闻名，还提供如山地卡丁车、滑板车等多样化的户外活动。她说：“我们尝试了悬崖步道，挑战胆量之余，也享受了壮丽的山景，这种感觉难以用言语形容。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419015" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419015-740665.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14419015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419015-740665-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419015" class="wp-caption-text">Nina分享了她造访First地区的冒险经历。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14419008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419008" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419008-740660.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14419008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419008-740660-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419008" class="wp-caption-text">Nina回忆道：“挑战胆量的悬崖步道，能俯瞰群山与村落，全景令人屏息。”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了极限活动，Nina提到格林德瓦的美食也令人印象深刻。在雪景围绕的餐厅里，享用热腾腾的瑞士餐点成为旅程中的亮点。她说：“每一口都能感受到奶香与温暖，这种体验无法复制。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419012-740663.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419012-740663-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419012" class="wp-caption-text">在雪景围绕的餐厅里，享用瑞士餐点成为旅程中的亮点。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>格林德瓦与韩剧迷的连结</h2>
<p>对于《爱的迫降》迷来说，格林德瓦无疑是重温剧中场景的最佳选择。Nina建议游客可以规划一日游，从格林德瓦搭乘火车前往剧中取景的各大地点，不仅能欣赏自然美景，还能重现剧中经典片段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14419014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14419014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419014-740664.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14419014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14419014-740664-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14419014" class="wp-caption-text">从格林德瓦搭乘火车前往剧中取景的各大地点，不仅能欣赏自然美景，还能重现剧中经典片段。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>格林德瓦以其迷人的冬季景色、丰富的户外活动与韩剧记忆，吸引着无数游客与摄影家。Nina的旅程为我们呈现了这片雪地童话的全貌，从冬日的纯粹之美，到韩剧场景中的浪漫，她的每一张照片都像是一封写给大自然的情书，邀请我们一同前往这片梦幻之地。无论是滑雪、探索雪雕、还是感受韩剧浪漫，格林德瓦的冬天绝对值得每一位旅行者亲身体验。</p>
