<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英崙余风采堂春宴 贺年不忘敬老扶幼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466186-DSC02945-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙余风采堂3月22日晚上举办春宴。公所元老、职员及舞狮团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年03月25日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“希望借这次的春宴，能增进彼此之间的情感交流，大家对公所有进一步的认识。”纽英崙余风采堂主席余立昌说。
</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">3月22日晚上，余风采堂在波士顿华埠的帝苑酒楼举办蛇年春宴，并向九旬长者和优秀学子赠送红包，发扬敬老扶幼之美德。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466184-DSC02933.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466184-DSC02933-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466184" class="wp-caption-text">“余南狮”舞狮贺岁，春宴开场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466187-DSC02948.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466187-DSC02948-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466187" class="wp-caption-text">余氏妇女组成员献唱贺年歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400"><strong>各地宗亲</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>聚会贺年</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400">今年的纽英崙余风采堂春宴，一如去年，没邀请侨社和政界嘉宾，只有堂内宗亲和他们私人邀请的友人参加。但宴会厅内仍汇聚300多位来宾，包括波士顿地区余氏同宗，也有来自纽约、旧金山等地的全美余风采堂代表。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">余立昌谈到“进一步认识”公所，他笑言，很多人觉得要参加公所，就必须通晓中文；但其实很多会员都并非在中文环境成长，包括在场的两位全美余氏元老——余健全和余宏基，都是“在美国土生土长的竹笙”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466200" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466200-DSC03111.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466200-DSC03111-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466200" class="wp-caption-text">主席余立昌除了致辞之外，还上台献唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466188-DSC02978.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466188-DSC02978-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466188" class="wp-caption-text">余氏妇女组在春宴中表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">其他参与晚宴的“竹笙”宗亲，还包括纽英崙余风采堂另一位主席余雅伦，中华广教学校副校长余绍强，还有主动出资为公所建筑油漆的Paul Yee父女等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">“呼吁每一位余姓乡亲，往公所走一走，看一看，参加各项公所的活动，欢迎有能力者加入公所的行列，壮大公所。”余立昌说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466183-DSC02896.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466183-DSC02896-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466183" class="wp-caption-text">晚会主持人是余艳玲（左）和余绍强。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">当晚，全美余风采堂元老余超群、余健全、余宏基都上台致辞，为来宾们送上新年祝福。来自旧金山的余健全说，这是他第一次来波士顿参加春宴，感到非常高兴。来自纽约的余宏基，是元老团最年轻的成员，他以英语开场，也以粤语道贺“恭喜发财”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466189-DSC02989.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466189-DSC02989-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466189" class="wp-caption-text">元老余超群致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466190-DSC02990.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466190-DSC02990-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466190" class="wp-caption-text">元老余健全致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466191-DSC02993.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466191-DSC02993-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466191" class="wp-caption-text">元老余宏基致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400"><strong>喜发红包</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>歌舞欢欣</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400">这场周六晚间举办的春宴，由余艳玲和余绍强主持。晚会节目由“余南狮”舞狮贺岁开场。五头彩狮在锣鼓声中跳跃进场，挂出贺年横幅，庆祝蛇年新春。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">接下来，余风采堂妇女组献唱两首贺年歌曲，以及伴随《旧欢如梦》歌曲表演舞蹈，亦收获满堂掌声与喝彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466195" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466195-DSC03034.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466195-DSC03034-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466195" class="wp-caption-text">余氏妇女组在春宴中表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466194" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466194-DSC03016.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466194-DSC03016-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466194" class="wp-caption-text">余宝爱献唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">在依循传统颁发红包的环节，主持人感谢余建元家族慷慨解囊，连续第八年为攻读硕士学位的同宗青年提供奖学金。今年获赠奖学金红包的年轻人，是在哈佛大学就读电脑科学研究生的余柏豪（Jimmy Yee）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466197" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466197-DSC03050.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466197-DSC03050-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466197" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛大学研究声余柏豪（Jimmy Yee，左）收到奖学金红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466198" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466198-DSC03061.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466198-DSC03061-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466198" class="wp-caption-text">优秀余氏青年获赠奖学金红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">其他七名获赠红包的青年，都是在大学内获得3.5以上GPA或校内奖学金的优秀学生。他们分别是余嘉乐（Brandon Yee）、余明乐（Ethan Yii）、余慧欣（Amanda Yee）、余嘉慧（Angela Yee）、余汉恩（Grace Yee）、余汉荣（Thomas Yee）和余正跃（Zhengyue Yu）。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">在敬老方面，三位九旬耆英，余景新、国济夫人、苏德，也都获赠红包。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466193" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466193" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466193-DSC03000.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466193-DSC03000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466193" class="wp-caption-text">九旬耆英获赠敬老红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466196" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466196-DSC03041.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466196-DSC03041-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466196" class="wp-caption-text">余氏元老、职员们上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">晚宴期间，元老及职员们上台向来宾敬酒，还热情献唱歌曲。主席余雅伦扮作“财神”，游走宴席之间，向大家派发糖果，吸引来宾们纷纷与他合影。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">在卡拉OK环节，余宝爱、余翠梅、余丽媖等资深成员都上台献唱歌曲。台下舞池还有不少来宾携手跳舞。现场气氛非常热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466199" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466199-DSC03082.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466199-DSC03082-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466199" class="wp-caption-text">主席余雅伦扮作“财神”，向来宾们送糖果。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466201-DSC03118.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466201-DSC03118-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466201" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们热情演唱《朋友》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466202" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466202-DSC03124.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466202-DSC03124-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466202" class="wp-caption-text">华林武馆的余翠梅（左）献唱歌曲，得到献花。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466203" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466203-DSC03135.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466203-DSC03135-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466203" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们在舞池欢乐歌舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">全美余风采堂认北宋“忠襄公”余靖为祖先。后人至今通过他留下的《武溪集》，学习余公的原则，正如余风采堂中锦旗所写：“武溪长流，风采永续”。◇</p>
