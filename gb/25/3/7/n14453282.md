<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>雪白世界的召唤 藏王树冰的奇幻邂逅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453283-750003-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本藏王树冰的奇幻邂逅。（nina Chen提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年03月07日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）每年冬季，日本山形县藏王连峰的树冰奇观如约而至，吸引无数旅人前来朝圣。而这一次，摄影家 Nina 也踏上了这趟白色梦境之旅，与大自然的冰雪雕塑来场最纯粹的邂逅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453284-750004.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453284-750004-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453284" class="wp-caption-text">摄影家 Nina 踏上了这趟白色梦境之旅，与大自然的冰雪雕塑来场最纯粹的邂逅。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>藏王雪怪的传奇</h2>
<p>
藏王树冰，又称“雪怪（Snow Monsters）”，是由于强烈的西伯利亚寒流携带水汽，在零下的严寒气温与猛烈的山风作用下，使得亚高山带的针叶树层层结冰，形成形态各异、仿佛巨型怪兽的奇特景观。最佳观赏期通常落在1月下旬至2月，而 Nina 这次的旅程则是在三月初，虽然雪怪们已开始有些消瘦，但仍保有壮观的姿态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453297" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453297-750018.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453297-750018-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453297" class="wp-caption-text">在零下的严寒气温与猛烈的山风作用下，使得亚高山带的针叶树层层结冰，形成形态各异。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14453286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453286-750005.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453286-750005-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453286" class="wp-caption-text">藏王树冰，又称“雪怪（Snow Monsters）”。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>出发，搭乘缆车迈向树冰之境</h2>
旅程从“藏王温泉缆车站”开始，Nina 与朋友们搭上通往“藏王树冰高原站”的缆车，一路上欣赏着高山树木渐渐被冰雪包裹的变化。然而，三月的天气变幻莫测，大片雪雾让视线受阻，让缆车上的景色变得朦胧神秘。朋友们因寒风刺骨、装备不足而选择留在缆车站内取暖，Nina 则决定独自一人勇闯这片冰雪王国。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453288-750008.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453288-750008-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453288" class="wp-caption-text">Nina从缆车看到的窗外景象。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14453287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453287-750007.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453287-750007-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453287" class="wp-caption-text">Nina与友人在“藏王树冰高原站”合影。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14453289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453289" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453289-750009.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14453289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453289-750009-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453289" class="wp-caption-text">“大片雪雾让视线受阻，让缆车上的景色变得朦胧神秘。”Nina说。（曹新出提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>孤身寻找雪怪——树冰的壮阔世界</h2>
漫步在皑皑白雪间，Nina 眼前的世界仿佛静止，只有呼啸的寒风与脚下细碎的雪声。面对高达数公尺的雪怪，她笑称：“大家说我的树冰看起来比他们从缆车上看到的更大，或许是因为我亲自走进去寻找吧！”</p>
<p>由于风雪不断，行走并不容易，每走一步都像是在与大地搏斗。当她终于抵达树冰原的标志时，迎面而来的狂风几乎将她吹倒。即便如此，这份亲身接触雪怪的体验仍让她觉得无比值得。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453291-750011.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453291-750011-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453291" class="wp-caption-text">“遇到大风雪，白茫茫一片。”Nina表示。（曹新出提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14453290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453290" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453290-750010.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14453290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453290-750010-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453290" class="wp-caption-text">Nina表示保暖装备一定要齐全，不要穿布鞋。（曹新出提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>极寒中的奇幻体验</h2>
这场探险并非一帆风顺，雪原的道路布满深坑，让她每一步都必须格外小心，避免滑落。她形容道：“每一只雪怪旁边都有一圈深坑，这让我无法靠得太近，但也显示出这里的安全措施做得很好。”即使风雪再猛烈，亲眼见证这片壮观景象的激动，让她忘却寒冷，只想用手机记录这难得的时刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453292-750012.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453292-750012-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453292" class="wp-caption-text">这场探险并非一帆风顺，雪原的道路布满深坑，让Nina每一步都必须格外小心（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>风雪下的坚持——见证大地的壮丽</h2>
在这场大风雪中，藏王山顶的视线几乎完全被白雾吞噬，让她的探索变得更具挑战性。她回忆道：“当我终于走上最后几步，却不断滑下来，最后干脆用屁股噜上去，才成功抵达目标！”这股不放弃的精神，让她得以真正置身于雪怪环绕的梦幻场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453293-750013.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453293-750013-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453293" class="wp-caption-text">Nina回忆道：“当我终于走上最后几步，却不断滑下来，最后干脆用屁股噜上去，才成功抵达目标！”（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>带着满满感动下山</h2>
回程时，Nina 沿着原路小心翼翼地踏上归途，内心仍充满着与雪怪相遇的悸动。她笑说：“其实来回只走了不到50分钟，但这段路却让我永生难忘！”</p>
<p>回到缆车站，她与朋友们再次聚首，分享着刚刚的冒险故事。这场独自探索的经历，让她更加体会到，真正的壮丽风景，不是仅仅透过窗户观看，而是勇敢地踏入其中，亲自感受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453294" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453294-750014.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453294-750014-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453294" class="wp-caption-text">回程时，Nina 沿着原路小心翼翼地踏上归途，内心仍充满着与雪怪相遇的悸动。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>雪白世界的梦幻记忆</h2>
藏王树冰的旅程，对 Nina 而言，不仅仅是一场摄影之旅，更是一场与大自然深度交流的体验。她用双脚丈量这片冰雪奇境，用手机记录下大自然鬼斧神工的美丽，也用心感受了这场独一无二的冒险。</p>
<p>这次的旅程告一段落，但 Nina 知道，这片雪白的奇幻世界，将永远留存在她的记忆与影像之中，成为生命中最值得珍藏的片刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453295" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453295-750015.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453295-750015-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453295" class="wp-caption-text">藏王树冰的旅程，对 Nina 而言，不仅仅是一场摄影之旅，更是一场与大自然深度交流的体验。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
