<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>巴黎时装周 蔡依林、林志玲同场观秀造型曝光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454962-2503100254251487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>林志玲（左）与蔡依林身穿2025_早秋系列服装出席巴黎时装秀。（VALENTINO／公关提供／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年03月10日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）2025秋冬巴黎时装周正进行中，近日各家精品举办大秀，其中VALENTINO（范伦铁诺）秀场星光闪耀，迎来两位台湾女星，分别是品牌代言人蔡依林，以及“第一名模”林志玲，成为秀场活动焦点。</p>
<p>本季秀场活动由创意总监<span lang="EN-US">Alessandro Michele</span>打造，秀上服装透过层叠的剪裁、半透明材质与戏剧化的造型作为主要的设计重点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14454965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14454965" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454965-2503100254301487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14454965" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454965-2503100254301487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="679" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14454965" class="wp-caption-text">VALENTINO品牌代言人蔡依林身穿2025 早秋系列服装出席巴黎时装秀。（VALENTINO／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>44岁的蔡依林（Jolin）自去年（2024年）成为该品牌全球代言人之后，并于同年3月首度以代言人身份出席品牌时装秀，而本季她也再度现身秀场，以绿、橘色搭配，穿着织有花卉图样连身短裙、亮橘蕾丝裤袜，搭配绿色蝴蝶结帽及白色蕾丝手套，与手上的猫咪提包，走复古俏丽风。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14454966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14454966" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454966-2503100254321487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14454966" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454966-2503100254321487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14454966" class="wp-caption-text">VALENTINO品牌代言人蔡依林身穿2025 早秋系列服装出席巴黎时装秀。（VALENTINO／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同为秀场焦点的林志玲（志玲姊姊）也是本季秀场嘉宾之一。50岁的她状态极佳，特别选穿黑色薄蕾丝拼接立体花卉洋装，裙摆左侧还有开衩造型设计，展现迷人的时尚感，名模气场强大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14454964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14454964" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454964-2503100254271487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14454964" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454964-2503100254271487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14454964" class="wp-caption-text">林志玲身穿 VALENTINO 2025 早秋系列服装出席巴黎时装秀。（VALENTINO／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14454967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14454967" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454967-2503100254341487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14454967" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14454967-2503100254341487.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="678" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14454967" class="wp-caption-text">林志玲身穿 VALENTINO 2025 早秋系列服装出席巴黎时装秀。（VALENTINO／公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
