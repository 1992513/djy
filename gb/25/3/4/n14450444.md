<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉县将举办庆祝妇女节活动并表扬杰出女性</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450445-749059-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将举办庆祝活动并表扬杰出女性。（嘉义县政府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年03月04日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将于3月8日举办“当WOMEN同在一起 我嘉1”庆祝活动，县长翁章梁与府内女性主管共同倡议，从社福、教育、文化、健康、就业、安全、经济七大主题，号召大众支持正面影响女性的政策、资源和活动，创造平等、友善的社会，4日举办宣传记者会，广邀乡亲支持参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14450446" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14450446" style="width: 496px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450446-749060.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14450446" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450446-749060.jpg" alt="" width="496" b="331"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14450446" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将举办庆祝活动并表扬杰出女性。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>太保流行舞蹈班为活动热情揭开序幕，此次将公开表扬社福领域谢孟洁、新住民领域魏珍群、警政领域林柔杏、社区规划领域许芳瑜、农业领域郑怡雯、工会领域曾明月、文化领域蔡明纯、教育领域陈媺慈等八位优秀女性也在场共襄盛举。</p>
<p>社会局表示，嘉义县配合联合国推进“性别主流化”，每年结合企业推动性别友善职场，办理女性就业创业及领导力课程，也鼓励农渔会女性参与公共事务，提升选任人员女性比例、制定改善及优先补助等措施，加速落实性别平权。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14450447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14450447" style="width: 504px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450447-749061.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14450447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450447-749061.jpg" alt="" width="504" b="336"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14450447" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将举办庆祝活动并表扬杰出女性。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“当WOMEN同在一起 我嘉1”象征嘉义县包山包海，妇女上山下海认同感；小至生活琐事，大至保障及维护个人权利的选择权；收到他人鼓励及支持时，女性愿意参与各项社交及表态活动，更代表大家汇聚并支持加速的性别平等行动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14450448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14450448" style="width: 495px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450448-749062.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14450448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450448-749062.jpg" alt="" width="495" b="330"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14450448" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将举办庆祝活动并表扬杰出女性。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14450450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14450450" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450450-749063.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14450450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450450-749063.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="320"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14450450" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将举办庆祝活动并表扬杰出女性。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁表示，女性在社会角度上依然偏向弱势，虽台湾相比其他国家，在女性意识更进步、友善，仍要透过大家努力，持续让性别平权抬头，盼县府的所作所为令大众反思妇女节意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14450451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14450451" style="width: 486px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450451-749064.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14450451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450451-749064.jpg" alt="" width="486" b="324"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14450451" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将举办庆祝活动并表扬杰出女性。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14450452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14450452" style="width: 490px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450452-749065.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14450452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450452-749065.jpg" alt="" width="490" b="327"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14450452" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将举办庆祝活动并表扬杰出女性。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14450454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14450454" style="width: 488px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450454-749066.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14450454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14450454-749066.jpg" alt="" width="488" b="326"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14450454" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县响应国际妇女节，将举办庆祝活动并表扬杰出女性。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3月8日当天除了表扬杰出女性、精彩舞台节目、妇女节特展、好礼摸彩活动、闯关集章游戏，还有专属女性的免费服务，社会局亦规划妇女节特展、艺术家分享讲座以及女性经济座谈，详情请至“嘉义县妇女节”FB粉丝专页。◇</p>
