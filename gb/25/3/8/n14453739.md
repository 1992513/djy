<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园市中山国小师生赴日交流 日本首相致电欢迎</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453740-750042-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>3月3号是日本的女儿节，伊奈町小室小学做了精美的欢迎海报以及女儿节的娃娃装饰来欢迎中山国小的师生（左：伊奈町小室小学的校长田口岩信；右：中山国小校长蔡淑华）。（桃园市中山国小提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年03月08日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市中山国小师生前往日本进行国际教育交流暨参访活动，由蔡淑华校长带领教师、家长及二十名高年级学童参与。交流活动不仅获得日本地方政府与民间大力协助，甚至收到日本首相石破茂的电报表达对两国三校师生交流的欢迎与祝贺，堪称台湾国小参与国际交流之佳话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453744" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453744-750046.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14453744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453744-750046-600x817.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="614" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453744" class="wp-caption-text">日本首相石破茂贺电欢迎与祝福。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蔡淑华指出，中山国小长年来积极推动国际教育，已与23个国家进行同步或非同步的线上交流，且一年内的书信交流遍及35个国家。今年适逢中山国小创校70周年，为让学生获得沈浸式文化体验，蔡淑华安排此次出国交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453742" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453742-750044.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14453742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453742-750044-600x704.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="529" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453742" class="wp-caption-text">伊奈町小室小学校长致赠中山国小特制的花艺纪念品，祝福友谊长存。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3月3日，师生来到日本埼玉县伊</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14453743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453743-750045.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453743-750045-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453743" class="wp-caption-text">五年级朱恩铄同学以中文和英语致词，双语流畅，颇受日方师生好评。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>欢迎仪式上，蔡淑华赠中山国小七十周年校庆红色小书包、保温杯及台湾特产给对方，传递喜乐与温馨，小室小学山口校长回赠给中山国小订制花艺艺术品，象征友谊长存，学生朱恩铄代表学校上台以中文和英文致词，口齿流畅获好评。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453741-750043.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453741-750043-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453741" class="wp-caption-text">欢迎仪式双方互赠纪念品，中山国小蔡淑华校长致赠创校70周年校庆红色小书包、纪念校旗以及台湾特色名产，包含高山茶、龙潭花生糖及佳德凤梨酥。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日方学校将中山的学生分配至六个班级，透过闯关活动体验多项日本传统文化，如折纸、书法、跳绳、歌牌、剑球与翻花绳等，让学生亲身感受日本文化的魅力。</p>
<p>学生入班上课时，东京第一场初雪缤纷落下，课程结束要离开时正值大雪纷飞，小室小学的学生摇摆国旗夹道欢送，大家在大雪纷飞中道别，离情依依，格外感人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453748-750050.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453748-750050-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453748" class="wp-caption-text">埼玉县今年的第一场初雪，而且下得很大，大雪纷飞中道别，特别不舍，对日方手持国旗夹道欢送，内心充满感恩与感动！（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14453747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453747" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453747-750049.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14453747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453747-750049-600x773.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="580" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453747" class="wp-caption-text">埼玉县今年的第一场初雪，而且下得很大，大雪纷飞中道别，特别不舍，对日方手持国旗夹道欢送，内心充满感恩与感动！（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3月4日，中山国小师生拜访朝霞市第十小学校，除了入班学习，还在学校欢迎会上与日本学童手牵手跳舞，共享欢乐时光。此外，双方学生也一同进行学校打扫活动，体验日本学校生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453746-750048.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453746-750048-600x345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453746" class="wp-caption-text">双方大合照。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日本学校对传统文化的重视，令中山学童感到十分新奇。例如，中山学童五年级洪芮瑜表示：“日本教室后面都贴着同学的书法作品，而我第一次写书法居然是在日本！”五年级张聿廷则说：“在台湾，体育课通常是打球和跑步，但在日本，各种民俗童玩也被视为体育活动，让我觉得非常惊讶。”六年级的郭竤曜同学对日本学生入校脱鞋、跪着擦地及精简的营养午餐，也表示印象深刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453745-750047.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14453745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453745-750047-600x428.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453745" class="wp-caption-text">学生入班上课与中午用餐情形。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中山国小赴日交流，获得当地政府与侨界的重视与支持。除了有朝霞市富冈胜则市长亲自协调接待学校，即将于3月18日上任的准市长松下昌代女士更亲自到校致词。学生入班体验时，当地教育局的“给食课”课长与营养师也特别到校与蔡淑华校长互相交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14453749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453749" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453749-750051.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14453749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453749-750051-600x735.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="550" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453749" class="wp-caption-text">穿和服参访浅草寺的日本文化体验，也让中山国小全体师生感到新奇与欢喜。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14453750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14453750" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453750-750052.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14453750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14453750-750052-600x585.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="439" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14453750" class="wp-caption-text">穿和服参访浅草寺的日本文化体验，也让中山国小全体师生感到新奇与欢喜。（桃园市中山国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蔡淑华说，这次难得的国际交流机会，让中山国小师生亲身体验日本的教育文化与学校生活，不仅拓展了学生的国际视野，也加深了两国学童之间的友谊，为未来的台日教育合作奠定良好基础。◇</p>
