<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨声演出“满堂红” 6月《紫钗记》将登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466903-DSC02753-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿侨声音乐剧社3月22日举办86周年的第一场演出，吸引不少观众。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年03月26日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）欢庆86周年，波士顿侨声音乐剧社3月22日举办粤曲折子戏欣赏会，以精湛的唱功和优美的乐曲，收获满场喝彩。今年的另一场重磅演出，将是6月的《紫钗记》大型粤剧表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466895-DSC02680.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466895-DSC02680-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466895" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿侨声音乐剧社3月22日举办86周年的第一场演出，吸引不少观众。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">3月的这场演出，在波士顿华埠昆士中学（Quincy Upper School）礼堂举办，包括7段经典戏曲，从周六中午12:30一直演到接近下午5点。司仪李卢莲馨开场盛装登台，高兴地说，看到几乎座无虚席的剧场，这场演出可谓是“满堂红”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466894-DSC02671.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466894-DSC02671-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466894" class="wp-caption-text">梁雁英和陈翠红带来《李娃传之风雪情》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466900" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466900-DSC02735.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466900-DSC02735-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466900" class="wp-caption-text">伍国荣和黄绮玲表演《血溅未央宫》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天的节目多为粤曲演唱，包括梁雁英和陈翠红带来的《李娃传之风雪情》，伍国荣和黄绮玲表演的《血溅未央宫》，黄伟健和陈艳珠演绎的《塞外草暖文姬乐》，罗伟华和黄宝娥带来的《泣血水绘园》，还有梁雁英和陈秀娥演唱的《胡风汉月两牵情》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466902" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466902-DSC02748.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466902-DSC02748-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466902" class="wp-caption-text">黄伟健和陈艳珠演绎《塞外草暖文姬乐》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466904" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466904-DSC02763.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466904-DSC02763-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466904" class="wp-caption-text">热情观众为演员鲜花。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466905" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466905-DSC02788.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466905-DSC02788-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466905" class="wp-caption-text">陈少声和陈翠秀表演《醉打金枝》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">除此之外，侨声音乐剧社还上演了两场古装折子戏，分别是陈少声和陈翠秀表演的《醉打金枝》，以及伍国荣和雷玉霞演绎的《一把存忠剑之斩经堂》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466901" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466901-DSC02745.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466901-DSC02745-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466901" class="wp-caption-text">乐师们在幕后演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466906" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466906-DSC02802.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466906-DSC02802-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466906" class="wp-caption-text">罗伟华和黄宝娥带来《泣血水绘园》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466907" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466907-DSC02809.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466907-DSC02809-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466907" class="wp-caption-text">梁雁英和陈秀娥演唱《胡风汉月两牵情》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天来观赏演出的嘉宾，包括波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）、中华公所主席雷国辉及夫人（至孝笃亲公所主席）陈秀珍、北美舜裔笃亲总公所元老陈建立及夫人黄海兰等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">费连向侨声音乐剧社的社长黄国威和李伍碧香颁赠了市议会褒奖。昆士中学校长张可仁，也派代表到场，感谢侨声为社区献上精彩演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466908" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466908-DSC02824.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466908-DSC02824-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466908" class="wp-caption-text">伍国荣和雷玉霞演绎《一把存忠剑之斩经堂》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466897" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466897-DSC02697.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466897-DSC02697-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466897" class="wp-caption-text">司仪李卢莲馨感谢观众捧场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466896-DSC02687.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466896-DSC02687-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466896" class="wp-caption-text">前排嘉宾莅临侨声音乐剧社的演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">雷国辉称赞，侨声音乐剧社在86年历史期间传承传统文化，为粤曲票友乃至众多广东人提供欣赏机会。他还特别赞许在幕后吹拉弹唱的12名乐师，与观众向他们报以热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14466898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466898-DSC02716.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466898-DSC02716-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466898" class="wp-caption-text">颁发市议会褒奖。左起：中华公所主席雷国辉、侨声社长李伍碧香、黄国威、市议员费连（Ed Flynn）、昆士中学校长张可仁代表、司仪李卢莲馨。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14466899" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14466899" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466899-DSC02720.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14466899" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14466899-DSC02720-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14466899" class="wp-caption-text">演出的成功，离不开义工们的工作。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">侨声音乐剧社预告，今年6月28日下午5:30至9:30，他们还将在波士顿爱默森学院派拉蒙中心（Emerson Paramount Center）演出《紫钗记》，为观众奉献8场大型古装粤剧。◇</p>
