<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>FASCA亚特兰大分会学员家长分享经验</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463333-111-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>全体合影 （FASCA亚特兰大分会）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年03月21日讯】海外青年文化大使协会亚特兰大分会于3月16日举行了3月例会，活动现场气氛温馨，计有该会戴念华等咨询导师、亚特兰大华侨文教服务中心主任阎树荣、陆复泰侨务委员、亚特兰大台湾商会吴政兴会长伉俪、世华工商妇女企管协会吕怡蓉会长及师生家长约100人参与。此次月会的亮点之一是由现就读GeorgiaTech博士班的Senior FASCA学员Jeffery Liu分享他在FASCA的成长经历。Jeffery Liu回顾了自己从内向的性格到逐渐变得乐于表达自我、分享经历的转变，并表示FASCA对他大学申请过程中的帮助不可或缺，让他在面对挑战时更加自信。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14463352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14463352" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463352-116-e1742518245313.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14463352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463352-116-e1742518245313.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14463352" class="wp-caption-text">咨询导师戴念华致词 （FASCA亚特兰大分会）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，学员家长Christine Ko也在会中发表了她的观察与心得。她分享了两位性格迥异的女儿在FASCA课程中各自的成长过程，并提到参加FASCA后，亲子关系中增添了更多有趣的话题，让家庭间的互动更加紧密。这一分享让与会的家长们深有共鸣，并对课程的价值有了更多的了解。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14463340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14463340" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463340-113-e1742517953446.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14463340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463340-113-e1742517953446.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="185"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14463340" class="wp-caption-text">学员家长分享参与FASCA经验 （FASCA亚特兰大分会）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后，学员们带来了精彩的表演，包括三太子、舞狮、舞蹈及音乐演奏等，展现了他们在课程中努力练习的成果。每一场表演都引来观众的热烈掌声，气氛热烈而充满活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14463341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14463341" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463341-114-e1742518035206.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14463341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463341-114-e1742518035206.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="185"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14463341" class="wp-caption-text">学员家长分享参与FASCA经验 （FASCA亚特兰大分会）</figcaption></figure>
<div class="mceTemp"></div>
<figure id="attachment_14463348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14463348" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463348-115-e1742518138538.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14463348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463348-115-e1742518138538.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="225"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14463348" class="wp-caption-text">学员练习金环舞成果展 （FASCA亚特兰大分会）</figcaption></figure>
<p>午餐时间，与会者共同品尝了由家长们亲手准备的台湾美食，让大家在品味佳肴的同时，享受了一次亲切的交流。当天的活动不仅是对学员成长的庆祝，也让每一位参与者感受到家庭与社群的支持。下午的活动则进一步让学员进行分组研习，针对各种技艺、团队合作等方面进行深入培训。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14463338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14463338" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463338-112-e1742517879389.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14463338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14463338-112-e1742517879389.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14463338" class="wp-caption-text">团队合作培训 （FASCA亚特兰大分会）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次月会除了提供师生家长间互动交流和学习，也彰显了FASCA对于侨界青成长与家庭互动的积极影响，期待未来能有更多的侨界青年和家庭受益于这样的活动。◇</p>
