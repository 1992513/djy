<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大大携手 让爱延续 不惧冷风 苗栗场火热展开捐血家年华</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461249-752324-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>全国七县市首长呼吁热血民众捐血，在各地指定日期时间，去慨捐。（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年03月18日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）在14度冷冽寒风下，超过200位来自苗栗县部苗、大千、为恭、李综合医院，以及苗栗县政府教育处、行政处、卫生局、消防局、警察局以及特搜队的热血人，一同来到苗栗县政府广场前，卷起袖子，响应“大大携手 让爱延续”捐血家年华。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14461250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461250" style="width: 510px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461250-752325.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461250-752325.jpeg" alt="" width="510" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461250" class="wp-caption-text">民众热情伸手捐血。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14461251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461251" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461251-752326.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461251-752326.jpeg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461251" class="wp-caption-text">大千综合医院也在现场替捐血民众医疗资询。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大大基金会延续去年走遍全台湾六县市，募集1万1810袋鲜血的公益活动，今年再度启动“大大携手让爱延续”捐血家年华，目标1万2000袋，更把活动脚步扩大到全台湾七县市，继台北、新北、桃园、新竹县之后，“大大携手 让爱延续”捐血家年华第五站来到苗栗县。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14461252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461252" style="width: 528px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461252-752327.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461252-752327.jpeg" alt="" width="528" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461252" class="wp-caption-text">捐血车内满座。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动由曾经获得全国民族舞蹈优等的泰安国中小舞蹈队，在青少年男女对舞旋律中展开，苗栗县长钟东锦携手大大数位基金会荣誉董事长李大维，以及苗栗县议长李文斌、副议长张淑芬、苗栗市长余文忠、造桥乡长徐连斌以及苗栗县议员一同出席，呼吁苗栗县市的热血人，一起卷起袖子：“捐血一袋、救人一家”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14461253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461253" style="width: 468px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461253-752328.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461253-752328.jpeg" alt="" width="468" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461253" class="wp-caption-text">县长钟东锦表示，看到台湾爱的一面，捐血是非常有意义的一件事，可以说是一举数得，大大数位基金会发起这么有意义的活动，苗栗县市一定会倾力相助。也感佩太太努力，她欣慰体重逹标也可以捐血了。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14461254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461254" style="width: 469px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461254-752329.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461254-752329.jpeg" alt="" width="469" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461254" class="wp-caption-text">大大数位基金会荣誉董事长李大维表示，感谢钟东锦县长与县府团队感谢并支持大大数位基金会再度来到苗栗县办理活动，一定能开展不同境界。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14461255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461255" style="width: 477px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461255-752330.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461255-752330.jpeg" alt="" width="477" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461255" class="wp-caption-text">苗县警局呼吁全民动起来防诈，打诈，一听二挂三查证，报案110，反诈骗资询专线165。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14461256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461256" style="width: 483px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461256-752331.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461256-752331.jpeg" alt="" width="483" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461256" class="wp-caption-text">苗县卫生局，地方医疗院所代表和与会贵宾齐力祝捐血袋逹标。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钟东锦县长表示，现场看到这么多人加入捐血行列，他觉得：“看到台湾爱的一面”，他更说捐血是非常有意义的一件事，尤其今年初他的夫人健康指数达标：“捐血中心说她可以捐血了，她非常高兴自己健康管理成功”，所以捐血不但是救人，还可以促进新陈代谢，也会提醒自己做好健康管理，：“可以说是一举数得，大大数位基金会发起这么有意义的活动，苗栗县市一定会倾力相助”。</p>
<p>大大数位基金会荣誉董事长李大维，代表基金会董事长戴永辉，携手信和有线电视与吉元有线电视，各自捐赠苗栗县政府100万元，合计200万元，钟东锦县长也感谢大大宽频的善举，他表示：“苗栗县的资源有限，谢谢大大数位去年和今年的奖助学金，共帮助了1000位品行优良和清寒学子，让需要帮助的学生都因此获益，尤其代表苗栗县外出参加运动比赛的小小选手们，都能因此有比较好的差旅住宿。”</p>
<p>李大维荣誉董事长也感谢钟东锦县长的大力支持：“大大数位基金会非常高兴再度来到苗栗县办理活动，基金会戴永辉董事长对于捐血活动向来不遗余力，这次捐血活动在钟县长的领导下，一定能开展不同境界”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14461255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461255" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461255-752330.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461255-752330.jpeg" alt="" width="460" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461255" class="wp-caption-text">苗县警局呼吁全民动起来防诈，打诈，一听二挂三查证，报案110，反诈骗资询专线165。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14461256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461256" style="width: 470px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461256-752331.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461256-752331.jpeg" alt="" width="470" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461256" class="wp-caption-text">苗县卫生局，地方医疗院所代表和与会贵宾齐力祝捐血袋逹标。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14461258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461258" style="width: 462px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461258-752332.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461258-752332.jpeg" alt="" width="462" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461258" class="wp-caption-text">县长钟东锦欣喜与捐血县府团队合影。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苗栗县议长李文斌致词表示，非常高兴能参加大大携手让爱延续的捐血家年华，更感谢大大数位基金会连续两年的捐赠，让苗栗的清贫学生可以获得补助；新竹捐血中心黄莉雯组长也感谢大大数位基金会从三月起跑的全省捐血活动，她表示：“天气变冷时候，捐血量就会下滑，昨天寒流来，捐血量立刻下跌二成。所以我由衷感谢主办方和县府的支持，让医疗用血不虞匮乏。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14461259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461259" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461259-752333.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461259-752333.jpeg" alt="" width="448" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461259" class="wp-caption-text">获得全国民族舞蹈优等的泰安国中小舞蹈队 拉开序幕。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14461260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14461260" style="width: 447px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461260-752334.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14461260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14461260-752334.jpeg" alt="" width="447" b="198" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14461260" class="wp-caption-text">大大携手 让爱延续 不惧冷风 苗栗场火热展开。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“大大携手让爱延续”捐血家年华活动，自3月1日起跑，目标募集1万2000袋，在经历台北、新北、桃园、新竹四县市首长以及热血奉献人的共襄盛举下，迄今为止已经获得超过5000袋鲜血，预估在苗栗县活动，将一举突破五成，达到6000袋目标。</p>
