<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>乐天桃猿棒球队赴仁海宫祈福 祝福球队夺冠</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469215-754766-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>球团祭读疏文。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年03月28日讯】（大纪元记者陈建霖台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政前往中坜仁海宫，出席“乐天桃猿职业棒球队中华职棒36年祈福活动”。张善政表示，中华职棒36年例行赛即将开打，乐天桃猿棒球队主场首战将于本周日下午登场，祝福乐天桃猿棒球队旗开得胜、出战顺利，相信在中坜仁海宫天上圣母的庇佑下，乐天桃猿将能实现桃园市民与球迷的期待，在赛季夺得耀眼战绩，将总冠军奖杯抱回桃园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14469216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14469216" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469216-754767.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14469216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469216-754767.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14469216" class="wp-caption-text">乐天桃猿棒球队至中坜仁海宫祈福。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14469217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14469217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469217-754768.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14469217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469217-754768.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14469217" class="wp-caption-text">祈福仪式。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中坜仁海宫董事长王介禧表示，感谢张善政对宗教文化与体育发展的全力支持，让宗教信仰与棒球运动相结合，成为全民共享的盛事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14469218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14469218" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469218-754769.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14469218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469218-754769.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14469218" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政与球团合照。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年也适逢中坜仁海宫创建200周年，特别准备金狮及发财金赠予球团，期盼为球团带来好运，祝福乐天桃猿棒球队旗开得胜，勇夺佳绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14469219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14469219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469219-754770.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14469219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/03/id14469219-754770.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14469219" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政致赠666幸运骰子。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>体育局表示，感谢乐天桃猿长期与市府共同合作，为桃园球迷推出许多在地化的服务，培养属地球迷。市府近年也大力推动球场“外野看台电视墙更新”“场地排水及喷灌系统改善”“夜间照明系统优化”“外野看台地坪及座椅更新”等各项优化改善工程，提供球员良好的比赛场地，同时也为球迷带来更舒适的观赛环境及体验，邀请市民朋友在新赛季期间，进场为球队一起加油打气。◇</p>
