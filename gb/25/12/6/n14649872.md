<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>岁末同乐会 松年学员以音乐与舞蹈展现学习成果</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649882-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>松年学院音乐课2025秋季学期同乐会合影。（图/HTISC提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月06日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）休士顿台湾松年学院音乐课2025秋季学期同乐会，于12月4日（周四）上午10点在松年学院大厅温馨举行。董事长陈美芬、院长许胜弘、多位授课老师及音乐班与合唱团团员齐聚一堂，度过一场充满欢笑与感动的岁末成果展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649883-02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649883-02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649883" class="wp-caption-text">（左至右）黄诗婷、周景仰、陈美芬、许胜弘、陈淑芬、王丽纯、温雅如、张淑贞及Lika Okamoto老师们合影。（图/HTISC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动首先由“松年长笛重奏团”揭开序幕。长笛老师温雅如刚生产不久，抱着尚未满月新生宝宝上台教学与陪伴学生演奏，令全场感动不已。演奏者包含潘如钰、张陈琼珲、林云杰与陈秀峰，以温柔悠扬的笛声为活动带来美好开场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649884-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649884-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649884" class="wp-caption-text">松年长笛重奏团。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接续登场的是钢琴班，由指导老师黄诗婷带领平均年龄75岁以上的学员上台演奏，其中最高龄者为91岁的曾敏。虽然多位学员因紧张而即兴“创作新曲”，与平时练习略有出入，但台下笑声与掌声不断。黄诗婷老师幽默表示：“紧张时就会自己创作，音乐本来就是快乐的泉源，只要大家开心，老师也开心。”演奏者包括黄惠美、张陈秋玉、吴明娴、唐蕙初、蔡淑华、曾敏、潘忠昌、周理玲及张陈琼珲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649885" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649885-04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649885-04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649885" class="wp-caption-text">木笛班和老师周景仰（左三）合奏演出。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>木笛班则由指导老师周景仰带来生动介绍，说明木笛（直笛Recorder）的高、中、低音多种样貌。他特别表扬院长许胜弘，在繁忙工作之余仍努力学习，甚至因过度疲累在课堂中打瞌睡，却仍能吹出声音，引得全场大笑。演奏者包括许胜弘、张陈琼珲、Tim Kwok及张佩珍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649886-05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649886-05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649886" class="wp-caption-text">HTISC Choir松年合唱团合影。（图/HTISC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天阵容最庞大的HTISC Choir松年合唱团，指挥杨明耕、伴奏陈淑芬，带来《回忆》与《春光好》等曲目。合唱团的表演精神饱满、歌声和谐，展现了一整季努力的成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649888" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649888-07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649888-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649888" class="wp-caption-text">舞蹈班王丽纯老师（左一）、张陈琼珲（前）、Mary Tang及LiLing Chou。（图/HTISC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>压轴表演由王丽纯老师指导的松年舞蹈班，以一段轻快的Cha-Cha热力登场。舞者包括Li Chun Huang、Mary Tang、Caroline Chang及LiLing Chou，舞姿优雅又充满节奏感，为活动画下精彩句点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649887-06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649887-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649887" class="wp-caption-text">学院准备了丰盛午餐在欢乐气氛中大家共度美好时光。（图/HTISC提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>值得一提的是全能学员张陈琼珲，在多项演出中表现沉稳完美，被同学们称为“全优生”。活动最后，学院准备了丰盛午餐，师生在欢乐气氛中共度美好时光。</p>
