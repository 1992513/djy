<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>香港广福悼念区清场前 大批市民前往悼遇难者</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650787-b4a7efe80769433cbe2a8c0118e8a940-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>位于广福休憩处悼念区留言区7日晚清场，大批市民把握最后机会前往悼念。（林卓轩／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月08日讯】（大纪元记者林一山香港报导）香港宏福苑于11月26日发生5级火灾，官方称造成159人死亡。自事故发生后，邻近的广福休憩处，一直有市民前往悼念。凉亭位置有一批义工驻守，设留言区，提供纸笔让市民抒发悲哀。</p>
<p>12月6日现场出现告示，表示该处的悼念活动将于7日深夜结束，大批市民把握最后机会献花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14650789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14650789" style="width: 507px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650789-03c19c6393217ef91cf19867393db8f6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14650789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650789-03c19c6393217ef91cf19867393db8f6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="507" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14650789" class="wp-caption-text">位于广福休憩处悼念区留言区7日清场，大批市民把握最后机会前往悼念。（林卓轩／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14650791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14650791" style="width: 512px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650791-36e2cbd7dc167b2ec6259f379056a7be.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14650791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650791-36e2cbd7dc167b2ec6259f379056a7be-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="512" b="341" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14650791" class="wp-caption-text">位于广福休憩处悼念区留言区7日清场，大批市民把握最后机会前往悼念。（林卓轩／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14650795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14650795" style="width: 518px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650795-fab8da0347adc6a37ac55ebde902162c.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14650795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650795-fab8da0347adc6a37ac55ebde902162c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="518" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14650795" class="wp-caption-text">有外籍人士聚集在广礼楼楼下，向着火灾现场祈祷。（日谷／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警方回应传媒查询时表示，已与现场人士经商讨及协调，该批人士因此同意自行离开现场。</p>
<p>有义工向传媒表示，近日有执法部门与义工沟通，对方提出了大火灾后超过一星期，要求义工们结束留言区。多名义工已坚持多日，出现疲态，商议后亦同意是时候结束留言区。</p>
<p>因此，广福休憩处悼念活动将于7日晚11时59分59秒结束，随后会慢慢执拾凉亭物品。清场后，纸鹤和鲜花将会火化献给死难者；而现场留言字句和心意卡，将会运至仓库存放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14650796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14650796" style="width: 516px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650796-a239558a48872b42e2cc3ca7cef6045d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14650796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650796-a239558a48872b42e2cc3ca7cef6045d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="516" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14650796" class="wp-caption-text">礼楼楼下仍然有社工团体留守。（日谷／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14650797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14650797" style="width: 509px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650797-d030d8f0891de493959b5ed340ba8c95.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14650797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14650797-d030d8f0891de493959b5ed340ba8c95-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="509" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14650797" class="wp-caption-text">有热心市民免费提供花束。（日谷／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>广福休憩处现场告示亦提醒，不鼓励市民以过激、仇恨或任何勾起市民情绪之字眼。有义工称，相信市民也明白和合作。</p>
<p>广福休憩处外则有警员驻守，义工指警方主要维持秩序，未有阻止悼念行为。</p>
<p>12月6日有多名市民献花、写留言、折纸鹤等。有中六学生称，虽然学业较繁重，但觉得有些事情比读书更重要、更有意义。</p>
<p>有不少长者从蓝田、将军澳等远道而来，甚至有人坐轮椅，只为悼念死者，义工称反映香港社会“好有爱、好有人情味”。</p>
