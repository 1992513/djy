<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】豪华冒险专家 2025 Lexus GX 550 Overtrail+ AWD</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659106-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025 Lexus GX 550 Overtrail+。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年12月20日讯】2025年Lexus GX是与新款Toyota Land Cruiser有着密切联系，所以新一代GX与Land Cruiser有八分相似，然而GX着重豪华的设计与内装，比去年测试的Overtrail更高一级，设备更齐全也更全面。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d3c3t179l61pwn.cloudfront.net/E2aknO2NFXlj"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/xHauN6ROUyE?si=3SUlYxd6g4B8o6zI" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>当Toyota初发表全新一代Land Cruiser时，大众对Land Cruiser的外表，都觉得带有英国Land Rover品牌的设计。前脸隐藏着Lexus的Spindle设计，再加上比旧版更细小的头灯。此外，尾闸由以往横揭式改为上揭式，在开启时占用地方更少，而尾闸上部分的挡风玻璃与旧款一样，可独立上揭打开。今年测试车装上了车顶行李架，除了在进入室内停车场要留意最高限制外，在高速公路行驶也会有风噪问题，所以除非真的要去Camping带东西之外，建议不用时拆下来留家里。这种框架还可连上配件和装备，例如车顶帐篷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659073" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659073-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659073-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659073" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Lexus GX 550 Overtrail+。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>专为新一代GX推出的Overtrail及Overtrail+两个版本，均会标配直径33英寸的265/70R18越野轮胎，以及18吋的哑灰色轮圈。2025年款GX与更高一级的LX车系共用同一款GA-F底盘，这款底盘经过强化，可承受越野时的扭曲力，并可提高悬挂性能，保持汽车稳定性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659079" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659079-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659079-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659079" class="wp-caption-text">33英寸越野轮胎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试的GX 550装备了3.4升双涡轮增压V6引擎，最高马力输出为349匹，而峰值扭矩则有479磅英尺，比旧款的V8引擎的301匹马力更高。拖力更高达 9,063磅，比旧有的6,500磅增加不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659093-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_21.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659093-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659093" class="wp-caption-text">3.4升双涡轮增压V6引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>GX问世以来就以其越野能力著称，所以新一代GX延续这一传统，全时四驱系统作为标准配置，可应付各种地形。除了四驱系统之外，GX一律均标配Torsen限滑锁定中央差速器，将引擎动力以50:50 的比例分配，防止车轮打滑。在转弯加速时，Torsen差速器会根据后左和右车轮的负载情况，以分配驱动力。而最高级的Overtrail和Overtrail+车型独有电子控制的后差速器锁，可将动力均匀地分配到左右后轮，确保车轮有足够的牵引力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659094" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659094-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659094" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659094-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_08-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659094" class="wp-caption-text">雾灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，Lexus GX 550 Overtrail 和 Overtrail+标配电子动态悬挂系统（E-KDSS），在越野驾驶和公路行驶稳定性之间提供高水平的平衡。该系统锁定和解锁前后稳定杆，以帮助控制和增强车轮的摆动。根据驾驶条件、速度、转向输入和驾驶者选择的多地形选择（MTS）设置，该系统可以独立锁定或解锁前后稳定杆。当处于低速档（L4）时，可以启动爬行控制（Crawl Control）。爬行控制可控制引擎输出和液压制动压力，以帮助减少轮胎打滑。GX的接近角达到26度、跨越角24度和离去角21度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659095" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659095-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659095-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659095" class="wp-caption-text">尾挡风玻璃可上揭打开。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MTS系统可根据所选模式控制驱动力、液压制动压力和悬挂控制，低档（L4）时有四种模式：自动、沙地、泥地和岩石。高档（H4）则有五种模式：自动、泥土、沙地、泥地和深雪。当多地形选择开启时，多地形监视器启动，帮助在越野驾驶时增加对车辆周围和底部的可见性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659096" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659096-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659096-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659096" class="wp-caption-text">四驱系统控制。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内装方面，GX采用了Lexus最新的设计，有12.3英寸虚拟仪表板、中控台大萤幕、全自动的恒温系统及座椅等。14英寸的中控台萤幕，尺寸不仅大，像素也高，触控反应也很快，内置有导航系统及卫星收音机（SXM），最开心是使用iPhone的Google Map导航时，GX的HUD（抬头显示器）也会配合显示导航指示，可见Lexus内置系统与手机系统的融合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659097" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659097-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659097-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659097" class="wp-caption-text">12.3英寸虚拟仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14659098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659098" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659098-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_23.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659098-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659098" class="wp-caption-text">14英寸的中控台萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>座椅是以黑色皮革制造，并且有冷热控制，可自动控制温度，Overtrail+版加装了前座按摩功能。在手枕后方的储物空间，换上了一个冷冻箱“Cool Box”，随时可享用冷冻饮料，长途行程最适用。在车后的行李舱左侧，装有一个压缩机，可为自己或其他汽车轮胎充气。除了越野型号Overtrail及Overtrail+之外，其他GX型号均会有第三排座椅，全车可载七人，而Overtrail及Overtrail+则只可载五人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659100-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659100-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659100" class="wp-caption-text">黑色皮革座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与去年测试Overtrail一样，将GX 550 Overtrail+开到越野测试其能力，在不同路况也能轻松应付，急转弯车身也很稳定，尤其有了DAC、MTS及Crawl Control的帮助，在沙地起步也绝不含糊；而在碎石路段，GX随着地形起伏，但悬挂表现出色，减少了汽车颠簸。在高速公路上使用Adaptive Cruise Control，在某些弯道上出现转向不足，要手动更正才可，抹了一把汗，另外加满汽油的时候，仪表板也只显示可行走不足300英里，可见GX的食量惊人。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659101" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659101-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659101-2025_Lexus_GX_550_Overtrail_Plus_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659101" class="wp-caption-text">冷冻箱“Cool Box”。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
