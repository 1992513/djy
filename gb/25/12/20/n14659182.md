<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>海南全岛封关后 网友抱怨物价飙高遭删文</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659216-903730c2bc66e2a503fb021b8aa6b12b-600x400.png" />
<h6>海南封关后民众吐槽菜价变贵（网路截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月21日讯】12月18日，海南自贸港正式启动全岛封关，官方大肆宣传。不过当地民众说封关后物价变贵，特别菜价离谱。但网上的质疑声音遭封杀。</p>
<p>大陆自媒体“潍坊万事通”19日发文表示，海南是封关了，封关的目的，就是为了打造海南的经济。但是在网上可以看到，有一些当地的网友发帖抱怨说，封关之后，物价都开始变贵了。</p>
<p>这篇文章被下架。</p>
<p>12月18日，歌手赵柔柔在微博帖称，海南封关政策对本地居民没有实惠，物价尤其是蔬菜、肉类、餐饮和外卖大幅上涨，而本地人工资仍仅1800–3000元，生活压力大。该帖随后被删除。12月19日，她再次发帖呼吁降物价、涨工资，让普通海南居民真正受益，并批评政策反复、宣传与实际效果不符。这则帖子也被删除。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659711" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659711-9df8ef123d6249438b7024937d76e374.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659711-9df8ef123d6249438b7024937d76e374-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659711" class="wp-caption-text">（网路截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有网友晒出了海南封关后的菜价。菠菜要32元（人民币，下同）/斤，芹菜要25元/斤。还有网友表示他出门去买了一根油条，结果被收了7块钱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659211-G8fi31RakAEc3vE.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659211-G8fi31RakAEc3vE-600x872.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="872" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659211" class="wp-caption-text">（网路截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14659212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659212-G8fi9SjasAAdeEE.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659212-G8fi9SjasAAdeEE-600x634.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="634" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659212" class="wp-caption-text">（网路截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>20日，有博主拍视频说，封关第三天，海口香蕉4.68元/斤，内地香蕉仅1.68元/斤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14659213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659213-8e252fa9d4fd9c7cfc78e0723699a951.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659213-8e252fa9d4fd9c7cfc78e0723699a951-600x421.png" alt="" width="600" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659213" class="wp-caption-text">（网路截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友留言：“产地比运到外省还贵，带动是那个环节出了问题？”“我刚问卖葡萄的，一斤卖9块！”“海南过上了世界富人待遇了。”</p>
<div class="mceTemp">
<figure id="attachment_14659712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14659712" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659712-G8dAOYAakAAd8ks.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14659712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14659712-G8dAOYAakAAd8ks-600x1091.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="1091" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14659712" class="wp-caption-text">（网路截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但也有封关后便宜了的东西，比如免税城里苹果手机最高可以减少2140元。也有网友在抖音上说：“海南封关后猫山王榴莲23元一斤，没封关前60元多。”</p>
