<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>拾起南院一二事 故宫南院十周年回顾登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657262-817242-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）在今（2025）年国立故宫博物院庆祝百年院庆之际，故宫南部院区开馆迄今业已满十周年，为回首南院规划历程及开馆营运成果，自即日起在故宫南院3楼观景台与展厅2楼廊道推出“十之一二：故宫南院十周年回顾”，目的在让观众轻松看见故宫南院走过10年为文化均富的努力；从多媒体、影像与虚实对照的展示之中，再奔向下个里程碑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14657263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657263" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657263-817243.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657263-817243-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657263" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14657264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657264" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657264-817244.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657264-817244-450x357.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657264" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14657265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657265" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657265-817245.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657265-817245-450x320.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="320" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657265" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>故宫萧宗煌院长表示，今年是故宫100年院庆，台北建院落地融合第60年，同时也是南院开馆第10年，几个数字代表国立故宫博物院的发展，而南院的10周年回顾象征故宫100年院庆的尾声，南北携手传递艺术文化。南院自 2015 年 12 月 28 日开馆以来，透过策展、教育活动、志工培训与园区经营，展现博物馆在地扎根、文化平权与公共服务精神。10年累积，藉由“十之一二”主题，呈现十年的美好片段，萧院长感谢十年来各界的支持，故宫南院可见的成长与变化，将与观众共同迈向崭新的篇章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14657266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657266" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657266-817246.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657266-817246-450x566.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="566" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657266" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14657267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657267" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657267-817247.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657267" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657267-817247-450x333.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657267" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14657268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657268" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657268-817248.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657268-817248-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657268" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次展示分四个段落，包括“拾光胶囊”、“拾常筑景”、“拾启有二”与“拾掇补遗”，再加“大事年表”统整记事。“拾光胶囊”特别精选故宫南院10年来观众在博物馆里的各种表情，见到观众从童年到乐龄的多种参与样貌，南院吉祥物“哈奴曼”在展场内外出现，向观众挥手、合影，也是许多人莅临南院时的珍贵记忆。</p>
<p>“拾常筑景”以筹建及营建过程作为主线；观众身处于博物馆内，从最初的荒芜，到绿意盎然的景致，每一处地景都是朝向观众的需求而调整。从这里回顾南院选址的文化承诺、张大千大师的巨石跨海落脚的历程、一馆建筑中的文化意象、览月桥的趣味设计及糖铁五分车驶入南院所牵起的历史与观光连结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14657269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657269" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657269-817249.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657269-817249-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657269" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14657270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657270" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657270-817250.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657270-817250-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657270" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14657271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14657271" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657271-817251.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14657271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14657271-817251-450x637.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="637" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14657271" class="wp-caption-text">拾起南院一二事，故宫南院十周年回顾登场。（故宫南院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“拾启有二”象征故宫南院迈向下一阶段的崭新启航。以正兴建中的“二馆”为主轴，作为南院新的起点；二馆以典藏青铜器转译的“绿宝盒”为设计理念，不同于一馆水墨流线的造型，代表故宫南院永续经营、地方串联与艺术文化推广上的再启动。观众可从展览场地窗户一窥二馆的建筑面貌，从虚实之间感受故宫南院以当代语汇诠释传统艺术的态度。</p>
<p>“拾掇补遗”则是故宫南院10年来同仁奉献热情与创意所制作的教育推广品。特别精选曾经得过iF设计大奖的“移动的博物馆”教具箱、曾收下MUSE创意大奖的历届夏日亲子艺术月的可爱设计，还有陪伴百万学子参观后所做的文化体验教案，当然也有为资深大哥大姐制作的卡牌游戏；故宫南院感谢时光使馆员沉浸于艺术的真善美，更感谢观众让博物馆变得更有趣。</p>
<p>即日起“十之一二：故宫南院十周年回顾”展示至2026年3月15日止，故宫南院邀请观众莅临，一起观赏属于这片土地、这座博物馆的美好记忆，在回望中感受力量与祝福，一起迎接充满希望的未来。</p>
