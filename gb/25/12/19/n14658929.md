<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>深化台美连结 休士顿台商会岁末年会温馨举行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658934-02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>李雄（中）和理事团队合影。（陆希／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月20日讯】（大纪元记者陆布休斯顿报导）休士顿台商会2025年圣诞联谊年会，于12月17日晚间在北海道日式餐厅包场举行，吸引逾百位会员与嘉宾齐聚一堂，在温馨热闹的气氛中回顾年度成果并展望未来发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14658933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14658933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658933-01.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14658933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658933-01-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14658933" class="wp-caption-text">（左至右）李俊毅、Jacey R. Jetton夫妇、萧伊芳、李雄和杨淑雅合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚贵宾云集，包括经文处（TECO）处长萧伊芳偕同组长杨淑雅、李俊毅、林佳罗及法务秘书王茜，侨教中心副主任房亭妤等出席共襄盛举；主流贵宾则有德州前众议员Jacey R. Jetton，以及休斯顿警察局汽车盗窃部门主管Nhatthien Nguyen等人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14658941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14658941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658941-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14658941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658941-03-600x433.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14658941" class="wp-caption-text">（左至右）杨淑雅、Nhatthien Nguyen和李雄合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动由前会长张园立主持。他特别感谢理事团队为年会的筹划与场地布置所付出的心力，并安排无菜单料理（Omakase）展现巧思；同时鸣谢多位荣誉会长王敦正、陈舜哲、张智嘉、方台生等与赞助商，强调其长期支持对台商会永续经营的重要性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14658936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14658936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658936-04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14658936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658936-04-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14658936" class="wp-caption-text">即将荣升返台的林佳罗（左）和李雄合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>萧伊芳致词时表示，经文处同仁借此机会向长年在海外深耕的台商朋友致上最诚挚的感谢。她指出，正因台商社群在德州与美南地区的努力付出，协助驻外单位推动各项工作，台美关系方能稳健深化，并代表政府祝福与会嘉宾佳节愉快、平安顺心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14658937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14658937" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658937-05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14658937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658937-05-600x423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14658937" class="wp-caption-text">出席晚会的嘉宾们合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长李雄身着象征台商会核心团队的帽子与背心致词，感谢理事“永远无悔”的奉献精神，并逐一介绍理事团队，同时感谢副会长张园立、张硕仁及顾问周政贤对今年双十国庆系列活动的全力支援。李雄也回顾年度成果，并说明2026年发展方向，包括会员拓展、跨境商机布局，以及德州与休斯顿对台商的吸引力，未来亦不排除规划赴墨西哥参访。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14658938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14658938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658938-06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14658938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658938-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14658938" class="wp-caption-text">林翠梅（左）抽中处长萧伊芳赞助现金礼卡合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>年会中亦向即将荣升返台的TECO两位组长杨淑雅与林佳罗致上祝贺与欢送，并致赠纪念奖座。Nhatthien Nguyen警官专长于网路犯罪与汽车盗窃防制，并赠送新书表达祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14658939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14658939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658939-07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14658939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658939-07-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14658939" class="wp-caption-text">资深会员相欢聚一堂。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，Jacey R. Jetton提及德州设立Texas Trade Office的过程，肯定台湾对国际社会的友谊与贡献；他提到，“我母亲是韩国人，她很惊讶我选择了台湾。”因为我了解台湾的重要性。会中还介绍了多位新进会员，涵盖食品制造、台湾美食进军美国市场、物流仓储等产业，展现台商多元实力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14658940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14658940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658940-08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14658940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14658940-08-600x423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14658940" class="wp-caption-text">理事庄帏婷（左）和吴玉敏（右）合作进行抽奖节目。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动最后由理事庄帏婷主持抽奖，采取“小奖多元”方式，让现场欢笑声不断，为年会画下圆满句点。</p>
