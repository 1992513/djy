<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黄展唐和伍伟业接安良会长 薪火传承服务侨社</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663396-DSC05854-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月26日，波士顿安良工商会举办新届职员就职典礼。新届会长就职。左起：会长伍伟业、经文处组长黄耀良、卸任会长陈伟民、元老陈仕维、会长黄展唐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月28日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）波士顿安良工商会在100多年历史中秉持宗旨，“安国安家昌隆会务振中华，良谋良策繁荣侨社致工商”，如今的会务逐渐交由新一代中坚力量传承。</p>
<p>12月26日中午，波士顿安良工商会在其华埠驻地举办新届职员就职典礼。黄展唐和伍伟业接任会长，期盼与各团体保持合作，服务社区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663402" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663402-DSC05872.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663402-DSC05872-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663402" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿安良工商会举办新届职员就职典礼，诸多嘉宾到场致贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年的两位会长皆是近几年崭露头角的侨社新一代中坚力量。黄展唐是华埠“唐朝酒家”的老板，曾任安良工商会帮办，去年当选会长，今年得以连任。伍伟业是“华埠餐厅”老板，曾任伍胥山公所主席，安良工商会2024年会长，2025年帮办，如今再次担任会长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663404-DSC05879.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663404-DSC05879-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663404" class="wp-caption-text">新旧会长获中华民国侨委会褒奖。左起：会长伍伟业、经文处组长黄耀良、侨教中心主任高家富、会长黄展唐、卸任会长陈伟民。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们会尽力做到最好，希望会务蒸蒸日上。”黄展唐说。</p>
<p>“感谢大家到来，感谢大家支持，在如此之寒冷的一天。”伍伟业说。</p>
<p>卸任会长陈伟民也感谢大家在他任内给予支持，并期待与新届干部及侨社友人携手服务社区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663398-DSC05862.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663398-DSC05862-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663398" class="wp-caption-text">卸任会长陈伟民感谢会员们的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14663399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663399" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663399-DSC05864.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663399-DSC05864-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663399" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长黄展唐承诺尽力推动会务。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14663400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663400" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663400-DSC05866.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663400-DSC05866-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663400" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长伍伟业感谢嘉宾们在寒冷天气下到场致贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在嘉宾们的见证下，新届干部宣誓“恪守会纲，破除姓界，维护会员权益”，正式开启2026年任期。他们还依照传统，敬拜关圣神像，祈求新年会务昌盛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663397" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663397-DSC05858.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663397-DSC05858-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663397" class="wp-caption-text">安良工商会新届干部面对关圣神像宣誓就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>资深侨领赞薪火传承</strong></h2>
<p>全美安良工商会元老、中华民国侨务委员陈仕维说，卸任会长陈伟民及各位干部在过去一年的付出，令大家铭记于心；两位新届会长，在侨社是才能出众且比较年轻的一辈，成功经营生意，热心服务侨社。陈仕维期盼，侨社所有团体都持续有年轻人接过传承，大家共同维护社区的安定繁荣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663407" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663407" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663407-DSC05888.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663407-DSC05888-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663407" class="wp-caption-text">全美安良工商会元老陈仕维期盼薪火传承。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中华公所主席雷国辉、洪门致公堂元老伍伯和、余丽媖、主席朱耀钟等友好团体代表皆到场致贺，使安良大厅内座无虚席。</p>
<p>雷国辉称赞，安良工商会是侨社的“中流砥柱”，多年来一直支持中华公所，也致力于维护侨社的安定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663405" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663405-DSC05883.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663405-DSC05883-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663405" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉感谢安良维护侨社安定。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>伍伯和回顾道，他1961年就加入了安良工商会，向来秉持奉公守法的原则。如此长久的服务历程，得到来宾们的热烈掌声。他说，洪门作为安良工商会的盟友，衷心祝贺安良会务昌隆，成员们身体健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663406" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663406-DSC05887.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663406-DSC05887-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663406" class="wp-caption-text">洪门致公堂元老伍伯和称赞两个团体的盟友关系。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>安良与中华民国渊源深厚</strong></h2>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处组长黄耀良、侨教中心主任高家富都出席了就职典礼，感谢安良工商会自100多年前至今持续支持中华民国，并向新旧会长颁赠侨委会褒奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663408" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663408-DSC05893.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663408-DSC05893-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663408" class="wp-caption-text">安良工商会新届干部敬拜关圣神像。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄耀良说，安良充满传统气氛的就职典礼令他感动，也看到安良传承至今的忠义精神。中华民国从成立之前，直到今天，都一直得到安良的帮助。现在，安良也成为受到美国社会各界肯定的重要公益力量。他说，处长廖朝宏当天由于感冒而不能到场，但托他向安良表达感谢和敬意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14663401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663401" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663401-DSC05868.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663401-DSC05868-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663401" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处组长黄耀良感谢安良工商会长期支持中华民国。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14663403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14663403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663403-DSC05876.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14663403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14663403-DSC05876-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14663403" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富向安良会员们表达祝贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高家富特别用粤语致词，代表侨委会，向新届会长表达祝贺，对卸任会长表达感谢，也向安良工商会送上新年祝福。◇</p>
