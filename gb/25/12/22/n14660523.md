<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新英格兰台商会欢聚尾牙 赖怡音接会长</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660513-DSC05795-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会12月21日举办尾牙餐会。新届会长就职。左起：卸任会长王志维、经文处处长廖朝宏、新届会长赖怡音、侨教中心主任高家富、新届副会长彭声翔。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月23日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨摩顿市报导）12月21日冬至佳节，新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会在摩顿市（Malden）的五味海鲜自助火锅店举办尾牙餐会及换届典礼，感谢会员们支持各项公益活动，庆祝新会长赖怡音就职。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14660510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660510-DSC05783.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660510-DSC05783-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660510" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会12月21日举办尾牙餐会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14660509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660509" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660509-DSC05772.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660509-DSC05772-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660509" class="wp-caption-text">会员及嘉宾带来缤纷礼品。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>会员与嘉宾同乐 人人有奖品</strong></h2>
<p>当天有60多人参加了尾牙餐会，除了新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会会员之外，还有友好团体如波克莱台湾商会、中华专业人员协会、哈佛大学台湾学生会、波士顿台湾影展协会等等。现场欢声笑语，气氛热闹。</p>
<p>在大家煮火锅欢宴的同时，卸任会长王志维特别唱歌助兴，赢得热烈掌声和欢呼。现场还有抽奖环节，向大家派送商会干部和友好团体捐赠的礼品，最终人人有奖，皆大欢喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14660517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660517" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660517-DSC05817.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660517-DSC05817-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660517" class="wp-caption-text">卸任会长王志维唱歌助兴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14660515" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660515-DSC05804.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660515-DSC05804-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660515" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛台湾学生会会长李典璋（左）向商会赠送感谢状。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈佛台湾学生会会长李典璋向商会赠送了感谢状，感恩商会长期支持学生会的活动。</p>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任高家富也分别致词，感谢商会一直支持台商、服务侨胞、增进台美关系。驻纽约经济组代表谢丞渊也参加了餐会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14660511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660511" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660511-DSC05792.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660511-DSC05792-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660511" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏感谢商会成员服务侨胞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14660512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660512-DSC05794.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660512-DSC05794-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660512" class="wp-caption-text">驻纽约经济组代表谢丞渊参加餐会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖朝宏谈到，今年美国成为台湾厂商海外投资的首选地点，这使台商会的服务机会越来越多，地位越来越重要。“接下来会看到更多更多的新朋友来到我们新英格兰地区。希望在我们新会长的领导之下，我们商会能够越来越茁壮。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14660514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660514" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660514-DSC05802.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660514-DSC05802-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660514" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任高家富向卸任会长王志维、新届会长赖怡音颁赠侨委会褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14660516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660516" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660516-DSC05816.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660516-DSC05816-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660516" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长赖怡音（中）与波士顿台湾影展协会的两位会长合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>新届干部就职 盼扩展服务</strong></h2>
<p>在来宾们的见证下，连任两届的会长王志维卸下职务，交棒给新会长赖怡音。</p>
<p>王志维对于过去4年担任会长的经历及会员们的支持表达感恩，感慨说认识了很多好朋友，体验到一个很温暖的大家庭。他期望，商会未来继续茁壮成长，为侨胞提供更多服务。</p>
<p>王志维还特别感谢卸任副会长欧怡君、执行秘书林思妤，以及两位新届副会长彭声翔、蔡高进的服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14660520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660520" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660520-DSC05830.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660520-DSC05830-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660520" class="wp-caption-text">王志维感谢卸任副会长欧怡君（右）和新届副会长彭声翔。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14660521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660521-DSC05832.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660521-DSC05832-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660521" class="wp-caption-text">王志维感谢卸任执行秘书林思妤（右）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新届会长赖怡音从2017年至今担任新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会及北美台商会理事。她从波士顿大学取得生物医药学士和硕士学位，后于本特利大学（Bentley University）获得工商管理硕士学位。目前她是麻州生物创新研究所（Bioinnovations Institute）的财务总监。</p>
<p>赖怡音向大家表达了冬至及新年祝福。她期待，未来更多年轻人加入商会，乃至担任会长，更有力地帮助台商在美国乃至世界各地发展。她也呼吁商会前辈们积极参加活动，为年轻人提供经验和鼓励。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14660518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660518" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660518-DSC05820.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660518-DSC05820-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660518" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长赖怡音向来宾们敬茶致谢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14660519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660519-DSC05825.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660519-DSC05825-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660519" class="wp-caption-text">商会资深理事柯乃南（左）领取抽奖礼品。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14660522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14660522" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660522-DSC05839.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14660522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14660522-DSC05839-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14660522" class="wp-caption-text">陈家骅（左2）领取抽奖礼品。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新英格兰大波士顿台湾商会创办于1996年，由时任波士顿办事处商务组组长、后曾任中油董事长的林圣忠推动成立。到了2003年，时任会长蔡坤喜认为尾牙是台湾企业界的优秀传统，因而带领商会效仿。接下来历届干部也都延续传统，年底邀集会员聚餐。◇</p>
