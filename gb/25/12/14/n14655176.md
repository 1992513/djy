<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>麻州州长及民众搭“北极特快车”庆圣诞节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655174-DSC05620-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月13日傍晚，在波士顿南站，民众穿着节庆服饰，搭乘“北极特快列车”，欢庆圣诞节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月15日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）12月13日傍晚，波士顿南站（South Station）回响着圣诞节音乐，以及成百上千民众的欢笑声。麻州州长及民众在这里搭乘“北极特快列车”出行，欢庆圣诞节。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d1tvvb2j7fpfrx.cloudfront.net/M2lNENExpE3w"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/embed/1i5th8ep2ql4sbSwEuij9G0Rv19f1c" allowfullscreen></a>
</div>
<p>这场庆典由麻州捷运署（MBTA）的合作伙伴“Keolis Commuter Services”主办。数百民众带着孩子搭乘充满圣诞节气氛的火车，出站行驶一个小时，沿途以唱歌、读故事等形式庆祝佳节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14655169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655169-DSC05587.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655169-DSC05587-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655169" class="wp-caption-text">民众穿着节庆服饰，搭乘“北极特快列车”，欢庆圣诞节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14655175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655175-DSC05621.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655175-DSC05621-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655175" class="wp-caption-text">12月13日傍晚在波士顿南站，民众搭乘“北极特快列车”，欢庆圣诞节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我知道你们大家都在辛勤忙碌，我觉得你们在和圣诞老人的麋鹿合作，创造今年这场前往北极的非常奇幻的旅程。”州长希莉（Maura Healey）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14655170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655170-DSC05591.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655170-DSC05591-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655170" class="wp-caption-text">麻州州长Maura Healey、MBTA总经理伍华伟向孩子们表达节日祝贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14655171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655171-DSC05599.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655171-DSC05599-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655171" class="wp-caption-text">麻州州长Maura Healey向民众表达节日祝贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这段节日行程以邀请制派发车票，由麻州10多个公益团体向它们服务的民众发出邀请。庆典当天，萨福克县地区检察官海登（Kevin Hayden）、波士顿市议员沃雷尔（Brian Worrell）等多位政要嘉宾都来到南站，向民众表达节日祝贺。主办方还向民众派发“北极特快线”特别纪念饼干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14655172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655172-DSC05614.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655172-DSC05614-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655172" class="wp-caption-text">参加活动的嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14655173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655173-DSC05618.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655173-DSC05618-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655173" class="wp-caption-text">12月13日傍晚在波士顿南站，民众搭乘“北极特快列车”，欢庆圣诞节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MBTA总经理伍华伟（Phillip Eng）说，这场庆典是回馈社会的活动。“今天我们增加一段特别的旅程，带你们大家，你们的朋友、家人、爱人前往北极。像州长讲的那样，我们将带你们安全到站，准时到站，还能将你们带回家。”他说。◇</p>
