<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗县议员关切苗美术馆、营养午餐、废弃物倾倒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649512-814205-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>苗县议会第20届笫6次县政质询（许享富／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年12月05日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏苗栗台湾报导）苗栗县议员黎尚安、许樱萍、陈品安5日进行总质询，分别关切总图是否设置美术馆、学校免费营养午餐扩及老师及连续2起校安问题防护等。县长钟东锦认同总图可结合美术馆，免费营养午餐明年先将校长及班导师纳入，另外，接连2起校安事件，钟东锦表示，县府有1925咨询专线，也希望大家如果发现身边的人有异状时，要互相打电话关心。</p>
<p>黎尚安议员指出，县内有许多知名艺术家，如版画家陈永钦老师、书法家谢奕富老师荣获版画及书法双首奖，但县内艺文空间长年不足，建议县府争取中央兴建的总图书馆，将部分楼层作为美术馆使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649515" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649515-814207.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649515-814207-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649515" class="wp-caption-text">议员黎尚安质询提出，苗美术馆的设置、新盛隆村的道路拓宽、侨文国小的西侧的归还黎宗亲会的问题（县长提双赢的解决，保留地上物的留用。）（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14649516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649516" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649516-814208.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649516-814208-600x405.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649516" class="wp-caption-text">议员黎尚安质询。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦指出，总图结合美术馆确实有必要，他认为，目前文化观光局内美术馆的展览动线不对、方位也不好，未来总图搬迁后，可以将文观局原址改建为美术馆及行政大楼，甚至中正堂如果年限到了以后，改建时可挖地下室连接苗栗火车站设置美食街，做更完善的整体规划。</p>
<p>钟东锦表示，如果财画法早日实施，这个构想就可以早日启动，他希望中央朝野要尽快协商，建立可长可久的制度，让地方有所依循。</p>
<p>许樱萍议员感谢钟县长明年起将学生营养午餐提高到60元，并由县府及乡镇市各负担一半30元经费。她建议应该要扩及到校长及老师，让校长和老师跟学生一起吃，为营养午餐的菜色把关。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649517" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649517-814209.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649517-814209-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649517" class="wp-caption-text">议员许樱萍质询提出，苗建案能否与地方先沟通、营养午餐品质、敬老爱心卡、苗青创中心活化，供青创的展售，不同于夜市。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14649518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649518" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649518-814210.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649518-814210-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649518" class="wp-caption-text">议员许樱萍质询。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14649519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649519-814211.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649519-814211-600x418.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649519" class="wp-caption-text">议员许樱萍质询提出。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钟东锦指出，苗栗县因财政比较困难，但为照顾县内学生健康，因此打造出全台第一个由县府与乡镇市合办免费营养午餐的县市。针对议员建议让所有师生都吃营养午餐，他表示，全县有六千多位教师，初估每年需增加7千多万元经费，经费不小，但他认为可将校长先纳入跟学生们一起吃，也可掌握营养午餐的品质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649514" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649514-814206.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14649514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649514-814206-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649514" class="wp-caption-text">钟县长表示，感谢乡镇公所能齐心与县府合作办营养午餐，暂将校长与导帅纳入用餐与评核，也是全国首创。苗美术馆的设计将现有原总图、文光局和中正堂一并考入，做更完善整体规划。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈品安议员就近日连续发生校园安全议题指出，县府相关单位应该主动关怀，而不是被动的等电话再处理，尤其也要把教职员及家长也纳入为辅导对象。她认为，县府应该把连续出现的校安事件当成战争来处理，建立更完善的社会安全网，把身边的每一个人都接住。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14649520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649520" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649520-814212.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649520-814212-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649520" class="wp-caption-text">议员陈品安质询提出，苗栗毒地案外案、废弃物非法倾倒苑里、秋茂园殡葬设施争议、通霄铁道米仓建筑群的观光亮点等（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14649524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14649524" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649524-814218.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14649524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14649524-814218-600x388.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14649524" class="wp-caption-text">议员陈品安 殡葬问题。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
