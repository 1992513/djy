<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>余雅伦接余风采堂主席 宗亲吃汤圆庆冬至</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652838-DSC05485-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙余风采堂12月7日举办换届典礼。新届职员及嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月11日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）代表余氏宗亲的纽英崙余风采堂12月7日举办换届典礼，由前副主席余雅伦升任主席。宗亲们敬拜祖先，还乐享台山汤圆，欢庆冬至节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14652831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14652831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652831-DSC05464.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14652831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652831-DSC05464-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14652831" class="wp-caption-text">宗亲们敬拜祖先。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14652832" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14652832" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652832-DSC05465.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14652832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652832-DSC05465-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14652832" class="wp-caption-text">宗亲们敬拜祖先。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在波士顿华埠的余风采堂会所，顾问余宗涛主持典礼。宗亲及嘉宾们首先向余氏祖先忠襄公敬香，行三鞠躬礼。随后在元老余超群的见证下，原副主席余雅伦接过印信，就任主席；而原主席余立昌则退居副主席。</p>
<p>“我接手主席的职位，希望大家多多帮忙，多多指教，多多支持。”余雅伦说。</p>
<p>余立昌笑言“光阴似箭”，转眼担任主席已有四年。未来期盼年轻有为的余雅伦主席继续带领余风采堂提升族务，吸引更多乡亲参加活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14652833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14652833" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652833-DSC05467.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14652833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652833-DSC05467-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14652833" class="wp-caption-text">新届主席就职。左起：副主席余立昌、主席余雅伦、元老余超群。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>嘉宾道贺 齐庆佳节</strong></h2>
<p>当天，侨教中心主任高家富携助理林建璋参加了典礼，并代表侨委会和驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处，向余雅伦、余立昌颁赠褒奖。</p>
<p>纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉出席致贺。他说，余风采堂有近百年历史，向来与中华公所合作服务侨社。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14652836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14652836" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652836-DSC05479.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14652836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652836-DSC05479-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14652836" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富（右）向新届主席、副主席颁赠褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14652835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14652835" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652835-DSC05471.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14652835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652835-DSC05471-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14652835" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉向余风采堂新届职员致贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在顾问余宝爱介绍了新届职员之后，余氏宗亲们端出热气腾腾的台山汤圆，欢庆即将到来的冬至节。高汤鲜美可口，加入了猪肉、鸡肉、虾仁、蔬菜等诸多食材，令大家赞不绝口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14652837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14652837" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652837-DSC05484.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14652837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652837-DSC05484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14652837" class="wp-caption-text">余宝爱介绍新届职员。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14652839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14652839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652839-DSC05490.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14652839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652839-DSC05490-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14652839" class="wp-caption-text">宗亲们吃台山汤圆庆冬至。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14652840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14652840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652840-DSC05491.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14652840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14652840-DSC05491-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14652840" class="wp-caption-text">宗亲们吃台山汤圆庆冬至，高汤内食材丰富。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
