<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨声音乐社庆圣诞节 筹备明年两场演出</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656630-DSC05635-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿侨声音乐剧社12月14日欢庆圣诞节，嘉宾合影。左起：波士顿市议员Ed Flynn、中华公所主席雷国辉、侨声社长李伍碧香、黄国威、社员雷玉霞、梁永基。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）圣诞节即将到来之际，波士顿侨声音乐剧社12月14日在中华公所举办庆典聚会，自制美食招待来宾，并公布明年的演出计划。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656631-DSC05638.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14656631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656631-DSC05638-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656631" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿侨声音乐剧社12月14日欢庆圣诞节。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>社员烹饪佳肴 嘉宾齐庆节日</strong></h2>
<p>当晚中华公所大厅内美食飘香，有咖喱鱼丸、香烤牛排、炸春卷、炒杂菜、蔬菜沙拉、芋头西米露、木瓜糖水等诸多佳肴，都由侨声音乐剧社的社员们制作，令大家交口称赞。</p>
<p>社长黄国威感谢各位嘉宾及社员，尤其从纽约远道而来的社员们的光临。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656633-DSC05643.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14656633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656633-DSC05643-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656633" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿侨声音乐剧社12月14日欢庆圣诞节。社员们制作了诸多美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656634" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656634-DSC05644.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14656634" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656634-DSC05644-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656634" class="wp-caption-text">社员们制作了咖喱鱼丸、香烤牛排等美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉说，侨声音乐剧社有87年历史，举办了许多盛大演出，为华人增光不少，对侨社贡献良多，而且也长期支持中华公所的工作。</p>
<p>波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）亦来到现场，向侨声送上节日祝福。他也感谢社员们坚持传承粤曲艺术，传播中华文化。</p>
<p>餐会期间，黄国威和黄兆培分别演唱歌曲，深情投入，收获掌声。现场还有抽奖环节，气氛热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656635-DSC05653.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14656635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656635-DSC05653-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656635" class="wp-caption-text">当晚有抽奖环节，中奖者欢乐领奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656632-DSC05639.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14656632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656632-DSC05639-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656632" class="wp-caption-text">部分社员与市议员Ed Flynn合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>2026年奉献两场演出</strong></h2>
<p>侨声另一位社长李伍碧香除了感谢社员为这场晚会准备美食之外，也介绍了明年的演出计划。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656628" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656628-DSC05628.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14656628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656628-DSC05628-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656628" class="wp-caption-text">黄国威（左）和卢善柔演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656629-DSC05633.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14656629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656629-DSC05633-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656629" class="wp-caption-text">黄兆培演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年2月7月，侨声音乐剧社将为社区带来折子戏和粤曲演唱表演。6月他们将在剧院再次演出《帝女花》全剧。李伍碧香期盼大家明年继续支持侨声的演出。◇</p>
