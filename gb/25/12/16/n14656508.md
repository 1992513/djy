<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进铜墙铁壁里 摄影家在西屿弹药本库的凝视</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656517-816823-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>从洞窟到地面库舍，西屿弹药本库完整保留日治时期军事工程的原貌，也成为今日少见能亲身走入的“铜墙铁壁”。（陈锡兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年12月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬澎湖报导）走进澎湖西屿弹药本库，摄影家陈锡兴的第一个感觉，不是壮观，而是安静。洞窟里的声音很快被吸收，只剩下脚步声在地面回荡。他说，那一刻就很清楚，这里原本就不是给人停留的地方，而是为了存放弹药而存在。厚重的铜板一片片拼接在墙面上，铆钉排列整齐，拱形天花板顺着洞体延伸。相机对准正中央，画面自然形成对称，仿佛整个空间早已决定好拍摄的位置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656509" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656509-816815.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656509-816815-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656509" class="wp-caption-text">铜板覆面的洞窟通道笔直延伸，墙面以铆钉固定，形成厚实而冷冽的金属结构。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656510" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656510-816816.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656510-816816-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656510" class="wp-caption-text">洞道在转弯处微微收敛，厚重金属墙面包覆整个空间，让人感受到这座弹药库为防爆、防潮所做的层层设计。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>铜墙铁壁 结构自己会说话</h2>
<p>陈锡兴在洞窟弹药库里拍得很慢。他刻意让镜头保持水平，不去追求角度变化。“这种地方不需要设计构图，结构本身就已经很清楚。”墙面因为长年潮气与氧化，呈现出深浅不一的色泽，灯光从地面向上打，铜墙显得沉稳而厚实。拍到洞窟尽头那面完整封闭的墙时，他停留了一段时间，才按下快门。那是一面没有开口的墙，却让人感受到最直接的防御意图。</p>
<h2>从洞窟走到地面 军事思维的另一种样子</h2>
<p>离开洞窟后，陈锡兴转向地面型的清凉弹药库。灰白色的建筑低矮厚重，窗户狭小，墙体几乎没有多余装饰。他刻意把建筑拍得完整，不避开斑驳与裂痕。“这不是老化，是使用过的痕迹。”在他看来，这些细节比修复后的整齐更真实，也更接近这座建筑原本的状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656511" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656511-816817.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656511-816817-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656511" class="wp-caption-text">以自然通风与温湿控制为设计重点的清凉弹药库，木构空间取代金属墙面，呈现与洞窟弹药库截然不同的使用思维。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656512" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656512-816818.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656512-816818-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656512" class="wp-caption-text">隐身于地景之中的库口低调而封闭，厚实墙体与简单入口，正是军事设施“不显眼即是安全”的最佳体现。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一口水井 看见长期驻守的准备</h2>
<p>在库区角落，还留有一口水井。圆形井口简单朴实，旁边是石墙与沙包。陈锡兴特别为这个画面停下来，他说，水井代表的是另一层军事现实，长时间驻守与自给自足。这样的设施不显眼，却比任何说明文字都清楚，让人理解当年这里不只是仓库，而是一个完整运作的军事据点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656513" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656513-816819.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656513-816819-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656513" class="wp-caption-text">厚重的钢制防爆门半掩，门栓与转轴清楚可见，这扇门曾是阻绝爆炸冲击的最后一道防线。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656514" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656514-816820.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656514-816820-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656514" class="wp-caption-text">嵌在铜墙中的温度计，记录着洞内恒定的环境条件。对弹药而言，稳定比速度更重要。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>防爆门前 时间没有被关上</h2>
<p>弹药库入口那扇厚重的防爆门，是他拍摄时最有感觉的地方之一。门板锈蚀，边角剥落，门框上仍留有编号。门半掩着，里面是昏暗的洞道。“它没有被关起来，反而像是把时间留在那里。”陈锡兴说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656515" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656515" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656515-816821.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656515-816821-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656515" class="wp-caption-text">导览图标示出洞窟弹药库、清凉弹药库与监守卫舍的位置，揭示整个军事设施的完整配置与动线规划。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656516" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656516-816822.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656516-816822-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656516" class="wp-caption-text">这样的设计，为的是在爆炸时分散冲击力，保护整个库体结构。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>留下空间本身 就是最好的保存</h2>
<p>陈锡兴认为西屿弹药本库最珍贵的地方，在于人们仍能实际走进去。不是隔着玻璃看，不只是文字介绍，而是站在铜墙之中，感受温度、湿气与距离。“这里不需要多说什么，只要站在里面，你就会知道它曾经是什么。”而他的照片，正是替这座安静的军事空间，留下仍然清楚的证言。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14656518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656518" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656518-816824.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656518-816824-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656518" class="wp-caption-text">刻有“澎湖县县定古迹 西屿弹药本库”字样的石碑，标志这处军事设施已转为文化资产，从战略据点走入公共记忆。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656519" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656519-816825.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656519-816825-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656519" class="wp-caption-text">弹药本库内设有独立水井，供应日常与紧急使用。简单的圆形井口，反映当年军事设施对自给自足的高度要求。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14656520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14656520" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656520-816828.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14656520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14656520-816828-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14656520" class="wp-caption-text">军事用途转化为今日的历史现场，静静存在于聚落边缘。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
