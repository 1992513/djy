<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中医师解析频尿成因 自我调理改善夜尿</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571297-786021_medium.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>半夜起床尿尿，已成为老人家的生活日常，但是超过2次就算不正常。根据国际尿控协会（International Continence Society），夜间睡眠期间需起床排尿超过2次（含），则属夜尿（Nocturia）症状。</p><p><strong>⊙西医观点</strong></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p>现代医学解释造成夜尿的原因，大致分为以下几类：</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p><strong>1.睡前饮水过多</strong>：睡前大量饮水，或摄取含咖啡因、酒精等利尿的饮品。</p><p><strong>2.疾病</strong>：由膀胱过动症、糖尿病、心脏衰竭、慢性肾脏病、焦虑症、失眠症等引起。</p><p><strong>3.药物影响</strong>：某些药物，譬如利尿剂，会使尿液变多。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p><strong>4.其它</strong>：怀孕、摄护腺肥大等，增加夜尿的风险。</p><p><strong>⊙中医观点</strong></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p>中医将频尿分为下列三种类型：</p><p><strong>肾阳虚</strong>：常见于老年人、久病体虚、阳虚体质者；
<strong>心肾不交</strong>：伴有夜间惊醒、梦遗、失眠；
<strong>肝郁气滞</strong>：常因情绪因素诱发或加重。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p>中医从上述类型来辨证论治，依据不同体质给予相应的中药、针灸、穴位按摩或食疗等治疗方式，并配合调整生活习惯，如睡前少饮水、避开咖啡因饮品等。</p><h2><span style="color:#008000">四大方法自我调理 缓解频尿</span></h2><p>藉由这四大方法自我调理，可减缓夜尿症状：</p><p><span style="color:#0000ff">1. 睡前3小时少碰饮品</span></p><p>睡前3小时少喝水，且减少饮用利尿的酒、茶叶、咖啡等。睡前如果口渴，可以喝一口水，一小口一小口地慢慢吞下。</p><p><span style="color:#0000ff">2. 骨盆底肌运动</span></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>骨盆肌肉无力可能导致膀胱无力、夜尿，甚至尿失禁等问题。透过练习“凯格尔运动”，或是屁股夹紧动作，以有效地改善情况。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p>凯格尔运动：请看视频 ??《<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KY7YGjKTsPo" target="_blank" rel="noopener" target="_blank"
