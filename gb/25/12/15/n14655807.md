<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中华公所大同村庆圣诞节 人人有礼物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655803-DSC05565-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月13日，中华公所及大同村居民欢庆圣诞节。阮鸿灿扮演的“圣诞老人”欢乐进场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年12月16日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）12月13日下午，波士顿华埠的中华公所大厅内人满为患，热闹喜庆。大同村居民在这里庆祝圣诞节，每一位参与者都获得礼物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14655801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655801-DSC05560.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655801-DSC05560-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655801" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所董事们在圣诞节彩灯下合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大同村是属于中华公所的平价住房产业。每年圣诞节前，大同村居民会都会携手中华公所团队，举办庆祝活动。</p>
<p>今年的庆典之前，中华公所及大同村管理公司的职员们已经包好了上百份礼物，还为中华公所大厅妆点了“Merry Christmas”彩灯和缤纷的圣诞树。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14655802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655802" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655802-DSC05563.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655802-DSC05563-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655802" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉与圣诞节礼物合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到了周六，阮鸿灿扮演的“圣诞老人”首先摇着铃铛进场，向大家抛洒糖果。现场每一位参与庆祝的居民都领到了抽奖券，抽取礼品和红包，最终人人有奖，而且还得到中华公所赠送的烧腊套餐，皆大欢喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14655800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655800" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655800-DSC05554.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655800-DSC05554-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655800" class="wp-caption-text">义工们为居民准备烧腊套餐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14655804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655804-DSC05574.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655804-DSC05574-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655804" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员Ed Flynn（左）、Erin Murphy（右）与圣诞老人合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）、墨菲（Erin Murphy）也都到场，向居民表达圣诞节祝福，向中华公所及大同村颁赠市议会贺状。</p>
<p>中华公所主席雷国辉感谢居民长期支持他们，也感谢负责管理大同村产业的Beacon公司，以及大同村居民会的工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14655805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655805-DSC05578.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655805-DSC05578-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655805" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员颁赠贺状，左起：中华公所英文书记阮鸿灿、大同村管理办公室经理翁丽芳、中华公所主席雷国辉、市议员Ed Flynn、Erin Murphy、中华公所财政余宝爱、核数张青梅。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14655806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14655806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655806-DSC05585.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14655806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/12/id14655806-DSC05585-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14655806" class="wp-caption-text">参与活动的居民皆获赠礼物。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大同村管理办公室经理翁丽芳亦代表居民，感恩中华公所为居民谋求福利，每年都这么照顾大家。◇</p>
