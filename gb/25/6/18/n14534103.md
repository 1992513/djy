<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>舞动“焕”新夜 梦舞团年度演出登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534087-DJY06776-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蔡君柔带领的“CJT梦舞团”6月14日晚在勒星顿（Lexington）举办年度演出。（章佩儿/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月18日讯】（大纪元记者章佩儿勒星顿镇报导）6月14日晚，CJT梦舞团（CJT Dream Dance）在麻州勒星顿基督学院（Lexington Christian Academy）礼堂举办了主题为“Revival | 焕”的夏日舞蹈盛会。这是蔡君柔（Mia Tsai）老师在大波士顿地区从事舞蹈教学15年来，举行的第7场年度夏日舞蹈演出。演出融合中西舞蹈之美，汇集了中国古典舞、现代舞、K-Pop/Hip-Hop流行舞、芭蕾舞和爵士舞等多元风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14534081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534081" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534081-DJY06651.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534081-DJY06651-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534081" class="wp-caption-text">“CJT梦舞团”6月14日晚在勒星顿举办年度演出。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534082" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534082-DJY06669.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534082-DJY06669-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534082" class="wp-caption-text">“CJT梦舞团”6月14日晚在勒星顿举办年度演出。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>CJT梦舞团一向强调舞蹈风格的多元性，致力于通过舞蹈桥梁，实现东西方艺术的对话。蔡君柔老师表示，梦舞团希望“向西方传达东方舞蹈艺术的古典美，同时向东方传达西方舞蹈的现代美”，通过多元舞蹈形式，让观众感受中西文化交融之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14534079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534079" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534079-DJY06626.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534079-DJY06626-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534079" class="wp-caption-text">工作人员欢迎观众进场。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534080-DJY06643.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534080-DJY06643-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534080" class="wp-caption-text">主持人欢迎观众欣赏晚会。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“Revival（焕）”这一主题，寓意着浴火重生和焕然一新的开始。蔡君柔老师解释道：“我们希望可以有容光焕发，然后重生一次的感觉，浴火凤凰。所以我们希望可以脱胎换骨，让大家看到我们新的一面。”</p>
<p>她希望借由凤凰涅槃的意象，鼓舞舞者和观众感受重获新生、焕发光彩的力量。这一理念也贯穿于整场演出的编排中。节目既有表现蝶变重生的现代舞“凤凰”（Rising from the Ashes），也有象征涅槃的三部曲大型舞蹈作品“焕Revival”系列。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14534083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534083" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534083-DJY06715.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534083-DJY06715-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534083" class="wp-caption-text">“CJT梦舞团”演出精彩舞蹈。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534084" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534084-DJY06735.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534084-DJY06735-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534084" class="wp-caption-text">“CJT梦舞团”演出精彩舞蹈。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534088" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534088-DJY06788.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534088-DJY06788-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534088" class="wp-caption-text">“CJT梦舞团”演出精彩舞蹈。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次演出的演员阵容横跨老中青三代，年龄从3岁至60岁不等。除了梦舞团的学员外，不少曾在全美各地舞蹈赛事中摘金夺银的新秀舞者，也受邀登台。值得一提的是，蔡君柔老师今年带领CJT梦舞团，在多个舞蹈比赛中表现优异，斩获了“最佳评审奖”、“最佳编舞奖”、“团体舞第一名”、“独舞第一名”等多项冠军荣誉。</p>
<p>这些在舞坛崭露头角的梦舞团学生和杰出舞者们，也在当晚的舞台上赢得满堂喝彩。观众们欣赏到千变万化的舞蹈风格，包括刚柔并济的中国舞，情感丰沛的现代舞，以及激情动感的流行舞等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14534085" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534085" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534085-DJY06746.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534085" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534085-DJY06746-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534085" class="wp-caption-text">“CJT梦舞团”演出精彩舞蹈。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534086" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534086-DJY06755.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534086-DJY06755-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534086" class="wp-caption-text">“CJT梦舞团”演出精彩舞蹈。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534089-DJY06875.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534089-DJY06875-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534089" class="wp-caption-text">“CJT梦舞团”演出精彩舞蹈。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演出的成功离不开幕后艰辛的付出。蔡君柔老师分享今年筹备过程中的一个特别挑战，就是她在怀孕中仍带领团队排练。尽管身体上有疲惫，但团队提前开始准备，并在学生们的配合下，她也能将困难化为动力。</p>
<p>“今年的准备过程比较特别，有一个宝宝，所以体力上有些吃力。但我们很早就开始准备，这些学生也非常乖，一切都非常顺利。”蔡老师笑言。她对学生们的勤奋与聪明大加赞赏。她表示，正是这份对梦想的坚持和热爱，让演出得以顺利呈现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14534090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534090" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534090-DJY06904.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534090" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534090-DJY06904-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534090" class="wp-caption-text">大量观众到场欣赏舞蹈晚会。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534091" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534091-DJY06910.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534091-DJY06910-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534091" class="wp-caption-text">演出结束，演员谢幕。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534093-DJY06929.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534093-DJY06929-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534093" class="wp-caption-text">蔡君柔老师发表感言，致谢观众和各方支持者。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“再辛苦我们也要坚持每年办一次演出。”蔡君柔说。她相信，每个孩子都有自己的舞蹈梦想，舞台能够让他们“发光发亮”。从2018年的“A Dance in Bloom”到今年的“Revival | 焕”，梦舞团一年一度的夏日舞蹈盛会，让更多热爱舞蹈的年轻人有机会站上舞台，实现梦想、绽放光彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14534095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534095" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534095-DJY06935.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534095-DJY06935-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534095" class="wp-caption-text">舞团演员们合影。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14534096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14534096" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534096-DJY06948.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14534096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14534096-DJY06948-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14534096" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富（右）到场欣赏演出，称赞节目精彩。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨教中心主任高家富首次观赏了梦舞团的演出。他在谢幕后上台向蔡君柔致意，并称赞演出成功。</p>
<p>蔡君柔的预产期在8月。她说孩子是女生，希望有一天能和女儿同台演出。◇</p>
