<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>杨采妮将登台北电影节 与张之亮同颁百万首奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537035-2506230937391487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台北电影奖颁奖嘉宾：香港导演张之亮与演员杨采妮共同揭晓百万首奖。（台北电影节／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月23日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）第27届台北电影节颁奖典礼将于7月5日举行，今（26）日公布第二波颁奖嘉宾名单，影展最高荣耀的“百万首奖”将由香港导演张之亮与演员杨采妮共同揭晓。两人曾于电影《自梳》中合作，本次将于北影舞台重聚。</p>
<p>本届“卓越贡献奖”将颁发给长年深耕影评领域的张昌彦，并由同为资深影评人、亦曾获此殊荣的黄建业担任颁奖人，象征影评同侪之间的专业致敬与长久情谊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14537036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537036-2506230940581487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14537036" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537036-2506230940581487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537036" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖嘉宾：春风（洪瑜鸿）将与柯震东搭档，揭晓声音设计与最佳新演员两奖。（台北电影节／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，今日揭晓的七组颁奖嘉宾亦各具亮点。以《白衣苍狗》入围最佳男配角的春风（洪瑜鸿），将与柯震东搭档揭晓声音设计与最佳新演员两奖项；柯佳嬿与金曲歌后蔡健雅则将携手公布最佳配乐与最佳男配角，展现影视与音乐的跨界魅力；林辰唏与程予希将联手颁发最佳剪辑与最佳纪录片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14537037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537037" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537037-2506230937411487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14537037" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537037-2506230937411487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537037" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖嘉宾：柯佳嬿与蔡健雅。（台北电影节／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14537038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537038-2506230937501487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14537038" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537038-2506230937501487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537038" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖嘉宾：程予希与林辰唏。（台北电影节／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最佳视觉效果与最佳摄影将由今年的“非常演员”刘奕儿与蔡凡熙共同揭晓；去年以《周处除三害》获得最佳男配角的李李仁，则将与本届“非常演员”钟欣凌一同登台，颁发杰出技术与最佳女配角两项荣誉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14537039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537039-2506230937431487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14537039" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537039-2506230937431487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537039" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖嘉宾：刘奕儿与蔡凡熙。（台北电影节／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14537040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537040" style="width: 594px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537040-2506230937481487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14537040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537040-2506230937481487.jpg" alt="" width="594" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537040" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖嘉宾：李李仁将与本届“非常演员”钟欣凌同台颁奖。（台北电影节／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导演马志翔将与以《小雁与吴爱丽》入围最佳女主角的夏于乔一同揭晓最佳编剧奖；最佳男主角则由北影影帝王柏杰与傅孟柏联袂颁发。七组嘉宾涵盖不同世代与领域，不仅展现影坛多元风貌，也为典礼增添更多期待焦点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14537065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537065" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537065-2506231002011487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14537065" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537065-2506231002011487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537065" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖嘉宾：马志翔与夏于乔。（台北电影节／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14537066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537066" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537066-2506231001591487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14537066" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537066-2506231001591487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537066" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖嘉宾：王柏杰与傅孟柏。（台北电影节／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本届典礼亦邀请影坛实力派演员于子育担任表演嘉宾，睽违多年再度于典礼舞台开口演唱。于子育自2020年于金马奖演唱《孤味》主题曲后，她特别选唱三部女性主题电影的代表歌曲，并结合逾30部描绘女性经验的影像片段，带来一场视觉与听觉交织的动人演出。</p>
<p>于子育表示，这次演出不仅是对经典的致敬，更是作为演员重新诠释歌曲的一次挑战；透过表情、眼神与声音说故事，让每一首歌都成为观众心中某段记忆的延续。她也形容这场表演是一封献给所有影人的情书，从“揭开下个十年的序章”到“未完待续的旅程”，以歌声致意，向每位走在电影路上的创作者献上敬意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14537067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537067" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537067-2506231005561487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14537067" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537067-2506231005561487.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537067" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖典礼表演嘉宾于子育。（台北电影节提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>延续台北电影节邀请演员为典礼播放影片进行配音的传统，今年特别邀请2021年“非常新人”韩宁担任典礼配音。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14537068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14537068" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537068-2506231005581487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14537068" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14537068-2506231005581487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14537068" class="wp-caption-text">台北电影奖颁奖典礼今年特别邀请2021年“非常新人”韩宁担任典礼配音。（台北电影节提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025台北电影节将于6月20日至7月5日在台北市中山堂、信义威秀影城、光点华山电影馆举办。7月5日台北电影节颁奖典礼将由华视主频12台全程LIVE转播。台北电影节官方YT频道及 “LINE TODAY 电影”也将同步转播。</p>
