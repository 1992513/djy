<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>时光与烟霞之间 旧台中火车站的记忆之窗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528549-772503-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>摄影家林桔春分享了他十年前拍摄的一组照片，正是2015年台铁128周年庆活动于台中火车站举行的纪实。（林桔春提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾报导）六月九日是台湾铁路节，摄影家林桔春分享了他十年前拍摄的一组照片，正是2015年台铁128周年庆活动于台中火车站举行的纪实。影像中，黑色蒸汽火车DT668停靠在月台边，蒸气喷吐，群众围绕，镜头捕捉到一段铁道文化难得的场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14528550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528550" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528550-772504.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528550-772504-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528550" class="wp-caption-text">影像中，黑色蒸汽火车DT668停靠在月台边，蒸气喷吐，群众围绕。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14528548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528548" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528548-772502.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528548-772502-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528548" class="wp-caption-text">旧台中火车站。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林桔春说：“我曾在台湾铁路总局审计处服务一年半，那是观光号与光华号奔驰的年代。铁路节让我特别有感触，因为我自己就是台铁人。”那一天，他也与当年的站长合影，照片中笑容诚挚，是一种对铁道文化最深刻的致敬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14528551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528551" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528551-772505.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528551-772505-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528551" class="wp-caption-text">林桔春说：“我曾在台湾铁路总局审计处服务一年半，那是观光号与光华号奔驰的年代。”（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14528552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528552" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528552-772506.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528552-772506-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528552" class="wp-caption-text">十年前活动的那一天，林桔春与当年的站长合影，照片中笑容诚挚，是一种对铁道文化最深刻的致敬。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14528549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528549" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528549-772503.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528549-772503-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528549" class="wp-caption-text">摄影家林桔春分享了他十年前拍摄的一组照片，正是2015年台铁128周年庆活动于台中火车站举行的纪实。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000">红砖与钟楼 台中车站的欧风建筑</span></h2>
<p>
1917年完工的台中车站，是日本时代的砖造巴洛克风格建筑，钟楼矗立在主体上方，见证超过百年的风雨与人流。无论是从正门抬头望见的对称三角山墙与精致饰雕，或是走进候车大厅的挑高拱门与光影交错，都能让人感受到历史的厚重与建筑的优雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14528554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528554" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528554-772508.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528554-772508-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="252"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528554" class="wp-caption-text">1917年完工的台中车站，是日本时代的砖造巴洛克风格建筑，钟楼矗立在主体上方。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14528553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528553" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528553-772507.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528553-772507-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528553" class="wp-caption-text">无论是从正门抬头望见的对称三角山墙与精致饰雕，或是走进候车大厅的挑高拱门与光影交错，都能让人感受到历史的厚重与建筑的优雅。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里曾服务过无数旅人，也曾目送多少离乡背井的青年。如今虽已另建新站，但老车站依然静静矗立，如同一位阅历深沉的长者，在城市心脏中守望人群。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14528555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528555" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528555-772509.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528555-772509-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="252"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528555" class="wp-caption-text">如今虽已另建新站，但老车站依然静静矗立，如同一位阅历深沉的长者，在城市心脏中守望人群。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14528561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528561" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528561-772515.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528561-772515-450x294.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="294"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528561" class="wp-caption-text">十年前街头艺人在火车站门口表演。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14528559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528559" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528559-772513.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528559-772513-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528559" class="wp-caption-text">蒸汽火车的鸣笛声、老建筑的砖块纹理……都像时光盒子中未曾散去的声音与影像。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000">静默一角 阿旺师父的擦鞋人生</span></h2>
走进车站大厅的另一端，有一张老椅、几条皮带、磨得发亮的鞋具，以及一位正在低头工作的擦鞋师父——他是被誉为“台湾国宝级擦鞋匠”的黄藤旺老师父，大家亲切称他为“阿旺师”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14528556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528556" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528556-772510.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528556-772510-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528556" class="wp-caption-text">被誉为“台湾国宝级擦鞋匠”的黄藤旺老师父，大家亲切称他为“阿旺师”。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14528557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528557" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528557-772511.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528557-772511-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="252"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528557" class="wp-caption-text">阿旺师说：“我擦的不只是鞋，是客人要见重要人、要面试、要赴约的信心。”（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿旺师曾接受媒体采访，分享自己年轻时随父亲学艺，数十年如一日地在车站替人擦鞋。他说：“我擦的不只是鞋，是客人要见重要人、要面试、要赴约的信心。”照片中可见他专注地替外籍旅客服务，手法俐落，眼神温和，那是一种深耕于生活里的职人精神。
</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000">光阴的回声</span></h2>
铁道，是人与城市之间的缝合线。蒸汽火车的鸣笛声、老建筑的红砖纹理、职人低调的手艺……都像时光盒子中未曾散去的声音与影像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14528558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528558" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528558-772512.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528558-772512-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528558" class="wp-caption-text">铁道，是人与城市之间的缝合线。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林桔春的镜头不只纪录风景，更捕捉了“人”与“记忆”的互动。十年前的火车节活动、黄师父的擦鞋角落，还有那陪伴无数人南来北往的古老站体，皆是台中人共同的情感记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14528560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14528560" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528560-772514.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14528560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528560-772514-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14528560" class="wp-caption-text">林桔春的镜头不只纪录风景，更捕捉了“人”与“记忆”的互动。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当高铁与捷运改变了移动的节奏，或许我们更该停下脚步，记得这一站的风华——那个被岁月雕琢、由人情温暖的台中火车站。</p>
