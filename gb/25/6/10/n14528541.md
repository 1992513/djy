<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>半夜痛风突然发作 该如何是好？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/gout5_728192224-600x359.jpg" />
<h6>半夜痛风突然发作，常常使您从睡梦中惊醒，该如何是好？(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><b>编者按：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">半夜痛风突然发作，怎么办？本文分别从中医与西医角度，解析痛风的成因，并为您介绍实用的自我调养原则与有效的防治手段。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">痛风最常发作在大脚趾的关节处（第一跖趾关节）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">发作时，受累关节会出现</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">发热、肿胀与压痛，甚至连轻轻触碰都令人难以忍受。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>预防与改善痛风 中医讲究整体调养</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">中医将痛风归为“痹证”、“历节风”或“痛痹”等病证，</span><b>主要由热、湿邪侵袭，引发气血运行不畅，加之脾虚运化失调、肝肾亏虚等内因所致</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">预防与改善痛风，中医采用下列方法以调理脏腑功能与体质：</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13798295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13798295" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13798295-shutterstock_2029397909.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13798295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/id13798295-shutterstock_2029397909-450x231.jpg" alt="为什么有些人体内尿酸高，却没有痛风？因为引发痛风还有一个关键。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13798295" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">中医将痛风归为“痹证”、“历节风”或“痛痹”等病证</span>。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>一、自我调养原则</b></span></h3>
<p><b>1.清热祛湿通络以土茯苓为佳品</b></p>
<p><b>?作法：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">每日以50?90克土茯苓干品泡水代茶饮用，可治疗痛风。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">土茯苓性平味甘，入肝胃经，有“清热泻火、祛湿通络”的功效。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?若配伍薏苡仁、赤小豆、白术，则有“健脾化湿”的作用。</span></p>
<p><b>2.宜温阳散寒</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?痛风好发于夜间或天冷时，故应注重保暖避湿，尤其脚部保温最为关键。</span></p>
<p><b>3.避开寒饮冷食，多吃清热利湿食物</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?避开冰冷生寒饮品，如冷饮、冰啤酒等，坚持喝温热水或药茶，以保以护脾胃阳气。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?勿食煎炸、烧烤、辛辣刺激物等食品。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?多吃清热利湿的食物，如绿豆、苦瓜、冬瓜、丝瓜等。</span></p>
<p><b>4.顺应时令，调和情志</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?保持良好情绪，避免劳倦忧思，并顺应四时变化。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?中医提倡“春夏养阳、秋冬养阴”，冬春换季时，痛风体质者应特别注意保暖与节制饮食。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>二、以外治法辅助</b></span></h3>
<p><b>?针灸：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">中医常通过针刺“足三里、阳陵泉、三阴交、阴陵泉”等穴位，调理肝、脾、肾功能，改善代谢与气血运行。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12706405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12706405" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/ZuSanLi-Acupuncture-Point-1-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12706405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/ZuSanLi-Acupuncture-Point-1-1-450x300.jpg" alt="按摩、艾灸八个关键穴位，帮助你提高防疫、抵抗病毒的能力。（健康1+1／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12706405" class="wp-caption-text">足三里穴。（健康1+1／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11106217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11106217" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/xuewei2-e1557867628612.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11106217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/xuewei2-e1557867628612-450x280.jpg" alt="膝关节痛，按摩脾经上的阴陵泉穴和它所对应的阳陵泉穴。（谈古论今话中医提供）" width="450" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11106217" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">阴陵泉穴在小腿内侧，胫骨内侧末端凸起的后下方凹陷处。阴陵泉穴在阳经上的对应穴位，叫阳陵泉穴。（谈古论今话中医提供）</span></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11463954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11463954" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/sanyinjiao-point.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11463954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/sanyinjiao-point-450x290.jpg" alt="三阴交穴。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11463954" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">三阴交穴在小腿内侧，内踝尖上四横指处，胫骨内侧缘后际。（Shutterstock）</span></figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><b>拔罐、刮痧、艾灸</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：这些方法亦可帮助祛寒湿、通经络。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><b>局部放血</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：是一种非常有效的疗法。需由专业医师操作，以避免感染及误用。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用对象：痛风急性期、红肿热痛明显、有瘀血阻络者。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">功效：清热泻火、活血通络、解毒止痛。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>西医防治痛风 侧重降尿酸值</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">西医认为，痛风的根本是尿酸代谢紊乱；尿酸浓度过高，超出血液可溶解的上限时，会形成结晶，沉积在关节和组织中，引起炎症反应。主要诱因包括：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?摄入过多高嘌呤食物（红肉、动物内脏、海鲜）；</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">?饮酒，尤其是啤酒与烈酒；</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">?肥胖、代谢症候群；</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">?肾功能变差时，尿酸排出效率跟着下降。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">如何降低尿酸浓度以防治痛风？西医建议如下：</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.避开高风险食材</b></span></h3>
<p><b>?</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">避吃</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">高嘌呤（普林）食物，例如红肉（如牛肉、羊肉）、动物内脏（如肝、肾）、海鲜类，特别是贝类与甲壳类（如贻贝、扇贝、虾、蟹），以及部分鱼类，如凤尾鱼、沙丁鱼、鳟鱼与金枪鱼等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14040058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14040058" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14040058-shutterstock_1326676574.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14040058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14040058-shutterstock_1326676574-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14040058" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">避吃高嘌呤（普林）食物</span>。（健康1+1／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://www.hpa.gov.tw/EngPages/Detail.aspx?nodeid=1425&amp;pid=8448" target="_blank" rel="noopener">急性发作时，不宜大量食用整粒黄豆或豆制品</a>。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?勿摄取酒精饮料（特别是啤酒）。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?避喝高果糖玉米糖浆（HFCS）的饮料，如手摇饮、汽水、运动饮料、瓶装果汁等。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.饮水适度且适量</b></span></h3>
<p><b>?灵活调整饮水量：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">喝水可稀释并促进排出尿酸。应视季节、出汗量、体质等灵活调整饮水量。建议多喝水，但注意不要喝过量而引起水中毒。（延伸阅读：</span><span style="color: #0000ff;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/22/7/30/n13792325.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener">很多人喝水喝错了！1种喝法当心伤脑甚至致命</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14211302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14211302" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211302-Shutterstock_1548860402-1080x720.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14211302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id14211302-Shutterstock_1548860402-1080x720-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14211302" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="font-weight: 400;">喝水可稀释并促进排出尿酸。</span>(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>?温饮胜于冷饮：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">饮水的关键是多次少量、以温水为宜，也就是要避免喝冰水，以免损伤脾胃功能。</span></p>
<p><b>?茶与咖啡勿饮用过量：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">茶与咖啡都不是痛风患者的禁忌。研究显示，每天适量饮用咖啡（如1～2杯）能降低发作风险。不过，过量饮用咖啡恐引起心悸、失眠、胃部不适等问题，因此应适度饮用。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.控制体重、坚持运动</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">运动有助于改善代谢功能，控制体重，但应避免突然的剧烈运动，导致体内乳酸增加，反而抑制尿酸排出。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13949686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13949686" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949686-shutterstock_566995273.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13949686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13949686-shutterstock_566995273-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13949686" class="wp-caption-text">运动。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.定期监测尿酸指数</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">高尿酸或有痛风史者，应定期监测尿酸指数。必要时，在医生指导下服用降尿酸药物。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">痛风虽非急症，却因反复发作严重影响生活品质。通过中西医结合的方式——中医注重体质辨证与整体调养，西医重视代谢机制与科学饮食，可大幅降低发作频率并改善体质。</span><strong>◎◇</strong></p>
