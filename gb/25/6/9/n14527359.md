<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>打造家庭菜园 一起来种豌豆吧！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527399-P.69-5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>菜价一直上涨怎么办？一起“在家种豌豆”吧！（苹果屋提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong></p>
<p>营造家庭菜园，只要一个盆栽，不洒药也能种出鲜甜好吃的蔬菜！常吃的、想吃的都可以自己种，从选苗到采收，轻松打造自给自足的家庭菜园。
</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>栽种果实类<strong>：</strong>豌豆</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14527408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527408-P.68-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527408-P.68-1-450x682.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="682"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527408" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>DATA</b></p>
<p><b>科目：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">豆科</span><br />
<b>难易度：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">★</span><br />
<b>栽种场所：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">日照充足处</span><br />
<b>生育适温：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">15～20℃</span><br />
<b>株间：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">20～30cm</span><br />
<b>连作障碍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">有</span><br />
<b>易发生的病虫害：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">白粉病、细菌性斑点病、蚜虫、斑潜蝇</span><br />
<b>盆器尺寸：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">大型?直径35cm?高34cm</span><br />
<b>栽种时程：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">如图</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527407" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527407" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527407-P.68-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527407-P.68-2-450x473.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="473"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527407" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●选苗</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">?前端的芽没有折损。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">?叶片颜色深。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">?茎上的节间无徒长。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">?植株健壮。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527406" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527406-P.68-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527406-P.68-3-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527406" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●</b><b>定植</b></span></h2>
<p><b>立支柱：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">架设圆形支柱。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527405" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527405-P.68-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527405-P.68-4-450x309.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="309"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527405" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>把幼苗种进盆栽内：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">在支柱旁边挖出比黑软盆还要大一点的洞，把幼苗从黑软盆取出放进盆栽内，覆土后轻压，再浇满水。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527404" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527404-P.68-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527404-P.68-5-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527404" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #ff0000"><b>★</b><b>POINT</b></span></h3>
<p><b>不要太早定植：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">幼苗若在冬季来临前就先长大，会无法负荷寒冬，最好让幼苗（植高10～20cm）度冬。若植株生长到超过高度，可放置在吹不到寒风的地方。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●</b><b>疏苗</b></span></h2>
<p><b>疏苗至留下二株：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">待植株长至15～20cm，进行疏苗，只留下二株健壮、茎粗的幼苗，其余的从底部剪除。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527403" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527403-P.69-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527403-P.69-1-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527403" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>超过三株才须疏苗：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">只有在盆栽内有生长复数株幼苗时才须疏苗。若盆栽内只有二株幼苗则无须疏苗。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527401" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527401-P.69-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527401-P.69-3-450x202.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="202"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527401" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●诱引</b></span></h2>
<p><b>将藤蔓绑在支柱上：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">当植株长至20cm左右，藤蔓开始下垂时，便诱引到支柱上用绳子绑起。之后只要发现藤蔓长长，便随时诱引。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527402" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527402-P.69-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527402-P.69-2-450x309.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="309"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527402" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>将长长的藤蔓固定在一起：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">生长中的藤蔓会自然攀上支柱，而藤蔓也会互相缠绕在一起，最好将藤蔓诱引至支柱上。</span><b></b></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●追肥</b></span></h2>
<p><b>施肥：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">度冬后在气温变暖前的二</span><span style="font-weight: 400">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400">三月左右，将肥料</span><span style="font-weight: 400">施于植株周围。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527400-P.69-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527400-P.69-4-450x322.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="322"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527400" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●</b><b>采收</b></span></h2>
<p><b>从蒂头剪下：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">待果实长大到圆润饱满，豆荚开始生皱纹后，用剪刀采收。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527399" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527399-P.69-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527399-P.69-5-450x312.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="312"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527399" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527398" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527398-P.69-6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527398-P.69-6-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527398" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #ff0000"><b>★品尝美味小诀窍</b></span></h3>
<p><b>慢慢放凉是让表皮漂亮的关键：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">豌豆汆烫完，若马上泡冷水，会因为急速冷却而让豆荚产生皱纹。建议</span><span style="font-weight: 400;color: #800000">迅速汆烫后关火，直接放在原锅热水中降温。这么做便能</span><span style="font-weight: 400">让豌豆的表皮平整，</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><span style="color: #800000">得到美丽翡翠色的豌豆</span>。</span><strong>（网站专文）</strong><strong>（本文摘编自《<span style="color: #0000ff"><a style="color: #0000ff" href="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010983341" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow noreferrer">好种易活！盆栽种菜全图解：无农药、安心吃</a></span>！全年栽种时程表X 55种蔬菜培育秘诀，新手也能四季都丰收》，苹果屋提供）◇</strong></p>
