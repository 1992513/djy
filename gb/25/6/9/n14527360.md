<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在家就能享受田园乐趣 盆栽种菜︰栉瓜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527425-P.77-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小盆栽就能享受田园乐趣 盆栽种菜︰栉瓜。（苹果屋提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>营造家庭菜园，只要一个盆栽，不洒药也能种出鲜甜好吃的蔬菜！常吃的、想吃的都可以自己种，从选苗到采收，轻松打造自给自足的家庭菜园。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>栽种果实类<strong>：</strong>栉瓜</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14527437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527437" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527437-P.76-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527437-P.76-1-450x702.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="702" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527437" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>DATA</b></p>
<p><b>科目：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">葫芦科</span><br />
<b>难易度：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">★★</span><br />
<b>栽种场所：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">日照充足处</span><br />
<b>生育适温：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">20～25℃</span><br />
<b>株间：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">80～100cm</span><br />
<b>连作障碍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">不太发生</span><br />
<b>易发生的病虫害：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">白粉病、蚜虫</span><br />
<b>盆器尺寸：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">大型?宽34cm?长34cm?高36cm</span><br />
<b>栽种时程：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">如图</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527436-P.76-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527436-P.76-2-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527436" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>●选苗</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?叶片颜色深。</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">?没有被虫蛀。</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">?茎上的节间无徒长。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527435" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527435-P.76-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527435-P.76-3-450x353.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="353" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527435" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>●定植</b></span></h2>
<p><b>把幼苗种进盆栽内：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">在土壤中挖出比黑软盆还要大一点的洞，把幼苗从黑软盆取出放进盆栽内，覆土后轻压，再浇满水。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527434" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527434-P.76-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527434-P.76-4-450x305.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527434" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>●追肥</b></span></h2>
<p><b>施肥：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">定植后过一个月左右，将肥料施于植株周围。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527433" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527433-P.76-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527433-P.76-5-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527433" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #ff0000;"><b>★</b><b>POINT</b></span></h3>
<p><b>不用担心叶片上的白色花纹：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">栉瓜叶片上的白色花纹（图左），是天生的，此特征容易跟白粉病（图右）混淆，只要辨识清楚就不用过度担心。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527432" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527432-P.76-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527432-P.76-6-450x193.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="193" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527432" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>●立支柱</b></span></h2>
<p><b>架设支柱：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">当茎干长长，植株无法平衡时，就需要架设支柱，用绳子把茎和支柱固定在一起。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527431-P.77-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527431-P.77-1-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527431" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>植株摇摇晃晃时就立支柱：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">虽然不会长藤蔓，但因植株会长很大，为了预防倒塌及折损，必须立支柱帮助固定。之后若再继续长大，到时候再加用绳结固定。</span></p>
<p><b>让植株底部保持清爽：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">变黄的叶片和枯萎的雄花都是生病的来源，若发现有这种现象要立即摘除。</span><b></b></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527430" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527430-P.77-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527430-P.77-2-450x293.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527430" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>●人工授粉</b></span></h2>
<p><b>准备好雄花：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">剪下雄花后，剥除花瓣，让雄蕊整根露出来。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527429" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527429-P.77-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527429-P.77-3-450x304.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527429" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>进行授粉：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">将雄花的花粉沾在雌蕊上。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527428-P.77-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527428-P.77-4-450x312.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527428" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>分辨雄花与雌花：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">仔细看花的根部，有鼓起的是雌花，没有鼓起的是雄花。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527427" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527427-P.77-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527427-P.77-5-450x220.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="220" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527427" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>剪除畸形果：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">因为授粉不完全才会长出畸形果，这会使得果实腐烂，养分无法传送给其他果实，所以只要发现畸形果，就要立即用剪刀从根部剪掉。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527426" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527426-P.77-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527426-P.77-6-450x355.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527426" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>●采收</b></span></h2>
<p><b>从果实根部剪下：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">待栉瓜长到20～25cm后，用剪刀采收。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527425" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527425-P.77-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527425-P.77-7-450x310.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527425" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
