<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>体验鲜采现吃的乐趣！盆栽种菜︰白萝卜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527336-P.113-6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>感受“鲜采现吃”乐趣！盆栽种菜︰白萝卜。（苹果屋提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<div class="info">
<div class="tags"><strong>编者按：</strong>营造家庭菜园，只要一个盆栽，不洒药也能种出鲜甜好吃的蔬菜！常吃的、想吃的都可以自己种，从选苗到采收，轻松打造自给自足的家庭菜园。</div>
</div>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>栽种果实类<strong>：</strong>白萝卜（小型品种）</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14527346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527346" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527346-P.112-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527346-P.112-1-450x694.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="694"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527346" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>DATA</b></p>
<p><b>科目：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">十字花科</span><br />
<b>难易度：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">★</span><br />
<b>栽种场所：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">日照充足处</span><br />
<b>生育适温：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">15～25℃</span><br />
<b>株间：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">15cm</span><br />
<b>连作障碍：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">有</span><br />
<b>易发生的病虫害：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">青虫、蚜虫、菜心螟、斑潜蝇</span><br />
<b>盆器尺寸：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">中型（深型）?宽22cm?长45cm?高27cm</span><br />
<b>栽种时程：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">如图</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527345" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527345-P.112-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527345-P.112-2-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527345" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●</b><b>播种</b></span></h2>
<p><b>点播播种：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">挖出五个直径3cm、深约1cm的洞，洞的间隔距离约15cm。每个洞不重叠地放入4～5粒种子，补土后轻压。再浇满水。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527344" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527344-P.112-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527344-P.112-3-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527344" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #ff0000"><b>★</b><b>POINT</b></span></h3>
<p><b>以“直播栽培”为基本：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">白萝卜是不耐移植的蔬菜，若以黑软盆育苗后再移植到盆栽内，根会过短、也易分歧，所以一定只能在盆栽内播种栽培。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●</b><b>疏苗</b><b>1</b></span></h2>
<p><b>疏苗至留下二株：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">待本叶完全展开，拔除细小、弯曲的幼苗，每个洞各留下二株幼苗。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527343-P.112-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527343-P.112-4-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527343" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>覆土：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">将土壤覆盖至植株底部，轻压土面。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527341" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527341-P.113-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527341-P.113-1-450x304.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="304"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527341" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527342-P.112-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527342-P.112-5-450x162.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="162"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527342" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<ul>
<li>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●</b><b>疏苗</b><b>2</b></span></h2>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>疏苗至留下一株：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">当本叶长出4～5片，拔除叶片有折损及孱弱的幼苗，每个洞各留下一株植株。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527340" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527340-P.113-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527340-P.113-2-450x304.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="304"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527340" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527338" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527338-P.113-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527338-P.113-4-450x307.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="307"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527338" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527339" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527339-P.113-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527339-P.113-3-450x159.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="159"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527339" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●</b><b>追肥</b></span></h2>
<p><b>施肥：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">播种后过一个月左右，将肥料施于植株周围。之后以一个月一次为基准追肥。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527337" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527337-P.113-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527337-P.113-5-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527337" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>●</b><b>采收</b></span></h2>
<p><b>整株拔起：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">待根部的直径长至6</span><span style="font-weight: 400">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400">7cm左右，握住茎叶底部往上拔起。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14527336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527336" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527336-P.113-6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14527336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527336-P.113-6-450x303.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="303"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527336" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>长到适当大小就赶紧采收：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">一旦错过采收期，白萝卜会开始裂开造成内部空洞，口感变得不佳。请依白萝卜的大小依序采收，注意别错过最适时机。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #ff0000"><b>★品尝美味小诀窍</b></span></h3>
<p><b>白萝卜上下分开料理：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">白萝卜有上半部甘甜、软嫩、粗壮，下半部呛辣、偏硬、细长的特征，因此建议分开来，使用在不同的料理中。卤白萝卜时适合用上半部，口感、味道都会很好；而下半部切成小块状来炒或煮较为合适。◇</span><strong>（网站专文）</strong><strong>（本文摘编自《<span style="color: #0000ff"><a style="color: #0000ff" href="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010983341" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow noreferrer">好种易活！盆栽种菜全图解：无农药、安心吃</a></span>！全年栽种时程表X 55种蔬菜培育秘诀，新手也能四季都丰收》，苹果屋提供）</strong></p>
