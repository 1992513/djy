<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>副主任黄依莉荣退欢送会 温馨举行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527926-02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>副主任黄依莉荣退欢送会切蛋糕大合影。（陆希／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月10日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）6月8日（星期日）下午3点，由中华公所主办的“副主任黄依莉荣退欢送会”在侨教中心展览室隆重举行。会议由共同主席叶林素芬（Jewelry Lim Yep）及前主席唐心琴主持，侨社20多个社团代表出席，送上纪念礼物，感谢黄依莉四年来对侨民社区的贡献并祝贺她荣退后前程似锦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527925-01.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527925-01-600x474.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="474" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527925" class="wp-caption-text">叶林素芬（Jewelry Lim Yep）、黄依莉（中）和李雄（左）合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>唐心琴特别赞扬黄依莉在中文教育方面对学校老师的指导和鼓励，深受大家感佩。西南区管委会主席李雄颁发特别认证证书，并赠送纪念衫与德州徽章扣环，让她留念休斯顿的美好时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527928-04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527928-04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527928" class="wp-caption-text">侨联总会休士顿办事处代表们和黄依莉（右二）合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527929-05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527929-05-600x433.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527929" class="wp-caption-text">美南大专校联会代表们和黄依莉（右四）合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527930-06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527930-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527930" class="wp-caption-text">伍氏公所Joyce Woo Eng\黄依莉（中）和叶林素芬（右）合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>多个团体代表依次送上心意：菲律宾工商会会长密麦兰、侨务促进委员江丽君、世华工商妇女美南分会前会长张琼文、侨务顾问洪良冰、救国团团友会、同源会、侨联总会休士顿办事处主任李迎霞、研究中山学术研究会唐心琴、华侨协会程美华、伍氏公所及美南大专校联会等，均带来礼物及祝福。程美华特别以自制点心表达心意；李迎霞则将四年前黄依莉抵达时的报导剪报集结成册，提诗及日期纪念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527927-03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527927-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527927" class="wp-caption-text">来自奥斯汀的团体歌唱表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527931-07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527931-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527931" class="wp-caption-text">蛋糕“高升”部分特别献给黄依莉。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，奥斯汀中华联谊会及客家会的四位代表，受李迎霞邀约特别出席，并带来《送别》与《夜来香》团体表演，为欢送会增添温馨色彩。</p>
<p>最后，大家齐聚共同主席叶林素芬的邀请下，与黄依莉一起切下写有“步步高升”的蛋糕，将“高升”部分特别献给她，并在合影与享用蛋糕饮料的欢乐氛围中，画下完美句点。</p>
