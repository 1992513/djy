<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>文协国乐团“乐闻四方”演绎多元乐曲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527280-DSC06070-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿文协国乐团6月7日举办“乐闻四方”年度音乐会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月09日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨勒星顿镇报导）从古典乐曲《良宵》到南美音乐《自由探戈》，从《文成公主》乐章到日本动漫主题曲《哆啦A梦》，文协国乐团6月7日晚上的年度音乐会“乐闻四方”展现无界的多元艺术。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527285-DSC06141.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527285-DSC06141-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527285" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿文协国乐团6月7日举办“乐闻四方”年度音乐会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>传统融合现代 观众称赞</strong></h3>
<p>
文协国乐团的41周年音乐会，当晚在麻州勒星顿镇（Lexington）卡利纪念礼堂（Cary Memorial Building）举办，吸引多族裔的许多观众到场欣赏。观众向音乐家们报以热烈掌声，并在演出之后称赞节目精彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527282-DSC06077.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527282-DSC06077-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527282" class="wp-caption-text">陈志新担任乐团指挥。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527279" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527279-DSC06061.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527279-DSC06061-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527279" class="wp-caption-text">陈志新担任乐团指挥。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这是非常棒的演出，我觉得最精彩的部分，就是他们音乐的多样种类，他们有比较传统的中国乐曲，也有西方音乐。”第一次欣赏文协国乐团演出的Izzy Leginsky说。他还说，他最喜欢的曲目，是笙协奏曲《文成公主》。</p>
<p>同样第一次聆听这个乐团演奏的Leo Vanciu也称赞，每个曲目的演绎都非常好，其中他最喜欢的节目就是《自由探戈》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527281-DSC06071.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527281-DSC06071-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527281" class="wp-caption-text">古筝老师杨信宜参与乐团演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>用心改编 节目丰富</strong></h3>
文协国乐团的演出，由波士顿大学音乐博士陈志新担任指挥。今年的音乐会，还邀请香港资深演奏家郭承祖担任特邀嘉宾，演绎笙协奏曲《文成公主》。</p>
<p>陈志新介绍说，这场演出包含了多种音乐，其中的现代动漫音乐，需要特别改编，才能适合国乐演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527283-DSC06121.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527283-DSC06121-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527283" class="wp-caption-text">下半场演出以打击乐开场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527284-DSC06130.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527284-DSC06130-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527284" class="wp-caption-text">香港资深演奏家郭承祖担任特邀嘉宾，演绎笙协奏曲《文成公主》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>关于音乐会的主题，陈志新谈到，“乐闻四方”的意思，就是“没有国界，音乐都是共通的”。他说，希望向观众展现，中国乐器不仅仅能演奏民乐，也能精彩演绎西方或其它音乐。</p>
<p>“蛮多都可以的，中乐、西乐，中国乐器都可以玩得来，没问题的。”陈志新说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527278-DSC06057.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527278-DSC06057-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527278" class="wp-caption-text">乐团创办人之一欧阳东美（左1）、GBCCA会长赵宁（左2）为两名即将高中毕业、离开乐团的成员颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚在主持人宣布节目开始之后，大波士顿中华文化协会（GBCCA）会长赵宁首先致词，欢迎观众到场，感谢音乐家们奉献演出。随后，赵宁及乐团创办人之一欧阳东美，为即将从高中毕业并离开乐团的两名成员（即两名主持人）Jason Katz和Cecilia Zhang颁奖。</p>
<p>侨教中心主任高家富夫妇都到场欣赏演出，并向乐团表达41周年祝贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527277-DSC06025.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527277-DSC06025-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527277" class="wp-caption-text">左起：乐团创办人之一欧阳东美、侨教中心主任高家富、古筝老师杨信宜。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演出的上半场，以轻快、刚健的合奏乐曲《松》开场，接下来是著名探戈舞曲《一步之遥》，由二胡曲改编为吹管合奏曲的《良宵》，日本动漫乐曲《千与千寻》，中国民乐《赶街》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14527287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527287-DSC06167.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527287-DSC06167-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527287" class="wp-caption-text">乐团指挥陈志新和特邀嘉宾郭承祖演出后收获鲜花。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14527288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14527288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527288-DSC06172.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14527288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14527288-DSC06172-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14527288" class="wp-caption-text">乐团参演成员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中场休息过后，下半场第一个节目是陈志新带领的打击乐《节庆锣鼓》，接下来有南美音乐《自由探戈》，特邀嘉宾郭承祖带领的笙协奏曲《文成公主》，民乐《春风得意》，动漫乐曲《哆啦A梦》，以及颇具西域风情的《丝绸之路》。演出最后，为答谢观众的热情掌声，国乐团还特别奉上安可曲《男儿当自强》。演出在观众的欢呼声中落下帷幕。◇</p>
