<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2025泳渡澎湖湾5000公尺热血登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536149-775227-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025泳渡澎湖湾5000公尺热血登场。（西屿乡公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月22日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾澎湖报导）22日一早天光尚未完全明亮，澎湖西屿大菓叶海滩便已涌入人潮，为的正是这一年一度的“2025泳渡澎湖湾”活动。今年路线全长5000公尺，自大菓叶起点横渡外海，挑战极限、考验体能与意志，吸引来自全台选手齐聚参与，包括铁人三项选手、开放水域爱好者以及立桨选手，场面壮观热烈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14536151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536151-775229.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536151-775229-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536151" class="wp-caption-text">今年路线全长5000公尺，自大菓叶起点横渡外海，挑战极限、考验体能与意志。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14536150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536150-775228.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536150-775228-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536150" class="wp-caption-text">西屿乡长李添进亲自带领加油团出席。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>全员集结 黄帽起跑</h2>
<p>清晨六点钟，选手们戴上统一的黄色泳帽，穿戴救生浮标与防寒泳衣，分批排队检录。尽管海风微凉，气氛却极为热络，不少人高举V字手势合</p>
<p>影、彼此加油打气。现场也有多位选手带着五颜六色的漂浮装备与号码识别装置，为长距离海泳做好万全准备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14536152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536152-775230.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536152-775230-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536152" class="wp-caption-text">全员集结黄帽起跑。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14536153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536153" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536153-775231.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536153-775231-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536153" class="wp-caption-text">现场也有多位选手带着五颜六色的漂浮装备与号码识别装置，为长距离海泳做好万全准备。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>乡长领队 全力相挺</h2>
<p>西屿乡长李添进亲自带领加油团出席，并与来自各地的选手击掌加油。他表示：“大菓叶不只是西屿最美的海岸线之一，也是体验海上活动的绝佳场地。看到大家为挑战自我来到这里，令人感动也振奋！”在象征性的起跑仪式上，乡长与嘉宾高举手臂、挥舞加油棒，场面热闹非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14536150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536150-775228.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536150-775228-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536150" class="wp-caption-text">西屿乡乡长李添进与来宾高举右手，为选手们加油打气！（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14536155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536155-775233.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536155-775233-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536155" class="wp-caption-text">尽管海风微凉，气氛却极为热络，不少人高举V字手势合影、彼此加油打气。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>五公里挑战 强者齐聚</h2>
<p>本届活动中不乏熟面孔，有历年参赛者挑战连霸，也有初试啼声的新血报名。从岸边望去，数百名泳者在海面上整齐排列，救生艇、摩托艇与SUP陪游组也一一就位。随着一声令下，队伍鱼贯而出，黄色泳帽与橘红浮标在阳光映照下，如同在海上绽放的莲花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14536157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536157-775235.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536157-775235-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536157" class="wp-caption-text">SUP陪游组一一就位。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14536156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536156-775234.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536156-775234-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536156" class="wp-caption-text">本届活动中不乏熟面孔，有历年参赛者挑战连霸，也有初试啼声的新血报名。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>SUP陪游 展现团队力量</h2>
<p>除了泳渡主体，SUP（立桨）陪游团队同样是活动中的焦点。他们肩负引导、协助与安全监控的重任，穿着防晒衣、戴上墨镜，站在板上展现十足专业与风范。不乏女性选手穿着亮丽装束拍照留念，成为活动另一道美丽风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14536159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536159-775237.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536159-775237-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536159" class="wp-caption-text">除了泳渡主体，SUP（立桨）陪游团队同样是活动中的焦点。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14536160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14536160" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536160-775238.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14536160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14536160-775238-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14536160" class="wp-caption-text">SUP组肩负引导、协助与安全监控的重任，穿着防晒衣，展现十足专业与风范。（西屿乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>海泳推广 全民响应</h2>
<p>“泳渡澎湖湾”不仅是一场体能竞赛，更是推广海洋教育与全民运动的重要平台。许多参赛者表示，他们每年都特地安排时间来参加，因为这不只是一场比赛，更是与大海对话、超越自我的旅程。今年活动也特别设置“亲子观战区”与“环保志工区”，呼应永续与共融的精神。</p>
