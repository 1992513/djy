<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃青局前进台北加盟展 设计库团队获逾400万采购额</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539839-776569-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>青年设计团队与企业买主签署合作备忘录。（桃园市青年局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月27日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市青年事务局首度携手“台湾连锁加盟展”平台，于台北世贸一馆举办“设计解决方案商机日”。本次活动邀集12组“桃园设计库”青年设计团队，与来自全台30位企业买主一对一洽谈，现场共促成40场媒合、8份合作备忘录，预估创造429万元采购销售额，展现设计与商业需求的精准对接成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14539840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14539840" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539840-776570.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14539840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539840-776570.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="585" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14539840" class="wp-caption-text">青年事务局侯佳龄局长致词。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为强化设计团队的实战力，青年局于活动前即展开系列辅导，包括提案优化、买主需求分析与商业简报工作坊，协助团队针对企业需求量身打造专业提案。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14539841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14539841" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539841-776571.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14539841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539841-776571.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14539841" class="wp-caption-text">青年设计团队与企业买主洽谈情形。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参与团队服务范围涵盖视觉设计、品牌包装、空间规划、数位行销等，成功吸引连锁餐饮、文创品牌与商圈协会等产业买主洽谈合作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14539842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14539842" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539842-776572.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14539842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539842-776572.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14539842" class="wp-caption-text">青年设计团队详细了解企业买主需求过程。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青年事务局长侯佳龄指出，“桃园设计库”致力于挖掘与培育青年设计人才，不仅提供创意舞台，更注重市场对接与商业能力培养。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14539843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14539843" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539843-776573.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14539843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539843-776573.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14539843" class="wp-caption-text">青年设计团队与企业买主说明设计理念。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次透过加盟展平台的跨界合作，让设计团队于实际采购情境中提升应对能力，突破接案困境，为青年与产业建立长期合作桥梁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14539844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14539844" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539844-776574.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14539844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14539844-776574.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="272" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14539844" class="wp-caption-text">青年设计团队与企业买主大合照。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青年局强调，未来将持续以“设计即商机”为核心，深化设计与产业链接，拓展桃园设计团队的市场触角，打造青年设计服务专业化、全台接单化的支持体系。◇</p>
