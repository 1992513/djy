<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>登顶太麻里小百岳 夏日山海间的心灵对话</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538553-776212-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>连绵山峦与纵谷构筑出一幅台湾南端最壮阔的地貌。（青山农场提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台东报导）6月进入夏季多变气候，天气忽晴忽雨的南回山区，一群满怀期待的旅人。他们踏上“小百岳No.94——太麻里山”的登山行程，不仅为了挑战自我，更为了在山峦海岭之间，寻找一场与自然、与自我的深度对话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14538542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538542" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538542-776201.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14538542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538542-776201-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538542" class="wp-caption-text">不仅为了挑战自我，更为了在山峦海岭之间，寻找一场与自然、与自我的深度对话。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>太麻里山，海拔1,340公尺，是金针山区最具代表性的中级山之一。这天的挑战队伍自青山农场出发，沿着茶园山径而上，行经云雾交错的林间与视野辽阔的棱线。每位成员在“登顶合影”那一刻露出灿烂笑容，围绕着三角点留下这趟征服与共好的纪录，格外动人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14538541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538541-776200.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14538541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538541-776200-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538541" class="wp-caption-text">踏上“小百岳No.94——太麻里山”的登山行程。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14538543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538543" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538543-776202.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14538543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538543-776202-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538543" class="wp-caption-text">太麻里山，海拔1,340公尺，是金针山区最具代表性的中级山之一。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14538544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538544" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538544-776203.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14538544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538544-776203-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538544" class="wp-caption-text">挑战队伍自青山农场出发，沿着茶园山径而上，行经云雾交错的林间与视野辽阔的棱线。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>登山便当 山上最幸福的一餐</h2>
<p>登顶途中，最期待的莫过于“登山便当”时刻。青山农场准备的餐盒中，色彩缤纷、营养丰富的餐点令人眼睛一亮：玉子烧、地瓜、青花菜、小番茄与自家栽种的野菜，一一铺陈在金属便当盒中，在山林绿意与群山辽望的衬托下，每一口都尝得出主人的用心与土地的滋味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14538545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538545-776204.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14538545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538545-776204-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538545" class="wp-caption-text">登顶途中，最期待的莫过于“登山便当”时刻。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14538546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538546" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538546-776205.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14538546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538546-776205-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538546" class="wp-caption-text">色彩缤纷、营养丰富的餐点令人眼睛一亮：玉子烧、地瓜、青花菜、小番茄与自家栽种的野菜。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>山海辽望 张开双臂拥抱南方的壮丽</h2>
<p>从山顶望去，太平洋如画布般铺展眼前，连绵山峦与纵谷构筑出一幅台湾南端最壮阔的地貌。许多伙伴忍不住迎风而立，仿佛向天地诉说“我来过，也留下了脚印”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14538547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538547" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538547-776206.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14538547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538547-776206-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538547" class="wp-caption-text">许多伙伴忍不住张开双臂，迎风而立，仿佛向天地诉说“我来过，也留下了脚印”。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>青山农场绣球花季 仲夏蓝色惊艳</h2>
<p>除了登顶太麻里山，眼下正值青山农场的绣球花季。阶梯入口与巨竹步道一带，紫蓝绣球花虽略过盛期，依然风韵犹存。而位于乌心石林荫下的花海，正值满开状态，层层叠叠地缤纷盛放，让旅人们纷纷驻足拍照留影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14538548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538548-776207.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14538548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538548-776207-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538548" class="wp-caption-text">青山农场绣球花季，仲夏蓝色惊艳。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14538549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14538549" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538549-776208.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14538549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14538549-776208-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14538549" class="wp-caption-text">紫蓝绣球花虽略过盛期，依然风韵犹存。（青山农场提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>一场与土地的对话 从登顶到农场生活</h2>
<p>旅程最后，一行人返回青山农场门口，与农场主人蔡政铭合影。这座以友善农耕、山林教育为理念的基地，不只是登山的起点，更是与地方对话的窗口。当大家指向“青山农场”的木制牌匾时，仿佛也指向了内心对自然的向往。</p>
<p>这一日的太麻里，不仅让人饱览山海壮阔、体验登顶成就，更有机会走入盛夏蓝色绣球花的花季浪漫。欢迎您踏上这条兼具挑战与疗愈的旅程，在自然中重新找回纯粹的感动。</p>
