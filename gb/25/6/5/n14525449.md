<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台实境秀挑战登顶三大圣山 180天内横跨三大洲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14525461-20250605-SETN-DSC_6971_0.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />

不死鸟”郭泓志则将再度挺进台湾圣棱线，挑战这条连结雪山山脉主峰与大霸尖山的棱脉。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex djy-ad"
