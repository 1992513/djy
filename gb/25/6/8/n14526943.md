<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>首届珍珠海岸马拉松接力 千人体验优美海岸风光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526944-771862-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>首届珍珠海岸马拉松接力大合照。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月08日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市副市长苏俊宾8日上午前往新屋区永安渔港，出席首次举办的“桃园市珍珠海岸马拉松接力赛”。苏俊宾表示，市府每年皆以创新方式推广全民运动，今（114）年首度以四人一组接力方式，规划总长21公里的马拉松竞赛，而平日市府负责珍珠海岸计划的相关局处，包含工务局、都发局、环保局等也都组队报名。苏俊宾也与环保局、都发局及体育局等首长组队参赛，显示市府跨局处对于珍珠海岸发展的重视。</p>
<p>苏俊宾指出，珍珠海岸计划是桃园滨海地区重要发展蓝图，赛道即串联永安渔港、海螺文化园区、新屋绿色走廊等核心亮点，非常值得民众前来游憩欣赏。市府团队也藉由赛事参与，实地踏查沿线建设环境，并持续积极向中央争取资源进行优化改善。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14526945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14526945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526945-771863.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14526945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526945-771863-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14526945" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市副市长苏俊宾致词。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14526946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14526946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526946-771864.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14526946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526946-771864-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14526946" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市副市长苏俊宾参与接力赛展现活力。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14526947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14526947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526947-771865.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14526947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526947-771865-600x408.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="408"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14526947" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市副市长苏俊宾与得奖队伍合影。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>体育局说明，本次赛事分“奖金挑战组”与“休闲欢乐组”两大类别，共吸引超过300支队伍、1,200名选手热情参与。本届“奖金挑战组”更汇聚国内众多顶尖跑者，包括万金石马拉松三连霸纪录保持人周庭印，一起共同角逐本次赛事的最高荣誉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14526948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14526948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526948-771866.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14526948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526948-771866-600x376.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="376"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14526948" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市副市长苏俊宾与参赛者展现青春与动感。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14526949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14526949" style="width: 585px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526949-771867.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14526949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526949-771867.jpg" alt="" width="585" b="438"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14526949" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市副市长苏俊宾与市府团队跳跃合影。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>体育局表示，市府配合教育部体育署“114年运动i台湾2.0计划”，结合中央资源推展地方全民运动，去（113）年本市推动成果更以98.8高分获体育署评核为“特优”县市。此外，市府积极推动的“桃园珍珠海岸计划”已获中央核定计划总经费4.9亿元，将串连新屋、观音及大园等桃园沿海地区别具特色的湿地、沙丘、藻礁、牵罟文化与石沪景观等景点，打造兼具文化底蕴与观光潜力的美丽客庄。◇</p>
