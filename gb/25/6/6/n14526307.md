<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夏日最香排骨节！Alliston、万锦市接力开烤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526314-1-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月07日讯】由 Ontario Festival Group 主办的年度排骨节（Ribfest）火热登场，两场活动将分别于 Alliston 与万锦市接力举办，带来让人食指大动的美味盛宴与亲子同乐的夏日嘉年华。</p>
<p class="p1">Ontario Festival Group 邀请北美顶尖烤肉团队，由烧烤高手们（<span class="s1">Pitmasters</span>）为大家带来荣获大奖、令人垂涎的招牌美食，包括：熏烤肋排、嫩滑手撕猪肉、多汁烟熏牛胸肉、<span class="s1">BBQ </span>烤鸡、烟熏香肠、巨型牛肋骨（<span class="s1">Dino Ribs</span>）等。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/6/6/n14526307.md#1/funnel-cake" rel="attachment wp-att-14526315"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14526315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14526315-funnel-cake-450x473.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="473" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">当然少不了丰富的节日小吃：烤玉米、玉米热狗、起司肉汁薯条（<span class="s1">Poutine</span>）、漏斗蛋糕（<span class="s1">Funnel Cake</span>）等，再配上一杯冰凉啤酒，夏日享受满分！</p>
<p class="p1"><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">活动亮点还包括：</span></strong></p>
<p>现场音乐表演（整个周末轮番上阵）</p>
<p>儿童游乐区、脸部彩绘、手工艺市集等丰富活动</p>
<p>Alliston 免费热气球体验！</p>
<p class="p1"><b>门票</b>：</p>
<p class="p1">入场费<span class="s1"> $5</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">10</span>岁及以下儿童免费入场</p>
