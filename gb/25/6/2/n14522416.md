<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>过了青春期还狂冒青春痘？中医教你全方位调理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/acne_720905023-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>过了青春期还狂冒青春痘？中医教你全方位自我调理以提高战“痘”力，摆脱恼人的痤疮，重拾光滑肌肤。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><b>编者按</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">过了青春期还狂冒青春痘？中医教你全方位自我调理，提高战“痘”力，摆脱恼人的痤疮，重拾光滑肌肤。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>西医谈青春痘成因</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">很多民众有个既定观念：“只有青春期才会长痘痘”。其实不然，临床上有不少成年人受其困扰。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">现代医学称痘痘为“痤疮”，好发部位为脸部、背部</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">甚至胸口</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">皮脂腺分泌旺盛，毛孔清洁不干净时，就容易造成毛孔阻塞，受到存在于皮肤表面的痤疮杆菌感染后，就会发炎、形成痤疮。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">相较于青少年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">成年人皮脂腺分泌较不旺盛，痤疮的成因也不尽相同，工作压力大、睡眠不足、贺尔蒙失调以及嗜吃刺激性食物</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">这些都可能导致痤疮。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>中医观点：痤疮位置窥见脏腑问题</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14522429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522429" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522429-1dc532386f0b8f775ad646990fec0902.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14522429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522429-1dc532386f0b8f775ad646990fec0902-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522429" class="wp-caption-text">（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">以中医的角度来看，长在面部不同部位的痤疮，代表着不同脏腑功能失衡。</span></p>
<p><b>?额头冒出痤疮：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">代表心火较旺，常见于过度疲劳及失眠的人。</span></p>
<p><b>?鼻子出现痤疮：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">喜欢吃刺激性食物而造成胃火旺所引起。</span></p>
<p><b>?双侧脸颊长痤疮：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">左脸代表肝，情绪紧绷、压力大的人容易产生肝火，是而痤疮好发于左脸；</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">右脸代表肺，过敏体质的人，痤疮则显现在右脸。</span></p>
<p><b>?下巴有痤疮：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">对应的脏腑为肾，与贺尔蒙浓度的变化有关，许多女性在月经周期间常受此困扰。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">脸上的痘痘虽可反应出脏腑的健康情况，但不是绝对的判断依据，特别是某个部位反复长出痘痘，应寻求专业医师的协助，作进一步的诊察并对症下药。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">狂冒青春痘</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">？</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 中医师教你日常生活战</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">痘</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">守则</span></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">若有痤疮问题，自我调理可从以下几点着手：</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>守则一、保持良好生活习性</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.饮食须均衡，避吃过度调味及高热量的食物，例如烤、辣、炸、过咸、过甜等；宜多摄取蔬菜、水果等富含维生素Ｃ的食材。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.晚上11点至凌晨3点为肝脏进行修复的黄金时段，这段时间应就寝休息，才可让肝脏维持正常运作。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.从事规律运动以利气血循环顺畅，且较不易累积湿与热，从而降低发生发炎的概率。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.面部适当清洁，保持清爽，不过度涂抹保养品或化妆品，以减少肌肤的负担。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>守则二、穴位按摩清热</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">按摩这些穴位，有酸胀感即可。建议每日按摩双侧穴位各15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20次，每次5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10秒。</span></p>
<p><b>1.曲池穴</b></p>
<figure id="attachment_14522426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522426" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522426-f8f4712ecf12264e41f1efd80dfc8cbf.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14522426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522426-f8f4712ecf12264e41f1efd80dfc8cbf-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522426" class="wp-caption-text">曲池穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>位置：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于手肘横纹的外侧凹陷处，属于大肠经的穴位。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">清热消肿，舒缓痘痘发炎的情况。</span></p>
<p><b>2.合谷穴</b></p>
<figure id="attachment_14298937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298937" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298937-c8e1a43b368a977165e8685776873892.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14298937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298937-c8e1a43b368a977165e8685776873892-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298937" class="wp-caption-text">合谷穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">疏散风热，缓解痘痘引起的疼痛。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>守则三、中药茶饮调养</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14522428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522428-9440d553fbe1532f486010515b0e64d1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14522428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522428-9440d553fbe1532f486010515b0e64d1-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522428" class="wp-caption-text">中药茶饮调养。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
