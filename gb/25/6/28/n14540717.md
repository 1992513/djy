<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>石光山海之间 马祖北竿芹壁之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540728-776847-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>林永贤以长曝光捕捉夜里的芹壁，黄光从石屋窗间透出，错落有致，如星星落地。（林永贤提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬马祖报导）马祖北竿，一座与世独立的岛屿，在夏日与海风交织的节奏中，展开一场石屋与海洋的对话。摄影家林永贤在6月23日，用镜头记录下芹壁聚落的白昼与夜晚，带领我们走进这座闽东式石头村的时光廊道，重拾生活的静谧与视觉的纯粹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14540720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540720" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540720-776839.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540720-776839-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540720" class="wp-caption-text">林永贤带领我们走进这座闽东式石头村的时光廊道，重拾生活的静谧与视觉的纯粹。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14540721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540721" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540721-776840.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540721-776840-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540721" class="wp-caption-text">踏上前往芹壁的滨海公路，沿途尽是蜿蜒山路与绝美海湾。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>芹壁石屋群 岛屿最诗意的立体山城</h2>
<p>
踏上前往芹壁的滨海公路，沿途尽是蜿蜒山路与绝美海湾，直到一个转弯，灰白石屋层层堆叠于山壁之上，如诗如画。林永贤的作品中，捕捉到石</p>
<p>头屋依着山势错落排布，整座聚落宛如漂浮于海雾与绿意之间的幻影。</p>
<p>这些用当地花岗岩筑起的闽东式建筑，历经战火与岁月仍屹立不摇，是马祖地区极具代表性的历史聚落。石墙上的苔痕与老木窗框，是时间的印记；而午后阳光洒下时，整个村落仿佛被温柔唤醒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14540722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540722-776841.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540722-776841-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540722" class="wp-caption-text">林永贤的作品中，捕捉到石头屋依着山势错落排列。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14540723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540723-776842.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540723-776842-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540723" class="wp-caption-text">石墙上的苔痕与老木窗框，是时间的印记；而午后阳光洒下时，整个村落仿佛被温柔唤醒。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14540724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540724" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540724-776843.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540724-776843-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540724" class="wp-caption-text">林永贤的镜头从高处俯瞰整个海岸线，芹壁对望着的是海上的小礁岩，远方连接着塘岐与白沙港。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一条蜿蜒海路 通往内心的宁静</h2>
林永贤的镜头从高处俯瞰整个海岸线，芹壁对望着的是海上的小礁岩，远方连接着塘岐与白沙港，而近处的山海线则描出一幅柔美的弧线。这里没有喧嚣市声，只有风与浪，还有偶尔一台小车缓缓经过弯道，像时间在岛上的低语。</p>
<p>在他的一张作品中，芦苇摇曳在道路一侧，前方石屋错落，山峦苍翠，令人不禁屏息凝视，感受那份与世无争的幽静。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14540725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540725" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540725-776844.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540725-776844-450x598.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="598"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540725" class="wp-caption-text">走入芹壁的村道，两侧石屋比肩而立，小巷之间蜿蜒曲折，一扇扇窗、一株株植物，如生活的低语在角落悄然绽放。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14540726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540726" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540726-776845.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540726-776845-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540726" class="wp-caption-text">芹壁地标。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>花与窗 巷弄间的柔光记忆</h2>
走入芹壁的村道，两侧石屋比肩而立，小巷之间蜿蜒曲折，一扇扇窗、一株株植物，如生活的低语在角落悄然绽放。林永贤取景于村内转角，一株百合倚墙盛开，背景正是海天一色与远方的石屋群，画面犹如生活版的油画。</p>
<p>村中的咖啡馆与文创店点缀其间，木椅白桌、藤编灯饰，皆不喧宾夺主，只为陪衬这片岁月静好的空间。这里的午后，喝一杯手冲咖啡，看海发呆，是旅人最奢侈的选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14540729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540729" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540729-776848.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540729-776848-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540729" class="wp-caption-text">林永贤拍摄到夜里的芹壁海湾，海水反射着路灯与屋灯的光芒，轻轻拍打石岸。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>夜幕低垂点灯的芹壁 如星星坠地</h2>
当夜色缓缓降临，芹壁村的灯光渐次亮起。林永贤以长曝光捕捉夜里的芹壁，黄光从石屋窗间透出，错落有致，如星星落地。阶梯旁、观景台上，三三两两的游客静坐闲聊，气氛温暖如画。</p>
<p>林永贤拍摄到夜里的芹壁海湾，海水反射着路灯与屋灯的光芒，轻轻拍打石岸。这不只是摄影的美感，更是一种时间凝结的魔法，让人忘记现实的喧嚣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14540730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540730-776849.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540730-776849-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540730" class="wp-caption-text">林永贤特地带上相机登顶，拍下一幅气势恢弘的全景照：北竿机场跑道横亘于山海之间，金黄沙滩与蓝绿海岸线交错。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>坪顶观景台 俯瞰北竿之心</h2>
从芹壁再往高处而行，可达“坪顶观景台”。林永贤特地带上相机登顶，拍下一幅气势恢弘的全景照：北竿机场跑道横亘于山海之间，金黄沙滩与蓝绿海岸线交错；再远些，是连绵的岛屿与天际交会。</p>
<p>这样的画面，只有在云淡风轻的天气与摄影家的巧思下，才能拍到。这不只是地理上的高度，更是视野上的放松，与心境的开阔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14540734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14540734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540734-776852.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14540734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14540734-776852-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14540734" class="wp-caption-text">来芹壁，不需赶行程，只需放慢脚步。走在石阶巷弄，看海、吹风、喝茶、听浪，便已足够。（林永贤提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>芹壁 值得慢行的美丽聚落</h2>
摄影家林永贤透过一系列日夜交错、角度多元的作品，为我们勾勒出芹壁不只是一处观光景点，更是一段可以倾听的故事。每一扇窗后都有记忆，每一块石头都有岁月，每一道光影之下，都是马祖与自然、人文共筑的日常。</p>
<p>来芹壁，不需赶行程，只需放慢脚步。走在石阶巷弄，看海、吹风、喝茶、听浪，便已足够。</p>
