<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>罗PD考验SEVENTEEN随机舞蹈 场面混乱引笑声</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532120-250616012754100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>SEVENTEEN在综艺节目《NANA bnb with SEVENTEEN》中挑战随机舞蹈。（Hami Video提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月16日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）知名制作人“罗PD”罗暎锡全新韩国综艺节目《NANA bnb with SEVENTEEN》已连续两周夺下Hami Video影剧馆综艺冠军，本周节目迎来最终章。罗PD随机播放SEVENTEEN十年来的所有主打歌来考验他们舞蹈，结果场面混乱逗趣。成员在节目中也展现了好厨艺。</p>
<p>队长S.COUPS先是自信满满说“我们是翻跳团欸”，但成员WOOZI先是说记不住走位，接着12人乱跳舞，罗PD眼见场面失控大喊“够了够了！”没有预期的混乱场面让团员都不禁大喊超失败。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14532123" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14532123" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532123-250616012757100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14532123" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532123-250616012757100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14532123" class="wp-caption-text">《NANA bnb with SEVENTEEN》中，SEVENTEEN成员们胸前都贴上各种食材名称。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而成员们的胸前都贴上各种食材，在歌曲《HOME》音乐播出时，代表虾子的WONWOO圆佑喘着气往正中间移动，其他“食材”开始陆续退后，已在台下观战的MINGYU珉奎质疑“他们根本是在逛大街”，在一旁的JOSHUA也说“第一次看到这种编舞”，S.COUPS瞪大双眼解释“这边我们在走位啊”，HOSHI试图唤醒记忆“这是演唱会版本！”SEUNGKWAN胜寛一句“我们版本太多了，这是要付费才能观看的！”危机处理的紧急发言引起全场爆笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14532124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14532124" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532124-250616012800100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14532124" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532124-250616012800100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14532124" class="wp-caption-text">SEVENTEEN在综艺节目《NANA bnb with SEVENTEEN》中展现逗趣面貌。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了游戏环节，SEVENTEEN的好手艺也再次展现，不仅大火炉煮出来的铁锅盖火锅色香味俱全，THE 8徐明浩的自制特调辣酱更受到全员好评，DINO赞不绝口，说“比市面上卖得还要好吃！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14532122" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14532122" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532122-250616012802100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14532122" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532122-250616012802100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14532122" class="wp-caption-text">SEVENTEEN在综艺节目《NANA bnb with SEVENTEEN》中展现厨艺。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而节目中12人份的大餐结束后的“餐盘山”以及DK认错女团成员的搞笑失误也成为热议话题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14532121" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14532121" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532121-250616012805100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14532121" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14532121-250616012805100707-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14532121" class="wp-caption-text">SEVENTEEN在综艺节目《NANA bnb with SEVENTEEN》中展现逗趣面貌。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，据节目PD申孝静透露，拍摄时曾出现房间毯子和床垫不够的情况，但SEVENTEEN完全没有抱怨，默默让出资源，这番幕后小故事曝光后，网友纷纷赞赏“成员有礼貌又懂事”。</p>
<p>《NANA bnb with SEVENTEEN》共3集于Hami Video全台独家上架。</p>
