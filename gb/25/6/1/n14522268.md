<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>庆祝AAPI月 华夏文协邀移民分享故事</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522263-DSC05750-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月31日，麻州摩顿市（Malden）华夏文化协会举办AAPI月庆祝活动。主讲人和表演团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月02日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨摩顿市报导）“亚裔美国人和太平洋岛民（AAPI）传统月”的最后一天，麻州摩顿市华夏文化协会（Chinese Culture Connection）在当地老人中心举办庆祝活动，邀请来自亚洲的5位移民分享他们的故事。</p>
<p>在5月31日端午节举办的活动中，移民代表们不仅回顾了他们艰辛的移民历程，还展示了各自的文化艺术。最后，听众还享用了源自不同亚洲国家的美食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14522250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522250" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522250-DSC05691.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522250-DSC05691-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522250" class="wp-caption-text">华夏文化协会举办AAPI月庆祝活动。数十名观众参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14522249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522249" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522249-DSC05687.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522249" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522249-DSC05687-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522249" class="wp-caption-text">华夏文化协会会长洪梅介绍活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据华夏文化协会会长洪梅介绍，该协会的AAPI月庆典从2020年的疫情时期开始举办，得到市长柯廷森（Gary Christenson）的鼓励，至今已来到第4届。这个活动的目标，是为亚裔移民提供平台，让他们分享移民美国之路的挑战和成就，向公众展现亚裔移民对美国社会的贡献。</p>
<p>“我们很自豪地看到，这个项目持续成长，与我们的社区产生共鸣。”洪梅说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14522256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522256-DSC05717.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522256-DSC05717-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522256" class="wp-caption-text">观众听亚裔移民分享故事。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>移民回顾辛苦与成就</strong></h3>
<p>当天来到现场分享故事的移民，包括越南华侨及公益组织创办人翁贤（Shyan Ong）、大波士顿巴基斯坦协会主席Ammara Nawaz Khan、阿富汗移民教育家Bahara Hussaini、缅甸移民企业家Hla Hla Win，以及麻州藏传佛教寺院住持Ngawang Geshe-La。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14522253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522253-DSC05707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522253-DSC05707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522253" class="wp-caption-text">越南华侨及公益组织创办人翁贤（Shyan Ong）分享故事。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14522255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522255-DSC05715.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522255-DSC05715-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522255" class="wp-caption-text">大波士顿巴基斯坦协会主席Ammara Nawaz Khan分享故事。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14522257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522257-DSC05722.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522257-DSC05722-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522257" class="wp-caption-text">阿富汗移民教育家Bahara Hussaini分享故事。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14522258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522258-DSC05726.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522258-DSC05726-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522258" class="wp-caption-text">缅甸移民企业家Hla Hla Win分享故事。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14522259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522259-DSC05737.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522259-DSC05737-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522259" class="wp-caption-text">麻州藏传佛教寺院住持Ngawang Geshe-La诵念经文。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>越南移民翁贤是华侨后代。她创办了公益组织“好饭”（Ho Fan, Inc.），致力于节约食物，比如从学校收集吃不完的餐点，用其支持流浪者收容所。</p>
<p>面对数十名观众，翁贤从她小时候与父母逃难讲起，一直谈到在美国建立事业和回馈社会。</p>
<p>她回顾道，当越南被共产党占领的时候，她还是个爱爬树的小孩，在树上看到众多士兵在路上奔走。她也记得，当时很多人为了逃离共产党的迫害，需要贿赂官员，并花很多钱从蛇头处买船票，才能得到离开越南的机会。而她和父母就是逃难群体中的一员。</p>
<p>她还记得逃难的小船上挤满400多人，大家逃到一个岛上，没有住所，没有卫生间。难民们需要自己砍树枝搭建躲雨棚，也不得不在海边解决排泄问题。她记得，有些人在逃难路上死于饥饿或脱水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14522260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522260-DSC05738.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522260-DSC05738-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522260" class="wp-caption-text">“好饭”团体展示书法。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>后来，翁贤和家人被联合国难民组织送到了美国加州，安置在酒店。他们得到别人捐赠的衣物。能在美国建立自己的事业，她至今感激，先辈们克服种种困难，为了孩子能享有更好的生活而做出巨大牺牲。</p>
<p>翁贤感慨：“我们的移民成功，得益于整个社区的努力。”难民们来到美国后，得到了邻里的帮助，以及政府的资助，才获得了教育和住房。</p>
<p>她说，她回馈社会的方式，包括为社区节约食物，以及带领“好饭”团队从事舞狮表演。翁贤表示，她见过饿死的人，不愿看到食物被浪费，因而希望将这些食物用于帮助流浪者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14522252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522252-DSC05698.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522252-DSC05698-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522252" class="wp-caption-text">“好饭”团体表演舞狮。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14522251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522251-DSC05693.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522251-DSC05693-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522251" class="wp-caption-text">“好饭”团体表演舞狮。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>展示文艺 市长致贺</strong></h3>
<p>当天的活动，以舞狮献瑞拉开序幕。“好饭”舞狮团的3头彩狮在锣鼓声中进场，翻腾舞动，为观众送上祝福。</p>
<p>在分享故事之余，移民代表们还展示了各自的文化和艺术。比如，巴基斯坦协会主席Ammara Nawaz Khan用母语演唱歌曲；麻州麦德福市（Medford）藏传佛教寺院住持Ngawang Geshe-La在音乐伴奏下诵念经文。华夏文化协会还邀请“亦知音古筝乐团”到场，演绎古筝音乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14522261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522261-DSC05744.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522261-DSC05744-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522261" class="wp-caption-text">“亦知音古筝乐团”演奏乐曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14522254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522254-DSC05709.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522254-DSC05709-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522254" class="wp-caption-text">市长柯廷森（Gary Christenson）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14522262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14522262" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522262-DSC05745.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14522262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522262-DSC05745-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14522262" class="wp-caption-text">主办方提供多样美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摩顿市市长柯廷森来到现场，向移民们表达祝贺。他特别用中文问候说，“恭喜恭喜恭喜你，周末快乐”，赢得热烈掌声。</p>
<p>“在我成长过程中，我家人说，我们在这里的全部目的，就是要分享、学习和成长。而这正是我与洪梅和华夏文化协会交往时发生的事。”柯廷森说。◇</p>
