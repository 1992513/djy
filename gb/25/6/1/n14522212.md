<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台北举行“《国有器官》全球播映”活动 引关注</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14522213-250601125520100311.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
