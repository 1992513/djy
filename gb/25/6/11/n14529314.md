<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鬼斧神工的海岸线 澎湖竹湾紫菜礁玄武岩石柱群</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529315-772759-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>澎湖的玄武岩 是经过多次火山活动，熔岩从浅海或地下裂隙涌出地面，冷却后堆积而成。（陈锡兴提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月11日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬专访报导）在澎湖西屿竹湾，有一处让人屏息的地质奇观“紫菜礁玄武岩石柱群”。摄影家陈锡兴于2025年6月再度造访这片奇景，他形容这是澎湖最壮观、最具代表性的玄武岩石柱群，绵延超过300公尺，如石墙矗立、石阶层叠，直插碧海蓝天之间，堪比世界自然奇景“北爱尔兰的巨人堤道”。</p>
<p>这些玄武岩，是火山熔岩自浅海或地底裂隙涌出后冷却形成，因冷却速度一致，形成整齐的柱状节理。经过千万年风化与海蚀，柱状岩体或垂直矗立、或横卧如石阶，形态千变万化，是大自然鬼斧神工的代表作之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529316-772760.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529316-772760-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529316" class="wp-caption-text">竹湾海岸线 风化岩石与海蚀平台，和最引人赞叹的柱状玄武岩，与浪花互相冲击，激荡出竹湾最美丽的火花。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14529317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529317-772761.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529317-772761-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529317" class="wp-caption-text">“化石孔龙蛋”是石英砂褐铁矿与风化石，长时间形成了这般景象。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14529318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529318-772762.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529318-772762-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529318" class="wp-caption-text">“顶湾内” 紫菜礁之南，有条岩石浮出水面，名为“插啄平台”，再南突出一条岩石，名为“尾崙仔平台”。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>紫菜礁：潮间带的黑金恩赐</h2>
<p>紫菜礁因盛产天然紫菜而得名。这片礁体深入大海，终年受浪花拍击，是澎湖竹湾村民的食物与经济来源。每逢退潮时，礁上布满海女与村民采收紫菜的身影。这里被誉为“地理教室”，结合地质、生态、产业等多重面向，让人一次窥见澎湖的自然与人文之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529319-772763.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529319-772763-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529319" class="wp-caption-text">连绵不断的玄武岩石柱群（石阶） 堤岸更是可比美“北爱尔兰的巨人堤道”。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14529320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529320" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529320-772764.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529320-772764-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529320" class="wp-caption-text">澎湖最大，最长，绵延300多公尺最壮观的玄武岩石柱群，堪称鬼斧神工世界级的天然景观。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>石中化石：恶地与“孔龙蛋”</h2>
<p>沿着玄武岩石柱的南侧行走，会来到一片赭红色的风化台地，这里是由石英砂与褐铁矿交互作用、长期经雨水侵蚀所形成的恶地地形。摄影家陈锡兴称这片地貌为“蛮荒世界”，其中还可见如圆形巨石般的风化结构，他戏称为“化石孔龙蛋”。这些圆形风化石形成的原因，在于岩层中矿物质分布不均，经年累月风雨雕琢，自成一格，趣味盎然。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529321-772765.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529321-772765-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529321" class="wp-caption-text">竹湾北部全景。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14529322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529322-772766.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529322-772766-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529322" class="wp-caption-text">退潮后露出的堤岸更是可比美“北爱尔兰的巨人堤道”，陈锡兴称它是世界级的玄武岩奇景。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>顶湾内：垂钓天堂</h2>
<p>在紫菜礁的南端，有两座天然平台“插啄”与“尾崙仔”，中间围成一处静谧海湾，名为“顶湾内”。这里风平浪静，成为钓客的天堂。退潮时，更可见海中岩石纹理如章鱼脚般延展，与远方石柱形成奇幻交错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529323" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529323-772767.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529323-772767-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529323" class="wp-caption-text">玄武岩 的中文名称源自日语，得名于兵库县城崎温泉附近的玄武洞，由小藤文次郎于1884年（明治十七年）命名。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>千万年地质的时光诗</h2>
<p>澎湖的玄武岩年龄从1,700万年至800万年不等，紫菜礁一带的玄武岩年代落在1,400万至1,000万年前。这里不仅是地质学家的宝藏地，也成为摄影家、旅人、学者探索自然、感受历史的所在。</p>
<p>玄武岩这名称，源自日本兵库县的“玄武洞”，最早由地质学者小藤文次郎于1884年命名。而“玄武”一词更来自中国神话四象，象征北方与黑色，为这片黝黑壮丽的石柱添上神话色彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529324-772768.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529324-772768-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529324" class="wp-caption-text">荒凉恶地“石英砂褐铁矿区”。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14529325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529325" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529325-772769.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14529325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529325-772769-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529325" class="wp-caption-text">紫菜礁观景台，左上为褐铁矿风化石区，西北崖下即是紫菜礁区。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>拍摄建议与注意事项</h2>
<p>紫菜礁为退潮限定景点，前往请务必查询潮汐表，穿着防滑鞋具，留意海边岩石湿滑与突来浪涌。清晨时分光线最为柔和，亦最适合拍摄玄武岩柱群的线条与海水色泽。</p>
<p>澎湖不只有海与风，还有千万年前火山的记忆化为磅礴石柱，静静守望海天交界的永恒。若你愿意放慢脚步，走入竹湾紫菜礁，将会发现这片土地，有着地球最深沉的语言。</p>
