<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>JIMIN与柾国今退伍：当兵辛苦盼大众善待军人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14528942-250610223356100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）和JIMIN（朴智旻）6月11日自涟川郡退伍并问候大家。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月11日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）继昨（10）日退伍的RM（金南俊）与V（金泰亨）之后，2023年12月以陆军现役身份入伍的防弹少年团（BTS）成员JIMIN（朴智旻）与Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国），今日退伍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529044-250611021854100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529044" title="2218977276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529044-250611021854100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2218977276" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529044" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）和JIMIN（朴智旻）6月11日自涟川郡退伍并问候大家。(ANTHONY WALLACE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>两人在京畿道涟川郡的陆军第5师团炮兵旅团服兵役。JIMIN担任炮兵，Jung Kook则是以料理兵的身份服兵役。他们今日在距离部队不远的涟川运动场与媒体和粉丝见面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529014-250610223353100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529014" title="2219556368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529014-250610223353100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2219556368" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529014" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）和JIMIN（朴智旻）6月11日自涟川郡退伍并问候大家。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14529008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529008" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529008-250610215123100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529008" title="2219555234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529008-250610215123100707.jpg" alt="2219555234" width="400" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529008" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）6月11日自涟川郡退伍并问候大家。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>接过公司工作人员准备的花束后，两人害羞地拿着花束遮住脸，相视而笑。他们也配合媒体的请求，整齐划一的行举手礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529042-250611021847100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529042" title="2218977339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529042-250611021847100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2218977339" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529042" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）和JIMIN（朴智旻）6月11日自涟川郡退伍。(ANTHONY WALLACE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14529040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529040-250611021822100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529040" title="2219554872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529040-250611021822100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2219554872" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529040" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）和JIMIN（朴智旻）6月11日自涟川郡退伍时腼腆地拿花束遮脸。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>

<p>Jung Kook首先感谢迎接他们退伍的记者，接着腼腆地表示自己久违地站在摄影机面前，没有化妆也让他感到有些不好意思，不知自己该说些什么。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529011" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529011-250610215113100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529011" title="2219555826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529011-250610215113100707.jpg" alt="2219555826" width="400" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529011" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）6月11日自涟川郡退伍并问候大家。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>JIMIN也表示：“感谢记者们远道而来，也有粉丝前来，由衷感谢大家。从疫情开始到退伍经过了很长的时间，感谢大家等待我们。今天（11日）退伍了，我想以后我们会继续描绘自己的画，会好好的做准备，再向大家展现更好的样貌。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529015-250610223350100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529015" title="2219556395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529015-250610223350100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2219556395" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529015" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）和JIMIN（朴智旻）6月11日自涟川郡退伍并问候大家。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Jung Kook慰问部队的前后辈们以及比他们早一些退伍的同期同袍，还有部队的干部，并说“为了一起生活，大家很辛苦，感谢大家的照顾”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529016-250610223330100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529016" title="2219555496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529016-250610223330100707-600x401.jpg" alt="2219555496" width="600" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529016" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）成员Jung Kook（田柾国／田桢国）和JIMIN（朴智旻）6月11日自涟川郡退伍并问候大家。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>JIMIN则说，“尽管我不曾轻看军队，但真的很辛苦，不是轻松的地方。（大家）一起辛苦，一起吃饭，一起在外面睡觉，情感也变得很深。想将这些整理起来，在开心的演出中一点一点地分享给大家”，并表示他会怀着感谢之心铭记这些回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14529013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14529013" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529013-250610215144100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14529013" title="2219555852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14529013-250610215144100707.jpg" alt="2219555852" width="400" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14529013" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）成员JIMIN（朴智旻）6月11日自涟川郡退伍并问候大家。(Han Myung-Gu/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后，JIMIN还表示：“想告诉国民们，军队不是轻松的地方。有很多人很辛苦。希望大家走在路上遇到军人时，能以温暖的话语问候他们。”Jung Kook及JIMIN说，他们会在直播中与大家分享军中的点滴。</p>
