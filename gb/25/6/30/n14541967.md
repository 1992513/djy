<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>CARE FEST爱心募款园游会 团结社区为学童募资</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542008-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025 CARE FEST爱心募款园游会嘉宾和受助校方师生合影。（陆希／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月30日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）2025年“CARE FEST爱心募款园游会”于6月28日（星期六）上午11点在中华文化中心体育馆热闹登场。活动由爱心组织联合十多个社团共同举办，吸引数百民众踊跃参与。所有当日义卖及善款收益，将全数用于购买学用品，捐赠予受助学校Cunningham Elementary School，展现社区对教育的关怀与支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14542011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14542011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542011-04.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14542011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542011-04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14542011" class="wp-caption-text">活动赞助单位代表们和受助校方师生合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开幕仪式由前会长李成纯如（Alice Lee）主持，介绍Cornerstone Christian Academy七年级学生江馨睿（Alyssa Jiang）演唱美国国歌，并由Cunningham音乐老师Alexandra Mclean带领表演学生与来宾进行效忠誓言，为活动揭开序幕。</p>
<p>爱心组织会长李素涵致词表示，该活动至今已迈入第32周年，感谢所有赞助者、义工和团队的长年支持。她指出：“我们的努力能为社区孩童提供实质帮助，是每一位参与者的功劳。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14542009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14542009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542009-02.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14542009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542009-02-600x429.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="429"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14542009" class="wp-caption-text">Chris Hollins在活动中颁发奖状给会长李素涵（右）。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当日贵宾云集，包括休斯顿审计长Chris Hollins、经文处政务组长陈育仁、侨教中心主任王盈蓉、中华文化服务中心执行长潘承斌（Gary Poon）及Cunningham小学校长Karen Harris等。</p>
<p>Chris Hollins在活动中颁发奖状，表彰CARE组织历年来为社区服务的杰出成就，提到为700名孩童提供学用品、募集超过三万美元善款，以及促进数十个社团携手合作等成果。他强调：“即使是最微小的善举，也能在社区间产生深远影响。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14542010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14542010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542010-03.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14542010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542010-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14542010" class="wp-caption-text">潮州会馆的醒狮穿梭场中，深受欢迎。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14542012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14542012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542012-05.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14542012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542012-05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14542012" class="wp-caption-text">园艺专家吴哲芳夫妇，现场义卖培育的亚洲果树苗。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14542014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14542014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542014-07.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14542014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542014-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14542014" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳红合唱团的表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场由Cunningham小学学生以歌声表达感谢，随后节目演出轮番登场，包括中华文化中心养生班、天下第一班、中华太极养生协会德艺班、夕阳红合唱团等团体，带来舞蹈、歌唱、乐器等中华文化演出。广西同乡会的欢乐腰鼓、潮州会馆三只醒狮穿梭场中，深受大小朋友喜爱，为活动增添热闹气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14542013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14542013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542013-06.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14542013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14542013-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14542013" class="wp-caption-text">爱心组织特别为表演师生准备午餐盒。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>园游会同时设有抽奖及造型气球摊位，义卖摊位则由美华协会、JCCAA大专校联会、中华保健中心、金城银行等十多个单位参与，提供各式中式美食、饮料、小吃与手工艺品，现场人潮络绎不绝。</p>
<p>今年的爱心义卖活动，不仅筹措善款，更凝聚社区力量，传递助人为乐的精神，也为更多孩童创造希望的起点。</p>
