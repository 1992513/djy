<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>头份2025夏日艺文活动 陈冠州的积木王国揭开序幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541941-777368-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>乐高达人“大黑白”陈冠州欣和市长贵宾在101作品前。（许享富／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月30日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）为迎接暑假到来，头份市的年度盛事“夏日艺文活动”，即将于七月热烈登场，活动内容丰富多元，从精彩演出到创意展览，让整个暑假充满艺文气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14541930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541930" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541930-777357.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541930-777357-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="456" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541930" class="wp-caption-text">台北万华龙山寺。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14541931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541931" style="width: 485px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541931-777358.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541931-777358-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="485" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541931" class="wp-caption-text">龙山寺内部细腻呈现。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>首先登场的活动是6/30-7/29为期一个月的“积木国王的积木王国-乐高艺术创作展”，由知名乐高达人“大黑白”陈冠州带来他耗时十多年所创作的台湾街景艺术，如台北101、龙山寺等等，邀请大家进入色彩缤纷的积木世界，体验满满的台湾味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14541932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541932" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541932-777359.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541932-777359-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="449" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541932" class="wp-caption-text">大楼 与细部 呈现（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14541933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541933" style="width: 483px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541933-777360.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541933-777360-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="483" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541933" class="wp-caption-text">台湾梅花鹿（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14541934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541934" style="width: 492px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541934-777361.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541934-777361-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="492" b="328" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541934" class="wp-caption-text">街景，庙的龙柱等细节（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>身为头份人的“大黑白”陈冠州也亲临记者会现场，分享创作历程与灵感来源，并宣布展览期间将举办共三场次的创作分享会(7/5、7/19、7/26)，除了分享创作概念，还会带领小朋友们一起拼积木。此外，去年深受小朋友喜爱的戏剧体验营，活动将于7/15~7/18同步开跑，透过沉浸式课程设计，让孩子们发挥创意、认识表演艺术、勇敢站上舞台，度过难忘的艺术暑假，两场活动报名皆已额满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14541928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541928" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541928-777356.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541928-777356-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="455" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541928" class="wp-caption-text">他从小就喜欢乐高，体验乐高组装的乐趣、舒压。因乐高费用高与课业想放弃，后为缴毕业创作品的压力，才重新投入乐高创作。后来甚至辞职，利用仅剩三个月存款，购料。将他旧的制式乐高成品，打掉重新创作融入的作品不仅精细还原建筑细节，更融入了许多台湾文化和故事，例如知名的龙山寺，终获厂商乐高签约，材料源源不绝，以乐高作品，向世界展现台湾的独特魅力。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后登场的重头戏，则是由知名艺文团队“即将成真火舞团”打造成团十周年的钜作《日出?昇龙》，这是一场融合魔幻、火舞与剧场元素的儿童火舞剧，8月9日晚间七点在头份运动公园罗马广场震撼登场，邀请亲子一同感受光与影交织的奇幻魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14541935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541935" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541935-777362.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541935-777362-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541935" class="wp-caption-text">台湾街景小镇计划。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14541936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541936" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541936-777363.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541936-777363-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="524" b="349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541936" class="wp-caption-text">西方玩具 盖台湾建筑（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14541937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541937" style="width: 488px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541937-777364.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541937-777364-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="488" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541937" class="wp-caption-text">内装满满的台湾建筑。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>头份市长罗雪珠表示，希望透过这一系列夏日艺文活动，让市民与游客在炎炎夏日中，能感受到文化艺术的能量，也让孩子们拥有一个充实又精彩的暑期生活。诚挚邀请大家走一趟头份，不论是看展、赏剧，还是亲子同乐，都能收获满满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14541938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541938" style="width: 485px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541938-777365.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14541938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541938-777365-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="485" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541938" class="wp-caption-text">客家伯公庙街景。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
