<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>深圳街头惊现“人头” 网传因欠薪被害</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523940-d8611b265236482867ff7bd2bd36c1f0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月2日，网传深圳龙华区有人头被从高楼丢落街头，据称是员工讨薪不果将老板杀死。（网页截图合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年06月04日讯】6月2日，网传深圳龙华区有一颗“人头”被从高楼丢落街头，据称是员工讨薪不果将老板杀死，然后该员工自己也跳楼身亡。</p>
<p>据网友在社交平台发文称，6月2日，深圳龙华新宝大厦发生一起凶杀案，嫌疑人涉嫌将受害者砍头、肢解，并将头部和脚部从高处抛下，现场发现一颗人头和一只脚。</p>
<p>一网友还表示，当天“我早上去上班，听见后面有声音，然后一个塌了的圆的就滚我前面去了，短头发的。吓得我班都没上。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14523909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14523909" style="width: 316px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523909-p3656852a862667667-ss.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14523909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523909-p3656852a862667667-ss.png" alt="" width="316" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14523909" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14523907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14523907" style="width: 422px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523907-6b8fde84a1013df3cd46d547053ad5d7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14523907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523907-6b8fde84a1013df3cd46d547053ad5d7.jpg" alt="" width="422" b="524" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14523907" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网传<ahref="https://www.ganjingworld.com/shorts/1hmlun0l6tq1yKGjQxqCKv18a1491c?t=7.338666">视频</a>显示，在大厦门口的地面上，有一颗血淋淋的人头。另一段视频显示，在不远处的地上，还有一只黑色的鞋子。</p>
<p>根据知情网友透露，老板拖欠员工工资，员工讨薪不果把老板杀了。死者是个女的，头被扔到楼下，身体还挂在6楼。男子将她杀了之后，自己也跳楼身亡了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14523908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14523908" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523908-GshIOGPbMAAqkOe-600x1003-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14523908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523908-GshIOGPbMAAqkOe-600x1003-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1003" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14523908" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14523946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14523946" style="width: 519px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523946-GshIODpb0AAfgIO.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14523946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523946-GshIODpb0AAfgIO.jpg" alt="" width="519" b="615" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14523946" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14523910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14523910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523910-p3656854a187648215-ss.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14523910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14523910-p3656854a187648215-ss.png" alt="" width="600" b="522" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14523910" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6月3日，顶端新闻记者致电深圳龙华区民治街道办事处，工作人员回复称，社区已经前往处理，目前不了解事件细节，也不清楚欠薪问题是否属实。</p>
<p>当晚，深圳市公安局龙华分局发布通报“辟谣”称，陈某某（女，21岁）是自杀，并患有“精神障碍疾病”，于6月1日从外省来深，6月2日7时30分许步行至龙华区宁远路附近某大厦，“乘电梯到30楼”，于8时51分翻越楼梯间窗户跳下，“坠落过程中身体碰撞6楼平台的围墙边缘导致躯体受损分离”。</p>
<p>目前，相关消息已被全网封杀，只剩下官方通报。</p>
