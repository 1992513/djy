<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>反送中6周年游行 在台港人吁对抗中共极权</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14530427-2506122339482378.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
