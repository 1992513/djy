<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园芦竹羊稠步道复育数千甲虫 VR体验甲虫生态</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541240-777015-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>打造实体与数位整合的沉浸式学习场域，拓展环境教育的深度与广度。（桃园市府农业局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年06月29日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）“2025台湾百大行脚趣．羊稠步道甲虫季”，即日起至7月6日于甲虫生态复育区登场，今年迈入第11年举办，主打步道生态教育，并与南崁高中结合生态导览VR黑科技 、儿童话剧、闯关冒险、亲子DIY、九宫格认识动物等各式活动；桃园市副市长苏俊宾长期关注在地生态，29日到场参与VR导览等体验，身历其境关注甲虫复育状态。</p>
<p>苏俊宾指出，透过亲眼观察、接触，孩子能更深刻理解自然与保育的重要，这类实境体验是最有效的学习方式。市府也结合在地科技厂商与南崁高中资源，导入虚拟实境（VR）技术，搭配甲虫生态教学与步道导览，打造实体与数位整合的沉浸式学习场域，拓展环境教育的深度与广度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14541238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541238" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541238-777013.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14541238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541238-777013-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541238" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市副市长苏俊宾长期关注在地生态，29日到场参与VR导览等体验，身历其境关注甲虫复育状态。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14541239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541239" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541239-777014.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14541239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541239-777014-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541239" class="wp-caption-text">导入虚拟实境（VR）技术，搭配甲虫生态教学与步道导览。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14541241" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541241" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541241-777016.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14541241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541241-777016-600x318.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="318"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541241" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市副市长苏俊宾欣赏甲虫生态教学。（桃园市府农业局提供）<span style="font-size: 16px">）</span></figcaption></figure>
<p>农业局长陈冠义表示，桃园芦竹区羊稠森林步道总长约3.3公里，更曾获选全台百大必访步道之一，为了复育适宜的甲虫栖地环境，羊稠社区发展协会组成巡护队，日夜轮班守护，并配合市府投入的经费支持甲虫复育活动，让步道甲虫数量从近百只提升至数千只，每年6、7月即可见成群的独角仙及锹形虫聚集于光蜡树上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14541242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541242" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541242-777017.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14541242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541242-777017-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541242" class="wp-caption-text">桃园芦竹羊稠步道复育数千甲虫，大家开心合影。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14541243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14541243" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541243-777018.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14541243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541243-777018-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14541243" class="wp-caption-text">限定生态导览。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动去年吸引约20万次人次参与，今年以步道生态教育为主轴，透过VR导览，就可以尽情徜徉在羊稠步道仔细端倪甲虫生态，适合亲子一起体验，边玩边认识生态。</p>
<figure id="14541245" aria-describedby="caption-14541245" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541245-777020.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541245-777020.jpg" alt="在光腊树上随处可见独角仙及锹形虫觅食。" width="500"/></a><figcaption id="caption-14541245" class="wp-caption-text">在光腊树上随处可见独角仙及锹形虫觅食。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14541244" aria-describedby="caption-14541244" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541244-777019.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/06/id14541244-777019.jpg" alt="在光腊树上随处可见独角仙及锹形虫觅食。" width="500"/></a><figcaption id="caption-14541244" class="wp-caption-text">在光腊树上随处可见独角仙及锹形虫觅食。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近期甲虫大量羽化，在光腊树上随处可见独角仙及锹形虫觅食，即日起至7月6日的假日，现场安排有限定生态导览、趣味闯关、甲虫造型手作DIY、九宫格连线、小丑气球秀、儿童剧、VR体验、闯关活动及打卡送好礼活动等亲子活动，提供现场报名免费参加，邀请大小朋友一同走进羊稠森林步道（桃园市芦竹区仁爱路三段666-1号旁）认识甲虫生态，详情请上脸书“羊稠俱乐部”、“桃园好农”查询。◇</p>
