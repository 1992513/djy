<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国际狮子会300B-3区捐赠桃园医疗车辆 提升市民健康</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492292-761186-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政感谢狮子会的爱心与协力，让桃园的医疗服务变得越来越好。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年04月26日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政26日出席“国际狮子会300B-3区第2届年会暨第3届会员代表大会”。张善政表示，国际狮子会300B-3区汇集狮兄狮姐的爱心，捐赠市府3部医疗巡回车及2部视力检视车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14492293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14492293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492293-761187.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14492293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492293-761187-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14492293" class="wp-caption-text">国际狮子会300B-3区捐赠桃园医疗车辆大合照。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中更配备了免散瞳眼底照相仪等专业医疗设备，总价值超过新台币1,000万元，对于维护桃园市民朋友的健康提供极大帮助，感谢狮子会的爱心与协力，让桃园的医疗服务变得越来越好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14492294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14492294" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492294-761188.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14492294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492294-761188-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14492294" class="wp-caption-text">国际狮子会300B-3区捐赠2部视力筛检车。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14492295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14492295" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492295-761189.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14492295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492295-761189-600x319.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14492295" class="wp-caption-text">国际狮子会300B-3区捐赠3部医疗巡回车。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国际狮子会300B-3区总监周矢绫表示，300B-3区长期致力于推动各项社会公益活动，包括捐赠医疗巡回专车、协助糖尿病筛检、关怀饥饿族群、推动净滩环保行动及参与灾害救援等，未来仍会坚守致力公益的初衷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14492296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14492296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492296-761190.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14492296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492296-761190-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14492296" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政和来宾们合影。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局表示，感谢国际狮子会300B-3区此次捐赠多辆医疗巡回专车，并配备多项专业医疗器材与设备，不仅持续扩大桃园市医疗量能，也展现出公私协力、共同提升市民福祉的精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14492297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14492297" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492297-761191.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14492297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14492297-761191-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14492297" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政与来宾们一同为国际狮子会300B-3区捐赠之医疗车辆揭开覆盖的红布。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>未来桃园市府将持续携手民间团体，共同建构更完善、高品质的医疗与长照服务，让桃园成为更幸福、健康的宜居城市。◇</p>
