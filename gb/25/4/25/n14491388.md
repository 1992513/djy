<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>廉政班桃园开讲  AI、警犬、滞洪池一次看透收获多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491389-760952-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>刘印宫副局长与训练班学员合照。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年04月25日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）法务部廉政署廉政人员训练班第54期学员25日至桃园市政府教学参访，由执行秘书陈立志率领学员与随行人员共70人，前往桃园市政府环境保护局、警察局及水务局等机关实地参访。活动涵盖环境稽查、科技警政与都市防灾等领域，强调跨域融合、实务操作与互动交流，期能增进学员对行政实务的理解与认识。</p>
<p>在环境保护局部分，学员实地观摩AI污染辨识系统与稽查流程，了解如何运用科技强化查核效率与行政透明度。政风处长吴沧俯表示，廉政工作与各行政机关息息相关，廉政人员应深入机关思考预警机制，从“理论”转化为“实务”的过程，学习如何将“稽核”转化为“兴革”，并在预防制度设计与行政作业流程中，发挥风险预防的角色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14491391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491391-760954.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491391-760954-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491391" class="wp-caption-text">警犬队与拉布拉多警犬合影。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14491393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491393" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491393-760956.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491393-760956-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491393" class="wp-caption-text">领犬员吴宥彤与布拉多警犬。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14491392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491392-760955.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491392-760955-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491392" class="wp-caption-text">布拉多警犬。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在警察局部分，学员首先观摩了警犬队勤务展演，桃园警犬队于113年底成军，目前编制6位领犬员与6只拉布拉多警犬，专责缉毒与侦爆勤务。展演中介绍了警犬队的编制与训练流程，并实地呈现警犬在服从训练、气味辨识及模拟查缉等项目的执勤表现，互动过程中，警犬稳定执行指令、亲切回应学员，引发现场热烈回响，为本次参访增添温馨与活泼的气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14491394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491394-760957.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491394-760957-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491394" class="wp-caption-text">表演展示。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14491390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491390" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491390-760953.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491390-760953-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491390" class="wp-caption-text">警犬队为学员简介。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警察局副局长刘印宫表示，该局为全国第三个成立警犬队的警察机关，警犬队已投入国庆、演唱会、灯会等重大维安勤务，未来将持续协助警方执行缉毒、防爆与安检等任务，期盼学员透过此次参访，能更加了解实务运作并拓展视野。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14491396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491396-760959.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491396-760959-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491396" class="wp-caption-text">学员与布拉多警犬合影。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14491395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491395-760958.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491395-760958-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491395" class="wp-caption-text">学员与布拉多警犬合影。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学员前往数位科技战情中心，实际操作诈骗网站分析与无人机线上侦查模拟任务，体验第一线单位如何整合资料、即时反应。刘印宫指出，战情中心整合AI、大数据、币流追踪与无人机等科侦技术，有助于即时分享刑案资讯、加速侦查进程，是桃园推动科技警政的重要平台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14491397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491397" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491397-760960.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491397-760960-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491397" class="wp-caption-text">学员与布拉多警犬合影。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下午行程安排参访大树林滞洪池（水の秘密基地），由水务局人员导览，介绍滞洪池设施的运作原理，不仅具备城市防洪功能，也结合景观与环境教育推广，让学员从工程面深入理解地方政府在因应气候变迁的策略与实践。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14491398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14491398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491398-760961.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14491398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14491398-760961-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14491398" class="wp-caption-text">学员与布拉多警犬合影。（桃园市桃园警分局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动跳脱传统参观流程，结合跨领域政策观察、实地操作与互动展示等多元活动设计，让学员在参与中学习、在观察中思考，进一步厚植未来实务应用与跨域协作的能力。◇</p>
