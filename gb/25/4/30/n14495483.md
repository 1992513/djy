<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义市2025 富邦家庭照顾者喘息之旅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495484-762195-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年04月30日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市长期照顾管理中心今(30)日于爱木村文化馆办理“风铃守木?心静之森”?2025富邦家庭照顾者喘息之旅活动，嘉义市副市长林瑞彦、台北富邦银行基金会总干事毛智弘及卢明芬专案经理亲临现场与家庭照顾者们互动，给予爱的关怀与鼓励。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14495485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495485-762196.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495485-762196-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495485" class="wp-caption-text">副市长林瑞彦赠送礼品予毛总干事及卢专案经理。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林瑞彦副市长表示，感谢台北富邦银行公益慈善基金会与市府合作迈入第15年提供家庭照顾者“喘息之旅”，共服务了嘉义市2,450人次家庭照顾者，让平日主要照顾者能暂时放下照顾重担，有机会外出参与活动舒缓压力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14495486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495486-762197.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495486-762197-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495486" class="wp-caption-text">大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林瑞彦副市长进一步说明，家庭照顾者负荷需要被看见并获得支持，嘉义市会尽力协助，建构完整的家庭照顾者支持网络，联系媒合各项资源，协助乡亲解决问题。除长照多元服务外，亦陆续规划针对“蜡烛2头烧之上班族照顾者（约有1/5）”、“老老照顾者（约有1/5）”、“预备照顾者”、“新手照顾者”、“资深照顾者”等服务对象样态与需求，客制化制定服务方案如出院宅急便、互助喘息服务、乐龄健康训练场、忆嘉人、守护嘉忆人等在地服务方案。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14495487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495487-762198.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495487-762198-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495487" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市家庭照顾者资源。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台北富邦银行基金会总干事毛智弘表示，嘉义市被誉为“桧木之都”，今年带领家庭照顾者至爱木村，希望藉由桧木香气与大自然接触，制作守护神风铃，让大家身心都能获得照顾，同时藉由活动也感受到市府团队对家庭照顾者的用心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14495488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495488-762199.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495488-762199-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495488" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市互助喘息办理单位。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局局长廖育玮呼吁，为避免家庭照顾者情绪与生理上的耗竭，减轻照顾者压力，长照资源的介入是改变照顾困境的重要因素，所有家庭照顾者一旦出现压力征兆，请把专业人员当作照顾伙伴、善用长照资源，有任何需求，不要自己扛着，请拨打长照专线1966，长照中心会有专人提供咨询服务及资源连结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14495489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495489-762200.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495489-762200-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495489" class="wp-caption-text">学员依手机示范内容手作猫头鹰守护神风铃。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14495490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495490-762201.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14495490-762201-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495490" class="wp-caption-text">成品再经彩绘各具特色，这是前期学员留下的作品。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
