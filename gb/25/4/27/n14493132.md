<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>旧金山纪念4.25 民众支持法轮大法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14493137-2504271256461749.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
