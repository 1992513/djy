<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>最受外国人喜爱韩国艺人 BTS与李敏镐连年夺冠</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14477076-220617100137100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）发行专辑《Proof》专辑资料照。（环球唱片提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年04月07日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）韩国文化体育观光部和韩国国际文化交流振兴院7日发表以2024年调查为基准的“2025年海外韩流实态调查”，防弹少年团（BTS）连续7年获选为最受喜爱的K-POP歌手，最受喜爱的韩国演员则由李敏镐夺冠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14476930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476930-220531225854100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476930" title="BTS to the daily press briefing at the White House" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476930-220531225854100707-600x388.jpg" alt="BTS to the daily press briefing at the White House" width="600" b="388"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476930" class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于白宫记者会中谈论反亚裔仇恨犯罪与歧视议题资料照。(Kevin Dietsch/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>此调查是自去年11月29日至12月27日，对海外28个国家的2万6,400名曾接触韩国文化相关的民众进行网络问卷调查。</p>
<p>该调查显示，最受外国人喜爱的K-POP歌手前两名，由连续7年夺冠的防弹少年团（BTS）（24.6%）和连续6年获得亚军的BLACKPINK（12.3%）拿下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14476929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476929-231226074823100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476929" title="1796751940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476929-231226074823100707-600x400.jpg" alt="1796751940" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476929" class="wp-caption-text">BLACKPINK获英国国王查尔斯三世颁发“大英帝国员佐勋章”资料照。(Victoria Jones &#8211; Pool/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>外国人喜爱的K-POP歌手第三名至第十名则为IU（3.1%）、PSY（2.2%）、TWICE（1.8%）、BTS成员柾国（Jung Kook）（1.8%）、BLACKPINK成员Lisa（1.7%）、EXO（1.4%）、BIGBANG（1.4%）、BIGBANG成员GD（1.4%）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14476931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476931-220603140817100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476931" title="IU Lee Ji-eun" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476931-220603140817100707-600x400.jpg" alt="IU Lee Ji-eun" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476931" class="wp-caption-text">IU（李知恩）出席第75届戛纳电影节（坎城影展）资料照。(LOIC VENANCE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14476933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476933-230322234732100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476933" title="TWICE" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476933-230322234732100707-600x480.jpg" alt="TWICE" width="600" b="480"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476933" class="wp-caption-text">TWICE于纽约帝国大厦出席The Healing Power of Music点灯活动资料照。(Noam Galai/Getty Images for Empire State Realty Trust)</figcaption></figure>
<p>外国人最喜爱的韩国演员今年由李敏镐以7.0%的得票率夺冠。他自2014年起已连续12年夺得冠军。并列亚军的是孔刘（2.4%）与宋慧乔（2.4%）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14476934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476934-241122033728100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476934" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476934-241122033728100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476934" class="wp-caption-text">李敏镐主演《问问星星吧》剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14476935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476935-191213045018100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476935" title="GongYoo" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476935-191213045018100707-600x400.jpg" alt="GongYoo" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476935" class="wp-caption-text">孔刘访台北出席媒体见面会资料照。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14476926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476926-240924041829100311.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476926" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476926-240924041829100311-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476926" class="wp-caption-text">韩国演员宋慧乔2024年9月24日访台资料照。（宋碧龙／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最受喜爱的演员排行榜第四名及第五名为金秀贤（2.3%）、李钟硕（李钟奭）（2.0%）；第六名起则是玄彬（炫彬）（1.8%）、全智贤（1.5%）、马东石（1.5%）、IU李知恩（1.5%）及车银优（1.4%）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14476939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476939-1610210710272384.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476939" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476939-1610210710272384-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476939" class="wp-caption-text">韩星李钟硕访台北出席记者会资料照。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14476927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476927-250117053533100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476927" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476927-250117053533100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476927" class="wp-caption-text">玄彬为韩国电影《哈尔滨》访台宣传资料照。（网银国际影视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14476937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476937" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476937-201210092812100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476937" title="eunwoo" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476937-201210092812100707-600x400.jpg" alt="eunwoo" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476937" class="wp-caption-text">车银优于韩剧《女神降临》中饰演学霸校草。（friDay影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最受喜爱的韩剧由《鱿鱼游戏》第一季（9.7%）连续4年获得冠军（去年12月26日公开的第二季未在本次调查范围内），亚军则是《泪之女王》（6.5%）、季军由《爱的迫降》（2.2%）拿下。第四名及第五名则是《女神降临》（2.1%）及去年收获高人气的《背着善宰跑》（1.8%）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14476922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476922-240430045950100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476922" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476922-240430045950100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476922" class="wp-caption-text">金智媛与金秀贤演出《泪之女王》剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14476923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476923-2408060920231487.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476923" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476923-2408060920231487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476923" class="wp-caption-text">边佑锡与金惠奫主演《背着善宰跑》剧照。（CJ_ENM_STUDIOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>电影方面则是连续6年位居第一名及第二名的《寄生上流》（8.3%）和《尸速列车》（6.5%）维持冠亚军，第三名至第五名则为《破墓》、《绝地逃生》与《Wonderland》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14476920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476920-200131084317100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476920" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476920-200131084317100707-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476920" class="wp-caption-text">主演《尸速列车》的孔刘自曝讨厌看恐怖电影。（friDay影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14476919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14476919" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476919-250326002126100707.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14476919" title="2152126079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14476919-250326002126100707-600x400.jpg" alt="2152126079" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14476919" class="wp-caption-text">《Wonderland》主演群出席记者会资料照。(Chung Sung-Jun/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，外国人提到韩国时最先想到的印象，连续8年由“K-POP”拿下第一名（17.8%），接着是韩国饮食（11.8%）、电视剧（8.7%）、美容（6.4%）、电影（5.6%）。</p>
<p>而在韩国文化相关的各领域“人气度”方面，名列前茅的是韩国饮食（53.7%）、音乐（51.2%）、美容（50.8%）、电视剧（49%）。</p>
