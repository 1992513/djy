<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】蕃茄豆腐滚三文鱼骨汤～营养可口</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481754-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蕃茄豆腐滚三文鱼骨汤。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>营养丰富的蔬菜鱼汤，抓好食材份量，放对食材种类，熬出来的汤头美味可口，光喝汤就满足了！</h3>
<p>材料：</p>
<p>蕃茄12 oz<br />
马铃薯10 oz<br />
豆腐半盒<br />
三文鱼骨颈1.5磅<br />
洋葱半个<br />
姜3片<br />
芫荽梗1.5 oz<br />
蕃茄汤1罐<br />
蔬菜汤8杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_14481764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14481764" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481764-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14481764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481764-1-450x338.jpg" alt="材料" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14481764" class="wp-caption-text">材料（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>调味：<br />
盐1.5茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<p>作法：</p>
<p>1. 三文鱼骨洗干净后切小块，用厨房纸巾吸干水分备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14481755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14481755" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481755-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14481755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481755-2-450x288.jpg" alt="三文鱼骨洗干净后切小块，用厨房纸巾吸干水分备用。" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14481755" class="wp-caption-text">三文鱼骨洗干净后切小块，用厨房纸巾吸干水分备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 蕃茄、马铃薯、洋葱和豆腐，全部切小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14481757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14481757" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481757-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14481757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481757-3-450x338.jpg" alt="蕃茄、马铃薯、洋葱和豆腐，全部切小块备用。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14481757" class="wp-caption-text">蕃茄、马铃薯、洋葱和豆腐，全部切小块备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蔬菜汤煮滚备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14481759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14481759" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481759-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14481759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481759-4-450x338.jpg" alt="蔬菜汤煮滚备用。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14481759" class="wp-caption-text">蔬菜汤煮滚备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，把三文鱼骨分两次煎成金黄，再加入洋葱炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14481760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14481760" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481760-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14481760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481760-5-450x338.jpg" alt="热锅下油，把三文鱼骨分两次煎成金黄，再加入洋葱炒香。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14481760" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，把三文鱼骨分两次煎成金黄，再加入洋葱炒香。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 赞酒后加入热蔬菜汤，放入番茄、马铃薯、豆腐和姜，煮15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14481761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14481761" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481761-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14481761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481761-6-450x338.jpg" alt="赞酒后加入热蔬菜汤，放入番茄、马铃薯、豆腐和姜，煮15分钟。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14481761" class="wp-caption-text">赞酒后加入热蔬菜汤，放入番茄、马铃薯、豆腐和姜，煮15分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 最后加入蕃茄汤及芫荽梗煮3分钟，调味后即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14481762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14481762" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481762-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14481762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14481762-7-450x253.jpg" alt="最后加入蕃茄汤及芫荽梗煮3分钟，调味后即完成。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14481762" class="wp-caption-text">最后加入蕃茄汤及芫荽梗煮3分钟，调味后即完成。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主厨梁志生经历：</p>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
