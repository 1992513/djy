<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欢迎投入志愿服务 桃市青年局召募“桃青志工队”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472155-755510-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>原乡孩童手作艺术品。（桃园市青年事务局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年04月01日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市政府青年事务局青年志工资源中心启动“桃青志工队”招募，凡年龄15岁至45岁、设籍、就学或就业于桃园市，对志工服务有兴趣的青年皆欢迎加入，发挥自身所长回馈于社会，创造公益影响力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472154" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472154-755509.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472154-755509.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472154" class="wp-caption-text">净滩志工服务。（桃园市青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃青志工队目前设置三组分队，包括透过文字及影像记录服务过程感动的“闻青记者队”、协助活动方案规划及教材制作的“草青志工队”以及于青年局创业基地、设计库、社会企业中心平镇馆等场馆担任课程助教、服务台咨询的“职青志工队”，青年志工透过分队发挥专长并接触了解青年事务局推广的青年业务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472156" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472156-755511.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472156-755511.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472156" class="wp-caption-text">爱奇儿陪顾服务。（桃园市青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青年局除提供志工基本服务时数证明、志愿服务纪录册发放外，也因应服务活动属性及分队功能，办理志工基础训练、特殊训练、在职训练等多元训练并办理各类主题服务活动，从训练到实作，提供青年志愿服务完整的培训，以利志工提供更高的志愿服务品质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472157-755512.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472157-755512.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472157" class="wp-caption-text">社区蔬食关怀送餐服务。（桃园市青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃市青年局长侯佳龄表示，青年志工代表着青年对社会责任和公益事业的关注与参与，欢迎大家报名加入桃青志工队，参与志工训练及志愿服务，建立服务学习履历，发掘自身兴趣，进而使个人成长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472158" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472158-755513.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472158-755513.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472158" class="wp-caption-text">长者陪伴服务。（桃园市青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14472159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472159" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472159-755514.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472159-755514.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472159" class="wp-caption-text">志工服务行前训练及讨论。（桃园市青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青年局将持续推动多元的青年志愿服务相关训练及活动，4月份即将办理自媒体行销宣传志工训练及身心障碍儿童艺术创作服务活动，提供更多让青年参与社会的机会，深化志愿服务精神在桃园的实践与传承。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472162" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472162-755516.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472162-755516.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472162" class="wp-caption-text">影片拍摄及剪辑训练。（桃园市青年事务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>更多活动详情请上桃园市政府青年事务局官网、脸书粉丝专页或“桃青参一咖”脸书粉丝专页查询。◇</p>
