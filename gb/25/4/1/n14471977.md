<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英崙溯源公所蛇年晚宴 敬老扶幼喜迎春</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471945-DSC03212-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙溯源公所3月30日举办蛇年春宴，职员、嘉宾们敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年04月01日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“溯初宗族开三姓，源衍子孙聚一堂。”这幅挂在纽英崙溯源公所门外的对联，彰显雷、方、邝三姓宗亲建立协会、敬老扶幼的传统。这样的文化，在3月30日的蛇年春宴中依旧延续。
</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">当晚在波士顿华埠帝苑酒楼，纽英崙溯源公所宗亲、全美总公所代表，以及政要嘉宾总计约250人齐聚一堂，欢庆蛇年新春，向耆英和优秀学生赠送红包。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14471943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14471943" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471943-DSC03209.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14471943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471943-DSC03209-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14471943" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙溯源公所3月30日举办蛇年春宴，约250位来宾参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14471929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14471929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471929-DSC03161.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14471929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471929-DSC03161-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14471929" class="wp-caption-text">职员、嘉宾和舞狮团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400"><strong>三姓宗族迎新</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>不忘传统</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400">伴随喧闹的锣鼓声，美东国术会“胡清白鹤派”的三头彩狮摇摆进入宴会厅，跳跃舞动，奉送生菜和橘子，向来宾们致以美好祝福。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">这场晚会的司仪，是陈文珊和方柳君。欢迎政要嘉宾及外地宗亲的东道主代表，是纽英崙溯源公所主席邝元杰。他主持向青少年学生以及耆英们赠送红包，其中最年长者，今年刚满100岁。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">邝元杰笑言，溯源公所虽然是侨社中比较小的团体，但也一直坚持“敬老扶幼”的传统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14471928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14471928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471928-DSC03159.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14471928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471928-DSC03159-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14471928" class="wp-caption-text">胡清白鹤派舞狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14471927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14471927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471927-DSC03147.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14471927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471927-DSC03147-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14471927" class="wp-caption-text">方柳君（左）和陈文珊主持晚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p style="font-weight: 400">另一位主席雷展灏致词，首先用粤语感谢全体来宾的光临，祝福大家“恭喜发财”、“身体健康”。随后，在美国出生、成长的他换用英文，表达辞旧迎新，以全新视野和心态追寻未来目标的心愿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14471932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14471932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471932-DSC03173.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14471932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471932-DSC03173-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14471932" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙溯源公所主席雷展灏致欢迎词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p style="font-weight: 400">除了本地宗亲之外，当晚也有来自旧金山总堂，以及纽约分会的代表出席宴会，包括总堂主席雷振泽，原宗公所主席邝炳威等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">雷振泽代表总堂同宗，与纽英崙宗亲一起庆贺中华传统新年，送上“蛇年行大运”的美好祝福。他感谢纽英崙溯源公所宗亲们在过去一年“弘扬中华文化，服务侨社”，也期盼大家在新一年里继续团结努力，使公所更兴旺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14471934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14471934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471934-DSC03178.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14471934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471934-DSC03178-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14471934" class="wp-caption-text">总堂主席雷振泽为纽英崙宗亲们送上新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p style="font-weight: 400">纽英崙溯源公所的另一位主席邝炎彬称赞，公所各项活动，包括郊游、助学金、敬老金等，都离不开各位宗亲的尽心尽力。他期待大家延续“守望相助的初心”，在新一年秉承“溯源溯本，敦亲睦族”的宗旨，支持和传承公所的使命。</p>

<h3 style="font-weight: 400"><strong>嘉宾道贺</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>颁赠褒奖</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400">溯源公所蛇年春宴，得到波士顿地区多位政要嘉宾的莅临道贺，包括麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）、波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）、墨菲（Erin Murphy），还有麻州参议员柯林斯（Nick Collins）办公室的代表等等。他们分别向溯源公所颁赠了麻州议会和波士顿市议会的褒奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14471937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14471937" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471937-DSC03188.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14471937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471937-DSC03188-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14471937" class="wp-caption-text">政要嘉宾们向溯源公所颁赠麻州议会和波士顿市议会褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">麦家威用粤语向来宾们致以“恭喜发财”的祝福，还笑言，他刚刚了解了很多关于“蛇年”的文化，感谢侨社宗亲们的“历史课”。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">“我非常赞赏你们在华埠所做的一切，给予的支持，让我们在这里一直拥有强韧的社区。”麦家威说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14471936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14471936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471936-DSC03184.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14471936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14471936-DSC03184-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14471936" class="wp-caption-text">麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）感谢溯源公所宗亲们服务社区。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉，也是溯源公所成员。他谈到，今年是溯源堂复会41周年。“41年来，溯源堂与我们各个公所和谐相处，共同合作，令到我们唐人街的侨社更加稳定，更加和谐。”他说。</p>
