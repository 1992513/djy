<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>飞马之春 镜头下的萨尔兹堡米拉贝尔花园</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472397-755663-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>萨尔兹堡米拉贝尔花园。（沈台兴提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年04月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）在科技业打拼数十年后，沈台兴选择以旅行与摄影，开启他退休后的第二人生。这位定居于云林元长乡的前高科技公司副总，走过世界多地，而这一次，他把脚步停在了奥地利萨尔兹堡，一座依偎在阿尔卑斯山下的巴洛克小城。在三月的早春时节，他以一系列摄影作品，记录下米拉贝尔花园的浪漫与诗意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472398-755664.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14472398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472398-755664-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472398" class="wp-caption-text">奥地利萨尔兹堡，一座依偎在阿尔卑斯山下的巴洛克小城。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>萨尔兹堡的古典灵魂</h2>
<p>萨尔兹堡，是音乐神童莫札特的故乡，也是电影《真善美》的梦幻背景。城市本身就像一场古典交响曲，节奏分明、旋律优雅。走在石板路上，沈台兴说：“抵达奥地利的维也纳，往西到阿尔卑斯山脉下的萨尔兹堡，参观米拉贝尔花园，一路感受春天的气息，黄、白、粉红花大肆盛开。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472399" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472399-755665.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14472399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472399-755665-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472399" class="wp-caption-text">萨尔兹堡，是音乐神童莫札特的故乡，也是电影《真善美》的梦幻背景。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14472400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472400" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472400-755666.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472400-755666-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472400" class="wp-caption-text">沈台兴:“参观米拉贝尔花园，一路感受春天的气息，黄、白、粉红花大肆盛开。”（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>米拉贝尔的巴洛克浪漫</h2>
<p>米拉贝尔宫建于1606年，由当时的萨尔兹堡总主教为其情人莎乐美所建，如今已成为世界著名的旅游景点。宫殿前的花园，对称的几何图形、神话雕像与喷泉围绕其间，是巴洛克园林设计的经典代表。沈台兴以镜头捕捉飞马喷泉（Pegasus Fountain）的力与美，那正是《真善美》中玛丽亚与孩子们欢唱“Do-Re-Mi”的场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472401" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472401-755667.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14472401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472401-755667-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472401" class="wp-caption-text">米拉贝尔宫建于1606年，由当时的萨尔兹堡总主教为其情人莎乐美所建。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14472402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472402" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472402-755668.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472402-755668-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472402" class="wp-caption-text">沈台兴以镜头捕捉飞马喷泉（Pegasus Fountain）的力与美，那正是《真善美》中玛丽亚与孩子们欢唱“Do-Re-Mi”的场景。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>初春的玉兰，写下春天序曲</h2>
<p>三月的米拉贝尔花园，草花尚未铺满，但春意已悄然展开。沈台兴说：“黄、白、粉红花大肆盛开，米拉贝尔花园草花还未蔓延，绿叶还未长，但紫玉兰、白玉兰盛开。”画面中，紫玉兰高贵典雅，花瓣在灰蓝天色下更显深邃；白玉兰洁白无瑕，如雪般轻柔。两者在园中交织，如一场静谧却盛大的春日庆典。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472404" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472404-755670.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472404-755670-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472404" class="wp-caption-text">白玉兰洁白无瑕，如雪般轻柔。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14472403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472403" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472403-755669.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472403-755669-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472403" class="wp-caption-text">沈台兴:“米拉贝尔花园草花还未蔓延，绿叶还未长，但紫玉兰、白玉兰盛开。”（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>飞马与雕像的神话对话</h2>
<p>米拉贝尔花园的另一大特色，是那些与希腊神话有关的雕像群。沈台兴的照片中，不只飞马翱翔于池上岩石，还有环绕喷泉四周的神祇与小矮人雕像。他说：“广大园区希腊神话的主题雕像搭配盛巴洛克的建筑，以及周围景观加上盛开的花，还是蛮好看。”这些雕像不只是装饰，更如时光的见证者，静静守护着这片四季更迭的园地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472412" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472412-755685.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472412-755685-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472412" class="wp-caption-text">米拉贝尔花园的另一大特色，是那些与希腊神话有关的雕像群。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14472405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472405" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472405-755671.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14472405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472405-755671-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472405" class="wp-caption-text">这些雕像不只是装饰，更如时光的见证者，静静守护着这片四季更迭的园地。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>街道与河畔的春日诗意</h2>
<p>除了花园本身，沈台兴也没有错过花园周边街道与萨尔察赫河畔的春景。他说：“路旁的树木抽出嫩芽，花坛里的花朵绽放，为城市增添了活力。”那日他漫步于河岸，垂柳依依、水光粼粼，远眺霍亨萨尔兹堡城堡，仿佛走入一幅静谧的画卷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14472408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472408" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472408-755673.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14472408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472408-755673-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472408" class="wp-caption-text">萨尔察赫河畔的春景。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14472406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14472406" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472406-755672.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14472406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14472406-755672-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14472406" class="wp-caption-text">沈台兴说：“路旁的树木抽出嫩芽，花坛里的花朵绽放，为城市增添了活力。”（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>记忆中的《真善美》 缤纷与安静的谐和共舞</h2>
<p>对沈台兴来说，米拉贝尔花园不只是一个观光地，更是一段记忆的共鸣。他说：“四十年前电影《真善美》曾来取景！”从飞马喷泉到阶梯场景，都是电影中经典画面。他的镜头，也如同一首视觉诗，为观者重现那份纯真与自由。</p>
<p>黄金花海排列成蜿蜒曲线，与整座花园形成视觉上的韵律，他说：“初春的米拉贝尔花园，万物复苏，生机盎然。”</p>
<p>这样的旅程，不只是对一个园艺美景的欣赏，更像是一位退休者对人生第二幕的注解。沈台兴透过镜头，与世界对话，也与过去的自己重逢。</p>
<p>在春天里，他遇见了花，也遇见了自己。</p>
