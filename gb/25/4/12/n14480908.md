<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园溪海休闲农业区西瓜节 邀市民体验种瓜乐趣</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480910-757847-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政与小朋友一同体验种瓜。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年04月12日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政12日前往大园区，出席“溪海休闲农业区西瓜节种植日活动”，亲自示范说明瓜苗种植技巧，并与游客一同体验西瓜种植，感受农耕乐趣。张善政表示，大园西瓜素以果实硕大、香甜多汁闻名，并以“华宝”品种最具代表性，果实可逾10公斤，深受市场喜爱。同时，目前也是彩色海芋盛开最美的时节，欢迎市民朋友至溪海地区欣赏花海并体验西瓜种植乐趣，感受缤纷花海与农村风情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14480909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14480909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480909-757846.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14480909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480909-757846-600x281.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14480909" class="wp-caption-text">桃园溪海休闲农业区西瓜节大合照。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政指出，西瓜节种植日每年皆吸引许多民众参与，参加者在田间种下瓜苗、立下姓名牌后，由在地农友、农会与协会专业照料，让瓜苗得以自然茁壮 ，参加者可于3个月后再度回到农场收成，体验“种瓜得瓜”的食农教育与田间乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14480911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14480911" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480911-757848.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14480911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480911-757848-600x330.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14480911" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政致词（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>农业局表示，为根本解决传统塑胶农膜造成的环境问题，市府积极推动环保农膜评估与试验。去（113）年仙草花节引进工研院研制的寡糖农膜，今（114）年则扩大试用回收纸浆制成的纸质农膜，应用于西瓜、洋香瓜、草莓与仙草等作物，并藉溪海西瓜节向市民推广。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14480912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14480912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480912-757849.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14480912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480912-757849-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14480912" class="wp-caption-text">西瓜幼苗。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14480914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14480914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480914-757851.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14480914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480914-757851-600x328.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="328" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14480914" class="wp-caption-text">溪海休闲农业区西瓜节。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14480913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14480913" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480913-757850.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14480913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14480913-757850-600x229.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="229" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14480913" class="wp-caption-text">来宾一同体验种瓜。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大园区公所指出，环保纸膜具遮光、阻气、保湿及保温等功能，作物收成后更可直接翻耕入土成为肥料，无须回收，若全面取代传统农膜，预估每年可减碳4万公吨。虽价格约为传统农膜的2.5倍，但市府提供三分之二补助，农民实际负担反而低于以往成本，桃园市府将持续推动并扩大补助，鼓励更多农民使用。◇</p>
