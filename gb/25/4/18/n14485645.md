<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台湾故宫刺绣书画四展 交织艺术乐章</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485719-2504180300102378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾国立故宫博物院北部院区书画展全新登场，推出四档书画新展，图为故宫“织造精彩”特展，展出故宫院藏缂绣珍品。（台湾故宫提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年04月18日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾报导）台湾国立故宫博物院（台湾故宫）表示，北部院区书画展全新登场，推出四档书画新展融合书法、绘画及缂绣，跨越视觉与听觉，透过多元媒材交织和谐共鸣的艺术乐章。</p>
<p>台湾故宫表示，画作不仅能凝视，也能用心倾听；千丝万缕的织绣工艺，则以精致的纹理与质感，带领观众开启更深层的感官想像。</p>
<p>台湾故宫北部院区的四档书画新展，除了主题展“织造精彩—清宫缂丝刺绣展”，精选院藏缂绣珍品，展现清宫收藏与织造工艺的丰富面貌；另一主题展“悦耳—倾听画里的声音”，聚焦古代画家如何以笔墨描绘“有声画”；例行展览“国宝聚焦”、“笔墨见真章—故宫书法导赏”亦更新展件。</p>
<p>故宫策展人、书画文献处副研究员许文美表示，缂丝是古代珍贵的丝织品，最迟自唐代起，便将原本应用于毛织的“通经断纬”技法运用于丝织，使织品能精准细致地呈现图案。宋代以后，刺绣工艺日益成熟，常以名家书画为蓝本，涵盖山水、花鸟、人物等题材，展现细腻写真的艺术风格。</p>
<p>许文美说，这次展览精选故宫院藏缂绣珍品，分为“清宫收藏古代缂绣”、“织造华美缂绣”与“以御笔书画诗文为中心的织绣”三大单元，呈现清宫织绣艺术的华丽风貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14485736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14485736" style="width: 247px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485736-2504180241492378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14485736" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485736-2504180241492378.jpg" alt="" width="247" b="599"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14485736" class="wp-caption-text">国宝 宋缂。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一单元“清宫收藏古代缂绣”，展出清宫珍藏的古代缂绣书画。国宝《缂丝富贵长春轴》为存世宋缂精品，以蓝色丝线为底，交织牡丹、月季（又名长春花）、菊花、芙蓉等花卉，缂工细密，色彩明丽，寓意“富贵长春”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14485727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14485727" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485727-2504180304372378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14485727" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485727-2504180304372378.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14485727" class="wp-caption-text">清 缂丝村农韶庆图 轴。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14485726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14485726" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485726-2504180302532378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14485726" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485726-2504180302532378.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14485726" class="wp-caption-text">清 缂丝场圃秋成图 轴。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二单元“织造华美缂绣”，则呈现清宫缂绣的丰富题材与精美工艺。《缂丝村农韶庆图》与《缂丝场圃秋成图》两件挂轴，图案源自清康熙年间起相当重视的《耕织图》，前者表现村夫捣米，老少围炉取暖的温馨场景；后者则是农人簸扬谷物，整理稻穗的丰收图景，表现清代对农耕社会的理想描绘。皇帝的御笔书画诗文，也是清宫缂绣重要的样稿来源，尤以乾隆时期最为盛行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14485721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14485721" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485721-2504180237132378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14485721" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485721-2504180237132378.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14485721" class="wp-caption-text">清 孔宪培妻于氏恭绣 御制万年枝上日初长诗意。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第三单元“以御笔书画诗文为中心的织绣”，展出孔子七十二代孙孔宪培之妻于氏率家人绣制的《御制万年枝上日初长诗意》，构图华美，色彩鲜丽，宫殿楼阁彷若仙境。左上御制诗为乾隆皇帝为其生母崇庆皇太后所作，其中“寿康”一词寓意“长寿安康”，亦呼应崇庆皇太后颐养居所“寿康宫”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14485722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14485722" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485722-2504180238362378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14485722" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485722-2504180238362378.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="599"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14485722" class="wp-caption-text">清 缂丝大寿字。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>许文美表示，《清 缂丝大寿字》以慈禧太后书写“寿”字为原型，朱红大字内缂饰四季花卉，再以笔设色添染细节，展现书法、绘画与织艺交融的创意巧思。</p>
<p>从织绣工艺延伸至书画作品，宋代皇后的华丽衣饰与冠冕纹样，同样展现宫廷艺术的精致典雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14485723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14485723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485723-2504180240142378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14485723" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485723-2504180240142378-600x390.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="390"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14485723" class="wp-caption-text">国宝 宋代后半身像册（右幅宋高宗后半身像；左幅宋孝宗后半身像）。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本季故宫“国宝聚焦”精选展出《宋高宗后半身像》与《宋孝宗后半身像》，选自2017年指定为国宝文物的《宋代后半身像》册，共收录十二位北宋与南宋皇后珍贵画像。</p>
<p>许文美说，画中两位皇后穿戴宋代后妃最高等级的礼服与冠饰，称为“祎衣”（翟衣）与“九龙花钗冠”，用于受册、亲蚕、朝会等重要场合。“祎衣”上有成对雉鸡纹饰，“九龙花钗冠”饰以九龙、西王母与群仙仪仗，点缀飞鸟、花卉等图案，象征皇后母仪天下及呼应西王母尊贵祥瑞的神圣形象。</p>
<p>许文美表示，以笔墨勾勒声音意象的画境世界，也是本季书画新展一大亮点。此次“悦耳—倾听画里的声音”主题展，精选描绘自然与人文声音意境的“有声”画作，如“听松”、“听泉”、“听雨”、“听琴”等主题，邀请观众驻足倾听画里的声音。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14485725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14485725" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485725-2504180301332378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14485725" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485725-2504180301332378-600x390.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="390"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14485725" class="wp-caption-text">传宋 许道宁 松下曳杖 册。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14485724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14485724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485724-2504180259192378.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14485724" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14485724-2504180259192378-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14485724" class="wp-caption-text">唐 颜真卿 刘中使帖 册。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她说，其中《传宋 许道宁 松下曳杖》描绘湖畔苍松翠竹成荫，策杖文士自小径走出，驻足凝听松韵天籁，巾帽衣带随风扬起，显得诗意盎然。至于展出故宫历代书法名迹的“笔墨见真章—故宫书法导赏”例行展，许文美说，本季推荐唐代书法名家颜真卿《刘中使帖》。</p>
