<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>面对高压心慌意乱 中医师教你排忧解郁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508240925071770-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>面对高压心慌意乱 中医师教你排忧解郁。（陈柏州／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">面对高压情境，如股市狂泻、失业、房市套牢，人们因此而情绪低落、焦虑甚至失眠。中医提供排忧解郁的方法。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">基于中医理论“肝主疏泄”功能，肝在五行属木（*），负责调节气机、情志和血流的平衡。肝气不畅或郁滞时，会导致情绪低落、烦躁易怒、胸闷胁痛等症状，称之为“肝气郁结”。中医藉由“疏肝解郁”的方法来舒缓症状，就是通过调理肝脏功能，恢复气机通畅，以改善身心状态。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>按摩三个穴位 缓解压力、疏通肝气</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">通过按摩三个常用穴位，可以快速缓解压力、疏通肝气。</span></p>
<p><b>?合谷穴（大肠经）：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于手背，拇指与食指之间的虎口处。按摩此穴能舒缓紧张情绪、头痛等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14298999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298999" style="width: 365px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298999-2ed3bd4ff75d30ab5adbb0b55a91163c.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14298999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298999-2ed3bd4ff75d30ab5adbb0b55a91163c-597x400.jpg" alt="" width="365" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298999" class="wp-caption-text">合谷穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><b>内关穴（心包经）：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于前臂内侧，腕横纹上约2寸（三指宽）处。按摩此穴可安神、缓解胸闷和焦虑。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14327039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14327039" style="width: 382px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327039-dab5e8f0148b1bb19089cd3cd760f9f1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14327039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14327039-dab5e8f0148b1bb19089cd3cd760f9f1-597x400.jpg" alt="" width="382" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14327039" class="wp-caption-text">内关穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><b>神门穴（心经）：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于手腕内侧，小指侧腕横纹处。按摩此穴有助于镇静心神、改善失眠。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14354639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354639" style="width: 390px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354639-3f7ea6a5a7e3916a260b318baa57f567.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14354639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354639-3f7ea6a5a7e3916a260b318baa57f567-597x400.jpg" alt="" width="390" b="261" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354639" class="wp-caption-text">神门穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>建议：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">每天按摩这些穴位3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5次，每次5分钟左右，力度以感到酸胀但不痛为宜。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>三款中药茶饮 疏肝解郁、安神助眠</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">饮用中药茶饮从体内调理，能疏肝解郁、安神助眠。介绍三款简单易做的茶饮：</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>玫瑰花茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>作法：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">取5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10朵干燥玫瑰花，用热水冲泡，加入少许蜂蜜调味。每日饮用1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2杯。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">玫瑰花性温，入肝经，有助于疏肝解郁、缓解低落情绪、活血化瘀。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>疏肝茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>作法：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">取柴胡3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5克、薄荷3克、甘草2克，以沸水冲泡10分钟后饮用。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">柴胡是疏肝解郁的经典药材，此茶饮可舒缓胸闷、烦躁。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>枸杞菊花茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>作法：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">取菊花5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8朵、枸杞10粒，倒入热水冲泡。每日饮用1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2杯。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">菊花清肝明目，枸杞滋补肝肾。此茶饮能舒缓压力引起的眼睛干涩和情绪不稳。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>日常作息三大养肝原则 排忧解闷</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">日常作息遵循下列三大养肝原则：</span></p>
<p><b>?调整作息：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">避免熬夜，保持肝血充足，可减少口干苦的症状。</span></p>
<p><b>?饮食调理：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">多吃绿色食物（如：菠菜、芹菜）来养肝，少吃辛辣、油腻食物以免加重肝火。</span></p>
<p><b>?情志调摄：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">适度运动（如：太极、散步）或冥想，帮助舒展身心及气机流通。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">若身体感到严重不适，务必咨询专业医事人员。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">*补充说明：肝在五行属木，“木”代表生长、条达、升发等特性。◎◇</span></p>
