<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【歌曲】Light of Faith 信念的光</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;"><strong>Light of Faith</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">Let your faith be the light on your way<br />
Even storms can&#8217;t take the dawn away<br />
With Dafa inside, no fear when night falls<br />
Every step paints an eternal call</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">Through the long and lonely night you roam<br />
Dreams like stars once locked inside your soul<br />
Fall and rise again — you learn to grow<br />
Tears have washed your spirit’s glow</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">Who says you can’t learn to fly?<br />
You’re the captain of your sky<br />
When great waves block your shine and song<br />
Sing it louder — prove them wrong</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">Not every road will cheer you on<br />
But you know why your path goes on<br />
When your heartbeat drums with dreams so bright<br />
You become the starlit night</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;"><strong>信念的光</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">（中文大意）<br />
让信念指引你前行<br />
哪怕风雨遮住黎明<br />
心中有大法 就不怕黑暗来临<br />
每一步 都踏出永恒风景</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">走过孤单的长夜 无人同行<br />
梦像星辰 尘封的心被唤醒<br />
跌倒又爬起 才学会坚定<br />
眼泪洗净灵魂更清晰</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">谁说你不能飞翔？<br />
你是命运的船长<br />
当巨浪遮挡你的光芒<br />
你要更大声地歌唱</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">让信念指引你前行<br />
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">不是每条路 都会有掌声<br />
但你知道 自己为何启程<br />
当梦想成为脉搏的节奏<br />
你就是夜空最亮的恒星</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">就让信念指引你前行<br />
未来因你而更加动听<br />
愿你披荆斩棘 仍怀赤子之心<br />
追随大法 永远不停息</p>
<p style="padding-left: 200px;">——净泉音乐原创</p>
