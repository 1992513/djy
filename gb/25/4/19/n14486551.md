<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>古坑下雪了！荷苞山盛开的桐花如四月雪</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486564-759601-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>每年一度的“云林桐花祭”4月19日、20日于古坑乡荷苞山桐花公园浪漫登场（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年04月19日讯】（大纪元记者 廖素贞台湾云林报导）“古坑下雪了！”今年荷苞山的桐花，开的比往年还要茂盛，还要漂亮！车子一停靠桐花公园旁，迎面而来的是满地雪白的落英，不觉让人怀疑这里是不是下雪了？进入园区一眼望去，满山满谷盛开的油桐花，在风中摇曳生姿，时而像雪花飘落一样，欢迎着来此踏访的每一位游客，地面上一层层白色的花瓣，更增添油桐花的浪漫！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14486553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14486553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486553-759574.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14486553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486553-759574-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14486553" class="wp-caption-text">满山盛开的桐花吸引游客拍照打卡（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14486554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14486554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486554-759576.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14486554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486554-759576-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14486554" class="wp-caption-text">满地落英吸引民众拍照打卡。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14486556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14486556" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486556-759581.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14486556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486556-759581-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14486556" class="wp-caption-text">盛开的桐花吸引亲子拍照。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14486558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14486558" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486558-759582.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14486558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486558-759582-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14486558" class="wp-caption-text">今年的桐花开得特别茂盛！又特别漂亮！（廖素真／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>副县长谢淑亚指出，古坑荷苞山桐花公园的桐花，因地理环境的关系，是全台最早盛开的地方之一，每到4、5月就会满山遍野开满雪白般的油桐花，当微风徐徐吹来时，有如下雪般的浪漫景象，令人赏心悦目；每到这个季节，就吸引大批游客前来，今年以“云林古坑-春天的桐话”为主题，藉由“伯公祭仪”宣扬客家人敬天信神的文化同时结合在地观光，让更多民众认识古坑美景，更透过客家桐花祭的办理，让长年居住移居古坑的客家人更有归属感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14486552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14486552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486552-759572.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14486552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486552-759572-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14486552" class="wp-caption-text">今年活动结合艺术，在桐花步道也将展出“福智国民中小学”与“东和国中”书法与绘画作品，现桐花之美，让游客在花间，细细品味花与艺术的心灵对话。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年活动结合艺术、信仰、文化与慢生活的体验，让旅人慢慢赏花之余，也能深入体验古坑的客家风情。在桐花步道也将展出“福智国民中小学”与“东和国中”书法与绘画作品，现桐花之美，让游客在花间，细细品味花与艺术的心灵对话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14486562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14486562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486562-759594.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14486562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486562-759594-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14486562" class="wp-caption-text">爱花惜花的民众捡拾落英串成发饰延续花的生命（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14486561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14486561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486561-759593.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14486561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486561-759593-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14486561" class="wp-caption-text">满地落英如雪花（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14486559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14486559" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486559-759583.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14486559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/04/id14486559-759583-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14486559" class="wp-caption-text">今年的桐花开得特别茂盛！又特别漂亮！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古坑乡长林慧如表示，花祭期间，邀请游客于具有历史意义的“荷苞工作站”品尝在地咖啡，感受美丽的白桐花与香郁的黑咖啡的浪漫风景。此外，还能品尝荷苞社区特制的客家麻糬，手工制作、Q弹香甜，享受客家特色的味蕾飨宴。这个春天一场赏花盛宴，不仅要让古坑这片因桐花而美丽的土地被看见，更要让古坑咖啡文化与客家文化交织的独特历史被记忆，挚邀请您走进古坑，让“四月雪”的浪漫、客家文化的深韵，成为您最美的春日记忆 。</p>
