<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>峡谷深处的静默 造访冰岛羽毛河峡谷</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606790-798998-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>抬头望去，峡谷的裂缝像深刻的伤痕，却又呈现出壮丽的姿态。（Nina Chen提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）2025年9月29日，台湾空拍达人Nina Chen站在冰岛南部的羽毛河峡谷（Fjaerárgljúfur Canyon）边缘。她说：“这里诞生于九千年前的冰河时期，被誉为世界最美丽的峡谷。”蜿蜒的河水像一条银带，把整个峡谷切开。高耸的岩壁披上厚厚青苔，天色虽阴，但绿意与水声让人感觉大地仍在呼吸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14606785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14606785" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606785-798993.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14606785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606785-798993-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14606785" class="wp-caption-text">台湾空拍达人Nina造访冰岛羽毛河峡谷。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14606786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14606786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606786-798994.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14606786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606786-798994-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14606786" class="wp-caption-text">Nina Chen说：“这里诞生于九千年前的冰河时期，被誉为世界最美丽的峡谷。”（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14606787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14606787" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606787-798995.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14606787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606787-798995-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14606787" class="wp-caption-text">高耸的岩壁披上厚厚青苔，天色虽阴，但绿意与水声让人感觉大地仍在呼吸。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>徒步而上的路不同于艰难的健行路线，羽毛河峡谷的观景台步道并不费力。Nina 笑说：“我们选择徒步而上，不用太过辛苦，就能看见大自然的鬼斧神工。”沿途风声与脚下的碎石声交织，游客不多，显得宁静。抬头望去，峡谷的裂缝像深刻的伤痕，却又呈现出壮丽的姿态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14606789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14606789" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606789-798997.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14606789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606789-798997-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="806" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14606789" class="wp-caption-text">Nina 笑说：“我们选择徒步而上，不用太过辛苦，就能看见大自然的鬼斧神工。”（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>俯瞰的震撼走到高处俯瞰，眼前景象更为震撼。河水在深谷底部流转，岩石似被刀剑削出，两侧断崖绵延数公里。Nina 说，在这样的地形里，能感受到冰河与时间合力雕刻的力量，“峡谷不是一眼就能看完，它要你慢慢走，慢慢看。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14606792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14606792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606792-799000.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14606792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606792-799000-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14606792" class="wp-caption-text">Nina 说，在这样的地形里，能感受到冰河与时间合力雕刻的力量。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14606791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14606791" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606791-798999.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14606791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606791-798999-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14606791" class="wp-caption-text">走到高处俯瞰，眼前景象更为震撼。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>峡谷的宁静虽然天空低沉，云层压得厚重，但峡谷里有一种难得的宁静。草坡上的黄褐色与岩壁青苔交错，远方偶有游客的身影，却没有喧嚣。Nina 在峡谷边停下手机，静静望着河流说：“这里像是一首缓慢的歌，没有高潮，却让人不断回味。”羽毛河峡谷，并不是以壮大或险峻取胜，而是以历史与时间刻画出的线条，呈现出独特的美感。Nina 用手机记录，也用心感受。对她而言，这趟行走不是征服，而是与大自然对话的一段旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14606794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14606794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606794-799002.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14606794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14606794-799002-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14606794" class="wp-caption-text">羽毛河峡谷，并不是以壮大或险峻取胜，而是以历史与时间刻画出的线条，呈现出独特的美感。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
