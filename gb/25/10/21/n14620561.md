<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>青菜炒肉搭配酱汁 煮出美味佳肴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620583-P.25-1-e1761034625921-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>青菜炒肉。（台湾广厦提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>每天为了变不出好菜而烦恼吗？只要好好活用文章里教的酱汁搭配，轻轻松松就能变出美味的佳肴！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>青菜炒肉</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620583" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620583-P.25-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620583-P.25-1-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620583" class="wp-caption-text">青菜炒肉。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>基本作法</b></p>
<p><b>材料（2人份）：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">高丽菜…2～3片</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">猪肉薄片…100g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">红萝卜…1/4根</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">蚝油…1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">青椒…2个</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐、胡椒粉…各少量</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">洋葱…1/2个</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">沙拉油…1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">豆芽菜…1/2袋</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">〈混搭酱料〉*</span></p>
<p><b>作法：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.高丽菜切成大块，红萝卜切成适口大小，青椒去蒂去籽后纵切成4等分，洋葱切丝，豆芽菜去掉须根。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.猪肉薄片以蚝油、盐、胡椒粉腌入味。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.平底锅放1/2大匙沙拉油加热，放入作法2的猪肉炒到变色后起锅。再加上1/2大匙的沙拉油，放入作法1的蔬菜拌炒后再把猪肉回锅，淋上〈混搭酱料〉仔细拌匀。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>〈混搭酱料〉*</b></span></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.基本的青菜炒肉酱</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620582" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620582-P.25-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620582-P.25-2-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620582" class="wp-caption-text">基本的青菜炒肉酱。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>材料：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">酒…1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油…1小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐…1/4小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">胡椒粉…少量</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">所有材料拌匀，在基本作法3加入炒熟的肉、蔬菜中拌炒。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.味噌炒肉酱</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620581" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620581-P.25-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620581-P.25-3-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620581" class="wp-caption-text">味噌炒肉酱。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">味噌…1.5大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酒…1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">味醂…2.5小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖…1.5小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油…1小匙</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.辣味炒肉酱</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620580" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620580-P.25-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620580-P.25-4-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620580" class="wp-caption-text">辣味炒肉酱。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">葱末…1/2根</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">姜末…1/2匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">辣豆瓣酱…1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">沙拉油…1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">番茄酱…1.5大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酒、醋…各1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖…1小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐…少量</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">鸡高汤粉…1/4小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">热开水…1/4杯</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.咖喱酱油炒肉酱</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620579" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620579-P.25-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620579-P.25-5-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620579" class="wp-caption-text">咖喱酱油炒肉酱。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">咖喱粉…1/2～1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酒…2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油…1.5大匙</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>5.蚝油炒肉酱</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620578" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620578-P.25-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620578-P.25-6-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620578" class="wp-caption-text">蚝油炒肉酱。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">蚝油、酒…各1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐…1/2小匙</span><br />
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>料理小教室</b></span></h2>
<p><b>胡椒粉要怎样灵活运用呢？</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">粗粒胡椒粉的特征是具有香气浓郁、带有刺激辣味，细粒状常会出现在各式料理中。白胡椒粉的香味高雅，可用在清淡的食谱上；黑胡椒粉可利用其独特的风味，缓和肉的腥味。其实都可以用在所有的食谱上，善用其特色更能依个人喜好活用。◇</span></p>
