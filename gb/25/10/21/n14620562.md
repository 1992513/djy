<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>素面搭配不同的调味露 变化出更多口味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620595-P.96-e1761032621231-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>素面。（台湾广厦提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><b>编者按：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">运用巧思，将素面的基本款搭配各种不同的调味露，就能变化出吃不腻的口感。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>素面</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620595" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620595-P.96.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620595-P.96-300x448.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620595" class="wp-caption-text">素面。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>基本作法</b></p>
<p><b>材料（2人份）：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">素面…3把</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">〈调味露〉*</span></p>
<p><b>作法：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.锅里放充足的水加热到沸腾，放入素面、用长筷搅拌，依包装上的标示时间烹煮。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.起锅放在滤筛上用水冲洗，冷却后继续冲水或是用冰水搓洗，取出、沥干水分。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.盛碗，沾着〈调味露〉一起享用。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>〈调味露〉*</b></span></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.基本的素面调味露</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620594" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620594-P.97-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620594-P.97-1-300x309.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620594" class="wp-caption-text">基本的素面调味露。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>材料：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">高汤…1杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">味醂、酱油…各1/2大匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">材料放进小锅里开中火煮到沸腾，捞掉浮沫后即可熄火。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.南蛮调味露</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620593" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620593-P.97-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620593-P.97-2-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620593" class="wp-caption-text">南蛮调味露。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">咖喱粉…1小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">面味露（2倍浓缩）…1/4杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">水…1.5杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">沙拉油…2小匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">所有材料拌匀即完成。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.芝麻调味露</b></span></h3>

<figure id="attachment_14620590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620590" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620590-P.97-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620590-P.97-4-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620590" class="wp-caption-text">味噌豆浆调味露。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>料理小教室</b></span></h2>
<p><b>素面、凉面条、乌龙面：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">都是面粉加盐和水揉制成的干燥面食，因其粗细不同而有不同名称。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">素面是日本的面类当中最细的面，可做成凉面、汤面、炒面等料理。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">凉面条的粗细介于素面和乌龙面之间，在关东地方也有人当素面食用。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">乌龙面有的像赞岐乌龙面那样又粗又有嚼劲，也有像稻庭乌龙面那样又细又滑的细面条，种类很多。◇</span></p>
