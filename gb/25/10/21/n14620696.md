<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>结高尔夫球赛 昆州台湾慈善基金会推动公益</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620712-DSC00564_ratio-3-to-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台侨团体联合慈善晚会于10月16日晚间在枫林餐厅举行，由温锦文董事长及沈易霖主委代表捐赠澳币3万6千澳元予布里斯本市长慈善基金，由施林纳市长（Adrian Schrinner）夫妇代表接受。（倪尔森／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月21日讯】（大纪元特约记者倪尔森、琳达澳洲布里斯本报导）第十八届台湾杯高尔夫球赛于10月14日在黄金海岸Royal Pines Resort Golf Club举行，吸引144位各界人士踊跃参与。本次活动由第十八届台湾杯筹备会主任委员沈易霖、昆士兰台湾慈善基金会董事长温锦文共同召集筹办，结合运动竞技与慈善公益，展现台侨社区的凝聚力与爱心。</p>
<h1><strong>球赛精彩奖项丰富</strong></h1>
<p>比赛采用新新贝利亚制以电脑计算成绩，设有18洞技术奖、男女远距离等22项大奖。经过激烈角逐，总杆冠军由叶仕伟获得，净杆冠军则由Simon Chun夺得。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620711" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620711-DSC00552-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620711-DSC00552-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620711" class="wp-caption-text">高尔夫球赛总杆冠军由叶仕伟获得，布里斯本施林纳市长（Adrian Schrinner）代为颁发。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h1><strong>慈善晚会贵宾云集</strong></h1>
<p>10月16日晚间，台侨联合慈善晚会在布里斯本南区枫林餐厅隆重举行，近330位贵宾齐聚一堂。出席嘉宾包括布里斯本市长施林纳（Adrian Schrinner）伉俪、澳洲联邦议员坎贝尔（Julie-Anna Campbell）、洛根市长夫人雷鸣（Mrs Alison Raven）、布里斯本副议长黄文毅、驻布里斯本台北经济文化办事处范厚禄处长伉俪，以及昆士兰台湾中心许俊涌主任伉俪等政商界及侨界领袖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620710-DSC00587-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620710-DSC00587-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620710" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰台湾慈善基金会董事与贵宾合影。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h1><strong>捐赠善款回馈社区</strong></h1>
<p>晚会重头戏为温锦文董事长及沈易霖主委代表基金会，捐赠澳币3万6千元予布里斯本市长慈善基金，由施林纳（Adrian Schrinner）市长伉俪代表接受，全场报以热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620709-DSC00541-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620709-DSC00541-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620709" class="wp-caption-text">台侨团体联合慈善晚会上，布里斯本施林纳市长（ Adrian Schrinner）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布里斯本市长施林纳致词时表示，台湾杯高尔夫球赛举办18年来，已为市长慈善基金募集共46万8千澳元，这些善款真正改变了许多需要帮助者的生命。他赞扬台湾社区总是在有需要时挺身而出，为布里斯本、昆士兰及澳洲做出卓越贡献。</p>
<h1><strong>各界致词展现跨文化情谊</strong></h1>
<figure id="attachment_14620708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620708-DSC00531-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620708-DSC00531-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620708" class="wp-caption-text">台侨团体联合慈善晚会上，沈易霖主委致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主任委员沈易霖在致词中表示，昆士兰台湾慈善基金会自2007年成立以来，不仅举办比赛，更是凝聚在澳台湾人及连接整个社区的桥梁。透过长青活动中心（Taiwan Care）守护长者身心健康，透过支持布里斯本市长慈善基金，将关怀送到最需要的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620707-DSC00538-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620707-DSC00538-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620707" class="wp-caption-text">台侨团体联合慈善晚会上，办事处范厚禄处长致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>范厚禄处长表示，台湾最美的风景是人，侨胞在昆士兰热心回馈社会的努力，证明台湾人在哪里都是最美的风景。他感谢温锦文董事长秉持“老吾老以及人之老”的精神创立慈善基金会，展现“台湾Can Help”的良善力量与软实力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620706-DSC00543-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620706-DSC00543-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620706" class="wp-caption-text">台侨团体联合慈善晚会上，联邦议员坎贝尔（Julie-Anna Campbell）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>澳洲联邦议员坎贝尔（Julie-Anna Campbell）致词时，将高尔夫球赛比喻为“桥梁”，连接台湾社区的经济贡献与社区福祉。她赞扬当地台湾社区一直专注于创新，透过科技推动社区成果，并恭贺基金会多年来募集数十万澳元的慈善成就。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620705-DSC00546-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620705-DSC00546-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620705" class="wp-caption-text">台侨团体联合慈善晚会上，洛根市长夫人雷鸣（Alison Raven）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛根市长夫人雷鸣（Alison Raven）代表市长John Raven出席，她特别致敬布里斯本市长施林纳夫人（Nina Schrinner）在慈善事业上的持续奉献与倡导工作。作为洛根市慈善信托基金的副主席，她表示深受启发，希望在洛根也能建立同样的慷慨精神，为社区带来正面影响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620704-DSC00547-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620704-DSC00547-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620704" class="wp-caption-text">台侨团体联合慈善晚会上，台裔黄文毅副议长致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布里斯本副议长黄文毅以“蝴蝶效应”比喻慈善基金会的影响力，表示众人的努力如同蝴蝶翅膀，将台湾人的热情与善心传递给社会更多人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620703-DSC00573-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620703-DSC00573-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620703" class="wp-caption-text">台侨团体联合慈善晚会上，布里斯本市长施林纳夫人（ Nina Schrinner）致谢词。</figcaption></figure>
<p>布里斯本市长施林纳夫人（Nina Schrinner）代表市长慈善基金致谢时，特别表彰温锦文董事长18年来的卓越领导，赞扬他为台湾社区及澳洲社会的伟大贡献，称他为“我们社区的伟人、台湾社区的伟人，以及伟大的澳洲人”。</p>
<p>当晚温董事长并颁发感谢状给沈易霖主任委员，感谢辛劳筹备球赛与晚宴活动，并宣布下届台湾杯高尔夫球赛主委将由杨信生担任。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14620702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620702-DSC00598-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620702-DSC00598-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620702" class="wp-caption-text">慈善晚会上，提供机票摸彩的长荣航空代表与中奖者欣金斯（Craig Hinkins）合影。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14620701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620701-DSC00599-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14620701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620701-DSC00599-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620701" class="wp-caption-text">慈善晚会上，提供机票摸彩的中华航空代表与中奖者林豊源伉俪合影。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动在精致美食、丰富摸彩及华航、长荣航空提供的机票大奖中圆满落幕，再次展现台侨社区“以球会友、行善助人”的精神。</p>
