<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>4款“煮汁”让鱼料理更鲜美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620607-P.38-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>煮汁让鱼料理更鲜美。（台湾广厦提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>喜欢吃鱼，只要调配出不同的“煮汁”，就可以天天换口味，再也不用伤脑筋！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>煮鱼</b><strong>?</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14620607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620607" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620607-P.38-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620607-P.38-1-300x198.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="198" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620607" class="wp-caption-text">煮鱼。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>基本作法</b><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><b>材料（2人份）：</b><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">红大目仔2片</span><br />
<strong>〈煮汁〉*</strong><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">高汤（或水）1.5杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">薄切姜片1片</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><b>作法：</b><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.将〈煮汁〉和高汤（或水）、姜片放进锅里煮。</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.煮滚后打开锅盖，放入先前稍微煎过的红大目仔，盖上内盖蒸煮，一面添加煮汁一面煮。</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><b>〈煮汁〉*</b><strong>?</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.基本的煮鱼：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于红肉鱼等</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620606" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620606-P.38-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620606-P.38-2-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620606" class="wp-caption-text">基本的煮鱼。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>材料：</b><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">煮汁〔酒、砂糖 各2大匙、酱油 2大匙〕</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">材料放进锅里煮开即完成。</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.味噌煮鱼：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于青鱼等</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620605" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620605-P.38-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620605-P.38-3-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620605" class="wp-caption-text">味噌煮鱼。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">煮汁〔酒 1大匙、盐 3小匙、薄切姜片 1片〕</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">调味〔砂糖 1.5大匙、味噌 3大匙〕</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">〈煮汁〉的材料放进锅里煮开，然后加入〈调味〉再煮一下即完成。</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.柠檬奶油煮鱼：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于白肉鱼等</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620603" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620603-P.38-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620603-P.38-4-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620603" class="wp-caption-text">柠檬奶油煮鱼。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">煮汁〔白酒 1/4杯、盐 1/2小匙〕</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">调味〔奶油 1.5大匙〕</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">润饰〔柠檬汁2大匙〕</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">平底锅放适量沙拉油（分量外）热锅，并排放入两片鱼。煎2～3分钟到两面上焦色，取出。放入所有〈煮汁〉的材料，煮2～3分钟。再把先前煎的鱼放进锅里，加入〈调味〉煮一下，最后撒上柠檬汁即可熄火。</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.番茄汁煮鱼：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于青鱼等</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620604" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620604-P.38-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620604-P.38-5-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620604" class="wp-caption-text">番茄汁煮鱼。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">煮汁〔味醂 2小匙、砂糖 1小匙、酱油 2.5大匙、番茄汁 1/4杯〕</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><b>作法：</b></p>
<p><strong>?</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">把材料放进锅里，用大火煮开即可。</span><strong>?</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>料理小教室</b></span></h2>
<p><strong><b>用姜消除腥味：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">姜的香味成分—姜烯酚、姜粉、姜辣素，具有消除臭味的效果。这些成分以接近表皮含量最多，因此使用诀窍在于连皮使用，或是削皮时消薄一点。除了消除腥臭味之外，也具有杀菌效果。◇</span></strong></p>
