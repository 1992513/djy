<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>饭团不需要配菜 用调味料口感更佳</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620626-P.60-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>烤饭团。（台湾广厦提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>只要改变调味方式，一菜就能多吃，天天换口味，一日三餐，再也不用伤脑筋！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>饭团</b></span></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.梅肉柴鱼</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620628" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620628-P.60-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620628-P.60-1-300x301.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620628" class="wp-caption-text">梅肉柴鱼。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>材料（2人份）：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">梅干…中型4个</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">柴鱼片…3g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油、鸡粉…各少量</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">米饭…约400g</span></p>
<p><b>作法：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">梅干去籽、用菜刀拍成泥状，加入柴鱼片、酱油、鸡粉，再度拍打。放在饭的中央，捏成饭团。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.紫苏魩仔鱼柚香胡椒粉</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620629" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620629-P.60-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620629-P.60-2-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620629" class="wp-caption-text">紫苏魩仔鱼柚香胡椒粉。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油…1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">魩</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">仔鱼…4大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">青紫苏（切细丝）…4片</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">柚香胡椒粉…少量</span><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">将材料和饭拌匀、捏成饭团，柚香胡椒粉可视个人喜好增减。</span></p>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">美乃滋…1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">胡椒粉…少量</span><br />
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">将材料和饭拌匀、捏成饭团即完成。</span></p>

<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>酱油</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">说到代表日本的调味料，非酱油莫属。美丽的色泽和浓郁的香气，具备甘味、酸味、盐味、苦味、美味等“五元味”，是极珍贵稀有的万能调味料。为了突显这种独特的香气和美味，诀窍在于调味的最后添加。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.浓口酱油：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">即浓味酱油，以明亮的红褐色和丰富的香气为特征，除了加热烹调，也可以当沾酱、浇酱使用。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620617" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620617-P.61-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620617-P.61-1-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620617" class="wp-caption-text">浓口酱油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.再仕入酱油：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">采取二度酿造的制法，色泽、味道、香气都很浓醇。当沾酱、调味用，也有“甘露酱油”的称呼。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620616" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620616-P.61-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620616-P.61-2-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620616" class="wp-caption-text">再仕入酱油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.溜酱油：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">特征为具有黏稠、浓厚口感以及独特香气，加热后会呈现漂亮的红色，因此常用在照烧或烤仙贝。也很适用当生鱼片沾酱。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620615" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620615-P.61-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620615-P.61-3-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620615" class="wp-caption-text">溜酱油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.薄口（淡口）酱油：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">特征是盐分比浓口酱油高、色泽较淡，适用于用在凸显食材原味、特色的汤品上。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620614" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620614-P.61-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620614-P.61-4-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620614" class="wp-caption-text">薄口（淡口）酱油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>5.白酱油：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">盐分和淡口酱油相同，颜色更淡薄。由于酦酵时间短，味道较清淡。具有特有的香气，常用在汤品和茶碗蒸等菜肴。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620613" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620613-P.61-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620613-P.61-5-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620613" class="wp-caption-text">白酱油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>料理小教室</b></span></h2>
<h3><b>鱼酱</b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">顾名思义，就是鱼的酱油，即鱼贝类酦酵后制成的液体调味料。日本自古就有制造鱼酱的文化，但只有部分地区仍保留酱油的名称。然而，在保留名称的地区，到现代仍然是家乡菜不可或缺的调味料。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">由于味道独特，所以好恶分明，一经加热就变得柔和且美味升级，是非常优秀的调味料。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.咸鱼汁：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">也称为鱼酱汁，在用盐腌制大鳍毛齿鱼时，从渗出的汁液过滤出的产物，独特的香气和丰富的甘味是其特征。秋田的家乡菜“咸鱼汁锅”很有名。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620612" style="width: 132px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620612-P.61-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14620612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620612-P.61-6-300x712.jpg" alt="" width="132" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620612" class="wp-caption-text">咸鱼汁。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.玉筋鱼酱油：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">用产于濑户内海的玉筋鱼制成，也是日本历史最悠久的酱油。也可以用作豆腐和生鱼片的沾酱。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620611" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620611" style="width: 157px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620611-P.61-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14620611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620611-P.61-7.jpg" alt="" width="157" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620611" class="wp-caption-text">玉筋鱼酱油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.いしり（能登的鱼酱）：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">富山名产，用沙丁鱼和花枝制成，用于炒蔬菜调味，肉类菜肴也很搭。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620610" style="width: 106px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620610-P.61-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14620610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620610-P.61-8.jpg" alt="" width="106" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620610" class="wp-caption-text">いしり（能登的鱼酱）。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.鱼露：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">泰国最具代表性的调味料，独特的香气和浓厚的美味是特征。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14620609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620609" style="width: 127px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620609-P.61-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14620609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620609-P.61-9.jpg" alt="" width="127" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620609" class="wp-caption-text">鱼露。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
