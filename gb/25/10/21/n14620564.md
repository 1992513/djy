<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>涮涮锅蘸料千变万化 你喜欢哪一种？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14376493-shutterstock_1771758302-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>涮涮锅蘸料的千变万化，更加衬托肉质的美味。（Shutterstock）?</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">涮涮锅蘸料的千变万化，更加衬托肉质的美味。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>涮涮锅</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620608" style="width: 261px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620608-P.108-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14620608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620608-P.108-1-300x451.jpg" alt="" width="261" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620608" class="wp-caption-text">涮涮锅。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>基本作法</b></p>
<p><b>材料（容易制作的分量）：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">牛肉薄片…600～800g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">喜好的蔬菜</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">〈涮涮锅的蘸料〉*</span></p>
<p><b>作法：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.牛肉从包装袋取出，以方便一片一片挟取的方式摆盘，蔬菜切成适口大小。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.锅里放高汤煮滚后，用筷子挟牛肉进锅里快速涮一下再起锅，沾着〈涮涮锅的蘸料〉享用。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>〈蘸料〉*</b></span></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.柑橘醋酱油</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620602" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620602-P.108-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620602-P.108-2-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620602" class="wp-caption-text">柑橘醋酱油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>材料：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">柚子（或柑橘类榨汁）…2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">薄口酱油…1/4杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖…1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酒…1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">味醂…1.5大匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">酒和味醂放进耐热容器，直接送进微波炉（500W）加热1分30秒，取出冷却后，加入剩下的材料拌匀即可。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.梅肉酱油</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620601" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620601-P.108-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620601-P.108-3-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620601" class="wp-caption-text">梅肉酱油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">梅肉…5大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油…1杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">味醂、酒…各1/3杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖…1大匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">所有材料放进锅里拌匀，开较强的中火煮滚后，熄火、使慢慢降温到不烫手的程度即可。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.芝麻酱</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620600" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620600-P.108-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620600-P.108-4-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620600" class="wp-caption-text">芝麻酱。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">芝麻酱…2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油、煮掉酒精成分的味醂、高汤…各1.5大匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">所有材料拌匀。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.盐味酱</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620599" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620599-P.108-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620599-P.108-5-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620599" class="wp-caption-text">盐味酱。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">高汤…1/2杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">蒜泥…1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">白芝麻粉…1小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">芝麻油…1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐…1/4小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">细葱末…1～2根</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">日本太白粉、水…各1大匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">芝麻油放锅里，开中火加热，放入大蒜、细葱爆香后，加入高汤煮到滚，再加入盐、芝麻粉拌匀。加入太白粉水勾芡后熄火。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>5.柠檬山药泥酱</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14620598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14620598" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620598-P.108-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14620598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14620598-P.108-6-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14620598" class="wp-caption-text">柠檬山药泥酱。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">山药…120g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">洋葱…1/8个</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">柠檬汁…1.5大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油…1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">高汤…1/4杯</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">山药和洋葱磨成泥状，所有材料拌匀即可。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>料理小教室</b></span></h3>
<p><b>涮涮锅：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">涮涮锅使用的像甜甜圈长相的锅具，据说是从中国传到日本。由于表面积比普通锅大，因此具有防止温度降低的效果，是涮涮锅最适用的锅具。◇</span></p>
