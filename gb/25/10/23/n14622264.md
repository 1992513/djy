<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>房源急缺 珀斯22区周租破千元</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622265-1b59995ae3ab7c3b92612d9a58c9cca1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>最新报告显示，由于珀斯住房供应的严重短缺，现在平均租金已逼近每周750澳元，其中22个区的独立房的每周租金已经超过一千澳元。图为珀斯一套独立房待租。（周鑫／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月23日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">最新报告显示，由于珀斯住房供应的严重短缺，仅过去三个月内，独立房每周租金上涨了近</span><span lang="en-AU">7</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，现在平均租金已逼近每周</span><span lang="en-AU">750</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，其中</span><span lang="en-AU">22</span><span lang="zh-TW">个区的独立房的每周租金已经超过一千澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，根据房地产数据机构</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Cotality</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">发布的《季度租赁报告》，在住房供应持续紧张的背景下，租客成为最大输家。目前珀斯出租房的空置率仅为</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1.1</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，低于一年前的</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1.4</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，远低于被认为市场平衡的水平——</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">3</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>报告指出：“在全澳范围内，住房供应不足仍是推高租金的主要因素。”</p>
<p>珀斯独立房平均租金在全国排名第三，仅次于悉尼（每周<span lang="en-AU">841</span>澳元）和达尔文（每周<span lang="en-AU">743</span>澳元）。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在珀斯，房租最高的前十名地区中，</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Dalkeith</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">区最贵，每周中位租金为</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1428</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，其后依次为</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Cottesloe</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1428</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Swanbourne</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1422</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">CityBeach</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1388</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Mount</span> <span lang="en-AU">Claremont</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1374</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Mosman</span> <span lang="en-AU">Park</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1272</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Nedlands</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1272</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Floreat</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1258</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Claremont</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1215</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Burns</span> <span lang="en-AU">Beach</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1206</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">单元房市场同样没有给租客带来多少缓解，平均租金仍高达每周</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">674</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，过去一个季度，周租金上涨了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">5.4</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，而空置率仅为</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯的单元房租金在本季度涨幅全国第二，仅次于达尔文。自</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2020</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">年以来，珀斯单元房租金上涨了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">71.9</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，独立房租金在过去五年中也暴涨了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">64.5</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>与此同时，房东则迎来了全国最丰厚的租金回报。</p>
<p>租金回报率是房屋租金收入占房屋总价值的百分比，比例越高代表收益越好。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯独立房的租金回报率为</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">4</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，高于全国主要城市平均的</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">3.1</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">；单元房的回报率更高，达</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">5.4</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，远超全国主要城市平均水平——</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">4.5</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">如果租客想寻找相对便宜的租房选择，可以关注</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Orelia</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">区（每周</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">469</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Dudley</span> <span lang="en-AU">Park</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">478</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）、</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Shoalwater</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">523</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）以及</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Mandurah</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">538</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）的单元房。</span></p>
