<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿中华文化协会开放日 20社团参与</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622432-1-1-1GBCCA-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>10月18日，纽英崙青少年中文夏令营团长赵铨雄（右一）、文协副会长吴德惠（右二）及团员在开放日为新年度招生。（文协提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月23日讯】（大纪元讯）大波士顿中华文化协会（简称文协，GBCCA）于10月18日在会所举办开放日，介绍该会长期致力于传承与推广中华文化，提供不同年龄层参与的多元社团活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622430-1-1-10GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622430-1-1-10GBCCA-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622430" class="wp-caption-text">文协干事们：（右起）韩莲芳，会长赵宁，副会长吴德惠，陈玉瑛，吴宇怀，严安莉，梁慧玲等。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次开放日的内容十分丰富，涵盖社团展示、互动表演与茶点交流，让参与者体验文化魅力、感受社团多样风貌与社区热情。活动内容亮点：</p>
<p>1.摊位展示——此次约有20个社团参与，其中包括华心中文学校、华心艺术学校的武安武艺学馆、纽英崙青少年中文夏令营、云鹤社、文协合唱团、卡拉OK社、向日葵艺术坊。以及由年轻人主导的父子同乐、有氧舞动与艺术探索等3个新社团。同时邀请了友团NECAA及余心乐有机农产品参与推广。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622427-1-1-2GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622427-1-1-2GBCCA-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622427" class="wp-caption-text">华心中文学校校长陈瑞虹（右起）、萧云现场介绍该校。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622418" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622418-1-1-3GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622418-1-1-3GBCCA-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622418" class="wp-caption-text">新增社团——“父子游戏”展位。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.精彩表演——两场由吴萍萍主持、文协社团带来的演出节目，包含由杨晓蓓指导的波士顿健身腰鼓队《红红的日子》、武安武艺学馆章德邻与林欣怡带领学生呈现祥狮献端与多项武术展示、徐莉老师班的尊巴舞、张欢老师武术班由谈继欣带领的太极表演、林老师二胡班演奏、国乐社社长杨信宜的国乐说明。还有由Jason Wang带领极受欢迎的扯铃表演、卡拉OK社长杨哲修和社员们高歌演唱、以及杨晓华带来的秧歌舞演出。全场气氛热烈、掌声不断。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622429" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622429-1-1-4GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622429-1-1-4GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622429" class="wp-caption-text">（右起）波士顿侨教中心主任高家富到访，文协会长赵宁和副会长吴德惠接待。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622419" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622419-1-1-5GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622419-1-1-5GBCCA-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622419" class="wp-caption-text">腰鼓舞演出。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天活动吸引多族裔、不同年龄层的社区人士参观，许多人表达希望加入或持续关注音乐、武术、文化、语言与休闲类社团，以增进亲子互动与文化参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622420" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622420" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622420-1-1-7GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622420" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622420-1-1-7GBCCA-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622420" class="wp-caption-text">参观者专注卡拉OK的看板。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622428-1-1-8GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622428-1-1-8GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622428" class="wp-caption-text">（右起）文协董事长葛幼梅，韩莲芳和陈嘉美坐镇云鹤社摊位。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次活动由文协干事会及志工团队共同筹划，当天会长赵宁、韩莲芳夫妇，副会长吴德惠，董事长葛幼梅，华心中文学校陈瑞虹、萧云，云鹤社龙俐丽、汤耀武，纽英崙青少年中文夏令营团长赵铨雄等人，均亲自在现场接待解说。波士顿侨教中心主任高家富也到场参观致意。</p>
<p>会场更有志工陈玉瑛与梁慧玲准备的点心与瓶装水，让宾客得以在轻松氛围中交流。并且还提供抽奖活动，增添趣味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622424" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622424-1-1-6GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622424-1-1-6GBCCA-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622424" class="wp-caption-text">志工陈玉瑛（右）与梁慧玲准备的点心与瓶装水，供大家使用。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622431" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622431-1-1-9GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622431-1-1-9GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622431" class="wp-caption-text">青少年中文夏令营摊位规模最大，引起兴趣，已有家庭准备报名2026年的活动。（文协提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次文协开放日不仅是一场社区文化盛会，也为大家提供一个共享美好时光、认识新朋友与体验文化的机会。下一场文协的大型活动为2026年1月31日，在Newton市Zervas School学校举办庆祝农历新年的年会，详情请上网站：gbcca.org。◇（图文：文协提供）</p>
