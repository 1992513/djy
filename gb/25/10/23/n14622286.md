<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>秋日“屿”光 用镜头记录马祖的味道与人情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622295-804018-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>划着立桨，漂浮在平静的马港海面上，背后是静静守望的妈祖巨像。那一刻，夕阳、笑容与浪花交织成最动人的画面。（赖小芳提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月23日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬马祖报导）马祖的秋天安静而有光。风吹过海面，带着盐与草的味道。摄影家赖小芳笑着说：“我喜欢这样的季节，阳光不刺眼，海浪也变得温柔。人和海的距离，好像也更近了。”这一次，她带着相机走进芙蓉澳园区，记录淡菜从海里捞起到餐桌的一整个过程。她说，那不是为了拍美食，而是想留下渔人日常的模样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622288-804011.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622288-804011-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622288" class="wp-caption-text">人们换上手套、拿起锈斑的菜刀，在木板上敲打淡菜，去除藤壶与泥沙。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622289-804012.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622289-804012-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622289" class="wp-caption-text">以岛上渔获入菜的小巧前菜登场，淡菜酱配柑橘皮、腌萝卜花与溏心蛋。（赖小芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海上的日常“敲淡菜”是她印象最深的一幕。几位游客戴着手套，用锈斑的菜刀轻敲贝壳，去掉藤壶和泥沙。旁边的老渔民阿池笑着教大家：“这样敲，贝壳才不会破。”“那一刻，我觉得马祖的海不只是蓝色的，它有人的气味。”赖小芳说。她的镜头里不只记录风景，更记下人与海的对话。那张满是贝壳与手套的照片，她说自己拍了很久，“我想拍到那种‘劳动的光’，不是太阳的光，而是人努力生活的亮。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622287-804010.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622287-804010-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622287" class="wp-caption-text">这一幕是芙蓉澳养殖区最常见的日常，也是岛屿居民与海共生的缩影。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622293-804016.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622293-804016-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622293" class="wp-caption-text">刚从海上捞起的淡菜经过清蒸与调味，贝壳敞开，橙黄的肉质饱满鲜甜。这锅海味，仿佛凝缩了整个马祖的秋天。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>味觉里的故事她拍下那碗“油渍淡菜猫饭”。“那味道很单纯，却有一种深海的温柔。”她笑着形容。桌上还有前菜、小蛋塔、柑橘气泡饮，都是用当地食材设计的限定料理。“我觉得这不只是吃饭，而是一场岛屿的展览。”她的镜头缓慢移动，光线落在汤匙与淡菜上，记录下海洋进入生活的样子。她还拍到旅人划着SUP立桨，背后是夕阳和妈祖巨像。“那天的光太特别了，像是在祝福每个人。有人在海上喊，也有人安静地划。我想，这就是马祖，它会用自己的方式拥抱你。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622290-804013.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622290-804013-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622290" class="wp-caption-text">一杯冰镇柑橘气泡饮带来清新的海岛风味，酸甜交织，如秋风拂面，洗去旅途的盐味与倦意。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622292-804015.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622292-804015-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622292" class="wp-caption-text">餐后的柚香乳酪塔，外层酥脆、内馅柔软，搭配窗外洒落的阳光，是属于马祖慢旅午后的甜美句点。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622291-804014.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622291-804014-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622291" class="wp-caption-text">在热腾腾的白饭上，淋上橄榄油与酱汁，淡菜的咸香海味与米饭融合，成为一碗属于马祖的暖心料理。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622294" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622294-804017.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622294-804017-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622294" class="wp-caption-text">旅人们抬起桨板，在余晖中向大海致意。金色的阳光映照海面，为这趟“海上朝圣之路”揭开序幕。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>夜色里的火光夜里，大家围坐在庭院。木桌上是一碗碗淡菜汤面，热气腾起，灯光柔和。她按下快门，记录下那一瞬的温度。“我喜欢拍人吃饭的样子，那是真实的时刻。大家都放下手机，专心品尝眼前的食物。那一刻的笑容，比星星还亮。”她说，每一次拍摄都像是一场新的认识。“我不是在拍马祖的风景，而是在拍岛上的呼吸。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14622297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622297" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622297-804020.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622297-804020-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622297" class="wp-caption-text">一碗热腾腾的淡菜汤面冒着白雾。新鲜淡菜搭配蔬菜与香菇，汤头清甜，像是海风轻拂过味蕾的温度。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14622300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14622300" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622300-804023.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14622300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14622300-804023-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14622300" class="wp-caption-text">旅人们围坐在户外木桌边，灯光与炊烟交织。大家品尝着淡菜料理与汤面，分享旅途见闻。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用影像说岛语对赖小芳来说，摄影不只是记录，更是一种倾听。“有时候，我会放下相机，只听海浪的声音。”她说，“我希望别人看到照片时，也能听到那个声音。”这趟秋日之旅，她用相机捕捉了淡菜、夕阳、咖啡香与笑声，也捕捉到马祖岛上独有的“慢”。“我拍的每一张照片，都是一段对话。人和岛、海和风、光和影，都在说故事。”最后，她望着照片里那碗热汤微笑：“那碗汤，其实是马祖的味道，简单、诚实、和温暖。”</p>
