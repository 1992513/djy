<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】力量与日常的新平衡 2025 BMW M5 PHEV</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614048-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_02-600x400.jpg" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月11日讯】在高端性能轿车市场中，2025款BMW M5以其插电式混合动力系统的转型，标志着M系列的一次显着演进。这款车首次融合传统V8引擎与电动技术，旨在平衡强劲动力与日常可用性。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d78f5frq5siq8.cloudfront.net/S2bBBHtfz4Pi"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/4VDmTBJ7DrI?si=4i1hY2_TECTZo5l3" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>作为BMW的中型性能轿车，它提供717马力和738磅英尺扭矩的综合输出。起售价约为12万美元，这不仅吸引资深车迷，还迎合注重科技与可靠性的中产群体。相比纯燃油前辈，新M5在保留运动基因的同时，回应了能源转型潮流，使其更适用于多样化场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614021-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614021-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614021" class="wp-caption-text">2025 BMW M5 PHEV。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614024" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614024" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614024-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614024" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614024-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614024" class="wp-caption-text">前20英寸后21英寸轮胎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025款M5的外观延续5系家族语言，但通过M专属元素强化力量感。车身长200.6英寸、宽77.6英寸、高59.4英寸，轴距118.3英寸，整体比例低矮宽阔。前脸以大型肾形格栅为主，搭配狭长LED大灯和锐利保险杠线条。侧面流线型设计结合空气动力学套件，包括主动格栅和侧裙，尾部以四出排气管和扩散器收尾，强调性能导向，全碳纤维制造的车顶可大大减轻车重。新M5传承BMW经典美学的同时，适应混合动力布局，使车辆更具现代感，而非单纯激进风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614025" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614025-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_07.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614025-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614025" class="wp-caption-text">LED大灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614026-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614026-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614026" class="wp-caption-text">M5徽章。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>M5的内饰设计在豪华与实用间取得平衡，注重日常使用的便利性，同时保留高端性能轿车的精致氛围。座舱采用Merino皮革和铝合金饰板，可选碳纤维或木纹装饰，质感上乘。前排运动座椅提供出色包裹性和8向电动调节，填充适中，兼顾长途舒适与动态支撑。氛围灯提供多种色调选择，夜间驾驶沉浸感十足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614027" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614027-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_48.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614027-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_48-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614027" class="wp-caption-text">M5内饰设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>空间表现优于传统性能轿车。前排头部和腿部空间充裕，可舒适容纳三位成人。行李厢容积约16.5立方英尺，受电池组影响略有压缩，但配备电动尾门和可折叠后排座椅，适合家庭出行或日常装载。中央扶手箱可容纳小型平板，四个杯架分布在前中后排，门板储物格深度适中，可放置水瓶或手机。这些配置增强了M5的日常便利性，满足家庭用户需求。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614028" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614028-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_32.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614028-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_32-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614028" class="wp-caption-text">12.3英寸仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台的Curved Display组合了12.3英寸数字仪表与14.9英寸触控萤幕。该系统支持无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto，界面层级分明、功能集中。物理按键与触控并存，提升操作便利性。标准Bowers &amp; Wilkins音响系统，19扬声器输出很震撼。可选全景天窗和加热/通风座椅进一步提升舒适度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614035-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_38.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614035-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_38-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614035" class="wp-caption-text">14.9英寸触控萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>动力系统由 4.4 升双涡轮 V8 与电机联合驱动，综合输出约 717 马力和 738 磅英尺扭矩，配合约 14.8 kWh 的电池，实现平顺且线性的加速体验。低速电动模式安静顺滑，而高负载时 V8 强劲介入，带来典型 BMW 风琴式油门响应和精准动力回馈，让驾驶者能感受到清晰的动力曲线与操控乐趣，0-60英里时的加速约3.4秒。自适应悬挂和后轮转向提升了过弯稳定性和敏捷感，即便整备重量达到 5,390 磅，方向盘依旧轻盈，高速表现稳定，兼顾日常通勤与周末驾驶的激情体验。这种调校保持了 BMW 一贯的驾驶感，既不过于生硬，也不失运动乐趣，让性能与舒适达到平衡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614036-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614036-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614036" class="wp-caption-text">4.4 升双涡轮 V8 与电机联合驱动。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全配置是M5的卖点，标配BMW Driving Assistant，包括自动紧急制动、行人检测、车道偏离警告和保持辅助。可选Driving Assistance Professional包添加自适应巡航和hands-free模式，提升长途可靠性。被动安全结构与10个气囊布局经IIHS和NHTSA测试出色，美国4年/50,000英里基础保修及3年免费保养，降低长期不确定性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614038-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614038-2025_BMW_M5_PHEV_17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614038" class="wp-caption-text">车侧的充电端口。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025款BMW M5以先进混合动力和技术配置，展现豪华性能轿车的成熟进化。它将“轻、脆、野”性能情绪融入更复杂的技术方案，提供充足动力储备、周到日常便利与成熟安全保障。它或许不是最耀眼的超级轿车，但极有可能成为“既能周末放肆，也能平日安心”的家庭伙伴。对于注重品质与效率的消费者，这款M5值得考虑。◇</p>
