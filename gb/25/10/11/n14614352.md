<p>【大纪元2025年10月11日讯】<span class="s1">每年深受欢迎的<strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">万锦市</span><span style="color: #0000ff;">圣诞市集假日家居展</span></strong></span>（<span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>Markham Home for the Holidays</b></span>）<span class="s1">将于</span> <b>2025 </b><span class="s1"><b>年</b></span><b> 11 </b><span class="s1"><b>月</b></span><b> 21 </b><span class="s1"><b>日至</b></span><b> 23 </b><span class="s1"><b>日</b></span> <span class="s1">在</span> <strong>万锦市展览场</strong>（Markham Fairgrounds<span class="s1"><b>）</b></span> <span class="s1">盛大举行，迎接第</span> 38 <span class="s1">届年度展会。</span><span class="s2">这场活动不只是购物，更是一场充满节庆氛围的冬季体验，让你提前感受圣诞的温馨与魔法。</span></p>
