<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>投资者涌向新州偏远地区 回报率优于悉尼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/L1080087-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新州的Bathurst、Young、Lismore、Inverell和Casino由于房价相对可负担且生活方式优势明显，加上具有吸引力的回报率，因此需求激增。图为Bathurst。（简沐/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月22日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">（大纪元记者朱丽娅悉尼编译报导）新州偏远地区市场的收益率超越悉尼，越来越多的投资者将目光投向了这些区域。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">据澳洲房地产投资网消息，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Raine &amp; Horne</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">房地产网络报告称，新州偏远地区市场近几个月来咨询量大幅增长，年度同比增长了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">20%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">至</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">30%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">，投资者关注强劲的租房需求和更高的投资回报。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Raine &amp; Horne</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">的高级经纪人</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Craig Betalli</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">表示，投资者对偏远地区市场的兴趣受到了三次降息的推动，促使他们重新思考如何实现高回报的投资策略。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">“借贷成本下降增强了投资者的信心，许多人都在询问如何最大限度利用这些降息带来的优势。”</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Betalli</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">说，“偏远地区市场提供了很好的条件，投资者可以用更少的资金获得更大的回报，同时还能够利用强劲的租房需求和更高回报率的优点。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">最新</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Cotality</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">数据显示，悉尼的独立房中位价为</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">152.1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">万澳元，投资毛回报率约为</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">3%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">。相比之下，新州偏远地区的中位价为</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">80.2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">万澳元，平均回报率为</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">4.2%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Betalli</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">表示，新州的</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Bathurst</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Young</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Lismore</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Inverell</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">和</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Casino</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">由于房价相对可负担且生活方式优势明显，加上具有吸引力的回报率，因此需求激增。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">他说：“现在不仅是经验丰富的投资者和自管退休基金者在寻找这些机会，很多首次投资者也希望能以较低的门槛进入市场。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">在新州北部城镇</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Casino</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">，当地</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Raine &amp; Horne </span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">房地产公司的老板</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Kate Morgan</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">表示，新州北部河流区的投资者需求持续增长，但供应仍然紧张。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Morgan</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">说：“我们收到很多投资者的咨询，但问题在于市场上缺乏可供出售的房源。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">她表示，当地一套标准的三居室出租房售价约为</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">50</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">万澳元，周租金在</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">480</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">至</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">500</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">澳元之间，显示出该镇高达</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">5%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">的毛租金回报率。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Morgan</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">还呼吁考虑出售房产的房主，抓住当前投资者需求旺盛、市场供应有限的有利时机。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Raine &amp; Horne</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">表示，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Bathurst</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">在今年大部分时间内，由首次购房者和自住买家主导房产市场之后，投资者正在逐渐重获市场份额。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Raine &amp; Horne Bathurst</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">的总监</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Grant Maskill-Dowton</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">表示，虽然在过去六个月中投资者的销售大幅下降，但在过去两个月里形势已经发生了转变。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">“我们现在收到越来越多关于租金回报、空置率和现金流的咨询。”他说，“年初时并没有这样，这说明投资者正在重返市场，并认真进行市场调研。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">此外，他们还观察到空置率极低，仅为</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">0.05%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">，再加上可观的租金回报，进一步增强了投资者信心。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Maskill-Dowton</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">说：“例如，一套售价</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">70</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">万澳元的房产，在</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Bathurst</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">的租金可能为每周</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">600</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">澳元左右。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">“现在来看房的投资者都非常认真”，他说，“有些是本地人，我们甚至还收到悉尼买家的咨询，这说明</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Bathurst</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">已经重新成为投资者关注的焦点。”</span></p>
