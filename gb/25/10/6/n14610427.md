<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园女孩足球体验营 激发运动热情迎接国际女孩日</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610428-799970-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>足球体验营大合照。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月06日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）为回应联合国订定每年10月11日的“国际女孩日”，桃园市政府妇幼发展局于国立中央大学依仁堂举办“桃园女孩日系列活动—足球体验营”，邀请超过50名女孩齐聚球场，共同感受足球运动的魅力，展现自信与活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14610429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14610429" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610429-799971.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14610429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610429-799971-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14610429" class="wp-caption-text">足球体验营女孩练习中。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动邀集多位专业教练与裁判规划多元有趣的课程，从足球技巧教学、趣味团康到友谊竞赛，带领女孩们实际体验足球的节奏与挑战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14610430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14610430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610430-799972.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14610430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610430-799972-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14610430" class="wp-caption-text">足球体验营女孩练习传球。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>课程内容包括带球绕杆、传球接力、足球保龄球目标射击、射门及守门挑战等项目，现场欢笑声不断，气氛热烈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14610431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14610431" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610431-799973.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14610431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610431-799973-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14610431" class="wp-caption-text">足球体验营郑教练指导中。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杜慈容局长亲临现场，颁发专属结业证书与礼品，鼓励女孩勇于尝试新事物，培养坚定与勇敢的运动精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14610432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14610432" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610432-799974.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14610432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610432-799974-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14610432" class="wp-caption-text">足球交流赛国小组赛后颁奖与杜慈蓉局长大合照。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>女孩们在依仁堂现场观赏台湾女子五人制足球比赛并由教练现场讲解，进一步了解女子足球的发展历程与专业精神，启发对多项运动的兴趣与热情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14610433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14610433" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610433-799975.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14610433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610433-799975-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14610433" class="wp-caption-text">足球交流赛大合照。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杜慈容接受纬来体育台访谈表示，桃园市多年来持续回应联合国“国际女孩日”精神，致力推动以女孩为主体的政策与资源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14610434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14610434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610434-799976.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14610434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610434-799976-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14610434" class="wp-caption-text">足球交流赛国小组-彩绿教练指导战术中。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年以足球为主题，象征女孩勇于挑战、突破限制的力量，期待透过体验营与交流赛，让更多女孩在运动场上建立自信，发现自己的光芒，也让桃园成为更性别友善的城市。</p>
<p>在台茂购物中心7楼天空足球场举办“桃园女孩足球交流赛”，分为国小组及国高中组进行比赛。女孩们在球场上挥洒汗水、齐心协力争取胜利，展现团队合作与竞技精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14610436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14610436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610436-799978.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14610436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610436-799978-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14610436" class="wp-caption-text">妇幼发展局杜慈容局长欢迎各位家长孩子参加2025桃园女孩日足球交流赛。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>赛后由杜局长亲自颁发优胜奖牌及“MVP”、“金手套奖”、“金靴奖”等特别奖项，为中秋佳节增添一份难忘的青春记忆。</p>
<p>妇幼发展局表示，足球体验营与交流赛报名反应热烈，资讯公布2天即额满，显示女孩对运动体验的高度兴趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14610435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14610435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610435-799977.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14610435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14610435-799977-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14610435" class="wp-caption-text">足球交流赛国小组-赛前团队加油打气。（桃园市府妇幼局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>未来将持续推动以“女孩为主体”的政策与支援资源，鼓励女孩在运动场上及各领域中勇于挑战、突破自我，培养自信与多元能力，打造更友善的成长环境。◇</p>
