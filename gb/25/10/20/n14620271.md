<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>预防肌少症、记忆衰退 来碗蒸蛋吧！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14630699-99244a938a86df9ac9d8825e07e2e52d.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr><b>编者按：</b><span style="font-weight:400">熟龄族要</span><span style="font-weight:400">预防肌少症、记忆衰退等健康问题，蛋白质饮食必不可少，</span><span style="font-weight:400">而鸡蛋是蛋白质的上上选。本文教你快速制作</span><span style="font-weight:400">营养鲜</span><span style="font-weight:400">美</span><span style="font-weight:400">、</span><span style="font-weight:400">口感丰富的鸡蛋料理。</span></p><h2><span style="color:#3e7100"><b>蒸蛋料理特色</b></span></h2><p><span style="font-weight:400">．补充优质蛋白质，预防肌少症。</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．容易消化、吸收。</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．质地软，适合各种年龄层，特别是牙口不好的长辈。</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．制备简易，不需要花太多时间。</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．食材多样化、营养更丰富</span><span style="font-weight:400">，如添加秋葵或花椰菜，富含鲜味的菇类等超级食物。</span></p><p><b>⊙材料：</b></p><p><span style="font-weight:400">．生鸡蛋：2颗</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．洋菇：3朵</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．秋葵/花椰菜：秋葵4支或切开的花椰菜 2小朵</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．大虾仁：4尾</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．温开水：180毫升</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．盐巴或酱油：少许</span>
<span style="font-weight:400">．葱花、蒜头：适量（视个人喜好添加）</span></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p><b>⊙作法：</b></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p><span style="font-weight:400">1. 将洋菇、秋葵或花椰菜、虾仁洗净、沥水。</span></p><p><span style="font-weight:400">2. 把洋菇、秋葵切成小片或花椰菜切成小朵，放在碗中备用。</span></p><p><span style="font-weight:400">3. 鸡蛋用筷子搅匀，缓缓倒入温开水，一边倒一边搅拌。</span></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p><span style="font-weight:400">4. 加入盐巴或酱油并拌匀。</span></p><p><span style="font-weight:400">5. 用汤匙捞掉蛋汁上的气泡或用筛子过滤蛋液，添加洋菇、秋葵或花椰菜、虾仁。</span></p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p><span style="font-weight:400">6. 放进电锅，外锅加水，启动开关。或放在瓦斯炉上，加热约15分钟。</span></p><h2><span style="color:#008000"><b>营养小知识</b></span></h2><div><figure style="max-width:419px" style="font-weight:400">肌少症是中老年人健康的隐形杀手。随着年龄的增长或是缺少活动，骨骼肌产生退化性流失或老化，包含骨骼肌的质量和强度都会下降。50岁以上的长者，其骨骼肌每年大约流失0.5～1％。</span></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p><span style="font-weight:400">生物价值高</span><span style="font-weight:400">，人体能很好地吸收与利用。</span><span style="font-weight:400">

</span><b>3. 增强记忆力，维持心血管健康：</b><span style="font-weight:400">蛋黄富含卵磷脂，不仅可以增强记忆力，还有助于代谢胆固醇，维持心血管健康。</span><span style="font-weight:400">
</span><b>4. 提升免疫力：</b>鸡蛋<span style="font-weight:400">内含的锌与微量元素硒能强化免疫系统，帮助身体对抗感染。</span><span style="font-weight:400">
</span><b>5. </b><b>护眼功效强</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight:400">现代人长时间使用电子产品，鸡蛋中有叶黄素与玉米黄质素，有助于过滤有害的蓝光，减少白内障与黄斑部病变的风险。</span></p><p><b>6. 改善睡眠品质：</b><span
