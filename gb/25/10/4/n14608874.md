<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】家用堡垒 2025 Subaru Ascent Limited Bronze Edition</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608894-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_01-600x400.jpg" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月04日讯】作为Subaru旗下最大的三排SUV，2025年Ascent延续去年的车型外，并新增了Bronze Edition版本，以铜色饰件和20英寸铜色轮毂增添辨识度。前脸依旧是Subaru标志性的六角形格栅，搭配锐利的LED头灯和日行灯，整体造型硬朗低调。下方的银色护板强调了冒险气质，契合品牌一贯的户外基因。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://do0q2uqs2ncwe.cloudfront.net/H8up"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/YtGZSrQQhEQ?si=H31gkND_Kq-wu7-S" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>车身侧面线条简洁有力，配备实用的车顶行李架。D柱后倾，增添了一丝动感。尾部造型相对简约，贯穿式尾灯与双边双出排气的搭配，给人一种干净俐落的感觉。后行李箱开口高度略高，装载大型物品时需要稍加注意。整体来看，Ascent的设计更偏向实用和稳重，而非张扬浮夸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608880-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14608880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608880-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608880" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Subaru Ascent Limited。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>进入车内，可以看到Subaru在细节上的取舍更倾向于舒适与便利——这也是不少人所说的“Toyota化”趋势。车内提供多达19个杯架，储物空间丰富，中央扶手箱足够放下一台平板电脑，门板兜能轻松容纳大水瓶。这类贴合家庭需求的小设计，在日常使用上非常实用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608881" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608881-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_38.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14608881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608881-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_38-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608881" class="wp-caption-text">Ascent车内舒适便利。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控部分是11.6英寸垂直触控屏，支援无线CarPlay和Android Auto。操作逻辑清晰，但触控响应速度一般，算是“好用够用”，谈不上惊喜。仪表则仍以传统机械指针为主，配合小尺寸液晶资讯屏，讯息呈现直觉但不算前卫。整体内装用料以软性材质和金属饰条为主，耐用实用，但精致感上不如部分同级车型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608882-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_25.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14608882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608882-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608882" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>空间方面是Ascent的优势。提供7座或8座布局，第二排腿部空间可达38.6英寸，第三排成年人也能应付短途乘坐。行李厢常规容积为17.8立方英尺，放倒第三排可扩展至43.1立方英尺，全部放平后达到75.6立方英尺，装载能力不输同级别主流对手。需要指出的是，第三排座椅较薄，长途舒适性有限。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608883" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608883" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608883-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_46.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14608883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608883-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_46-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608883" class="wp-caption-text">空间方面是Ascent的优势。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>动力总成依旧是2.4升涡轮增压水平对卧四缸发动机，输出260匹马力和277磅英尺扭矩，匹配CVT变速箱，全系标配对称全时四驱。0-60英里／小时加速约7.5秒，日常超车从容。CVT的动力输出线性，但在急加速时会出现“橡皮筋感”，这是这一类变速箱的普遍特征。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608884-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14608884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608884-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608884" class="wp-caption-text">2.4升涡轮增压Boxer发动机。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>油耗方面，根据EPA数据，城市油耗约19 MPG，高速约25 MPG。结合19.3加仑油箱，续航里程接近500英里，适合长途旅行。</p>
<p>得益于低重心的Boxer布局，转向表现稳健，X-Mode越野模式和坡道辅助在湿滑路面或轻度非铺装路面上提供额外信心。底盘调校偏舒适，滤震表现不错，但高速时车身稳定性还有提升空间。整体驾驶风格较强调均衡与安全感，而非激情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608885" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608885" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608885-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14608885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608885-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608885" class="wp-caption-text">20英寸铜色轮毂。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全一直是Subaru的核心卖点。2025年Ascent全系标配EyeSight驾驶辅助系统，涵盖自适应巡航、车道维持、盲点监测、后方交叉警报和自动紧急刹车。今年新增了自动紧急转向和反向自动刹车功能，让主动安全更完整。根据IIHS数据，该车仍保持Top Safety Pick+的评级。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608886-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14608886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608886-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608886" class="wp-caption-text">Ascent的前脸。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在三排SUV市场，2025 Subaru Ascent并不是最豪华、也不是最炫目的选择。但它凭借全时四驱、安全配置和宽敞空间，依然保持着一席之地。与市场上一些更强调豪华感的车型不同，Ascent更注重耐用、实用和家庭便利性，这使它对多孩家庭或经常需要应对恶劣天气的地区尤其有吸引力。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608887-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14608887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14608887-2025_Subaru_Ascent_Ltd_03-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608887" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Subaru Ascent Limited。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
