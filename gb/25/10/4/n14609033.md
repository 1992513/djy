<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亲子共读 桃市图《穿梭绘本 环游世界》国际绘本展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609036-799693-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《穿梭绘本_环游世界》国际绘本展启动仪式。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月04日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市立图书馆10月推出年度儿童主题特展——《穿梭绘本 环游世界》国际绘本展，展期自即日至10月29日于总馆 1 楼享读拾光盛大登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609034" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609034-799691.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609034-799691-600x262.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="262" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609034" class="wp-caption-text">《穿梭绘本_环游世界》国际绘本展大合照。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609044-799701.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609044" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609044-799701-600x367.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609044" class="wp-caption-text">策展人苏懿祯（左）与刘仲成局长（右）解说绘本。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4日上午10时举办开幕典礼暨绘本音乐会，邀请桃园市教育局长刘仲成莅与亲子观众一同参与，以音乐与故事开启一场跨越国界的阅读旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609043-799700.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609043-799700-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609043" class="wp-caption-text">透过故事朗读、亲子手作、专业分享，带领大小读者探索多元文化的阅读风景。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609035-799692.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609035-799692-600x346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609035" class="wp-caption-text">《穿梭绘本_环游世界》国际绘本展，展期自10月2日至10月29日于总馆1楼享读拾光盛大登场。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“The World through Picture Books”计划由国际图书馆协会联盟（IFLA）儿童及青少年组主导，邀请全球图书馆员精选各国代表性绘本。展览由日本上野国立国会图书馆国际儿童图书馆典藏借出，展示来自 42国、360册原文绘本，涵盖欧洲、亚洲、美洲、非洲与大洋洲五大洲，呈现儿童文学的文化多样性与阅读想像力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609037" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609037-799694.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609037-799694-600x265.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="265" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609037" class="wp-caption-text">《穿梭绘本_环游世界》国际绘本展开幕典礼暨绘本音乐会。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>策展苏懿祯表示，展览缘起于环游欧洲在保加利亚时，发现IFLA绘本藏书，进而促成国际绘本展的灵感。即使看不懂文字也没关系，展区充满互动游戏，而每个国家的装帧、图像都是可以观察的地方，邀请大家藉由绘本开启一趟美好的旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609041-799698.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609041-799698-600x298.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609041" class="wp-caption-text">教育局刘仲成局长化身机长，与亲子共读展开跨文化冒险。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609038-799695.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609038-799695-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609038" class="wp-caption-text">本次展览由日本上野国立国会图书馆国际儿童图书馆典藏借出，展示来自42国、360册原文绘本。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>刘仲成局长表示，桃园市立图书馆长期致力于推动阅读扎根，并积极引进国际资源，让市民能透过绘本与世界对话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609045" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609045-799702.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609045-799702-600x332.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609045" class="wp-caption-text">图书馆就是你我最大的书房，鼓励大家多走访图书馆，享受亲子共读时光，共创幸福家庭。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《穿梭绘本 环游世界》不仅是一场展览，更是一个跨文化交流的平台，能让孩子与家庭从中培养国际视野。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609039-799696.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609039-799696-600x332.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609039" class="wp-caption-text">国际图书馆协会联盟（IFLA）儿童及青少年组主导，邀请全球图书馆员精选各国代表性绘本。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阅读是最亲近的国际语言，绘本能带领孩子跨越文字藩篱，展开一段与世界对话的旅程，他强调“图书馆就是你我最大的书房，鼓励大家多走访图书馆，享受亲子共读时光，共创幸福家庭。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609040-799697.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609040-799697-600x351.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609040" class="wp-caption-text">教育局刘仲成局长化身机长，带领民众观看绘本展。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展览期间安排共9场活动，包括4场母语说故事活动（阿拉伯、瑞典、爱尔兰、萨尔瓦多）、4场文化体验研习活动（北非花砖、斯拉夫布偶、波斯文字、拉丁美洲皮纳塔），以及1场专题讲座“从地方到国际──图书馆如何展开跨文化阅读的推广实验”。透过故事朗读、亲子手作、专业分享，带领大小读者探索多元文化的阅读风景。</p>
<p>桃园市立图书馆邀请市民与亲子家庭，把握展期走进图书馆，展开一段从桃园出发、穿越五大洲的阅读旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609042-799699.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609042-799699-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609042" class="wp-caption-text">涵盖欧洲、亚洲、美洲、非洲与大洋洲五大洲，呈现儿童文学的文化多样性与阅读想像力。（桃园市立图书馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展览地点：桃园市立图书馆总馆1楼享读拾光（桃园市桃园区南平路 303 号），展览免费参观；活动需事先报名（名额有限），更多资讯：请关注“桃园市立图书馆”官方网站与脸书粉丝专页ttps://www.typl.gov.tw/zh-tw/Activity/Content/8586。◇</p>
