<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加强北约东翼防御 德国向波兰派遣战斗机</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14617045-GettyImages-2236435237-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025年9月23日，在德国东北部罗斯托克南部拉格军事空军基地举行的新闻日演示中，德国空军的一架欧洲战斗机“台风”正在进行空中加油。 (TOBIAS SCHWARZ/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月16日讯】（大纪元记者王亦笑德国报导）德国政府宣布将向波兰派遣战斗机，以进一步加强北约东翼的空域防御能力。该决定是德国国防部长皮斯托里乌斯在北约国防部长会议期间宣布的，旨在应对近期俄罗斯无人机和战机入侵波兰及爱沙尼亚等国空域的事件。</p>

<h2>斥资百亿欧元助力无人机项目</h2>
<p>除了战斗机部署，皮斯托里乌斯还宣布，德国政府计划在未来几年投资<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">100</span>亿欧元用于各类无人机的研发和采购，包括攻击型无人机。他表示，俄罗斯的侵略行为表明，北约必须持续增强防御能力，不能有丝毫懈怠。</p>
<p>北约秘书长马克<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">·</span>吕特（<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">Mark Rutte</span>）在会议上指出，北约已具备在紧急情况下保卫整个联盟领土的能力。他提到，近期俄罗斯米格<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">-31</span>战机入侵爱沙尼亚空域的事件并未构成直接威胁，但北约需要通过技术升级，特别是改进无人机防御系统，以更高效地应对低成本无人机的威胁，而非依赖昂贵的防空导弹。</p>
<p>吕特还表示，北约目前已允许在必要时击落被视为威胁的敌方战机，但强调不主张将击落作为常规操作。北约欧洲盟军最高司令、美国将军格林克维奇（<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">Alexus Grynkewich</span>）也呼吁成员国尽量放宽对参与北约任务的限制，以提升作战灵活性。</p>
<p>会上，北约成员国还讨论了在俄罗斯边境附近加强军事存在的可能性，以牵制俄罗斯在乌克兰战场的军事行动。英国国防部长希利（<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">John Healey</span>）表示：<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">“</span>我们必须以强大的力量应对这一升级态势。<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">”</span></p>
<h2>承诺<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">20</span>亿欧元援助乌克兰军事</h2>

<p>皮斯托里乌斯强调：<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">“</span>乌克兰战场的最新动态要求我们坚定继续支持乌克兰的决心。<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">”</span>他指出，俄罗斯在战场上的失败正试图通过攻击乌克兰城市来掩盖，而乌克兰军队已显着减缓了俄军的推进势头。据估计，自<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">2025</span>年初以来，俄军伤亡人数已达<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">30</span>万。</p>
<p>德国通过向波兰派遣<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">“</span>台风<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-feature-settings: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Times;">”</span>战斗机、投资无人机项目以及对乌克兰的大规模军事援助，展现了其在北约框架内加强东翼防御和支持盟友的决心。北约成员国在面对俄罗斯的持续威胁时，正通过加强空域巡逻、优化技术手段和协调军事援助，展现团结与应对能力。</p>
