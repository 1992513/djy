<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2025双十国庆 南故宫水舞与无人机秀</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614570-801433-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“那个瞬间真的震撼，我一度放下手机，只想用眼睛看。”沈台兴笑说。（沈台兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾嘉义报导）十月十一日的傍晚，嘉义南故宫湖畔，天边染着晚霞。已退休、曾任台湾科技业副总的沈台兴，从云林元长开车半小时特地前来。他说自己原本只是想看看南故宫的水舞秀，没想到一到现场，夕阳、灯光、水面反射交织成画，成了他镜头里最难忘的一夜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614554-801416.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614554-801416-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614554" class="wp-caption-text">10月11日晚上七点半水舞秀登场前，天空染上粉紫与橘红色调，南故宫湖畔灯光倒映水面，游客纷纷驻足等待表演开始。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614555" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614555-801417.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14614555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614555-801417-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614555" class="wp-caption-text">水舞秀正式启动，南故宫湖面光束交错，喷泉随节奏起伏，点亮天空，展现科技与艺术融合之美。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614559" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614559-801421.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14614559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614559-801421-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614559" class="wp-caption-text">以强烈蓝光雷射划破黑夜，水花反射如星河闪耀，现场气氛震撼人心。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>傍晚的等待　天空先开场太阳下山前的南故宫，有着不同于白天的静谧。沈台兴站在湖边，看着天际慢慢变成橙红色，钢构桥的轮廓在光里显得格外清晰。“那时候风很柔，水面反光像一层金粉。”他边说边举起手机，拍下那几乎静止的一刻。对他来说，这样的画面比任何高阶设备的光效都更打动人。“我在科技业待了一辈子，但这一刻我觉得最美的科技，是能让人静下来欣赏自然的节奏。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614560" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614560-801422.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614560-801422-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614560" class="wp-caption-text">湖畔灯光映照建筑剪影，水面泛着金色光晕，为即将展开的水舞秀铺陈浪漫氛围。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614557-801419.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614557-801419-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614557" class="wp-caption-text">紫光、绿光、红光与水柱交织成梦幻画面，天际无人机同步闪动，为国庆增添壮丽气势。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>七点半准时启动　光与水的对话晚上七点半，音乐响起，南故宫湖面瞬间被雷射光与水柱点亮。蓝光、绿光、红光交错，巨大的“光之眼”装置在湖中央亮起，水雾化作萤幕，投影出国庆的主题影像。“那个瞬间真的震撼，我一度放下手机，只想用眼睛看。”沈台兴笑说。他形容那场表演像是一场“水与光的对话”，每一道光线都像有节奏的语言，随着音乐呼吸。当红色光束照亮全场，象征国庆的喜悦，现场民众拍手欢呼，甚至有人高举手机跟着录影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614569-801432.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614569-801432-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614569" class="wp-caption-text">音乐响起，南故宫湖面瞬间被雷射光与水柱点亮。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>科技与艺术的融合沈台兴过去长期在科技产业工作，他特别注意到水舞与无人机的结合。“这不是单纯的娱乐，而是一种科技展示。”他指着夜空中闪烁的无人机说：“这些飞行器能在夜空排出队形、形成图案，靠的是定位与光控精准的算法。”语气里带着工程师的习惯，但多了几分欣赏。对他来说，科技若能被转化为艺术，才是真正贴近生活的进步。“这样的国庆活动，不是让人看数据，而是看感动。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614564" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614564-801427.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14614564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614564-801427-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614564" class="wp-caption-text">无人机在空中组成葫芦造型，与下方的水舞同时登场，寓意“福禄安康”，国庆夜色更添吉祥喜气。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614565" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614565-801428.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14614565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614565-801428-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614565" class="wp-caption-text">无人机变幻不同造型，与地面水舞遥相呼应，科技与艺术在同一时间绽放。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水面倒影的余韵演出进行到中段时，光之眼变成一圈深蓝，湖面反射出一个更完整的圆。雷射光从中心射出，水花随音乐节奏跳动。“那一幕像是在看宇宙的呼吸，”沈台兴说，“我想到自己工作时，常在实验室看波纹的数据图，但今晚的波纹，是活的。”他特别提到现场的音响震撼却不刺耳，节奏掌握得恰到好处，“那种整体感，让人觉得自己也被包在光里。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614558" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614558-801420.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14614558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614558-801420-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614558" class="wp-caption-text">水舞间隙，主舞台光环化作纯蓝色光圈，倒映于平静湖面，象征“科技之眼”守望南故宫的夜。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614563" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614563-801425.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614563-801425-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614563" class="wp-caption-text">蓝色“眼之舞台”倒映在湖面上，静谧中蕴藏待放的能量。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无人机升空　夜空的祝福表演尾声，无人机缓缓升空，点点白光排列成国庆图案，在云层间闪烁。底下的水舞渐渐收敛成柔光，整个湖面像镜子一样映出“中”字与红白光圈。“那时候很多人都静下来，没有人说话。”沈台兴形容，那一刻的安静比任何掌声都深刻。“那不是炫技，而是一种尊重。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614562" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614562-801424.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14614562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614562-801424-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614562" class="wp-caption-text">以红色主题灯光压轴登场，象征庆祝双十国庆的热情与祝福，光之眼与拱桥相互辉映。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614561-801423.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614561-801423-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614561" class="wp-caption-text">南故宫湖岸灯火与残霞交织成金红色调，倒映出节庆的温度与期待。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614566" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614566-801429.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14614566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614566-801429-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614566" class="wp-caption-text">蓝色水舞跃起，无人机在空中排出南鲲鯓代天府象征的神舟图案，气势磅礡，向国家祝寿。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>科技人的感动活动结束后，沈台兴站在湖边，看着人潮慢慢散去。他说自己退休后常拍照，但这一晚特别有意义：“我觉得这是一种新的国庆方式，用科技去说故事，也让人重新感受到节日的精神。”他笑着补充：“元长到南故宫开车半小时不远，但这半小时换到的，是一夜的感动。”从日落到夜幕，从静谧到绚烂，南故宫的水舞与无人机秀不只是视觉演出，也是一场科技与情感交织的表现。沈台兴的镜头，记录的不只是画面，更是一位科技人对光、影与时间的再一次理解。</p>
