<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>玛家凉山部落的凝聚力 国庆日舞出团结的光彩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614656-801438-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>玛家乡凉山社区2025年欢度双十升旗。（周永田提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）十月的南国阳光和煦，屏东玛家乡凉山部落的校园操场上，传来熟悉的歌曲节奏与金色拉花的闪光。这不是城市里的大型庆典，而是一场由小部落自发筹办的国庆活动。虽然场地简朴，气氛却格外热烈。近年来，地方自主举办国庆活动的风气已不多见，多数人习惯在电视上看烟火、在社群里按赞。但在玛家乡这个部落，族人们用最直接的方式，把节日过得踏实而真诚。没有华丽舞台、没有特效灯光，却有满场的笑声与鼓掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614671-801458.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614671-801458-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614671" class="wp-caption-text">许多山区部落的活动逐渐减少，热闹的节庆成了回忆。但凉山人选择留下、选择一起动起来。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614662-801449.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614662-801449-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614662" class="wp-caption-text">队伍穿过凉山社区彩绘巷弄，沿路旗帜飘扬、歌声响起，节庆氛围热烈。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614661-801448.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614661-801448-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614661" class="wp-caption-text">以橘帽与白衬衫复古造型参加游行，手挥国旗步入山城街道，成为庆典中亮眼的一幕。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614675" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614675" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614675-801462.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614675-801462-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614675" class="wp-caption-text">神情专注，像是在告诉后辈，只要愿意动，生命就永远在跳舞。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从银发到童心　屏东玛家一场全龄共乐的国庆日活动最吸睛的，是由部落长者组成的健康操表演队。黑色上衣、彩色裙?、头戴红黄绿花圈的他们，手里挥舞着金色拉花，随着音乐有节奏地摆动。每个动作不求完美，却都充满认真与活力。舞台下的观众有年轻人、有小孩，甚至有刚从外地返乡探亲的青年，也都被这一幕吸引。许多人拿起手机拍下影片，笑着说：“这才是我们自己的节日。”其中，一位超过八十岁的长者坐在椅子上跟着挥舞拉花，动作虽缓慢，但神情专注，像是在告诉后辈，只要愿意动，生命就永远在跳舞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614660" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614660-801447.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614660-801447-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614660" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友比出胜利手势，单纯的笑容为整场活动增添无限朝气。 （周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614658-801445.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614658-801445-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614658" class="wp-caption-text">年轻女孩参与社区游行活动，神情专注投入，展现新世代对家乡的热情与责任感。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614677" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614677-801463.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614677-801463-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614677" class="wp-caption-text">屏东玛家一场全龄共乐的国庆日。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>没有补助的庆典　用双手撑起一份自豪这场活动没有外来补助，也不是由公部门主导，而是由部落耆老、妇女会、青年会共同筹办。有人准备道具、有人布置场地、有人煮餐食，所有人都自愿付出。“我们只是希望国庆这天，部落也能热闹一下。”筹办成员笑着说。看似一句平凡的话，却藏着深厚的社区力量。在少子化与人口外移的压力下，许多山区部落的活动逐渐减少，热闹的节庆成了回忆。但凉山人选择留下、选择一起动起来，用实际行动维系文化与情感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614664-801451.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614664-801451-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614664" class="wp-caption-text">全家一起出动，参与这项特别的庆典。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614663-801450.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614663-801450-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614663" class="wp-caption-text">排湾族妇女脸贴“I LOVE TAIWAN”贴纸，胸前挂满串珠，以自信步伐参与游行。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614666-801453.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614666-801453-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614666" class="wp-caption-text">青年学子与孩童们并肩走在队伍中，身挂传统串珠，象征文化传承与活力并存。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614674" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614674" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614674-801461.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614674" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614674-801461-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614674" class="wp-caption-text">有人准备道具、有人布置场地、有人煮餐食，所有人都自愿付出。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>舞动的不只是健康 ?更是世代的传承表演结束后，长者们围坐在场边歇息，谈笑声此起彼落。孩子们跑上前，模仿着刚才的舞步，长辈看了直笑。这一幕，成了最动人的画面。“我们年轻的时候，国庆一定要升旗、唱国歌、跳舞。现在年纪大了，能再一次动起来，心里很开心。”一位长者缓缓说着。她的话里没有豪言壮语，却有一份对土地与传统的深情。在这里，舞蹈不只是表演，更像一种部落语言。每一次举手、每一次微笑，都在传递“我们还在这里，我们还愿意一起努力”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14614659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614659-801446.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614659-801446-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614659" class="wp-caption-text">妇女头绑红巾、胸前佩珠，头顶国旗饰帽，笑容洋溢在阳光下，象征族群与国家的融合。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614665-801452.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14614665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614665-801452-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614665" class="wp-caption-text">在游行中高唱歌曲，脸上挂着真诚笑容，传递节庆的欢乐与感动。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14614670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14614670" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614670-801457.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14614670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14614670-801457-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14614670" class="wp-caption-text">“我们年轻的时候，国庆一定要升旗、唱国歌、跳舞。现在年纪大了，能再一次动起来，心里很开心。”一位长者缓缓说着。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小部落，大能量活动的尾声，天空透出午后的光。操场中央的族人手牵手，高唱一连串的歌谣。那歌声没有技巧，也没有排练过多的整齐，却带着真诚与温度。这场小小的国庆活动，也许不会上新闻，但它让人看到最珍贵的力量。当一群人愿意聚在一起、愿意为彼此而动，哪怕只是短短一个上午，那份团结与快乐，已是最真实的国庆礼赞。在这个看似偏远的部落里，凉山人用自己的方式，安静却坚定地说出了心里的话：“我们爱这块土地，也爱一起生活的日子。”</p>
