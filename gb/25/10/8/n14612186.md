<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>呼叫音乐节秋天登场 田馥甄等歌手、乐团齐聚</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612201-2510080942121487.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
