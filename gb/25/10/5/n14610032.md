<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿双十国庆招待会 三州议长称赞台湾自由</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609920-DSC02070-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>10月3日晚上，来自新英格兰地区各州的政要嘉宾切蛋糕，庆祝中华民国台湾双十国庆。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）10月3日晚上，新英格兰地区三个州的议长出席驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处“双十国庆”招待会，与其他数百嘉宾同庆佳节，并赞赏台湾在民主自由方面的成就。</p>
<p>当晚在波士顿洲际酒店（InterContinental Boston），来自新英格兰地区各州的政商名流、侨社领袖、外国领事、留学生团体等各界人士欢聚一堂，迎接10月10日即将到来的中华民国（台湾）114年国庆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609921-DSC02076.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609921-DSC02076-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609921" class="wp-caption-text">10月3日晚上，驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处举办“双十国庆”招待会。图为处长廖朝宏（左）及夫人。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609914-DSC02015.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609914-DSC02015-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609914" class="wp-caption-text">诸多嘉宾参加“双十国庆”招待会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609915-DSC02019.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609915-DSC02019-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609915" class="wp-caption-text">诸多嘉宾参加“双十国庆”招待会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>坚持民主自由价值 台湾与美国各州建立友谊</strong></h3>
<p>新罕布什尔州众议院议长帕卡德（Sherman Packard）、麻萨诸塞州参议院代议长布朗斯伯格（William Brownsberger）、罗德岛州众议院代议长肯尼迪（Brian Patrick Kennedy）都携所在州的其他议员出席晚会，称赞台湾的发展成就，并参与切蛋糕庆生典礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609924-DSC02090.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609924-DSC02090-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609924" class="wp-caption-text">左起：新罕布什尔州众议院职员Anastasia M. Childs、议长Sherman Packard、经文处处长廖朝宏、廖夫人。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>帕卡德议长说，台湾和新罕布什尔州因为文化交流、教育合作及共同的民主价值，而持续强化联系。他祝福说：“真正的中国（中华民国），台湾这个岛屿，以及那里的杰出人民万岁。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609925-DSC02102.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609925-DSC02102-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609925" class="wp-caption-text">美国联邦及新英格兰各州政府颁赠褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布朗斯伯格代议长说，他特别敬佩台湾在冲突和压力面前捍卫自由理念的决心。他也感谢台湾裔美国人对麻州乃至整个美国的贡献。</p>
<p>肯尼迪代议长谈到，他去年曾访问台湾，旅途非常愉快。他还说，罗德岛众议院、参议院各位议员，以及州内居民，都非常珍视与台湾的友谊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609917-DSC02042.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609917-DSC02042-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609917" class="wp-caption-text">麻萨诸塞州参议院代议长William Brownsberger称赞台湾捍卫自由的决心。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609918-DSC02046.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609918-DSC02046-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609918" class="wp-caption-text">罗德岛州众议院代议长Brian Patrick Kennedy回顾访问台湾的经历。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609926-IMG_9222.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609926-IMG_9222-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609926" class="wp-caption-text">罗德岛州众议院代议长Brian Patrick Kennedy与军校学生们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>庆祝台湾成就 感谢各界支持</strong></h3>
<p>国庆招待会开始，佛蒙特州军事院校诺维奇大学（Norwich University）的学生持美国和中华民国国旗进场。两名勒星顿中文学校的学生吴亚桐和陈希言领唱两国国歌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609911" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609911-DSC02005.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609911-DSC02005-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609911" class="wp-caption-text">主持人宣布晚会开始。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609912-DSC02006.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609912-DSC02006-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609912" class="wp-caption-text">佛蒙特州军事院校诺维奇大学（Norwich University）的学生护卫国旗进场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609913" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609913-DSC02008.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609913-DSC02008-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609913" class="wp-caption-text">两名勒星顿中文学校的学生吴亚桐和陈希言领唱国歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏欢迎来宾，回顾台湾取得的成就，感谢美国和各州政要、侨胞、外国领事及社会各界对台湾的支持。</p>
<p>廖朝宏说，世界正在经历剧变，民主自由受到挑战，而台湾正直面这种挑战。在此期间，台湾与各民主国家的关系日渐紧密。去年，台湾是美国的第7大贸易伙伴，美国是台湾的第2大贸易伙伴。而且美国正成为台湾企业，比如台积电的首选投资地点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609916-DSC02031.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609916-DSC02031-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609916" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609923-DSC02087.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609923-DSC02087-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609923" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿侨社领袖参加晚会。左起：中华公所主席雷国辉、中华总会会长余丽媖、侨务委员陈仕维。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“美国与台湾的贸易，比美国和英国、法国甚至印度都多。”廖朝宏说。</p>
<p>与此同时，很多由台湾人创办的企业都青睐新英格兰地区。今年，波士顿经文处接待了一个台湾企业代表团，包括20多家高科技企业的代表。台湾人也在文化方面扩展影响，比如波士顿台湾影展协会每年举办，向越来越多的观众展示台湾电影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609919" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609919-DSC02063.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609919-DSC02063-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609919" class="wp-caption-text">来自新英格兰地区各州的政要嘉宾切蛋糕，庆祝中华民国台湾双十国庆。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609922-DSC02082.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609922-DSC02082-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609922" class="wp-caption-text">台湾年轻人演唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉宾们致词之后，还共同切开“双十”蛋糕，在庆祝生日的乐曲中，向中华民国台湾表达祝福。◇</p>
