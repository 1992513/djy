<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>拌酱让蔬菜更美味 大厨教你做“凉拌酱汁”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14260841-shutterstock_1782195797-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>秋葵。(Shutterstock)</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">和食小菜的基本款，利用丰富的变化，让蔬菜更美味。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>拌菜</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14609587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609587" style="width: 355px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609587-P.116-1-e1759654033560.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609587-P.116-1-e1759654033560.jpg" alt="" width="355" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609587" class="wp-caption-text">拌菜。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>基本做法</b></p>
<p><b>材料（2人份）：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">秋葵 10根</span></p>
<p>〈拌酱〉*</p>
<p><b>做法：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.锅子加水煮到滚，再放入洗净的秋葵汆烫，捞出、沥干。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.将秋葵对半斜切、放进碗里，加入〈拌酱〉拌匀。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>拌酱</b></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1. 基本的芝麻拌菜</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于果菜、叶菜类等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609586" style="width: 320px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609586-P.116-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609586-P.116-2.jpg" alt="" width="320" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609586" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>材料：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拌酱【白芝麻粉 2大匙</span>，<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖、水 各1大匙，酱油1.5大匙】</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">将所有材料放进碗里拌匀，再加入配菜拌匀即可。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2. 芝麻味噌拌菜</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于鸡肉、叶菜类等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609585" style="width: 280px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609585-P.116-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609585-P.116-3.jpg" alt="" width="280" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609585" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拌酱【白芝麻粉2大匙，味噌2小匙，味醂2大匙，芝麻油1小匙，辣油少量】</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3. 芝麻美乃滋拌菜</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于叶菜类等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609584" style="width: 303px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609584-P.116-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609584-P.116-4-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="303" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609584" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拌酱【白芝麻粉2大匙，美乃滋1大匙，酱油2小匙、砂糖1小匙】</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4. 韩式辣酱拌菜</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于鱼贝类、豆类等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609583" style="width: 309px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609583-P.116-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609583-P.116-5-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="309" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609583" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拌酱【韩式辣酱2小匙，白芝麻粉、酱油、芝麻油各1小匙】</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>5. 美乃滋白酱拌菜</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于鱼贝类、叶菜类等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609582" style="width: 308px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609582-P.116-6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609582-P.116-6-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="308" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609582" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拌酱【嫩豆腐 1/2块，美乃滋 2大匙，白芝麻粉 1/2大匙，砂糖 1/2小匙，盐 1/2小匙】</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">用厨房纸巾包裹豆腐、吸掉多余水分，所有材料放进碗里，一边压碎豆腐、一边搅拌均匀，加入配菜拌匀即可。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>6. 凉拌</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于豆类等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609581" style="width: 305px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609581-P.116-7.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609581-P.116-7-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="305" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609581" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拌酱【蒜泥 1/4小匙，盐1/4小匙，芝麻油 1/2小匙，砂糖少量】</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在放了配菜的碗里加入拌酱拌匀即可。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>7. 醋味凉拌</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于根茎类等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609579" style="width: 309px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609579-P.116-8.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609579-P.116-8-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="309" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609579" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拌酱【柠檬汁1大匙，醋3大匙，盐1/2小匙，白芝麻粉适量】</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>8. 芥末酱凉拌</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14609580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609580" style="width: 312px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609580-P.116-9.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609580-P.116-9-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="312" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609580" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拌酱【白芝麻粉、颗粒芥末酱、酱油、芝麻油各1小匙】</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在放了配菜的碗里加入拌酱拌匀即可。◇</span></p>
