<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>锁住鲜甜滋味！零油烟的纸包烧料理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609855-P.9-1-e1759671067183-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纸包烧。（台湾广厦提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><b>编者按：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">纸包烧，居家就能轻松作出鲜嫩多汁、风味浓郁的料理。它不需要复杂的器具或技巧，可以保留食材原味与鲜甜，还能达到零油烟的效果。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">就在打开铝箔纸的瞬间，立刻闻到四处飘散的幸福香气。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>纸包烧</b></span></h3>
<p><b>基本作法：</b></p>
<p><strong>材料（2人份）：</strong></p>
<p>虾…2只<br />
蛤蜊…2个<br />
扇贝柱…2个<br />
鸿喜菇…1/8包（15g）<br />
花枝…1杯<br />
大蒜…2瓣<br />
盐、胡椒粉…各适量<br />
沙拉油…适量<br />
〈烧烤酱料〉*<br />
〈蘸料〉**</p>
<p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
<p>1.虾去肠泥，洗净后拭去水分，撒上适量盐。花枝去膜，切成适口大小，撒上适量盐。扇贝柱撒上适量盐。把鸿喜菇根部去除，蛤蜊吐沙后洗净。</p>
<p>2.摊开铝箔纸，在中央涂上适量的沙拉油，将所有食材放在上面，淋上〈烧烤酱料〉，撒适量胡椒粉后包起来。</p>
<p>3.放入以200℃预热好的烤箱中，烘烤10～15分钟，倒入〈蘸料〉即完成。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>〈烧烤酱料〉*</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.基本的纸包烧：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于白肉鱼</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609854" style="width: 304px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609854-P.9-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609854-P.9-2-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="304" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609854" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>材料：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油、奶油各1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">白酒2大匙</span></p>
<p><b>做法：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油、白酒和奶油混合，加入基本作法2中，淋在蔬菜和菇类上面。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.芝麻味噌烧：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于白肉鱼</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609853" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609853-P.9-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14609853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609853-P.9-3-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609853" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">味噌2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">高汤或水2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">白芝麻粉1小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐、沙拉油各少量</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">材料拌匀后，在基本作法2中加入。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.柠檬盐烧：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于鸡肉等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609852" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609852-P.9-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14609852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609852-P.9-4-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609852" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">葱末1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">柠檬汁1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">蒜泥、芝麻油各1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐1小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">粗粒黑胡椒粉 适量</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>〈蘸料〉**</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.梅酱：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于白肉鱼、鱼贝类等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609851" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609851-P.9-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14609851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609851-P.9-5-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609851" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">梅肉1个</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">高汤1/3杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油、醋</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">各1小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">太白粉水2小匙（太白粉1：水1）</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">梅肉放入小锅，加入高汤、酱油、醋拌匀。开小火待沸腾后加入太白粉水，汤汁变黏稠则熄火，在基本作法3中加入。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.柠檬酱油酱：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于鸡肉、白肉鱼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609850" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609850-P.9-6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14609850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609850-P.9-6-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609850" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">高汤、柠檬汁各2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酱油1小匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">材料拌匀后，在基本作法3中添加。</span></p>
