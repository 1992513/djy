<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在家自制炸物沾酱 吃出不同层次口感</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609626-30dc26b0f61f07296d8c578efe0ee719-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>炸物。（台湾广厦提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按︰</strong></p>
<p>常见的炸物沾酱包含塔塔酱、卡士达酱、番茄酱、优格酱、酸奶油酱、和风酱及中式酱料等，多种风味的沾酱可提供不同层次口感，而且也有解腻效果，能让炸物更好吃。以下大厨介绍8种在家就可以自制的沾酱，大家可以动手做做看。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><b>炸物的沾料</b></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14609624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609624" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609624-P.55-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14609624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609624-P.55-1-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609624" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.简易酱料：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于可乐饼、炸肉饼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609625" style="width: 275px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609625-P.55-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609625-P.55-2-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="275" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609625" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>材料：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">辣酱油、红酒、番茄酱各2大匙</span></p>
<p><b>做法：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">材料放进锅里搅拌均匀，迅速煮滚。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.起司塔塔酱：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于西式油炸饼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609623" style="width: 304px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609623-P.55-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609623-P.55-3-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="304" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609623" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">全熟水煮蛋碎末…1个</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">茅屋起司…50g</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">美乃滋…4大匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">所有材料快速拌匀。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.卡士达酱：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于西式油炸饼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609622" style="width: 309px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609622-P.55-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609622-P.55-4-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="309" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609622" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">水 1/2杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">柠檬汁 2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖 3大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">卡士达粉1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐 少量</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">材料放进锅里搅拌均匀，迅速煮滚即可。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.番茄酱：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于可乐饼、炸肉饼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609621" style="width: 273px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609621-P.55-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609621-P.55-5-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="273" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609621" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">水煮番茄（罐装）1罐（400g）</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">洋葱碎末 1/4个</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">月桂叶 1小片</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">大蒜 1瓣</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">橄榄油 2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐 1/2小匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">胡椒粉 适量</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖 1/2小匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">把大蒜、月桂叶浸在橄榄油，闻到香料味道后加进洋葱，用大火爆香，待所有材料炒软后转中火再炒，水煮番茄放进锅里，边压碎边煮。加入盐、胡椒粉、砂糖调味，挑出大蒜煮到汤汁收干。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>5.优格酱：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于可乐饼、炸肉饼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609619" style="width: 239px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609619-P.55-6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609619-P.55-6-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="239" b="239" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609619" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">优格 4大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酸奶油、番茄酱各1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">辣酱油、盐、胡椒粉各少量</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">把盐、胡椒粉以外的材料放进耐热容器里，送进微波炉（500W）加热约1分钟，取出，用盐、胡椒粉调味。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>6.酸奶油酱：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于西式油炸饼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609618" style="width: 304px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609618-P.55-7.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609618-P.55-7-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="304" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609618" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">酸奶油 1/3杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">洋葱泥 1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">细葱（切末）1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐、胡椒粉各少量</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">酸奶油置于室温软化，和其他材料充分拌匀。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>7.中式酱料：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于西式油炸饼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609617" style="width: 307px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609617-P.55-8.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609617-P.55-8-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="307" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609617" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">葱末 1/2支</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">姜末、蒜末各1/2瓣</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖 1.5大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">酒、醋、番茄酱各1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">豆瓣酱、芝麻油各1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">辣油少量</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">葱、姜、大蒜和适量沙拉油（分量外）一起爆香，再和其他材料拌匀。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>8.和风梅酱：</b></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">适用于西式油炸饼等</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14609616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609616" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609616-P.55-9.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609616-P.55-9-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609616" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">梅干大型2个</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">面味露（2倍浓缩）3大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">醋、沙拉油各1大匙</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">所有材料拌匀即完成。◇</span></p>
