<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>摄影机与绳索之间的距离 探三貂岭合谷瀑布</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609639-799879-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>队员稳稳抓绳而下，体验“迎水而行”的极限挑战，与瀑布融为一体。（陈锦尧提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月05日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬新北市报导）“每一步都得慎重，因为水下的石头滑得像玻璃。”陈锦尧说这句话时，太阳正从山缝里探出来，洒在他湿透的防水衣上。这位以空拍见长的摄影家，近年也投入极限登山与溪降的世界，让镜头不只停留在空中，而是与山壁、水流一起呼吸。这次他带回的影像，拍摄地点位于新北市三貂岭合谷瀑布，一条不易抵达的瀑布群。要进入这里，得沿着蜿蜒的林道走上近两小时，再背着装备下切到溪谷，攀绳、横越、垂降，一步都不能出错。“摄影不只是拍，而是用身体去经历风景。”他笑着说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609637" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609637-799877.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609637-799877-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609637" class="wp-caption-text">三貂岭合谷瀑布如银练倾泻，水汽在岩壁间化为细雾，映照出山林间最清透的光。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609638" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609638-799878.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609638-799878-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609638" class="wp-caption-text">专业溪降教练与队员在瀑顶架设绳索，检查装备，准备迎接垂降挑战，红绳在岩壁间画出安全的轨迹。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609640" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609640-799880.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609640-799880-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609640" class="wp-caption-text">完成垂降的队员在谷底相互支援，绳索延伸至水面，映出高低错落的岩层与潺潺水光，气氛紧凑而和谐。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大山的尺度那道高耸的瀑布自岩壁倾泻而下，水势如白练般拍击石面。陈锦尧说，这不是观光客能轻易到达的地方。上瀑约二十公尺，下瀑再接十公尺，落差与水流压力都让人无法轻忽。“一开始看觉得很壮观，但等到真的站在边缘要下去时，心跳会比水流还快。”他用绳索一点一点下降，脚边的水溅起、手机的镜头被雾气打湿。拍摄的时候，他常得一手握绳、一手操作快门。“这些照片不是美化自然，而是让人看到自然的真实力量。”在他的镜头里，光线穿透雾气，映出绿与白的层次；远景里的队员们成了微小的身影，更衬出山谷的辽阔与震撼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609641" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609641-799881.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609641-799881-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609641" class="wp-caption-text">队员再次系上安全绳索，准备第二段下降。远处山谷静静流淌的溪水，见证人与自然的互信与勇气。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609646" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609646-799886.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609646-799886-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609646" class="wp-caption-text">队员仰望瀑顶，准备迎接垂降的刺激旅程。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609647-799887.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609647-799887-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609647" class="wp-caption-text">水势湍急处，成员互相协助确认绳结安全。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>攀降与飞行陈锦尧本身是登百岳的常客，也是无人机摄影的高手。这次，他同时用绳索与手机、空拍机捕捉整个下降的过程。“空拍能记录整体结构，地面摄影则能感受压力与湿度。两者加起来，才是完整的山林。”他回忆那天的情景，天空蓝得刺眼，风却不大，是最适合飞无人机的天气。“我让机子停在瀑布正前方，刚好能拍到人与水的比例。那画面很小，但也最能显出人与自然的对话。”画面里的红色绳索，是安全线，也是人类与自然连结的线条。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14609648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609648" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609648-799888.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609648-799888-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609648" class="wp-caption-text">教练与队员沟通路线，细节决定安全与成败。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609649-799889.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14609649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609649-799889-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609649" class="wp-caption-text">攀溪队员顺流而行，在岩石间探索溪谷原始之美。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14609643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14609643" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609643-799883.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14609643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14609643-799883-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14609643" class="wp-caption-text">迎面而来的水势，考验着每一步的稳定与勇气。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>山难之外的敬畏近年登山与溪降活动兴盛，意外事件也时有所闻。陈锦尧坦言，这样的活动确实有风险。“山不是给人征服的，而是要学会尊重它。”他说，出发前的路线规划、天气评估、安全绳确认，每一环都不能省略。“再好的体力、再贵的装备，都不如一颗谦卑的心。”他说，许多人看到照片会觉得刺激，但实际上，更多的是安静与专注。当水声在耳边回荡、绳索紧绷着身体，所有杂念都被山谷收走，只剩下“此刻”两个字。</p>
