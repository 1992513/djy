<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：巴黎大胜领跑 英超三强携手凯旋而归</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14621159-GettyImages-2242433840.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
