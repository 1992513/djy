<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“云和泠泠”古筝演出融合古今音乐之美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624711-DSC03773-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿古筝乐团10月25日举办年度演出。参与演出的音乐家们向观众致谢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月28日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨勒星顿镇报导）以威武的鼓令震撼开场，随后古筝乐团带来悦耳的音乐，还融入大提琴元素，奏出东西合璧的乐章。这是波士顿古筝乐团10月25日的年度演出，也得到水底鱼打击乐团的热情助力。</p>
<p>这场音乐会在麻州勒星顿镇（Lexington）的苏格兰共济会博物馆图书馆（Scottish Rite Masonic Museum &amp; Library）举办，约有100观众到场欣赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14624703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624703-DSC03713.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624703-DSC03713-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624703" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿古筝乐团10月25日举办年度演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14624709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624709-DSC03761.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624709-DSC03761-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624709" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿古筝乐团得到观众的热烈掌声。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古筝乐团杨信宜老师，以及她的学生胡凯楹、胡巧颖、李泽恩、潘洁裬，总共演绎了10首乐曲，包括传统筝曲如《景春萝》，地方民歌如《拔根芦柴花》，现代歌曲如《但愿人长久》，还有携手大提琴手彭亦勤共同演奏的《蝴蝶与蓝》等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14624701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624701-DSC03704.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624701-DSC03704-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624701" class="wp-caption-text">杨信宜老师带领学生胡凯楹、胡巧颖、李泽恩、潘洁裬演奏乐曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14624702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624702-DSC03708.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624702-DSC03708-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624702" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿古筝乐团的杨信宜老师演奏乐曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水底鱼打击乐团的陈志新老师也带领学生们加盟演出，开场表演鼓乐《香港活力鼓令廿四式：勇冠三军》，在下半场开场演奏《安徽花鼓灯》，皆是动感十足、热情奔放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14624700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624700" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624700-DSC03699.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624700-DSC03699-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624700" class="wp-caption-text">水底鱼打击乐团的陈志新老师带领学生们加盟演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14624705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624705-DSC03728.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624705-DSC03728-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624705" class="wp-caption-text">古筝演奏家携手大提琴手彭亦勤共同演奏的《蝴蝶与蓝》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演出结束，杨信宜捧着观众奉上的鲜花，高兴地说，很多新、老朋友来支持这场演出，给了很好的反馈。乐团将来也会继续努力，为大家带来优秀的音乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14624710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624710-DSC03769.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624710-DSC03769-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624710" class="wp-caption-text">陈志新（左）和杨信宜在演出之后合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前担任文协国乐社社长的杨信宜，其夫陈志新也表示，舞台上几代学生共同演出，舞台下有长期支持古筝乐团的老观众，也有很多新面孔；希望大家未来能继续支持这个乐团。</p>
<p>关于《云和泠泠》的音乐会主题，杨信宜解释说，“云和”是古代人对古筝的别称，“泠泠”形容水的声音，表示古筝的音乐就像流水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14624699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624699-DSC03684.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624699-DSC03684-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624699" class="wp-caption-text">参加演出的音乐家们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿古筝乐团已有20多年的历史，获得过诸多殊荣。杨信宜说，她希望乐团扎根传统，所以每次音乐会都会挑选一些传统曲目。同时，她也采用西方乐团的重奏方式展现古筝音乐，让学生学习以西方的技法来展现中国传统音乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14624707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624707-DSC03755.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624707-DSC03755-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624707" class="wp-caption-text">当晚的音乐会遭遇意外，设备故障导致火灾警报响起，引来消防队。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14624706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624706-DSC03746.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624706-DSC03746-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624706" class="wp-caption-text">火灾警报响起，大家依据规定移步室外。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14624708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14624708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624708-DSC03759.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14624708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14624708-DSC03759-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14624708" class="wp-caption-text">消防员到场检查，确认没有火情。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚的音乐会还有意外插曲。博物馆储藏室的湿度控制设备故障，引发火灾警报，导致演出暂停，所有观众、演员和舞台人员不得不到移步室外。等消防员赶到现场检查，确认没有火情之后，大家才能回到博物馆的剧场。演员们很快重新就位，观众则报以热烈掌声和欢呼。◇</p>
