<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美容小妙招：巧用家居用品</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625204-shutterstock_2152845249-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>家是个宝藏，很多用品可以随手取来用于美容护肤。（Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月29日讯】（大纪元记者夏瑜编译报导）家，甜蜜的家。我们的家不仅是休憩的港湾，还有着各种用品可以帮助我们保持皮肤、头发、牙齿、指甲的健康。我们甚至还能找到一些提升化妆技巧的产品。看看“商业内幕”介绍的小妙招吧。</p>
<p>用厨房里的食材制作面膜：将热水、燕麦片、酸奶、蜂蜜、蛋清混合，敷在脸上，能焕活肌肤。在水中加入苹果醋，就是一款清洁并紧致肌肤的洗面奶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625205" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625205-0b837c3fb7f326c120b5e1115f7ae7b5-e1761674884369.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625205-0b837c3fb7f326c120b5e1115f7ae7b5-e1761674884369.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625205" class="wp-caption-text">用厨房里的食材制作面膜。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>盐和油去角质：把食盐、橄榄油、椰子油混合成糊状，淋浴时涂抹在皮肤上。它就像身体磨砂膏一样，可以去除角质和干燥死皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625207" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625207" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625207-ed552206fe54cf4358a345c91135f1de-e1761674979661.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625207-ed552206fe54cf4358a345c91135f1de-e1761674979661.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625207" class="wp-caption-text">盐和油混合后去角质。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>咖啡滤纸护理油性皮肤：用干净的咖啡滤纸轻拍整个面部，能去除皮肤上多余的油脂。对于油性皮肤的人来说，这是一个很好的方法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625208" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625208-cbd491efd2d41e536db02e84032c2e09-e1761675094730.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625208-cbd491efd2d41e536db02e84032c2e09-e1761675094730.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625208" class="wp-caption-text">咖啡滤纸拍脸吸油。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>盐和小苏打洁白牙齿：将牙刷浸入半茶匙盐和半茶匙小苏打中，经常用这种混合物刷牙，可以去除牙上污渍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625210" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625210" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625210-205f234b399eb1f0e4eeae835f53f024-e1761675243992.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625210-205f234b399eb1f0e4eeae835f53f024-e1761675243992.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625210" class="wp-caption-text">用盐和小苏打刷牙洁齿。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冷杓不仅可消除眼部浮肿，在化妆时也很有用：将杓子放入冰箱或冰柜中冷藏几个小时，然后取出直接放在眼睛下方，可以帮助缓解眼部浮肿。但它的好处远不止于此。将杓子放在上眼皮上，然后以杓子为底，涂抹睫毛膏，可以更用力地按压，并让睫毛卷翘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625213" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625213-3ea0e403b9ce6245955be39ad2b7a41b-e1761675595512.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14625213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625213-3ea0e403b9ce6245955be39ad2b7a41b-e1761675595512.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625213" class="wp-caption-text">冷杓可消除眼部浮肿。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>想画出完美的猫眼妆，不妨试试发夹或透明胶带：用眼线笔画出完美的猫眼妆可能不太容易，但家里有两样东西或许能帮上忙。首先，把波比发夹的两端涂上眼线膏，然后把它印在眼角，这样你就有了完美的眼形，接下来只需把它填满即可。如果这种方法不管用，可以在同一位置斜贴一条胶带，然后沿着胶带边缘画眼线，打造完美妆容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625215-acac0c578db09a826a767505fb887fe3-e1761675829640.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625215-acac0c578db09a826a767505fb887fe3-e1761675829640.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625215" class="wp-caption-text">小小发夹可辅助画眼妆。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>使用烹饪喷雾，在家也能拥有长久美甲效果：在家自己做美甲后，为了确保妆效长长久久，你可以在指甲上喷一些烹饪喷雾，固定你的甲妆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625216" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625216-5de62be496f1d83818e8cd8e707fcc1d-e1761676010858.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625216-5de62be496f1d83818e8cd8e707fcc1d-e1761676010858.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="640" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625216" class="wp-caption-text">用烹饪喷雾固定甲妆。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>必要时，火柴盒能当指甲锉用： 如果你需要锉指甲，但又在家里找不到指甲锉，那就用火柴盒吧。擦火柴的地方可以用来锉指甲，效果还不错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625217-aedbdac07cd6c510f3a2bcbee3ab2c1a-e1761676111501.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625217-aedbdac07cd6c510f3a2bcbee3ab2c1a-e1761676111501.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625217" class="wp-caption-text">火柴盒能当指甲锉用。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>咖啡不仅提神醒脑，还能护理秀发：在冲泡咖啡的同时，你可以像平常一样冲个澡，洗个头。咖啡冲泡完成后，让咖啡冷却，然后倒入碗中。回到淋浴间，将冷咖啡慢慢地浇在头发上。戴上浴帽，静待20～30分钟，再冲洗干净。咖啡中的咖啡因有助于恢复头发生长，增加秀发光泽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625218" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625218-2a456ad12c108108acfb0475ad680e80-e1761676205255.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625218-2a456ad12c108108acfb0475ad680e80-e1761676205255.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625218" class="wp-caption-text">咖啡能护理秀发。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>想让香水留香更久，就在皮肤上涂抹凡士林：有些人喜欢喷香水，为了延长香味的停留时间，可以在皮肤的某些部位（例如手腕）先涂抹凡士林，然后直接将香水喷在该部位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625219-70c0614bf0287c6931aaf836697697e9-e1761676350598.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625219-70c0614bf0287c6931aaf836697697e9-e1761676350598.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625219" class="wp-caption-text">凡士林可延长香水的停留时间。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>化妆品沾到衬衫领子上，用剃须膏去除：化妆可让人变美，但也可能留下一团糟，比如把化妆品掉在衣服领子上。别慌，在污渍处涂上剃须膏，然后用湿毛巾擦掉，立马光洁如新，斑点消失无踪。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625220" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625220-bab2bd933b6a9db9d8ab01da8047114b-e1761676439661.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14625220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625220-bab2bd933b6a9db9d8ab01da8047114b-e1761676439661.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625220" class="wp-caption-text">用剃须膏去除化妆品污渍。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
