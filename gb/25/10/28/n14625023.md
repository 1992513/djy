<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在新北山区 摄影家用相机记下秋天最安静的时刻</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625024-805117-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>薄雾覆着山谷，晨光斜照在蜿蜒的产业道路上。新北市的深秋，安静得只听得见风声。（Kimball Wen提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新北市报导）新北市的高山与茶园，每到秋天就会安静许多。晨雾沿着山坡往下滑，叶片开始变色，风带着干爽的味道。摄影家 Kimball Wen 连续好几天上山取景，把相机对着山谷、树叶与光影，拍下了一系列关于“秋天”的画面。他不是追大景，也不等热门的日出、云海。他说：“看到什么就拍什么，不特别安排。”相机拍下的，是山里最普通、却也是最干净的一些瞬间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625025" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625025-805118.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625025-805118-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625025" class="wp-caption-text">掉落在路边的枫叶被阳光照亮，失去部分叶肉后留下的纹理更显脆弱，也更美。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625026" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625026-805119.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625026-805119-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625026" class="wp-caption-text">秋风轻吹，芒花在阳光中闪着细碎的银光，像一片小小的浪。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>茶园的云河 轻轻飘着几张照片里，整片茶园像从云里浮出来。白色的雾慢慢往山谷堆积，茶树的线条被雾压得更柔和。那天，他站在土路旁哼着小曲：“哼着歌云河里的茶乡秋诗翩翩呢”画面看起来没什么激烈的色彩，只是雾、山坡、几个采茶人的身影。但透过镜头，看得出山里的时间是慢的。雾散开时，茶园的纹路清楚可见；雾再起来，整座山又像被盖回去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625027" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625027-805120.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625027-805120-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625027" class="wp-caption-text">山间的晨雾沿着溪谷堆叠，路像是通往云海的入口。这是新北秋天最安静的时刻。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625028-805121.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625028-805121-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625028" class="wp-caption-text">层层山岚在日出前静静铺开，远处的山脊被染成灰蓝色。深秋的冷意正慢慢上来。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625029" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625029-805122.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625029-805122-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625029" class="wp-caption-text">几株高耸的芒草立在云海前面，像守着山谷的哨兵。冬天快到了。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>叶子比风还安静他拍了许多叶片的特写。有的薄得能透光，有的卷起来像一条细长的黄带，有的被蜘蛛丝挂在半空中，看起来像停住不动。红叶最显眼。逆光照着时，叶脉一条条浮出，像手掌的纹路。绿叶、黄叶、红叶同时出现在同一根枝条上，就像一棵树把三个季节放在一起。他说他喜欢这样的细节，没有声音，也不需要旁白。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625030" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625030-805123.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625030-805123-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625030" class="wp-caption-text">砍伐后留下的树桩，在光影里呈现粗糙的纹路。新生的草与茶树已在旁边冒出。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625031" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625031" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625031-805124.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625031-805124-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625031" class="wp-caption-text">豆娘停在覆满青苔的岩石尖端，阳光把它的翅与身影拉长，映出深山幽谷里最细致的宁静。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625032-805125.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625032-805125-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625032" class="wp-caption-text">老屋的石墙仍然斑斑可见手工砌石的痕迹，阳光斜照，影子映在墙面上，像是岁月留下的印记。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云雾里的自己在其中一段文字里，他写道：“云河呀云河云河里有个我随风飘过从没有找到真正的我”问他这句话是不是感伤？他笑说：“只是山里很静，安静到会想一些多余的事。人到山里，心就比较没有杂音。”几张照片里，雾厚得像棉布，山线看不清楚，只剩下一棵孤单的树在画面中央。没有解释那些画面代表什么，他只是记录下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625033" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625033-805126.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625033-805126-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625033" class="wp-caption-text">院子里的九重葛满开，紫红色花朵把早晨照得更加明亮，像是一座开在山中的小花园。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625034" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625034-805127.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625034-805127-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625034" class="wp-caption-text">蕨叶在蓝天下层层展开，阳光透过叶面，细密的脉络像绿色羽衣，带着鲜明的生命力。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625035" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625035-805129.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14625035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625035-805129-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625035" class="wp-caption-text">溪水清澈见底，阳光射入水面，折射出柔和金光，鱼影也随水纹缓缓游过。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一叶而知秋最让他停下来很久的，是一片挂在空中的枯叶。看起来像是要掉，又像被谁留住，轻轻晃着。阳光照在背面，叶子的洞孔、斑点、折痕通通看得见。他写了一句很短的话：“对着光影发呆…往事凝眸的记忆一叶。而知秋”没有剧情，也没有哲学，只有一片叶子和午后的光。</p>
<p>枯叶被细线吊在空中，轻轻晃动，像是被风留住的瞬间。（Kimball Wen提供）枫叶在枝头转亮，橙、红、粉交错，深秋的颜色在阳光里清楚发亮。（Kimball Wen提供）溪水清澈见底，阳光射入水面，折射出柔和金光，鱼影也随水纹缓缓游过。（Kimball Wen提供）</p>
<p>秋天的旅途 没有速度Kimball Wen 说，这些照片不是要呈现壮观，也不是要讲大道理。只是秋天到了，风变干、光变柔，很多平常不起眼的细节都变得清楚。不论是蝴蝶停在小花上，或是雾在山谷移动，都慢慢地、悄悄地来。他把相机收回背包里，准备下山时说了一句：“秋日的人生风景旅途诗写中…”没有结语，也没有宏大的想法。结束轻旅行之后，照片留着，山留下来，秋天还在继续。</p>
