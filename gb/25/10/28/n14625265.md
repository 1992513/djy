<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>哈佛及MIT台湾学生乐享联谊运动会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625234-DSC03778-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>哈佛大学及MIT台湾同学会10月26日联合举办运动会。红衣哈佛队，蓝衣MIT队在下午的男子篮球比赛中激烈角逐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月29日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）10月26日，哈佛大学及麻省理工学院（MIT）台湾同学会联合举办年度运动会，7个项目总共120多名会员参加。赛场上欢声笑语，参赛者及观众同享愉快时光。</p>
<p>当天的活动都在MIT体育中心举办。上午9:30，运动会以节奏紧张的大队接力赛拉开序幕。接下来，全天的活动还包括桌球、排球、羽球、乐乐棒球、篮球，以及桥牌比赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625235-DSC03783.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625235-DSC03783-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625235" class="wp-caption-text">观众为篮球比赛的选手欢呼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据哈佛台湾同学会会长李典璋介绍，桥牌是今年的新增活动，原因是很多同学都希望运动会能有益智类项目。其它比赛都是历年的传统项目。</p>
<p>经过一天的欢乐比赛，MIT台湾同学会以总积分130分的优势，赢过哈佛台湾同学会的90分，因而再次延续多年的传统，拿下优胜奖杯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625236-DSC03800.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625236-DSC03800-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625236" class="wp-caption-text">男子篮球比赛结束，双方选手拍手以示友谊。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当然，哈佛的同学们也有不少精彩表现。比如在下午的男子篮球赛中，哈佛队在21比54的大幅落后情况下，最后一秒中场投篮命中，扳回3分，赢得观众的热烈欢呼和掌声。</p>
<p>接下来的女子篮球赛中，哈佛队的4号选手攻势勇猛，屡屡突破防守，投篮命中。最终哈佛队以9比7的优势赢得这场比赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625237" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625237-DSC03810.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625237-DSC03810-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625237" class="wp-caption-text">女子篮球比赛选手运球突破防守。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场运动会还有一些欢乐瞬间。比如在男女混合篮球赛中，按照规则，男生防守女生时不准举手，但选手们一时难以改变习惯性的防守动作，所以在比赛开始的几分钟多次犯规，引发观众大笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625240" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625240-DSC03843.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625240-DSC03843-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625240" class="wp-caption-text">男女混合篮球比赛是当天最后一个项目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625239" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625239-DSC03839.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625239-DSC03839-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625239" class="wp-caption-text">经文处科技组组长蔡孟勋（右）、台学联顾问冯文鸾到场观赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MIT物理博士赵恩宏说，他当天参加了接力、棒球和篮球比赛，感觉非常好玩。他笑言，虽然比赛负责人提前一周组织了训练，但其实大家并没有练习太多，只要比赛中没人受伤或吵架就好。他说，最重要的是能交到朋友，大家都是波士顿地区的学生、学者，或许将来还能在学术方面合作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625241" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625241" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625241-DSC03855.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625241-DSC03855-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625241" class="wp-caption-text">比赛之后，两校学生会举办庆功宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625243" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625243-DSC03872.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625243-DSC03872-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625243" class="wp-caption-text">经文处教育组组长黄薳玉（左）为赛事MVP颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚，两个同学会举办了庆功晚宴，为每场比赛的“最有价值选手”（MVP），以及比赛总分优胜方颁奖。驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处教育组组长黄薳玉、科技组组长蔡孟勋也都到场同乐，向同学们表达祝贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625244" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625244-DSC03877.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625244" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625244-DSC03877-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625244" class="wp-caption-text">经文处科技组组长蔡孟勋（左）为赛事MVP颁奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625245" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625245-DSC03886.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625245-DSC03886-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625245" class="wp-caption-text">MIT台湾同学会再次获得比赛总分优胜，但他们忘记带会旗。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场比赛的成功，离不开三位总召的辛苦奔走。他们是哈佛台湾同学会的魏嵚典、方浚懿，以及MIT台湾同学会的宋致远。他们协调了与比赛相关的各项工作，包括招募选手、宣传活动、安排赛程等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14625246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625246" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625246-DSC03895.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625246-DSC03895-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625246" class="wp-caption-text">参与比赛的选手和工作人员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14625247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14625247" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625247-DSC03899.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14625247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14625247-DSC03899-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14625247" class="wp-caption-text">左起：哈佛台湾同学会会长陈曦、李典璋，运动会总召宋致远、魏嵚典、方浚懿，MIT台湾同学会会长林晏平、张浩扬。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们谈到，由于都是第一次做总召工作，他们必须努力结识和沟通各方人员。而且因为学业和工作繁忙，他们都必须抓紧时间，往往只能在课余时间从事运动会工作，总算最后使活动顺利举办。◇</p>
