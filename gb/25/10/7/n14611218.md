<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欣逢中华民国114年国庆 布办处举办庆祝酒会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612713-DSC09951_ratio-3-to-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>布里斯本办事处于10月3日晚间在SunPAC艺术表演中心举办“庆祝中华民国114年国庆酒会”，范处长伉俪与政要、贵宾们上台举杯庆祝。（倪尔森／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月07日讯】（大纪元特约记者倪尔森、琳达澳洲布里斯本报导）驻布里斯本台北经济文化办事处于10月3日晚间在SunPAC艺术表演中心举办“庆祝中华民国114年国庆酒会”，澳洲政要、台侨荣誉职人员、商侨学界及媒体等近250人出席庆贺。</p>
<h2><strong>精彩开幕表演展现台湾文化魅力</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612702-DSC09823-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612702-DSC09823-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612702" class="wp-caption-text">Calamvale Community College太鼓队击鼓表演为酒会揭开序幕。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会由王泓翔（James Wang）与陈宣如（Sharon Chen）主持，在Calamvale Community College Dean Simmons老师带领的太鼓队震撼鼓声中揭开序幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612703-DSC09843-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612703-DSC09843-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612703" class="wp-caption-text">台侨第三代Abby Lee韵律体操的精彩表演。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后，甫获2025年澳洲全国韵律体操赛“初级青少年组全能冠军”的台侨第三代Abby Lee精彩演出，展现台裔新生代的杰出成就。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612704-DSC09853-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612704-DSC09853-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612704" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本FASCA团队（海外青年文化大使）领唱国歌。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年布里斯本刚成军的FASCA团队（海外青年文化大使）则领唱国歌，为酒会增添青春活力。</p>
<h2><strong>范厚禄处长强调台澳深化合作</strong></h2>
<p>范厚禄处长在致词中指出，台湾在地缘政治及全球供应链居关键地位，瑞士洛桑管理学院2025年“世界竞争力年报”中台湾排名全球第6，美国哈佛大学“全球关键新兴科技指数报告”更显示台湾在人工智慧、半导体、生物科技、太空及量子科技等5大领域位居前位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612705-DSC09860-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612705-DSC09860-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612705" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本台北经济文化办事处范厚禄处长致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾与昆士兰关系日益增强，范处长强调，在赖总统“价值外交”理念与“经济日不落国”愿景下，政府持续推动“总合外交”政策，将台湾打造为“AI人工智慧岛”，实践“Taiwan can help and lead”理念。</p>
<h2><strong>澳洲政要肯定台澳友谊与合作</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612706-DSC09888-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612706-DSC09888-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612706" class="wp-caption-text">联邦议员坎贝尔（Julie-Ann Campbell）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>联邦议员坎贝尔（Julie-Ann Campbell）强调：“布里斯本南区台湾社区是经济成长的关键驱动力，台裔社区在体育、音乐、文化等各领域都扮演重要角色。”</p>
<p>她自豪地表示，南区是全昆州最多元文化的社区，多元文化正是澳洲的成功故事，呼吁大家团结一致，共创更美好的澳洲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612707-DSC09898-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612707-DSC09898-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612707" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰副议长克劳斯（Jon Krause）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昆士兰副议长克劳斯（Jon Krause）表示，“台湾是昆州第二大贸易伙伴，双方不仅共享贸易关系，更共享民主与法治等核心价值。”<br />
他特别提到今年5月率领跨党派国会代表团访台的经验，参访了台达电子、创浦智能等企业，以及国防安全研究院等机构，深刻体会到台湾的创新实力与热情款待，这次访问让代表团成为更坚定的台湾之友。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612708-DSC09911-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612708-DSC09911-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612708" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰反对党副领袖迪克（Cameron Dick）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昆士兰反对党副领袖迪克（Cameron Dick）指出：“去年有4,000名台湾年轻人来昆州求学，5万名台湾游客来昆州度假，台昆关系透过贸易、教育、科技与文化交流将持续深化。”</p>
<p>迪克特别强调，面对近期联邦议会某些针对移民的不当言论，他要向所有台湾朋友表明，“我看见你们、我相信你们、我认可你们，因为你们是昆士兰家庭和澳洲家庭的一部分。”他呼吁团结对抗分裂力量，因为团结才能让社会更强大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612709-DSC09923-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612709-DSC09923-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612709" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰州议员马丁（James Martin）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自Stretton选区的州议员马丁（James Martin）赞扬布里斯本拥有全澳最大的台裔人口，台湾社区在商业投资、艺术文化和教育等方面做出巨大贡献。昆州有众多优秀的台湾社团组织，这些组织透过文化节庆、商业交流、音乐艺术和体育活动凝聚社区。</p>
<p>他特别提到，从珍珠奶茶、台湾炸鸡到臭豆腐，台湾社区为布里斯本带来最美味的料理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612710-DSC09926-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612710-DSC09926-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612710" class="wp-caption-text">市议员迪克森（Julia Dixon）代表布里斯本市长施林纳（Adrian Schrinner）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市议员迪克森（Julia Dixon）代表布里斯本市长施林纳（Adrian Schrinner）转达祝贺，市长在贺词中表示：“今年主题反映了我们在布里斯本共同珍视的价值观，作为一个尊重传统同时拥抱未来的城市，我们与台湾的友谊透过商业、文化和社区连结日益增强。愿这次庆典强化我们之间的纽带，彰显我们在繁荣、创新和持久伙伴关系上的共同愿景。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612711" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612711-DSC09932-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612711-DSC09932-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612711" class="wp-caption-text">Redland City市长米切尔（Jos Mitchell）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Redland City市长米切尔（Jos Mitchell）感谢台湾社区的贡献，他表示，“新移民为社区带来丰富色彩，透过文化活动、美食、音乐和时尚让社区更加精彩。”他幽默地表示，代表女儿感谢台湾人对珍珠奶茶销量50%成长的贡献。</p>
<h2><strong>侨界代表展望未来合作</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612712" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612712-DSC09935-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612712-DSC09935-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612712" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员罗富龄代表昆士兰台侨感谢致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨务委员罗富龄代表昆士兰台侨致词表示，过去两年台澳合作成果丰硕，台湾是澳洲第六大贸易伙伴，期盼未来昆士兰侨界能配合布里斯本办事处与澳洲政府更紧密的交流合作。</p>
<h2><strong>台湾美食与文化交流</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612701-DSC09967-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612701-DSC09967-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612701" class="wp-caption-text">百家千味餐厅提供著名台式美食小吃供宾客享用。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场安排中华航空、长荣航空设摊，百家千味餐厅提供著名台式美食小吃，包括刈包、盐酥鸡、卤味、卤肉饭、炒米粉、珍珠奶茶和冬瓜茶等经典台湾美食供与会来宾品尝，让澳洲友人感受到浓浓的台湾人情味。</p>
<p>酒会在范处长夫妇与政要们上台敬酒祝贺中华民国生日快乐后，于温馨欢乐的气氛中圆满落幕。此次国庆酒会不仅展现台澳深厚友谊，更彰显旅昆台侨对当地社会的卓越贡献，为台澳关系的持续深化奠定坚实基础。</p>
