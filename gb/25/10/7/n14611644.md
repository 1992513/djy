<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英崙客家乡亲会庆中秋 欢迎新会员</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611642-DSC02948-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙客家乡亲会10月4日欢庆中秋节，部分乡亲穿着橙色镶花服装，与秋色相配。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月08日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨布鲁克林镇报导）在黄绿交错、秋意初现的10月5日，纽英崙客家乡亲会在麻州布鲁克林镇（Brookline）举办年度餐会，庆祝中秋团圆，也欢迎新会员加入这个大家庭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14611641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611641-DSC02939.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611641-DSC02939-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611641" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙客家乡亲会10月4日欢庆中秋节。参与者们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天中午，布鲁克林镇Larz Anderson公园烧烤区热火朝天，美食飘香。据客家乡亲会会长宋玉琴介绍，会员、嘉宾及家属总计近100人参加这次聚餐，难能可贵的是有多位乡亲由罗德岛远道而来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14611643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611643-DSC02953.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611643-DSC02953-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611643" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙客家乡亲会新会员们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14611639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611639-DSC02921.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611639-DSC02921-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611639" class="wp-caption-text">乡亲们享用客家美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>乡亲们特别欢迎10多位新会员第一次参加活动，例如从罗德岛来的高海、夏淑曼夫妇，波士顿台湾影展协会成员黄文彦等等。高海虽是在美国成长的白人，却能以流利的中文和大家交谈。他笑言，他在台湾学好了中文。</p>
<p>宋玉琴邀请所有新会员合影，并表示，很高兴大家欢聚一堂，庆祝中秋节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14611636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611636-DSC02906.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611636-DSC02906-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611636" class="wp-caption-text">第一次参加聚餐的乡亲及家属与会长宋玉琴合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14611638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611638-DSC02920.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611638-DSC02920-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611638" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾们齐享美食。左1为侨教中心主任高家富，左2为会长宋玉琴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>客家乡亲们一如既往地带来丰盛菜肴，例如林上田和蔡高进在现场烹饪客家小炒和客家板条，曾正泉开大火炒粉，锅气弥漫，引人围观；蔡坤喜医师送来外焦里嫩的烤鸭；毕儒宗带来花生麻糬；刘悦慧和邱西蔷制作客家凤梨木耳；郑玉春用自家种植的蔬菜香拌凉菜等等。</p>
<p>当天多位客家乡亲穿着带有传统特色的橙色镶花服装，正与公园的秋色相配，得到来宾们的夸赞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14611634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611634" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611634-DSC02883.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611634" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611634-DSC02883-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611634" class="wp-caption-text">蔡高进明炉烹饪客家小炒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14611633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611633-DSC02880.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611633-DSC02880-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611633" class="wp-caption-text">曾正泉在现场炒粉。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长宋玉琴也感谢赞助客家乡亲会的5位嘉宾，包括范庆达、柯乃南、蔡坤喜、苏来富和黎美惠。比如创办卫斯理（Wellesley）丰田经销店的柯乃南慷慨捐赠300美元；带着大白狗出席餐会的裁缝师范庆达也捐赠500美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14611640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611640-DSC02923.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611640-DSC02923-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611640" class="wp-caption-text">参加餐会的柯乃南（左）和陈裕逢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14611635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611635-DSC02889.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611635-DSC02889-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611635" class="wp-caption-text">带着大白狗出席餐会的裁缝师范庆达。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14611637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14611637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611637-DSC02919.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14611637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14611637-DSC02919-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14611637" class="wp-caption-text">长期支持客家乡亲会的陈家骅（左）和陈裕逢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽英崙客家乡亲会于今年5月庆祝了15周年。侨教中心主任高家富在10月的餐会中称赞，客家乡亲们在海外都发展得很好，而且“非常团结，非常有向心力”。值中秋佳节，他祝愿大家“月圆人团圆”。◇</p>
