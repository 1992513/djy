<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>府城天险的晨光 鹿耳门溪口的历史与守望</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623604-804441-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>镇门宫建筑色彩鲜明，以红、黄、黑三色为主调，屋檐飞翘，展现闽南建筑的庄严气势。（陈锡兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月26日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）清晨的鹿耳门溪口，阳光刚从树梢洒下，雾气仍未散去，映照着“府城天险”四字的巨石。这里位于台南安南区，是清朝时期“台湾八景”之一。当年文人雅士以诗文记录府城的壮阔风景，“府城天险”即以地势险要、潮涌相搏而得名。如今，溪口的水势已经平缓，唯有海浪拍岸的声音，仍在诉说着过往的历史。从空拍视角俯瞰，鹿耳门溪蜿蜒入海，堤岸两侧绿树成荫，形成一个天然的水道。昔日这里正是郑成功率军自鹿耳门登陆的地点，被称为“骑鲸人码头”。如今岸边立着石碑，刻着“府城天险”，是游客与摄影人常驻足之处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14623601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623601" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623601-804438.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623601-804438-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623601" class="wp-caption-text">从高空俯瞰鹿耳门溪汇入台湾海峡的壮阔景象，堤岸、林带与海浪构成自然与历史交织的画面。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14623598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623598" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623598-804435.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623598-804435-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623598" class="wp-caption-text">“府城天险”石碑立于堤岸尽头，背后是海天一线的波涛，记录清代台湾八景之一的历史印记。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14623599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623599" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623599-804436.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623599-804436-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623599" class="wp-caption-text">晨曦穿过榕树枝叶洒落地面，光影交错，映出鹿耳门的静谧与生命感。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑成功与镇门宫鹿耳门一带有座“镇门宫”，主祀开台圣王郑成功。庙宇建筑为两层楼式样，红、黄、黑三色鲜明对比，屋顶飞檐起角，带有浓厚的闽南建筑风格。早晨阳光斜照，庙宇在光影之间显得特别庄严。宫前矗立一尊石像，郑成功双手持印、身着战袍，神情坚毅。这座雕像的方向正对着出海口，象征护国庇民，也暗喻着先民开疆辟土的精神。镇门宫门口的门神也别具特色，画的是全台唯一的荷兰人赤脚门神，“鹿风”与“耳顺”。画师以鲜明的红蓝色调描绘他们的铠甲与神情，寓意守护地方、化敌为友。这种融合异国文化的图像，见证了鹿耳门在历史交会下的包容与变迁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14623597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623597" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623597-804434.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623597-804434-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623597" class="wp-caption-text">宫门彩绘门神为全台唯一的荷兰人赤脚门神“鹿风”“耳顺”，绘工生动，寓意化敌为友、镇守海口。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14623596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623596" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623596-804433.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623596-804433-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623596" class="wp-caption-text">镇门宫前郑成功石像正面向出海口，手执印玺、神情坚毅，象征开台圣王守护南疆的精神。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海风与光影的守望沿着庙前步道走去，可以看到一片小广场，地面嵌着台湾岛形的浮雕。这是当地居民共同维护的象征地标，代表着从这里出发、守护整个台湾。晨光从榕树枝叶间洒落，光线穿过薄雾，在地面上留下斑斓的光影。这一刻的静谧，让人仿佛听见历史的低语。不远处，四草守望哨静静立在岸边。这里是游客休憩、观赏海景的好地方。当潮水退去时，海平线与天际连成一线，渔筏点点，如画一般。风声、浪声、鸟鸣声交织成府城海口的清晨乐章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14623602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623602" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623602-804439.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623602-804439-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623602" class="wp-caption-text">园区中央以台湾岛形浮雕为设计核心，象征从此地出发、守护整个台湾。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14623594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623594" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623594-804432.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623594-804432-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623594" class="wp-caption-text">清晨从空中俯瞰鹿耳门溪口，“府城天险”一带的地形清晰可见，堤岸两侧绿荫成带，溪水蜿蜒入海，展现古称“天险”的形势。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>历史的延续“府城天险”不只是风景，更是一段时代记忆的延续。从郑成功的登陆，到清代八景的题咏，再到现今的生态复育与文化保存，这片土地始终在变化，也始终有人守望。陈锡兴镜头下的鹿耳门溪口，有海、有庙、有光，也有历史留下的呼吸。当第一道晨光洒在镇门宫前的石像上，这里的一切都提醒着人们：府城之险，不仅在地势，更在那份历史的深度与人心的坚定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14623600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623600-804437.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623600-804437-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623600" class="wp-caption-text">岸边望向出海口，海浪推叠、潮音不息，是摄影爱好者捕捉晨光与浪线的热门地点。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14623603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14623603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623603-804440.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14623603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14623603-804440-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14623603" class="wp-caption-text">石碑上“府城天险”四字苍劲有力，立于海风之中，成为安南区最具代表性的历史地标。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
