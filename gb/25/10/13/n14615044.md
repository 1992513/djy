<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>海湾人举行联盟杯冠军横幅揭幕式</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615073-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-1-s-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>海湾人举行冠军横幅揭幕仪式，新的荣誉标志自此高悬流明球场上空。（理查德/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年10月13日讯】（大纪元记者理查德西雅图报导）10月11日晚，美职联（MLS）球队西雅图海湾人队（Seattle Sounders FC）在主场最后一场常规赛中，举行“联盟杯”（Leagues Cup，美职联与墨西哥联赛联合的夏季锦标赛）冠军横幅揭幕仪式，并赠送白色康乃馨花给球迷，感谢他们本赛季的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14615074" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615074" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615074-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-2-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615074" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615074-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-2-s-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615074" class="wp-caption-text">海湾人主教练布莱恩·施梅策（Brian Schmetzer）带领球员向观众赠送康乃馨，深表谢意。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当海湾人队在流明球场（Lumen Field）揭开象征冠军荣耀的巨型横幅时，全场球迷起立欢呼，掌声与欢呼声此起彼伏。这不仅是见证西雅图的足球辉煌时刻，也为本赛季主场常规赛画上温馨句号。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14615076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615076" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615076-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-3-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615076-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-3-s-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615076" class="wp-caption-text">入场球迷每人都收到一块印有球队队徽和康乃馨的方巾。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14615078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615078" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615078-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-4-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615078-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-4-s-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615078" class="wp-caption-text">流明球场LED屏幕上绽放的白色康乃馨与观众席间的战旗交相辉映，构成本季最动人的画面。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本赛季，海湾人队在联盟杯决赛中击败梅西领衔的迈阿密国际队，完成了“大满贯”（集齐美国职业足球大联盟杯（MLS Cup）、支持者盾牌（Supporters&#8217; Shield）、美国公开杯（U.S. Open Cup）与联盟杯（Leagues Cup）四项主要冠军）的拼图，成为美国职业足球大联盟史上首支达成此成就的球队。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14615079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615079" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615079-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-5-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615079-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-5-s-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615079" class="wp-caption-text">赛前，球队展出联盟杯奖杯，邀请小球迷合影留念，将这份荣耀回馈给“翡翠之城”西雅图。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14615080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615080" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615080-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-6-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615080-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-6-s-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615080" class="wp-caption-text">球迷开心地展示方巾。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14615081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615081" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615081-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-7-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615081-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-7-s-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615081" class="wp-caption-text">球迷拿着方巾喜笑颜开。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>联盟杯这项夏季赛事汇聚北美顶尖球队，竞争激烈，海湾人队的胜利让球迷热血沸腾。</p>
<p>值得一提的是，海湾人队一线阵容中，有超过10名球员出自华盛顿州本地青训体系。这不仅彰显球队自1974年成立以来，半个世纪深耕社区、扎根本土的传统，更让这座城市的足球荣耀代代相传。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14615083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615083" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615083-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-8-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615083-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-8-s-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615083" class="wp-caption-text">后防中坚亚历克斯·罗尔丹（Alex Roldan）将纪念足球抛向观众席，感谢球迷多年陪伴。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为球队延续半世纪的温馨传统，海湾人队每个赛季的最后一场主场常规赛，都会向球迷赠送白色康乃馨，象征感恩与祝福。这一仪式已融入球队文化，见证着球队与西雅图市民间的深厚情感纽带。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14615084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615084" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615084-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-9-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615084-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-9-s-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615084" class="wp-caption-text">年轻后卫卡拉尼·科萨-里恩齐（Kalani Kossa-Rienzi）将自己的护腿板赠予球迷。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>赛后，球员们手持康乃馨向看台挥手致意。有些人将花束抛向球迷，也有球员赠出签名护腿板或纪念足球，分享胜利的喜悦。看台上白色康乃馨花飘扬，绿茵场上笑声洋溢——流明球场在灯光下，闪耀温情与荣耀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14615086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615086" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615086-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-10-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615086-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-10-s-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615086" class="wp-caption-text">作为本场进球功臣，小将罗斯洛克和观众搞怪互动。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14615087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14615087" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615087-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-11-s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14615087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14615087-8-SoundersCelebratesLeaguesCup-11-s-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14615087" class="wp-caption-text">小球迷们在得知自己的照片将登在媒体时笑容绽放。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看台上的孩子们得知自己的照片将登上媒体，露出纯真开心的笑容。每一张笑脸，就像一朵花、一颗希望的种子，在他们心中悄然生根发芽。这或许正是海湾人队能在美职联稳健运作、成为首支大满贯冠军并领跑积分榜的秘密。</p>
