<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>昆士兰侨界举办国庆晚宴 凝聚侨心深耕台澳友谊</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612747-DSC00272-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>昆士兰侨界于10月4日晚间在布里斯本南区的枫林餐厅，举办庆祝中华民国114年双十国庆晚宴，贵宾与台侨荣誉职人员、社团会长合影。（倪尔森／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月07日讯】（大纪元特约记者倪尔森、琳达澳洲布里斯本报导）澳洲昆士兰侨界于10月4日晚间在布里斯本南区的枫林餐厅盛大举办中华民国114年双十国庆庆祝晚宴，吸引约360位贵宾与侨胞热情参与。</p>
<p>驻布里斯本办事处范厚禄处长伉俪、昆士兰台湾中心许俊涌主任率全体同仁应邀出席。</p>
<h2><strong>侨界齐心筹办 </strong><strong>展现团结力量</strong></h2>
<p>今年国庆筹备会由侨务委员罗富龄担任主任委员，陈秋燕与徐瑞云担任副主任委员。国庆晚宴由世界华人工商妇女企管协会澳洲分会李琼英会长带领团队承办，并由王泓翔与陈宣如共同主持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612747-DSC00272-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612747-DSC00272-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612747" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰侨界于10月4日晚间在布里斯本南区的枫林餐厅，举办庆祝中华民国114年双十国庆晚宴，贵宾与台侨荣誉职人员、社团会长合影。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚宴在中华民国与澳洲国歌声中揭开序幕，特别安排两位小朋友领唱澳洲国歌，象征新生代对多元文化的传承。侨务委员会徐佳青委员长特别录制祝贺影片，向海外侨胞致意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612753" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612753-DSC00208-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612753-DSC00208-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612753" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，罗富龄主任委员致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗富龄主任委员致词时表示，台湾已成为澳洲第六大贸易伙伴，两国建立了坚实的伙伴关系。她感谢驻布里斯本办事处、昆士兰台湾中心的支持，以及世华澳洲分会与所有志工的辛勤筹备，并祝福台湾与澳洲和平繁荣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612754-DSC00217-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612754-DSC00217-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612754" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，罗富龄主任委员致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>范厚禄处长致词时感谢侨界每年10月举办一系列庆祝活动，让“光辉的十月”在昆士兰热闹非凡。他赞扬侨胞热心公益、回馈社会，展现“台湾Can Help”的良善价值。范处长也提到，在赖总统领导下，台湾将持续推动以民主、和平、繁荣为核心的总和外交，期盼加入CPTPP（跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定）及参与联合国等国际组织。</p>
<h2><strong>政要致词肯定台澳关系</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612755-DSC00219-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612755-DSC00219-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612755" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，联邦议员坎贝尔（Julie Ann Campbell）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>联邦议员坎贝尔（Julie Ann Campbell）强调，布里斯本南区是昆士兰最多元文化的社区，台湾社区透过美食、家庭聚会及经济贡献，让当地社区更加强大。她赞扬不同文化背景的经验融合，共同创造更美好的社区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612756" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612756-DSC00225-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612756-DSC00225-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612756" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，联邦参议员斯卡尔（Paul Scarr）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>联邦参议员斯卡尔（Paul Scarr）表示，双十节是庆祝台湾澳洲人对澳洲贡献的日子，也是庆祝澳台之间紧密联系的时刻。他强调将继续致力于加强两国关系，并以共同价值观携手前进。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612757-DSC00227-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612757-DSC00227-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612757" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，布里斯本市长施林纳（Adrian Schrinner）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布里斯本市长施林纳（Adrian Schrinner）特别指出，东南昆士兰拥有全澳洲最大的台湾侨民人口。他盛赞台湾对世界的贡献，包括最先进的半导体、卓越的制造业、珍珠奶茶、顶尖棒球选手，但最重要的是优秀的人民。他表示台裔澳洲人是最优秀的公民，为社区做出巨大贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612743-DSC00232-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612743-DSC00232-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612743" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，昆士兰工党影子厅长以诺（Leanne Enoch）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昆士兰工党影子厅长以诺（Leanne Enoch）致词时表示，双十节不仅是台湾民主旅程与成就的象征，更代表台湾人民在国内外展现的韧性。她强调昆士兰很荣幸能与台湾社区共同庆祝，并赞扬台湾人带来的温暖、文化与慷慨精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612744" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612744-DSC00247-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612744-DSC00247-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612744" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，洛根市市长雷门（Jon Raven）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛根市市长雷门（Jon Raven）分享他刚从台湾返回的经历，范处长特别在台北与他会面，介绍许多商业机会。他赞扬台湾社区的商业才能与让世界变得更美好的决心无与伦比。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612745-DSC00250-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612745-DSC00250-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612745" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，昆士兰警察代理督察理查森（Cindel Richardson）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昆士兰警察代理督察理查森（Cindel Richardson）透露，昆士兰警方最近率领21名警员前往台湾进行首次文化考察，参访国家警政署及法务部调查局，就社区警务、家庭暴力、毒品、重大犯罪及多元文化执法等议题进行深入交流。她特别感谢台湾社区领袖及警方联络官的大力支持，使文化交流之旅圆满成功。</p>
<h2><strong>精彩演出与摸彩活动</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612748-DSC00281-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612748-DSC00281-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612748" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，小提琴与钢琴合奏《台湾民谣组曲》。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会安排了丰富多元的表演节目，包括小提琴与钢琴合奏《台湾民谣组曲》、世华澳洲分会排舞、国乐协会演奏《宝岛颂》、萨克斯风吹奏《夜来香》与《可爱的玫瑰花》，以及乐福乐团口琴演奏《Top of the World》与《黄色玫瑰花》等精彩节目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612749" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612749-DSC00293-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612749-DSC00293-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612749" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，世华澳洲分会表演排舞。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会高潮为摸彩活动，由长荣航空（经济舱）与中华航空（豪华经济舱）提供台北布里斯本来回机票作为大奖，为庆祝活动增添惊喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612750" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612750-DSC00295-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612750-DSC00295-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612750" class="wp-caption-text">昆士兰双十国庆晚宴，昆士兰国乐协会演奏《宝岛颂》。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
