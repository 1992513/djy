<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>庆祝114年双十国庆 昆士兰侨界举办升旗典礼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612772-DSC00003_ratio-3-to-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025年10月4日上午，澳洲昆士兰侨界在昆士兰台湾中心前广场举办升旗典礼，庆祝中华民国114年双十国庆。（倪尔森／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年10月07日讯】（大纪元特约记者倪尔森、琳达澳洲布里斯本报导）10月4日上午8时，澳洲昆士兰侨界在昆士兰台湾中心前广场举办盛大升旗典礼，庆祝中华民国114年双十国庆。典礼由昆士兰华语广播协会帅如珊会长主持，吸引了五百多位侨胞及贵宾热烈参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612773-DSC00023-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612773-DSC00023-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612773" class="wp-caption-text">升旗典礼全体侨界与贵宾挥舞台澳两国国旗，热烈庆祝中华民国114年双十国庆。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>出席贵宾包括驻布里斯本台北经济文化办事处范厚禄处长伉俪及全体同仁、昆士兰台湾中心许俊涌主任、布里斯本侨务荣誉职人员、各社团代表、台湾各银行经理、华航及长荣代表，以及澳洲政府代表。</p>
<h2><strong>筹办团队用心规划</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612771" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612771-DSC09990-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612771-DSC09990-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612771" class="wp-caption-text">升旗典礼由四位FASCA海外青年文化大使担任旗手，迎两国国旗进场。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年双十国庆升旗典礼由侨务委员罗富龄担任筹备会主任委员，侨务委员陈秋燕、徐瑞云担任副主委，昆士兰台湾商会承办，昆士兰台侨各社团协办。典礼由四位FASCA海外青年文化大使担任旗手，迎两国国旗进场并升旗。在播放澳大利亚与中华民国国歌后，两国国旗在国旗歌声中缓缓升起，全体侨胞挥舞台澳两国国旗，场面庄严感人。</p>
<h2><strong>侨务委员罗富龄致词</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612774" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612774-DSC00036-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612774-DSC00036-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612774" class="wp-caption-text">筹备会主任委员罗富龄侨务委员致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>筹备会主任委员罗富龄侨务委员在致词中表示，很高兴看到许多朋友莅临，特别是第二代、第三代台湾侨胞的参与。她指出，过去一年台湾面对全球挑战，但也获得民主盟友的支持，感谢澳洲一直支持台湾参与WHO及国际组织。</p>
<h2><strong>范厚禄处长感谢侨界支持</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612775" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612775-DSC00041William-Fan-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612775-DSC00041William-Fan-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612775" class="wp-caption-text">驻布里斯本台北经济文化办事处范厚禄处长致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻布里斯本台北经济文化办事处范厚禄处长在致词时表示，很荣幸与大家一起参加升旗典礼，庆祝中华民国114年国庆。他特别感谢罗富龄侨务委员及黄元佐会长带领的志工团队辛勤筹办，使活动圆满成功。范处长也特别感谢昆士兰警察局（QPS）的朋友们莅临，表示他们的出席对台湾社区意义重大，让侨胞们感受到支持与温暖。</p>
<h2><strong>澳洲政府代表齐声支持</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612776" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612776" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612776-DSC00047Senator-Paul-Scarr-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612776" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612776-DSC00047Senator-Paul-Scarr-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612776" class="wp-caption-text">联邦参议员斯卡尔（Paul Scarr）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从不缺席台侨活动的联邦参议员斯卡尔（Paul Scarr）致词时表示：“三级政府代表出席升旗典礼，反映出台湾昆士兰社区受到各级政府的高度重视。”他赞扬台湾澳洲社区对澳洲的贡献，并承诺将持续深化澳台之间的连结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612777" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612777-DSC00058-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612777-DSC00058-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612777" class="wp-caption-text">昆州反对党副领袖迪克（Cameron Dick）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>致词中特别展示礼物袋内T恤的昆士兰反对党副领袖迪克（Cameron Dick）表示：“T恤以昆士兰代表色栗色（maroon）为底，结合了台北101大楼和布里斯本故事桥的图案。他表示这座桥象征昆士兰与台湾之间坚固且不可分割的友谊。”迪克强调台湾社区对昆士兰的重要贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612778" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612778-DSC00066-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612778-DSC00066-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612778" class="wp-caption-text">昆州影子厅长以诺（Leanne Enoch）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以原住民语言开场的工党影子厅长以诺（Leanne Enoch）向祖先及澳洲第一民族致敬。她表示：“双十国庆对台湾人民及全球台湾社区意义重大，台湾社区透过商业、教育、文化交流、美食及节庆活动，将台湾丰富的文化融入昆士兰多元社会。”她强调多元文化不仅是多样性，更是尊重、包容与团结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612779" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612779" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612779-DSC00070James-Martin-MP-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612779-DSC00070James-Martin-MP-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612779" class="wp-caption-text">昆州议员马丁（James Martin）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布里斯本是全澳洲最大的台湾出生人口聚居地，昆州议员马丁（James Martin）表示，这是全澳洲最盛大的双十国庆庆祝活动，展现了澳洲作为成功多元文化移民国家的特质。他幽默地说，如果没有移民和台湾社区，Sunnybank会很无聊，只能吃到肉派和炸鱼薯条，而不会有Market Square的美食和繁荣商圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612780" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612780-DSC00075Margie-Nightingale-MP-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612780-DSC00075Margie-Nightingale-MP-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612780" class="wp-caption-text">昆州新任议员南丁格尔（Margie Nightingale）致词。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为昆州新任议员南丁格尔（Margie Nightingale）感谢温锦文董事长经营的Inala Plaza购物中心。她强调昆士兰与台湾共享自由、民主和人权等基本价值，并感谢台湾社区的慷慨贡献。</p>
<p>布里斯本市议员金（Emily Kim）赞扬裕峰集团（YFG）20年来免费提供场地给台湾社区使用，展现台湾社区领袖的慈善精神。</p>
<p>代表市议员马克思（Kim Marks）出席的斯温顿（Henry Swindon）也表达了对台湾社区的感谢，强调三级政府代表齐聚一堂的重要意义。</p>
<h2><strong>表彰贡献与文化交流</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14612782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612782" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612782-DSC00108-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612782-DSC00108-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612782" class="wp-caption-text">FASCA文化大使表演大使之舞“完美世界”。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼中，主办方颁发感谢状给对台湾有贡献的各界人士。“台湾国际棒球发展交流协会”黄思杰理事长率领的棒球队也专程参加升旗典礼。最后由FASCA文化大使表演大使之舞“完美世界”，为典礼划下完美句点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612781" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612781-DSC00094-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612781-DSC00094-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612781" class="wp-caption-text">典礼中主办方颁发感谢状给对台湾有贡献的各界人士。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昆士兰台湾商会黄元佐会长代表承办单位感谢所有贵宾及侨胞的参与，让这个美好的日子充满欢乐与意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14612783" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14612783" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612783-DSC00177-600x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14612783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/10/id14612783-DSC00177-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14612783" class="wp-caption-text">“台湾国际棒球发展交流协会”黄思杰理事长率领的棒球队与贵宾合影。（倪尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所有参加升旗活动的来宾都获得由罗富龄侨务委员提供的“Team Taiwan”T恤一件及早餐，充分展现侨界的热情与团结。此次国庆庆祝活动在温馨热烈的气氛中圆满结束，再次彰显澳洲昆士兰台湾社区的凝聚力与影响力。</p>
