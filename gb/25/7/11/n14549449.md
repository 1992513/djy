<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549502-0a8cfdd303ef96fb0531667ba29163ed-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff

<h6>澳洲徒步野生动物园（Walkabout Wildlife Sanctuary）带您与可爱的动物们零距离亲密接触。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月11日讯】想在自然森林环境中接触澳洲独特的野生动物吗？走进澳大利亚徒步野生动物园（Walkabout Wildlife Sanctuary），你就走进了动物们的世界。在这里，游客们可以漫步在澳洲特有的野生动物之间，和动物们零距离亲密接触，感受野生动物的气息。</p>
<p><center><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d2zw89wk00y815.cloudfront.net/Z7wv4AjWhfuw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/QSKftmAu3x0?si=iH_m-jN4dP_eGJOG&amp;start=151" width="600" b="380" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"><span data-mce-type="bookmark" style="display: inline-block; width: 0px; overflow: hidden; line-height: 0;" class="mce_SELRES_start">?</span></a></center></p>
<figure id="attachment_14549490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549490-Picture1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14549490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549490-Picture1-600x480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549490" class="wp-caption-text">游客可与野生动物一起漫步。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>澳大利亚徒步野生动物园努力为游客创造独特的野生动物观赏环境。公园还安排有节目，专门由专业饲养员讲解有关各种动物习性丶植物用途及有关土着人生活的知识（包含在门票内）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14549500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549500" style="width: 486px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549500-Picture3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14549500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549500-Picture3.jpg" alt="" width="486" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549500" class="wp-caption-text">游客们也可以单独聘请园内私人导游，制定自己喜爱的游园计划，为你提供最佳选择和体验与野生动物在自然中的感受。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走进了这里，就好像走进了动物们的世界，一切都那么自然、随意。和普通动物园不同的是，这里的动物更原生态一些，因为这里本来就是他们的家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14549499" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549499" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549499-Picture4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14549499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549499-Picture4-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549499" class="wp-caption-text">公园还可以留宿在园内设有舒适的套间和野营的帐篷，你会发现这亦会是一个奇特的如梦之旅。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14549497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549497" style="width: 392px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549497-Picture6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14549497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549497-Picture6.jpg" alt="" width="392" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549497" class="wp-caption-text">点起篝火与野生动物一起联欢。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>远离喧闹的市区，置身于丛林中，与野生动物共眠，呼吸大自然的新鲜空气。</p>
<p>每当夜幕降临，宁静的夜幕却难掩森林夜习动物们活跃的踪行踪。对人们来讲这是一个不可知的丶迷一样的世界，体验从未有过的“森林之夜”，步入一个全新的，令人激动的“原始野生的夜晚”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14549496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549496" style="width: 437px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549496-Picture7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14549496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549496-Picture7.jpg" alt="" width="437" b="246" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549496" class="wp-caption-text">迷一样的美丽夜晚。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14549495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549495" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549495-Picture8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14549495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549495-Picture8-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549495" class="wp-caption-text">Tassin 女士亲自照料小动物。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些小动物们的性情都非常温和，在Tassin的精心照料下很愿意与人亲近。怎么样，你是不是也想来体验一下呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_14549494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549494" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549494-Picture9.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14549494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549494-Picture9-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549494" class="wp-caption-text">孔雀开屏——袋鼠也爱看（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14549493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549493" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549493-Picture10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14549493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549493-Picture10-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549493" class="wp-caption-text">澳洲特有动物——袋鼠。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14549492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549492" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549492-Picture11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14549492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549492-Picture11-600x270.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549492" class="wp-caption-text">新野生动成员加入，来自遥远的非洲。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14549491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549491" style="width: 353px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549491-Picture12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14549491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549491-Picture12.jpg" alt="" width="353" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549491" class="wp-caption-text">动物园园区导览图。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了野生小动物，园内还有非常多澳洲农场特有的小动物，比如羊驼。大家在动物园入口可以从Tassin那里花一块钱买一包羊驼最喜欢吃的零食就可以喂给它们了。</p>
<p>院内设有小卖部，可以买到喜欢的小纪念品。 园区内无餐厅，仅有一间小卖部于上午10点至下午4点营业，食品库存有限，尽可自备食物，在院内享用午餐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14549503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14549503" style="width: 554px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549503-Picture13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14549503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14549503-Picture13.jpg" alt="" width="554" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14549503" class="wp-caption-text">享受休闲时光好去处。（澳洲徒步野生动物园提供）</figcaption></figure>
