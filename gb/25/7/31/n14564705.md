<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大阪夜里散步 看城市同时也在看世界</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564716-784820-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>从戎桥望向心斋桥筋，满满人潮涌入这条购物街，体验大阪不夜城的热力。（何美慧提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月31日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾报导）夜里拉长的身影走在灯火通明的心斋桥街头，这一晚，云林平原社区大学校长何美慧没有搭车、也没有刻意安排路线，而是选择以脚步感受大阪的夜晚。她笑着说：“旅行的时候，我喜欢晚上自己出去走一走。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14564712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564712" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564712-784816.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564712-784816-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564712" class="wp-caption-text">夜色中的“久左卫门”街口，计程车静候游人，巷道里灯光交错，城市节奏放缓成旅人的脚步。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14564706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564706" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564706-784810.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564706-784810-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564706" class="wp-caption-text">大阪串炸老店“串カツ田中”灯火通明，晚间依旧座无虚席，串炸香气扑鼻。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饭店不是重点 位置才是这次的住宿原以为是喜来登，结果来到的是万豪集团旗下的“Four Points Flex”。何美慧说，虽然只是三星旅馆，但地点选得好，就在心斋桥交通方便的区段，走到哪都很近。“其实房间舒不舒服其次，最重要是晚上我可以自己走出去看看。”她说，住在蛋黄区，让她可以直接融入城市节奏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14564709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564709" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564709-784813.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564709-784813-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564709" class="wp-caption-text">心斋桥一隅的开放式建筑空间，在暖黄灯光与绿意中错落，仿佛夜里的秘密花园。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14564711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564711" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564711-784815.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564711-784815-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="603" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564711" class="wp-caption-text">高耸的Candeo酒店灯光闪烁，拥有舒适住宿与绝佳地理位置，是探索大阪的不二之选。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14564708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564708-784812.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14564708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564708-784812-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564708" class="wp-caption-text">整齐陈列的YAMAHA Pacifica电吉他，如艺术品般排列，仿佛是音乐爱好者的梦幻殿堂。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从名牌街到热闹巷弄晚上九点多，她从饭店出来，走上御堂筋。这条大路两旁都是名牌店，像是LV、PRADA、IWC等等，每家店的橱窗都设计得很用心。“比起东京表参道，这里的气势还更强一点。”人行道宽，虽然有很多灯饰，但风还是吹得进来。她说，那种微风让人在夏天的夜晚走起来不会太闷。拐进旁边的巷弄，就是截然不同的世界。何美慧走过串炸店、章鱼烧店、居酒屋，还有那家招牌是大螃蟹模型的餐厅“かに道乐”，“每个人经过都要拍照，我也拍了一张。”她笑说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14564710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564710" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564710-784814.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564710-784814-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564710" class="wp-caption-text">高级时尚品牌Louis Vuitton与Prada于心斋桥夜里点灯，玻璃帷幕闪耀，成为都市中最耀眼的风景线。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14564707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564707" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564707-784811.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564707-784811-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564707" class="wp-caption-text">黄灯高挂的章鱼烧小铺飘着面糊香，摊前的手写菜单与手工制作的模样。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>老房子像在说故事她绕进了一栋古味浓厚的大楼，看到里面贴着全馆1000日圆的活动，很多年轻人在翻找衣服，气氛很轻松。再走出来，刚好看到一栋百年老屋“HARIJYU”，木造建筑很显眼，虽然周围全是现代建物，但它就这样站在那里，一动也不动。“很像一位不说话的老人，看着城市变来变去，但他不变。”她这么形容。后面是松竹座，老戏院的外观非常气派。再往前是心斋桥筋入口广场，“那边的人非常多，走在里面会听到很多北京话，感觉像被中国观光团包围。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14564721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564721" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564721-784826.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564721-784826-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564721" class="wp-caption-text">绿光映照下的“はり重”木造立面招牌，透露出一股古早味与低调的职人精神。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14564722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564722-784827.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564722-784827-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564722" class="wp-caption-text">大阪难波著名的“松竹座”，以巴洛克风格建筑展现百年戏剧历史，是关西艺文与表演艺术的重要场域。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后被两只凤凰吸引住她原本准备走回旅馆，经过大丸百货时停下来。橱窗里展示着两只非常特别的金银凤凰，像是在燃烧，又像在飞舞。这是艺术家名和晃平的作品，叫做《Ho-Oh》。何美慧看了很久，说那雕刻细节非常惊人，像火焰又像羽毛。“那真的是我这晚最感动的地方，一栋300多年历史的百货公司，展示这么厉害的艺术品，真的很有意思。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14564719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564719" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564719-784823.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564719-784823-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564719" class="wp-caption-text">作品由名和晃平打造，展现出静中带动、光中藏焰的视觉力量。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14564720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564720" style="width: 435px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564720-784824.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564720-784824-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="435" b="580" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564720" class="wp-caption-text">橱窗内另一件展示作品，名为〈天草白磁〉，以黑白对比与竹构成为主题，展现出日本当代简约之美。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14564718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564718" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564718-784822.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564718-784822-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="604" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564718" class="wp-caption-text">名和晃平（Kohei Nawa）设计的金色凤凰雕塑，闪耀于大丸百货橱窗内，融合灵性与现代艺术之美。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走路 会看到不同的风景这一晚她总共走了三个多小时，不是刻意安排什么景点，但她说反而看到更多。“人、店、建筑、声音，甚至是风、空气味道……都会留下印象。”她说，这就是她喜欢晚上散步的原因。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14564717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564717" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564717-784821.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564717" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564717-784821-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="604" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564717" class="wp-caption-text">宗右卫门町 Soemon-cho 夜市街道。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14564715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14564715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564715-784819.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14564715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14564715-784819-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14564715" class="wp-caption-text">昭和四十六年创业的元祖スタミナ丼，以硕大碗公造型招牌吸引目光，是老字号的饱足保证。（何美慧提供）</figcaption></figure>
