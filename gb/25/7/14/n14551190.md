<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>恋上花莲夏天 视觉与心灵的轻声对话</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551202-780703-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>陈明忠说：“拍这些照片，不是为了证明花莲有多美，而是希望大家记得这里的节奏。”（陈明忠提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）当第一道晨光从太平洋边际攀上东海岸，花莲的清晨，像是天地间默契的邀约，一场视觉与心灵的轻声对话。摄影家陈明忠站在湿润的砾石岸边，按下快门的刹那，他用影像捕捉了这座城市最温柔的时刻。</p>
<p>这不是刻意安排的旅程，而是熟悉土地召唤之下的自然回归。从花莲港边的浪头到港边的红灯塔，从冲浪少年破浪而出的瞬间，到划着独木舟的旅人静静滑过黎明余晖，这些画面并非观光广告里的华丽滤镜，而是真真实实发生在花莲人与这片海洋之间的日常。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551197" style="width: 472px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551197-780698.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551197-780698-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="472" b="629" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551197" class="wp-caption-text">日出朝霞染红海面，浪花轻拂砾滩，一颗石头静静伫立，仿佛听见大海的低语。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551191" style="width: 444px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551191-780692.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551191-780692-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="444" b="592" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551191" class="wp-caption-text">花莲港的碎浪拍石，晨光乍现。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>冲浪与日光共舞的片刻</h2>
<p>清晨，花莲东岸已经被一层金蓝相交的光线包裹。少年脱下T恤，光着膀子推着立式冲浪板，站进拍岸的浪中。他不是为了炫技或表演，而是为了与海保持一种连结。这里没有商业气息的沙滩伞，只有踏实的礁岩与泡沫。那一刻，波光闪烁，浪花像是海洋给予的掌声。</p>
<p>陈明忠将手机镜头拉远，少年身影在浪峰之上像一笔挥洒自如的书法，力与美，安静地并存。他说：“这样的画面，不需要任何修饰，它本来就美得令人屏息。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551206" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551206" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551206-780712.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14551206" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551206-780712-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551206" class="wp-caption-text">波光闪烁，浪花像是海洋给予的掌声。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551196" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551196-780697.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14551196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551196-780697-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551196" class="wp-caption-text">冲浪者奋力冲进浪中，那是一场与自我对话的旅程，也是一段与海的契约。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>天空与海面上的浪漫行旅</h2>
<p>而另一侧，几艘彩色独木舟缓缓划入海中，背后是晨曦中微亮的城市轮廓。船桨划过水面，激起一圈圈涟漪，那是人与自然沟通的方式之一。夕阳的余焰还未完全退场，橘红与紫蓝交织的天际倒映在水面，整座海仿佛成了画布。</p>
<p>岸边的队员们穿着救生衣，互相招呼着，像是准备启程的冒险者，却多了几分从容与信赖。他们的笑声轻轻地飘上来，没有喧闹，只有一种热爱生活的声音。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551198" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551198-780699.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14551198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551198-780699-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551198" class="wp-caption-text">成排独木舟在晨光中前行，远方是点亮的花莲市景。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551195" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551195-780696.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14551195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551195-780696-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551195" class="wp-caption-text">准备上场，划破晨光的第一道浪。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>马背与晨光之间的邂逅</h2>
<p>沿着溪口转向沙洲，晨曦中，摄影家陈明忠将露营车停靠在海滩，看到两匹马稳稳地踏着沙泥缓步前行。这不是电影场景，而是花莲人的生活诗篇。</p>
<p>马儿的倒影映在潮间带的湿地上，彩霞染红了整片水面。骑士坐姿挺直，与马的节奏同步，就像是这片土地的守望者。一位女孩牵着马，停在水洼旁，她回头的瞬间，与云层里的霞光交会出一种沉静的幸福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551200" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551200-780701.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14551200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551200-780701-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551200" class="wp-caption-text">粉橘晨光映照水面，两匹马与骑士站在小岛沙洲上，恍若梦境，实则真实存在于花莲的清晨。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551194" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551194-780695.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14551194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551194-780695-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551194" class="wp-caption-text">出海口美丽的云彩。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551199" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551199-780700.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14551199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551199-780700-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551199" class="wp-caption-text">马影与车影交会的海之曙光。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>不只是风景 这是花莲的节奏</h2>
<p>陈明忠说：“拍这些照片，不是为了证明花莲有多美，而是希望大家记得这里的节奏。”</p>
<p>花莲的美，不只是山海风光，也不只是晨昏光影的变幻，而是这里人们与自然共处的方式：顺着海的节拍生活、靠着天的颜色作息。</p>
<p>疫情与风灾、地震，曾让这座城市沉寂，游客锐减，生计一度受挫。但如今，海浪依旧翻涌，太阳照常升起，花莲已准备好，迎接每一位愿意用心感受她的旅人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551203-780704.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14551203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551203-780704-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551203" class="wp-caption-text">疫情与风灾、地震，曾让这座城市沉寂，游客锐减，生计一度受挫。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一起让花莲 再度发光</h2>
<p>邀请您再访花莲，不只是为了拍下一张美照，更是为了与在地人一起守护这片土地的呼吸节奏。带上家人朋友，来一趟不疾不徐的清晨旅行；放下繁忙节奏，倾听浪声、看一场日出，让心跟着花莲重新启动。</p>
<p>用双脚重新踏上这片土地，也让花莲的观光与生活，再度闪耀。</p>
