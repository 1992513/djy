<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>盛夏黄金峡谷沁凉旅程 南澳碧旦溪溯溪记</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551099-780619-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>陈锦尧说：“这趟行程能平安完成，要感谢同行勇士们彼此帮助，每一次拉手、每一声提醒，都是我们这趟旅程最珍贵的风景。”（陈锦尧提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾宜兰报导）炎夏七月，摄影家陈锦尧带着镜头与装备，走进宜兰南澳山区的碧旦溪，记录这条有“黄金峡谷”之称的溪谷风貌。尽管近日中南部频繁降雨，北部却是久违的好天气，也让这趟溯溪之旅显得格外晴朗透彻。</p>
<p>“漂漂河都划不动了！”陈锦尧在现场笑说，碧旦溪此日水量偏少，但阳光洒落在石壁与溪水间，反而让岩面矿物纹理更为鲜明，层层金橘色泽如画如梦，成为此次溯溪最令人惊艳的天然背景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551102" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551102-780623.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551102-780623-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="803" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551102" class="wp-caption-text">尽管近日中南部频繁降雨，北部却是久违的好天气，也让这趟溯溪之旅显得格外晴朗透彻。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551098" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551098-780618.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551098-780618-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="798" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551098" class="wp-caption-text">安全为先，共同完成的冒险旅程。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>峡谷壮丽 水声与欢笑交织</h2>
<p>这趟行程巧遇社团山友们同行，大家从一开始的警戒紧绷，到后来在溪中自在漂浮、相互嬉戏，彼此间的信任与合作，也在攀岩与戏水中逐渐建立起来。</p>
<p>几位队员齐坐在小瀑布上，任凭溪水拍打身旁，笑声与水花齐飞。过程中，有人紧抓绳索攀登湿滑石壁，一旁有人在水中接应，每一步都凝聚了伙伴间的默契与信念。</p>
<p>摄影家表示，由于手机装入防水套内不易横握，此行照片皆以直式构图为主，“但也因此更贴近现场人的表情与岩壁的纹理。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551093" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551093-780613.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551093-780613-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="803" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551093" class="wp-caption-text">小瀑布成为天然溜滑梯，队员们在惊呼中滑行而下。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551094" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551094-780614.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551094" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551094-780614-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551094" class="wp-caption-text">攀绳、戏水、穿越峡谷，每一步都是与自然对话的方式。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>不只是冒险 更是与自然的对话</h2>
<p>碧旦溪以其蜿蜒穿梭的水道与千层岩壁闻名，色泽由墨绿转为赭红，峡谷两侧笔直挺立，仿佛步入一条时间冻结的天然画廊。溯溪队伍行经处，或拉绳而上、或水中横越、或静静仰望石壁的纹路，无不为大自然的雕工所折服。</p>
<p>行进间的短暂停歇，也成为彼此交流的时光。有人躺在水中放空漂浮，有人则在幽暗石缝中拍下嬉戏镜头，一名参与者说：“这里的水不深，但沁入心脾。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551103" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551103-780625.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551103-780625-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="805" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551103" class="wp-caption-text">走进宜兰南澳山区的碧旦溪，记录这条有“黄金峡谷”之称的溪谷风貌。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551096" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551096-780616.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551096-780616-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551096" class="wp-caption-text">独坐岩边，享受峡谷静谧，让自己与大自然同步呼吸。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551092" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551092" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551092-780612.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551092" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551092-780612-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551092" class="wp-caption-text">南澳碧旦溪岩壁前，山友们合影留念，色彩斑斓的岩层映衬着笑容。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>安全为先 共同完成的冒险旅程</h2>
<p>陈锦尧也特别提到：“这趟行程能平安完成，要感谢同行勇士们彼此帮助，每一次拉手、每一声提醒，都是我们这趟旅程最珍贵的风景。”</p>
<p>照片中不难看出多元年龄层的参与者，从银发族到年轻冒险者，每个人脸上都洋溢着挑战后的成就感。这是一场不需竞争的冒险，只需要彼此扶持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551097" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551097-780617.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551097-780617-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551097" class="wp-caption-text">峡谷探险。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551095" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551095-780615.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14551095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551095-780615-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551095" class="wp-caption-text">同行山友合影。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>峡谷记忆 在夏日定格</h2>
<p>午后时分，峡谷的光影逐渐转淡，溪水也渐渐沉静。结束这趟旅程后，山友们陆续离开溪谷，身上湿漉漉，却带着难以言喻的满足。</p>
<p>陈锦尧最后写下：“谢谢太阳、溪水与山友，让这趟旅程，在我记忆里闪闪发光。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551100" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551100-780620.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551100-780620-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="798" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551100" class="wp-caption-text">山友们陆续离开溪谷，身上湿漉漉，却带着难以言喻的满足。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
