<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马祖·东引 星光下的海味岛屿</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551110-776590-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>星光下的海味岛屿：马祖东引。（陈竑任提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾马祖报导）当银河在夜色中洒落，当红糟香在锅里慢慢酝酿，一座岛屿的灵魂，就这么在视觉与味觉中悄然苏醒。“东引”是马祖列岛最北的国境前哨，不只有军事史与壮丽海崖，在陈竑任的镜头与脚步下，它更是一场专属于旅人的五感盛宴。</p>
<figure id="14551125" aria-describedby="caption-14551125" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551125-776606-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551125-776606-450x600.jpg" alt="东引岛上的景点之一。" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551125" class="wp-caption-text">东引岛上的景点之一。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>岛上星光 摄影师眼中的银河之道</h2>
<p>“如果你够幸运，就会在东引的夜晚，看见银河降临大海。”</p>
<figure id="14551112" aria-describedby="caption-14551112" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551112-776593-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551112-776593-450x300.jpg" alt="如果你够幸运，就会在东引的夜晚，看见银河降临大海。" width="449" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551112" class="wp-caption-text">如果你够幸运，就会在东引的夜晚，看见银河降临大海。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈竑任一边调整相机快门，一边带领旅人走向义坑海崖。这里，是岛上最适合观星的地点之一。无光害、无干扰，只有满天星斗与海风呼啸。他说，那晚的银河，如同从天而降的瀑布，静静悬挂在东海之上，倒映在漆黑海面，如梦如幻。</p>
<p>在另一侧的战地公路旁，他拍下公路蜿蜒与山势融合的画面，远方天际依然闪着银白色的星光。这样的画面，不靠修图，只靠等待与执著。</p>
<p>“如果你够幸运，就会在东引的夜晚，看见银河降临大海。”陈竑任说。</p>
<figure id="14551113" aria-describedby="caption-14551113" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551113-776594-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551113-776594-450x300.jpg" alt="无光害、无干扰，只有满天星斗与海风呼啸。" width="452" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551113" class="wp-caption-text">无光害、无干扰，只有满天星斗与海风呼啸。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>曙光初现 国旗、日出与历史并列的清晨</h2>
<p>在晨光尚未爬上山头前，陈竑任已在“国之北疆”的牌楼下就定位。这里可眺望“毋忘在莒”军事碑亭，也是日出时最感人的场景之一。</p>
<figure id="14551114" aria-describedby="caption-14551114" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551114-776595-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551114-776595-450x338.jpg" alt="这里可眺望“毋忘在莒”军事碑亭，也是日出时最感人的场景之一。" width="451" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551114" class="wp-caption-text">这里可眺望“毋忘在莒”军事碑亭，也是日出时最感人的场景之一。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>太阳缓缓升起，将红白国旗染上金边，海平线渐渐由暗转亮。那一刻，他的快门声几乎无声，因为太庄严了，连呼吸都得放轻。</p>
<p>“这不只是美景，更是一座岛的日常节奏。东引人，每天都在第一道阳光中迎战生活。”</p>
<figure id="14551115" aria-describedby="caption-14551115" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551115-776596-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551115-776596-450x338.jpg" alt="东引人，每天都在第一道阳光中迎战生活。" width="449" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551115" class="wp-caption-text">东引人，每天都在第一道阳光中迎战生活。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>从坑道到便当店 战地文化的美味延续</h2>
<p>“泰利食堂，是这座岛的味觉心脏。”陈竑任这么形容。</p>
<p>走进这间军事风格浓厚的小店，旅人会被红糟、鱼汤与炸物的香气包围。一份红糟猪肉堡，一口咬下是咸香中带微甜，猪肉嚼劲中蕴藏马祖特有的腌渍工法，那股淡淡的酒香，像是军中伙房走出来的小惊喜。</p>
<p>接着上桌的是凉拌淡菜，一颗颗饱满的橘红贝肉，铺陈在艺术感十足的蛤壳盘上，旁边点缀洋芋片，让人几乎不忍入口，但香辣微酸的酱汁勾人魂魄，吃了才知什么叫“一口入魂”。</p>
<figure id="14551118" aria-describedby="caption-14551118" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551118-776599-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551118-776599-450x338.jpg" alt="凉拌淡菜。" width="451" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551118" class="wp-caption-text">凉拌淡菜。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14551116" aria-describedby="caption-14551116" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551116-776597-450x294.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551116-776597-450x294.jpg" alt="“泰利食堂，是这座岛的味觉心脏。”陈竑任这么形容。" width="451" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551116" class="wp-caption-text">“泰利食堂，是这座岛的味觉心脏。”陈竑任这么形容。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海味全开 蒸鱼与炸小卷的鲜甜协奏</p>
<p>泰利食堂的主菜，不是浮夸的大菜，而是每一道都蕴含着渔村节奏的诚意：</p>
<p>姜丝鱼汤：汤头清澈带乳白，加入当日现捞海鱼、海带、姜丝与青葱，鲜香扑鼻，喝下去像在海中深呼吸。</p>
<figure id="14551117" aria-describedby="caption-14551117" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551117-776598-450x343.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551117-776598-450x343.jpg" alt="姜丝鱼汤。" width="451" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551117" class="wp-caption-text">姜丝鱼汤。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>粉丝虾煲：三只大虾伫立锅中，汤汁融合蒜香与虾膏精华，粉丝饱吸汤汁，柔软中带弹。</p>
<p>清蒸石斑鱼：用最简单的方式蒸出最丰富的层次，蒜蓉、酱油与青葱画龙点睛，一尾鱼，吃出一个港口的故事。</p>
<figure id="14551121" aria-describedby="caption-14551121" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551121-776602-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551121-776602-450x338.jpg" alt="清蒸石斑鱼。" width="451" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551121" class="wp-caption-text">清蒸石斑鱼。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走进这间军事风格浓厚的小店，旅人会被红糟、鱼汤与炸物的香气包围。</p>
<figure id="14551120" aria-describedby="caption-14551120" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551120-776601-450x349.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551120-776601-450x349.jpg" alt="粉丝虾煲。" width="450" b="349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551120" class="wp-caption-text">粉丝虾煲。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>“炸到酥、炒到香” 街头的豪迈吃法</h2>
<p>在地人最推荐的，还有那盘炸红魽小卷——外层炸得酥脆，里面则仍保有海鲜的原汁原味。没有多余调味，只靠海味与火候征服味蕾。</p>
<figure id="14551119" aria-describedby="caption-14551119" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551119-776600-450x359.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551119-776600-450x359.jpg" alt="在地人推荐：炸红魽小卷。" width="449" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14551119" class="wp-caption-text">在地人推荐：炸红魽小卷。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有九层塔姜丝炒海瓜子、鱼露炒地瓜叶，每一口都像回到阿嬷厨房。</p>
<p>餐后甜品，是老酒蛋花汤。微甜中带酒香，是一种让人瞬间昏昏欲睡的舒缓力道——像这座岛，外表强悍，内心温柔。</p>

<h2>东引 是一场值得细细品味的旅行</h2>
<p>在陈竑任的带领下，东引不再只是“最北的7-Eleven”、不只是战地遗迹或前线据点，而是一场结合银河与海味的感官旅程。</p>
<p>“走进东引，不只要看，更要吃。”他笑着说。</p>
