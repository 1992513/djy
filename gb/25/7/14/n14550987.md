<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>利维拉里亚?莱罗书店：葡萄牙最富丽堂皇的书店</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550990-shutterstock_1348224422-1080x720-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在莱罗书店令人惊叹的主体空间中，华丽的装潢细节带来丰富的视觉体验，这使得书籍反倒退居次位。空间的正中央是引人注目、叉形曲折而上的楼梯，深红色的台阶两旁衬托着雕花扶手和栏杆。从一楼可以直接看到二楼天花板壮观的彩色玻璃。落地木质的书架以精心制作的大型四叶草雕纹隔开，有些书格是开放的，有的则用玻璃轻掩。（Nido Huebl/Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
<p>游客们常用“神奇”来形容葡萄牙波尔图的利维拉里亚?莱罗书店（Livraria Lello in Porto, Portugal）。店面的外观虽然低调，却有着独特的窗饰（石雕）和葡萄牙极具标志性的的彩绘瓷砖。书店内部的装潢华美丰富：精巧的木工、鲜艳的彩绘玻璃，以及精心雕琢的复杂灰泥工艺。</p>
<p>莱罗书店由葡萄牙工程师法兰西斯科?赛维尔?埃斯特维斯（Francisco Xavier Esteves）于1906年设计，他融合了两种主流风格，呈现出独特的结构巧思。其主要建筑风格为新哥特式，大量采用尖拱，并以玫瑰花饰和枝叶图案点缀，营造出恢弘的中世纪教堂式室内氛围。同时，楼梯奢华流畅的线条则体现了新艺术之风，以有机形态和线性节奏模拟自然。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551007" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551007-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551007-1-450x395.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="529"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551007" class="wp-caption-text">相较于两侧相邻的店铺，莱罗书店新哥特风格的外观相当突出，这包括二楼三扇窗户上方的窗饰以及尖塔，或称小尖顶。此外，经典的新哥特式四叶草图案装饰着两侧狭窄的柱状结构，将书店正面与两栋相邻的建筑区隔开来。宽阔的、装饰性的石雕拱门勾勒出书店的正门。（Diego Grandi/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551008-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14551008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551008-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551008" class="wp-caption-text">大楼梯底部曲线优美，体现了新艺术风格，以金色雕花树叶图案装饰，与淡蓝色的背景形成鲜明对比，尽显奢华。在标志性的红色阶梯两侧，是科林斯式木质立柱，柱顶饰有两层莨苕叶纹。（Andrea Izzotti/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551009-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14551009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551009-3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551009" class="wp-caption-text">二楼是开放式书廊，可以从红色叉形楼梯的左右两侧进入。光线透过巨大的彩绘玻璃天窗倾泻而下，天花板和墙壁可见精巧的灰泥工艺及拱肩状的细节，凸显书店的设计艺术。（R.M. Nunes/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551010-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14551010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551010-4-600x273.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="273"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551010" class="wp-caption-text">书店的天花板设计旨在呈现手工雕刻的木质外观，蓝金色的灰泥装饰令人惊艳。扇形和玫瑰花饰是设计中融入的新哥特式和新艺术风格元素。（Florin Cnejevici/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551011" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551011-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551011-5-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551011" class="wp-caption-text">二楼的天窗由荷兰彩绘玻璃艺术家塞缪尔?凡?克里肯（Samuel Van Krieken）设计。这扇天窗因其尺寸（26英尺乘11英尺）及强烈的色彩而成为室内的焦点。天窗中央刻有拉丁文“Decus in Labore”，意为“工作中的荣耀”，这是书店的座右铭。（Felix Lipov/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551012-6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14551012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551012-6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551012" class="wp-caption-text">在左右两边的哥特式画框内，有两位分别代表艺术与科学的女性肖像画，画框上方有两个四叶草图案。二楼的三扇窗户上方悬挂着一幅新艺术风格的画作，“莱罗兄弟”的字样点缀著书店的入口。（Kiev.Victor/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
