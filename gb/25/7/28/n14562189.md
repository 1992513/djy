<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯房市出现两极化 业主喜忧参半</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年07月28日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">最新数据显示，珀斯的房地产市场正在经历过山车般的走势，整个大都会地区的房价出现了大幅上涨与下跌的两极化现象。</span></p>
<p>据《今日珀斯》报导，价格的波动性是房市晚期繁荣周期中的常见特征，因为一些郊区继续上涨，而另一些在超出合理价值后开始回落。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">临河郊区</span><span lang="en-US">Applecross</span><span lang="zh-TW">的房价涨幅最大，在上一个财政年度上涨了</span><span lang="en-US">29.3%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，中位价从</span><span lang="en-US">215</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元涨至当前的</span><span lang="en-US">278</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">West Leederville</span><span lang="zh-TW">的房主获得了第二高的利润，在过去一年里房价上涨了约</span><span lang="en-US">50</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，涨幅为</span><span lang="en-US">34%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，中位价达到了</span><span lang="en-US">189</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">西澳房地产协会数据显示，在上一个财政年度，房价按比例计算涨幅最大的郊区是</span><span lang="en-US">Bateman</span><span lang="zh-TW">，涨幅达</span><span lang="en-US">37%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，即中位价上涨了</span><span lang="en-US">35</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元至</span><span lang="en-US">130</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Bicton</span><span lang="zh-TW">区单元房的涨幅最高，中位价从</span><span lang="en-US">49.5</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元上涨至</span><span lang="en-US">77</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，涨幅高达</span><span lang="en-US">55.6%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。此外，</span><span lang="en-US">Hamilton Hill</span><span lang="zh-TW">的单元房价格也上涨了</span><span lang="en-US">43%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span><span lang="en-US">53.5</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Orelia</span><span lang="zh-TW">的单元房——作为珀斯最便宜的郊区之一——价格也上涨了</span><span lang="en-US">39%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span><span lang="en-US">32</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<h2>去年房价下跌的区</h2>
<p>但对于珀斯业主而言，并非都是好消息，过去一个财政年度内，有七个区的房价出现了下跌。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Claremont</span><span lang="zh-TW">的房价一年内下跌了</span><span lang="en-US">13%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，即减少了</span><span lang="en-US">30</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，中位价降至</span><span lang="en-US">201</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元；</span><span lang="en-US">Cottesloe</span><span lang="zh-TW">的房价下跌了</span><span lang="en-US">10%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，即减少了</span><span lang="en-US">35</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，中位价降至</span><span lang="en-US">315</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p>西部地区的一些热门郊区的房价在过去一年内也下跌，包括<span lang="en-US">Kallaroo</span>（下降<span lang="en-US">0.08%</span>）、<span lang="en-US">Marmion</span>（下降<span lang="en-US">1.9%</span>）、<span lang="en-US">North Fremantle</span>（下降<span lang="en-US">3.4%</span>）、<span lang="en-US">North Beach</span>（下降<span lang="en-US">5%</span>）以及<span lang="en-US">Dalkeith</span>（下降<span lang="en-US">5.7%</span>）</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">不过，在整个大珀斯地区，房价总体变化相对温和，上涨了</span><span lang="en-US">16.4%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，中位价为</span><span lang="en-US">78.6</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">由于购房能力受限，单元房市场的表现超过了独立房，中位价上涨了</span><span lang="en-US">20%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span><span lang="en-US">54</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p>据称独立屋和单元房市场的价格高峰至少已经过去。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯独立房中位价的年增长率在</span><span lang="en-US">2024</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">月达到</span><span lang="en-US">25%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的峰值，而单元房中位价的年增长率则在</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">1</span><span lang="zh-TW">月达到</span><span lang="en-US">22.9%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的峰值。</span></p>
