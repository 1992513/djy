<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳弱势用户被多收电费230万</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年07月28日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者夏墨竹澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">最近，西澳政府拥有的能源零售与发电商</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">公司遭到投诉，称其自</span><span lang="en-US">2009</span><span lang="zh-TW">年以来，向</span><span lang="en-US">2845</span><span lang="zh-TW">名最弱势客户，多收了总计</span><span lang="en-US">229</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的电费。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据“</span><span lang="en-US">WA Today</span><span lang="zh-TW">”网站报导，在西澳公用事业监管机构经济监管局（</span><span lang="en-US">Economic Regulation Authority</span><span lang="zh-TW">）接到投诉后，</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">公司被迫退还多收的款项并道歉。</span></p>
<p>该机构发现，<span lang="en-US">Synergy</span>因未在<span lang="en-US">10</span>个工作日内告知客户被多收费用，违反了其行为准则。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span>向该机构报告称，尽管客户的电力账户已被关闭，它仍继续接受客户的<span lang="en-US">Centrepay</span>付款。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Centrelink</span>用户可以开设<span lang="en-US">Centrepay</span>账户，在收到剩余款项之前扣除水电等公用事业费用。</p>
<p>经济监管局表示，这相当于自<span lang="en-US">2009</span>年以来多收了约<span lang="en-US">229</span>万澳元——约<span lang="en-US">1000</span>名客户被多收超过<span lang="en-US">500</span>澳元。<span lang="en-US">Synergy</span>表示，大约一半的退款金额不超过<span lang="en-US">250</span>澳元。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">管理局主席埃德威尔（</span><span lang="en-US">Steve Edwell</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，鉴于</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">作为该州最大零售商的地位，且客户依赖</span><span lang="en-US">Centrelink</span><span lang="zh-TW">的补贴，此次违规行为尤其令人担忧。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">公司已收到强制执行通知，要求其退还自</span><span lang="en-US">2023</span><span lang="zh-TW">年以来对</span><span lang="en-US">459</span><span lang="zh-TW">名客户多收取的</span><span lang="en-US">24</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。经济监管局表示，预计该公司将偿还全部</span><span lang="en-US">229</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">自</span><span lang="en-US">4</span><span lang="zh-TW">月份开始联系受影响客户以来，</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">公司已偿还了</span><span lang="en-US">24</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元欠款中的约</span><span lang="en-US">30%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">反对党能源发言人托马斯（</span><span lang="en-US">Steve Thomas</span><span lang="zh-TW">）称此次多收费为</span><span lang="en-US">15</span><span lang="zh-TW">年的系统性失败。</span></p>
<p>能源厅厅长桑德森（<span lang="en-US">Amber-JadeSanderson</span>）表示，<span lang="en-US">Centrepay</span>的客户应该相信该系统正在为他们的最佳利益服务。她表示，她已启动对计费系统的独立审查，并得到<span lang="en-US">Synergy</span>的保证，他们正在努力识别所有受影响的客户。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">公司首席执行官贝克（</span><span lang="en-US">Kurt Baker</span><span lang="zh-TW">）为此道歉。</span></p>
<p>贝克表示，<span lang="en-US">Synergy</span>正在审查其系统，以查找更多超额付款情况。</p>
