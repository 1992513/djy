<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：迎美国独立日 波士顿海港节开幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543657-DSC08058-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025年度波士顿海港节（Boston Harborfest）7月2日开幕，政要嘉宾们向国旗敬礼。（刘景烨／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年07月03日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）美国独立日（Independence Day）前夕，波士顿海港节（Boston Harborfest）7月2日在市中心开幕，为居民和游客带来娱乐节目、历史导览等数以百计的活动。</p>
<p>“波士顿海港节是庆祝波士顿的航海和革命历史的，令人自豪的传统。”海港节官网介绍说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543655" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543655" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543655-DSC08054.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543655" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543655-DSC08054-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543655" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿海港节7月2日开幕，吸引许多游客围观。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14543653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543653-DSC08044.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543653-DSC08044-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543653" class="wp-caption-text">2025年度波士顿海港节7月2日开幕。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开幕式当天中午，波士顿市中心梅西百货（Macy’s）室外的广场搭建了舞台。随着主持人宣布节庆开幕，美国海军仪仗队持国旗进场，穿着殖民时期服装的“米德尔塞克斯县志愿者”（Middlesex County Volunteers）乐队奏响美国国歌。</p>
<p>嘉宾致词之后，工作人员在舞台下切开“Boston Harborfest 2025”巨大红蓝色蛋糕，分给周围的游客，带来愉快、热烈的气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543654-DSC08049.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543654-DSC08049-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543654" class="wp-caption-text">乐队演奏美国国歌。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14543652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543652-DSC08034.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543652-DSC08034-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543652" class="wp-caption-text">现场准备了巨大的蛋糕，庆祝美国生日。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一名来自麻州马尔伯勒（Marlborough）的男游客高兴地说：“喔！这个蛋糕非常棒。我很高兴能成为海港节的一部分，享受各种活动。”</p>
<p>另一名来自加拿大多伦多的女游客惊呼：“这个蛋糕看起来是引起轰动的，我走过来时简直难以置信，他们做得很棒！”她还称赞，这场海港节开幕式，非常有爱国情怀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543667-DSC08163.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543667-DSC08163-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543667" class="wp-caption-text">工作人员向游客们分发蛋糕。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14543660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543660" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543660-DSC08090.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543660-DSC08090-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543660" class="wp-caption-text">海军士兵们参加活动。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>根据海港节网站信息，从7月2日到4日期间，波士顿地区将有诸多历史导览活动，例如自由之路、宪法号博物馆、麻州政府大楼、邦克山纪念碑等历史景点的导览；也有一系列公共娱乐节目，例如梅西百货“伟大美国”时装秀、啤酒花园、独立日游行、音乐会和烟火表演等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543656" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543656-DSC08057.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543656" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543656-DSC08057-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543656" class="wp-caption-text">“遇见波士顿”（Meet Boston）副主席David O&#8217;Donnell欢迎来宾。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>政要和军官参与庆典</strong></h3>
<p>
海港节的开幕式，由“波士顿市中心联盟”（Downtown Boston Alliance）和“遇见波士顿”（Meet Boston）团体主办。来到现场致词的嘉宾，有波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）、墨菲（Erin Murphy）；市政府旅游、体育和娱乐主任博德斯（John Borders IV）；有美国海军宪法号（USS Constitution）和唐纳德?库克号（USS Donald Cook）军舰，以及海岸警卫队的代表；也有英国驻波士顿总领事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543659-DSC08085.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543659-DSC08085-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543659" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员Ed Flynn致词。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14543666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543666-DSC08139.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543666-DSC08139-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543666" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员Erin Murphy致词。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14543658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543658-DSC08080.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543658-DSC08080-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543658" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市政府旅游、体育和娱乐主任John Borders IV致词。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“作为美国的国家之战舰，宪法号是自由民主的鲜活象征，这是由她名字中的那份文件所确立的。虽然她不是美国革命的一部分，但她的木料也带有建国那一代人的遗产。”宪法号军舰指挥官谢弗（Crystal L. Schaefer）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543662-DSC08104.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543662-DSC08104-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543662" class="wp-caption-text">宪法号军舰指挥官Crystal L. Schaefer致词。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14543661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543661-DSC08099.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543661-DSC08099-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543661" class="wp-caption-text">唐纳德?库克号军舰指挥官Roosevelt B. White Jr.致词。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宪法号有200多年历史，是美国海军创立时建造的首批军舰之一，也是目前美国海军舰龄最长的现役军舰。从1797年到1855年，宪法号积极保卫海上航线，在战斗中保持着不败纪录，摧毁或俘虏了总共33艘敌对军舰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543664-DSC08124.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543664-DSC08124-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543664" class="wp-caption-text">美国海岸警卫队波士顿队长Patrick Sullivan致词。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14543665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543665-DSC08136.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543665-DSC08136-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543665" class="wp-caption-text">英国驻波士顿总领事David Clay回顾两国友谊。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>英国驻波士顿总领事克莱（David Clay）强调了两国当今的友谊。“这是我的第一场海港节，第一次在波士顿参加7月4日庆典。我在纽约住过几年，但我从几个可靠的来源处听说，波士顿做得更好一点。”他说。这段赞美引发观众的欢呼和掌声。◇</p>
