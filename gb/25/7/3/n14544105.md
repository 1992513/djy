<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>海边小镇惠特比——炎炎夏日渡假好去处</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月03日讯】 英国正值盛夏，热浪一波接着一波，大家总想要找寻一个纳凉好去处。说到英国人最青睐的英国海边渡假小镇，就不得不提英国东北部大名鼎鼎的惠特比（Whitby）。很多人知道惠特比是因为爱尔兰作家Bram Stoker的小说《德古拉》，因此每年都有许多哥特文化爱好者慕名而来。对于大多数只想短暂休憩一下、看看海的朋友来说，一日游是比较实际的选择。</p>
<p>从利兹、约克或者纽卡斯尔驾车出发，不到两小时便可以到达惠特比。长长的埃斯克河（River Esk）将惠特比小镇分隔成东西两岸。西岸有着长长的惠特比沙滩、海港和鲸鱼骨拱门。沿着埃斯克河岸向海边走去，美丽的海滩便映入眼帘。河对岸古朴的红白相间的房屋鳞次栉比，与山水和谐地融为一体，像是南欧的某个漂亮海滩，色彩和谐又富有层次感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14544106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14544106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544106-208a7a39bd1a334e3a9e1383b485262b-e1751546222582.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14544106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544106-208a7a39bd1a334e3a9e1383b485262b-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14544106" class="wp-caption-text">(惠特比海边一角- Photo by author）</figcaption></figure>
<p>向海里看去，灯塔向着海平面延伸出去，十分显眼地矗立在海中央。时间够充裕的话，去灯塔那边走走，回头一望，惠特比修道院另一个角度的景致就呈现在眼前。如果当天天空足够蓝，海面与天空的交界线都模糊了，水天相接带来的壮丽蓝色让人无法挪开视线。海边一排排彩色小木屋也点缀着海滩，经常来此渡假的英国人会在这里休息和娱乐。吹着海风，吃着冰激凌休闲地在沙滩上散着步，整个人都放松下来了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14544111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14544111" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544111-ebc2db80d492080b209c6f5b71a869b8-e1751546278274.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14544111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544111-ebc2db80d492080b209c6f5b71a869b8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14544111" class="wp-caption-text">(海边的灯塔- Photo by author）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14544114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14544114" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544114-7aaf90bc1b8c078f9fa68030e4de4363-e1751546937931.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14544114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544114-7aaf90bc1b8c078f9fa68030e4de4363-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14544114" class="wp-caption-text">(海边彩色小木屋- Photo by author）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果说惠特比西岸有着令人过目不忘的海景，那东岸拥有的便是悠久的历史文化故事和琳琅满目的商铺。著名的“199级台阶”是来到小镇一定要体验的。长长的台阶通往圣玛丽教堂（Church of Mary）。每走一步回头往下俯瞰，身后的景色都像电影画面一样不断切换，时而像是南法小镇一隅，淡黄色的房屋墙体与石板路烘托出素雅宁静的氛围；时而红白相间的房子就会跳到你的眼前，和波澜壮阔的海面形成一幅唯美的画卷。如果在黄昏时来，应该能在此处欣赏到绝美的落日海景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14544125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14544125" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544125-fe45b7c4d47e28dda6e14270469f36c6-e1751547010353.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14544125" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544125-fe45b7c4d47e28dda6e14270469f36c6-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14544125" class="wp-caption-text">(“199级台阶”- Photo by author）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14544126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14544126" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544126-f91ace6183bce8e775eac516fc8b39af-e1751547074349.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14544126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544126-f91ace6183bce8e775eac516fc8b39af-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14544126" class="wp-caption-text">(小镇一隅- Photo by author）</figcaption></figure>
<p>爬上了台阶后，圣玛丽教堂前的一片墓地就会出现在眼前。途经教堂后再继续往前走，你就会看到著名的惠特比修道院（Whitby Abbey）。有着接近1400年历史的古老哥特式修道院在悬崖峭壁上矗立着，俯瞰着大海。其最初由诺森布里亚国王建立，曾经在早期英国基督教历史上起着关键的作用。修道院历经了战争和气候的摧残，虽如今只能看到遗迹，但你可以在这饱经岁月风霜洗礼的残垣断壁中感受到历史的印记。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14544127" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14544127" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544127-27ab2ecee805c064f88197bfb504f181-e1751547131913.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14544127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544127-27ab2ecee805c064f88197bfb504f181-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14544127" class="wp-caption-text">(小镇一隅2- Photo by author）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14544128" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14544128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544128-057e6d16f5fc695f57abf6f74bb59c2d-e1751547189636.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14544128" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14544128-057e6d16f5fc695f57abf6f74bb59c2d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14544128" class="wp-caption-text">(街头沙雕艺术- Photo by author）</figcaption></figure>
<p>慢慢走下台阶，前方就是各式各样的商铺、传统市场和餐厅。小镇不大，但游客熙熙攘攘，装饰精美、色彩明艳的店铺和咖啡馆让人目不暇接。运气好还能看到街头艺人创作的沙雕艺术。天气太热了的话，停下来找间咖啡馆坐坐，或者去品尝一下全英最好吃的炸鱼薯条也是不错的选择。<br />
赶快来惠特比待上一天吧，放慢节奏，与您的家人一同享受英国的炎炎夏日时光。◇</p>
