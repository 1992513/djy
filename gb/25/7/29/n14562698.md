<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澳价值守护联盟谴责香港当局通缉澳洲公民冯崇义</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562745-e9ff66a9366b342f871c6c6b.jpg" />
<h6>澳洲价值守护联盟（AVA）发表的正式声明，强烈谴责香港当局悬赏缉捕澳洲公民冯崇义。（AVA 网站）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月29日讯】【编者按】近日，香港警方再度援引《港版国安法》，对包括澳洲公民冯崇义博士在内的多名海外异见人士发布悬赏通缉，意图跨境延伸中共式打压，引发澳洲社会高度关注。作为澳洲价值守护联盟创始人之一，冯博士长期坚守言论自由与民主价值，其遭悬赏不仅是对个人安全的威胁，更挑战了澳洲的主权与法律秩序。澳洲价值守护联盟（AVA）发表了正式的中英文声明，强烈谴责香港当局的跨国打压行径，呼吁澳洲政府采取反制措施，捍卫公民权利与国家尊严。</p>
<h2>澳洲价值守护联盟声明：强烈谴责香港局悬赏缉捕澳洲公民冯崇义</h2>
<p>2025年7月29日</p>
<p>澳洲价值守护联盟强烈谴责香港当局悬赏缉捕澳洲公民、悉尼科技大学副教授冯崇义博士。香港当局此举企图跨境实施《国安法》，不仅严重威胁了澳洲公民的人身安全和言论表达自由，更是对澳大利亚国家主权的公然践踏。</p>
<p>冯博士是成立于2016年的澳洲价值守护联盟的创始人之一，是正直敢言的学者，为维护澳洲民主自由价值，阻遏共产专制渗透作出了重要贡献。由于他的崇高声望，他受邀加入“香港议会选举筹备委员会”，因此被香港当局列入悬赏缉捕名单。</p>
<p>澳洲价值守护联盟欢迎黄英贤外长和影子外长兼参议院反对党领袖米开丽亚·卡什（Michaelia Cash） 的有关声明，支持澳洲香港社区和公民的言论和集会自由。</p>
<p>我们肯请澳洲联邦和地方警察加强对冯崇义博士和其他专制批评者的安全保护，让每一位澳洲公民都享有言论自由和免于恐惧的自由。</p>
<p>我们肯请澳洲政府就香港政府的跨国镇压行为做出具体的反制措施。对那些直接参与决策或执行跨国镇压的香港政府官员，予以点名公开，列为“不受欢迎人员”，并禁止其永久入境澳洲。</p>
<h2>AVA condemns Hong Kong Authorities for issuing Arrest Warrant with bounty on Australian citizen Chongyi Feng</h2>
<p>The Australian Values Alliance strongly condemns the Hong Kong Authorities for issuing an arrest warrant with bounty imposed on Dr Chongyi Feng, an Australian citizen and an Associate Professor at the University of Technology, Sydney.</p>
<p>The Act of Hong Kong Authorities is an attempt to apply the Hong Kong National Security Law across borders. It is not only a severe threat to the life and the freedom of expression and assembly of an Australian citizen, but also a blatant infringement of the national sovereignty of Australia.</p>
<p>Dr Feng is one of the founding members of the Australian Values Alliance which was established in 2016. He is an academic of integrity and courage who has played a part in maintaining values of democracy and freedom of Australia and containing the massive infiltration from Communist dictatorship.</p>
<p>Due to his high reputation, he was invited to join the Hong Kong Parliament Electoral Organizing Committee, which offered an excuse for the Hong Kong Authorities to place him into the list of Arrest Warrants with bounties.</p>
<p>The Australian Values Alliance welcomes the announcements on this case made by Hon Senator Penny Wong, Minister for Foreign Affairs, and Hon Michaelia Cash, Shadow Minister for Foreign Affairs and Leader of the Opposition in the Senate, and supports the freedom of expression and assembly of the Hong Kong community and citizens in Australia.</p>
<p>We hereby request the Australian Federal Police and the New South Wales Police to reinforce personal safety protection for Professor Chongyi Feng and other dictatorship critics so that every citizen in Australia can enjoy freedom of expression and freedom from fear.</p>
<p>We also request the Australian government to take solid actions against the transnational repression by Hong Kong Authorities. Hong Kong officials who are directly involved in policy-decision or execution of transnational repression should be named and listed as persona non grata and banned from entry to Australia permanently.</p>
<p>The Australian Values Alliance</p>
<p>July 29, 2025</p>
