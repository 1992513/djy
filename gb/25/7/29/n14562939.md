<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>从观夕平台到亲水公园 走进共享的黄昏风景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562941-784247-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黄昏中的二仁溪桥。（郑建南提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月29日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）台南的海边，不急不躁。傍晚走在这里，不需要刻意拍照、打卡、摆姿势，光是坐着听浪、看夕阳，就已经很够。摄影家郑建南说：“台南市区沿岸拍夕阳地点由北到南依序为四草大桥、观夕平台、渔光岛、黄金海岸、亲水公园。渔光岛后接四鲲鯓，再接黄金海岸。”</p>
<p>这些地方都不收门票，也不太有过度观光化的痕迹，有的是一种很朴实的海味，还有老台南人对大海的依恋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562940-784246.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562940-784246-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562940" class="wp-caption-text">风浪撞击岸边的消波块，水花直冲天际，展现黄昏时分的海岸震撼。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14562946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562946-784252.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562946-784252-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562946" class="wp-caption-text">一台白色机车停在海边，身后是傍晚潮浪翻涌的海面与深蓝天色。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14562945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562945-784251.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562945-784251-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562945" class="wp-caption-text">每天太阳下山后，半小时内天空会呈现蓝调的色彩。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>观夕平台：黄昏时分最美的一刻</h2>
<p>
观夕平台这个名字，就很直白。来这里，就是看夕阳，有人携手漫步，有人静静站着看天光染海，一对对情侣、家庭、小孩在沙滩上嬉戏，没有谁急着离开。</p>
<p>橘红色的夕阳快落到海平线，两位女孩肩靠肩看着天边的光，像是在静静说话，也像在心里留下些什么。</p>
<p>这里除了适合情侣拍婚纱照，也常见学生来拍毕业照，有人带着家人来野餐，也有摄影师等着拍下“天空之镜”出现的一瞬间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562952-784258.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562952-784258-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562952" class="wp-caption-text">有家人、有情侣，享受属于他们的黄昏节奏。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14562950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562950-784256.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562950-784256-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562950" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳即将落下，沙滩上满是看夕阳的人们。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>黄金海岸：适合一家大小来的地方</h2>
从观夕平台继续往南，会到黄金海岸。黄昏时分，夕阳照在退潮的沙滩上，水面像玻璃一样亮。小孩蹲着在湿沙中玩水，后面一群人映着夕阳剪影，温暖又自然。</p>
<p>摄影家郑建南说：“这一带是戏水、拍照的好地点，也非常适合全家一起来玩水。”不像观光景点那样拥挤，黄金海岸有的是空旷的宽度和脚踩沙的自由。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562942" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562942-784248.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14562942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562942-784248-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562942" class="wp-caption-text">一对年轻情侣在海堤边散步，谈笑间夕阳慢慢收起最后一道光。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14562953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562953-784259.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562953-784259-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562953" class="wp-caption-text">观夕平台也是拍天空之镜最佳海滩。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>渔光岛与港堤：慢慢走、慢慢看</h2>
往回北边走，是渔光岛与四鲲鯓交界。渔光岛近年成了不少人周末的散步选择。港堤上一排人影静静钓鱼、散步，有艘渔船从远方靠近，画面很安静，却让人忍不住多看几眼。</p>
<p>这里不是大声喧哗的观光区，是比较像自己人会来走走的地方。老人在防波堤边泡茶聊天、妈妈在堤下看孩子堆沙堡，一切都很日常。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562948-784254.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562948-784254-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562948" class="wp-caption-text">骑士坐在机车上看着海，车灯与尾灯映照在地面，夜海静默无声。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14562947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562947-784253.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562947-784253-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562947" class="wp-caption-text">傍晚的海滨步道，警示牌提醒潮水危险，夜灯与海音陪人静静的走。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>亲水公园与二仁溪桥 黄昏灯影中的车聚</h2>
最南边是亲水公园，紧邻黄金海岸。这里除了散步的居民，更多是年轻人骑车来拍照、看海。有时候整排摩托车停在海边，大家彼此不认识，但都在享受同一片蓝天与晚霞。</p>
<p>“观海步道为当地居民散步运动，赏景的好地方，年轻人约会最佳地点。”郑建南这样形容。</p>
<p>接着会经过二仁溪桥。傍晚时分，桥灯一盏盏亮起，金色光影洒在水面上，像是电影片头的画面。有人牵车经过，也有人独自靠栏杆边望海，画面很简单，但真实得动人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562944-784250.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562944-784250-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562944" class="wp-caption-text">亲水步道与海岸树影。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14562943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562943" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562943-784249.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562943-784249-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562943" class="wp-caption-text">骑士缓缓骑过二仁溪桥，身影与灯光融合成温暖画面。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>沿着海边走 不需要目的地</h2>
摄影家最后提到，这些点都不用收门票，也没太多商业设施，走一走、停一停，天色慢慢暗，海风吹来，光影映在眼里，也印在心里。</p>
<p>来这里的人，有的来约会、有的来疗愈自己、有的只是下班后想放松一下。</p>
<p>台南这条沿海的黄昏散步线，没有标准玩法，只有属于每个人不同的静静时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562949-784255.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14562949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562949-784255-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562949" class="wp-caption-text">黄昏时分，美丽景色，让人人都沉醉了。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
