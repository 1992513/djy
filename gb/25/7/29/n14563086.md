<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>百年古厝风华重现 东里家风的时光与记忆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563087-784304-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“东里家风”，是进入苗栗苑里百年古厝的第一眼印象，也是一段家族记忆与地方风土的开场。（廖翠环提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月29日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾苗栗报导）从大马路转进小巷，沿着古里的红瓦砖墙缓缓前行，庭前大瓮嵌入围墙，是一种朴实浓厚的时光提示“东里家风”到了。古厝伫立于苗栗县苑里镇中沟溪畔，庭院中摆满老农具、浇水壶、谢篮和蓑衣，让人一瞬仿佛回到阿公阿嬷的老家，这是摄影家廖翠环眼中的温柔时光，也是她镜头下岁月的痕迹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14563088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563088" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563088-784305.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563088-784305-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563088" class="wp-caption-text">悬挂于柱上的招牌书写“日日春酒家”，或许是旧时的店名，也可能是今人怀旧命名。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563090" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563090-784307.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563090" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563090-784307-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563090" class="wp-caption-text">一瓮瓮大小不一的酒瓮静静地坐在砖平台上，诉说着过去酿酒、腌制与储藏的日常。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563091" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563091-784308.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563091-784308-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563091" class="wp-caption-text">红底金字的“爱宁兰大酒家”竖牌立于门前，像是等待旅人走进老厝的开场，仿佛时光未曾走远。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑氏家风的根与廊“东里家风”古厝，建于咸丰年间（约1893年），为苑坑聚落著名三合院，由郑仕原第?4?代后裔郑新财兴建。依循传统格局：坐北朝南、合院串连中轴与护龙，院前半月池象征招财、院埕隔墙则保护私域。建材采用红砖瓦、交趾陶与泥塑，正厅雕饰菊花剪黏、堂号泥塑、悬鱼石灰塑，精巧细致?。郑家祖籍广东潮州，清代时来自莿坑（薪山号）的郑巽，是清乾隆年间第一代来台先祖。其后子孙兴旺，逐步扩建家族聚落，最后兴有集郑家老厝、东里家风与日式郑宅三栋建筑成为当地聚落核心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14563092" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563092" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563092-784309.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563092" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563092-784309-450x598.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="598" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563092" class="wp-caption-text">特殊造型的砖窗保有通风与美观功能。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563093" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563093-784310.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563093-784310-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563093" class="wp-caption-text">橘红与深蓝的窗框构图，大胆却和谐，是老屋在保留岁月痕迹中依然展现的活泼色彩。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563094" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563094-784311.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563094" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563094-784311-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563094" class="wp-caption-text">小电视、旧椅子与酒瓮，草丛间的日常风景，勾勒出一幅安静又自然的乡村生活画面。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>岁月里漫步 廖翠环笔下的古厝生活摄影家廖翠环描述：红砖、红瓮、缎布、蓑衣交织成一段岁月的片段。铁制鸡罩仿佛喂养过老母鸡、稻耙与犁具仿佛仍回响着阿公呼唤“驾驾驾”的吆喝，那些椅寮让她回想起自己摔落地面三天三夜的糗事。这样的细节，在她镜头与文字里，活了过来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14563095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563095" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563095-784312.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563095-784312-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563095" class="wp-caption-text">瓮口缠绕着粗麻绳，既是提绳也是封口，实用与工艺兼备，体现早期农村生活的智慧。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563096" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563096-784313.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563096-784313-450x598.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="598" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563096" class="wp-caption-text">这件古老的蓑衣由植物纤维编织而成，是农夫雨天外出的保护衣，现今成为历史的见证。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古厝的再生 也为社区注入新生命休耕多年、荒废30年后，“东里家风”在在地社区与文化推展协会的努力下，转型为古厝民宿与文化园区?。这里不仅提供导览、餐饮与住宿服务，也是许多台湾剧组取景的热门场域?。现代的再生计划，聘请唐山师傅工法修复老瓦、搭建龙柏树意象庭院、将红眠床、长板凳、古装租借等元素融入游程，让游客仿佛穿越时空，体验昔日富裕家庭的生活，也带动地方就业与弱势族群参与?。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14563097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563097" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563097-784320.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563097-784320-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563097" class="wp-caption-text">那些椅寮让廖翠环回想起自己摔落地面三天三夜的糗事。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563098" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563098-784321.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563098-784321-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563098" class="wp-caption-text">廖翠环的文字与镜头，拼凑出一幅活生生的古厝记忆画卷。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563099" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563099" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563099-784322.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563099" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563099-784322-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563099" class="wp-caption-text">“东里家风”是一栋生动的时间容器。它见证了郑氏家族从清代至今的嬗变，也承载着苑里这块平原上的客家与闽南共生文化。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在古厝里 传承“东里家风”郑家代代相传的家训与风范，成为地方精神的象征。“东里家风”不只是建筑本身，也隐含家族与文化传承的核。从祖先耕作、农耙、对联谱系、堂号雕塑，以及红瓦剪黏艺术，都提醒后人勿忘根源，而这份风范也映照于今日空间再利用的理念：保存历史、活化文化、立足在地?。</p>
<p>廖翠环的照片中，小巷、瓦片、瓮壶、老椅，每一件器物都是故事。她的文字与镜头，拼凑出一幅活生生的古厝记忆画卷。从照片里的蓑衣、铁水壶到大瓮，每一角落都流露出古味。这不只是一次旅行，更像一次亲情密码的回响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14563101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563101" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563101-784338.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563101-784338-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563101" class="wp-caption-text">屋檐下高挂“囍”字红灯笼，诉说着家族喜事与节庆记忆，也象征着老宅的人情与温度。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563102" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563102-784339.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563102-784339-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563102" class="wp-caption-text">墙上挂着编织篮与四爪铁耙，展示农村生活不可或缺的劳作器具，让人忆起早期农家一日作息。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14563103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14563103" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563103-784340.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14563103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14563103-784340-450x323.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14563103" class="wp-caption-text">屋顶上的燕尾脊翘起，如同老屋的眉角，搭配雕饰花砖与斜屋檐，构成富有在地文化特色的建筑美感。（廖翠环提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>历史与当下的交响“东里家风”是一栋生动的时间容器。它见证了郑氏家族从清代至今的嬗变，也承载着苑里这块平原上的客家与闽南共生文化。今日在廖翠环的镜头与文字中，也在社区努力者的复育计划里，成为百年古厝的新生典范。走进这座古厝，你看见的不只是红砖与瓮壁，更是岁月的印记、文化的回音，以及在薪火相传中仍闪亮的“东里家风”。</p>
