<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园平镇工厂大火黑烟窜天如巨兽 “塑胶制品”烧光光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562830-784173-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园消防人员紧急到场，拉起水线扑灭火势。（桃园消防局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月29日讯】（大纪元记者陈建霖台湾桃园报导）桃园市平镇区平东路一家工厂，29日上午5时43分许突然窜出火舌，桃园消防局接获通报后，立即派遣消防人员50名，消防车20辆、救护车2辆前往救援，火势于22分钟后6时15分控制，上午7时45分扑灭，仍在进行残火处理，幸无人伤亡，详细起火原因仍待进一步调查厘清。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562831" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562831-784174.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14562831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562831-784174.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562831" class="wp-caption-text">大量塑胶制品全面燃烧，燃烧面积待查。（桃园消防局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>发生火警的工厂传出阵阵爆炸声响，桃园消防人员紧急到场，拉起水线扑灭火势，火警现场位于平东路某工厂，现场为2层楼建筑物，3楼为铁皮加盖，大火不断窜出窗外飘出阵阵浓烟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562832" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562832" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562832-784175.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14562832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562832-784175.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562832" class="wp-caption-text">所幸火势后续获得控制，并于7时45分扑灭。（桃园消防局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14562833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562833" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562833-784176.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14562833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562833-784176.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562833" class="wp-caption-text">发生火警的工厂传出阵阵爆炸声响。（桃园消防局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所幸火势后续获得控制，并于7时45分扑灭，起火点初判为2楼，大量塑胶制品全面燃烧，燃烧面积待查。详细起火原因及财损，仍有待进一步调查厘清。◇</p>
