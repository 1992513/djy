<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大雨道路变形 芦竹警应变疏导养工处急抢修</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月29日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）29日清晨桃园地区降下大雨，桃园市芦竹区富国路与芦宏路口，及富国路二段600号前，因大雨冲刷，造成路面柏油多处隆起破损，影响往来车辆通行安全。芦竹分局获报后，迅速派遣警力赶赴现场处置，并通报市府养护工程处紧急派员抢修，将交通冲击降至最低。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562751" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562751-784110.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14562751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562751-784110.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="518" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562751" class="wp-caption-text">芦竹富国路与芦宏街口，因大雨冲刷，造成路面柏油多处隆起破损。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14562753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562753" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562753-784112.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14562753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562753-784112.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562753" class="wp-caption-text">发现大雨导致柏油隆起，影响行车甚钜。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦竹分局南竹派出所员警至现场后，发现大雨导致柏油隆起，影响行车甚钜。为维护用路人安全，员警立即于受损路面前后摆设交通锥，引导车流改道，亦立即通报桃园市政府养护工程处派员到场处理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562752-784111.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14562752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562752-784111.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562752" class="wp-caption-text">芦竹富国路与芦宏街口，因大雨冲刷，造成路面柏油多处隆起破损。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>养工处接获通报后，随即进行路面修复，请路过该处之驾驶注意安全，减速慢行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14562754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14562754" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562754-784113.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14562754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14562754-784113.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="504" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14562754" class="wp-caption-text">芦竹富国路二段600号前。（桃园市芦竹警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦竹分局呼吁，近日天候不稳，降雨频繁，易造成路面状况不佳。请用路人行经积水或路况不明之路段时，务必减速慢行，并随时留意路况，确保行车安全。◇</p>
