<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>房屋保险费高企 房主降低保费的五个策略</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14293637-CP215053303-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加拿大正经历更多极端天气事件：亚伯塔野火、安省洪水、草原城市罕见冰雹，这些灾害重创保险公司财务。（加通社）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月30日讯】（<span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1adg3ll r-1g7jtus r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">大纪元记者李平多伦多报导）气候变化导致加拿大乃至全球极端天气事件频发且更严重，从暴雨洪水到野火冰雹，灾害损失日益加剧，保险公司支付的索赔金额屡创新高，房主保险费也随之攀升。</span></span></span><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1adg3ll r-1g7jtus r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">本文探讨气候变化推高保费的原因，并提供实用方法助房主控制保险成本。</span></span></span></p>
<div><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1adg3ll r-1g7jtus r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">加拿大正经历更多极端天气事件：亚伯塔野火、安省洪水、草原城市罕见冰雹，这些灾害重创保险公司财务。保险公司依赖历史天气模式设定保费，但洪水与野火等事件频率增加、难以预测，打破传统算法。洪水现为多数社区最常见且代价高昂的灾害，还可能破坏电网，导致居民在严寒中无暖气可用。</span></span></span></div>
<div></div>
<div><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1adg3ll r-1g7jtus r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">加拿大保险局报告显示，2024年气候灾害赔付达80亿加元，较上年增近3倍，是21世纪初的12倍。这些损失直接推高保费，行业研究预测2025年全国房屋保费平均上涨5.3%，亚伯塔更高达9%。</span></span></span></div>
<h2><strong><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1adg3ll r-1g7jtus r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-b88u0q r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">房主降低保费的五个策略</span></span></span></span></strong></h2>
<div>房主难以完全避免气候相关的保费上涨，但可采取以下方法降低成本：</div>
<div>
<ol dir="ltr" start="1">
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-b88u0q r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">提高免赔额</span></span><br />
<span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">免赔额是索赔时自付的金额。提高免赔额可降低每月保费，因这减少了保险公司的风险。但需确保有足够储蓄支付更高免赔额，且小额损失建议自行承担，避免不必要的索赔。</span></span></li>
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-b88u0q r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">捆绑购买保险</span></span><br />
<span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">多家保险公司为同时购买房屋与汽车保险的客户提供折扣。捆绑购买是简单有效的省钱方式。</span></span></li>
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-b88u0q r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">加固房屋</span></span><br />
<span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">在高风险地区，加固房屋可降低损失风险与保费，例如： </span></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">外墙涂防火涂层；</span></span></li>
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">安装污水泵或排水系统防洪水；</span></span></li>
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">使用抗强风玻璃窗与高强度屋顶；</span></span></li>
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">加装防盗警报器。</span><br />
<span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">这些升级降低索赔可能性，部分改造还可获联邦或省级税收减免。改造后，务必通知保险公司以调整保费。</span></span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-b88u0q r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">询问个人折扣</span></span><br />
<span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">保险公司可能提供老年人、军人、忠诚度或免抵押贷款等折扣，但通常需主动询问代理商以确认资格。</span></span></li>
<li><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-b88u0q r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">比较保险报价</span></span><br />
<span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">若现有保险公司折扣不理想，可通过在线保险聚合服务比较多家公司报价。输入房屋信息后，24小时内可获具竞争力的定制报价。</span></span></li>
</ol>
<p><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1adg3ll r-1g7jtus r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">气候变化使天气更难预测，房屋受损风险增加，保险费随之上涨。个人难以改变大环境，但透过上述策略，房主可有效控制保费，甚至在某些情况下降低成本。</span></span></span></p>
