<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>巴里排骨节烧烤天堂 美食与音乐一网打尽</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14554986-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月19日讯】一年一度的<strong>巴里</strong><b>排骨节（</b><span class="s1"><b>Barrie Ribfest 2025</b></span><b>）</b> 即将于<span class="s1"> <b>7</b></span><b>月</b><span class="s1"><b>25</b></span><b>日（周五）至</b><span class="s1"><b>7</b></span><b>月</b><span class="s1"><b>27</b></span><b>日（周日），</b>在<b>巴里商场</b><span class="s1"><b>Georgian Mall</b>（<span class="LrzXr">509 Bayfield St</span>）</span>的停车场火热登场！由<span class="s1"> Ontario Festival Group </span>精心策划，这场美食嘉年华绝对让你食指大动、全家同欢！</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" data2-src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?height=476&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Freel%2F30356142487363850%2F&amp;show_text=true&amp;width=267&amp;t=0" width="267" b="591" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></div>
<p class="p1">来自北美顶尖的“烧烤大师”（<span class="s1">Pitmasters</span>）将现场现做<b>得奖级烤猪肋排、烟熏牛腩、嫩滑手撕猪肉、</b><span class="s1"><b>BBQ</b></span><b>鸡肉、香肠</b>，还有<b>巨无霸“Dino”</b><b>牛肋排</b>，香气扑鼻，令人无法抗拒！</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/7/18/n14554985.md#1/bloominonion-profile-1" rel="attachment wp-att-14554987"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14554987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14554987-BloominOnion-Profile-1-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">除了烧烤美食，现场还有经典节庆小吃：<b>玉米热狗、烤玉米、起司肉汁薯条（</b><span class="s1"><b>Poutine</b></span><b>）、漏斗蛋糕等</b>，搭配冰凉啤酒，夏天就该这样过！</p>
<p class="p1"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/7/18/n14554985.md#1/kidszone-profile" rel="attachment wp-att-14554988"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14554988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14554988-Kidszone-Profile-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a></p>
<p class="p1">巴里排骨节活动亮点不只是美食，小朋友可在<b>儿童游戏区</b>和<strong>脸部彩绘</strong>玩到忘我、乐翻天。</p>
<p class="p1">舞台上全天候<b>现场音乐表演</b>，从致敬乐队（<span class="s1">tribute band</span>）到本地乐队表演，让大家都超开心！另有手工市集、特色摊位等你来挖宝！</p>
<p class="p1"><strong>活动时间：</strong></p>
<p>7<span class="s2">月</span>25<span class="s2">日（周五）中午</span>12<span class="s2">点</span> – <span class="s2">晚上</span>11<span class="s2">点</span></p>
<p>7<span class="s2">月</span>26<span class="s2">日（周六）中午</span>12<span class="s2">点</span> – <span class="s2">晚上</span>11<span class="s2">点</span></p>
<p>7<span class="s2">月</span>27<span class="s2">日（周日）中午</span>12<span class="s2">点</span> – <span class="s2">晚上</span>7<span class="s2">点</span></p>
<p class="p1">入场<strong>只需<span class="s1"> 5 </span>元</strong>，<strong><span class="s1">10</span>岁及以下</strong>儿童免费！<span class="s1">购票、获取更多信息，请前往<ahref="https://ribfestx.com/event/barrie-ribfest-2025/"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>主办方网站。</strong></span></a></span></p>
<p class="p1"><strong>免费停车、免费音乐会、欢迎宠物同行</strong>！</p>
<p class="p1">本次活动收益将捐助给<span class="s1"> <b>Royal Victoria </b></span><b>区域健康中心</b>以及 <span class="s1"><b>Football Ontario</b></span>，吃喝玩乐之余还能做公益，一举多得！</p>
