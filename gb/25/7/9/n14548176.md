<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美三区台美人夏令会北卡登场　三区乡亲共襄盛举共话台美未来</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548177-d4f2125436bea6fe00e562da3c734a08-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>开幕典礼播放赖总统录影致词
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年07月10日讯】2025年美中西、美南及美东南三区联合举办的台美人夏令会，于7月4日至6日在北卡罗来纳州洛丽市隆重登场，吸引三区众多台美乡亲约400人齐聚一堂。大会主题为“异乡的归属，家乡的连结—台美同行，共创双赢”，展现台美人对故乡情感与对未来共同愿景的深厚连结。</p>
<p>侨务委员会副委员长阮昭雄、驻亚特兰大办事处代理处长李瑞征与亚特兰大侨教中心主任阎树荣均出席盛会。会中邀请台湾立法委员苏巧慧及多位民选议员、学者专家分享台湾最新政经社会发展，提供与会者丰富的时事资讯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14548178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548178" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548178-fd20d44827ee7b88fe36704e65cc0390-e1752095984146.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548178-fd20d44827ee7b88fe36704e65cc0390-e1752095984146.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548178" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄于开幕典礼中致词</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14548181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548181" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548181-83bd6285f461d7c511ec0529547196c5-e1752096159799.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548181-83bd6285f461d7c511ec0529547196c5-e1752096159799.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548181" class="wp-caption-text">全美台湾同乡会会长宋明丽开幕典礼中致词</figcaption></figure>
<p>7月4日开幕典礼中，首先播放总统赖清德与侨务委员会委员长徐佳青的祝贺影片，随后由全美台湾同乡会会长宋明丽致词感谢北卡团队筹备用心，让此次大会顺利进行，美东南区夏令会总召谢金朱则欢迎来自全美的与会乡亲。阮昭雄代表徐佳青向全体与会人士致意，并向与会者说明就当前侨务政策</p>
<figure id="attachment_14548183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548183" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548183-1da3e7466bd3798133848dffa0ee8286-e1752096275877.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548183-1da3e7466bd3798133848dffa0ee8286-e1752096275877.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548183" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员会委员长徐佳青录影致词</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14548187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548187" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548187-e4ef7b65ce9838a5134645901511c547-e1752096430364.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548187-e4ef7b65ce9838a5134645901511c547-e1752096430364.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548187" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄进行侨务政策简报</figcaption></figure>
<p>除专题讲座外，阮昭雄也于7月5日中午与各地代表座谈，广泛听取及回应各地乡亲关心议题。晚间举办的“Taiwan Night 台湾之夜”由各地分会代表展现才艺，包括北卡台湾会唱团、声乐家李佳蔚教授与休士顿黑猫黑狗团、巴乐康乐队等团体带来热情演出，气氛热络温馨，增进彼此间交流情谊。会议结束后，大会安排三日旅游行程，造访北卡著名的大烟山国家公园，让与会者亲身感受壮丽自然风光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14548188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548188" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548188-c74235a953620690bafb4d12b4a61dcb-e1752096535609.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548188-c74235a953620690bafb4d12b4a61dcb-e1752096535609.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548188" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄与各地区代表座谈交流</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14548190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548190" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548190-717286cc29e9c0a707b2d6ea1c278b82-e1752096641514.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548190-717286cc29e9c0a707b2d6ea1c278b82-e1752096641514.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548190" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄上台鼓励台湾之夜表演团队</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14548192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548192" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548192-0cd7c9774950b1b9ec9923028ee4e5ea-e1752096765331.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548192-0cd7c9774950b1b9ec9923028ee4e5ea-e1752096765331.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548192" class="wp-caption-text">北卡台音合唱团于台湾之夜中演出</figcaption></figure>
<p>美南、美东南及美中西三地区的台美人夏令会原采各自办理方式进行，自2024年决定联合办理并由美南地区筹办于丹佛首次召开，今年由美东南地区北卡洛丽办理，7月6日的闭幕典礼中，大会宣布2026年夏令会将由芝加哥接棒主办，与会乡亲相约明年再会。</p>
