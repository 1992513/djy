<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨委会副委员长阮昭雄访视北卡　与侨界广泛交流凝聚向心力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548203-f95ea7db236f57825cddc5d8613c1bb4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>北卡侨界欢迎餐会合影
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年07月10日讯】侨务委员会副委员长阮昭雄偕同科长赖贞利于7月4日至6日间前往北卡罗莱纳州首府洛丽地区访视侨界，深入了解当地侨情并广泛倾听侨胞心声。此行行程紧凑，涵盖侨民、侨教及台湾留学生事务等面向，充分展现对海外侨界的关怀与支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14548205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548205" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548205-939f76c668449c0a9c98857befb9764b-e1752097580509.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548205-939f76c668449c0a9c98857befb9764b-e1752097580509.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548205" class="wp-caption-text">北卡侨务座谈办理情形</figcaption></figure>
<p>7月4日晚间，北卡侨界特别在台湾人经营的Akashi日本料理餐厅举行欢迎餐会，热情接待来访贵宾。包括侨务委员陈可征、侨务荣誉职人员及社团代表均踊跃出席。阮昭雄亲切与侨民互动，并回应在场侨胞提出的各项建议与问题，现场气氛热络温馨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14548215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548215" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548215-16088547ac97abe9373046d406a5cb47-e1752098401571.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548215-16088547ac97abe9373046d406a5cb47-e1752098401571.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548215" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄与全体座谈会参与者合影</figcaption></figure>
<p>7月5日，阮昭雄前往北卡佛光山拜会监寺妙舟法师，双方就该寺在美国弘扬佛教、文化推广、慈善服务及与侨界互动等主题深入交流。当日恰逢洛丽中文学校台湾华语学习中心（TCML）借用佛光山场地上课，阮昭雄也特别了解教学情形，肯定其对华语文教育的贡献。下午行程则参访获侨务委员会2002年海外台商精品银质奖陈壐全博士创立的兰茵科技公司，深入了解其植物种植研发成果，并与北卡大学（UNC）东亚系主任萧丽玲博士就台美学生交流进行意见交换。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14548206" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548206" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548206-20514cf94e25bb0acb73fc5bea598918-e1752097772652.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548206" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548206-20514cf94e25bb0acb73fc5bea598918-e1752097772652.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548206" class="wp-caption-text">阮副委员长与北卡佛光山监寺妙舟法师交流</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14548214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548214" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548214-749913af63a99b625ca1c3ce22582b87-e1752098293806.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548214-749913af63a99b625ca1c3ce22582b87-e1752098293806.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548214" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄听取兰茵科技创办人陈壐全博士简报</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14548208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548208" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548208-1be09a05f4863e4965c1117947ad3dd9-e1752097902595.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548208-1be09a05f4863e4965c1117947ad3dd9-e1752097902595.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548208" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄与FASCA洛丽分会家长及学员合影</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14548216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548216" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548216-4bb983e43474ac5ea7f1917783399ee7-e1752098504937.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548216-4bb983e43474ac5ea7f1917783399ee7-e1752098504937.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548216" class="wp-caption-text">FASCA北卡洛丽分会会长分享FASCA对其产生之正面影响</figcaption></figure>
<p>7月6日上午，阮昭雄前往杜克大学与台湾同学会成员及北卡台湾艺文筹备会代表座谈，了解留学生需求及侨界活动情况。下午则主持北卡侨务座谈会，与各界代表就侨教推展、青年培育、侨团发展及留学生议题深入交流。会中海外青年文化大使协会洛丽分会(FASCA)学员及家长分享参与心得及提出建议，侨界亦对第二、三代儿童学习华语及台湾文化提供殷切需求，希望台湾能提供儿童夏令营供其回国参加强化华语能力，另为鼓励青年参与侨团事务，与会者都认为有必要加强与台湾留学生的互动引进年青人影响力。此外亚特兰大华侨文教中心亦透过此一难得机会向与会人士简介本年度侨务委员会议中心议题相关侨务工作推动策略进行简报，供与会者参考提供建言。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14548210" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548210" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548210-d770b2576219ff523991dd4b9fbb0516-e1752098028213.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548210-d770b2576219ff523991dd4b9fbb0516-e1752098028213.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548210" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄与杜克大学台湾同学会会长合影</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14548212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14548212" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548212-fd17fcc95282044c79744e629e9b4fa1-e1752098150101.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14548212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548212-fd17fcc95282044c79744e629e9b4fa1-e1752098150101.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14548212" class="wp-caption-text">阮昭雄与UNC陈丽玲教授就台美学生交流交换意见</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次访问行程获得当地侨界热烈回响，不少侨胞表示，这是首次有侨委会高层来访北卡，令人倍感振奋。透过座谈会及面对面交流，不仅促进不同世代侨民间的理解，也有助于凝聚社区向心力。北卡侨界一致期盼日后能有更多机会与侨委会保持密切联系与合作。</p>
