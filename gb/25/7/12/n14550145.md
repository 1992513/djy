<h6>2025 Hyundai Sonata Hybrid Ltd。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年07月12日讯】2025年Hyundai Sonata是经过Facelift的第八代Sonata，无论是在外型上或设备上都有了显着的更新，就如驾驶一辆全新设计的汽车一样。</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" loading="lazy" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://dd92z3w2j4w9k.cloudfront.net/Z7hl9AycBJAk"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/zVW10_DjS9w?si=MeRpURVs6zWxt3AY" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Facelift后的Hyundai Sonata，前脸采用无缝条灯与隐藏式头灯，搭配新款栏栅与更大的进气口，展现出更有动感的外形；车尾的H型尾灯与黑色扰流板式行李箱盖，强调宽阔姿态与高科技感。侧面是稳重成熟设计，以现时流行的四门Coupe面貌示人，引擎盖呈曲线从挡风玻璃伸延至栏栅上方，并带有三条骨线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550149" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550149-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550149-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_05-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550149" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Hyundai Sonata Hybrid Ltd。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新的Sonata是首款采用Hyundai第三代汽车平台的车型，新平台提高了安全性、效率和驾驶性能。该平台通过使用多负载路径结构，热冲压和超高强度钢板来提高碰撞安全性。多负载路径结构可以增加车辆在碰撞中吸收的能量，而且更加坚固，所以新Sonata在高速转弯时的表现更好。测试载装上了17&#8243;合金轮圈及215／55R17轮胎，并采用Serenity White白色车身，配灰色及棕色内装。并首次装上电动尾门，提升日常使用便利性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550150-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550150-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_07-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550150" class="wp-caption-text">新款头灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14550151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550151" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550151-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_09.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550151-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_09-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550151" class="wp-caption-text">车侧指挥灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025年款Hyundai Sonata动力共有三种：2.5L汽油引擎、2.5L涡轮增压引擎（N Line专用）及2.0L混合动力（HEV）。这次测试的是Hybrid（HEV），搭载e-Motion Drive，提供平顺驾驶体验，新增换档拨片与再生制动模式，提升燃油效率。由2.0L四缸引擎和39kW马达组成的e-Motion Drive，综合马力192 hp，车载锂电池为270V。至于2.5升的自然吸气汽擎，由入门的SE版及SEL版才配备，马力为191 hp及181 lb-ft；另一种是N Line的2.5L直四涡轮增压引擎，比旧有1.6L增大了不少，所以马力也由180 hp提升至290 hp，而峰值扭矩为195 lb-ft增至311 lb-ft。Sonata是前置引擎前轮驱动模式，SE/SEL、N Line和Hybrid各自采用不同变速箱，分别为8速自动变速箱、N 8速湿式双离合及6速自动变速箱。新增加的HTRAC AWD四驱系统就只有SEL版本才有。Sonata Hybrid设有三种驾驶模式，分别为“Eco”、“Sport”及“My Drive”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550152" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550152-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_18.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550152-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_18-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550152" class="wp-caption-text">2.5L汽油引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于新设备，测试的顶级Sonata Limited，拥有大量新颖玩意，不亚于豪华品牌。首先是虚拟的仪表板，是一个12.3&#8243;的萤幕，色彩丰富，解像度也高。仪表板设计是最传统的两大圆仪表，但破格地是左圆车速、右圆引擎转速的排列。并且在使用左右指挥灯时，会显示盲点影像。Facelift后的“悬浮”主题内装，可结合环境氛围灯光，营造未来感。与仪表板组成的全景曲面显示幕，也是12.3&#8243;导航影音系统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550153" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550153-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_25.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550153-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_25-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550153" class="wp-caption-text">车内新颖玩意。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中一样新功能是“Digital Key（数码车匙）”，透过NFC及BLE（低耗电蓝牙技术），让驾驶者可使用手机专用App来锁上或解锁汽车、启动汽车。虽然很多人都不会愿意借自己汽车给其他人，但如果要借出汽车予亲友时，只需分享数码车匙便可，极为方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550154" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550154-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_19.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550154-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_19-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550154" class="wp-caption-text">12.3&#8243;萤幕仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看到遥控车匙上有两个按键，汽车带有前移箭头及后移箭头，它可使用车侧感应器侦测空位，再配合镜头让驾驶者设定停车位置，继而便可使用遥控车匙上的按钮“遥控”汽车驶入停车位置，Hyundai称之为“Remote Smart Parking Assist (RSPA) ”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550155" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550155-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_20.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550155-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_20-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550155" class="wp-caption-text">全景曲面显示幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Sonata Limited版标配BOSE品牌音响系统，并有12个扬声器，附有“Sounds of Nature（大自然之声）”功能，有多种“大自然音效”选择，包括“森林”、“海浪”、“下雨”、“雪地”等，可表现出BOSE系统的功力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550156" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550156-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_22.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550156-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_22-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550156" class="wp-caption-text">新式变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试的Limited版则标配了前座乘客的6向电动座椅，此外，SEL、SEL Plus及Limited版均有会标配Safe Exit Warning（SEW）系统，SEW是当汽车驶出平行泊位时，会利用盲点监察系统检测后方有否汽车驶近，并会以声响及仪表板提示驾驶者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550157" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550157-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550157-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_16-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550157" class="wp-caption-text">新设计尾灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中期升级的Hyundai Sonata以“感性运动”为设计核心，结合先进科技、动感外观与全轮驱动选项，强化市场竞争力。而且Hybrid版的耗油性也大幅降低，平均行车也可达列45 MPG，最后入油时也只花了article content begin -->2块钱，令人喜出望外。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550158" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550158-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14550158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550158-2025_Hyundai_Sonata_Hybrid_Ltd_11-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550158" class="wp-caption-text">侧镜。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
