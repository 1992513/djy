<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】惠州梅菜扣肉～简单做就成功！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555342-17-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>惠州梅菜扣肉。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>老师傅传授“惠州梅菜扣肉”做法，步骤明确，只要跟着做，油亮香Ｑ、肥而不腻的醇香好滋味，就轻松上菜了！</h3>
<h3>材料：</h3>
<p>五花肉2块共5磅<br />
惠州梅菜2又1/2磅<br />
姜、葱、八角、芫荽梗、蒜子各适量<br />
老抽适量<br />
水4杯（水的份量要淹过猪肉）<br />
芫荽适量（装饰用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555348" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555348-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555348-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555348" class="wp-caption-text">（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>冰糖1.5 oz<br />
生抽2汤匙<br />
蚝油2汤匙<br />
花雕酒4汤匙<br />
胡椒粉1/4茶匙</p>
<h3>勾芡用酱汁：</h3>
<p>扣肉汁3杯<br />
家乐鲜酱油1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉水2汤匙</p>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 梅菜先用水浸开，中间要多次换水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555349" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555349-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555349-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555349" class="wp-caption-text">梅菜先用水浸开，中间要多次换水。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 浸好的梅菜清洗干净，沥干水后切成小粒备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555350" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555350-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555350" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555350-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555350" class="wp-caption-text">浸好的梅菜清洗干净，沥干水后切成小粒备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 烧热一锅水，加入姜、葱、八角，放入五花肉煮30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555351" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555351-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555351-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555351" class="wp-caption-text">烧热一锅水，加入姜、葱、八角，放入五花肉煮30分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 五花肉皮向上放在砧板上，用松肉针在猪皮上均匀戳洞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555352" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555352-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555352-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555352" class="wp-caption-text">五花肉皮向上放在砧板上，用松肉针在猪皮上均匀戳洞。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 在猪皮上涂老抽，备用。热锅下油4杯，大火烧热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555354" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555354-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555354-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555354" class="wp-caption-text">在猪皮上涂老抽，备用。热锅下油4杯，大火烧热。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 一手拿锅盖，另一只手用钳子把猪肉（皮向下）放进滚油中，立即盖上盖子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555355" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555355-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555355-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555355" class="wp-caption-text">一手拿锅盖，另一只手用钳子把猪肉（皮向下）放进滚油中，立即盖上盖子。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 锅中若没有爆响的声音便可打开盖子，取出猪肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555356" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555356-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555356-9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555356" class="wp-caption-text">锅中若没有爆响的声音便可打开盖子，取出猪肉。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 将猪肉放入水中冲去油分，取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555357" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555357" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555357-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555357" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555357-10-450x338.jpg" alt="将猪肉放入水中冲去油分，取出备用。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555357" class="wp-caption-text">将猪肉放入水中冲去油分，取出备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 热锅下油，爆香姜、葱、芫荽梗和蒜子，放入梅菜兜炒均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555358" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555358-11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555358-11-450x338.jpg" alt="热锅下油，爆香姜、葱、芫荽梗和蒜子，放入梅菜兜炒均匀。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555358" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，爆香姜、葱、芫荽梗和蒜子，放入梅菜兜炒均匀。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 加水4杯，放入所有调味料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555359" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555359" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555359-12.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555359" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555359-12-450x338.jpg" alt="加水4杯，放入所有调味料。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555359" class="wp-caption-text">加水4杯，放入所有调味料。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 把五花肉放在梅菜上面，汁液要盖到猪肉，用慢火煮1.5小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555360" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555360-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555360-13-450x338.jpg" alt="把五花肉放在梅菜上面，汁液要盖到猪肉，用慢火煮1.5小时。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555360" class="wp-caption-text">把五花肉放在梅菜上面，汁液要盖到猪肉，用慢火煮1.5小时。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 这时的五花肉已经有八成软，取出肉，捞出梅菜，汁液留着用于勾芡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555361" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555361" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555361-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555361-14-450x338.jpg" alt="这时的五花肉已经有八成软，取出肉，捞出梅菜，汁液留着用于勾芡。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555361" class="wp-caption-text">这时的五花肉已经有八成软，取出肉，捞出梅菜，汁液留着用于勾芡。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>13. 猪肉凉后切成一公分厚块。取一深碗，梅菜放在底部，五花肉块排放在上面，再放到蒸锅中，用慢火炖30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555362" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555362-15.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555362-15-450x253.jpg" alt="猪肉凉后切成一公分厚块。取一深碗，梅菜放在底部，五花肉块排放在上面，再放到蒸锅中，用慢火炖30分钟。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555362" class="wp-caption-text">猪肉凉后切成一公分厚块。取一深碗，梅菜放在底部，五花肉块排放在上面，再放到蒸锅中，用慢火炖30分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>14. 将勾芡用酱汁煮滚。取出猪肉，淋上芡汁，放上芫荽装饰便可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555363" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555363" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555363-16.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555363" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555363-16-450x253.jpg" alt="将勾芡用酱汁煮滚。取出猪肉，淋上芡汁，放上芫荽装饰便可。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555363" class="wp-caption-text">将勾芡用酱汁煮滚。取出猪肉，淋上芡汁，放上芫荽装饰便可。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食谱说明：</h3>
<p>? 梅菜比较咸，所以浸泡时要多次换水，将梅菜浸淡。<br />
? 为了防止梅菜的叶子有沙，要尽量清洗干净。<br />
? 在猪皮上涂老抽，能够让肉的表面上色。<br />
? 要将猪肉放进热油之前，一定要准备好锅盖，一放入猪肉后立即盖上盖子，这样才能避免油爆出而灼伤自己。◇</p>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
