<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】夏果彩椒炒带子～家常开胃菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555396-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夏果彩椒炒带子。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>创意的家常菜，加入对味的坚果，让整道菜更具营养和特色。这是一道色香味俱全的炒菜佳肴，赶快学起来！</h3>
<h3>材料：</h3>
<p>急冻带子适量（不需要预先解冻）<br />
夏威夷果仁2 oz<br />
红、黄、青椒各2 oz<br />
紫洋葱3 oz<br />
姜、蒜片少许<br />
姜汁酒适量<br />
柠檬1个<br />
XO酱1汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555398" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555398-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555398-2-450x338.jpg" alt="（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555398" class="wp-caption-text">彩椒（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>鸡汤1/4杯<br />
烹大师1茶匙<br />
盐1/8茶匙<br />
糖1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555399" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555399-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555399-3-450x253.jpg" alt="（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555399" class="wp-caption-text">调味（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 煮热一锅水，加入姜汁酒、柠檬（榨汁后连皮放入）和带子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555400-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555400-4-450x338.jpg" alt="煮热一锅水，加入姜汁酒、柠檬（榨汁后连皮放入）和带子。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555400" class="wp-caption-text">煮热一锅水，加入姜汁酒、柠檬（榨汁后连皮放入）和带子。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 盖上锅盖，关火浸泡5分钟，捞出带子沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555401" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555401-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555401-5-450x253.jpg" alt="盖上锅盖，关火浸泡5分钟，捞出带子沥干备用。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555401" class="wp-caption-text">盖上锅盖，关火浸泡5分钟，捞出带子沥干备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 彩椒、洋葱切成小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555402" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555402-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555402-6-450x338.jpg" alt="彩椒、洋葱切成小块备用。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555402" class="wp-caption-text">彩椒、洋葱切成小块备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，爆香料头、洋葱和XO酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555403" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555403-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555403-7-450x253.jpg" alt="热锅下油，爆香料头、洋葱和XO酱。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555403" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，爆香料头、洋葱和XO酱。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放入彩椒炒一会再加入浸熟的带子，继续翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555404" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555404-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555404-8-450x253.jpg" alt="放入彩椒炒一会再加入浸熟的带子，继续翻炒。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555404" class="wp-caption-text">放入彩椒炒一会再加入浸熟的带子，继续翻炒。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 加入调味汁液快手兜匀便可上碟，上面放上夏威夷果仁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555405" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555405-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555405-9-450x253.jpg" alt="加入调味汁液快手兜匀便可上碟，上面放上夏威夷果仁。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555405" class="wp-caption-text">加入调味汁液快手兜匀便可上碟，上面放上夏威夷果仁。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>食谱说明：</strong></span></p>
<p>? 夏威夷果仁不需要放进去炒，因为吸收了汤汁后会变得不脆，放在炒好的菜上面便可。<br />
? 带子记着不要开火去煮，浸泡好的带子口感嫩滑。</p>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
