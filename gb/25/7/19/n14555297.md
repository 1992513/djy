<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】荷兰豆百合蘑菇炒三文鱼球</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555302-11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>荷兰豆百合蘑菇炒三文鱼球。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>三文鱼（鲑鱼）要怎么腌渍，同时做成鱼球，需要一点小技巧，今天的食谱就来公布这个小秘诀！</h3>
<p>材料：</p>
<p>荷兰豆4 oz<br />
鲜百合7 oz<br />
蘑菇4 oz<br />
三文鱼肉14 oz<br />
红萝卜片2 oz<br />
姜、蒜片少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555304" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555304-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555304-1-450x338.jpg" alt="材料。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555304" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味（此份量分成两份用）：</h3>
<p>鸡汤2/3杯<br />
烹大师1茶匙<br />
糖1/2茶匙<br />
盐1/2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1茶匙</p>
<h3>腌三文鱼材料：</h3>
<p>盐1/4茶匙<br />
烹大师1/2茶匙<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙<br />
生油1茶匙</p>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 荷兰豆撕去头尾洗净。鲜百合切去末段蒂部，一片片百合剥出来洗净。蘑菇切片。</p>
<p>2. 用刀切去三文鱼的皮，再把鱼肉切成厚小块状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555305-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555305-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555305" class="wp-caption-text">用刀切去三文鱼的皮，再把鱼肉切成厚小块状。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 三文鱼用腌料腌15分钟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555306" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555306-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555306-3-450x245.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555306" class="wp-caption-text">三文鱼用腌料腌15分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 烧滚水后放鱼肉，立即关火盖锅盖，浸1.5分钟后捞出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555307" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555307-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555307-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555307" class="wp-caption-text">浸1.5分钟后捞出。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 滚水中加油、盐，放荷兰豆、百合、红萝卜，烫1分钟捞起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555308-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555308-5-450x253.jpg" alt="滚水中加油、盐，放荷兰豆、百合、红萝卜，烫1分钟捞起" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555308" class="wp-caption-text">滚水中加油、盐，放荷兰豆、百合、红萝卜，烫1分钟捞起。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜片，放蘑菇、荷兰豆、百合、红萝卜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555309-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555309-6-450x253.jpg" alt="热锅下油，爆香。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555309" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，爆香食材。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 放入一半调味汁液，兜匀后盛出<span style="color: #800000;"><strong>（留下少许蔬菜炒第二次）</strong></span>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555310" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555310-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555310-7-450x253.jpg" alt="放入一半调味汁液，兜匀后盛出" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555310" class="wp-caption-text">放入一半调味汁液，兜匀后盛出（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 再次热锅下油，放进鱼肉及留出的蔬菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555311-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555311-8-450x338.jpg" alt="再次热锅下油，放进鱼肉及留出的蔬菜。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555311" class="wp-caption-text">再次热锅下油，放进鱼肉及留出的蔬菜。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 加入剩余调味汁液，快手翻炒一会之后便可装盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555312" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555312-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555312-9-450x338.jpg" alt="装盘。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555312" class="wp-caption-text">装盘。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食谱说明：</h3>
<p>? 这道菜分成两次炒，这样鱼球比较不会散。<br />
? 鱼球是用水浸熟的，鱼肉的口感会比较嫩滑。◇</p>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
