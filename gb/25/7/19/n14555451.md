<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】味噌蒟蒻牛舌汤～温润暖胃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555455-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>味噌蒟蒻牛舌汤。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>牛舌料理方式有多种，今天来做成汤品，加入各种蔬菜达到去腥效果，融合了牛舌的蛋白质，营养满满的，看起来美味可口！</h3>
<h3>材料：</h3>
<p>牛舌8 oz<br />
蒟蒻8 oz<br />
牛蒡12 oz<br />
秀珍菇6 oz<br />
红萝卜3 oz<br />
洋葱半个<br />
九层塔叶3棵<br />
牛舌高汤4杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555459" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555459-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555459-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555459" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>味噌2汤匙<br />
烹大师1汤匙<br />
味醂4汤匙<br />
盐半茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555460-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555460-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555460" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 牛舌切粒。牛蒡、红萝卜去皮后切片。洋葱切丝，秀珍菇切去末段后剥散。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555461" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555461-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555461-3-450x253.jpg" alt="牛舌切粒。牛蒡、红萝卜去皮后切片。洋葱切丝，秀珍菇切去末段后剥散。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555461" class="wp-caption-text">牛蒡、红萝卜去皮后切片。洋葱切丝，秀珍菇切去末段后剥散。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 汤锅内放入牛舌高汤，煮滚后放入牛蒡和红萝卜先煮10分钟，把牛蒡先煮软。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555462" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555462-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555462-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555462" class="wp-caption-text">汤锅内放入牛舌高汤，煮滚后放入牛蒡和红萝卜先煮10分钟，把牛蒡先煮软。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 随后加入牛舌、蒟蒻、秀珍菇和洋葱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555463" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555463-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555463-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555463" class="wp-caption-text">加入牛舌。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 味噌先用一些热汤把它磨溶，再与其它调味料一起放入汤内，煮10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555464-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555464-6-450x371.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555464" class="wp-caption-text">味噌先用一些热汤把它磨溶，再与其它调味料一起放入汤内，煮10分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 最后加入九层塔拌匀便可。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555465" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555465-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555465-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555465" class="wp-caption-text">最后加入九层塔拌匀便可。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
