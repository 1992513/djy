<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苑里镇表扬模范父亲暨好人好事代表 县长夫人恭贺所有得奖者</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555257-781902-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>苑里镇模范父亲表扬。（苗县府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月19日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苑里镇公所7月19日举办114年模范父亲暨好人好事代表表扬活动，苗栗县长夫人陈美琦到场恭贺，祝福所有爸爸们“父亲节快乐”，并感谢家眷热情参与，各个都以爸爸为荣，让人十分感动，期许好人好事代表的事迹能够作为大家的榜样，在父亲对家庭的坚持、付出，及善心人士对弱势、社区的贡献，让社会更加幸福、美满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555257-781902.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555257-781902-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555257" class="wp-caption-text">苑里镇模范父亲表扬。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年度共有25名模范父亲、1名好人好事代表，其中模范父亲最年长为高龄91岁山脚里的邱莲树先生，另有玉田里余启全、中正里郭东盛、福田里梁福春、泰田里傅和添、田心里刘正一、上馆里钟信行、房里里杨辉灶、海岸里吕文忠、南势里赖丰二、山柑里赖金龙、水坡里吴光华、苑坑里林春茂、苑南里曾宝霖、西势里刘顺修、苑东里陈振明、社苓里缪振昌、旧社里郑苍宏、苑港里朱进益、石镇里黄增村、蕉埔里陈南辉、新复里徐进发、客庄里郑朝栋、西平里林城、苑北里林堃荣；以及好人好事代表为田心里许丹瑜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555254" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555254-781899.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555254-781899-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555254" class="wp-caption-text">苑里镇模范父亲表扬（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14555255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555255" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555255-781900.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555255-781900-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555255" class="wp-caption-text">苑里镇模范父亲表扬.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14555256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555256" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555256-781901.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555256-781901-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555256" class="wp-caption-text">苑里镇模范父亲表扬.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>19日苑里镇公所于渔香园餐厅举办模范父亲暨好人好事代表表扬活动，县长夫人陈美琦恭喜受奖人及家庭，并与模范父亲、家眷共同合影留念，场面隆重、温馨，其中有位模范父亲辞世，但儿子们都到场为父亲接下荣耀，令县长夫人感动不已，代表深厚的亲子情感及美好的家庭氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555258" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555258-781903.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555258-781903-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555258" class="wp-caption-text">苑里镇好人好事代表表扬.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长夫人陈美琦感谢所有模范父亲，夫人表示，因为有他们的付出与坚持，才会有这么美好的家庭，有美好家庭社会就会更加和谐安康，而好人好事代表更是成为大家的楷模，社会上要多一些这样的人发挥自己所长，出钱出力协助需要协助的人，社会就会更加美好。</p>
