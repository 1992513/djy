<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】蒲烧鲷鱼蔬菜“炒饭”～创意家常菜！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555539-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蒲烧鲷鱼蔬菜炒饭。（梁厨美食提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>“蒲烧鲷鱼”大家一定不陌生，台式便当里的主角、日式寿司里的灵魂，但你有想过把它拿来炒饭吗？没错，这次我们就来做点不一样的——“蒲烧鲷鱼蔬菜炒饭”，看看这个少见的组合会擦出什么火花，相信会让你一口接一口停不下来！</h3>
<h3>材料：</h3>
<p>蒲烧鲷鱼腹肉8 oz<br />
洋葱1/2个<br />
红萝卜2 oz<br />
油菜粒3 oz<br />
鸡蛋2个<br />
芫荽碎少许<br />
白饭1.4磅</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555531" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555531-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555531-1-450x338.jpg" alt="洋葱、红萝卜和油菜梗切成小粒备用。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555531" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>生抽1汤匙<br />
蒲烧汁2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555532" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555532-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555532-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555532" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 洋葱、红萝卜和油菜梗切成小粒备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555533" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555533-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555533-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555533" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱、红萝卜和油菜梗切成小粒备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 烤箱预热至350℉，放入鲷鱼烤7分钟，烤好后取出切粗粒备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555534-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555534-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555534" class="wp-caption-text">鲷鱼烤好后取出切粗粒备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 鸡蛋打散。白饭可以用新鲜白饭来做，或用冷饭，放入微波炉加热2分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555535-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555535-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555535" class="wp-caption-text">白饭可以用新鲜白饭来做，或用冷饭，放入微波炉加热2分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，先把蔬菜粒炒香盛出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555536-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555536-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555536" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，先把蔬菜粒炒香盛出备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再下少许油，放入蛋液炒至半熟，再加入白饭，翻炒均匀至金黄色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555537-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555537-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555537" class="wp-caption-text">下少许油，放入蛋液炒至半熟，再加入白饭，翻炒均匀至金黄色。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 再放回蔬菜粒及鲷鱼粒一起翻炒，加入调味及芫荽兜匀便可。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555538-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555538-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555538" class="wp-caption-text">放回蔬菜粒及鲷鱼粒一起翻炒，最后加入调味及芫荽兜匀。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
