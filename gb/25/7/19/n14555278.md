<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】南瓜虾滑日式猪骨汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555279-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>南瓜虾滑日式猪骨汤。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>南瓜一年四季都有产出，搭配猪骨汤和“主厨特选食材”料理，料多味美还有海鲜味，清甜营养好入口！</h3>
<p>材料：</p>
<p>南瓜12 oz<br />
洋葱3 oz<br />
小蕃茄8 oz<br />
芫荽梗1.5 oz<br />
姜粒1汤匙<br />
虾滑6 oz<br />
蟹钳肉6 oz<br />
日式猪骨汤4杯<br />
鲜奶1杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555280" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555280-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555280-1-450x338.jpg" alt="材料" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555280" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>调味：</p>
<p>盐半茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
粟粉水3汤匙</p>
<p>作法：</p>
<p>1. 南瓜连皮切成粗粒备用。洋葱切小粒，芫荽梗切碎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555281" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555281-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555281-2-450x338.jpg" alt="南瓜连皮切成粗粒备用。洋葱切小粒。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555281" class="wp-caption-text">南瓜连皮切成粗粒备用。洋葱切小粒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 虾滑切成小粒备用。石蟹钳剥壳后取出蟹肉备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555282-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555282-3-450x253.jpg" alt="石蟹钳剥壳后取出蟹肉备用。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555282" class="wp-caption-text">石蟹钳剥壳后取出蟹肉备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油，放入洋葱、姜粒炒香，再放入南瓜翻炒一会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555283" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555283-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555283-4-450x338.jpg" alt="热锅下油，放入洋葱、姜粒炒香，再放入南瓜翻炒一会。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555283" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，放入洋葱、姜粒炒香，再放入南瓜翻炒一会。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 加入猪骨汤，盖上锅盖，用中火煮10分钟。开盖后放蕃茄、芫荽、虾滑和蟹肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555284-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555284-5-450x338.jpg" alt="放蕃茄、芫荽、虾滑和蟹肉。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555284" class="wp-caption-text">放蕃茄、芫荽、虾滑和蟹肉。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 然后再加入鲜奶，煮8分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555285" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555285-e7aa1d09ea9248927ecde50b7f647afb.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555285-e7aa1d09ea9248927ecde50b7f647afb-450x338.jpg" alt="加入鲜奶，煮8分钟" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555285" class="wp-caption-text">加入鲜奶，煮8分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 最后加入调味料和粟粉水，煮成稠滑状。便可装入汤碗，放上芫荽装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555286" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555286-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555286-7-450x338.jpg" alt="加入调味料和粟粉水，煮成稠滑状。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555286" class="wp-caption-text">加入调味料和粟粉水，煮成稠滑状。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食谱说明：</h3>
<p>? 这次使用的猪骨汤膏是日本出产，一汤匙可以加一杯水做成日式猪骨汤。<br />
? 牛奶是用南日本雾岛山蔍牛乳，很有奶味，香滑浓郁。◇</p>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
