<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】蒜苔炒湖南腊肉～经典菜！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555583-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蒜苔炒湖南腊肉。（梁厨美食提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<h3 class="sub_title">“湖南腊肉”是远近驰名的家乡味，经过秘制酱料长腌制，再以炭火、甘蔗皮、稻谷等天然素材熏香，造就出浓郁独特的烟熏口味。今天，老师傅亲自下厨，教我们如何将这道经典腊肉菜炒得香气四溢，咸香入味！</h3>
<h3>材料：</h3>
<p>蒜苔12 oz（Garlic core）<br />
湖南腊肉12oz（Hunan style cured pork）<br />
洋葱丝1/2个<br />
萝卜仔2 oz（radish）<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555574" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555574-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555574-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555574" class="wp-caption-text">湖南腊肉12oz。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>鸡汤1/4杯<br />
糖1/4茶匙<br />
蚝油1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555575" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555575-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555575-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555575" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 腊肉烫过后切成片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555576" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555576-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555576-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555576" class="wp-caption-text">腊肉烫过后切成片。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 切好的腊肉隔水蒸7分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555577" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555577-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555577-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555577" class="wp-caption-text">切好的腊肉隔水蒸7分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蒜苔切4cm长段，洗干净备用。萝卜仔切片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555578" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555578-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555578-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555578" class="wp-caption-text">蒜苔切4cm长段，洗干净备用。萝卜仔切片。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、洋葱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555579" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555579-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555579-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555579" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、洋葱。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放入蒜苔和萝卜仔，加水2汤匙，炒半分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555580" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555580-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555580-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555580" class="wp-caption-text">放入蒜苔和萝卜仔，加水2汤匙，炒半分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 再放回腊肉，搅匀调味汁倒入，兜匀后便可上碟。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555581" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555581-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555581-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555581" class="wp-caption-text">再放回腊肉，搅匀调味汁倒入，兜匀后便可上碟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
