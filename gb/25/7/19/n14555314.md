<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】瑶柱蟹味菇焖栉瓜脯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555315-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>瑶柱蟹味菇焖栉瓜脯。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>夏天吃“瓜”清凉退火，今天来做一道“栉瓜脯”特色菜。</h3>
<h3>材料：</h3>
<p>栉瓜1.5磅<br />
蟹味菇6 oz<br />
蒸熟的瑶柱3 oz<br />
姜4片<br />
鸡汤2杯<br />
粟粉水2汤匙<br />
麻油少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555316-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555316-1-450x338.jpg" alt="材料。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555316" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>蚝油1汤匙<br />
瑶柱汁2汤匙<br />
糖1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 栉瓜去皮，切开成4长条，再次切半，然后切除瓜瓤，洗净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555317-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555317-2-450x338.jpg" alt="栉瓜去皮，切开成4长条，再次切半，然后切除瓜瓤，洗净备用。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555317" class="wp-caption-text">栉瓜去皮，切开成4长条，再次切半，然后切除瓜瓤，洗净备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 蟹味菇切去末段根部，剥后洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555318-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555318-3-450x338.jpg" alt="蟹味菇切去末段根部，剥后洗干净。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555318" class="wp-caption-text">蟹味菇切去末段根部，剥后洗干净。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油2汤匙，先把姜片放入煎香，再放入栉瓜，同样煎香约2分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555319-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555319-4-450x338.jpg" alt="热锅下油2汤匙，先把姜片放入煎香，再放入栉瓜，同样煎香约2分钟。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555319" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油2汤匙，先把姜片放入煎香，再放入栉瓜，同样煎香约2分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 加入鸡汤、瑶柱，盖上锅盖煮12分钟至栉瓜稍软。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555320" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555320-4d773103cdfb86f635a1ee17145dbbfc.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555320-4d773103cdfb86f635a1ee17145dbbfc-450x338.jpg" alt="加入鸡汤、瑶柱，盖上锅盖煮12分钟至栉瓜稍软。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555320" class="wp-caption-text">加入鸡汤、瑶柱，盖上锅盖煮12分钟至栉瓜稍软。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再加入调味料及蟹味菇兜匀，煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555321-8ac524ffa09dc1307b57f09a64a63abf.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555321-8ac524ffa09dc1307b57f09a64a63abf-450x338.jpg" alt="再加入调味料及蟹味菇兜匀，煮5分钟。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555321" class="wp-caption-text">再加入调味料及蟹味菇兜匀，煮5分钟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 最后用粟粉水勾芡，淋上麻油便可上碟。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555322-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555322-7-450x338.jpg" alt="最后用粟粉水勾芡，淋上麻油便可上碟。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555322" class="wp-caption-text">最后用粟粉水勾芡，淋上麻油便可上碟。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
