<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】XO酱韭菜洋葱炒刀蚬～一次学两道菜！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555324-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>XO酱韭菜洋葱炒刀蚬。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>今天这道料理“XO酱韭菜洋葱炒刀蚬”，如果没有一次吃完，剩下的料理还可以再变换成“湿炒刀蚬韭菜河粉”，下次教您怎么做，敬请期待！</h3>
<h3>材料：</h3>
<p>刀蚬6只<br />
韭菜4 oz<br />
洋葱2 oz<br />
甜红椒2 oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许<br />
XO酱2茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555325" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555325-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555325-1-450x338.jpg" alt="（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555325" class="wp-caption-text">材料（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>调味：</h3>
<p>蚝油1汤匙<br />
生抽1汤匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
鸡汤1/4杯<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙</p>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 刀蚬用刀起出肉，切除肠脏，清洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555326" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555326-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555326-2-450x338.jpg" alt="刀蚬用刀起出肉，切除肠脏，清洗干净备用。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555326" class="wp-caption-text">刀蚬用刀起出肉，切除肠脏，清洗干净备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 韭菜用头的部分，切成两段长，洗干净备用。洋葱切丝，红椒切丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555327" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555327-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555327-3-450x338.jpg" alt="韭菜用头的部分，切成两段长，洗干净备用。洋葱切丝，红椒切丝。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555327" class="wp-caption-text">韭菜用头的部分，切成两段长。洋葱切丝。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 烧热一锅水，加入少许姜汁酒，放入刀蚬肉烫20秒，捞出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555328" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555328-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555328-4-450x253.jpg" alt="烧热一锅水，加入少许姜汁酒，放入刀蚬肉烫20秒，捞出备用。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555328" class="wp-caption-text">烧热一锅水，加入少许姜汁酒，放入刀蚬肉烫20秒，捞出备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、XO酱和洋葱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555329-5b9dbf64ad51e9febbe91b96bc7c0673.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555329-5b9dbf64ad51e9febbe91b96bc7c0673-450x338.jpg" alt="热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、XO酱和洋葱。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555329" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、XO酱和洋葱。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 加入韭菜、红椒兜炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555330" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555330-cadb8eb8599b7933e1f1933ae95bad2a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555330-cadb8eb8599b7933e1f1933ae95bad2a-450x253.jpg" alt="加入韭菜、红椒兜炒。" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555330" class="wp-caption-text">加入韭菜、红椒兜炒。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 接着加入刀蚬。搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入兜匀即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555331-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14555331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555331-7-450x338.jpg" alt="接着加入刀蚬。搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入兜匀即完成。" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555331" class="wp-caption-text">接着加入刀蚬。搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入兜匀即完成。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">食谱说明：刀蚬有很多幼沙，一定要清洗干净。不要烫太久否则会韧。</span></strong></p>
<h3>主厨梁志生经历：</h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
