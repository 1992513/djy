<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>雨后的大目降 时光缓步中的文化聚落</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555086-781878-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>近年深受游客喜爱的疗愈打卡点，结合和风与童趣。（郑建南提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月19日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）“6月29日下了一场大雨的午后”，摄影家郑建南如此注记这张照片的拍摄时刻。镜头低伏于水洼边缘，一幢充满和洋风格的古建筑映照在地面薄水中，墙体的金黄与瓦顶的沉静，交织出一段静谧的午后时光。地点正是台南新化，昔日称为“大目降”的历史聚落。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555087" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555087-781879.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14555087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555087-781879-600x694.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555087" class="wp-caption-text">摄影家郑建南于雨后低角度捕捉建筑倒影，屋瓦与窗櫺在水面交织出一幅静谧而深沉的画面。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14555088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555088" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555088-781881.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14555088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555088-781881-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555088" class="wp-caption-text">园区内巨大招财猫装置，深受游客喜爱的打卡点。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>日式武德馆的再现记忆</h2>
<p>这幢建筑是新化武德殿，落成于日治时期昭和十二年（1937年），当时作为日本警察与青年训练武术的场所。武德馆在各地日治行政中心普遍设有，而新化这座特别保存良好，为砖木混合建筑，屋顶采用传统日本黑瓦，立面留有弧形飞檐与典雅圆窗，散发东洋古典的气韵。</p>
<p>在文化部与地方社区的努力下，如今的武德殿已成为大目降文化园区的一部分，转型为文创展览与表演空间，让历史与当代在此交错呼吸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555081" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555081-781873.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14555081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555081-781873-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555081" class="wp-caption-text">新化武德殿外观，昭和十二年落成的日式建筑。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14555084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555084" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555084-781876.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14555084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555084-781876-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555084" class="wp-caption-text">左右展示柔道与剑道装备，中间陈列武士刀与屋瓦，完整体现日本时代的武道教育空间。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>武道与工艺的精神场域</h2>
<p>走进武德殿内部，挑高屋顶与裸露的木梁构成一种庄严感。地板由原木铺设，反射着窗外洒入的日光。墙边展示着剑道与柔道的道服、传统日式刀具及瓦片模型，仿佛在述说那个讲求精神与纪律的年代。</p>
<p>另一侧墙上挂着一套完整的剑道护具，旁边展示的是一袭洁白的柔道服，两者相对而立，不仅是运动装备的呈现，更是传承武道精神的象征。</p>
<p>访客步入其中，能感受到空间的静谧，宛如历史的气息仍在场内回荡。偶有社区团体在此进行太极、剑道练习，让这栋历史建筑不再只是被观赏的遗迹，而是一个仍在呼吸的文化场域。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555082" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555082-781874.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14555082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555082-781874-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555082" class="wp-caption-text">展示区呈现日式武道精神，紫柄武士刀与传统瓦片并陈，象征技艺与工艺的并行传承。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14555080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555080-781872.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14555080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555080-781872-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555080" class="wp-caption-text">武德殿内部结构，挑高屋顶与裸露木梁构成日式建筑特有的庄严氛围。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14555083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555083" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555083-781875.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14555083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555083-781875-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555083" class="wp-caption-text">一袭洁白柔道服静静伫立，仿佛等待下一位习武者进入，展现昔日青少年训练武德的画面。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>旧地新生 文史与创意共舞</h2>
<p>新化大目降文化园区不只保存了武德殿，还包括日式宿舍群、神社遗构与清代的街屋建筑。户外的鸟居、石灯与红砖步道，在风雨交错中保有一种跨时代的诗意。</p>
<p>而在一旁的“猫之驿”，则以可爱的巨大招财猫与日式茶屋氛围，吸引年轻族群驻足拍照、品茗小憩。红色鸟居下的木匾写着“猫之驿”，是怀旧与疗愈兼具的新地标。</p>
<p>不论是历史控还是喜欢文青风的旅人，都能在大目降找到适合自己节奏的片刻。这里的时光不急不徐，就如同郑建南镜头下的倒影，让人愿意放慢脚步，好好感受雨后微光下的文化呼吸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14555089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14555089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555089-781882.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14555089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14555089-781882-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14555089" class="wp-caption-text">以日式神社意象打造的入口，木牌题字“猫之驿”，融合历史建筑与文创氛围。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
