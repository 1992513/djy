<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小小探险家齐聚桃园开南大学 暑假营队超好玩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553305-781359-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>那一年，我们在开南大学的暑假营队。（开南大学提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月17日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）校园优美的开南大学，不仅是偶像剧的取景基地，更积极发挥桃园地区的社会责任与教育资源，办理“2025年度暑期夏令营”，吸引众多小朋友参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14553306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14553306" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553306-781360.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14553306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553306-781360.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="571" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14553306" class="wp-caption-text">消耗汗水与体力，小朋友校园竞技。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>营队设计活动的多元化，能让小孩在休闲和学习中得到成长。开南大学进修及推广教育处表示，这次营队课程内容丰富，从激发创意思维的魔法科学实验、挑战团队合作与体能的趣味闯关活动，到提升语感与自信表达的主题美语互动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14553308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14553308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553308-781362.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14553308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553308-781362.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="508" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14553308" class="wp-caption-text">学习动手乐趣的餐点手作DIY。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14553311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14553311" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553311-781365.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14553311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553311-781365.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="512" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14553311" class="wp-caption-text">科学实验与团体活动。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有学习动手乐趣的餐点手作DIY，以及发挥开南大学棒球队的体育特色，设计锻炼反应力与团队精神的“乐乐棒球+5人棒球”，可以说，每一天都让校园充满小朋友惊喜与笑声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14553307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14553307" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553307-781361.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14553307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553307-781361.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14553307" class="wp-caption-text">让小孩在休闲和学习中得到成长。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>王小弟弟开心地说，我最喜欢魔法科学课，因为可以动手做出好多特别的东西！李小妹妹则笑着表示，和朋友一起挑战闯关游戏很刺激，老师们也都很有耐心！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14553309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14553309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553309-781363.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14553309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553309-781363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14553309" class="wp-caption-text">利用大学生的厨艺教室，小朋友也能做出美味餐点。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>暑期营队透过一系列设计用心的活动，让孩子们在快乐中学习、在探索中成长，不仅累积了丰富的经验，也交到了许多新朋友。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14553310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14553310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553310-781364.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14553310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14553310-781364.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14553310" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友享受美食。（开南大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动由开南大学进修推广教育处举办，未来将持续规划更多精彩课程，尤其配合开大国际化表现，还会推出“双语营队”，欢迎家长与孩子们共同关注参与。◇</p>
