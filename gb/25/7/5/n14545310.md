<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>张善政见证颁奖 桃园超大西瓜90台斤超大南瓜542.8 台斤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545311-778470-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政5日出席“114年桃园市西瓜评鉴暨观音区超级大南瓜颁奖典礼”。（桃园市府农业局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政5日出席“114年桃园市西瓜评鉴暨观音区超级大南瓜颁奖典礼”，评鉴结果出炉，超级大西瓜重达90台斤，由吴文忠夺冠；大西瓜及小西瓜组分别由李荣锋及廖石水夺冠；超级大南瓜重达542.8台斤，由陈荣富夺冠，同时亦荣获全国季军，充分展现桃园农友优异栽培技术与用心成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14545312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545312" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545312-778471.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14545312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545312-778471-600x589.jpg" alt="" width="456" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545312" class="wp-caption-text">超级大南瓜重达542.8台斤，由陈荣富夺冠。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政表示，桃园市西瓜种植总面积为177.37公顷，其中观音区就占130.18公顷，为本市西瓜种植最大区域。期许透过评鉴活动，提升种植技术与农产品品质，也让更多民众认识在地优质瓜果，强化桃园农特产品曝光率，带动农产经济。</p>
<p>农业局长陈冠义表示，今年共计15组报名角逐超级大西瓜、大西瓜22组、小西瓜61组，参赛果农分别代表观音区、大园区及新屋区农会，透过竞赛切磋交流，提升技术同时行销桃园在地优质瓜果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14545313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545313" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545313-778472.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14545313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545313-778472-600x344.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545313" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政等品尝西瓜。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14545314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545314" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545314-778473.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14545314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545314-778473-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545314" class="wp-caption-text">黄色西瓜。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>农友吴文忠种出90台斤西瓜，获得超大西瓜冠军；而大西瓜冠军为李荣锋，得奖西瓜重量46斤、甜度13.2度；小西瓜冠军得奖农友为廖石水，得奖西瓜重量9台斤6两、最甜西瓜16.7度；此外，超级大南瓜冠军542.8台斤，由瓜农陈荣富摘下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14545315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545315" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545315-778474.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14545315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545315-778474-600x728.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="546" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545315" class="wp-caption-text">红肉西瓜。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14545316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545316-778475.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14545316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545316-778475-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545316" class="wp-caption-text">西瓜评鉴标准包含外观、果肉质地、风味口感、糖度及果重平均（单位台斤）。（桃园市府农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈冠义说明，西瓜评鉴标准包含外观、果肉质地、风味口感、糖度及果重平均（单位台斤），只要居住及种植于桃园市之瓜农，大西瓜（华宝）28台斤以上、小西瓜（黄肉品种）8台斤以上、超级大西瓜50台斤以上，即符合报名资格，西瓜果肉未熟、过熟、酸败者则淘汰。◇</p>
