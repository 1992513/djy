<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>GEMS EXPO 2025：宝石博览会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545601-1-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月06日讯】全球珠宝、宝石与设计爱好者引颈期盼的盛事——Gems Expo 2025，将于2025年7月25日至27日再度在多伦多闪耀登场。</p>
<div class="post-entry blockquote-style-1">
<div id="penci-post-entry-inner" class="inner-post-entry entry-content">
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-326853" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-441-1170-500-1024x438-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="438" />这场极具规模与专业水准的展览，汇聚了来自世界各地的宝石商、设计师、收藏家与工艺师。Gems Expo 2025 将呈现顶尖珠宝工艺与最新设计潮流，是业界人士与消费者不容错过的一大亮点。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-326850" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-119-1170-500-1024x438-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="438" /></p>
<p>Gems Expo 2025 不只是宝石与珠宝的展示平台，更是一场集结艺术、设计、工艺与国际视野的专业盛会。无论是珠宝行业从业者，或是对美学与天然能量感兴趣的大众，皆能在这数天的展会中收获灵感、知识与珍宝。让我们在7月的多伦多，共赴这场闪耀无比的艺术与工艺盛典！</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-326849" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-99-1170-500-1024x438-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="438" />??<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-326857" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-606-1170-500-1024x438-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="438" /><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>国际宝石与矿石展示</strong></span></p>
<p>Gems Expo 汇集多样化的天然宝石、矿石、半宝石与水晶原石，包括祖母绿、红宝石、蓝宝石、紫水晶、月光石、玛瑙、碧玺等。无论是专业买家、设计师还是收藏初学者，都能在这里寻找到心仪的宝石原材料与精品成品。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-326855" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-506-1170-500-1024x438-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="438" /><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-326848" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-16-1170-500-1024x438-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="438" /><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>独立设计师珠宝专区</strong></span></p>
<p>展会中特设独立珠宝设计师区域，呈现来自加拿大及国际设计师的创作，从手工银饰、金工、宝石镶嵌到创意混材设计，展现时尚与工艺的完美结合。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-326854" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-0463-1170-500-1024x436-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="436" /><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-326851" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-307-1170-500-768x341-1.jpg" alt="" width="905" b="402" /><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>工具与材料供应商参展</strong></span></p>
<p>除成品展示外，Gems Expo 也吸引大量珠宝制作工具、设计软体、DIY材料供应商参与，为从业者与兴趣者提供一站式采购与学习机会。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-326852" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo0347-1170-500-1024x438-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="438" /><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>实作示范与讲座活动</strong></span></p>
<p>展览期间将安排珠宝制作现场示范与专题讲座，由业界专家、宝石鉴定师、设计师分享趋势与知识。主题涵盖：宝石鉴别、设计灵感、永续材料应用等，是提升专业视野的绝佳机会。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-326858" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/gem-expo-1170-500-1024x438-1.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="438" /><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>现场销售与收藏咨询</strong></span></p>
<p>Gems Expo 同时开放一般民众参观选购，无论是为婚礼寻找独一无二的订制珠宝，或是想选购天然水晶作为能量收藏，都可与专业卖家直接交流，获得个人化建议与保障来源的保证。</p>
