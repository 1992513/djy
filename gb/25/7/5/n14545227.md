<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>龙门海蚀洞 海与天之间的秘境画框</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545239-778487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这样的场域，是大自然的摄影棚，不需道具、不需滤镜，只需天光与海风参与，就已经足够。（陈锡兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬澎湖报导）澎湖湖西乡龙门村，一段看似平凡的玄武岩海岸，在潮汐拉开帷幕后，悄悄展现一处天然秘境。</p>
<p>这里不是热门观光地，也无华丽设施，有的只是岩层与海水，风与光的默契配合。退潮之际，海岸边一道低矮的洞口现身，从外观看不出太多端倪，真正的惊喜，藏在走入的那一刻。</p>
<p>摄影家陈锡兴于2025年7月4日踏入洞内，镜头对准洞口望向外海，他说：“第一次站在洞里往外看，海天一色的蓝简直像静止的画布，让人舍不得移开目光。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14545229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545229-778477.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14545229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545229-778477-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545229" class="wp-caption-text">龙门海岸在退潮时露出完整的玄武岩地形，岩面龟裂如鳞，延展至海天交界处。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14545230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545230" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545230-778478.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14545230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545230-778478-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545230" class="wp-caption-text">这里不是热门观光地，也无华丽设施，有的只是岩层与海水，风与光的默契配合。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>火山遗迹? 凿出一扇通往蓝色的窗</h2>
<p>
龙门海岸是典型的玄武岩地形，粗犷、破碎，表面布满柱状与球状节理，如同经历千锤百炼的肌理。这些岩层来自万年前火山活动，经海浪侵蚀、盐分风化，再加上海水潮汐每日规律的来回，终于在岩壁上开出这扇“窗”。</p>
<p>洞穴不深，但内部总有一小滩积水。水面静时，蓝天与砾石倒映其中，虚实难辨；风起时，水光微动，画面像是被打开的梦境。站在洞内，前方是太平洋无垠的蓝，脚下则是镜面般的水洼，实景与倒影交叠，令人分不清哪里是真实，哪里是幻境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14545233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545233-778481.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14545233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545233-778481-600x364.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="364"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545233" class="wp-caption-text">站在洞内，前方是太平洋无垠的蓝。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14545231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545231-778479.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14545231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545231-778479-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545231" class="wp-caption-text">龙门海岸是典型的玄武岩地形，粗犷、破碎，表面布满柱状与球状节理，如同经历千锤百炼的肌理。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14545232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545232-778480.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14545232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545232-778480-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545232" class="wp-caption-text">洞穴不深，但内部总有一小滩积水。水面静时，蓝天与砾石倒映其中，虚实难辨。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>退潮限定? 美景只献给愿意等待的人</h2>
这处洞穴并不总是敞开，必须等海水退去才现身。若没抓准时间，水位一涨，通往洞口的路就被淹没了。当地摄影人熟门熟路，总是在退潮时刻才往这里走。</p>
<p>从岸边出发，必须踏过层层玄武岩。有的裂纹如鳞片，有的像龟甲，一路崎岖而壮观。途中还会经过几处小型潮池，藏着火山砾石、海藻、贝类与小生物，像是海洋的小书签，每一页都有秘密。</p>
<p>经过一段缓坡与碎石，洞穴便静静现身。外观看似平凡，真正令人屏息的，是走进洞里，望出去的那个瞬间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14545234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545234" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545234-778482.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14545234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545234-778482-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545234" class="wp-caption-text">退潮限定，美景只献给愿意等待的人。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14545235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545235" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545235-778483.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14545235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545235-778483-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545235" class="wp-caption-text">途中还会经过几处小型潮池，藏着火山砾石、海藻、贝类与小生物，像是海洋的小书签，每一页都有秘密。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>自然的画框? 静止的构图</h2>
“任何姿势，都能成为一幅画。”陈锡兴如此形容这座洞穴的魅力。他补充道，洞口本身就是一个天然画框，站、坐、转身、远眺、抬手，每个角度都能构出属于自己的风景。</p>
<p>尤其是水面倒影与洞外风光的双重构图，常让摄影人流连忘返。一张张作品仿佛拍摄于幻境，却全然真实无修。</p>
<p>这样的场域，是大自然的摄影棚，不需道具、不需滤镜，只需天光与海风参与，就已经足够。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14545228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545228" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545228-778476.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14545228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545228-778476-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545228" class="wp-caption-text">龙门海蚀洞，海与天之间的秘境画框。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14545236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14545236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545236-778484.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14545236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14545236-778484-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14545236" class="wp-caption-text">一张张作品仿佛拍摄于幻境，却全然真实无修。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一处被时间保护的海岸</h2>
龙门村不在主流观光动线上，旅人多半直奔西屿、吉贝或望安，而这片原始海岸，反倒保留了属于澎湖最原初的模样。</p>
<p>玄武岩、潮间带与海蚀洞彼此交织成一座低调的地质展馆。站在洞口那一刻，你会发现，这里没有喧嚣、没有打卡点，只有阳光、水声与一片从画框中望出去的蓝。</p>
<p>那是一种纯粹，一种让人愿意驻足许久的宁静。</p>
<p>而龙门，就这样静静地等着下一个愿意低头走入、抬头望海的旅人。</p>
