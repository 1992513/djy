<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【旅行趣】台东慢旅：走进山与海的温柔怀抱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560562-fa8ce1f3dda3b0ee5ede58ffc8d8c384-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>有一种蓝叫“台东蓝”，令人片刻宁静的奇幻色彩，俯拾即是，只需旅人驻足去感受。（图／王芊芊提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>走进台东，就像翻开一本绚烂的风景画册，天空蓝、大地绿渲染成最美的记忆。无论乘着热气球飞上天际，或在绿野间随风漫步，台东总有一种温柔的气息，等待旅人找回内心的淡定与宁静。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14560558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560558" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560558-cd9727e35d66e5b06d1b28c7b0f2b93a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14560558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560558-cd9727e35d66e5b06d1b28c7b0f2b93a-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560558" class="wp-caption-text">五彩缤纷的热气球缓缓升空，像一颗颗梦想泡泡飘向天际。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14560567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560567-3239ba0736fdaf065d50735ad2c39068.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14560567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560567-3239ba0736fdaf065d50735ad2c39068-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560567" class="wp-caption-text">造型各异其趣的热气球们随着音乐喷火起舞，将鹿野高台点缀成最绮丽的舞台。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>鹿野高台热气球嘉年华</h3>
<p>日落时分，坐在视野辽阔的鹿野高台草地上，看着五彩缤纷的热气球缓缓升空，像一颗颗梦想泡泡飘向天际。当夜幕降临，光雕音乐秀热闹登场，造型各异其趣的热气球们随着音乐喷火起舞，将整座高台点缀成最绮丽的舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14560569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560569" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560569-a62f6d337ee253c5e22965fce54be366.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560569-a62f6d337ee253c5e22965fce54be366-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560569" class="wp-caption-text">骑上自行车，穿梭在池上的田间小径，仿佛回到追着稻浪嬉戏的童年。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14560563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560563" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560563-a28278002bd888c2639ee88aeec186e0.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560563-a28278002bd888c2639ee88aeec186e0-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560563" class="wp-caption-text">池上金城武树，优雅的伫立在田中央，为旅人庇荫遮凉。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>池上骑行，在稻浪中飞翔</h3>
<p>池上这片绿意盎然的平原，坐落于中央山脉与海岸山脉之间，是孕育稻作的沃土。在农村长大的我，长年生活于都市丛林中，逐渐遗忘了那最熟悉的泥土气味。骑着带棚自行车，就像跳上一片会飞的叶子，顺着伯朗大道与天堂路滑行，穿梭在池上的田间小径，宛如回到追着稻浪嬉戏的童年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14560568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560568" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560568-af0c2316c4865ef780e8808d039bd117.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560568-af0c2316c4865ef780e8808d039bd117-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560568" class="wp-caption-text">静静坐在大坡池畔，等待的不是鱼，而是一份久违的自在。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>大坡池畔的静谧时光</h3>
<p>位于台东池上的大坡池，是一座被群山环抱的天然湖泊。站在池畔望去，湖面如镜，倒映着中央山脉的绵延山影与流动云彩。沿着环湖步道漫步或骑行，让人沉浸在大自然的静谧之中，仿佛时间也慢了下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14560565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560565" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560565-9f234371dce04da76f25f35e403a3a19.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560565-9f234371dce04da76f25f35e403a3a19-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560565" class="wp-caption-text">长滨茄苳林荫大道，沿途有宽阔的大草原。草浪随风摇曳，时间仿佛也慢了下来。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>茄苳林荫大道，东海岸的私房景点</h3>
<p>长滨乡的茄苳林荫大道，坐落于东13乡道上，是一段绵延约一公里的绿色隧道，两侧茄苳老树绵延成荫，为日治时期的战备植林遗迹。走进茄苳林荫大道，仿佛步入一条通往静谧自然的长廊。沿途宽阔的草原随风摇曳，令人心旷神怡。在这里缓步、驻足，感受时光缓缓流动，是体验东海岸纯净风光的绝佳秘境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14560566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560566" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560566-45b6fdf66d205794f7fc7d3784f4f747.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560566-45b6fdf66d205794f7fc7d3784f4f747-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560566" class="wp-caption-text">夏末初秋，金针花沿着六十石山尽情绽放，宛如金色浪潮层层翻涌。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14560559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560559" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560559-d29e74a69543e02bbecb85dc7eb665bc.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560559-d29e74a69543e02bbecb85dc7eb665bc-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560559" class="wp-caption-text">六十石山金针花海，是花东纵谷最迷人的季节限定风景。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>六十石山金针花海</h3>
<p>每到夏末初秋，金针花沿着六十石山的山坡尽情绽放，宛如金色浪潮层层翻涌。与远方山峦、蓝天白云交织成一幅壮丽的画卷，置身其中，仿佛走进了童话山城，是花东纵谷一年一度最迷人的季节限定风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14560561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560561" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560561-03a350fabade3919410567ec87f09026.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560561-03a350fabade3919410567ec87f09026-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560561" class="wp-caption-text">台东慢旅，无须赶路。最美的风景，也许就在路上。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14560560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560560" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560560-fbc039c7e234243b247891c5cb688b3a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560560-fbc039c7e234243b247891c5cb688b3a-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560560" class="wp-caption-text">台东有漫长的海岸线，澎湃的浪涛层层涌来，仿佛将一切忧烦，卷入深蓝的海沟封藏。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>结语</h3>
<p>台东不喧闹却动人。旅行的意义，或许就是在途中，重新遇见那个被生活遗忘的自己。当你合上这本画册，也许会发现，走进山与海的温柔怀抱，疲惫的心灵已被悄悄疗愈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14560564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14560564" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560564-a936167251358f8dd0b6d318d45fdc77.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14560564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14560564-a936167251358f8dd0b6d318d45fdc77-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14560564" class="wp-caption-text">壮丽的大山很美，但更动人的，是藏在山里的人情味。（图／王芊芊提供）</figcaption></figure>
