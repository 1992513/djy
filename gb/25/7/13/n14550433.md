<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>屏东县牡丹乡旭海村海祭音乐会 原民会曾智勇出席</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550434-780377-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>曾智勇LjaucuZingrur主任委员致词。（原住民族委员会提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月13日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）原民会曾智勇Ljaucu?Zingrur主委出席“屏东县牡丹乡海祭音乐会”，与当地族人一同参与兼具文化仪式与当代表演的盛会，曾智勇表示：“文化不只是传承，更要发光，原民会将持续支持部落文化创新与青年参与，让文化力成为原住民族前行的动力。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550435-780378.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14550435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550435-780378-600x354.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="354" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550435" class="wp-caption-text">原民会曾智勇LjaucuZingrur主委、伍丽华SaidhaiTahovecahe委员、屏东县周春米县长等来宾一同欢庆屏东县牡丹乡旭海村海祭音乐会。（原住民族委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>旭海村每年举行的“海祭”不仅是部落岁时祭仪，更是一场跨世代连结与族群认同的重要仪式。而海祭音乐会，透过乐舞演出与青年创作，展现文化创新的实践成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550436-780379.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14550436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550436-780379-600x247.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="247" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550436" class="wp-caption-text">屏东县牡丹乡旭海村海祭音乐会“旭海部落青年会”开幕表演。（原住民族委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14550439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550439-780382.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14550439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550439-780382-600x229.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="229" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550439" class="wp-caption-text">屏东县牡丹乡旭海村海祭音乐会“大溪地悦舞集Ori Hō’oa”演出。（原住民族委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曾智勇Ljaucu?Zingrur指出，原住民族的文化力来自土地、语言、歌谣与生活，而“海祭音乐会”正是文化与当代表达融合的展现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550437-780380.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14550437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550437-780380-600x242.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="242" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550437" class="wp-caption-text">屏东县牡丹乡旭海村海祭音乐会“三十而立”演出。（原住民族委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曾智勇强调，原民会透过各项计划补助，支持部落举办文化活动，也鼓励青年投入传统祭仪、乐舞创作与文化产业发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14550438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14550438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550438-780381.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14550438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14550438-780381-600x242.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="242" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14550438" class="wp-caption-text">屏东县牡丹乡旭海村海祭音乐会“你太白了，去晒黑”演出。（原住民族委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曾智勇Ljaucu?Zingrur表示，感谢部落耆老、艺文创作者及参与筹备的青年朋友们，齐心让旭海的文化被更多人看见与听见，原民会将与部落站在一起，持续推动文化活化与创新，让传统根基在当代土壤中茁壮。◇</p>
