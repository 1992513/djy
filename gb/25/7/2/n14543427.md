<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>盐水溪的暮色情书 夏日河海之恋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543429-777960-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>单车与桥影在霞光中交会，仿佛时间静止。（郑建南提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）每年夏天的傍晚，台南的盐水溪总会静静演出一场光与影的浪漫。那天摄影家郑建南站在出海口，举起相机，捕捉这片河海交界处的微妙情感。霞光柔软，风声轻轻，就连空气中似乎都搅着一丝恋爱的甜味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543435-777966.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543435-777966-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543435" class="wp-caption-text">傍晚的云霞在水面上展开，一艘艘小船像回家的旅人，等候潮汐。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14543428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543428-777959.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14543428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543428-777959-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543428" class="wp-caption-text">捕捉夕阳的刹那。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>原始水岸的静谧</h2>
<p>盐水溪，是台南少见仍保有原始风貌的水域。这里没有高楼遮蔽视线，只有辽阔的水平线和被风吹皱的水面。黄昏来临时，天空染上金橘与粉蓝，倒映在溪水上，像是一封写给大海的诗，一笔一画都诚恳得让人屏息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543433" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543433-777964.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543433-777964-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543433" class="wp-caption-text">穿着白纱的新人伫立在潮水边，用最简单的姿态，许下最深的情意。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>盐水溪畔的人们</h2>
<p>郑建南的照片里，有骑单车穿越桥面的人影，也有牵着手漫步沙滩的恋人；有人独自蹲在河口，像是在等光线落进他心里最刚好的位置。这些画面看似日常，却因光线的巧合与风景的衬托，像是为这座城市写下的几句诗句，刚好刚好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543431-777962.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14543431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543431-777962-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543431" class="wp-caption-text">两位旅人相对无语，却共享这片最美的黄昏。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>生活就在风景里</h2>
<p>其中几幅作品特别动人：几艘停在泥滩上的小渔船，船身覆着帆布与工具，对比着天边绚烂的晚霞。没有刻意布置，却像一场刚刚好的巧遇。郑建南说，他拍下的不只是风景，而是“人怎么跟这块土地一起过生活”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543434-777965.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543434-777965-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543434" class="wp-caption-text">几艘简朴竹筏与渔船，伫立河口，见证盐水溪与海的交界，也是讨海人生活的节奏。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>那些被光晕揽住的瞬间</h2>
<p>黄昏是一天中最柔软的时刻。阳光斜斜洒下，溪面闪烁着金色波纹，岸边草木渐渐沉静下来。这时候的盐水溪，就像一位准备写信的老朋友，默默递上情绪饱满的画面。郑建南说：“拍夕阳像在等天边寄来的讯息，每一次都不一样，每一次都值得等。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543430-777961.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543430-777961-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543430" class="wp-caption-text">夏末日落，盐水溪静静展开温暖色调的画布。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>一段属于黄昏的记忆</h2>
<p>这份等待，也成了观者与风景的约定。在盐水溪的岸边，每个驻足的旅人，或许都收到了一封写给灵魂的黄昏情书。不只是一张照片、一幅画面，而是一段温柔的记忆。如果哪天你经过台南，别急着转弯，沿着溪边走一走，也许就在下一个转角，收进心里一幕永不褪色的夏日光景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14543432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14543432" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543432-777963.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14543432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14543432-777963-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14543432" class="wp-caption-text">摄影师现身沙洲，用镜头迎接黄昏的最终光芒。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
