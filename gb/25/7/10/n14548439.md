<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>视导汉光军演 赖清德赞M1A2T为战力最强战车</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14548516-2507100208112378.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
