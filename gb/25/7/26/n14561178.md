<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>荷．再现 在叶脉间听见自然与艺术的对话</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561191-783636-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《荷．再现》联展自即日起展至10月26日止，地点在云林斗南他里雾生活美学馆。（廖昭坤提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月26日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）炎夏七月，走进云林斗南他里雾生活美学馆，一幅幅色彩斑斓、带着层层肌理与荷叶纹理的画作静静展开，像是从池水中跃出的一页诗篇。7月26日，《荷．再现——莳华雅叙师生联展》隆重开展，由虎尾科技大学多媒体设计系廖敦如教授策展，集结11位女性艺术创作者，联手呈现一场以荷叶为主题的视觉飨宴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14561179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561179" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561179-783624.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561179-783624-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561179" class="wp-caption-text">策展人廖敦如教授（右）于展场内为来宾解说创作理念，分享荷叶肌理如何成为画布中富含诗意的主角。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14561180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561180" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561180-783625.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561180-783625-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561180" class="wp-caption-text">瓶花系列－玫瑰花。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14561181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561181" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561181-783626.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561181-783626-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561181" class="wp-caption-text">瓶花系列－山茶花。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>将叶脉化为画笔　用自然肌理书写生活美学</h2>
<p>策展人廖敦如教授表示，此次展览以荷叶为媒介，结合海绵皴擦与厚涂压克力技法，跳脱传统工笔的框架，以更自由、层层堆叠的方式，呈现自然肌理的韵律与色彩。展场内的每一件作品，不仅传达艺术家对自然的感知与敬意，也像是她们生活中静心凝视植物后的对话纪录。</p>
<p>“今天可能是荷，明天可能是玫瑰，你家里的任何一株植物，其实都可以成为创作素材。”廖教授鼓励现场观众，不需艺术科班出身，也能从生活中体会创作之乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14561182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561182" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561182-783627.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561182-783627-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561182" class="wp-caption-text">瓶花系列－菊花。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14561183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561183" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561183-783628.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561183-783628-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561183" class="wp-caption-text">作品《莲映》（左）与《夏叶赏荷》（右），呈现荷叶肌理在色彩层叠中的变化与光影呼应，是女性艺术家对自然温柔凝视的回应。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>再现宋代文人的生活风雅</h2>
<p>这场联展的魅力，不只止于画作。开幕当日同步举行“夏日茶席会”，融入宋代文人四艺：焚香、挂画、插花与点茶。展场一隅，雅致茶席陈设，来宾围坐品茗，茶香与画意相互交织，营造出一种古风今韵兼容的当代表现。</p>
<p>云林县副县长谢淑亚感动地说：“今日现场的茶席、音乐与画作交融，让人仿佛回到古人生活之中。这样的美学实践，不只是艺术展现，更是我们现代人需要的精神滋养。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14561184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561184" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561184-783629.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561184-783629-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561184" class="wp-caption-text">展场一隅设有茶席雅集，宾客齐聚共品香茗，沉浸于宋代文人四艺氛围，让画与茶共构生活之美。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14561185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561185" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561185-783630.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561185-783630-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561185" class="wp-caption-text">廖敦如教授（左）与贵宾合影留念，背后为其师生创作的瓶花系列作品，象征师生情谊与艺术传承。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>女性艺术的温柔绽放</h2>
<p>“莳华雅叙”艺术团队成员皆为花艺创作出身的女性，长期与植物相伴，使她们在创作时自然流露出柔和而细腻的笔触与情感。此次联展作品不仅有荷叶、花瓶与牡丹等视觉主题，更融入自然界的色彩与韵律，有如一首首以画笔谱出的诗。</p>
<p>展出作品如《瓶花系列之一—花开》、《瓶花系列五—山茶花》与《莲映》、《夏叶赏荷》等，皆以浓烈又和谐的色彩搭配层层荷叶纹理，呈现画面深度与节奏，让观者仿佛步入荷塘深处，感受风拂水面、叶动心境的片刻静谧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14561186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561186" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561186-783631.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561186-783631-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561186" class="wp-caption-text">活动现场颁发感谢状，肯定艺术团队推广生活美学的努力与贡献，现场气氛温馨热烈。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14561187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14561187" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561187-783632.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14561187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14561187-783632-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14561187" class="wp-caption-text">他里雾生活美学馆展场内宾客云集，纸灯、茶席、画作交织出一场属于夏日的艺术飨宴。（廖昭坤提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>展期至十月　邀您一同走进荷叶的世界</h2>
<p>《荷．再现》联展自即日起展至10月26日止，地点在云林斗南他里雾生活美学馆。现场设有导览、茶席与互动空间，适合亲子共游、艺术爱好者欣赏。</p>
<p>展览不只是一场视觉飨宴，更是艺术生活化的实践。从荷叶出发，画出生活，也让我们在快节奏的日常里，有机会驻足、凝视，与自然重新连结。</p>
