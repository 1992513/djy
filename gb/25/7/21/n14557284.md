<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【歌曲】时代的使命</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;"><strong>时代的使命 / The Mission of This Era</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">The sun and Venus clash across the sky,<br />
太阳与金星交锋天际，<br />
Earth immersed in divine revelation.<br />
大地沉浸于神的启迪。<br />
Each city awaits its awakening—<br />
每座城市静候苏醒——<br />
Light slowly rises, like dawn’s first breath,<br />
光缓缓升起，如晨曦破晓，<br />
Piercing the mist, caressing the land.<br />
穿透迷雾，轻抚大地。</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">From afar, celestial music resounds,<br />
远方传来天籁之音，<br />
Dancers spin like the wheels of time.<br />
舞者旋转，如时光之轮。<br />
Heavenly harmonies fill the air,<br />
天乐响起，万象凝听，<br />
This is no performance, nor for vain acclaim—<br />
这不是表演，非为虚名，<br />
It is the voice of the divine, through mortal dust,<br />
那是神语，穿越红尘<br />
To bring truth once more to humankind.<br />
只为真理，再现人间。</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">Ancient vows are being fulfilled,<br />
远古的誓言正在实现，<br />
The name of prophecy now borne upon His shoulders.<br />
预言之名，由祂肩担。<br />
Shen Yun sets forth, a journey vast,<br />
神韵启程，万里征战，<br />
The giant from the East has come—<br />
来自东方的巨人，<br />
Not for a throne,<br />
非为王座，<br />
But to awaken this slumbering world.<br />
只为唤醒沉睡的人间。</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">Let culture return to its sacred root,Let conscience rise from forgotten ground.<br />
让文化归根，良知复苏，<br />
He wields no sword, but beauty and grace,<br />
祂用艺术抵御谎言，<br />
Breaking chains with truth and light,<br />
用美与正直，解开枷锁，<br />
And stirring kindness deep in the soul.<br />
唤醒心灵深处的善。</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">He bears no blade, yet walks with sun and moon,<br />
祂不持剑，同行日月，<br />
Uplifting the torch of truth in his hand.<br />
手中高举真相的火炬，<br />
With compassion and unyielding strength,He traverses the night,<br />
慈悲刚毅，穿越黑夜，<br />
To seek the lost nations and wandering spirits.<br />
寻回失落的民族与魂灵。</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">The cosmos watches in silent awe,<br />
宇宙静默观望，<br />
No name spoken, no question asked.<br />
未言其名，未问所来。<br />
Only an unseen force is felt—<br />
只见无形的力量，<br />
Planting seeds of light within the dark.<br />
在黑暗中播下光明的种子。</p>
<p style="padding-left: 160px;">He is the Messiah of olden tales,<br />
祂是传说中的弥赛亚，<br />
A rebirth of divinity, art, and heritage.<br />
神圣、艺术、文化的复新，<br />
His presence stirs ten thousand hearts to rise,<br />
万千心灵觉醒的呼唤——<br />
Calling the world to remember: Truth, Compassion, Forbearance—<br />
唤醒世人，记住：真、善、忍<br />
Hidden in every soul that longs for light.<br />
藏于每一颗渴望光明的心。</p>
