<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>王功渔港新风景 潮间带体验X公仔艺术</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551808-780810-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>彩绘阶梯，蛤蜊与夕照。（吴淑也提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年07月15日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾彰化报导）走进今年夏天的王功渔港，你会发现这里不再只是卖蚵仔、看夕阳的老地方，而是蜕变为结合童趣装置，与海洋体验的新型滨海风景线。从潮间带的铁牛车，到堤岸上大眼海龟和五彩热带鱼，王功村这次是真的不一样了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551811" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551811-780813.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551811-780813.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551811" class="wp-caption-text">张牙舞爪的巨型螃蟹雕塑立于堤岸，眼神灵动、色彩鲜艳，是小朋友最爱合照的欢乐场景之一。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551809" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551809-780811.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551809-780811.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551809" class="wp-caption-text">结合可爱王功鱼人偶、音符、灯塔与地标石碑，打造热闹又具代表性的迎宾区。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551806" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551806-780808.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551806-780808.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551806" class="wp-caption-text">游客搭乘在地人称“永昌叔”的铁牛车，迎着海风出发前往潮间带，体验挖文蛤与生态导览。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>坐上海牛车 摸蛤兼看海风</strong></p>
<p>“请在九点前离开潮间带”，黄底红字的警示牌挂在墙上，提醒游客潮汐无情。即使如此，仍浇不熄游人对这片泥滩的热情。这里最具特色的，是仍保留的“海牛车”铁牛三轮体验。许多游客赤脚踩在泥滩上，蹲着挖文蛤，旁边是缓缓前行的铁牛车，载着一家老小笑声连连，这是王功最地道的节奏，慢慢来，认真玩。</p>
<p>摄影家吴淑也捕捉的画面里，小孩开心比出胜利手势，大人则卷起裤管拎着小桶，这不是观光行程，而是回到土地与海洋的身体记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551817" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551817" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551817-780818.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551817-780818.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551817" class="wp-caption-text">王功潮间带的景色。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551818" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551818" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551818-780819.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551818" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551818-780819.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551818" class="wp-caption-text">潮汐告示提醒安全。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551816" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551816-780817.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551816-780817.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551816" class="wp-caption-text">蚵架密布的潮间带静静延伸，轻舟在波光中漂浮，一旁旅人驻足海边，展开一场贴近土地与海的微旅行。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>大眼海龟与彩鱼列队 堤防变成艺术走廊</strong></p>
<p>顺着港边堤防往上走，眼前忽然出现一只卡通版的海龟，圆滚滚的大眼睛和腮红，让人忍不住驻足合照。往旁边一看，一整排彩绘热带鱼公仔跃然草坡上，仿佛海底世界浮上海面，在太阳下摇曳。这些新设施让整条堤岸变得童趣盎然，也为王功渔村注入一股轻松的呼吸。</p>
<p>“这里变得很不一样耶，好像海洋乐园！”一位游客边拍照边赞叹。不只是孩子开心，大人也忍不住多拍几张。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551821" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551821" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551821-780822.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551821-780822.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="598" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551821" class="wp-caption-text">海水中踏浪前行的牛只彩绘，线条写实、眼神温润。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551810" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551810-780812.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551810-780812.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551810" class="wp-caption-text">整面彩绘墙将“WANGGONG”字母融合海天景致与牛只图像，创意十足。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551814" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551814" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551814-780816.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551814" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551814-780816.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551814" class="wp-caption-text">五彩缤纷的鱼儿造景沿堤防一路排列，仿佛潮间带的海洋生命浮上海岸。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>芳苑灯塔与风车群 成为打卡新标志</strong></p>
<p>再往前走，黑白条纹的芳苑灯塔竖立在海岸边，像守望村庄的老朋友。它是这里的精神象征，也与新设的公共艺术、风车景观相互呼应。摄影家吴淑也以大角度构图，把风车、海岸与灯塔纳入画面中，一种既现代又质朴的美油然而生。</p>
<p>在观景平台上，黄色拼砖绘出蜿蜒曲线，空间宽敞，许多家庭就地而坐吹海风，也有人推着小孩的车在拍照。远方那只色彩鲜明的大螃蟹装置，在蓝天背景下格外抢眼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551813" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551813-780815.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551813-780815.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551813" class="wp-caption-text">头戴耳机、笑容可掬的章鱼公仔坐镇堤防旁，将音乐与大海融合为一体，是王功全新吉祥物风格象征。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551805" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551805-780807.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551805-780807.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551805" class="wp-caption-text">黑白条纹的芳苑灯塔矗立滨海，是渔村的老灵魂。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14551820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551820" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551820-780821.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551820-780821.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551820" class="wp-caption-text">观夕平台上的亲子时光。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>渔村与艺术的温柔结合</strong></p>
<p>王功，原本是一个以蚵田、潮间带生活为主的小村庄，如今，透过公部门与在地居民的合作，一步步将风景、产业与艺术融合为一体。不是强加于地的人工装饰，而是自然地延伸了生活的样貌。</p>
<p>灯塔不再只是灯塔，海龟也不只是装饰，它们成了旅人与土地对话的桥梁。而这些看似可爱的装置艺术，背后其实也在述说着这片土地与海洋共生的历史与当代故事。</p>
<p>如果你还停留在王功只是个蚵仔的代名词，那这个夏天，一定要再走一趟，感受它重新翻页后的风景与心情。童趣、海风与笑声，正在等你靠岸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14551819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14551819" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551819-780820.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14551819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14551819-780820.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="602" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14551819" class="wp-caption-text">蟹将军霸气登顶迎宾石碑，向每一位来访旅人道声欢迎，也展现王功村亲海生活的在地特色。（吴淑也提供）</figcaption></figure>
