<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中国房价6月持续下跌 投资与销售双双下滑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id14372602-GettyImages-2151570640-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为2024年5月9日中国房地产开发商万科在中国东部浙江省杭州市正在兴建的住宅大楼。(STR/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年07月16日讯】（大纪元记者李清源报导）中国房价6月持续下跌，<span class="s1">其中一线城市房价</span><span class="s1">连续</span><span class="s2">19</span><span class="s1">个月呈现负成长。上半年房地产</span>投资<span class="s1">同比下降</span><span class="s2">11.2%，<span class="s1">商品房销售面积同比</span><span class="s1">下降</span>7.5%</span>。</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">根据中共国家统计局<span class="s2">7</span>月<span class="s2">15</span>日公布的数据，中国房地产市场持续走低。</span><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">70</span><span class="s1">个大中城市新建商品住宅售价环比下跌</span><span class="s2">0.3%</span><span class="s1">，跌幅较</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">月扩大</span><span class="s2">0.1</span><span class="s1">个百分点，创下自</span><span class="s2">2024</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s2">10</span><span class="s1">月以来的最大单月跌幅</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">据6</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">70</span><span class="s1">个大中城市商品住宅销售价格变动情况统计</span><span class="s1">数据，在</span><span class="s2">70</span><span class="s1">个城市中，有</span><span class="s2">44</span><span class="s1">个城市的新建住宅价格出现下跌，数量较上月增加</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">个。其中一线城市价格下滑</span><span class="s2">0.2%</span><span class="s1">，连续</span><span class="s2">19</span><span class="s1">个月呈现负成长。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">月一线城市，新房销售价格年减</span><span class="s2">1.4%</span><span class="s1">，降幅较</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">月收敛</span><span class="s2">0.3</span><span class="s1">个百分点，从</span><span class="s2">2023</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s2">12</span><span class="s1">月以来连续</span><span class="s2">19</span><span class="s1">个月呈现负成长。</span><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">月一线城市新房销售价格仅上海市上涨</span><span class="s2">6%</span><span class="s1">；北京、广州、深圳分别年减</span><span class="s2">4.1%</span><span class="s1">、</span><span class="s2">5.1%</span><span class="s1">、</span><span class="s2">2.5%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">二三线城市新房销售价格分别年减</span><span class="s2">3.0%</span><span class="s1">、</span><span class="s2">4.6%</span><span class="s1">，降幅较</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">月分别收敛</span><span class="s2">0.5</span><span class="s1">个、</span><span class="s2">0.3</span><span class="s1">个百分点。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">路透社报导，</span><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">月中国新房价格创下近八个月来最大跌幅，显示刺激政策成效有限，市场仍未摆脱下行压力。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">除价格下滑外，房地产投资与销售数据亦同步下行。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">《财经》杂志报导，</span><span class="s2">2025</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">至</span><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">月全国房地产开发投资总额为</span><span class="s2">5.4</span><span class="s1">兆元人民币，同比下降</span><span class="s2">11.2%</span><span class="s1">，跌幅较前五个月扩大</span><span class="s2">0.6</span><span class="s1">个百分点。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">据中共国家统计局</span><span class="s2">7</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">15</span><span class="s1">日公布的数据，</span><span class="s2">2025</span><span class="s1">年上半年全国商品房销售面积为</span><span class="s2">5.58</span><span class="s1">亿平方米，较去年同期下降</span><span class="s2">7.5%</span><span class="s1">；其中，住宅销售面积为</span><span class="s2">4.23</span><span class="s1">亿平方米，年减</span><span class="s2">7.5%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">2025</span><span class="s1">年上半年商品房销售总额为</span><span class="s2">5.5</span><span class="s1">兆元人民币，同比下降</span><span class="s2">5.5%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">《华尔街日报》表示，在高失业率、居民收入预期走弱及房价持续走低等背景下，市场缺乏有效购买力支撑，许多潜在买家选择延迟或放弃置业计划，整体购房意愿疲软。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">虽然中共已陆续推出多项支持措施，但楼市回稳的效果仍未显现。</span><span class="s1">专家指出，下半年若无重大政策变化，房价仍可能持续承压。</span></p>
