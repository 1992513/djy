<p>在家自种蔬菜及香草，随手现采鲜摘，直上餐桌，无毒最安心！
</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>认识姜</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">日本名／生姜</span><span style="font-weight: 400">科名／姜科姜属</span><span style="font-weight: 400">原产地／印度、中国</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14584335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584335" style="width: 234px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584335-image_2025-08-30T19_49_12.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14584335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584335-image_2025-08-30T19_49_12.png" alt="" width="234" b="340"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584335" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575790" style="width: 302px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575790-P.80-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575790-P.80-1-450x429.jpg" alt="" width="302" b="288"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575790" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>种姜：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">姜是由春天时种植的“种姜”上长出的新姜而得来。</span></p>
<p><b>新姜? vs. 老姜：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">采收下来新的姜被称为“新姜”，而放了一阵子的则被称为“老姜”。</span></p>
<p><b>姜母：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">初次种植得来的种姜，在下次即被称为“姜母”。姜母的效能较强，纤维质也较硬的关系，主要多是餐厅熬煮汤须使用。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575787" style="width: 327px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575787-P.81-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575787-P.81-1-450x379.jpg" alt="" width="327" b="275"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575787" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>延长保存期限的方法</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">将姜洗净后放入罐中，并加入可以完全将姜浸泡之中的水量。并每</span><span style="font-weight: 400">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400">天换水一次，便可再保存一个月左右的时间。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575789" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575789-P.80-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14575789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575789-P.80-3.jpg" alt="" width="338" b="340"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575789" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>功效与用途</b></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff"><b>一</b><span style="font-weight: 400">、</span><b>药用价值</b><span style="font-weight: 400">：</span></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">姜的根茎也能作为中药材来使用。拥有散热作用、健胃、抑止呕吐，也可用于感冒初期症状与防止肠胃机能低下。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">加入姜的粉葛汤能够温暖身体与提高免疫力，很推荐感冒时饮用。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff"><b>二、料理应用广泛：</b></span></h3>
<p><b>?制成糖浆、饮料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">将一颗柠檬外皮洗净切成薄片，姜</span><span style="font-weight: 400">50</span><span style="font-weight: 400">克洗净后连皮切薄片。在玻璃罐里交叠放入柠檬与姜，再加入</span><span style="font-weight: 400">200</span><span style="font-weight: 400">克的蜂蜜，放置一晚即可使用。可以加热水或苏打水做成饮料外，还可以直接盖在优格上成为好吃的糖浆。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575788" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575788-P.80-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575788-P.80-2-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575788" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>?腌渍食材</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">在日本，是作为辛香料与腌渍不可或缺的食材，一般多以甜醋腌渍成寿司姜片与醋渍红白生姜。</span></p>
<p><b>?姜汤祛寒</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">“姜烯酚”具有辛辣味，感冒时，喝温热的生姜汤，能帮助血液循环以及温暖身体的效果。</span></p>
<p><b>?生姜解热</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">生姜有解热作用，能使身体降温，必须依体质状况调整使用。</span></p>
<p><b>?搭配寿司或生鱼片 可抗菌</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">因抗菌效果高，有与寿司或生鱼片一起食用的习惯。</span></p>
<p><b>?和白饭一起吃 能开胃：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">使用味醂和酱油调味的姜丝搭配煮好的白饭，风味更是绝佳，是食欲不佳时的最佳选择。</span>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>姜叶可用于料理</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">姜叶可加在味噌汤、做成天妇罗，也可用包卷猪肉片烧烤等料理方法。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575786" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575786-P.81-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575786-P.81-2-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575786" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>日本“生姜祭典”</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">药效高的生姜，被认为可驱邪。而在日本也有神社举办姜市集；甚至在东京芝大神宫会有持续</span><span style="font-weight: 400">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400">天的例行性大祭典，也被称做“生姜祭典”、“生姜市集”。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">过去这一带因为种植姜田，所以姜农、摊贩也很多，即使现在到了大祭，神宫内也有小摊贩，免费发放奉献给神佛的姜，同时也贩卖料理使用的生姜。</span><b>栽培须知</b><span style="font-weight: 400">喜欢高温潮湿，每年要在不同的场所培育。</span><b>?</b><b>种植：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">姜是在热带地方生长的关系，种植的标准在没有霜且气温</span><span style="font-weight: 400">20</span><span style="font-weight: 400">度以上。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">将每个约</span><span style="font-weight: 400">50</span><span style="font-weight: 400">克的种姜，种植于</span><span style="font-weight: 400">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400">公分深的土里，注意</span><span style="font-weight: 400">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400">年间不要在相同地方与使用同样土壤种植。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">种植后直到长出芽约需要一个月左右时间。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">25</span><span style="font-weight: 400">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400">30</span><span style="font-weight: 400">度的环境能够健康成长，因喜欢高温潮湿，请注意避免干燥，在地面种植也注意浇水。</span><span style="font-weight: 400">地面可以铺上干稻草等也很有效果。</span><b>?</b><b>收成：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">在夏天柔软的根茎可以做为“姜叶”来享受，秋天时，圆而厚的根茎可以作为“姜根”进行采收。</span></p>

<h2><span style="color: #008000"><b>6种生姜元气料理食谱</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff"><b>姜汁牛奶布丁</b></span><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400">香港与澳门的名产</span><b>材料（</b><b>1</b><b>人分）</b><span style="font-weight: 400">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400">姜汁?1大匙</span><span style="font-weight: 400">牛奶?180毫升</span><span style="font-weight: 400">砂糖?3大匙</span><b>做法</b><span style="font-weight: 400">：</span></p>
<ol>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400">1.姜磨碎挤出姜汁，过筛后备用。</span></li>
</ol>
<ol start="2">
<li><span style="font-weight: 400">2.将牛奶倒入耐热容器，加入砂糖轻轻混和后，放入微波炉以</span><span style="font-weight: 400">600w</span><span style="font-weight: 400">加热</span><span style="font-weight: 400">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400">分钟左右后取出。</span></li>
<ol start="3">
<li><span style="font-weight: 400">3.轻轻搅拌</span><span style="font-weight: 400">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400">后，一口气将</span><span style="font-weight: 400">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400">倒入。并盖上盖子不需要搅拌。放置</span><span style="font-weight: 400">15</span><span style="font-weight: 400">分钟凝固。</span></li>
<figure id="attachment_14575781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575781" style="width: 304px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575781-P.82-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575781-P.82-2-450x332.jpg" alt="" width="304" b="224"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575781" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff"><b>姜味噌沾酱</b></span></h3>
<p><b>用途</b><span style="font-weight: 400">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400">加关东煮非常好吃</span><b>材料（</b><b>2</b><b>人分）</b><span style="font-weight: 400">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400">味噌 125克</span><span style="font-weight: 400">姜?50克</span><span style="font-weight: 400">清酒?1/2杯</span><span style="font-weight: 400">高汤?1/2杯</span><span style="font-weight: 400">味醂?1/2杯</span><b>做法</b><span style="font-weight: 400">：</span></p>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400">1.将酒放入小锅中加热，去除酒精。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400">2.接着，锅内放入味噌、味醂、高汤边搅拌均匀。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400">3.沸腾后停火，放凉。</span></li>
<ol start="4">
<li><span style="font-weight: 400">4.将姜洗净并带皮磨碎，加入锅内搅拌即完成。</span></li>
<figure id="attachment_14575780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575780" style="width: 347px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575780-P.82-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575780-P.82-3-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="347" b="298"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575780" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff"><b>姜汁人参鸡汤</b></span></h3>
