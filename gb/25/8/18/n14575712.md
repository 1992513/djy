<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>生姜妙用多 6道元气食谱让你爱吃姜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575782-P.82-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>生姜茶。（苹果屋出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>在家自种蔬菜及香草，随手现采鲜摘，直上餐桌，无毒最安心！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>认识姜</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">日本名／生姜</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">科名／姜科姜属</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">原产地／印度、中国</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575790" style="width: 302px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575790-P.80-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575790-P.80-1-450x429.jpg" alt="" width="302" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575790" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>种姜：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">姜是由春天时种植的“种姜”上长出的新姜而得来。</span></p>
<p><b>新姜? vs. 老姜：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">采收下来新的姜被称为“新姜”，而放了一阵子的则被称为“老姜”。</span></p>
<p><b>姜母：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">初次种植得来的种姜，在下次即被称为“姜母”。姜母的效能较强，纤维质也较硬的关系，主要多是餐厅熬煮汤须使用。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575787" style="width: 327px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575787-P.81-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575787-P.81-1-450x379.jpg" alt="" width="327" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575787" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>延长保存期限的方法</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">将姜洗净后放入罐中，并加入可以完全将姜浸泡之中的水量。并每</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">天换水一次，便可再保存一个月左右的时间。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>功效与用途</b></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>一</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span><b>药用价值</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">姜的根茎也能作为中药材来使用。拥有散热作用、健胃、抑止呕吐，也可用于感冒初期症状与防止肠胃机能低下。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">加入姜的粉葛汤能够温暖身体与提高免疫力，很推荐感冒时饮用。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>二、料理应用广泛：</b></span></h3>
<p><b>?制成糖浆、饮料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">将一颗柠檬外皮洗净切成薄片，姜</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">50</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">克洗净后连皮切薄片。在玻璃罐里交叠放入柠檬与姜，再加入</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">200</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">克的蜂蜜，放置一晚即可使用。可以加热水或苏打水做成饮料外，还可以直接盖在优格上成为好吃的糖浆。</span></p>
<p><b>?腌渍食材</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">在日本，是作为辛香料与腌渍不可或缺的食材，一般多以甜醋腌渍成寿司姜片与醋渍红白生姜。</span></p>
<p><b>?姜汤祛寒</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">“姜烯酚”具有辛辣味，感冒时，喝温热的生姜汤，能帮助血液循环以及温暖身体的效果。</span></p>
<p><b>?生姜解热</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">生姜有解热作用，能使身体降温，必须依体质状况调整使用。</span></p>
<p><b>?搭配寿司或生鱼片 可抗菌</b><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">因抗菌效果高，有与寿司或生鱼片一起食用的习惯。</span></p>
<p><b>?和白饭一起吃 能开胃：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">使用味醂和酱油调味的姜丝搭配煮好的白饭，风味更是绝佳，是食欲不佳时的最佳选择。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575788" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575788-P.80-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575788-P.80-2-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575788" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>姜叶可用于料理</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">姜叶可加在味噌汤、做成天妇罗，也可用包卷猪肉片烧烤等料理方法。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575786" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575786-P.81-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575786-P.81-2-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575786" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>日本“生姜祭典”</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">药效高的生姜，被认为可驱邪。而在日本也有神社举办姜市集；甚至在东京芝大神宫会有持续</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">天的例行性大祭典，也被称做“生姜祭典”、“生姜市集”。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">过去这一带因为种植姜田，所以姜农、摊贩也很多，即使现在到了大祭，神宫内也有小摊贩，免费发放奉献给神佛的姜，同时也贩卖料理使用的生姜。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575785" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575785-P.81-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575785-P.81-3.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="289" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575785" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>栽培须知</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">喜欢高温潮湿，每年要在不同的场所培育。</span></p>
<p><b>?</b><b>种植：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">姜是在热带地方生长的关系，种植的标准在没有霜且气温</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">度以上。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">将每个约</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">50</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">克的种姜，种植于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">公分深的土里，注意</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年间不要在相同地方与使用同样土壤种植。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">种植后直到长出芽约需要一个月左右时间。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">25</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">度的环境能够健康成长，因喜欢高温潮湿，请注意避免干燥，在地面种植也注意浇水。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">地面可以铺上干稻草等也很有效果。</span></p>
<p><b>?</b><b>收成：</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在夏天柔软的根茎可以做为“姜叶”来享受，秋天时，圆而厚的根茎可以作为“姜根”进行采收。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14575784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575784" style="width: 773px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575784-P.81-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575784-P.81-4-450x88.jpg" alt="" width="773" b="151" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575784" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>6种生姜元气料理食谱</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>姜汁牛奶布丁</b></span><b>：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">香港与澳门的名产</span></p>
<p><b>材料（</b><b>1</b><b>人分）</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">姜汁?1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">牛奶?180毫升</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">砂糖?3大匙</span></p>
<p><b>做法</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span></p>
<ol>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">姜磨碎挤出姜汁，过筛后备用。</span></li>
</ol>
<ol start="2">
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">将牛奶倒入耐热容器，加入砂糖轻轻混和后，放入微波炉以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">600w</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加热</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分钟左右后取出。</span></li>
<ol start="3">
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">轻轻搅拌</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">后，一口气将</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">倒入。并盖上盖子不需要搅拌。放置</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分钟凝固。</span></li>
<figure id="attachment_14575781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575781" style="width: 304px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575781-P.82-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575781-P.82-2-450x332.jpg" alt="" width="304" b="224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575781" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>姜味噌沾酱</b></span></h3>
<p><b>用途</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加关东煮非常好吃</span></p>
<p><b>材料（</b><b>2</b><b>人分）</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">味噌 125克</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">姜?50克</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">清酒?1/2杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">高汤?1/2杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">味醂?1/2杯</span></p>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">将酒放入小锅中加热，去除酒精。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">接着，锅内放入味噌、味醂、高汤边搅拌均匀。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">沸腾后停火，放凉。</span></li>
<ol start="4">
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">将姜洗净并带皮磨碎，加入锅内搅拌即完成。</span></li>
<figure id="attachment_14575780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575780" style="width: 347px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575780-P.82-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575780-P.82-3-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="347" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575780" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>姜汁人参鸡汤</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">带骨鸡腿肉?1只</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">糯米 1/6杯</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">青葱 1/2根</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">姜泥 1大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">大蒜?1/2片</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">枸杞?1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">松子 1/2大匙</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">红枣 2颗</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">水?700毫升</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">盐 1小匙</span></p>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">将糯米洗净并浸泡</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小时。并将青葱斜切成薄片。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">锅内放入所有材料，开中火沸腾后，转小火熬约</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小时。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">取出鸡肉并去掉骨头，再放入锅内依喜好洒上盐与胡椒。</span></li>
