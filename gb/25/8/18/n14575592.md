<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>熬夜伤身超乎想像 来听听西医和中医说分明</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/stay-up-late_1385327570-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>熬夜会让皮肤状态变差、肌肤松弛、黑眼圈加深，降低睡眠品质。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><b>编者按：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">熬夜对健康的影响，远超你我的想像。若不得已需要熬夜，中医师提供四大补救措施，帮您降低熬夜的伤害。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>熬夜伤身坏处多 西医和中医说分明</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14575603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575603-2985f3c65da2cfe6afadebc6fc32fb1a.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575603-2985f3c65da2cfe6afadebc6fc32fb1a-450x563.png" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575603" class="wp-caption-text">（昌盛堂中医诊所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><b>西医观点</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">熬夜会让</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">皮肤状态变差、肌肤松弛、黑眼圈加深，并加快骨骼流失速度，降低睡眠品质，同时影响记忆力与认知功能，也容易造成血糖与血脂控制不良。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">此外，研究显示，即使仅仅熬夜一晚，也会改变体内部分免疫细胞的比例，使免疫系统处于急性发炎的状态。若这种情况长期反复发生，可能增加慢性发炎的风险，进而提升心血管疾病、癌症及神经退化性疾病的发生概率。</span></p>
<p><b>中医观点</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">中医</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">强调，晚上11点至凌晨3点是肝胆经循行的时间，是人体排毒与修复的重要时段，如果此时未进入熟睡状态，会使肝胆气血失调，影响气血生成与代谢，长期下来，会进一步影响心、脾、肺、肾等脏腑的功能，造成全身的阴阳失衡。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>减轻熬夜危害 采取四个补救措施</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14575602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575602" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575602-069fdaefa9055dff93a9c512034127d8.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575602-069fdaefa9055dff93a9c512034127d8-450x563.png" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575602" class="wp-caption-text">（昌盛堂中医诊所提供）</figcaption></figure>
