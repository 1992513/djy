<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>今夏去日本？这八道消暑美食不可错过</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565634-shutterstock_2644128697-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本宫城县仙台市（Sendai, Miyagi）盛夏常见的枝豆麻糬（Zunda Mochi），以毛豆酱覆盖麻糬，色泽鲜绿、营养丰富，是节庆与家庭聚会的传统甜点。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年08月02日讯】（大纪元记者李言综合报导）日本夏季年年升温，各地也顺势端出富有风土特色的消暑佳肴，从爽滑清凉的冰镇面食，到水润鲜甜的时令蔬果，无不体现日本人顺应时令、贴心入微的生活艺术。</p>
<h2>北部（东北地方）</h2>
<h3>冰镇拉面（Hiyashi Ramen）</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14565629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565629" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565629-shutterstock_2568350091.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565629-shutterstock_2568350091.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565629" class="wp-caption-text">日本山形县山形市（Yamagata, Yamagata）发源的冰镇拉面（Hiyashi Ramen），以冷酱油汤头搭配冰凉面条，是酷暑中最受欢迎的夏日面食。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>冰镇拉面于1952年在山形县（Yamagata Prefecture）问世，将酱油汤头与拉面一同冰镇，有时碗中还加冰块。炎夏时节，当地人愿意排队等上一碗，早已成为地区代表美食。</p>
<h3>枝豆麻糬（Zunda Mochi）</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14565628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565628" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565628-shutterstock_2277384791.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565628-shutterstock_2277384791.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565628" class="wp-caption-text">日本宫城县仙台市（Sendai, Miyagi）盛夏常见的枝豆麻糬（Zunda Mochi），以毛豆酱覆盖麻糬，色泽鲜绿、营养丰富，是节庆与家庭聚会的传统甜点。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>宫城县仙台市（Sendai, Miyagi）的枝豆麻糬以毛豆制成绿色酱料，拌入糖盐后覆盖在麻糬上，富含蛋白质与维生素，是夏季家庭聚会与祭典常见甜点，现在也延伸到奶昔、霜淇淋等现代版本。</p>
<h2>东部（关东与中部）</h2>
<h2>柳川锅（Yanagawa Nabe）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14565625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565625-image.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565625-image.png" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565625" class="wp-caption-text">日本东京都（Tokyo）传统料理柳川锅（Yanagawa Nabe），以鳅鱼、牛蒡与蛋炖煮而成，营养丰富，是夏季滋补强身的经典热食。（大纪元制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以鳅鱼、牛蒡和蛋煮成的柳川锅，虽为热食，却因富含铁质与蛋白质，被视为夏季补身料理。江户时代便已流行至今，是东京老字号餐馆常见的传统菜色。</p>
<h3>水信玄饼（Mizu Manju）</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14565624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565624-shutterstock_1408225181.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565624-shutterstock_1408225181.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565624" class="wp-caption-text">日本岐阜县大垣市（Ogaki, Gifu）以冷泉传统制成的水信玄饼（Mizu Manju），晶莹剔透、口感柔滑，是盛夏必尝的清凉和菓子。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自岐阜县（Gifu Prefecture）“水之都”大垣市（Ogaki），水信玄饼外皮用葛粉与蕨粉制成，内馅为红豆，冰镇后口感如雨滴般晶莹柔滑，是夏季视觉与味觉兼具的传统甜点。</p>
<h2>西部（关西地区）</h2>
<h3>水茄子（Mizunasu）</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14565622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565622-shutterstock_2318489167.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565622-shutterstock_2318489167.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565622" class="wp-caption-text">日本大阪府泉州地区（Senshu, Osaka）特产的水茄子（Mizunasu），水分丰富、口感清脆，是可生食的夏季消暑蔬菜。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>大阪府泉州地区（Senshu, Osaka）特产的水茄子，水分高、口感脆，可生吃或作为糠渍享用。农家自江户时代便以此作为田间消暑良品，如今也有人将其搭配火腿生食，清爽鲜美。</p>
<h3>宇治金时（Uji Kintoki）</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14565620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565620" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565620-shutterstock_1723904149.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565620-shutterstock_1723904149.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565620" class="wp-caption-text">日本京都市（Kyoto）夏季经典甜品宇治金时（Uji Kintoki），以抹茶刨冰搭配红豆馅与白玉，展现浓厚茶文化与季节风味。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>京都夏日代表刨冰，以抹茶糖浆搭配红豆馅，再加上白玉、炼乳或抹茶冰淇淋，层次丰富、色泽诱人。据传灵感来自德川家康，是京都茶文化的夏日延伸。</p>
<h2>南部（九州）</h2>
<h3>流水素面（Nagashi Somen）</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14565617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565617" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565617-shutterstock_1404670649.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565617-shutterstock_1404670649.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565617" class="wp-caption-text">日本宫崎县高千穗町（Takachiho, Miyazaki）发源的流水素面（Nagashi Somen），将冰镇面条顺竹管滑流，是深受欢迎的夏季清凉体验。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>流水素面源自宫崎县高千穗町（Takachiho, Miyazaki），食用时将素面放入冷泉竹管中流动，用筷子夹取，沾酱食用。既是料理也是游戏，常成为亲子与游客夏季共享的趣味体验。</p>
<h3>寒汤圆（Kanzarashi）</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14565614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565614" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565614-shutterstock_1922516861.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565614-shutterstock_1922516861.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565614" class="wp-caption-text">日本长崎县岛原市（Shimabara, Nagasaki）以清澈泉水制成的寒汤圆（Kanzarashi），冰凉甜润，是当地迎接夏季来客的传统点心。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>长崎县岛原市（Shimabara, Nagasaki）市泉水丰富，当地人以糯米粉搓成小圆团，在冰糖水中冷藏食用。这道甜点最初为延长米粉保存期限而生，后来成为招待夏季来客的清凉和菓子。</p>
