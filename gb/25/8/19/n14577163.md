<h6>2026 Honda Passport TrailSport Elite。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月20日讯】在今年的Mudfest中，已率先试驾了这款2026年Honda Passport TrailSport，凭着其优越的越野能力，能把越野路上一个又一个障碍克服，也能完成Extreme（极端）越野路段，完全表现出其卓越的越野能力。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d2oemtubh889hy.cloudfront.net/L0kmfDpUL4Ng"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/USmXZja7ClI?si=jdfUhytIe_vN3QFo" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>2026年款Passport，是全新设计车款，属于Passport车系的第四代产品，Passport有三种主要车型，分别为RTL、TrailSport和TrailSport Elite，这周测试的是Honda TrailSport Elite。Honda Passport TrailSport Elite外形粗犷，前方底部装上了两个红色挂勾，牵引高达5,000磅，适用于船只、露营车等。珍珠白色的车色，在阳光下也会闪闪发亮，头灯组由橙色LED灯围绕，份外好看。再加上前方作为保护的钢制护板，再加上Continental CrossContact ATR全地形轮胎，增强越野能力。橙色的“TrailSport”徽章份外显眼，还配备了电动折叠后视镜，以及加热的挡风玻璃雨刷，在寒冷天气下也不会被卡住。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577164-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_12.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577164-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577164" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Honda Passport TrailSport Elite。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Passport搭载了在林肯厂方制造的3.5升DOHC V6引擎，并搭配10速自动变速箱，提供285 hp马力和262 lb-ft扭矩，比上一代增加了5 hp。Passport采用Honda轻型卡车架构，该平台首次应用于全新Honda Pilot，具备极高刚性，专为越野能力设计。测试时，引擎动力充沛，城市及高速公路都难不到它，而在之前的Mudfest活动中，Passport的动力又可应付各种越野路段。Passport提供七种驾驶模式，包括雪地、运动、沙地等模式。Sport模式下的加速表现迅猛，动力输出顺畅，Normal模式则提供更平顺、舒适的驾驶体验。车内的变速控制是由Acura移值过来的变速按键，由上至下为P、R、N及D／S，按键大小各有不同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577167-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577167-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577167" class="wp-caption-text">3.5升DOHC V6引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>i-VTM4矩矢量全轮驱动及其四模式智能牵引力管理系统，i-VTM4使用主动扭矩矢量将高达70％的发动机扭矩发送到后轴，并将100％的扭矩发送到左后轮或右后轮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577168-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_09.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577168-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577168" class="wp-caption-text">Continental CrossContact ATR全地形轮胎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>进入车内，2026年Passport TrailSport的内饰设计以实用性和舒适性为核心，同时融入现代科技。标配12.3&#8243;触控萤幕，支援Google Built-In、Amazon Alexa以及无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto，操作直观且反应灵敏。TrailSport车型采用条纹座椅面料，搭配类似粗麻布的仪表板和车门饰板，增添运动与户外风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577169-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577169-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577169" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>TrailSport Elite则提供更高级的皮革座椅和座椅通风功能，兼顾豪华感。后排腿部和头部空间宽敞，后货舱提供44 cu.ft的储物空间，折叠第二排后可扩展至84 cu.ft，足以应对家庭出游或户外活动的需求。每款车型均标配备用轮胎，且可选配全尺寸备胎，放置于货舱内，体现Honda对实用性的重视。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577170-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577170-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577170" class="wp-caption-text">12.3&#8243;触控萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全方面，Passport TrailSport搭载Honda Sensing系统，包括碰撞缓解制动、车道保持辅助、交通标志识别等功能，确保日常驾驶的安全性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577171-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_23.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577171-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577171" class="wp-caption-text">变速按键。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管Passport TrailSport主打越野，但其在日常驾驶中的表现同样令人满意。悬挂系统平衡了舒适性与稳定性，车内隔音效果出色，即使搭载全地形轮胎，胎噪也控制得宜。与Toyota 4Runner和Ford Bronco等车身框架式SUV相比，Passport TrailSport的单体式车身设计在越野能力上稍逊，但在公路驾驶中提供更舒适的体验。而相较于自家的CR-V TrailSport，Passport TrailSport提供更高阶的越野装备，如护板和专属悬挂调校，适合更严苛的地形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577172-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577172-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577172" class="wp-caption-text">Passport TrailSport主打越野。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>凭借其强大的V6引擎、专为越野设计的装备以及宽敞的内部空间，使其在竞争激烈的市场中脱颖而出，对于寻求冒险与家庭用车平衡的消费者而言，Passport TrailSport无疑是一款值得考虑的选择。其价格合理、可靠性高，且 Honda的工程品质为其加分不少。无论是穿越城市街道还是探索乡野小径，2026年Passport TrailSport都能成为您的可靠伙伴。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577173-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_24.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577173-2026_Honda_Passport_Trailsport_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577173" class="wp-caption-text">驾驶模式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
