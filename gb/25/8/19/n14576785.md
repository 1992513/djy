<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【首尔自由行】 第一天：明洞商圈与必吃美食</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>这是两个女儿与妈妈的五天四夜自由行，真的是久违的三人行，平时大家各忙各的，要三个人都有空实属难得。日期敲定后，大家就尽量把事情排开，也就有了这次的首尔行。</p>
<p>在这里先介绍我们的家庭成员，Lana是妈妈，Nina是大女儿，Winnie是小女儿。出发前一个礼拜开始规划行程，订机票、订饭店、购买eSIM、预约韩服、预约一日游、列采买清单。</p>
<p>这次搭乘“真航空”，也算是廉价航空，有点惊讶的是这班机是大飞机，机舱宽敞，座位数很多，坐起来还算舒适，空间也大。在网路订票，清楚写着没有机上餐，所以出发前我们就在桃园国际机场第一航厦“老董牛肉面”吃饱了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14576798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14576798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576798-20c163003b18ff7dc32be7a91f3261cf.jpg"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14576798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576798-20c163003b18ff7dc32be7a91f3261cf-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14576798" class="wp-caption-text">“牛肉面”配上“珍珠奶茶”。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>上机后就开始补眠，醒来前面多了个三明治，上面还清楚写着我们的名字，别人都没有，只有我们三个人有，怎么都想不明白。三明治是冷藏过的，摸起来凉凉的，吃起来口味还是不错的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14576797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14576797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576797-bea5cde3f8d77520b679f7bf9ce75fe6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14576797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576797-bea5cde3f8d77520b679f7bf9ce75fe6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14576797" class="wp-caption-text">“真航空”机舱内部。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14576796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14576796" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576796-7981e755de0b1b083c8cb7621853a5ef.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14576796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576796-7981e755de0b1b083c8cb7621853a5ef-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14576796" class="wp-caption-text">醒来后，前面多了份三明治和水。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>抵达仁川机场后，到地下1楼，搭“机场快线AREX”到首尔车站，约43分钟抵达，再从首尔车站转搭地铁到明洞站，两站就到了。我们住宿的饭店“Skypark III Hotel”就在明洞站9号出口旁边，看中的是它的地理位置方便。隔壁有换汇所、7-11，饭店后面就是明洞商圈，吃东西、逛街都很方便。</p>
<p>“机场快线AREX”的车票，是事先在网路购买的，需在实体机台划位，有座位才能上车。若还没来得及买韩国交通卡（T money），可以在仁川机场的CU便利商店购买。机场也有银行可以换汇，但汇率比较没那么好，所以比较不推荐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14576795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14576795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576795-1af4fa02e0b5abc44c173a12cb83e69c.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14576795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576795-1af4fa02e0b5abc44c173a12cb83e69c-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14576795" class="wp-caption-text">我们入住的饭店 Skypark III Hotel。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14576794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14576794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576794-23d5387c4c01f9083d7f71409008000b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14576794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576794-23d5387c4c01f9083d7f71409008000b-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14576794" class="wp-caption-text">饭店隔壁的换汇所，汇率很好，旁边就是7-11。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>放完行李，我们去明洞商圈吃饭，先在韩国知名连锁咖啡店买了杯咖啡，再来就直奔“安东炖鸡”，是我们每次到韩国必吃的店。店内炖鸡分三种：最受欢迎的是无骨炖鸡，再来就是海鲜炖鸡以及带骨的炖鸡，都有中、大份量可以选择。我们三个人每次都点无骨炖鸡，中份量。鸡肉炖的非常嫩，里头加了红萝卜、马铃薯、高丽菜，以及非常入味的宽冬粉，非常的好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14576793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14576793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576793-20c84c93bb99bb1daf78b262984fceda.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14576793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576793-20c84c93bb99bb1daf78b262984fceda-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14576793" class="wp-caption-text">我们点了无骨炖鸡（中）。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吃饱后就散步逛街，明洞有很多小吃摊以及美妆用品店，好吃又好逛。我们出发前就已经做过功课，也列了必买清单，采买就很快了。逛了两个多小时，把基本想买的都买了，每个人都是一大袋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14576792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14576792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576792-8e0c46b7752a58b113842f418152c195.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14576792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14576792-8e0c46b7752a58b113842f418152c195-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14576792" class="wp-caption-text">在明洞商圈逛街。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
