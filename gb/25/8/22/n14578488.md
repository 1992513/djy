<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“发芽．木雕小小兵创作营”结业    三义木雕博物馆开办</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578499-789359-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大师讲座照片（苗县府文观局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月22日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）三义木雕博物馆自8/10(日)至8/21(四)间，办理“发芽．木雕小小兵创作营”。共培训13位学员，并于8月21日举行结业式，活动圆满落幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14578565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578565" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578565-789365.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14578565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578565-789365-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578565" class="wp-caption-text">始业式合照。（苗县府文观局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三义木雕博物馆长年推动“木雕传统技艺养根计划”，其中“发芽．木雕小小兵创作营”更是本计划长期成果的体现。本营队透过“森林时装秀”与“森林派对”概念，带领学员发挥创意、体验角色扮演，并结合专业导览及大师讲座，让学员深入认识木雕技艺，培养创作表达能力与艺术想像力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14578518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578518" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578518-789360.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14578518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578518-789360-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578518" class="wp-caption-text">结业式。（苗县府文观局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14578535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578535-789361.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14578535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578535-789361-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578535" class="wp-caption-text">雕刻过程。（苗县府文观局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14578545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578545" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578545-789362.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14578545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578545-789362-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578545" class="wp-caption-text">小小解说员。（苗县府文观局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>透过课程与创作，学员不仅习得木雕基本技巧，更透过艺术实作深化对生活美学与在地文化的理解，进一步提升对艺术的赏析与分享能力。</p>
<p>学员创作成果现正于博物馆一馆一楼展出，共20件作品。包含13位学员的创作成果及7件讲师示范作品，展出期间从即日起至10/31(五)。欢迎有兴趣的民众前来观展，共同见证木雕艺术的创新与延续。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14578557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578557" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578557-789363.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14578557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578557-789363-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578557" class="wp-caption-text">作品照片。（苗县府文观局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14578562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578562" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578562-789364.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14578562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578562-789364-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578562" class="wp-caption-text">作品照片。（苗县府文观局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次创作营邀请台湾木雕协会的10位新锐木雕师担任课程讲师，营队讲师多为博物馆所举办“亚洲青年木雕艺术研习营”的学生。这些新生代木雕师及师承父执辈学习木雕的第二代三义青年，承袭传统技艺并融合学术知识、致力于木雕艺术的创新与传承，为三义注入新的文化动能。</p>
<p>参与营队的学员除了有三义木雕师的第三代，更吸引到周围乡镇的学生前来参与。透过小小导览员活动，学员在三义木雕博物馆内的导览，也让游客对三义木雕产生更多兴趣。</p>
<p>苗栗县政府文化观光局表示，木雕技艺是三义地区最具代表性的文化特色。藉由“发芽．木雕小小兵创作营”，可见学员投入木雕创作的热情与潜力，象征着木雕艺术的延续与希望，未来将持续推动艺文教育向下扎根，厚植在地文化实力。</p>
