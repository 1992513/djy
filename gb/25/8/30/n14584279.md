<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>百年桧木香山车站 新竹仅存的日式木造铁道记忆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584280-791687-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香山车站正面全景，日治时期木构车站建筑至今仍保留原貌。（林桔春提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新竹报导）摄影家林桔春站在香山车站月台边，举起相机记录这座历经近百年岁月的车站。他说：“台铁古色古香的百年车站之宝——香山车站，也是台湾目前唯一全桧木造的车站，建造于1928年，是新竹市的市定古迹，分享给大家。”对多数旅客而言，香山车站或许只是纵贯线南端的新竹市小站，但对熟悉在地历史的人来说，这里是一段重要的铁道记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14584298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584298" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584298-791705.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14584298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584298-791705-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584298" class="wp-caption-text">桧木的使用在当时属高规格待遇，除了耐候，也能营造温润氛围。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584287-791694.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14584287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584287-791694-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584287" class="wp-caption-text">售票窗口与公告栏，保留传统格局并融入现代资讯。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584281" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584281-791688.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14584281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584281-791688-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584281" class="wp-caption-text">车站入口旁立有“香山车站”站牌，见证历史与现代交会。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1928年的起点香山车站启用于1928年，当时日本政府为方便南北铁路运输及地方居民通勤，在香山设站。车站建筑采全桧木兴建，散发淡淡木香，也因木材质地坚韧耐用，即使历经风雨仍保有稳固结构。桧木的使用在当时属高规格待遇，除了耐候，也能营造温润氛围。如今走进车站，抬头可见格状木天花，墙面与窗櫺皆保留日治时期的格窗设计，阳光透过木框映在地面，光影间透着岁月痕迹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14584289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584289-791696.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14584289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584289-791696-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584289" class="wp-caption-text">木格天花板与古典吊灯，完整保留日治时代的设计语汇。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584284" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584284-791691.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14584284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584284-791691-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584284" class="wp-caption-text">候车区木椅与长廊空间，让旅客在静谧中等待列车。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584282-791689.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14584282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584282-791689-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584282" class="wp-caption-text">木制格栅窗保留百年风貌，展现古朴工艺。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全台唯一全桧木造车站全桧木建筑的稀有，使香山车站在全台铁道系统中独具地位。站体外观延续日式车站简洁俐落的线条，入口处木梁交错，朴实却耐看。站务室、候车室、值班站长室等空间，皆保留原有木构架与拉门，让人仿佛回到昭和年代。近年市府与台铁合作进行修缮，保留木料原色与细节，让它在现代化铁道网中依旧延续百年前的模样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14584295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584295" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584295-791702.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14584295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584295-791702-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584295" class="wp-caption-text">车站值班站长室外观，木造结构与玻璃窗保留浓厚日式风格。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584288-791695.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14584288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584288-791695-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584288" class="wp-caption-text">候车室内的木窗与长椅，映照出怀旧氛围。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584285" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584285-791692.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14584285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584285-791692-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584285" class="wp-caption-text">入口处木作门廊，细节雕饰承载昔日匠心。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车站与地方生活香山车站位于新竹市区西南方，临近海岸与台一线。早年当地盛产稻米、蔬果，居民透过火车将农产运往新竹、竹南等地销售。随着公路运输兴起，货运功能渐淡，但对通勤族与学生而言，车站仍是每日往返的重要起点。现今的香山车站为简易站，设有两座月台与跨站天桥，连接新式电梯与雨棚，方便旅客通行。旧木造站房则作为售票与候车空间，承载人来人往的日常。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14584290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584290" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584290-791697.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14584290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584290-791697-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584290" class="wp-caption-text">香山车站月台一景，列车停靠在第一月台，旅客上下车的主要动线。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584293-791700.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14584293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584293-791700-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584293" class="wp-caption-text">月台及连通的天桥电梯，方便旅客跨越轨道到达不同月台。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584292-791699.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14584292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584292-791699-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584292" class="wp-caption-text">香山车站月台。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>铁道迷与摄影人的景点林桔春说，香山车站是许多铁道迷的“口袋名单”，不仅因它的桧木建筑，也因紧邻的月台可拍到南北行列车交会。晴朗的午后，绿色涂装的区间车、白色普悠玛号或橘蓝色自强号驶入站内，与木造站房形成新旧交织的画面，格外耐人寻味。对摄影人而言，香山车站的美在于它没有刻意粉饰的质朴，木纹上的斑驳是岁月的签名，斜照的光线、月台边的草色，都能成为构图的一部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14584286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584286" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584286-791693.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14584286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584286-791693-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584286" class="wp-caption-text">屋檐转角的优雅曲线雕花，增添古建筑韵味。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14584294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14584294" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584294-791701.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14584294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584294-791701-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14584294" class="wp-caption-text">列车进入香山车站月台，月台雨棚支柱排列延伸至远方。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>保留下来的价值2014年，香山车站被指定为新竹市市定古迹，原因不仅是建筑稀有，更因它是铁道文化的实物见证。市府文化局指出，未来将持续与台铁合作，确保建筑安全与使用功能并存，让车站继续服务居民，也让更多人能亲眼看见桧木车站的风采。走出站房，桧木的香气依旧在空气中轻轻飘散。对往来的旅客而言，这里或许只是一段旅程的中继，但对熟悉它的人来说，香山车站是一页未完的故事，还会继续写下去。</p>
