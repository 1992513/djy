<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾防长与美参院军委会主席会晤 盼深化合作</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14584478-2508301148112378.jpg" />

Wicker）及参议员费雪（Deb Fischer）等人。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px"
