<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>老工厂的新生活 富兴工厂1962</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575261-788401-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“富兴工厂1962”字样低调嵌在墙上，门口的植栽顺着阳光生长，传递着老屋持续呼吸的生命力。（Nina提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台中报导）2025年八月夏末，走进台中后火车站一带，阳光下的富兴工厂1962显得特别醒目。这里原是“盛香堂”，台湾最早的化妆品公司之一，当年曾是产业发展的重要基地。如今，透过清筑设计团队的重新规划，这座六十多年历史的老工厂，翻转成为兼具创意和文化的聚落。</p>
<p>园区入口并不张扬，灰色外墙还留着工业时代的痕迹，窗框和栏杆显得有点斑驳，但当踏入其中，映入眼帘的是完全不同的氛围。老建筑的骨架被保留下来，但空间里注入了新的能量，变成一个集合设计品牌、展览、阅读与市集的生活场域。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575262" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575262-788402.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575262-788402-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575262" class="wp-caption-text">咖啡香气弥漫的馆内一隅，简约工业风的吧台与木质设计融合，成为游客休憩与对话的场域。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575259" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575259-788399.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575259-788399-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575259" class="wp-caption-text">从空拍视角看见工厂院落与彩绘外墙，绿意植栽与红砖屋瓦并存，展现老屋新生的活力。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575260" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575260-788400.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575260-788400-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575260" class="wp-caption-text">阳光下的富兴工厂 1962，老屋立面仍保留当年的坚实结构。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>旧建筑里的新创意走到一楼展区，最先看到的是各式编织装置与设计作品。黄色的藤编大叶子从墙边探出来，几张用藤条编织的座椅摆放在角落，上头挂着手作包、圆形的织品和镜子。一位戴着草帽的年轻人站在这里，带着笑容，身旁是两座穿着大衣的假人模特儿。这一幕让人感觉像走进了一间既现代又带点怀旧味道的艺廊。再往里走，另一处空间展示着夏日服饰与藤编包，模特儿穿着清爽的服装，旁边三位游客正低头仔细挑选。中央的装置用彩色布料，与竹编拼凑成大型植物造型，将冷调的工业空间点缀得活泼起来。转个弯，则是一间老派氛围浓厚的唱片店。木框窗户、堆满磁带的展示架，还有禁止拍照的手写告示，都让人瞬间掉进时光隧道。透过玻璃望进去，满墙的黑胶唱片、卡带与老式音响一字排开，仿佛回到七、八〇年代的台湾流行音乐时代。这里既是收藏者的宝库，也成为年轻人重新认识声音历史的入口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575256" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575256-788396.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575256-788396-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575256" class="wp-caption-text">昔日的工厂空间，如今被保留下来，木框玻璃屋、复古沙发与展示柜交织出浓浓时代感。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575257-788397.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575257-788397-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575257" class="wp-caption-text">改造后的空间再现复古理发厅氛围，柜台、镜面与灯饰一应俱全，让人穿越回 1960 年代。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中庭里的热闹从二楼走到阳台往下望，中庭正是富兴工厂1962的心脏。午后的阳光洒落，几十位民众聚在一起，或是逛摊位，或是坐下来聊天。白色的布棚和一串串小灯泡在空中交错，氛围像是欧洲小镇的广场。孩子在树荫下追逐，大人则停在摊位前，随手挑选手作品或饮品。靠墙的位置，挂着几个放大版的化妆粉饼和口红装置，提醒着这里的前身曾经是化妆品工厂。老墙面与新装置并存，正好展现“老屋翻新 × 文创聚落”的精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575263" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575263-788403.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575263-788403-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575263" class="wp-caption-text">一处安静的展览角落，花艺与摄影作品相映，让老工厂化身为美感生活的温柔据点。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575264" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575264-788404.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575264-788404-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575264" class="wp-caption-text">穿过木门与玻璃窗的回廊，光线映照“1962”的字样，走向户外天井，仿佛从历史步入当下。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575265" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575265-788405.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575265-788405-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575265" class="wp-caption-text">花艺工作室散发温润气息，干燥花与手作摆设在窗橱中展开，为旧时工业场景添上柔美色彩。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一处可停留的角落除了展览与市集，富兴工厂1962也提供静下来的空间。二楼的一间大房间改造成阅读与讲座场地，书墙上陈列各式杂志与设计刊物，沙发、懒骨头椅随意摆放，让人能轻松翻阅书本或参加分享会。下午时分，阳光从窗外洒进来，光影在地板与桌椅间游走，氛围安静而舒适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575267" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575267-788406.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575267" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575267-788406-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575267" class="wp-caption-text">探头进入工厂的地下通道，带着几分神秘与探险感。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575268" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575268-788407.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575268-788407-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575268" class="wp-caption-text">墙上展览“荒芜与重生”的影像故事，记录了工厂从闲置到活化的过程，每张黑白照片都刻画着岁月痕迹。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575269" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575269-788408.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575269-788408-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575269" class="wp-caption-text">一幅浪漫的生活风景。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>老建筑的新生活富兴工厂1962并不是单纯的改造，而是一种生活方式的再生。这里既有历史，也有创意；既能让人忆起旧时代的工厂记忆，又能让设计师和创意品牌在此实践新的可能。正如许多来访的年轻人说的：“这里不是冷冰冰的博物馆，而是可以生活的地方。”在这座老工厂里，市集、展览、唱片、阅读和咖啡香交织在一起，让人看见旧建筑与新生活的最好融合。走出园区时，午后的阳光依旧炙热，但心里却有一股清爽。这不是单纯的怀旧，而是一种实实在在的生活体验。富兴工厂1962，正以它的故事和空间，提醒着我们：老屋不必被遗忘，它还能开启一段全新的篇章。</p>
