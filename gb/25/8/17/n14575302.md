<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>秋来茶香浓 苗栗兴隆的采茶日常</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575304-788348-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>茶园里的新叶在阳光下闪耀着光泽，正是制作高品质白露茶的重要来源。（林桔春提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾苗栗报导）秋意渐起，苗栗头份市兴隆里的茶园，八月份一年之中最具生气的时节。走进茶园，首先映入眼帘的，是一排排低头忙碌的采茶工人。他们头顶各式遮阳布，或是花布、蓝布，甚至闪亮的铝箔布，在烈日下形成一片特殊的“茶园色彩”。有人坐在小凳上，有人背着竹篓，双手俐落摘取一心二叶。这样的画面，不需要多余言语，就能感受到茶乡的节奏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575306-788350.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575306-788350-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575306" class="wp-caption-text">茶农们分列于茶行之间，彼此默契配合，将最新鲜的茶叶送进背上的大竹篓。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575303" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575303-788347.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575303-788347-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575303" class="wp-caption-text">苗栗头份市兴隆里的日新茶园，在夏末初秋时分展现一片盎然绿意，这里出产的“白露茶”向来以品质闻名。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>人影与茶丛　构成秋日乐章采茶人们在茶行间排列整齐，像是一支默契绝佳的队伍。远处，金属水塔与翠绿树林静静伫立，与眼前劳动的景象交织出一幅生动图景。阳光下，每一个弯腰、伸手的动作都显得扎实有力。那是一种熟练的节奏，更是一种生活的韵律。短暂休息时，工人抬起头，喝口水，再次埋首茶丛。那一抹微笑，写满辛劳却也透出满足。茶园里的劳作，不仅是生计，更是与土地深厚的牵系。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575307-788351.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575307-788351-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575307" class="wp-caption-text">茶农小心翼翼地摘取最适合制茶的一心二叶，手势俐落，展现多年经验。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575305-788349.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575305-788349-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575305" class="wp-caption-text">在日新茶园里，茶农们戴着斗笠、披着布巾，在烈日下弯腰采收，一篮篮新鲜茶菁逐渐满盈。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>茶乡意象　诚挚邀约在茶园入口，一座高高的茶乡意象雕塑矗立，上头写着“茶乡之美 诚挚邀约”、“雅客闻香来”、“兴隆出好茶”。这不仅是一句标语，而是一份邀请。兴隆里长年以茶闻名，特别是东方美人茶，因茶芽曾被小绿叶蝉吸食而产生独特风味，经过繁复制程，带出熟果香与甘醇口感。苗栗县每年举办的东方美人茶评鉴，吸引数百件茶样参加，这不只是比赛，更像是一场茶农心血的验收。兴隆茶园也在其中屡获肯定，成为地方骄傲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575308-788352.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575308-788352-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575308" class="wp-caption-text">采茶人们头戴布巾或铝箔布遮阳，在炎热中依旧不辞辛劳，只为确保茶叶的新鲜与品质。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575317" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575317-788361.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575317-788361-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575317" class="wp-caption-text">茶乡地标。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古厝与茶园　农村的底色茶园不远处，是一排红砖三合院。屋脊线条优雅，白色屋檐与蓝天相映，前方的茶园像绿色地毯般延展。古厝与茶园同框，画面朴实却动人。这里的茶，不只是商品，它承载着一代代茶农的心血，与农村文化紧紧相扣。地上的竹篓，是茶农最重要的工具。它编织精细，结实耐用。当它装满茶菁的时候，也装满了一个秋天的汗水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575310-788354.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575310-788354-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575310" class="wp-caption-text">夏末的茶园里，采茶工人们共同努力，场面壮观，形成一道独特的农村景致。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575311" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575311-788355.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575311-788355-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575311" class="wp-caption-text">一排排茶农坐在小椅凳上，背后竹篓逐渐装满新鲜茶叶，映衬着蓝天与茶园的翠绿。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一壶茶香　暖透心房秋日午后，微风带着干爽，却因茶香而添上几分温润。即便还没啜饮一口茶，那萦绕鼻尖的清香，已经先一步解了心中的渴。这里的茶园，不只养育茶树，更养育了一份沉静安稳。在兴隆茶园，采茶人的身影、古厝的底色、雕塑的邀请，合力写下了茶乡的诗篇。秋意未深，茶香已浓。这是一份来自土地的礼物，也是一壶还未入口就能暖心的茶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575318" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575318-788362.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14575318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575318-788362-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575318" class="wp-caption-text">一只编织精巧的背篓静静放在草地上，它是茶农采收时不可或缺的工具，也记录了无数汗水。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575309-788353.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575309-788353-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575309" class="wp-caption-text">日新茶园主人许时稳（右一，白帽）与采茶工交流，确保每片茶叶的品质与采收进度。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575316-788360.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575316-788360-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575316" class="wp-caption-text">古朴的红砖三合院静静伫立，前方的茶园绿意盎然，构成传统农村最典型的画面。（林桔春提供）</figcaption></figure>
