<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义企经会30周年 新卸任理事长交接盛典</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575240-788381-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义市企业经理协进会于16日隆重举办“第23届与第24届理事长交接暨企业之夜”盛典，邀集产、官、学界菁英齐聚一堂，共襄盛举。图为颁发第24届理监事及干部的当选证书和聘书。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市企业经理协进会于16日假嘉义市耐斯王子大饭店5楼阿里山厅，隆重举办“第23届与第24届理事长交接暨企业之夜”盛典，邀集产、官、学界菁英齐聚一堂，见证理事长交接的重要时刻。此次盛会不仅象征领导传承，更彰显协进会持续推动嘉义市产业发展、促进企业交流合作的重要使命。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575242" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575242-788382.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575242-788382-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575242" class="wp-caption-text">图为历届理事长大合照。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市企业经理协进会自成立以来，致力于整合嘉义及周边地区企业资源，促进会员间的交流合作，并透过举办各类讲座、培训课程与交流活动，协助企业经理人提升专业管理能力与国际视野。协进会长期扮演企业与政府、社会间的桥梁，积极推动产业升级、创新转型，成为嘉义地区企业界的重要核心平台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575243" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575243-788383.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575243-788383-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575243" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市副市长林瑞彦（中）和卸任理事长辜耀义（左2）、新任理事长林启辉（右2）等人合照。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575244" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575244-788384.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575244" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575244-788384-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575244" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市副市长林瑞彦（左）致赠纪念品给卸任理事长辜耀义（右）。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动于16日下午14时展开，嘉宾报到后，历届理事长陆续进场，大会随即进行介绍来宾、理事长与主办单位代表致词、颁发表扬奖项等仪式，并完成第24届理监事及干部的聘书颁发与新会员宣誓，展现嘉义市企业经理协进会的传承与新气象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575245" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575245-788385.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575245-788385-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575245" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市副市长林瑞彦（左）颁发当选证书给新任理事长林启辉（右）。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575246" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575246-788387.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575246-788387-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575246" class="wp-caption-text">在大会当中，特地安排邓陈铭总经理（环绮国际贸易股份有限公司）以“2025 转型新时代”为题，深入剖析企业面对全球市场变化与数位转型的策略与契机。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575247" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575247-788391.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575247-788391-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575247" class="wp-caption-text">在大会当中，特地安排谢丰裕总经理（大医生技）以“从企业到社会责任：大医生技的永续之路”为题，分享如何在经营绩效与社会责任间取得平衡，实践ESG精神。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在大会当中，特地安排专题演讲环节，邀请三位重量级讲者带来精彩分享：</p>
<p>包括由邓陈铭总经理（环绮国际贸易股份有限公司）以“2025 转型新时代”为题，深入剖析企业面对全球市场变化与数位转型的策略与契机。由谢丰裕总经理（大医生技）以“从企业到社会责任：大医生技的永续之路”为题，分享如何在经营绩效与社会责任间取得平衡，实践ESG精神。林铭贤总经理（北回培英文教协会会长）则以“甜蜜的不归路”为题，带来充满启发与人文关怀的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575248" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575248-788392.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575248-788392-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575248" class="wp-caption-text">在大会当中，特地安排林铭贤总经理（北回培英文教协会会长）以“甜蜜的不归路”为题，带来充满启发与人文关怀的故事。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>晚间的“企业之夜”在知名主持人蔡佳静的精彩带领之下，安排了联谊与餐叙，促进跨产业对话与合作，为未来的商机铺路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575250" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575250-788394.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575250-788394-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575250" class="wp-caption-text">新任理事长林启辉（左）致赠纪念品给卸任理事长辜耀义（右）。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575251" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575251-788412.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14575251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575251-788412-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575251" class="wp-caption-text">新任理事长林启辉（左）和下一届理事长萧伯纳（右）合照。（嘉义市企业经理协进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本届活动由第23届理事长辜耀义与第24届理事长林启辉共同邀请，于温馨与隆重的氛围中圆满落幕，展现嘉义市企业经理协进会持续促进企业成长、强化地方经济发展的决心与行动力。◇</p>
