<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>首届波士顿华人排球邀请赛 28队伍竞技</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575416-DSC00162-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>8月16日，首届“Boston Mini”波士顿华人排球邀请赛在麻州Everett举办。年轻人们在球场上竞赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年08月18日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨埃弗雷特市报导）8月16日在麻州埃弗雷特市（Everett）的绿河公园（Rivergreen Park），喝采声、加油声、哨声不绝于耳，这是首届“Boston Mini”波士顿华人排球邀请赛。</p>
<p>这场为期两天的9人排球赛，由纽英崙中华公所和埃弗雷特市政府共同举办，吸引了美东地区总计28个队伍参加，包括18个男子队伍和10个女子队伍，例如波士顿洪青体育会队、旋风队、纽约黑海龟队、纽约骑士队等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575427-DSC00255.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575427-DSC00255-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575427" class="wp-caption-text">首届“Boston Mini”波士顿华人排球邀请赛在麻州Everett的公园举办。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575417" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575417-DSC00169.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575417-DSC00169-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575417" class="wp-caption-text">年轻人们在球场上竞赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在艳阳高照的赛场，球员们在热烈的加油声中发球、接球、扣球。每当得分的时候，球员们皆欢呼庆祝；失分之后，他们则互相击掌打气。现场氛围非常热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575418" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575418-DSC00181.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575418-DSC00181-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575418" class="wp-caption-text">年轻人们在球场上竞赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575419" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575419-DSC00191.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575419-DSC00191-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575419" class="wp-caption-text">年轻人们在球场上竞赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“比赛更多是为了欢乐。你看到那个小奖杯，那确实是一种骄傲，因为孩子们都认识彼此。但更基本的是享受欢乐，重燃友情。”纽英崙至孝笃亲公所队教练Kevin Luke说。</p>
<p>他谈到，他打过10年排球，随后担任高中排球教练10年，过程中不仅提升孩子们的体育能力，也帮助他们培养体育文化。</p>
<p>Kevin Luke感慨，参加北美赛事的时候，赢下冠军是很难的事，因为来自美西和加拿大的一些队伍非常强力，但是比冠军更重要的，是告诉孩子们参与体育活动的意义，让他们长期坚持参加排球队。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575420" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575420" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575420-DSC00199.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575420" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575420-DSC00199-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575420" class="wp-caption-text">年轻人们在球场上竞赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575421" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575421-DSC00213.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575421-DSC00213-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575421" class="wp-caption-text">年轻人们在球场上竞赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575428-DSC00259.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575428-DSC00259-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575428" class="wp-caption-text">球员们在赛场上互相加油打气。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>埃弗雷特欢迎华人排球赛</strong></h3>
<p>开赛第一天，埃弗雷特市市长德马利亚（Carlo DeMaria）到场观看了比赛。这位曾经打过排球的市长，在各个分赛场间巡视，还不时点评球员们的表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575422-DSC00224.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575422-DSC00224-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575422" class="wp-caption-text">左起：中华公所义工Martin Chan、埃弗雷特市市长Carlo DeMaria、中华公所英文秘书阮鸿灿。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575423" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575423" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575423-DSC00227.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575423" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575423-DSC00227-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575423" class="wp-caption-text">球赛主办方代表合影。左起：Fanny Chan、Kevin Luke、市长Carlo DeMaria、中华公所英文秘书阮鸿灿、Douglas Goon。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575424" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575424-DSC00234.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575424-DSC00234-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575424" class="wp-caption-text">市长Carlo DeMaria（右）与球员们交谈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在比赛开幕式，德马利亚笑言，他很高兴能够欢迎“Boston Mini”比赛和球员们来到埃弗雷特社区，并鼓励大家在比赛之后，到市内消费，支持当地商家。</p>
<p>“我也是移民的儿子，我们的城市是移民门户城市，接受每个人，热爱每个人。我希望大家在我们的社区享有美好时光。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575425" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575425-DSC00245.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575425-DSC00245-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575425" class="wp-caption-text">主办方代表与部分参赛球员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575426" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575426-DSC00250.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575426-DSC00250-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575426" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所英文秘书阮鸿灿（左）与董事李源沛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>纽英崙中华公所英文书记阮鸿灿、董事李源沛也都到场观赛。阮鸿灿向所有参赛者表达了欢迎，并感谢埃弗雷特市提供如此宽阔的场地，让这场9人排球赛得以顺利举办。◇</p>
