<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超揭幕：曼城利物浦强势 阿森纳小胜曼联</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575522-GettyImages-2230323965.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
