<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“夏趴一下”2025苗栗海洋观光季摇滚之夜 通霄海水浴场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575123-788282-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夏趴一下”2025苗栗海洋观光季摇滚之夜 通霄海水浴场.（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗县年度盛事“2025苗栗海洋观光季”摇滚之夜于8月16日晚间在通霄海水浴场隆重登场，大型晚会活动结合音乐演出、海洋文化、沙雕艺术与渔村市集，打造一场夏夜海岸的视听飨宴。</p>
<p>本次晚会卡司阵容坚强，邀请到人气团体玖壹壹、Ozone 、AKB48 Team TP、沉默绅士、金曲歌后乔幼、民歌手李丽芬、实力唱将丽小花等轮番登台演出，吸引上千名观众涌入现场，用音乐向民众传递海洋保育、节能节电、永续发展等概念。除沙雕艺术节展出大师艺术创作之外，还有农渔特产市集展售、纪念小物免费发送、有奖征答，从白天到晚上都有活动，热闹非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575129" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575129" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575129-788287.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575129" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575129-788287-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575129" class="wp-caption-text">2025海洋观光季，涌入大量人潮，带动通霄镇地方经济高潮.（苗县府／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575128" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575128-788286.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575128" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575128-788286-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575128" class="wp-caption-text">农业处DIY摊位（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苗栗县长钟东锦率领县府团队首长群上台向民众致意，他表示今天入场观赏沙雕的人数已经超过五万人次，希望笑声、欢乐能像海风一样吹进每个人的心理，通霄海水浴场是很好的场地，承诺每年的暑假县府会持续办理沙雕艺术节、海洋观光季、慢鱼活动等，让老、中、青民众学习如何和海洋共存共荣，让海洋的资源生生不息，永续发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575126" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575126-788285.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575126-788285-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575126" class="wp-caption-text">AKB48 Team TP.表演.（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14575125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575125" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575125-788284.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575125" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575125-788284-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575125" class="wp-caption-text">金曲歌后乔幼和观众互动（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钟东锦并感谢中油、台电的赞助和各界大力支持，让海洋观光季开办10多年来，已成为暑假期间一项老少咸宜的年度盛会，每年都吸引可观游客带动地方经济繁荣。</p>
<p>为便利民众参与，县府农业处也在通霄渔港设置临时停车专区，并在通霄渔港、通霄火车站规划免费接驳巴士服务，鼓励大众搭乘公共运输前往，并呼吁参加民众注意安全、共同维护环境整洁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14575124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14575124" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575124-788283.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14575124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14575124-788283-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14575124" class="wp-caption-text">县长夫人欢迎大家来苗栗旅游（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
