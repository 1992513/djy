<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>白布帆堤防的初秋   甜根子花迎风</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580041-790221-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>甜根子花在蓝天映衬下挺立如诗，映出初秋最纯净的色彩。（Nina　提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾苗栗报导）初秋的阳光正好，大安溪沿岸的白布帆堤防，甜根子花的花季开跑了。空拍达人Nina 8月24日特地走访，记录下这片沿着河岸绵延盛开的雪白花海。“现在正是初开，很雪白。”Nina笑着说。她的镜头从空中俯瞰，整条堤防旁的甜根子花像一条铺展的白色缎带，贴着河岸，随风摆动。远方的山峦层层相叠，衬着蓝天，花影与河流的线条在阳光下显得格外分明。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580048" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580048-790228.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580048-790228-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580048" class="wp-caption-text">蜿蜒的溪流穿越大片花毯，远山、河道与白花构成大安溪特有的季节风景。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580038-790218.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580038-790218-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580038" class="wp-caption-text">微风拂过，大片甜根子花在阳光下闪烁，仿佛银浪翻涌。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580037" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580037-790217.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580037-790217-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580037" class="wp-caption-text">狼尾草与白色甜根子花并肩绽放，为河岸小径增添层次色彩。（Nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580035-790215.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580035-790215-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580035" class="wp-caption-text">蓝天下，甜根子花迎风摇曳，宛如片片白雪铺满河岸。（Nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>初开的洁白甜根子花是台湾河川地景的常客，每到花期，花穗由嫩绿转为雪白，柔软的花絮迎风摇曳，带着淡淡的草香。这时候的花，正值初开，颜色纯净，还未被日晒与风吹染上米黄或灰色。走近堤防，可以看到花穗在阳光下闪着银光，像细致的羽毛堆叠在枝头。风吹过，大片花穗同时倾斜，仿佛浪潮翻涌。这一刻，即便什么话都不说，也能感觉到河岸的呼吸与季节的转换。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580039-790219.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580039-790219-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580039" class="wp-caption-text">近看甜根子花穗轻盈柔软，细致纹理在阳光下泛着银光。（Nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580036-790216.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580036-790216-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580036" class="wp-caption-text">河堤旁的甜根子花海一望无际，远山层层，映衬出初秋的清爽气息。（Nina 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580043-790223.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580043-790223-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580043" class="wp-caption-text">沿着小径前行，甜根子花随风轻舞，秋日的浪漫气息扑面而来。（Nina　提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>河岸的节奏白布帆堤防平时是防洪的重要屏障，如今却多了一层季节的妆点。河面那头的山脉静静矗立，近处的花海随风而动，远近之间的节奏感很自然。堤防边的小路，偶尔有单车或行人经过，停下来拍照、聊天，然后继续前行。Nina用空拍捕捉到的不只是花的形状，还有这片地景与周边环境的关系。从高空俯视，甜根子花的白色与河道、绿地交织成自然的图案，像是一幅不经意完成的画。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580044-790224.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580044" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580044-790224-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580044" class="wp-caption-text">初秋的大安溪河滩，甜根子花迎风摇曳，宛如铺上一层洁白的羽毛毯。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580047" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580047-790227.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580047-790227-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580047" class="wp-caption-text">溪水与花海相依，波光粼粼间，甜根子穗随风轻摆，传递初秋的讯息。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580042-790222.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580042-790222-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580042" class="wp-caption-text">远处的山影与近处的花海交织，河岸风光如画般静美。（Nina　提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>季节的讯号甜根子花的出现，是河川秋天的讯号。它们生长在砂石地与堤防旁，对土壤和水分的需求不高，却能在风中展现韧性。对当地人来说，看到花开，就知道夏天的炎热已经退去，河岸的景色将进入另一种安静的节奏。沿着大安溪走一段路，可以看到不同阶段的花——有的才刚吐穗，颜色还带着绿意；有的已经完全绽放，白得耀眼。这种层次，也让河岸的景观更加丰富。“甜根子花的花期不长，等满开的时候，颜色会更饱满，但我喜欢这种刚开的白。”Nina边收起空拍机，边看着远方的花海说。她的照片和影相里，没有刻意安排的人物，只有自然的线条与光影。对她来说，摄影不只是记录景象，也是留住当下的空气与温度。今年的白布帆堤防，被甜根子花染上的这抹白，正静静地在风里摇曳。</p>
