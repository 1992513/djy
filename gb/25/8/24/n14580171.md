<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>高空与老屋之间 台北两种咖啡时光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580172-790278-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>豆留文青前身是锅炉房，旁边烟囱高 36公尺，后方高楼映衬。（Nina提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台北报导）8月初，空拍达人Nina在信义区找到一个看城市的好地方——【KA SKY COFFEE】。咖啡厅位在富邦A25商业大楼33楼，大片落地玻璃将台北的街景摊开在眼前。她站在窗边，先看见的是不远处的陶朱隐园，那栋旋转造型的豪宅顶楼直升机坪清楚可见，接着视线移到台北101和微风南山，周围的商办大楼像是舞台上的配角，衬着这两栋地标。店里分成窗边高脚椅区与内侧沙发区，沙发区只接受预订，现场想坐只能等高脚椅的位置。Nina坐在窗边，手里的咖啡反倒不是重点，倒是眼前的城市让她看得出神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580185-790291.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580185-790291-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580185" class="wp-caption-text">窗边与内侧座位分区，大片落地窗让客人边喝咖啡边看风景。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580182-790288.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580182-790288-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580182" class="wp-caption-text">展馆内的蓝色长条狗造型雕塑，造型逗趣可爱。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580180-790286.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580180-790286-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580180" class="wp-caption-text">信义区Fubon Art Center前的大型白色字母人雕塑，吸引路人驻足拍照。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580179" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580179-790285.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14580179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580179-790285-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580179" class="wp-caption-text">从富邦A25大楼33楼望去，旋转造型的豪宅陶朱隐园与顶楼直升机坪一览无遗。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>米其林必比登的晚餐离开咖啡厅后，她被同学带去吃晚餐，地点是连续六年入选米其林必比登的双月餐厅。现场要排队，但她觉得值得。桌上先来一碗鲜鱼汤，清甜温暖；吻仔鱼煎面的香气从碗里溢出；蚵仔肥美，煎菇有淡淡焦香。她一边夹菜，一边听隔壁桌客人聊着这里的汤头和食材。那是一种很生活的场景，没有刻意的摆拍，只有单纯的好味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580186-790292.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580186-790292-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580186" class="wp-caption-text">双月餐厅招牌料理之一吻仔鱼煎面、鲜鱼汤与烤菇摆满桌面。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580187-790293.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580187-790293-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580187" class="wp-caption-text">双月餐厅的海鲜拼盘鲜鱼汤、蚵仔、烤豆干与菇类，份量十足。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>老烟囱的咖啡香同月下旬，Nina又到另一间让她喜欢的咖啡馆——豆留文青。这里原本是锅炉房，旁边有36公尺高的烟囱，后方是高楼大厦。老屋和新建筑同框，让她觉得有意思。她说：“完全就是我的菜，等到11点开门一定要进去喝杯咖啡。”屋内保留了高挑的屋顶和木窗，钢架结构裸露着，空间宽敞。她点了一杯特别的“烟囱咖啡”，服务生问要不要帮忙拍照。焦糖浆淋下时，杯里的烟囱浮起来，像是冒着热气的小屋。她提醒自己要先搅匀底部的黑芝麻酱，入口时芝麻与咖啡的味道很搭，“真的很像烟噗噗的烟囱”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580175" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580175-790281.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14580175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580175-790281-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580175" class="wp-caption-text">焦糖浆淋下时，杯中的烟囱浮起来，喝前要先搅拌芝麻酱。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580173-790279.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580173-790279-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580173" class="wp-caption-text">保留老屋结构的空间里，现代化吧台与甜点柜充满活力。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580189-790295.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580189-790295-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580189" class="wp-caption-text">豆留文青，这里原本是锅炉房，旁边有36公尺高的烟囱。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580174-790280.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580174-790280-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580174" class="wp-caption-text">阳光透过木窗洒进室内，空间明亮且保有旧时氛围。（Nina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>空间的细节豆留文青不只是咖啡香，还有不少细节：甜点柜里的可颂与饼乾刚出炉，墙上挂着日式风格的画，角落设有绘马挂所，让客人可以写下心愿。阳光透进木窗，落在桌面和饮料上，让时间显得很慢。在不同高度看城市一个是高空，一个是老屋，Nina在同一个月里体验了两种截然不同的咖啡时光。高空的景色让人看见城市的全貌，老屋的氛围则让人听见空间里的故事。她说，这两种感觉都很好，值得留在记忆里。</p>
