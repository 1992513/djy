<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>建筑艺术家   黑头织布鸟的巧手人生</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580009-790179-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一对黑头织布鸟在巢穴旁飞行与互动，展现繁殖期的热闹景象。（王国衍提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台中报导）在台湾中南部的农田边缘或河川旁的林木间，有时会看到一抹亮眼的金黄在枝叶间飞舞。那是黑头织布鸟，一位天生的建筑艺术家。雄鸟会独自飞来飞去，嘴衔着刚啄断的草叶，倒挂在细枝末梢，一点一点地把草叶编成悬吊的球状巢穴。筑巢前，它会先选好位置，并测试枝条的支撑力。这是它的“地基工程”，若枝条不够稳固，整个工程就不会开始。</p>
<p>从结构到美观的细心讲究黑头织布鸟的巢，不只是鸟儿的家，也是它的求偶礼物。雄鸟在编织时，会用草叶打出不同类型的结，随着经验增加，结法也会更多样、更牢固。熟练的工匠懂得选择韧性佳的草叶，减少在打结时断裂的可能。巢穴的入口永远朝下，方便它们进出，却能避免蛋滚落，也能防止蛇等天敌入侵。巢内常有巧妙的隔间设计，空间分配符合繁殖需求，简单实用却不失美感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580003-790173.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580003-790173-600x463.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="463" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580003" class="wp-caption-text">雄鸟倒挂于树枝下方，衔草续补编织中的鸟巢。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580006-790176.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580006-790176-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580006" class="wp-caption-text">黑头织布鸟双爪倒挂，细心整理鸟巢结构。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580002" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580002-790172.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580002-790172-600x447.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580002" class="wp-caption-text">黑头织布鸟展翅接近树上悬挂的编织巢，动作敏捷优雅。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>求偶的鸣唱与“监工”的母鸟当巢穴完成后，雄鸟会攀在巢口附近，不断发出响亮的鸣叫声，等待母鸟前来检视。这时的鸣唱，不只是歌声，更像是在展示自己的诚意与能力。母鸟在一旁细看每一个细节，如果不满意，雄鸟就得拆掉重来，直到母鸟点头为止。有时，一棵树上能看到多个未完成或被弃用的巢，那是一次次“验收不合格”的纪录。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580007-790177.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580007-790177-600x697.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="697" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580007" class="wp-caption-text">一只雄鸟在巢口与另一只鸟互动，画面充满生态动感。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580004-790174.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580004-790174-600x465.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="465" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580004" class="wp-caption-text">金黄色羽毛在阳光下闪耀的黑头织布鸟，飞向新建的巢穴。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580008-790178.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580008-790178-600x476.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="476" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580008" class="wp-caption-text">雄鸟展翅鸣叫，显示领域与筑巢的活力。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大自然的工程高手黑头织布鸟的筑巢行为，不只是生存本能，更是一种自然界的建筑艺术。从选址、测试、结构设计到室内隔间，每一步都充满巧思与目的性。对它们而言，这是一场结合了力学、美学与情感的工程。它们的勤奋与专注，让人不禁想起人类的建筑师。不同的是，它们不用蓝图，不需工具，全靠一双翅膀与一张小小的喙，就能完成一座既安全又美观的家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14580014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580014-790190.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580014-790190-600x664.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="664" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580014" class="wp-caption-text">从选址、测试、结构设计到室内隔间，每一步都充满巧思与目的性。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14580013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14580013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580013-790182.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14580013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14580013-790182-600x521.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="521" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14580013" class="wp-caption-text">展翅的黑头织布鸟高举头部，检视刚完成的巢穴入口。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>在枝头延续的故事如今，这些黄黑相间的小小身影，依旧在枝头来回忙碌。它们飞去河畔啄来新鲜的草叶，又飞回巢边，继续加固结构。偶尔，有另一只黄羽的母鸟飞来，停在不远处，专注地观察。不论工程是否马上获得认可，雄鸟都会再试一次。它们用一次次的编织，换来伴侣的信任与生命的延续。这样的耐心与坚持，正是黑头织布鸟最动人的地方。</p>
