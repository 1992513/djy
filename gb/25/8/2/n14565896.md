<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马祖云台山的风光 摄影家的居游纪事</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565897-785188-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>马祖云台山上的夕阳。（赖小芳提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬马祖报导）7月底连续两日阴雨终于停歇，傍晚时分，马祖的空气透着凉意，霞光从云缝中洒下。马祖自由摄影师赖小芳与同事在公车站不期而遇，一个眼神便决定同行前往芙蓉澳散步。这是马祖居游的第54日，也是属于她的生活与工作交会的片刻。她说：“旅行。工作。生活。～马祖列岛居游纪事54。”此刻正值淡菜肥美的季节，路过海岸，村民口中的“马祖美味”令人食指大动；然而真正让她举起相机的，仍是那片山与海交叠、暮色浸染的风景。镜头里，一艘艘货轮缓行于蓝灰色海面，海面微波如绸缎，远处风机沿着山脊排开，如一列静默的守望者。每一次取景，都是她与岛屿对话的方式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14565911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565911" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565911-785203.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14565911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565911-785203-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565911" class="wp-caption-text">赖小芳说：“旅行。工作。生活。～马祖列岛居游纪事第54天。”（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14565899" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565899" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565899-785190.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14565899" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565899-785190-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565899" class="wp-caption-text">海岛渐远入云烟，天海一线沉静悠远。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14565910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565910-785201.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14565910" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565910-785201-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565910" class="wp-caption-text">马祖淡菜。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>夕阳无限好 只是近黄昏当天的散步最后来到了南竿的云台山。这里是马祖的第一高峰，海拔248公尺，亦是台湾小百岳第98座。站在山顶的瞭望平台，极目远眺，岛屿的轮廓渐次铺展，夕阳则静静沉落于海天交界。她轻声念出李商隐的诗句：“向晚意不适，驱车登古原。夕阳无限好，只是近黄昏。”这诗，仿佛就是为马祖的傍晚而写。夕阳落下的刹那，石狮围栏在橙金色的霞光中隐约发亮。那些曾是军事设施的山头，现在成为旅人眺望海景的所在，而她镜头中凝结的，不只是风景，更是一段历史转身后的安静与和解。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14565907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565907" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565907-785198.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14565907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565907-785198-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565907" class="wp-caption-text">云台山高处望群岛，晨光微现。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14565904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565904" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565904-785195.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14565904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565904-785195-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565904" class="wp-caption-text">层层海岛如画，天边云雾间轻浮群屿。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从“烟台”到“云台”的时光变迁云台山的前身名为“烟台山”，清朝道光年间，为了防御海盗而在山顶设置烟台预警。1949年后，国军进驻马祖，云台山凭借其地势优势成为重要军事据点，周围设有碉堡、炮台与雷达设施，如今多已转为展示用途。赖小芳的镜头下，绿瓦建筑与迷彩墙面仍保留军事风貌，外墙上以浮雕描绘过往场景：士兵、炮阵、指挥员，都曾在此山守望着海峡对岸的消息。山顶建有“马祖云台山军情馆”，如今作为展示军事历史与观光用途，墙上的金字低调地诉说着过往，而站在平台的旅人，面对的则是无边海景与转变后的和平风貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14565902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565902" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565902-785193.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14565902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565902-785193-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565902" class="wp-caption-text">马祖军情馆外观，红白天线伫立高处。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14565898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565898-785189.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14565898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565898-785189-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565898" class="wp-caption-text">双船静行蓝海间，晨雾笼罩静谧如画。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14565900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14565900" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565900-785191.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14565900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14565900-785191-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14565900" class="wp-caption-text">云台山军情馆，战地记忆的绿瓦建筑。（赖小芳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>远方，仍是海日暮时分，海面渐深，风车剪影渐淡，岛屿如浮于雾中。从云台山望去，可见东引、西引等岛的层层轮廓，近岸一尊观音雕像隐身于林中，面海而立，仿佛守护着来来往往的船只与岛民的安宁。“那就去芙蓉澳走走吧！”赖小芳的语气轻快，却也道出旅居生活的自由节奏。在马祖的日子里，风景是一日三餐的佐料，历史则是脚下每一步的回音。她以相机记录，也以诗意的日常过活。“现在是淡菜肥美的时候，有经过可以来品尝属于马祖的美味。”她笑说，这里不仅风景美、空气新鲜，还有专属于海岛的味道等待发掘。</p>
