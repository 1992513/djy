<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>圣米歇尔山 潮汐之间的天空之城</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583937-791673-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>圣米歇尔山（Mont Saint-Michel）位于法国诺曼地海湾，远远望去，宛如漂浮在海天之间的一座城堡。（王德合提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月29日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾报导）圣米歇尔山（Mont Saint-Michel）位于法国诺曼地海湾，远远望去，宛如漂浮在海天之间的一座城堡。它实际上是一座独立的花岗岩岛，四周潮水涨退分明，涨潮时如孤城矗立于海面，退潮时又与大地相连。岛上最醒目的建筑，是历经数百年兴建与修缮的修道院，周围环绕着村庄与层层防御工事，形成独特的历史景观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14583931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14583931" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583931-791667.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14583931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583931-791667-600x892.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="669" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14583931" class="wp-caption-text">王德合和太太于圣米歇尔山前合影留念。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14583933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14583933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583933-791669.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14583933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583933-791669-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14583933" class="wp-caption-text">无论是沿着堤道步行，还是赤脚踏过退潮的沙洲，每一步都像是在追寻历史的足迹。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>千年朝圣的足迹　世界文化遗产的荣耀圣米歇尔山与罗马、圣雅各之路并列为天主教三大朝圣地。传说八世纪时，大天使圣米歇尔在梦中向当地主教显现，指示在此兴建圣堂。自此，这座小岛成为信仰与精神的象征。修道院建筑融合罗马式与哥德式风格，高耸的尖塔顶端立有镀金圣米歇尔雕像，守护着岛屿与旅人。1874年，圣米歇尔山被法国政府列为历史遗迹；1979年更被联合国教科文组织（UNESCO）登录为世界文化遗产。这里不仅是宗教圣地，也是中世纪军事与建筑技术的展现，厚重的石墙、狭窄的街巷与环形防御设计，见证了它曾在百年战争中屹立不摇的历史。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14583932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14583932" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583932-791668.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14583932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583932-791668-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="798" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14583932" class="wp-caption-text">1874年，圣米歇尔山被法国政府列为历史遗迹；1979年更被联合国教科文组织（UNESCO）登录为世界文化遗产。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14583935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14583935" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583935-791671.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14583935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583935-791671-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14583935" class="wp-caption-text">圣米歇尔山修道院尖塔直指蓝天。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>潮汐变幻的魅力　岛上的人文与艺术画家王德合在不同时间拍下了圣米歇尔山的多种面貌：有时被潮水环绕，仿佛海上孤城；有时退潮露出大片滩地，游人可徒步接近。岛上的高处俯瞰，可见长长的栈道蜿蜒通向陆地，潮光映照，与天际连成一线。这样的景象，也让它被誉为现实版的“天空之城”。走进修道院，阳光透过彩绘玻璃窗洒落在石雕与回廊之间，宁静而庄严。近年，岛上也举办现代艺术展览，例如悬挂在教堂中空间的大型月球装置，与千年石壁形成鲜明对比，让历史与当代在同一空间对话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14583928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14583928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583928-791664.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14583928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583928-791664-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14583928" class="wp-caption-text">远望圣米歇尔山，沿着堤道前行，海天一色映照孤城。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14583930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14583930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583930-791666.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14583930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583930-791666-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14583930" class="wp-caption-text">阳光洒落古老浮雕，细节清晰如新。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>朝圣与旅行的交会对许多旅人而言，圣米歇尔山不只是观光景点，更是一场心灵的旅程。无论是沿着堤道步行，还是赤脚踏过退潮的沙洲，每一步都像是在追寻历史的足迹。正如王德合镜头中的画面，这座“天空之城”静静矗立，见证着潮起潮落，也迎接着每一位来访的人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14583936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14583936" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583936-791672.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14583936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583936-791672-600x634.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="477" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14583936" class="wp-caption-text">赤脚走在退潮沙洲，远眺圣米歇尔山。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14583929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14583929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583929-791665.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14583929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14583929-791665-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14583929" class="wp-caption-text">退潮时的孤影，广阔滩地上，圣米歇尔山静立天际。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
