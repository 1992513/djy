<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国华TAITRA台湾食品展将于8月11日开幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14569965-DSC_0133-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾凤梨酥。（大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月08日讯】继国华台湾食品公司今年4月在多伦多SIAL加拿大食品展获得热烈回响，该公司与TAITRA合作，将台湾严格甄选的特色美食带到国华超市温哥华旗舰店。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570368" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570368-f02f66a848601ce0005ec12e4c47ff83.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570368-f02f66a848601ce0005ec12e4c47ff83-600x231.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="231"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570368" class="wp-caption-text">（国华台湾食品公司提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570370-1daa90681d9380bdf7cad5322d8b6dbd.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570370-1daa90681d9380bdf7cad5322d8b6dbd-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570370" class="wp-caption-text">2025年8月11日至11月15日，位于列治文的国华超市将举办TAITRA Taiwan Select食品展。（国华台湾食品公司提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次国华TAITRA Taiwan Select食品展将于8月11日开幕，展出商品皆为台湾严选品牌，除了展售为人熟知的手信坊凤梨酥外，还有甘心乐意柳丁汁、甘蔗果汁、台东县农会菊花、低硫金针、花莲县新秀地区农会腌渍酸菜、新竹县关西镇农会即溶仙草粉、台中市新社区极上干香菇、南投县农会幸福梅好蜜梅、紫苏梅、金门金合利钢刀、金门良金高粱酒糟酸白菜、弘昌碾米台湾20号牛奶香米、华刚茶业台湾庄园茶礼、民雄金桔果酱等精品，涵盖台湾农特产品产业的代表作与创新之选，件件展现台湾职人精神与食品品质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570371" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570371-88352fda44d0a8e4c12b8429c37db722.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570371-88352fda44d0a8e4c12b8429c37db722-600x600.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570371" class="wp-caption-text">（国华台湾食品公司提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8月11日下午1时，国华温哥华列治文旗舰店将举行国华TAITRA Taiwan Select食品展开幕剪彩仪式，届时将有精彩活动，现场有丰富多样的台湾特色美食供消费者选购。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570373-fae910973a396f6419a1872f61f6d423.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570373-fae910973a396f6419a1872f61f6d423-600x600.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570373" class="wp-caption-text">（国华台湾食品公司提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>地址：#120-4551 3号路，列治文。◇</p>
