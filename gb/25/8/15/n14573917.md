<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【看电影学英文】《Meru》(5)</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574724-1281-600x400.webp" />
<h6>《梅鲁峰》（干净世界提供，甄宇合成）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>今天作为【看电影 学英文】《Meru》系列的最后一期，除了词组和单词之外，还会聊到一些俚语。</p>
<h2>小试牛刀</h2>
<p>下面是影片《<ahref="https://www.ganjingworld.com/s/z8aEM0bE3v" rel="nofollow"><u>梅鲁峰</u></a>》中三句<span dir="ltr" lang="zh-TW">台词</span>，若是你，会怎么翻译？</p>
<p>1）I’ve really blown it for my wife and kids.<br />
“我真的会为了太太和孩子们吹走它”吗？</p>
<p>2）We’re gunning for the summit ridgeline.<br />
“我们正在射击峰顶山脊”吗？</p>
<p>3）Jimmy made the case for Renan to come along.<br />
“金国威为了让瑞楠同去而做了个箱子”吗?</p>
<p>如果你对上面几句的答案也不太有把握，那下面的内容可能会对你有所帮助。</p>
<h2>俚语和词组</h2>
<p><strong>1、Be gunning for something?</strong></p>
<p>Gun 除了枪的意思，还可以在俚语中作动词，有猛踩油门、加速的意思。</p>
<p>Be gunning for something 意思是努力取得或赢得某事物。</p>
<p>例句：</p>
<p>Jimmy: We&#8217;re gunning for the summit ridgeline and Conrad’s burrowing through this overhanging cornice.<br />
金国威：我们正努力朝着峰顶山脊攀登，而康拉德正在挖掘这个悬空的雪檐。</p>
<p>其中 cornice 一般指靠近天花板起装饰作用的顶角线条，也指在山崖边缘突出的、硬化的雪团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573949-1280-1.webp"><img decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573949-1280-1-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573949" class="wp-caption-text">康拉德正在挖掘雪檐，努力朝峰顶山脊攀登。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Be gunning for someone 意为经常指责某人，时常找某人的茬，不时挑衅。</p>
<p>She&#8217;s been gunning for me ever since I got the promotion she wanted.<br />
自从我得到了她想要的晋升，她就一直针对我。</p>
<p><strong>2、Blow it</strong></p>
<p>可用作感叹词，表达懊恼、生气之意，也可以是搞砸、错失机会的意思。</p>
<p>Be careful. Don&#8217;t blow it!<br />
当心点，别搞砸了。</p>
<p>Jon: He’s thinking, &#8220;man, I cannot die now, because I&#8217;ve really blown it for my wife and kids.&#8221;<br />
强：他在想：“天啊，我现在不能死，因为我真的已经让妻子和孩子们失望了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573950-1280-2.webp"><img decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573950-1280-2-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573950" class="wp-caption-text">康拉德攀登时想到不能让妻儿失望。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3、Get one’s ass kicked</strong></p>
<p>俚语，被修理，输得很惨。</p>
<p>Jon: Conrad first tried Meru in 2003 and got his ass kicked.<br />
强：康拉德在2003年首次尝试攀登梅鲁峰，但被挫败。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573951-1280-3.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573951-1280-3-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573951" class="wp-caption-text">康拉德首次尝试攀登鲨鱼鳍线路失败了。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Ass 的意思是驴子，蠢人，屁股，常用来指代人，但属于粗话，知其意即可。常见的词组有：</p>
<p>kick one&#8217;s ass 揍某人一顿，或比赛中轻易打败某人<br />
kick ass （两词间没有 one&#8217;s）了不起，很厉害<br />
kiss one&#8217;s ass 巴结某人<br />
a pain in the ass 让人头疼的人或事，讨厌鬼，不如 a pain in the neck 体面<br />
save one&#8217;s ass 帮了大忙，帮收拾烂摊子</p>
<p>当 ass 和某些形容词组合的时候，起强调作用，往往有负面含义，如：</p>
<p>hard-ass/ hard ass 硬汉、顽固的人、狠角色、难缠的人<br />
smart ass 自以为很聪明的人，自以为是的人<br />
lazy ass 懒鬼、懒虫</p>
<p><strong>4、Give it a go</strong></p>
<p>试试看，常常用来表示头一次尝试，话中还隐含着可能不太喜欢那么做，或可能无法顺利完成，但还是愿意冒险一试的意思。</p>
<p>Renan: In the end, Conrad and Jimmy left the decision up to me. Even though it’d only been 5 months since the accident, I decided I wanted to give it a go.<br />
瑞楠：最后，康拉德和金国威把决定权交给了我。虽然事故才过去五个月，我还是决定试试看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573953-1280-5.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573953-1280-5-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573953" class="wp-caption-text">瑞楠想跟团队一起回到梅鲁峰，甘愿冒险一试。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了战场上射击范围、火力范围的意思，还有因危险设备移动、落物或不安全行为而直接面临受伤风险的情况，也可以说?on the firing line。</p>
<p>Jimmy: Renan is in the line of fire, snow and ice falling 150 feet, just slamming Renan.<br />
金国威：瑞楠处在危险地带，雪和冰从150英尺高处坠落，猛烈撞击着他。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573956" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573956-1280-6.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573956" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573956-1280-6-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573956" class="wp-caption-text">瑞楠在危险地带，被坠落的冰雪猛烈撞击。（GJW+电影截图，甄宇合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6、Make the case</strong></p>
<p>Case 在中文中作为名词有多种意思，包括情况、案件、例子、个案、箱子、外壳等。</p>
<p>Make the case 是给出很好的理由来说服别人，提供充分理由。</p>
<p>Conrad: It was this moment when three of us got together, and Jimmy made the case for Renan to come along.<br />
康拉德：就在我们三个人聚在一起的时候，金国威提出让瑞楠同去的充分理由。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573957" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573957-1280-7.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573957" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573957-1280-7-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573957" class="wp-caption-text">三人聚在一起时，金国威提出让瑞楠同去的充分理由。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Case 在口语中很常见，如：</p>
<p>That will be the case.<br />
情况就是这样了。</p>
<p>Just get off my case!<br />
别再抓着我的把柄不放了。</p>
<p>Our new boss is such a hard case.<br />
我们的新主管很难搞。</p>
<p>I am on the case.<br />
我正在处理。</p>
<p>常见短语还有：</p>
<p>in that case 既然如此<br />
in any case 再说，相当于 also<br />
(just) in case 以防万一<br />
a case in point 证明<br />
as the case might be 前述任何一种情况下</p>
<h3>7、Om, shanti</h3>
<p>这个Shanti 不是山体，也不是三体，而是梵语，“Om, shanti”是佛教或印度教祈祷结尾时说的话，大意是祝愿安宁平和。</p>
<p>顺便说，《Om Shanti Om》是一部2007年的印度贺岁片，影片里有爱情、有歌舞、有玄幻、有打斗、还有惊悚，当年一举打破印度宝莱坞票房纪录，女主角的名字就叫 Shanti。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573958" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573958-1280-8.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573958" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573958-1280-8-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573958" class="wp-caption-text">康拉德竟然在攀登时冒出一句梵语。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>单词</h2>
<p><strong>1、Beta</strong></p>
<p>Beta (β) 是希腊语字母表的第二个字母，在攀登专业领域，beta 指的是关于如何攀登某条路线的资讯，所需的特定攀登技巧，以及如何应用这些技巧来克服路线中遇到的主要挑战。</p>
<p>Jimmy: We knew there were a number of teams who attempted Shark’s Fin after us. And then this great Slovenian climber called Silvo Caro called Conrad to ask for beta, ask for information on the route.<br />
金国威：我们知道之后有几支队伍尝试攀登鲨鱼鳍路线。后来这位很棒的斯洛文尼亚攀登者 Silvo Caro 打电话给康拉德，询问相关资讯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573959" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573959-1280-9.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573959-1280-9-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573959" class="wp-caption-text">斯洛文尼亚攀登者询问鲨鱼鳍路线的资讯（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在软体开发中，beta 版是指面向大众的公开测试版，alpha 版则是内部测试版；在金融领域，beta 或贝塔系数，是衡量投资相对于整体市场波动性的指标；在当代网络语境中，beta male/ beta?boy 指性格温和的平凡男性或男孩，主要是相对于许多方面的能力和表现都在众人之上的女性或女孩而言(alpha female/ alpha?girl)，当然也有alpha male 和 beta female。</p>
<p><strong>2、Bum?</strong></p>
<p>Bum 用作名词时，指游民、流浪汉、懒汉，笨蛋，热衷某种活动的人。</p>
<p>beach bum 海滩客，热衷海滩活动者<br />
stumble bum 糊涂的人或事<br />
on the bum 过流浪生活；依赖他人生活</p>
<p>Jimmy:? I heard about this kid who&#8217;s a super talented climber. He was living on the road, like most climbing bums, especially climbers that are coming into the scene except for he didn’t even have a car. He was literally dropped off in the desert.<br />
金国威：我听说过这孩子（瑞楠），是个极具天赋的攀登者。他跟大多数登山者一样，居无定所，甚至连车都没有。他就是搭顺风车进入沙漠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573960" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573960" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573960-1280-10.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573960" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573960-1280-10-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573960" class="wp-caption-text">瑞楠像大多数攀登者一样居无定所。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Bum 作形容词时，指残废的，无价值的，假的，不公平的，错误的，不愉快的。</p>
<p>a bum knee 一个不好的膝盖<br />
a bum check 一张空头支票<br />
a bum deal 一笔不利的交易<br />
a bum rap 误判，不公平的惩罚或责骂</p>
<p>Bum 作动词时，指乞讨，流浪，闲荡。</p>
<p>bum around 闲逛，无所事事，流浪<br />
bum out 使失望，使沮丧，使烦恼</p>
<p>此外，bum 还有屁股的意思，但属粗话，知其意即可。</p>
<p><strong>3、Vagabond</strong></p>
<p>没有固定住所的流浪者，浪迹天涯的人。</p>
<p>Conrad: Alex did a remarkable job of balancing the family. We are all sort of vagabonds.<br />
康拉德：亚历克斯在平衡家庭方面做得非常出色。我们在某种程度上都像是流浪者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573961" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573961-1280-11.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573961-1280-11-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573961" class="wp-caption-text">大多数攀登者都像流浪者。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>需要注意的是，vagrant 与 vagabond 都有流浪的意思，但 vagabond 是有意选择生活在传统社会结构之外的生活方式，带有更浪漫或中性的色彩；而 vagrant 通常指出于贫困和社会依赖状态的流浪汉或游民，带有更多负面含义，如可能从事非法活动等。</p>
<p><strong>4、Phenom</strong></p>
<p>杰出的人或事物，奇迹，尤指体育奇才。</p>
<p>Jon: Climbing is a weird craft, you know, passed down from generation to generation. The way you learn is you have a mentor. Conrad learned at the feet of Mugs Stump. Conrad was this young phenom, and Mugs was one of the greatest climbers in the world.<br />
强：攀登是一门奇特的技艺，你知道的，代代相传。学习的方式是找到一位导师。康拉德在马格斯?斯坦普的指导下学习。康拉德是年轻奇才，而马格斯则是世界上最伟大的攀登者之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573962" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573962-1280-12.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573962-1280-12-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573962" class="wp-caption-text">康拉德是年轻奇才，在马格斯指导下学习。<span dir="ltr" lang="zh-Hant">。</span>（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>5、Frayed</strong></p>
<p>Frayed 是形容词，本意是磨损的、脱线的，也指心情焦虑、沮丧、恼火。</p>
<p>Jimmy: We&#8217;re all just kind of frayed. Sixteen days up here, just giving it everything we have. It’s been really slow.<br />
金国威：我们都有点疲惫不堪了。花16天到了这个高度，倾尽所有。进展真的很慢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573963" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573963-1280-13.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573963-1280-13-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573963" class="wp-caption-text">攀登了16天之后，他们疲惫不堪、倾尽所有<span dir="ltr" lang="zh-Hant">。</span>（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6、Pitch</strong></p>
<p>在专业登山运动领域，<span dir="ltr" lang="zh-Hant">pitch 指的是在登山路线上，两个主保护点之间的距离（一个绳距不超过一个动力绳的绳长）。 Multi-pitch 就是多段攀登。</span></p>
<p>Jimmy: On the summit push, I think all of us, probably Conrad most, were dreading the upper pitches because he knew he was the only one that could do them.<br />
金国威：在冲顶过程中，我想我们所有人都害怕上面那段攀登，可能康拉德最甚，因为他知道自己是唯一胜任之人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573964-1280-14.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573964-1280-14-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573964" class="wp-caption-text">三人都害怕冲顶的那段攀登，康拉德最甚。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Pitch 这个单词还有很多意思，如：沥青、水准、程度、竭力争取、（屋顶的）坡度、投掷、搭（帐篷）等，在生活和工作中都很常见。</p>
<p>He speaks at a lower pitch.<br />
他讲话时声音较低。</p>
<p>If we all pitch in together, it shouldn&#8217;t take too long.<br />
如果我们齐心协力，那并不需要很长的时间。</p>
<p>She made a pitch for the job but she didn&#8217;t get it.<br />
她努力争取这份工作，但没有成功。</p>
<p>You gave a good pitch.<br />
你讲得很有说服力。</p>
<p>可能大家听说过 elevator pitch ，电梯游说或电梯简报，即在共乘电梯那麽短的时间内，把一个想法表达清楚，或引起对方兴趣，为后续见面做铺垫，电梯简报需要特别简短有力。</p>
<p><strong>7、Epiphany</strong></p>
<p>在基督宗教中每年1月6日是Epiphany，基督教称为主显节，东正教称为神显日和洗礼节。不特指宗教节日时，有降临、下凡、登场、显现，也有灵光乍现、顿悟之意。</p>
<p>Jon: But Conrad after that first time said, “huh, here’s the deal. I see this, this epiphany. You gotta do this big wall style and get your stuff up there.”<br />
强：康拉德第一次尝试失败后说，“哈，原来如此。我看到了这个启示。你得把装备背到上面去，进行大岩壁攀登。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14573965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14573965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573965-1280-15.webp"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-djy_600_400 wp-image-14573965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14573965-1280-15-600x400.webp" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14573965" class="wp-caption-text">康拉德从第一次攀登失败中得到启示。（GJW+电影截图）</figcaption></figure>
