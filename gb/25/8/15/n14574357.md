<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>疗愈园艺！居家种出“芝麻菜”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574371-P.29-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>播种到收成只需一个月。（苹果屋出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>在家自种香草及蔬菜，随手现采鲜摘，直上餐桌，无毒最安心！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【芝麻菜】</span></h2>
<p>别名／芸芥、火箭菜、火箭生菜<br />
科名／十字花科芝麻菜属<br />
原产地／地中海沿岸?亚洲西部</p>
<figure id="attachment_14574375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574375" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574375-P.28-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14574375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574375-P.28-1-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574375" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">多重功效</span></h3>
<p>因别名“火箭”而被广泛认识，也是意大利料理必备香草。拥有丰富的钙、维他命C与铁质。</p>
<p>独特的辣味是“异硫氰酸烯丙酯”，具有杀菌与提升新陈代谢的效果。“异硫氰酸烯丙酯”的辣味成分多存在于萝卜、芜菁、小松菜等十字花科青菜，因有抗氧化作用对身体抗老也有效果。也能提高免疫机能，预防癌症发生。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">常见料理用途</span></h3>
<p><strong>?叶子：</strong>它具有芝麻香气，洒在披萨或意大利面上很受欢迎；也可拌入无花果或柿子等作成水果沙拉。</p>
<p>开花的叶子就会变硬，只吃叶子的时候，就从花茎摘取即可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14574374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574374" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574374-P.28-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14574374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574374-P.28-3-450x333.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574374" class="wp-caption-text">芝麻叶洒在烤好的披萨上，透过食物的余温，能缓和其呛辣味。<br />（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>?食用花卉：</strong>芝麻叶的小白花也与叶子有同样的风味，除了沙拉外，也可加在天妇罗或凉拌等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14574372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574372" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574372-P.28-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14574372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574372-P.28-4-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574372" class="wp-caption-text">奶油色的花也可食用。加入沙拉更能增添风味。（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>?适合搭配重口味食材：</strong>起司、培根、鳀鱼、肝脏等味道较重的食材，一同搭配更能增添风味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【火箭生菜】</span></h2>
<p>也被称为野生芝麻菜的野生种。香味与辣味较重的关系，配合其他食材一起享用，更美味。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">栽培须知</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14574371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574371" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574371-P.29-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14574371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574371-P.29-1-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574371" class="wp-caption-text">播种到收成只需一个月。（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>?种植：</strong>将种子散播并盖上一层薄土，直到发芽前，须注意不要太干燥，并使用喷雾浇水。</p>
<p><strong>?收成：</strong></p>
<p>叶子变重时，留下少数的幼苗采收。<br />
生长较快者，约一个月即能采收食用。<br />
留下株苗中心2～3片叶子，先从外侧的叶子采收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14574368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574368" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574368-P.29-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14574368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574368-P.29-4-450x396.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574368" class="wp-caption-text">从外侧的叶子开始采收，或是整株采收。（苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5月中旬容易有害虫，建议在上旬采收。</p>
<p><strong>?成长：</strong></p>
<p>半日照生长的叶子，较柔软容易食用。<br />
到了初夏花开时叶子会变硬。<br />
自然落下的种子也能发芽，直到花开。</p>
