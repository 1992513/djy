<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>旺市Assembly Park波斯美食节点亮您的夏日</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571683-1-600x400.jpg" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月12日讯】作为本地居民和游客夏日必访的亮点，波斯美食节将在充满活力的旺市Assembly Park举行，邀请大家沉浸于波斯文化的视觉、听觉和味觉盛宴。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-330767" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/SKY05591-1-1-600x618-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="618" /></p>
<p>多伦多的多元文化是其强大魅力所在，而波斯美食节正是这一精神的完美体现。你将在这里体验到：由专业大厨精心制作的地道波斯美食。无论是炭火烧烤的香嫩烤肉，还是芳香四溢的藏红花米饭，亦或是细腻的玫瑰水甜点，美食爱好者们都能在这里尽情享受。</p>
<p><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" loading="lazy" data2-src="https://www.instagram.com/reel/DNOTdjwRPUn/embed/captioned/?cr=1&amp;v=14&amp;wp=864&amp;rd=https%3A%2F%2Fntdca.com&amp;rp=%2Fcn%2F%25e6%25b3%25a2%25e6%2596%25af%25e7%25be%258e%25e9%25a3%259f%25e7%25af%2580-%25e9%25bb%259e%25e4%25ba%25ae%25e4%25bd%25a0%25e7%259a%2584%25e5%25a4%258f%25e6%2597%25a5%2F#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A3567.7999999970198%2C%22ls%22%3A1655.8999999985099%2C%22le%22%3A3270.599999997765%7D" b="1347" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>节日不仅仅是美食的盛会。来宾还可欣赏现场音乐表演，感受充满活力的传统与现代舞蹈秀，观赏艺术展览，并参与富有互动性的文化活动，充分体验波斯社区的温情与热忱。节日设有适合各年龄层的家庭活动和手工艺摊位，无论是首次来访还是老朋友，都能找到属于自己的乐趣。</p>
<p><a id="instagram-embed-1" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" loading="lazy" data2-src="https://www.instagram.com/reel/DNHDtf4St9r/embed/captioned/?cr=1&amp;v=14&amp;wp=864&amp;rd=https%3A%2F%2Fntdca.com&amp;rp=%2Fcn%2F%25e6%25b3%25a2%25e6%2596%25af%25e7%25be%258e%25e9%25a3%259f%25e7%25af%2580-%25e9%25bb%259e%25e4%25ba%25ae%25e4%25bd%25a0%25e7%259a%2584%25e5%25a4%258f%25e6%2597%25a5%2F#%7B%22ci%22%3A1%2C%22os%22%3A3590.599999997765%2C%22ls%22%3A1655.8999999985099%2C%22le%22%3A3270.599999997765%7D" b="1383" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-1" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></p>
<p>适合全家的友好氛围和活动！</p>
<p>欢迎所有人的热情社区氛围——无论你是否波斯裔，都能在这里欢聚一堂。</p>
