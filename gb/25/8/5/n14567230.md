<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>萨默维尔地铁站新通道剪彩 方便居民商户</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567215-DSC09683-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>8月4日上午，麻州萨默维尔市（Somerville）的East Somerville地铁站新行人通道剪彩，多位政要嘉宾到场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年08月05日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨萨默维尔市报导）麻州萨默维尔市（Somerville）的“East Somerville”绿线地铁站，已于今年5月开放了新修建的行人通道，方便不同方向的乘客进出车站，而且还加建了“Bluebikes”共享自行车站。</p>
<p>8月4日上午，麻州及萨默维尔市政要来到这个地铁站，为新通道剪彩，并对麻州捷运署（MBTA）提出了新期待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567206" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567206" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567206-DSC09552.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567206" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567206-DSC09552-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567206" class="wp-caption-text">麻州副州长德里斯科（Kim Driscoll，左）、萨默维尔市长巴兰坦（Katjana Ballantyne）查看地铁站的新行人通道。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567207" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567207" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567207-DSC09563.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567207-DSC09563-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567207" class="wp-caption-text">麻州副州长德里斯科（Kim Driscoll，左）、萨默维尔市长巴兰坦（Katjana Ballantyne）查看地铁站的新行人通道。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“East Somerville”绿线地铁站于2022年投入使用，最初只在McGrath Highway方向设有出入口。而车站另一侧Inner Belt Road社区的乘客，则需要绕行才能进入地铁站。Inner Belt Road社区内有“Form Energy”、“Triumvirate Environmental”等大型公司的办公楼。</p>
<p>于是，萨默维尔市政府、MBTA、当地商家及建筑商合作，在绿线地铁站Inner Belt Road一侧修建了新通道，并且引入Bluebikes共享自行车站。</p>
<p>此外，新通道还连通了Inner Belt Road社区和2023年修缮完工的萨默维尔社区自行车道（Somerville Community Path），让居民更安全地享有骑行和步行体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567208" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567208-DSC09584.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567208-DSC09584-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567208" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾们参与剪彩典礼。左起：MBTA总经理伍华伟（Phillip Eng）、麻州副州长德里斯科（Kim Driscoll）、萨默维尔市长巴兰坦（Katjana Ballantyne）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567829" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567829-IMG_8823.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567829-IMG_8823-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567829" class="wp-caption-text">萨默维尔市地铁站旁的自行车道。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Form Energy公司总裁威利（Ted Wiley）带着多名员工参加了剪彩典礼。他特别强调，包括他在内的许多员工，都会时而骑自行车上下班；他还买了Bluebikes年度会员。</p>
<p>威利说，早在一年多之前，他还没有这个习惯，因为当时只能在繁忙的公路上骑自行车，让他觉得不安全。后来车站旁边修建了新自行车道，这让他非常高兴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567211-DSC09644.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567211-DSC09644-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567211" class="wp-caption-text">Form Energy公司总裁威利（Ted Wiley）称赞新行人通道。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567212-DSC09650.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567212-DSC09650-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567212" class="wp-caption-text">Form Energy公司位于地铁站及新通道附近，多名员工参加剪彩典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567214" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567214-DSC09675.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567214-DSC09675-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567214" class="wp-caption-text">麻州众议员康诺利（Mike Connolly）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>麻州副州长德里斯科（Kim Driscoll）、萨默维尔市长巴兰坦（Katjana Ballantyne）、MBTA总经理伍华伟（Phillip Eng），以及代表东萨默维尔地区的麻州众议员康诺利（Mike Connolly），都出席了剪彩典礼。</p>
<p>巴兰坦笑言，她留意到MBTA通勤火车轨道，与新行人通道只隔了一道栅栏。她对伍华伟说，希望有朝一日通勤火车会在这里设置站点。◇</p>
