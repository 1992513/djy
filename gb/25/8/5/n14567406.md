<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>带妈妈一起家族旅行─金山篇</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>上次为了庆祝母亲节，家人带着妈妈一起去宜兰旅行的感觉很幸福。这次父亲节来临之际，老弟突然问我和哥哥有没有空，想带妈一起去出去走走。想到我们各自婚嫁后，除了逢年过节，相聚在一起的时间不多，老姐我当然尽量配合喽！大嫂却在临行前一晚还犹豫不决，老弟说不用考虑那么多，半天就回来了！</p>
<p>这次就近旅行，大家可以睡饱再出发，途经外木山滨海区和沙滩，今年的夏天高温频发，令人热到有些冲动想去海边游泳….，不过这裹离核电厂和发电厂不远，想玩水还是去东北角好了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567437-20101889-DF5E-4BB2-B041-623AC5F0AE2D.jpg"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567437-20101889-DF5E-4BB2-B041-623AC5F0AE2D-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567437" class="wp-caption-text">大家在狮头山公园会合。狮头山公园步道入口旁有免费的停车场。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狮头山公园步道入口旁有免费的停车场，大家在狮头山公园会合，看我弟排的旅游行程，第一站就是爬狮头山，心想：老妈懒得动，连去大卖场都走不动，怎会排爬山的行程？老人家爬得上去吗？</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567436-7116B76A-C462-4E44-912F-7811DB4CF801.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14567436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567436-7116B76A-C462-4E44-912F-7811DB4CF801-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567436" class="wp-caption-text">狮头山公园的步道铺上柏油，平坦的路走起来难度不高。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567466" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567466-S__57565208_0.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567466-S__57565208_0-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567466" class="wp-caption-text">狮头山的军事历史。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原来金山的狮头山公园以前是北海岸的军事基地，园区的一些步道铺上柏油，平坦的路走起来难度不高，可供休息的凉亭也不少，不少人扶老携幼，带全家人一起来散步，路上遇到头发花白的90多岁老人还鼓励老妈要常常出来走走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567447" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567447-S__57565188.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567447-S__57565188-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567447" class="wp-caption-text">“烛台双屿”是因为板块运动，被推挤出海面的沉积岩，历经风吹雨打和海浪冲刷成像是从海中冒出了两个烛台，地质景观很特殊。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567465" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567465-S__57565207_0.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567465-S__57565207_0-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567465" class="wp-caption-text">“烛台双屿”是因为板块运动，被推挤出海面的沉积岩，历经风吹雨打和海浪冲刷成像是从海中冒出了两个烛台，地质景观很特殊。（图／陈欣玉）</figcaption></figure>
<p>家人们一起在“烛台双屿”景点前合影留念，这“烛台双屿”是因为板块运动，被推挤出海面的沉积岩，历经风吹雨打和海浪冲刷成像是从海中冒出了两个烛台，地质景观很特殊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567448" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567448-S__57565189.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14567448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567448-S__57565189-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567448" class="wp-caption-text">八角亭吃早餐。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走到八角亭，晨起的运动让人很快就感到饿了。拿起大嫂在金山工作的亲戚一早买来要排队才买得到的“金山王肉包”，一口气吃了葱肉包和酸菜包，包子裹的葱还鲜绿着，好吃得令大伙想去金山老街逛时，要多买一点带回家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567449-S__57565190.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567449-S__57565190-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567449" class="wp-caption-text">午餐来到金山老街必吃的“金山庙口金包里鸭肉”。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>趁着中午12点前，我们来到了许多游客来到金山老街必吃的“金山庙口金包里鸭肉”，有点懊悔肚子被刚吃的包子填饱；没想到美食当前，大家还是吃到盘底朝天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567450-S__57565191.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567450-S__57565191-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567450" class="wp-caption-text">庙口的摊位像是简易的办桌菜，一盘盘地分装，摆在台面上，让游客自己挑想吃的各式菜肴。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这家店的点餐流程很特别：庙口的现煮的摊位像是简易办桌菜，一盘盘地分装摆在台面上，让游客自己挑想吃的各式菜：炸柳叶鱼、炒虾子、卤桂竹笋??，游客穿梭在老街，端着盘裹的菜走一小段路，到不远处的其它店面找位子坐下，和期待着美食上桌的亲友分享。</p>
<p>老弟第一次跟着排队的游客端了菜以后，慌张地问：“然后咧？！”因为我们这些新来的游客，不知道用餐的座位远在其它栋房子裹。而店家要等客人吃完才会前来餐桌旁算钱结账。想来全台应该很少会有这么特别的用餐方式，我不禁为店家担心：难道不怕客人把菜端走，没结账就跑掉了吗？或许是基于互相信任，才能衍生出这种特有的台湾文化吧？挺有趣的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567451-S__57565192.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567451-S__57565192-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567451" class="wp-caption-text">金山老街又名金包里老街，是目前北海岸仅存的清代老街，已有三百多年的历史。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吃完午餐，眼看天空黑压压的快下午后雷阵雨，我们快步穿越老街，买了手工蛋卷和金山有名的地瓜甜点便打道回府。如果是冬天的话，我还会自己一人开车来看海，独享一个人可以泡的个人汤屋（北投的温泉屋因安全考量，多要求要两人才能泡个人汤屋），享受金山特有的海底温泉，来趟不累的小旅行。</p>
<p>回家后，老哥Line来谢谢弟弟找的狮头山这个走起来轻松，风景又漂亮的景点，说下次有机会换他找个景点约我们一起去玩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567467" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567467-S__57565209_0.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14567467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567467-S__57565209_0-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567467" class="wp-caption-text">狮头山公园常见的动物、植栽和昆虫。（图／陈欣玉提供）</figcaption></figure>
