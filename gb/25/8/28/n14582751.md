<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>秋光映照月牙湾 渔光岛甜根子草季节限定美景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582752-791149-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>前面的乌云，乍看之下宛如一座小岛。（郑建南提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）位于台南安平帆船俱乐部月牙湾训练中心的沙洲上，每到秋季，大片甜根子草随风摇曳。摄影家郑建南说，这片草原与夕阳、海景交织时，会出现短暂却动人的色泽。“逆光下，甜根子草呈现金黄色泽，在夕阳光反烧的映照下，就像一幅现成的画。”他形容，秋天的微风一拂过，草穗随风起伏，像海浪一波波推来，是属于这个季节的限定风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14582753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582753" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582753-791150.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582753-791150.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582753" class="wp-caption-text">逆光下甜根子草呈现金黄色泽。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14582758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582758" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582758-791155.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582758-791155.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582758" class="wp-caption-text">晚霞映照，白色草浪如雪铺展。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>沙洲上的稀客</strong></h2>
<p>甜根子草多半生长在河床两岸，能在渔光岛的沙洲上成片绽放，并不多见。郑建南说，这片沙洲背后是防波堤与宽阔的海平面，潮水声与风声交错，是他喜欢在傍晚前来的原因。“配合夕阳、海景、花草，就构成了一幅美丽图画。”他特别强调，这片景色不像观光景点那般喧闹，更多的是安静的呼吸与自然的节奏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14582757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582757" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582757-791154.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582757-791154.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582757" class="wp-caption-text">隐约可见前方的堤坊上的海平面。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14582760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582760" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582760-791157.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582760-791157.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582760" class="wp-caption-text">霞光映亮，甜根子草泛金光。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>枯木映衬的秋意走进月牙湾，除了洁白或金黄的草浪，还能看到散落在草丛间的枯木。郑建南笑说，“枯木加上甜根子草，更显秋意。”在他的镜头里，枯木像是守护者，静静站在海风与草浪之间，让画面多了一层时间的感觉。对他而言，这样的搭配更能呈现季节的深度与情感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14582755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582755-791152.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582755-791152.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582755" class="wp-caption-text">枯木加上甜根子草更显秋意。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14582759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582759" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582759-791156.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582759-791156.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582759" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳余晖下，草丛间透着静谧。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>夕阳西下的瞬间黄昏时分，太阳逐渐倾斜，天边染上橘红色。郑建南喜欢捕捉光影变化的那几分钟，“在夕阳西下光线照射之下，甜根子草呈现金黄的光芒，煞是美丽。”远方的堤防和海平面在余晖里变得柔和，偶尔还能看到渔船的剪影慢慢驶过，让整个画面多了故事感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14582762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582762" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582762-791159.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582762-791159.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582762" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳穿过枝影，映亮金色草穗。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14582754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582754" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582754-791151.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582754-791151.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582754" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳光反烧。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>海边的日常与季节感</strong></h2>
<p>虽然甜根子草是此地的主角，但在月牙湾，也能看见不同层次的生活景象。有人在防波堤上散步，有人在岸边准备渔具，也有人坐着静静看海。郑建南说，拍摄这片景色时，他常被眼前的安静与背后的生活气息所吸引，“这里的景，不是只有风景照的美，还有生活感。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14582756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582756" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582756-791153.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582756-791153.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582756" class="wp-caption-text">渔光岛月牙湾。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14582761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582761" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582761-791158.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582761-791158.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582761" class="wp-caption-text">枝影与草穗交织黄昏剪影。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>留住限定的时光</strong></h2>
<p>甜根子草花期短暂，海边风势又强，稍晚一点，草穗就会因风雨或潮水而改变姿态。郑建南提醒，有兴趣的朋友最好趁着秋日午后到访，既能感受金色草浪，也能在退潮时走近海边看看。他说，拍摄这片甜根子草，最迷人的地方不只是画面，而是当下那种伴着海风、夕阳、与自然呼吸的感觉。“在这样的景里，你会不自觉放慢脚步，甚至什么都不做，只是看着它随风摇动。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14582763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14582763" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582763-791160.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14582763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14582763-791160.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14582763" class="wp-caption-text">微光映照，甜根子草静静摇曳。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
