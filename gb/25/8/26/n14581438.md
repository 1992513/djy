<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【首尔自由行】第五天 回台湾前不忘享受美食</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上集：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/8/26/n14581409.md#1">【首尔自由行】第四天：羊驼世界小确幸行程</a></p>
<p>昨天的一日行程回来晚了，没吃到安东炖鸡，今天要回台湾了，要趁回台湾之前补上。退房后，寄放行李，算准了安东炖鸡开店的时间，再次满足了口腹之欲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14581446" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14581446" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581446-5-days-1.jpg"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14581446" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581446-5-days-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14581446" class="wp-caption-text">我们的午餐“安东炖鸡”。（图／Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们三个人带了三大两小的行李箱，在整理行李时，我就有点担心行李会过重。因为这次帮亲朋好友代购了不少东西。吃饱回到饭店，就请柜台人员帮我们叫车，要搭车去首尔车站。因为我们的行李箱很重，再加上韩国地铁大多是楼梯，实在是扛不动。坐在饭店大厅等车时，赫然发现门口有一台行李秤重机，立马推着行李来秤重，还好全部的行李都过关。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14581445" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14581445" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581445-5-days-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14581445" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581445-5-days-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14581445" class="wp-caption-text">饭店门口有一台行李秤重机。（图／Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14581444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14581444" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581444-5-days-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14581444" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581444-5-days-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14581444" class="wp-caption-text">行李秤重机，还好行李没超重。（图／Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到首尔车站，我们要搭机场快线（AREX）去仁川机场。我们已事先在网路买好票，但需划位才能搭乘。找到橘色划位机台，接连好几班车都没有空位，所以确定搭不了AREX了，而且也没有办法退票，只能选择其他方式到仁川机场。这真的是在考验我们的应变能力，我们曾考虑扛着行李重新叫计程车，但那会耽误不少时间，万一又塞车，可能会耽误搭机的时间。最后，我们选择搭普通车到仁川机场，普通车就在机场快线（AREX）旁边月台搭车，班次多，虽然每站都会停，但时间能掌控。普通车上也有好多人都是带着行李搭车的，我们还很幸运有位子坐，很多人还都是站着的。我算了一下，普通车只比机场快线多坐了35分钟，我们到达机场的时间还很充裕，还好我们有提早出发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14581443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14581443" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581443-5-days-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14581443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581443-5-days-4-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14581443" class="wp-caption-text">搭普通车去仁川机场。（图／Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到达仁川机场，刚开始有点看不懂要在哪里办理登机，后来明白了，原来现在都要旅客在机台操作，自助办理登机，以及自助托运行李，还好旁边会有服务人员协助。托运完行李，找到退税机台，要先申报退税。我们也不是很清楚哪些东西可以退税，哪些东西已经退过税了，还有哪些东西是不能退税的，反正有条码的就刷，机台就会告诉你。出境通关后再次找到退税机台，在这里可以领取退税现金。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14581442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14581442" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581442-5-days-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14581442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581442-5-days-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14581442" class="wp-caption-text">自助办理登机的机台。（图／Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14581441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14581441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581441-5-days-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14581441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581441-5-days-6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14581441" class="wp-caption-text">自助托运行李。（图／Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们回程搭的是大韩航空，也是大飞机，机舱宽敞，座椅舒适，座位前方有个小萤幕，可以随时掌握飞机的动态以及看影片。机上有附餐点，我觉得还蛮好吃的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14581440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14581440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581440-5-days-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14581440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581440-5-days-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14581440" class="wp-caption-text">机上餐。（图／Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14581439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14581439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581439-5-days-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14581439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14581439-5-days-8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14581439" class="wp-caption-text">机上餐。（图／Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
