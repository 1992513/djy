<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠双树酒店承办治安会议 获警民褒奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568729-DSC09780-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在8月5日的波士顿华埠“灭罪安全夜”活动中，社区代表们向华埠双树酒店颁发“社区服务奖”。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年08月07日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）在8月5日的波士顿华埠“灭罪安全夜”（National Night Out）活动中，双树酒店（Double Tree Hotel）获得褒奖，原因是他们长期承办每月华埠治安会议。</p>
<p>当天在华埠大同村的活动现场，来自波士顿市政府、警察局及民间团体的嘉宾，共同为双树酒店代表颁发“社区服务奖”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14568725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568725" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568725-DSC09746.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568725-DSC09746-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568725" class="wp-caption-text">各界嘉宾及华埠治安巡逻队参加波士顿“灭罪安全夜”活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14568723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568723-DSC09735.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568723-DSC09735-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568723" class="wp-caption-text">阳光幼儿园的孩子们带来舞蹈表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14568724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568724-DSC09741.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568724-DSC09741-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568724" class="wp-caption-text">阳光幼儿园的孩子们带来舞蹈表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>华埠灭罪安全夜活动主持人及华埠治安会议共同主席曾雪清说，华埠地区的双树酒店自2000年开业以来，一直支持华埠社区公共安全，持续免费为华埠治安会议提供场地和茶点。</p>
<p>曾雪清特别称赞：“我确信每个人都尝试过他们著名的巧克力饼干，他们的‘双树饼干’。这是我们每月会议的奠基石之一，可能是吸引人们参加的原因。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14568730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568730" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568730-DSC09802.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568730-DSC09802-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568730" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿警察参与华埠“灭罪安全夜”活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14568719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568719-DSC09710.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568719-DSC09710-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568719" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿警察向人们发放冰沙饮品。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>华埠治安会议通常在每个月的第一个周三举办，邀请警察向社区代表报告治安情况，也讨论社区商家和活动对治安的影响。</p>
<p>双树酒店市场营销主任阿尔贝蒂（David W. Alberti）代表公司领奖，并强调，华埠分店有80%的员工都是华埠居民，酒店一直坚持支持本地社区的宗旨。</p>
