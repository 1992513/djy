<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>妇女新运会庆祝85周年 褒奖梅伍银宽</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568004-DSC09498-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>8月2日，纽英崙妇女新运会在华埠帝苑酒楼庆祝85周年。职员们上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年08月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）由蒋宋美龄在波士顿创办，纽英崙妇女新运会迎来85周年。8月2日晚上，近300来宾在华埠帝苑酒楼欢宴庆祝，并褒奖做出卓越贡献的成员梅伍银宽。</p>
<p>晚宴气氛非常热闹，妇女新运会成员们大多穿着红色的喜庆衣服赴宴，各界来宾也都喜气洋洋。来自驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处、波士顿市议会、麻州政府等官方机构的代表，各自向妇女新运会表达赞誉。华埠侨社团体，如中华公所、国民党、至孝笃亲公所等等，也都到场共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14568003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568003-DSC09493.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568003-DSC09493-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568003" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙妇女新运会在华埠帝苑酒楼庆祝85周年，近300来宾参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>二战时期成立 服务社区至今</strong></h3>
<p>这场晚宴由张潆今、林黎辉主持。妇女新运会会长李卢莲馨、张青梅首先上台致词，欢迎来宾。</p>
<p>两位会长谈到，妇女新运会成立于1940年抗战时期，初期成员们募捐救国，为抗日战争做出贡献。经过85年的历史，如今的协会成员们希望发扬关爱社区的宗旨，将继续为居民提供各种健康讲座、营养保健活动。两位会长也感谢来宾，尤其赞助商们的鼎力支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567996" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567996-DSC09444.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567996-DSC09444-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567996" class="wp-caption-text">张潆今（左）、林黎辉担任主持人。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567999-DSC09469.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567999-DSC09469-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567999" class="wp-caption-text">颁赠市议会褒奖。左起：会长李卢莲馨、市议员Ed Flynn、会长张青梅。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567995-DSC09433.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567995-DSC09433-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567995" class="wp-caption-text">侨社团体参与晚宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妇女新运会特别制作了85周年纪念册，其中包括多份历史文件，例如蒋宋美龄于中华民国30年（1941年）发布的妇女新运会成立证明书，蒋中正和蒋宋美龄的照片和题字，以及该协会早期成员合影等等。</p>
<p>纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉向妇女新运会表达祝贺与感谢。驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任高家富，也都上台向该协会道贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14567997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567997-DSC09457.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567997-DSC09457-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567997" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉表达祝贺。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14567998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14567998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567998-DSC09462.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14567998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14567998-DSC09462-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14567998" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏感谢妇女新运会的服务。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“85年是一段很长的旅程，是服务和共情的传承。”廖朝宏说。他特别缅怀该协会元老，今年去世的李伍绮莲，称赞她热心服务，是社区楷模。廖朝宏特别谈到，李伍绮莲虽有90岁高龄，但仍然每年参加“双十国庆”游行，为此要代表台湾政府向她表示感谢。</p>
<p>波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）和麻州审计长办公室代表奇利亚克（Matt Chilliak）也都参加了晚宴，并分别向妇女新运会颁赠波士顿及麻州政府的褒奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14568000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568000" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568000-DSC09475.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568000-DSC09475-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568000" class="wp-caption-text">麻州审计长办公室代表Matt Chilliak（中）向两位会长颁赠褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>服务耆英会和妇女会 梅伍银宽获奖</strong></h3>
<p>当晚，两位会长李卢莲馨、张青梅特别向该协会元老梅伍银宽颁赠“卓越领导奖”，感谢她在妇女新运会和中华耆英会50多年的服务。</p>
<p>梅伍银宽在波士顿侨社可谓颇有名声。据两位主持人介绍，她从1973年开始担任中华耆英会行政主任，不断扩大其服务规模，为数千亚裔长者提供食物、医疗服务和健康讲座。</p>
<p>梅伍银宽主导将康乐楼改造成长者住房，还参与创办中华颐养院，“样样都亲力亲为，样样都想着长者怎么能过得更好”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14568001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568001-DSC09486.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568001-DSC09486-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568001" class="wp-caption-text">两位会长李卢莲馨、张青梅向资深成员梅伍银宽（中）颁发褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>历年来，梅伍银宽曾担任塔夫茨医疗中心（Tufts Medical Center）董事、麻州州长耆英事务委员会成员、华埠社区议会共同主席、妇女新运会主席等等。2020年，她获得塔夫茨大学（Tufts University）颁发的荣誉博士学位。</p>
<p>梅伍银宽上台领奖时，深情感谢主办方给予她这份荣誉，并表示，她很自豪能在社区内参与服务。她也向妇女新运会表达了祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14568009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568009-DSC09517.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568009-DSC09517-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568009" class="wp-caption-text">两位会长李卢莲馨、张青梅向来宾们敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14568002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568002" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568002-DSC09489.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568002-DSC09489-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568002" class="wp-caption-text">茉莉花舞蹈团的小朋友们表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>载歌载舞 欢乐享宴</strong></h3>
<p>致词及颁奖之后，来宾们享用美食，同时欣赏文艺表演。茉莉花舞蹈团的小朋友们首先演绎4支舞蹈。接下来，多位来宾上台献唱歌曲，侨教中心主任高家富也唱了一首《我只在乎你》，收获热情喝采。两位会长李卢莲馨、张青梅带头进入舞池跳舞，亦带动欢乐气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14568006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568006-DSC09515.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568006-DSC09515-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568006" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富献唱歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14568005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14568005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568005-DSC09511.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14568005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14568005-DSC09511-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14568005" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们伴随歌声跳舞，会长亲自示范。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场还有抽奖环节，主办方向幸运来宾送出帝苑酒楼、明记鲜鸡等商家的礼券，更有50美元和100美元红包大奖。最后多位来宾合唱《朋友》，为85周年庆典划上句号。◇</p>
