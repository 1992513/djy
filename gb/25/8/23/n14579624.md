<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东里铁马驿站 旧站月台上的初秋日常</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579640-790084-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>像是把夏末的云块绑住，让旅人可以就近打量。（郑建南提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月23日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬花莲报导）花莲县富里乡的东里铁马驿站，前身是东里车站的旧站体。摄影家郑建南说：“前身是东里车站于2007年因铁路改线而废止，现作为当地文化象征而保留的历史痕迹。”官方资料也记载，东里车站因改线于2007年迁移，旧站月台与站房保留下来，往后规划为单车驿站与散步空间。 驿站也是玉富自行车道北上的起点之一，骑士从这里望向阡陌、秀姑峦溪与远山，是花东纵谷最亲切的入口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579635" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579635-790074.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14579635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579635-790074-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579635" class="wp-caption-text">“时光列车”作品介绍牌立于月台边，串连艺术与田园景色。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14579627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579627" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579627-790066.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579627-790066-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579627" class="wp-caption-text">铁轨延伸至远方，串起过往与今日的时光记忆。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>月台上的新风景走到旧月台，几节以竹编造形的《时光列车》停妥，圆圆的车窗、像瓶口的车头，和蓝天白云一起入镜。这件新作是今年“纵谷大地艺术季”的作品之一，让历史月台多了当代的对话。月台外侧的草地上，三座“云”立在黑色梯子上，像是把夏末的云块绑住，让旅人可以就近打量。这组作品为〈飘飘云朵〉，为艺术家赖冠仲创作，提醒游客别攀爬以策安全。 去年的报导还把它比喻成“云端的天梯”，说在这里能想像“攀梯入云”。装置放在东里的田中央，搭配花东纵谷晴朗的天空，成了到访者自然的打卡站。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579639-790078.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579639-790078-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579639" class="wp-caption-text">东里铁马驿站全景，见证铁道与单车旅人的交会。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14579630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579630-790069.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579630-790069-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579630" class="wp-caption-text">层层白云造景与远山遥望，形成梦幻的视觉景深。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14579625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579625-790064.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579625-790064-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579625" class="wp-caption-text">竹编造型装置排列于旧月台上，与远山景色交织出独特风景。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>初秋的光八月下旬的日头正直，云层薄，山线清楚。郑建南把镜头放低，让旧铁轨引出视线，远处是稻田的反光与山棱的阴影；换个角度，银色兔子坐在咖啡窗前，像在等车，也像在等人。室内墙上有以火车与稻浪为题的马赛克画，提醒这里曾是通勤与运输的节点。窗台边，一只白底虎斑猫晒着日光，旅客经过时不以为意，它也不以为意，这是驿站最日常的画面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579636-790075.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579636-790075-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579636" class="wp-caption-text">东里铁马驿站入口，保留着浓厚的旧车站风貌。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14579637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579637-790076.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579637-790076-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579637" class="wp-caption-text">室内墙上的马赛克拼贴画，重现火车行驶于东里风光的景象。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14579629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579629-790068.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579629-790068-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579629" class="wp-caption-text">前身为东里车站的铁马驿站，保留了历史轨迹与在地记忆。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>田野与人驿站后方还留着旧房舍，墙面斑驳，草丛长得快，把时间的速度呈现出来。草地另一头，三朵“云”排成不同距离，和自然天空的云互相映照；有时火车从新线驶过，长镜头能把“真的云”与“假的云”放在同一幅里。地方旅游网站也常把这里与新、旧站并列介绍，提醒旅人：在东里，铁道与田野是一起看的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579631-790070.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579631-790070-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579631" class="wp-caption-text">这组作品为〈飘飘云朵〉，为艺术家赖冠仲创作，提醒游客别攀爬以策安全。（ 郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14579632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579632-790071.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579632-790071-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579632" class="wp-caption-text">绿野加上美丽的白云，装置艺术更美丽了。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>留住一个地方的方法驿站外的人行道与单车道串起社区，小农在亭子里卖自家作物，游客歇脚、喝杯咖啡，再往北骑到玉里，或往南接富里、六十石山。有人专程为“云”而来，有人为“列车”停留；更多人是像照片里那样，沿着月台慢慢走，看光线怎么在竹编与山边移动。旧站没有被拆掉，反而被转成日常生活的场景，这大概就是留住地方的方法：让人继续来，让故事继续在同一块月台上发生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579638-790077.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14579638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579638-790077-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579638" class="wp-caption-text">驿站的慵懒猫咪，午后时光在窗台上享受日光。（郑建南提供）</figcaption></figure>
