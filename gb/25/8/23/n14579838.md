<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>永恒优雅 日本护肤9法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579839-shutterstock_305302817-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本女性白皙细腻，姿态娴雅，美丽一如樱花。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者夏瑜编译报导）“水润的肌肤，浓密的黑发，忧郁的眼睛，生动的脸庞，个个都是美人……”据说，这是首任德国驻日公使布隆特对日本女人的赞美之词。</p>
<p>日本女人的肌肤为什么总是光彩照人？也许MSN网站介绍的日本9大生活方式可以让我们一窥究竟。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">1. 从市场挑选新鲜食材</span></p>
<p>日本文化最令人钦佩的一点是他们偏爱应季新鲜果蔬。日本料理的食材直接来自市场或农场。这些营养丰富的食物能够消炎、滋润肌肤，并促进整体健康，直接有助于保持肌肤的年轻柔腻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579840-1_shutterstock_2597063663-e1755992983715.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14579840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579840-1_shutterstock_2597063663-e1755992983715.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579840" class="wp-caption-text">日本农贸市场的新鲜蔬果。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">2. 用不同的方法补水</span></p>
<p>补水不仅仅是喝白水。日本人以茶文化闻名，抹茶和绿茶等饮品是他们日常补水的重要组成部分。这些茶不仅清爽怡人，还富含抗氧化剂，能够帮助肌肤排毒，让人由内而外焕发健康光泽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579841-2_shutterstock_300587603-e1755993100406.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14579841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579841-2_shutterstock_300587603-e1755993100406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579841" class="wp-caption-text">抹茶和绿茶。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">3. 日常体育锻练</span></p>
<p>日本生活方式的一大亮点是将体育锻练完美地融入日常。无论是步行去商店、骑自行车，还是练习柔道、剑道等传统武术，运动都是他们每日起居不可或缺的一部分。这种积极的生活方式可以增进心血管健康，促进血液循环，让肌肤容光焕发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579842" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579842-3_shutterstock_2083169314-e1755993179991.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14579842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579842-3_shutterstock_2083169314-e1755993179991.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579842" class="wp-caption-text">日常步行。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">4. 忽视肌肤就是忽视健康</span></p>
<p>对日本人来说，护肤并非奢侈品，而是必需品。他们的护肤流程注重温和多层地保湿，维护肌肤屏障，采用天然简约的产品，使肌肤长期清透亮泽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579843" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579843-4_shutterstock_2438484665-e1755993278399.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14579843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579843-4_shutterstock_2438484665-e1755993278399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579843" class="wp-caption-text">护肤天然简约。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">5. 睡眠优先</span></p>
<p>日本人非常重视美容觉。优质的睡眠帮助身体再生和修复皮肤细胞，让你每天早晨都拥有更清新、更幼嫩的面容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579844-5_shutterstock_613277189-e1755993337813.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14579844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579844-5_shutterstock_613277189-e1755993337813.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579844" class="wp-caption-text">美容觉。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">6. 发酵食品的力量</span></p>
<p>味噌、纳豆、腌菜等发酵食品在日本饮食中占有重要地位。它们富含益生菌，有助肠道健康，进而使皮肤光滑洁净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579845-6_shutterstock_2526847863-e1755993411123.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14579845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579845-6_shutterstock_2526847863-e1755993411123.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579845" class="wp-caption-text">发酵食品。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">7. 少量均衡的饮食</span></p>
<p>控制食量是日本文化的另一个值得借鉴的理念。少量、均衡的饮食能够确保身体获得所需的营养，而不会给消化系统带来过重的负担，这对于整体和肌肤的健康大有裨益。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579846" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579846-7_shutterstock_133013792-e1755993468115.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14579846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579846-7_shutterstock_133013792-e1755993468115.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579846" class="wp-caption-text">少量、均衡的饮食。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">8. 极简主义生活方式</span></p>
<p>在日本人的生活中，少即是多。家居如此，护肤亦然。干净、整洁的日常护理可避免皮肤刺激，有助于长期保持皮肤秀润。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">9. 规律沐浴</span></p>
<p>每天沐浴，尤其是用温水沐浴，是日本人根深蒂固的习惯。沐浴不仅关乎卫生，还能放松身心、缓解压力、促进血液循环——所有这些都会反映在皮肤上。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14579847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14579847" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579847-9_shutterstock_2273050713-e1755993565112.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14579847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14579847-9_shutterstock_2273050713-e1755993565112.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14579847" class="wp-caption-text">温水沐浴。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
