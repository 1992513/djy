<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>玛家乡收获祭暨全乡运动会 在雨中展现原乡力量</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577733-789290-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>屏东玛家乡一年一度的收获节，在细雨与笑声中热闹登场。（周永田提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月20日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）屏东玛家乡一年一度的收获祭暨全乡运动会，在细雨与笑声中热闹登场。尽管天公不作美，活动当天雨势一度倾盆，却浇不熄族人的热情与信念。各部落族人换上最华丽的传统服饰，踏着稳健的舞步，唱出祖先的歌，呈现出原民文化的精髓与部落的凝聚力。活动从村长登上竹制瞭望台鸣示开场，象征部落守望与团结。接着，长者们的歌舞暖场揭开序幕，从坐姿伸展到站立挥舞，节奏稳健，脸上挂满笑容。青年队伍则以勇士舞踏出刚劲步伐，在雨中显得更加豪迈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577740" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577740-789297.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577740-789297-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577740" class="wp-caption-text">穿着传统背心与配饰的男子们手挽着手，神情专注地共舞，展现部落的凝聚力。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14577741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577741-789298.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577741-789298-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577741" class="wp-caption-text">族人与贵宾同欢。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14577739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577739" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577739-789296.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577739-789296-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577739" class="wp-caption-text">穿着鲜艳传统服饰并肩而立，歌声交织成热闹的节庆氛围。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14577742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577742-789299.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577742-789299-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577742" class="wp-caption-text">展演-inaljan光亮之处。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>排湾部落传统领袖高冠芬Zuljezulj Taugadu特别穿上由祖母亲手缝制、已有半世纪历史的传统服饰，参与剧情展演。“这件衣裳承载着家族的记忆与祝福，今天能在族人面前穿上它，是一种荣耀。”她感性地说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577732-789289.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577732-789289-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577732" class="wp-caption-text">排湾部落头目高冠芬（左一）带着族人一起参加收获祭活动。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高冠芬表示，这次表演由专业老师蒋伟光编写剧本并精心指导，内容呈现“Paiwan Padain 共融共荣”的精神。正式演绎时，大雨不断渗入眼中，几度张不开眼睛，但族人们依然坚定地一字一句唱出歌词，唱出祖先传承的智慧。“泥泞阻不了脚步，青年们踏出的是坚毅与荣耀；我们笑着走进场，那是一种信念与力量。”她笑说，大雨冲淡了脸上的妆，却冲不掉族人的笑容，那才是最美的妆扮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14577747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577747-789304.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577747-789304-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577747" class="wp-caption-text">六位排湾部落族人，穿着不同色系传统礼服的族人站在雨中，展现部落服饰的多样与华丽。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14577746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577746-789303.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577746-789303-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577746" class="wp-caption-text">长者双手高举金黄小米，笑容灿烂，将丰收的喜悦献给天地与族人。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14577748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14577748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577748-789305.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14577748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14577748-789305-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14577748" class="wp-caption-text">将手中的谷粒洒下，寓意五谷丰收与族人平安。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的收获节不只是歌舞的飨宴，更是一场文化的凝视与传承。排湾族服饰上繁复的珠绣、贝片与金属饰件，象征身份与家族故事；舞蹈的节奏和歌词，传递着土地与祖灵的连结。即使在雨中，族人肩并肩、手牵手的画面，仍让现场观众感受到满满的力量与温暖。玛家乡收获节不仅是庆祝农作丰收，更是部落精神的展现。每一次的聚会，都是族人彼此祝福、传递文化与凝聚情感的重要时刻。正如高冠芬所愿：“愿天佑部落族人平安健康，贺玛家乡乡运昌隆。”</p>
