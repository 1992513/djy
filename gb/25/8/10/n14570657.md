<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>艺术家王德合的维也纳三日行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570676-786710-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>走进艺术史博物馆，迎面而来的是雄伟的大理石阶梯与精致雕塑。（王德合提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾报导）“维也纳的第一眼，其实让我有点失望。”来自台湾的画家王德合，谈起今年八月初的维也纳行，语气带着一丝诚实的笑意。他与太太于8月5日自捷克布拉格搭巴士前往奥地利首都维也纳，历时4个半小时。这座城市因丰富的音乐历史遗产，被誉为“音乐之都”、“艺术之都”。对艺术家夫妻而言，这是早在计划表上的必访之地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570661-786687.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570661-786687-600x433.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570661" class="wp-caption-text">布鲁赫尔《巴别塔》。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570670" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570670-786702.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570670-786702-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570670" class="wp-caption-text">“刚到时有点失落，但三天下来，这里的音乐、美术馆、街头艺人和建筑，把我完全收服。”王德合笑说，（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570671-786704.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570671-786704-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570671" class="wp-caption-text">王德合与太太于8月5日自捷克布拉格搭巴士前往奥地利首都维也纳，历时4个半小时。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从圣史蒂芬大教堂开始的熟城术抵达后，他们先依惯例直奔位于老城区中心的圣史蒂芬大教堂。“我们每到一个欧洲城市，都会先找主教堂，因为重要景点几乎都绕在周边，很快就能熟悉环境。”他们搭电梯登上北塔俯瞰全城。不同于布拉格红瓦白墙、尖塔林立的景色，维也纳的屋顶色彩沉稳，夹杂不少现代建筑。“那时心里想，嗯…怎么有点普通。”</p>
<p>巴洛克教堂的夜晚乐声改变印象的转折，来自一场音乐会。在被金碧雕饰包围的圣彼得教堂，夫妻俩买了当晚八点半的弦乐四重奏门票。“曲目有莫札特、巴哈、韦瓦第、舒伯特，音符在高耸穹顶回荡，整个人都被包进音乐里。”王德合形容，那是视觉与听觉的双重享受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570665" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570665-786694.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14570665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570665-786694-600x875.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="658" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570665" class="wp-caption-text">巴洛克风华下的乐音——维也纳教堂内，弦乐四重奏。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570666-786695.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570666-786695-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570666" class="wp-caption-text">在金碧辉煌的祭坛前演奏，乐声与空间的华丽装饰相互辉映。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>街头的东方旋律圣史蒂芬大教堂广场，同样充满音乐氛围。他们遇到两位穿红裙的女孩弹奏古筝，悠扬的东方乐音在哥德式建筑前响起，吸引了众多路人驻足。“很奇妙，东方旋律与欧洲广场，居然如此和谐。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570680" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570680-786714.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14570680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570680-786714-600x1046.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="785" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570680" class="wp-caption-text">王德合直呼，这里不只是收藏艺术的地方，本身就是一件艺术品。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570673" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570673" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570673-786706.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14570673" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570673-786706-600x1001.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="751" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570673" class="wp-caption-text">广场上，两位身着红色礼服的年轻音乐家演奏古筝，东方琴音在哥德式尖塔下回荡，为古城增添异国情调。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570679" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570679-786713.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570679-786713-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570679" class="wp-caption-text">圣史蒂芬大教堂前。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与大师的相遇在市区与公园间漫步，两人看见许多音乐巨匠的雕像。贝多芬端坐于绿荫下，神情专注；市立公园的金色约翰·史特劳斯雕像，手持小提琴定格于圆舞曲的瞬间，仿佛空气中都在旋转。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570678" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570678-786712.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14570678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570678-786712-600x1028.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="773" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570678" class="wp-caption-text">在城中公园，王德合来到贝多芬纪念碑前，向这位音乐巨匠致敬。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570667-786698.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570667-786698-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570667" class="wp-caption-text">圆舞曲之王——金光闪耀的约翰·史特劳斯雕像，在市立公园中拉响永恒的旋律。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>十小时的艺术马拉松“如果只能选两个博物馆，我一定推艺术史博物馆和列奥波多博物馆。”王德合说。艺术史博物馆馆藏涵盖各时代名作，是艺术爱好者的殿堂；列奥波多博物馆虽然名气不如前者，但收藏大量奥地利国宝画家席勒的作品，还有克林姆等大师的画作。因为周四艺术史博物馆延长开放至晚间九点，他们干脆安排“一日双馆”。上午11点进馆，晚上9点离开，十小时的艺术轰炸，虽然走到腿软，却满足到不想停下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570675" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570675" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570675-786709.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14570675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570675-786709-600x1018.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="764" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570675" class="wp-caption-text">博物馆中的古埃及展厅，带领观者穿越千年时空。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570659" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570659-786685.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14570659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570659-786685-600x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="506" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570659" class="wp-caption-text">光影交错中，映出大师内敛又深邃的眼神。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14570660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570660" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570660-786686.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14570660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570660-786686-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570660" class="wp-caption-text">热闹的乡村婚宴场景，描绘出朴实却充满人情味的生活画面。（王德合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>维也纳的黄昏与全景黄昏时分的圣卡尔教堂，双根立柱与绿色圆顶在阳光中闪耀。台阶上，人们或坐或聊，气氛闲适。最后一天，他们登上美泉宫后方山丘远望:对称的花园、笔直的林荫道与金黄色宫殿，构成了教科书般的巴洛克图示。“那时我想，维也纳的美，不在第一眼，而是在慢慢相处之后。”“刚到时有点失落，但三天下来，这里的音乐、美术馆、街头艺人和建筑，把我完全收服。”王德合笑说，维也纳果然名副其实，是会让人想再来一次的城市。</p>
