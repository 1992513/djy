<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这些美容工具值得拥有</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570989-shutterstock_2063208461-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>合适的美妆工具让你的化妆流程更顺畅，妆容更精致。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年08月11日讯】（大纪元记者夏瑜编译报导）良好的护肤或化妆流程不仅关乎使用的产品，也关乎手中的工具。合适的工具可以提升美妆效果，节省时间，甚至让日常护理更显奢华。但是，市面上琳琅满目的美容小工具，究竟哪些才是真正值得拥有的呢？</p>
<p>Vocal网站推荐了下面的美妆工具，从经典款到尖端设备，应有尽有。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">1. 玉石滚轮或刮痧石</span></p>
<p>为何值得：这些古老的工具经受住了时间的考验，自有它的道理。用玉石滚轮或刮痧石按摩面部，有助于消肿、促进血液循环和淋巴引流。许多使用者还发现它有放松舒缓的功效。</p>
<p>使用方法：将工具放入冰箱冷藏，凉后在清洁、湿润的肌肤上，轻柔地自下向上进行按摩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570994-1_shutterstock_1765657037-e1754858263766.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14570994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570994-1_shutterstock_1765657037-e1754858263766.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570994" class="wp-caption-text">玉石滚轮和刮痧石（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">2. 高品质化妆刷</span></p>
<p>为何值得：好的化妆刷能显着提升妆容的持久度。它能让美妆完美晕染开来，防止留下条痕，还能减少化妆品用量。</p>
<p>选购要点：柔软密实的刷毛，耐用的手柄，包含粉底刷、散粉刷、腮红刷、眼影刷和晕染刷的套装。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570995" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570995-2_shutterstock_1624820701-e1754858384286.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14570995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570995-2_shutterstock_1624820701-e1754858384286.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="539" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570995" class="wp-caption-text">化妆刷套件（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">3. 美妆蛋或化妆海绵</span></p>
<p>为何值得：湿润的化妆海绵能为粉底、遮瑕膏、腮红等，打造出自然的妆效。对于追求完美妆容的人来说，它是必备之选。</p>
<p>小贴士：每3个月更换一次化妆海绵，并定期清洗，以防止细菌滋生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570996" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570996-3_shutterstock_417030571-e1754858492519.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14570996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570996-3_shutterstock_417030571-e1754858492519.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570996" class="wp-caption-text">化妆海绵（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">4. LED光疗面具</span></p>
<p>为何值得：这种高科技面具利用不同波长的光线来治疗痤疮、消炎并促进胶原蛋白生成，长期使用能带来如同居家水疗般的效果。</p>
<p>最适合：易长痘痘的肌肤，想要抗衰老和提升肌肤光泽的人士。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570998-4_shutterstock_2376579441-e1754858566670.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14570998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570998-4_shutterstock_2376579441-e1754858566670.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570998" class="wp-caption-text">LED光疗面具（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">5. 洁面刷</span></p>
<p>为何值得：洁面刷比单纯用手能去除更多污垢、油脂和彩妆，让肌肤更洁净、更亮丽。有些洁面刷还能温和去角质。</p>
<p>小贴士：如果你是敏感肌肤，请每周使用2～3次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14570999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14570999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570999-5_shutterstock_1600647763-e1754858643680.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14570999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570999-5_shutterstock_1600647763-e1754858643680.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14570999" class="wp-caption-text">洁面刷（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">6. 微电流仪</span></p>
<p>为何值得：微电流仪使用低强度电流刺激面部肌肉，提拉肌肤，促进血循。经常使用后，面部更加紧致，轮廓更加清晰。</p>
<p>最适合：抗衰老，面部轮廓塑造，促进护肤品吸收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14571000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14571000" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571000-6_shutterstock_2226364605-e1754858712991.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14571000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571000-6_shutterstock_2226364605-e1754858712991.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14571000" class="wp-caption-text">微电流仪（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">7. 微针滚轮</span></p>
<p>为何值得：微针滚轮使用微型针头在皮肤上制造微创，刺激胶原蛋白生成，改善肌肤纹理，淡化疤痕和细纹。</p>
<p>注意：务必使用清洁、优质的滚轮，并遵循安全说明。如果你是敏感性肌肤或活跃的痤疮患者，请先咨询皮肤科医生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14571002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14571002" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571002-7_shutterstock_2157993101-e1754858773610.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14571002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571002-7_shutterstock_2157993101-e1754858773610.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14571002" class="wp-caption-text">微针滚轮（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">8. 加热睫毛夹</span></p>
<p>为何值得：加热睫毛夹比传统睫毛夹可让睫毛卷翘更持久，效果更令人惊艳，而且不会夹伤或拉扯睫毛。</p>
<p>使用方法：轻轻加热，然后从根部向上提起睫毛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14571003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14571003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571003-8_shutterstock_1170610939-e1754858830796.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14571003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571003-8_shutterstock_1170610939-e1754858830796.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14571003" class="wp-caption-text">加热睫毛夹（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">9. 斜口镊子</span></p>
<p>为何值得：一把好用的镊子对于精准修眉和去除杂毛至关重要。Tweezerman斜口镊子因其锋利和抓握力强而备受青睐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14571005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14571005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571005-9_shutterstock_2082662701-e1754859008248.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14571005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571005-9_shutterstock_2082662701-e1754859008248.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14571005" class="wp-caption-text">斜口镊子（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">10. 家用脱毛仪</span></p>
<p>为何值得：IPL（强脉冲光）设备可以逐渐减少毛发生长，让你无需频繁出入美容院。</p>
<p>注意：并非适合所有肤色或发色，请仔细阅读说明。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14571006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14571006" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571006-10_shutterstock_1841099521-e1754859066502.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14571006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571006-10_shutterstock_1841099521-e1754859066502.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="633" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14571006" class="wp-caption-text">家用脱毛仪（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">11. 绸缎枕套</span></p>
<p>为何值得：减少头发断裂，防止毛躁，比棉质枕套更温和亲肤。而且，它触感奢华！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14571007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14571007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571007-11_shutterstock_1684035700-e1754859139571.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14571007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14571007-11_shutterstock_1684035700-e1754859139571.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14571007" class="wp-caption-text">绸缎枕套（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">12. 头皮按摩器</span></p>
<p>为何值得：促进血液循环，帮助头发健康生长，在忙碌的一天后放松身心。◇</p>
