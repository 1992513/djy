<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【首尔自由行】第三天：弘大商圈逛街 美食吃不停</h2>
<a href="https://d3myewunzonann.cloudfront.net/J6jYsDVhmv6v"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上集：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/8/19/n14576817.md#1" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">【首尔自由行】 第二天：穿韩服游北村韩屋村</a></p>
<p>今天是逛街行程，要去逛弘大商圈，我们带了两个空的20吋行李箱出门，搭地铁到弘大入口站（Hongik Univ.）。在弘大地铁地下街，买了奶油鸡蛋糕，好吃。从9号出口出来，要先去吃中餐。Winnie带我们去吃一家她曾经吃过，觉得还不错的弘大知名韩国烤肉店。我们点了三盘脸颊肉和一盘五花肉，有专人帮忙烤，烤得刚刚好，很好吃，吃得很开心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14578226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578226-3-days-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14578226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578226-3-days-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578226" class="wp-caption-text">我们的中餐——韩国烤肉。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>弘大商圈不只好逛，还是韩国练习生和街头艺人的表演天堂。这里的衣服走的是年轻潮流路线，到处都是街头小店，款式新、价格亲民。基本上都是现金价，刷卡需额外支付手续费，所以出发前我们就先在饭店隔壁的换汇所换了一些韩元。逛了一个下午，行李箱塞满了我们的战利品，开心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14578225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578225-3-days-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14578225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578225-3-days-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578225" class="wp-caption-text">很好逛的弘大商店街。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14578224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578224-3-days-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14578224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578224-3-days-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578224" class="wp-caption-text">街头艺人表演。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚餐一致通过吃部队锅，上网找了附近一家评价还不错的店。我们点了一个部队锅、一盘辣炒猪肉，以及两包拉面。这家店的味道也很不错，吃得非常满足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14578227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578227-3-days-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14578227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578227-3-days-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578227" class="wp-caption-text">我们的晚餐，部队锅加拉面。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14578223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578223-3-days-5.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14578223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578223-3-days-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578223" class="wp-caption-text">我们的晚餐，辣炒猪肉。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>很充实的一天！最后，当然不能错过韩国超人气炸鸡。店内很多人，我们点完餐，店员请我们到门外等候。等了好一会儿，终于等到了，我们要带回饭店当宵夜。这个炸鸡真的很好吃，外皮酥脆、酱料咸甜适中，搭配腌渍的白萝卜，绝配！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14578222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14578222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578222-3-days-6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14578222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14578222-3-days-6-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14578222" class="wp-caption-text">好吃的韩国炸鸡。（图/Lana提供）</figcaption></figure>
