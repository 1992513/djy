<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台风过后的天空画卷 奇美博物馆的火烧云奇景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574851-788192-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>奇美博物馆在清晨点灯，壮观景色让摄影家愿意早起，前来取景拍照。（王建中提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年08月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）“每年夏季台风通过或引进的西南气流后的2-3天，是爱好风光摄影的朋友很忙碌的时候。因为除了很容易有火烧云外，也有很多好天气很难遇到的题材，如西南气流的雷雨胞和台风引起的大浪……有些经典画面就是利用这时间拍到。”摄影家王建中这样形容自己最常守候的时刻。这回，杨柳台风过境后，清晨他来到台南奇美博物馆，用镜头记录下难得的光影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14574839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574839-788180.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574839-788180-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574839" class="wp-caption-text">台风过后的清晨，奇美博物馆在金色晨光中展现恢宏姿态，倒映在水面的景致分外澄澈。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14574850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574850-788191.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574850-788191-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574850" class="wp-caption-text">台风过后的天空画卷，奇美博物馆的火烧云奇景。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>霞光映照的古典博物馆清晨时分，天空被染成一片炽烈的红与橙，像火焰般铺展。奇美博物馆的白色穹顶与雕像，映衬着霞光显得格外清晰。照片里的拱桥一字排开，桥上的雕像凝望天际，静静守候，仿佛与天空对话。台风过后的空气澄澈，湖面平静如镜。馆舍的身影与流动的云彩倒映其中，让人一时间分不清哪里是天空，哪里是水面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14574848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574848-788189.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574848-788189-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574848" class="wp-caption-text">拱桥与馆舍，在紫蓝晨光中倒映湖面，展现别样的静谧。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14574845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574845-788186.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574845-788186-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574845" class="wp-caption-text">日出前，染红天空与河水，雕像与桥拱的身影交错，成就一幅壮丽的台南。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>倒影中的浪漫与宁静另一张画面里，雕塑池中的骏马与神话人物，被染上霞彩，倒影随着水波微微颤动。红、橙、紫交织的天空，仿佛铺洒在水里，整个景象像是一幅油彩画。走近馆前花园，色彩更加浓烈。蜿蜒的步道引领视线，通往金色圆顶，在霞光映照下，建筑散发温润的光泽。那是一种经过风雨洗礼后的宁静与壮丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14574846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574846" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574846-788187.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574846-788187-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574846" class="wp-caption-text">馆舍与桥影逐渐融入晨光，灯光点亮后别有一番浪漫氛围。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14574841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574841-788182.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574841-788182-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574841" class="wp-caption-text">通往博物馆的白色大理石拱桥，在彩霞衬托下如同穿越时空的古文明遗迹。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14574849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574849-788190.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574849-788190-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574849" class="wp-caption-text">雕像凝望天际，见证风雨后的宁静与美丽。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14574844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574844-788185.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574844-788185-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574844" class="wp-caption-text">湖面如镜，将奇美博物馆的古典圆顶与彩霞一并收藏，画面宛如一幅油彩。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晨光初临的另一种风景随着晨光降临，灯光渐次点亮，拱桥上的雕像被黄灯映照出轮廓。此时天空转为紫蓝，最后一抹朝霞与人工光线交织在湖面，呈现另一种静谧。白日的绚烂消失了，取而代之的是温和的晨景，显得庄严而安定。这正是王建中所说，台风后几天最令人期待的光影时刻。在风雨之后照片里的奇美博物馆，既有火烧云的壮阔，也有早晨的安详。王建中的镜头没有华丽的修饰，却忠实捕捉台风过后天空的变化。“有些经典画面，就是在这样的时候出现的。”他的一句话，道出了摄影人长年等待的坚持。这些影像，不只是美景，也是时间与自然留下的印记。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14574842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574842" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574842-788183.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574842-788183-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574842" class="wp-caption-text">桥上雕像静静伫立，仿佛守护着这座城市的文化殿堂，与流动的云彩一同对话。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14574840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574840" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574840-788181.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14574840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574840-788181-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574840" class="wp-caption-text">博物馆前的白色雕像在火红云霞下更显庄严，与余晖交织出一幅古典画卷。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14574843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14574843" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574843-788184.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14574843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14574843-788184-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14574843" class="wp-caption-text">日出透过云层绽放，照亮前方花圃与馆舍，呈现台风过后难得的静谧。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
