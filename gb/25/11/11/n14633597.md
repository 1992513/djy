<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金马62公开颁奖阵容 翁倩玉将现身 李安不缺席</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14633603-2511110057521487.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
