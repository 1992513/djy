<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>央行调查：明年3月前加拿大经济衰退概率35%</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/08/id14570962-CP160514146-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加拿大央行近期依据自身经济预测持续行动，例如调降政策利率。（加通社）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月12日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元记者周行多伦多报导）加拿大央行周一发布的</span><span class="s3">2025</span><span class="s1">年第三季度市场参与者调查（</span><span class="s3">MPS</span><span class="s1">）显示，加拿大经济衰退风险仍高于三分之一。</span></p>
<div>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">这项针对银行、资产管理经理及投资公司的调查指出，市场预期未来</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">个月内加拿大出现经济下滑的概率为</span><span class="s3">35%</span><span class="s1">，与</span><span class="s3">2025</span><span class="s1">年第二季度调查结果持平，较第一季度</span><span class="s3">38%</span><span class="s1">略降，但仍是去年同期水准的近两倍。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s3">Better Dwelling</span><span class="s1">网站编辑分析指出，表面看似平静，实则乐观情绪大幅下滑。更令人担忧的是数据分布恶化：在</span><span class="s3">2025</span><span class="s1">年第三季度，最乐观受访者（第</span><span class="s3">25</span><span class="s1">百分位）认为未来</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">个月衰退概率翻倍至</span><span class="s3">20%</span><span class="s1">；最悲观者（第</span><span class="s3">75</span><span class="s1">百分位）则估计为</span><span class="s3">50%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">调查显示，未来</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">个月衰退预期高于其他任何时段。</span><span class="s3">2025</span><span class="s1">年第三季度调查中，未来</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">至</span><span class="s3">12</span><span class="s1">个月衰退中位数概率为</span><span class="s3">30%</span><span class="s1">，与第二季度持平，但较年初大幅下降。此概率趋稳现象显示，市场视衰退风险迫在眉睫</span><span class="s3">——</span><span class="s1">若真发生，将迅速来临。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">中期风险几乎未变。</span><span class="s3">2025</span><span class="s1">年第三季度调查显示，未来</span><span class="s3">12</span><span class="s1">至</span><span class="s3">18</span><span class="s1">个月衰退概率为</span><span class="s3">25%</span><span class="s1">，与上季度持平；未来</span><span class="s3">18</span><span class="s1">至</span><span class="s3">24</span><span class="s1">个月则略升至</span><span class="s3">23%</span><span class="s1">，高于一年前的</span><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1adg3ll r-1g7jtus r-1x3r274"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">20%。</span></span></span><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1adg3ll r-1x3r274 r-p1pxzi"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3"><span class="css-1jxf684 r-bcqeeo r-1ttztb7 r-qvutc0 r-poiln3 r-1x3r274">加拿大央行近期依据自身经济预测持续行动，例如调降政策利率。</span></span></span></p>
