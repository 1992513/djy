<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园会稽国中与社区志工整合老人营养健康团圆餐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627989-806129-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>会稽国中李文义校长感谢中华义厨协会共同烹煮十道素食健康美味料理。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月02日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）11月2日重阳节，桃园市会稽国中举办“学校与社区志工整合老人营养健康团圆餐”跨域合作，协助华山基金会桃园区独居长辈30位，到会稽国中吃素食团圆餐。其中有两位百岁人瑞到场进行健康团圆餐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627988" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627988-806128.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627988-806128-600x202.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="202" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627988" class="wp-caption-text">11月2日重阳节，桃园市会稽国中举办“学校与社区志工整合老人营养健康团圆餐”跨域合作，协助华山基金会桃园区独居长辈30位，到会稽国中吃素食团圆餐。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14627992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627992" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627992-806132.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627992-806132-600x359.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627992" class="wp-caption-text">有两位百岁人瑞到场进行健康团圆餐。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会稽国中李文义校长感谢中华义厨协会共同烹煮十道素食健康美味料理，再由会稽国中40位学生志工，扮演爷爷奶奶的半天孙子孙女陪伴独居长辈健康团圆餐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627990" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627990-806130.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14627990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627990-806130-600x569.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627990" class="wp-caption-text">十道素食健康美味料理。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用餐期间由麦克家族公益表演团体进行怀旧歌舞演唱，会稽国中童军团带领独居长者进行闯关互动游戏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627991" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627991-806131.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14627991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627991-806131-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627991" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中童军团设计与长辈互动闯关游戏，预防肌少症。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李文义在担任桃园市健康与体育辅导团副召集人经验中表示，今年(2025年)台湾正式进入超高龄社会，从老老互助及真实落实老人健康饮食的点，拉成支援体系合作的线，再到关住长者健康，预防肌少症，落实运动与营养教育实践的面，加上台师大健康促进与卫生教育系合作理念，让师生开创社区、教育与公益整合的新模式。</p>
<p>活动由释觉元住持结合台师大健康促进与卫生教育学系健康专业，与会稽国中以及公益教育慈善团体组成，团队成员包含会稽里志工队、华山基金会桃园站、中华民国全国义厨联合会、同济会安亲会、万能科技大学教授彭宣绮101厨师朱俊廷，世界自由地区选美第三名邱邑函（也是会稽国中校友）一起服务上菜关心独居长者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627993-806133.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627993-806133-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627993" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中童军团设计与长辈互动闯关游戏。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14627998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627998-806138.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627998-806138-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627998" class="wp-caption-text">会稽国中举办幸福陪伴独居长者健康团圆餐。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市家长协会荣誉理事长吕朝福；超高龄社会，老年人口的营养需求日益受重视，很高兴能协助这场健康团圆餐，老老互助更是未来趋势，特别是针对“肌少症”的营养议题，深远影响长者生活品质，更值得关注。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627994-806134.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627994-806134-600x323.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627994" class="wp-caption-text">左一为世界自由地区选美第三名邱邑函（也是会稽国中校友）也热心参与。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14627996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627996" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627996-806136.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14627996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627996-806136-600x643.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="482" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627996" class="wp-caption-text">世界自由地区选美第三名邱邑函（也是会稽国中校友）一起服务上菜关心独居长者。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14627999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627999-806139.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627999-806139-600x314.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627999" class="wp-caption-text">中华义厨协会共同烹煮十道素食健康美味料理，再由会稽国中40位学生志工，扮演爷爷奶奶的半天孙子孙女陪伴独居长辈健康团圆餐。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学校课程与社区生活紧密结合，研发健康团圆餐办桌菜，结合华山基金会桃园站30位独居长辈，由义厨们依照营养师建议健康制备菜色，学校招募40位学生志工，扮演半天孙子孙女，透过整合型高龄志工培训成为老老互助健康宣讲的“高老师”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627997" style="width: 510px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627997-806137.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14627997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627997-806137-600x536.jpg" alt="" width="510" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627997" class="wp-caption-text">世界自由地区选美第三名邱邑函（也是会稽国中校友）一起服务上菜关心独居长者。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14627995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627995" style="width: 516px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627995-806135.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14627995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627995-806135-600x523.jpg" alt="" width="516" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627995" class="wp-caption-text">素食团圆餐。（桃园市会稽国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>童军团谢谢李文义给他们与独居长辈互动和服务的机会，桃园市家长协会荣誉理事长吕朝福及刘正保桃园区总会长都到场并肯定学生的表现。◇</p>
