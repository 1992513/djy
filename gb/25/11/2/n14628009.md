<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>孕妈咪闯关园游会 宏其妇幼医院32周年庆共度欢乐周末</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628010-806145-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院于花田盛事筑梦庄园举办32周年庆，图中为妇科名医宏其妇幼医院长张红淇。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月02日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院11月2日下午1时至5时，于花田盛事筑梦庄园（桃园市中坜区普忠路618号）举办32周年庆“亲子同欢嘉年华会”。现场有桃园市妇幼局陈先琦副局长、桃园市卫生局陈玉泽主秘、魏筠市议员、谢美英市议员、彭俊豪市议员、黄崇真市议员等来宾莅临祝贺。活动内容丰富多元，包括宝宝爬行比赛、造型比赛、亲子趣味竞赛、孕妈咪闯关园游会、准爸比巧手赛等，欢迎所有家庭一同参与，共度欢乐周末。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628012" style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628012-806147.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14628012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628012-806147-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="595" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628012" class="wp-caption-text">宝宝爬行比赛。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宏其妇幼医院长张红淇医师表示，医院32年来持续提升医疗品质，只为提供妇女朋友与新生宝宝最完善的照顾。在产科方面，我们致力实现“个人化孕产旅程”，由个人专属的主治医师进行产检并亲自接生，让孕妈咪安心无忧；同时打造智慧化产房与病房，透过科技设备提供更安全、舒适的待产与休养环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628011-806146.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628011-806146-600x282.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628011" class="wp-caption-text">“亲子同欢嘉年华会”。现场有桃园市妇幼局陈先琦副局长、桃园市卫生局陈玉泽主秘、魏筠市议员、谢美英市议员、彭俊豪市议员、黄崇真市议员等来宾莅临祝贺。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妇科方面，宏其也不断引进尖端科技，包括达文西微创手术及海扶刀，提供更精准、恢复更快的治疗选择，守护女性健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628013-806148.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628013-806148-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628013" class="wp-caption-text">亲子趣味竞赛。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，为帮助更多家庭实现生育梦想，本院积极发展试管婴儿技术，以先进的生殖医学与专业团队，提供高成功率与人性化的疗程支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628014-806149.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628014-806149-600x391.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628014" class="wp-caption-text">妇科名医宏其妇幼医院长张红淇与接生的母子合影。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14628018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628018" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628018-806153.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14628018" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628018-806153-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628018" class="wp-caption-text">父子造型比赛。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张红淇进一步说明，从个人化的产检纪录、豪华精装出生证明，到每年举办的盛大亲子活动，宏其希望成为每个家庭最坚实的伙伴。“32年是一个里程碑，感谢乡亲一直以来的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628015-806150.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628015-806150-600x346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628015" class="wp-caption-text">“亲子同欢嘉年华会”大家开心合影。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14628016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628016" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628016-806151.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14628016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628016-806151-600x744.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="558" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628016" class="wp-caption-text">父子造型比赛。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们承诺会继续努力，整合从产前、生产到产后的完整服务，并持续与台北医学大学合作，引进最新技术，成为桃园地区最值得信赖的妇幼医院。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628017" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628017" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628017-806152.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14628017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628017-806152-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628017" class="wp-caption-text">父子造型比赛。（中坜区宏其医疗社团法人宏其妇幼医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周年庆活动不仅是医院的生日派对，更是回馈社区、凝聚家庭情感的温馨盛会。邀请社会大众莅临指导，共同见证宏其妇幼医院32年来的成长与蜕变。◇</p>
