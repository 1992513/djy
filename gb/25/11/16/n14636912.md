<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】雅枝竹甘笋栗子素汤～滋补养气！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636915-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>滋补养气的“素汤”，好喝又养身。（梁厨美食提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>冬天养生一定要喝煲汤，精华营养就在一碗汤里！“素汤”给人寡味不好入口的印象，这一款汤品清单来自香港老师傅的秘方，食材好取材，料理步骤不藏私，赶快学起来！</h3>
<h3>材料：</h3>
<p>雅枝竹2个<br />
鹿茸菇2 oz<br />
粟米1条<br />
红萝卜2条<br />
栗子5 oz<br />
腰果3 oz<br />
百合1 oz<br />
莲子1 oz<br />
蜜枣4粒<br />
姜4片</p>
<figure id="attachment_14636919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14636919" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636919-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14636919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636919-1-450x279.jpg" alt="材料。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14636919" class="wp-caption-text">材料示意图，不包含雅枝竹在内。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>作法：</h3>
<p>1. 雅枝竹剥掉底部几片叶，切除茎部留用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14636920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14636920" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636920-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14636920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636920-2-450x338.jpg" alt="雅枝竹剥掉底部几片叶，切除茎部留用。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14636920" class="wp-caption-text">雅枝竹剥掉底部几片叶，切除茎部留用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 雅枝竹切开一半，剪掉中心的茸毛，洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14636921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14636921" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636921-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14636921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636921-3-450x253.jpg" alt="雅枝竹切开一半，剪掉中心的茸毛，洗干净备用。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14636921" class="wp-caption-text">雅枝竹切开一半，剪掉中心的茸毛，洗干净备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 鹿茸菇浸泡并洗净，红萝卜去皮，切滚刀大块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14636922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14636922" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636922-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14636922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636922-4-450x338.jpg" alt="鹿茸菇浸泡并洗净，红萝卜去皮，切滚刀大块。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14636922" class="wp-caption-text">鹿茸菇浸泡并洗净，红萝卜去皮，切滚刀大块。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 粟米剥掉外衣，切开4段备用，急冻栗子洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14636923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14636923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636923-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14636923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636923-5-450x338.jpg" alt="粟米（玉米）剥掉外衣，切开4段备用，急冻栗子洗干净。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14636923" class="wp-caption-text">粟米（玉米）剥掉外衣，切开4段备用，急冻栗子洗干净。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 莲子和百合用水浸泡，腰果和蜜枣，洗净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14636924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14636924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636924-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14636924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636924-6-450x338.jpg" alt="腰果洗净备用。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14636924" class="wp-caption-text">腰果洗净备用。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 汤锅内放入16碗水，放入所有材料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14636925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14636925" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636925-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14636925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636925-7-450x338.jpg" alt="汤锅内放入16碗水，放入所有材料。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14636925" class="wp-caption-text">放入所有材料。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 大火煲滚15分钟后转为小火煲1.5小时，加盐调味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14636926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14636926" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636926-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14636926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636926-8-450x600.jpg" alt="大火煲滚15分钟后转为小火煲1.5小时，加盐调味。（梁厨美食提供）" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14636926" class="wp-caption-text">大火煲滚15分钟后转为小火煲1.5小时，加盐调味。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">主厨梁志生经历：</span></h3>
<p>1.掌厨经验已超过四十年<br />
2.曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
3.曾任海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
4.作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
5.曾任美国富商George Bowles 御用厨师</p>
