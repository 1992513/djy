<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>专业化妆师：液体唇膏这样画</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636175-shutterstock_2552946359-e1763170389481.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>【大纪元2025年11月15日讯】（大纪元记者夏瑜编译报导）乍一看，如何使用液体唇膏似乎手到擒来，相当容易。但实际上，它并不像涂上你最喜欢的唇彩那么简单。</p><p>液体配方将经典唇膏的色彩与唇彩棒的控制性相结合，涂抹时湿润，干燥后形成防晕染的质地，因持久力而受到化妆师和粉丝的喜爱。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p>“与普通唇膏相比，液体唇膏持妆时间更长。”美妆品牌Urban Decay 全球艺术总监史蒂夫．卡萨吉基安（Steve Kassajikian）说，“此外，液体唇膏不会出现脱色或晕染。”</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><div><figure style="max-width:640px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636206-shutterstock_2488122771-e1763170878654.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F11%2Fid14636206-shutterstock_2488122771-e1763170878654.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636206-shutterstock_2488122771-e1763170878654.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F11%2Fid14636206-shutterstock_2488122771-e1763170878654.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="液体唇膏持妆时间更长。（Shutterstock）" width="640" b="auto"/></picture></a><figcaption>液体唇膏持妆时间更长。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure></div><p>专业化妆师在高端女性时尚网站Glamour上分享了提升液体唇妆效果的技巧。</p><p><span style="color:#0000ff;font-family:文鼎粗黑;font-size:large">怎样做好唇部准备？</span></p><p>专家表示，使用液体唇膏最常见的错误就是没有做好充分的准备。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p>“涂抹液体唇膏前的唇部护理对于确保唇妆的顺滑至关重要。”明星化妆师劳伦．达梅利奥．文特尔（Lauren D'Amelio Ventre）强调。</p><p>卡萨吉基安讲解，“用温和的唇部磨砂膏（lip scrub）去除唇部干燥皮肤，然后涂上润唇膏（lip balm），让双唇恢复水润。”等到嘴唇吸收了一些水分后，擦去润唇膏。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636182-shutterstock_2344780115-e1763170767250.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F11%2Fid14636182-shutterstock_2344780115-e1763170767250.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636182-shutterstock_2344780115-e1763170767250.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F11%2Fid14636182-shutterstock_2344780115-e1763170767250.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="用温和磨砂膏去除唇部死皮，为液体唇膏做准备。（Shutterstock）" width="450" b="466"/></picture></a><figcaption>用温和磨砂膏去除唇部死皮，为液体唇膏做准备。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure></div><p>下一步可以选择涂抹唇部打底膏（lip primer），“但如果你已经做好了去角质和保湿的准备，就不需要打底了。”文特尔说。</p><p><span style="color:#0000ff;font-family:文鼎粗黑;font-size:large">如何使用液体唇膏？</span></p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p><span style="font-family:文鼎粗黑;font-size:large">步骤1：描唇</span></p><p>在为唇部做好上妆准备后，专家建议用唇线笔勾勒唇线。</p><p>“我总是喜欢在涂抹液体唇膏之前先用唇线笔勾勒，不仅描出完美唇形，还能起到屏障的作用。”卡萨吉基安表示。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>另一个好处是什么？避免出错。“液体唇膏干得很快，容易弄脏脸部，所以在使用时，一定要控制好用量，依照唇线慢慢来，以免晕染。”文特尔说。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636177-shutterstock_2260712599-e1763170527531.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F11%2Fid14636177-shutterstock_2260712599-e1763170527531.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636177-shutterstock_2260712599-e1763170527531.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F11%2Fid14636177-shutterstock_2260712599-e1763170527531.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="先用唇线笔勾勒唇线，之后再涂抹液体唇膏。（Shutterstock）" width="450" b="450"/></picture></a><figcaption>先用唇线笔勾勒唇线，之后再涂抹液体唇膏。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="font-family:文鼎粗黑;font-size:large">步骤2：涂抹</span></p><p>“首先擦掉管口多馀的唇膏”，卡萨吉基安建议，然后“沿着唇线笔描画的轮廓，用唇刷勾勒”。接着，从唇部中心点开始，向外涂抹，“始终从薄薄的一层开始，再根据你喜欢的妆效，逐渐加厚。”</p><p><span style="font-family:文鼎粗黑;font-size:large">步骤3：完成</span></p><p>虽然你可以涂上液体唇膏，等它干透就结束，但额外的步骤可能会让你的妆容更上一层楼。如果你偏爱哑光妆效，并且希望妆容持久，可以轻轻地在嘴唇上撒点定妆粉，或者喷上保湿喷雾。如果你想要浓郁的色彩和闪亮的妆效，文特尔建议最后再涂一层唇彩（lip gloss）。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_5"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_5"></div></div></div><p><span style="color:#0000ff;font-family:文鼎粗黑;font-size:large">什么妆容最适合搭配液体唇膏？</span></p><p>液体唇膏几乎可以融入任何妆容，但它在自然妆容和浓烈妆容中的效果会有所不同。</p><p>“我最喜欢的是干净清新的打扮，双唇是点睛之笔。”卡萨吉基安说。他喜欢单色系的妆容，唇膏的色调会反映在眼影和腮红上。</p><p>与他的看法相同，文特尔建议，如果你想让双唇最引人注目，那么淡妆是最大限度发挥液体唇膏作用的方法，“对浓妆而言，焦点通常不是嘴部，但在简约妆容中，双唇会非常抢眼。”</p><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636217-shutterstock_2425357237-e1763171163867.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F11%2Fid14636217-shutterstock_2425357237-e1763171163867.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14636217-shutterstock_2425357237-e1763171163867.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F11%2Fid14636217-shutterstock_2425357237-e1763171163867.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="淡妆让液体唇膏的唇效更为吸睛。（Shutterstock）" width="450" b="675"/></picture></a><figcaption>淡妆让液体唇膏的唇效更为吸睛。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="color:#0000ff;font-family:文鼎粗黑;font-size:large">液体唇膏的色号该如何选择？</span></p><p>液体唇膏以高显色度而闻名。卡萨吉基安表示，在选择色号（shade）时，可以参考自己的肤色。对于面部白皙的人，他推荐浅粉色、桃色、带有珊瑚色调的裸色。肤色稍黑的人，尤其适合淡紫色（mauve）、浆果色（berry）和玫瑰色。皮肤色泽再深的话，巧克力棕色、李子色（plum）或红色绝对不会出错。</p><p>除此，液体唇膏还适合打造高对比度的妆容，例如白净皮肤+深酒红色双唇，或者深色皮肤+亮樱桃红双唇。</p><p>对于妆容，无论你喜欢自然还是大胆，哑光还是亮泽，添置几支优质的液体唇膏绝对不会让你后悔。◇</p><p>责任编辑：齐守善
