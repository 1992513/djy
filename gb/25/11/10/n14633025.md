<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台民团声援李卓人、邹幸彤 吁港府无条件放人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14633084-2511100417262378.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
