<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中坜雷霆除暴 查缉毒驾新利器“毒品唾液快筛”上线</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640851-810701-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中坜警分局雷霆除暴勤前教育。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月22日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）中坜分局已全面正式启用配发之毒品唾液快筛试剂作为查缉毒驾新利器，未来查处毒驾将更即时、更有效率。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14640849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14640849" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640849-810699.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14640849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640849-810699-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14640849" class="wp-caption-text">查缉毒驾新利器“毒品唾液快筛”。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中坜警分局也将持续结合桃警团队警力强化临检、扩大盘查，以高密度警力持续守护民众安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14640850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14640850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640850-810700.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14640850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640850-810700-600x382.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14640850" class="wp-caption-text">中坜警分局辖内治安热点部署4处临检点。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中坜警分局21日晚间执行“雷霆除暴”扩大临检勤务，结合刑事警察大队、保安警察大队霹雳小组、交通警察大队及外事科等桃警团队，共动员99名警力，在中山路270巷、永兴街等辖内治安热点部署4处临检点，并前往 辖内KTV、酒店、撞球场、旅馆等23处特种营业场所执行临检，同步针对外籍人口加强盘查，全面净化治安。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14640852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14640852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640852-810702.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14640852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640852-810702-600x368.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14640852" class="wp-caption-text">中坜警分局雷霆除暴KTV临检。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14640854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14640854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640854-810704.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14640854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640854-810704-600x408.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14640854" class="wp-caption-text">中坜警分局雷霆除暴酒店临检。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内坜派出所于晚间22时许，在永兴街路检点查获 44 岁诺姓泰籍男子饮酒后骑乘电动车，酒测值高达 0.75 mg/L，警方当场予以扣车并逮捕，依公共危险罪移送侦办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14640853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14640853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640853-810703.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14640853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640853-810703-600x392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14640853" class="wp-caption-text">中坜警分局雷霆除暴酒店临检。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>仁爱派出所巡逻员警也于22时45分，在新中北路查获34岁万姓男子持有毒品案，当场查扣丧尸烟弹（俗称依脱迷酯）13颗、K他命及彩虹烟等毒品，警询后依毒品罪嫌移送侦办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14640855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14640855" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640855-810705.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14640855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640855-810705-600x256.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14640855" class="wp-caption-text">查获丧尸烟弹及其他毒品。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>23时临检KTV时，普仁派出所亦查获正在欢唱的33岁黄男为不能安全驾驶通缉犯，随即被警方带回办理归案。</p>
<p>中坜警分局本周计查获各类刑案82件78人，其中包含诈欺案件30件31人、毒品案件13件12人及通缉犯21件19人，展现警方“强势执法、压制不法”的决心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14640856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14640856" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640856-810706.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14640856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640856-810706-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14640856" class="wp-caption-text">查扣丧尸烟弹（俗称依脱迷酯）13颗、K他命及彩虹烟等毒品。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中坜警分局长林鼎泰强调，毒驾危害与酒驾同等严重，一次事故就可能造成多个家庭破碎。◇</p>
