<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>受新海诚经典作品启发 黄玠玮创作《i人情歌》</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640753-20251121-Universal-Music-VVV02418-1.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>【大纪元2025年11月22日讯】（大纪元记者杨逸帆报导）黄玠玮Zooey Wonder在2025冬季发表最新创作歌曲《i人情歌》，以难得的纯爱情歌题材，藉由细腻且温厚的嗓音，唱出一段深藏于心的恋曲。歌曲于21日数位上线、MV首播。</p><div><figure style="max-width:450px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14640751-20251121-Universal-Music-VVV02830-1.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px"
