<h6>2025 Lexus RX 350h。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月08日讯】作为 Lexus 最畅销的中型 SUV，2025 款 RX 350h 延续家族设计语言，车身线条流畅而不张扬，细节处体现精致感。动力方面，它搭载 2.5 升四缸发动机与电动机组合，总功率 246 匹马力，搭配电子无级变速箱（eCVT）。在北美市场，RX 350h 标配全轮驱动（AWD），纯汽油版 RX 350 则可提供前驱或全驱选择。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d3cacbcgqcawbc.cloudfront.net/X0xBOKf4tNVm"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/lUEUUZarUV0?si=Xp8jjm5yjZUzMwY1" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>在日常驾驶中，这套成熟混合动力系统表现从容自如。低速阶段电机驱动安静顺滑，提速时电机与发动机衔接自然。官方数据显示 0–60 英里/小时加速约 7.4 秒，对于城市与高速场景的日常驾驶已属充裕。全驱版的稳定性尤佳，后轮可由电机独立驱动，抓地力与安全感明显提升。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631731-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_06-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14631731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631731-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_06-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631731" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Lexus RX 350h。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>EPA 官方油耗为城市 37 mpg、高速 34 mpg。实际驾驶中，平均油耗约 34 mpg，表现稳定可靠。对中型豪华 SUV 而言，此成绩优于大多数非混合动力对手。长途驾驶时，RX 350h 综合续航可达 600 英里以上，兼顾节能与舒适，特别适合家庭用户。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631710-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_09.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14631710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631710-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631710" class="wp-caption-text">RX 350h前脸。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14631711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631711" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631711-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14631711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631711-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631711" class="wp-caption-text">尾闸连结了两侧已灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基于 GA-K 平台打造的 RX 350h，底盘刚性提升明显，悬挂系统在应对城市路面起伏时表现从容，既化解细碎颠簸，又保持车身稳定。车厢隔音优异，低速穿梭与高速巡航均安静舒适。F Sport 车型在外观与内饰上更具动感，提供专属运动座椅与可选自适应可变悬挂（AVS），使驾乘氛围更具激情。转弯时车身姿态稳健，整体以舒适为核心，兼顾驾驶信心与乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631712" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631712-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14631712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631712-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631712" class="wp-caption-text">发动机与马达混合动力系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>RX 350h 的内饰延续 Lexus 一贯精致风格，大面积软质皮革与开孔木饰兼具触感与视觉美感。14英寸中控萤幕直观易用，支持无线 CarPlay 和 Android Auto，反应迅速。前排座椅支撑性佳，长途驾驶仍舒适；前排座椅可调倾斜角度，腿部与头部空间充裕，适合家庭出行。行李厢容积 46 立方英尺，放倒后排可扩展至 58 立方英尺，可容纳婴儿车或露营装备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631719-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14631719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631719-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631719" class="wp-caption-text">RX仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025 RX 350h 全系标配 Lexus Safety System+ 3.0，包括自动紧急制动（带行人与自行车识别）、自适应巡航、车道保持与盲点监测。在 NHTSA 与 IIHS 碰撞测试中均获高分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631720" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631720-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14631720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631720-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631720" class="wp-caption-text">14英寸中控萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>科技方面，RX 采用与 Toyota 混合动力车相似的电子换档（shift?by?wire）架构，保留 R/N/D/S 挡位逻辑，首次使用需些许适应，但习惯后操作干脆利落，亦释放中控空间。车门搭载 Digital Latch 电子执手技术，轻触即可开门，即使断电亦可透过内部机械释放，兼具便利与安全，提升内饰一致性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631721" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631721-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_25.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14631721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631721-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631721" class="wp-caption-text">电子换档架构。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025 RX 350h 并非以极致运动或突破性创新取胜，而在于稳健、耐用与省心。预算在 5 万至 6 万美元的买家，可从中获得舒适静谧的驾驶体验、出色燃油经济性与一流安全配置。驾驶激情或许不及部分德系对手，但这种取舍符合 Lexus 一贯理念：服务家庭与长途出行，而非追逐速度极限。RX 350h 可说是一款“有惊喜、无遗憾”的豪华混动 SUV。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631722-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_28.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14631722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631722-2025_Lexus_RX_350h_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631722" class="wp-caption-text">前排座椅支撑性佳。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
