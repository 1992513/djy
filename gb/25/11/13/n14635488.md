<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>三星杯：杨鼎新负朴廷桓 四强中韩各占一半</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635495-532109work-002-zxcvz.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
