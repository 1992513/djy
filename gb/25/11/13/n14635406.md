<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>风雨后的小生命 陈明忠的手机微距摄影</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635413-808903-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>褐色蛾摊开翅，伏在老木头上，斑点与木纹交错出低调的静美。（陈明忠提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月13日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）这一两年，花莲人像在考验里生活。地震、台风、光复偃塞湖涌入村落的泥水、万荣山谷里突如其来的土石流，一次接着一次，让人心里也像被挖空。天气终于放晴那天，陈明忠拿起他那支旧型号手机，走向田边与山脚，用最简单的方式重新触碰世界。他说没有别的，就是想“看看外面是不是还好”。没带其他器材，也没刻意寻景，就只是弯着腰、蹲着，靠近那些在风雨里努力活着的小生命。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14635412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635412" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635412-808902.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635412-808902-600x772.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="582" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635412" class="wp-caption-text">细长的身体、轻巧的姿势，在枝条上停着，像在观察四周的变化。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不起眼的昆虫　最实在的力量第一只映入眼帘的是叶片上的红黑小甲虫。雨后的叶面还亮着水光，小甲虫的壳像被擦过一层油一样滑亮。它慢慢往前爬，脚步很轻，但方向稳稳的。陈明忠看着它说：“它比我们还淡定。”接着，是一只挂在蕨叶下的粉蝶。光从背后打过来，翅脉透着细致的纹路，像是被时间刻在薄翼上。它静着不动，好像也在享受久违的阳光。这样的画面，不是壮阔，却让人心里松一口气，原来生命还在照自己的节奏走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14635407" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635407" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635407-808897.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635407-808897-600x780.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="586" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635407" class="wp-caption-text">雨后的叶片还带着亮光，小甲虫慢慢往前爬，壳色鲜明，像在向阳光报到。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14635408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635408-808898.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635408-808898-600x796.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635408" class="wp-caption-text">粉蝶挂在卷曲的蕨叶下方休息，逆光照出翅脉，安静又柔和。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花朵之间的小访客紫花里，一只深色细长的甲虫在花心穿梭。黄与紫的撞色，本来就抢眼，但镜头里的它，更像是把整朵花撑起来的小舞者。还有一只翅膀透明、眼睛带着金属绿光的小型蝇类，落在金黄的花蕊上。它的身形轻，看起来脆弱，但每一个细节都明亮、有层次。陈明忠说：“它们都没有抱怨，雨过了就继续忙。”一句简单话，却像在讲花莲人的日子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14635410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635410-808900.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635410-808900-600x755.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="566" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635410" class="wp-caption-text">深色细长的甲虫在紫花花心穿梭，黄色花蕊在镜头里显得特别热闹。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14635417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635417" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635417-808908.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635417-808908-600x791.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635417" class="wp-caption-text">金黄的花心里，一只蜜蜂埋头采粉，翅脉透明细致，整朵花都亮了起来。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14635409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635409-808899.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635409-808899-600x764.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="573" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635409" class="wp-caption-text">金黄色斑纹的蝴蝶停在红花上，细细吸取花蜜，动作轻柔。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>微距镜头里的坚韧不只色彩鲜艳的昆虫，那些沉着色调的，也让人停下脚步。木头上停着的褐色蛾，翅膀张得开开的，边缘破损，看起来像历经风雨。但它不急着飞，只安稳地贴在木纹上，仿佛在等太阳把它烘干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14635415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635415-808905.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635415-808905-600x823.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="617" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635415" class="wp-caption-text">橘色头胸与金属蓝绿的背甲形成强烈对比，是雨后最亮眼的一角。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>手机，是他手中最诚实的创作工具因为一些缘故，他目前已无法使用专业相机长距离拍摄。“手机能拍就拍，不强求。”他笑着说。只是这些照片放上网后，总是有人以为他用了什么高阶镜头，结果听到是旧手机，都忍不住竖起大拇指。他不追求“惊天动地”的作品，只想把眼前的生命拍好。“看见就记录，手机够不够好不重要。”他常说这句话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14635419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635419" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635419-808910.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635419-808910-600x738.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="555" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635419" class="wp-caption-text">长满细刺的仙人掌顶端，冒出一朵亮黄的小花，花茎细却挺直。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在困难里　看见生命自己往外生长这些小昆虫的世界很小，小到一片叶子就是舞台，一根草就足够歇息。但陈明忠透过手机，提醒我们：生命的力量不在大小，而在于“愿不愿意继续”。花莲的山河动过、哭过，受伤过。但在风雨间的缝隙里，这些小生命依然撑着、爬着、振着翅。他的照片没有要安慰谁，也不想刻意放大什么意义，只是静静地呈现，“它们还在，我们也还在。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14635420" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635420" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635420-808911.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635420" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635420-808911-600x740.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="556" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635420" class="wp-caption-text">伪装得像叶子一样的天蚕蛾趴在树叶上，阳光一照，纹路清楚又立体。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14635414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635414" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635414-808904.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635414-808904-600x706.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="531" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635414" class="wp-caption-text">翅膀像画过线稿般清晰，透光的纹路在阳光下更显独特。（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>光照到哪里　生命就从哪里长回来阳光重新落下的那天，陈明忠拍到的不是昆虫，而是我们每个人正在努力的样子：跌倒、淋湿、惊魂未定，但仍然愿意再往前一步。灾后的花莲依旧在修复，但他的镜头告诉我们：“生活还是会动起来，小生命也会教我们重新站稳。”这些作品不华丽、不刻意，却真实、有呼吸感。那就是他用旧手机拍出的力量，也是花莲人的力量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14635421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14635421" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635421-808912.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14635421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14635421-808912-600x756.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14635421" class="wp-caption-text">淡黄花刚绽放，花瓣薄而透光（陈明忠提供）</figcaption></figure>
