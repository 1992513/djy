<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2025铜锣杭菊生活节“菊内人”29日盛大登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645636-812530-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>秘书长陈斌山邀请全国游客来体验杭菊花海的魅力。（苗县府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月29日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗县富农农产运销合作社以“菊内人”为主题，举办的2025铜锣杭菊生活节，11月29日至30日将在樟树村盛大登场，结合赏景体验、食农文化、低碳旅游与国际交流，苗栗县政府秘书长陈斌山邀请全国游客在杭菊盛放之际，走进铜锣乡找到属于自己的“菊内人时刻”，体验杭菊花海的魅力，共赏最美秋日铜锣风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14645635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14645635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645635-812529.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14645635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645635-812529-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14645635" class="wp-caption-text">秘书长与来宾敲响铜锣宣告杭菊生活节正式开始。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14645643" aria-describedby="caption-14645643" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645643-812537-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645643-812537-450x300.jpg" alt="开幕式由铜锣之光赖仁政老师以悠扬的客家八音揭开序幕." width="598" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14645643" class="wp-caption-text">开幕式由铜锣之光赖仁政老师以悠扬的客家八音揭开序幕。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今日开幕仪式由客委会主任秘书廖育珮与苗栗县政府秘书长陈斌山、立委沈发惠主任陈诗弦、议员黄芳椿、罗贵星、陈品安、陈春暖、徐筱菁、翁杰、农改场副场长钟国雄、敏实科技大学校长曾信超、铜锣乡公所主秘赖文良及地方代表等一同出席敲响铜锣。</p>
<p>客委会主任秘书廖育珮表示，富农农产运销合作社用心规划铜锣杭菊生活节活动，代表地方产业发展进入新的局面，除了物产本身，亦结了加工、服务，朝向6级化发展，透过小旅行、文创等带动经济效益，增进农民收入。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14645637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14645637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645637-812531.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14645637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645637-812531-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14645637" class="wp-caption-text">客委会主任秘书廖育珮致词。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>富农农产运销合作社理事主席陈弘智说明，这项活动首度将“杭菊花田美景”、“客家风土办桌”、“透明食安教育”结合在同一个活动，不只赏花，而是从产地到餐桌的完整文化旅游体验，11月29、30日将在樟树村登场，活动地点请搜寻“2025铜锣杭菊生活节主会场”地标。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14645640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14645640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645640-812534.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14645640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645640-812534-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14645640" class="wp-caption-text">铜锣乡杭菊花海。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14645639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14645639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645639-812533.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14645639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645639-812533-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14645639" class="wp-caption-text">现场游客如织。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14645638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14645638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645638-812532.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14645638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645638-812532-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14645638" class="wp-caption-text">活动现场人潮汹涌。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈弘智指出，此次邀请敏实科技大学餐饮管理系助理教授李又贞、陈丰志等共同设计菜单，推出客家杭菊宴，包括菊花炖汤、花香甜点、茶饮与创意套餐等，让旅人不只拍照赏景，更能体验客家美食，将在粉丝专页开放预约报名。</p>
<p>陈秘书长强调，这次活动有三大亮点，办理客家杭菊宴，以菊入菜，呈现铜锣农食的深度魅力；推出“透明食安馆”，透过食材品检展示、产地故事分享与导览讲解，公开从田间到餐桌的每一到环节；推动国际文化交流邀请外籍文化团体参与志工，并以语言互动、杭菊小旅行等形式，让游客在花海中感受跨国交流的热度，展现铜锣从地方走向世界的文化底蕴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14645642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14645642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645642-812536.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14645642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645642-812536-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14645642" class="wp-caption-text">现场农民采摘杭菊。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14645641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14645641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645641-812535.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14645641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14645641-812535-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14645641" class="wp-caption-text">现场展售的杭菊多元化商品。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秘书长陈斌山赞许杭菊生活节结合了产、官、学、研各界资源，更有效率地推广全台唯一的特色农产杭菊，尤其铜锣杭菊必须农药零检出，经过相当严格的品质管制，落实为消费者的食安把关，地方同心协力把这些农特产品行销到各地，人潮、钱潮自然源源不绝涌入，活络地方经济，增加农民与业者收入。</p>
