<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾高尔夫协会心怀感恩办“感恩杯”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629722-DSC00173-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>开球前大家合照。（岳磊/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月05日讯】(大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大报导) 乔治亚州台湾高尔夫球协会（TGA）于11月2日（周日）在亚特兰大东郊Lithonia的Mystery Valley Golf Club高尔夫球场举办了2025年的“感恩杯”高尔夫球友谊赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14629727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629727" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629727-DSC00158-e1762303888448.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14629727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629727-DSC00158-e1762303888448.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629727" class="wp-caption-text">赛前热身。（岳磊/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14629728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629728" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629728-DSC00159-e1762303983587.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14629728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629728-DSC00159-e1762303983587.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629728" class="wp-caption-text">赛前热身。（岳磊/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在现会长Jessica杨的号召下，八十多位球友们踊跃报名，分成超过二十组。有从北卡开车来参加的，还有远从密苏里州飞来参加的球友，也有不少台湾人第二代参加。此外，亚特兰大快乐高尔夫球协会也有许多球友来共襄盛举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14629730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629730" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629730-DSC_5852-e1762304143433.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14629730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629730-DSC_5852-e1762304143433.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629730" class="wp-caption-text">赛前热身。（岳磊/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14629731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629731" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629731-DSC_5861-e1762304218946.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14629731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629731-DSC_5861-e1762304218946.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629731" class="wp-caption-text">赛前热身。（岳磊/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>球友们首先在俱乐部内享用了香蕉、咖啡、蜂蜜姜茶、西点面包、蛋糕等以及“苏姐台湾美食”提供的台式排骨与卤肉饭便当，然后挥竿暖身后便浩浩荡荡的乘坐数十辆球车直奔球场。这天是典型的北乔治亚州秋高气爽的天气，阳光和煦，红叶穿插在松林中，敞徉在果岭上让大家都感到心旷神怡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14629732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629732" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629732-DSC_5843-e1762304325696.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14629732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629732-DSC_5843-e1762304325696.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629732" class="wp-caption-text">打球前的美食。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14629733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629733" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629733-DSC_5844-e1762304403804.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14629733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629733-DSC_5844-e1762304403804.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629733" class="wp-caption-text">打球前的美食。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>球赛结束后，众人到Chamblee中国城地段刚装修好的豪华气派的福临门海鲜餐厅参加晚宴，有龙虾、螃蟹等佳肴以及红酒与烈酒。亚特兰大侨教中心的阎树荣主任也光临捧场。除了颁奖，还有热闹的摸彩活动，奖品有国窖酒，台北经济文化办事处赞助的高粱酒，咖啡机，笔记型电脑等总值超过4,000元的奖品。</p>
