<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中坜警大学宣导防制性骚与反诈 多国语守护外籍生安全</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629165-806647-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中央大学外籍生犯罪预防宣导。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月04日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）中坜警分局为加强外籍学生法治观念与人身安全意识，持续与辖内各大专院校密切合作，也应邀前往校园举办的“境外新生座谈会”宣导讲座，针对来自印尼、越南、泰国、菲律宾及其它国家的外籍学生进行多语版犯罪预防宣导，协助同学认识台湾法令、融入在地生活，建立正确求助与自我保护观念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14629166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629166-806648.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14629166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629166-806648.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629166" class="wp-caption-text">万能科大外籍学生犯罪预防宣导。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14629169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629169" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629169-806651.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14629169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629169-806651.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629169" class="wp-caption-text">健行科大宣导。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宣导内容除反诈骗、交通安全法规外，亦特别结合“跟踪骚扰防制法”与“性骚扰防治法”案例，以简报影片与多国语言图卡方式说明，只要让他人感到被冒犯或不适，影响日常生活，即可能构成性骚扰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14629167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629167-806649.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14629167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629167-806649.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629167" class="wp-caption-text">万能科大外籍学生犯罪预防宣导。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14629171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629171-806653.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14629171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629171-806653.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629171" class="wp-caption-text">中原大学外籍生犯罪预防宣导。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警方提醒，如遇相关情形，应明确拒绝并积极搜证，可拨打1955外来人士服务专线或113保护专线寻求协助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14629168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629168-806650.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14629168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629168-806650.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629168" class="wp-caption-text">南亚技术学院。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中坜警分局指出，为协助外籍学生更了解台湾社会的法律与安全资源，已制作印、越、泰、菲、英文等多国语版本宣导文宣，并分送至健行科大、元智大学、万能科大、中原大学、南亚技术学院及中央大学等校区张贴或发放，期能透过多语宣导提升防诈、防骚与交通安全意识。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14629170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629170" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629170-806652.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14629170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629170-806652.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629170" class="wp-caption-text">健行科大宣导。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14629172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14629172" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629172-806654.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14629172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629172-806654.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14629172" class="wp-caption-text">健行科大宣导。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中坜警分局局长林鼎泰表示，辖内大学聚集众多境外学生，警方除持续强化校园巡守与外籍生安全网络，更积极结合校方举办专题讲座，透过友善、易懂的多语宣导方式，协助外籍学子安心在台学习与生活，共同打造多元共融、安全友善的校园环境。◇</p>
