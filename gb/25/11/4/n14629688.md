<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：拜仁击败大巴黎 取得开局“16连胜”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14629702-GettyImages-2244530204.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
