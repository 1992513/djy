<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾同乡青藤会50周年庆共织半世纪温暖乡情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638557-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>青藤长者大合照：见证半世纪的情谊，笑容洋溢。（主办方提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月19日讯】多伦多台湾同乡青藤会<span class="s1">11</span>月<span class="s1">15</span>日在文教中心举行<span class="s1">50</span>周年庆祝大会，约<span class="s1">200</span>位长者与社区人士齐聚一堂，共同见证这个温馨的里程碑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638561" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14638561" rel="attachment wp-att-14638561"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14638561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638561-5-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638561" class="wp-caption-text">林金枝女士与会长黄崇斌共同切蛋糕，象征青藤精神代代相传。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1">活动由会长黄崇斌致欢迎词揭开序幕，他感谢前辈多年来的耕耘与会员持续的陪伴，让青藤会成为台裔长者的温暖归属。随后，会员齐声高唱会歌《青藤青藤走天涯》，场面感人。</p>
<p class="p2"><strong>长者庆生</strong></p>
<p class="p2">当天特别为<span class="s1">11</span>月份<span class="s1">9</span>位寿星庆生，大家齐唱生日快乐歌并发红包。今年由<span class="s1">98</span>岁精神矍铄的林金枝女士代表切蛋糕，场面热烈又温馨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638560" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14638560" rel="attachment wp-att-14638560"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14638560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638560-4_-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638560" class="wp-caption-text">寿星合照：9位寿星与会员共享喜悦。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p2">现场播放青藤会史回顾影片，让大家重温前辈足迹，唤起珍贵的共同记忆。</p>
<p class="p2"><strong>嘉宾致词</strong></p>
<p class="p2">驻多伦多办事处处长梁毅鹏、文教中心主任蓝柏青、副主任许彩凤、多伦多市议员<span class="s1"> Rachel Chernos Lin</span>，以及蓬莱文教基金会董事长王兴富伉俪等嘉宾到场祝贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638558" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14638558" rel="attachment wp-att-14638558"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14638558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638558-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638558" class="wp-caption-text">市议员 Rachel Chernos Lin（左二）致颁市政府贺函，与梁毅鹏处长（右三）、蓝柏青主任（右二）、会长黄崇斌（左一）、前副会长罗益世先生合影。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p2"><span class="s1">Rachel Chernos Lin </span>致词时强调，青藤会活动对促进长者健康、友谊与社交至关重要，并代表市政府颁发贺函，表彰青藤会自<span class="s1">1975</span>年以来对社区的杰出贡献。</p>
<p class="p2"><strong>文娱表演嗨翻全场</strong></p>
<p class="p2">大会安排文娱表演，神鼓队以整齐划一的鼓声震撼全场，展现磅礡气势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638563" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14638563" rel="attachment wp-att-14638563"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14638563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638563-7-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638563" class="wp-caption-text">鼓声震撼人心，气势非凡。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p2">文化种子舞蹈老师钟敏带领<span class="s1">8</span>位青藤男士热情投入“草裙舞”与“爱的恰恰”，南洋风情与欢乐氛围让全场嗨翻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638559" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14638559" rel="attachment wp-att-14638559"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14638559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638559-3_-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638559" class="wp-caption-text">8位青藤男士舞姿投入，笑声与掌声不断。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p2"><strong>半世纪的情谊</strong></p>
<p class="p2">青藤会<span class="s1">50</span>周年庆，不仅是对过去的回顾，更是对未来的展望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638562" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/?attachment_id=14638562" rel="attachment wp-att-14638562"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14638562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638562-6-450x203.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638562" class="wp-caption-text">王兴富董事长伉俪（左一及右二）长期支持青藤会，由会长颁赠感谢状。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p2">从温馨的庆生到震撼的表演，从嘉宾的支持到会员的笑容，这场盛会展现了台湾文化在多伦多的深厚根基，也凝聚了跨世代的社区情谊。</p>
