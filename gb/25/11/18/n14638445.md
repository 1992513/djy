<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>哈佛MIT台湾学生“学术白话文”交流科研</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638444-IMG_9385-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>11月16日，哈佛大学和MIT台湾同学会在MIT举办“学术白话文”科研交流活动。参与者合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）11月16日下午，哈佛大学和MIT台湾同学会在MIT理论物理学中心举办“6乘6学术白话文”活动，邀请两校资深的人文和自然科学研究者，分享他们的研究内容，获得热烈反响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638434-DSC04912.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638434-DSC04912-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638434" class="wp-caption-text">11月16日，哈佛大学和MIT台湾同学会在MIT举办“学术白话文”科研交流活动。MIT台湾同学会副会长刘正心欢迎听众。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14638436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638436-DSC04918.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638436-DSC04918-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638436" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛台湾同学会会长陈曦欢迎听众。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两校台湾同学会每年举办两场“学术白话文”交流活动，得到驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处教育组、科技组的支持。在周日的活动中，主办方邀请6位专家到场，每人以6分钟用浅白语言介绍自己的研究内容，再用6分钟回答听众的问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638435-DSC04915.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638435-DSC04915-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638435" class="wp-caption-text">经文处科技组组长蔡孟勋称赞“学术白话文”活动内容丰富。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14638443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638443" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638443-DSC04946.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638443-DSC04946-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638443" class="wp-caption-text">交流会最后，主办方向6位主讲人赠礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MIT机械系博士研究生杨宗佑讲解“引擎活塞的数位孪生”；</p>
<p>哈佛大学访问学者邱琳婷分享“关于‘策展’二三事”；</p>
<p>MIT理论物理系博士后研究员赵恩宏介绍“量子色动力学”；</p>
<p>哈佛大学社会学博士研究生赵振辰分析“大学科系与性别工资差距的变化趋势”；</p>
<p>MIT化工系访问学者李昱硕解释“电化学触媒反应机制”；</p>
<p>哈佛大学物理系博士研究生韦彦丞讲解“量子网路和盲量子计算”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638437-DSC04919.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638437-DSC04919-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638437" class="wp-caption-text">MIT机械系博士研究生杨宗佑分享。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14638438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638438-DSC04926.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638438-DSC04926-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638438" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛大学访问学者邱琳婷分享。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14638439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638439-DSC04930.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638439-DSC04930-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638439" class="wp-caption-text">MIT理论物理系博士后研究员赵恩宏分享。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每一位讲者，都从科学原理、研究方法、应用领域等不同方面，分享了他们的研究项目。</p>
<p>比如，杨宗佑谈到他研究汽车引擎设计的经验，为提升用电脑计算各项参数的效率，往往将一个复杂问题拆分成若干个小问题分别计算。</p>
<p>邱琳婷回顾了她策划重要展览的三次经历，也引用哈佛和MIT策展方对同一主题采用不同展现方式的例子，分享了透过“策展”来描述展品背后的文化、信仰、科技等层面的心得。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14638440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638440-DSC04935.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638440-DSC04935-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638440" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛大学社会学博士研究生赵振辰分享。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14638441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638441-DSC04936.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638441-DSC04936-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638441" class="wp-caption-text">MIT化工系访问学者李昱硕分享。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14638442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14638442" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638442-DSC04939.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14638442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14638442-DSC04939-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14638442" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛大学物理系博士研究生韦彦丞分享。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MIT台湾同学会会长林晏平、副会长刘正心、哈佛台湾同学会会长李典璋、陈曦等干部都参加了活动，向听众表达欢迎，向主讲人表达感谢。</p>
<p>经文处科技组组长蔡孟勋也到场听讲。他说，自己有科学研究背景，每次参加台湾同学会的学术交流活动都学到很多，大家有这样的学习、交流机会是非常好的。◇</p>
