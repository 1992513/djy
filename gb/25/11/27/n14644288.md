<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台湾故宫“北宋西园雅集传奇”特展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644348-2511270652232378-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾国立故宫博物院（故宫）推出“千年神遇─北宋西园雅集传奇”特展，图为宋代李公麟的〈丽人行〉。（台湾故宫提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月27日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾报导）2025台湾国立故宫博物院（故宫）百年院庆，集结宋代书画名品的“千年神遇─北宋西园雅集传奇”特展于10月开幕。</p>
<p>台湾故宫表示，人一生中能有几次千载难逢？大家都很幸运地遇到了第一个，就是故宫百年院庆；第二个绝对不能错过，乃是为了故宫建院百年及台北开馆一甲子特别精心规划的“千年神遇─北宋西园雅集传奇”特展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14644363" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644363" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644363-2511270655392378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14644363" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644363-2511270655392378.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="662" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644363" class="wp-caption-text">宋徽宗的〈文会图〉（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14644367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644367" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644367-2511270650322378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14644367" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644367-2511270650322378-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644367" class="wp-caption-text">宋代王诜的〈渔村小雪图〉（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“西园雅集”是千百年来文人梦寐以求的传奇，相传北宋年间，皇室驸马王诜曾于汴京西园举办一场风雅聚会，当时的文艺天才如苏轼、黄庭坚、李公麟、米芾等人皆为座上宾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14644358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644358" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644358-2511270700112378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14644358" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644358-2511270700112378-600x1597.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="1198" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644358" class="wp-caption-text">明代仇英的〈西园雅集图〉（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14644366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644366" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644366-2511270651372378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14644366" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644366-2511270651372378-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644366" class="wp-caption-text">宋代米芾的〈蜀素帖〉（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>后由李公麟绘图、米芾书文来纪念这场难得的“西园雅集”，尽管历代对此集会真假时有讨论，但并未阻碍时人对西园雅集的向往之情，千年来这场传说中的风雅韵事不断被传颂，成为历代雅集仿效的原型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14644359" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644359" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644359-2511270659112378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14644359" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644359-2511270659112378-600x387.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644359" class="wp-caption-text">李公麟的〈五马图〉局部（日本东京国立博物馆藏，台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14644360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644360" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644360-2511270658252378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14644360" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644360-2511270658252378-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644360" class="wp-caption-text">李公麟的〈五马图〉局部（日本东京国立博物馆藏，台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“千年神遇─北宋西园雅集传奇”特展为重现这场文艺盛事，展出多幅国宝级作品，包括现存苏轼〈赤壁赋〉文学名作中唯一的亲笔墨宝〈书前赤壁赋〉、黄庭坚传世行楷代表作〈自书松风阁诗〉、米芾神作〈蜀素帖〉等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14644361" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644361" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644361-2511270657352378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14644361" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644361-2511270657352378-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644361" class="wp-caption-text">宋代苏轼书写的〈前赤壁赋〉（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14644362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644362" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644362-2511270656362378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14644362" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644362-2511270656362378-600x414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644362" class="wp-caption-text">宋代苏轼书〈次韵三舍人省上诗〉。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，台湾故宫更向东京国立博物馆商借来李公麟的〈五马图〉与舒城李氏的〈潇湘卧游图〉及向法国赛努奇亚洲艺术博物馆借展李公麟的〈山庄图〉这3件传世名迹，让千年前的艺坛群星重聚故宫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14644364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644364" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644364-2511270654472378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14644364" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644364-2511270654472378-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644364" class="wp-caption-text">宋代黄庭坚书〈寒山子庞居士诗〉（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14644365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644365" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644365-2511270653322378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14644365" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644365-2511270653322378-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644365" class="wp-caption-text">宋代黄庭坚〈自书松风阁诗〉（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>值得一提的是失踪达八十年的〈五马图〉，于2019年才现身于世人眼前，首度海外展出即是到台北参与台湾故宫百年院庆展览。展览以三大单元带领观众重回名士云集的北宋年间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14644356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644356" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644356-2511270700572378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14644356" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644356-2511270700572378-600x276.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="276" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644356" class="wp-caption-text">潇湘卧游图（日本东京国立博物馆藏，台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一单元“西园雅集”呈现西园雅集在不同时代的延续与转化，展陈相关与会人士的书画作品，并囊括书斋逸趣、宴饮酬酢、耆英雅聚等主题，丰富观众对雅集图认识的视角。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14644368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14644368" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644368-2511270649202378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14644368" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644368-2511270649202378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="1149" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14644368" class="wp-caption-text">元赵孟頫西园雅集图。（台湾故宫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二单元“舒心畅意”呈现雅集中最富盛名的苏轼、黄庭坚、米芾、李公麟等人的作品，经过历史不断地淘选，他们的作品成为典范，无一不是融合诗文书画与性灵的杰作，其作品与文艺观开拓文人书画的舒心畅意新取径。</p>
<p>第三单元“百代典范”选展后人临仿这批北宋文艺精英的作品，具体呈现其历史上的重要地位及影响力以及不同时代对北宋大家典范风格的理解与诠释。</p>
<p>策展人、书画文献处处长何炎泉比喻说，西园雅集是一场由北宋第一偶像苏轼所导演的剧码，率领黄庭坚、李公麟、米芾三位主角担纲演出，加上其余12位魅力演员，组成历史上永远无法逾越的文艺天团与卡司阵容，让后世无数粉丝向往不已。</p>
<p>这股影响力也传播至韩国与日本，成为东亚文明史上最璀灿的一页。不管千年前的聚会真实与否，千年后他们的作品本尊即将聚首外双溪。他呼吁民众千万不能错过这千载难逢的文化飨宴，诚挚邀请您一起走进未来的传说中。</p>
