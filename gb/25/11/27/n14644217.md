<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>料理实境《星厨之战》引热议 带动主厨餐厅商机</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14644219-2511270347051487.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
