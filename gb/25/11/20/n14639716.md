<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>沿海小镇Albany房价年涨22% 创西澳偏远区新高</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月20日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">西澳沿海小镇</span><span lang="en-AU">Albany</span><span lang="zh-TW">的房地产市场持续火热，房价涨幅居西澳偏远地区首位、全国第二。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，房产分析机构</span><span lang="en-US">Cotality</span><span lang="zh-TW">的最新数据表明，</span><span lang="en-US">Albany</span><span lang="zh-TW">在过去</span><span lang="en-US">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">个月上涨</span><span lang="en-US">22.2%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。，中位价升至</span><span lang="en-US">69</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">8667</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。排在其后的分别是中西部地区（增长</span><span lang="en-US">19.6%</span><span lang="zh-TW">）和</span><span lang="en-US">Manjimup</span><span lang="zh-TW">（增长</span><span lang="en-US">15.5%</span><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">全国范围内，</span><span lang="en-US">Albany</span><span lang="zh-TW">的涨幅仅次于北领地达尔文郊区</span><span lang="en-US">Palmerston</span><span lang="zh-TW">的</span><span lang="en-US">22.3%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，位居第二。</span></p>
<p>报告指出，本轮涨幅主要集中在房市的“中低价位区间”。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">整体来看，西澳偏远区房地产市场在过去一个月的增长率居全澳地区市场之首，录得</span><span lang="en-US">1.8%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的涨幅。目前该市场已达历史高位，自</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">月降息以来上涨</span><span lang="en-US">9.6%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，过去</span><span lang="en-US">12</span><span lang="zh-TW">个月上涨</span><span lang="en-US">13.5%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，过去五年累计上涨</span><span lang="en-US">89.1%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">仅</span><span lang="en-US">Albany</span><span lang="zh-TW">一地，自</span><span lang="en-US">2020</span><span lang="zh-TW">年以来，房价就已上涨</span><span lang="en-US">92%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Cotality</span><span lang="zh-TW">研究总监劳莱斯（</span><span lang="en-US">Tim Lawless</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，澳大利亚整体房地产市场在</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">月上涨</span><span lang="en-US">1.1%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，主要得益于</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">月降息带来的市场动能。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">他说，</span><span lang="en-US">Albany</span><span lang="zh-TW">的房屋销售量目前比去年同期高出</span><span lang="en-US">7.5%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，比十年平均水平高出</span><span lang="en-US">30%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>“自<span lang="en-US">2022</span>年年中以来，<span lang="en-US">Albany</span>的年度房价增长速度一直高于全国平均水平，这与加息周期的开始时间大致一致<span lang="en-US">.</span>”劳莱斯表示。</p>
<p>“<span lang="en-US">Albany</span>相对可负担的房价，可能是支撑市场需求的关键因素之一。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“尽管过去五年房价已上涨</span><span lang="en-US">92%</span><span lang="zh-TW">（超过珀斯的</span><span lang="en-US">84%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，几乎是全国平均增幅</span><span lang="en-US">47%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的两倍），但目前的房产中位价仍仅为</span><span lang="en-US">69</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">8667</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“与珀斯（</span><span lang="en-US">88</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">4471</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）或悉尼（</span><span lang="en-US">125</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">6156</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元）相比，</span><span lang="en-US">Albany</span><span lang="zh-TW">的房价依然具有极高吸引力，特别是对那些不必居住在首府城市的购房者而言。”</span></p>
<p>不过，劳莱斯指出，这一涨势也带来了一定隐忧。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“与此同时，房源供应量创下新低。</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">月在售房源仅</span><span lang="en-US">284</span><span lang="zh-TW">套，较去年同期下降</span><span lang="en-US">34%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，比十年平均水平低</span><span lang="en-US">73%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>“在如此紧缺的供应环境下，买家普遍感到焦虑。房屋在市场上的中位售出时间仅<span lang="en-US">14</span>天，卖家在竞争有限的情况下享受着极强的议价优势。”</p>
