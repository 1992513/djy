<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳所有能源零售商多收客户费用</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月20日讯】（大纪元记者林达澳洲珀斯编译报导）能源监管局（<span lang="en-US">ERA</span>）最近调查发现，过去两年，西澳全部七家能源零售商均向数以千计客户多收了费用。多收费用的原因是儅客户关闭了能源账户后，但是这些零售商继续从关闭账户中自动扣款。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据“</span><span lang="en-US">9</span><span lang="zh-TW">号台”新闻网站报导，自</span><span lang="en-US">2023</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">月以来，西澳政府拥有的能源零售与发电商</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">已从</span><span lang="en-US">16,000</span><span lang="zh-TW">名客户那里多收取了近</span><span lang="en-US">360</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的款项。</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">月披露的信息还显示，自</span><span lang="en-US">2009</span><span lang="zh-TW">年以来，</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">已从</span><span lang="en-US">17.4</span><span lang="zh-TW">万名客户那里多收取了</span><span lang="en-US">4000</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p>尽管能源监管局（<span lang="en-US">ERA</span>）的调查主要集中在<span lang="en-US">Synergy</span>上，但也向其它电力和天然气供应商索取了相关数据。其中电力供应商的行业规范于<span lang="en-US">2023</span>年<span lang="en-US">2</span>月实施，天然气供应商的规范则在<span lang="en-US">2024</span>年实施。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">数据显示，自这些规范生效以来，</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">仍是违规最严重的公司，其次是</span><span lang="en-US">Horizon Power</span><span lang="zh-TW">，客户被多收了</span><span lang="en-US">21.3</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元；</span><span lang="en-US">Kleenheat</span><span lang="zh-TW">客户被多收了</span><span lang="en-US">17.5</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元；</span><span lang="en-US">Alinta Energy</span><span lang="zh-TW">客户被多收了</span><span lang="en-US">15.5</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元；</span><span lang="en-US">AGL Sales</span><span lang="zh-TW">客户被多收了</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">3408</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元</span><span lang="zh-TW">；</span><span lang="en-US">Origin Energy</span><span lang="zh-TW">客户被多收了</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">3098</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元</span><span lang="zh-TW">；</span><span lang="en-US">ENGIE</span><span lang="zh-TW">的客户被多收了</span><span lang="en-US">1</span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US">2018</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">结果显示，</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">是唯一一家收到违规通知（</span><span lang="en-US">breach notice</span><span lang="zh-TW">）的公司。</span><span lang="en-US">Synergy</span><span lang="zh-TW">必须在明年四月底前向客户退款，否则将罚款</span><span lang="en-US">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。但调查发现，其它六家供应商已建立相关程序，用于识别并退还已关闭账户的多收款项。</span></p>
<p>州长库克称这种情况非常严重，并表示政府对此异常愤怒。</p>
<p>库克说：“一旦发现错误，我们一定会加以纠正，毫不留情，并会坚持这么做。”</p>
