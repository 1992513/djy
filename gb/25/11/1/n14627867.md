<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北美招牌饮食：10道美国特色的标志性菜肴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627868-shutterstock_1733027573-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美国饮食融和了来自世界各地的风味，但也有土生土长的特色美食。（Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月02日讯】（大纪元记者夏瑜编译报导）饮食不仅仅提供身体必需的能量，更是一个国家文化、历史和身份认同的一部分。虽然许多深受美国人喜爱的菜肴源自全球，但也有一些令人惊喜的食谱诞生于美国本土。“美食乐趣”网站介绍了10道标志性的菜肴，它们体现了十足的美国特色。</p>
<h3>1. 美式芝士汉堡（The All-American Cheeseburger）</h3>
<p>很少有食物能像芝士汉堡一样象征美国。无论是经典的三层塔，还是堆满各种配料的进阶版本，多汁的牛肉饼、入口即化的芝士、松软的面包，都是无可比拟的美味。最适合在后院野餐或你最喜欢的当地餐厅享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627870-1_shutterstock_2678929679-e1762037339858.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627870-1_shutterstock_2678929679-e1762037339858.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627870" class="wp-caption-text">美式芝士汉堡（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>2. 芝士通心粉（Mac and Cheese）</h3>
<p>芝士通心粉香滑浓稠，且有无限的定制选择，在美国可谓“根基深厚”，尤其是在美国第三任总统托马斯．杰斐逊推广之后。他在参访法国巴黎期间迷恋上它，不可自拔。如今，它是工作日晚的正餐主食，也是节庆假期的暖心美点。试试裹上面包屑烘烤，口感更香脆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627871" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627871-2_shutterstock_2298557017-e1762037394677.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627871-2_shutterstock_2298557017-e1762037394677.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627871" class="wp-caption-text">芝士通心粉（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>3. 南方炸鸡（Southern Fried Chicken）</h3>
<p>金黄酥脆、香气入骨，炸鸡是真正的美式经典菜肴。它的起源可以追溯到美国南方，那里的白脱奶卤汁和香料秘方世代相传。搭配饼干、肉汁、凉拌卷心菜，堪称完美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627872" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627872-3_shutterstock_1885543711-e1762037467762.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627872-3_shutterstock_1885543711-e1762037467762.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627872" class="wp-caption-text">南方炸鸡（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>4. 新英格兰蛤蜊浓汤（New England Clam Chowder）</h3>
<p>这款浓汤口感醇厚，奶油味浓郁，蛤蜊和土豆满满当当，乃是美国东北风味的代表。它起源于新英格兰海岸，几个世纪以来一直是温暖人心的美味。最好坐在海边，拂着海风，面包为碗，悠然享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627873" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627873-4_shutterstock_2620277129-e1762037570950.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627873-4_shutterstock_2620277129-e1762037570950.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627873" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰蛤蜊浓汤（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>5. 德州烧烤（Texas BBQ）</h3>
<p>德州的烧烤不仅仅是美食，更是一份传承。从烟熏牛腩到多汁肋排，秘诀在于数小时的慢火熏烤和精心调制的酱料。搭配玉米面包、腌菜，以及──大把的餐巾纸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627874" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627874-5_shutterstock_2262789629-e1762037670771.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627874-5_shutterstock_2262789629-e1762037670771.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627874" class="wp-caption-text">德州烧烤（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>6. 布法罗鸡翅（Buffalo Wings）</h3>
<p>这款香辣鸡翅于1964年诞生于纽约州布法罗，随后迅速风靡全国。淋上黄油辣酱，佐以芹菜和蓝纹奶酪，绝对是比赛日的必备佳肴。蘸着牧场沙拉酱，感受现代风味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627875-6_shutterstock_1176069616-e1762037729192.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627875-6_shutterstock_1176069616-e1762037729192.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627875" class="wp-caption-text">布法罗鸡翅（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>7. 考伯沙拉（Cobb Salad）</h3>
<p>这道丰盛的沙拉出自20世纪30年代洛杉矶著名的布朗德比餐厅（Brown Derby restaurant），包含鸡肉、培根、牛油果、蓝纹奶酪和鸡蛋，证明了沙拉也可以如此可口。如果你想要清淡又饱腹的佳肴，这道沙拉绝对是不二之选。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627876-7_shutterstock_1921604333-e1762037799262.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627876-7_shutterstock_1921604333-e1762037799262.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627876" class="wp-caption-text">考伯沙拉（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>8. 玉米面包（Cornbread）</h3>
<p>玉米面包是美洲原住民和南方烹饪的主食，独具美国风味。不管甜、咸，无论是煎锅烤制还是松饼制作，它都是烧烤或炸鸡的终极搭档。抹上蜂蜜黄油，更能增添真正的南方特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627877" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627877-8_shutterstock_570457096-e1762037853885.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627877-8_shutterstock_570457096-e1762037853885.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627877" class="wp-caption-text">玉米面包（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>9. 费城芝士牛排三明治（Philly Cheesesteak）</h3>
<p>这款三明治于20世纪30年代的费城首次亮相，将薄切牛肉、浓香芝士、辣甜洋葱夹在柔软的长形面包里。美国东海岸经典，在正店或小吃摊都是热门菜色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627878-9_shutterstock_2141482527-e1762037917119.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627878-9_shutterstock_2141482527-e1762037917119.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627878" class="wp-caption-text">费城芝士牛排三明治（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>10. 纽约芝士蛋糕（New York Cheesecake）</h3>
<p>纽约芝士蛋糕香醇浓郁，丝滑绵密，名符其实的“甜点之王”。它于曼哈顿横空出世，以奶油芝士为主料，每一口都令人沉醉其中。冷藏后风味尤佳。 ◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627879" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627879-10_shutterstock_1640333968-e1762037984509.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14627879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627879-10_shutterstock_1640333968-e1762037984509.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627879" class="wp-caption-text">纽约芝士蛋糕（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
