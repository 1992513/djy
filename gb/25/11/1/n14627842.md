<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国际象棋世界杯：璀璨开幕 点亮智力之战</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627843-WC-2025-Opening-FRONT-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025年10月31日，国际象棋世界杯在印度果阿举行开幕式。各位贵宾和印度政要揭幕世界杯冠军奖杯。（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月02日讯】（大纪元记者叶诚允报导）10月31日，万众瞩目的2025FIDE世界杯在印度果阿正式上演。开幕典礼在Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee体育馆盛大举行，历时两个半小时，气氛热烈隆重，为这项全球最高级别的棋坛盛事揭开序幕。<br />
</p>
<p><center><a title="YouTube video player" loading="lazy" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://db1uzmukbk5lb.cloudfront.net/S5bv3MHn5xKg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/rymg9zuJXqg?si=t1viShpxnxxcDAF1" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center></p>
<p>来自全球八十多个国家的206位顶尖棋手将在未来数周展开激烈角逐，争夺总额高达200万美元的奖金，以及三张通往2026年世界冠军候选人赛的珍贵门票。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627850-WC-2025-Opening-Anand-Trophy-01.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627850-WC-2025-Opening-Anand-Trophy-01-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627850" class="wp-caption-text">精美的象征激情、精准和荣耀的黄铜镀金世界杯冠军奖杯。该奖杯以印度传奇棋王“维斯瓦纳坦·阿南德”命名。（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场超过千人齐聚一堂。出席典礼的贵宾包括：印度青年与体育部部长Dr. Mansukh Mandaviya、果阿邦首席部长Dr. Pramod Sawant、果阿邦体育与文化部长Dr. Ramesh Tawadkar、FIDE主席阿尔卡季·德沃尔科维奇（Arkady Dvorkovich），以及印度国际象棋协会主席尼廷·纳朗（Nitin Narang）等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627844-WC-2025-Opening-FRONT.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627844-WC-2025-Opening-FRONT-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627844" class="wp-caption-text">观众起立，全场奏响印度国歌。（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在庄严的印度国歌声中，政要与贵宾共同完成象征光明与祝福的“点灯仪式”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627849-WC-2025-2025-Lighting-Lamp-01.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627849-WC-2025-2025-Lighting-Lamp-01-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627849" class="wp-caption-text">印度文化中独特的“点灯仪式”。（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼上播放了主题影片《The Spirit and Story of Chess》，展现国际象棋的历史与精神，并以“在战术与意志同步交会之时，冠军就会诞生”作结，赢得全场掌声。</p>
<p>在致词环节，印度总理莫迪（Narendra Modi）透过录音向选手致意，期望赛事能激励更多年轻人投入这项运动，并宣布2025年国际象棋世界杯正式开幕。他强调：“世界杯不仅是竞技舞台，更是启发年轻世代追梦的契机。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627846" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627846-WC-2025-OPening-Modi-Message.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627846-WC-2025-OPening-Modi-Message-600x390.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627846" class="wp-caption-text">印度国际象棋协会主席尼廷·纳朗先生宣读了印度总理莫迪发来的录音致辞。（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国际棋联（FIDE）主席阿尔卡季·德沃尔科维奇则表示，“果阿作为这次国际象棋的竞技场，传奇在此铸就，新的冠军在此诞生。”</p>
<p>最后他祝愿所有参赛者好运，公平竞赛，并补充道：“虽然我们尚未知晓谁将夺冠，但有两件事可以确定——这将是一场精彩绝伦的盛事；而选手们也将体验到无与伦比的印度式热情款待。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627851" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627851-WC-2025-Opening-Dvorkovich.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627851-WC-2025-Opening-Dvorkovich-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627851" class="wp-caption-text">FIDE主席阿尔卡季·德沃尔科维奇谈到，206名参赛棋手中，只有三人能够获得2026年候选人赛的参赛资格，而候选人赛是通往世界冠军赛的必经之路。（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后，开幕仪式的歌舞表演是印度传统与现代艺术相结合，多位音乐与影视界名人带来震撼演出，结合舞蹈、灯光与音乐，展现果阿的多彩文化与热情活力。</p>
<p>Hormuzd Khambata舞蹈团以绚烂舞姿呈现果阿文化率先登场，接着由歌手Hema Sardesai演唱主题曲《The Spirit of Goa》，现场气氛达到高潮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627848-WC-2025-Opening-Dance.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627848-WC-2025-Opening-Dance-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627848" class="wp-caption-text">印度Hormuzd Khambata舞蹈团。（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</figcaption></figure>
<p>压轴登场的著名印度歌星乌莎·乌图普（Usha Uthup），则以经典曲目《Skyfall》为这场盛大的开幕仪式画下华丽句点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627845-WC-2025-OPening-Uthup.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627845-WC-2025-OPening-Uthup-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627845" class="wp-caption-text">曾获有印度电影奥斯卡奖之称的“Filmfare Awards”的歌星乌莎·乌图普压轴登场。（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开幕仪式结束，举行抽签仪式。本届唯一参赛女选手——刚夺得女子世界杯冠军的印度国际象棋特级大师迪芙亚·德什穆克（Divya Deshmukh），登台为本届赛事头号种子、印度名将古克什（Gukesh D）抽到“黑棋”，确定所有奇数编号选手首轮都将持黑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627847" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627847-WC-2025-Opening-Divya.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627847-WC-2025-Opening-Divya-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627847" class="wp-caption-text">印度女子国际象棋特级大师Divya Deshmukh（中）表示：“能在家乡为祖国而战，格外荣幸，更是前进的动力。”（Credit: Michal Walusza/FIDE World Cup Goa 2025）</figcaption></figure>
