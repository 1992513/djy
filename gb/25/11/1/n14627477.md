<h6>2026 Toyota Prius Plug-in Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月01日讯】在2026年的混动车市场，Toyota Prius Plug-in Hybrid（简称PHEV）以其前卫设计、卓越效率和令人惊喜的驾驶体验，继续引领潮流。作为第五代Prius的最新力作，它不仅继承了品牌的环保基因，还融入了现代感和实用性，起售价约$37,500（包括目的地费）。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d3qxeoyqwzrtyq.cloudfront.net/U3gBOKKQEahc"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/0zGiYF-bX5o?si=YbjOFkVsu_6zGz_B" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>特别版如Nightshade Edition为追求个性的用户提供独特选择，但核心魅力仍在于这款车本身的出色表现。如果你想要一辆兼顾省油、舒适和科技的日常座驾，2026 Prius PHEV无疑是首选。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627478-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627478-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627478" class="wp-caption-text">2026 Toyota Prius Plug-in Hybrid。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第五代Prius以流线型车身和锐利线条重新定义了混动车的外观，彻底摆脱了“环保车=乏味”的刻板印象。由于转向半径小，停车和掉头轻松自如。空气动力学系数低至0.24Cd，不仅提升燃油效率，还赋予车辆优雅姿态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627480-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627480-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_08-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627480" class="wp-caption-text">锐利线条配头灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026年款Prius新增Nightshade Edition，是基于XSE车型，加入大量黑化元素，包括黑色车标、门槛饰条、门把手与保险杆饰件，营造出运动氛围。其19英寸黑色铝合金轮毂进一步凸显力量感，测试车颜色是Karashi，让Prius在优雅之外，多了一份锋芒与个性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627481-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_10.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627481-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627481" class="wp-caption-text">黑色轮毂是Nightshade Edition的配件。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Prius PHEV的内饰简约而精致，XSE等级即配备SofTex合成皮革座椅，提供前排加热功能、驾驶座8向电动调节，并搭载8英寸Toyota Audio Multimedia触控萤幕，操作流畅，支持无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto。升级至XSE Premium，可进一步获得12.3英寸大萤幕、前排乘客座位8向电动调节与前排座位通风功能，以及JBL高级音响系统，带来更豪华的体验。后排空间适合两人长途乘坐，腿部和头部余裕满足日常需求，整体兼顾舒适性与实用性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627482" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627482" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627482-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_31.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627482-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627482" class="wp-caption-text">Prius内装。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>20.3立方英尺后备箱可容纳周末购物或运动装备；前排杯架和中控拉出式托盘提升实用性。部分车型提供双层玻璃，能进一步提升静谧性。高配还可选装太阳能车顶，为空调等系统提供补充电力。整体而言，内饰科技感与舒适度兼得，适合城市通勤和长途旅行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627485-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627485-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627485" class="wp-caption-text">20.3立方英尺后备箱。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026 Prius PHEV搭载第五代Toyota Hybrid System，结合2升四缸发动机和双电动机，总输出220匹马力，匹配ECVT无级变速箱，0-60英里／时加速约6.6秒，加速平顺而敏捷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627486-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627486-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627486" class="wp-caption-text">第五代Toyota Hybrid System。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纯电续航是亮点，39英里纯电续航，覆盖大部分通勤场景；使用家用墙插或Level 2充电器，约2.5小时充满。混动模式下，综合油耗达48 MPG（Nightshade Edition），或114 MPGe（电+油）。部分媒体实测显示，高速巡航下EV模式效率仍然可观，混动油耗约47 MPG。13.6 kWh电池置于后轴下方，不占用行李空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627487-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_24.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627487-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627487" class="wp-caption-text">小尺寸仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Prius PHEV悬挂调校偏运动，低重心让弯道表现自信；方向盘反馈精准，转向轻盈。EV模式下加速安静顺滑，混动模式下发动机介入自然。城市拥堵中，Traffic Jam Assist可实现低速跟车和转向辅助，减轻驾驶疲劳。NVH（噪音、振动、粗糙度）控制达到同级领先水准，即便选装19英寸轮毂，路噪也仅有轻微增加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627489-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627489-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627489" class="wp-caption-text">12.3英寸大萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全配置全面，Toyota Safety Sense 3.0标配，包括前撞预警、行人/骑行者检测、自动刹车、自适应巡航、交通标志识别、盲点监测、后方横穿警报、车道保持/偏离预警、自动远光和拥堵辅助。可选Advanced Park自动泊车让停车更轻松。碰撞测试表现优异，获得IIHS Top Safety Pick+和NHTSA五星评级。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627490-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_28.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627490-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627490" class="wp-caption-text">回弹式变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2026 Toyota Prius Plug-in Hybrid Nightshade Edition以 $37,795 的售价，带来39英里纯电续航、最高50 MPG 混动效率和出色的驾驶体验。即便没有家用充电桩，它依然能提供优异的油耗表现和日常便利性。虽然后排空间稍显紧凑，但对于城市驾驶者而言，这是一款兼顾环保、舒适与驾驶乐趣的理想座驾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14627512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14627512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627512-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14627512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14627512-2026_Toyota_Prius_Prime_XSE_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14627512" class="wp-caption-text">前座空间大。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
