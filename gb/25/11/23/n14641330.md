<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>BU台湾学生会复会 携哈佛举办感恩节餐会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641323-DSC05038-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>11月22日，波士顿大学（BU）台湾学生会携哈佛大学台湾学生会举办感恩节餐会，70多人参加。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月23日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）值感恩节前夕，今年刚复会的波士顿大学（BU）台湾学生会联合哈佛台湾学生会及新英格兰台学联，于11月22日下午举办餐会，为当地台湾学生、学者们带来欢乐。</p>
<p>这场餐会在哈佛大学Lehman Hall举办，有来自哈佛、BU、麻州大学（UMass）等多所学校的学生参与，并有台湾龙舟队等友好团体加入，总计70多人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14641318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641318-DSC05004.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641318-DSC05004-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641318" class="wp-caption-text">参与者在哈佛大学Lehman Hall报到。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14641324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641324-DSC05044.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641324-DSC05044-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641324" class="wp-caption-text">同学们乐享美食。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场有私厨企业“Chop Chop Eats”提供的烤鸡和其它美食，香飘满屋；也有“饭团外卖”赞助的珍珠奶茶，备受欢迎。主办方设计了波士顿趣闻抢答游戏，话题涵盖BU吉祥物、波士顿篮球队、棒球队等诸多方面。同学们争相举手抢答，非常热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14641321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641321-DSC05011.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641321-DSC05011-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641321" class="wp-caption-text">同学们领取抽奖券。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14641326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641326" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641326-DSC05046.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641326-DSC05046-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641326" class="wp-caption-text">主持人介绍抢答游戏规则。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14641327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641327" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641327-DSC05048.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641327-DSC05048-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641327" class="wp-caption-text">活动参与者们热闹抢答。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>气氛最热烈的环节，还是最后的抽奖。BU学生会会长叶宇恺捐出一辆自行车作为一等奖，其它还有二等奖JBL耳机，以及三等奖保温水壶。大家纷纷为获奖者鼓掌，欢笑声不绝于耳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14641328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641328-DSC05065.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641328-DSC05065-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641328" class="wp-caption-text">一等奖自行车的中奖者（右）得到掌声。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14641320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641320-DSC05010.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641320-DSC05010-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641320" class="wp-caption-text">现场有饭团外卖赞助的奶茶。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><strong>BU台湾学生会疫情后中断 复会后首办大型活动</strong></h2>
<p>据BU电脑科学系研究生叶宇恺介绍，BU台湾学生会在2020年疫情爆发后就中断了，4年都没有举办活动。直到今年，这个学生会才由叶宇恺、黄景轩等8名热心同学恢复。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14641329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641329-DSC05067.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641329-DSC05067-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641329" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿大学台湾学生会8位干部合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>叶宇恺说，他参加过哈佛、MIT等各校台湾学生会的活动，深受启发；而且想到BU是当地台湾学生最多的学校之一，台湾学生数量排该校留学生来源国第5名；因而希望恢复BU的学生会。在这过程中，他们也得到驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处的鼓励，以及新英格兰台湾同学会联合会的支持。</p>
<p>他说，本次感恩节餐会，是BU台湾学生会复会之后的第一个大活动。他希望以后继续与各校同学联谊，而不是仅限于波士顿大学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14641319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641319-DSC05006.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641319-DSC05006-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641319" class="wp-caption-text">左起：BU台湾学生会会长黄景轩、叶宇恺、哈佛台湾学生会会长李典璋。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>兼任哈佛台湾学生会和新英格兰台学联会长的李典璋也参与筹备这次餐会。他向同学们表达节日祝福，欢迎同学们参与12月22日的学联圣诞节聚餐，也呼吁哈佛的同学们参加12月的学生会干部改选。</p>
<p>“非常非常高兴这个BU TSA在今年的时候重磅回归！我们非常希望跟BU学生会一起举办更多有趣的活动给同学们。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14641322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14641322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641322-DSC05024.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14641322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14641322-DSC05024-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14641322" class="wp-caption-text">台北市市长蒋万安发来视频祝福（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，波士顿大学台湾校友会会长Jack，以及台北市市长蒋万安，都发来了视频祝福。蒋万安谈到台北市和波士顿市的“姊妹市”友谊，也鼓励大家通过努力与成就，让世界看到台湾人的自信和勇敢。◇</p>
