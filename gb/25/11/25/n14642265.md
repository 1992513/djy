<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进鼻头角“吉他洞”专业带路的隐密海岸行程</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642268-811318-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>海蚀平台边缘的突出岩层，下方的浪花持续拍打，岩面布满被侵蚀出的刻痕。（洪煮刃提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新北报导）新北瑞芳鼻头角的观景平台大家都去过，但藏在海岸线底下的那串海蚀洞与岩缝，大多数人一生一次也未必遇上。登山专业领队洪煮刃带着友人沿着海岸线前进，顺着退潮的短短窗口走进岩壁深处，拍下许多平常看不到的画面，巨石相互挤压、海水从裂缝底部涌上、头顶的落石像被卡住一样悬在半空。那些画面真实地提醒：这不是一般步道，而是一段需要经验带路的地形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14642271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642271" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642271-811321.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642271-811321-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642271" class="wp-caption-text">从深缝往外拍，两侧岩壁高耸压迫，外面涌动的海面刚好从狭口露出。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14642275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642275" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642275-811325.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642275-811325-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642275" class="wp-caption-text">登山口，全队在阶梯前合照，为这段海岸岩缝与洞穴的整段探勘做纪念。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>大岩壁之间的入口? 海蚀缝从脚下开始</h2>
<p>队员站在巨岩之间，必须用双手扶住岩壁保持平衡。脚下的石面并不平整，有些甚至是海浪长年冲蚀造成的碎裂。这里不是自然形成的步道，也不是游客区能看到的景象，而是一条“海蚀裂隙”，海水沿着岩层的弱线反复进出，把缝隙越削越深，最后变成肉眼可见的通道。</p>
<p>这种地形有个共同点：看起来稳，其实很多地方是中空的。洪煮刃也不断提醒，不熟路况不能乱走，脚踩的每一块石头都可能不是原生地形，而是多年崩落堆起的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14642276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642276" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642276-811326.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642276-811326-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642276" class="wp-caption-text">海蚀的节理非常明显与壮观。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14642270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642270" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642270-811320.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642270-811320-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642270" class="wp-caption-text">岩层之间形成天然通道，崩落石堆满整个缝隙，前方有一道微弱的海光。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海蚀的节理非常明显与壮观。（洪煮刃提供）岩层之间形成天然通道，崩落石堆满整个缝隙，前方有一道微弱的海光。（洪煮刃提供）</p>
<h2>洞室里的亮光? 海浪切出来的“天窗”</h2>
<p>其中几张照片，是从洞里往外拍的。队员站成剪影，前方是一个朝向海面的开口。洞内的岩层呈现整齐的水平纹路，那是这片海岸特有的砂岩结构，被海浪从侧边慢慢削出深度。</p>
<p>洞口上方的开口，是海蚀洞常见的结构，像是被挖空的“天窗”。海浪从底部推进、岩壁从内部削弱，上方岩层承受不了重量时会坍落，最后就形成一个透光的洞。</p>
<p>这个角度也说明：能不能进洞，完全要看潮。浪不小，如果潮再高一点，整段通道会直接被浪封住。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14642266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642266" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642266-811316.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642266-811316-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642266" class="wp-caption-text">新北鼻头角被海水长年削出的不规则岩壁。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>海蚀平台的纹路? 真实的“海浪刻痕”</h2>
<p>走出洞口后的海蚀平台，从照片上可以看出像龟裂般的纹路，那不是人工，而是浪与砂粒长年刻出的线。在地质学中叫“海蚀沟”与“海蚀凹穴”，常出现在砂岩或泥岩的沿海。可惜的是，这种平台表面常带有湿气与海藻，看起来平坦，实际上滑得很。</p>
<p>洪煮刃拍摄的画面中，海浪在旁边拍岸，退潮线非常明显，提醒走的人：海浪能到的地方，就不算安全地面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14642277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642277" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642277-811327.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642277-811327-600x1257.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="943" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642277" class="wp-caption-text">洪煮刃表示第一次看到这样的地质，太震撼了。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>往下望的裂口? 海水像在呼吸</h2>
<p>有一张俯拍的照片，岩壁裂缝中海水不断往上冲，看起来像在喘息，那是典型的海蚀沟槽。海底若有连通的通道，浪一大，整股力道就会沿着裂缝往上送。</p>
<p>这也是这一路最危险的地形之一：掉下去不是落地，是直接掉进浪里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14642269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642269" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642269-811319.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642269-811319-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="603" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642269" class="wp-caption-text">俯视海蚀缝，海水顺着狭窄裂口往上冲，强度能从白浪看得出来。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14642284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642284" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642284-811334.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642284-811334-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642284" class="wp-caption-text">洪煮刃也不断提醒，不熟路况不能乱走，脚踩的每一块石头都可能不是原生地形。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>岩缝越走越窄? 落石就在头上</h2>
<p>另一段则是全队穿越狭窄的缝。上方横躺着一块大石，像被卡住一般，但其实那是坍塌后刚好被夹住的“夹持型崩块”。这种地方的特点是：</p>
<p>石头看似稳固，其实没有固定</p>
<p>下方碎石表示此处曾经掉落过</p>
<h2>任何震动都可能让碎石位移</h2>
<p>洪煮刃带着队员一个一个通过，也是依照地形判断风险，不是“能走就走”，而是“确认安全再走”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14642285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642285" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642285-811335.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642285-811335-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642285" class="wp-caption-text">这里不是自然形成的步道，也不是游客区能看到的景象，而是一条“海蚀裂隙”。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14642279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642279-811329.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642279-811329-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642279" class="wp-caption-text">这是一段对地形要求高、必须靠专业领队带路的海蚀洞体验。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>最关键的一段 ? 要靠绳索上下的陡坡</h2>
<p>洪煮刃抓着粗绳下降，是这段路最具有挑战性的部分。绳子上段绑在岩块上，看得出绳子经过海风与潮气的长期磨损。这里没有固定设备，一切都靠当地山友与领队的经验维持。</p>
<p>一般人看到绳子会以为是保护，但洪煮刃很清楚：绳子只是辅助，真正重要的是判断重心与踩点。一旦脚没踩好，就是往海边滑下去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14642274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642274" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642274-811324.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642274-811324-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642274" class="wp-caption-text">洪煮刃抓着固定绳索移动，粗绳绑在突出岩块上，是此段必须依靠的安全点。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14642280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642280" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642280-811330.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642280-811330-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642280" class="wp-caption-text">团员对这个海蚀洞穴很有兴趣，忍不住坐上去拍照留念。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>真正走出来时? 才感受到海浪的声音</h2>
<p>最后一张洞口照片，看出去是湛蓝的海。站在洞里看海，跟站在岸边看海完全不同，海浪声在岩缝中被放得更大，甚至能感到气流从洞口往里面推。</p>
<p>这样的景象不常见，是因为要看见它，必须同时符合：</p>
<p>要退潮</p>
<p>要天气稳定</p>
<p>要知道从哪个裂缝能进</p>
<p>还要有人带</p>
<p>这些“条件都刚好”的时候，才走得到。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14642283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642283" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642283-811333.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642283-811333-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642283" class="wp-caption-text">鼻头角灯塔景色相当美丽。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14642267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14642267" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642267-811317.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14642267" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14642267-811317-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14642267" class="wp-caption-text">从洞里望向海面，队员站在巨石顶端成剪影，外面的亮光照出海蚀洞的深度。（洪煮刃提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>不是景点? 是一段靠判断力才能抵达的密境</h2>
<p>大家站在鼻头角的阶梯前，看得出走完后的放松与成就感。这不是观光导览，也不是一般步道，而是一段对地形要求高、必须靠专业领队带路的海蚀洞体验。</p>
<p>鼻头角的这些岩缝、海蚀洞与海蚀平台，是海岸地质的真实缩影。但它的危险也是真实的，浪、落石、潮汐、湿滑，每一项都不容大意。洪煮刃一路细心带队，也用照片提醒大家：有些地方不是不能去，而是要知道怎么去、何时去、跟谁去。</p>
<p>这也是海岸密境最珍贵的地方。</p>
