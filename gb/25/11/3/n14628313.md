<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>香烤马铃薯料理 全家人都爱吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628318-76ece8f137bb02b21c25631f7a168d03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>洋葱红萝卜起司薯丝派。（王知涵／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>马铃薯是西方社会的主食之一，有饱足感，重要性就如同东方社会的米饭，因此衍生出各式各样的马铃薯料理。尤其是烤马铃薯，更是年轻族群的最爱。</p>
<p>其实，香烤马铃薯料理有多种做法，最常见的是将马铃薯切块或整颗烤熟，过程中可先水煮、油炸或直接烘烤，再加入盐、胡椒、香草或起司等调味料，烤至外酥内软即可食用。</p>
<p>现在介绍简单制作两道香烤马铃薯料理－－洋葱红萝卜起司薯丝派及火腿起司薯泥，一次可以做出一大盘，让全家人都吃个够。</p>
<h3><span style="color: blue;">第一道︰洋葱红萝卜起司薯丝派</span></h3>
<p><strong>食材：</strong><br />
马铃薯去皮后500g（大约2颗）<br />
红萝卜50g<br />
洋葱50g<br />
蛋1颗<br />
黄色切达起司片60g（3片）<br />
玉米粉60g</p>
<p><strong>调味料：</strong><br />
糖5g<br />
盐2.5g<br />
黑胡椒粉 少许<br />
奶油20g</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628326" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628326-88bae12dcd4441f919e1a657fa35f2c9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628326-88bae12dcd4441f919e1a657fa35f2c9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628326" class="wp-caption-text">所有食材一起拌匀。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14628327" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628327" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628327-9a08524ff5943640b5e1e6f64fb226ef.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628327-9a08524ff5943640b5e1e6f64fb226ef-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628327" class="wp-caption-text">烤模抹油或放烘焙纸，然后把薯丝糊放入烤模中。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>做法：</strong><br />
1. 马铃薯去皮洗净，然后刨丝放入密闭容器防氧化，因为氧化后会变色不美观。</p>
<p>2. 红萝卜刨丝，洋葱切成小片，黄色切达起司片撕成小片。</p>
<p>3. 所有食材加调味料拌匀。</p>
<p>4. 烤模抹油或上烘焙纸（方便成品脱模），把薯丝糊放入烤模。</p>
<p>5. 烤箱170℃烤50分就完成了，食用时沾蕃茄酱会更美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628323-b423d7dee84d982c2430e3002b9a9282.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628323-b423d7dee84d982c2430e3002b9a9282-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628323" class="wp-caption-text">火腿起司薯泥。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: blue;">第二道︰火腿起司薯泥</span></h3>
马铃薯去皮后740g（大约3颗）<br />
牛奶20g<br />
火腿片 3片<br />
料理起司丝 60g<br />
起司粉15g</p>
盐1.5g<br />
黑胡椒粉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628321-f25f8b0bc820faf0692b108f329eb5d1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628321-f25f8b0bc820faf0692b108f329eb5d1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628321" class="wp-caption-text">所有食材拌匀，火腿片和起司丝各放三分之二量。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
1. 马铃薯去皮洗净切小块，然后放入电锅蒸熟，外锅要放1量米杯的水。</p>
<p>2. 火腿片切小片。</p>
<p>3. 蒸熟的马铃薯趁热压成泥。</p>
<p>4. 将薯泥、牛奶、起司粉，以及2/3火腿片及40g起司丝，还有盐、黑胡椒粉一起拌匀。</p>
<p>5. 烤模抹油或上烘焙纸（方便成品脱模），把薯泥放入烤模，用汤匙压平整型。</p>
<p>6. 表面铺上1/3火腿片和20g起司丝。</p>
<p>7. 烤箱170℃烤20分钟就完成了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14628322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14628322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628322-627e9f053759871ce80149202a0a727e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14628322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14628322-627e9f053759871ce80149202a0a727e-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14628322" class="wp-caption-text">表层放火腿片和起司丝各三分之一量。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
