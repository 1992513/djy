<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>任贤齐力挺家乡赛事 领军白安等群星登台献唱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14632305-2511090205501487.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />

nothing》、《爱你的馀地》与《下天堂》三首新作品，并在现场宣布将于12月推出首张专辑《下天堂》。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px
