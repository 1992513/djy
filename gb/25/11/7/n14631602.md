<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>营养师推荐Costco十种食材 轻松搞定一天饭菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14331232-8_shutterstock_753873151-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>家庭示意图。(Shutterstock)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月08日讯】（大纪元记者夏雨综合报导）美国仓储式连锁店Costco（好市多）一向出售大包装食品和日常杂货，是人们囤货的采购地。另一方面，Costco也有丰富多彩的预制食品、冷冻食品和罐装食品，可帮助繁忙上班族家庭快速准备日常餐食。</p>
<p>注册营养师Maxine Yeung在“商业内幕”刊文，分享了Costco出售的食材如何帮助她打理一家人饮食的经验。她是两个蹒跚学步孩子的妈妈，且并不喜欢计划和准备饭菜。</p>
<p>她在文章中写道，尽管如此，自己仍然会尽可能高效地为家人提供营养均衡的膳食，而像Costco这样的超市能帮她做到这一点。</p>
<p>“我喜欢Costco提供的各种新鲜、冷冻和常温保存的商品，这让我可以轻松地做出营养均衡的饭菜、配菜和零食。此外，批量购买还能节省我的时间，避免多次往返超市。”Yeung说。</p>
<p>以下是Yeung通常在家常备的一些Costco食材，供读者参考。</p>
<h2>1. 早餐：蛋白煎蛋饼<ahref="https://www.costcobusinessdelivery.com/veggies-made-great-spinach-egg-white-frittatas%2C-20-ct.product.100361660.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Veggies Made Great&#8217;s egg-white frittatas</a>）</h2>
<p>Veggies Made Great品牌煎蛋饼只需几分钟即可烹饪完成，可在烤箱、空气炸锅或微波炉中加热。每个蛋白煎蛋饼含有5克蛋白质，热量仅为70卡路里，是快速又美味的早餐或零食之选。</p>
<h2>2. 午餐必备：扁豆<ahref="https://www.costcobusinessdelivery.com/tasty-bite-organic-indian-madras-lentils%2C-10-oz%2C-8-ct.product.100363934.md#1">Tasty Bite Organic Indian Madras Lentils</a>）</h2>
<p>Tasty Bite品牌的马德拉斯扁豆（Madras lentils）可微波炉加热。只需60秒，就能享用到扁豆、红豆和香料混合在浓郁番茄酱中的美味。每袋含有11克蛋白质和11克膳食纤维。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14245018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14245018" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14245018-shutterstock_613555523-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14245018" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id14245018-shutterstock_613555523-450x300.jpg" alt="Composition With Bowl Of Lentils On Wooden Table" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14245018" class="wp-caption-text">小扁豆是近年流行的健康食品。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3. PuraVida冷冻煎锅菜<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/groups/1102293830698807/posts/1765134591081391/">PuraVida Flame Grilled Chicken Stir-Fry</a>）</h2>
<p>PuraVida冷冻煎锅菜包含鸡胸肉、甜椒、四季豆、蘑菇和胡萝卜等多种食材，并以美味的菠萝酱油调味。</p>
<p>直接从冰箱取出烹饪，大约15分钟即可完成。每份炒菜含有12克蛋白质和2克膳食纤维。</p>
<h2>4. 冷冻西兰花<ahref="https://www.costcobusinessdelivery.com/kirkland-signature-organic-broccoli-florets%2C-1-lb%2C-4-ct.product.100354708.md#1">Kirkland Signature Organic Broccoli Florets</a>）</h2>
<p>Kirkland Signature品牌有机西兰花让家庭餐更加丰盛。每袋4磅装的西兰花包含四袋，只需几分钟就能用微波炉完全煮熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14293460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14293460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14293460-shutterstock_2253518761-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14293460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14293460-shutterstock_2253518761-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14293460" class="wp-caption-text">示意图：冷冻西兰花已经切成一口大小，使用前无需提前解冻。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>5. Tattooed Chef的冷冻烤盘蔬菜<ahref="https://www.reddit.com/r/Costco/comments/1bita9a/tattooed_chef_sheet_pan_veggies_are_back_1299/">Tattooed Chef Sheet Pan Vegetables</a>）</h2>
<p>这款Tattooed Chef品牌的冷冻什锦蔬菜包含抱子甘蓝、红薯、西兰花、红洋葱和西葫芦，已经预先处理好，可以直接使用。</p>
<p>只需拌入油和额外调味料（如果需要），铺在烤盘上烘烤即可。</p>
<h2>6. 冷冻鱼片是简单又健康的蛋白质选择</h2>
<p>Costco冷冻区有很多易于处理的鱼类选择，从需要自行调味的普通三文鱼，到美味即食产品，例如来自<ahref="https://www.costcobusinessdelivery.com/alaskan-leader-seafoods-black-cod%2C-miso-marinated%2C-30-oz.product.2001174515.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Alaskan Leader Seafoods的味噌腌制黑鳕鱼</a>。</p>
<p>只需将几片鱼片和蔬菜（例如上面介绍的什锦蔬菜）一起放在烤盘上，晚餐就一次性完成了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13361944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13361944" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13361944-08cbae3d60857511b5efcf39a16ebc53-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13361944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13361944-08cbae3d60857511b5efcf39a16ebc53-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13361944" class="wp-caption-text">图为烤三文鱼片和蔬菜沙拉。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>7. 罐装豆类是植物蛋白的便捷之选</h2>
<p>Costco有多种选择。Yeung喜欢S&amp;W品牌的有机黑豆<ahref="https://www.costco.com/p/-/sw-organic-black-beans-15-oz-8-count/100381488?storeId=10301&amp;partNumber=100381488&amp;catalogId=10701&amp;langId=-1&amp;krypto=IIGkzwQZzg61Qe4NOtAUJHXUTOWzuF1xg44dK%2FXnrP06ioEWTGYz45sItU1CCmV679OZKn69iJJX7VGX78uhIwUkOSgIJfgQRYj3HGvxvHfccXE3Lvkn6uzcZts2YpcV" target="_blank" rel="noopener">organic black beans from S&amp;W</a>），可以用来做米饭、沙拉和卷饼。每半杯（约120克）含有7克蛋白质。</p>
<h2>8. Seeds of Change牌有机藜麦和糙米<ahref="https://www.costcobusinessdelivery.com/seeds-of-change-organic-quinoa-and-brown-rice%2C-8.5-oz%2C-6-ct.product.11989150.md#1">Seeds of Change Organic Quinoa and Brown Rice</a>）</h2>
<p>烹饪谷物需要时间。Costco出售的Seeds of Change的藜麦和糙米即食袋装食品，只需微波炉加热90秒即可享用，让营养均衡的餐食变得轻而易举。</p>
<p>每份（1杯）含有5克蛋白质和5克膳食纤维。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14330533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14330533" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330533-shutterstock_2462440147-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14330533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/09/id14330533-shutterstock_2462440147-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14330533" class="wp-caption-text">藜麦，Quinoa，不属于“谷物类”植物，但有着胜过许多谷物的营养水平。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>9. 大麻籽富含对心脏有益的脂肪酸</h2>
<p>Omega-3脂肪酸有很多健康益处，能够促进心脏健康。然而，在日常饮食中很难摄入足够的Omega-3脂肪酸。</p>
<p>为了帮助家人多摄入Omega-3脂肪酸，Yeung推荐Kirkland Signature品牌的大麻籽<ahref="https://www.costco.com/p/-/kirkland-signature-organic-hemp-hearts-3-pack-6-lbs-total/4000186427">Hemp Hearts</a>，2磅装），这种大麻籽富含Omega-3脂肪酸。</p>
<p>只需一汤匙去壳大麻籽，就能提供超过4克的不饱和脂肪和3克植物蛋白。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14547436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14547436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14547436-shutterstock_2452060671-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14547436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14547436-shutterstock_2452060671-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14547436" class="wp-caption-text">大麻籽。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>10. 冷冻蒜蓉黄油虾<ahref="https://www.costcobusinessdelivery.com/kirkland-signature-garlic-butter-shrimp%2C-16-oz%2C-2-ct.product.2001146258.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener">garlic-butter shrimp</a>）</h2>
<p>腌制和处理海鲜需要时间，对繁忙上班族来说倍感压力。Kirkland Signature品牌蒜蓉冷冻虾无需清洗、去壳，开袋即食。非常适合为意面、墨西哥卷饼、炒菜或沙拉增添风味和蛋白质。</p>
