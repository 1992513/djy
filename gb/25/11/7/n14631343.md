<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>高压容器回收兑换宣导活动 提升民众安全与环保意识</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631345-807337-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>高压容器回收宣导（大润发）。（苗县府环保局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月07日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）为提升民众资源回收意识，推广高压容器（像是喷雾罐、瓦斯罐等）正确回收观念，苗栗县环保局于今年6月至9月间，分别在头份市大全联一楼停车场、苗栗市家乐福停车场、公馆乡全联福利中心大同店，以及苑里镇全联福利中心中山店举办“高压容器回收兑换宣导活动”，共计办理四场次，累计回收高压容器达678个。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631344" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631344-807336.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14631344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631344-807336.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631344" class="wp-caption-text">高压容器安全回收。（苗县府环保局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动现场除设置高压容器回收兑换摊位外，也透过宣导图卡解说、宣导品兑换，让大家轻松学会高压容器的“安全回收小撇步”，提醒大家避免因不当弃置导致爆裂或火灾等安全隐忧，更完整的高压容器回收办法及相关资讯迳洽环保局脸书粉丝专页“苗准环保 ㄈㄞˋㄊㄨㄥˇㄈㄞˋㄊ一ㄝˇ”， <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/mlepb1/?locale=zh_TW" target="_blank" rel="noopener">https://www.facebook.com/mlepb1/?locale=zh_TW</a>）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631346" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631346-807338.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14631346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631346-807338.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631346" class="wp-caption-text">高压容器回收宣导（家乐福）。（苗县府环保局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14631347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631347" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631347-807339.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14631347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631347-807339.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631347" class="wp-caption-text">高压容器回收宣导（公馆全联）。（苗县府环保局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14631348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631348" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631348-807340.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14631348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631348-807340.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631348" class="wp-caption-text">高压容器回收宣导（苑里全联）。（苗县府环保局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>环保局表示，回收前多一分留意，不仅能保障清洁队员的人身安全，更能预防气爆、火灾等公共危害。四场次活动，民众参与回收行动，展现苗栗县民对环保行动的高度支持与实际行动力，让资源回收成为大家生活的一部分，轻松做环保、一起爱地球！◇</p>
