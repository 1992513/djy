<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线 轻松低碳亲子旅行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631474-807467-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义县文化观光局举办搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线主题游程，轻松玩出低碳亲子旅行。（嘉义县政府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年11月07日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）义县文化观光局锁定亲子客群，在11月4日至5日举办“来嘉趣陶x变身小小陶匠”二日游套票主题游程，以台湾好行公车旅游方式，参访嘉义县各大观光景点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631471" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631471-807464.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14631471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631471-807464-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631471" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县文化观光局举办搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线主题游程，轻松玩出低碳亲子旅行。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14631472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631472" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631472-807465.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14631472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631472-807465-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631472" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县文化观光局举办搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线主题游程，轻松玩出低碳亲子旅行。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动第一天安排前往以陶艺闻名的“板陶窑交趾剪黏工艺园区”，亲手体验陶偶彩绘，发挥无限想像力；随后前往香火鼎盛的新港奉天宫，感受宗教文化魅力，晚间入住新港德旺商旅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631473" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631473-807466.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14631473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631473-807466-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631473" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县文化观光局举办搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线主题游程，轻松玩出低碳亲子旅行。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二天走访日治时期百年县定古迹“培桂堂”，最后再到“蒜头糖厂蔗埕文化园区”，怀旧五分车、体验糖业风华，并且在园区内的北欧工坊享用美食，为旅程画下甜蜜句点。</p>
<p>此趟旅程每位小朋友都对板陶窑园区内满满的亲子游乐设施感到兴奋，纷纷表示没想到嘉义县这么好玩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631475-807468.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14631475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631475-807468-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631475" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县文化观光局举办搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线主题游程，轻松玩出低碳亲子旅行。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14631476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631476" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631476-807469.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14631476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631476-807469-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631476" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县文化观光局举办搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线主题游程，轻松玩出低碳亲子旅行。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为强化亲子族群服务，今年度文化观光局也针对亲子族群推出“童趣嘉年华二日套票”，内含二日乘车券、咩咩上树萌宠乐园门票、阿美凤梨DIY体验券、棒棒积木住宿抵用券、旺莱山商品兑换券，未来将上架至kkday及klook两大平台贩售。</p>
<p>此次旅行团以“亲子同游、寓教于乐”为主题，结合创意手作、文化探索与轻松旅游，期望让孩子在玩乐中学习。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14631477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631477" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631477-807470.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14631477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631477-807470-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631477" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县文化观光局举办搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线主题游程，轻松玩出低碳亲子旅行。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14631478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14631478" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631478-807471.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14631478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14631478-807471-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14631478" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县文化观光局举办搭乘台湾好行故宫南院线主题游程，轻松玩出低碳亲子旅行。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民众可从嘉义高铁站、嘉义火车站或民雄火车站三大转运站搭乘“台湾好行故宫南院线”，沿线停靠国立故宫博物院南部院区、蒜头糖厂蔗埕文化园区、顶菜园、板陶窑、新港奉天宫、民雄金桔观光工厂、旺莱山凤梨文化园区、嘉义观光酒厂及卡普秀医美观光工厂等景点。</p>
<p>更多旅游资讯可至“台湾好行故宫南院线”Facebook粉丝专页及“嘉义县文化观光局官网”查询。◇</p>
