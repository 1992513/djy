<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿中华文化协会合唱公演 勾起美好回忆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630454-1-1-1GBCCA-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大波士顿中华文化协会合唱团年度音乐会，以“拾缀往昔”为主题，指挥王丽文，何涛二胡伴奏。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年11月06日讯】（记者冯文鸾威斯顿市报导）大波士顿中华文化协会合唱团于11月1日下午3点在Westgate Church举行了年度音乐会。今年以“拾缀往昔”为主题，在指挥王丽文的带领下，团员演唱了拉丁文圣歌、戏曲歌曲、校园民歌、儿歌组曲，横跨多个音乐类型，深获听众的赞赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14630452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14630452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630452-1-1-3GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14630452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630452-1-1-3GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14630452" class="wp-caption-text">大波士顿文协合唱团年度公演。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>团长徐宗玲开场致词表示，今年是台湾校园民歌50周年，因此选了《雨中即景》、《渔唱》、《橄榄树》、《听泉》4首校园民歌聊以致敬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14630455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14630455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630455-1-1-6GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14630455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630455-1-1-6GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14630455" class="wp-caption-text">团长徐宗玲开场致词。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她还介绍，本次最特别的是戏曲部分，请来了何涛演奏二胡及伴奏，以及请刘玮珊指导如何念《苏三起解》的京剧腔。王丽文特别编写儿歌组曲，里面多首大家耳熟能详的儿歌，能勾起大家童年美好的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14630456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14630456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630456-1-1-2GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14630456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630456-1-1-2GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14630456" class="wp-caption-text">特邀嘉宾何涛二胡演奏名曲“Csárdás” 。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>王丽文在指挥演唱前，先说明合唱起源于拉丁文的圣歌，多半歌词是拉丁文圣经，起起伏伏的旋律，后来就演变成重唱。因此他们当天开场就先演唱《Ave Verum Corpus》和《Ｏ Mysterium》这2首拉丁文圣歌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14630451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14630451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630451-1-1-4GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14630451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630451-1-1-4GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14630451" class="wp-caption-text">大波士顿文协合唱团年度公演。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在儿歌组曲第一首《本事》的第一句歌词“记得当时年纪小，我爱谈天你爱笑”唱出时，很多人都跟着哼，恍惚时光倒回童年。接下来，合唱团将《静夜星空》、《当我们小的时候》、《采莲谣》、《我家门前有小河》、《郊游》、《造飞机》、《吹喇叭》、《我是一只小茶壶》、《捕鱼歌》、《妹妹背着洋娃娃》、《童年》这12首歌截取部分歌词，重新组合，让人听了过瘾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14630453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14630453" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630453-1-1-8GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14630453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630453-1-1-8GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14630453" class="wp-caption-text">听众陶醉在歌声中。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>听众柯大姐最喜欢压轴的《再回首》，这是一首由姜育恒主唱的经典流行歌曲，也在前年的电视剧“繁花”中当插曲。在演出结束后，听众大喊安可，王丽文表示没准备安可曲，所以就把《再回首》再唱一遍，依然获得热烈的掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14630449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14630449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630449-1-1-7GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14630449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630449-1-1-7GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14630449" class="wp-caption-text">主要的钢琴伴奏陈禹安。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14630448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14630448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630448-1-1-5GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14630448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630448-1-1-5GBCCA-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14630448" class="wp-caption-text">郭嘉颐为母亲何涛伴奏，父亲唢呐演奏家郭雅志在旁边助阵。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14630450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14630450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630450-1-1-9GBCCA.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14630450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/11/id14630450-1-1-9GBCCA-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14630450" class="wp-caption-text">演出后，团员接受观众的鲜花。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天有两位钢琴伴奏，一位是主要的钢琴伴奏陈禹安，另一位是特地为母亲、二胡演奏家何涛伴奏的郭嘉颐，她目前就读于伯克利音乐学院，主修爵士乐。其父亲是唢呐演奏家郭雅志，他当天也亲临现场为妻女助阵。◇</p>
