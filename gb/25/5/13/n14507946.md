<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚裔传统月“魅力台湾”惊艳多伦多舞台</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14507956-IMG_9454-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>万锦巿市长薛家平（Frank Scarpitti）亲临会场表达支持，肯定台湾艺术团队对促进多元文化交流的贡献。（OCAC多伦多侨教中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年05月14日讯】<span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">加拿大亚裔传统月文化访问团（美加东团）于</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">2025</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">年</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">5</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">10</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">日晚间，在大多伦多地区万锦市万民剧院（Peoples Theatre for the Performing Arts）精彩登场。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">由国立台湾体育运动大学艺术团队倾力呈现的《魅力台湾</span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">》（<span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;">Amazing Taiwan</span></span></span>）舞台演出，吸引逾</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">450</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">位主流民众与侨胞热情参与，为加拿大亚裔传统月系列活动注入来自台湾的文化能量与满满感动。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">此次演出由台体大</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">16</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">人专业团队精心策划，透过融合舞蹈、音乐、戏剧与民俗元素，巧妙诠释台湾多元族群文化与岛屿生命力。节目内容涵盖原住民、闽南及客家文化，透过丰富创意与现代肢体语汇，描绘台湾的自然情怀与人文底蕴。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14507954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14507954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/5/13/n14507946.md#1/img_9191-4" rel="attachment wp-att-14507954"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14507954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14507954-IMG_9191-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14507954" class="wp-caption-text">描绘台湾婚庆办桌文化的趣味之作《食尚玩家》。（<span class="x193iq5w xeuugli x13faqbe x1vvkbs xlh3980 xvmahel x1n0sxbx x1lliihq x1s928wv xhkezso x1gmr53x x1cpjm7i x1fgarty x1943h6x xudqn12 x3x7a5m x6prxxf xvq8zen xo1l8bm xzsf02u" dir="auto">OCAC多伦多侨教中心</span>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">演出内容层层铺展，从以柔和肢体展现海洋与岛屿连结的《太平洋的风》，到唤起</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">70</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">年代流行记忆的《群星会》；从描绘婚庆办桌文化的趣味之作《食尚玩家》，到致敬祖灵与自然的作品《彩虹的尽头》，再到洋溢青春甜蜜气息的《</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">Honey Honey Oh Honey</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">》，以及结合舞龙与武术、展现客家节庆精神与坚毅不拔的《龙兄虎弟》。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">压轴曲《魅力台湾》更以高喊“</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">Hero! Hit! </span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">冠军 </span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">Team Taiwan!</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">”点燃全场情绪，观众掌声如雷，不少人甚至感动落泪。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14507955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14507955" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/5/13/n14507946.md#1/img_9408" rel="attachment wp-att-14507955"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14507955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14507955-IMG_9408-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14507955" class="wp-caption-text">谢幕时，演员们与驻多伦多台北经济文化办事处梁毅鹏处长、文化中心蓝柏青主任以及嘉宾们一同向观众致意，气氛热烈欢欣。（<span class="x193iq5w xeuugli x13faqbe x1vvkbs xlh3980 xvmahel x1n0sxbx x1lliihq x1s928wv xhkezso x1gmr53x x1cpjm7i x1fgarty x1943h6x xudqn12 x3x7a5m x6prxxf xvq8zen xo1l8bm xzsf02u" dir="auto">OCAC多伦多侨教中心</span>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">演出现场气氛欢欣，观众反应热烈感人，不少侨胞在演后于<span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;">Line</span></span></span>侨界群组及社群媒体留言表示：“超高水准演出”、“满满台湾元素”、“非常感动”，充分展现台湾文化的温度与感染力，不仅凝聚侨胞情谊，更以艺术为桥，深刻传递台湾文化的魅力与自信。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">万锦市市长薛家平（</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">Frank Scarpitti）</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">与市议员李思韵（</span></span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: large;">Isa Lee）</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">亲临现场，肯定台湾艺术团队对促进加国多元文化交流所做的努力。驻多伦多台北经济文化办事处梁毅鹏处长与文化中心蓝柏青主任亦率全体同仁出席，展现对文化外交工作的重视与支持。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 标楷体;"><span style="font-size: large;">活动结束后，主办单位多伦多台湾影展徐弘益会长特别于“度小月”餐厅设宴招待访问团举行庆功宴。翌日，蓝柏青主任亲赴机场送机，为本阶段巡演行程画下完美句点。</span></span></p>
