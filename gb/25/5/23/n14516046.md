<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516060-2025_Mudfest_Honda_Passport_04-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff

<h6>Honda Passport。（Cam／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年05月23日讯】（大纪元记者李奥美国西雅图报导）美西北地区最大型的户外试车活动，由美国西北汽车报业协会（NWAPA）主办的年度盛事Mudfest，不知不觉已经过了30年，今年是活动的第31屇，经历了3个世纪，汽车也有了不少改变，依旧的是多位汽车媒体人要从众多参选车中选出年度最佳户外活动车（NW Outdoor Activity Vehicle of the Year）。</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" loading="lazy" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://dp5vkf7bbqo9d.cloudfront.net/C2uSjPP7p"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/6vn_fhLHCWg?si=6lttXt8A2hE7xZXC" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>今年活动由四月份推迟到五月份，天气更佳，在Ridge Motosports Park进行的Mudfest首天，虽然艳阳高照，但风势也强，工作人员顶着烈日寒风工作，值得致敬！今天有20位车评人参与测试及评分，要在20辆汽车中选出各类别最佳汽车及年度户外活动车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516069" style="width: 1600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516069-2025_Mudfest_VW_Taos_04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14516069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516069-2025_Mudfest_VW_Taos_04.jpg" alt="" width="1600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516069" class="wp-caption-text">Volkswagen Taos。（Cam／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动分为两天进行，第一天是在赛道行驶，测试汽车在道路上的各项表现；第二天则是越野测试，以泥泞与沙石上的表现来分高低。今屇测试的汽车共有20辆，汽车种类分为“两排座休旅车（Two-Row Family SUV）”、“三排座休旅车（Three-Row Family SUV）”、“豪华休旅车（Luxury SUV）”和“皮卡（Pickup Truck）”，最后会经投票选出以上四种类别车辆外，也会选出“极限能力车（Extreme Capability Vehicle）”和“最超值休旅车（Best Value SUV or Pickup）”，以及专为电车而设的“电动化多用途车（Electrified Utility Vehicle）”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516061" style="width: 1600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516061-2025_Mudfest_Hyundai_Ioniq5_06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14516061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516061-2025_Mudfest_Hyundai_Ioniq5_06.jpg" alt="" width="1600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516061" class="wp-caption-text">Hyundai IONIQ 5 XRT。（Cam／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在“两排座休旅车”类别中，测试车数量也是最多，共有8辆，分别有Acura ADX A-spec Advance、Honda Passport TrailSport Elite、Hyundai IONIQ 5 XRT、Hyundai Tucson XRT、Mazda CX-50 Hybrid Premium Plus、Subaru Forester Touring Hybrid、Toyota 4Runner TRD Pro和Volkswagen Taos SE Black 4Motion。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516067" style="width: 1600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516067-2025_Mudfest_Toyota_4Runner_06.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14516067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516067-2025_Mudfest_Toyota_4Runner_06.jpg" alt="" width="1600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516067" class="wp-caption-text">Toyota 4Runner。（Cam／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“三排座休旅车”有4辆，为Jeep Wagoneer Series II Overland 4&#215;4、Kia Sorento X-Pro、Mitsubishi Outlander Trail Edition及Nissan Armada Pro-4X。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516068" style="width: 1600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516068-2025_Mudfest_Toyota_Tacoma_05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14516068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516068-2025_Mudfest_Toyota_Tacoma_05.jpg" alt="" width="1600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516068" class="wp-caption-text">Toyota Tacoma。（Cam／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“豪华休旅车”类别同样有5辆，其中有三辆Genesis品牌，包括Genesis GV70 3.5T Sport Prestige、Genesis GV80 Coupe 3.5T E-SC、Genesis GV80 3.5T Prestige、Ineos Grenadier LWB SW Fieldmaster及Lexus LX 700h Overtrail。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516063" style="width: 1600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516063-2025_Mudfest_Ineos_Grenadier_XLWB_09.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14516063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516063-2025_Mudfest_Ineos_Grenadier_XLWB_09.jpg" alt="" width="1600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516063" class="wp-caption-text">INEOS Grenadier XLWB Trialmaster。（Cam／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于“皮卡”类别，则只有3辆测试车，包括Ineos Grenadier XLWB Trialmaster、Toyota Tacoma Trailhunter和RAM 1500 RHO Crew Cab 4&#215;4。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516059" style="width: 1600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516059-2025_Mudfest_Genesis_GV80_Coupe_03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14516059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516059-2025_Mudfest_Genesis_GV80_Coupe_03.jpg" alt="" width="1600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516059" class="wp-caption-text">Genesis GV70。（Cam／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>首日是测试各车在柏油路面及赛道上的表现，主办方在场内设置了加速区、刹车区，用于测试汽车的0-60mph加速及60-0mph刹车。在设置的路障间行驶，测试汽车的过弯表现。继而可驶入赛道上走一圈，试验汽车的实际行驶表现。今年与去年一样增加了弯路区域，可更多机会测试各汽车的过弯能力。第二日在越野路段行驶，有碎石路段、水坑、凹凸不平路段、不同倾斜度的山坡等，务求测试出汽车的越野能力及涉水能力，天气虽是阴天，但没下雨，所以到处是沙尘滚滚。其中有些路段为极端（Extreme）路况，只会让部分经强化的车辆测试。部分车厂会有厂方人员陪同，讲解该款汽车的特别功能。</p>
<p>INEOS是新汽车品牌，Grenadier Fieldmaster是传统的方形越野车，而Trailmaster则是皮卡车款，车体坚固越野能力强，车内像极飞机驾驶舱，有着各式按键，然而INEOS Grenadier两辆汽车的越野表现是比在赛道上佳，尤其在过弯能力上；Hyundai Ioniq 5 XRT换上了越野轮胎，并且防撞器是黑色，在方向盘上也加了“Terrain Mode”选择，而且也能完成整段越野路程；新一代Toyota Tacoma Trailhunter虽与去年的TRD Off-Road都是Tacoma，但Trailhunter使用输出更强的2.4L i-FORCE MAX Hybrid引擎，并且Crawl功能及MTS系统，令越野能力更好。使用同样动力系统的Toyota 4Runner，越野能力绝不亚于Tacoma；Lexus LX 700h Overtrail拥有豪华的内装，而且行车舒适，但也有足够能力完成Extreme路段；RAM 1500 RHO皮卡在整个Mudfest经常都看到它的身影，其3L涡轮增压引擎最力马力达到540匹及521磅呎扭矩，排气声雄厚，长行程的悬挂系统，可进行“跳跃”动作；以Integra为本而开发的Acura ADX，配有AWD系统，虽只配1.5升涡轮增压引擎，表现不错而且有足够能力完成普通越野路段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516065" style="width: 1600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516065-2025_Mudfest_Mitsubishi_Outlander_04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14516065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516065-2025_Mudfest_Mitsubishi_Outlander_04.jpg" alt="" width="1600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516065" class="wp-caption-text">Mitsubishi Outlander。（Cam／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过两天的道路及越野测试，车评人以操控、起步及刹车表现、外观、内装及科技设备，越野操控及越野能力来评分，最后投票选出各类别冠军，以及年度户外活动车：</p>
<p>最佳两排座休旅车 – Hyundai IONIQ 5 XRT<br />
最佳三排座休旅车 – Jeep Wagoneer Series II Overland<br />
最佳豪华休旅车 – Genesis GV70 3.5T Sport Prestige<br />
最佳皮卡 – Ram 1500 RHO Crew Cab 4&#215;4<br />
最佳极端越野能力车 – Ram 1500 RHO Crew Cab 4&#215;4<br />
最佳电动化多用途车 – Hyundai IONIQ 5 XRT<br />
最超值休旅车 – Subaru Forester Touring Hybrid</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516066" style="width: 1600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516066-2025_Mudfest_RAM_1500_04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14516066" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516066-2025_Mudfest_RAM_1500_04.jpg" alt="" width="1600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516066" class="wp-caption-text">RAM 1500 RHO。（Cam／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>综合了各种分数及评价，最后的2025年“最佳户外用途车”，为Ram 1500 RHO Crew Cab 4&#215;4所夺得。◇</p>
