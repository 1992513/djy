<h6>2025 Nissan Murano Platinum。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年05月24日讯】在跨界休旅车市场竞争日益激烈的今日，Nissan以全新2025年Murano第四代车型重新定义豪华与科技的完美结合。这款车不仅以高端设计和先进科技吸引目光，更为日常通勤与长途旅行注入新意。全新Murano以优雅的外观、精致的内饰、先进的驾驶辅助系统，从竞争者中脱颖而出。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d2zq9v5psazuww.cloudfront.net/U4xNXxrYc"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/AyStkGsSTdg?si=SncugDez2LbPH0hs" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>全新Murano的外观设计以“前卫优雅”为核心，呈现出无缝、稳固且干净的视觉效果。车身宽度增加超过2.6&#8243;，搭配优雅外扩的后轮拱，展现出强烈的力量感。前脸设计极为简洁，超薄“水晶立方”LED头灯位于光泽黑色饰条两侧，与格栅分离，呈现出Nissan标志性的V-motion图形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516680" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516680-2025_Nissan_Murano_03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516680-2025_Nissan_Murano_03-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516680" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Nissan Murano Platinum。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尾部设计同样令人印象深刻，尾闸更为干净俐落，后雨刷巧妙置于后车窗上方，排气管隐藏于防撞器下方。横跨车尾的LED尾灯有时尚感，夜晚点亮起时更形独特，一看便知这车是Murano。21&#8243;轮圈（Platinum等级专属）进一步强化了车辆的气势，让Murano在静止时依然散发动感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516681" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516681-2025_Nissan_Murano_06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516681-2025_Nissan_Murano_06-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516681" class="wp-caption-text">以“前卫优雅”为核心。（李奥／大纪元）<span style="font-size: 16px;">）</span></figcaption></figure>
<p>进入Murano的内舱，犹如进入一间现代化的奢华客厅。内饰以极简主义为基调，水平设计主题让空间更显开阔。标准配备的双12.3&#8243;显示萤幕无缝融入缝线材质的环境中，触感柔软的材质遍布座椅与中控台，展现出高级质感。名为“Murano Glass”的仪表板饰板灵感来自威尼斯玻璃工艺，融入色彩变换图形与纹理，与光线互动，彰显精湛工艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516682" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516682-2025_Nissan_Murano_13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516682-2025_Nissan_Murano_13-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516682" class="wp-caption-text">尾闸设计更为干净俐落。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车内装有64色氛围照明、全景天窗、加热/冷却/按摩前座椅以及加热方向盘，无一不提升乘客的奢华体验。后排乘客同样备受礼遇，后门窗遮阳帘、加热后座椅以及更宽敞的膝部空间，得益于前座椅更薄设计，让长途旅行更舒适。Murano首次采用的Zero Gravity后座设计提供更佳支撑力，后车门开启角度更大，方便进出或安装儿童安全座椅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516683" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516683-2025_Nissan_Murano_34.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516683-2025_Nissan_Murano_34-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516683" class="wp-caption-text">车内装有64色氛围照明。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025年Murano搭载一系列先进科技，让驾驶更轻松且充满信心。Nissan Safety Shield 360为标准配备，提供全方位的安全保障。此外，Murano首次搭载的3D智能环景监控系统，允许驾驶者从8个虚拟视角观察车辆周围环境，较鸟瞰视野更为全面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516684" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516684-2025_Nissan_Murano_19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516684-2025_Nissan_Murano_19-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516684" class="wp-caption-text">双12.3&#8243;显示萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14516685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516685" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516685-2025_Nissan_Murano_21.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516685-2025_Nissan_Murano_21-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516685" class="wp-caption-text">双12.3&#8243;显示萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>ProPILOT Assist系统在Murano SV等级提供智能巡航控制与转向辅助，进阶的ProPILOT Assist 1.1（SV及Platinum等级）利用导航数据，根据路线提前调整弯道速度，提升驾驶流畅度。其他安全升级包括乘客膝部气囊、侧视镜从A柱移至车门以改善前方视野，以及后座的座椅下方强化结构与安全带负载限制器，全面提升后排乘客的碰撞保护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516686" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516686-2025_Nissan_Murano_25.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516686-2025_Nissan_Murano_25-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516686" class="wp-caption-text">9速自动变速箱以按钮方式变档。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Murano的双12.3&#8243;显示萤幕为标准配置，中间触控萤幕提供娱乐与资讯功能，支援无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto。左侧显示萤幕作为仪表组，可切换多种显示模式，满足不同驾驶需求。SL与Platinum等级搭载Google内建功能，将Google Maps、Google Assistant和Play Store直接整合至车辆系统，带来熟悉且高效的操作体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516687" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516687-2025_Nissan_Murano_28.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516687-2025_Nissan_Murano_28-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516687" class="wp-caption-text">方向盘右方按钮可控制两边萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新Murano搭载2L VC-Turbo可变压缩比涡轮增压引擎，输出241 hp马力与260 lb-ft扭矩，较前代增加20 lb-ft扭矩，搭配9速自动变速箱与换档拨片，提供灵敏且动感的驾驶体验。怠速启停功能为标准配置，提升燃油效率。Murano SV提供前轮驱动或智能全轮驱动选项，SL与Platinum等级标配全轮驱动，确保多种路况下的稳定性。Murano的拖曳能力达1,500磅，满足轻度拖曳需求。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516688" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516688-2025_Nissan_Murano_17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516688" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516688-2025_Nissan_Murano_17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516688" class="wp-caption-text">2L VC-Turbo可变压缩比涡轮增压引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新2025年Nissan Murano以其优雅的外观、奢华的内饰、先进的科技与安全的全面升级。无论是日常通勤还是长途旅行，Murano都能以舒适、便利与信心陪伴驾驶者与乘客。从细腻的设计细节到强大的功能阵容，这款车不仅满足了对豪华跨界车的期待，更在竞争激烈的市场中脱颖而出。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14516690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14516690" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516690-2025_Nissan_Murano_30.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14516690" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14516690-2025_Nissan_Murano_30-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14516690" class="wp-caption-text">Zero Gravity座位。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
