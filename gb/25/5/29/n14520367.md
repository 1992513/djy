<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>珀斯过半郊区房产销售下滑</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年05月29日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者金凇澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">最新数据显示，珀斯超过一半的郊区的房地产销售量持续下滑，曾经炙手可热的区如</span><span lang="en-AU">Rockingham</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-AU">Baldivis</span><span lang="zh-TW">如今已成为房地产“萧条区”。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据“</span><span lang="en-US">Domain</span><span lang="zh-TW">”网站报导，</span><span lang="en-US">Hotspotting</span><span lang="zh-TW">公司总经理格雷厄姆（</span><span lang="en-US">Tim Graham</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年秋季《房价预测指数》显示珀斯房市持续承压，去年第四季度的数据也证实了此前销售活动下滑的报告。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">他说：“截至去年第四季度，珀斯有</span><span lang="en-US">57%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的郊区呈现出房产销售负增长趋势，另有</span><span lang="en-US">17%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的郊区未展现出明确的走势。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">“仅有</span><span lang="en-US">26%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的郊区录得销售活动正增长。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">格雷厄姆指出，尽管过去一年整体销售持续下滑，仍有部分市场表现坚挺。独立房市场方面，</span><span lang="en-US">Como</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Dianella</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Morley</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Piara Waters</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US">Wellard</span><span lang="zh-TW">仍在增长；而公寓市场则以</span><span lang="en-US">Cannington</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Leederville</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Maylands</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Wembley</span><span lang="zh-TW">、</span><span lang="en-US">Yokine</span><span lang="zh-TW">和</span><span lang="en-US">West Perth</span><span lang="zh-TW">为代表。</span></p>
<p>相比之下，表现疲弱的郊区包括<span lang="en-US">Aveley</span>、<span lang="en-US">Balga</span>、<span lang="en-US">Butler</span>、<span lang="en-US">Cooloongup</span>、<span lang="en-US">Ellenbrook</span>、<span lang="en-US">Mount Lawley</span>、<span lang="en-US">Palmyra</span>、<span lang="en-US">Port Kennedy</span>、<span lang="en-US">Rockingham</span>和<span lang="en-US">Waikiki</span>。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">Hotspotting</span><span lang="zh-TW">公司董事赖德（</span><span lang="en-US">Terry Ryder</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，最新研究发现，达尔文正逐渐确立其作为澳大利亚最具活力房地产市场之一的地位，销售活动激增，有多达</span><span lang="en-US">79%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的市场展现出积极的增长信号。</span></p>
<p>赖德还指出，西澳偏远地区的房地产市场在过去<span lang="en-US">18</span>个月中整体保持稳定。</p>
<p>“<span lang="en-US">Bunbury</span>的市场在过去一年持续走弱，大多数郊区区被归类为‘持平’或‘下滑’，曾引领房市的<span lang="en-US">Withers</span>也包含在内。”</p>
<p>“<span lang="en-US">Geraldton</span>在过去六个月的销售活动也有所减少，仅有三个城区显示交易量上升，而有五个处于下滑或持平状态。”</p>
<p>但西澳偏远地区也有亮点，<span lang="en-US">Karratha</span>、<span lang="en-US">Kalgoorlie-Boulder</span>、<span lang="en-US">Albany</span>、<span lang="en-US">Broome</span>和<span lang="en-US">Busselton</span>的销售活动仍在上升。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据西澳房地产协会（</span><span lang="en-US">REIWA</span><span lang="zh-TW">）的最新数据，</span><span lang="en-US">REIWA</span><span lang="zh-TW">偏远地区发言人麦肯齐（</span><span lang="en-US">Peta McKenzie</span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，</span><span lang="en-US">Geraldton</span><span lang="zh-TW">是</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年</span><span lang="en-US">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">月季度表现最强的区域中心。其独立房中位售价从去年第四季度的</span><span lang="en-US">46</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元上涨</span><span lang="en-US">6.5%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span><span lang="en-US">49</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p>麦肯齐表示，投资者的活跃度对本地购房者造成了压力。</p>
<p>她说：“<span lang="en-US">REIWA</span>成员报告称，投资者经常以略高的报价优先抢购房产，领先于本地自住买家。”</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">本季度内，西澳地区有</span><span lang="en-US">8</span><span lang="zh-TW">个偏远地区中心录得房价上涨，</span><span lang="en-US">Port Hedland</span><span lang="zh-TW">是唯一房价下跌的区域中心。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据澳大利亚统计局发布的数据，</span><span lang="en-US">2025</span><span lang="zh-TW">年第一季度内，新投资房贷数量下降了</span><span lang="en-US">3.7%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，新购房贷款下降</span><span lang="en-US">3.4%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
