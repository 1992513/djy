<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>元长乐龄欢庆端午 百人齐心包粽传爱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520465-769783-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>元长乐龄欢庆端午。（沈台兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年05月29日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）端午将至，云林县元长乡吴地大东庄天后宫于5月28日热闹举办“2025年庆端午佳节活动”，由在地社区、志工与乐龄团体共同策划，动员超过百位乡亲协力包制8000颗粽子，不仅现场热腾腾供应，也特别分送给吴地辖内的中低收入户与独居长辈，让传统节庆注入温暖的人情味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14520466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520466" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520466-769784.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520466-769784-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520466" class="wp-caption-text">端午将至，云林县元长乡吴地大东庄天后宫于5月28日热闹举办“2025年庆端午佳节活动”。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14520467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520467" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520467-769785.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520467-769785-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520467" class="wp-caption-text">由在地社区、志工与乐龄团体共同策划，动员超过百位乡亲协力包制8000颗粽子。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动现场热闹滚滚，处处洋溢浓浓的乡土情谊与节庆气氛。红色喜气的帐篷下，社区妈妈们手脚俐落地包着粽子，有人负责洗粽叶、有人舀米、也有人绑绳封口，分工有序、配合默契，不一会儿便堆满一整桌的粽香成果。参与阿嬷笑说：“能为社区尽点力，又能传承我们的粽艺，真有意义！”</p>
<p>其中，社区长辈们专注地将糯米与馅料包裹进粽叶中，动作熟练娴熟，仿若演绎一场端午工艺秀。志工阿姨面对镜头也会亲切的比出大拇指，脸上堆满笑容，象征着节庆团结的精神与满满的正能量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14520468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520468" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520468-769786.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520468-769786-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520468" class="wp-caption-text">社区妈妈们手脚俐落地包着粽子，有人负责洗粽叶、有人舀米、也有人绑绳封口，分工有序。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14520469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520469" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520469-769787.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520469-769787-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520469" class="wp-caption-text">参与阿嬷笑说：“能为社区尽点力，又能传承我们的粽艺，真有意义！”（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14520470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520470" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520470-769788.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520470-769788-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520470" class="wp-caption-text">社区长辈们专注地将糯米与馅料包裹进粽叶中，动作熟练娴熟，仿若演绎一场端午工艺秀。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了包粽义举，活动也邀请到在地的“元长乐龄乐团”演出助兴。舞台上，一群爷爷级音乐家精神奕奕地操着电吉他、萨克斯风、爵士鼓与长笛，带来怀旧与活力兼具的音乐表演。鼓手节奏稳健、长笛悠扬轻巧，观众纷纷报以热烈掌声。有乐团成员笑说：“我们虽然退休了，但舞台永远不退休！”这场音乐演出不仅展现银发活力，也为活动增添许多欢乐色彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14520471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520471" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520471-769789.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520471-769789-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520471" class="wp-caption-text">除了包粽义举，活动也邀请到在地的“元长乐龄乐团”演出助兴。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14520472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520472" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520472-769790.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520472-769790-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520472" class="wp-caption-text">舞台上，一群爷爷级音乐家精神奕奕地操着电吉他、萨克斯风、爵士鼓与长笛，带来怀旧与活力兼具的音乐表演。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动更获得多位地方长官支持与到场祝贺，云林县议员林文彬、黄美瑶也特地莅临现场，他表示，社区的团结与互助精神令人敬佩，这样的活动不仅传承文化，也深化邻里情谊，展现地方最美的力量。</p>
<p>大东庄天后宫表示，这场活动已举办多年，每年都吸引越来越多居民参与。除了延续节庆意义，更期望透过“粽子传爱”，让社区中最需要关怀的家庭与长辈也能感受到节日的温暖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14520473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520473" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520473-769791.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520473-769791-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520473" class="wp-caption-text">活动更获得多位地方长官支持与到场祝贺，云林县议员林文彬也特地莅临现场。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14520474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520474" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520474-769792.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520474-769792-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520474" class="wp-caption-text">大东庄天后宫表示，这场活动已举办多年，每年都吸引越来越多居民参与。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14520475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14520475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520475-769793.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14520475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14520475-769793-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14520475" class="wp-caption-text">期望透过“粽子传爱”，让社区中最需要关怀的家庭与长辈也能感受到节日的温暖。（沈台兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年包制的8000颗粽子中，特别分配数千颗由社工团队协助发送至弱势户与独居长者手中，让他们不再孤单过节。志工们强调：“这不只是粽子，更是我们对社区长辈的关心与陪伴。”</p>
<p>从包粽子、到音乐演出、再到人与人之间的温情交流，整场活动在欢笑声中圆满落幕。这场充满节庆氛围的庆典，不仅唤起端午的传统记忆，更凝聚元长乡民的心，让人感受到节日背后最深刻的情感，守望相助的社区力量。</p>
