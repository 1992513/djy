<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2025百想艺术大赏获奖名单揭晓 话题作获奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499970-250201030516100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>IU李知恩与朴宝剑主演《苦尽柑来遇见你》剧照。（Netflix提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年05月05日讯】（大纪元记者乐慧萱编译报导）5日，第61届百想艺术大赏在韩国首尔举行，由申东烨、秀智和朴宝剑携手主持。当晚大赏分别由电影《哈尔滨》、综艺《黑白大厨：料理阶级大战》荣获，影帝影后颁给了曹政奭和全道嬿（或译全度妍），朱智勋与金泰梨捧走了视帝视后。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14499955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499955" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499955-241015003526100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14499955" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499955-241015003526100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499955" class="wp-caption-text">《黑白大厨：料理阶级大战》在全球收获高人气。（Netfilx提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年综艺节目中话题度最高的《黑白大厨：料理阶级大战》捧走了电视部门的大赏；而由IU和朴宝剑主演的《苦尽柑来遇见你》荣获最佳作品奖、最佳剧本奖、最佳男配角奖和最佳女配角奖四座奖项。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14499969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499969" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499969-250405064621100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14499969" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499969-250405064621100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499969" class="wp-caption-text">IU与朴宝剑主演《苦尽柑来遇见你》剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最佳导演奖由执导《如此亲密的背叛者》的宋妍华导演获得，新人奖颁给了秋泳愚（或译秋英宇）和蔡元彬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14499998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499998-250505123714100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14499998" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499998-250505123714100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499998" class="wp-caption-text">秋泳愚（或译秋英宇）在《玉氏夫人传》中饰演与贵族大小姐有媒妁之言的宋公子。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>电影《哈尔滨》捧走了大赏和最佳作品奖，由吴胜旭执导的电影《左轮手枪》获得最佳导演奖和最佳女主角奖，《战，乱》荣获最佳剧本奖、最佳艺术奖和男子新人演技奖三个奖，边佑锡与金惠奫拿下人气奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14499954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499954-2408060920231487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14499954" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499954-2408060920231487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499954" class="wp-caption-text">《背着善宰跑》8月11日台视首播。图为边佑锡与金惠奫。（CJ_ENM_STUDIOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
