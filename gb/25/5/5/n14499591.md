<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>头份青年学子创意供桌大赛 迎永贞宫妈祖四月八绕境</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499595-763306-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>青年炉主创意供桌大赛。（头份市公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年05月05日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）2025头份四月八澎湃庆城艺系列活动，青年炉主创意供桌大赛，5日上午在头份六合综合运动场盛大展开，头份市12所国小以及4所国中同学，组队报名参赛，主办单位还邀来社区艺文团队暖场，现场十分热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14499593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499593" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499593-763304-rotated.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499593-763304-rotated.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="604" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499593" class="wp-caption-text">罗雪珠市长参观青年炉主创意供桌大赛。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14499594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499594" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499594-763305.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499594-763305.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499594" class="wp-caption-text">罗雪珠市长参观青年炉主创意供桌大赛。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14499596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499596" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499596-763307.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499596-763307.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499596" class="wp-caption-text">青年炉主创意供桌大赛。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14499597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499597" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499597-763308.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499597-763308.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499597" class="wp-caption-text">青年炉主创意供桌大赛。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>头份市公所表示，头份四月八最重要的核心活动，就是早期农业时代客家人迎妈祖，用心准备丰盛牲礼，在迎妈祖祭神以后，才有丰富的鸡鸭鱼肉供品，可以顺道宴客，也就是所谓的四炆四炒。而客家人迎神的供品有分三牲、五牲，妈祖是天上圣母，所以用五牲大礼来迎接妈祖，祈求妈祖庇佑乡里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14499598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499598" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499598-763309.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499598-763309.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499598" class="wp-caption-text">青年炉主创意供桌大赛得奖学校。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14499599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499599" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499599-763310.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499599-763310.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499599" class="wp-caption-text">青年炉主创意供桌大赛得奖学校。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所谓的五牲，强调要有三牲，要有两只脚的，像是鸡、四只脚的猪肉、水里游的鱿鱼，再加上豆干、鸡蛋等搭配，丰富的就用鸡鸭鱼肉都到齐，来展现诚意。</p>
<p>16所国中小的同学，则是利用不同的创意，利用摆盘、装饰，甚至是现代的公仔娃娃，呈现迎妈祖的可爱创意，各校同学今年发挥创意呈现五牲的意义，经过大会评审评分，国小组金质奖由永贞国小夺下；银质奖则是斗焕国小、信德国小；铜质奖则是侨善国小、信义国小、六合国小；文化传承奖为新兴国小、蟠桃国小；妈祖精神奖为后庄国小、头份国小；最具创意奖为尖山国小、建国国小。国中组金质奖由则是由头份国中拿下，文化传承奖为建国国中慈辉分校、妈祖精神奖为兴华高中国中部、最具创意将为文英国中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14499600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499600" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499600-763311.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499600-763311.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499600" class="wp-caption-text">青年炉主创意供桌大赛得奖学校。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14499601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499601" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499601-763312.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499601-763312.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499601" class="wp-caption-text">青年炉主创意供桌大赛得奖学校。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14499592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14499592" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499592-763303-rotated.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14499592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14499592-763303-rotated.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14499592" class="wp-caption-text">罗雪珠市长参观青年炉主创意供桌大赛。（头份市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>头份市长罗雪珠在中午十二点，妈祖銮轿抵达六合综合运动场后，带领全场一起拜妈祖迎妈祖，同时在妈祖见证下颁发比赛奖金，并披上青年炉主的背带，象征四月八的精神永续传承，也盼望这项青年炉主的活动能够永续传承，让头份世世代代的年轻朋友，莫忘头份四月八妈祖绕境的起源与文化价值。◇</p>
