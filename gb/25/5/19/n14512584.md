<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中医推荐5茶饮 5穴道 延缓失智症</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13744669-shutterstock_1913297833-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中医认为，透过按摩穴位可以促进气血循环、养肾补脑、安神益智，对改善记忆和认知功能非常有帮助。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">全球老龄化社会中，失智症已成为日益严重的健康问题，为患者及家属带来沉重的身心压力。除了现代医学专注延缓失智症进展，中医也能提供辅助疗法，改善患者的生活品质。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>改善失智症 勤按摩五个穴道</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14512603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14512603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14512603-a20c3e4f6a6cb124a8ab36e39ee70178.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14512603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14512603-a20c3e4f6a6cb124a8ab36e39ee70178-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14512603" class="wp-caption-text">5穴道。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">中医认为，引起失智症的原因是脑髓不足、肾精亏虚、痰浊阻滞及气血不足，导致大脑功能下降，出现记忆力减退、判断力降低及情绪不稳等症状。临床上，患者常见表现为日常活动能力下降，重复问同样问题、或无法辨认熟悉的人事物。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">透过按摩穴位可以促进气血循环、养肾补脑、安神益智，对改善记忆和认知功能非常有帮助。每天按摩15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20次，每次按摩10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15秒，感到有酸胀感即可。以下是常用的五个穴位：</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.百会穴</b></span></h3>
<p><b>位置：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于头顶，两耳尖连线的正中点。</span></p>
<p><b>作用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">本穴是“大脑的控制中心”。能提升大脑活力、清理脑内积累的有害物质、增强记忆力。针灸或指压此穴都可促进气血流通，强化大脑功能。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14299000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14299000" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14299000-bb00387f95efd2efd4163c3c9222850d.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14299000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14299000-bb00387f95efd2efd4163c3c9222850d-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14299000" class="wp-caption-text">百会穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.风池穴</b></span></h3>
<p><b>位置：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于脖子后方，枕骨下缘与脊椎之间的凹陷处。</span></p>
<p><b>作用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">因气血不足引发的认知障碍和记忆衰退，按压该穴有助于活血通络，疗效很好。还可纾缓头痛、眩晕以及改善思维迟钝。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14512601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14512601" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14512601-bdf07eea0204d1c637cf37c1140d67f4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14512601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14512601-bdf07eea0204d1c637cf37c1140d67f4-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14512601" class="wp-caption-text">风池穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.太冲穴</b></span></h3>
<p><b>位置：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于脚背，第一、第二趾交界，向上约2公分处。</span></p>
<p><b>作用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">本穴是肝经的重要穴道。由于情绪不稳引起的记忆力问题和思维迟钝，此穴功效包括疏肝理气，调节情绪，缓解焦虑和抑郁。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14298997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14298997" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298997-fcbb87341f40dcebc5f9d64c1818ee52.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14298997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id14298997-fcbb87341f40dcebc5f9d64c1818ee52-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14298997" class="wp-caption-text">太冲穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.风府穴</b></span></h3>
<p><b>位置：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于脖子后方正中间，颈部与头部交界的凹陷部位。</span></p>
<p><b>作用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">促进大脑的血液循环，疏通经络，提高认知能力，延缓脑部退行性病变。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14512599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14512599" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14512599-219d35aae3b092eb7eb65218485ec04b.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14512599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14512599-219d35aae3b092eb7eb65218485ec04b-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14512599" class="wp-caption-text">风府穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>5.神门穴</b></span></h3>
<p><b>位置：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">位于手腕内侧，掌侧横纹处，紧贴手掌的边缘。</span></p>
<p><b>作用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">本穴是安神定志的要穴。老年性失智症常伴随焦虑、失眠、心悸等症状，按摩该穴能舒缓这些症状，改善睡眠质量，进而改善认知功能。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14354639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14354639" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354639-3f7ea6a5a7e3916a260b318baa57f567.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14354639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id14354639-3f7ea6a5a7e3916a260b318baa57f567-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14354639" class="wp-caption-text">神门穴。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>延缓不同失智症 中医推五道中药茶饮</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14512604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14512604" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14512604-4309a13f38301fee5fba79b699e4b5f8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14512604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14512604-4309a13f38301fee5fba79b699e4b5f8-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14512604" class="wp-caption-text">5茶饮。（马光医疗网提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">依据失智症不同成因，搭配适合的中药茶饮，可以减轻病症，增强记忆力，改善认知功能。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">以下茶饮作法皆是将材料稍微洗净，然后放入杯中，用500cc沸水冲泡，静置10分钟后即可饮用。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.核桃芝麻茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>适用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">病患因肾精亏虚引发记忆力减退。</span></p>
<p><b>材料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">核桃仁5钱、黑芝麻5钱、红枣（去核）2颗。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">补肾养精、益智安神。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.薏仁茯苓茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>适用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">痰浊阻滞型的失智患者。</span></p>
<p><b>材料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">薏仁5钱，茯苓5钱，陈皮3钱。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">健脾祛湿、化痰利浊。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.当归黄耆茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>适用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">病人由于气血不足造成记忆力衰退。</span></p>
<p><b>材料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">当归3钱，黄耆3钱，枸杞子3钱。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">补气养血、活血安神。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.桂圆姜茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>适用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">情绪低落并伴随记忆减退者。</span></p>
<p><b>材料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">桂圆肉5钱、生姜1片。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">安神补脑、温阳益气。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>5.枸杞山药茶</b></span></h3>
<p><b>适用：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">肾精不足型的失智病患。</span></p>
<p><b>材料：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">枸杞子3钱、山药3钱。</span></p>
<p><b>功效：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">补脾养肾，增强记忆力和专注力。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>降低失智风险 从日常生活养护大脑</b></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">预防失智症，建议从良好的日常生活习惯着手，包括下列面向：</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>1.从事规律运动</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">规律的运动能促进大脑血液循环，提升脑部活力，有助于降低认知功能退化的风险。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>2.适量摄取抗氧化物质</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">适量摄取富含抗氧化物质的食物，如深色蔬菜、水果、坚果等，可有效减少自由基对脑细胞的伤害，保护神经系统健康。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>3.重视心理健康</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">保持稳定心情，避免长期处于压力或焦虑状态，能预防因情绪因素导致的认知衰退。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><b>4.定期训练大脑</b></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">透过阅读、益智游戏或学习新事物，可以活化脑部功能，延缓退化，有助于维持思维敏捷与记忆力。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">失智症是一群症状的组合（症候群），亲友应协助患者寻求专业医疗建议，及早治疗，并从多方面努力才能提供最佳的照护。</span><strong>◎◇</strong></p>
