<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世界法轮大法日 纽约州政要褒奖恭贺（下）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511376-2025513.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
