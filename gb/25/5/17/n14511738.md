<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>为推广传统水墨画 叶志庆于斗南文荟馆举办个展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511750-766604-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>叶志庆和他的家人！（廖素贞／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年05月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）?为推广传统水墨画“水墨画家叶志庆个展”，即日起至5/30日止，在历史建筑斗南文荟馆展出，这次展出的有山水、花鸟、人物、风景画等作品，还有高难度的陶版画，他的作品不但令人赏心悦目，又很耐人寻味！值得您细细品味观赏，喜欢传统水墨的朋友，一定不能错过这场展出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511739" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511739-766594.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511739-766594-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511739" class="wp-caption-text">叶志庆和他的山水作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>叶又嘉（笔名叶志庆）别号南林居士，1963年生于云林县斗南镇石龟里，从小不爱读书，喜欢画佛像，1979年16岁国中毕业后，即由其二舅简木椿带到台北学画，在因缘际会下，受教于当时的名画家李奉魁、高齐润、于百龄、林脩武等名师的指导，打下扎实的基本画功，也建立了他独特的画风，他擅长以细腻笔画勾勒大自然山水、花鸟、人物，笔触生动而活泼，兼具传统写实画风，对山水乡土表现更具传神！他以传统水墨工笔画技巧，展现台湾早年的农村景象最为人称道！也是很多台湾收藏家喜欢的作品！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511740" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511740-766595.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511740-766595-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511740" class="wp-caption-text">叶志庆笔触细腻的山水画，耐人寻味又令人赏心悦目！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14511741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511741" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511741-766596.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511741-766596-450x409.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="409"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511741" class="wp-caption-text">传神的仕女画，也是叶志庆的拿手作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>天赋异禀非科班出身的叶志庆，于1992-2019年在中南部各县市办理个展多次，也参与海峡两岸联展， 2019年获云林社教惠风奖，平时为人谦卑低调，在水墨路上却不断努力精进、超越自我，他的作品曾荣登多项专集，包括：1995年杰出中青代名家书画巡回展专集、1996年台湾美术年鉴专集及亚洲金牌奖国画巡回展专集，1997及1998年连续荣登中华民国美术家名鉴专集，是云林县内杰出的资深水墨画家！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511742" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511742-766597.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511742-766597-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511742" class="wp-caption-text">叶志庆(右)和友人设于作品前。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14511744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511744" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511744-766598.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511744-766598-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511744" class="wp-caption-text">叶志庆这次更以陶板画，展现传统建筑与古早乡土情怀（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从小生长于台湾典型农家的叶志庆，对于记录台湾乡土风情与传统水墨创作情有独钟，这次更以陶板画，展现传统建筑与古早乡土情怀，他说，在陶板作画跟在宣纸上作画不同，必须层层堆叠上色，最后再喷上铀后，必须经过三进三出的高温窑烧，才能完成作品，为了这次作品，帮他烧陶的朋友还特别花了10万元，重新购置一套新的电窑，让他很感动！</p>
<p>叶志庆说，这次举办画展的目的是为了推广传统水墨技法，希望有更多的云林学子来观赏，因为这套传统水墨技法即将失传，现在学生学的大多是西方的水彩画或油画，他的作品虽然得过不少奖的肯定，却敌不过一张正规的学历和相关的证照，而无法到学校任课教学，这是他最感无奈的事！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511746-766600.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511746-766600-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511746" class="wp-caption-text">儿时的浊水溪作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14511747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511747" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511747-766601.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511747-766601-450x424.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="424"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511747" class="wp-caption-text">叶志庆耐人寻味的铁道作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14511748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511748" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511748-766602.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511748-766602-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511748" class="wp-caption-text">叶志庆公开亮相他最近完成的日本神社作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14511749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511749" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511749-766603.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14511749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511749-766603-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511749" class="wp-caption-text">叶志庆的现代派作品，展现了他运墨的高超功力。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次展出的铁道作品，相当引人注目，一条连结斗南火车站及后里通霄隧道的铁路，在作者细腻的笔触下，却变得风情万种，微带S型的铁道，美化了整个空间的线条，在远处隐约可以看到二个戴着帽子的人悠闲的看着远方的蓝天白云，整条铁道布满着错落有致的小石头，各就其位的躺着，让整个画面充满宁静祥和，铁道上的枕木仔细看，都不一样，曾经走过人生低潮，豁达乐观的叶志庆说，“他的画作没有哀伤的，带给人只有美好与祥和！”</p>
<p>还有笔触细腻的山水画，传神的仕女画，都令人赞赏不已！值得大家来观赏。</p>
