<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>盆栽种菜︰鲜甜好吃的韭菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519708-P.37-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>好吃的韭菜可以自己种，从选苗到采收，轻松打造自给自足的家庭菜园！（苹果屋提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>营造家庭菜园，只要一个盆栽，不洒药也能种出鲜甜好吃的蔬菜！常吃的、想吃的都可以自己种，从选苗到采收，轻松打造自给自足的家庭菜园。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">栽种叶菜类︰韭菜</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14519717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519717" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519717-P.36-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14519717" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519717-P.36-1-450x692.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="461" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519717" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>DATA</p>
<p><strong>科目：</strong>石蒜科<br />
<strong>难易度：</strong>★<br />
<strong>栽种场所：</strong>日照充足处<br />
<strong>生育适温：</strong>20～25℃<br />
<strong>列间：</strong>10～15cm<br />
<strong>连作障碍：</strong>有<br />
<strong>易发生的病虫害：</strong>几乎没有<br />
<strong>盆器尺寸：</strong>小型?宽20cm?长50cm?高18cm<br />
<strong>栽种时程：</strong>如图</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519716" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519716" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519716-P.36-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519716" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519716-P.36-2-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519716" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">●播种</span></h2>
<p><strong>条播播种：</strong>挖出两条深约1cm的浅沟，两列的间隔距离10～15cm。种子不重叠地散播在土壤中，补土后轻压，再浇满水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519715-P.36-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519715-P.36-3-450x312.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519715" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">●追肥</span></h2>
<p><strong>施肥：</strong>播种后过一个月左右，将肥料施于两侧，并稍微拨松土壤表面使两者混合。之后以二～三周一次为基准追肥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519714" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519714-P.36-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519714-P.36-4-450x303.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519714" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14519713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519713" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519713-P.36-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519713-P.36-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519713" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>覆土：</strong>将土壤覆盖至植株底部，轻压土面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519711" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519711-P.37-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519711-P.37-1-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519711" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">●采收</span></h2>
<p><strong>从植株底部剪下：</strong>待植株长至25～30cm，底部留下3～4cm的长度，再用剪刀采收下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519708" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519708-P.37-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519708-P.37-4-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519708" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>施肥：</strong>采收后再将肥料施于两侧，稍微拨松土壤表面使两者混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519709" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519709-P.37-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519709-P.37-3-450x303.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519709" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">★POINT</span></p>
<p><strong>全部采收完毕再追肥：</strong>这些被留下来的植株还会再发新芽、再次长大，所以为了促进生长，等全部采收完毕后要一并进行追肥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519712" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519712-P.36-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519712-P.36-6-450x230.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519712" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>再次采收</strong>：待植株再次长到25～30cm，一样留下3～4cm的长度，再用剪刀剪下来。之后也以同样的方式重复采收即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519710" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519710-P.37-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519710-P.37-2-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519710" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>播种一次可以收割好几年：</strong>韭菜是多年生植物，只要进行换盆，便可以重新生长。冬季植株枯萎后，把地上部全割除来度冬，等到了春季来临，它会重新发芽，便能再次采收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519707" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519707-P.37-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519707-P.37-5-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519707" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
