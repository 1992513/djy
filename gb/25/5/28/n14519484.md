<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>无农药安心吃！盆栽种菜：白花椰菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519494-P.20-1-e1748421958344-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>只要一个盆栽，不洒药也能种出鲜甜好吃的蔬菜！常吃的、想吃的都可以自己种。（苹果屋提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>营造家庭菜园，只要一个盆栽，不洒药也能种出鲜甜好吃的蔬菜！常吃的、想吃的都可以自己种，从选苗到采收，轻松打造自给自足的家庭菜园。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">栽种叶菜类<strong>：</strong>白花椰菜</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14519494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519494" style="width: 303px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519494-P.20-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14519494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519494-P.20-1-450x691.jpg" alt="" width="303" b="465" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519494" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>DATA</p>
<p><strong>科目：</strong>十字花科<br />
<strong>难易度：</strong>★★<br />
<strong>栽种场所：</strong>日照充足处<br />
<strong>生育适温：</strong>20～25℃<br />
<strong>株间：</strong>40cm<br />
<strong>连作障碍：</strong>有<br />
<strong>易发生的病虫害：</strong>青虫<br />
<strong>盆器尺寸：</strong>大型?直径35cm?高32cm<br />
<strong>栽种时程：</strong>如图</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519493" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519493-P.20-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519493-P.20-2-450x423.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519493" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">●选苗</span></h2>
<p>?有3～5片本叶。<br />
?有子叶更好。<br />
?叶片漂亮又结实。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519492" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519492-P.20-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519492-P.20-3-450x323.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519492" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">●定植</span></h2>
<p><strong>把幼苗种进盆栽内：</strong>在土壤中挖出比黑软盆还要大一点的洞，把幼苗从黑软盆取出放进盆栽内，覆土后轻压，再浇满水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519491" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519491-P.20-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519491-P.20-4-450x321.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519491" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>架设临时支柱：</strong>将木棍插在幼苗旁边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519490" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519490-P.20-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519490-P.20-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519490" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">★POINT</span></p>
<p><strong>临时支柱可防止倒塌：</strong>若幼苗很强壮站得很稳，可以不用架临时支柱，但为了预防被风吹倒，还是架着会比较安心。只要能稍微支撑住幼苗即可，因此用免洗筷也行，待植株茁壮后即可拔除。</p>
<p><strong>摘除黄叶或耗叶：</strong>不健康的叶片是问题的来源。生长过程中若发现有变黄的叶片要立即摘除。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519489" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519489-P.21-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519489-P.21-1-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519489" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">●追肥</span></h2>
<p><strong>施肥1：</strong>定植后过一个月左右，将肥料施于植株周围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519488" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519488-P.21-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519488-P.21-2-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519488" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>施肥2：</strong>自第一次施肥后过一个月左右，再次将肥料施于植株周围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519487" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519487-P.21-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519487-P.21-3-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519487" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">●采收</span></h2>
<p><strong>切断茎部：</strong>等到花蕾的直径长到约15cm大，就用菜刀从茎部割下白花椰菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14519486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14519486" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519486-P.21-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14519486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14519486-P.21-4-450x311.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14519486" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>★品尝美味小诀窍</strong></span></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">用</span>比沸水还低</strong>的<strong><span style="color: #800000;">80℃汆烫</span></strong>：十字花科的蔬菜特色就是含有些微呛辣味，烹煮时必须用适当的温度汆烫才能保留其特色。建议用比沸水还要再低温的80℃来汆烫<span style="color: #800000;">（在一公升的滚水内加入300ml的冷开水即约80℃）</span>。</p>
