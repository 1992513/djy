<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多台湾商会30周年庆典盛大举行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511116-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>多伦多台湾商会30周年庆晚会现场盛况，政商云集、节目精彩，展现台湾社群凝聚力与多元活力。（主办方提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年05月16日讯】多伦多台湾商会（Taiwan Merchants Association of Toronto, TMAT）于2025年5月3日举办主题为“荣耀三十，展望未来”的30周年庆祝晚宴。活动在 The Garden Banquet and Convention Centre 盛大登场，吸引超过500位来宾共襄盛举，场面热闹非凡，展现出加拿大台湾社群强大的凝聚力与深远影响力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511107" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/5/16/n14511103.md#1/1-4152" rel="attachment wp-att-14511107"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14511107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511107-1-450x345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="460" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511107" class="wp-caption-text">多伦多台湾商会30周年庆晚会现场盛况，政商云集、节目精彩，展现台湾社群凝聚力与多元活力。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会星光熠熠，政要云集，包括加拿大联邦参议员 Michael L. MacDonald、联邦国会议员 Jamie Schmale、前国会议员 Bob Saroya、安省省议员 Daisy Wai、多伦多市议员 Lily Cheng、宾顿市市长 Patrick Brown，以及万锦市长 Frank Scarpitti 等多位重量级嘉宾亲临现场，向台湾商会表达肯定与祝贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/5/16/n14511103.md#1/3-2162" rel="attachment wp-att-14511109"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14511109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511109-3-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511109" class="wp-caption-text">多伦多台湾商会30周年庆晚会现场盛况，政商云集、节目精彩，展现台湾社群凝聚力与多元活力。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动内容丰富多元，结合传统与现代的精彩演出，包括鼓队、乐团及舞团的精彩表现。其中一大亮点为会长李沧伟与众多嘉宾携手登台共舞，象征商会与社区携手并进、共舞未来，现场气氛热烈高涨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/5/16/n14511103.md#1/2-2687" rel="attachment wp-att-14511108"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14511108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511108-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511108" class="wp-caption-text">多伦多台湾商会30周年庆晚会现场盛况，政商云集、节目精彩，展现台湾社群凝聚力与多元活力。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 data-start="567" data-end="580"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>慈善捐赠 回馈社会</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14511113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511113" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/5/16/n14511103.md#1/7-653" rel="attachment wp-att-14511113"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14511113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511113-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511113" class="wp-caption-text">多伦多台湾商会30周年庆晚会现场盛况，政商云集、节目精彩，展现台湾社群凝聚力与多元活力。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="582" data-end="748">延续长年致力公益的精神，商会慈善基金会在活动当晚向三个慈善机构捐赠总额达 1 万 7,000 元的善款，受赠单位包括 St. Michael’s Hospital、Sunnybrook Foundation 与 Knight’s Table Food Bank。</p>
<p data-start="582" data-end="748">各单位代表亲自出席接受捐赠，场面温馨感人，展现台湾社群关怀回馈社会的实质行动。</p>

<h3 data-start="750" data-end="766"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong data-start="750" data-end="766">奖学金表扬 鼓励优秀学子</strong></span></h3>
<p data-start="768" data-end="868">为鼓励台裔青年学子积极进取、承接社群未来，今年商会奖学金基金会扩大办理奖助计划，首度开放高中生申请。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511114" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/5/16/n14511103.md#1/8-537" rel="attachment wp-att-14511114"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14511114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511114-8-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511114" class="wp-caption-text">多伦多台湾商会30周年庆晚会现场盛况，政商云集、节目精彩，展现台湾社群凝聚力与多元活力。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p data-start="768" data-end="868">当晚，颁发奖学金予三位杰出学生，包括两位大学生与一位高中生，以资鼓励，期望培养更多优秀的新生代领袖。</p>
