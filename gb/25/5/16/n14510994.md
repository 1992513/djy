<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>松年学院春季音乐成果发表 热闹登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511002-6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>音乐班学员颁发结业证书之后合影。（陆希／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年05月17日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）休士顿台湾松年学院于2025年5月15日（星期四）上午10点30分，在该学院会议厅举行“2025春季音乐班成果同乐会”，由长笛指导老师温雅如、钢琴老师黄诗婷、木笛老师周景仰、合唱老师杨爱莲与院长许胜弘共同筹划，学员数十人热情参与，展现一整学期的学习成果与音乐才华。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14510997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14510997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14510997-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14510997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14510997-1-600x432.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="432" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14510997" class="wp-caption-text">（左至右）杨爱莲老师、温雅如老师、院长许胜弘、周景仰老师及黄诗婷老师合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当日发表会包括有长笛、钢琴、乌克丽丽、木笛、合唱等多个班级。特别的是，合唱团首次加入成果发表，获得热烈掌声。活动中，多位老师及学生通力合作，大部分学员更是跨班参与演出，展现高度的学习热情与努力。虽然董事长陈美芬因事务繁忙未能出席，但特别由院长许胜弘代为表达对师生们的赞赏与支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14510999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14510999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14510999-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14510999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14510999-3-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14510999" class="wp-caption-text">直笛老师周景仰（左一）带领学员演奏。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14511000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511000" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511000-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14511000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511000-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511000" class="wp-caption-text">乌克丽丽班表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14511001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511001-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14511001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511001-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511001" class="wp-caption-text">长笛班的表演。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>各班表演内容丰富：长笛班由温雅如老师指导，Emily与Caroline演出精彩的二重奏，并搭配多首生动曲目，部分演出更加入即兴元素与观众互动；木笛班（直笛班）虽仅三月开始授课，但学员进步显着，表演包括《大力士的舞蹈》《Blessed be you, Heaven&#8217;s Queen》等曲目，学员来自多元背景，课堂以中英文进行，气氛活泼热烈；乌克丽丽班因赖忠智老师缺席，由班长周景仰领军完成演出，获得好评。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14510998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14510998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14510998-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14510998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14510998-2-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14510998" class="wp-caption-text">松年合唱团演出。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>合唱团由杨爱莲老师指挥带领，演唱《阳光和小雨》，音色温柔动人。杨爱莲老师也独唱由李砚谱曲、辛弃疾词作的艺术歌曲《青玉案》，展现高超的声乐功力，赢得全场喝采。</p>
<p>会中院长许胜弘说明暑期课程安排，部分班级将持续开课，惟钢琴课因师资休假将调整时数，请学员主动与老师确认课表。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14511003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14511003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511003-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14511003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14511003-7-600x443.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14511003" class="wp-caption-text">93岁的学员曾敏（左）和黄诗婷老师钢琴四手联弹。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动尾声颁发奖状予各班学员与指导老师，并安排大合影留念。会后邀请所有与会者留下享用简单午餐，以促进学员之间交流与友谊，为本次音乐成果发表画下圆满句点。</p>
