<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>紫醉金迷  头社盆地的金针花与马鞭草交织而舞</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496450-762503-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>透过轮作与生态农法的搭配，农民在春初便开始整地种植，四月起陆续绽放。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年05月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾南投报导）四月底五月初，日月潭南侧的头社活盆地，悄然被花海染上金橘与紫的梦。这里是全台金针花最早开花之地，而今年，园区更种下大片马鞭草，成就一幅令人屏息的春日画卷。行走其间，宛若踏入一场自然编织的诗意地毯。</p>
<p>“本周开始，给你满满的‘紫醉金迷’，不论晴天阴天雨天、晨曦或夕阳，只要你喜欢，就是最好的景色。” —— 空拍达人 Nina（2025.04.30）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496444" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496444-762497.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496444" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496444-762497.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1152" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496444" class="wp-caption-text">四月底五月初，日月潭南侧的头社活盆地，悄然被花海染上金橘与紫的梦。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14496443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496443" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496443-762496.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496443-762496.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496443" class="wp-caption-text">今年园区种下大片马鞭草，成就一幅令人屏息的春日画卷。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>活盆地的地质奇景? ?让花在云雾与山影中绽放</h2>
<p>头社活盆地因地壳断层而形成，四周群山环绕、水汽丰沛。这片低地具备良好的排水与日夜温差，正是金针花与马鞭草的理想生长环境。</p>
<p>金针花（萱草），花语为“忘忧”，象征温柔的思念与母爱；每朵花只开一天，却日日绽放，如同土地的呼吸。马鞭草则以其坚韧、清雅的姿态迎风起舞，花语为“忍耐与魅力”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496449" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496449-762502.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496449-762502.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496449" class="wp-caption-text">马鞭草则以其坚韧、清雅的姿态迎风起舞，花语为“忍耐与魅力”。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14496448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496448" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496448-762501.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496448-762501.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1152" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496448" class="wp-caption-text">金针花（萱草），花语为“忘忧”，象征温柔的思念与母爱。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>掌握花期的节奏? ?最早绽放的金针花海</h2>
<p>日月潭头社的金针花从四月底就开始盛开，为台湾金针花季最早的地点之一。透过轮作与生态农法的搭配，农民在春初便开始整地种植，四月起陆续绽放，盛期将持续至六月中旬。</p>
<p>今年特别增种的马鞭草，须种于排水良好、阳光充足的地区，与金针花错落相植，使视觉层次更加丰富。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496451" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496451-762504.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496451-762504.jpg" alt="" width="2048" b="1536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496451" class="wp-caption-text">马鞭草，须种于排水良好、阳光充足的地区。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>花海中? ?空拍与人景共舞</h2>

<p>“请游客帮我一起挥挥手，大家都很配合，增添趣味～有人问，是谁在操控？哈哈，人就在你眼前ㄋㄟ” “空拍机飞在花毯上，也有人看不见，一直问在哪里ㄚ？完美隐身。” —— Nina</p>
