<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>地震后四川青川又遭冰雹突袭 房屋农田受损</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14495959-2a2b3da5c306446c13379f83cfa540f6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>4月30日17时45分，四川广元市青川县突遭短时冰雹天气袭击，路面瞬间成“冰河”。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年05月01日讯】4月30日17时45分，四川广元市青川县突遭短时冰雹天气袭击，导致部分农田和经济作物受损。</p>
<p>网传视频显示，冰雹密度很大，路面瞬间成“冰河”，白茫茫一片。由于冰雹突然来袭，有摊主来不及收摊。一视频拍摄者还看到，有摩托车骑手被“冰河”阻在路口不敢过马路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14495962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495962" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14495962-9389f9cf2459c24ded084dbdb6e41998.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14495962-9389f9cf2459c24ded084dbdb6e41998-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495962" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14495963" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14495963-ffc6405fc0150fb11b19889ebce46066.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14495963-ffc6405fc0150fb11b19889ebce46066-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495963" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14495964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14495964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14495964-ab7881c8b8993c1ddf093fbcbb60985e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14495964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14495964-ab7881c8b8993c1ddf093fbcbb60985e-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14495964" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据川观新闻报导，受强对流天气影响，广元市青川县遭遇冰雹突袭。冰雹天气持续约10分钟，主要集中在青川县乔庄镇、大院回族乡两个乡镇。</p>
<p>受此影响，该县部分瓦房、雨棚等建筑物屋顶破损；部分农田及经济作物受灾，具体受损面积和作物种类，农业部门还在核实当中。暂无人员伤亡报告。</p>
<p>当天下午5点37分，青川县气象台发布冰雹橙色预警：青川县乔庄镇、骑马乡、蒿溪回族乡、沙州镇、木鱼镇等乡镇6小时内可能出现冰雹伴随雷电天气，并存在雹灾风险。</p>
<p>不过，据封面新闻报导，截至当晚发稿，当地天气已转晴，冰雹天气已结束。</p>
<p>在此之前，4月28日13时8分，在四川广元市青川县附近发生4.1级左右地震。</p>
