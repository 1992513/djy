<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世华亚城成功举办第二届贸易展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496784-0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>遴选出的美国高中生Brian Navarro-Mclean（左三）和他的母亲Roseanne Mclean（左二）高兴地与世华理事们、嘉宾合影 (毕倩/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年05月02日讯】（大纪元记者毕倩亚城报导）由世界华人工商妇女企管协会亚特兰大分会（GFCBW-Atlanta Chapter）主办的“2025年度商展”于4月27日下午在The Standard Club隆重登场，吸引了超过150位宾客和社区民众热情参与，场面热闹非凡，活动圆满成功。</p>
<p>本次商展设有25个摊位，涵盖了时尚服饰、精致手工艺品、珠宝饰品、美容保健、专业保险咨询、大学申请与升学辅导、夏令营推广，以及新鲜空运来美的台湾知名素食小吃，琳琅满目的商品和服务吸引了现场络绎不绝的人潮，展现出华人社区强大的创业能量与文化魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496787" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496787-1-e1746146961282.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496787-1-e1746146961282.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496787" class="wp-caption-text">丰盛基金会摊位 (毕倩/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14496788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496788" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496788-2-e1746147049721.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496788-2-e1746147049721.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496788" class="wp-caption-text">中华总会负责人给来宾发奖 (毕倩/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14496789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496789" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496789-3-e1746147156787.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496789-3-e1746147156787.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496789" class="wp-caption-text">手工艺编织摊位 (毕倩/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动当天，侨教中心阎树荣主任与各位侨务荣誉职人员在现场与民众亲切互动，并肯定了世华亚城在促进社区凝聚力与推广华人文化所做出的努力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496790" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496790-4-e1746147232550.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496790-4-e1746147232550.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496790" class="wp-caption-text">中华总会与嘉宾合影。后排左一：阎树荣主任 (毕倩/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外， John’s Creek市警局局长Mark Mitchell与Captain Coble到场推广John’s Creek社区警务与公共服务。透过他们的分享，现场来宾更加了解如何与警方合作，共同营造更安全、友善的居住环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496792" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496792-5-e1746147350737.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496792-5-e1746147350737.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496792" class="wp-caption-text">世华亚城姐妹们详见欢，左一：会长Janice Lu (毕倩/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次商展亮点包括：</p>
<ul>
<li>每小时抽出惊喜奖品，让现场气氛高潮不断；</li>
<li>提供免费茶点，让参与者一边逛展、一边轻松交流；</li>
<li>邀集25家不同领域的专业摊商，展示最新产品与服务；</li>
<li>邀请专家举办商业讲座，分享创业与职涯发展的宝贵经验；</li>
<li>促进商业媒合与网络拓展，为本地华商与企业家搭建交流平台。</li>
</ul>
<p>从台湾空运来的特色素食零嘴、手工编织珠宝、精美手绣作品，道地的台湾美食如珍珠奶茶、台式三明治、三杯鸡、手工香肠、肉圆等，皆受到现场民众的热烈欢迎，摊位前人潮络绎不绝，现场洋溢着温馨又热闹的气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496795" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496795-6-e1746147503588.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496795-6-e1746147503588.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496795" class="wp-caption-text">台湾美食摊位 (毕倩/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>特别值得一提的是，世华亚城全体会员也在现场以合影方式表达对台湾加入WHO（世界卫生组织）“挺台湾、入世卫”的支持，与全球89个分会相呼应，共同为台湾发声，展现世华姐妹团结一心、关怀国际议题的精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496797" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496797-7-e1746147600504.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496797-7-e1746147600504.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496797" class="wp-caption-text">世华亚城创会长陈家琪讲话（中：陈家琪）(毕倩/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>现任会长吕怡蓉介绍，此次贸易展得到丰盛基金会董事长曾担任世华亚城第二任会长的邱婷兰女士的大力支持与赞助。当天，邱女士兴致勃勃给笔者介绍了两位到场嘉宾-今年被乔州扶轮社遴选出的唯一一位美国高中生Brian Navarro-Mclean将参与台湾的文化交流。他和母亲来到贸易展并表示已开始学习中文，他对8月份的台湾之行相当期待。届时由乔州扶轮社资助的Brian将去台北一所高中学习10个月。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14496798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14496798" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496798-8-e1746147675511.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14496798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14496798-8-e1746147675511.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14496798" class="wp-caption-text">世华亚城理事会成员与嘉宾合影 (毕倩/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天的贸易展也得到许多世华亚城理事会姐妹们的支持。来宾们在欢乐的氛围中度过了一个愉快和满载而归的下午。</p>
