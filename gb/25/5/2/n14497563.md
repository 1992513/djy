<h6>2025 BMW M3。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年05月03日讯】BMW M3的崇高地位，举足轻重，所以BMW也极为重视，自1986年推出首辆E30的M3，在当年赛事中都获得佳绩，续而推出了E36（二代）、E46（三代），而这次测试的是第六代G80的M3，属四门版本的M3，也是M3系列中的入门车款。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://dervszfjn9udk.cloudfront.net/F7w&#29983/embed/YDIAV3pz-F0?si=RqOHHYt_xzGKb_8k" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>第六代M3拥有巨型肾形格栅、M双横条、肌肉感轮拱和侧裙脚，满足冷却与空气动力学需求。标配有碳纤维车顶，新设计BMW LED大灯整合近远光，搭配箭形日行灯与转向灯，可选自适应LED与矩阵远光灯，内嵌蓝色元素彰显科技感。车侧有M3的徽章排气口，车尾有同样的徽章。M3标配有专为M车而锻造的轻合金轮圈，采用特定轮圈尺寸， 测试车前轴配19&#8243;轮圈，而后轴配20&#8243;轮圈，并装有275/35ZR19 Michelin高性能轮胎（前轴）及285/30ZR20 Michelin高性能轮胎（后轴），增强汽车操控性能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14497578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497578" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497578-2025_BMW_M3_04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497578-2025_BMW_M3_04-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497578" class="wp-caption-text">2025 BMW M3。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14497585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497585" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497585-2025_BMW_M3_08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497585-2025_BMW_M3_08-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497585" class="wp-caption-text">巨型肾形格栅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>底盘技术还包括M Servotronic转向系统和整合式刹车系统，标配了M Compound刹车和可选配的M Carbon陶瓷刹车。前轴配备六活塞固定卡钳，后轴配备单活塞浮动卡钳。M Carbon陶瓷刹车不仅配备更大且更耐热的刹车碟，M Carbon也比M Compound刹车轻约14 kg。M3备有前双节弹簧支柱和后五连杆轴的M专属悬挂，在极端动态操控与日常使用都能有最佳体验。标配的自适应M专属悬挂可根据需要调整电子控制阻尼器的反应，驾驶者可以从三种不同驾驶模式设置中选择，以根据需求和个人偏好改变车辆的操控性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14497587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497587" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497587-2025_BMW_M3_06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497587-2025_BMW_M3_06-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497587" class="wp-caption-text">BMW LED大灯。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14497589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497589-2025_BMW_M3_15.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497589-2025_BMW_M3_15-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497589" class="wp-caption-text">典型的BMW尾灯设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>引擎技术源自BMW的赛车经验，3升直六引擎具高转速特性，采用无套筒曲轴箱、锻造曲轴与铁涂层缸孔，并配备3D列印缸盖核心加强冷却效能。TwinPower双涡轮增压、高精度喷射和可变气门正时技术提高了性能输出。入门级M3的动力输出为473 hp，0-60 mph加速约需4.1秒，而高一级的Competition后驱车型引擎通过电脑调校，输出增至503 hp，最高版本的Competition xDrive则增加至523 hp，0-60 mph也只需3.4秒，加速表现出色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14497590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497590" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497590-2025_BMW_M3_21.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497590-2025_BMW_M3_21-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497590" class="wp-caption-text">3升直六引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BMW M3的内饰用上了M系专用皮革，测试车采用了Kyalami Orange/Black双色皮革，看起来相当清新，并装有选配的Alcantara方向盘，平底设计与红色标记强化赛车氛围。座椅是M系列专用碳纤维桶椅，有电动调整与加热，座椅为汽车减重达9.6 kg，兼顾赛车功能与舒适性，椅背颈部位置有“M3”装饰并会发亮。内饰多处也使用碳纤维材质提升质感及减重。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14497591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497591" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497591-2025_BMW_M3_33.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497591-2025_BMW_M3_33-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497591" class="wp-caption-text">采用了Kyalami Orange/Black双色皮革的内装。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14497592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497592" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497592-2025_BMW_M3_23.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497592-2025_BMW_M3_23-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497592" class="wp-caption-text">碳纤维桶椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BMW M3装有12.3&#8243;仪表板，与14.9&#8243;中控台高解析度萤幕，曲面萤幕面向驾驶者，令驾驶者更能专注于道路上，配备了HUD（抬头显示器）也是同一用途。升级的BMW个人助理配合最新一代操作系统8.5版，驾驶者与车辆之间的互动完全依赖触控和语言。M3有三区恒温系统，前座椅加热及方向盘加热，在冷天也可暖心暖身。</p>
<p>除了基于云端的导航系统BMW Maps外，标配的BMW Live Cockpit Plus还有两个USB端口i和智能手机整合，支持使用Apple CarPlay和Android Auto。 BMW M3标配10个扬声器和205瓦放大器的HiFi扬声器系统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14497593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497593" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497593-2025_BMW_M3_29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497593-2025_BMW_M3_29-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497593" class="wp-caption-text">方向盘有M1及M2两种设定记忆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14497594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497594" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497594-2025_BMW_M3_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497594-2025_BMW_M3_26-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497594" class="wp-caption-text">6速手动变速。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>测试车装有6速手动变速，每一档也能轻松挂上，离合器也比以往更容易操作，在挂上一个档时，离合器也还没放开，便会在仪表板显示档位，那就不会有入错档的情况了。在方向盘左右位置有各有一个红色键，分别是M1及M2，是预设的设定，将喜欢的悬挂硬度、方向盘反应，油门反应预先设定好，那么以后便可一键达到要求，非常方便。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14497595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14497595" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497595-2025_BMW_M3_27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14497595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14497595-2025_BMW_M3_27-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14497595" class="wp-caption-text">引擎启动键。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
