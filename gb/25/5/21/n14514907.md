<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠博爱楼剪彩 政要嘉宾致贺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514899-DSC05366-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿华埠“博爱楼”可负担住房5月21日剪彩，诸多嘉宾莅临。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年05月22日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）波士顿华埠“博爱楼”（Pok Oi Residences）已投入使用，新增85个单元可负担住房。5月21日中午，政要嘉宾们莅临现场，为这栋新楼剪彩。</p>
<p>“50多年前，中华公所先贤创建了大同村。今日，我们承继前人的事业，建造博爱楼，以大同世界和博爱精神，继续造福波士顿华埠。”纽英崙中华公所主席雷国辉说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14514898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514898-DSC05361.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514898-DSC05361-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514898" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿华埠“博爱楼”可负担住房5月21日剪彩，诸多嘉宾莅临。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14514893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514893-DSC05300.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514893-DSC05300-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514893" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉讲解“大同”、“博爱”的涵义。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中华公所旗下产业博爱楼，位于波士顿华埠Harrison街。该处建楼之前是大同村停车场。这栋建筑从2023年初开工，7月举办破土庆祝，到2025年2月开放入住。</p>
<p>根据中华公所的资料，楼内共有85个单元，租金根据租客的年收入来计算，其中17套租给年收入低于区域收入中位数（AMI）30%的租客，28套要求50%的AMI，18套要求60%的AMI，还有22套要求80%的AMI。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14514890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514890-DSC05234.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514890-DSC05234-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514890" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏及侨教中心主任高家富等嘉宾参观“博爱楼”可负担住房。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14514901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514901" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514901-DSC05382.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514901-DSC05382-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514901" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）参观“博爱楼”可负担住房。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在剪彩典礼中，新租客代表Richard对中华公所表达谢意。“我很感激能有这样的机会，一年前我曾住在我的车里。如果有更多像这样的建筑，更多的机会就会被创造出来。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14514897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514897" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514897-DSC05353.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514897-DSC05353-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514897" class="wp-caption-text">新租客代表Richard对中华公所表达感谢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>庆贺住房发展成就</strong></h3>
<p>
剪彩典礼在当天正午开始。嘉宾们首先致词，向中华公所的住房发展成就表达祝贺；随后他们在博爱楼正门前剪断彩带，庆祝新可负担住房投入使用。在接下来的参观过程中，住房单元的精美装修和壮观外景，赢得嘉宾们的连声称赞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14514900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514900" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514900-DSC05380.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514900-DSC05380-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514900" class="wp-caption-text">在“博爱楼”公寓内，可看到城市景色。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14442767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442767" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442767-DSC01642.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442767-DSC01642-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442767" class="wp-caption-text">今年2月20日，纽英崙中华公所部分董事参观华埠新建成的“博爱楼”可负担住房，纷纷表达赞赏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）说，相比其它城市的唐人街，波士顿华埠的一大特色，就是它不仅仅是商业区和旅游景点，还是一个社区生态系统，“一个让你感觉属于这里的地方”。</p>
<p>“我们总是希望华埠尽可能地保持为居民区，尽可能地保持可负担性。”吴弭说。</p>
<p>她特别谈到，博爱楼拥有隔音窗户、通风过滤系统，并且符合最高的环保和可持续性标准，让居民享有清洁的环境。</p>
<p>吴弭还称赞这栋建筑的其它一些优点，包括向居民免费供水，靠近公共交通设施，提供多样房型等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14514892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514892" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514892-DSC05259.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514892-DSC05259-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514892" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏称赞了博爱楼的建设速度。他笑言，他第一次拜访中华公所时，听说这栋楼可以在1年多的时间内建成，并不是太相信，因为在台湾，这样的项目可能要建几年。所以当他上个月接到剪彩活动的邀请时，他感到意外惊喜。为此，廖朝宏感谢中华公所及各合作伙伴使这个项目成为现实。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14514896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514896-DSC05320.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514896-DSC05320-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514896" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参与剪彩的嘉宾，还包括波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）、麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）、侨教中心主任高家富，以及多位中华公所董事，还有中华耆英会、塔夫茨医疗中心等当地团体的代表等等。</p>
<p>参与博爱楼项目建设和管理的开发商，是“灯塔社区”（Beacon Communities）开发公司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14514894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514894-DSC05309.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514894-DSC05309-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514894" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14514895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514895-DSC05315.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514895-DSC05315-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514895" class="wp-caption-text">麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该公司总裁科恩（Josh Cohen）简要回顾了自2017年以来参与筹备这个项目的历程。“Covid、通货膨胀、建筑价格，各种逆风因素，让这一成功非常具有挑战性。它完成了，建好了，租出去了，建得非常漂亮，这需要很多努力、极强的韧性才能实现。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14514891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14514891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514891-DSC05236.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14514891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/05/id14514891-DSC05236-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14514891" class="wp-caption-text">灯塔社区公司总裁科恩（Josh Cohen）感谢各方合作伙伴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中华公所董事陈建立作诗感慨：“十年树木百年人，博爱剪彩为社群，中华公所千秋业，灯塔大同共感恩。”◇</p>
