<h6>台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年1月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月11日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾采访报导）台湾武陵农场樱花季8日登场，摄影师宋永泰表示，他们7日到武陵农场赏樱，虽然寒流来袭，但一大早上山恰逢难得晴天，有看到吉野樱、山樱花、河津樱盛开美景，并拍到很多美照与读者分享。</p>
<p>宋永泰说，台湾有三大樱花季，包括武陵樱花季、九族樱花季及阿里山樱花季，其中武陵农场樱花盛开，为台湾指标性赏樱景点。他曾去过武陵农场六七次，若从住家高雄开车要七八个小时，因为路途遥远且在北横公路上开车较危险，所以这次他们选择跟团赏樱，从高雄搭乘游览巴士，先在宜兰住一晚。从宜兰至武陵农场车程约2—2.5小时，隔天上山可以悠闲自在地赏樱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14434627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434627" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434627-2502102136282378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434627" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434627-2502102136282378.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434627" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>台湾寒流来袭，他说，武陵樱花季气温低，可能降雪或结冰，需注意交通管制。今年武陵农场樱花因气候寒冷，盛开时间延后，红粉佳人与昭和樱，目前花况不如预期，还在含苞待放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14434630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434630" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434630-2502102136332378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434630" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434630-2502102136332378.jpg" alt="" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434630" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14434634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434634" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434634-2502102136442378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434634" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434634-2502102136442378.jpg" alt="" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434634" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>武陵农场内樱花陆续绽放，他指出，游客应选择樱花盛开区域，才能欣赏到吉野樱、山樱花、河津樱等樱花品种。他们来到武陵农场入口，就看到河津樱浅粉大爆发，高兴的是他们到山上时出太阳，园区内桃红色的山樱花已经盛开，人站在樱花树下晒太阳，看着漂亮的樱花感觉很浪漫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14434633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434633-2502102136392378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434633" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434633-2502102136392378-600x329.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434633" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14434635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434635-2502102136462378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434635" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434635-2502102136462378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434635" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“台中武陵农场景点丰富，四季皆宜游览。”宋永泰说，除了武陵樱花季最为&#33879;称之外，四季景致以春之花海、夏之果蔬、秋之枫红、冬之瑞雪&#33879;称，尤其以七家湾文化遗址及国宝鱼樱花钩吻鲑最为珍稀，228连假即将到来，武陵农场也相当适合全家游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14434641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434641-2502102136552378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434641" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434641-2502102136552378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434641" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14434640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434640-2502102136552378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434640" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434640-2502102136552378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434640" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>喜欢赏樱的宋永泰比较日本、韩国及台湾赏樱经验提到，在国外的樱花季有时候预订了赏樱行程，但到了当地发现樱花还没开，那就是铩羽而归，若没有调整心态，花了很多钱还很扫兴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14434632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434632" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434632-2502102136372378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434632" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434632-2502102136372378.jpg" alt="" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434632" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14434636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434636" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434636-2502102136492378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434636" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434636-2502102136492378.jpg" alt="" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434636" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰指出，台湾赏樱景点众多，在台湾国内赏樱都很开心，因为资讯取得方便，还可以轻松规划一日来回行程，像这次他们的行程安排与景点选择皆符合预期，成功捕捉到樱花盛开的最佳画面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14434629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14434629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434629-2502102136302378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14434629" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14434629-2502102136302378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14434629" class="wp-caption-text">台湾武陵农场樱花季2025年2月8日登场。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>他分享，现在除了有樱花可赏之外，桃花、李花也盛开，游客可以欣赏梅、樱同框美景，趁着武陵樱花季登场，游客可以充分体验武陵农场之美。</p>
