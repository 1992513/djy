<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金城银行恭贺大家顺风顺水顺财神！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432214-414fbf7507f25836d5627af5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>金城银行百利总行员工合影。（大宇/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月07日讯】（大纪元讯）庆祝黄历新年到来，金城银行祝贺所有客户和朋友身体健康、心想事成，蛇年大吉！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432219-fe92b0c0110cb4b84dbe555a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432219-fe92b0c0110cb4b84dbe555a-600x369.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432219" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）金城银行林懿明、曾柏桦、杨明耕、Al Duran、谭秋晴和公关部副总裁侯秀宜与财神合影，象征金城银行2025年大发好寓意。（大宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金城银行在2025年1月29日特别举办龙狮表演，并邀请Petrosky Elementary School二至四年级的小朋友共同庆祝新年。参观完龙狮表演之后，由Petrosky Elementary School蒋秀莲老师带领的学生们表演中文贺年歌曲，小朋友活泼逗趣的表演为现场增添热闹气氛！金城银行杨明耕副董事长、曾柏桦副董事长、Al Duran副董事长、董事谭秋晴副市长、林懿明执行长、总经理吴国驹在现场发放红包并和客户及同仁同欢，庆祝新春！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432218" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432218-3870793cf78f01580546bbe4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432218-3870793cf78f01580546bbe4-600x264.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="264" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432218" class="wp-caption-text">Petrosky Elementary School二至四年级的小朋友共同庆祝新年。（大宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14432216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432216-4715f35ad368343aa24e10c9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432216-4715f35ad368343aa24e10c9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432216" class="wp-caption-text">金城银行董事们发红包给Petrosky Elementary School大小朋友。（大宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金城银行在蛇年开春即宣告喜讯，2024年总资产额突破20亿美元，银行存款总额增加1.93亿元，达16.7亿元；未偿还贷款总额增加2.3亿元，达17.1亿元。金城银行2024年业务表现稳健成长，经由稳健的信用风险管理，强化信用管理机能，并扩充专职中小企业贷款（SBA）营运单位。为庆祝黄历新年，金城银行特别推出8个月定期存款，年利率4.50%APY, 如您需要更多相关讯息或有任何疑问，欢迎与各分行联络查询。</p>
<p>林懿明执行长表示，金城银行的成功是各部门共同努力的成果，我们在回顾过去的一年的同时，也展望新的一年带来的机会。让我们保持创新、增强适应能力，并专注于为我们的客户和我们服务的社区提供卓越的服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432213-1aff8d578f00fb0f915afc53.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432213-1aff8d578f00fb0f915afc53-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432213" class="wp-caption-text">精彩的舞狮表演。（大宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14432215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432215-630cd9510d86a66889126512.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432215-630cd9510d86a66889126512-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432215" class="wp-caption-text">金城银行百利总行员工和舞狮和财神合影。（大宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>时逢新春佳节，金城银行吴光宜董事长、林懿明执行长暨银行董事和全体同仁敬祝大家蛇年行大运、富贵喜年来！金城银行为您的财务把关，与您共创利益、共享金色未来！有关本行最新资讯请参阅网站：<ahref="https://www.goldenbank-na.com/">www.goldenbank-na.com</a>。</p>
