<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中国博主镜头下：新年假期后城市依然萧条</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432086-e356fa0840b2a4048536c6efece4d35e-600x400.png" />
<h6>许多中国博主拍到，虽然正式上班日已过了几天，城里依然萧条，他们不禁发出疑问。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月07日讯】（大纪元记者宁海钟综合报导）中国经济下行，百业萧条已成常态。2025年中国新年之前，许多人提前返乡，未过完年又提早返城上班或再找工作。不过，许多中国博主拍到，虽然正式上班日已过了几天，城里依然萧条，他们不禁发出疑问。</p>
<p>2月7日大年初十，已是中国新年假期后正式上班的第三天，全国不同地方的博主当天拍下萧条的景象。</p>
<p>一位博主在广州拍下2月7日的现状：“今天正月初十了，带你们看看，广州制衣村、桥南新街的现状，对比去年同时期，还是要萧条许多，很多人都在纠结，是留在家里，还是去江浙沪，还是来广州，我个人建议，选择适合自己的才是最好的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432084" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432084-132a1b72e5b1ef72f6c1a17d4cfb5dd6.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14432084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432084-132a1b72e5b1ef72f6c1a17d4cfb5dd6-600x275.png" alt="" width="600" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432084" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一位博主拍摄海鲜市场，“当初的繁华似锦，如今的萧条落寞，是海鲜这行落寞了，还是其它行业也如此……”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432070" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432070-85f5725a9397b04bbf2df84c769ee2e0.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14432070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432070-85f5725a9397b04bbf2df84c769ee2e0-600x280.png" alt="" width="600" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432070" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一位博主说，“成都中心区气象一片萧条，乌云密布。倒闭，关门，破产，清算。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432074" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432074" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432074-61f63072b1511baf812117aa03dbe43b.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14432074" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432074-61f63072b1511baf812117aa03dbe43b-600x273.png" alt="" width="600" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432074" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一位博主说，“这就是中国现在的状况，房子无人居住，门面全部关闭，街上空无一人，道路四通八达，绿化整洁干净。表面繁荣昌盛，实则经济萧条、危机四伏。房地产的崩盘，标志着中国经济开始大滑坡！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432075" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432075" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432075-7e1c57b66152deaf5d7e60869b5ccc2e.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14432075" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432075-7e1c57b66152deaf5d7e60869b5ccc2e-600x270.png" alt="" width="600" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432075" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有博主说：“房价仅次于北上广深的厦门，中山路上空荡荡，倒闭了很多店铺。曾经熙熙攘攘的中山路，现游客稀少。海蛎煎涨到了30元（人民币，下同）一份。厦门到底经历了什么？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432086" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432086-e356fa0840b2a4048536c6efece4d35e.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432086-e356fa0840b2a4048536c6efece4d35e-600x357.png" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432086" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有博主拍视频说：“年初十的深圳！去年年初六才有这种冷清，今年太反常了，冷清街道不见人影，很多店铺都没开门！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432080-d6f5d0296f362183faec01188c29cba6.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14432080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432080-d6f5d0296f362183faec01188c29cba6-600x277.png" alt="" width="600" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432080" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有博主说，“如今大虞城也是这么萧条。”虞城隶属河南省商丘市。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432079" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432079-9a4d054a55e79bd7c23e740e41280738.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14432079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432079-9a4d054a55e79bd7c23e740e41280738-600x274.png" alt="" width="600" b="274" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432079" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一位博主写道：“今年比任何时候都要泠清了，经济大萧条了，这个时候还很多商家没开门，看来钱都不好挣了，今年要倒闭不少工厂和店铺了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432085" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432085" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432085-f73a9a29412147f37d0a45629322b269.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14432085" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432085-f73a9a29412147f37d0a45629322b269-600x295.png" alt="" width="600" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432085" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也有网友留言说，可能还要等一等，今年的开工应该会稍微晚点，可能过了正月十五（再过一周）以后。</p>
<p>近年来，中国经济持续恶化，到处是空荡荡的商场。很多中小民企、小商家苦苦挣扎，倒闭潮接二连三。2024年，近300万家餐饮门店倒闭，创下历史新高。2024年也有超过1.46万家晶元企业倒闭。做传统实业的也举步维艰，去年年底，浙江许村镇仅一个月就有26家服装厂老板跑路。外资撤离潮之下，中国工厂没有倒闭的也大量向东南亚搬迁。</p>
<p>早几天，提前回深圳的打工者秦先生告诉大纪元记者，现在一般的工厂，开工都在正月十五以后。今年正式一点的工厂开工不会超过初八初十。他怕回去迟了，岗位可能就没了。</p>
<p>也有网民发视频表示，大环境不好，打工都没地方去，“我们这些没学历没技术的被淘汰了，我闯国外去了”。他要去菲律宾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432089-d2386dbcdda2c572872a7d2b95569db9.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14432089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432089-d2386dbcdda2c572872a7d2b95569db9-600x430.png" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432089" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
