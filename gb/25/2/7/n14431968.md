<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>张善政视察士校埤塘生态公园改善 打造优质休憩场域</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431970-743327-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政致词。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月07日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政7日前往中坜区，视察“士校埤塘生态公园篮球场旁空地改善工程”。</p>
<figure id="14431969" aria-describedby="caption-14431969" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431969-743326-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431969-743326-450x299.jpg" alt="完成活化的士校埤塘生态公园。" width="601" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14431969" class="wp-caption-text">完成活化的士校埤塘生态公园。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政表示，桃园素有千埤之乡的美称，因此市府持续开辟埤塘公园并改善相关设施，其中士校埤塘生态公园是本市第4座完成活化的埤塘生态公园，工程妥善活化闲置空间、串联周边设施，为完善士校埤塘生态公园休憩环境的最后一块拼图。</p>
<figure id="14431971" aria-describedby="caption-14431971" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431971-743328-450x298.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431971-743328-450x298.jpg" alt="桃园市长张善政视察士校埤塘公园。" width="603" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14431971" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政视察士校埤塘公园。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>未来市府也会持续开辟、活化并改善埤塘生态公园，提供在地乡亲更多优质休闲游憩场域，打造宜居城市。</p>
<figure id="14431973" aria-describedby="caption-14431973" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431973-743330-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431973-743330-450x300.jpg" alt="士校埤塘公园的步道。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14431973" class="wp-caption-text">士校埤塘公园的步道。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14431972" aria-describedby="caption-14431972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431972-743329-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431972-743329-450x253.jpg" alt="桃园市长张善政和与会来宾合影。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14431972" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政和与会来宾合影。（桃园市府新闻处 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>工务局表示，本工程总经费约1,200万元，以士校埤塘生态公园自然环境为基础，沿用整体生态、森林、水岸意象及铺面红砖元素，透过铺面与高程整合串联既有步道系统，并设置多样化的全龄活动设施，打造整体无障碍步道系统，完善士校埤塘公园的服务机能与步道动线。◇</p>
