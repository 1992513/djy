<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>锣鼓喧天迎瑞气 美南银行新年活动热闹登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432228-070c9d7e0c666b81bc8e0743-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美南银行举办蛇年黄历新年庆祝活动。（大宇/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月08日讯】（大纪元记者孙玉玟休斯顿报导）1月29日星期三中午，美南银行举办蛇年黄历新年庆祝活动，特邀李氏金龙醒狮团在百利大道总行大厅带来精彩的舞狮表演。锣鼓喧天，瑞狮翻腾起舞，为银行增添浓厚的节庆氛围，象征蛇年吉祥如意、财源广进。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14432227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432227-05c3b8655699a9837413b207.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432227-05c3b8655699a9837413b207-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432227" class="wp-caption-text">美南银行董事长李承穗献上诚挚的新年祝福。（大宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14432226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432226-1a469288eda85bc11c3425e7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432226-1a469288eda85bc11c3425e7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432226" class="wp-caption-text">李氏金龙醒狮团瑞狮翻腾起舞。（大宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14432224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14432224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432224-81158e5e69d4020ed7239d2a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14432224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14432224-81158e5e69d4020ed7239d2a-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14432224" class="wp-caption-text">现场热闹非凡，喜庆洋溢。（大宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美南银行董事长李承穗率董事会成员及员工欢庆黄历新年。现场热闹非凡，气氛喜庆洋溢，银行贵宾、客户前来感受传统文化的魅力与年节欢愉。在一片欢声笑语中，美南银行董事会成员及员工们诚挚祝愿大家蛇年行大运，财源滚滚，万事顺遂。</p>
