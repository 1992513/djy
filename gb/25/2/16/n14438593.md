<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英崙专协庆元宵 精妙灯谜添乐趣</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438585-DSC01532-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙中华专业人员协会2月15日举办元宵节庆典，主持人Jessica展示灯谜，大家认真思考。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月17日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）谜面“人来风”，猜常用语二字；谜面“驾车游，哈哈”，猜常用语三字……约20条构思巧妙的灯谜，令参与者们时而认真思索，时而开怀大笑。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这是纽英崙中华专业人员协会2月15日的元宵节庆祝活动。嘉宾及侨团代表总计约50人，齐聚波士顿华埠的华人经济发展协会大厅，享用美食，竞猜灯谜，气氛愉快。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14438584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438584" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438584-DSC01500.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438584-DSC01500-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438584" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华专业人员协会2月15日举办元宵节庆典，参与者们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14438576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438576-DSC01420.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438576-DSC01420-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438576" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华专业人员协会2月15日举办元宵节庆典。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14438578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438578-DSC01434.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438578-DSC01434-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438578" class="wp-caption-text">主持人Jessica和先生卢彦君。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">猜灯谜是华人的元宵节传统，也是专协每年的元宵节庆典中最引人关注的活动。每年的灯谜都由袁尚贤博士原创。今年，袁尚贤博士虽然未能出席，但仍创作了约20道谜题，为这场周日餐会的参与者们增添兴致。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14438586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438586" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438586-DSC01541.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438586-DSC01541-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438586" class="wp-caption-text">猜中灯谜者获奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14438587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438587-DSC01543.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438587-DSC01543-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438587" class="wp-caption-text">猜中灯谜者获奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">主持人Jessica首先照例宣读了猜谜原则，即“断章取义、歪打正着、牵强附会、切忌犯面、力求典雅”。随后，她为大家展示谜题，还根据大家的反应情况给出提示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14438588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438588-DSC01548.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438588-DSC01548-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438588" class="wp-caption-text">猜中灯谜者获奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14438589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438589" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438589-DSC01580.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438589-DSC01580-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438589" class="wp-caption-text">猜中灯谜者获奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14438590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438590" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438590-DSC01586.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438590-DSC01586-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438590" class="wp-caption-text">猜中灯谜者获奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这些谜题有的立意巧妙，如“人来风”的答案是“客气”，“驾车游，哈哈”的答案是“开玩笑”；</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">有的涉及文艺或科学知识，如“三天又三天”（猜理化三字）的答案是“再结晶”，“看鼠辈登基”（猜成语四字）的答案是“望子成龙”；</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">也有的直白简单，如“蜀中平常人”（猜名人）的答案是“川普”，“此山之真面目是有学问的专头曼”（猜专协会员）的答案是会长卢彦君；</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">还有少量谜题涉及英文，如“Without a desk”（猜7字母单词）的答案是“Notable”。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">凡此种种谜题，吸引参与者们踊跃竞猜。每当有人猜中谜底的时候，大家都会报以热烈掌声。主持人将来宾捐赠的礼物，赠予猜中者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14438592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438592" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438592-DSC01589.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438592-DSC01589-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438592" class="wp-caption-text">猜灯谜“冠军桌”，人人都有奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14438591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438591-DSC01587.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438591-DSC01587-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438591" class="wp-caption-text">大家为猜中灯谜的人鼓掌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>欢庆元宵</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>嘉宾祝贺</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">餐会期间，专协董事长游子扬、会长卢彦君向来宾们表达欢迎与感谢，并送上新年祝福。建中校友会是这场餐会的协办方。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“不知道大家昨天是不是有庆祝情人节。但不管你们有没有时间庆祝，今天专协陪你们一起庆祝春节，跟情人节，还有我们的元宵节。”卢彦君说。他还说，专协正在转变活动方式，希望与更多年轻人有更多互动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14438740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438740" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438740-DSC01443.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438740-DSC01443-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438740" class="wp-caption-text">专协董事长游子扬送上元宵节祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14438577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438577-DSC01429.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438577-DSC01429-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438577" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影，左起：专协会长卢彦君、经文处组长蔡孟勋、专协董事长游子扬、经文处处长廖朝宏、组长黄耀良、侨教中心主任高家富。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、组长蔡孟勋、黄耀良、侨教中心主任高家富、中华公所主席雷国辉、黄氏宗亲会妇女部主任黄周丽桃、华人经济发展协会会长蒋宗壬等嘉宾也都参加了这场餐会。场地门口的“元宵节快乐”标语，由在场的中华书法会老师池元山书写。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14438582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438582-DSC01467.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438582-DSC01467-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438582" class="wp-caption-text">黄氏公所妇女部主任黄周丽桃（左1）、中华公所主席雷国辉（左2）到场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p style="font-weight: 400;">“过去几年大家看到我们经济的发展，我们在全球这种供应链非常重要的角色，我想这些都跟各位在这边的贡献和帮忙有关。”廖朝宏说。他还称赞，专协的元宵节活动，在侨界已颇具名气，很有文化气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14438581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14438581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438581-DSC01457.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14438581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14438581-DSC01457-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14438581" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏感谢大家支持中华民国台湾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
