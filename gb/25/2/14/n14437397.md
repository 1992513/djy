<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义玉女番茄精品礼盒 微风超市甜蜜开卖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437403-745010-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义玉女番茄礼盒“喜番?大好き”，微风超市甜蜜开卖。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月14日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）嘉义县政府推出玉女番茄精品礼盒“喜番?大好き”，本周将在台北微风百货超市限量开卖，精致小番茄礼盒象征嘉义县农产品的卓越品质，也为嘉义顶级玉女番茄开启精品市场的新篇章，让消费者能品味独特的甜蜜风味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14437398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14437398" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437398-745005.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14437398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437398-745005-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14437398" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义玉女番茄礼盒“喜番?大好き”，微风超市甜蜜开卖。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府推出“喜番?大好き”玉女番茄礼盒，名称融合“喜欢”与“小番茄”的谐音，象征番茄受人喜爱，也展现进军日本市场的愿景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14437399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14437399" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437399-745006.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14437399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437399-745006-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14437399" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义玉女番茄礼盒“喜番?大好き”，微风超市甜蜜开卖。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“喜番?大好き”采用嘉义县温室栽培的顶级玉女番茄，每颗皆由职人精心挑选，确保番茄甜度高、色泽饱满、皮薄多汁。礼盒内还有嘉义梅山出产的梅粉，撒在番茄上再一口吃下，口感酸甜交织，层次丰富。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14437400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14437400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437400-745007.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14437400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437400-745007-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14437400" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义玉女番茄礼盒“喜番?大好き”，微风超市甜蜜开卖。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14437401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14437401" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437401-745008.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14437401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437401-745008-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14437401" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义玉女番茄礼盒“喜番?大好き”，微风超市甜蜜开卖。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14437402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14437402" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437402-745009.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14437402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14437402-745009-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14437402" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义玉女番茄礼盒“喜番?大好き”，微风超市甜蜜开卖。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义县政府农业处指出，嘉义县的设施小果番茄闻名全台，农民以先进的温室设施栽培，搭配智慧农业管理，造就番茄甜度高、果香浓郁，在全国设施小果番茄竞赛中屡获佳绩。</p>
<p>此精品礼盒今年1月首次在嘉义优鲜新春记者会亮相，随即吸引市场高度关注，县长翁章梁更在电视节目中亲自推荐，翻转大众对传统农产品的印象，让嘉义优鲜优质农产跃升精品市场，迈向国际舞台。◇</p>
