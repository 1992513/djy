<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>BALLISTIK BOYZ访台开唱 唱中文歌令粉丝惊喜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444621-250224061720100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本男团BALLISTIK BOYZ from 放浪一族在台举办演唱会“BALLISTIK BOYZ LIVE 2025 in TAIPEI”。（LDH爱梦悦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月24日讯】由日高龙太、加纳嘉将、深堀未来、海沼流星、奥田力也、松井利树、砂田将宏7位成员所组成的日本唱跳男团BALLISTIK BOYZ from 放浪一族（简称BALLISTIK BOYZ），22日首次在台举办演唱会“BALLISTIK BOYZ LIVE 2025 in TAIPEI”，成员们翻唱中文歌曲，让粉丝感到惊喜。</p>
<p>演出由深堀未来、奥田力也、松井利树作词作曲的《7》揭开序幕，随后带来了《HIGHER EX》、《ANTI-HERO&#8217;S》、《VIVA LA EVOLUCION》、《We Never die》、《Ding Ding Dong》等歌曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444622-250224061746100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444622" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444622-250224061746100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444622" class="wp-caption-text">日本男团BALLISTIK BOYZ from 放浪一族在台举办演唱会“BALLISTIK BOYZ LIVE 2025 in TAIPEI”。（LDH爱梦悦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>已是第四次访台的他们这次也以中文和台语个别向粉丝问候：“大家好我们是BALLISTIK BOYZ！”“我是流星，我回来了、我想你们”“我是龙太、long time no see！”“我是力也、揪想哩耶～”“我是未来，We like you！”等，除了善用谐音梗逗趣地和台下粉丝们打招呼外，也以流利的中文提到：“这是我们第一次的海外演唱会，你们开心吗？我们也很开心！”让人感受到团员们努力学中文并积极与台湾粉丝互动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444623-250224061740100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444623" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444623-250224061740100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444623" class="wp-caption-text">日本男团BALLISTIK BOYZ from 放浪一族在台举办演唱会“BALLISTIK BOYZ LIVE 2025 in TAIPEI”。（LDH爱梦悦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BALLISTIK BOYZ以轻快的《All About U》及抒情曲《good day good night》，让现场观众们沉浸在温柔旋律之中；紧接着日高龙太惊喜翻唱了他的偶像周杰伦的《说了再见》，松井利树也翻唱了高尔宣的《Without You》，两人不仅以标准的中文发音演唱外，细腻的诠释和精心准备的心意也让粉丝们感动和惊艳不已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444624-250224061733100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444624" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444624-250224061733100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444624" class="wp-caption-text">日本男团BALLISTIK BOYZ from 放浪一族在台举办演唱会“BALLISTIK BOYZ LIVE 2025 in TAIPEI”。（LDH爱梦悦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们还将近来在各大影音平台爆红的“肯恰那～叮叮叮叮叮”改编成中文版和台下粉丝互动，并特别感谢来自各地粉丝，表示他们因为BALLY&#8217;S（粉丝名）和师兄AKIRA及工作人员的全力支持，他们才能实现梦想举办这场意义非凡的首次海外单独演唱会，希望未来也能在日本国内外多多举办Live演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444625-250224061743100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444625" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444625-250224061743100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444625" class="wp-caption-text">日本男团BALLISTIK BOYZ from 放浪一族在台举办演唱会“BALLISTIK BOYZ LIVE 2025 in TAIPEI”。（LDH爱梦悦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演唱会最后，师兄AKIRA更惊喜登场献花为BALLISTIK BOYZ打气，并和他们一起跳了《PASION》炒热气氛，BALLISTIK BOYZ也承诺会再尽快回到台北和大家相见，为这次的演唱会写下完美的句点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444627" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444627-250224061722100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444627" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444627-250224061722100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444627" class="wp-caption-text">日本男团BALLISTIK BOYZ from 放浪一族在台举办演唱会“BALLISTIK BOYZ LIVE 2025 in TAIPEI”。（LDH爱梦悦提供）</figcaption></figure>
