<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨声音乐剧社春宴 传统粤曲娱嘉宾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444758-DSC01704-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>侨声音乐剧社2月22日举办蛇年春宴，会员们上台献唱《欢乐年年》。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“凤烛灿，温香软玉醉花间，巧语低唱，动君心湖泛，似梦幻……”</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">弦乐箫声，合奏出婉转优美的粤曲，伴随黄伟健、黄宝娥的深情演唱，演绎出《鼓舞倾情》中韩世忠、梁红玉的动人故事。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">这是2月22日晚，波士顿侨声音乐剧社的春宴现场。在华埠帝苑酒楼，近300位来宾不像其它春宴那样观看舞狮献瑞，而是欣赏侨声“唱家班”带来的传统粤曲表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444761" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444761-DSC01714.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444761-DSC01714-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444761" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社2月22日举办蛇年春宴，300多位来宾参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444747-DSC01651.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444747-DSC01651-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444747" class="wp-caption-text">茉莉花舞蹈团的小朋友们表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444748-DSC01657.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444748-DSC01657-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444748" class="wp-caption-text">茉莉花舞蹈团的小朋友们表演舞蹈，随后合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">晚宴开始前，茉莉花舞蹈团的小朋友们，首先为来宾们表演舞蹈。随着司仪李卢莲馨盛装登台，侨声音乐剧社的10位乐手也在台上准备就绪。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在嘉宾致词之后，黄伟健、黄宝娥伴随着乐手们演奏的古典粤曲，唱响经典篇目《梁红玉之鼓舞倾情》。接下来，梁雁英和陈秀娥合唱《胡风汉月两牵情》。社长黄国威也亲自登台，与黄绮玲合唱《花灯拾翠》。侨声成员们的平稳唱功、精准演绎，赢得来宾们的热烈掌声和欢呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444749" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444749-DSC01665.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444749-DSC01665-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444749" class="wp-caption-text">司仪李卢莲馨介绍节目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444759" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444759-DSC01707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444759-DSC01707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444759" class="wp-caption-text">黄伟健和黄宝娥演唱《梁红玉之鼓舞倾情》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">除了粤曲之外，侨声“唱家班”在流行歌曲演唱方面同样表现精彩，例如晚宴开始时的大合唱《欢乐年年》，还有社长黄国威演唱的《旧欢如梦》等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444762-DSC01723.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444762-DSC01723-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444762" class="wp-caption-text">黄国威和黄绮玲演唱《花灯拾翠》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444760" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444760-DSC01712.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444760-DSC01712-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444760" class="wp-caption-text">乐师们在台上演奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>侨声庆</strong><strong>86</strong><strong>周年</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>欢度春宴</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">侨声音乐剧社2024年刚庆祝85周年，在波士顿派拉蒙剧场（Paramount Theatre）演绎长篇粤剧《梦断香销四十年》。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">如今蛇年春宴，社长黄国威高兴地向会员、侨社代表及嘉宾们致以新年祝福，与大家一起迎接侨声86周年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444752" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444752-DSC01678.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444752-DSC01678-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444752" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn，中）颁赠褒奖。左起：梁永基、黄国威、费连、李伍碧香、雷玉霞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444755-DSC01691.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444755-DSC01691-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444755" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾合影，左起：经文处组长黄耀良、侨教中心主任高家富、经文处处长廖朝宏、至孝笃亲公所主席陈秀珍、中华公所主席雷国辉。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">另一位社长李伍碧香感言，这86年的历史，多得在座各位的支持，以及乐师们的辛苦排练，因而举办了很多成功的演出。她也期待大家继续支持侨声未来的演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444750" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444750-DSC01666.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444750-DSC01666-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444750" class="wp-caption-text">社长黄国威欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444753" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444753-DSC01681.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444753-DSC01681-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444753" class="wp-caption-text">社长李伍碧香期待大家继续支持侨声的演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">总筹雷玉霞随后为来宾们介绍了今年的两场重磅演出。第一场将于3月22日中午12:30，在华埠昆士中学（Josiah Quincy Upper School）礼堂登场，为观众呈现粤曲演唱和折子戏；第二场是古装长剧《紫钗记》，订于6月28日下午，在派拉蒙剧场上演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444754-DSC01685.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444754-DSC01685-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444754" class="wp-caption-text">总筹雷玉霞介绍今年的重磅演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444763" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444763-DSC01725.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444763-DSC01725-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444763" class="wp-caption-text">侨声会员们上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在周六晚的春宴期间，多位嘉宾到场，为参与者们送上蛇年祝福，包括驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任高家富、中华公所主席雷国辉夫妇、波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444756" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444756-DSC01692.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444756-DSC01692-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444756" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444757-DSC01697.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14444757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444757-DSC01697-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444757" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p style="font-weight: 400;">廖朝宏称赞，侨声在86年的历史中致力于传承粤曲文化，付出了巨大努力，在波士顿地区推广这种独特文化。◇</p>
