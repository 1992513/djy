<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨爱国小泰雅文化学习场域揭牌 传承发扬泰雅文化</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444413-747131-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市侨爱国小泰雅文化学习场域揭牌典礼。（桃园市府新闻处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者陈建霖台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政24日前往大溪区，出席“桃园市侨爱国小泰雅文化学习场域揭牌典礼”。张善政表示，侨爱国小作为桃园市原住民族教育资源中心，肩负原住民文化传承的责任，此次启用的文化学习场域建筑，不仅是文化的保存，更是泰雅文化的彰显，未来市府将跨局处资源整合，持续推动原住民族教育。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444414" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444414-747132.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14444414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444414-747132.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444414" class="wp-caption-text">泰雅传统家屋介绍。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444415" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444415-747133.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14444415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444415-747133.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444415" class="wp-caption-text">侨爱国小太鼓队表演。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政接受学童自制新闻台“侨爱电视台”专访时表示，侨爱国小此次特别安排太鼓队与“太阳之爱”合唱团演出，充分展现原住民族的音乐天赋，希望原住民学童在课业之余，亦不忘自身传统文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444416" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444416-747134.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14444416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444416-747134.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444416" class="wp-caption-text">烤火寮为部落族人凝聚情感的重要场域。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444417" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444417-747135.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14444417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444417-747135.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444417" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长张善政及来宾与学童合影。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育局指出，侨爱国小泰雅文化学习场域的建筑，反映部落在生活发展中的精巧思维，包括家屋、谷仓、望楼、鸡舍与烤火寮等。此外，学校团队与耆老共同考究泰雅传统建筑工法，让学生透过体验学习，加深对族群文化的认同与自信，也促进不同族群学生的相互理解，落实“全民原教”的理念。◇</p>
