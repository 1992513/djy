<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>点亮嘉义光跃台湾 2026台湾灯会嘉义县接棒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444664-747352-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>点亮嘉义、光跃台湾，2026台湾灯会由嘉义县接棒。（嘉义县政府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海改写报导）台湾灯会于23日圆满落幕，嘉义县副县长刘培东率领县府一级主管前往桃园市，参加接灯仪式，正式接下2026承办的重任，明年3月3日至15日，嘉义县将再次迎接来自全台与世界各地的游客，带来全新的赏灯体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444665" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444665-747353.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14444665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444665-747353-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444665" class="wp-caption-text">点亮嘉义、光跃台湾，2026台湾灯会由嘉义县接棒。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444666" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444666-747354.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14444666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444666-747354-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444666" class="wp-caption-text">点亮嘉义、光跃台湾，2026台湾灯会由嘉义县接棒。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444667" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444667-747355.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14444667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444667-747355-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444667" class="wp-caption-text">点亮嘉义、光跃台湾，2026台湾灯会由嘉义县接棒。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在闭幕仪式上，嘉义县街舞培训代表队带来精彩演出，为现场增添活力。桃园市长张善政将象征台湾灯会传承的灯艺交接给刘培东，并与交通部观光署长周永晖共同带领现场民众，欣赏主灯的最后展演，历时12天的台湾灯会，在璀璨灯影中正式画下句点。</p>
<p>刘培东感谢中央部会及交通部观光署的肯定，并表示今年灯会亮点众多，县府团队也特地前来观摩学习，嘉义县曾于2007及2018年成功举办台湾灯会，明年将再次承办，希望能带给大家耳目一新的嘉义印象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444668" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444668-747356.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14444668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444668-747356-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444668" class="wp-caption-text">点亮嘉义、光跃台湾，2026台湾灯会由嘉义县接棒。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周永晖指出，台湾灯会已发展36年，不仅获Discovery频道评选为“全球最佳节庆活动”，也四度获得“十大夜景遗产”的殊荣，更被国际媒体誉为“没有云霄飞车的迪士尼”，如今台湾灯会已成为全球夜间经济发展的重要指标，透过举办台湾灯会的机会，得以展现城市的魅力与新风貌。他也邀请大家，共同期待2026年台湾灯会在嘉义县的精彩盛况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14444670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444670" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444670-747358.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14444670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444670-747358-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444670" class="wp-caption-text">点亮嘉义、光跃台湾，2026台湾灯会由嘉义县接棒。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14444671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14444671" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444671-747359.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14444671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14444671-747359-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14444671" class="wp-caption-text">点亮嘉义、光跃台湾，2026台湾灯会由嘉义县接棒。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义县长翁章梁表示，县府已积极筹办灯会，初步规划场域范围涵盖县府前广场、台糖用地、太子大道并串联至故宫南院；灯会主题订为“点亮嘉义，光‘跃’台湾”，活动将持续13天，一定“天天都精彩”，希望透过台湾灯会，展现嘉义县的发展与蜕变。◇</p>
