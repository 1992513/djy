<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>周董情义相挺 今年澳洲唯一场演唱会献给五月天</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443833-2502230541411487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>五月天《回到那一天》演唱会第六度澳洲开唱，周杰伦（右三）登台相挺，再现“周五”梦幻合体。（相信音乐、杰威尔音乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月23日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）知名乐团“五月天”5525《回到那一天》25周年巡回演唱会，22日在澳洲的ccor Stadium体育场开唱，他们力邀好友周杰伦（周董）担任嘉宾，让“周五”组合再现。这也是周董今年澳洲唯一一场演出。</p>
<p>当嘉宾周杰伦乘坐着“FLY TO 25TH 专机”登场，全场瞬间尖叫声破表。连周杰伦都说：“原来这就是五月天演唱会，全部嗨起来！”并笑称自己来当嘉宾就是为了不用买门票。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14443835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443835" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443835-2502230541471487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14443835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443835-2502230541471487.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443835" class="wp-caption-text">五月天《回到那一天》演唱会第六度澳洲开唱，周杰伦登台相挺，再现“周五”梦幻合体。（相信音乐、杰威尔音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿信感谢周杰伦不只来当嘉宾，还在社群用力地帮五月天宣传。周杰伦回说：“我把它当自己演唱会，因为我今年没有要在澳洲开演唱会，所以我过年的‘第一个工作’就是来当五月天嘉宾。”</p>
<p>阿信笑着调侃：“杰伦讲出心里的话，让我听了很难过，原来你把它‘当工作’！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14443839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443839" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443839-2502230541381487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14443839" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443839-2502230541381487.jpg" alt="" width="454" b="682" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443839" class="wp-caption-text">五月天《回到那一天》演唱会澳洲场，周杰伦义气相挺唱好唱满。（相信音乐、杰威尔音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了五月天这群好友，周杰伦一路情义相挺，唱好唱满。当日除了合作演唱自己的金曲《安静》《稻香》《双截棍》，也特别选唱五月天的新歌《任性》，周杰伦说：“新歌旋律技巧很高，我也很喜欢。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14443838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443838" style="width: 457px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443838-2502230549351487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14443838" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443838-2502230549351487.jpg" alt="" width="457" b="722" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443838" class="wp-caption-text">五月天《回到那一天》演唱会第六度澳洲开唱，周杰伦（右）登台相挺。（相信音乐、杰威尔音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当唱完《任性》，周杰伦向观众说“谢谢”准备退场，升降台却迟迟不降下去，周董问：“你们地洞在哪？”阿信笑言：“我去杰伦大巨蛋演唱会，他真的硬生生把我抓住了。”因此五月天这次“任性”地把周杰伦留下来，和他们一起点歌，与歌迷度过更多“周五”合体的时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14443840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443840" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443840-2502230549381487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14443840" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443840-2502230549381487.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443840" class="wp-caption-text">五月天《回到那一天》演唱会第六度澳洲开唱，周杰伦（左）登台相挺。（相信音乐、杰威尔音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而接下来，五月天也将带着《回到那一天》25周年巡回演唱会，前往美国站（拉斯维加斯），将于3月29于Allegiant Stadium举办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14443834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443834" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443834-2502230541441487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14443834" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443834-2502230541441487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443834" class="wp-caption-text">五月天“任性”地把最完整的《回到那一天》演唱会搬来澳洲。（相信音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14443836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443836" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443836-2502230541511487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14443836" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443836-2502230541511487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443836" class="wp-caption-text">五月天“5525《回到那一天》25周年巡回演唱会”，于2月22日在雪梨Accor Stadium体育场开唱。（相信音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
