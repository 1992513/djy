<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这7种物品 不要在“车库甩卖”上购买</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433815-shutterstock_1787043008-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在车库甩卖上可以淘到宝，也可能购入不安全或不清洁的东西。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月10日讯】（大纪元记者夏瑜编译报导）很多人喜欢车库甩卖（garage sale）。俗话说，“汝之垃圾，彼之宝藏。”从书籍玩具到家居装饰，在车库甩卖上购物比添置新的便宜多了。但在买二手货时，出于清洁和安全原因，有些东西最好不要考虑。</p>
<p>雅虎网站表示，你在车库甩卖时应该避免入手7种东西。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">1. 床垫</span></p>
<p>虽然床垫是很诱人的大件物品，但最好还是不要买二手的。你永远不知道谁用过它，它有多肮脏，或者里面是否藏有臭虫之类。正规商店不能合法销售二手床垫，所以听从商店的建议，放弃那张便宜的床垫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14433828" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14433828" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433828-1_shutterstock_2378750589-e1739165572414.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14433828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433828-1_shutterstock_2378750589-e1739165572414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14433828" class="wp-caption-text">床垫虽然诱人，但最好不要买二手的。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">2. 汽车座椅</span></p>
<p>安全是汽车座椅的首要考虑因素，因此对其严格监管是有充分理由的。二手车座椅可能看起来不错，但也许已经过期、发生过事故或被召回。购买新的汽车座椅来保护你的孩子是明智之举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14433829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14433829" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433829-2_shutterstock_2430015343-e1739165665963.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14433829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433829-2_shutterstock_2430015343-e1739165665963.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14433829" class="wp-caption-text">安全是汽车座椅的首要考虑因素。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">3. 拼图或游戏</span></p>
<p>儿童游戏似乎很值得购买，但有一个问题：车库甩卖的游戏经常缺少零件或可能不卫生。为了避免以后失望，最好从可靠的来源购买拼图和棋盘游戏。但如果盒子未开封──有时会出现这种情况──那么一定要把它抢走！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14433831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14433831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433831-3_shutterstock_2200960107-e1739165784174.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14433831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433831-3_shutterstock_2200960107-e1739165784174.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14433831" class="wp-caption-text">拼图游戏如果尚未开封，还是值得购买的。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">4. 二手化妆品</span></p>
<p>绝对不能对二手化妆品动心。即使看起来没用过，打开后的化妆品也会滋生细菌，可能导致皮肤刺激或感染。如果你发现化妆品仍密封在原包装中，请务必在购买前检查其有效期。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14433833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14433833" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433833-4_shutterstock_1786171625-e1739165933527.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14433833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433833-4_shutterstock_1786171625-e1739165933527.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14433833" class="wp-caption-text">用过的化妆品绝对不要考虑。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">5. 凉鞋和人字拖</span></p>
<p>二手凉鞋或人字拖卖相很好，但可能携带前主人留下的细菌或霉菌，毕竟是贴身之物。最好只考虑全新的、有标签的鞋子。如果你决定破例，那么一定要在上脚之前彻底消毒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14433836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14433836" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433836-5_shutterstock_1603958662-e1739166081135.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14433836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433836-5_shutterstock_1603958662-e1739166081135.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14433836" class="wp-caption-text">如果喜欢二手鞋子，穿前要彻底消毒。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">6. 床上用品和毛巾</span></p>
<p>床上用品和毛巾属于灰色地带。虽然你可以清洗它们，但旧床单通常会有磨损、污渍或挥之不去的气味。如果你买了一套旧毛巾，可以考虑用它们做宠物床垫或清洁抹布。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14433852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14433852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433852-6_shutterstock_2492199537-e1739166285614.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14433852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433852-6_shutterstock_2492199537-e1739166285614.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14433852" class="wp-caption-text">贴身之物还是全新的好。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">7. 帽子</span></p>
<p>时尚的帽子会一下子勾住你的眼球，但帽子上可能留有油脂、护发产品，甚至──虱子。如果你实在舍不得，请确保在上头之前彻底清洁它，以避免任何不受欢迎的意外。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14433860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14433860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433860-7_shutterstock_626074802-e1739166411111.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14433860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14433860-7_shutterstock_626074802-e1739166411111.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14433860" class="wp-caption-text">二手帽子上可能留有油脂，甚至虱子。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然车库甩卖很热闹，而且提供一些超值优惠，但某些物品不值得你冒险。祝你享受找到宝藏的快乐！ ◇</p>
