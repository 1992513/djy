<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>参观博爱楼可负担住房 CCBA董事惊呼价值百万</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442758-DSC01600-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff
- 2月20日，纽英崙中华公所部分董事参观华埠新建成的“博爱楼”可负担住房。（刘景烨/大纪元）

<h6>2月20日，纽英崙中华公所部分董事参观华埠新建成的“博爱楼”可负担住房。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月22日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）由纽英崙中华公所建设的新可负担住房“博爱楼”（Pok Oi Residences）已经完工。在2月20日的参观途中，公所董事们不断惊呼“真漂亮”、“（价值）起码过百万”。</p>

</div>
<figure id="attachment_14442765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442765-DSC01634.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442765-DSC01634-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442765" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所部分董事参观博爱楼，称赞公寓环境优美。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位于波士顿华埠Harrison街的博爱楼，从2023年初开工，7月举办破土庆祝，到2025年2月完成，将提供85个单元的可负担住房。</p>
<p>据中华公所主席雷国辉介绍，这些单元包括30套单卧室公寓，40套双卧室，15套三卧室。</p>
<p>这些单元的租金，根据租客的年收入来计算，其中17套租给年收入为区域收入中位数（AMI）30%的租客，28套是50%的AMI，18套是60%的AMI，还有22套是80%的AMI。</p>
<p>比如就单卧室公寓而言，年收入低于区域收入中位数30%的租客，月租金为813美元；年收入低于AMI的80%，月租金为2343美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14442762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442762-DSC01625.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442762-DSC01625-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442762" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所部分董事参观博爱楼，称赞公寓环境优美。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14442766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442766-DSC01638.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442766-DSC01638-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442766" class="wp-caption-text">董事陈建立眺望窗外风景。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>新楼设施完备</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>装修华美</strong></h3>
<p>在周五的参观途中，中华公所董事们从“大同村”一侧的停车场进入新博爱楼。据主席雷国辉介绍，这些停车位将以抽签形式分配，每个位置的每月租金为100美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14442756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442756" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442756-DSC01595.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442756-DSC01595-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442756" class="wp-caption-text">地面层有宽阔的停车场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>进入1楼室内，大家首先看到摆满自行车停放架的单车房，以及配备崭新洗衣机和烘干机的洗衣房。大家纷纷称赞机器尺寸够大，价格实惠。董事陈建立还“亲身”测试烘干机的大小，同意这机器内部足够钻进一个人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14442757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442757-DSC01597.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442757-DSC01597-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442757" class="wp-caption-text">楼内有自行车停放设施。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14442759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442759" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442759-DSC01613.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442759-DSC01613-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442759" class="wp-caption-text">董事黄国威查看洗衣房内的设备。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，董事们来到1楼的社区活动厅。厅内不仅有明亮、宽敞的环境，还配备水池、冰箱、洗碗机等设施。在多个团体任职的余宝爱笑言，计划下次侨社会议来这里举办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14442760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442760" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442760-DSC01617.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442760-DSC01617-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442760" class="wp-caption-text">董事们参观1楼活动厅。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14442764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442764-DSC01631.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442764-DSC01631-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442764" class="wp-caption-text">楼内走廊铺设地毯，董事们称赞像酒店。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>搭乘电梯，上到6楼，董事们分别参观了三卧室、双卧室，以及为残疾人设计的宽敞单元。这些公寓都铺设了浅色木地板，配备崭新的冰箱、电热炉和微波炉，还有宽阔的隔音玻璃窗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14442761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442761" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442761-DSC01619.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442761-DSC01619-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442761" class="wp-caption-text">董事们来到博爱楼6楼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14442763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442763" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442763-DSC01627.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442763-DSC01627-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442763" class="wp-caption-text">董事们参观卧室。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>站在窗前瞭望，董事陈秀珍感慨：“真是好风景！”余丽媖也笑言，可以看到街对面的“Ink Block”豪华公寓，那边是价值百万的公寓，而这边是低收入住房。</p>
<p>在三卧室公寓内，董事关丽莎脱口而出“真漂亮”；黄绍培则感叹：“如果卖的话也不便宜的，起码过百万！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14442768" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442768" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442768-DSC01649.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442768" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442768-DSC01649-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442768" class="wp-caption-text">公寓拥有完备的厨房设施。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>新楼申请者众</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>董事忧不能造福华人</strong></h3>
<p>博爱楼可负担住房的租客申请已于年初截止。在今年1月的中华公所月会中，雷国辉介绍，这栋85单元的可负担住房，总共收到4600多份入住申请，其中有200多份符合租客资格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14442767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14442767" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442767-DSC01642.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14442767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14442767-DSC01642-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14442767" class="wp-caption-text">董事们参观博爱楼可负担住房。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当时董事陈仕维评论说，华埠其它一些可负担住房项目中，只有约40%的租客是华人。占用波士顿华埠这么多资源来建设可负担住房，却不能服务更多的华人，这样做是否值得，应该深思。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14038146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14038146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14038146-DSC08693.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14038146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14038146-DSC08693-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14038146" class="wp-caption-text">2023年7月19日，波士顿市长吴弭等嘉宾参与庆祝“博爱楼”破土。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前尚不清楚博爱楼将有多少华人租客。但根据相关法规，领取政府补助的住房项目，不得歧视租客的种族、肤色、性别和残疾情况。</p>
<p>在博爱楼参观活动之前，董事郑慧民在中华公所邮件内表示，希望中华公所的可负担住房可以更大程度地造福华人家庭。</p>
<p>另一位董事何远光还说，虽然不能挑选租客的种族，但可以为华人提供教育服务，帮助他们提升收入和按时报税，以满足住房项目申请资格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14420080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14420080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14420080-50-Herald-Street.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14420080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id14420080-50-Herald-Street-600x340.png" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14420080" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所正在策划“50 Herald Street”新可负担住房项目。（波士顿规划发展局）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在博爱楼之后，中华公所正在规划“50 Herald Street”（现C-Mart中国超市）新可负担住房项目。郑慧民提出，新项目必须有所改进，以人为本，尽可能满足华人需要。◇</p>
