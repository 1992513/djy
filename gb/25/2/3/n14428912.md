<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>草坪头樱花季 春日限定的花海盛宴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428925-742428-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Nina分享道：“草坪头的樱花季就像是一首春天的诗歌，每一张照片都在诉说大自然的美好与和谐。”（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月03日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾南投报导）每年的草坪头樱花季，总是让人不禁期待那片粉红与雪白交织的梦幻景色。今年的樱花季于1月25日盛大开幕，活动将持续至3月2日。这片位于信义乡的美丽山谷中，超过4,000棵樱花树与李花竞相绽放，为游客带来了一场春天的视觉盛宴。</p>
<p>今年樱花季的亮点不仅是漫山遍野的樱花海，还有茶园步道、溪流倒影。摄影达人Nina也于大年初五，捕捉了一幅幅动人心弦的春日画面，让我们透过她的镜头一览草坪头的绝美春光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14428913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428913" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428913-742416.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428913-742416-600x473.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428913" class="wp-caption-text">位于信义乡的美丽山谷中，超过4,000棵樱花树绽放了。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14428914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428914-742417.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428914-742417-600x467.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428914" class="wp-caption-text">摄影达人Nina于大年初五，捕捉了一幅幅动人心弦的春日画面。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>白雪点缀的李花诗意画卷</h2>
<p>Nina捕捉了洁白的李花盛开的样貌。枝头的李花在蓝天的映衬下显得格外纯净，花朵层次分明，仿佛为大地披上了一层轻柔的雪衣。她运用了浅景深技巧，模糊的背景将焦点牢牢地集中在花朵细腻的纹理上，展现出李花的纯粹之美。</p>
<p>李花的雪白，不仅装点了草坪头的山间，更与远处翠绿的茶园交织出一幅柔美的田园画卷。这样的景色让人流连忘返，也成为摄影爱好者的必拍场景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14428916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428916-742419.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428916-742419-600x429.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428916" class="wp-caption-text">Nina运用了浅景深技巧，模糊的背景将焦点牢牢地集中在花朵细腻的纹理上。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14428915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428915-742418.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428915-742418-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428915" class="wp-caption-text">Nina捕捉了洁白的李花盛开的样貌。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14428917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428917-742420.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428917-742420-600x457.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428917" class="wp-caption-text">李花的雪白，不仅装点了草坪头的山间，更与远处翠绿的茶园交织出一幅柔美的田园画卷。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>粉红樱花的浪漫交响曲</h2>
<p>粉色山樱与河津樱成为主角，在Nina的镜头下，樱花繁茂的枝条垂挂在小水池上，与清澈的水面相映成趣。随着微风轻拂，花瓣飘落于水中，仿佛一场浪漫的春日交响曲。画面里，蓝天白云成为樱花的天然背景，让粉红的花海更加鲜明，宛如一幅印象派的艺术作品。</p>
<p>特别是在茶园步道附近，樱花盛开的景象令人震撼。河津樱那饱满的粉色花瓣与绯寒樱的深红色形成鲜明对比，整片茶园仿佛被花海环抱，无论从哪个角度拍摄，都能感受到大自然的生机盎然。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14428918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428918-742421.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428918-742421-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428918" class="wp-caption-text">在Nina的镜头下，樱花繁茂的枝条垂挂在溪流上，与清澈的水面相映成趣。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14428920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428920-742423.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428920-742423-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428920" class="wp-caption-text">河津樱那饱满的粉色花瓣，与绯寒樱的深红色形成鲜明对比。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>黄绿相间的花卉乐章</h2>
<p>Nina的镜头也记录了几棵开满的福尔摩沙樱，将春天的色彩层次推向另一个高潮。这些白色的花朵在阳光下微微透光，与地上散落的花瓣形成了绝佳的对比，为整个画面增添了一抹温暖与柔和。背景中的溪流与落叶，更让画面多了一份诗意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14428921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428921-742424.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428921-742424-600x457.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428921" class="wp-caption-text">背景中的溪流与落叶，更让画面多了一份诗意。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>人文气息与自然之美的交融</h2>
<p>除了花卉，Nina还记录了园区内的木栅栏与漫步其中的旅人。木栅栏将农村的质朴气息融入自然景观中，而游客的身影则为静谧的花海增添了动态的生命力。这些画面呈现出草坪头不仅是一处赏花胜地，更是一个适合放松身心、融入自然的休憩场所。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14428922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428922-742425.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428922-742425-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428922" class="wp-caption-text">游客的身影则为静谧的花海增添了动态的生命力。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>光影与构图的巧妙运用</h2>
<p>Nina的作品之所以如此引人入胜，很大程度上归功于她对光影的巧妙运用。她善于在顺光与侧光下拍摄，让花朵的颜色更加鲜明，细节更加立体。此外，蓝天与白云作为高对比背景，使整体画面更具视觉冲击力。</p>
<p>构图上，Nina常使用三分法与引导线技巧，将观者的目光自然地引导至花朵或枝条。她还善于变换拍摄角度，从低角度仰拍花朵与天空，或是平视捕捉水面的倒影，为画面增添趣味性与故事感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14428923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428923-742426.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428923-742426-600x457.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428923" class="wp-caption-text">Nina的作品之所以如此引人入胜，归功于她对光影的巧妙运用。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14428924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428924-742427.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428924-742427-600x464.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="464" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428924" class="wp-caption-text">Nina还善于变换拍摄角度，从低角度仰拍花朵与天空。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>花季活动与游园资讯</h2>
<p>草坪头樱花季期间，园区内为维护环境整洁，需酌收每人50元清洁费。为因应大量人潮，主办方在爱玉桥旁设置了免费停车场，并提供接驳车服务，让游客能轻松抵达园区入口。</p>
<p>Nina分享道：“草坪头的樱花季就像是一首春天的诗歌，每一张照片都在诉说大自然的美好与和谐。”她特别提到，无论是李花的纯白，还是樱花的粉红，这些春日限定的色彩，总能让人感受到一年之初的希望与喜悦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14428927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14428927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428927-742431.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14428927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428927-742431-600x459.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="459" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14428927" class="wp-caption-text">草坪头拥有浓厚的人文气息与休闲氛围。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>抓住春天的尾巴，前往草坪头赏花吧！</p>
<p>随着樱花季进入高峰，草坪头已成为南投最热门的旅游景点之一。这里不仅有美丽的花海，还有浓厚的人文气息与休闲氛围。趁着春天还在，规划一场与家人或朋友的花季之旅，亲身感受这片春日仙境的美好吧！</p>
