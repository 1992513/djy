<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>迎新年 驻蒙特利尔台北办事处举办新春侨宴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429129-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025年2月1日，驻蒙特利尔台北经济文化办事处假座唐人街金丰酒家，举行新春侨宴。（大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月02日讯】（大纪元记者谭雅加拿大蒙特利尔报导）2025年2月1日，驻蒙特利尔台北经济文化办事处假座唐人街金丰酒家，举行新春侨宴，与侨界人士欢聚一堂，庆祝乙巳年新年。</p>
<p>驻蒙台北经文办事处陈珮莹处长代表在致辞中表示，代表中华民国政府感谢侨界对办事处的支持，并祝福大家新春愉快，身体健康，家庭幸福，龙马精神。渥太华侨务委员马稚凯亦到场向侨界人士表达节日问候。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14429130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14429130" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429130-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-2-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14429130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429130-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14429130" class="wp-caption-text">驻蒙台北经文办事处陈珮莹处长（中）、渥太华侨务委员马稚凯（左二）与侨界人士合影。（Bryant Chang提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14429131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14429131" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429131-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-3-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14429131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429131-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14429131" class="wp-caption-text">2025年2月1日，在驻蒙特利尔台北经文处举办的新春侨宴上，进行了侨界轮值主席的交接仪式。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14429133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14429133" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429133-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-4-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14429133" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429133-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14429133" class="wp-caption-text">陈珮莹处长向协助举办国庆活动的团体和个人颁发感谢状。图为满地可中华会馆主席黄蔚岳接受感谢状。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宴会期间，还举行了蒙特利尔侨界轮值主席的交接仪式，并向协助举办国庆活动的团体和个人颁发感谢状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14429135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14429135" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429135-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-5-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14429135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429135-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14429135" class="wp-caption-text">陈珮莹处长一行拜访蒙特利尔唐人街的传统侨社。（Bryant Chang提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14429138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14429138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429138-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-6-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14429138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429138-20250201-Montreal-Taiwan-banquet-6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14429138" class="wp-caption-text">陈珮莹处长一行拜访蒙特利尔唐人街的传统侨社。（Bryant Chang提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>午宴在欢乐、喜庆的气氛中结束。随后陈珮莹处长一行拜会唐人街的传统侨社。</p>
