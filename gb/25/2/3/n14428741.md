<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾近代艺术家黄水文诗书画挖宝回顾（下）1981-1990年代</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14428742-15aa7c57db1edeaa5017d2b899536e55-600x400.png" />
<h6>台湾近代艺术家黄水文和他晚年的胶彩画作。（黄健次提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/25/1/29/n14424933.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener">【续上文：台湾近代艺术家黄水文诗书画挖宝回顾（中）1960-1980年】</a></p>
<h3><strong>黄水文诗书画挖宝回顾：1981-1990年代</strong></h3>
<p><strong>【图片说明】<br />
</strong>时至1981年，由台中林之助教授等本土美术界人士提出“胶彩画”特别名词以之区别传统国画， 省美展成立了国画第二部。 黄水文就被好礼邀请出品， 睽违20年后他也欣然再度回归省美展。他的画作与时俱进的改变，展现出比较现代化的画风。如〈清秋〉是为一例。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14431346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431346" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431346-ac7ffbcd3e5d7336b4a2b5ed1c580ed4.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14431346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431346-ac7ffbcd3e5d7336b4a2b5ed1c580ed4-600x462.png" alt="" width="601" b="463" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431346" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文1981年胶彩画 〈清秋〉。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1977退休后进入老年，黄水文更是专注于诗文，因之也陆续绘制了许多幅文人画。（＊“文人画”就是唐宋以来具有艺术文化修养的文人、士大夫透过绘画、书法、文学和篆刻，描画自己的思想、意趣，投射生命精神、理想的写意画之通称。）林玉山仙门生故旧画家们，大多跟随林大师将水墨与胶彩融合，而得到活泼生动的表现结果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14431355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431355" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431355-51a3ba3d081b185b66d2f488aef2eb41.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14431355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431355-51a3ba3d081b185b66d2f488aef2eb41-600x294.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431355" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文1980年代文人画写意图。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14431413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431413" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431413-5252d5ddccf7b8e9a5f684687b71df04.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14431413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431413-5252d5ddccf7b8e9a5f684687b71df04-600x962.png" alt="" width="450" b="721" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431413" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文的写意画。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14431412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431412" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431412-0b5669291f66d8c5621f4e33ee1bc020.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14431412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431412-0b5669291f66d8c5621f4e33ee1bc020-600x780.png" alt="" width="451" b="586" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431412" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文的写意画。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14431432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431432" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431432-6231f55b6b77820774cdb17ca7637a83.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14431432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431432-6231f55b6b77820774cdb17ca7637a83-600x1202.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="902" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431432" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文的写意画。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14431354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431354" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431354-dae937f8df7f8cf6beac1f18e1a6fbd0.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14431354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431354-dae937f8df7f8cf6beac1f18e1a6fbd0-600x347.png" alt="" width="600" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431354" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文晚年的写意画更加纯熟。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">到了1980年代晚期， 黄水文白内障手术后不久视力不济，1990以后就没能继续工笔绘制胶彩画了。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14431356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431356" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431356-8a320ab0fc600bac70a6fb54b98915a2.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14431356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431356-8a320ab0fc600bac70a6fb54b98915a2-600x364.png" alt="" width="600" b="364" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431356" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文1983年与胶彩画作合影。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1987年的省美展评审参展图，一幅花卉胶彩画，就成了他的绝响，家属也不明此画流落何方。幸好有此图片留存了经典之作的面貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14431360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431360" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431360-db2bd7aaa652335d7572ed84a50158a8.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14431360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431360-db2bd7aaa652335d7572ed84a50158a8-600x700.png" alt="" width="449" b="524" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431360" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文1987年最后的花卉胶彩画。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1990初头几年，黄水文还偶而动笔写字，而后逐渐完全失明， 进入长照情况，由子女轮流奉养。美人迟暮，艺术家也叹见白头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14431396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431396-d885af5e0fe1e64dcdb2c7febcf95ee1.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14431396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431396-d885af5e0fe1e64dcdb2c7febcf95ee1-600x287.png" alt="" width="600" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431396" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文晚年书法作品及诗书手稿。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14431395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14431395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431395-e446e45470499ec7c2896b54f616c75f.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14431395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14431395-e446e45470499ec7c2896b54f616c75f-600x751.png" alt="" width="600" b="751" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14431395" class="wp-caption-text">黄水文1990封笔之画、及后来出版的诗选、家属在美自行刊印小册。（黄健次提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【结语】</strong></p>
<p>在台湾近代诗书画艺坛，黄水文一生低调，作无形之画&#8211;诗，画有形之诗&#8211;画，在诗书画中自得其乐。他的画作存留甚少，但在精不在多，得到典藏，也曾作为邮票之画发行；他的诗句得到故乡诸罗风水共鸣，常被桃城（嘉义）后人选来吟唱；他的书法以隐名之姿留存于嘉义市中山公园尊师亭。综观其人一生平凡中有不凡。</p>
<p><strong>正是：</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 80px;"><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>藻绘百物略有成，色染宣纸身后名。</strong></span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 80px;"><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>笔挥花卉散香味，墨泼鸟犬闻啼声，</strong></span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 80px;"><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>工笔胶彩显美术，写意小品呈雅兴，</strong></span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 80px;"><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>丹青妙手诗书画，诸罗隐士东篱情，</strong></span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 80px;"><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>但留文房四宝物，无税砚田子耕营。</strong></span></p>
<p>（全文完）＠</p>
