<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义县ASC、BAP国际验证水产品家数全国第一</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446253-747971-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义县ASC、BAP国际验证水产品家数全国第一。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月26日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）嘉义县政府2月26日举办“嘉义县优质渔产品ASC及BAP国际验证授证暨成果发表会”，县长翁章梁授证表扬取得验证的养殖户及水产加工厂，也邀请渔业署副署长陈建佑共同见证通路业者与取得验证业者签署采购意向。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14446254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14446254" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446254-747972.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14446254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446254-747972-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14446254" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县ASC、BAP国际验证水产品家数全国第一。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义县今年取得ASC（水产养殖管理委员会）及BAP（最佳水产养殖规范）验证业者有12家，养殖场验证面积达33.5公顷、年产值达6.3亿元。取得国际验证数及鱼种品项取得验证数皆为全国第一，也是全国第ㄧ间金目鲈养殖场取得ASC验证，以及全国唯一鱼苗场取得国际永续验证标章，翁县长一一授证表扬他们对永续渔业的用心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14446256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14446256" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446256-747973.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14446256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446256-747973-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14446256" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县ASC、BAP国际验证水产品家数全国第一。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14446257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14446257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446257-747974.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14446257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446257-747974-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14446257" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县ASC、BAP国际验证水产品家数全国第一。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会中邀请台湾主妇联盟生活消费合作社、圣德科斯生机食品、佳鲜国际有限公司和汉神国际实业公司及万里环保事业公司等5家通路业者，与12家取得国际验证的业者签署“嘉义县永续水产品采购意向”，预估今年采购金额可达2亿元，相关商品已在国内好市多、全联、主妇联盟、圣德科斯、万里生机等通路贩售，这些产品也将外销至美国、加拿大和欧盟等国家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14446259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14446259" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446259-747975.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14446259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446259-747975-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14446259" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县ASC、BAP国际验证水产品家数全国第一。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14446260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14446260" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446260-747976.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14446260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446260-747976-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14446260" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县ASC、BAP国际验证水产品家数全国第一。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁表示，农业处积极辅导养殖户及加工厂取得国际验证，加强产品外销力道，也协助媒合通路业者让更多消费者品尝到嘉义县最优质的渔产品，县府将持续支持渔民拓展海外市场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14446261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14446261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446261-747977.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14446261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446261-747977-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14446261" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县ASC、BAP国际验证水产品家数全国第一。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈建佑表示，嘉义优鲜在国内外市场已有不错的表现，台湾有近20%的养殖鱼产品外销，若业者能取得国际认证，有助于提升竞争力。◇</p>
