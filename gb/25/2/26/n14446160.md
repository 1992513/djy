<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳两党推出新一轮竞选承诺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14446166-693b13ef3d0c5b208a0162b05f85b8e6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>合成图。（左）西澳自由党领袖梅塔姆女士（ Libby Mettam ）于 2025 年 2 月 24 日在珀斯举行的记者会上；（右）西澳州长库克（ Roger Cook ）于 2025 年 2 月 24 日参观 珀斯电源系统制造商Magellan Power。(AAP Image/Richard Wainwright)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月26日讯】（大纪元记者林达澳洲珀斯编译报导）距离西澳州选举仅数周之际，工党和自由党日前又推出了一系列竞选承诺。工党承诺提高住房可负担性，自由党拟加大力度打击犯罪。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据“</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">WA</span> <span lang="en-AU">Today</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">”新闻网站报导，</span><span lang="zh-TW">周二（</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US">18</span><span lang="zh-TW">日），西澳工党承诺将首次购房者印花税房价免税门槛从</span><span lang="en-US">45</span><span lang="zh-TW">万提升至</span><span lang="en-US">50</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">周六（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">16</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">日</span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">，州长库克（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Roger</span> <span lang="en-AU">Cook</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）和住房厅厅长凯里（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">John</span> <span lang="en-AU">Carey</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）宣布，若工党库克政府成功连任，将向西澳政府贷款机构</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Keystart</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">共享产权计划注资</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">2.1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">亿澳元，并建造多达</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1000</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">套新单元房</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据该政策，政府将在购买新公寓和联排别墅（期房和在建房）时承担最高</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">35</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">的产权份额（或</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">25</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元），旨在使低收入居民的首付和房贷还款更易负担。</span></p>
<p>该计划的收入限制单身最高<span lang="en-AU">123,000</span>澳元，夫妇<span lang="en-AU">189,000</span>澳元。</p>
<p>工党还承诺为“可建可租”开发项目提供<span lang="en-US">75%</span>的地税减免，为社会住房注资<span lang="en-US">7500</span>万澳元。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">周六</span><span lang="zh-TW">当天，西澳自由党宣布，如果当选，将在执政前</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">100</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">天内斥资</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">200</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，审查《量刑管理法》（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Sentence</span> <span lang="en-AU">Administration</span> <span lang="en-AU">Act</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">），并强化西澳囚犯审查委员会（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">WA</span> <span lang="en-AU">Prisoners</span> <span lang="en-AU">Review</span> <span lang="en-AU">Board</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></p>
<p>在一系列打击犯罪的新措施中，将投入<span lang="en-AU">1</span>亿澳元用于对危险罪犯增加<span lang="en-AU">300</span>个<span lang="en-AU">GPS</span>追踪手环，并公开所有从西澳警方逃脱的囚犯和假释犯的姓名。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">影子总检察长戈伊兰（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Nick</span> <span lang="en-AU">Goiran</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）解释了每个追踪器</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">33</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的成本分配，称这</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">亿澳元还包括强化惩教服务部门。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">戈伊兰和自由党领袖梅塔姆女士（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Libby</span> <span lang="en-AU">Mettam</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，新政策旨在恢复刑事司法系统的信誉。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">自由党</span><span lang="zh-TW">之前也承诺将首次购房者印花税房价免税门槛提升至</span><span lang="en-US">55</span><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">周五（</span><span lang="en-US">2</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span lang="en-US">14</span><span lang="zh-TW">日），两党在卫生医疗方面也做出承诺。自由党承诺斥资</span><span lang="en-US">1.1</span><span lang="zh-TW">亿澳元扩建</span><span lang="en-US">Joondalup</span><span lang="zh-TW">保健院（</span><span lang="en-US">Joondalup Health Campus</span><span lang="zh-TW">），提供</span><span lang="en-US">60</span><span lang="zh-TW">张病床。工党则承诺允许全科医生能够诊断多动症（</span><span lang="en-US">ADHD</span><span lang="zh-TW">）和开药。目前，只有精神科医师或儿科医师可以诊断儿童的</span><span lang="en-US">ADHD</span><span lang="zh-TW">，而在某些情况下，病人等待诊断的时间几乎长达一年。</span></p>
