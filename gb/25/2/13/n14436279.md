<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宝可梦提灯抢手 台湾灯会超可爱“蛇年拍拍尺”曝光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436280-744609-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>青蛙在打呼-互动设施。（桃园市青年事务局 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月13日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）来台湾灯会除了逛灯区外，记得要领取隐藏限量蛇年拍拍尺！本次“青春光剧场灯区”以亲子及游戏为主题，打造“跳跳跳马戏团 The Bouncing Circus”充满马戏团氛围的展区，展区内规划满满的童趣灯具和互动性装置，内容包括有好玩的跳跳床、迷宫和充气公园以及各种趣味灯区，非常适合亲子同乐，让灯会不只好好看，还能好好玩！</p>
<figure id="14436281" aria-describedby="caption-14436281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436281-744610-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436281-744610-450x253.jpg" alt="限量隐藏版小物-蛇年拍拍尺。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14436281" class="wp-caption-text">限量隐藏版小物-蛇年拍拍尺。（桃园市青年事务局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个颠覆传统的创意马戏团灯区，也邀请国际马戏团队来演出，将杂耍、马戏表演、游行等小朋友最爱的童趣内涵，一次集结，打造“灯会界的游乐园”，无差别融化大人小孩的心！</p>
<figure id="14436282" aria-describedby="caption-14436282" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436282-744611-450x799.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436282-744611-450x799.jpg" alt="青春光剧场-限量小物蛇年夜光拍拍尺。" width="451" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14436282" class="wp-caption-text">青春光剧场-限量小物蛇年夜光拍拍尺。（桃园市青年事务局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市青年事务局长候佳龄表示，桃园是六都中最年轻、最有活力的城市！这次“青春光剧场”用创新思维打造“跳跳跳马戏团”，展览不再仅能观赏，还能跟灯具进行互动，民众可以在领取限量蛇年拍拍尺夜光手环后，带着夜光拍拍尺，走进两周限定、不分昼夜的沈浸式乐园。从斑马纹的充气拱门“伸缩夜晚 Inflatable Night”开始，进入白天与黑夜交织的奇幻结界。</p>
<figure id="14436283" aria-describedby="caption-14436283" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436283-744612-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436283-744612-450x300.jpg" alt="“月亮在唱歌”迷宫互动装置充满迷幻感。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14436283" class="wp-caption-text">“月亮在唱歌”迷宫互动装置充满迷幻感。（桃园市青年事务局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展区内散落着七组灯光装置，包括“小丑在做梦 Clown is Dreaming”“蜥蜴往上爬 Lizard is Climbing Up”“月亮在唱歌 Moon is Singing”“马儿快跑 Horse is Speeding”“青蛙在打呼 Frog is Snoring”“狐狸藏星星 Fox is Hiding Stars”及“猴子大眼瞪小眼 Monkeys&#8217; Staring Eyes”，每一组灯饰都等待着参观者探索他们背后的有趣故事与惊喜。</p>
<figure id="14436291" aria-describedby="caption-14436291" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436291-744615-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436291-744615-450x300.jpg" alt="“小丑在做梦”灯具。" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14436291" class="wp-caption-text">“小丑在做梦”灯具。（桃园市青年事务局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14436284" aria-describedby="caption-14436284" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436284-744613-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436284-744613-450x300.jpg" alt="“快乐这里那里”-互动充气公园。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14436284" class="wp-caption-text">“快乐这里那里”-互动充气公园。（桃园市青年事务局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中“快乐这里那里-充气公园”，让民众能透过踩踏行动转化为动力，体验快乐源自互动的乐趣；“月亮在唱歌 Moon is Singing”则是是一座迷宫装置，色彩缤纷、充满梦幻感，仿佛童年马戏的游乐园倒影。</p>
<figure id="14436286" aria-describedby="caption-14436286" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436286-744614-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436286-744614-450x300.jpg" alt="“蜥蜴往上爬”灯具。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14436286" class="wp-caption-text">“蜥蜴往上爬”灯具。（桃园市青年事务局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“青蛙在打呼 Frog is Snoring”是个充满弹性的跳跳床，像是大青蛙在打呼一般，让大人小孩都能在上面感受弹跳的快乐。</p>
<figure id="14436292" aria-describedby="caption-14436292" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436292-744616-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14436292-744616-450x300.jpg" alt="“马儿快跑”灯具。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14436292" class="wp-caption-text">“马儿快跑”灯具。（桃园市青年事务局 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灯区隐藏版限量纪念小物，是结合今年蛇年及灯区的马戏主题所推出的夜光版蛇年拍拍尺，自灯会开幕日 2月12日起，每周一到四下午五点、五六日下午四点，都可以到场索取，发完为止，每天将在“青春光剧场灯区”（地点：位于领航北路一段与环溪一街交叉路口），限量发放300组，增加大小朋友赏灯的趣味性！更多灯会资讯请搜寻2025台湾灯会在桃园。◇</p>
