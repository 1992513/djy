<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国小软式组学生棒球联赛开幕 副县长投球开场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441650-746326-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>副县长与参赛选手比旗开得胜手势。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月20日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾台北报导）113学年国小软式组学生棒球联赛开幕典礼，2月20日上午在头屋乡后龙溪高滩地棒垒球场盛大展开，苗栗县副县长邱俐俐亲自投球开场并为选手们加油打气，希望各位选手都能全力以赴，在竞技中的氛围中培养团队默契，勇夺佳绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14441648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441648" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441648-746324.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14441648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441648-746324-450x268.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="268" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441648" class="wp-caption-text">国小组软式棒球开幕全体大合影。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14441649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441649" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441649-746325.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14441649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441649-746325-450x287.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441649" class="wp-caption-text">副县长开球。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>113学年度棒球相关联赛由大南国小棒球工作小组协助办理，共计约办理62场次，国小软式棒球联赛选拔共有12队参赛，约180名教练选手，每位选手们精神抖擞，准备展开一场场精彩对决，将选拔2校代表苗栗县出征4月份于嘉义的全国赛事。</p>
<p>今日棒球联赛开幕典礼，苗栗县副县长邱俐俐、教育处督学哈嘉琪、苗栗县体育会副总干事陈鸿文及参赛校长们皆到场为参赛选手加油打气，感谢裁判及工作人员的辛劳付出。开球仪式则由邱副县长担任投手，哈督学担任打击手，象征比赛正式启动，祝福选手们都能获得满意的成绩。而邱副县长更亲自在棒球上亲笔签名并写下〝旗开得胜〞，相信今天参赛的球队能发挥运动员精神，展现出不凡的球技实力，争取最佳成绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14441651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441651" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441651-746327.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14441651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441651-746327-450x310.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441651" class="wp-caption-text">通霄国小棒球队与副县长合影。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14441652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441652" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441652-746328.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14441652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441652-746328-450x279.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441652" class="wp-caption-text">侨成国小棒球队与副县长合影。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>副县长邱俐俐表示今天是非常重要的战役，今天夺得代表权之后还要再集训，4月份代表苗栗参加全国比赛，大家一定要有信心。看到每位选手们手指脚趾都已经痒痒准备要下场大战一场，感谢大家的付出及坚持，希望每位选手们都能夺得非常好的成绩，尤其在4月份时出征为苗栗县夺得佳绩，来回报自己的练习及师长的指导。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14441654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441654" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441654-746330.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14441654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441654-746330-450x321.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441654" class="wp-caption-text">国小组软式棒球比赛。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14441653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441653" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441653-746329.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14441653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441653-746329-450x275.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441653" class="wp-caption-text">副县长与大同国小合影。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>希望透过棒球赛事培养小选手的团队合作精神，并推广棒球运动，让更多孩子爱上这项运动。</p>
