<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>ZEROBASEONE首部专属电影 3月12日在台上映</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441702-250220065516100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国人气男团ZEROBASEONE（ZB1）首部专属电影《ZEROBASEONE THE FIRST TOUR [TIMELESS WORLD] IN CINEMAS》照。（车库娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月20日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）韩国人气男团ZEROBASEONE（ZB1）首部专属电影《ZEROBASEONE THE FIRST TOUR [TIMELESS WORLD] IN CINEMAS》收录了ZEROBASEONE于2024年所举办的巡回演唱会“TIMELESS WORLD”精彩舞台实况。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14441703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441703" style="width: 419px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441703-250220065513100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14441703" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441703-250220065513100707.jpg" alt="" width="419" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441703" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团ZEROBASEONE（ZB1）首部专属电影《ZEROBASEONE THE FIRST TOUR [TIMELESS WORLD] IN CINEMAS》海报照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>ZEROBASEONE是由韩国选秀节目《BOYS PLANET》中，选出金地雄、章昊、成韩彬、SEOK MATTHEW、金泰来、RICKY、金奎彬、朴干旭、韩维辰9位成员所组成，并于2023年出道的期间限定团体。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14441706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441706-250220065511100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14441706" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441706-250220065511100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441706" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团ZEROBASEONE（ZB1）首部专属电影《ZEROBASEONE THE FIRST TOUR [TIMELESS WORLD] IN CINEMAS》照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_14441707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441707-250220065508100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14441707" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441707-250220065508100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441707" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团ZEROBASEONE（ZB1）首部专属电影《ZEROBASEONE THE FIRST TOUR [TIMELESS WORLD] IN CINEMAS》照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>ZEROBASEONE出道后一点一滴累积经验，出道一年就在8个都市举办世界巡回演唱会，吸引14万名观众参与。本作不仅有ZEROBASEONE《In Bloom》、《Feel the POP》、《GOOD SO BAD》等精彩舞台实况，还有从未公开的幕后花絮，以及成员们的个人访谈。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_14441705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14441705" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441705-250220065506100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14441705" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14441705-250220065506100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14441705" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团ZEROBASEONE（ZB1）首部专属电影《ZEROBASEONE THE FIRST TOUR [TIMELESS WORLD] IN CINEMAS》照。（车库娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>观众们将可透过3月12日在台湾上映的《ZEROBASEONE THE FIRST TOUR [TIMELESS WORLD] IN CINEMAS》再次看到他们精彩的演出。</p>
