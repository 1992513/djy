<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园航空城计划联外道路系统 张善政视察航青路工程</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429435-742546-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>航青路临捷运A16横山站。（桃园市工务局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月04日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市长张善政4日前往视察“桃园航空城计划区段征收工程－航青路”，航青路主要是沿着桃园捷运机场线A15、A16至A17站捷运桥下两侧兴建的一条大道，全线新辟完成后，将成为连接大园市区及青埔特区的重要道路，并快速衔接国道2号大园交流道，往西转航城路一段也可直达桃园航空客运园区，不仅强化公共交通运输网络，还能有效纾解既有道路的交通负荷，提升区域间往返的便利性。</p>
<figure id="14429442" aria-describedby="caption-14429442" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429442-742556-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429442-742556-450x300.jpg" alt="航青路-设置人行道及自行车道。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429442" class="wp-caption-text">航青路-设置人行道及自行车道。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14429437" aria-describedby="caption-14429437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429437-742548-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429437-742548-450x338.jpg" alt="航青路临青峰公园。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429437" class="wp-caption-text">航青路临青峰公园。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14429436" aria-describedby="caption-14429436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429436-742547-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429436-742547-450x338.jpg" alt="航青路临桃园捷运青埔厂。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429436" class="wp-caption-text">航青路临桃园捷运青埔厂。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>航青路为全新规划的道路，包含新建航青一桥与航青二桥，全长约4.7公里、路宽50公尺，由北至南邻接产专区、乙种工业区、商业区及住宅区，同时串联玫瑰溪畔公园、青峰公园及10处绿地；工程内容除道路主体工程外，并设置有绿植带、人行道、自行车专用道，且有完善的排水工程、污水管线、共同管道及智能路灯等设施。</p>
<figure id="14429438" aria-describedby="caption-14429438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429438-742549-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429438-742549-450x338.jpg" alt="航青路临玫瑰溪畔公园。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429438" class="wp-caption-text">航青路临玫瑰溪畔公园。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>航空城工程处表示，航青路属都市计划中的园林道路，其道路景观以种植茄苳树、阿勃勒、光蜡树、枫香及苦楝等树种，随着四季更迭呈现不同色彩变化，塑造出都市生态绿廊。</p>
<figure id="14429440" aria-describedby="caption-14429440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429440-742551-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429440-742551-450x337.jpg" alt="航青路衔接领航北路。" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429440" class="wp-caption-text">航青路衔接领航北路。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14429439" aria-describedby="caption-14429439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429439-742550-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429439-742550-450x338.jpg" alt="航青路临玫瑰溪畔公园。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429439" class="wp-caption-text">航青路临玫瑰溪畔公园。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>人行道提供宽敞、友善无障碍的行人空间，铺面采用彩色喷花地坪及高压砖等工法；自行车专用道提供都会区绿色交通运输路网，运用透水沥青铺面工法，迳流水透过入渗、截流可增加地下水涵养，进一步供给周遭绿植带，实践水资源再利用。</p>
<figure id="14429441" aria-describedby="caption-14429441" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429441-742553-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429441-742553-450x338.jpg" alt="航青路衔接航青一桥（北往南）。" width="601" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429441" class="wp-caption-text">航青路衔接航青一桥（北往南）。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025台湾灯会由桃园市政府主办，主灯区位在桃园高铁站前广场。为因应灯会期间人潮众多及停车需求，航空城工程处在确保品质的前提下，优化航青路的施工流程、调整工序加紧施工。</p>
<figure id="14429444" aria-describedby="caption-14429444" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429444-742559-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429444-742559-450x338.jpg" alt="航青路东侧乙种工业区。" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429444" class="wp-caption-text">航青路东侧乙种工业区。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14429443" aria-describedby="caption-14429443" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429443-742558-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429443-742558-450x253.jpg" alt="航青路空拍。" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429443" class="wp-caption-text">航青路空拍。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同时，由交通局在A16捷运站旁车专区用地及邻近的文大用地设置3,600席临时停车场，民众停放车辆后可转乘桃园捷运进入主灯区赏灯，满足灯会期间停车及通行需求。</p>
<figure id="14429445" aria-describedby="caption-14429445" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429445-742560-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429445-742560-450x300.jpg" alt="航青一桥-设置人行道及自行车道。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429445" class="wp-caption-text">航青一桥-设置人行道及自行车道。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>航空城区段征收第一期工程建设面积达1,756公顷（包含优先开发区及其余地区），为优化周边居民的交通需求，今年将完成168条道路及5座桥梁，并计划于今年工程验收完成后陆续开放使用，逐步建构更完整的交通运输系统。</p>
<figure id="14429446" aria-describedby="caption-14429446" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429446-742561-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14429446-742561-450x300.jpg" alt="玫瑰溪畔公园原地保留大榕树。" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14429446" class="wp-caption-text">玫瑰溪畔公园原地保留大榕树。（桃园市工务局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>未来，市府将持续推动航空城相关建设，透过完善道路系统与地下管道规划，符合周边多元使用需求，优化交通运输与土地利用，进一步促进区域发展，以打造智慧创新与宜居的国际门户，提升市民的生活品质与城市竞争力。◇</p>
