<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>安良各地会员 齐聚波城欢庆蛇年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435903-DSC01404-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波城安良工商会2月9日举办蛇年春宴，嘉宾们上台敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月13日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）“安国安家昌隆会务振中华，良谋良策繁荣侨社致工商。”
</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">波士顿安良工商会欢庆蛇年新年，2月9日晚在华埠帝苑酒楼筵开36桌，与侨社代表、政要嘉宾，以及从美国各地前来的会员一起把盏言欢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14435892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435892" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435892-DSC01301.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435892-DSC01301-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435892" class="wp-caption-text">波城安良工商会举办蛇年春宴，职员、嘉宾、各地代表及舞狮团体合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">“祝福你们和家人享有健康富足的新年，期待在社区见到各位，与所有人合作。”波士顿安良工商会会长陈伟民说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">另一位会长黄展唐也感谢大家在百忙之中抽空参加春宴，并向来宾们送上新年祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14435893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435893-DSC01306.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435893-DSC01306-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435893" class="wp-caption-text">会长陈伟民致送新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14435899" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435899" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435899-DSC01348.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435899" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435899-DSC01348-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435899" class="wp-caption-text">会长黄展唐致送新年祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14435889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435889" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435889-DSC01284.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435889" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435889-DSC01284-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435889" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾莅临安良春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">当天的宴会，由安良醒狮团的舞狮贺岁拉开序幕。三头彩狮伴随着锣鼓声摇头摆尾，翻腾跳跃，将寓意吉祥的生菜和橘子送给嘉宾及宾客。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">晚宴由波士顿会员阮鸿灿、梅宇国主持。参与这场宴席的300多位来宾，既有麻州当地的侨社代表和安良会员，也有罗德岛、纽约、芝加哥等地的安良工商会代表，比如全美安良工商会总理陈启灵、关永发，元老陈仕维、胡英僚等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">友社代表包括中华公所主席雷国辉夫妇、全美洪门元老余丽媖、全美舜裔笃亲公所元老陈建立、世界凤伦公所总长司徒宗达等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14435891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435891-DSC01293.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435891-DSC01293-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435891" class="wp-caption-text">安良醒狮团为来宾们献瑞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14435894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435894-DSC01312.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435894-DSC01312-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435894" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏夫妇，以及侨教中心主任高家富都参与了晚宴。廖朝宏特别感谢安良“在过去几十年毫不动摇地支持中华民国台湾”，也感谢安良职员团队长期服务社区。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">雷国辉代表中华公所，感谢安良工商会合作举办诸多活动。陈启灵也称赞宴会成功，并祝福大家新年幸运、富足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14435895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435895-DSC01313.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435895-DSC01313-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435895" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任高家富致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14435896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435896-DSC01319.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435896-DSC01319-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435896" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14435897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435897" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435897-DSC01323.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435897-DSC01323-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435897" class="wp-caption-text">全美安良工商会总理陈启灵致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn）参与宴会，并向两位会长陈伟民、黄展唐颁赠褒奖。波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）、麻州参议员柯林斯（Nick Collins）都派代表参加了晚宴。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">费连在送上新年祝福的同时，还特别谈到，华埠许多商家都对他表示，希望Kneeland街有更多的停车位。因此他会将这些信息带回市议会，呼吁其他议员支持增加停车位的计划。这段发言得到大家的热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14435898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435898-DSC01330.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435898-DSC01330-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435898" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员费连（Ed Flynn，中）向两位会长颁赠褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14435901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435901" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435901-DSC01387.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435901-DSC01387-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435901" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）陈仕维、谭达儿、黄展唐热情献唱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14435902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435902" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435902-DSC01390.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435902-DSC01390-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435902" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们献唱经典歌曲《朋友》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">除了庆祝新年之外，安良工商会也不忘支持公益，当晚向5个非营利团体捐赠红包，包括中华颐养院、中华耆英会、广教中文学校、侨立中文学校，以及波士顿青年服务组织（YES）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14435904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14435904" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435904-DSC01418.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14435904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14435904-DSC01418-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14435904" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿及全美安良工商会代表们向来宾敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400">当晚恰逢美式足球“超级杯”（Super Bowl）大赛，一些来宾在饮宴的同时，也闲谈酋长队和老鹰队的比赛情况。帝苑酒楼以少见的速度飞快上菜，使宴会在大约8:30的时候就来到了尾声，人们可以回家看比赛。◇</p>
