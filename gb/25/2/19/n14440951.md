<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>李浚赫访台 自曝喜欢导演李安及人生首选电影</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440963-250219090624100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>李浚赫访台出席《我的完美秘书》记者会。（Hami Video提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年02月19日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）在韩剧《我的完美秘书》中饰演秘书刘恩浩的演员李浚赫受邀访台，他今天（19日）在台北万豪酒店出席记者会，对台湾粉丝的热情留下深刻印象的他，准备了亲笔签名的绘本送给粉丝，并感谢粉丝们的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14440960" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14440960" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440960-250219090709100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14440960" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440960-250219090709100707.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14440960" class="wp-caption-text">李浚赫访台出席《我的完美秘书》记者会照。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李浚赫一登场就以标准中文问好，中华电信则特别送上象征“红红火火”之意的火龙果花篮，祝福他未来继续火红下去，更特别订制完美秘书公事包邀他一起开香槟庆祝《我的完美秘书》的好成绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14440961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14440961" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440961-250219090645100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14440961" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440961-250219090645100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14440961" class="wp-caption-text">李浚赫访台出席《我的完美秘书》记者会。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《我的完美秘书》讲述只懂工作的女强人CEO姜智允（韩志旼饰）与秘书刘恩浩（李浚赫饰）的职场罗曼史，李浚赫饰演无懈可击的完美秘书，剧里温柔体贴的面貌掳获大批粉丝的心，不仅在韩国写下收视佳绩，在Hami Video独家韩剧《我的完美秘书》上架以来连续6周夺得影剧馆+冠军，更攀升为Hami Video历年戏剧人气榜第一名，成为Hami Video最强戏剧冠军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14440956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14440956" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440956-250219090638100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14440956" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440956-250219090638100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14440956" class="wp-caption-text">李浚赫与韩志旼主演《我的完美秘书》剧照。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今日记者会上，中华电信个人家庭分公司总经理胡学海、副总经理陈淑玲皆有出席，胡学海表示，身边的女性朋友知道Hami Video邀请到李浚赫访台，都感到非常期待，而昨天也听闻有粉丝专程从海外飞回台湾到桃园机场接机，可见李浚赫的魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14440957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14440957" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440957-250219090706100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14440957" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440957-250219090706100707.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14440957" class="wp-caption-text">李浚赫访台出席《我的完美秘书》记者会，中华电信个人家庭分公司总经理胡学海（左）和副总经理陈淑玲（右）特别出席活动。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次中华电信除了准备火龙果花篮外，在下榻酒店还替李浚赫准备他爱吃的洋芋片、牛轧饼干、小泡芙等台湾点心，更安排他品尝不同的台湾美食，让李浚赫能好好享受台湾行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14440964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14440964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440964-250219090653100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14440964" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440964-250219090653100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14440964" class="wp-caption-text">李浚赫访台出席《我的完美秘书》记者会照。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>出道18年首访台湾，李浚赫以西装秘书打扮出席记者会，一登场便掀起阵阵赞叹声。他抵达桃园机场时见到500人接机，让他受宠若惊，对台湾粉丝的热情留下深刻印象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14440958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14440958" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440958-2502180945431487.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14440958" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440958-2502180945431487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14440958" class="wp-caption-text">《我的完美秘书》男星李浚赫（前）于2月18日晚间搭机抵台，吸引大批粉丝到场接机。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李浚赫还透露此行访台做了不少功课，希望有机会能好好享受台湾行。此外，热爱看电影的他，自曝非常喜欢李安导演，其中《少年Pi的奇幻漂流》更是他的人生首选。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14440965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14440965" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440965-250219090703100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14440965" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440965-250219090703100707.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14440965" class="wp-caption-text">李浚赫访台出席《我的完美秘书》记者会。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天有40名幸运粉丝参与记者会，李浚赫特地从韩国准备自己推出的绘本《再见爆米花》并亲自签名，送给前来共襄盛举的粉丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14440959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14440959" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440959-250219090656100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14440959" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14440959-250219090656100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14440959" class="wp-caption-text">李浚赫访台出席《我的完美秘书》记者会，与全场媒体粉丝大合照。（Hami Video提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动上他也回复幸运粉丝提问，分享《我的完美秘书》的拍摄趣事，无法到场的粉丝则藉由直播追星。看到粉丝的热烈支持，他也再次表达感谢，并约定未来有机会一定会再来到台湾与粉丝们见面。</p>
