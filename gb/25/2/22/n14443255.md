<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台中优质椪柑“抢鲜”登陆大温超市</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443269-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2025年2月21日，列治文加廉超市(PriceSmart Foods)举办台湾台中优质椪柑推广行销活动。（梁月／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月22日讯】（大纪元记者梁月加拿大温哥华采访报导）您知道吗？来自台中肥沃土地孕育出来的椪柑， “抢鲜”来到加拿大了！</p>
<p>2月21日，列治文加廉超市(PriceSmart Foods)热闹举办台湾台中优质椪柑推广行销活动，店内顾客门庭若市，纷纷购买来自台中的宝藏水果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14443256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443256" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443256-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14443256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443256-1-450x260.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443256" class="wp-caption-text">2025年2月21日，列治文加廉超市(PriceSmart Foods)举办台湾台中优质椪柑推广行销活动。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14443257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443257-7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14443257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443257-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443257" class="wp-caption-text">台中优质椪柑果实饱满，酸甜适中。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14443258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443258" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443258-8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14443258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443258-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443258" class="wp-caption-text">台中优质椪柑果实饱满，酸甜适中。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现在正是台中椪柑最适宜食用的时候，果实饱满，酸甜适中，果肉Q弹多汁，营养丰富。来到加拿大后广受好评。</p>
<p>今年除了可在加拿大西岸的Save-On-Foods，中部省份的Associated Grocers等购买外，还扩点到安大略省，如Asia Food Mart等多家大型超市。</p>
<p>此次活动由台中市府农业局与台中东势区农会携手合作，台中农业局表示，今年仅椪柑一个单品在北美的销量就达82吨。</p>
<p>温哥华台湾贸易中心主任邱湿敏主任表示，随着美加关税升级，美国进口的柳橙可能会涨价，加拿大业界也在分散风险，“那当然台湾就是一个很重要的选项。”</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Price</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Smart</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">Foods</span>的区域总监Jacky Ho表示，该超市将“拥抱台湾水果”，将来考虑进口更多台湾甜美可口的芒果等水果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14443259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443259" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443259-15.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14443259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443259-15-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443259" class="wp-caption-text">温哥华台湾贸易中心主任邱湿敏主任致辞。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14443261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443261-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14443261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443261-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443261" class="wp-caption-text">2025年2月21日，列治文加廉超市举办台湾台中优质椪柑推广行销活动。<span style="font-weight: 400;">Price</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Smart</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">Foods</span>的区域总监Jacky Ho与驻温哥华台北经济文化办事处刘立欣处长（左）合影。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台中市府余俊贤科员介绍说，过去椪柑主要热销在东南亚，主要是当地华人送礼用，但是今年开始，台中市府希望椪柑不局限在华人过年才享用，而是打开更大的市场大门。现在北美市场就“多了一个华人以外的市场”与更多族裔的人可以分享“不要局限在华人” 。所以在包装上也有不同风格，有华人喜爱的喜气礼盒装，也有西人接受的简易六组装和散装售卖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14443262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443262" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443262-11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14443262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443262-11-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443262" class="wp-caption-text">顾客在挑选台中椪柑。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14443263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443263" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443263-4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14443263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443263-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443263" class="wp-caption-text">台中优质椪柑果实饱满，酸甜适中。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14443264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14443264" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443264-6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14443264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14443264-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14443264" class="wp-caption-text">台中优质椪柑果实饱满，酸甜适中。（梁月／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻温哥华台北经济文化办事处刘立欣处长也来到现场，她说台湾椪柑酸甜适中，是非常好吃的水果。也希望台湾与加拿大在未来的合作中能有更多的水果贸易往来，特别想把加拿大可口的蓝莓带去台湾。</p>
<p>事不宜迟，趁新鲜，赶快去加廉超市品尝来自台湾的那一抹甘甜吧！◇</p>
