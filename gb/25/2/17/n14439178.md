<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗县府召开通霄、苑里地区县长下乡座谈会 热烈讨论47项提案</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439180-745416-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>县长与县府团队召开下乡座谈。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年02月17日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）县长钟东锦2月17日上午率县府团队在通霄镇综合活动中心举办“县长下乡座谈会”，邀请通霄、苑里镇长、正副主席、代表、里长、社区发展协会理事长、农渔会3巨头等地方各界人士面对面交流，进一步倾听民意，深入了解地方建设需求。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14439179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439179-745415.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439179-745415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439179" class="wp-caption-text">通苑地区下乡座谈会。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14439181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439181" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439181-745417.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439181-745417.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439181" class="wp-caption-text">县长回应地方反映的问题。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦上任2年多来，由衷感谢乡亲对县政的支持及2位县籍立委、县议会、各乡镇市公所的督促指教与协力合作，由于他深切了解苗栗还有进步的空间及发展潜力，为回应地方需求，从2月7日起特别规划6场次下乡座谈会，亲自走访地方向乡亲报告目前苗栗各项重要施政计划的推动进度及成果，同时聆听各界的宝贵意见和建议。</p>
<p>这6场次的县长下乡座谈会，首场头份、三湾、南庄2月7日在兴华高中体育馆举行；卓兰、大湖、狮潭、泰安2月11日狮潭乡竹木村社区活动中心；西湖、铜锣、三义2月13日铜锣湾会馆；通霄、苑里2月17日通霄综合活动中心；苗市、公馆、头屋2月19日苗栗市千玺会馆；竹南、后龙、造桥2月21日竹南镇新南里活动中心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14439182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439182" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439182-745418.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439182-745418.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439182" class="wp-caption-text">县议员反映问题。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14439184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439184-745420.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439184-745420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439184" class="wp-caption-text">苑里镇长反映意见。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天上午举行的县长下乡座谈会，通霄镇长张可欣、镇代会主席徐淑云、副主席黄启森、苑里镇长刘育育、主席洪晃琦、副主席林俊廷、代表、里长、社区发展协会理事长及镇农会、通苑区农渔会3巨头等几乎全员到齐，出席非常踊跃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14439188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439188" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439188-745424.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439188-745424.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439188" class="wp-caption-text">通霄里长反映问题。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14439189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439189" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439189-745425.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439189-745425.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439189" class="wp-caption-text">通霄里长临时动议。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会中，2乡镇共计提出47项提案及4项临时动议案，反映公有零售立场改建、铜科串联通霄联外道路、苗41线拓宽、温泉开发、农水路改善、野溪整治、渔港堤防改善、重划区农路重新铺设、里活动中心修缮等问题；县长钟东锦或相关局处长逐项回应，说明处理方式，提案人对于县府的回应均感满意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14439183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439183-745419.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439183-745419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439183" class="wp-caption-text">通霄镇长说明提案。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中，有关通霄镇长张可欣提案，反映希望县府争取中央补助办理铜锣科学园区串联通霄镇县乡道联外道路可行性研究计划，以带动通霄镇发展及就业机会，县长钟东锦回应有关铜科联外道路串联通霄县乡道可行性研究计划，中央已核定补助规划费，今天下午即将办理招标作业，待规划完成后，马上委托立委陈超明协助争取中央补助办理，中央既然补助规划经费，显示中央亦认同联外道路串联通霄县乡道的必要性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14439185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439185" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439185-745421.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439185-745421.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439185" class="wp-caption-text">交工处长回应问题。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14439186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439186" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439186-745422.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439186-745422.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439186" class="wp-caption-text">文观局长说明处理情形。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14439187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14439187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439187-745423.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14439187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/02/id14439187-745423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14439187" class="wp-caption-text">工商处长回应提案处理情形。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦并指出，苑里因农田多，过去有很多重划农路规划比较狭小，现在都希望拓宽，但是土地取得较困难，若有希望县府协助的地方，县府一定全力以赴帮助解决问题，比较偏乡的地方道路联外失修，不利地方发展，县府会努力将一定年限的道路重新铺设路面，提供乡亲安全的行车路况。◇</p>
