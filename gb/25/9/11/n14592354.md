<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>极西之地的凝视</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592359-794345-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>潮汐间隙显露的沙带，缓缓伸向海面深处。（陈锡兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月11日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）国圣灯塔，立于台湾极西点的风沙之上。铁骨架构，在阳光下投下斜斜的影子，仿佛为海岸线画上一道座标。陈锡兴说，许多旅人爱在地图上收集“极点”，而他选择用空拍重新定义它的美丽——不是单纯的地理符号，而是一场视觉与时间的对话。灯塔脚下的沙纹，随风变幻。早晨，它们细密如织；午后，风势加重，纹理被重新雕刻。走近时，能听见沙粒在脚底轻微移动的声响，像低声的问候。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14592355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592355" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592355-794340.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14592355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592355-794340-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="603" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592355" class="wp-caption-text">国圣灯塔仰望。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14592357" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592357" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592357-794342.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592357" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592357-794342-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592357" class="wp-caption-text">海风在沙丘上留下细密的纹理，日日改写地景。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14592356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592356" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592356-794341.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592356-794341-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592356" class="wp-caption-text">顶头额沙洲自灯塔脚下延伸，仿佛一条通往天际的细线。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>顶头额沙洲的流动地图顶头额沙洲在镜头中展开，如一条延伸至天际的灰白长带。退潮时，沙洲裸露，与远方的云影相接；涨潮时，浪花吞没低处，只留下高地与植被相互依偎。陈锡兴在飞行路径中，捕捉它的变化——这是一张永远未完成的地图，随着季节与潮汐，被不断涂改。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14592362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592362" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592362-794348.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592362-794348-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592362" class="wp-caption-text">浪花推进又退去，留下一道道深浅交错的水纹。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14592360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592360" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592360-794346.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592360-794346-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592360" class="wp-caption-text">沙丘鸟瞰。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>月牙湾的静与动在沙洲的一侧，是弯月般的海岸线。空拍俯瞰，海水以不同深浅的蓝，描绘出渐层的轮廓。消波块排列成弧，挡住了部分浪势，也划出月牙的内缘。浪花推进、退去，节奏安稳。陈锡兴说，这里的海像一位知心的朋友，不会喧闹，却始终在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14592358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592358" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592358-794344.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592358-794344-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592358" class="wp-caption-text">空拍下的沙洲与海岸交会，海天相连如同一笔长画。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14592361" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592361" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592361-794347.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592361-794347-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592361" class="wp-caption-text">消波块弧线构成的海湾，静静拥抱着蓝色海水。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>天空之境的渔塭沙洲的另一侧，是井然排列的渔塭。阳光倾斜时，水面映出云影，渔塭化为一格格“天空之境”。从空中望去，绿色的埂线交错，像一幅精心缝制的布面。这里不只是养殖场，更是海岸人家的日常与生计。往南延伸，是台湾最大的七股潟湖。水道蜿蜒，浮棚与蚵架点缀在水面上，宛如音符。离岸处，还有“网仔寮沙州”，仿佛一条守护线，隔开了外海的汹涌与潟湖的平静。这片生态丰富的水域，是候鸟的驿站，也是渔民的海上农田。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14592363" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592363" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592363-794349.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592363" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592363-794349-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592363" class="wp-caption-text">井然排列的渔塭映着云影，水面如镜，反射着午后的光。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14592364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592364" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592364-794350.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592364-794350-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592364" class="wp-caption-text">沙洲尽头与外海相接，浪与风在这里交谈。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>夕阳的收尾陈锡兴喜欢在黄昏时分结束拍摄。当太阳沉向海平线，灯塔的身影拉得很长，顶头额沙洲被染上金色。风停了，沙面的纹理变得清晰，仿佛要将这一刻封存。浪声低低，光影缓缓退场，极西之地恢复到它的日常节奏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14592366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592366" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592366-794353.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592366" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592366-794353-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592366" class="wp-caption-text">国圣灯塔附近能找到的植物。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14592365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14592365" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592365-794351.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14592365" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14592365-794351-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14592365" class="wp-caption-text">陈锡兴喜欢在黄昏时分结束拍摄。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
