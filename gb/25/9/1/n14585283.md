<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>七大护肾饮料排行榜</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><strong>编者按：</strong>很多人看了洪医师的视频《<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/24/6/30/n14280832.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener">含糖饮料超伤肾</a>》后，提问说：难道所有的饮料都伤肾吗？喝什么饮料可以护肾？下面我们就来看看护肾饮料有哪些。</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">手摇饮料店</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">可说是台湾的特产之一，路上随时可见人人拿着一杯手摇饮料。根据台湾财政部统计资料，2022年4月，全台饮料店创下历史新高，达2.2万家，比便利商店多出1万家，高雄跟台南为店家最多的前两个县市，高雄市有3,656家，占比15.88%，台南市有3,333家，占比14.48%。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">台湾年轻人喝饮料习惯早已融入生活中，不禁让人担心：这些年轻人的肾脏是否经得起衰老的考验？</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">建议大家摄取以下七种对肾脏健康有益的饮料，并依据国际医学或营养学文献，说明其保护肾脏的机制。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">首先提醒两点：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">第一、每天喝</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">足相当于体重</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的水</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分，仍以白开水为优先。若想喝一点饮料，可选择下列饮料。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">第二、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">这些护肾饮料还是有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">高磷钾的问题，因此仅适用于肾功能健康者。若你是肾衰竭的肾友，谨守限蛋白、限磷、限钾的原则之余，可以少量饮用；而且务必追踪血磷钾。如果你血中的磷跟钾已经超过5，就只能喝白开水，任何饮料都不要碰。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>第七名黑豆水</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11429136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11429136" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/black-bean-tea3_792479851.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11429136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/black-bean-tea3_792479851-450x300.jpg" alt="黑豆水含丰富的花青素等营养素，有助于抗癌、预防脑中风等疾病。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11429136" class="wp-caption-text">黑豆水。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Journal of Medicinal Food</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的研究，黑豆富含花青素及其他抗氧化合物，能</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">减少氧化的损伤，让受损的肾脏功能恢复正常，特别适用于早期预防肾脏受损。</span></p>
<p><b>叮咛：</b></p>
<p><b>?</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">每天喝</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">一杯</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">240</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">毫升。</span></p>
<p><b>?</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">过量摄取恐增加肾脏代谢植物性蛋白的压力</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">反而易</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">伤肾。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>第六名生姜茶</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12482255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12482255" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_527406304.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-12482255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_527406304-450x300.jpg" alt="换季或气温变化会引起流鼻水、鼻塞等过敏症状加重，此时可饮用姜茶舒缓。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12482255" class="wp-caption-text">姜茶。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Food and Chemical Toxicology</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的研</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">究发现</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，生姜含有姜辣素，具有显着的抗发炎作用，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">有助于降低肾脏的炎症反应，还可以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">改善肾功能的相关数值。</span></p>
<p><b>?</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">每日</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">喝</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杯，约</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">240</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">480</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">毫升。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?饮用过量可能引发肠胃不适，或提高出血风险</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>第五名柠檬水</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14018036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14018036" style="width: 312px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14018036-shutterstock_649504519.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14018036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14018036-shutterstock_649504519-450x338.jpg" alt="Mason jar glass of lemonade with lemons and straw isolated on a white background" width="312" b="234" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14018036" class="wp-caption-text">柠檬水。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">JAMA</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Journal of the American Medical Association</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）的研究指出，柠檬水中的柠檬酸能促使尿液中</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">PH</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">值维持在碱性，帮助预防肾结石。</span></p>
<p><b>叮咛：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">用</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">毫升新鲜柠檬汁稀释成</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">250</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">500</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">毫升的柠檬水，每天摄取</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杯。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>第四名蔓越莓汁</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14585578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585578" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585578-shutterstock_2427885287.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585578-shutterstock_2427885287-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585578" class="wp-caption-text">蔓越莓汁。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Nutrition Reviews</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">研究显示，蔓越莓中的原花青素可以防止细菌附着在尿路的上皮细胞，从而减少尿路感染的风险，这对肾脏有间接的保护作用，特别是对慢性肾脏病且反复泌尿道感染的患者。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?每天喝一杯约</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">240</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">毫升。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?过量饮用会使草酸盐累积，增加泌尿道结石风险。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>第三名绿茶</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13890275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13890275" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890275-tea-copy-600x400.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13890275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/12/id13890275-tea-copy-600x400-450x299.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13890275" class="wp-caption-text">绿茶<span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span>(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kidney International Reports</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的研究发现，绿茶中的儿茶素具有强大的抗氧化及抗发炎作用，有助于减缓肾脏细胞过激的氧化反应，改善慢性肾衰竭的进程。该研究提到，定期摄取绿茶能降低蛋白尿的发生率。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?每天</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">喝</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杯的绿茶，约</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">500</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">750</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">毫升。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">茶叶含有草酸</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，饮用过量恐提高泌尿道结石的风险。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>第二名黑咖啡</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14542538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14542538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14542538-shutterstock_560673883.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14542538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/07/id14542538-shutterstock_560673883-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14542538" class="wp-caption-text">黑咖啡。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">American Journal of Kidney Diseases</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的研究显示，适量摄取咖啡有助于减少慢性肾脏病的风险，改善肾丝球的过滤率</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">eGFR</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。咖啡中的抗氧化剂绿原酸、咖啡豆醇等可降低肾脏细胞的氧化损伤跟炎症反应。</span></p>
<p><b>?</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">每天喝</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">杯，约</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">240</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">720</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">毫升。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">?饮用过多的咖啡因，可能造成心悸、头痛、脱水或脱钙的情形，反而伤肾。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>第一名酸樱桃汁</b></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14585579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585579" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585579-shutterstock_2634452319.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585579-shutterstock_2634452319-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585579" class="wp-caption-text">酸樱桃汁。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Journal of the American College of Nutrition指出</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，酸樱桃中的花青素和其他多酚化合物，能显着地减少炎症指标，如</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">CRP</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（反映蛋白</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">C</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）。由于慢性肾衰竭的进展通常与系统性的发炎相关，酸樱桃的抗炎特性针对肾脏有间接的保护作用。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据Arthritis &amp; Pheumatism</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">研究，酸樱桃汁可以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">降低尿酸的水平，减少痛风发作频率，间接保护肾脏健康。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">失眠往往是糖尿病、慢性肾衰竭患者肾功能恶化的因子。其他文献发现，酸樱桃汁是天然的褪黑激素跟色胺酸，有助于调节睡眠。</span></p>
<p><b>叮咛：</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">每天摄取</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">240</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">毫升，大概</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">盎司。</span><strong>◎◇</strong></p>
