<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>基隆情人湖环山步道的静与动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585587-792162-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>从高处俯瞰情人湖周边海湾，防波堤环抱着港口，金色沙滩与翠绿海水相映成趣。（Kimball Wen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾基隆报导）走进基隆西北角，海风在耳边轻拍，咸味夹杂着阳光的气息。情人湖环山步道，是当地人和游客熟知的秘境，既能眺望无垠大海，也能在林间小径享受静谧时光。摄影家 Kimball Wen 带着相机，循着蜿蜒的山径，寻觅属于这片湖与海的“情人”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14585591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585591" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585591-792166.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585591-792166-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585591" class="wp-caption-text">登山小径出口处，阳光穿过树叶，在地面与行人间勾勒出剪影。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14585592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585592" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585592-792167.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585592-792167-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585592" class="wp-caption-text">逆光下的叶片透着光泽，与蓝天白云构成清新的画面。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14585593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585593" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585593-792168.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585593-792168-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585593" class="wp-caption-text">林间绿意中，竹笋从节间冒出，昭示着生命的蓬勃。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海蓝蓝的呼唤站上观景点，海面延展到天际，远方的基隆屿静静矗立，宛如守候在外海的一位老朋友。微风吹拂，海蓝蓝的色调层层堆叠，偶尔有白色船影划破宁静，捎来一丝动感。Kimball Wen 说：“你自辽阔微风，海蓝蓝的袭来。”那是一种迎面而来的温柔，没有声响，却让人心头一暖。沿着山腰往下望，港湾被防波堤拥抱，沙滩上散落着五颜六色的遮阳伞。盛夏午后，海水清澈，游人戏水、划桨，笑声在风中传递。这里既有海的开阔，也有生活的日常气息。防波堤外的浪花翻涌，与内湾的平静形成对比，正如 Kimball Wen 的文字：“美丽心事无语，也是?种告白。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14585588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585588" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585588-792163.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585588-792163-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585588" class="wp-caption-text">深蓝与浅绿交错的海面，远方渔船点缀在一望无际的太平洋上。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14585589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585589-792164.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585589-792164-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585589" class="wp-caption-text">情人湖畔的环湖步道，游人漫步其间，享受午后宁静时光。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14585590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585590" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585590-792165.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585590-792165-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585590" class="wp-caption-text">旅人倚着栏杆眺望湖景，谈笑间流露出轻松与惬意。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林间的光影步道穿梭在树影间，阳光斑驳地洒落，映在覆满苔藓的树干上，映在湖面倒影里。湖水静谧，倒映着枝叶的曲线，仿佛另一个世界。这里的情人湖不大，却足以让人放慢脚步，听见自己的心跳。在湖畔的木栈道上，不时可见散步的情侣、聊天的长者、牵手而行的亲子。每个人都有不同的故事，而湖静静地收纳了这些片段。Kimball Wen 用镜头捕捉的不只是风景，更是人与自然之间那份微妙的依存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14585594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585594" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585594-792169.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585594-792169-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585594" class="wp-caption-text">小船破浪驶进港湾，水面留下一道道涟漪。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14585595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585595" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585595-792170.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585595-792170-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585595" class="wp-caption-text">游客在浅滩划桨、嬉水，享受盛夏的海上时光。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14585596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585596" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585596-792171.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585596-792171-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585596" class="wp-caption-text">远眺孤立于海上的基隆屿，绿色山体与深蓝海水交织出壮丽景致。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>告白的方式午后的阳光渐渐倾斜，海面泛起金色的光带。一艘白色小船驶入港湾，浪花如轻吻般追逐着船尾。“有一船帆白浪，划破人间苍茫。”那一刻，海与天、湖与人，都在无声中完成了告白。情人湖环山步道，让人一次拥抱湖光与海色。它不只是风景区，更像一封长长的情书，写给来访的人，也写给守候在这片土地上的情人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14585597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585597" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585597-792172.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585597-792172-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585597" class="wp-caption-text">情人湖下方的沙滩热闹非凡，戏水、划桨与遮阳伞构成缤纷画面。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14585598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585598" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585598-792173.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585598-792173-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585598" class="wp-caption-text">环山步道高处俯瞰港湾，沙滩、堤防与海面色彩层次分明。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14585599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14585599" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585599-792174.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14585599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14585599-792174-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14585599" class="wp-caption-text">湖水如镜，映照着交错的树枝线条，宛如一幅自然水墨画。（Kimball Wen提供）</figcaption></figure>
