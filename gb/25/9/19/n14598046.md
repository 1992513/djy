<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澳洲雪梨台湾校友会欢庆成立两周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598049-TAASA-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>澳洲雪梨台湾校友会(Taiwan Alumni Association, Sydney, Australia, TAASA)日前在驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处举办年会。图为驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处吴正伟处长致词。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月19日讯】澳洲雪梨台湾校友会（<span lang="EN-US">Taiwan Alumni Association, Sydney, Australia, TAASA）</span>日前在驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处举办年会，现任会长新州自由党上议院党鞭暨州务影子厅长<span lang="EN-US">Hon. Chris Rath</span>上议员亲临出席。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598061-TAASA-3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598061-TAASA-3-600x294.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598061" class="wp-caption-text">澳洲雪梨台湾校友会(Taiwan Alumni Association, Sydney, Australia, TAASA)日前在驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处举办年会。图为吴正伟处长（左5)及TAASA会长Chris Rath上议员（右6）与全体出席人员合影。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14598052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598052" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598052-TAASA-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598052-TAASA-2-600x342.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598052" class="wp-caption-text">澳洲雪梨台湾校友会(Taiwan Alumni Association, Sydney, Australia, TAASA)日前在驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处举办年会。图为TAASA年会情形。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="x_MsoNormal">活动于播放<span lang="EN-US">TAASA</span>过去两年办理活动的精彩集锦揭开序幕，吴处长于致词时表示，年会主要目的是回顾过去，展望未来；特别感谢<span lang="EN-US">Chris Rath</span>会长展现的领导力，不仅在州议会办理推动台湾参与<span lang="EN-US">WHO</span>的论坛，也参加许多官方及台湾社群的活动，增进澳洲友人对台湾的了解。另外也要感谢<span lang="EN-US">Chris Rath</span>上议员同意续任第二届的<span lang="EN-US">TAASA</span>会长，相信在他的带领下，<span lang="EN-US">TAASA</span>不仅会务蓬勃发展，未来更能强化台澳双边的合作与交流。</p>
<p class="x_MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Chris Rath</span>会长则肯定办事处在促进台湾与新州及澳洲合作交流的积极与努力，并重申对台湾民主自由与社会进步发展的支持；<span lang="EN-US">TAASA</span>副会长及新州自由党青年部会长<span lang="EN-US">Cooper Gannon</span>、麦觉理大学（<span lang="EN-US">Macquarie University)</span>教授郭美芬、黄吕琛等也发言给予建议，支持<span lang="EN-US">TAASA</span>持续筹办更多有意义的交流活动。办事处最后也简报外交部及教育部台湾奖学金与华语奖学金等项目供<span lang="EN-US">TAASA</span>会员参考。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598056" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598056-TAASA-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598056-TAASA-1-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598056" class="wp-caption-text">澳洲雪梨台湾校友会(Taiwan Alumni Association, Sydney, Australia, TAASA)日前在驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处举办年会。图为TAASA续任会长-新州上议员Chris Rath致词。（驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="x_MsoNormal">为了让澳洲校友们重温正港台湾味，办事处特别准备台湾家乡味的精致饭盒、饮料及台中俊美凤梨酥，让大家在美食及欢乐气氛中分享在台湾的生活趣事。</p>
<p class="x_MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">TAASA</span>成立的目的是让澳洲朋友们有一个相互交流、分享资讯及人脉网络建构的平台，<span lang="EN-US">TAASA</span>于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月成立，全球各地相关的<span lang="EN-US">TAA</span>校友组织约有<span lang="EN-US">80</span>个。本次活动也邀请新州雪梨地区主要大学台湾同学会新任会长与干部等共同与会，鼓励更多台澳青年学子的互动，深化台澳关系。</p>
<p>（<span data-olk-copy-source="MessageBody">驻雪梨台北经济文化办事处供稿）</span></p>
