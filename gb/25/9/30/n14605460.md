<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>经文处护留学生安全 邀警员谈防诈骗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605459-DSC01982-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>9月27日，驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处教育组在华埠龙凤酒楼举办会议，邀请各高校台湾同学会代表参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年09月30日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）9月27日中午，驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处教育组在华埠龙凤酒楼举办会长联席会议，邀请各高校台湾同学会代表联谊，并请波士顿警察局专家讲解防范诈骗的要点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14605458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605458" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605458-DSC01976.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605458-DSC01976-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605458" class="wp-caption-text">参加会议的经文处官员、同学会代表和讲座嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605448-DSC01928.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605448-DSC01928-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605448" class="wp-caption-text">教育组在华埠龙凤酒楼举办会议，邀请各高校台湾同学会代表参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>教育组与同学会增强联系</strong></h3>
<p>
当天，驻波士顿教育组聚集了来自新英格兰地区多所大学的台湾同学会代表，其中多来自麻州高校如哈佛大学、MIT等，也有来自罗德岛设计学院和布朗大学的同学会代表。透过交流与座谈，大家增进彼此联系，并加强与教育组及新英格兰地区台湾同学会联合会（FTSANE）间的互动合作。</p>
<p>会议于上午11点开始，由新英格兰地区台湾同学会联合会的李典璋会长主持。各校同学会会长及干部们进行自我介绍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14605452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605452-DSC01952.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605452-DSC01952-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605452" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰地区台湾同学会联合会的李典璋会长主持会议。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605451-DSC01947.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605451-DSC01947-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605451" class="wp-caption-text">参与会议的同学介绍自己。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605450-DSC01941.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605450-DSC01941-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605450" class="wp-caption-text">新英格兰地区台湾同学会联合会的干部们介绍自己。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，教育组黄薳玉组长介绍了教育组业务与服务内容，也宣传了教育部玉山学者计划，教育部海外人才经验分享及国际连结计划，“TYRA Mentor-Mentee Program”项目，还有办事处与留学生相关的各项服务。她也鼓励留学生多多利用教育组的资源。</p>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处廖朝宏处长也到场致词。他感谢各校同学会持续发挥团结互助的精神，协助海外学子适应异乡生活。他也强调，办事处及教育组将持续支持各校同学会发展，尽力提供协助，成为同学们在海外的坚强后盾。他也祝福同学们在新的学期学业顺利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14605457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605457-DSC01973.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605457-DSC01973-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605457" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处廖朝宏处长到场致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605449-DSC01930.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605449-DSC01930-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605449" class="wp-caption-text">经文处教育组黄薳玉组长介绍业务。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<h3><strong>警方专家分析诈骗手法</strong></h3>
餐会期间，波士顿警察局资深金融犯罪分析师科文（Sandra Kirwin）为同学们带来防诈骗专题讲座。另外还有3名警员到场与大家交流。</p>
<p>科文分析了常见的诈骗手法，并特别指出，网路诈骗案件迅速增加，数量在各种诈骗案中排第一，年轻人正成为主要受害者。常见的诈骗类型，包括银行卡盗刷、报税诈骗、虚假网购、网络支付诈骗、虚假出租房等等。她也提及，波士顿地区去年被诈骗的总额大约是4千万美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14605453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605453" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605453-DSC01955.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605453-DSC01955-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605453" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿警察局资深金融犯罪分析师科文（Sandra Kirwin）讲解诈骗案件。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605454-DSC01959.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605454-DSC01959-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605454" class="wp-caption-text">科文和参加会议的学生交谈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605455-DSC01964.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14605455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605455-DSC01964-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605455" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿经文处感谢警察们帮助学生防范诈骗。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>科文还谈到台湾的诈骗案情况，提醒同学们在海外生活时，一定要与台湾的家人朋友保持联系，以免他们被假扮亲人的网络骗子伤害。</p>
<p>现场学生提问，假如将钱转给了骗子，是否有可能追回？科文回答说，追回钱款的可能性很低，所以务必谨慎防骗，尤其在各种交易中都要提防“好到不真实”的优惠。◇</p>
