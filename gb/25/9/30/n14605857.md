<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>追光之旅 Nina的极光记录</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605858-798662-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Nina拍到的冰岛极光。（Nina Chen提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾报导）来自台湾的空拍达人 Nina，九月底走访瑞典与冰岛，为的就是亲眼见到传说中的极光。她带着手机与脚架，在零度以下的夜里静静守候，盯着天空的变化。“一开始很担心会不会白跑一趟，因为天气常常阴晴不定，有时候云层一来，什么都看不见。”Nina 回忆。她说，极光不像照片上总是绚烂夺目，更多时候其实只是淡淡的一抹绿光，要长时间曝光才能看见清晰的线条。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14605859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605859" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605859-798663.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605859-798663-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605859" class="wp-caption-text">来自台湾的空拍达人 Nina，九月底走访瑞典与冰岛，为的就是亲眼见到传说中的极光。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605860" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605860-798664.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605860-798664-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605860" class="wp-caption-text">Nina带着手机与脚架，在零度以下的夜里静静守候，盯着天空的变化。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605861" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605861-798665.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605861-798665-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605861" class="wp-caption-text">Nina 说，极光不像照片上总是绚烂夺目，更多时候其实只是淡淡的一抹绿光。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一抹绿意冰岛南部的平原上，夜里的草原被寒风吹得沙沙作响。就在云缝拉开的一刻，天空浮现一丝绿色的光带，仿佛有人在天际轻轻刷上一笔。Nina 说：“刚开始看到的时候，其实很微弱，要靠长曝才拍得出来，但现场的感觉很特别，像是天空在慢慢呼吸。”她记得自己当时蹲在路边，双手冻得发麻，却因为眼前的光影感到无比踏实，“那一刻真的觉得自己来对了”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14605862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605862" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605862-798666.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605862-798666-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605862" class="wp-caption-text">冰岛南部的平原上，夜里的草原被寒风吹得沙沙作响。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605863-798667.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605863-798667-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605863" class="wp-caption-text">Nina 说：“刚开始看到的时候，其实很微弱，要靠长曝才拍得出来。”（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605864-798668.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605864-798668-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605864" class="wp-caption-text">Nina 记得自己当时蹲在路边，双手冻得发麻，却因为眼前的光影感到无比踏实。”（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与相机的对话拍摄极光需要耐心，也需要技巧。Nina 把她的经验分享给同行的朋友：“必备脚架….蓝芽遥控…开启的夜间模式，十秒曝光，比较有极光的线条感 ，曝光30秒就会像水流一样柔和。”她笑说，这段话几乎成了她这趟旅程的口头禅。每一次按下快门，她都在尝试不同的曝光时间，想捕捉极光最适合的形态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14605865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605865" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605865-798669.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605865-798669-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605865" class="wp-caption-text">“必备脚架….蓝芽遥控…开启的夜间模式，十秒曝光，比较有极光的线条感。”Nina说。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605866" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605866-798670.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605866-798670-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605866" class="wp-caption-text">在瑞典北部，Nina 选择到林地边缘拍摄，画面里常常有几株瘦高的树与夜空同框。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从瑞典森林到冰岛海岸在瑞典北部，Nina 选择到林地边缘拍摄，画面里常常有几株瘦高的树与夜空同框。她说，这样的场景让她想起台湾山里的松树，却又因极光的存在多了陌生的气氛。“有时候云层厚得像棉被，极光就在背后闪动，拍出来的颜色很奇妙，像是紫色和绿色交错。”她形容。到了冰岛，她则是在宽阔的道路旁等待。草丛、远方微弱的灯火，和天空的绿光构成了一张张不同的影像。她最喜欢的一张，是极光刚好和流星出现在同一个画面里，“真的是幸运”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14605867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605867" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605867-798671.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605867" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605867-798671-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605867" class="wp-caption-text">每一次按下快门，Nina都在尝试不同的曝光时间，想捕捉极光最适合的形态。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605868" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605868-798672.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605868" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605868-798672-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605868" class="wp-caption-text">“有时候云层厚得像棉被，极光就在背后闪动，拍出来的颜色很奇妙，像是紫色和绿色交错。” Nina说。 （Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14605869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14605869" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605869-798673.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14605869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14605869-798673-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14605869" class="wp-caption-text">草丛、远方微弱的灯火，和天空的绿光构成了一张张不同的影像。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>极光之外除了拍摄，Nina 也在旅途中留下许多感触。她说，很多人想像极光是壮观的烟火秀，但实际上更像是静静流动的画布，需要时间和心情去体会。对 Nina 来说，拍摄极光不是炫耀的战利品，而是一段过程。她希望透过这些照片，让更多人理解大自然的细腻与不可预测。“不是每一次都能成功，但只要愿意等待，总会有属于自己的极光。”她说。</p>
