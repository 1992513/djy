<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大罗兰溪的秋天记忆 摄影家的溯溪日记</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595778-795324-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>领队细心引导，确保每位参与者都能安心体验溪流之美。（陈锦尧提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新北报导）走进新北乌来福山，顺着导航前往蝴蝶公园，便能抵达大罗兰溪。这里的水，清澈得像未经尘世打扰，石缝间的水声，与山谷的风声交织，成为秋季最自然的乐章。摄影家、同时也是登山高手的陈锦尧，选择在入秋记录下这条溪流的样貌。他说，来大罗兰溪，最重要的不是挑战，而是安全。溯溪必备的“三宝”——岩盔、救生衣、防寒衣袜子，再搭配一双传统的“菜瓜布鞋”，才能真正贴近这片溪流。鞋底粗糙的摩擦力，往往比昂贵的专业鞋更能稳稳抓住湿滑的石面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14595774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14595774" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595774-795320.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14595774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595774-795320-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14595774" class="wp-caption-text">安全头盔、救生衣、防寒衣，准备齐全，才能放心在溪谷里享受冒险。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14595775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14595775" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595775-795321.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14595775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595775-795321-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14595775" class="wp-caption-text">在溪边岩石上稍作休息，分享彼此的冒险心得，体验的不只是运动，更是友情。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在溪流之间队员穿着鲜艳的安全装备，穿梭在巨石与清流之间。有人俯身逆水而上，有人暂歇在水潭边，抬头仰望绿意的天际。溪水拍打石头，激起一层层白浪，将画面衬托得更为生动。“溯溪不是单纯的运动，它像是一种和自然的对话。”陈锦尧说。水的力道提醒人谦卑，石头的缝隙引导人小心前行。每一步都需要专注，但在专注中，也能找到一份久违的心静。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14595779" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14595779" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595779-795325.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14595779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595779-795325-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14595779" class="wp-caption-text">大罗兰溪的激流中，队员穿梭在巨石与清澈水流之间，享受溯溪带来的挑战与凉意。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14595771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14595771" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595771-795317.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14595771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595771-795317-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14595771" class="wp-caption-text">夏日的乌来大罗兰溪，水质清澈见底，穿戴好安全装备，开启溯溪探险的一天。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古圳步道的旁白溯溪之外，福山一带还有另一种缓慢的选择“古圳步道”。顺着古圳走，可以听见溪流声伴随脚步，溪水流过石缝，蜻蜓、蝴蝶在空中飞舞，偶尔还能看见鱼群在水中穿梭。这些画面，也被陈锦尧的镜头记录下来。他不只专注于人与溪流的互动，也留意溪边的蕨类、山壁的苔痕，甚至溪水映照出的光影。这些细节，让大罗兰溪的故事不只停留在冒险，更延伸成一种对自然的细腻凝视。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14595773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14595773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595773-795319.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14595773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595773-795319-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14595773" class="wp-caption-text">急流冲刷过身体，清凉沁入心脾，这是大自然最直接的拥抱。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14595776" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14595776" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595776-795322.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14595776" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595776-795322-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14595776" class="wp-caption-text">再冷的溪水，也浇不熄大家的热情，这就是夏天最棒的户外活动。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水声里的夏天九月的午后，大罗兰溪的水声最为响亮。陈锦尧的影片里，潺潺的水声比任何配乐都真切。观者不需要亲临现场，也能透过影像感受到溪谷的凉意。他形容：“大罗兰溪的水，干净得像一面镜子。”在水里，身影与天空同时被折射；而当人浸入溪中，仿佛成了山水画里的一笔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14595777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14595777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595777-795323.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14595777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595777-795323-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14595777" class="wp-caption-text">激流中紧握彼此的手，团队合作是溯溪最美的画面。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>真实的风景不同于观光化的热闹，乌来的大罗兰溪仍保有朴实。这里的溯溪，不只是挑战体能，而是一次重新认识自然的旅程。在陈锦尧的镜头下，大罗兰溪既有激流的力量，也有细水的柔情。它提醒人们，在快速的日常之外，仍有一片溪谷静静流淌，等待被倾听。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14595772" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14595772" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595772-795318.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14595772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14595772-795318-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14595772" class="wp-caption-text">溯溪的魅力，不只在于挑战，更是与自然亲密接触时，那份自在与喜悦。（陈锦尧提供）</figcaption></figure>
