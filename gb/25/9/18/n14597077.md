<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纪念麦克阿瑟将军 扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡举办围棋大赛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597083-186020-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>围棋大赛获奖者与韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡合影。（主办单位提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年09月18日讯】（大纪元记者黄小堂纽约报导）位于新泽西州自由州立公园（Liberty State Park）莫里斯运河公园的韩战退伍军人纪念碑，9月13日举办了一场别具意义的围棋大赛。活动由韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡举办，旨在纪念韩战中为自由与民主牺牲的美国士兵，并借此传达对美国的深切感恩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14597081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597081" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597081-186018.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14597081" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597081-186018-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597081" class="wp-caption-text">韩裔联邦参议员金安迪（Andy Kim）与韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡合影。（主办单位提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14597085" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597085" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597085-186022.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14597085" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597085-186022-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597085" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州第三选区国会众议员苏奥齐（Tom Suozzi，中）与韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡合影。（主办单位提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14597080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597080" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597080-186024.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14597080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597080-186024-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597080" class="wp-caption-text">韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡（左）与国会众议员孟昭文在华府合影。（主办单位提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14597079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597079" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597079-186023.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14597079" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597079-186023-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597079" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州第三选区国会众议员苏奥齐（Tom Suozzi）与韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡合影。（主办单位提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>徐孝锡院长在致词时回顾历史指出，韩战爆发后，南韩一度陷入存亡危机，直至麦克阿瑟将军成功策划仁川登陆作战，才彻底扭转战局。他表示：“我80年的人生幸福都要感谢麦克阿瑟将军，后代也要继承他的名字。为了纪念他举办了这个比赛。受恩就要报恩，既然救了我们的生命，我也要报答麦克阿瑟将军的祖国美国。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14597078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597078" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597078-186027.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14597078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597078-186027-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597078" class="wp-caption-text">韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡与纪念韩战牺牲英雄铜像合影。地上的图像为韩国地图。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了围棋比赛，徐院长也特别颁发奖章给美国唯一获联邦认证的韩战老兵协会（KWVA），以表彰其守护自由的贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14597086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597086" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597086-186033.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14597086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597086-186033-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597086" class="wp-caption-text">韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡（右）与韩战老兵协会会长李先生合影。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>韩战老兵协会会长李先生在会上表示，能听到韩战老兵的故事，并得到韩国友人的跨国支持，令他倍感温暖。他说：“非常感谢徐院长千里迢迢来到这里，为我们付出心力。”</p>
<p>此次比赛吸引纽约与新泽西州的百余名专业与业余棋手参与，年龄层涵盖学生至长者。参赛学生表示，围棋不仅深奥且富有文化底蕴，更能启发人生智慧。另一位参赛者则分享围棋让他看见自己的弱点，提升判断与计算能力，学会面对恐惧与建立信心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14597087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597087" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597087-186035.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14597087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597087-186035-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597087" class="wp-caption-text">百余名专业与业余棋手参与围棋大赛。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14597088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597088" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597088-186037.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14597088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597088-186037-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597088" class="wp-caption-text">百余名专业与业余棋手参与围棋大赛。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14597089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597089-186039.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14597089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597089-186039-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597089" class="wp-caption-text">韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡与社区人士比赛下围棋。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>徐孝锡院长指出，围棋自两千年前自中国传入韩国，古人以此修心养性。在今日快节奏的社会中，围棋能帮助年轻人远离缺乏情感的电玩游戏，回归专注与平静。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14597084" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597084" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597084-186021.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14597084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597084-186021-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597084" class="wp-caption-text">围棋大赛获奖者与韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡合影。（主办单位提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>围棋大赛也获得纽约当地社区代表支持。亚美选民联盟主席朴容范（Terence Park）协助推动比赛，虽尚未熟悉围棋，但表达愿意学习并长期支持。徐院长表示，明年比赛可能继续在自由州立公园举办，也可能移师西点军校（West Point），未来计划在社会各界的支持下，争取扩大规模举办比赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14597082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14597082" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597082-186019.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14597082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14597082-186019-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14597082" class="wp-caption-text">韩国扁康韩医院院长徐孝锡与亚美选民联盟主席朴容范合影。（主办单位提供）</figcaption></figure>
