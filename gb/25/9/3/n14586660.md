<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>叶黄素可防黄斑病变 开心果是謢眼好朋友</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586661-7e411090dc99af9a6c9d5f59f49c7d06.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>都市人常常用眼过度，滑手机、看萤幕，容易出现视力模糊、干眼、老花眼等问题。近来热卖的开心果，原来是“护眼好朋友”。有<ahref="https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S002231662401099X?via%3Dihub" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class">研究</a>发现，每天吃2盎司（57克）开心果，且持续12周，可以令黄斑色素光密度显著增加，显示开心果有助防止黄斑部病变与视力退化。</p><div><figure style="max-width:640px" class="aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586662-fbb9ef79b92888f5764e72ce283c6ca4.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><picture><source srcSet="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F09%2Fid14586662-fbb9ef79b92888f5764e72ce283c6ca4.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" type="image/webp"/><source srcSet="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586662-fbb9ef79b92888f5764e72ce283c6ca4.jpg" type="image/jpeg"/><img src="/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fi.epochtimes.com%2Fassets%2Fuploads%2F2025%2F09%2Fid14586662-fbb9ef79b92888f5764e72ce283c6ca4.jpg&amp;w=1200&amp;q=75" alt="近来热卖的开心果，原来是“护眼好朋友”。（vivooo/shutterstock）" width="640" b="auto"/></picture></a><figcaption>近来热卖的开心果，原来是“护眼好朋友”。（vivooo/shutterstock）</figcaption></figure></div><p>除了开心果之外，还要吃什么改善各种眼睛问题？本文与大家深入探讨。</p><p></p><p>常用电子产品人士：接触蓝光时间长，对眼睛造成大压力。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_2"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_2"></div></div></div><p>年长人士：身体功能退化，营养素消化吸收率下降，建议额外补充。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_1"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_1"></div></div></div><p>深绿色蔬菜摄取不足人士：蔬果摄取不足，抗氧化营养素也会相对不足。</p><p>虽然开心果有助防止黄斑部病变与视力退化，而且有高维他命E及一些必需脂肪酸，但2盎司（57克）开心果大概含有接近30克油脂，已经超过建议每日油脂摄取量的一半，再加上三餐烹调用油，令摄取的油脂及总热量过高。黄苡菱建议每日吃1盎司左右（20<strong>～</strong>30克）开心果，搭配新鲜的深绿色蔬菜跟橘黄色蔬果。</p><p>深绿色蔬菜跟橘黄色蔬果是叶黄素、玉米黄素的主要来源。深绿色蔬菜的叶绿素含量较高，覆盖了叶黄素的颜色。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_3"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_3"></div></div></div><p>常见的富含叶黄素的蔬菜水果有：</p><p>深色绿叶蔬菜：蕃薯叶、小棠菜、菠菜、芥蓝菜、西兰花、羽衣甘蓝。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_3"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_3"></div></div></div><p>黄色橘色蔬菜：玉米、南瓜、红萝卜、彩椒。</p><p>水果：奇异果、木瓜、橙、橘子。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_4"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_4"></div></div></div><p></p><p></p><p>干眼症：维他命A、鱼油DHA、<ahref="https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7281326/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" class="health-article-hover-class">虾红素</a>（虾、鲑鱼、海藻类）。</p><p>眼睛疲劳：虾红素、花青素，可以协助眼睛肌肉放松，适合经常近距离使用电子产品的人补充。</p><p>青光眼眼压高：花青素能改善眼内部血液循环，对眼睛的微血管有保健及舒缓作用。另外可摄取虾红素。</p><div class="hidden lg:block"><div class="m-auto items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden hidden md:flex" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_D_article_300x250_4"><div id="DJY_D_article_300x250_4"></div></div></div><p>白内障：控制血糖、血压、血脂，对预防白内障很重要。另外可补充维他命B1。</p><div class=" lg:hidden"><div class="m-auto flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden print:hidden" style="min-height:266px;padding:8px 8px 8px 8px" data-testid="ad-container-DJY_M_article_336x280_5"><div id="DJY_M_article_336x280_5"></div></div></div><p>老花眼：老花跟老化、氧化压力有关，可摄取β胡萝卜素、维他命E、叶黄素、玉米黄素、维他命C等抗氧化营养素。</p><p>黄苡菱分享了自己的护眼方式：“不管是自己煮饭还是外食，我都很喜欢吃深绿色蔬菜，如菠菜、地瓜叶和苋菜，我也很喜欢吃胡萝卜。另外，如果没有吃到足够的鱼，我会吃鱼油来补充DHA。DHA可以帮助眼睛保湿，避免眼睛干涩。”◇</p><p><em>身处纷乱之世，心存健康之道，就看<span
