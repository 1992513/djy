<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳两小镇房价年涨逾两成 全澳最高</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年09月03日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张小凡澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">最新数据显示，西澳两个沿海小镇的房价在过去一年里全国涨幅最高。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据“西澳人报”报导，分析机构</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Cotality</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的数据显示，</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Albany</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">小镇的房价在截至</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">7</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">月的一年中上涨了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">23</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，中位数达到</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">68</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">765</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。与此同时，</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Geraldton</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">小镇的房价在同期上涨了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">20.8</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，达到中位数</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">51</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">万</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">6850</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">从全国来看，州府城市的房价在过去一个季度上涨了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1.8</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，而偏远地区仅上涨</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1.7</span>%<span lang="en-AU">——</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">这是九个月以来，州府城市房价涨幅首次超过偏远地区。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Albany</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">小镇炙手可热的市场还表现出全国最短的销售周期，房屋在市场上平均只停留</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">12</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">天，而卖家给予的价格折扣是全国最低之一，平均仅</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">1.7</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">此外，澳洲偏远地区的租金在过去一年上涨了</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">5.6</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">，而大都会城市的租金仅上涨</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">3</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Cotality</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">的经济学家埃齐女士（</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Kaytlin</span> <span lang="en-AU">Ezzy</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">）将州府城市房价回升归因于澳大利亚储备银行今年早些时候的降息。经济学家普遍预计，今年至少还会有一次降息。</span></p>
<p>“房价增速在<span lang="en-AU">2</span>月和<span lang="en-AU">5</span>月的降息后出现了显着跃升。”埃齐表示。</p>
<p>她还说，紧张的空置率——即市场上可供出租的房源极少——是推动租金上涨的主要原因。</p>
<p>“尽管可负担性不断恶化，但偏远地区迁入人数的增加以及低于平均水平的租赁房源供应，正在对租金形成强劲的上行压力。”她说。</p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">从全国来看，西澳金矿小镇</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">Kalgoorlie-Boulder</span></span><span lang="zh-TW">地区对房地产投资者最有利，租金回报率高达</span><span lang="en-US"><span lang="en-AU">9.3</span>%</span><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></p>
<p>截至<span lang="en-AU">7</span>月，珀斯的独立房房价中位数为<span lang="en-AU">83</span>万<span lang="en-AU">537</span>澳元。</p>
