<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>反共最前哨东引指挥部65周年 老兵重返记忆的岛屿</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589181-793360-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>反共最前哨东引指挥部65周年（陈竑任提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月07日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾马祖报导）东引，这座位于台湾最北方的海上要塞，被誉为“反共最前哨”。今年适逢陆军马祖东引地区指挥部成军65周年，昔日驻守于此的退役军人纷纷返岛，与现役官兵一同庆贺这个别具意义的日子。摄影家周永田，也是当年驻扎在东引的老兵之一，此行他特地携太太同行，踏上这片熟悉的土地。一踏上东引，迎面而来的是熟悉的海风与军歌声。当年并肩作战的同袍，今日再度相聚，彼此的笑容中带着岁月沉淀的温暖。那份曾经共同守卫前线的情感，随着军鼓响起，又重新在心中燃起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14589169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14589169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589169-793348.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14589169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589169-793348-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14589169" class="wp-caption-text">退役老兵与现役官兵、地方人士齐聚在莒山广场前，庆祝东引指挥部65周年队庆，场面热烈。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14589168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14589168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589168-793347.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14589168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589168-793347-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14589168" class="wp-caption-text">反共救国军退役老兵挥舞国旗进场，气势昂扬，象征昔日精神延续至今。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14589170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14589170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589170-793349.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14589170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589170-793349-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14589170" class="wp-caption-text">身穿蓝色纪念衫的退役老兵列队进场，精神抖擞，步伐稳健。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>庆典现场的热血庆祝活动在东涌运动公园展开，战技展演与联合作战模拟震撼全场，展现了平日严格训练的成果。官兵们动作整齐、眼神坚定，传达出“平时能救灾、战时能作战”的决心。除了军事演练，现场还安排了原民舞表演，色彩鲜明的传统服饰与鼓声，为严肃的军事氛围增添了文化与生命力。退役老兵们看着年轻一代在舞台上舞动，仿佛看见了接棒守护的希望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14589171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14589171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589171-793350.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14589171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589171-793350-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14589171" class="wp-caption-text">穿着缤纷传统服饰的原住民族舞者，在队庆活动中表演热情舞蹈，为军事庆典增添浓厚文化气息。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14589174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14589174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589174-793353.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14589174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589174-793353-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14589174" class="wp-caption-text">官兵进行甲车火力展示，模拟战时防卫情境，展现东引指挥部的即战力。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14589173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14589173" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589173-793352.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14589173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589173-793352-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14589173" class="wp-caption-text">65年来，这里经历了战争威胁与和平年代，守护的不仅是领土，更是家园与信念。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再遇老朋友活动间隙，周永田遇见了许多当年的同袍，却像昨天才刚一起走过营区的巡逻路线般亲切。　专业导览员陈竑任熟稔地介绍着东引的新变化，带着老将军和老兵们穿梭在各个角落，拍下许多值得珍藏的照片。“很多地方变了，但海风的味道没变。”周永田笑着说。那片军民同心的情感，依旧在岛上流动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14589186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14589186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589186-793365.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14589186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589186-793365-600x287.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14589186" class="wp-caption-text">东引中柱港码头的清晨。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>日出的守望</h2>
<p>翌日清晨，陈竑任带着大家来到东引灯塔，等待日出。当金色的阳光从海平面升起，照亮那座白色灯塔时，静静地按下快门。这一刻，不只是美景，更是无数守夜人的象征。那是当年每个值勤清晨都会看到的景象，提醒着他们，即使在最遥远的海角，仍有一盏灯为航行者指引方向。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14589175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14589175" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589175-793354.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14589175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14589175-793354-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14589175" class="wp-caption-text">东引美丽的日出。（陈竑任提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>传承的使命从“反共救国军”到今日的“忠义部队”，东引指挥部的历史刻画着无数军人的足迹。65年来，这里经历了战争威胁与和平年代，守护的不仅是领土，更是家园与信念。老兵们的重返，不只是怀旧，更是将那份使命传递给年轻一代，无论时代如何变迁，守护海疆的决心不会改变。</p>
