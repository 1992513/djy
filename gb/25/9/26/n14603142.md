<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>县府中秋慰劳国军致赠加菜金 感谢官兵坚守岗位保家卫国</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603143-797840-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>县府秋节慰问国军官兵。（苗县府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月26日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）中秋佳节将至，县府秘书长陈斌山9月26日上午前往苗县后备指挥部慰劳国军，致赠22个部队加菜金，向坚守岗位的官兵表达深切的关怀与感谢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14603148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603148" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603148-797845.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14603148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603148-797845.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603148" class="wp-caption-text">秘书长颁发秋节加菜金。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14603147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603147" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603147-797844.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14603147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603147-797844.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603147" class="wp-caption-text">颁赠秋节加菜金。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府秘书长陈斌山今天会同县议员郑碧玉、县议会机要秘书李文星、造桥乡长徐连斌、县府民政处长巫恒昭、兵役科长赖木山、苗县后备指挥部顾问团长徐怡玲、副团长郭桂湘、潘秋慧、张竣淋、戴礼杰、军人服务站长陈中吉等人，在苗县后备指挥部颁赠驻守苗县及签有支援协定书等22个部队，共计23万元加菜金。</p>
<p>秘书长陈斌山表示，中秋节是象征团圆的重要节日，然而国军官兵肩负守护国家安全的责任，许多官兵必须留守营区，无法与家人团聚，然而也因国军官兵在第一线坚守岗位、保家卫国，百姓才能安心生活。</p>
<p>尤其，近几年来无论台风、地震等的防救灾、战备或重大任务，都能看见国军第一时间投入支援，成为地方最坚强的后盾，更展现出“军民一家亲”的情谊，让乡亲倍感温暖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14603145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603145" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603145-797842.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14603145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603145-797842.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603145" class="wp-caption-text">顾问团致赠加菜金。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14603146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603146" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603146-797843.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14603146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603146-797843.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603146" class="wp-caption-text">县议会致赠加菜金。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈斌山并指出，希望今天的秋节慰问活动能够让国军官兵感受到社会各界的关怀与支持，县府亦将持续力挺国军，强化全民防卫力量，共同守护家园。</p>
<p>苗县后备指挥部指挥官徐铭志感谢各界长期对全民国防的支持，国军官兵在各项重要节日都能够坚守岗位，为保家卫国尽一份心力，让民众安居乐业。</p>
<p>今天县议会机要秘书李文星代表议长李文斌、苗县后备指挥部顾问团长徐怡玲亦分别致赠加菜金，向国军官兵贺节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14603144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603144" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603144-797841.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14603144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603144-797841.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603144" class="wp-caption-text">中秋佳节慰问国军官兵活动。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
