<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>首届休斯顿国际扯铃大赛 选手同场竞技展创意</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586455-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>首届休斯顿国际扯铃大赛，主辨方、优胜选手和嘉宾们大合影。（陆希／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年09月03日讯】（大纪元记者陆希休斯顿报导）由National Diabolo League（NDL）主办、休士顿侨教中心与海外青年文化大使（FASCA）协办的首届“2025休斯顿国际扯铃大赛”，于8月30日至31日在侨教中心盛大举行，吸引来自各地的爱好者齐聚一堂。比赛分为个人赛与团体赛两大类别，参赛者通过创意编排与现场演出，展现扯铃结合艺术、节奏与技巧的无限魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586456-02.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586456-02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586456" class="wp-caption-text">团队赛优胜组成员和嘉宾们大合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>历经两天的激烈竞赛，8月31日下午举行颁奖典礼，现场座无虚席。经文处处长萧伊芳、侨教中心主任王盈蓉、副主任房亭妤、侨务委员刘秀美与李雄皆应邀出席，与参赛选手及家属逾百人共享成果。萧伊芳致词时，特别感谢选手为训练所付出的时间与努力，更肯定家人默默的支持，让年轻人能勇敢站上舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586459-05.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586459-05-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586459" class="wp-caption-text">战斗赛最终夺冠者Kevin Lam。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>比赛总监陈琬淇（Alyssa Chan）介绍各项成绩：战斗赛是一场非常激烈的晋级比赛，最终由Kevin Lam夺冠。</p>
<p>团队赛部分，第4名是Spyro Spinners队，第3名是NGL TS Kinda Hard队，第2名是NGL TS Kinda light vruh队，并获得50美元礼品卡；第1名是Brain Cells队，并由处长萧伊芳颁发100美元礼品卡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586457-03.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586457-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586457" class="wp-caption-text">个人青少年组优胜者和嘉宾们合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>个人青少年组方面，前五名依序为Quenton Lee、Justin Nieh、Maximus Chen、Eric Chen与Nigel Shiue Lian Lin；</p>
<p>个人公开组则由Jason Pao夺冠，Kevin Lam获第二，Jerry Han居第三，Archie Ramlogan获第四，Joy Deng获第五。个人组优胜者分别获得不同专业扯铃器材，包括限量版“Nigel&#8217;s USA Pink Magpores”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586458" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586458-04.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586458-04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586458" class="wp-caption-text">个人公开组优胜者和嘉宾们合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>FASCA的指导老师戴珮青介绍，NDL由刚自高中毕业的FASCA扯铃队长王楚桓，与德州大学奥斯汀分校学生陈琬淇、洪鹏安共同成立。邱佩冠老师指出，这场比赛由青年们与侨教中心携手筹划，目的在打造安全友善的舞台，让爱好者切磋交流、挑战自我，也借此推广扯铃作为兼具运动与表演艺术的传统文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586461-06.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586461-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586461" class="wp-caption-text">王珞桦（左）和陈琬淇合影。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈琬淇分享筹办心路，表示因大学时期接触扯铃的正面经验，希望通过比赛提供更多交流与学习机会。她与副总监洪鹏安分工协力，即使居住奥斯汀，仍获得休斯顿社区的支持与赞助，让活动顺利成行。</p>
<p>本次比赛评审王珞桦则指出，赛事采取技术70%、艺术30%的标准，总分100%。青少年组参赛者多为17岁以下学生，而公开组则有已在职的青年。她特别提到，美国缺乏完整扯铃训练体系，不少参赛者多靠观看YouTube自学，或与社群成员交流练习。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586462-07.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586462-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586462" class="wp-caption-text">扯铃比赛计分采技术为主。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586463-08.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586463-08-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586463" class="wp-caption-text">扯铃可加上杂耍技巧。（陆希／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>担任裁判的王珞桦进一步表示，扯铃的魅力在于高度的创作空间。选手能自行设计招式，甚至融合杂耍（juggling）元素，为传统技艺注入新生命。</p>
<p>此次休斯顿首届国际扯铃大赛，不仅展现青年无限创意，更让这项源自童玩的表演艺术迈向国际舞台。</p>
