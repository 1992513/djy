<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>山海之间的实景纪录 镜头下的金崙与清水断崖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586228-792354-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>清水断崖直落入海，碧蓝海水在阳光下闪烁，壮阔景象令人心旷神怡。（康明义提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）摄影家康明义形容，这次上台东金崙山上的拍摄点“真是不容易……又远又坎坷又陡”。蜿蜒的山路一路盘上，越过几段狭窄陡坡后，眼前才出现那片熟悉又辽阔的蓝。金崙聚落静静地依着山与海而居，南回高架桥沿着海岸延伸，铁路与公路并行，将人与物资带进这片山海之间的土地。从高处望去，太平洋在阳光下呈现层次分明的蓝，远方的云层交替变化，为画面添了几分动态感。山脚下的河川冲积地清晰可见，雨季冲刷过的河道与平日的宁静形成对比。康明义说，这样的景色，不只是风景照，更记录了金崙的生活背景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586223-792349.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586223-792349-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586223" class="wp-caption-text">云层间的光影洒落在太平洋上，金崙聚落依偎在山与海之间，静谧又壮阔。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586224-792350.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586224-792350-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586224" class="wp-caption-text">金崙外环的高架桥宛如银龙，沿着蔚蓝海岸延伸，见证南回交通的脉动。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海岸曲线与日常节奏金崙外海的曲线，从山顶俯瞰，像是柔和却稳定的笔触，延伸到视线的尽头。高架桥上偶尔驶过的车辆，速度虽快，但在这片开阔的背景下，显得特别渺小。铁道旁的房屋、球场，以及零星的农地，都像是画布上的细节，静静存在。在康明义的照片里，金崙的颜色很真实——山的绿、海的蓝、土地的灰褐，以及居民屋顶的鲜亮色块。这些颜色构成了金崙的日常，也让人想起这里的慢节奏生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586225-792351.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586225-792351-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586225" class="wp-caption-text">绿意与蓝海交织的画面，在金崙山上尽收眼底，辽阔视野令人屏息。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586226-792352.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586226-792352-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586226" class="wp-caption-text">从山峦俯视，金崙湾的海岸线蜿蜒优雅，远方与天色相接。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586227-792353.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586227-792353-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586227" class="wp-caption-text">骑士停驻金崙山腰，静看聚落与大海相依，将壮丽景致留在记忆里。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>清水断崖的恢复离开南回，康明义的镜头转向花莲的清水断崖。他提到：“有别于地震当下的断壁残垣，清水断崖几乎已恢复昔日壮丽的景观，吸引不少游客在崇德观景台驻足观赏。”站在观景台上，面前是笔直落入海中的峭壁，脚下是浪潮拍打岩岸的声音。海面在阳光映照下，反射出一片银光，与蓝天相互映衬。断崖的岩石层理与植被，在近距离观察时，能看见岁月与自然力量的痕迹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586229-792355.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586229-792355-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586229" class="wp-caption-text">远眺太平洋，云影与海面交织出流动的画面，静谧而深远。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586231-792357.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586231-792357-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586231" class="wp-caption-text">浪花拍击岩岸，白色泡沫与深蓝海色形成鲜明对比。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>游客的驻足与凝视崇德观景台上的人不多，但每位到访者都花了些时间停留。有的倚着栏杆拍照，有的静静看着远方的海面。海面上偶尔可见小小的船点缀其中，衬托出海域的广阔。旅人站在平台上阅读解说牌，背景是无边的海与云。这一幕没有刻意安排，却捕捉到人与景互动的自然状态。</p>
<p>从金崙到清水断崖，两地相距遥远，一个藏在南回山路深处，一个挺立在花东纵谷的东缘。但在康明义的影像中，它们有着共同的特点，都是山海交会之处，都是需要亲自走一趟、花时间抵达的地方。康明义不急于在一处拍下大量照片，他更在意的是观察当下的天色、光线与环境的真实面貌。他的照片不追求夸张的色彩，而是忠实呈现眼睛所见，让人透过影像，感受到现场的空气与温度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586233-792359.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586233-792359-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586233" class="wp-caption-text">阳光洒落的海面。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586230" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586230-792356.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586230-792356-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586230" class="wp-caption-text">骑士伫立平台，望向无垠海面，将这份壮丽收进记忆。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586232-792358.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586232-792358-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586232" class="wp-caption-text">午后的光线穿透云层，在太平洋上铺展一条闪亮的银带。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>回到现场的感觉无论是金崙的陡坡之后、还是清水断崖的观景台边，照片之外的故事都在康明义的叙述中被补全。拍摄的路程、抵达时的心情、与景相处的时间，这些都是影像的一部分。对他而言，摄影不只是“按下快门”，而是将一次完整的亲历过程记录下来。山路的颠簸、海风的咸味、阳光在云缝间洒落的瞬间，都是无法透过网路即时传达的感受。康明义说，照片只是开端，真正的收获，是在这些景色中找到属于自己的节奏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586235-792361.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586235-792361-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586235" class="wp-caption-text">被绿意包围的清水断崖旧隧道，静静诉说着过往的交通故事。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
