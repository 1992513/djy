<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>十分钟的等待与绽放 八里远眺的守候</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586404-792448-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>金白色烟火排列成横向队列，线条分明。（王国衍提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月02日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾新北市报导）“为了拍摄那十分钟的烟火秀，务必早出晚归，回到家已经深夜了。”摄影家王国衍说，拍摄烟火是一场体力与耐力的考验。8月24日晚上，七十多岁的他背着相机与脚架，从台中开车到八里淡江大桥一带取景，远远望向淡水渔人码头的夜空，只为捕捉那瞬间的光与影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586402" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586402-792446.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586402-792446-600x343.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586402" class="wp-caption-text">从八里远望，金色与红色交织的烟火在港湾夜空中盛开，与情人桥和城市灯火相映成趣。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586395-792438.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586395-792438-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586395" class="wp-caption-text">绿色与白色烟火如同盛开的花束，在夜空展开花瓣，渔人码头灯光与岸边轮廓清晰可见。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586391-792434.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586391-792434-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586391" class="wp-caption-text">八里淡江大桥一带远望，淡水蓝与浅柠檬黄的烟火在港湾上空同时绽放。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“码头印象”的色彩与节奏活动当晚的第三场烟火秀，是“2025淡水渔人码头仲夏缤纷乐”系列之一，以“码头印象”为主题。主色调是淡水蓝与浅柠檬黄，搭配霞草型与时雨型烟火，像海浪拍击与水光流动。从八里望去，蓝色弧线在夜空舒展，与河面倒影呼应，港湾的轮廓在一片光影中显得分外清晰。王国衍形容，拍烟火最大的挑战是预判。每一发升空的速度、爆开的形状、绽放的位置都不尽相同，需要在瞬间调整快门速度与光圈。他会在施放前观察风向，确保烟雾不会遮住主体，并在脑中先构思好构图，让港湾灯光与烟火同时入镜。这样拍摄的照片，不仅记录了烟火的美，也保留了场域的独特氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586394-792437.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586394-792437-600x390.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586394" class="wp-caption-text">多色烟火同时绽放，红、绿、蓝交错铺满天际，远拍视角完整呈现港湾上空的绚丽。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586401" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586401-792445.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586401-792445-600x465.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="465" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586401" class="wp-caption-text">满天绿色烟火在港湾上空连绵展开，带来清新而强烈的视觉冲击。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586398-792443.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586398-792443-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586398" class="wp-caption-text">金色与蓝色烟火在夜空交错，形成强烈对比，展现“码头印象”主题的色彩律动。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>十分钟的专注从第一发高空弹冲上夜空，到最后一束烟花缓缓熄灭，不到十分钟。这段时间，他专注捕捉不同层次的色彩与形状，让照片呈现立体感与流动感。12吋高空烟火弹接连升空，与中低空烟火交错铺满整片夜空，像一幅色彩不断变换的画作。烟火秀分为三个章节，每一段都有不同的节奏与色彩变化。淡水蓝与柠檬黄的清新组合，带来海港的律动感；多层次的霞草型烟火如同浪花翻涌；时雨型烟火则像细密的水光倾泻，与港口的海风相互呼应。远处的渔人码头灯火闪烁，成为画面中稳定而柔和的背景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586406" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586406-792451.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586406-792451-600x382.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586406" class="wp-caption-text">金色与绿色交错的双巨型烟火同时升空，下方蓝白烟火群衬托出立体感。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586397" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586397-792440.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586397-792440-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586397" class="wp-caption-text">整齐排列的圆形烟火在夜空排开，色彩鲜明，构图中保留了海岸线的静谧。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14586396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586396-792439.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586396-792439-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586396" class="wp-caption-text">满天缤纷烟火层层堆叠，颜色由冷转暖，将港湾夜空点缀得如同巨幅画卷。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>耐心与浪漫　完整的港湾画面他说，拍摄烟火必须放下急躁，“有时候，一发烟火的美只能用一次机会捕捉。”因此他会在每一次爆点前，深吸一口气，准备在最佳时机按下快门。现场观众的惊呼声、快门的连拍声，与海浪的节奏交织成一种属于仲夏的声音。王国衍笑说，远拍的好处是可以看见烟火在港湾上空完整铺展的画面，尤其当风向理想时，烟雾会被吹散，留下一片干净的夜空，让烟火的层次更加分明。等待虽然漫长，但当最后一轮烟火高挂夜空，色彩层层堆叠、徐徐散落，所有的辛苦与疲惫都化为心底的满足。“等了一整天，只为那短短十分钟。”他说，这是摄影的浪漫，也是属于仲夏夜的记忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14586403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14586403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586403-792447.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14586403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14586403-792447-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14586403" class="wp-caption-text">长曝光捕捉下的绿色弧线烟火，宛如海浪翻涌，呼应“码头印象”主题的港湾意象。（王国衍提供）</figcaption></figure>
