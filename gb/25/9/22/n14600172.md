<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>曾文溪畔 甜根子草开成一片白</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600173-796840-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>从大内桥上望去，河川两岸尽是白色花海，气势壮观，被誉为“九月雪”。（陈锡兴提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月22日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台南报导）每年九月，只要南部秋风吹起，走到曾文溪、大内、二溪等河川两岸，最先吸引眼光的，往往不是流水，而是一整片洁白的甜根子草花穗。随风摇曳的白花，像铺满大地的雪，因此有了“九月雪”的美名。摄影家陈锡兴说，站在大内桥上，放眼望去就是壮观又浪漫的花海，游客常能同时把雪白花海、山景与桥梁拍入一张照片。尤其清晨阳光斜照，白絮在水面上映出银光，走在两公里长的堤防步道，既能赏景，也能一路拍照。</p>
<div class="mceTemp"></div>
<figure id="attachment_14600178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600178-796845.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600178-796845-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600178" class="wp-caption-text">甜根子草的花絮映照在水面上，随风流动，像是河流的轻盈羽毛。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14600174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600174" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600174-796841.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14600174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600174-796841-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600174" class="wp-caption-text">甜根子草盛开，白絮般的花穗在风中摇曳，宛如大地铺上一层细雪。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14600175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600175-796842.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600175-796842-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600175" class="wp-caption-text">游客可同时捕捉甜根子草、远山与蓝天的同框美景，留下秋日限定的浪漫印记。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花草的本名与生命力甜根子草，学名 Saccharum spontaneum，是禾本科植物。它喜欢在河川冲积平原或砂质地带生长，根系发达，能固土、抗风蚀，也因此常见于堤防与溪流两岸。花期在每年九到十月，花序细长，颜色由银白到淡紫，阳光下会闪着亮光。这种草在民间用途不少。根茎可制药，有清热、利尿的功效；纤维可当成编织或饲料。对于地方环境来说，它更像是一种守护者，稳定河岸土壤，也提供候鸟与昆虫暂栖的空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14600181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600181-796848.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600181-796848-600x375.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600181" class="wp-caption-text">花海映衬远方恶地地形，白色与翠绿交错，呈现南部特有的景观。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14600182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600182-796849.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600182-796849-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600182" class="wp-caption-text">风吹过草原，花穗倾斜成浪，远山与蓝天成为天然背景。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14600180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600180-796847.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600180-796847-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600180" class="wp-caption-text">曾文溪畔的甜根子草随秋风吹拂，大片花穗洁白如雪，形成壮丽花海。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影家的镜头今年花况特别好，王建中说，已经好几年没见到这么壮观的景象了。他特地走到二溪，拍下羊群穿梭花海的场面。成群山羊低头啃草，后方牧羊人骑着摩托车，还有狗在前方奔跑带路。这一幕，让人联想到塞外草原的景象。他笑说，这样的组合很少见。甜根子草原本就有辽阔的氛围，加上羊群同时出现，画面更显得生动。对摄影人来说，这是一次难得的素材。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14600183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600183-796850.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600183-796850-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600183" class="wp-caption-text">成群羊只在花海间低头觅食，与甜根子草交织出塞外风情。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14600184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600184-796851.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600184-796851-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600184" class="wp-caption-text">牧羊人骑着摩托车，带领羊群前行，狗儿在前方奔跑守护。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14600186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600186-796852.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600186-796852-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600186" class="wp-caption-text">特写镜头下的羊群，眼神专注，与秋日草原形成温暖对比。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秋日限定的风景陈锡兴捕捉到花海与天空同框的画面。当风从溪谷吹过，成片的花穗往同一方向倾斜，宛如翻涌的白浪。走在堤岸上，不只眼睛被白色占满，耳边还能听见草穗彼此摩擦的声音。这样的景象只出现在短短一两个月。等到花期结束，白穗随风飘落，整片草原又恢复成寻常的绿色。也因此，每年九月、十月，总会吸引摄影人、自行车队专程前来，留下属于秋天的印记。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14600179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14600179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600179-796846.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14600179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14600179-796846-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14600179" class="wp-caption-text">在防汛道路上回望，白浪般的甜根子草紧邻绿树，构成鲜明对比。（陈锡兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自然与生活交织甜根子草虽常被视为野草，但它在河川生态与地方生活中都有角色。对农人来说，它象征季节更替；对摄影家而言，则是一种能拍出“台湾秋天”的素材。今年“九月雪”的盛况，让许多人再度感受到南部河川的生命力。</p>
