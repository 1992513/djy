<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>悉尼未来半年预计房价涨幅最大的十个区</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/02/1505022336012124-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>悉尼房地产市场现状报告称，预计未来六个月内，悉尼部分郊区将迎来最大的增长回报。（简沐/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月16日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">（大纪元记者朱丽娅悉尼编译报导）贷款机构</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Shore Financial</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">最新发布的悉尼房地产市场现状报告称，预计未来六个月内，悉尼部分郊区将迎来最大的增长回报。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">据澳洲房地产投资网消息，该报告每季度会对悉尼</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">600</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">多个郊区的数据进行分析，根据房产中位价将它们分为五个五分位组，并预计在未来六个月内上涨最高的区域。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">这五个五分位组为：</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">第一组：悉尼腹地（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Heartland Sydney</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">）；</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">第二组：悉尼普郊区（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Suburban Sydney</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">）；</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">第三组：悉尼上升区（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Rising Sydney</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">）；</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">第四组：悉尼专业人群聚集区（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Professional Sydney</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">）；</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">第五组：悉尼富裕区（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Affluent Sydney</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">）。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Shore Financial</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">机构总裁</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Theo Chambers</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">表示，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">月份发布的报告预测相当准确。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">“在悉尼腹地、普通城区和上升区这三个价格较低的组别中，预计表现非常好，正如我们预期的那样，坚实的需求部分得益于</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">月和</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">5</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">月的降息。”</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Chambers</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">说，“但悉尼专业人群聚集区和富裕区这两个高价组别的表现低于预期，这可能是由于可负担性压力所致。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Chambers</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">表示，尽管一些分析师预计未来六个月大悉尼地区将出现非常强劲的增长，但</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Shore Financial</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">预测的是划分更细的增长格局。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Chambers</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">说：“在某些区，未来半年增长率极有可能超过</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">5%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">，这将使它们有望实现两位数的年增长率。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">他还表示，许多此类城区的房源库存水平较低，买家必须积极竞价才能成功购房。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">而悉尼其它一些地区预测房价将停滞，某些情况下甚至回落，原因是可负担性问题以及较高水平的供应。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Chambers</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">表示，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">2025</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">年进一步的降息将继续推高房价，但不会重现</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">2021</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">年的疯狂上涨。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">“如果</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">2025</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">年利率进一步下降，买家的信心和借贷能力将增强，这会带来需求的增加。”</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Chambers</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">说。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">不过，他也强调“买家并没有无限的现金可花”，并引用澳洲统计局的数据称，全国工资上涨了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">14.4%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">，但物价上涨了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">19.2%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">，这实际上相当于通胀调整后的“减薪”。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">Chambers</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: 'Noto Serif SC','serif';">总结道，“换句话说，悉尼买家正面临可负担性限制，这将形成短期内的增长上限。”</span></p>
