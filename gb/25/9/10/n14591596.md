<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>阿里山森林铁道将推出蒸汽专列桧木列车</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591597-794070-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为阿里山森林铁道蒸汽专列桧木列车。（嘉义县政府提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月10日讯】（大纪元记者蔡上海台湾嘉义报导）嘉义县文化观光局宣布，10月16日将推出阿里山森林铁道蒸汽专列桧木列车。今年最后一场由百年蒸汽火车头搭载桧木列车的限定专列，将带领游客穿越历史悠久的阿里山森林铁道，重温百年蒸汽火车的传奇魅力，并借此展示嘉义独特的文化底蕴与自然风光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14591598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14591598" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591598-794071.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14591598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591598-794071-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14591598" class="wp-caption-text">图为阿里山国家森林游乐区。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14591599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14591599" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591599-794072.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14591599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591599-794072-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14591599" class="wp-caption-text">图为奋起湖老街。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14591600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14591600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591600-794073.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14591600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591600-794073-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14591600" class="wp-caption-text">图为民雄日式招待所文创园区。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义是全台唯一拥有营运中林业铁道文化资产的县市。阿里山森林铁道不仅是一条运输路线，更是一段承载历史与情感的文化记忆。这条百年铁道从嘉义平原蜿蜒而上，直达壮丽的阿里山山脉，沿途风光如画，深刻书写了人文历史的篇章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14591601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14591601" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591601-794074.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14591601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591601-794074-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14591601" class="wp-caption-text">图为向禾休闲渔场。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14591602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14591602" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591602-794075.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14591602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591602-794075-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14591602" class="wp-caption-text">图为欣欣水泥森活园。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14591603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14591603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591603-794076.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14591603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591603-794076-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14591603" class="wp-caption-text">图为咩咩上树萌宠乐园。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义县文化观光局携手林铁处与雄狮旅游，推出秋季限定的蒸汽火车游程，特别为亲子家庭设计多样化景点，以满足全家人的需求。各团次将提供不同的亲子游程，让游客在阿里山森林铁道的蒸汽列车上，享受充满趣味与教育意义的旅程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14591605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14591605" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591605-794078.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14591605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591605-794078-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14591605" class="wp-caption-text">图为嘉义县科学教育中心-太空教育馆。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14591606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14591606" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591606-794079.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14591606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14591606-794079-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14591606" class="wp-caption-text">图为欧乐沃筑梦城堡。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亲子游程推荐：</p>
<p>?阿里山国家森林游乐区：漫游于步道之间，呼吸森林的芬多精，享受大自然的疗愈。</p>
<p>?欣欣水泥森活园：动手制作水泥多肉盆，体验创意与手作的乐趣。</p>
<p>?向禾休闲渔场：亲手摸蛤蛎、烤蚵、划船、钓鱼或捞海菜，吃喝玩乐一次满足。</p>
<p>?民雄日式招待所文创园区：挑战飞盘高尔夫，创造难忘的亲子回忆。</p>
<p>?嘉义县科学教育中心-太空教育馆：在3D剧场中观赏太空知识，让孩子探索宇宙的奥秘。</p>
<p>?咩咩上树萌宠乐园：与超萌动物互动，享受欢乐亲子时光。</p>
<p>?欧乐沃筑梦城堡：拥有台版迪士尼美誉的乐园，拍照打卡，捕捉每一个快乐瞬间。</p>
<p>?邹族逐鹿文创园区：亲近梅花鹿，了解动物的世界，培养孩子的好奇心与爱护之心。</p>
<p>?奋起湖老街：品味怀旧的奋起湖铁路便当，细细品味岁月流淌的美好。</p>
<p>家长们可根据需求，选择最适合自己家庭的团次，与孩子一起在蒸汽火车的旅程中创造珍贵的回忆。</p>
<p>文观局表示，先前7月18日推出的“栩悦号”及8月25日“福森号”蒸汽专列均大获好评，现场吸引许多铁道迷与家庭旅客参与。随着蒸汽鸣笛声响起，大家纷纷拍照留念，许多人直呼能搭乘蒸汽火车是一个难得又兴奋的体验，整段旅程充满难忘的瞬间。</p>
<p>文观局诚挚邀请全国游客把握最后机会，安排2日、3日或4日游，透过上网订购(https://reurl.cc/nYXLYD)搭乘蒸汽专列桧木列车，感受阿里山铁道的独“嘉”魅力。欲了解更多亲子游程推荐，可至“嘉义县亲子旅”官网查询(https://familytour.chiayi.travel/)。◇</p>
