<h6>2025 MINI Countryman SE ALL4。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月13日讯】今年，MINI推出全新设计的Countryman，并同时推出全电动版，称之为MINI Countryman SE ALL4，其中的“S”是与汽油版的“S”一样代表Sport，而“E”就是Electric的意思。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d1f01xri6nyv0g.cloudfront.net/M2kFcFPWQv4w"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Gvs19ZOB_4A?si=MiR5nVNwlFMm-Y0m" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>MINI Countryman是小型SUV尺寸，但是在MINI品牌中已是最巨大的了，而新一代再增大一点，新款车型长度增加5&#8243;，高度增加3&#8243;，离地间隙约为8&#8243;。车身大了，Countryman的外型仍然保留了各种MINI的特点，短前悬、短引擎盖与长轴距设计展现敏捷姿态，简洁的外形设计提升空气力学效率。重新设计的八角形前格栅与独特的LED头灯令人更容易辨认，而车尾灯组采用英国国旗的“米”分割到左右尾灯，但与Cooper的三角形不同，Countryman是直立长方形。在C柱后方的饰板有“ALL4”，以显示其四驱的能力。搭配18&#8243;六幅式MINI合金轮圈，配以225／55R18轮胎，增添运动气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593609-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593609-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593609" class="wp-caption-text">2025 MINI Countryman SE ALL4。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>延长续航里程并突显现代感。可选配三种前后灯光签名，突显不同车身设计细节。侧面看去，略微弯曲的车顶无缝衔接至全新C柱，搭配环车黑色饰条与MINI特色轮圈，增添运动气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593579-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593579-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593579" class="wp-caption-text">双电动马达。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MINI Countryman SE配双电动马达，可输出最大马力308 hp，扭矩达364 lb-ft，0-60 mph加速仅需5.4秒，最高时速112 mph。强劲的加速特性与灵敏的操控，延续MINI标志性的Go-Kart车驾驶感。搭载64.7 kWh锂电池，支援最高130 kW的直流快充，仅需29分钟即可从10%充电至80%，续航里程约为212 miles。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593580-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_29.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593580-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593580" class="wp-caption-text">内装采用运动风格。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内装方面，Favoured Trim配备舒适的运动座椅，以及全新设计的运动方向盘，采用布条取代传统六点钟位置的辐条，提升质感。肩部与肘部空间增加近1&#8243;，提升驾驶者与乘客的舒适度；更宽敞的内部空间与储物空间，是MINI Countryman的最大卖点，后座折叠后，行李厢容积可达51 cu-ft，轻松容纳大型物品。前台采用双色布料设计，延伸至车门内板，内藏LED灯，可营造温暖感。中间的9.4&#8243;圆形OLED显示萤幕，配备高质感玻璃表面，内里运行第9代MINI OS系统，可透过触控或语音控制所有资讯娱乐与辅助系统。MINI体验模式提供不同模式，包括Trail、Personal、Core、Timeless、Go-Kart、Vivid、Green和Balance，除了萤幕的设计不同，也会以气氛灯发出不同颜色，令体验也不同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593581-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593581-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593581" class="wp-caption-text">9.4&#8243;圆形OLED显示萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14593582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593582-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593582-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593582" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板由HUD代替。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车速显示会在中控台圆形萤幕上显示，所以MINI Countryman删除了仪表板，只以可升降的HUD代替。选配的全景玻璃车顶为车内增添光线，营造明亮舒适的氛围。电动尾闸是这一代Countryman的亮点之一，它既可以透过遥控车匙开启，也可在车内按键打开，最特别的是它也具有“踢脚”开门功能，只要带着车匙，走近尾闸，用脚在车尾底部（近中间位置）踢一下，感应器便会开启尾闸，也可以同样方法关门，但关门前更会有声响提示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593583-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_21.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593583-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593583" class="wp-caption-text">启动键、变速杆等都在中控台中部。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14593584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593584" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593584-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_24.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593584-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593584" class="wp-caption-text">杯座后的小储物箱。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MINI Countryman SE配备创新驾驶辅助系统，首次提供选配的Driving Assistant Professional，支援高速公路上的Level 2半自动驾驶。该系统能识别变换车道所需的交通间隙，并将车辆调整至最佳速度以完成辅助切线，提升驾驶便利性与安全性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593585" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593585-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593585-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593585" class="wp-caption-text">运动座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MINI Countryman SE 结合全电动动力、宽敞空间与个性化设计，适合家庭使用并延续MINI的Go-Kart驾驶乐趣。其现代化外观、先进内装科技与高效能电池续航，搭配创新驾驶辅助系统，为跨界电动休旅车市场树立新标竿。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593600" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593600-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593600-2025_MINI_Countryman_EV_11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593600" class="wp-caption-text">充电端口分为AC及DC。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
