<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>摄影家王建中捕捉屏东溪清晨光影</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604245-798171-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黎明初光，屏东溪水如绢般倾泻而下，拦沙坝在晨曦里泛着柔和光影。（王建中提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）9月28日清晨的屏东溪畔，拦沙坝水声轰鸣，从远方传来阵阵激流。摄影家王建中带着相机，守候在河岸边。他说，这个地点交通不算困难，“这个点交通算不错很好找，只有快到达时小路很多，要特别注意一下。”在清晨五点多的时刻，天色逐渐转亮，水流在长曝下像丝绸般延展，映照着粉紫的天光。王建中解释，因为这里的水量大又急，传统的长曝手法并不适合，“以前的慢瀑光三秒以上的方法反而不适用，控制在一秒内会让水流的流动感明显，不会太模糊。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604246" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604246-798172.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604246-798172-450x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604246" class="wp-caption-text">水势奔腾，与远方的铁桥剪影交织成壮阔的画面。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604247" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604247-798173.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604247-798173-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604247" class="wp-caption-text">天边微曦渐亮，溪水翻涌，映照出紫红色的晨色。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>光线的变化从天边微光到日出的过程，天空颜色不断更替。先是淡淡的粉红，随后转为蓝紫，再到阳光出现时，水面染上金黄与橘红。远方的铁桥剪影静静伫立，成为画面中的背景线条，衬托着激流的奔放。王建中说，秋季时太阳正好从北大武山的山头升起，这样的时机一年仅有几个月能遇见。“今天刚好拍到精彩的画面。”他语气带着欣喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604248" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604248-798174.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604248-798174-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604248" class="wp-caption-text">拦沙坝的线条延伸向远方，与桥梁、山峦相互呼应。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604249" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604249-798175.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604249" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604249-798175-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604249" class="wp-caption-text">摄影者伫立岸边，专注捕捉清晨水流的韵律。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影与等待在拦沙坝旁，常能见到摄影者架起脚架，等待最佳光影。王建中认为，与其说是拍照，不如说是和时间对话。他形容清晨站在溪畔，水汽混合著空气的清新，让人感觉整个人都被冲刷一遍。“摄影需要等待，等光线、等水位、等天空的颜色都到位，那才是决定按下快门的时刻。”他强调耐心的重要，也分享了自己在水边的体会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604250" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604250-798176.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604250-798176-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604250" class="wp-caption-text">长曝下的溪水，如丝带般流畅，衬托出晨空的静谧。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604251" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604251-798177.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604251-798177-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604251" class="wp-caption-text">粉橘色的天光倒映水面，流动与静止交织。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>人工与自然之间屏东溪拦沙坝虽是人工设施，但因水势冲击形成层层落差，看起来像天然瀑布。人工的线条与自然的力量交会，构成了摄影人喜爱的景象。王建中说，这样的场景让他思考工程与自然的平衡，“它既不是纯粹的自然，也不是完全的人造，而是在两者之间找到一个相处的方式。”对他而言，这里的照片不只是风景影像，也是人与环境互动的痕迹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604252" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604252-798178.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604252-798178-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604252" class="wp-caption-text">朝阳探出山峦，第一缕金光洒落屏东溪面。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604253" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604253-798179.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604253-798179-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604253" class="wp-caption-text">旭日升起，水波闪耀金光，溪面沸腾成一片耀眼的晨曲。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曙光的力量当太阳终于从山头探出，水面闪烁着强烈光芒，水流被映照成熔金般的色泽。这股力量不只来自大自然，也来自摄影者守候的耐心。王建中说，他拍的不只是画面，而是一段时间的记录，“每天的日出都是新的开始，而照片只是替它留下见证。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604254" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604254-798180.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604254-798180-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604254" class="wp-caption-text">初升的阳光照耀在拦沙坝上，奔腾的溪水泛起金黄光泽，宛如熔金流淌。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604255" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604255-798181.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604255-798181-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604255" class="wp-caption-text">溪水在朝阳映照下化作金色浪潮，涌动不息，与远方铁桥剪影交织成壮丽画面。（王建中提供）</figcaption></figure>
