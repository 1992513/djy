<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>赞丹夜晚的歇脚处 再访熟悉的积木屋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604171-798150-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Nina  Chen相隔五年，再度造访荷兰赞丹。（Nina  Chen提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬荷兰报导）2025年9月25日傍晚六点多，天空被染上了橘粉色的晚霞。来自台湾的空拍达人Nina Chen拎着行李走进了赞丹（Zaandam），这座以运河与独特建筑闻名的小城。这里对她而言并不陌生，2019年她曾与小儿子住在著名的“积木屋”旅馆，如今再次踏入，回忆与当下交叠，仿佛时间在此停留。旅人往往在“抵达”的瞬间，才真切感受到一整天的重量。Nina笑说，这一天光是搬行李就像完成一场小型耐力赛，好几次拖着行李在城里穿梭。直到与飞狗巴士几乎同一时间抵达住宿处，那股“终于到达”的踏实，才让她放下肩上的负担。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604172" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604172-798151.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604172-798151-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604172" class="wp-caption-text">2025年9月25日傍晚六点多，天空被染上了橘粉色的晚霞。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604173" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604173-798152.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604173-798152-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604173" class="wp-caption-text">来自台湾的空拍达人Nina Chen拎着行李走进了赞丹（Zaandam），这座以运河与独特建筑闻名的小城。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚餐的惊喜与挑战在歇脚之前，最重要的是填饱肚子。Nina走进了一间餐厅，原以为能在河港城市品尝鱼料理，却发现菜单上并没有海鲜。于是她点了一份猪肋排，没想到端上桌的瞬间，分量之大让她愣了一下。厚实的肋排铺满整个大盘，旁边还附上沙拉、酱料与一大份薯条。“这份量太惊人了！”她忍不住笑着说。虽然味道鲜美，但吃到一半便已感到饱足。薯条则几乎原封不动，还完整地待在碗里。旅行的餐桌上，有时食物不只是饱腹，更是一种记忆的标记，这一夜的惊讶与饱足，成了她在赞丹的第一个印象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604174" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604174-798153.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604174-798153-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604174" class="wp-caption-text">2019年她曾与小儿子住在著名的“积木屋”旅馆，如今再次踏入，回忆与当下交叠。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604175" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604175-798154.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604175-798154-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604175" class="wp-caption-text">Nina走进了一间餐厅，原以为能在河港城市品尝鱼料理，却发现菜单上并没有海鲜。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>城市夜色的陪伴饱餐后，Nina沿着运河散步。夜晚的赞丹多了一份静谧，街灯映在水面，树影随风摇曳。她拍下几座小桥，简单的白色栏杆在黑夜里显得格外清晰。建筑的玻璃窗透出光亮，店家虽已陆续打烊，但仍能看见人们走动的身影。与白天的热闹相比，夜晚的城市步调缓了下来。她慢慢走过每一个转角，经过餐厅、商店，最后停在那栋标志性的“积木屋”旅馆前。夜色下，绿色与蓝色交错的外墙在灯光衬托下更显奇特，倒映在水面，像是一幅拼贴画。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604176" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604176" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604176-798155.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604176" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604176-798155-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604176" class="wp-caption-text">厚实的肋排铺满整个大盘，旁边还附上沙拉、酱料与一大份薯条。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604177" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604177-798156.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604177-798156-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604177" class="wp-caption-text">饱餐后，Nina沿着运河散步，赞丹多了一份静谧。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604178" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604178-798157.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14604178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604178-798157-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604178" class="wp-caption-text">赞丹街景。（Nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>旅途里的小确幸旅行不全然是壮阔的景色与精彩的景点，更多时候，是这些琐碎却鲜明的片段：行李的重量、餐点的惊喜、夜色的静谧。这些小细节堆叠起来，构成了旅程真正的记忆。对Nina而言，这一天的结尾或许不是壮游式的大事件，而是一份“吃不完的猪肋排”、一条“夜里的运河”与一栋“久别重逢的积木屋”。这些画面串连起她的旅途，也让下一段旅程有了更温柔的开场。</p>
