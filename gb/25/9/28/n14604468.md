<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>屏东长治新潭村伯公文化祭热闹登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604469-798279-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>每年的“伯公文化祭”成了凝聚屏东长治乡的重要盛事。（周永田提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾屏东报导）在屏东长治乡新潭村，伯公信仰延续自古代的土地崇拜。村民相信伯公庇佑地方五谷丰收、四境平安，因此每年的“伯公文化祭”成了凝聚乡里的重要盛事。 9 月 28 日下午，村民们再度齐聚一堂，举行踩街嘉年华与一连串文化活动，场面热闹非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604471" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604471-798281.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604471-798281-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604471" class="wp-caption-text">9 月 28 日下午，村民们再度齐聚一堂，举行踩街嘉年华与一连串文化活动，场面热闹非凡。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604470-798280.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604470-798280-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604470" class="wp-caption-text">村民敲锣打鼓，为踩街揭开序幕。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604479" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604479-798289.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604479-798289-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604479" class="wp-caption-text">无论是踩街的欢笑，还是掷筊的紧张时刻，都展现出社区居民共同守护文化的心意。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>踩街嘉年华缤纷登场下午三点半，踩街活动由三座屋福德祠出发，沿途经过长兴路、长治圆环、新生街等地。队伍由各社区、学校与社团组成，打扮创意十足：有人装扮农夫，敲锣打鼓前导；有长辈穿上鲜艳服装挥舞彩旗；也有社区妇女换上和服，手持团扇随队舞动。整条街道瞬间成了嘉年华会场，沿途居民纷纷出门观赏，掌声与笑声不断。踩街结束后，福德祠前广场接续展开艺文表演。客家歌谣、舞蹈、太鼓轮番上阵，让民众感受传统文化的多元风貌。摊位区则聚集客家美食，有粄条、菜包、擂茶等在地风味，香气四溢，吸引许多人排队购买，形成另一股热闹景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604472-798282.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604472-798282-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604472" class="wp-caption-text">下午三点半，踩街活动由三座屋福德祠出发，沿途经过长兴路、长治圆环、新生街等地。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604473-798283.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604473-798283-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604473" class="wp-caption-text">队伍由各社区、学校与社团组成，打扮创意十足。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604474-798284.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604474-798284-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604474" class="wp-caption-text">也有社区妇女换上和服，手持团扇随队舞动。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>伯公殿试掷筊添喜气最受瞩目的活动莫过于“伯公殿试掷筊”。参与者必须凭运气掷出圣筊，依结果可获得不同奖金，状元奖高达一万元。大家围在祠前欢呼加油，每次掷筊都牵动全场情绪，不仅添增娱乐性，更寓意祈求来年顺利平安。会场一角还摆设巨大的“平安龟”，象征长寿与福气，吸引村民上香祈福。此外，伯公创意三轮车展示更是一大亮点，居民用心装饰车身，将伯公神像与吉祥图腾安放其上，边踩边走，将祝福送往大街小巷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604475" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604475-798285.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604475-798285-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604475" class="wp-caption-text">非常吸睛的伯公超大型公仔。（周永田提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>传统文化的温度活动虽以嘉年华形式呈现，但其背后仍传递着对土地的感恩。无论是踩街的欢笑，还是掷筊的紧张时刻，都展现出社区居民共同守护文化的心意。正如村民所说：“有伯公保佑，大家就安心。”这份朴实的信念，正是客庄文化最真挚的力量。整场伯公文化祭，不仅是一场热闹的活动，更是客家人与土地连结的体现。透过踩街、艺文、祈福与分享，美好与祝福也在村落间流动。</p>
