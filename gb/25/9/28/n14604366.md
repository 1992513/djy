<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北港扶轮社携手社大乐团  板头车站音乐会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604367-798252-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>海线社区大学学员萨克斯风吹奏，增添佳节氛围。（吴昭志提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾嘉义报导）9 月 27 日，在嘉义新港乡板头车站，音乐声伴随着微凉秋意，为这个小镇带来一场充满温度的中秋聚会。这场活动由北港扶轮社与海线社大中阶班共同举办，不仅是音乐的交流，更是一场人与人之间真诚互动的相聚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604372-798258.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604372-798258-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604372" class="wp-caption-text">北港扶轮社社长陈孟意特别带领社员参与，一同在板头车站与居民共度佳节。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604368" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604368" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604368-798253.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604368-798253-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604368" class="wp-caption-text">这场活动由北港扶轮社与海线社大中阶班共同举办。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>音乐开场 社区齐聚傍晚时分，副班长王雅玲带领海线社大中阶班的学员登上舞台。整齐的萨克斯风合奏，让车站的广场充满悠扬旋律。观众席上，社区居民与亲子们早已就座，听着熟悉的乐曲，脸上浮现微笑。这场演出是社大第 26 场巡演，却依然保持着新鲜与热情。乐手们专注的神情，带来的不只是音乐表演，更是一份对社区的承诺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604371" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604371-798257.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604371-798257-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604371" class="wp-caption-text">这场演出是社大第 26 场巡演，却依然保持着新鲜与热情。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604370-798256.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604370-798256-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1066"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604370" class="wp-caption-text">观众席上，听着熟悉的乐曲，脸上浮现微笑。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>扶轮社长带领 欢度佳节北港扶轮社社长陈孟意特别带领社员参与，一同在板头车站与居民共度佳节。不同于制式的典礼，社员们穿着轻松，和民众并肩而坐，随着音乐拍手或点头，气氛自然融洽。“中秋是一个团圆的日子，我们希望借着音乐，和大家拉近彼此的心。”陈社长说。这份初心，让活动不仅仅是一场演出，而是一次社区彼此照顾的展现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604378-798264.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604378-798264-600x897.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="897"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604378" class="wp-caption-text">板头车站的铁道旁，留下的是一张张合影与一段段难忘的旋律。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604377-798263.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604377-798263-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604377" class="wp-caption-text">音乐之外，彼此的陪伴与支持，才是这个社团最深的底蕴。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>助教真情流露 感动全场活动进行到一半时，助教王淑娟的一段突如其来的话，打破了音乐的节奏。她语气真挚，感谢老师与团队的辛劳，并提到一路走来的点滴。台上的吴昭志老师听着，眼眶泛红，现场不少人也跟着鼻酸，气氛相当感人。这份不经意的真情流露，比任何曲目都更打动人心。音乐之外，彼此的陪伴与支持，才是这个社团最深的底蕴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604379-798265.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604379-798265-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1066"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604379" class="wp-caption-text">王淑娟语气真挚，感谢老师与团队的辛劳，并提到一路走来的点滴。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604376" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604376-798262.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604376-798262-600x934.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="934"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604376" class="wp-caption-text">社区居民与亲子们早已就座。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604373-798259.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604373-798259-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1066"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604373" class="wp-caption-text">教师节前夕，学员们致赠蛋糕，谢谢吴昭志老师细心教导。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>幕后团队 默默付出除了台前的演出，幕后也有许多人默默努力。吴昭志老师带领的萨克斯风工作团队，细心协调乐手、检查器材，确保演出顺利。而主持人洪翠黛则以自然幽默的方式串场，让活动更有节奏，也更贴近社区居民。当晚的灯光不奢华，却足以映照出舞台上每一张专注的脸庞。乐声一曲接着一曲，观众的掌声一次比一次热烈。随着夜色渐深，活动在乐团合奏声中画下句点。板头车站的铁道旁，留下的是一张张合影与一段段难忘的旋律。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604369" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604369-798255.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604369-798255-600x362.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="362"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604369" class="wp-caption-text">整齐的合奏，让车站的广场充满悠扬旋律。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14604374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604374-798260.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14604374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604374-798260-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604374" class="wp-caption-text">北港海线社区大学和北港扶轮社成员，提早庆中秋。（吴昭志提供）</figcaption></figure>
