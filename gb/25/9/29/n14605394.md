<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西雅图海湾人队2比2战平温哥华白浪队</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14608058-8-SoundersDrewWithVancouver-1-s-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西雅图球员庆祝鲁斯纳克打入反超一球。（理查德/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年09月30日讯】（大纪元记者王若晨西雅图编译报导）西雅图海湾人队9月27日在西雅图流明球场（Lumen Fields）与温哥华白浪队以2比2比分战平。</p>
<p>海湾人队比赛开局不顺，亚历克斯．罗尔丹和耶马尔在前20分钟先后因伤退场，迫使海湾人被动调整。上半场双方互有攻守.海湾人队鲁斯奈克送出妙传，但罗斯特罗克的头槌被对方守门员没收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608059" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14608059-8-SoundersDrewWithVancouver-2-s.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14608059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14608059-8-SoundersDrewWithVancouver-2-s-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608059" class="wp-caption-text">海湾人队的克里斯汀·罗尔丹在比赛中与温哥华球员争抢头球。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下半场比赛节奏骤然加快。第52分钟，白浪队库巴斯前场逼迫海湾人队员失误，穆勒断球后直塞，怀特单刀冷静推射破门，为白浪队打破僵局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608060" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14608060-8-SoundersDrewWithVancouver-3-s.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14608060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14608060-8-SoundersDrewWithVancouver-3-s-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608060" class="wp-caption-text">海湾人队替补上场的米努恩古突破温哥华队的防守。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但两分钟后海湾人队鲁斯奈克开出角球，拉根高高跃起头槌攻入赛季首球，将比分拉平。</p>
<p>第55分钟，海湾人队罗斯特罗克突入左路传球，皮球落到鲁斯奈克脚下，他近距离劲射得手，将比分反超。</p>
<p>但第69分钟，白浪队拉博尔达接到角球头槌破门，将比分再度扳平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14608061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14608061" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14608061-8-SoundersDrewWithVancouver-4-s.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14608061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14608061-8-SoundersDrewWithVancouver-4-s-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14608061" class="wp-caption-text">温哥华球员庆祝进球。（理查德/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>补时阶段惊险不断。第97分钟，海湾人队守门员弗雷飞身化解对方的自由球。最终，双方2比2握手言和，各取一分。</p>
<p>此役过后，海湾人30战积45分（12胜9平9负），继续排名西部第五。下周六，西雅图将在主场迎战波特兰伐木者队，这场比赛对西部季后赛席位的争夺至关重要。</p>
