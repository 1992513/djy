<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台版迪士尼—嘉义“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593188-2509120253002378-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff
- 台版迪士尼──台湾嘉义市欧乐沃筑梦城堡。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）

<h6>台版迪士尼──台湾嘉义市欧乐沃筑梦城堡。（摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年09月12日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾采访报导）台湾摄影师宋永泰表示，嘉义县大林镇“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”自8月1日试营运后，成为爆红的新观光景点，被称为“台版迪士尼”，涌进许多人潮，成为热门打卡点。</p>
<p>宋永泰表示，他们日前开车到嘉义县造访“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”，城堡分为三大主题区：“玛雅物语”、“筑梦城堡”及“普罗旺斯小镇”，其中“玛雅物语”是可可的起源地，但并非纯粹的玛雅文化展示，而是以可可工厂为主题，带领游客了解可可巧克力的美好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593235-2509120253112378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593235" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593235-2509120253112378-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593235" class="wp-caption-text">台版迪士尼──台湾嘉义市“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p><center><a title="YouTube video player" loading="lazy" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d34v2ua3ud12rg.cloudfront.net/N5fEFFnXKWYw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ozzLeYzu8c0?si=Q9MgZbyqSETfwuoH" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center>宋永泰说，“筑梦城堡”以粉红色和蓝色为主色调，外观类似迪士尼风格，因此被称为“台版迪士尼”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593195" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593195-2509120253022378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593195" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593195-2509120253022378-600x333.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593195" class="wp-caption-text">台版迪士尼──台湾嘉义市“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>“筑梦城堡”搭配周遭环境营造出童话氛围。宋永泰表示，他们在参观“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”时，巧遇创办人毛荣海及邱郁宜，得知因为疫情的关系，前后历经10年，终于完成儿时的梦想，共同打造出一座梦幻的城堡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593209" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593209-2509120253042378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593209" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593209-2509120253042378-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593209" class="wp-caption-text">台版迪士尼──台湾嘉义市“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>他表示，“普罗旺斯小镇”的地上全部都铺满了砖，看起来很像欧洲的风情。如果你去过意大利、法国，还有很多欧洲国家的地面，就是一块块砖叠起来的马路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593189" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593189-2509120252572378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593189" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593189-2509120252572378.jpg" alt="" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593189" class="wp-caption-text">台版迪士尼──台湾嘉义市“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>宋永泰表示，“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”整体设计动线流畅，园区方便游客参观与拍照，而且建筑雕工精细，适合摄影爱好者细细品味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593233-2509120253062378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593233" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593233-2509120253062378-600x278.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593233" class="wp-caption-text">台版迪士尼──台湾嘉义市“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>他也分享，城堡园区空间紧凑，拍摄动线简洁，适合摄影爱好者利用自然光与角度创造丰富构图。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14593234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14593234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593234-2509120253092378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14593234" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14593234-2509120253092378-600x259.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="259" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14593234" class="wp-caption-text">台版迪士尼──台湾嘉义市“欧乐沃筑梦城堡”。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，他强调，摄影技巧与美学的累积需长时间培养，很多好的照片，在拍摄时需耐心等待合适光线与场景，并利用借位等技巧提升画面层次。</p>
