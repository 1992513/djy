<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金风萧瑟 燥气袭人 秋季护肤8法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604060-shutterstock_767232055-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>秋风清，秋叶黄，时序移换，护肤重点也须相应调整。（Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2025年09月28日讯】（大纪元记者夏瑜编译报导）随着季节由夏转秋，我们的肌肤也相应发生变化。夏日炎热潮湿，皮肤会感觉油腻；秋天凉爽干燥，皮肤会感觉缺水。“佳容计划”网站提供了8个秋季护肤小贴士，让你顺利过渡，享受金秋。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">1. 由内而外补水</span></p>
<p>秋风渐起，气温转凉，肌肤的水分含量也会下降。皮肤干燥，就会分泌过多的皮脂来弥补水分，从而引发痘痘。</p>
<p>这时我们需要由内而外，全面补水：多喝水，多吃富含水分的食物；使用加湿器，涂抹保湿精华液和润肤霜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604063-1_shutterstock_693515320-e1758999125717.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14604063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604063-1_shutterstock_693515320-e1758999125717.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604063" class="wp-caption-text">秋燥需要多补水。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">2. 用厚重面霜取代轻薄保湿霜</span></p>
<p>夏季湿润，保湿霜的选择以“少即是多”为原则。秋季降临，是时候升级到厚重的面霜了，它不仅能补水，还能在肌肤和周围环境之间形成一道屏障。</p>
<p>请选择含有润肤剂和封闭剂成分的产品。润肤剂如矿物油、乳木果油、神经酰胺、脂肪酸等，使肌肤不易干燥粗糙；封闭剂则包括角鲨烯、蜂蜡、羊毛脂、凡士林等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604064" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604064-2_shutterstock_2198833315-e1758999269438.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14604064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604064-2_shutterstock_2198833315-e1758999269438.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604064" class="wp-caption-text">秋季适合厚重面霜。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">3. 注意防晒</span></p>
<p>所谓骄阳似火，尽管秋季日光不如夏季强烈，但仍然需要每天涂抹防晒霜。实际上，此原则四季有效，即使刮风下雪或阴雨绵绵，也是如此。防晒霜可阻止加速皮肤老化的自由基损伤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604065" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604065-3_shutterstock_2361029909-e1758999377643.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14604065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604065-3_shutterstock_2361029909-e1758999377643.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604065" class="wp-caption-text">秋季也不能忽略涂抹防晒霜。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">4. 温和去角质</span></p>
<p>草木摇落，秋燥伤津，外界环境已然肃杀，再使用果酸（AHA）和水杨酸（BHA）这样的强效化学去角质成分对皮肤就会比较刺激了。此时应给皮肤一个喘息之机，专注于保湿和防护。你可以继续每周使用1～2次温和的去角质产品，切忌过度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604066" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604066-4_shutterstock_1694507101-e1758999507719.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14604066" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604066-4_shutterstock_1694507101-e1758999507719.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604066" class="wp-caption-text">秋季去角质不宜过强。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">5. 嘴唇也需呵护</span></p>
<p>时序入秋，许多人首先会遇到的皮肤问题之一就是嘴唇干裂。请在包里随时准备一支润唇膏，随时随地涂抹。许多润唇膏除了滋润功能，还可在唇上形成一层保护膜。别忘了，嘴唇也需要防晒，所以买一支SPF30以上的润唇膏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604067" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604067-5_shutterstock_2527588219-e1758999627924.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14604067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604067-5_shutterstock_2527588219-e1758999627924.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604067" class="wp-caption-text">嘴唇干裂是秋季常见问题，需时时呵护。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">6. 润肤露必不可少</span></p>
<p>护肤并不只是面部，也包括身体。当炎夏转为凉秋之际，是时候入手一瓶润肤露了。在刚刚出浴，皮肤还湿润时，从颈到脚涂抹足量的润肤露，这样有助于锁住水分，防止秋季皮肤干燥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604068" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604068-6_shutterstock_2257947435-e1758999775396.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14604068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604068-6_shutterstock_2257947435-e1758999775396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604068" class="wp-caption-text">秋季皮肤干燥，全身涂抹润肤露必不可少。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">7. 使用保湿洁面乳</span></p>
<p>很多人喜欢泡沫丰富的洁面乳，彻底清除积聚的污垢、油脂，甚至死皮细胞。但秋凉时节，留下一些油脂有助于保持皮肤湿润。最好选择一款温和又保湿的洁面乳，比如乳霜洁面乳或油性洁面乳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604069" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604069-7_shutterstock_2312577887-e1758999910229.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14604069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604069-7_shutterstock_2312577887-e1758999910229.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604069" class="wp-caption-text">秋季洁面乳宜选择保湿型。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 文鼎粗黑; font-size: large;">8. 推荐使用面部精油</span></p>
<p>在干燥的月份，使用面部精油是恢复肌肤水分平衡的好方法。一些非常适合秋季使用的面部精油包括白芒花油、玫瑰果油、角鲨烷油和沙棘油。这些精油富含抗氧化剂、抗炎成分和保湿成分。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14604070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14604070" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604070-8_shutterstock_1194953227-e1759000006255.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14604070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14604070-8_shutterstock_1194953227-e1759000006255.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14604070" class="wp-caption-text">玫瑰果油可恢复肌肤水分平衡。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
