<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】电光逸动 尊贵新驱 2025 Cadillac Optiq Luxury 2</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603725-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_01-600x400.jpg" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月27日讯】2025 Cadillac Optiq作为Cadillac纯电家族的入门级SUV，以年轻化设计和科技感内装重新定义豪华紧凑型电动SUV。车身尺寸为190×75.3（84含后视镜）×64.7英寸，轴距116英寸，相比XT5更短但轴距更长，视觉修长低趴。前脸采用黑水晶格栅，嵌入114颗LED灯组，搭配垂直LED头灯，迎宾灯效惊艳。双色车身、隐藏式门把手、全景天窗强化现代感，顶级21英寸轮圈增添运动气息。尾部扰流板和低趴后窗设计时尚，但牺牲部分收纳空间。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d2aqxvnyguf0uq.cloudfront.net/A4df"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/62FOKE49-po?si=CmK5HfXVWD30AbNP" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>内装是核心亮点。33英寸9K曲面LED显示器整合仪表板和多媒体，操作流畅，视觉震撼。内装选用回收纺织品、金属饰条和碳纤维风格面板，环保与豪华兼顾。座椅通风加热、后座加热及126色气氛灯提升舒适度。AKG 19个扬声器音响支援杜比全景声，音效同级顶尖，高音通透，低音沉稳，不逊于Volvo的B&amp;O。后排腿部空间37.8英寸，行李厢26至57立方英尺，实用性优异。但缺Apple CarPlay和Android Auto，控制介面略复杂，需适应。中控保留A/C等实体按键，回馈优异。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14603689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603689-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_36.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14603689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603689-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_36-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603689" class="wp-caption-text">内装是核心亮点。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Optiq静态表现以大胆设计和科技感取胜，向年轻消费者传递前卫豪华的讯号，但操作直觉性稍显不足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14603690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603690" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603690-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_22.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14603690" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603690-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_22-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603690" class="wp-caption-text">33英寸9K曲面LED显示器整合仪表板和多媒体。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14603692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603692-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14603692" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603692-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_26-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603692" class="wp-caption-text">33英寸9K曲面LED显示器整合仪表板和多媒体。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Optiq北美标配双电机四驱，85 kWh电池，输出300匹马力、354磅英尺扭矩，EPA续航302英里（约486公里）。驾驶体验均衡，前置永磁同步马达与后置感应马达组合实现高效动力分配。前轮驱动模式节能，四轮驱动模式下0-60英里／小时为5.7秒，满足日常需求。运动、巡航、雪地及自订模式可调节转向和加速。前麦弗逊，后5连杆以及Passive Plus双阀减震器兼顾舒适与运动，过弯稳定，侧倾控制得当。转向回馈清晰，力道适中。Super Cruise支援40万英里预映射道路的Hands-free驾驶，长途巡航表现优异。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14603694" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603694" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603694-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_16.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14603694" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603694-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_16-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603694" class="wp-caption-text">配双电机四驱。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但再生刹车略生硬，单踏板模式（可调normal/high或关闭）需适应。快充性能出色，85 kWh电池10分钟补79英里续航（150 kW）。Optiq动态表现沉稳且灵动，适合追求科技与驾驶乐趣的消费者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14603695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603695-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14603695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603695-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_08-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603695" class="wp-caption-text">85 kWh电池10分钟补79英里续航。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2025 Cadillac Optiq凭借前卫设计、先进科技和均衡性能，在紧凑型豪华电动SUV市场站稳脚跟。静态方面，33英寸巨幕、豪华内装和宽敞空间吸引年轻消费者。动态方面，双马达四驱和Super Cruise提供出色性能与智慧化体验，尽管再生刹车和控制逻辑有优化空间。起售价为52,895美元，性价比优于Audi Q4 e-tron和Genesis GV60。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14603697" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603697" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603697-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14603697" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603697-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603697" class="wp-caption-text">LED大灯设计前卫。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14603698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14603698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603698-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14603698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14603698-2025_Cadillac_Optiq_Luxury2_04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14603698" class="wp-caption-text">黑水晶格栅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Optiq将Cadillac豪华基因与电动化年轻趋势结合，目标指向新一代消费者。但缺少手机互联功能和操作复杂性可能令人犹豫。环保材质质感见仁见智，未能完全传递高端感，Cadillac电动化之路仍需努力。Optiq不是最令人惊艳的电动 SUV，但以均衡表现和亲民价格成为市场先锋。2026年Optiq-V或进一步提升吸引力。对于追求豪华、科技与驾驶乐趣的消费者，Optiq值得一试。◇</p>
