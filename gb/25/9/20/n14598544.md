<h6>2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe Willys。（李奥／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2025年09月20日讯】8月盛夏也是暑假快将结束，学生也准备新学年的开始，这次借着带孩子回寄宿学校之时，租了一辆2025年Jeep Wrangler 4xe Willys，一尝油电越野车的滋味。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="YouTube video player" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d1qri8o0k49d18.cloudfront.net/H3dBAFI67CUp"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Rzhylb6y600?si=UhzDeO0MTdoI5QAK" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>Jeep为Wrangler推出不少版本，其中油电版的Wrangler 4xe也有多款，包括Sport、Willys、Sahara、Rubicon、Rubicon X及Backcountry多款，这次租的算是入门级的Willys，仍是使用2018年推出的第四代车架。鲜红色的车身，就像艳阳一般耀眼，在车群中也很特出，再加上“高人一等”的离地距，在野外是有实际需要，但是在市区只会令人注目。强悍而经典的外型设计，延续了Wrangler的传统。这辆Wrangler配备了17&#8243;越野轮胎，总直径达到33&#8243;，并在车尾背上一个同等尺寸的后备胎，份外粗犷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598547" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598547-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_06-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598547-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_06-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598547" class="wp-caption-text">2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe Willys。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Wrangler 4xe配备了2升涡轮增压引擎配双电动马达，引擎马力输出270 hp及295 lb-ft扭矩，而与马达的总马力为375 hp及470 lb-ft扭矩，装有96组400V锂电池，总电量为17 kWh，充满电后可纯电力行走约21英里 ，搭配了8速自动变速箱，所以在市区行走，也是非常方便，可用作平日代步之用。虽然Wrangler 4xe是可充电油电车，但却只配J1772插口，并没配高速DC充电口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598591-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598591-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_19-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598591" class="wp-caption-text">车侧的J1772插口。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于Wrangler配备越野轮胎，在高速公路上开车时的路噪声很大，声音大的有时候还以为掉了什么东西，而且在高速驾驶也会有飘忽不定的感觉，转弯时也要稍早作反应。当进入乡郊道路时，凹凸不平的道路特显了Wrangler的优点，离地距为10.9&#8243;也容易通过障碍。厚实的越野轮胎也吸收了不少震荡，其余由恳挂系统消化，再传入车厢已经相当轻微。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598592" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598592-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_09-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598592-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_09-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598592" class="wp-caption-text">离地距为10.9”。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14598593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598593" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598593-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598593-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_13-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598593" class="wp-caption-text">后行李舱门采用横开式。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>内装设计上，Wrangler已是极为简洁，双圆仪表板配中间小萤幕，是传统的设计。中控台顶部的12.3&#8243;触控式萤幕，运行Uconnect 5系统，可无线连接Apple Carplay，用手机地图作为导航最方便不过了。另外，萤幕也可用作倒车萤幕及可显示耗油量等。中控台下方是各式按键，Axle Locker及Off-Road+按键也设在中控台底部，令中控台更为挤拥。先进的音响控制、导航系统、蓝牙等都全部使用触控萤幕，只剩下空调、吹风位置等才用实体键。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598595" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598595-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_36.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598595-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_36-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598595" class="wp-caption-text">简洁内装设计。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但是，又为什么要将车窗开关设在中控台呢？因为Wrangler的其中一个特点是可以变身，由一辆满身装甲的越野车，变身成为一辆四面通空透风的越野车。车门既然是可以拿下来的，所以需要把车门弄得简单一些，所以尽量把其他功能移到其他地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598596" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598596-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_27.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598596-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_27-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598596" class="wp-caption-text">车窗开关设在中控台。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Wrangler的车顶由塑料制造，前座车顶分为左右两部分，并可独立取下；而后半部分车顶则需整个取下，车顶拿下来后，便剩下防滖架以保护乘客。安全气囊及车灯是装在车内的防滚架上。四个车门也可取下。此外，前挡风玻璃更可向前方放下，再把前座上方的防滚架拆除，那么前方的180度都没有遮掩了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598601" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598601-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_24.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598601-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_24-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598601" class="wp-caption-text">传统仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14598603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598603" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598603-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_26.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598603-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_26-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598603" class="wp-caption-text">12.3&#8243;触控式萤幕。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Jeep Wrangler绝对是一个价格比很好的越野车选择，但为了日常使用更节省，加了PHEV的功能，能以纯电力行走20多英里也够不少人上下班（在上班时再充电），但对于开长途就用处不大，只是Hybrid功能有助减低耗油，在整个旅程综合耗油为18 mpg。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14598604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14598604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598604-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_28.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14598604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2025/09/id14598604-2025_Jeep_Wrangler_4xe_28-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14598604" class="wp-caption-text">变速杆。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
