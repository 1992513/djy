<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】自制苹果果丹皮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105416-b0ce29bed84d97921dd4939e6047dcaa-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>使用家庭烤箱就能自制苹果果丹皮。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>果丹皮是中国流传甚广的美味小吃，正值美东地区苹果丰收时节，今天用农场的新鲜苹果和梨自制了美味的果丹皮。</p>
<h2>食材准备：</h2>
<p>混合品种的苹果、梨，数量如下图，正好制作一整片17X11英寸（Half Sheet）的果丹皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14105418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105418" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105418-a0d1ccf4daf014b258c027bd12cd1c37.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14105418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105418-a0d1ccf4daf014b258c027bd12cd1c37-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105418" class="wp-caption-text">红苹果较甜，所以多用了几个；富有胶质的青苹果1个；黄苹果和沙梨增加风味层次。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>厨具准备：</h2>
<p>Instant pot（快煮电压力锅），容量3夸脱<br />
陶瓷不沾锅（忌用铁锅），容量3夸脱<br />
17X11英寸浅烤盘<br />
烘焙纸，耐受425华氏度高温<br />
烘焙硅胶刮板</p>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1. 水果洗净、去皮，切成四瓣后削去果籽、果蒂。</p>
<p>2. 水果加入Instant pot后启动煮饭功能。这里没有加水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14105417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105417" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105417-beb220b0ed30b6b2fd4885ab4745ecda.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14105417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105417-beb220b0ed30b6b2fd4885ab4745ecda-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105417" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14105415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105415" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105415-6db74d3725c77de86c3ffdfdc79eefcc.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14105415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105415-6db74d3725c77de86c3ffdfdc79eefcc-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105415" class="wp-caption-text">煮好的果肉。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 水果倒入不沾锅，在明火灶上用中小火慢煮。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14105400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105400" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105400-2222041fc9ea23beae688647ed1f1cff.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14105400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105400-2222041fc9ea23beae688647ed1f1cff-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105400" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 直到形成不冒气泡的粘稠果胶。时长约1小时。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14105404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105404-e699f2925cf350c86fc3d6fa576ccad5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14105404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105404-e699f2925cf350c86fc3d6fa576ccad5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105404" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 烤箱预热200华氏度，开启热风对流功能。</p>
<p>6. 把果泥倒入铺有烘焙纸的烤盘，用硅胶刮板整平表面。果泥厚度约为0.7毫米。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14105403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105403-80af090a8754867e9f36e0d284cb12a6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14105403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105403-80af090a8754867e9f36e0d284cb12a6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105403" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 烤盘放在烤箱的中层烤架，烤1小时。</p>
<p>8. 时间到了，若果丹皮能轻松从烘焙纸上撕下来，即可出炉。觉得不够干，可将温度调低至120华氏度再烤1小时。</p>
<p>9. 想要更干、耐嚼的口感，可低温多烤30分钟至1小时。</p>
<p>10. 出炉的果丹皮脱离烘焙纸即刻放在网格架上冷却。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14105402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105402" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105402-fb3d98f0c9c4c5570d874a4aeb30d961.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14105402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105402-fb3d98f0c9c4c5570d874a4aeb30d961-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105402" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14105401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14105401" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105401-4e0716f9e8a66e9907533883814c2b7f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14105401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14105401-4e0716f9e8a66e9907533883814c2b7f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14105401" class="wp-caption-text">果丹皮放入梅森罐密封保存，最好一周内吃完。长期保存需要放入冰箱冷冻。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>提醒：</strong>用明火收汁时，如果没时间守在灶台旁边，可以缩短烹煮时间，直接把果泥倒入烤盘，并延长低温烘烤时间。◇</p>
