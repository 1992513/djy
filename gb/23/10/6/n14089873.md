<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全球宝石商汇聚10月27-29日多伦多宝石展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089925-9c092b83cd2a7b2225d55a98dc582673-600x400.png" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月07日讯】多伦多宝石博览会（Gem Expo）将于10月27日至29日在多伦多凯悦酒店举行。届时，来自世界各地的宝石商将汇集一堂，为您展示一系列来自全球各个角落的令人惊叹的宝石和矿物。</p>

<p>展会上，来自世界各地的近 40 家供应商，将展示蕴含当地丰富历史和文化特色的宝藏，种类有用于收藏的原始宝石和矿物，治疗作用的水晶，家具装饰品，以及在珠宝店难以找到的纯手工珠宝成品。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/10/6/n14089873.md#1/fb2c9043e14ebb4a75b075982e055272" rel="attachment wp-att-14089896"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14089896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089896-fb2c9043e14ebb4a75b075982e055272-450x214.png" alt="" width="616" b="293" /></a></p>
<p>摩洛哥的亚硒酸盐水晶石、阿富汗的锂紫玉宝石、多明尼加的利玛石，以及斯里兰卡的红宝石，都将亮相多伦多宝石展，让你大开眼界。展会<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">也将展出加拿大自产珍宝，如安省雷湾（Thunder Bay）的紫水晶和卑诗省特有的森林水晶。</span></span></span></p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/10/6/n14089873.md#1/be906a76b79b6158e0a1a1b92a74e454" rel="attachment wp-att-14089894"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14089894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089894-be906a76b79b6158e0a1a1b92a74e454-450x236.png" alt="" width="616" b="323" /></a></p>
<p>在多伦多宝石展上，您更有机会与众多珠宝设计师和工匠见面，其中许多人曾获得国际大奖。展会设有众多课程，能激发人们的创造力，可向国际知名工匠学习新技能。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/10/6/n14089873.md#1/e19f2d527729c971731fd346f6eb558b" rel="attachment wp-att-14089899"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14089899" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089899-e19f2d527729c971731fd346f6eb558b-450x213.png" alt="" width="617" b="292" /></a></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">准备好会见宝石切割大师吧！他们提供宝石和半宝石，包括红宝石、蓝宝石、祖母绿、海蓝宝石、月光石、碧玺、波罗的海琥珀、珍珠等等。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14089898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089898-cefcd0034c17cf48062bf4361d914fda-450x222.png" alt="" width="616" b="304" /></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">串珠爱好者，别错过各种各样串珠、纯银、饰品及辅料的天堂。</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-14089907 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14089907-1280-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="617" b="347" /></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">矿物爱好者，一定会兴奋不已。展会上的水晶和矿物，可与任何博物馆或私人收藏家的藏品相媲美。</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p>展会还将举行拍卖，拍卖所得将用以帮助和支持多伦多地区的慈善机构。多伦多宝石博览会不仅是一场宝石展，更是一场<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">文化交汇、为当地人才提供展示才能的机会、促进精神健康和慈善事业的盛会。</span></span></span></p>
