<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林双十国庆路跑及升旗典礼　乡亲活力无限</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091813-640104-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>升旗典礼庄严隆重。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月10日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）云林县政府在国庆日当天，特别于县府前广场举办升旗典礼暨路跑活动，邀请黄金战士陈致远为活动代言人，鼓励全民以健康路跑的方式共同庆祝国家生日。县长张丽善带领县府主管及乡亲一同高唱国歌，共同欢庆这特别的日子。</p>
<p>张丽善县长表示，云林县政府将在明年承办全台运动会，因此特别结合路跑活动，希望促进本县的运动发展、加强家庭亲子互动，提升假日休闲娱乐的乐趣。同时，透过此活动，展现云林县政府推动品德教育的成果，彰显云林人家庭和乐以及热情的一面。</p>
<figure id="14091812" aria-describedby="caption-14091812" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091812-640103-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091812-640103-450x338.jpg" alt="县长张丽善带领贵宾一起参加８点的升旗典礼。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14091812" class="wp-caption-text">县长张丽善带领贵宾一起参加８点的升旗典礼。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14091810" aria-describedby="caption-14091810" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091810-640101-450x352.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091810-640101-450x352.jpg" alt="双十路跑活动受到民众欢迎。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14091810" class="wp-caption-text">双十路跑活动受到民众欢迎。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年参与的民众相当多，包括坐在娃娃车里的小朋友，还有94岁的廖奶奶都在亲人的陪同下，一起走完或跑完三公里健康路程。</p>
<figure id="14091815" aria-describedby="caption-14091815" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091815-640105-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091815-640105-450x338.jpg" alt="94岁的二崙乡亲，也在儿子的陪同下完成３公里健跑路程。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14091815" class="wp-caption-text">94岁的二崙乡亲，也在儿子的陪同下完成３公里健跑路程。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次参加路跑的参赛者可获得各种完赛礼品，包括活动纪念衫、精美国旗提袋、实用帆布袋、完赛纪念奖牌、国旗图案口罩。此外，县政府还提供云林币APP下载的民众100枚云林币，前300名更可获得额外的国旗口罩、小国旗和神秘小礼物。</p>
<figure id="14091816" aria-describedby="caption-14091816" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091816-640106-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091816-640106-450x338.jpg" alt="着国旗装参赛相当显眼。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14091816" class="wp-caption-text">着国旗装参赛相当显眼。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14091811" aria-describedby="caption-14091811" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091811-640102-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14091811-640102-450x338.jpg" alt="小朋友活力无限。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14091811" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友活力无限。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
