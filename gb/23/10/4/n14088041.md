<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>文化部艺术入校计划启动  狮头工艺大师传承有人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088044-639276-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>钟进权大师亲自指导学童们，教授制作狮头的工艺。（西螺七崁武术文教基金会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月04日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）文化部艺术入校计划正在云林西螺地区展开，特聘著名狮头工艺大师钟进权，将他的丰富经验传授给当地的学童们。</p>
<p>现年78岁的钟进权，在十几岁时学习了西螺七崁振兴社武术，习得拳术、兵器对打和舞狮的技艺。自小他就观察老师傅制作狮头，并受到老师傅的启发，开始了他制作狮头的精彩生涯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14088043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14088043" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088043-639275-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14088043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088043-639275-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14088043" class="wp-caption-text">狮头工艺大师钟进权的狮头作品。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14088042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14088042" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088042-639274-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14088042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088042-639274-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14088042" class="wp-caption-text">钟进权和他亲手制作的狮头。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钟师傅平时以农务为生，但在农闲之余，他不忘专心制作狮头。多年来，他累积了数十个狮头的制作经验，而这些数十年的岁月已难以计数。如今，他已经从农务中退休，但他仍然坚守着手工纸糊狮头的传统工艺。更重要的是，他愿意将这门“艺术性、特殊性及地方性”浓厚的传统民俗艺术传承给后辈。9月24日也将精致的狮头，移至西螺森活武树园区做特展。</p>
<p>文化部艺术入校计划目前正进行于西螺地区的两所学校，包括西螺安定国小和西螺文兴国小。钟进权大师亲自指导学童们，教授制作狮头的工艺，这项计划已进行到第三周的黏贴纸张阶段，十月初将进入狮头彩绘的环节。这样的传承工作有助于保存并传播台湾文化的精髓，也让下一代能够学习和欣赏这项珍贵的传统工艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14088045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14088045" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088045-639277-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14088045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088045-639277-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14088045" class="wp-caption-text">已进行到第三周的黏贴纸张阶段。（西螺七崁武术文教基金会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14088046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14088046" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088046-639278-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14088046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14088046-639278-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14088046" class="wp-caption-text">这样的传承工作有助于保存并传播台湾文化的精髓。（西螺七崁武术文教基金会提供）</figcaption></figure>
