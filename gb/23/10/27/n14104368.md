<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>崙背国小获建筑界奥斯卡奖  建筑园冶奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104369-643795-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>崙背国小获建筑界奥斯卡奖，建筑园冶奖。（李秉承提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月27日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）云林县崙背国小洋哥厨房，27日喜获建筑界奥斯卡奖-建筑园冶奖，为该校提供食品安全和健康饮食的中央厨房工程，从无到有的建设过程中，以地方特产洋香瓜的创意来发想，成为当地的标志性建筑和社群媒体打卡热点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14104369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104369" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104369-643795-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14104369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104369-643795-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104369" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国小获建筑界奥斯卡奖，建筑园冶奖。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14104370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104370" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104370-643796-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14104370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104370-643796-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104370" class="wp-caption-text">为该校提供食品安全和健康饮食的中央厨房工程。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14104371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104371" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104371-643797-450x487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14104371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104371-643797-450x487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="487" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104371" class="wp-caption-text">成为当地的标志性建筑和社群媒体打卡热点。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>崙背国小洋哥厨房以年轻、活力和充满自信的“年轻力量”为主题，充分体现了当地对健康饮食的承诺。该计划在地方社区中，普遍被误认为是一家餐厅或旅馆，受到广泛关注工程从五大桩基申请、地基挖掘、混凝土浇筑、钢筋绑扎，一直到厨房设备的安装等多个阶段，经历了巨大、原材料涨价、法规要求等停顿困难。和一系列的齐心协力下，该工程成功克服了所有挑战，用200多天的时间完成了两层楼筏式基础的中央厨房建设。</p>
<p>崙背国小校长李秉承表示，洋哥厨房的获奖是对专案团队辛勤工作的再次认可，感谢所有长官和学校同仁的大力支持，感谢他们为崙背偏远地区孩子提供更高品质的食品，和健康护理所做的努力该计划的成功，不仅提升了学童的食品品质，也使崙背地区成为建筑界的标竿，值得骄傲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14104373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104373" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104373-643799-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14104373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104373-643799-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104373" class="wp-caption-text">用200多天的时间完成了两层楼筏式基础的中央厨房建设。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14104374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104374" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104374-643800-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14104374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104374-643800-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104374" class="wp-caption-text">27日喜获建筑界奥斯卡奖-建筑园冶奖。（李秉承提供）</figcaption></figure>
