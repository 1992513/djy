<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>112年嘉义市中小学联合运动会27日盛大开幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104234-643714-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>教育处长郭添财表扬办理本次运动会的有功人员。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月27日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）112年嘉义市中小学联合运动会－开幕典礼于今（27）日上午10时在嘉义市东区体育馆盛大举行，由民生国中带来的“健康活力跃青春”大会操与世贤国小同学带来的“舞动青春 WooSa GO!”精彩表演开场，透过中小学联合运动会挖掘各项运动的优秀选手，带动本市体育运动的蓬勃发展。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14104227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104227" style="width: 503px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104227-643707-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14104227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104227-643707-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="503" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104227" class="wp-caption-text">世贤国小带来“舞动青春 WooSa GO!”精彩表演开场。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14104226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104226" style="width: 503px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104226-643706-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14104226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104226-643706-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="503" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104226" class="wp-caption-text">民生国中表演大会操。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今日开场表演同学们展现出青春活力与热情，也透过舞蹈编排将WooSa? WooSa 原民部落用语“不要紧张、不要害怕”的意涵，为所有选手加油打气，希望每位选手都能全力拚搏，展现坚持不放弃的信念，赢得佳绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14104228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104228" style="width: 522px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104228-643708-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14104228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104228-643708-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="522" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104228" class="wp-caption-text">引导会旗进场的仪队。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次赛会特别安排112年全国中等学校运动会木球银牌的选手廖竑玉和跆拳道78公斤以上对打铜牌的选手吴秉承一起担任圣火传递，并邀请2023世界中学运动会 U18女子组2000障碍金牌及全国U18女子2000障碍田径最高纪录保持人朱婕瑀选手担任宣誓代表，传递运动员在竞赛中的坚持与态度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14104229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104229" style="width: 522px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104229-643709-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14104229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104229-643709-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="522" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104229" class="wp-caption-text">会旗进场。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市政府教育处长郭添财代表黄敏惠市长致词时表示，期待嘉义市成为一个生活运动化，运动生活化的乐龄勇壮城市，健康是幸福的唯一保障，要保持健康的生活要从平时、随时、习惯的运动做起，除了有像今天的竞技赛事之外，我们也期待全民的运动不管是相关的赛事或者是平常的娱乐，乐龄运动，期待大家都能共同来参与，再次代表市长期许大家都有好成绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14104230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104230" style="width: 504px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104230-643710-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14104230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104230-643710-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="504" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104230" class="wp-caption-text">木球银牌的选手廖竑玉和跆拳道78公斤以上对打铜牌的选手吴秉承传递并点燃圣火。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次中小学联合运动会订于10月27日至11月3日举办共计8天，赛会预计办理田径、游泳、羽球、篮球、足球、桌球、跆拳道、网球、排球、角力、柔道、躲避球、木球及特奥滚球等14项竞赛项目，预计有4,417人次参与赛会，赛事精彩可期，邀请市民朋友到场为选手们加油！各赛项最新消息及嘉义市学童专访的精彩报导，将收录于“桃城体育网”（https://www.cysport.tw），欢迎大家上网浏览给予鼓励。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14104231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104231" style="width: 495px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104231-643711-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14104231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104231-643711-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="495" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104231" class="wp-caption-text">圣火点燃成功。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>年底即将举办的第26届诸罗山杯国际软式少年棒球邀请赛也是全国少棒比赛的年度盛事，透过这些赛事邀请各县市的民众来体验嘉义市的美好，也让嘉义市民的心凝聚起来，打造一个人人运动、人人健康的幸福城市。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14104232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104232-643712-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14104232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104232-643712-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104232" class="wp-caption-text">升会旗。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14104233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14104233" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104233-643713-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14104233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14104233-643713-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14104233" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市政府教育处长郭添财代表黄敏惠市长致词时表示，期待嘉义市成为一个生活运动化，运动生活化的乐龄勇壮城市。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
