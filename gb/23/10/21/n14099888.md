<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【车评】承传 Boxer精髓 2024 Subaru Impreza RS</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099904-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2024 Subaru Impreza RS。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月21日讯】全新第六代Subaru Impreza在去年洛杉矶国际车展上发表，也紧接在今年春季开始发售，而终于在这周可以试驾这款代号GU特2024年Impreza，并且是最高版本的Impreza RS。</p>
<p><a loading="lazy" title="【新车试驾】6代Impreza更有型 RS版更强 2024 Subaru Impreza RS" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/zh-TW/embed/1g73ajoaqu04cZIit2kfgaJYH1eo1c" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>2024年款Subaru Impreza是全新设计，无论在设计、性能、安全和技术方面都经过了全新的升级。这一代Impreza只会有五门掀背版，完全放弃了四门轿车设计。拥有更运动风格的外貌，2024年Impreza使用“Subaru全球平台（Subaru Global Platform）”，扭曲刚性提高了10%，全内骨架结构和额外的结构黏合剂（从26.2呎增加到88.5呎）有助于提高车身刚性，同时减轻了重量，也带来更平稳行车及更宁静的车厢 ，操控更为灵敏。RS版加强了外观的运动元素，包括前车门和后行李箱上的RS徽章；18吋深灰色合金轮圈；前脸栏栅、侧裙、侧镜、升级LED头灯和LED雾灯的黑色饰条。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14099889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14099889" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099889-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14099889" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099889-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14099889" class="wp-caption-text">2024 Subaru Impreza RS。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>入门版和Sport版配备了2升4缸直喷Subaru Boxer水平对向引擎，最大马力输出152匹，扭矩输出145磅呎；最高级的Impreza RS则使用2.5升4缸直喷Subaru Boxer水平对向引擎，马力与扭矩更强，达到182匹及178磅呎。两款引擎都标配Lineartronic CVT无段变速箱，Sport和RS版还配备方向盘拨片，可手动模式控制8速的升降，让驾驶者可随心随意控制变速箱。依旧标配了Subaru的“对称全轮驱动系统（Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive）”，提升了主动扭矩矢量控制，转向和操控更为灵敏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14099891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14099891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099891-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_18.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14099891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099891-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14099891" class="wp-caption-text">2升4缸直喷水平对向引擎。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2024 Impreza内装设计简约，最引人注目的是全新的11.6吋Subaru Starlink多媒体系统，这是Impreza首次搭载这一系统。高解析度触摸屏支援无线Apple CarPlay和Android Auto，让驾驶者可以轻松地连接智能手机，使用全屏幕来控制汽车的各项功能，如音响、恒温系统和引擎自动开关等功能。系统还具备Bluetooth免提通话、AM/FM立体声、后视摄像头、SiriusXM收音机等。所有车型均标配电动门锁、电动车窗和电动侧镜、迎宾照明、智能遥控钥匙、独立轮胎压力监测系统，以及60/40可折叠后排座椅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14099892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14099892" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099892-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_30.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14099892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099892-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_30-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14099892" class="wp-caption-text">内装设计简约。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>RS版还带10向电动调节驾驶座椅，而车内使用仿金属和模拟碳纤维的内饰饰条，黑色布料座椅带有红色布料侧条，地毯地垫上有RS标志。RS还有皮革包裹方向盘和自动变速杆、铝合金踏板、加热前座椅和后USB充电端口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14099893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14099893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099893-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_19.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14099893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099893-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14099893" class="wp-caption-text">传统仪表板。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，新Impreza还搭载了最新一代的EyeSight驾驶辅助技术，EyeSight可以识别十字路口的自行车和行人，并在必要时提醒驾驶者并启动刹车以避免碰撞。Impreza还提供了自动紧急转向功能、盲点检测、车道保持辅助和后方交通警告的车型，以在速度低于50英里/小时时协助控制转向，以帮助避免碰撞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14099894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14099894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099894-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_20.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14099894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099894-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14099894" class="wp-caption-text">11.6吋Subaru Starlink多媒体系统。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>总的来说，全新的2024 Subaru Impreza在驾驶上维持了一向Subaru的动态表现，但在设计上、性能、安全和技术方面都有进步。虽然市场上小型轿车都加入了四驱选择，Subaru的“对称全轮驱动系统”仍是给人更有信心，而且入门版也是标配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14099895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14099895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099895-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_21.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14099895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099895-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14099895" class="wp-caption-text">自动变速箱。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>RS版的2.5升引擎动力表现也更好，在超车越线也能轻松完成任务。若是Subaru要以RS取代旧有WRX地位，难免令一众车迷失望，希望Subaru能提供涡轮增压版引擎的更强选择，并有270匹以上马力才能满足车迷！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14099898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14099898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099898-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_28.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14099898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099898-2024_Subaru_Impreza_RS_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14099898" class="wp-caption-text">双色座椅。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
