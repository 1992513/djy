<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林武术盛事 “西螺七崁武术嘉年华”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100319-642551-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>吸引了来自全国各地的武术爱好者和民众前来同欢度两天的活动。（云林县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月21日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）一年一度的云林运动盛事，“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%9E%BA%E4%B8%83%E5%B4%81.md#1">西螺七崁</a>武术嘉年华”，21日在西螺森活武树园盛大开幕，吸引了来自全国各地的武术爱好者和民众前来同欢度两天的活动。这次活动同步结合了“西螺米酱节”，让人们品味当地的特色美食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100324-642556.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14100324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100324-642556-600x401.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100324" class="wp-caption-text">吸引许多来自全国各地的武术爱好者。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14100320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100320-642552.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14100320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100320-642552-600x401.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100320" class="wp-caption-text">小小传统武术传人。（云林县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14100323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100323-642555.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14100323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100323-642555-600x401.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100323" class="wp-caption-text">云林武术盛事“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%9E%BA%E4%B8%83%E5%B4%81.md#1">西螺七崁</a>武术嘉年华”。（云林县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云林县长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E4%B8%BD%E5%96%84.md#1">张丽善</a>表示，这个年度盛事以“斗阵．展功夫-西螺七崁武术嘉年华”的主题，吸引了大批乡亲携家带眷参与。</p>
<p>张县长进一步指出，透过武术嘉年华活动，人们能更深入了解武术文化。她特别强调了“牵箍”的表演，展现了武术的精神，团结、同心协力才能战胜一切。</p>
<p>云林县议员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%98%8E%E5%93%B2.md#1">李明哲</a>则提到，开幕当天的精彩活动包括“七崁武术大会师”，这是七崁武馆老师父联合展演的盛会。另外，基金会首次策划了“七崁武术剧场”，由当地的师父担任演出，首演剧码为“凤舞薪传西螺凤山馆之七崁传奇武术剧场”，希望观众们能感受到武术的魅力。</p>
<p>“西螺七崁武术嘉年华”将持续进行两天，带动西螺地区的运动精神，并提供人们一个难得的机会，欣赏和参与这项古老而有力的文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14100322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14100322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100322-642554.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14100322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14100322-642554-600x401.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14100322" class="wp-caption-text">传统武术展演，虎虎生风。（云林县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
