<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小火烤15分钟 叉烧肉软嫩又多汁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092605-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为叉烧肉。（台湾广厦提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>不必用卤汁长时间炖煮，以烧烤的方式，在烤架上将肉以小火烤15分钟至焦黄色，便能完成简易型叉烧肉。专家传授烤出美味的4大秘诀：预先调味，掌握加热的温度、时间和切肉窍门，即可享受这道软嫩、多汁的叉烧肉料理。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>不必炖煮、轻松烹制</strong></span></h2>
<p>以下要介绍的是<strong><span style="color: #800000;">简易型的叉烧肉</span></strong>做法。<strong><span style="color: #800000;">不必用卤汁慢炖、直接使用烤架将肉烤到焦黄色即可。关键就在于先把肉分切好，并仔细预先调味。</span></strong>此外，<strong><span style="color: #800000;">烤好的肉以切断纤维的方向切片</span></strong>，可以凸显肉的分量，<strong><span style="color: #800000;">呈现软嫩的口感！</span></strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>材料（容易制作的分量）</strong></span></h2>
<p>猪肩胛肉（块） 500g</p>
<p>A<br />
酱油 60ml<br />
砂糖 70g<br />
蜂蜜 30g<br />
甜面酱?1/2大匙<br />
味噌?2小匙<br />
蚝油 2小匙<br />
白芝麻酱 2小匙<br />
芝麻油 2小匙<br />
盐 1小匙<br />
花椒 1小匙<br />
蒜头（磨泥）1小匙<br />
姜（磨泥）1/2小匙<br />
肉桂棒? <u></u>1根<br />
丁香? 5个<br />
八角? 1个<br />
胡椒粉 少许</p>
<p>喜欢的蔬菜（莴苣或茗荷等）适量</p>
<p>注：<strong>肩胛肉（梅花肉）：最能品尝出猪肉的美味</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092580" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092580-P.110-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14092580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092580-P.110-2.jpg" alt="" width="440" b="274" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092580" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>瘦肉和脂肪的比例均匀，表面有脂肪，脂肪与瘦肉之间有筋。味道浓郁、美味，是最能体现猪肉美味的部位。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>前置处理</strong></span></h2>
<p><strong>1.制作调味酱</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092581" style="width: 560px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092581-P.111-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092581-P.111-1-560x400.jpg" alt="" width="560" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092581" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将A放入耐热碗里均匀混合。不必覆盖保鲜膜，直接用微波炉加热到沸腾，取出后静置到恢复室温。</p>
<p><strong>2.切肉</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092582" style="width: 559px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092582-P.111-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092582-P.111-2-559x400.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092582" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将肉块立起来、切成2～2.5公分的厚度。由于采用烘烤的方式，先<strong><span style="color: #800000;">分切成小块较容易入味，也方便加热</span></strong>。</p>
<p><strong>3.切断筋膜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092583" style="width: 560px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092583-P.111-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092583-P.111-3-560x400.jpg" alt="" width="560" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092583" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用刀刃切入步骤2的两面，<strong><span style="color: #800000;">切断筋膜可预防肉遇热收缩、烤出外形完整的肉块。</span></strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>正式烹调</strong></span></h2>
<p><strong>4.用调味酱腌渍</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092584" style="width: 562px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092584-P.111-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092584-P.111-4-562x400.jpg" alt="" width="562" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092584" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将肉块、腌渍酱料放进塑胶袋里，封好袋口、放进冰箱里腌渍约1小时。</p>
<p><strong>5.搓揉塑胶袋</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092585" style="width: 564px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092585-P.111-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092585-P.111-5-564x400.jpg" alt="" width="564" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092585" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在腌渍过程中，从冰箱取出搓揉几次，让调味酱入味。最后起锅时会使用到袋中的调味酱，请保留下来。</p>
<p><strong>6.烤肉</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092586" style="width: 561px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092586-P.111-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092586-P.111-6-561x400.jpg" alt="" width="561" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092586" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>取出肉块，刮掉多余的调味酱，放在烤架上以<strong><span style="color: #800000;">小火烤13～15分钟</span></strong>，</p>
<p><strong>7.原地静置</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092587" style="width: 563px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092587-P.111-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092587-P.111-7-563x400.jpg" alt="" width="563" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092587" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">让烤过的肉块静置约3分钟。利用余温加热，可烤出软嫩多汁的口感。</span></strong></p>
<p><strong>8.清除烧焦部分</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092588" style="width: 562px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092588-P.111-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092588-P.111-8-562x400.jpg" alt="" width="562" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092588" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烘烤时出现烧焦的部分会带有苦味，请用剪刀仔细清除干净。保留滴在网子下方的酱汁。</p>
<p><strong>9.切叉烧肉</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092589" style="width: 562px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092589-P.111-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092589-P.111-9-562x400.jpg" alt="" width="562" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092589" class="wp-caption-text">切肉时，与纤维垂直方向切断纤维，容易咀嚼，能品尝到肉的软嫩。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将烤好的叉烧肉切片，和喜欢的蔬菜一起装盘。将腌渍酱、烤网下酱汁微波加热约2分钟到沸腾后，再均匀淋在肉上面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092579" style="width: 386px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092579-P.110-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-14092579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092579-P.110-1-386x400.jpg" alt="" width="386" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092579" class="wp-caption-text">叉烧肉。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>美味秘诀！</strong></span></h2>
<p><strong>不采取炖煮改用烘烤的方式</strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">如果选择脂肪含量较多的猪肩胛肉制作叉烧肉</span></strong>，用烘烤的方式来料理比较适合。加热前先分切肉块，烘烤时一边去除油脂、一边烤出香喷喷的美味。◇</p>
