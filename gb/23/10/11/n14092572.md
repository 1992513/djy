<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北海道烤羊肉太好吃！主厨揭2烧烤要领</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092901-P.190-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本北海道成吉思汗烤羊肉。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>说到日本最知名的羊肉料理，就是北海道的“成吉思汗烤肉”。不仅广受当地居民喜爱，也是来自世界各地的观光客指名享用的料理。东京“成吉思汗烤肉店FUJIYA”的主厨东藤正宪先生，北海道出身，拥有出神入化的切肉刀工，传授最正统的吃法。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>从肉的准备、烤肉方法、蔬菜搭配和调味酱都很讲究</strong></span></h2>
<p>羊肉特有的羊骚味，主要原因在于多余的脂肪。想要放心大啖羊肉，重点在于“新鲜度”和“前置处理”。</p>
<p>在烤肉时最重要的一点是，要保留羊肉的半熟程度。利用猪油增添美味，烤到表面呈焦黄色再翻面，里面呈淡淡的粉红色为最佳状态，事实上只要烤几秒即能完成。万一烤过头，肉很容易变硬，因此要快速烤好、趁热品尝。</p>
<p>搭配的烤蔬菜如果也能用心准备，吃起来会更加美味。</p>
<p>此外，用来沾肉的调味酱也不可马虎。虽然市售的调味酱就很美味，但是如果味道太重，很容易抢走羊肉本身的风味。建议使用以酱油为基底的调味酱或柚子醋酱，才能真正提引出羊肉自身的美味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>软嫩的成吉思汗烤肉</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>材料</strong></span></h3>
<p>羔羊肉（喜欢的部位）100～150g<br />
洋葱 1个<br />
豆芽 1袋<br />
豆苗 1袋<br />
猪油 10g<br />
喜欢的调味酱 适量</p>
<p><strong>注：羔羊肉</strong><strong>：</strong>羊肉的英文有“lamb”和“mutton”的差别，前者是指“羔羊肉”，后者则是指“成羊肉”，两者具有成长阶段的差异。成羊肉：（在日本）指出生后1年以上的成羊，有时亦指长出1颗以上恒齿的羊。口感比羔羊肉硬。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>蔬菜</strong></span></h3>
<p>洋葱和豆芽都是北海道的常见食材，搭配豆苗也很赞。选择比较细的豆芽，味道更搭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092894" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092894-P.189-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092894-P.189-2-450x455.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="455" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092894" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也可以和口味清爽的菇类一起烤。蒟蒻是日本的传统食材，口感Q弹且营养满分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092895" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092895-P.189-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092895-P.189-3-450x366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092895" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>执行步骤</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.以中火预热成吉思汗烤肉盘（或铁板），将猪油放在中央，也可以用牛油取代。<strong><span style="color: #800000;">油脂的香气可以使烤肉吃起来更加美味。</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092896" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092896-P.189-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092896-P.189-4-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092896" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.洋葱切成八等分，散置于成吉思汗烤肉盘的周边，秘诀在于立起来放稳，并维持相同的间距。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092897" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092897-P.189-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092897-P.189-5-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092897" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.以画圆的方式，在洋葱的内侧铺满豆芽。上面再以相同的方式，铺放切成容易入口长度的豆苗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092898" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092898-P.189-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092898-P.189-6-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092898" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.将羊肉铺放在成吉思汗烤肉盘上方空出来的部分，尽量铺满整个锅面，肉才不易烤焦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092899" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092899" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092899-P.190-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092899" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092899-P.190-1-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092899" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.当<strong><span style="color: #800000;">肉的</span></strong>周边开始泛白、<strong><span style="color: #800000;">背面烤出焦黄色之后即可翻面。为避免加热过头，之后再稍微烤一下即可。</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14092900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092900" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092900-P.190-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092900-P.190-2-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092900" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.将肉和蔬菜一起沾调味酱再享用。调味酱视个人喜好调配，味道清爽的柚子酱、浓郁的调味酱皆可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092901" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092901-P.190-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092901-P.190-3-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092901" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>烧烤的秘诀！烤肉盘的周边要铺满蔬菜</strong></span></h2>
<p>成吉思汗烤肉盘的特征是呈弧形的形状和周边的沟槽，火力是从下方导入并循环加热，这种设计的用意在于慢火烤羊肉的过程中，肉汁会顺势流入沟槽里。在烤肉盘的周边铺满蔬菜，肉汁便可渗透到蔬菜当中。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>以</strong><strong>8</strong><strong>：</strong><strong>2</strong><strong>的比例烤正面和反面</strong></span></h3>
<p>羊肉不可烤过久，肉片放在成吉思汗烤肉盘上之后，不宜翻动，请务必耐心等到侧面泛白。待出现焦黄色之后翻面，然后只需稍微烤一下即可，如此即可品尝到最软嫩又肉汁饱满的羊肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092891" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092891-P.188-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092891-P.188-1-450x610.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="610" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092891" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>各个羊肉部位的特色？</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>羔羊瘦肉</strong></span></h3>
<p>味道清淡、肉质软嫩，没有羊骚味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092902" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092902-P.190-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092902-P.190-4-450x355.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092902" class="wp-caption-text">羔羊瘦肉（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>羔羊五花肉</strong></span></h3>
<p>脂肪比较多，味道浓郁。由于筋膜多，所以切工要多花点心思。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092903" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092903-P.190-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092903-P.190-5-450x356.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092903" class="wp-caption-text">羔羊五花肉（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>羊肩里肌肉</strong></span></h3>
<p>最软嫩、味道浓郁的部分，成吉思汗烤肉菜单上的首选。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092904-P.190-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092904-P.190-6-450x370.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092904" class="wp-caption-text">羊肩里肌肉（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>羊舌</strong></span></h3>
<p>和牛舌不同，相对小很多，因此是稀少部位。咬下去脆脆的，味道浓郁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14092905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14092905" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092905-P.190-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14092905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14092905-P.190-7-450x360.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14092905" class="wp-caption-text">羊舌（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">注：<strong>适合烧烤料理的羊肉部位</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>羊五花肉：胸部与腹部的肉</strong></span></h3>
<p>位于胸侧的“胸肉”与腹侧的“羊腩”合称为五花肉，脂肪和骨头周边都具有美味。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>羊上脑：位于羊肩旁边的里肌肉具有浓郁的味道</strong></span></h3>
<p>带有适度的霜降，肉质软嫩。味道浓郁、羊骚味比较少，羊肉入门者比较容易接受。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>羊肩肉：羊膝上方的前肢</strong></span></h3>
<p>瘦肉和脂肪分布均匀，肉质偏硬、筋比较多。具有羊肉特有的羊骚味。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>羊里肌肉：品质高级的背部肌肉</strong></span></h3>
<p>相当软嫩的瘦肉，在羊肉当中属于比较高价的部位。背骨与肋骨相连的带骨里肌肉。</p>
