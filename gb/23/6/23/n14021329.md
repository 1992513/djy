<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Dawson&#8217;s Landing社区第四期地块开始发售</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021807-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-00-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Dawson’s Landing社区。（Qualico集团提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月23日讯】Qualico Communities-卡尔加里宣布了切斯特米尔（Chestermere）领先社区Dawson&#8217;s Landing社区最新发布的住宅地块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14021809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021809-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-01.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14021809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021809-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-01-600x378.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021809" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区展示屋。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新一期地块预售6月22日开始，其中包括来自建筑商Broadview Homes、NuVista Homes、Truman Homes和Sterling Homes的令人兴奋的新住宅。“对于那些希望以更低的价格购买更宽敞住宅的人来说，前置车库双拼屋是一种新的住宅类型。”卡尔加里Qualico Communities营销与沟通协调莫妮卡·比利克（Monica Bilyk）表示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14021810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021810-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14021810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021810-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-03-600x360.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021810" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区展示屋。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有其它多种住宅类型供选择：Morrison Homes和Truman Homes的后巷车道双拼屋，Trico Homes的后巷车道独立屋以及Broadview Homes、NuVista Homes和Sterling Homes的前置车库独立屋。房屋起价40多万元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14021819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021819" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021819-026-20221030-highres.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14021819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021819-026-20221030-highres-600x400.jpg" alt="Dawson’s Landing社区，独立屋，双拼屋" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021819" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区展示屋。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14021816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021816" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021816-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-08.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14021816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021816-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-08-600x381.jpg" alt="Dawson’s Landing社区,独立屋,双拼屋" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021816" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区展示屋。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14021813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021813" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021813-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14021813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021813-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-05-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021813" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区展示屋。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_14021814" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021814" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021814-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-06.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14021814" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021814-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-06-600x370.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021814" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区展示屋。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了第四期地块发布外，居民现在还可以享受Dawson&#8217;s Landing社区首个已完工的游乐场和两个Stormwater Kidney路径区。“作为第一个建造Stormwater Kidney的社区，雨水池和公园为社区居民提供了很好的体验。”比利克表示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14021815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021815-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14021815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021815-20230602-Qualico-Communities-Dawsons-Landing-02-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021815" class="wp-caption-text">Dawson’s Landing社区公园。（Qualico集团提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如有兴趣预订新一期地块，可于周一至周四下午2点至晚上8点，周末和节假日中午12点至下午5点参观展示屋。</p>
<p>展示屋地点:</p>
<p>前置车库独立屋和前置车库双拼屋<br />
Broadview Homes – 468 Dawson Circle<br />
NuVista Homes – 472 Dawson Circle<br />
Sterling Homes – 476 Dawson Circle</p>
<p>后巷车道双拼屋<br />
Morrison Homes – 384 Dawson Drive</p>
<p>后巷车道独立屋<br />
Trico Homes – 101 Dawson Circle</p>
<p>前置车库双拼屋<br />
Truman Homes – 251 Chelsea Road</p>
