<h2><strong>毛花紫露草（Tradescantia cerinthoides）鸭跖草科</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14023063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023063-608157_medium.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14023063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023063-608157_medium-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023063" class="wp-caption-text">非常淡雅的紫色花朵，花瓣里面是白色的，带有金黄色的花蕊。</figcaption></figure>
<p>又称油画婚纱。多年生草本观叶植物。叶呈长椭圆形，叶背紫红色，叶片上会有一些白色、粉紫色和绿色相间的斑纹。非常淡雅的紫色花朵，花瓣里面是白色的，带有金黄色的花蕊。花萼密被长柔毛，这些柔毛模拟花粉粒，用来招揽传粉者造访。是常被称作油画婚纱的一种紫露草，原生的品种，现在几乎找不到了，只剩下园艺观赏的品种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14023064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023064" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023064-608158_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14023064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023064-608158_medium-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023064" class="wp-caption-text">作者：郑清海（台湾）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14023065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14023065" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023065-608159_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14023065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14023065-608159_medium-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14023065" class="wp-caption-text">常被称作油画婚纱的一种紫露草，原生的品种，现在几乎找不到了，只剩下园艺观赏的品种。</figcaption></figure>
