<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>六四34周年 卡城市民中领馆前烛光悼英灵</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012208-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>向放置在学生中心的民主女神像献花及黙哀，悼念六四死难英灵。（卡城民主促进会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月08日讯】毋忘六四，决不舍弃！今年是六四34周年。</p>
<p>六月四日当天，50多名卡加利巿民聚集在卡加利大学，向放置在学生中心的民主女神像献花及黙哀，悼念六四死难英灵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14012220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14012220" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012220-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14012220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012220-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-04-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14012220" class="wp-caption-text">（卡城民主促进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14012219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14012219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012219-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14012219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012219-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-02-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14012219" class="wp-caption-text">（卡城民主促进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚上八时，逾百卡城民众出席中领馆前的“六四烛光悼念晚会”。集会人士手持“毋忘六四”、“打倒中共”、“习帝下台”、“中国要民主”、“结束一党专政”、“光复香港”等标语牌，亦有民众高举加拿大及中华民国国旗，更有部分青年挥舞“光复香港，时代革命”旗帜。现场沿马路旁放置一排电子烛光，点点烛光，悼念六四死难英魂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14012221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14012221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012221-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-03.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14012221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012221-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14012221" class="wp-caption-text">六四烛光悼念晚会。（卡城民主促进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>悼念仪式在播放民运歌曲《血染的风采》、《明天会更好》、《自由花》后开始。</p>
<p>首先全体黙哀一分钟，继而由主办单位“加拿大卡城民主促进会”会长汤般若致词。他表示，自香港沦共，维园烛光被灭后，卡城烛光悼念可以说是延续维园的烛光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14012222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14012222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012222-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-05.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14012222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14012222-20230604-Calgary-Association-for-the-Chinese-Democracy-05-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14012222" class="wp-caption-text">六四烛光悼念晚会。（卡城民主促进会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他强调：“我们今日之活动，绝非只为纪念一次天安门被屠杀之学生，而是借此指控共匪无时无刻不对百姓之控制、迫害及谋杀之罪恶行为。共匪所犯之罪行以亿万计，绝非止于一两次之恶行。我们藉六四纪念日，告诉全世界自由人士，认清共产党独裁政权之邪恶面目。特别是我们香港人，曾享有人身自由、言论自由、创作自由、思想自由、经济自由，现在受共匪统治，深知其间之对比。所以散居世界各地之香港人，应与其他受共产党迫害之人士，联合起来，推翻共匪政权。”</p>
<p>之后陆续有民众发言，表达个人对六四的感受。</p>
