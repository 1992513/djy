<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】竹笙黄耳海参羹～夏季趋热佳肴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019548-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>竹笙黄耳海参羹。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>暑气蒸熏，气温湿热，食欲容易不振。煮一道营养丰富的羹汤配上白米饭，可以打开味蕾，一下子呼噜吃了好几碗。讲起煲汤、羹汤，粤菜的确闻名，今天介绍的这一道羹汤，主要材料包含竹笙、黄耳和海参。</p>
<p>海参的蛋白质丰富想必很多饕客都知道，加上Ｑ弹的口感更是深受多人喜爱。但是竹笙呢？它是一种蕈类，有多种氨基酸和维生素及矿物质，可以清热利湿、润肺化痰，常被用于各种药膳和汤品，也有人用来清炒。黄耳是菇菌类，具有清热功能，可以止咳化痰。这些食材综合在一起熬煮，在炎热夏季，的确给身体一帖清热的食补。所以，不妨尝试下厨做看看！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>竹笙黄耳海参羹</b></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>湿竹笙4 oz<br />
湿黄耳4 oz<br />
湿海参4 oz<br />
蒸熟瑶柱2 oz<br />
素上汤3杯<br />
芫荽少许（装饰用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019541" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019541-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14019541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019541-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019541" class="wp-caption-text">材料示意图。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14019549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019549" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019549-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14019549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019549-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019549" class="wp-caption-text">竹笙烹煮前应先泡温水。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>烹大师1茶匙<br />
盐1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
粟粉3汤匙（加开水勾芡用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019543-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14019543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019543-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019543" class="wp-caption-text">调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 竹笙、海参和黄耳全部切成小块备用。所有材料用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019544" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019544-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14019544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019544-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019544" class="wp-caption-text">竹笙、海参和黄耳全部切成小块备用。所有材料用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 汤锅内放入素上汤，煮滚后放入所有材料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019545" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019545-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14019545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019545-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019545" class="wp-caption-text">汤锅内放入素上汤，煮滚后放入所有材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 再煮滚后用粟粉水勾芡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019546" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019546-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14019546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019546-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019546" class="wp-caption-text">煮滚后用粟粉水勾芡。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将煮好的汤装在汤碗中，放上芫荽装饰便可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14019547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14019547" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019547-51ac8627b6952115e11c81d9602b581a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14019547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14019547-51ac8627b6952115e11c81d9602b581a-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14019547" class="wp-caption-text">将煮好的汤装在汤碗中，放上芫荽装饰便可。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
