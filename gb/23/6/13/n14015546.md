<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黄氏公所庆祝双亲节 近百乡亲同乐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015513-DSC07866-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙黄氏公所6月12日庆祝双亲节，主席黄伟健（右）、黄光沐（左）向宗亲们送上祝福。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年06月14日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）在母亲节和父亲节中间，纽英崙黄氏公所6月12日庆祝“双亲节”，邀集近百宗亲在华埠肥牛火锅城聚会联欢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14015512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14015512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015512-DSC07861.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14015512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015512-DSC07861-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14015512" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙黄氏公所6月12日庆祝双亲节，近百宗亲参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14015511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14015511" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015511-DSC07860.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14015511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015511-DSC07860-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14015511" class="wp-caption-text">左起：主席黄伟健、中文书记黄光野、主席黄光沐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天傍晚，肥牛火锅城3楼热气腾腾，人声鼎沸。近百宗亲欢聚一堂，乐享火锅，庆祝双亲节。黄氏公所主席黄伟健、黄光沐向每一桌宗亲致意，祝福大家团团圆圆，身体健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14015514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14015514" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015514-DSC07868.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14015514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015514-DSC07868-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14015514" class="wp-caption-text">火锅热气腾腾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14015515" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14015515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015515-DSC07869.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14015515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015515-DSC07869-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14015515" class="wp-caption-text">节日蛋糕色彩缤纷。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">黄氏公所妇女部主席黄周丽桃笑言，往年庆祝双亲节，他们都是在公所里自己做菜。今年感觉太累了，所以就下楼到肥牛火锅城聚餐，也是很好很热闹的。这一天也是公所例会的日子，所以大家先来开会，然后聚餐，一举两得。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14015516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14015516" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015516-DSC07884.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14015516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14015516-DSC07884-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14015516" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙黄氏公所6月12日庆祝双亲节，切蛋糕。左起：主席黄伟健、黄光沐、妇女部主席黄周丽桃。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">晚宴末尾，服务员推出来一个有“双亲节快乐”字样的芋头夹心大蛋糕。黄伟健、黄光沐、黄周丽桃等宗长一起切开蛋糕，与大家分享，为大家送上节日祝福。◇</p>
