<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：加拿大摄影师深入荒野 邂逅稀有猞猁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020158-et-web-lynx-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加拿大一位职业画家兼野生动物摄影师在北部偏远地区幸运地遇到了六只罕见的加拿大猞猁。（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年06月21日讯】（英文大纪元记者<ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/six-lynx-were-all-around-me-sitting-in-the-snow-photographers-journey-to-capture-rare-wildlife_5221768.md#1">Anna Mason</a>报导／张雨霏编译）当加拿大摄影师让-西蒙?贝金<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/jsb_wildlife_photography/">Jean-Simon Bégin</a>）在一场暴风雪中遇到了罕见的加拿大猞猁（Canada lynx，又译加拿大山猫）而且不是一只而是六只时，他感觉自己“就像做了一场梦”。</p>
<p>“我相信我是世界上为数不多的与六只野生山猫有过这种亲密接触的人之一。”来自魁北克市的贝金告诉《大纪元时报》。</p>
<p>这位32岁的职业画家兼野生动物摄影师在加拿大北部的偏远地区搜寻了好几天，才有了这次难得的“偶遇”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14020149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020149" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020149-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020149-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-3.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020149" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14020147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020147" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020147-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020147-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-1.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020147" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管该国北部栖息着大量这种特殊的野猫，但从12岁起就拿着相机四处拍摄动物的贝金，直到二十多岁才成功捕捉到一只加拿大猞猁的身影。他说，寻找猞猁成了他“最着迷的事情”。</p>
<p>几年前，在贝金第一次前往那个地方（为了“保护该地点及这些动物”而拒绝透露具体位置）时，他设法和一只孤独的猞猁待了10分钟左右。</p>
<p>“这种动物很难找到”，他说，“它坐在那里并一直盯着我看，然后开始舔自己。”</p>
<p>这是一次感人的经历，但贝金渴望体验更多。今年早些时候，他再次抵达那里，并得到了意想不到的巨大收获。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14020148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020148" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020148-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020148-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-2.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020148" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14020152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020152" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020152-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020152-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-7.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="561" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020152" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“加拿大猞猁非常好动，并且拥有广阔的领地”，贝金说，“所以你必须有好的运气。”</p>
<p>这种探险的最大困难不是外在的危险，而是内心的煎熬。</p>
<p>“一天奔波八个小时却没有任何收获，情绪降到最低点是很正常的。这是一场重要的心理战，你必须凭着坚强的毅力坚持下去。”他说。</p>
<p>?<ahref="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_fXQjQE287g">请点看相关影片</a></p>
<p>虽然连续几天还是一无所获，但贝金战胜了脑海中时不时冒出来的负面想法，继续前进。一天晚上，那个偏远地区迎来了一场巨大的暴风雪。次日早上，贝金迎风冒雪赶往他前一天看到留有猞猁家族足迹的地方：“我沿着森林中的雪路走了整整六个小时。到了之前看到脚印的地方，我惊奇地发现这群野猫竟踩过我留下的脚印。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14020155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020155" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020155-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020155-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-10.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020155" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14020151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020151" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020151-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020151-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-6.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020151" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当他行经一条雪道和一条雪地摩托车道的交汇处时，他眼角的余光突然瞥见摩托车道的中间有一个看起来像“黑点”一样的东西。没过多久，他就意识到那是一只坐在地上的猞猁，于是立即紧握相机趴在地上静静地等待。那只动物大约在100米外。</p>
<p>“突然，一只小猞猁从树林里跑了出来，接着又出现三只。最后，另一只成年猞猁走了过来和它们坐在一起”，贝金说，“我面前一共出现了六只猞猁。这是非常特别的事情，我简直不敢相信。”</p>
<p>这群野猫可以清楚地看到这位摄影师，但没有任何要离开的迹象。接下来，不可思议的事情发生了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14020150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020150" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020150-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020150-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-5.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020150" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14020157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020157" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020157-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-14.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020157-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-14.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020157" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>几只小猞猁一个接一个地排成一排跟在妈妈身后，一起朝贝金走去。前行10米左右后，这群猞猁会停下来坐一会儿，然后接着朝前走。</p>
<p>“最终，六只山猫都到了我身边，它们坐在雪地里，敏锐的目光一直环顾四周的森林，似乎在搜寻猎物。它们根本无视我的存在。”贝金说。</p>
<p>毋庸置疑，对于独自一人在加拿大荒野中冒险的贝金来说，这的确是一个激动人心的时刻。这些珍贵的照片一经发布，就迅速在网络上传播开来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14020154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020154" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020154-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020154-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-9.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020154" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14020156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020156" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020156-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020156-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-11.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020156" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我发现世界各地的人们在Instagram和Facebook上持续关注这些照片长达一个月”，他说，“世界上每一位野生动物摄影师都看到了它们。这个事件非常罕见，引起了很多人的注意。”</p>
<p>这次寻找加拿大猞猁的旅行不仅给贝金带来了艺术才华方面的赞誉，也成为他一生中最难忘的时刻之一，同时还加强了他野外生存的专业技能。</p>
<p>这位青年才俊在2021年获得了“年度最佳加拿大摄影师”称号，迄今为止已经积累了七十多张加拿大猞猁的“好照片”。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14020153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14020153" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020153-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14020153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14020153-jean-simon-begin-canadian-lynx-quebec-8.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14020153" class="wp-caption-text">（Jean-Simon Bégin提供）</figcaption></figure>
