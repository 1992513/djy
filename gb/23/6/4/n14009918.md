<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>母爱无边 法国华裔社区共聚庆祝母亲节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009923-6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月4日是法国母亲节，法国华裔社区共聚庆祝节日。（大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月05日讯】6月4日是法国母亲节，法国华裔社区选择了这一天为伟大的母亲们庆祝节日。</p>
<p>欧洲梅兰协会联合法国海外华裔联谊会在巴黎13区举办了庆祝活动。主办方准备了丰富的抽奖礼物，为出席活动的母亲们献上一朵朵美丽的康乃馨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009922" style="width: 564px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009922-3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14009922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009922-3-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="564" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009922" class="wp-caption-text">主办方为母亲们献上美丽的康乃馨。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14009924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009924" style="width: 411px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009924-8.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14009924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009924-8-600x733.jpg" alt="" width="411" b="503" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009924" class="wp-caption-text">主办方为母亲们献上美丽的康乃馨。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“母爱无边”，欧洲梅兰协会总会长詹致远先生发言分享母亲节的来历，他希望通过庆祝活动，将此传统文化的孝道礼仪和普世价值的美德传承给年轻一辈。</p>
<p>欧洲梅兰协会（Association Euro-Medlan）是一个民间组织，成立至今已有40年，在法国和非洲设有4个分会，致力促进西方与亚洲人的文化交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009921-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009921-1-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009921" class="wp-caption-text">6月4日是法国母亲节，法国华裔社区共聚庆祝节日。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>法国海外华裔联谊会于2022年8月成立，会长罗强先生兼侨务顾问是本次庆祝活动的主办人之一，为此，他提前一个多月就作筹备了。</p>
<p>法国海外华裔联谊会名誉会长、侨务委员施龙先生，台湾代表处侨务组刘素秋组长、代表处颜嘉良公使出席参加了活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009925" style="width: 435px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009925-9.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14009925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009925-9-600x525.jpg" alt="" width="435" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009925" class="wp-caption-text">图为前辈们以书法题词向母亲们表达浓浓的敬意。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动现场，前辈们纷纷以朗诵诗词和书法的方式，向母亲们表达浓浓的敬意。</p>
