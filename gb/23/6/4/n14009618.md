<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鼓励学生参与公共事务 桃青年局凝聚17校交流</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009619-617654-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>青年局侯佳龄局长与班学联会学生合影。（桃园市青年局提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月04日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市青年事务局重视校园社团发展，办理“班学联会交接日”，携手桃园市高中职校17校班联会相互交流，透过班联会同学们的经验分享，以及各校办理特色活动等议题，凝聚共识将热忱传承给予接棒的学弟妹们。</p>
<p>交流活动采分组进行，分为主席/会长组、副主席/副会长组、学权组、出纳/秘书/总干事组及公关组，并邀请“法律白话文运动”主编王鼎棫担任讲师，分享校园自治案例和签约的法律常识。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009620" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009620-617655.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009620-617655-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009620" class="wp-caption-text">青年局侯佳龄局长勉励学生。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14009621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009621" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009621-617656.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009621-617656-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009621" class="wp-caption-text">法律白话文王鼎棫主编与学生分享学生自治知识。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>藉由分组，同学们也分享各校班联会的实际经验，包含如何筹办活动、增加学生代表、社群行销及和学校沟通等议题，同学们更在“热血备忘录”写下班联会成员的愿望，希望可以整合更多资源为更多同学们服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009622-617657.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009622-617657-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009622" class="wp-caption-text">班学联会学生分享讨论成果。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14009625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009625-617660.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009625-617660-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009625" class="wp-caption-text">活动抽奖。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>担任学校班联会主席的梁同学表示，校园发生问题不是只有对外表达意见而已，而是透过有效的沟通，才能让问题得以解决。而担任会长的杨同学也指出，参与公共事务可以帮助拓展学习经验，未来会兼顾学业和会长的工作，也希望未来能为学生权益努力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009623-617658.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009623-617658-600x352.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="352"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009623" class="wp-caption-text">班学联会学生分享讨论成果。。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14009624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009624-617659.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009624-617659-600x433.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="433"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009624" class="wp-caption-text">侯佳龄局长聆听班学联会学生分享。（桃园市青年局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?</p>
<p>桃园市青年事务局侯佳龄局长分享，过去服务的经验，常常严谨要求自己带着团队朝向更好的目标前进，期许班联会同学们带着过程中学到的宝贵经验，未来在社会上服务更多人，青年局将提供给资源，鼓励班联会办理跨校性与自主性发起的活动，持续推动学生参与公共事务。◇</p>
