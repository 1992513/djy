<figure id="attachment_14009705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009705-614357_medium.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009705-614357_medium-600x441.png" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009705" class="wp-caption-text">暗送馨香微醺意，饮酒赋诗乐开怀。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>紫藤属Wisteria豆科</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14009704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009704-614356_medium.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009704-614356_medium-600x439.png" alt="" width="600" b="439" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009704" class="wp-caption-text">紫藤属全株含有一种称为紫藤素（Wisterin）的皂素，尤其种子含量最多，如果误食可能造成头晕、恶心、困惑、言语障碍、呕吐、胃痛、腹泻甚至昏厥。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14009703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009703" style="width: 510px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009703-614355_medium.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14009703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009703-614355_medium.png" alt="" width="510" b="768" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009703" class="wp-caption-text">羽状复叶，叶互生，长15~35公分。花紫色、粉红色或白色，总状花序。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>紫藤属攀缘植物，全株都可能有毒性，尤以种子毒性最高。可攀爬高达20公尺，并向侧面延展10公尺。羽状复叶，叶互生，长15~35公分。花紫色、粉红色或白色，总状花序。紫藤属全株含有一种称为紫藤素（Wisterin）的皂素，尤其种子含量最多，如果误食可能造成头晕、恶心、困惑、言语障碍、呕吐、胃痛、腹泻甚至昏厥。原产于东亚与北美洲，是相当受欢迎的园艺植物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14009708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009708-614360_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009708-614360_medium-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009708" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14009707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009707-614359_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009707-614359_medium-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009707" class="wp-caption-text">世界上最大株的紫藤属植物是一株1894年栽种，位于美国加州马德雷的中国紫藤，全株延伸的面积超过一英亩。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14009706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14009706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009706-614358_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14009706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14009706-614358_medium-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14009706" class="wp-caption-text">原产于东亚与北美洲，是相当受欢迎的园艺植物。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>世界上最大株的紫藤属植物是一株1894年栽种，位于美国加州马德雷的中国紫藤，全株延伸的面积超过一英亩。</p>
