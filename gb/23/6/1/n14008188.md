<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：一片茫雾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14008225-shutterstock_704384086-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本 小豆岛天使之路(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><strong>2017年10月10日游日本小豆岛</strong></p>
<p>世界之大<br />
烟尘滚滚，扰扰嚷嚷<br />
何处得安详宁静<br />
我寻寻觅觅<br />
千里迢迢，越山过海……<br />
气喘吁吁，汗流浃背……</p>
<p>渺渺茫茫里<br />
惊见这一奇景：<br />
僻处海隅<br />
地小被称如豆<br />
少少的人口<br />
曲径通幽<br />
红绿灯几无<br />
疑似鸡犬相闻<br />
疑似与世无争<br />
屋宇多为木造<br />
随处可见橄榄树<br />
随处有着温泉<br />
仿佛上天在这里书写着<br />
安详宁静……</p>
<p>当今世上还有这样的地方？<br />
正自疑猜<br />
忽听背后有人呢喃：<br />
这里住着<br />
无怀氏之民与葛天氏之民<br />
猛回头想探个究竟<br />
却见眼前一片茫雾……</p>
<p><strong>All is a piece of the Blear Fog</strong>? ? ?<strong>Hsu ChiCheng</strong></p>
<p><strong>Toured to Shodoswhima Japan 2017/10/10</strong></p>
<p>So great is the world<br />
And is so surge the smoke and dust, so harass and confusion<br />
Where can we find peace and easy<br />
I search and search<br />
From a thousand li away, exceed the mountains, across the waters…<br />
Out of breath, soaked with sweat…</p>
<p>I view this wonderful sight<br />
In the vague：<br />
Be in a remote corner coastal areas and territorial seas<br />
It’s small likes a bean<br />
With little population<br />
The roads full of twists and turns<br />
It’s nearly short of traffic light<br />
One may doubt there are cocks chirp and dogs bark all together<br />
One may doubt there are no dispute with each other<br />
The houses almost made with woods<br />
There are olives everywhere<br />
There are hot springs everywhere<br />
It seems that God wrote there<br />
Where can we find peace and easy?</p>
<p>Is there still such a place in the world today?<br />
I just guess<br />
Suddenly there is a person twittering:<br />
There live the people of Wu Hei Shi and Ge Tain Shi<br />
I turn back to look the true<br />
But it’s a piece of the blear fog?@</p>
