<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>万神殿：联系上天与地球的舞台</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024556-1-shutterstock_1674588145a-1200x833-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>从罗通达城市广场 (Piazza della Rotonda) 放眼望去，典型的神庙立面造型映入眼帘，三角楣饰（pediment）（门楣或屋顶上方的装饰结构）位于正面一排八根石柱门廊（portico）上方。后方高耸着混凝土建造的巨型穹顶，穹顶内部由形似乐鼓的造型墙支撑，以厚砖和混凝土构成。(picturepixx/Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>近2千年来，万神殿（The Pantheon）给君王和人类产生了极大的影响，更是历史上最重要的古典建筑。万神殿座落于罗马市中心的罗通达城市广场（the Piazza della Rotonda），于二世纪时由罗马帝王哈德良（Emperor Hadrian）重建。</p>
<p>从广场放眼望去，典型的神庙立面映入眼帘。三角楣饰（pediment）位于正面一排八根石柱门廊（portico）上方。石柱的底座是大型大理石，柱顶是科林斯柱头（Corinthian capitals），而柱身则是以哈德良帝王从埃及进口的花岗岩建造。巨大的花岗岩石柱像树干般支撑着天篷（canopy），提供民众遮雨避暑，以及聚会、碰面或休憩的场所。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024557-2-shutterstock_154192568a-1200x800.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14024557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024557-2-shutterstock_154192568a-1200x800-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024557" class="wp-caption-text">立面石柱有大型的大理石底座，柱顶是科林斯柱头（Corinthian capitals），柱身则是以花岗岩制成。(Taras Vychyna/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14024558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024558" style="width: 396px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024558-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14024558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024558-3.jpg" alt="" width="396" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024558" class="wp-caption-text">巨大的花岗岩石柱像树干一样支撑着天篷，提供民众遮雨避暑，以及聚会、碰面或休憩的场所。(J.H. Smith/Cartiophotos)</figcaption></figure>
<p>正门入口后方高耸着一座混凝土建造的巨型穹顶。通过门廊石柱后，可见两扇巨大的青铜门通往室内。室内墙壁展示来自古罗马帝国各地深浅不一的大理石，彰显哈德良帝王的影响所及甚远。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024554" style="width: 609px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024554-1_11_Pantheon-exterior-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14024554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024554-1_11_Pantheon-exterior-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="609" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024554" class="wp-caption-text">正门入口后方高耸着一座混凝土建造的巨型穹顶。(Sean Pavone/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14024560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024560" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024560-7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14024560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024560-7-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024560" class="wp-caption-text">穿过石柱后，可见两扇巨大的青铜门（照片左边）通向室内。(J.H. Smith/Cartiophotos)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14024562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024562-shutterstock_1163853259.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14024562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024562-shutterstock_1163853259-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024562" class="wp-caption-text">室内墙壁展示古罗马帝国各地的深浅不一的大理石，显示哈德良帝王的影响深远。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>万神殿内部空间之宽阔令人赞叹。直径和高度皆是142英尺的球体，将室内空间呈现出完美的几何造型。圆形象征着精神的完美或神性，这样的概念反复出现在室内的设计。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024561-11.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14024561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024561-11-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024561" class="wp-caption-text">直径和高度皆是142英尺的球体，将室内空间呈现出完美的几何造型。圆形象征着精神的完美或神性，这样的概念反复出现在室内的设计。(J.H. Smith/Cartiophotos)</figcaption></figure>
<p>天花板向上延伸成一个巨大的穹顶，营造出宽阔的空间。穹顶顶端有一个直径27英尺的圆形天窗（oculus），方便阳光进入室内，雨水也能洒落至大理石地板上，借此强化与外部空间还有上方大气层的联系。</p>
<p>随着地球绕行太阳转动，太阳光透过圆形天窗射进一道光束，投射成一个圆形光影在室内移动，就好像舞台上的聚光灯，提醒我们身处人世间这个舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024555" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024555-1_11_pantheon-oculus-1200x800.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14024555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024555-1_11_pantheon-oculus-1200x800-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024555" class="wp-caption-text">穹顶的圆孔天窗就像舞台上的聚光灯，提醒我们身处人世间这个舞台。(Martijn Smeets/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>穹顶下的大厅可见许多石柱、壁龛。位于二楼环绕的窗户似乎跟外部的空间相通，让人犹如置身于城市广场的建筑物之中，不像是在室内的感觉。</p>
<p>广阔的空间唤起一种连结和完整的意涵。万神殿以供奉众神而建造，欢迎有信仰的人来到神的面前。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14024559" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14024559" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024559-6.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14024559" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14024559-6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14024559" class="wp-caption-text">万神殿前方的碑文提到，罗马将军兼政治家马库斯?阿格里帕（Marcus Agrippa）在更早以前曾于此处委托建造一座神庙，但后来遭焚毁。(J.H. Smith/Cartiophotos)</figcaption></figure>
