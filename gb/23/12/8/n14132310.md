<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园食农圣诞市集 认养非犬猫特殊宠物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132315-652052-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>天竺鼠。（桃园市动保处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月08日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%8A%82.md#1">圣诞节</a>要来啰！要不要来<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%A4%E5%85%BB.md#1">认养</a>可爱的特殊<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A0%E7%89%A9.md#1">宠物</a>（以下简称特宠）陪伴你度过这个圣诞呢？桃园市政府动物保护处的“非犬猫特殊宠物”认养活动将在12月16日下午1点办理，想成为兔爸爸、鸽妈妈、鹦鹉饲主的民众，欢迎携带国民身份证及运输笼，踊跃参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132311" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132311-652048.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14132311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132311-652048-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132311" class="wp-caption-text">球蟒。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民众饲养的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A0%E7%89%A9.md#1">宠物</a>以犬猫为大宗，而饲养非犬猫的宠物（如：兔子、天竺鼠等）也有逐年上升的趋势。近几年来由动保处收容民众弃养或走失的非犬猫宠物数不胜数，若经公告后仍无饲主领回，则以不定期办理<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%A4%E5%85%BB.md#1">认养</a>活动的方式，让非犬猫宠物能有完善的照养生活环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132312" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132312-652049.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14132312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132312-652049-600x797.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="598" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132312" class="wp-caption-text">蜜袋鼯。（桃园市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14132313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132313" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132313-652050.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14132313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132313-652050.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="975" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132313" class="wp-caption-text">鹌鹑。（桃园市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>预计于2023年12月16日下午1点于桃园市立图书馆新总馆（桃园市桃园区南平路303号）办理的非犬猫特殊宠物认养活动，包括垂耳兔、蜜袋鼯、鹌鹑、鹦鹉及天竺鼠等，共计23只动物开放认养。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132316-652053.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14132316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132316-652053-600x797.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="598" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132316" class="wp-caption-text">吸蜜鹦鹉。（桃园市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14132314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132314" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132314-652051.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132314-652051-600x277.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132314" class="wp-caption-text">天竺鼠。（桃园市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>动物保护处强调，有意认养之民众除需设籍桃园市并年满18岁，也会要求拥有饲养特殊宠物的能力，希望想饲养的民众应谨慎评估自身能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132317-652054.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14132317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132317-652054.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="974" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132317" class="wp-caption-text">折衷鹦鹉。（桃园市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>认养成功的市民必须参加1小时的生命教育课程，课程内容包括如何饲养、照护特殊宠物，如饲料、饮水，以及环境与卫生管理及异常状况的注意事项及护理工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132318-652055.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14132318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132318-652055.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="975" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132318" class="wp-caption-text">和尚鹦鹉。（桃园市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动详情可参考桃园动保处官方网站<ahref="https://animal.tycg.gov.tw/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://animal.tycg.gov.tw/</a>）或脸书粉丝专页，并办理认养作业，让我们一起关注动物保护，为这个社会带来更多正能量！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132319-652056.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14132319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132319-652056.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="975" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132319" class="wp-caption-text">金太阳鹦鹉。（桃园市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14132320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132320-652057.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14132320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132320-652057-600x277.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132320" class="wp-caption-text">垂耳兔。（桃园市动保处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市动物保护处欢迎民众共同踊跃参与，携手扎根生命教育，爱护及守护动物，共创友善对待动物的环境——“懂它再养它，终养不弃养”。◇</p>
