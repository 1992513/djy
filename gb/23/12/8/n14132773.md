<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】日本关东煮 在家轻松做</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132785-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本关东煮，一锅到底方便烹饪，是忙碌现代人的懒人料理。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>前言：</strong>关东煮（oden）是日本的大众料理，“关东煮”为关西人给这种料理的名称。材料通常包括鱼饼、煮鸡蛋、萝卜、蒟蒻、海带结、蒟蒻丝等，这些材料会放在昆布或者鲣鱼汤里煮。可以用来配饭，也可以当作小吃来吃。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">日本关东煮</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>日本鱼饼1包<br />
大白菜12 oz<br />
豆腐1盒<br />
红萝卜3 oz<br />
水3杯<br />
葱段2条</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132780" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132780-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14132780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132780-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132780" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>关东煮汤包1个<br />
烹大师1茶匙<br />
盐1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132779" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132779" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132779-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14132779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132779-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132779" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 大白菜洗干净后切大块，豆腐切小块，红萝卜切小片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132778" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132778-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14132778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132778-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132778" class="wp-caption-text">大白菜洗干净后切大块，豆腐切小块，红萝卜切小片。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 汤锅内放入3杯水，放入大白菜、红萝卜、豆腐、汤包和其它调味，煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132782" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132782-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14132782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132782-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132782" class="wp-caption-text">汤锅内放入3杯水，放入大白菜、红萝卜、豆腐、汤包和其它调味，煮5分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 接着再放入鱼饼，多煮5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132783" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132783" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132783-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14132783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132783-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132783" class="wp-caption-text">接着再放入鱼饼，多煮5分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 最后放入少许葱段即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132784" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132784-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14132784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132784-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132784" class="wp-caption-text">最后放入少许葱段即完成。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
