<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】蟹肉炒蛋白鲜奶 滑嫩鲜香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133055-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>以脆滋滋的锅巴和新鲜烫蔬菜为基底，上面摆盘炒蛋白鲜奶和蟹肉的融合，口感香酥滑嫩，多层次口感让人味蕾很满足。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>“<strong>蟹肉炒蛋白鲜奶”是一</strong>道创意港式料理，使用蛋白和鲜奶作为烩炒食材，融合蟹肉的鲜味，品尝时Ｑ软带劲的滑嫩口感，是多层次风味的菜谱，相当适合作为宴客料理。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">蟹肉炒蛋白鲜奶</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>鲜蟹肉4安士<br />
蛋白6个（另外留1个蛋黄）<br />
忌廉奶1/4杯<br />
鲜奶1/2杯<br />
菜心适量<br />
锅巴4块</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133045" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133045-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133045-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133045" class="wp-caption-text">材料。(梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14133046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133046" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133046-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133046-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133046" class="wp-caption-text">菜心、锅巴。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>盐1/4茶匙<br />
烹大师1/4荼匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
粟粉3茶匙（用少许水开匀）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133047" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133047-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133047-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133047" class="wp-caption-text">调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 蛋白打散，去掉泡沫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133048" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133048-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133048-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133048" class="wp-caption-text">蛋白打散，去掉泡沫。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 锅中加入鲜奶与忌廉奶，隔水加热。加入调味料和蛋白拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133049" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133049-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133049-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133049" class="wp-caption-text">锅中加入鲜奶与忌廉奶，隔水加热。加入调味料和蛋白拌匀。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 锅巴炸好放盘子中央。菜心用油盐水烫熟围在盘边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133051" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133051-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133051-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133051" class="wp-caption-text">锅巴炸好放盘子中央。菜心用油盐水烫熟围在盘边。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 鲜蟹肉用热水浸过后沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133052" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133052-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133052-7-450x235.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="235" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133052" class="wp-caption-text">鲜蟹肉用热水浸过后沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 锅中加少许油，转中火，倒入鲜奶蛋白炒匀，不要太熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133053" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133053-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133053-8-450x189.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="189" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133053" class="wp-caption-text">锅中加少许油，转中火，倒入鲜奶蛋白炒匀，不要太熟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 加入蟹肉至蛋奶凝结，便可装盘，放上蟹钳及蛋黄在中央。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133054" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133054" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133054-9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133054-9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133054" class="wp-caption-text">加入蟹肉至蛋奶凝结，便可装盘，放上蟹钳及蛋黄在中央。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h3>
<p>鲜奶加忌廉奶能让牛奶容易凝结。而隔水加热让牛奶温度升高，炒的时候较快凝聚，吃起来更嫩滑。◇</p>
