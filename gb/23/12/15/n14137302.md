<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】黑糖姜汁烤葡萄柚</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137306-19add4200cc7e6f35b4def87e65f0fd8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>糖烤葡萄柚是冬日的舒适小食，温暖的酸甜滋味让人难忘。（李若琳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月15日讯】大家喜欢吃烤橘子吗？今天来尝一尝烤葡萄柚吧。葡萄柚也是柑橘属水果，果肉细腻、多汁，加糖烤热了吃，酸甜好滋味堪称经典。</p>
<p>这里介绍烤葡萄柚的三种方法：微波炉、气炸锅和灸烤（broil）。其中，微波炉需要的时间最短，果肉加热最充分、糖浆入味。用传统的灸烤方法，果肉则多了几分焦香。气炸锅版本与日常烤水果一样，果肉的风味取决于烘烤时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137308-a60cf3ebcb717b1e34f39859218397bf.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14137308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137308-a60cf3ebcb717b1e34f39859218397bf-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137308" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备：</h2>
<p>1个红肉葡萄柚，star grapefruit（甜酸）或者deep red（多汁）</p>
<p>1汤匙黑糖</p>
<p>1茶匙新鲜姜</p>
<h2><strong>料理贴士：</strong></h2>
<p>食材中的黑糖可换用棕糖、生糖，新鲜姜可换用姜粉。这里使用黑糖和新鲜姜汁。</p>
<p>葡萄柚加热前需要切开，方便糖浆入味和享用。切葡萄柚的方法：先对半切，挑出可见的果核。接着，用水果刀沿着囊衣划开每一瓣果肉，再以斜角沿果皮环切一圈，使每瓣果肉脱离。切的时候注意不要切穿果皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137309-d6e72a9a66a5ab9fd5aed18a810d20e2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137309-d6e72a9a66a5ab9fd5aed18a810d20e2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137309" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137310-f28f08f0734b3f78cfbd58feb7db0439.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137310-f28f08f0734b3f78cfbd58feb7db0439-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137310" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137312-cafb69830c00316d0b96ddde4a5ce064.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137312-cafb69830c00316d0b96ddde4a5ce064-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137312" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，若葡萄柚不能平放，用刀切掉底部一片果皮，但不要切到果肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137313" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137313-2573eaf19f03b5d1c31ddb4d3c1be7f9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137313-2573eaf19f03b5d1c31ddb4d3c1be7f9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137313" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>切好后，用压蒜器挤压新鲜姜块，把姜汁直接淋在果肉上，然后撒上黑糖。待黑糖基本溶于果汁中即可加热。吃的时候，用餐勺轻轻一刮，果肉就下来了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137314" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137314-992f0c842336c5f35f4de39609521b42.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137314-992f0c842336c5f35f4de39609521b42-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137314" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137316-4aabb687435c98a27653843c06ceb8dd.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137316-4aabb687435c98a27653843c06ceb8dd-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137316" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14137317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137317-fed15d94fea455c42ea6b17a5014fe19.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137317-fed15d94fea455c42ea6b17a5014fe19-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137317" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以下是黑糖姜汁葡萄柚的三种做法，加热方式不同，风味各异。实际的烹煮时间可以根据自己喜欢的风味增减。</p>
<h2>微波炉做法：</h2>
<p>1.准备好的葡萄柚放入微波炉安全碗中。微波炉（950W）加热1分30秒。<br />
2.不想要果肉煮太软，加热1分钟使表面的糖融化就可以。<br />
气炸锅做法：</p>
<p>1.气炸时果汁容易外溢，可以在撒糖之前喝掉一些果汁或将水果放入容器气炸。<br />
2.以400华氏度气炸。个头小的葡萄柚，炸10分钟中间果肉就热了。对于大号葡萄柚或想烤熟果肉，可以多烤几分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137318-f2f0bb7aa6c3ba6cf253a25478fc8c6c.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137318-f2f0bb7aa6c3ba6cf253a25478fc8c6c-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137318" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>灸烤做法：</h2>
<p>1.将烤架放在顺数第二层的位置，离开顶部火源一段距离，以避免果皮烧焦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137321-ceac475b116af656062a4e24cd2cad30.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137321-ceac475b116af656062a4e24cd2cad30-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137321" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.葡萄柚放入烤盘，高火灸烤12分钟。若使用小型电烤箱灸烤，做法相同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14137322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14137322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137322-8552721eb82dfdf59f8fec5715a4c077.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14137322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14137322-8552721eb82dfdf59f8fec5715a4c077-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14137322" class="wp-caption-text">（李若琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
