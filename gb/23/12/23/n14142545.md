<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2023诸罗山杯国际软式少棒赛开幕 258队参赛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142546-655128-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>本届参赛队伍数创下新高。（空拍）（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月23日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）第26届诸罗山杯国际软式少年棒球邀请赛开幕典礼于今（23）日在嘉义市立棒球场隆重举行，典礼在博爱国小管乐队及民生国中热舞社等精彩表演中展开，为接下来六天的鏖战揭开序幕，人文城市管乐之都体育赛事也都是参与国际性城市交流时结交的好邦交队伍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142547" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142547-655129-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142547-655129-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142547" class="wp-caption-text">博爱国小开场演出。（空拍）（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于疫情解封，今年外国球队计有日本爱媛、日本茨城、iBA东西、东北宫城、香港纯阳、香港观塘、深水埗、韩国JEONBUK、韩国JEONNAM、BCS新加坡及日侨雷公队等11个队伍跨海参赛，国际上的赛事能见度大幅提升，本届刷新纪录，共有258队参赛，含高年级192队、低年级66队，报名非常踊跃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142548" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142548-655130-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142548-655130-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142548" class="wp-caption-text">副市长林瑞彦致词。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今日开幕典礼由林瑞彦副市长代表黄敏惠市长出席，为来自世界各地选手送上鼓励，并为赛事开球点燃战火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142549" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142549-655131-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142549-655131-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142549" class="wp-caption-text">博爱国小管乐团队大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林瑞彦副市长表示，本届赛事在各界期盼中隆重登场，将于嘉义县市13面球场分别举行，市府会用最大的热情来接待来自国内、外的选手菁英与各地乡亲。本次邀请赛日本爱媛县观光体育文化部文化局森居基修局长等人特别率队远道而来参与，爱媛县为庆祝台日棒球交流一百周年纪念，目前更于爱媛县“少爷剧场(BOTCHAN THEATER)”以“KANO”嘉义农林学校棒球队为主题，演出有关该球队教练近藤兵太郎（爱媛县松山市出生）之舞台剧，也在今年与嘉义市缔结交流促进备忘录，期待未来透过文化、体育作深化交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142550" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142550-655132-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142550-655132-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142550" class="wp-caption-text">艺术家曾琡棻（中）现场指导大家跳火肌舞。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育处处长郭添财表示，本届诸罗山杯国际软式少年棒球邀请赛特别邀请嘉义在地棒球菁英─中华职棒的乐天桃猿队廖健富选手担任代言人，廖选手从博爱国小少棒队、民生国中青少棒队扎下根基，曾于金龙杯全国青棒菁英赛中缴出0.895的打击率拿下打击奖，也参与2016年亚洲青棒锦标赛，并获选为赛会最佳十人（捕手），更于2017年中华职棒季中选秀会获得Lamigo桃猿队第一指名，并参与2023年亚洲棒球锦标赛，成为参加诸罗山杯小选手们的优秀楷模。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142551" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142551-655133-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142551-655133-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142551" class="wp-caption-text">去年高年级冠军，将冠军旗缴回。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育处也特别再次邀请各地民众来到现场观赛给予小选手们鼓励，体验当年KANO永不放弃的精神，顺道逛逛具有人文气息的嘉义市，领略人文城市的风貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142552" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142552-655134-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142552-655134-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142552" class="wp-caption-text">去年低年级冠军，将冠军旗缴回。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>火肌舞指导老师艺术家曾琡棻（着粉红衣）与副市长等人合照。（记者李撷璎／摄影</p>
