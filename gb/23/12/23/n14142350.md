<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>6种实用的床头柜设计 睡觉更加安稳舒适</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142610-shutterstock_2293400029-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在床边打造机能型的床头柜，将空间运用到极致。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>说起床头柜，不禁让人联想到传统的床边小立柜，方便我们随手拿取乳液、茶杯、眼罩或睡前读物等等。如果你需要更多机能，将床头空间运用到极致，让房间看起来更加轻盈，那么不一定只限于使用现成的家具。</p>
<p>从视觉角度来看，床头板和床头柜可以起到心理安慰作用，让人感觉更加安稳舒适。我们也可以请室内设计师、木工师傅或系统柜公司打造自己专属的床头柜。下面来看看床头柜怎么规划最实用～</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>一、床头柜高于床铺，睡眠更安定</strong></span></h2>
<p>比较浅眠的人听到一点点动静都可能睡不好，或者有的人习惯蜷曲着侧睡，似乎需要更多安全感；为了让睡眠品质更好，不妨选择包覆式造型的床头板——左右二侧弯曲延伸到床边。</p>
<p>如果你的床头板是简易的造型，那么让床头柜高度高过于床铺，也是提升安定感的好办法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142612" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142612-shutterstock_2213008463.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142612-shutterstock_2213008463-600x778.jpg" alt="Mockup Frame In Cozy Bedroom Interior Background 3d Render" width="600" b="778" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142612" class="wp-caption-text">简单、轻便的床边桌高度超过床铺，睡觉时更有安全感。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142615" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142615-shutterstock_2257332201.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142615-shutterstock_2257332201-600x400.jpg" alt="Burning Candles And Lamp On End Table In Bedroom Near" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142615" class="wp-caption-text">在高脚、小巧俐落的床边桌上装饰小灯和香氛，夜晚有个好眠。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>二、悬浮、壁挂式层架</strong></span></h2>
<p>这是极简风格的设计，不会占用地板空间，还能提供充足的收纳空间。你可以装置在任何喜爱的高度，也能当做小桌子来使用，让房间看起来更加轻盈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142625-shutterstock_2004946460.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142625-shutterstock_2004946460-600x400.jpg" alt="Bedroom With Modern Decor Including Large Bed Floating Nightstands And" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142625" class="wp-caption-text">悬浮式床头柜让房间看起来更开阔。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142626" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142626-shutterstock_2396057341.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142626-shutterstock_2396057341-600x400.jpg" alt="Luxury Hotel Bedroom Interior With Bed And Nightstand On Grey" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142626" class="wp-caption-text">悬浮式床头柜搭配床头板很有风格。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142654-shutterstock_1870587415.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142654-shutterstock_1870587415-600x400.jpg" alt="Small And Charming Bedroom With Comfortable And Decorated Bed Window" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142654" class="wp-caption-text">将层板钉在墙面上也能当床头柜使用。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>三、结合橱柜与墙面</strong></span></h2>
<p>卧室床头柜就像客厅的主墙设计，墙面、柜体的色调和质感如果相互映衬、搭配，房间将更有个人特色风格。壁柜的设计能扩充收纳机能，如果加装柜门的话能减少杂乱或压力感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142657-shutterstock_2260047037.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142657-shutterstock_2260047037-600x338.jpg" alt="Modern Bedroom With Wooden Headboard In White And Yellow Tones" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142657" class="wp-caption-text">床头柜连结壁柜，房间设计更有整体感。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>四、梳妆台和书桌功能的床头柜</strong></span></h2>
<p>我们在房间里同时放置书桌和化妆桌会占去较多空间，如果将床头柜结合小书桌或梳妆台功能使用，能让你的卧室看起来更宽敞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142671-shutterstock_2223304133.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142671-shutterstock_2223304133-600x400.jpg" alt="Wooden Bedroom Interior With Bed And Dresser With Mirror Work" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142671" class="wp-caption-text">床头柜结合更多用途，减少家具摆设更宽敞。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>五、结合内嵌式置物柜</strong></span></h2>
<p>在床头后方或侧边设计内嵌式置物柜，可以摆放香氛、盆栽、书籍、手机等许多小物品，这样在床上拿取东西好方便，同时也增加了卧室的线条感和空间感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142675" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142675" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142675-shutterstock_2317706437.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142675-shutterstock_2317706437-600x400.jpg" alt="3d Illustration Of High-end Luxury And Stylish Apartment Interior Architecture" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142675" class="wp-caption-text">内嵌式壁柜用做床头柜，房间看起来更整洁。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>六、落地层架当作床头柜</strong></span></h2>
<p>我们可以灵活运用落地式层架来做床头设计，落地式层架不仅可以当书架、收纳柜，还可以空出其中一层来做床头边柜使用，如果你喜欢也能展示收藏品，实用和艺术功用兼具。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142678" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142678-shutterstock_2393854681.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142678-shutterstock_2393854681-600x400.jpg" alt="Interior Of Bedroom With Big Bed And Lamps" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142678" class="wp-caption-text">深度较浅的直立式层柜，也适合用做床边柜。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142680" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142680-shutterstock_2221859787.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14142680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142680-shutterstock_2221859787-600x400.jpg" alt="Big Bed With Soft Pillows In Light Bedroom" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142680" class="wp-caption-text">开放式层架可以有多种用途。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
