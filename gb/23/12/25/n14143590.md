<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南投山区梅花盛放 信义与水里提早传来花讯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143592-655490-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>自12月中旬起，南投山区陆续梅花花讯传出。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月25日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾南投报导）南投信义乡和水里乡的山区，冷空气接力报到，湿冷天气为赏梅季节揭开序幕。陈董梅园主人表示，受前一波湿冷天气影响，梅树已现点点花芽，部分早开梅树更零星绽放花朵，预告赏梅季即将到来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143591" style="width: 532px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143591-655489.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14143591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143591-655489-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="532" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143591" class="wp-caption-text">南投山区梅花盛放，信义与水里提早传来花讯。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自12月中旬起，南投山区陆续梅花花讯传出。梅园主人指出，梅花可能提前开放的原因之一是受到去年4、5月份的干旱影响，梅树为了顺利繁衍可能自我调节提前开花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143593" style="width: 536px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143593-655491.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14143593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143593-655491-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="536" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143593" class="wp-caption-text">水里乡的陈董梅园中，已有许多梅树提前开花，展现天寒“梅”景。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>受东北季风低温和冷空气的影响，南投山区梅树状态良好，信义乡的乌松崙石家梅园和水里乡的陈董梅园中，已有许多梅树提前开花，展现天寒“梅”景。然而，大多数梅树仍在默默吐露花芽，预计从花芽到花开需约20天。考虑到未来天气将持续转凉，预计信义与水里的梅花将在明年元旦左右盛大绽放，目前已经开放2成，这波花期可能会延长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143595" style="width: 569px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143595-655494.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14143595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143595-655494-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="569" b="320" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143595" class="wp-caption-text">陈董梅园空拍景色，期待满开的时候。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冷冽的空气中，老树白梅绽放，梅花晶莹剔透，为冬日增添一抹新希望的祝福。南投山区的梅园成为冬日必访景点，游客可在清新自然美丽的环境中赏梅拍照，感受乡村的趣味。呼吁大众爱护这片免费开放的梅园，共同享受赏梅的愉悦时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143594" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143594-655492.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14143594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143594-655492-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143594" class="wp-caption-text">老树白梅绽放，梅花晶莹剔透，为冬日增添一抹新希望的祝福。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
