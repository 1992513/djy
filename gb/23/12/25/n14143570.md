<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宝来白雪木盛开 白梅接力报到</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143571-654226-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>隐身六龟宝来山峦上的白雪木秘境，近年是冬季旅游赏花必追的热门景色。（茂林国家风景区管理处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月25日讯】（大纪元记者方金媛台湾高雄报导）每逢圣诞节前夕，正是白雪木盛开的花季，如积雪般的白色花海点缀着六龟宝来竹林休闲农业园区山野间，同时还有12月分接续绽放的梅花同框，山野景色犹如人间仙境，吸引各县市游客上山追这片动人花景，享受山城冬季浪漫气氛。</p>
<p>“白雪木”俗称圣诞白雪，在台湾因细小雪白色的苞叶形似白雪花而得名，全球各地广泛称为“圣诞初雪”，在日本又有“白雪公主”之称，宛如不会融化的白雪梦幻美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143575" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143575-654232-450x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14143575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143575-654232-450x338.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143575" class="wp-caption-text">隐身六龟宝来山峦上的白雪木秘境，近年是冬季旅游赏花必追的热门景色。（茂林国家风景区提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14143574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143574" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143574-654231-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14143574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143574-654231-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143574" class="wp-caption-text">六龟宝来季节限定花景，圣诞白雪木，如积雪般梦幻。（茂林国家风景区 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>茂林国家风景区管理处处长柯建兴表示，沿着竹林休闲农业区三叉工坊山中小径可欣赏白雪木，茂密的白雪木树丛开满山腰，就像走进白茫茫的另类“雪景”里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143580" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143580-654290-450x339.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14143580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143580-654290-450x339.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143580" class="wp-caption-text">宝来赏梅景点赖家古厝周遭梅树环抱。（茂林国家风景区提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>梅花1月接续绽放</h2>
<p>竹林休闲农业园区的梅园占地约200公顷种植超过万棵梅树，每年1月将是赏梅最佳时机，山野景色犹如人间仙境，别错过道地特产梅子料理，采用宝来盛产的梅子入菜为最大特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14143582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143582" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143582-654292-450x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14143582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143582-654292-450x338.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143582" class="wp-caption-text">宝来热门赏梅胜地接续盛开，梅园花开约1成，每年12月底至1月都是观赏梅花的最好时节。（茂林国家风景区提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14143581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14143581" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143581-654291-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14143581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14143581-654291-450x337.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14143581" class="wp-caption-text">宝来热门赏梅胜地接续盛开，梅园花开约1成，每年12月底至1月都是观赏梅花的最好时节。（茂林国家风景区提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此时正是泡汤好时节，六龟城区是高雄首屈一指的温泉胜地，欣赏完白雪木花海后，可顺道来泡宝来、不老著名的“美人汤”，让温暖汤泉浸润全身，彻底洗涤疲惫的身心灵。◇</p>
