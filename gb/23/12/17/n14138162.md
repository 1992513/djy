<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>空手道好手勇夺全国亚军 虎虎生风耀光复</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138163-653792-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>17日在云林县虎尾国小举办全国空手道比赛。（黄有智提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）就读云林虎尾镇光复国小三年级的凯杰，17日在虎尾国小大礼堂举办的全国空手道比赛中，展现卓越实力，成功摘得国小三年级男童组“型”的银牌，为光复国小争光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138164" style="width: 511px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138164-653793-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138164-653793-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="511" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138164" class="wp-caption-text">全国空手道赛事激烈，选手们都希望拿到最佳成绩。（黄有智提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自一年级新生时，凯杰就以优异表现一鸣惊人，在空手道比赛中屡次取得佳绩。校长黄有智表示，凯杰的杰出成绩早已激发学校对空手道的重视，并在17日比赛中再度展现实力，荣获全国亚军。黄校长强调，这不仅是一面奖牌，更是对凯杰多年来努力与付出的最佳肯定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138167" style="width: 522px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138167-653796-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138167-653796-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="522" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138167" class="wp-caption-text">黄有智校长表示:人生路远，努力夺金，不是唯一价值，只希望凯杰身心健康、平安。（黄有智提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138166" style="width: 441px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138166-653795-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138166-653795-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="441" b="588" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138166" class="wp-caption-text">自一年级新生时，凯杰就以优异表现一鸣惊人，在空手道比赛中屡次取得佳绩。（黄有智提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>空手道之路充满挑战，需要体能和意志的双重锻炼，但凯杰坚持努力奋斗，一周自主练习五天，追逐金牌的同时，也注重身心的健康。校方强调，无论比赛结果如何，凯杰的付出与努力已经成为光复国小的骄傲。人生路漫漫，而夺金不是唯一的价值，学习过程中的成长与收获同样重要。</p>
<p>光复国小期望凯杰能够继续保持身心健康，展现更多优异的表现。他们相信，在师长的鼓励和家长的陪伴下，凯杰的潜能将继续被激发，为学校赢得更多光荣。这一次的成就，是对凯杰努力的最好回馈，也成为光复国小耀眼光辉的一道风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138165" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138165-653794-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138165-653794-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="498" b="374" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138165" class="wp-caption-text">空手道之路充满挑战，需要体能和意志的双重锻炼。（黄有智提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138168" style="width: 493px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138168-653797-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138168-653797-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="493" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138168" class="wp-caption-text">在师长的鼓励和家长的陪伴下，凯杰的潜能将继续被激发。（黄有智提供）</figcaption></figure>
