<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>杉林溪森林园区“冬韵盛宴” 银杏湖美极了</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138221-653828-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冬天云海翻涌 多层次颜色极致美景。（爱上杉林溪提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾南投报导）南投杉林溪森林园区的冬意正浓，银杏湖边落羽松梦幻的溪流倒影，还有水杉枯木映衬蓝天、叶牡丹及陆莲花盛开中，游客务必趁最佳观赏期，一同沉浸在冬日的浪漫之中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138223-653830.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14138223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138223-653830-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138223" class="wp-caption-text">杉林溪银杏湖边落羽松梦幻的溪流倒影。（爱上杉林溪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138222-653829.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14138222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138222-653829-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138222" class="wp-caption-text">杉林溪的叶牡丹及陆莲花盛开中。（爱上杉林溪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>全新打造的银杏湖 让冬天更灿烂梦幻</h2>
<p>园区的银杏湖由1332万元投资打造，种植了130棵大小银杏，最大的树龄高达百年。湖畔广植银杏树，另一侧则是水杉林，构成层层叠叠的彩色意境。在冷风的吹抚下，银杏湖换上枯黄落羽松的衣裳，成为园区最瞩目的景点之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138224-653831.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14138224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138224-653831-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138224" class="wp-caption-text">银杏湖的水杉枯木映衬蓝天。（爱上杉林溪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138227-653834.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14138227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138227-653834-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138227" class="wp-caption-text">台湾南投山林之美，值得游客专程踏访。（爱上杉林溪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>深秋交织的美丽? 梦幻的溪流倒影</h2>
<p>湖畔的景色缤纷交错，树影倒映在梦幻的溪流中宛如画中世界。水杉转枯，与银杏湖水相得益彰，湖光倒映之下更显美不胜收。游客漫步在步道上，仿佛置身异国风情，感受冬天浪漫情怀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138226-653833.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14138226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138226-653833-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138226" class="wp-caption-text">游客漫步在杉林溪步道上，仿佛置身异国风情，感受冬天浪漫情怀。（爱上杉林溪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>冬天云海翻涌 多层次颜色极致美景</h2>
<p>网友感叹“湖畔旁点缀着缤纷色彩，画面美的让人着迷”，纷纷被这片冬色美景所陶醉，杉林溪冬季的云海当然也不能错过，台湾南投山林之美，值得游客专程踏访。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138225-653832.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14138225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138225-653832-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138225" class="wp-caption-text">网友感叹“湖畔旁点缀着缤纷色彩，画面美的让人着迷”。（爱上杉林溪提供）</figcaption></figure>
