<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】料理剩食火鸡肉～九个食谱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146460-f935d39050a415c843b807310d5a1229-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>料理剩火鸡肉可以参照鸡肉食谱，做法包括沙拉、肉丸、马芬、砂锅菜等。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月29日讯】圣诞节大餐过后又是新年，准备新食谱的同时，还要处理剩菜。今天分享九个易作食谱，料理过节剩下的火鸡肉、土豆和抱子甘蓝等食材。食谱中的火鸡肉可以换用烤鸡胸肉。</p>
<h2>一、意大利香醋拌苤蓝</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146462-0174339669a8c345f3c98df32406d626.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146462-0174339669a8c345f3c98df32406d626-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146462" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2杯苤蓝（又称大头菜），去皮切幼条</p>
<p>1/2杯鸡胸肉丝</p>
<p>1/4杯生花生</p>
<p>1汤匙意大利香醋（balsamic vinegar）</p>
<p>1/2茶匙苹果醋</p>
<p>帕马森起司（Parmesan）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146463-75eb2a4601df02ecca2b466fe479614d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146463-75eb2a4601df02ecca2b466fe479614d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146463" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1. 花生洗净，蒸5分钟（蒸烤箱则以400华氏度蒸5分钟）。放至室温加醋拌匀。<br />
2. 鸡丝与苤蓝一起蒸3分钟（蒸烤箱则以400华氏度蒸5分钟）。短时间蒸煮的苤蓝甜而脆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146465" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146465-a02d47d47869fb2342b85ae1552bfdda.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146465-a02d47d47869fb2342b85ae1552bfdda-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146465" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 出锅的苤蓝拌入油醋汁，撒上现磨的帕马森起司和醋渍花生就可以享用了。</p>
<h2>二、芜菁叶炒鸡丝</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146467" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146467-d325f852bb6bf761fc33dd8597cbbae5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146467-d325f852bb6bf761fc33dd8597cbbae5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146467" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食材准备</h3>
<p>250克芜菁叶，切段</p>
<p>150克鸡肉丝</p>
<p>1/4杯郫县豆瓣酱</p>
<p>3汤匙无糖苹果酱</p>
<p>1杯鸡高汤</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146470-8c88b1e5d3a68ea3c923472fbe3c8daa.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146470-8c88b1e5d3a68ea3c923472fbe3c8daa-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146470" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 选用带脂肪、鸡皮的火鸡肉，切成丝。<br />
2. 锅中放油炒香豆瓣酱。先加入芜菁叶柄翻炒，再加入叶子。<br />
3. 蔬菜炒软，加入火鸡肉翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146472-6a33dfdd6d48d90431930489e10971af.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146472-6a33dfdd6d48d90431930489e10971af-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146472" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 添加高汤上盖煮10分钟。出锅前拌入苹果酱。</p>
<h2>三、虫草花鸡汤</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146481-b916efbce2d3f8a3c2b57ed8cab660c9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146481-b916efbce2d3f8a3c2b57ed8cab660c9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146481" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>准备食材</h3>
<p>1/4杯虫草花</p>
<p>2.5杯无盐鸡高汤</p>
<p>1个鸡蛋，打散</p>
<p>芡粉水，2汤匙玉米淀粉混合3汤匙水</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146483-985b915ca233067e4f40875f8f9e30b5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146483-985b915ca233067e4f40875f8f9e30b5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146483" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1. 煮沸高汤，加入虫草花、鸡丝。煮沸后上盖小火煮10分钟，放盐、芡粉水调味。<br />
2. 关火淋入蛋液。静置1分钟盛盘。</p>
<h2>四、微波炉烤土豆</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146484" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146484-8005c46fc4c3667f8b2fe912d2a05e7f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146484-8005c46fc4c3667f8b2fe912d2a05e7f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146484" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1个褐皮土豆，半磅</p>
<p>意大利辣香肠片</p>
<p>适量鸡胸肉丝、芥末酱、切达起司、马苏里拉起司、有盐草饲黄油（Grass-fed butter）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146487-9e05fbc3adc12118eb9585a5cbe8c309.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146487-9e05fbc3adc12118eb9585a5cbe8c309-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146487" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 用叉子在土豆上戳些洞，放入带盖容器，微波炉（950W）加热5分钟，翻面再加热3分钟。<br />
2. 取叉子插入土豆，能轻松插入说明熟了，然后留在微波炉内静置3分钟。<br />
3. 用叉子在土豆中间剥开一条缝，刮松土豆泥，铺上黄油片。鸡丝混合芥末酱放入，再加起司，放入烤箱烤至起司融化。</p>
<h2>五、青酱抱子甘蓝</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146488-d03978e5b467186c8bcc5ddcba5ba9ff.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146488-d03978e5b467186c8bcc5ddcba5ba9ff-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146488" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食材准备</h3>
<p>1杯切成丝的抱子甘蓝</p>
<p>1个青辣椒</p>
<p>2汤匙市售意面青酱（pesto sauce）</p>
<p>1汤匙初榨橄榄油</p>
<p>1/2杯鸡胸肉丝</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146489-05baf015d6c77c944dee347af0a1a32f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146489-05baf015d6c77c944dee347af0a1a32f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146489" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1. 鸡丝与橄榄油、青酱拌匀。<br />
2. 抱子甘蓝和青辣椒切成丝，拌匀，用不沾锅炒3分钟，添加鸡丝再炒1分钟出锅。</p>
<h2>六、XO酱蛋羹</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146492" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146492-2652b706c15c7a46f73bd041dc26e8d9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146492-2652b706c15c7a46f73bd041dc26e8d9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146492" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1个大号鸡蛋，打散</p>
<p>1/2杯鸡高汤，微波炉加热30秒至不烫手</p>
<p>1汤匙鸡肉碎</p>
<p>适量XO酱</p>
<p>1. 冰箱取出鸡蛋混合盐，用小勺打散。加入鸡汤，用勺子贴碗底顺时针搅拌至融合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146494" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146494-e4c631f86f6a04f4718e04b23865a831.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146494-e4c631f86f6a04f4718e04b23865a831-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146494" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 取薄而浅的小碗，铺垫鸡丝，倒入蛋液。<br />
3. 中火蒸1分钟待蒸汽上升，改小火蒸8分钟。搭配XO酱享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146495" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146495-a2eaf5c84fa9fd87d1058587294f9469.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146495-a2eaf5c84fa9fd87d1058587294f9469-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146495" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>七、葱椒鸡酥饼</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146496" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146496-661b9d96b896e40a0bcf5145cf2f728f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146496-661b9d96b896e40a0bcf5145cf2f728f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146496" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食材准备</h3>
<p>1盒市售酥皮（puff pastry sheets），2张可做12个酥饼</p>
<p>2杯鸡丝，蒸5分钟</p>
<p>1/2汤匙四川乾花椒</p>
<p>1/4杯切碎的葱白</p>
<p>1/8茶匙盐</p>
<p>1汤匙芝麻香油</p>
<p>1汤匙生抽</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146497" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146497-c0831329d5ac2f095c69f1929c9693ce.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146497-c0831329d5ac2f095c69f1929c9693ce-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146497" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1. 花椒冲洗，浸泡片刻。香葱取中部以下葱白部分，混合花椒、盐剁碎。加入香油、生抽，拌入鸡丝。<br />
2. 预热烤箱400华氏度。<br />
3. 每张酥皮切成6片，鸡丝放在酥皮一侧，另一侧对折，压实封口，再用叉子环边压出条纹。<br />

<h2>八、波特菇三明治</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146499" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146499" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146499-e45baf4c6c601bcfe1ea98276761f9b4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146499-e45baf4c6c601bcfe1ea98276761f9b4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146499" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6个波特菇（portabella）</p>

<p>现磨黑胡椒</p>
<p>1小撮干欧芹</p>
<p>1/4茶匙盐</p>
<p>黄色豌豆芽（golden pea shoots），切寸段</p>
<figure id="attachment_14146500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146500" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146500-70eaf76990e79f890aee541e88c77073.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146500-70eaf76990e79f890aee541e88c77073-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146500" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>

3. 拧下菌柄，食材摆入菌盖。</p>
<h2>九、土豆煎饼</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14146501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146501" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146501-956950786c8994b03de673031daf7efc.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146501-956950786c8994b03de673031daf7efc-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146501" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>食材准备</h3>
<p>1杯鸡肉丝</p>
<p>1杯切成薄片的抱子甘蓝</p>
<p>1个小洋葱，切幼条</p>
<p>2个褐皮土豆，1磅</p>
<p>1/4茶匙黑胡椒粉</p>
<p>1/4茶匙孜然粉</p>
<p>1/4茶匙卡宴辣椒粉</p>
<h4>橄榄油</h4>
<figure id="attachment_14146503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14146503" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146503-d525fa9daac69e518131752f49daa86b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14146503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14146503-d525fa9daac69e518131752f49daa86b-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14146503" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>做法：</h3>
<p>1. 土豆去皮切丁，放入带盖容器，微波炉（950W）加热5分钟，静置待用。<br />
2. 鸡肉与黑胡椒粉、孜然粉、卡宴辣椒粉混合。<br />
3. 锅中（8至10英寸的平底锅均可）加2汤匙橄榄油，中火炒洋葱至变色，加入1/4茶匙盐、抱子甘蓝炒1分钟关火，拌入鸡肉盛出待用。<br />
4. 土豆混合1/2茶匙盐捣成泥，拌入其它食材。<br />
5. 锅中放入1/4杯橄榄油，加入土豆泥摊成饼，中火煎至金黄。煎好的饼倒扣盘中，再滑入锅中煎另一面。</p>
