<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>电线聪明收纳 让你的生活空间不再乱糟糟</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128918-P.92-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>把杂乱的电线集中整理，清爽的桌面让工作更有效率。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>大部分人家中的电视柜和电脑后方，总是缠绕着一堆电线，因为觉得没有人会看见，不知不觉就置之不理了。只要活用收纳架或层架，就能让杂乱的电线变整齐。让地板维持不放任何东西的状态是很重要的，清洁地面也会变得比较轻松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128919" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128919-P.92-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128919-P.92-2-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128919" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桌上型电脑、外接式硬碟、手机充电器等等，工作桌下面的东西又杂又多。只要在工作桌的桌面下装设收纳架，立刻让杂乱感消失不见。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128920-P.92-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128920-P.92-3-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128920" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不容易沾染灰尘的Garage铁网电线收纳架。使用插座孔间隔很大的延长线，就算是体积大的变压器插头，也可以毫不费力地使用。</p>
<h2>活用空隙空间：电视背面的配线整理术</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14128921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128921-P.93-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128921-P.93-1-600x393.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128921" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>电视机的背面线材总是乱七八糟，又容易积灰尘。解决妙招是在电视背面设置层架，把电线收在一起，打扫起来又快又方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128922-P.93-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128922-P.93-2-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128922" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">POINT</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_14128923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128923" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128923-P.93-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14128923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128923-P.93-3-300x211.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="211" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128923" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了清楚知道是什么电线而装上标签。也写上开始使用的日期，做安全上的管理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128924" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128924-P.93-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14128924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128924-P.93-4-300x215.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="215" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128924" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>ELECOM的延长线。底部装上磁铁，即使插着变压器插头时也不会因为过重而倾倒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128925" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128925-P.93-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14128925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128925-P.93-5-300x212.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="212" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128925" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用49元商店的电线保护管，把好几条的电线包在一起，外观看起来轻巧不凌乱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128926" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128926-P.93-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14128926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128926-P.93-6-300x214.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="214" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128926" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用49元商店的挡书板和万用架，自己DIY制作的游戏把手收纳架。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128927" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128927-P.93-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14128927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128927-P.93-7-300x212.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="212" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128927" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>只要固定在电视背面的螺丝孔里，就能简单架设。也可以当作游戏机的放置场所。</p>
<h3>使用层架增加收纳的场所</h3>
<p>关于电视背面的整理，我使用日本山崎的电视背面收纳架。如此一来，原本小东西散乱的电视周边就变得很整齐。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">ATTENTION</span></h3>
<p>˙电线用束带或保护管包在一起就会变整齐<br />
˙选延长线的时候，也要考虑防雷击等安全性<br />
˙仔细整理好电线周边，就能防止触电或火灾</p>
