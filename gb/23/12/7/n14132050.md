<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>雷国辉原班人马续任中华公所职员</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132057-DSC05033-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙中华公所12月5日选举投票，原班职员等额当选。左起：财政陈余宝爱、中文书记翁宇才、主席雷国辉、英文书记阮鸿灿、核数张青梅。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月08日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）在12月5日的选举投票后，纽英崙中华公所现任5位职员等额当选。他们将于明年1月再次就职。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132054" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132054" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132054-DSC05030.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14132054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132054-DSC05030-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132054" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所12月5日选举投票，董事们在场等候开票。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">本次选举只有一组候选人报名参加，即主席雷国辉、中文书记翁宇才、英文书记阮鸿灿、财政陈余宝爱和核数张青梅。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在周二的投票日，中华公所董事会46名成员中有36人出席投票，除去4张空白票，总共有32张有效选票。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132053" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132053-DSC05028.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14132053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132053-DSC05028-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132053" class="wp-caption-text">关丽莎记录票数。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14132055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132055" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132055-DSC05031.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14132055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132055-DSC05031-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132055" class="wp-caption-text">黄国威和梁添光记录票数。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当晚6点开票后，雷国辉获得28票，翁宇才19票，阮鸿灿20票，陈余宝爱26票，张青梅22票。由于没有竞争，这5位职员均等额当选。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132052" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132052-DSC05027.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14132052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132052-DSC05027-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132052" class="wp-caption-text">陈建立唱票。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14132056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132056" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132056-DSC05032.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14132056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132056-DSC05032-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132056" class="wp-caption-text">开票后各人得票情况。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">雷国辉此前在政见发表会中提到，他期待未来增添专业人员，完善运作机制，继续为社区提供可负担住房。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132059" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132059-IMG_4809.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14132059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132059-IMG_4809-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132059" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所大楼已是美国历史古迹。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">雷国辉还说，泰勒街（Tyler Street）90号的中华公所大楼一直有很大一部分空置。现在美国内政部颁发的“国家历史建筑”铜牌已经做好。雷国辉希望在中华大楼设立访客中心，向游客推广历史文化，介绍华人在波士顿的生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132060" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132060-IMG_4870.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14132060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132060-IMG_4870-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132060" class="wp-caption-text">开票结束后，职员们在肥牛火锅城宴请在场中华公所成员。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14132058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132058" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132058-DSC05041.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14132058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132058-DSC05041-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132058" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任潘昭荣（左1）到场祝贺职员们当选。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">周二晚开票之后，侨教中心主任潘昭荣来到中华公所，向新届职员道贺。随后，5位职员作东，请在场的中华公所成员到华埠肥牛火锅城聚餐。◇</p>
