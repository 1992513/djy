<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新光与义贤学子交流乐 品味爱与勇气</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138854-654069-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>18日台中市太平区新光国小与义贤国小欢聚一堂，进行校际交流活动。（蔡智惠提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月18日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台中市报导）18日台中市太平区新光国小与义贤国小欢聚一堂，进行校际交流活动，同时展开了台中大坑鱼丸伯举办的义煮活动。这场精彩的互动不仅拉进校际距离，更让学子们感受到“重新开始的勇气”，充满爱和希望的力量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138857" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138857-654072.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138857-654072-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138857" class="wp-caption-text">鱼丸伯历经破产永不放弃的真实故事，呼应活动主题。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138859" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138859-654074.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138859-654074-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138859" class="wp-caption-text">台中市新光国小与云林二崙义贤国小，学子交流乐（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新光国小萨克斯风社团和义贤国小幼童军齐聚一堂，开启了一场充满音乐、创意和美食的交流之旅。由米雅外师主持的国际教育课程为学子们带来全新视野，而幼女童军则投入厨房，与鱼丸伯团队携手为义贤师生精心烹制鱼丸及虱目鱼肚特餐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138858" style="width: 536px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138858-654073.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138858-654073-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="536" b="402"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138858" class="wp-caption-text">此次活动不仅是校际交流的契机，更是一场感恩与回馈爱的义煮活动。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138863" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138863-654077.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138863-654077-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138863" class="wp-caption-text">由米雅外师主持的国际教育课程为学子们带来全新视野。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>校内特色的木工体验更丰富了萨克斯风社团的活动体验，让学生们在木作乐趣中感受到团队协作的重要性，并进行线偶的创作活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138855" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138855-654070.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138855-654070-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138855" class="wp-caption-text">义贤国小体验木作课程。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138856" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138856-654071.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138856-654071-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138856" class="wp-caption-text">学生们在木作乐趣中感受到团队协作的重要性，并进行线偶的创作活动。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>音乐会和表演环节中，两校学生以优美的演奏和歌声展现才华，为整个交流活动增添了愉快的氛围。鱼丸姊姊也分享了鱼丸伯历经破产永不放弃的真实故事，呼应活动主题，鼓励学生在人生的起伏中坚持希望，以乐观态度重新定义自己的人生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14138861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138861" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138861-654075.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138861-654075-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138861" class="wp-caption-text">新光国小萨克斯风表演。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14138862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14138862" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138862-654076.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14138862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138862-654076-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="533" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14138862" class="wp-caption-text">义贤国小直笛表演。（蔡智惠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中午时分，享用由鱼丸伯团队烹制的虱目鱼肚特餐，小朋友们给予满满的赞誉，感谢所有参与活动的大人们无私的奉献。</p>
<p>此次活动不仅是校际交流的契机，更是一场感恩与回馈爱的义煮活动。期待这样的爱心活动能够温暖每位参与者的心，激发更多人将爱心传递出去，为社会注入更多正能量。</p>
<p>教务主任蔡智惠期待未来更多类似活动，将爱心和感恩的精神延续，用光芒温暖周遭的每个人。</p>
