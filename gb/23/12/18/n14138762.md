<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>张书伟、谢京颖登记结婚 演员好友齐聚见证</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14138780-2312180455221487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>张书伟（左）、谢京颖资料照。 （黄宗茂／大纪元制图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月18日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E4%B9%A6%E4%BC%9F.md#1">张书伟</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E4%BA%AC%E9%A2%96.md#1">谢京颖</a>因戏结缘，两人因合作八点档《市井豪门》后开始交往，先前两人大方认爱后，今（18）日决定到户政事务所办结婚登记，并在社群分享喜讯。而俩人的演员好友们也都到场见证。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139490-2312190153251487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14139490" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139490-2312190153251487-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139490" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E4%B9%A6%E4%BC%9F.md#1">张书伟</a>（右）和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E4%BA%AC%E9%A2%96.md#1">谢京颖</a>于12月18日到户政事务所办结婚登记，并在社群分享喜讯。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张书伟、谢京颖今日在脸书同步发文 <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/shuwei1104/?locale=zh_TW" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点这里</a>）宣布结婚喜讯，文中感性提及：“一张书约感谢是你，一起走过四季，接下来还要携手走过世纪，谢谢爱我们的家人朋友们，谢谢大家的祝福 未来的日子还请多多指教。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139492" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139492-2312190153281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14139492" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139492-2312190153281487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139492" class="wp-caption-text">张书伟（左）和谢京颖于12月18日到户政事务所办结婚登记，并在社群晒出婚戒，分享喜讯。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张书伟和谢京颖两人交往不到一年时间，就决定今天一起到户政事务所办理登记结婚，<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/photo?fbid=921656999316164&amp;set=pcb.921657115982819&amp;locale=zh_TW" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点这里</a>），到场见证并祝福他们的艺人有郑人硕、小薰、蓝苇华、李又汝、唐振刚等人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139491" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139491-2312190153221487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14139491" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139491-2312190153221487.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="607" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139491" class="wp-caption-text">张书伟（右）和谢京颖于12月18日到户政事务所办结婚登记，并在社群分享喜讯。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
