<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>真人版《幽游白书》剧照公开 绫野刚演户愚吕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131098-231206082226100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>町田启太在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“小阎王”剧照。（Netflix提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月06日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）日本高人气动漫作品《幽游白书》即将以改编的真人版登场，Netflix今（6）日释出了《幽游白书》正式预告、主视觉海报，以及令原作粉丝好奇的12位主要角色的海报与个人预告和剧照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131103-231206082907100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131103" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131103-231206082907100707-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131103" class="wp-caption-text">真人版《幽游白书》主角群剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《幽游白书》故事叙述食人妖怪不断破坏人间，浦饭幽助（北村匠海 饰）为了保护众人以及守护人间界，必须成为优秀的“灵界侦探”，于是向幻海（梶芽衣子 饰）学习如何控制能力，并与藏马（志尊淳 饰）、飞影（本乡奏多 饰）以及桑原和真（上杉柊平 饰）组队除妖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131104" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131104-231206082916100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131104" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131104-231206082916100707-600x317.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131104" class="wp-caption-text">北村匠海在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“浦饭幽助”剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131105-231206082912100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131105" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131105-231206082912100707-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131105" class="wp-caption-text">本乡奏多在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“飞影”剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131106-231206082229100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131106" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131106-231206082229100707-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131106" class="wp-caption-text">志尊淳在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“藏马”剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131107" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131107-231206082857100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131107" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131107-231206082857100707-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131107" class="wp-caption-text">上杉柊平在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“桑原和真”剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131108-231206082235100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131108" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131108-231206082235100707-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131108" class="wp-caption-text">梶芽衣子在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“幻海”剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一共五集的《幽游白书》真人版，为了捕捉原著漫画的精髓，剧组与在好莱坞制作特效负有盛名的Scanline VFX合作，使用最新拍摄技术及170台摄影机，以360度环景的方式捕捉演员动态，使导演月川翔可以挑选最佳角度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131112" style="width: 405px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131112-231206081140100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131112" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131112-231206081140100707.jpg" alt="" width="405" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131112" class="wp-caption-text">稲垣吾郎在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“左京”海报照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>视觉特效导演Tomofumi Akahane表示：“和Scanline VFX合作，我们能在视觉效果上达到更高标准，让观众无法分辨是否使用了特效。”</p>
<p>将漫画重新改编成真人版影集最难呈现的部分，是战斗中的户愚吕兄弟。户愚吕兄拥有身体任意变形的能力，而户愚吕弟能随意扩展他自己的肌肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131109-231206082223100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131109" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131109-231206082223100707-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131109" class="wp-caption-text">绫野刚在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“户愚吕弟”剧照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本作的视觉效果顾问Ryo Sakaguchi是首位荣获奥斯卡科学技术奖的日本人，他表示：“演员和角色的身体尺寸不同，所以要调整他们的比例是一项艰钜的工程。这不单单是改变他们的身高，还需确保他们保持与对手演员视线的一致性。”制作团队也制作出户愚吕兄弟的3D模型，并将它们连接到演员身上进行拍摄，使拍摄更加顺利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131110" style="width: 405px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131110-231206081131100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131110" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131110-231206081131100707.jpg" alt="" width="405" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131110" class="wp-caption-text">绫野刚在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“户愚吕弟”海报照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，许多演员在拍摄过程中并不习惯与视觉特效团队合作，制作团队提出了多种方法协助演员表演，包括让特技演员身穿蓝色特效服与演员对戏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131111" style="width: 405px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131111-231206081126100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131111" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131111-231206081126100707.jpg" alt="" width="405" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131111" class="wp-caption-text">町田启太在真人版《幽游白书》中饰演“小阎王”海报照。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>动作导演Takahito Ouchi表示：“演员们该如何想像一个实际上不在他们面前的敌人呢？无论告诉他们敌人的位置多少次，他们都需要被激励，让整个场景看起来就像是真的战斗。这些现场感源自于演员自身的热情。”</p>
<p>由北村匠海、志尊淳、本乡奏多、上杉柊平、町田启太及绫野刚等人演绎的真人版《幽游白书》，将于12月14日于Netflix公开。</p>
