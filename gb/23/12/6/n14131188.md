<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>耶诞初雪“白雪木”！台中新社体验冬季仙境</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131196-651804-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>白雪木的繁殖方法以扦插为主。（吴淑珠提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月06日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台中市报导）台中新社山区，隐藏一处私人景观餐厅，它被誉为“耶诞初雪”的绝佳去处。这个地方拥有一片纯白如雪的花海，即日起至2024年1月5日，将有机会一次欣赏两种季节限定美景，与白雪木和落羽松共同入镜，进入梦幻仙境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131189" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131189-651794.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14131189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131189-651794-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="678" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131189" class="wp-caption-text">白雪木陪衬其中，模特儿更美丽了。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131190-651795.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131190-651795-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131190" class="wp-caption-text">大片白雪木，壮观又漂亮。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位于台中新社山上，坐拥宽阔山林景观，庄园主人精心整理，进入一片森林秘境，四季不断有不同的植物花卉景观盛开。而现在正是白雪木盛开的季节，花况约已达5成，雪白花苞已经冒出枝头，预计接近耶诞节时，这片白雪的大景将变得更加美丽，绝对是一场不容错过的视觉盛宴！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131195" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131195-651803.jpeg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131195-651803-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131195" class="wp-caption-text">现在正是白雪木盛开的季节，花况约已达5成。（吴淑珠提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>白雪木原产于热带地区，通常需要充足的阳光和温暖潮湿的气候来茁壮成长。它在开阔、阳光充足、遮荫最少的地方生长，而在新社私人庭园餐厅”，这种理想的生长环境得以实现，使白雪木展现出最美丽的姿态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131194" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131194-651799.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14131194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131194-651799-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131194" class="wp-caption-text">白雪木原产于热带地区，通常需要充足的阳光和温暖潮湿的气候来茁壮成长。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131191" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131191-651796.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14131191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131191-651796-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131191" class="wp-caption-text">原来模特儿穿白色的衣服，会让白雪木更吸睛。（黄连兴提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>若对白雪木感兴趣，也可以尝试自己栽培它。通常，白雪木的繁殖方法以扦插为主，选取一年生枝条剪成长12-15厘米的插条，扦插在沙床上，15-20天后就能生根，40天后可移栽。此外，您也可以使用盆栽苗种植，选择避风、阳光充足、排水良好的地块，全年均可种植，但以春季为佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131192" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131192-651797.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14131192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131192-651797-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131192" class="wp-caption-text">台中新社山区，隐藏在一处私人景观餐厅，它被誉为“耶诞初雪”的绝佳去处。（陈南荣提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14131193" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131193" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131193-651798.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14131193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131193-651798-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131193" class="wp-caption-text">新社私人餐厅，纯白如雪的花海，即日起至2024年1月5日（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
