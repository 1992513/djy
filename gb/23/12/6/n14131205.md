<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】味噌酱烤三文鱼豆腐～咸香好滋味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132770-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>味噌酱烤三文鱼豆腐。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>调味酱往往是一道菜肴美味可口的关键，也是变化出不同菜谱的角色。今天介绍的“烤味噌料理”是日式餐点常见的菜单，使用“味噌<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%96%87%E9%B1%BC.md#1">三文鱼</a>汁酱”让烹饪者省时省力又避免料理失败，浓郁香气，只要一瓢酱汁淋在三文鱼片上，再放入烤箱烘烤，不到十分钟就香味四溢。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%B3%E5%99%8C%E9%85%B1.md#1">味噌酱</a>烤<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%96%87%E9%B1%BC.md#1">三文鱼</a>豆腐</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>三文鱼片12 oz<br />
有机豆腐一盒14 oz<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A6%E7%AC%8B.md#1">芦笋</a>12 oz<br />
红萝卜4 oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132762" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132762-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14132762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132762-1-450x291.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="291" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132762" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【腌料】</span></h3>
<p>盐少许<br />
烹大师适量<br />
胡椒粉适量<br />
粟粉适量<br />
蛋白适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>味噌三文<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E6%B1%81%E9%85%B1.md#1">鱼汁酱</a>1包</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. ?三文鱼片加入所有腌料拌匀，腌15分钟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132763" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132763-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14132763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132763-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132763" class="wp-caption-text">三文鱼片加入所有腌料拌匀，腌15分钟备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. ?豆腐切成长条块状，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A6%E7%AC%8B.md#1">芦笋</a>切成小段，红萝卜切成小段备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132764" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132764-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14132764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132764-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132764" class="wp-caption-text">豆腐切成长条块状。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. ?烤箱预热至375℉。豆腐吸干水后用油煎香备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132765" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132765-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14132765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132765-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132765" class="wp-caption-text">烤箱预热至375℉。豆腐吸干水后用油煎香备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.? 三文鱼片略煎一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132766" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132766-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14132766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132766-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132766" class="wp-caption-text">三文鱼片略煎一下。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. ?芦笋和红萝卜用油盐水烫1分钟，捞出沥干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132767-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14132767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132767-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132767" class="wp-caption-text">芦笋和红萝卜用油盐水烫1分钟，捞出沥干。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. ?烤盘上喷上少许油，把豆腐、芦笋和红萝卜排在盘底。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132768" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132768" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132768-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14132768" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132768-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132768" class="wp-caption-text">烤盘上喷上少许油，把豆腐、芦笋和红萝卜排在盘底。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. ?放三文鱼片，淋上味噌三文<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E6%B1%81%E9%85%B1.md#1">鱼汁酱</a>，放入烤箱烤8分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14132769" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14132769" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132769-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14132769" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14132769-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14132769" class="wp-caption-text">放三文鱼片，淋上味噌三文鱼汁酱，放入烤箱烤8分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【食谱说明】</span></h3>
<p>这次选用的味噌三文鱼汁酱（Authentic Miso Salmon Sauce），其成分包括红白味噌、水、白米、黄豆、海盐、清酒和糖。这种酱汁是在美国制造，可以做沙拉酱，或者用来烤海鲜和肉类。◇</p>
