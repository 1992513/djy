<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】白酱蘑菇洋葱猪排～酸甜好下饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131181-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>港式家常菜——白酱蘑菇洋葱猪排。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>圣诞长假，烤牛排、煎<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E6%8E%92.md#1">猪排</a>是在家下厨的简单菜单，而且可以一饱口腹之欲。今天我们来品尝不一样的风味，让粤式猪排餐温暖您的味蕾。别忘了，开始煎猪排前要先腌制20分钟，也就是说这份菜需要一些前置作业时间，虽然它是一锅到底的菜谱。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E9%85%B1.md#1">白酱</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%98%91%E8%8F%87.md#1">蘑菇</a>洋葱<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E6%8E%92.md#1">猪排</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>猪排4块约1磅<br />
洋葱1/2个（切丝）<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%98%91%E8%8F%87.md#1">蘑菇</a>片4 oz<br />
青豆、红萝卜粒3 oz<br />
马莎拿酒1/2杯<br />
鲜奶2/3杯<br />
忌廉奶1/2杯<br />
粟粉水4汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131182" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131182-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14131182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131182-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131182" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>烹大师1茶匙<br />
盐1/2茶匙<br />
糖1/2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131183" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131183-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14131183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131183-2-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131183" class="wp-caption-text">马莎拿酒、鲜奶、忌廉奶、粟粉水、烹大师、<br />盐、糖、胡椒粉。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【猪排腌料】</span></h3>
<p>盐1/2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
姜汁酒1汤匙<br />
粟粉1汤匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 猪排用刀背轻剁后，加入所有腌料，腌20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131197" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131197-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14131197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131197-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131197" class="wp-caption-text">猪排用刀背轻剁后，加入所有腌料，腌20分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 热锅下油，先把猪排两面煎香至八成熟，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131199" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131199-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14131199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131199-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131199" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，先把猪排两面煎香至八成熟，备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 炒香洋葱、蘑菇、青豆和红萝卜后，加入马莎拿、鲜奶和所有调味一起煮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131200" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131200-5.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14131200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131200-5-450x256.png" alt="" width="450" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131200" class="wp-caption-text">炒香洋葱、蘑菇、青豆和红萝卜后，加入马莎拿、鲜奶和所有调味一起煮。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 放回猪排煮2分钟后，用粟粉水勾芡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131201" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131201-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14131201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131201-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131201" class="wp-caption-text">放回猪排煮2分钟后，用粟粉水勾芡。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 最后加入忌廉奶拌匀便可上碟。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14131203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14131203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131203-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14131203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14131203-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14131203" class="wp-caption-text">最后加入忌廉奶拌匀便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
