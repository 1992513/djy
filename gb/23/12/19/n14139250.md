<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>庆中华公所100周年 侨声音乐社献唱经典粤曲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139245-DSC05383-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>为庆祝中华公所100周年，侨声音乐剧社12月17日献唱8段经典粤曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月19日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）乐器和鸣，唱声悠扬：“开帘风动竹，疑是故人来。”侨声音乐剧社社长黄国威、名誉社长雷玉霞唱响这首《拾钗缘》，作为祝贺纽英崙中华公所100周年的压轴节目。</p>
<p>12月17日，侨声音乐剧社在中华公所礼堂举办粤曲演唱会，与华埠居民和友人一起庆祝圣诞、喜迎新年，并祝贺中华公所100周年生日快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139232-DSC05322.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139232-DSC05322-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139232" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社献唱经典粤曲，首场是雷玉霞和陈秀娥演唱的《沈园遗恨》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139233-DSC05324.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139233-DSC05324-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139233" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社庆祝中华公所100周年粤曲演出。乐师们为演唱者伴奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从中午12点到下午3:30，8场经典粤曲桥段轮番上演，在乐师们婉转动听的伴奏中，为观众展现这些古典故事中的悲欢离合，赢得热烈掌声。</p>
<p>在现场听曲的退伍军人会成员黄国麟笑言，他虽然不会唱，但是很喜欢听粤曲。这让他想起小时候在广东农村的生活，那时候没什么别的娱乐活动，就是听大戏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139242" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139242-DSC05356.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139242-DSC05356-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139242" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社庆祝中华公所100周年粤曲演出。乐师们为演唱者伴奏。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139234-DSC05328.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139234-DSC05328-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139234" class="wp-caption-text">李相文和黄绮玲演唱《绝唱胡笳十八拍》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139239" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139239-DSC05344.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139239-DSC05344-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139239" class="wp-caption-text">侨声音乐剧社献唱经典粤曲，华埠退伍军人会的个别成员也在场聆听。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周日的表演由雷玉霞和陈秀娥演唱的《沈园遗恨》拉开帷幕。接下来上场的，有李相文和黄绮玲演唱的《绝唱胡笳十八拍》，梁雁英和陈素瑜演唱的《花迷蝶醉》，黄伟健和陈艳珠演唱的《塞外草暖文姬乐》，黎雅雯和黄宝娥演唱的《南唐残梦》，马文龙和陈翠红演唱的《魂梦绕山河》，梁雁英和陈翠秀演唱的《梦会太湖》，以及压轴登场的《拾钗缘》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139237" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139237-DSC05336.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139237-DSC05336-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139237" class="wp-caption-text">梁雁英和陈素瑜演唱《花迷蝶醉》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139238" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139238-DSC05340.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139238-DSC05340-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139238" class="wp-caption-text">黄伟健和陈艳珠演唱《塞外草暖文姬乐》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>据黄国威介绍，侨声音乐剧社有100多名成员，其中活跃的、经常参加活动的是50到60人。他和中华公所主席雷国辉都感谢表演者们，尤其是不间断演奏的乐师们为大家带来精彩演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139244" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139244-DSC05377.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139244" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139244-DSC05377-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139244" class="wp-caption-text">马文龙和陈翠红演唱《魂梦绕山河》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>当天，波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）也到场聆听了部分曲目，并向侨声颁发了市议会褒奖。</p>

<p>“侨声的音乐非常棒，我很喜欢听，以及学一学粤曲，”费连说，“我也要感谢中华公所，祝贺100周年，感谢你们将这个社区团结在一起。”</p>
<p>新年将至，费连还特别用粤语祝福大家：“恭喜发财。”◇</p>
