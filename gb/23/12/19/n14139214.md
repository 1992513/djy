<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中华公所欢庆圣诞节 大量好礼送居民</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139014-DSC05265-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>12月16日，纽英崙中华公所与大同村同庆圣诞节。“圣诞老人”阮鸿灿将礼物送给居民。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月19日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）圣诞节将至，纽英崙中华公所礼堂内已经安装了色彩缤纷的圣诞树，桌子上堆满了包装精美的礼物盒——这些都是在12月16日的派对中送给大同村居民的礼物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139008-DSC05197.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139008-DSC05197-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139008" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞老人摇着铃铛进场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“每年中华公所和大同村一直都很慷慨，照顾我们大同村居民，也很努力很支持我们办事处为居民做事情，整到我们大同村越来越漂亮，越来越干净，你们的居家环境越来越舒适。”大同村物业管理经理翁丽芳说，赢得在场上百居民的热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139009-DSC05205.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139009-DSC05205-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139009" class="wp-caption-text">大同村物业管理经理翁丽芳感谢中华公所的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139010-DSC05213.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139010-DSC05213-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139010" class="wp-caption-text">上百居民参与庆祝。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">周六，中华公所携大同村居民欢庆圣诞节，延续多年以来向居民们赠送礼物的传统。伴随着清脆的铃声，扮做“圣诞老人”的阮鸿灿驼着礼物包裹走进屋，迎来一阵欢呼和掌声。随后，中华公所主席雷国辉为居民们抽奖，核数张青梅和中文书记翁宇才将一个个礼物盒送到中奖居民的手中。到庆典结束之后，每一位居民还都领到了简餐饭盒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139013-DSC05242.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139013-DSC05242-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139013" class="wp-caption-text">（右起）中华公所主席雷国辉、中文书记翁宇才、核数张青梅、英文书记阮鸿灿祝居民节日快乐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139016-DSC05281.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139016-DSC05281-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139016" class="wp-caption-text">每一位参与庆祝的居民都有餐盒可领。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">翁丽芳介绍说，今天的礼物都是由中华公所、大同村管理处、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处等机构捐赠的。她亦代表业主，感谢中华公所多年以来对大同村的支持。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">大同村联谊会主席黄国威感谢雷国辉、翁丽芳等职员为居民们谋福利，令大家生活舒适。另一位联谊会主席阮谢少珍说：“今天大家光临，我们济济一堂，希望每一年都有这样的机会让大家坐在一起。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139011-DSC05228.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139011-DSC05228-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139011" class="wp-caption-text">大同村联谊会主席黄国威、阮谢少珍感谢中华公所为大同村居民谋福利。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139012-DSC05233.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14139012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139012-DSC05233-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139012" class="wp-caption-text">大家感谢大同村管理处的职员们努力工作。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在欢庆节日的同时，翁丽芳还邀请每一位大同村职员上台，向“鞠躬尽瘁”的他们表达感谢。雷国辉亦感谢了华埠治安巡逻队的义工们对社区的贡献。◇</p>
