<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花莲“云山水梦幻湖” 冬季限定落羽松美景登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139603-654322-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>花莲景点“云山水梦幻湖”，唯美的落羽松景点。（Calvin Liu提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月19日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）花莲寿丰乡美拍胜地“云山水梦幻湖”被誉为全台最美落羽松秘境，吸引众多游客和摄影爱好者。这片湖泊平静如梦，被花莲群山环绕，仿佛仙境一般，令人感受到山中的宁静与舒适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139607" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139607-654326-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14139607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139607-654326-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139607" class="wp-caption-text">来花莲切勿错过这片湖泊、蓝天绿地和白云所营造的天堂般美景。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139604" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139604-654323-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14139604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139604-654323-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139604" class="wp-caption-text">被花莲群山环绕，仿佛仙境一般。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影家Calvin Liu表示云山水梦幻湖风景区，开始收入园费120元，50元可抵折价券，车可以停在外面，再走进园区。游客可以轻松自在地欣赏湖景，其中湖区的落羽松瀑布、跳石步道以及云山水植物农场成为必拍的画面，同时也是网美们争相打卡的花莲IG景点。景区内的住宿选择众多，包括有熊的森林、云山水88Villa、云山水朴耕园等，让游客可以在湖畔度过一个完美的夜晚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139606" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139606-654325-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14139606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139606-654325-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139606" class="wp-caption-text">云山水落羽松瀑布。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14139605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139605" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139605-654324-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14139605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139605-654324-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139605" class="wp-caption-text">湖区的落羽松瀑布、跳石步道以及云山水植物农场成为必拍的画面。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每年12月至1月，湖区掀起一阵追逐落羽松的热潮，欣赏这片翠绿转为金黄至艳红的多层次色彩，堪称全台最迷人的枫叶美景。花莲云山水成为林依晨为黑松汽水拍摄广告后的经典之地，其空灵湖景令人惊艳，仿佛置身欧洲仙境。</p>
<p>来花莲切勿错过这片湖泊、蓝天绿地和白云所营造的天堂般美景，云山水梦幻湖，将成为您花莲之行的必访之地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14139609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14139609" style="width: 509px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139609-654330-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14139609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14139609-654330-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="509" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14139609" class="wp-caption-text">欣赏云山水这片翠绿转为金黄至艳红的多层次色彩。（Calvin Liu提供）</figcaption></figure>
