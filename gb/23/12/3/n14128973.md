<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>萧瑟神池壮阔铁杉林 小关山巧遇夕阳云海</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128976-651060-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小关山林道，遇见了媲美北大武山的喜多丽断崖的云海。（谢子仁提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年12月03日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾高雄报导）在台湾中央山脉南一段的小关山，12月初一场惊艳的山岳奇遇，让登山爱好者和自然爱好者，无比的惊喜和身在美丽画面之中。小关山标高3248公尺，自然景观和壮丽山川一直以来都是登山者的梦想之地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128980" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128980-651064.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128980-651064-600x373.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128980" class="wp-caption-text">小关山山顶云雾翻腾。（谢子仁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14128975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128975" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128975-651059.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128975-651059-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128975" class="wp-caption-text">小关山最高点3248公尺。（谢子仁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小关山不仅令人赞叹的登山之路充满挑战，更以其独特的地形地貌而闻名，被形容为“小关难缠、云水无水、卑南不死、藤枝必亡”。</p>
<p>而在小关山林道附近，山友遇见了媲美北大武山的喜多丽断崖的云海、壮阔的铁杉林，尤其是翻涌的夕阳云海。这个美丽的绝景瞬间，让所有在场的山友们都赞赏不已，拍下了这难忘的瞬间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128974-651058.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128974-651058-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128974" class="wp-caption-text">小关山尝试野营，先在山脚营地试装，可以更贴近大自然。（谢子仁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14128979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128979" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128979-651063.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128979-651063-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128979" class="wp-caption-text">因林道难走，又非百岳，所以山径为原始天然。（谢子仁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小关山的登山道路极为崎岖，全程都需要经过颠簸的车程，而路况差。从宝来到登山口的路程需时近2个小时，山友如果要前往小关山，必须事前申请入山证，或在台20线宝来派出所现场办理入山手续。</p>
<p>尽管神池目前已经干涸，气温下探10度以下，但小关山的美景和惊喜总是值得一探。这次的奇遇不仅让山友们留下难以忘怀的回忆，也让小关山成为了更多探险者，和自然爱好者心中的热门登山路线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14128977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128977-651061.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128977-651061-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128977" class="wp-caption-text">神池目前已经干涸，气温下探10度以下。（谢子仁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14128978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14128978" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128978-651062.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14128978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14128978-651062-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14128978" class="wp-caption-text">小关山壮阔美丽的黑森林。（谢子仁提供）</figcaption></figure>
