<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】松子马蹄磨菇炒火鸡粒～圣诞菜谱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141938-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>松子马蹄磨菇炒火鸡粒～圣诞菜谱。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：这道“松子马蹄磨菇炒火鸡粒”可以作为圣诞节菜式之一，好看又好吃！</p>
<div id="artbody" class="post_content">
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
</div>
<p>火鸡肉 8oz<br />
马蹄4oz<br />
蘑菇4oz<br />
洋葱1/4个<br />
红甜椒1/4个<br />
青椒2个<br />
葱花适量<br />
松子1.5 oz<br />
蔓越莓酱2汤匙<br />
薄荷叶少许（装饰用）<br />
生菜6片<br />
草莓、黑草莓和覆盆子各适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142435" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142435-IMG_0474.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142435-IMG_0474-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142435" class="wp-caption-text">材料。(梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>黑胡椒粉1/4茶匙<br />
盐1/2茶匙<br />
蘑菇粉1/2茶匙<br />
花雕酒1汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142437" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142437-IMG_0484.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142437-IMG_0484-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142437" class="wp-caption-text">调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1.火鸡肉切粗粒备用。洋葱、红甜椒、青椒、蘑菇、马蹄全部切成小粒备用。绿叶生菜拣一些比较细的叶片洗净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14141934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14141934" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141934-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14141934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141934-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14141934" class="wp-caption-text">火鸡肉切粗粒备用。洋葱、红甜椒、青椒、蘑菇、马蹄全部切成小粒备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142436-IMG_0481.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142436-IMG_0481-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142436" class="wp-caption-text">绿叶生菜拣一些比较细的叶片洗净备用。(梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.锅烧热，先放入蘑菇粒炒香，然后将蘑菇拨开，中间放一点油，放入洋葱、青椒、红椒和马蹄，一起翻炒约半分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14141936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14141936" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141936-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14141936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141936-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14141936" class="wp-caption-text">锅烧热，先放入蘑菇粒炒香。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142439" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142439-IMG_0489.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142439-IMG_0489-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142439" class="wp-caption-text">将蘑菇拨开，中间放一点油，放入洋葱、青椒、红椒和马蹄，一起翻炒约半分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.再加入火鸡粒一起炒1分钟，加入所有调味后，放入葱花拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14142440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142440" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142440-IMG_0491.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142440-IMG_0491-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142440" class="wp-caption-text">再加入火鸡粒一起炒1分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14142442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14142442" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142442-IMG_0493.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14142442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14142442-IMG_0493-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14142442" class="wp-caption-text">加入所有调味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14141937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14141937" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141937-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14141937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141937-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14141937" class="wp-caption-text">放入葱花拌匀。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【装盘】</span></h3>
<p>盘子中央放一片生菜，上面放草莓、黑草莓和覆盆子。旁边也摆一些生菜片，把炒好的材料放在生菜片上，淋蔓越莓酱，放上松子、薄荷叶，便可享用。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14141939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14141939" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141939-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14141939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14141939-5-450x478.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="478" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14141939" class="wp-caption-text">装盘。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
