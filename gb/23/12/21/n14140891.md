<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新州现疫情 儿童和年轻患者急诊人数激增</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13213043-GettyImages-1248157933-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2020年6月4日一名疑似COVID-19染疫患者被送往悉尼圣文森特医院（St Vincent's Hospital）急诊部红色区的隔离病房。(Lisa Maree Williams/Getty Images)</h6>

<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">新州卫生厅表示，过去两周，前往急诊就诊的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', HelveticaNeue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">COVID-19</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">患者持续增加，尤其是儿童和</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', HelveticaNeue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">17</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', HelveticaNeue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">34</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">岁的年轻患者。</span></span></span></p>
