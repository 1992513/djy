<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】微波炉马铃薯脆片</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130395-7ef50e5b3f5136a19159b310d44fecad-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>居家自制的微波炉马铃薯脆片，口感不输市售，少油盐更健康。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>透过家中的微波炉自制马铃薯脆片，味道不输市售！烹煮过程只需要5分钟，不加油脂也超脆。</p>
<p>食谱选用育空黄金马铃薯（YUKONG GOLD）。它是培育品种，淀粉量适中，烘烤、煎薯饼、微波都适合，味道微甜，颜色金黄。</p>
<p>此外，这里使用蔬菜切片工具，薄而厚度均匀，因此可以在5分钟内煮熟一批薯片。若采手工切片，请根据薯片厚度调整烹煮时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130402" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130402-f3491226a843598a3b05c6a78fe53353.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14130402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130402-f3491226a843598a3b05c6a78fe53353-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130402" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>食材准备：</h2>
<p>育空黄金马铃薯<br />
酪梨油，可选<br />
细海盐，可选<br />
蘸酱，细香葱加酸奶油（Sour Cream）</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1. 取一个尽可能大的盘子，铺上烘焙纸。</p>
<p>2. 铺烘焙纸的方法：先把烘焙纸剪出盘子形状，然后抓捏成团再展开铺上盘子。烘焙纸形成凹凸不平的纹路可防粘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130401" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130401-6067191eb7441bef4d230687b7af775b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14130401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130401-6067191eb7441bef4d230687b7af775b-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130401" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14130400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130400" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130400-8887787f755fc6c369c117cb8804e095.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14130400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130400-8887787f755fc6c369c117cb8804e095-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130400" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14130399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130399" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130399-c1bcd236b0926bf16c889bf967c5fecd.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14130399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130399-c1bcd236b0926bf16c889bf967c5fecd-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130399" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 马铃薯削成薄片，浸泡水中，洗掉淀粉，用厨房纸巾擦去水分（因为薯片水分多需要增加烹煮时间）；也可以跳过洗淀粉这一步，直接将薯片单层平铺在盘中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130398" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130398-8eec7529481c11d1249759a71c8f45c2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14130398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130398-8eec7529481c11d1249759a71c8f45c2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130398" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14130397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130397" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130397-71337eec5348dd434f8a22979829f2e0.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14130397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130397-71337eec5348dd434f8a22979829f2e0-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130397" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14130396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130396" style="width: 535px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130396-bae3c856ecd726bd9635451dff4c03d3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14130396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130396-bae3c856ecd726bd9635451dff4c03d3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="535" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130396" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 用刷子为薯片均匀涂抹油脂；可以跳过这一步，不涂油脂的薯片也会脆，带有甜味。涂油的薯片比较香。</p>
<p>5. 这里使用950瓦特的微波炉。高火加热5分钟。也可以先加热4分钟，看情况再增加1分钟。功率小的微波炉需要延长烹煮时间。</p>
<p>6. 薯片在4分钟左右开始变色，请待在微波炉前观察。看到有薯片变金黄色即按下暂停键，取出它们；余下的薯片继续加热30秒~1分钟，至金黄色、酥脆。</p>
<p>7. 薯片加热至金黄色、局部淡棕色时甜味突出，变成褐色则生出苦味，所以需要留意薯片加热3分钟后的变化，及时出炉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130403-e2d8432ebd1d0092f0801aa74edab36e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14130403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130403-e2d8432ebd1d0092f0801aa74edab36e-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130403" class="wp-caption-text">作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 出炉的薯片直接撒盐和辣椒粉等调料、蘸酱享用。◇</p>
