<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】港式咖喱大蟹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130460-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>港式咖喱大蟹（梁志生提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong></p>
<p>粤菜的咖喱蟹料理一直都是餐厅必点菜。在寒冷的冬天，品尝暖呼呼的海鲜味，只能说只有“幸福”两个字。这道菜肴加入咖喱粉和黄姜粉炒香，还有洋葱、红椒粒等调味可以增香去腥，料理上不困难，却是香色俱全。此外，加入的忌廉奶取代了椰奶，也让海外港人更感受到家乡味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">咖喱大蟹?</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>加州邓格拉斯大蟹1只（已经煮熟，约2磅）<br />
洋葱1/2个（切丝）<br />
红椒1/4个（切丝）<br />
椰菜花8 oz<br />
切碎的洋葱、红椒粒4 oz<br />
姜、蒜蓉少许<br />
芫荽少许（装饰用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130449" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130449-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14130449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130449-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130449" class="wp-caption-text">加州邓格拉斯大蟹1只（已经煮熟，约2磅）（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14130450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130450" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130450-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14130450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130450-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130450" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱和红椒（切丝）、椰菜花、切碎的洋葱、红椒粒、姜、蒜蓉、芫荽。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>咖喱粉2汤匙<br />
黄姜粉1汤匙<br />
鸡汤3杯<br />
盐1/2茶匙<br />
糖1汤匙<br />
忌廉奶1/4杯<br />
粟粉水2汤匙（勾芡用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130451" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130451-3.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14130451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130451-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130451" class="wp-caption-text">调味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 大蟹斩成小块，蟹盖同样清洗干净后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130455" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130455-4.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14130455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130455-4-450x357.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130455" class="wp-caption-text">大蟹斩成小块，蟹盖同样清洗干净后备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 椰菜花煮2分钟捞起备用。热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、洋葱粒和红椒粒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130456" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130456-c91960010a89f6c6689bd5db9ed0665b.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14130456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130456-c91960010a89f6c6689bd5db9ed0665b-450x321.png" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130456" class="wp-caption-text">椰菜花煮2分钟捞起备用。热锅下油，爆香姜、蒜蓉、洋葱粒和红椒粒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 放入咖喱粉和黄姜粉炒香。加入洋葱丝和红椒丝拌炒，再加入鸡汤、盐和糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130457" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130457-6.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14130457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130457-6-450x326.png" alt="" width="450" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130457" class="wp-caption-text">加入鸡汤、盐和糖。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 把蟹放进锅中，加入椰菜花一起翻炒，盖上锅盖煮2分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130458" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130458-7.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14130458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130458-7-450x261.png" alt="" width="450" b="261" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130458" class="wp-caption-text">把蟹放进锅中，加入椰菜花一起翻炒，盖上锅盖煮2分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 加入忌廉奶拌匀，加入粟粉水，翻炒均匀后便可装盘，最后放上芫荽装饰。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14130459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14130459" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130459-8.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14130459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14130459-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14130459" class="wp-caption-text">加入忌廉奶拌匀，加入粟粉水，翻炒均匀后便可装盘，最后放上芫荽装饰。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
