<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿安良工商会新届就职 众宾云集</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145017-DSC05454-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿安良工商会12月26日举办就职典礼，新届职员及嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年12月28日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨、章佩儿波士顿报导）香烟飘飘，人才济济，波士顿安良工商会的大厅内座无虚席。众人先向关圣帝君像鞠躬敬香，随后见证安良新届职员宣誓就职。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">12月26日中午，波士顿地区侨社近百位来宾齐聚华埠的安良工商会大楼，参与2024年度职员的就职典礼，并祈盼在新的一年继续团结协作，维护华人权益。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14145004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145004-DJY02024.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145004-DJY02024-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145004" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿安良工商会12月26日举办就职典礼，众多嘉宾参与。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>敬拜神明</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>新届职员就任</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天正午，随着司仪梅宇国宣布典礼开始，全体来宾首先向关圣帝君像及两位保护神鞠躬致敬。新届职员则向神像上香、敬供品。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我们怀着感恩之心，过去一年以来，关圣帝君及两位保护神保佑大家，以及我们会务昌隆，大家身体健康。所以我们有一种感恩之心。”梅宇国说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14145001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145001-DJY02032.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145001-DJY02032-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145001" class="wp-caption-text">新届职员向关圣帝君敬香。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14145007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145007-DSC05413.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145007-DSC05413-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145007" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长陈伟民和伍伟业向关圣帝君敬香。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14145006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145006-DSC05409.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145006-DSC05409-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145006" class="wp-caption-text">来宾们向关圣帝君鞠躬。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">随后，在侨界代表及政要嘉宾总计近百人的见证下，新届职员宣读了就职誓词：“恪守会纲，发展会务，巩固大局，破除姓界，维护会员权益，不惊不徐，无偏无私。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14145002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145002" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145002-DJY02038.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145002-DJY02038-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145002" class="wp-caption-text">新届职员宣誓就职。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14145005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145005-DSC05407.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145005-DSC05407-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145005" class="wp-caption-text">司仪梅宇国向来宾们介绍会务。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">今年陈伟民和伍伟业连任会长，并分别以简短的致词感谢大家的支持，祝福大家身体健康。这两位比较年轻的安良成员都以英语为主要语言，不过今年陈伟民改以侨社惯用的粤语致词。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14145008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145008-DSC05417.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145008-DSC05417-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145008" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长陈伟民感谢大家的支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14145009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145009-DSC05419.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145009-DSC05419-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145009" class="wp-caption-text">新届会长伍伟业祝福大家身体健康。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">关于波士顿安良工商会的新一届职员，全美安良工商会元老陈仕维称赞说，其可谓是“老、中、青兼备，各有各的方位，各有各的努力付出”。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">陈仕维谈到，过去一年在中华公所带领下，安良工商会参与侨社事务，帮助唐人街繁荣生意。“过去一年我最欣慰的，就是我们侨社真正做到和谐。无论过去大家有几多不同地方的不同意见，但都能以一个民主、文明的方式去解决我们之间的分歧。”他说。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">陈仕维还说，过去侨社间曾发生过不同形式的斗争，浪费了很多精力。他希望，大家在新的一年能继续团结协作，为唐人街出力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14145016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145016-DSC05447.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145016-DSC05447-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145016" class="wp-caption-text">全美安良工商会元老陈仕维期盼侨社团结协作。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>政要嘉宾到场祝贺</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">周二有多位嘉宾参与了安良工商会的就职典礼，包括麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）、波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任潘昭荣、中华公所主席雷国辉、洪门致公堂总理余丽媖，还有波士顿市长办公室华埠联络员黄楚岚等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14145012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145012-DSC05441.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145012-DSC05441-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145012" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏、侨教中心主任潘昭荣向安良工商会赠予侨委会贺状。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">廖朝宏特别以不久前学会的粤语对大家说：“今天很开心来到这里与大家见面。”他感谢安良工商会对中华民国的支持，对侨界的贡献。他与潘昭荣还向两位连任主席赠送了侨委会的贺状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14145003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145003-DJY02055.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145003-DJY02055-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145003" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏感谢安良对中华民国台湾的支持。（章佩儿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14145013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145013-DSC05443.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145013-DSC05443-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145013" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所主席雷国辉祝贺两位会长就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14145015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145015-DSC05445.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145015-DSC05445-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145015" class="wp-caption-text">洪门总理余丽媖参与安良工商会就职典礼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">麦家威和费连都说，很荣幸能在议会中代表华埠社区，也感谢各位对这个社区的服务。“华埠社区的优点就是邻里互助，商家与居民互助。”费连说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14145010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145010-DSC05431.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145010-DSC05431-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145010" class="wp-caption-text">麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）感谢安良会员对华埠社区的贡献。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14145011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14145011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145011-DSC05436.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14145011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14145011-DSC05436-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14145011" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）称赞华埠社区的互助精神。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">就职典礼最后，来宾们再次向神像鞠躬致敬，祈盼新一年身体健康、会务昌盛。典礼结束后，安良工商会还在翠苑酒楼席开8桌，宴请参与典礼的来宾。</p>
