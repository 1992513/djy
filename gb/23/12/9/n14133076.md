<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】腰果菜脯炒海参筋 香脆开胃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133181-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>开胃小菜加入坚果，搭配海味和蔬菜，一盘营养就补足。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>吃对了食物就能补充好营养。开胃小菜或是宴客菜都会有炒坚果或是冷盘料理，其中腰果和海参筋是好搭档，除了口感酥脆Ｑ弹，营养价值也是令人满意。如果再加入一些适合热炒不软烂的蔬菜，例如青椒、红椒、洋葱，除了增添色泽之外，融合后的香味更是不同凡响。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">腰果菜脯炒海参筋</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>腰果3 oz<br />
甜菜脯3 oz<br />
海参筋8 oz<br />
青椒2 oz<br />
红椒2 oz<br />
洋葱3 oz<br />
姜、蒜片少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133174" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133174-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133174-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133174" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>蚝油1汤匙<br />
糖1/2茶匙<br />
生抽1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许<br />
粟粉1汤匙<br />
水1/3杯<br />
美极鲜酱油少许（后下）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 将所有材料切成粒状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133175" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133175-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133175-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133175" class="wp-caption-text">将所有材料切成粒状。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 海参筋用沸水烫1分钟后取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133176" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133176" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133176-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133176" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133176-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133176" class="wp-caption-text">海参筋用沸水烫1分钟后取出备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油，放入菜脯翻炒一会，再加入青、红椒、洋葱、姜和蒜片一起翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133177" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133177-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133177-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133177" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，放入菜脯翻炒一会，再加入青、红椒、洋葱、姜和蒜片一起翻炒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 接着再加入海参筋。搅匀调味汁液（美极鲜酱油除外）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133179" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133179-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133179-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133179" class="wp-caption-text">接着再加入海参筋。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 慢慢倒进调味汁液，翻炒均匀，最后淋上少许美极鲜酱油，盛盘后放上腰果。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14133180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14133180" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133180-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14133180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id14133180-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14133180" class="wp-caption-text">慢慢倒进调味汁液，翻炒均匀，最后淋上少许美极鲜酱油，盛盘后放上腰果。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
