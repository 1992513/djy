<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>陪母亲吃烤肉大餐 Northern 160 BBQ是首选</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993249-fd905fdd8ceebb97a8878f1959435c04-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>刚刚开业的Northern 160 BBQ为您准备好了丰盛的烤肉大餐，新张期间还有各种惊喜的礼物。（大纪元图片）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月11日讯】（大纪元纽约工商讯）平日里吃惯了母亲为我们精心制作的爱心美味，当母亲节这个感恩的日子来临时，再忙也要陪母亲吃一顿大餐。刚刚开业的Northern 160 BBQ为您准备好了丰盛的烤肉大餐，新张期间还有各种惊喜的礼物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13993257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993257-0b5e6556216921ee4a2edc4a460dc32b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13993257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993257-0b5e6556216921ee4a2edc4a460dc32b-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993257" class="wp-caption-text">刚刚开业的Northern 160 BBQ餐厅。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>上等食材 黑安格斯牛肉</h2>
<p>提起韩国料理，人们马上想到的就是“烧烤”：热铁板上刷一层清油，置牛排、牛舌、牛腰及各色海鲜于上，瞬间便会肉香袅袅，加之烤肉“滋滋”冒油的声音，不禁令人垂涎。</p>
<p>Northern 160 BBQ餐厅选用的是上等黑安格斯牛肉，安格斯牛肉有着大量细致、分布均匀的大理石纹理（Marbling），而无过量的残脂，肉质细嫩而多汁，是美国产的最好吃的牛肉。</p>
<h2></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13993261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993261-Screenshot-2023-05-10-154924.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13993261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993261-Screenshot-2023-05-10-154924-450x290.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993261" class="wp-caption-text">Northern 160 BBQ餐厅选用的是上等黑安格斯牛肉。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>秘制酱料 无添加剂</h2>
<p>吃烤肉一定要有好的酱料相配。一般市面上烤肉酱口味偏重(太油太咸)，往往会盖住食物的天然滋味。Northern 160 BBQ采用的是餐厅自制的特色烤肉酱。</p>
<p>厨房长卓明烈有25年餐饮经验，曾在在纽约曼哈顿韩国著名宫廷料理店掌厨13年。他精心选用天然食材，独家秘制酱料，无添加剂，酱料和烤肉相配，能将肉品的醇香美味激发出来，吃后不口渴，不油腻，美味又健康。</p>
<h2>新张酬宾 好礼相送</h2>
<p>装饰一新的Northern 160 BBQ能容纳130多人，还有30人的单间，非常适合亲朋好友年节聚会。餐厅外有停车位。新张酬宾期间，点两份烤肉，赠送一份豆腐煲+韩国小酒/饮料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13993265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993265" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993265-XXT36227.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13993265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993265-XXT36227-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993265" class="wp-caption-text">新张酬宾期间，点两份烤肉，赠送任一份豆腐煲+韩国小酒/饮料。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13993264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993264" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993264-XH2S1513.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13993264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993264-XH2S1513-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993264" class="wp-caption-text">装饰一新的Northern 160 BBQ能容纳130多人，还有30人的单间。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吃韩餐一定少不了小菜相配，Northern 160 BBQ的小菜品种非常丰富，还有特价午餐，凡用餐者还可获赠鸡蛋羹和螃蟹酱（晚餐）。</p>
<p>经理金艺红，精通英语、韩语、中文，可为华人提供更暖心、温馨的服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13993251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13993251" style="width: 269px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993251-ef12955d22871db3d7e64ecedacdf312.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13993251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13993251-ef12955d22871db3d7e64ecedacdf312-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="269" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13993251" class="wp-caption-text">经理金艺红精通中文，可为华人提供更贴心的服务。（餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>
