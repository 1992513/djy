<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北兴国中率团赴日参访岐阜县恵那东中学</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006444-616734-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>用英文介绍北兴国中。（北兴国中提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年05月30日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）为学子树立国际交流楷模，嘉义市北兴国中25日、26日，由张仁泽校长、黄家豪会长、余儒宜主任和曾钰婷老师带领国际交流社和双语营的25名学生访问了日本岐阜县恵那东中学。恵那东中学的西尾英宪校长全程接待，两校同学以全英文的方式进行了文化交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006445" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006445" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006445-616735.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006445" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006445-616735-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006445" class="wp-caption-text">交换礼物。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自2022年6月以来，北兴国中曾钰婷老师和惠那东中学尾関朋代老师与两校学生，每两个月举行一次线上英语交流，这得益于惠那东中学前任校长吉村良以及余儒宜主任的努力协调，彼此之间的友谊更加深厚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006446" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006446" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006446-616736.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006446" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006446-616736-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006446" class="wp-caption-text">交换礼物。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>交流活动在恵那东中学的体育馆举行的开幕典礼上，全校学生热烈欢迎了北兴国中的学生。随后，北兴国中的学生用全英语介绍了他们的学校和一天的学校生活等内容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006447" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006447-616737.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006447-616737-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006447" class="wp-caption-text">合照（左一起）余儒宜主任、黄家豪会长、西尾英宪校长、张仁泽校长 、教务主任胜川秀。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着，北兴国中的学生分头进入各个教室，展开了破冰小活动，并根据恵那东中学的课表体验了当地的课程，还参与了全校性的拔河活动。起初紧张的学生们逐渐放松下来，并享受着用英语进行交流的乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006448-616738.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006448-616738-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006448" class="wp-caption-text">合照台日交流。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在第二天的交流活动中，恵那东中学的师长号召志愿学生与北兴国中的同学一起游览了当地的岩村町和恵那车站周边，深入体验了恵那市的历史和文化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006449-616739.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006449-616739-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006449" class="wp-caption-text">和服体验。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张仁泽校长表示，他非常感谢吉村良前校长的大力协助，以及恵那东中学西尾英宪校长领导的团队，才能让北兴的学生有这难得的日本入班学习体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006450-616740.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006450-616740-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006450" class="wp-caption-text">乡野踏查。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄家豪会长表示，代表家长会感谢北兴国际交流社的余儒宜主任及曾钰婷老师多年来的努力，让北兴国中走向国际，世界走进北兴，实现了国际教育的愿景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006451-616741.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006451-616741-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006451" class="wp-caption-text">与日本学生上课交流。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国际交流社的王毓婷社长表示，这次能够到日本入班学习是一次非常棒的体验。日本的教室非常整洁，给她留下了深刻的印象。而第二天的和服体验更是特别，充分感受到了日本文化的细腻之处，感谢所有师长的指导。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006452-616742.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006452-616742-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006452" class="wp-caption-text">与日本学生上课交流。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>副社长力百恩同学表示，从在校内视讯相见欢开始，一直到真正互相拥抱实体交流相见欢开始，心中是期待是兴奋是感动，最感动的课程是拔河竞赛，校方特意安排两校学生混合分成四组，在过程中虽然彼此陌生，但为了赢得胜利同心协力团结一心完成比赛，这就是交流最重要的意义。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14006453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14006453" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006453-616743.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14006453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14006453-616743-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14006453" class="wp-caption-text">体育馆的欢迎仪式。（北兴国中提供）</figcaption></figure>
