<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>碧贵园功夫茶楼 湾区正宗北京烤鸭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997920-IMG_6420-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>碧贵园烤鸭一上架，让人垂涎三尺。（旧金山新唐人）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年05月16日讯】文：腊梅<br />
碧贵园功夫茶楼，从外面看平平常常，但是楼内全新装修，最重要的是，北京烤鸭特价$28.80！片、切、手撕??怎么吃都无敌美味！一桌围一圈，热热闹闹，不管是同事、家人、好友一起吃，所有的晦气都会全部无影无踪！</p>
<p>碧贵园功夫茶楼的李老板21岁就开始做厨师，已有35年的厨师经验。李老板2011年来美国，从做厨师再到自己创业，用心开了碧贵园功夫茶楼，苦心经营，跟员工打成一片，一起吃饭、一起工作、一起娱乐、一起聊天，就像兄弟姐妹。<figure id="attachment_13997933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997933-43f66c2c5b0815d6243dd9ac80114bb4.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997933-43f66c2c5b0815d6243dd9ac80114bb4-600x406.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="406" class="size-large wp-image-13997933" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997933" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园砂锅炖鸡。（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_13997934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997934-a3b17339b8151d6dbe1534c87764316f.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997934-a3b17339b8151d6dbe1534c87764316f-600x395.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="395" class="size-large wp-image-13997934" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997934" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园牛仔骨木瓜。（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>最近，碧贵园刚刚从大陆航运过来烤鸭的架子，用这种专属的架子把刚刚出炉的正宗北京烤鸭架上，家人、亲朋好友的眼睛都会直直地盯着烤鸭，恨不得赶紧吃上一口！<figure id="attachment_13997921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997921-ed22752942058d62578dbe664a4a0348.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997921-ed22752942058d62578dbe664a4a0348-600x493.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="493" class="size-large wp-image-13997921" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997921" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园功夫茶楼的北京烤鸭特价$28.80！（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>北京烤鸭，片皮，口齿留香；切肉，清香鲜嫩；骨架炖汤，滋润心田的淳香。</p>
<p>李老板做菜用心、做事也用心，人好做出的东西也好吃！我邀过许多朋友到碧贵园用餐聚会，大家都满嘴留香，赞不绝口！<figure id="attachment_13997922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997922-046c36cd62acf801ec50a422881876b7.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997922-046c36cd62acf801ec50a422881876b7-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-13997922" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997922" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园酸菜鱼。（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>碧贵园从地毯、墙壁、天花板全新布置；漂亮的窗台、白色雅致的窗纱，用餐环境宾至如归，让一天的疲惫，或是一周工作完的劳累，全都在饭席间化为乌有。<figure id="attachment_13997924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997924-e8c712c1c77adc630d05b95250ea0f13.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997924-e8c712c1c77adc630d05b95250ea0f13-600x389.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="389" class="size-large wp-image-13997924" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997924" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园清汤牛腩。（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>这里的经理或员工都会主动接待你，安排舒适的座位，让你备感温馨。他们有包间、卡拉OK厅、大小会议厅、麻将室??完全符合华人朋友的用餐习惯。<figure id="attachment_13997925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997925-082a6c01ddd98518b09d7b43a600f82b.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997925-082a6c01ddd98518b09d7b43a600f82b-600x420.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="420" class="size-large wp-image-13997925" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997925" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园麻辣烤盘鱼。（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>功夫茶缸、茶壶、茶盘、小茶碗??精美精致，是你接待生意伙伴、亲朋好友的最佳餐馆。<figure id="attachment_13997926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997926-fd6ba85c7466e753d669b2759d69c2b2.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997926-fd6ba85c7466e753d669b2759d69c2b2-600x325.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="325" class="size-large wp-image-13997926" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997926" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园手工粤菜点心。（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_13997928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997928-7dc3cbbee3b70559b0489c05b1714285.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997928-7dc3cbbee3b70559b0489c05b1714285-600x550.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="550" class="size-large wp-image-13997928" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997928" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园各式小粤式茶点。（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_13997932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13997932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997932-feb64d085c2f8043f3104ae3dc1d4785.jpeg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13997932-feb64d085c2f8043f3104ae3dc1d4785-600x366.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="366" class="size-large wp-image-13997932" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13997932" class="wp-caption-text">碧贵园生焗瓦罉鸡。（旧金山新唐人）</figcaption></figure></p>
