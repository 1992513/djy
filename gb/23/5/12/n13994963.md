<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【组图】第14届牛顿台湾日盛况空前 估计超过2000人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994950-1-2-1TaiwanDay-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月6日是第14届“牛顿台湾日”，广教学校及Bailemos舞蹈工作室带来的舞蹈，观众围满了露天舞台，后面摊位帐蓬林立。（冯文鸾/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月13日讯】（大纪元记者冯文鸾牛顿市报导）“天佑台湾”，这是很多上周六“第14届牛顿台湾日”（Newton Taiwan Day）<wbr />参加者的感受。的确。历经了近一周的阴风冷雨，<wbr />老天却赐给这个年度大活动一个温暖晴朗的好天气，<wbr />难怪从一早就不断的涌进人潮，据估计可能超过2000人。</p>
<p>上午11点准时升旗典礼，然后就开始游行，<wbr />在中华民俗艺术工作坊的“龙舞吉祥”大鼓声中，<wbr />政要嘉宾及各社团代表纷纷走上街头。<wbr />队伍中有侨教中心的两尊三太子，<wbr />北一女校友会队伍前一位超胖的大绿人特别引人注目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13994953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994953-1-2-4TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994953-1-2-4TaiwanDay-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994953" class="wp-caption-text">游行队伍由中华民俗艺术工作坊打头阵。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13994955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994955" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994955-1-2-5TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994955-1-2-5TaiwanDay-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994955" class="wp-caption-text">人们买了美食，在园游会角落的野餐桌共同享受午餐。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>短暂游行之后，先来个大合照，随即开幕式由吴萍萍和朱苏珊主持。<wbr />嘉宾政要一一致辞，到场的主流政要有牛顿市长Ruthanne Fuller、<wbr />麻州州众议员黄子安及Medford市议员曾御宸。<wbr />黄子安还代表麻州众议院颁发贺状给牛顿台湾日主任委员蒋宗壬。</p>
<p>此外，经文处孙俭元处长、侨务委员郭竞儒和陈仕维、<wbr />纽英崙中华公所雷国辉主席、侨教中心潘昭荣主任等，<wbr />亦分别致词及分享“台湾日活动”的心得。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13994951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994951-1-2-2TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994951-1-2-2TaiwanDay-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994951" class="wp-caption-text">5月6日“牛顿台湾日”嘉宾合影，（左起）潘昭荣、陈仕维、郭竞儒、曾御宸、吴萍萍、朱苏珊、Ruthanne Fuller、John Rice、雷国辉、蒋宗壬、黄子安、孙俭元。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13994956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994956" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994956-1-2-6TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994956" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994956-1-2-6TaiwanDay-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994956" class="wp-caption-text">政大校友会联合北一女校友会一起摆摊。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13994957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994957" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994957-1-2-7TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994957" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994957-1-2-7TaiwanDay-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994957" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙客家乡亲会展示客家文物。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年大会有45个摊位，各式台湾美食、游戏、宣传等，<wbr />例如猛男鸡排摊位前大排长龙，<wbr />有人抱怨排了一个小时结果轮到她的时候竟然卖完了。<wbr />还有专协的Deer Musik 小鹿音乐的粉浆蛋饼、奶茶以及Ed Chen台湾车轮饼有怀旧的感觉，都很受欢迎。</p>
<p>黄子安议员在波士顿台湾世卫协进会的展位上和黄柏荣医生相谈甚欢<wbr />，两人就气功与保健方面交流很愉快。<wbr />据会长林思妤表示原本打算举办捐血活动，<wbr />可是红十字会那天无法安排，因此改为保健咨询。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13994952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994952-1-2-3TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994952-1-2-3TaiwanDay-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994952" class="wp-caption-text">5月6日“牛顿台湾日”，麻州众议员黄子安到台湾世卫协进会的展位和黄柏荣医生相谈甚欢。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13994958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994958" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994958-1-2-8TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994958" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994958-1-2-8TaiwanDay-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994958" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心的两尊三太子出巡了。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了琳琅满目的摊位，还有露天舞台的各项表演。如中华民俗艺术工作坊的大鼓、舞龙、舞狮、扯铃、<wbr />民族舞蹈一气呵成；<wbr />中华广教学校及Bailemos舞蹈工作室的舞蹈；<wbr />李芳芝学生的太极拳；黄正昇医师校园民歌集锦；黄郁维大提琴；<wbr />陈秀惠老师NIA带动跳；以及压轴的大波士顿You &amp; Me流行音乐乐团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13994959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994959" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994959-1-2-9TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994959-1-2-9TaiwanDay-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994959" class="wp-caption-text">专协带来台湾车轮饼。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13994960" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13994960" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994960-1-2-10TaiwanDay.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13994960" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13994960-1-2-10TaiwanDay-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13994960" class="wp-caption-text">摊位和表演人士大合照。（冯文鸾/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天有不少族裔的观众，甚至远从临近的新罕布什州，<wbr />罗德岛州来的。有位刚搬到美国的刘女士第一次参加，<wbr />因为不知道要用现金，所以就买不了太多食物。<wbr />不过她说很讶异这个活动如此盛大热闹，也算开了眼界。◇</p>
