<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>香煎琵琶燕窝 款待父母的好滋味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000745-10-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香煎琵琶燕窝。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong> ?<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8.md#1">梁厨</a>设计了这一道“香煎琵琶<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%87%95%E7%AA%9D.md#1">燕窝</a>”，让想对父母表示孝心的子女，在家就可以亲自动手制作，不会因为忙碌或厨艺不精而却步，做法清晰简单。</p>
<p><a title="2023年5月3日 香煎琵琶燕窝全过程做法" src="https://www.ganjing.com/zh-TW/embed/1fqq99egv8p2K49AHWVJvqjM11iv1c" width="640" b="360" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<h3>【材料】</h3>
<p>湿<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%87%95%E7%AA%9D.md#1">燕窝</a> 3 oz<br />
蛋白 4个<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%B6%E6%9F%B1.md#1">瑶柱</a> 1.2 oz<br />
小白菜 9棵<br />
鱼子、芫荽叶 少许（装饰用）<br />
草莓 适量（装饰用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000746-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000746-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000746" class="wp-caption-text">准备材料：湿燕窝、蛋白、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%B6%E6%9F%B1.md#1">瑶柱</a>、小白菜、鱼子、芫荽叶、草莓。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【调味】</h3>
<p>烹大师 1/2茶匙<br />
盐 1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉 少许<br />
鸡汤 1杯<br />
粟粉 1茶匙（加少许水备用）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000747" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000747-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000747-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000747" class="wp-caption-text">调味料（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【作法】</h3>
<p>1. 燕窝先用水泡开，再用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。小白菜洗净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000750" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000750-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000750-3-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000750" class="wp-caption-text">燕窝先用水泡开，再用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 准备9根汤匙，先涂上少许油备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000751" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000751-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000751-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000751" class="wp-caption-text">准备9根汤匙，先涂上少许油备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蛋白打散后加入烹大师、盐、胡椒粉、燕窝和瑶柱，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000752-f2d4c048d9eb7d752e2fc49341ee3230.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000752-f2d4c048d9eb7d752e2fc49341ee3230-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000752" class="wp-caption-text">蛋白打散后加入烹大师、盐、胡椒粉、燕窝和瑶柱，搅拌均匀。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将“作法3”倒入每一根汤匙中，分别放上一小片芫荽叶装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000753-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000753-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000753" class="wp-caption-text">将“作法3”倒入每一根汤匙中，分别放上一小片芫荽叶装饰。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 蒸锅内放入燕窝隔水蒸5分钟，取出后用小刀把燕窝挖出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000756" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000756-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000756-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000756" class="wp-caption-text">蒸锅内放入燕窝隔水蒸5分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 小白菜用油盐水烫熟后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000757" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000757-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000757-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000757" class="wp-caption-text">小白菜用油盐水烫熟后备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 热锅下油，放入燕窝两面煎香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14000758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14000758" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000758-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14000758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14000758-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14000758" class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，放入燕窝两面煎香。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 鸡汤加盐、胡椒粉和粟粉水煮成琉璃芡备用。<br />
9. <strong>摆盘：</strong>盘子中央摆上草莓装饰，放上小白菜，煎好的琵琶燕窝间隔排在小白菜中间。淋上琉璃芡，再放少许鱼子装饰。◇</p>
