<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马自达2024全新旗舰式SUV CX-90休斯顿开售</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988347-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.12.25-AM-600x400.png" />
<h6>Jeff Haas Mazda车行外景。（大纪元图片）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月05日讯】（大纪元休斯顿工商报导）令人瞩目的马自达最新款的SUV CX-90今年推向市场，这款旗舰式，中大型SUV融合了该品牌最新技术，实现马自达进军豪车档级的目标。今天就可以来休斯顿最大的马自达车行Jeff Haas Mazda来体验这款高级SUV。</p>
<p>Jeff Haas Mazda总经理Todd Crabtree说：“马自达一直向高端车发展，这里是首款百分之百的高端车CX-90。”</p>
<p>马自达全新车款CX-90配备有最新的后驱平台，搭载马自达量产车最强劲的3.3升直列6缸发动机，以及八速自排变速箱。CX-90有三种动力车型，包括纯燃油版提供280匹马力，插电混合版车型323匹马力，以及动力最强大的Turbo S提供340匹马力。全车四轮驱动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13988348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13988348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988348-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.10.17-AM.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13988348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988348-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.10.17-AM-600x339.png" alt="" width="600" b="339"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13988348" class="wp-caption-text">马自达2024全新旗舰式SUV CX-90 （大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13988351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13988351" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988351-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.14.44-AM.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13988351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988351-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.14.44-AM-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13988351" class="wp-caption-text">马自达2024全新旗舰式SUV CX-90 （大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Jeff Haas Mazda 新车销售 Sutoyo Sugiharto说：“有二、三个孩子的家庭，带祖辈一起出行，这款车是最合适的。”</p>
<p>三排座椅提供6人座、7人座，以及8人座的布局。内观设计优雅现代，采用金属、枫木和面料等材质，前方两个座椅都配备电子控制和温度控制，驾驶座位具有座椅设置记忆功能，中控面板搭载大尺寸全液晶显示屏，CX-90还配置了Carplay, 无线充电，手机启动，辅助驾驶等功能，以及分布于三排座的USB接口和空调，车窗还有收放式遮阳板。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13988350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13988350" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988350-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.13.56-AM.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13988350" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988350-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.13.56-AM-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13988350" class="wp-caption-text">马自达2024全新旗舰式SUV CX-90 （大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13988349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13988349" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988349-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.11.54-AM.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13988349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13988349-Screen-Shot-2023-05-04-at-11.11.54-AM-600x339.png" alt="" width="600" b="339"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13988349" class="wp-caption-text">马自达2024全新旗舰式SUV CX-90 （大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>CX-90外观的前脸具有视觉冲击力的大尺寸中网设计，金属镀铬贯穿车体，风格大气雅致，前脸两侧有大进气口，21英寸的轮圈，彰显运动气息，前叶板装点INLINE6的铭牌，突出CX-90骄傲的动力搭载。</p>
<p>CX-90车款价格四万多起价，今天就来Jeff Haas Mazda体验CX-90旗舰SUV，他们有中文，英文，越南语和菲律宾语等多语种服务，联系电话：713-932-6004，地址：16711 Katy Fwy, Houston, TX 77094。</p>
