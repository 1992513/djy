<p><strong>小编语：</strong>?想炒出一盘丰富滋味的米粉不是难事！梁厨这次教大家炒米粉“要先煎后炒”，第一次先用干煎的方式煎香，第二次加入调味料和水滚烫后再下米粉炒香。至于配料鸡蛋丝、叉烧丝和各类蔬菜也要分开处理。最后再把所有配料和米粉炒在一起，味道融合，营养也满满，淀粉、菜、肉都有了，一锅到底的好料理，是省时省力又好吃的佳肴。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">甜杂锦菜叉烧炒米粉</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>米粉适量<br />
甜杂锦菜3 oz<br />
叉烧丝适量<br />
荞头3粒<br />
洋葱、红椒、西芹各3 oz（切丝备用）<br />
鸡蛋2颗<br />
芫荽、芝麻适量（装饰用）</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>水1杯<br />
蚝油1汤匙<br />
茄汁3汤匙<br />
美极鲜酱油1/4茶匙<br />
胡椒粉少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 各种材料切丝备用。鸡蛋打散，煎成蛋饼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004611" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004611" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004611-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14004611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004611-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004611" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋打散，煎成蛋饼。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 煎好的蛋饼再切丝备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004612" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004612-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14004612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004612-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004612" class="wp-caption-text">煎好的蛋饼再切丝备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热锅下油，先将米粉煎香盛出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004613" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004613-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14004613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004613-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004613" class="wp-caption-text">米粉煎香盛出备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 下油爆香叉烧丝及各类蔬菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004649" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004649-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14004649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004649-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004649" class="wp-caption-text">下油爆香叉烧丝及各类蔬菜。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 加入水及调味煮滚后放回米粉，翻炒均匀好便可装盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004650" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004650-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14004650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004650-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004650" class="wp-caption-text">加入水及调味煮滚后放回米粉，翻炒均匀好便可装盘。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放上蛋丝、芫荽和芝麻即完成。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004651" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004651-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14004651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004651-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004651" class="wp-caption-text">放上蛋丝、芫荽和芝麻即完成。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
