<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：美国哈士奇一双锐利蓝眼 爆红网络</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998749-Screenshot-2023-05-17-161700-600x400.png" />
<h6>美国一只哈士奇因长着一双锐利的蓝眼睛和高大的身材而在互联网上走红。（Nicole Spahn提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月17日讯】（英文大纪元记者<ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/siberian-husky-as-huge-as-a-bear-captivates-everyone-with-his-piercing-blue-eyes_5222072.md#1">Louise Chambers</a>报导／张雨霏编译）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E5%A3%AB%E5%A5%87.md#1">哈士奇</a>（学名<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E4%BC%AF%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A%E9%9B%AA%E6%A9%87%E7%8A%AC.md#1">西伯利亚雪橇犬</a>，昵称<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%8C%E5%93%88.md#1">二哈</a>）凭借其英俊的外表和坚毅的眼神越来越受人喜欢，甚至已经成为全球盛行的宠物犬。而美国一只哈士奇更是因长着一双锐利的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E7%9C%BC%E7%9D%9B.md#1">蓝眼睛</a>和高大的身材而在互联网上风靡一时。</p>
<p>这只雄性<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E5%A3%AB%E5%A5%87.md#1">哈士奇</a>全名叫做SeQuoi Tenko，其中Sequoi是红杉树（世界上最高的树木之一）的意思，因为它的主人妮可?斯潘（Nicole Spahn）希望它将来长得能像红杉一样高大、强壮有力。妮可习惯简称它为Quoi。</p>
<p>Quoi生于2020年1月7日，当它约两个月大时，妮可正式领养了它。“我很高兴拥有一只哈士奇，我可以和它一起做运动。”32岁的妮可告诉《大纪元时报》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13998740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998740" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998740-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13998740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998740-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko2.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="532" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998740" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13998742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998742" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998742-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko31.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13998742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998742-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko31.jpeg" alt="" width="401" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998742" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妮可第一次看到SeQuoi的照片时，被它额头上的一个白点吸引住了。她立刻觉得他们“属于彼此”。</p>
<p>“有趣的是，那个白点现在已经消失了，除了胸前有一个白点和爪子上的几个白点外，它基本上是纯红色的。”妮可说。</p>
<p>虽然妮可从未见过Quoi的父母，它们都是在美国养犬俱乐部（AKC）注册的纯种<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E4%BC%AF%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A%E9%9B%AA%E6%A9%87%E7%8A%AC.md#1">西伯利亚雪橇犬</a>，但她知道它的母亲是短毛、纯黑的，而它的父亲大部分毛色是纯红色，同时身上也有几个白色斑点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13998744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998744" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998744-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko54.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13998744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998744-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko54.jpeg" alt="" width="401" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998744" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13998739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998739" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998739-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13998739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998739-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko1.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998739" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妮可居住在怀俄明州杰克逊霍尔郊外的提顿谷（Teton Valley），她和Quoi都喜欢怀俄明州的雪，一人一狗几乎是形影不离。由于妮可在家办公，所以他们白天只能在家度过，但经常利用早上和晚上的空闲时间到户外“冒险”。</p>
<p>“如果是冬天，我们会去野外滑雪”，她说，“在夏季，我们会一起骑山地车或越野摩托车。”</p>
<p>这对人狗“搭档”喜欢尝试新事物。他们乘坐过摩托艇和雪地摩托、划过桨、一起远足，还曾到野外露营探险。</p>
<p>“我认为我们之间能够形成牢固的纽带，是因为我能够满足它作为一只西伯利亚雪橇犬的很多需求。”妮可说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13998746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998746" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998746-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko39.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13998746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998746-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko39.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998746" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13998743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998743" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998743-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko35.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13998743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998743-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko35.jpeg" alt="" width="401" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998743" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她形容Quoi是“完美的”，它非常喜欢小孩子，而且超级友善，并将它的性格描述为“漂亮而又愚蠢，顽皮中带着威严”。</p>
<p>然而，饲养一只哈士奇绝非易事。“西伯利亚雪橇犬属于工作犬”，妮可说，“它们以‘逃家’和‘拆家’而闻名，尤其是精力过剩、无处发泄的时候。因此，它们需要一个有充足时间陪伴它们的主人。</p>
<p>“总的来说，哈士奇比其它犬种需要更多的训练。它们也因非常挑食而闻名，所以在它们的零食和食物上需要投入大笔资金。当它们厌倦某种食物时，主人要及时帮它们调换。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13998745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998745" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998745-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko37.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13998745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998745-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko37.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998745" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13998747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998747" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998747-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko46.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13998747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998747-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko46.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998747" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>截至目前，这只体型巨大的Quoi凭借其毛茸茸的红色皮毛和明亮锐利的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E7%9C%BC%E7%9D%9B.md#1">蓝眼睛</a>已经累积了超过18万的<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/huskyquoi/">Instagram</a>粉丝。</p>
<p>然而，令妮可困扰的是，虽然Quoi从照片中看起来很健康，但它实际上患有一种非常难治愈的疾病——癫痫病，而且是由遗传引起的。</p>
<p>为了确保狗的基因健康，妮可建议人们从动物救援组织领养年长的狗，那里寄养着无数渴望得到温暖和关爱的狗狗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13998766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13998766" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998766-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko33.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13998766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13998766-siberian-husky-Sequoi-Tenko33.jpeg" alt="" width="399" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13998766" class="wp-caption-text">（Nicole Spahn提供）</figcaption></figure>
