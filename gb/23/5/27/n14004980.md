<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：横跨半世纪的澳洲沉船之迷终解开</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004991-Screenshot-2023-05-27-165610-600x400.png" />
<h6>澳洲最大的海难事件谜团之一在50年后终于得以解开。（CSIRO提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月27日讯】（英文大纪元记者<ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/ocean-researchers-solve-mystery-of-tasmanian-shipwrecked-freighter-that-was-lost-50-years-ago_5281865.md#1" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">Michael Wing</a>报导／张雨霏编译）大约50年前，澳洲一艘船只在塔斯马尼亚海岸沉没，并导致3名船员罹难。几十年来，当局一直努力找寻这艘44米长货船的残骸，直到近期，这一沉船之谜终于得以解开。</p>
<p>澳洲联邦科学与工业研究组织<ahref="https://www.csiro.au/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">CSIRO</a>）的海洋探险家前不久搭乘“调查者”号研究船（RV Investigator），展开了一项为期38天的海洋考察，以研究塔斯马尼亚州西海岸附近的一个水下滑坡块体（underwater landslide）。</p>
<p>与此同时，这支由塔斯马尼亚大学领导的探险队还肩负着另一项艰巨的任务，即搜索一艘在近50年前沉没的沿海货轮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004989" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004989-CSIRO-research-vessel-RV-Investigator-starboard-sideCSIRO-copy.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14004989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004989-CSIRO-research-vessel-RV-Investigator-starboard-sideCSIRO-copy.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004989" class="wp-caption-text">“调查者”号研究船。（CSIRO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14004983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004983" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004983-00_Approximate-location-of-the-wreck-of-MV-Blythe-Star.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14004983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004983-00_Approximate-location-of-the-wreck-of-MV-Blythe-Star.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004983" class="wp-caption-text">MV Blythe Star沉没的大致位置。（CSIRO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14004984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004984" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004984-03e_CSIRO-project-team-identify-letters-S-T-A-R-on-bow_drop-camera_CSIRO-copy.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14004984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004984-03e_CSIRO-project-team-identify-letters-S-T-A-R-on-bow_drop-camera_CSIRO-copy.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="338"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004984" class="wp-caption-text">CSIRO研究人员乘坐“调查者”号研究船探索MV Blythe Star。（CSIRO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1973年10月13日，这艘44米长的“MV Blythe Star”号机动船满载货物从塔斯马尼亚州首府霍巴特（Hobart）启航，准备前往该州西北方向的离岛国王岛（King Island，又译金岛）。据报导，由于重心不稳，该货船航行不久就突然倾斜进水并很快倾覆。</p>
<p>虽然MV Blythe Star上的所有10名船员设法逃离下沉船只并登上了一艘充气救生艇，但其中3名船员在获救前不幸遇难。近半个世纪以来，MV Blythe Star的沉没位置一直是个谜团。</p>
<p>结合当地渔民提供的信息和水下调查报告的数据，在塔斯马尼亚西南角（South West Cape）以西10.5公里处有一艘身份不明的沉船，“调查者”号研究船就是根据这一线索展开了此次探险任务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004990" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004990-01d-MV-Blythe-Star_190deg_CSIRO-2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14004990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004990-01d-MV-Blythe-Star_190deg_CSIRO-2.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004990" class="wp-caption-text">水下摄像机传来的沉船画面。（CSIRO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14004985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004985" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004985-04a_Drop-camera_Part-of-ship-name-S-T-A-R-identified-on-bow_CSIRO.png" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14004985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004985-04a_Drop-camera_Part-of-ship-name-S-T-A-R-identified-on-bow_CSIRO.png" alt="" width="599" b="449"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004985" class="wp-caption-text">沉船的船首。（CSIRO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14004986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004986" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004986-04dDTCView-of-upper-boat-deck-showing-stairs-centre-and-davits-cranes-for-life-boatsCSIRO-copy.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14004986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004986-04dDTCView-of-upper-boat-deck-showing-stairs-centre-and-davits-cranes-for-life-boatsCSIRO-copy.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="337"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004986" class="wp-caption-text">船甲板。（CSIRO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>CSIRO在一份新闻稿中透露，塔斯马尼亚大学的研究人员于2023年4月12日确定该沉船为MV Blythe Star。</p>
<p>该团队利用多波束回声测深仪和两部水下摄像装置，系统地绘制出了这艘沉船残骸的全景地图，并识别出船头上的“STAR”字样，从而使它的身份得到确认。</p>
<p>研究人员发现，尽管MV Blythe Star有一些损坏痕迹，尤其是在船尾，但大体上完好无损，整艘沉船端坐在海底。海底测绘数据显示，该沉船位于水下约150米处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14004987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004987" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004987-04e_DTC_View-of-stern-of-MV-Blythe-Star_CSIRO-copy.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14004987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004987-04e_DTC_View-of-stern-of-MV-Blythe-Star_CSIRO-copy.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="337"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004987" class="wp-caption-text">船尾。（CSIRO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14004988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14004988" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004988-05-CSIRO-project-team-onboard-CSIRO-research-vessel-RV-InvestigatorCSIRO.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14004988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id14004988-05-CSIRO-project-team-onboard-CSIRO-research-vessel-RV-InvestigatorCSIRO.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14004988" class="wp-caption-text">CSIRO 探险队。（CSIRO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据了解，与此次沉船事件相关的部门和人员，包括最近成立的Blythe Star Memorial Group所有成员，将于今年10月在霍巴特举办一场活动，以纪念这场跨越50年的海上悲剧。</p>
<p>“在MV Blythe Star悲剧发生后，对该事件的审查直接导致澳大利亚海事安全法发生了重大变化，从而显着提高了海员未来的海上安全”，CSIRO表示，“CSIRO很高兴能够协助解开这个长达50年的谜团，并确认了MV Blythe Star的最终安息之地。”◇</p>
