<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：沉睡美国湖底逾百年货船终被寻获</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986535-ironton-shipwreck-deck-1894-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年05月03日讯】（英文大纪元记者<ahref="https://www.theepochtimes.com/ocean-researchers-find-1800s-shipwreck-frozen-in-time-that-crashed-on-lake-huron-because-of-this_5219050.md#1" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer"><span style="font-weight: 400">Michael Wing</span></a>报导／张雨霏编译）长久以来，有不少船只在美国密歇根州休伦湖（Lake Huron）北部的桑德湾（Thunder Bay）地区沉没。1894年9月一个晴朗的夜晚，一艘58米长的木船“艾恩顿”号（Ironton）与一艘近62米长的木货轮“俄亥俄”号（Ohio）就在该水域相撞。</p>
<p>由于事发时两艘船都满载货物，因此相撞后便急速下沉。虽然“俄亥俄”号上的16名船员全部被附近的船只救起，但“艾恩顿”号上只有2名船员生还，其余5人包括船长在内却不幸遇难。</p>
<p>一个多世纪以来，许多科学家及海洋考古学家一直努力找寻这两艘大型货船的残骸，近期终于传来了好消息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13986534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986534" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986534-ironton-shipwreck-bowsprit.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13986534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986534-ironton-shipwreck-bowsprit.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="337"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986534" class="wp-caption-text">美国海洋研究人员终于在密歇根州休伦湖寻获1894年沉没的“艾恩顿”号货船。（NOAA Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary, Undersea Vehicles Program UNCW, Ocean Exploration Trust提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>密歇根州桑德湾国家海洋保护区（Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary）及合作伙伴于2017年在撞船区域扩大搜寻范围，首先成功找到了“俄亥俄”号沉船的残骸，但“艾恩顿”号的最终安息地一直是个谜团。</p>
<p>直到2019年一艘3.6米长的柴油动力自动驾驶测绘船BEN的出现，才使得研究团队有了新突破。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13986541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986541-tbnmsasv-bencopy.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13986541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986541-tbnmsasv-bencopy.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986541" class="wp-caption-text">3.6米长的柴油动力自动驾驶测绘船BEN。（Ocean Exploration Trust/NOAA Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同一年，桑德湾国家海洋保护区与由巴拉德（Robert Ballard）博士创立的海洋勘探信托基金（Ocean Exploration Trust）合作，通过多波束声纳成像技术，并结合部署BEN测绘船，最后成功锁定了一艘与“艾恩顿”号特征相匹配的沉船残骸，但其真实身份还有待进一步确认。</p>
<p>直到2021年，该研究团队又返回沉船地点展开更彻底的调查，他们此次与五大湖水研究所等多个机构合作部署水下机器人（ROV），最终获得了显示沉船状况的高分辨率影片，“艾恩顿”号的神秘面纱才终于被揭开。由于受到休伦湖冰冷的淡水保护，该沉船现如今看起来几乎完好无损。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13986536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986536" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986536-ironton-side-scan-sonar.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13986536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986536-ironton-side-scan-sonar.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="451"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986536" class="wp-caption-text">“艾恩顿”号的声纳图像。（NOAA Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13986537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986537-noaa-ironton-shipwreck-multibeam.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13986537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986537-noaa-ironton-shipwreck-multibeam.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="408"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986537" class="wp-caption-text">“艾恩顿”号的高分辨率声纳图像。（Ocean Exploration Trust/NOAA Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桑德湾国家海洋保护区负责人杰夫?格雷（Jeff Gray）在一份<ahref="https://sanctuaries.noaa.gov/news/mar23/ironton-discovery.md#1" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">新闻稿</a>中说，这艘58米长的木制帆船端端正正地沉没在休伦湖水面以下约300英尺（约合91米）处，三根桅杆垂直朝向天空，保存状况非常好。</p>
<p>“利用这些尖端技术，我们不仅找到了一艘遗失了一个多世纪的古老沉船，而且还在更多地了解我们国家最重要的自然资源之一——五大湖。”格雷说，“这一发现将有助于保护休伦湖及其丰富的历史。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13986535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986535" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986535-ironton-shipwreck-deck-1894.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13986535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986535-ironton-shipwreck-deck-1894.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="337"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986535" class="wp-caption-text">这艘58米长的木制帆船端端正正地沉没在休伦湖水面以下约300英尺处。（NOAA Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary, Undersea Vehicles Program UNCW, Ocean Exploration Trust提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13986540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986540" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986540-ironton-crew.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13986540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986540-ironton-crew.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986540" class="wp-caption-text">美国海洋研究人员终于在密歇根州休伦湖寻获1894年沉没的“艾恩顿”号货船。（Ocean Exploration Trust/NOAA提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据了解，19世纪末期，美国五大湖地区的贸易往来处于“繁盛期”，成千上万的帆船、轮船在芝加哥、底特律及克利夫兰等港口城市之间往来，运载包括小麦、煤炭、玉米、木材和铁矿石在内的大批货物，但估计有200多艘船只在此沉没。◇</p>
