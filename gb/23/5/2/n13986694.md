<h2><strong>桃Prunus persica蔷薇科</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13986713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986713-603912_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13986713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986713-603912_medium-600x445.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986713" class="wp-caption-text">花丛生1~3朵，花色繁多，有粉红、白、红及深红，但以粉红色居多，花期1~2月。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13986715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986715" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986715-603915_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13986715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986715-603915_medium-600x801.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986715" class="wp-caption-text">落叶小乔木，经二千余年的栽培，已衍生为观赏及结实两大品系，品种多达数百种。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>又称甜桃、桃仔、桃仔树、山苦桃、毛桃、白桃、红桃、桃仁。落叶小乔木，经二千余年的栽培，已衍生为观赏及结实两大品系，品种多达数百种。桃和樱花一样都选在春季开放，株高可达5公尺，树皮灰色，小枝光滑。叶互生，卵形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13986714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986714-603914_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13986714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986714-603914_medium-600x799.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986714" class="wp-caption-text">一树桃花千朵红，艳色无双舞春风。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13986712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986712" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986712-603911_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13986712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986712-603911_medium-600x905.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="905" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986712" class="wp-caption-text">嫩绿新叶轻摆手，浅笑千愁渺无踪。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花丛生1~3朵，花色繁多，有粉红、白、红及深红，但以粉红色居多，花期1~2月。核果阔卵形，先端锐尖，外面密生短柔毛，果肉肥厚，果核有皱沟。果实可食，可生津、润肠活血、消积，是重要的观赏树木及温带果树。核仁及花可入药。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13986709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13986709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986709-603917_medium.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13986709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13986709-603917_medium-600x445.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13986709" class="wp-caption-text">果实可食，可生津、润肠活血、消积，是重要的观赏树木及温带果树。核仁及花可入药。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
