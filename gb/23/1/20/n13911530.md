<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>制作风味盐5法 不会做菜也能端出惊艳料理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911580-20230120_salt_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>只要维持惯用的烹调手法，将调味改成风味盐，就能如同魔法般瞬间改变料理本质；让不擅长做菜的人，端出惊艳料理。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：调味是料理的灵魂，而盐是调味的核心！全台湾最懂得运用香草的料理老师，带你以拌合、研磨、炒焙手法，将植物香气与盐结合，做出一罐就有丰富层次的迷迭香盐、花椒盐、印度咖喱盐……成为料理高手的秘密，只在指间的那一撮。</p>
<h2>制作风味盐的基本工具</h2>
<p>风味盐的制作要领在于香气的调配、释放与融合，仅需要将香味来源与盐充分混合即可。我自己最常使用到的工具包含以下三种，依照自己的需求选用即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13911534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911534-P.28.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13911534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911534-P.28-600x687.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="515" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911534" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>研磨钵</h3>
<p>少量制作时很方便，一边打碎香料一边让香气与盐充分融合。</p>
<h3>调理机</h3>
<p>大型或小型的都可以，可以让大片的叶状香草与盐质地更细致均匀。</p>
<h3>研磨器</h3>
<p>挑选平刀的产品，能够将胡椒、花椒等颗粒状的香料打碎。</p>
<h2>风味盐的基础制作方式</h2>
<p>简单来说，就是香气物质的形体变化，与盐拌合一起，让香气被盐吸附，成为风味盐。依照香味来源的形状、质地、含水量等因素，大约有以下几种方式。大家可以依据持有的香气香料状态，以及想要达到的风味、型态，自行变换或混用作法。</p>
<h3>1. 直接拌合法</h3>
<p>这是最简单的制法，大多用于质地与盐相近，粉末或碎粉状的干燥香草、香料，只要与盐搅拌混合后静置即可。随着时间，香气与盐会越来越融合。</p>
<p><strong>基本作法 示范：鼠尾草盐</strong><br />
将干燥的香草（香料）与盐一起混拌均匀即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13911537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911537-P.29-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911537-P.29-1-600x408.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911537" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13911538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911538" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911538-P.29-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911538-P.29-2-600x408.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911538" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>POINT<br />
?大片叶子如月桂叶，必须先用调理机尽量击碎，并过筛去除杂枝后，再与盐混拌。如此，香气才能够释放出来。<br />
?若加入完整颗粒的香料如八角，拌匀装罐后可先放置一星期，让盐充分吸附香气之后再使用。<br />
?新鲜紫苏须先以微波加热、脱水后，捏成粉碎状，再与盐混匀。</p>
<h3>2. 研磨拌合法</h3>
<p>多用于透过研磨香气更能释放，或是混合不同质地的香气物质时。与直接拌合法相较，将香草、香料与盐一起研磨后，盐会明显带有香草、香料的颜色，且香气会在研磨过程中大量释放出来，能立即感受到明显香气。</p>
<p><strong>基本作法 示范：罗勒盐</strong><br />
将干燥香草（香料）与盐放入研磨钵（或研磨机）中，一起混拌研磨均匀即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13911540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911540-P.30-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911540-P.30-1-600x456.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911540" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13911541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911541-P.30-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911541-P.30-2-600x454.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="454" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911541" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>3. 研磨拌合风干法</h3>
<p>若是使用新鲜的香草制作风味盐，由于香草叶子含有水分，在与盐拌合后，必须再经过风干的过程，风味盐才不易腐坏，更定味。</p>
<p><strong>基本作法 示范：迷迭香盐</strong><br />
1. 将新鲜香草洗净，用纸巾吸干水分后，取下叶子，用调理器打成碎状。<br />
2. 加入盐一起打碎打匀。<br />
3. 平铺在容器中，放置在通风处（或用除湿机或风扇）吹干至干燥即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13911542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911542" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911542-P.30-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911542-P.30-3-600x488.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="488" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911542" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13911543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911543" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911543-P.30-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911543-P.30-4-600x490.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="490" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911543" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>4. 渗入法</h3>
<p>此作法常见于制作柑橘风味的盐，例如柠檬、柳橙等。重点在于须用手细细搓揉柑橘皮，让内含的精油释放出来并与盐完全结合。</p>
<p><strong>基本作法 示范：柠檬盐</strong><br />
1. 将新鲜柑橘的外皮用刨刀挫下碎屑（留意不要刨下带苦的白色部分）。<br />
2. 容器内放入柑橘皮屑与盐，用手搓揉柑橘皮屑，使其释放精油至盐中即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13911544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911544" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911544-P.31-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911544-P.31-1-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911544" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13911545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911545-P.31-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911545-P.31-2-600x426.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="426" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911545" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>5. 炒焙法</h3>
<p>花椒这类的香料，香气经过炒焙会特别浓烈。因此若要拿来当作盐的风味来源，比起直接搅拌或研磨，更建议用小火加热炒过。</p>
<p><strong>基本作法 示范：花椒盐</strong><br />
将香料与盐放入干锅中，翻炒至香气出来、盐略变色后，倒出、静置放凉即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13911546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911546" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911546-P.31-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911546-P.31-3-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911546" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13911548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13911548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911548-P.31-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13911548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13911548-P.31-4-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13911548" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>自制风味盐的建议量&amp;保存方式</strong></h3>
<p>书中制作完成的风味盐大多是100g左右，让大家在家方便操作，方便保存。</p>
<p>尽量选择干爽干燥一点的盐，才能吸附更多的香气，粗盐细盐都可以，看制作料理品项选择盐的大小颗粒，例如：炖肉、熬汤选择粗粒盐，可以在锅中慢慢融化。抓腌调味使用细粒盐，较容易马上溶解。</p>
<p>原则上，干爽状态的风味盐保存放在通风无光照、温差不要过大的地方；有果汁、新鲜植物汁液制作的风味盐，需要冷藏保存。</p>
<p>盛装的容器以密封好的为佳，尤其需长时间保存时，首选是盖子上有硅胶条的玻璃罐。如果家中没有足够密封罐，我很常使用的还有塑胶盒，轻巧好堆叠，外出携带也没有负担感。⊙◇</p>
