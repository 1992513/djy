<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多新年艺术节LunarFest缤纷活动迎兔年</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: left;">【大纪元2023年01月21日讯】<span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">走过三年的疫情肆虐，面对重新开放的新局面，多伦多新年艺术节 </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><ahref="https://lunarfestgta.ca/"><span style="color: #1155cc;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;"><u>LunarFest</u></span></span></a>?今</span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">年恢复实体举办。别错过2023年<span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">1</span></span>月<span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">28-29</span></span>日（周六、周日）在?<span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">Varley?</span></span>美术馆的一系列缤纷活动。</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">2023年?<span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><ahref="https://lunarfestgta.ca/"><span style="color: #1155cc;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;"><u>LunarFest</u></span></span></a>?</span>活动以“</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">Year to Imagine</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">”为策展主题，在自然、传统与文化之间寻找人类社会的未来，透过引介多元且富饶趣味的艺术创作及工作坊，带领观众在一年之初重新想像这个世界与自己，迎接充满活力的兔年。</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">“以艺术和故事为媒介，将每个社区聚拢在一起相互交流，这样庆祝新年的方式将打造出加拿大式的年节传统。”主办团队发言人吴权益表示。</span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">主办方今年与台湾工艺研究发展中心合作，邀请知名染织艺术家陈景林等人共同策展台湾色 </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">(Colours of Formosa)</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">，于</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">1</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">28-29</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">日（周六、周日）在 </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">Varley </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">美术馆内展出一系列天然染布工艺。艺术家们从四季气候变化获得启发，自当地植物中萃取色彩，挥洒出一片人文风景。作品既展现大自然的季节色调，更凸显现代工艺师如何将日常染布转化为艺术品的无限想像。</span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">LunarFest 每年邀集加拿大原住民、各族裔及国际艺术家齐聚过新年，让农历新年成为跨族裔竞相参与的年度盛会，目前已是加拿大规模最大的年节活动。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">自</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">2021</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">年开始于 </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">Varley </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">美术馆前广场策画“原始之光</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">(Lunar Lanterns of Indigenous Lights)</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">”，创意且有意义的将东岸原住民艺术家与亚洲传统习俗连结在一起，呼应双边文化，共同庆祝农历新年。<br />
</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13912069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13912069" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/1/20/n13912059.md#1/8a13d34fba26192cb0cf73b9783bf0a1" rel="attachment wp-att-13912069"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13912069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13912069-8a13d34fba26192cb0cf73b9783bf0a1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13912069" class="wp-caption-text">2022 原始之光。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">今年</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">LunarFest </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">再次邀请各地原住民艺术家参与展览，以“燃起想像</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial, serif;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu, serif;">(Imagination Ignites)</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">”为主题，透过艺术家的视觉转译，向大自然与族裔传统学习，勇敢展开对未来的美好想像。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiu;">2023年原始之光于1月11日展出至二月底。</span></span></p>
