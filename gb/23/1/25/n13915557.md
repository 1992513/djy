<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中华公所恢复全侨春宴 睽违3年政要嘉宾云集</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915541-DSC03774-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>1月23日纽英崙中华公所兔年春宴，无视贺岁之后，嘉宾们拉响礼炮。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年01月26日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）自2020年1月以来，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8B%B1%E5%B4%99%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E5%85%AC%E6%89%80.md#1">纽英崙中华公所</a>首次恢复举办全侨<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AE%B4.md#1">春宴</a>。2023年1月23日，政要、嘉宾及各侨团成员总计500多人，按传统在大年初二晚齐聚<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>帝苑酒楼，热闹地迎接癸卯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%94%E5%B9%B4.md#1">兔年</a>。</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%94%E5%B9%B4.md#1">兔年</a>将是充满能量的、活泼的、幸福的一年，让我们高兴地庆祝兔年。恭喜大家兔年好运，‘兔’气扬眉，兔年大吉。”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E5%85%AC%E6%89%80.md#1">中华公所</a>主席雷国辉向全体来宾祝福说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13915542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915542" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915542-DSC03782.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915542-DSC03782-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915542" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8B%B1%E5%B4%99%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E5%85%AC%E6%89%80.md#1">纽英崙中华公所</a>癸卯兔年<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E5%AE%B4.md#1">春宴</a>，多位政要、嘉宾参与庆祝，与舞狮团队合影。后排左起：警察总监陈孔恩、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E5%85%AC%E6%89%80.md#1">中华公所</a>中文秘书翁宇才、财政余宝爱、安良工商会总理陈仕维、中华公所核数张青梅、台北经文处处长孙俭元、波士顿市议员Ruthzee Louijeune、Ed Flynn、警察局长Michael Cox、市长吴弭、麻州众议员Aaron Michlewitz、中华公所英文秘书阮鸿灿、主席雷国辉。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13915549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915549" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915549-DSC03870.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915549-DSC03870-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915549" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿警察局多位警员参加中华公所春宴，并与主席雷国辉合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚参加宴会的嘉宾包括波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）、市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）、市议员路易琼（Ruthzee Louijeune）和墨菲（Erin Murphy）、警察局长考克斯（Michael Cox）、消防局副局长黄瑞瑜、麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元，还有多位波士顿警员，多位麻州和波士顿政府的部门代表，以及塔夫茨医疗中心、华美银行等一些友好团体的代表。50多桌来宾，坐满了酒楼的整个宴会厅。</p>
<p>在餐宴开始前，全体来宾首先为加州华人枪击案的死者肃立默哀。“当我们能够享有农历新年庆祝的时候，我希望我们从思念他们开始。”吴弭说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13915543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915543" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915543-DSC03788.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915543-DSC03788-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915543" class="wp-caption-text">1月23日春宴开始前，全体来宾纪念加州华人枪击案受害者。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13915544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915544" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915544-DSC03794.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915544-DSC03794-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915544" class="wp-caption-text">1月23日春宴开始前，参与宴会的波士顿警察纪念加州华人枪击案受害者。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>疫情后</strong> <strong>全侨欢庆兔年</strong></h3>
<p>今年波士顿侨界的首场春宴，由中华公所宴请所有侨团，包括担任中华公所会员的35个宗亲协会，以及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>治安巡逻队、广教学校、中华象棋会等华埠的公益团体。晚宴一开始，主持人阮鸿灿和翁宇才还特别感谢了每一个赞助商机构。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13915545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915545-DSC03800.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915545-DSC03800-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915545" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所兔年春宴的主持人是英文秘书阮鸿灿和中文秘书翁宇才。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13915546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915546" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915546-DSC03847.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915546-DSC03847-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915546" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所兔年春宴有500多人参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天晚宴由胡清白鹤派的舞狮献瑞拉开序幕。5头彩狮在锣鼓声中摇头晃脑地进入宴会厅，为来宾们献上发财（白菜）和成功（橙子）的祝福。随后，前排嘉宾们拉响礼炮，让彩色礼花在热烈掌声中四处飘舞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13915540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915540-DSC03756.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915540-DSC03756-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915540" class="wp-caption-text">胡清白鹤派舞狮献瑞，祝福中华公所兔年春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13915555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915555-DSC03806.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915555-DSC03806-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915555" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭祝贺纽英崙中华公所兔年春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市长吴弭用中文说：“非常荣幸能够跟大家在一起庆祝新年，每一年都非常高兴看到我们中国城，和所有的朋友在一起。”</p>
<p>她感谢华人社区为波士顿所做的贡献，亦对大家在疫情期间的艰辛表达慰问。接下来，吴弭期待与大家一起度过精彩的兔年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13915551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915551" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915551-DSC03880.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915551-DSC03880-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915551" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾祝贺纽英崙中华公所兔年春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13915539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915539" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915539-DSC03751.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915539-DSC03751-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915539" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾祝贺纽英崙中华公所兔年春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参与宴会的3位市议员向中华公所赠送了市议会的贺状。贺状提到，上百年来，华裔曾遭受过歧视和伤害，而中华公所一直致力于支持和服务华裔民众，提供各种项目，增进华人的福利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13915553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915553-DSC039121.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915553-DSC039121-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915553" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华公所2023年春宴，全体在场董事合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在嘉宾致词之后，春宴经典菜式依次上桌，来宾们觥筹交错，同时亦欣赏几个舞蹈团带来的表演。中华公所职员雷国辉、余宝爱、张青梅、阮鸿灿、翁宇才上台敬酒，向满堂来宾送上新年祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13915548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915548-DSC03855.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915548-DSC03855-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915548" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所职员向春宴来宾敬酒，左起：余宝爱、雷国辉、翁宇才、张青梅、阮鸿灿。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阮鸿灿还一如往年那样扮演“财神”，在《财神到》等喜庆歌曲中，向每一桌来宾致送祝福和红包。</p>
<p>晚宴最后，嘉宾们携手登台，歌唱一首《朋友》，不同族裔的朋友们共同鼓掌和欢笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13915552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915552-DSC03882.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915552-DSC03882-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915552" class="wp-caption-text">“财神”贺岁，在中华公所春宴中发送红包。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13915550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13915550" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915550-DSC03877.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13915550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13915550-DSC03877-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13915550" class="wp-caption-text">中华公所成员及嘉宾们合唱《朋友》。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在春宴之后，中华公所还将于1月29日举办华埠舞狮迎新年庆典，并再度邀请璞石中华文化工作室在中华公所大厅举办文化村活动，届时有写春联，拓印等迎新年文化。此外，今年7月将迎来纽英崙中华公所创会100周年。董事们也在之前的月会中提议举办庆祝活动。◇</p>
