<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>初九天公生  张善政至芦竹山脚天公庙参香祈福</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13918328-597197.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长张善政前往芦竹山脚天公庙，出席“拜天公活动”参香祈福。（桃园市府新闻处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月30日讯】（大纪元记者陈建霖台湾桃园报导）桃园市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%96%84%E6%94%BF.md#1">张善政</a>前往芦竹山脚天公庙，出席“拜天公活动”参香<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%88%E7%A6%8F.md#1">祈福</a>，并发送福袋，向市民朋友拜年。张善政表示，看到许多乡亲前来参香、拿福袋，令人相当感动，正月初九<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%85%AC%E7%94%9F.md#1">天公生</a>，欢迎大家一起来沾喜气，他也祝福乡亲兔年行大运、身体健康、阖家平安。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13918329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13918329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13918329-597198-e1675055087720.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13918329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13918329-597198-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13918329" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市长<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%96%84%E6%94%BF.md#1">张善政</a>发送福袋。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张善政代表市府致赠香油礼金予庙方，并发送福袋给民众，现场排队领取福袋的民众非常踊跃，人潮络绎不绝，场面相当热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13918330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13918330" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13918330-597199-e1675055055746.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13918330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13918330-597199-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13918330" class="wp-caption-text">现场排队领取福袋的民众非常踊跃。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民政局表示，依照民间习俗，正月初九是“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%85%AC%E7%94%9F.md#1">天公生</a>”，也就是玉皇大帝寿诞，信众会有祭拜仪式，以感谢过去一年的保佑，同时祈求来年运势顺遂。芦竹山脚天公庙是社区居民的信仰及活动中心，与老人会馆、图书馆毗邻而建，也是芦竹区唯一主祀玉皇大帝的庙宇。◇</p>
