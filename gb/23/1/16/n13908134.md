<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽约法拉盛大学点大道查获非法大麻店</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908136-163404-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>警方收缴的毒品。（109分局推特）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年01月16日讯】（大纪元记者林丹纽约报导）纽约市警局<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/109%E5%88%86%E5%B1%80.md#1">109分局</a>在1月11日在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a>大学点大道（College Point Blvd.）18-35号查获一个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B3%95.md#1">非法</a>销售<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%BA%BB.md#1">大麻</a>和毒品的窝点。收缴大麻、迷幻药“迷幻蘑菇”（Psychedelic Mushrooms）和500元现金。两人被捕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13908135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13908135" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908135-163403-e1673848893229.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13908135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13908135-163403-450x600.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13908135" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a>大学点大道的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B3%95.md#1">非法</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%BA%BB.md#1">大麻</a>店被警方查获。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/109%E5%88%86%E5%B1%80.md#1">109分局</a>推特）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市议员帕拉迪诺（Vickie Paladino）发推文说，她在去年10月份曾亲自警告过这个非法的窝点，指出不会容忍非法活动。◇</p>
