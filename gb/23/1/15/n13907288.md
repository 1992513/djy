<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园角板山的梅花与蝴蝶成为石门水库新焦点</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907289-595845-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园角板山的梅花与蝴蝶万千形态惹人心醉。（读者冯颕霞  提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年01月15日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市石门水库及角板山行馆附近的梅花陆续绽放，雪白梅花芳香扑鼻，目前园区梅树的白色小花朵，已挂满枝头相继怒放，花况已开8成，不只蝴蝶闻香而来，也吸引大批游客上山一睹梅花风采，北水局欢迎大家到园区溪洲公园、中线及高线附近的步道观赏盛开的梅花，漫步在梅花朵朵开的步道之间，清新的空气蕴含着清雅的梅花香，有如人间仙境，美不胜收。</p>
<p>本报读者，医院管理专家刘兴宽与夫人冯颕霞到石管局角板山，忍不住说，梅花的树形很漂亮，不但是花开得漂亮，它的树形状也很漂亮！而蝴蝶更美，蝴蝶形态各异，五颜六色，非常漂亮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13907290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13907290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907290-595846.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13907290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907290-595846-600x503.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="503" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13907290" class="wp-caption-text">四溢清香引来了可爱的小蜜蜂，小蜜蜂围在兰花旁边“嗡嗡”的唱着歌，好像在说：哇，花粉的味道真香甜。（读者冯颕霞 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13907292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13907292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907292-595848.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13907292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907292-595848-600x864.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="864" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13907292" class="wp-caption-text">五颜六色的小蝴蝶在美丽的花头上站着，好象在赞美花朵的美丽。（读者冯颕霞 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13907296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13907296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907296-595851.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13907296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907296-595851-600x763.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="763" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13907296" class="wp-caption-text">蝴蝶是美丽的精灵，是翩翩的天使、飘飘的舞者，真美！（读者冯颕霞 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13907291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13907291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907291-595847.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13907291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907291-595847-600x504.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="504" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13907291" class="wp-caption-text">五颜六色的小蝴蝶在美丽的花头上站着，好象在赞美花朵的美丽。（读者冯颕霞 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>角板山蝴蝶不但美丽，彷如花枝招展，各自缤纷，它们飘然，轻盈，从容，跟少女一样，惹人爱恋，使人羡慕，蝴蝶御风而动，凌空畅游，翩翩起舞，上下翻飞，时而停顿，时而折返，让人忍不住想多看一眼。</p>
<p>寒冬腊月每年1月初，百花凋谢，石门水库种植的300棵低海拔梅树，愈冷愈开花。此刻还有秋叶枫红的景致，交错着宛如白雪的梅花，别有一番诗情画意的景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13907293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13907293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907293-595849.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13907293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907293-595849-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13907293" class="wp-caption-text">小蝴蝶很可爱，有飘逸的翅膀。（读者冯颕霞 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13907294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13907294" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907294-595850.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13907294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13907294-595850-600x899.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="899" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13907294" class="wp-caption-text">小蝴蝶很可爱，有飘逸的翅膀。（读者冯颕霞 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>梅花盛开，或独傲枝头，或簇簇相拥，万千形态惹人心醉，四处洋溢的梅香沁人心脾，由于山上有露出冬阳，天气比较温暖，蝴蝶、蜜蜂也忙碌了起来，只是晚上寒流来临，花间的蝴蝶将等到气温上升才会再出现！</p>
<p>冯颕霞说，角板山蝴蝶真美，北水局虽然梅花尚未完全绽放，但是花量和花色还是很漂亮的，值得大家一游！◇</p>
