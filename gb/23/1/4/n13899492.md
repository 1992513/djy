<h2>穗花棋盘脚</h2>
<p>
Barringtonia racemosa （L.） Blume ex DC. 玉蕊科</p>
<p>又称水茄苳、水贡仔、细叶棋盘脚树，玉蕊。常绿乔木，枝下垂。叶长椭圆状倒卵形，基部尖至圆，先端短渐尖，长可达35 公分，宽可达14公分。穗状花序顶生或腋生，下垂长达20~80公分。花瓣长椭圆形，淡绿或淡玫瑰色。果卵形长椭圆状，长可达7公分，具不明显四棱。木材质软而轻，易腐朽。常绿，枝叶密，耐盐分，常栽植于海滨地区，作为防风林用。近来常被栽种在庭园，作为绿化植栽观赏用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13899497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13899497" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899497-451644_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13899497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899497-451644_medium-600x794.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="794"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13899497" class="wp-caption-text">入夜传来暗清香，满树烟花齐怒放。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13899503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13899503" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899503-451641_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13899503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899503-451641_medium-600x799.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="799"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13899503" class="wp-caption-text">近来常被栽种在庭园，作为绿化植栽观赏用。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>穗花棋盘脚的树皮含丰富单宁，尚未开发供经济用。根清热、治热病；叶可治皮肤搔痒、水痘；果实可治咳嗽，哮喘，腹泻；种子清热利湿可治目赤肿痛，黄胆，腹痛，疝气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13899502" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13899502" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899502-451640_medium.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13899502" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/01/id13899502-451640_medium-600x445.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="445"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13899502" class="wp-caption-text">树皮含丰富单宁，尚未开发供经济用。根清热、治热病。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
