<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>限定美味！在家自制煸虾油、炼虾汤 作法一次看</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id13985891-20230428_shrimp_oil_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在家自制煸虾油的作法。（台湾广厦提供／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>常有朋友跟我哀嚎，买了虾子取出虾仁后剩下的虾壳可以怎么运用？其实我觉得虾壳才是珍宝呢，不仅可以炼虾油提升风味，还能熬虾汤让菜色更鲜美，以下是我的独门作法，大家可以参考喔！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;">秘制虾油做法及保存方法</span></h2>
<p>虾油可以提升料理风味，拌面炒饭或搭配任何海鲜料理都超搭！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;">虾油材料</span></h3>
<p>?虾壳、虾头15～20只<br />
?食用油，油量要盖过虾子的1/3或1/2</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;">料理步骤</span></h3>
<p>?先将虾壳、虾头剥好备用。<br />
?准备一油锅放入足够的油量，先加热至160度。<br />
?接着，放入虾壳和虾头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983583" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983583-P.36-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983583-P.36-1-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983583" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?开大火不停的拌炒至虾头和虾壳都转红，有炸脆的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983584" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983584-P.36-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983584-P.36-2-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983584" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?将虾壳沥出，再用锅铲压虾壳一边沥油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983585" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983585-P.37-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983585-P.37-1-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983585" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?锅筛虾油倒入消毒过的玻璃瓶，放凉后放进冰箱保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983586" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983586-P.37-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983586-P.37-2-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983586" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>★自制虾油要尽快使用，冷藏一般可以保存两周左右。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;">独门自制虾高汤及保存方法</span></h2>
<p>剥下来的虾壳及虾头别急着丢掉，熬成虾汤，当成高汤让菜色增添鲜味更美味喔！</p>
<h3>虾汤材料</h3>
<p>洋葱丁适量<br />
蒜末适量<br />
虾壳、虾头4～6只<br />
开水500ml</p>
<h3>料理步骤</h3>
<p>先将虾壳、虾头剥好备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983587" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983587-P.37-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983587-P.37-3-450x441.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="441" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983587" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在锅中倒入1～2汤匙油。<br />
将洋葱丁及蒜末爆香。<br />
放入虾壳炒至虾壳转成红色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983588" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983588-P.37-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983588-P.37-4-450x440.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="440" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983588" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>倒入开水煮沸3～5分即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983589-P.37-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983589-P.37-5-450x442.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983589" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>★做好的虾高汤可与所需之菜色一同拌炒，加入海鲜炖饭或是意大利面亦可增添风味。放凉后也可倒入制冰盒放冷冻库，需要时再取出加热即可。</p>
