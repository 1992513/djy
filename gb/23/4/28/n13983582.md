<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>孩子的开胃餐：凤梨虾球 在家做并不难</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983598-P.133-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>甜蜜蜜凤梨虾球香甜滋味，让孩子多吃好几碗饭。</strong></p>
<p>我很少在外面吃凤梨虾球，原因是常常会吃到面粉壳比虾仁还要大的虾球，自己做的虾球除了可以控制粉量，也可以控制美乃滋的多寡，而且做法比想像中的简单！学会做虾球就可以搭配许多不同的酱汁做呈现！</p>
<h3>适用虾种</h3>
<p>?泰国虾仁、草虾仁、白虾仁</p>
<h3>材料：（2人份）</h3>
<p>?泰国虾仁（约20～30只）<br />
?盐1/4匙<br />
?鸡蛋2颗<br />
?太白粉50克<br />
?地瓜粉200克<br />
?美乃滋30克<br />
?凤梨罐头300克</p>
<h3>料理步骤：</h3>
<p><strong>1. 准备材料</strong><br />
?冷冻泰国虾仁先用流动水冲洗至每只虾仁分开，沥干水分。<br />
?罐头凤梨片切四段。准备好太白粉、地瓜粉及鸡蛋等配料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983599" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983599-P.134-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983599-P.134-1-450x323.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983599" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 烹调料理</strong><br />
?准备炸虾球：先将太白粉、蛋液、地瓜粉分开放好。<br />
?将虾仁开背取肠泥洗净、擦干后，依序沾裹太白粉→蛋液→地瓜粉，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983600-P.134-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983600-P.134-2-450x324.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983600" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>

<p>?起一油锅先以120～150度的中油温，放入虾仁炸熟、成型，约3～4分钟，虾仁转变金黄色起锅。接着，再转大火二度回炸，将虾仁炸熟捞起，将内部多余的油逼出，起锅备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983604" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983604-P.135-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983604-P.135-4-450x323.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983604" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>

<p>?拌炒凤梨虾球：锅中放少许的油，倒入凤梨片及虾球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983606" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983606-P.135-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983606-P.135-6-450x323.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983606" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
