<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黄金虾油炒饭 简单好做 浓郁鲜美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983590-P.105-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黄金虾油炒饭。（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>虾油提点让炒饭浓郁鲜美</strong></p>
<p>炒饭的关键在于粒粒分明的饭与锅气，这道菜步骤2和3就是虾油炒饭的灵魂，也是炒饭干爽不粘腻的精髓。有人说：“用一般的油炒饭可以吗？”当然也是没问题的，但就像前一篇所说，改用虾油会让料理的风味更增添丰富度与厚度。</p>
<h3>适用虾种</h3>
<p>?泰国虾仁、草虾仁、白虾仁</p>
<h3>材料：（2人份）</h3>
<p>?泰国虾仁（约20～30只）<br />
?虾油2大匙<br />
?白饭2碗<br />
?鸡蛋2颗<br />
?油葱酥1大匙<br />
?蒜头2瓣<br />
?盐1匙</p>
<h3>料理步骤：</h3>
<p><strong>1. 准备材料</strong><br />
?冷冻泰国虾仁先用流动水冲洗至每只虾仁分开，沥干水分。<br />
?若有多余的虾壳可以炸出虾油，过滤备用。<br />
?准备隔夜白饭。蒜头洗净去皮切末，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983591" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983591-P.106-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983591-P.106-1-450x322.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983591" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 烹调料理</strong><br />
?隔夜饭打蛋液下去和饭抓匀，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983592" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983592-P.106-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983592-P.106-2-450x324.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983592" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?准备一只不銹钢炒锅，倒入虾油，放入蒜末爆香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983593" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983593-P.106-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983593-P.106-3-450x323.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983593" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13983594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983594" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983594-P.107-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983594-P.107-1-450x324.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983594" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?放入虾仁拌炒，放入蛋液饭快速炒散，使饭粒分明。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983595" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983595-P.107-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983595-P.107-2-450x322.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983595" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13983596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983596" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983596-P.107-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983596-P.107-3-450x322.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983596" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?最后放入油葱酥和盐调味，拌匀即可起锅享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13983597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13983597" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983597-P.107-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-13983597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13983597-P.107-4-450x324.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13983597" class="wp-caption-text">（图片来源：台湾广厦有声图书有限公司）</figcaption></figure>
