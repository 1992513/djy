<h2>牧场（彩墨）</h2>
<p>杜甫诗：“星垂平野阔，月涌大江流。”他所吟咏的大地是何等的恢宏壮阔啊。李白也写过这样的诗句：“山随平野尽，江入大荒流。”同样也是气象万千，千古传诵不绝的名句——雄阔荒古又原始大块的壮美。</p>
<p>本图依李、杜两人的诗意来构图——春夏之际，大草原上芳草无垠，草木华滋，无边无际，牛群在草原上悠闲地吃草，一些白鹭也在牛群之外翱翔，无忧无虑，风月无边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13974348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13974348" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13974348-62f0c48df503178cb1eea011d96ae7cb.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13974348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id13974348-62f0c48df503178cb1eea011d96ae7cb.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13974348" class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—徐明义画集8—牧场（彩墨）。70×70cm。（图片来源：徐明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Prairie/ink and color painting</h2>
<p>Poem of Du Fu: &#8220;The bright stars hanging low in the sky, the ground is so flat and wide, the big wave river is flowing to East under the moon.&#8221; What magnificent the ground is he chants! Li Bai also wrote similar poem: &#8220;The mountains end their way at the wide fields, the momentum of river flowing seemed the it also flows into the desert of faraway champaign. It is also a meteoric, legendary word throughout the ages – what a magnificent beauty of grand and primitive.</p>
<p>This painting’s composition made is according to Lee’s and Du’s poetic – In Spring and Summer season, the prairie is filled with vast and endless grass, grass and trees breeds boundless, A herd of cattle leisurely graze on the prairie, some egrets are also soaring outside the herd of cattle, carefree and the boundless spirit.@</p>
