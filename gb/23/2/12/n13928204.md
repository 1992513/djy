<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NAB预测2023年西澳房价暴跌13.1%</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月11日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者</span><span lang="zh-TW">林达</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">日前澳洲国民银行（</span></span>National Australia Bank<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，简称</span></span>NAB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）发出警告，</span></span>2023<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年珀斯房价将暴跌。</span></span></p>
<p>NAB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">最新的住宅物业调查预测，珀斯住宅价格将下降</span></span>13.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，然后在</span></span>2024<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年上涨</span></span>1.2%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">这与当地人与西澳房地产专业人士的预期相反，后者对西澳住房信心最高，预期今年房价将上涨</span></span>1.2%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，未来两年将上涨</span></span>2%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p>NAB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">集团首席经济学家奥斯特（</span></span>Alan Oster<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，西澳人“过于乐观了些”。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">他说，利率上升将对房价产生重大影响。从</span></span>3<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月开始，加息的影响将越来越明显，并在</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月达到顶峰，届时大多数固定贷款都将到期。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">周二，储备银行（</span></span>RBA<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）在今年的第一次董事会会议上宣布</span><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%81%AF.md#1"><span lang="zh-TW">加息</span></a></span>25<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个基点，使官方现金</span><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%A9%E7%8E%87.md#1"><span lang="zh-TW">利率</span></a><span lang="zh-TW">上升到</span></span>3.35%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span></span>10<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年来的最高点。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">奥斯特说：“我们认为重力最终会在市场发挥作用，珀斯房价将下降。珀斯房价去年上涨了</span></span>3.6%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，而全国其它地区则下跌了</span></span>7%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。即便如此，情况并不妙。如果我以年回报率计算过去六个月，珀斯持平。在过去的</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">到</span></span>12<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个月里，却已下降了很多，当时珀斯房价增长率接近</span></span>30%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。”</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">报告称，高昂的建筑成本也是新开发项目的制约因素。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">然而，西澳房地产协会（</span></span>Real Estate Institute of WA<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）预测珀斯的房地产价格将在</span></span>2023<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年温和增长。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">尽管如此，</span></span>NAB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">此前的房地产预测已被证明与事实不符。它说</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年珀斯房价将下跌</span></span>7.5%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span>2021<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年下跌</span></span>4.6%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。但根据</span></span>RP Data<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的数据，</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年房价上涨了</span></span>7.3%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span>2021<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年上涨了</span></span>13.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。当时</span></span>NAB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">对珀斯房价的预测反而更接近东海岸城市的房价水平。</span></span></p>
<p>NAB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">最近的调查预测，全国首府城市的平均房价今年将下降</span></span>11%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span>2024<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年将增长</span></span>2.6%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">报告还称，租赁市场看不到缓解迹象。接受调查的房地产专业人士预计，西澳的租金增幅将为全澳最高，增幅</span></span>4.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，两年内增幅为</span></span>4.3%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。对全国租金平均水平的预期是一年内增长</span></span>3.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，两年内增长</span></span>3.3%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">责任编辑：杨新云</span></p>
