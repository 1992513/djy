<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>卡尔加里船舶与户外活动展将于2月10~12日登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921968-20230123-Calgary-Boat-and-Outdoors-Show-boating-03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>水上滑板/冲浪。（卡尔加里船舶与户外活动展提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月04日讯】远足、登山、露营、钓鱼、狩猎、划船……探索自然之美，享受户外乐趣。卡尔加里船舶与户外活动展（Calgary Boat &amp; Outdoors Show）将于2月10~12日在牛仔公园BMO中心举行。</p>
<p>该展会以船只、船舶配件、户外相关产品和服务为特色。超过150家参展商齐聚一堂，与会者可借此机会购物、学习和娱乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13921974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921974-20230123-Calgary-Boat-and-Outdoors-Show-01.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921974-20230123-Calgary-Boat-and-Outdoors-Show-01-600x400.jpg" alt="越野车" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921974" class="wp-caption-text">越野车。（卡尔加里船舶与户外活动展提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>钓鱼：展会的最经典部分。休闲垂钓者和垂钓高手者将参加展会，与他们对话，为捕鱼季节做好所需的装备。</p>
<p>狩猎：装备、枪支、射箭设备、动物标本剥制术、向导和装备商，加上所有配件，让你的狩猎季节顺利进行。</p>
<p>划船：船舶与户外展是亚省休闲游艇的主要销售场所。来自亚省各地的品牌汇集在一个屋檐下，为渴望水上活动的消费者提供服务。</p>
<p>露营：无论你是初次露营者还是经验丰富的露营爱好者，都可以亲身体验各种品牌的最新装备和装置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13921972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921972-20230123-Calgary-Boat-and-Outdoors-Show-camping-02.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921972-20230123-Calgary-Boat-and-Outdoors-Show-camping-02-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921972" class="wp-caption-text">露营。（卡尔加里船舶与户外活动展提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>飞蝇钓：令人惊叹的亚省户外体验──站在河流当中，甩出鱼线，利用轻盈的拟饵吸引游鱼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13921970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921970" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921970-20230123-Calgary-Boat-and-Outdoors-Show-fishing-04.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921970-20230123-Calgary-Boat-and-Outdoors-Show-fishing-04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921970" class="wp-caption-text">飞蝇钓。（卡尔加里船舶与户外活动展提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>木屋度假：开启休闲的生活方式，打造家外之家──湖上小屋所需的一切产品尽皆在此。</p>
<p><strong>时间</strong><br />
2月10日（周五）上午10点~晚9点<br />
2月11日（周六）上午10点~晚7点<br />
2月12日（周日）上午10点~晚5点</p>
<p><strong>地点：</strong>BMO Stampede Park，20 Roundup Way SE</p>
