<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>家庭日长周末多伦多好去处 加拿大最大钓鱼展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13925635-324334797_495370769174176_4969320687104729701_n-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月09日讯】在即将到来的2月17日至19日加拿大家庭日长周末，多伦多国际中心将举办为期三天的2023春季钓鱼和船艇展（Spring Fishing &amp; Boat Show）：加拿大最大的钓鱼展。</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-206869" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/316548512_531753558967463_8122694343770236912_n.jpg" alt="" width="1920" b="1080" /></p>
<p>喜欢钓鱼的朋友，别错过这个家庭日长周末好去处！</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-206855" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/屏幕截图-2023-02-05-100148.png" alt="" width="1269" b="624" /></p>
<p>钓鱼爱好者的天堂！</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-206858" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/329135600_717218913475009_7172776158290927707_n.jpg" alt="" width="843" b="843" /></p>
<p>在2023年春季钓鱼和船艇展上，加拿大钓鱼界名人Bob Izumi等众多钓鱼好手，将举办研讨会，上演钓鱼真人秀。</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-206860" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/328992055_958518835024900_4415058328495558354_n.jpg" alt="" width="843" b="843" /></p>
<p>想换一艘新船？在春季钓鱼和船艇展上，有机会比较各种型号的船艇，寻找一款最符合您心意的！</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-206861" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/328644653_860860218306453_5525881377993983881_n.jpg" alt="" width="843" b="843" /></p>
<p>主办方与赞助商合作，每天将向前75位客人送出一包赠品！</p>
