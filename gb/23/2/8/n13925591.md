<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>家庭日长周末多伦多好去处 加拿大最大钓鱼展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13925635-324334797_495370769174176_4969320687104729701_n-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年02月09日讯】在即将到来的2月17日至19日加拿大家庭日长周末，多伦多国际中心将举办为期三天的2023春季钓鱼和船艇展（Spring Fishing &amp; Boat Show）：加拿大最大的钓鱼展。</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-206869" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/316548512_531753558967463_8122694343770236912_n.jpg" alt="" width="1920" b="1080" /></p>

<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-206855" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/屏幕截图-2023-02-05-100148.png" alt="" width="1269" b="624" /></p>
<p>钓鱼爱好者的天堂！</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-206858" src="https://ntdca.com/cn/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/329135600_717218913475009_7172776158290927707_n.jpg" alt="" width="843" b="843" /></p>
