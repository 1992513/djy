<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>客家同乡会新年餐会  乡亲欢聚佳肴满桌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923460-IMG_1307-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月02日讯】亚特兰大客家同乡会于1月28日(周六)傍晚在桃乐维尔(Doraville)的协胜公会会馆举行了今年的新年餐会，搭约有80人共襄盛举。驻亚特兰大台北经济文化办事处的王翼龙处长以及华侨文教中心的欧宏伟主任也盛情捧场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923466" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923466-IMG_1309-e1675643372294.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13923466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923466-IMG_1309-600x832.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="832" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923466" class="wp-caption-text">王翼龙处长讲话(客家同乡会提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13923471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923471" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923471-IMG_1308-e1675643967527.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13923471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923471-IMG_1308-600x950.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="950" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923471" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员会感谢状。(客家同乡会提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13923465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923465" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923465-IMG_1310-e1675643249105.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13923465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923465-IMG_1310-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923465" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心欧主任讲话(客家同乡会提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>王处长与欧主任还带来侨委会童振源委员长颁发的、给客家同乡会的感谢状，铭谢同乡会积极策划办理侨务委员会成立90周年的庆祝活动。</p>
<p>宴会佳肴满桌，包括客家粽子、炒米粉、客家小炒、客家咸汤圆等令人垂涎欲滴的传统客家美食。在众人大快朵颐后，还有抽奖及卡拉OK歌唱节目更将气氛推至高潮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13923533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13923533" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923533-17562092927328-1-e1675650555626.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13923533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13923533-17562092927328-1-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13923533" class="wp-caption-text">客家美食佳肴满桌。(客家同乡会提供)</figcaption></figure>

<p>这次宴会圆满结束，同乡会很感谢侨务委员范委员、陆委会、及张委员的慷凯支持及赞助。今年抽奖奖品满满，几乎人人有奖。主办者也很感谢客家同乡会理事们及朋友们的赞助及出力，这次活动才有办法不收餐费，让大家尽兴的有的吃又有的带，快乐过新年。</p>
