<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿市府热闹迎兔年 彰显亚裔文化</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921180-DSC04169-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>璞石中华文化工作室应邀参加波士顿市政府2月1日的迎兔年庆典，捏面老师许淑芬（右一）教孩子们做兔子。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月03日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）2月1日下午，波士顿市政府大楼内回响着锣鼓和掌声。<wbr />这是市政府一年一度的黄历新年庆祝活动。</p>
<p>这个庆典从2016年，吴弭（Michelle Wu）担任市议会议长的时候开始举办。经过2021、<wbr />2022这两年的暂停，<wbr />今年市民们终于可以回到这个宽敞的大厅迎接兔年了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13921184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921184-DSC04221.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921184-DSC04221-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921184" class="wp-caption-text">洪青体育会舞狮贺岁，在波士顿市政府庆祝兔年。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下午1:30过后，<wbr />洪青体育会的两头彩狮首先在锣鼓声中跳上阶梯，翻腾起舞。<wbr />随着礼炮声响，礼花飘散，狮子们也向满堂观众丢出橘子和生菜，<wbr />献上新年祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13921186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921186-DSC04230.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921186-DSC04230-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921186" class="wp-caption-text">“Boston Music Project”的青少年乐手们2月1日在波士顿市政府带来交响乐演出。（刘景烨/大纪元</figcaption></figure>
<p>在洪青舞狮之后，“波士顿音乐项目”（Boston Music Project）的青少年们带来了交响乐演奏。<wbr />波士顿华埠昆士小学（Quincy School）的孩子们献上了扇子舞表演。<wbr />广教中文学校的学生们也带来了另一场舞狮贺岁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13921187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921187-DSC04240.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921187-DSC04240-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921187" class="wp-caption-text">2月1日，波士顿市政府庆祝兔年，广教学校的学生们舞狮贺岁。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13921188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921188-DSC04251.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921188-DSC04251-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921188" class="wp-caption-text">2月1日，波士顿市政府庆祝兔年，昆士小学的孩子们表演扇子舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于市长吴弭外出处理紧急事务，市政府联邦关系事务主任Sam Hyun代表她向来宾们致以欢迎。</p>
<p>“今天我们与朋友们一起，在这里庆祝这个大日子。<wbr />我们也将创造未来，打破‘竹子天花板’，<wbr />拆除阻隔了许多社群的障碍，<wbr />确保每个人都能享有黄历新年所代表的快乐和富足。”Sam Hyun说。</p>
<p>他谈到，虽然亚裔是波士顿地区人数增长最快的族裔，<wbr />但他们在很长时间内都没有得到公众和媒体应有的关注。现在，<wbr />市政府有这么多亚裔官员，<wbr />这个庆祝新年的活动也有这么多人来参与，<wbr />证明了亚裔正是这个城市不可缺少的、有机的一部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13921185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921185-DSC04226.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921185-DSC04226-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921185" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市政府联邦关系事务主任Sam Hyun代表市长欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13921189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921189-DSC04252.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921189-DSC04252-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921189" class="wp-caption-text">2月1日，波士顿市政府庆祝兔年，许多观众坐在阶梯上看表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天的庆典有市政府提供的美食摊位，<wbr />还有璞石中华文化工作室和波士顿中华书法会带来的文化摊位。</p>
<p>璞石的义工们带来了“捏面人”手工艺教学，吸引了很多孩子聚集，<wbr />高高兴兴地制作小兔子。孩子们人数实在太多了，<wbr />以至于要分成好几批来参与。捏面老师许淑芬今年特地绞尽脑汁，设计许多不同造型的兔子，深获大人小孩的喜爱。</p>
<p>中华书法会的两位书法家黄周丽桃和伍振中执笔挥毫，<wbr />书写挥春送给大家，也吸引了不少人聚集和学写。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13921181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921181-DSC04206.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921181-DSC04206-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921181" class="wp-caption-text">璞石中华文化工作室的手工艺摊位吸引孩子们参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13921182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921182-DSC04212.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921182-DSC04212-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921182" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿中华书法会的黄周丽桃和伍振中为人们书写挥春。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13921190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13921190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921190-DSC04260.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13921190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13921190-DSC04260-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13921190" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议会议长Ed Flynn带领议员们参加2月1日的黄历新年庆祝活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在庆典期间，市政府大厅的阶梯上有上百人席地而坐，欢声笑语。<wbr />市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）带领大多数议员前来参与庆祝，<wbr />并向市民们尤其是亚裔居民致意。中华公所主席雷国辉、<wbr />洪门总理余丽媖等几位侨领也都到场欢庆兔年。◇</p>
