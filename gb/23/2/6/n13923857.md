<h6>台湾阿里山公路沿途樱花绽放。（苏家弘提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年02月06日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾采访报导）台湾阿里山是国际级的旅游胜地，自然生态摄影师苏家弘分享现在阿里山公路樱花绽放，还能看到很多鸟类，春天的景色宜人，让他感受到鸟语花香。</p>
<p>台湾阿里山国家风景区管理处表示，初春，是阿里山最灿烂的时节，樱花的粉嫩替春季揭开序幕，浪漫的紫藤紧接着报到。从12月至3月，阿里山国家风景区的樱花陆续绽放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13924049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13924049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924049-2302060839342378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13924049" title="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924049-2302060839342378-600x337.jpg" alt="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13924049" class="wp-caption-text">美丽的黄山雀。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13924051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13924051" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924051-2302060931362378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13924051" title="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924051-2302060931362378-600x450.jpg" alt="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13924051" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山迷糊步道福尔摩沙白色樱花绽放（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><center><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d2bd0i85jg8bcc.cloudfront.net/I8d&#29983/embed/Ps09OCDMSss" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center>台湾阿里山国家风景区管理处说，阿里山国家森林游乐区内的樱花树集中在3月中至4月中开花，而3月也是欣赏紫藤最适宜的时间。在春天来访阿里山，让花季缤纷的色彩渲染出游回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13924052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13924052" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924052-2302060931302378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13924052" title="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924052-2302060931302378-600x450.jpg" alt="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13924052" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山迷糊步道福尔摩沙白色樱花绽放。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13924050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13924050" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924050-2302060853342378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13924050" title="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924050-2302060853342378-600x450.jpg" alt="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13924050" class="wp-caption-text">茶腹?觅食虫虫。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><center><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d2bd0i85jg8bcc.cloudfront.net/I8d&#29983/embed/bUWN5szaGsc" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center>阿里山樱花季3月将登场。苏家弘表示，现在阿里山地区樱花已经陆续绽放，阿里山公路上沿路从52K至86K，都可以看到盛开的红色绯寒樱（山樱花），还有河津樱和一些红梅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13924055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13924055" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924055-2302060850462378.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13924055" title="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/02/id13924055-2302060850462378-600x450.jpg" alt="组图：台湾阿里山公路樱花绽放 鸟语花香" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13924055" class="wp-caption-text">白耳画眉与福尔摩沙樱。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p><center><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d2bd0i85jg8bcc.cloudfront.net/I8d&#29983/embed/r06GpkqGxFI" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center>苏家弘说，阿里山公路上65.4K及65.8K的迷糊步道入口处，可以欣赏山樱花和白色福尔摩沙樱，现在有一些山鸟都在觅食，所以游客也可以到那边去拍摄及观察，但提醒大家不要惊扰鸟类。</p>

<p><center><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d2bd0i85jg8bcc.cloudfront.net/I8d&#29983/embed/G4qPtLBGut8" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center>他表示，阿里山公路上81.9K附近福尔摩沙白樱花在盛开。福尔摩沙白樱花是美丽宝岛的原生种，是代表台湾的樱花。白色的花瓣衬以黄色的花萼，显得非常清新淡雅。</p>
