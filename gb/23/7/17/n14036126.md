<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>SF9五成员于台北开唱 粉丝提前为达渊庆生</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036146-230717080546100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国男团SF9访台举办“2023 SF9 FAN-CON：ONE DAY PROJECT OF9-Target NO.886 in TAIPEI”演唱会。（华艺娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年07月17日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）韩国男团SF9成员达渊、朱豪、柳太阳、辉映与澯熙，年初造访台湾举办专辑签售会后便与Fantasy（官方粉丝名）相约再见面，而他们16日于台湾TICC台北国际会议中心举办了“2023 SF9 FAN-CON：ONE DAY PROJECT OF9-Target NO.886 in TAIPEI”演唱会。</p>
<p>SF9成员们于演出开场接连带来了《Puzzle》、《Good Guy》、《Dreams》三首歌曲。达渊表示：“今天又能在这里演出，我非常开心！我真的非常想你们！”澯熙说：“好久没有来这里了，真的好紧张但也很开心，我们今天好好地玩哦！”辉映则表明：“我们1月在这里进行签售会，今天能在舞台上跟大家见面，心情激动又兴奋！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14036150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14036150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036150-230717080538100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14036150" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036150-230717080538100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14036150" class="wp-caption-text">韩国男团SF9为“2023 SF9 FAN-CON：ONE DAY PROJECT OF9-Target NO.886 in TAIPEI”访台，图为澯熙。（华艺娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14036152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14036152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036152-230717080534100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14036152" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036152-230717080534100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14036152" class="wp-caption-text">韩国男团SF9为“2023 SF9 FAN-CON：ONE DAY PROJECT OF9-Target NO.886 in TAIPEI”访台，图为辉映。（华艺娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>柳太阳说道：“大家过得好吗？今天准备好了一起玩了吗？”而朱豪则先请求粉丝给予尖叫声，接着说：“今天我们的状态非常好，你们准备好要好好玩了吗？”本次的演唱会还设计了游戏桥段，让大家看到五位成员可爱的一面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14036147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14036147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036147-230717080550100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14036147" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036147-230717080550100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14036147" class="wp-caption-text">韩国男团SF9为“2023 SF9 FAN-CON：ONE DAY PROJECT OF9-Target NO.886 in TAIPEI”访台，图为柳太阳。（华艺娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14036154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14036154" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036154-230717080536100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14036154" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036154-230717080536100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14036154" class="wp-caption-text">韩国男团SF9为“2023 SF9 FAN-CON：ONE DAY PROJECT OF9-Target NO.886 in TAIPEI”访台，图为朱豪。（华艺娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动中，柳太阳、辉映、澯熙演出了《Valhalla》；达渊带来《Stay》、朱豪准备了《The Last Festival》，他们还演唱了《Love Again》、《Driver》、《Scream》、《Mamma Mia》、《Play Hard》，以及两首安可《Beautiful Light》与《Go High》，共13首歌曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14036155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14036155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036155-230717080543100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14036155" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036155-230717080543100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14036155" class="wp-caption-text">韩国男团SF9访台举办“2023 SF9 FAN-CON：ONE DAY PROJECT OF9-Target NO.886 in TAIPEI”演唱会。（华艺娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，由于达渊即将于7月24日迎来生日，粉丝们特别在活动中惊喜为他庆生，还为了本次SF9访台准备了电视墙播出、应援车等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14036149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14036149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036149-230717080541100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14036149" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14036149-230717080541100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14036149" class="wp-caption-text">韩国男团SF9为“2023 SF9 FAN-CON：ONE DAY PROJECT OF9-Target NO.886 in TAIPEI”访台，图为达渊。（华艺娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>达渊还将在生日前的7月22日，于首尔举办两场粉丝见面会“ALL DA WANT FOR BIRTHDAY”。</p>
