<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>疫情后首次 客家会户外烤肉百人联欢</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027686-DSC08238-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英伦客家乡亲会7月1日举办烧烤聚会，参与者们及嘉宾合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年07月04日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨布鲁克林镇报导）在风和日丽的7月1日中午，纽英伦客家乡亲会在疫情之后的第一个大型户外活动——烤肉联欢在一片炭烟和肉香中开场了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14027679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027679" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027679-DSC08201.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027679-DSC08201-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027679" class="wp-caption-text">客家乡亲生火烤肉。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14027680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027680" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027680-DSC08208.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027680-DSC08208-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027680" class="wp-caption-text">烹饪客家小炒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>湖波荡漾，草木青葱。在风景优美的布鲁克林镇（Brookline）Larz Anderson公园，嘉宾和乡亲们享用美食，谈笑风生，气氛非常欢乐。主办方表示，这次活动有上百人报名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14027678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027678" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027678-DSC08199.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027678-DSC08199-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027678" class="wp-caption-text">纽英伦客家乡亲会7月1日举办烧烤聚会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14027681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027681-DSC08220.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027681-DSC08220-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027681" class="wp-caption-text">客家乡亲准备食物。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽英伦客家乡亲会会长刘秀春说，这次户外烤肉，是客家会在疫情后的第一次大型户外活动，让乡亲们有机会聚会。为此，她要感谢各位乡亲的支持，更要感谢各位工作人员的付出，像负责统筹食物的郑玉春、现炒客家菜的林上田等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14027683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027683-DSC08224.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027683-DSC08224-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027683" class="wp-caption-text">客家乡亲烹饪客家小炒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14027682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027682" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027682-DSC08222.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027682-DSC08222-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027682" class="wp-caption-text">客家乡亲烹饪客家小炒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在公园湖畔的聚会亭里，烤肉的香气伴随炭火的烟雾飘散，芹菜、香乾和鱿鱼在铁锅里爆炒。现场还有烤彩椒、炒米粉、烧仙草、绿豆汤等美食，令乡亲们大饱口福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14027684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027684-DSC08228.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027684-DSC08228-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027684" class="wp-caption-text">大家享用美食，其乐融融。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14027685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027685" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027685-DSC08230.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027685-DSC08230-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027685" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元携夫人倪雪娥，带着爱犬一起参加了这次活动。侨务咨询委员蒋宗壬及夫人史美芳也到场同乐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元携夫人倪雪娥，带着爱犬一起参加了这次活动。孙俭元笑言：“每次来参加客家乡亲会的活动，包含之前庆祝‘天穿日’的活动，都可以感受到客家乡亲的热情跟团结。尤其是美食，令人流连忘返。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14027677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027677" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027677-DSC08186.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027677-DSC08186-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027677" class="wp-caption-text">纽英伦客家乡亲会会长刘秀春感谢大家参与活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>即将调任回台北的孙俭元说，这是他最后一次参加客家会的活动，感谢大家在过去3年的支持，也希望大家继续协助新任处长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14027676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027676" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027676-DSC08183.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027676-DSC08183-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027676" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任潘昭荣（左）及纽英伦客家乡亲会前会长谢如键参与活动。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参与这次烤肉的嘉宾还有侨教中心主任潘昭荣、侨务委员郭竞儒、侨务咨询委员蒋宗壬及夫人史美芳等等。嘉宾们各自带来了礼品用于抽奖。郭兢儒还捐赠了现金红包，作为压轴奖品。在抽奖主持人陈裕逢的热情宣讲下，乡亲们纷纷为中奖者欢呼，现场非常热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14027687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027687-DSC08261.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027687-DSC08261-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027687" class="wp-caption-text">会长刘秀春（右）和抽奖主持人陈裕逢带动气氛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14027689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14027689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027689-DSC08256.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14027689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027689-DSC08256-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14027689" class="wp-caption-text">侨务委员郭兢儒（右）捐赠红包，并与获奖者合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>客家会已于5月20日举行年会，在理事、嘉宾共约40人见证下，<wbr />卸任会长谢如键交棒给新会长刘秀春，<wbr />副会长由邱西蔷和毕儒宗接任。◇</p>
