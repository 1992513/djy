<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>TD南亚节7月29、30日多伦多街头举行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042614-Screenshot-2023-07-26-113748-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月27日讯】一年一度的TD南亚节是多伦多最受欢迎的街头节之一。今年将于7月29日和30日，再度回归多伦多杰拉德印度市场（Gerrard India Bazaar），举行为期两天的家庭娱乐活动、美食、购物、现场娱乐表演等。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/27/n14042594.md#1/2-2-99" rel="attachment wp-att-14042609"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14042609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042609-2-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="631" b="355" /></a></p>
<p>TD南亚节已成为当地人和游客珍视的传统活动。预计将有超过25万名游客参加，热闹非凡。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/27/n14042594.md#1/rosena-fung-gibbia" rel="attachment wp-att-14042613"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14042613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042613-rosena-fung-gibbia-450x450.png" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a>活动将带您踏上穿越南亚的旅程，体验真实而迷人的南亚地区的多样性和美丽。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/27/n14042594.md#1/296945305_5823742157656629_646518746542645233_n" rel="attachment wp-att-14042612"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14042612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042612-296945305_5823742157656629_646518746542645233_n-450x565.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="565" /></a></p>
<p>200多位才华横溢的艺术家将到场表演，展示来自南亚的各种传统和现代风格的音乐和舞蹈。</p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/27/n14042594.md#1/296656619_5823556361008542_8102284618035177382_n-700x300" rel="attachment wp-att-14042618"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14042618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042618-296656619_5823556361008542_8102284618035177382_n-700x300-450x193.jpg" alt="" width="632" b="271" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">活动还提供独特的购物体验。</span></span><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">各种南亚工艺品、珠宝、时装和艺术品供应商，将带来</span></span><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">设计复杂的服装、手工制作的珠宝等精美物品。寻求独一无二的作品和文化工艺品的您，绝对不能错过。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/27/n14042594.md#1/296522982_5823775114320000_4810945428770479228_n-700x300-1" rel="attachment wp-att-14042610"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14042610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042610-296522982_5823775114320000_4810945428770479228_n-700x300-1-450x193.jpg" alt="" width="632" b="271" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">美食爱好者会发现自己仿佛置身于天堂。TD南亚节的美食区，将以一系列美味的南亚美食勾起人们的味蕾。来客</span></span><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">将有机会品尝由专业厨师准备的传统菜肴，感受正宗的南亚风味。</span></span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14042611" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14042611" style="width: 632px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/27/n14042594.md#1/296707680_5823774797653365_8987360183318415583_n-700x300-1" rel="attachment wp-att-14042611"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14042611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14042611-296707680_5823774797653365_8987360183318415583_n-700x300-1-450x193.jpg" alt="" width="632" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14042611" class="wp-caption-text">过往活动照片。（主办方网站）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亲自前往多伦多杰拉德印度市场，体验充满活力和激情的南亚传统文化、艺术和美食吧。</p>
