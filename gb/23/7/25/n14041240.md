<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多越南美食节 8月10日盛大开幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14041271-362288998_793735152684508_2151635997999495008_n-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff

<h6>往年活动照片。（主办方网站）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月25日讯】多伦多越南美食节Taste of Vietnam，将于8月10日在市政厅前的Nathan Phillips Square广场盛大开幕，为期三天。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/25/n14041240.md#1/350334430_785475569593195_5053635989300334915_n" rel="attachment wp-att-14041268"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14041268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14041268-350334430_785475569593195_5053635989300334915_n-450x663.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="663" /></a></p>
<p>继2019年和2022年后，2023年越南美食节第三次在多伦多举行。今年的主题是“莲”（SEN），活动将通过越南的标志性象征——莲花，来庆祝越南文化之美，向无私奉献的越南妇女致敬。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/25/n14041240.md#1/362238846_793735126017844_5556198352421473471_n" rel="attachment wp-att-14041283"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14041283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14041283-362238846_793735126017844_5556198352421473471_n-450x709.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="709" /></a></p>
<p>越南有54个民族，文化多元。除了美食盛宴，美食节还安排有现场表演、音乐、娱乐活动、文化时装秀。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/25/n14041240.md#1/362246458_793733729351317_2001117222051204644_n-1" rel="attachment wp-att-14041270"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14041270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14041270-362246458_793733729351317_2001117222051204644_n-1-450x365.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="365" /></a></p>
<p>传统的服饰展示，令人垂涎的正宗越南菜肴，一定让您大饱眼福口福。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/7/25/n14041240.md#1/7-387" rel="attachment wp-att-14041267"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14041267 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14041267-7-450x298.png" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a></p>
