<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>研究：打流感疫苗小学生人数若增加10%  西澳可省400万医疗费</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年06月24日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张小凡澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span></span>Telethon<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">儿童研究所（</span></span>Telethon Kids Institute<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）的一项研究发现，如果更多的小学适龄儿童接种疫苗，今年冬天西澳可以预防近</span></span>4<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万例流感病例。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，截至今年</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>11<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日，只有</span></span>12.5%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">至</span></span>15<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁西澳青少年接种了流感疫苗，只有</span></span>16%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个月至</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的儿童接种了疫苗。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">该研究所的布莱斯（</span></span>Chris Blyth<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）教授表示，研究发现，西澳不到五分之一的小学生接种了流感疫苗。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">他说，如果接种人数增加</span></span>10%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，将相当于为西澳节省约</span></span>400<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的医疗费用，该研究证实了目前西澳卫生部资助的学校疫苗接种计划。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">这项研究的结果在本周于珀斯举行的</span></span>2023<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年传染病和免疫会议上公布。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据报导，流感病例激增，导致大量住院治疗，一人死亡</span><span lang="zh-TW">——</span></span>3<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的塞格夫（</span></span>Muhammed Saadiq Segaff<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）于</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月在珀斯儿童医院死于流感。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">截至</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>10<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日，根据西澳</span></span>1500<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的</span></span>2023<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年疫苗接种计划，共提供了</span></span>55<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万</span></span>7882<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">次免费流感疫苗接种。</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁以上的人可以在月底前到药剂师那里免费接种疫苗。</span></span></p>
<p>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份，西澳卫生部报告了</span></span>3187<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例感染病例。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">根据最新的应通报传染病报告，</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月头</span></span>10<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">天报告了</span></span>2269<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例病例。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">仅在截至</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>11<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日的一周内，就有</span></span>1375<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例流感感染病例</span><span lang="zh-TW">——</span><span lang="zh-TW">比前七天增加了</span></span>2%——<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">其中</span></span>15%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">为</span></span>B<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">型流感，</span></span>85%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">为</span></span>A<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">型流感。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">同期共录得</span></span>178<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">宗呼吸道合胞病毒</span></span>(RSV)<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个案，较前一周上升</span></span>25%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">在截至</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>11<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日的八周内，西澳急诊科收到了</span></span>943<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例流感或流感样疾病的报告。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据西澳卫生部最新的“病毒观察”报告，入院人数被认为高于往年的这个时候。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">在过去的一周中，西澳报告了</span></span>2775<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例新的中共病毒病例，比前一周下降了</span></span>20%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">责任编辑：杨新云</span></p>
