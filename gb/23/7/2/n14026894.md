<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：麻雀，吱吱喳喳</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14027001-sejrspodie-2740498_1280-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>(Pixabay)

<hr>
<p>麻雀，吱吱喳喳<br />
可是只要有一刹时没听到<br />
为什么他们不再叫？<br />
<p>同样的，小孩子，吱吱喳喳<br />
在乡间农村<br />
在乡间农村的<br />
田野、前埕、后院、广场<br />
叫声吵杂<br />
吱吱喳喳，吱吱喳喳<br />
听不胜听，令人烦躁不悦<br />
可是几年以后，听不到了<br />
反而觉得怪怪的<br />
为什么那些小孩子不再叫？<br />
他们搬哪里去了？</p>
<p><strong>The Sparrows, Chatter and chatter</strong></p>
<p>The sparrows, chatter and chatter<br />
On the countryside<br />
The field, front courtyard, back yard and square<br />
However only no hear of them in a moment<br />
Why do the sparrows ceased to chatter again?<br />
Where are they moved?</p>
<p>It’s the same<br />
In the countryside<br />
The field, front yard, back yard and square<br />
Of the countryside<br />
They hubbub, chatter and chatter<br />
All can’t bear to hear, let’s to be restless<br />
However, after some years we can’t hear of them<br />
On the contrary, we will feel it’s queer<br />
Why do the children ceased to chatter again?<br />
Where are they moved? @</p>
