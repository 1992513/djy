<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林狮子纪念公园启用 小东重划区将成新兴生活圈</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026303-622136-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林家扶舞龙队精彩的演出，赢得县长张丽善(左四)、施松吉(左三)及与会贵宾的肯定。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年07月01日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）云林县政府与<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">国际狮子会</a>合作，于斗南镇小东市地<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E5%88%92%E5%8C%BA.md#1">重划区</a>建置冬藏草原公园暨国际狮子会300-D1区45周年纪念公园，今(7/1)日举行竣工及捐赠典礼，县长张丽善、法务部政务次长蔡碧仲、国际狮子会300-D1区总监施松吉等人共同揭幕启用。</p>
<p>张丽善致词表示，很高兴<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">国际狮子会</a>与云林县政府合作，今天冬藏草原公园在云嘉南狮兄狮姊慷慨解囊下，募得约新台币1,300万元，成立狮子纪念公园，从高速公路下来就可以看到雄伟的狮子矗立眼前，成为云林崭新地标。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026302" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026302-622135-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14026302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026302-622135-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026302" class="wp-caption-text">国际狮子会300-D1区发挥热心公益及社会服务不遗余力的精神，今将狮子纪念公园工程结余款捐赠给云嘉南四个家扶中心及斗六分局。（云林县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14026301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026301" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026301-622134-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14026301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026301-622134-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026301" class="wp-caption-text">斗南镇小东市地<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E5%88%92%E5%8C%BA.md#1">重划区</a>公(二)冬藏草原公园暨国际狮子会300-D1区45周年纪念公园，占地约1200坪，增加斗南在地、云林乡亲及狮友们来此休闲、散步机会，为乡亲打造美学舒适城市生活。（云林县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14026305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026305-622138-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14026305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026305-622138-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026305" class="wp-caption-text">狮子公园入口主题石雕。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14026306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026306" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026306-622139-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14026306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026306-622139-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026306" class="wp-caption-text">由狮子会300-D1区捐赠的雄伟 “狮子家族石雕”，是整个公园最瞩目的焦点。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14026300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026300" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026300-622133-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14026300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026300-622133-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026300" class="wp-caption-text">斗南小东市地重划区公(二)冬藏草原公园暨国际狮子会300-D1区45周年纪念公园，今(7/1)日举行竣落成，张丽善县长今特地出席揭牌启用典礼。（云林县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张丽善指出，小东重划区整体开发规模达新台币6亿5000万，共开发45.68公顷，完成分配已达8成，还有8笔土地将进行标售， 将以低污染工业发展为主，创造2500个就业机会，促进地方经济。</p>
<p>为打造消费市场，县府将斥资6亿兴建“斗南小东休闲娱乐中心”，设置室内外泳池、商店街等，盼成为彰云嘉投的新消费地标。此外，县府也规划打造电动车产业储能、转运空间，以顺应未来净零减碳趋势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026304" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026304-622137-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-14026304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026304-622137-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026304" class="wp-caption-text">狮友们在狮子石雕前开心合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>施松吉总监表示，冬藏公园由云林科技大学设计学院规划设计为狮子纪念公园，占地约1200坪，内有狮子林、勇气大道、彩虹步道、狮子家庭主题石雕等。感谢张县长支持，提供休闲地方，在国际狮子会300-D1区历经45个年头，将改制为300E-1区之际，特别留下永久休憩场地建置一座纪念公园，并将结余款捐赠给云嘉南四个家扶中心及斗六分局各20万元的助学金及急难救助器材。欣见家扶精彩的才艺表演，施总监另加码5万元给各家扶，共爱心捐赠120万元。</p>
<p>施总监感谢狮友热心公益及社会服务不遗余力的精神，期待国际狮子会300E-1区再创另一个辉煌的45年。</p>
