<h6>“串串香”是四川地区特色小吃之一。（旧金山大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
“串串香性价比高，每串article content begin -->
	<p>【大纪元2023年07月01日讯】文：余亨之?大纪元<br />
“串串香性价比高，每串$0.5，吃很多串不同的菜品，口味丰富，不用担心吃不完浪费。本店是中半岛以北唯一的一家串串香火锅店。”串串香（Gu&#8217;s Skewers Hot Pot）老板Nancy说。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">特价：串串香自助餐午餐特价$19.99/晚餐$28.99</span></strong></p>
	<!-- article content end.5，吃很多串不同的菜品，口味丰富，不用担心吃不完浪费。本店是中半岛以北唯一的一家串串香火锅店。”串串香（Gu&#8217;s Skewers Hot Pot）老板Nancy说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026144" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026144-dda3a5047438d4393a90be635fd4b0b0.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026144-dda3a5047438d4393a90be635fd4b0b0-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026144" class="wp-caption-text">“串串香”鸳鸯锅，一边是麻辣牛油锅，一边是乌鸡菌汤。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“串串香”是四川地区特色小吃之一，和吃火锅差不多。只不过它是将不同的食材菜品串在一根根竹签上，将其放进滚烫的火锅中煮熟，然后拿着竹签蘸香油碟吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026145-3a04f74ff8e11207ac4195c5b68d9c41.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026145-3a04f74ff8e11207ac4195c5b68d9c41-600x383.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026145" class="wp-caption-text">食材菜品串在一根根竹签上，将其放进滚烫的火锅中煮熟。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nancy端上一个鸳鸯锅放在桌子上，一边是麻辣牛油锅，一汪红火又热辣的牛油在沸腾着，这款锅底适合重口味的人，爽麻爽辣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026146-f41c4dc73ca7ed10b91d918856bceb56.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026146-f41c4dc73ca7ed10b91d918856bceb56-600x433.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026146" class="wp-caption-text">“串串香”鸳鸯锅。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一边是乌鸡菌汤，乳白色的浓汤中翻滚着枸杞、茶树菇，香气扑面而来，乌鸡菌汤鲜美浓郁。乌鸡，自古以来都是名副其实的“药效鸡种”，《本草纲目》里也多次记载乌鸡的滋补、养生。非常适合老年人的一款汤头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026147-2ea326e1b499f999ec5a6d02fb2a4871.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026147-2ea326e1b499f999ec5a6d02fb2a4871-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026147" class="wp-caption-text">“串串香”的各种火锅料。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“此外，我们还有牛骨、鸡骨熬炖6小时的白汤；白汤加上番茄、大枣、枸杞、黄瓜皮的番茄汤，非常开胃，小孩、女生特别喜欢；还有，中等麻辣的清油锅；微辣的老坛酸菜锅底。”Nancy说，“我们也有中间没有隔层的、单一口味的锅，满足个人需求。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026148-fb6dfd1153cdc644288c96db96936fda.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026148-fb6dfd1153cdc644288c96db96936fda-600x393.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026148" class="wp-caption-text">“串串香”有各种蘸酱。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“串串香”非常注重菜品口味的搭配，仅仅牛肉这个种类，就有麻辣牛、香菜牛、泡椒牛、芹菜牛、酸豆角牛和葱牛肉等不同款风味的串串。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026149-e1a47e69227b6b9b4b3460f4d586ad92.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026149-e1a47e69227b6b9b4b3460f4d586ad92-600x383.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026149" class="wp-caption-text">“串串香”的各种火锅料。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们注重健康、注重菜品的保鲜度，串串都是当天开门营业时现串的，然后放冰柜里边保存；我们的汤底都不放鸡精、味精。”Nancy说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026150-999d9dc85bb968f69ebcaf97c79d74dc.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026150-999d9dc85bb968f69ebcaf97c79d74dc-600x383.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026150" class="wp-caption-text">“串串香”的各种火锅料。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14026151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026151-5b22ff43086d2018d38017b4fed5df8c.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026151-5b22ff43086d2018d38017b4fed5df8c-600x383.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026151" class="wp-caption-text">“串串香”的各种串串。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>她介绍店内的菜品有黄喉、肥牛、鱼片、掌中宝、鹌鹑蛋、鸡胗、黑毛肚、火腿肠、肫肝、鸭肠、蟹柳、肉丸、木耳、香菇、豆腐皮、豆筋、藕片等六十多种。</p>
<p>Mike G.在yelp上留言：“餐厅后台将所有的肉类和蔬菜，串在竹签上。 我们选择了辣羊肉、辣牛肉、日本豆腐、卷心菜和鹌鹑蛋??都非常好！我很满意自己的选择。如果您正在寻找独特的火锅体验，串串香就是您的不二之选！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026154" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026154-c00450a3e0187186214b16d4a96a49a4.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026154-c00450a3e0187186214b16d4a96a49a4-600x383.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026154" class="wp-caption-text">“串串香”的各种串串。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>串串香，今年5月份新张。由于定位独特、新奇，整个湾区中半岛以北，只此一家串串香火锅店，因此来尝鲜的食客络绎不绝，回头客多，有的已经来了四五次了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14026155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14026155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026155-01.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14026155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14026155-01-600x314.png" alt="" width="600" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14026155" class="wp-caption-text">今年五月才开张的旧金山“串串香”（Gu&#8217;s Skewers Hot Pot）。（旧金山大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“6月底，店里将增加九宫格卤味涮品，把卤制好的鸡脚、鸡翅、猪蹄、牛肉、猪大肠、牛筋等，装在九宫格或六宫格的盘子，放在火锅边涮着吃，比单纯地吃卤味，口感会更丰富，更有滋味。”Nancy说。◇<br />
