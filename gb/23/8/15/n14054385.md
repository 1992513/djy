<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>钮扣下的布破了怎么办？基本修补技巧DIY</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14057175-shutterstock_561480826-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>衣服的缝份脱线了怎么办？教你基本的修补方法。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：衣服穿久了有时侧边的缝线会脱落，或者<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%AE%E6%89%A3.md#1">钮扣</a>下方的布容易有破洞，衣服因此不穿了很可惜。其实只要学会基本的缝补技巧就能让衣服恢复原来的样子，自己<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%AE%E8%A1%A5.md#1">修补</a>很有成就感喔，省钱又环保。本文教你如何在家修补侧边裂开的缝线和修补钮扣下的破洞，希望这些小小的技巧，能带给你一些生活的乐趣。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%AE%E8%A1%A5.md#1">修补</a>侧边裂开的缝线</h2>
<p>只要一用力就容易裂开的侧边缝线，只要布面没破损，用对针缝就能完美修补起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054479" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054479-P.84-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054479-P.84-1-300x282.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054479" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 先确认布面是否有破损。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054480" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054480-P.84-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054480-P.84-2-300x282.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054480" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 用锥子把绽开的缝线往反面挑出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054481" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054481-P.84-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054481-P.84-3-300x280.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054481" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将布料的折线对齐，对准裂开的起始点，别上珠针。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054482" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054482" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054482-P.84-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054482-P.84-4-300x282.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054482" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 缝线穿针后打始缝结，在折线的位置从由里向外出针。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054483" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054483-P.84-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054483-P.84-5-300x281.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054483" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 拆下珠针，朝没有露出缝线的折线下针，挑起约0.3～0.4cm。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054484" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054484-P.84-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054484-P.84-6-300x279.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054484" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 抽出缝线后，朝对侧的折线下针，挑起约0.3～0.4cm的布出针。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054485" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054485-P.84-7.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054485-P.84-7-300x283.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054485" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 重复步骤5～6的动作，一直缝到起始点后，在折线稍微靠近缝份的侧边打收尾结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054486" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054486-P.84-8.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054486-P.84-8-300x282.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054486" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 在布与布之间下针，将线轻轻往下拉，就能将收尾结藏在里面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054487" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054487-P.84-9.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054487-P.84-9-300x281.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054487" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 剪掉多余的线头，完成。</p>
<h2>修补钮扣下的破洞</h2>
<p>用力拉扯钮扣时不仅会让钮扣脱落，布面也可能会被勾破，此时只要修补布料再缝钮扣，就能完美隐藏破洞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054489" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054489-P.85-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054489-P.85-1-300x278.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054489" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 剪掉绽开的缝线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054490" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054490-P.85-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054490-P.85-2-300x283.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054490" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 取一块薄布衬，裁剪下比钮扣大一圈的形状，贴在破洞的反面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054491" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054491-P.85-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054491-P.85-3-300x281.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054491" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 缝线穿针后打结。从正面破洞的中心下针，在破洞的外侧出针，每一针的来回都要跨过破洞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054492" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054492-P.85-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054492-P.85-4-300x278.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054492" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 尽可能紧密地缝，把破洞补满，不留任何缝隙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054493" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054493-P.85-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054493-P.85-5-300x281.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054493" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 破洞补满的状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054494" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054494-P.85-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054494-P.85-6-300x278.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054494" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将针从反面刺出，从钮扣反面的位置穿出，在破洞上方缝上钮扣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054495" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054495-P.85-7.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054495-P.85-7-300x280.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054495" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 打收尾结，剪线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054496" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054496-P.85-8.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054496-P.85-8-300x280.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054496" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 固定好钮扣的样子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054497" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054497-P.85-9.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-14054497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054497-P.85-9-300x279.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054497" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
