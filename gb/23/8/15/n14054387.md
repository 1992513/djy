<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>袜子破洞了别丢！“欧式织补”缝出新创意</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14057146-20230819_sewing_socks_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>用“欧式织补”开启永续环保生活。（苹果屋提供／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>所谓“织补（Darning）”，是一种起源于欧洲的传统缝补方法。一般来说，进行织补需要使用“磨菇辅助器”。以下教你只用手边现有的器具，也能轻松织补的方法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054499" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054499" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054499-P.90-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14054499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054499-P.90-1-450x547.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="547" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054499" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>心爱的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%9C%E5%AD%90.md#1">袜子</a>或衣物破洞了，先别急着丢弃！只要利用简单的修补，稍微花一点时间改造，就能赋予衣物全新样貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054500" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054500-P.90-2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14054500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054500-P.90-2.jpg" alt="" width="440" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054500" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>使用的缝线为毛线或绣线。利用容器的盖子、平坦的石头，或是任何形状偏圆、大小如手掌的物品皆可，垫在破洞的下方。</p>
<h2><strong>变化版</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_14054501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054501" style="width: 446px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054501-P.90-3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14054501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054501-P.90-3.jpg" alt="" width="446" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054501" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以不同的颜色紧密地缝上经纱（纵向）和纬纱（横向），或是在周边添加刺绣，可以自由发挥创意绣上喜欢的图案。</p>
<h3>HOW TO MAKE</h3>
<figure id="attachment_14054502" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054502" style="width: 382px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054502-P.90-4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14054502" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054502-P.90-4.jpg" alt="" width="382" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054502" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在破洞周围以平针缝绕一圈，范围比破洞略大，开始手缝的线要预留足够长度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054503" style="width: 383px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054503-P.90-5.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14054503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054503-P.90-5.jpg" alt="" width="383" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054503" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在平针缝的外侧下针，在破洞的外侧出针，每一针的来回都要跨过破洞，打造出经纱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054504" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054504" style="width: 383px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054504-P.90-6.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14054504" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054504-P.90-6.jpg" alt="" width="383" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054504" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>搭好经纱后，接着由右向左入针，像织布般地一条接一条地交互挑起经纱，打造出纬纱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14054505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14054505" style="width: 377px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054505-P.90-7.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-14054505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14054505-P.90-7.jpg" alt="" width="377" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14054505" class="wp-caption-text">（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>结束织补时，在反面做几次回针缝，剪掉多余的线头。图为完成的样子。</p>
