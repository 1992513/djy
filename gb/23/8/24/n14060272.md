<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>动手做美味面包：生吐司 当早餐健康可口</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060523-6ca1fcb20f09ea61d41f592abd898010-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>自制健康又美味的生吐司。（幸福文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这几年日本非常流行的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">生吐司</a>也红到台湾，连不喜欢吃面包的人也难敌这股生吐司旋风。这款吐司是由大阪市的“乃が美”公司所开发的商品，标榜吐司有着新鲜鲜奶油的自然甘甜而且蓬松柔软，不需要再烘烤就可以直接食用，像生巧克力般有着入口即融的感觉。</p>
<p>“乃が美”的社长阪上雄司并不是专业的面包师傅，年轻时曾经开过烧肉店，还担任大阪一家专业摔角公司的主席。他在一次参观疗养院的活动中，注意到院内的老人们会将早餐的吐司面包边留下来，因为这个部位较干硬难以入口，而且有些儿童还会对鸡蛋过敏。他被这件事启发，开始研究尝试，希望做出一款不含鸡蛋，适合每个年龄层、柔软甘甜的美味面包。</p>
<p>2013 年10 月2 日，“乃が美”于大阪府上本町开幕，一开始销售并不顺利，一整天也只卖出30 条吐司。但经由顾客口碑相传，媒体也开始报导，马上打响了知名度。现在在日本已经有了125 家店面，店外随时都排着长长的人龙。</p>
<p>因为贴心而诞生的吐司由蜂蜜、鲜奶油及牛奶制作，低温烘烤使面包有了柔美的外观及口感。制作时，注意最后发酵不要太满进炉，烤温不要太高，就能够完成这款美味的梦幻吐司。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>材料</strong>（份量：12两吐司1条，10*20*10cm）</span></h2>
<p>高筋面粉300g</p>
<p>细砂糖15g</p>
<p>盐3/4 茶匙（约3g）</p>
<p>蜂蜜25g</p>
<p>速发干酵母1/2 茶匙（约2g）</p>
<p>牛奶（室温）170g</p>
<p>动物性鲜奶油 50g</p>
<p>无盐奶油20g</p>
<p><strong>* 请依照CH1“面团基本操作”揉好面团并发酵1 小时至两倍大。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h2>
<p>1.工作桌上撒些高筋面粉，将发酵完成的面团移出到桌面，表面也撒些高筋面粉。用手将面团中的空气压下去并挤出来，平均分切成2 等份。将面团光滑面翻折出来滚圆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060362" style="width: 303px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060362-be88232f49bff7f8acc33a8fc820b9ea.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060362-be88232f49bff7f8acc33a8fc820b9ea.jpg" alt="" width="303" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060362" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14060363" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060363" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060363-f5bfd35350932705637ce08f1d950965.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060363" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060363-f5bfd35350932705637ce08f1d950965.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060363" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.覆盖干净的布，让面团休息15 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060364" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060364-a2102d5c9878f95c216a5bc31ec92e89.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060364-a2102d5c9878f95c216a5bc31ec92e89.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060364" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.在面团表面撒些高筋面粉，擀成长方形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060367" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060367-9dd97ed1563147fcfd0d5f69bfee7b90.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060367-9dd97ed1563147fcfd0d5f69bfee7b90.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060367" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14060370" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060370" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060370-f72d13915478e49761a8a4b07d4636ee.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060370-f72d13915478e49761a8a4b07d4636ee.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060370" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.由短边向内卷起成柱状，盖上干布，再让面团休息15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060372" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060372-ebc04a897c6fbf1f6ce3ad245d73e0c4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-14060372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060372-ebc04a897c6fbf1f6ce3ad245d73e0c4.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060372" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
