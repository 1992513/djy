<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加拿大婚纱展9月8-10日多伦多会议中心举行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14059973-44365e6400e003a9f4b79db0946aec13-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年08月24日讯】<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">加拿大婚纱展</span></span></span>（Canada’s Bridal Show）是加拿大规模最大、最负盛名的婚纱礼服及婚庆用品展，拥有超过38年的历史，为新人提供最全面的婚礼解决方案，让新娘新郎梦想成真。</p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/24/n14059955.md#1/1280-6" rel="attachment wp-att-14059969"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14059969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14059969-1280-450x290.jpg" alt="" width="560" b="361" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">秉承传统，装饰优雅，加拿大婚纱展在帮助新人打造完美婚礼方面有着悠久的历史。</span></span></span>2023年<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">加拿大婚纱展</span></span></span>将于<span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">9月8日至9月10日，在多伦多会议中心（Metro Toronto Convention Centre）举行。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/24/n14059955.md#1/1280-1-4" rel="attachment wp-att-14059966"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14059966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14059966-1280-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="561" b="374" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"> 从精致的新娘礼服和文雅的西装，到迷人的套装和时尚趋势，加拿大婚纱展为新娘和新郎提供了难得的机会，新人能够亲身体验最新设计的美感和工艺，感受奢华面料的质感，选择搭配得体的完美配饰。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/24/n14059955.md#1/1280-2-5" rel="attachment wp-att-14059967"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14059967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14059967-1280-2-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="561" b="375" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p>亲临加拿大婚纱展，新人将发现婚礼的每一个细节都将是值得铭记的幸福瞬间。完美的蛋糕不仅仅是甜点，还是反映情侣个性的杰作；摄影师和摄像师不仅仅是专业人士，他们还是一定要留下永恒回忆的大日子的故事讲述者。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/24/n14059955.md#1/6f003b4ba9a451a9df9ffdea583a621c" rel="attachment wp-att-14059972"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14059972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14059972-6f003b4ba9a451a9df9ffdea583a621c-450x448.png" alt="" width="560" b="558" /></a></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">在为期三天的精彩活动中，加拿大婚纱展将为情侣们提供一个与顶级供应商交流、体验最新潮流、激发想像力的平台，引导新人完成婚礼策划的复杂过程。 </span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/8/24/n14059955.md#1/1280-3-3" rel="attachment wp-att-14059968"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14059968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14059968-1280-3-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="561" b="374" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">享有盛誉的加拿大婚纱展，不仅仅是一场表演，更是一场爱、优雅和梦想成真的盛会。</span></span></span></p>
