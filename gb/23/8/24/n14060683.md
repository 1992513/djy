<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【飞向欧洲】凯旋门——俯瞰巴黎全景的观景台</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060688-297f6229868f3e695cdae8ccadbbd6a5-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>享受悠长假期，本期《飞向欧洲》，我们飞到法国巴黎的凯旋门感受拿破仑当年的磅礴气势。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>在香港这个大都市里，快速的生活节奏让人喘不过气来。每天重复的工作，连做梦都想着那些方程式，想着那些报告，想着哪里可以找到需要的资料。</p>
<p>一直都很想放个悠长的假期，在旅游的黄金档期，就让我们去到世界的另一边，感受一下其它民族的悠闲文化吧！</p>
<p>终于来到法国巴黎啦，我们的第一站是著名的凯旋门，哗！好高大啊！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060690" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060690-d7af23bf2c3222c56b383cdc3f271f2b.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14060690 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060690-d7af23bf2c3222c56b383cdc3f271f2b-600x344.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060690" class="wp-caption-text">凯旋门有四十九米高，整座都刻有精致的巨形浮雕，讲述的是拿破仑的丰功伟业。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>凯旋门是巴黎的地标之一，叫凯旋门，当然就是打仗赢了凯旋而归的意思。公元十八世纪，法国国王拿破仑征战各国，称霸整个欧洲。1805年，拿破仑为了炫耀国力，庆祝战争的胜利，迎接凯旋而归的法军将士，下令修建这道凯旋门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060691" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060691-a1a018779c431554299cf2a8f5613107.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14060691" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060691-a1a018779c431554299cf2a8f5613107-600x344.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060691" class="wp-caption-text">终于来到法国巴黎，著名的凯旋门高大庄严。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>凯旋门有四十九米高，整座都刻有精致的巨形浮雕，讲述的是拿破仑的丰功伟业，正门右面的雕刻叫作《出征》，它讲述来自马赛的义勇军，击退敌军，解救国家的危机，左面的雕刻讲的是胜利女神把桂冠颁授给拿破仑的情景，凯旋门的顶部是一个瞭望台，可以走楼梯或者坐电梯上去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060692-fe64471f38834bdb99fb9b86a754e0d5.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14060692" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060692-fe64471f38834bdb99fb9b86a754e0d5-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060692" class="wp-caption-text">凯旋门正门左面的雕刻讲的是胜利女神把桂冠颁授给拿破仑的情景。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14060693" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060693" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060693-6f17ee00faa044856155fc0951d10ab7.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14060693" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060693-6f17ee00faa044856155fc0951d10ab7-600x389.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060693" class="wp-caption-text">凯旋门正门右面的雕刻叫作《出征》，它讲述来自马赛的义勇军击退敌军，解救国家的危机。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14060694" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060694" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060694-8bafc6a28349db746df5ea4b89dfff81.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14060694" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060694-8bafc6a28349db746df5ea4b89dfff81-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060694" class="wp-caption-text">凯旋门上的浮雕。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们现在就上到了凯旋门上面的观景台了，在这里可以看到整个巴黎的全景，这条就是著名的香榭丽舍大道。</p>
<p>从观景台看过去，大道两边种的大树修剪得齐刷刷，加上巴黎十二条大街都以凯旋门为中心，向四周放射，看上去相当有气势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14060695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060695-e42b27d13b275647ab3fe7e5368e3c38.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14060695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060695-e42b27d13b275647ab3fe7e5368e3c38-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060695" class="wp-caption-text">凯旋门上面的观景台可以看到整个巴黎的全景。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14060696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14060696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060696-6231bb755e3fe8354ef77cb9b65f4b02.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14060696" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14060696-6231bb755e3fe8354ef77cb9b65f4b02-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14060696" class="wp-caption-text">凯旋门上面的观景台可以看到整个巴黎的全景。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
