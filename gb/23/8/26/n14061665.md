<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不吃香菜？可用这些芳香食材替代</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061679-shutterstock_2348239699-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香菜有人爱吃，有人觉得它散发怪味。如果抗拒香菜期望合适的替代，至少有12种芳香植物可选。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年08月26日讯】（大纪元记者李若琳综合报导）绿油油的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8F%9C.md#1">香菜</a>气味明亮，将寡淡的菜肴变得有滋有味，受不少人喜欢。可你知道吗， 具有相似芳香效果的食材还有下面这些，对不吃香菜的人或者家中香菜用完时是很好的替代品。</p>
<h2>讨厌<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8F%9C.md#1">香菜</a>，是天生的？</h2>
<p>尽管香菜对部分人而言有难以抗拒的“香”，但有些人是天生厌恶的，原因是什么呢？康奈尔大学（Cornell University）的一项研究给出了答案。</p>
<p>研究人员对3万受测者进行测试发现，香菜含有醛类，部分人的特殊基因使他们对醛类的嗅觉敏感。而虫子和肥皂释放的气味也有醛类，所以香菜对这些人而言可能是虫子、金属或肥皂等东西。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061680" style="width: 593px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061680-shutterstock_638650837-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14061680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061680-shutterstock_638650837-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="593" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061680" class="wp-caption-text">研究发现，讨厌香菜和基因有关。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>爱上香菜，可能吗？</h2>
<p>由此可见，讨厌香菜是天生的。好在这只是对气味敏感，非食物过敏，然而对香菜的好感是可以后天培养的。</p>
<p>如何培养？在喜欢的食物中由少到多添加香菜是办法之一。创造这类积极的体验，每一次都可能改变你对香菜的负面印象，从而开始欣赏它。</p>
<p>如果实在不能接受香菜，那也不是世界末日。没有香菜，依然可以烹饪很多料理。或者，寻找其它芳香植物替代未尝不可。</p>
<h2>新鲜蔬菜类</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14061682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061682" style="width: 593px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061682-shutterstock_2148129427-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-14061682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061682-shutterstock_2148129427-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="593" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061682" class="wp-caption-text">平叶<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E8%8A%B9.md#1">欧芹</a>。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E8%8A%B9.md#1">欧芹</a>：</strong>欧芹与香菜同科，带有胡椒香气，受美国、欧洲和中东料理欢迎。它有平叶（Flat-leaf parsley）和卷叶（Curly parsley）两种。平叶欧芹的味道浓郁，通常捣碎用于酱料调味，可等量替换香菜。卷叶欧芹更适合装饰摆盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061683-shutterstock_144727729-600x398.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061683-shutterstock_144727729-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061683" class="wp-caption-text">卷叶欧芹。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>芹菜叶子：</strong>料理芹菜时，因为芹菜叶子长老了苦味较重，一般只留下茎秆丢弃叶子。其实取少部分叶子切碎入菜不会影响口感，还有提香效果，尤其适合煮汤、炖肉和砂锅菜（casseroles）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061693" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061693" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061693-shutterstock_1791372134-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061693" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061693-shutterstock_1791372134-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061693" class="wp-caption-text">甜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>：</strong>香菜的气味独特，罗勒亦然，而且品种众多，偏甜辛辣都有。如果为亚洲菜增香，可以尝试泰国罗勒（Thai basil）、柠檬罗勒（lemon basil）或圣罗勒（holy basil），甜罗勒（sweet basil）于沙拉、意大利料理十分相衬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061684-shutterstock_44877826-600x401.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061684-shutterstock_44877826-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061684" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B3%E8%90%9D.md#1">莳萝</a>。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B3%E8%90%9D.md#1">莳萝</a>：</strong>莳萝（Dill）散发淡淡的八角味，叶子和种子能为菜肴带来不同的增香效果。种子的辛香味较强，用来腌渍和酱料调味更胜一筹。细细碎碎的叶子带有些许柠檬香，拌入米饭、土豆泥，或者沙拉上撒一点，有画龙点睛的效果。无论调味，还是装饰，改用莳萝叶子替代香菜将带来美妙的体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061685" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061685-shutterstock_227132083-600x397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061685-shutterstock_227132083-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061685" class="wp-caption-text">越南香菜（Rau Ram）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>越南香菜：</strong>东南亚菜以善用香料闻名，香草于菜肴就像油盐一样不可或缺，越南香菜（Rau Ram）即是其中之一。它的外观和味道都有点像薄荷，为许多越南名菜担当灵魂配菜。用于替代香菜，为沙拉或汤底增添风味都非常对味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061686-shutterstock_1667911831-600x373.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061686-shutterstock_1667911831-600x373.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061686" class="wp-caption-text">刺芹（Culantro）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>刺芹：</strong>刺芹（Culantro）具有高度芳香的叶子，味同香菜，但更强烈，是越南、泰国菜的爱用配菜。替换时，减量添加可模仿香菜的效果。刺芹耐煮，适合中途加入汤和炖菜，而香菜为保留原味要在料理快结束才加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061687-shutterstock_134863445-600x397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061687-shutterstock_134863445-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061687" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜龙蒿（Tarragon）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>龙蒿：</strong>法国料理要是没有龙蒿（Tarragon），不知要黯淡多少。新鲜的嫩叶和根呈绿色，含有茴香、罗勒的清新，于肉类料理、沙拉酱创造出一种优雅而精致的芳香。和香菜一样，最好在料理快熟时添加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061689-shutterstock_694065304-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061689-shutterstock_694065304-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061689" class="wp-caption-text">巴巴罗叶（Papalo）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>巴巴罗叶：</strong>于南美洲料理，巴巴罗叶（Papalo）和香菜都是不可或缺的调味菜。它的味道介于芝麻菜和香菜之间，点缀沙拉、三明治、为鳄梨酱调味……模仿香菜的味道一点都不赖。烹煮时，最好在结束料理前一刻添加。</p>
<p>混合香草：香菜的味道因品种、产地多有不同，有的辛辣味偏重，有些散发芹菜清香多些……寻找香菜的替换食材，建议选择欧芹和巴巴罗叶；对于其它的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B3%E9%A6%99%E9%A3%9F%E6%9D%90.md#1">芳香食材</a>，取几种香气接近的拼凑出类似香菜的复合味道，也是好办法。</p>
<h2>香料类</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14061690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061690" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061690-shutterstock_1810663975-600x398.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061690" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061690-shutterstock_1810663975-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061690" class="wp-caption-text">香菜籽。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>干香菜（Dried cilantro）和香菜籽：</strong>香菜一旦干燥，类似芹菜的清新香气几乎消失殆尽。香菜籽（Coriander seeds）是香菜的种子，呈黄褐色，有开裂的果瓣，和许多种子香料一样带有柔和的木香味，香气低调；研碎后撒入汤汁，立马让人想起香菜根的泥土味。</p>
<p>这两种香料的芳香效果柔和，是希腊、南美、东南亚和印度料理常用成分，用于咖喱、腌肉酱、面包等，因此在超市的香料区很容易买到。事实上，若作香菜替代品，它们较干欧芹逊色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061691" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061691-shutterstock_626219219-600x376.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061691" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061691-shutterstock_626219219-600x376.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061691" class="wp-caption-text">整粒孜然（Cumin）和孜然粉。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>孜然：</strong>亚洲、拉丁美洲和中东地区喜用孜然（Cumin）调味，尤其与羊肉一拍即合。它的香气持久，稍微加热烘烤就能带出美妙的坚果香气，入菜、烘焙都合适。其大小如香菜籽，用法也相似，可尝试模仿香菜为料理提香，但注意用量，太多会释放苦味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061692-shutterstock_481206751-600x397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-14061692" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061692-shutterstock_481206751-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061692" class="wp-caption-text">整粒香芹籽（Caraway seeds）。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>香芹籽：</strong>好吃的欧式香草面包，香芹籽（Caraway seeds）是一味，德国泡菜也有它的身影。这种植物天生就是芳香好物，叶子和种子的芳香本领各有所长。种子带有胡椒的辛辣味，又不失温暖的香甜。叶子味道温和，类似欧芹、莳萝。</p>
