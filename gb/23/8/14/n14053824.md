<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】铁板煎牛肉配凉拌小松菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053851-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在家做餐厅菜：铁板煎牛肉配凉拌小松菜。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>小编语：</strong>铁板料理一直以来都广受人们喜爱，尤其是在煎肉排的时候，肉类的油脂在煎烤下会散发香气，让人垂延三尺，如果搭配一些凉拌蔬菜，吃起来更不腻味，当然在烹饪时别忘了调味是非常重要的！下面我们介绍这一道菜是使用牛肉片进行铁板煎烤，搭配凉拌蔬菜，分开料理却一点都不麻烦，在家就有餐厅级的享受，可以满足家人的味蕾，而且顾及到健康。</p>
<h2 style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>铁板煎牛肉配凉拌小松菜</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>腌好的安格斯牛肉片12 oz<br />
洋葱半个（切丝）<br />
红椒1/4个（切丝）<br />
柴鱼片0.5 oz<br />
炒芝麻1茶匙<br />
小松菜4棵<br />
鸿禧菇6 oz</p>
<figure id="attachment_14053852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14053852" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053852-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14053852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053852-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14053852" class="wp-caption-text">腌好的安格斯牛肉片（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【凉拌小松菜调味】</span></h3>
<p>味醂1汤匙<br />
酱油2汤匙<br />
白砂糖1/4茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_14053845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14053845" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053845-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14053845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053845-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14053845" class="wp-caption-text">调味料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 小松菜整棵洗干净，鸿禧菇切去末端拆散后洗净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14053838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14053838" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053838-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14053838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053838-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14053838" class="wp-caption-text">小松菜整棵洗干净，鸿禧菇切去末端拆散后洗净备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 烧热一锅水，鸿禧菇烫30秒捞出沥干。放入小松菜煮1分钟，捞出浸冰水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14053839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14053839" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053839-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14053839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053839-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14053839" class="wp-caption-text">烧热一锅水，鸿禧菇烫30秒捞出沥干。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 小松菜沥干水后切小段，放入一个大碗中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14053840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14053840" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053840-5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14053840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053840-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14053840" class="wp-caption-text">小松菜沥干水后切小段，放入一个大碗中。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 再放入鸿禧菇，加入调味，放上芝麻及柴鱼片便可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14053843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14053843" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053843-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14053843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053843-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14053843" class="wp-caption-text">再放入鸿禧菇，加入调味，放上芝麻及柴鱼片便可。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 烧热小铁板，放上洋葱和红椒测试温度后，再铺上牛肉片，两面煎约30秒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14053842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14053842" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053842-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14053842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053842-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14053842" class="wp-caption-text">烧热小铁板，放上洋葱和红椒测试温度后，再铺上牛肉片，两面煎约30秒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 牛肉熟了之后取出装盘，旁边再放上凉拌小松菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14053850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14053850" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053850-8.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14053850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14053850-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14053850" class="wp-caption-text">牛肉熟了之后取出装盘，旁边再放上凉拌小松菜。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h3>
<p>1. 做这道菜所用的小铁板是我在日本北海道旅行时买的，下面放上一个小酒精罐点着火，半罐酒精便可烧完12oz的牛肉，香气扑鼻，即烧即食。</p>
<p>2. 这次买的牛肉是已经腌好的，所以不用再调味。</p>
<p>3. 小松菜烫完后再浸冰水，可以保持翠绿。</p>
<p>4. 牛排配上凉拌小松菜，可减轻油腻感。◇</p>
