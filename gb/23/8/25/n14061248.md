<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】黄豆酱牛蒡炆牛腩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061259-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黄豆酱牛蒡炆牛腩。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%86.md#1">炆</a>”是粤菜常见的烹调方法之一，是采用慢火烹煮食材，当食材煮熟后会变软，并且保留少量的原汁，再和其他的素材融合其风味。这种做法并不困难，但是需要花费时间和耐心，但却是很多人的心头好，保留食材的鲜甜和风味，配上白饭往往可以吃上好几碗。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">黄豆酱<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%92%A1.md#1">牛蒡</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%86.md#1">炆</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%85%A9.md#1">牛腩</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%85%A9.md#1">牛腩</a>角3磅<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%92%A1.md#1">牛蒡</a>3条约1磅<br />
西芹4条<br />
红萝卜2条<br />
洋葱1个<br />
八角、陈皮、草果各少许<br />
姜4安士<br />
蒜粒8粒<br />
红葱头2粒<br />
葱段2条（切成葱花）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061257-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14061257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061257-1-450x297.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061257" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>
<p>黄豆酱3汤匙<br />
绍酒1汤匙<br />
生抽1汤匙<br />
老抽1茶匙<br />
蚝油2汤匙<br />
冰糖1 oz</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 牛蒡去皮后切小段，西芹、红萝卜、洋葱切大块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061251" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061251-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14061251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061251-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061251" class="wp-caption-text">牛蒡去皮后切小段，西芹、红萝卜、洋葱切大块。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 姜去皮切粒，蒜粒拍扁，红葱头去皮切小粒。牛腩烫过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061252" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061252-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14061252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061252-3-450x245.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061252" class="wp-caption-text">牛腩烫过。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 爆香姜、蒜、红葱头和黄豆酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061253" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061253-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14061253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061253-4-450x353.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="353" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061253" class="wp-caption-text">爆香姜、蒜、红葱头和黄豆酱。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 放回牛腩翻炒，赞酒加水盖过牛腩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061254" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061254-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14061254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061254-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061254" class="wp-caption-text">放回牛腩翻炒，赞酒加水盖过牛腩。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放入牛蒡、冰糖和辛香料，用中小火炖1.5小时，煮至肉八成熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061255" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061255-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14061255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061255-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061255" class="wp-caption-text">放入牛蒡、冰糖和辛香料，用中小火炖1.5小时，煮至肉八成熟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 加入洋葱、红萝卜和西芹，再放入剩余调味料，多煮20分钟，撒上葱花即完成。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_14061256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14061256" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061256-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14061256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id14061256-7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14061256" class="wp-caption-text">加入洋葱、红萝卜和西芹，再放入剩余调味料，多煮20分钟，撒上葱花即完成。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
