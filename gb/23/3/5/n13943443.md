<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>共同贸易集团举办日本清酒品尝会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943447-769dbc5ed44bb2cb8d9f17c6f338c038-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>来宾品酒交流。（主办方提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月05日讯】（大纪元工商记者David Liu洛杉矶报导）2月27日（周一），共同贸易集团（Mutual Trading Co., Inc.）于艾尔蒙地市（El Monte）又举办了一场日本清酒品鉴会。本次展会以B to B的形式，只针对各商家的老板或经理开放，当日邀请了数十位来自洛杉矶各大餐馆以及零售商的老板或经理来到现场，品味各式日本清酒的醇香和美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943448" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943448-37acdc82f7285ec87d0343afebafc28e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943448-37acdc82f7285ec87d0343afebafc28e-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943448" class="wp-caption-text">来宾排队品酒。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13943452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943452-279998b769d0ee762088a8e648f6b420.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943452-279998b769d0ee762088a8e648f6b420-600x358.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943452" class="wp-caption-text">来宾排队品酒。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日本清酒因其卓越的质量，在美国很受欢迎。展听一侧的墙面橱窗上，摆放着数百瓶花色各异、包装精美、档次不同的清酒。大厅四周设有不同的展位，每个展位上都有共同贸易集团的清酒持证顾问为来宾“斟酒”，并介绍不同清酒的特色。当天展出的种类中有两款高档清酒。一款叫做“飞龙乘云” 纯米大吟酿；另一款叫做“日本之樱“ 龙力纯米大吟酿。酒瓶打开后，展厅内清香四溢，吸引着来宾排队逐一品味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13947558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13947558" style="width: 550px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13947558-image.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13947558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13947558-image.png" alt="" width="550" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13947558" class="wp-caption-text">每个展位上都有共同贸易集团的清酒持证顾问为来宾“斟酒”，并介绍不同清酒的特色。（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13943455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943455" style="width: 573px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943455-ef46864a2bd1f397a55faac91d9eff05.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13943455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943455-ef46864a2bd1f397a55faac91d9eff05.png" alt="" width="573" b="515" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943455" class="wp-caption-text">“飞龙乘云” 纯米大吟酿（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13943454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943454-9a41753e522d324c98792d4d51fad4cf.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943454-9a41753e522d324c98792d4d51fad4cf-600x394.png" alt="" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943454" class="wp-caption-text">“日本之樱”龙力纯米大吟酿（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Mutual Trading Company Group 共同贸易集团成立于1926 年，传承日本料理传统的同时，致力于提供创新产品和服务，为零售和餐饮服务客户提供顶级日本食品、酒精饮料和餐厅用品的进口、出口、制造和分销业务，所代理的产品种类超过8000 种以上。</p>
<p>共同贸易集团（Mutual Trading Co. Inc.）</p>
