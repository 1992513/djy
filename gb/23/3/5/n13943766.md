<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>至孝笃亲公所春宴 近700人欢庆兔年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943759-DSC04832-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽英崙至孝笃亲公所3月4日举办盛大春宴，职员们敬酒。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年03月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）3月4日晚，波士顿华埠迎来了今年规模最大的春宴。纽英崙至孝笃亲公所宗亲及嘉宾总计近700人齐聚帝苑酒楼，庆祝兔年的到来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943758" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943758-DSC04827.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943758-DSC04827-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943758" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙至孝笃亲公所兔年春宴，迎来近700人参与。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>近</strong><strong>700</strong><strong>来宾庆祝兔年</strong></h3>
<p>
“由于疫情，我们自从2020年以来一直没有办中国新年宴会。2023年兔年带来了胜利。今年至孝笃亲公所迎来100周年，我们也在策划庆典。”至孝笃亲公所主席陈文珊说。</p>
<p>她还说，至孝笃亲公所将一如既往地支持移民和繁荣社区，让华埠成为“对每个人来说都更好的地方”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943757-DSC04816.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943757-DSC04816-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943757" class="wp-caption-text">至孝笃亲公所接受波士顿市议会的贺状。左起：北美舜裔笃亲总公所总理陈建立、至孝笃亲公所主席陈文珊、市议员Ruthzee Louijeune、Ed Flynn、Erin Murphy、至孝笃亲公所主席陈铁坚。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13943750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943750" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943750-DSC04766.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943750-DSC04766-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943750" class="wp-caption-text">3月4日晚在帝苑酒楼，余宝爱领唱美国国歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚，帝苑酒楼的主宴会厅内坐满了人群。由于人数太多，有约10桌来宾移到主厅旁边的小厅内参加宴会。</p>
<p>晚上7点刚过，晚会主持人黄海兰宣布全体起立，由余宝爱领唱美国国歌。她的高亢歌声赢得热烈的掌声。随后，陈耀祥武馆的3头彩狮在锣鼓声中跳跃进场，为嘉宾和宗亲们送上新年祝福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943751" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943751-DSC04782.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943751-DSC04782-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943751" class="wp-caption-text">陈耀祥武馆舞狮贺岁，庆祝纽英崙至孝笃亲公所兔年春宴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>政要嘉宾齐贺至孝笃亲公所</strong></h3>
“感谢你们在波士顿担当如此重要的核心部分，我很自豪能代表这个社区，也很高兴再次在现场看到大家。”波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943754-DSC04799.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943754-DSC04799-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943754" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她还感谢了在场的各位公职人员，特别与来宾们一起为波士顿警察鼓掌，感谢他们致力于维护治安的工作。在致词的最后，吴弭还提到，晚会现场“最重要的一人”是年过九旬的“Uncle Frank”陈毓礼，感谢他为社区做出的贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943749" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943749-DSC04759.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943749-DSC04759-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943749" class="wp-caption-text">参与春宴的嘉宾们与年过九旬的“Uncle Frank”陈毓礼合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13943752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943752" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943752-DSC04789.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943752-DSC04789-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943752" class="wp-caption-text">在3月4日晚的至孝笃亲公所春宴中，波士顿市长吴弭及各位嘉宾为舞狮鼓掌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天到场的嘉宾还包括波士顿市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）及议员墨菲（Erin Murphy）和路易琼（Ruthzee Louijeune），麻州众议员麦家威（Aaron Michlewitz）和参议员柯林斯（Nick Collins），麻州老人事务局局长陈倩，波士顿警察局社区关系总监陈孔恩和新任A-1分区警长马丁（Shawn Martin），驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长孙俭元和侨教中心主任潘昭荣，中华公所主席雷国辉等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943755-DSC04804.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943755-DSC04804-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943755" class="wp-caption-text">至孝笃亲公所接受麻州州长的贺状。左起：北美舜裔笃亲总公所总理陈建立、至孝笃亲公所主席陈文珊、麻州老人事务局局长陈倩、至孝笃亲公所主席陈铁坚。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中，3位市议员向至孝笃亲公所赠予了市议会贺状，陈倩也带来了麻州州长希莉（Maura Healey）的贺状，由北美舜裔笃亲总公所总理陈建立、纽英崙至孝笃亲公所主席陈文珊和陈铁坚接受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943756" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943756-DSC04810.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943756-DSC04810-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943756" class="wp-caption-text">至孝笃亲公所接受麻州议会的贺状。左起：至孝笃亲公所主席陈文珊、北美舜裔笃亲总公所总理陈建立、麻州参议员Nick Collins、至孝笃亲公所主席陈铁坚。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“经过几年的疫情，今天筵开70席，这样的盛况，是我们华埠第一次这么高兴。”中华公所主席雷国辉说。</p>
<p>他还说，从现场的盛况可以看出，至孝笃亲公所在政府和主流社会有着广泛的人脉，也与中华公所等侨团有着紧密的联系。为此，雷国辉感谢这个代表陈、胡、袁宗亲的公所为社区带来的贡献。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13943753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13943753" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943753-DSC04796.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13943753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13943753-DSC04796-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13943753" class="wp-caption-text">至孝笃亲公所3月4日晚举办兔年春宴，嘉宾们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在晚宴期间，至孝笃亲公所一如往年地向公益团体捐款。今年获捐的团体是中华总会、中华颐养院、中华耆英会、广教中文学校和波士顿亚裔青年服务组织（Boston Asian Youth Essential Service），它们分别得到了300美元的捐款。</p>
<p>纽英崙至孝笃亲公所的职员，以及来自北美总公所和外地公所的宗亲们一起上台敬酒，祝贺全体来宾新年身体健康、心想事成。◇</p>
