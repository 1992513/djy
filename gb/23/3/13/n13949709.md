<p>【大纪元2023年03月14日讯】<span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">多伦多台商会</a>（</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Taiwan Entrepreneurs Society Taipei/Toronto</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">，</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">TESTT</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">）将于</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">3</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">月</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">25</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">日（周六）在万锦市希尔顿酒店宴会厅</span><span style="font-size: medium;">举行“</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">TESTT 2023 Concord Adex</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86%E4%B9%8B%E5%A4%9C.md#1">台商之夜</a>”联欢餐舞会，以庆祝台商会成立</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">30</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">周年。 </span></span></p>

<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;">徐弘益还表示，将过去</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">30</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">年，用每</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">10</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">年来跟大家回顾，希望所有参与的贵宾能够享受多伦多台商会带给你愉快及难忘的夜晚。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;">多伦多台商会立足商界，不忘关注社区、回馈社区。台商会慈善基金会过去</span><span style="font-family: Calibri, serif;"><span lang="en-CA"><span style="font-family: 新细明体,
