<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>K-town churroland登陆纽约 韩式吉拿棒、拉丝热狗热卖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953887-e75eb26b561c13dc18634a611df8c44c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>各式口味的热狗。（K-town churroland提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元纽约讯】西班牙油条，又称作吉拿棒（Churros），源自西班牙的街边美食，但越来越风靡世界，现在来自韩国的人气餐车Jjunine_Churros的吉拿棒产品专卖店——K-town churroland Churros刚刚登陆纽约，就位于人气韩国一条街，来这里可以品尝这种独特诱人的韩式吉拿棒、拉丝热狗点心。</p>
<h2>韩式吉拿棒 香脆Q弹</h2>
<p>吉拿棒（Churros）在纽约并不罕见，但这款韩式吉拿棒风味与众不同。据K-town churroland老板金东垠（Dongun Kim）介绍，这是全美第一家加盟店，并将在贝赛开设第二家店，产品制作过程、原材料均遵循韩国的配方，希望为纽约人带来正宗的韩国风味小吃。</p>
<p>K-town churroland的吉拿棒采用韩国特制面粉制成，生面团通过一台机器的花边挤筒挤出来，成为带有花纹的韩式油条生胚，然后再放进油锅里炸。炸好的吉拿棒洒上各种美味调料，就成为一款诱人的点心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13953908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13953908" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953908-19c07c77ccee591b7044f7e31e5a080b.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13953908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953908-19c07c77ccee591b7044f7e31e5a080b-600x561.png" alt="" width="400" b="374" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13953908" class="wp-caption-text">K-town churroland的吉拿棒采用韩国特制面粉制成。（店家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经典的基本款吉拿棒分为条状（Stick Chu）和圈形（Ring Chu）两种，但是随着店家的用心制作，现在已经开发出圆环形的油条““吉纳圈”，炸好后沾上不同的调料，一条条如彩虹般多彩，呈现丰富的口味。除了原味，还有巧克力杏仁、巧克力奥利奥、巧克力火花、奶油起司、爽脆薄片、肉桂奶油等多种口味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13953911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13953911" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953911-5e54b146580a0a8579784189460fd4f3.png"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13953911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953911-5e54b146580a0a8579784189460fd4f3-600x601.png" alt="" width="399" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13953911" class="wp-caption-text">圈形（Ring Chu）吉拿圈。（店家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>吉拿圈+冰淇淋 甜美沁人心脾</h2>
<p>刚出锅的热呼呼的现榨“吉拿圈”，配上软软的冰淇淋相当合拍，这道点心也是K-town churroland的招牌之一。一款基本搭配是原味的Ice-chu，也就是一杯原味冰淇淋，搭配一条吉拿圈和一条原味吉拿棒，脆口中透着冰凉，甜人心脾，尝上一口，年轻浮躁的心立刻得到疗愈。</p>
<p>喜爱巧克力的人则可以尝试另一款经典搭配——Chocho ice-chu，点缀巧克力的冰淇淋，杯中配1个吉拿圈、1个吉拿棒，加一小块奥利奥圆饼，看起来令人垂涎，喜爱巧克力的人不可错过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13953915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13953915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953915-fa616ba446243f8fca81ce3780af49d9.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13953915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953915-fa616ba446243f8fca81ce3780af49d9-600x422.png" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13953915" class="wp-caption-text">原味吉拿棒+冰淇淋（Ice-chu）。（店家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13953916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13953916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953916-a78725b5a04089a9977dd6e0bc739803.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13953916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953916-a78725b5a04089a9977dd6e0bc739803-600x436.png" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13953916" class="wp-caption-text">Chocho ice-chu，巧克力的冰淇淋+1个吉拿圈、吉拿棒、奥利奥圆饼。（店家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>迷人的韩式热狗、奶茶</h2>
<p>K-town churroland的各种韩式玉米热狗具有正宗的甜咸口味，以及乳酪带来的拉丝口感，是最具吸引力的招牌。这里除了最热卖的吉拿圈热狗、土豆热狗、芝士乳酪热狗，还有原味玉米热狗、甜红薯热狗、拉面芝士热狗、水果彩虹热狗等诸多种类。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13953893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13953893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953893-2.-potato.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13953893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953893-2.-potato-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13953893" class="wp-caption-text">受欢迎的土豆热狗。（K-town churroland提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一家拥有年轻人喜爱的快餐食物餐厅，怎少得了最受欢迎的奶茶？这里提供经典的黑糖奶茶，还有蓝莓、芒果、柠檬、芋头以及混合奶茶等10个以上的口味，迷人的清香奶茶味加上喜爱的水果香，搭配热腾腾的热狗或香脆的吉拿棒，好吃到令人欲罢不能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13953888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13953888" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953888-IMG_5116.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-13953888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953888-IMG_5116-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13953888" class="wp-caption-text">K-town churroland 刚刚登陆纽约，图为人气热狗和吉拿圈、奶茶。（K-town churroland 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>So Yun B.品尝了K-town churroland的甜红薯热狗（Sweet Potato mozzarella cheese corn dog）等点心后，在点评网留言道：“外面没有加糖，但是你可以很享受地瓜的甜味。还有churros，外脆里软，太不可思议了，美味又合算，你一定要到这家店品尝！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13953886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13953886" style="width: 348px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953886-348s.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-13953886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id13953886-348s.jpg" alt="" width="348" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13953886" class="wp-caption-text">位于法拉盛的纽约首家K-town churroland加盟店。（K-town churroland提供）</figcaption></figure>
