<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>统计局：2071年澳海外移民或高达1410万</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13780441-GettyImages-1201969636-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为2020年1月26日澳洲国庆日，参加澳洲入籍仪式的移民。（Rohan Thomson/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月23日讯】<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">（大纪元记者夏楚君澳洲悉尼编译报导）根据澳洲统计局（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">ABS</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）的最新数据，到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2071</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年，澳洲的人口将几乎翻一番，其中，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%A4%96%E7%A7%BB%E6%B0%91.md#1">海外移民</a>人口或高达1400多万。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">澳洲统计局称，到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2071</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年，澳洲的人口将增加到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">3430</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">4560</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万，具体数字将取决于生育率、死亡率和净移民水平等因素。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">虽然人口预计会增长，但国家规模的人口自然增长——即在澳洲出生的人数减去死亡人数，在未来二十年内实际上可能会出现负增长。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">据<span lang="en-US">9</span>号新闻网报导，根据统计局的一系列预测，到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2071</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E4%BA%BA%E5%8F%A3.md#1">澳洲人口</a>将每年自然减少约</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">118,000</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">人。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">统计局还预计，到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2071</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年，将有</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">920</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1410</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%A4%96%E7%A7%BB%E6%B0%91.md#1">海外移民</a>移居澳洲。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">统计局假设 ，如果净移民人数为零，那么澳洲的人口将从</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2037</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年开始下降，到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2071</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年降至不足</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2400</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万，不过，这纯粹是一种假设，根本不可能发生。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">统计局肯定会发生的人口变化是人口<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E9%BE%84%E5%8C%96.md#1">老龄化</a>，中位年龄将从去年的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">38.5</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">岁上升到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2071</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">43.8</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">岁至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">47.6</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">岁。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">统计局表示：“在预计<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E4%BA%BA%E5%8F%A3.md#1">澳洲人口</a>将发生的变化中，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E9%BE%84%E5%8C%96.md#1">老龄化</a>被普遍认为是最显着的变化，人口的年龄结构将发生重大变化。”</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;">“<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">近几十年来，由于生育率低于死亡率，人口老龄化是一个明显的趋势。”统计局说。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;">“<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">在所有（预测）的趋势中，这一趋势将持续下去。</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="hi-IN">”</span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">数据显示，墨尔本人口将在</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2032</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2046</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年期间超过悉尼，成为全国最大的城市，人口在</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">650</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">990</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万之间。不过，新州仍将是人口最多的州，居民人数在</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1080</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1380</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万之间。</span></span></span></p>
