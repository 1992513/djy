<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>摄影师心动泰安波斯菊 和中社火红扫帚草</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112143-646107-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>斗南重光国小带学生爬火炎山。（杨松田提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月08日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾苗栗报导）在秋天繁花盛开的季节，台湾中部呈现出最美丽的一面，吸引了无数摄影师，前来欣赏并记录这些美丽的景色。今年11月初，苗栗泰安休耕后的波斯菊花田，和台中后里区中社花园的火红扫帚草，成为花季的重点，空拍机俯看下的景色，景色壮观又漂亮。</p>
<figure id="14112140" aria-describedby="caption-14112140" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112140-646104-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112140-646104-450x338.jpg" alt="苗栗泰安秘境拍到的美景。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14112140" class="wp-caption-text">苗栗泰安秘境拍到的美景。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14112138" aria-describedby="caption-14112138" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112138-646102-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112138-646102-450x338.jpg" alt="与远方的火炎山相呼应。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14112138" class="wp-caption-text">与远方的火炎山相呼应。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>泰安的波斯菊花田在休耕后，绽放出绚丽多彩的花朵，与远方的乐园、火炎山相得益彰。这一壮观的场面吸引了摄影师，他们纷纷前来捕捉这难得的自然奇观。其中，空拍达人Nina Chen在摄影达人的引领下，首先从泰安秘境的角度欣赏火炎山。在日落前的柔和光线下，她创作了让自己沉醉的作品，让拍了一整天的她，直呼值得。</p>
<figure id="14112142" aria-describedby="caption-14112142" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112142-646106-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112142-646106-450x675.jpg" alt="摄影专家Wish Lin也趁着晴朗的天气，捕捉了绝美的照片。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14112142" class="wp-caption-text">摄影专家Wish Lin也趁着晴朗的天气，捕捉了绝美的照片。（Wish Lin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14112141" aria-describedby="caption-14112141" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112141-646105-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112141-646105-450x338.jpg" alt="泰安的波斯菊花田在休耕后，绽放出绚丽多彩的花朵。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14112141" class="wp-caption-text">泰安的波斯菊花田在休耕后，绽放出绚丽多彩的花朵。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秋天的台湾，是赏花季美景的最佳时光，不容错过。无论是波斯菊的花海，还是火红扫帚草的盛放，感受台湾的独特的魅力。</p>
<figure id="14112139" aria-describedby="caption-14112139" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112139-646103-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14112139-646103-450x253.jpg" alt="后里区中社花园。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14112139" class="wp-caption-text">后里区中社花园。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
