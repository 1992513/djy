<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>王功渔港潮间带生态 摸蛤兼洗裤超好玩！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120458-648496-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>王功专属的交通工具：海牛车。（林茂男提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月20日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾彰化报导）彰化县的王功渔港不仅拥有壮丽的芳苑灯塔和日落美景，更因其未受污染的潮间带而丰富多彩的生态资源。在退潮时，螃蟹和寄居蟹就在游客的脚边爬行，为亲子游提供了独特的体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120459" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120459-648497-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120459-648497-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120459" class="wp-caption-text">享受挖蛤蜊的乐趣。（林茂男提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了让游客更深入地体验这片美丽的自然环境，王功渔港发展出了一种新型观光模式——采蚵铁牛车、海牛车。游客可以在旅客服务中心前选择不同的业者，价位和项目差不多。费用包括铁牛车、海牛车的来回接驳、私人地盘挖蛤以及珍珠蚵的现烤现吃。</p>
<p>一辆辆铁牛车、海牛车穿梭在潮间带，仿佛在摩西分海一般，营造出一种独特的景观。每位业者都拥有自己的地盘，并用竹篱圈出区域，挖蛤蜊只能在限定的区域内进行，因此游客需注意不要挖过界。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120464-648510-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120464-648510-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120464" class="wp-caption-text">可爱的海牛车，成为大家拍照的模特儿。（林茂男提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14120460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120460-648498-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120460-648498-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120460" class="wp-caption-text">一辆辆铁牛车穿梭在潮间带，仿佛在摩西分海一般，营造出一种独特的景观。（林茂男提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14120462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120462" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120462-648501-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120462-648501-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120462" class="wp-caption-text">王功渔港潮间带生态，摸蛤兼洗裤超好玩！（陈炳臣提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在挖蛤蜊之前，游客应注意以下事项：</p>
<p>简化身上行李，将贵重物品随身携带。<br />
穿着拖鞋或凉鞋，因为地面有壳。<br />
上厕所不易，建议在出发前如厕。<br />
带上太阳眼镜和帽子，以保护自己免受阳光和海风的影响。<br />
若有幼儿一同前往，请穿游泳尿布，因为一般尿布吸水后会变得特别沉重。<br />
携带一套干净的换洗衣物，以应对意外情况。</p>
<p>业者都会传授挖蛤的技巧，并指出凹凸不平的地方表示已被开发过，无需浪费时间和力气。而王功蚵农们也会以友善的方式提供生态导览，让游客更深入了解这片环境的生态多样性。王功渔港的野生蛤蜊位于约5公分的深度，使用工具挖掘几次后，当水变得清澈时，蛤蜊就会浮现。</p>
<p>最后，不容错过的是这里无敌的夕阳浪漫景色，可以向当地居民寻求建议，前往潮间带拍照，保证会有意想不到的美景等着您。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120461" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120461-648499-450x260.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120461-648499-450x260.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120461" class="wp-caption-text">在退潮时，螃蟹和寄居蟹就在游客的脚边爬行，为亲子游提供了独特的体验。（林茂男提供）</figcaption></figure>
