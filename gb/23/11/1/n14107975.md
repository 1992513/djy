<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>甘斯维尔台商会金秋烤肉 众人大快朵颐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107977-DSC_1470-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>主办方与众位贵宾合照。（吕尚明/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年11月02日讯】（大纪元记者吕尚明亚特兰大报导）北乔州的秋天是令人身心舒畅的季节，甘斯维尔台湾商会借着这金秋之际于卡明（Cumming）的Fowler Park举办了烤肉野餐活动。餐会由会长孔繁华及各理事与志工筹备，并得益于多人慷慨赞助，不仅有丰盛的美食，还有多采多姿的趣味活动。当天活动影片请看此链接:? <ahref="https://ganjing.one/fxenN4">https://ganjing.one/fxenN4</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_14107979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107979" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107979-DSC_1419-e1698878047106.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107979-DSC_1419-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107979" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）创会会长张崧轩，经文处陈新发组长，与商会现会长孔繁华一展歌喉。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14107980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107980" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107980-DSC_1404-e1698878149795.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107980-DSC_1404-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107980" class="wp-caption-text">商会前会长谷企平（右）与友人全家合照。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>餐会由有侨界“金牌主持人”之称的孙德华以生动活泼的方式开场主持。在会长孔繁华欢迎大家之后，接着侨教中心的欧宏伟主任、经文处的陈新发组长也上前致词。此外甘斯维尔商会创会会长张崧轩、前世界台湾商会联合总会总会长王德博士，还有侨务咨询委员吕志全与王祥瑞都上前感谢大家参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14107981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107981" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107981-DSC_1441-e1698878262561.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107981-DSC_1441-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107981" class="wp-caption-text">（左起，前排）张崧轩，陈新发（后排）吕志全，欧宏伟，王祥瑞，王德。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次餐会的菜色多得令人眼花撩乱，不仅有烤鸡翅、烤台湾香肠、大虾、牛肉、烧猪肉等，还有炒米粉、卤肉饭、炒茭白笋、卤蛋、卤海带等等，大家都吃得赞不绝口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14107983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107983-DSC_1412-e1698878741815.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107983-DSC_1412-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107983" class="wp-caption-text">烤肉香气四溢。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14107990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107990" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107990-DSC_1474-e1698878852459.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107990-DSC_1474-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107990" class="wp-caption-text">佳肴满桌，色香味俱全。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>依照过去的惯例，商会财务长秦文皓（James）主持了精彩的趣味问答活动，准备了许多与乔州经济息息相关的问题，不仅猜对者都获得现金红包，大家也增长了许多知识。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14107992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107992" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107992-DSC_1502-e1698878945867.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107992-DSC_1502-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107992" class="wp-caption-text">秦文皓主持趣味问答。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14107993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107993-DSC_1540-e1698879089132.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107993-DSC_1540-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107993" class="wp-caption-text">众人欢聚一堂。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了摸彩活动，这次主办方还在摸彩中穿插了Bingo游戏，得胜者也有现金犒赏。在最后的压轴大奖摸彩中幸运者得了张崧轩赞助的头奖，一套价值二千元的沙发后，众人还感到意犹未尽想继续玩Bingo游戏。于是赞助者慷慨地再掏腰包赞助奖金，让大家玩个尽兴。这次餐会可说是宾主尽欢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14107994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107994-DSC_1571-e1698879192483.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107994-DSC_1571-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107994" class="wp-caption-text">吕志全发奖金给Bingo游戏获胜的小朋友。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14107995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107995-DSC_1627-e1698879295560.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14107995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107995-DSC_1627-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107995" class="wp-caption-text">Eddie（右）中了张崧轩赞助的大奖，沙发一套。（吕尚明/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
