<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】山羊起司烘蛋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107763-a3275aa37f0089ee5fbedb70bad39156-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>山羊起司烘蛋柔软多汁，不蘸酱也美味。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年11月01日讯】想品尝羊奶起司，但又担心羊奶味太重，今天的起司烘蛋是很好的尝试。</p>
<p>这道料理用了蜂蜜山羊起司，羊奶味比较淡。甜味起司与甜椒、鸡蛋、菠菜平衡得很好。搭配双层蛋饼，表面焦香，中间厚厚的蛋乳有如熔岩蛋糕，温暖、绵滑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14107764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107764" style="width: 594px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107764-b36c0bf10cc37a42e142397eae5be6b4-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14107764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107764-b36c0bf10cc37a42e142397eae5be6b4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="594" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107764" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4人份的食材</p>
<p><strong>【材料】</strong></p>
<p>6个大鸡蛋<br />
1/2茶匙盐<br />
1小撮现磨黑胡椒<br />
100克沙拉菠菜，洗净沥干水<br />
1/2杯红色甜椒，切小细丁<br />
1包蜂蜜山羊起司，113克/4OZ</p>
<p>9英寸铸铁锅和锅盖<br />
10英寸的盘子</p>
<p><strong>【作法】</strong></p>
<p>1.在大碗中放入所有鸡蛋、盐和黑胡椒，打散。</p>
<p>2.开火烧热铁锅，放入油翻炒甜椒至变色。</p>
<p>3.添加菠菜翻炒1分钟至变软，关火；将之倒入蛋液，让蛋液没过食材，静置8分钟。</p>
<p>4.时间到了烧热铁锅，加入油，倒入一半蛋液（蛋液中的食材先不下锅）。</p>
<p>5.晃动铁锅，让蛋液均匀分布，煎成蛋饼。无流动蛋液时，用盘子盖住锅，翻转锅，将煎好的蛋饼倒扣在盘中。</p>
<p>6.锅中加油，倒入余下的蛋液和食材。将事先分成若干小块的起司铺在蛋饼上。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14107765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107765" style="width: 593px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107765-2251edd35b0813c0493be4c12fa8fd4e-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14107765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107765-2251edd35b0813c0493be4c12fa8fd4e-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="593" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107765" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.晃动锅，并用叉子稍微搅动蛋液，使蛋液均匀分布凝固成蛋饼。</p>
<p>8.待蛋液七成凝固，把之前煎好的蛋饼从盘子里慢慢推入锅中，像锅盖一样盖住锅中蛋饼。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14107766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107766" style="width: 588px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107766-29b91b3b2154edc5d5fcfebc163b589d-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14107766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107766-29b91b3b2154edc5d5fcfebc163b589d-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="588" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107766" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.盖上锅盖，用铁锅余温焖20分钟。</p>
<p>10.时间到了，此时的蛋饼仍然温热。切开蛋饼，中间蛋液熟了即可享用。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_14107769" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14107769" style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107769-a6014d72a8b70ef921f890c48efa0a4e-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14107769" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14107769-a6014d72a8b70ef921f890c48efa0a4e-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="595" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14107769" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
