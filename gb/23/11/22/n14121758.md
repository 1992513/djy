<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第20届苗县议会 陈永贤 萧咏萱 张志宇议员质询</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121762-648894-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>期望文观局对苗栗特色能再精致明亮，如图片。（许享富／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月22日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）县议员陈永贤、萧咏萱、 张志宇议员提出质询，头份国中校舍与交通、图书馆问题、头份交流道改善计划；苗县观光推广、婚后受孕检测福利与头份尖下里排水不良等问题；县内污水道淤塞与市内下水道堵塞解决问题、流浪猫狗处理、电动车火灾消防处治、公馆国小中前行穿线规划与农业化制设置问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14121759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121759" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121759-648890.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14121759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121759-648890-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121759" class="wp-caption-text">县长钟东锦表示，苗县是北部最大农余、禽畜排放，务必自处理，日本取经，专家严格审查，全县监督下以企业永续经营，仿肉品市场管理模式。先解决稻草、鸡肥的问题，再逐步增加项目，卖碳权，增加县府收入，解决财政困窘。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14121760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121760" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121760-648891.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14121760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121760-648891.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121760" class="wp-caption-text">议员陈永贤质询，有关头份国中校舍、交通与图书馆问题、头份交流道改善计划等问题。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14121761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121761" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121761-648892.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14121761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121761-648892-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121761" class="wp-caption-text">议员萧咏萱提出，苗县观光推广、婚后受孕检测福利与头份尖下里排水不良等问题质询。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14121764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121764" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121764-648896.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14121764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121764-648896-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121764" class="wp-caption-text">张志宇议员提出：县内污水道淤塞与市内下水道堵塞解决问题、流浪猫狗处理、电动车火灾消防处治、公馆国小中前行穿线规划与农业化制设置问题。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长钟东锦表示，议员所提的头份国中、头份交流道、公馆国小改善等问题，县府团队会统合民意解决。至于畜牧循环中心计划的化制厂，取经日本与技术，参考专家的意见，同时采肉品市场官股占大股的模式来经营、监督。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14121765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121765-648898.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14121765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121765-648898-600x405.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="405"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121765" class="wp-caption-text">微型化设备引进，以期冲击最小。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14121763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14121763" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121763-648895.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14121763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14121763-648895-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14121763" class="wp-caption-text">婚后受孕福利。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苗县是北部县市最大的农畜排放最大量，为了长久之计，必须有自己经营的处理场，内容可以变动，先处理稻草、鸡肥问题，再来农余处理、禽猪问题，虽然发电量不大，但可以卖碳权，为县府带来一笔额外收入，以企业永续经营来为县府谋最大福利为宗旨。◇</p>
