<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大雪山国家森林游乐区 枫红迎接秋冬美景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125166-649892-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>11月下旬游客纷纷涌至大雪山游乐区，欣赏橙、红、黄等多种色彩的枫树交织成的壮丽景观。（李政雄提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月27日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾台中报导）大雪山国家森林游乐区，位于台中市和平区，随着秋天的到来，枫叶悄然转红，开启了令人陶醉的秋冬美景。11月下旬游客纷纷涌至该区，欣赏橙、红、黄等多种色彩的枫树交织成的壮丽景观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125169-649895-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14125169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125169-649895-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125169" class="wp-caption-text">目前位于大雪山林道49公里处的小雪山区域，已经迎来第一波枫叶转红的景象。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大雪山国家森林游乐区地处海拔2,000至2,600公尺之间，气温平均约在12-20度，使其成为赏枫的理想去处。区内分布着多种枫树品种，如台湾红榨槭、青枫、枫香、尖叶槭等，随着季节的变化，这些枫树的叶子逐渐转为橙色、红色和黄色，呈现出多彩多姿的秋季风情。</p>
<p>目前位于大雪山林道49公里处的小雪山区域，已经迎来第一波枫叶转红的景象，特别是雪山神木步道上，游客可以欣赏到红黄相间的枫叶，与挺拔的雪山神木相映成趣，成为摄影爱好者的最爱。此外，在大雪山林道35公里处的收费站区域以及43公里处的游客中心周边，枫叶也开始逐渐转红，为即将到来的枫红季节预热，为游客们带来了期待已久的美丽枫景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125168" style="width: 336px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125168-649894-600x1066.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14125168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125168-649894-600x1066.jpg" alt="" width="336" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125168" class="wp-caption-text">大雪山游乐区神木。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14125165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125165-649891-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14125165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125165-649891-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125165" class="wp-caption-text">大雪山国家森林游乐区 ，枫红现迎接秋冬美景。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云林二崙社区志工们相偕一起前往踏青，欣赏白云和枫红一起跳舞，领队李政雄表示，虽然早晚天气较冷，但白天天气非常好，蓝天白云和青山相映，让赏枫心情更加美丽轻松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14125167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125167-649893-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14125167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125167-649893-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125167" class="wp-caption-text">云林二崙社区志工前往大雪山游乐区踏青。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14125170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14125170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125170-649896-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14125170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14125170-649896-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14125170" class="wp-caption-text">大雪山国家森林游乐区地处海拔2,000至2,600公尺之间，气温平均约在12-20度，使其成为赏枫的理想去处。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
