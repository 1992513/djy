<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>米饭薄片舞台！5分钟做出美味料理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114296-207c212ebcfb8034b35848eac4f64d63-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>肉卷饭团。（幸福文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>对于那些喜欢将食物冷冻保存的人，我一定要介绍这个秘诀。</p>
<p>总是下意识把饭搓成球状冷冻起来的人，请试试看将饭塑成厚1公分，像智慧型手机尺寸那样的四方形薄片吧！</p>
<p>只要这个动作就可以将饭堆叠在冷冻库里方便收纳，不占空间，而且使用时也可以立刻均匀解冻，依此形状简单变化出各色各样不同的料理。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">快速：5分 (煮饭的时间不计)</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>四角饭团</strong></span></h3>
<p>用微波炉解冻后只要放上配料再用海苔卷起来就完成了。如果垫着保鲜膜制作，连洗的工夫都省了。</p>
<p><strong>米饭薄片的多样舞台</strong></p>
<p><strong>虽然用法很像冷冻米饭，但实际使用过就会了解它有多方便。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14114291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114291" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114291-090f2b65a5387b1bdb5f20684f9b393f.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114291-090f2b65a5387b1bdb5f20684f9b393f-450x609.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="609" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114291" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>肉卷饭团</strong></span></h3>
<p>用四片猪里肌薄片将冷冻的米饭包卷起来。在平底锅里倒入芝麻油加热后放入，正反面各煎两分钟。之后将4 大匙的水、1 大匙的砂糖、4 小匙的酱油混合均匀，倒入锅中煮四分钟左右，待汤汁收干后即可完成。可依各人喜好添加黄芥末。<strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14114296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114296" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114296-207c212ebcfb8034b35848eac4f64d63.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114296-207c212ebcfb8034b35848eac4f64d63-450x357.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114296" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>鸡蛋盖饭</strong></span></h3>
<p>在冷冻的米饭上放个鸡蛋，淋上鲣鱼酱油，再摆上撕碎的海苔，然后用微波炉当一下。记得要把蛋黄戳上三个左右的洞以避免炸开。不用包保鲜膜，微波两分到两分半钟即可。<strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_14114300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114300" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114300-01c13a5d333ea0656c54f45bc847b6df.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114300-01c13a5d333ea0656c54f45bc847b6df-450x349.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114300" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>焗烤饭 </strong></span></h3>
<p>将奶油放入平底锅里加热，放入冷冻的米饭正反面各加热两分钟，煎成恰到好处的锅巴。倒入1/2 杯的牛奶、放入两片培根以及大量的起司，之后盖上盖子边蒸边烘，<strong>直到起司溶化为止。不用烤箱也可以享受美味的焗烤饭。</strong><span style="color: #000000;">◇</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_14114298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114298" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114298-e94faa500da2758ba7b9b314bb72670e.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114298-e94faa500da2758ba7b9b314bb72670e-450x306.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114298" class="wp-caption-text">（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
