<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>彰化芬园猫罗溪 牛群成为热门景点</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116815-647470-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>彰化芬园猫罗溪,牛群成为热门景点。（nina Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月15日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾彰化报导）彰化县芬园乡与台中市乌日区交界处的猫罗溪畔，二百多头牛群逐水草、泡澡的景象而成为热门景点。每天早上9点左右，牛群由饲主放牧，下午4点左右自行返回牛棚，吸引了众多民众前来观赏拍照。</p>
<figure id="14116819" aria-describedby="caption-14116819" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116819-647474-450x263.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116819-647474-450x263.jpg" alt="这里的芒花盛开的季节，大约从9月至11月。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14116819" class="wp-caption-text">这里的芒花盛开的季节，大约从9月至11月。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14116817" aria-describedby="caption-14116817" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116817-647472-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116817-647472-450x338.jpg" alt="下午4点左右自行返回牛棚，吸引了众多民众前来观赏拍照。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14116817" class="wp-caption-text">下午4点左右自行返回牛棚，吸引了众多民众前来观赏拍照。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>猫罗溪牛群之所以爆红，是因为其景象宛如在大草原，牛群在秋芒花中觅食十分逗趣，并常常在溪中戏水，形成独特的自然生态景观，这里的芒花盛开的季节，大约从9月至11月。</p>
<figure id="14116816" aria-describedby="caption-14116816" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116816-647471-450x232.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116816-647471-450x232.jpg" alt="二百多头牛群逐水草、泡澡的景象而成为热门景点。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14116816" class="wp-caption-text">二百多头牛群逐水草、泡澡的景象而成为热门景点。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14116818" aria-describedby="caption-14116818" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116818-647473-450x252.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116818-647473-450x252.jpg" alt="牛群在秋芒花中觅食十分逗趣，并常常在溪中戏水。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14116818" class="wp-caption-text">牛群在秋芒花中觅食十分逗趣，并常常在溪中戏水。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而，观看猫罗溪牛群需要注意以下事项：首先，牛群是野生动物，请勿随意靠近或喂食，以免惊扰或引发攻击；其次，牛群可能会渡溪，请注意自身安全；同时，请勿随意丢弃垃圾，共同维护环境的美好。</p>
<figure id="14116821" aria-describedby="caption-14116821" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116821-647481-450x322.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14116821-647481-450x322.jpg" alt="每天早上9点左右，牛群放牧出来。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14116821" class="wp-caption-text">每天早上9点左右，牛群放牧出来。（nina Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
