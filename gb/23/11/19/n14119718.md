<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>清幽迷人的风情 六十石山的别样魅力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119725-648233-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夜景让人无法想像，游客直呼太美了。（黄荣群提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）11月的花莲六十石山，不仅在金针花季时美不胜收，即使在花期结束后，六十石山也有着独特的风情。其翠绿山坡绵延数十里，令人心胸开阔，成为赏景、观星的理想之地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119724-648232.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14119724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119724-648232-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119724" class="wp-caption-text">美不胜收。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119720" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119720-648227.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14119720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119720-648227-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119720" class="wp-caption-text">像一条游龙。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每年的8月至9月，花莲金针山都迎来金针花季，吸引着游客前来欣赏这片美丽的金针花海。然而，即使在金针花盛开期过后，金针山依然保留着其自然的魅力。这个山区地势崎岖，经过蜿蜒的山路，登上观赏亭，您可以俯瞰花东纵谷的壮丽景色，或者在夜晚仰望满天星空，都会给您留下难以忘怀的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119725" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119725-648233.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14119725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119725-648233-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119725" class="wp-caption-text">夜景让人无法想像，游客直呼太美了。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摄影家黄荣群表示，六十石山以其特有的美景而闻名，其中包括夜景、云隙光（耶稣光）等，总能带给游客惊喜和感动。</p>
<p>在游览六十石山时，不要错过品尝当地的金针风味餐，让您更深入地体验当地文化。而如果您想欣赏最佳的山景，那么忘忧亭绝对是俯瞰整片六十石山美景的最佳地点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119726" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119726-648234.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14119726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119726-648234-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119726" class="wp-caption-text">好像坐拥黄金城。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论您是赏花爱好者、星空观察家，还是追求大自然美景的旅人，花莲金针山都将带给您一场难忘的冒险之旅。请记住，每年的8月至9月是赏金针花的最佳时机，而在夕阳时分和云隙光（耶稣光）出现的下午3点至5点，六十石山更是一个不容错过的绝佳时刻。花莲金针山，清幽迷人的风情，等待着您的探索。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14119722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119722-648230.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14119722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119722-648230-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119722" class="wp-caption-text">8月进入金针山花季。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14119723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14119723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119723-648231.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14119723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14119723-648231-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14119723" class="wp-caption-text">六十石山以其特有的美景而闻名。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
