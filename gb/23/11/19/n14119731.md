<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>掌握铸铁锅烹调4原则 蒸煮煎烤道道美味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122641-e7bf502dc276971fdf24ac343d13de84-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>铸铁锅有各种烹调方法。（台湾广厦提供／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：铸铁锅耐用、功能多，对于新手来说也是很好用的锅具。了解铸铁锅的4 个基本烹调原则之后，你就可以用铸铁锅煎、炒、炸、炖煮、蒸、烤、盐焗还有做无水料理了。</p>
<h2>铸铁锅的基本烹调原则</h2>
<p><strong>1. 食材下锅前先回温、擦干</strong></p>
<p>冰食材下锅后锅内迅速降温，食材表面无法瞬间熟透、起梅纳反应，就会容易沾黏，香气、色泽也不足。</p>
<p><strong>2. 先热好锅再下油</strong></p>
<p>铸铁锅涂层有很多毛细孔，先加热让毛孔扩张，再加入冷油润锅，让油吃进毛孔，就能在表面形成不沾的光滑薄膜。</p>
<p><strong>3. 食材下锅后减少翻动</strong></p>
<p>很多新手常犯的错误，就是食材一下锅不停翻面，这样温度降很快，食材表面还黏在锅上又被翻起，很容易就沾锅。</p>
<p><strong>4. 避免造成剧烈的温差</strong></p>
<p>瞬间的冷热温差很容易让锅子坏掉。因此煮完切勿直接冲冷水洗。若需要加水，以冷锅加冷水，热锅加热水为主。</p>
<h2>铸铁锅的各种烹调方式</h2>
<figure id="attachment_14120408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120408-P.26-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14120408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120408-P.26-1.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120408" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>煎</strong></span></h2>
<p>?开中小火，热锅到把手摸起来温热的程度。<br />
?倒入冷油润锅（让锅底均匀有油），加热到油出现油纹，表示油温足够。<br />
?放入常温、擦干水分的食材（此时不要转小火，导致温度下降）。<br />
?不要翻动，煎到食材表面熟、不会黏锅后再翻面。<br />
＊如果食材油脂多，例如五花肉、带皮鸡腿，不用加油，直接入锅逼出油即可。</p>
<p><strong>BOX 煎出迷人的恰恰荷包蛋</strong></p>
<p>简单的荷包蛋，其实不简单！因为蛋的水分多，也不像其他肉类油脂高，很容易沾锅。使用常温蛋，确实热锅后加入略多一些的油润锅，是成功煎出恰恰荷包蛋的秘诀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120409-P.26-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14120409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120409-P.26-2.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="639" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120409" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;">炒</span></h2>
<p>热锅冷油，以中小火加热到油纹出现，再放入食材翻炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120410-P.26-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120410-P.26-3-450x587.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="587" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120410" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"> 炸</span></h2>
<p>冷锅冷油，以中小火加热到插入筷子会大量冒泡，再依序放入食材炸到酥脆。</p>
<p>＊油量大时冷锅冷油加热，不用担心油快速过热变质，或因倒入大量油而产生剧烈温差。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120411-P.26-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120411-P.26-4-450x593.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120411" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"> 炖煮</span></h2>
<p>热锅冷油，待油热后将食材炒香，再加入热水炖煮至需要的程度。<br />
＊若没有要先炒的食材，直接冷锅放入所有食材、冷水开始煮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120412" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120412-P.27-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120412-P.27-1-450x433.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120412" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"> 蒸</span></h2>
<p>将铸铁锅放入电锅，加水按下开关即可。</p>
<p>＊蒸的时候可以盖上锅盖，避免水汽滴入锅中。</p>
<p>＊也可以用蒸锅加水先煮沸，再放入铸铁锅蒸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120413" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120413-P.27-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120413-P.27-2-450x431.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120413" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"> 烤</span></h2>
<p>耐热的铸铁锅可以直接当烤盘或模具，搭配烤箱使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120414" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120414-P.27-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120414-P.27-3-450x427.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120414" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"> 盐焗</span></h2>
<p>锅底先铺一层盐，放入食材再盖一层盐，以瓦斯炉小火加热至熟即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14120415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14120415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120415-P.27-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14120415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14120415-P.27-4-450x433.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14120415" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"> 无水料理</span></h2>
<p>以水分多的食材堆叠，上盖后以中小火煮到食材本身的水分释放、入味。</p>
