<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Optus故障一日 其竞争对手新客户激增</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/10/id13842726-GettyImages-1430562199-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2023年11月8日，澳洲各地的Optus电信服务均出现大规模中断。Optus用户正在转向新电信服务商。(Brendon Thorne/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月09日讯】<span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #333333;">（大纪元记者夏楚君澳洲悉尼编译报导）</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Optus</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">的竞争对手报告称，在</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Optus</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">发生大规模服务中断仅一天后，它们的新客户激增。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">周三（</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;">11</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">月</span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">8</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">日）</span></span></span><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">Optus</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #222222;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-family: 'Liberation Serif', serif;">1000</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #222222;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">多万用户</span></span></span><span style="color: #222222;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">无法连接互联网，也无法拨打或接听电话。</span></span></span><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">他们不得不在业务中使用现金支付、借用电话，这导致许多人决定换一家新的电信公司。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #333333;">Optus</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">的竞争对手没有对事件发表评论，但却看到了新客户注册数量的增加。</span></span><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">其中，Vodafone</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">门店的注册用户数量急剧上升。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">该电信公司发言人告诉</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">9</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">号新闻网，“在过去的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">24</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">小时，我们所有品牌（包括</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Vodafone</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">、</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">TPG iiNet</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">、</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">felix</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">和</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Lebara</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）的交易活动都增加了四倍多，这成为最繁忙的销售日，而且增长势头不减。”</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">更换电信服务商实际上是一个非常简单的过程，将一个电话号码从现有电信公司转到新的电信公司通常只需</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">5</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">60</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">分钟。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Vodafone</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">表示：“我们的商店和在线支持团队随时准备帮助客户连接，并在从其他运营商转移号码时如出现轻微延迟会提供临时号码。”</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"> <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Boost Mobile</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">在</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Telstra</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">网络上运营，但与其网络合作伙伴的计划不同，该公司的注册用户也大增，周三的日均销售额是平时的五倍，网站流量是平时的两倍。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #333333;">Boost Mobile</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">总经理海恩斯（</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Jason Haynes</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">）对</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">9</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">号新闻网表示，他们欢迎所有新客户，并向他们承诺提供“最可靠的网络和一个永远为他们的业务努力工作的团队”。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #333333;">与此同时，最精通技术的人也意识到，有了现代智能手机，不用去商店就可以更换电信公司。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #333333;">许多电信公司通过网站或应用程序提供</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">eSIM </span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">注册服务，只需扫描二维码就能启用</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">eSIM</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">。</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Kogan Mobile</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">周三就看到了</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">eSIM</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">客户的大幅增长。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #333333;">Kogan代表</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">表示：“我们刚刚统计了数据，昨天（周三） </span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Kogan Mobile</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">的</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">eSim</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">销售额比平日高出 </span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">400%</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">。”</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;">“<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">这实际上是</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Kogan Mobile</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">本财年</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">eSim</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">销售量最高的一天。”</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #333333;">虽然</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Telstra</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">无法提供其新客户注册数据，但业内人士表示，根据</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Telstra</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">商店及其合作伙伴（如 </span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">JB Hi-Fi</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">）的排队情况来看，毫无疑问，它们在过去的一段时间里新客户数量也出现了大幅增长。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">用户决定更换服务商不仅仅是因为</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Optus周三发生</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的故障，包括</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Kogan</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">在内的几家电信公司都在积极推广新用户优惠，第一个月优惠</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">60%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，以吸引新用户。</span></span></span></p>
