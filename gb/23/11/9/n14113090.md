<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【一箪食】西洋菜蓝纹鸡汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113113-6d468bef865859aeb49ec90b8adda407-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西洋菜蓝纹鸡汤。（作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年11月09日讯】西洋菜清甜可口，适合煮汤。今天介绍一道10分钟上桌的美味西洋菜汤。汤底主料为法国绵羊奶半硬质蓝纹起司ROQUEFORT。起司经过至少90天熟成，乳香细致、浓郁。</p>
<p>6人份的食材准备：</p>
<p>750毫升无盐鸡汤<br />
100克西洋菜，摘掉硬茎部分<br />
100克蓝纹起司（ROQUEFORT）<br />
淡奶油（Light cream）或淡奶（Evaporated milk），调味用，可不添加</p>
<figure id="attachment_14113117" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14113117" style="width: 578px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113117-184956dc9e3cefff5a1dc13cb62f9cb5-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14113117" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113117-184956dc9e3cefff5a1dc13cb62f9cb5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="578" b="434" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14113117" class="wp-caption-text">食材。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1.取容量2夸脱的汤锅，放入鸡汤煮沸。</p>
<p>2.加入西洋菜煮3分钟至变软。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14113120" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14113120" style="width: 554px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113120-d8ca9f8a91dd4fa885905d9a73825660-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14113120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113120-d8ca9f8a91dd4fa885905d9a73825660-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="554" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14113120" class="wp-caption-text">西洋菜。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.汤汁加入蓝纹起司，用食物调理机搅打融合再倒回锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14113122" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14113122" style="width: 559px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113122-ebc7f5a3ae5b2936b9747462ae668758-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14113122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14113122-ebc7f5a3ae5b2936b9747462ae668758-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14113122" class="wp-caption-text">西洋菜蓝纹鸡汤。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.加入淡奶或淡奶油调味（蓝纹起司很咸，汤中不用加盐），小火加热、搅拌至食材融合。◇</p>
