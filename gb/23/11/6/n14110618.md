<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>顶石棹高山、茶园、云海  摄影爱好者流连</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110621-645641-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一睹壮观的山凹处云海。（黄荣群提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月06日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾嘉义报导）嘉义阿里山乡顶石棹，位于海拔1200～1600公尺之间的山区，一直以来都以其令人心旷神怡的美景而闻名。特别是当游客登上观景台时，他们不仅能一睹壮观的山凹处云海，还可以欣赏到茶园和竹林的壮观风光。</p>
<figure id="14110622" aria-describedby="caption-14110622" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110622-645642-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110622-645642-450x300.jpg" alt="采茶姑娘一字排开，壮观吸睛。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14110622" class="wp-caption-text">采茶姑娘一字排开，壮观吸睛。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14110624" aria-describedby="caption-14110624" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110624-645644-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110624-645644-450x300.jpg" alt="甜美的笑容，搭配蓝天，成就一张美丽作品。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14110624" class="wp-caption-text">甜美的笑容，搭配蓝天，成就一张美丽作品。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>整齐的茶园，沿着山坡一层层往下延伸，缓坡木梯令人轻松穿梭其间。摄影爱好者尤其喜爱这条路线，每回来顶石棹都会上来走走，以捕捉这里壮观景色的瞬间。</p>
<p>阿里山茶文化也在近年来蓬勃发展，瑞峰、瑞里、太兴等茶席推广取得广泛成功。而位于阿里山公路的石棹茶区是阿里山高山茶的代表之一，这里有众多大小茶厂，为这片美丽的山区，增添了独特的茶文化韵味，尤其是采茶季节，五彩缤纷的采茶姑娘头饰、服饰，就能让摄影者拍个不停，乐此不疲。</p>
<figure id="14110619" aria-describedby="caption-14110619" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110619-645639-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110619-645639-450x300.jpg" alt="摄影爱好者上下来回，就为了捕捉这里景色的瞬间。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14110619" class="wp-caption-text">摄影爱好者上下来回，就为了捕捉这里景色的瞬间。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14110620" aria-describedby="caption-14110620" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110620-645640-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110620-645640-450x300.jpg" alt="七彩的服饰搭配绿色茶海，非常美丽。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14110620" class="wp-caption-text">七彩的服饰搭配绿色茶海，非常美丽。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一个令人着迷的景点是隙顶，海拔1250公尺，位于阿里山公路54K附近。这个地方不仅是眺望壮观日出的好地方，也因山与云的交会，形成独特的云瀑景观，吸引摄影爱好者在此守候，捕捉大自然的美丽变幻。</p>
<figure id="14110625" aria-describedby="caption-14110625" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110625-645645-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110625-645645-450x300.jpg" alt="二延平步道。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14110625" class="wp-caption-text">二延平步道。（wish Lin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14110623" aria-describedby="caption-14110623" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110623-645643-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14110623-645643-450x300.jpg" alt="辽阔的视野。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14110623" class="wp-caption-text">辽阔的视野。（黄荣群提供）</figcaption></figure>
