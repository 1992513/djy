<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】雪燕椰奶南瓜盅～养颜炖汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114867-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>雪燕椰奶炖南瓜盅～养颜炖汤。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong> “炖雪燕”在港式饮茶是常见的糖水，很受欢迎，主要是口感清甜、营养满满，如果再和椰奶搭配就更加顺口。忙碌的现代人都想要保有好气色，但是没有时间下厨，如果又想控制体重，真是很烦恼能够一次补充好营养又不饿肚子的美食圣品是什么？</p>
<p>答案揭晓：“雪燕椰奶炖南瓜盅”。只要事先处理好南瓜和食材，放入蒸锅，用大火炖20分钟就可以食用了，是不是很简单又方便呢？</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">雪燕椰奶炖南瓜盅</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>南瓜1个（约3磅）<br />
发好的雪燕5 oz<br />
冰糖1 oz<br />
椰奶8 oz<br />
南瓜肉1.5 oz</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114860" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114860-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114860-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114860" class="wp-caption-text">材料依序左上至右下（雪燕、椰奶、南瓜、南瓜肉、冰糖）（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 南瓜洗净后切去顶部约一吋瓜盖，挖出瓜肉和瓜籽，瓜的边缘刻上V字形状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114861" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114861-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114861-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114861" class="wp-caption-text">南瓜切去顶部约一吋瓜盖，挖出瓜肉和瓜籽，瓜的边缘刻上V字形状。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 椰奶加冰糖煮热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114862" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114862-3.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114862-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114862" class="wp-caption-text">椰奶加冰糖煮热。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 雪燕及南瓜肉放入沸水中烫1分钟，捞出后放进南瓜内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114863-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114863-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114863" class="wp-caption-text">雪燕及南瓜肉放入沸水中烫1分钟，捞出后放进南瓜内。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 锅内烧热水，放上蒸架。南瓜盛在碗中放到蒸架上，椰奶倒入瓜内至9分满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114864-93d5acd3c7d548a4c7d9df7350c44c40.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114864-93d5acd3c7d548a4c7d9df7350c44c40-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114864" class="wp-caption-text">椰奶倒入瓜内至9分满。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 盖上南瓜盖，再盖上锅盖，用大火炖20分钟便可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114865" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114865-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14114865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114865-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114865" class="wp-caption-text">盖上南瓜盖。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h3>
<p>南瓜看起来皮很硬，但其实很快便会煮软，所以不用炖太久。椰奶本身并不甜，所以加入冰糖增加甜味。◇</p>
