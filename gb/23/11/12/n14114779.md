<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>探访台江秘境 体验渔村生活</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114795-79879d4090be694798b36f19d7a29a11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>坐上胶筏船体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。（安平雅／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月12日讯】游船在如镜的水面上平稳前行，耳边是导览生动有趣的介绍，脑子里已经想着一会儿在水上一试身手，看看是否会收到满满渔获？</p>
<p>渔村生活对于很多人想必都是新鲜体验，当日，我们来到台湾第八座国家公园台江国家公园内，体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。</p>
<p>“台江渔乐园”是由民间保育团体建立，旨在维护台江湿地生态之永续，推进生态环境的教育工程。</p>
<p>当日，我们乘坐的巴士来到四草大桥下，经过外海时，看到海浪不断涌向岸边，脑海中浮现出曾经出海时在船上呕吐不止的情景。开始纠结犹豫，是否要参与这次行程。</p>
<p>跟导游确认后，说我们游览的是内海，船会很平稳。</p>
<p>我们到达后，登上船，发现这里真的是风平浪静。</p>
<p>原来，一桥之隔的外海与内海真的是截然不同的景象。</p>
<p>“台江渔乐园”的船只停靠在四草大桥下，我们沿着小径登上船。导览员请一位团员示范正确的救生衣穿法，幽默的讲解令人莞尔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114793-f820623b705cd1b7e9eff3ab61bd5e06-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14114793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114793-f820623b705cd1b7e9eff3ab61bd5e06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114793" class="wp-caption-text">坐上胶筏船在台江国家公园内，体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。导览员讲解救生衣的穿法。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>船只出发后，看到海上有人专心垂钓。这些竹筏是出租的，按日计费。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114794-359c2f4c6e37f0551b76014025dbef16-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14114794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114794-359c2f4c6e37f0551b76014025dbef16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114794" class="wp-caption-text">坐上胶筏船在台江国家公园内，体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。垂钓爱好者在浮棚区域钓鱼。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导览员还讲解了水边的植物、鸟类。这里的植物包括五梨跤、水笔仔、榄李等台湾的红树林景观。红树林的名称由来是源于红树科植物，因其木材呈红色，从树皮中可以提炼出“单宁”做为红色染料，所以称之为“ 红树”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114796" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114796-567fe26cf39f179c539fd55798a26dbb-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14114796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114796-567fe26cf39f179c539fd55798a26dbb-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114796" class="wp-caption-text">坐上胶筏船在台江国家公园内，体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。水域上的红树林。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14114797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114797-IMG_4654-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14114797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114797-IMG_4654-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114797" class="wp-caption-text">坐上胶筏船在台江国家公园内，体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。水域上的红树林。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在船尾，船长撒下的“蜈蚣网”捞起后，网中的渔获竟然就是一只水母，和一只约2寸长的小鱼，团友应该是看不下去小鱼的挣扎，一把抓起直接丢入水中放生了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114789" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114789-IMG_4672-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14114789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114789-IMG_4672-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114789" class="wp-caption-text">坐上胶筏船在台江国家公园内，体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。“蜈蚣网”收网后的渔获水母。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>体验捕鱼的时刻到了。</p>
<p>我们的船靠近一个固定的竹筏，大家上到竹筏上，竹筏的一头是传统捕鱼工具。</p>
<p>我们尝试的就是这种唐代就有文字记载的四角吊网渔法，它利用杠杆原理将渔网从水中吊起，这种捕鱼方式也被称为吊罾。</p>
<p>团友们合力将吊罾拉起时，收获就只有两只螃蟹，聊胜于无啊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14114790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114790" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114790-IMG_4675-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14114790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114790-IMG_4675-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114790" class="wp-caption-text">坐上胶筏船体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。大家合力拉起吊罾 。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14115126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14115126" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14115126-IMG_4679-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14115126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14115126-IMG_4679-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14115126" class="wp-caption-text">团友们合力拉起吊罾后，收获就只有两只螃蟹。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>竹筏另一头是个小房子，被称为“探更寮”，因外形像桶，又称“桶间寮”。昔日的探更寮，能让看守鱼塘的人避风遮雨。现代人仿古搭建的探更寮，冬暖夏凉，成为体验渔家生活的休闲项目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14115133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14115133" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14115133-IMG_4680-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14115133" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14115133-IMG_4680-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14115133" class="wp-caption-text">竹筏上被称为“探更寮”的小房子，以前是给看守鱼塘的人避风遮雨用的。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>之后，我们来到养殖蚵的浮棚上，船长拎起水下的它们让大家近距离观察。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14115127" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14115127" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14115127-IMG_4695-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14115127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14115127-IMG_4695-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14115127" class="wp-caption-text">船长在蚵棚上寻找水下的牡蛎。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14114791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14114791" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114791-IMG_4685-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14114791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14114791-IMG_4685-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14114791" class="wp-caption-text">坐上胶筏船体验“台江渔乐园”游览项目。水中捞起的养殖牡蛎。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>虽然这一趟下来没有什么渔获，但渔家生活体验让我们觉得新鲜有趣，也了解到些许渔民的辛苦。</p>
