<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>112学年度工业类技艺竞赛颁奖 选手士气高昻</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123373-649384-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾师大工学院院长郑庆民说，个人是40年前工科技艺竞赛的选手，72年当上选手，当时参加比赛并没有得名，但也没有成为阻扰个人职涯发展的因素，反而更加努力。（李撷璎／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月24日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）教育部为展现历年教学及技艺推动成果，于本(112)年11月21日~23日为期3天在嘉义高工举办“112学年度全国高级中学学校学生工业类技艺竞赛”，经过3天高强度的比赛，成绩已顺利出炉，并于24日在校内乐群堂办理颁奖典礼，本次共颁发29个职种178支金手奖291个优胜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123374" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123374-649385-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14123374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123374-649385-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123374" class="wp-caption-text">教育部政务次长林明裕表示，“没拿到奖项的同学不要泄气，答应我的请鼓掌”，要继续努力不懈。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由教育部政务次长林明裕将奖项一一颁发给各得奖选手，并拍照留念。</p>
<p>嘉义市府教育处副处长林喜信代表市长黄敏惠颁奖给捐赠单位，并表示，感谢教育部让这个活动在嘉义市举行，有机会让城市更加进步。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123375" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123375-649386-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14123375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123375-649386-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123375" class="wp-caption-text">点将家企业老板陈董，本次赞助870万现金给得奖选手及指导教师。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育部政务次长林明裕表示，“没拿到奖项的同学不要泄气，答应我的请鼓掌”，要继续努力不懈，教育部为了让选手开展国际视野，自108年起并规划金手奖第一、二名赴海外产业进行观摩及学习。继续努力，预祝各位顺利成功。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123376" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123376-649387-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14123376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123376-649387-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123376" class="wp-caption-text">企业热情赞助五台山叶机车给得奖选手。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾师大工学院院长郑庆民讲评，感谢执行单位嘉义高工所领导的团队完美完成本次比赛，提供这么好的场地，本次新增机器手臂职种，在工业4.0生产自动化与AI（人工智慧）的时代，机器手臂的应用愈来愈广，感谢厂商上银科技等免费提供3支机器手臂，让比赛能顺利完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123377" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123377-649388-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14123377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123377-649388-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123377" class="wp-caption-text">企业热情赞助，左为纳智捷汽车；右为福特六和汽车给得奖选手。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑庆民院长说，个人是40年前工科技艺竞赛的选手，72年当上选手，当时参加比赛并没有得名，但也没有成为阻扰个人职涯发展的因素，反而更加努力。另外，也期盼各位得奖的同学不要让自己的能力就停在今天，在技能领域还要继续精进，拥有这些技能是个人事业发展最大的助力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123378" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123378-649389-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14123378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123378-649389-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123378" class="wp-caption-text">企业热情赞助台湾马自达汽车给得奖选手。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>成绩出炉，嘉义高工本次荣获3个第1名，最难得的是新增的机器手臂有两位同学得第1，分别是丁彦宇、赖圣展获得第1名金手奖，指导老师是刘荣辉、陈敏雄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14123379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123379" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123379-649390-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14123379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123379-649390-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123379" class="wp-caption-text">选手参赛实况。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14123380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14123380" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123380-649391-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14123380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14123380-649391-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14123380" class="wp-caption-text">由教育部政务次长林明裕将奖项一一颁发给各得奖选手，并拍照留念。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
