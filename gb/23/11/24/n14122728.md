<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林登山队攀登八大秀 百岳之巅再现壮举</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122737-649201-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>往八通关山1.5k。（廖庭辉提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年11月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾南投报导）云林西螺镇民代表，同时也是登山爱好者廖庭辉，在11月初成功挑战台湾八大秀，包括八通关山、大水窟山、秀姑峦山三座百岳的简称。这项壮举不仅是对台湾自然美景的探索，也是对体力和毅力的极限考验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14122729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14122729" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122729-649193.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14122729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122729-649193-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14122729" class="wp-caption-text">乐乐山屋。（廖庭辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14122730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14122730" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122730-649194.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14122730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122730-649194-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14122730" class="wp-caption-text">往八通关山1.5k。（廖庭辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖庭辉在完成台湾四大五岳之一的小关山后，曾一度放下攀登百岳的念头，认为这项挑战实在太辛苦。然而，他后来发现自己只完成了四座台湾五岳，而最难攀登的中央山脉最高峰秀姑峦山仍未登顶。于是，他决定再次挑战自己，尝试攀登八大秀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14122731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14122731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122731-649195.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14122731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122731-649195-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14122731" class="wp-caption-text">中央金矿山屋。（廖庭辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14122732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14122732" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122732-649196.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14122732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122732-649196.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14122732" class="wp-caption-text">大水窟山三角点。（廖庭辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的登山行程计划原本为四天，但因遇到香港团队规划六天的行程，廖庭辉和他的好友们挑战了三天八大秀。第一天，他们从东埔登山口出发，走了25公里，爬升超过1000米，相当考验体力。第二天，他们从中央金矿山屋出发，攀登大水窟山，这段路极少人走，他们几乎是开路前行，花了五个小时才完成这一段路程。随后，他们赶回秀姑坪，再度攀登秀姑峦山，这段路段悬崖峭壁多，路标不明确，非常具挑战性。第三天，他们走回中央金矿山屋，并攀登八通关山，总路程超过28公里，再度面临艰困路段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14122733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14122733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122733-649197.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14122733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122733-649197-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14122733" class="wp-caption-text">秀姑峦山三角点。（廖庭辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14122734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14122734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122734-649198.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14122734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122734-649198-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14122734" class="wp-caption-text">巴奈伊克山屋。（廖庭辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>廖庭辉在完成这项挑战后，成功登顶八大秀中的八通关山、大水窟山和秀姑峦山，并在社群媒体上分享了他的登山心得。他表示，这次的三天八大秀之旅虽然极具挑战性，但也充满了成就感，让他再次感受到攀登百岳的魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14122736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14122736" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122736-649200.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14122736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122736-649200-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14122736" class="wp-caption-text">八通关大草原。（廖庭辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14122735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14122735" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122735-649199.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14122735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14122735-649199-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14122735" class="wp-caption-text">八通关山三角点。（廖庭辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
