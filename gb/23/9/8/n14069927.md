<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【飞向欧洲】太阳王路易十四开辟的香榭丽舍大道</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069928-314170181a61cde0697edb4e3c9af503-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>香榭丽舍大道为什么那么出名呢？它是“太阳王”路易十四下令建造的，有三百年历史，这里曾经是贵族和上流社会的社交场所。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>法国巴黎的香榭丽舍大道为什么那么出名呢？它是“太阳王”路易十四下令建造的，有三百年历史，这里曾经是贵族和上流社会的社交场所。另一方面来说，这里是巴黎的名牌街，想逛名牌店的朋友来这里一定没错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14069930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14069930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069930-017aa3dd0f70fb6513e36e03fb6cf3bd.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14069930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069930-017aa3dd0f70fb6513e36e03fb6cf3bd-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14069930" class="wp-caption-text">香榭丽舍大道全长两公里，由凯旋门一直延伸到罗浮宫。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14069931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14069931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069931-628f8a03c1a72e089ddf99ced37f94d7.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14069931 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069931-628f8a03c1a72e089ddf99ced37f94d7-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14069931" class="wp-caption-text">香榭丽舍大道周围的建筑都风格一致、非常整齐。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>香榭丽舍大道全长两公里，太阳王当时叫设计师把罗浮宫花园中心道路，一直打通到现在的凯旋门圆形广场。香榭丽舍大道上有各种豪华咖啡厅、剧院和名牌店，其中最具代表性的当然是LV旗舰店。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14069932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14069932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069932-d517f96deec485d8aaaa72eb3a0e23cf.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14069932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069932-d517f96deec485d8aaaa72eb3a0e23cf-600x398.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14069932" class="wp-caption-text">香榭丽舍大道是巴黎的名牌街，想逛名牌店的朋友来这里一定没错。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14069934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14069934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069934-68c8de0d87f55bf4ce43efffaee28c5f.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14069934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14069934-68c8de0d87f55bf4ce43efffaee28c5f-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14069934" class="wp-caption-text">香榭丽舍大道上最具代表性的当然是LV旗舰店。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>

<p>在这个浪漫的大道上散步的时候，我还遇到一位法国绅士，给我介绍了一首香榭丽舍大道的著名歌曲“Les Champs-Elysées”（香榭丽舍大道）。</p>
