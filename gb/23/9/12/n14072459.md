<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>国际雪地摩托与全地形车展10月回归多伦多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072544-b1ce112b76bbeeb714b8fa571663d495-600x400.png" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月13日讯】备受期待的国际雪地摩托与全地形车展，将于2023年10月20日至22日隆重回归著名的多伦多国际中心（International Centre）。</p>

</div>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">多伦多国际雪地摩托全地形车展（Toronto International Snowmobile, ATV &amp; Powersports Show），是一年一度的盛会，今年已是第36届。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14072551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072551-1-450x198.png" alt="" width="597" b="263" /></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">车展将展示最先进、最好的雪地摩托、全地形车和相关设备。</span></span> <span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">来自世界各地的制造商和供应商将展示他们的尖端型号、创新配件和高性能装备。</span></span> <span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">爱好者和买家都可以探索多种选择，为下一次令人兴奋的购买做出明智的决定。</span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/12/n14072459.md#1/1280-1-5" rel="attachment wp-att-14072542"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14072542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072542-1280-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="518" b="291" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">与会者将有机会触摸、感受最新车辆并与之互动 ，更深入了解新车的功能。</span></span> <span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">由专家主持的工作坊和研讨会，将为新手和经验丰富的骑手提供有关维护、骑行技术和安全操作的宝贵见解。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/12/n14072459.md#1/1280-8" rel="attachment wp-att-14072543"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14072543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072543-1280-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="518" b="291" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p>抓住这个与行业专家和其他爱好者交流的独特机会，与志趣相投的人齐聚一堂，互相取经，培养友情，增强社区意识。因为冬季运动不仅仅是一种爱好，而是一种生活方式。</p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/12/n14072459.md#1/dsc_9497-1024x576-1" rel="attachment wp-att-14072545"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14072545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14072545-DSC_9497-1024x576-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="518" b="291" /></a></span></span></span></p>
<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">车展还提供惊险刺激的娱乐活动。专业骑手令人惊叹的特技，极限骑术的动态展示，都将带给人们兴奋不已的独特体验。</span></span></span><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">食品摊贩、商品摊位和适合全家人的充满乐趣的活动，将确保每个人都乐在其中。 </span></span></span></p>
