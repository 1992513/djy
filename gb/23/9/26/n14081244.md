<h6>9月23日，波士顿洪青体育会庆祝75周年。21头彩狮游行穿过华埠街道。（刘景烨/大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月26日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）锣鼓声响，21头彩狮在街上围成一圈，翻腾舞动。满地的鞭炮突然炸响，不绝于耳。这是9月23日上午，波士顿洪青体育会楼下的场景。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://doqmgyop4u892.cloudfront.net/Q0yBRDdJ7Ddm"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/embed/1g6771mlhj92N3RgcGTC8qYWA1ae1c" allowfullscreen></a>

<p>当天，洪青体育会在其华埠Beach街的驻地欢庆75周年。洪青及洪门致公堂的宗长们高举会旗，率舞狮队游行穿过华埠街区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14081234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081234-DSC01769.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081234-DSC01769-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081234" class="wp-caption-text">9月23日，彩狮在洪青体育会楼下献瑞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14081236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081236-DSC01797.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081236-DSC01797-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081236" class="wp-caption-text">9月23日，波士顿洪青体育会庆祝75周年。21头彩狮游行穿过华埠街道。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这很棒，今天有很多人出来，有21头狮子，代表着我们洪门，就是我们洪青的父辈团体。”波士顿洪青体育会会长谢健敦说。</p>
<p>他还谈到，洪青举办很多体育活动，有排球、篮球、舞狮、武术等等。参与这些活动，让他收获很多兄弟情谊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14081242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081242" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081242-DSC01917.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081242-DSC01917-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081242" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿洪青体育会会长谢健敦参与庆祝洪青75周年。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14081238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081238" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081238-DSC01844.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081238-DSC01844-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081238" class="wp-caption-text">舞狮游行之后，彩狮在洪门致公堂楼下排开，桌上是代表洪门的“洪”字。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天，数百人的游行队伍从Beach街洪青体育会出发，经过Harrison街和Kneeland街，转入洪门致公堂所在的Tyler街。在洪门楼下，洪青的兄弟们依次表演武术，赢得周围人群的喝采和掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14081243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081243" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081243-DSC01926.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081243-DSC01926-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081243" class="wp-caption-text">洪门楼上挂有横幅，楼下有舞狮献瑞和诸多观众。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>洪门总理余丽媖说，很高兴在这里举办这么大的庆祝会来欢庆洪青75周年。她提到，她是家族里第3代洪门成员，她的祖父和父亲都曾是这个团体的一员。余丽媖于1970年代加入洪门，这么多年来，对这个团体很有归属感。</p>
<p>此外，余丽媖是波士顿洪门史上首位女主席，也是美国洪门致公堂第一位女总理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14081235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081235-DSC01776.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081235-DSC01776-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081235" class="wp-caption-text">总理余丽媖走在游行队伍前方。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14081239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081239" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081239-DSC01858.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081239-DSC01858-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081239" class="wp-caption-text">舞狮游行之后，洪青兄弟们表演武术。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>波士顿洪青体育会是全美洪青体育会中历史最悠久，组织最庞大的一团。在白天的游行庆典和祭祖之后，当天晚上，他们还在波士顿威斯汀酒店（Westin Hotel）举办庆祝晚会。驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏，以及多位嘉宾都到场祝贺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14081240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081240" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081240-DSC01899.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081240-DSC01899-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081240" class="wp-caption-text">舞狮游行之后，洪青兄弟们表演武术。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14081241" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14081241" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081241-DSC01914.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14081241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14081241-DSC01914-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14081241" class="wp-caption-text">麻州众议员黄子安（Donald Wong）是洪门成员，他参与9月23日的洪青75周年庆典。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>麻州众议员黄子安（Donald Wong）也是洪门成员。他说，他于1960年代加入洪青，看到这个团体传承着武术、舞狮等传统文化，也兴办诸多体育活动，这些对于华埠和其它地区的年轻人来说都是有益的。</p>
<p>“我热爱洪青体育会。我住在郊区，它让我看到我的文化，尤其是舞狮、武术，也让我在唐人街交到更多朋友。”黄子安说。◇</p>
