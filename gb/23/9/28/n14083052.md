<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东南国中花卉异象 梅花、樱花、杜鹃花齐开惊喜登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083056-638100-600x400.png" />
<h6>东南国中的校园出现了令人惊喜的异象。（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）云林县西螺镇东南国中，可能受到海葵台风影响，28日校园出现了令人惊喜的异象，多种花卉在不同季节竟然同时绽放，为校园带来了一场视觉盛宴。</p>
<figure id="14083053" aria-describedby="caption-14083053" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083053-638097-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083053-638097-450x338.jpg" alt="九月底的白天气温仍然高达摄氏三十几度，但梅花已经冒出娇嫩的绿芽并开出花朵。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14083053" class="wp-caption-text">九月底的白天气温仍然高达摄氏三十几度，但梅花已经冒出娇嫩的绿芽并开出花朵。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14083059" aria-describedby="caption-14083059" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083059-638118-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083059-638118-450x338.jpg" alt="1月开花，9月又见梅花开。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14083059" class="wp-caption-text">1月开花，9月又见梅花开。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14083055" aria-describedby="caption-14083055" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083055-638099-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083055-638099-450x338.jpg" alt="樱花也加入了这场突如其来的花卉盛宴。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14083055" class="wp-caption-text">樱花也加入了这场突如其来的花卉盛宴。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管九月底的白天气温仍然高达摄氏三十几度，但梅花已经冒出娇嫩的绿芽并开出少许的花朵，校内的杜鹃花也竟然在这个时候开了三朵白色杜鹃花，而樱花也加入了这场突如其来的花卉盛宴。值得一提的是，校园内的枫香树都还没有转红，这样多元的花卉生态令全校师生大为惊喜。</p>
<p>东南国中的园丁表示，这种情况可能是台风造成花卉的时序错乱，导致植物们提前开花。这样的天候和花卉异象确实相当罕见。</p>
<figure id="14083058" aria-describedby="caption-14083058" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083058-638117-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14083058-638117-450x338.jpg" alt="9月杜鹃花又开了。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14083058" class="wp-caption-text">9月杜鹃花又开了。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
