<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“重光国小”全国首个童军教育实验学校</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073670-635619-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>培养学生的自我认知、冒险精神。（重光国小提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E5%9D%91.md#1">古坑</a>重光国小改制为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A5%E5%86%9B%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">童军教育</a>实验学校，14日上午举行了课程启动仪式。学校感谢家长、学者以及参与推动童军教育的教师们的协助。仪式上，学校规划参加了观摩活动，吸引了关注童军教育的各界人士和全体师生参与。</p>
<figure id="14073658" aria-describedby="caption-14073658" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073658-635607-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073658-635607-450x338.jpg" alt="团集会进行中" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073658" class="wp-caption-text">团集会进行中（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>重光国小百年传承，在校长杨松田和全体教职员热诚和卓越领导下，学校取得了显着的成绩。学校经过学校事??务会议的批准，自主申请成为实验学校。</p>
<figure id="14073659" aria-describedby="caption-14073659" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073659-635608-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073659-635608-450x337.jpg" alt="可爱的小童军" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073659" class="wp-caption-text">可爱的小童军（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14073657" aria-describedby="caption-14073657" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073657-635606-450x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073657-635606-450x800.jpg" alt="小小童军绽放光芒" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073657" class="wp-caption-text">小小童军绽放光芒（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>重光国小杨松田校长表示，感谢<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>县政府的大力支持，重光国小成为全国首个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A5%E5%86%9B%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">童军教育</a>实验学校。在全校教师的积极参与下，全体教师都具备了幼童军木基训和木章训的基本素质。在112学年，学校还吸引了10名区外学童前来就读，这也是对学校用心和努力的肯定。</p>
<figure id="14073660" aria-describedby="caption-14073660" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073660-635609-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073660-635609-450x338.jpg" alt="闯关游戏" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073660" class="wp-caption-text">闯关游戏（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14073661" aria-describedby="caption-14073661" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073661-635610-450x339.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073661-635610-450x339.jpg" alt="守纪律" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073661" class="wp-caption-text">守纪律（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14073669" aria-describedby="caption-14073669" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073669-635618-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073669-635618-450x337.jpg" alt="包括童军户外探索、童军科技、童军多语运用和童军技艺传承。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073669" class="wp-caption-text">包括童军户外探索、童军科技、童军多语运用和童军技艺传承。（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>童军教育是一项世界性的运动，也是青少年梦想和建造梦想的策略，它综合了个性与团队、技能与情感、品德与能力，以培养学生的自我认知、冒险精神和好奇心。除了深耕和倡导学科知识领域，学校还通过跨学科的童军课程来拓展学生的人际互动和潜力发展。</p>
<figure id="14073662" aria-describedby="caption-14073662" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073662-635611-450x339.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073662-635611-450x339.jpg" alt="团长带着小童军一起闯关" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073662" class="wp-caption-text">团长带着小童军一起闯关（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14073663" aria-describedby="caption-14073663" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073663-635612-450x339.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073663-635612-450x339.jpg" alt="团体中学习友爱" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073663" class="wp-caption-text">团体中学习友爱（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14073667" aria-describedby="caption-14073667" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073667-635616-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073667-635616-450x338.jpg" alt="童军教育是一项世界性的运动" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073667" class="wp-caption-text">童军教育是一项世界性的运动（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14073668" aria-describedby="caption-14073668" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073668-635617-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073668-635617-450x600.jpg" alt="开心的园地" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073668" class="wp-caption-text">开心的园地（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以学校本位课程为出发点，希望通过童军教育的新时代意义，从童军的三大制度、格言、诺言和规则出发，采用四学期的全日制课程，周一至周四的课后辅导到下午4点50分，周五下午4点结束，为每个学生提供全面的照顾。</p>
<figure id="14073664" aria-describedby="caption-14073664" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073664-635613-450x339.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073664-635613-450x339.jpg" alt="学习旗语" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073664" class="wp-caption-text">学习旗语（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14073665" aria-describedby="caption-14073665" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073665-635614-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14073665-635614-450x337.jpg" alt="他们在做什么呢？" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14073665" class="wp-caption-text">他们在做什么呢？（重光国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
