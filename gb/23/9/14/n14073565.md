<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新州拨款数亿 永久聘用逾千名护士和助产士</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/07/id14032046-shutterstock_1637163589-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>为解决医护人员短缺问题，新州政府前承诺拨款5.72亿，永久聘用1112名护士和助产士。（Shutterstock）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月14日讯】<span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">（大纪元记者夏楚君澳洲悉尼报导）<span style="color: #4d5156;">为解决医护人员短缺问题，新州政府前承诺拨款5.72</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">亿，永久聘用</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1112</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">名护士和助产士。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">周四（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">9</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">14</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">日），政府表示，在下周公布的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #4d5156;">预算中承诺的这笔投资将在</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #4d5156;">2024</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">至</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2025</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年开始的三个财年内支出。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #4d5156;">新州州长柯民思（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #000000;">Chris Minns</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span lang="en-US">）</span></span><span style="color: #4d5156;">表示，必须加强医护劳动力，以减少择期手术等候名单和急诊室等候时间。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">他说，“新州卫生系统正面临着巨大的压力。我们不能失去辛勤工作的护士和助产士。”</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">“我们将兑现承诺，支持我们不可或缺的一线卫生工作者，使他们能够继续为我们的社区提供所需和应得的医疗服务。”</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2023</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年第二季度，新州有超过</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">750,000</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">名患者到急诊科就诊，危及生命的</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">类病例数量创历史新高，达到</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">17,949</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">例。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">新州<span style="color: #4d5156;">卫生厅长帕克（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #4d5156;">Ryan Park</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）表示，新的永久职位对于确保政府能够实现急诊室和病房配备足够医护人员的承诺至关重要。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #4d5156;">“</span>医护<span style="color: #4d5156;">人员配备的安全水平意味着有更多的护士和助产士在医院工作，这是政府在兑现重建基本医疗服务承诺方面迈出的重要一步。”</span></span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #4d5156;">1112</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">名<span style="color: #4d5156;">护士和助产士将在</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #4d5156;">15</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个地方卫生区?</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">(LHD)?</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">以及儿童医院工作。</span></span></span></p>
<p align="left"><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #4d5156;">除了</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">5.72</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">亿元的预算注资外，政府还承诺在第一个任期内增聘</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1200</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">名护士和助产士。</span></span></span></p>
