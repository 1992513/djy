<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第7届“屏东原住民收获节”八乡联合盛大登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075014-635926-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第七届屏东原住民收获节（廖俪芬／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>报导）屏东县政府致力于推广原住民族的独特文化，促进族群之间的交流，并为更多的人民提供认识原住民文化之美的机会。自106年起，屏东县政府开始筹办全县性的联合生产力节，并成功举办至今已进入第7年。今年的“收获拿~么多”联合生产力节16日和17日，在屏东市体育场举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075018" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075018-635930.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075018" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075018-635930-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075018" class="wp-caption-text">主视觉相当吸睛（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075020" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075020-635932.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075020" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075020-635932-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075020" class="wp-caption-text">乡长们都亲自来为乡亲加油打气（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075027" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075027-635939.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075027-635939-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075027" class="wp-caption-text">志工宣导（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“收获拿么多”是排湾族人称为“masalut”、鲁凯族人称为“tangidrakakalane”的词语，意为感谢上天的眷顾和丰收的喜悦。在过去的一年中，尽管环境发生变化，但得益于上天给予的良好种子、丰沛雨水和阳光，让原住民族人在丰收祭中一起围舞欢庆，将收成献给天空，感恩纪念上天的关怀，并一同分享友爱和互助的情谊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075017" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075017" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075017-635929.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075017-635929-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075017" class="wp-caption-text">老中青齐聚一堂（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075015-635927.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075015-635927-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075015" class="wp-caption-text">１６日上午先举办原住民八乡竞技（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075016-635928.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075016-635928-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075016" class="wp-caption-text">开场先做暖身操（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075021-635933.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14075021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075021-635933-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075021" class="wp-caption-text">同心协力一起向前跑（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动有精彩刺激的传统竞技暨武术竞赛，传统竞技和武术竞赛，包括飞镖、传统拔河、踩踏高跷、竹筒取水、相对三脚、亲子拉槟榔叶、大小vuvu操健康等。此外，还有传统乐舞表演、传统文化祭仪演示，以及最壮观的千人传统围舞，象征不同族群之间的多元共融。</p>
