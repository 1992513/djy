<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗栗龙凤渔港秋季限定 吹吹风电影院 轻松逛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075186-636015-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>与会来宾欣喜户外电影院活动赞。（许享富／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月16日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）风睿能源 (SRE )自2020年开始，每年以海上风机景致与唯美夕阳为背景，搭起浪漫电影布幕，打造最具标志性的秋日限定露天电影院，邀请大家走入有着绝美海景的龙凤渔港，在港边特有的闲适氛围中，享受最独特的户外观影体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075185-636014.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14075185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075185-636014-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075185" class="wp-caption-text">竹南龙渔港的离岸风力发电机。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2023年秋天，吹吹风电影院推出四大活动，不仅露天放映今年甫获奥斯卡金像奖最佳动画片提名的《青春养成记》，打造最疗愈又放松的Movie Night，现场更有餐车主厨风味市集、充满趣味的精彩街头演出，以及限量报名的人体彩绘体验，为大小朋友画下最独特的吹吹风印记！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075181" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075181-636010.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14075181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075181-636010-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="451" b="677"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075181" class="wp-caption-text">文观局秘书彭德俊表示感谢风睿能源公司的离岸风电场的绿电投资，尤其吹吹风电影院活动、龙凤渔港夕阳、夜市轻松逛均为渔港带来的更多的观光人潮。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075179-636008.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14075179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075179-636008-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075179" class="wp-caption-text">与会来宾欢迎来欣赏《青春养成记》。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075184" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075184-636013.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14075184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075184-636013-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075184" class="wp-caption-text">舒适豪华躺式电影院 欢渡愉快周末。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年风睿能源（SRE）四度携手全台最潮的“Skyline Film屋顶电影院”，在海上风机景致与唯美夕阳下，带来最浪漫的户外观影体验！现场更有餐车主厨风味市集、充满趣味的欢乐街头表演，以及限量报名的人体彩绘体验，让您感受不一样的龙凤渔港、惬意度过美好周末时光！</p>
<p>风格餐车、街头表演、人体彩绘，七大风格餐车进驻，主厨精心设计活动限定料理，从美式烧烤、港式小点到义式冰淇淋，全方位满足你的味蕾！参与餐车品牌｜巴诺Buono手工窑烤披萨、Cool Eat Food Truck 酷嗑美式快餐车、Dai柴 行动餐车、Tanuki 狸たぬき66 移动贩卖车、杯包嗑、WalkBarista 瓦克咖啡、寻光小径义式冰淇淋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075183-636012.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14075183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075183-636012-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075183" class="wp-caption-text">品尝各式风格餐车美食与龙凤渔港夕阳氛围欢渡愉快周末。 （许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14075182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075182-636011.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14075182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075182-636011-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075182" class="wp-caption-text">街头艺人的表演，欢渡愉快周末。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林雍尧表示，为了推广多元渔港文化，提供在地与周边民众一个优质又放松的休憩活动，广获好评的吹吹风电影院；16日假日晚上乡亲民众全家大小，在海风吹拂中，享受愉快的假日电影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14075180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14075180" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075180-636009.jpeg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14075180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14075180-636009-600x900.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="675"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14075180" class="wp-caption-text">风睿能源公司（SRE）董事长林雍尧指出，永续经营在地生态与产业结合，辧该活动感谢县府团队协力完成，台湾做绿能的目的，不是为了盖风场，而是要有足够的绿电支持台湾的厂商出口，尤其台湾厂商很多是承诺RE100国际企业的供应链，为欧盟未来的碳税课征提供减税依据。同时也不忘地方的繁荣尽力。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
