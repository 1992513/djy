<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：双重沉醉</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>一大早<br />
那只白头翁便吹响它的高音小喇叭<br />
麻雀和斑鸠等适时加入<br />
一曲相当规模的交响乐曲便开始演奏了<br />
繁复，回荡……</p>
<p>我被吸引住了<br />
痴痴地望着，聆听着<br />
不自觉被带回我小时候家屋后那片竹林<br />
只见在那片竹林里<br />
我不自禁地抬起头盯向树梢</p>
<p>那时竹林上方的鸟<br />
如白头翁、麻雀、斑鸠及乌鹙等<br />
正竞相演奏纷繁的乐曲<br />
我痴痴地聆听<br />
沉醉其中</p>
<p>啊，沉醉<br />
是小时候的我<br />
还是现在的我<br />
恐是双重沉醉吧<br />
重叠反复地沉醉……</p>
<p><strong>Double Intoxication?</strong> ? ?Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>In the early morning<br />
The pycnonotus sinensis plays his trumpet of the hitch<br />
And the sparrow as well as the turtledove join them timely<br />
A song of some more scale starts to play<br />
Thrivingly, resounded&#8230;</p>
<p>I&#8217;m attracted<br />
Watching fancily, listening&#8230;<br />
I&#8217;m carried back to the bamboo forest behind my home when I was young<br />
I only view the stretch bamboo forest<br />
I can&#8217;t help to look the top of the bamboo</p>
<p>At that time, the birds of the top of the tree<br />
Just as the pycnonotus sinensis, turtledove, sparrow, black drongo etc.<br />
Was playing the complicated music completely with each other<br />
I listen it fancily<br />
Intoxicatedly</p>
<p>Oh, intoxication<br />
It’s me in my when I was young<br />
Or is me of now?<br />
May it be double intoxication<br />
The intoxication piles up and repeatedly @</p>
