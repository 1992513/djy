<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北美最大的乌克兰文化节与您相约多伦多街头</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066453-img-1558_orig-600x400.jpg" />///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月04日讯】多伦多乌克兰节（Toronto Ukrainian Festival ）将于2023年9月15日至17日回归多伦多Bloor West Village社区，盛大的第27届活动将展现丰富多彩的乌克兰文化，让人们大开眼界。</p>
<p><strong><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/3/n14066414.md#1/spirit-of-ukraine-banner" rel="attachment wp-att-14066435"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14066435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066435-spirit-of-ukraine-banner-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="516" b="258" /></a>现场表演</strong></p>
<p>为期3天的多伦多乌克兰节，邀请了乌克兰艺术家、音乐家和舞者，在多个舞台上进行现场表演。来Bloor West Village社区吧，亲身体验令人着迷的舞蹈、深情的旋律以及乌克兰民间音乐的活力。</p>
<p><strong><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/3/n14066414.md#1/cover60_orig" rel="attachment wp-att-14066431"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14066431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066431-cover60_orig-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="516" b="258" /></a> 手工市场</strong></p>
<p>逛一逛展示传统乌克兰工艺品、艺术品和纪念品的市场，把玩一番精心设计的复活蛋，瞧瞧手工制作的服装和珠宝的独具匠心，或许能够寻到一款心仪的宝藏。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/3/n14066414.md#1/cover101_orig" rel="attachment wp-att-14066432"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14066432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066432-cover101_orig-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="516" b="258" /></a></p>
<p><strong>美食佳肴</strong></p>
<p>品尝乌克兰美食，才不虚此行。品味乌克兰风味的饺子，还有红菜汤、香肠和甜点等多种正宗菜肴。每一口都是踏上乌克兰味蕾之旅。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/3/n14066414.md#1/cover102_orig" rel="attachment wp-att-14066433"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14066433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066433-cover102_orig-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="516" b="258" /></a></p>
<p><strong>文化展览</strong></p>
<p>内容丰富的展览，将让你深入了解乌克兰源远流长的历史和文化，从古老的传统，到当代的影响，尽情体验其丰富的内涵吧。</p>
<p><strong><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/3/n14066414.md#1/cover110_orig" rel="attachment wp-att-14066434"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14066434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066434-cover110_orig-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="516" b="258" /></a></strong></p>
<p><strong>互动工作坊</strong></p>
<div>节日上的各种工作坊，还为渴望深入了解乌克兰的朋友提供了学习乌克兰舞蹈步骤、练习传统手工艺，甚至学几个乌克兰词汇的难得机会。</div>
