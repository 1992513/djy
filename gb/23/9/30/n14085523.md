<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西螺大桥观光文化节 庆祝西螺大桥通车70周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085532-638446-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>清晨被雾霾笼罩的西螺大桥桥下风景。（高铭洲提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）西螺大桥-台湾的代表性历史建筑，今年70岁了，为庆祝这一重要里程碑，云林西螺镇公所举办了相当精彩热闹的“2023西螺大桥观光文化节”系列活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14085533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085533" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085533-638447.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14085533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085533-638447-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085533" class="wp-caption-text">西螺大桥70岁了。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14085525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085525" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085525-638438.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14085525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085525-638438-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085525" class="wp-caption-text">西螺武馆也来共襄盛举。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14085526" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085526" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085526-638439.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14085526" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085526-638439-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085526" class="wp-caption-text">社区妈妈们个个美丽大方。 （李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14085524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085524" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085524-638437.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14085524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085524-638437-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085524" class="wp-caption-text">踩街大队，色彩缤纷亮丽。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>西螺镇长廖秋萍表示，这个观光文化节是西螺地方年度的盛事，透过各项精彩活动，不仅可以凝聚当地力量，连结当地商圈和农特产品，还可以让当地居民和远道而来的游客更深入地了解西螺。</p>
<p>今年特别推出了“西螺好行”免费观光巴士，搭乘时间为9月30日至10月1日，每天早上9点30分至下午5点。这条巴士路线将游览西螺大桥桥头、东市场等两个主要活动场地，并串联埤头里的绘本公园、公馆里的福鹿公园、佛美神仙故事馆、瑞春酱油观光工厂等西螺著名观光景点，为游客提供全方位的西螺之旅。参加者只需到活动服务台领取活动折页，收集满5个印章，即可兑换限量的西螺特产米酱平安旗组。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14085530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085530" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085530-638442.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14085530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085530-638442-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085530" class="wp-caption-text">30日晚间的艺术联展。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14085531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085531-638443.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14085531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085531-638443-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085531" class="wp-caption-text">社区妈妈们卖力的演出。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主要活动在9月30日和10月1日这两天，西螺大桥、广场、东市场都将举办精彩纷呈的活动，包括农特产品市集、踩街嘉年华、慢活健行、摄影比赛、着色比赛、宠物嘉年华亲子剧场等等，吸引许多来自全国各地的游客前来体验参与，今年还有桥下的甜根子花海壮观美丽。</p>
<p>此外，不容错过的还有10月1日闭幕时的盛大烟火秀，诚挚邀请全国各地的人们一同来西螺，一起庆祝西螺大桥通车70周年的重要时刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14085529" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085529" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085529-638441.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14085529" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085529-638441-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085529" class="wp-caption-text">越南籍妈妈们精彩舞蹈表演，象征族群融和。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14085527" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14085527" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085527-638440.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14085527" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14085527-638440-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14085527" class="wp-caption-text">踩街大队在福兴宫广场定点表演。（李政雄提供）</figcaption></figure>
