<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>罗德岛台湾日龙舟赛 台湾龙舟队夺冠</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067655-DSC00681-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>9月2日，罗德岛台湾日龙舟赛在波塔基河上热闹开赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波塔基市报导）一年一度的罗德岛龙舟赛暨台湾日9月2日在波塔基市（Pawtucket）举办，迎来25个参赛队伍和成千上万的游客。波士顿台湾龙舟队（BTBC）继取得全国赛第6名之后，又在罗德岛斩获冠军。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src="https://www.ganjing.com/embed/1g4id3fibgi4hHFNxiUHz3ud712f1c" allowfullscreen></a>
</div>
<figure id="attachment_14067657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067657-DSC00720.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067657-DSC00720-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067657" class="wp-caption-text">9月2日，罗德岛台湾日龙舟赛在波塔基河上热闹开赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>台湾龙舟队夺冠</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">周六上午，300多名参加龙舟赛的船员来到波塔基河畔，整装待发。这其中包括哈佛大学龙舟队、北京大学校友会龙舟队、海洋州龙舟队等俱乐部队伍，也有罗德岛州政府、波塔基市政府、警察局等当地政府机构组成的业余队伍。经过几个小时的激烈角逐，波士顿台湾龙舟队（BTBC）喜获第一名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067654-DSC00667.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067654-DSC00667-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067654" class="wp-caption-text">各龙舟队伍在河上竞渡。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">即将接任BTBC队长的邱伟哲说，此前他们刚在佛罗里达州的全美比赛中斩获第6名，也因而拿到了明年去意大利参加世锦赛的资格。为此，他非常感谢侨委会、经文处及各位侨胞的大力支持。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“其实一开始我们大家都不会划龙舟，也是从零开始，大家一起手把手，把这个运动练起来，一起流汗，一起吃苦，”邱伟哲说，“我们会继续的想办法，把我们这个龙舟的文化传递下去。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067659-DSC00738.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067659-DSC00738-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067659" class="wp-caption-text">龙舟队划向竞赛起点。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>台湾日庆典</strong><strong>?</strong><strong>节目丰富</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天除了龙舟比赛之外，现场还有民俗艺术表演、台湾美食，以及一些商贩摊位等等，非常热闹。中华民俗艺术工作坊一如既往地带来了击鼓、舞蹈、扯铃等节目表演，还设有美食摊位，出售台湾小吃。另外，经文处也在现场摆设支持台湾加入联合国的展摊，希望获得更多力量声援。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067658-DSC00728.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067658-DSC00728-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067658" class="wp-caption-text">经文处摊位呼吁人们支持台湾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14067653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067653-DSC00641.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067653-DSC00641-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067653" class="wp-caption-text">中华民俗艺术工作坊带来舞蹈表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14067660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067660" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067660-DSC00754.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067660-DSC00754-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067660" class="wp-caption-text">小吃摊位吸引不少人驻足。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">下午1:45，龙舟比赛结束了，而帐篷里的活动气氛刚迎来高潮——“吃饺子大赛”的选手们正在观众的欢呼声中登上舞台。比赛的主持人是前克兰斯顿市（Cranston）市长冯伟杰，参加比赛的人有18名女选手和31名男选手。伴随着冯伟杰激情澎湃的解说，选手们狼吞虎咽，力求在2分钟之内吃下更多的饺子。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">比赛之后，男、女组的冠军分别获得由长荣和华航两家航空公司赞助的“美国－台北”来回机票。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067662-DSC00771.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067662-DSC00771-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067662" class="wp-caption-text">吃饺子大赛，女子组选手们争分夺秒吞咽。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14067663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067663-DSC00801.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067663-DSC00801-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067663" class="wp-caption-text">吃饺子大赛，男子组选手们狼吞虎咽。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14067661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067661" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067661-DSC00755.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067661-DSC00755-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067661" class="wp-caption-text">吃饺子大赛吸引很多观众前来喝采。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14067652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067652-DSC00625.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067652-DSC00625-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067652" class="wp-caption-text">州长麦基（Daniel McKee）向嘉宾和赞助商长荣、华航的代表赠送感谢状。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>政要嘉宾到场欢庆</strong></h3>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“在罗德岛，我们真诚团结台湾社区和亚裔社区，也团结罗德岛州的所有社区。因此在我继续担任罗德岛州长时，我们的关系会非常积极地保持下去。”罗德岛州长麦基（Daniel McKee）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067649-DSC00564.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067649-DSC00564-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067649" class="wp-caption-text">罗德岛州长麦基（Daniel McKee）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">他在龙舟赛开幕式中说，每年的劳动节（Labor Day）周末，他都非常享受这个活动。麦基还谈到，比赛用的龙舟都是由台湾人捐赠的。他指着一条龙舟上的“Forever Friends”字样说：“永远的朋友，我们就是这样，我们是永远的朋友。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067648-DSC00548.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067648-DSC00548-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067648" class="wp-caption-text">波塔基市市长格雷比安（Donald R. Grebien）致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14067651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067651-DSC00586.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067651-DSC00586-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067651" class="wp-caption-text">罗德岛州众议员冯芭安（Barbara Ann Fenton-Fung）、参议员卡诺（Sandra Cano）表示，罗州和台湾将继续增进合作。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天还有多位嘉宾来到现场参与庆祝，包括罗德岛龙舟赛的创始人叶超和吴子平，罗德岛州众议员冯芭安（Barbara Ann Fenton-Fung）、参议员卡诺（Sandra Cano）、波塔基市市长格雷比安（Donald R. Grebien）等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14067650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14067650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067650-DSC00567.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14067650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14067650-DSC00567-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14067650" class="wp-caption-text">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏首次参加罗德岛台湾日，非常高兴。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏也带领办事处多位官员来到现场。今年8月刚到波士顿的廖朝宏说，非常高兴看到现场这么热闹，这么多朋友来参加龙舟赛和庆祝台湾日。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“台湾跟罗德岛的关系，回到远从二、三十年前，我们就建立了非常浓厚的关系。然后我想，最近几年在文化、在经贸上，我们的交流都比以往更多。然后我也当然期待未来我能够带入更多的这个经贸商务活动。”廖朝宏说。◇</p>
