<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>餐桌上的好滋味：奶油柠檬鱼料理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079662-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>奶油柠檬鱼。（日日幸福出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>清清淡淡的鱼块，沾上微酸的奶油柠檬酱，搭配出绝妙好滋味。屋外下着大雨，一墙之隔的屋内却有灿烂阳光。</p>
<h3><strong>材料：</strong></h3>
<p>约3～4人份</p>
<p>鲷鱼片400g、盐1/4茶匙、低筋面粉2～3大匙</p>
<h3><strong>调味酱汁：</strong></h3>
<p>橄榄油15g、无盐奶油15g、低筋面粉2茶匙、柠檬汁1大匙（15cc）、冷水100cc、盐1/4茶匙</p>
<h3><strong>步骤：</strong></h3>
<p>1.鲷鱼片切成大块状，洒上1/4茶匙盐混合均匀，腌渍20分钟。</p>
<p>2.将腌好的鱼块沾上一层薄薄的低筋面粉，拍掉多余的粉。</p>
<p>3.锅中倒入2大匙橄榄油，油热后，放入沾好粉的鱼块。</p>
<p>4.以中小火将鱼块煎至两面金黄色，即可盛起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078577" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078577-03796857b8d408d3c04daaa16c6c6aa6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14078577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078577-03796857b8d408d3c04daaa16c6c6aa6-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078577" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将调味酱汁中的橄榄油及无盐奶油倒入锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078578" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078578-9528947b6e830371d2329bed00b5d31b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14078578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078578-9528947b6e830371d2329bed00b5d31b-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078578" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.奶油溶化后，将低筋面粉加入，用打蛋器混合均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078579" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078579-c42a873e38244e261b93e324c08ed579.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14078579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078579-c42a873e38244e261b93e324c08ed579-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078579" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.柠檬汁及水分2～3次慢慢加入，混合均匀煮沸（全程使用小火，边加边使用打蛋器不停搅拌）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078580" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078580-7dc05c5bf809a750dd3c87906e05369a.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14078580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078580-7dc05c5bf809a750dd3c87906e05369a-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078580" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.最后将盐加入调味。</p>
<p>9.适量的酱汁淋在盘子上，煎好的鱼块放上，一块搭配食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14078581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14078581" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078581-f85c824b4471c302e63da4ef25a94972.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-14078581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14078581-f85c824b4471c302e63da4ef25a94972-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14078581" class="wp-caption-text">（日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>小叮咛</strong>：</span></h2>
<p>1.鲷鱼片可以使用任何白肉鱼代替。</p>
<p>2.奶油柠檬酱汁的咸度，请依照个人喜好斟酌。</p>
<h3>●<strong>工具与材料说明</strong>：</h3>
<h3><strong>常见量匙份量</strong>：</h3>
<p>1大匙（1T）=15cc<br />
1茶（小）匙（1t）=5cc<br />
1/2茶匙（1/2t）=2.5cc<br />
1/4茶匙（1/4t）=1.25cc</p>
<p><strong>奶油：</strong>由牛奶中的脂肪提炼出来，增添料理香气及滑顺口感。奶油分为无盐及有盐两种，在料理中使用都可以；若使用含盐奶油，请自行斟酌配方中添加盐的份量。</p>
