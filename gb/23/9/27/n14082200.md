<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>COVID-19成澳人2022年第三大死因</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月27日讯】<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">（大纪元记者夏楚君澳洲悉尼报导）去年，澳洲的死亡人数比</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2021</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年增加了近</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万人，其中，</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">新冠病毒（中共病毒）成为澳洲人第三大死因，该病毒也成为自</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1970</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年来第一个跻身前五大死因的传染病毒。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">澳洲统计局周三（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">9</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">27</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">日）公布的数据显示，</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2022</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年，每</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">20</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个死亡人数中就有</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">人以上死于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">病毒。在逾</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">19</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">万的死亡人数中， </span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">9,859</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">人直接死于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">病毒，还有</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2,782</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">名人的死亡证明中将</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">列为导致死亡原因。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">数据显示，</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Omicron</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">变种病毒是一种毒性更强的病毒株，去年在澳洲传播期间，它是造成死亡人数激增的主要原因。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">统计局</span><span style="color: #000000;">死亡率统计主任莫兰（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #000000;">Lauren Moran</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">）表示，</span><span style="color: #000000;">“按性别细分， </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US"><span style="color: #000000;">COVID-19</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">是男性死亡的第三大原因（</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">5,484</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">例），女性死亡的第四大原因（</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">4,375</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">例）。死于</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">的人的死亡中位数年龄高达</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">85.8</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">岁”。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">新州和维州死于</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">的人数最多，但该病毒也是每个州和领地的十大死因之一。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2020</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年，</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">病毒是第</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">38</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">位主要死亡原因，而</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2021</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年仅为第</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">33</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">位，去年则跃升了</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">30</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">位，达到第三位。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">除 </span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19 </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">病毒外，其它主要死因保持不变，其中，心脏病位居榜首，其次是痴呆症（包括阿兹海默症）。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">缺血性心脏病导致</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">18,500</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">人死亡，占死亡人数的十分之一，其中</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">60%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">以上是男性。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">痴呆症（包括阿兹海默症）是女性死亡的主要原因，也是男性死亡的第二大原因。 女性死亡人数中痴呆症几乎占三分之二。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">“</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2022</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年的前五位死亡原因是缺血性心脏病、痴呆症（包括阿兹海默症）、</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">COVID-19</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">、脑血管疾病（中风）和肺癌，这些疾病加起来占所有死亡人数的三分之一以上”，莫兰说。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">统计局称，去年自杀是澳洲过早死亡的主要原因，有超过</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">3000</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">人自杀。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">男性约占死亡人数的四分之三，而自杀者的中位数年龄为</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">45.6</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">岁，约为预期寿命中位数的一半。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">澳洲卫生与福利研究所（</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">AIHW</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">）</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">7</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">月份的一份报告称，在过去的</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">50</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">年中，澳洲男性的预期寿命增加了</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">13.7</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">岁（达到</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">81.3</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">岁），女性增加了</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">11.2</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">岁（达到</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">85.4</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">岁）。自</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">20</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">世纪初以来，预期寿命以每年</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">3</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">个月的速度增长。</span></span></p>
