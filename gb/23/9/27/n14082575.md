<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第八届台湾夜市盛大举办 人流创纪录达一万多人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082590-10-600x400.png" />
<h6>民众在台湾夜市前的台湾标志前合影。（大纪元图片）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月28日讯】（大纪元记者易永琦休斯顿报导）第八届台湾夜市9月23日在休斯顿盛大举办。今年首次移至休斯顿基督教大学的停车场举行，场地是以往的2，3倍大，今年来此的游客也创纪录，超过往年，主办方说达到一万多人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14082591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082591-01.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082591-01-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082591" class="wp-caption-text">台湾夜市上熙熙攘攘的人群。 （大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14082592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082592" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082592-02.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082592-02-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082592" class="wp-caption-text">忙碌的摊位。 （大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾夜市从下午三点开始，一直到晚上8点多。现场人潮熙熙攘攘，人头攒动，各个摊位前都聚集着前来品尝美食的人们。现场50多个摊位中，台湾名小吃摊琳琅满目，有传统的车轮饼、蚵仔煎、肉粽、台湾香肠、猪血糕、盐酥鸡、雪花饼、肉羹，还有炸蛋葱油饼、面线汤、咖喱饺、咖喱鱼蛋、炸蘑菇、烤玉米、茶叶蛋、萝卜糕、凉拌面、润饼、营养三明治等，还有西米露、奶茶、各式冰品饮料，价格实惠，数不胜数。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14082594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082594" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082594-04.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082594-04-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082594" class="wp-caption-text">台湾夜市上的车轮饼。 （大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14082597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082597" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082597-07.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082597-07-600x339.png" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082597" class="wp-caption-text">台湾夜市上的营养三明治独具特色。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14082595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082595" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082595-05.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082595-05-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082595" class="wp-caption-text">刚从蒸锅出来的猪血糕。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14082596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082596" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082596-06.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082596-06-600x336.png" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082596" class="wp-caption-text">台湾香肠供不应求。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>休斯顿台湾同乡会、活动主办方代表陈建宾表示，希望藉由台湾夜市，让大休斯顿知道很多台湾人在美国，他们对美国社会做出很多贡献，同时台湾人移民美国，安居乐业，美台双方互助互惠。</p>
<p>12位在美国政商学界，体育和艺术节的知名台湾人照片和简介，安放在活动入口处，让人们了解，展现台湾人对美国社会的贡献。他们中包括半导体公司AMD总裁，波士顿市长，电影导演，美妆时尚设计师，体育明星，科学家等等。政界人物Andrew Yang也到场同乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14082598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082598" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082598-08.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082598-08-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082598" class="wp-caption-text">炸蛋葱油饼香脆爆浆，制作团队相当自豪。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14082599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082599" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082599-09.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082599-09-600x339.png" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082599" class="wp-caption-text">驻休士顿台北办事处也在现场设立摊位。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻休士顿台北经济文化办事处也在现场设立摊位，处长萧伊芳表示，今年和侨教中心一起来向人们宣传台湾，无论是留学或者投资台湾，以及台湾对国际社会的贡献，同时呼吁人们支持台湾参与联合国体系。萧伊芳还表示，现场的摊位数量创纪录，她已经品尝了炸蛋葱油饼，咖喱鱼蛋等等，非常美味。</p>
<p>现场的52个摊位中，有26个食物摊位，其他还有一些非营利社团，通过销售美食小吃，来为社团募款。陈建宾表示，很多是比较小的社团，休斯顿台湾同乡会如同母鸡带着一群小鸡，透过这样的活动，来帮助他们一年里所需的募款。这些社团中不仅有台湾人的社团，还有一个“香港人”社团，出售面线等香港小吃，摊位上装点着支持香港自由民主的小物件，前来光顾香港人摊位，购买小吃的人们络绎不绝，不少人表示“要用行动支持香港人”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14082593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082593" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082593-03.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082593-03-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082593" class="wp-caption-text">现场的舞台上也节目一轮接一轮。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14082600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14082600" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082600-11.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14082600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14082600-11-600x337.png" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14082600" class="wp-caption-text">台湾夜市中有多种多样的游戏摊位，吸引人们的兴趣。（大纪元图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了美食小吃，现场舞台上的节目也一轮接一轮，歌舞，功夫，太极拳，本地政要和代表，休斯顿警察局副局长Ban Tien，哈里斯郡第四区区长Lesley Briones, 西南管理区委员会主席李雄等也登台亮相，祝贺台湾夜市的成功举办。</p>
