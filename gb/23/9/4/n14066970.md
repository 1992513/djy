<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>海葵台风重创屏县农作 蜜枣柠檬香蕉严重受损</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066971-633501-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>屏东县长周春米前往屏北地区勘察农损。（屏东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月04日讯】（大纪元记者简惠敏台湾<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%91%B5.md#1">海葵</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E9%A3%8E.md#1">台风</a>重创屏北地区作物，周春米偕同农粮署及农改场前往九如乡、里港乡及高树乡勘查蜜枣、茄子、柠檬、香蕉等<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E4%BD%9C.md#1">农作</a>物<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%BE%E6%8D%9F.md#1">灾损</a>状况，并请各公所加速农作物受损面积勘查并于系统速报，县府统计后尽速办理灾情确认作业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14066972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14066972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066972-633502.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14066972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066972-633502-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14066972" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>县长周春米前往屏北地区勘察农损。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周春米表示，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E4%BD%9C.md#1">农作</a>受损不是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E9%A3%8E.md#1">台风</a>发生当下的一、二天，还有后续延迟性的损害，今年七月杜苏芮台风，加上苏拉台风、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%91%B5.md#1">海葵</a>台风接连侵台，屏东长达一个半月期间遭遇风雨后突然阳光曝晒又遭水伤，忽冷忽热对作物最为损害，县府将尽快统计<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%BE%E6%8D%9F.md#1">灾损</a>，请求农业部列入天灾全品项现金救助及低利贷款，减轻农民经济负担。</p>
<p>周春米呼吁受灾农民，为加速农损救助时程，请多多利用农委会研发“农产业天然灾害现地照相APP”注册后，拍摄作物受灾照片上传，以利汇整提报农业部办理作物现金救助及低利贷款，协助受损农产业迅速复耕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14066973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14066973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066973-633503.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14066973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066973-633503-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14066973" class="wp-caption-text">屏东县长周春米前往屏北地区勘察农损。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14066974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14066974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066974-633504.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-14066974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14066974-633504-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14066974" class="wp-caption-text">海葵台风重创屏北地区作物，蜜枣、茄子、柠檬、香蕉等农作物严重受损。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
