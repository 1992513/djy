<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【飞向欧洲】法国巴黎罗浮宫 超级大宝藏</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079362-b8e7614c7baafb3386737d60bed5bc6d-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>来到巴黎，罗浮宫是我最想去的地方之一，因为这里是法国国王收藏宝贝的地方！（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>来到巴黎，罗浮宫是我非常想去的地方之一，因为这里是法国国王收藏宝贝的地方！</p>
<p>法国各代国王都有收藏艺术品的习惯，这样一代一代积累下来，全部都集中在罗浮宫，使得至今罗浮宫的藏品达到四十万件之多，所以说罗浮宫绝对是个超级大宝藏。这里也是世界的美术殿堂，要想了解这一次人类文明最完美、最正统的艺术，一定要来这里看一下！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079395-47d4d137cf5b0448dcfbca0e092a580e.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079395-47d4d137cf5b0448dcfbca0e092a580e-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079395" class="wp-caption-text">法国各代国王都有收藏艺术品的习惯，这样一代一代积累下来，全部都集中在罗浮宫。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大家知道这个雕像是谁吗？没错，这是路易十四，法国的太阳王。路易十四高大的骑马雕像树立在广场正中央，就像展示着当年的辉煌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079401" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079401-9c27e2c882c03ac864946363316bd612.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079401-9c27e2c882c03ac864946363316bd612-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079401" class="wp-caption-text">罗浮宫外耸立的高大的路易十四的雕像。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗浮宫是全球游客最多的博物馆，每天都有上万人来这里参观。有些人特意从玻璃金字塔入口进去，所以这个门口经常大排长龙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079396-9c504e59646da5fe3ef73d6f1fbf3adc.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079396-9c504e59646da5fe3ef73d6f1fbf3adc-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079396" class="wp-caption-text">有些人特意从中央的玻璃金字塔入口进去，所以这个门口经常大排长龙。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一进来我们就来到位于一楼的法国雕塑厅。哗！雪白逼真的雕塑，纯净得像天堂，这些美丽的雕像来自太阳王的城堡花园，花园的名字叫马利城堡，所以这个展览厅也叫马利中庭。据说太阳王路易十四要求雕塑品要充满生机和愉悦，所以这里每个雕像都栩栩如生，表情带着微笑，在阳光照射下感觉特别温暖。在另一边，还有一个迷你版的路易十四雕像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079399" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079399-50843765f16271492fa349f28250f089.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079399-50843765f16271492fa349f28250f089-600x443.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079399" class="wp-caption-text">罗浮宫一楼的法国雕塑厅内，雪白逼真的雕塑，纯净得像天堂。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14079400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079400" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079400-083f21674e83de913ffaa8edc1e11d52.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079400-083f21674e83de913ffaa8edc1e11d52-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079400" class="wp-caption-text">这个展览厅叫马利中庭，据说太阳王路易十四要求雕塑品要充满生机和愉悦，所以这里每个雕像都栩栩如生，表情带着微笑，在阳光照射下感觉特别温暖。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14079402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079402" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079402-aee5cbcc71a9a821f3ecdd9464bcd4fc.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079402-aee5cbcc71a9a821f3ecdd9464bcd4fc-600x449.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079402" class="wp-caption-text">路易十四的小型雕像。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗浮宫真的是名不虚传，我刚才进去转了一圈，简直是惊叹得不得了，只能说一个字：“哗！”这回真是没白来了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079427-bfd8cf943e145876033e06281769e926.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079427-bfd8cf943e145876033e06281769e926-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079427" class="wp-caption-text">罗浮宫内精美的收藏品。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实罗浮宫本身就是一件伟大的艺术品，长廊、屋顶都有精美的雕刻作为装饰，显得古朴端庄，精致典雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079428" style="width: 319px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079428-b8e0300d1d751496494d80ec3aa2f272.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14079428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079428-b8e0300d1d751496494d80ec3aa2f272-600x885.jpeg" alt="" width="319" b="471" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079428" class="wp-caption-text">高耸的拱廊和金碧辉煌的天顶壁画，极具皇家气派，气势滂薄。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14079434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079434-6296f23a2cc40e987b41e5f0afc8683c.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079434-6296f23a2cc40e987b41e5f0afc8683c-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079434" class="wp-caption-text">罗浮宫的内部装饰气度非凡。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>置身罗浮宫，感觉自己就像个公主，家里的摆设像这样精美就好了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079398-ade44e56cbcd1f0ffba804ae666178cc.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079398-ade44e56cbcd1f0ffba804ae666178cc-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079398" class="wp-caption-text">罗浮宫富丽堂皇，绝对是个超级大宝藏。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14079429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079429" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079429-cd127f06a961a55028becf2a5be7bc17.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079429-cd127f06a961a55028becf2a5be7bc17-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079429" class="wp-caption-text">罗浮宫的内部装饰非常华丽。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14079430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079430-04960d37907ff8182871fa8776e3ab4d.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079430-04960d37907ff8182871fa8776e3ab4d-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079430" class="wp-caption-text">罗浮宫华丽的房间。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14079431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079431" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079431-7c1ba5075302ec9e75d9e87f53960ff2.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079431-7c1ba5075302ec9e75d9e87f53960ff2-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079431" class="wp-caption-text">罗浮宫内华丽的餐厅。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到绘画廊又是完全不一样的感觉，站在一幅幅巨大的油画面前，我显得如此渺小。有不少画是描述着当时人的生活，在大自然中弹琴唱歌看书，画中人物的衣服色彩鲜艳，质地柔软，和大自然融为一体，真是太美了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079432" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079432-c5a51d462e482af17db6d9259db7ca27.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079432-c5a51d462e482af17db6d9259db7ca27-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079432" class="wp-caption-text">有不少画是描述着当时人的生活，在大自然中弹琴唱歌看书，画中人物的衣服色彩鲜艳，质地柔软，和大自然融为一体。（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>转了一圈，我找到太阳王的画像啦，穿着大袍，拿着权杖，好威风！还有一点，他穿着高跟鞋！所以大家千万别以为高跟鞋是女士们的专利，当年就是因为太阳王路易十四穿高跟鞋太帅了，引发了高跟鞋热潮，整个欧洲的王公贵族都跟着学。你看，这才是真正的带领潮流嘛！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14079435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14079435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079435-2b58a8f80a39958787bb31f177c32118.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14079435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14079435-2b58a8f80a39958787bb31f177c32118-600x390.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14079435" class="wp-caption-text">当年因为太阳王路易十四穿高跟鞋太帅了，引发了高跟鞋热潮，整个欧洲的王公贵族都跟着学！（《飞向欧洲》提供，Rachel Tso摄影）</figcaption></figure>
