<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/23/9/15/n14074108.md#1/1280-10" rel="attachment wp-att-14074141"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-14074141" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074141-1280-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="557" b="557" /></a></span></span></span></p>

<p><span class="HwtZe" lang="zh-TW"><span class="jCAhz ChMk0b"><span class="ryNqvb">当地的美食车将带来各种各样令人垂涎的美味佳肴，准备好大快朵颐吧。</span></span></span></p>
