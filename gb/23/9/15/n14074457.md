<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南投“小黄山风景区”甜根子盛开　银白花海壮观</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074458-635802-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>清水溪景观示范园区的生态池（nina　Chen提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月15日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾南投报导）南投竹山的“小黄山风景区”正迎来一波美丽的景象，原因是甜根子草盛开了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14074460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14074460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074460-635804.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14074460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074460-635804-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14074460" class="wp-caption-text">小黄山风景区甜根子满开了哦（nina　Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在初秋时节，这片区域的河床上出现了白色的海浪，但这不是因为河水暴涨，而是因为甜根子草花期已到，整个河床都被它的美丽景色占据。来到南投竹山的“小黄山风景区”，可以欣赏到这壮观的景象，满山遍野的甜根子草在风中摇曳，令人惊艳！</p>
<figure id="attachment_14074463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14074463" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074463-635807.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14074463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074463-635807-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14074463" class="wp-caption-text">“小黄山风景区”位于南投竹山和云林林内的交界处（nina　Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“小黄山风景区”位于南投竹山和云林林内的交界处，因为清水溪西岸地质呈现两种岩性的交互变化，所以山峰显得险峻雄伟，有如中国名胜黄山一般，因此得名为小黄山。这个风景区拥有多个生态池，甜根子草遍布在整个区域，白茫茫的景色一望无际，秋季的美丽就在这片地方展现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14074464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14074464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074464-635808.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14074464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074464-635808-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14074464" class="wp-caption-text">浪漫的景致（nina　Chen提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小黄山风景特区长期志工蔡君硕表示，清水溪甜根子花季节已经到来，他把赏景路线的杂草割除了，也会在现场担任导览解说服务，希望大家一起维护整洁，把美丽的照片带回家，不留下任何垃圾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14074459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14074459" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074459-635803.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14074459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074459-635803-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14074459" class="wp-caption-text">西螺大桥今年甜根子花不如往年（魏进福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而浊水溪西螺大桥下的甜根子，因为海葵台风的影响，大部分东倒西歪，往年壮观的银白花海，今年恐怕失色不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14074461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14074461" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074461-635805.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-14074461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14074461-635805-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14074461" class="wp-caption-text">海葵台风带来丰沛泥水（魏进福提供）</figcaption></figure>
