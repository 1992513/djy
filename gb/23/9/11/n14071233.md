<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新英格兰台学联迎新 联动两大侨界活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071223-DSC00866-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>9月9日，新英格兰台学联在侨教中心举办迎新派对，同学们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2023年09月11日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨牛顿市报导）秋季学期开始，新英格兰台湾同学会联合会一如既往地为新生举办欢迎派对。今年特别的是，“台湾文化导览”和“台湾音乐祭”这两大侨界活动也在同天、同地举办，使现场更为热闹，同学们的活动更加丰富。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">9月9日中午，台学联在牛顿市（Newton）的侨教中心举办迎新派对，总计有60多名同学报名参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14071225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071225-DSC00876.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071225-DSC00876-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071225" class="wp-caption-text">9月9日，新英格兰台学联在侨教中心举办迎新派对。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14071232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071232-DSC00898.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071232-DSC00898-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071232" class="wp-caption-text">学联为大家准备了丰盛的午餐。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“在这能够找到台湾的温暖，我觉得是非常值得的，”新英格兰地区台学联的会长蔡朗安说，“很荣幸能够在学联这个组织里生活，这是我生活中的一部分。”</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">会长蔡朗安、副会长李承训及学联的其他干部当天都来到侨教中心与同学们交流，也欢迎大家加入学联团体。学联为同学们准备了丰盛的午餐，在餐后还有破冰游戏如宾果、谁是卧底等等。现场气氛非常欢乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14071224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071224-DSC00872.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071224-DSC00872-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071224" class="wp-caption-text">学联干部们与嘉宾们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14071222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071222-DSC00849.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071222-DSC00849-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071222" class="wp-caption-text">学联会长蔡朗安（右）、副会长李承训（左）欢迎同学们到来。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在侨教中心欢迎同学们的嘉宾还包括驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长廖朝宏、教育组组长黄薳玉、科技组组长蔡孟勋、侨教中心主任潘昭荣等等。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“我希望鼓励我们每个同学，在你的学校课业之余，能够多扩展你的人脉，多交朋友，”廖朝宏说，“也可以透过你们来介绍我们台湾，让更多在美国的朋友认识、了解我们的台湾。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_14071218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071218" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071218-DSC00812.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071218-DSC00812-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071218" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长廖朝宏致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14071219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071219-DSC00824.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071219-DSC00824-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071219" class="wp-caption-text">教育组组长黄薳玉致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14071220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071220" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071220-DSC00834.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071220-DSC00834-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071220" class="wp-caption-text">科技组组长蔡孟勋致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14071221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071221-DSC00846.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071221-DSC00846-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071221" class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任潘昭荣致词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">黄薳玉介绍了教育组和新英格兰台学联的历史，并感谢20多年来的历届学联干部为同学们服务。她还鼓励同学们多多参加台湾人和美国主流社会的活动。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">“可能你会结识到你一辈子的良师益友，或是你一辈子的事业伙伴，甚至呢也是你终身的伴侣。”黄薳玉说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14071226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071226-DSC00890.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071226-DSC00890-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071226" class="wp-caption-text">学联往届干部带来了宠物。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14071228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14071228" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071228-DSC00893.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14071228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14071228-DSC00893-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14071228" class="wp-caption-text">台湾龙舟队的邱伟哲对猫咪很感兴趣。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">当天恰逢“台湾文化导览”和“台湾音乐祭”都在侨教中心旁边举办。在学联的迎新派对之后，同学们也纷纷来到户外，为上台演唱的侨胞歌手们喝采。◇</p>
