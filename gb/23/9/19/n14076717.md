<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花莲玉里之光！ 康明义夺全台十大有机米冠军赛稻米达人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076718-636440-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>玉里的稻田，就像一幅画。（康明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月19日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾花莲报导）花莲玉里农友康明义再次以卓越的稻米生产成就，荣膺112（2023）年全台稻米达人冠军赛“全台十大有机米”，为花莲与台湾的农业增添一份光彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076727" style="width: 394px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076727-636485.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-14076727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076727-636485.jpg" alt="" width="394" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076727" class="wp-caption-text">获得全台十大有机米。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076722-636444.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14076722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076722-636444-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076722" class="wp-caption-text">破晓时间，画面非常美丽。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076724-636446.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14076724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076724-636446-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076724" class="wp-caption-text">这次海葵台风前夕。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076723-636445.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14076723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076723-636445-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076723" class="wp-caption-text">早晨云雾随风飘移。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>康明义出色的农业技能，得到这个显着的好成绩。每一天，他都怀着喜悦的心情，尊敬地走访田间，亲眼看到小稻子从茁壮成长，这是他最快乐的时刻，巡田水更是他钟爱的日常。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076719-636441.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14076719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076719-636441-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076719" class="wp-caption-text">看着稻子一天一天长大，心里很踏实。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076721" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076721-636443.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14076721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076721-636443-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076721" class="wp-caption-text">花莲中央山脉丰沛的水，用来灌溉。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076720" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076720-636442.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-14076720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076720-636442-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076720" class="wp-caption-text">插秧时间，跟着花东地区的季节走。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>康明义表示：“自己的工作自己安排，没有要求完成工作压力，因此可以早早结束工作回家。”尽管在工作中没有即完成工作压力，但他坚持要自我督导，踏实耕耘。在用心栽培，按步就班经营管理下，再次取得卓越的成绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14076725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076725" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076725-636447.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14076725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076725-636447-600x749.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076725" class="wp-caption-text">这次参加比赛的米。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14076726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14076726" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076726-636448.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14076726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14076726-636448-600x751.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14076726" class="wp-caption-text">玉溪地区比赛拿到冠军。（康明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三次入围全台十大，康明义农友的成功故事激励着我们，展现了勤奋和坚持不懈的力量。他的成就是花莲人的骄傲，也是台湾农业的榜样。◇</p>
