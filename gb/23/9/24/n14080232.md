<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西螺启用“森活武树园区” 促进低碳与武术文化传承</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080240-637416-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>24日下午隆重剪彩开幕。（县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2023年09月24日讯】（大纪元记者廖俪芬台湾云林报导）邻近云林西螺大桥的“森活武树园区”24日下午正式启用。约1.5公顷园区，原本被倾倒废弃物的闲置空地，经环保局规划改造，成为兼具在地特色与生态教育的公园。园区重要推手李明哲议员表示，园区的目标是提升环境品质，同时培育西螺七崁武术人才。</p>
<figure id="14080238" aria-describedby="caption-14080238" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080238-637414-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080238-637414-450x338.jpg" alt="凤山馆成员。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14080238" class="wp-caption-text">凤山馆成员。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="14080236" aria-describedby="caption-14080236" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080236-637412-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080236-637412-450x338.jpg" alt="云林传统武术教练齐聚一堂。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-14080236" class="wp-caption-text">云林传统武术教练齐聚一堂。（凤山馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14080237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080237" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080237-637413-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14080237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080237-637413-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080237" class="wp-caption-text">传统武术教练钟进权和它制作的狮头。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长张丽善亲临剪彩并强调，气候变迁已成为急迫问题，全球各国积极提出净零排放的目标。云林县将今年定为低碳生活行动年，并积极展开全境植树活动，以应对极端气候和温室气体问题。森活武树园区不仅符合联合国SDGs的永续环境目标，还结合了西螺武术文化，成为环境保护和文化传承的重要基地。</p>
<p>开幕会特别邀请八十岁的传统武术教练钟进权、以及黄应华、王明中，和西螺凤山馆理事长陈建中带领凤山馆成员，一起庆贺这个重要的日子，未来这个园区将成为传统武术的训练场地，以及交流园区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_14080239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080239" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080239-637415-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14080239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080239-637415-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080239" class="wp-caption-text">森活武树园区的生态池。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_14080241" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14080241" style="width: 561px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080241-637417-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-14080241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id14080241-637417-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="561" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14080241" class="wp-caption-text">森活武树园区的新地标。（廖俪芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
