<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南田听海传奇石 山云鸟月图呼之欲出</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%94%B0%E7%9F%B3.md#1">南田石</a>是山与海的奇遇、天雕的杰作。粗犷的沉积岩经年累月的听涛、承受海浪无时无刻磅礡的淘洗，石头上终于浮现出细致的白色纹路、特殊图案。</p>
<p><h2>放眼望去颗颗宝石</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%94%B0%E7%9F%B3.md#1">南田石</a>产于台湾台东县东南岸的达仁乡南田村及南至屏东县旭海一带海岸。这一段辽阔的太平洋海岸清朝时就有南北向阿塱壹古道贯穿，腹地由北而南有达仁溪、塔瓦溪及里仁溪等河川穿过。岸上不是一路平坦，而是有观音鼻及牡丹鼻两处突出海崖，崩解的岩石经海水长年冲蚀下，形成一颗颗鹅卵石，堆叠铺满海岸滩头十里路。放眼望去，沿岸触目都是颗颗滚圆透着灰朴色泽的石头，石叠石，石听海，海连天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5827388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5827388" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1411180833572699-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5827388" class="wp-caption-text">台湾东部阿塱壹古道拥有特殊石头海岸地景，也是南田石出产地。（中央社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>南田旧称“难田”或“烂田”，取谐音定名为南田。有趣的是，烂田因为石头成了宝田。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5827395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5827395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1502060216041858-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5827395" class="wp-caption-text">南田石“大地之美”：蝶舞（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<h2>“南田听海”南田石</h2>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5827404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5827404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1502060214521858-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5827404" class="wp-caption-text">南田石“大地之美”：山崖（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5827418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5827418" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1502060215331858-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5827418" class="wp-caption-text">南田石“大地之美”：花树（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />南田石黝黑坚硬的质地在把握、触摸下却异常细致光滑，沉积岩中的白色石英质，经由潮汐、海浪的不断拍打，崭露头角出了岩表，产生独一无二的纹路。天长地久、经年累月的冲刷淘洗之后，各个石头带上风格洗练的简约图案，有的类似风、云、雪、月；有的形似鸟、兽、虫、鱼；有的则像似植物或人像。想要在每块石头找到完全的图案，就好像在茫茫人海中找寻分身一般。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5827427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5827427" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1502060215461858-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5827427" class="wp-caption-text">南田石“大地之美”：风中林鸟（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5827440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5827440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1502060216221858-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5827440" class="wp-caption-text">南田石“大地之美”：云海（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5827451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5827451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1502060222021858-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5827451" class="wp-caption-text">南田石“大地之美”：月出（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />南田滩上的大小石头有的重达上吨，有的不及吋大。太平洋的海浪日日夜夜撞击这些石头，海浪的天籁撞进了石头的心里，雕出了特殊的纹理。经过了上油处理的南田石，吸油之后的石头展现黑色光泽更能衬托白色清晰的图案纹采。</p>
