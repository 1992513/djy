<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】奶油大蒜薯仔汤</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年08月01日讯】有40多年料理经验，曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨、海城酒楼 （Ocean City）主厨的粤菜大厨梁志生先生，本周为大家带来──奶油大蒜薯仔汤。</p>
<p><h2> 材料：</h2>
<p>大蒜（Leek）：2颗<br />洋葱：1/2个<br />薯仔：1个<br />鸡汤：2杯<br />忌廉（奶油）：1杯<br />香葱（Chives）：少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_5896526" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896526" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311932302768-600x409.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896526" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2> 调味料：</h2>
<p>美斯香料粉（Mace）：1/4茶匙<br />盐、胡椒粉：适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_5896532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896532" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311928432768-600x478.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896532" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2> 做法：</h2>
<p>1.将大蒜、洋葱切碎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5896540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311928462768-600x446.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896540" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将薯仔切成薄片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5896548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311928482768-600x488.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896548" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将切碎好的食材用2汤匙牛油炒香，随后加入薯仔片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5896555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311928512768-600x473.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896555" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.全部拌炒均匀后，加入鸡汤，用中火煮15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5896564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896564" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311928542768-600x504.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896564" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.15分钟后，把汤料放进搅拌机，转Puree键打至绵滑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5896576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896576" style="width: 423px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311928572768.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896576" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.随后加入香葱、忌廉及调味料，转至Pulse，再打至均匀，便可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5896585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896585" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311928592768.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896585" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2> 主厨小技巧：大蒜（Leek）将较老的绿色切除，只留根部及约1吋绿色部分。处理时从中间处切十字，这样大蒜即不会散掉，中间的石砂也容易清洗。</h2>
<figure id="attachment_5896592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5896592" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1507311929022768-600x378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5896592" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>梁师傅小贴士</h2>
<p>?大蒜薯仔汤可以冷冻着吃，最适合炎热夏天享用，若放冷冻柜可以保存久一些。<br />?大蒜（Leek）、香葱（Chives）美斯香料粉（Mace）在西人超市都可买到。</p>
<p>若对食谱制作步骤有疑问，可寄E-mail至大纪元美食组信箱：food.djy@gmail.com询问食谱相关问题。</p>
