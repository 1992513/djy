<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】 韭菜花炒石班球</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年07月10日讯】有40多年料理经验，曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨、海城酒楼 （Ocean City）主厨的粤菜大厨梁志生先生，本周为大家带来──韭菜花炒石班球。 </p>
<p><h2>材料: </h2>
<p>韭菜花：12oz<br />石班肉：10oz<br />红萝卜花：6片<br />姜蒜片：少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_5887026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091950162768-600x347.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887026" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2>调味: </h2>
<p>鸡汤：1/4杯<br />盐：1/4茶匙<br />糖：1/4 茶匙<br />胡椒粉：少许<br />粟粉（玉米粉）：1/2 茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_5887040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091950212768-600x474.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887040" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2> 做法:</h2>
<p>1.韭菜花切除1/4较老的茎部不要，余下的切成4公分长度，冲洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5887053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887053" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091950272768-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887053" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将石班肉切成骨牌型厚片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5887064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887064" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091950342768-600x425.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887064" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.用少许盐、胡椒粉、粟粉拌匀腌味10分钟，最后拌入少许生油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5887079" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887079" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091950392768-600x413.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887079" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.热镬下油一杯，把石班肉泡嫩油（新油）至七成熟后捞起晾油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5887090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887090" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091950462768-600x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887090" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.倒出余油，放入姜蒜片、红萝卜花、韭菜花一起炒一会，加上一汤匙水及少许盐兜匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5887103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091950532768-600x334.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887103" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.最后再把班肉回镬，搅匀调味汁液，轻力快手炒拌均匀， 赞少许绍酒便可上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5887113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5887113" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507091950592768-600x375.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5887113" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2> 主厨小技巧：石斑可选近头部处，口感较好。处理时，从鱼鳍处下刀，贴骨切半；再持刀顺骨头将其切除。</h2>
<p><h2> 梁师傅小贴士 </h2>
<p>?韭菜花味清甜，但炒的时间不要过久，要保持它爽脆口感。<br />?石班肉很快熟，走嫩油可令肉质嫩滑，七成熟捞起后再加调味汁炒匀便熟透了。</p>
<p>若对食谱制作步骤有疑问，可于每周一～四晚上7时～7时15时；每周日下午6时～6时30分于AM1400《希望之声广播电台》的梁厨美食（粤）节目（普同话、粤语皆可）询问或留言给梁志生师傅。或可寄E-mail至大纪元美食组信箱：food.djy@gmail.com询问食谱相关问题。</p>
