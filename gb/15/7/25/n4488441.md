<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】Xo酱西芹炒腰花</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年07月25日讯】有40多年料理经验，曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨、海城酒楼 （Ocean City）主厨的粤菜大厨梁志生先生，本周为大家带来──Xo酱西芹炒腰花。</p>
<p><h2>材料: </h2>
<p>西芹：8oz<br />猪腰：10 oz<br />红萝卜花：2 oz<br />姜、蒜片：各少许<br />Xo酱：1汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_5888598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888598" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241845392768-600x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888598" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2>调味: </h2>
<p>水：1/4杯<br />蚝油：1汤匙<br />黄糖：1/2茶匙<br />麻油、胡椒粉：各少许<br />粟粉（玉米粉）：1/4茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_5888612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888612" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241845422768-600x562.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888612" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2> 做法:</h2>
<p>1.猪腰横切一半开两边，把中间的筋膜切除干净，再划钻石型花纹、切块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5888626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888626" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241845442768-600x398.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888626" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.用一茶匙苏打粉加冷水，把猪腰放进捞匀，腌半小时，再冲洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5888637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241845472768-600x387.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888637" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.西芹撕去根，斜刀切成小块； 红萝卜切成小花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5888652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241845502768-600x375.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888652" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.烧一锅水，加入一汤匙姜汁酒，把猪腰放入滚水烫2分钟，待变色、微卷时，便捞起晾干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5888668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888668" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241845532768-600x365.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888668" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.热镬下油，放入姜、蒜片爆香，加入西芹、红萝卜花及少许水、 盐翻炒一会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5888683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241845562768-600x414.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888683" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.再加入Xo酱、猪腰炒匀，一边倒入调匀的调味汁，一边用镬铲快手翻炒均匀便可上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5888695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241845582768-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888695" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2> 主厨小技巧：处理猪腰筋膜时，用小刀贴于筋膜于肉间隙处，以微微斜度下刀，但刀面仍持水平状。</h2>
<figure id="attachment_5888707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5888707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://www.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507241846012768-600x381.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5888707" class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2> 梁师傅小贴士</h2>
<p>?猪腰要彻底清除里面的红色筋膜，用苏打粉加冷开水腌半小时再冲洗干净，这样可以除掉异味。<br />?猪腰经姜汁酒烫过后可以增香味，再加入Xo酱令菜式更惹味好吃。</p>
<p>若对食谱制作步骤有疑问，可于每周五晚上7时20分～8时；每周日下午6时20分～7时于AM1400《希望之声广播电台》的梁厨美食（粤）节目（普同话、粤语皆可）询问或留言给梁志生师傅。或可寄E-mail至大纪元美食组信箱：food.djy@gmail.com询问食谱相关问题。</p>
