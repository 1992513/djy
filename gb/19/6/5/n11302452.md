<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>1款衬衫 穿出6种时尚风格的穿搭术</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_33_124-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本女孩认证最有感的时尚穿搭扩充术。（图/时报出版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>与其花钱买新衣，不如在旧衣中穿出新意！想要营造时尚氛围，穿法上的变化、颜色搭配、配件、发型、体态与姿势等各种要素所构成的“整体比例”，会比“穿什么”更重要，换言之，关键在于“该怎么穿”。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;">基本的衬衫</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_11304554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11304554" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_101.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11304554 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_101-450x644.jpg" alt="衬衫" width="450" b="644" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11304554" class="wp-caption-text">基本款衬衫。（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">棉质衬衫</span></strong>：一年四季皆可穿，是非常实用的单品。可以当外套、当披肩，也可以直接穿或系在腰上，当毛衣或大衣的内搭也很好看，这种万用性质正是衬衫迷人的地方。袖子松松地挽起还能增添女人味。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>牛仔衬衫：</strong></span>不易皱，是旅行的好帮手。不过打扮起来容易偏中性，所以穿法和首饰要女性化些。一年四季皆可穿。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">白衬衫：</span></strong>最简约大方，却也因此不易挑选、难以穿出自我风格。建议挑不同材质营造不同的感觉。顺带一提，我的居家服就是无印的水洗衬衫。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">亚麻衬衫：：</span></strong>麻料吸湿、快干、触感冰凉，最适合酷热的夏天。亚麻是愈用愈有味道的材质，我每年都很期待添购一、两件。介意皱褶的人不妨用熨斗稍微烫一下。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">蓝衬衫：</span></strong>蓝衬衫不但实穿百搭，而且本身就很好看，与所有基础色都能搭配，容易穿出自我风格。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">格子衬衫：</span></strong>格子衬衫看起来很像小朋友，但只要选黑色就能给人成熟的印象。在穿搭上可以用黑白色系简约俐落地统一，也可以用鲜艳的色彩点缀，打造出成熟中带点可爱的风格。</p>

<h3><span style="color: #339966;">6种时尚风格的穿搭术</span></h3>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">1.简单配色加上有亮泽感的红，让寻常的打扮变得与众不同</span></strong></p>
<p>小部分用红色点缀，搭配具有垂坠感的罩衫与长裤，便宜服饰也能摇身一变为晚宴服。链子包斜斜地背更好看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11304789" style="width: 322px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_35-1_122.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11304789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_35-1_122.jpg" alt="" width="322" b="709" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11304789" class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">2.用金属光泽的高跟鞋增添俐落感与通透感</span></strong></p>
<p>系了皮带的长裤配上格子衬衫，率性又自然。点缀用的蓝色披肩非常适合与黑白色系搭配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11304790" style="width: 405px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_35-2_123.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11304790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_35-2_123.jpg" alt="" width="405" b="678" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11304790" class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">3.偏黄的暖色系用白衬衫营造清新感</span></strong></p>
<p>鲜艳亮眼的黄很适合搭配同色系的焦糖色与驼色。腰部系上皮带，可将造型收束起来、增添俐落感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11304624" style="width: 345px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_36-1_109.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11304624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_36-1_109.jpg" alt="" width="345" b="653" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11304624" class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">4.混搭颜色与材质的成熟休闲风</span></strong></p>
<p>蓝色与粉红色的配色成熟可爱且令人过目难忘。包包与鞋子以大地色统一，调节视觉比例。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11304638" style="width: 375px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_36-2_110.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11304638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_36-2_110.jpg" alt="" width="375" b="649" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11304638" class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>5.将传统的格子衬衫穿出浑然天成的女人味</strong></span></p>
<p>休闲的法兰绒格子衬衫，配上窄裙与高跟鞋营造俐落感。搭深蓝色或棕色都很迷人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11304612" style="width: 332px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_37-1_107.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11304612 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_37-1_107.jpg" alt="" width="332" b="667" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11304612" class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">6.工作裤配垂坠感罩衫增添女人</span><span style="color: #993300;">味</span><span style="color: #993300;">，避免过度阳刚</span></strong></p>
<p>卡其色本身带有灰色，适合与灰色搭配。工装风格的工作裤选版型合身一点的，就能穿得帅气有型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11304617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11304617" style="width: 345px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_37-2_108.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11304617 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190606_shouhui_fashion_37-2_108.jpg" alt="" width="345" b="671" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11304617" class="wp-caption-text">（图/时报出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
