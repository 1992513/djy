<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>白头鹰伤困高速路 机车骑士相救感叹超乖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/motorcyclist-saves-eagle-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff
- 受了轻伤的白头鹰在救命恩人怀里非常温顺。(Courtesy of <a target="_blank" href="https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dandon Miller
```
<h6>受了轻伤的白头鹰在救命恩人怀里非常温顺。(Courtesy of <a target="_blank" href="https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dandon Miller
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Video-link.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10743778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Video-link.jpg" alt="" width="578" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2019年06月04日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳综合报导）白头鹰（bald eagle，又称白头海雕）是美国的国鸟，也是北美最有标志性的鸟类。上个世纪里它们一度成为“濒危”动物，如今数量已有所回升。前不久，一只硕大的白头鹰被困在美东一段高速路上，造成交通拥堵，热爱野生动物的路人没有袖手旁观。</p>
<p>在阵亡将士纪念日到来前的星期六，丹顿·米勒<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dandon Miller</a>）正从费城骑重机车回戈登威尔（Gordonville）的家。前方的车流明显减速，于是米勒将车转向，来到双向高速公路的外侧；看到交通拥堵的原因，他心里一惊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11298137" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/bald-eagle-4.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11298137 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/bald-eagle-4-450x706.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Dandon Miller&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="706" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11298137" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dandon Miller</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“路中间有一只白头鹰”，米勒告诉动物媒体“渡渡鸟”（The Dodo），“有个人在那里，好像推了它一下，看它是否能走开或飞走。而它只是扑扇翅膀，没有离开的意思。”</p>
<p>米勒非常喜欢野生动物，他意识到自己必须出手搭救，同时保证现场每个人的安全。</p>
<p>原来，这只大鸟受了轻伤，飞不起来，不过它脚爪的力道还是足够大，米勒只好脱下他的法兰绒衬衣，包裹在它身上。令他惊讶的是，这只猛禽被包在衬衣里时像个小宝宝那样乖，仿佛心里知道米勒在救助自己。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11298135" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/bald-eagle-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11298135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/bald-eagle-2.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Dandon Miller&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="561" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11298135" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dandon Miller</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>米勒回忆说，“把它抱起来时，它非常镇静，只是人们纷纷开始拍照时它有一点激动，不过我们控制了局面。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11298136" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/bald-eagle-3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11298136 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/bald-eagle-3-450x536.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Dandon Miller&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11298136" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dandon Miller</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>离开公路后，他迅速拨打911报警，最终与当地的野生鸟类康复中心“Tri-State Bird Rescue and Research”取得了联系。警察和救援人员赶到之前，这只近7公斤重的大鸟在米勒怀里静静待了45分钟。</p>
<p>米勒说，时间好像很快就过去了。“它相当温柔，我知道它不会伤害我的。抱着它的时候我没有什么想法，我只是帮它保持冷静，让它知道它是安全的、我不会让它掉在地上。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11298134" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11298134" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/bald-eagle-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11298134 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/bald-eagle-1-450x451.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Dandon Miller&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11298134" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/dandon.miller" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dandon Miller</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>米勒连连惊叹道，“我怀抱那只鸟，它那样平静，真太令人震惊了，真是太棒了。”在场警官也附和表示，“绝对温柔！要知道这可是只捕食性的巨鸟。”专家估计，这只雌性白头鹰在5岁上下，雌鹰一般比雄鹰体型大一些，翼展可以达到约1.8到2.3公尺。</p>
<p>视频中可见，白头鹰被装进纸盒前显得有些紧张，脚爪紧抓米勒的衬衣不肯松开。</p>
<p><center><a loading="lazy" data2-src=""></a><a href="https://d1q5zi9q4mpi9f.cloudfront.net/B3i&#29983/embed/Sn25g8M6Nu4" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center>经过几天治疗，它的情况已明显好转，。该中心的史丹塞尔（Rebecca Stansell）透露，“它一只眼睛受了轻伤，还有点软组织损伤，但没有骨折。我们这里的野生动物兽医将它麻醉后给它做了治疗……我们对它的完全康复很乐观。”</p>
<p><center><a loading="lazy" data2-src=></a><a href="https://d1q5zi9q4mpi9f.cloudfront.net/B3i&#29983?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Ftristatebirdrescue%2Fphotos%2Fa.220780611280312%2F3011762028848809%2F%3Ftype%3D3&amp;width=600&amp;show_text=false&amp;height=450&amp;appId" width="600" b="450"></a></center><center><a loading="lazy" data2-src=></a><a href="https://d1q5zi9q4mpi9f.cloudfront.net/B3i&#29983?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Ftristatebirdrescue%2Fphotos%2Fa.220780611280312%2F3011761435515535%2F%3Ftype%3D3&amp;width=600&amp;show_text=false&amp;height=497&amp;appId" width="600" b="497"></a></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p>对于这次救援义举，米勒很谦虚，“我确信其他人也会赶快行动，不过是啊，我很开心我能把它尽快带离公路。”</p>
<p>救援过程中，米勒那件穿了八年的法兰绒衬衣被鹰爪钩破了好几处，留下了几个大洞。不过他很高兴“物尽其用”，心爱的衬衣由此更添纪念意义，他肯定还会再穿。</p>
<p><strong>更新</strong>：据救援中心6月5日向大纪元透露，这只白头鹰已于4日成功野放。来看下面激动人心的视频。</p>
