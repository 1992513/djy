<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>湾区中半岛特色早午餐吧</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年06月03日讯】文：勤宜?大纪元<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E6%94%BB%E7%95%A5.md#1">湾区美食攻略</a>本次带你吃早午餐，不同价位，风格迥异，一样的美味！</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E6%97%A9%E5%8D%88%E9%A4%90.md#1">硅谷早午餐</a>True Food Kitchen</h2>
<p>一个全美的连锁店，提供丰盛而健康的早午餐，连素食者都很满意。就餐环境宽敞明亮，整洁利落，让人立马生出好感。关键是这家餐馆的理念：食物应该使人愉悦而不是相反，新鲜有机、营养丰富且味道好的餐饮供应是他们的追求。<br />
客人吃下去他家的食品后身心都会感到愉悦，这是他们孜孜以求的目标，也是吸引很多食客前往享受饮食。大厨们积极创意，也有医生和营养学家的参与和贡献，一起为食客准备美味又健康的饮食。除了服务店内的食客，也提供线上订餐、店外的现场饮食制作和服务。店内也时常举办健身活动，喜欢乐活的你，不要错过！<figure id="attachment_11297130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11297130" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/6e311aa092e10b38d1489a3f2294df7c.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/6e311aa092e10b38d1489a3f2294df7c-600x440.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="440" class="size-large wp-image-11297130" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11297130" class="wp-caption-text">特制绿色沙拉。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E6%97%A9%E5%8D%88%E9%A4%90.md#1">硅谷早午餐</a>，True Food Kitchen网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11297131" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11297131" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/Image-5-18-19-at-11.32-AM.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/Image-5-18-19-at-11.32-AM-600x440.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="440" class="size-large wp-image-11297131" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11297131" class="wp-caption-text">True Food Kitchen追求丰盛而健康的早午餐。（硅谷早午餐，True Food Kitchen网站）</figcaption></figure></p>

<h2>硅谷早午餐Three Craft Kitchen &amp; Bar</h2>
<p>有25年经营服务经验的餐吧，由3位对餐饮充满热情的女主人领衔主持。人气很旺，说明被食客认可的程度，虽然时常会需要多一点耐心等候领位。食客对她们家的牛扒、烟熏蘑菇、烟熏三文鱼、小牛排骨、汉堡及沙拉等评价都不错，服务也到位。这家餐吧也提供宴会、聚会等订餐服务。<figure id="attachment_11297132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11297132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/Image-5-18-19-at-3.10-PM.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/Image-5-18-19-at-3.10-PM-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" class="size-large wp-image-11297132" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11297132" class="wp-caption-text">Three Craft Kitchen &amp; Bar 的3位女主人。（硅谷早午餐，Three Craft Kitchen &amp; Bar网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11297135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11297135" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/DSC2990.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/DSC2990-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" class="size-large wp-image-11297135" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11297135" class="wp-caption-text">肉饼和洋葱泥、季节时蔬。（硅谷早午餐，Three Craft Kitchen &amp; Bar网站）</figcaption></figure></p>

<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E6%97%A9%E5%8D%88%E9%A4%90.md#1">湾区早午餐</a>Dad’s Luncheonette</h2>
<p>一家颇具美国简易快餐风格的早午餐馆，主打汉堡、三明治，让喜欢汉堡、三明治的食客吃得心花怒放：汉堡里的肉美味多汁。食客点评是个性价比颇高的舒适小餐馆。除了三明治、汉堡，食客也推荐美味可口的草本沙拉，蔬菜都来自当地的农场，新鲜健康。周一到周三都不营业，周四到周日营业，因为位置有限，去之前最好打个电话确认营业时间和座位。<figure id="attachment_11297136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11297136" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/fb7615dd37fe9e595a4ec91410627cc3.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/fb7615dd37fe9e595a4ec91410627cc3-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" class="size-medium wp-image-11297136" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11297136" class="wp-caption-text">Dad’s Luncheonette三明治。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E6%97%A9%E5%8D%88%E9%A4%90.md#1">湾区早午餐</a>，Dad’s Luncheonette网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11297139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11297139" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/59467628_349232869278279_5749870843241063620_n.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/59467628_349232869278279_5749870843241063620_n-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" class="size-medium wp-image-11297139" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11297139" class="wp-caption-text">Dad’s Luncheonette时常推出令人惊喜的甜点。（湾区早午餐，Dad’s Luncheonette网站）</figcaption></figure></p>
