<figure id="attachment_11658920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua202.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658920 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua202-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658920" class="wp-caption-text">202. 夜里，倩惊恐地从厕所跑出来，大喊：“里面有鬼！”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua203.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658921 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua203-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658921" class="wp-caption-text">203. 从此，她夜里上厕所，都让我陪着她。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua204.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658926 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua204-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658926" class="wp-caption-text">204. 女监做很多手工活，比如制作给死人用的花圈。更多的是做出口外贸的产品。后来我们才知道，那都是违法的。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua205.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658929 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua205-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658929" class="wp-caption-text">205. 我们在班里串出口的珠子画。鲍红丢了一根缝衣针，到处翻找。丢一根针就要扣分，影响减刑。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua206.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658930 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua206-600x426.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="426" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658930" class="wp-caption-text">206. 没有找到缝衣针，鲍红精神崩溃了，后来给送进了精神病队。其实，因为表现好，鲍红已经是女监减刑最快的“劳动改造积极分子”了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua207.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658932 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua207-600x438.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658932" class="wp-caption-text">207. 夜里，我在被子里偷偷写信，记下张燕被害死的详细情况。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua208.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658933 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua208-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658933" class="wp-caption-text">208. 安姐把信藏在胸罩里，她很快就会出监，把消息带出去。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua209.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658936 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua209-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658936" class="wp-caption-text">209. 一天夜里，班里的电门被突然关上了，出了什么事？</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua210.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658938 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua210-600x423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658938" class="wp-caption-text">210. 第二天清晨，盖儿头在厕所告诉我，“又来了一个法轮功！昨天夜里折腾她，她一直喊，所以夜里就把咱们的门给关了。”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua211.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658940 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua211-600x429.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658940" class="wp-caption-text">211. 出了厕所，我就听见通道里有人喊，声音很大。小双看着窗外，背对我说：“这回你还要忍吗？外面又折磨人了，我今天可不管你了……”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658943" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua212.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658943 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua212-600x432.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="432" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658943" class="wp-caption-text">212. 我明白了，从班里冲出去，一边跑一边喊：“不许打人！不许打人！”盖儿头、小双和倩都假装追赶我，盖儿头还故意让自己摔了一跤。后来听说因为我这一喊，她们那天没敢继续折磨人。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua213.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658944 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua213-600x442.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658944" class="wp-caption-text">213. 她们三人因为没有管住我，被郑梅批评了一顿。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua214.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658945 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua214-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658945" class="wp-caption-text">214. 郑梅在门口大声呵斥我，说我管闲事。然后，她用非常小的声音说，王大在监控室盯着呢。我们俩头上，就是监控头。虽然郑梅表情严厉，我心里很高兴，我知道她已经和以前不一样了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11658949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11658949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua215.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11658949 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/wenhua215-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11658949" class="wp-caption-text">215. 几个月后，我终于刑满要出监了，郑梅送我。我对她的关照表示感谢，郑梅说，“我应该感谢你呀，因为你让我懂得很多！”</figcaption></figure>
