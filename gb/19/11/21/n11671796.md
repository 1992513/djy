<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【无鱼坊的摄影心视界】红艳木鱼树上挂</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/6545475f612af0feb03ae92be7974dd4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>落叶乔木，树高可达25公尺。掌状复叶簇生枝端，叶形美观。（郑清海／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年11月22日讯】（大纪元记者郑清海报导）</p>
<p><strong>红红木鱼高高挂</strong><br />
<strong> 初春迎风开小花</strong><br />
<strong> 淡红翠绿掌状叶</strong><br />
<strong> 四时多变展风华</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11671799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11671799" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/c41fe13c17fc697891314fa5540462af.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11671799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/c41fe13c17fc697891314fa5540462af-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11671799" class="wp-caption-text">（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11671800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11671800" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/9abff55eabe9ce61365c7bad6ce8b82d.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11671800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/9abff55eabe9ce61365c7bad6ce8b82d-450x328.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="328"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11671800" class="wp-caption-text">四季各具风采，具有非常高的观赏价值。 （郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>掌叶蘋婆Sterculia foetida Linn. 梧桐科</strong></p>
<p><strong>自然阅读趣：</strong></p>
<p>又称掌裂叶蘋婆，香蘋婆，裂叶蘋婆、假蘋婆、猪屎花。落叶乔木，树高可达 25 公尺。掌状复叶簇生枝端，叶形美观。圆锥花序顶生，开花时具强烈异味如猪屎，让人掩鼻。蓇葖果很像挂在枝头的红色小木鱼，很有趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11671806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11671806" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/d37d44585654390184613df9f0973256.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11671806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/d37d44585654390184613df9f0973256-450x594.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="594"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11671806" class="wp-caption-text">红红木鱼高高挂，初春迎风开小花。（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11671805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11671805" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/91107f51facae6be77dad12c255dacf2.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11671805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/91107f51facae6be77dad12c255dacf2-450x603.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="603"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11671805" class="wp-caption-text">淡红翠绿掌状叶，四时多变展风华。（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>初春淡红色的新叶，夏天翠绿的掌状复叶，秋天时，小花成了拳头大的果实，冬天时叶落枝枯挂满开裂的红果黑子，四季各具风采，具有非常高的观赏价值。因此常被作为行道树及庭园绿荫树，只是开花时会散发一股难闻的猪屎般臭味，有些难闻。紫黑色种子，可生吃或榨油，味道有点像花生。药效：果壳具解热、消散、收敛之效。根能治黄疸病，淋病，叶可治疮伤及皮肤溃疡。◇</p>

<figure id="attachment_11671801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11671801" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/fad13c384ce5c8803129e4d2438d85a6.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11671801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/fad13c384ce5c8803129e4d2438d85a6-450x602.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="602"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11671801" class="wp-caption-text">根能治黄疸病，淋病，叶可治疮伤及皮肤溃疡。（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
