<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】蟹肉南瓜藜麦羹</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年11月17日讯】天气越来越冷了，能喝上一碗热乎乎的汤羹应该是冬日最美的享受。粤菜以煲汤见长，看似简单的汤品，最能体现厨师的真功夫。</p>
<p>今天我们为大家介绍粤菜名厨罗子昭制作的“蟹肉南瓜藜麦羹”，这道菜以南瓜、螃蟹和藜麦为主料。成品汤羹香浓味美，极具滋补养生功效。</p>
<p>南瓜含有丰富的维生素A、B、C、矿物质和磷、钙、镁、锌等元素，因含有丰富的微量元素钴和果胶，被誉为是“最佳美容食品”。</p>
<p>秋天的螃蟹膏肥脂满，蟹肉又是名副其实的高蛋白低脂肪低糖食物，具有清热解毒、养筋活血、通经络、补骨添髓的功效，堪称滋补佳品。</p>
<p>藜麦属于易熟易消化食品，口感独特，有淡淡的坚果清香或者人参香，被联合国粮农组织推荐为最适宜人类的全营养食品。</p>
<p>罗厨介绍说，南瓜和蟹肉一起做羹，除各自独特的味道之外，结合后的味道更加融洽，口味香浓润滑，尤为鲜美。因为两种食材都有对方没有的鲜、甜、香、滑，互补后做出的汤羹堪称完美。</p>
<p>制作高汤是这道菜的关键，需要鸡脚、老鸡、猪肉、猪脚和葱、姜、胡萝卜等配料，慢火熬制2个小时，配料的添加量和添加时间、火候的掌握等都是决定味道的关键，过程中还要不断地捞去浮沫儿和杂质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659350" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/456ad11925a342abd34df2ba4cccad00.jpg"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11659350 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/456ad11925a342abd34df2ba4cccad00-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659350" class="wp-caption-text">“蟹肉南瓜藜麦羹”以南瓜、螃蟹和藜麦为主料，汤羹香浓味美，极具滋补养生功效。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>★蟹肉南瓜藜麦羹（8人份）</p>
<p>主料：螃蟹2只、南瓜2磅、蒸好的藜麦0.5磅。</p>
<p>高汤食材：鸡脚8只、老鸡半只、猪肉2磅、猪脚2只，葱、姜、胡萝卜适量。</p>
<p>调料：油、盐、胡椒粉、白酒、生粉水。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1.?南瓜切小块，蒸20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659405" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1-24.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11659405 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659405" class="wp-caption-text">第一步先把南瓜切块。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将0.5磅蒸好的南瓜打成泥，剩下的1.5磅保留备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/2-18.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/2-18-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659409" class="wp-caption-text">将0.5磅蒸好的南瓜打成泥。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.?藜麦洗净后蒸熟或煮熟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659413" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/3-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/3-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659413" class="wp-caption-text">藜麦洗净后蒸熟。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.?鸡脚去指甲，葱叶切段，姜切丝，猪肉切块，老鸡切块，猪脚切块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659414" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/4-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/4-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659414" class="wp-caption-text">葱叶切段，姜切丝，鸡脚去指甲，猪肉、老鸡及猪脚均切块。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.?制作高汤：<br />
①烧开水，放入鸡脚、老鸡、猪肉和猪脚，煮透洗净，捞出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659418" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-1-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11659418 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-1-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659418" class="wp-caption-text">烧开水后放入鸡脚、老鸡、猪肉和猪脚。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>②用油炸香胡萝卜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659419" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-2-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659419" class="wp-caption-text">炸香胡萝卜。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>③爆香姜、葱，添加煮好的肉品，淋白酒1汤匙，放入滚水12 cups，将肉蒸煮透。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659420" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659420" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-3-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659420" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-3-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659420" class="wp-caption-text">爆香姜、葱，添加煮好的肉品。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>④去除葱叶，过程中需不断捞去浮沫儿和杂质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659424" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-4-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-4-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659424" class="wp-caption-text">去除葱叶。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑤放入炸香的胡萝卜块，加盖慢火炖煮约2小时，至肉质全烂，骨水也完全出来为好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659425" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-5-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-5-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659425" class="wp-caption-text">放入炸香的胡萝卜块。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>⑥将锅内食材全部捞出，仅留汤品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659426" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-6-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-6-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659426" class="wp-caption-text">将锅内食材全部捞出。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将蒸过的南瓜粒、蒸好的藜麦、蟹肉放入高汤，添加5汤匙生粉水，开锅后加入1茶匙胡椒粉和2茶匙盐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11659428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/6-6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/6-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659428" class="wp-caption-text">将蒸过的南瓜粒放入高汤。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11659429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11659429" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/6-2-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11659429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/6-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11659429" class="wp-caption-text">添加5汤匙生粉水。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
