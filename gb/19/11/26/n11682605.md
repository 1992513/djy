<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】瓦罉煀鸡</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>“五月花”号帆船，满载不堪忍受宗教迫害的清教徒，经过66天的颠簸，在北美普列茅斯登陆。饥寒交迫中，印第安人拿出玉米、南瓜、鸡、野鸭，送给新来的移民……</p>
<p>关于<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%9F%E6%81%A9%E8%8A%82.md#1">感恩节</a>的故事，每年这时候都被人们重复地讲述。美国总统林肯将感恩节定为全国的节日，对这个有意义的日子，人们珍爱延续至今。</p>
<p>在美国人的心中，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%9F%E6%81%A9%E8%8A%82.md#1">感恩节</a>比圣诞节还重要，因它的假期长达4天，不仅令人陶醉浸浴于节庆欢愉的气氛中，更是全国性传统习俗中的家庭团聚日，在他乡的家人都会赶回家过节，就好比中国过年的传统习俗。</p>
<p>“感恩节盛餐”是一年中最被重视的一餐，家家户户都会准备丰盛的大餐，其中“烤火鸡”是必备传统佳肴。今年的感恩节，不妨换一道适合华人口味的美食，我们为您推荐罗厨制作的“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%93%A6%E7%BD%89%E7%85%80%E9%B8%A1.md#1">瓦罉煀鸡</a>”。</p>
<p>这道“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%93%A6%E7%BD%89%E7%85%80%E9%B8%A1.md#1">瓦罉煀鸡</a>”所用的鸡是“三黄鸡”。“三黄鸡”因其外貌“三黄”即羽毛黄、爪黄、喙黄的特点而得名，据说，名字还是由朱元璋钦赐的呢。三黄鸡属农户大自然放养，所以经水煮后，肉质嫩滑，皮薄骨软，味道鲜美，营养丰富。</p>
<p>瓦罉本来是指瓦煲，就是最古老的老式陶土砂锅。用瓦煲锅做鸡，能使汤汁料材在锅里与鸡充分混合，味道更鲜美。而长时间慢热也使罉内的鸡得以最大限度地保留其营养价值。</p>
<p>罗厨认为，“品味‘瓦罉煀鸡’能感受到最传统的瓦罉烹调带来的无双美味，这道菜堪称名副其实的‘原汁原味’。”</p>
<p>★瓦罉煀鸡</p>
<p>主料：三黄鸡1只，净重约3.2磅。</p>
<p>辅料：蒜头100克、红葱头200克、葱10根、姜150克、自制葱油3汤匙。</p>
<p>调料：海盐1汤匙、盐焗粉2汤匙、生抽2汤匙。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将鸡洗净沥干水分。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1-44.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-11685685 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1-44-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 红葱头切块，姜切块，葱5根打结、5根切段。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/2-29.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-11685686 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/2-29-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>3. 用葱、香菜擦匀鸡的全身。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/3-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><br />
4. 用盐焗鸡粉和海盐擦匀鸡的全身，包括内腔。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685688" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/4-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><br />
5. 加保鲜膜密闭腌制15分钟到半个小时。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><br />
6. 将鸡用热水烫过，特别是鸡腔里面要用热水反复烫热，然后用厨房专用纸将水分吸干。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/6-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><br />
7. 用生抽将鸡腌制上色。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/7-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><br />
8. 挂起来在户外风干，约1小时即可。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/8-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/8-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 将瓦罉烧热，放2汤匙自制葱油。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/9-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><br />
10. 将红葱头块、姜块放入罉内，然后慢慢煎香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/10-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/10-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 再加葱油1汤匙，放入风干的的鸡，两边煎香后拿出，放入葱结垫底。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/11-19.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/11-19-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
12. 将煎香的鸡放回罉内，加盖。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/12-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/12-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
13. 沿着罉沿撒3瓶盖白酒。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/13-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/13-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
14. 用湿毛巾围罉沿，焗15分钟后，翻面再焗25分钟至熟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/14-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/14-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
15. 把罉边围着的毛巾拿开，打开锅盖。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/15-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11685707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/15-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
16. 取出鸡切件上碟，用罉底余油淋上即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11685710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11685710" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/16-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11685710 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/16-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11685710" class="wp-caption-text">取出鸡。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11685708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11685708" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/16-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11685708 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/16-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11685708" class="wp-caption-text">把做好的鸡切件。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11685709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11685709" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/16-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11685709 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/16-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11685709" class="wp-caption-text">鸡切件上碟，用罉底余油淋上即可。</figcaption></figure>
