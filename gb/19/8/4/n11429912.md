<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>热气球升空 勇闯火鸡城 2019儿童艺术嘉年华登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/797ce4f095e7495d0c306426962be8fc-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>热气球升空 勇闯火鸡城 2019儿童艺术嘉年华热闹登场。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年08月04日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）2019嘉义儿童艺术嘉年华在8月3、4日(周六日)热闹展开！当风力太大、不是单一风向时热气球都不适合升空。但4日老天爷成全好事，藤篮内市长黄敏惠及三位市议员都升空了，启球仪式于上午10时许在制材所大草原顺利完成。</p>
<p>启球仪式完成后在遮阳棚下的40组80位家长及孩童热切渴望的心，完全写在脸上，第1组完成升空后，风力变大了，但见热气球经不起风力的摧残，倾斜度加大，此时工作人员请第2组的人回遮阳棚稍事休息等待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11429915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11429915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/eed883fdd0b2769bf2bef1cf0c5d3994-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11429915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/eed883fdd0b2769bf2bef1cf0c5d3994-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11429915" class="wp-caption-text">熊熊的火柱，市长黄敏惠要让火鸡城起飞了。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11429916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11429916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2fc9cd143dc65de85bcfce20499cef4d-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11429916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/2fc9cd143dc65de85bcfce20499cef4d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11429916" class="wp-caption-text">市长黄敏惠与市议员搭上热气球升空。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11429917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11429917" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/c910804571f4c657f82896b08d79024b-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11429917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/c910804571f4c657f82896b08d79024b-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11429917" class="wp-caption-text">主持人也上热气球作体验。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谈到搭热气球实在不容易，假如你觉得这个风可以放风筝，那风力太大热气球就不能飞了。本次为了安全一次只能搭4个人，在燃烧器加热下使球内的空气成热空气，体积膨胀、密度减少，变轻了，因为热对流的原理(热上冷下)，所以球体外的空气会往上推，才会使热气球升空，为了安全周围系以3条绳子固定，本次算是以绳子固定热气球定点升降的系留体验服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11429918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11429918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/e3dba5cfcaa19b0f21ffbc57e1224bcc-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11429918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/e3dba5cfcaa19b0f21ffbc57e1224bcc-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11429918" class="wp-caption-text">小小朋友被陀螺的高速旋转所吸引。这是闯关游戏的其中之一。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市是木都，对于艺文教育更是重视，暑假就是要带着孩子来参加勇闯火鸡城，尽享儿童艺术嘉年华，一起在制材所看大桧木与火车铁道，坐热气球拍可爱照片。市长黄敏惠4日也带着小朋友坐木马，在广大的制材厂看热气球升空。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11429919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11429919" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/aa38926aa1563f70344e7d03316a3467-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11429919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/aa38926aa1563f70344e7d03316a3467-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11429919" class="wp-caption-text">妈妈带着小兄弟俩也来参与勇闯火鸡城活动。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄敏惠表示，“勇闯火鸡城- 2019儿童艺术嘉年华”是特别为孩子们办的活动，透过闯关让家长与孩子畅游嘉义制材所，在游戏中体验以往嘉义木都的生活与文化，这就是木都2.0的用意。今天我们让现代的热气球、传统的木都结合在一起，传统中有创新，让孩子与家长都有满满的笑容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11429920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11429920" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/84a926e13d58c5ecc82a2302ea7d64a0-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11429920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/84a926e13d58c5ecc82a2302ea7d64a0-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11429920" class="wp-caption-text">拼图闯关。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化局表示，嘉年华主题以“勇闯火鸡城”做为名称，充满冒险精神，整体共打造有“璀璨热气球”、“艺起SHOW TIME”、“全家总动员闯关活动”、“文创市集”、“彩绘种子DIY”等五大主题，让参与的民众有如置身童话故事般的热闹缤纷。</p>
