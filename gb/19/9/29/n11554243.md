<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>连环画：走过劫难（6）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua138-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>连环画：走过劫难（138）（轩远工作室提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #993300;"><em>这个故事的每一细节都是真实的，逼真还原了中国北京的看守所、北京新安女子劳教所、北京女子监狱的场景及发生的事件，只是掩去了人物的真实姓名。</em></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff9900;">五、北京老女监</span><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/19/9/20/n11535637.md#1">接上文</a>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11554259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua132.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554259 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua132-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554259" class="wp-caption-text">132. 从此，我又住在警察的办公室里了。她们让我“锻炼”，其实就是变相的体罚虐待。比如，长时间下蹲，腿都蹲麻蹲肿了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua133.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554261 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua133-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554261" class="wp-caption-text">133. 还要求我练习“马步蹲裆”。鲍红说，“这是多好的锻炼机会啊，我陪着你！”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua134.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554263 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua134-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554263" class="wp-caption-text">134. 督察（监狱设置的监督警察的警察）进来查看，鲍红说：“没事没事，我们锻炼身体呢。”于是督察就走了。当时我正“飞着”，这是一种常见的体罚方法。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554264" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua135.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554264 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua135-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554264" class="wp-caption-text">135. 有时候是拔军姿，全身肌肉必须都绷紧，头必须紧挨墙壁，一会儿人就汗流浃背了。鲍红不在的时候，小双和美娟就对我放松要求，暂时我就可以活动活动，不用把腿绷得太紧。有时小双在门口听走廊的声音：队长来了！</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua136.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554266 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua136-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554266" class="wp-caption-text">136. 然后她就冲上来，赶紧踢我一脚：站好！我明白，她是装给警察看的。警察有时透过门上撕开的纸缝往里面偷窥。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554267" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua137.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554267 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua137-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554267" class="wp-caption-text">137. 每天“拔军姿”下来，美娟都要给我捶腿，一天十几个小时，腿已经僵硬不会动了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554269" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua138.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554269 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua138-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554269" class="wp-caption-text">138. 上厕所时，一蹲下我就睡着了。已经熬得我五天五夜没有睡觉了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554270" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua139.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554270 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua139-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554270" class="wp-caption-text">139. 后来我虚脱倒下了。郝大夫来给我号脉，“心律不齐”，他对范爱华说，“不能再熬她了，容易出危险。”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554272" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua140.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554272 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua140-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554272" class="wp-caption-text">140. 于是范爱华对“包夹”说，让她缓一缓，睡三个小时再叫醒她。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua141.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554273 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua141-600x421.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554273" class="wp-caption-text">141. 我终于睡上了几个小时。然后她们继续熬我。鲍红试探我神智是否清醒，她伸出一个手指问我：这是几？</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua142.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554274 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua142-600x426.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="426" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554274" class="wp-caption-text">142. 恍惚中，前面出现一扇门，我径直走过去，原来是一面墙，我出现幻觉了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554275" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua143.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554275 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua143-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554275" class="wp-caption-text">143. 她们拿针扎我，我忍着不动声色。她们说我“精神出了问题”。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua144.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554278 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua144-600x423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554278" class="wp-caption-text">144. 方竞给我拉到警察的仪容镜前：“你炼法轮功炼出精神病来了！看看你自己，像不像个鬼？”那时我已经几个月没有洗脸洗头了，脸上直掉皮，头发也擀毡了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554280" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua145.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554280 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua145-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554280" class="wp-caption-text">145. 她们要送我到精神病队，我大喊：“我不是精神病，是你们熬的！”郝大夫说：“她这种情况，如果出问题，我们可不能负责。”警察这才作罢。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua146.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554285 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua146-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554285" class="wp-caption-text">146. 不知道她们还会用什么办法折磨我，如果强制我吃精神病的药，我控制不了自己，还不如死了呢。于是我朝墙一头撞去。这下警察害怕了。她们害怕我死，再出现“事故”。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua147.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554286 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua147-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554286" class="wp-caption-text">147. 吴芳是大学生警察，是监狱的心理咨询员。她对我进行了各种心理测试，最后说：“你需要心理矫治。”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua148.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554288 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua148-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554288" class="wp-caption-text">148. 她与范爱华研究出一种“科研成果”，即用心理学的“脱敏”方法整治法轮功学员。她们给我戴高帽子，在地上、墙上写满诽谤侮辱法轮功的话，强拉我去踩踏，称之为给我“脱敏”。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua149.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554290 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua149-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554290" class="wp-caption-text">149. 她们给我脱去外衣，强行把写满法轮功经文的纸条塞进我的内衣。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554293" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua150.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554293 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua150-600x423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554293" class="wp-caption-text">150. 折磨我的同时，警察还对我进行“心理调节”，她们给我增加“放风”时间，称之对我的“特殊优待”。 目的是让我心情平稳地接受“教育转化”。一只野兔子突然从身边跑过，它钻进一个排水口，居然跑出了女监的大墙！</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554300" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua151.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554300 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua151-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554300" class="wp-caption-text">151. 散步的时候，吴芳对我很无奈地说：“你们是有期徒刑，我们警察都是无期徒刑啊！”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11554304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11554304" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua152.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11554304 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua152-600x421.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11554304" class="wp-caption-text">152. 远处，是快要竣工的新女监。吴芳指着说，“新女监设施非常好，是国际标准的现代化监狱。不过，也有不好的地方，警察办公室都有监控了。”我心想：那你们就没有办法在办公室折磨我了。</figcaption></figure>
