<h6>连环画：走过劫难（4）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><em>这个故事的每一细节都是真实的，逼真还原了中国北京的看守所、北京新安女子<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%B3%E6%95%99%E6%89%80.md#1">劳教所</a>、北京女子<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%91%E7%8B%B1.md#1">监狱</a>的场景及发生的事件，只是掩去了人物的真实姓名。</em></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>三、重获自由之后 再次被抓</strong></span></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/19/9/7/n11505871.md#1">接上文</a>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11523200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523200" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua83.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523200 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua83-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523200" class="wp-caption-text">83. 后来她们把我捆在“小号”的床上折磨。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523214" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua84.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523214 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua84-600x421.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523214" class="wp-caption-text">84. 我越来越虚弱，警察怕我死在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%B3%E6%95%99%E6%89%80.md#1">劳教所</a>，提前释放了我。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua85.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523215 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua85-600x393.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523215" class="wp-caption-text">85. 重获自由，我却无家可归了。丈夫和我离婚，儿子也带走了。病危的我躺在出租屋里，百感交集，我和丈夫曾经那么恩爱幸福……</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua86.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523216 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua86-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523216" class="wp-caption-text">86. 同修张燕来了，扶着我炼功。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523218" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua87.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523218 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua87-600x383.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523218" class="wp-caption-text">87. <ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>又一次救了我的命。很快我的身体就康复了，我把儿子也接回身边。翻墙上网，我看到了5号大姐的照片，才知道她的真名，才知道，她已经死在了看守所！而且在看守所里认识的那批外地学员，据说都被统一拉到了东北辽宁，而且都没了音讯！我的心非常非常难受。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua88.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523219 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua88-600x389.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523219" class="wp-caption-text">88. 这种事情，每天还在各地继续发生，而所有的电视报纸都在说谎！迫害还不是最可怕的，最可怕的是，迫害被掩盖、被扭曲，甚至被歌颂！于是我和张燕一起制作真相资料。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua89.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523221 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua89-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523221" class="wp-caption-text">89. 散发资料时，因为小区“红袖标”举报，我和张燕被抓。当时，举报一个<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>学员，能得到500元的“奖励”。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua90.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523222 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua90-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523222" class="wp-caption-text">90. 我们被关进了看守所，分开关在两个监室里。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua91.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523223 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua91-600x343.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523223" class="wp-caption-text">91. 一进监室，我就开始打坐。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua92.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523224 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua92-600x408.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523224" class="wp-caption-text">92. 不允许我自己请律师，但为了显示其“程序正当”，官派律师要给我提供免费的有罪辩护，我拒绝了。后来我们被转到“老七处”，就是北京有名的“半步桥”，专门关押大案、要案及政治犯的地方。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">四、半步桥——“七处”的日子</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11523225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua93.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523225 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua93-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523225" class="wp-caption-text">93. 夜里，七处监舍的门自己就“咚咚”响起来，还有猫头鹰笑，她们说，有冤魂，也可能有人很快就要“上路”了。上路，就是被判死刑者被执行死刑。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523250" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua94.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523250 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua94-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523250" class="wp-caption-text">94. 第二天清晨，果然有人“上路”。通道里，有死刑犯蒙着头、趟着镣子，被押送着从门口经过。因情杀而被判死刑的美娟非常焦虑恐惧，她正在上诉，不知自己能不能免于一死。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua95.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523251 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua95-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523251" class="wp-caption-text">95. 监室里，有人用扑克算命，想算出自己的未来；有人把毛巾上的彩色线拆下来，用牙刷毛刺绣鸳鸯；在生死面前，我再次感到，爱情是多不靠谱啊。我不再忧虑我的未来。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua96.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523254 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua96-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523254" class="wp-caption-text">96. 美娟趟着“死链儿”（判死刑的人戴的脚铐，俗称狗链儿），每次穿换衣服都很困难。我经常帮她穿衣服、宽慰她。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523271" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua97.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523271 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua97-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523271" class="wp-caption-text">97. 她们请笔仙（七处经常玩的一种游戏，与扶乩类似）占卜刑期。一个年轻的经济犯鲍红，算出刑期是12年，她是“职务侵占”进来的。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523272" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua98.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523272 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua98-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523272" class="wp-caption-text">98. 鲍红后来接判，果然是12年。她非常痛苦。为逃避下监，她吃屎，想装疯闯出去，受到警察的奚落。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua99.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523273 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua99-600x802.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523273" class="wp-caption-text">99. 我教绝望的鲍红炼法轮功，巡视的警察在上面警告我们。在老七处，警察是在监室上方巡筒。后来鲍红先下监了。我没想到，她再次与我相遇时，已是另一种面貌了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523274" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua100.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523274 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/wenhua100-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523274" class="wp-caption-text">100. 这些人的欢乐与期盼，有时就是警察从门口小洞里塞进来的一盒残羹剩饭，那是赏赐给她们的。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523303" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/101.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523303 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/101-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523303" class="wp-caption-text">101. 等待判刑下监的人，每天都在狭小的监舍里排着队走步，她们锻炼、减肥，她们还在为以后的日子筹划，她们谈论孩子、丈夫、父母，还有爱情、菜谱和时装。而这些，已经离我很远很远了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523304" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/102.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523304 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/102-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523304" class="wp-caption-text">102. 去法院时，我和张燕被关在一辆囚车里。我们一说话，前面的警察就用电棍电我们，不许我们交流。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11523305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11523305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/103.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11523305 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/103-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11523305" class="wp-caption-text">103. 开庭宣判。我和张燕都被判了四年有期徒刑。罪名是：利用x教组织破坏法律实施。</figcaption></figure>
