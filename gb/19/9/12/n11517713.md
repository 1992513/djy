<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>性能强悍的超级皮卡霍顿Colorado ROX</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年09月13日讯】（大纪元工商记者赵凯编译报导）经过霍顿营销团队和合作伙伴Retro Vehicle Enhancement （RVE）几个月的精心制作，按运动美学概念打造的霍顿Colorado Rox 皮卡在今年6月登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11517720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11517720" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-072-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11517720 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-072-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11517720" class="wp-caption-text">Holden Colorado Rox (Simon Watts/www.bwmedia.co.nz)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“霍顿Colorado Rox 皮卡不是那种普通的概念车，它是一辆可以开上路的超级皮卡。”霍顿新西兰营销经理马克·沃尔(Marc Warr)说。这款车表现出令人印象深刻的车展外观，提供无与伦比的越野能力。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11517721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11517721" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-069-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11517721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-069-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11517721" class="wp-caption-text">Holden Colorado Rox (Simon Watts/www.bwmedia.co.nz)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们希望将我们最强有力的Colorado打造成霍顿设计过的最令人惊叹、最引人注目的机器。”霍顿董事总经理马克·埃博罗(Marc Ebolo)说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11517726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11517726" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-004-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11517726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-004-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11517726" class="wp-caption-text">Holden Colorado Rox (Simon Watts/www.bwmedia.co.nz)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们与合作伙伴RVE合作，花了相当长的时间和相当多的精力将这款概念车变成现实” 埃博罗说。提供改造的RVE公司总部位于奥克兰，成立于50多年前，专门从事售后车辆开发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11517722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11517722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-049-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11517722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-049-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11517722" class="wp-caption-text">Holden Colorado Rox (Simon Watts/www.bwmedia.co.nz)</figcaption></figure>
<p>马克·沃尔说：“这仍然是一辆Colorado，但是经过升级改造后，它已经变成一辆拥有超级尺寸、大胆、耀眼的车。”</p>
<p>Colorado Rox的特点表现在：35英寸的全地形轮胎，为了能够装入这款超大尺寸的轮胎，车悬架抬高4英寸，车身抬高2英寸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11517723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11517723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-032-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11517723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-032-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11517723" class="wp-caption-text">Holden Colorado Rox (Simon Watts/www.bwmedia.co.nz)</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.5吨牵引力，500Nm扭矩。用碳纤维制作的新引擎盖，轻、强度大、实用性强。由于车身离地间隙高，特别定制了在原有踏板下的小台阶。配备车顶置物架，可以放备胎和安装前，侧和后灯。车辆内部升级，配前后RVE运动座椅，并有手工制作的皮革内饰。</p>
<p>霍顿Colorado ROX自6月初亮相以来，已经参加了40多个不同的活动。如在霍克斯湾、惠灵顿、陶波、汉密尔顿和奥克兰的活动，以及各种经销商活动，加上Fieldays和Speed Show的活动，并在Speed Show上获得了“Best Outdoor Site”奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11517724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11517724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-019-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11517724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/Roxwaiuku220819-019-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11517724" class="wp-caption-text">Holden Colorado Rox (Simon Watts/www.bwmedia.co.nz)</figcaption></figure>
<p>9月13日- 15日在奥克兰，霍顿Colorado ROX将参加超级车锦标赛，届时红牛霍顿车队车手、2016年系列赛冠军、新西兰本土车手谢恩·范.吉斯伯根（Shane van Gisbergen）将和7次系列赛冠军、 4次巴瑟斯特冠军杰米·温卡普（Jamie Whincup）在Pukekohe举行的维珍澳大利亚超级车锦标赛中展开激烈的正面交锋。</p>
<p>更多霍顿Colorado ROX信息和活动详情可以访问以下网站:<ahref="https://www.holden.co.nz/cars/colorado/colorado-rox" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.holden.co.nz/cars/colorado/colorado-rox</a></p>
