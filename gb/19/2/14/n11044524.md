<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>骑猪追梦提灯游古道 欢声笑语同庆元宵</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/dfbb135b04c09ff4523a8271f3afa6a1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	在云霄古道代表位置，众人齐聚为“提灯游古道”开场。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月14日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5%E8%8A%82.md#1">元宵节</a>来嘉义市云霄<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E9%81%93.md#1">古道</a>玩，不但可以提灯游古道更可骑猪，2019年元宵节营造出别出心裁的巷弄创意装置<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AF%E4%BC%9A.md#1">灯会</a>，在全长450公尺古道上，建置了六个创意灯饰灯区，创意项目由塑胶花盆制成的人物、座椅，包括108年生肖猪，可供人骑乘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11044530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11044530" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/7678e6f8e78edcbc5a5db7c70938c872.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11044530 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/7678e6f8e78edcbc5a5db7c70938c872-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11044530" class="wp-caption-text">主办单位为灯笼及灯区作介绍，并提灯合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了让<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5%E8%8A%82.md#1">元宵节</a>更具怀旧的气氛，由嘉义市政府民政处、九华山地藏庵、林森里办公室及内安云霄社区共同以云霄<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E9%81%93.md#1">古道</a>为题材，在今年元宵节营造出六个创意灯饰灯区：入口灯区、出口灯区、象征诸事吉祥的灯区、古道爱情灯区、时来运转灯区及告白区等，以上是带偶回家工作室陈威光老师的创意作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11044532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11044532" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/c04fd001fa25dc0dd40dd0a679ec1778.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11044532 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/c04fd001fa25dc0dd40dd0a679ec1778-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11044532" class="wp-caption-text">带偶回家工作室陈威光老师设计的创意造型都很合古城嘉义民风。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云霄古道是古诸罗城三条入城古道之一，当年先民来自福建省漳州云霄县定居此地，因此旧名为“云霄厝”(闽南语为“温蕉厝”)，早年生活清苦，云霄厝的妇女都以剖香脚为业，赚取微薄工资贴补家用。这里曾是南台湾最大的香脚产地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11044534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11044534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/579a37b4b24552566fb4a5bc6b02b938.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11044534 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/579a37b4b24552566fb4a5bc6b02b938-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11044534" class="wp-caption-text">陈威光老师设计的由塑胶花盆制成的灯饰夜晚点灯投射，很吸睛。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>居住云霄古道两侧的乡亲都是地藏王菩萨忠实粉丝，政坛上就出现许多知名的人士如：许世贤博士一门三市长、现任监察院院长张博雅、前副总统萧万长、前行政院院长张俊雄、及李鸿熹教授等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11044536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11044536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/9657c30bdbe081b95ede8a7b6a437752.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11044536 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/9657c30bdbe081b95ede8a7b6a437752-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11044536" class="wp-caption-text">贵宾把玩灯笼。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AF%E4%BC%9A.md#1">灯会</a>的点灯仪式订在2月17日(日)下午7时，邀请市长黄敏惠及地藏庵董事长黄俊森主持点灯祈福后，并带领大家一起提灯、赏灯、游古道，走完全程将致赠纪念品以兹鼓励。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11044537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11044537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/39f2a951923c70ee9f26e222f6f86087.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11044537 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/39f2a951923c70ee9f26e222f6f86087-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11044537" class="wp-caption-text">云霄古道上的壁贴。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为让大家回味传统元宵氛围，地藏庵特别采购500个古早味蜡烛灯笼，2月17日点灯仪式当天下午6:30起，在地藏庵广场发送给参加提灯游古道的民众，送玩为止。另外云霄古道巷弄灯会展期自2月17日至24日，8天的时间，每天晚上7时至9时亮灯，敬请把握赏灯时刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11044538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11044538" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/157931fe1d2f7543d7f499362b965751.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11044538 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/157931fe1d2f7543d7f499362b965751-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11044538" class="wp-caption-text">云霄古道的介绍。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市天后宫等单位在元宵佳节前夕提灯祈安绕境，造访云霄古道巷弄灯会会场，更添加今年巷弄灯会的喜乐气氛。</p>
