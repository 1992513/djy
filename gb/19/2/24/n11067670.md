<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Sunnyvale安心社区 购物就学都便利</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者李欧整理报导）按照往常，湾区第一季的抢房正在开始攀升热度，所以买家手脚要加速了！本周又开始湾区赏房Open House介绍，今年的第一次就来介绍Sunnyvale，这是一个华人喜爱的城市。4个房产的价位都落在两百万附近，适合小家庭居住，不论上班、就学、购物都很方便。</p>
<p>一、219 West Olive Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA 94086<br />
<figure id="attachment_11067731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/899dd00c5772c972797dd96ca2066fed.png" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/899dd00c5772c972797dd96ca2066fed-600x471.png" alt="" width="600" b="471" class="size-large wp-image-11067731"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067731" class="wp-caption-text">硅谷地产：219 West Olive Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA 94086。</figcaption></figure><br />
这个Sunnyvale地中海风格的住宅真的很独特！建于1995年，没有HOA&#8217;s！位置方便，步行可到达Sunnyvale的Downtown Murphy Street、Cal Train、Target，有各种餐馆和咖啡馆，Downtown还有周六农夫市集！<br />
厨房有不銹钢器具和新地板，更新的浴室，和许多订制装潢；家庭间设有温暖的壁炉；房间数也足够满足小家庭的需要。这处房产近期上市价才作更新，从约220万降为200万，是一处可以考虑的温馨美房。<figure id="attachment_11067734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/338892082-0.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/338892082-0-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11067734"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067734" class="wp-caption-text">1图：湾区赏房：亮丽的地中海式房产，位在干净的街区。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11067736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067736" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/02.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/02-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11067736"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067736" class="wp-caption-text">2图：客厅温馨且挑高天花板。（MLS）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>二、990 Ticonderoga Drive, Sunnyvale, CA 94087<br />
<figure id="attachment_11067737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067737" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1533a44e9103703e04ffa1c1b894c71a.png" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1533a44e9103703e04ffa1c1b894c71a-600x476.png" alt="" width="600" b="476" class="size-large wp-image-11067737"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067737" class="wp-caption-text">硅谷地产：990 Ticonderoga Drive, Sunnyvale, CA 94087。</figcaption></figure><br />
虽然建造年份较早，但室内保持如新。位在非常理想的Cherry Chase＆De Anza Park社区，距离Westmoor Village购物中心、De Anza公园（就在对面）和所有科技园区仅几个街区。<br />
厨房和用餐区可直通庭院，订制蜂蜜枫木橱柜、巴西樱桃木地板非常温暖。屋内更新了新的地毯，将给新屋主一个清新的味道。屋外有前后院，后院小天地适合与家人喝茶聊天，也与房间相通；车道可停两辆车。街区干净、安全。<figure id="attachment_11067738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/03-1.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/03-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11067738"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067738" class="wp-caption-text">3图：湾区赏房：享受安心的社区环境。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11067739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067739" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/04.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/04-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11067739"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067739" class="wp-caption-text">4图：厨房有枫木橱柜、樱桃木地板。（MLS）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>三、1569 Benton Street, Sunnyvale, CA 94087<br />
<figure id="attachment_11067740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067740" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/54168ebd3a519b60228a79623d5895f9.png" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/54168ebd3a519b60228a79623d5895f9-600x420.png" alt="" width="600" b="420" class="size-large wp-image-11067740"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067740" class="wp-caption-text">南湾地产：1569 Benton Street, Sunnyvale, CA 94087。</figcaption></figure><br />
这是一个相当便利的街区，大华超市、韩国超市、各种餐厅、学校和医院等都在附近，距离苹果的太空船办公室只有3分钟车程。轻松步行至Laurelwood小学和Raynor公园。<br />
室内有一个大客厅，两层楼5间房间可供多种选择；家庭室面向私人后院，可通往厨房；厨房有双烤箱和充足的存储空间。主卧室套房开放明亮，有硬木地板。近期才更换的炉子、地毯、瓷砖和修补硬木。<figure id="attachment_11067742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/05.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/05-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11067742"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067742" class="wp-caption-text">5图：湾区上市房：前院草坪，车道可停两辆车。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11067743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/06.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/06-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11067743"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067743" class="wp-caption-text">6图：家庭室与厨房、庭院相通。（MLS）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>四、113 Debussy Terrace, Sunnyvale, CA 94087<br />
<figure id="attachment_11067744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067744" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/45cd8368cd29d51347a588a3660b99d6.png" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/45cd8368cd29d51347a588a3660b99d6-600x474.png" alt="" width="600" b="474" class="size-large wp-image-11067744"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067744" class="wp-caption-text">湾区赏房：113 Debussy Terrace, Sunnyvale, CA 94087。</figcaption></figure><br />
8图：狭长型厨房。（MLS）<br />
这是一个2000年兴建的美丽社区，此房位在该街第一户，相对便利。社区公园和Gazebo区提供户外娱乐；附近有购物中心、社区中心；联外道路是Sunnyvale Saratoga Rd，可直通Cupertino。<br />
房子有两层楼，大门是高级的双开门，屋内有大理石地板、硬木地板、高天花板；美食厨房有花岗岩台面，充足的橱柜；超大主人套房设有步入式衣柜，主浴室配有天窗；屋内有中央空调。每月有HOA费用100美元。<figure id="attachment_11067746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/07.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/07-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11067746"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067746" class="wp-caption-text">7图：湾区上市房：两楼美丽社区独立房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11067747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11067747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/08.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/08-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-11067747"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11067747" class="wp-caption-text">8图：狭长型厨房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure></p>
