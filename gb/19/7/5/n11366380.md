<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夏天午餐不用蒸 来个“冷便当”：酪梨拼盘</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-600x400.png" />
<h6>酪梨墨西哥卷饼披萨（左）和酪梨奶昔（右）。（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>便当就是要加热？NO！许多食物/料理常温就能吃！<br />
加热便当才好吃？NO！日本人的便当就从来不加热。不反复加热，营养反而不易流失！<br />
冷便当可以瘦身？YES！冷饭的热量比热饭低，减轻卡路里！</p>
<p>作者金希柍是结婚十年的在职妈妈，育有两名儿子。为了先生健康而开始自制的轻食便当。她的便当以“kongkong便当”之名而为人所知，特色是忙碌的早晨也能火速完成，连色、香、味、营养都十分讲究。</p>
<p><span style="color: #339966;">“活力冷便当”五大特色</span>：</p>
<p>1、打开即食免加热：利用早晨20分钟制作完成，中午免再加热，即可享用美味便当。<br />
2、跳脱传统便当菜色：捏成饭团饭卷、组合成沙拉拼盘，有如餐厅级料理，让人耳目一新。<br />
3、轻松料理少油烟：切一切、拌一拌、卷一卷即完成，极少需要开火，做菜不再满头大汗。<br />
4、原型食物、营养均衡不发胖：一主菜三配菜的设计，少淀粉、高纤高蛋白，营养不发胖。<br />
5、丰盛饱足还能当下午茶：轻食料理却不减饱足感。如果只吃主菜就饱了，副菜也可以作为下午茶点心。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>酪梨奶昔</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">材料</span></strong></p>
<p>?酪梨（处理后80g）1/2 颗<br />
? 香蕉（100g）1 根<br />
? 羽衣甘蓝（包饭用，50g）10 片<br />
? 柠檬汁 1 大匙<br />
? 椰浆（或低脂牛奶，100ml）1/2 杯<br />
? 迷迭香少许</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-1.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11366385 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-1.png" alt="" width="146" b="193" /></a></p>
<p>1.将处理好的酪梨、香蕉和羽衣甘蓝切成一口大小，再将除了迷迭香以外的食材放入调理机中磨碎。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-2.png"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11366387 size-full alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-2.png" alt="" width="139" b="179" /></a></p>
<p>2.装入碗中后，摆上迷迭香。</p>
<p>Tip:椰浆<br />
从椰子果肉中挤出来的液体，大量使用于东南亚料理中，放入奶昔中会增添独特香气。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>酪梨墨西哥卷饼披萨</strong></span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>材料</strong></span></p>
<p>? 酪梨3 颗<br />
? 全麦墨西哥饼皮（直径20 公分）1 张<br />
? 蓝莓10 粒</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-3.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-3.png" alt="" width="134" b="165" /></a></p>
<p>1.竖着拿酪梨，再用刀子深深切入酪梨中，切到果核后转360 度。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-4.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-4.png" alt="" width="138" b="165" /></a></p>
<p>2.扭开酪梨，将它分成两半。将刀刃嵌入果核中，扭转后取出果核。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-5.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-5.png" alt="" width="138" b="174" /><br />
</a>3.将1/2 颗酪梨装入碗中捣碎。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-6.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p168-6.png" alt="" width="139" b="176" /></a></p>
<p>4.将全麦墨西哥卷饼分成披萨装饰18等分，再均匀抹上③的酪梨泥，并用剩下的酪梨做装饰，接着摆上蓝莓。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>披萨装饰1</strong></span></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-1.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-1.png" alt="" width="143" b="185" /></a></p>
<p>1.将1/2 颗酪梨尽量横切成薄片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-2.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-2.png" alt="" width="137" b="175" /></a></p>
<p>2.用手将酪梨展开成一长条。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-3.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-3.png" alt="" width="140" b="179" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170.png" alt="" width="119" b="173" /></a></p>
<p>3.将酪梨往内卷起来， 做出花朵造型。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>披萨装饰2</strong></span></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-5.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-5.png" alt="" width="141" b="182" /><br />
</a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-6.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-6.png" alt="" width="142" b="183" /></a></p>
<p>2.用手稍微压一下再展开。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-7.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-7.png" alt="" width="137" b="179" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-4.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11366434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/p170-4.png" alt="" width="111" b="145" /></a></p>
<p>3.用饼干模具压出造型。<br />
*剩下的酪梨也可以一起加入墨西哥卷饼披萨的步骤③中。@</p>
