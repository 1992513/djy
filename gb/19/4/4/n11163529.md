<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大成漫画：二会促党亡</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>图一<br />
年年开二会，<br />
年年都截访，<br />
它俩双胞胎，<br />
党生蛤蟆养。<br />
你看又来了，<br />
万人上战场，<br />
激怒老百姓，<br />
憎恨共产党。<br />
中共很清楚，<br />
二会促党亡，<br />
去年似疯狗；<br />
今年像豺狼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033027" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033027-2-92.jpeg"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033027-2-92-450x296.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033027" class="wp-caption-text">图二：中共两会期间，无数来自中国各地的民众进京申诉，但截访人员在附近公交车站、高法两侧街道查找截访目标。（大成漫画提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图二<br />
警察打头阵，<br />
武警横挎枪，<br />
城管举根棍，<br />
尖刀插裤裆。<br />
联防满街窜，<br />
见人就搭腔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033029" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033029-3-2.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033029-3-2-450x313.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033029" class="wp-caption-text">图三：中共两会期间，不少访民前往北京维权，遭警察、武警、截访人员驱赶阻挠。（大成漫画提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图三<br />
协警想卧底，<br />
手举小旗晃，<br />
上访坐俺车，<br />
直达豆各庄；<br />
五毛扮访民，<br />
总有人被诓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033030-4-1.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033030-4-1-450x312.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033030" class="wp-caption-text">图四：中共两会期间，不少访民前往北京维权，但遭到一些黑社会人士威胁等。（大成漫画提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图四<br />
街头有暗哨，<br />
警笛哇哇响，<br />
访民堵在家，<br />
门外站流氓；<br />
调来黑社会，<br />
维稳是干将。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033031" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033031" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033031-5-11.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033031-5-11-450x307.jpeg" alt="" width="601" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033031" class="wp-caption-text">图五：中共两会期间，不少访民前往北京维权，遭到便衣警察袭击。（大成漫画提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图五<br />
车站有便衣，<br />
检票看长相，<br />
似乎像访民，<br />
抓错也无妨；<br />
一把揪过来，<br />
啪啪扇耳光，<br />
手铐锁双臂；<br />
全身麻绳绑，<br />
破布塞进嘴，<br />
有话不能讲；<br />
扯腿扔进车，<br />
当夜就过堂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033034" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033034-6-1.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033034-6-1-450x333.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033034" class="wp-caption-text">图六：中共两会期间，为阻拦访民进京，中共维稳升级。（大成漫画提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图六<br />
我说裆中央，<br />
你也太窝囊：<br />
开个破二会，<br />
吓成这熊样；<br />
对民施高压，<br />
不怕有吴广？<br />
自信哪去了？<br />
骗人尽说谎。<br />
心虚直冒汗，<br />
网传擦汗狼，<br />
嘴上耍牛逼，<br />
腿软心里慌。<br />
代表进驻地，<br />
四周铁丝网，<br />
到底谁怕谁？<br />
里外都要防。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033036-7-1.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033036-7-1-450x366.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="488" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033036" class="wp-caption-text">大成漫画图七</figcaption></figure>
<p>图七<br />
老天让党亡，<br />
处处显败象，<br />
经济大滑坡；<br />
鸡屁直下降。<br />
民企在外逃，<br />
国企似夕阳；<br />
商战无胜算，<br />
先倒是银行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033038-8-1.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033038-8-1-450x323.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033038" class="wp-caption-text">大成漫画图八</figcaption></figure>
<p>图八<br />
镇压法轮功，<br />
已经没市场，<br />
百姓都反感；<br />
妄议党中央。<br />
大法传世界，<br />
都骂蛤蟆江，<br />
好人蹲监狱；<br />
遍地是流氓。<br />
酷吏在遭报，<br />
哀乐天天响，<br />
你看公检法：<br />
人人都沮丧，<br />
尖人都蔫退，<br />
只剩二逼狂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033039-9.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033039-9-450x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033039" class="wp-caption-text">大成漫画图九</figcaption></figure>
<p>图九<br />
参与迫害者，<br />
上苍有本账，<br />
罪大神来判，<br />
寿命都不长；<br />
三十啷当岁，<br />
黑发见阎王，<br />
撇下小儿女，<br />
哭爹又喊娘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13033048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13033048" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033048-10.jpeg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-13033048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/id13033048-10-450x323.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13033048" class="wp-caption-text">大成漫画图十</figcaption></figure>
<p>图十<br />
二会尽扯蛋，<br />
真话没人讲，<br />
都是党的人，<br />
配合演双簧；<br />
都知末日到，<br />
来日不方长。<br />
只要能出逃，<br />
哪怕八等舱，<br />
上火满嘴泡，<br />
尿尿都发黄。</p>
