<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>土耳其书展 市长来到法轮功学员展位</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2019年11月份在土耳其梅尔辛市（Mersin）书展上，梅尔辛市市长瓦普﹒塞塞尔（Vahap Se?er，右二）来到法轮功摊位上。（明慧网）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年12月14日讯】11月下旬，土耳其法轮功学员在土耳其梅尔辛市（Mersin）书展上向民众介绍法轮功。包括市长、著名作家、记者、演员在内的各界民众来到展位上，了解法轮功真相，还有人表示要炼功。</p>
<p>明慧网报导，书展开幕的第一天，梅尔辛市长瓦普<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>塞塞尔（Vahap Se?er）和梅齐利（Mezitli）市长尼采<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>塔汉（Ne?et Tarhan）来到法轮功学员的展位。市长塞塞尔表示，他在上一次的活动中也见过法轮功学员，称他们做得很好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722710" style="width: 544px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_03.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-11722710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="544" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722710" class="wp-caption-text">土耳其法轮功学员在书展上向民众介绍法轮功。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>著名作家艾哈迈德<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>乌米特（Ahmet Umit）了解了法轮功真相后，对法轮功很感兴趣。他说：“那些学法炼功的人，改善了身心健康，人们一定会对此感兴趣的。”</p>
<p>法轮功是李洪志先生于1992年在中国传出，教导人们遵循“真、善、忍”原则做好人。来自社会各阶层成千上万的人修炼后身心受益。法轮功至今洪传至世界一百多个国家和地区，受到各族裔民众的赞赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722728" style="width: 493px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_05.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11722728 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_05-600x487.jpg" alt="" width="493" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722728" class="wp-caption-text">著名作家哈迈德﹒乌米特。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作家穆阿兹<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>伊尔米耶<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>希格（Muazzez Ilmiye Cig）说，她以前听说过法轮功。“‘真、善、忍’原则太好了，我一定会仔细阅读资料。”她微笑着凝视着小册子，一再对法轮功学员表示感谢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722734" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_08.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11722734 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_08-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="535" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722734" class="wp-caption-text">作家伊尔米耶希格（右一）。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>梅尔辛国民教育局的分社经理兼作家米卡伊尔<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>埃伦（Mikail Eren）请了法轮功书籍，并高兴地对法轮功学员说：“太好了！”他计划学习功法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722746" style="width: 316px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11722746 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_10-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="316" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722746" class="wp-caption-text">梅尔辛国民教育局分社经理、作家米卡伊尔<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>埃伦（左一）。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>著名记者穆斯塔法<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>巴尔贝（Mustafa Balbay）高兴地举起《转法轮》（法轮功的主要著作）。</p>
<p>至今《转法轮》已被翻译成40多种语言，在全世界发行。1996年1月《转法轮》名列北京市畅销书排行榜中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722751" style="width: 545px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11722751 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_11-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="545" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722751" class="wp-caption-text">著名记者穆斯塔法<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>巴尔贝（左二）。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>著名演员艾利亚斯<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>萨尔曼（?lyas Salman）高兴地从法轮功学员手中接过法轮功真相资料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722775" style="width: 418px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_06-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-11722775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_06-1-600x574.jpg" alt="" width="418" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722775" class="wp-caption-text">著名演员艾利亚斯<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>萨尔曼。（左一）（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈伦<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>贝（Harun Bey）医生曾接触过法轮功，珍藏有《转法轮》，但还没有开始阅读。他说，“‘真、善、忍’是每个人都应该遵循的做人原则。我知道法轮功好，能让人受益。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722723" style="width: 301px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_04.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11722723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_04-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="301" b="535" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722723" class="wp-caption-text">哈伦?贝医生（右一）。（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11722737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722737" style="width: 297px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_07.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11722737 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_07-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="297" b="528" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722737" class="wp-caption-text">图莱护士（左一）说她对法轮功很感兴趣，想学功。</figcaption></figure>
<p>埃尔桑<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>古克森（Ersan G?k?en）说：“法轮功非常好，我阅读过《转法轮》，还在网上阅读过其它经文（法轮功创始人李洪志先生所着），并且在家炼过功。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722742" style="width: 308px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_09.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11722742 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_09-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="308" b="548" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722742" class="wp-caption-text">埃尔桑<span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span>古克森 （左一）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参观书展的内斯利罕（Neslihan）看到法轮功的摊位时非常高兴。 她说大约十五年前她炼过法轮功，还请了《转法轮》。她当时很喜欢炼功，但后来就不炼了。她索要了当地炼功点的信息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11722715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11722715" style="width: 309px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_02.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11722715 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-13-turkey-book-fair_02-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="309" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11722715" class="wp-caption-text">在书展期间市民学炼法轮功功法（明慧网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2003年，法轮功洪传到土耳其，并迅速发展，于2005年成立了土耳其法轮大法协会。</p>
<p>16年来，土耳其的法轮功学员举办了大量的洪法活动，受到人们的欢迎。#</p>
