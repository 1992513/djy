<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>空山祭绝美光节 揭开龙崎山城秘境</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月18日讯】（大纪元记者赖友容台湾台南报导）大型艺术光节活动“2019龙崎光节－空山祭”，21日在台南最小行政区登场，活动期间有艺术家创作座谈、虎形山自然生态导览、手作工作坊、崎聚市集及互动体验，走一趟龙崎深入山城秘境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11730482" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730482" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1fe2301b2748b9c413133755d8b08975.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730482 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1fe2301b2748b9c413133755d8b08975-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730482" class="wp-caption-text">开展记者会于18日在张永旺作品-竹苗的入口意象前进行。（赖友容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“空山祭”以龙崎虎形山公园为基地，展出由7组艺术家与社区居民学生、生活环境互动激荡出的1个动画故事《空空的山》及9件现地创作作品，活动以光虫生命故事为灵感、独树一格的寓言式策展。展期至109年2月2日止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11730484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730484" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4c9b0785bca9164f2f0b501507677943.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730484 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4c9b0785bca9164f2f0b501507677943-450x297.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730484" class="wp-caption-text">龙崎社区居民共同创作作品。 (南市文化局)</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化局长叶泽山表示，龙崎国际级白垩土地形地貌，拥有多样昆虫与鸟的生态，值得保存，当地居民赶走了废弃物掩埋场，可继续与生态共生，将透过光节引领民众了解生态的价值与人的关系，很多故事在其中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11730483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/51673f3ccb0980b3631438f280c44186.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730483 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/51673f3ccb0980b3631438f280c44186-600x352.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730483" class="wp-caption-text">入口步道装设投影灯，光影随风舞动-龙崎之想像生物。 (南市文化局)</figcaption></figure>
<p>空山祭灵魂之作－寓言动画《空空的山》，讲述人们与光虫一起踏上旅程，在山里认识朋友、找到自我与生命意义的故事。</p>
<p>其中《竹苗》拱门诉说龙崎“竹之故乡的历史”；还有当地学童共同创作的《龙崎之想像生物》，透露这段奇幻旅程即将开始。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11730488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730488" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/47ced5004623066bd018467c31a33dca.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730488 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/47ced5004623066bd018467c31a33dca-450x249.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="249" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730488" class="wp-caption-text">游于此处莫忘抬头欣赏光之吊桥，流光宛如银河，与共生价值相互辉映。 (南市文化局)</figcaption></figure>
<p>《流光桥》在夜间如流星闪烁，引领人们进入空山秘境；以在地竹材打造的竹方结构《劳作》，正是光虫首位朋友蚂蚁的窝；《团结织网》以回收塑胶材质与在地居民共织蛛网，象征人民团结；《月夜》的机械鸟鸣，创造真假难辨的超现实幻境；《宁静?秘境》散发出静谧梦幻氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11730767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730767" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/a562766372ef5edb238ae18435c2b668.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730767 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/a562766372ef5edb238ae18435c2b668-600x345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730767" class="wp-caption-text">《团结织网》以回收塑胶材质与在地居民共织蛛网，象征人民团结。（南市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11730485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730485" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2e3e11b083cbd8f4b2c4ac7c6e24c2d0.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730485 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2e3e11b083cbd8f4b2c4ac7c6e24c2d0-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730485" class="wp-caption-text">《宁静?秘境》散发出静谧梦幻氛围。 (南市文化局)</figcaption></figure>
<p>但紧接而来的却是激烈故事转折《乌云来了》，以4处散落的纽泽西护栏及爆裂光虫，交织一幕末日景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11730486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730486" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4096cac5089c7da6ac4e5a749b9d3569.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730486 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4096cac5089c7da6ac4e5a749b9d3569-450x245.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730486" class="wp-caption-text">《乌云来了》，以4处散落的纽泽西护栏及爆裂光虫，交织一幕末日景象。 (南市文化局)</figcaption></figure>
<p>所幸最后作品《共生》以上百只光虫围聚欢庆，让生命故事延续希望，游于此处莫忘抬头欣赏光之吊桥，流光宛如银河，与共生价值相互辉映。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11730491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730491" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ea8e2de4e3b35736105c94ae3ee99a88.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730491 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ea8e2de4e3b35736105c94ae3ee99a88-450x278.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730491" class="wp-caption-text">光虫提灯造型可爱。 (南市文化局)</figcaption></figure>
<p>光节特别为游客设计互动体验活动，游客可于望龙吊桥前服务台领取由艺术家创作的竹编光虫小提灯，与小光虫共同体验这趟旅程（需原处归还）。</p>
<p>此外，游客可在服务台领取竹片，写下心愿及祝福，交由工作人员挂于展区内阶梯步道，让大自然吹动这片心愿彩竹，共同“竹梦”〈萤光效果〉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11730490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11730490" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8468466c76330fbf3657fca9d2734321.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11730490 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8468466c76330fbf3657fca9d2734321-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11730490" class="wp-caption-text">游客可在竹片写下心愿及祝福，将挂于阶梯步道旁，共同“竹梦”。 (南市文化局)</figcaption></figure>
<p>为因应人潮，活动期间假日增开红干线公车班次，“龙崎文衡殿－虎形山公园”间也有免费接驳车（每30分一班），文衡殿停车场有300车位可利用。</p>
<p>此外，文化局提醒游客，虎形山山势如猛虎雄踞，观展动线约2公里，走完全程约需40分钟，途经陡坡斜道、延绵楼梯与吊桥，且展区内夜间昏暗。◇</p>
