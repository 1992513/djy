<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】避风塘炒龙虾</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>圣诞老人的脚步越来越近，那清脆的铃铛声仿佛已在耳边响起，温暖的烛火和醉人的美酒仿佛就在眼前。圣诞节已是全美最大、最热闹的节日，各族裔的人们都沉浸在圣诞的欢乐气氛中。</p>
<p>美食总是节日里的重要角色，火鸡、蔓越梅酱、土豆泥、蛋酒、姜饼都是圣诞节不可或缺的传统食物，香气诱人的菜肴既带来享受，也增添了浓浓的节日气氛。</p>
<p>今天我们为您介绍罗厨制作的避风塘炒龙虾，希望这酥香浓郁、鲜辣香脆的好味道，为您的圣诞大餐增色添味。</p>
<p>在香港粤菜馆的食谱中有一款经典的菜式叫“避风塘菜式”，名声十分响亮。避风塘，其实是当地渔民在飓风时节躲避台风的一个个渔港。</p>
<p>每到台风季节，香港的渔民们就会把船停到避风塘里躲避暴风雨。几天不能出海捕鱼，日子长了，水上渔家开始以特殊的烹调方式来制作新鲜的海鲜食材，把海鲜油炸后入菜，竟有意想不到的美味收获，特别是加上大量炸香蒜蓉的方法，浓浓的蒜香、酥脆的口感，味道十分吸引人，也由此形成了“避风塘”菜肴的风格。</p>
<h2>避风塘炒龙虾</h2>
<p>主料：龙虾1只（1.5磅）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/63b602250e30ea4b742ddc4d0ae3336c.jpg"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/63b602250e30ea4b742ddc4d0ae3336c-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：中芹6根、红、青尖椒各2个（切丝）、蒜头10颗（切蒜米）、吐司2片（切碎）、红菜椒半只（切条）、圆葱2颗（切丝）、西芹两条（切丝）、香菜4条（切段）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c2331742332d056425a589de3bd21967.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c2331742332d056425a589de3bd21967-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>调料：豆豉、自制椒盐、糖、生抽、生粉。</p>
<h2>做法：</h2>
<p>1. 将龙虾洗干净后，去内脏，切件。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-21.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-21-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 在龙虾的刀切面上撒上生粉约2汤匙。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2-15.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 将龙虾用八成油温炸至六成熟时，放入蒜米一同炸香，这时转入文火。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3-13.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 蒜米和龙虾一同炸香后，再把吐司粒放入油中一同炸脆（此时油温要高一些）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4-11.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 炸香后快点捞起，沥干油分。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5-10.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 油锅洗干净，把配料放入锅中，用温火炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/6-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/6-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7. 放入自制椒盐1茶匙、糖半茶匙、生抽1/3汤匙、豆豉2汤匙一同爆香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/7-1-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733690" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/7-1-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/7-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></p>
<p>8.?将炸香的龙虾放入锅中，和配料一起炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8-1-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8-1-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8-2-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733697" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 炒好的龙虾盛盘上桌。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/9-4.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11733698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/9-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
