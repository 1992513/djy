<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>隋棠新戏与柯震东合作 郭子乾伸魔爪很喜感</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413342122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>影集《乩身》正式开拍12月10日在台北举行记者会。图左起为柯震东、隋棠、王柏杰。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年12月10日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）隋棠、柯震东、王柏杰、郭子乾、姚爱寗今天（10日）出席影集《乩身》开拍记者会，隋棠表示柯震东是一个会让她很期待合作的演员，这是吸引力之一，也很喜欢柯之前演出的电影《再见瓦城》。<br />
<figure id="attachment_11713733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11713733" style="width: 385px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413212122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413212122-ss1.jpg" alt="隋棠" width="450" b="599" class="size-full wp-image-11713733" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11713733" class="wp-caption-text">隋棠出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<figure id="attachment_11713735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11713735" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100412392122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100412392122-ss1.jpg" alt="姚爱寗" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11713735" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11713735" class="wp-caption-text">姚爱寗出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<figure id="attachment_11713737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11713737" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413062122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413062122-ss1.jpg" alt="柯震东" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11713737" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11713737" class="wp-caption-text">柯震东出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>剧中由郭子乾饰演的反派角色对隋棠伸出魔爪，但隋棠笑说，虽然影集中气氛相当沉重，不过每次跟郭子乾对戏，“都会想笑场，不要再让我笑了！”</p>
<p>郭子乾从综艺圈转战戏剧圈，首次拍影集就当反派，他表示“毕竟平常都是拍综艺节目，这次难得有戏当然要拍，我饰演一个由善转邪的角色”。而柯震东饰演被迫披罪除魔的男主角，他表示已经5、6年没拍打戏，跟同剧演员对戏很过瘾、很有感觉，大家都非常会演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11713726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11713726" style="width: 384px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413372122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413372122-ss1.jpg" alt="郭子乾、隋棠" width="450" b="599" class="size-full wp-image-11713726" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11713726" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）郭子乾、隋棠出席开拍记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11713728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11713728" style="width: 380px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413302122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413302122-ss1.jpg" alt="柯震东、王柏杰《乩身》" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11713728" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11713728" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）柯震东、王柏杰出席开拍记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该剧改编自作家星子的奇幻作品，已于11月底开拍，这次影集风格跟台湾宫庙文化有关，第一集预算就超过三千万台币，监制庄淳淳直言对影集相当有信心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11713739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11713739" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413472122-ss1.jpg"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1912100413472122-ss1-600x500.jpg" alt="隋棠、柯震东、郭子乾、姚爱审、王柏杰" width="600" b="500" class="size-large wp-image-11713739" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11713739" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）隋棠、柯震东、郭子乾、姚爱寗、王柏杰出席开拍记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
