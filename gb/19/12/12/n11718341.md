<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>诗意之冬——雪菊金英 清香扑鼻</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/03e1d17e8b92191c9ebc1157b321e8a9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月12日讯】（大纪元记者朱孝贞台湾台北报导）秋冬的台湾，各色菊花在凛冬中绽放，虽然菊花没有百花的娇艳，也无令人赞叹的香味，但其清雅、脱俗的气质，自古被称作“花中君子”。</p>
<p>深秋初冬时节，有阳光的温暖天气，非常适合出游赏花。日前台北士林官邸展出约18万盆的菊花，集合了各种大菊、悬崖菊、大立菊、造型菊、大菊、小菊及菊科花草等，不仅争奇斗艳、令人称奇，户外大型造景艺术，更是满庭芬芳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718354" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/d3cccecfe8aa0b63a25a7bec3fa9d944.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718354 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/d3cccecfe8aa0b63a25a7bec3fa9d944-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718354" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸菊花展。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11718345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8c4b99a8f7d2419833f27672bb8b0bbd.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718345 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8c4b99a8f7d2419833f27672bb8b0bbd-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718345" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸菊花展。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>士林官邸菊花展是台北最大菊花展，自2011年第一次展出至今，已迈入第8年。走进官邸正门入口处，右边停放着当年蒋夫人乘坐的礼车展示。旁边展示着多盆单株开了上千朵的菊花，甚是令人惊艳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b381d10dc6d9ea84fb8c834438e9a7ee.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718343 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b381d10dc6d9ea84fb8c834438e9a7ee-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718343" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸千娇百媚菊花展。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11718346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718346" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c5bd303e1806b68d0dbb25471d524302.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718346 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c5bd303e1806b68d0dbb25471d524302-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718346" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸千娇百媚菊花展。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菊花展每一年的单株大立菊都在突破纪录，2004年单株开180朵花，在当时造成轰动，令人兴奋不已。没想到，2010年更突破千朵，又让人啧啧称奇。2015年竟然开了2,430朵，可谓历年之最。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718347" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/650f86cff085e4396edcc78025e61d2c.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718347 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/650f86cff085e4396edcc78025e61d2c-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718347" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸千娇百媚菊花展。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11718348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/9823ac2b6f166a7fe2a19f6eaba384cf.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718348 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/9823ac2b6f166a7fe2a19f6eaba384cf-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718348" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸菊花展中鲜少见到的菊花。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年开得最多的是白鹤品种的菊花，单株的花开了2,042朵，经过23个月的辛苦栽培期而成。单株花开二千朵以上实属不易，非常值得看，真是生平第一次看到这么大盆的菊花。心想明年会开出几朵的花？不觉已开始期待明年的菊花展了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718349" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e8156331b8be546fd6f97e0b73ca1eaf.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718349 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e8156331b8be546fd6f97e0b73ca1eaf-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718349" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸展出难得一见的绿色菊花。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11718357" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718357" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2d74c88a0423ecb244bb6f63047ac584.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718357 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2d74c88a0423ecb244bb6f63047ac584-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718357" class="wp-caption-text">今年开得最多的白鹤品种的菊花，单株的花开了2,042朵。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>往里走，花园里映入眼帘的是高达8公尺高的“公主造型绿雕”，公主的一头长发、衣服、裙摆都是满满的花朵，旁边有一头巨大长颈鹿在弹钢琴，也都是花朵组成，真像是走进卡通世界般的梦幻，看了心情都舒畅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718356" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/9026a766477f92928a0d67b0fffdcac7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718356 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/9026a766477f92928a0d67b0fffdcac7-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718356" class="wp-caption-text">蛋糕造型的菊花展示。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>音乐主题菊花展</h2>
<p>“乐在菊中”的主题，在面积9.28公顷的士林官邸，以多种特色菊花打造音乐世界。现场播放各国名曲，有莫札特“小星星伴奏曲”、周杰伦《爷爷泡的茶》和五月天《派对动物》等共有13首经典国内外名曲。在观赏菊花之美的同时，聆听优美的乐曲，享受扑鼻的花香与芬多精，感受秋高气爽宜人好天气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718350" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/a67dac9272c8e8893225540cd1c6f220.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718350 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/a67dac9272c8e8893225540cd1c6f220-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718350" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸音乐主题菊花展区。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于假日游园人数非常多，想要拍照需要排队等候，等候之时，刚好把握一点点的等候时间空档，专注欣赏不同菊花之美，让等待也变成愉快的时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718351" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/79c14b898dce08f65576b590b4726d78.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718351 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/79c14b898dce08f65576b590b4726d78-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718351" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸音乐主题菊花展区。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>大菊品种展示专区</h2>
<p>还有近百种的大菊品种展示专区，充分展现栽培技术及细心栽培的成果，每朵花硕大得比人的头大；此外，更有学生组的“青春花坛创意大赏”及专业级的“达人杯造园景观竞赛”，这些作品也显示出高手云集的园艺造景成果，也是值得欣赏的一部分；满园各式的造型菊、悬崖菊、菊花绿雕、大菊、中菊、菊科草花及花球，搭配美轮美奂的各国乐曲场景布置，美不胜收，总计展场布置菊花量超过18万盆！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11718355" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/f9c82805db813b477b462e49a8bc53a9.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11718355 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/f9c82805db813b477b462e49a8bc53a9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11718355" class="wp-caption-text">士林官邸菊花展。（朱孝贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在欣赏美丽的菊花，能喝咖啡、蛋卷等美食，真是眼睛看到的、鼻子闻到的、耳朵听到的、口里尝到的以及心灵的感动，真是一趟五感的美妙菊花之旅。明年记得趁早来一场视觉、听觉、嗅觉、味觉的菊花飨宴。</p>
<p>士林官邸原为前总统蒋中正及夫人宋美龄的官邸，记录不少蒋中正家庭的生活点滴。蒋中正逝世后，宋美龄长期旅居美国，士林官邸在1996年对外开放参观。平时官邸里都种玫瑰花，也会有各种花展。◇</p>
