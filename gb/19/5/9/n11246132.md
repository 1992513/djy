<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>柯基犬生宝宝凑一打 饲主开咖啡馆人气爆棚</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-19-600x400.jpg" />///image2=```diff

<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年05月10日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳综合报导）泰国曼谷有间咖啡馆，顾客一进门就会受到12位可爱柯基犬的欢迎，它们会跳上客人膝盖，还会伸出爪子和你握手。咖啡馆有个可爱的名号，叫做“Corgi In The Garden”（花园里的柯基）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11246197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11246197" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11246197 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-1-600x450.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Corgi in the Garden&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11246197" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Corgi in the Garden</a>?Dog Cafe)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11246213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11246213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Corgi-in-the-Garden-15.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11246213 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Corgi-in-the-Garden-15-600x600.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Corgi in the Garden&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11246213" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Corgi in the Garden</a>??Dog Cafe)</figcaption></figure>
<p>咖啡馆开业后，很快就受到游客和当地居民的欢迎，这一点也不奇怪，因为这种“底盘”超稳的狗狗面容含笑，几乎人人见了它们都会嘴角上翘，近几年在泰国人气超高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11246198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11246198" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-2.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11246198 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-2-600x600.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Corgi in the Garden&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11246198" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Corgi in the Garden</a>?Dog Cafe)</figcaption></figure>
<p>店主怎么会想到开一间柯基咖啡馆呢？这还要从她家里的“柯基婴儿潮”说起。</p>
<p>咖啡馆的女主人Tanchanok Kanawaong对柯基犬超级喜欢。“柯基犬既可爱又好玩，我喜欢它们逗人发笑的性格”，她说，“我喜欢大型犬，同时养过10只哈士奇和金毛猎犬。它们都走了之后，我就想找性情差不多、但体型小一点的狗，最终决定养一只柯基小公狗。”</p>
<p>随后，Kanawaong又抱回了一只柯基，然后又说是一只……很快，家里就有了5只柯基。其中两只生下了7个宝宝，这下女主人有了12只柯基，这让她很想“和人分享这份爱和喜悦”，“那时我有了这个想法，想看着它们跑来跑去、散播能量。”</p>
<p>果不其然，顾客见到它们就会微笑，而这些名字叫做猪排、南瓜、三文鱼、豆豆、玉米笋等的狗狗也非常喜欢和人互动，更不用说全天无休的小食伺候！</p>

<p>由于它们都经过训狗师训练，会些简单的小花招，顾客总会赏零食给它们。Kanawaong强调，专业训狗师只用积极的办法训狗。</p>
<p>不过，她也很小心不让狗狗长得太胖，因此每天都会在咖啡馆的花园里带它们跑步锻炼不下三次；咖啡馆的名号也是这样来的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11246235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11246235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Corgi-in-the-Garden-17.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11246235 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Corgi-in-the-Garden-17-600x401.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Corgi in the Garden&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11246235" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Corgi in the Garden</a>?Dog Cafe)</figcaption></figure>

<p>为了保护狗狗健康，咖啡馆的营业时间也比较短，一周开六天，每周二至五都只从中午12点开到下午6点，周六、周日则是早上10点开门。</p>
<p>咖啡馆安排1小时的分段见面时间，每次让50位顾客和狗狗互动1小时，每人收费11美元；但因座位有限（共22张桌子），不接受预订，只按先来后到。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11246209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11246209" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-13.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11246209 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-13-600x400.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Corgi in the Garden&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11246209" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Corgi in the Garden</a>?Dog Cafe)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“每天早上它们都会在后院跑步，然后回来梳毛，为迎接顾客做好准备。”Kanawaong说，“见面时段会间隔1小时，以便它们能够休息”，这样就保证了小动物的午睡时间 。</p>
<p>“我还想说明一点，保持柯基身心健康一直是我们的最大考量，因此我们制定了游戏规则。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11246203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11246203" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-7.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11246203 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/corgi-in-the-garden-dog-cafe-7-600x600.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Corgi in the Garden&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11246203" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/corgiinthegarden/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Corgi in the Garden</a>?Dog Cafe)</figcaption></figure>

<p>柯基犬是起源于英国威尔士的小型牧羊犬，有两个品种，分别是潘布鲁克（Pembroke）和卡迪根（Cardigan）柯基犬。咖啡馆养的是潘布鲁克柯基犬，是人气更高的品种。</p>
<p>据美国彭布罗克威尔士柯基犬俱乐部（Pembroke Welsh Corgi Club of America）的说法，彭布罗克柯基犬的祖先可以追溯到10世纪。有人猜测它们是由维京人带到英国潘布鲁克郡的瑞典瓦汉德犬（Swedish Vallhund，又称原种犬，俗称瑞典柯基犬）繁育出来的，但它们也可能是佛兰芒织工带到威尔士的史奇派克犬（Schipperkes）和博美犬（Pomeranians）的后代。</p>
<p>在科学家科伦（Stanley Coren）的《狗的智商》一书中，潘布鲁克柯基犬的聪明度排在第11名，它们也被认为是出色的工作犬。</p>

<p>除了在亚洲国家，柯基犬在美国也很受欢迎，纽约、波士顿、洛杉矶等大城市每年会举办柯基狗狗大聚会。英女王伊丽莎白二世喜欢柯基犬也是出了名的，曾养过30只纯种柯基，比利时人还得到灵感推出了一部动画片《女王的柯基》（<em><ahref="https://www.imdb.com/title/tt6563576/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">The Queen’s Corgi</a></em>，2019）。</p>
