<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全新豪车亮相：卡城Country Hills奔驰车行发布会</h2>
///image2=
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月08日讯】卡城奔驰车行Mercedes-Benz Country Hills于4月25日晚上6时至9时举办新车发布会，全新一代A级轿车、豪华SUV GLE、G级越野车等新车正式登陆卡城。活动现场约有近500名嘉宾参加。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/DSC_0436-r1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11240705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/DSC_0436-r1.jpg" alt="卡城Country Hills奔驰车行发布会" width="600" b="399" /></a></p>
<p>据车行华人销售Andy Lai介绍，全新一代A级轿车和GLE均搭载了奔驰最先进科技和技术，从外观到内饰都全面升级，彰显出旗舰车型S级般的豪华。奔驰公司表示，两款新车有望成为旗下新一代的畅销车型。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/DSC_0439-r1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11240694" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/DSC_0439-r1.jpg" alt="卡城Country Hills奔驰车行发布会" width="600" b="430" /></a></p>
<p>卡城奔驰车行Mercedes-Benz Country Hills是加拿大最大奔驰车行，拥有丰富库存。车行展示厅停放超过70台新车，呈现奔驰全系豪华车型，包括超跑、SUV、轿车、AMG车系等。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/DSC_0440-r1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11240710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/DSC_0440-r1.jpg" alt="卡城Country Hills奔驰车行发布会" width="600" b="388" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/DSC_0445-r1.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11240711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/DSC_0445-r1.jpg" alt="卡城Country Hills奔驰车行发布会" width="600" b="417" /></a></p>
<p>近期Mercedes-Benz Country Hills车行提供租赁利率（45个月）优惠，从原本的3.99%降至3.49%。目前到货新车数量有限，更多详情请咨询车行。</p>
<p>等候的顾客可在车行内餐厅就餐（自费），车行还提供其他服务包括市内接送、机场接待以及即将运营的SPA屋。车行还提供豪华会议室，可对外免费开放。</p>
