<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新诗：山明水秀暮秋光</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>?</p>
<figure id="attachment_11585709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11585709" style="width: 312px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/75c42058c9535608b48714143ae3cd82.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11585709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/75c42058c9535608b48714143ae3cd82.jpg" alt="" width="312" b="615"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11585709" class="wp-caption-text">山明水碧暮秋光（作者：张松元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>是谁让烟山水濛凝住岁月<br />
看水纹回回如梦<br />
川流桥下溅出悲戚水花<br />
天外飞瀑直下，湍急如雷哗哗<br />
舟帆乘风而来，荡开晨雾<br />
谁因此以苍茫的眼睛试着吟哦这粲然扬波<br />
看溪水邀舞青山</p>
<p>是啊! 秋光飘荡如歌<br />
秋水潺潺相和<br />
溢满欢愉的季节深深深如许<br />
漾开水色音符缝制晨衣<br />
针绣翠绿山峦为晚装<br />
任光影交换颜色<br />
再唱一曲深邃眼眸里隐藏的生命芬芳@</p>
