<h6>连环画：走过劫难（7）（轩远工作室提供）

<p><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">五、北京老女监</span><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/djy/blob/master/gb/19/9/29/n11554243.md#1">接上文</a>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11575681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua153.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575681 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua153-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575681" class="wp-caption-text">153. 一天，女监教育科的警察突然带进一个男人，说他是北京“610”的工作人员，专门转化“顽固的法轮功”。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575685" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua154.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575685 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua154-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575685" class="wp-caption-text">154. 中午，女监请这男人吃饭，“包夹”们也都沾光吃上了鸡腿。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua155.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575689 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua155-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575689" class="wp-caption-text">155. 下午，这男人进来说，“这回让你好好打坐炼功。” 他帮“包夹”把我双盘捆绑上，腿和手脚都绑紧了，他对我说：“这可不是体罚虐待，是帮助你修炼，考验你的盘腿能力。”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua156.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575696 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua156-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575696" class="wp-caption-text">156. 不许我上厕所，方竞拿来一个医院病人用的尿盆。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575700" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua157.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575700 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua157-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575700" class="wp-caption-text">157. 在众多警察面前，我被当众扒下裤子用尿盆小解。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua158.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575707 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua158-600x429.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575707" class="wp-caption-text">158. 为了防止我喊出声，她们用一块抹布堵住我的嘴。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua159.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575708 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua159-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575708" class="wp-caption-text">159. 轮到安姐值班，她就悄悄给我松了松手上的绳子。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua160.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575710 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua160-600x437.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575710" class="wp-caption-text">160. 方竞进来看我。为了显示自己卖力，盖儿头拿了一个凳子压在我的后背取乐。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua161.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575714 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua161-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575714" class="wp-caption-text">161. 方竞说，她从其它监狱学到一招，叫“劈叉”，“和打坐一样，不会造成骨折，因为筋是有弹性的。”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575716" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575716" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua162.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575716 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua162-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575716" class="wp-caption-text">162. 我的腿被她们劈开后，盖儿头坐上我的后背。方竞用脚扒拉我的腿：“不行，要劈成180度！”她们想办法折磨人，那是她们的“工作”，她们也很“辛苦”。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua163.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575719 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua163-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575719" class="wp-caption-text">163. 最后她们都累坏了。方竞对我说：“为了挽救你，法律允许的、不能允许的，我们都做了。”难以喘息的疼痛中，我仰望上天，我知道，神就在上方。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua164.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575722 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua164-600x433.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575722" class="wp-caption-text">164. “劈叉”之后，我只能爬着上厕所了。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">六、新女监</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11575724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua165.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575724 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua165-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575724" class="wp-caption-text">165. 2003年底，整个老女监搬进了新女监。新女监“漂亮得简直就像大学校园”。我和我的“包夹”被转入一个“模范文明”监区，大队长是王庆华，她被叫成“王大”，她几乎总是面带微笑。在新女监的心理咨询室，王大与我谈话，她让我坐在宽大的奶白色沙发上，说，“现在是人性化文明管理了，忘掉过去吧，要向前看……”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575726" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua166.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575726 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua166-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575726" class="wp-caption-text">166. 终于让我正常洗漱睡觉了，我的生活境遇改善了。她们给我剪头，王大亲自叮嘱：“给她好好剪！”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575729" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua167.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575729 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua167-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575729" class="wp-caption-text">167. 王大给我们监室送了小乌龟，说“人文化管理”，让我们养小动物调剂服刑生活，“培养爱心”。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua168.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575731 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua168-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575731" class="wp-caption-text">168. 这个监区重视宣传，通道里，画了很多歌颂警察的卡通画。犯人管王庆华叫王妈。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11575747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11575747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua169.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-11575747 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua169-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11575747" class="wp-caption-text">169. 过年前排练联欢会节目，她们拉我一起扭秧歌。</figcaption></figure>
