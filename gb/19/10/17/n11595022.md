<h6>连环画：走过劫难（8）（轩远工作室提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #993300;"><em>这个故事的每一细节都是真实的，逼真还原了中国北京的看守所、北京新安女子劳教所、<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E4%BA%AC%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%90%E7%9B%91%E7%8B%B1.md#1">北京女子监狱</a>的场景及发生的事件，只是掩去了人物的真实姓名。</em></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #5c0606;">六、新女监（<a style="color: #5c0606;" href="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/19/10/8/n11575626.md#1">接上文</a>）</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11595050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595050" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua177.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595050 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua177-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595050" class="wp-caption-text">177. 已经是深夜了，警察还是不许大家睡觉，让全班陪我“反思”。美娟熬不住，血压升高高，一下就晕倒了。倩说：“都是你！你太自私了！”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595057" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua178.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595057 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua178-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595057" class="wp-caption-text">178. 早饭发一个鸡蛋，我偷偷把自己的鸡蛋给了美娟。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595060" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua179.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595060 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua179-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595060" class="wp-caption-text">179. 监区要求<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>学员必须学太极拳，说可以强身健体。我说，“我的病是炼法轮功炼好的，太极拳能治病吗？我不学。”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595065" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua180.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595065 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua180-600x435.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595065" class="wp-caption-text">180. 全体必须听关于进化论的讲座，警察坐在后面。大家都要鼓掌，我不鼓掌。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595067" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua181.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595067 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua181-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595067" class="wp-caption-text">181. 我对郑梅说：“我不认为我是猴子变的。”</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595073" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua182.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595073 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua182-600x462.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="462" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595073" class="wp-caption-text">182. 于是，他们在班里给我一遍遍看“反对迷信，相信科学”的录像。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595075" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595075" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua183.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595075 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua183-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595075" class="wp-caption-text">183. 监区排练“千手观音”的舞蹈，安姐硬拉我上场。我不去。我知道，我一上场，她们就会拍照，对外宣传我认罪了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595077" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua184.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595077 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua184-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595077" class="wp-caption-text">184. “三八”妇女节，王大用自己的奖金给大家发小礼物，很多犯人感动得热泪盈眶。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua185.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595080 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua185-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595080" class="wp-caption-text">185. 为表示其“人性化”管理，王大给我安排了家人的电话接见。我终于见到了儿子和外地赶来的老母亲。我对母亲说，“我的好朋友张燕不是病死的……”警察马上按下我的电话，不许我再说。我的接见被取消了。说我违反规定，“泄露监管机密”。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595081" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595081" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua186.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595081 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua186-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595081" class="wp-caption-text">186. 郑梅带着包夹在库房批斗我。我被说成“没有亲情”、“有人格缺陷”。倩甚至哭了起来。她说我太“自私”了、“没有人性”，她说她替我家人难过。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595082" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua187.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595082 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua187-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595082" class="wp-caption-text">187. 我给大家讲，我曾经有一个美满幸福的家庭，正是中共对<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>的迫害，才导致我的家庭破裂。我还说出张燕的死，我问她们：“你们为了减刑、立功，就不择手段虐待我们，到底是谁没有人性呢？”大家都不说话了，郑梅也不说话了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595083" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua188.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595083 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua188-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595083" class="wp-caption-text">188. 夜里，鲍红惊恐地大喊，她又做噩梦了，她已经多次做噩梦了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595085" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595085" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua189.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595085 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua189-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595085" class="wp-caption-text">189. 第二天，在厕所里，鲍红哭着告诉我，她受不了了，夜里总是梦见张燕站在她眼前。她知道自己做了坏事，她还说了一些张燕被打死的详细情况。那时，新女监只有厕所没有监控。包夹与我说真话，都在厕所。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595087" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua190.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595087 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua190-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595087" class="wp-caption-text">190. 我偷偷写了一封控告信，控告警察指使服刑人员虐待张燕致死。小双假装没看见。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua191.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595089 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua191-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595089" class="wp-caption-text">191. 我找机会，想将控告信投入筒道里的举报箱。但举报信根本就塞不进去。原来，那都是摆设，上面盖的塑料花，把信箱口都堵死了。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595091" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua192.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595091 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua192-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595091" class="wp-caption-text">192. 郑梅找我谈话。她告诉我，女监不可能让我把信传出去的。她还小声说，原来她也以为张燕是病死的。余光中我发现，王大在门口偷听呢。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11595093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua193.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595093 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua193-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595093" class="wp-caption-text">193. 在图书室里，他们给我上佛教课。警察想用佛教“转化”我，让我信佛教。</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_11595097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11595097" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua195.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11595097 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/wenhua195-600x437.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11595097" class="wp-caption-text">195. 在厕所里，我劝鲍红，不要相信她们说的所谓“佛教”，那些保佑你发财的“佛教”不会保佑你，反而会招来不好的东西。</figcaption></figure>
