<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：法轮功纽约布鲁克林游行  震撼人心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/LD70759.jpg" class="" data-thumbnail="true" />
<h6></h6>
