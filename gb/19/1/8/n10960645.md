<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>墙壁施工有顺序 更需要耐心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46526242_10205311481183304_7544193910365487104_n-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这是第一道手续，抹石膏泥。（建筑装修交流版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月08日讯】文：朱建冲?加州建筑检验师<br />
美加的住宅，独立屋（Single Family）几乎百分之百九十以上都是木造，屋内防火灾用石膏板（Sheets Rock）来作隔间，通常用5/8吋石膏片，叫做One Hour Fire Rate。</p>
<p>石膏片与石膏片的接驳，是用纸条加上石膏泥来抹平，这个叫做Finish Work，从level 3到Level 4或是5，因为石膏泥含有水分，湿的，第一道之后，须要等待3天以上，许多Contractor使用暖气机哄干，或是除湿机干燥！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10960695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46526242_10205311481183304_7544193910365487104_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-10960695 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46526242_10205311481183304_7544193910365487104_n-600x291.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="291" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960695" class="wp-caption-text">这是第一道手续，抹石膏泥。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>外墙水泥（Stucco）也是须要等待，第一道到第二道手续，等24小时；第二道到第三道手续要等待7天！程序上贪快，外墙水泥以后就会裂开！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10960697" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960697" style="width: 250px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46764759_10205311481703317_5623890173780557824_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-10960697 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46764759_10205311481703317_5623890173780557824_n.jpg" alt="" width="250" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960697" class="wp-caption-text">这是Finish Work第一道手续，用石膏泥加上纸条补接缝。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>屋内磁砖也是如此，铺完砖块需要等待7天，除非天气热、旱燥，不然不能上填缝剂（Grout）补接缝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10960700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960700" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46525460_10205311480783294_4428483737444941824_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10960700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46525460_10205311480783294_4428483737444941824_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960700" class="wp-caption-text">这是差不多最后一道手续，把石膏泥抺平，再用砂纸磨平之后才能粉刷。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>你自己不懂，只有被这些大师玩弄。屋主是付银子的老板，你难道事先不问问看，对方是否有基本的建筑常识？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10960710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46520117_10205311481783319_6890909571959750656_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10960710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46520117_10205311481783319_6890909571959750656_n-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960710" class="wp-caption-text">这是外墙的水泥（Stucco）。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10960713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960713" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46513509_10205311521224305_6041445124771151872_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-10960713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46513509_10205311521224305_6041445124771151872_n-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960713" class="wp-caption-text">这个外墙要准备作最后一道手续。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>地砖黏不住 哪里做错了？</h2>
<p>有网友来询问，DIY做的地磗为什么贴不住！会脱落？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10960703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46522847_10205311481263306_735783405962133504_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10960703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46522847_10205311481263306_735783405962133504_n-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960703" class="wp-caption-text">看人家水泥搅拌均匀，这个师傅厉害，磁砖与磁砖之间不放垫片（Spacers）。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在美国安装所有的产品，都会附带说明书，他们的政策，还有美国人工贵，希望屋主自己可以动手安装。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10960718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960718" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46631808_10205310316834196_836828271151677440_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-10960718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46631808_10205310316834196_836828271151677440_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960718" class="wp-caption-text">这是是纯石灰与细砂的水泥粉，加水搅拌均匀就可以使用。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>磁砖种类繁多，为了保持磁砖亮丽，厂商会在磁砖表面涂上一层水蜡。考承包商许可证（Contractor License）也考这个问题，磁砖使用之前，是否要浸泡水？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10960720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960720" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46733940_10205310316594190_9107598083834773504_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-10960720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46733940_10205310316594190_9107598083834773504_n-300x307.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960720" class="wp-caption-text">这种水泥齿牙纹必须要与磁砖背面齿纹成反方向，增加黏着面。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我自己曾经闹过笑话，以前承接一单快餐店，柜台前全部铺设磁砖。快餐店第3天开张，到了下午柜台前磁砖掉落一半，店长打电话来投诉。当晚我立刻带着人马又重新铺设磁砖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10960721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960721" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46507298_10205310317034201_5186929692963241984_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-10960721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46507298_10205310317034201_5186929692963241984_n-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960721" class="wp-caption-text">水泥搅拌不均匀，水分不够，而且受力不平均磁砖就会裂开。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10960723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10960723" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46523999_10205310317554214_1435552866902212608_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-small wp-image-10960723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/46523999_10205310317554214_1435552866902212608_n-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10960723" class="wp-caption-text">磁砖铺设之后，要等待7天才能补接缝。（建筑装修交流版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有些工作不是自己可以闭门造车，肚子容量多少就吃多少饭。我也有踩到地雷的时候！我来看图解说一些贴地砖的知识。#</p>
