<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《飙风特警》飞车追逐 曹政奭柳俊烈亲自上阵</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034544100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>廉晶雅、曹政奭、柳俊烈、孔晓振、全慧珍出席《飙风特警队》媒体试映会。（全景林／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年01月25日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心报导）韩国电影《飙风特警队 (??)》昨（24）日于首尔东大门举行媒体试映会。主要演员柳俊烈、孔晓振、廉晶雅、曹政奭、全慧珍等人皆出席活动现场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11002145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034541100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002145" title="Yum Jung Ah, Cho Jung Seok, Ryu Jun Yeol, Kong Hyo Jin, Jeon He Jin" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034541100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Yum Jung Ah, Cho Jung Seok, Ryu Jun Yeol, Kong Hyo Jin, Jeon He Jin" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002145" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）廉晶雅、曹政奭、柳俊烈、孔晓振、全慧珍出席《飙风特警队》媒体试映会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《飙风特警队》是一部为了追缉一位F1赛车出身的赛车手兼企业家郑再哲（曹政奭饰），警方特别成立肇事逃逸小组“飙风特警队”以侦办与调查案件的故事。而柳俊烈及孔晓振在戏中则饰演追缉曹政奭的警察。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11002191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034451100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002191" title="?Cho Jung Seok" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034451100707-600x400.jpg" alt="?Cho Jung Seok" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002191" class="wp-caption-text">曹政奭于《飙风特警队》中饰演“郑再哲”一角。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11002189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034454100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002189" title="Ryu Jun Yeol" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034454100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Ryu Jun Yeol" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002189" class="wp-caption-text">柳俊烈于《飙风特警队》中饰演肇事逃逸组的警察“民载”。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过去大多饰演甜美可爱角色的孔晓振，本次于《飙风特警队》中首次饰演警察。对于自己所饰演的角色，她表示自己在实际演出时，比原先剧本里的安排所表现的还要更加帅气一些。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11002161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002161" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034503100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002161" title="Kong Hyo Jin" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034503100707.jpg" alt="Kong Hyo Jin" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002161" class="wp-caption-text">孔晓振于《飙风特警队》中饰演女警官。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>孔晓振并谈及，为了演绎好这个角色，同时掌握好该角色在剧中与其他人物的互动，她也与导演讨论了很多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11002179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034506100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002179" title="Kong Hyo Jin" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034506100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Kong Hyo Jin" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002179" class="wp-caption-text">孔晓振出席《飙风特警队》媒体试映会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她还表示，这次在拍片过程中都没有人受伤，能够在开心的状态下拍摄完此戏，让她感到很开心，因此也希望大家能开心的享受这部电影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11002167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002167" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034500100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002167" title="Kong Hyo Jin" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034500100707.jpg" alt="Kong Hyo Jin" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002167" class="wp-caption-text">孔晓振出席《飙风特警队》媒体试映会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而首度挑战反派角色的曹政奭，对于自己饰演的“郑再哲”一角，表示：“虽然他是个坏的角色，但与其说他坏或是危险，不如说他是个奇怪的人。”他说明，该角色是为了生存、只考虑到自己，最终才变成一个不择手段的坏人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11002151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002151" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034516100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002151" title="?Cho Jung Seok" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034516100707.jpg" alt="?Cho Jung Seok" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002151" class="wp-caption-text">曹政奭出席《飙风特警队》媒体试映会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，柳俊烈及导演也透露，为了让大家更能感受到角色的情感，《飙风特警队》中的飞车追逐场景，大部分都是由演员们亲自上阵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11002152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002152" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034513100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002152" title="Ryu Jun Yeol" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034513100707.jpg" alt="Ryu Jun Yeol" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002152" class="wp-caption-text">柳俊烈出席《飙风特警队》媒体试映会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11002187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11002187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034533100707.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11002187" title="Yum Jung Ah" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190125034533100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Yum Jung Ah" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11002187" class="wp-caption-text">近日因演出《SKY CASTLE天空之城》而掀起话题的廉晶雅，出席《飙风特警队》媒体试映会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由柳俊烈、孔晓振、曹政奭及廉晶雅等人主演的韩国电影《飙风特警队》，将于1月30日在韩国上映。</p>
