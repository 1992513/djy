<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鲁娜墨西哥厨房Luna Mexican Kitchen</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0496-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>旧金山吃货：鲁娜墨西哥厨房Luna Mexican Kitchen。（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提供）</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年03月18日讯】文：百合?美食专栏作家<br />
这次品尝2019年上榜的平价米其林餐馆Michelin Gourmand——Luna Mexican Kitchen。餐厅不大，坐落于圣荷西小镇里面，我们从头抬、主食、甜点全部点过一遍，另加美味果汁，让我一一来介绍。</p>
<p>一走进餐厅便可以见到墨西哥大妈现场制作的传统手工润饼，服务小姐特别给我们特辣蘸酱，品尝看看他们的玉米片，对我来说其实可以再辣一点！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11120401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11120401" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0498.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11120401 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0498-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11120401" class="wp-caption-text">鲁娜墨西哥厨房Luna Mexican Kitchen室内用餐环境。（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们先叫了一份头抬Ceviche：Lime marinated wild black cod、Cucumber、Red Onion、Avocado、Habanero Pepper、Cilantro ~ Tortilla Chips。酸橘汁腌鱼，新鲜的凉拌黑鳕鱼搭配玉米片，十分开胃爽口。</p>
<p>看到旁边几位大妈全都叫同样的菜，于是伸头过去问问。原来吃的是受到大众妇女喜爱的El Buddha Bowl：Quinoa、Sweet Potato、Avocado、Black Beans、Cabbage Slaw、Chipotle Cashew Cream、Salsa Fresca。名字一听就知道是素食，赶快叫来一试，果然新鲜好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11120403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11120403" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/El-Buddha-Bowl.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-11120403 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/El-Buddha-Bowl-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11120403" class="wp-caption-text">湾区墨西哥餐：El Buddha Bowl。素食碗：藜麦、红薯、鳄梨、黑豆、卷心菜沙拉、墨西哥辣椒腰果奶油、莎莎弗雷斯卡。（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外又叫了一份Citrus Beet Salad：Organic Arugula、Red And Green Beets、Grapefruit、Organic Pickled Red Onion、Goat Cheese、Candied Walnuts ~ Champagne Vinaigrette。刚刚有没有提到这家是崇尚自然健康食品，所有的新鲜蔬果都是有机种植，美味、可口、新鲜、营养！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11120406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11120406" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Citrus-Beet-Salad.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11120406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Citrus-Beet-Salad-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11120406" class="wp-caption-text">湾区墨西哥餐：Citrus Beet Salad。柑橘甜菜沙拉：有机芝麻菜、红色和绿色甜菜、葡萄柚、有机腌红洋葱、山羊奶酪、蜜饯核桃，搭配香槟油醋汁。（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主食我们挑了两样：<br />
Enchilada Suizas：Two Chicken Enchilada、Queso Oaxaca、Jack Cheese、Crema、Tomatillos Salsa ~ Arroz Verde、Refried Beans。这里的鸡全部都是农场的走地鸡，吃起来鲜嫩有劲，不像是灌了水的软趴趴肉鸡，调料也做得十分恰当，好像都是少盐少油的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11120407" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11120407" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Enchilada-Suizas.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11120407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Enchilada-Suizas-600x563.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11120407" class="wp-caption-text">湾区墨西哥餐：Enchilada Suizas。安吉拉卷：两个安吉拉鸡肉卷、瓦哈卡奶酪、杰克起司、奶油、黏果酸浆莎莎，搭配绿色米饭、豆泥。（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>墨西哥人炖猪肉十分有一套，所以当然要叫一客猪肉来尝鲜——Chile Verde &#8211; pork braised in salsa verde ~ arroz verde, refried beans ~ hand- made corn tortillas。<br />
今天叫的菜大家都一致公认这一道最好吃，所以要是来这一家餐厅千万不要错过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11120408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11120408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Chile-Verde.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11120408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Chile-Verde-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11120408" class="wp-caption-text">圣荷西美食：Chile Verde。绿色智利：绿色莎莎酱炖猪肉，搭配绿色米饭、豆泥、手制玉米饼。（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>得提一提他们的饮料，从墨西哥进口的新鲜Aguas Fresco特制果汁。以及我们后来叫的一道甜点——Chocoflan：Rich Flourless Chocolate Cake Topped with Flan, Seasonal Fruit, Whipped Cream。巧克力蛋糕布丁，不知道是怎么样把布丁放到巧克力蛋糕上面的？蛋糕还不是用面粉做的，吃起来没有罪恶感，很好！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11120410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11120410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0520.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11120410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0520-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11120410" class="wp-caption-text">圣荷西美食：Chocoflan。巧克力蛋糕布丁：丰富的无面粉巧克力蛋糕配上果馅、时令水果、鲜奶油。（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11120411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11120411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0502.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-11120411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0502-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11120411" class="wp-caption-text">圣荷西美食：Aguas Fresco特制果汁。（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11120413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11120413" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0497.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-large wp-image-11120413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/IMG_E0497-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11120413" class="wp-caption-text">Luna Mexican Kitchen的饮料吧，他们的饮品不能错过！（湾区美食俱乐部/旧金山吃货提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实这一家真正的招牌菜是各式烤肉，有两人份、三人份、四人份，价格不菲，而且我们几个女生也吃不下那么多肉。但是这里所有的肉制食品全部是从农场“走地”的Range Style。</p>
<p>所有的蔬菜食品，包括豆类、做润饼的玉米全都是有机种植，可谓是十分的健康。这里服务一流，可以说是我在北加州吃过最好吃的墨西哥餐点。他们接受订位，餐厅后面有停车位，但是不多。有空可以来试试，早餐由9点开始，午餐11点开始。好吃！#</p>
