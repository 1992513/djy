<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>图说英国一周：克鲁夫茨狗狗秀（Crufts）</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年03月16日讯】</p>
<figure id="attachment_11117980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11117980" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1134985448-e1552728627296.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11117980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1134985448-e1552728627296.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11117980" class="wp-caption-text">(Christopher Furlong/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这只名为迪伦（Dylon）的蝶耳狗力克群狗，夺得今年克鲁夫茨狗狗秀的总冠军。它的主人非常高兴。</p>
<p>克鲁夫茨狗狗秀是全世界规模最大的狗狗比赛。今年有200多种约2.7万只狗参加比赛，包括3,600多只来自海外的小狗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11117981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11117981" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1129223859-e1552728690789.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11117981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1129223859-e1552728690789.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11117981" class="wp-caption-text">( OLI SCARFF/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>加上根杆子，就可以拿这把“拖布”去拖地了。</p>
<p>克鲁夫茨狗狗秀上的一只匈牙利牧羊犬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11117982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11117982" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1129054132-e1552728738439.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11117982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1129054132-e1552728738439.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11117982" class="wp-caption-text">( OLI SCARFF/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>克鲁夫茨是全世界爱狗者们的盛会，图为一名来自泰国的参赛者带来的狮子狗。不过，这么长的毛，这只小狗在泰国一定会感到很热吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11117984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11117984" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1134918158-e1552728784915.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11117984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1134918158-e1552728784915.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11117984" class="wp-caption-text">( Christopher Furlong/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“主人说，美丽要付出代价，那我就忍一下吧。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11117985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11117985" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1129203462-e1552728832300.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11117985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1129203462-e1552728832300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11117985" class="wp-caption-text">(OLI SCARFF/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>养狗还是个力气活儿。图为一名狗主人在跟她养的比利牛斯山犬（也叫大白熊犬）玩耍。这种狗成年雄性狗的体重可以达到50多公斤。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_11117986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11117986" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/105967796_shedintree-e1552728879511.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11117986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/105967796_shedintree-e1552728879511.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11117986" class="wp-caption-text">（Dorchester消防站）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3月10日，狂风席卷英国。图为Dorchester的一间花园小屋被大风吹到了树上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11117987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11117987" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/105941352_hunterston1-e1552728927675.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11117987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/105941352_hunterston1-e1552728927675.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11117987" class="wp-caption-text">（法国电力公司）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苏格兰Hunterston B核电站三号核反应堆发现裂缝。3月初进行的检查中发现，核反应堆共有370处头发丝粗细的裂缝，相当于反应堆芯十分之一的石墨砖上有裂缝。</p>
<p>这个反应堆已经在一年前停用。运行这个电站的法国电力公司（EDF Energy）打算获得许可重开这个反应堆，并且将运行极限扩大到允许有最多700处裂缝。但是此前需要证明他们可以在任何情况下随时关闭反应堆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11117988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11117988" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1132749674-e1552729016638.jpg"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-11117988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/GettyImages-1132749674-e1552729016638.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11117988" class="wp-caption-text">( Leon Neal/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这幅画据说价值一亿镑。它描述的是圣经里面的一段故事，伯图里亚（Bethulia）被亚述人的军队围困，漂亮的寡妇朱迪斯（Judith）引诱了亚述人的将军赫罗弗尼斯（Holofernes），然后将他杀死，解了这座城市的围。旁边的老妇是朱迪斯的仆人。</p>
<p>2014年，在法国图卢兹的一栋大别墅的阁楼里，有人发现了这幅画。专家鉴定认为，它是在1607年左右绘制的，作者是意大利著名的巴洛克画家卡拉瓦乔（Michelangelo Merisi da Caravaggio）。</p>
<p>2016年，法国政府对这幅画设立了出口禁令，并且要求卢浮宫考虑是否要买下它。但是卢浮宫决定不买。</p>
<p>发现这幅画的人表示，这不能说明它不是真迹，原因可能是卢浮宫不想花钱。他们要价一亿镑，这相当于卢浮宫购买艺术品15年的预算，而且卢浮宫里已经有三幅卡拉瓦乔的画作了。</p>
<p>近日，这幅画来到伦敦，供有兴趣的人观看，正式的拍卖仪式将于今年7月27日举行，而且没有保留价。</p>
